HIGHER EDUCATION DEPARTMENT
CONSTITUTION OF THE AUTHORITIES BY THE FIRST VICE-CHANCELLOR OF ANNA UNIVERSITY,
TIRUNELVELI UNDER ANNA UNIVERSITY, TIRUNELVELI ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 348, Higher Education (I-1), 7th September 2009, Aavani 22, Thiruvalluvar Aandu 2040.]
No. II(2)/HE/454(c)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 56 read with Section 18 of the
Anna University, Tirunelveli Act, 2007 (Tamil Nadu Act 28 of 2007), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
specifies the 16th September 2009, as the date on or before which, the first Vice-Chancellor of
Anna University, Tirunelveli shall constitute the Syndicate, the Academic Council, the Finance Committee,
the Planning Board, the Boards of Studies and the Faculties of that University.
K. GANESAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
