In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-sections (1) and (3) of Section 10 of the Tamil Nadu 
Dr. M.G.R. Medical University Act, 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 37 of 1987), the Chancellor hereby appoints 
Dr. Sudha Seshayyan, Director and Professor, Institute of Anatomy, Madras Medical College, Chennai as the 
Vice-Chancellor of the said University for a period of three years with effect from the date of her assuming 
office or till she attains the age of seventy years, whichever is earlier.
