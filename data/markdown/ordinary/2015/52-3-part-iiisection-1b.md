AGRICULTURE DEPARTMENT
Amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Agricultural Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 250, Agriculture (AAI), 17th November 2015.]
No. SRO B-99/2015.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Agricultural
Service (Section I of Part III in Volume II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1969).
The amendments hereby made shall  come into force on the 26th November 2015.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules,—
(1) in rule 1, (a) Under the heading, “A - SELECTION CATEGORIES” under Class V, the expression “Category 7,
Deputy Director of Horticulture” shall be omitted;
(b) under the heading, “B - ORDINARY CATEGORIES” under class IX, the expression “Category-7,
Assistant Director of Horticulture including Lecturers in Horticulture and Curator, Government Botanical Gardens,”
shall be omitted;
(2) in rule 2, in sub-rule (a), in the Tabular columns, in column (1), “category 7”, under Class V and the corresponding
entries in columns (2) and (3) thereof shall be omitted;
(3) in the Annexure, in the Tabular columns, in column (1) under class IX, the expression “Category-7,
Assistant Director of Horticulture including Lecturers in Horticulture and Curator, Government Botanical
Gardens”, and the corresponding entries in columns (2) and (3) thereof shall be omitted.
Amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Agricultural Subordinate Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 251, Agriculture (AAI), 17th November 2015.]
No. SRO B-100/2015.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Agricultural
Subordinate Service (Section I in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual Volume, 1970).
The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on and from the 17th March 1986.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules,—
(1)  “in rule 1, under “CLASS-I”, the expression “I-A Horticultural Officer” shall be omitted;
(2)  in rule 2, in sub-rule (a), in the Tabular columns, under ”Class-I”, the expression “Category-1-A Horticultural
Officer” in column (1) and the corresponding entry in column (2) thereof shall be omitted;
(3) in rule 3, in the Table, under Class-I, the expression “1-A Horticultural Officer” in column (1) and the
corresponding entry in column (2) thereof shall be omitted;
(4) in rule 5, the expression “(1) (b) Horticultural Officers” shall be omitted;
