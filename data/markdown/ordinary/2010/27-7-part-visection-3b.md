(U.O. No. 60026/R.1/RC/2010-F.No.4101/R.1/RC/2009.)
No. VI-3(b)/27/2010.
It is hereby notified that as per sub-clause (1) (h) and (i), Clause 10 of Statutes of Tamil Nadu Veterinary and
Animal Sciences University, the following two specialists of eminence and three progressive farmers specialized
in Animal Husbandry and Fisheries have been nominated as members of the Research Council of Tamil Nadu
Veterinary and Animal Sciences University. Their term shall be three years with effect from 22nd April 2010.—
(h) Two Specialists of Eminence:
(i) Dr. T.M. Gowri Shankar, Director, Technical and Marketing, Natural Feeds (P) Limited, No. 19, Karupasamy
Street, K.K. Pudur, Coimbatore-641 038.
(ii) Dr. D. Narahari, Professor (Retd), No. 31/15, East First Main Road, IIIrd Floor, Shenoy Nagar,
Chennai-600 030.
(i) Three Progressive Farmers:
(i) Thiru S.K.M. Shivakumar, Managing Director, SKM Egg Products India Limited, Cholangapalayam,
Erode-638 154.
(ii) Thiru R. Lakshmanan, Managing Director, Shanthi Poultry Farm (P) Limited, No. 6/15, Main Road,
Pappampatty, Coimbatore-641 016.
(iii) Thiru A. Sivakumar, No. 31, Subramaniam Salai, R.S. Puram, Coimbatore-641 002.
Chennai-600 051,
