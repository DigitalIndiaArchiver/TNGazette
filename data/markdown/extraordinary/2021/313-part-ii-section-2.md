(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999). The Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the land/lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule 
below measuring 1.75.77 Hectare of land to the same, a little more or less needed for industrial purpose wit for the 
formation of new Broad Gauge Railway line between Villupuram - Dindigul situated in Pullampadi (South) Village of 
Lalgudi Taluk in the District of Tiruchirappalli.
The Plan of lands are kept in the oﬃ  ce of the Collector, Tiruchirappalli and Revenue Divisional Oﬃ  cer, Lalgudi may
