ENVIRONMENT AND FORESTS DEPARTMENT
Amendments to the Tamil Nadu Timber Transit Rules, 1968.
[G.O. Ms. No. 167, Environment and Forests (FR 13), 4th November 2010.]
No. SRO A-4/2011.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Sections 35 and 36 of  the Tamil Nadu Forest Act,
1882 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1882), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the
Tamil Nadu Timber Transit Rules, 1968.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules, in rule 3,—
(1) in sub-rule (i) under the heading fees for the expression “Rs. 100/-each” the expression “Rs.50/-each”
shall be substituted:
(2) in sub-rule (ii) under the heading fees for the expression “Rs. 100/-each” the expression “Rs.50/-each”
shall be substituted:
V. IRAI ANBU,
 Secretary to Government.

No. SRO A-5/2011.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 27 of the Right to Information Act, 2005
(Central Act 22 of 2005), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Tamil Nadu
Right to Information (Fees) Rules, 2005:—
