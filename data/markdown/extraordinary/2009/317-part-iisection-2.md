DTP—II-2 Ex. (317)
SCHOOL EDUCATION  DEPARTMENT
DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF THE TAMIL NADU SCHOOLS
(REGULATION OF COLLECTION OF FEE) ACT, 2009.
[G.O. Ms. No. 318, School Education (X2), 7th December 2009.]
No. II(2)/SE/646(c)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (3) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu
Schools (Regulation of Collection of Fee) Act, 2009 (Tamil Nadu Act 22 of 2009), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby appoints the 7th December 2009 as the date on which the said Act shall come into force.
M. KUTRALINGAM,
Principal Secretary to Government.
