In exercise of the powers conferred by the second 
paragraph of Section 4 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 
1873 (Central Act III of 1873), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, 
after consultation with the High Court, Madras hereby ﬁ xes 
the number of Subordinate Judges to be appointed for the

Judge assumes  charge of that Court.

hereby ﬁ xes Gudalur in the Nilgiris District as the place at 
which the Subordinate Judge’s Court shall be held.

In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 10 of 
the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 
1873), the Governor  of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that the 
Subordinate Judge’s Court at Udhagamandalam shall cease 
to have jurisdiction over the District Munsif Court, Gudalur 
including two Villages viz., Naduvattam and Masinagudi of 
Udhagamandalam Taluk and hereby ﬁ xes the local limits of 
the District Munsif  Court at Gudalur including two Villages 
viz., Naduvattam and Masinagudi of Udhagamandalam 
Taluk as  the local limits of jurisdiction of the Subordinate 
Judge’s Court at Gudalur with effect on and from the date 
on which the Subordinate Judge assumes charge of the 
Subordinate Judge’s Court at Gudalur.
