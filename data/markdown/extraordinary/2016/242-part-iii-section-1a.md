In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-sections (1) and (2) of section 16 of the Tamil Nadu Entertainments 
Tax Act, 1939 (Tamil Nadu Act X of 1939), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to 
the Tamil Nadu Entertainments Tax Rules, 1939:-
2. The amendments hereby made shall come into force on the 14th October, 2016.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules,-
1. for rule 20, the following rule shall be substituted, namely:-“20. (1) Any proprietor, who pays the tax on the 
basis of returns shall, after getting registration, apply to the Entertainments Tax Ofﬁ cer atleast three days before the 
entertainment, furnishing details as to the date, time and nature of the entertainment in electronic Form III, and also the 
rates of payment for admission to it. Except in cases where the Entertainments Tax Ofﬁ cer has by an order in writing, 
exempted the proprietor  from  doing so, the application shall be accompanied by the register in electronic Form II and 
the tickets of all classes  proposed for issue, the tickets of each class being printed in a different colour and bearing 
separate serial numbers. Each ticket shall have a foil and counterfoil, the foil bearing the words “To be detached at the 
entrance and given to the buyer” and the counterfoil bearing the words “To be detached at the entrance and retained 
by the proprietor”. The serial number shall be printed on both foil and the counterfoil. The foil shall be at least double 
the length of the counterfoil, the foil and the counterfoil shall be of the sizes at least 8 cm x 4 cm and 4 cm x 4 cm 
respectively or any larger proportionate sizes: 
Provided that the electronic tickets for admission to entertainments including advance booking coupons issued by 
the proprietor online or in counter shall be with the inscription of the name of the theatre in both Tamil and English 
and other details including the rate of admission and taxes either in Tamil or in both Tamil and English.  Such tickets 
shall be exchanged with the tickets already sealed by the Entertainments Tax Ofﬁ cer at the ticket counter.
 
(2) Tickets already printed otherwise than as above shall be valid till the old stocks of tickets are exhausted.

2. for rule 21-B, the following rule shall be substituted, namely:-  
 
“21-B. (1) The proprietor of every place from where television exhibition is provided shall, within thirty days 
from the date of commencement of such television exhibition, submit to the Entertainments Tax Ofﬁ cer an application 
for registration in electronic Form I along with the supporting documents.
 
(2) Every application for registration in electronic Form I shall be ﬁ led  with the recent passport size photographs 
along with digital signature of the proprietor, in the case of proprietorship concern, or the managing partner or anyone 
of the partners, in the case of a partnership concern and the authorized person, in all other cases:
     Provided that the applicant holding no digital signature shall submit the application electronically and furnish 
the manually signed hard copy of the electronic application before the registering authority within ten days from the 
date of such submission, as proof, along with copies of relevant documents necessary for registration like Voter identity 
card, Driving license, Passport, Permanent Account Number card, Unique Identiﬁ cation Card (Aadhaar card), etcetera: 
 
 
Provided further that the proprietor, managing partner, or the authorized person, as the case may be, shall 
appear before the Entertainments Tax Ofﬁ cer, when called upon to do so and sign before him on the copies of the 
photographs in case of submission of digitally unsigned application.
 
(3) On receipt of the application in electronic Form I, the Entertainments Tax Ofﬁ cer shall after satisfying 
himself as to the correctness of the information furnished in the application and the uploaded documents and after 
making such enquiry as he considers necessary within thirty days from the date of receipt of application, issue a 
digitally signed electronic certiﬁ cate of registration in Form I-A to the applicant. 
 
(4) Where a proprietor of television exhibition transfers his business to another, the transferee shall apply for a 
fresh Certiﬁ cate of Registration. The Entertainments Tax Ofﬁ cer shall issue the Certiﬁ cate of Registration after following 
the procedure laid down in sub-rule (3). 
 
(5) For any amendments other than transfer of business, such as change in constitution and place of business, 
the proprietor shall ﬁ le an application in electronic Form I-AA with supporting documents and with digital signature 
certiﬁ cate electronically: 
     Provided that the applicant holding no digital signature shall submit the application electronically and furnish 
the manually signed hard copy of the electronic application before the registering authority within ten days from the 
date of such submission, as proof, along with copies of relevant documents necessary for registration like Voter identity 
card, Driving license, Passport, Permanent Account Number card, Unique Identiﬁ cation Card (Aadhaar card), etcetera:
 
 
Provided further that the proprietor, managing partner, or the authorized person, as the case may be, shall 
appear before the Entertainments Tax Ofﬁ cer, when called upon to do so and sign before him on the copies of the 
photographs in case of submission of digitally unsigned application.”;
