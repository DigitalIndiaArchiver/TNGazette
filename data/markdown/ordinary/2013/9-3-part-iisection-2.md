Notifications Under the Indian Stamp Act.
Provisions for the Consolidation of duty Chargeable in
respect of issue of "K" Series Non-Convertible
Redeemable Secured Debentures by the Newlink
Overseas Finance Limited under the Act.
[G.O. Rt. No. 107, Commercial Taxes and Registration (J1),
12th February 2013.]
No. II(2)/CTR/235/2013.— In exercise of the powers conferred
by clause (b) of sub-section (1) of Section 9 of the Indian Stamp
Act, 1899 (Central Act II of 1899), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby provides for the consolidation of duty of Rs.75,000/-
(Rupees seventy five thousand only) chargeable under the said
Act in respect of issue of “K” Series Non-Convertible Redeemable
Secured Debentures by the Newlink Overseas Finance Limited
in the State of Tamil Nadu.
Provision for the Consolidation of duty Chargeable in
respect of issue of policies by the New India Assurance
Company Limited for certain period under the Act.
[G.O. Rt. No. 108, Commercial Taxes and Registration (J1),
12th February 2013.]
No. II(2)/CTR/236/2013.—In exercise of the powers conferred
by clause (b) of sub-section (1) of section 9 of the Indian Stamp
Act, 1899 (Central Act II of 1899), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby provides for the consolidation of duty of Rs. 18,000/-
(Rupees eighteen thousand only) chargeable under the said Act
in respect of issue of policies by the New India Assurance
Company Limited in the State of Tamil Nadu for the period from
1st February 2013 to 28th February 2013.

HOUSING AND URBAN DEVELOPMENT DEPARTMENT.
Exemption from the Provisions of Development Regulations
of Second Master Plan for Chennai Metropolitan Area
relating to the Construction of Commercial Building at
T. Nagar, Chennai under the Tamil Nadu Town and
Country Planning Act.
[G.O.(3D). No.15, Housing and Urban Development [UD(VI)],
13th February 2013.]
No.II(2)/HOU/238/2013.—In exercise of the powers conferred
by Section 113 of Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning Act
(Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby exempts construction of Basement Floor + Stilt (Part) /
Ground Floor (Part) + First Floor + Mezzanine Floor + Second
Floor + Mezzanine Floor + Third Floor of Commercial Building
at Old Door No.156, New Door No. 20, North Usman Road,
T.Nagar, Chennai-600 017, In T.S.No.5237, Block No. 119 of
T. Nagar, Village Chennai, by relaxing the Regulation 26(4) E
and 26(4) D relating to Side Set Back (North) and (South) and
Floor Space Index of Development Regulations of Second Master
Plan for Chennai Metropolitan Development Authority Area 2026,
Subject to collection of penal fee equivalent to
(a) 1.25 x guideline value of equivalent land for violated
area for set back spaces violation amounting to floor Space
Index violation and
(b) 2.00 x guideline value equivalent land for the
violated floor area for FSI violation for regularization of set
back violation and FSI violation by the Chennai Metropolitan
Development Authority, and also subject to the condition that,
NOC shall be obtained from the Director of Fire and Rescue
Services prior to issue of completion certificate.
K. PHANINDRA REDDY,
Secretary to Government.
