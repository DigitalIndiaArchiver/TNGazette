JUDICIAL NOTIFICATIONS
Draft Amendment to Rule 1, Order VII of the Madras High Court Appellate Side.
(Roc. No. 194-A/2010/F1.)
No. SRO C-6/2012.
By virtue of the powers under clause 37 of the Letters Patent and all other powers conferred hereunto
enabling the High Court, Madras, hereby make the following amendment to Rule 1 of the Order VII of the High Court,
Madras, Appellate Side, 1965.
AMENDMENT
The following is the amendment to Rule 1 of Order VII of the Madras High Court Appellate Side Rules. After the
second proviso, the following third proviso shall be inserted:
“Provided further that in case the Court Orders Notice of Motion or Notice before admission on any Writ Petition,
the process fee paid for the Writ Petition at the time of filing may be utilized for effecting service. Even after such service,
if any respondents remain unserved and the Court admits the Writ Petition subsequently and issues rule nisi, then for
the unserved respondents, fresh process fee (Batta) will have to be paid by the petitioners”.
High Court, Madras,
G. CHOCKALINGAM,
19th June 2012.
