Under sub-section (1) of section 51 of the Coimbatore City Municipal Corporation Act, 1981 (Tamil Nadu
Act 25 of 1981) read with section 8 of the Thoothukudi City Municipal Corporation Act, 2008 (Tamil Nadu Act
27 of 2008) and in supersession of all previous notifications issued on the subject,  the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby divides the City of Thoothukudi into Sixty territorial divisions for the purpose of election of Sixty
councillors and hereby fixes the boundaries of the said sixty divisions, as described in the Schedule below.
  2. The delimitation shall take effect from the next ordinary elections to the council of Municipal Corporation
of Thoothukudi.
