ENVIRONMENT AND FORESTS DEPARTMENT
Amendment to Special Rules for Tamil Nadu Forest Service
[G.O. Ms. No. 45, Environment and Forests (FR-1), 25th May 2009.]
No. SRO B-36/2009.—In exercise of  the powers  conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Forest
Service (Section 15 in Volume II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1969).
2. The amendment hereby made shall come into force with immediate effect.
AMENDMENT
In the said Special Rules, in Rule 5, after sub-rule (2), the following sub-rule shall be added namely:—
“(3) Every person who has not attained the age of 54 years, appointed to the post of Assistant Conservator of
Forests by recruitment by transfer, shall undergo six weeks promotion linked compulsory training course in the Training
Institutions under the control of Ministry of Environment and Forests or the State Training Institutions or other established
Training Institutions in the Country within the period of probation (i.e.) within the total period of two years on duty within
a continuous period of three years.”
DEBENDRANATH SARANGI,
Principal Secretary to Government (in-charge)
HOME DEPARTMENT
Amendment to Special Rules for Tamil Nadu Jail Subordinate Service
[G.O. Ms. No. 401, Home (Pri.I), 18th May 2009.]
No. SRO B-37/2009.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Jail
Subordinate Service (Section 17 in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1970)
2. The amendment hereby made shall come into force with effect on and from the 18th May 2009.
AMENDMENT
In the said Special Rules, in Part II, under the heading “BRANCH III Probation Officers”, in Rule 2, after clause (ii),
in the proviso, for the expression “1:2”, the expression “2:1” shall be substituted.
S. MALATHI,
Principal Secretary to Government.

AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules, in Annexure IV, under the heading “Transport Department”, in the entries in column (2),
against the entry “Superintendents” in column (1) thereof,—
(1) for clause (b) of item (iii), the following clause shall be substituted, namely:—
“(b) one year (excluding the period of leave, if any availed) as Assistant in the Office of the State Transport
Authority.”;
(2) for item (iii), the following item shall be substituted, namely:—
“(iii) Service for a period of not less than,—
(a) two years as Assistant in the Offices of the State Transport Authority or State Transport Appellate
Tribunal or Joint Transport Commissioner or Deputy Transport Commissioner or Assistant Accounts Officer
(Internal Audit Wing); and
(b) one year as Accountant in any of the Offices of Regional Transport Officer of the Transport Department.”.
K.N. VENKATARAMANAN,
Secretary to Government.
