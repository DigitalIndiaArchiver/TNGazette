No.II(2)/CFCP/1001/2018.—In exercise of the powers 
conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 172 of the 
Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act, 1983 (Tamil Nadu Act 
30 of 1983), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby authorises 
the Additional Registrar of Co-operative Societies, Chennai 
Region and  the Regional Joint Registrars of Co-operative 
Societies, to exercise, within their respective jurisdiction, 
the powers vested in the Government by sub-section (7) 
of Section 33 of the said Act for the purpose of Appointing 
/ continuing the Managing Director to the Employees 
Co-operative Credit Societies which have an annual turnover 
of not less than rupees five crores and continuously 
made proﬁ t for not less than three years as per the 
audited accounts, in the cadre not higher than that of the 
Co-operative Sub-Registrar of the Co-operative Department, 
for a period of one year from the 9th November 2018.
DAYANAND KATARIA, 
Principal Secretary to Government.
