HIGHER EDUCATION DEPARTMENT
Appointment of certain person as Vice-Chancellor of Anna
University, Coimbatore for certain period under Anna
University, Coimbatore Act.
[G.O.(1D) No. 96, Higher Education(I-1), 21st May 2010,
Vaigasi 7, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No.II(2)/HE/399/2010.—In exercise of the powers conferred
under Section 11(1) and 3 of Anna University, Coimbatore
Act, 2006, His Excellency the Governor - Chancellor hereby
appoints Dr. K. Karunakaran, Professor and Director, Centre
for Water Resources and Head, Department of Civil Engineering,
Anna University, Chennai as the Vice-Chancellor of Anna
University, Coimbatore for a period of three years with effect
from the date of his assuming office.
K. GANESAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
HOME DEPARTMENT
Grant of Licence to grant certificates of Marriage between
Indian Christians under Indian Christian Marriage Act.
[G.O. Ms. No. 570, Home (Courts-IV), 11th June 2010.
No.II(2)/HO/400/2010.—The Governor of Tamil Nadu, under
and by virtue of the authority, contained in and conferred by
Section 9 of the Indian Christian Marriage Act, 1872 (Central
Act XV of 1872) hereby grants a licence to R. Sam Diwakar
(Victory Foundation) No. 600-A, Nesamani Street,
Balakrishnapuram, Dindigul District to grant certificates of
marriage between Indian Christians in accordance with the
provisions of the said Act, within the territories comprising the
State of Tamil Nadu.
K. GNANADESIKAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
