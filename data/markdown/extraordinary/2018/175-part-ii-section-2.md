The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied 
that the lands specified in the schedule below have to be 
acquired for industrial purpose to wit for the formation of new 
broad gauge Railway Line between Tindivanam to Nagari 
and it having already been decided that the entire amount 
of compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid 
out of the lands controlled or managed by Government. The 
following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of section 3 
of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes 
Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
Notice
Under sub–section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu 
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 
(Tamil Nadu  Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu 
hereby acquires the lands specified in the Schedule below and 
measuring 6.37.40 hectares/acres to the same, a little more or 
less needed for industrial purposes, to wit, for the formation of 
New broad gauge Railway Line between Tindivanam to Nagari.
 The plans of the lands are kept in the office of the Tahsildar, 
Arcot. and may be inspected at any time during office hours.
