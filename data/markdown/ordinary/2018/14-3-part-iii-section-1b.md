No. SRO B-30/2018.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution 
of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the rules published with the 
Tamil Development - Culture Department, Notification No. SRO B-319 of 1987, at pages 429-431 of Part III—Section-1 (b) of the 
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 14th October 1987 as subsequently amended.
Amendments
In the said Rules:-
	
(1) for rule 3, the following rule shall be substituted, namely:- 
	
	
"3. Appointment:- Appointment to the post shall be made:- 
	
	
	
(i) by recruitment by transfer from among the holders of the post of Superintendents in	
	
 
 
        the Office of the Directorate of Tamil Development classified under the Tamil Nadu   
	
	
	
   Ministerial Service; and 
	
	
	
(ii) by direct recruitment: 
	
Provided that appointment to the post by recruitment by transfer and direct recruitment shall be made in the 
ratio of 1:1: 
	
Provided further that a person working in the office of the Directorate of Tamil Development shall be eligible to apply 
for appointment by direct recruitment also.": 
	
(2) in rule 4, in the Table under the heading "Method of Appointment", serial number (3) and the entries relating 
thereto, in column (1) and the corresponding entries in column (2) thereof, shall be omitted. 
	
	
	
	
R. Venkatesan,
	
	
	
	
Secretary to Government.

No. SRO B-31/2018.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, 
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Highways 
Engineering Service.(Section 17 of Part III-A of the Service Rules in Volume-II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 2016) 
Amendments
In the said Special Rules, (1) in rule 3, in sub-rule (a), for the entries in column (2), against the category 
"4.Assistant Divisional Engineers" in column (1), the following entries shall be substituted namely:- 
"Promotion from among the holders of the post of Assistant Engineers in Category 5; or Recruitment by 
transfer from among the holders of the post of Junior Engineers in the Tamil Nadu Highways Engineering 
Subordinate Service."; 
(2) in rule 4, sub-rules (b) and (c) shall be omitted; 
(3) for rule 9, the following rule shall be substituted, namely:- 
"9. Filling up of vacancies in the post of Assistant Divisional Engineers - So far as qualified and suitable candidates 
are available, out of every four vacancies successively arising among Assistant Divisional Engineers, the first three shall be 
filled or reserved to be filled by promotion from among the Assistant Engineers in Category 5, possessing the Bachelor of 
Engineering (Civil) and the fourth vacancy shall be filled or reserved to be filled by recruitment by transfer from among the 
Junior Engineers of the Tamil Nadu Highways Engineering Subordinate Service possessing a Diploma in Civil Engineering 
awarded by the State Board of Technical Education and Training." 
Provided nothing contained in the rule shall adversely affect the serving Assistant Engineers possessing the qualification 
of B.E (Mechanical) as on 02-04-2018 and the serving Junior Engineer possessing the qualification of D.M.E (Mech) as on 
02-04-2018
