NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
COMMERCIAL TAXES AND REGISTRATION DEPARTMENT
ALTERATION OF THE LIMITS AND FORMATION OF NEW REGISTRATION SUB-DISTRICTS OF PUDUR IN THE
REGISTRATION DISTRICT OF TUTICORIN UNDER THE REGISTRATION ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 98, Commercial Taxes and Registration (G), 10th July 2009,
Aani 26, Thiruvalluvar Aandu 2040.]
No. II(2)/CTR/347(b)/2009.
Under Section 5 of the Registration Act, 1908 (Central Act XVI of 1908), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby,
with effect on and from the 11th July 2009 alters the limits of the Registration sub-districts specified in
column (3) and the Registration districts specified in column (4) of the Table below in the Revenue district of
Tuticorin by excluding the corresponding Villages specified in column (2) thereof from the said Registration
sub-districts and Registration districts and forms a new Registration sub-district of Pudur in the
Registration district of Tuticorin and in the said Revenue district.
The limits of the said Villages shall be the same, as determined for the purpose of revenue administration.
