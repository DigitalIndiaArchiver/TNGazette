The Governor of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied 
that the lands specified in the Schedule below are 
required for Highways purpose to wit for upgrading 
State Highways Road (SH-37) Oddanchatram – 
Dharapuram – Tiruppur Road  from KM 37/400 
to  KM 126/200 in Thangachiyammapatti Village, 
Oddanchatram Taluk,  Dindugul District and it having 
already been decided that the entire amount of 
compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be 
paid out of the funds controlled and managed by 
Divisional Engineer (Highways)  Tamil Nadu Road 
Sector Project-II, Salem and after having considered 
the cause shown by the owner or other persons 
having interest on such lands as the case may be, 
II-2 Ex. (129)
[ 1 ]
do hereby publish the following notice under sub-section 
(1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 
(Tamil Nadu Act, 34 of 2002)
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the 
Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 
2002), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the 
dry lands specified in the Schedule below and measuring 
to an extent of 28820 Square Metres for purpose of 
upgrading the State Highways Road, (SH-37) Oddanchatram 
– Dharapuram – Tiruppur Road from KM 37/400 to KM 
126/200 in Thangachiyammapatti Village, Oddanchatram 
Taluk  in Dindugul District.  
The plan of the lands under acquisition  are kept in 
the office of the Special District Revenue Officer (Land 
Acquisition Officer), Tamil Nadu  Road  Sector Project-II, 
Salem and Office of the Special Tahsildar  (L.A), Tamil 
Nadu  Road Sector Project - II, Tiruppur and may be 
inspected at any time during office hours.
