The Government of Tamil Nadu, having been satisfied
that the lands specified in the schedule below have to be
acquired for Industrial purpose, to wit, for the Extension of
runway at Coimbatore Airport and it having already been
decided that the entire amount of compensation to be
awarded for the lands to be paid out of the funds controlled
or managed by Government. The following notice is issued
under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Acquisition of land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Government of Tamil Nadu
hereby acquires the lands specified in the schedule below
and measuring 98100 Sq.mts. (24.24 Acre) to the same, a
little more or less needed for Industrial purpose, to wit, for
the extension of runway at Coimbatore Airport.
The plan of the lands are kept in the office of the Special
Tahsildar (Land Acquisition) extension of runway at
Coimbatore Airport. Unit-II, Block-14, Kalapatty Village,
Coimbatore North Taluk and may be inspected at any time
during office hours.
