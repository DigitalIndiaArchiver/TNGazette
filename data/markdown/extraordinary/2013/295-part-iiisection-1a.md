AMENDMENTS TO THE TAMIL NADU MUNICIPAL CORPORATIONS
PUBLIC HEALTH SUB-ORDINATE SERVICE RULES, 1996.

In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 106 of the Madurai City Municipal Corporation Act, 1971
(Tamil Nadu Act 15 of 1971), Section 108 of the Coimbatore City Municipal Corporation Act, 1981
(Tamil Nadu Act 25 of 1981), Section 8 of the Tiruchirappalli City Municipal Corporation Act,1994
(Tamil Nadu Act  27 of 1994), Section 8 of Tirunelveli City Municipal Corporation Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu
Act 28 of 1994) and Section 8 of the Salem City Municipal Corporation Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 29 of 1994),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu
Municipal Corporations Service Rules, 1996:—
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules, in Part-III, under the heading “SPECIAL RULES”, in the Tamil Nadu Municipal
Corporations Public Health Sub-ordinate Service Rules, 1996, in rule 4, in the Table, in column (3), against
the entry “Sanitary Inspector” in column (1),
(1)  against the entry “By deputation” in column (2), the expression “Food Inspectors’ Course and” shall
be omitted.
(2)  against the entry “By Promotion” in column (2), the expression “Food Inspectors’ Course and” shall
be omitted.
K.  PHANINDRA REDDY,
Secretary to Government.
