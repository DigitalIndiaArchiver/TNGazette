NOTICE UNDER SECTION 15(1) OF TAMIL NADU HIGHWAYS ACT, 2001 READ WITH THE TAMIL NADU LAND  
 ACQUISITION LAWS (REVIVAL OF OPERATION, AMENDMENT AND VALIDATION) ACT, 2019
No. VI(1)/12(b)/2022.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that the lands speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below to be acquired 
for Highway’s purpose to wit for Construction of Bypass road of Sankarankovil through KM 0/000 to KM 7/021 and it 
having already been decided that the amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid out of the 
funds controlled or managed by Divisional Engineer (Highways). The following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of 
section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) the Governor 
of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below measuring an extent of 34746 Square metres 
of dry land to the same, a little more or less needed for Highways purpose to wit, for the Construction of New Bypass 
Road in South Sankarankovil village of Sankarankovil Taluk, Tenkasi District .
 The plan of the lands is kept in the oﬃ  ce of the District Revenue Oﬃ  cer, Tenkasi may be inspected on any day 
during oﬃ  ce hours.
