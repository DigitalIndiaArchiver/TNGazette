 WHEREAS, a written complaint petition signed by ten Ward Members out of the sanctioned strength 
of fourteen Members of the Kadamalaikundu-Myladumparai Panchayat Union Council, Theni district against 
Tmt.S.Chandra Santhosam, Chairman of the said Kadamalaikundu-Myladumparai Panchayat Union Council, 
Theni district has been submitted to the Government as per clause (b) of sub-section (1) of section 207 of the 
Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994);
AND WHEREAS, a show cause notice under sub-section (1) of the said section 207 was issued against 
Tmt.S.Chandra Santhosam, Chairman, Kadamalaikundu-Myladumparai Panchayat Union Council, Theni district 
to submit her explanation within ten days from the date of receipt of the said notice.  She has submitted her 
explanation;
AND WHEREAS, a copy of the show cause notice along with the Explanation submitted by Tmt. S.Chandra 
Santhosam, Chairman, Kadamalaikundu-Myladumparai Panchayat Union Council, Theni district were forwarded to 
the Revenue Divisional Oﬃ  cer, Periyakulam, Theni district with a proposal for the removal of the said Chairman 
from oﬃ  ce, for ascertaining the views of the said Panchayat Union Council under sub-section (2) of the said 
section 207;
 AND WHEREAS, the Revenue Divisional Oﬃ  cer (i/c), Periyakulam, Theni district convened the Panchayat 
Union Council meeting on 10.06.2022 by following the procedure prescribed in the said section 207.  Then, 
the Revenue Divisional Oﬃ  cer (i/c), Periyakulam, Theni district has forwarded the minutes of the said meeting 
to the Government as per sub-section (11) of the said section 207, wherein it is ascertained that, 11 ward 
members of the said Council were present and out of 11 members 10 members conﬁ rmed the complaints 
already forwarded to the Government and requested to remove Tmt.S.Chandra Santhosam from the oﬃ  ce of 
Chairman, Kadamalaikundu-Myladumparai Panchayat Union Council, Theni district under the said section 207;

AND WHEREAS, the Government, after considering the complaint petition of the ten ward members of 
the Kadamalaikundu-Myladumparai Panchayat Union Council, Theni district, the explanation submitted by the 
said Chairman and the views of the said Panchayat Union Council, have decided to remove Tmt.S.Chandra 
Santhosam, Chairman, Kadamalaikundu-Myladumparai Panchayat Union Council, Theni district from the oﬃ  ce 
of the Chairman of the said Panchayat Union Council;
NOW THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (12) of section 207 of the 
Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby removes 
Tmt.S.Chandra Santhosam, Chairman, Kadamalaikundu-Myladumparai Panchayat Union Council, Theni district 
from the oﬃ  ce of the Chairman of the said Panchayat Union Council with immediate eﬀ ect.
