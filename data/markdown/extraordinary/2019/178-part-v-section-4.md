NOTIFICATIONS BY THE ELECTION COMMISSION OF INDIA
APPOINTMENT OF DATES FOR VARIOUS STAGES OF BYE-ELECTION TO TAMIL NADU 
LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY FROM 195. Thiruparankundram ASSEMBLY CONSTITUENCY 
AND FIXATION OF HOURS OF POLL TO FILL A VACANCY CAUSED BY THE death OF 
A MEMBER
No.  SRO G-39/2019.
The following Notification of the Election Commission of  India, Nirvachan Sadan, Ashoka 
Road, New Delhi-110 001, dated 22nd April, 2019 [2 Vaisakha, 1941 (Saka)] is published:-
No.100/TN-LA/21/2019: - Whereas, the seat of Shri A.K. Bose, in the Legislative Assembly of 
the Tamil Nadu State, elected from 195-Thiruparankundram Assembly Constituency has become 
vacant by reason of his death on 02-08-2018; and 
2.     Whereas in Election Petition Number 2 of 2017 challenging the election of Shri A K Bose 
and seeking further order to declare petitioner duly elected, Madras High Court vide Judgement 
dated 22-03-2019 to the said Election Petition No. 2 of 2017 and O.A. No. 955 of 2018 declared 
the election of Shri A K Bose from said constituency as null and void but declined to grant the 
further prayer to declare the petitioner elected; and
3.	
Whereas, a bye-election is to be held for the purpose of filling the vacancy so caused; 
and
4.	
Now, therefore, in pursuance of sub-section (1) of Section 150 and Sections 30 and 
56 of the Representation of the People Act, 1951 (43 of 1951), the Election Commission of India 
hereby: -
	
(A)	 Calls upon the said 195-Thiruparankundram Assembly Constituency in the State 
of Tamil Nadu to elect, before the 27-05-2019 (Monday) and in accordance with the provisions 
of the said Act and of the rules and orders made thereunder, a person, for the purpose of filling 
the said vacancy in the Legislative Assembly of the State; and

(B)	 Appoints, with respect to the said election,-
	
	
(a)	
the 29-04-2019  (Monday), as the last date for making nominations;
	
	
(b)	
the 30-04-2019 (Tuesday), as the date for scrutiny of nominations;
	
	
(c)	
the 02-05-2019 (Thursday), as the last date for withdrawal of candidatures; 
	
	
(d)	
the 19-05-2019 (Sunday), as the date, on which a poll shall, if necessary, be taken;
	
	
(e)	
the 27-05-2019 (Monday), as the date before which the election shall be completed, and 
 	
(C)	 Fixes the hours from 7.00 A.M. to 6.00 P.M. as the hours during which the poll shall, if necessary, be taken 
on the date specified above, for the election.
