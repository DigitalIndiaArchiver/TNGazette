The Governor of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that 
the lands speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below are required for 
Highways purpose to wit for four laning and strengthening of 
Oddanchatram – Dharapuram – Tiruppur Road (SH-37) from 
Km. 37/400 to Km 126/200 in Thangachiyammapatti, 
Ambilikai, Mandavadi, Kariyampatti, and Poosaripatti 
Villages, Ottanchatram Taluk in Dindigul District and 
it having  already been decided that the entire amount of 
compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid out 
of the funds controlled and managed by Divisional Engineer 
(Highways)  Tamil Nadu Road Sector Project-II, Salem and 
after having considered  the cause shown by the owner or 
other persons having interest on such lands as the case may 
be, do hereby publish the following notice under sub -section 
(1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001
(Tamil Nadu Act, 34 of 2002)
NOTICE
 
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu 
Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002), the Governor 
of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the dry lands speciﬁ ed in the 
Schedule below and measuring to an extent of 2433 Square 
Metres for the purpose of four laning and strengthening 
of Oddanchatram – Dharapuram – Tiruppur Road (SH-37) 
from Km 37/400 to Km. 126/200 in Thangachiyammapatti, 
Ambilikai, Mandavadi, Kariyampatti, and Poosaripatti 
Villages, Ottanchatram Taluk in Dindigul District.
The plan of the lands under acquisition  are kept in the 
ofﬁ ce of the Special   District     Revenue   Ofﬁ cer  (Land 
Acquisition   Ofﬁ cer), Tamil   Nadu  Road Sector  Project-II,
Salem  and  Ofﬁ ce   of  the  Special  Tahsildar (L.A), 
Tamil   Nadu  Road  Sector Project-II, Tiruppur and may be 
inspected  at any time during ofﬁ ce hours.
