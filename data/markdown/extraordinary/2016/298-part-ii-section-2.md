The Governor of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that
the lands specified in the schedule below have to be
acquired for Industrial Purpose, to wit for the formation of
ASH DYKE to Neyveli Tamil Nadu Power Ltd., (NLC) and it
have already been decided that the entire amount of
compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid as
per the orders of the Madurai Bench of Madras High Court
National Mega Lok Adalat, dated 12.03.2016 as a special
case from and  out of the funds controlled or managed by
the Neyveli Tamil Nadu Power Ltd., (NLC), Tuticorin and
having considered the cause shown by the owners or as
other persons having interest the said lands as the case
may be do hereby Published the following Notification under
sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition
of Land for Industrial Purposes Act 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act
10 of 1999)
NOTIFICATION
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act 1997 (Tamil
Nadu Act 10 of 1999) the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
acquired the lands specified in the schedule below and
measuring to an total extent of 115.87.5  hectares (Survey
Nos. 2 etc., Block Nos. 1 to 9) to the same, a little more or
less are needed for Industrial Purpose, to wit, for the
formation of  ASH DYKE to Neyveli Tamil Nadu Power Ltd.,
(NLC), Melapandiyapuram Village,  Ottapidaram Taluk,
Tuticorin District.
The plan of the lands are kept in the office of the Special
Tahsildar, Land Acquisition to ASH DYKE for Neyveli
Tamil Nadu Power Ltd., (NLC), Tuticorin may be inspected
at any time during office hours.
