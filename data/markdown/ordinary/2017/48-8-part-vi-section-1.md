Cancellation of Registration of O.1005, Karukurichi Co-operative Stores Ltd.
(G.L.F. 3/2003)
No.VI(1)/346/2017.
“Under Section 140 of the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act 1983, the Deputy Registrar, of Co-operative Societies,
Cheranmahadevi orders the cancellation of registration of O.1005 Karukurichi Co-operative Stores Ltd., with effect from.
31-08-2010.
Cheranmahadevi,

Variations to the Approved Master Plan for Coimbatore Local Planning Area.
(Roc. No. 3520/2016/LPA-1)
[G.O.(2D) No.123, Housing and Urban Development [UD4(1)] Department, dated  28-06-2017]
No.VI(1)/347/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 32 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning
Act 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972) and in exercise of powers conferred by the G.O.Ms.No.94, Housing and Urban
Development [UD4(1)] Department, dated 12-06-2009 which has been published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette
No. 27 Part II—Section 2, Page No. 228, dated 15-07-2009 the following variations are made to the Master Plan for the
Coimbatore Local Planning Area approved under the said Act and published in the Housing and Urban Development
Department Notification No.II(2)/Housing/4377/94 at Page 1078 of Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government
Gazette dated the 9th November 1994.
VARIATIONS
 In the said Master Plan, in the “LAND USE SCHEDULE” under the heading “COIMBATORE LOCAL PLANNING AREA
COIMBATORE CORPORATION AREA under the sub heading (a) Non Notified Detailed Development Plan Area in
Chettipalayam Village.
(i)
Against the entry “AGRICULTURAL” (AG -35) for the expression, “593 to 611”  the following entry 593  (Except
593/3)  “594 to 611”  shall be substituted.
(ii) Against the entry “RESIDENTIAL” the expression “593/3” shall be added.”
Coimbatore-12,
S. DHANARASU,
23rd November 2017.
Member-Secretary(in-charge),
Coimbatore Local Planning Authority.
