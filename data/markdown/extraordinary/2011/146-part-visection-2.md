TAMIL NADU STATE ELECTION COMMISSION, CHENNAI-600 106.
[ELECTIONS— URBAN  LOCAL BODIES—MAY 2011—CASUAL INDIRECT ELECTIONS TO FILL UP THE
VACANCIES IN THE OFFICES OF CHAIRMAN, VICE-CHAIRMAN OF VIKKIRAVANDI TOWN
PANCHAYAT—VILLUPURAM DISTRICT—POSTPONED—NOTIFIED.]
(S.O. No. 9/TNSEC/ME-2.)
No. VI(2)/287(b)/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 27 and sub-section (1) of Section 43-B of the Tamil Nadu
District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920) and sub-rule (4) of rule 99 of the Tamil Nadu Town
Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities, Municipalities and Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006, the Tamil Nadu
State Election Commission interalia notified the election to the Offices of the Chairman, Vice-Chairman of
Vikkiravandi Town Panchayat of Villupuram District to be held on 31-5-2011 as per the S.O. No. 8/2011/ME2,
dated 19th May 2011.
Whereas in the reference Rc. No. PC3/Pdl.17/2011, dated 18th May 2011, the District Collector, Villupuram
District has reported that the situation in the said local body area is very sensitive and not conductive to hold the
Indirect Elections due to the murder of Chairman and Vice-Chairman of the said Town Panchayat and communal
problem. Further, recommended to postpone the said election for the Offices of Chairman and Vice-Chairman of
Vikkiravandi Town Panchayat.
In view of the report sent by the District Election Officer/District Collector, Villupuram District and in exercice of
the powers conferred under rule 115 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities, Municipalities
and Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission hereby orders for the
postponement of the election to the Offices of Chairman and Vice-Chairman of Vikkiravandi Town Panchayat notified
in the S.O. No. 8/2011/ME2, dated 19th May 2011. Accordingly, the following amendments are issued to
the said S.Os.
