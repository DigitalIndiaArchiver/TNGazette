Tamil Nadu Administrative Tribunal (Salaries and Allowances and Conditions of Service of Chairman,
Vice-Chairman and Members) Amendment Rules, 2010.
[G.O. Ms. No. 77, Personnel and Administrative Reforms (SAT), 21st May 2010, ¬õè£C 7, F¼õœÀõ˜ Ý‡´-2041.]
No. SRO A-22/2010.—The following Notification issued by the Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances and Pensions,
Department of Personnel and Training, Government of India, New Delhi, the 29th March, 2010 is republished:—
G.S.R. 225(E)—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) read with clause (c) of sub-section (2) of
Section 35 and Section 36-A of the Administrative Tribunal Act, 1985 (13 of 1985), the Central Government hereby makes
the following rules further to amend the Tamil Nadu Administrative Tribunal (Salaries and Allowances and Conditions of
Service of Chairman, Vice-Chiarman and Members) Rules, 1988, namely:—
1. (1) These rules may be called the Tamil Nadu Administrative Tribunal (Salaries and Allownaces and Conditions of
Service of Chairman, Vice-Chairman and Members) Amendment Rules, 2010.
(2) They shall be applicable to the Chairman, Vice-Chairman and Members of the Tamil Nadu Administrative
Tribunal appointed before the 19th February 2007.
(3) They shall be deemed to have come into force on the 29th June, 1988.
2.
In Tamil Nadu Administrative Tribunal (Salaries and Allowances and Conditions of Service of Chairman, Vice-
Chairman and Members) Rules, 1988, in  rule (8), after sub-rule (2) the following sub-rule shall be inserted, namely:—
“(3) Gratuity:—The Members who have completed five years of service in the Tribunal shall be entitled for a
retirement gratuity equal to one fourth of the emoluments for each completed period of six months of service subject to
a maximum of sixteen and half time of the emoluments:
Provided that the total amount of gratuity payable to a Member under this clause and the amount of gratuity
drawn by him in respect of services rendered in an organization or department before joining Tamil Nadu Administrative
Tribunal shall not exceed the maximum amount of gratuity specified by the Central Government for officers of All India
Serivce at the time of his retirement from Government service.”
[No. A-11014/8/2009-AT]
RAJEEV KAPOOR,
Joint Secretary.
Explanatory Memorandum
The Central Government has decided to grant retirement gratuity to the Members of the Tamil Nadu Administrative
Tribunal who were appointed before 19th February 2007 for the service rendered by them in the Tamil Nadu Administrative
Tribunal (Salaries and Allowances and Conditions of Service of Chairman, Vice-Chairman and Members) Rules, 1988 are
being amended with retrospective effect.
2. It is certified that no Chairman, Vice-Chairman and Member of the Tamil Nadu Administrative Tribunal is likely to
be affected adversely by the proposed amendment being given retrospective effect.

No. SRO A-23/2010.—The following Notification issued by the Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances and Pensions,
Department of Personnel and Training, Government of India, New Delhi, the 9th December, 2009 is republished:—
G.S.R. 881(E)—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) read with clause (c) of the sub-section (2) of
Section 35 and Section 36-A of the Administrative Tribunal Act, 1985 (13 of 1985), the Central Government hereby makes
the following rules further to amend the Tamil Nadu Administrative Tribunal (Salaries and Allowances and Conditions of
Service of Chairman, Vice-Chiarman and Members) Rules, 1986, namely:—
1. (1) These rules may be called the Tamil Nadu Administrative Tribunal (Salaries and Allowances and Conditions of
Service of Chairman, Vice-Chairman and Members) Amendment Rules, 2009.
  (2) They shall be applicable to the Chairman, Vice-Chairman and Members of the Tamil Nadu Administrative
Tribunal appointed before the 19th February, 2007.
2. In rule 8 of the Tamil Nadu Administrative Tribunal (Salaries and Allowances and Conditions of Service of Chairman,
Vice-Chairman and Members) Rules, 1988 in sub-rule (2), for the words “rupees four thousand seven hundred and sixty
per annum”, the words “rupees fourteen thousand five hundred and thirty two per annum” shall be substituted with effect
from the 1st day of January, 2006.
[F.No. A-11014/8/2009-AT]
C.B. PALIWAL,
Joint Secretary.
Explanatory Memorandum
With a view to implement the recommendations of the Sixth Pay Commission regarding Central Government Employees'
penson, the Central Government has decided to revise the pension of the Chairman, Vice-Chairman and Members of the
Tamil Nadu Administrative Tribunal who were appointed before the 19th February 2007 with effect from the 1st day of
January, 2006. Accordingly, the Tamil Nadu Administrative Tribunal (Salaries and Allownaces and Conditions of Service
of Chairman, Vice-Chairman and Members) Rules 1986 are being amended with restrospective effect from the 1st day
of January, 2006.
2. It is certified that no Chairman, Vice-Chairman and Member of the Tamil Nadu Administrative Tribunal is likely to
be affected by the proposed amendment being given retrospective effect.
