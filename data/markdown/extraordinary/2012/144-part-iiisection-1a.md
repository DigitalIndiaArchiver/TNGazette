 AMENDMENT TO  CENTRAL SALES TAX (TAMIL NADU)  RULES, 1957.
[G.O. Ms. No. 67, Commercial Taxes and Registration (C-1), 1st  June  2012,
Vaikasi 19,  Thiruvalluvar  Aandu-2043.]
No. SRO A-17(b)/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-sections (3) and (4) of Section 13 of the Central Sales Tax Act,
1956 (Central Act 74 of 1956), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the
Central Sales Tax (Tamil Nadu) Rules, 1957:—
AMENDMENTS.
In the said Rules, -
(1) in rule 2,—
(a) in clause (h), for the expression “Assistant Commercial Tax Officer”, the expression
“Deputy Commercial Tax Officer” shall be substituted;
         (b) in clause (i), for the expression “Deputy Commercial Tax Officer”, the expression “Commercial
Tax Officer” shall be substituted;
         (c) in clause (j), for the expressions “Commercial Tax Officer” and “a Commercial Tax Officer”, the
expressions “Assistant Commissioner” and “an Assistant Commissioner” shall respectively  be substituted;
(d) in clause (k), for the expressions “Deputy Commissioner” occurring in two places, the expression
“Joint Commissioner” shall be substituted;

     (2) in rule 4-B,—
       (a) in sub-rule (2), for the expression “Commercial Tax Officer”, the expression “ Assistant
Commissioner” shall be substituted;
(b) in sub-rule (4), in clause (a), for the expression “Deputy Commissioner”, the expression
“Joint Commissioner” shall be substituted;
     (3) in rule 7,—
         (a) in sub-rule (1), for the expression “an Assistant Commercial Tax Officer”, the expression
“a Deputy Commercial Tax Officer” shall be substituted;
         (b) in sub-rule (2), for the expression “an Assistant Commercial Tax Officer”, the expression
“a Deputy Commercial Tax Officer” shall be substituted;
         (c) in sub-rule (3), for the expression “an Assistant Commercial Tax Officer”, the expression
“a Deputy Commercial Tax Officer” shall be substituted;
(4) in rule 10, in sub-rule (9), for the expression “Commercial Tax Officer”, the expression
“Assistant Commissioner” shall be substituted;
     (5) in FORM No. 2, for the expression “Deputy/Assistant Commercial Tax Officer”, the expression
“Commercial Tax Officer/Deputy Commercial Tax Officer” shall be substituted;
     (6) in FORM No. 3, for the expression “Commercial Tax Officer/Dy. Commercial Tax Officer/Asst.
Commercial Tax Officer”, the expression “Assistant Commissioner/Commercial Tax Officer/Deputy Commercial
Tax Officer” shall be substituted;
(7) in FORM No. 11, for the expression “Deputy Commissioner”, the expression “Joint Commissioner”
shall be substituted.
