DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF THE TAMIL NADU AGRICULTURAL LABOURERS - FARMERS
(SOCIAL SECURITY AND WELFARE) REPEAL ACT, 2011.
[G.O. Ms. No. 264, Revenue [LR.1(2)], 10th  September 2011.]
No. II(2)/REV/396(h)/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu
Agricultural Labourers - Farmers (Social Security and Welfare) Repeal Act, 2011, (Tamil Nadu Act 13 of 2011)
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the 10th day of September 2011 as the date on which the said
Act shall come into force.
R. RAJAGOPAL,
Principal Secretary to Government.
