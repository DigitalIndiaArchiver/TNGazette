NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION AND WATER SUPPLY DEPARTMENT
NOTIFICATION REGARDING TRANSFER OF SURAPET WATER TREATMENT PLANT/SCHEME FROM
TAMIL NADU WATER SUPPLY AND DRAINAGE (TWAD) BOARD TO CHENNAI METROPOLITAN WATER
SUPPLY AND SEWERAGE BOARD UNDER CHENNAI METROPOLITAN WATER SUPPLY AND
SEWERAGE ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 123, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (MW 2), 15th July 2009.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/358(a)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 26 of the Chennai Metropolitan Water
Supply and Sewerage Act, 1978 (Tamil Nadu Act 28 of 1978), the Governor of Tamil Nadu herein appoints
the 1st day of August 2009, as the date on which all properties, assets and liabilities of the Tamil Nadu
Water Supply and Drainage Board relating to Surapet Water Treatment Plant including all works in progress,
and all other works and services connected with the said place situated in or outside the said plant
shall stand transferred to, and vest in, the Chennai Metropolitan Water Supply and Sewerage Board.
NIRANJAN MARDI,
Secretary to Government.
