Variations to the Approved Sankarankoil Detailed Development Plan No. 2 of
Sankarankoil Local Planning Area
(Roc. No. 22812/2000/DP3.)
No. VI(1)/68/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred under the Section 33(2) of Town and Country Planning Act 1971, the Director
of Town and Country Planning, Chennai hereby confirms the following wariation to the approved Sankarankoil Detailed
Development Plan No. 2, Sankarankoil Local Planning Area approved by the Director of Town and Country Planning in
Proceedings Roc. No. 54133/95/DP2, dated 4th August 1994 and published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No. 2, Part
VI—Section 1, Pages 88, 89, dated 13th January 1999 and the said draft Notification was published in Tamil Nadu
Government Gazette No. 8, Part VI—Section 1, dated 2nd March 2005.
Since no objections and suggestions have been received on this draft notification within the stipulated time the same are
hereby confirmed and orderd as below.
VARIATIONS
1.
The entire Sankarankoil Detailed Development Plan No. 2 of Sankarankoil Local Planning Area approved by the
Director of Town and Country Planning in Proceedings Roc. No. 54133/93 DP2 is varied by Proceedings of the Director of
Town and Country Planning Roc. 22812/2000/DP3, dated 9th February 2005 of Detailed Development Plan No. 2 of
Sankarankoil Local Planning Area.
2.
The Plan thus varied is number as Map No. 5 DDP(V)DTCP No. 4/2005 and
3.
The Detailed Development Plan clauses and scheduled annexed with this plan made enforceable from the date of
publication of this Notification in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.

Variation to the Approved Master Plan for the Tiruchirappalli Local Planning Area
(Roc. No. 692/2010/TLPA-2.)
No. VI(1)/70/2012.
In exercise of the powers delegated to the Local Planning Authority for reclassification Streamlining—procedure under
Town and Country Planning Act 1971 published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, Issue No. 27, Part II—Section 2,
Page No. 228, dated 15th  July 2009, the Member-Secretary, Tiruchirappalli Local Planning Authority hereby makes the
following variation under Section (4) of Section 32 of Town and Country Planning Act 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972) to
the Master Plan of Tiruchirappalli Local Planning Area approved under the said Act and published with the Housing and
Urban Development Department Notification No. II(2)/HOU/453/09 at page 319 of Part II—Section 2 of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 2nd September 2009.
VARIATIONS
In the said Master Plan, in the “Land Use Schedule” under the heading (b) Non notified Detailed Development
Plan area in the Tiruchirappalli District, Tiruchirappalli Local Planning Area.
(i)
against the entry “Commercial” the old expression S.F. No. 1308/14, 1308/16B1, New Ward AL, Block No. 22,
T.S. No. 78/1 and 78/2 shall be added.
(ii)
againt the entry “MIXED RESIDENTIAL” the expression S.F. No. 130 B/14, 130B 16/B1, New Ward AL,
Block No. 22, T.S. No. 78 shall be substituted by the expression 118 to 132 except 130B/14 and 130 B16/B1, New Ward
AL, Block No. 22, T.S. No. 78/1 and 78/2.
