TAMIL NADU STATE LEGAL SERVICES AUTHORITY, CHENNAI
Nomination of Chairman for the High Court Legal Services Committee.
(TNSLSA No. 4443/E/2012)
No. VI(2)/260/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred under Sub Section (2) of Section 8A of the Legal Services Authorities Act, 1987,
(Central Act 39 of 1987) the Hon’ble the Chief Justice, High Court, Madras is pleased to nominate the
Hon’ble Mrs. Justice Chitra Venkataraman, Judge, High Court, Madras, to be the Chairman of the High Court Legal Services
Committee with effect from 21st September 2012.
High Court Compus, Chennai-600 104.
G. PALANIAPPAN,
25th September 2012.
District Judge/Member-Secretary.

and Anglo-Indian Schools as the case may be for cancellation:—
