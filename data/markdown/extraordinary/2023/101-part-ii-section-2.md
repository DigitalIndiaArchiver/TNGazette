CONSTITUTION OF A DISTRICT AND SESSIONS COURT AT MAYILADUTHURAI DISTRICT UNDER THE 
TAMIL NADU CIVIL COURTS ACT AND CODE OF CRIMINAL PROCEDURE.
 [G.O.Ms. No. 109, Home (Courts-III), 9th March 2023,  மாசி 25, சுபகிருது, திருவள்ளுவர் ஆண்டு-2054.]
No. II(2)/HO/301(d-1)/2023.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III 
of 1873), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby establishes a District Court at Mayiladuthurai by converting the 
existing Additional District Court at Mayiladuthurai.

[G.O.Ms. No. 109, Home (Courts-III), 9th March 2023,  மாசி 25, சுபகிருது, திருவள்ளுவர் ஆண்டு-2054.]

1873), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby fixes the number of District Courts in the cadre of District Judge to be 
established for Mayiladuthurai District as one and also alters the number of District Courts in the cadre of District 
Judge for Nagapattinam District as two with effect on and from the date on which the District Judge, Mayiladuthurai 
assumes charge of that Court.

ESTABLISHMENT OF A CHIEF JUDICIAL MAGISTRATE COURT AT MAYILADUTHURAI DISTRICT
UNDER THE CODE OF CRIMINAL PROCEDURE.
 [G.O.Ms. No. 110, Home (Courts-III), 9th March 2023, மாசி 25, சுபகிருது, திருவள்ளுவர் ஆண்டு-2054.]
