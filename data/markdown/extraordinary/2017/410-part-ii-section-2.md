APPOINTMENT OF COMMISSION OF INQUIRY TO INQUIRE INTO THE DEMISE OF THE 
LATE HON’BLE CHIEF MINISTER OF TAMIL NADU SELVI J JAYALALITHAA ON 5th DECEMBER 2016 
TO EXTENSION OF THE PERIOD OF THE COMMISSION OF INQUIRY BY SIX MONTHS 
FROM 25th DECEMBER 2017 UNDER THE COMMISSION OF INQUIRY ACT.
AMENDMENT TO NOTIFICATION
[G.O. Ms. No. 1039, Public (Law & Order-F), 22nd December 2017, Margazhi 7, Hevilambi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2048.]
No. II(2)/PULO/1026(f)/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Commissions of Inquiry Act, 1952 (Central Act 
LX of 1952), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Public (S.C.) Department Notiﬁ cation 
No.ll(2)/PUSC/793(g)/2017, published at page-1 of Part ll - Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, 
dated the 25th September 2017.
 AMENDMENT
 In the said Notiﬁ cation, in paragraph 3, for the expression “three  months”, the expression “nine  months” shall be 
substituted.
