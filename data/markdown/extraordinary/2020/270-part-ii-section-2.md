The Government of Tamil Nadu, having been satisﬁ ed that 
the lands speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below have to be acquired 
for industrial purpose of setting up Petroleum Product Storage 
Terminal of Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Limited at Sivadi 
Village and it having already been decided that the entire 
amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands to be 
paid out of the funds controlled or managed by Government. 
The following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 
3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes 
Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999.)
NOTIFICATION
Under sub section (1) of Section 3 of the
Tamil Nadu Acquisition of land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu 
hereby acquires the dry lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below 
and measuring 26.28.0 Hectares to the same a little more 
or less needed for Industrial Purposes, to wit, for setting up 
of new Petroleum Products Storage Terminal by Hindustan 
Petroleum Corporation Limited in Sivadi Village, Nallampalli 
Taluk, Dharmapuri District.
The plan of the lands are kept in the oﬃ  ce of the Special 
Tahsildar, Land Acquisition, HPCL, Dharmapuri District and 
may be inspected at any time during oﬃ  ce hours.
