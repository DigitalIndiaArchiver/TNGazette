No. VI(1)/33(a-1)/2023.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁed that the lands speciﬁed in the Schedule below to be acquired 
for the formation of Western By-pass Road in Tirunelveli District from NH-7 in Tharuvai Village of Palayamkottai Taluk in 
Tirunelveli District, and it having already been decided that the amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands are to 
be paid out of the funds controlled or managed by Divisional Engineer Highways Department. The following notice is issued 
under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002), the Governor 
of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands speciﬁed in the Schedule below measuring an extent of 77595 Sq.mtrs to the same, 
a little more or less needed for the formation of Western By-pass Road in Tirunelveli District from NH 7 in Tharuvai Village 
of Palayamkottai Taluk in Tirunelveli District.
The plan of the lands are kept in the Office of the Special District Revenue Officer (LA), Chennai – Kanyakumari Industrial 
Corridor Project, Tirunelveli functioning in the Door No. B-39, 19th Cross Street, Maharaja Nagar, Palayamkottai – 627 011 
and Office of the Special Tahsildar (LA), Chennai – Kanyakumari Industrial Corridor Project, Tirunelveli functioning in the 
Highways Assistant Divisional Engineer Office upstairs A.R.Line Road, Tirunelveli may be inspected on any day during 
office hours.
