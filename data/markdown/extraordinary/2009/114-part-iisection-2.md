APPOINTMENT OF COMMISSION OF INQUIRY INTO THE CAUSES AND CIRCUMSTANCES RESULTING
IN THE DEATH OF THIRU SURESH CONSISTING  OF A SINGLE MEMBER, NAMELY
HON'BLE THIRU S. TIRUPATHY UNDER THE COMMISSIONS OF INQUIRY ACT.
Amendment  to  Notification
[G.O. Ms. No. 407, Public (Law and Order-F), 4th May 2009.]
No. II(2)/PULO/195(b)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Commissions of Inquiry
Act, 1952 (Central Act LX of 1952), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment
to the Public (Law & Order-F) Department Notication No.II(2)/PULO/526(c)/2008 published at
pages 1-2 of Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the
6th November 2008 as subsequently amended.
AMENDMENT
In the said Notification, in paragraph 4, for the expression “six months”, the expression “seven months”
shall be substituted.
D. JOTHI JAGARAJAN,
Secretary to Government.
