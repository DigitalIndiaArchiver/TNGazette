(S.O. No. 77/2022/TNSEC/ME-2, dated the 23rd August 2022)
No. VI(2)/96(a)/2022.
In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (1) of section 43-B and section 27 of the Tamil Nadu 
District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920) and sub-rule (3) and (4) of rule 99 of the Tamil Nadu 
Town Panchayats, Municipalities and Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission 
hereby fixes 06.09.2022 as the date for convening meetings to elect the Chairman, Vice Chairman, as the case may 
be, and 07.09.2022 as the date for convening meetings to elect the Members of Taxation Appeals Committee, Members 
of Appointment Committee and Members of Contract Committee, as the case may be, shown in the Annexure, in 
accordance with the following programme:-
