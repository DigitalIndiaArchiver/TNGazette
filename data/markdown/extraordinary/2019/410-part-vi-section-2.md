Read:
S.O.No.16/2016/TNSEC/ME-1, dated  the 7th September 2016.
No. VI(2)/98(b)/2019.
In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-rule (2) of rule 10 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Elections) 
Rules, 1995 and sub-rule (2) of rule 11 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities, 
Municipalities and Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006 and in supersession of the earlier Notifications, the 
Tamil Nadu State Election Commission hereby directs that the Assistant Returning Officer appointed under the 
said Rules to assist the Returning Officer shall, subject to the control of the Returning Officer, be competent to 
perform all the duties and functions of the Returning Officer except the following:- 
1. Issue of notice of election; and 
2. Declaration of result of election, other than the Office of Councillor in respect of the Corporation.
       (By Order of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission)
