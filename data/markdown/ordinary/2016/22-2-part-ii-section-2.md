Constitution of Sub-Court at Tittagudi in Cuddalore District
under the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act.

Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby alters the number of
Subordinate Judges to be appointed under the said Act for
Cuddalore District as Eleven with effect on and from the
date on which Subordinate Judge assumes charge of the
Subordinate Judge’s Court at Tittagudi.

NOTIFICATION - III
 [G.O. Ms. No. 397, Home (Cts-III), 11th May 2016.
No.II(2)/HO/349/2016.—In exercise of the powers conferred
by section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central
Act III of 1873), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby fixes
Tittagudi as the place at which the Subordinate Judge’s Court
shall be held.
NOTIFICATION - IV
 [G.O. Ms. No. 397, Home (Cts-III), 11th May 2016.
No.II(2)/HO/350/2016.—In exercise of the powers conferred
by section 10 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central
Act III of 1873), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs
that the subordinate Judge’s Court at Virudhachalam shall
cease to have jurisdiction over the District Munsif-cum-Judicial
Magistrate Court at Tittagudi and hereby fixes the local limits
of the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court at Tittagudi
as local limits of jurisdiction of the Subordinate Judge’s Court
at Tittagudi with effect on and from the date on which the
Subordinate Judge assumes charge of the Subordinate Judge’s
Court at Tittagudi.
