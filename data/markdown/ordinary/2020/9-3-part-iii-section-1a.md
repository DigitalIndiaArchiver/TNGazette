No. SRO A-9/2020.—The Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Fundamental 
Rules and the Instructions thereunder.
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the date of issue of order.
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖග.
In the said Fundamental Rules, in rule 106, under the heading, "Instructions  under Rule 106" in Instruction 15, 
the following proviso shall be added, namely:-
 
"Provided that on implementation of the Integrated Financial and Human Resources Management 
System, the condition that the Government servant should apply for the credit of unavailed joining time within 
six months from the date of his transfer need not be insisted by the authorities concerned".
