HANDLOOMS, HANDICRAFTS, TEXTILES AND KHADI DEPARTMENT
APPOINTMENT OF COMMISSIONER OF TEXTILES AND COMMISSIONER OF HANDLOOMS 
AS REGISTRAR OF CO-OPERATIVE SOCIETIES UNDER THE TAMIL NADU 
CO-OPERATIVE SOCIETIES ACT, 1983.
[G.O. Ms. No.181, Handlooms, Handicrafts, Textiles and Khadi (C1), 
28th November 2022, கார்த்திகை 12, சுபகிருது, திருவள்ளுவர் ஆண்டு-2053.]
No.II(2)/HHTK/896(a)/2022.
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act, 1983 
(Tamil Nadu Act No. 30 of 1983), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoint the Commissioner of Textiles and 
Commissioner of Handlooms as Registrars of  Co-operative Societies for the State of Tamil Nadu in respect of the 
Co-operative Societies  under  their administrative control.
