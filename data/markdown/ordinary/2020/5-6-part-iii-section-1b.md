In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 read with Article 313 of the Constitution of India and of 
all other powers hereunto enabling, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Fundamental 
Rules and the Instructions thereunder.
2. 
Amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 16th day of July 2019.

under rule 85, under the heading “II. Special Casual Leave”, after rule 13, the following rule shall be added, namely:-
“14. Special casual leave  for a period of 10 days shall be granted to a Government Servant, who is undergoing 
Chemotherapy or Radiotherapy treatment for cancer, every time, starting from the day prior to the date of Chemotherapy or 
Radiotherapy, over and above the other eligible leave to him, subject to the production of Medical Certiﬁ cate from the Medical 
Oﬃ  cer in-charge of the medical institution where he has undergone the said treatment”
