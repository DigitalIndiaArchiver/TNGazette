NOTIFICATION REGARDING THE ENGAGEMENT OF PALLAVAN TRANSPORT CONSULTANCY

(Tamil Nadu Act 43 of 1998), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notifies the engagement of
Pallavan Transport Consultancy Services Limited for the preparation of Feasibility Study on Mono Rail
Project in Chennai.
