ACQUISITION OF LANDS
[G.O. Ms. No. 105, Industries (MIG.2), 21st May 2012.]
No. II(2)/IND/259(e)/2012.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied
that the lands specified in the schedule below have to be
acquired for Industrial purpose, to wit, for  setting up of
SIPCOT   Industrial Complex Phase II by SIPCOT it having
already been decided that the entire amount of
compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid out
of the funds controlled or managed by Government, the
following Notice is issued under sub-section (1) of
Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial
Purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTIFICATION
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby acquired the lands specified in the schedule below
and measuring 28.06.5 hectares, be the same, a little more
or less needed for Industrial Complex by SIPCOT.
The plan of the lands are kept in the Office of the Special
Tahsildar Land Acquisition, SIPCOT Unit- I, Thoothukudi
and may be inspected at any time during office hours.
