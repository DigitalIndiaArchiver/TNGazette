No. SRO B-8/2023.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, 
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Forest 
Subordinate Service (Section 12 of Part III-B in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 2016) 
	
2. The amendments hereby made shall come into force on the 1st January 2023.
Amendments
In the said Special Rules	 for rule 2-A the following rules shall be substituted:
"2-A, Appointment as Forest Watcher: Notwithstanding anything contained in sub-rule (a) of rule 2, the post of Forest 
Watcher shall be filled up from among the Social Forestry Workers, Plot Watchers or the Scheduled Tribes engaged as 
Anti-Poaching Watchers who have rendered service as such for a period of not less than ten years in total as on the 
1st January of the year in which recruitment to the post of Forest Watcher is made and the Scheduled Tribe Youths living 
in forest settlements of Eastern or Western Ghatsand other than Scheduled Tribes engaged as Anti-Poaching Watchers, 
who have rendered sevice as such for a period of not less than ten years in total as on the 1st January of the year in which 
recruitment to the post of Forest Watcher is made.
	
Provided that fifty five percent of the total vacancies shall be filled up by direct recruitment following the rule 
of reservation and forty five percent of the total vacancies shall be filled up equally from among the Scheduled Tribes 
Anti-Poaching Watchers, Scheduled Tribe Youths living in the forest settlements of Eastern or Western Ghats (by direct 
recruitment) and Anti-Poaching Watchers other than Scheduled Tribes.
