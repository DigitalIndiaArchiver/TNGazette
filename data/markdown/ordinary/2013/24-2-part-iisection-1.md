NOTIFICATION
In exercise of the powers conferred by section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Survey and Boundaries Act, 1923 (Tamil Nadu Act
8 of 1923), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby orders for the initial survey of non-surveyed coastal land abutting to the south
of survey numbers 595, 596, 598 and 600 in the Village of Kanniyakumari and 495, 497, 498, 531, 532, 533, 552 and 553
in the Village of Agastheswaram in Agastheswaram Taluk in Kanniyakumari District.
RAJEEV RANJAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
