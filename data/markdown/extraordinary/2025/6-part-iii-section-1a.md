AMENDMENTS TO THE TAMIL NADU URBAN LOCAL BODIES RULES, 2023
[G.O.Ms.No.3, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (MC.2), 7th January 2025,

No. SRO A- A/2025.
In exercise of the powers conferred by section 198 of the Tamil Nadu Urban Local Bodies Act, 1998 (Tamil Nadu 
Act 9 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby makes the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu Urban Local 
Bodies Rules, 2023:- 
Amendments.
   In the said Rules,- 
(1)	 in rule 322, in sub-rule (1), in THE TABLE,— 
 
(a) in the third column, under the heading “Devices”, for the figure “(2)”, the figure “(3)” shall be substituted;
	
(b)	 for the entries in columns (2), (3) and (4) against the category ‘5’ in column (1) thereof, the following entries 
shall, respectively be, substituted, namely:-
“
 (i)
Public utilities like Street 
lamp posts/ Kiosks/
Booths.  
Display/Panel boards with 
or without lights.
Not exceeding 0.6 m x 
1.0 m.
(ii)
Centre Median, Traffic 
island (Small Format 
Display).
Display/Panel boards with 
lights (Centre Median).  
Not exceeding 0.6m x 
1.0m
”;
(2)	
in rule 326, for sub-rule (4), the following sub-rule shall be substituted, namely:-
“(4) The maximum height of a hoarding as measured from the base of the floor shall not be more than 10 metres from 
the ground level or the first floor or second floor, as the case may be, for the devices mentioned in Category 1 in rule 322. 
The erection of such hoardings is permitted only upto the second floor.”

(b)	 to sub-rule (8), the following proviso shall be added, namely:—
“Provided that hoardings (small format display) shall be permitted in centre medians and traffic islands.”;
(3)	 in rule 334, for sub-rule (1), the following sub-rule shall be substituted, namely:—
	
“(1)	 The licence fee shall be levied and shall be collected for one year or two years or three years, as the case 
may be, as desired by the licensee.”.
