No. II(1)/HOPE/8(e)/2020.	 
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of section 18-B of the Tamil Nadu  Prohibition  Act, 1937 
(Tamil Nadu Act X of 1937), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Prohibition 
and Excise Department Notification No.II(1)/PE/239(d)/81, published at page 2 of Part II-Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu 
Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 1st May 1981, as subsequently amended.
2. The amendments hereby made shall come into force on 29th May, 2020.
Amendments.
	
In the said Notification, in the TABLE, under the entry "Indian Made Foreign Spirits (brand names of which are 
specified in the Annexure) other than sacramental wine" in column (1),-
(1)	
under the heading "(i) ORDINARY BRANDS", for the expression "which does not exceed Rs.684 per case 
in respect of brands manufactured in the State", the expression "which does not exceed Rs.780 per case in 
respect of  brands manufactured  in  the  State"  shall be substituted;
(2)	
under the heading "(ii) MEDIUM BRANDS", for the expression "between   Rs.685 to Rs.753  per case in 
respect of brands manufactured in the State", the expression "between Rs.781 to Rs.849 per case in respect 
of brands manufactured in the State"  shall be substituted; and
(3)  under the heading "(iii) PREMIUM BRANDS",-    
(i)	
in clause (a) for the expression "Rs.754 to Rs.2253 per case”, the expression “Rs.850 to Rs.2349 per case” 
shall be substituted; and 
(ii)	 in clause (b), for  the  expression  “Rs.2254   and  above  per case”, the   expression “Rs.2350 and 
above per case”  shall be substituted.
