REVENUE DEPARTMENT
Cancellation of Notification relating to the Holding of Lands of Tvl. National Fireworks Factory of
Sivakasi Taluk, in Virudhunagar District under Tamil Nadu Land Reforms (Fixation of Ceiling on Land) Act.
[G.O.(Ms.) No. 387, Revenue [L.R-I(2)], 2nd November 2012.]
No. II(1)/REV/34/2012.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 18-C of the Tamil Nadu Land Reforms
(Fixation of Ceiling of Land) Act, 1961 (Tamil Nadu Act 58 of 1961), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby Cancels the
Revenue Department Notification No. II(1)/REV/66/95, published at Pages 1 and 2 of Part II—Section1 (Supplement) of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 3rd May 1995.
RAJEEV RANJAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
