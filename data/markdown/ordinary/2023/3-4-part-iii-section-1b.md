No. SRO B-2/2023.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, 
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Panchayat 
Development Engineering Subordinate Service (Section 56 in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 2016):- 
2. 
(1) The amendments (1), (2) and (3) hereby made shall come into force on 25.08.2022. 
    (2) The amendment (4) hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on 15th September 2016.

In the said Special Rules,- 
 
(1)  in rule 4, for sub-rule (a), the following sub-rule shall be substituted, namely:- 
 
 
(a) "No person shall be eligible for appointment by direct recruitment to the categories, if he has completed 
the age of 35 years on the ﬁ rst day of July of the year in which the vacancy is notiﬁ ed."; 
 
(2)  in rule 5, sub-rule (b), shall be omitted;
   
(3)  in rule 7,- 
 
 
(i) in sub-rule (a), the expression "to a post in Category-I" shall be omitted; 
 
 
(ii) in sub-rule (b), the expression "to a post in Category-I" shall be omitted; 
     (4)  in rule 10, for the expression "The rule of reservation in General rule 22", the expression "The principle of 
reservation of appointments as provided in Section 27 of the Tamil Nadu Government Servants (Conditions of Service) Act, 2016 
(Tamil Nadu Act 14 of 2016)" shall be substituted.
