No. SRO B-1/2013.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu
Ex-servicemen’s Welfare Service (Section 25-B in Volume II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1969).
2.  The amendment hereby made shall come into force on and from 03-01-2013.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules,—
  (I) in rule 2, in the Table, - (i) after entry (ii) in column (2) against the category “3-Assistant Director of
Ex-servicemen’s Welfare” in column (1), the following entry shall be added, namely:—
 “or
   (iii) By recruitment by transfer from among the holders of the posts of Welfare Organiser in the Department of
Ex-servicemen’s Welfare”.
   (ii) for the Explanation in column (2), against the category “3 – Assistant Director of Ex-servicemen’s Welfare” in
column (1), the following explanation shall be substituted, namely:—
   Explanation:—The ratio between direct recruitment and recruitment by transfer from the post of Superintendent and
Welfare Organiser in the department of Ex-servicemen’s Welfare for the post of Assistant Director of Ex-servicemen’s
Welfare shall be 1:1:1.  However, when no qualified candidates are available for appointment by recruitment by transfer, direct
recruitment shall be resorted to fill up such vacancies also.
(2)  In rule 3, in the Table, for the entry (2) in column (3), against the category “3. Assistant Director
of Ex-servicemen’s Welfare” in column (1), and against the entry “Recruitment by transfer” in column (2), the following
entry shall be substituted, namely:—
“(2) Must have served either as Superintendent or as Assistant Secretary in the Department of Ex-servicemen’s
Welfare for a period of not less than three years or as Welfare Organiser in the Department of Ex-servicemen’s Welfare
for a period of not less than six years”.
A. CHITTARANJAN MOHANDOSS,
Secretary to Government.
