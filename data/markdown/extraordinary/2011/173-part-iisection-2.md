HOME DEPARTMENT
AMENDMENT TO THE NOTIFICATION OF THE APPOINTMENT OF PRESIDING JUDGE OF THE
DESIGNATED COURT UNDER THE TERRORIST AND DISRUPTIVE ACTIVITIES
(PREVENTION)  ACT.
[G.O.Ms. No. 316, Home (Courts II), 8th June 2011.]
No. II(2)/HO/253(b)/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 9 of the Terrorist and Disruptive
Activities (Prevention) Act, 1987 (Central Act 28 of 1987), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, with the concurrence
of the Chief Justice, High Court, Madras makes the following amendment to the Home Department
Notification No. II(2)/HO/3929 (b-2)/91, published at page 1 of Part II — Section 2 of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 11th July 1991, as subsequently amended:—
AMENDMENT
In the said Notification, for the expression “Thiru P. Ramalingam, I Additional City Civil and Sessions
Judge, Chennai”, the following expression shall be substituted, namely:—
“Tmt. S. Kalavathi, I Additional City Civil and Sessions Judge, Chennai”.
SHEELA RANI CHUNKATH,
Principal Secretary to Government.
