of 586 sq.mtr. (or) 6307.704 sq.ft. to the same, a little more or less needed for Industrial purposes, to wit, for the formation of 
'45 - Perumbakkam Metro Station’.
 The plan of the land/lands is/are kept in the office of the Collector, Chengalpattu and office of the District Revenue Officer 
(LA), Chennai Metro Rail Limited, Koyambedu, Chennai - 600 107 and may be inspected on any day during office hours.
