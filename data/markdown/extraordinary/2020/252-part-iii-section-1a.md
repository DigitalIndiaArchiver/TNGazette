AMENDMENTS TO THE TAMIL NADU GOODS AND SERVICES TAX RULES, 2017.
[G.O. Ms. No.98, Commercial Taxes and Registration (B1), 26th June 2020, Aani 12, Saarvari,  Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2051.]
SRO A-22(f)/2020.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 164 of the Tamil Nadu Goods and Services Tax Act, 2017 (Tamil Nadu 
Act 19 of 2017), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, on the recommendations of the Council, hereby makes the following rules 
further to amend the Tamil Nadu Goods and Services Tax Rules, 2017, namely:- 
1.  (1) These rules may be called the Tamil Nadu Goods and Services Tax (Seventh Amendment) Rules, 2020.
 
(2) They shall be deemed to have come into force with eﬀ ect from the 01st day of April, 2020.
