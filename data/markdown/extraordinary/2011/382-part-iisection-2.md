MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION AND WATER SUPPLY DEPARTMENT
APPOINTMENT OF CHAIRMAN TO CHENNAI METROPOLITAN WATER SUPPLY AND SEWERAGE BOARD-
RESCINDED-UNDER THE CHENNAI METROPOLITAN WATER SUPPLY AND SEWERAGE ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 149, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (MW), 7th October 2011,
Purattasi 20, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/422(a)/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 4-A of the Chennai Metropolitan Water Supply
and Sewerage Act, 1978 (Tamil Nadu Act 28 of 1978), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby rescinds the Municipal
Administration and Water Supply Department Notification No. II(2)/MW/442(b)/2006, published at page 1 of
Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 24th August 2006.
S. KARUTHIAH PANDIAN,
Secretary to Government.
