No. VI(1)/268(a-1)/2021.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that the lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below are acquired 
under Chennai – Kanniyakumari Industrial Corridor Project for Highways purpose to the Widening and Strengthening 
of SH-09, Cuddalore - Madapattu Road (from K.M.24/400 to KM.25/600) and at 16. Panapakkam Village in Panruti 
Taluk, Cuddalore District, and it having already been decided that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded 
for the lands to be paid out of the funds controlled and managed by the Divisional Engineer(H), Cuddalore (CKICP), 
Tamil Nadu after having considering the cause shown by the owner or other persons having interest on such lands 
as the case may be do hereby publish the following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the 
Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (TN Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
 
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) read 
with the Tamil Nadu Land Acquisition Laws (Revival of Operation, Amendment and Validation) Act, 2019, the Governor 
of Tamil Nadu hereby notiﬁ ed that the Dry lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below and measuring to an extent of 985 
sq.mtr., are acquired in 16. Panapakkam Village in Panruti Taluk, Cuddalore District, to the same, a little more or less 
is needed for Chennai-Kanniyakumari Industrial Corridor Project for State Highways purpose, to wit for Widening and 
Strengthening in SH-09, Cuddalore - Madapattu Road (K.M. 24/400 to KM.25/600). 
The plan of the lands under acquisition are kept in the oﬃ  ce of the Special District Revenue oﬃ  cer (Land Acquisition), 
Chennai - Kanniyakumari Industrial Corridor Project, Kanchipuram and oﬃ  ce of the Special Tahsildar (Land Acquisition), 
Chennai – Kanniyakumari Industrial Corridor Project, Cuddalore may be inspected at any time during the oﬃ  ce hours.
