Winding up of Certain Co-operative Societies and Appointment of Official Liquidators in Certain Districts.
No. IND.01/DGL/2010, Dindigul District Stapathigal and Temple Products Manufacturers Industrial
Co-operative Society Limited.
(Rc. No. 25663/ICN/2012.)
No. VI(1)/375/2013.
Under the powers conferred on the Registrar of Industrial Co-operatives under Section 137(2)(b) of the Tamil Nadu Co-
operative Societies Act, 1983 (Act 30 of 1983), the affairs of the Dindigul District Stapathigal and Temple Products
Manufacturers Industrial Co-operative Society Limited. No. IND.01/DGL/2010, Dindigul have been ordered to be wound up with
immediate effect by the Industries Commissioner and Director of Industries and Commerce and the Registrar of Industrial
Co-operatives, Mandavelipakkam, Raja Annamalaipuram, Chennai-28 vide Proc. No. 25663/ICN/2012,
dated 27th August 2013.
Under Section 138(1) of the same Act, Assistant Director of Industries and Commerce (Industrial Co-operatives), District
Industries Centre, Dindigul has been appointed as the Official Liquidator of the said society for the purpose of liquidation.
K. DHANAVEL,
Secretary,

and Director of Industries and Commerce and

No. A-1975. Mahalipatti Rope and Tape Makers Cottage Industrial Co-operative Production and Sales Society
Limited, Madurai District.
(Rc. No. 35904/ICF/2011.)
No. VI(1)/376/2013.
Under the powers conferred on the Registrar of Industrial Co-operatives under Section 137(2)(b) of the Tamil Nadu Co-
operative Societies Act, 1983 (Act 30 of 1983), the activities of the Mahalipatti Rope and Tape Makers Cottage Industrial
Co-operative Production and Sales Society Limited No. A-1975, Madurai District have been ordered to be
wound up with immediate effect by the Industries Commissioner and Director of Industries and Commerce and the Registrar
of Industrial Co-operative Societies, Mandavelipakkam, Raja Annamalaipuram, Chennai-28 vide Proc. No. 35904/ICF/2011,
dated 2nd September 2013.
Under Section 138(1) of the same Act, Assistant Director of Industries and Commerce (In-Charge / Official Liquidator,
TAICO Bank, Chennai has been appointed as the Official Liquidator of the said society for the purpose of liquidation.

Variations to the Approved New Town Development Plan of the Hosur New Town Development Area.
(Roc No. 1103/2011/A2.)
[G.O.Ms. (2D) No. 29, Housing and Urban Development (UD IV-1), 13th February 2012.]
No. VI(1)/378/2013.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 32 of the Tamil Nadu  Town and Country Planning Act 1971
(Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972) the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following variations to the New Town
Development Plan of the Hosur New Town Development Area Approved under this said Act and published in the Housing and
Urban Development Department Notification No. II(2)/HOU/2861/84 at page No. 447, of Part II, Section 2, of the Tamil Nadu
Government Gazette, dated the 23rd May 1984.
VARIATIONS
In the said Hosur New Town Development Plan under the heading “General Industrial use zone in Village No. 21
Achettipalli Village, Achettipalli Panchayat the expression S.F. No. 402/3 with an extent of 3.71 Acres shall be added.
Under the heading, Agricultural use Zone (Dry) Village No. 21 Achettipalli Village, Achettipalli Panchayat the expression
S.F. No. 402/3 with an extent of 3.71 Acres shall be deleted.
Hosur,
P. RANGANATHAN,
18th September 2013.
Member Secretary (In-charge),
