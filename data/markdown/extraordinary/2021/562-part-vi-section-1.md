Notice under Section 15(1) of Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001(TN Act 34 of 2002) read with the

[Roc. No. M1/1530621/2021]
No. VI(1)/330(d-1)/2021.
The Governor of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that the land specified in the Schedule below and situated in the 
Oragadam Town in the Taluk of Ambattur in district of Chennai are required for widening of road in Highways purpose 
for formation of road widening of six lane from four lane from Thirunindravur to Padi in Chennai - Tiruthani - Renigunda 
Road and it having already been decided that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the land is to be 
paid out of the funds controlled or managed by the Chief Engineer (Construction and Maintenance), Highways Department 
after having considered the cause shown by the owner or other persons having interest on such lands, as the case 
may be, do hereby publish the following notice under Sub-Section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 
2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
Under Sub-Section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002), the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the land specified in the Schedule below measuring an extent of 0.05.41 
Hectares or 541 Square meters of dry lands to the same, a little more or less needed for Highways for formation 
of Widening of six lane from four lane from Thirunindravur to Padi in Chennai – Tiruthani – Renigunda Road Project 
located Oragadam (Ward A) Town in Ambattur Taluk of Chennai District.
The Plan of the lands is kept in the office of the Special District Revenue Officer (Land Acquisition), Chennai - 
Tiruthani - Renigunda Road Widening Project, Chennai-54 and in the office of the Special Tahsildar (Land Acquisition), 
(Unit - II) Chennai – Tiruthani - Renigunda Road Widening Project, Avadi and may be inspected at any time during 
office hours.
