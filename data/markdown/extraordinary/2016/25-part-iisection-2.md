In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 85 of the  Electricity Act, 2003,
(Central Act 36 of 2003) the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby constitutes a Selection Committee consisting of the
following Members for the purpose of selecting a Member of the Tamil Nadu Electricity Regulatory Commission:-
1.
Thiru. Justice T. Sudanthiram
–
Chairperson
(Retired Judge of the Madras High Court)
2.
The Chief Secretary to
–
Member
Government of Tamil Nadu -
3.
The Chairperson of the
Central Electricity Authority,
–
Member
2. The Selection Committee shall-
(a) finalise the selection of member within three months from the date on which the reference is made to it;
(b) recommend a panel of two names for every vacancy referred to it; and
(c) satisfy itself, before recommending any person for appointment as Member of Tamil Nadu Electricity
Regulatory Commission that such person does not have any financial or other interest which is likely to affect
prejudicially his function as Member.
N.S. PALANIAPPAN,
Additional Chief Secretary to Government.
