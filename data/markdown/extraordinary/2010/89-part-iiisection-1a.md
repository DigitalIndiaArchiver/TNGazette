HOME, PROHIBITION AND EXCISE DEPARTMENT
AMENDMENT TO THE TAMIL NADU LIQUOR RETAIL VENDING (IN SHOPS AND BARS) RULES, 2003.
[G.O. Ms. No. 17, Home, Prohibition and Excise (VI), 29th March 2010.]
No. SRO A-11(a)/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Sections 17-C, 17-D, 21 read with Section 54 of the Tamil Nadu
Prohibition Act, 1937 (Tamil Nadu Act X of 1937), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following
amendment to the Tamil Nadu Liquor Retail Vending (in Shops and Bars) Rules, 2003.
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 29th November 2009.
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, for rule 7, the following rule shall be substituted, namely:—
“7. Renewal of licence.—The licence issued to the Tamil Nadu State Marketing Corporation Limited under
rule 4 is renewable on payment of an annual privilege fee of Rs. 250 Crores (Rupees two hundred and fifty crores
only) as renewal fee in four equal instalments, on or before 28th November, 28th February, 28th May and
28th August”.
S. KARUTHIAH PANDIAN,
Secretary to Government (In-charge).
