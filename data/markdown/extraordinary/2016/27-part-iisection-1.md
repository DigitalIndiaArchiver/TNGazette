VACATION OF SEAT IN TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY DUE TO RESIGNATION OF
THIRU PALA. KARUPPIAH, MEMBER OF THE TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY.
[Letter No. 1043/2016-1, Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Secretariat (B.III), 5th February, 2016,
Thai 22, Manmadha, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2047.]
No. II(1)/LAS/E/2016.
Whereas, Thiru Pala. Karuppiah a Member of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly representing the
18. Harbour Assembly Constituency, in his letter dated 28th January, 2016 addressed to me as Speaker of the
Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly, has resigned his seat as Member of the said Assembly with effect from
28th January, 2016.
Now, therefore, I, P. DHANAPAL, Speaker of the said Assembly hereby accept the resignation of
Thiru Pala. Karuppiah, as envisaged under Rule 22 of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Rules as it is in
accordance with Rule 21 of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Rules. Under Article 190 (3)(b) of the Constitution
of India, his seat has, therefore, become vacant with effect from the above date.
Secretariat,
P. DHANAPAL,
Chennai-600 009,
Speaker,
Dated 5th February, 2016.
Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly.
A.M.P. JAMALUDEEN,
Secretary,
  Legislative Assembly Secretariat.
