No.SRO A-12/2021.— In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 32 of the Tamil Nadu Manual 
Workers (Regulation of Employment and Canditions of Work) Act, 1982 (Tamil Nadu Act 33 of 1982), the Governor of 
Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu Manual Workers (Regulation of Employment and 
Conditions of Work) Rules, 1986 namely:- 
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖගඛ.
In the said Rules,-
 
(1) in rule 3 in clause (b),-
 
 
(a) for the expression "Joint Commissioner of Labour", the expression "Additional Commissioner of Labour", 
shall be substituted;
 
 
(b) for the expression "Deputy Commissioner of Labour", the expression "Joint Commissioner of Labour", shall 
be substituted;
(2) in rule 18,-
 
(a) in sub-rule (1), for the expression "Deputy Commissioner of Labour", the expression "Joint Commissioner of 
Labour", shall be substituted;
 
(b) in sub-rule (2), for the expression "Labour Officer or Inspector of Plantation" the expression "Assistant Commissioner 
of Labour or Assistant Commissioner of Labour (Plantation)", shall be substituted.
Amendment to the Tamil Nadu Manual Workers (Construction Workers) Welfare Scheme 1994.

No.SRO A-13/2021.— In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 4 of the Tamil Nadu Manual Workers (Regulation 
of Employment and Conditions of Work) Act, 1982 (Tamil Nadu Act 33 of 1982) the Governor of Tamil Nadu after consultation 
with the Advisory Committee, hereby makes the following amendment to the Tamil Nadu Manual Workers (Construction 
Workers) Welfare Scheme, 1994, namely:- 
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖග.
In the said Scheme,- for the expression "Labour Officer (Social Security Scheme)", wherever it occurs, the expression 
"Assistant Commissioner of Labour (Social Security Scheme)", shall be substituted.
