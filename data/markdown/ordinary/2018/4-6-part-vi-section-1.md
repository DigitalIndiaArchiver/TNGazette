Conﬁ rmation of Variation to the sanctioned Kottur Road Extension Town Planning Scheme of Pollachi 
Local Planning Area.
(Roc.No. 16582/2014/DP2)
No.VI(1)/28/2018.—
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 33(2) of Town and Country Planning Act 1971, the Commissioner 
of Town and Country Planning, Chennai in Proceeding Roc. No. 16582/14/DP2, dated: 20-12-2017 hereby conﬁ rms the 
following variation to the sanctioned Kottur Road Extension Town Planning Scheme of Pollachi Local Planning Area 
sanctioned in G.O. No. 2450 Rural Development and Local Administration Department, dated: 15-12-1969 and published 
in Fort St. George Gazette No: 51 Part-II—Section-2 page 65 to 69 dated: 24-12-1969 and the said draft notiﬁ cation 
published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No. 15 Part VI—Section I page: 128&129, dated: 16-04-2008 in Publication 
No. VI(1)/145/2008. 
Since no objections and suggestions have been received on this draft notiﬁ cation within the stipulated time, the 
same are hereby conﬁ rmed and ordered as below: 
VARIATION
1. The entire Kottur Road Extension Town Planning Scheme of Pollachi Municipality sanctioned in G.O.No. 2450 
Rural Development and Local Administration Department , dated: 15-12-1969 is Comprehensively varied by the Kottur 
Road Extension Detailed Development Plan of Pollachi Local Planning Area. 
2. The plan thus varied is numbered as Map No. 5, DDP(V)/DTCP No. 05/2008 and, 
3. The Detailed Development Plan clauses and schedules annexed with this plan are made enforceable from the 
date of publication of this notiﬁ cation in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
Chennai-600 002, 
BEELA RAJESH,
20th December 2017. 
Commissioner of Town and Country Planning.
