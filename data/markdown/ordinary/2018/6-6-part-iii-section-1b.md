     FINANCE Department
Amendment to Adhoc Rules relating to the Temporary Post of Assistant Director in Government Data Centre in 
Tamil Nadu General Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 8, Finance (Co-operative Audit), 9th January 2018,  
Margazhi 25, Hevilambi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2048.] 
No. SRO B-9/2018.
In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of 
Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Rules published with the Finance Department Notification 
No. SRO B-181/95, dated the 3rd August 1995, published on page 200 of Part III—Section 1 (b) of the Tamil Nadu 
Government Gazette, dated the 6th September 1995.
Amendment 
In the said Rules, for rule 4, the following rule shall be substituted, namely:-
	
"4. Qualification:-No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post, unless he has served as Supervisor 
Grade-I in Government Data Centre for a period of not less than one year or as Supervisor Grade-II in Government Data 
Centre for a period of not less than three years".
Amendment to Adhoc Rules relating to the Temporary Posts of Supervisor Grade-I in the Department of  
Government Data Centre in Tamil Nadu Treasuries and Accounts subordinate Services.
[G.O. Ms. No. 9, Finance (Co-operative Audit), 9th January 2018,  
Margazhi 25, Hevilambi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2048.] 
No. SRO B-10/2018.
In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of 
Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Rules published with the Finance Department Notification 
No. SRO B-214/77, dated the 14th April 1977, published at pages 244-245 of Part III-Section 1 (b) of the Tamil Nadu 
Government Gazette, dated the 4th May 1977.
Amendment
In the said Rules, in rule 5, in "The Table" for the entries in column (2), against the entries "(i) By promotion", in 
column (1), the following entries shall be substituted, namely:-
	
"Must have worked as Supervisor Grade II in the Government Data Centre for a period of not less than one year.".

health and family welfare department
Amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Medical Services.
[G.O. Ms. No. 13, Health and Family Welfare (AA1), 9th January 2018,  
Margazhi 25, Hevilambi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2048.] 
No. SRO B-12/2018.
In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu 
hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Medical Services (Section 21 in Volume II of 
the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 2016). 
Amendment
In the said Special Rules, in Part II, under the heading 'Branch I- Medical',- 
	
(1)	 in the Annexure [referred to in rule 8 (c)], for the entries in column (2) against the entries "Class X Dieticians in 
Government Hospitals" in column (1) thereof, the following entries shall be substituted, namely:- 
	
	
"(i) 	 Must possess M.Sc degree in Food service Management and Dietetics or Foods and Nutrition or Food 
Science and Nutrition or Home Science with Nutrition and Dietetics as major or Home Science Emerging Technology, Food 
Service Management and Dietetics from any recognised University approved by the University Grants Commission: 
	
	
	
Provided that the candidate must also possess their Bachelor's degree in the same subject or with the 
fol!owing subjects as majors during their bachelor's degree course: 
	
	
	
(a)	 Nutrition / Clinical Nutrition; or 
	
	
	
(b) 	 Dietetics; or 
	
	
	
(c) 	 Food Service Management. 
	
	
(ii) 	 Previous practical experience for a period not less than two years in Multi Speciality Hospital (minimum 100 
beds strength) under the supervision of a qualified, experienced senior Dietician:"
j. radhakrishnan,
Principal Secretary to Government.
