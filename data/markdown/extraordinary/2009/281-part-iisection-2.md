HOME, PROHIBITION AND EXCISE DEPARTMENT
 
EXEMPTION FROM PAYMENT OF FEE PAYABLE BY THE TAMIL NADU STATE MARKETING CORPORATION
LIMITED, CHENNAI FOR CERTAIN PERIOD UNDER TAMIL NADU LIQUOR (SUPPLY BY WHOLESALE)
RULES.
[G.O. Ms. No. 70, Home, Prohibition and Excise (VIII), 2nd November 2009.] 
No. II(2)/HOPE/570(b)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Rule 32 of the Tamil Nadu Liquor (Supply by Wholesale)
Rules, 1983, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby exempts the Tamil Nadu State Marketing Corporation
Limited, Chennai-600 008, from the operation of the provisions of sub-rule (3) of Rule 15 of the said Rules,
so as to enable the said Corporation to remit the arrears of Special Privilege Fee for the financial year
2008-2009 before the end of November 2009 and the arrears of Special Privilege Fee for the months from
April 2009 to June 2009, before the end of December 2009.
2. This notification shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st day of April 2008 and shall remain
in force upto and inclusive of the 31st day of December 2009.
D. JOTHI JEGARAJAN,
Secretary to Government.
