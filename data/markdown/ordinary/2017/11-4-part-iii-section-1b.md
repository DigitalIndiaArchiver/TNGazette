FINANCE DEPARTMENT
Amendment to the Ad hoc Rules relating to the Temporary Post of System Programmer in Finance
Department in Tamil Nadu General Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 41, Finance (OP-I), 22nd February 2017,  Maasi 10, Thunmugi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2048.]
No. SRO B-15/2017.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following Amendment to the Ad hoc rules published with the Finance Department
Notification No. SRO B-44/2011, dated the 16th November 2011 at Pages 76 to 77 of the Part III-Section 1(b) of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 7th December 2011:-
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 26th August 2010.
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in rule 1 for the expression "System Programmer", the expression "System Analyst" shall be
substituted.
K. SHANMUGAM,
Additional Chief Secretary to Government .
