RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND PANCHAYAT RAJ DEPARTMENT 
AMENDMENT TO AD HOC RULES  FOR THE POST OF ASSISTANT ENGINEER (RD) 
TAMIL NADU PANCHAYAT DEVELOPMENT SERVICE.
[G.O. Ms. No. 172, Rural Development and Panchayat Raj (E3), 11th December 2018.]
No. SRO B-105(a)/2018.
In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor 
of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the adhoc Rules for the temporary post of Assistant 
Engineer in the Rural Development Department published with the Rural Development Department Notiﬁ cation 
No.SRO B-63/2000 at pages 75 to 77 of Part III-Section 1(b) of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, 
dated: 24th May, 2000.
2. The amendment hereby made shall come into eﬀ ect from the date of notiﬁ cation.
AMENDMENT
In the said rules, for rule 8, the following rule shall be substituted, namely:-
“8. Tests.– (1) Every person appointed to the post of Assistant Engineer by direct recruitment shall 
pass the following tests within the period of Probation, failing which the appointing authority shall, by order, 
discharge him from the post unless the probation is extended by the appointing authority.
 
 
(a) The Account Test for Public Works Department Oﬃ  cers and Subordinates and 
 
 
(b) The Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994.
 
 
 
 (2) Every person appointed to the post of Assistant Engineer by recruitment by transfer shall 
pass the tests prescribed in sub-rule (1) within a period of two years from the date of his joining as Assistant 
Engineer, failing which he shall be reverted to the lower post till he passes both of the said tests”.
