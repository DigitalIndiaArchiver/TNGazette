[G.O. Ms. No. 213, Industries (NLC), 24th December 2013, Margazhi 9, Vijaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2044.]
No. II(2)/IND/964(b)/2013.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that the lands specified in the schedule below have to be acquired for
Industrial Purpose to wit for Second Mine Expansion Scheme of Neyveli Lignite Corporation Limited and it having already been
decided that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid out of the funds controlled or managed
by Neyveli Lignite Corporation Limited.  The following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purpose Act, 1997.
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purpose Act, 1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands specified in the Schedule below and
measuring 4.52.5 hectares to the same a little more or less needed for Industrial purposes, to wit, for Second  Mine
Expansion Scheme of Neyveli Lignite Corporation Limited.
The plan  of the lands are kept in the office of the Special Deputy  Collector / Land Acquisition, Neyveli-2 and may be
inspected at any  time during office hours.
