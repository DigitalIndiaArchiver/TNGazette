ALLOCATION OF SUBJECTS AMONG MINISTERS
[G.O. Ms. No. 544, Public (Special-B), 27th June 2011, Aani 12, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No. I/PUSP/16(a)/2011.
Under Clause (3) of Article 166 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
directs that
(i)
Thiru T.K.M. Chinnayya shall continue to hold the portfolios of "Environment and Pollution Control".
Thiru T.K.M. Chinnayya be designated as Minister for Environment.
(ii) The portfolios "Rural Industries including Cottage Industries and Small Industries", hitherto held by
Thiru M.C. Sampath be allotted to Thiru C. Shanmugavelu. Thiru C. Shanmugavelu be designated as Minister for Rural
Industries.
(iii) The portfolios "Industries, Steel Control, Mines & Minerals and Special Initiatives", hitherto held by
Thiru C. Shanmugavelu be allotted to Thiru S.P. Velumani. Thiru S.P. Velumani be designated as Minister for Industries.
(iv) The portfolios "Implementation of Special Programmes including Implementation of the Election Manifesto",
hitherto held by Thiru S.P. Velumani be allotted to Thiru M.C. Sampath. Thiru M.C. Sampath be designated as Minister for
Special Programme Implementation.
(v) The portfolios "Animal Husbandry, Milk and Dairy Development", hitherto held by Thiru C. Karuppasamy
be allotted to Thiru N.R. Sivapathi. Thiru N.R. Sivapathi be designated as Minister for Animal Husbandry.
(vi) The portfolios "Sports and Youth Welfare", hitherto held by Thiru N.R. Sivapathi be allotted to
Thiru C. Karuppasamy. Thiru C. Karuppasamy be designated as Minister for Sports and Youth Welfare.
DEBENDRANATH  SARANGI,
