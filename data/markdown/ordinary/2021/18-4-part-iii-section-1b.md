HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE Department
Amendment to the Special  Rules for the Tamil Nadu Public Health Subordinate Service.
[G.O.Ms.No. 200, Health and Family Welfare (L1), 7th April 2021, Panguni 25,  
Saarvari, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2052.]
No. SRO B-19/2021.— In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, 
the Governor of Tarnil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Public Health 
Subordinate Service (Section 26 in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 2016).
Amendment
In the said Special Rules, in the Annexure (referred to in rule 5(b), under the heading "Class I", for item (ii) in column(2) 
against the entry "Statistical Assistant" in column (1) thereof, the following item shall be substituted, namely:- 
	
"(ii)	 For appointment by direct recruitment - A post graduate degree in Statistics or Bio-Statistics."
Amendment to Adhoc Rules for the Tamil Nadu Public Health Subordinate Service.
[G.O.Ms.No. 201, Health and Family Welfare (L1), 7th April 2021, Panguni 25,  
Saarvari, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2052.]
No. SRO B-20/2021.— In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution 
of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the adhoc rules published with 
the Health and Family Planning Department notification S.R.O.No.A-796 of 1969, at page 815 of Part V of the 
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 12th November 1969.
Amendment
In the said Rules, in rule 5, for sub-rule (b), the following sub-rule shall be substituted, namely:- 
 
"(b) Other Qualification:-  No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post unless the possess a degree in 
Statistics or a degree in Bio-Statistics." 
	
j. radhakrishnan,
Principal Secretary to Government.
