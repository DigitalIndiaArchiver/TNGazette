The Governor of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that the
lands specified in the schedule below have to be acquired for
Industrial purpose, to wit for the formation of Ultra Mega Power
Project and it having already been decided that the entire amount
of compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid out
of the funds controlled or managed by the Coastal Tamil Nadu
Power Limited, New Delhi and having considered the cause
shown by the owners or as other persons having interest the
said lands as the case may be do hereby published the following
notice under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Act  1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10
of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Industrial Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999) the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands specified in the schedule
below and measuring 11.16 acres to the same, a little more or
less are needed for Industrial purpose, to wit, for the formation
of Ultra Mega Power Project No.53.  Vilangadu Village,
Cheyyur Taluk.
The Plan of the lands are kept in the office of the Special
Tahsildar (Land Acquisition), Unit-V, Ultra Mega Power Project,
Cheyyur, Kancheepuram  may be inspected at any time during
office hours.

Whereas it appears to the Government of Tamil Nadu that
the lands specified below and situated in the Village Somenahalli
and Pangunatham Villages in Dharmapuri  Taluk, Dharmapuri
District are needed for a public purpose to wit, for the construction
of 765/400 K.V.  Power Sub-Station,  by the Power Grid
Corporation of India limited, notice to that effect is hereby given
to all whom it may concern, in accordance with the provision of
sub-section (1) of Section  4 of the Land Acquisition Act
1894(Central Act of 1894).
And, whereas it has become necessary to acquire the
immediate possession of the lands specified in the schedule
below, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that the lands
be acquired under the provisions of sub-section (2) of Section
17 of the said Act.
Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-
section (2) of Section 4 of the said Act. The Governor of Tamil
Nadu hereby authorizes the  Sub-Collector,   Dharmapuri her
staff and workmen, to exercise the powers conferred by the
said sub section and under clause (c) of Section 3 of the said
Act. The Governor of TamilNadu hereby appoints the
Sub-Collector, Dharmapuri to perform the functions of Collector
under the said Act.
Under sub-section (4) of Section 17 of the Act, Governor of
Tamil Nadu directs in view of the urgency of the case the
provisions of Section 5-A of the Act shall not apply to this case.
