COMMERCIAL TAXES AND REGISTRATION DEPARTMENT
NOTIFICATIONS UNDER THE TAMIL NADU VALUE ADDED TAX ACT, 2012.
DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF THE TAMIL NADU VALUE ADDED TAX
(FOURTH AMENDMENT) ACT, 2012.
[G.O. Ms. No. 81, Commercial Taxes and Registration (B1), 18th June  2012,
Aani 4, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2043.]
No. II(2)/CTR/308(f-1)/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax
(Fourth Amendment) Act, 2012  (Tamil Nadu Act 19 of 2012),  the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints
the 19th day of June 2012, as the date on which the said Act shall come into force.
DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF THE TAMIL NADU VALUE ADDED TAX
(FIFTH AMENDMENT) ACT, 2012.
[G.O. Ms. No. 82, Commercial Taxes and Registration (B1), 18th June  2012,
Aani 4, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2043.]
No. II(2)/CTR/308(f-2)/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax
(Fifth Amendment) Act, 2012  (Tamil Nadu Act 23 of 2012),  the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the
19th day of June 2012, as the date on which the said Act shall come into force.
SUNIL PALIWAL,
Secretary to Government.
