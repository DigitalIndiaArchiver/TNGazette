Amendment to Notification 
[G.O. Ms. No. 920, Public (Law & Order-F), 28th November 2018, Karthigai 12, Vilambi,  
Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2049.]
No. II(2)/PULO/1000(a)/2018.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Commissions of Inquiry Act, 1952 
(Central Act LX of 1952), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Public 
(Law and Order-F) Department Notification No. II(2)/PULO/76(f)2017, published at Pages 1-2 of 
Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 31st January 2017, as 
subsequently amended.
Amendment
In the said Notification, in paragraph 4, for the expression “twenty two months” the expression “twenty five months” 
shall be substituted. 
P. SENTHILKUMAR, 
Principal Secretary to Government (FAC).
