TAX ACT, 2017 AND TAMIL NADU GOODS AND SERVICES TAX RULES, 2017.
(No. 11/2022 -TNGST/  --PP2/GST-15/20/2022, Chennai, Thursday, December 8, 2022, Karthigai 22, 
Subakiruthu, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2053.)
No. VI(1)/559(c-1)/2022.
In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (3) of Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Goods and Services Tax Act, 2017 (Act 19 
of 2017), the Commissioner of State Tax hereby delegates the powers conferred on the Commissioner under clause (b) of proviso to 
sub-section (1) of Section 30 of the Tamil Nadu Goods and Services Tax    Act, 2017 to the Additional Commissioner (Revenue, Collection 
and Monitoring).
2. This notiﬁ cation shall come into force with immediate eﬀ ect.

Tamil Nadu Goods and Services Tax Act, 2017 (Act 19 of 2017) and sub-rule (2) and (4) of rule 83 and sub-rule (2) of rule 83B  of the Tamil 
Nadu Goods and Services Tax Rules, 2017 and in supersession of the Notiﬁ cation issued by the Commissioner of State Tax in Notiﬁ cation 
No. 13/2019/PP2/34099/2019, dated the  26th  September, 2019, published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary,

supersession, the Commissioner of State Tax hereby notiﬁ es that the Additional Commissioner (Review, Appeal and Revision),
