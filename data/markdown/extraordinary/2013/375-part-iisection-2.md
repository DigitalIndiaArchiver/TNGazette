The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied
that the lands specified in the schedule below have to be
acquired for the  Expansion of Existing Thoothukudi Air Port
Purpose, and it having already been decided that the entire
amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands are to
be paid out of the funds controled or managed by Transport
Department, the following notice is issued under sub-section
(1) of Section 3 of Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for
Industrial Purpose Act 1997.
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purpose Act 1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999). The Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby acquired the lands specified in the schedule below
and Measuring 23.24.0 hectares to the same, a little more
or less needed for the Existing Thoothukudi Airport
Expansion Purposes, to wit for the establishment of
Airport Development by the Transport Department of
Tamil Nadu State Government.
The plan of the lands are kept in the office of the Collector,
Thoothukudi and may be inspected at any time during office
hours.

Whereas in excise of the power conferred by the clause
(1) of the article 258 of the constitution, the President has
entrusted to the Government of Tamil Nadu in  letter
No. 4(10/65 Central II Food and Agriculture Community
Development and Co-operation (Agriculture) dated 23rd April
1966, with their consent to the functions of the Central
Government under the Land Acquisition Act 1894 (Central
Act 1 of 1894) in relations to acquisitions of land for the
Purposes of the Union in this State and whereas it has
already been decided that the compensation should be paid
by the public revenues.
Now, therefore in exercise of the powers conferred by
Section-6 of the said Act, the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby declares that the lands specified in the schedule
below measuring 0.49.85 hectares, be the same a little
more or less, is needed for a Public Purpose, to wit for the
formation of broad gauge railway line between
Mayiladuthurai and Thiruvarur at Sannanallur Village in
Nannilam Taluk of Thiruvarur District and under Sections
3 and 7 of the same act. The Revenue Divisional Office.
Thiruvarur is appointed to perform the functions of the
Collector under Act, and directed to take order for the
acquisition of the said lands. Under Sub-sections (1) (2) of
Section 17 of the Act, the Governor of Tamil Nadu further
directs that the possession of the said lands may be taken
immediately after the publication of the notice mentioned in
Section 9(1) of the Act. A plan of the land is kept in the office
of the Revenue Divisional Officer, Thiruvarur and may be
inspected at any time during office hours.
