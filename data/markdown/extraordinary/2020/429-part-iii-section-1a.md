AMENDMENT TO THE TAMIL NADU TRANSPARENCY IN TENDERS RULES, 2000
[G.O. Ms. No.374, Finance (Salaries), 19th October 2020, Aippasi 3, Saarvari, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2051.]
No. SRO A-37(a)/2020.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 22 of the Tamil Nadu Transparency in 
Tenders Act, 1998 (Tamil Nadu Act 43 of 1998), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following 
amendment to the Tamil Nadu Transparency in Tenders Rules, 2000. 
2. The amendment hereby made shall come into force on the 19th day of October 2020. 
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖග.
In the said Rules, for rule 33, the following rule shall be substituted, namely:- 
“33. Low Value Procurement:- For the purposes of clause (d) of Section 16 of the Act, ‘Low Value Procurement’ 
means any procurement, which is less than rupees ﬁ fty lakhs in value for construction, which is less than 
rupees twenty lakhs in value for vehicles and which is less than rupees twenty ﬁ ve lakhs in value for all other 
categories of procurement inclusive of consultancy for construction".
 
 
S. KRISHNAN,
 
Additional Chief Secretary to Government.
