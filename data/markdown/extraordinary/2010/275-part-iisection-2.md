ENERGY DEPARTMENT
NOTIFICATION REGARDING EXEMPTION IN RESPECT OF THE ELECTRICITY TAX PAYABLE UNDER THE
TAMIL NADU TAX ON CONSUMPTION OR SALE OF ELECTRICITY ACT.
[G.O.Ms. No. 85, Energy (B1), 9th September 2010, ÝõE 24, F¼õœÀõ˜ Ý‡´-2041.]
No. II(2)/EGY/565(b)/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred by the sub-section (2) of Section 14 of the Tamil Nadu Tax on Consumption
or Sale of Electricity Act, 2003 (Tamil Nadu Act 12 of 2003), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes exemption
in respect of the Electricity Tax payable under the said Act on the consumption of electricity for own use by
H.T. consumers using their captive generating plants namely, gensets for a further period from
1st April 2010 to 31st March 2011.
P.W.C. DAVIDAR,
Principal Secretary to Government.
