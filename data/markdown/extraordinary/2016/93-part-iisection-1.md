DECLALRATION OF MONDAY, THE 16TH MAY 2016 AS A PUBLIC HOLIDAY ON ACCOUNT OF
POLL FOR GENERAL ELECTIONS TO TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY, 2016.
[G.O. Ms. No. 407, Public (Miscellaneous), 22nd April, 2016,
Chithirai-9, Thunmuki, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2047.]
No. II(1)/PUMIS/7(j)/2016.
Under the “Explanation” to Section 25 of the Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881 (Central Act XXVI of 1881) read
with the Notification of the Government of India, Ministry of Home Affairs No. 20-25-26 Public-I, dated 8th June 1957,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby declares Monday, the 16th May 2016, the date on which the poll for the General
Elections to Legislative Assembly, 2016 will take place in Tamil Nadu, to be a public holiday.
K. GNANADESIKAN,
Chief Secretary.
