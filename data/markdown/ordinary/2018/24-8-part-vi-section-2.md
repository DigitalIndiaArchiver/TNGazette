In exercise of the power delegation the Collector under the Tamil Nadu Panchayat Act, 1994, the Collector of Tiruvallur 
District under Section 134(2) of the Tamil Nadu Panchayat Act, 1994 that from and after the date of this notification, 
the lands situated in the under mentioned Tiruvotriyur I & II Village and Tiruvottriyur Taluk in Tiruvallur District can be 
give land usage permission to Railway Department for constructing fourth railway track between Ennore—Korukkupet 
described in the following scheme and the measuring the extent noted below, be the same, a little or more or less 
shall be excluded from the operation of the said Act.
