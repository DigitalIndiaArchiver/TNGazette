No.SRO.B-48/2018.-In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following rules:-
2. The rules hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on and from the 27th December, 2012.
RULES
The provisions contained in The Tamil Nadu State and Subordinate Service Rules/Tamil Nadu Government Servants 
(Conditions of Service) Act, 2016 (Tamil Nadu Act 14 of 2016) and applicable to the holders of the permanent posts in the 
Tamil Nadu General Service shall apply to the holders of the temporary post of Assistant Registrar (Administration and Court 
work) sanctioned from time to time, for the Circuit Bench of State Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission at Madurai 
in Co-operation, Food and Consumer Protection Department, subject to the modiﬁ cations speciﬁ ed in the following rules:-
2. 
Constitution:- The post shall constitute a distinct class in the said service.
3. 
Appointment:- Appointment to the post shall be made.
(i) by promotion from among the holders of the post of the Appeal Examiner and Court Ofﬁ cer  
in the State Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission.
(or)
(ii) by recruitment by transfer from among the holders of the post of Assistant Registrar in the 
High Court Service
(or)
(iii) by recruitment by transfer from any other service.
4. 
Appointing Authority:- The Appointing Authority for the post of Assistant Registrar (Administration and Court Work) 
shall be the Registrar, State Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission. 
5. 
Qualiﬁ cations:-
 
a) 
Any Bachelor degree from any University recognized by the University Grants Commission
and
 
A degree in Law of any University recognized by the University Grants Commission
or
 
b) 
A degree in Law under ﬁ ve year integrated course from any University recognized by the University Grants  
 
 
Commission.
6. 
Probation:- Every person appointed to the post under the method of recruitment by transfer shall, from the date 
on which he joins duty, be on probation for a total period of one year on duty within a continuous period of two years.
7. 
Pay:-The holder of the post shall be paid a monthly pay calculated in the pay band of Rs.15,600-39,100 PB3 with 
Grade Pay Rs.6600/-.
KUMAR JAYANT,
Principal Secretary to Government.

PLANNING, DEVELOPMENT AND SPECIAL INITIATIVES DEPARTMENT
Amendments to the Special Rules for Class XLIII of the Tamil Nadu General Subordinate Service.
[G.O.Ms.No.53, Planning, Development and Special Initiatives (ST.3), 4th June 2018, 
Vaikasi 21, Vilambi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2049.]
No.SRO B-49/2018.-In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, 
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for CLASS XLIII of the 
Tamil Nadu General Subordinate Service (Section 13 under Part-II of the Service Rules in Volume-III of the Tamil Nadu Services 
Manual, 1969).
2. 
The amendments hereby made shall come into force with effect on and from the date of publication of the Notiﬁ cation.
AMENDMENTS
 
In the said Special Rules, in part-II, in Class XLIII-ECONOMICS AND STATISTICS DEPARTMENT, in rule 4, in 
THE TABLE, in item 2 in column (3) against the enty ''Promotion'' in column (2) and the entry ''Statistical Inspectors'' in 
column (1) thereof,-
 
 
(a) for the expression "ﬁ ve years" the expression "three years" shall be substituted.
 
 
(b) for the expression "three years" in the second proviso, the expression "two years" shall be substituted.
ASHISH VACHHANI,
Secretary to Government.
