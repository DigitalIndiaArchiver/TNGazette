No. II(1)/HOPE/26/2022.—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 18-B of the
Tamil Nadu Prohibition Act, 1937 (Tamil Nadu Act X of 1937), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following 
Amendments to the Prohibition and Excise Department Notification No.II(1)/PE/239(d)/81, published at page 2 in
Part II—Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 1st May 1981, as subsequently 
amended:-
Amendments
In the said Notification, in the TABLE, under the entry "Indian Made Foreign Spirits other than sacramental wine" 
in column (1), in the Annexure,-
	
(i) under the heading "ORDINARY BRANDS", under the sub-heading "Thiruvalargal Southern Agrifurane 
Industries Private Limited", the entry "TI's Duchess Brandy" shall be omitted.
	
(ii) under the heading "PREMIUM BRANDS", under the sub-heading "Thiruvalargal Southern Agrifurane 
Industries Private Limited",  the entry "TI's Crown Prince Brandy" shall be omitted.

No. II(1)/HOPE/27/2022.—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 18-B of the
Tamil Nadu Prohibition Act, 1937 (Tamil Nadu Act X of 1937), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following 
amendments to the Prohibition and Excise Department Notification No.II(1)/PE/239(d)/81, published at page 2 of
Part II—Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 1st May 1981, as subsequently 
amended:-
Amendments
In the said Notification, in the TABLE, in the Annexure under the entry "Indian Made Foreign Sprits (brand names 
of which are specified in the Annexure) other than sacramental wine" in column (1),-
	
(1)	 under the heading "ORDINARY BRANDS", under the sub-heading "Thiruvalargal Southern Agrifurane 
Industries Private Limited", the following entries shall be omitted, namely:-
	
	
"Haywards Doctor Brandy",
	
	
"Haywards XXX Rum",
	
	
"Haywards Special Whisky",
	
	
"Haywards Special Brandy",
	
	
"SAFL Super Star Brandy",
	
	
"SAFL Super Star Whisky", and
	
	
"SAFL Super Star XXX Rum";	
	
(2)	 under the heading "MEDIUM BRANDS", under the sub-heading "Thiruvalargal Southern Agrifurane 
Industries Private Limited", the following entries shall be omitted, namely:-
	
	
"Director's Special Classic Whisky",
	
	
"Diamond Gin",
	
	
"MGM White Rum",	 and
	
	
"White Mischief Vodka":
	
(3)	 under the heading "PREMIUM BRANDS", under the sub-heading "Thiruvalargal Southern Agrifurane 
Industries Private Limited", the entry "Special Appointment Deluxe Brandy" shall be omitted.
	
S.K. PRABAKAR,
	
Additional Chief Secretary to Government.
