UNIVERSITY AUTHORITIES.
THE TAMIL NADU DR. M.G.R MEDICAL UNIVERSITY, CHENNAI.
Notice of election of one Professor to the Senate of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R  Medical University, Chennai
by the Teachers of all Affiliated Pharmacy Colleges from among themselves under Section 18 (3) (d)
of the Tamil Nadu DR. M.G.R Medical University,  Chennai, Act 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 37 of 1987.)
[No. EIII(4)/13803/2013.]
No .VI-3(b)/25/2014.
Notice is hereby given that an election by postal voting is to be held for electing  one Professor to the Senate of the
Tamil Nadu DR. M.G.R .Medical University, Chennai, as per clause (d) of sub-section (3) of Section 18 of The Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R
Medical University, Chennai, Act 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 37 of 1987) by the Teachers of all Affiliated Pharmacy Colleges from
among themselves to fill up the existing vacancy. The term of membership in the regular vacancy will be for a period of three
years from the date of election or up to the date on which he/she ceases to hold the post of
Professor of an Affiliated Pharmacy College, whichever is earlier.
2. Forms of Nomination papers may be obtained from the office of the  undersigned from 24-07-2014 to 01-08-2014 between
11.00 a.m and 3.00 p.m. (on all working days.)
3. Nomination papers duly filled in shall be submitted in an envelope addressed to the Registrar I Returning Officer and
superscribed “Nomination for election to the Senate under Section 18 (3) (d)” as  indicated below, by Registered Post with
Acknowledgement Due so as to be received by the Registrar / Returning Officer not later than 4.00 p.m. on 03-08-2014 or to be
delivered to the Returning Officer during office hours in person or by his/her proposer or by messenger under acknowledgement
within the date and hour mentioned above:—
Face of Envelope for Sending Nomination Papers
Nomination for election to the under Section 18(3)(d)
Registered Post Acknowledgement Due
Delivered by hand.

The Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R Medical University,

4. The nomination papers which are not sent by Registered Post  with Acknowledgement Due or are not delivered in person
by the nominee or by his /her proposer or by messenger, as required above, shall be declared invalid.
5. Nomination papers will be taken up for scrutiny at 4.45 p.m. on 04-08-2014.
6. List of valid nominations will be published at 5.30 p.m. on 04-08-2014 in the University premises.
7. Notice of withdrawal of candidates may be made as per the General Statutes of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical
University on or before 3.00 p.m. on 11-08-2014.
8.List of final valid nominations will be published in the University premises at 5.30 p.m. on 11-08-2014. A copy of final valid
nomination and the final list of voters will be despatched to the candidates.
9.  A numbered declaration paper, a ballot paper with the name of the candidates  bearing on it, the Returning Officer’s  initials,
a cover for sending Ballot Paper and an  envelope containing the address of the Returning Officer will be sent to the address
entered against the name of the voter in the final electoral roll through the Heads of the  Institutions/College in which he/she is
working, on 25-08-2014 (Monday). No Ballot  Paper will be issued in the Office to the voters on the day of election. The cover
containing the Ballot Paper and the declaration form in the envelope be sent by  Registered Post, so as to reach the Registrar
/Returning Officer not later than 4.00 p.m. on 10-09-2014 the day fixed for poll.

10. The Voter may, either in person or by an agent, deposit the envelope addresses to the Returning Officer in the ballot box
provided for the purpose at the office of the Returning Officer between 11.00 a.m. and 4.00 p.m, on 10.09.2014 (Wednesday).
11. Scrutiny and counting of votes and declaration of results will be from 10.30 a.m, on 11-09-2014 (Thursday)
till the process is over.
NOTE:
1. The intimation of valid nominations would be sent to the candidates with a copy of the notification of the programme for
the election containing last date of withdrawal and date of despatching the ballot paper to the Returning Officer
by post, etc.
2. If the number of candidates, who are validly nominated and who have not withdrawn their candidates in the manner
prescribed and within the time specified, does not exceed or is less than the number of vacancies to be filled, such
candidate shall be declared to be duly elected.
