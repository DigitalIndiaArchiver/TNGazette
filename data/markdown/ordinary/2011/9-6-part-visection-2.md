TAMIL NADU STATE LEGAL SERVICES AUTHORITY
Nomination of Chairman for the High Court Legal Services Committee.
(TNSLSA No. 1123/E/2011.)
No.VI(2)/168/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred under Sub Section (2) of Section 8A of the Legal Services Authorities
Act, 1987 (Central Act 39 of 1987) the Hon’ble the Chief Justice, High Court, Madras is pleased to nominate the
Hon’ble Tmt. Justice R. Banumathi, Judge, High Court, Madras, to be the Chairman of the High Court Legal Services
Committee with effect from 21 February 2001.
High Court Campus, Chennai-104.
K. RAMAKRISHNAN,
23rd February 2011.
District Judge/ Member Secretary (in Charge.)
