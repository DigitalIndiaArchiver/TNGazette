[Courts and Judges—District Judge—Thiru C.S. Murugan, District Judge
of Tamil Nadu State Judicial Service—Deputation from Tamil Nadu
State Judicial Service to the Puducherry Judicial Service—Orders—
Issued.]
The following Government Order is Published:—
[G.O.(D) No. 31, Public (Special-A),  22nd February 2012, Maasi 10,
Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2043.]
READ:
Form the Registrar General, High Court, Madras, D.O.Letter
No. 47/2011-Con.B2, dated 21st December 2011.
Order: No. 31, Public (Special A), 22nd February 2012.
The Government have considered the recommendation of the High
Court, Madras to depute Thiru C.S. Murugan, District Judge of the Tamil
Nadu State Judicial Service functioning as Principal District Judge, Dindigul
to the Puducherry Judicial Service for being posted as Chief Judge,
Puducherry, in the place of Thiru T. Ramasamy, who has been ordered
to be repatriated to his parent Department viz., Tamil Nadu State Judicial
Service.
2. The Government accordingly direct that Thiru C.S. Murugan, District
Judge of Tamil Nadu State Judicial Service functioning as Principal
District Judge, Dindigul be deputed to the Puducherry Judicial Service for
being posted as Chief Judge, Puducherry in the place of
Thiru T. Ramasamy, with effect from the date of assuming office
as such.
(By Order of the Governor)
DEBENDRANATH SARANGI,
Chief Secretary.
