ACQUISITION OF LANDS
(Roc.No.CPRRP/CLA/15(1)/IV-36/2022) 
NOTICE U/S 15(1) OF TAMIL NADU HIGHWAYS ACT, 2001 (TAMIL NADU ACT 34 OF 2002), READ WITH TAMIL NADU 
LAND ACQUISITION LAWS. (REVIVAL OF OPERATION, AMENDMENT AND VALIDATION) ACT, 2019. 
No. VI(1)/594(a)/2022.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that the lands speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below are acquired under 
Chennai Peripheral Ring Road Project for Highways purpose to wit, for formation of road connectivity from Ennore Port to 
Mahabalipuram (from K.m. 60.881 to K.m. 65.967) at No. 104, Polivakkam (Block-IV) Village, Tiruvallur Taluk, Tiruvallur 
District and it having already been decided that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands to be paid 
out of the funds controlled and managed by the Highways Department and after having considering the cause shown by the 
owner or other persons having interest on such lands, as the case may be, do hereby publish the following notice under 
sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) read with the Tamil Nadu 
Land Acquisition Laws (Revival of Operation, Amendment and Validation) Act, 2019.
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002), read with the 
Tamil Nadu Land Acquisition Laws (Revival of Operation, Amendment and Validation Act, 2019) the Governor of Tamil Nadu 
hereby notiﬁ es that the Dry Manai, Natham, Wet Manai, Wet, Dry and Manavari Patta lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below 
and measuring to an extent of 56,890 Sq.mtrs. of lands are to be acquired in No. 104, Polivakkam (Block-IV) Village, Tiruvallur 
Taluk, Tiruvallur District to the same a little more or less needed for the purpose to wit for formation of road connectivity 
from Ennore Port to Mahabalipuram (from K.m. 60.881 to K.m. 65.967) as part of Chennai Peripheral Ring Road Project, 
Highways Department. 
The Plan of the lands under acquisition are kept in the Oﬃ  ce of the Special District Revenue Oﬃ  cer (Land Acquisition), 
Chennai Peripheral Ring Road Project, Section-III, Chennai-600 058 and in the Oﬃ  ce of the Special Tahsildar (Land Acquisition), 
Chennai Peripheral Ring Road Project, Unit-IV, Tiruvallur and may be inspected at any time during the oﬃ  ce hours.
