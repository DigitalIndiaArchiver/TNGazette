RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND PANCHAYAT RAJ DEPARTMENT
AMENDMENT TO THE TAMIL NADU PANCHAYATS (PROHIBITION AND REGULATION OF THE USE OF
PUBLIC OR PRIVATE SPRINGS, TANKS, WELLS) RULES.
[G.O. Ms. No. 124, Rural Development and Panchayat Raj (PR-1), 3rd November 2017.]
No. SRO A-46(l)/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 242 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act,
1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to
the Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Prohibition and Regulation of the Use of Public or Private Springs, Tanks, Wells)
Rules, 1999:-
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, for the marginal heading of rule 2, the following marginal heading shall be substituted,
namely:-
“Power of the Village Panchayat to regulate the use
 of Public Spring, Tank, Well or Water-Course.- ’’.
