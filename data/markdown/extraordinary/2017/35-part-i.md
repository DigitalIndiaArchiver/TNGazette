The following Notification of the Government of India, Ministry of Law and Justice (Department of Justice),
Jaisalmer House, 26, Man Singh Road, New Delhi-110 011, dated 16th February, 2017 is republished:—
In exercise of the powers conferred by  Article 223 of the Constitution of India, the President is pleased
to appoint Shri Justice Huluvadi Gangadharappa Ramesh, senior-most Judge of the Madras High Court, to
perform the duties of  the office of Chief Justice of that High Court with effect from the date Shri Justice Sanjay
Kishan Kaul, relinquishes charge of the office of the Chief Justice of the Madras High Court consequent upon
his appointment as a Judge of the Supreme Court of India.
