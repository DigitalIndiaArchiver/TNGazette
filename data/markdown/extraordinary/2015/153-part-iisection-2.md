HOME, PROHIBITION AND EXCISE DEPARTMENT
EXEMPTION FROM PAYMENT OF EXCISE DUTY FOR EXPORT OF BEER TO OTHER STATES
UNDER THE TAMIL NADU PROHIBITION ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 20, Home, Prohibition and Excise (III), 21st July 2015]
No. II(2)/HOPE/397(c)/2015.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of  Section 18-H of the Tamil Nadu Prohibition
Act, 1937 (Tamil Nadu Act X of 1937), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes an exemption in regard
to the excise duty payable on the bottle beer and can beer, permitted to be exported to other States from
the State of Tamil Nadu, by the Export Licensees subject to the condition that the Export Licensees
shall furnish the bank guarantee as a performance guarantee as required under rule 32-B of the
Tamil Nadu Brewery Rules, 1983.
APURVA VARMA,
    Principal Secretary to Government.
