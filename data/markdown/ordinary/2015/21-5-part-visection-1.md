Confirmation of Variation to the Approved Vishnukanchi Detailed Development Plan No. 1 of
Kancheepuram Local Planning Area.
(Roc. No. 7119/2015/DPI)
No. VI(1)/134/2015.
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 33(2) of Town and Country Planning Act 1971. The Director of
Town and Country Planning, Chennai-600 002, Proceeding Roc. No. 7119/2015/DP1, dated 24th April 2015, hereby
confirms the following variation to the approved Vishnukanchi Detailed Development Plan No. 1 Kancheepuram
Local Planning Area approved by the Commissioner of Town and Country Planning Proceedings Roc. No. 36714/98/DP2,
dated 30th September 1999 and published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No. 3, Part VI Section—1,
Page 30 and 31, dated 26th January 2000 Publication No.VI(1)/43/2000 and the said draft Notification was
published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No. 39, Part VI Section—1, Page No. 355, dated 15th October 2014,
Publication No. VI(1)/308/2014.
2. Since no objections and suggestions have been received on this draft Notification within the stipulated time, the
same are here by confirmed and ordered as below.
CONFIRMATION OF VARIATION
1.The entire Vishnukanchi Detailed Development Plan No.1 of Kancheepuram Local Planning Area, approved by the
Commissioner of Town and Country Planning Proceeding Roc. No. 36714/98, DP2 is varied by Vishnukanchi Detailed
Development Plan No.1 of Kancheepuram Local Planning Area.
2. The Plan thus varied is numbered as Map No.5 DDP(V)DTCP No. 11/2014 and
3.The Detailed Development Plan clauses and schedules annexed with this plan are made enforceable from the date
of publication of Notification of Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
Chennai-600 002,
R. KIRLOSH KUMAR,
20th April 2015.
Director of Town and Country Planning.
