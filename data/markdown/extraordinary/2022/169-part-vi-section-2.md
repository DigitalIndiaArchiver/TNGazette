[ELECTIONS - Urban Local Bodies - Indirect Elections to fill up Casual vacancies in the office

(S.O.No. 30/2022/TNSEC/ME-II Dated, the 16th  March 2022.)
No. VI(2)/31(d)/2022
	
	
In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (1) of Section 43-B and section 27 of the Tamil Nadu District 
Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920) and sub-rule (4) of rule 99 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Municipalities 
and Corporation (Elections) Rules, 2006, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission hereby fixes 26.3.2022 as the date for 
convening a meeting to  elect the Chairman, Vice Chairman, as the case may be, as shown in the Annexure, in accordance with 
the following programme:-
1
Last date for service of notice for the meetings of election 
of Chairman, Vice Chairman, as the case may be 
17.03.2022
2
Statutory interval
18.03.2022  
to 
25 .03.2022
3
Date for conduct of meeting for the election of Chairman, 
Vice Chairman, as the case may be
26.03.2022
