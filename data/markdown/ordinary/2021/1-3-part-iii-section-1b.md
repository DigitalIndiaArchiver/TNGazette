No. SRO B-1/2021.— In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, 
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for Tamil Nadu General Service, 
(Section 16 of Volume II of the Tamil Nadu Service Manual, 2016.)
The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have been come into force on and from 27th April, 2000.
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖග
In the said Special Rules,-
(1) 
In Part-I, under the heading Class XLVI, for the expression "Personal Assistant in Fisheries Department", the 
following expression shall be substituted namely:-
"Deputy Director of Fisheries (Personnel) and Personal Assistant in Fisheries Department";

(a) 
for the heading "Class XLVI - Personal Assistant in Fisheries Department", the expression "

DEPARTMENT" shall be substituted;

"1. Constitution.-This class shall consist of the following categories, namely:-
