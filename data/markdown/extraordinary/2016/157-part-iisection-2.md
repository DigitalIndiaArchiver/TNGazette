COMMERCIAL TAXES AND REGISTRATION DEPARTMENT
  REDUCTION IN THE RATE OF TAX PAYABLE BY ANY DEALER ON THE SALE OF WHEAT
FOR CERTAIN PERCENTAGE AND CERTAIN PERIOD UNDER THE
TAMIL NADU VALUE ADDED TAX ACT
[G.O. (Ms)  No. 99, Commercial Taxes and Registration (B2), 29th June 2016,
Aani-15,Thunmugi Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2047.]
No.II(2)/CTR/401(a)/2016.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of section 30 of the Tamil Nadu Value
Added Tax Act, 2006 (Tamil Nadu Act 32 of 2006), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes a
reduction in the rate of tax payable by any dealer on the sale of wheat from five per cent to
two per cent.
2.  This Notification shall be deemed to have come into force on the 12th July 2011 and shall
remain in force upto and inclusive of the  31st March 2012.
