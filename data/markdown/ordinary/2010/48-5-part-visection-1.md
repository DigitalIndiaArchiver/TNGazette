Variations to the Approved Master Plan for the Coimbatore Local Planning Area.
(Roc. No. 1682/2008/LPA-3.)
No. VI(1)/399/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 32 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning
Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972) and in exercise of powers conferred by the G.O. Ms. No. 94, Housing and
Urban Development UD4(1) Department, dated 12th June 2009 which has been published in the Tamil Nadu Government
Gazette No. 27, Part II—Section 2, page No. 228, dated 15th July 2009 the following variations are made to the master
plan for the Coimbatore Local Planning Area approved under the said Act and published in the Housing and Urban
Development Department Notification No. II(2)/HOU/4377/94 at page 1078 of Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu
Government Gazette, dated the 9th November 1994.
VARIATIONS
In the said Master Plan, in the "LAND USE SCHEDULE" under the heading No. 78 MYLAMPATTI Village.
(i)
Against the entry "MR-24 Residential" the expression "4/4, 4/5, 4/6" shall be inserted in the front.
(ii) Against the entry "Ag 49-Agricultural" the expression "1 to 19" shall be modified as '1 to 3', 4' except
(4/4, 4/5, 4/6), '5 to 19'.
Coimbatore,

CHENNAI METROPOLITAN DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY.
Formulation of Guidelines for Special Transfer of Development Rights.
(Letter No. C1/2158/2010.)
No. VI(1)/401/2010.
Development Regulations forming part of the Second Master Plan for Chennai Metropolitan Area provides for award
of Special Transferable Development Right in the cases of slums located on water ways, road margins or any other
land belonging to the Government departments, in order to bring the land to its original status or to make the land
available for any public use.  In exercise of the powers vested with the Chennai Metropolitan Development Authority
under Development Regulation No. 9-A (ii), the Authority in Resolution No. 117/2010, dated 17th September 2010
formulated the guidelines for Special Transfer of Development Rights. A copy of the same is kept at the Information
Counter of Chennai Metropolitan Development Authority, Chennai-600 008 for perusal of the general public and the
same is available for sale at Rs. 10 per copy. The guidelines are also available on the official website of CMDA
(www.cmdachennai.gov.in) which can be downloaded free of cost.
Chennai-600 008,
DAYANAND KATARIA,
25th October 2010.
Member-Secretary,
Chennai Metropolitan Development Authority.
