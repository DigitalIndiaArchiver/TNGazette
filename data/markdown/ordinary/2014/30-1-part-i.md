[Courts and Judges-District Judge-Thiru G. Dhanaraj, District
Judge of Tamil Nadu State Judicial Service-Deputation from
Tamil Nadu State Judicial Service to the Puducherry Judicial
Service-Orders-Issued.]
The following Government Order is published:-
[G.O.(D) No.268, Public (Special A), 18th July 2014,
Aadi  2, Jaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2045.]
READ:
From the Registrar General, High Court, Madras, D.O.
Letter No. 7314-A/2014-B2, dated 1st July 2014.
Order: No. 268, Public (Special-A), 18th July 2014.
The Government have considered the recommendation of
the High Court, Madras to depute Thiru G. Dhanaraj, District
Judge of Tamil Nadu State Judicial Service, functioning as
Special Judge, Special Court under TNPID Act, Coimbatore to
the Puducherry Judicial Service for being posted as Presiding
Officer, Industrial Tribunal-cum-Labour Court, Pondicherry in
the existing vacancy.
2. The 
Government 
accordingly 
direct 
that
Thiru G. Dhanaraj, District Judge of Tamil Nadu State Judicial
Service, now functioning as Special Judge, Special Court
under TNPID Act, Coimbatore to the Puducherry Judicial
Service for being posted as Presiding Officer, Industrial
Tribunal-cum-Labour Court, Pondicherry, on deputation basis,
in the existing vacancy.
(By Order of the Governor)
MOHAN VERGHESE CHUNKATH,
Chief Secretary.
