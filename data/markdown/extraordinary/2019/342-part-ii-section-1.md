DECLARATION TO PREPARE AND UPDATE THE POPULATION REGISTER-REGARDING
(Letter No. 33915/L.O/2019-1, 26th August 2019.)
No. II(1)/PULO/12(f)/2019–The following Notification of the Government of India Ministry of Home 
Affairs, (Office of The Registrar General Citizen Registration, India) New Delhi, dated the 31st July, 2019 
is republished:— 
S.O.2753(E).—In pursuance of sub-rule(4) of rule 3 of the Citizenship (Registration of Citizens and Issue 
of National Identity Cards) Rules, 2003, the Central Government hereby decides to prepare and update 
the Population Register and the field work for house to house enumeration throughout the country except 
Assam for collection of information relating to all persons who are usually residing within the jurisdiction 
of Local Registrar shall be undertaken between the 1st day of April, 2020 to 30th September, 2020.
