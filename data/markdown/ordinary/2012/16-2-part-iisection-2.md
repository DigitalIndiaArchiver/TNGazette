Exemption of certain shops of Nalli Silk Sarees and it
branches for certain period under Tamil Nadu Shops
and Establishments Act.
[G.O. (D) No. 130, Labour and Employment (K2),
3rd April 2012.]
No. II(2)/LE/224/2012.вЂ”In exercise of the powers conferred
by Section 6 of the Tamil Nadu Shops and Establishments Act,
1947 (Tamil Nadu Act XXXVI of 1947), the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby exempts the shops of Nalli Silk Sarees,
situated at No. 100, Usman Road, T. Nagar, Chennai-600 017,
Nalli Silk Sarees, situated at No. 7, III Cross Street, Kasturbha
Nagar, Chennai-600 020 and Nalli Silk Sarees, situated at No.
AA-13, II Main Road, Anna Nagar, Chennai-600 040, from the
provisions of sub-section (1) of Section 11 of the said Act, for
a period  of three years with effect on and from the date of
publication of this Notification in the Tamil Nadu Government
Gazette, subject to the following conditions, namely:вЂ”
(i)
Weekly holiday should be given to the employees on
rotation basis.
(ii)
If any genuine complaint is received from the employees,
the exemption granted will be cancelled.
