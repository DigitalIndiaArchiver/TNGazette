HOUSING AND URBAN DEVELOPMENT DEPARTMENT
DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF THE TAMIL NADU TOWN AND COUNTRY PLANNING
(AMENDMENT) ACT, 2008.
[G.O. Ms. No. 253, Housing and Urban Development (UD I), 10th December 2008.]
No. II(2)/HOU/601(a)/2008.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country
Planning (Amendment) Act, 2008 (Tamil Nadu Act 61 of 2008), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints
the 10th day of December 2008, as the date on which the said Act shall come into force.
R. SELLAMUTHU,
Principal Secretary to Government.
