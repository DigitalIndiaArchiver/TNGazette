CONSTITUTION OF  TAMIL NADU ELECTRICITY REGULATORY COMMISSION OF SELECTION
COMMITTEE FOR SELECTING A MEMBER OF THE COMMISSION UNDER THE ELECTRICITY ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 46, Energy (A1), 3rd May 2010.]
No. II(2)/EGY/232(e)/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 85 of the Electricity Act, 2003 (Central
Act 36 of 2003), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby constitutes a Selection Committee consisting of the following
for the purpose of selecting a Member of the Tamil Nadu Electricity Regulatory Commission:—
(1)
Thiru Justice P. Shanmugam, Retired Judge
..
Chairperson
(2)
The Chief Secretary to Government of
Tamil Nadu
..
Member
(3)
The Chairperson of the Central
Electricity Regulatory Commission,
New Delhi
..
Member
2.
This Selection Committee shall—
(a) finalise the selection of Member within three months from the date on which the reference is
made to it;
(b) recommend a panel of two names for every vacancy referred to it;
(c) satisfy itself, before recommending any person for appointment as Member of the Tamil Nadu
Electricity Regulatory Commission, that such person does not have any financial or other interest
which is likely to affect prejudicially his functions as Member.
P.W.C.  DAVIDAR,
Secretary to Government.
