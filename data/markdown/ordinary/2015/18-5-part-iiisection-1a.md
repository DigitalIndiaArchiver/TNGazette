 Amendments  to  the  Tamil  Nadu  Plantations  Labour  Rules.
[G.O. Ms. No. 66, Labour and  Employment (K2), 10th April 2015.]
No. SRO A-10/2015.—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-sections (1)  and (2) of Section 43 of the Plantations
Labour Act, 1951 (Central Act LXIX of 1951), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the
Tamil Nadu Plantations Labour Rules, 1955, the draft of the same having been previously published as required by the said
sub-section (1) of Section 43 of the said Act.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules,—
(1) in Chapter VI,-
(a) in the heading, the words “AND MATERNITY” shall be omitted;
(b) rules 72, 73, 74, 75, 76, 77, 78, 79 and 80 shall be omitted;
(2) Form Numbers 8, 9, 10 and 11 shall be omitted.
 KUMAR JAYANT,
Secretary to Government (In-charge.)
