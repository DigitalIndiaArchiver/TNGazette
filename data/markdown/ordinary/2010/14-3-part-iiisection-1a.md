Amendment to the Tamil Nadu Liquor (Supply by Wholesale) Rules, 1983.
[G.O. Ms. No.15, Home, Prohibition and Excise (VIII), 27th March 2010, Panguni 13, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No. SRO A-12/2010.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Sections 17-D and 54 of the Tamil Nadu Prohibition
Act, 1937 (Tamil Nadu Act X of 1937), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the
Tamil Nadu Liquor (Supply by Wholesale) Rules, 1983.
2.
The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st April 2009.
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in rule 15, in sub-rule (3), in item (i), for the expression "69.72", the expression "73.30"
shall be substituted.
Amendments to the Tamil Nadu Liquor (Licence and Permit) Rules, 1981.
[G.O. Ms. No. 16, Home, Prohibition and Excise (VIII), 29th March 2010.]
No. SRO A-13/2010.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 54 read with Sections 17-C, 17-D, 17-E,
18-B, 18-C, 20 and 21 of the Tamil Nadu Prohibition Act, 1937 (Tamil Nadu Act X of 1937), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby makes the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu Liquor (Licence and Permit) Rules, 1981:—
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules,—
1. in rule 17,—
(1) in sub-rule (a), under the heading "(A) Licences for liquor used for consumption", in the entry against
“F.L.9",—
(a) for the expression "Meenambakkam Civil Airport", the expression “International Civil Airports in the
State of Tamil Nadu" shall be substituted;
(b) for the word "Chennai", the expression "the places where the International Civil Airports are situated"
shall be substituted;
(2) in  sub-rule (b), in clause XI,— (a) in the heading,—
(i)
for the expression “Meenambakkam Civil Airport", the expression "International Civil Airports in the
State of Tamil Nadu" shall be substituted;
(ii) for the word "Chennai", the expression "the places where the International Civil Airports are situated"
shall be substituted;
(b) in the entry against "F.L.9", for the word, "Chennai", the expression "the Places where the International
Civil Airports are situated" shall be substituted;
2.
in rule 43,—
(1) in the heading,—
(a) for the expression "Meenambakkam Civil Airport" the expression "International Civil Airports in the
State of Tamil Nadu" shall be substituted;
(b) for the word "Chennai”, the expression "the places where the International Civil Airports are situated"
shall be substituted;
