GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI
Taking over of the Hazrath Fazilsha Avulia Thaikka @ Thaikka Palli, Kottar,
Kanyakumari District under the District Management
(Rc. No. 14827/07/B1/KK.)
No.VI-3(a)/26/2013.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act 1995, the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board
in its Resolution in Item No.89/12, in Rc. No.14827/07/B1/Tnv, dated 28th November 2012 has ordered to take over the
management of Hazrath Fazilsha Avulia Thaikka @ Thaikka Palli, Kottar, Kanyakumari District under the direct control of
the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of one year from the date of Board’s order dated 28th November 2012, and to
appoint the Superintendent of Wakfs, Tirunelveli as its Executive Officer.
F. ABDUL RAZICK,
Chennai-600 001,
Chief Executive Officer,
22nd January 2013.
