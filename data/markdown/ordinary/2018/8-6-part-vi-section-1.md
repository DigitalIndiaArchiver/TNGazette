Diversion of the Movement of Traffic at Santhome High Road, in Chennai
(Rc. No.Imp-I/Tr./132/14943/2017.)
No. VI(1)/76/2018.
1. In exercise of powers vested vide in G.O. Ms. No.793 Home (Tr.V) Department dated: 21-08-2001 and in 
accordance with Section 115 of Motor vehicles Act, 1998, the Commissioner of Police, Greater Chennai Police, is 
pleased to notify the following traffic diversions which were temporarily made and implemented in and around Santhome 
High Road are made permanent here after in the interest of Public safety and convenience and to ensure free and 
smooth flow of vehicles. 
2. The traffic diversions are as follows:- "Those vehicles coming on Kamarajar Salai and intending to proceed 
towards Adayar may take a diversion at Light House and may take the route via., Foreshore Bye-Pass road 
(Nochi Kuppam Bye-Pass road) and South Canal to reach Greenways road during the restricted timings". 
3 The existing timings i.e. 07.30 a.m to 10.30 a.m and 02.00 p.m to 06.00 p.m for traffic diversion at Santhome 
High Road passed earlier by the Government may be modified as 06.30 a.m to 10.30 a.m in the morning and 02.00 
p.m to 08.30 p.m in the evening for the safety of public and students.	
Vepery, Chennai-7,	
A.K. Vishwanathan,	
2nd February 2018.	
Commissioner of Police,

Members Elected under Section 5(1)(f) of Tamil Nadu Medical Registration Act, 1914. 
(Ref:- TNMC/Govt. Press/Elec.5(1)(f)/2017-2018).
No. VI(1)/84/2018. 
   In consonance with the orders of the Hon'ble High Court, Madras dated 28-06-2017 made in W.P.No.15073/2017, 
I, Justice K. Venkataraman, Administrator for the Tamil Nadu Medical Council, Chennai (Elections) conducted election 
for the post of Seven Members of Tamil Nadu Medical Council under rule 10 (1) and 18 of the rules framed in 
Part III for Subsequent Elections in the Tamil Nadu Medical Registration Act,1914, 1. Dr. K. Senthil, 2. Dr. T. Jeyasingh, 
3. Dr. R. Thiagarajan, 4. Dr. S. Dhalapathy, 5. Dr. T. Sadagopan, 6. Dr. M.K. Ponnuraj, 7. Dr.R.V.S. Surendran, have 
been elected from the Registered Medical Practitioners as Members of the Tamil Nadu Medical Council, Chennai under 
clause (1) of sub-section (f) of Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Medical Regjstration Act IV of 1914 as amended by 
Tamil Nadu Medical Registration (Amendment Act, XVI of 1938) for a term of five years with effect from 16th February, 2018.
