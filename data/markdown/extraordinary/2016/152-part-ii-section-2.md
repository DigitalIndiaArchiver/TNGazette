COMMERCIAL TAXES AND REGISTRATION  DEPARTMENT
EXEMPTION IN RESPECT OF THE TAX PAYABLE ON THE SALE OF COPRA PROCURED UNDER PRICE
SUPPORT SCHEME FOR CERTAIN PERIOD UNDER TAMIL NADU VALUE ADDED TAX ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 98, Commercial Taxes and Registration (B2), 15th June 2016,
Aani 1, Thunmugi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2047.]
No. II(2)/CTR/390(a)/2016.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 30 of the Tamil Nadu Value Added
Tax Act, 2006 (Tamil Nadu Act 32 of 2006), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes an exemption in respect
of the tax payable under the said Act on the sale of copra procured under Price Support Scheme by the
Tamil Nadu Co-operative Marketing Federation Limited (TANFED) and Primary Co-operative Societies for
National Agricultural Co-operative Marketing Federation Limited (NAFED).
2. The exemption hereby made shall come into force on the 15th June 2016 and shall remain in force upto
and inclusive of the 14th December 2016.
MD. NASIMUDDIN,
Principal Secretary to Government (FAC).
