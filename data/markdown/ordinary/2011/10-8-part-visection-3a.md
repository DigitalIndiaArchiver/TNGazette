GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI.
Extension of period of Direct Management of Soorsha Makkan Wakf, Triplicane, Chennai.
(Rc. No. 4772/06/Chennai/B6.)
No. VI-3(a)/16/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995,
the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in Item No. 238/10 (Rc. No. 4772/06/B6/Chennai), dated 30th December 2010 has
ordered to extend  the period of direct management of Soorsha Makkan Wakf, Triplicane, Chennai for a further period of
one year from 27th January 2011.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
27th January 2011.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
Taking over the Direct Management of Khan Mian Pallivasal, Kandiaperi, Tirunelveli District.
(Rc. No. 3281/07/B1/TNV.)
No. VI-3(a)/17/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995,
the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its proceedings in Item No.173/10 in Rc. No. 3281/07/B18/TNV, dated 6th October 2010, has
ordered to take over the management of Khan Mian Pallivasal, Kandiaperi, Tirunelveli District under the direct control of the
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of one year from the date of publication in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette and
to appoint the Superintendent of Wakfs, Tirunelveli as its Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
8th February 2011.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
Taking over the Direct Management of Rowlath Jannath Pallivasal (Gudimiyandithope Pallivasal),
Gudimiyandithope, Sholinganallur, Kancheepuram District.
(Rc. No. 12623/07/B4/Kpm.)
No. VI-3(a)/18/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995,
the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in Item No. 205/10, dated 20th January 2011 in Rc. No. 12623/07/B3/Kpm. has
ordered to take over the Management of Rowlath Jannath Pallivasal (Gudimiyandithope Pallivasal), Gudimiyandithope,
Sholinganallur, Kancheepuram District under the direct control of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of one year from
the date of publication in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette and to appoint the Superintendent of Wakfs/Poonthamalli
as its Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
3rd February 2011.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.

ADVERTISEMENT OF PETITION
Notice is hereby given that a Petition for winding up of the abovenamed Respondent Company
M/s. Pyramid Saimira Theatres Limited, by the High Court of Judicature at Madras was on this the 7th day of December 2009
presented to the said Court by the Petitioner/Creditor abovenamed and the said petition is directed to be heard before the
Court on the 11th day of April 2011.
Any creditor contributory or other persons desirous of supporting or opposing the making of an order on the said petition
should send to the Petitioner or his Advocate notice of his intention signed by him or his
advocate with his name and address, so as to reach the Petitioner or his advocate not later then 5 days before the date fixed
for the hearing of the Petition, and appear at the hearing for the purpose in person or by his advocate.
A copy of the petition will be furnished by the undersigned to any creditor or contributory on payment of the prescribed
charges for the same.
Any Affidavit intended to be used in opposition to the Petition should be filed in Courts and a copy served on the
Petitioner or his Advocate, not less than 5 days before the date fixed for the hearing.
Dated at Chennai, this the 7th day of  March 2011.
No. 37, Law Chamber,
R. MURARI AND PREETI MOHAN,
High Court Buildings,
Counsel for Petitioner.
