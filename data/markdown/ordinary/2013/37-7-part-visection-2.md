HIGH COURT  LEGAL  SERVICES  COMMITTEE,  CHENNAI
NOMINATION  OF MEMBER   FOR  THE  HIGH  COURT  LEGAL  SERVICES COMMITTEE.
(HCLSC/491/2006.)
NO.VI(2)/200/2013.
In exercise of the Powers conferred under the Sub 2 (b) Section of Section 8-A of the Legal Services Authorities
Act 1987 (Central Act 39 of 1987) read with regulation 3 (3) (a) (vi) framed for the High Court Legal Services Committee,
the Hon’ble the Chief Justice, High Court, Madras is pleased to nominate Thiru S. Arun Kumar, Senior Advocate as Member
of the High Court Legal Services Committee, Chennai in the Place of Thiru R. Muthukumarasamy, Senior Counsel, now
the Present President of Madras Bar Association, High Court, Madras.
Thiru. S. Arun Kumar,
Senior Advocate,
175, Law Chambers,
High Court,
Madras-600 104.
Chennai-600 104,
K. ARUL,
3rd September 2013.
District Judge/Secretary,
High Court Legal Services Committee.
