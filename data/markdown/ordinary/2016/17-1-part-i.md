No.I/PUSP/8/2016.—The following Notification of the
Government of India, Ministry of Law and Justice (Department
of Justice), Jaisalmer House, 26, Man Singh Road,
New Delhi-110 011, dated 1st April, 2016 is republished:—
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (1) of
Article 222 of the Constitution of India, the President, after
consultation with the Chief Justice of India, is pleased to
transfer Shri Justice Rajiv Shakdher, Judge of the Delhi
High Court, as a Judge of the Madras High Court and to
direct him to assume charge of his office in the Madras High
Court on or before 15th April, 2016.
[No. K-11017/30/2016-US.I]
