Amendments to the Tamil Nadu Legislators (Railway fare to Former Members) Rules, 2018.

No. SRO A-5/2019.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 14 read with Section 12-D of the Tamil Nadu Payment of Salaries Act, 
1951 (Tamil Nadu Act XX of 1951), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu 
Legislators (Railway fare to former Members) Rules,2018:- 
Amendments 
(1) 	
In the said Rules,- in rule 2, in clause (c) , the word "travelling" shall be omitted; 
(2) 	
in the Appendix,-(i) in Form-A, in serial No.10, for the expression "Railway fare (excluding total Charges)", 
the expression "Railway Fare (excluding tatkal charges)" shall be substituted; 
(ii)	
in Form-B, for the expression "[file relating to assignment of New Head of Account has been sent to 
Finance Department]" the following expressions shall be substituted, namely:- 
	
"2071. Pension and other retirement benefits - 01. Civil 111. Pensions to Legislators - 
	State's Expenditure - AA. State Legislative Assembly Members - 27. Pensions - 09. Others 
	
(DPC :2071-01-111-AA-2790) 
	
"2071. Pension and other retirement benefits - 01. Civil 111. Pensions to Legislators - State's 
Expenditure - AD. Members of Legislative Council - 27. Pensions - 09. Others 
	
(DPC: 2071-01-111-AD-2794)".
