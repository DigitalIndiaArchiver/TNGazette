The Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai.
Notice of Election of One member to the Governing Council of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R  Medical University,  
Chennai by the University Professors from among themselves. 
[Notification No: EIII (3)/37621/2018]     
No.VI-3(b)/33/2018.
Notice is hereby given that an election by direct polling is to be held in the University premises for electing of One 
member to the Governing Council by the Professors of this University of The Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, 
Chennai, as per clause (c) of sub-section (4) of Section 21 of The Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai, 
Act, 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 37 of 1987) by the University Professors from among themselves to fill up the existing one 
vacancy. The term of membership in the Governing Council will be for a period of three years from the date of publication 
of the results of the election (i.e. 05-10-2018) or the date on which he/she ceases to be member of this University. 
2. Forms of Nomination may be obtained from the Registrar, who is the Returning Officer, from 07-09-2018 to 
18-09-2018 between 11.00 a.m. and 3.00 p.m. (on all working days) 
3. The filled - in Nomination paper may be sent through the enclosed envelope addressed to the Registrar / 
Returning Officer and superscripting as "Nomination for election to the Governing Council" as indicated below, and 
sent by Registered Post (Acknowledgement Due), so as to be received by the REGISTRAR / Returning Officer not later 
than 4.00 p.m. on 18-09-2018 or delivered to the Returning Officer during office hours in person or by his/ her proposer 
or by messenger under acknowledgement on or before the date and hour mentioned above.

The nomination papers which are not sent by Registered Post with Acknowledgment Due or not delivered in person 
by the nominee or by his proposer or by messenger as required above shall be declared as invalid. 
4. Nomination papers will be taken up for scrutiny at 4.15 p.m. on 18-09-2018
5. List of valid nominations will be published at 5.00 p.m. on 18-09-2018 in the University premises. 
6. The candidate may withdraw his/her candidature by notice in writing sent by Registered Post so as to reach 
the Returning Officer or delivered to the Returning Officer or any other person duly authorized by him/her under 
acknowledgement not later than 3.00 p.m. on 24-09-2018. The candidate, who has withdrawn his/her candidature, shall 
not be allowed to cancel the withdrawal or to be re-nominated as a candidate for the same election.

7. List of final valid nominations will be published in the notice board of the University as well as in the University 
Website at 5.00 p.m. on 24-09-2018. A copy of the Final list of valid nominations will also be sent to the candidates 
concerned. 
8. Polling will be conducted in the premises of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University at No.69, Anna Salai, 
Guindy, Chennai-600 032 between 10.00 a.m. and 3.00 p.m. on 05-10-2018.
9. Scrutiny and counting of votes will be done from 3.30 p.m. on 05-10-2018. and the result will be declared on 
the same day after the process is over. 
Note: 
1. The intimation of valid nominations would be sent to the candidates with a copy of the notification of the 
programme for the election containing last date of withdrawal and date of poll, etc. 
2. If, for example, the final valid nomination is one and such candidate has not withdrawn his/her candidature or 
where there are more nominations and all the candidates except one have withdrawn their nominations in the manner 
prescribed, within the time specified for withdrawal, such candidate shall be declared to be duly elected and the result 
of the election will be published on the same day.
