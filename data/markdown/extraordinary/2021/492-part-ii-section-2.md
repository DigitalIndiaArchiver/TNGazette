WHEREAS on considering the prevailing Covid-19 pandemic scenario and as per the directives of Government of 
India, Ministry of Home Aﬀ airs, State-wide lockdown/restrictions was enforced throughout the State with eﬀ ect from 
4.00 A.M. of 10-05-2021 to 31-10-2021, in order to control the spread of COVID-19. 
 2. The Hon’ble Chief Minister has conducted review meeting with oﬃ  cers to evaluate the Corona prevention activities, 
Spread of corona virus in neighbouring states and measures to be taken to control the spread of Corona infection and 
also taken into account the direction issued by the Ministry of Home Aﬀ airs, Union Government regarding the possibility 
of Corona spread due to gathering of public during these upcoming festivals. 
3. Now, based on the above review meeting, under Disaster Management Act, 2005 the Government hereby order 
the following:-
The following activities are permitted with eﬀ ect from 14-10-2021
 All Shops, Hotels and Bakeries shall be permitted to operate till 11.00 p.m.
 Public worship in religious places are permitted on all days including Friday, Saturday, and Sunday.
 All Private tuition centres.
 All Government and Private employment camps and job fair by following SOP.

The following activities are permitted with eﬀ ect from 01-11-2021
 Monthly Mass contact programmes conducted by the District Administration.
 Private Exhibitions by following SOP.
 Anganwadi activities, All staﬀ  including the in-charge and cook should be vaccinated. 
 Permission to Public in Beaches on Sundays. 
 Marriage related gathering – Number of guests not to exceed 100. 
 Funeral/ last rites related gathering – Number of persons not to exceed 50.
Decision regarding Play schools and Nursery schools (L.K.G., U.K.G.) will be intimated later.
All allowed activities in existence shall continue to be permitted with existing restrictions.
Public gatherings in religious festivals/consecrations, political, socio-cultural and religion related congregation, 
which were already prohibited, will continue to be prohibited. 
General
 
The following important Standard Operating Procedures shall be strictly adhered to all the shops and places 
of Public gathering:-
● 
Shops shall have mandatory hand hygiene (hand sanitizer with dispenser), Thermal screening at 
the entrance. 
● 
Management shall ensure that the workers and customers wear face masks compulsorily.
● 
All shops and establishments shall ensure that the doors and windows are kept open for cross 
ventilation. In order to ensure social distancing norms, more number of persons shall not be 
allowed at a time inside the shops.
● 
Speciﬁ c markings with suﬃ  cient distance may be made at the entrance of the shops to manage 
the general public standing in the queue.
● 
Action will be taken against the commercial/other organizations which violate the above guidelines 
and prescribed limit of customers. 
(ii) 
‘Test- Track- Treat- Vaccination- Covid-19 appropriate behaviour’ principle shall be strictly adhered to.
(iii) 
In order to contain the spread of Covid-19, the Containment Zones shall be demarcated upto Micro Level 
and the containment measures including vaccination to the people in this area shall be initiated by the 
District Collectors and the Local Bodies as per the Standard Operating Procedures.
(iv) 
Within the demarcated Containment Zones, containment measures shall be scrupulously followed, as under:
● 
Only essential activities shall be allowed in the Containment Zones. No activity, except medical 
emergencies and supply of essential goods and services shall be permitted.
● 
There shall be intensive house-to-house surveillance by surveillance teams formed for the purpose.
4. The District Administration, Greater Chennai Corporation and local bodies requested to take all necessary actions 
to prevent large public gathering in public places during the festival seasons.
5. The public are requested to be careful and avoid moving to crowded places and to wear face mask and 
maintain social distance without fail during the upcoming festival season by following preventive measures. All commercial 
establishment are also requested to follow the preventive measure without any lapses.
6. The public are also requested to seek medical advice of the nearest hospital as soon as symptoms of infection 
appear.

7. The Commissioner, Greater Chennai Corporation/ District Collectors and Commissioners of Police/ Superintendents 
of Police concerned shall take all necessary measures for the strict enforcement of National Directives for Covid-19 
management viz., wearing of face masks, hand hygiene and social distancing, screening & hygiene, frequent 
sanitization of entire workplace etc., and adherence of the Standard Operating Procedures issued for the permitted 
activities. The Commissioner, Greater Chennai Corporation/ District Collectors concerned may initiate penal action for 
imposing ﬁ nes on persons violating the National Directives.

legal provisions as applicable.
