 NOTIFICATION BY THE ELECTION COMMISSION OF INDIA
ELECTION COMMISSION’S NOTIFICATION REGARDING EXIT POLL AT THE BYE-ELECTION TO THE
TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY FROM 219. SANKARANKOVIL (SC) ASSEMBLY CONSTITUENCY
No. SRO G-9/2012.
The following Notification of the Election Commission of India, Nirvachan Sadan, Ashoka Road, New Delhi-110 001,
dated 6th March 2012  is republished:—
No.576/EXIT/2012/SDR/BYE-LA&HP).—In exercise of the powers under sub-section (1) of Section 126A of the Representation
of the People Act, 1951, and having regard to the provisions of clause (b) of sub-section (2) of the said Section, the Commission
hereby notifies the period between 8 A.M. on 17th March 2012 (Saturday), i.e. the day of poll in 85. Piravom Assembly Constituency
in Kerala and 5.30 P.M. on 18th March 2012 (Sunday) i.e., the day of poll in 15. Udupi Chikmagalur Parliamentary Constituency
in Karnataka and 07. Adilabad,  16. Kamareddy, 74. Mahbubnagar, 81. Nagerkurnool, 85. Kollapur, 99. Ghanpur (Station) (SC) and
235. Kovur Assembly Constituencies in Andhra Pradesh, 84. Mansa Assembly Constituency in Gujarat, 219. Sankarankovil (SC)
Assembly Constituency in Tamil Nadu and 89. Athagarh Assembly Constituency in Odisha, as the period during which conducting
any exit poll or disseminating in any manner whatsoever, the result of any exit poll in connection with the bye-elections announced
vide the Commission's Press Note dated: 16th February 2012 and 22nd February 2012 would be prohibited.
It is further clarified that under Section 126(1)(b) of the Representation of the People Act, 1951, displaying any election matter
including results of any opinion poll or any other poll survey, in any electronic media, would be prohibited during the period of
48 hours ending with the hour fixed for conclusion of poll in respect of each of the said bye-elections, in connection with aforesaid
bye-elections.
