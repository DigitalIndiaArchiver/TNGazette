No. SRO A-6/2015.—The following amendment is issued to the Tamil Nadu Chief Minister's Medal for Excellence in Finger
Print Science Rules, made in the Government Order No.788, Home (SC) Department, dated 8th September 2005.
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, for rule 12, the following shall be substituted:—
12. “The Medal Board in each Range consisting of Superintendents of Police under a Senior Superintendent of
Police as Chairman will recommend eligible names through Zonal Inspectors General of Police and Commissioners of Police
to the Head of State Crime Record Bureau. The Head of State Crime Record Bureau will vet the proposals, select the
suitable nominees and forward his recommendation to Government, through the Director General of Police, Tamil Nadu by
31st July, each year”.
2. This order will come into force with immediate effect.
APURVA VARMA,
Principal Secretary to Government.
