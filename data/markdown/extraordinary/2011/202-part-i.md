APPOINTMENT OF MINISTER FOR THE STATE OF TAMIL NADU
[G.O. Ms. No. 575, Public (Special-B), 4th July 2011, Aani 19, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No. I/PUSP/16(d-1)/2011.
I, Surjit Singh Barnala, Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoint Thiru P. Chendur Pandian, with effect from the
afternoon of 4th July, 2011, to be the Minister of my Council of Ministers.
SURJIT  SINGH  BARNALA,
Governor of Tamil  Nadu.
ALLOCATION  OF  SUBJECTS  AMONG  MINISTERS
[G.O. Ms. No. 576, Public (Special-B), 4th July 2011, Aani 19, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No. I/PUSP/16(d-2)/2011.
Under Clause (3) of Article 166 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby allocates
the subjects khadi and Village Industries Board, Bhoodan and Gramadhan to Thiru P. Chendur Pandian, appointed
as a Minister in the G.O. Ms. No. 575, Public (Special-B) Department, dated 4th July 2011. Consequent on the above
allocation, Thiru P. Chendur Pandian be designated as Minister for Khadi and Village Industries.
DEBENDRANATH  SARANGI,
Chief  Secretary.
