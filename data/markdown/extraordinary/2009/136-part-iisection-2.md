TAMIL  NADU  GOVERNMENT  GAZETTE  EXTRAORDINARY
NOTIFICATION
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Agricultural Labourers—Farmers (Social Security
and Welfare) Act, 2006 (Tamil Nadu Act 29 of 2006) the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following
amendments to the Revenue Department Notification No. II(2)/REV/22(c)/2007, published at pages 1-3 of
Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 18th January 2007:—
AMENDMENTS
In the said Notification,—
(a) Under the heading “1. Members representing the Government”, for item 1 and entries thereto, the following shall
be substituted, namely:—
“ 1. Thiru K.P. Ramalingam,
Chairman”:
175, Uzhavar Illam,
Koneripatti,
Rasipuram-637 408,
Namakkal District.
(b) Under the heading “ II. Members representing Agricultural Labourers, Farmers and Employers” item 1 and entries
thereto shall be omitted.
K. DEENABANDHU,
Principal Secretary to Government.
