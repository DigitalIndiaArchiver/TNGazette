WHEREAS on considering the recommendations of the expert team of Doctors and Public Health Specialists and based 
on the directive of Government of India, Ministry of Home Aﬀ airs, State-wide lockdown was extended from time to time and 
lastly extended till 24:00 hrs of 30-09-2020 under the Disaster Management Act, 2005 in the order 9th read above.
2. In the order 4th read above, Restaurants were permitted to function with eﬀ ect from 8.6.2020 with dine-in facilities from 
6.00 A.M. to 8.00 P.M. with 50% of seating capacity maintaining social distancing and without air conditioning throughout the 
State and Standard Operating Procedures also issued for the Customers / Owners / Managers / Workers of the Restaurants 
in Tamil Nadu in the order 5th read above. 
3. Further in the Order 8th read above, among others issued orders permitting the Restaurants in Greater Chennai 
Police Limits to function with dine-in facilities with 50% of seating capacity by following the Standard Operating Procedure 
issued already, from 6.00 A.M. to 7.00 P.M without air conditioning facility. In the order 9th read above, the Restaurants were 
permitted to function upto 8.00 P.M. 
4. In the order 9th read above, the Government have also permitted the Shopping Malls, all showrooms and big format 
shops (viz. jewellery and textiles shops) throughout the State with air conditioning facility by following the guidelines issued 
by Government of India for air conditioning and ventilation. 
5. Accordingly the Principal Secretary and Commissioner of Revenue Administration has sent the amendment proposal to 
the G.O.Ms.No.447, Revenue and Disaster Management Department, dated 31.08.2020 to permit the Restaurants to function 
with air conditioning facility by following the guidelines issued by Government of India for air conditioning and ventilation. 
6. Now therefore the Government hereby accept the proposal of the Principal Secretary and Commissioner of Revenue 
Administration and amend the G.O.Ms.No.447, Revenue and Disaster Management Department, dated 31.08.2020 as follows: 
The following content in Section (vii) under Caption II - (Permitted Activities) may be replaced as detailed below:- 
vii. Following the Standard Operating Procedures already issued by Government, Restaurants
are permitted to function from 6.00 A.M. to 8.00 P.M. with air conditioning facility by 
following the guidelines issued Government of India for air conditioning and ventilation and that 
take away (parcel food) will be permitted upto 9.00 P.M. Further, Tea Shops are permitted to function from 
6.00 A.M. to 8.00 P.M.
