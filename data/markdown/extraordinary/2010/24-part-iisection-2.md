NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
DTP—II-2 Ex. (24)
LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT DEPARTMENT
ESTABLISHMENT OF TAMIL NADU STREET VENDING AND SHOPS AND ESTABLISHMENTS WORKERS'
WELFARE BOARD UNDER THE TAMIL NADU MANUAL WORKERS (REGULATION OF EMPLOYMENT
AND CONDITIONS OF WORK) ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 8, Labour and Employment (I-1), 29th January 2010.]
No. II(2)/LE/68(c)/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 6 of the Tamil Nadu Manual Workers
(Regulation of Employment and Conditions of Work) Act, 1982 (Tamil Nadu Act 33 of 1982), the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby establishes the “Tamil Nadu Street Vending and Shops and Establishments Workers'
Welfare Board” for the employment in street vending and employment in shops and establishments
in the State of Tamil Nadu.
T. PRABHAKARA RAO,
Principal Secretary to Government.
