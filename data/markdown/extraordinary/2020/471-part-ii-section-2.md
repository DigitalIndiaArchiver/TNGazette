STANDARDS AND COLLEGES FROM 16.11.2020 - DEFERRED UNDER THE DISASTER MANAGEMENT ACT, 2005.

Considering the recommendations of the expert team of Doctors and Public Health specialists and based on the 
directives of Government of India, Ministry of Home Aﬀ airs, State-wide lockdown was extended from time to time and 
lastly extended till 24:00 Hrs of 30.11.2020 under the Disaster Management Act, 2005 with certain restrictions and many 
relaxations along with guidelines in G.O.Ms.No.613, Revenue and Disaster Management (DM IV), Department, Dated : 
31.10.2020
2. In the above orders, religious congregations, all social / political / cultural/ academic and other functions were 
permitted from 16.11.2020 with a maximum ceiling of 100 persons after duly following the Standard Operating Procedures 
and Schools (for the students studying in 9th,10th,11th and 12th Standards only) all Colleges, Research Institutions and 
other educational / coaching institutions were permitted to function from 16.11.2020 following the Standard Operating 
Procedures (SOP). 
3. Now in the Government Press Release dated 12.11.2020, it has been stated that in view of the festive season, 
large gathering of people are being witnessed in the shops and bus stands and as such it has been brought to the 
notice of the Government through media reports and ﬁ eld inspection that the general public are not following the social 
distancing norms and also not wearing the face masks at public places which would ultimately lead to increase in the 
rate of infection of Covid-19. It has been further stated that the second wave of Covid-19 has hit the foreign countries 
and hence it becomes essential to intensify the preventive measures for Covid-19. It has also been stated that in view 
of the above mentioned facts, permission already granted for social/ political/ entertainment/ cultural/academic and religious 
congregations has now been revoked until further orders.

4. In the Government Press Release dated 12.11.2020, it is also stated that re-opening of Schools for the students 
studying in 9th,10th,11th and 12th Standards has been deferred until further orders. It has also been stated that as per 
the guidelines dated 5.11.2020 released by the University Grants Commission, Colleges and Universities shall be permitted 
to function from 2.12.2020 only for research scholars and ﬁ nal year students pursuing post-graduation in science and 
technology stream. It has also been stated that the hostels would also be permitted in the Colleges /Universities which 
have been permitted to open up and that the Standard Operating Procedures for the Colleges/Universities and Students 
would be issued separately. Further announcement with regard to the opening up of Colleges for the Students studying 
in other streams would be issued separately.
5. In accordance with the Government Press Release No.857, dated 12.11.2020, the Additional Chief Secretary/
Commissioner of Revenue Administration has sent proposal to amend the G.O.Ms.No.613, Revenue and Disaster 
Management (DM IV), Department, Dated : 31.10.2020.
6. In accordance with the Government Press Release No.857, dated 12.11.2020 and based on the proposal of 
Additional Chief Secretary/Commissioner of Revenue Administration, the Government after careful examination hereby 
issue following amendment to the G.O.Ms.No.613, Revenue and Disaster Management (DM IV), Department, Dated : 
31.10.2020 as detailed below: 
I. The following content may be added as Section (v), (vi) and (vii) under Caption I – (Under Prohibited 
Activities):-
 
v. Permission already granted for social/ political/ entertainment/ cultural/academic and religious congregations 
from 16.11.2020 stands revoked and all social/ political/ entertainment/ cultural/academic and religious congregations shall 
remain prohibited until further orders.
 
vi. Permission granted for re-opening of schools for the students studying in 9th,10th,11th and 12th Standards 
stands deferred and all schools shall remain closed until further orders.
vii. All Colleges / Universities except those permitted only for research scholars and ﬁ nal year students pursuing 
post-graduation in science and technology stream shall remain closed. The online classes shall continue for the other 
students.
II. The following content in Section (i) and (ii) under Caption II – (Under Permitted Activities) may be read as:-
 
i. 
As per the guidelines dated 5.11.2020 released by the University Grants Commission, all Colleges / 
Universities shall be permitted to function from 2.12.2020 only for research scholars and ﬁ nal year students pursuing 
post-graduation in science and technology stream. 
 
ii. 
All hostels including Working Men/Women Hostels shall be permitted to function from 16.11.2020. Further, 
the hostels in the Colleges /Universities which have been permitted to open up only for research scholars and ﬁ nal year 
students pursuing post-graduation in science and technology stream shall be permitted to function from 2.12.2020. However, 
the School Hostels and hostels in other Colleges shall remain closed. Further, the Standard Operating Procedures for 
the Colleges/Universities would be issued separately.
