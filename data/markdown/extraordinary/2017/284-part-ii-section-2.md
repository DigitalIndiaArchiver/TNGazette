NOTICE OF ELECTION
ELECTION OF MEMBERS  OF THE TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD
[Letter No. 5840/T1/2016-14, Backward Classes, Most Backward Classes and Minorities Welfare (T1) 6th September 2017]
No. II(2)/BCMBCMW/718(a-2)/2017.— 
Notice is hereby given that :-
An election is to be held for two members each to the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board by electoral colleges consisting of-
 
i) Muslim Members of the State Legislature
 
ii) Mutawallis of the Waqfs having an annual income of rupees one lakh and above as speciﬁ ed under 
sub-rule (13) of Rule 2 of the Tamil Nadu Waqf Board (Conduct of Election for Members) Rules,1997.
2. Nomination papers in Form – I may be delivered by a candidate whose name ﬁ nds place in the ﬁ nal electoral 
roll to Thiru A.M. Aandiyappan, Returning Ofﬁ cer and Deputy Secretary to Government, Backward Classes, Most Backward 
Classes and Minorities Welfare Department, Chennai–9 or to Thiru S. Syed Khaseem, Assistant  Returning Ofﬁ cer 
and Under Secretary to Government,  Backward Classes, Most Backward Classes and Minorities Welfare Department, 
Chennai–9  between 11.00 A.M. and 3.00 P.M. on any day (Other than Public Holidays) not later than 
14th September 2017.
3. Forms of nomination papers may be obtained from 07.09.2017 (Thursday) onwards at the place and time 
aforesaid.
4. The nomination of each candidate belonging to the electoral colleges of the State Legislature shall be proposed 
and seconded by members belonging to the respective electoral college; Provided that  there is no need  for a proposer 
or seconder  when total number  of members belonging to each of the said electoral college is  six or less than six.
5. The nomination of each candidate belonging to the electoral college of Mutawallis shall be proposed and seconded 
by not less than ﬁ ve members from the said electoral college. 
6. The proposer and the seconder shall belong to the electoral college of the candidate nominated.
7. Any member belonging to a particular electoral college may be subscribed either as proposer or as seconder to 
as many nomination paper as there are vacancies to be ﬁ lled to the said category, but not more.
8. If a candidate belongs to more than one category of electoral colleges, he shall choose one of the electoral 
colleges. He shall contest, propose or second a candidate belonging to the said electoral college only.
9. A member of an electoral college, who proposes or seconds the candidature of another member shall not be 
eligible for himself contesting the election. 
10. The Nomination papers will be taken up for scrutiny at 11.00 A.M. on 15th September 2017 at the Ofﬁ ce of 
the Returning Ofﬁ cer and Deputy Secretary to Government, Backward Classes, Most Backward Classes and Minorities 
Welfare Department, Chennai – 9.
11. Any candidate may withdraw his candidature by a notice in writing as speciﬁ ed in Form – III signed by 
him may be delivered to either of the Ofﬁ cers speciﬁ ed in the paragraph (2) above at his ofﬁ ce before 3.00 P.M. 
on 18th September 2017.
12. No person who suffers from any of the disqualiﬁ cations under the Act shall be eligible to contest the election.
13. If the number of candidates who were duly nominated and who have not withdrawn their candidature is equal to 
the number of vacancies to be ﬁ lled, the Election Authority shall declare all such candidates to have been duly elected.
14. In the event of the election being conducted, the poll will be held on 4th October 2017 between 
10.00 A.M. to 4.00 P.M. at the Ofﬁ ce of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board, No.1, Jaffer Syrang Street, Vallal Seethakkathi 
Nagar, Chennai –600 001.
