Amendments to Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Secretariat Service Rules
(S.O. Ms. No. 245, Legislative Assembly Secretariat, 14th November 2009.)
No. SRO B-74(a)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (3) of Article 187 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu,
after consultation with the Speaker of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly, hereby makes the following amendments to
the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Secretariat Service Rules :—
2. The amendments hereby made shall come into force with effect on and from the date of publication of the Notification.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules,—
(1) in APPENDIX II, for the entries in column (4) against Class “II” in column (1), Category “6” in column (2), and the
post “Assistants” in column (3) thereof, the following entries shall be substituted, namely:—
“(i) Direct recruitment; or
(ii) Promotion from Category 8 of Class II and categories 2 and 3 of Class III; or
(iii) Promotion from Category 4 of Class III and categories 3 and 4 of Class IV; or
(iv) Recruitment by transfer from the category of Assistant in the Tamil Nadu Secretariat Service or from the category
of Junior Assistant or Assistant in the Tamil Nadu Ministerial Service or in the Tamil Nadu Judicial Ministerial Service”; .
(2) in APPENDIX III, in column (5), after item (ii) against Class “II” in column (1), Category “6” in column (2), the post
“Assistants” in Column (3) and the method of appointment Recruitment by Transfer in column (4) thereof, the following
entries shall be added, namely:—
“(iii) Must have put in a service for a period of not less than five years, either in the category of Assistant or in the
category of Junior Assistant or in both the categories put together in the Tamil Nadu Ministerial Service or in the Tamil Nadu
Judicial Ministerial Service”.
M. SELVARAJ,
