GENERAL NOTIFICATION
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI
Taking over the Direct Management of Barafath Charities @ Sheik Dawood Meelad Waqf, Chennai District.  
(Lr. Rc. No. 5098/95/B9/Chen.)
No. VI-3(a)/115/2018.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act 1995, the Tamil Nadu 
Wakf Board in its resolution  item No.87/18 in Rc.No.5098/95/B9/Chennai dated 28-08-2018 has ordered to take over the 
Management of BARAFATH CHARITIES @ SHEIK DAWOOD MEELAD WAQF, CHENNAI DISTRICT under the control of 
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of six months from 28-08-2018 and to appoint the Superintendent of Wakfs, 
Chennai as its Executive Ofﬁ cer.
