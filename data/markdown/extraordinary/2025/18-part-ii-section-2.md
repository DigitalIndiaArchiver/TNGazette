Under section 28-A of the Representation of the People Act, 1951 (Central Act XLIII of 1951), the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby declares the Director General of Police, Tamil Nadu, Chennai-4, the 
Additional Director General of Police (Law and Order), Chennai-4 and the Superintendent of Police, Erode 
District  and all Police Officials subordinate to them upto the level of Police Constables and any other Police 
Officers/ Officials, performing in any way the election related duties for the conduct of Bye-Election to be 
held on the 5th February 2025 to the Legislative Assembly of this State from 98.Erode (East) Assembly 
Constituency, as Designated Officers and all the said Officers shall be deemed to be on deputation to the 
Election Commission for the period commencing on and from the date of the notification calling for the said 
Election and ending with the date of declaration of the result of the said Election and accordingly, the said 
Officers shall, during that period, be subject to the control, superintendence and discipline of the Election 
Commission.
