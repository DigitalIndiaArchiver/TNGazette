GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI.
Taking over the Direct Management of Sadathullah Khan Masjid Wakf, Kancheepuram District.
(W.E.A. No. 5/06/Kpm/E5.)
No. VI-3(a)/31/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995, the Tamil Nadu
Wakf Board in its resolution in item No. 107/07, dated 1st June 2010 in W.E.A. No. 5/06/E5/Kpm. has ordered to take
over the Management of Sadathullah Khan Masjith Wakf, Kancheepuram District, under the direct control of the
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of one year from the date of publication in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette
and to appoint the Superintendent of Wakfs/Kancheepuram and Tiruvallur as its Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
24th March 2011.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
Taking over the Direct Management of Shikul Mulk Dilawar Jung Bahadur Wakf, Chennai.
(Lr. Rc. No. 4746/11/B6/Chennai.)
No. VI-3(a)/32/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995, the Tamil Nadu
Wakf Board in its Proceedings in resolution in item No. 14/05 in W.E.A. 15/04/E5/Chennai, dated 2nd June 2010  has
ordered to take over the Management of Shikul Mulk Dilawar Jung Bahadur Wakf, Chennai, under the direct control
of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of two year from the date of publication in the Tamil Nadu Government
Gazette and has appointed the Superintendent of Wakfs, Chennai as its Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
7th April 2011.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
