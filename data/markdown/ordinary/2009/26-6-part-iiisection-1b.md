Ad hoc Rules relating to Tamil Nadu Medical Service
[G.O. Rt. No. 1045, Health and Family Welfare (A1), 17th June 2009, Aani 3, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
SRO B-42/2009.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following Rules:—
2. The rules hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 21st February 1987.
RULES
The General and the Special Rules applicabe to the holders of the permanent post of Clinical Reader in Category
9 in Class I, in Branch -I, Medical of the Tamil Nadu Medical Service shall apply to the holders of the temporary post
of Associate Reader in Respiratory Diseases, subject to the Modifications specified in the following rules:—
2. Constitution.—The post shall consitute a distinct category in the said class of the said branch of the said service.
3. Appointment.—Appointment to the post shall be made as follows:—
(i)
by promotion from the category of Clinical Tutor or Clinical Lecturer; or
(ii) by transfer from any other category included in the cadre of civil surgeon in Class I in Branch I-Medical of
the said service.
4. Qualification.—No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post by promotion or by transfer, unless he
possesses the following qualifications, namely:—
(a) M.D. in Paediatrics;
(b) D.M. (Pulmonology) or Ph.D., Paediatric Respiratory Diseases;
(c) Must have worked as Clinical Lecturer or Clinical Tutor for a period of not less than five years in Paediatric
Pulmonology (Respiratory Diseases) Department; and
(d) Must possess teaching experience for a period of not less than four years after completion of M.D. (Paediatrics):
Provided that the period referred to in clauses (c) & (d) may run concurrently:
(e) Provided further that, if no person possessing the qualification in clause (b) is available, persons possessing
the remaining qualifications shall be considered for appointment.
5. Pay.—There shall be paid to the holders of the post, a monthly pay calculated in the scale of Rs. 1575-90-1845-
100-2245-110-2685; raisfced Rs. 3000-100-3500-125):
Provided that with effect on and from the 1st February 1999, the monthly pay shall be calculated in the scale of
Rs. 12,000-375-16,500.
V.K. SUBBURAJ,
Principal Secretary to Government.
