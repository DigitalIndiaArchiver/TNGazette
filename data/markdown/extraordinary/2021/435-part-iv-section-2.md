The following Act of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly received the 
assent of the Governor on the 22nd September 2021 and is hereby 
published for general information:—
ACT No.18 OF 2021.
(By order of the Governor)
C. GOPI RAVIKUMAR,
Secretary to Government (Legislation),
Law Department.
Tamil Nadu Act 
XIV of 1955.
      An Act further to amend the Tamil Nadu Court-fees and Suits 
Valuation Act, 1955.
      BE it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu 
in the Seventy- second Year of the Republic of India as follows:—
  1. (1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu Court-fees and Suits 
Valuation (Amendment) Act, 2021.
        (2) It shall come into force at once.
  2. In the Tamil Nadu Court-fees and Suits Valuation Act, 1955, 
in Schedule I, for the entries in columns (2) and (3) against Article 7 in 
column (1) thereof, the following entries shall, respectively, be substituted, 
namely:—        
 
Short title and 
commence- 
ment. 
Amendment of 
Schedule-I.
“Certificate under the Indian     	
  Succession Act, 1925.
Three per cent on the debt and security or 
the aggregate amount or value of the debts 
and securities specified  in the Succession 
Certificate, subject to a maximum of twenty 
five thousand rupees.”.
