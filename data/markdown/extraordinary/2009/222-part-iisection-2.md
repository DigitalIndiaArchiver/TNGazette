FINANCE DEPARTMENT
(Salaries)
NOTIFICATION REGARDING THE PROCUREMENT OF THE PROVISION OF COMMON USER GROUP MOBILE PHONE
SERVICES FROM BHARAT SANCHAR NIGAM LIMITED BY TAMIL NADU FIRE AND RESCUE SERVICES DEPARTMENT
UNDER TAMIL NADU TRANSPARENCY IN TENDERS ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 403, Finance (Salaries), 24th August 2009, Aavani 8, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. II(2)/FIN/424(c)/2009.
Under clause (f) of Section 16 of the Tamil Nadu Transparency in Tenders Act, 1998 (Tamil Nadu Act 43 of 1998),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notifies the procurement of provision of Common User Group Mobile Phone services
from Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL) by the Tamil Nadu Fire and Rescue Services Department.
K. GNANADESIKAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
