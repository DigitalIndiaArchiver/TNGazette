There is a channel situated in S.No. 760/2 of Sirumalai
village in an extent of 8.32.5 hectares at the northern upper
side within the four boundaries of the proposed Sirumalai west
Forest Block–I Bit-I.  This is a separate survey field.  This
channel originates at the boundary of S.Nos. 695 and 760-2
of Sirumalai village within the forest area.  The breadth of the
Channel at the Eastern edge is 131 meter.  This channel runs
westwards and southwards and ends at the boundary of
S.Nos. 695 and 760-2 of Sirumalai village.  The breadth of the
channel at the southern edge is 49.4 meter.  This channel
originates as Odai from S.No. 695, gets converted into channel
in S.No. 760-2 and then reconverted into Odai in S.No. 695
and passes westwards and then ends near Sirumalaiyaru
Reservoir after crossing S.No. 452 of village No. 37. Oruthattu
village. This channel has been in existence even prior to  the
preliminary notification under Section 4 of the Tamil Nadu
Forest Act, 1882.  Therefore, permission has been granted to

the public were permitted to use the existing channel in its
