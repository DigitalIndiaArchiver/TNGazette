No. II(1)/HOPE/5(c-1)/2020.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 18-B of the Tamil Nadu Prohibition Act, 1937 (Tamil Nadu 
Act X of 1937), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Prohibition and Excise Department 
Notification No.II(1)/PE/239(d)/81, published at page 2 of Part II—Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, 
dated the 1st May 1981, as subsequently Amended.
Amendments.
In the said Notification, in the TABLE, under the entry “Indian Made Foreign Spirits (brand names of which are specified in 
the Annexure) other than sacramental wine”,—
(1)  	 for the expression “Rs.140.00 (Rupees one hundred and forty only) per proof litre” in column (2) against 
the entry under the heading “(i) ORDINARY BRANDS” in column (1), the expression “Rs.150.00 
(Rupees one hundred and fifty only) per proof litre” shall be substituted;
(2) 	 for the expression “Rs.155.00 (Rupees one hundred and  fifty five only) per proof litre” in column 
(2) against the entry under the heading  “(ii) MEDIUM BRANDS” in column (1), the  expression 
“Rs.165.00 (Rupees one hundred and sixty five only) per proof litre” shall be substituted;
(3)	  under   the  heading  “(iii) PREMIUM BRANDS” in column (1),— 
(i)	
for the expression “Rs.180.00 (Rupees one hundred and eighty only) per proof litre” in column 
(2), the expression “Rs.190.00 (Rupees one hundred and ninty only) per proof litre” shall be 
substituted;
(ii)	
for the expression “Rs.210.00 (Rupees two hundred and ten only) per proof litre” in column 
(2), the expression “Rs.220.00 (Rupees two hundred and twenty only) per proof litre” shall be 
substituted.

No. II(1)/HOPE/5(c-2)/2020.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 18-B of the Tamil Nadu Prohibition Act, 1937 
(Tamil Nadu Act X of 1937), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Prohibition and Excise 
Department Notification No.II(1)/PE/239(d)/81, published at page 2 of Part II—Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette 
Extraordinary, dated the 1st May 1981, as subsequently amended.
Amendment.
In the said Notification, in the TABLE, for the expression “Rs.10.00 (Rupees ten only) per bulk litre” in column (2) against 
the entry “Beer” in column (1), the expression “Rs.11.00 (Rupees eleven only) per bulk litre” shall be substituted.
