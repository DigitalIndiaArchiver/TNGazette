GENERAL  NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI.
Taking over the Direct Management of 5th George Arabic Madarasa Wakf, Pettai, Namakkal District.
(R.c. No. 4161/12/B2/NMKL.)
No. VI-3(a)/138/2013.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995, the
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in item No.60/12 in Rc.No.4161/12/B2/NMKL, dated: 22-08-2013 has ordered
to take over the Management of 5th George Arabic Madarasa, Pettai, Namakkal District under the direct control of the
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of one year from the date of Board’s order (i.e.) 22-08-2013 and to appoint the
Superintendent of Wakfs, Salem as its Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,
F. ABDUL RAZICK,
17th October 2013.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
Taking over the Direct Management of Masjid E Sayeedia and Madarasa E Ghousia, Anna Salai,
Chennai District.
(Lr. R.c. No. 15908/12/B9/CHN.)
No. VI-3(a)/139/2013.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995, the
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in item No.49/13 in Rc.No.15908/12/B9/CHN, dated 22-08-2013 has ordered
to take over of Masjid E Sayeedia and Madrasa E Ghousia, Anna Salai, Chennai District under the Direct Control of
theTamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of one year from the date of Board’s order (22-08-2013). And to appoint
Wakf Inspector, Chennai (Central) as its Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,
F. ABDUL RAZICK,
17th October 2013.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
Taking over of Sellancheri Thaikka and Dargah, Sellancheri, Cuddalore District under the
Direct Management of the Board.
(Lr. R.c. No. 6574/11/B4/CUDD.)
No. VI-3(a)/140/2013.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995, the
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in item No.157/12 in Rc.No.6574/11/B4/CUDD, dated 22-08-2013 has
ordered to take over of Sellancheri Thaikka and Dargah, Sellancheri, Cuddalore District under the Direct Control of
theTamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of one year from the date of Board’s order (22-08-2013) and to
appoint Superintendent of Wakfs, Cuddalore as its Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,
F. ABDUL RAZICK,
17th October 2013.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
