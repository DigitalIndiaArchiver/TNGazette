AUTHORISATION OF - NOTIFIED.]
(S.O. No. 3/2016/TNSEC/ME-1)
No. VI(2)/91(b-1)/2016.
In exercise of the powers conferred by rule 8 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities, Municipalities
and Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006 and in supersession of the Notification No. I - VI(2)/427(d-1)/2011, published at
Page 1 of Part VI - Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary No. 286, dated 29th August 2011, the
State Election Commission hereby authorises the Executive Officers of the respective Town Panchayats, Third Grade
Municipalities or any other officer appointed by the State Election Officer / District Election Officer as the case may be, as
the Electoral Registration Officers for the purpose of preparing and publishing electoral rolls in respect of the respective
Town Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities and the wards lying within the respective Town Panchayats, Third Grade
Municipalities.
NOTIFICATION-II
(S.O. No. 3/2016/TNSEC/ME-1)
No. VI(2)/91(b-2)/2016.
In exercise of the powers conferred by rule 8 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities, Municipalities
and Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006 and in supersession of the Notification No. II - VI(2)/427(d-2)/2011, published at

State Election Commission hereby authorises the Commissioners of the respective Municipalities as the Electoral Registration
Officers for the purpose of preparing and publishing electoral rolls in respect of the respective Municipalities and the wards

NOTIFICATION-III
(S.O. No. 3/2016/TNSEC/ME-1)
No. VI(2)/91(b-3)/2016.
In exercise of the powers conferred by rule 8 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities, Municipalities
and Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006 and in supersession of the Notification No. III - VI(2)/427(D-3)/2011, published

the State Election Commission hereby authorises the Revenue Officer of the Chennai City Municipal Corporation as the
Electoral Registration Officer for the purpose of preparing and publishing electoral rolls in respect of the said Corporation
and the wards lying within the said Corporation.

[ELECTIONS - PANCHAYATS - CONDUCT OF - PREPARATION OF ELECTORAL ROLLS - APPOINTMENT OF BLOCK
DEVELOPMENT OFFICER (BLOCK PANCHAYAT) AS PANCHAYAT ELECTORAL REGISTRATION OFFICER -
NOTIFICATION ISSUED.]
(S.O. No. 4/2016/TNSEC/PE-1)
No. VI(2)/91(b-9)/2016.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 30 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil
Nadu Act 21 of 1994) read with rule 7 of the Tamil Nadu panchayats (Elections) Rules, 1995, the Tamil Nadu State Election
Commission hereby designates the Block Development Officers (Block Panchayat) to be the Panchayat Electoral Registration
Officers for the  purposes of preparing and publishing panchayat electoral rolls in respect of wards of panchayats lying within
their respective jurisdiction in connection with the conduct of Ordinary Elections, 2016 to Panchayats and subsequent Casual
Elections in the State.
(By Order of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission)
