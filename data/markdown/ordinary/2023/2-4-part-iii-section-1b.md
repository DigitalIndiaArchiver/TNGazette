Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the adhoc rules published with the Information and Tourism 
Department Notification SRO No. B-308/86 at pages 410 to 412 of Part III—Section 1(b) of the Tamil Nadu Government 
Gazette dated the 23rd July, 1986.
Amendment
