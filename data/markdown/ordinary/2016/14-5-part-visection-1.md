JUDICIAL NOTIFICATIONS
Conferment of Powers
(Roc. No. 14492/2016/B6.)
No. VI(1)/128/2016.
No. 33/2016.—In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 13  of the Code of Criminal  Procedure 1973
(Central Act 2 of 1974), the High Court hereby appoints Tmt. L. Maithily, Probationary Deputy Collector by
direct recruitment, for the year 2011-2013, for a period of 180 days, on part time basis, to be the Special Judicial
Magistrate in Kancheepuram District, from the date of assumption of office. The powers may be withdrawn
as and when she completes her training in Kancheepuram District and confers upon her the ordinary powers
conferrable under this Code on a Magistrate, to try such cases, which were earlier tried and dealt with by an
erstwhile Judicial Second Class Magistrate, for completing her Magisterial Training in Kancheepuram District.
High Court, Madras,
P. KALAIYARASAN,
17th March 2016.
