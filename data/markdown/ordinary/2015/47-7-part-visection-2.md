Erratum to Tamil Nadu Electricity Regulatory Commission – State Advisory Committee (Second Amendment)
Regulation – 2012
Errata to Notification
(Notification No.TNERC/SACR/2-41, dated 28th October 2015)
The following errata is issued to the Tamil Nadu Electricity Regulatory Commission Notification No.TNERC/SACR/2-33,
dated 5-7-2012 published in Issue No.30 of Tamil Nadu Government Gazette dated 1st August 2012, Part VI – Section 2 at
page 357-358
ERRATA
In the said Tamil Nadu Government Gazette at page 358 in the second line from top for the words “resides and Chennai
and back” read the words “resides to Chennai and back”.
Chennai–600 008,
S. GUNASEKARAN,
28th October 2015.
Secretary
Tamil Nadu Electricity Regulatory Commission.


Nov. 25, 2015]

EXPLANATORY NOTE
An amendment was issued to the State Advisory Committee Regulations 2004 (Principal Regulations) substituting
Regulations 12(4) as follows”-
“(4) (i) For the members other than those who are governed by sub-regulation (3) above, the payment of daily allowance
shall be at the rates specified by the Commission from time to time which may also be indicated in their appointment orders.
The payment of Travelling Allowance shall be as follows:-
(ii) For the members who reside outside Chennai 2nd Air-conditioned class train fare from the nearest Railway Station
of the place where he normally resides and Chennai and back shall be reimbursed by the Commission towards the Travelling
Allowance.”
The above amendment was notified by TNERC in Notification No.TNERC/SACR/2-33, dated 5-7-2012 which was
published in Issue No.30 of Tamil Nadu Government Gazette dated 1st August 2012 Part VI – Section 2 at page 357-358
The Committee on Delegated Legislations 2014-15 took up the above Notification for examination on 23rd April 2015 and
observed that for the words “resides and Chennai and back”, the words “resides to Chennai and back” may be substituted.
Accepting the above observation of the Committee on Delegated Legislation, in consultation with the Energy Department,
TNERC decided to issue an Erratum to Notification No.TNERC/SACR/2-33, dated 5-7-2012 by substituting the words “resides
and Chennai and back” with the words “resides to Chennai and back”
The above notification issued is intended to achieve the above object.
Chennai–600 008,
S. GUNASEKARAN,
28th October 2015.
