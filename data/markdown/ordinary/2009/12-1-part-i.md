READ:
(1) From the Government of India, Ministry of Petroleum
and Natural Gas, New Delhi, Letter No. A. 19011/1/
2004-Estt., dated 04-03-2009.
(2) From the Government of India, Ministry of Personnel,
Public Grievances and Pensions, New Delhi,
Office
Memorandum F. No. 2/1/2009-CS-I(D),
dated 3-3-2009.
Order: No. 247, Public (Special-A), 18th March 2009.
The Government of Tamil Nadu have agreed for the
deputation of Thiru P. Karupasamy, an officer of Central
Secretariat Service, Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances
and Pensions, New Delhi, presently working as Director
(CSS), Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas, New Delhi
with this State Government for a period of three years with
effect from the date of his reporting for duty in the Government
of Tamil Nadu.
2. Thiru P. Karupasamy, Director (CSS), Ministry of
Petroleum and Natural Gas, New Delhi on deputation to the
Government of Tamil Nadu is posted to act as Additional
Commissioner of Labour, Chennai.
3. During the period of his deputation, he is allowed to
draw his grade pay in his time scale of pay, allowances and
entitlements as applicable to him in his parent cadre and
other perquisites as eligible to the post to which he is
posted.
(By Order of the Governor)
K.S. SRIPATHI,
Chief Secretary.

In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (1) of
