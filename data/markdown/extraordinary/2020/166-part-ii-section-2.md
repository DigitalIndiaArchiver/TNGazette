Order:
In the order first read above, Government have established a Borstal  School in the premises of transit yard in 
Central Prison, Puzhal under  section 3(1) of the Tamil Nadu Borstal Schools Act, 1925 (Tamil Nadu Act V  of 1925) 
for detaining adolescent offenders belonging to Chennai,  Kancheepuram and Tiruvallur Districts. 
2. The Director General of Police, Chennai-4, in his letter second read  above has stated that, 14 cases 
are registered involving 129 foreign  nationals (including some women foreigners) in the State for their violation 
of VISA conditions and other offences. The Commissioners of Police/  Superintendents of Police concerned are 
sending proposals to obtain the  G.O. under section 3 (2) (e) of the Foreigners Act, 1946, to lodge them in a 
Special Camp till their deportation and they may be released on bail shortly.  At present, Trichy Special Camp

is the only Special Camp functioning in the  State and it has 40 rooms available (including recently renovated 
20 rooms), in which 80 persons as the maximum can only be accommodated.  At present, 73 foreigners have 
been accommodated. About 20 foreigners  are yet to be accommodated upon their release on bail from Central 
Prison,  Puzhal, on whom already G.O. has been issued. Presently, Trichy Special Camp is facing space constraint 
to accommodate any foreigners. In the  event of release of the above said 129 foreign nationals (including 
women  foreigners), they have to be accommodated in a special camp, till their  deportation. However, as 
mentioned above, there is no space for  accommodating the said foreign nationals in Trichy Special Camp. 
He has  therefore, requested the Government to designate Borstal School, Puzhal  Prison as a Special Camp, 
as suggested by the Director General of Police/  Inspector General of Prisons to accommodate the foreign 
nationals against  whom 14 cases have been registered recently in the State for violation of  VISA conditions 
and other offences and therefore notification earmarking  Borstal School, Puzhal Prison as Special Camp may 
be issued by the  Government at the earliest. 
3. The Director General of Police/ Inspector General of Prisons,  Chennai-8 in his letter third read above has stated 
that, in the meeting held  with the Director General of Police on 13.04.2020, it was informed that the  capacity of Trichy 
Special Camp is only 80 in which at present 73 foreigners  have been accommodated. Further, many foreign nationals 
who have been  remanded to judicial custody for violating visa conditions have to be  accommodated in Special Camp 
in the event of their release on bail by the  courts concerned. He has, therefore, requested the Government that the 
Borstal School, Puzhal which has the capacity of 110 persons can serve as  Special Camp to accommodate the foreign 
nationals on their release from  prison. 
4. After examining the above proposal of the Director General of  Police, Chennai-4 based on the report of 
the Director General of Police /  Inspector General of Prisons, Chennai-8 third read above, Government have 
decided to rescind the earlier Notification issued in G.D. (Ms.) No.549,  Home Department, dated 19.08.2014, 
establishing a Borstal School in the  premises of the transit yard in Central Prison, Puzhal, under Section 3 (1) 
of the Tamil Nadu Borstal Schools Act, 1925 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1925), by  invoking the powers conferred 
under Section 3 (1) of the Tarnil Nadu  Borstal Schools Act, 1925 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1925) read with section 
15  of the Tamil Nadu General Clauses Act, 1891 (Tamil Nadu Act 1 of 1891); 
6. Accordingly, the following Notification shall be published in the  Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, 
dated the 22nd April 2020:- 
NOTIFICATION
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub- section (1) of Section 3 of  the Tamil Nadu Borstal Schools Act, 1925 
(Tamil Nadu Act V of 1925) read  with section 15 of the Tamil Nadu General Clauses Act, 1891 (Tamil Nadu Act 1 of 
1891), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby rescinds Notification  No. 11(2)/ HQ/640/2014 published at page 
582 of part II- Section 2 of the  Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 10th September 2014.
 
(By order of the Governor)
