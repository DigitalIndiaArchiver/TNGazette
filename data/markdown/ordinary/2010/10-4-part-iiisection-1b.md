No. SRO B-17/2010.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Sections 8 and 10 of the Tamil Nadu District Police
Act, 1859 (Central Act XXIV of 1859) and Sections 9 and 11 of the Chennai City Police Act, 1888 (Tamil Nadu Act III
of 1888), read with the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes
the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Police Subordinate Service (Section 31 in Volume III
of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual).
2.
The amendments hereby made shall  be deemed to have come into force with effect on and from the
23rd February 2010.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules, in ANNEXURE-II, under “Class-I”, in column (3),—
(1) against the entries “Category-.2 Sub-Inspectors” and “Category-2A Sub-Inspector of Police (Finger Print)
(Men and Women)” in column (1), in item 3, after the second proviso; the following proviso shall be added, namely:—
“Provided also that in respect of category-2 Sub-Inspectors, the minimum qualifying marks in the written
examination, for the open quota candidates shall be 25 marks out of 70 marks and for the departmental quota candidates
shall be 30 marks out of 85 marks.”;
(2) aganist the entry “Category-8 Constables, Men and Women (other than Band Constables, Office of the
Director General of Police) and Reserve Constables” in column (1), in item 3, after the TABLE under the heading “For
Women Candidates”, for the sentence “Out of 85 marks a candidate shall secure minimum of 40 marks in the test”,
the following sentence shall be substituted, namely:—
“The minimum qualifying marks in the written examination for the recruitment of Constables (Men and Women)
shall be 28 marks out of 80 marks, excluding the 5 marks for Extra qualifications”.
Amendment to the Tamil Nadu Fire Subordinate Service Rules.
[G.O. Ms. No. 233, Home (Police-XVII), 26th  February 2010.]
No. SRO B-18/2010.—In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (b) of sub-section (2) of Section 27 of the
Tamil Nadu Fire Service Act, 1985 (Tamil Nadu Act 40 of 1985) read with the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution
of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Tamil Nadu Fire Subordinate
Service Rules (Section 13 in Volume-II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1969).
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in rule 4, in sub-rule (b), for items (i) and (ii), the following items shall be substituted, namely:—
“(i) 10% of the vacancies under direct recruitment shall be reserved for sports personnel. If it is not possible to
fill up the said sports quota of 10%, the gap shall be filled up by the wards cum dependents of the serving personnel
of the Fire and Rescue Services Department.
(ii) 10% of the vacancies under direct recruitment shall be reserved for wards cum dependents of the serving
personnel as well as the retired, deceased or medically invalidated personnel of the Fire and Rescue Services Department:
Provided that 10% of the above 10% vacancies specified in item(ii) shall be reserved for the wards cum dependents
of the serving ministerial staff of the Fire and Rescue Services Department.
Explanation:
The term ‘wards cum dependents’ means and includes—
(1)
Son
(2)
Unmarried Daughter
(3)
Legally Adopted Son
(4)
Legally Adopted unmarried Daughter”.
