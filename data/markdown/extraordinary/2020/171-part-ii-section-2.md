NOTIFICATION
Hon’ble Chief Minister reviewed the situation due to the outbreak of COVID-19 in the State and based on the 
discussion with the Medical and Public Health Experts, the Government have issued orders in the reference seventh 
read above to further intensify the lock down measures in the Urban areas and ordered the following measures under 
the Disaster Management Act, 2005 :
•  To intensify the present lock down measure, a complete lock down  will be implemented 
in the Corporation limits of Chennai, Coimbatore and Madurai  from 26.4.2020 Sunday 6.00 
A.M. to 29.4.2020 Wednesday 9.00 P.M.
•  Complete lock down will also be implemented in Corporation limits of Salem and Tiruppur 
from 26.4.2020 Sunday 6.00 A.M. to 28.4.2020 Tuesday 9.00 P.M.
During the above lock down period the certain essential services were permitted. Now, the Government after careful 
examination ordered to exempt the following essential services during the above lockdown period.
	
1.	 Functioning of Tresuries and Pay and Accounts Office.
	
2.	 Essential Manufacturing such as Medical Equipments, Pharmaceuticals, Food items etc
	
3.	 Telecom Services (Telecom Service Providers/Internet Service Providers/Internet Providers)
Further passes will continue to be issued by concern District Collectors/Commissioner, Greater Chennai Corporation
in respect Chennai City only for Marriages already fixed, Medical emergencies and funerals.	
(By order of the Governor)
	
K. SHANMUGAM,
Chief Secretary.
