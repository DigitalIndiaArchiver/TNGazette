MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION AND WATER SUPPLY DEPARTMENT
VARIATION NOTIFICATIONS UNDER THE TAMIL NADU PARKS, PLAY-FIELDS AND OPEN SPACES
(PRESERVATION AND REGULATION) ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 41, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (MC.II), 8th February 2011.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/55(c-1)/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Parks, Play-fields and
Open Spaces  (Preservation and Regulation) Act, 1959 (Tamil Nadu Act 26 of 1959), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby makes the following variation to the approved list of Parks and Play-fields published in the Education
Department Notification No.75 of 1967 in Part II—Section 3 at pages 534 to 540 of the
Fort St. George Gazette, dated the 14th June 1967, the draft of the same having been previously published,
as requires by sub-section (2) of the said section :—
VARIATION
In the said Notification, in the Schedule, under the heading ‘Play-fields’,—
(1) the entry ‘293’ in column (2) and the entries relating thereto in columns (3) to (8) against the entry
“20 St. Joseph’s College Play-field” in column (1) shall be omitted;
(2) for the entry ‘294/1’, in column (2) and the entry “0,6160” in column (3) against the entry
“20 St. Joseph’s College Play-field” in column (1) thereof, the entry  “New S.No. 40 part” and the entry “0 22,000”
shall respectively be substituted.

No. II(2)/MAWS/55(c-2)/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Parks, Play-fields and Open
Spaces  (Preservation and Regulation) Act, 1959 (Tamil Nadu Act 26 of 1959), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the
following variation to the approved list of Parks and Play-fields published in the Education Department Notification No.75 of 1967
in Part II—Section 3 at pages 534 to 540 of the Fort St. George Gazette, dated the 14th June 1967, the draft of the same having
been previously published, as requires by sub-section (2) of the said section :—
VARIATION
In the said Notification, in the Schedule, under the heading ‘Play-fields’, the entry ‘‘17 Periyar Training School’’ in Column (1)
and the corresponding entries relating thereto in columns (2) to (8) shall be omitted.
