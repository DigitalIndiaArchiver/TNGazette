AMENDMENT TO THE TABLE OF FEES UNDER THE REGISTRATION ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 76, Commercial Taxes and Registration (J2), 15th October 2014.]
No. II(2)/CTR/607(a-1)/2014.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 78 of the Registration Act, 1908 (Central Act XVI of 1908),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Table of Fees published with the
erstwhile Judicial Department Notification No.177, dated the 30th March 1909, at pages 316 to 319 of
Part—I of the Fort St. George Gazette, dated the 30th March 1909, as subsequently amended:—
2. The amendment hereby made shall come into force on the 16th October, 2014.
AMENDMENT
In the said Table of Fees, in Article 1, in the third proviso to clause (b), the expression
“among family members” shall be omitted.
APPOINTMENT OF DISTRICT REGISTRARS AS ‘COLLECTORS’ FOR THE PURPOSES OF
CERTAIN SECTIONS UNDER THE INDIAN STAMP ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 77, Commercial Taxes and Registration (J1), 15th October 2014.]
No. II(2)/CTR/607(a-2)/2014.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-clause (b) of clause (9) of Section 2 of the Indian Stamp Act,
1899 (Central Act II of 1899), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints all the District Registrars
appointed under the Registration Act, 1908 (Central Act XVI of 1908) as Collectors for the purposes of
Sections 49, 52, 53 and 54 of the Indian Stamp Act, 1899 (Central Act II of 1899), within their respective
jurisdiction.
