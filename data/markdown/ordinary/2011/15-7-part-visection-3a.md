TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI.
Revoking of Direct Management of Vatlagundu Tholugai Pallivasal Nilakottai Taluk, Dindigul District
and Appointment of Ad-hoc Committee.
(Rc. No. 616/06/B8/Dgl.)
No. VI-3(a)/27/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred under the provisions of the Wakf Act, 1995, the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board had
earlier made necessary notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated 14th October 2009 for taking over
the above Vatlagundu Tholugai Pallivasal under its Direct Management.
Now the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in item No. 10/2011 (Rc. 616/06/B8/Dgl), dated 17th February 2011
has ordered to revoke the Direct Management of the above Wakf by appointing an Adhoc Committee for a period of
one year.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
23rd February 2011.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
Masjid-E-Sayeedia Wakf, Triplicane, Chennai, Revoking of Direct Management
and Appointment of Managing Committee.
(Rc. No. 9582/08/B6/Chennai.)
No. VI-3(a)/28/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions of the Wakf Act, 1995, the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board
in its resolution in item No. 213/2008, dated 28th July 2009 had ordered to take over the above Masjid-E-Sayeedia
Wakf, Chennai under the direct control of the Board and necessary notification was also published in the Tamil Nadu
Government Gazette on 21st October 2009. The Executive Officer appointed by the Board is looking after affairs of
the Wakf. Now the Board in its resolution in item No. 19/2011, dated 18th February 2011 has appointed 10 members
Managing Committee for the administration of the above Wakf for a period of 3 years by revoking Direct Management.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
25th February 2011.
Chief Executive Officer,
