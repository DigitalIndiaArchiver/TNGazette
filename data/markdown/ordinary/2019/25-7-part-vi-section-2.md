Amendments to the Tamil Nadu Electricity Regulatory Commission - Conduct of Business Regulations, 2004
(Notiﬁ cation No.TNERC/CBR/1-2, dated 18-3-2019)
No. VI(2)/53/2019.
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (zk) of sub-section (2) of Section 181 read with sub-section (1) 
of Section 92 of the Electricity Act, 2003 (Central Act 36 of 2003) and all other powers enabling it in this behalf, the
Tamil Nadu Electricity Regulatory Commission hereby makes the following amendment to the Tamil Nadu Electricity 
Regulatory Commission – Conduct of Business Regulations, 2004, the draft of the same having been previously published 
as required by sub-section (3) of Section 181 of the said Act.
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖග
In the said Regulations, in regulation 31, for sub-regulation (6), the following sub-regulation shall be substituted, 
namely;-
“(6) (a) All orders of the Commission shall be deemed to have been communicated to the parties from 
the date of hosting of the same in the website of the Commission and for the purpose of compliance of the 
orders of the Commission by any party, the downloaded copy of the order of the Commission, as attested by 
the counsel for any of the parties to the proceedings before the Commission with his name, seal along with 
enrolment number, is deemed to be suﬃ  cient for such compliance and shall be accepted and acted upon by a 
party which is required to comply with the orders of the Commission.
 
(b) In all cases concerning initiation of proceedings by any aﬀ ected or interested person under Regulation 
16(1) of the Conduct of Business Regulation consequent to the direction of the High Court or any other forum, 
the downloaded and printed copy of the order of the High Court or any other forum and attested by the counsel 
for any of the parties before the Commission with his name, seal along with enrolment number shall be deemed 
to be suﬃ  cient for initiating such proceedings under Regulation 16(1) of the Conduct of Business Regulation, 
subject to veriﬁ cation of authenticity of the same with the website of such Forum by the designated oﬃ  cer”.
(By Order of the Tamil Nadu Electricity Regulatory Commission)
