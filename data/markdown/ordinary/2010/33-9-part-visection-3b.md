Elections to the syndicate, senate and the standing committee of the Academic Council
by the Academic Council.
(No.V.1/College/8-2010/253)
No. VI-3(b)/34/2010.
It is hereby notified that the election to (i) THE SYNDICATE; (ii) THE SENATE and (iii)THE STANDING COMMITTE OF
THE ACADEMIC COUNCIL, will be held under Section 18(a) - Other Members (2) Section 14 (a) - Other Members (2) and
Section 24(k) of the Madras University Act, 1923 respectively.
(i) THE SYNDICATE—[Section 18(a)-Other members (2)]
“Five members elected by the Academic Council from among its members of whom four shall be teachers of affiliated
colleges and the remaining shall be a teacher of an approved college”.
Affiliated colleges (Four Members):
The newly elected members will hold office as members of the Syndicate for a period of three years from the date of
receipt of nomination/the date of withdrawal/the date of election/the date of declaration of the result/the date of the occurrence
of vacancy whichever is applicable for the election.
Approved colleges (One Member):
The newly elected member will hold office as a member of the Syndicate for the residual period upto 24-08-2012 from
the date of election/the date of withdrawal/the date of election/the date of declaration of results whichever is appllicable
for the election.
(ii) THE SENATE—[Section 14(a) - Other members (2)]
“Ten members elected by the Academic council from among is own body of whom not less than five shall be teachers
of affiliated colleges”.
The newly elected members will hold office as members of the Senate for a period of three years from the date of receipt
of nomination/the date of withdrawal/the date of election/the date of declaration of the result/the date of the occurrence of
vacancy whichever is applicable for the election.
(iii) THE STANDING COMMITTEE OF THE ACADEMIC COUNCIL [Section 24(k)]
“There shall be a Standing Committee of the Academic Council which shall consist of the Vice-Chancellor and
five other members of the Academic Council of whom
(i)  two shall be the Principals or Teachers of Affiliated Colleges,
(ii)  two shall be members of the Syndicate and
(iii)  one shall be a teacher of a Department of Study and Research in the University”.
Among the above three clauses, the election will be held to fill up the vacancies under clauses (i and ii) to elect
two members from the Principals or Teachers of Affiliated Colleges and two members from the members of the Synidicate,
as per Chapter - XVI of the University Calendar Volume-I.
Nomination forms are available in the Registrar’s Office. Nomination should  be sent  so as to be received by the

upto 4.00 p.m.
