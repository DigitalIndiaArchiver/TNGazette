This Election Petition praying that this Hon'ble Court be pleased to.
(a) declare that the election of the returned candidate, viz, 1st respondent
herein from Ramanathapuram Constituency (Tamil Nadu) in the election held on
13th May 2009 and result was declared on 16th May 2009 as null and void.
(b) declare that the petitioner as duly elected member of Member of
Parliament from Ramanathapuram Constituency in the election held on 13th May
2009 and result was declared on 16th May 2009.
(c) directing the 1st respondent to pay the cost of the proceedings.
That the above Election Petition coming on this day before this
court for hearing in the presence of Mr. ARL. Sundaresan, Senior Counsel
for M/s. P. Andiraj, A.R. Numbunayagan, Advocate for the 1st respondent and
of Mr. G. Murugendran the 13th respondent appearing in person and of
Mr. M.R. Raghavan, Standing Counsel for the respondents 15 to 17 and the Election
Petitioner not appearing in person or by advocate and upon reading the petition and
affidavit of the Election Petitioner filed herein.
The Court made the following order:—
This Election Petition was posted today for trial. When the matter was called out,
neither the petitioner nor the counsel for the petitioner was present though it has been
specifically posted under the caption 'for trial'. Hence, this Election Petition stands
dismissed for want of prosecution. No costs.
