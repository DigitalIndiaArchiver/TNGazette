after the date of publication of this notification in the District Gazette and Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, the land vested to the
undermentioned Village and Panchayat the undermentioned taluk of Cuddalore District on the Schedule and measuring the extent
noted below be the same a little more or less shall be excluded from the operation of the said Act.
