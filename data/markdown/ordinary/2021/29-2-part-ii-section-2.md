No.II(2)/AGFW/408/2021.—In exercise of the powers 
conferred under clause (a) of sub-section (1) of Section 9 of 
the Tamil Nadu Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) 
Act, 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 1989), the Governor of 
Tamil Nadu hereby confirms the declaration of exclusion 
of Kallakurichi, Chinnasalem, Thiyagathurgam, Ulundurpet, 
Tirunavalur, Sankarapuram, Mungilthuraipattu, Manalurpet 
regulated market area and Vadakkanandal sub-regulated 
Market area from the notified area of Villupuram Market 
Committee. The intention to make such a declaration have 
been previously published as required by Section 3 of the 
said Act.

No.II(2)/AGFW/409/2021.—In exercise of the powers 
conferred under sub section (1) of Section 4 of the 
Tamil Nadu Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) 
Act 1987, (Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 1989), the Governor of 
Tamil Nadu hereby declares the Kallakurichi district to be 
notified area for the purpose of the said Act in respect of 
Paddy, Groundnut (Pods and Kernals) whole or split, Gingelly 
or Sesamum Seeds or Ellu, Cotton (Kapas, Lint, waste), 
Blackgram (Ulundu) in all forms, Greengram (Pachaippayaru) 
in all forms, Cashewnuts in all forms, Sugarcane Jaggery 
in all forms (Jaggery Powder, brown sugar, etc.), Coriander 
(Dhania Seeds), Turmeric in all forms (bulb and finger), 
Cowpea (Karamani) in all forms, Varagu, Neem Seeds and 
Cotton Seeds. The intention to make such a declaration 
have been the previously published as required by section 
3 of the said Act.
