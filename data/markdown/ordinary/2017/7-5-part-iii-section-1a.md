No. SRO A-12/2017.—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-sections (1) and (2) of section 180 read with
sub-section (5) of Section 33-A of the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act, 1983 (Tamil Nadu Act 30 of 1983), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Tamil Nadu State Co-operative Societies Election
Commissioner (Conditions of Service) Rules, 2013:-
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in rule 6, the expression “and other leave benefits like surrender of earned leave” shall be
omitted.
PRADEEP YADAV,
Principal Secretary to Government.
