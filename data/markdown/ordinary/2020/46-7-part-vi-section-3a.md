GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAQF BOARD, CHENNAI.
Taking over of the Direct Management of Trust Estate of M.K.P. Marcayar and 
Maqdoomi Masjid, George Town, Chennai District.
(R.c .No. 12765/16/B9/Chen)
No.VI-3(a)/97/2020.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Waqf Act 1995, the Tamil Nadu 
Waqf Board in its resolution in item No.12/2020 in Rc.No. 12765/16/B9/Chen dated: 7-10-2020 has resolved to take over 
the Management of TRUST ESTATE OF M.K.P. MARCAYAR AND MAQDOOMI MASJID, GEORGE TOWN, CHENNAI 
DISTRICT under the Direct Management of Tamil Nadu Waqf Board for a period of one year from 7-10-2020 to 
6-10-2021 and the Superintendent of Waqfs, Chennai is appointed as its Executive Oﬃ  cer.
Chennai-600 001,                                                       D. FARITHA BANU,
4th November 2020.                                           Chief Executive Oﬃ  cer / Estate Oﬃ  cer,  
 
                                                                   Tamil Nadu Waqf Board.
Taking Over of The Direct Management of Anjuman-E-Mohammadhi Waqf, 
Villivakkam Tiruvallur District.
(R.c .No. 15035/05/B4/TVLR)
No.VI-3(a)/98/2020.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Waqf Act 1995, the Tamil Nadu 
Waqf Board in its resolution in item No.17/20 in Rc.No. 15035/05/B4/TVLR dated: 7-10-2020 has resolved to take over 
the Management of Anjuman-e-Mohammadhi under the Direct Management of Tamil Nadu Waqf Board's order and to 
appoint the Superintendent of Waqf Poonamallee as its Executive Oﬃ  cer.
Chennai-600 001,                                                         D. FARITHA BANU,
4th November 2020.                                             Chief Executive Oﬃ  cer / Estate Oﬃ  cer,
