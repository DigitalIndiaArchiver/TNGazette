Section 28 of the Water (Prevention and Control of 
Pollution) Act, 1974 and Section 31 of Air (Prevention 
and Control of Pollution) Act, 1981 provide that the State 
Government may constitute a State Appellate Authority 
consisting of one or three persons as it may think ﬁ t to 
be appointed to help in the adjudication of disputes relating 
to Environment and Pollution. 
2. In pursuance of the orders of the Supreme Court 
in its Judgement in Civil Appeal No.368-371 of 1999 of 
SLP (Civil) No. 10317-10320/98 and Civii Appeal Nos.372, 
373/99 in SLP (Civil) No.10380 & 13380/98, the Authority 
is composed of a retired Judge of High Court as Chairman 
and Experts / Scientists in the ﬁ eld of Pollution Control as 
Members. Accordingly, the Appellate Authority for Tamil Nadu 
was constituted vide Government order 1st cited. 
3. In the Government order 2nd read above, 
Government have constituted the Appellate Authority under 
the Chairmanship of Hon'ble Justice S.S. Subramani. 
Subsequently, Government have issued orders at various 
time appointing the Chairman and the Members of Appellate 
Authority, Tamil Nadu Pollution Control Board. 
4. In Government order 3rd read above, Government 
have appointed Justice T. Sudanthiram as Chairman, 
Appellate Authority for adjudication of disputes relating to 
Environment and Pollution Control for the period of 3 years 
which expires on 27-03-2018. In the Government Order 
4th read above, the tenure of the Appellate Authority of 
Tamil Nadu Pollution Control Board was extended from 
19-02-2016 to 18-02-2019. 
5. In the circumstances stated above, since the 
tenure of the Present Chairman, Appellate Authority of 
Tamil Nadu Pollution Control Board expires on 27-03-2018, 
the Government have decided to extend the tenure of Justice 
T. Sudanthiram, Present Chairman, Appellate Authority of 
Tamil Nadu Pollution Control Board till 18-02-2019 coterminous 
with the term of Appellate Authority of Tamil Nadu Pollution 
Control Board.
(By Order of the Governor) 
 
Md. NASIMUDDIN,
 
Principal Secretary to Government.
