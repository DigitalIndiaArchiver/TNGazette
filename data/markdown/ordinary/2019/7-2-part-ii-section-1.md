No. II(1)/AG/1/2019.—
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 65 of the Tamil Nadu Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) Act, 
1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 1989), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Schedule 
of the said Act.
Amendments
In the said Schedule, in column (2),- 
	
(1) against the entry "Ill. Oil Seeds", in column (1), the existing item "5. Coconut in all forms except Tender Coconut" 
shall be omitted. 
	
(2) against the entry "XV. Miscellaneous", in column (1) after the item 4. Silk Cotton in all forms, the following item 
shall be added, namely:- 
"5. Coconut in all forms except Tender Coconut".
