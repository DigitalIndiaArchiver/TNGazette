2. In the Government Order ﬁ rst read above, orders 
have been issued, among other things, that in future, the 
Co-operative Housing Society should undertake consignment 
scheme only with the prior approval of the Government. 
3. The Registrar of Co-operative Societies (Housing) 
in his letter second read above has stated that, the South 
Chennai Co-operative Housing Society has submitted a house 
site scheme at Nedunkundram Village, Chengalpet Taluk, 
Kancheepuram District on Consignment basis at an extent 
of 28.20 acres and furnished the following in this regard:- 
1. The CMDA has accorded sanction for layout vide 
Letter No. L1/14228/2019, dated 13/03/2020. The proposed 
layout has 716 Number of plots including 219 plots meant 
for Economically Weaker Sections (EWS) and 2 shop sites. 
II. The Additional Government Pleader of Madras 
High Court, in his legal opinion, has stated that 
Thiru J. Vijay Surana is the power holder of the whole 
land for these properties. 
Ill. The copies of documents, Government Pleader opinion 
about the marketable title to the property, Encumbrance 
Certiﬁ cate as mentioned in the Government Pleader Opinion, 
Copy of the CMDA approved plan along with proceedings 
therein and the copy of the Gift Deed for having handed 
over the Road portion and park areas earmarked in the 
approved layout to the local body. 
IV. The Board of Management of the society in its 
meeting held on 30.12.2019 vide Resolution No.1 has 
resolved to request the Government through Registrar of 
Co-operative Societies (Housing) to accord necessary 
permission to implement the scheme with stamp duty 
exemption. 
4. The Registrar of Co-operative Societies (Housing) has 
further informed that as per G.O.Ms.No. 2179, Agriculture 
(Co-operation) Department, dated 29-06-1966, the Co-operative 
Societies are exempted from payment of Stamp Duty. 
5. The Registrar of Co-operative Societies(Housing), has 
therefore, requested the Government to accord permission 
to process and implement the house site scheme at 
Nedunkundram Village, Chengalpet Taluk, Kancheepuram 
District on Consignment basis at an extent of 28.20 acres 
with reference to the existing guideline for the implementation 
of the layout "under consignment scheme" with certain 
conditions. 
6. The Government after careful consideration, accept the 
proposal of the Registrar of Co-operative Societies (Housing) 
and accord approval, as a special case, to implement the 
consignment scheme at Nedunkundram Village, Chengalpet 
Taluk, Kancheepuram District through the South Chennai 
Co-operative Housing Society as per G.O.D. No.16, 
Housing and Urban Development Department, dated 
11-01-2010 with stamp duty exemption under consignment 
basis subject to the condition that no more such exemption 
shall be considered as routine course and with the following 
conditions:- 
a. The scheme should be implemented on consignment 
basis.
b. The society should get the approval in the next 
general body for the implementation of the scheme. 
c. Purchase price and Selling price on consignment 
basis of the proposed site are to be ﬁ xed by a "Pricing 
Committee" consisting of Regional Deputy Registrar (Housing) 
Chennai as Chairman, President of the society, Secretary 
of the Society, Supervisor/Co-operative Sub-Registrar and 
Co-operative Sub-Registrar of the O/o. the Deputy Registrar 
(Housing) Chennai Region as members. 
d. The society shall advertise in the leading dailies 
approved by the Director of information and Public Information 
for assessing the demand of the scheme. 
e. The Society should complete booking/Purchasing the 
plots from the landowner within 15 months. However this 
scheme should be completed within 18 months in total. If 
the scheme could not be completed within the stipulated 
time and if required necessary proposal should be submitted 
well in advance seeking permission from the Government 
for the continuance of the scheme. Unsold plots in this 
scheme should be returned to the land owner. 
f. Up to date Encumbrance Certiﬁ cate should be obtained 
and ascertained that there is no pending encumbrance. 
g. 
This scheme shall be executed only with the 
advance/payments received from the members. Society 
funds should not be utilized, under any circumstances. 
7. This order issues with the concurrence of Finance 
Department vide its U.O.No. 9688/Fin(Hg&UD)/2021, dated 
22-02-2021.
