APPOINTMENT OF THIRU JUSTICE M. YUSUF EQBAL, JUDGE OF THE JHARKHAND HIGH COURT
AS CHIEF JUSTICE OF THE MADRAS HIGH COURT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 509, Public (Special-A), 10th  June 2010,
Vaikasi 27, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No. I/PUSP/7(a)/2010.
The following Notification of the Government of India, Ministry of Law and Justice (Department of Justice),
Jaisalmer House, 26, Man Singh Road, New Delhi-110 011, dated 10th June 2010 is republished:—
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (1) of Article 217 of the Constitution of India, the President is
pleased to appoint Shri Justice M. Yusuf Eqbal, Judge of the Jharkhand High Court, to be the Chief Justice of the
Madras High Court with effect from the date he assumes charge of his office.
