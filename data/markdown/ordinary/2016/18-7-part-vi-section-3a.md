TAMIL NADU WAQF BOARD, CHENNAI
Taking over of the Saidani Bi Magbira Waqf, Uttathur, Trichy District Under the Direct Management.
(Rc. No. 2830/16/B5/Trichy.)
No.VI-3(a)/55/2016.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the Provisions under Section 65 of the Waqf Act 1995 (as amended), the
Tamil Nadu Waqf Board in its resolution in item No. 56/16, Rc.No. 2830/16/B5/TRICHY, dated 02-03-2016 has ordered to
take over the management of Saidani Bi Magbira Waqf, Uttathur, Trichy District under the direct control of the Tamil Nadu
Waqf Board for a Period of one year from 02-03-2016 and appointed the Superintendent of Waqf, Trichy as its Executive
Officer.
