NotificationS  by  government
public department
(Law and Order-F)
appointment of commission of inquiry to inquire into causes and circumstances 
leading to the law and order disturbances which look place on 23rd january 
2017 at Chennai, madurai, coimbatore and other parts of the state following the 
agitation for holding of jallikattu under the commissionS of inquiry act.
Amendment to Notification
[G.O. Ms. No. 165, Public (Law and Order-F), 28th February 2019,Maasi 16, Vilambi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2050.]
No. II(2)/PULO/205(e)/2019.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Commissions of Inquiry Act, 1952 
(Central Act LX of 1952), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Public (Law 
and Order-F) Department Notification No.ll(2)/PULO/76(f)/2017, published at pages 1-2 of Part ll—Section 2 of the 
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 31st January 2017, as subsequently amended.
Amendment
In the said Notification, in paragraph 4, for the expression “twenty five months”, the expression “twenty eight months and 
two weeks”  shall be substituted.
	
P. SENTHILKUMAR,
