SCHOOL EDUCATION DEPARTMENT
AMENDMENTS TO THE ADHOC RULES RELATING TO THE PUBLIC LIBRARIES DEPARTMENT
POST OF DIRECTOR OF PUBLIC LIBRARIES IN TAMIL NADU EDUCATIONAL SERVICE.
[G.O. (2D) No. 73, School Education (PL1), 27th December 2017, Margazhi 12,
Hevilambi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2048.]
No. SRO B-83(b)/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor
of Tamil Nadu  hereby makes the following amendments to the Rules published with the Education Department
Notification SRO No. B-90-74 at Pages 101-102 of Part III—Section 1(b) of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette,
dated the 13th March 1974, as subsequently amended:-
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules,-
(1) in rule 4, for sub-rule (a), the following sub-rule shall be substituted, namely:-
“(a) Age.-No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post by direct recruitment unless he has
completed 45 years of age on the first day of July of the year in which the selection for appointment is made
or notification is issued, as the case may be.”;
(2) rule 5 shall be omitted.
PRADEEP YADAV,
Principal Secretary to Government.
