No. II(2)/HO/801/2017.—Thiru P.Thirumalai Nambi,
Special Sub-Inspector of Police, Gangaikondan Police
Station, Tirunelveli City, of the  Tamil Nadu Police
Subordinate Service has been awarded the Tamil Nadu
Chief Minister’s Police Medal for Gallantry, in recognition of his
brave Act on 6-11-2016 for securing  the  absconding accused
Ramesh @ Ramaiah belonging to Kondanagaram, Suthamalli
Police Station limit, concerned in Thevarkulam  P.S.Cr.No.78/
2016 who involved in various murder, robbery, theft and
snatching cases registered in Tirunelveli district for the last 10
years. In the melee, the Special Sub-Inspector has sustained
severe injuries on his left thigh due to the attack of the said
accused with iron rod.  Due to the murderous attack of the
accused the Special Sub-Inspector underwent critical care for
one month in the hospital and he is unable to perform his
routine duty and has to go for treatment for some more months.
Thiru P.Thirumalai Nambi, Special Sub-Inspector of Police,
Gangaikondan Police Station, Tirunelveli City, is eligible for
a Cash Prize of Rs. 5,00,000/- (Rupees five lakhs only) as
per rules.
