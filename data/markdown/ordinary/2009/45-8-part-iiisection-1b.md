Amendments to Ad hoc Rules relating to Temporary post of Tractor Cleaner for Tamil Nadu Agricultural
Engineering Subordinate Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 230, Agriculture (AA3), 20th October 2009]
No. SRO B-71/2009.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Ad hoc Rules published with the Agriculture
Department Notification No. 80 of 1950, dated the 28th April 1950 at page 1252 of Part 1 of the Fort St. George Gazette,
dated the 9th May 1950:—
1.
The amendment 1(a) (5); hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on 17-9-69.
2.
The amendments 2(b); 6(a); 6(c); 6(e); shall be deemed to have come into force on 9-11-77.
3.
The amendments 2(a); 6(b); 6(d); 6(f); 6(g); shall be deemed to have come into force on 1-2-81.
4.
The amendments 3; 4(2); 7; shall be deemed to have come into force on 25-7-86.
5.
The amendments 1(b); shall be deemed to have come into force on 8-1-90.
6.
The amendments 2(c); 4(1); shall come into force on 20th October 2009.
AMENDMENTS
(1) In the said Rules, in Rule 1,—
(a) for the expression “Cleaners of tractors, Lorries and Jeeps” the expression “Cleaners” shall be substituted;
(b) for the expression category 6 of the “Madras Inferior Service” the expression “Tamil Nadu Agricultural Engineering
Subordinate Service” shall be substituted;
(2) In Rule 2,—
(a) the expressions “District Agricultural Officer concerned,” or, and “as the case may be” shall be omitted;
(b) for the expression “the Assistant Agricultural Engineer” the expression “the Assistant Executive Engineer
(Agricultural Engineering)” shall be substituted;
(c) for the expression “the Assistant Executive Engineer (Agricultural Engineering)” the expression “Executive
Engineer (Agriculture Engineering)” shall be substituted.
(3) In Rule 3, after the expression “Direct Recruitment” the expression “or by recruitment by transfer from the post
of Mazdoor in Tamil Nadu Basic Service in Agricultural Engineering Department,” shall be added;.
(4) for Rule 4, the following rule shall be substituted, namely:—
“4 No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post by the methods specified in column (1) of the Table
below, unless the possess the qualifications specified in column (2) thereof:—
(1)
(2)
(1) By direct recruitment
(i) Must have passed VIII Standard.
(ii) Must possess good physique.
(iii) Must possess general knowledge of work connected with cleaning;
greasing and oiling any Machinery, and
(iv) Must possess a license to drive light Motor Vehicle issued by
a competent authority.
(2) By recruitment by transfer
(i) Must have passed VI standard and should know to read;
(ii) Must possess good physique and knowledge of Agricultural
