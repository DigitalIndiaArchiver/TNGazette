No. VI(1)/171(a-1)/2024.
Government has accorded Revised Administrative Sanction in G.O.(Ms).No. 171 Public works (N1) Department dated 
13.07.2020, to an extent of 120.99.61 hectares of land to be acquired in 33 villages in Mettur and Sankari divisions of 
Salem District for the purpose of Diversion of flood surplus water from Mettur Dam to the dry tanks in Sarabanga Basin 
in Salem District by Lift irrigation project. According to the provision of section 11(1) of Right to Fair Compensation and 
Transparency in Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Act, 2013 notification has also been published on 
24.09.2020 for extent an 117.34.96 hectares.
Whereas it appears that the appropriate Government has decided that the land in Survey Number 177/15B and 19 
others an extent of 1.75.68 hectares in Avadathur, Surapalli, Pottaneri and Nangavalli villages in Mettur Taluk and land in 
Survey Number 2/1A1 and 52/5 an extent of 0.11.25 hectares in Kasuvareddipatty and Mallikuttai villages in Omalur Taluk 
of Salem District need to be acquired for the above said project. Hence, the notification has been published under Central 
Act, 30/2013, Section 11(1) for the above project. The details of lands under acquisition are as specified in the schedule 
below.
