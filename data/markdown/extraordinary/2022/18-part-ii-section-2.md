In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of section 6 of the Coimbatore City Municipal Corporation Act, 
1981 (Tamil Nadu Act 25 of 1981) read with section 8 of the Kumbakonam City Municipal Corporation Ordinance, 2021 
(Tamil Nadu Ordinance 7 of 2021), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby constitutes the following six Standing Committees for 
the purpose specified in sub-section (1) of Section 6 of the said Tamil Nadu Act 25 of 1981, namely:— 
(1)  Standing Committee on Accounts; 
(2)  Standing Committee on Education; 
(3)  Standing Committee on Public Health; 
(4)  Standing Committee on Taxation and Finance,  
(5)  Standing Committee on Town Planning; and 
(6)  Standing Committee on Works.
SHIV DAS MEENA,
Additional Chief Secretary To Government.
