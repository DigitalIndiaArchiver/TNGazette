No. SRO B-62/2014.–In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the
Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the
Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Medical Subordinate Service (Section 21 in Volume III of the
Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1970).
AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules, in Part-II, under the heading "Branch IV General", in Annexure-II referred
to in rule 7, under Class-III, under the heading "by direct recruitment", against Category 3 Plaster
Technician Grade-II, the following entries shall be substituted, namely:-
"(i) A pass in HSC (Academic) Examination with eligibility for University Course of study.
(ii) Must have passed one year Certificate Course in Orthopaedic Technician conducted at
Tamil Nadu Government Medical Institutions.”
J. RADHAKRISHNAN,
Secretary to Government.
