AGRICULTURE DEPARTMENT
 Amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Agricultural Extension Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 22, Agriculture(AAI), 29th January 2015.]
No. SRO B-20/2015.
In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Agricultural Extension Service
(Section 36  in Volume II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1969):—
2. The Amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force with effect from 26th,  December, 1990.
AMENDMENT
In the said Special Rules, for the expression, "District Office Manual" wherever it occurs the expression
"Tamil Nadu Government Office Manual" shall be substituted.
RAJESH LAKHONI,
Agricultural Production Commissioner
and Secretary to Government.

In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby makes the following rules:-
2. The rules hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on and from the 6th September 2002.
RULES
The General and Special Rules applicable to the holders of the permanent posts in Branch I - Medical of the
Tamil Nadu Medical Service shall apply to the holders of the temporary post of Assistant Medical Officer cum Assistant
Professor in Government College of Yoga and Naturopathy / Government Hospitals and Dispensaries in Indian System
of medicine sanctioned from time to time, subject to the modifications specified in the following rules:-
2. Constitution.- The post shall constitute a separate category in a distinct class in the said service.
3. Appointment.- Appointment to the post shall be made by direct recruitment.
4. Appointing Authority.- The appointing authority to the post of Assistant Medical Officer cum Assistant Professor
is Government.
5. Qualifications.- (a) Age.- No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post by direct recruitment, if he has
completed or will complete thirty five years of age on the 1st day of July of the year, in which the selection for
appointment is made.
(b) Other Qulifications.- No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post unless he possesses the
following qualifications, namely:-

Must have passed a degree or diploma in Naturopathy with not less than four years academic duration
awarded by a recognised University; and
(ii)
Must have registered his name with the Tamil Nadu Board of Indian Medicine, Chennai.
6. Reservation of appointment.- The rule relating to reservation of appointments (General Rule 22) shall apply for
appointment to the post by direct recruitment.
7. Probation.- Every person appointed to the post by direct recruitment shall, from the date on which he joins duty,
be on probation for a total period of two years on duty within a continuous period of three years.
8. Tests.-
(a) Every person appointed to the post shall within the period of his Probation, pass the Account Test for
Executive Officers; and
(b) Every person appointed to the post shall pass the Departmental Test in Tamil Nadu Medical Code within
a period of four years from the date of appointment to the post, failing which increment in the time scale of pay
applicable to the post shall be withheld without cumulative effect till the individual passess the said test.
9. Private Practice.- The holders of the post shall be allowed consulting practice:
Provided that a Assistant Medical Officer cum Assistant Professor appointed to act as an Inspecting Medical
Officer in the Directorate of Indian Medicine and Homeopathy, shall not engage in private practice.
10. Posting and transfers.- Posting and transfer shall be made by the Director of Indian Medicine and Homeopathy.
11. Pay.- There shall be paid to the holders of the post a monthly pay calculated in the scale of
Rs. 8000-275-13500 with effect from 06-09-2002:
Provided that with effect on and from the 1st January 2006 the monthly pay shall be calculated in the
Pay Band of PB 3 Rs. 15600-39,100 with Grade Pay of Rs. 5400/-
12. Savings.- Nothing contained in these rules shall adversely affect the holders of the post on the date of
publication of these rules in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
J. RADHAKRISHNAN,
Secretary to Government.
