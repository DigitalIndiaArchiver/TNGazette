PUBLIC DEPARTMENT

APPOINTMENT OF COMMISSION OF INQUIRY TO INQUIRE INTO CAUSES AND CIRCUMSTANCES LEADING TO 
THE LAW AND ORDER DISTURBANCES WHICH LOOK PLACE ON 23rd JANUARY 2017 AT CHENNAI, MADURAI, 
COIMBATORE AND OTHER PARTS OF THE STATE FOLLOWING THE AGITATION FOR HOLDING OF JALLIKATTU 
UNDER THE COMMISSIONS OF INQUIRY ACT.
AMENDMENT TO NOTIFICATION 
[G.O. Ms. No. 93, Public (Law & Order-F), 30th January 2018, Thai 17, Hevilambi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2049.]
No. II(2)/PULO/100(j)/2018.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Commissions of Inquiry Act, 1952 
(Central Act LX of 1952), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Public 
(Law and Order-F) Department Notiﬁ cation No. II(2)/PULO/76(f)2017, published at Pages 1-2 of Part II—Section 2 of the 
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 31st January 2017, as subsequently amended.
AMENDMENT
In the said Notiﬁ cation, in paragraph 4, for the expression “twelve months” the expression “sixteen months” 
shall be substituted. 
P. SENTHILKUMAR,
Principal Secretary to Government (FAC).
