RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND PANCHAYAT RAJ DEPARTMENT
AMENDMENT TO THE TAMIL NADU PANCHAYATS (FIXATION OF STRENGTH OF MEMBERS AND 
DIVISION OF WARDS) RULES, 1995.
[G.O.Ms No.92, Rural Development and Panchayat Raj (PR-1), 3rd July 2018.]
No.SRO A-36(b)/2018.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 242 of  the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 
of 1994), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Fixation 
of Strength of Members and Division of Wards) Rules, 1995:-
AMENDMENT.
In the said Rules, in rule 3, after sub-rule (3), the following sub-rule shall be added, namely:-
 
“(4) Notwithstanding anything contained in sub-rules (1), (2) and (3), the number of wards in every Village 
Panchayat, Panchayat Union Council and District Panchayat and the number of members to be returned by each 
ward shall be the same irrespective of their population as they exist on the date of commencement of the Tamil Nadu 
Delimitation Commission Act, 2017 (Tamil Nadu Act 23 of 2017), until the Government direct otherwise”.
  
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
   
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
   HANS RAJ VERMA,
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Additional Chief Secretary to Government.
