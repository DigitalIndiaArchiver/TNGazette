In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 22  of the Tamil Nadu Lifts and Escalators 
Act, 1997(Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1997), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the 
Tamil Nadu Lifts and Escalators Rules,1997:-
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖගඛ
In the said rules, -
(1) in rule 2, for sub-rule (e), the following sub-rule shall be substituted, namely:-
 
“(e) Local Authority means - 
 
(i) any Municipal Corporation established under any law for the time being in force; or body of Port Commissioners 
or other authority legally entitled to, or entrusted by the Union or any State Government with the control or management 
of any area or local fund;
 
(ii) any Town Panchayat or Municipal Council constituted under the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act,1920
(Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920); or
 
(iii) any District Panchayat or any Panchayat Union Council or any Village Panchayat constituted under the 
Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act,1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994);”
(2) in rule 3, in sub-rule (1), in clause (b),-
 
(1) in sub-clause (xvii), for the expression “Urban Local Body”, the expression  “Local Authority” 
shall be substituted; 
 
(2) in sub-clause (xviii), in item (a), for the expression “5 landings”, the expression  “upto ﬁ ve landings” 
shall be substituted;

(3) in sub-clause (xix), for the expression “Urban Local Body”, the expression “Local Authority” shall be substituted;
(3) in rule 4, in sub-rule (1), in clause (c), in sub-clause (i), for the expression “5 landings”, the expression “upto 
ﬁ ve landings” shall be substituted;
(4) in rule 5, in sub-rule (1) - 
 
(1) in clause (a), in sub-clause (i), for the expression “5 landings”, the expression “upto ﬁ ve landings” shall be 
substituted;
 
(2) in clause (b), for the expression “Urban Local Body”, the expression  “Local Authority ” shall be substituted;
(5) in rule 13, in sub-rule (4), -
 
(1) for the expression “one year”, the expression  “two years” shall be substituted;
 
(2) for the expression “every year”, the expression “on its expiry” shall be substituted;
(6) in the Annexure-I, in para 3, for the expression “a year’’, the expression “two years’’ shall be substituted;
(7) in the Annexure-II, in para 3, for the expression “a year’’, the expression “two years’’ shall be substituted;
(8) in Form A, in the tabular column, for the expression “Urban Local Body”   in column (2) against Serial No.3(b) 
in column (1) thereof, the expression, “Local Authority” shall be substituted; 
(9) in Form G, under the heading “Enclosure”, in Serial Number (5), for the expression “Urban Local Body”, the 
expression  “Local Authority” shall be substituted; 
(10) in Form M, in the heading, for the expression “ESCALATORS”, the expression “LIFTS”  shall be substituted.
