GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD
No. 1, Jaffar Syrang Street, Vallal Seethakathi Nagar, Chennai-600 001.
Extension of the period of the Direct Management of Dadasha Makkan, Perambur, Chennai-600 012.
(Rc. No.619/B1/99/Chennai.)
No. VI-3(a)/262/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred and as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995, the Tamil
Nadu Wakf Board in its Proceedings in item No. 89/07 in Rc. No. 619/C2/99/Chennai, dated 28th February 2008 has
ordered to take over the Management of Dadasha Makkan Wakf, Perambur, Chennai-600 012 under the Direct Control
of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of 6 months from the date of the Pronouncement of Board’s order on
27th May 2008 and appointed Executive Officer of the Direct Management of Wakfs at Chennai, Kancheepuram,Tiruvellore
Districts  as its Executive Officer.
And as per the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board resolution in item No. 7/09 in Rc. No. 619/B1/99/Chennai, dated
26th February 2009, the Board has extended the period of Direct Management upto 25th August 2009.
As the period of the Direct Management was lapsed on 25th August 2009 and due to administrative reasons, the
Hon'ble Chairman, Tamil Nadu Wakf Board has ordered to extend the period of the Direct Management of the Dadasha
Makkan Wakf, Perambur, Chennai-12 for further period of one year from 26th August 2009 and the Tamil Nadu Wakf
Board, in its resolution in item No. 133/09 in Rc. No. 619/99/B1/Chennai, dated 24th September 2009 has ratified the
action of the Hon'ble Chairman, Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for extending the period the Direct Management of the above
Wakf for further period of one year 26-08-2009 to 25-08-2010.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
26th October 2009.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
Extension of the period of the Direct Management of Hazarath Syed Hussain Sha Khaderi Dargha and
Pallivasal alias Dimmasamy Dargha and Pallivasal, Sembium, Perambur, Chennai-600 011.
(Rc. No.1040/B1/05/Chennai.)
No. VI-3(a)/263/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred and as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995, and as
per the Judgement in O.A. No. 3/2006, dated 20th December 2007, in the City Civil Court, Chennai, the Tamil Nadu
Wakf Board in its resolution in item No. 80/08 in Rc. No. 1040/05/C2/Chennai, dated 28th May 2008 has ordered to
take over the Management of Hazarath Syed Hussain Sha Khaderi Mosque and Dargha alias Dimmasamy Dargha and
Mosque, Sembium, Perambur, Chennai-600 011 under the Direct Control of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period
of six months from the date of the Board’s order and appointed the Superintendent of Wakfs, Chennai Zone as its
Executive Officer.
Further the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in item No. 257/08, in Rc. No. 1040/05/B1/Chennai, dated
26th February 2009 has extended the period of the Direct Management of the above Wakfs upto 25th August 2009.
As the period of the Direct Management of the above wakfs were lapsed on 25th August 2009, the Tamil Nadu
Wakf Board in its resolution in item No. 135/09 in Rc. No. 1040/05/B1/Chennai, dated 24th September 2009 has
extended the period of the Direct Management of the Hazarath Syed Hussain Sha Khaderi Dargha and Pallivasal alias
Dimmasamy Dargha and Pallivasal, Sembium, Perambur, Chennai-600 011 for further period of Six months for
26-08-2009 to 25-02-2010.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
26th October 2009.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
