FINANCE DEPARTMENT
Amendment to Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Treasuries and Accounts Subordinate Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 80, Finance (T & A-II), 15th March 2010, Panguni 1, Thiruvalluvar Aandu—2041.]
No. SRO B-32/2010.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Treasuries
and Accounts Subordinate Service (Section 31-A in Part-II, Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1970).
2.
The amendment hereby made shall come into force on the 15th March 2010.
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in rule 4, in the TABLE, for the entry in column (3), against "Category 2" in column (1) and the
corresponding entry in column (2) thereof, the following entry shall be substituted, namely:—
“Must have undergone Sub-Treasury Training for a period of six months".
R. THIAGARAJAN,
Special Secretary to Government.
