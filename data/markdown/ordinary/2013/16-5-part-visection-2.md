TAMIL NADU STATE ELECTION COMMISSION, CHENNAI.
Collectors and Local Authorities
[Elections-Local Bodies-List of Recognised Political Parties and their symbols, list of registered  but unrecognised Political
Parties entitled for preference and lists of free symbols-Notified.]
(S.O.No. 4/2013/TNSEC/ME1)
No. VI(2)/60/2013.
Whereas, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission has issued an Order viz., the Tamil Nadu Local Bodies Election
Symbols (Reservation and Allotment) Order, 2011 to provide for specification, reservation, choice and allotment of Symbols at
elections to local bodies in the State of Tamil Nadu and for matters connected therewith; and
Whereas, it has become expedient to notify the list of recognised Political Parties and the Symbols respectively reserved for
them, the list of registered-unrecognised Political Parties and the lists of free symbols for the use in the local bodies election:
Now, Therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by paragraphs 4 and 6 of the Tamil Nadu Local Bodies Election Symbols
(Reservation and Allotment) Order, 2011 and in supersession of the earlier notifications, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission
hereby notifies,—
(a) in Table 1,
the Recognised Political Parties and the symbols respectively reserved for them;
(b) in Table 1-A,
the list of Registered-unrecognised Political parties.
(c) in Table 2,
the free Symbols for the use in election of Members of Village Panchayats;
(d) in Table 3,
the free Symbols for the use in election of Presidents of Village Panchayats;
(e) in Table 4,
the free Symbols for the use in election of Members of Panchayat Union Councils, Members of Town
Panchayats, Councillors of Municipalities and Councillors of Municipal Corporation; and
(f)
in Table 5,
the free Symbols for the use in election of Members of District Panchayats, Chairman of Town Panchayats/
Municipalities and Mayors of Corporations.
In respect of party based Elections, every independent candidate shall declare in the nomination paper
first filed by him/her or on his/her behalf, his/her choice of three free symbols from the list of free symbols
for that election in Table 4 or Table 5 as the case may be, indicating the order of his/her preference.
Every candidate who is setup by a registered-unrecognised political party shall declare in the nomination
paper first filed by him/her or on his/her behalf, his/her choice of three free symbols collectively from the
list of free Symbols notified in Table 4 and Table 5 for the party based elections.
(By Order of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission)
Chennai-600 106,
B. JOTHI NIRMALA,
1st April 2013.
Secretary,
Tamil Nadu State Election Commission.
