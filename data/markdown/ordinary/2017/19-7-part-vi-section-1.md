JUDICIAL NOTIFICATIONS
ADJOURNMENT OF COURT OF SMALL CAUSES, CHENNAI FOR SUMMER VACATION, 2017
(Dis No. 1820/I7)
No.VI(1)/143/2017.
The Presidency court of Small Causes, Chennai will remain closed for the Summer Vacation of 2017.
The Summer vacation (for the year 2017) of this Court will be from 01-05-2017 to 31-05-2017. The First turn will
be from 01.05.2017 to 16.05.2017 and the Second turn of the vacation will be from 17.5.2017 to 31.05.2017.
The Office of the Court of Small Causes, Chennai will function from  Monday to Wednesday and Friday each
week from 10 A.M to 4.30 P.M and on  Thursday (i.e) court sitting day, from 10 A.M to 5.45 P.M. during the Summer
Vacation 2017.
Tmt. C. Uma Maheswari, IX Judge, Court of Small Cause, Chennai will be the  Vacation Judge for First turn from
01-05-2017 to 16.05.2017
Thiru. G. Vijayakumar, VII Judge, Court of Small Cause, Chennai will be the  Vacation Judge for Second turn from
17-05-2017 to 31-05-2017.
Thiru/Tmt R. Karlmarx, XI Judge, Court of Small Cause, Chennai will be the  Additional Vacation judge for First
turn from 01-05-2017 to 16-05-2017. The Additional  Vacation Judge will deal with Rent Control Original petitions.

Tmt. V. Akila, XVI Judge, Court of Small Cause Chennai will be the Additional  Vacation Judge for second turn
from 17-05-2017 to 31-05-2017, the Additional Vacation  Judge will deal with Rent Control Original petitions.
Thiru N. Kothandaraj, X Judge, Court of small Causes, Chennai will be the  Vacation Officer for the period from
01-05-2017 to 31-05-2017 (First turn and Second turn).
The Filing Section will be functioning on every Wednesdays and the  sitting of the Judges will be on every
Thursdays during the Summer Vacation  2017. The Vacation Judges unless otherwise notified will sit on all
Thursday of  every week during the Vacation, to dispose the emergent petitions. No fresh  plaint petition, no Rent
Control Appeals or other matters will be received  during the period of vacation, unless it is urgent and it is
accompanied by an  application duly supported by an Affidavit showing the urgency.
The Copyist Section of the Court of Small Causes will be kept open during Summer  Vacation between
10.30. A.M to 3.00 P.M for the purpose of receiving copy application  and also for delivering the certified copies, which
were already made ready. Further no fresh certified copy will be made ready during summer vacation.
The Nazir Section will function during summer Vacation and Process  Application will be received between
10.30. A.M to 3.00 P.M on Wednesdays.  Warrants will be issued only on Wednesdays and Thursdays of the
respective  weeks, which were already filed and made ready. Warrants which are being  filed on Wednesday will
be made ready and will be issued only on the next day  (i.e) Thursday. If the Warrants returned for reasons, (i.e.
since Police  protection not available), the same will be re-issued only on next Wednesday  or Thursday, if the
Petitioner/Decree holder wants to execute the Warrant and  makes such endorsement.
The Office of the Registrar will be kept open during summer vacation but the  urgent matters will be received on
Wednesdays during the vacation and such matters will  be posted before the Vacation Judges and the Additional
Vacation Judges for the hearing  on the succeeding Thursday in the open court.
Money will be received in the Treasury Section on Wednesdays and Thursdays of  every week and money will
be paid out only on Thursdays during the Vacation, when the  Vacation Judges are holding courts and on orders made
by the vacation Judges for such payments.

Constitution of Fast Track Courts at Magisterial Level at Coonoor in the Nilgiris District.
(D.No.1197/2017)
No.VI(1)/144/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred under sub section (1) of Section 14 of the  Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973,
(Central Act 2 of 1974), and in supersession of the  previous notifications(s) dated 03.12.2012 issued on the subject,
the Chief Judicial  Magistrate, Udhagamandalam, The Nilgiris District hereby authorizes the Fast Track Court  at
Magisterial Level at Coonoor, which was constituted in G.O.Ms. No. 1320, Home (Cts.I)  Department, dated 11-09-2007,
as amended from time to time, to exercise the jurisdiction over the local limits over which, the Judicial Magistrate,
Coonoor was hitherto exercising  jurisdiction in respect of criminal cases arising under Section 138 of the Negotiable
Act, and  all other Judicial Magistrates exercising jurisdiction within the said local limits shall cease  to have jurisdiction
to deal with the cases arising under Sec. 138 of N.I. Act with effect from  the date of publication of this notification
in the Nilgiris District Gazette.
Udhagamandalam, The Nilgiris,
N. SIVAKUMAR,
20th April 2017.
