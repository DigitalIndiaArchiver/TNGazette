In the Government Order first read above, the Government
have issued orders for the ad-interim attachment of movable
and immovable properties of Messers Jothi Chit Fund,
Nagapattinam District, specified in the Schedule thereto under
section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Protection of Interests of Depositors
(in Financial Establishments) Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 44 of
1997), for default in return of depositors’ money, In the letter
second read above, the Inspector General of Police, Economic
Offences Wing-II, Chennai, has stated that in the particulars of
the property mentioned against Serial No.2 of the Schedule to
the Government Order, the location of the property has been
mentioned as Vazhakarai Village, Thirukuvalai Taluk, Thiruvarur
District. But on verification, the actual location of the attached
property against Serial No.2 of the Schedule lies in Vazakkarai
Village, Nagapattinam Taluk, Nagapattinam District. Hence, the
Additional Director General of Police has requested to issue
necessary 
amendment 
to 
the 
above 
said
Government Order.
2. The Government after careful examination have decided
to accept the proposal of the then Inspector General of Police,
Economic Offences Wing-II, Chennai.
3. Accordingly, in exercise of the powers conferred by
section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Protection of Interests of Depositors
(in Financial Establishments) Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 44 of
1997), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following
amendment to G.O.(Ms.)No.845, Home (Police XIX)
Department, dated 12.12.2011:-
AMENDMENT
In the said Government Order, in the schedule, in the
tabular column under the heading “(b) Details of immovable
properties to be attached:-”, in column (3), against Serial No.2
in column (1) for the expression “Agricultural land 1.33 1/3
cent, at vazakkarai Village, Thirukuvalai Taluk, Thiruvarur
District.”, the expression “Agricultural Land 1.33 1/3 cent at
Vazakkarai Village, Nagapattinam Taluk,Nagapattinam District.”
shall be substituted.
(By order of the Governor.)
