No. VI(2)/121(b-1)/2019.
WHEREAS, in the Tamil Nadu Municipal Laws (Fifth Amendment) Ordinance, 2019 (Tamil Nadu Ordinance 8 of 2019), the 
Government have issued amendment to the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920, Chennai City Municipal Corporation 
Act, 1919, Madurai City Municipal Corporation Act, 1971, Coimbatore City Municipal Corporation Act, 1981 relating to change 
in the manner of election of Mayor of Corporations, Chairman of the Municipalities and Town Panchayats from Direct to 
Indirect method.
NOW, THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred under paragraph 3 of the Tamil Nadu Local Bodies Election 
Symbols (Reservation and Allotment) Order, 2019, the following amendments are issued to S.O.No.15/2019/TNSEC/ME1 
dated 24.9.2019 published vide Notiﬁ cation No.370 at page 1 of Part VI  Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette 
Extraordinary dated 27th September 2019:-
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖගඛ
In the said Notiﬁ cation, under the paragraph:- 
 
“Sl.Nos.3 and 4 and expressions thereof shall be deleted”.
