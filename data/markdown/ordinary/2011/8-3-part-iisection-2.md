2. Dearness Allowance (for industrial workers).вҖ”

Chennai City Consumer Price Index for the year 2000. (i.e.
475 points with base 1982=100) and for every raise of one
point over and above 475 points, an increase of Rs. 4.35
(Rupees four and thirty five paise only) per point per month
shall be paid as dearness allowance.
(ii) The dearness allowance shall be calculated every
year on 1st April on the basis of the average of the Consumer
Price Index for the preceding 12 months, i.e., from January
to December.

the date of publication of this Notification in the Tamil Nadu
Government Gazette based on the average Chennai City
Consumer Price Index for the previous year.
(iv) To arrive at daily rates of wages the monthly
wages shall be divided by 26.
(v) To arrive at monthly rates of wages the daily rates
of wages shall be multiplied by 30.

distinction in the payment of wages shall be made between
men and women employees.
(vii) wherever the existing wages are higher than the
minimum wages fixed herein, the same shall be continued to
be paid.
