NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
HIGHER EDUCATION DEPARTMENT
DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF THE TAMIL NADU STATE COUNCIL
FOR TECHNICAL EDUCATION ACT, 2010.
[G.O. Ms. No. 153, Higher Education (J2), 17th June 2010,
Aani 3, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No. II(2)/HE/379(b)/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (3) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu State
Council for Technical Education Act, 2010 (Tamil Nadu Act 4 of 2010), the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the 17th June 2010 as the date on which the said Act
shall come into force.
K. GANESAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
