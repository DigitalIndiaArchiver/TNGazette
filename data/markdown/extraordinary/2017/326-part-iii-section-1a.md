RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND PANCHAYAT RAJ DEPARTMENT
AMENDMENT TO THE TAMIL NADU PANCHAYATS (DISCLOSURE OF ASSETS AND LIABILITIES BY
CHAIRMAN, VICE-CHAIRMAN AND MEMBERS OF DISTRICT PANCHAYAT) RULES, 1999.
[G.O. Ms. No. 110, Rural Development and Panchayat Raj (PR-1) 13th October 2017]
No. SRO A-46(a)/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of  Section 242 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994
(Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994), the Governor of  Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Tamil
Nadu Panchayats (Disclosure of Assets and Liabilities by Chairman, Vice-Chairman and Members of District
Panchayat) Rules, 1999:-
AMDEDMENT
In the said Rules, in rule 2, for the expression “member of the family”, the expression “family member” shall
be substituted.
HANS RAJ VERMA,
Additional Chief Secretary to Government.
