TRANSPORT DEPARTMENT
ACQUISITION OF LANDS
[G.O. Ms. No. 45, Transport (E), 7th March 2012.]
No. II(2)/TRA/145(b)/2012.
The Governor of Tamil Nadu, having been satisfied that the Lands specified in the schedule below have to be acquired for
industrial purpose, to wit, for the Madurai. Airport Expansion Scheme and it having already been decided that the entire amount
of compensation to be, awarded for the lands is to be paid out of the lands, controlled or managed by Government. The following
notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 ( Tamil Nadu Act
10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands specified in the schedule below and measuring an extent of
8.25.74 Hectares (or) 20.40 Acres  of land to the same, a little more or less needed for industrial purpose, to wit, for the expansion
of Madurai Airport.
The Plan of the lands are kept in the office of the Special Tahsildar (Land Acquisition), Maurai Airport Expansion Scheme,
Unit-I, Block No. III, Madurai South Taluk, Madurai District and may be inspected at any time during office hours.

Madurai District, Madurai South Taluk, 43. Ayanpappakudi Village.
