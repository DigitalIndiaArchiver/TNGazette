In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the rules issued with the Higher Education
Department Notification No. SRO B-159/97, dated the 17th December 1997 at pages 170 to 171 in
Part III—Section I(b) of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 17th December 1997.
1.
The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on 25th May 2010.
In the said Adhoc Rules,—
(1) for rule 3, the following shall be substituted, namely:-
“3. Appointment.—Appointment to the post shall be made only by direct recruitment”.
(2) in rule 4, in sub-rule (b),
(i) for the expression “A First Class Bachelor’s degree in the branch of  Engineering relating to
the post concerned”, the following expression shall be substituted, namely,–
“A Bachelor’s Degree in the appropriate branch of  Engineering / Technology / Architecture
with not less than sixty percent of marks or equivalent:
Provided that if the candidate has a Master’s Degree in the appropriate branch of Engineering / Technology
/ Architecture, the candidate should possess a first class or equivalent in the appropriate Branch either at
Bachelor’s or Master’s degree level.” ;
