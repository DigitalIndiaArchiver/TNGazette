Election of one member to the Academic Council of Madurai Kamaraj University by the Teachers of PG Colleges
(Ref. No. ME-2/AC/Elec./PG Coll/2013.)
No. VI-3(b)/2/2013.
   It is hereby notified that the following election will be held under Section mentioned hereunder as per the Madurai
Kamaraj University Act, 1965:
   “Election will be held to elect ONE MEMBER to the Academic Council from each Post-Graduate College who is not
the Principal of such College elected by the Teachers of the College including Physical Directors but excluding the Principal,
Tutors, Demonstrators and Physical Instructors from among themeselves under Section 24 (a) Class-II (4) of the Madurai
Kamaraj University Act, 1965. The Principals of the Colleges concerned are requested to hold the election and communicate
the result”.
