No. II(2)/TRA/319(g-1)/2012.
Whereas in exercise of the powers conferred by clause (1)
of Article 258 of the Constitution, the President has
entrusted to the Government of Tamil Nadu in Letter
No. 4(1)/65 Central (II) Food, Agriculture, Community
Development and Co-operation (Agriculture), dated 23rd April
1966, with their consent the functions of the Central
Government under the Land Acquisition Act, 1894 (Central
Act I of 1894) in relation to acquisition of land for the purposes
of the Union in this State and whereas it has already been
decided that the Compensation should be paid by the public
revenues.
Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by
Section 6 of the said Act, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
declares that the lands specified in the Schedule below and
measuring 0.36.00 hectares, be the same a little more or less,
is needed for a public purpose, to wit, for the formation of New
Broad Gauge Railway Line between Needamangalam and
Mannargudi under Sections 3 and 7 of the same Act. The
Revenue Divisional Officer Mannargudi is appointed to perform
the functions of the Collector under the Act and directed to take
order for the acquisition of the said lands. Under sub-sections
(1) and (2) of Section 17 of the Act, the Governor of Tamil Nadu
further directs that the possession of the said lands may be
taken immediately after the publication of the Notice mentioned
in Section 9(1) of the Act. A plan of the land is kept in the office
of the Revenue Divisional Officer, Mannargudi and may be
inspected at any time during office hours.
