Tamil Nadu Fisheries University is constituted with the following members:-

The Vice – Chancellor;
(b)
The Secretary to Government, in-charge of Fisheries;

The Secretary to Government, in-charge of Finance;

The Secretary to Government, in-charge of Law;
(g)
The Director of Fisheries; and
(h)
The Registrar
