No. VI(2)/9(b)/9(b)/2020.
WHEREAS, the Government have bifurcated the Villupuram District into Villupuram and Kallakurichi Districts; the Tirunelveli 
District into Tirunelveli and Tenkasi Districts; the Kancheepuram District into Kancheepuram and Chengalpattu Districts and 
trifurcated the Vellore Districts into Vellore, Tirupathur and Ranipet Districts vide G.O. (Ms).No(s). 425, 429, 431, Revenue 
and Disaster Management  Department, Revenue Administration Wing, [RA1(1)] Section, dated 12-11-2019 respectively; 
NOW, THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred by rule 9 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Municipalities 
and Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006 and as per S.O.No.10/2019/TNSEC/ME1, dated 28-08-2019 published, inter-alia, 
in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary Issue No.350, dated 03-09-2019 at page No.1 of Part VI - Section 2 
vide Notiﬁ cation No.VI(2)/82(a-2)/2019, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission hereby directs the District Election Oﬃ  cers 
of newly constituted districts viz. Kallakurichi, Tenkasi, Chengalpattu, Tirupathur and Ranipet Districts to appoint within their 
respective jurisdictions the Executive Oﬃ  cers of the Town Panchayat or the Commissioner of Municipality  respectively or 
any other oﬃ  cer of the Government as Returning Oﬃ  cer for the conduct of Election to the Town Panchayat or Municipality 
as the case may be, under the said Rules. 
(By Order of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission)
