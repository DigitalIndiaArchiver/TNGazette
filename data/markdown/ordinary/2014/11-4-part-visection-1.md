Variation to the Approved Master Plan for the Mamallapuram Local Planning Area.
(Roc.No.420/2013/MLPA)
No.VI(1)/129/2014.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub section (4) of Section 32 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning
Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972), Government in the order G.O.(2D) No. 11 Housing and Urban Development [UD4(1)]
Department, dated 20-1-2014 and G.O.(2D) No. 14, Housing and Urban Development [UD4(1)] Department, dated 7-2-2014
has permitted to change of land use of survey numbers with condition making the following variation to the Master Plan
for the Mamallapuram Local Planning Area, approved under the said Act and published in the G.O.Ms.No.153,
H&UD [UD4(2)], dated 20-6-2013, Notification No.II (2)/HOU/501/2013, at Page No.481 of Part-II, Section 2 of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated 17th July 2013.
VARIATIONS
In the said Master Plan, in the “LAND USE SCHEDULE” of Village 52, Thaiyur, under the heading.
7. Agriculture Use Zone, the Survey Numbers the expression “1450 to 1455” shall be deleted and 1450 (except
1450/1B, 1F, 1G, 1H, 1I), 1451 (except 1451/17, 18, 19, 20) and 1452 to 1455 shall be substituted.
And under the heading 1. MIXED RESIDENTIAL USE ZONE, the Survey Numbers “1450/1B, 1F, 1G, 1H, 1I; 1451/17,
  18, 19 and 20”  shall be added.
Conditions:
The conditions specified in NOC of the Divisional Engineer Highway’s Chengalpattu has to be fulfilled.
K. SHANMUGAM,
Chengalpattu.
Member-Secretary(In-Charge),
Mamallapuram Local Planning Authority.

Constitution of the Sub-Court at Arakkonam in Vellore District
[Roc. No. 45/A/2010/G/Judn]
No. VI(1)/131/2014.
In exercise of the powers conferred under the first proviso to Section 13 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873
(Central Act III of 1873) the High Court, Madras hereby directs that appeals from the decrees or orders of the District Munsif
Courts, Sholinghur and Arakkonam be preferred in the court of Subordinate Judge, Arakkonam with effect from the date on
which the Subordinate Judge, Arakkonam assumes charge of that court.
High Court, Madras,
P. KALAIYARASAN,
4th March 2014.
Registrar General.
