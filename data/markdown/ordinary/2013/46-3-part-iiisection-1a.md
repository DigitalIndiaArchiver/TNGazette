Amendments to the Tamil Nadu Motor Transport Workers Rules.
[G.O. Ms. No. 258, Labour and Employment (H1), 5th November 2013, Aippasi 19, Vijaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2044.]
No. SRO A-35/2013.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 40 of the Motor Transport Workers Act, 1961
(Central Act 27 of 1961), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu Motor
Transport Workers Rules, 1965, the draft of the same having been previously published, as required by sub-section (1) of
Section 40 of the said Act.
