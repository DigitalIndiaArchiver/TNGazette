ANNEXURE - I
The following Act of Parliament received the assent of the President on the 2nd January, 2023 and is hereby 
republished for general information:-
MINISTRY OF LAW AND JUSTICE 
(Legislative Department)
New Delhi, the 3rd January, 2023 / Pausa 13, 1944 (Saka) 
THE CONSTITUTION (SCHEDULED TRIBES) ORDER (SECOND AMENDMENT) ACT, 2022 
No.1 OF 2023 
An Act further to amend the Constitution (Scheduled Tribes) Order, 1950 to modify the list of Scheduled Tribes 
in the State of Tamil Nadu. 
Be it enacted by Parliament in the seventy-third year of the Republic of India as follows:- 
 
1.	 This Act may be called the Constitution (Scheduled Tribes) Order (Second Amendment)  
Act, 2022. 
2.	 In the Constitution (Scheduled Tribes) Order, 1950, in the Schedule, in Part XIV.- 
Tamil Nadu, after entry 36, the following entry shall be inserted, namely :- 
"37. Narikoravan, Kurivikkaran. ".
