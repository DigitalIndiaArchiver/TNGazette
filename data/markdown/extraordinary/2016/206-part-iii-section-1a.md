AMENDMENTS TO THE TAMIL NADU PANCHAYATS (ELECTIONS) RULES, 1995
[G.O.Ms.No. 101, Rural Development and Panchayat Raj (PR-I), 15th September 2016.]
No. SRO A-16(d)/2016.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 242 read with Sections 11, 20, 32 and 57 of the Tamil Nadu
Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following
amendments to the Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Elections) Rules, 1995:-
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules,-
(1) in rule 94, to sub-rule (5), the following proviso shall be added namely:-
“Provided further that to workout the quorum, the President of the Village Panchayat shall not be taken into
account and the total number of ward members and those present alone shall be considered.” ; and
(2) after rule 94, the following rule shall be inserted, namely:-
“94-A. Electors.--The ward members and the President of the Village Panchayat shall be the voters in the
election of the Vice-President of that Village Panchayat.”.
