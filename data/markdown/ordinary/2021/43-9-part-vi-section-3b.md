A Nominee by the Members of the Senate to the Search Committee for the Appointment of the Vice-Chancellor
(MSU/M&E/VCPANEL/SENATE/ELECT/2021)
No.VI-3(b)/25/2021.
It is hereby notiﬁ ed, by direction, that an election will be held for nominating a person by the Senate of the 
University to serve on the Search Committee of three persons who are to recommend a panel of three names to His 
Excellency Governor-Chancellor for the appointment of the Vice-Chancellor under Section 11(1)/&(2) of the Manonmaniam 
Sundaranar University Act, 1990 as amended in Tamil Nadu Universities Law (Amendment) ordinances 2017 published 
in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette dated 27th May 2017.
The election will be held in accordance with rules and procedures as per the Statutes of this University.
Each member of the Senate shall have a right to nominate not more than one person who is not a member of 
any of the authorities of the University to the Search Committee.
Every nomination shall be in writing signed by the proposer who shall be a member of the Senate and Seconded 
in writing by another member of the Senate called the seconder in the prescribed form. The nomination paper and 
declaration paper may be downloaded from this University website. No nomination shall be valid unless it contains the 
signatures of both the proposer and the seconder and the nomination form shall also be signed by the person proposed 
expressing his/her consent to be nominated. Every nomination must be presented before the Registrar either by the 
proposer or by the seconder or by the person proposed, before 3.00 p.m. on Friday, the 12th November 2021.
The scrutiny of nomination will take place 3.30 p.m. on the same day at the oﬃ  ce of the Registrar, Manonmaniam 
Sundaranar University, Abishekapatti, Tirunelveli-627 012. The Proposer or the Seconder or the proposed nominees are 
entitled to be present at the time of the scrutiny.
The following is the Programme for election:
1.
Date of issue of Election Notiﬁ cation
Wednesday, the 27th October 2021
2.
Date of commencement of nomination
Wednesday, the 27th October 2021

Last date and hour for receipt of Nominations.

3.00 p.m.
4.

Friday, the 12th November 2021 at 3.30. p.m.
5.
Publication of list of valid Nomination of candidates
Friday, the 12th November 2021 at 4.30 p.m
6.
Last day and hour by which the candidates may 
withdraw their candidature by notice in writing 
Friday, the 19th November 2021 upto 3.30 p.m.
7.
Publication of list of Final valid Nomination of candidates
Friday, the 19th November 2021 upto 4.30 p.m.
8.
Last date of despatch of Intimation papers to the 
Electors if there is a poll
Tuesday, the 23rd November 2021
9.
Day and hour of polling at the University Buildings
Friday, the 3rd December 2021 from 11.00 am 
to 1.00 p.m.
10.
Day and hour of Scrutiny, counting of votes and 
declaration of result will take place at the University 
Buildings, Abishekapatti, Tirunelveli.
Friday, the 3rd December 2021 at 3.00 p.m.
If the number of persons validly nominated and who have not withdrawn is more than one, an election will be held 
on Friday, the 3rd December 2021 from 11.00 a.m to 1.00 p.m.
