Taking over the Direct Management of Hazarath Murthuzawali Masthan Dargah, Arcot, Vellore District.
(Lr. Rc. No. 7452/B4/02/VLR)
No. VI-3(a)/120/2010.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995, the Tamil Nadu
Wakf Board in its proceedings in item No. 150/2007 in Rc. No. 7452/B4/02/VLR, dated 8th December 2009 has ordered
to take over the Management of Hazarath Murthuzawali Masthan Dargah, Arcot, Vellore District under the direct control
of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board from the date of pronouncement of Board’s Order (i.e. from 25th August 2010) and
to appoint the Inspector of Wakfs, Vellore as its Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
27th August 2010.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
Taking over the Direct Management of Ramzan Sha Thaikkal Wakf, Udayarkudi, Kattumannar Koil Taluk,
Cuddalore District.
[Rc. No. 7494/00/B5/Cuddalore]
No. VI-3(a)/121/2010.
The Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its order in Item No. 127/10, Rc.No. 7494/00/B5/Cuddalore, dated 7th July 2010
has resolved to bring the Management of Ramzan Sha Thaikkal Wakf, Udayarkudi, Kattumannar Koil Taluk,
Cuddalore District under Direct Management for a period of Six Months from the date of Publication of Notification in
the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette and Superintendent of Wakfs, Cuddalore has been appointed as Executive Officer
of the above Wakf under Section 65 (1) of Wakf Act, 1995.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
6th September 2010.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
