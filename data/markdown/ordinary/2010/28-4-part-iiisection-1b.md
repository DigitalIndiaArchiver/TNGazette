ANIMAL HUSBANDRY, DAIRYING AND FISHERIES DEPARTMENT
Amendment to Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Animal Husbandry Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 94, Animal Husbandry, Dairying and Fisheries (AH7), 16th June 2010.]
No. SRO B-51/2010.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Animal
Husbandry Service (Section 2 in Volume-II of Tamil Nadu Service Manual 1969).
2. The amendment hereby made shall come into force on the 16th June of 2010.
AMENDMENT
In the said Special Rules, in rule 6, in the tabular columns there under, under Class III, in column (3) for the
entry in Item (b) against "Category-2 (Research Officer)" in column (1) thereof, the following entry shall be substituted
namely:—
"A degree of Ph.D., or M.Sc., or M.V.P.H. or M.V.Sc., in Micro-Biology including Dairy Micro-Biology, Bacteriology,
Virology, Pathology, Parasitology, Preventive  Medicine, Bio-Chemistry, Pharmacology, Animal Bio-Technology,
Animal Genetics, with Dairy Micro-Biology forming part of Dairy Science or Veterinary Physiology with Micro-Biology
as ancillary".
C. MUTHUKUMARASWAMY,
Secretary to Government.
