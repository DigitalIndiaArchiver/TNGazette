[The Chennai Metropolitan Area Groundwater (Regulation) Act, 1987 – Authorisation of Revenue Divisional Officer
as Competent Authority in respect of Revenue Villages of Kancheepuram and Thiruvallur Districts - Orders – Issued.]
The following Government Order is published:—
READ:
1.
Act amendment issued in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, No.156,
dated 11-8-2014.
2.
From the Managing Director, Chennai Metropolitan Water Supply and Sewerage Board,
Letter No.CMWSSB/O&M/JG/048/2014, dated 28-8-2014.
Order:  No. 73, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (MA-1) Department  , 30th April 2015.
In the reference 1st cited, as per the orders of the High Court of Madras, dated 27-08-2014, in W.P. No.27912/2013
the Chennai Metropolitan Area Groundwater (Regulation) Act, 1987 was amended and enacted in the Tamil Nadu
Legislative Assembly on 11-8-2014.
2. According to clause (b) of sub-section (1) of Section 8 of the Chennai Metropolitan Area Groundwater
(Regulation) Act, 1987, in relation to the villages specified in the schedule thereto, the Collector of Chengalpattu or
any officer not below the rank of Tahsildar, authorized by the Government in this behalf shall be the Competent
Authority for the purposes of the said Act. The Government has now decided to authorize the Revenue Divisional
Officer, Kancheepuram and Thiruvallur Districts as competent authority in relation to the villages specified in the
Schedule to the said Act for administrative reasons.

3. Accordingly, the Government hereby authorise the concerned Revenue Divisional Officer of Kancheepuram
and Thiruvallur Districts, as the case may be, as the Competent Authority for the purpose of the said Act in relation
to revenue villages in Kancheepuram and Thiruvallur Districts specified in the schedule to the said Act.
