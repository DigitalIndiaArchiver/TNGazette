Under sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Registration Act, 1908 (Central Act XVI of 1908), the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby with effect on and from 22.07.2021 alters the limits of the Registration 
Sub-District of Kaveripakkam in the Registration District of Arakkonam by transferring the village Pallur 
which forms part of the Registration Sub-District of Kaveripakkam in the Registration District of Arakkonam 
to the Registration Sub-District of Nemili in the Registration District of Arakkonam.
