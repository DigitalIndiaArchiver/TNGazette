Notifications under Tamil Nadu State Housing
Board Act.
Exemption of the Scheme for the Area Development for
certain Residential Plots and Commercial Plots at
Sholinganallur Neighbourhood Scheme under the Act.
[G.O. Ms. No. 257, Housing and Urban Development
(HB4(1)), 12th December 2008.]
No. II(2)/HOU/1/2009.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by Section 69 of the Tamil Nadu State Housing
Board Act, 1961 (Tamil Nadu Act, 17 of 1961) the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby exempts the scheme for the area
development for thirty four residential plots and one block of
commercial plot in Group Housing Site-II in Survey No. 235
part at Sholinganallur Neighbourhood Scheme, Phase-III, at
a cost of Rs. 36.63 lakhs (Rupees thirty six lakhs and sixty
three thousands only) submitted by the Tamil Nadu Housing
Board from the provisions of Section 49 except clause (b) of
sub-section (2) of that section of the said Act.
Sanction of the Scheme for the Area Development for
certain Residential Plots and Commercial Plot at
Sholinganallur Neighbourhood Scheme under the Act.
[G.O. Ms. No. 257, Housing and Urban Development
(HB4(1)), 12th December 2008.]
No. II(2)/HOU/2/2009.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by sub-section (2) of section 53 of the Tamil Nadu
State Housing Board Act, 1961 (Tamil Nadu Act 17 of 1961),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby sanctions the scheme
for the area development for thirty four residential plots and
one block of commercial plot in Group Housing Site-II in
Survey No. 235 part at Sholinganallur Neighbourhood
Scheme, Phase-III, at a cost of Rs. 36.63 lakhs (Rupees
thirty six lakhs and sixty three thousands only) submitted by
the Tamil Nadu Housing Board.
Declaration of the Scheme for the Area Development for
certain Residential Plots and Commercial Plot at
Sholinganallur Neighbourhood Scheme under the Act.
[G.O. Ms. No. 257, Housing and Urban Development
(HB4(1)), 12th December 2008.]
No. II(2)/HOU/3/2009.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by section 152 of the Tamil Nadu State Housing
Board Act, 1961 (Tamil Nadu Act 17 of 1961) the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby declares that in the areas in respect
of which the scheme for the area development for thirty four
residential plots and one block of commercial plot in Group
Housing site-II in Survey No. 235 part at Sholinganallur
Nieghbourhood Scheme, Phase-III, at a cost of Rs. 36.63
lakhs (Rupees thirty six lakhs and sixty three thousands
only) is in force for the period during which the said scheme
remains in force the powers and functions exercisable by
the President, Sholinganallur Town Panchayat, under the
Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act
V of 1920) and in respect of receiving plans, sanctioning
plans, regulating the construction of buildings, laying of roads,
laying of underground drainage, water mains electricity
connections and providing all amenities to the owners of
buildings and all the powers connected therewith shall be
exercised by the Tamil Nadu Housing Board.
