[G.O. Rt. No. 905, Commercial Taxes and Registration (J1),
17th October 2012.]
No.II(2)/CTR/731/2012.—In exercise of the powers conferred
by clause (b) of sub-section (1) of Section 9 of the Indian Stamp
Act, 1899 (Central Act II of 1899), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby provides for the consolidation of duty of Rs.3,50,000/-
(Rupees three lakhs and fifty thousand only) chargeable under
the said Act in respect of issue 35,000 Fully Convertible
Debentures for a face value of Rs.10,000 each to Messrs
Infrastructure Leasing and Financial Services Limited by the IL
and FS Tamil Nadu Power Company Limited.
[G.O. Rt. No. 906, Commercial Taxes and Registration (J1),
17th October 2012.]
No.II(2)/CTR/732/2012.—In exercise of the powers conferrted
by clause (b) of sub-section (1) of Section 9 of the Indian Stamp
Act, 1899 (Central Act II of 1899), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby provides for the consolidation of duty of Rs.1,00,000/-
(Rupees one lakh only) chargeable under the said Act in respect
of issue 10,000 Fully Convertible Debentures for a face value of
Rs.10,000 each to Messrs Infrastructure Leasing and Financial
Services Limited by the IL and FS Tamil Nadu Power Company
Limited.
