NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
PUBLIC DEPARTMENT
(Elections)
DECLARATION OF WEDNESDAY 4th DECEMBER 2013 AS A PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN CONNECTION WITH THE
BYE-ELECTION TO TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY FROM 83. YERCAUD (ST) ASSEMBLY
CONSTITUENCY IN TAMIL NADU.
[G.O. Ms. No. 1273, Public (Elections-I), 6th November, 2013.]
No. II(1)/PUE/21(b)/2013.
Under the Explanation to Section 25 of the Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881 (Central Act XXVI of 1881),
read with the Notification of the Government of India, Ministry of Home Affairs, No. 20-25-26, Public-I, dated the
8th June, 1957, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby declares Wednesday, the 4th December, 2013, the date on
which the poll for the Bye-election to Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly from 83. Yercaud (ST) Assembly
Constituency will take place, to be a public holiday for the areas comprised in the above Assembly Constituency
lying in Salem District.
2.
Further, all employees working in Salem District but registered as voters in 83. Yercaud (ST)
Constituency, shall also be given a paid holiday on 4th December 2013.
3.
All Government offices including industrial establishments of the Government and Government
Controlled bodies and all the Educational Institutions throughout the area comprised in 83. Yercaud (ST)
Assembly Constituency lying in Salem District will remain closed on 4th December 2013.
SHEELA BALAKRISHNAN,
Chief Secretary.
