themselves who are members of the Academic Council are to be elected in accordance with the system of proportional 
representation by  means of the single transferable Vote. The Election schedule is given below. 
2.  The persons getting elected will hold ofﬁ ce as a Member of the Syndicate for a period of Three Years from the date 
of declaration of the results.
