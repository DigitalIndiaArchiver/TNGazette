WITHDRAWAL NOTIFICATION
In exercise of the powers delegated to the Collectors in G.O.Ms. No. 70, Local Administration dated 12th January 1960 the
Collector of Cuddalore District as per Tamil Nadu Panchayat Act, 1994 (T.N. Act XXI of 1994) direct that from and after the date
of publication of this notification in the District Gazette and Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, the land vested to the undermentioned
village and Panchayat  the undermentioned Taluk of Cuddalore District on the Schedule and measuring the extent noted below be
the same a little more or less shall be excluded from the operation of the said Act.
SCHEDULE
Cuddalore District, Bhuvanagiri Taluk, Valamadevi keezhpathi Panchayat, Valamadevi keezhpathi Village.
