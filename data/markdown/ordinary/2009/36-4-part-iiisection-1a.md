 LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT DEPARTMENT
Amendment to Tamil Nadu Standards of Weights and Measures (Enforcement) Rules, 1989.
[G.O. 2(D) No. 75, Labour and Employment (G1), 10th August 2009.]
No. SRO A-29/2009.—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-sections (1) and (2) of Section 72 of the Standards
of Weights and Measures (Enforcement) Act, 1985 (Central Act 54 of 1985), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, after consultation
with the Central Government, hereby makes the following amendment to the Tamil Nadu Standards of Weights and
Measures (Enforcement) Rules, 1989, the draft of the same having been previously published as required under
sub-section (4) of Section 72 of the said Act.
2. The amendment hereby made shall come into force with effect on and from the date of publication of this Notification
in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in Schedule XII, under the heading “1. Weights”, after the sub-heading “(e) Carat Weights” and the
entries thereunder, the following sub-heading and entries shall be inserted, namely:—
