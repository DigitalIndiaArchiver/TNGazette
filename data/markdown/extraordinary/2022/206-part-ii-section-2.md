RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND PANCHAYAT RAJ DEPARTMENT
 REMOVAL OF CHAIRMAN OF  VIRUDHACHALAM PANCHAYAT UNION COUNCIL,
CUDDALORE DISTRICT UNDER THE TAMIL NADU PANCHAYATS ACT, 1994.

WHEREAS, a written notice of the intention to make a motion expressing want of confidence in Thiru K. Chelladurai, 
Chairman, Virudhachalam Panchayat Union Council, Cuddalore district, signed by fifteen members, out of the sanctioned 
strength of nineteen members of the said Council, together with a copy of the motion proposed to be made and a written 
statement of charges against the said Chairman have been delivered to the Revenue Divisional Officer, Virudhachalam, 
Cuddalore district in person by two members as per sub-section (2) of section 212 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 
1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994);
AND WHEREAS, the Revenue Divisional Officer, Virudhachalam, Cuddalore district caused a copy of the statement 
of charges along with a copy of the notice of motion to be delivered, to the said Chairman Thiru K.Chelladurai and he 
submitted his reply to the charges;
AND WHEREAS, a motion expressing want of confidence in Thiru K. Chelladurai, Chairman, Virudhachalam Panchayat 
Union Council, Cuddalore district had been carried with a support of sixteen members out of the sanctioned strength 
of nineteen members of the said Panchayat Union Council in the meeting convened by the Revenue Divisional Officer, 
Virudhachalam, Cuddalore district on the 05th March 2022 after following the procedures specified in the said section 
212 and forwarded the same to Government;
NOW THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (13) of section 212 of the Tamil Nadu 
Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby removes Thiru K.Chelladurai, 
Chairman, Virudhachalam Panchayat Union Council, Cuddalore district from the office of the Chairman of the said 
Panchayat Union Council with immediate effect.
	
P. AMUDHA,
	
Principal Secretary to Government.
