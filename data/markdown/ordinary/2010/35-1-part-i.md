No. I/PUSP/18/2010.—The following Notification of the Government of India,
Department of Personnel and Training, New Delhi, dated 5th August 2010
is republished:—
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-rule (2) of Rule 5 of the Indian
Administrative Service (Cadre) Rules, 1954  and  with the concurrence of the
Governments of Tamil Nadu and Orissa, the Central Government hereby transfers
Ms. Guha Poonam Tapaskumar, IAS (T.N: 2008) from Tamil Nadu cadre to Orissa
cadre on the ground of marriage to Dr. Satyajit Naik, IPS (OR: 2008).
[No. 13017/19/2010-AIS(I).]
R.K. GUPTA,
Under Secretary to Government of India.
