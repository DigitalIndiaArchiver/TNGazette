No. SRO B-17/2017.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Rules published with the Home Department
Notification No. SRO B-217/85, published at pages 229 to 251 of Part III—Section 1(b) of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette,
dated the 5th June 1985, as subsequently amended:-
2. The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force with effect on and from the
11th February 2010.
AMENDMENT
In the said  Rules, in rule 3, in the TABLE, in column (2), against the entries “21.Electrician Grade-II” and
“22.Batteryman” in column (i) to item (iii), the following proviso shall be added, namely:-
“Provided that nothing contained in these rules shaII adversely affect any person who was already promoted as
Electrician Grade-II or Batteryman from the erstwhile post of Helper on or before the 11th February 2010 and they shall
be entitled for promotion as Fitter Grade-I, fixation of pay on par with their junior and restoration of their seniority in the
post of Fitter Grade-II”.
NIRANJAN MARDI,
Principal Secretary to Government.

Amendments to the Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission Regulations.
[G.O. Ms. No. 41, Personnel and Administrative Reforms (M), 9th March 2017,  ñ£C 25, ¶¡ºA,

No. SRO B-18/2017.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Article 318 of the Constitution of India, the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission Regulations, 1954
(Volume-I of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1987).
2. The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 2nd September 2011.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Regulations,-
(1) in regulation 13, in clause (1), in the first and second provisos, for the expression “Accountant”, the expression
“Assistant Section Officer-cum-Programmer” shall be substituted;
(2) in ANNEXURE-III, under the heading “STAFF OF THE COMMISSION”, in the tabular column, for the expression
“Accountant” in column (1), and the figure “10” in the column (2) thereof, the expression “Assistant Section Officer-cum-
Programmer” and the figure “26” shall, respectively, be substituted;
(3) in ANNEXURE V,-
(A) in clause 1,-
(i) sub-clause (a) shall be renumbered as sub-clause (a) (i);
(ii) in sub-clause (a) (i) as so renumbered, the expression “Accountants” shall be omitted;
(iii) in sub-clause (a) (i) as so amended, the following sub-clause shall be added namely:-
“(ii) (a) the post of Assistant Section Officer-cum-Programmer shall constitute as a distinct category and the Secretary
shall be the authority competent to make appointment, in respect of the said post;

(b) Appointment to the post of Assistant Section Officer-cum-Programmer shall be made by direct recruitment or
by promotion from the category of Data Entry Operator in the Commission.
(c) (i) No person shall be eligible for appointment to the category of Assistant Section Officer-cum-Programmer
by direct recruitment, if he has completed or will complete the age of 30 years on the 1st day of July of the year in which
the selection for appointment is made.
(ii) No person shall be appointed to the category of Assistant Section Officer-cum-Programmer by the method
of direct recruitment or by promotion unless he possesses the qualification of a Master Degree in Computer Application
or Master Degree in Science (Information Technology/Computer Science).
(d) The principle of reservation of appointments as provided in Section 27 of the Tamil Nadu Government Servants
(Conditions of Service) Act, 2016 (Tamil Nadu Act 14 of 2016) shall apply to the category of Assistant Section Officer-
cum-Programmer to be filled by direct recruitment”;
(B) in clause 2,-
(1) in sub-clause (a)(i),-
(a) the expression “/Accountant” occurring in two places shall be omitted;
(b) before the proviso, the following paragraph shall be inserted, namely:-
“Every person appointed to the post of Assistant Section Officer-cum-Programmer by direct recruitment shall, from
the date on which he joins duty, be on probation for a total period of two years on duty within a continuous period of
three years”;
(2) in sub-clause (a)(ii),-
(a) for the expression “/Accountant” in the main provision, the expression ”Assistant Section Officer-cum-Programmer”
shall be substituted;
(b) in the proviso, the expression “/Accountant” shall be omitted;
(3) in sub-clause (b),-
(a) the expression “/Accountant” shall be omitted;
(b) the following paragraphs shall be added at the end, namely:-
“Every person appointed to the post of Assistant Section Officer-cum-Programmer by direct recruitment shall complete
the foundational training at Civil Service Training Institute, Bhavanisagar and shall pass the test conducted at the end
of training within the period of probation:
Every person appointed to the post of Assistant Section Officer-cum-Programmer shall pass the Tamil Nadu Government
Office Manual Test and the Departmental Test in Tamil Nadu Services Manual Volume-I within the period of his probation.
If a person appointed to the post of Assistant Section Officer-cum-Programmer does not pass the prescribed Tests within
the period of his probation, he shall not be allowed to draw his second and subsequent increments until he passes
the said Tests. Such ineligibility to draw increments shall not have the effect of postponing his future increments after
he has passed the Tests”;
(4) in sub-clause (c), for the expression “Accountants”, the expression "Assistant Section Officer-cum-Programmers"
shall be substituted;
(C) in clause 3, the following paragraph shall be added, namely:-
“The service rules applicable to the post of Assistant Section Officer in the Commission, shall, except or otherwise
provided in these Regulations, be applicable to the holders of the post of Assistant Section Officer-cum-Programmer in
the Commission”;
(D) in clause 8, for the expression “Assistant promoted to officiate as Accountant”, the expression “Data Entry Operator
promoted to officiate as Assistant Section Officer-cum-Programmer” shall be substituted.
S. SWARNA,
Secretary to Government.
