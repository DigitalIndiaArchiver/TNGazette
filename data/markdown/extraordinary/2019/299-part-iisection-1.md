NOTIFICATION REGARDING CONDUCT OF PRE-TEST CENSUS OF INDIA, 2021
(Letter No. 27392/L.O/2019-1, 15th July 2019) 
No. II(1)/PULO/11(a)/2019—
The following Order of the Gazette of India Ministry of Home affairs (Office of the Registrar General, India) New Delhi, the 
25th June, 2019 is republished:—
S.O. 2221(E).— In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 17A of the Census Act, 1948 (37 of 1948), the 
Central Government hereby extends the provisions of the said Act, for conduct of pre-test of Census of India, 2021. 
The pre-test shall be conducted from 12th August 2019 to 30th September, 2019 in all the States and Union Territories.
