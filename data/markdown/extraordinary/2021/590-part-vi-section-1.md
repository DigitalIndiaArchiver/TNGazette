NOTICE UNDER SECTION 15(1) OF TAMIL NADU HIGHWAYS ACT, 2001(TN ACT 34 OF 2002) READ WITH THE 
TAMIL NADU LAND ACQUISITION LAWS (REVIVAL OF OPERATION, AMENDMENT AND VALIDATION) ACT, 2019.
No. VI(1)/358(a-1)/2021. 
 The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that the lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below are acquired 
under Chennai – Kanniyakumari Industrial Corridor Project for Highways purpose for the formation of Bypass in of 
SH-86 Omalur - Sankari - Tiruchengode - Paramathi Road from KM 38/900 to 39/100 & 39/800 to 40/250 & 41/400 
to 42/380 KM at Kasthuripatti (Part-2) (Sankari By-Pass) Village in Sankari Taluk, Salem District, and it having already 
been decided that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands to be paid out of the funds controlled 
and managed by the Divisional Engineer(H), Salem (CKICP), Tamil Nadu after having considering the cause shown by 
the owner or other persons having interest on such lands as the case may be do hereby publish the following notice 
is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (TN Act 34 of 2002).
 NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) read 
with the Tamil Nadu Land Acquisition Laws (Revival of Operation, Amendment and Validation) Act 2019, the Governor 
of Tamil Nadu hereby notiﬁ ed that the Dry lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below and measuring to an extent of 33 
sq.mtr., are acquired in Kasthuripatti (Part-2) (Sankari By-Pass) Village in Sankari Taluk, Salem District, to the same, a 
little more or less is needed for Chennai-Kanniyakumari Industrial corridor Project for State Highways purpose for the 
formation of Bypass in SH-86 Omalur - Sankari - Tiruchengode - Paramathi Road from KM 38/900 to 39/100 & 39/800 
to 40/250 & 41/400 to 42/380 KM. 
 The plan of the lands under acquisition are kept in the office of the Special District Revenue officer 
(Land Acquisition), Chennai - Kanniyakumari Industrial Corridor Project, Salem and oﬃ  ce of the Special Tahsildar 
(Land Acquisition), Chennai – Kanniyakumari Industrial Corridor Project, Sankari Unit at Omalur Taluk and may be 
inspected at any time during the oﬃ  ce hours.
