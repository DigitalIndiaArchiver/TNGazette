NOTIFICATIONS BY THE ELECTION COMMISSION OF INDIA.
APPOINTMENT OF DATES FOR VARIOUS STAGES OF ELECTIONS AND FIXATION OF HOURS OF POLL
FOR ALL THE ASSEMBLY CONSTITUENCIES IN THE STATE OF TAMIL NADU.
No. SRO G-11/2011.
The following Notification of the Election Commission of India, Ashoka Road, New Delhi-110 001, dated the 19th March, 2011
[28 Phalguna, 1932 (Saka)] is published:—
No. 464/TN-LA/2011.—WHEREAS, the Governor of the State of Tamil Nadu has, by Notification issued under
sub-section (2) of Section 15 of the Representation of the People Act, 1951 (43 of 1951) and published in the official Gazette of
the State on the 19th March, 2011, been pleased to call upon all Assembly Constituencies in the State, to elect members to the
Legislative Assembly of the State, in accordance with the provisions of the said Act and the rules and orders made  thereunder;
Now, therefore, in pursuance of Sections 30 and 56 of the said Act, the Election Commission of India hereby—
(A) appoints, with respect to the said election in each of the constituency—
(a) the 26th March, 2011 (Saturday), as the last date for making nominations;
(b) the 28th March, 2011 (Monday), as the date for the scrutiny of nominations;
(c) the 30th March, 2011 (Wednesday), as the last date for the withdrawal of candidatures;
(d) the  13th April, 2011 (Wednesday), as the date on which a poll shall, if necessary, be taken; and
(e) the 16th May, 2011 (Monday), as the date before which the election shall be completed; and
(B) fixes the hours from 08.00 A.M. to 5.00 P.M., as the hours during which the poll shall, if necessary, be taken on the
date specified above, for the election.
