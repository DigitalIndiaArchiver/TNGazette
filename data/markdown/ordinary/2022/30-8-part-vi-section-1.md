Constitution of District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court at Kodavasal in Tiruvarur District
[Roc. No.1347/A/2014/G/Judn.(Phase-II)]
No. VI(1)/322/2022.
In exercise  of the powers conferred by Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III 
of 1873) the High Court, Madras, hereby ﬁ xes Kodavasal in Tiruvarur District as the place at which the District 
Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Kodavasal, shall be located.
NOTIFICATION-II
[Roc. No.1347/A/2014/G/Judn.(Phase-II)]
No. VI(1)/323/2022.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 11 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 
1873) the High Court, Madras, hereby directs that in Tiruvarur District, the District Munsif Court, Tiruvarur and District 
Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Nannilam shall cease to have local jurisdiction over the entire Kodavasal Taluk 
and the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Kodavasal shall have and exercise local jurisdiction over the 
entire Taluk of Kodavasal with eﬀ ect from the date on which the Distirct Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate, Kodavasal 
assumes charge of that Court.
NOTIFICATION-III
[Roc. No.1347/A/2014/G/Judn.(Phase-II)]
No. VI(1)/324/2022.
The High Court, Madras hereby directs and notiﬁ es that the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate, Kodavasal 
in Tiruvarur District shall exercise all the powers conferred on a District Munsif under Section 12 of the Tamil Nadu 
Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873) as amended up-to-date in regard to original suits and proceedings not 
otherwise exempted from his cognizance of which the amount or value of the subject matter does not exceed One 
Lakh Rupees within the local limits of his jurisdiction with eﬀ ect from the date on which the District Munsif-cum-Judicial 
Magistrate, Kodavasal assumes charge of that Court.
NOTIFICATION-IV
[Roc. No.1347/A/2014/G/Judn.(Phase-II)]
No. VI(1)/325/2022.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 28 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 
1873) the High Court, Madras, hereby directs that the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate, Kodavasal, shall have and 
exercise jurisdiction of a Court of Small Causes under the Provincial Small Cause Courts Act, 1887 for the trial of suits 
cognizable by a Court of Small Causes upto the pecuniary limits of Rs.5000/- (Rupees ﬁ ve thousand only) with eﬀ ect 
from the date on which the District Munsif-cum-judicial Magistrate Court, Kodavasal, assumes charge of that Court.
High Court, Madras,

Conferment of Magisterial Powers
(Roc.No. 74215/2022/B7)
No. VI(1)/326/2022.
No. 144/2022.—In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 13 of the Criminal Procedure Code 1973 
(Central Act 2 of 1974), the High Court hereby appoints the following (i) 4 Deputy Tahsildars in Namakkal  District 
and (ii) 8 Deputy Tahsildars in Tiruvallur District,  to undergo Magisterial Training for a period of 120 days, from 
the date of assumption of charge and confers upon them the ordinary powers conferable under this Code on a Judicial 
Magistrate to try such cases which were earlier triable and dealt with by an erstwhile Judicial Second Class Magistrate.
