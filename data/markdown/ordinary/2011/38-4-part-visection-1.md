JUDICIAL NOTIFICATION
Proposal for appointment of Notary for the area comprised in Thoothukudi District.
(D.No. 7633)
No. VI(1)/370/2011.
Whereas Thirumathi M. Lizzy Joy Antonyammal, B.Sc., L.L.B., Advocate, residing at No.15 A, Ratchanyapuram,
Thoothukudi-1, Thoothukudi District, has applied to this court to appoint her as a Notary for the Thoothukudi limits area
comprised in  Thoothukudi District.  Notice is hereby given that those having objections may file their objections before
the Principal District Court, Thoothukudi within Fourteen (14) days from the date of publication of this notice.
Principal District Court,
J. PRABHUDASS,
Thoothukudi,
