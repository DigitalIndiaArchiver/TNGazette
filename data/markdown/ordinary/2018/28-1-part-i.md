Service - Deputation From Puducherry Judicial Service to
Tamil Nadu State Judicial Service - Orders- Issued.]
The following Government Order is Published:-
[G.O. (D) No. 199, Public (Special-A), 20th June 2018,
Aani 6,Vilambi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2049.]
READ:
From the Registrar General, High Court, Madras, 
D.O. Letter No. 8/2018-Con.B2, dated 05-06-2018.
Order: G.O. (D) No. 199, Public (Special-A), 20th June 2018.
The Government have considered the recommendation 
of the High Court, Madras to depute Thiru G.Sendil Kumar, 
District Judge of Puducherry Judicial Service, now functioning 
as Presiding Ofﬁ cer, Industrial Tribunal-cum-Labour Court, 
Puducherry to the Tamil Nadu State Judicial Service for 
being posted as I Additional District Judge, Cuddalore in the 
existing vacancy. 
2. The Government accepted the above recommendation 
of the High Court, Madras and accordingly direct that Thiru 
G.Sendil Kumar, District Judge of Puducherry Judicial Service, 
now functioning as Presiding Ofﬁ cer, Industrial Tribunal-cum-
Labour Court, Puducherry on deputation to the Tamil Nadu 
State Judicial Service be posted as I Additional District Judge, 
Cuddalore in the existing vacancy.
(By Order of the Governor) 
 
GIRIJA VAIDYANATHAN, 
 
Chief Secretary.
