MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION AND WATER SUPPLY DEPARTMENT
DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF TAMIL NADU MUNICIPAL LAWS (SECOND AMENDMENT) ACT 2012
(TAMIL NADU ACT 16 OF 2012)
[G.O. Ms. No. 21, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (M.A.1), 1st February 2013,
Thai 19, Nandhana, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2044.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/140(d)/2013.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Municipal Laws
(Second Amendment) Act, 2012,  (Tamil Nadu Act 16 of 2012), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby  appoints  the
1st  day of February 2013, as the date on which the said Act shall come into force.
C.V. SANKAR,
    Principal Secretary to Government.
