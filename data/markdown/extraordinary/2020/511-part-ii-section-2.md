3. The Transport Department has stated that both State Transport Undertakings and Private transport buses need to 
operate with 100% seating capacity without standees due to expected increase in number of commuters including buses 
operated/engaged by Industries to Transport their employees and schools and colleges engaging buses to transport students. 
The Transport Department has also stated that State Transport Undertakings may be permitted to increase the number of 
bus services based on demand, by following the Standard Operating Procedures in GO.Ms.No.262, Revenue and Disaster 
Management Department, dated 31.5.2020.
4. Now, therefore the Government hereby order to permit the State Transport Undertakings/ Private bus operators including 
buses operated/ engaged by Industries to Transport their employees and schools and colleges engaging buses to transport 
students, to operate buses with 100% seating capacity and also permit the State Transport Undertakings to increase the 
number of bus services based on demand by following the Standard Operating Procedures(SOPs) as per G.O.Ms.No.262, 
Revenue and Disaster Management Department, dated 31.5.2020 except restriction of occupancy to 60% of the seating capacity.
