GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
Grant of No Objection Certificate
[Rc. No. E2(1)/414/25013/2012.]
FORM-A2
[Notice under  Rule 35A(4) of Tamil Nadu Cinemas (Regulation) Rules, 1957.]
No.VI(1)/143/2012.
Whereas Tr. Gopu Rajagopal, Authorized signatory of Classic Mall Development Company Private Limited, No. 142,
Velachery Main Road, Velachery, Chennai-42 has applied for No Objection Certificate for Construction of the Permanent
Multiplex Complex containing 11 Screens at Door No. 142, Velachery Main Road, Velachery, Chennai-42 in
T.S. Nos. 6/2 and 6/3, Block No. 20,  Velachery Village, Guindy-Mambalam Taluk, Chennai District, Chennai-42,
belonging to  Messrs. Classic Mall Development Company, Chennai-42.
1. Notice is hereby given that any person having any objection to the grant of “No Objection Certificate” above said
site on the grounds of non-compliance by the applicant with any of the provisions of the Tamil Nadu Cinemas
(Regulation) Act 1955 (Tamil Nadu Act IX of 1955) or with any of the Rules there under should file his objection in
writing with the Licensing Authority (Commissioner of Police) within 15 days of publication of this Notice.
2. Any objection filed after the period shall be liable to be summarily rejected.
TRIPATHI,
Egmore, Chennai-600 008,
Commissioner of Police,
6th April 2012.
Chennai Police.

JUDICIAL NOTIFICATIONS
Arrangements made by Sessions Judge, Chennai Division, Chennai for attending
Urgent Criminal matters during Summer Recess, 2012.
(Dis. No. 4365/2012/E1.)
(Roc. No. 142/2012/E1.)
No.VI(1)/146/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 10(3) of the  Criminal Procedure Code, 1974,  the Sessions
Judge, Chennai Division makes the following arrangements for attending Urgent Criminal Work during Summer Recess,
2012 (1-5-2012 to 3-6-2012).
2. Thiru K. Dakshinamoorthy, V Additional Judge, proceeding on the transfer as I Additional Judge, City Civil Court,
Chennai wiil be incharge of Criminal Work during Summer Recess from 1-5-2012 to 17-05-2012.
3. Tmt. M.D. Sumathi, who is posted as II Additional Judge, City Civil Court, Chennai will be incharge of Criminal
Work during Summer Recess from 18-5-2012 to 3-6-2012.
4. The Vacation Sessions Judges will hold Courts on Wednesdays in every week during the vacation for the
disposal of urgent Criminal matters.
5. The Chief Administrative Officer, City Civil and Sessions Court, Chennai will be the Vacation Officer during
Summer Recess, 2012. In his absence, Sherishtadar-I will be the Vacation Officer.
PROCEDURE FOR THE RECEIPT AND DISPOSAL OF URGENT CRIMINAL MATTERS DURING
SUMMER RECESS, 2012.
6. All urgent applications, Crimical Appeals, etc., shall be received only on Tuesdays upto 2.00 P.M. except on
1-5-2012 which is a holiday. Instead 1-5-2012, last working day before vacation is the filing day. The matters will be
placed for orders before the Vacation Sessions Judge only on Wednesdays during Vacation at 10.30 A.M. Applications
and other matters shall not be presented at the residence of the Vacation Sessions Judge.
7. In all Bail Applications, including the applications for Anticipatory Bail, notice shall be given to the City Public
Prosecutor before filing the papers in Court and necessary endorsements shall be obtained from the City Public
Prosecutor.
8. Clear and authenticated typed copies of the Judgments should be filed whenever certified copies are not easily
decipherable.
9. In all cases wherein notices are to be issued sufficient number of  copies of memorandum of grounds, petitions
and affidavits shall be filed for service on the City Public Prosecutor as well as the respondents concerned.
Summer Recess, 2012.
(Dis. No. 4365/2012/E1.)
(Roc. No. 143/2012/E1.)
No.VI(1)/147/2012.
1. Notice is hereby given that the City Civil and Sessions Court, Chennai will be closed for the Summer Recess,
2012 for the period from 1-5-2012 to 3-6-2012 (Both days inclusive).
2. Thiru. S. Natarajan, B.Sc., B.L., XIII Assistant Judge, proceeding on transfer as X Assistant Judge, City Civil
Court, Chennai w.e.f. 30-4-2012 A.N.  will be the Vacation Judge from 1-5-2012 to 17-5-2012.
3. Thiru. P. Saravanan, B.L., XVII Assistant Judge, City Civil Court, Chennai will be the Vacation Judge from
18-5-2012 to 3-6-2012.
4. The Vacation Judges will sit in Court on Thursdays in each week during Vacation to dispose of only urgent
matters.
