No. VI(1)/393/2010.

Town and Country Planning, Chennai hereby confirms the following variation of conversion of part of residential use
into commercial use in T.S. Nos. 158A/1pt and 159A/1pt to the Approved Sanganoor Detailed Development Plan
No. 2 of Coimbatore Local Planning Area. The said Notification was published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette
(No. 34), Part VI—Section 1, Page No. 260-261, dated 31st August 2005.

same are hereby confirmed and ordered as below:—
