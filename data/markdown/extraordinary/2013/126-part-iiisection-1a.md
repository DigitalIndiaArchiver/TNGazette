In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-sections (1) and (2) of Section 180 of the Tamil Nadu
Co-operative Societies Act, 1983 (Tamil Nadu Act 30 of 1983), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes
the following amendment to the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Rules, 1988:—
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in rule 145, in THE TABLE, for the expression “Deputy Registrar of Co-operative
Societies” in column (2) against item 25 and the entry relating thereto in column (1) thereof, the expression
“Co-operative Sub-Registrar” shall be substituted.
M.P. NIRMALA,
Secretary to Government.
