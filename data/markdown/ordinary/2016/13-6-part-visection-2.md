EXPLANATORY STATEMENT
        The Commission has been mandated to fix Renewable Energy Purchase Obligation (RPO) under section 86(1)(e)
of the Electricity Act, 2003. Accordingly, the Commission notified the RPO for FY 2011-12 in its RPO Regulations.
Distribution Licensee, Open Access Consumers and Captive Consumers are the obligated entities under the above
Regulation. A stay was granted by the Hon’ble Madras High Court on a batch of writ petitions filed by the open access and
captive consumers, restraining the operation of the Regulation in so far as it seeks to include the open access and captive
consumers. Now, the Distribution Licensee  has to fulfil the RPO as specified in the Regulations. The RPO fixed by the
Commission for FY 2011-12 continues to be applicable for further period of FY 2012-13, 2013-14 & 2014-15 as per the
Commission’s RPO Regulations.
