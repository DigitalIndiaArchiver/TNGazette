CONSTITUTION OF DISTRICT MUNSIF'S COURT AT KATPADI IN VELLORE DISTRICT
UNDER THE TAMIL NADU CIVIL COURTS ACT.
NOTIFICATION - I
[G.O. Ms. No. 234, Home (Courts-III), 18th March 2009.]
No. II(2)/HO/136(a-1)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 4 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu, after consultation with the High Court, Madras, hereby fixes the number of District Munsif to be
appointed to the District  Munsif's Court, Katpadi in Vellore District as One with effect on and from the date on which the
District Munsif, Katpadi assumes charge of that Court.
NOTIFICATION - II
[G.O. Ms. No. 234, Home (Courts-III), 18th March 2009.]
No. II(2)/HO/136(a-2)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 4 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby alters the number of District Munsifs to be appointed for Vellore District as fourteen with
effect on and from the date on which the District Munsif, Katpadi assumes charge of that Court.
NOTIFICATION - III
[G.O. Ms. No. 234, Home (Courts-III), 18th March 2009.]
No. II(2)/HO/136(a-3)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 10 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby varies the local limits of the jurisdiction of the Subordinate Judge's Court in Vellore
District to the effect that the said Subordinate Judge's Court, Vellore, shall have and exercise original jurisdiction over the
District Munsif's Court, Katpadi in Vellore District in addition to its existing  jurisdiction with effect on and from the date
on which the District Munisif, Katpadi assumes charge of that Court.
S. MALATHI,
Principal Secretary to Government.
