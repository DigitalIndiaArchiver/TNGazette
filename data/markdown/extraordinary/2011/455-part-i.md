CERTAIN MINISTERS RELIEVED FROM THEIR MINISTERSHIP OF COUNCIL OF MINISTERS.
[G.O. Ms. No. 1168, Public (Special-B), 9th December 2011, Karthigai 23, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No. I/PUSP/24(a-1)/2011.
I, K. Rosaiah, Governor of Tamil Nadu accept the recommendation of the Chief Minister that the following Ministers
may be relieved from the Council of Ministers and direct that they be relieved of their duties and responsibilities as Ministers with
immediate effect:—
1. Thiru M. Paranjothi, Minister for Hindu Religious and Charitable Endowments, Law, Courts and Prisons.
2. Thirumathi Selvi Ramajayam, Minister for Social Welfare.
K. ROSAIAH,
Governor of Tamil Nadu.
ALLOCATION OF SUBJECTS AMONG MINISTERS.
[G.O. Ms. No. 1169, Public (Special-B), 9th December 2011, Karthigai 23, Thiruvalluvar Aandu--2042.]
No. I/PUSP/24(a-2)/2011.
Under Clause (3) of Article 166 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that the portfolios
"Tamil Official Language and Tamil Culture, Law, Courts, Prisons, Personnel and Administrative Reforms and Prevention of
Corruption" hitherto held by Thiru M. Paranjothi be allotted to Thiru C.Ve. Shanmugam, in addition to the portfolios of School
Education, Archaeology, Sports and Youth Welfare already held by him. Thiru C.Ve. Shanmugam be designated as Minister for
School Education, Sports and Youth Welfare, Law, Courts and Prisons.
DEBENDRANATH  SARANGI,
Chief Secretary.
