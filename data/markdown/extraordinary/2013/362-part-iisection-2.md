TOURISM, CULTURE AND RELIGIOUS ENDOWMENTS DEPARTMENT
DATE OF COMMENCEMENT OF THE TAMIL NADU HINDU RELIGIOUS AND CHARITABLE ENDOWMENTS
(AMENDMENT) ACT, 2013 (TAMIL NADU ACT 26 OF 2013).
[G.O. Ms. No. 314, Tourism, Culture and Religious Endowments (RE 4.2), 5th December 2013,
 Karthigai 19, Vijaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2044.]
No. II(2)/TCRE/913(f)/2013.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Hindu Religious and Charitable
Endowments (Amendment) Act, 2013 (Tamil Nadu Act 26 of 2013), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the
9th day of December 2013 as the date on which the said Act shall come into force.
