NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND PANCHAYAT RAJ DEPARTMENT
DRAFT AMENDMENTS TO THE TAMIL NADU STATE EMPLOYMENT GUARANTEE FUND RULES, 2008
[G.O. Ms. No. 108, Rural Development and Panchayat Raj (CGS.1), 3rd December 2010.]
AMENDMENTS TO NOTIFICATON
No. SRO A-35(b)/2010.
Whereas certain draft amendments to the Tamil Nadu State Employment Guarantee Fund Rules, 2008 proposed to be
made in exercise of the powers conferred by Section 32 of the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee
Act, 2005 (Central Act 42 of 2005),  has been published with the Rural Development and Panchayat Raj (CGS.1) Department
Notification No. SRO A-34(b)/2010, dated the 25th October, 2010 in  Part III – Section 1(a) of the Tamil Nadu Government
Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 25th October, 2010. In the said notification, objections and suggestions from the persons
likely to be affected thereby has been invited before the expiry of a period of fifteen days from the date of publication of
the said notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette;
And whereas Gazette copies of the said draft amendments to the said Rules in which they were published were made
available to the public on the  25th October, 2010.
And whereas no objection and suggestion has been received from any person in this regard.
Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 32 of the Mahatma Gandhi National
Rural Employment Guarantee Act, 2005 (Central Act 42 of 2005), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following
amendments to the Tamil Nadu State Employment Guarantee Fund Rules, 2008.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules,—
(1)
for the expressions “Tamil Nadu State Employment Guarantee Fund” wherever it occurs, the expression
“State Employment Guarantee Fund – Tamil Nadu” shall be substituted;
(2)
in rule 6, the expression  “including the expenses of the State Employment Guarantee Council and the
Society” shall be omitted.
K. ALLAUDIN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
