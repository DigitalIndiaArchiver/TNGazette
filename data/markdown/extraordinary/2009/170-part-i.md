RE-ALLOCATION OF SUBJECTS AMONG MINISTERS.
[G.O. Ms. No. 602, Public (Special-B), 12th  July 2009,
Aani 28, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. I/PUSP/10(a)/2009.
Under Clause (3) of Article 166 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby directs that the subjects Public Works, Irrigation including Minor Irrigation
and Programme Works hitherto held by Thiru Duraimurugan, Minister for Public Works and
Law be re-allocated to Chief Minister in addition to the subjects held by him. Consequent on
the above, Thiru Duraimurugan be re-designated as Minister for Law.
K.S. SRIPATHI,
Chief Secretary.
