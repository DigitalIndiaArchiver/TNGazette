No. II(2)/HWMP/216(b-1)/2020.
The Governor of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied 
that the lands specified in the Schedule below have to be 
acquired for the Highways purpose of widening of SH-40 
Tiruchendur – Ambasamudram via Palayamkottai Road 
from km 0/750 to 54/000 and from km 64/200 to 84/900 at

District and it has already been decided that the entire amount 
of compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid 
out of the funds controlled or managed by the Divisional 
Engineer (H), Chennai Kanyakumari Industrial Corridor 
Project, Tirunelveli after considering the cause shown by the 
owner (or) other person having interest on such lands as the 
case may be do hereby publish the following notice under 
sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 
(Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
 Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil 
Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the land specified in 
the Schedule below measuring to an extent of 1558 sq.mts 
of dry lands in Gopalasamudram Village, Chernmahadevi 
Taluk, Tirunelveli District for the purpose of widening of 
SH-40 Tiruchendur – Ambasamudram – Palayamkottai Road 
(from km 0/750 to 54/000 and from km 64/200 to 84/900).
The plan of the lands is kept in the Office of the 
Special District Revenue Officer (LA), Chennai Kanyakumari 
Industrial Corridor Project, Tirunelveli, and the Office of 
the Special Tahsildar (LA) Chennai Kanyakumari Industrial 
Corridor Project, Tirunelveli and may be inspected at any 
time during office hours.

Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil 
Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) 
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the land 
specified in the Schedule below measuring to an extent 
of  886 sq.mts of  dry  lands in  No.9 Polambakkam 
Village, Cheyyur Taluk, Chengalpattu District for the purpose 
of Strengthening and widening of S.H-115 Cheyyur to Polur 
Road from Km 16/700 to 18/000 Km.
The plan of the lands are kept in the Office of the 
Special District Revenue Officer (Land Acquisition), Chennai 
Kanyakumari Industrial Corridor Project, Kancheepuram, and 
Office of the Special Tahsildar (Land Acquisition), Chennai 
Kanyakumari Industrial Corridor Project, Cheyyur Unit, may 
be inspected at any time during office hours.
