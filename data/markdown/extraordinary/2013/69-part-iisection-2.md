NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
HIGHER EDUCATION DEPARTMENT
ONE MEMBER ELECTED FROM AMONG THE MEMBERS OF LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY AS A MEMBER
TO THE SYNDICATE OF ANNA UNIVERSITY, CHENNAI UNDER THE ANNA UNIVERSITY ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 33, Higher Education  (I-1), 12th March 2013, Maasi 28, Nandhana,
Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2044.]
No. II(2)/HE/241(g)/2013.
Under clause (g) of “Class II-Other Members” in sub-section (2) of Section 17 of the
Anna University Act, 1978 (Tamil Nadu Act 30 of 1978), the following member is elected to the
Syndicate of Anna University:—
Class II - Other Members
(g) One member elected by the Members of the Legislative Assembly of the State from among
themselves:—
Thiru M. Rengasamy,
Member of the Legislative Assembly,
Thanjavur Constituency.
2. As per clause (a) of sub-section (5) of Section 17 of the said Act, the members of the Syndicate,
other than the ex-officio members, shall hold office for a period of three years.
      APURVA VARMA,
Principal Secretary to Government.
