In exercise of the powers conferred under Rule 48-A of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Elections) Rules, 1995 and
sub-rule (5) of Rule 45 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities, Municipalities and Corporations
(Elections) Rules, 2006 and in supersession of the State Election Commission’s earlier Notification, the
State Election Commission hereby directs that all electors shall produce any one of the documents specified by this
Commission for establishing their identity in order to exercise their franchise, when they come to the polling stations for
voting and specifies the following documents for establishing the identity of such electors:—
(i)  Electoral Photo Identity Card (EPIC) provided under the authority of the Election Commission of India.
(ii) Passports.
(iii) Driving Licence issued upto 31-12-2009.
(iv) Income Tax Identity (PAN) Cards.
(v) Service Identity Cards with photograph, issued to its employees by State/Central Government,
Public Sector undertakings, Local Bodies or Public Limited Companies.
(vi) Passbooks with photograph, issued by Public Sector Banks/Post Office and Kisan Pass Books
(Accounts opened on or before 31-12-2009).
(vii) Pension Documents with photograph, such as ex-servicemen’s Pension Book/Pension Payment Order,
ex-servicemen’s Dependent Certificates, Old Age Pension Order, Widow Pension Order issued upto 31-12-2009.

(viii) Freedom Fighter Identify Cards with photo.
(ix) Property documents such as Pattas, Registered Deeds, etc., with photograph.
(x) SC/ST/OBC certificates issued by competent authority with photograph (issued upto 31-12-2009).
(xi) Arms Licence issued upto 31-12-2009.
(xii) Certificate of physical handicap with photograph issued by the competent authority issued
upto 31-12-2009.
(xiii) Job Cards issued under NERG Scheme with photograph issued upto 31-12-2009.
(xiv) Health Insurance Scheme Smart Cards with photograph (Ministry of Labour’s Scheme issued
upto 31-12-2009).
2. It is clarified that any document, as enumerated above, which is available only for the Head of family, shall be allowed
for the purpose of identification of other members of the family:
Provided the other members of the family can be identified on the basis of such document.
 3. The State Election Commission makes it clear that failure of refusal on the part of the electors to produce any one of
the said documents may result in the denial of supply of a ballot paper to them and permission to vote.
