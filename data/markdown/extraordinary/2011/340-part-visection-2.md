In pursuance of sub-rule (I) (a) of rule 47 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Elections) Rules, 1995 and rule 45 and 67 of the
Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities, Municipalities and Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006, the Tamil Nadu
State Election Commission,  hereby directs that before delivering the ballot paper or ballot papers to the elector, or before allowing
to record his / her vote in EVM  indelible ink should be applied as a line from the top end of the nail to the bottom of the first joint
of the left forefinger.
