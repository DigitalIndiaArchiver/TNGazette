GENERAL  NOTIFICATION
Grant of No Objection Certificate.
(Rc. No. E2(1)/451/31308/2009.)
FORM-A2
[Notice under Rule 35A(4) of Tamil Nadu Cinemas (Regulation) Rule 1957.)
No. VI(1)/423/2011.
Whereas Thiru Mathivathanan, No. 91-92, Jawaharlal Nehru Salai, Kasi Theatre Complex, Ashok Nagar,
Chennai-600 083, has applied to the Commissioner of Police, Chennai Police, Chennai-600 008, for the grant of
“No Objection Certificate” for the construction of the permanent multiplex complex containing Four screens along with
shops, food court etc, at T.S.No. 208/5, Block No. 132, Survey No. 346, 347 and 348/2, Pillaiyar Koil Street, Jafferkhanpet,
Ashok Nagar, Kodambakkam Village, Mambalam-Guindy Taluk, Chennai-600 083.
1. Notice is hereby given that any person having any objection to the grant to “No Objection Certificate” to
the above said site on the grounds of non-compliance by the applicant with any of the provisions of the Tamil Nadu
Cinemas (Regulation) Act, 1955 (Tamil Nadu Act IX of 1955) or with any of the Rules there-under should file his
objection in writing with the Licensing Authority (Commissioner of Police) within 15 days of publication of this Notice.
2.  Any objection filed after the period shall be liable to be summarily rejected.
TRIPATHI,
Egmore, Chennai-600 008.
Commissioner of Police,
8th November 2011.
Chennai Police.
JUDICIAL  NOTIFICATIONS
Constitution of Separate District Munsif Court, Tiruchendur in Thoothukkudi District by converting the
existing Principal District Munisif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Tiruchendur.
(Roc. No. 2895/2000/G/Judn.)
No. VI(1)/424/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873)
the High Court, Madras, hereby fixes Tiruchendur in Thoothukkudi District as the place at which the District Munsif
Court, Tiruchendur, shall be held.
NOTIFICATION-II
(Roc. No. 2895/2000/G/Judn.)
No.VI(1)/425/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 11 of the  Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873)
the High Court, Madras hereby directs that in Thoothukkudi District, the District Munsif Court, Tiruchendur, shall  have
local jurisdiction over the entire Revenue Taluk of Tiruchendur except Alwarthirunagari Firka with effect from the date
of constitution of separate  District Munsif Court at Tiruchendur by converting the Principal District Munsif-cum-Judicial
Magistrate Court, Tiruchendur.

NOTIFICATION-III
(Roc. No. 2895/2000/G/Judn.)
No.VI(1)/426/2011.
The High Court, Madras hereby directs and notifies that the District Munsif, Tiruchendur in Thoothukkudi District shall
exercise all the powers conferred on a District Munsif under Section 12 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873
(Central Act III of 1873)  as amended up-to-date in regard to original suits and proceedings not otherwise exempted
from his cognizance of which the amount or value of the subject matter does not exceed One Lakh Rupees within the
local limits of his jurisdiction with effect from the date of constitution of separate  District Munsif Court at Tiruchendur
by converting the Principal District, Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Tiruchendur.
NOTIFICATION-IV
(Roc. No. 2895/2000/G/Judn.)
No. VI(1)/427/2011.
In exercise of the power conferred by Section 28 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873)
the High Court, Madras, hereby directs that the District Munsif, Tiruchendur shall have and exercise jurisdiction of a court
of Small Causes under the provincial Small Causes Court Act, 1887 for the trial suits cognizable by a court of small
causes upon the pecuniary limits of Rs.5000/- (Rupees five thousand only) with effect from the date of constitution of
separate District Munsif Court at Tiruchendur by Converting the Principal District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court,
Tiruchendur.
High Court, Madras,

the Master Plan for the Salem Local Planning Area approved under the said Act and published in the Housing and Urban
Development Department Notification No. II(2)/HOU/233/2005 on Page 168 of Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu
Government Gazette, dated the 13th April 2005.

under the heading “VILLAGE IN VIRAPANDI PANCHAYAT UNION, V.No. 82. UTTAMASOLAPURAM”

117 (Except 117/18,19), for the expression “119 P” the expression 119 P (Except 119/1 to 9) shall be substituted.

entries shall be inserted namely “EDUCATIONAL USE E-36” S.F. Nos. 117/18,19, 119/1 to 9 shall be added.
