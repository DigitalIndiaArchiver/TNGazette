No. I/PUSP/26/2018.—The following Notiﬁ cation of the 
Government of India, Ministry of Law and Justice (Department 
of Justice), Jaisalmer House, 26, Man Singh Road, 
New Delhi-110 011, dated 3rd August, 2018 is  republished.—
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (2) of 
Article 124 of the Constitution of India, the President is pleased 
to appoint Kumari Justice Indira Banerjee, Chief Justice of 
the Madras High Court, to be a Judge of the Supreme Court 
of India with effect from the date she assumes charge of her 
ofﬁ ce.

of the Government of India, Ministry of Law and Justice 
Department of Justice, Jaisalmer House, 26, Man Singh Road, 
New Delhi-110 011, dated 6th August, 2018 is  republished.—
In exercise of the powers conferred by Article 223 of the 
Constitution of India, the President is pleased to appoint Shri 
Justice Huluvadi Gangadharappa Ramesh, senior-most Judge 
of the Madras High Court, to perform the duties of the ofﬁ ce 
of Chief Justice of that High Court with effect from the date 
Kumari Justice Indira Banerjee, relinquishes charge of the 
ofﬁ ce of the Chief Justice of the Madras High Court consequent 
upon her appointment as a Judge of the Supreme Court of 
India till Shrimati Justice Vijaya Kamlesh Tahilramani assumes 
the charge of her ofﬁ ce as the Chief Justice of the Madras High 
Court.
