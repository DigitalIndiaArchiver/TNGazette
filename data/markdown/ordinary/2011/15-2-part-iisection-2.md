[G.O. (2D) No. 10, Highways and Minor Ports (HS.1),
31st March 2011, Panguni 17, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No. II(2)/HWMP/171/2011.—The Governor of Tamil Nadu
having been  satisfied that the lands specified in the Schedule
below are situated in 41 Punjai Lakkapuram, 45 Nanjai Uthukuli
and 46 Pudur-A and B Villages  have to be acquired for
Public purpose to wit for the formation of Outer Ring Road for
Erode Town and it having already been decided that the
entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands is
to be paid out of the funds, controlled or managed by the
District Revenue Officer, Erode and having  considered the
cause shown by the owner or other persons having interest
in the said lands, as the case may be do hereby publish the
following notice under sub section (1) of Section 15 of  the
Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu
Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands specified
in the schedule below  situated in 41 Punjai Lakkapuram,
45 Nanjai Uthukuli and 46 Pudur-A and B Villages measuring
an  extent of 126064 Square Metres to the same, a little more
or less are needed for Public purpose, to wit for the formation
of Outer Ring Road for Erode Town.
The Plan of the lands are kept in the office of the Tahsildar,
Erode and may be inspected at any time during office hours.
