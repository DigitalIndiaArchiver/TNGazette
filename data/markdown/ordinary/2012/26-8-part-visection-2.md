COLLECTORS AND LOCAL AUTHORITIES
TAMIL NADU STATE LEGAL SERVICES AUTHORITY, CHENNAI.
Constitution of Taluk Legal Services Committee at Needamangalam in Tiruvarur District
and Appointment of District Munsif-Cum-Judicial Magistrate As Ex-Officio Chairman of
Needamangalam Taluk Legal Services Committee.
(TNSLSA No.2265/E/2012.)
No. VI(2)/196/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 11(A)(1) of the Legal Services Authorities Act, 1987 (Central Act
39/1987) read with Rule 3(a) of the Tamil Nadu Taluk Legal Services Committee Rules 1997, the Tamil Nadu State Legal
Services Authority hereby constitutes a Taluk Legal Services Committee at Needamangalam in Tiruvarur District and
appoints the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate, Needamangalam as ex-officio Chairman, Needamangalam Taluk Legal
Service Committee to  exercise the Powers and perform the functions conferred on, or assigned to the Taluk Legal Services
Committee under the Act.
Chennai-600 104,
K. GANESAN,
18th June 2012.
District Judge/Member Secretary.
GOVERNMENT  EXAMINATIONS / EDUCATIONAL  OFFICERS
LOSS OF CERTIFICATES
It is hereby notified that the Original Higher Secondary Certificates / Matriculation Certificates / Diploma Certificates/
School Certificates of the following pupils are reported to have been lost beyond recovery. Necessary steps are
being taken to issue the duplicate of the said Certificates. If the Originals were to be found by anybody, they
should be treated as invalid and sent to the Secretary, Board of Higher Secondary Examination / Directorate
of Government  Examinations, Chennai-6 / Director of Technical Education, Chennai-25 / Inspector / Inspectress of
Matriculation and Anglo-Indian Schools as the case may be for cancellation:—
