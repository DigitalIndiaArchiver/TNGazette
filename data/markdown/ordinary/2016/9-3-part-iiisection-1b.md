2.

The General and Special Rules applicable to the holders of  permanent posts in Class IV of the Tamil Nadu Zoo
Subordinate Service shall apply to the holders of the temporary post of Driver cum Garbage Collection Worker, sanctioned

the following rules:—

3. Appointment:- Appointment to the post shall be made by direct recruitment.
4. Appointing authority:- The appointing authority for the post sanctioned to the Zoo specified in coloumn (1) of the

HOME DEPARTMENT
Framing of Adhoc Rules for the Temporary Posts of Data Entry Operators in Police Department in Tamil Nadu
General Subordinate Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 220, Home (POL-XV), 18th February 2016, ñ£C 6, ñ¡ñî, F¼õœÀõ˜ Ý‡´-2047.]
No. SRO B-22/2016.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following rules:-
2. These rules shall be deemed to have come into force with effect on and from the 1st day of May 2007.
RULES
The General Rules applicable to the holders of the permanent post in the Tamil Nadu General Subordinate Service shall
apply to the holders of the temporary post of Data Entry Operator in Police Department, subject to the modifications specified
in the following rules:-
2. Constitution: The post shall constitute a distinct class in the said Service.
3. Appointment:- All appointments to the post shall be made by direct recruitment, only under compassionate appointment
scheme.
4. Appointing Authority.- The appointing authority for the post shall be the Superintendent of Police concerned in the
districts and the Deputy Commissioner of Police concerned in the City Commissionerates.
5. Qualifications.- No person shall be appointed  to the post unless he possesses the minimum general educational
qualification prescribed in Schedule I to Part II General Rules of the Tamil Nadu State and Subordinate Service Rules.
6. Reservation of Appointments:- The rule relating to reservation of appointments (Rule 22 of Part II General Rules of
the Tamil Nadu State and Subordinate Service Rules) shall not apply for  appointment  to the post.
7. Probation.- Every person appointed to the post of shall, from the date on which he joins duty, be on probation for a
total period of two years on duty within a continuous period of three years.
8. Pay:- There shall be paid to the holder of the post, a monthly pay calculated in the scale of pay of
Rs.3050-75-3950-80-4590 on 1-5-2007.
Provided that the pay of the holders of the post, who were paid in the pre-existing scale of Rs.3050-75-3950-80-4590/- at
any time during the period from 1-5-2007 to 31-5-2009, shall be fixed in the revised pay scale, under the Tamil Nadu
Revised Scales of Pay Rules, 2009.
9. Training:- Every person appointed to the post shall undergo a course of training in computer operations for a period
of three months in the Police Training College, Chennai. The Syllabus for the training shall be as prescribed by the Director
General of Police. The period of training shall count for probation and increment.
10. Savings:- Nothing contained in these rules shall adversely affect any person appointed to the post before 18-2-2016.
APURVA VARMA,
Principal Secretary to Government.
