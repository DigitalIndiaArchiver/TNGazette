No. SRO B-21/2014.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the
Tamil Nadu Industries Service (Section 19 in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual 1970):—
2.
(i) The amendments 1 (a), 2(a) (i), 2(b)(i), 3 and 4(a) hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the
6th June 1991.
(ii) The amendments 1(b), 2(a) (ii), 2 (a) (iii), 2(b) (ii), 4(b), 4(c) and 4(d) hereby made shall be deemed to have come
into force on the 31st August, 2000.
AMENDEMNTS
In the said Special Rules,—
1. in rule I, under the heading "Category 1",-
(a) the expression "Joint Director of Industries and Commerce (Rural Industries Projects)" shall be omitted;
(b) the expression "Joint Director of Industries and Commerce (Chemicals)" and the expression
"Joint Director of Industries and Commerce (Electrical and Electronics)" shall be omitted;
2. In rule 2,-
(a) in sub-rule (a), in the tabular column,-
(i)
under the heading “Posts” in the first column, for the expression “Joint  Director of Industries and
Commerce (Engineering) and Joint Director of Industries and Commerce (Rural Industries Project)”, the expression
“Joint Director of Industries and Commerce (Engineering)” shall be substituted;
(ii)
the expression “Joint Director of Industries and Commerce (Chemicals)" in the first column and the corresponding
entries relating thereto in the second column shall be omitted;
(iii) the expression “Joint Director of Industries and Commerce (Electrical and Electronics)” in the first column and
the corresponding entries relating thereto in the second column shall be omitted;
(b) in sub-rule (b),-
(i)
the expression “Joint Director of Industries and Commerce (Rural Industries Project)” shall be omitted
(ii)
the expression “Joint Director of Industries and Commerce (Chemicals)” shall be omitted;
3.
the proviso to rule 5 (A) shall be omitted;
4.
in the ANNEXURE in the tabular Column,-
(a) under the heading “Category,” the expression “Joint Director of Industries and Commerce (Rural Industries Project)”
in the first column, shall be omitted;
(b) under the heading “Qualifications” for the entries in the third column against the Category “Joint Director of Industries
and Commerce (Engineering)” in the first column, the following entries shall be substituted, namely:-
(a) (i)
Degree in Mechanical or Electrical or Electronics or Instrumentation or Automobile or Chemical Engineering
or a degree in Mining and Metallurgy or a Diploma in Automobile Engineering of the any University or Institution recognized by
the All India Council for Technical Education or atleast a Second Class degree in any branch of Chemistry or in Industrial
Chemistry or an Associateship Diploma of the Institute of Chemists (India) or M.Sc. Degree in Physics with Electronics as a
special subject, or a Diploma in Mechanical or Electrical or Electronics or Automobile or Chemical Engineering or Mining and
Metallurgy or Instrumentation issued by the Technical Diploma Examination Board, Madras (State Board of Technical Education
and Training, Tamil Nadu) or by any other recognized Institution or Board; and

No. SRO B-22/2014.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 read with Article 313 of the
Constitution of India and of all other powers hereunto enabling, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following
amendment to the Fundamental Rules.
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 30th October 2012.
Amendment.
In the said Fundamental Rules, in rule 110, in sub-rule (b) in clause (xxi), for the expression "Director General of Police",
the expression "Additional Director General of Police (Administration)" shall be substituted.
ANITA PRAVEEN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
PLANNING, DEVELOPMENT AND SPECIAL INTIATIVES DEPARTMENT
Amendments to Adhoc Rules for the Tamil Nadu Statistics Service
[G.O. Ms. No. 19, Planning, Development and Special Initiatives (ST.1), 18th February 2014,
Maasi, 6, Vijaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu 2045.]
No. SRO B-23/2014.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the adhoc rules published with the Planning, Development
and Special Initiatives Department Notification No. SRO B 33/2013, published in pages 45-49 in Part III - Section 1 (b)
of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 23rd October 2013.
The amendment hereby made shall come into force on and from 18th February 2014.
AMENDEMNTS
In the said Rules, in rule 5, in “THE TABLE”,-
(i)
against the entry “Director” in column (1), the entry “By Promotion” in column (2) and the corresponding entries in
column (3) thereof, shall be omitted;
(ii) against the entry “Reader in Official Statistics” in column (1), the entry “By Transfer from the Post of Joint Director
of Statistics” in column (2) and the corresponding entries in column (3) thereof, shall be omitted;
(iii) against the entry "Lecturer in Official Statistics" in column (1), the entries "By Transfer from the post of Assistant
Director of Statistics" and "By Recruitment by Transfer from the post of Statistical Officer" in column (2) and the corresponding
entries in column (3) thereof, shall be omitted.
S. KRISHNAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
