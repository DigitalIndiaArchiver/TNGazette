HOME, PROHIBITION AND EXCISE DEPARTMENT
AMENDMENTS TO TAMIL NADU LIQUOR (LICENCE AND PERMIT) RULES, 1981.
[G.O. Ms. No. 23, Home, Prohibition and Excise (VIII), 10th April 2013.]
No. SRO A-4(a)/2013.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Sections 17-C, 17-D, 17-E, 18-B, 18-C, 20 and 21 read with
Section 54 of the Tamil Nadu Prohibition Act, 1937 (Tamil Nadu Act X of 1937), the Governor of Tamil Nadu,
hereby makes the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu Liquor (Licence and Permit) Rules, 1981.
2.The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force with effect on and from the
1st April 2012.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules, in rule 17, in sub-rule (b),—
(1) in clause II, for the sentence beginning with the expression “The privilege and licence” and ending with the expression
“the club concerned”, the following sentence shall be substituted, namely:—
“The privilege and the licence in Form F.L. 2 shall be issued by the Commissioner on payment of an annual privilege fee
of Rs. 10,00,000/- (Rupees ten lakhs only) for Chennai City and Rs. 6,00,000/- (Rupees six lakhs only) for the other areas, a
licence fee of Rs. 5,000/- (Rupees five thousand only) and an application fee of Rs. 1,000/- (Rupees one thousand only) on an
application made in Form F.A. 1-2 by the Honorary Secretary of the club concerned.";
(2) in clause III, for the first and second tables, the following tables shall, respectively, be substituted, namely:—
