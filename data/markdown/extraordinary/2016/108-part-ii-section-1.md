DECLARATION OF MONDAY, THE 23RD MAY 2016 AS A PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN CONNECTION WITH THE
GENERAL ELECTION TO TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY 2016 FROM 134. ARAVAKURICHI
AND 174. THANJAVUR ASSEMBLY CONSTITUENCIES IN TAMIL NADU.
[G.O. Ms. No. 505, Public (Miscellaneous), 19th May, 2016, Vaikasi 6,
Thunmugi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2047.]
No. II(1)/PUMIS/7(l)/2016.
Under the “Explanation” to Section 25 of the Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881 (Central Act XXVI of 1881)
read with the Notification of the Government of India, Ministry of Home Affairs No. 20-25-26 Public-I, dated
the 8th June 1957, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby declares Monday, the 23rd May 2016, the date on which
the poll for the General Elections to Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly, from 134. Aravakurichi and 174. Thanjavur
Assembly Constituencies will take place, to be a public holiday for the areas comprised in the above Assembly
Constituencies lying in Karur and Thanjavur Districts respectively.
2.
Further, all employees working in Karur and Thanjavur Districts but registered as voters in 134.Aravakurichi
and 174. Thanjavur Assembly Constituencies shall also be given a paid holiday on 23-05-2016.
3. All Government Offices including industrial establishments of the Government and Government controlled
bodies and all the educational institutions throughout the area comprised in 134.Aravakurichi and 174. Thanjavur
Assembly Constituencies lying in Karur and Thanjavur Districts respectively will remain closed on 23-05-2016.
K. GNANADESIKAN,
Chief Secretary.
