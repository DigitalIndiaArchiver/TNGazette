ADI DRAVIDAR AND TRIBAL WELFARE DEPARTMENT
NOTIFICATION RELATING TO GROUPING OF THE SUB-CASTES KUDUMBAN, PANNADI, KALLADI,
KADAIYAN, DEVENDRAKULTHAN, PALLAN AND VATHIRIYAN UNDER THE COMMON NAME OF
DEVENDRA KULA VELLALAR.
[G.O. Ms. No. 17, Adi Dravidar and Tribal Welfare (CV-1), 2nd February 2011,

No. II(1)/ADTW/4(a)/2011.
Whereas, in G.O. (Ms.) No. 15, Adi Dravidar and Tribal Welfare (CV-1) Department, dated the 27th day
of January 2011, the Government have constituted One man Committee headed by Justice M.S. Janarthanam,
retired High Court Judge to examine the request relating to grouping of the sub-castes, ‘Kudumban’, ‘Pannadi’,
‘Kalladi’, ‘Kadaiyan’, ‘Devendrakulthan’, ‘Pallan’ and ‘Vathiriyan’ under the common name of “Devendra Kula
Vellalar”.
And Whereas, the Government now specify that the terms of reference of the said Committee
as follows:—
The Committee shall examine the request for grouping of Sub-Castes of  Kudumban, Pannadi, Kalladi,
Kadaiyan, Devendrakulthan, Pallan and Vathiriyan under the common name of “Devendra Kula Vellalar” and to
give its recommendation to the Government.
THANGA KALIYAPERUMAL,
Secretary to Government.
