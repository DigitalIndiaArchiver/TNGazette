GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI
Extension of Period of Direct Management of Hazarath Syed Baduruddin Shaheed Dargah,
Zamin Pallavaram, Kancheepuram District.
(Lr.Rc.No.629/83/B4/KPM)
No.VI-3(a)/101/2015.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act 1995, the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board
in its resolution in item No.76/15 in Rc.No.629/83/B4/KPM dated: 28-05-2015 has resolved to ratify the period of Direct
Management of Hazarath Syed Baduruddin Shaheed Dargah, Zamin Pallavaram, Kancheepuram District from 23-10-2014
to 27-05-2015 and to extend the period of Direct Management of the Wakf for a further period of One Year from the date
of Board’s Order (28-05-2015).
Chennai-600 001,
K. RASHEETH ALI,
31st August 2015.

ADVERTISEMENT OF PETITION
Notice is hereby given that a petition for winding-up of the above named Respondent company by the High Court
at Madras was on the 22nd day of June 2015 presented to the said Court by Ganeshi Lal Jain and Suresh Kumar
Jain, both being sons of late Mr. G.D. Jain and both residing at No.1/6-B, Pusa Road, New Delhi-110 005, as creditors
of the above named respondent company and that the said petition is directed to be heard before the Court on the
01st day of October 2015.
Any creditor, contributory or other person desirous of supporting or opposing the making of an order on the said
petition should send to the petitioners or their advocate notice of his intention signed by him or his advocate with his
name and address, so as to reach the petitioners or their advocate not later than 5 days before the date fixed for
the hearing of the petition, and appear at the hearing for the purpose in person or by his advocate. A copy of the
petition will be furnished by the undersigned to any creditor or contributory on payment of the prescribed charges for
the same.
Any affidavit intended to be used in opposition to the petition should be filed in the Court and a copy served on
the Petitioners or their advocate, not less than 5 days before the date fixed for hearing.
Dated at Chennai on this the 26th day of August 2015.
