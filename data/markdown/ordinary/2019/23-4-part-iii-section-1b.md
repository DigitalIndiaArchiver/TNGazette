6. Training.- Every person appointed to the post of Junior Assistant or Assistant, if he has not undergone the foundational 
for a period of two months, prior to such appointment, shall undergo the foundational training within two years from the date 
of appointment of the post:
Provided that every person appointed to the post of Assistant or Junior Assistant, after completion of 53 years of age 
need not undergo the foundational training.
7. Transfers & Postings.- The Commissioner/Director of Agriculture is the Competent Authority for transferring and postings 
for all the posts except Deputy General Manager. The Agricultural Production Commissioner and Principal Secretary /Secretary 
to Government, Agriculture Department is the competent authority  for transferring and posting for Deputy General Manager.
8. Preparation of Annual List.- For the purpose of preparation of the annual list of approved candidates for appointment 
to the posts in the service by promotion and recruitment by transfer, the crucial date on which the candidates should have 
acquired the prescribed qualiﬁ cations shall be the 1st April of Every Year.
