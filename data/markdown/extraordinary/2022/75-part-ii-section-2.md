Order No.92, Public (Law Officers,) 29th January 2022.
In the G.O first read above, orders were issued freezing of Boundaries of Administrative Units viz., 
municipalities, revenue villages, towns, tehsils, police stations, sub-divisions, districts etc., and ban on formation 
of new Units in the State of Tamil Nadu from 1st January 2020 to 31st March 2020.
2. In the G.O. 2nd read above, the date of freezing of Boundaries of Administrative Units was extended upto 
31st December 2020.

3. In the G.O. 3rd read above, the date of freezing of Boundaries of Administrative Units was further extended upto 
31st March 2021.
4. In the G.O. 4th read above, the date of freezing of Boundaries of Administrative Units was furthermore extended 
upto 31st December 2021.
5. In the letter 4th read above, the Director of Census Operations, Tamil Nadu in his letter dated 28-12-2021 has 
enclosed a copy of the letter dated 23-12-2021 received from the Director of Census, NPR/ NRC, Ministry of Home Affairs, 
Government of India and has stated that owing to the prevailing pandemic conditions due to the variant Omicron of Covid-19 
and the States being pre-occupied in the vaccination drive against Covid-19, the time period of conduct of Census is not 
yet decided. In the above circumstances, it was decided by the competent authority to further extend the date of freezing of 
boundaries up to 30-06-2022 vide ORGI Lr.F.No.9/33/2019-CD(Cen) dated 24-12-2021 and therefore he has requested  to 
notify the extension of freezing date of boundaries of administrative units upto 30-06-2022 in the State Gazette and to issue 
necessary instructions to Departments concerned to give effect to the changes in the  jurisdiction of administrative units if 
any, latest by 30-06-2022.  
6. In the circumstances stated above, the Government hereby extend the Date of Freezing of Boundaries of Administrative 
Units viz., municipalities, revenue villages, towns, tehsils, police stations, sub-divisions, districts, etc. and ban on formation of 
new units in the State of Tamil Nadu upto 30th June 2022 in connection with Census Operations. 
7. All the pending proposals, if any affect the changes in the existing Boundaries of municipalities, revenue villages, 
towns, tehsils, police stations, sub-divisions, districts etc., may therefore be finalized on or before 30th June, 2022 under 
intimation to the Director of Census Operations, Tamil Nadu. 
8. The Commissioner of Revenue Administration, Chennai, the Director General of Police, Chennai, the Commissioner 
of Rural Development and Panchayat Raj, Chennai, the Director of Municipal Administration, Chennai, the Director of Town 
Panchayats, Chennai, the Collectors of all Districts, the Commissioner of Greater Chennai Corporation, the Commissioners, 
Municipal Corporations are directed to adhere to the above decision of the Government.
