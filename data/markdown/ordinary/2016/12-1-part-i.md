No.I/PUSP/7/2016.—The following Notification of the Government of India,
Ministry of Law and Justice (Department of Justice), Jaisalmer House,
26, Man Singh Road, New Delhi-100 011,  dated 26th February 2016 is
republished:—
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (1) of Article 222 of the
Constitution of India, the President, after  consultation with the Chief Justice of
India, is pleased to transfer Shri Justice Chinnasamy Swaminathan Karnan,
Judge of the Madras High Court, as a Judge of the Calcutta High Court and to direct
him to assume charge of his office in the Calcutta High Court on or before
11th March, 2016.
