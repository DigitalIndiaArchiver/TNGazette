No. I/PUSP/4/2015.—The following Notification of the
Government of India, Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances
and Pensions, Department of Personnel and Training,
New Delhi, the 22nd January, 2015 is republished:—
In pursuance of the order dated 12-08-2014 as modified by
order dated 17-10-2014 of the Hon’ble CAT, Madras Bench in
OA No.310/00065/2014 and, in exercise of the powers
conferred by Rule 8(1) of the Indian Administrative Service
(Recruitment) Rules, 1954, read with Regulation 9(1) of the
Indian Administrative Service (Appointment by Promotion)
Regulations, 1955 and Rule 3 of the Indian Administrative
Service (Probation) Rules, 1954, the President is pleased to
appoint Smt. M. Asia Mariam of the State Civil Service of
Tamil Nadu to the Indian Administrative Service against the
vacancies determined by Government of India under Regulation
5(1) of the said Regulations in consultation with the State
Government for the Select List of 2012, on probation until
further orders and to allocate her to the Tamil Nadu Cadre,
under Rule 5(1) of the Indian Administrative Service (Cadre)
Rules, 1954
[No.14015/38/2014-AIS (I)-B.]
NITIN GUPTA
                  Under Secretary to the Government of India.
(By Order of the Governor)
K. GNANADESIKAN,
Chief Secretary to Government.
