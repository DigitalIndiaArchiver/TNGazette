PUBLIC DEPARTMENT

APPOINTMENT OF CERTAIN PERSONS AS MEMBER OF THE STATE HUMAN RIGHTS COMMISSION
UNDER THE PROTECTION OF HUMAN RIGHTS ACT.
[G.O.Ms. No. 774, Public (Human Rights), 27th August 2011.]
No. II(2)/PUHR/383 (d)/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 22(1) of the Protection of Human Rights Act, 1993 (Central
Act 10 of 1994), as amended by Central Act 43 of 2006, the Governor of Tamil Nadu is pleased to appoint
Thiru K. Baskaran, Principal District Judge, Madurai and Thirumathi Jayanthi, IAS  (Retd.), as Members of the
State Human Rights Commission, Tamil Nadu in the existing vacancies.
 DEBENDRANATH  SARANGI,
Chief Secretary.
