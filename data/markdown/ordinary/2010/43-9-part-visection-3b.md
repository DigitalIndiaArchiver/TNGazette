Election of (i) Two Members to the Syndicate by the Senate from among its members and (ii) Two Members to the
Academic Council by the Senate from among its Members [who are not Engaged in Teaching]
(No. V.1/Miscellaneous/10-2010/408.)
No. VI-3(b)/44/2010.
It is hereby notified that the election of [i]  TWO MEMBERS to the Syndicate by the Senate from among its members and
[ii] TWO MEMBERS to the Academic Council by the Senate from among its members [who are not engaged in teaching], will
be held under Section 18 [a] - Other Members [1] and Section 23 [a] - Other Members [1] of the Madras University Act, 1923
respectively, on the day of the Ordinary Meeting of the Senate scheduled on 30th October, 2010.
TWO VACANCIES
[i] SYNDICATE [Section 18(a) - Other Members(1)]:
“SIX MEMBERS to be elected by the Senate from among its members”.
The newly elected TWO MEMBERS will hold office as Members of the Syndicate for the Residual Period, i.e., upto
27-03-2012 (i.e., from the date of declaration of result).
TWO VACANCIES
[ii] THE ACADEMIC COUNCIL [Section 23 (a) - Other Members Sub-Section (1)]:
“FIVE MEMBERS to be elected by the Senate from among its members who are not engaged in teaching”.
The newly elected TWO MEMBERS will hold office as members of the Academic Council for a period of three years from
the date of receipt of nomination/the date of withdrawal/the date of election/the date of declaration of results whichever is
applicable for the election.
The election will be conducted according to the Statutes contained in Chapter XIX, Volume I, University Calendar 2001
and the provisions of the Madras University Act, 1923.
Nomination forms are available in the Registrar’s Office on  requisition by the candidate, Nomination should be sent so
as to be received by the Registrar, University of Madras, University Buildings, Chepauk, Chennai-600 005, Not later than
Monday, the 4th October 2010 upto 4.00 p.m. The scrutiny of nominations will take place on the same day, i.e., 04-10-2010
at 4.15 p.m. in the Registrar’s Office, Centenary Building, Chepauk, Chennai-5.
