LAW DEPARTMENT
(LAW SCHOOL)
DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF THE TAMIL NADU NATIONAL
LAW SCHOOL ACT, 2012.
[G.O. Ms. No. 703, Law (LS), 29th October  2012.]
No. II(2)/LAW/701(d)/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (3) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu National
Law School Act, 2012 (Tamil Nadu Act 9 of 2012), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the
1st November 2012 as the date on which the said Act shall come into force.
 G. JAYACHANDRAN,
Secretary to Government.
