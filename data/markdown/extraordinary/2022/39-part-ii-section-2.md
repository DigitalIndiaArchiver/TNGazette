CRUELTY TO ANIMALS ACT, 1960 (CENTRAL  ACT 59 OF 1960), AS AMENDED BY THE PREVENTION OF CRUELTY TO 
ANIMALS (TAMIL NADU AMENDMENT), ACT, 2017 (ACT 1 OF 2017).
[G.O. (D) No. 13, Animal Husbandry, Dairying, Fisheries and Fishermen Welfare (AH3),
