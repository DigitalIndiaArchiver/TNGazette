HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE DEPARTMENT
Vesting of Property and Scheme for the Administration
relating to Endowment Fund for вЂњDr. Anton Goldwin
Fernandez Gold MedalвЂќ for the best outgoing Student
of MBBS Final Year in Kanyakumari Medical College,
Kanyakumari under the Chritable Endowments Act.
[G.O. Ms. No. 253, Health and Family Welfare (H2),
2nd September 2014, Aavani 17, Jaya, Thiruvalluvar
Aandu-2045.]
No. II(2)/HFW/596/2014.вЂ”In the matter of the Charitable
Endowments Act, 1890 (Central Act VI of 1890) and in the
matter of Endowment Fund for вЂњDr.Anton Goldwin Fernandez
Gold MedalвЂќ for the best outgoing student of MBBS final year
in Kanyakumari Medical College, Kanyakumari.
I VESTING OF PROPERTY
Whereas an application has been made to the Government
of Tamil Nadu under clause (b) of sub-section 1 of Section
6 of the Charitable Endowments Act, 1890 (Central Act VI of
1890) by St.Mary Health Educational and Research Trust,
Nagercoil, the donor of the Endowment Fund sepcified above
for vesting Rs.2,00,000/- (Rupees Two Lakhs only) in the
Treasurer of Charitable Endowments appointed under the said
Act for the State of Tamil Nadu. The Governor of Tamil Nadu,
in exercise of the powers conferred by Section 4 of the said
Act, hereby directs that the said amount be vested in the
Treasurer aforesaid and be held by him and his successors-
in office, subject to the provisions of the said Act and to any
rules, which from time to time, may be or shall deemed to
have been made thereunder, upon trust for the purpose and
the subject to the conditions setforth in the Schemes settled
under Section 5 of the said Act, for the Administration of the
said Fund.
II SCHEME FOR ADMINISTRATION
Whereas an application has been made to the Government
of Tamil Nadu under Clause (b) of sub-section1 of Section 6
of the Charitable Endowments Act, 1890 (Central  Act VI of
1890) by St.Mary Health Educational and Research Trust,
Nagercoil, the donor of the Endowment Fund specified above,
for settling the Scheme for the administration of the said Fund.
The Governor of Tamil Nadu in exercise of the powers conferred
by Section 5 of the said Act, hereby settles the Scheme setforth
in the schedule below for the administration of the said Fund
and appoint from the date of publication of this Notification in
the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette as the date on which
the Scheme as settled shall come into operation.
SCHEDULE
1. The Endowment Fund shall be called as
вЂњDr.Anton Goldwin Fernandez Gold MedalвЂќ for the best
outgoing student of MBBS final year from the Kanyakumari
Medical College in the memory of Dr.Anton Goldwin
Fernandez, by St.Mary Health Educational and Research
Trust, Nagercoil.
2. The Dean, Government Kanyakumari Medical College
Asaripallam, Kanyakumari shall be the administrator of the Fund
and shall deposit the amount furnished by the donor St.Mary
Health Educational and Research Trust, Nagercoil with the
Treasurer of Charitable Endowments, Government of Tamil
Nadu, Chennai. Who will invest or reinvest the amount and
proceeds in Government securities to the best interest of the
Fund.
3. The interest accrued annually shall be the utilized for
purchase of Gold Medal every year and it shall be given to
the best outgoing final year student of MBBS every year on
the College Day.
4. In future, if the interest generated is not enough to make
a Gold Medal, the authorities may inform to the Trust to enable
them to contribute more funds for making the
Gold Medal.
5. The student shall be a regular student and he shall earn
more than 85% attendance in all the subjects.
6. The best outgoing student shall be selected by the Board
of Examiners and its Chairman and recommended by the
Dean, Kanyakumari Medical College, Kanyakumari. The
recommendation shall be duly signed by the Chairman and
Examiners and endorsed by the Dean, Kanyakumari Medical
College, Kanyakumari.
7. The Managing Trustee of St. Mary Health Educational
and Research Trust shall be one of the Members of
Dr. Anton Goldwin Fernandez Gold Medal Committee of
Kanyakumari Medical College.
J.RADHAKRISHNAN,
Secretary to Government.

Whereas complaints have been received from a number of
depositors that Messers Green Life Farms & Poultry, Erode
District, a financial establishment, has defaulted the return of
deposits made by the depositors after maturity;
And Wheresas, the Government are satisfied that the said
financial establishment is not likely to return the deposits to the
depositors and hence, the Government have to protect the
interests of such depositors;
And Whereas, proposal for attachment of property worth
Rs.28,68,080 were earlier identified and Government have
issued ad-interim order attaching the properties of the said
financial establishment vide Government Order first read
above.
And Whereas, the movable and imovable properties
specified in the Schedule to this order are alleged to have
been procured by the said financial establishment from and
out of the deposits collected from the depositors;
Now, Therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by
Section 3 of the Tamil  Nadu Protection of Interests of
Depositors (in Financial Establishments) Act, 1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 44 of 1997), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby makes an ad-interim order attaching the movable and
immovable properties standing in the name of the partners of
Messers Green Life Farms & Poultry, Erode District and other
persons specified in the Schedule to this order and transfers
the control over the said movable and immovable properties to
the Competent Authority, namely, the Competent Authority and
District  Revenue Officer, Erode District appointed under the
said Act.
2. The Competent Authority and  District Revenue Officer,
Erode District is requested to pursue further action in
accordance with the procedure laid down in sub-sections (3)
and (4) of Section 4 of the said Act and also the formalities
prescribed in the Tamil Nadu Protection of Interests of
Depositors (in Financial Establishments) Rules, 1997.
3. The Special Public Prosecutor, Special Court for the
Tamil Nadu Protection of Interests of Depositors (in Financial
Establishments) Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 44 of 1997)
Coimbatore District is requested to render necessary
assistance to the Competent Authority for filing the application
before the said Special Court.
(By order of the Governor)
