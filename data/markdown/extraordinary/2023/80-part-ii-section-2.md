CONSTITUTION OF DISTRICT PLANNING COMMITTEE IN CHENGALPATTU, RANIPET, TIRUPATHUR, 
KALLAKURICHI AND TENKASI DISTRICTS UNDER THE TAMIL NADU PANCHAYATS ACT, 1994
[G.O. Ms. No. 22, Rural Development and Panchayat Raj (PR.II), 7th March 2023,

No. II(2)/RDPR/225(b-1)/2023.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 241 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994 
(Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby constitutes District Planning Committees for the following 
Districts, namely:—
	
(1) Chengalpattu.
	
(2) Ranipet.
	
(3) Tirupathur.
	
(4) Kallakurichi.
	
(5) Tenkasi.
