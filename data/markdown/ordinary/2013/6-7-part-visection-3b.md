Election of Two members to the Syndicate, Four members to the Senate and Two members to the Standing
Committee of the Academic Council by the Academic Council.
(No.V.1/ACElection/01-2013/698.)
No.VI/3(b)/4/2013.
It is hereby notified that the election to [i] THE SYNDICATE, [ii] THE SENATE and [iii] THE STANDING COMMITTEE
OF THE ACADEMIC COUNCIL will be held under Section 18[a]-Other Members [2], Section 14[a]-Other Members [2] and Section
24[k] of the Madras University Act,1923.
[i] To the Syndicate-Section 18[a]-Other Members [2]
TWO MEMBERS to be elected by the Academic Council from among its members of one shall be teacher of affiliated colleges
and one shall  be a Teacher of Approved Colleges.
The person getting elected will hold office as a member of the Syndicate for the residual period / full term (three years) from
the date of receipt of nomination/ the withdrawal / the period of present member term to be ceased / the date of election whichever
is applicable for the election, as the case may be.
[ii] To the Senate-Section 14[a]-Other Members [2]
Four Members to be elected by the Academic Council from among its members.
[iii] To the Standing Committee of the Academic Council [Section 24[k]
TWO MEMBERS viz., One Member shall be member of the Syndicate and One Member shall be the Principal or Teacher
of Affiliated Colleges.
The newly elected members will hold office as a member of the Standing Committee of the  Academic Council for the residual
period from the date of election/the date of withdrawal/the date of election/the date of declaration of results whichever is applicable
for the election.
Nomination forms are available in the Registrar’s Office. Nomination should be sent so as to be received by the Registrar,
