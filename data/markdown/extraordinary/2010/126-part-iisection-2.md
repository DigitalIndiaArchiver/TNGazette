FINANCE DEPARTMENT
(Salaries)
ENTRUSTMENT OF THE IMPROVEMENT WORK OF AUDITORIUMS RELATED TO WORLD TAMIL CLASSICAL LANGUAGE
CONFERENCE TO CODISSIA INTEC TECHNOLOGY CENTRE, COIMBATORE UNDER THE TAMIL NADU
TRANSPARENCY IN TENDERS ACT, 1998.
[G.O. No. 125, Finance (Salaries), 30th April 2010, Chithirai 17, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No. II(2)/FIN/232(c)/2010.
Under clause (f) of Section 16 of the Tamil Nadu Transparency in Tenders Act, 1998 (Tamil Nadu Act 43 of 1998), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notifies the entrustment of the improvement work of auditoriums and formation of mini
auditoriums for the conduct of World Tamil Classical Language Conference to Coimbatore District Small Scale Industries
Association (CODISSIA) Intec Technology Centre, Coimbatore, Subject to the conditions laid down in para 9 of the G.O.
(Ms.) No. 119, Tamil Development, Religious Endowments and Information Department, dated 13th April 2010.
R. THIAGARAJAN,
Special Secretary to Government.
