CO-OPERATION, FOOD AND CONSUMER PROTECTION DEPARTMENT
Ad hoc Rules relating to the Temporary Post of  Registrar, State Consumer Disputes
Redressal Commission in Tamil Nadu General Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 241, Co-operation, Food and Consumer Protection, 29th June 2001.]
No. SRO B-77/2015.
In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby makes the following rules:—
2. The rules hereby made shall be deemed to have  come  into force on the 7th October, 1991.
RULES.
The General Rules applicable to the holders of the permanent post in the Tamil Nadu General Service, shall apply to the
holder of the temporary post of Registrar, State Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission, Chennai, subject to the
modifications specified in the following rules:—
2. Constitution:- The post shall constitute a distinct class in the said, service.
3. Appointment:- Appointment to the post shall be made as follows:—
(i) by recruitment by transfer from Category 5 of Division-I-Assistant Registrar—in the Chennai High Court, Service with
experience for a period of not less than two years in the said category; or (ii) from Category-I-Subordinate Judge in the
Tamil Nadu State Judicial  Service.
4. Appointing Authority:- The appointing authority for the post shall be the Government in consultation with the
Chief Justice, High Court, Chennai.
5. Qualification:- No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post unless he possesses a B.L. degree.
6.  Probation:- Every person appointed to the post from Category-5-Assistant Registrar in the Chennai High Court Service
shall, from the date on which he joins duty, be on probation for a total period of one year on duty with a continuous period
of two years.
7. Pay:- There shall be paid to the holder of the post a monthly pay calculated in the Pre-Revised Scale of
Rs. 3,700-125-4700-150-5000.
8. Savings:- Nothing contained in these rules shall adversely affect any person appointed to the post prior to the date of
Publication of the Rules in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
  LAKSHMI PRANESH,
  Secretary to Government.
