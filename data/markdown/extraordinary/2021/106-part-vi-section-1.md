NOTICE UNDER SECTION 15 (1) OF TAMIL NADU HIGHWAYS ACT, 2001 (TN ACT 34 OF 2002) 
READ WITH TAMIL NADU ACT 38 OF 2019
No.VI(1)/56(a)/2021.  
The Government of Tamil Nadu having  been satisﬁ ed that the lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below are acquired 
under Tamil Nadu Road Sector Project-II  for Highways purpose for the construction of Road over bridge in Highways 
at km 0/2-4 of Thirumangalm Municipality Road Over Bridge inlieu of existing L.C No 377 at Railway  km 512/400-
500 in between Thirumangalam and kallikudi railway station  under the provisions of Tamil Nadu Highways Act 2001 
Thirumangalam and Vadakarai Town, Thirumangalam Taluk, Madurai District and it having already been decided that the 
entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands to be paid out of the funds  controlled and  managed by 
the Divisional Engineer(H), Madurai, Tamil Nadu after having considering the cause shown by the owner or other persons 
having interest on said lands as the case may do hereby publish the following notice is issued under sub-section (1) 
of section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (TN Act 38/2019).
NOTICE
Under  sub-section (1)  of section 15 of the Tamil  Nadu  Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 38/2019) the 
Governor  of  Tamil Nadu  hereby  notiﬁ ed  that  the Dry & Ryot manai and Ryot Punjai lands  speciﬁ ed in the 
schedule below and measuring to an extent of 8399.5 Sq.Mrts of lands are acquired in  Thirumangalam and Vadakarai 
Town, Thirumangalam Taluk, Madurai District to be the same, a little more or less needed for Highways  purpose, to 
construction of Road over bridge in Highways at km 0/2-4 of Thirumangalm Municipality Road Over Bridge inlieu of 
existing L.C No 377 at Railway  km 512/400-500 in between Thirumangalam and kallikudi railway station  under the 
provisions of Tamil Nadu Highways Act 2001.
The plan of the lands under acquisition is kept in the oﬃ  ce of the District Revenue oﬃ  cer, Madurai may be inspected 
at any time during the oﬃ  ce hours.
