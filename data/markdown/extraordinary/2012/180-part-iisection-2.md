No. II(2)/TDREI/328(a)/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Hindu
Religious and Charitable Endowments (Amendment) Act, 2012 (Tamil Nadu Act 26 of 2012), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the 27th  day of June 2012, as the date on which the
said Act shall come into force.
M. RAJARAM,
Secretary to Government.
