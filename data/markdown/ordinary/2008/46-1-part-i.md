PUBLIC DEPARTMENT
(Special-A)
Secretariat, 26th November 2008.
No. I/PUSP/40/2008.
[Courts and Judges—District Judges—Thiru E.M.K.S. Siddharthar,
District Judge of Puducherry Judicial Service—Repatriation
from the Tamil Nadu Judicial Service to Puducherry
Judicial Service—Orders—Issued.]
The following Government Order is published :—
[G.O. (D) No. 160, Public (Special-A), 6th November 2008,
Aippasi 21, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2039.]
READ :
1. G.O. (D) No. 968, Home (Courts-IA) Department,
dated 30th October 2000.
2. From the Registrar General, High Court of Madras
D.O. Letter No. 10193/2008-B2, dated 29th October
2008.
ORDER :  No. 160, Public (Special-A), 6th November 2008.
The Government have considered the recommendation of the
High Court of Madras to repatriate Thiru E.M.K.S. Siddharthar,
District Judge of Puducherry Judicial Service, functioning on
deputation in the Tamil Nadu State Judicial Service, as Sessions
Judge, Mahalir Neethimandram, Madurai, to his parent department
viz., Puducherry Judicial Service for being posted in the
Union Territory of Puducherry.
2. The Government accordingly direct that Thiru
E.M.K.S. Siddharthar, District Judge of Puducherry Judicial Service
functioning as Sessions Judge, Mahalir Neethimandram, Madurai
be repatriated to his parent department, viz., Puducherry Judicial
Service for being posted in the Union Territory of Puducherry.
(By Order of the Governor)
K.S. SRIPATHI,
Chief Secretary.
