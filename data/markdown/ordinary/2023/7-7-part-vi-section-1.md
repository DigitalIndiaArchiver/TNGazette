Constitution of a District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court at Ponnamaravathy in Pudukkottai District
(Roc.No. 1347/A/2014/G./Judn.)
No. VI(1)/114/2023.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873) the 
High Court, Madras, hereby fixes Ponnamaravathy in Pudukkottai District as the place at which the District Munsif-cum-
Judicial Magistrate Court,  Ponnamaravathy, shall be located. 	
NOTIFICATION-II 
(Roc.No. 1347/A/2014/G./Judn.)
No. VI(1)/115/2023.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 11 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873) the 
High Court, Madras, hereby directs that in Pudukkottai District, the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Thirumayam 
shall cease to have local jurisdiction over the entire Ponnamaravathy Taluk and the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate 
Court, Ponnamaravathy shall have and exercise local jurisdiction over the entire Taluk of Ponnamaravathy with effect from 
the date on which the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate, Ponnamaravathy assumes charge of that Court. 
NOTIFICATION-III
(Roc.No. 1347/A/2014/G./Judn.)
No. VI(1)/116/2023.
The High Court, Madras hereby directs and notifies that the District Munsif-curn-Judicial Magistrate, Ponnamaravathy 
in Pudukkottai District shall exercise all the powers conferred on a District Munsif under Section 12 of the Tamil Nadu Civil 
Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873) as amended up-to-date in regard to original suits and proceedings not otherwise 
exempted from his cognizance of which the amount or value of the subject matter does not exceed One Lakh Rupees within 
the local limits of his jurisdiction with effect from the date on which the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate, Ponnamaravathy 
assumes charge of that Court.
