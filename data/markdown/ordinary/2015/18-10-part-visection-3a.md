TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI
Extension of Period of Direct Management of Madrasa Islamia Waqf, Mahfuz Khan Garden,
George Town, Chennai District.
(Rc. No. 2073/2011/B9)
No. VI-3(a)/16/2015.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act 1995, the Tamil Nadu
Wakf Board in its resolution In item No. 58/14 in Rc. No. 2073/11/B9/CHN, dated 05-03-2015 has resolved to ratify
the period of direct management of Madrasa Islamia Waqf, Mahfuz Khan Garden, George Town, Chennai from
26-08-2009 to 04-03-2015 and to extend the period of direct management of the Wakf for a further period of six months
from the date of Board's Order (05-03-2015).
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED MEERAN,
29th April 2015.
District Revenue Officer /
Chief Executive Officer (In-charge),
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
Extension of Period of Direct Management of Neelam Basha Dargah, Triplicane, Chennai District.
(Rc. No. 10720/2009/B6)
No. VI-3(a)/17/2015.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act 1995, the Tamil Nadu
Wakf Board in its resolution In item No. 3/15 in Rc. No. 10720/09/B6/CHN, dated 05-03-2015 has resolved to ratify
the period of direct management of Neelam Basha Dargah, Triplicane, Chennai from 11-08-2014 to 04-03-2015 and
to extend the period of direct management of the Wakf for a further period of six months from the date of Board's
Order (05-03-2015).
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED MEERAN,
