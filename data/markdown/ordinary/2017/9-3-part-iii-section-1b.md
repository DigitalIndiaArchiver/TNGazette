No. SRO B-12/2017.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Article 318 of the Constitution of India, the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission Regulations, 1954
(Volume-I of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1987).
2. The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 9th August 2011.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Regulations,—
(1) after regulation 7-C, the following regulation shall be inserted, namely:-
    “7-D. The Chairman and the Members of the Commission shall be under the purview of the State Vigilance
Commission and Director of Vigilance and Anti-Corruption.”;
(2) in Annexure II, in rule 7-A, the expression “except the Chairman and Members of the Commission,” shall be
omitted.
S. SWARNA,
Secretary to Government.
