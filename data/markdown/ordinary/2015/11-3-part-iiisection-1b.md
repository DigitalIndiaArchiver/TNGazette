In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the  Notification issued with the Backward Classes and Most
Backward Classes Welfare Department Notification No. SRO B-89/97, published at page 92 of Part III—Section 1 (b) of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 16th July 1997.
2. The Amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on and from the 5th August 1993.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Notification,—
(1) in the preamble, for the expression, “Section 82 in Volume- II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1969”, the
following expression shall be substituted, namely:—
“Class LXXXIA in Section 16, Volume II of Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1969.”;
(2) for the heading “SECTION 82 THE TAMIL NADU BACKWARD CLASSES AND MOST BACKWARD CLASSES
WELFARE SERVICE” the following heading shall be substituted, namely:—
“Class LXXXIA - THE TAMIL NADU BACKWARD CLASSES AND MOST BACKWARD CLASSES WELFARE
SERVICE”.

In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu General Service
(Section 16 of Part III-A in Volume II of the Tamil Nadu Service Manual, 1969).
2. The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on and from the 5th August 1993.
AMENDMENT
In the said Special rules, in Part I, for the expression, “Class LXXXI: Tamil Nadu Backward Classes and Most Backward
Classes Welfare Service - Post of District Backward Classes and Minorities Welfare Officers - Special Rules - Issued.
(BC & MBCW Department, C.No. 3940/BC I/94)”, the following expression shall be substituted, namely:—
“Class LXXXIA : Tamil Nadu Backward Classes and Most Backward Classes Welfare Service - Post of District Backward
Classes and Minorities Welfare Officers - Special Rules- Issued. (BC & MBCW Department, C.No. 3940/BC I/94)”
