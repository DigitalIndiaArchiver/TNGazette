JUDICIAL NOTIFICATION 
In consonance with the instruction issued by The Hon'ble High Court, Madras in its Notification No. 294/2021 in 
R.o.c. No. 78555-A/2021/C3, Dated:07.12.2021 it is hereby notified that the Criminal Court only of Thiruvarur District 
the following days are declared as holidays for Criminal Courts of Thiruvarur District during the Year 2022. 
(D. No. 02/2022)
No.V(1)/60/2022.
The Holidays for all the Subordinate Courts in the State of Tamil Nadu, for the year, 2022 are as follows:- 
(a) All Sundays are Holidays for all the Subordinate Courts including Family Courts functioning in the State of Tamil Nadu. 
(b) The following 6 Saturdays are working days for all the Subordinate Courts including Family Courts functioning in the 
State of Tamil Nadu. 
22.01.2022, 05.03.2022, 23.04.2022, 06.08.2022, 03.09.2022 and 15.10.2022. 
(c) Except the above Saturdays, all other Saturdays are holidays for all the Subordinate Courts, except Family Courts. 
(d) All the Family Courts Functioning in the State of Tamil Nadu shall work on 1st and 3rd Saturdays of every month 
except those Saturdays which are declared as Public Holidays. All other Saturdays are declared as holidays, except 22.01.2022 
and 23-04-2022 which are declared as working days.
