Ad hoc Rules relating to Tamil Nadu Technical Educational Subordinate Service Government Engineering Colleges.
[G.O. Ms. No. 145, Higher Education (B1), dated 21st May 2009, Vaigasi 7, Thiruvalluvar Aandu 2040.]
No. SRO B-40/2009.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India
and in supersession of the rules published with the Education Department Notification No. S R O B-648/78, dated the
27th November 1978, at pages 806-811 of Part III—Section 1 (b) of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the
20th December 1978, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following rules:—
The rules hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force with effect from 27th June 1988.
RULES
The General and Special Rules applicable to the holders of the permanent posts in Class I of the Tamil Nadu
Technical Educational Subordinate Service shall apply to the holders of the temporary post of Air Condition Plant Operator
Grade-I in the Government Colleges of  Engineering and Technology under the control of the Director of Technical
Education, Tamil Nadu.
2.
Constitution.—The post shall constitute a distinct Category in the said Class of the said Service.
3.
Appointment.—Appoint to the post shall be made by.—
(i)
promotion from any post in the Services on a lower scale of pay; or
(ii) transfer from any other Category or Class in the Service; or
(iii) recruitrment by transfer from any other service; or
(iv) direct recruitment.
4.
Appointing authority.—The appointing authority for the post shall be the Additional Director of Technical Education.
5.
Qualifications:—
(a) Age.—No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post by direct recruitment, if he has completed or
will complete 36 years of age on the first day of July of the year in which the selection for appointment is made:
Provided that the age limit shall be increased by five years in the case of candidates belonging to Schedule
Castes and Scheduled Tribes who possess a general educational qualification which is not higher than the Minimum
General Educational qualification.
(b) Other Qualifications.—No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post unless he possesses the
following qualifications, namely:—
(1) (a) (i)
A Diploma in Refrigeration and Air-Conditioning; or
(ii) A Diploma in Mechancial Engineering with Refrigeration and Air Conditioning as elective; and
(b) Practical experience in repair, overhauling, reconditioning and maintenance of different types of refrigeration
and air-conditioning systems for a period of not less than one year in a recognized workshop; or
(2) (i)
An Industrial Training Institute Certificate or National Trade Certificate or National Apprenticeship Training
Certificate in Refrigeration and Air-Conditioning Mechanic Trade; and
(ii) Practical experience in repair, overhauling, reconditioning and maintenance of difference types of refrigeration
and air-conditioning equipments for a period of not less than three years in a recognised workshop.
Explanation.—For purpose of these rules,—
(1) the expression “A Diploma” shall mean a diploma awarded by the State Board of Technical Education and
Training, Tamil Nadu;
(2) the expression “A trade certificate” shall mean a trade certificate awarded by the Government of India or the
Director of Industries and Commerce or the Director of Employment and Traning, Chennai;

(3) the expression “A recognized workshop” shall mean a workshop satisfying the definition of “Factory” in Section
2(n) of the Factories Act, 1948 Central Act LXIII of 1948) and includes a workshop attached to a Technical Educational
Institution;
(4) Practical experience prescribed above should have been acquired after obtaining the diploma certificate
prescribed for the post.
6.
Probation.—(a) Every person appointed to the post by direct recruitment shall from the date on which he joins duty,
be on probation for a total period of two years on duty within a continuous period of three years.
(b) Every person appointed to the post by direct recruitment by transfer shall from the date on which he joins
duty, be on probation for a total period of one year on duty within a continuous period of two years.
7.
Pay.—There shall be paid to the holder of the post a monthly pay calculated in the scale of
Rs. 1200-30-1560-40-2040:
Provided that with effect on and from the 1st January 1996 the monthly pay shall be calculated in the scale
Rs. 4000-100-6000.
8.
Savings.—Nothing contained in these rules shall adversely affect any person holding the post on the date of
issue of these rules.
K. GANESAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
