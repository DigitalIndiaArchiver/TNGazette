by clause (b) of sub-section (1) of Section 9 of the Indian

of Tamil Nadu for the period from 1st April 2014 to 30th June
2014.

HOME DEPARTMENT
Remission of the irrecoverable Balance Amount of Arrears
of Tax Payable in respect of the contract carriage for
certain period under the Tamil Nadu Motor Vehicles
Taxation Act.
[G.O. Ms. No. 281, Home (Tr.I), 10th April 2014.]
No.II(2)/HO/325/2014.—In exercise of the powers conferred
by clause (1) of Section 20-A of the Tamil Nadu Motor Vehicles
Taxation Act, 1974 (Tamil Nadu Act 13 of 1974), the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby remits a sum of Rs. 25,935/- (Rupees
Twenty five thousand nine hundred and thirty five only) being
the irrecoverable balance amount of arrears of tax payable
under the said Act, in respect of the contract carriage bearing
Registration No. KA01A5656 for the period from 1-10-2001 to
31-12-2001.
This order does not require the concurrence of Finance
Department vide its G.O. Ms. No. 519, dated
29-09-1997.
Constitution of Two Special Courts, at Dharmapuri for
Dharmapuri District as Motor Accident Claims Tribunals
under the Motor Vehicles Act.
[G.O. Ms. No. 290, Home (Tr.IV), 22nd April 2014.]
No.II(2)/HO/326/2014.—In exercise of the powers conferred
by sub-section (1) of Section 165 of the Motor Vehicles Act,
1988 (Central Act 59 of 1988), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby constitutes two Motor Accidents Claims Tribunals at
Dharmapuri for Dharmapuri District.
NOTIFICATION-II
[G.O. Ms. No. 290, Home (Tr.IV), 22nd April 2014, Chithirai
8, Jaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2045.]
No.II(2)/HO/327/2014.—Under sub-section (2) of Section 165
of the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988 (Central Act 59 of 1988), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the Additional District
Judge to deal with Motor Accidents Claims Original Petition
cases at Dharmapuri as the member of the Motor Accidents
Claims Tribunal at Dharmapuri and also appoints the Special
Subordinate Judge to deal with Motor Accidents Claims Original
Petition cases at Dharmapuri as the Members of another Motor
Accidents Claims Tribunal, at Dharmapuri in Dharmapuri
District.
