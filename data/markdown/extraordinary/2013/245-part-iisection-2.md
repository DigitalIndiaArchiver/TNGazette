The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied
that the lands specified in the schedule below have to be
acquired for industrial purpose to wit to SIPCOT Industrial
Park at Pellakuppam Village, Tindivanam Taluk, and it having
already been decided that the entire amount of
compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid out
of the funds controlled or managed by the Government of
Tamil Nadu.  The following Notice is issued under
sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTIFICATION
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil
Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
acquires the lands specified in the schedule below and
measuring 17.47.5 hectares to the same, a little more or
less needed for Industrial Purpose, to wit, for SIPCOT
Industrial Park at Tindivanam.
The plan of the lands are kept in the Office of the Special
Tahsildar (Land Acquisition), Unit I, SIPCOT, Tindivanam,
Villupuram District and may be inspected at any time during
office hours.
