The Governor of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that the
lands specified in the Schedule below are required for the
purpose of the construction of Road Over Bridge in lieu of
existing Railway Level Crossing No.47 in between
Guduvancherry and Singaperumal Koil Railway Station and its
having already been decided that the entire amount of
compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid out of
the funds controlled and managed by the Chief Engineer
(Projects) Highways Department,Chennai-15 and after having
considered the cause shown by the owner or other person
having interest in the said lands, as the case may be do
hereby publish the following Notice under sub-Section (1) of
Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act,2001
(Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002)
NOTICE
Under sub-section(1) of Section 15 of Tamil Nadu Highways
Act,2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands specified in the schedule
below and measuring 25244 square meters to the same,a little
more or less needed for the purpose of the construction of
Road Over Bridge in lieu of existing Railway Level Crossing
No.47  between Guduvancherry and Singaperumal Koil Railway
Stations.
 The plan of the lands is kept in the office of the Special
Tahsildar (Land Acquisition) Inner Ring Road Scheme, Alandur
Municipality Campus, Alandur,   Chennai-16.
