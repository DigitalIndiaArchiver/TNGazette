GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
Final Closing and Cancellation of Registration of  Ind. No. 1/1997. The Rajiv Gandhi Auto Drivers Industrial
Co-operative Society Ltd.
(LF. 1061/ICA/2001)
No.VI(1)/113/2016.
“In the circumstances stated by the Assistant Director of Industries and Commerce (Industrial Co-operatives),
District Industries Centre, Sivaganga/Official Liquidator of the Rajiv Gandhi Auto Drivers Industrial Cooperative Society
Ltd. Ind. No. 1/1997 in the final closure proposals dated 20-2-2016 and in exercise of the powers delegated under
Section 140(1) of Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act, 1983, the registration of the Rajiv Gandhi Auto
Drivers Industrial Co-operative Society Limited. Ind. No. 1/1997 has been ordered to be cancelled and the affairs
have been finally closed with effect from 3-3-2016 vide the Proceedings No. LF. 1061/ICA/2001 dated 3-3-2016 of
Additional Chief Secretary/Industries Commissioner and Director of Industries and Commerce and Registrar of
Industrial Co-operatives, Chennai-32.”
Chennai-600 032,
AMBUJ SHARMA,
3rd March 2016.

Director of Industries and Commerce and
Registrar of Industrial Cooperatives.
Confirmation of Variation to the Approved Extension of Thiruthangal Road - Pallapatti Road North Detailed
Development Plan of Sivakasi Local Planning Area.
(Roc. No. 26546/2008/DP1)
No. VI(1)/114/2016.
1. In exercise of the powers conferred Under Section 33(2) of Town and Country Planning Act 1971, the
Director of Town and Country Planning, Chennai-2 hereby Confirms the Variation to the approved “Extension of Thiruthangal
Road - Pallapatti Road North Detailed Development Plan” of Sivakasi Local Planning Authority, varied by Director of Town
and Country Planning Proceedings in Roc. No. 26546/2008/DP-2, dt. 23-05-2011 and was published in Tamil Nadu
Government Gazette No. 22, Part-VI, Section 1, Page No. 251 dt. 15-06-2011 (Publication No. VI(1)/256/2011)
2. Since no objections and suggestions have been received on this draft notification within the Stipulated time, the
same are here by Confirmed and Ordered as below:-
CONFIRMATION OF VARIATION
1. The entire “Extension of Thiruthangal Road - Pallapatti Road North Detailed Development Plan” of sivakasi Local
Planning Authority, Varied by the Director of Town and Country Planning Proceedings in Roc. No. 26546/2008/DP-2,
dated 23-05-2011 under section 33(1) of Town and Country Planning Act 1971 is hereby Confirmed under section 33(2)
of Town and Country Planning Act 1971.
2. The varied plan is numbered as Map No. 5/DDP(V)/DTCP. No. 08/2011.
3. The Detailed Development Plan clauses and schedules annexed with this plan are made enforceable from the date
of Publication of this notification in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.

JUDICIAL NOTIFICATIONS
Constitution of a Judicial Magistrate Court at Kumbakonam by converting the II Additional District
Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Kumbakonam.
(Roc. No. 2826/A/2010/G4)
No. VI(1)/116/2016.
Whereas the Government of Tamil Nadu have issued orders in G.O.Ms.No. 14, Home (Cts.II) Department dated
06-01-2016 that II Additional District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court at Kumbakonam be converted as Judicial
Magistrate Court at Kumbakonam.
Hence, it is hereby informed that on and from the date on which the II Additional District Munsif-cum-Judicial
Magistrate Court No. I, Kumbakonam converted as Judicial Magistrate Court, Kumbakonam, the nomenclature of the
II Additional District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court No. I, Kumbakonam shall be changed as the Judicial
Magistrate Court No. I, Kumbakonam.
High Court, Madras,
P. KALAIYARASAN,
4th March 2016.
Registrar-General.
