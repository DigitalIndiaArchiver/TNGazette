Under Sub-Statute (1) of Statue 5 of Chapter XXI of the General Statutes of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University,
Chennai, it is hereby published that the following members have been elected to the Senate of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R.
Medical University, Chennai, Election of One Professor to the Senate by the Teachers of each affiliated Medical College from
among themselves under Section 18(3) (a) of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai Act, 1987 (Tamil Nadu
Act 37 of 1987).
The membership will be for a period of three years as noted against each or until they hold the post of Professor of
the concerned affiliated College of this University, or till the date of their superannuation whichever is earlier.

Election of One Nominee by the Members of the Senate to the Vice-Chancellor Search Committee for
recommending a Panel of Names for Appointment as Vice-Chancellor.
[Lr. No. EI(1)/20031/2012.]
No. VI-3(b)/11/2012.
Under Statute 7 of Chapter V of the General Statutes of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai and
as directed by the Governing Council of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai, the Registrar (FAC) of the
University hereby notifies that a permanent vacancy is about to occur in the office of the Vice-Chancellor with
effect from 11-12-2012 F.N. consequent on the expiry of the term of office of the present Vice-Chancellor,
Prof. Dr. MAYIL VAHANAN NATARAJAN.
