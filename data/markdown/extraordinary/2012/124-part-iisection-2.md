NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
COMMERCIAL TAXES AND REGISTRATION DEPARTMENT
  ALTERATION OF LIMITATION OF THE REGISTRATION SUB-DISTRICT OF NALLUR  SUB-REGISTRY TO PENNADAM
SUB-REGISTRY IN VIRUDHACHALAM REGISTRATION DISTRICT UNDER THE REGISTRATION ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 62, Commercial Taxes and Registration (G), 14th May 2012.]
No. II(2)/CTR/251(a)/2012.
Under sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Registration Act, 1908 (Central Act XVI of 1908), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
alters that with effect on and from the 1st June 2012, the village Narasingamangalam, bearing number 117,  which now
forms part of the Registration sub-district of Nallur in the Registration district of Virudhachalam be transferred to, and form
part of the Registration sub-district of Pennadam in the said Registration district.
2.
The limits of the said village shall be the limits which shall, from time to time, be determined for the purpose of
revenue administration.
SUNIL PALIWAL,
Secretary to Government.
