Amendments to the Adhoc Rules in respect of Ofﬁ cers of the Co-operative Audit Department.
[G.O. Ms. No. 268, Finance (Co-operative Audit), 6th August 2018, Ý® 21, M÷‹H, F¼õœÀõ˜ Ý‡´-2049.]
No. SRO B-69/2018.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, 
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the adhoc rules published with the Finance 
Department Notiﬁ cation No. S.R.O.B -222/87, dated the 26th June 1987, at pages 294 to 298 of Part Ill-Section I(b) of the 
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 22nd July 1987. 
2. The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 14th June 1994. 
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules, 
(1) in rule 6, in sub-rule (b), in the Table, in clause (b), against the post 2. Assistant Director of Co-operative Audit" in 
column (1) and the method of appointment "(ii) Recruitment by transfer" in column (2) thereof, for the entry "(ii) Departmental 
Test for Ofﬁ cers of the Co-operative Department in Co-operation, Auditing, Book-keeping and Banking and Rural Economics 
conducted by the Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission" in column (3), the following entry shall be substituted, namely:- 
"(ii) Departmental Test for Ofﬁ cers of the Co-operative Department in Co-operation, Auditing, Banking, Co-operative 
Management and Administration and Book-keeping conducted by the Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission;"; 
(2) in rule 9, in sub-rule (a), in clause (ii), for the expression "Co-operation, Auditing, Book-keeping and Banking and 
Rural Economics", the expression "Co-operation, Auditing, Banking, Co-operative Management and Administration and 
Book-keeping" shall be substituted.
  K. SHANMUGAM,
Additional Chief Secretary to Government.
