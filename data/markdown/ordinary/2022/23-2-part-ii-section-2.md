No. II(2)/HFW/442/2022.—In exercise of the powers 
conferred by Section 37 of the Tamil Nadu Homoeopathy 
System of Medicine and Practitioners of Homoeopathy Act, 
1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 5 of 1972), the Tamil Nadu Homoeopathy 
Medical Council makes the following amendment to the 
Tamil Nadu Homoeopathy Medical Council Regulations, 1973, 
with the previous sanction of the Government, namely:-
Amendment
In the said Regulations, for regulations 25, 25-A, 25-B 
the following regulations shall be substituted, namely:-
“25. No employees shall, ordinarily, be continued in 
service after he has attained the age of,-
(i).	62 years or 2 years after the age of superannuation 
ordered by the Government from time to time, in 
case of Registrar;
(ii)	60 years or the age of superannuation ordered by 
the Government from time to time in the case of 
other categories.
The Council shall maintain a record of the services of 
each of its employees recording therein his rank, emoluments, 
promotions, leave earned and availed of. All changes effected 
shall be attested by the President in the case of Registrar 
and in other cases by the Registrar.
25-A. Under extraordinary circumstances, the Registrar, 
if he is found physically fit, may be retained in service

Government, even after he attains 62 years of age or even 
after the completion of 2 years after the age of superannuation 
ordered by the Government from time to time.
25-B. Applications, if any, for extension shall be made 
by the employee sufficiently early and subject to his being 
physically and otherwise fit, the Council shall not, grant
