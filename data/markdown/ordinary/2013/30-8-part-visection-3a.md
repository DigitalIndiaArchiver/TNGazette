GENERAL  NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI
Extension of Direct Management of Masjid-E-Mahmood Wakf, Palavakkam, Kanchipuram District.
(Rc. No. 12987/06/B9/KPM.)
No. VI-3(a)/126/2013.
In continuation of the Board Resolution in Item No. 29/10, R.C.No. 12987/B9/Chennai, dated 30th March 2010 the period
of Direct Management of Masjid-E-Mahmood Wakf, Palavakkam, Kanchipuram District has been extended upto 19th June
2014 by the Chief Executive Officer, Tamil Nadu Wakf Board, in item No. 61/13 R.C.No. 12987/06/B9/KPM, dated 20th June
2013.
Chennai-600 001,
F. ABDUL RAZICK,
1st July 2013.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
Extension of Direct Management of Bawanathar Mosque Wakf (Malaikovil Mosque),
 Thiruvarambur, Tiruchy Taluk and District.
(Rc. No. 2510/11/B5/TRY.)
No. VI-3(a)/127/2013.
In continuation of the Board Resolution in Item No. 196/2001, WEA.12/2001/E5/TRY dated 5th February 2004, the
period of Direct  Management of Bawanathar Mosque Wakf,  Malaikovil, Thiruvarambur, Tiruchy District has been
extended upto 30th June 2014 by the Chief Executive Officer, Tamil Nadu Wakf Board, in item No. 62/13
R.C.No. 2510/11/B5/TRY, dated 1st July 2013.
Chennai-600 001,
F. ABDUL RAZICK,
2nd July 2013.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
