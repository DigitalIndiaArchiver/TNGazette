 [G.O.Ms.No. 121, Labour and Employment(M2), 31st July 2012.]
No. SRO A-24/2012.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 112 of the Factories Act (Central Act LXIII of
1948), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu Factories Rules, 1950,
the draft of the same having been previously published as required by Section 115 of the said Act.
