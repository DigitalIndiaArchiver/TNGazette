HOME DEPARTMENT
(Courts-IV)
APPOINTMENT OF THIRU SUBRAMONIUM PRASAD ADVOCATE AS ADDITIONAL ADVOCATE GENERAL-VI OF
TAMIL NADU TO REPRESENT THE GOVERNMENT OF TAMIL NADU BEFORE THE SUPREME COURT OF INDIA.
[G.O. Ms. No. 800, Home (Courts-IV), 16 October 2012.]
No. I/HO/33(a)/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Article 165 and Article 162 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby appoints Thiru Subramonium Prasad, Advocate, to be the Additional Advocate General-VI of Tamil Nadu.
2.  The Additional Advocate General-VI of Tamil Nadu will look after the cases pertaining to the State of Tamil Nadu before
the Supreme Court of India, New Delhi in addition to Additional Advocate General-IV.
                                 R.  RAJAGOPAL,
