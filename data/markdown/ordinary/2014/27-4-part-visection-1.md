Government Gazette, dated the 9th November 1994.

(other than Corporation Area)” under the sub-heading (b) non Notified Detailed Development Plan Area (c) COIMBATORE

Variations to the New Town Development Plan of Hosur New Town Development Area.
(Roc. No. 1095/2013/HNTDA)
[G.O. Ms. (2D) No. 30, Housing and Urban Development (UD IV-1) Department, dated 25th February 2014.]
No. VI(1)/248/2014.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 32 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning
Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972) the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following variations to the
New Town Development Plan of the Hosur New Town Development Area Consented under this said Act and
published in the Housing and Urban Development Department Notification No. 12 at pages No. 159 and 160 of
Part VI—Section 1, of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 26th March 2003.
VARIATIONS
In the said Hosur New Town Development Plan under the heading “Industrial use zone” in Poonapalli Village and
Panchayat, the expression of S.F. Nos. 35/1, 35/1B1, 35/1B2, 45/1, 45/2, 45/3, and 45/4, with an extent of 1.30.0 hectares
(3.21 acres) shall be added, subject to the following conditions:—
1.
The building constructed if any, shall be removed in case of widening of the road in future according to the
traffic density and IRC guidelines as per the Divisional Engineer (Highways), Krishnagiri letter.
2.
The small odai in the  FMB sketch shall be maintained in the boosthithi as per the Hosur Tahsildar letter.
Under the heading, “Agricultural dry use zone, in Poonapalli Village and Panchayat the expression of S.F. No. 35/
1
,
35/1B1, 35/1B2, 45/1, 45/2, 45/3 and 45/4 with an extent of 1.30.0 hectares (3.21 acres) shall be deleted.
