In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby makes the following Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Information Technology Service (Section 52 in Volume II
of Tamil Nadu Services Manual).
RULES
1.
Short title and commencement.—
(a) These rules may be called the Tamil Nadu Information Technology Service Rules.
(b) They shall come into force on the  14th June  2017.

3A. Appointment by deputation.— Means appointment of temporary nature only for a limited period by the end of
which, the Officer will have to be reverted to the parent cadre/department:
Provided that under deputation, suitable Officers having requisite qualifications and experience working in other
departments of the State Government or Central Government or Quasi Government or Central Public Sector Undertakings
or State Public Sector Undertakings shall be considered for appointment.
3B. Tenure of deputation.— (1)The period of deputation shall be subject to maximum period of three years. However,
extension may be granted beyond the period of three years limit upto a period of one year, after obtaining orders of the
Government, in cases where such extension is considered necessary.
(2) The Director / Commissioner of e-Governance may extend the period of deputation in excess of the period
prescribed in the recruitment rules, where it is absolutely necessary subject to certain conditions which may be detailed
explicitly by the borrowing department.
4.
Reservation of Appointment.— The rule of reservation of appointment [Section 27 of the Tamil Nadu Government
Servants (Conditions of Service) Act, 2016 (Tamil Nadu Act No.14 of 2016)] shall apply to the post of Assistant System
Engineer and Assistant System Analyst appointed by direct recruitment.

6.
Qualifications.— Age. - No person shall be eligible for appointment to the category of Assistant System Engineer
/ Assistant System Analyst by direct recruitment, if he has not completed twenty one years of age and has completed or
will complete the age of thirty years on the 1st day of July of the year in which the selection for appointment is made:
Provided that the candidate belonging to MBCs / DCs / BCs / BC(M)s and DWs shall be eligible for appointment
to the category of Assistant System Engineer / Assistant System Analyst by direct recruitment, if he has not completed
or will complete 32 years of age on the 1st day of July of the year in which the selection for appointment is made:
Provided further that the candidate belonging to SCs, SC(A)s, STs shall be eligible for appointment to the category
of Assistant System Engineer / Assistant System Analyst by direct recruitment, if he has not completed or will complete
35 years of age on the 1st day of July of the year in which the selection for appointment is made.

8.
Probation. - Every person appointed to the post of Assistant System Engineer or Assistant System Analyst by direct
recruitment shall, from the date on which he joins duty, be on probation for a total period of two years on duty within
a continuous period of three years:
Provided that every person appointed to the category of Assistant System Engineer / Assistant System Analyst by direct
recruitment shall undergo the Foundational Training as prescribed by the Director / Commissioner of e-Governance and
pass the examinations conducted by the end of such training within the period of his probation.
9.
Preparation of annual list of approved candidates and crucial date. - (a) For the purpose of drawing up of
annual list of approved candidates for appointment to the post of Joint Director by promotion, the crucial date on which
the candidate should be qualified shall be the 1st day of April every year.
(b) For the purpose of drawing up of annual list of approved candidates for appointment to the post of Senior
System Engineer or Senior System Analyst by promotion, the crucial date on which the candidate should be qualified
shall be the 1st day of May every year.
