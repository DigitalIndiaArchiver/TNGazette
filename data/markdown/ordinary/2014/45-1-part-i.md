[G.O. Ms. No. 1003, Public (Special-A), 12th November
2014, Aippasi 26, Jaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2045.]
No.I/PUSP/39/2014.—The following Notification of the
Government of India, Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances
and Pensions Department of Personnel and Training,  New
Delhi, dated the 26th August 2014 is republished:—
In exercise of the powers conferred by Rule 6 (1) of the
Indian Administrative Service (Cadre) Rules, 1954, and with
the concurrence of the Government of Tamil Nadu and the
Government of Rajasthan, the Central Government hereby
accords approval for inter cadre deputation of Mr. Shankar Lal
Kumawat, I.A.S. (TN:10) from Tamil Nadu Cadre to
Rajasthan Cadre for a period of 3 years on personal
grounds.
[No.13017/02/2014-AIS(I)]
S.S. SHUKLA,
Under Secretary to Government of India.
(By Order of the Governor)
MOHAN VERGHESE CHUNKATH,
Chief Secretary.
