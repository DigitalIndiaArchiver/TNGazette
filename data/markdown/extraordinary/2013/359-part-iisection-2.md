AMENDMENT TO NOTIFICATION
In exercise of the powers conferred by Sections 131 and 133 of the Electricity Act, 2003 (Central Act 36
of 2003), read with clauses 9 and 12 of the Tamil Nadu Electricity (Reorganisation and Reforms) Transfer
Scheme, 2010, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the said Scheme:-
2.  The amendment hereby made shall come into force on the 1st May, 2013.
AMENDMENT
In the said Scheme, in clause 9, in sub-clause (1), for the expression “for a period of two years and six months”,
the expression “for a period of three years” shall be substituted.
