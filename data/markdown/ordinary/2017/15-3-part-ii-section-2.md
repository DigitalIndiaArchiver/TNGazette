shall be to evaluate the varieties of seeds and to release
these varieties. The tenure of office of the committee shall
be for a period of 5 years with effect from the date of issue

year to consider various matters falling within its purview.

more than one meeting in a year to consider very important
subjects if necessary. The proceedings of the committee
shall be submitted to the Government by the Director of
Agriculture immediately after every meeting. The head
quarters of the Committee shall be at Chennai.

Section 28 of the Water (Prevention and Control of
Pollution) Act, 1974 and Section 31 of Air (Prevention and
Control of Pollution) Act, 1981 provides that the State
Government may constitute a State Appellate Authority
consisting of one or three persons as it may think fit, to be
appointed to help in the adjudication of disputes
relating to Environment and Pollution.
2. In pursuance of the orders of the Supreme Court in
its Judgment in Civil Appeal No. 368-371 of 1999 of SLP
(Civil) No. 10317-10320/98 and Civil Appeal Nos. 372,
373/99 in SLP (Civil) No.10380 & 13380/98 the Authority has
been composed of a retired Judge of High Court as Chairman
and Experts / Scientists in the field of Pollution Control as
Members. Accordingly, the Appellate Authority for Tamil Nadu
was constituted vide G.O.Ms.No. 208, Environment and
Forests (EC.I) Department, dated 15-09-2000.
3. Accordingly, the Appellate Authority of Tamil Nadu
Pollution Control Board was reconstituted vide G.O.Ms.
No. 24, Environment and Forests (EC.I) Department,
dated 08-02-2007 and in G.O.Ms.No. 66, Environment and
Forests (EC.I) Department, dated 07-05-2013 orders have
been issued extending the tenure of the Appellate Authority
for a further period of three years from 19-02-2013 to
18-2-2016

experts/Scientists Members of the Appellate Authority for
Tamil Nadu Pollution Control Board.

(EC.I) Department, dated: 17-2-16 the tenure of the Appellate
Authority of Tamil Nadu Pollution Control Board was extended
for a further period of three years from 19-02-2016 to
18-02-2019. However, the tenure of the members of Appellate
Authority of Tamil Nadu Pollution Control Board was not
extended since the proposal in this regard was not sent to
the Government by the Tamil Nadu Pollution Control Board.
