The Government of Tamil Nadu announce with profound regret the death of Thiru Pranab Mukherjee, 
Former President of India on 31st August 2020 at Army Research and Referral Hospital.  State Mourning 
will be observed for seven days from 31st August 2020 to 6th September 2020 (both days inclusive). 
The National Flag will be ﬂ own at half mast on all buildings where it is ﬂ own regularly, during the period of 
mourning throughout the State. There will be no oﬃ  cial entertainment during this period.
