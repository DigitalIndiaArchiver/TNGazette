In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the
Tamil Nadu Forest Subordinate Service (Section 12 of Part III B of the Tamil Nadu Services
Manual 1970).
2. The amendments hereby made shall come into force with effect from the date
of issue of the order.
AMENDMENT
In the said Special Rules:—
In rule 3-C, under the heading “Selection of Forester, Forest Guards, Forest Guard with Driving
Licence and Forest Watcher by direct recruitment”,
for the words, “The eligible candidates shall attend physical and endurance tests as prescribed
in these rules, followed by written and oral tests. The selection shall be made in the order of physical
and endurance tests, written and oral tests. The candidates shall have to qualify themselves at every
stage before they move to the next stage”, thereof,
the following words shall be substituted namely:—
“Based on the written examination, the successful candidates will be called for
 appearing in walking test, physical standards verification and personality test for
 final selection”
In rule 3-D, under the heading “Physical standards and Endurance-test”, after the words,
“promotion from among the holders of the posts of Assistant Draughtsman”, thereof,
the following entries shall be inserted namely:-
“to the post of”
In rule 3-D, in sub-rule (a), under the heading “Physical Standards”, against the category
“Female Candidates” Chest (cms), Normal, thereof,
the following entries shall be substituted namely:-—
Chest (cms)
Normal
Female Candidates

In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Adhoc Rules published with the Information and
Public Relations Department.
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on 18th November 2014.
AMENDMENT
1) In the said G.O. Rule 5(b).—“Other Qualifications” in the table under column 1 against SI. No.1 “Mono Key Board
Operator” in the column 2 the following qualifications shall be substituted:-
“(i) Must have passed SSLC
(ii) Must possess a certificate in Desk Top Publishing (DTP) course conducted by Government Institute I
Government recognized Institute
(iii) Must possess practical experience in Layout Designing”.
3. This order issues with the concurrence of Personnel and Administrative Reforms Department vide its
U.O. No. 46206/Prl.Secy/2014, dated 5th December 2014.
