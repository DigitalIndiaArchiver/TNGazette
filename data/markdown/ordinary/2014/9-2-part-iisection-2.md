Notifications under the Minimum Wages Act.
Revision of Minimum rates of wages for employment in
Coaching Academics other than those run by
Government and local bodies under the Act.
[G.O. (2D) No.2, Labour and Employment (J1),
27th January 2014.]
No.II(2)/LE/242/2014.—In exercise of the powers conferred
by clause (b) of sub-section (1) of Section 3 and sub-section
(2) of Section 5 of the Minimum Wages Act, 1948 (Central
Act XI of 1948) and in supersession of the Labour and
Employment Department Notification No.II(2)LE/460/2003,
published on pages 263 and 264 of Part II-Section 2 of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 25th June 2003,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu, after consultation with the
Advisory Board, hereby revises the minimum rates of wages
payable to the classes of employees in the employment in
Coaching academics, that is to say, Tutorial Colleges,
Technical Institutes, Primary or Nursery Schools, English
Medium Schools run on commercial lines without Government
grant other than those run by Government and local bodies
as the case may be, in the State of Tamil Nadu, specified
in column (1) of the Schedule below, as specified in the
corresponding entries in column (2) thereof, the draft of the
same having been previously published as required by clause
(b) of sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the said Act.
2.
This Notification shall come into force with effect on
and from the date of its publication in the Tamil Nadu
Government Gazette.

No.II(2)/LE/244/2014.—Whereas, the Government of
Tamil Nadu are of the opinion that Minimum rates of wages
should be fixed under the Minimum Wages Act, 1948 (Central
Act XI of 1948) in respect of the Employment of Sweeping and
Cleaning excluding activities prohibited under the Employment
of Manual Scavengers and Construction of  Dry Latrines
(Prohibition) Act, 1993 (Central Act 46 of 1993) in the sphere
of this state.
2.
And Whereas, notice of their intention to add to Part I
of the Schedule to the Minimum Wages Act, 1948
(Central Act XI of 1948) the employment aforesaid, in exercise
of the powers conferred by Section 27 of the said Act, is
hereby given as required by that section of the said Act.
3.
Any objection or suggestion, from any persons or
organizations likely to be affected thereby received within
three months from the date of publication of this Notification
in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, will be considered
by the Government of Tamil Nadu. Objections and
Suggestions, if any, should be addressed to the Secretary
to Government of Tamil Nadu, Labour and Employment
Department, Secretariat, Chennai-600 009 through the
Commissioner of Labour, Teynampet, Chennai-600 006.
