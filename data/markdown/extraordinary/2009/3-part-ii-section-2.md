MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION AND WATER SUPPLY DEPARTMENT
DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF THE TAMIL NADU MUNICIPAL LAWS
(SEVENTH AMENDMENT) ACT, 2008.
[G.O. Ms. No. 2, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (Elec.), 2nd January 2009.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/(b)/2009.
 In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Municipal Laws
(Seventh Amendment) Act, 2008 (Tamil Nadu Act 57 of 2008), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints
the 2nd January 2009 as the date on which the said Act shall come into force.
NIRANJAN MARDI,
 Secretary to Government.
