No. I/EF/21/2013.—The following Notification No. 23012/04/2012-
IFS.II, dated 14th January 2013 of Goverment of India, Ministry of
Environment  and Forests, New Delhi is republished:—

(Tamil Nadu cadre) is permitted to change his name from V. Palani to
S.V. Pazhani and henceforth be known as S.V. Pazhani for
official purposes.
