The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that the lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below are acquired 
under Chennai – Kanniyakumari Industrial Corridor Project for Highways purpose to wit for Widening of SH-23,
Mayiladuthurai - Tiruvarur Road  at KM (31/850 to 32/806) in Moongilkudi (Additional)  Village in Nannilam Taluk, 
Tiruvarur  District, and it having already been decided that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for 
the lands to be paid out of the funds controlled and managed by the Divisional Engineer(H), Kumbakonam (CKICP), 
Tamil Nadu after having considering the cause shown by the owner or other persons having interest on such lands 
as the case may be do hereby publish the following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the
Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (TN Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
 
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) read 
with the Tamil Nadu Land Acquisition Laws (Revival of Operation, Amendment and Validation) Act, 2019, the Governor 
of Tamil Nadu hereby notiﬁ ed that the Dry and Wet lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below and measuring to an extent 
of 110 sq.mtr., are acquired in Moongilkudi (Additional)  Village in Nannilam Taluk, Tiruvarur   District, to the same, a 
little more or less is needed for Chennai-Kanniyakumari Industrial Corridor Project for State Highways purpose, to wit 
for Widening of SH-23, Mayiladuthurai - Tiruvarur Road  at KM (31/850 to 32/806).
