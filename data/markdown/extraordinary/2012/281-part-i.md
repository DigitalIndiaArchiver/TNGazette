APPOINTMENT OF MINISTER FOR THE STATE OF TAMIL NADU
[G.O. Ms. No. 848, Public (Special-B), 6th October  2012, Purattasi 20, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2043.]
No. I/PUSP/33(b-1) /2012.
I, K. Rosaiah, Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoint Thiru P. Mohan with effect from  the
Forenoon of 6th October 2012, to be the member of my Council of Ministers.
K. ROSAIAH,
Governor of Tamil Nadu.
ALLOCATION OF SUBJECTS AMONG MINISTERS
[G.O. Ms. No. 849, Public (Special-B), 6th October  2012, Purattasi 20,  Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2043.]
No. I/PUSP/ 33(b-2)/2012.
Under Clause (3) of Article 166 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby allocates the subjects
Rural Industries including Cottage Industries and Small  Industries to Thiru P. Mohan, appointed as a Minister in the
Government Order 6th read above. Consequent on the above allocation, Thiru P. Mohan be designated as Minister for Rural
Industries.
DEBENDRANATH  SARANGI,
Chief Secretary.
