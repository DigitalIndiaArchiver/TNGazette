In exercise of the powers conferred by Rule 11 of the Tamil Nadu Local Bodies (Election of Members to the District
Planning Committee) Rules, 1999, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission, for the purpose of filling up of one casual
vacancy in the office of member in the urban segment of the District Planning Committee, Tiruvarur District, hereby calls
upon the voters in the said urban segment to elect a member to the District Planning Committee, in accordance with the
following programme of election:—
