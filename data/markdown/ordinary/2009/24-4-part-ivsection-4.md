The following Act of Parliament received the assent of the President on the
16th March, 2009 and is hereby published for general information:—
THE CENTRAL INDUSTRIAL SECURITY FORCE (AMENDMENT) ACT, 2009
ACT  No. 22 OF 2009
An Act further to amend the Central Industrial Security Force Act, 1968.
BE it enacted by Parliament in the Sixtieth Year of the Republic of India as
follows:—
1.
(1)
This Act may be called the Central Industrial Security Force
(Amendment) Act, 2009.
(2)
It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 10th day of January,
2009.
2.
In the Central Industrial Security Force Act, 1968 (hereinafter referred to as
the principal Act), in section 2,—
(a)
after clause (ca), the following clause shall be inserted, namely:—
‘(cb) “joint venture” means a venture jointly undertaken by the Central
Government or State Government with private industrial undertaking;’;
Short title and
commencement.
Amendment of
section 2.
50 of 1968.
