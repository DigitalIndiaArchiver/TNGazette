The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that the lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below are acquired under 
Tamil Nadu Road Sector Project II for Highways purpose for Widening of SH-222, Omalur – Mecheri Road at KM (1/150 to 
14/320) Omalur, Pachanampatti and Karuppanampatti Villages in Omalur Taluk, Salem District and it having already been 
decided that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands to be paid out of the funds controlled and 
managed by the Divisional Engineer(H), Salem (TNRSP II), Tamil Nadu after having considering the cause shown by the 
owner or other persons having interest on such lands as the case may be do hereby publish the following notice is issued 
under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (TN Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
 
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) read 
with the Tamil Nadu Land Acquisition Laws (Revival of Operation Amendment and Validation) Act ,2019 the Governor of 
Tamil Nadu hereby notify that the Dry lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below and measuring to an extent of 4820 sq.mts. 
of Dry lands are acquired in Omalur, Pachanampatti and Karuppanampatti Villages in Omalur Taluk, Salem District, to the 
same, a little more or less needed for Tamil Nadu Road Sector Project II for State Highways purpose for Widening of SH-
222, Omalur – Mecheri Road at KM(1/150 to 14/320).
The plan of the lands under acquisition are kept in the oﬃ  ce of the Special District Revenue oﬃ  cer (Land Acquisition), 
Tamil Nadu Road Sector Project II, Salem and oﬃ  ce of the Special Tahsildar (Land Acquisition), Tamil Nadu Road Sector 
Project II in Salem, may be inspected at any time during the oﬃ  ce hours.
