ACQUISITION OF LANDS
(Roc. No. M1/24608/2013.)
No. VI(1)/417(a)/2013.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied
that the lands specified in the schedule below have to be
acquired for a Public Purpose and it having already been
decided that the entire amount of compensation to be
awarded for the lands is to be paid by the Public Works
Department.   The following declaration is issued under
section 6 of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894 (Central Act I of
1894).
DECLARATION
Under section 6 of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894 (Central
Act I of 1894), the Principal Secretary and Commissioner of
Land Administration, Chepauk, Chennai 600 005 hereby
declares that the lands specified in the schedule below are
needed for the Public Purpose, to wit, for the Purpose of
formation of New Channel under Chennampatti Anaicut
Scheme  in S.No. 223/6B2 etc. Measuring a total extent of
1.06.15 hectare of dry and wet lands (0.91.65 hectare of
wet and 0.14.5 hectare of dry) in Mallankinar Village of
Kariyapatti Taluk, Virudhunagar District.
A plan of the land is kept in the office of the Land
Acquisition Officer/ RDO, Aruppukottai, Virudhunagar District
and the same may be inspected at any time during office
hours.
