TAMIL NADU STATE LEGAL SERVICES AUTHORITY
Appointment of Subordinate Judge, Theni as Secretary of Theni District Legal Services Authority under
Tamil Nadu State Legal Services Authority Act.
(TNSLSA No. 8647/E/2008.)
No. VI(2)/794/2008.
Under the powers vested under Section 9(3) of the Legal Services Authorities Act, 1987 (Central Act, 39/1987) as
amended, the Tamil Nadu State Legal Services Authority appoints the Subordinate Judge, Theni to be the Secretary of the
Theni District Legal Services Authority to exercise such powers and perform such duties under the Chairman of the District
Legal Services Authority, Theni as may be assigned to him by the Chairman.

T. MATHIVANAN,
Chennai-600 104,
