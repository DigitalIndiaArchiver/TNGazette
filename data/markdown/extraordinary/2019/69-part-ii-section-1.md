LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY SECRETARIAT
VACANCY OF SEAT IN TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY DUE TO DISQUALIFICATION
UNDER ARTICLE 191(1)(e) OF THE CONSTITUTION OF INDIA READ WITH SECTION 8(3) OF THE 
REPRESENTATION OF PEOPLE ACT 1951.
[Letter No. 1596/2019-2, Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Secretariat (B.III), 19th February 2019,
Maasi 7, Vilambi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2050.]
No.II(1)/LAS/1(b)/2019.
Whereas, consequent upon his conviction by the Special Court for cases relating to elected Members of 
Parliament and Members of Legislative Assembly of Tamil Nadu, Chennai in Sessions Case No.505/2018, 
Thiru P. Balakrishna Reddy, Member of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly representing 55. Hosur Constituency of Krishnagiri 
District stands disqualiﬁ ed from the Membership of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly from the date of his conviction 
i.e., 7th January, 2019 in terms of the provisions of sub-clause (e) of Clause (1) of Article 191 of the Constitution of 
India read with Section 8 (3) of the Representation of the People Act, 1951; (Central Act 43 of 1951).
2. Now, therefore, I, Speaker of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly, for the reasons stated above, hereby 
declare that in accordance with sub-clause (a) of Clause (3) of Article 190 of the Constitution of India, the seat of 
Thiru P. Balakrishna Reddy, Member of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly representing 55. Hosur Assembly Constituency 
shall be deemed to have become vacant from the date of his conviction, i.e., the 7th day of January 2019.
