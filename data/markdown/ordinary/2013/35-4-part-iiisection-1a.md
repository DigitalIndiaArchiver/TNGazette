Amendment to the Tamil Nadu Catering Establishments Rules.
[G.O. (D) No. 382, Labour and Employment (H1) 5th August 2013  Aadi, 20, Vijaya, Thiruvallurvar Aandu, 2044].
No. SRO A-25/2013.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 29 of the Tamil Nadu Catering Establishments
Act, 1958 (Tamil Nadu Act XIII of 1958), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the
Tamil Nadu Catering Establishments Rules, 1959:-
AMENDMENTS
in the said Rules,-
(1)
in rule 38, in sub-rule (9), for the expression “wage slip” in two places where it occurs, the expression
“wage slip cum leave card” shall be substituted;
(2)
FORM No. XI shall be omitted;
(3)
In FORM No. XV, under PART I in the eighth column for  the expression “Adult/Adolescent/Child”, the
expression “Adult/Adolescent” shall be substituted;
(4)
In FORM No. XVII, for the heading “WAGE SLIP” the heading “WAGE SLIP CUM LEAVE CARD” shall be
substituted.
