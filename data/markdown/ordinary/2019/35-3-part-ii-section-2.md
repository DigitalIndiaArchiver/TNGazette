No.II(2)/HO/692/2019.—The following members of the 
Indian Police Service and State Police Service/Subordinate

1.
Dr. C. Sylendra Babu, IPS,  
Director General of Police,  
Railways, Chennai.
2.
Thiru P. Kandaswamy, IPS,  
Additional Director General of Police,  
Administration, Chennai.
3.
Dr. R. Dhinakaran, IPS,  
Additional Commissioner of Police,  
Law and Order, North,
Greater Chennai Police.
4.
Thiru J. Nagarajan,
Inspector of Police,
Kitchipalayam Police Station,
Salem City.
5. 
Thiru C. Senthilkumar,
Inspector of Police, 
Organised Crime Intelligence Unit,
Thanjavur District.
6.
Thirumathi S. Daisy, 
Women Head Constable 19067,
G-4 IMH Police Station, East Zone,
Greater Chennai Police.
2. The recipients of the Medal are eligible to receive a 
cash reward of Rs. 25,000/- (Rupees twenty five thousand 
only) each, irrespective of the rank and a gold Medal, as 
per the rules governing the award of this Medal.
3. The Medals will be presented to the recipients by 
the Hon'ble Chief Minister at a function, to be held in 
due course.
