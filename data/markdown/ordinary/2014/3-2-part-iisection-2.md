conferred by sub-section (2) read with clause (b) of
sub-section (3) of Section 17 of the Pre-conception
Pre-natal Diagnostic Techniques (Prohibition of Sex
Selection) Act, 1994 (Central Act 57 of 1994), and in
supersession of the Health and family Welfare Department
Notification No. II(2)/HF/331/2008, published at page 252
of Part-II-Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette,
dated the 30th July 2008, the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby appoints the Joint Director of Health Services of
each District of the State as the Appropriate Authority of
that District concerned and the Chief Civil Surgeon/Senior
Civil Surgeon Medical officer of a Taluk Hospital as the
Sub-district Appropriate Authority of that Taluk concerned
of Tamil Nadu for the purpose of the said Act.

HIGHWAYS AND MINOR PORTS DEPARTMENT
Acquisition of Lands.
[G.O.(D) No. 264, Highways and Minor Ports (HQ-1), 27th December 2013, Margazhi 12, Vijaya,
Thiruvallurvar Aandu 2044.]
II(2)/HWMP/32/2014.—The Governor of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that the lands specified  in the schedule below
have to be acquired for the Highways  purpose for the construction of Road over bridge at Km.132/6 of Madurai – Thoothukudi
Road (State Highways) in lieu of existing  Level Crossing 485 at Railway Km. 651/12-13 at Thoothukudi between Meelavittan
and Thoothukudi Railway Stations in Thoothukudi District  and it having already been decided that the entire amount of compensation
to be awarded for the lands is to be paid out of funds controlled and managed  by the Divisional Engineer (Projects),
Tirunelveli-7, after considering the cause shown by the owner or other person having interest on such lands as the case may
be, do hereby publish the following notice  under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act 2001
(Tamil Nadu Act 34/2002).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002), the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the dry  lands specified in the Schedule below and measuring to an extent of  1530.50 Sq.mts.
(0.15.30.5 Hectares) in Thoothukudi Town in Thoothukudi Taluk and District  to the same, a little  more or less needed for
the construction of  Road over bridge at Km.132/6 of Madurai – Thoothukudi Road (State Highways) in lieu of existing Level
Crossing 485 at Railway Km. 651/12-13 at Thoothukudi between Meelavittan and Thoothukudi Railway Stations.
The plan of the lands is kept in the office of the Revenue Divisional Officer, Thoothukudi, Thoothukudi District and may
be inspected at any time during office hours.

LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT DEPARTMENT
Proposal for inclusion of the employment in Hand Embroidery and Jari in Part-I of the
Schedule to under the Minimum Wages Act.
AMENDMENT TO NOTIFICATION
[G.O.Ms. No. 284, Labour and Employment (J2), 11th December 2013.]
No. II(2)/LE/33/2014.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 27 of the Minimum Wages Act, 1948
(Central Act XI of 1948) the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to Part I of the Schedule to the said
Act, the notice of the intention of the Government of Tamil Nadu in respect thereof having already been published, as required by
the said Section of the said Act.
AMENDMENT
In the said Act, in the Schedule, in Part I, after the expression “91. Employment in Distillery Industry”, the expression
“92” Employment in Hand Embroidery and Jari” shall be added.
