No.I/PUSP/13/2016.вҖ”The following Notification of the
Government of India, Ministry of Law and Justice (Department
of Justice), Jaisalmer House, 26, Man Singh Road,
New Delhi-110 011, dated 19th April , 2016 is republished:вҖ”
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (1) of
Article 222 of the Constitution of India, the President,
after consultation with the Chief Justice of India, is pleased
to transfer Shri Justice V. Ramasubramanian, Judge
of the Madras High Court, as a Judge of the High Court of
Judicature at Hyderabad for the States  of Telangana and
Andhra Pradesh and to direct him to assume charge of his
office in the High Court of Judicature at Hyderabad for the
States of Telangana and Andhra Pradesh on or before
3rd May, 2016.
