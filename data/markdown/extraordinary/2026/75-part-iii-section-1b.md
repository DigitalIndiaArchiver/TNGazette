5.	
Probation.- Every person appointed to a category by direct recruitment shall, from the date on which he joins duty, 
be on probation for a total period of two years on duty within a continuous period of three years, for categories in 
Class I and Class II and total period of one year on duty within a continuous period of two years for categories 
coming under Class III and Class IV.
6.	
Training.- (i) Every person possessing B.Ed., (Special Education) from any recognized University in Tamil Nadu 
appointed to the category-1 of Class I, by direct recruitment, shall undergo six months special programme in the 
Elementary Education recognized by National Council for Teacher Education, within the period of probation.
  (ii)	 Every person possessing Diploma in Education (Special Education) from any recognized institution in 
Tamil Nadu appointed to the category-2 of Class I, by direct recruitment, shall undergo six months special programme 
in Elementary Education, recognized by National Council for Teacher Education, within the period of probation. 
7. 	 Reservation of Appointments.- The principle of rule of reservation of appointments contained in section 27 of the 
Tamil Nadu Government Servants (Conditions of Service) Act, 2016 (Tamil Nadu Act No.14 of 2016), shall apply for 
appointment to a category by direct recruitment.
	
Notwithstanding anything contained in section 26 of the Tamil Nadu Government Servants (Conditions of Service) 
Act, 2016, appointment to a category in this service shall be made on gender basis according to these rules (i.e.,) 
Female staff shall be appointed in girls hostel and Male staff shall be appointed in boys hostel.
	
Note: Roster Register to be maintained separately for Male and Female staff for each categories in Class I, 
Class III and Class IV for which recruitment is made.
