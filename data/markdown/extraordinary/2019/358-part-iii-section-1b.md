In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to adhoc rules for the temporary post of Assistant Engineer in the Rural 
Development Department published with the Rural Development Department, Notiﬁ cation No.SRO-B-63/2000 at pages 75-77 of 
Part III—Section 1(b) of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 24th May 2000.
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on and from the 24-02-2014.
