The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied
that the lands specified in the schedule below have to
be acquired for Industrial Purpose to wit for  Second
Mine Expansion Scheme of Neyveli Lignite Corporation
Limited and it having already been decided that the
entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the
lands is to be paid out of the funds controlled or
managed by Neyveli Lignite Corporation Limited.
The following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of
Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for
Industrial Purpose Act, 1997.
NOTICE
Under sub section (1) of Section 3 of the
Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purpose
Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor  of
Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands specified in the
Schedule below and measuring 5.68.5 hectares to the
same a little more or less needed for Industrial purposes,
to wit, for Second  Mine Expansion Scheme of Neyveli
Lignite Corporation Limited.
The plan  of the lands are kept in the office of the
Special Deputy  Collector / Land Acquisition, Neyveli-2
and may be inspected at any  time during office hours.

     The Government of Tamil Nadu, having been satisfied that the lands specified in the schedule below have to be 
acquired for Industrial Purposes to wit for  expansion of cement grinding unit and it having already been decided 
that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid out of the funds controlled or 
managed by M/s. Ultra Tech Cement Limited,   the following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of   Section 3 of 
the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999). 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
NOTIFICATION 
 
     Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands specified in the Schedule 
below and measuring 2.93.0 hectares or 7.24 acres  to the same, a little more or less needed for Industrial 
purposes, to wit, for expansion of cement grinding unit by M/s. Ultra Tech Cement Limited at Chitheri Village, 
Arakkonam Taluk, Vellore District.  
 
      The plan  of the lands are kept in the office of the District  Collector, Vellore  and may be inspected at any  time 
during office hours.
