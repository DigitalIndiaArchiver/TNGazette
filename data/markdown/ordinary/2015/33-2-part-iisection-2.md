No. II(2)/LE/486/2015.—In exercise of the powers conferred
by Section 4 of the Tamil Nadu Manual Workers (Regulation
of Employment and Conditions of Work) Act, 1982
(Tamil  Nadu Act 33 of 1982), the Governor of Tamil Nadu,
after consultaion with the Advisory Committee, hereby makes
the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu Domestic
Workers Social Security and Welfare Scheme, 2007.
AMENDMENTS.
In the said Scheme,-
(1) in clause 8, in sub-clause (3), in item (b) for the
expression “two years” the expression “five years” shall be
substituted;
(2) in clause 14, in sub-clause(1),-
(i) for the expresion "clause (9);" the expression "clause
8" shall be substituted;
(ii) for the expression "two years", the expression "five
years" shall be substituted.
