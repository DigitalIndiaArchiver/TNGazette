Winding up the affairs of the Theni District Electrical Contractors Industrial Co-operative Society Limited.
IND. No. 9/2006, Theni and appointment of Official Liquidator.
No.VI(1)452/2013.—
Under the powers conferred on the Registrar of Industrial Co-operatives under Section 137(2)(b) of the
Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act, 1983 (Act 30 of 1983), the activities of the Theni District Electrical Contractors
Industrial Co-operative Society Ltd., IND. No. 9/2006 Theni have been ordered to be wound up with immediate effect by the
Principal Secretary/Industries Commissioner and Director of Industries and Commerce and the Registrar of
Industrial Co-operatives, Mandaivelipakkam, Raja Annamalaipuram, Chennai-28 vide Proc. No. 2842/ICD/2013,
dated 20th November 2013.
Under Section 138(1) of the same Act, Assistant Director of Industries and Commerce(IC), District Industries Centre, Theni
has been appointed as Official Liquidator of the said Society for the purpose of liquidation.
SWARAN SINGH,
Chennai-600 028,
Principal Secretary / Industries Commissioner and
20th November 2013.
Director of Industries and Commerce and
Registrar of Industrial Co-operatives.

(Roc. No. 560/2011/MLPA)
No. VI(1)/454/2013.—
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub section (4) of Section 32 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning Act,
1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972), Government in the order G.O.(2D) No. 84, Housing and Urban Development
UD 4(1) Department, dated 22-8-2013 has permitted to change of land use of survey numbers with condition making the
following variation to the  Master Plan for the Mamallapuram Local Planning Area, approved under the said Act and
published in the G.O.Ms. No. 153 H & UD [UD4(2)], dated 20-06-2013, Notification  No.II (2)/HOU/501/2013, at page No.481
of Part-II, Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette dated 17th July 2013.
VARIATIONS
In the said Master Plan, in the “LAND USE  SCHEDULE” of Village 37, Kanathur Reddikuppam, under the heading
7.
Agriculture Use Zone, the Survey Numbers the expression “18 to 20” shall be deleted and 18
(except 18/1, 2, 3, 8A, 8D, 10), 19 (except 19/1, 2, 3, 4, 6B) and 20 shall be substituted.
And under the heading 1. RESIDENTIAL USE ZONE, the Survey Numbers “18/1, 2, 3, 8A, 8B, 10;  19/1,
2, 3, 4, 6B” shall be added.
Conditions:
1.
The conditions specified in the NOC of Thasildar, Chengalpattu in K.Dis.No.1753/2012/A1,
dated 21st April 2012 has to be fulfilled.
2.
The 9.0m approach road has to formed by the applicant in his land from the Public access to the Kulam
Poramboke in S.No. 30/11 and Kulam Poramboke in S.No. 18/9 and the same should be handover to local
body by way of gift deed at the time of applying Planning Permission.
