NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
DTP—II-2 Ex. (51)
TAMIL DEVELOPMENT, RELIGIOUS ENDOWMENTS AND INFORMATION DEPARTMENT.
DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF THE TAMIL NADU HINDU RELIGIOUS AND CHARITABLE
ENDOWMENTS (AMENDMENT) ACT, 2010.
[G.O. Ms. No. 69, Tamil Development, Religious Endowments and Information (RE4-2),
1st March 2010, Maasi 17, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No. II(2)/TDREI/123(b)/2010.
In exercise of the powers  conferred by sub-section (2) of  Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Hindu Religious
and Charitable Endowments (Amendment) Act, 2010 (Tamil Nadu Act 12 of 2010), the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the 1st day of March 2010 as the date on which the provisions of the said Act
shall come into force.
G. MUTHUSAMY,
Secretary to Government.
