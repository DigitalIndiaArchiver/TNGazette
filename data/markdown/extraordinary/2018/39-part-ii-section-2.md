The Governor of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed 
that the lands  speciﬁ ed in the schedule below 
have to be acquired for industrial purpose to wit for 
Tamil Nadu Electricity Board and it having already 
been decided that the entire amount or compensation 
to be awarded for the lands is to be paid out of 
the funds controlled of managed  by  Tamil Nadu 
Electricity Board. The following notice is issued under 
sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu 
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the 
Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes 
Act,1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor 
of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands speciﬁ ed in 
the Schedule below and measuring 1.49.00  hectare 
to the same, a little more or less needed for industrial 
purposes, to wit, for Bhavani Barrage Kattalai 
Kathavani -II  scheme of Tamil Nadu Electricity Board.
The plans of the lands are kept in the ofﬁ ce 
of the District Revenue Ofﬁ cer, Erode  and may be 
inspected at any time during ofﬁ ce hours.
