ENERGY  DEPARTMENT
NOTIFICATION REGARDING DIRECTIONS TO SUPPLY OF POWER BY PRIVATE POWER PRODUCERS
TO TAMIL NADU ELECTRICITY BOARD OR CONSUMERS WITHIN THE STATE UNDER
THE ELECTRICITY ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 10, Energy (C3), 27th February 2009.]
No. II(2)/EGY/104(c)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 11 of the Electricity Act, 2003 (Central
Act 36 of 2003),  the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby issues the following directions in the circumstances
arising in the public interest namely:—
(i) All power generation units operating Tamil Nadu shall operate and maintain generating stations
to maximum capacity and Plant Load Factor (PLF); and
(ii) All generating stations shall supply all exportable electricity generated to the State grid for supply
to either Tamil Nadu Electricity Board, or to any other HT consumers within the State as per the regulations
notified in this regard by the Tamil Nadu Electricity Regulatory Commission.
P.W.C. DAVIDAR,
Secretary to Government (incharge).
