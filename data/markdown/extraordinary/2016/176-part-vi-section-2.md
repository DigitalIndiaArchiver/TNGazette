TAMIL NADU STATE ELECTION COMMISSION, CHENNAI.
[ELECTIONS—CONDUCT OF ELECTIONS TO PANCHAYATS—APPOINTMENT OF DISTRICT
ELECTION OFFICERS—NOTIFICATION ISSUED.]
(S.O. No. 2/2016/TNSEC/PE-I)
No. VI(2)/91(a)/2016.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-rule (2) of rule 6 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Elections) Rules,
1995, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission hereby appoints the District Collectors concerned excepting the
Collector of Chennai as the District Election Officers for their respective Districts. Subject to the superintendence,
direction and control of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission, they shall co-ordinate and supervise the
preparation and publication of electoral rolls of panchayats and the conduct of elections to the offices of members
of Village Panchayats, Panchayat Union Councils and the District Panchayats, Presidents and Vice-presidents of
Village Panchayats, Chairpersons and Vice-Chairpersons of Panchayat Union Councils and District Panchayats
in their respective Districts. They shall also perform such other functions as are entrusted to them by the Tamil
Nadu State Election Commission, from time to time, in connection with the conduct of Ordinary Elections, 2016
to Panchayats and subsequent Casual Elections in the State.
(By Order of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission)
