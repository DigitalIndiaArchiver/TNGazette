Notifications under Tamil Nadu
Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1974.
Exemption from payment of Motor Vehicle Tax for
certain person under the Act.
[G.O. Ms. No. 310, Home (Transport-I) 17th April 2009.]
No. II(2)/HO/242/2009.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by clause (1) of Section 20 of the Tamil Nadu Motor
Vehicles Taxation Act, 1974 (Tamil Nadu Act 13 of 1974),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes exemption in
regard to the tax payable in respect of the Motor Vehicles
bearing Registration (Number) (i) DL 4C AB 0965 (Honda
Accord) and (ii) DL 9C D 2989 (Maruti) belonging to
Thiru P.C. Alexander, former Governor of Tamil Nadu and
former Member of Parliament, (Rajyasabha).
Exemption from payment of tax for “Hop-On Hop-Off
tour”pertaining to Tamil Nadu State Transport
Corporation (Villupuram) Limited, for certain period
under the Act.
[G.O. Ms. No. 327, Home (Transport-1) 22nd April 2009.]
No. II(2)/HO/243/2009.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by clause (1) of Section 20 of the Tamil Nadu Motor
Vehicles Taxation Act, 1974 (Tamil Nadu Act 13 of 1974),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes an exemption in
regard to the tax payable in respect of four Motor Vehicles,
bearing Registration Numbers. (i) TN 21 N 1283,
(ii) TN 21 N 1284, (iii) TN 21 N 1285 and (iv) TN 21 N 1287
belonging to the Tami Nadu State Transport Corporation
(Villupuram) Limited, Villupuram, for a period of 5 years on
and from 25th February 2009, subject to the condition that
the said Motor Vehicles have to be used only for “Hop-On
Hop-Off” tour scheme in Chennai city.
S. MALATHI,
Principal Secretary to Government.
