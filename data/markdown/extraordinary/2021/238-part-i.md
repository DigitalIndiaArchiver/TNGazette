Appointment of Thiru R. Shunmugasundaram Senior Advocate as  
Advocate General of Tamil Nadu at High Court of Madras.
[G.O. Ms. No. 272, Public (Law officers), 8th May 2021, Chithirai 25, Pilava, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2052.]
No. I/PULO/4(b)/2021.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Article 165(1) of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby 
appoints Thiru. R. Shunmugasundaram, Senior Advocate, to be the Advocate General of Tamil Nadu, from the date of his 
taking charge vice Thiru. Vijay Narayan, resigned.
