HEALTH AND FAMILY PLANNING DEPARTMENT
Adhoc Rules relating to Temporary Post of Health Education Extension Officer in the Regional Family Planning
Training Centres at Salem and Egmore for Tamil Nadu Public Health Services.
(G.O. Ms. No. 825, Health and Family Planning, 29th March 1973.)
No. SRO B-21/2011.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following rules:—
The rules hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 29th October 1965.
RULES
The General and the Special Rules applicable to the holders of the permanent posts in the Tamil Nadu Public Health
Service shall apply to the holders of the temporary posts of Health Education Extension Officers in the Regional Family
Planning Training Centres at Salem and Egmore, subject to the modifications specified in the following rules.
2. Constitution:—The post shall constitute a distinct category in the said service.
3. Appointment:—Appointment to the post shall be made as follows:—
(i) Direct recruitment; or
(ii) by recruitment by transfer from any category in the Tamil Nadu Public Health Subordinate Service or from the
Tamil Nadu Medical Subordinate Service or from any other Service.
4. Qualifications:
(a) Age:—No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post by direct recruitment unless he has completed
or will complete 25 years of age but has not completed or will not complete 35 years of age on the 1st day of July of the
year in which selection for appointment is made.
(b) Other Qualifications:—No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post unless he possesses the
following qualifications, namely:—
2. Experience in teaching and or social work including Family Planning for a period of not less than 5 years.
Provided that preference shall be given to those with a diploma in Health Education.
5. Probation: Every person appointed to the post shall from the date on which he joins duty, be on probation for a total
period of two years on duty within a continuous period of three years.
6. Pay: There shall be paid to the holder of the post a monthly pay calculated in the scale of Rs. 225-10-275-15-350.
Provided that as and from 2nd October 1970 a monthly pay shall be calculated in the scale of
Rs. 400-15-475-20-575-25-650.

RULES
The General and the Special Rules applicable to the holders of permanent posts in the Tamil Nadu Public Health
Subordinate Service shall apply to the holders of the temporary posts of Health Education Extension Officer (non-gazetted)
in the Regional Family Planning Training Centre at Egmore, subject to the modifications specified in the following rules.
2. Constitution:—The post shall constitute a distinct category in the said service.
3. Appointment:—Appointment to the post shall be made as follows:—
(i) by direct recruitment; or
(ii) by transfer from any other category in the Tamil Nadu Public Health Subordinate Service; or
(iii) by recruitment by transfer from the Tamil Nadu Medical Subordinate Service of from any other Service.
4. Appointing Authority: The appointing authority for the post shall be the Director of Health Services and Family
Planning, Madras.
5. Qualifications:
(a) Age:—No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post by direct recruitment unless he has completed
or will complete 25 years of age but has not completed or will not complete 35 years of age on the 1st day of July of the
year in which selection for appointment is made.
(b) Other Qualifications: No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post unless he possesses the following
qualifications:-
1.
Second Class Masters degree in Social Science, Social Works or Education.
2.
Experience in teaching and / or Social Work including. Family Planning for a period of not less than 5
years. Provided that preference shall be given to those with a Diploma in Health Education.
6. Probation: Every person appointed to the post shall from the date on which he joins duty, be on probation for a total
period of two years on duty within a continuous period of three years.
7. Pay: There shall be paid to the holder of the post a monthly pay calculated in the scale of Rs. 225-10-275-15-350.
Provided that as and from 2nd October 1970 a monthly pay shall be calculated in the scale of
Rs. 400-15-475-20-575-25-650.
