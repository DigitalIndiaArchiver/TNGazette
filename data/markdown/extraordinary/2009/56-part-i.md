APPOINTMENT OF MINISTER FOR THE STATE OF TAMIL NADU.
[G.O. Ms. No. 197, Public (Special-B), 27th February 2009,
Maasi 15, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. I/PUSP/(a-1)/2009.
I, Surjit Singh Barnala, Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoint Dr. (Tmt.) Poongothai, with effect from the
Forenoon of the 27th February 2009 to be the Minister of my Council of Ministers.
SURJIT SINGH BARNALA,
 Governor of Tamil Nadu.
ALLOCATION OF SUBJECTS AMONG MINISTERS.
[G.O. Ms. No. 198, Public (Special-B), 27th February 2009,
Maasi 15, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. I/PUSP/(a-2)/2009.
Under Clause (3) of Article 166 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that the subject,
Information Technology hitherto held by Hon'ble Chief Minister be allocated to Dr. (Tmt.) Poongothai, appointed as Minister
in the G.O.Ms. No. 197, Public (Special-B) Department, dated 27th February 2009. Consequent on the above allocation,
Dr. (Tmt.) Poongothai be designated as Minister for Information Technology.
   K.S. SRIPATHI,
    Chief Secretary.
