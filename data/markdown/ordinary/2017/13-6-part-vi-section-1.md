No.VI(1)/85/2017.

the Master Plan of Madurai Local Planning area. The Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following variations
to the Master plan  for Madurai Local Planning Area reconsented under the said Act and Published with the Housing
and Urban Development Department Notification No.23/HOU/variation to Madurai Local Planning Area at page
398 to 418 Part II-Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 16th June 2010.
