ANIMAL HUSBANDRY, DAIRYING AND FISHERIES DEPARTMENT
Amendment to Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Fisheries Subordinate Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 27, Animal Husbandry, Dairying and Fisheries (FS-2), 19th February 2016,
Maasi 7, Manmadha, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2047.]
No. SRO B-23/2016.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Fisheries
Subordinate Service (Section 11 in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Service Manual, 1970).
2.
The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 18th December of 1998.
AMENDMENT
In the said Special Rules, in rule 2, in sub-rule (a), in the Table, under the heading "Method of Recruitment" in
column (2), against the entry "2. Inspector of Fisheries" in column (1), thereof, for the first proviso, the following proviso shall
be substituted, namely:-
"Provided that the vacancies arising in the category of Inspector of Fisheries shall be filled up by direct recruitment and
by promotion by following the ratio of 1:1 (50:50).
Nothing contained in this proviso shall affect the appointment made during the year 1998-2000 by
following the ratio 1:3:".
NOTIFICATION-II
[G.O. Ms. No. 27, Animal Husbandry, Dairying and Fisheries (FS-2), 19th February 2016,
Maasi 7, Manmadha, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2047.]
No. SRO B-24/2016.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Fisheries
Subordinate Service (Section 11 in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Service Manual, 1970).
2.
The amendment hereby made shall come into force with effect from date of issue of this order.
AMENDMENT
In the said Special Rules, in rule 2, in sub-rule (a), in the Table, under the heading "Method of Recruitment" in
column (2), against the entry "2. Inspector of Fisheries" in column (1), thereof, for the first proviso, the following proviso shall
be substituted, namely:-
"Provided that the vacancies arising in the category of Inspector of Fisheries shall be filled up by direct recruitment and
by promotion by following the ratio of 3:2 (60:40):".
S. VIJAYAKUMAR,
 Secretary to Government.
