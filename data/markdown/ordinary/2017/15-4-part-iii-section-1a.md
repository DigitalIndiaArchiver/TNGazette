AGRICULTURE DEPARTMENT
Amendments to the Tamil Nadu Admission in Agricultural Institution Rules, 2007.

No. SRO A-15/2017.—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 10 of the Tamil Nadu
Admission in Professional Educational Institutions Act, 2006 (Tamil Nadu Act 3 of 2007), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby makes the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu Admission in Agricultural Institutions Rules, 2007.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules, in rule 4,-
(i)
in clause (i), under the heading “Academic stream”, under the sub-heading “I. Agricultural Science Courses”,
after the serial number (9) and the entry relating thereto, the following serial number and the entry shall,
respectively be inserted, namely:-
“(10) B.Sc., (Sericulture)”;
(ii) in clause (ii),-(a) in sub-clause (a), for the expression “and Bachelor of Science in Home Science”, the
following expression shall be substituted, namely:-
“Bachelor of Science in Home Science and Bachelor of Science in Sericulture”;
(b)
in sub-clause (c), in the TABLE, in column (1), after the expression “(4) Bachelor of Science in Home
Science”, the following expression shall be inserted, namely:-
“(5) Bachelor of Science in Sericulture”.
GAGANDEEP SINGH BEDI,
Agricultural Production Commissioner and
Principal Secretary to Government.
