MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION AND WATER SUPPLY DEPARTMENT
DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF THE TAMIL NADU MUNICIPAL LAWS
(THIRD AMENDMENT) ACT, 2012
[G.O. Ms. No. 81, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (Election), 5th September 2012.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/573/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Municipal Laws
(Third Amendment) Act, 2012 (Tamil Nadu Act 17 of 2012), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the
1st day of October 2012, as the date on which the said Act shall come into force.
SHEELA BALAKRISHNAN,
 Additional Chief Secretary to Government.
