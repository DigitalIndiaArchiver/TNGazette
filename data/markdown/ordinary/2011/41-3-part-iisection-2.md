Draft variation under Tamil Nadu Parks, Play-fields and
Open Spaces (Preservation and Regulation) Act.
[G.O. Ms. No. 151, Municipal Administration and Water
Supply (MA.5),  13th October 2011.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/438/2011.—Whereas, in G.O. Ms. No. 223,
Municipal Administration and Water Supply Department, dated
24-11-1998 a list of parks and play-fields in the area within the
jurisdiction of Kadayanallur Municipality was approved under
sub-section (2) of Section 4 of the Tamil Nadu Parks, Play-
fields and Open Spaces (Preservation and Regulation) Act,
1959 and the same was published in the Tamil Nadu
Government Gazette;
And, whereas, the Commissioner of Town and Country
Planning has requested the Government to modify the ward
number and survey number of the parks contained in the list
approved in the said Government Order. The Commissioner
of Town and Country Planning has also informed that there
is no change in the extent of the notified parks and they remain
the same;
And, whereas, the Government after careful examination  of
the proposal of the Commissioner of Town and Country Planning
to modify the Ward numer and Survey number of the parks
as notified in the said Government Order have decided to
accept the same.
Now, therefore, the following draft of variation to the approved
list of parks and play-fields published in the Municipal
Administration and Water Supply Department Notification
No. II(2)/MW/54/99 at page 22 of Part II—Section 2 of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 27th January 1999,
which it is proposed to make in exercise of the powers conferred
by sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Parks,
play-fields and Open Spaces (Preservation and Regulation)
Act, 1959 (Tamil Nadu Act 26 of 1959) is hereby published as
required under sub-section (2) of Section 5 of the said Act.
Notice is hereby given that objection or suggestion, if any,
received in writing, in respect of the said draft variation to the
said list from any person interested within seven days from the
date 
of 
publication 
of 
this 
notification 
in 
the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, will be taken into
consideration by the Government of Tamil Nadu Objection and
Suggestion, if any should be addressed to the Secretary to
Government of Municipal Administaration and Water Supply
Department, Secretariat, Fort Saint George, Chennai-600 009.
DRAFT VARIATION
In the said Notification, in the Schedule, in the tabular
column under the heading “List of parks, play-fields and open
spaces in the area within the jurisdiction of Kadayanallur
Municipal Council”, in column (3), for the expressions against
Serial Numbers 1, 2 and 3 in column (1), the following
expressions shall, respectively, be substituted, namely:–
“Ward No. 3, Survey No. 83/B2”, "Ward No. 20 Survey
Nos. 59 to 61” and “Ward No. 25 Survey No. 721/2”.
S. KARUTHIAH PANDIAN,
Secretary to Government.

RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND PANCHAYAT RAJ
DEPARTMENT
Disqualification of Ward Member of Peravur Panchayat,
Kuthalam Panchayat Union, Nagapattinam District under
Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act.
[G.O. (D) No. 502, Rural Development and Panchayat Raj
(P.R.IV), 20th October 2011, Aippasi 3,
Thiruvalluvar Andu-2042.]
No. II(2)/RDPR/439/2011.—Whereas, a question has arisen
as to whether Thiru R. Kumar, 2nd Ward Member, Peravur
Panchayat, Kuthalam Panchayat Union, Nagapattinam District
has become disqualified under clause (g) of sub-section (3) of
Section 38 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994
(Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994), as he has ceased to reside in the
Village Panchayat for period of three years;
PRINTED AND PUBLISHED BY THE DIRECTOR OF STATIONERY AND PRINTING, CHENNAI
ON BEHALF OF THE GOVERNMENT OF TAMIL NADU
And, whereas, the opinion of the Tamil Nadu State Election
Commission has been south for regarding the disqualification of
the said member of the said Panchayat, as required under
sub-section (2) of Section 41 of the said Act;
And, whereas, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission,
after conducting an enquiry, has stated that on verification of the
relevant records it has been found that, Thiru R. Kumar, 2nd
Ward Member of the said Village Panchayat, has gone to foreign
country for employment from 8th April 2009 to 26th April 2011
and he produced his passport, and Air ticket before the
Commission and he has not objected the above fact and has
opined that the said Thiru R. Kumar, 2nd Ward Member, Peravur
Panchayat, Kuthalam Panchayat Union, Nagapattinam District
is disqualified to continue as member of the said Peravur
Panchayat, under clause (g) of sub-section (3) of Section 38 of
the said Act;
Now, therefore, under sub-section (1) of Section 41 of the
Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby decides that Thiru R. Kumar,
2nd Ward Member, Peravur Panchayat, Kuthalam Panchayat
Union, Nagapattinam District has become disqualified from
holding the office of Member of the said Peravur Panchayat,
under clause (g) of sub-section (3) of Section 38 of the said Act,
as he has ceased to reside in the said Village Panchayat for a
period from 8th April 2009 to 26th April 2011.
N.S. PALANIAPPAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
