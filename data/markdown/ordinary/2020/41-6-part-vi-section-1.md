No.VI(1)/356/2020.—
 Notice under Section 15(1) of Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001(TN Act 34 of 2002) read with the Tamil Nadu Land 
Acquisition Laws (Revival of Operation, Amendment and Validation) Act, 2019.
The Governor of Tamil Nadu  having been satisﬁ ed that the lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below are required 
for the Highways purpose to wit for formation of ring road to Mannargudi town branching and merging at  km 35/2 
Kumbakonam-Mannargudi-Adhirampattinam road (SH66). and it has already been decided that the entire amount of 
compensation to be awarded for land is to be paid by Divisional Engineer Highways Tiruvarur and after considering 
the cause shown by owner or other person having interest on such lands as the case may be do here by publish 
the following notice under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Highways Act, 2001(TN Act34/2002) read with the 
Tamil Nadu Land Acquisition Laws (Revival of Operation, Amendment and Validation) Act, 2019.
NOTICE
 Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) read with 
the Tamil Nadu Land Acquisition Laws(Revival of Operation, Amendment and Validation) Act, 2019, the Government of 
Tamil Nadu here by acquires the land speciﬁ ed in the schedule below measuring to an extent of 1.63.24 hectares of 
lands a little more or less required to wit for forming ring road to Mannargudi town branching and merging at km 35/2 
of Kumbakonam-Mannargudi-Adhirampattinam road (SH66) in Kumarapuram Village, Mannargudi Taluk in Tiruvarur District,
The plan of land is kept in the oﬃ  ce of the District Revenue Oﬃ  cer Tiruvarur and may be inspected at any time 
during oﬃ  ce hours.
