GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
VARIATION TO THE APPROVED KARANTHAI DETAILED DEVELOPMENT PLAN NO.    PART-VII OF THANJAVUR LOCAL
PLANNING AREA.
(Roc. No. 29052/2005/DP3.)
No. VI(1)/216/2013.
 In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (1) of Section 33 of Town and Country Planning Act 1971.
(Act No. 35 of 1972) the Commissioner of Town and Country Planning, Chennai in the Proceeding Roc. No. 29052/
2005 DP3, dated 10-4-2013 proposes to make the following variation to the approved Karanthai Detailed
Development Plan No.  Part-VII of Thanjavur Local Planning Area, approved by the Director of  Town and Country Planning
Proceeding in Roc. No. 28925/1977 D1, dated 11-4-1979 and the fact of this approval in Form No. 12, Published in
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, Part-VI Section-1, Page 531, dated 5-9-1979, Publication No. VI(1)/929/1979.
2. Any person affected or interested in this draft variation may with in sixty days from the date of publication of the
notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, can represent in person or submit in writing to the Member
Secretary, Thanjavur Local Planning Authority any objection and suggestions relating there to.
3. The variation with plan may be inspected at free of cost at any time during office hours at the above said Local
Planning Authority Office.
VARIATION
1. The entire Karanthai Detailed Development Plan Part-VII of Thanjavur Local Planning Area by the Director of Town
and Country Planning’s Proceeding Roc No. 28925/1977 D1, dated 11-4-1979 is varied by the Karanthai Detailed
Development Plan Part-VII of Thanjavur Local Planning Area.
2. The Plan thus varied is numbered as Map No. 5 DDP/(V)/DTCP No. 9/2013.
3. The Detailed Development Plan clauses and Schedules annexed with this plan are made enforceable from the
date of publication of the confirmed variation notification to be issued under Section 33(2) of the Act and published
in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
Chennai-600 002,
A. KARTHIK,
10th April  2013.
