that the lands specified in the schedule below have to be
acquired for Industrial Purposes,  to wit, for Second Mine

of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997.
NOTICE

Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil
Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
acquires the lands specified in the Schedule below and
measuring 4.55.5 hectares  to  be the same, a little more
or less needed for Industrial purposes, to wit, for Second
Mine Expansion Scheme of Neyveli Lignite Corporation
Limited.

Special Deputy  Collector / Land Acquisition, Neyveli-2 and
may be inspected at any  time during office hours.
