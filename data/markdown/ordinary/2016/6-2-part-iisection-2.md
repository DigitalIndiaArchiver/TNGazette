surrender of exemption be considered;
NOW THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-
section (4) of section 17 of the Employees’ Provident Funds
and Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1952 (Central Act 19 of
1952), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby cancels the exemption
granted under clause (a) of sub-section (1) of
Section 17 of the said Act to the said establishment,
M/s. Madras Gymkhana Club, Chennai-2, with effect from the
1st November 2010.
(By order of the Governor)
