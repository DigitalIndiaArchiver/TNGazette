Note: 
 
(a) In the case of Special buildings and Group development where additional 25% Floor Space Index is 
permitted for stilt parking, the Premium Floor Space Index will be restricted to 0.2 i.e., the normally permissible Floor 
Space Index would be 1.8 and Premium Floor space Index would be upto 0.2. 
 
(b) In case of Multi-stroreyed / Group developments, hospital buildings where an additional Floor Space Index 
of 0.25 is allowable over and above the normally permissible Floor Space Index, the extra Floor Space Index (additional 
Floor Space Index of 0.25 and Premium ﬂ oor Space Index) would be restricted to the limits speciﬁ ed in the regulation 
6B. Premium Floor Space Index charges would be collected only for the Floor Space Index over and above the additional 
Floor Space Index of 0.25 which is allowable.
(3)  The additional beneﬁ t by way of Premium Floor Space Index accrued to the developer is related to the 
proportional land extent. 
For example- 
(a)  In case where normally permissible Floor Space Index is 1.50, for every 1 sq.m of additional ﬂ oor area 
constructed availing premium Floor Space Index, proportionate land required will be 2/3 or 0.66 sq.m. 
(b)  In case where normally permissible Floor Space Index is 2.0 tp 2.5 for every 1 sq.m. of additional ﬂ oor 
area constructed by availing premium ﬂ oor Space Index, the proportionate land required will be 1/2 or 
0.50 sq.m. to 1/5 or 0.4 sq.m. respectively. 
 
The amount to be paid by the applicant towards the Premium Floor Space Index (hereinafter referred 
to as Premium Floor Space Index charge) shall be equivalent to the cost of the proportionate land 
stated above, as per the guideline value of the Registration Department on the date of application for 
Planning Permission. 
 
(4)  Payment of Premium Floor Space Index fees would be 50% by way of cash and 50% by way of utilization 
of Development Rights Certiﬁ cate (DRC)/Special Development Rights Certiﬁ cate (SDRC) issued under Regulations for 
grant of Transferable Development Rules. In case the owner is unable to mobilize Development Rights Certiﬁ cate (DRC)/
Special Development Rights Certiﬁ cate (SDRC) to the extent of 50% of Premium Floor Space Index fees, then he may 
apply to the Director of Town and Country Planning explaining the reasons and if the Director of Town and Country 
Planning is satisﬁ ed as to the reason for non-availability of Development Rights Certiﬁ cate (DRC)/Special Development 
Rights Certiﬁ cate (SDRC), he may permit payment of entire Premium Floor Space Index fees by cash. 
 
(5)  The applicant shall remit the Premium Floor Space Index charge in one lumpsum to the respective Member 
Secretary of the Urban planning Authorities before getting Planning Permission. 
 
(6)  All the amount collected towards the award of Premium Floor Space Index shall be remitted into Government 
Account, to be allotted separately for this purpose. 
 
(7) The applicants shall at the time of ﬁ ling their planning permission application, state their willingness to avail 
the Premium Floor Space Index and give an undertaking to remit the Premium Floor Space Index charges at the above 
said rates before getting the Planning Permission/Technical Clearance. 
Any objections or suggestions regarding the above variations may be informed to the Member Secretary (i/c), Erode 
Local Planning Authority, Tamil Nadu Housing Board Complex, I Floor, Surampatti Nall Road, Erode - 638 009 within 
60 days from the date of publication of this draft notiﬁ cation in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.

PRECEPT
(Ref. No. TNMC/Elec.5(1)(f)/2017.)
No. VI(1)/334/2017.
 
In accordance with rule 2, in regard to the Elections to the Tamil Nadu Medical Council, Chennai, 
under clause (f) of sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Madras Medical Registration Act, 1914 (Act IV of 1914) as 
amended by the Madras Medical Registration (Amendment) Act XVI of 1938, notice is hereby given that an election of 
Seven members of the Council to be elected from amongst themselves by the registered Medical Practitioners whose 
names appear in the printed Medical Registers (Electoral Roll list soft copy) available in the Tamil Nadu Medical Council 
website and Ofﬁ ce of the Council updated upto the year 2017 (30-06-2017) will be held. 
Nomination paper in Form II under the rule will be supplied by the Honourable Administrator to any elector who 
may apply for the same. These must be ﬁ led in accordance with the instructions thereon and returned to the Hon'ble 
Administrator not later than 3 P.M on Tuesday the 21st November, 2017. The scrutiny of nomination papers will take 
place at 11 A.M on Wednesday the 22nd November, 2017 at the ofﬁ ce of the Honorable Administrator. A list of valid 
nominations will be published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette on Wednesday the 29th November, 2017. Last 
date for withdrawal of nomination is 3 P.M. on Wednesday the 6th December, 2017 . Publication of ﬁ nal list of valid 
nominations is on Wednesday the 13th December, 2017. Voting papers and other forms to be sent by the Administrator 
to all Electors registered up to 30-06-2017 by Speed Post (within Chennai Electors) and by Registered Post (Other than 
Chennai Electors) on or before Friday, the 12th January, 2018 with instructions printed therein and the voting papers 
are returnable to the Honourable Administrator not later than 5 P.M. on Monday, the 12th February, 2018. The Scrutiny 
and counting of votes will take place at the ofﬁ ce of the Honourable Administrator from 13th February 2018 (Tuesday) 
11.A.M. onwards. 
If for any reason of the dates ﬁ xed for the various proceedings in the conduct of the election happens to 
fall on a holiday the proceedings ﬁ xed for that day will take place in the next day and at the time ﬁ xed already. 
 
All Communications regarding the elections should be addressed as hereunder.

Election of Seven Members to the Tamil Nadu Medical Council, Chennai
(Ref. No. TNMC/Elec.5(1)(f)/2017.)
No. VI(1)/336/2017.
 
Notice is hereby given pursuant to the provisions of rule 5 in regard to the elections to the Tamil Nadu 
Medical Council, Chennai under Clause (f) sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Madras Medical Registration Act, 1914 
as amended by the Madras Medical Registration (Amendment Act XVI of 1938) that the election of seven members to 
the Tamil Nadu Medical Council, Chennai, to serve for a term of ﬁ ve years is to be held and the electorate is called 
upon to elect the members. 
Nominations of eligible persons to ﬁ ll the vacancies are invited. 
Each candidate must be nominated by separate nomination paper but any person entitled to vote at the election 
may sign the nomination paper of any number of candidates not exceeding the number to be elected and for which he 
is entitled to vote. Every nomination paper must be in Form II giving all the details as required therein. 
The nomination paper must reach the undersigned not later than 3 P.M. on Tuesday the 21st November, 2017 from 
whom nomination papers may be obtained on application. Nomination papers which are not received by. the Administrator 
by the aforesaid date and time will be invalid. Each candidate standing for election shall deposit or cause to be deposited 
with the Honourable Administrator, the sum of Rupees Fifty in Cash on or before 21st November, 2017 (3 P.M) and 
no candidate shall be deemed to be duly nominated unless such deposit has been made.
