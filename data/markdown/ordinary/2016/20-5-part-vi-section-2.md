WITHDRAWAL NOTIFICATION
In exercise of the powers delegated to the Collectors in G.O.Ms. No. 70, Local Administration, dated 12th January 1960
the Collector of Cuddalore District as per Tamil Nadu Panchayat Act 1994 (T.N.Act XXI of 1994) direct that from and after the
date of publication of this notification in the District Gazette and Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, the land vested to the
undermentioned Village and Panchayat the undermentioned Taluk of Cuddalore District on the Schedule and measuring the
extent noted below be the same a little more or less shall be excluded from the operation of the said Act.
SCHEDULE
Cuddalore District, Vridhachalam Taluk, Periyakurichi Panchayat, Periyakurichi Village.

Nomination of Chairman for the High Court Legal Services Committee
(TNSLSA No. 2250/E/2016)
No. VI(2)/60/2016.
In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (2) of Section 8A of the Legal Services Authorities
Act, 1987 (Central Act 39 of 1987) the Hon’ble the Chief Justice, High Court, Madras is pleased to nominate
Hon’ble Mr. Justice S. Manikumar, Judge, High Court, Madras to be the Chairman of the High Court Legal Services
Committee with effect from 1-5-2016.

LOSS OF CERTIFICATES
