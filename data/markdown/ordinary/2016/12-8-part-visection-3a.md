TAMIL NADU WAQF BOARD, CHENNAI
Kailasala Bawa Makkan Dargha, Periyakulam Taluk, Theni District Purchase of
Additional Property to the Waqf.
(Rc. No. 14684/02/B8/Theni)
No.VI-3(a)/41/2016.
The Waqf namely Kailasala Bawa Makkan Dargha, Periyakulam Taluk, Theni District Propose to purchase additional
property to the Waqf in T.S.No. 1666/1, 2 Door No. 23 & 23/1 Vadakarai Pallivasal Street Periyakulam Taluk, Theni District
measuring 724½ Sq ft. at the rate of Rs.450/- per Sq ft., from the Waqf Fund.
Objections and suggestions are invited on the above proposal U/s. 53 of the Waqf Act 1995 within 15 days from the date
of this notification as per Tamil Nadu Waqf Board’s  Resolution No.12/16 in R.C.No. 14684/02/B8/Theni Dt 27.01.2016
Chennai-600 001,
K. RASHEETH ALI,
14th March 2016.
District Revenue Officer/
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Waqf Board.
Kattu Nainar Dargha, Periyakulam Taluk, Theni District. Purchase of Additional Property to the Waqf
(Rc. No. 14684/02/B8/Theni)
No.VI-3(a)/42/2016.
The Waqf namely Kattu Nainar Dargha, Periyakulam Taluk, Theni District Propose to purchase additional property to the
Waqf in T.S.No. 1666/1, 2 Door No. 23 & 23/1 Vadakarai Pallivasal Street, Periyakulam Taluk, Theni District measuring
1256¼ Sq ft. at the rate of Rs.450/- per Sq ft., from the Waqf Fund.
Objections and suggestions are invited on the above proposal U/s. 53 of the Waqf Act 1995 within 15 days from the date
of this notification as per Tamil Nadu Waqf Board’s  Resolution No.12/16 in R.C.No. 14684/02/B8/Theni Dt 27.01.2016
Chennai-600 001,
K. RASHEETH ALI,
14th March 2016.
District Revenue Officer/
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Waqf Board.
Taking over of Bakarsha Oliyullah Makizambu Thaikka Pallivasal, Rajakiri, Papanasam Taluk,
Thanjavur District, under Direct Management.
(Rc. No. 5708/15/B7/TNJ)
No.VI-3(a)/43/2016.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 (1) of the Waqf Act 1995 as amended by
Act 27/2013, the Tamil Nadu Waqf Board in its resolution in item No. 31/16 in Rc.No.5708/15/B7/TNJ
dated: 18-02-2016 has resolved to take over Bakarsha Oliyullah Makizambu Thaikka Pallivasal, Rajakiri, Papanasam Taluk,
Thanjavur District under the direct Management of the Tamil Nadu Waqf Board for a period of one year from the Board’s
Order dated: 18-02-2016 and appoint the Waqf Superintendent, Thanjavur as its Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,
K. RASHEETH ALI,
17th March 2016.
District Revenue Officer/
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Waqf Board.

Taking over of Ahle Hadees Pallivasal Waqf, Contonment, Poonthamallee, Tiruvallur District,
under Direct Management.
(Rc. No. 7951/06/B4/TVLR)
No.VI-3(a)/44/2016.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Waqf Act 1995, the Tamil Nadu Waqf
Board in its resolution in item No. 25/16 in Rc.No. 7951/06/B4/TVLR dated, 18-02-2016 has ordered to take over the Ahle
Hadees Pallivasal, Contonment, Poonthamallee, Tiruvallur District under direct management of the Tamil Nadu Waqf
Board for a period of One year from the date of the Board's order (18-02-2016) and to appoint Waqf Inspector,
Tiruvallur (I/c) as its Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,
K. RASHEETH ALI,
17th March 2016.
District Revenue Officer/
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Waqf Board.
