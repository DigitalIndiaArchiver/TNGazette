master  plan for the Dindigul Local  Planning Area approved under the said Act and published in the Housing 
and Urban Development Department Notiﬁ cation No:II(2)/HOU/611/2001, at page No:308 of Part-II—Section 2 of the 
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette dated the 15th August 2001.

changed as 664 to 666 ( except 665/5,6,7).
