HOME DEPARTMENT
Ad-interim order attaching of Money and Properties due
to default in return of deposits by Thiru Kannian alias
Babu, Managing Director and others of the said
Financial Establishment under Tamil Nadu Protection
of Interests of Depositors in Financial Establishment
Act.
[G.O. Ms. No. 215, Home (Police XIX),
23rd February 2010.]
No. II(2)/LE/174/2010.—Whereas, complaints have been
received from a number of depositors that M/s. Suprabath
Chits and Investments (Private) Limited & its Sister Concerns
Viz. M/s. Balaji Hire Purchase (Private) Limited and
M/s. Krishna Finance, Chennai Financial Establishments which
have defaulted the return of deposits made by the depositors
after maturity.
2. And, Whereas, the Government are satisfied that the
said Financial Establishments are not likely to return the
deposits to the depositors and hence, the Government have
to protect the interests of such depositors;
3. And, Whereas, except some ancestral properties, the
movable and immovable properties specified in Schedules I
and II to this order are alleged to have been procured by the
said Financial Establishments from and out of the deposits
collected from the depositors;

4. Now, Therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by
Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Protection of interests of
Depositors (in Financial Establishments) Act, 1997 (Tamil
Nadu Act 44 of 1997), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
makes and ad-interim order attaching the cash and other
properties of said Thiru K. Kannian Alias Babu, Managing
Director and others of M/s. Suprabath Chits and Investments
(private) Limited & its Sister Concerns Viz. M/s. Balaji Hire
Purchase (Private) Limited and M/s. Krishna Finance, Chennai
and the properties of Thiru T. Kannappan, Director of the said
Financial Establishment specified in Schedules I and II to this
order and transfers the control over the said cash and other
properties to the Competent Authority, namely, the Competent
Authority and District Revenue Officer, Chennai appointed
under the said Act, for the purpose.
5. The Competent Authority is requested to pursue further
action in accordance with the procedure laid down in
sub-sections (3) and (4) of Section 4 of the said Act and also
the formalities prescribed in the Tamil Nadu Protection of
interests of Depositors (in Financial Establishments)
Rules, 1997.
6. The Special Public Prosecutor, Special Court for the
Tamil Nadu Protection of Interests of Depositors (in Financial
Establishments) Act, 1997 is requested to render necessary
assistance to the  Competent Authority in filing the application
before the said Special Court.

Amendment to Notification
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 5 of the
Tamil Nadu Agricultural Labourers-Farmers (Social Security
and Welfare) Act, 2006 (Tamil Nadu Act 29 of 2006), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following
amendments to the Revenue Department Notification
No. II(2)/REV/22(c)/2007, published at pages 1-3 of
Part II-Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette
Extraordinary, dated the 18 January 2007, as subsequently
amended.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Notification,
1. Under the heading “1. Members representing the
Government”, after item 1, the following shall be inserted,
nemely:—
“1-A. Thiru K. Sellamuthu, Joint Chairman”,
President, Uzhavar Ulaipalar Party,
Kappalpatti Post,
Ottanchatram,
Dindigul District.
2. Under the heading “II. Members representing
Agricultural Labourers, Farmers and Employers”, item 7 and
the entries relating thereto shall be omitted.
K. DHANAVEL,
Secretary to Government.
