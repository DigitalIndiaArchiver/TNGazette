No.II(2)/HO/951/2019:—The following Notiﬁ cation of the 
Government of India President's Secretariat, New Delhi, the 
15th August, 2019 is Republished:—
No.103-Pres/2019-The President is pleased to award the 
Fire Service Medal for Gallantry to the under mentioned 
oﬃ  cer of Tamil Nadu Fire & Rescue Services:-
 
SHRI UMAPATHI DHANDAPANI,
  
FIREMAN. 
2. On 13-03-2019 at 0200 hrs a rescue call received 
at Fire & Rescue Station, Pollachi, Distt. Coimbatore from 
Gomangalam Police Station that a car heading towards 
Coimbatore had fallen into the PAP canal, full of water, 
near Gedimedu, in Pollachi-Palani NH road. Immediately 
a rescue team from Pollachi Station rushed to the 
spot. On arrival, the crew observed that the canal is 
20 feet wide, 15 feet deep. Water is ﬂ owing up to the 
brim with water current approximately 1000 cubic feet 
per second. Fireman Sh.Umapathi Dhandapani without 
caring to the risk involved to his own life, he plung into 
the canal with the help of a crane and identiﬁ ed the car at 
the bottom of the canal. The ﬁ reman noticed a young lady, 
who had lost her life, in semi-nude condition. He arranged 
one saree from the persons standing on the banks of the 
canal and approached the dead body below the water level 
and lifted up her after neatly covered with saree. Again he 
approached to the bottom of the canal and made arrangement 
for lifting of the car and 7 other dead bodies. 
3. Fireman, Sh.Umapathi Dhandapani thus displayed 
conspicuous gallantry, exemplary courage, professional 
expertise and devotion to duty of a very high order. 
4. These awards are made for Gallantry under Rule 3(i) 
of the Rules governing the award of Fire Service Medal for 
Gallantry and consequently carries with it special allowance 
admissible under rule 5 (a) w.e.f. 13-03-2019.
