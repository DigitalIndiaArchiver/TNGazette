ADVERTISEMENT OF PETITION
Notice is hereby given that a petition for winding up of the above named company by the High Court at Madras was on
the 3rd day of January 2011 presented to the said Court by Mr. Nikhil Nayak, represented by its Power Agent, M/s. Mahindra
Logistics Ltd., Corporate Office, 2nd Floor, 271, Business Park, Vishweshwar Nagar Road, Goregaon (E), Mumbai-400 063
as creditor and the petition is directed to be heard before the Court on the 6th day of June, 2011.
Any creditor, contributory or other person desirous of supporting or poising the making of an order on the said petition
should sent to the petitioner or his advocate notice of his intention signed by him or his advocate with his and name and
address, so as to reach the petitioner or his advocate not later than 5 days before the date fixed for the hearing of the
petition, and appear at the hearing for the purpose in person or by advocate. A copy of the petition will be furnished by the
undersigned to any creditor or contributory on payment of the prescribed charges for the same.
Any affidavit intended to be used in opposition to the petition should be filed in Court and a copy served on the petitioner
or his advocate not less than 5 days before the date fixed for the hearing.
