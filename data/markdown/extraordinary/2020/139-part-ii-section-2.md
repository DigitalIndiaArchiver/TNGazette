The Governor of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that the 
lands specified in the Schedule below  have to be acquired 
for Highways purpose for the Formation of Mettupalayam 
Bypass at Karamadai Village, Coimbatore and it has already 
been decided that the entire amount of compensation to be 
awarded for the lands is to be paid out of the funds controlled 
or managed by the Divisional Engineer (H)  Construction 
and Maintenance after considering the cause shown by the 
owner (or) other person having interest on such lands as the 
case may be do hereby publish the following notice  under 
sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways 
Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu 
Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) the Governor 
of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the land specified in the 
Schedule below measuring to an extent of 144790.0 sq.mts of 
Ryotwari Dry lands in  Karamadai Village, Coimbatore District 
for the Formation of Mettupalayam Bypass in Coimbatore 
District .
The plan of the lands is kept in the office of the 
Sub Collector, Coimbatore and may be inspected at any time 
during office hours.
