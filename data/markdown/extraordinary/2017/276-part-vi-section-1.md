JUDICIAL NOTIFICATION
CONSTITUTION OF SUB COURT AT MUDUKULATHUR IN RAMANATHAPURAM DISTRICT
UNDER TAMIL NADU CIVIL COURTS ACT
(Roc. No. 979/2014/G/Judn.)
No. VI(1)/273(a)/2017.
“In exercise of the powers conferred under the first  proviso to Section 13 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts
Act, 1873  (Central Act III of 1873), the High Court, Madras hereby  directs that appeals from the decrees
or orders of the District  Munsif Court, Mudukulathur (viz., Mudukulathur and  Kadaladi Taluks) and District
Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate  Court, Kamuthi (viz., Kamuthi Taluk) be preferred in the court  of Subordinate
Judge, Mudukulathur with effect on and from  the date on which the Subordinate Judge, Mudukulathur  assumes
charge of that Court.”
High Court, Madras,
SELVI R. POORNIMA,
24th August 2017.
Registrar General (In-charge)
