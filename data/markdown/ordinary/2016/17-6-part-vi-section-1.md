GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
Variation to the Review Approved Vellore Master Plan for the Vellore Local Planning Area
(Roc.No.104/2016/VLPA.)
No. VI(1)/172/2016.
In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (4) of Section 32 of Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning
Act 1971, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following variation of the Review Master Plan approved
with the Housing and Urban Development Department Notification No. II(2)/HOU/(g-1)/2011 and published in
Part II—Section 2 No. 75 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated 25th February 2011.
VARIATION
In the said Master Plan under the permitted land use under the sub-heading “Agricultural use zone” the expressions
S.F. Nos. 45/2 to 9 of Vandranthangal Village shall be deleted and S.F.Nos. 45/2 to 4, 45/5A, 45/6 to 9 & 45/11 to
13 shall be included. Under sub-heading “Educational use zone” the expression S.F. No. 45/5B of Vandranthangal
Village shall be included after the S.F.No. 33/2 in the Review Approved Vellore Master Plan.
Vellore-9,
R. VAZHAVANDAN,
18th April 2016.
Member-Secretary (In-charge),
Vellore Local Planning Authority.
