NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
DTP—II-2 Ex. (169)
ANIMAL HUSBANDRY, DAIRYING AND FISHERIES DEPARTMENT
DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF THE TAMIL NADU FISHERIES UNIVERSITY ACT, 2012
(TAMIL NADU ACT 21 OF 2012).
[G.O. Ms. No. 102, Animal Husbandry, Dairying and Fisheries (FS4), 19th June  2012,
Aani 5, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2043.]
No. II(2)/AHDF/308(g-1)/2012.
Under sub-section (4) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Fisheries University Act, 2012 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 2012),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the 19th June 2012, as the date on which the said Act shall come into
force.
DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF THE TAMIL NADU VETERINARY AND ANIMAL SCIENCES
UNIVERSITY (AMENDMENT) ACT, 2012 (ACT No. 20 OF 2012)
[G.O. Ms. No. 103, Animal Husbandry, Dairying and Fisheries (FS4), 19th June  2012,
Aani 5, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2043.]
No. II(2)/AHDF/308(g-2)/2012.
Under sub-section (2) of  Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Veterinary and Animal Sciences University (Amendment)
Act, 2012 (Tamil Nadu Act 20 of 2012), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the 19th June 2012, as
the date on which the said Act shall come into force.
GAGANDEEP SINGH BEDI,
Secretary to Government.
