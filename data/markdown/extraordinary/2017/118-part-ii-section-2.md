APPOINTMENT OF VACATION CIVIL JUDGES FOR THE SUMMER VACATION OF CIVIL COURTS FOR
THE YEAR 2017 UNDER THE TAMIL NADU CIVIL COURTS ACT
[G.O. (D).No. 525, Home (Cts-III), 28th April 2017.]
No. II(2)/HO/317(b)/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 30 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act,
1873 (Central Act III of 1873), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, in consultation with the High Court, Madras,
hereby appoints the Judicial Officers specified in column (2) of the Table below to be vacation Civil Judges
for the District Courts and Subordinate courts in the Districts specified in the corresponding entries in
column (3) thereof for the Summer Vacation of the Civil Courts for the year 2017.
