GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
Variations to the Approved Master Plan for the Coimbatore Local Planning Area.
(Roc. 2358 /2014 /LPA-1 )
No. VI(1)/206/2016.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 32 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning
Act 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972) and in exercise of powers conferred by the G.O.Ms.No.94, Housing and Urban
Development (UD4(1) Department, dated 12.06.2009 which has been published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette
No.27, part II, Section 2, Page No. 228, dated 15.07.2009 the following variations are made to the master plan for the
Coimbatore Local Planning Area approved under the said Act and published in the Housing and Urban Development
Department Notification No.II(2)/HOU/4377/94 at page 1078 of Part II-section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette
dated, the 9th November 1994.
VARIATIONS
In the said Master Plan, in the “LAND USE SCHEDULE” under the heading  “(COIMBATORE LOCAL PLANNING AREA
(OTHER THAN CORPORATION AREA)” under the sub-heading (a) Notified Detailed Development Plan Area in Madukkarai
Taluk, Chettipalayam Village,
         i. Against the entry “Agricultural – Ag 35”  for the expression 298 to 303,  the
expression 298 to 303 (except 298/1, 298/2pt) shall be substituted.
ii.
Against the entry “Residential –MR 16” the expression 298/1, 298/2pt shall be
added before the entry 214 to 217.
Coimbatore,
C. MATHIVANAN,
1st June 2016
Member Secretary (in-charge),
Coimbatore Local Planning Authority.
