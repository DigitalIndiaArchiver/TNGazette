GENERAL  NOTIFICATIONS
Variations to the Approved Hosur New Town Development Plan for the Hosur New Town Development Authority.
(Roc. No. 959/06/A2.)
No. VI(1)/411/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4) of  Section 32 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning
Act 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972) and in exercise of powers conferred by the G.O. Ms. No. 94, Housing and Urban
Development [(UD 4(1)] Department,  Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Issue No. 27, Part II—Section 2, page No. 228,
dated 15th July 2009 of the following variations are made to the Hosur New Town Development Plan approved under the
said Act and published in the Housing and Urban Development Department Notification No. II(2)/HOU/286/84 at page 447
of Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated 23rd May 1984.
VARIATIONS
In the said New Town Development Plan in Annexure 3 in the tabular column under the heading Industrial use
zone (IGI) against serial No. 32. In the survey numbers belonging to Achettipalli Village the expression
S.F. Nos. 1185/2A, 2B, 1186/2A, 2B, 2D, 1187/A, 1188/A shall be added.
Hosur,
C. BASKARATHONDAIMAN,
10th November 2011.
Member-Secretary (in-charge),
Hosur New Town Development Authority.
Approval of Krishnapuram Detailed Development Plan No. 1 of Tirunelveli Local Planning Authority, Tirunelveli.
Errata to Notification
(Roc. No. 837/08/TLPA)
The General notification published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Issue No. 28, Part VI—Section 1,
page No. 300, dated 27th July 2011 in publication No. VI(1)/304/2011, shall be read with the following corrections:—
1. In the heading the name “Krishnapuram Detailed Development Plan No. 1” shall be replaced as
“Keelanatham Detailed Development Plan No. 3”
2.
In the Form No. 12 in paragraph No. 1 after the word April 2011 has approved the “Krishnapuram Detailed
Development Plan No. 1” shall be replaced as “Keelanatham Detailed Development Plan No. 3”
Tirunelveli2,
S.P. MOHAMED ALI,
10th November 2011.
Member-Secretary, (in-charge),
Tirunelveli Local Planning Authority.
