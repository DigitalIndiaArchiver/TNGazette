ORDER OF THE ELECTION COMMISSION OF INDIA 
DISQUALIFICATION OF THIRU V.KRISHNAMOORTHY CANDIDATE CONTESTED FROM 98- ERODE (EAST) ASSEMBLY 
CONSTITUENCY IN BYE-ELECTION TO LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF TAMIL NADU, 2023 FOR FAILURE TO 
LODGE THE ACCOUNTS OF ELECTION EXPENSES UNDER SECTION 10-A OF THE REPRESENTATION OF 
THE PEOPLE ACT, 1951 BY THE ELECTION COMMISSION OF INDIA.
No. SRO G-34/2024.
The following order of the Election Commission of India, Nirvachan Sadan, Ashoka Road, New Delhi-110 001, 
dated.30th September, 2024 [08 Ashwin, 1946 (Saka)] is re-published:-
No.76/TN-LA/98/BYE/2023/CEMS-II
WHEREAS, the Bye Election to Legislative Assembly of Tamil Nadu, 2023 for 98 – Erode (East) Assembly 
Constituency was held in pursuance of the Election Commission of India Notification No. 100/TN-LA/1/2023, dated 
31st January, 2023; and
WHEREAS, as per Section 78 of the Representation of the People Act, 1951, every contesting candidate at an 
election shall, within 30 days from the date of election of the returned candidate, lodge with the District Election Officer 
an account of his election expenses which shall be a true copy of the account kept by him or his election agent under 
Section 77 of the said act; and
WHEREAS, the result of the Bye-election for 98 – Erode (East) Assembly Constituency of Tamil Nadu, 2023 was 
declared by the Returning Officer on 02nd March, 2023 and hence the last date for lodging the account of Election 
Expenses was 01st April, 2023; and
WHEREAS, as per the report dated 11.04.2023 of District Election Officer, Erode under sub rule (1) of Rule 89 
of the Conduct of Elections Rules, 1961 received through the CEO, Tamil Nadu vide his letter no. 486/Ele-IV/2023-11, 
dated 13.04.2023 stating that V. Krishnamoorthi, a contesting candidate from 98-Erode (East) Assembly Constituency 
in Bye Election to Legislative Assembly of Tamil Nadu, 2023 has failed to lodge account of his election expenses within 
the prescribed time (delay of 02 days), as required under law and
WHEREAS, on basis of the said report of the District Election Officer, Erode, a ‘Show-Cause Notice’ 
No. 76/TN-LA/98/ BYE/2023/CEMS-II, dated 10th August, 2023 was issued under sub rule (5) of Rule 89 of the Conduct 
of Elections Rules, 1961 by the Election Commission of India to V. Krishnamoorthi, for not lodging the account of 
Election Expenses within the prescribed time; and
WHEREAS, through the above said ‘Show Cause Notice’ and under sub rule (6) of Rule 89 of the Conduct of 
Elections Rules, 1961, V. Krishnamoorthi was directed to submit his representation in writing to the Commission 
explaining the reasons for not lodging the account within the time with the District Election Officer, Erode within 
20 days from the date of receipt of the notice; and

WHEREAS, the District Election Officer, Erode in his report no.1232/2023/K1; dated 18.10.2023 reported that the 
Show Cause Notice served to V.Krishnamoorthi through registered post on 30.08.2023; and
WHEREAS, the District Election Officer, Erode in his Supplementary Reports no.1232/2023/K1, dated 22.12.2023 
and 31.01.2024 on account of candidate received through CEO Tamil Nadu letters No. 486/Ele-IV/2023-20, dated 
02.01.2024 and 486/Ele-IV/2023-22; dated 17.02.2024 has reported that V.Krishnamoorthi has not submitted any 
representation on delayed submission of account of his election expenses. Further, after receipt of the said notice, 
V. Krishnamoorthi has neither furnished any reason or explanation to the Election Commission of India for failure to 
lodge the account of his election expenses on time as prescribed under law, and
WHEREAS, Section 10A of the Representation of the People Act, 1951 provides that:
	
“If the Election Commission is satisfied that a person-
	
(a) has failed to lodge an account of election expenses within the time and in the manner required by or 
under this Act, and
	
(b) has no good reason or justification for the failure.
the Election Commission shall, by order published in the Official Gazette, declare him to be disqualified and any 
such person shall be disqualified for a period of three years from the date of the order.”;
WHEREAS, on basis of the facts and available records, the Commission is satisfied that V. Krishnamoorthi has 
failed to lodge his account of election expenses in the prescribed time and has no good reason or justification for the 
failure to do so, and
NOW, THEREFORE, in pursuance of Section 10A of the Representation of the People Act. 1951, the Election 
Commission of India hereby declares V. Krishnamoorthi, resident of - 3/367 Alankattu Pudur, Periyapalayam, Uthukuli 
(PO) Tiruppur(DT) 641 607 and a contesting candidate from 98-Erode (East) Assembly Constituency in Bye Election 
to Legislative Assembly of Tamil Nadu, 2023, to be disqualified for being chosen as and for being a member of either 
House of the Parliament or the Legislative Assembly or the Legislative Council of a State or Union Territory for a 
period of three years from the date of this order.
