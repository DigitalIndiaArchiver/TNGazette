 Extension of Direct Management of  Khazimar Peria Pallivasal, Madurai Town and District. 
(Rc. No. 13575/02/B8/MDU)
No. VI-3(a)/41/2018.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the waqf Act, 1995 as amended by Act 27 of 2013, 
the period of Direct Management of Khazimar Peria Pallivasal, Madurai is extended for a further period of one year from 
23rd October 2017 or till elections are held whichever is earlier.
