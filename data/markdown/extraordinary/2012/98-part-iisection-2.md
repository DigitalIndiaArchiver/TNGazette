NOTICE

(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands specified in the Schedule below and
measuring 4.50.0 hectares to the same a little more or less needed for Industrial purposes, to wit, for Second  Mine
Expansion Scheme of Neyveli Lignite Corporation Limited.
The plan  of the lands are kept in the office of the Special Deputy  Collector / Land Acquisition, Neyveli-2 and may
be inspected at any  time during office hours.
