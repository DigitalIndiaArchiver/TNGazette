No. SRO B-31/2013.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Medical Service
(Section 21 in Volume II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1969).
2. The Amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 11th January 2008.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules, in Part II, in Branch-I Medical—
(1)  for the categories “Non-Clinical Readers”, “Non-Clinical Tutors or Assistants”, ”Clinical Readers” and “Clinical
Tutors or Assistants” wherever they occur, the following categories shall, respectively be substituted, namely:-
“Non-Clinical Associate Professors”, “Tutors I Assistant Professors”, ”Clinical Associate Professors” and “Senior
Residents I Assistant Professors”;
(2)  in rule 1, under ‘CLASS I’, after the expression “Category 20 - Civil Surgeon Specialists”, the following expression
shall be added, namely~-
“Category 21- Professor”;
(3)  in rule 2, in sub- rule (a), under the heading “Civil Surgeon’s Cadre” in Column (1), after the expression “Category
20 - Civil Surgeon Specialists”, the following expression shall be added, namely:-
“Category 21 - Professor”;
(4) in rule 3, in sub-rule (a), under the heading ‘CLASS I’, after the expression ”Category 20 - Civil Surgeon
Specialists” in the first column and the entries relating thereto in the second column, the following expression and entries
shall, respectively be added, namely:-
“Category 21- Professor:
By Promotion from among the holders of the post of Associate Professors”;
(5) in the Annexure [referred to in rule (8c)], -
(i) for Notes (a), (b), (c) and (d) after “Class-I, Category 10”, the following Notes shall be substituted, namely:-
“(a) No person shall be appointed to the post of Associate Professor in any subject, either Clinical or Non - Clinical
unless he possesses teaching experience for a period of not less than five years as Assistant Professor
in the speciality concerned.
(b)  No person shall be appointed to the post of Associate Professor in the Super speciality unless he possesses
teaching experience for a period of not less than two years as Assistant Professor in the speciality
concerned”;
(ii) After Notes (a) and (b) as so substituted, Notes “(e)” and “(f)” shall be re- lettered as “(c)” and “(d)” respectively;
(iii) in Note (c) as so re-Iettered,
(a) For the expression “clauses (a) to (d) above”, the expression “this Annexure” shall be substituted;
(b) For the expression “University of Madras”, the expression “Tamil Nadu Dr.M.G.R. Medical Univesity” shall be
substituted;
(iv) in Note (d) as so re-Iettered, for the expression “clauses (a) to (e) above”, the expression “this Annexure” shall
be substituted;

7. PROBATION:- (i) Every person appointed to any category by direct recruitment shall, from the date on which he
joins duty be on probation for a total period of two years on duty with in a continuous period of three years.

duty, be on probation for a total period of one year on duty with in a continuous period of two years.
