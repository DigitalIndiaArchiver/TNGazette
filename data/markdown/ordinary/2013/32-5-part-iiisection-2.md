JUDICIAL NOTIFICATIONS
Amendment to the Madras High Court Service Rules.
(Roc. No. 05/2013/RAC.)
No. SRO C-8/2013.—
In exercise of the powers conferred by Article 229 (1) and (2) of the Constitution of India and of all other
powers connected thereto, the Hon’ble the Acting Chief Justice is pleased to make the following amendment to the
Madras High Court Service Rules:—
AMENDMENT
In the provisos to Rule 6 (b) (2) of Madras High Court Service Rules, the words "twenty five percentage”
wherever occur, shall be substituted with the words “seventy five percentage”.
The above amendment shall come into effect from 26th July 2013.
High Court, Madras,
P. KALAIYARASAN,
30th July 2013.
Registrar-General.
