ENERGY DEPARTMENT
AUTHORIZATION OF THE TAMIL NADU ELECTRICITY BOARD TO CONTINUE TO FUNCTION AS THE
STATE TRANSMISSION UTILITY AND A LICENSEE UNDER THE ELECTRICITY ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 43, Energy (B2), 30th April 2010.]
No. II(2)/EGY/232(d-1)/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to clause (a) of Section 172 of the Electricity Act, 2003
(Central Act 36 of 2003), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby authorizes the Tamil Nadu Electricity Board to
continue to function as the State Transmission Utility and a Licensee under the provisions of the said Act for a
further period upto 15-6-2010 beyond 15-3-2010 as mutually decided by the Central Government and this
Government.
NOTIFICATION REGARDING EXEMPTION IN RESPECT OF THE ELECTRICITY TAX PAYABLE UNDER THE
TAMIL NADU TAX ON CONSUMPTION OR SALE OF ELECTRICITY ACT.
[G.O.Ms. No. 44, Energy (B1), 30th April 2010.]
No. II(2)/EGY/232(d-2)/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred by the sub-section (2) of Section 14 of the Tamil Nadu Tax on Consumption
or Sale of Electricity Act, 2003 (Tamil Nadu Act 12 of 2003), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes
exemption in respect of the Electricity Tax payable under the said Act on the consumption of electricity for own
use by H.T. consumers using their captive generating plants namely, gensets for a further period from
1st October 2009 to 31st March 2010.
P.W.C. DAVIDAR,
Secretary to Government.
