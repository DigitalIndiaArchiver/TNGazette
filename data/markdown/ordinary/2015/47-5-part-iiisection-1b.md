No. SRO B-86/2015.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu  Commercial
Taxes Subordinate Service (Section—4 in Volume—III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1970).
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules, in the Annexure, in the tabular column, in column (3), against the entry “2. Deputy Commercial Tax
Officer” in column (1) and against the entry “Recruitment by transfer from among the Assistants in the Tamil Nadu Ministerial
Service and Assistant Section Officers in the Departments of Secretariat” in column (2),—
(1) for clause (a), the following clause shall be substituted, namely:—“(a) service of not less than two years as Assistant
in the Commercial Taxes Department including the office of the Commissioner of Commercial Taxes or in the office of the
Tamil Nadu Sales Tax Appellate Tribunal or Service of not less than two years as Assistant Section Officer in the Commercial
Taxes and Registration Department of Secretariat. Such Service shall not include the period spent on unearned leave and
extraordinary leave.”; and
(2) in clause (b), the Provisos and explanations after sub-clause (iv) shall be omitted.
Md. NASIMUDDIN,
Principal Secretary to Government. (FAC)

No. SRO B-87/2015.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following rules:—
2. The rules hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force with effect on and from the 15th June, 2015.
RULES
The General Rules applicable to the holders of the permanent posts in the Tamil Nadu General Service shall apply to the
holder of the temporary post of Administrative Officer (Legal), in the Office of the Director of Public Health and Preventive
Medicine, Chennai, subject to the modifications specified in the following rules:—
2. Constitution:—The post shall constitute a distinct class in the said service.
3. Appointing Authority:—The appointing authority for the post shall be the Director of Public Health and Preventive
Medicine, Chennai.
4. Method of Appointment:—Appointment to the post shall be made by recruitment by transfer from among the holders
of the post of Superintendent in the Tamil Nadu Ministerial Service in the Department of Public Health and Preventive Medicine.

5. Qualification:—No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post by recruitment by transfer unless he possesses
the following qualifications, namely:—
(a) A Bachelor Degree in Law, and
(b) Must have put in a service for a period of not less than ten years in the Tamil Nadu Ministerial Service in the
Directorate of Public Health and Preventive Medicine.
6. Probation:—Every person appointed to the post by recruitment by transfer shall from the date on which he joins duty
be on probation for a total period of one year on duty within a continuous period of two years.
7. Pay:—There shall be paid to the holder of the post, a monthly pay calculated in the Pay band-3 of Rs. 15600-39100
with Grade Pay of Rs.5400/-.
J. RADHAKRISHNAN,
Secretary to Government.

No. SRO B-88/2015.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Education Department Notification No.SRO A-560/78,
published at page Nos..679-682 of  Part-III-Section 1(b) of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated 8th November, 1978.
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 8th November 1978.

hereunto enabling, and in supersession of the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Educational Service in section 8 of

and Junior Professors, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Legal
Educational Service which will be included as section 33 under Part III-A of Volume-II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual,
1969.”.
