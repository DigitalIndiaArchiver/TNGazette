 WHEREAS, a written notice of the intention to make a motion expressing want of conﬁ dence in Tmt.S.Chandra, 
Vice-Chairman, Vedasandur Panchayat Union Council, Dindigul district, signed by twelve members, out of the sanctioned 
strength of ﬁ fteen members of the said Council, together with a copy of the motion proposed to be made and a written 
statement of charges against the said Vice-Chairman have been delivered to the Revenue Divisional Oﬃ  cer, Palani, Dindigul 
district in person by twelve members as per sub-section (2) of section 212 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994 
(Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994); 
 
AND WHEREAS, the Revenue Divisional Oﬃ  cer, Palani, Dindigul district caused a copy of the statement of charges 
along with a copy of the notice of motion to be delivered, to the said Vice-Chairman Tmt.S.Chandra and she has not submitted 
her reply to the charges; 
 
AND WHEREAS, a motion expressing want of conﬁ dence in Tmt.S.Chandra, Vice-Chairman, Vedasandur Panchayat 
Union Council, Dindigul district had been carried with a support of twelve members out of the sanctioned strength of ﬁ fteen 
members of the said Panchayat Union Council in the meeting convened by the Revenue Divisional Oﬃ  cer, Palani, Dindigul 
district on the 16th November 2022 after following the procedures speciﬁ ed in the said section 212 and forwarded the same 
to Government; 
 
NOW THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (13) of section 212 of the Tamil Nadu 
Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby removes Tmt.S.Chandra, Vice-
Chairman, Vedasandur Panchayat Union Council, Dindigul district from the oﬃ  ce of the Vice-Chairman of the said Panchayat 
Union Council with immediate eﬀ ect.
