[ Part VI—Sec.1
Variation to the Approved Master Plan for the Tiruchirappalli Local Planning Area.
(Roc. No. 1725/2010/TLPA-2)
FORM No. 14.
[G.O. (2D) No.102, Housing and Urban Development  (UD-4-1) Department, dated 1st July 2011.]
No.VI(1)/345/2011.
1. In exercise of the powers delegated to Local Planning Authority for reclassification streamlining procedure under Town
and Country Planning Act, 1971 published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Issue No.27, Part II Section 2 Page No.228,
dated 15th July 2009, The Member-Secretary, Tiruchirapplli Local Planning Authority hereby makes the following variation
under Section (A) of Section 32 of Town and Country Planning Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972) to the Master Plan
of Tiruchirappalli Local Planning area approved under the said Act and published with the Housing and Urban Development
notification No.II (2)/HOU/453/09 at page 319 of Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette dated the
2nd September 2009.
VARIATION
In the said Master Plan in the “Land Use Schedule” under the heading Tiruchirappalli Corporation sub-heading
Ward-D, Block 27,
(i)  against the entry commercial, the expression 72 shall be added
(ii)  against the entry Mixed residential use Zone, the expression 70 to 73 shall be substituted by the expression
70 to 73 (except 72).
C. MATHIVANAN,
Tiruchirappalli-01,
Member-Secretary (In-charge),
8th July 2011.

Variations to the Approved Master Plan for the Erode Local Planning Area.
(Roc.No.685/2009/ELPA-2)
No.VI(1)/346/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4) of the Section 32 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning
Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972) and in exercise of Powers conferred by the G.O.Ms No.94, Housing and Urban
Development (UD4(1) Department, dated 12th June 2009 Which has been published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette
No.27 Part II—Section 2, Page No.228, dated 15th July 2009 the following variations are made to the master Plan for the
Erode Local Planning Area approved under the said Act and Published in the Housing and Urban Development Department
Notification No. II(2)/HOU/1104/90, at page 115 of Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the
28th February 1990.
VARIATIONS
In the said Master Plan, in the land use schedule tabular column,
(i) Under the heading “Local Planning area Villages” under the sub-heading “MR-16” V.No.67. Periyasemur against
the entry in column No. 7, the expression. “1 to 90” shall be read as the expression 1 to 49 (except 49/11B, 12D, 17C,
18B, 19B, 20,21, 22B,23B and 24) to 90” and
(ii) Under the heading “ Local Planning Area Villages” under the sub-heading “1-12, V.No.67. Periyasemur against
the entry in column No. 7, the expression 49/ 11B, 12D, 17C, 18B,  19B, 20, 21, 22B, 23B, and 24 shall be added before
the expression 197/p.
K.  SABAPATHY,
Erode,
