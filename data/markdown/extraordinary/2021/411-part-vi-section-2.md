WHEREAS, the ordinary election to ﬁ ll up the oﬃ  ce of the Ward No.11 of the Tiruvarur District Panchayat was, 
inter-alia, notiﬁ ed to be held in accordance with the programme of election dates ﬁ xed in the notiﬁ cation vide Tamil Nadu 
State Election Commission S.O.No.85/2019/TNSEC/PE1 and S.O.No.87/2019/TNSEC/PE1, dated 07.12.2019 and published;
WHEREAS, the District Election Oﬃ  cer/District Collector, Tiruvarur District, based on the report of the Returning 
Oﬃ  cer, has reported on 20.12.2019 for adjourning the poll of the above said ward member election, under rule 36(c) 
of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Elections) Rules, 1995, due to the death of Thiru. G. Mahalingam, a validly nominated 
candidate of a recognized political party viz., All India Anna Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam, who expired on 19.12.2019 
i.e., after publication of his name in Form No.9 and before the commencement of the poll;
WHEREAS, due to outbreak of COVID-19 Pandemic situation from March, 2020 prevailed for more than eighteen 
months, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission is not in a position to conduct fresh poll for the above said ward;
WHEREAS, the Election Commission of India published ﬁ nal electoral rolls for the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly 
Constituencies on 20.01.2021 and based on which, elections to the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Constituencies were 
conducted on 06.04.2021;

WHEREAS, rule 14 and rule 17 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Elections) Rules, 1995, provide that the electoral 
roll of a Panchayat shall be prepared by reference to the qualifying date, by the Panchayat Electoral Registration Oﬃ  cer, 
by integrating the particulars in the various entries available in such part or portion thereof of the current electoral roll 
of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly and the various amendments issued to it, from time to time, as they relate 
to the Ward of the Village Panchayat or a portion thereof; and that the electoral roll shall be prepared before every 
ordinary or casual election;
WHEREAS, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission directed the District Election Oﬃ  cers/District Collectors of 
newly constituted 9 Districts viz., Chengalpattu, Kancheepuram, Vellore, Ranipet, Tirupathur, Viluppuram, Kallakurichi, 
Tirunelveli and Tenkasi and other 28 Districts where the oﬃ  ces were not ﬁ lled up due to certain reasons during ordinary 
elections 2019 and the oﬃ  ces reported by the District Election Oﬃ  cers/District Collectors as vacant up to 30.6.2021 due 
to the reason of death and resignation, the panchayat electoral rolls should be prepared with the entries available in 
the current electoral rolls of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly;
WHEREAS, the Panchayat Electoral Registration Oﬃ  cer concerned prepared panchayat electoral rolls with the entries 
available in the current electoral roll of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly and the same was published on 31.8.2021 
as per rule 20 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Elections) Rules, 1995; 
WHEREAS, after considering the time gap and publication of Panchayat Electoral Rolls based on the current 
Assembly Constituencies Electoral Rolls, the Commission desired the adjourned election of Ward 11 of the Tiruvarur 
District Panchayat could not be conducted at present;
AND WHEREAS, after taking into account of all above material events and unforeseen circumstances and in 
pursuance of rule 36 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayat (Elections) Rules, 1995, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission 
has decided to conduct fresh election for the Ward No.11 of Tiruvarur District Panchayat on a later date;
NOW, THEREFORE, in view of the above said reason and in exercise of the powers conferred under Article 
243-K of the Constitution of India and rule 143 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Elections) Rules, 1995, the Tamil Nadu 
State Election Commission hereby orders that the adjourned election to Ward No.11 of the Tiruvarur District Panchayat 
comprised in Mannargudi Panchayat Union notiﬁ ed and published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary 
issue No.455, Part VI – Section 2, dated 9th December, 2019 vide notiﬁ cation number VI(2)/121(a)/2019 at page No.6 
under column (3), Ward No.11 of the Tiruvarur District Panchayat comprised in 148 Mannargudi, is  hereby cancelled. 
Fresh election will be held on the date to be ﬁ xed by the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission.
