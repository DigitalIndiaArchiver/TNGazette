ACQUISITION OF LANDS
(Rc. No. CPRRP/CLA/15(1)/016/2022)
NOTICE UNDER SECTION 15(1) OF TAMIL NADU HIGHWAYS ACT, 2001 (TAMIL NADU ACT 34 OF 2002), READ WITH 
TAMIL NADU LAND ACQUISITION LAWS. (REVIVAL OF OPERATION, AMENDMENT AND VALIDATION) ACT, 2019.
No. VI(1)/149(a)/2022.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that the lands speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below are acquired under 
Chennai Peripheral Ring Road Project for Highways purpose to wit, for formation of road connectivity from Ennore Port to 
Mahabalipuram (from Km 57.668 to Km 60.881) at No.97.Vengathur (Block-II) Village Tiruvallur Taluk, Tiruvallur District and it 
having already been decided that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands to be paid out of the funds 
controlled and managed by the Highways Department and after having considering the cause shown by the owner or other 
persons having interest on such lands, as the case may be, do hereby publish the following notice under sub-section (1) of 
Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) read with the Tamil Nadu Land Acquisition Laws 
(Revival of Operation, Amendment and Validation) Act, 2019.
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002), read with the 
Tamil Nadu Land Acquisition Laws (Revival of Operation, Amendment and Validation Act, 2019) the Governor of Tamil Nadu 
hereby notiﬁ es that the Dry patta lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below and measuring to an extent of 95, 866 Sq.Mtrs. (09.58.66 
Hectares) of lands are acquired in No.97.Vengathur (Block-II) Village Tiruvallur Taluk, Tiruvallur District to the same a little more 
or less needed for the purpose to wit for formation of road connectivity from Ennore Port to Mahabalipuram (from Km 57.668 
to Km 60.881) as part of Chennai Peripheral Ring Road Project, Highways Department. 
The Plan of the lands under acquisition are kept in the oﬃ  ce of the Special District Revenue Oﬃ  cer (Land Acquisition), 
Chennai Peripheral Ring Road Project, Section-III, Chennai-600 058 and in the oﬃ  ce of the Special Tahsildar (Land Acquisition), 
Chennai Peripheral Ring Road Project, Unit-IV, Tiruvallur and may be inspected at any time during the oﬃ  ce hours.
