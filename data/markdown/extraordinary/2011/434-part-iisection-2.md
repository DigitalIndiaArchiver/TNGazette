The Governor  of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that the
lands specified in the schedule below have to be acquired for
Industrial Purpose , to wit for the  formation of ULTRA MEGA
POWER PROJECT and it have already been decided that the
entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands is
to be paid out of the funds controlled or managed by the
Coastal Tamil Nadu Power Limited, New Delhi  and having
considered the cause shown by the owners or other persons
having interest the said lands as the case may be do hereby
published the following notice under sub-section (1) of
Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial
Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Industrial  Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby acquired the lands specified in the
schedule below and measuring an extent of  5.98.5 hectares to
the same, a little more or less are needed for Industrial purpose,
to wit, for the formation of Ultra Mega Power Project  No. 53.
Vilangadu Village, Cheyyur Taluk.
The plan of the lands are kept in the office of the Special
Tahsildar, Land Acquisition, Unit-V, Ultra Mega Power Project,
Cheyyur may be inspected at any time during office hours.

Kancheepuram District, Cheyyur Taluk,

 (Unit- V, Block-12.)

Total—5.98.5 hectares.
