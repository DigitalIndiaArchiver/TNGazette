INDUSTRIES DEPARTMENT
ACQUISITION OF LAND
[G.O. Ms. No.69, Industries (MIE-2), 19th March 2012.]
No. II(2)/IND/158(b)/2012.
The Government of Tamil Nadu have been satisfied that
the lands specified in the schedule below have to be acquired
for industrial purposes for setting up of Mahindra Industrial
Park Limited and it having already been decided that the entire
amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be
paid out of the funds controlled or managed by Mahindra
Industrial Park Limited (now renamed as Mahindra World City
Developers Limited), Administrative Block, Central Avenue,
Mahindra World City, Chengalpattu Taluk, Kancheepuram
District.  The following notice is issued under sub-section (1)
of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial
Purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTIFICATION
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Acquisition of Land  for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby acquires the lands specified in the schedule below  and
measuring 0.01.0 hectare to the same, a little more or less
needed for Industrial Purposes, i.e., Mahindra Industrial Park
Limited.
The plan of the lands are kept in the office of the RDO,
Chengalpattu and may be inspected at any time during office
hours.
THE SCHEDULE
 Kancheepuram District, Chengalpattu  Taluk,
220. Veerapuram Village.
Block No. VII
Ryotwari, wet, S.No.98/3 belonging to and registered in the
name of Duraisamy Naicker, bounded on the north by S.No.97,
east by S.No.98/4, south by S.No.98/7 and west by
S.No.97/2—0.01.0 hectare.
