In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 164 of the Tamil Nadu Goods and Services Tax Act, 
2017 (Tamil Nadu Act 19 of 2017), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, on the recommendations of the Council, 
hereby makes the following rules further to amend the Tamil Nadu Goods and Services Tax Rules, 2017, 
namely:-
 
1. Short title and commencement. -(1) These rules may be called the Tamil Nadu Goods and 
Services Tax (Ninth Amendment) Rules, 2021.
(2) Save as otherwise provided in these rules, they shall be deemed to have come into force from 
the 1st day of December, 2021.
 
2. In the Tamil Nadu Goods and Services Tax Rules, 2017, —
 
 
(i) 
in rule 137, with eﬀ ect from the 30th day of November 2021, for the words “four years”, 
the words “ﬁ ve years” shall be substituted.
