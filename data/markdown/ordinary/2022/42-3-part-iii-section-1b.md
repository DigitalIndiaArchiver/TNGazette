No. SRO B-50/2022.—In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (xxxiii) of sub-section (2) of Section 96, 
Section 111, and sub-section (2) of Section 138 of the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988 (Central Act 59 of 1988), the Governor of 
Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Tamil Nadu Motor Vehicles (Regulation and Control of School 
Buses) Special Rules, 2012, the draft of the same having been previously published, as required by sub-section (1) of 
Section 212 of the said Act. 
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖග
In the said Rules, in rule 6, after sub-rule (16), the following sub-rule shall be added, namely:- 
"(17) Fixing of cameras and sensor.- In every School Bus,—
 
(a) A camera shall be ﬁ xed at the front end.
 
(b) A camera shall be ﬁ xed at the rear end in such a manner that the driver while taking reverse can have a   
 
 
complete view of the rear side.
 
(c) A sensor shall also be ﬁ xed at the rear end to give warning signal".
