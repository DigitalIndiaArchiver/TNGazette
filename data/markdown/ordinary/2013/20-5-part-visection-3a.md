NOTICE CONVENING MEETING OF THE EQUITY SHAREHOLDERS
Notice is hereby given that by orders dated the 10th of April 2013 passed in Company Application No. 343 of 2013
and 26th April, 2013 passed in O.S.A. No.201 of 2013, the Hon’ble High Court of Judicature at Madras has directed a Meeting
to be held of the Equity Shareholders of the Applicant/Transferor Company for the purpose of considering, and if
thought fit, approving, with or without modification, the scheme of Amalgamation of the Applicant/Transferor Company
with M/s. Vindhya Spinning Mills Private Limited.
In pursuance of the said orders and as directed therein, further notice is hereby given that a meeting of the Equity
Shareholders of the Applicant/Transferor Company will be held at the Registered office of the Applicant /Transferor
Company located at No. 4, Chairman Shanmuga Nadar Road, Sivakasi-626 123 on the 14th day of June, 2013
at 1.30 PM at which time and place the Equity Shareholders are requested to attend.
Copies of the Scheme of Amalgamation and the Statement under Section 393 can be had free of charge at the
Registered Office of the Applicant/Transferor Company or at the Office of its Advocates, M/s. Thriyambak J. Kannan
and P.S. Suman, at No. 37 Law Chambers, High Court Buildings, Chennai-600 104.
The quorum for the meeting has been fixed by the Hon’ble Court as 5 members of the Applicant/Transferor Company
present in person or by proxy.
Persons entitled to attend and vote at the meeting may vote in person or by proxy, provided that all proxies in the
prescribed form are deposited at the Registered Office of the Applicant/Transferor Company at No. 4, Chiarman Shanmuga
Nadar Road, Sivakasi-626 123 not later than 48 hours before the meeting.
Forms of Proxy can be had at the Registered Office of the Applicant/Transferor Company.
The Court has appointed Mr. K. Radhakrishna Murthy, Advocate, Failing which, Mr. D.Gentley Rathnaraj, Advocate as
Chairman of the said meeting.  The abovementioned Scheme of Amalgamation, if approved at the meeting, will be
subject to the subsequent approval of the Hon’ble High Court of Judicature at Madras.
Dated on this the 6 day of May, 2013.
