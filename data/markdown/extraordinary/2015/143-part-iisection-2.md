The Governor of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that the lands specified in the schedule below are  required under
TNRSP II for Highways purpose to wit for upgrading SH 70 Vridhachalam to Parangipettai Road from Km 00/000 KM to 35/800
KM  Vridhachalam Town, Budhamur, Ponneri (Kulappakkam),  Mavadandal (Kulappakkam) Kumaramangalam, Gopalapuram,
Keenanur, Kammapuram, Siruvarappur, Sathappadi, Aadhanur (U) in Virudhachalam Taluk and Valaiyammadevi (Melpadi),
Valaiyammadevi (Kilpadi) Erumbur, Sethiyathoppu, Miralur, Manjakollai, Seiyapadi, Oodaiyur (Buvanagiri), Vandurayanpattu,
Alichikudi, Adhivaraghanatham, Suttukuzhi, Bhuvanagiri (Mel) Villages Chidambaram Taluk, Cuddalore District and it having
already been decided that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid out of the funds controlled
and managed by the Highways Department and after having considered the cause shown by the owner  or other persons having
interest in the said lands as the case may be do hereby publish the following notice under sub-section (1) of section 15 of the
Tamil Nadu Highways Act 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of  Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002)  the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the dry lands specified in the schedule below and measuring to an extent of 2.98.89 hectare  or 29889
square meter lands specified in the schedule below a little more or less needed for TNRSP –II Highways purpose, to wit for
upgrading the  State Highways Road (SH 70) Vridhachalam to Parangipettai Road from KM 00/000 KM to 35/800 in Vridhachalam
Town, Budhamur, Ponneri (Kulappakkam), Mavadandal (Kulappakkam) Kumaramangalam, Gopalapuram, Keenanur, Kammapuram,
Siruvarappur, Sathappadi  at Vridhachalam Taluk and Aadhanur( U),Valaiyammadevi (Melpadi), Valaiyammadevi (Kilpadi) Erumbur,
Sethiyathoppu, Miralur, Manjakollai, Seiyapadi, Oodaiyur (Buvanagiri), Vandurayanpattu, Adhivaraghanatham, Suttukuzhi, Villages at
Chidambaram Taluk, Cuddalore District.
The plan of the lands under acquisition are kept in the office of the Special District Revenue Officer (Land Acquisition),
Tamil Nadu Road Sector Project Chennai and office of the Special Tahsildar, Tamil Nadu Road Sector Project II, Kancheepuram
may be inspected at any time during office hours.
