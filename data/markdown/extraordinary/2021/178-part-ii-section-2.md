WHEREAS on considering the recommendations of the expert team of Doctors and Public Health Specialists and 
based on the directives of Government of India, Ministry of Home Aﬀ airs, State-wide lockdown was extended from time 
to time and lastly extended till 24:00 hrs of 31.3.2021 under the Disaster Management Act, 2005 in GO.Ms.No.318, 
Revenue and Disaster Management (DM-IV) Department, dated 28.2.2021.
2. In G.O.(Ms).No.30, Revenue and Disaster Management (DM IV), Department, Dated 13.01.2021, Schools were 
permitted to function for 10th and 12th Standards from 19.1.2021. Further in G.O.(Ms).No 84, Revenue and Disaster 
Management (DM IV), Department, Dated 31.1.2021 among other things, Schools were also permitted to function for 
9th and 11th Standards from 8.2.2021 and Hostels for the above students were also permitted.
3. The Director of Public Health and Preventive Medicine has stated that, in the light of increasing trend 
of COVID cases, from a Public Health point of view it would not be advisable to continue the classes at least for the 
9th, 10th and 11th Standard students. The Director of Public Health and Preventive Medicine has however stated that 
as far as Class XII is concerned, they may be permitted to function as the number of students will be lesser and as 
they also have to face the Board Examination by strict follow-up of all provision of SOP’s.

4.   Now, therefore, the Government of Tamil Nadu hereby order that Schools shall be closed for 
classes 9th, 10th   and 11th with eﬀ ect from 22.03.2021 until further orders. However online/digital mode of

In addition, the following shall also be allowed: Conduct of Board Examination for Class 10 for Boards other than 
Tamil Nadu State Board as scheduled by those Boards and Conduct of Special Classes to students who are appearing 
for the above Board Examination for Class 10 and functioning of Hostels for those students and to issue the following 
amendment:

shall continue for classes 9th, 10th and 11th.
