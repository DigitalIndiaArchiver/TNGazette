Order:- No.214, Agriculture (AP5), 12th September 2020.
On 21-02-2020, the Government of Tamil Nadu has enacted the Tamil Nadu Protected Agricultural Zone Development Act, 
2020 in the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly with the following objectives:-
 
(a) to use the available agricultural lands for sustainable development of agriculture and ensure that the agricultural 
activities are not unduly constrained by non-agricultural use or other development;
 
(b) to develop the protected agricultural zone through integrated farming and enhanced cultivation practices by promotion 
of technologies to increase production and productivity of crops and farms;
 
(c) to promote optimum usage of water including ﬂ ood management and other inputs in the ﬁ eld of agriculture;
 
(d) to promote agro-based and allied industries for sustainable agricultural development; and
 
(e) to promote research and development including skill building in the ﬁ eld of agriculture and allied sectors.
2. As per sub section (1) of Section 23 of the Tamil Nadu Protected Agricultural Zone Development Act, 2020, the Government 
have framed Rules viz., Tamil Nadu Protected Agricultural Zone Development Rules, 2020 vide Government Order second read 
above and it has been notiﬁ ed in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated 26-8-2020.
3. As per Section 5 (1) of the said act, there shall be an authority, by the name, Tamil Nadu Protected Agricultureal Zone

2. The Transport Commissiosner has also stated that the Regional Transport Oﬃ  ce, Chennai (East) is situated at 
Pulianthope. The people residing at Pulianthope and adjacent to the Regional Transport Oﬃ  ce, Chennai (East) Oﬃ  ce situated at 
Pulianthope area for obtaining LLR, Driving Licence, Permit and all other works related to Regional Transport Oﬃ  ce have  to go to 
Regional Transport Oﬃ  ce, Chennai (North) which is about 10Kms from Pulianthope. Hence, they are suﬀ ering a lot for obtaining 
driving license and other related services.
3. The Transport Commissioner has further stated that the people those who are residing at Pulianthope, Chennai-12 are 
frequently approaching Regional Transport Oﬃ  ce, Chennai (East) for obtaining LLR, Driving License and other related works, But, 
they are not able to entertain and further when they are directed to approach Regional Transport Oﬃ  ce, Chennai (North), they 
refused to go there and also argue that they are getting supply of ration commodities (PDS) from Pulianthope, paying their EB Bill, 
Property Tax, Water Tax etc., in that area except Regional Transport Oﬃ  ce related works.
4. The Transport  Commissioner has, therefore, requested the Government to issue orders to transfer the Pulianthope, 
(Pin Code Chennai 600 012) area from the Regional Transport Oﬃ  ce, Chennai (North) jurisdiction to Regional Transport Oﬃ  ce, 
Chennai (East) jurisdiction in the interest of Public.
5. The Government, after careful examination of the proposal of the Transport Commissioner, Chennai have decided to 
transfer the Pulianthope, (Pin Code Chennai 600 012) area from the Regional Transport  Oﬃ  ce, Chennai (North) jurisdiction to 
Regional Transport Oﬃ  ce, Chennai(East) jurisdiction in the interest of the General Public
