LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY SECRETARIAT
DEATH OF THIRU S. JEYAPAUL,  MEMBER OF THE TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY.
[Letter No. 10950/2010-1, Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Secretariat (B.III), 27th July 2010,
Aadi 11, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No. II(1)/LAS/15(e-1)/2010.
“The Honourable Speaker has learnt with deep regret the death of Thiru S. Jeyapaul,
a Member of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly on 25th July 2010”.
VACATION OF SEAT IN TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY.
[Letter No. 10950/2010-1, Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Secretariat (B.III), 27th July 2010,
Aadi 11, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No. II(1)/LAS/15(e-2)/2010.
“A vacancy has occurred among the Members of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly, consequent on
the death of Thiru S. Jeyapaul, a Member elected to the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly
from the 230. Colachel Assembly Constituency in Kanniyakumari District”.
