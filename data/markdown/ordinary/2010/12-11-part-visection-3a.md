 GENERAL  NOTIFICATION
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD
Taking over the Direct Management of Palani Dharma Paribalana Sangam Wakf, Palani Town, Palani Taluk,
Dindigul District under the Direct Control of Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
(R.C. No. 15864/05/B15/DGL.)
No. VI-3(a)/24/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995, the Tamil Nadu
Wakf Board in its proceedings in item No. 27/09, dated 8th December 2009 has ordered to take over the management
of the Palani Dharma Paribalana Sangam Wakf (G.S. Nos. 287/Mdu and G.S. 293/Mdu), Palani Town and Taluk, Dindigul
District under the direct control of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of one year from the date of
the issuing the Board's Order i.e. 19th March 2010 and to appoint the Superintendent of Wakfs, Madurai
as its Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
19th March 2010.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
