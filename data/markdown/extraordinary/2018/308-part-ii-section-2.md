APPOINTMENT OF COMMISSION OF INQUIRY  TO INQUIRE INTO CAUSES AND CIRCUMSTANCES LEADING TO 
THE LAW AND ORDER DISTURBANCES WHICH LOOK PLACE ON 23RD JANUARY 2017 AT CHENNAI, MADURAI, 
COIMBATORE AND OTHER PARTS OF THE STATE FOLLOWING THE AGITATION FOR HOLDING OF JALLIKATTU 
UNDER THE COMMISSIONS OF INQUIRY ACT.
AMENDMENT TO NOTIFICATION
[G.O. Ms. No. 657, Public (Law & Order-F), 31st August 2018, Aavani 15, 
Vilambi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu - 2049.]
No. II(2)/PULO/777(c)/2018.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Commissions of Inquiry Act, 1952 (Central Act 
LX of 1952), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Public (Law and Order-F) Department 
Notiﬁ cation No.II(2)/PULO/76(f)/2017, published at pages 1-2 of Part II-Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette 
Extraordinary, dated the 31st January 2017, as subsequently amended.
AMENDMENT 
In the said Notiﬁ cation, in paragraph 4, for the expression "nineteen months", the expression "twenty two months" shall 
be substituted.
