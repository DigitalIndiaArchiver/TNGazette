No.I/PUSP/34/2014.—The following Notification of the Government of India, Ministry
of Personnel, Public Grievances and Pensions, Department of Personnel & Training,
New Delhi, dated the 29th September 2014 is republished:—
    Dr. K.S. Palanisami, I.A.S., (TN) appointed to the Indian Administrative Service
vide Notification No. 14015/21/2009-AIS(I)-B, dated 2nd August 2010, has been
permitted to change his name from Dr. K.S. Palanisami to Dr. K.S. Palanisamy. He
will accordingly be recognized as Dr. K.S. Palanisamy for all official purposes.
