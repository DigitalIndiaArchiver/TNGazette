Tamil Nadu Wakf Board hereby publishes the Wakf Hazarath Sulthan Syed Ibrahim Shaheed Oliyullah Dargha, situated in
Erwadi Village, Kadaladi Taluk, Ramanathapuram District as Sunni Wakf as per the Survey Report forwarded by the
Government of Tamil Nadu as in the annexure.
