In exercise of the powers conferred by clauses (a) and (b) of sub-section (1) of Section 4 of the Tamil Nadu

to exclude from the jurisdiction of Sivakasi Special Grade Municipality, the following territorial areas comprised in the 
survey numbers indicated against them and to constitute the said areas as Thiruthangal Municipality within the boundaries 
speciﬁ ed thereunder:—
