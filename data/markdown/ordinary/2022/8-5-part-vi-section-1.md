Variations to the Review Approved Tiruppur Master Plan for the Tiruppur Local Planning Area
(Roc. No. 178/2021/TD/TLPA)
No. VI(1)/65/2022.
In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (4) of Section 32 of Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning 
Act 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972) and exercise of powers confirmed by the G.O.Ms.No. 94, Housing and Urban 
Development [UD4(1)] Department, dated 12.6.2009 which has been published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette 
No. 27, Part II—Section 2, dated 15.07.2009 
Land use zone conversion from Agricultural use zone into Residential use zone ordered in G.O.(2D).No. 229, 
Housing and Urban Development [UD4(CLU1)] Department, dated 08.09.2021. The following variations are made 
to the Master Plan of Review Approved Tiruppur Local Planning Authority under the said Act and published in the 
G.O.Ms.No. 280, Housing and Urban Development [UDIV(2)], dated 27.10.2006 Notification No.II(2)/HOU/573/2006 at 
page 375 of Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette dated the 22nd November 2006.
Draft Variations
In the Review Approved Tiruppur Master Plan under the heading permitted Land use in various survey numbers 
of Tiruppur Local Planning Area under heading in Village CHETTIPALAYAM VILLAGE page S-42 in S.F.No.110 the 
following entries should be made.
	
(i)	
Against the entry of ‘‘AGRICULTURAL USE (AG-17)’’ use zone for the expression 110 to 113, the expression 
110 part, 111 to 113 shall be substituted.
	
(ii)	
Against the entry of ‘‘MIXED RESIDENTIAL USE (MR)’’ the expression 110/2A1, 110/2A2 shall be added.
	
(iii)	 Against the entry of ‘‘MIXED RESIDENTIAL USE (MR)’’ the expression 110/2A1, 110/2A2 shall be added 
after the entry 60.
Tiruppur.

JUDICIAL NOTIFICATIONS
The period of Adjournment for the Vacation and Holidays for District Courts and all other Subordinate Civil Courts 
including District Munsif Courts in respect of Erode District for the year 2022
(A.No. 573/2021)
(D.No. 215)
No. VI(1)/70/2022.
In consonance with the instructions issued by the hon’ble Madras High Court, in its Official Memorandum in 
ROC.No.78555-A/2021/C3 dated 07.12.2021, it is hereby notified that the period of Adjournment for the Vacation and Holidays for 
District Courts and all other Subordinate Civil Courts including District Munsif Courts in respect of Erode District for the year 2022 are as 
follows:- 
SUMMER VACATION 
The Principal District and Sessions Court, Erode, the I Additional District and Sessions Court, Erode, the II Additional 
District and Sessions Court, Erode, the III Additional District and Sessions Court, Erode at Gobichettipalayam, the IV Additional 
District and Sessions Court, Erode at Bhavani, the Special District Court (To deal with MCOP Cases), Erode, the Courts of 
the Subordinate Judges/Assistant Sessions Judges of Erode, Bhavani, Gobichettipalayam, Sathyamangalam and Perundurai, 
the Special Sub Court (To deal with MCOP Cases), Erode, the District Munsif’s Courts of Erode, Bhavani, Gobichettipalayam, 
Sathyamangalam, Perundurai , Anthiyur and the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court and Kodumudi (Civil side) will 
be closed from Sunday, the 1st day of May 2022 to Tuesday, the 31st day of May 2022. 
DUSSERA   HOLIDAYS 
The Principal District and Sessions Court, Erode, the I Additional District and Sessions Court, Erode, the II Additional 
District and Sessions Court, Erode, the III Additional District and Sessions Court, Erode at Gobichettipalayam, the IV Additional 
District and Sessions Court, Erode at Bhavani, the Special District Court (To deal with MCOP Cases), Erode, the Courts of 
the Subordinate Judges/Assistant Sessions Judges of Erode, Bhavani, Gobichettipalayam, Sathyamangalam and Perundurai, 
the Special Sub Court (To deal with MCOP Cases), Erode, the District Munsif’s Courts of Erode, Bhavani, Gobichettipalayam, 
Sathyamangalam, Perundurai, Anthiyur and the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Courts Kodumudi (Civil side) will be 
closed from Saturday, the 1st day of October, 2022 to Sunday, the 9th day of October, 2022. 
CHRISTMAS HOLIDAYS 
The Principal District and Sessions Court, Erode, the I Additional District and Sessions Court, Erode, the 
II Additional District and Sessions Court, Erode, the III Additional District and Sessions Court, Erode at Gobichettipalayam, 
the IV Additional District and Sessions Court, Erode at Bhavani, the Special District Court (To deal with MCOP 
Cases), Erode,the Courts of the Subordinate Judges/Assistant Sessions Judges of Erode, Bhavani, Gobichettipalayam, 
Sathyamangalam and Perundurai, the Special Sub Court (To deal with MCOP Cases), Erode, the District Munsif’s Courts of 
Erode, Bhavani, Gobichettipalayam, Sathyamangalam, Perundurai, Anthiyur and the District Munsif’-cum-Judicial Magistrate 
Court and Kodumudi (Civil side) will be closed from Saturday, the 24th day of December 2022 to Saturday, the 
31st day of December, 2022.

The office of the Official Receiver, Erode remain open throughout the Summer Vacation.
No Plaint or Petition except applications for grant of copies and for service and execution of process will be received 
during the adjournments. 
The Offices of the Copyist and Process Service Establishments will be kept open throughout the Summer Vacation. 
No arrest warrant will be executed during the Vacation. 
The Office of the other sections will remain closed. However arrangements shall be made:- 
	
(a)	 For Functioning the Copyist and Process Service Establishments. 
	
(b)	 For transmitting records to the High Court for reference in Appeals and revisions. and 
	
(c)	 For attending all Administrative Correspondences.
