Under Section 22 of the Tamil Nadu Music and Fine Arts University Act, 2013 (Tamil Nadu Act 30 of 2013), the
Academic Council of the Tamil Nadu Music and Fine Arts University is constituted with the following members:—
CLASS I - EX-OFFICIO MEMBERS:-
1.  The Vice-Chancellor
2.  The Commissioner of Art and Culture
3.  The Deans of Faculties
4.  Director of Libraries
5.  Principals of all Government Music Colleges
6.  Principals of all Government College of Fine Arts
7.  Principal of Government College of Architecture and Sculpture, Mamallapuram.
