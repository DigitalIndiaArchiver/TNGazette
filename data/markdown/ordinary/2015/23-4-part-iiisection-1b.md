No. SRO B-49/2015.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu General Subordinate
Service (Section 13 in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1970).
2. The amendments hereby made shall come into force from the date of issue of the order.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules, in Part II, in Class XLVI,-
(1) in rule 2, in "TABLE", for the entry "By Direct recruitment" in column (2) against the entry "Driver" in column (1) thereof,
the following entries shall be substituted, namely:-
"(1)
By direct recruitment;
 (2)
By promotion from the post of Store Attendant in National Cadet Corps unit in the Tamil Nadu General Subordinate
Service; and
 (3)
By recruitment by transfer from the posts of Office Assistant, Boat Keeper and Chowkidar in National Cadet Corps
unit in the Tamil Nadu Basic Service :
Provided that seventy per cent of the vacancies shall be filled up by direct recruitment, twenty per cent of the vacancies
shall be filled up by promotion and ten per cent of the vacancies shall be filled up by recruitment by transfer";
(2)
in rule 4, in "THE TABLE", for the entries "Must not have completed 30 years of age on the first day of July of the
year in which the selection for appointment is made" in column (2), against the entry "II. Drivers" in column (1) thereof, the
entries "For direct recruitment, one must not have completed 30 years of age on the first day of July of the year in which the
selection for appointment is made" shall be substituted.
HEMANT KUMAR SINHA,
Additional Chief Secretary to Government.
