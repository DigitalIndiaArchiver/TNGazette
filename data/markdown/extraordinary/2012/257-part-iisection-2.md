The Governor of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that the
lands specified in the Schedule below have to be acquired for
Industrial purpose, to wit for the formation of ULTRA MEGA
POWER PROJECT, CHEYYUR and it has already been
decided that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded
for the lands is to be paid out of the funds controlled or
managed by the Officer Authorised Signatory, Coastal
Tamil Nadu Power Ltd., New Delhi and having considered  the
cause shown by the owners or other persons having interest
the said lands, the Joint Commissioner (Land Reforms),
Villupuram concerned, the interested party for the lands covered
under Tamil Nadu Land Reforms (fixation of ceiling on land)
Act 1961, as the case may be, do here by publish the following
notice under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Acquisition of Lands  for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997
(Tamil Nadu  Act 10 of 1999), by relaxing G.O(Ms.)No.2954,
Revenue dated 29th May 1973.
NOTICE
 Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Acquisition of Lands  for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997
(Tamil Nadu  Act 10 of 1999) the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby acquired the lands specified in the Schedule below and
measuring an extent of  9.42.0 Hectare to the same, a little
more or less are needed for INDUSTRIAL purpose. To wit for
the formation of ULTRA MEGA POWER PROJECT No. 54
CHITHARKADU VILLAGE, Cheyyur Taluk,  Kancheepuram
District.
The plan of the lands are kept in the office of Special
Tahsildar, Land Acquisition, Unit-II ULTRA MEGA POWER
PROJECT Scheme, Cheyyur may be inspected at any time
during office hours.
