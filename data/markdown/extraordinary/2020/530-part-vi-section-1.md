 The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that the lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below are acquired 
under Chennai – Kanniyakumari Industrial Corridor Project for Highways purpose to wit for Widening of SH-64, 
Kumbakonam - Sirkazhi Road from 3/280 KM to 6/060 KM at 59.Thepperumanallur Village in Thiruvidaimaruthur Taluk, 
Thanjavur District, and it having already been decided that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the 
lands to be paid out of the funds controlled and managed by the Divisional Engineer(H), Kumbakonam (CKICP), Tamil

case may be do hereby publish the following notice under sub-section (1) of section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways 
Act, 2001 (TN Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) read with 
the Tamil Nadu Land Acquisition Laws (Revival of Operation, Amendment and Validation) Act, 2019, the Governor of

of Wet, Dry and Manai lands are acquired in 59.Thepperumanallur Village, Thiruvidaimaruthur Taluk, Thanjavur District, 
to the same, a little more or less is needed for Chennai – Kanniyakumari Industrial Corridor Project for State Highways
