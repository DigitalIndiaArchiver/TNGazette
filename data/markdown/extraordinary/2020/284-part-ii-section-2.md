NOTICE OF ELECTION
Election of Members of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board
Notice is hereby given that :-
 
An election is to be held for two members each to the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board by electoral colleges consisting of--- 
 
 
i) 
Muslim Members of Parliament from the State
 
 
ii) 
Muslim Members of the State Legislature
 
 
iii) 
Muslim Ex-Members of Bar Council
 
 
iv) 
Mutawallis of the Waqfs having an annual income of rupees one lakh and above as speciﬁ ed under 
sub-rule (13) of Rule 2 of the Tamil Nadu Waqf Board (Conduct of Election for Members) Rules,1997.
2. Nomination papers in Form – I may be delivered by a candidate whose name ﬁ nds place in the ﬁ nal electoral roll 
to Thiru A.M. Aandiyappan, Returning Oﬃ  cer and Additional Secretary to Government, Backward Classes, Most Backward 
Classes and Minorities Welfare Department, Chennai – 9 or to Thiru S. Syed Khaseem, Assistant Returning Oﬃ  cer and 
Deputy Secretary to Government, Commercial Taxes and Registration Department, Chennai - 9 or to Thiru K. Hemanth 
Kumar, Assistant Returning Oﬃ  cer and Under Secretary to Government, Backward Classes, Most Backward Classes and 
Minorities Welfare Department, Chennai – 9, between 11.00 A.M. and 3.00 P.M. on any day (Other than Public Holidays) not 
later than 23rd July 2020.
3. Forms of nomination papers may be obtained from 16-07-2020 (Thursday) onwards at the place and time aforesaid.
4. The nomination of each candidate belonging to the electoral colleges of Members of Parliament, the State Legislature 
or the State Bar Council shall be proposed and seconded by members belonging to the respective electoral college; Provided 
that there is no need for a proposer or seconder when total number of members belonging to each of the said electoral 
college is six or less than six.
5. The nomination of each candidate belonging to the electoral college of Mutawallis shall be proposed and seconded 
by not less than ﬁ ve members from the said electoral college. 
6. The proposer and the seconder shall belong to the electoral college of the candidate nominated.
7. Any member belonging to a particular electoral college may be subscribed either as proposer or as seconder to as 
many nomination paper as there are vacancies to be ﬁ lled to the said category, but not more.
8. If a candidate belongs to more than one category of electoral colleges, he shall choose one of the electoral colleges. 
He shall contest, propose or second a candidate belonging to the said electoral college only.
9. A member of an electoral college, who proposes or seconds the candidature of another member shall not be eligible 
for himself contesting the election. 
10. The Nomination papers will be taken up for scrutiny at 11.00 A.M. on 24th July 2020 at the Oﬃ  ce of the Returning 
Oﬃ  cer and Additional Secretary to Government, Backward Classes, Most Backward Classes and Minorities Welfare Department, 
Chennai – 9.

11. Any candidate may withdraw his candidature by a notice in writing as speciﬁ ed in Form – III signed by him may 
be delivered to either of the Oﬃ  cers speciﬁ ed in the paragraph (2) above at his oﬃ  ce before 3.00 P.M. on 29th July 2020.
12. No person who suﬀ ers from any of the disqualiﬁ cations under the Act shall be eligible to contest the election.
13. If the number of candidates who were duly nominated and who have not withdrawn their candidature is equal to the 
number of vacancies to be ﬁ lled, the Election Authority shall declare all such candidates to have been duly elected.
14. In the event of the election being conducted, the poll will be held on 19th August 2020 between 10.00 A.M. to 
4.00 P.M. at the Oﬃ  ce of the Tamil Nadu Waqf Board, No.1, Jaﬀ er Syrang Street, Vallal Seethakkathi Nagar, Chennai – 600 001.
