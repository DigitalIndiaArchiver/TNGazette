HOME DEPARTMENT
(Prison.)
Declaration of Transit yard in Central Prison, Puzhal
Complex as Borstal School and Borstal School
Poonamalle declared as Special Sub-Jail.
NOTIFICATION I
[G.O.Ms. No. 549, Home (Prison-IV), 19th August 2014,
ÝõE 3, üò, F¼õœÀõ˜ Ý‡´-2045.]
No. II(2)/HO/540/2014.—Under sub-section (1) of Section 3
of the Tamil Nadu Prisons Act, 1894 (Central Act IX of 1894),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby declares that the Transit  Yard
in Central Prison, Puzhal Complex shall cease to be a prison.
NOTIFICATION II
[G.O.Ms. No. 549, Home (Prison-IV), 19th August 2014,
ÝõE 3, üò, F¼õœÀõ˜ Ý‡´-2045.]
No. II(2)/HO/541/2014.—In exercise of the powers conferred
by sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Borstal
Schools Act, 1925 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1926), the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby establishes a Borstal School in the
premises of Transit Yard in Central Prison, Puzhal for detaining
adolescent offenders belonging to Chennai, Kancheepuram and
Tiruvallur Districts.
NOTIFICATION III
[G.O.Ms. No. 549, Home (Prison-IV), 19th August 2014,
ÝõE 3, üò, F¼õœÀõ˜ Ý‡´-2045.]
No. II(2)/HO/542/2014.—In exercise of the powers conferred
by sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Borstal Schools Act, 1925 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1926), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby declares that the Borstal School,
Poonamallee shall cease to be a Borstal School.
NOTIFICATION IV
[G.O.Ms. No. 549, Home (Prison-IV), 19th August 2014,
ÝõE 3, üò, F¼õœÀõ˜ Ý‡´-2045.]
No. II(2)/HO/543/2014.—Under sub-section (1) of Section 3
of the Tamil Nadu Prisons Act, 1894, (Central Act IX of 1894),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby declares that the places
and buildings hitherto functioned as Borstal School, Poonamallee
shall be a Prison namely Special Sub-Jail for the purpose of
detention of remand prisoners in Chennai City.
APURVA VARMA,
Principal Secretary to Government.
