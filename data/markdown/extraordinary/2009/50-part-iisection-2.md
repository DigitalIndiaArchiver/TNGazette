HANDLOOMS, HANDICRAFTS, TEXTILES AND KHADI DEPARTMENT.
NOTIFICATIONS UNDER TAMIL NADU RELIEF UNDERTAKINGS (SPECIAL PROVISIONS) ACT.
DECLARATION OF TAMIL NADU TEXTILE CORPORATION LIMITED, COIMBATORE AS RELIEF
UNDERTAKING FOR CERTAIN PERIOD.
[G.O. (2D) No. 7, Handlooms, Handicrafts, Textiles and Khadi (C2), 24th February 2009,
 Maasi 12, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. II(2)/HHTK/104(a-1)/2009.
Whereas, the Government are satisfied that with view to enabling the continued running of Tamil Nadu
Textile Corporation Limited, Coimbatore, a State Industrial Undertaking as a measure of preventing, or of
providing relief against unemployment, it is necessary and expedient to declare the said State Industrial
Undertaking as a relief Undertaking in the Public interest.
Now, Therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Relief Undertakings
(Special Provisions) Act, 1969 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1969), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby declares that
Tamil Nadu Textile Corporation Limited, Coimbatore, a State Industrial Undertaking, shall for a period of one
year with effect on and from the date of publication of this notification, be a relief undertaking for the purpose
of the said Act.

DIRECTION REGARDING SUSPENSION FROM OPERATION OF ALL CONTRACTS, ASSURANCES OF
PROPERTY, ETC., OF TAMIL NADU TEXTILE CORPORATION LIMITED, COIMBATORE.
[G.O. (2D) No. 7, Handlooms, Handicrafts, Textiles and Khadi (C2), 24th February 2009,
 Maasi 12, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. II(2)/HHTK/104(a-2)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by clauses (a) and (b) of Section 4 of the Tamil Nadu  Undertakings
(Special Provisions) Act, 1969 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of  1969), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs
that in relation to the Relief Undertaking, namely the Tamil Nadu Textile Corporation Limited, Coimbatore:—
(a) The Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 (Central Act XIV of 1947) shall apply with the omission of
Chapter V-B and Section 33-C; and
(b) All contracts, assurances of property, agreements, assignments, standing orders or other instruments
in force, to which the said relief undertaking is a party or which may be applicable to the said relief undertaking,
immediately before the date with effect on and from which the said relief undertaking was declared as a
relief undertaking, shall be suspended in operation and that all the rights, privileges, obligations and liabilities
accruing or arising there under before the said date, shall be suspended with effect on and from the said
date.
VISHWANATH SHEGAONKAR,
Secretary to Government.
