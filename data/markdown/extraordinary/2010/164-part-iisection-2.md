DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF THE INDIAN STAMP AND THE REGISTRATION (TAMIL NADU
AMENDMENT) ACT, 2006.
[G.O. Ms. No. 74, Commercial Taxes and Registration  (J1), 1st June 2010.]
No. II(2)/CTR/312(f-1)/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (3) of Section 1 of the Indian Stamp and the Registration
(Tamil Nadu Amendment) Act, 2006 (Tamil Nadu Act 13 of 2008), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the
1st day of June 2010 as the date on which sub-section (2) of Section 2 of the said Act shall come into force.
CONSTITUTION OF VALUATION COMMITTEE UNDER THE CHAIRMANSHIP OF THE INSPECTOR
GENERAL OF REGISTRATION UNDER THE INDIAN STAMP ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 76, Commercial Taxes and Registration  (J1), 1st June 2010.]
No. II(2)/CTR/312(f-2)/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 47-AA of the Indian Stamp Act, 1899 (Central
Act II of 1899), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby constitutes the Valuation Committee under the Chairmanship of
the Inspector General of Registration for estimation, publication and revision of market value guidelines of properties
in any area in the State, comprising the following members, namely:—
(i)
Director of Town and Country Planning;
(ii)
Chief Engineer (Buildings), Public Works Department;
(iii)
Joint Commissioner of Land Administration;

publication of the data relating to the market value guidelines in accordance with the decision of the Committee.
