EXPLANATORY STATEMENT
Consequent to the separation of the functions of State Load Despatch Centre from the Transmission Licensee in the 
State of Tamil Nadu, the State Load Despatch Centre (SLDC) has started ﬁ ling petitions on its own for approval of levy 
and collection of fees and charges from the Generating Companies and Licensees engaged in Intra-State Transmission 
of Electricity for meeting its operational needs.  In view of the same, it has been decided by the Commission  to collect 
fees from the SLDC in respect of such application/petition to be ﬁ led before the Commission by the State Load Despatch 
Centre (SLDC) from time to time as is being done by the Transmission and Distribution Licensees.  
This amendment seeks to give effect to the above decision.  
(By order of the Tamil Nadu Electricity Regulatory Commission)
