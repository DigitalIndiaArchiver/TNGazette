IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT MADRAS
(Ordinary Original Civil Jurisdiction)
[Roc. No. 3717/2011(O.S.).]
C.P. No. 57 of 2010
In the matter of Section 433 and 434 of the Companies Act, 1956.
And
In the matter of Winding up of Vaishnovi Infrastructure Engineering Pvt. Ltd., a company incorporated under the
provisions of the Companies Act, 1956, and having its registered office at GST Road, NH 45, Kallabhiranpuram,
Madhuranthakam, Kancheepuram District-603 308.
No. VI-3(a)/35/2011.
M/s. Hoe Leong Corporation Ltd., 6, Clementi Loop, Singapore-129 814.—Petitioner.
Versus
M/s. Vaishnovi Infrastructure Engineering Pvt. Ltd., GST Road, NH 45, Kallabhiranpuram, Madhuranthakam,
Kancheepuram District-603 308—Respondent.
ADVERTISEMENT OF PETITION
Notice is hereby given that a petition for winding up of the above named company by the Hon'ble High Court, Madras
was on the 9th day of February 2010, presented to the said Court by M/s. Hoe Leong Corporation Ltd., 6, Clementi Loop,
Singapore-129 814 (Creditor) and that the said petition is directed to be heard before the Court on the 13th day
of June 2011.
Any creditor, contributory or other person desirous of supporting or opposing the making of an order on the said petition
should send to the petitioner or his advocate notice of this intention signed by him or his advocate not later than 2 days
before the date fixed for the hearing of the petition and appear at the hearing for the purpose in person or by his advocate.
A copy of petition will be furnished by the undersigned to any creditor or contributory on payment of the prescribed charges
for the same.
Any affidavit intended to be used in opposition to the petition should be filed in Court and a copy served on the petitioner
or his advocate, not less than 5 days before the date fixed for the hearing.
Counsel for Petitioner,
302, Anna Salai, Teynampet,
M/s. FOX MANDAL & ASSOCIATES,
Chennai-600 006.
C. SAKTHIMANIKANDAN,
Solicitors and Advocates.
High Court, Madras,
K. INDUMATHI,
9th May 2011.
Deputy Registrar (in-charge),
Appellate Side.
 PRINTED AND PUBLISHED BY THE DIRECTOR OF STATIONERY AND PRINTING, CHENNAI
ON  BEHALF OF THE GOVERNMENT OF TAMIL NADU
