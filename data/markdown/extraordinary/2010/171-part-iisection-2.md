In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Agricultural Labourers-Farmers
(Social Security and Welfare) Act, 2006 (Tamil Nadu Act 29 of 2006), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes
the following amendments to the Revenue Department Notification No. II(2)/REV/22(c)/2007, published at
pages 1-3 of Part II-Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 18th January
2007, as subsequently amended.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Notification,  under the heading “1. Members representing the Government”,—

“1. Thiru K. Sellamuthu,
..
Chairman.”;

(President, Uzhavar Ulaipalar Party)
Kappalpatti Post,
