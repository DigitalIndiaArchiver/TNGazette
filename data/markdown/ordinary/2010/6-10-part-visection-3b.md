MADURAI KAMARAJ UNIVERSITY
 Election to the Authorities of the University
(No. ME2/AC/Election/2010.)
No. VI-3(b)/9/2010.
It is hereby notified that the following elections will be held under Section mentioned hereunder as per the
Madurai Kamaraj University Act, 1965 to elect:—
(i) Three Members to the Syndicate by the Academic Council from among the members of whom two shall
be Teachers of affiliated Colleges and the remaining shall be a person other than a teacher of an affiliated College
under Section 19 (a) Class II (2) of the Madurai Kamaraj University Act, 1965.
(ii) Five Members to the Senate by the members of the Academic Council from among themselves of whom
not less than three shall be Teachers of affiliated Colleges under Section 15(a) Class III (2) of the Madurai
Kamaraj University Act, 1965.
(iii) Six Members to the Standing Committee of the Academic Council by the members of the Academic
Council of whom three shall be members of the Academic Council who are Principals or Teachers of affiliated
Colleges and the remaining three shall be persons other than members of the Syndicate.
The last date and hour for receipt of nominations is Friday, the 26th February 2010 at 04-00 p.m. by the
Registrar-in-charge, Madurai Kamaraj University, Palkalai Nagar, Madurai-625 021 and the date and time regarding
the further election process can be had by the candidates from the Registrar-in-charge.
Palkalai Nagar,
DR. V. ALAGAPPAN,
Madurai-625 021,
Registrar (Incharge).

UNIVERSITY OF MADRAS
Elections to the Senate and the Standing Committee of the Academic Council by the Academic Council
(No. V-1/AC-ELE/01-2010/41.)
No. VI-3(b)/10/2010.
It is hereby notified that Election to (i) the Senate by the Academic Council and (ii) the Standing Committee of the
Academic Council by the Academic Council shall be held under Section 14(a) — other members (2) and Section 24(k)
of the Madras University Act, 1923, respectively.
(I) SENATE:
Section 14(a) - Other members (2) of the Madras University Act, 1923:
"Ten members elected by the Academic Council from among its own body of whom not less than five
shall be teachers of affiliated Colleges”.
For filling  up of casual vacancies, FOUR MEMBERS have to be elected to the Senate by the Academic Council from
among its members.

The newly elected members shall hold office as members of the Senate for the residual period upto
28th September 2010 from the date of receipt of nomination/the date of withdrawal/the date of election/the date of declaration
of the result whichever is applicable for the election.
(ii) STANDING COMMITTEE OF THE ACADEMIC COUNCIL:
Section 24(k) of the Madras University Act, 1923:
"There shall be a Standing Committee of the Academic Council which shall consist of the Vice-Chancellor and five
other members of the Academic Council of whom.—
(i) two shall be the Principals or Teachers of Affiliated Colleges,
(ii) two shall be members of the Syndicate and
(iii) one shall be teacher of a Department of Study and Research in the University”.
For filling up of casual vacancies, THREE MEMBERS have to be elected to the Standing Committee of the Academic
Council of whom two members from Principals or Teachers of Affiliated Colleges and one member from the members
of the Syndicate.
The newly elected members will hold office as members of the Standing Committee of the Academic Council for the
residual period upto 25th September 2010 from the date of receipt of nomination/the date of withdrawal/the date of election/
the date of declaration of the result whichever is applicable for the election.
Consequent to the amendment of the Madras University Act, 1923 by the Tamil Nadu Universities Laws (Amendment)
Act, 1997 [No. 728, Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated 16th December 1998], amending the Sections
5-A, 14, 18 and 23 of the Madras University Act, 1923 (Act No. 41 of 1998) published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette
Extraordinary, dated 16th December 1998, “Restriction for election or nomination to Senate, Syndicate and Academic Concil
in certain cases (1) Notwithstanding anything contained in Sections 14, 18 or 23, any person who has completed two terms
of three years each, continuously in any one or two of the following authorities of the Universities namely, (i) THE SENATE,
(ii) THE SYNDICATE and (iii) THE ACADEMIC COUNCIL, shall be eligible, after a period of THREE YEARS HAS ELAPSED
FROM THE DATE OF HIS CEASING to be such member, for election or nomination to any of the above mentioned authorities.
Provided that for the purpose of this sub-section, if a person was elected or nominated to one authority and such person
became a member of another authority by virtue of the membership in the first mentioned authority, the period for which he
held office in the first mentioned authority alone shall be taken into account.
The election will be conducted according to the Statutes contained in Chapter XIX, Volume I, University Calendar 2001
and the provisions of the Madras University Act, 1923.
Nomination forms are available in the Registrar’s Office. Nomination should be sent so as to be received by the Registrar,
University of Madras, University Buildings, Chepauk, Chennai-5, NOT LATER THAN FRIDAY, THE 29th JANUARY, 2010
upto 4-00 p.m.
The scrutiny of nominations will take place on the same day, i.e. 29th January 2010 at 4-15 p.m. in the Registrar’s  Office,
Centenary Building, Chepauk, Chennai-600 005.
Nomination paper shall be enclosed in an envelope addressed to the Registrar by name and superscribed as indicated
below and sent by Registered Post (Ack. Due), so as to be received by the Registrar within the date and hour fixed, or
delivered to the Registrar either in person or by messenger. Nomination papers that are not enclosed in an envelope
superscribed and sent by Registered Post or are not delivered in person or by messenger as required above, shall be declared
invalid. An elector shall be eligible to subscribe either as proposer or as seconder in nomination papers only as many times
as there are vacancies but not more.
