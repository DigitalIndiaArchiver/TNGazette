   Thanjavur, Letter No. C4/9465/2004, dated 28-10-2013 and 23-06-2014.
Order: No. 163, Agriculture (S2), 28th October 2014.
In the Government Order first read above, orders have been issued for the closure of the Tamil Nadu Sugarcane Farm
Corporation Limited with effect from the date of issue of the above said order. Subsequently, the Board of Directors of
the Tamil Nadu Sugarcane Farm Corporation Limited vide minutes second read above, have resolved to close the company
under the Fast Track Exit Scheme announced by Ministry of Corporate Affairs, New Delhi.
(2) To Implement the proposed closure, the Managing Director (i/c), Tamil Nadu Sugarcane Farm Corporation Limited/Joint
Director of Agriculture, Thanjavur in his letters third read above has stated that the said company has already handed over its
assets to Joint Director of Agriculture, Thanjavur as per the directions of the Government of Tamil Nadu pending finalization of
transfer and modalities of adjustment in Accounts which is a unit of Agriculture Department in the Government of Tamil Nadu.
(3) In respect of pending court case relating to compensation amount from Thiru Arooran Sugars (P) Ltd, he has stated that
the Tamil Nadu Sugarcane Farm Corporation can transfer the court cases to the Joint Director of Agriculture,
Thanjavur, on behalf of the Government of Tamil Nadu. Accordingly, all the court cases along with loans and advances (Assets)
and other liabilities are being transferred to the Joint Director of Agriculture, Thanjavur, which is a unit of Agriculture) Department
in the Government of Tamil Nadu.
(4) The Managing Director (i/c), Tamil Nadu Sugarcane Farm Corporation Limited/Joint Director of Agriculture, Thanjavur has
therefore requested to issue ‘No Objection Certificate’ for the closure of Tamil Nadu Sugarcane Farm Corporation Limited,
Vadapathimangalam, Thanjavur District.
(5) The Government have examined the proposal of the Managing Director (i/c), Tamil Nadu Sugarcane Farm Corporation
and accordingly issue the following orders:-
(a) “No Objection Certificate” is issued to Tamil Nadu Sugarcane Farm Corporation for the purpose of filing an application
with Registrar of Companies for removal of the name of defunct Company Viz., Tamil Nadu Sugarcane Farm Corporation Ltd.,
since the Corporation is ‘inoperative’ and has not been carrying over any business activity for the last one year;
(b) Joint Director of Agriculture, Thanjavur shall engage a practicing Company Secretary as a consultant duly following
the procedures, who in turn would facilitate for filing necessary application to Registrar of Companies for striking off
the name of the Tamil Nadu Sugarcane Farm Corporation under Fast Tract Exit mode duly following the Guidelines
issued by Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Government of India, New Delhi and under the relevant provisions of Companies
Act, 2013;
