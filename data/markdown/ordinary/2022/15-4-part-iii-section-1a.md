No. SRO A-9/2022.—In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (e) of sub-section (2) of Section 64 of the Water 
(Prevention and control of Pollution) Act, 1974 (Central Act 6 of 1974), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, in consultation with the 
Tamil Nadu Pollution Control Board, hereby makes the following amendment to the Appointment of Chairman and Member 
Secretary of the Tamil Nadu Pollution Control Board Rules, 2019:-
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖග
In the said Rules, in rule 2, in sub-rule (4), in clause (b), the Serial Number 3A shall be modified as :-
 
”3A. Age limit “Must not have completed 60 years’’
SUPRIYA SAHU,
Additional Chief Secretary to Government.
TOURISM, CULTURE AND RELIGIOUS  ENDOWMENTS DEPARTMENT
Amendment to the Appointment of Auditor Rules 
[G.O.Ms.No.76, Tourism, Culture and Religious  Endowments (RE4-2), 29th March 2022 
Panguni 15, Pilava, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2053.]
               
No. SRO A-10/2022.—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 116 read with sub-section(2) 
of Section 87 of the Tamil Nadu Hindu Religious and Charitable Endowments Act, 1959 (Tamil Nadu Act 22 of 1959), the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Appointment of Auditor Rules,1961.
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖග
In the said Rules, for rule 2, the following rule shall be substituted, namely:-
“Audit of the accounts of all religious institutions and charitable endowments under the control of the Hindu Religious 
and Charitable Endowments Administration department shall be done by the Hindu Religious Institutions Audit Department, 
which is under the administrative control of Finance Department, Government of Tamil Nadu.”.
                                                                                 
CHANDRA MOHAN. B, 
Principal Secretary to Government.
