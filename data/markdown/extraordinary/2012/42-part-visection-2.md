WHEREAS, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission has fixed 20-11-2011 and 21-11-2011 as the dates for convening
meetings to elect Members and Chairmen of Standing Committees of all Corporations in accordance with the programme of
election notified in the S.O. No.115/2011/TNSEC/ME-1, dated 8-11-2011 of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission.
WHEREAS the Returning Officers/Commissioners of Erode, Vellore and Thoothukkudi City Municipal Corporations
have reported that the fixation of strength of members of Standing Committees have been resolved on 16-11-2011 and requested
this Commission to refix the dates for convening meetings to elect the respective Members and Chairmen of Standing
Committees.
WHEREAS, in view of reports received from the Returning Officers/Commissioners of City Municipal Corporations concerned,
the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission has considered necessary to fix the date for convening meetings to
elect the Members and Chairmen of Standing Committees in respect of Erode, Vellore and Thoothukkudi City Municipal
Corporations.

WHEREAS the Hon’ble High Court, Madras in its Order dated 2-12-2011 in W.P.No.25330/2011 has ordered that the
Tamil Nadu State Election Commission shall notify fresh election and to fix the date for convening a meeting to elect
Vice-Chairman of Thirukazhukundram Town Panchayat, Kancheepuram District and to appoint independent Observer to over see
the election and to appoint Returning Officer to conduct the election. Hence, the District Election Officer / District Collector,
Kancheepuram District and the Returning Officer, Thirukazhukundram Town Panchayat are directed to ensure the conducive
atmosphere so as to participate the Ward Members in the said election. The District Election Officer / District Collector,
Kancheepuram District is also directed to nominate an officer not below the rank of Deputy Collector to be appointed as
Independent Observer by the Commission for the aforesaid election. The District Election Officer / District Collector, Kancheepuram
District is further directed to appoint another Returning Officer in the place of Returning Officer already appointed for the conduct
of said election, as ordered by the Hon’ble High Court.
WHEREAS, in view of the Order of the Hon’ble High Court, Madras, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission has
considered necessary to fix the date for convening meeting to elect the Vice-Chairman of Thirukazhukundram Town Panchayat.
NOW, THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (1) of Section 43-B and Section 27 and of the
Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920) and sub-rule (2) of rule 99 of the Tamil Nadu Town
Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities, Municipalities and Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006, the Tamil Nadu State Election
Commission hereby fixes 27-2-2012 as the date for convening a meeting to elect Vice-Chairman of Thirukazhukundram Town
Panchayat in accordance with the following programme:-
1.
Last date for service of notice for the meetings of election
19-2-2012
of Vice-Chairman
