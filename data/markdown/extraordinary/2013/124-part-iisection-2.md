DTP—II-2 Ex. (124)
MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION AND WATER SUPPLY DEPARTMENT
CLASSIFIES THE SEMBAKKAM TOWN PANCHAYAT IN KANCHEEPURAM
DISTRICT INTO SECOND GRADE MUNICIPALITY UNDER THE
TAMIL NADU MUNICIPALITIES ACT, 1920.
[G.O. Ms. No. 68, Municipal Administration & Water Supply (MA.5),
16th May 2013, Vaikasi 2, Vijaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2044]
No. II(2)/MAWS/385(b)/2013.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (6) of Section 4 of
the (Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920), (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920)
the Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby classifies the Sembakkam Town Panchayat
in Kancheepuram District into Second Grade Municipality.
PHANINDRA REDDY,
Secretary to Government.
