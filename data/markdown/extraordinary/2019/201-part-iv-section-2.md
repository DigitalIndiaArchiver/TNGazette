of the President on the 3rd May 2019 and is hereby published for general 
information:—

(By order of the Governor)
S.S. POOVALINGAM,
Secretary to Government,
Law Department.
     An Act further to amend the Tamil Nadu Forest Act, 1882.
Bඍ it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the 
Sixty-ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows:—
1.  (1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu Forest (Amendment) Act, 2019.
 
(2)  It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by 
notiﬁ cation, appoint.

2. After section 16 of the Tamil Nadu Forest Act, 1882, the following section shall be 
inserted, namely:—
        “16-A.  Declaration of certain land to be a Reserved Forest.—  Notwithstanding 
anything contained in this Act, the Government may, by notiﬁ cation, declare any land 
in a janmam estate, vested with the Government under the Gudalur Janmam Estates 
(Abolition and Conversion into Ryotwari) Act, 1969 (Tamil Nadu Act 24 of 1969) and 
determined as forest under that Act, as reserved forest.”.
