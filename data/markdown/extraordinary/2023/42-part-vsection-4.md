NOTIFICATION BY THE ELECTION COMMISSION OF INDIA 
APPOINTMENT OF DATES FOR VARIOUS STAGES OF BYE-ELECTION TO TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE 
ASSEMBLY FROM 98. ERODE (EAST) ASSEMBLY CONSTITUENCY AND FIXATION OF HOURS 
OF POLL TO FILL A VACANCY CAUSED BY THE DEATH OF A MEMBER
No. SRO	G-3/2023.
The following Notification of the Election Commission of India, Nirvachan Sadan, Ashoka Road, 
New Delhi-110 001, dated 31st January, 2023 [11 Magha, 1944 (Saka)] is published:
No.100/TN-LA/1/2023:- Whereas, the seat of Thiru E. Thirumahan Everaa, in the Legislative Assembly 
of the Tamil Nadu State, elected from 98-Erode (East) Assembly Constituency has become vacant on 
04-01-2023, by reason of his death; and
2. Whereas, a bye-election is to be held for the purpose of filling the vacancy so caused; and
3. Now, therefore, in pursuance of sub-section (1) of section 150, and sections 30 and 56 of the 
Representation of the People Act, 1951 (43 of 1951), the Election Commission of India hereby:-
(A) Calls upon the said 98-Erode (East)Assembly Constituency in the State of Tamil Nadu to elect, 
before the 04-03-2023 (Saturday) and in accordance with the provisions of the said Act and of the rules and 
orders made thereunder, a person, for the purpose of filling the said vacancy in the Legislative Assembly of 
the State; and
(B)	 Appoints, with respect to the said election,-
	
(a)	 the 07-02-2023  (Tuesday), as the last date for making nominations;
	
(b)	 the 08-02-2023 (Wednesday),  as the date for scrutiny of nominations;
	
(c)	 the 10-02-2023 (Friday), as the last date for withdrawal of candidatures;
