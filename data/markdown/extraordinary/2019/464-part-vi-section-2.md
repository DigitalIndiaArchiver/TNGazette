Read:  
	
S.O.No.60-87/2019/TNSEC/PE-1, dated  7th December 2019.
No. VI(2)/125(c)/2019.
WHEREAS, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission has notified for the conduct of rural local body elections as per 
Statutory Order read above; 
WHEREAS, there is no provision in Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Elections) Rules, 1995 for affixing the photo of the Polling Agents 
in their appointment letter in Form 11; and  
WHEREAS, it is felt expedient to emphasise on the production of photo identity proof as evidence by the Polling Agent at the 
time of entry into the polling station for the ensuing Ordinary Elections to Rural Local Bodies as a measure to curtail impersonation;
NOW, THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (1) of Section 239 of the Tamil Nadu 
Panchayats Act, 1994 and rule 40 and 143 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Elections) Rules, 1995, the Tamil Nadu State Election 
commission specifies that the Polling Agents (including the relief agents) shall show any one of the twelve  documentary 
evidences in original as prescribed for the electors in S.O.No.100/2019/TNSEC/EG2, dated 11-12-2019 as proof of his/her 
identity to the Presiding Officer and Police Personnel at the time of entry into the Polling Station. The documentary evidence 
shown by him must have his/her photo identity proof of it.  Further, the Commission specifies that every Contesting Candidate or 
his/her Election Agent shall appoint one Polling Agent for each Polling Station and only one Relief Agent to act as the Polling Agent 
of such candidate at each Polling Station in the ensuing Ordinary Elections to Rural Local Bodies and any one of them shall be 
present in the Polling Station at a time.
The above identity proof has been prescribed only for the purpose of verifying the identity of the person appointed as Polling 
Agent and not for any other purpose of scrutinizing other things.
(By Order of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission)
