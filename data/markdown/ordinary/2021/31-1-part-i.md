Order: No. 433, Public (Special-A), 23rd July 2021.
Thiru B.Krishna Moorthy, IOFS, Director, Ordnance 
Factory Board, New Delhi Office, Department of Defence 
Production, Ministry of Defence, New Delhi has requested 
for inter cadre deputation with Government of Tamil Nadu as 
Additional Secretary in one of the Secretariat Department or in 
an equivalent post in one of the HOD's office/ PSU's/Boards/ 
Bureaus, etc. The Government of Tamil Nadu have agreed for 
the deputation of Thiru B. Krishna Moorthy, IOFS, Director, 
Ordnance Factory Board, New Delhi Office, Department of 
Defence Production, Ministry of Defence, New Delhi with 
this state Government for a period of three years with effect 
from the date of his reporting for duty in the Government of 
Tamil Nadu. The Government of India, Ministry of Defence, 
Department of Defence Production have accepted the above 
said proposal in the reference third read above. 
2.  Thiru B. Krishna Moorthy, IOFS, Director, Ordnance. 
Factory Board, New Delhi Office, Department of Defence 
Production, Ministry of Defence, New Delhi on deputation to 
the Government of Tamil Nadu is posted to act as the Project 
Director, Tamil Nadu Defence Industrial Corridor in Tamil Nadu 
Industrial Development Corporation Ltd. (TIDCO), Chennai in 
the Additional Secretary Level in the State Government on 
deputation basis for the period of 3 (Three) years with pay 
protection. The Project Director, Tamil Nadu Defence Industrial 
Corridor in Tamil Nadu should directly report to the Chairman 
and Managing Director, TIDCO. He should perform such other 
responsibilities as may be entrusted to him from time to time 
by the Principal Secretary, Industries Department or Chairman 
and Managing Director, Tamil Nadu Industrial Development 
Corporation Limited.
3. During the period of his deputation, he is allowed 
to draw his pay in his time scale of pay, allowances and 
entitlements as applicable to him in his parent cadre and 
other perquisites as eligible to the post to which he is posted.
(By Order of the Governor.)
	
v. irai anbu,
