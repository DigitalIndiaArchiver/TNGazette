NOTIFICATION – III
[G.O. Ms. No.1, Industries (MIE.1) 6th January 2020, 
ñ£˜èN 21, Mè£K, F¼õœÀõ˜ Ý‡´&2050.]
No. II(1)/IND/1(b-3)/2020.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-sections (1) 
and (2) of Section 13 of the Tamil Nadu Industrial Township 
Area Development Authority Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 33 of 
1997), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that the 
provisions of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 
(Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920) and the rules made thereunder 
shall apply to the Ponneri Industrial Township Area, subject 
to the modiﬁ cations and authorisations speciﬁ ed below:-
I. (a) All provisions of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities 
Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920) except sections 3A 
to 3N, 4, 4A, 4B, 5, 7, 7A, 8, 9, 12, 12A, 12B, 12C, 13B, 
27, 31, 37, 38A, 40, 40A, 40B, 40C, 41, 43 to 60H, 68, 
77A, 77AA, 77B, 217A to 217Q, 365, 366 and 373 and 
Schedules I, II and IX.
 (b) All rules made under the Tamil Nadu District 
Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920) except 
rules framed and relatable to the sections and Schedules 
excluded in clause (a) above.
(c)  Reference to “Municipal Council”, Municipal Councils”, 
“Council”, “Municipality”, Municipal Councillor”, “Councillors”, 
and Commissioner” shall unless the context otherwise 
requires, be construed as references to “Authority”, “Authority”, 
“Authority”, “Industrial Township Area”, “Member”, “Members” 
and “Chief Executive Oﬃ  cer”, respectively.
II. (a) The Authority shall levy all or any of the taxes 
and fees which may be levied by virtue of the provisions 
of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 
(Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920) extended to the Industrial 
Township Area as speciﬁ ed in this notiﬁ cation.
 (b) The Chief Executive Oﬃ  cer of the Authority shall 
exercise and perform in regard to the Industrial Township 
Area, the powers and duties assigned to the executive 
authority of a municipality.
