FINANCE  DEPARTMENT.
Amendment to the Ad hoc Rules relating to the Temporary Post of Punch Operator in Government
Data Centre in Tamil Nadu General Subordinate Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 294, Finance (CA) 1st August 2012 Aadi: 18 Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2043]
No. SRO A-18/2013.— In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of  India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the rules published with the Finance Department
Notification S.R.O. No.200 of 1979, at page 241 of Part III- Section 1 (b) of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette,
dated the 18th December 1987.
2. The amendment hereby made shall have come into force on the 1st August 2012.
AMENDMENT.
In the said Rules, in rule 5, in sub-rule (b), in the Table, for the entry in column (3) against the post “Punch Operator”
in column (1) thereof, the following entries shall be substituted, namely:-
“(i)  Must have passed +2 (Plus two) Higher Secondary Grade Examination conducted by the Government of
Tamil Nadu ; or an equivalent Examination;
(ii)  Must have passed the Government Technical Examination in Typewriting in English and in Tamil by senior
Grade conducted by the Department of Technical Education, Government of Tamil Nadu; and
(iii)  Must have passed “Certificate Course in Computer Office Automation conducted by the Department of Technical
Education, Government of Tamil Nadu.”
Amendments to the Ad hoc Rules relating to Certain Post in the Co-operative Department in  Tamil Nadu
Co-operative  Subordinate Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 120, Finance (Co-operative Audit) 17th April 2013, Chithirai-4, Vijaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2044]
No. SRO A-19/2013.— In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of  India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the rules Published with the Finance Department
Notification No. SRO.149, dated:31-3-1986, at pages 165-173 of Part III section-I  of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette,
dated the 16th April 1986.
(2) The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 17th June 1981.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules, in sub-rule (b), to the rule 8, in the Table:-
1 (i) after the entry (iii) in column (3), against the entry (ii) by promotion in column (2), relating to the post “1.
Co-operative Audit Officer” in column (1) thereof, the following proviso shall be added, namely:-
“Provided that the members of composite Co-operative Department who have been permanetly absorbed in the
Co-operative audit Department in Government Order Ms.No.26, Finance (Audit-II) Department, dated:12-1-1989 need not
pass the Account Test Subordinate Officers Part-II”.
(ii)  after the entry (iv) in column (3), against the entry (ii) by promotion in column (2), relating to the post “2. Senior
Co-operative Auditor” in column (1) thereof, the following proviso shall be added, namely:-
“ Provided that the members of composite Co-operative Department who have been permanently absorbed in the
Co-operative Audit  Department in Government order. Ms.No.26, Finance (Audit-II) Department, dated:12-1-1989 need not
pass the Account Test for Subordinate Officers Part-I”.
(iii)  After the entry (iv) in column (3), against the entry (iii) by promotion in column (2), relating the post “3. Junior
Co-operative Auditor” in column (1) thereof, the following proviso shall be added, namely:-
“Provided that the members of composite Co-operative Department who have been permanently absorbed in the
Co-operative Audit Department in Government order Ms.No. 26, Finance (Audit-II) Department, dated:12-1-1989 need not
pass the Account Test for Subordinate Officers Part-I;”.
