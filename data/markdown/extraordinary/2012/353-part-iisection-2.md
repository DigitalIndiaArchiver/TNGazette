RECONSTITUTION OF STATE MINORITIES COMMISSION WITH CHAIRMAN AND MEMBERS UNDER
TAMIL NADU STATE MINORITIES COMMISSION ACT, FOR CERTAIN PERIOD.
[G.O. Ms. No. 148, Backward Classes, Most Backward Classes and Minorities Welfare (MWRU),
28th December 2012, Margazhi 13, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2043.]
No. II(2)/BCMBCMW/901(c-1)/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 3 read with sub-section (1) of Section 4 of
the Tamil Nadu State Minorities Commission Act, 2010 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 2010), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby reconstitute the Tamil Nadu State Minorities Commission to exercise the powers conferred on, and to
perform the functions assigned to it under the said Act with the following members, namely:-
