UNIVERSITY AUTHORITIES
MADURAI KAMARAJ UNIVERSITY
(University with Potential for Excellence)
Election to the Authorities of the University
(Ref. No. ME-2/AC/Elec./PG Coll/2009.)
No. VI-3(b)/20/2009.
It is hereby notified that the following election will be held under Sections mentioned hereunder as per the Madurai
Kamaraj University Act, 1965:—
"Election will be held to elect ONE MEMBER to the Academic Council from each Post-Graduate College who is not
the Principal of such College elected by the Teachers of the College including Physical Directors but excluding the Principal,
Tutors, Demonstrators and Physical Instructors from among themselves under Section 24 (a) Class-II  (4) of the Madurai
Kamaraj University Act, 1965. The Principals of the Colleges concerned are requested to hold the election and communicate
the result".
Palkalai Nagar, Madurai-625 021,
DR. I. SINGARAM,
16th September 2009.
Registrar.

Election of a Member of Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly from among themselves to the Board of
Management of Tamil Nadu Veterinary and Animal Sciences University.
(Lr. No. 12976/R.1/BOM/2009.)
No. VI-3(b)/21/2009.
It is hereby notified that as per Section 18 (2) (h) under heading CLASS-II-OTHER MEMBERS of the Act of Tamil
Nadu Veterinary and Animal Sciences University (Tamil Nadu Act 42 of 1989) the following person has been elected from
among the member of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly to serve as a member in the Board of Management of
Tamil Nadu Veterinary and Animal Sciences University. His term shall be three years or till he ceases to be the member
of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly whichever is earlier with effect from 11th August 2009:—
