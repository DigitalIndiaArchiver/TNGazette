COMMERCIAL TAXES AND REGISTRATION DEPARTMENT
DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF THE INDIAN STAMP AND THE REGISTRATION
(TAMIL NADU AMENDMENT) ACT, 2006.
[G.O. Ms. No. 152, Commercial Taxes and Registration (J1), 31st August 2010.]
No. II (2)/CTR/554(a)/2010.
 In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (3) of Section 1 of the Indian Stamp and the
Registration (Tamil Nadu Amendment) Act, 2006 (Tamil Nadu Act 13 of 2008), the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the 1st day of September 2010 as the date on which clause (d) of
sub-section (1) of Section 2 of the said Act shall come into force.
MD. NASIMUDDIN,
Secretary to Government.
