No. SRO B-48/2017.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor 
of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for Class LV in the Tamil Nadu General Service:—
(1) amendment (1) hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 18th October 2001. 
(2) amendments (2) (a), (2) (b), (3), (4) (a), 4 (b) and (5) hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on 
the 12th October 2010.
AMENDMENTS.
In the said Special Rules, in PART II,—
(1) the expression “and Rural Works” wherever it occurs, shall be omitted;
(2) in rule 1, -
 
(a) for the expression “Accounts Ofﬁ cer and Financial Adviser to Chief Engineer (Highways)”, the expression “Chief 
Accounts Ofﬁ cer to the Director General/Chief Engineer (Highways)” shall be substituted;
 
(b) for the expression “Deputy Accounts Ofﬁ cer”, the expression “Accounts Ofﬁ cer” shall be substituted; 
(3) In rule 2, for the expression “Ofﬁ ce of the Chief Engineer (Highways) and Chief Engineer (National Highways)”, the 
expression “Ofﬁ ce of the Director General/Chief Engineer (Highways)” shall be substituted;
(4)  in rule 3, -
 
(a) in sub-rule (a), for the expression “Deputy Accounts Ofﬁ cer”, the expression “Accounts Ofﬁ cer” shall be 
substituted;
 
(b)  in sub-rule (b), for the expression “Ofﬁ ce of the Chief Engineer (Highways), or Chief Engineer (National 
Highways)” the expression “Ofﬁ ce of the Director General/Chief Engineer (Highways)” shall be substituted; 
(5)  in rule 4, for the expression “Chief Engineer (Highways)”, the expression “Director General/Chief Engineer (Highways)” 
shall be substituted.
RAJEEV RANJAN,
Additional Chief Secretary to Government.
