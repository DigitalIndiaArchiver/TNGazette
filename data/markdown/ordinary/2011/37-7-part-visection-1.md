Proposal for appointment of Notary in the area of Thoothukkudi District.
(D. No. 7143.)
No. VI(1)/364/2011.
Whereas Thiru R. Jayapal, M.A.B.L., D.H.E., Advocate, residing at No. 1/164 (New) 2G/637, Rajeev Nagar North,
Thoothukkudi, Thoothukkudi District, has applied to this court to appoint him as a Notary for the Thoothukkudi limits area
comprised in Thoothukkudi District. Notice is hereby given that those having objections may file their objections before
the Principal District Court, Thoothukkudi within Fourteen (14) days from the date of publication of this Notice.
Principal District Court,
J. PRABHUDASS,
Thoothukkudi,
Principal District Judge.
7th September 2011.
Destruction of Old Records - Ribe Cases - Rule 128 of Criminal Rules of Practice.
(Roc. No. 299/2011)
No. VI(1)/365/2011.
Notice is hereby given that all the old case records and all the documents, files in the cases enumerated below will
unless previously claimed be Destroyed at the expiry of the month from the date of publications of this Notificatiion.
The Parties who have filed any documents in these cases should apply to the III Metropolitan Magistrate of this court
for the return of documents, account book etc., within a month from the date of publication of this notification in the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, failing which the documents will be destroyed along with the case records.
1. Year 1982 to 1985 - Entire records of warrant cases disposed of in the year 1985.
2. Year 2000 to 2005 - Entire records in all summons cases.
3. Year 1975 to 1986 - Entire records in all the M.P in M.C.
4. Year 2002 to 2004 - Entire records in Petty Cases, Evening Court Petty Cases.
George Town, Chennai,
N. VAITHIYANATHAN,
13th September 2011.
III Metropolitan Magistrate.
