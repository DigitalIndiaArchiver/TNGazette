In Accordance With Rule 2, In Regard to the Elections to the Tamil Nadu Medical Council, Chennai, under clause (F) 
of sub-Section (1) of Section 5 of The Madras Medical Registration Act, 1914 (Act IV of 1914) as Amended by the Madras 
Medical Registration (Amendment) Act XVI of 1938, Notice is hereby given that an Election of Seven Members of the Council 
to be Elected from amongst themselves by the Registered Medical Practitioners whose names appear in the printed Medical 
Registers (Electoral Roll-Soft Copy) updated upto 30th June 2022 available in the Medical Council oﬃ  ce, will be held.
Nomination paper in form II under the rule will be supplied by the returning Oﬃ  cer to any elector who may apply for 
the same from 19.10.2022. These must be ﬁ led in accordance with the instructions thereon and returned to the returning 
Oﬃ  cer not later than 3.P.M on Monday, the 31st October, 2022. The scrutiny of nomination papers will take place at 11.A.M 
on Tuesday the 1st November, 2022 at the oﬃ  ce of the returning oﬃ  cer. A list of valid nominations will be published in the 
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette on Wednesday, the 9th november, 2022. Last date for withdrawal of nomination is 3 p.M. 
On wednesday, the 23rd november, 2022. Publication of ﬁ nal list of valid Nominations is on Wednesday, the 30th november, 
2022. Voting papers and other forms connected therewith will be sent by Registered post by the returning Oﬃ  cer on or 
before Monday, the 19th December, 2022 to all electors registered upto 30.06.2022 with instructions printed therein and the 
voting papers are returnable to the returning Oﬃ  cer by Registered Post not later than 5 P.M. on Thursday, the 19th January, 
2023. The scrutiny and counting of votes will take place at the oﬃ  ce of the returning Oﬃ  cer from Friday, the 20th January, 
2023 11.A.M. onwards.
 
If for any reason the dates ﬁ xed for the various proceedings in the conduct of the election happens to fall on a 
holiday the proceedings ﬁ xed for that day will take place in the next day and at the time ﬁ xed already.
 
All Communications regarding the elections should be addressed as hereunder.
