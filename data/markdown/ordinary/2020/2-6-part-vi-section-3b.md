Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai Act.
[Rc. No. EIII(1)/49630/2018.] 
No.VI-3(b)/1/2020.
Under Sub-Statute (1) of Statute 5 of Chapter XXI of the General Statutes of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical 
University, Chennai, it is hereby published that the following members have been elected to the Senate of the Tamil 
Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai, by the teachers of each aﬃ  liated Medical College from among themselves 
Under Section 18(3) (a) of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai Act, 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 37 of 1987).
The membership will be for the period as noted against each or till they hold the post of Professor of the concerned 
aﬃ  liated College of this University, or till the date of their superannuation whichever is earlier.
