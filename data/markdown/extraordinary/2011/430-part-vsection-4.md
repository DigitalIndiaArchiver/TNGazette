NOTIFICATIONS BY THE ELECTION COMMISSION OF INDIA.
ELECTION SYMBOLS (RESERVATION AND ALLOTMENT) ORDER, 1968
No. SRO G-31/2011.
The following Notification of the Election Commission of India, Nirvachan Sadan, Ashoka Road,
New Delhi-110 001, dated 4th November, 2011 [13 Kartika, 1933 (Saka)] is republished:—
No. 56/2011/PPS-(ii).—In pursuance of sub-paragraph (2) of paragraph 17 of the Election Symbols (Reservation and
Allotment) Order, 1968, the Election Commission of India hereby makes the following further amendment to its Notification
No. 56/2011/PPS-II, dated 8-3-2011, namely:—
In Table IV (List of free Symbols), appended to the said Notification the existing entry “40. Road Roller”
shall be deleted.
