GENERAL  NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAQF BOARD, CHENNAI-600 001.
Taking over the West Chintamani Mosque Waqf, Chintamani Village, Trichy District under
Direct  Management of Tamil Nadu Waqf Board. 
(Lr. Rc. No. 2949/05/B5/TRY)
No. VI-3(a)/44/2022.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under section 65 of the Waqf Act 1995, the Tamil Nadu Waqf 
Board in its Resolution Item No.101/22 In R.C.No. 2949/05/B5/TRY dated: 13.07.2022 has ordered to take over 
WEST CHINTAMANI MOSQUE WAQF, CHINTAMANI VILLAGE, TRICHY DISTRICT under the Direct Management of Tamil Nadu 
Waqf Board and to appoint the Waqf Inspector, Trichy as its Executive Oﬃ  cer. 
Chennai-600 001, 
A.B. RAFIULLA,
11th August 2022. 
Chief Executive Oﬃ  cer,
 
Tamil Nadu Waqf Board.
Taking over the Rahmanya Jumma Pallivasal Waqf, Sembium Madhavaram High Road, Perambur, Chennai District 
under Direct Management of Tamil Nadu Waqf Board.
(Lr. Rc. No. 11155/18/B9/CHEN)
No. VI-3(a)/45/2022.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under section 65 of the Waqf Act 1995, the Tamil Nadu Waqf 
Board in its Resolution Item No.116/22 In R.C. No. 11155/18/B9/CHEN dated: 13.07.2022 has ordered to take over Rahmanya 
Jumma Pallivasal Waqf, Sembium Madhavaram High Road, Perambur, Chennai District under the Direct Management 
of Tamil Nadu Waqf Board and to appoint the Superintendent of Waqfs, Chennai as its Executive Oﬃ  cer.
Chennai-600 001, 
A.B. RAFIULLA,
11th August 2022. 
Chief Executive Oﬃ  cer,
 
Tamil Nadu Waqf Board.
