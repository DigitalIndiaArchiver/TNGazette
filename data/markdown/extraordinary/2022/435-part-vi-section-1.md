of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).

10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands specified in the Schedule below and measuring 537 Sq.mt 
to the same, a little more or less needed for industrial purposes, for the formation of 4th line between Korukkupet and Ennore 
Railway station.
The plans of the lands are kept in the office of the District Collector, Chennai Taluk Office, Tondiarpet and Revenue Divisional 
Officer, Tondiarpet  may be inspected at any time during office hours.
