No. SRO B-68/2015.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the
Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Rules published with the
Health and Family Welfare Department Notification No. SRO B-446/79, published at Pages 549-550 of Part III—Section 1(b)
of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 10th October 1979.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules.—
1.
in rule 1, for the expression “Government General Hospital, Madras”, the expression “Government Medical College and
Hospitals” shall be substituted;
2.
in rule 4, for the expression “the Dean, Government General Hospital, Madras”, the expression “Dean/Head of the
Institution concerned” shall be substituted;
3. in rule 5,—
(i) in sub-rule (a), for the expression “26 Years”, the expression “30 years” shall be substituted;
(ii) in sub-rule (b), under “THE TABLE”, for the entries “(ii) By direct recruitment and recruitment by transfer”, in
column (1) and the corresponding entries in column (2) thereof, the following entries shall, respectively, be substituted,
namely:—
 (ii) By recruitment by transfer
(i) Must have passed Higher Secondary Course with the
     from any other service
   following Science Subjects,
     and by direct recruitment.
(a) Physics, Chemistry, Botony and Zoology or
(b) Physics, Chemistry, Biology with any one of the
    related subjecs; and
(ii) Must have passed one year Certificate Course in Pump
   Technician Conducted at Government Medical Institutions
 
or in any other Institution recognized by the State or
  Central Government.
4. in rule 7, after the proviso, the following provisos shall be added, namely:—
“Provided further that on and from the 1st June 1988, the monthly pay shall be calculated in the scale
of pay of
Rs. 1200-30-1560-40-2040:
Provided also that on and from the 1st January 1996, the monthly pay shall be calculated in the scale of pay of
Rs. 4000-100-6000:
Provided also that on and from 1st January 2006, the monthly pay shall be calculated in the Pay Band of
Rs. 5200-20200 with Grade Pay of Rs. 2400.”.
J. RADHAKRISHNAN,
Secretary to Government.
