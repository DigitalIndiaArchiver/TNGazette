TRANSPORT  DEPARTMENT
ACQUISITION OF LANDS
[G.O. Ms. No. 24,  Transport (I.2), 20th February 2009.]
No.II(2)/TRA/80(b)/2009.
Whereas it appears to the Government of Tamil Nadu
that the lands specified in the schedule below and situated
in No. 7, Perambalur (South) Village, Perambalur Taluk,
Perambalur District are needed for a public purpose, to wit
for the construction of a new building for the Head Post
Office, Perambalur notice to that effect is hereby given to all
to whom it may concern in accordance with the provisions
of sub-section (1) of Section 4 of Land Acquisition Act, 1894
(Central Act I of 1894).
And, whereas it has become necessary to acquire the
immediate possession of Lands specified in the schedule
below, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that the
lands be acquired under the provision of sub-section (2)
of Section 17 of the said Act.
Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by
sub-section (2) of Section 4 of the said Act, the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby authorizes, the Revenue Divisional Officer,
Perambalur, his staff and workmen to exercise to powers
conferred by the said sub-section and under clause (c) of
Section 3 of the said Act, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
appoints the Revenue Divisional Officer, Perambalur to
perform the functions of the Collector, under the said Act.
Under sub-section (4) of Section 17 of the said Act, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that in view of the
urgency of the case the provisions of Section 5(A) of the
said Act, shall not apply to this case.
