 WHEREAS on considering the prevailing Covid-19 pandemic scenario and as per the directives of Government of India, 
Ministry of Home Aﬀ airs, State-wide lockdown/restrictions was enforced throughout the State upto 31-01-2022 in order to 
control the spread of COVID-19. 
 2. Now, the proposal of the Principal Secretary/ Commissioner of Revenue Administration is accepted and the Government 
hereby issue the following orders under Disaster Management Act, 2005:-
 
i. In the present scenario of increasing trend in the Covid–19 Omicron variant cases, by considering the public interest, 
complete lockdown to be enforced on 23.01.2022 (Sunday).
 
ii. During this complete lockdown day, all allowed essential activities and restricted activities in existence 
shall continue to be permitted as that were implemented during last complete lockdown on 16-01-2022 (Sunday).
 
iii. Also, in the interest of passengers coming from outstation, regular autos, rental cars booked through apps 
shall be allowed to pick up passengers at places like Chennai Central, Egmore railway station and Koyambedu bus 
stand. This applies to district railway station and mofussil bus stands.
3. Any person violating these measures will be liable to be proceeded against as per the provisions of Section 51 to 60 
of the Disaster Management Act, 2005, besides legal action under Section 188 of the IPC, and other legal provisions as 
applicable. 
 
V. IRAI ANBU,
