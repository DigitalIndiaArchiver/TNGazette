In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu District Limits Act, 1865 
(Tamil Nadu Act 1 of 1865), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that with eﬀ ect on and from 
28th December 2020.
     (i) 
 Nagapattinam District is bifurcated into Nagapattinam and Mayiladuthurai Districts as 
follows:-
         
 
a. 
 Nagapattinam  District  with Head Quarters at  Nagapattinam.
         
 
b. 
 Mayiladuthurai  District  with Head Quarters at Mayiladuthurai.
      (ii) 
 Two Divisions of Nagapattinam and Vedaranyam (new) and Four Taluks of Nagapattinam, 
Keelvelur, Thirukkuvalai and Vedaranyam shall constitute the Nagapattinam District.
 (iii) 
 Two Divisions of  Mayiladuthurai and Sirkali (new) and Four Taluks of Mayiladuthurai, 
Tharangambadi, Kuttalam and Sirkali shall constitute the Mayiladuthurai  new District.
