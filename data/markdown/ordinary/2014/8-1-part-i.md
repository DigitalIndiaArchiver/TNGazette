No. I/PUSP/8/2014.—The following Notification of the
Government of India, Ministry of Law and Justice
(Department of Justice), Jaisalmer  House, 26, Man Singh
Road, New Delhi-110 011. Dated 11th February 2014
is republished.
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (2) of
Article 124 of the Constitution of India, the President is
pleased to appoint Shri Justice Rajesh Kumar Agrawal,
Chief Justice of the Madras High Court, to be Judge of the
Supreme Court of India with effect from the date he
assumes charge of his office.
