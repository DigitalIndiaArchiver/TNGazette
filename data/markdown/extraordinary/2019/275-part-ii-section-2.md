The Hosur City Municipal Corporation Act, 2019 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 2019) wherein Section 3 inter-alia provides for 
the establishment of Municipal Corporation for the city of Hosur and section 10 enables the Government to appoint Special 
Oﬃ  cer to the Hosur City Municipal Corporation;
AND WHEREAS, it is necessary to appoint Special Officer to exercise the powers and to discharge the 
functions of the Hosur City Municipal Corporation under the Hosur City Municipal Corporation Act, 2019 
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 2019), until the day on which the ﬁ rst meeting of the council of the said Corporation is held after 
ordinary elections to the said Corporation after the date of commencement of the said Act or until the 31st day of December 
2019, whichever is earlier.
NOW, THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of section 10 of the Hosur City Municipal 
Corporation Act, 2019 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 2019), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the Commissioner of Hosur 
City Municipal Corporation, as the Special Oﬃ  cer of the Corporation.
2. This Notiﬁ cation shall come into force with eﬀ ect on and from the 1st July 2019.
