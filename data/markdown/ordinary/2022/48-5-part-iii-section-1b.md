No. SRO B-53/2022.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, 
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Agriculture 
Extension Service (Section 36 of Volume II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual). 
The Amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on and from the 15th September, 2016. 
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖගඛ 
In the said Special Rules, - 
 
(1) for rule 5, the following rule shall be substituted, namely:-  
 
    "(5) Reservation of appointment - The principle of reservation of appointments as provided in Section 27 of the 
Tamil Nadu Government Servants (Conditions of Service) Act, 2016 (Tamil Nadu Act 14 of 2016) shall apply for 
appointment to the posts of Assistant Director of Agriculture (Extension) and Agricultural Oﬃ  cer (Extension) by direct 
recruitment, separately." 
 
(2)  in the Annexure {referred to in rule 6 (b)}, in Class IV, for  item (ii) in column (3) against the entry "Agricultural 
Oﬃ  cer (Extension)" in column (1) and the corresponding entries relating thereto in column (2), thereof, the following 
item shall be substituted, namely :- 
       "(ii) Must possess adequate knowledge of Tamil as deﬁ ned in the Explanation to sub-section (1) of Section 21 
of the Tamil Nadu Government Servants (Conditions of Service) Act, 2016 (Tamil Nadu Act 14 of 2016)." 
C. SAMAYAMOORTHY, 
Agricultural Production Commissioner and 
Secretary to Government.
