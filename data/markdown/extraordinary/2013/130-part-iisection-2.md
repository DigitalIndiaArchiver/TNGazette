NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
DTP—II-2 Ex. (130)
BACKWARD CLASSES, MOST BACKWARD CLASSES AND MINORITIES WELFARE DEPARTMENT
APPOINTMENT OF CERTAIN OFFICIAL AS ELECTION AUTHORITIES FOR CONDUCTING ELECTION OF
MEMBERS OF TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD UNDER THE WAKF  ACT.
[G.O. (D.) No. 21, Backward Classes, Most Backward Classes and Minorities Welfare (T),
20th May 2013, Vaikasi 6, Vijaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2044.]
No. II(2)/BCMBCMW/385(e)/2013.
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (a) of sub-rule (1) of rule 3 of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board (Conduct of Election
for Members) Rules, 1997 and in supersession of the Backward Classes, Most Backward Classes and Minorities Welfare
Department Notification No.II(2)/BCMBCMW/352(b)/2013, dated the 3rd May 2013, published at page 1 of
Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 3rd May 2013, the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints Thiru K. Arulmozhi, I.A.S., Principal Secretary to Government, Backward Classes,
Most Backward Classes and Minorities Welfare Department, Chennai-9 as Election Authority for the purpose of
conducting election of Muslim Members of Parliament from the State and Muslim Members of State Legislature
to the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board as specified in the said Rules.
K. ARULMOZHI,
Principal Secretary to Government.
