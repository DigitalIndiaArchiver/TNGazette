(Transferee).
No. VI-3(a)/67/2009.
M/s. Equity Eximp Private Limited, a company incorporated under the provisions of the Companies Act, 1956, having its
registered office at No. 12/5, 29th Cross Street, Indira Nagar, Chennai-600 020—Applicant/3rd Transferor Company.
NOTICE OF  PETITION
A petition under Sections 391 to 394 of the Companies Act, 1956, to sanction a Scheme of Amalgamation of
Messrs Dencove Investments Private Limited, with (1) Messrs Multi Exim Private Limited, (2) Messrs Interkrish Exim Private
Limited and (3) Messrs Equity Eximp Private Limited was presented by Messrs Equity Eximp Private Limited, on the
1st of April 2009 and the said petition is fixed for hearing before the Company Judge on 27th April 2009.
Any person desirous of supporting or opposing the said petition should send to the petitioner’s Advocate, notice of his
intention, signed by him or his  Advocate, with his name and address, so as to reach the petitioner’s advocate not
later than two days before the date fixed for the hearing of the petition. Where he seeks to oppose the petition,
grounds of opposition or a copy of the affidavit shall be furnished with such notice. A copy of the petition
will be furnished by the undersigned to any person requiring the same on payment of the prescribed charges for the
same.
Dated at Chennai this 1st day of April 2009.
“Haji S. Madharsha & Sons Building”,
M/s. B. GIRIDHARA RAO,
V-Floor,

No. 148, II Line Beach,
Advocates for Petitioner.
