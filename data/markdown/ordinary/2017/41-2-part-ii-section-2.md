No.II(2)/BCMBCMW/839/2017.—Thiru H. Mohammed
Hyder Ali had preferred an appeal petition under sub-section
(2) of Section 65 of the Waqf Act, 1995 (Central Act 43 of
1995) against taking over the direct management of
Anjuvannam Peer Mohammadia Muslim Association,
Thuckalay Village, Kalkulam Taluk, Kanyakumari District by
the Tamil Nadu Waqf Board in its resolution in item
No.151/15 in Rc. No. 144/11/Ý1/è.°ñK, dated 18-08-2015.
The Committee of Anjuvannam Peer Mohammadia Muslim
Association, Kanyakumari District with the Muthawali
Thiru. H. Mohammed Hyder Ali was elected for three years
tenure period from 22-06-2014 to 21-06-2017. Based on
certain complaints and after conducting enquiry, the
Tamil Nadu Waqf Board assumed the direct management
of Anjuvannam Peer Mohammadia Muslim Association,
Kanyakumari District.
2. On careful consideration of the contentions raised in
the aforesaid appeal and the submissions made by the
appellant during the personal hearing, it is found that the
Tamil Nadu Waqf Board had given sufficient opportunity of
being heard to the petitioner and the petitioner had utilized
those opportunities and submitted his arguments before
the Board and that the Board did not act against the
principles of natural justice. Accordingly, the Government
have come to the conclusion that there is no incorrectness
or illegality in the order passed by the Tamil Nadu Waqf
Board vide its resolution in item No.151/15 in
Rc. No. 144/11/Ý1/è.°ñK, dated 18-08-2015. Hence, the
appeal preferred by Thiru. H. Mohammed Hyder Ali under
sub-section (2) of Section 65 of the Waqf Act 1995 is devoid
of merits and the same is hereby rejected.
3. Further, in as much as the three years tenure period
of the President and the Committee of the Anjuvannam Peer
Mohammadia Muslim Association, Kanyakumari District has
expired on 21-06-2017, the Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Waqf Board is directed to conduct the election
for the management of the Anjuvannam Peer Mohammadia
Muslim Association, Kanyakumari District, as per procedure.
A. KARTHIK,
Secretary to Government.

No.II(2)/HOPE/858/2017.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 18-B read with
clause (iii) of sub-section (2-A) of Section 54 of the
Tamil Nadu Prohibition Act, 1937 (Tamil Nadu Act X of 1937),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the
following amendment to the Prohibition and Excise
Department Notification No. II(1)PE/239(d)/81, published at
Page 2 of Part II—Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Government
Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 1st May 1981, as
subsequently amended.
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to
have come into force on and from the 21st September 2015.
AMENDMENT
In the said Notification, in the Table, in the Annexure,
under the headings “ORDINARY BRANDS”, MEDIUM
BRANDS” and “PREMIUM BRANDS”, for the sub-heading
“Thiruvalargal United Spirits Limited”, the sub-heading
“Thiruvalargal Enrica Enterprises Private Limited” shall
respectively be substituted.
NIRANJAN MARDI,
