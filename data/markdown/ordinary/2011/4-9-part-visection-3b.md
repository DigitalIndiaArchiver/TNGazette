Election of One nominee by the Senate and Two by the Syndicate to the Committee for recommending
a panel of Three names to the Chancellor for the appointment of the Vice-Chancellor.
(ГҜ.ГЁ. ГўвҖЎ 408/ГЁ/2011.)
No. VI-3(b)/9/2011.
It is hereby notified that in accordance with sub-sections (1) and (2) of Section 12 of the Tamil University Act, 1982 and
under the heading вҖңD. Committee for recommending a panel for Vice-ChancellorshipвҖқ in Chapter XXV of the  Tamil University
Statutes, elections will be held for nominating one person by the Senate of the University and two persons by the Syndicate
of the University to serve on the Committee of five who are to suggest a panel of three names to the Chancellor for the
appointment of the Vice-Chancellor. The election will be held in accordance with the Statutes under Chapter XXV.
Each member of the Senate shall have the right to nominate not more than one person who is not a member of any of the
authorities of the University to the said Committee. Similarly each member of the Syndicate shall have the right to nominate
not more than two persons who are not members of any of the authorities of the University to the said Committee.
Every nomination by a member of the Syndicate shall  be in writing signed by the proposer and seconded in writing by another
member of the Syndicate called the seconder in the prescribed form. Similarly every nomination by a member of the Senate
shall by in writing signed by the proposer and seconded in writing by another member of the Senate called the Seconder in
the prescribed form. The prescribed form may be obtained from the Registrar on a written requisition signed by any member
of the Syndicate and the Senate. No nomination shall be valid unless it contain the signatures of both the proposer and the
seconder and the nomination shall also be signed by the person proposed expressing his consent to be nominated.
Every nomination must be presented before the Registrar either by the proposer or the seconder or the  person proposed
before 3 p.m. on Thursday the 17th February 2011.
The Scrutiny of nomination will take place at 3.30 p.m. on the 17th February 2011 at the office of the Registrar,
Tamil University, Thanjavur. The proposer or the seconder or the proposed nominee are entitled to be present at the time of
scrutiny.
