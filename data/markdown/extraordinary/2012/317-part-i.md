No. I/PUSP/36(a)/2012.
The following Notification of the Government of India, Ministry of Law and Justice, Department of Justice, Jaisalmer House,
26, Man Singh Road, New Delhi-110 011, dated 7th November, 2012 is republished:—
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (1) of Article 217 of  the Constitution of India, the President is pleased to
appoint (i) Kumari Justice R. Mala, (ii) Smt. Justice Aruna  Jagadeesan and (iii) Shri  Justice  G.M. Akbar Ali, Additional
Judges of  Madras High Court,  to be  Judges of the Madras High Court, in that order of seniority, with effect from the date
they  assumes charge of their respective offices.
