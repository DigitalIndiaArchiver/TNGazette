Order:- No. 432, Home(SC), 27th October 2020
The Tamil Nadu Chief Minister's Police Medal for 
Outstanding Devotion to Duty (Anna Medal) on the Occasion 
of the Birthday of Dr. Anna on 15th September, 2011 was 
awarded to Thiru Pon.Balasubramanian, SSI(601) in the order 
second read above and notiﬁ ed in the Tamil Nadu Government 
Gazette third read above.
2. The Director General of Police/Head of Police Force, 
Tamil Nadu, Chennai in his letter fourth read above has informed 
that instead of Service Book of Thiru Pon.Balasubramanian, 
Special Sub-Inspector of Police (601) the Service Book Thiru 
Pon. Balasubramanian, Special Sub-Inspector of Police (383) 
S/o R. Ponupillai who retired on 30-06-2013 was veriﬁ ed, 
inadvertently and recommended that he had not been awarded 
any punishment.
3. The Government have examined the request of 
Director General of Police/Head of Police Force, Tamil Nadu, 
Chennai with reference to the rules governing the award of the 
Tamil Nadu Chief Minister's Police Medal for Outstanding 
Devotion to Duty (Anna Medal), and have decided to accept 
his request and to forfeit the aforesaid Medal awarded on 
the occasion of Dr. Anna's Birthday, 2011 to Thiru Pon. 
Balasubramanian, Special Sub-Inspector of Police (601) and 
order accordingly.
 
S.K. PRABAKAR,
 
Additional Chief Secretary to Government.
