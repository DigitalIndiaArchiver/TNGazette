In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 14 of the Tamil Nadu Tax on
Consumption or Sale of Electricity Act, 2003 (Tamil Nadu Act 12 of 2003), the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby exempts the Cancer Institute (WIA), Adyar,  Chennai-600 020 in respect of the
electricity tax payable under the said Act on the consumption of electricity for own use by the captive
Generating Plant.
