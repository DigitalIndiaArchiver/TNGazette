[G.O. Ms. No. 135, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (Election) 29th December 2017.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/1041(c-1)/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred by section 375-B of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu
Act V of 1920), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Municipal Administration and
Water Supply Department Notification No. II(2)/MAWS/687(c-1)/2016, published at pages 1 to 11 of Part II—section 2 of
the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, Extraordinary, dated the 24th October 2016, as subsequently amended.
2. This Notification shall come into force with effect on and from the 1st January 2018.
AMENDMENT
In the said Notification, for the expression “until the 31st day of December 2017”, the expression “until the 30th day of
June 2018” shall be substituted.
