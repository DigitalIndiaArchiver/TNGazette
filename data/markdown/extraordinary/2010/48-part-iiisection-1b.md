In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Jail Subordinate Service (Section 17 in Volume III of the
Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1970).
AMENDMENTS.
In the said Special Rules, in PART II, in BRANCH I – EXECUTIVE STAFF, in the ANNEXURE,—
(1) under “CLASS I – GENERAL SECTION”, in column (3), against the entry “Warders in Central District and Special Jails,
Special Sub-Jails and Petty Officers in Borstal Schools Grade-II” in column (1) thereof—
(i)
in item (b), for the expression “168 centimeters”, the expression “170 centimeters” shall be substituted;
(ii) in the proviso to item (b), for the expression “165 centimeters”, the expression “167 centimeters”
shall be substituted;
(2) under “CLASS II – WOMEN’S SECTION”, in column (3), against the entry “Women Warders Grade-II” in column (1)
thereof—
(i)
in item (b), for the expression “157 centimeters”, the expression “159 centimeters” shall be substituted; and
(ii)
in the proviso to item (b), for the expression “155 centimeters”, the expression “157 centimeters”
shall be substituted.
S. MALATHI,
Principal Secretary to Government.
