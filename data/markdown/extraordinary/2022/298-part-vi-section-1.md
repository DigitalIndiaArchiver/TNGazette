Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the Wet lands specified in the Schedule below measuring to an extent of 31446 Sq.mtrs., to the 
same, a little more or less needed Highways purpose, for forming Bypass Road for Valangaiman Town.
	
The plans of the lands are kept in the Office of the District Revenue Officer, Tiruvarur and may be inspected during 
office hours.
