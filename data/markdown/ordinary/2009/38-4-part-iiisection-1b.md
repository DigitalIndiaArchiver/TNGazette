HIGHER EDUCATION DEPARTMENT
Ad hoc Rules for the post of Carpenter, Grade II in the Technical Education Division under the control of the
Directorate of Technical Education in Tamil Nadu Engineering Subordinate Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 341, Higher Education (B1), 31st August 2009, Aavani 15, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. SRO B-52/2009.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following rules.
2.
The rules hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st January 1990.
RULES
The General and Special Rules applicable to the holders of the permanent posts in the Tamil Nadu Engineering
Subordinate Service shall apply to the holders of the temporary post of Carpenter, Grade II, sanctioned from time to time,
in the Technical Education Divisions under the control of the Director of Technical Education, Tamil Nadu, subject to the
modifications specified in the following rules:—
2.
Constitution.—The post shall constitute a separate category under a distinct class in the said service.
3.
Appointment—Appointment to the post shall be made by direct recruitment.
4.
Appointing authority.—The appointing authority for the post shall be the Executive Engineer.
5.
Qualification.—(a) Age.—No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post by direct recruitment if he has
completed or will complete 26 years of age on the first day of July of the year in which the selection for appointment
is made:
Provided that the age limit shall be increased by 5 years in the case of candidates belonging to the Scheduled Castes
or Scheduled Tribes.
(b) Other qualifications.—No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post unless he possesses the practical
experience as Carpenter for not less than two years.
6.
Probation.—Every person appointed to the post shall  from the date on which he joins duty, be on probation for
a total period of two years on duty within a continous period of three years.
7.
Unit of appointment and discharge.—For the purpose of appointment and discharge for want of vacancies Technical
Education Circle shall be treated as a single unit.
8.
Scale of Pay.—There shall be paid to the holder of the post a monthly pay calculated in the scale of
Rs. 825-15-900-20-1200.
K. GANESAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.

Amendment to Ad hoc Rules for the post of Orthoptist in the Tamil Nadu Medical Subordinate Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 252, Health and Family Welfare (C2), 18th August 2009, Aavani 2, Thiruvalluvar Andu-2040.]
No. SRO B-54/2009.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to Health and Family Planning Department
Notification No. SRO B-51/2007 at page 108 in Part III—Section 1(b) of Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the
26th September 2007.
2.
The amendment hereby made shall come into force with effect on and from the date of issue of this order.
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules in rule 5 for sub-rule (b), the following sub-rule shall be substituted, namely:-
“(b) Other qualification.—No person shall be eliglible for appointment to the post unless he possesses the following
qualification, namely:—
(i)
Must have passed the Tamil Nadu Higher Secondary Board Examination with Physics and
Mathematics subjects;
(ii) Must possess a Diploma in Optometry awarded by the Regional Institute of Ophthalmology and Government
Ophthalmic Hospital, Chennai-600 008.
“Provided that preference shall be given to the candidates trained in Paediatric Ophthalmology”.
V.K. SUBBURAJ,
Principal Secretary to Government.
