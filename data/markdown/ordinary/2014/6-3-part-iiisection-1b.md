FINANCE DEPARTMENT
Amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Local Fund Audit Subordinate Sevice.
[G. O. Ms. No. ,16, Finance (Local Fund), 20th January 2014, Thai 7, Vijaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2045.]
No. SRO B-12/2014.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Local Fund Audit
Subordinate Service.
2. This amendment shall be deemed to have come into force with effect from 26th December 1990.
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in the rule 9, in sub-rule (1), in item (2), for the expression “The District Office Manual”, the
expression, “The Tamil Nadu Government Office Manual” shall be substituted.
K. SHANMUGAM,
Principal Secretary to Government.

No. SRO B-13/2014.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Handlooms and Textiles
Subordinate Service (Section 22 in Part-III-B of the Special Rules in Volume-III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1970).
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 26th December 1990.
AMENDMENT
In the said Special Rules for the expression “District Office Manual Test” wherever it occurs, the expression
“Tamil Nadu Government Office Manual Test” shall be substituted.
HARMANDER SINGH,
Principal Secretary to Government.
