Declaration of Villupuram Medical College, Villupuram to

Tamil Nadu Anatomy Act, 1951.

7th January 2011, Marghazhi 23, Thiruvalluvar
Aandu-2041.]
No. II(2)/HF/46/2011.—Under clause (e) of Section 2 of
the Tamil Nadu Anatomy Act, 1951 (Tamil Nadu Act XVIII
of 1951), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby declares the
Government Villupuram Medical College, Villupuram, to be
a Teaching Medical Institution for the purposes of that Act.

Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001
(Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu
Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34/2002), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands specified
in the schedule below measuring 1312.5 Square metres to
the same, a little more or less required for the purpose
of Widening and Strengthening of Chennai—Ennore Road
at Thiruvottriyur of Ward—H, Block—10, 24 and  Ward—‘I’
Block-1.
The plan of the land under acquisition is kept in the
Office of the Special Deputy Collector (Land Acquisition),
Tamil Nadu Urban Development Project-III, Chennai at
Poonamallee and may be inspected at any time during
office hours.

RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND PANCHAYAT RAJ DEPARTMENT.
Disqualification of certain Ward Member of Pavattakudi Village Panchayat, Nannilam Panchayat Union,
Thiruvarur District under Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act.
[G.O.(D) No. 21, Rural Development and Panchayat Raj (P.R.IV), 14th January 2011.]
No. II(2)/RDPR/55/2011.—Whereas, the opinion of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission has been sought for,
regarding the disqualification of Thiru A. Shiek Allaudin, 3rd Ward Member, Pavattakudi Village Panchayat, Nannilam
Panchayat Union, Thiruvarur District as required under sub-section (2) of Section 41 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994
(Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994) as he has absented himself from the three consecutive meetings of the said Village Panchayat.
And, Whereas the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission, after conducting the enquiry, has stated that a Notice
No. Na.Ka. 1417/2010/1ê2, dated 17-06-2010 sent to Thiru A. Shiek Allaudin, 3rd Ward Member, Pavattakudi Village
Panchayat, Nannilam Panchayat Union, Thiruvarur District by the Commission was returned with the postal endorsement as
“Left India - Returned to sender”. Besides, Thiru R. Thyagarajan, Village Administrative Officer, Pavattakudi has given a written
statement during the enquiry that Thiru A.Shiek Allaudin, 3rd Ward Member, Pavattakudi Village Panchayat has gone of foreign
with regard to employment. The said Commission has also stated that on verification of the attendance register and records
of Pavattakudi Village Panchayat, it has been confirmed that, Thiru A.Shiek Allaudin, 3rd Ward Member  of the said Village
Panchayat has not attended more than three consecutive meetings of the said Village Panchayat held on or after 20th March,
2008 and thereby opined that the said Thiru A. Shiek Allaudin, 3rd Ward Member, Pavattakudi Village Panchayat, Nannilam
Panchayat Union, Thiruvarur District is disqualified to continue as Ward Member of the said Pavattakudi Village Panchayat,
under clause (i) of sub-section (3) of Section 38 of the said Act;
Now, therefore, under sub-section (1) of Section 41 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby decides that Thiru A. Shiek Allaudin, 3rd Ward Member, Pavattakudi Village Panchayat,
Nannilam Panchayat Union, Thiruvarur District, has become disqualified from holding the office of 3rd Ward Member of the
said Pavattakudi Village Panchayat, under clause (i) of sub-section (3) Section 38 of the said Act as he has absented himself
from three consecutive meetings of the said Village Panchayat held on or after 20th March 2008.
