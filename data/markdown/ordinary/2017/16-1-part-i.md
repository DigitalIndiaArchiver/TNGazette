OBITUARY NOTICE
[G.O. Rt. No. 1284, Public (Special-B), 12th April 2017,
Panguni 30, Thunmugi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2048.]
No.I/PUSP/10/2017.—The Government of Tamil Nadu
record with deep and profound regret the untimely death of
one of its Senior Officers, Thiru S. Murugaia, I.A.S., State
Commissioner for the Welfare of Differently Abled, Chennai
on 11th April, 2017 at the age of 59 years.
2. He started his career as Probationary Deputy Collector,
Salem during 1982 and got conferment in the Indian
Administrative Service on 29-03-2004. He held various posts
in Government of Tamil Nadu which includes Revenue
Divisional Officer, Mayiladuthurai, District Supply Officer,
Dharmapuri, Deputy Secretary to Governor, Raj Bhavan,
District Revenue Officer Pudukkottai, District Collector,
Dindigul, Joint Transport Commissioner (Road Safety),
Chennai, Joint Commissioner (Enforcement/Admin),
Commercial Taxes, Joint Secretary (Protocol), Public
Department, Joint Commissioner, Revenue Administration,
Transport Commissioner, Director, ULC & UT, Managing
Director, SIDCO, Inspector General, Registration and State
Commissioner for the Welfare of Differently Abled, Chennai.
3. In the death of Thiru S. Murugaia, lAS, the Government
of Tamil Nadu have lost one of its senior officers. The
Government places on record its condolence to the bereaved
family members.
GIRIJA VAIDYANATHAN,
Chief Secretary.
