NOTIFICATIONS  BY  THE  ELECTION  COMMISSION  OF  INDIA
REMOVAL OF DISQUALIFICATION OF A CANDIDATE CONTESTED FROM 214.VASUDEVANALLUR (SC) ASSEMBLY
CONSTITUENCY IN THE GENERAL ELECTIONS TO THE TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY HELD IN MAY, 2006
UNDER SECTION 10-A OF THE REPRESENTATION OF THE PEOPLE ACT, 1951.
No. SRO G-16 /2009.
The following order of the Election Commission of India, Nirvachan Sadan,  Ashoka Road, New Delhi-110 001,
dated 4th April, 2009 [14 Chaitra, 1931 (Saka)] is published:—
Whereas, Shri D. Anand, resident of son of Daniel, 127, Karthik Cottage, SBI Colony, Courtallam Road,
Melagaram- 627 818, Tankasi Taluk, Tamil Nadu who had  contested the General Election to the Legislative Assembly of
Tamil Nadu held in 2004 from 214. Vasudevanallur (SC) Assembly Constituency, was disqualified by the Election Commission
of India, vide its Order No. 76/TN-LA/2007, dated 18th March, 2008 under Section 10A of the Representation of the
People Act, 1951, for failure to lodge his account of election expenses as required by the said Act and the Rules and Order
made thereunder; and
Whereas, the District Election Officer and District Collector, Tirunelveli District had intimated vide his letter
No. Roc. L3/23305/2006, dated   -12-2007 (date not mentioned, received in the Commission on 1-1-2008) that the candidate
had not filed his account of election expenses as on the date of reporting; and
Whereas, the above said candidate was disqualified by the Commission on the basis of the above mentioned report
of the District Election Officer and District Collector, Tirunelveli District for failure to lodge his accounts of election expenses
at all, vide its Order No. 76/TN-LA/2007, dated 18th March, 2008; and
Whereas, Shri D. Anand filed an application dated 3rd February, 2009 stating that after getting notice dated
28-2-2006  he had filed his Account of election expenses on 5-7-2007 with Special Tahsildar (Election), Tirunelveli. The
matter was referred to District Election Officer and Collector, Tirunelveli for his comments who in turn informed the
Commission vide his letter No. Reg/L3/216/1/2009, dated 31-3-2009 that when a report to Election Commission of India
was submitted vide letter No. L3/23305/2006, dated 28-12-2007, it was inadvertently omitted to report the fact that the
candidate had submitted his account on 5-7-2007. The report further mentioned that the candidate has maintained
day-to-day account in the register but he had not produced vouchers in support of expenses. In all he has shown an
expenditure of 1, 62, 642, will below the limit prescribed; and

Now, therefore, the taking into consideration the submission made by the District Election Officer and District
Collector, Tirunelveli District, the Commission in exercise of the power conferred by Section 11 of the Representation of
the People Act, 1951, has decided to remove the disqualification imposed upon the above said candidate Shri D. Anand
w.e.f. from 4th April, 2009 consequently the name of the said Shri D. Anand appearing at SI. No. 30 in the Commission’s
Order No. 76/TN-LA/2007, dated 18-3-2008, shall be deemed to have been omitted from the said order with effect
from 4th April, 2009.
