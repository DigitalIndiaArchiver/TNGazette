No. I/PUSP/3/2015.—The following Notification of the Government of India, Ministry
of Personnel, Public Grievances and Pensions, Department of Personnel and Training,
New Delhi, dated the 27th January, 2015 is republished:—
Smt. Mariam Farzhana Sadhiq, I.A.S., (TN) appointed to the Indian Administrative
Service on the basis of the Civil Services Examination held in 2007 and borne on the
cadre of Tamil Nadu has been permitted to change her name from
Smt. Mariam Farzhana Sadhiq to Mariam Pallavi Baldev. She will accordingly be
recognized as Mariam Pallavi Baldev for all official purposes.
