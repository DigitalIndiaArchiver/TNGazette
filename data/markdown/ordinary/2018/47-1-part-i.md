No.I/PUSP/32/2018.—The following Notification of the 
Government of India, Ministry  of Law and Justice (Department 
of Justice), Jaisalmer House, 26, Man Singh Road, 
New Delhi-110 011, dated  the 8th November, 2018 
is republished:—
ln exercise of the powers conferred by clause (1) of article 
222 of the Constitution of India, the President, after consultation 
with the Chief Justice of India, is pleased to transfer Shri Justice 
Huluvadi Gangadharappa Ramesh, Judge of the Madras High 
Court, as a Judge of the Madhya Pradesh High Court and to 
direct him to assume charge of his office in the Madhya Pradesh 
High Court on or before 22nd November, 2018.
