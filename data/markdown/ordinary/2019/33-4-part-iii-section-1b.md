Amendments to the Adhoc Rules relating to the Temporary Post of Librarian Grade-I in Public Libraries 
Department in Tamil Nadu Educational Subordinate Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 129, School Education  (PL1), 23rd July 2019, Aadi 7, Vikari, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2050.]
 No. SRO B-53/2019.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby makes the following amendments to the Adhoc Rules published with the School Education 
Department Notiﬁ cation No. SRO B-28/2006 in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Part-III-Section 1(b), dated the 
17th May 2006 at Pages 38 to 55.
2. The Amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 3rd October 2007.
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖගඛ.
In the said rule.—
(i) in rule 3, in the Table, under Class I, for the expression "who have completed the age of 55 years," in item (i) in 
column (2), against the category "Librarian Grade-I" in column (1), the expression "who have put minimum 3 years of service 
in the post of Librarian Grade-II," shall be substituted;
(ii) In rule 5, in sub-rule (b), in the Table, under Class I, for the entries in item (ii) in Column (3) against the category 
"Librarian Grade-I" in column (1) and the corresponding entry "by promotion from Non-Graduates," in column (2) thereof, the 
following entries shall be substituted, namely:-
"(ii) minimum 3 years of experience in the post of Librarian Grade-II"
