No. SRO B-1/2011.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor or Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu General
Subordinate Service Class XXXVIII (Section 13 in Volume-III of the Tamil Nadu Service Manual 1970) will be issued and
the amendment shall be deemed to have come into force on the 22nd January 1992.
AMENDMENT
In the said Special Rules, in Part-II, in Class XXXVIII, Divisional Accountants in Highways and Rural Works Department,
in rule-2,—after clause (i), the following clause shall be inserted, namely:—
(ii) by recruitment by transfer from the post of Superintendent in the Highways and Rural Works Department
in the Tamil Nadu Ministerial Service, with reference to the seniority in the category of Assistant in Tamil Nadu
Ministerial Service.
2. The existing clauses (ii) and (iii) shall be renumbered as clauses (iii) and (iv) respectively.
G. SANTHANAM,
Secretary to Government.
