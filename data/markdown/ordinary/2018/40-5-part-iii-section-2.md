JUDICIAL NOTIFICATIONS
 
Amendment to Appellate Side Rules, 1965.
(Roc. No. 41940-A/2016/F1)
No. SRO C-25/2018.
By virtue of powers conferred under clause 37 of the Letters Patent and of all other powers hereunto enabling, 
the High Court, Madras hereby makes the following amendments to Rules 1 and 2 of Order I of the Rules of the 
High Court, Madras Appellate Side, 1965. 
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖග 
I. 
In Order I Rule 1 sub-rule 6(a) of the Rules of the High Court, Madras Appellate Side, 1965, 
a sum of Rs. 25 lakhs shall be substituted for Rs.15 lakhs; 
II.  
In Order I Rule 2 sub-rule 5(c) of the Rules of the High Court, Madras Appellate Side, 1965, 
a sum of Rs. 25 lakhs shall be substituted for Rs.15 lakhs.
High Court, Madras, 
C. KUMARAPPAN,
14th September 2018. 
Registrar General.
