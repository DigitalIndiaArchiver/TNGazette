The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied
that the lands specified in the schedule below has to be
acquired for industrial purpose to wit,  for the Industrial
Purpose of setting up SIPCOT Industrial Complex and it
having already been decided that the entire amount of
compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid
out of the funds controlled or managed by the
Government, the following Notice is issued under
sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands specified in the
DTP—II-2 Ex. (178)
schedule below and measuring 1.54.0 hectares to the
same, a little more or less needed for formation of the
approach road from SIPCOT Industrial Complex in
Olaipatti Village, Uthangarai Taluk, Krishnagiri District.
The plans of the lands are kept in the Office of the
Revenue Divisional Office, Krishnagiri and may be
inspected at any time during office hours.
