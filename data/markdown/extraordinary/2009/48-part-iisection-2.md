NOTIFICATION REGARDING ENTRUSTMENT OF THE PROJECT ON APPLICATION OF GEOGRAPHICAL
INFORMATION SYSTEM (GIS) IN ELECTORAL ROLLS UPDATION, RATIONALIZATION OF POLLING
STATIONS IN THE DISTRICTS OF TAMIL NADU EXCEPT THIRUVALLUR DISTRICT TO
THE BHARATHIDASAN UNIVERSITY, TIRUCHIRAPPALLI UNDER THE TAMIL NADU TRANSPARENCY
IN TENDERS ACT, 1998.
[G.O. Ms. No. 73, Finance (Salaries), 23rd February 2009, Maasi 11, Thiruvalluvar Aandu–2040.]
No. II(2)/FIN/80(d)/2009.
Under clause (f) of Section 16 of the Tamil Nadu Transparency in Tenders Act, 1998 (Tamil Nadu Act 43
of 1998), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notifies the entrustment of the project on Geographical
Information System (GIS) by the Public (Elections-II) Department for electoral Rolls updation, rationalization
of Polling Stations as per the terms and conditions of the orders issued in Government Order Ms. No. 88,
Public (Elections-II) Department,  dated 22nd January 2009 in all the districts of Tamil Nadu except
Thiruvallur district to the Bharathidasan University, Tiruchirappalli.
K. GNANADESIKAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
