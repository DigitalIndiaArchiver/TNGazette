(Roc. No. 32697/2004/DP2.)
No. VI (1)/18/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (1) of Section 33 of Town and Country Planning Act, 1971

Notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette can represent in person or submit in writing to the Member-
Secretary, Melur Local Planning Authority any objections and suggestions relating thereto.

Planning Authority Office.

Adjournment of Civil and Sessions Courts in Tiruvarur District for Summer Holidays 2009
 and Holidays for all Subordinate Civil and Sessions Courts of Tiruvarur District for 2009.
(D.No. 1552/2008.)
No. VI(1)/24/2009.
In consonance with the instructions issued by HonвҖҷble High Court, Madras in its Official Memorandum
Roc.No.4295-A/2008/C3, dated 19th December 2008, it is hereby notified that the Civil and Sessions Courts in Tiruvarur
Districts will be closed for summer vacation during the year 2009 as hereunder and the following days are declared
as holidays for Subordinate Civil and Sessions Courts of Tiruvarur District during the year 2009.
(I)  SUMMER  VACATION 2009.
The  District and Sessions Court of Tiruvarur District at Tiruvarur, the Courts of Subordinate and Assistant Sessions
Judge, Tiruvarur  the Court of Subordinate and Assistant Sessions Judge of Mannargudi and District Minsif Courts of
Tiruvarur, Tiruthuraipoondi, Mannargudi and Nannilam will be closed from Friday  the 1st day of May 2009 to Sunday,
the 31st day of May 2009 (both days inclusive).
The Office of the Part-time Official Receiver, Tiruvarur will remain open throughout the Summer Vacation.
No plaints or petition except applications for grant of copies and for grant of copies and for service and execution
of process will be received during the above holidays.
The Office of the Copyist and Process Service Establishments will be kept open throughout the vacation, but
no arrest warrants will be executed during the period of adjournment.
The Office of the other Establishments shall remain closed. Arrangements shall  however be made for the following:вҖ”
1. For furnishing necessary records to the Copyist and Process Service Establishments for preparation of copies
and process respectively.
2. For transmitting records in Appeal and Sessions cases to High Court, Madras.
3. For all administrative matters.
The vacation Civil Judge appointed will attend to all urgent Civil Matters during Summer Vacation.
Urgent application for bail should be made to High Court of Judicature, Madras in the absence of the Sessions
Judge from Sessions Division during the above adjournment.
