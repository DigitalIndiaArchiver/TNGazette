NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
FINANCE DEPARTMENT
(Salaries)
ENTRUSTMENT OF THE PREPARATION OF THREE MASTER PLANS TO INSTITUTE OF REMOTE SENSING,
ANNA UNIVERSITY, CHENNAI UNDER THE TAMIL NADU TRANSPARENCY IN TENDERS ACT, 1998.
[G.O. Ms. No. 220, Finance (Salaries), 28th July 2010, Aadi 12, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No. II(2)/FIN/486(b-1)/2010.
Under clause (f) of Section 16 of the Tamil Nadu Transparency in Tenders Act, 1998 (Tamil Nadu Act 43 of 1998), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notifies the entrustment of the preparation of Three Master Plans for Mamallapuram Local
Planning Area, Ariyalur Local Planning Area and Sriperumbudur  New Town Development Area using Geographical
Information System (GIS) to the Institute of Remote Sensing, Anna University, Guindy, Chennai under IKONOS/Quick
Bird Method.
PROCUREMENT OF RICE FROM THE ANDHRA PRADESH STATE CIVIL SUPPLIES CORPORATION LTD. BY THE
TAMIL NADU CIVIL SUPPLIES CORPORATION FOR PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION SYSTEM UNDER TAMIL NADU
TRANSPARENCY IN TENDERS ACT, 1998.
[G.O. (Ms.) No. 221, Finance (Salaries), 28th July 2010, Aadi 12, Thiruvalluvar Aandu 2041.]
No. II(2)/FIN/486(b-2)/2010.
Under clause (f) of Section 16 of the Tamil Nadu Transparency in Tenders Act, 1998 (Tamil Nadu Act 43 of 1998), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notifies the procurement of 2.0 lakh tonnes fine variety of boiled rice from Andhra Pradesh
State Civil Supplies Corporation procured from the millers of Andhra Pradesh at the levy rate fixed by the Government of India,
by the Tamil Nadu Civil Supplies Corporation for the Public Distribution System (PDS).
K. SHANMUGAM,
Principal Secretary to Government.
