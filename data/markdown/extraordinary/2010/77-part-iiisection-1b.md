LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY SECRETARIAT
AMENDMENT TO THE TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY SECRETARIAT
SERVICE RULES.
(S.O. Ms. No. 32, Legislative Assembly Secretariat, 4th March 2010.)
No. SRO B-18(a)/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (3) of Article 187 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu, after consultation with the Speaker of the Tamil Nadu Legislative
Assembly hereby makes the following amendment to the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly
Secretariat Service Rules:—
2. The amendment hereby made shall come into force with effect on and from the date of
publication of the Notification.
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in rule 2, after clause (15), the following clause shall be added, namely:—
“(16) “Service” means the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Secretariat Service.”
M. SELVARAJ,
Secretary.
