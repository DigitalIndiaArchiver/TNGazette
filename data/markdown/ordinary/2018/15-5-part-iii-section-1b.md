No. SRO B-32/2018.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Article 318 of the Constitution of India, the Governor 
of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission  Regulations, 1954 
(in Volume-I of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1987).
2. The amendments hereby made shall come into force on the 8th March 2018.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Regulations, in regulation 7, in clause (3), in sub-clause (iii),-
 
(i) in item (a), for the expression "Rupees Nine thousand two hundred and forty’', the expression "Rupees Thirty 
thousand’’, shall be substituted;
 
(ii) in item (b), for the expression "Rupees Eight thousand and four hundred", the expression "Rupees Twenty four 
thousand", shall be substituted;
    S. SWARNA,
 
 
 
 
Secretary to Government.
