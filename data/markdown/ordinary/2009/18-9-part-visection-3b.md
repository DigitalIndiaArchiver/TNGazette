THE TAMIL NADU DR. M.G.R. MEDICAL UNIVERSITY, CHENNAI-600 032.
Result of Election held to the Senate of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai—Election
of One Professor by the Teachers of all affiliated Dental Colleges from among themselves under
Section 18(3)(b) of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai, Act, 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 37
of 1987).
[Rc. No. EI(2)/35983/2008.]
NO. VI-3(b)/9/2009.
Under Sub-Statute (1) of Statute 5 of Chapter XXI of the General Statutes of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical
University, Chennai, it is hereby published that Dr. S. Jayachandran, Professor and Head of the Department of Oral Medicine
and Radiology, Tamil Nadu Government Dental College and Hospital, Chennai-600 003 has been elected to the Senate of the
Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai by the Teachers of all affiliated Dental Colleges from among themselves
under clause (b) of sub-section (3) of Section 18 of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai, Act, 1987
(Tamil Nadu Act 37 of 1987) for a period of three years from 23rd March 2009 to 22nd March 2012 (or) so long as he holds
the post of Professor of the affiliated Dental Colleges of this University, whichever is earlier.
Chennai-600 032,
DR. SUDHA SESHAYYAN,
8th May 2009.
