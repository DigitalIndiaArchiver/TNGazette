Date of coming into force of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Second Amendment) Act, 2019  
(Tamil Nadu ACt 13 of 2019)
[G.O. Ms. No. 34, Rural Development and Panchayat Raj (PR-I), 28th February 2019.]
No. II(2)/RDPR/205(g)/2019.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats 
(Second Amendment) Act, 2019 (Tamil Nadu Act 13 of 2019), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints 
the 1st March 2019, as the date on which the said Act shall come into force.
	
HANS RAJ VERMA,
	
Additional Chief Secretary to Government.
