(S.O.No. 82/2022/TNSEC/ME-2, dated, the 24th November 2022)

In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (1) of section 43-B and section 27 of the Tamil Nadu District 
Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920) and sub-rule (4) of rule 99 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, 
Municipalities and Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission hereby fixes 19.12.2022 
as the date for convening meetings to elect the Vice Chairman and Members of Taxation Appeals Committee, as the 
case may be, shown in the Annexure, in accordance with the following programme:-
