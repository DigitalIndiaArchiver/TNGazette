No. SRO A-67/2018.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 26 of the Payment of Wages Act, 1936 (Central Act 
4 of 1936), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Tamil Nadu Payment of Wages Rules, 
1937, the draft of the same having been previously published, as required by sub-section (5) of Section 26 of the said Act.
Draft Amendment.
In the said Rules, in the proviso to rule 18, after the expression "the Tamil Nadu Factories Rules, 1950", the expression, 
"or Combined Annual Return in Form XXV under Schedule XIII as required in rule 242 of the Tamil Nadu Building and other 
Construction Workers (Regulation of Employment and Conditions of Service) Rules, 2006" shall be inserted.
