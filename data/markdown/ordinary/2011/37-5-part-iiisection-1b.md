HOME DEPARTMENT
Amendments to the Tamil Nadu State Judicial Service (Cadre and Recruitment) Rules, 2007.
[G.O. Ms. No. 504, Home (Courts-I) 13th September 2011.]
No. SRO B-33/2011.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Articles 233, 233-A, 234 and 235 and the proviso to Article 309
of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu in consultation with the High Court, Madras, hereby makes the following
amendments to the Tamil Nadu State Judicial Service (Cadre and Recruitment) Rules, 2007:—
AMENDMENTS
In the said  Rules, in the Schedule to rule 5, in column (3) against the entry "Civil Judge" in column (1),—
(1) in clause (3), the last paragraph beginning with the expression "Rules 12 (d) (ii), 52 and 53 of the General Rules and
ending with the expression “and Assistant Public Prosecutors” shall be omitted; and
(2) after clause (3), as so amended the following clause and explanation shall be added, namely :—
"(4) Must be a Law Graduate, working in a Group A, B or C Post in any court of law in Tamil Nadu or an equivalent post
(i.e.) Grade I, II or III post in any court, of law in the Union Territory of Puducherry (who are otherwise eligible) subject to the
condition that such Law Degree is recognized by the Bar Council of India. If the candidate has joined the Law Degree course
after entering the service, he must have obtained prior permission from the concerned Head of Office before Joining the such
Law course. The candidate must have put in three years of service after acquiring the Law degree.
(and)
Age: Must not have attained the age of 40 years in case of reserved categories and 35 years in case of others as on
1st July of the year in which selection for appointment is made.
Explanation.—Rules 12 (d)  (ii) 52 and 53 of the General Rules for the Tamil Nadu State  and Subordinate Services shall
not apply in the recruitment of fresh law graduates and service candidates. The said rules shall, however, apply in the recruitment
of practicing advocates or Pleaders and Assistant Public Prosecutor.’’
RAMESHRAM MISHRA,
Principal Secretary to Government.
RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND PANCHAYAT RAJ DEPARTMENT
Amendment to Ad hoc Rules relating to Temporary post of Assistant Engineer in the Rural Development and
Panchayat Raj Department.
[G.O. Ms. No. 53, Rural Development and Panchayat Raj (E3), 6th September 2011.]
No. SRO B-34/2011.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the rules published with the Rural Development Department Notification
No. SRO B-63/2000 at pages 75 to 77 of Part III—Section 1(b) of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 24th May 2000.
2. The amendment hereby made shall come into force on the 6th September 2011.
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in rule 5, in THE TABLE under sub-rule (b), in column (2), for item (2) against the entries “(b) Recruitment
by transfer" in column (1) thereof, the following item shall be substituted, namely:—
“(2) Must have rendered service for a total period of not less than five years on duty as Overseer or Junior Draughting Officer
either separately or put together in both posts.”.
N.S. PALANIAPPAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
