No. SRO B-40/2011.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Sections 8 and 10 of the Tamil Nadu District Police
Act, 1859 (Central Act XXIV of 1859) and Sections 9 and 11 of the Chennai City Police Act, 1888 (Tamil Nadu Act III of 1888)
read with the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India and all other powers hereunto enabling, the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Police Subordinate Service
(Section 34 in Volume-III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1986).
AMENDMENT
In the said  Special Rules, in rule 11, after the expression “by Tamil Nadu Uniformed Services Recruitment Board”,
the expression “on statewide basis” shall be added.
RAMESHRAM MISHRA,
  Principal Secretary to Government.
