MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION AND WATER SUPPLY DEPARTMENT
Amendment to the Tamil Nadu Municipal Corporations General Service Rules, 1996.
[G.O. Ms. No. 40, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (MC-4), 24th February 2010,
Maasi 12, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No. SRO A-10/2010.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 106 of the Madurai City Municipal Corporation
Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 15 of 1971), Section 108 of the Coimbatore City Municipal Corporation  Act, 1981 (Tamil Nadu
Act 25 of 1981), Section 8 of the Tiruchirappalli City Municipal Corporation Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 1994),
Section 8 of the Tirunelveli City Municipal Corporation Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 28 of 1994), Section 8 of the Salem
City Municipal Corporation Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 29 of 1994), Section 8 of the Tiruppur City Municipal Corporation
Act, 2008 (Tamil Nadu Act 7 of 2008), Section 8 of the Erode City Municipal Corporation Act, 2008 (Tamil Nadu Act
8 of 2008), Section 8 of the Vellore City Municipal Corporation Act, 2008 (Tamil Nadu Act 26 of 2008), and Section 8
of the Thoothukudi City Municipal Corporation Act, 2008 (Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 2008), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Municipal Corporations General Service
Rules, 1996 (Part III of the Tamil Nadu Municipal Corporation Service Rules, 1996):—
AMENDMENT
In the said Special Rules, in rule 3, in THE TABLE, in column (2), against the post “Assistant Commissioner
(Ward Office)” in column (1) thereof, after entry (iii) the following entry shall be added namely:—
“(iv) by deputation from the holders of the post of Deputy Collector in Tamil Nadu Civil Service”.
NIRANJAN MARDI,
Secretary to Government.
