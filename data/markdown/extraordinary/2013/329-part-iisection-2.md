COMMERCIAL TAXES  AND REGISTRATION DEPARTMENT
DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF THE TAMIL NADU VALUE ADDED TAX
(FIFTH AMENDMENT) ACT, 2013.
[G.O. Ms. No. 139, Commercial Taxes and Registration (BI), 8th November 2013,
Aippasi 22, Vijaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2044.]
No. II(2)/CTR/850(e)/2013.
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (a) of sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Value Added
Tax (Fifth Amendment) Act, 2013 [Tamil Nadu Act 28 of 2013], the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the
11th day of November 2013 as the date on which Section 2 of the said Act shall come into force.
S.K. PRABAKAR,
Principal Secretary to Government.
