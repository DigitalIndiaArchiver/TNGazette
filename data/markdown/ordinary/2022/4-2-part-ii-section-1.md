Based on the announcement made by the then Hon’ble Chief Minister on 09.02.2020, a bill was passed on 19.02.2020 
in the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly and the Government of Tamil Nadu has enacted "Tamil Nadu Protected Agricultural 
Zone Development Act, 2020” and published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette on 21.2.2020 by converting Thanjavur, 
Nagapattinam (Mayiladuthurai is now announced as District), Thiruvarur districts and Aranthangi, Avudaiyarkoil, Karambakudi, 
Manamelkudi and Tiruvarangulam blocks of Pudukottai and Kattumannarkoil, Melbhuvanagiri, Keerapalayam, Parangipettai 
and Kumaratchi blocks of Cuddalore Districts as Protected Agricultural Zone.  .

3. Subsequently, in the Government order second read above, the Government framed “Tamil Nadu Protected Agricultural 
Zone Development Rules, 2020” and the same was published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette (TNGG) on 26.8.2020 
vide reference third read above. Under section 8 of the TANPAZD Act, 2020 and rule 4 of TANPAZD Rules, 2020, the Authority 
has been empowered to carry out various functions like fostering infrastructure for Food Security, Policies for protection and 
development, steps to Increase agricultural production and productivity, promotion of agro based and allied lndustries in the 
Tamil Nadu Protected Agriculture Zone besides suggesting measures for preserving agricultural land for farming, allied activities 
for providing stability to the farming economy, etc., for overall development of this zone.
4. Consequently, in the Government Order fourth read above, the Government have nominated the following members 
under section 5(1)(xxii) among others, of the TANPAZD Authority constituted by the Government, and it was also notiﬁ ed in
