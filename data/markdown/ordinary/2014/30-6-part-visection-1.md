Grant of No Objection Certificate
(Rc. No. E2(1)/267/14829/2014.)
FORM-A2
(Notice under rule 35A(4) of Tamil Nadu Cinema (Regulation) Rule, 1957)
No. VI (1)/260/2014.
Whereas Tr. B. Raghavendra Reddy, Managing Partner,  BSR Builders Engineers and Contractors,
BSR Janus 2nd Floor, No.28 Tank Road, Chennai-34 has applied for No Objection Certificate for construction of the permanent
Multiplex Building with (8 Screen) Theatre and Shopping Mall at Door No's 1/609 Post Office Road, Chennai in
Survey Nos.  69/5 A-1, 69/6A1, 69/5A 2 part and 69/6 A2 part of Okiyam, Thoraipakkam Village, Tambaram Taluk, Kancheepuram
District.
Notice is hereby given that any person having any objection to the grant of “No Objection Certificate” above said site on the
grounds of non-compliance by the applicant with any of the provisions of the Tamil Nadu Cinemas (Regulation)
Act, 1955 (T.N.Act IX of 1955) or withy any of the Rules there under should file his objection in writing with the Licensing  Authority
(Commissioner of Police, Greater Chennai) within 15 days of publication of this Notice.
1. Any objection filed after the period shall be liable to be summarily rejected.
