No.I/PUSP/4/2018.—The following Notification of the 
Government of India, Ministry of Law and Justice (Department 
of Justice) Jaisalmer House, 26, Man Singh Road, 
New Delhi-110 011, dated 2nd January, 2018 is republished:—
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (1) of 
Article 222 of the Constitution of India, the President, after 
consultation with the Chief Justice of India, is pleased to 
transfer Shri Justice Rajiv Shakdher, Judge of the Madras 
High Court, as a Judge of the Delhi High Court and to direct 
him to assume charge of his ofﬁ ce in the Delhi High Court 
on or before 16th January, 2018.
