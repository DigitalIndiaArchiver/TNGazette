under the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning Act.
[G.O. Ms. No. 237, Housing and Urban Development [(UD 4(2)],
1st November 2010.]
No. II(2)/HOU/700/2010.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 30 of the Tamil Nadu
Town and Country Planning Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of
1972), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby publishes the
approval of the Government under Section 28 of the said Act
for the modified master plan for the Karur Local Planning
Area submitted by the Director of Town and Country Planning.
The approved master plan for the Karur local planning
area with all its enclosures shall be kept open to the inspection
of the public in the office of the Karur Municipality
during office hours.
ASHOK DONGRE,
Secretary to Government.
