GENERAL  NOTIFICATIONS
Final Closing and Cancellation of Registration of certain Co-operative Societies.
XN. 846. Perambur Corporation Girls Higher Secondary School Student Co-operative Store.
(R.C. No. 391/1999/Liqui.).
No. VI(1)/241/2014.
In the Circumstances stated by the Co-operative Sub-Registrar (Prosecution and Liquidation) in his final closure report
dated 22-05-2014 as the affairs of the Perambur Corporation Girls Higher Secondary School Student Co-operative Store
XN. 846. have been complete by wound up and in exercise of the powers delegated under Section 140 (1) of the Tamil Nadu
Co-operative Societies Act 30 of 1983 the registration of the Perambur Corporation Girls Higher Secondary School Student
Co-operative Store XN. 846. is hereby cancelled and its affairs are finally closed with effect from 30-05-2014 in proceeding
R.C.No. 391/1999/Li, dated 30-05-2014 of the Deputy Registrar (Non Credit) Chennai.
XNC. 814. Perambur Corporation Boys Higher Secondary  School Student Co-operative Store.
(R.C. No. 391/1999/Liqui.).
No. VI(1)/242/2014.
In the Circumstances stated by the Co-operative Sub-Registrar (Prosecution and Liquidation) in his final closure report
dated 27-05-2014 as the affairs of the Perambur Corporation Boys Higher Secondary  School Student Co-operative Store
XNC. 814. have been complete by wound up and in exercise of the powers delegated under Section 140 (1) of the
Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act 30 of 1983 the registration of the Perambur Corporation Boys Higher Secondary
School Student Co-operative Store XNC. 814. is hereby cancelled and its affairs are finally closed with effect from 30-05-2014
in proceeding R.C.No. 391/1999/Li, dated 30-05-2014 of the Deputy Registrar (Non Credit) Chennai.
XNC. 868. Tanbosco Anbu Illam Wastepaper Picker Co-operative Society.
(R.C. No. 391/1999/Liqui.).
No. VI(1)/243/2014.
In the Circumstances stated by the Co-operative Sub-Registrar (Prosecution and Liquidation) in his final closure report
dated 27-05-2014 as the affairs of the Tanbosco Anbu Illam Wastepaper Picker Co-operative Society XNC. 868. have been
complete by wound up and in exercise of the powers delegated under Section 140 (1) of the Tamil Nadu Co-operative
Societies Act 30 of 1983 the registration of the Tanbosco Anbu Illam Wastepaper Picker Co-operative Society XNC. 868. is
hereby cancelled and its affairs are finally closed with effect from 30-05-2014 in proceeding R.C.No. 391/1999/Li,
dated 30-05-2014 of the Deputy Registrar (Non Credit) Chennai.
