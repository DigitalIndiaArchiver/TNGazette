Cancellation of Notification Relating to the Holdings of Lands of Tmt. Janakiammal, wife of Srinivasalu Naidu, Palani
Taluk, Dindigul District under the Act.
[G.O. Ms. No. 177, Revenue [LR.I(2)], 30th May 2014.]
No. II(1)/REV/19/2014.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 18-C of theTamil Nadu Land Reforms (Fixation of
Ceiling on Land) Act, 1961 (Tamil Nadu Act 58 of 1961) the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby cancels the
Revenue Department Notification No. II (1)/REV/III/93, published at page 1 of Part II—Section 1 (Supplement) of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 28th July 1993.
GAGANDEEP SINGH BEDI,
Secretary to Government.

INTRODUCTION OF AC 25 KV TRACTION
PROFORMA 10-02
"WARNING TO ROAD USERS"
No. II(1)/TDI/21/2014.—It is notified for information of the public that in connection with introduction of 25 kV ac electric traction
over the section of Newly laid BG UP line between Perani (excl.) and Tindivanam (incl.) Station from km/loc. 143/3 (ch.143/70.40)
to km/loc.124/15 (ch.124/432.60) in Chennai Division of Southern Railway, the height gauges have been erected at all the level
crossings with clear height of 4.67 m above road level with a view to prevent Loads of excessive height from coming into contact
or dangerous proximity to live traction wires.
Public are hereby notified to observe the height specified above for the purpose of loading vehicles and to see that the loads
carried in road vehicles do not infringe the height gauges under any circumstances.
The dangers of a Load Excessive Height are as follows:—
(i) Danger to the height gauge and consequent obstruction to the road as well as the Railway Line.
(ii) Danger to the materials or equipment carried or for the Vehicle itself.
(iii) Danger of fire and rise of life due to contact with or dangerous proximity to the conductors.
