WARRANT OF APPOINTMENT OF GOVERNOR
[G.O. Ms. No. 785, Public (Special-B), 31st August 2011, Aavani 14, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No. I/PUSP/20(a-1)/2011.
By virtue of the power vested in me by Article 155 of the Constitution of India, I Pratibha Devisingh Patil, President of India,
hereby appoint shri K. Rosaiah, to be the Governor of Tamil Nadu with effect from the date he assumes charge of this office.
Given at Rashtrapati Bhavan, New Delhi, this Twenty-fifth day of August in the year two thousand elevan
(3rd Bhadrapada, 1933 Saka), in the sixty-second year of the Republic of India.
PRATIBHA DEVISINGH PATIL,
President of India.
DEBENDRANATH SARANGI,
Chief Secretary.
PROCLAMATION OF ASSUMPTION OF OFFICE OF THE GOVERNOR
[G.O.Ms.No. 786, Public (Special-B), 31st August 2011, Aavani 14, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
PROCLAMATION
No. I/PUSP/20(a-2)/2011.
Whereas Thiru Surjit Singh Barnala, Governor of Tamil Nadu has on the 31st August, 2011 vacated the Office of the Governor
of Tamil Nadu and Whereas the President of India has appointed Thiru K. Rosaiah, as Governor of Tamil Nadu in the place of
the said Thiru Surjit Singh Barnala.
It is hereby notified that Thiru K. Rosaiah, has entered upon his Office on 31st August 2011.
DEBENDRANATH SARANGI,
Chief Secretary.
