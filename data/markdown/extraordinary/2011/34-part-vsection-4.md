ORDER OF THE ELECTION COMMISSION OF INDIA
DISQUALIFICATION OF A CANDIDATE CONTESTED FROM 33. THENI PARLIAMENTARY CONSTITUENCY IN THE GENERAL
ELECTIONS TO LOK SABHA HELD IN MAY, 2009 FOR FAILURE TO LODGE ACCOUNTS OF ELECTION EXPENSES
UNDER SECTION 10A OF THE REPRESENTATION OF THE PEOPLE ACT, 1951.
No. SRO G-3/2011.
The following order of the Election Commission of India, Nirvachan Sadan, Ashoka Road, New Delhi-110 001,
dated 6th December, 2010 [15 Agrahayana, 1932 (Saka)] is republished:—
No. 76/TN-HP/2009(18).—Whereas, the Election Commission of India is satisfied that Smt. Parvathi, A., a contesting
candidate at the  General Election to Lok Sabha, 2009 from 33-Theni Parliamentary Constituency has failed to lodge her account
of election expenses as required by the Representation of the People Act, 1951 and the Rules made there under;
And, whereas, the said candidate could not submit her representation / account of election expenditure till date under the
said rule;
And, whereas, in the supplementary report of the District Election Officer and Collector, Theni has stated that she has not yet
filed her account of election expenses;
Now, therefore, in pursuance of Section 10A of the said Act, the Election Commission hereby declares Smt. Parvathi, A.,
to be disqualified for being chosen as and for being a member of either house of Parliament or the Legislative Assembly or
Legislative Council of a State or Union Territory for a period of three years from the date of this order.
(By Order)
S.K. RUDOLA,
Secretary,
Election Commission of India.
