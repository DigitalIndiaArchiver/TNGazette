Holidays  for all the Criminal Courts in Thiruvarur District for the year 2021. 
(D.No. 22/2021.)
No.VI(1)/56/2021.
In Consonance with the instruction issued by The Hon'ble High Court, Madras in its Oﬃ  cial Memorandum in R.O.C. 
No. 66806-A/2020/C3, Dated : 27-11-2020 it is hereby notiﬁ ed that the Criminal Court only of Thiruvarur District the following 
days are declared as holidays for Criminal Courts of Thiruvarur District during the Year 2021. 
The Holidays for all the Subordinate Courts in the State of Tamil Nadu, for the year, 2021 are as follows:
(a)   All Sundays are Holidays for all the  Subordinate Courts including Family Courts  functioning in  the State of 
Tamil Nadu. 
(b) 
The following 10 Saturdays are working days for all the Subordinate Courts including Family Courts  functioning 
in the State of Tamil Nadu. 
 
23-01-2021, 06-03-2021, 17-04-2021, 05-06-2021, 03-07-2021, 07-08-2021, 
      04-09-2021, 23-10-2021, 20-11-2021 and 04-12-2021. 
(c)   Except the above Saturdays, all other Saturdays are holidays for all the Subordinate Courts, except Family 
Courts. 
(d)  All the Family Courts functioning in the State of Tamil Nadu shall work on 1st and 3rd Saturdays of every 
month except those Saturdays which are declared as Public Holidays.  All other Saturdays are declared as 
holidays, except 23-01-2021 and 23-10-2021 which are declared as working days.
