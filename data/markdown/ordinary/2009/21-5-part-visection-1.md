Grant of No Objection Certificate
[Rc. No. E2(1)/451/31308/2009.]
FORM - A2
[Notice under Rule 35 A(4) of Tamil Nadu Cinemas (Regulation) Rule, 1957]
No. VI(1)/168/2009.
Whereas, Thiru K. Bapaiah, Director, Messrs Anandram Developers (P) Ltd., No. 45 and 47, Arcot Road,
Saligramam, Chennai-93, son of Thiru Prakash Rao, aged 61, residing at New No. 22, Old No. 15, 3rd Main Road,
Seethammal Colony, Alwarpet, Chennai-18 has applied to the Commissioner of Police, Chennai City, Chennai-8 for the
grant of “No Objection Certificate” for the construction of permanent multiplex complex contains 11 Screens, at Door
Nos. 45 & 47, Arcot Road, Saligramam, Chennai, T.S. Nos. 8/2 to 8/30, 13, 14 and 15, Block No. 44, Egmore,
Nungambakkam Taluk, Saligramam Village, Chennai-93. belonging to Messrs Anandram Developers (P) Ltd., Chennai-93.
(1) Notice is hereby given that any person having any objection to the grant of “No Objection Certificate” above said
site on the grounds of non-compliance by the applicant with any of the provisions of the Tamil Nadu Cinemas
(Regulation) Act, 1955 (T.N. Act IX of 1955) or with any of the Rules thereunder should file his objection in writing with
the Licensing Authority (Commissioner of Police) within 15 days of Publication of this Notice.
(2) Any Objection filed after the Period shall be liable to be summarily rejected.
Chennai Police,
K. RADHAKRISHNAN,
Egmore, Chennai-8,
Commissioner of Police.
17th May 2009.
Winding up of the affairs of the Coconut and Allied Products Industrial Co-operative Service Society Ltd.,
No. Ind. 4, Kancheepuram District and appointment of Official Liquidator.
(Rc. No. 16647/ICH/2008.)
No. VI(1)/169/2009.
Under the powers conferred on the Registrar under Section 137 (2) (b) of Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act,
1983, the affairs of the Coconut and Allied Products Industrial Co-operative Society Ltd., No. Ind. 4, Kancheepuram, are
ordered to be wound up with immediate effect by the Industries Commissioner and Director of Industries and Commerce,
Chepauk, Chennai-600 005.
Under Section 138(1) of the same Act, the Assistant Director (I/c), Office of the General Manager, District Industries
Centre, Kancheepuram is appointed as the Official Liquidator for the purpose of liquidation.
G. SANTHANAM,
Chepauk, Chennai-600 005,
Industries Commissioner and
6th May 2009.
Director of Industries and Commerce /
Registrar of Industrial Co-operative Societies.
