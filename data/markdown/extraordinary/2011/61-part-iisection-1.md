AMENDMENT

(1) in the preamble, for the expression “examine the request”, the expression “legally examine the
request” shall be substituted; and
(2) in ‘the terms of reference’, for the expression “examine the request for”, the expression
“legally examine the request ralating to” shall be substituted.
