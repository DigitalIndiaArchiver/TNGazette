In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (3) of Section 15 of the Right to Information Act, 2005 (Central
Act 22 of 2005),  the   Governor  of  Tamil  Nadu hereby appoints Dr. V. Saroja,  Thiru Christopher Nelson, IPS., (Retd),

Tamil Nadu Information Commission, subject to the out come of the SLP No.9680 and 12830 of 2012 filed by
Thiru. C. Manoharan and Thiru.A. Arumuga Nainar.
5. The State Information Commissioner shall hold office for a term of five years from the date on which he enters
upon his office or the date on which he attains the age of sixty-five years, whichever is earlier.
