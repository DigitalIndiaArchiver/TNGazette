No. SRO A-33/2017.—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-sections (1) and (2) of Section 40 of the Motor

amendments to the Tamil Nadu Motor Transport Workers Rules, 1965, the draft of the same have been previously
published as required by the proviso to sub-section (1) of Section 40 of said Act.
2. This Notification shall come into force with effect on and from the date of its publication in the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
