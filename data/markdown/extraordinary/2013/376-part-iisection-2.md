No. II(2)/PWD/934(d)/2013.
Whereas it appears to the Government of Tamil Nadu that
the lands specified in the Schedule below and situated in
Valayankulam Village (Reach I) Madurai South Taluk, Madurai
District are needed for a public purpose, to wit, for the
extension of Nilaiyur Channel notice to that effect is hereby
given to all to whom it may concern in accordance
with provisions of Section 6 of the Land Acquisition Act,
1894 (Central Act I of 1894).
And, whereas, it has become necessary to acquire the
immediate possession of the lands specified in the Schedule
below, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that the lands
be acquired under the provision of sub-section (2) of the
Section 17 of the said Act.
Now, therefore in exercise of the powers conferred by
Section 6 of the said Act, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
authorizes the Revenue Divisional Officer, Madurai and his
staff and workmen to exercise the powers conferred by the
said Section.
Under sub-section (2) of Section 17 of the said Act, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that in view of the
urgency of the case, the provisions of Section 5A of the said
Act shall not apply to this case.
