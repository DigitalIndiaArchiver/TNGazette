The Governor of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that 
the lands specified in the Schedule below are required for 
Highways purpose to wit for upgrading  State Highways 
Road (SH-37) Oddanchatram – Dharapuram – Tiruppur 
Road  from KM 37/400 to  KM 126/200 in Ottanchatram, 
Thangachiyammapatti, Kapliyapatti, Ambilikai, Kariyampatti, 
Poosaripatti and Parithiyur Villages, Ottanchatram Taluk in 
Dindigul District and  Koluthupalayam, Varapalayam and 
Madathupalayam Villages, Darapuram Taluk in Tiruppur 
District and it having  already been decided that the entire 
amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands is 
to be paid out of the funds controlled and managed by 
Divisional Engineer (Highways)  Tamil Nadu Road Sector 
Project-II, Salem and after having considered  the cause 
shown by the owner or other persons having interest on 
[ 1 ]
such lands as the case may be, do hereby publish the 
following notice under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the 
Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the 
Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 
2002), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the dry 
lands specified in the Schedule below and measuring to an 
extent of 11195 Square Metres for purpose of upgrading 
the State Highways Road, (SH-37)  Oddanchatram – 
Dharapuram – Tiruppur Road from KM 37/400 to KM 126/200 
in Ottanchatram, Thangachiyammapatti, Kapliyapatti, 
Ambilikai, Kariyampatti, Poosaripatti and Parithiyur 
Villages, Ottanchatram Taluk in Dindigul District and 
Koluthupalayam, Varapalayam and  Madathupalayam 
Villages, Darapuram Taluk in Tiruppur District.
The plan of the lands under acquisition are kept in 
the office of the Special District Revenue Officer (Land 
Acquisition), Tamil Nadu Road  Sector Project-II, Salem 
and  Office of the Special Tahsildar (L.A), Tamil Nadu Road 
Sector  Project-II, Tiruppur and may be inspected at any 
time during office hours.
