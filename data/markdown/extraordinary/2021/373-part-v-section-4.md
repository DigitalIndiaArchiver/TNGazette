NOTIFICATIONS BY THE ELECTION COMMISSION OF INDIA 
APPOINTMENT OF DATES FOR VARIOUS STAGES AND FIXATION OF HOURS OF POLL FOR BYE-ELECTION TO 
COUNCIL OF STATES TO FILL UP THE VACANCY CAUSED DUE TO DEATH OF THIRU A. MOHAMMEDJAN 
VACANT FROM 23.03.2021.
No. SRO  G-30/2021.
The following Notification of the Election Commission of India, New Delhi, dated: 24th August, 2021 [Bhadrapada 02, 
1943 (Saka)] is republished:-
No.100/CS-TN/03/2021(1):- WHEREAS, the seat of Shri A. Mohammedjan, in the Council of States, elected by the 
elected members of the Legislative Assembly of Tamil Nadu, has become vacant on 23.03.2021 by the reason of his death, 
before the expiration of his term of office which was up to and including 24.07.2025; and
WHEREAS, a bye-election is to be held for the purpose of filling the vacancy so caused;
NOW, THEREFORE, in pursuance of Section 147, sub-section (1) of Section 39 and Section 56 of the Representation 
of the People Act, 1951 (43 of 1951), the Election Commission of India hereby-
(A)	 calls upon the elected members of the Legislative Assembly of Tamil Nadu to elect, before 15th September, 2021 
and in accordance with the provisions of the said Act and of the rules and orders made thereunder, a person for the purpose 
of filling the said vacancy in the Council of States;
(B)	 appoints, with respect to the said election;
(a)	 the 31st August, 2021 (Tuesday), as the last date for making nominations;
(b)	 the 01st September, 2021 (Wednesday), as the date for the scrutiny of the nominations;
(c)	 the 03rd September, 2021 (Friday), as the last date for the withdrawal of candidatures;
(d)	 the 13th September, 2021 (Monday), as the date on which a poll shall, if necessary, be taken; and 
(e)	 the 15th September, 2021 (Wednesday), as the date before which the election shall be completed; and
