LEGISLATIVE  ASSEMBLY  SECRETARIAT
DEATH OF HONOURABLE THIRU N. MARIAMPITCHAI, MEMBER OF THE TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY.
[Letter No.5727/2011-1,Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Secretariat (B-III), 25th May 2011,
Vaikasi 11, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No. II(1)/LAS/12(g-1)/2011.
"The Honourable Speaker Pro-tem has learnt with deep regret the death of Honourable Thiru N. Mariampitchai,
a Member of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly on 23rd May 2011".
VACATION OF SEAT IN TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY
[Letter No. 5727/2011-1,Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Secretariat (B-III), 25th May 2011,
Vaikasi 11, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No. II(1)/LAS/12(g-2)/2011.
"A vacancy has occurred among the Members of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly, consequent on the death of
Honourable Thiru N. Mariampitchai, a Member elected to the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly from the
140. Tiruchirappalli West Assembly Constituency in Tiruchirappalli District."
