Order: No. 239, Agriculture (AE2), 2nd November 2015.
In the Government Order first read above, the Government issued orders for the closure  of the Tamil Nadu Agro
Industries Development Corporation, with effect from the date of issue of order except Sunflower Oil Complex at Pochampalli.
2. In the Government Order second read above, orders have been issued to privatize the Sunflower Oil Complex at
Pochampalli in view of accumulated losses and low capacity utilization of Oil seed extraction. In the Government Order 3rd
read above, permission has been granted under Section 25(N) of the Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 to Tamil Nadu Agro
Industries Development Corporation to retrench 86 workmen of Sunflower Oil Complex, Pochampalli, Dharmapuri District
subject to payment of terminal benefits. Out of 86 employees, 72 employees have received their retrenchment compensation.
Based on the direction of the High Court of Madras, the case was transferred to Industrial Tribunal (I.D. No. 7) and disposed.
3. In view of the above, on based on the recommendations of Finance (BPE) Department, the Government after careful
examination, issue the following orders:-
(i)
“No Objection Certificate” is issued to Tamil Nadu Agro Industries Development Corporation Limited, for the
purpose of filing an application with Registrar of Companies for removal of the name of defunct company (viz.)
Tamil Nadu Agro Industries Development Corporation Limited, under Fast Track Exit mode defunct companies
under Section 560 of Companies Act, 1956, since the Corporation is ‘inoperative’ and not carrying over any
business activity for the last one year;
(ii)
The Director of Agricultural Marketing and Agri Business shall engage a practicing company secretary as a
‘Consultant’ duly following the procedures, who in turn will facilitate for filing necessary application to Registrar
of Companies for striking off the name of Tamil Nadu Agro Industries Development Corporation Limited under
Fast Track Exit mode, duly following the guidelines by Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Government of India,
New Delhi and under the relevant provisions of Companies Act 2013;
(iii)
The Director of Agricultural Marketing and Agri Business is directed to ensure and settle the charges said to be
pending against the concerned Banks, so that the process of getting the name of Tamil Nadu Agro Industries
Development Corporation Limited struck off from the Register of Registrar of Companies becomes easier;
(iv)
All the Court cases and the litigations pending against the Tamil Nadu Agro Industries Development Corporation
Limited shall be transferred to the Director of Agricultural Marketing and Agri Business for taking further
appropriate action;
(v)
All the assets and liabilities of Tamil Nadu Agro Industries Development Corporation Limited shall be transferred
to the Director of Agricultural Marketing and Agri Business, which is a unit of Agriculture Department, Government
of Tamil Nadu, for taking appropriate action;
