 Notice of Election
Notice of election of one Professor to the Senate of the Tamil Nadu Dr.M.G.R Medical University, Chennai by the 
Teachers of all Aﬃ  liated Nursing Colleges from among themselves under Section 18(3)(c) of the Tamil Nadu 
Dr. M.G.R Medical University, Chennai, Act 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 37 of 1987).
(R.C.No. EIII(1)/09067/2022)
No. VI-3(b)/35/2022.
 Notice is hereby given that an election by postal voting is to be held for electing one Professor to the Senate of the 
Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R Medical University, Chennai, as per clause (c) of sub-section (3) of Section 18 of The Tamil Nadu 
Dr. M.G.R Medical University, Chennai, Act 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 37 of 1987) by the Teachers of all Aﬃ  liated Nursing Colleges from 
among themselves. The term of membership in the regular vacancy will be for a period of three years from the date of election or 
upto the date on which he/she ceases to hold the post of Professor of an Aﬃ  liated Nursing Colleges, whichever is earlier.
