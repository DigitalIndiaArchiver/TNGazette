HIGHWAYS AND MINOR PORTS DEPARTMENT
ACQUISITION OF LANDS
[G.O. Ms. No. 4, Highways and Minor Ports (HF2),
8th January 2010, Margazhi 24, Thiruvalluvar
Aandu-2040.]
No. II(2)/HWMP/15(d)/2010.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied
that the lands specified in the schedule below have to
be acquired for Highways purpose to wit for widening of
Jayamkondam to Kumbakonam Road and it having
already been decided that the entire amount of
compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid
out of the lands controlled or managed by the Divisional
Engineer (Highways), Tamil Nadu Road Sector Project,
Kumbakonam after considering the cause shown by the
owner or other person having interest on such  land  as
the case may be the following notice is issued under
sub-section (i) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways
Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu
Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands
specified in the schedule below and measuring 0.00.59
hectares to the same, a little more or less needed for Highways
purpose, to wit for widening of Jayamkondam to Kumbakonam
Road.
The plan of the lands is kept in the Office of the Special
Tahsildar (Land .Acquisition), Tamil Nadu Road Sector Project,
Thanjavur, Thanjavur District and may be inspected at any time
during office hours.
SCHEDULE
Thanjavur District, Thiruvidaimarudur Taluk,
Veerakkan Village.
Manai, S.No. 145-6A, Land Owner/Person Interested Kasi
Thirumadam—0.00.09 hectare.
Dry, S.No.211-3A1B, Land Owner/Person Interested
Rodapsandees son of Fedaricsandees—0.00.50 hectare.
Total—0.00.59 hectare.
G. SANTHANAM,
Secretary to Government.
