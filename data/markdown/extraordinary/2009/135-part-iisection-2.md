BACKWARD  CLASSES,  MOST  BACKWARD  CLASSES  AND  MINORITIES
 WELFARE  DEPARTMENT
 APPOINTMENT OF THIRU KAVIKO ABDUL RAHMAN AS MEMBER OF TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD
UNDER THE WAKF ACT.
[G.O. (2D) No. 17, Backward Classes, Most Backward Classes and Minorities Welfare (T1),
1st June 2009.]
No. II(2)/BCMBCMW/261(b)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 21 of the Wakf Act, 1995 (Central Act 43 of 1995), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints Thiru Kaviko Abdul Rahman as a member of Tamil Nadu Wakf Board
in the place of Thiru Salahuddin Mohamed Ayub, who has resigned his office.
T.N. RAMANATHAN,
 Secretary to Government.
