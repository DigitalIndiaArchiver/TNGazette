In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (1) of Section 10-D and Section 67 of the Coimbatore City Municipal
Corporation Act, 1981 (Tamil Nadu Act 25 of 1981) read with Section 8 of the Tiruchirappalli City Municipal Corporation Act, 1994
(Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 1994) Section 27 and sub-Section (1) of Section 43-B of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920
(Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920) and Sub-rule (4) of rule 99 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities,
Municipalities and Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission hereby fixes 4-9-2009 as the
date for convening a meeting to elect Vice-Chairman, Member of Appointment Committee, Member of Works Committee, Member
of Taxation Appeal Committee as the case may be, shown in the Annexure in accordance with the following programme:—
1.
Last date for service of notice for the meetings of election of Chairman of Ward
Committee of Corporation, Chairman/Vice-Chairman/ Member of Appointment
Committee, Member of Taxation Appeal Committee
2.
Statutory interval
3.
Date for conduct of meeting for the election of Chairman of Ward Committee of
Corporation, Chairman/Vice-Chairman/Member of Appointment Committee, Member
of Taxation Appeal Committee
(By order of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission)
