PUBLIC DEPARTMENT

DECLARATION OF THURSDAY THE 24TH APRIL 2014 AS A PUBLIC HOLIDAY ON ACCOUNT OF POLL FOR
GENERAL ELECTIONS TO LOK SABHA, 2014 AND THE BYE-ELECTION TO TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE
ASSEMBLY FROM 28.ALANDUR ASSEMBLY CONSTITUENCY
[G.O.Ms. No. 236, Public (Miscellaneous), 29th March 2014, Panguni 15, Vijaya, Thiruvalluvar Andu 2045.]
No. II(1)/PUMIS/8(b)/2014.
Under the “Explanation” to Section 25 of the Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881 (Central Act XXVI of 1881) read with
the Notification of the Government of India, Ministry of Home Affairs No. 20-25-26 Public-I, dated the 8th June 1957,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby declares Thursday, the 24th April 2014,  the date on which the poll for the General
Elections to Lok Sabha, 2014 and the Bye-election to Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly from 28.Alandur Assembly
Constituency will take place in Tamil Nadu, to be a public holiday.
(By Order of the Governor)
SHEELA BALAKRISHNAN,
Chief Secretary.
