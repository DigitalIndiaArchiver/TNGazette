NOTIFICATIONS BY THE ELECTION COMMISSION OF INDIA.
NOTIFICATION OF ELECTION COMMISSION OF INDIA REGARDING APPOINTMENT OF
RETURNING OFFICER FOR THE PRE-DELIMITED ASSEMBLY CONSTITUENCY IN
THE STATE OF TAMIL NADU.
No. SRO G-48/2009.
The following Notification of the Election Commission of India, Nirvachan Sadan, Ashoka Road, New Delhi-110 001,
dated: 17th December 2009 [26 Agrahayana, 1931 (Saka)] is published:—
No. 434/TN/2009-(3).—In pursuance of the provisions of Section 21 of the Representation of the People Act, 1951
(43 of 1951), the Election Commission hereby designates in consultation with the Government of Tamil Nadu, the officer of
the Government specified in column (2) of the Table below as the Returning Officer of the pre-delimited Assembly Constituency
as specified in column (1) of the said table against such officer:—
The appointment of the Returning Officer in respect of 83. Pennagaram Assembly Constituency shall cease to be
effective on completion of process of election in the constituency concerned.

dated: 17th December 2009 [26 Agrahayana, 1931 (Saka)] is published:—
No. 434/TN/2009-(4).—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 22 of the Representation of the
People Act, 1951 (43 of 1951), the Election Commission hereby designates in consultation with the Government of
Tamil Nadu, the officers of the Government of Tamil Nadu specified in column (2) of the Table below as the
Assistant Returning Officers of the pre-delimited Assembly Constituency as specified in column (1) of the said table against
such officer:—
The appointment of the Assistant Returning Officers in respect of 83. Pennagaram Assembly Constituency shall cease
to be effective on completion of process of election in the constituency concerned.
