Amendment to Adhoc Rules for the Post of Deputy Director in the Department of Archaeology
[G.O. Ms. No. 194, Tourism,  Culture and Religious Endowments (MA2), 2nd August 2013,
Aadi-17, Vijaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2044.]
No. SRO B-29/2013.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the rules published with the Education Department
Notification No. SRO B-132/82, dated the 27th March 1982, at the pages 155 to 156 of part III-Section I(a) of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 21st April 1982, as subsequently amended:-
AMENDMENTS
In the said  Rules:—
(1)
In rule 3, for clause (ii), the following clause shall be substituted, namely:-
(ii)
"by promotion from persons holding the post of Registering Officer or Assistant Director or Assistant
Superintending Epigraphist in the Archaeology Department; or”.
(2)
In rule 4, in the Table under sub rule (b) for the entries in column (2) against the entry “1-A.  Promotion” in column
(1) thereof, the following entries shall be substituted, namely:-
“Must have served as Assistant Director or Registering Officer or Assistant Superintending Epigraphist for a period
of not less than seven years in the Archaeology Department”
R. KANNAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
