No. II(1)/AG/14/2019.—In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (d) of sub-section (1) of Section 9 read with 
Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) Act, 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 1989), the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby declares his intention that the marketing of the Agricultural produce, namely, maize in all forms, Cholam 
(Jowar) in all forms, Ragi in all forms and Cumbu (Bajra) in all forms shall cease to be regulated in the notified market areas 
of the following Market Committees, namely:-
1.	
Kancheepuram;
2. 	 Vellore;
3.	
Thiruvannamalai;
4.	
Cuddalore;
5.	
Villupuram;
6.	
Salem;
7.	
Namakkal;
8.	
Dharmapuri;
9.	
Coimbatore;
10.	 Erode;
11.	 Tirupppur;
12.	 Trichy;
13.	 Thanjavur;
14.	 Pudukkottai;
15.	 Madurai;
16.	 Theni;
17.	 Dindigul;
18.	 Tiruvarur;
19.	 Nagapattinam; and
20.	 Perambalur.
2.	
Any objection or suggestion which may be received by the Government of Tamil Nadu within a period of one month 
from the date of publication of this notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette will be considered by the Government.
3.	
Objection or suggestion, if any, should be addressed to the Agricultural Production Commissioner and Principal 
Secretary to Government, Agriculture Department, Secretariat, Chennai-600 009, through the Commissioner of Agricultural 
Marketing and Agriculture Business, Thiru. Vi.Ka. Industrial Estate, Guindy, Chennai-600 032.
	
GAGANDEEP SINGH BEDI,
	
	
	
	
Agricultural Production Commissioner and 		
	
	
	
	
Principal Secretary to Government.
