NOTIFICATIONS BY THE ELECTION COMMISSION OF INDIA
ATTESTATION OF DECLARATION IN FORM 13A FOR POSTAL BALLOT 
No. SRO G-11/2021.
The following Notiﬁ cations of the Election Commission of India, Nirvachan Sadan, Ashoka Road, New Delhi-110 001, 
dated. 3rd March, 2021[Phalguna 12, 1942 (Saka)] is published:
No. 3/4/2021/SDR/VOL.I.
Whereas, sub-rule (2) of Rule 24 of the Conduct of Elections Rules, 1961, speciﬁ es that the elector shall sign the 
declaration in Form 13A in the presence of, and have the signature attested by, a stipendiary magistrate or such other oﬃ  cer 
speciﬁ ed under the said sub-rule, as may be appropriate, to whom he is personally known to or to whose satisfaction he 
has been identiﬁ ed.
Whereas, sub-rule (2)(c) of Rule 24 speciﬁ es that in the case of a voter on election duty, any gazette oﬃ  cer (or the 
presiding oﬃ  cer of the polling station at which he is on election duty) shall attest the signature of the elector.
Whereas, sub-rule (2)(e) of Rule 24 further speciﬁ es that in any other case, such oﬃ  cer as may be notiﬁ ed in this behalf 
by the Election Commission shall attest the signature of elector.
Whereas, as informed by Chief Electoral Oﬃ  cer, Tamil Nadu, the Government of Tamil Nadu while abolishing the system 
of Classiﬁ cation of Government servants into Gazetted and Non-Gazetted Oﬃ  cers, has classiﬁ ed the Government servants 
into four Groups viz., Group A, Group B, Group C and Group D.
The Election Commission, in view of the abolition of classiﬁ cation of Gazetted and Non-Gazetted oﬃ  cers in the State 
of Tamil Nadu, hereby notiﬁ es that all the Group A and Group B oﬃ  cers in the state who have been permitted to attest 
the true copies of certiﬁ cated, testimonials, etc., shall function as the Oﬃ  cer for the purpose of attesting the declaration 
given by the elector on election duty in Form 13A, under sub-rule (2)(c) of Rule 24 of the Conduct of Election Rules,1961.
(By Order)
