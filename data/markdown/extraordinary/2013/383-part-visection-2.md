[ELECTIONS–ORDINARY  ELECTIONS, 2011–TIRUVALLUR  DISTRICT–CONTESTED  CANDIDATES–FAILURE  TO  LODGE
ELECTION EXPENSES–ORDERED TO DISQUALIFY FOR THE PERIOD OF THREE  YEARS  TO   CONTEST  LOCAL
BODY ELECTIONS–REPRESENTATIONS RECEIVED–ORDERS ISSUED WITHHOLDING THE DISQUALIFICATION ORDERS.]
(S.O.No.46/2013/TNSEC/PE-2.)
No. VI(2)/262(a)/2013.
WHEREAS, with regard to Tiruvallur District, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission issued orders in the references read
above, disqualifying the contested candidates those who have failed to lodge their election expenses accounts as required by or
under the relevant section of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 and of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994 and the
rules made thereunder, for a period of three years from the date of issue of orders for being elected as Chairman or Councillor
or Ward Member or President as the case may be.
WHEREAS, several written representations along with the acknowledgement receipts for submission of election expenses
accounts before the Officers concerned are being received from the candidates of Tiruvallur District who have been disqualified
vide orders read above.
WHEREAS, considering the Prima-Facie of the representations, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission has constituted a
Committee to inquire into this matter and report.
In the above context, in exercise of the powers conferred under Article 243-ZA and Article 243-K of the Constitution of India
and rule 139 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities, Municipalities and Corporations (Elections) Rules,
2006 and rule 143 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Elections) Rules, 1995, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission, in
anticipation of the inquiry report hereby orders to withhold the statutory orders read above until further orders.
(By Order of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission)
