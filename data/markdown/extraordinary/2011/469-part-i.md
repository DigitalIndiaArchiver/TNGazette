APPOINTMENT OF JUDGE FOR THE MADRAS HIGH COURT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 1225, Public (Special-A), 24th December 2011, Margazhi 8, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No. I/PUSP/25(b)/2011.
The following Notification of the Government of India, Ministry of Law and Justice, Department of Justice Jaisalmer House,
26, Man Singh Road, New Delhi-110 011, dated 23rd December, 2011 is republished:—
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (1) of Article 217 of the Constitution of India, the President is pleased
to appoint Shri Justice T. Raja, Additional Judge of Madras High Court, to be a Judge of the Madras High Court, with effect
from the date he assumes charge of his office.
