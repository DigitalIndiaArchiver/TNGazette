MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION AND WATER SUPPLY DEPARTMENT
APPOINTMENT OF CERTAIN OFFICIAL AS ENGINEERING DIRECTOR OF THE TAMIL NADU WATER
SUPPLY AND DRAINAGE BOARD UNDER THE TAMIL NADU WATER SUPPLY AND DRAINAGE
BOARD  ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 131, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (WS-3), 29th July 2009.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/375(a)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of  Section 4 read with Section 5 of the
Tamil Nadu Water Supply and Drainage Board Act, 1970 (Tamil Nadu Act 4 of 1971), the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby appoints Thiru P. Kumaresan, Chief Engineer, Hogenakkal Water Supply and Flourisis
Mitigation Project, Dharmapuri as the Engineering Director of the Tamil Nadu Water Supply and Drainage
Board, with effect on and from the 29th July 2009.
NIRANJAN  MARDI,
 Secretary to Government.
