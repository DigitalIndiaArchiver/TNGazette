HOME DEPARTMENT
Amendment to the Tamil Nadu Special Police Youth Brigade Rules, 2013.
[G.O. Ms. No. 209, Home (Pol. 14), 17th February 2016.]
No. SRO A-8/2016.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 12 of the Tamil Nadu Special Police
Youth Brigade Act, 2013 (Tamil Nadu Act 3 of 2013), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment
to the Tamil Nadu Special Police Youth Brigade Rules, 2013:-
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in rule 4, to sub-rule (4), the following proviso shall be added, namely:-
“Provided that all the physical measurements shall be rounded off to the nearest Centimeter or the
nearest 0.5 Centimeter, as the case may be”.
APURVA VARMA,
Principal Secretary to Government.
