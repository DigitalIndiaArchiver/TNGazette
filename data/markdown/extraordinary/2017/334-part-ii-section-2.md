APPOINTMENT OF MEMBERS IN THE EXISTING VACANCIES OF THE STATE HUMAN
RIGHTS COMMISSION UNDER THE PROTECTION OF HUMAN RIGHTS ACT.
[G.O. (Ms) No.900, Public (Human Rights) 17th October 2017, Hevilambi, Puratasi 31,
Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2048.]
No. II(2)/PUHR/858(d)/2017.
In exercise of powers conferred under Section 22 (1) of the Protection of Human Rights Act,
1993 (Central Act 10 of 1994) as amended by Central Act 43 of 2006, the Governor of Tamil Nadu
is pleased to appoint Thiru D. Jayachandran, District Judge (Retired) and
Dr. A. Chittaranjan Mohandoss, I.A.S., (Retired), as Members of the State Human Rights
Commission, in the existing vacancies for a period of five years or till they attain the age of
seventy years, whichever is earlier with effect from the date of assumption of charge.
GIRIJA VAIDYANATHAN,
Chief Secretary.
