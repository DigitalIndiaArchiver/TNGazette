Adhoc rules relating to temporary post of Joint Director (Training and Rescue Operations),
State Training Centre, Tambaram.
[G.O.Ms.No.68, Home (Police-xvii), 12th January 2012.]
No. SRO B-4/2012.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following rules:-
2. The rules hereby made shall  be deemed to have come into force with effect on and from the
11th day of September 2009.
RULES.
The General and the Special Rules applicable to the holders of the permanent post of Deputy Director of Fire Service in the
Tamil  Nadu Fire Service shall apply to the holder of the temporary post of  Joint Director (Training and Rescue Operations)
in the State Training  Centre, Tambaram, subject to the modifications specified in  the following rules:-
2. Constitution: The post shall constitute a separate  category in a distinct   class of the said service.
3. Appointment: Appointment to the post shall be made by Promotion from the holders  of the post of Deputy Director
of Fire Service in Class-II, Category 2 of the said service.
4. Qualification: Must have put in service for a period of not less than 5 years as Deputy Director of Fire Service.
5. Pay: There shall be paid to the holders of the post monthly pay  calculated at the rate of
Rs. 15,600-39,100+Grade Pay 7,600/-
RAMESHRAM MISHRA,
Principal Secretary to Government.
