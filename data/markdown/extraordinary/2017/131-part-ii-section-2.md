APPOINTMENT OF VACATION CIVIL JUDGES FOR THE SUMMER VACATION OF CIVIL COURTS FOR
THE YEAR 2017 UNDER THE TAMIL NADU CIVIL COURTS ACT
Amendment to Notification
[G.O. (D).No. 582, Home (Cts-III), 11th May 2017.]
No. II(2)/HO/385(a)/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 30 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act,
1873 (Central Act III of 1873), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, in consultation with the High Court, Madras,
hereby makes the following amendment to the Home Department Notification No. II(2)/HO/317(b)/2017
published in Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the
28th April, 2017, namely:—
AMENDMENT
In the said notification, in the Table, against Sl.No. 25, in column (2), for the existing entries, the following
entries shall be substituted, namely:—
i.
Principal District Judge, Tiruppur (for the first and second week of May, 2017)
ii.
I Additional District Judge, Tiruppur (for the rest of the Summer Vacation, 2017 i.e. the third and fourth
week of May, 2017).
NIRANJAN MARDI,
Additional Chief Secretary to Government.
