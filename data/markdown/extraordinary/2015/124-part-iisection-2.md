The Governor of Tamil Nadu having  been satisfied that the lands specified in the schedule below are required
under  TNRSP-II  for highways purpose to wit for upgrading  SH-04 Arcot to Villupuram  Road  at Km 29/800 to
110/200 and  113/200 KM to 114/600 KM Thellur, Vinnamangalam, Nesal & Velapadi Villages, Arani Taluk
Indiravanam, Gangai Soodamani, Kannanur, Polur Taluk, Thiruvannamalai District, Valathi, Ottampattu, Jayamkondan,
Kannalam, Devanur, Palapattu, Ponpathi, Kavarai, Chellapiratti, Cheettampoondi Villages, Gingee Taluk, Nemoor,
Poondi, Vembi, Narasinganur Villages, Vikkiravandi Taluk, Ganjanur, Gingee Pudur, Mandakapattu,  Arasalapuram,
Muttathur, Nagar, Villupuram Town and Villages, Villupuram Taluk, Villupuram District  and it having already been
decided that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid out of the funds
controlled and  managed by the Highways Department and considering the cause shown by the owner or other
persons having interest in the said  lands as the case may be do hereby publish the following notice is issued
under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001(Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the dry lands specified in the schedule below and measuring to an extent
of 1.01.10  hectare or 10110 sq.mt lands in specified in the schedule below a little more or less needed for
TNRSP-II  Highways purpose, to wit for upgrading the State Highways-04 Arcot to Villupuram Road at Km 29/800
to 110/200  k.m. and  113/200 to 114/600 k.m  in Indiravanam, Kannanur in Polur Taluk, Nemoor, Muttathur Villages
in Vikkiravandi Taluk, Mandakapattu, Arasalapuram Village in Villupuram Taluk and  Thellur & Vinnamangalam
Villages in Arani Taluk in Thiruvannmalai & Villupuram District.
The plan of the lands  under acquisition are kept in the office of the Special District Revenue Officer (Land
Acquisition), Tamil Nadu Road Sector Project –II, Chennai and office of the Special Tahsildar Tamil Nadu Road
Sector Project-II, Thiruvannamalai, may be inspected at any time during office hours.
