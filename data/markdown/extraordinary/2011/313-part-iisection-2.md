Whereas it appears to the Government of Tamil Nadu that
the Lands specified in the Schedule below and situated in the
Block 1 of Pathamadai Village in Adbasamudram Taluk in
Tirunelveli District are needed for a public purpose to wit for the
formation of a Flood Carrier Channel by interlinking Tamirabarani,
Karumeniaru and Namiyaru Rivers for the benefit of the dry
areas in Tirunelveli and Tuticorin Districts. Notice to that effect
is hereby given to all to whom it may concern in accordance
with the provisions of the sub-section (1) of Section 4 of the
Land Acquisition Act, 1894 (Central Act I of 1894).
And, whereas, it has become necessary to acquire immediate
possession of the lands specified in the schedule below, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that the lands be acquired
under the provision of sub-section (1) of Section 17 of the said
Act.
Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by
sub-section (2) of Section 4 of the said Act, the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby authorizes the Special Tahsildar
(Land Acquisition), Interlinking of River Project - Unit I,
Ambasamudram Taluk at Cheranmahadevi and his staff and
workmen to exercise the powers conferred by the said
sub-section.
Under sub-section (4) of Section 17 of the said Act, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that in view of urgency
of the case under clause (c) of Section 3 of the said Act, the
provisions of Section 5-A of the said Act shall not apply to this
case.
