 Result of Election of One Professor Held to the Senate of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai 
by the Teachers of All Aﬃ  liated Pharmacy Colleges from among themselves under Section 18(3)(d) of the 
Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai, Act, 1987. (Tamil Nadu Act 37 of 1987)
(R.C.No. EIII(4)/41451/2017) 
No.VI-3(b)/26/2018.
Under sub - statute (1)  of Statute 5 of Chapter XX1 of the General Statutes of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R 
Medical University, Chennai, it is hereby published that Dr. B. Sangameswaran, M.Pharm, Ph.D., Professor and Principal, 
S.S.M. College of Pharmacy, Jambai - 638 312, Bhavani (TK), Erode District has been elected unopposed as a member 
to the Senate of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R Medical University, Chennai under Section 18 (3) (d) of the Tamil Nadu 
Dr. M.G.R Medical University, Chennai Act, 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act, 37 of 1987) by the teachers of all aﬃ  liated Pharmacy 
Colleges from among themselves for a period of three years from 12-03-2018 to 11-03-2021 or the date on which he 
ceases to hold the post of Professor of Aﬃ  liated Pharmacy College of this University, whichever is earlier.
