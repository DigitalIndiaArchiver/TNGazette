No.I/PLAR/4/2017.—On completion of six years of tenure,
Thiru T. Kuppusamy, ceases to hold office as Member,
Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission on the afternoon of
22-02-2017 without prejudice to the criminal case filed on
13-10-2011 by the Director of Vigilance and Anti-Corruption
in Cr.No.22/2011 under Section 120(B) IPC, 13(2)  read with
13(1)(d), 15 of the Prevention of Corruption Act, 1988 and
Sections 175 & 186 IPC and without prejudice to the Writ
Petition filed by him in W.P.No.24518 of 2011, pending before
the Hon'ble High Court of Madras.
  GIRIJA VAIDYANATHAN,
Chief Secretary.

No.I/PUSP/5/2017.—The following Notification of the
Government of India, Ministry of Law and Justice (Department
of Justice), Jaisalmer House, 26, Man Singh Road,
New Delhi-110 011, dated 15th February, 2017 is
republished:—
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (2) of
Article 124 of the Constitution of India, the President is
pleased to appoint Shri Justice Sanjay Kishan Kaul,
Chief Justice of the Madras High Court, to be a Judge of the
Supreme Court of India with effect from the date he assumes
charge of his office.
