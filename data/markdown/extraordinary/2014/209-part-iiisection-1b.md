The following errata is issued to the Higher Education Notification No. SRO B-44/2009, published at
pages 80-103 of Part III—Section 1(b) of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 22nd July 2009:—
In the said Notification,—
(i) in page 86, in rule 4, for “by recruitment”, read “by direct recruitment”;
(ii) in page 99, in the Annexure, in class IV, in column (4) against Category 3 “Physical Training
Instructor” in column (2) thereof, after item (i), the following item shall be added, namely:—
“(ii) State Government Teachers’ Certificate in Physical Education awarded
to Ex-Servicemen (Higher Grade)”.

In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Technical
Educational Subordinate Service (Section 45, in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1970).
2. The amendments hereby made shall come into force on the date of publication of these rules.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules,—
(1) in rule 1, in the Tabular column,—
(a) against Class-II in column (1),  categories 1 and 2 in column (2) and the corresponding
entries in column (3) thereof, shall be omitted;
(b) against Class-III in column (1), category 1 in column (2) and the corresponding entries
in column (3) thereof, shall be omitted;
