No. SRO B-64/2019.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby makes the following rules:—
2. The rules hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 26th June 2012.
Rules.
The General and the Special Rules applicable to the holders of the permanent posts in the Tamil Nadu General Service 
shall apply to the holders of the temporary post of Joint Director (Administration) in the Survey and Land Records Department, 
Subject to the modifications specified in the following rules, namely:—
	 2. Constitution:- The post shall constitute a separate category in the said service.
	 3. Appointment:- Appointment to the post shall be made by promotion from among the holders of the post of Deputy 
Director (Administration) in the Survey and Land Records Department.
 4. Pay:- There shall be paid to the holder of the post, a monthly pay calculated in the Pay Band and Grade Pay of 
Rs.15600-39100 + Grade Pay 7600.
atulya misra,
Additional Chief Secretary to Government.

No. SRO B-65/2019.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Motor Vehicles 
Maintenance Subordinate Service (Section 39 of Part III-B in Volume-III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 2016).
2. (i) The amendment (1) hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force with effect on and from the 
21st September 1999.
	  (ii) The amendment (2) hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force with effect from the 15th September 
2016.
 Amendments.
In the said Special Rules.—
(1) for rule 5, the following rule shall be substituted, namely.- 
	
5. Rule of reservation. — ''The General Rule 22 of the Tamil Nadu State and subordinate Services Rules relating 
to reservation of appointment shall apply for appointment to all categories by the method of direct recruitment wherever the 
sanctioned strength is more than one in each category"; 
2) in rule 5, so amended for the expression ''The General Rule 22 of the Tamil Nadu State and Subrodinate Services 
Rules'' the following expressions shall be substituted, namely:- 
	
"Section 27 of the Tamil Nadu Government Servants (Conditions of Service) Act,2016''
