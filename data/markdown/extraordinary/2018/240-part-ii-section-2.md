The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that 
the lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below have to be acquired 
for industrial purpose to wit for the formation of New Broad 
Gauge Railway line between Tindivanam-Nagari and it having 
already been decided that the entire amount of compensation 
to be awarded for the lands are to be paid out of the lands 
controlled or managed by Government. The following notice 
is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil 
Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1)   of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu 
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu 
hereby acquires the lands speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below 
and measuring 13.24.16 hectares to the same, a little more or 
less needed for Industrial purposes, to wit, for the formation of 
New Broad Gauge Railway line between Tindivanam-Nagari. 
 The plan of the lands are kept  in the ofﬁ ce of the Tahsildar, 
Walajah Taluk and may be inspected at anytime during ofﬁ ce 
hours.
