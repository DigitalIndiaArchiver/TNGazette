PUBLIC (ELECTIONS) DEPARTMENT
NOTIFICATION CALLING UPON FOR 8. VELLORE PARLIAMENTARY CONSTITUENCY IN THE 
STATE OF TAMIL NADU TO ELECT MEMBER 
Secretariat, July 11, 2019.
No. SRO D-5/2019.
The following Notiﬁ cation of the Government of India, Ministry of  Law  and Justice (Legislative Department), 
New Delhi-110 001, dated 11th July, 2019 [20 Ashadha, 1941 (Saka)] is republished:-
S.O. (E).––Whereas it was decided to hold the general election for constituting a new House of the People, on the 
expiration of the term of Sixteenth House of the people;
And whereas in pursuance of the recommendation of the Election Commission, a notiﬁ cation number 1389 (E), 
dated 19th March, 2019 was issued by the  President calling upon the Parliamentary Constituencies in the State of 
Tamil Nadu to elect thirty nine Members which, inter-alia, included the 8-Vellore Parliamentary Constituency;
And whereas in pursuance of the recommendations of the Election Commission, a notiﬁ cation number 1609 (E), 
dated 16th April, 2019 was issued by the President partially rescinding the notification number 1389 (E), 
dated 19th March, 2019, in so far as it relates to calling upon the 8-Vellore Parliamentary Constituency;
And whereas the Election Commission of India vide its proceedings dated 4th July, 2019 recommended to hold election 
and issue of fresh notiﬁ cation calling election from 8-Vellore Parliamentary Constituency of Tamil Nadu;
Now, therefore, in pursuance of sub-section (2) of Section 14 of the Representation of the People Act, 1951 
(43 of 1951), the President is pleased to call upon the 8-Vellore Parliamentary Constituency in the State of Tamil Nadu to 
elect the Member in accordance with the provisions of the said Act and of the rules and orders made thereunder.
