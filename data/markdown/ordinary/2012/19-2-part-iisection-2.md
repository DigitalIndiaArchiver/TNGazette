[G.O (D) No. 68, Highways and Minor Ports (HP1),

No. II(2)/HWMP/252/2012.—The Governor of Tamil Nadu
having  satisfied that the lands specified in the schedule below
have to be acquired for Highways purpose for providing approach
road to the Kollidam  Bridge at Muttam Village at  Kattumannarkoil
Taluk in Cuddalore District and it  has already  been decided
that the  entire  amount  of compensation to be awarded for the
lands is to be paid out of the funds controlled and  managed  by
Divisional Engineer (H),  Projects, Cuddalore after  considering
the  cause shown by the owner or other person having interest
on such lands, as the  case may be, do hereby publish  the
following  notice  under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the
Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu
Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the dry lands specified in the
schedule below  measuring an extent of 04568 Sq. mts. to
the same, a little more or less required for providing approach
road to the Kollidam Bridge at Muttam Village at
Kattumannarkoil Taluk in Cuddalore District.
The plan of the land is kept in the Office of the Revenue
Divisional Officer, Chidambaram  and may be inspected at
any time during office hours.

Cuddalore District, Kattumannarkoil Taluk, Muttam Village.

No. II(2)/HWMP/253/2012.—The Governor of Tamil Nadu
having  satisfied that the lands specified in the Schedule below

entire amount of compensation  to be awarded for the lands is
to be  paid out of the funds controlled and managed by Divisional
Engineer (H), Projects, Cuddalore after considering cause shown
by the owner (or) other person having interest on such lands as
the case may be do hereby publish the notice under
sub-section (1) of Section 15 of Tamil Nadu Highways Act,
2001 (Tamil Nadu  Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE

Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the dry lands specified in
the Schedule below and measuring an extent of 0.20.5
hectare to the same,  a little more or less required, for
providing road over bridge in lieu of existing L.C.No.202 at
C.Kothangudi Village, Chidambaram Taluk  in Cuddalore
District.
The plan of the land is kept in the Office of the Revenue
Divisional Officer Chidambaram and may be inspected at
any time during office hours.
