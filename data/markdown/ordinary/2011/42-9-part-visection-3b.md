University, Chennai by the Senate from among its members.
[No. EIII(1)/32346/2011.]
No. VI-3(b)/30/2011.
Notice is hereby given that an election by direct polling is to be held in the University premises for electing Two members
to the Governing Council of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai, as per clause (a) of sub-section (4)
of Section 21 of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai, Act, 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 37 of 1987) by the
Senate from among its  members to be fill up the existing vacancies. The term of membership in the Governing Council
will be for a period of three years from the date of publication of the results of the election (i.e. 20-12-2011) or the date
on which he/she ceases to hold the post of Senate Member of this University whichever is earlier.
2. Forms of Nomination papers may be obtained from the Registrar (FAC), who is the  Returning Officer from
9th November 2011 to 25th November 2011 between 11.00 a.m. and 3.00 p.m. (on all working days.)
3. Nomination paper may be enclosed in an envelope addressed to the Registrar(FAC)/Returning Officer and
superscribed “Nomination for election to the Governing Council" as indicated below, and sent by Registered Post
(Acknowledgement Due), so as to be received by the Registrar(FAC)/Returning Officer not later than 4.00 p.m.
on 25th November 2011 or delivered to the Returning Officer during office hours in person or by his proposer or by
messenger under acknowledgement within the date and hour mentioned above.
