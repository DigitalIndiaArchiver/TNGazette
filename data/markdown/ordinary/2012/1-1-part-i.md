of Justice), Jaisalmer House, 26, Man Singh Road,

In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (1) of
Article 224 of the Constitution of India, the President is pleased

High Court, in that order of seniority, for a period of two years
with effect from the dates they assumes charge of their
respective offices.
