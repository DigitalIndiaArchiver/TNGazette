Nomination of Chairman for the HIgh Court Legal Services Committee
(TNSLSA No.3306/E/2013.)
No. VI(2)/150/2013.
In exercise of the powers conferred under sub section (2) of Section 8A of the Legal Services Authorities Act, 1987, (Central
Act 39 of 1987) the Hon’ble the Acting Chief Justice, High Court, Madras, is pleased to nominate the Hon’ble
Mr. Justice N. Paul Vasanthakumar, Judge, HIgh Court, Madras, to be the Chairman of the High Court Legal Services
Committee with effect from 15-7-2013.
High Court Compus,
K. ARUL,
Chennai - 600 104,
District Judge/ Member Secretary. (Fac),

LOSS OF CERTIFICATES
