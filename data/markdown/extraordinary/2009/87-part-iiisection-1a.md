HOME, PROHIBITION AND EXCISE DEPARTMENT
AMENDMENT TO THE TAMIL NADU LIQUOR RETAIL VENDING (IN SHOPS AND BARS) RULES, 2003.
[G.O. Ms. No. 13, Home, Prohibition and Excise (VI), 31st March 2009.]
No. SRO A-6(b)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Sections 17-C, 17-D, 17-E, 18-B, 18-C, 20 and 21 read with
Section 54 of the Tamil Nadu Prohibition Act, 1937 (Tamil Nadu Act X of 1937), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby makes the following amendment to the Tamil Nadu Liquor Retail Vending (in Shops and Bars)
Rules, 2003:—
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in rule 3, for sub-rule (2), the following sub-rule shall be substituted, namely:—
"(2) The application shall be accompanied by the treasury receipt in proof of the payment of annual
privilege fee of Rs. 1,00,00,000/- (Rupees one crore only), licence fee of Rs. 1,00,000/- (Rupees one lakh only)
and application fee of Rs. 1,000/- (Rupees one thousand only) for the grant of licence".
 .
S. MALATHI,
Principal Secretary to Government.
