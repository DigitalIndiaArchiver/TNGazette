In the G.O first read above, orders were issued freezing of Boundaries of Administrative Units viz., Municipalities, Revenue 
Villages, Towns, Tehsils, Police Stations, Sub-Divisions, Districts etc., and ban on formation of new Units in the State of 
Tamil Nadu from 1st January 2020 to 31st March 2020.

2. In the G.O. 2nd read above, the date of freezing of Boundaries of Administrative Units was extended upto 
31st December 2020.
3. In the G.O.3rd read above, the date of freezing of Boundaries of Administrative Units was further extended upto 
31st March 2021.
4. In the G.O.4th read above, the date of freezing of Boundaries of Administrative Units was furthermore extended upto 
31st December 2021.
5. In the G.O 5th read above, the date of freezing of Boundaries of Administrative Units was further extended upto 30.06.2022.
6. In the letter 6th  read above, the Director (Census), Office of the Registrar General, India, New Delhi in his letter dated 
14.06.2022 has stated that owing to the prevailing pandemic conditions due to the Covid-19 pandemic, danger of increased probability 
of its spread and the preoccupation/ involvement of the States / UTs in speeding up the vaccination drive against COVID-19 in 
the country, it would not be possible to start the work of ensuing Census this year. Also, the time period of conducting the ensuing 
Census is not yet decided. Further, as per Rule 8 (iv) of Census rules, 1990, the boundaries of the Administrative units shall be 
frozen from the date intimated by the Census Commissioner, which shall not be earlier than one year from the Census reference 
date. As such, it has now been decided by the competent authority to further extend the date of freezing of boundaries upto 
31st December 2022. The boundaries of the Administrative units for the ensuing Census will now be frozen on 31st December 2022. 
7. In the circumstances stated above, the Government hereby extend the Date of Freezing of Boundaries of Administrative 
Units viz., Municipalities, Revenue Villages, Towns, Tehsils, Police Stations, Sub-Divisions, Districts, etc., and ban formation of 
new units in the State of Tamil Nadu upto 31st December 2022 in connection with Census Operations. 
8.  All the pending proposals, if any affect the changes in the existing Boundaries of Municipalities, Revenue Villages, Towns, 
Tehsils, Police Stations, Sub-Divisions, Districts etc., may therefore be finalized on or before 31st December 2022 under intimation 
to the Director of Census Operations, Tamil Nadu. 
9. The Commissioner of Revenue Administration, Chennai, the Director General of Police, Chennai, the Commissioner of Rural 
Development and Panchayat Raj, Chennai, the Director of Municipal  Administration, Chennai, the Director of Town Panchayats, 
Chennai, the Collectors of all Districts, the Commissioner of Greater Chennai Corporation, the Commissioners, Municipal Corporations 
are directed to adhere to the above decision of the Government.
(By Order of the Governor)
