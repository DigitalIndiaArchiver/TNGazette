No.II(2)/HOU/162/2017.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by Section 113 of the Tamil Nadu Town and
Country Planning Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby relaxes the Development
Regulation No.28(2)(F) of Second Master Plan for Chennai
Metropolitan Area, 2026 relating to Maximum  height above
G.L exceeding 60 meter where the width of abutting road
is less  than 30.5 meter in favour of M/s. SPR Construction
Pvt. Ltd., (GPA of Binny  Limited) to issue Planning permission

Commercial Building; 3 Basement Floors + Ground Floor
+ 4 floors (anchor and shops) + 5th Floor (Part) with 11
Cinema Theatres; Residential Building: Combined basement
Floor for Block - 2 and Block 3;  Combined Stilt Floor parking
for Blocks 1 to 3 with 2 level parking; Combined  First floor
parking for Block 1 to 3 with 3 level parking; comprising of
Block 1;  36 floors (2nd floor to 37th floor) Residential - 216
dwelling units; Block-2: 36  floors (2nd floor to 37th floor)
Residential - 288 dwelling units; Block - 3; 45  floors (2nd
floor to 46th floor) Residential - 180 dwelling unit and
Block-4 (LIG); Stilt + 24 floors Residential - 264 dwelling
units - Totally 948 dwelling units and School Building:
Ground floor + 2 floors; at R.S. No.218/1, 218/2, 219/1 to 6,
220/2 of Block No.11 and R.S. No.221/2, 222/1&2 of Block
No.12, Perambur  Town, Division 73, Zone VI, Purasaiwalkam
- Perambur Taluk, Chennai.

In the Government Order first read above, the Government
had permitted  Tamil Nadu Industrial Investment Corporation
Limited (TIIC) for issue of Non SLR  Bonds to the tune of
Rs.150.00 crore during the financial year 2012-2013 with
Government guarantee for meeting their resource gap subject
to the payment of 0.50% Guarantee Commission.
2.
In the Government Order 4th read above, Government
of Tamil Nadu have issued administrative sanction for
providing Government Guarantee to  Tamil Nadu Industrial
Investment Corporation Limited (TIIC) for a sum of Rs.200.00
crore (Rupees Two Hundred crore only) towards issue of
Non-SLR Bonds  on private placement basis, to augment
the resources of the Corporation for the  financial year
2015-2016.
3.
In G.O. (Ms.) No.83, Finance (Loans & Advances)
Cell, dated 04-03-2016 Government has sanctioned
Government Guarantee for the repayment  of Principal together
with interest thereon in respect of issue of Non-SLR  Bonds
by Tamil Nadu Industrial Investment Corporation Limited
(TIIC) to the  tune of Rs.200 crores to augment the resources
of the TIlC for the Financial  year 2015-2016.
4.
In this letter 6th read above, the Principal Secretary/
Chairperson and Managing Director, Tamil Nadu Industrial
Investment Corporation Limited (TIIC)  has informed that the
Corporation could not issue the Bonds before the financial
year ended on 31-03-2016, as the process of issuing of
Non-SLR Bonds would  take about 4 months. Hence, the
TIIC proposes to go for the Non-SLR Bonds  issue of
Rs.200 crores during the current financial year 2016-2017.
The Principal  Secretary / Chairperson and Managing Director,
TIIC has further informed that  during the current financial
year 2016-2017, TIIC expects a Resource Gap of around
Rs.200 crores aparts from additional Bank Loans and other
fund sources  like Fixed Deposits. This resource gap of
Rs.200 crore has been planned to be met  by issue of
Bonds with the support of the Government of Tamil Nadu

by way of  guarantee for issue of Bonds. Owing to the fast
phased withdrawal of SIDBI  Refinance, the Issue of Bonds
(to meet the expected Resource Gap of Rs.200 crores  in
the current Financial Year 2016-2017) has become a
necessity for TIIC, to  sustain its operations and to actively
expand its assistance to industrial sector in  the State.
5.
The Principal Secretary / Chairperson and Managing
Director, TIIC has requested the Government of Tamil Nadu
to revalidate Government Guarantee for  Bonds issue of
Rs.200 crores issued vide Government Order fourth read
above to  enable TIIC to utilize the guarantee for raising of
Rs.200 crores, in the current  financial year 2016-2017
through Issue of Bonds on private placement basis to
augment the resources of the TIIC.
6.
The Government after careful examination, accept
the request of the Principal Secretary/Chairperson and
Managing Director, TIIC in para 5 above.  Accordingly,
administrative sanction issued in Government Order fourth
read  above for providing Government Guarantee to the
Tamil Nadu Industrial  Investment Corporation Limited (TIIC)
for a sum of Rs.200.00 crore towards issue  of Non-SLR
Bonds on private placement basis, to augment the resources
of the  TIIC is hereby revalidated for the financial year
2016-2017.
7.
Revised orders regarding Government guarantee will
be issued by Finance Department separately.
8.
This order issues with the concurrence of Finance
Department vide its U.O. No.1530 / L & A Cell 2017, dated
04-02-2017.
