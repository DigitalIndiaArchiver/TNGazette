No. II(2)/IND/608(a)/2009.
The Government of Tamil Nadu, having  been satisfied
that the lands specified in the schedule below have to be
acquired for industrial purposes to wit for setting up of Multi
Product Special Economic Zone, Perambalur and it having
already been decided that the entire amount of
compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid out
of the funds controlled or managed by Tamil Nadu Industrial
Development Corporation Limited (TIDCO).  The following
notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of  the
Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes
Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Acquisition of  Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil
Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of  Tamil Nadu hereby
acquires the lands specified in the schedule below and
measuring 19.72.0 hectares to the same, a little more or
less needed for industrial purposes, to wit for Multi project
Special Economic Zone, Perambalur.
The plan of the lands are kept in the office of the  District
Collector, Perambalur and may be inspected at any time
during office hours.
