JUDICIAL NOTIFICATIONS
Constitution of Seperate District Munsif Court at Sivakasi.
(Roc. No. 1422/2006/G/Judn)
No.VI(1)/441/2008.
In modification of the Notification No. VI (1)/1273 (a-9)/94 to No.VI(1)/1273 (a-12)/94 published at page 2 under
part VI Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated 7th December 1994, the following
Notification are issued in pursuance of G.O.Ms.No.1112, Home (Cts.III) Department, dated 27th August 2008 for
constitution of exclusive separate District Munsif Court, Sivakasi.
NOTIFICATION-I
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873)
the High Court, Madras, hereby fixes Sivakasi in Virudhunagar District as the place at which the District Munsif Court,
Sivakasi, shall be held.
NOTIFICATION-II
(Roc. No. 1422/2006/G/Judn)
No.VI(1)/442/2008.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 11 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873)
the High Court, Madras, hereby directs that in Virudhunagar District, the District Munsif Court, Sivakasi, shall have and
exercise local jurisdiction over the entire Revenue Taluk of Sivakasi except Mangalam Firka with effect from the date
on which the district Munsif, Sivakasi assumes charge of that court.

NOTIFICATION-III
(Roc. No. 1422/2006/G/Judn)
No.VI(1)/443/2008.
The High Court, Madras hereby directs and notifies that the District Munsif, Sivakasi in Virudhunagar District shall
exercise all the powers conferred on a District Munsif under Section 12 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873
(Central Act III of 1873) as amended up-to-date in regard to original suits and proceedings not otherwise exempted
from his cognizance of which the amount or value of the subject matter does not exceed One Lakh Rupees within
the local limits of his Jurisdiction with effect from the date on which the District Munsif, Sivakasi assumes charge of
that court.
NOTIFICATION-IV
(Roc. No. 1422/2006/G/Judn)
No.VI(1)/444/2008.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 28 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873)
the High Court, Madras, hereby directs that the District Munsif, Sivakasi shall have and exercise jurisdiction of a court
of Smalll Causes under the provincial Small Causes Court Act, 1887 for the trial suits cognizable by a court of small
causes upto the pecuniary limits of Rs.5000/- (Rupees five thousand only) with effect from the date on which the District
Munsif, Sivakasi, assumes charge of that Court.
Change of Nomenclature of the Court at Sivakasi.
(Roc. No. 1422/2006/G/Judn)
No. VI(1)/445/2008.
Whereas the Government of Tamil Nadu have issued orders in G.O.Ms.No. 1112, Home (Cts.III) Department,
dated 27th August 2008 that a separate District Munsif Court be constituted at Sivakasi and the existing District
Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Sivakasi be converted as exclusive Judicial Magistrate Court, Sivakasi.
Hence it is hereby informed that on and from the date on which the District Munsif Court, Sivakasi is constituted,
the nomenclature of the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Sivakasi shall be changed as the
Judicial Magistrate Court, Sivakasi.
High Court, Madras,
R. MALA,
