GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD
No.1, Jaffar Syrang Street, Vallal Seethakathi Nagar, Chennai-600 001.
Taking over the Direct Management of Masjidul Huda Wakf, Door No. 49, St. Xavier Street,
Seven Wells, Chennai-600 001.
(Rc. No. 16042/04/B1/Chennai.)
No. VI-3(a)/195/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred and as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995,
the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its proceedings in item No. 120/08 in R.C. 16042/04/B1/Chennai, dated 25th June 2008
has ordered to take over the management of Masjidul Huda Wakf, No.49, St. Xavier Street, Seven Wells, Chennai-
600 001, under the direct management of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of one year from the date of
pronouncement  of orders, dated 26th February 2009 and appointed the Superintendent of Wakfs, Chennai Zone as
its Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
30th June 2009.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
