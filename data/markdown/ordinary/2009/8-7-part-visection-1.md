GENERAL  NOTIFICATIONS
Comprehensive Variation to the Coleroon North Detailed Development Plan No. 3 of
Tiruchirappalli Local Planning Authority.
(Roc. No. 13717/2007/DP2)
No. VI(1)/94/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (1) of Section 33 of Town and Country Planning Act, 1971
(Act No. 35 of 1972) the Commissioner of Town and Country Planning in the Proceedings Roc. No. 13717/2007/DP2,
dated 6-2-2009 proposes to comprehensively vary the entire Coleroon North Detailed Development Plan No. 3 of
Tiruchirappalli Local Planning Authority approved by the Director of Town and Country Planning vide Proceedings
Roc. No. 4042/95/DP3, dated 20th February 1995 and published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, Part VI—Section
1, Page No. 131, dated 5th February 1995 (Publication No. VI(1)/724/95.
2.
Any person affected or interested in this draft Variation may within Sixty days from the date of publication of this
notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette can represent in person or submit in writing to the Member-Secretary.
Tiruchirappalli Local Planning Authority any objections and suggestions relating thereto.
3.
The variation with plan may be inspected free of cost at any time during office hours at the above said Local
Planning Authority office.
VARIATION
1.
The entire Coleroon North Detailed Development Plan No. 3 of Tiruchirappalli Local Planning Area is
comprehensively varied by the Commissioner of Town and Country Planning vide proceedings Roc. No. 13717/2007/DP2,
dated 6-2-2009.
2.
The plan thus varied is numbered as Map No. 5 DDP(V)/DTCP No. 1/2009.
3.
The Detailed Development Plan clauses and schedules annexed with this plan area made enforceable from the
date of publication of the confirmed variation notification to be issued under Section 33(2) of the Act and published in
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
Chennai-600 002,
ASHOK DONGRE,
6th February 2009.
Commissioner of Town and Country Planning.
Variation to the Approved Detailed Development Plan No. 14 of Pudukkottai Local Planning Area.
(Roc. No. 16153/2008/DP3)
No. VI(1)/95/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (1) of Section 33 of Town and Country Planning Act, 1971
(Act No. 35 of 1972), the Commissioner of Town and Country Planning, Chennai in his proceedings Roc. No. 16153/
2008/DP3, dated 10th February 2009 proposes to make the following variation to the approved Pudukkottai Detailed
Development Plan No. 14 in proceedings Roc. No. 37863/98/DP3, dated 9th September 99 by Commissioner of Town
and Country Planning, Chennai and the same was published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No. 9, Part VI—Section
1, Page 93, dated 8th March 2000.
2.
Any person affected or interested in the draft Variation may within Sixty days from the date of publication of this
notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, can represent in person or submit in writing to the Member-Secretary
Pudukkottai Local Planning Authority and objections and suggestions relating thereto.
3.
The variation with plan may be inspected free of cost at any time during office hours at the above said Local
Planning Authority office.
