Amendment Notification
The following Amendment is issued to the Notification published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No.10A, dated 
14th March 2012 under Part II - Section 2 (Supplement) at  pp 122 – 216.
Amendment
In the said Notification – under the heading “Standard VII-History” at pp 207-210, the following shall be substituted :-
Unit-1
North Indian Kingdoms
The Rajputs, Prathikaras, Palas, Tomars, 	 	
	   Chauhans and others.
Unit-2
The Kingdom of the Deccan
Chalukyas -  Rastrakutas – Hoysalas –Kakatiyas 	
	    and Yadavas
Unit-3
South Indian Kingdoms
Pallavas – imperial Cholas and the Pandyas
Unit-4
Arab and Turkish Invasions 
Muhammad bin Qasim – Muhammad of Ghazni 	
	   and Muhammad of Ghori
Unit-5
Sultanate of Delhi
Mamaluk, Khilji, Tughluq, Syyed and Lodi Dynasties
Unit-6
The Vijaya Nagar and Bhamini Empires
Unit-7
Bhakti &Sufi Movement
