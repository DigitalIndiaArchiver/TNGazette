NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
FINANCE  DEPARTMENT
(Salaries)
 ENTRUSTING THE WORK OF PROVIDING BROADBAND CONNECTIVITY AND OTHER SIMILAR SERVICES
FOR THE GENERAL ELECTIONS TO THE TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY, 2011 TO
M/s. BHARAT SANCHAR NIGAM LIMITED, UNDER TAMIL NADU TRANSPARENCY IN
TENDERS ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 97, Finance (Salaries), 28th March 2011, Panguni 14, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No. II(2)/FIN/144(d)/2011.
Under clause (f) of Section 16 of the Tamil Nadu Transparency in Tenders Act, 1998 (Tamil Nadu
Act 43 of 1998), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notifies the entrustment of the work of providing
Broadband connectivity to Polling Stations, Internet leased line connectivity to the office of Chief Electoral
Officer, District Election Officers and Returning Officers, purchase of SIM cards and any other similar
services required to be provided as per the directions of the Election Commission of India for the
ensuing General Elections to Tamil Nadu Assembly, 2011 to M/s. Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL).
K. SHANMUGAM,
Principal Secretary to Government.
