MADURAI KAMARAJ UNIVERSITY
(Election to the Authorities of the University)
Election to the Senate and the Academic Council
(Ref. No. ME2/S&AC/HM/Election/2010.)
No. VI-3(b)/4/2010.
It is hereby notified that election will be held to elect One Member each to the Senate and the Academic Council
from each of the following revenue districts by the Headmasters of High Schools/Higher Secondary Schools from among
themselves in the following districts in the University area under Section 15 (a) Class III (5) and Section 24(a) Class II (6)
of the Madurai Kamaraj University Act, 1965:—
1. Madurai District
2. Dindigul District
3. Virudhunagar District
4. Theni District
The last date and hour for receipt of nomination is fixed as 29th January 2010 at 04.00 p.m.
The Director of School Education or his nominee is to hold the election and to communicate the results by
15th February 2010.
Palkalai Nagar, Madurai-625  021,
DR. I. SINGARAM,
27th January 2010.
Registrar.
Election to the Senate
(No. ME2/S/Pri.Apprd. Coll./Election/2010.)
No. VI-3(b)/5/2010.
It is hereby notified that election will be held to elect Three Members to the Senate by the Principals of approved
Colleges from among themselves in accordance with the system of proportional representation by means of the single
transferable vote under Section 15 (a) Class III (4) of the Madurai Kamaraj University Act, 1965.
The last date and hour for receipt of nomination is fixed as 29th January 2010 at 04.00 p.m.
Palkalai Nagar, Madurai-625 021,
DR. I. SINGARAM,
27th January 2010.
Registrar.
Election to the Academic Council
(No. ME2/S/Teach.Apprd. Coll. / Election/2010.)
No. VI-3(b)/6/2010.
It is hereby notified that election will be held to elect Two Members to the  Academic Council by the teachers of
approved colleges including principals and physical directors but excluding tutors, demonstrators and physical instructors
of such colleges from among themselves under Section 24 (a) Class II (3) of the Madurai Kamaraj University Act, 1965.
The last date and hour for receipt of nomination is fixed as 29th January 2010 at 04.00 p.m.
Palkalai Nagar, Madurai-625 021,
DR. I, SINGARAM,
27th January 2010.
Registrar.

No. VI-3(b)/7/2010.
Under Section (1) of Statute 5 of Chapter XXI of the General Statutes of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University,
Chennai, it is hereby published that the following members have been elected to the Senate of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R.
Medical University, Chennai, Election of one Professor to the Senate by the Teachers of each affiliated Medical College
from among themselves under Section 18(3) (a) of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai Act, 1987
(Tamil Nadu Act 37/1987).
The membership will be for a period of three years as noted against each or so long as they hold the post of Professor
of the concerned affiliated College of this University, or till the date of their superannuation whichever is earlier.
