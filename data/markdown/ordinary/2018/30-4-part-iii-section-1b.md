No. SRO B-54/2018.—In exercise of the Powers conferred by the Proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, 
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Co-operative 
Subordinate Service (Section 5 in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1969).  
2. (a) The amendment (2) (A) (i) (a) hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on and from the 
5th November 1985.
   (b) The amendments (1) and (2) (A) (ii) (c) hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on and from the 
31st August 2002.
   (c) All other amendments hereby made shall come into force on the date of publication of this Notiﬁ cation in the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules,-
 
(1) for the expressions ''Steno-typist'' and ''Steno-typists'', wherever they occur, the expressions, ''Steno-typist 
Grade-III'' and ''Steno-typists Grade-III'' shall, respectively, be substituted;
 
(2) in rule 2,-
 
(A) in sub-rule (a),-
 
(i) against the entry ''2, Senior Inspectors'' in column (1), - 
 
 
(a) for the entries (1), (2) and (3) in column (2), the following entries shall be substituted, namely:-
 
 
''(1) Direct Recruitment;
 
 
 (2) Promotion from Junior Inspectors:'';
 
 
 (b) after the entries as so substituted, the following proviso shall be added, namely:-
 
 
 ''Provided that appointment to the post shall be made in the ratio of 3:1 between the direct recruitment and 
promotion.'';
 
(ii) against the entry ''3 Junior Inspectors'' in column (1),-
 
 
(a) in the entries (1) and (2) in column (2), the word ''or'' shall be omitted;
 
 
(b) entry (3) in column (2) shall be omitted;
 
 
(c) after the entry (2) in column (2) the following provisos shall be added, namely:-
''Provided that the appointment to the post shall be made in the ratio of 1:3 between the direct recruitment and recruitment 
by transfer:
 
Provided further that among the Vacancies earmarked for appointment by recruitment by transfer, ﬁ ve percent of 
the Vacancies shall be ﬁ lled from the category of Steno-typist Grade-III, who has put in not less than ﬁ ve years of service 
and opts for recruitment by transfer as Junior Inspector as on 1st May of the year in which the selection for appointment is 
made and the option so exercised shall be ﬁ nal'';
 
(iii) The 'NOTE' shall be omitted;
 
(iv) sub-rule (b) and the Explanation there under shall be omitted;
