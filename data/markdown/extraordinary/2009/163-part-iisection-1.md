LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY SECRETARIAT
DEATH OF THIRU D. SELVARAJ,  MEMBER OF THE TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY.
[Letter No. 12749/2009-1, Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Secretariat, (B.III), 6th July  2009,
Aani 22, Thiruvalluvar Aandu 2040.]
No. II(1)/LAS/20(a-1)/2009.
"The Honourable Speaker has learnt with deep regret the death of Thiru D. Selvaraj, a Member
of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly on 5th July 2009".
VACATION OF SEAT IN TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY.
[Letter No. 12749/2009-1, Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Secretariat, (B.III), 6th July  2009,
Aani 22, Thiruvalluvar Aandu 2040.]
No. II(1)/LAS/20(a-2)/2009.
"A vacancy has occurred among the Members of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly, consequent
on the death of Thiru D. Selvaraj, a Member elected to the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly from the
226. Srivaikuntam Assembly Constituency in Thoothukudi District".
