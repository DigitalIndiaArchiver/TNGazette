CHENNAI CITY MUNICIPAL CORPORATION
NOTIFICATION OF RESERVATION OF WARDS TO SCHEDULED CASTE (GENERAL), SCHEDULED
CASTE (WOMEN) AND WOMEN (GENERAL)
[R.D.(Elections) C.No.6416/2016]
No. VI(2)/120(h)/2016.
In exercise of the powers delegated by the Government under Sub-section (3), (4), (5) of Section-5 and Section-45 of
the Chennai City Municipal Corporation Act, 1919 in G.O.Ms.No.133, Municipal Administration and Water Supply Department,
dated 6th June 1996 and in accordance with the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Municipalities and Corporations
(Delimitation of Wards or Divisions and Reservation) Rules, 1996 issued in G.O.Ms.No.136, Municipal Administration and Water
Supply (Election) Department, Dated 11th June 1996, G.O.Ms.No.85, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (Election)
Department, Dated 18th June 2001 and in accordance with G.O.Ms.No.75, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (Election)
Department, Dated 1st August 2006 and in accordance to the amendment made to sub-section (4) and (5) of Section 5 of the
Chennai City Municipal Corporation Act, 1919 issued in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette (No.46) dated 20th February 2016 and
as per amendments made to sub-rule-4 (3) and 4-(4-A) of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Municipalities and Corporations
(Delimitation of Wards or Divisions and Reservation) Rules, 1996, the Commissioner, Greater Chennai Corporation, hereby
effects reservation of 32 Wards for Scheduled Caste and 92 Wards to Women (General) based on Census Population 2011
for the purpose of elections to be held for the Corporation Council, notified as follows:—
