GENERAL NOTIFICATION
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI
Taking Over the Direct Management of AL Lajnathud Deeniya Wakf, Perambur, Chennai District.
(Lr. Rc. No. 452/15/B9/CHN)
No.VI-3(a)/11/2016.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the wakf Act 1995, the
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its Resolution in Item No. 188/15 in Rc. No. 452/15/B9/Chennai, dated 13-11-2015 has
ordered to take over AL LAJNATHUD DEENIYA WAKF, PERAMBUR, CHENNAI DISTRICT under  Direct Management of
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of one year from 13-11-2015 to 12-11-2016 and to appoint the Superintendent of Wakfs,
Chennai as its Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,
K. RASHEETH ALI,
20th January 2016.
District Revenue Officer/
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
