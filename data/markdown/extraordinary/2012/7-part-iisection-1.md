“In exercise of the powers conferred upon me by clause (1) of Article 174 of the Constitution
of India and in supersession of the notification issued in S.O. Ms. No. 237, Legislative Assembly Secretariat, dated
the 29th December 2011 and published in an extraordinary issue of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated
the 29th December 2011, I, Dr. K. ROSAIAH, Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby summon the Tamil Nadu Legislative
Assembly to meet at 10.00 A.M. on Monday, the 30th January 2012 in the Assembly Chamber, Secretariat,
Chennai-600 009 instead of at 10.00 A.M. on Wednesday, the 18th January 2012 as already notified.
Raj Bhavan,
K. ROSAIAH,
