Government of India, Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances 
and Pensions, Department of Personnel and Training North 
Block, New Delhi, dated 22nd March, 2021 is republished. 
In exercise of the powers conferred by Rule 6(1) of the 
Indian Administrative service (Cadre) Rules, 1954 and with 
the concurrence of the State Governments of Uttar Pradesh 
and Tamil Nadu, the Central Government hereby accords 
approval for extension of inter cadre deputation tenure of 
Ms. Pinky Jowel, IAS (UP:2003) from Uttar Pradesh cadre to 
Tamil Nadu cadre on personal grounds for a period of two years 
beyond 24-05-2021 or until further orders, whichever is earlier.
