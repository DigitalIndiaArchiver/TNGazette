Order: No. 24, Public (Special-B), 11th January 2022.
Under Clause (3) of Article 166 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that 
	
(i) The subject "Sugars" hitherto dealt with by the Minister for Industries be allocated to the Minister for Agriculture and 
Farmers' Welfare in addition to the subjects already held by him. 
	
(ii) The subject "Airports" be dealt with by the Minister for Industries instead of the Minister for Transport. 
	
(iii) The subject "Overseas Manpower Corporation Limited (OMCL)" be dealt with by the Minister for Minorities Welfare 
and Non Resident Tamils Welfare instead of Minister for Labour Welfare and Skill Development. 
(By Order of the Governor)
