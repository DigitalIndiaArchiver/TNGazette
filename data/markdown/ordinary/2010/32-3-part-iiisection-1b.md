Amendments to Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Police Subordinate Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 625, Home (Pol.VI), 14th July 2010, ÝQ 30, F¼õœÀõ˜ Ý‡´-2041.]
No. SRO B-53/2010.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Sections 8 and 10 of the Tamil Nadu District Police
Act, 1859 (Central Act XXIV of 1859) and Sections 9 and 11 of the Chennai City Police Act, 1888 (Tamil Nadu Act III
of 1888) read with the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the
following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Police Subordinate Service (Section 34 in Volume III
of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1946).
2. The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force with effect from the 13th November 2007.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules, in ANNEXURE II, in the tabular column, in column (3),—
(i) against “Category-2 Sub-Inspectors” in column (1), in item 1 (iii), for the sentence beginning with the expression
"Both Men and Women candidates should take Physical Measurement test first” and ending with the expression
“Permitted to Physical endurance test.”, the following sentence shall be substituted, namely:—
“Both men and women candidates should appear for the written test first. Candidates who have passed
the written test alone will be Permitted for the Physical Measurement test and Physical endurance test.”;
(ii) against “Category-5 Reserve Sub-Inspectors other than the Band Master of the Director General of Police,
Madras,” in column (1), in item 1 (iii), for the sentence beginning with the expression “Both Men and Women candidates
should take Physical Measurement test first” and ending with the expression “Permitted to Physical endurance test.”, the
following sentence shall be substituted, namely:—
“Both men and women candidates should appear for the written test first. Candidates who have passed
the written test alone will be permitted for the physical measurement test and physical endurance test.”; and
(iii) against “Category-8 Constables Men and Women (other than Band Constables, Office of the Director General
of Police) and Reserve Constables” in column (1), in item 1 (ii) (b), for the sentences “Both men and women candidates
should take physical measurement test first. Persons who qualified in Physical Measurement test alone will be Permitted
to the Physical endurance test.”, the following sentences shall be substituted, namely:—
“Both men and women candidates should appear for the written test first. Candidates who have passed
the written test alone will be permitted for the physical measurement  test and physical endurance test.”
K. GNANADESIKAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.

No. SRO B-54/2010.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu makes the following Rules:—
2. The rules hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 6th May 1996.
RULES
The General and Special Rules applicable to the holders of the permanent posts in the Tamil Nadu General Subordinate
Service, shall apply to the holder of the temporary post of District Rehabilitation Officer (Now District Differently Abled
Welfare Officer), sanctioned from time to time, in the Department of Rehabilitation of the Differently Abled, subject to the
modifications specified in the following rules:—
2. Constitution.—The post shall constitute a separate category in a distinct class in the said service.
3. Appointment.—Appointment to the post shall be made by promotion from the holders of the post of
Junior Rehabilitation Officer.
4. Appointing Authority.—The appointing authority for the post shall be the State Commissioner for the Disabled,
(Now the State Commissioner for the Differently Abled).
5. Pay.—There shall be paid to the holder of the post a monthly pay calculated in the scale of Rs. 6,500-200-10,500
(pre revised).
 S.S. JAWAHAR,
     Secretary to Government.
