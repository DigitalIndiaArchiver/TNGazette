APPOINTMENT OF THIRU JUSTICE  DHARMA RAO ELIPE, SENIORMOST JUDGE OF THE MADRAS HIGH COURT,
TO PERFORM THE DUTIES  OF THE OFFICE OF THE CHIEF JUSTICE OF MADRAS HIGH COURT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 410, Public (Special-A), 29th  April 2010,
Chithirai 16, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No. I/PUSP/6(a)/2010.
The following Notification of the Government of India, Ministry of Law and Justice (Department of Justice),
Jaisalmer House, 26, Man Singh Road, New Delhi-110 011, dated 29th April, 2010 is republished:—
In exercise of the powers conferred by Article 223 of the Constitution of India, the President is pleased to
appoint Shri Justice Dharma Rao Elipe, Seniormost Judge of the Madras High Court, to perform the duties of the
office of the Chief Justice of that High Court with effect from the date Shri Justice Hemant Laxman Gokhale
relinquishes charge of the office of the Chief Justice of the Madras High Court consequent upon his appointment
as a Judge of the Supreme Court of India.
