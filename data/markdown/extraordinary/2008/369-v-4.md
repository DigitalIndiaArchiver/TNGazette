NOTIFICATIONS  BY  THE  ELECTION  COMMISSION OF INDIA.
APPOINTMENT OF DATES FOR VARIOUS STAGES OF BYE-ELECTION TO TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY
FROM 137. THIRUMANGALAM ASSEMBLY CONSTITUENCY AND FIXATION OF HOURS OF POLL TO FILL  A
VACANCY CAUSED BY THE DEATH OF A MEMBER.
No. SRO G-24/2008.
The following Notification of the Election Commission of India, Nirvachan Sadan,  Ashoka Road, New Delhi-110 001,
dated 15th December, 2008 [24  Agrahayana, 1930 (Saka)], is published:—
No. 100/TN-LA/1/2008:—Whereas, the seat of Shri Veera Elavarasan, in the Legislative Assembly of the Tamil Nadu
State elected from 137. Thirumangalam Assembly Constituency has become  vacant on 08-11-2008,  by  reason of his death, and
2. Whereas, a bye-election is to be held for the purpose of filling the vacancy so caused;
3.  Now, therefore, in pursuance of sub-section (1) of Sections 150, 30 and 56 of the Representation of the
People Act, 1951 (43 of 1951), the Election Commission of India hereby—
(A) Calls upon the said 137. Thirumangalam Assembly Constituency in the State of Tamil Nadu to elect,  before the
15-01-2009 (Thursday) and in accordance with the provisions of the said Act and of the Rules and Orders made thereunder,
a person, for the purpose of filling the said vacancy in the Legislative Assembly of the State; and
(B) Appoints, with respect to the said election:—
(a) the 22-12-2008, (Monday) as the last date for making nominations;
(b) the 23-12-2008, (Tuesday) as the date for the scrutiny of nominations;
(c) the 26-12-2008, (Friday) as the last date for the withdrawal of candidatures;
(d) the 09-01-2009, (Friday) as the date on which a poll shall, if necessary, be taken;
(e) the 15-01-2009, (Thursday) as the date before which the election shall be completed, and
(C) Fixes the hours from 7.00 A.M. to 5.00 P.M. as the hours during which the poll shall, if necessary, be taken on the
date specified above for the election.
