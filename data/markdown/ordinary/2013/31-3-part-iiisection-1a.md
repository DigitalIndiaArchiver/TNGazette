Amendments to the Tamil  Nadu Legislators‘ (Medical Attendance and Treatment to former Members) Rules.
[G.O.Ms. No. 787, Public (Establishment.I & Legislature), 23rd July 2013.]
No. SRO A-22/2013.—In exercise of the powers conferred by section 14 read with the proviso to sub-section (4) of
Section 12 of the Tamil Nadu Payment of Salaries Act, 1951 (Tamil Nadu Act XX of 1951), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby makes the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu Legislators‘ (Medical Attendance and Treatment to former
Members) Rules, 1982.
2. The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st April 2013.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules,—
(1) in rule 3, in the first proviso, for the expression “Rs. 10,000/- (Rupees ten thousand only)’’, the expression
“Rs. 12,000/- (Rupees twelve thousand only)” shall be substituted.
(2) in the Appendix in Certificate under Form ‘C’, for the expression, “Rs. 10,000/- (Rupees ten thousand only)”, the
expression “Rs. 12,000/- (Rupees twelve thousand only)” shall be substituted.
