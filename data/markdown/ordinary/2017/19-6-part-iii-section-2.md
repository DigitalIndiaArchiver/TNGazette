V.O. CHIDAMBARANAR PORT TRUST, TUTICORIN
RECRUITMENT RULES FOR THE POST OF SAFETY INSPECTOR (CLASS II)
(S-3/3/2017-E.I)
 Amendment to the Schedule II of Tuticorin Port Trust Employees
(Recruitment, Seniority and Promotion) Regulations, 2017
No. SROC-11/2017- In exercise of the powers conferred by Sub-Section (i) of Section 124, read with Sub Section (1)(a)
of the Major Port Trusts Act, 1963 (38 of 1963) the Board of Trustees, V.O. Chidambaranar Port Trust hereby approves the
revised manner of recruitment (Recruitment Rules) for Class III posts under Regulation 5 of Tuticorin Port Trust Employees
(Recruitment, Seniority and Promotion) Regulations, 2008 as set out in the Schedule  annexed to this Notification.
2. The said Recruitment Rules shall come into effect from the date of publication of this Notification in the Tamil Nadu
Government Gazette.
SCHEDULE
In the said Schedule under Regulations, in the Recruitment Rules for the post of Safety Inspector in Marine Department,
the existing provisions under Col. Nos. 8, 11 and 12 shall be substituted by the following—
Col. 8:
Under “Educational and other qualifications prescribed for direct recruitment” the following may be included in (ii) —
“OR equivalent certificate from Government of India Organisations/Department”
Col. 11:
under “Method of Recruitment” to be read as -– “By promotion failing which  deputation/absorption, failing both by
direct recruitment”.
Col.12:
            under “In case of promotion/deputation/absorption, grades from which it should be made” may be read as -
“By Promotion:
Sub-Officer with three years regular service in the grade in the Port, failing which:
Sub-Officer with five years regular service put together in the grade of Sub-Officer/Leading Fireman/Fireman Driver/
Fireman with a minimum regular service of two years as Sub-Officer.
Failing which Deputation/absorption:
Suitable Officers holding analogous posts in Major Port Trusts, Central/State and PSUs failing which next lower post
to Sub-Officer with ten years regular service. (Period of deputation ordinarily not exceeding three years).
