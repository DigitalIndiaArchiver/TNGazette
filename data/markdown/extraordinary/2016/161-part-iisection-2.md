Under clause (b) of sub-section (2) of Section 6 of the Legal Services Authorities Act, 1987
(Central Act 39 of 1987), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, in consultation with the Chief Justice of the
High Court of Madras, hereby nominates Honourable Justice Mr. Huluvadi G. Ramesh, the
Senior most Judge of the High Court of Madras as the Executive Chairman of the Tamil Nadu State
Legal Services Authority, Chennai with immediate effect.
APURVA VARMA,
Principal Secretary to Government.
