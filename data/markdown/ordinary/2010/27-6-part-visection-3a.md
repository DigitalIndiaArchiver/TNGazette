GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI
Taking over the Direct Management of Madharasathul Sakiya Bi Balika Thaikka Wakf, Dindigul District.
(Lr.Rc.No. 7636/B15/08/DGL.)
No.VI-3(a)/104/2010—
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act 1995, the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board
in its proceedings in item No. 3/2009 in Rc.No. 7636/B15/08/DGL, dated 30th March 2010 has ordered to take over the
Management of Madhrasthul Sakiya Bi Balika Thaikka Wakf, Dindigul District under the direct control of the Tamil Nadu Wakf
Board for a period of one year from the date of publication in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette and to appoint the Wakf
Inspector, Dindigul as its Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
15th June 2010.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
