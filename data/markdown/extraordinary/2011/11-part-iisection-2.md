REVENUE  DEPARTMENT
DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF THE TAMIL NADU PUBLIC PREMISES (EVICTION OF
UNAUTHORISED OCCUPANTS) AMENDMENT ACT, 2010.
[G.O. Ms. No. 10, Revenue [LD.6(2)], 5th January 2011.]
No. II(2)/REV/8(e)/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Public Premises
(Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) Amendment Act, 2010 (Tamil Nadu Act 33 of 2010), the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the 7th January 2011 as the date on which the said Act shall come
into force.
V.K. JEYAKODI,
Principal Secretary to Government.
