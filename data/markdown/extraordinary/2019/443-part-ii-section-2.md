No. II(2)/FIN/932(c)/2019.
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (bb) of Section 16 of the Tamil Nadu Transparency 
in Tenders Act, 1998 (Tamil Nadu Act 43 of 1998), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby declares to 
engage the India Infrastructure Finance Company Limited (IIFCL), as Advisor or Consultant to the 
Tamil Nadu Infrastructure Development Board for guidance and rendering advice on infrastructure 
projects, preparing concept papers, project appraisal, appraisal of bid documents for Public Private 
Partnership projects.
