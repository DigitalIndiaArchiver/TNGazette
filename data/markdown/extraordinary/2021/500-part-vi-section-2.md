TAMIL NADU STATE ELECTION COMMISSION, CHENNAI-600 106.
[ELECTIONS – TOWN PANCHAYATS, MUNICIPALITIES AND CORPORATIONS - CONDUCT OF - PREPARATION

NOTIFIED.]
NOTIFICATION - I
(S.O. No. 56/2021/TNSEC/ME-1)
No.VI(2)/109(a-1)/2021. 
In exercise of the powers conferred by rule 8 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Municipalities and Corporations 
(Elections) Rules, 2006 and in supersession of the Notiﬁ cation No.VI(2)/41(b-1)/2019, published at Page 1 of Part VI--Section-2 
of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary No.173, dated 16th April, 2019, the State Election Commission hereby 
authorises the Executive Oﬃ  cers of the respective Town Panchayats, or any other oﬃ  cer appointed by the State Election 
Oﬃ  cer / District Election Oﬃ  cer as the case may be, as the Electoral Registration Oﬃ  cers for the purpose of preparing 
and publishing electoral rolls in respect of the respective Town Panchayats, and the wards lying within the respective 
Town Panchayats.
NOTIFICATION - II
(S.O. No. 56/2021/TNSEC/ME-1)
No.VI(2)/109(a-2)/2021.
In exercise of the powers conferred by rule 8 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Municipalities and Corporations (Elections) 
Rules, 2006 and in supersession of the Notiﬁ cation No.VI(2)/41(b-2)/2019, published at Page 1 of Part VI--Section 2 of the 
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary No.173, dated 16th April, 2019, the State Election Commission hereby authorises 
the Commissioners of the respective Municipalities as the Electoral Registration Oﬃ  cers for the purpose of preparing and 
publishing electoral rolls in respect of the respective Municipalities and the wards lying within the respective Municipalities.

No.VI(2)/109(a-12)/2021.
In exercise of the powers conferred under rule 9 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Municipalities and Corporations 
(Elections) Rules, 2006 and in supersession of the Notiﬁ cation No.350 under No.VI (2)/82(a-2)/2019 published at Page 2 of 
Part VI – Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 3rd September, 2019 and Notiﬁ cation 
No.91 under No.VI(2)/9(b)/2020 published at page 1 of Part VI – Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette 
Extraordinary, dated the 28th February, 2020, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission hereby directs the District 
Election Oﬃ  cers except in the District of Chennai to appoint within their respective jurisdictions the Executive Oﬃ  cer of the 
Town Panchayat or the Commissioner of Municipality or City Municipal Corporation respectively or any other oﬃ  cer of the 
Government as Returning Oﬃ  cer for the conduct of election to the Town Panchayat or Municipality or City Municipal Corporation, 
as the case may be, under the said Rules.
