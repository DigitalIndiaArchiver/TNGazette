No. I/PUSP/7/2011.—The following Notification of
the  Government of India, Ministry of Law and Justice,
(Department of Justice), Jaisalmer House, 26, Man Singh Road,
New Delhi-110 011, dated 17th February 2011 is republished:—
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (1) of
Article 222 of the Constitution of India, read with article 222 (1A)
of the Constitution (Application to Jammu and Kashmir) Order,
1954, the President, after consultation with the Chief Justice of
India, is pleased to transfer Shri Justice Fakkir Mohamed Ibrahim
Kalifulla, Judge of the Madras High Court, as a Judge of the
Jammu and Kashmir High Court and to direct him to assume
charge of his office in the Jammu and Kashmir High Court on
or before 3rd March, 2011.
