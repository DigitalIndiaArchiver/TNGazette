(S.C.)
Order of Detention of certain person and direct to appear
before certain official under Conservation of Foreign
Exchange and Prevention of Smuggling Activtities Act.
[G.O. No. SR.I/105-14/2012, Public (SC), 23rd July 2012.]
No. II(2)/PUSC/454/2012.тWhereas, an order had been
made by the Government of Tamil Nadu in G.O.No.SR.I/105-
9/2012, Public (SC) Department, dated 21-6-2012 under
Section 3(1)(i) of the Conservation of Foreign Exchange and
Prevention of Smuggling Activities Act, 1974 (Central Act 52
of 1974), directing that Thiru Syed Mohamed Buhari,
S/o. Thiru Kader Mohideen, resident of No. 13/46-A,
Hussain Hajiar Nagar, 500 Plot, Keelakkarai-623 517,
Ramanathapuram District and No.15/176, Ahmed Street,
Keelakarai-623 517, Ramanathapuram District, be detained;
AND WHEREAS, the said Thiru Syed Mohamed Buhari,
S/o. Thiru Kader Mohideen, resident of No. 13/46-A,
Hussain Hajiar Nagar, 500 Plot, Keelakkarai-623 517,
Ramanathapuram District and No. 15/176, Ahmed Street,
Keelakarai-623 517, Ramanathapuram District, cannot be
found;
AND WHEREAS, the Government of Tamil Nadu have
reasons to believe that the said Thiru Syed Mohamed Buhari,
S/o. Thiru Kader Mohideen, resident of No. 13/46-A,
Hussain Hajiar Nagar, 500 Plot, Keelakkarai-623 517,
Ramanathapuram District and No. 15/176, Ahmed Street,
Keelakarai-623 517, Ramanathapuram District, in respect
of whom the detention order has been made, has absconded
and is concealing himself so that the said detention order
cannot be executed.
NOW THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred
by Section 7 (1) (b) of the Conservation of Foreign Exchange
and Prevention of Smuggling Activities Act, 1974 (Central Act
52 of 1974), the Government of Tamil Nadu hereby direct
the said Thiru Syed Mohamed Buhari, S/o. Thiru Kader
Mohideen, resident of No. 13/46-A, Hussain Hajiar Nagar,
500 Plot, Keelakkarai-623 517, Ramanathapuram
District and No. 15/176, Ahmed Street, Keelakarai-623 517,
Ramanathapuram District, to appear before the
Superintendent of Police, Ramanathapuram District,
Ramanathapuram at his office within a period of 15 days
(fifteen days) from the date of publication of this order in
the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
KUMAR JAYANT,
Secretary to Government.
