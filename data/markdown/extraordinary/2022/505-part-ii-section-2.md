[G.O. Ms. No.216, Industries, Investment Promotion and Commerce (GIM), 31st October 2022,
Aippasi 14, Subakiruthu, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2053.]
No.II(2)/IIPC/854(a-1)/2022
A declaration was published under sub-section 19(1) of the Right to Fair Compensation and Transparency in Land acquisition, 
Rehabilitation and Resettlement Act, 2013 (Central Act 30 of 2013) to acquire 113.25.50 hectares of land in Block 1 to 12 
of Unit-V vide G.O. (Ms) No.272, Industries (GIM) Department, dated 13.10.2021 for establishing a new launch station for 
SSLV in favour of ISRO at Madhavankurichi Village in Tiruchendur Taluk of Thoothukudi District in Part II—Section 2 of the 
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No.480, dated 13.10.2021 and in local Tamil and English Daily Newspaper Dated 01.11.2021.
 
As per Section 25 of the RFCTLARR Act, 2013 (Central Act 30 of 2013) an award shall be made within a period of 
12 months from the date of publication of the declaration under section 19(1) of the said Act.
While passing an award, it is noticed that in the same survey number, one sub-division is speciﬁ ed in square meters and 
another is speciﬁ ed in hectares having variations in guideline value for the lands in the Unit – V, Block-3 Scheduled below. 
Since, the guideline value varies, it is necessary to arrive a fair value among the lands.
Therefore, in order to assess the land and to ﬁ x the land value for passing awards, the Government hereby extends the 
time limit for further period of 12 months from 1.11.2022.
