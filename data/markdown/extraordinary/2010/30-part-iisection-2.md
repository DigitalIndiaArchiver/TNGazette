HIGHWAYS AND MINOR PORTS DEPARTMENT
AMENDMENTS TO NOTIFICATIONS UNDER INDIAN TOLLS ACT.
LEVY OF TOLLS ON MOTOR VEHICLES FOR IMPROVEMENT AND MAINTENANCE OF EAST COAST ROAD
CONNECTING CHENNAI WITH PONDICHERRY FOR CERTAIN PERIOD UNDER THE ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 40, Highways and Minor Ports (HV-2), 1st February 2010,
Thai 19, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No. II(2)/HWMP/68(e-1)/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (c) of sub-section (1) and clause (c) of sub-section-2 of the Indian
Tolls Act, 1851 (Central Act VIII of 1851), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Highways
Department Notification No. II(2)/HW/241/(c-1)/2002, dated the 21st March 2002, published at Pages 1-2 of Part II—
Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 21st March 2002 as subsequently amended.
AMENDMENT
In the said Notification, for the expression “31st March 2010”, wherever it occurs, the expression “31st March 2011”
shall be substituted.
EXEMPTION FROM LEVY OF TOLLS FOR IMPROVEMENT AND MAINTENANCE ON A LONG TERM
CONCESSION BASIS FOR CERTAIN PERIOD UNDER THE ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 40, Highways and Minor Ports (HV-2), 1st February 2010,
Thai 19, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No. II(2)/HWMP/68(e-2)/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (b) of sub-section 4   of the Indian Tolls Act, 1851 (Central Act VIII of 1851),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Highways Department Notification
No.II (2)/HW/241/(c-2)/2002, dated the 21st March 2002, published at Page 3 of Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government
Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 21st March 2002 as subsequently amended.
AMENDMENT
In the said Notification, for the expression “31st March 2010”, wherever it occurs, the expression “31st March 2011”
shall be substituted.
