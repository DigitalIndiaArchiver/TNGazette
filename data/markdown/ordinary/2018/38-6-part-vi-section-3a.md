GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI
Taking over the Direct Management of Chota Naksha Charities, Royapettah, Chennai District.  
(Lr. Rc. No. 14049/12/B6/Chennai)
No. VI-3(a)/114/2018.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act 1995, the Tamil 
Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution  item No.85/18 in Rc.No.14049/12/B6/Chennai dated 28-08-2018 has ordered 
to take over the Management of CHOTA NAKSHA CHARITIES, Royapettah, Chennai District under the control of 
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of one year from 28-08-2018 to 27-08-2019 and to appoint the Superintendent of 
Wakfs, Chennai as its Executive Officer.
Chennai - 600 001,	
N.S. MOHAMED ASLAM,
14th September 2018.	
Chief Executive Officer,
	
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
