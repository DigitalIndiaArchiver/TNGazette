Explanatory statement
1.	
Formats of applications for supply of electricity have been specified in ANNEXURE III to the Tamil Nadu 
Electricity Distribution Code. In order to supplement the efforts of the Government of Tamil Nadu in “ The Business 
Reform Action Plan 2017” which requires the required number of documents for availing new HT service connection to 
be reduced to two and further to aid the efforts of Government of Tamil Nadu in improving the ranking of the State of 
Tamil Nadu in the DIPP under “Assessment of State Implementation of Business Reforms 2017”, the Commission, on 
the basis of the request made by TANGEDCO in this regard, finds it expedient and necessary to amend the  Form 4 
specified in Annexure III of the Tamil Nadu Electricity Distribution Code.
2.	
The amendment seeks to achieve the said object.
