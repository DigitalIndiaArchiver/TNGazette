NOMINATION OF CERTAIN LAW CATEGORY PERSON AS MEMBER TO THE SENATE OF TAMIL NADU

Dr. M.G.R. MEDICAL UNIVERSITY ACT.
[G.O. (D) No. 1161, Health and Family Welfare (MCA1), 29th October 2009,
Aippasi 12, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.] 
No. II(2)/HF/570(a)/2009.
Under clause (h) of sub section (3) of Section 18 of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University Act,
1987, (Tamil Nadu Act 37 of 1987) the Chancellor is pleased to nominate Thiru. G. Surulivel, B.Sc., B.L.,
residing at No. 53, Thandavaraya Mudali Street, Old Washermenpet, Chennai representing Law Category
as a “Member” to the Senate of the said University for the residual period up to 3rd March 2011 in the place
of Thiru. Shanmuga Murugan, Advocate.
V.K. SUBBURAJ,
