Provision for the consolidation of Stamp duty chargeable
in respect of issue of policies in the State of Tamil
Nadu by Life Insurance Corporation of India under
Indian  Stamp Act.
[G.O. Rt. No. 65, Commercial Taxes and Registration (J1),
11th February 2009.]
No. II(2)/CTR/105/2009.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by Clause (b) of sub-section (1) of Section 9 of
the Indian Stamp Act, 1899 (Central Act II of 1899), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby provides for the consolidation
of duty of Rs. 2,00,00,000 (Rupees Two crore only) chargeable
under the said Act in respect of issue of policies by the Life
Insurance Corporation of India through its branches in the
State of Tamil Nadu for the period from 1st February 2009
to 31st March 2009.
K. VENKATACHALAM,
Deputy Secretary to Government.

The following errata is issued to the Transport Department
Notification No. II(2)/TRA/775(b)/2007, published at pages 1
and 2 of  Supplement of Part II--Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu
Government Gazette Extraordinary (Issue No. 343), dated 7th
December 2007 relating to Kadambathur Village, Tiruvallur
taluk and district :—
ERRATA
Against, S.No. 91-2, for east by S.Nos. 90-2A and
90-B2, south by S.No. 136-8A read as east by S.Nos. 90-2A
and 90-2B2, south by S.Nos. 136-8 and 136-1A.
Against, S.No. 131-1B, for east by S.No. 131-1A, read
as west by S.No. 131-1A.
