AGRICULTURE DEPARTMENT
Declaration of regulation of Agricultural Produces in the notified areas of Regulated Markets in 21 Markets
Committees under Tamil Nadu Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) Act.
Amendments to Notification
[G.O. Ms. No. 309, Agriculture (AM-2), 28th December 2010.]
No. II(1)/AG/5/2011.—In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (d) of sub-section (I) of Section 9 read with
Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) Act, 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 1989), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Agriculture Department Notification
No. II(1)/AG/60/2008, published at pages 108-114 of Part II-Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, (Issue No. 50),
dated the 24th December 2008.
Any objection or suggestion, which may be received by the Government of Tamil Nadu within a period of one month from
the date of publication of this Notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette will be considered by the Government.
Objection or suggestion, if any, should be addressed to the Agricultural Production Commissioner and Principal Secretary
to Government, Agriculture Department, Secretariat, Chennai-600 009, through the Commissioner of Agricultural Marketing
and Agri Business, Thiru.Vi.Ka. Industrial Estate, Guindy, Chennai-600 032.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Notification,—
(1) In “Annexure - I” under the heading “Agricultural Produces”.
(a) In item 3 oilseeds, for the expression “Coconut (unhusked or husked or kopra)”, the expression Coconut in
all forms except Tender Coconut shall be substituted.
(b) In item 5, Vegetables, for the expression “Green, Banana (Vaalakkai)”, the expression “Green Banana (Valaikkai)”,
shall be substituted;
(2) In Annexure-II, under the heading “Regulated Markets” in THE TABLE in column (3),—
(a) after the entry “14, Nasarethpettai” against the entry in Serial No. 1, in Column (1) and the corresponding
entry “Kancheepuram” in Column (2) thereof, the entry “15 Chengalpattu” shall be added;
(b) for  the entry “12 Kolathur”, against the entry in Serial No. 7, in Column (1) and the corresponding entry “Dharmapuri”
in column (2) thereof, the entry “12. Kambainallur” shall be substituted;
(c) for the entries “10. Kavundampadi”. “11. Gobi”, “13. Vellakkoil”, “14. Saithiyamangalam”, “15. Punjaipulliyampatti”,
“20. Vellangkoil” and “23. Alangeyam” against the entry in Serial No. 9, in column (1) and the corresponding entry “Erode”
in column (2) thereof, the entries “10. Kavundapadi”, “11. Gobichettipalayam”, “13. Vellakovil”, “14. Sathyamangalam”,
“15. Pubjaipuliampatti”, “20. Vellankoil”, “23. Alangiam” shall be substituted;
(d) for the entry “19. Arumbavur”, against the entry in Serial No. 10, in column (1) and the corresponding entry “Trichy”
in column (2) thereof, the entry “19. Kattuputhur” shall be substituted.
P. RAMA MOHANA RAO,
Agricultural Production Commissioner and
Principal Secretary to Government.

Reservation of seats in Educational Institutions and appointments or Posts in the service under the State for Backward
Classes, Backward class Christians,  Backward Class Muslims, Most Backward Classes and Denotified communities.
Amendment to Notification.
[G.O.Ms. No. 6, Backward Classes, Most Backward Classes and Minorities Welfare (BCC), 11th  January 2011,

No.II(1)/BCMBCMW/6/2011.—In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (a) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Backward
Classes, Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes (Reservation of seats in Educational Institutions and of appointments or posts
in the Services under the State) Act, 1993 (Tamil Nadu Act 45 of 1994), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following
amendment to the Backward Classes, Most Backward Classes and Minorities Welfare Department Notification
No. 11(1)/BCMBCMW/36(a)/2008, published at pages 1 to 9 of Part II-Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette,
Extraordinary, dated the 5th September 2008, as subsequently amended.
AMENDMENT.
In the said Notification, in the Schedule, under the heading “IV-List of Most Backward Classes”, for the entry “Narikoravar”
against Sl.No.24, the entry  “Narikoravar (Kuruvikars)” shall be substituted.
     T.N. RAMANATHAN,
Secretary to Government.
