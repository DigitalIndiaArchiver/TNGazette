No. SRO B-60/2017.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu General
Service (Section 16 of Volume II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 2016).
2. The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 15th September, 2016.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules, in Part-II, Under Class XII,-
(1)
in rule 6,—
(i) for the expression, “The provisions of General Rules 7,8 and 31 (a)”, the expression “The provisions of Sections
10,11 and 37 of the Tamil Nadu Government Servants (Conditions of Service) Act, 2016, (Tamil Nadu Act 14 of 2016)” shall
be substituted;
(ii) in the proviso, for the expression “General rules”, the expression “Tamil Nadu Government Servants (Conditions
of Service) Act, 2016” shall be substituted;
(2)
for rule 8, the following rule shall be substituted, namely:-
“8.
Non-applicability of certain provisions of the Tamil Nadu Government Servants (Conditions of Service) Act, 2016.
For appointment to category 1 in the Law Department, Sections 8 and 9 of the Act shall not apply”.
S. SWARNA,
Secretary to Government.

No. SRO B-62/2017.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Adhoc Rules for the Post of Additional Chief
Photographer in the Tamil Nadu General Subordinate Service published with the Public (Information and
Public Relations (Admin-I) Department Notification No.SRO B-162/80 at Pages 176-178 in Part III—Section 1(b) of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette dated the 26th March 1980.
2. The amendment hereby made shall come into force on and from the date of issue of this order.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules.-
(1)
In rule 3, for the entries in item (i), the following entries shall be substituted namely :-
“by promotion from among the holders of the post of Junior Photographer.”
(2)
For rule 5 and the table there under, the following rule shall be substituted, namely,-
“5.
Qualification.- No Person shall be eligible for appointment to the post by promotion unless he possess and
experience for a period of not less than five years as Junior Photographer in Information and Public Relations Department”.
(3)
Rule 6 shall be omitted.
