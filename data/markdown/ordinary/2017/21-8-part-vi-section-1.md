and Country Planning, Chennai Proceeding Roc. No. 5809/2013DPl, dated 21-05-2015 hereby confirms the following
variation to the approved Vaigundaperumal Koil Area Extension Detailed Development Plan Kancheepuram Local
Planning area, approved by the Commissioner of Town and Country planning in proceeding Roc. No. 535/2001/DPl,
dated:30-11-2003 and the fact of this approval in Form No.12 and Published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No.11,
Part VI- Section-1, Page No.81 dated: 24-03-2004, in Publication No.VI(1)/94/2004 and the said draft notification was
published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No.1, Part VI-Section-1, Page No.2, dated: 01-01-2014, Publication No.VI(1)/
1/2014.

same are hereby confirmed and ordered as below.

In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (3) of Section 16 of the code of Criminal Procedure, 1973,
(Central Act 2 of 1974), the Chief Metropolitan Magistrate, Egmore, Chennai hereby authorizes the Fast Track Court at

Department, dated 11-09-2007, to exercise the jurisdiction over the local limits over which, the Metropolitan Magistrate
No.II, X, XIII was hitherto exercising jurisdiction in respect of Criminal cases arising under Section 138 of the Negotiable
Act, and all other Metropolitan Magistrates exercising jurisdiction within the said local limits shall cease to have
jurisdiction to deal with the cases arising under Section 138 of N.I Act with effect from the date of publication of this
notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
