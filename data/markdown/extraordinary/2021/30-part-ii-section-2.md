DECLARATION
No. II(2)/PWD/33(e)/2021.
Whereas it appears to the Government that a total of  2.96.30 hectares of land is required 
in the Puthukudi  Village in Cheranmahadevi Taluk in Tirunelveli District for public purpose, namely 
Inter linking of Tamirabarani, Nambiyaru and Karumeniyar rivers for the  Flood Carrier Canal Project.
Now, therefore a declaration is made hereby that a piece of land measuring 2.96.30  hectares 
is under acquisition for the above said project in the Puthukudi Village in Cheranmahadevi Taluk 
in Tirunelveli District whose detailed description is as follows:

and Transparency in Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Act, 2013 (Central Act 30 of 2013). The number of families likely to be resettled due

acquired, are not needed.
