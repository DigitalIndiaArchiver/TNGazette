In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (c) of sub-section (1), read with  clause (c) of sub-section (2), of Section
2 of the Indian Tolls Act, 1851(Central Act VIII of 1851), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby levies tolls on motor vehicles
for the use of the Madurai  Ring Road between Meenakshi Mission Hospital and Kappalur  after the completion of the
Project of Widening the Ring Road from double lane to four lane by M/s. Sri Balaji Tollways (Madurai) Private Limited on
Build, Operate and Transfer basis, at the rates specified in column (3) of Table I below in respect of the motor vehicle
specified in column (2) thereof.  M/s. Sri Balaji Tollways (Madurai) Private Limited shall collect the tolls as specified in
the Table I below at the locations specified in Table (2) below:-

In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (b) of Section 4 of the Indian Tolls Act, 1851(Central Act VIII of 1851),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby specifies that no tolls shall be paid by the following classes of motor vehicles, for
the use of Madurai  Ring Road between  Meenakshi Mission Hospital  and Kappalur  after the completion of the project
of widening the Ring Road from double lane to four lane by M/s. Sri Balaji Tollways (Madurai) Private Limited on Build,
