MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION AND WATER SUPPLY DEPARTMENT
DRAFT VARIATION TO THE LIST OF PARKS, PLAY FIELDS AND OPEN SPACES IN RESPECT
OF THE AREA WITHIN THE JURISDICTION OF RAJAPALAYAM MUNICIPALITY
UNDER THE TAMIL NADU PARKS, PLAY-FIELDS AND OPEN SPACES (PRESERVATION AND
REGULATION) ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 78, Municipal Administration and Water Supply [MA. 5(1)], 26th May 2016,
Vaikasi 13, Thunmugi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2047.]
No.II(2)/MAWS/345(a)/2016.
WHEREAS ,  in  G.O.(Ms)No.157, Municipal Administration and Water Supply Department, dated 18-08-2008
a list of Parks, Play Fields and  Open Spaces in the area within the Jurisdiction  of the Rajapalayam
Municipality was revised under sub-section (4) of Section 5 of the Tamil  Nadu Parks, Play-fields and Open Spaces
(Preservation and Regulation) Act, 1959 (Tamil Nadu Act 26 of 1959) and the same was published as Municipal
Administration and Water Supply Department Notification No.II(2)/MAWS/407/2008, in pages 299 and 300 of Part
II–Section 2 of  the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 10th September 2008.  In the said list, among
others, an extent 0.08712 acres in S.No.233/4 of Ward 12 was notified as Open Space. Vide Serial Number 4
under the heading ‘Open Spaces’ in the Schedule to the said Notification;
 AND WHEREAS, the Director of Town and Country Planning has requested the Government to delete the
entries against the Sl.No.4 of the Schedule to the said Notification;
 AND WHEREAS, the Director of Municipal Administration has stated that the Municipal Council, Rajapalayam
in its Resolution No.148, dated 30-06-2014 has resolved to send proposal to the Town and Country Planning
Department for deletion of said entries from the said Notification;
AND WHEREAS, the said Property is a private property. But, according to Clause (c) of Section 2 of   said
Act, ‘Open space’ can be any land belonging to the Government or any Local authority only;
AND WHEREAS, the Government after careful examination has decided to accept the above proposal of
Director of Town and Country Planning;
DTP-II-2 Ex. (128)

TAMIL  NADU  GOVERNMENT  GAZETTE  EXTRAORDINARY
  NOW THEREFORE the following draft of variation to the list  of Parks, Play – fields and Open Spaces in respect of the
area within the jurisdiction of Rajapalayam Municipality, revised and published with the Municipal Administration and Water Supply
Department Notification No.II(2)/MAWS/407/2008, in pages  299 and 300 of Part II–Section 2 of  the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the  10th September 2008, which is proposed to made in exercise of the powers
conferred     by  sub- section (1) of the Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Parks, Play – fields and Open Spaces (Preservation and
Regulation) Act, 1959 (Tamil Nadu Act 26 of 1959) is hereby published as required under sub-section (2) of the said
Section 5. Notice is hereby given that objection or suggestion, if any, received in writing, in respect of the following draft variation
from any persons interested within seven days  from  the date of publication of this Notification in the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette will be considered  by the Government of Tamil Nadu. Objection or suggestion if any  should
be addressed to the Principal Secretary to Government, Municipal Administration and Water Supply Department, Secretariat,
Fort St. George, Chennai–600 009.
DRAFT VARIATION
In the said notification in the Schedule, under the Heading “Open Spaces”, Serial Number 4 in Column (1) and the entries
reading thereto in Columns (2) to (9) shall be omitted.
K. PHANINDRA REDDY,
Principal Secretary to Government.
