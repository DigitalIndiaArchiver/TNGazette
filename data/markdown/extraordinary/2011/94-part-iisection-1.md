 DELEGATION OF POWERS TO THE DISTRICT ELECTION OFFICERS OF TAMIL NADU WITHIN
THEIR JURISDICTION FOR THE PURPOSE OF REQUISITION OF PREMISES FOR THE
CONDUCT OF ELECTIONS UNDER THE REPRESENTATION OF THE PEOPLE ACT.
 [G.O. Ms. No. 219, Public (Elections-III), 3rd March 2011.]
No. II(1)/PUE/7(g)/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 166 of the Representation of the People
Act, 1951, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that the powers conferred or duties imposed
on the State Government by the provisions of Sections 160 to 165 of the said Act shall be exercised
and discharged by all the District Election Officers of Tamil Nadu within their jurisdiction for the
purpose of requisition of premises for the conduct of Elections.
PRAVEEN KUMAR,
Chief Electoral Officer and
Principal Secretary to Government.
