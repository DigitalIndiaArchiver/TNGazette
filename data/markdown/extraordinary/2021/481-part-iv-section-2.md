The following Act of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly received the 
assent of the Governor on the 13th October 2021 and is hereby 
published for general information:—
ACT No. 33 OF 2021.
An Act further to amend the Tamil Nadu Government Servants 
(Conditions of Service) Act, 2016.
 Bඍ it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the 
Seventy-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:—
Short title and 
commence-
ment.
1. (1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu Government Servants 
(Conditions of Service) Second Amendment Act, 2021.
 
(2)  (a) Sections 2, 4, 5, 6, 7 and 8 shall be deemed to have come into 
force on the 26th day of February 2021.
  
 
(b) Section 3 shall be deemed to have come into force on the 
7th day of June 2021.
Substitution of 
references 
to certain 
expressions 
by other 
expressions.
2. In the Tamil Nadu Government Servants (Conditions of Service) Act, 2016 
(hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), unless otherwise expressly provided 
for, for the expressions “Most Backward Classes and Denotiﬁ ed Communities” 
and “Most Backward Class and Denotiﬁ ed Community”, wherever they occur, 
with the words therein are in any order and are interpolated with the word “or” or 
“and” or “/”, the expression “Most Backward Classes (V), Most Backward Classes 
and Denotiﬁ ed Communities and Most Backward Classes” shall be substituted.
Tamil Nadu Act 
14 of 2016.
Amendment of 
section 67.
3. In section 67 of the principal Act,—
 
(i) in sub-section (1), for the expression “Personnel and Administrative 
Reforms Department”, the expression “Human Resources Management 
Department” shall be substituted;
 
(ii) in sub-section (3), for the expression “Personnel and Administrative 
Reforms”, the expression “Human Resources Management” shall be substituted.
