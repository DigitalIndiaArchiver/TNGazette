NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
ANIMAL HUSBANDRY, DAIRYING AND FISHERIES DEPARTMENT
DECLARATION OF PROHIBITING THE COASTAL AREAS OF “NO ENTRY ZONE / NO FISHING ZONE” IN
KALPAKKAM IN KANCHEEPURAM DISTRICT AND KUDANKULAM IN TIRUNELVELI DISTRICTS UNDER
THE TAMIL NADU MARINE FISHING REGULATION ACT.
       [G.O. Ms. No. 195, Animal Husbandry, Dairying and Fisheries (FS-4), 31st July 2013, Aadi 15,
Vijaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2044.]
No. II(2)/AHDF/532(c)/2013.
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (a) of sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Marine
Fishing Regulation Act,1983 (Tamil Nadu Act 8 of 1983) and in supersession of Animal Husbandry, Dairying
and Fisheries Department Notification No. II(2)/AHDF/605/2010, published at page 742 of Part II—Section 2
of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 6th  October 2010, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
prohibits the fishing by any kind of fishing vessels in a radius of 500 metres around the atomic installations
including the in-take structure and other structures, which are part and parcel of the atomic installations, in
the coastal areas of Kalpakkam in Kancheepuram District and Kudankulam in Thirunelveli District, in
Tamil Nadu.
S. VIJAYAKUMAR,
Secretary to Government.
