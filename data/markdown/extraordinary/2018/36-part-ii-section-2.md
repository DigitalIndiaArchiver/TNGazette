The Government of Tamil Nadu having been 
satisﬁ ed that the lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below 
have to be acquired for Industrial Purpose, to wit, for 
Second Mine Expansion Scheme of Neyveli Lignite 
Corporation  Limited and it having already been decided 
that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded 
for the lands is to be paid out of the funds controlled or 
managed by Neyveli Lignite Corporation (India)  Limited. 
The following notice is issued under sub-section (1) 
of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for 
Industrial Purposes Act, 1997.
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu 
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Government of 
Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands speciﬁ ed in the 
Schedule below and measuring   0.83.00 hectares 
Dry lands and 1150 sq.meters of R. Manai lands  to 
the same, a little more or less needed for Industrial 
Purposes, to wit, for Second Mine Expansion Scheme 
of Neyveli Lignite Corporation (India)  Limited. 
The plans  of the lands are kept in the ofﬁ ce of the 
Special Deputy  Collector / Land Acquisition, Neyveli-2 
and may be inspected at any  time during ofﬁ ce hours.
