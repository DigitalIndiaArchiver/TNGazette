TAMIL NADU STATE ELECTION COMMISSION, CHENNAI-600 106.
[ELECTIONS - PANCHAYATS - CONDUCT OF - HOURS OF POLL NOTIFIED.]
(S.O. No. 6/2021/TNSEC/PE-1)
Rඍඉඌ:
 (S.O. No. 17/2019/TNSEC/PEI, dated the 16th October 2019)
No. VI(2)/83(a)/2021.
WHEREAS, rule 25 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Elections) Rules, 1995, provides that the hours during which 
the poll will be open for the elections to Panchayats, being not less than ten hours between 7.00 a.m. and 5.30 p.m.; 
WHEREAS, due to prevailing COVID-19 pandemic situation and the guidelines (Standard Operating Procedures) 
are being issued by the Government, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission, as a precautionary measure and 
to facilitate the electors, having temperature more that 98.4°F with COVID-19 symptoms or undergoing treatment for 
COVID-19, to exercise franchise, has decided to extend the hours of poll to 12 hours instead of 10 hours in elections 
to panchayats; and 
WHEREAS, a diﬃ  culty has thus arisen for the extension of hours of poll; 
NOW, THEREFORE, for removing the diﬃ  culty, in exercise of the powers conferred under Article 243K of Constitution 
of India and rule 24 and rule 25 read with rule 143 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Elections) Rules, 1995 and in 
supersession of the Notiﬁ cation No.VI(2)/96 (a-1)/2019, Published in Part VI - Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government 
Gazette Extraordinary No. 397, dated October 21, 2019, at page 1, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission hereby 
ﬁ xes that the hours of poll in elections to Panchayats, shall be between 7.00 A.M. and 7.00 P.M. on the day notiﬁ ed 
for poll. 
(By Order of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission)
