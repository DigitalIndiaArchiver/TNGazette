MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION AND WATER SUPPLY DEPARTMENT
REMOVAL OF CHAIRMAN OF PERANAMALLUR TOWN PANCHAYAT IN THIRUVANNAMALAI DISTRICT
UNDER TAMIL NADU DISTRICT MUNICIPALITIES ACT.
[G.O. (D) No. 162, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (TP2), 29th April 2011.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/174(c)/2011.
Whereas, Thiru V. Mohan, Vice-Chairman of Peranamallur Town Panchayat, Thiru. D. Ettiyappan, Thiru
Duraimurthy, Thiru V.P. Babu, Thiru. Pitchandi, Tmt. Bhavani and Tmt. Pushpa members of the said Town
Panchayat have given a complaint petition to the Assistant Director of Town Panchayats, stating that
Tmt. V. Prema, Chairman of Peranamallur Town Panchayat had prepared a false resolution No. 46,
dated 20-3-2009 with out actually convening the meeting to accord permission for sale of 59 plots in unapproved
layout establishing as Sri Selva Vinayagar Nagar in Survey Nos. 273/1A, 1B (2) and 274 in Peranamallur Town
panchayat, Thiruvannamalai District and sold the lands in the unapproved layout against the Government rules,
on her own and registered the same and thereby caused revenue loss to the extent of Rs. 2.00 lakhs to the
said Town Panchayat. The Assistant Director of Town Panchayat, Vellore Region had conducted an enquiry on
the said complaint and submitted a report. The District Collector, Thiruvannamalai District had examined the
report and requested the Director of Town Panchayats in his letter dated 22-10-2009, to take action against Tmt.
Prema under sub-section (1) of Section 40 of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 through
Government, since the allegation against her had been proved;
And, Whereas, Thiru Ettiyappan and 6 other members have filed Writ Petition No. 658/2010  in the
High Court, Chennai to take appropriate action to remove Tmt. V. Prema from the post of Chairman of
Peranamallur Town Panchayat, as per the proceedings of District Collector Na.Ka. No. 3675/09/E,
dated 22-10-2009. The High Court in its order dated 12-01-2010 has directed the second respondent (Director
of Town Panchayats) to give an opportunity of hearing to the writ petitioners and the Chairman of the
Peranamallur Town Panchayat and to pass orders on the representation of the petitioner, dated 12-11-2009,
not later than 12 weeks from the date of receipt of a copy of that order;

she told that her letter pad was misused by somebody and she has nothing to do with it and hence legal action
should be taken;
And, Whereas, the Director of Town Panchayats has requested the Government to take action on the basis of the said
enquiry and as per the letter dated 22-10-2009 of the District Collector, Thiruvannamalai District, against Tmt. V. Prema,
Chairman of Peranamallur Town Panchayat, under sub-section (1) of Section 40 of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities
Act, 1920 and to issue appropriate orders.
And Whereas, a show cause notice was issued in Government letter dated 1st November 2010, under sub-section (2) of
Section 40 of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 regarding the removal of Chairman of Peranamallur Town
Panchayats;
And Whereas, Thirumathi V. Prema, Chairman of Pernamallur Town Panchayat has stated that she does not know about
the legal aspects and the mistake has been committed without her knowledge in the approval of layout and she is ready to
compensate the loss incurred to the said Town Panchayat and has admitted the mistake and requested to pardon her;
And Whereas, the Government have carefully considered the proposal of the Director of Town Panchayats and the
explanation of the said Chairman and are of the opinion that Thiumathi V. Prama, Chairman of Peranamallur Town Panchayat,
Thiruvannamalai District, has abused her powers, by registering unapproved layout with false documents;
Now, Therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 40 of the Tamil Nadu District
Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby removes Thirumathi V. Prema, from
the office of the Chairman of Peranamallur Town Panchayat, Thiruvannamalai District.
