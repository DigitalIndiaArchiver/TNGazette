No.I/PUSP/20/2018.—The following Notification of the 
Government of India, Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances 
and Pensions; Department of Personnel and Training New Delhi, 
dated 27th April 2018, is republished:-
In exercise of the powers conferred by Rule 6(1) of the 
Indian Administrative Service (Cadre) Rules, 1954 and with 
the concurrence of the Government of Uttar Pradesh and 
the Government of Tamil Nadu, the Central Government 
hereby accords approval for inter cadre deputation of 
Ms. Pinky Jowel, IAS (UP:2003) from Uttar Pradesh cadre 
to Tamil Nadu cadre, for a period of three years from the 
date of assumption of charge or until further orders, whichever 
is earlier.
[Notification No. 13017/39/2017-AIS(I)]
