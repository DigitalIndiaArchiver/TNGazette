APPROVED THE CONSTITUTION OF THE ACADEMIC COUNCIL AND NOMINATED  CERTAIN MEMBERS
FOR CERTAIN PERIOD UNDER TAMIL NADU TEACHERS EDUCATION UNIVERSITY ACT, 2008
(TAMIL NADU ACT 33 OF 2008) .
[G.O. (ID) No. 342, Higher Education (K2), 7th December 2012, Karthigai 22, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2043.]

In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 22(1) of the Tamil Nadu Teachers Education University
Act, 2008 (Tamil Nadu Act 33 of 2008), His Excellency the Governor-Chancellor has approved the constitution
of the Academic Council and nominated the following members for a period of three years with effect from
4th December 2012.
