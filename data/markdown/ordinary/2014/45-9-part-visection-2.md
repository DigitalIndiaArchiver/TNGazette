TAMIL NADU STATE LEGAL SERVICES AUTHORITY, CHENNAI
Nomination of Chairman for the High Court Legal Services Committee
(TNSLSA No. 6163/E/2014.)
No.VI(2)/271/2014.
In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (2) of Section 8A of the Legal Services Authorities Act 1987,
(Central Act 39 of 1987) the Hon’ble the Chief Justice, High Court, Madras is pleased to nominate the Hon’ble Mr. Justice
V. Dhanapalan, Judge, High Court, Madras to be the Chairman of the High Court Legal Services Committee with effect from
6th November 2014.
High Court Campus, Chennai-600 104.
RMT. TEEKAA RAMAN
11th November 2014.
District Judge /
Member-Secretary (FAC).
