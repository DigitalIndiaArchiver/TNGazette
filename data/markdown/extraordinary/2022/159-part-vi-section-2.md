TAMIL NADU STATE ELECTION COMMISSION, CHENNAI-600 106.
[ELECTIONS-INDIRECT ELECTIONS-URBAN LOCAL BODIES - ELECTION OF CHAIRMAN AND VICE CHAIRMAN OF 
ADUTHURAI TOWN PANCHAYAT OF THANJAVUR DISTRICT – CONDUCT OF NOTIFIED.]
 	
(S.O.No.25/2022/TNSEC/ME-II,  dated the 11th March 2022)
No.VI(2)/31(a)/2022.
In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (1) of section 43-B and section 27 of the Tamil Nadu District 
Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920) and sub-rule (3) of rule 99 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, 
Municipalities and Corporation (Elections) Rules, 2006, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission hereby fixes 
26-3-2022 as the date for convening a meeting to  elect the Chairman and Vice Chairman of Aduthurai Town Panchayat 
of Thanjavur District in accordance with the following programme:-
1.
Last date for service of notice for the meeting of 
election of Chairman and Vice Chairman 
17-03-2022
2.
Statutory interval
18-03-2022 to 25-03-2022
3.
Date for conduct of meeting for the election of Chairman 
and Vice Chairman
26-03-2022
(By Order of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission)
