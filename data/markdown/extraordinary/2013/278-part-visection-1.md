MEDICAL COUNCIL OF INDIA
ELECTION OF ONE MEMBER TO THE MEDICAL COUNCIL OF INDIA
 PRECEPT.
(No. 27686/MEIII/2/2013)
No. VI(-1)/379(a-1)/2013
     Notice is hereby given pursuant to the provision of section 3 (1) (c) of the Indian Medical Council Act, 1956,
(Central Act 102 of 1956) that the election of one member to the Medical Council of India to serve for a term of four years to be
held and the Electorate is called upon to elect the member.
Nomination of eligible persons to fill  up  the vacancy is invited.
Each candidate must be nominated by separate nomination paper but any person entitled to vote at the election may sign
the nomination paper of candidate and for which he is entitled to vote. Every Nomination paper must be in Form II giving all the
details as required there in. The Nomination paper can be downloaded from the website.www.tnhealth.org.
The Nomination paper must reach the undersigned not later than 5.00 p.m. on Thursday,  the 3rd  October 2013 from
whom nomination paper may be obtained on application. Nomination paper which are not received by the Returning Officer  by
the aforesaid date and time will be invalid. Each candidate standing for election shall deposit or caused to be deposited
with the Returning Officer the sum of Rs.50/- (Rupees fifty only) in cash or by Demand Draft in favour of the
Returning Officer / DME-MCI Election, payable at Chennai on or before 3rd October 2013  (3.00 P.M.) and no candidate shall
be deemed to be duly nominated unless such deposit has made. The deposit is not refundable.
