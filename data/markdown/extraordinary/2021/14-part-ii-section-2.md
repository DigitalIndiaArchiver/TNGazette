In the Government Order ﬁ fth read above, among other things, Cinemas/theatres/Multiplexes were permitted to open 
from 10.11.2020 with 50% of their seating capacity by following the Standard Operating Procedure.
2. Based on the decrease in the COVID-19 cases day by day, the Theatre Owners Association have represented 
to Government that Train, Bus, Air Transport, Shopping Complex and Film Shooting etc. Have been permitted with 
relaxations, and taking into consideration of the welfare of the Theatre Workers, to increase the seating capacity of 
Cinemas/theatres/Multiplexes from 50% to 100%. Accordingly, in G.O.Ms.No.001, Revenue and Disaster Management 
(DM-IV) Department, dated 4.1.2021, Notiﬁ cation was issued to permit to increase the seating capacity from existing 
50% to 100% in Cinemas/theatres /Multiplexes by following the Standard Operating Procedure.
3. In view of the Ministry of Home Aﬀ airs, Government of India, Guidelines, the Government hereby cancel the 
notiﬁ cation issued in GO.Ms.No.001, Revenue and Disaster Management (DM-IV) Department, dated 4.1.2021 and order 
that Cinemas/theatres /Multiplexes shall be permitted to function with 50% of their seating capacity, by following the 
Standard Operating Procedures until further orders.
4. Further, as per the direction of the Hon'ble High Court of Madras, theatres were permitted to screen additional 
shows.
