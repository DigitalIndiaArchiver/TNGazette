LOSS OF CERTIFICATES
It is hereby notiﬁ ed that the Original Higher Secondary Certiﬁ cates / Matriculation Certiﬁ cates / Diploma Certiﬁ cates / School 
Certiﬁ cates of the following pupils are reported to have been lost beyond recovery.  Necessary steps are being taken to issue the 
duplicate of the said Certiﬁ cates. If the Originals were to be found by anybody, they should be treated as invalid and sent to the 
Secretary, Board of Higher Secondary Examination / Directorate of Government Examinations, Chennai-6 Director of Technical 
Education, Chennai-25 / Inspector / Inspectress of Matriculation and Anglo-Indian Schools as the case may be for cancellation:–
