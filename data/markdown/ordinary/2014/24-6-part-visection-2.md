TAMIL NADU ELECTRICITY REGULATORY COMMISSION
Amendment to the Tamil Nadu Electricity Supply Code
[Notification  No. TNERC / SC /7-35 dated 03-06 -2014]
No. VI(2)/152/2014.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 181 read with Section 50 of the Electricity Act, 2003(Central Act 36 of
2003) and all other powers enabling it in this behalf, the Tamil Nadu Electricity Regulatory Commission hereby makes the
following amendments to the Tamil Nadu Electricity Supply Code, the draft of the same having been previously published
as required under sub-section (3) of the said section.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Regulations, in regulation 22, -
(1)
sub-regulation (4) shall be omitted;
(2)
in sub-regulation (5), for the expression “total arrears due”,
the expression “all the arrears / charges due from the consumer” shall be substituted;
(3)
in sub-regulation (6),—
(i)
in clause (i), -
(a)
after the word “disconnected”, the
expression “for more than six months” shall be inserted;
(b)
the expression “beyond the notice period of
three months” shall be omitted;
(ii) in clause (ii) the expression “in accordance with sub-regulation
(4)” shall be omitted.
(By Order of the Tamil Nadu Electricity Regulatory Commission)
S. GUNASEKARAN,

EXPLANATORY STATEMENT
The Hon’ble High Court of Madras in its order dated 30-10-2013 in WP.No.29504 of 2013 suggested to frame guidelines
or instructions so as to enable the enforcing authorities to invoke regulation 22 of the Tamil Nadu Electricity Supply Code,
2004 in proper perspective in the matter of granting instalments for payment of arrears due to the licensee.  Accordingly,
it has been decided to allow payment of arrears in instalments by revoking the termination of agreement notice issued to
the consumer in cases where a service connection remains disconnected for more than six-months for non-payment of
electricity charges by a consumer and who comes forward to remit 40% of the dues and charges within the period mentioned
in regulation 22(6)(ii) of the said Code and whose lines feeding service connection have not been dismantled.
2.0 The amendment seeks to give effect to the above decision.
