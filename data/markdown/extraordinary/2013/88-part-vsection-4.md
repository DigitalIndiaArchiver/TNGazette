ORDER OF THE ELECTION COMMISSION OF INDIA
DISQUALIFICATION OF CANDIDATES CONTESTED FROM 77. ULUNDURPETTAI, 80. KALLAKURICHI (SC), 193. MADURAI
CENTRAL, 189. MADURAI EAST, 190. SHOLAVANDAN (SC), 196. THIRUMANGALAM AND 197. USILAMPATTI ASSEMBLY
CONSTITUENCIES IN THE GENERAL ELECTIONS TO TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY HELD IN APRIL, 2011
FOR FAILURE TO LODGE ACCOUNTS OF ELECTION EXPENSES UNDER SECTION 10A OF THE REPRESENTATION
OF THE PEOPLE ACT, 1951.
No. SRO G-7/2013.
The following order of the Election Commission of India, Nirvachan Sadan, Ashoka Road, New Delhi-110 001, dated
4th March 2013 [13 Phalguna, 1934 (Saka)] is republished.
No. 76/TN-LA/2011.—Whereas, the Election Commission of India is satisfied that each of the contesting candidate
specified in column 4 of the Table below at the General Election to Legislative Assembly, 2011 from the State of Tamil Nadu
specified in column 2 and held from the constituency specified in column 3 against his/her name has failed to lodge the
account of his/her election expenses as shown in column 5 of the said Table as required by the Representation of the People
Act, 1951 and the Rules made thereunder.
And whereas, the said candidates have either not furnished any reason or explanation for the said failure even after due
notice by the Election Commission or after considering the representation made by them, if any, the Election Commission is
satisfied that they have no good reason or justification for the said failure.
Now, therefore, in pursuance of Section 10A of the said Act, the Election Commission hereby declares the persons
specified in column 4 of the Table below to be disqualified for being chosen as and for being a member of either House of
Parliament or the Legislative Assembly or Legislative Council of a State or Union Territory for a period of three years from
the date of this order:—
