HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE DEPARTMENT
Amendment to the Ad hoc Rules relating to Tamil Nadu Medical Service
[G.O. (D). No. 907, Health and Family Welfare (A1), 3rd August 2009, Aadi 18, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. SRO B-48/2009.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the rules published with the Health and Family
Welfare Department Notification No. SRO B-42/2009, at page 76 of Part III—Section 1(b) (Issue No. 26) of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the July 8, 2009, Aani 24, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.
The amendment hereby  made shall be deemed to have been come into force on the 21st April 1987.
AMENDMENT
In the said Notification, in rule 1, for the expression "Associate Reader in Respiratory Diseases", the expression
“Reader in Paediatrics (Respiratory Diseases)” shall be substituted.
V.K. SUBBURAJ,
Principal Secretary to Government.
HOME DEPARTMENT
Erratum
[Letter No. 4428/Police-17/2009-1, Home (Police-XVII), 31st July 2009.]
The following erratum is issued to the orders issued in G.O. Ms. No. 1224, Home (Pol.-17) Department, dated
24th September 2008:—
ERRATUM
In G.O. Ms. No. 1224, Home (Pol.17) Department, dated 24th September 2008, under the heading “READ”, the date
of G.O. shall be substituted as 19th August 1998 for the date 19th August 2008.
S. MALATHI,
