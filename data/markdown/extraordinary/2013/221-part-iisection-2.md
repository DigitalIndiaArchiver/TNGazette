NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
COMMERCIAL TAXES AND REGISTRATION DEPARTMENT
DATE OF COME INTO FORCE OF THE INDIAN STAMP (TAMIL NADU FOURTH AMENDMENT) ACT, 2012
TAMIL NADU ACT 16 OF 2013.
[G.O. Ms. No. 106, Commercial Taxes and Registration (J1), 31st July 2013.]
No. II(2)/CTR/532(a)/2013.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (3) of Section 1 of the Indian Stamp  (Tamil Nadu Fourth
Amendment) Act, 2012 (Tamil Nadu Act 16 of 2013), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the
1st day of August 2013 as the date on which the said Act shall come into force.
SUNIL PALIWAL,
Secretary to Government.
