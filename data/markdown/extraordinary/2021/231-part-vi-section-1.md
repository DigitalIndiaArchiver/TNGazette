The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that the Wet lands specified in the schedule below and 
measuring to an extent of 23614 Sq.metres under Chennai Peripheral Ring Road Project for Highways purpose for laying 
Northern Port Access Road at (Km 9/70 to Km 11/930) in Nalur I (Block-II) Village, Ponneri Taluk, Tiruvallur District, and 
it having already been decided that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands to be paid out of 
the funds controlled and managed by the Divisional Engineer(C&M), Tiruvallur, Tamil Nadu after having considering the 
cause shown by the owner or other persons having interest on such lands as the case may be do hereby publish the 
following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (TN Act 34 of 2002).
NOTIce
	
Under sub-section (1) of section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) Read 
with Tamil Nadu Land Acquisition Laws(Revival of Operation, Amendment and Validation) Act, 2019, the Governor of 
Tamil Nadu hereby notify that the Wet land specified in the schedule below and measuring to an extent 23614 Sq.metres 
of lands are acquired in No.98, Nalur-I (Block-II) Village in Ponneri Taluk, Tiruvallur District, to the same, a little more 
or less needed for Chennai Peripheral Ring Road (Km 9/70 to Km 11/930)
The plan of the lands under acquisition are kept in the Office of the Special District Revenue Officer (Land Acquisition), 
Tamil Nadu Road Sector Project–II, Guindy, Chennai-32 and Office of the Special Tahsildar (Land Acquisition), Chennai 
Peripheral Ring Road Project, Unit I, No.98, in Nalur-I (Block-II) Village, Ponneri, Tiruvallur District may be inspected 
at any time during the office hours.
