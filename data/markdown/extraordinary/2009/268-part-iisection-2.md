ACQUISITION OF LAND
[G.O. Ms. No. 188, Municipal Administration and Water

Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/522(a)/2009.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied
that the land specified in the schedule below has to be
acquired for the public purpose and it having already been
decided that the entire amount of compensation to be
awarded for the land is to be paid out of the public revenue,
the following Declaration is issued under Section 6 of the
Land Acquisition Act, 1894 (Central Act I of 1894):—
DECLARATION
Under Section 6 of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894
(Central Act I of 1894), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
declares that the land specified in the schedule below and
measuring 0.48.5 hectare, be the same, a little more or
less, is needed for a public purpose, to wit, for the
construction of Sump and Pump House in No. 4. Veerapandi
Village of Coimbatore North Taluk, Coimbatore District.
A plan of the land/lands is kept  in the Office of the
Revenue Divisional Officer, Coimbatore may be inspected
at any time during the office hours.
THE SCHEDULE
Coimbatore District, Coimbatore North Taluk,
No. 4. Veerapandi Village.
Ryotwari, dry, S.F. No. 575-1A, belonging to Ramanaicken
Thanneer Pandal Dharmam,  bounded on the north by
S.F. No. 574, east by S.F. No. 575-1B, south by S.F. No. 572-2
and west by Gudalur Village—0.48.5 hectare.
NIRANJAN MARDI,
Secretary to Government.
