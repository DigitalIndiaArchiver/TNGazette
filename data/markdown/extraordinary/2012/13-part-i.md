ALLOCATION OF SUBJECTS AMONG MINISTERS
[G.O. Ms. No. 43, Public (Special-B), 18th January 2012, Thai  4, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2043.]
No. I/PUSP/4(a-1)/2012.
Under Clause (3) of Article 166 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs the allocation
of portfolios among the Ministers as follows:—
(i)
The portfolios “Commercial Taxes & Registration and Stamp Act” hitherto held by Thiru Agri S.S. Krishnamoorthy
be allocated to Thiru C.Ve. Shanmugam and he be designated as Minister for Commercial Taxes & Registration, Law,
Courts and Prisons. Thiru C.Ve. Shanmugam will continue to hold the portfolios of “Personnel and Administrative Reforms
and Prevention of Corruption”.
(ii) The portfolios of “School Education and Archaeology, Sports and Youth Welfare, Tamil Official Language and
Tamil Culture” be allocated to Thiru Agri S.S. Krishnamoorthy and he be designated as Minister for School Education and
Sports & Youth Welfare.
(iii) The portfolios of “Industries, Steel Control, Mines & Minerals and Special Initiatives” hitherto held by
Thiru S.P. Velumani be allocated to Thiru P. Thangamani and he be designated as Minister for Industries.
(iv) The portfolios of “Revenue, District Revenue Establishment, Deputy Collectors, Weights and measures, Debt
Relief including legislation on Money lending, Chits, Registrartion of Companies” hitherto held by Thiru P. Thangamani be
allocated to Thiru S.P. Velumani and he be designated as Minister for Revenue.
DEBENDRANATH SARANGI,
Chief Secretary.
