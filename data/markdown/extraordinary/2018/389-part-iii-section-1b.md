AMENDMENTs TO the ad hoc rules relating to the Temporary post of Librarian and 
Information Officer in Anna Centenary Library in Public Libraries Department in 
Tamil Nadu Educational Service
[G.O. Ms. No. 230, School Education (PL2), 8th November 2018, Aippasi 22,  
Vilambi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2049.]
No. SRO B-95(a-1)/2018.
 In exercise of the powers conferred by the provisio to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor 
of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Ad hoc Rules published in the School Education 
Department Notification SRO No. B-16(a-1)/2016, published at Pages 1 and 2 in Part III – Section 1(b) of the 
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette dated 23rd February, 2016.
2. (a)   Amendments (1) and (4) shall be deemed to have come into force on 15th September, 2016.
     (b) 	  Amendments (2) and (3) shall be deemed to have come into force on  the  3rd March, 2010.
Amendments
In the said rule,
(1)	in the preamble to the rules, for the expression “The General” the expression “The Tamil Nadu Government 
Servants (Conditions of Service) Act, 2016 (Tamil Nadu Act of 14 of 2016)” shall be substituted.
(2)	rule 3, in item (ii), for the expression “direct recruitment by transfer” the expression “recruitment by transfer’ 
shall be substituted.

No. SRO B-95(a-2)/2018.
In exercise of the powers conferred by the provisio to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu 
hereby makes the following amendments to the Ad hoc Rules published with the School Education Department Notification 
SRO No. B-16(a-2)/2016, published at Pages 3 and 4 in Part III – Section 1(b) of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette 
dated 23rd February, 2016.
2.   (a)  Amendments (1) and (3) shall be deemed to have come into force on 15th September, 2016.
	
 (b)  Amendments (2) shall be deemed to have come into force on the 3rd March, 2010.
Amendments
In the said rule,
	
(1)	in the preamble to the rules, for the expression “The General” the expression “The Tamil Nadu Government Servants 
(Conditions of Service) Act, 2016 (Tamil Nadu Act of 14 of 2016)” shall be substituted.
	
(2)	after rule 3, the following rule shall be inserted namely,-
    	
“3A. The posts are within the purview of Tamil Nadu Public Service  Commission”.
	
(3)	in rule 6 for the expression “the rule relating to reservation of appointment (General Rule 22)” the expression 
“The provisions contained in Section 27 of the Tamil Nadu Government Servants (Conditions of Service) Act, 2016 
(Tamil Nadu Act 14 of 2016)” shall be substituted.
