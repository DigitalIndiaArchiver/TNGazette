The Governor of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that the
lands specified in the Schedule below have to be acquired
for Highways purpose to wit for laying  Road over Bridge in
lieu of Level Crossing No.279 situated between Samuthiram
and Manapparai Railway Station and it having already been
decided that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded
for the  lands is to be  paid out of the funds controlled and
managed by Divisional Engineer (Highways),Tiruchirappalli.
The following Notice is issued under sub-section(1) of Section
15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act,2001. (Tamil Nadu
Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu
Highways Act,2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002), the Governor
of Tamil Nadu  hereby acquires the lands specified in the
Schedule below and measuring an extent of 0.41.75.0 hectares
to the same, a little more or less required  for laying  Road
over Bridge  in lieu of Level Crossing No. 279 situated between
Samuthiram and Manapparai  Railway Station in Manapparai
Taluk, Tiruchirappalli  District.
The plan of the  lands is kept in the office of the District
Collector,  Tiruchirappalli   and may be inspected  at any time
during office hours.
