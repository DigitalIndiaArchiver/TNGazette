BILL INTRODUCED IN THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF THE
STATE OF TAMIL NADU
Under Rule 130 of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Rules, the following Bill
which was introduced in the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu on
17th February 2014 is published together with Statement of Objects and Reasons
for general information:—
L.A. Bill No. 2 of 2014
A Bill to amend the Chennai City Police (Extension to the
Cities of Salem, Tiruchirappalli and Tirunelveli) Act, 1997.
BE it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in
the Sixty-fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
1. (1) This Act may be called the Chennai City Police  (Extension to the
Cities of Salem, Tiruchirappalli and Tirunelveli) Amendment Act, 2014.
(2)  It shall come into force on such date as the State Government
may, by notification, appoint.
2.  In section 2 of the Chennai City Police (Extension to the  Cities of
Salem, Tiruchirappalli and Tirunelveli) Act, 1997, for clause (1), the following
clause shall be substituted, namely:-
“(1)” City of Salem “means the area declared by the State Government,
by notification, to be the City of Salem;”.

STATEMENT OF OBJECTS AND REASONS.
During the Collectors’ and Police Officers’ Conference held on 19-12-2012,
the Hon’ble Chief Minister has announced that Kondalampatti, Steel Plant,
Veeranam Police Stations and 14 Mother Villages adjoining Salem City will
be annexed to the Salem City Police limit. In order to give effect to the above
said announcement, the Government have decided to amend clause (1) of
section 2 of the Chennai City Police (Extension to the Cities of Salem,
Tiruchirappalli and Tirunelveli) Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 51 of 1997) on the
lines of definition of the term  ‘City of Chennai’ in the Chennai City Police
Act, 1888 (Tamil Nadu Act III of 1888).
2. The Bill seeks to give effect to the above decision.
