OBITUARY NOTICE
[G.O. Rt. No. 2654, Public (Special-B), 15th July 2015, Aani 30, Manmatha, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2046.]
No.I/PUSP/10/2015.—The Government of Tamil Nadu record with deep and profound regret the untimely death of one
of its Senior Officers, Thiru A. Mohammed Aslam, I.A.S., Commissioner, Minorities Welfare, Chennai on 15th July, 2015 at
the Age of 59 years.
2. He Started his career as Probationary Deputy Collector during 1987 and got conferment in the Indian Administrative
Service on 13-12-1999. He held various posts in Government of Tamil Nadu which includes Revenue Divisional Officer,
Tirunelveli District, District Backward Classes Welfare Officer, Vellore, District Revenue Officer Villupuram, Collectors
Villupuram / Virudhunagar Districts, Joint Secretary to Government, Transport Department, Commissioner Most Backward
Classes and Minorities Welfare, Indian Medicine and Homeopathy, Project Director Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan and Commissioner,
Minorities Welfare, Chennai.
3. In the death of Thiru A. Mohammed Aslam, IAS, the Government of Tamil Nadu have lost one of its senior officers.
The Government places on record its condolence to the bereaved family members.
K. GNANADESIKAN,
   Chief Secretary.
