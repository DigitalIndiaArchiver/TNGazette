No.SRO B-50/2018.-In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of 
the India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Rules published with the 
Finance Department Notiﬁ cation No.SRO-B/149/86, published at pages 165 to 173 of Part III — Section 1 (b) of the

Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖග
 
In the said Rules, in rule 8, in sub-rule (b), in the Table, for the entries in column (3) against the entry 
"(i) Direct recruitment" in column (2) and the corresponding entry "3. Junior Co-operative Auditor" in column (1), the following 
entries shall be substituted, namely:-
 
 
"Must possess B.B.M., (Bachelor of Bank Management) degree or B.Com., degree or B.Com.,  (Computer 
Application) degree or B.A. (Co-operation) degree or Diploma in Rural Services awarded by the Gandhigram Rural University 
or B.A. (Economics) degree or B.A. (Corporate Secretaryship) degree or B.B.E., degree awarded by any University recognized 
by University Grants Commission".
K. SHANMUGAM,
Additional Chief Secretary to Government.
