PUBLIC DEPARTMENT

APPOINTMENT OF THE UNDER SECRETARY TO GOVERNMENT (ESTATE) PUBLIC DEPARTMENT
AS THE ESTATE OFFICER OF THE MAIN BUILDING, NAMAKAL KAVINGAR MALIGAI, FORT ST. GEORGE,
CHENNAI-9.
[G.O. Ms. No. 350, Public (Buildings), 13th May 2014.]
No. II(2)/PUBG/321(b)/2014.
In exercise of the powers conferred by section 3 of the Tamil Nadu  Public  Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised
Occupants) Act, 1975 (Tamil Nadu Act 1 of 1976), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the Under Secretary
to Government (Estate), Public Department, Chennai-9 as the Estate Officer of the Main building, Namakal Kavingar
Maligai and all other areas within Fort St.George over which the Chief Secretary having Administrative Control to
exercise the powers conferred and perform the duties imposed by or under this Act.
MOHAN VERGHESE CHUNKATH,
Chief Secretary.
