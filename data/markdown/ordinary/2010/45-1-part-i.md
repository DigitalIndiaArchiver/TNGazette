ENVIRONMENT AND FORESTS DEPARTMENT
OBITUARY NOTICE
[G.O. Ms. No. 154, Environment and Forests (FR-SPL-A),
21st October 2010.]
No. I/EF/25/2010.— The Government of Tamil Nadu record
with deep and profound regret the untimely demise of one of their
able and good officers, Thiru Ajay Singh Panwar, IFS, Conserva-
tor of Forests on 19th July 2010 at the age of 46 years.
2. He was appointed to the Indian Forest Service on 2nd June
1986. He served in various capacities in the Forests Department
of the State.
3. The Government of Tamil Nadu desire to place on record
their appreciation of the invaluable services rendered by
Thiru Ajay Singh Panwar, IFS and convey their condolence to the
bereaved family.
V. IRAI ANBU,
Secretary to Government.
