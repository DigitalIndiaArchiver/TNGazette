for the public purpose, for the formation of Bye Pass Road in Arcot Town in Arcot Taluk in the District of Ranipet through 
Highways Department, and it has already been decided that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands

Vellore -1, after having considered the cause shown by the owners or the other persons having interest on such lands as the 
case may do hereby publish the following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways 
Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002).
Notice

acquired the lands specified in the schedule below and measuring to an extent of 6994 sq.mtrs. to the same, a little more or 
less is needed for the purpose for the formation of Bye Pass Road in Arcot Town comprised in S.No. 3/1 and etc., at Arcot 
Town, Arcot Taluk, Ranipet District. The plan of the lands are kept in the office of the Revenue Divisional Officer’s Office, 
Ranipet may be inspected on any working day during office hours.
