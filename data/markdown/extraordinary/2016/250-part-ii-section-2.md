APPOINTMENT OF SPECIAL OFFICERS FOR TOWN PANCHAYATS UNDER THE TAMIL NADU DISTRICT
MUNICIPALITIES ACT.

Aippasi 8, Thunmugi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2047.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/687(c-1)/2016.
WHEREAS the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission has issued a notification for the purpose of filling up of ordinary
vacancies in the offices of ward members / councillors of Town Panchayats, Municipalities and Corporations and called upon
the electors to elect their respective ward members / councillors in accordance with the programme specified therein;
AND WHEREAS the Hon’ble High Court of Madras in its interim order, dated the 4th October 2016, in W.P.No.33984/2016
has, among others, quashed the Election Notifications issued for the conduct of Local Body Election by the Tamil Nadu State
Election Commission and directed to issue fresh notifications, conduct election and complete the election process as per law,
at the earliest not later than the 31st December 2016 and to administer the Local Bodies by appointment of Special Officers till
the elections are held, as the present terms of the present Local Bodies are to expire soon and the same cannot be extended
beyond five years;
AND WHEREAS the Director of Town Panchayats and the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission have requested the
Government to appoint Special Officers to administer the Town Panchayats as the terms of councils are due to expire on the
24th October 2016;
AND WHEREAS the Governor of Tamil Nadu has promulgated the Tamil Nadu Municipal Laws (Third Amendment) Ordinance,
2016 (Tamil Nadu Ordinance 2 of 2016), whereby section 375-B was inserted in the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920
(Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920) enabling the State Government to appoint Special Officers to the Town Panchayats;
AND WHEREAS, in view of the circumstances aforesaid, it is necessary to appoint Special Officers to exercise the powers
and to discharge the functions of the Town Panchayats under the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act
V of 1920), until the day on which the first meeting of the councils of the Town Panchayats are held after ordinary elections to
the Town Panchayats, after the date of commencement of the said Ordinance or until the 31st day of December 2016, whichever
is earlier;
NOW, THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred by section 375-B of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act,
1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints,-

APPOINTMENT OF SPECIAL OFFICERS TO MUNICIPALITIES UNDER THE TAMIL NADU DISTRICT MUNICIPALITIES ACT.
[G.O. (Ms) No. 146, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (Election), 24th October 2016,
Aippasi 8, Thunmugi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2047.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/687(c-2)/2016
WHEREAS the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission has issued a notification for the purpose of filling up of ordinary
vacancies in the offices of ward members / councillors of Town Panchayats, Municipalities and Corporations and called upon
the electors to elect their respective ward members / councillors in accordance with the programme specified therein;
AND WHEREAS the Hon’ble High Court of Madras in its interim order, dated the 4th October 2016, in W.P.No.33984/2016
has, among others, quashed the Election Notifications issued for the conduct of Local Body Election by the Tamil Nadu State
Election Commission and directed to issue fresh notifications, conduct election and complete the election process as per law,
at the earliest not later than the 31st December 2016 and to administer the Local Bodies by appointment of Special Officers till
the elections are held, as the present terms of the present Local Bodies are to expire soon and the same cannot be extended
beyond five years;
AND WHEREAS the Commissioner of Municipal Administration and the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission have
requested the Government to appoint Special Officers to administer the Municipalities as the terms of councils are due to expire
on the 24th October 2016;
AND WHEREAS the Governor of Tamil Nadu has promulgated the Tamil Nadu Municipal Laws (Third Amendment) Ordinance,
2016 (Tamil Nadu Ordinance 2 of 2016), whereby section 375-B was inserted in the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920
(Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920) enabling the State Government to appoint Special Officers to the Municipalities;
AND WHEREAS, in view of the circumstances aforesaid, it is necessary to appoint Special Officers to exercise the powers
and to discharge the functions of the Municipalities under the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of
1920), until the day on which the first meeting of the councils of the Municipalities are held after ordinary elections to the
Municipalities after the date of commencement of the said Ordinance or until the 31st day of December 2016, whichever is earlier;
NOW, THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred by section 375-B of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920
(Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the Commissioners of the Municipalities as the Special
Officers of the respective Municipalities.
2. This Notification shall come into force on the 25th October 2016.

Aippasi 8, Thunmugi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2047.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/687(c-12)/2016
WHEREAS the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission has issued a notification for the purpose of filling up of ordinary
vacancies in the offices of ward members / councillors of Town Panchayats, Municipalities and Corporations and called upon
the electors to elect their respective ward members / councillors in accordance with the programme specified therein;
AND WHEREAS the Hon’ble High Court of Madras in its interim order, dated the 4th October 2016, in W.P.No.33984/2016
has, among others, quashed the Election Notifications issued for the conduct of Local Body Election by the Tamil Nadu State
Election Commission and directed to issue fresh notifications, conduct election and complete the election process as per law,
at the earliest not later than the 31st December 2016 and to administer the Local Bodies by appointment of Special Officers till
the elections are held, as the present terms of the present Local Bodies are to expire soon and the same cannot be extended
beyond five years;
AND WHEREAS the Commissioner of Municipal Administration and the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission have
requested the Government to appoint Special Officers to administer the Corporations as the terms of councils are due to expire
on the 24th October 2016;
AND WHEREAS the Governor of Tamil Nadu has promulgated the Tamil Nadu Municipal Laws (Third Amendment) Ordinance,
2016 (Tamil Nadu Ordinance 2 of 2016), whereby section 9-A was inserted in the Thoothukudi City Municipal Corporation Act,
2008 (Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 2008) enabling the Government to appoint Special Officer to the Thoothukudi City Municipal
Corporation;
AND WHEREAS, in view of the circumstances aforesaid, it is necessary to appoint Special Officer to exercise the powers
and to discharge the functions of the said Corporation under the Thoothukudi City Municipal Corporation Act, 2008 (Tamil Nadu
Act 27 of 2008), until the day on which the first meeting of the council of the said Corporation is held after ordinary elections
to the said Corporation after the date of commencement of the said Ordinance or until the 31st day of December 2016, whichever
is earlier;
NOW, THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred by section 9-A of the Thoothukudi City Municipal Corporation Act,
2008 (Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 2008), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the Commissioner of Thoothukudi City Municipal
Corporation as the Special Officer of the said Corporation.
2. This Notification shall come into force on the 25th October 2016.
