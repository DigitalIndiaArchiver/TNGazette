HOME DEPARTMENT
Amendments to the Tamil Nadu Home Guards Welfare and Benevolent Fund Rules.
[G.O. Ms. No. 933, Home (Pol. XIV), 21st December 2015.]
No. SRO A-1/2016.—The following amendments are issued to the Tamil Nadu Home Guards Welfare and Benevolent
Fund Rules, 1990 published with the Home Department Notification No.SRO A-111/90 at pages 136-137 in
Part III—Section 1(a) of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 1st August 1990.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules, in rule 4, in clause (a),
(1) for sub-clauses (i) to (v), the following sub-clauses shall be substituted, namely:-
“(i) One time lumpsum grant to the dependents of the deceased members of the Home Guards who die while
on duty. The maximum financial assistance shall be Rs.15,000/-;
(ii) One time lumpsum grant to the injured or disabled members of the Home Guards who got injured
or disabled while on duty. The maximum financial assistance shall be Rs.10,000/-;.
(iii) One time lumpsum grant to the members of the Home Guards in distress situation, while on duty.
The maximum financial assistance shall be Rs.5,000/-;-
(iv) One time lumpsum grant in deserving cases, to the children or deceased or disabled members of the
Home Guards for educational purposes like purchasing of books, stationery upto a maximum of two children to the
maximum of Rs.2,000/- per child upto school level and Rs.4,000/- each at college level;
(v) One time lumpsum grant for marriage of daughter of deceased or disabled members of the Home Guards
who died or got disabled while on duty. The maximum financial assistance shall be Rs. 7,500/-”; and
(2) in sub-clause (vi), for item (a), the following item shall be substituted, namely:-
“(a) One time lumpsum grant for the purpose of purchasing machines for self employment to
the widow, family of deceased or disabled members of the Home Guards for their livelihood in deserving cases.
The maximum financial assistance shall be Rs.5,000/-;”.
APURVA VARMA,
Principal Secretary to Government.
