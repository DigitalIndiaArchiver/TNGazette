AGRICULTURE  DEPARTMENT
APPOINTMENT OF CERTAIN PERSON AS VICE-CHANCELLOR OF TAMIL NADU AGRICULTURAL UNIVERSITY,
COIMBATORE FOR CERTAIN PERIOD UNDER THE TAMIL NADU AGRICULTURAL UNIVERSITY ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 186, Agriculture (AU), 31st August 2012.]
No. II(2)/AG/548(e)/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-sections (1) and (3) of Section 11 of the Tamil Nadu Agricultural
University Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 8 of 1971), the Chancellor of the Tamil Nadu Agricultural University hereby
appoints Dr. K. Ramasamy, Vice-Chancellor, Karpagam University, Coimbatore to be the Vice-Chancellor of the Tamil
Nadu Agricultural University, Coimbatore for a period of three years with effect from the date of his assumption of
charge.
SANDEEP SAXENA.
 Agricultural Production Commissioner and
Secretary to Government.
