ORDER:  No. 520, Public (Special-B), 25th May 2016.
Under Clause (3) of Article 166 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that :
(i) Thiru Sellur K.Raju shall continue to hold the portfolios of  “Co-operation, Statistics and Ex-Servicemen Welfare”.
Thiru Sellur K.Raju be designated as Minister for Co-operation.
(ii) Thiru R.Kamaraj shall continue to hold the portfolios of “Food, Civil Supplies, Consumer Protection and Price
Control”. Thiru R.Kamaraj be designated as Minister for Food & Civil Supplies.
(iii) Thiru O.S.Manian shall continue to hold the portfolios of “Handlooms & Textiles”. Thiru O.S.Manian be designated
as Minister for Handlooms & Textiles.
(iv)Thiru R.Doraikkannu shall continue to hold the portfolios of “Agriculture, Agricultural Engineering, Agro Service
Co-operatives, Horticulture, Sugarcane Cess, Sugarcane Development and Waste Land Development”. Thiru R. Doraikkannu
be designated as Minister for Agriculture.
(By Order of the Governor)
K. GNANADESIKAN,
Chief Secretary.
