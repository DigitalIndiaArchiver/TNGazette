 Amendments to Tamil Nadu Contract Labour (Regulation and Abolition) Rules.
[G.O. Ms. No. 61, Labour and Employment (H1), 3rd June 2009.]
No. SRO A-23/2009.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 35 of the Tamil Nadu Contract Labour (Regulation
and Abolition) Act, 1970 (Central Act 37 of 1970), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments
to the Tamil Nadu Contract Labour (Regulation and Abolition) Rules, 1975, the draft of the same having been previously
published as required by sub-section (1) of section 35 of the said Act:—
 AMENDMENT
In the said Rules,—

be substituted.
