HIGHER EDUCATION DEPARTMENT
EXTENSION OF THE TIME LIMIT FOR THE CONSTITUTION OF THE SYNDICATE AND ACADEMIC COUNCIL
FOR CERTAIN PERIOD UNDER THE ANNA UNIVERSITY TECHNOLOGY, CHENNAI ACT .
[G.O. (1D) No. 281, Higher Education (I-1), 17th December 2010, Margazhi 2, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No. II(2)/HE/738(b-1)/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of  Section 56 read with Section 18 of the
Anna University of Technology, Chennai Act, 2010 (Tamil Nadu Act 26 of 2010), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
extends the time limit for the constitution of the Syndicate and Academic Council within the further period of six months
with effect from 19th December 2010.
EXTENSION OF THE TIME LIMIT FOR THE CONSTITUTION OF THE SYNDICATE AND ACADEMIC COUNCIL
FOR CERTAIN PERIOD UNDER THE ANNA UNIVERSITY TECHNOLOGY, MADURAI ACT .
[G.O. (1D) No. 282, Higher Education (I-1), 17th December 2010, Margazhi 2, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No. II(2)/HE/738(b-2)/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 56 read with Section 18 of the
Anna University of Technology, Madurai Act, 2010 (Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 2010), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
extends the time limit for the constitution of the Syndicate and Academic Council within the further period of six months
with effect from 19th December 2010.
K. GANESAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
