No. II(2)/PUSP/787(a)/2016.
The Government of Tamil Nadu announce with profound regret, the demise of
Selvi J Jayalalithaa, Hon'ble Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu at 11.30 p.m. on Monday, the
5th December, 2016. State Mourning  will be observed for seven days from 6th December 2016,
during which period the National Flag on all Government Buildings will be flown at half-mast.
No official entertainment will also be held during this period.
(By Order of the Governor)
P. RAMA MOHANA RAO,
Chief Secretary.
