NOTICE UNDER SECTION 15(1) OF TAMIL NADU HIGHWAYS ACT, 2001 (TN ACT 34 OF 2002) READ WITH THE 
TAMIL NADU LAND ACQUISITION LAWS (REVIVAL OF OPERATION, AMENDMENT AND VALIDATION) ACT, 2019. 
No. VI(1)/488(c)/2022.
The Governor of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that the additional lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below have to be 
acquired for Highways Department to wit for strengthening and widening of SH-30  Malliakarai - Attur Road and it having 
already been decided that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the strengthening and widening is to be 
paid out of the funds controlled and managed by Divisional Engineer, TNRSP-II, Salem after considering the cause shown by 
the owner or other Persons having interest on such lands as the case may be do hereby publish the following notice  under 
sub-section 1 of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act No. 34 of 2002)     
NOTICE
 Under sub-section 1 of section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002), read with the 
Tamil Nadu  Land Acquisition Laws (Revival of Operation, Amendment and Validation) Act, 2019, the Governor of Tamil Nadu 
hereby declares that the additional lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below and measuring 98 Sq.mt in Thandavarayapuram 
Village of Attur Taluk, Salem District to the same, a little more or less needed for Highways, to wit for the strengthening and 
widening  in SH - 30 Malliakarai - Attur Road.
The plan of the lands are kept in the Oﬃ  ce of the Special District Revenue Oﬃ  cer (LA), Tamil Nadu Road Sector 
Project-II, Salem and Oﬃ  ce of the Special Tahsildar (LA)TNRSP - II,  Salem may be inspected at any time during oﬃ  ce hours.
