RE-ALLOCATION OF SUBJECTS AMONG MINISTERS.
[G.O. Ms. No. 264, Public (Special-B), 19th March 2010,
Panguni 5, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No. I/PUSP/3(a)/2010.
Under Clause (3) of Article 166 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby directs that the subject “Welfare of the Disabled” hitherto held by
Tmt. Geetha Jeevan, Minister for Social Welfare be renamed as “Welfare of the Differently
Abled” and the said subject be reallocated to Chief Minister in addition to the subjects
held by him.
2.
Under Clause (3) of Article 166 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby directs that the subject “Personnel and Administrative Reforms” hitherto
held by Thiru Arcot N. Veeraswami, Minister for Electricity and the subject “Prisons” hitherto
held by Thiru I. Periasami, Minister for Revenue and Housing be reallocated to
Thiru Duraimurugan, Minister for Law in addition to the subjects held by him. Consequent
on the above, Thiru Duraimurugan, be re-designated as Minister for Law, Courts and Prisons.
K.S. SRIPATHI,
Chief Secretary.
