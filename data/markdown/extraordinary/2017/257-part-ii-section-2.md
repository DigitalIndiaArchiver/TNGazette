APPOINTMENT OF COMMISSION OF INQUIRY TO INQUIRE INTO CAUSES AND CIRCUMSTANCES LEADING
TO THE LAW AND ORDER DISTURBANCES WHICH TOOK PLACE ON 23rd JANUARY 2017 AT CHENNAI,
MADURAI, COIMBATORE AND OTHER PARTS OF THE STATE FOLLOWING THE AGITATION FOR
HOLDING OF JALLIKATTU UNDER THE COMMISSIONS OF INQUIRY ACT.
AMENDMENT TO NOTIFICATION
[G.O. Ms. No. 664, Public (Law and Order-F), 1st August 2017, Aadi 16, Hevilambi,
Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2048.]
No. II(2)/PULO/595(e)/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Commissions of Inquiry Act, 1952
(Central Act LX of 1952), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Public
(Law and Order-F) Department Notification No.ll(2)/PULO/76(f)/2017, published at pages 1-2 of
Part ll—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 31st January 2017, as
subsequently amended.
AMENDMENT
In the said Notification, in paragraph 4, for the expression “six  months”, the expression “nine months”
shall be substituted.
    MYTHILI K. RAJENDRAN,
    Principal Secretary to Government (FAC).
