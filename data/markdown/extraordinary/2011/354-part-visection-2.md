NOTIFICATIONS  BY  HEADS  OF  DEPARTMENTS,  ETC.
TAMIL NADU STATE ELECTION COMMISSION, CHENNAI.
[ELECTIONS—ORDINARY ELECTIONS—CORPORATIONS, MUNICIPALITIES/THIRD GRADE MUNICIPALITIES AND TOWN
PANCHAYATS — COUNCILLORS/MEMBERS AND MAYORS/CHAIRMEN—CONDUCT OF—NOTIFIED.]
(S.O.No.45/2011/TNSEC/ME-1.)
No. VI(2)/468(a-1)/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (1) of Section 43-B of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities  Act, 1920
(Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920), sub-section (1) of Section 6-1 of the Chennai City Municipal Corporation Act, 1919 (Tamil Nadu Act
IV of 1919), sub-section (1) of Section 10-D of the Madurai City Municipal Corporation Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act XV of 1971),
sub-section (1) of Section 10-D of the Coimbatore City Municipal Corporation Act, 1981 (Tamil Nadu Act 25 of 1981) read with
Section 8 of the Tirunelveli City Municipal Corporation Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act  28 of 1994) Section 8 of the Salem City Municipal
Corporation Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 29 of 1994),  Section 8 of the Tiruppur City Municipal Corporation Act, 2008 (Tamil Nadu
Act 7 of 2008), Section 8 of the Erode City Municipal Corporation Act, 2008 (Tamil Nadu Act 8 of 2008), Section 8 of the Vellore
City Municipal Corporation Act, 2008 (Tamil Nadu Act 26 of 2008) and Section 8 of the Thoothukudi City Municipal Corporation Act,
2008 (Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 2008) and rule 22 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities, Municipalities and
Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission, for the purpose of filling up of ordinary vacancies
in the offices of the ward Members/Councillors and Chairmen/Mayors of Town Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities/ Municipalities
and Corporations specified in the Tables annexed to this Notification, hereby calls upon the electors of the said wards to elect their
respective Ward Members/Councillors and Chairmen/Mayors of Town Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities/Municipalities and
Corporations, in accordance with the following programme of election:—
PROGRAMME  OF  ELECTION
1. Publication of public notice of election and
commencement of receipt of nominations
22-09-2011
11.00 a.m. to 3.00 p.m.
2. Last date for making nominations
29-09-2011
3. Scrutiny of nominations
30-09-2011
11.00 a.m.
4. Withdrawal of candidatures
03-10-2011
Upto 3.00 p.m.
5. Dates of Poll
Phase -I
17-10-2011
7.00 a.m. to 5.00 p.m.
Phase -II
19-10-2011
7.00 a.m. to 5.00 p.m.
6. Date of commencement of counting of votes
21-10-2011
8.00 a.m.
7. Date before which the election shall be completed
23-10-2011
8. Date on which the first meeting of the newly elected
ward Members/Councillors and Chairmen/Mayors shall
25-10-2011
be held for assumption of office by taking oath or affirmation
9. Date on which the meeting of the ward members/Councillors
shall be held for the election of  deputy Mayor or
29-10-2011
Vice-Chairmen, as the case may be.
(By Order of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission)
Chennai-600 106,
V.M. XAVIER CHRISSO NAYAGAM,
21st September 2011.
Secretary,
Tamil Nadu State Election Commission.
