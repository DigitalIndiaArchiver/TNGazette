Errata to Notification
The following Errata are issued to the Notification published under Part VI-Section 2 in the Extraordinary issue of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette (Issue No.289) dated the 7th October 2013:—
(i) On page 1, under the heading of Notification No.VI(2)/207(a-1)/2013, in the first line, the phrase “Ordinary
Elections to Rural Local Bodies October 2011” shall read as ”Ordinary Elections to Urban Local Bodies October 2011”;
(ii) On page 327, under the heading of Notification No.VI(2)/207(a-27)/2013, in the first line, the phrase “Ordinary
Elections to Urban Local Bodies October 2011” shall read as “Ordinary Elections to Rural Local Bodies October 2011”.
