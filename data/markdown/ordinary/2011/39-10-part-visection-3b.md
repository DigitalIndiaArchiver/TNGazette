Nomination of a Member by the Government to the Board of Management of
Tamil Nadu Veterinary and Animal Sciences University.
(U.O.No. 60102/R.I/BOM/2011 - F.No. 6019/R.I/BOM/2011.)
No. VI-3(b)/26/2011.
It is hereby notified that as per sub-section (2), Section 18 under heading "CLASS-II OTHER MEMBERS" (b), Chapter IV
of Tamil Nadu Veterinary and Animal Sciences University Act, 1989 (Tamil Nadu Act 42 of 1989), Thiru R. Gunasekaran,
AISHWARYA GROUP, 4th K.M. Mohanur Road, Sanyasi Karadu, Vagurampatty, Namakkal-637 001, has been nominated by
the Government of Tamil Nadu to serve as a member in the Board of Management of Tamil Nadu Veterinary and Animal
Sciences University. His term shall be three years with effect from 5th September 2011 as per Section 18(5), Chapter IV of
Act, 1989 (Tamil Nadu Act 42 of 1989).

(Rc. No. EI(2)/1531/2011.)
No. VI-3(b)/27/2011.
Under Sub-Statute (1) of Statute 5 of Chapter XXI of the General Statutes of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University,
Chennai, It is hereby published that the following members have been elected to the Senate of the
Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai, Election of one Professor to the Senate by the Teachers of each
affiliated Medical College from among themselves under Section 18(3) (a) of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University,
Chennai Act, 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 37/1987).
The membership will be for a period of three years as noted against each or until they hold the post of Professor
of the concerned affiliated College of this University, or till the date of their superannuation whichever is earlier.
