RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND PANCHAYAT RAJ DEPARTMENT
AMENDMENT TO THE TAMIL NADU PANCHAYATS (PUBLIC LANDING PLACES, HALTING PLACES 
AND CART-STANDS) RULES, 
[G.O. Ms. No. 70, Rural Development and Panchayat Raj (PR-1), 31st May 2018] 
No. SRO A-30(a)/2018.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 154 and clause (xxiv) of sub-section (2) of Section 242 of 
the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994), the Governor of  Tamil Nadu hereby makes 
the following amendment to the Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Public landing places, halting places, cart-stands)
Rules, 1999:-
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in rule 7, in the TABLE, in column (2), for the expression “Minimum rate per diem", the 
expression “Minimum rate per day” shall be substituted.
 ERRATUM TO NOTIFICATION 
[Letter No. 13904/PR-1/2014-8, Rural Development and Panchayat Raj (PR-1), 31st May 2018] 
The following Erratum is issued to the Rural Development Department Notiﬁ cation No. SRO A-64(a-8)/99, 
published at pages 15-16 of Part III-Section 1(a) of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the
1st September 1999:-
ERRATUM
In the said Notiﬁ cation, in the preamble, the expression "sub-section 154", shall be read as "section 154".
