APPOINTMENT OF THIRU JUSTICE HEMANT LAXMAN GOKHALE, CHIEF JUSTICE OF ALLAHABAD HIGH COURT AS
CHIEF JUSTICE OF THE MADRAS HIGH COURT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 213, Public (Special-A), 4th  March 2009,
Maasi 20, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. I/PUSP/(b)/2009.
The following Notification of the Government of India, Ministry of Law and Justice (Department of Justice),
Jaisalmer House, 26, Man Singh Road, New Delhi-110 011, dated 25th February 2009 is republished:—
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (1) of Article 222 of the Constitution of India, the President,
after consultation with the Chief Justice of India, is pleased to transfer Shri Justice Hemant Laxman Gokhale,
Chief Justice, Allahabad High Court as Chief Justice of the Madras High Court and to direct him to assume charge
of the office of the Chief Justice of the Madras High Court on or before 12th March 2009.
