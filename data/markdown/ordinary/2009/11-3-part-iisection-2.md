HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE DEPARTMENT
Declaration of Government Dharmapuri Medical College,
Dharmapuri as Teaching Medical Institution under
Tamil Nadu Anatomy Act.
[G.O. (3D) No. 6, Health and Family Welfare (Z1),
3rd March 2009, Maasi 19, Thiruvalluvar Aandu 2040.]
No. II(2)/HF/137/2009.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by clause (e) of Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu
Anatomy Act, 1951 (Tamil Nadu Act 18 of 1951), the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby declares Government Dharmapuri
Medical College, Dharmapuri, to be a Teaching Medical
Institution for the purposes of the said act.
V.K. SUBBURAJ,
Principal Secretary to Government.

LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT DEPARTMENT
Secretariat, 25th March 2009.
Orders on Awards of Special Industrial Tribunal in respect of Industrial Disputes between
workmen and managements.
No. II(2)/LE/142/2009.
The following Government Order is published:—
[G.O. (Rt.) No. 112, Labour and Employment (D2),16th February 2009.]
READ:
1. G.O. (D) No. 688, Labour and Employment Department, dated 13-9-01.
2. G.O. (D) No. 570, Labour and Employment Department, dated 19-7-02.
3. G.O. (D) No. 562, Labour and Employment Department, dated 30-5-03.
4. Interim Awards passed by the Special Industrial Tribunal, Chennai-6 as per the details listed in the Annexure.
Whereas, the Government had referred the Industrial Dispute between the Workmen and the Managements of certain
Textile Mills in Tamil Nadu pertaining to the Charter of demands, for adjudication by the Special Industrial Tribunal, Chennai-6,
under Section 10(1)(d) read with Section 10(5) of the Industrial Disputes Act, 1947;
And, Whereas, the Interim Awards passed by the Special Industrial Tribunal in the above Industrial Dispute cases, in
respect of the Textile Mills mentioned in Col. (4) of the Annexure, on the date mentioned in Col. (6) of the Annexure, accepting
the Settlement they had entered into with their Workmen represented by the Union/Elected Representatives, mentioned in
Col. (5) of the Annexure, have been received by the Government;
Now, Therefore, in exercise of powers conferred under sub-section (1) of Section 17 of the
abovesaid Act, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that the said Interim Awards be published in the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
S. ARUMUGAM,
Deputy Secretary to Government.
