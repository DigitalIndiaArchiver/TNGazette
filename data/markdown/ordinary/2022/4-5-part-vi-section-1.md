(Roc. No. 08/2022/D5)
No. VI(1)/31/2022.
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 13 (2) of the Tamil Nadu HR & CE Act, 1959, the Commissioner, 
HR & CE (Admn.) Department, Chennai hereby delegates the powers and duties conferred on him under Section 79 (C) of 
the said Act to the Joint Commissioners subject to the following conditions:- 
 
(a) that it shall be subject to the provisions of the said Act and the rules framed under sub-section (2) of Section 13, 
 
(b) that the powers and duties imposed' on the Commissioner under Section 79 (C) of the Act may be exercised 
by the Commissioner also. 
 
(c) that the Commissioner shall have right of exercising the powers vested under Section 21 or 22 of the Act over 
the orders passed by the Joint Commissioner under Section 79 (C) of the Act.
