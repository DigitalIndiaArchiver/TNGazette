No. SRO B-1/2015.
In exercise of the  powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Industries Subordinate Service
(Section 16 in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Service Manual, 1970).
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on 2nd December 2014.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules, in the Annexure, in column (3) against “Class 1” in column (1) and the “Junior Engineer
(Industries)” in column (2) ,-
(1) in item 1, for the entries beginning with the expression “(1) Must possess a diploma” and ending with the
expression “Director of Technical Education and”, the following expression shall be substituted, namely:-
“(1)
Must possess a Diploma of any Discipline except Civil Engineering and Architectural Engineering
awarded by the State Board of Technical Education and Training, Tamil Nadu or of a recognized
Institution or Board; and”;
(2) in item 2, for the entries beginning with the expression “(1) Must possess a diploma” and ending with the
expression “Director of Technical Education and” the following expression shall be substituted, namely:-
“(1)
Must possess a diploma in any discipline except Civil Engineering and Architectural Engineering
awarded by the State Board of Technical Education and Training, Tamil Nadu or of a recognized
Institution or Board; and”.
KUMAR JAYANT,
Secretary to Government.
PUBLIC WORKS DEPARTMENT
Amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Engineering Subordinate Service.

No. SRO B-2/2015.
In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Engineering Subordinate Service
(Section 8 in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Service Manual, 1970).
2. The amendment hereby made shall come into force with effect on and from the date of issue of this order.
AMENDMENT
In the said Special Rules, in Part II, under the heading “Branch I - Engineering Branch”, in Schedule II, for the entries
in column (2) against the entry “Assistant Draughtsman” in column (1) thereof, the following entries shall be substituted,
namely,-
“(i)
A pass in the Trade Test Draughtsman Course, namely National Trade Certificate or National
Apprenticeship Certificate in the Industrial Training Institute conducted by the Director of Employment
and Training, Chennai and issued by the  Government of India, Ministry of Labour, National Council
for Vocational Training; or
(ii)
Diploma in Civil, Mechanical, Electrical or in Architecture awarded by the State Board of Technical
Education and Training, Chennai”.
N.S. PALANIAPPAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
