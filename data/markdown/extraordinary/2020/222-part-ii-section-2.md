The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that 
the lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below have to be acquired 
for industrial purpose to wit for the expansion of Vellore Airport 
in Abdullapuram Village, under RCS – UDAN Scheme for 
Airport Authority of India and it having already been decided 
that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the 
lands are to be paid out of the lands controlled or managed by 
Government. The following notice is issued under sub-section 
(1) of section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for 
Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under Sub – Section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu 
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil 
Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby 
acquires the lands speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below and 
measuring 4.15.95 hectares to the same, a little more or less 
needed for industrial purposes, to wit, for the expansion of 
Vellore Airport in Abdullapuram Village, under RCS – UDAN 
Scheme for Airport Authority of India.
  The plan of the lands are kept in the oﬃ  ce of the Tahsildar, 
Vellore and may be inspected at any time during oﬃ  ce hours.
