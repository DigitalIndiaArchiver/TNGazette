JUDICIAL NOTIFICATIONS
Amendments to the Madras High Court Service Rules, 2015.
(Roc. No. 9A/2015/RAC)
No. SRO C-2/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Article 229 (1) and (2) of the Constitution of India and of all other powers
hereunto enabling, the Hon’ble the Chief Justice is pleased to make the following amendments to the Madras High Court
Service Rules, 2015:-
AMENDMENT-I
In Rule 14(h) of the Madras High Court Service Rules, 2015, after the existing entries in the para to second proviso,
commencing with the words “The members in Category-3 of Division - II, viz., Interpreter. ... “ and ending with the
words “ ... Liaison Officer (Protocol).”, the following paragraph shall be added, as a separate para:
“The member in Category 4 of Division - II viz., Private Secretary to the Registrar General, also be considered,
if his/her Seniority reach the seniority level of members under consideration for such promotion from Category-2 of
Division Il viz., Senior Private Secretary to Hon’ble Judges / Private Secretary to Hon’ble Judges / Personal Assistant
to the Hon’ble Judges including Personal Assistant to the Hon’ble the Chief Justice and Private Secretary to the Hon’ble
the Chief Justice. In such event of promotion of Private Secretary to the Registrar General, he/she shall be accommodated
from out of the ratio of ‘1’ allocated to Category-2 of Division II viz., Senior Private Secretary to Hon’ble Judges / Private
Secretary to Hon’ble Judges / Personal Assistant to the Hon’ble Judges including Personal Assistant to the Hon’ble the
Chief Justice and Private Secretary to the Hon’ble the Chief Justice.”
AMENDMENT-II
In the Madras High Court Service Rules, 2015, in the existing para to second proviso of Rule 14(h), commencing
with the words “However, on such promotion ...” and ending with the words “ ... and Librarian, in that selection”, the words
occurring as “categories 1 to 3 of Division II” shall be substituted as “Categories 1 to 4 of Division - II.”
AMENDMENT-III
In Rule 14(j) of the Madras High Court Service Rules, 2015, after the existing entries, the following entries shall be
added:-
“The member in Category 4 of Division - II viz., Private Secretary to the Registrar General, also be considered,
if his/her Seniority reach the seniority level of members under consideration for such promotion from Category-2 of
Division II viz., Senior Private Secretary to Hon’ble Judges / Private Secretary to Hon’ble Judges / Personal Assistant
to the Hon’ble Judges including Personal Assistant to the Hon’ble the Chief Justice and Private Secretary to the Hon’ble
the Chief Justice. In such event of promotion of Private Secretary to the Registrar General, he/she shall be accommodated
from out of the ratio of’ ‘1’ allocated to Category-2 of Division II viz., Senior Private Secretary to Hon’ble Judges / Private
Secretary to Hon’ble Judges / Personal Assistant to the Hon’ble Judges including Personal Assistant to the Hon’ble the
Chief Justice and Private Secretary to the Hon’ble the Chief Justice.”
AMENDMENT-IV
Rule 14(1) of the Madras High Court Service Rules, 2015, shall be substituted as follows:-
“Promotion to Category-6B of Division-I viz., Sub Assistant Registrar (Insolvents, Accounts) shall be from
Category-1 of Division-Il viz. Court Officer/Section Officer / Appeal Examiner, including Section Officer (Property), Court Fee
Examiner, Deputy Sheriff and Liaison Officer (Protocol); Category-2 of Division-II viz. Senior Private Secretary to Hon’ble
Judges/Private Secretary to Hon’ble Judges/Personal Assistant to the Hon’ble Judges including Personal Assistant to the
Hon’ble the Chief Justice and Private Secretary to the Hon’ble the Chief Justice; Category-3 of Division-II viz. Interpreter
and Category-4 of Division - II viz., Private Secretary to the Registrar General. The vacancy shall be filled up in rotation
from the above said feeder categories, i.e., continuing from the vacancy filled up during immediate preceding selection.
However, the members in Category 3 of Division-II viz., Interpreter and Category-4 of Division-II viz., Private Secretary
to the Registrar-General shall be considered, only if their seniority reach the seniority level of the members under

consideration for such promotion from Category-2 of  Division-Il, viz., Senior Private Secretary to Hon’ble Judges / Private
Secretary to Hon’ble Judges / Personal Assistant to the Hon’ble Judges including Personal Assistant to the Hon’ble the
Chief Justice and Private Secretary to the Hon’ble the Chief Justice.
Explanation:
(a) If in a particular selection, the staff member from one of the feeder cadre’s viz., Category-1 of Division - II,
viz., Court Officer/Section Officer/Appeal Examiner including Section Officer (Property), Court Fee Examiner, Deputy Sheriff
and Liaison Officer (Protocol), has been promoted as Sub Assistant Registrar (Insolvents, Accounts), the subsequent
promotion to this post, shall be from the next feeder category, viz., Category - 2 of Division - II, viz., Senior Private
Secretary to Hon’ble Judges / Private Secretary to Hon’ble Judges / Personal Assistant to the Hon’ble Judges including
Personal Assistant to the Hon’ble the Chief Justice and Private Secretary to the Hon’ble the Chief Justice, thereafter from
Category- 3 of Division-II viz., Interpreter and thereafter from Category-4 of Division-II viz., Private Secretary to the Registrar
General.
(b) However, if the seniority level of staff member in either Category-3 of  Division - II viz., Interpreter or that of
Category-4 of Division - II, viz., Private Secretary to the Registrar General, does not reach the seniority level of the
members under consideration for such promotion from  Category-2 of Division-Il, viz., Senior Private Secretary to Hon’ble
Judges / Private Secretary to Hon’ble Judges / Personal Assistant to the Hon’ble Judges including Personal Assistant
to the Hon’ble the Chief Justice and Private Secretary to the Hon’ble the Chief Justice, their turn in the rotation for
promotion shall lapse and the rotation in cycle to continue.”
AMENDMENT-V
In Rule 14(m) of the Madras High Court Service Rules, 2015, after the existing entries, the following shall be added:-
“In case, at a given point of time, if less than three vacancies arise, such arising vacancies shall be filled up in
rotation, i.e., continuing from the vacancy filled up during the immediate preceding selection.
However, members in Category 3 of Division-II viz., Interpreter and Category-4 of Division-II viz., Private Secretary
to the Registrar-General shall be considered, only if their seniority reach the seniority level of the members under
consideration for such promotion from Category-2 of  Division-II, viz., Senior Private Secretary to Hon’ble Judges / Private
Secretary to Hon’ble Judges / Personal Assistant to the Hon’ble Judges including Personal Assistant to the Hon’ble the
Chief Justice and Private Secretary to the Hon’ble the Chief Justice.
In such event of promotion, Category-3 of Division-II, viz., Interpreter, shall be accommodated from out of the ratio
of ‘2’ allocated to  the Category-1 of Division-Il viz., Court Officer/Section Officer/Appeal  Examiner, including Section Officer
(Property), Court Fee Examiner,  Deputy Sheriff and Liaison Officer (Protocol), and Category-4 of Division-II, viz., Private
Secretary to the Registrar General, shall be accommodated  from out of the ratio of ‘1’ allocated to the Category-2 of
Division - II viz., Senior Private Secretary to Hon’ble Judges / Private Secretary to Hon’ble Judges / Personal Assistant
to the Hon’ble Judges including Personal Assistant to the Hon’ble the Chief Justice and Private Secretary to the Hon’ble
the Chief Justice.”
The above amendments shall come into force with effect from 03-02-2017.
