Tamil Nadu is satisﬁ ed that public interest requires that the 
"Critical Piping for High Pressure Boilers, Pressure Vessels, 
Fittings, etc." Industry should be declared as a public utility 
service for the purposes of the Industrial Disputes Act, 1947

etc." Industry to be a public utility service for the purposes 
of the said Act for a period of six months with eﬀ ect on 
and from the date of publication of this Notiﬁ cation in the 
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
