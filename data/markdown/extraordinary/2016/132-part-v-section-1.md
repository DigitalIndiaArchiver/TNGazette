NOTIFICATION PARTIALLY RESCINDING  THE  EARLIER  NOTIFICATION No. 90.
DATED APRIL 22, 2016 IN RESPECT OF 134. ARAVAKURICHI AND
174. THANJAVUR ASSEMBLY CONSTITUENCIES
Secretariat, May 28, 2016.
No. SRO D-5/2016.
Whereas, the General election to the Legislative Assembly of Tamil Nadu was called by the Governor of Tamil Nadu
on 22nd April, 2016, by the Notification under section 15 of the Representation of the People Act, 1951; and
Whereas, on the same date, the Election Commission also issued notification under sections 30 and 56 of the said
Act fixing, inter alia, 16th May, 2016, as the day on which a poll shall be taken in 134-Aravakurichi and 174-Thanjavur Assembly
Constituencies, among others; and
Whereas,  subsequently,  the  Commission  has,  for the  reasons  stated  in  its  two  orders  No. 464/TN-LA/2016, dated
14th  May, 2016 and 15th  May 2016, deferred the date of poll in the said 134-Aravakurichi and 174-Thanjavur Assembly
Constituencies, scheduled to be taken on the 16th May, 2016 (Monday), to 23rd May, 2016 (Monday) initially; and again for the
reasons stated in its order No. 464/TN- LA/2016, dated 20th May, 2016 deferred the date of poll to 13th June, 2016 (Monday); and
Whereas, the Election Commission of India, vide its proceeding No. 464/TN-LA/2016 dated 27th May 2016 has come to
conclusion that the election process in 134-Aravakurichi and 174-Thanjavur Assembly  Constituencies  cannot  be  allowed  to
proceed  and  ought  to  be  rescinded so  that    fresh elections may be conducted de-novo when the atmosphere become
conducive for holding of free and fair elections after a reasonable lapse of time.
Now, as recommended by the Election Commission of India vide it’s Order in Proceeding dated 27th  May 2016, the Notification
dated 22nd April 2016 is now being partially rescinded in respect of 134- Aravakurichi and 174-Thanjavur Assembly Constituencies.
(By Order of the Governor)
K. GNANADESIKAN,
Chief Secretary.
