PUBLIC (ELECTIONS-I) DEPARTMENT
EXTENSION OF PERIOD FOR FILING OF CLAIMS AND OBJECTIONS IN GRADUATES'
AND TEACHERS' CONSTITUENCIES OF TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE COUNCIL
No. SRO G-40/2010.
The following Notification of the Election Commission of India, New Delhi, dated 7th December,  2010 [Agrahayana, 16 1932
(Saka)] is published:—
No. 37/TN-LC/2010-CC & BE:—Whereas, the Commission had issued the programme for preparation of Electoral Rolls
of Newly Created Tamil Nadu Legislative Council from all Graduates and Teachers' Constituencies vide its letter
dated 30th September 2010. The period of filing claims and objection consequent to draft publication of the rolls was fixed from
22nd November 2010 to 7th December 2010.
Whereas the Chief Electoral Officer, Tamil Nadu has brought to the notice of the Commission that the preparation of rolls
has been adversely affected due to rains in large parts of the State of Tamil Nadu.
Now, therefore, taking into account the above facts, the Commission has decided to extend under rule 12 of Registration
of Electors Rules, 1960, the period of filing claims and objections in all the Graduates' Constituencies as well as Teachers'
Constituencies in Tamil Nadu for a period from 8th December 2010 to 17th December 2010.
