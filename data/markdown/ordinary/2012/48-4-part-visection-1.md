GENERAL NOTIFICATION
Comprehensive Variation to the Approved Detailed Development Plan No. 18 of
Tiruppur Local Planning Area.
(Roc. No.15214/2012/DP2.)
No. VI(1)359/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (1) Section 33 of Town and Country Planning Act 1971 (Act
No. 35 of 1972). The Commissioner of Town and Country Planning, Chennai in the Proceedings Roc No.1521/2012
DP2 dated 9-11-2012, proposes to make the following variation to the Tiruppur Local Planning Authority-Detailed
Development Plan No. 18, Approved in Director of Town and Country Planning, Chennai Proceedings Roc.No.7883/90
DP2 dated 1st July 1991 and published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Part-VI, Section-I, Page No.650, dated
28th August 1991.
2.
Any person affected or interested in this draft variation may within sixty days from the date of publication of
this notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, can represent in person or submit in writing to the Members
Secretary, Tiruppur Local Planning Authority any objection and suggestions relating thereto.
3.
The variation with plan may be inspected at free of cost at any time during office hours at the above said
Local Planning Authority Office.
VARIATION
1.
The entire Tiruppur Detailed Development Plan No. 18 of Tiruppur Local Planning Authority is comprehensively
varied by Commissioner of Town and Country Planning, Chennai vide Proceedings Roc No.15214/2012 DP2 dated
9th November 2012.
2.
The Plan thus varied is numbered as Map No.5 DDP(V)/DTCP No.24/2012.
3.
The Detailed Development Plan clause & schedules annexed with this plan are made enforceable from the
date of publication of the confirmed variation notification to be issued under Section 33(2) of the Act and published
in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
Chennai-600 002,
A. KARTHICK,
9th November 2012.

(Roc. No.18506/2010/DP2.)
No. VI(1)361/2012.
In exercise of the powers, conferred Under sub section (2) of 33 of Town and Country Planning Act 1971 (Act No.35
of 1972), The Commissioner of Town and Country Planning, Chennai in his proceeding letter Roc No.24957/2008 DP2
dated 23-3-2010, Proposes to make the following Variation to the conversion of Industrial use into Residential use (with
the condition of Buffer zone 15M distance shown in the variation map should be maintained as it is) in Ward J (10)
Block No.30, T.S. Nos.1810, 1811pt, 1812 pt, 1813, 1816, 1817pt with an extent of 6/94 acres to the approved
Coimbatore detailed Development Plan No.2 of Coimbatore Local Planning Authority, published in Tamil Nadu Government
Gazette No.2706, Public Health Deparment dated 30-11-33 & Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No.14, Part VI, Sec-1,
Page No.115, dated 14-4-10.
Since no objections and suggestions have been received on this draft notification with in the stipulated time, the
same are here by confirmed and ordered as below.
CONFIRMATION OF VARIATION
1.
Wherever the expression in Map No.4 T.P.No.20c 33-34 occurs the expression DDP/V/DTCP No.8/2010 shall
be added at the end.
2.
In Schedule Title 12, Sub title (of) Page No.14 Industrial use Area T.S.Nos.1810, 1811, 1812, 1813, 1816,
1817, shall be deleted and 1810pt, 1811pt, 1812pt, 1813pt, 1816pt, & 1817pt, shall be inserted.
3.
The above variation is to be included while submitting the Detailed Development Plan for comprehensive
variation U/s 33(1) of Town and Country Planning Act 1971.
