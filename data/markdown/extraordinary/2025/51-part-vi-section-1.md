The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that the lands specified in the Schedule below have to be 
acquired forIndustrial Purposes,for the Formation of ‘Corridor - 5 - Viaduct 11 (ECV-03)ELCOT to Sholinganallur’ and 
having already been decided that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid out of 
the funds managed by the Chennai Metro Rail Ltd. The following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 3 
of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act,10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the land/lands specified in the Schedule 
below and measuring 11 Sq.mtrs., (or) 118.404 Sq.ft., to the same, a little more or less needed for Industrial Purposes, 
to wit, for the Formation of ‘Corridor - 5 - Viaduct11 (ECV-03) ELCOT to Sholinganallur’.
The plans of the lands are kept in the Office of the Special District Revenue Officer (LA), Chennai Metro Rail
