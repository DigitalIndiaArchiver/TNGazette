Under Sub-Statute (1) of Statute 5 of Chapter-XXI of the General Statutes of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical 
University, Chennai it is hereby published that the following members  have been elected to the Senate of the Tamil Nadu 
Dr. M.G.R Medical  University, Chennai, by the teachers of  each affiliated Medical Colleges  from  among themselves 
Under Section 18(3)(a) of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R Medical University, Chennai Act, 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 37 of 1987). 
 The period of  membership of the following Professors shall be  (03)three years or as long as they hold the post 
of Professor in the same Medical College, or the termination of, whichever is earlier.
