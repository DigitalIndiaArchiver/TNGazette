No. SRO A-12/2015.—In exercise of the powers conferred by section 26 read with sections 35 and 63 of the Tamil Nadu
Forest Act, 1882 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1882), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the
Tamil Nadu Regulation of Wood Based Industries Rules, 2010.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules, in rule 14,—
(1) the existing provision shall be numbered as sub- rule (1) of rule 14 ;
(2) after sub-rule (1) as so numbered, the following sub-rule shall be added, namely:-
“(2) - Nothing contained in these rules shall apply to match splints industries, where sawing and veneering
of match splints species, either imported or exempted from the Tamil Nadu Timber Transit Rules, 1968, are a part
of the match stick or splint production.”
 HANS RAJ VERMA,
    Principal Secretary to Government.
