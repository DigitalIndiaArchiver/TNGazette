Whereas it appears to the appropriate Government has given an Administrative Sanction that a total of 
72.88.32 hectares of land is required in Suriyur Village in Thiruverumbur Taluk in Thiruchirappalli District for public 
purpose namely Cauvery-Vaigai-Gundaru interlinking Flood Carrier Canal Project. As per the Government Order No. 40, 
Public Works (2D) Department, Dated: 09.07.2020, M/s. “WAPCOS” is appointed to carry out the  Environmental  Impact 
Assessment study for the irrigation project (i.e) formation of Flood Water Carrier Canal from Cauvery River by interlinking 
Cauvery-Vaigai-Gundar Rivers in Karur, Trichy and Pudukottai  Districts in Tamil Nadu, under Section 3 (3) of The 
Environmental Protection Act 1986, the report was received.  The land is required for the  formation of Flood Carrier 
canal for the drought prone areas in Karur, Trichy and Pudukottai Districts.  As the Environmental  Impact Assessment 
study is being carried out for this irrigation project, Social Impact Assessment shall not apply according to the provision 
of sub-section 2 of Section 6 of Right to Fair Compensation and Transparency in Land Acquisition,  Rehabilitation 
and Resettlement Act, 2013. The Requisition body had not given enter upon. The Revenue Divisional Oﬃ  cer, Trichy is 
appointed as Administrator for the purpose of Rehabilitation and Resettlement. Therefore, it is notiﬁ ed that for the above 
said project in Suriyur  Block I Village in Thiruverumbur Taluk in Thiruchirappalli District land measuring an extent of 
17.17.57 Hec, the details of which are speciﬁ ed in the schedule below, is under acquisition.
