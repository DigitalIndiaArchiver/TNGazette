BHARATHIDASAN UNIVERSITY, PALKALAIPERUR, TIRUCHIRAPPALLI-620 024.
Election to (a) The Syndicate and (b) The Standing Committee on Academic Affairs.
(Ref. No. 033599/A2/2008.)
No. VI-3(b)/19/2009.
(1)
Syndicate:
Class II - Other Members-sub-section (b) (1) of Section 24 under Chapter IV of the Bharathidasan
University Act, 1981 - Two Members* elected by the Principals of the affiliated colleges from among
themselves, in accordance with the system of proportional representation by means of a single
transferable vote.
Filling of Casual Vacancy:
*Of the two members, the membership of one has fallen vacant and election has to be conducted as per
Section 47 under Chapter 10 of Bharathidasan University Act, 1981 (Tamil Nadu Act 2 of 1982). The tenure of this residual
vacancy is upto 26th December 2011 from the declaration date of result.
(2)
Standing Committee on Academic Affairs:
Class II - Other Members - sub-section (2) (a) of Section 23 under Chapter III of the Bharathidasan University
Act, 1981 - Two Members* elected by the Principals of the affiliated colleges from among themselves,
in accordance with the system of proportional representation by means of a single transferable vote.
Filling of Casual vacancy:
*Of the two members, the membership of one has fallen vacant and the election has to be conducted as per
Section 47 under Chapter 10 of Bharathidasan University Act, 1981 (Tamil Nadu Act 2 of 1982). The tenure of this residual
vacancy is upto 26th December 2011 from the declaration date of result.
