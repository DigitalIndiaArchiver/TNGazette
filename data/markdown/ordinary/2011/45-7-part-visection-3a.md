TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI.
Appointment of Executive Officer for Sharfunnisa Begum Sahiba Mosque Wakf, Chennai.
(Rc.No. 9534/10/B6/Chennai.)
No. VI-3(a)/152/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions of Section 38 of the Wakf Act, 1995,
and as per the Special Resolution No. 5, dated 14th June 1990, the Chief Executive Officer, Tamil Nadu Wakf Board
in Proceedings No.9534/10/B6/Chennai, dated 30th June 2011 has appointed the Superintendent of Wakfs, Chennai Zone
as the Executive Officer of Sharfunnissa Begum Sahiba Mosque Wakf, Teynampet, Chennai to look after the affairs
of the Wakf for a period of one year from the date of the order. Accordingly the Superintendent of Wakfs/Executive
Officer has taken over the charge of the Wakf on the Fore Noon of 2nd July 2011.
Chennai-600 001,
TURAB AHAMED,
28th September 2011.
Additional Secretary to Government/
Cheif Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
Appointment of Executive Officer for Hazrath Dastagir Sahib Dargha, Mosque and Burial Ground Wakf,
Krishnampet, Chennai.
(Rc.No. 3378/05/B6/Chennai.)
No. VI-3(a)/153/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions Section 38 of the Wakf Act, 1995,
and as per the Special Resolution No. 5, dated 14 June 1990, the Chief Executive Officer, Tamil Nadu Wakf Board
in Proceedings No.3378/05/B6/Chennai, dated 30th May 2011 has appointed the Executive Officer, Chennai and
Kancheepuram Districts Wakfs as the Executive Officer of Hazrath Dastagir Sahib Dargha, Mosque and Burial Ground
Wakf, Krishnampet, Chennai to look after the affairs of the Wakf for a period of one year from the date of the order.
Chennai-600 001,
TURAB AHAMED,
11th October 2011.
Additional Secretary to Government/
Cheif Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
