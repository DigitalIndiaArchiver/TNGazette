The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that
the lands specified in the schedule below have to be acquired
for industrial purpose to wit for setting up of Industrial Complex
by SIPCOT Oragadam Expansion Scheme and Complex and
it having already been decided that the entire amount of
compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid out of
the funds controlled or managed by the Government, the
following Notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 3
of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes
Act, 1997  (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999.)
NOTIFICATION
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil
Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
acquires the lands specified in the schedule below and
measuring 11.49.5 hectares to the same, a little more or less
needed for Industrial Complex  by SIPCOT.
The plans of the lands are kept in the Office of the Special
Tahsildar (Land Acquisition), Oragadam Expansion Scheme
Unit I, Sriperumbudur Taluk and may be inspected at any time
during office hours.
