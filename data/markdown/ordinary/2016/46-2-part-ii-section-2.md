No. II(2)/HOU/769/2016.—In exercise of powers conferred
under Section 113 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country
Planning Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972) the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby exempt the existing building of Ground
Floor + 2 Floors Bank building at Door No. 19, Mahalakshmi
Street, T. Nagar, Chennai - 600 017 from the provisions of
Development Regulation 26 (4) relating to road width, Floor
Space Index and Development Regulation 26 (4) E (i) (ii)
& (iii) relating to Front Set Back, Side Set Back (BS) Rear
Set Back of Second Master Plan for Chennai Metropolitan
Area 2026, subject to collection of premium FSI charges for
the excess FSI area and provision of required car parking
as per Development Regulation requirements and
furnishing of undertaking to assure that the additional land
amalgamated shall be used only for car parking and two
wheeler parking and it will not be put to any other
use later. The Chennai Metropolitan Development Authority
enforcement cell shall make entry in the caution register for
the above parking site and to monitor it periodically.

(i)

one point over and above

per month shall be paid as
dearness allowance.
(ii) The dearness allowance shall be
calculated 
every 
year 
on
the first April on the basis of the
average of indices of the

from January to December.

dearness allowance shall, thus,
be effective from the date of

Gazette, based on the Average
Chennai

Consumer

previous year, that is, from
January to December.
