In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the  Wakf Act, 1995 and as per the
Special Resolution No. 5, dated 14th June 1990 and the powers conferred in the orders of the Hon'ble High Court,
Madras in W.P. 16997/12, dated 25-03-2013, the Chief Executive Officer, Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in Proceedings No.
6320/07/B1/Tnv. dated 22-05-2013 has appointed the Superintendent of Wakfs, Tirunelveli as the Executive Officer of
Gulam Ali Sha Thaikka Wakf, Kailasapuram, Tirunelveli Junction, Tirunelveli District to look after the affairs of the Wakf
for a period of one year from the date of the order.
