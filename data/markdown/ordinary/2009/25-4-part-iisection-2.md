Exemption from certain Rule of Development Control Rules
for Chennai Metropolitan Area for construction of
Residential Building at Neelangarai Village, Chennai
under the Act.
[G.O. Ms. No. 95, Housing and Urban Development
(UD V), 12th June 2009.]
No. II(2)/HOU/312/2009.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by Section 113 of the Tamil Nadu Town and
Country Planning Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby exempts the building of
Ground Floor and First Floor residential building at
Plot No. 158 in S.No. 95-1A1A129 of Neelangarai Village,
Chennai from the provisions in Serial No. 1 of Annexure-
XIII under Rule 20 of Development Control Rules for Chennai
Metropolitan Area.
Variations to the approved Master Plan for Coimbatore
Local Planning Area under the Act.
[G.O. (2D) No. 148, Housing and Urban Development
(UD4.1), 12th June 2009.]
No. II(2)/HOU/313/2009.—In 
exercise 
of 
the
powers conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 32 of the
Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning Act, 1971
(Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby makes the following variations to the master plan for
the Coimbatore Local Planning Area approved under the said
Act and published with the Housing and Urban Development
Department Notification No. II(2)/HOU/4377/94 at page 1078
of Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette,
dated the 9th November 1994:—
VARIATIONS
In the said Master Plan, in the “LAND USE
SCHEDULES”,under the heading “SINGANALLUR D.D.P.
No. 35” under the sub-heading, “No. 54 SINGANALLUR
VILLAGE”,—
(1) against the entry “Agricultural” for the expression
“181 to 210”, the expression “181 to 191, 192 (except
192-1, 3, 4), 195 (except 195-1B, 1C, 1D, 2pt.), 196 (except
196pt.), 197 to 210” shall be substituted;
(2) against the entry  “Residential”, after the
expression “173pt”, the expression “192-1, 3, 4, 193, 194,
195-1B, 1C, 1D, 2pt., 196pt” shall be inserted.
