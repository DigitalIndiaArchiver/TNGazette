(Roc. No. CPRRP-CLA/15(1)/VI-8/2022)
NOTICE UNDER SECTION 15(1) OF TAMIL NADU HIGHWAYS ACT, 2001 (TAMIL NADU ACT 34 OF 2002), READ WITH 
TAMIL NADU LAND ACQUISITION LAWS (REVIVAL OF OPERATION, AMENDMENT AND VALIDATION) ACT, 2019.
No. VI(1)/70(a-1)/2022.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that the lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below are acquired 
under Chennai Peripheral Ring Road Project for Highways purpose to wit, for formation of Road connectivity from 
Ennore Port to Mahabalipuram (from 120+167 Km. to 121+430 Km.) at 72, Melkanagampattu Village, Tiruporur Taluk, 
Chengalpattu District and it having already been decided that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the 
lands to be paid out of the funds controlled and managed by the Highways Department and after having considering 
the cause shown by the owner or other persons having interest on such lands, as the case may be, do hereby publish 
the following notice under sub-section (1) of  Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 
of 2002) read with the Tamil Nadu Land Acquisition Laws (Revival of Operation, Amendment and Validation) Act, 2019.
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002), read 
with the Tamil Nadu Land Acquisition Laws (Revival of Operation, Amendment and Validation Act, 2019) the Governor of 
Tamil Nadu hereby notiﬁ es that the Wet, Dry lands and Grama Natham, speciﬁ ed in the schedule below and measuring to 
an extent of 44,063 Sq.Mtrs. of Wet, Dry patta lands and in new Survey No.15/2B etc., in 72, Melkanagampattu Village, 
Tiruporur Taluk, Chengalpattu District to the same a little more or less needed for the purpose to wit for formation of 
road connectivity from Ennore Port to Mahabalipuram (from 120+167 Km. to 121+430 Km.) as part of Chennai Peripheral 
Ring Road Project, Highways Department. 
The plan of the lands under acquisition are kept in the oﬃ  ce of the Special District Revenue Oﬃ  cer (Land Acquisition), 
Chennai Peripheral Ring Road Project, Section-V, Chennai-600 058 and in the oﬃ  ce of the Special Tahsildar (Land 
Acquisition), Chennai Peripheral Ring Road Project, Unit-VI, Chengalpattu and may be inspected at any time during the 
oﬃ  ce hours.
