In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 18-B of the Tamil Nadu Prohibition Act, 1937
(Tamil Nadu Act X of 1937), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Prohibition and
Excise Department Notification No.II(1)/PE/239(d)/81, published at page 2 of Part II-Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Government
Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 1st May 1981, as subsequently amended.
AMENDMENTS
 In the said Notification, in the TABLE, under the entry “Indian Made Foreign Spirits (brand names of which are specified
in the Annexure) other than sacramental wine”,-
   (1)  for the expression “Rs.125.00 (Rupees one hundred and twenty five only) per proof litre” in column (2), against
the entry under the heading “(i) ORDINARY BRANDS” in column (1), the expression “Rs.140.00 (Rupees one hundred and
forty only) per proof litre” shall be substituted;
  (2)  for the expression “Rs.145.00 (Rupees one hundred and  forty five only) per proof litre” in column (2), against
the entry under the heading “(ii) MEDIUM BRANDS” in column (1), the expression “Rs.155.00 (Rupees one hundred and
fifty five only) per proof litre” shall be substituted;
  (3) under the heading “(iii) PREMIUM BRANDS” in  column (1),—
(i) for the expression “Rs.170.00 (Rupees one hundred and seventy only) per proof litre” in column (2), the
expression “Rs.180.00 (Rupees one hundred and eighty only) per proof litre” shall be substituted;
(ii) for the expression “Rs. 200.00 (Rupees two hundred only) per proof litre” in column (2), the expression
“Rs.210.00 (Rupees two hundred and ten only) per proof litre” shall be substituted.
