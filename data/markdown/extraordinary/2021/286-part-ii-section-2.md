The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that the lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below have to be 
acquired for industrial purposes to wit for the formation of SIPCOT Panapakkam Industrial Park Scheme and it having 
already been decided that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid out of the 
funds controlled or managed by SIPCOT the following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the 
Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997.
N඗ගඑඋඍ
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999) the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below 
and measuring 10.83.5  Hectares  to the same,  a little more or less needed for the formation of SIPCOT Panapakkam 
Industrial Park Scheme. The plans of the lands are kept in the oﬃ  ce of the Special Tahsildar (LA), Panapakkam Scheme, 
Arakkonam Taluk and may be inspected at any time during oﬃ  ce hours.
