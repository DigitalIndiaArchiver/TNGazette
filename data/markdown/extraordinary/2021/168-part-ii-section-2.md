2. The Additional Chief Secretary/Commissioner of Revenue Administration has stated that earlier, Shopping Malls, 
All Showrooms, Big Format Stores, Restaurants, Tea Shops, Vegetable Shops and all Shops including Provision Shops 
were permitted to function upto 10.00 P.M. in the Government Order third read above.
3. The Additional Chief Secretary/Commissioner of Revenue Administration has also stated that Shopping Malls, 
All Showrooms, Big Format Stores, Restaurants, Tea Shops, Vegetable Shops and all Shops including Provision Shops 
shall be permitted to function upto 11.00 P.M. and requested to issue the amendment to G.O.Ms.No.447, Revenue 
and Disaster Management (DM II), Department, Dated 31.08.2020, G.O.Ms.No.541, Revenue and Disaster Management 
(DM II), Department, Dated 30.09.2020 and G.O.Ms.No.582, Revenue and Disaster Management (DM II), Department, 
Dated  22.10.2020 accordingly.
 4. Now therefore the Government hereby accept the proposal of the Additional Chief Secretary and Commissioner 
of Revenue Administration and to issue following amendments:- 
(I)    In G.O. Ms. No.447, Revenue and Disaster Management (DM IV) Department, date 31.08.2020, the content in Section  (vi) 
under Caption II- (Under Permitted Activities) shall be read as follows:
 
vi. All shops including Vegetable shops, Provision shops and Shopping Malls/ Market complexes, all Showrooms 
and Big Format Stores across the State are permitted to function till 11.00 P.M. by duly following the Standard Operating 
Procedures issued already. 
(II)   In G.O. Ms. No.541, Revenue and Disaster Management (DM-IV) Department, dated 30.09.2020, the content in        
  Section (i) under Caption II (Under Permitted Activities) shall be read as follows:
i. Following the Standard Operating Procedure already issued by Government, Restaurants and Tea Shops are 
permitted to function upto 11.00 pm and take away (parcel food) will be permitted upto 11.00 pm.
