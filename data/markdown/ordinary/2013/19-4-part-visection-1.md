Kumbakonam Local Planning Area.
(Roc. No. 26378/2000/DP3.)
No. VI(1)/219/2013.
 In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (1) of Section 33 of Town and Country Planning Act 1971.
(Act No. 35 of 1972) The Commissioner of Town and Country Planning, in the Proceedings Roc. No. 26378/2000/DP3,
dated 17th April 2013 proposes to make the comprehensive draft variation to the sanctioned Town Planning Scheme No.
2 of Kumbakonam Local Planning Area Sanctioned in G.O.No. 1235, Rural Development and Local Administration Department,
dated 20th May 1971 and  Published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, Part-II Section-2, Pages
1 to 23 dated 14th July 1971.
2. Any person affected or interested in this draft variation may within sixty days from the date of publication of this
notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, can represent in person or submit in writing to the Member Secretary,
Kumbakonam Local Planning Authority any objection and suggestions relating there to.
3. The variation with plan may be inspected at free of cost at any time during office hours at the above said Local
Planning Authority Office.
VARIATION
1. The entire “Town Planning Scheme No. 2 of Kumbakonam Local Planning Area sanctioned in G.O. No. 1235  Rural
Development and Local Administration Department, dated 20-5-1971” and Published in Tamil Nadu Government
Gazette Part-II, Section-2, Page 1 to 23, dated 14th July 1971, is varied by the “Kumbakonam Detailed Development
Plan No. 2 of Kumbakonam Local Planning Area”.
2. The Plan thus varied is numbered as Map No. 5 DDP/(V)/DTCP No. 10/2013.
3. The Detailed Development Plan clauses and Schedules annexed with this plan are made enforceable from the
date of publication of the confirmed variation notification to be issued under Section 33(2) of the Act and published
in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.

Magistrate Court, Peraiyoor, shall be held.

(Roc. No. 1720/2006/G/Judn)
No. VI(1)/224/2013.

the High Court, Madras, hereby directs that in Madurai District, the District Munsif Court, Thirumangalam, shall cease to
have local jurisdiction over the entire Revenue Taluk of Peraiyoor and the District Munsif - cum - Judicial Magistrate Court,

NOTIFICATION-III
(Roc. No. 1720/2006/G/Judn)
No. VI(1)/225/2013.
 The High Court, Madras hereby directs and notifies that the District Munsif - cum - Judicial Magistrate, Peraiyoor in
Madurai district shall exercise all the powers conferred on a district Munsif under section 12 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts
Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873) as amended up-to-date in regard to original suits and proceedings not otherwise
exempted from his cognizance of which the amount or value of the subject matter does not exceed One Lakh Rupees
within the local limits of his jurisdiction with effect from the date on which the District Munsif - cum - Judicial Magistrate,
Peraiyoor assumes charge of that court.
