SOCIAL WELFARE AND NUTRITIOUS MEAL PROGRAMME DEPARTMENT
Amendment to Ad hoc Rules relating to Temporary post of Multi Purpose Health Workers (Female) in the
Tamil Nadu Medical Subordinate  Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 189, Social Welfare and Nutritious Meal Programme (SW-1), 12th November 2010,
Aippasi 26, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No. SRO B-80/2010.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the rules published with the Backward Classes
Welfare, Nutritious Meal Programme and Social Welfare Department Notification SRO.No. B-440/89, at pages 292-293 of
Part III—Section 1(b) of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 13th December 1989.
2.
The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force with effect form the 14th August, 1989.
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in rule 5, in sub-rule (a), for the expression “thirty five years of age” the expression “forty years
of age” shall be substituted.
MOHAN PYARE,
Principal Secretary to Government.
