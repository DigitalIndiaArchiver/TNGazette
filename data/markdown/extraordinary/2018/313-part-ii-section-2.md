NOTIFICATIONS BY GOVERNMENT
LAW DEPARTMENT 
DATE OF COMMENCEMENT OF THE TAMIL NADU ESTABLISHMENT OF PRIVATE 
LAW COLLEGES (REGULATION) ACT, 2018 (TAMIL NADU ACT 22 OF 2018).
[G.O. Ms. No.255,  Law (LS), 4th September 2018, Aavani 19, Vilambi, Thiruvalluvar 
Aandu-2049.]
No. II(2)/LAW/800(b)/2018.       
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu 
Establishment of Private Law Colleges (Regulation) Act, 2018 (Tamil Nadu Act 22 of 2018), the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the 5th September 2018, as the date on which the said 
Act shall come into force.
