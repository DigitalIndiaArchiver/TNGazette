The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that
the lands specified in the schedule below has to be acquired
for industrial purpose to wit,  for the Industrial Purpose of setting
up SIPCOT Industrial Complex, Phase-I and it having already
been decided that the entire amount of compensation to be
awarded for the lands is to be paid out of the funds controlled or
managed by the Government, the following Notice is issued
under Sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil
Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act,
1997 (Tamil Nadu Act, 10 of 1999), the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands specified in the schedule
below and measuring 19.86.0 Hectares to the same, a little
more or less needed for Industrial purposes, to wit for the
establishment of Industrial Complex Phase-I, by the SIPCOT.
The plans of the lands are kept in the Office of the Collector,
Thoothukudi and may be inspected at any time during office
hours.
