ORDER: No. 762, Public (Special.B), 12th September 2017
In the reference first read above, the name of the Revenue Department in the Tamil Nadu Secretariat
has been changed as Revenue and Disaster Management Department. Also, in the  reference second
read above, necessary amendment has been issued to the Tamil Nadu Government Business Rules,
1978 renaming the Revenue Department as Revenue and Disaster Management Department.

2. In view of the above, the Government have decided to change  the Nomenclature of Minister for
Revenue as Minister for Revenue and Disaster Management. Accordingly, under clause (3) of Article 166
of  the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby allocate the portfolio “Disaster
Management” to Thiru R.B.Udhayakumar in addition to the portfolios now held by him and to designate
Thiru R.B.Udhayakumar as Minister for Revenue and Disaster Management.
