HOME, PROHIBITION AND EXCISE DEPARTMENT
Exemption from the operation of Tamil Nadu Indian-made Foreign Spirits (Manufacture Rules 1981) to Thiruvalargal
Mohan Breweries and Distilleries Limited, Indian Made Foreign Spirits Unit, Valasaravakkam, Chennai for
certain period.
[G.O. Ms. No. 36, Home, Prohibition and Excise(III), 21st June 2013.]
No.-SROA-18/2013.—Under rule 35 of the Tamil Nadu Indian-made Foregin Spirits (Manufacture) Rules, 1981,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby exempts Thiruvalargal Mohan Breweries and Distilleries Limited, Indian Made Foreign
Spirits Unit, Valasaravakkam, Chennai from the operation of sub-rule (2) of rule 11 of the said Rules, to replace the
existing 13  Mild Steel  Storage Vats bearing  Nos. 1 to  13 with new stainless steel tanks  with the same capacity, subject
to the condition that the said works should be completed within the period of three months from the date of issue
of  this Notificaion.
NIRANJAN MARDI,
Principal Secretary to Government.
