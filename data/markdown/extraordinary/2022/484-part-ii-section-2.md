[G.O. (D) No. 497, Labour Welfare and Skill Development (D2), 12th October 2022,

In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 8 of the Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 (Central Act 
XIV of 1947), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby orders as follows:-
Tmt. V.R. Latha, Principal District Judge, Dindigul is transferred and posted as Presiding Officer, II Additional 
Labour Court, Chennai in the existing vacancy.
