UNIVERSITY AUTHORITIES 
THE TAMIL NADU DR. M.G.R. MEDICAL UNIVERSITY, CHENNAI.
Two Members Elected to the Governing Council of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai By 
The Deans or Principals of Afﬁ liated Colleges Providing Courses of Study and Training in Medical Science 
From Among Themselves.
(Rc. No. EIII(3)/37790/2017-1)
No.VI-3(b)/18/2018.
Under sub-statues (1) of Statute 5 of Chapter-XXI of the General Statutes of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical 
University, Chennai it is hereby published that the following members are elected to the Governing Council of the 
Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai by the Deans or Principals of afﬁ liated colleges providing courses of 
study and training in medical science Under Section 21(4)(b) of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai, 
Act, 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 37 of 1987) in the regular vacancies. 
1. 
Dr.A. Meena, M.Pharm., Ph.D.
 
Principal 
 
K.K. College of Pharmacy
 
No. 161, Sankaralinganar Road,
 
Gerugambakkam, Chennai-600 122.
2. 
Dr. S. Vijayalakshmi, M.Sc., (N) Ph.D.,M.A., 
Principal 
Vignesh Nursing College 
131, Manalurpet Road 
Kizhanaikarai, Thiruvannamalai-606 603.
2. The tenure of the above members in the Governing Council of this University will be for a period of 3 years 
from 21-12-2017 to 20.12.2020 or the date on which they cease to be the Deans or Principals of afﬁ liated colleges 
providing courses of study and training in medical science, whichever is earlier.
