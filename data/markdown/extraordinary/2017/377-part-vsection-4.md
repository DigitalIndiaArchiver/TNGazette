NOTIFICATION BY THE ELECTION COMMISSION OF INDIA
APPOINTMENT OF DATES FOR VARIOUS STAGES OF BYE-ELECTION TO TAMIL NADU
LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY FROM 11. DR. RADHAKRISHNAN NAGAR ASSEMBLY
CONSTITUENCY AND FIXATION OF HOURS OF POLL TO FILL A VACANCY CAUSED BY THE
DEATH OF A MEMBER
No. SRO G-34/2017.
The following Notification of the Election Commission of India, Nirvachan Sadan, Ashoka
Road, New Delhi-110 001, dated  27th November, 2017  [6 Agrahayana, 1939 (Saka)] is
published:–
No.100/TN-LA/1/2017:-  WHEREAS,  the  seat  of  Selvi J. Jayalalithaa,  in  the Legislative
Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu, elected from 11. Dr. Radhakrishnan Nagar Assembly
Constituency, has become vacant on 05th December 2016, by reason of her death; and
2. WHEREAS, a bye-election is to be held for the purpose of filling the vacancy so caused;
and
3.  WHEREAS, in pursuance of sub-section (1) of Section 150, and Sections
30 and 56 of the  Representation of the  People Act,  1951 (43 of 1951), the  Election Commission
of India called   upon  the  said  11. Dr. Radhakrishnan Nagar Assembly  Constituency  in the
State of Tamil Nadu to elect,  a person, for the purpose of filling the said vacancy in the Legislative
Assembly of the State, vide notification dated 16th March 2017 appointing, inter alia,
12th April 2017 as the date of poll; and
4.  WHEREAS, subsequently, the Commission, for reasons stated in its
order No. 100/TN-LA/1/2017, dated 09th April 2017, rescinded the election in said
11-Dr. Radhakrishnan Nagar Assembly Constituency for which the poll was scheduled to be
taken on the 12th April, 2017; and

5.  WHEREAS, the Commission has now decided to hold the Bye-Election to Fill the vacancy from the
11-Dr. Radhakrishnan Nagar Assembly Constituency in the State of Tamil Nadu;
6. NOW, therefore in pursuance of sub-section (1) of Section 150. and sections 30 and 56 of the Representation of the
People Act, 1951 (43 of 1951), the Election Commission of india hereby:-
(A) Calls upon the said 11-Dr. Radhakrishnan Nagar Assembly Constituency in the State of Tamil Nadu to elect, before
the 26th December 2017 (Tuesday) and in accordance with the provisions of the said Act and of the rules and orders made
thereunder, a person, for the purpose of filling the said vacancy in the Legislative Assembly of the State; and
(B) Appoints,  with respect to the said Bye-Election from 11-Dr. Radhakrishnan Nagar Assembly Constituency :-
(a)
the 04-12-2017 (Monday), as the last date for making nominations;
(b)
the 05-12-2017 (Tuesday), as the date for the Scrutiny of nominations;
(c)
the 07-12-2017 (Thursday), as the last date for the withdrawal of candidatures;
(d)
the 21-12-2017 (Thursday), as the date on which a poll shall, if necessary, be taken; and
(e)
the 26-12-2017 (Tuesday), as the date  before which the election shall be completed; and
(C)
Fixes the hours from 8.00 A.M. to 5.00 P.M, as the hours during  which the poll shall, if necessary, be taken
on the date specified above, for the election.
