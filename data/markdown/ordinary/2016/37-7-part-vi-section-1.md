GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
 Winding up of the affairs of certain Co-operative Societies and Appointment of Official Liquidator.
TNH. 137. Paramankurichi Arignar Anna Industrial Weavers Co-operative Production and Sale Society Ltd.,
Paramankurichi in Tiruchendur Taluk, Thoothukudi District
(Rc.No. 8106/2011/E)
No. VI(1)/311/2016.
The affairs of the Paramankurichi Arignar Anna Industrial Weavers Co-operative Production and Sale Society Ltd.,
TNH. 137. in Paramankurichi Village in Tiruchendur Taluk, Thoothukudi District is ordered to be wound up under
Section 137 (2) (a) of the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act 30 of 1983 and the Handloom Officer (Statutory and
Liquidation) of the Office of the Assistant Director of Handlooms and Textiles, Tirunelveli is appointed as its Official
Liquidator under Section 138 of the same Act with effect from 25-07-2016.
0.1121. Madapuram Mahilchipuram Weavers Co-operative Production and Sale Society Ltd.,  Vallioor in
Radhapuram Taluk, Tirunelveli District
(Rc.No. 8112/2011/E)
No. VI(1)/312/2016.
The affaris of the Madapuram Mahilchipuram Weavers Co-operative Production and Sale Society Ltd., No. 0.1121.
Vallioor in Radhapuram Taluk, Tirunelveli District is ordered to be wound up under Section 137 (2) (b) of
Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act 30 of 1983, and the Handloom Officer (Statutory and Liquidation) of the Office
of the Assistant Director of Handlooms and Textiles, Tirunelveli is appointed as its Official Liquidator under Section 138
of the Same Act with effect from 25-07-2016.
TNH. No. 115. Mahakavi Bharathiyar Weavers Co-operative Production and Sale Society Ltd.,  Puliangudi,
Sivakiri Taluk, Tirunelveli District
(Rc.No. 650/2015/E)
No. VI(1)/313/2016.
The affaris of the Mahakavi Bharathiyar Weavers Co-operative Production and Sale Society Ltd., TNH. No. 115. in
Puliangudi, Sivakiri Taluk, Tirunelveli District is ordered to be wound up under Section 137 (2) (a) of
Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act 30 of 1983 and the Handloom Officer (Statutory and Liquidation) of the Office of
the Assistant Director of Handlooms and Textiles, Tirunelveli is appointed as its Official Liquidator under Section 138 of
the Same Act with effect from 25-07-2016.
Tirunelveli,
A. PANDI,
25th July 2016.
Assistant Director of
Handlooms and Textiles.
