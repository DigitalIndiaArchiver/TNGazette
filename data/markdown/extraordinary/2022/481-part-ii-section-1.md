with Notification of the Government of India, Ministry of Home Affairs, No. 20-25-26, Public-1, dated the 8th June 1957, 
the Government of Tamil Nadu hereby declares that, in addition to “Sundays” expressly defined as Public Holidays in 
the said “Explanation”, the following days shall be Public Holidays for the year 2023.
