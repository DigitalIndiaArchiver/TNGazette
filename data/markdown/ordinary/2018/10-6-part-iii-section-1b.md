In the said Adhoc Rules, - 
(1) in rule 3,- 
 
clause (ii) shall be omitted;  
(2) for rule 5, the following shall be substituted, namely:- 
"5. Qualiﬁ cations.- No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post by any of the methods speciﬁ ed in rule 3, 
unless he possess the following qualiﬁ cations, namely"- 
"(i) (a) A Bachelor's and Master's degree in the appropriate branch of Engineering / Technology / Architecture with 
not less than sixty percent of marks or its equivalent either at Bachelor's or Master's degree; and 
 
(b)  Ph.D from the appropriate discipline of Engineering / Technology / Architecture; or must have published ﬁ ve 
International Journal papers in the relevant areas of specializaticn, each Journal having a cumulative impact index of not 
less than 2.0, with incumbent as the main author and all the ﬁ ve publications being in the authors' area of specialization. 
(ii)  Experience for a period of not less than ten years in the relevant ﬁ eld of research / industry / teaching, out of 
which, not less than three years shall be in a position not below the rank of Head of Department in a Polytechnic or in a 
equivalent grade in other Institution: 
 
Provided that in the case of Architecture branch, professional practice of not less than ten years, as certiﬁ ed by 
the Council of Architecture, shall also be considered as a valid experience: 
 
Provided further that in the case of persons belonging to a service, the period spent by them on acquiring higher 
qualiﬁ cations in Engineering will be counted towards the experience in the particular category of posts held by them before 
proceeding for higher studies: 
 
Provided also that for persons holding the post of Assistant Director of Technical Education (Examination) in the 
Directorate of Technical Education, the period of service rendered in that post shall be counted only once towards the required 
teaching experience in the category of Head of Department in the Government Polytechnics.
 
 Explanations:- 
(a)  In the case of research experience, good academic record and technical books / research paper publications / 
Intellectual Property Rights / patents record shall be required. 
(b)  In the case of experience in industry, the same shall be at the managerial level equivalent to that of Head of 
Department with active participation in designing, planning, executing, analyzing, quality control, innovation, training, technical 
books / research paper publications / Intellectual Property Rights/patents record. 
Flair for Management and Leadership shall be considered as essential.
