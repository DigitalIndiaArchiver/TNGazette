NOTIFICATIONS UNDER TAMIL NADU TRANSPARENCY IN TENDERS ACT
THE ENTRUSTMENT OF THE PREPARATION OF DETAILED PROJECT REPORT (DPR) IN RESPECT OF
SECOND PHASE OF CHENNAI METRO RAIL PROJECT TO THE
DELHI METRO RAIL CORPORATION (DMRC) UNDER THE ACT.
[G.O. No. 55, Finance (Salaries), 24th February 2011, Maasi 12, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No. II(2)/FIN/90(d-1)/2011.
Under clause (f) of Section 16 of the Tamil Nadu Transparency in Tenders Act, 1998 (Tamil Nadu Act 43 of 1998),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notifies the entrustment of the preparation of Detailed Project Report (DPR) in respect of
second phase of Chennai Metro Rail Project to the Delhi Metro Rail Corporation (DMRC).
APPOINTMENT OF M/S WIPRO LIMITED AS IMPLEMENTING AGENCY IN THE STATE
PORTAL AND STATE SERVICES DELIVERY GATEWAY UNDER THE ACT.
[G.O. No. 56, Finance (Salaries), 24th February 2011, Maasi 12, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No. II(2)/FIN/90(d-2)/2011.
Under clause (f) of Section 16 of the Tamil Nadu Transparency in Tenders Act, 1998 (Tamil Nadu Act 43 of 1998), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notifies the appointment of M/s Wipro Limited as Implementing Agency in the State Portal and
State Services Delivery Gateway (SSDG).
K. SHANMUGAM,
Principal Secretary to Government.
