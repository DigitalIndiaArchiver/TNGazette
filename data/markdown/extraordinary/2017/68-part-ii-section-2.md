The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied
that the lands specified in the schedule below have to be
acquired for industrial purpose, to wit to form the frontage
of acess road from SIPCOT land to Singaperumal Koil Road
and it having already been decided that the entire amount
of compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid
out of the funds controlled or managed by the Government,
the following Notice is issued under sub-section (1) of
Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial
Purposes Act, 1997  (Tamil Nadu Act 10/99)
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil
Nadu Act, 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
acquires the lands specified in the schedule below and
measuring 1.11.0 hectares to the same, a little more or
less needed for industrial purpose, to wit, for formation of
the frontage of access road from SIPCOT land to
Singaperumal Koil Road.
The

lands

Sriperumbudur Scheme, Irungattukottai SIPCOT Complex,
Irungattukottai, Sriperumbudur Taluk, Kancheepuram District
and may be inspected at any time during office hours.
