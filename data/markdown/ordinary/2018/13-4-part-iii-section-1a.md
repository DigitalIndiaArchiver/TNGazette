No. SRO A-14/2018.—In exercise of the powers conferred in sub-section (1) of Section 52 of the Tamil Nadu Agricultural 
Produce Marketing (Regulation) Act, 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 1989), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the 
following amendments to the Tamil Nadu Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) Rules, 1991.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules, – 
(1) in rule 24, after sub-rule (2), the following sub-rule shall be added, namely:- 
“(3) Every application for grant of a licence under Section 8-A of the Act shall be in Form 1-A and subject to the 
conditions speciﬁ ed therein along with the copy of the receipt for payment of fee speciﬁ ed in rule 27 and sent to 
the Director through the concerned Market Committee.”; 
(2) after rule 25, the following rule shall be inserted, namely:-
“25-A. (1) The Director or the ofﬁ cer authorized by him shall grant a licence under Section 8-A of the Act in 
Form 4-A and subject to the conditions speciﬁ ed therein.
(2) An ofﬁ cer authorized by the Director shall maintain register of licences issued in Form 7-A.”;
(3) in rule 27, after sub-rule (6), the following sub-rule shall be added, namely:-
“(7) A licence fee of Rs.600 (Rupees Six Hundred only) shall be payable in respect of a licence speciﬁ ed in 
Section 8-A of the Act for three years.”;
(4) in rule 29,-
