Erratum to Amendment Notification.
(Roc. No. 907/2008/G2.)
The following Erratum is issued to the Notification No. SRO C-12/2010, published at page 124 in
Part III—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette (Issue No. 50), dated 22nd December 2010 in respect of scale
of pay of Interpreter is published:—

added “+Spl. Allowance.”
