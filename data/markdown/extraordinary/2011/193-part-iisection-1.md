NOTIFICATION  BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY SECRETARIAT.
LEGISLATIVE  ASSEMBLY  SECRETARIAT
AMENDMENT TO NOTIFICATIONS BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY SECRETARIAT
DELETION OF “TAMIL NADU STATE SPORTS DEVELOPMENT CORPORATION LIMITED” AND “TAMIL NADU
SPIRIT CORPORATION LIMITED” OF PUBLIC UNDERTAKINGS SPECIFIED IN APPENDIX-E OF
TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY RULES.
[Letter No. 7573/2011-1, Legislative Assembly Secretariat (B.III), 29th June 2011.]
No. II(1)/LAS/12(n-1)/2011.
"Under Rule 211(1) of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Rules, it is hereby notified that the name of "Tamil Nadu
State Sports Development Corporation Limited" and "Tamil Nadu Spirit Corporation Limited" be deleted from the list of "Other
Public Undertakings" specified in Appendix-E of the said Rules. Consequently the following amendment is also issued:—
AMENDMENT
In Appendix-E of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Rules, under the heading "Other Public Undertakings", the
"Tamil Nadu State Sports Development Corporation Limited" occurring against Serial Number 42 and "Tamil Nadu Spirit
Corporation Limited" Occuring against Serial No 53 shall be deleted”.
INCLUSION OF "ARASU CABLE T.V. CORPORATION LIMITED" OF PUBLIC UNDERTAKINGS SPECIFIED IN
APPENDIX-E OF TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY RULES.
[Letter No. 7573/2011-2, Legislative Assembly Secretariat (B.III), 29th June 2011.]
No. II(1)/LAS/12(n-2)/2011.
Under Rule 211(1) of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Rules, it is hereby notified that the "Arasu Cable T.V.
Corporation Limited" may be added to the list of "Other Companies" specified in Appendix-E of the said Rules, the working
of which shall be examined by the Committee on Public Undertakings of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly.
Consequently the following amendments is issued:—
