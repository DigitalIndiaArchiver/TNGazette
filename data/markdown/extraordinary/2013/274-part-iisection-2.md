The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied
that the lands specified in the Schedule below have to be
acquired for a public purpose and it having already been
decided that the entire amount of compensation to be
awarded for the lands is to be paid out of public revenue.
The following declaration is issued  under Section 6 of the
Land Acquisition Act, 1894 ( Central Act I of 1894).
DECLARATION
Under Section 6 of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894
(Central Act I of 1894), The Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
declare that the lands specified in the Schedule below and
measuring 22.18.0 hectares be the same or little more or
less is needed for a public purpose to wit for the formation
of Tamil Nadu Fisheries University at Nagapattinam in
Nagoor (Muttam) Village.
A plan of the lands is kept in the office of the Revenue
Divisional Officer, Nagapattinam and may be inspected at
any time during office hours.
