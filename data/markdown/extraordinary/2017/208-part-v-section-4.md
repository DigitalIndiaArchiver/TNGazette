NOTIFICATION BY THE ELECTION COMMISSION OF INDIA
PLACE AND TIME FOR COUNTING OF VOTES FOR PRESIDENTIAL ELECTION, 2017
 No. SRO    G 24 /2017
The following Notification of the Election Commission of India, New Delhi, dated. 3rd July 2017
(12 Ashadha, 1939 (Saka) is republished:
No.479/16/2017- In pursuance of rule 27 of the Presidential and Vice-Presidential Elections Rules, 1974,
the Election Commission of India hereby appoints the 20th July, 2017 (Thursday) as the day on which, and
11.00 A.M. of that day as the time at which, the counting of votes, for the Presidential Election, 2017, shall
take place at the office of the Returning Officer in Room No.62, Parliament House, New Delhi.
(By Order)
VARINDER KUMAR,
Principal Secretary,
Election Commission of India.
Secretariat,
RAJESH LAKHONI,
Chennai-600 009,
Chief Electoral Officer and
3rd July, 2017.
Principal Secretary to Government,
Public (Elections) Department.
