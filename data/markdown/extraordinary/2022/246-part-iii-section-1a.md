In exercise of the powers conferred by section 17 read with clause (j) of sub-section (2) of section 122 of the 
Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes 
the following amendments to the Master Plan for Chennai Metropolitan Planning Area (Preparation, Publication and Approval)
Rules, 1974:-
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖගඛ.
 In the said Rules,-
1. in rule 3, for the expression starting with “(vii) recreation” and ending with “rocky Lands”, the following expression shall 
be substituted, namely:-
 “(vii) open space and recreation, (viii) agriculture, (ix) non urban and (x) other uses such as undeveloped areas, water 
bodies, waste and rocky lands. The map shall be prepared in Geographical Information System (GIS) platform or any other 
digital platform with geo-referencing.”;
 2. to rule 3, as so amended, the following proviso shall be added, namely:-
 “Provided that a present land and building use map shall be prepared before every revocation or modiﬁ cation of the 
Master Plan.”;
 3. in rule 4, after sub-rule(ii), the following sub-rule shall be inserted, namely:-
 
“(iii) the Master Plan may also provide for all or any of the following matters namely:-
