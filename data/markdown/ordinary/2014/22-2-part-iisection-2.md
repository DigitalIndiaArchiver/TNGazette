HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE DEPARTMENT
Vesting of Properties and Scheme for Administration
relating to “Professor M. Natarajan Endowment Fund”
for final MBBS Students of Government Stanley Medical
College, Chennai under the Charitable Endowments
Act.
[G.O.Ms. No.126, Health and family Welfare (H2),
16th May 2014. Vaikasi-2, Jaya
Thiruvalluar Aandu-2045.]
No.II(2)/HFW/348/2014.—In the matter of the Charitable
Endowments Act, 1890 (Central Act VI of 1890) and in the
matter of Endowment Fund for “Professor M.Natarajan
Endowment Fund” for the final year MBBS students of
Government Stanley Medical Colege, Chennai.
I. VESTING OF PROPERTY
Whereas an application has been made to the
Government of Tamil Nadu under clause (b) of sub-section
(1) of Section 6 of the Charitable Endowments Act, 1890
(Central Act VI of 1890) by Padma Laureate Dr. Mayil
Vahanan Natarajan, the donor of the Endowment Fund
specified above for vesting Rs.1,00,000/- (Rupees one lakh
only) in the Treasurer of Charitable Endowments appointed
under the said Act for the State of Tamil Nadu. The Governor
of Tamil Nadu, in exercise of the powers conferred by
Section 4 of the said Act, hereby directs that the said
amount be vested in the Treasurer aforesaid and be held
by him and his successors in office, subject to the
provisions of the said Act and to any rules, which from time
to time, may be or shall deemed to have been made
thereunder, upon trust for the purpose and the subject to
the conditions setforth in the Schemes settled under Section
5 of the said Act, for the administration of the said Fund.
II. SCHEME FOR ADMINISTRATION
Whereas an application has been made to the
Government of Tamil Nadu under clause (b) of sub-section
(1) of Section 6 of the Charitable Endowments Act. 1890
(Central Act VI of 1890) by Padma Laureate Dr. Mayil
Vahanan Natarajan, the donor of the Endowment Fund
specified above, for settling the Scheme for the administration
of the said Fund. The Governor of Tamil Nadu in exercise of
the powers conferred by Section 5 of the said Act, hereby
settles the Scheme serforth in the schedule below for the
administration of the said Fund and appoint from the date of
publication of this Notification in the Tamil Nadu Government
Gazette as the date on which the Scheme as settled shall
come into operation.
SCHEDULE
1. The endowment fund shall be called as “Professor
M. Natarajan Endowment Fund” for the final year M.B.B.S
students of Government Stanley Medical College, Chennai.
2. The Dean, Government Stanley Medical College, Chennai
shall be the adminstrator of the Fund and shall deposit the
amount furnished by the donor Padma Laureate Dr. Mayil
Vahanan Natarajan, with the Treasurer of Charitable
Endowments, Government of Tamil Nadu, Chennai who will
invest or reinvest the amount and proceeds in Government
Securities to the best interest of the Fund.
3. Out of annual interest accrued on the said Fund, a
Gold Medal shall be awarded to one student based on the
examination.
4. The Medal shall be called as “Professor M. Natarajan
Gold Medal in Orthopaedics”
5. Final Year M.B.B.S. Students of Stanley Medical College,
Chennai alone will be eligible to participate in the examination.
6. The Head of the Department of Ortho and the panel of
examiners decided by him shall conduct the examination.
7. The decision of the panel of examiners in selecting the
students for Award based on the examination conducted by
them shall be final.
8. If the medal is not awarded to any student, then the
unutilized interest amount shall be added to the above said
corpus fund.
J. RADHAKRISHNAN,
Secretary to Government.
