No. SRO B-32/2016.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Health and Family Planning Department Notification
No. SRO B-24/2015, published at page 38 of Part III—Section 1(b) of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette,
dated the 1st April 2015.
2.  The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force with effect from 1st April 2015.
AMENDMENT
In the said Notification, in the preamble, after the existing paragraph, the following paragraph shall be added, namely:-
“2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force with effect from the 16th November 2012”.
J. RADHAKRISHNAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
