[G.O. Ms. No.100, Transport(I.1) 22nd July 2011.]
No. II(2)TRA/334(d-1)/2011.
Whereas in exercise of the powers conferred by clause (1)
of the Articles 258 of the constitution of India, the President of
India has, in the Ministry of Food, Agriculture, Community
Development and Co-operation (Department of Agriculture)
Notification No. 4(1)/65, General-II, dated the 23rd April, 1966
entrusted to the Government of Tamil Nadu with their consent,
the functions of the Central Government under the Land
Acquisition Act, 1894 (Central Act I of 1894) in relation to
Acquisition of Land for the purpose for the Union in this State;
And, Whereas, it has already been decided that the
Compensation shall be paid from the public revenues;
Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by
Section 6 of the said Act, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
declares that the Land specified in the Schedule below and
measuring 0.66.0 hectare of Ariyur Village, Namakkal Taluk,
Namakkal District, be the same a little more or less are needed
for public purpose for provide approach Road due to the formation
of Salem-Karur Broad Gauge Railwayline Project. A plan of the
Lands is kept in the Office of Special Tahsildar (Land Acquisition),
Salem-Karur Broad Gauge Railwayline Project, Namakkal and
may be inspected at any time during the Office Hours.
