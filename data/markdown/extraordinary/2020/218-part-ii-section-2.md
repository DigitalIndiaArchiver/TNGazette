Whereas in the order fourth read above, the Government have extended the State-wide lockdown 
till 24:00 hrs of 30-6-2020 under the Disaster Management Act, 2005 with various relaxations ordered in 
GO.Ms.No.217, Revenue and Disaster Management (DM-II) Department, dated 3-5-2020 and GO.Ms.No.245, 
Revenue and Disaster Management (DM-II) Department, dated 18-5-2020 and amendments issued thereon with 
further guidelines and relaxations in the order fourth referred above.

Now therefore based on the proposal of Principal Secretary/Commissioner of Revenue Administration, 
the Government issue the following amendment to GO.Ms.No.262, Revenue & Disaster Management (DM-II) 
Department, dated 31-5-2020:
A) The sub-clause viii under Clause I shall be replaced as 
“All cinema halls, gymnasiums, swimming pools, entertainment parks, theatres, bars, auditoriums, assembly 
halls, beaches, tourist places, zoological parks, museum and similar places of public gathering. Sports 
complexes and stadia will be permitted to open without spectators”.
 B) The following paragraph in Annexure I is hereby deleted.
 “Public transport carriers will charge 50% additional fare over and above existing fare for all type of operations 
temporarily to compensate loss due to operating at lesser seating capacity.”.
