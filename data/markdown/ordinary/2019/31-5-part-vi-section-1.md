JUDICIAL NOTIFICATIONS
Constitution of District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court at Thiruvidaimarudhur in Thanjavur District.
(Roc. No. 1347/A/2014/G/Judn)
No. VI(1)/369/2019.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 
1873) the High Court, Madras, hereby ﬁ xes Thiruvidaimarudhur in Thanjavur District as the place at which the District 
Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Thiruvidaimarudhur, shall be located. 
NOTIFICATION-II
(Roc. No. 1347/A/2014/G/Judn)
No. VI(1)/370/2019.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 11 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873) 
the High Court, Madras, hereby directs that in Thanjavur District, the Principal District Munsif Court, Kumbakonam, shall 
cease to have local jurisdiction over the entire Thiruvidaimarudhur Taluk and the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate 
Court, Thiruvidaimarudhur shall have and exercise local jurisdiction over the entire Taluk of Thiruvidaimarudhur with eﬀ ect 
from the date on which the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate, Thiruvidaimarudhur assumes charge of that Court. 
NOTIFICATION-III
(Roc. No. 1347/A/2014/G/Judn)
No. VI(1)/371/2019.
The High Court, Madras hereby directs and notiﬁ es that the District  Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate, Thiruvidaimarudhur
in Thanjavur District shall exercise all the powers conferred on a District Munsif under Section 12 of the Tamil Nadu 
Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873) as amended up-to-date in regard to original suits and proceedings not 
otherwise exempted from his cognizance of which the amount or value of the subject matter does not exceed One 
Lakh Rupees within the local limits of his jurisdiction with eﬀ ect from the date on which the District Munsif-cum-Judicial 
Magistrate, Thiruvidaimarudhur assumes charge of that Court.
NOTIFICATION-IV
(Roc. No. 1347/A/2014/G/Judn)
No. VI(1)/372/2019.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 28 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 
1873) the High Court, Madras, hereby directs that the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate, Thiruvidaimarudhur, shall 
have and exercise jurisdiction of a Court of Small Causes under the Provincial Small Cause Courts Act, 1887 for the 
trial of suits cognizable by a Court of Small Causes upto the pecuniary limits of Rs.5000/- (Rupees ﬁ ve thousand only) 
with eﬀ ect from the date on which the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Thiruvidaimarudhur, assumes charge 
of that Court.
High Court, Madras,
