CHENNAI PORT TRUST
Madras Port Trust Employees' (Retirement) (Amendment) Regulations, 2009
(RRC/13837/2007/S)
No.SRO C-8/2009.
The notification No. G.S.R. No. 684(E), dated the 16th September 2009 issued by the Government of India,
Ministry of Shipping and published in Part II—Section 3 sub-section (i) of the Extraordinary issue of the
Gazette of India, dated the 17th September 2009 is republished hereunder as required under Section 132 (2) of
the Major Port Trusts Act, 1963:—
G.S.R. No. 684(E).—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 124, read with sub-section (1)
of Section 132 of the Major Port Trusts Act, 1963 (38 of 1963), the Central Government hereby approves the Madras Port
Trust Employees’ (Retirement) (Amendment) Regulations, 2009, made by the Board of Trustees for the Port of Chennai
and set out in the Schedule annexed to this notification.
2. The said Amendment Regulations shall be deemed to have come into force on the 30th September 2007.
SCHEDULE
Madras Port Trust Employees' (Retirement) (Amendment) Regulations, 2009
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 28 of the Major Port Trusts Act, 1963 (38 of 1963), the Board of
Trustees of the Chennai Port Trust hereby makes the following regulations:
(1) These Regulations may be called the Madras Port Trust Employees’ (Retirement) (Amendment)
Regulations, 2009.
(2) In the Madras Port Trust Employees’ (Retirement) Regulations the following amendments shall be made:
(i)
Regulation 3 of the aforesaid regulations shall be substituted as under:
“Every employee of Board shall retire from service of the Port on the afternoon of the last date of the
month in which he/she attains the age of sixty years. However, in case of employees whose date of birth is the 1st of
the month, they shall retire from the service on the afternoon of the last day of the preceding month, on attaining the
age of sixty years.”
(ii)  In Regulation 4 of the aforesaid regulations the words ‘58 years’ shall be substituted by the words
‘sixty years’.
    (F.No. PR-12016/2/2007/-PE-I)
