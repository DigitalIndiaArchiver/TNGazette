Amendments to the Tamil Nadu Liquor (Supply by Wholesale) Rules.
[G.O.Ms. No.11, Home, Prohibition and Excise (VIII), 26th March 2013.]
No. SRO A-5/2013.—In exercise of the Powers conferred by Sections 17-D and 54 of the Tamil Nadu Prohibition
Act, 1937 (Tamil Nadu Act X of 1937), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the
Tamil Nadu Liquor (Supply by Wholesale) Rules, 1983:-
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force with effect on and from the 1st April
2012.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules in rule 15, sub-rule (3) in the tabulor column, in the third column, - (I) for the expression
"74.30" against item (i) in the first column, the expression "77.60" shall be substituted; and
(2) for the expression "17.25" against item (iv) in the first column the expression "20.91" shall be substituted.
[G.O. Ms. No. 12, Home, Prohibition and Excise (VIII), 26th March 2013.]
No. SROA-6/2013.—In exercise of the Powers conferred by Sections 17-C and 17D read with Section 54 of the Tamil
Nadu Prohibition Act, 1937 (Tamil Nadu Act X of 1937), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following
amendment to the Tamil Nadu Liquor (Supply by Wholesale) Rules, 1983:-
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 14th June 2012.
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules in rule 15, sub-rule (1-A) in the tabulor column, in the third column, for the expression "51.00"
against item (iii) in the first column, the expression "113.00" shall be substituted;
R. RAJAGOPAL,
Principal Secretary to Government.

Amendments to the Tamil Nadu Liquor (Licence and Permit) Rules.
[G.O. Ms. No.14, Home, Prohibition and Excise (VIII), 29th March 2013.]
No. SROA-10/2013.—In exercise of the Powers conferred by Section 54 read with Section 17-C, 17-D, 17-E,
18-B, 18-BB, 18-C, 20 and 21 of the Tamil Nadu prohibition Act, 1937  (Tamil Nadu Act X of 1937), the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu Liquor (Licence and Permit) Rules, 1981:-
2. The amendment hereby made shall be come into force on the 1st April 2013.
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules,—
(I) in rule 17, in sub rule (b), in clause XI-B, for the sentence beginning with the words "The application shall be
accompanied" and ending with the words" for the grant of licence", the following sentence shall be substituted, namely:—
"The application shall be accompanied by the  Treasury receipt in proof of the payment of  annual privilege fee of
Rs. 1,00,000/- (Rupees One Lakh only) licence fee of  Rs. 1,000/- (Rupees One Thousand only) and application fee
of Rs. 100/- (Rupees One hundred only) for the grant of licence.";
(2) in “Form F.A.I. 13", for the expression "(a) Application fee of Rs. 1,000/- (Rupees One thousand only)", "(b) Licence
fee of Rs. 1,00,000/- (Rupees One lakh only)" and "(c) Privilege fee Rs.1,00,00,000/- (Rupees One Crore only)", the
expressions "(a) Application fee of Rs.100/- (Rupees One hundred only)",  "(b) Licence fee of Rs. 1,000/- (Rupees one
thousand only)" and "(c) Privilege fee of Rs. 1,00,000/- (Rupees One lakh only)" shall, respectively, be substituted.
P.G. THIYAGARAJAN,
Deputy Secretary to Government.

Amendments to the Tamil Nadu Liquor Retail Vending (in Shops and Bars) Rules
[G.O.Ms.No. 18, Home, Prohibition and Excise (VI), 29th March 2013.]
No. SROA-11/2013.—In exercise of the Powers conferred by Sections 17-C, 17-D, 21 and 22-D read with Section
54 of the Tamil Nadu Prohibition Act, 1937 (Tamil Nadu Act X of 1937), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the
following amendment to the Tamil Nadu  Liquor Retail Vending (in Shops and Bars) Rules, 2003:-
AMENDMENTS
In “the Rules in rule 2, in clause (oo), for the expression "at the rate of 10%,” the expression" at the rate of
0.05% shall be substituted.
[G.O. Ms. No. 19, Home, Prohibition and Excise (VI), 29th March 2013.]
No. SROA-12/2013.—In exercise of the Powers conferred by Sections 17-C, 17-D, 21 and 22-D, read with Section
54 of the Tamil Nadu Prohibition Act, 1937 (Tamil Nadu Act X of 1937), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the
following amendments to the Tamil Nadu  Liquor Retail Vending (in Shops and Bars) Rules, 2003:—
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules for rule 7, the following rule shall be substituted, namely:-
"7. Renewal of licence.— The licence issued to the Corporation under rule 4 is  renewable by the  Commissioner
of Prohibition and Excise on payment of annual Privilege fee of Rs.1,00,000/- (Rupees one lakh only), licence fee of
Rs.1,000/- (Rupees one thousand only) and application fee of Rs. 100/- (Rupees one hundred only) on or before the
28th November."
[G.O. Ms. No.20, Home, Prohibition and Excise (VI), 29th March 2013.]
No. SROA-13/2013.—In exercise of the Powers conferred by Sections 17-C, 17-D, 21 and 22-D, read with
Section 54 of the Tamil Nadu Prohibition Act, 1937 (Tamil Nadu Act X of 1937), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes
the following amendment to the Tamil Nadu Liquor Retail Vending (in Shops and Bars) Rules, 2003:—
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, after rule 9, the following rule shall be inserted, namely:—
"9A. Grant of privilege to run the bar,— The privilege of running bars may be granted to private parties by tender. The
Board of the Corporation may decide the upset price and other terms and conditions of tender, from time to time, with
the prior approval of the Commissioner of Prohibition and Excise. The Corporation, as agency shall collect the tender
amount from the successful tenderers and remit the same to the Government on or before the 25th of the following month
and the Corporation may retain 1% of the amount so collected as agency commission".
G. MALARKODI,
Deputy Secretary to Government.
