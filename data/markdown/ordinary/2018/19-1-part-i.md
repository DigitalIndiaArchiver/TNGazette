OBITUARY NOTICE
[G.O. Rt. No. 1604, Public (Special-B), 30th April 2018, 
Chithirai 17, Vilambi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2049.]
No.I/PUSP/18/2018.—The Government of Tamil Nadu 
record with deep and profound regret the untimely death 
of one of its Senior Ofﬁ cers, Thiru Ashok Ranjan Mohanty, I.A.S., 
formerly Commissioner, Archaeology, Chennai on 30th April, 
2018 at the age of 59 years.   
2. He was appointed to the Indian Administrative Service 
on   20-05-1985. He started his career as Assistant Collector 
(Training) in Virudhunagar. He held various posts in Government 
of Tamil Nadu which includes Executive Ofﬁ cer of Arulmigu 
Dhandayuthapani Swami  Temple Palani, Collector Cuddalore, 
Director Sericulture, Managing Director Tamil Nadu Medical 
Service Corporation, Additional Secretary to Government 
MA & WS Department, Special Secretary to Government 
Commercial Taxes / Industries / Agriculture Departments and 
Secretary, TNPSC / SHRC.   
3. In the death of Thiru Ashok Ranjan Mohanty, I.A.S., 
the Government of Tamil Nadu have lost one of its senior 
ofﬁ cers. The Government places on record its condolence to 
the bereaved family members.
 
GIRIJA VAIDYANATHAN,
