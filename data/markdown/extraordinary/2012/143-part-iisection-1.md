DECLARATION OF TUESDAY, 12th JUNE, 2012 AS A PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN CONNECTION WITH THE
BYE-ELECTION TO TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY FROM 180. PUDUKKOTTAI ASSEMBLY
CONSTITUENCY IN TAMIL NADU.
[G.O. Ms. No. 475, Public (Elections-I), 1st June 2012.]
No. II(1)/PUE/14(d)/2012.
      Under the Explanation to Section 25 of the Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881 (Central Act XXVI of 1881), read with
the Notification of the Government of India, Ministry of Home Affairs, No. 20-25-26, Public-I, dated the 8th June, 1957,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby declares, Tuesday, 12th June, 2012, the date on which the poll for the
Bye-election to Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly from 180. Pudukkottai Assembly Constituency will take place, to be
a public holiday for the areas comprised in the above Assembly  Constituency lying in Pudukkottai District.
     2. Further, all employees working  in Pudukkottai district but registered as voters in   180. Pudukkottai Constituency,
shall also be given a paid holiday on 12-06-2012.
     3. All Government offices including industrial establishments of the Government and Government controlled bodies
and all the educational institutions throughout the areas comprised in 180. Pudukkottai Assembly Constituency lying in
Pudukkottai District will remain closed on 12-06-2012.
DEBENDRANATH  SARANGI,
Chief  Secretary.
