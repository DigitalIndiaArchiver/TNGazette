Nomination of Chairman for the High Court Legal Services Committee.
(TNSLSA No. 3472/E/2015.)
No. VI(2)/115/2015.
In exercise of the powers Conferred under sub-section (2) of Section 8A of the Legal Services Authorities Act, 1987,
(Central Act 39 of 1987) the Hon’ble the Chief Justice, High Court, Madras is pleased to nominate the
Hon’ble Mr. Justice R. Sudhakar, Judge, High Court, Madras to be the Chairman of the High Court Legal Services
Committee with effect from 17-6-2015.
High Court Campus,
RMT. TEEKAA RAMAN,
