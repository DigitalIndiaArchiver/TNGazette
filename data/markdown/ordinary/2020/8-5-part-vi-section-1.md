2. The Fast Track Mahila Court, Erode and the Family Court, Erode will function during the Summer Vacation 2020.
 3. All the Subordinate Courts in this District shown in the above table will re-open on 1st day of June 2020 after 
Summer Vacation.
 4. No Plaint, Petitions except applications for grant of copies and for service and execution of process should be 
received by the Civil Courts during the vacation.
 5. The Oﬃ  ce of the Copyist and Process Service Establishments will be kept open throughout the Vacation provided 
that no arrest warrant should be executed during the vacation.
 The Oﬃ  ce of the other sections will remain closed.
 The arrangements will however be made,
 
(i) for transmitting the case records to the Hon’ble High Court required for appeal, revision, etc.
 
(ii) for attending to all administrative correspondences.
 6. Under Section 30 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, if a Vacation Civil Judge is appointed to Erode District, 
Urgent Plaints, Appeals and other proceedings of the urgent nature will be received and disposed of by Vacation Civil 
Judge in terms and rules that may be framed in this regard.
 7. Arrangements for disposal of urgent criminal matters will be made, if a Vacation Judge  is appointed by the 
Hon’ble High Court, Madras for this District for disposing of them.  In absence of the Vacation Judge urgent bail 
applications should be made to the Hon’ble High Court, Madras.
 8. It is further notiﬁ ed that the following are the Holidays for all the Civil Courts in Erode District for the calendar 
year 2020.
