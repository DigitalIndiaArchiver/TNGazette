Variation to the Notification under the Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax Act.
[G.O. Ms. No. 136, Commercial Taxes and Registration (B2), 31st October 2011,
Aippasi 14, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No. II(1)/CTR/25/2011.—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (3) of Section 30 of the Tamil Nadu Value
Added Tax Act, 2006 (Tamil Nadu Act 32 of 2006), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following
variation to the Commercial Taxes and Registration Department Notification No.II(1)/ CTR/12(R-15)/2011,
published at page 7 of Part II—Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary,
dated the 11th July 2011:—
VARIATION
In the said Notification, the expression “Videsh Sanchar Nigam Limited” shall be omitted.
SUNIL PALIWAL,
 Secretary to Government.
