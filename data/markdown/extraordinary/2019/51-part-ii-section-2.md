The Government of Tamil Nadu has been satisﬁ ed 
that the lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below have 
to be acquired due for industrial purposes to wit for a 
formation of Tindivanam to Nagari New Broad Gauge 
Railway Line, and it having already been decided that

lands is to be paid out of the lands controlled or managed

sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition 
of land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 
10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu 
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu 
hereby acquires the lands in No.38, Vellathur Group 
Athivaragapuram Village in respect of Pallipattu Taluk of 
Tiruvallur District as speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below and 
measuring 69170 Sq.meters to the same, a little more or 
less needed for industrial purposes, to wit, for formation 
of Tindivanam to Nagari New Broad Gauge Railway Line.
