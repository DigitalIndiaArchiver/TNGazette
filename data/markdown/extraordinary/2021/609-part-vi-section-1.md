NOTICE UNDER SECTION 3(1) OF TAMIL NADU ACQUISITION OF LAND FOR INDUSTRIAL PURPOSES ACT, 
1997 READ WITH THE TAMIL NADU LAND ACQUISITION LAWS (REVIVAL OF OPERATION, AMENDMENT AND 
VALIDATION)  ACT,  2019
No.VI(1)/376(a)/2021.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that the land / lands specified in the Schedule below 
has / have to be acquired for industrial purposes, for formation of 'Cut and Cover Meenakshi College Ramp' and having 
already been decided that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the land / lands is / are to be paid out 
of the funds managed by the Chennai Metro Rail Ltd. The following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 3 
of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act 1997 
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the land/lands specified in the Schedule below 
and measuring 672 Sq.mt to the same, a little more or less needed for industrial purposes, to wit, for the formation 
of 'Cut and Cover Meenakshi College Ramp'.
The plan of the lands are in the office of the Collector, Chennai, Taluk Office, Egmore and office of the District 
Revenue Officer (LA), Chennai Metro Rail Limited, Koyambedu, Chennai–600 107 and may be inspected on any day 
during office hours.
