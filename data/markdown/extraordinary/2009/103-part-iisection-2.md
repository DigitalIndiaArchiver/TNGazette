HOME DEPARTMENT
DECLARATION OF CERTAIN POLICE OFFICERS PERSONNEL AS DESIGNATED OFFICERS
UNDER THE CONTROL OF THE ELECTION COMMISSION.

No. II(2)/HO/176(a)/2009.
Under Section 28A of the Representation of the People Act, 1951 (Central Act XLIII of 1951), the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby declares all Police Officers from the level of Director General of Police, Additional
Director General of Police (Law and Order), Additional Director General of Police and Commissioner of
Police, Chennai, Commissioner of Police, Chennai Suburban Commissionerate, Additional Directors General
of Police, Inspectors General of Police, Zonal Inspectors General of Police, North, South,
Central and West, Commissioners of Police, Salem, Madurai, Coimbatore, Tiruchirappalli and Tirunelveli
Range, Deputy Inspectors General of Police, Superintendents of Police of the Districts, Sub-Divisional
Police Officers, Officers immediately subordinate to them and up to the level of Head Constables and
Police Constables (including Home Guards) and any other Police Officer performing in any way election
related duties, as Designated Officers for the conduct of General Elections to the Lok Sabha, 2009.  All the
said Officers shall be deemed to be on deputation to the Election Commission for the period commencing
on and from the date of the Notification calling for the said Elections and ending with the date of declaration
of the results of the said Elections  and accordingly, the said Officers shall, during that period, be subject
to the control, superintendence and discipline of the Election Commission.
S. MALATHI,
Principal Secretary to Government.
