NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
HIGHWAYS AND MINOR PORTS DEPARTMENT
AGREEMENT BETWEEN TAMIL NADU ROAD DEVELOPMENT COMPANY LIMITED AND GOVERNMENT OF
TAMIL NADU LEVY OF TOLLS FOR IMPROVEMENT AND MAINTENANCE OF EAST COAST ROAD.
NOTIFICATION-I
[G. O. Ms. No. 132, Highways and Minor Ports (HV2), 20th July 2012.]
No. II(2)/HWMP/431(g-1)/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (c) of sub-section (1) and clause (c) of sub-section-2 of the
Indian Tolls Act, 1851 (Central Act VIII of 1851), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment
to the Highways Department Notification No. II(2)HW/241(c-1)/2002, dated the 21st March 2002, published at
Pages 1-2 of Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 21st March 2002 as
subsequently amended.
AMENDMENT
In the said Notification, for the expression 31st July 2012 wherever it occurs, the expression 31st January 2013
shall be substituted.
NOTIFICATION-II
[G. O. Ms. No. 132, Highways and Minor Ports (HV2), 20th July 2012.]
No. II(2)/HWMP/431(g-2)/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (b) of sub-section 4  of the Indian Tolls Act, 1851 (Central Act
VIII of 1851), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Highways Department Notification
No. II(2)HW/241(c-2)/2002, dated the 21st March 2002, published at Page 3 of Part II—Section 2 of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 21st March 2002 as subsequently amended.
AMENDMENT
In the said Notification, for the expression 31st July 2012 wherever it occurs, the expression 31st January 2013
shall be substituted.
