PERSONNEL AND ADMINISTRATIVE REFORMS DEPARTMENT
Ad hoc Rules relating to temporary post of Principal Private Secretary (Deputy Secretary Cadre)
for Tamil Nadu General Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 53, Personnel and Administrative Reforms (H2), 6th April 2010,
Г°Ж’В°Q 23, FВјГөЕ“ГҖГөЛң ГқвҖЎВҙ-2041.]
No. SRO B-35/2010.вҖ”In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following rules.
The rules hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 9th day of June 2008.
RULES
The Special rules applicable to the holders of the post of Private Secretary in category 4 in Class XII of the Tamil
Nadu General Service shall apply to the holders of the temporary post of Principal Private Secretary, subject to the
modifications specified in the following rules:вҖ”
1. Constitution.вҖ”The post shall constitute a separate category in the said Class of the said Service.
2.
Appointment.вҖ”(a) Appointment to the post of Principal Private Secretary in the Departments of Secretariat
other than Finance and Planning, Development and Special Initiatives Departments shall be made by promotion from
among the holders of the post of Senior Private Secretaries in the Departments of Secretariat under one unit other than
Finance and Planning, Development and Special Initiatives Departments.
(b)
Appointment to the category of Principal Private Secretary under one unit shall be made by the Secretary
to Government, Personnel and Administrative Reforms Department from among the eligible persons in the cadre of Senior
Private Secretary.
(c)
Selection of persons for appointment to the post shall be made on grounds of merit and ability, seniority
being considered only where merit and ability are approximately equal.
3.
Unit of Appointment.вҖ”All Departments of Secretariat except Finance and Planning, Development and Special
Initiatives Departments shall be treated as a single unit for this purpose.
4.
Pay.вҖ”There shall be paid to the holders of the post, a monthly pay calculated in the scale of pay of
Rs.15,600-39,100+Rs.7,600 (Pay Band 3).
K.N. VENKATARAMANAN,
Secretary to Government.
