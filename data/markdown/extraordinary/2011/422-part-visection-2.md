FRESH NOTIFICATION—ISSUED—REGARDING.]
(S.O. No. 114/2011/TNSEC/ME-1.)
No. VI(2)/559(a)/2011.
Whereas, the ordinary election to fill up the offices of the Councillor of Ward No. 15 of Madurai City Municipal Corporation,
Chairman of Vellakoil Municipality, Tiruppur District, Chairman of Karumathampatty Town Panchayat, Coimbatore District and Ward
Member of Ward No. 7 of Ezhudesam Town Panchayat, Kanniyakumari District, were inter-alia notified to be held in accordance
with the programme of election fixed in the notification of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission published with
S.O. No. 45/2011/TNSEC/ME-1, dated 21st September 2011.
Whereas, the District Election Officers/District Collectors of Coimbatore, Kanniyakumari, Tiruppur and the Returning Officer/
Commissioner, Madurai City Municipal Corporation have reported that due to the death of candidates of the respective Corporation,
Municipality and Town Panchayats, as the case may be, the election to fill up the said offices have been postponed as per rule
34(1)(c) of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities, Municipalities and Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006.
Whereas, after taking all material circumstances into account and in pursuance of rule 34 of Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats,
Third Grade Municipalities, Municipalities and Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission has
considered necessary to commence the election process afresh to the respective Corporation, Municipality and Town Panchayats.
Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (1) of Section 43-B of the Tamil Nadu District
Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920), sub-section (1) of Section 10(D) of the Madurai City Municipal Corporation
Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act XV of 1971) and rule 22 of Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities, Municipalities and
Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission, for the purpose of filling up of ordinary vacancies
in the offices of the Councillor / Ward Member and Chairmen of Corporation, Municipality and Town Panchayats, specified in the
Table annexed to this Notification, hereby calls upon the electors of the said wards to elect their respective Councillor/Ward Member
and Chairmen of Corporation, Municipality and Town Panchayats, in accordance with the following programme of election:—
