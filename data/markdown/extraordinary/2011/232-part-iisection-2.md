No. II(2)/ADTW/334(c)/2011.
Whereas the Government of Tamil Nadu have  been
satisfied that the land specified in the Schedule below have to
be withdrawn from the Land Acquisition which was required for
the public purpose to wit for the provision of house sites to
Adi Dravidar in No. 62. Peddikuppam Village, Gummidipoondi
Taluk, Tiruvallur District as the scheduled land is not required
at present for the said purpose.
Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred under
sub-section (1) of Section 48B of the Land Acquisition Act,
1894 (Central Act 1 of 1894), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby withdraws from the Acquisition of Land specified in the
Schedule below in respect of which the Draft Notification under
Section 4(1) of Land Acquisition, Central Act 1 of 1894 was
approved in G.O. No. (3D) 590, Adi Dravidar and Tribal Welfare
Department, dated 3rd June 1991 and published at page 7  of
Part II—Section 2 of Tamil Nadu  Government Gazette, dated
19th June 1991 Declaration under Section 6 of the said Act
approved in G.O.Ms. No. 146, Adi Dravidar and Tribal Welfare
Department, dated 22nd June 1992 published in
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, No. 374,
Part II—Section 2, dated 23rd June 1992 and Draft Direction
under Section 7 of the said Act was approved by the Government
in their Letter No. 42502/LA-II/93-1 Adi Dravidar and Tribal Welfare
Department, dated 9th March 1994 published in the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette at page No. 4, Extraordinary
Issue No. 137, Part II—Section 2, dated 11th March 1994 as it
is not required for the public purpose to wit, for the provisions
of house sites to Adi Dravidar in 62. Peddikuppam Village,
Gummidipoondi Taluk, Tiruvallur District.
THE SCHEDULE
Tiruvallur District, Gummidipoondi Taluk,
No. 62. Peddikuppam Village.
Government, dry, S.No. 242/9, belonging to K.A. Chellappan,
son of Asthagiripillai, bounded on the north by S.No. 236,
east by S.No. 244, south by S.No. 242/10 and west by
S.No. 242/8—0.08.5 hectare.
A.S. JEEVARATHINAM,
