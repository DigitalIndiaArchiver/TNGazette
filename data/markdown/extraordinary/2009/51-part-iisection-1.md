NOTIFICATIONS  BY  THE  LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY SECRETARIAT
LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY SECRETARIAT
VACATION OF SEAT IN TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY DUE TO RESIGNATION OF
THIRU R.S. RAJAKANNAPPAN, MEMBER OF THE TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY.
[Letter No. 4259/2009-1, TNLAS(B III), 25th February 2009.]
No. II(1)/LAS/(h)/2009.
Whereas, Thiru R.S. Rajakannappan, a Member of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly
representing the 197. Ilayangudi Assembly Constituency, in his letter, dated 25th February, 2009 addressed
to me as Speaker of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly, has resigned his seat as Member of the said
Assembly with effect from 25th February 2009.
Now, therefore, I, R. AVUDAIAPPAN, Speaker of the said Assembly hereby accept the resignation of
Thiru R.S. Rajakannappan, as envisaged under Rule 22 of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Rules
as it is in accordance with Rule 21 of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Rules. Under Article 190(3)(b)
of the Constitution of India, his seat has, therefore, become vacant with effect from the above date.
Secretariat,
R. AVUDAIAPPAN,
Chennai-600 009,
Speaker,
Dated 25th February, 2009.
Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly.
