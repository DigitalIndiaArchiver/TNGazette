NOTIFICATIONS BY THE ELECTION COMMISSION OF INDIA
NOTIFICATION OF ELECTION COMMISSION OF INDIA REGARDING AMENDMENT TO THE LIST OF
RETURNING OFFICERS FOR THE ASSEMBLY CONSTITUENCIES IN THE STATE OF TAMIL NADU.
No. SRO G-22/2011.
The following Notification of the Election Commission of India, Nirvachan Sadan, Ashoka Road, New Delhi-110 001,
dated 20th July, 2011 [29 Asadha, 1933 (Saka)] is republished:—
No. 434/TN/2011(3).—In pursuance of the provisions of Section 21 of the Representation of the People Act, 1951
(1951 of 43), the Election Commission, in consultation with the State Government of Tamil Nadu,  hereby makes
the following further amendment in its Notifications  No. 434/TN/2011 (3), dated 13th January, 2011 as amended  from time to time,
namely:—
In the column (2) of  the table appended to the said notification, the existing entry  “District Revenue Officer (Land Acquisition),
Tamil Nadu Road Sector Project, Chennai” against 16-Egmore (SC) Assembly Constituency as shown in column (1)
shall be substituted by the entry “District Revenue Officer (Land and Estate), Corporation of Chennai, Ripon Buildings,
Chennai-600 003”.
(By Order)
