Order:—No. 412, Home (SC), 4th May 2017.
The following amendment is issued to the Tamil Nadu Chief Minister’s Police Medal for Excellence in Public Service
Rules, made in the Government Order first read above:-
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, the following shall be substituted in Rule 7, namely:-
“The recipients of this Medal shall be granted a cash reward of Rs.25,000/- irrespective of the rank”
instead of the existing rules “The recipients of this Medal shall be granted a cash reward of Rs.5,000/- irrespective of
the rank”
2.
The following amendment is issued to the Tamil Nadu Chief  Minister’s Police Medal for Excellence in Investigation
Rules made in the Government Order second read above:-
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, the following shall be substituted in Rule 6, namely:-
“The recipients of this Medal shall be granted a cash reward of Rs.25,000/- irrespective of the rank” instead of the
existing rules “The recipients of this Medal shall be granted a cash reward of Rs.5,000/- irrespective of the rank”.
3.  This order will come into force with immediate effect.
(By Order of the Governor)
