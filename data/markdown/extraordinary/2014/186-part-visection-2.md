ORDER:
WHEREAS, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission inter-alia notified casual election to Ward No. 1 and 6 of Chengalpattu
Municipality, Kancheepuram District scheduled to be held on 18-9-2014 in the Notification first read above;
WHEREAS, a representation received from Thiru.G.S.Faziullah, Independent candidate of Ward No.1 and Thiru.K.Thanigaivelu,
Independent candidate of Ward No.6 of Chengalpattu Municipality stated that their nominations were rejected by the Returning
Officer without valid reasons;
WHEREAS, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission called for report from the  Election Observer appointed by the
Election Commission for Kanceepuram District;
WHEREAS, the Election Observer, Kancheepuram District vide reference second read above has reported that the Returning
Officer of the said elections to Ward No. 1 and 6 of Chengalpattu Municipality has rejected the nominations of Thiru.G.S.Faziullah,
Independent candidate of Ward No.1 and Thiru.K.Thanigaivelu, Independent candidate of Ward No.6 of Chengalpattu Municipality
by stating that the candidates have not filled up the particulars regarding income tax and kept blank the requisite columns in their
respective affidavits;

WHEREAS, sub-rule (5) of rule 27 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities, Municipalities and
Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006, provides that the Returning Officer shall not reject any nomination paper on the ground
of any defect which is not of a substantial character and as per proviso to sub-rule (7) of rule 27 of the aforesaid Rules, if an
objection is raised by the Returning Officer or is made by any other person, the candidate concerned may be allowed time to
rebut it not later than 11.00 a.m. on the next day but one following the date fixed for scrutiny and that the Returning Officer shall
record his decision on that nomination on the adjourned date;
WHEREAS, it is observed from the report received from the Election Observer that the Returning Officer of Chengalpattu
Municipality has not acted as per the proviso to sub-rule (7) of rule 27 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Third Grade
Municipalities, Municipalities and Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006 that the candidates concerned may be allowed time to
rebut it not later than 11.00 a.m. on the next day but one following the date fixed for scrutiny and that the Returning Officer shall
record his decision on that nominations on the adjourned date;
WHEREAS, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission has got convinced that Form 9 has not been issued by the Returning
Officer in accordance with the aforesaid rule provisions and thus an unforeseen circumstance has arisen;
NOW, THEREFORE, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission, in pursuance of rule 139 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats,
Third Grade Municipalities, Municipalities and Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006, hereby orders that Form 9 – List of
contesting candidates with regard to Ward No.1 and 6 of Chengalpattu Municipality, Kancheepuram District, is cancelled and
also orders that all the election proceedings of the Returning Officer for the said offices notified in the Statutory Order first read
above in pursuance of rule 22 to 30 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities, Municipalities and
Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006 so far as it relates to the above offices be held to be null and void.
(By Order of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission)
