BHARATHIDASAN UNIVERSITY, TIRUCHIRAPPALLI
Election to the Syndicate and Standing Committee on Academic Affairs
(Ref. No. BDU/Ord.Sen.Syn.Elec./A2/22325/2017.)
No.VI-3(b)/28/2017.
I. Election to the Syndicate :
Class II - Other Members - sub-sec. (b) (1) of Section 24 under Chapter IV of the Bharathidasan University Act,
1981 -  * Two Members Elected by Principals of affiliated Colleges from among themselves in accordance with the system
of proportional representation by means of the single transferable vote.
Class II - Other Members - sub-sec. (b) (2) of Section 24 under Chapter IV of the Bharathidasan University Act, 1981-
*Two Members Elected by Teachers of affiliated Colleges, other than the Principals, from among themselves who are the
members of the Senate, in accordance with the system of proportional representation by means of a single transferable
vote.
II. Election to the Standing Committee on Academic Affairs:
Class II - Other Members - sub-sec. (2) of Section 23 Under Chapter III of the Bharathidasan University Act,
1981 - Two members elected by Principals of affiliated Colleges from among themselves in accordance with the system
of proportional representation by means of the single transferable vote.
Nominations are invited in the prescribed form which can be obtained from the Registrar and Returning Officer on
a requisition and submission of a Demand Draft for Rs. 500/- (Five Hundred only) drawn in favour of Bharathidasan University,
Tiruchirappalli on or after the date of notification, i.e., 24-11-2017 with a stamped self-addressed envelope to the value
of Rs. 25/- (Rupees twenty five only) or in person.
Nomination paper shall be enclosed in an envelope superscribed as follows, as the case may be:-
“Nomination to the ————————————————————by————————————————”
The Members are requested to send their nomination by Registered Post (with Acknowledgement due) so as to reach
the Registrar and Returning Officer within the date and hour fixed or delivered to the Registrar and Returning Officer either
in person or by a messenger within the date and hour fixed.
Nomination papers that are not sent as stated above will be declared invalid.

THE TAMIL NADU Dr. M.G.R. MEDICAL UNIVERSITY, CHENNAI
Notice of Election of Two Members to the Governing Council of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University,
Chennai by the Senate from among its Members under Section 21(4)(a) of the T.N. Dr. M.G.R. Medical
University, Chennai, Act, 1987 (T.N. Act 37 of 1987.)
[No. E III(3)/37790/2017.]
No. VI-3(b)/29/2017.
Notice is hereby given that an election by direct polling is to be held in the University premises for electing
Two members to the Governing Council of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai, as per clause (a) of
sub-section (4) of Section 21 of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai, Act, 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 37
of 1987) by the Senate from among its members to be filled up the arising vacancies. The term of membership in the
Governing Council will be for a period of three years from the date of publication of the results of the election or the
date on which he/she ceases to hold the post of Senate member of this University whichever is earlier.
2. Forms of Nomination papers may be obtained from the Registrar, who is the Returning Officer from 21-11-2017 to
04-12-2017 between 11.00 a.m. and 3.00 p.m. (on all working days).
3. Nomination paper may be  enclosed in an envelope addressed to the Registrar / Returning Officer and superscribed
"Nomination for election to the Governing Council" as indicated below, and sent by Registered post (Acknowledgement
Due), so as to be received by the REGISTRAR / Returning Officer not later than 4.00 p.m. on 04-12-2017 or delivered to the
Returning Officer during Office hours in person or by his Proposer or by messenger under acknowledgement within the date
and hour mentioned above.
