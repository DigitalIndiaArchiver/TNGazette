APPOINTMENT OF CERTAIN PERSON AS VICE-CHANCELLOR OF DISTANCE EDUCATION,

No. II(2)/LAW/913(h)/2013.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-sections (1) and (3) of Section 12 of the Tamil Nadu
Dr. Ambedkar Law University Act, 1996 (Tamil Nadu Act  43 of 1997), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
appoints Dr. (Prof.) P. Vanangamudi, Director, Department of Distance Education, the Tamil Nadu
Dr. Ambedkar Law University, Chennai as the Vice-Chancellor of the Tamil Nadu Dr. Ambedkar Law
University, Chennai, for a period of three years from the date of his assuming office.
G. JAYACHANDRAN,
Secretary to Government.
