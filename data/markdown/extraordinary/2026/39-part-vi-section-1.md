The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that the lands specified in the schedule below to be acquired for 
the Highways purpose to wit to Development of Industrial Corridor of Cheyyar SIPCOT Phase – I for “Formation of Link 
Road Connectivity from Valayakaranai to Manampathy” at No.13.Elanagar Village, Uthiramerur Taluk, Kancheepuram 
District and it having already been decided that the amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid out 
of the funds controlled and managed by the Chief Engineer (C&M), Chennai after having considering the cause shown 
by the owner or other persons having interest on such lands if any, as the case may be, do hereby publish the following 
notice under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001(Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002), the Governor 
of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands specified in the Schedule below measuring an extent of 69141 Sq.mtrs., to the 
same, a little more or less needed for the Highways purpose to wit to Development of Industrial Corridor of Cheyyar 
SIPCOT Phase – I for the “Formation of Link Road Connectivity from Valayakaranai to Manampathy” at No.13.
Elanagar Village, Uthiramerur Taluk, Kancheepuram District.
The Plan of the lands is kept in the Office of the Special District Revenue Officer (Land Acquisition), Highways 
Kancheepuram and Office of the Special Tahsildar (LA) Highways, Unit – 6, Kancheepuram may be inspected on any 
day during office hours.
