ERRATA TO NOTIFICATION UNDER LAND ACQUISITION ACT
[G.O. Ms. No. 8, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (MC1), 23rd January 2009,
Thai 10, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
The following errata are issued to the Notification No. II(2)/MAWS/397(b)/2008 approved in
G.O. (Ms) No. 172, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (MC1) Department, dated 9th September 2008
and published at page No. 2 and 3 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, Issue No. 272,
dated 9th September 2008.
Against R.S. No. 3856-15 for “60 Square feet” read as “960 square feet and for “Total—18225 Square feet
or 7 Grounds 425 Square feet” read as “Total—18225 Square feet or 7 Grounds 1425 Square feet”
AMENDMENT TO NOTIFICATION UNDER LAND ACQUISITION ACT
[G.O. Ms. No. 9, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (MC1), 23rd January 2009,
Thai 10, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/32(c)/2009.
The following amendment is issued to the Notification No. II(2) MAWS/435(a)/2008 approved in
G.O. (Ms) No. 184, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (MC-1) Department, dated 24th September
2008 and published at page No. 1 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, Issue No. 282,
dated 24th September 2008.
AMENDMENT
In the said Notification, the preamble for the expression “6 (1) of the Act”, the expression “9(1) of the Act”
shall be substituted.
