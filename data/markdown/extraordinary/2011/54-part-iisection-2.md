NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
HOUSING AND URBAN DEVELOPMENT DEPARTMENT
ACQUISITION OF LANDS
[G.O. Ms. No. 34, Housing and Urban Development
[UD3(2)], 11th February 2011.]
No. II(2)/HOU/71(b)/2011.
Whereas, it appears to the Government of Tamil Nadu
that the Lands specified in the Schedule below and situated
in Adambakkam Village, Alandur Taluk, Kancheepuram
District are needed for a public purpose, to wit for the
purpose of implementation of Mass Rapid Transit System
alignment between Velachery to St. Thomas Mount, notice
to that effect is hereby given to all to whom it may concern
in accordance with the provisions of sub-section (1) of
Section 4 of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894 (Central Act I of
1894) and whereas, it has become necessary to acquire the
immediate possession of the Lands specified in the Schedule
below, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that the
lands be acquired under the provisions of sub-section (1) of
the Section 17  of the said Act.
Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by
sub-section (2) of Section 4 of the said Act, the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby authorizes the  Special Tahsildar
(Land Acquisition), Mass Rapid Transit System, Phase-II,
Extension at Thirumailai Railway Station, Chennai-4 his staff
and workmen to exercise the powers conferred  by the said
sub-sections.
Under Section 3(c) of Act, the Governor of Tamil Nadu,
hereby appoints the Special Tahsildar (L.A.), Mass Rapid
Transit System, Phase-II, Extension at Thirumailai Railway
Station, Chennai-4, to perform the functions of the Collector
under the said Act.
[ 1 ]
DTP—II-2 Ex. (54)
Under sub-section (4) of Section 17 of the said Act, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that in view of the
urgency of the case, the provisions of Section 5A of the said
Act, shall not apply in this case.
