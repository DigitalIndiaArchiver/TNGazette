GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAQF BOARD, CHENNAI
Taking over of Puliyankulam Khanmian Avuliyakkal Dhargah Waqf, Puliyankulam, Madurai District
Under Direct Management.  
(Rc.No.12358/09/B8/MDU.)
No. VI-3(a)/116/2018.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Waqf Act 1995, as amended by Act 
27/2013, the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in item No.78/18 in Rc.No.12358/09/B8/MDU, dated : 27-08-2018 
has ordered to take over the PULIYANKULAM KHANMIAN AVULIYAKKAL DHARGAH WAQF, PULIYANKULAM, MADURAI 
DISTRICT under the direct Management of the Tamil Nadu Waqf Board for a period of SIX MONTHS from the date of 
publication and to appoint the SUPERINTENDENT OF WAQFS, MADURAI as its Executive Officer.
Chennai - 600 001,	
N.S. MOHAMED ASLAM,
3rd October 2018.	
District Revenue Officer/	 	
	
	
Chief Executive Officer,
	
Tamil Nadu Waqf Board.
