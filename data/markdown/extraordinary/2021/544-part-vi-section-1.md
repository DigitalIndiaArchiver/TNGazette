and Extending in of SH-79 Tiruchengode - Erode Road from KM 86/300 to KM 89/070 at Kadachanallur

(1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (TN Act 34 of 2002).

same, a little more or less is needed for Chennai-Kanniyakumari Industrial Corridor Project for State Highways purpose 
of Strengthening and Extending in SH-79 Tiruchengode - Erode Road from KM 86/300 to KM 89/070.
