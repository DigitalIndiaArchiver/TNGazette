TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY SECRETARIAT
AMENDMENTS TO THE TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY RULES
[Letter No.2901/2021-19, TNLAS (Bills-III), dated 23.8.2021.]
No. SRO A-14(f)/2021.
The following amendments to the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Rules shall be deemed to have been approved by the 
Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly on 22.8.2021 under sub-rule (3) of rule 259 of the said Rules and shall come into force on 
the date of publication in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 23rd August 2021.
