No. SRO C-20/2017.
In pursuance of sub-section (2) of Section 124 read with sub-section (1) of Section 132 of the Major Port Trust Act,
1963 (38 of 1963), the V.O. Chidambaranar Port Trust hereby publishes the following regulations as required under said
sections, namely:-
1. Short Title And Commencement:
(1) These regulations may be called the V.O.Chidambaranar Port Trust (Issue of Entry Permit for
Vehicles/Equipments/Persons) (Amendment) Regulations, 2017.
(2) These regulations shall come into force from the date of its approval and final publication in the Official Gazette
by the Central Government.
2. In the Tuticorin Port Trust (issue of Entry Permit for Vehicles/Equipments/Persons) Regulations, 2005 (herein after
referred to as the principal regulations), in regulation (1), -
(i) In sub-regulation (1), for the words and brackets “Tuticorin Port Trust (issue of Entry Permit for Vehicles/
Equipments/Persons)” the words and brackets “V.O. Chidambaranar Port Trust (Issue of Entry Permit for Vehicles/
Equipments/Persons)” shall be substituted;
(ii) In sub-regulation (2), for the words “These will not apply” the words “These regulations shall not apply to
visitors” shall be substituted.
3. In the principal regulation, in regulation 2, -
(i) In clause (b), for the words “the Board of Trustees of the Port of Tuticorin”, the words” the V.O. Chidambaranar
Port Trust” shall be substituted;
(ii) In clause (d), for the. words “Tuticorin Port Trust”, the words, “V.O. Chidambaranar Port Trust” shall be
substituted.
4. In the principal regulations, in regulation 3, -
(i) In clause (ii) for the word “Chairman”, the words ”Traffic Manager” shall be substituted;
(ii) In clause (iii) after the words “designation”, the words “aadhaar number”, shall be inserted;
(iii) In clause (iv), after the words “The entry permit shall be”, the words “issued at the pass section and shall be”,
shall be inserted.
5. In the principal regulations, in regulation 4, for sub-regulation (c), the following sub-regulation shall be
substituted, namely:-
“(c) In the case of equipment, in addition to the documents, mentioned at sub regulation (2), certificates obtained
from the competent authority under the Dock Safety Regulations, 1990 i.e., Form-II, Form-IV and Form-VI
shall also be produced”.
6. In the principal regulations, in regulation 5, -

Regulations”, the following shall be substituted, namely:- ”In the case of entry of persons into the port, the following shall
be construed as violations under these regulations:”

“(i) furnishing of false information or misrepresentation of facts by the persons or agents for obtaining entry
permit”;

6.
Penalties; -
(a) For violation by persons in obtaining or using the entry permit, the permit of the person shall be suspended
or cancelled by the Traffic Manager.
(b) For violation in respect of vehicle entry, listed in sub-regulation (b) of regulation 5, penalty of two
hundred rupees and or banning of entry into the Port for a period up to one week on each occasion would be
imposed by the Traffic Manager and if violation of any kind is noticed on a third occasion within six months, the vehicle
shall be banned entry into the Port area for a period of one month, in addition to levy of fine by the
Traffic Manager.
(c) Dwell time and penalty for commercial vehicles inside the Port area shall ..
(i) for the purpose of levy of penalty for overstay of commercial vehicles inside the Port area, the time shall
count from Check Post I or II on entry to Check Post I or II on exit and for excess stay time within the Port
area beyond the specified duration shall involve penalty and any relaxation due to reasons beyond the
control of either parties is required, then, the relaxation of penalty is left to the discretion of the Traffic
Manager.
(ii) The Dwell time shall be fixed by the Port in consultation with the Port Users and notified to the users
in the form of a “Trade Notice”.
