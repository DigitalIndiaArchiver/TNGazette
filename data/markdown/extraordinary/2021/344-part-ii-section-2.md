2. In the G.O. 2nd read above, the date of freezing of Boundaries of Administrative Units was extended upto 
31st December 2020.
3. In the G.O.3rd read above, the date of freezing of Boundaries of Administrative Units was further extended upto 
31st March 2021.
 4. In the letter 4th read above, the Registrar General and Census Commissioner, India has stated that owing to 
the prevailing conditions due to COVID-19 pandemic in the Country, the time period for conducting the ensuing census 
is not yet decided. In view of this, the  date  of freezing of Boundaries of Administrative Units  has  been further 
extended upto 31.12.2021 and therefore the Registrar General and Census Commissioner, India has requested to issue 
necessary instructions to all concerned in this State to give eﬀ ect to the changes in the Administrative Boundaries, if 
any latest by 31.12.2021.
5. In the circumstances stated above, the Government hereby extend the date of freezing of Boundaries of 
Administrative Units  viz., municipalities, revenue villages, towns, tehsils, police stations, sub-divisions, districts, etc., in 
connection with Census Operations upto 31st, December 2021. 
6. All the pending proposals, if any aﬀ ect the changes in the existing Boundaries of municipalities, revenue villages, 
towns, tehsils, police stations, sub-divisions, districts etc., may therefore be ﬁ nalized on or before 31st December, 2021 
under intimation to the Director of Census Operations, Tamil Nadu. 
7. The Commissioner of Revenue Administration, Chennai, the Director General of Police, Chennai, the Commissioner 
of Rural Development and Panchayat Raj, Chennai, the Director of Municipal Administration, Chennai, the Director of Town 
Panchayats, Chennai, the Collectors of all Districts, the Commissioner of Greater Chennai Corporation, the Commissioners, 
Municipal Corporations are directed to adhere to the above decision of the Government.
