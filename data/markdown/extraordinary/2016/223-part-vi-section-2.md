WHEREAS, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission has issued an Order viz., the Tamil Nadu Local Bodies
Election Symbols (Reservation and Allotment) Order, 2016 to provide for specification, reservation, choice and allotment
of symbols at elections to local bodies in the State of Tamil Nadu and for  matters connected therewith; and
WHEREAS, it has become expedient to notify the list of recognised political parties and the symbols respectively
reserved for them, the list of registered - unrecognised political parties and the lists of free symbols for the use in the
local bodies election:
NOW, THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred by paragraphs 4 and 6 of the Tamil Nadu Local Bodies
Election Symbols (Reservation and Allotment) Order, 2016 and in supersession of the earlier notifications, the Tamil Nadu
State Election Commission hereby notifies,—
(a) in Table 1,
the recognised political parties and the symbols respectively reserved for them;
(b) in Table 1-A,
the list of registered - unrecognised political parties.
(c)
in Table 2,
the free symbols for the use in election of Members of Village Panchayats;
(d) in Table 3,
the free symbols for the use in election of Presidents of Village Panchayats;

the free symbols for the use in election of Members of Panchayat Union Councils, Members
of Town Panchayats, Councillors of Municipalities and Councillors of Municipal Corporations;

(f)

the free symbols for the use in election of Members of District Panchayats.
In respect of party based Elections, every independent candidate shall declare in the nomination
paper first filed by him/her or on his/her behalf, his/her choice of three free symbols from the
list of free symbols for that election in Table 4 or Table 5 as the case may be, indicating the
order of his/her preference.
Every candidate who is setup by a registered - unrecognised political party shall declare in the
nomination paper first filed by him/her or on his/her behalf, his/her choice of three free symbols
collectively from the list of free symbols notified in Table 4 and Table 5 for the party based
elections.
