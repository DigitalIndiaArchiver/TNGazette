No. SRO B-18/2021.— In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, 
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Basic Service 
(Section 19 in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 2016). 
2. The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 18th December 2020. 
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖගඛ
In the said Special Rules,- 
 
(1) in rule 1, under Class IV,- 
 
(a) against Category 7, for the expression “Cleaners”, the expression “Cleanliness Workers” shall be  
 
 
substituted; 
 
(b) against Category 8 Category 12 and Category 34, the expressions “Sanitary Workers”,     
 
         “Sweepers” and “Assistant Sweepers” shall, respectively, be omitted;
 
(c)  against Category 32, for the expression "Watchmen-cum-Sweepers”, the expression 
          "Watchmen-cum-Cleanliness Workers” shall be substituted; 
 
(d)  against Category 79, for the expression "Waterman-cum-Sweeper”, the expression  
 
 
 
 
"Waterman-cum-Cleanliness Worker” shall be substituted; 
(2) in rule 3, in the second proviso, for the expression “Sanitary Workers”, the expression “Cleanliness Workers” 
shall be substituted;  
(3) in rule 5, in sub-rule(1), in the sixth proviso, for the expression “Sweepers, Sanitary Workers”, the expression 
“Cleanliness Workers” shall be substituted.  
 
   S. SWARNA, 
 
Principal Secretary to Government.
