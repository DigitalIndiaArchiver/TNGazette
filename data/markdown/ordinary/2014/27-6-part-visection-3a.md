GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI.
Taking over Hazrath Syed Sha Ali Masthan Chaman Sha Ali Dargah, St. Thomas Mount, Alandur,
Kancheepuram District under Direct Management.
(Lr. Rc. No. 15014/12/B4/KPM)
No. VI-3(a)/56/2014.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 (1) of the Wakf Act, 1995, as amended by
Act 27/2013, the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in item No. 34/14 in Rc.No.15014/12/B4/KPM, dated 18th February
2014 has ordered to take over HAZRATH SYED SHA ALI MASTHAN CHAMAN SHA ALI DARGAH, St. Thomas Mount,
Alandur, Kancheepuram District under the Direct Control of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of one year from the
date of Publication in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette and to appoint Wakf Inspector, Kancheepuram as its
Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED MEERAN,
10th July 2014.
District Revenue Officer/Chief Executive Officer (i/c),
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
