Under section 28-A of the Representation of the People Act, 1951 (Central Act XLIII of 1951), the Governor of Tamil 
Nadu hereby declares the Director General of Police, Tamil Nadu, Chennai 600 004 and the Additional Director General of 
Police (Law and Order), Chennai-600 004 and the Superintendent of Police, Erode District and all Police Officials subordinate 
to them upto the level of Police Constables and  any other Police Officers / Officials performing in any way the election 
related duties for the conduct of Bye-Election to be held on the 27th February, 2023 to 98.Erode (East) Legislative Assembly 
Constituency as Designated Officers and all the said Officers shall be deemed to be on deputation to the Election Commission 
for the period commencing on and from the date of the notification calling for the said Election and ending with the date of 
declaration  of the  result  of  the  said  Election  and accordingly, the said Officers shall, during that period, be subject to 
the control, superintendence and discipline of the Election Commission..
(By Order of the Governor)
