ORDER OF THE  ELECTION  COMMISSION OF INDIA.
DISQUALIFICATION OF A CANDIDATE CONTESTED FROM 225. TIRUCHENDUR ASSEMBLY CONSTITUENCY IN THE
BYE-ELECTION TO THE TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE  ASSEMBLY, 2009  FOR FAILURE TO LODGE ACCOUNTS
OF ELECTION EXPENSES UNDER SECTION 10A OF THE REPRESENTATION OF THE PEOPLE ACT, 1951.
No. SRO/G-22/2010.
The following order of the Election Commission of India, Nirvachan Sadan,  Ashoka Road, New Delhi-110 001,
dated 6th August, 2010 [ 15  Shravana, 1932 (Saka)] is republished:—
No. 76/TN-LA/2009/Bye (1):—Whereas, the Election Commission of India is satisfied that Sh.R. Sornasekar a contesting
candidate at the Bye-Election to the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly,  2009 from 225. Tiruchendur Assembly Constituency has
failed to lodge his account of election expenses as required by the  Representation of the People Act, 1951 and the Rules made
thereunder;
And, whereas, the said candidate could not submit his representation/account of election expenditure till date
under the said rule.
And, whereas, in the supplementary report of the District Election Officer and District Collector, Thoothukkudi
has stated that he has not yet filed his account of election expenses;
Now, therefore, in pursuance of  Section 10A of the said Act, the Election Commission hereby declares Sh.R. Sornasekar
to be disqualified for being chosen as and for being a member of either house of Parliament or the Legislative Assembly or
Legislative Council of a State or Union Territory for a period of three years from the date of  this order.
(By Order)
