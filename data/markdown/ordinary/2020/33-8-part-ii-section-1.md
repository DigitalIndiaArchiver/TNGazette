 AMENDMENT TO NOTIFICATION
[G.O. Ms. No.55, Backward Classes, Most Backward Classes & Minorities Welfare (BCC), 15th July 2020, 
Aani 31, Saarvari, Thiruvalluvar Aandu -2051.]
No.II(1)/BCMBCMW/15/2020.—In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (a) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu 
Backward Classes, Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes (Reservation  of seats in Educational Institutions and 
of appointments or posts in the Services under the State) Act, 1993 (Tamil Nadu Act 45 of 1994), the Governor of 
Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Backward Classes, Most Backward Classes and Minorities 
Welfare Department Notiﬁ cation No.II(1)/BCMBCMW/36(a)/2008, Published at pages 1-9 of Part II-Section 1 of the 
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, Extraordinary, dated the 5th September 2008.
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖග
In the said Notiﬁ cation, in THE SCHEDULE, in the heading, LIST OF BACKWARD CLASSES, BACKWARD CLASS 
CHRISTIANS, BACKWARD CLASS MUSLIMS, MOST BACKWARD CLASSES AND DENOTIFIED COMMUNITIES, under the 
sub-heading I-List of Backward Classes, after Serial No. 109 and the entry relating thereto, the following Serial Number and 
the entry shall respectively, be inserted, namely:- 
"109-A Thiyya"
 
CHANDRA MOHAN. B,
 
Principal Secretary to Government.
