HOME, PROHIBITION AND EXCISE DEPARTMENT
AMENDMENT TO THE TAMIL NADU BREWERY RULES, 1983.
[G.O.Ms. No.21, Home, Prohibition and Excise (III), 21st July 2015.]
No. SRO A-11(a)/2015.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 54 of the Tamil Nadu Prohibition Act, 1937
(Tamil Nadu Act X of 1937), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the
Tamil Nadu Brewery Rules, 1983:-
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules,-
(1)  in rule 2, in clause (c),  after the expression “into bottles”,  the expression “cans” shall be
inserted;
(2)  in rule 26, in sub-rule (2), for the expression “twenty five paise”, the expression “rupees two”
shall be substituted;
(3)  in rule 32-A, in sub-rule (2), for the expression “rupees four  thousand”, the expression
“rupees one lakh” shall be substituted;
(4)   in rule 32-B,-
(a) for the expression “payment of excise duty and export   fee under rule 26”, the following
expression shall be substituted, namely :-
“payment of export fee under rule 26 and a bank guarantee at the rate of nine rupees per
bulk litre for Lager Beer and at eleven rupees per bulk litre for Strong  Beer as a performance guarantee”;

(b) for the proviso, the following proviso shall be substituted, namely :-
“Provided that on the production of satisfactory proof of the consignment having reached the destination, the
bank guarantee shall be returned to the export licensee and in the event of failure to produce satisfactory proof of
delivery of the consignment mentioned in the export permit, at the destination, the bank guarantee shall be invoked
and in addition, the export licensee shall also be liable to pay the excise duty payable in respect of such
consignment as if it were sold for local consumption within the State.”;
(5)   in FORM B-11, in item (7), for the expression “excise duty and export fee”, the expression “export fee
and bank guarantee”  shall be substituted;
(6)   in FORM B-12, in item (6), in the heading in column (4), for the expression “excise duty and export
fee”, the expression “export fee and bank guarantee”  shall be substituted.
