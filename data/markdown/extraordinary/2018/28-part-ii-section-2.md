HOME DEPARTMENT

DIRECTION TO THE STATE TRANSPORT AUTHORITY, TAMIL NADU AND THE REGIONAL TRANSPORT 
AUTHORITIES REGARDING THE FIXATION OF CESS ON FARES FOR STAGE CARRIAGES OPERATED 
UNDER THE MOTOR VEHICLES ACT.
AMENDMENT TO NOTIFICATION
[G.O.Ms.No. 38, Home (Transport-VII), 23rd January 2018.]
No. II(2)/HO/79(i)/2018.
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (i) of sub-section (1) of Section 67 of the Motor Vehicles 
Act, 1988 (Central Act 59 of 1988), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the 
Home Department Notiﬁ cation No. II(2)/HO/79(d-6)/2018, published at pages 7-8 in Part II—Section 2 of the 
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary dated the 19th January, 2018.
 
The amendment hereby made shall come into force on the 20th day of January 2018.
AMENDMENT
In the said notiﬁ cation, after para 2, the following para shall be added.
“3. The private operators shall also be permitted to collect the amount mentioned in para 2 to maintain their 
own Accident Compensation and Accident Prevention and Toll Fee Fund”.
 
 
NIRANJAN MARDI,
