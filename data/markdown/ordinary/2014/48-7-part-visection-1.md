Grant of No Objection Certificate
[Rc.No. E4(2)/254/39663/2014.]
Form-A2
[Notice under rule 35A(4) of Tamil Nadu Cinema (Regulation) Rule 1957.]
No. VI(1)/358/2014.
Whereas Thiru R. Rangarajan, Director, AGS Cinemas Private Limited, No. 34, Thirumalai Road,
T. Nagar, Chennai-600 017, has applied for “No Objection Certificate” for the construction of a multiplex containing Five
Screens with Food Court and Restaurant at S.Nos. 44/1, 45/1, 45/2A and 45/2B in Maduravoyal Village, Ambattur Taluk in
Thiruvallur District.
Notice is hereby given that any person having any objection to the grant of “No Objection Certificate” for the above said
site on the grounds of non-compliance by the applicant with any of  the provisions of the Tamil Nadu Cinemas (Regulation)
Act, 1955 (T.N. Act IX of 1955) or with any of the Rules there under should file his objection in writing with the Licensing
Authority (Commissioner of Police, Greater Chennai) within 15 days of publication of this Notice.
1.
Any objection filed after the period shall be liable to be summarily rejected.
Chennai-600 007,
S. GEORGE,
28th August 2014.
Commissioner of Police,
Greater Chennai Police.
“Winding up of the Affairs of the Ind.  No. 1338, Piranmalai Coconut Fibre Extraction & Allied Products
Industrial Co-operative Society Limited, Sivagangai District and Appointment of Official Liquidator”.
(Rc.No. 37201/ICH/2012)
No. VI(1)/359/2014.
“Under the powers conferred on the Registrar of Industrial Co-operatives under Section 137 (2)(b) of the Tamil Nadu
Co-operative Societies Act 1983 (Act 30 of 1983), the activities of the Piranmalai Coconut Fibre Extraction & Allied Products
Industrial Co-operative Society Limited, Ind. No. 1338, Sivagangai District have been ordered to be wound up with immediate
effect by the Principal Secretary/Industries Commissioner and Director of Industries and Commerce and the Registrar of
Industrial Co-operatives, Chennai-28 vide Proc. No. 37201/ICH/2012, dated 26-11-2014.
Under Section 138(1) of the same Act, the Assistant Director (Indl. Co-ops), TAICO Bank, Chennai has also been
appointed as the Official Liquidator of the said society for the purpose of liquidation”.
SWARAN SINGH,
Principal Secretary/Industries Commissioner
Chennai-600 028,
and Director of Industries and Commerce/
26th November 2014.
Registrar of Industrial Co-operatives.
