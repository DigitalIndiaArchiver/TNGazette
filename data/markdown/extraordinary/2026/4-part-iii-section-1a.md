AMENDMENTS TO THE TAMIL NADU CO-OPERATIVE SOCIETIES RULES, 1988
[G.O.Ms.No.138, Co-operation Food and Consumer Protection (CJ1), 22nd December 2025, 
மாார்கழி 7, விசுவாாவசு, திருவள்ளுவர் ஆண்டு-2056.]
No. SRO A-1(a)/2026.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-sections (1) and (2) of section 180 of the Tamil Nadu Co-operative 
Societies Act,  1983 (Tamil Nadu Act 30 of 1983), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments 
to the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Rules, 1988:-
Amendments.
In the said rules,–
(1) in rule 20, in sub-rule (1), in clause (c), the expression “family card number” shall be omitted;
(2) in rule 52, in sub-rule (7),–
(i) in clause (b), in item (i), the expression “family card number” shall be omitted;
(ii) in clause (e), in item (i), the expression “family card number” shall be omitted; and
(3) in Schedule-I, in Form No.16, Serial Number 6-A and the entry thereof shall be omitted.
	
SATYABRATA SAHOO,
	
Principal Secretary to Government.
