NOTIFICATIONS BY GOVERNMENT
PLANNING, DEVELOPMENT AND SPECIAL INITIATIVES DEPARTMENT.
Amendments to the Adhoc Rules relating to the Programmer in the
State Planning Commission in Tamil Nadu General Service
[G.O. Ms. No. 62, Planning, Development and Special Initiatives (SPC), 15th July 2016,
Aani 31, Thunmugi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2047.]
No. SRO B-44/2016.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the adhoc rules published in the Planning
and Development Department Notification No. SRO B-48/99, at page 54 of Part III—Section 1(b) of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 31st March 1999, as subsequently amended:-
2. (a) Amendments (1) and (2) shall be deemed to have come into force with effect from the 26th August 2010.
(b) Amendment (3) shall be deemed to have come into force with effect from the 31st January 2014.
(c) Amendments (4) and (6) shall be deemed to have come into force on the date of publication of this Notification
in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
AMENDMENTS
In the said rules,-
(1) in rule (1), for the expression, "Programmer", the expression, "System Analyst", shall be substituted.
(2) in rule 3,-
(a) in clause (ii), the expression "or" shall be omitted:
(b) in clauses (iii) and (iv) shall be omitted.
(3) in rule 5, for sub-rule (b), the following sub-rule shall be substituted, namely:-
"(b) Other qualifications.-
No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post unless he possesses the
following qualifications, namely:-
B.E. in Computer Science or Information Technology; or
M.C.A. or M.Sc., in Information Technology with two years experience in software";
(4) for rule 6, the following rule shall be substituted, namely:-
"6. Probation.- (a) Every person appointed to the post by direct recruitment shall, from the dated on which he joins duty,
be on probation for a total period of two years on duty within a continuous period of three years:
Provided that every person appointed to the post by recruitment by transfer shall, from the date on which he
joins duty, be on probation for a period of one year on duty within a continuous period of two years";
(5) to rule 8, the following proviso shall be added, namely:-
"Provided that with effect on and from the 1st January 2006, the monthly pay shall be calculated in the Pay Band
of PB-3 of Rs.15600-39100 with Grade Pay of Rs.5400";
(6) after rule 8, the following rule shall be added, namely:-
"9. Saving clause.- Nothing contained in rule 5 (b) shall adversely affect the persons who are already in service."
S. KRISHNAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
