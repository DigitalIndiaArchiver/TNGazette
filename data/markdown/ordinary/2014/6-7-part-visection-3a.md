TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI
Framing Scheme in respect of Sungam Pallivasal Wakf, Madurai Town and District.
(Rc. No. 17133/04/B8/MDU)
No. VI-3(a)/16/2014.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 69 of the Wakf Act 1995, the
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in item No. 67/13, dated 19th November 2013 has framed a Scheme for the Wakf
Viz., Madurai Town, Sungam  Pallivasal for better administration of the Wakf.
F. ABDUL RAZICK,
Chennai-600 001,
Chief Executive Officer,
5th February 2014.
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
Taking over of Jamia Masjid Wakf, Koonimedu, Tindivanam, Villupuram District under Direct Management.
(Rc. No. 17549/13/B4/Villupuram)
No. VI-3(a)/17/2014.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act 1995 as amended by
Act 27 of 2013,  the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in Item No. 158/13, dated 30th December 2013 in
Rc.No. 17549/13/B4/Villupuram has ordered to take over the management of Jamia Masjid Wakf, Koonimedu, Tindivanam,
Villupuram District  under the direct control of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board a period of one year from the date of the Board's
Order and to appoint the Wakf Inspector, Villupuram as its Executive Officer.
F. ABDUL RAZICK,
Chennai-600 001,
Chief Executive Officer,
5th February 2014.
