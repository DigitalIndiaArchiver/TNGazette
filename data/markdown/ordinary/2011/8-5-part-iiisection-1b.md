ENVIRONMENT AND FORESTS DEPARTMENT
Ad hoc Rules relating to Temporary Post of Assistant Conservator of Forests in the Department of Environment
[G.O. Ms. No. 19, Environment and Forests (EC2), 31st January 2011.]
No. SRO B-12/2011.—In exercise of the powers conferred by  the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following rules.
2. The rules hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 29th March 2005.
RULES
The General Special Rules applicable to the holders of the permanent post of Assistant Conservator of Forests
Category-2 of the Tamil Nadu Forest Service shall apply to the holders of the temporary post of Assistant Conservator
of Forests, in the Department of Environment, subject to the modifications specified in the following rules:—
2. Constitution.—The post shall constitute a distinct category in the said service.
3. Appointment.—By Transfer from category 2 of the Tamil Nadu Forest Service.
4. Pay.—There shall be paid to the holder of the post a monthly pay calculated in the scale of pay of
Rs. 8000-275-13500:
Provided that on and from 1st January 2006 the monthly pay shall be calculated in the scale of
Rs. 15600-39100 + Rs. 5400 as Grade Pay (PB.3).
V. IRAI ANBU,
  Secretary to Government.
HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE DEPARTMENT
 Amendments to the Ad hoc Rules relating to Posts of Prosthetic Craftsman, Prosthetic Technician Grade-II,
Prosthetic Technician Grade-I, Senior Prosthetic Technician and Prosthetic Craftsman (Helpers).
[G.O. Ms. No. 51, Health and Family Welfare (C2), 7th February  2011, Thai 24, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No. SRO B-13/2011.—In exercise of the powers conferred by  the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the  Rules issued with the
Health and Family Planning Department Notification S.R.O.No. A-836 of 1971, published at page 870, in Part V of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 13th October 1971, as subsequently amended.
AMENDMENTS
In the said  Rules,—
(1) in rule 1, for the expression “Prosthetic Craftsman (Helpers),” the expression “Leather Worker” shall be substituted;
(2) in rule 5, for sub-rule (b), the following sub-rule shall be substituted, namely:—
“(b) Other Qualifications.—No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post unless he possess the following
qualifications, namely:—
 (a) Must have passed Secondary School Leaving Certificate Examination; and
 (b) Must have undergone one year course in Footwear Manufacturing and obtained the certificate issued by the
Directorate of Technical Education or its equivalent from any other Institution recognized by the Government of Tamil Nadu
or Government of India”.

 Amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Municipal Commissioners' Subordinate Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 28, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (ME.1), 28th January 2011.]
No. SRO B-16/2011.—In exercise of the powers conferred by  the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Municipal
Commissioners' Subordinate Service (Section 23B of Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1970).
2. The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 15th March 2004.

(1) in rule 2, for clause (a), the following clause shall be substituted, namely:—
“(a) By direct recruitment from among the categories of Municipal Employees in Class-l or Class-I ‘A’
of the Tamil Nadu Municipal General Service and the candidates to be appointed shall be selected based on seniority and
assessment by a Selection Committee consisting of the following officials:—

(2) in rule 4, in the Table, in column (1), for item (a), the following item shall be substituted, namely:—
 “(a) By direct recruitment from the categories of Municipal Employees in Class I or Class I A of the Tamil Nadu
Municipal General Service.”;
(3) in rule 5, after the expression “15th March of every year”, the following expression shall be added, namely:—

possess the prescribed qualifications shall be 15th March of every year.” ;
