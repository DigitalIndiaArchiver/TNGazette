HIGHER EDUCATION DEPARTMENT
Amendment to ad hoc Rules relating to Technical Education Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 447, Higher Education (B1), 10th November 2008.]
No. SRO B-54/2008.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following Amendments to the rules published with the Education and
Public Health Department Notification No. SRO.A 670 of 1964, dated the 1964 at pages 670-672 of Part V of the
Fort St. George Gazette, dated the 8th July 1964.
The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 15th June 1993.
AMENDMENT
In the said rules, in rule 5, in Clause (i) in the entries under the first proviso, the entry,—
“5. Environmental Engineering"
shall be deleted.
K. GANESAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
HOME  DEPARTMENT
Amendments to Special Rules for Tamil Nadu Police Subordinate Services.
[G.O. Ms. No. 1357, Home (Pol.VI), 3rd October 2008, Purattasi 17, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2039.]
No. SRO. B-55/2008.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Sections 8 and 10 of the Tamil Nadu District  Police
Act, 1859 (Central Act XIV of 1859) and Sections 9 and 11 of the Chennai City Police Act, 1888
(Tamil Nadu Act III of 1888), read with the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Police Subordinate Service
(Section 34 in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1986).
2. The amendment hereby made shall come into force with effect from 3rd October 2008.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules, in Annexure II under the Tabular column in column (2), "(i) against the Category 2,
Sub-Inspectors of Police, Category 2A, Sub-Inspctors of Police (finger Print) (Men and Women)” in column (1) thereof,
the following proviso shall be added at the end namely:—
“Provided also that for the appointment of a candidate who is a member of Backward Class or Most Backward
Class or Denotified communities, the upper age limit shall be 30 years”;
(ii) against Category 5, “Reserve Sub-Inspectors (Men and Women)” in column (1) thereof, the following proviso
shall be added at the end namely:—
“Provided also that for appointment of a candidate who is a member of Backward Class or Most Backward Class
or Denotified communities, the upper age limit shall be 30 years.”;
(iii) against “Category 7, Constables (Men and Women) (Other than Band Constables, Office of the Director
General of Police) and Reserve Constables” in column (1) thereof, the following proviso shall be added, at the end
namely:—
“Provided also that for appointment of a candidate who is a member of Backward Class or Most Backward Class
or Denotified communities, the upper age limit shall be 26 years.”.

No. SRO.B-56/2008.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,

Subordinate Service (Section 17 in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1970).
