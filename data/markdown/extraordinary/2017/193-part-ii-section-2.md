HOME DEPARTMENT
CONSTITUTION OF DISTRICT LEGAL SERVICES AUTHORITIES AT DHARMAPURI, ARIYALUR AND
TIRUPPUR DISTRICTS UNDER THE STATE LEGAL SERVICES AUTHORITY ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 499, Home (Courts-IV), 7th June 2017.]
No. II(2)/HO/502(a)/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 9 of the Legal Services Authorities Act,
1987 (Central Act 39 of 1987), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, in consultation with the Chief Justice of the
High Court of Madras hereby constitutes a District Legal Service Authority for each of the Districts of
Dharmapuri, Ariyalur and Tiruppur to exercise the powers and perform the functions conferred on, or
assigned to the District Authority, under that Act.
NIRANJAN MARDI,
Additional Chief Secretary to Government.
