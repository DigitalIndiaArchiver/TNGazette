The Governor of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that the lands specified in the schedule below
are  required under TNRSP II for Highways purpose to wit for upgradation of the  SH-89 Nanguneri-
Parathavaram-Uvari Road Kilometre 0/000 to Kilometre 35/200 in South Nanguneri  and other Villages,
Nanguneri and Rathapuram Taluks, Tirunelveli District  and it having already been decided that the
entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid out of the funds controlled
and managed by the Divisional  Engineer  (Highways),  Tamil Nadu Road Sector Project –II, Tirunelveli
after having considered the cause shown by the owner  or other persons having interest in the said
lands as the case may be do hereby publish the following notice under sub-section (1) of Section 15
of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of
2002)  the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the dry  and Natham lands specified in the
schedule below and measuring  an extent of 7.14.80 hectare  or 71480 Square Metre to the same  a
little more or less needed for TNRSP –II Highways purpose, to wit for upgradation of  the
State Highways Road (SH-89) – Nanguneri - Parathavaram-Uvari Road Kilometre 0/000 to
Kilometre 35/200 in South Nanguneri and other Villages in Nanguneri and Rathapuram Taluks,
Tirunelveli District.
The plan of the lands under acquisition are kept in the office of the Special District Revenue Officer
(Land Acquisition Office), Tamil Nadu Road Sector Project-II,  Tiruchirappali  and office of the
Special Tahsildar, Tamil Nadu Road Sector Project II, Tirunelveli may be inspected at any time during
office hours.
