NOTIFICATION UNDER TAMIL NADU LAND REFORMS  (FIXATION OF CEILING ON LAND) ACT
Surplus Lands Holdings of Thiru Mohammed Ibrahim
[G.O. Ms No. 426, Revenue [LR I (2)], 28th November 2012.]
No. II(1)/REV/41/2012.—Under sub-section (1) of Section 18 of the Tamil Nadu Land Reforms (Fixation of Ceiling on Land)
Act, 1961 (Tamil Nadu Act  58 of 1961), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notifies that the surplus lands specified in the
schedule below are required for a public purpose. If any further details about the lands are required, they can be had from the
office of the Joint Commissioner (Land Reforms), Mayiladuthurai during Office hours.
