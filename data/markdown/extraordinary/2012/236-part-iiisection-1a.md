HOME, PROHIBITION AND EXCISE DEPARTMENT
AMENDMENT TO THE TAMIL NADU LIQUOR RETAIL VENDING (IN SHOPS AND BARS) RULES, 2003.
[G.O. Ms. No. 43, Home, Prohibition and Excise (VI),  24th August 2012.]
No. SRO A-23(a)/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Sections 17-C, 17-D, 21 read with Section 54 of the Tamil Nadu Prohibition Act, 1937
(Tamil Nadu Act X of 1937), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following Amendment to the Tamil Nadu Liquor Retail
Vending (in Shops and Bars) Rules, 2003.
2.
The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 29th November 2011.
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, for rule 7, the following rule shall be substituted, namely:—
“7 Renewal of Licence.—The licence issued to the Corporation under rule 4 is renewable by the Commissioner of Prohibition
and Excise on payment of an annual privilege fee of Rs. 500 Crores (Rupees five hundred crores only) as renewal fee in four equal
instalments, on or before 28th November, 28th February, 28th May and 28th August”.
R. RAJAGOPAL,
Principal Secretary to Government.
