In Government Order 1st read above, the Government 
have accorded administrative approval for  implementation 
of Revamped Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima  Yojana in all the 
districts of Tamil Nadu except Chennai from Kharif 2020 
season as per the existing Government of India guidelines 
(revised).
2. In Government Order second read above, the 
Government have ordered that the Revamped Pradhan 
Mantri Fasal Bima  Yojana shall be implemented in all 
the Districts of Tamil Nadu except Chennai during Special 
and Rabi 2020-2021 by adopting new Co-insurance Model 
on 80:20 proportionate sharing basis.
3. In Government Order third read above, the Government 
have approved the Cluster-wise Implementing  Agencies 
selected by the Tender finalising  Committee and also 
approved the district-wise, crop-wise, Actuarial Premium 
Rates quoted by the selected Insurance Companies. The 
Government have permitted the Director or Agriculture to 
issue work orders IFFCO-TOKIO for Clusters I,II&III and 
Agriculture Insurance Company of India Limited for Clusters, 
IV, V&VI.
4. In that Government Order the Government have 
accorded administrative sanction for a sum of Rs.1470.7124 
crore (Agriculture Rs. 1436.328 crore, Horticulture, Rs. 
34.3844, crore) towards the settlement of State share of 
premium subsidy.
5. It is submitted that the 48th State Level Coordination 
Committee on Crop Insurance Meeting for notification of 
Districts, Crops & Insurance Units for Rabi (Other Crops), 
2020-2021 for implementation of Revamped 
Pradhan 
Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (RPMFBY) during Rabi Season, 
2020-2021 for Rabi crops other than Paddy-II, Maize-II, 
Cotton-II & Onion-II was held on 12th November 2020.
6. In Continuation, in Government Order fifth read above, 
the Government have ordered for notification of the Crops, 
Districts, Firkas, Revenue Villages, Insurance Units and 
Approved Premium Rates for Agricultural and Horticultural 
Crops for the effective implementation of Revamped Pradhan 
Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (RPMFBY) during Rabi Season 
2020-2021, Among others, Banana is one of the notified 
Horticulture crops for 31 Districts (Thanjavur-6 Units) and 
Tapioca for 19 Districts (Thanjavur-Nil).
7. The Director of Horticulture and Plantation Crops 
in his letter sixth read above has stated that for only 6 
units in Banana have been notified for Rabi 2020-2021 and 
accordingly the said units have been notified and enrolment 
is in progress, Meanwhile, the Farmers of Thanjavur District 
requesting to insure the Banana planted in un-notified Firkas 
and also, the Deputy Director of Horticulture, Thanjavur has 
requested the Director of Horticulture and Plantation Crops to 
consider inclusion of 13 such new units of Banana coverage 
under Revamped Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima  Yojana during 
Rabi 2020-2021 for which the cut-off date for enrolment 
ends on 1st March 2021.
8. The Director of Horticulture and Plantation Crops in 
his letter sixth read above has requested the Director of 
Agriculture to include three units of Tapioca in Thanjavur 
District for coverage under Revamped Pradhan Mantri Fasal 
Bima Yojana during Rabi season 2020-2021.
9. In this regard, the Insurance Company has been 
requested by the Director of Horticulture and Plantation 
Crops as per para No. 7.2.1.7 of the Revamped Operational 
Guidelines.

10. In Accordance with the Guidelines or Revamped 
Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima  Yojana and with an aim to 
safeguard the interest of the farmers of Thanjavur District, the 
Director of Horticulture and plantation Crops has requested 
the Government to approve notification of the 13 Additional 
Insurance Units at Thanjavur Districts for Banana Crop 
and inclusion of 3 Insurance units of Tapioca during Rabi 
2020-2021 under Revamped Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima 
Yojana and to issue "Go Ahead" orders for enrolling farmers. 
He has also requested that the inclusion of 13 additional 
units and 3 Insurance Units of Tapioca may be placed 
in the next State Level co-ordination Committee on Crop 
Insurance (SLCCCI) meeting for ratification.
11. Further, IFFCO-TOKIO has accepted to include the 
new crop "Tapioca" for notification in 3 insurance Units of 
Thanjavur district at Firka level during Rabi 2020-2021 and 
has fixed 30% as Actuarial Premium Rate. He has also 
informed that the Tapioca was included in the last year 
tender and the Actuarial Premium Rate (APR) quoted by 
Agriculture Insurance Company of India Limited was 30%. 
The sum insured for Tapioca is Rs. 46,683/- per Hectare 
and the cutoff date of enrolment ends on 1st March 2021.
12. As per the operational guidelines, the Director of 
Agriculture has requested the Additional Secretary (Credit) 
and Chief Executive Officer to direct the Technical Support 
unit of Government of India to verify and approve the Actuarial 
Premium Rate fixed by IFFCO-TOKIO for Tapioca (30%) 
in 3 Insurance Units and Banana (25%) for 13 additional 
Insurance Units. He has also sent the the yield data for 
10 years. Expected area insured, expected sum insured.
13. As requested by the Director of Agriculture, the 
Government of India have issued go ahead order and 
requested to ensure that such increase in Insurance Units 
notified and notification of additional crops don’t have 
implications on L1 status of implementing insurance company.
14. In this context. In Government D.O. Letter Ninth read 
above, the Government have issued "Go ahead" order to the 
Director of Agriculture/Director of Horticulture and Plantation 
Crops for enrolling the Farmers in the 13 additional Insurance 
units at Thanjavur District for Banana Crop and inclusion of 
3 Insurance Units at Thanjavur District at the Sum insured 
and approved premium rates of last year for Tapioca for the 
Rabi season 2020-2021 under Revamped Pradhan Mantri 
Fasal Bima  Yojana subject to the condition mentioned 
in Government of India letter dated 18th December 2020.
15. The Government, after careful examination of the 
proposal of the Director of Agriculture accept the same and 
accordingly hereby, to issue notification for 13 additional 
Insurance units at Thanjavur District for Banana Crop and 
inclusion of 3 Insurance Units at Thanjavur District at the 
sum insured and approved premium rates for Tapioca for 
the Rabi season 2020-2021 under Revamped Pradhan Mantri 
Fasal Bima  Yojana as shown in the Annexure to this 
order subject to the condition mentioned in Government 
of India letter dated 18th December 2020 and publication 
in the State Gazette.
16. The claims under the Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima 
Yojana Scheme shall be settled on the basis of yield data 
furnished by the Director, Department of Economics and 
Statistics as per prescribed cut-off dates for the notified 
crops/districts/firkas/revenue 
Villages

scheme for Rabi 2020-2021 season and not on the basis 
of the Annavari Certificate/ Gazette notification declaring the 
area as Drought/Flood/Cyclone affected etc., by the District 
Collectors.

[G.O. Ms. No. 184, Home (Cts-III), 26th April 2021].
No.II(2)/HO/310/2021.—In exercise of the powers 
conferred by the Second paragraph of Section 4 of the

High Court, Madras hereby fixes the number of Subordinate 
Judges to be appointed for the Subordinate Judge's Court 
at Thiruvottiyur in Tiruvallur District as ONE with effect from 
the date on which the Subordinate Judge, Thiruvottiyur 
assumes charge of that Court.

[G.O. Ms. No. 184, Home (Cts-III), 26th April 2021].
No.II(2)/HO/311/2021.—In exercise of the powers

[G.O. Ms. No. 184, Home (Cts-III), 26th April 2021].
