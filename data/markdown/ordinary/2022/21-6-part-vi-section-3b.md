THE TAMIL NADU Dr. M.G.R. MEDICAL UNIVERSITY, CHENNAI
NOTICE OF ELECTION.
Notice of election of one Professor to the Senate of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai by the 
Teachers of all Affiliated Dental Colleges from among themselves under Section 18(3) (b) of the Tamil Nadu 
Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai, Act 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 37 of 1987).
[No. EIII(1)/09068/2022.]
No. VI-3(b)/15/2022.
Notice is hereby given that an election by postal voting is to be held for electing one Professor to the Senate 
of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai, as per clause (b) of sub-section (3) of Section 18 of the 
Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai, Act 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 37 of 1987) by the Teachers of all 
Affiliated Dental Colleges from among themselves. The term of membership in the regular vacancy will be for a period 
of three years from the date of election or upto the date on which he/she ceases to hold the post of Professor of an 
Affiliated Dental Colleges, whichever is earlier.
(2)	 Forms of Nomination papers may be obtained from the office of the undersigned from 25.05.2022 to 06.06.2022 
between 11.00 a.m and 4.00 p.m (on all working days)
(3)	 Nomination papers duly filled in shall be submitted in an envelope addressed to the Registrar / Returning 
Officer and super scribed "Nomination for election to the Senate Under Section 18 (3) (b)" as indicated below, by 
Registered Post with Acknowlegement Due so as to receive by the Registrar/Returning Officer, not later than 4.00 p.m. 
on 06.06.2022 or to be delivered to the Returning Officer during office hours in person or by his/her proposer or by 
messenger under acknowledgement within the date and hour mentioned above.

The nomination papers which are not sent by Registered Post with Acknowlegement Due or are not delivered in 
person by the nominee or by his/her proposer or by messenger, as required above, shall be declared invalid.
(4)	 Nomination papers will be taken up for scrutiny at 4.45 p.m on 06.06.2022.
(5)	 List of valued nominations will be published at 5.30 p.m on 06.06.2022 in the University premises.
(6)	 Notice of withdrawal of candidature made as per the General Statutes of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical 
University is on or before 3.00 p.m on 13.06.2022.
(7)	 List of final valid nominations will be published in the University premises at 5.30 p.m. on 13.06.2022. A copy 
of final valid nomination and the final list of voters will be despatched to the candidates.
(8)	 A numbered declaration paper, a ballot paper with the name of the candidates bearing on it, the Returning Officer's 
initials, a cover for sending Ballot Paper and an envelope containing the address of the Returning Officer will be sent 
to the address entered against the name of the voter in the final electoral roll through the Heads of the Institutions/
College in which he/she is working, on or before 24.06.2022 (Friday). No ballot paper will be issued in the office to 
the voters on the day of election. The cover containing the ballot paper and the declaration form in the envelope to 
be sent by Registered Post, so as to reach the Registrar/Returning Officer not later than 4.00 p.m. on 11.07.2022.
