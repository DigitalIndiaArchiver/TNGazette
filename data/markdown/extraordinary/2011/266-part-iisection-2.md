Whereas, it has been brought to the notice of the Government by the Vice-Chairman and more than two third
of the Members of the Manikandam Panchayat Union, Trichirappalli District that Thiru A.T.T. Senguttuvan, Chairman
of the above said Panchayat Union had conducted four meetings of Panchayat Union Council, dated 22-10-2007,
16-4-2008, 19-9-2008 and 12-01-2009 without intimating the same to the members of the above said Panchayat
Union Council and included 4 additional subjects enrolled as numbers 22, 14, 17 and 34 in the Agenda by himself
and spent a sum of Rs.121.80/- lakhs without following the procedures laid down in G.O.(Ms.)No. 260, RD (C4)
department, dated 17-12-1999;
And, Whereas, a show cause notice was issued against Thiru A.T.T. Senguttuvan, Chairman of the
Manikandam Panchayat Union asking him to submit his explanation within 10 days from the date of the receipt of
the above said notice under sub-section (1) of Section 207 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994.
(Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994). He has not submitted his explanation within the stipulated time;
And,  Whereas, a copy of the show cause notice together with relevant records were forwarded to the Revenue
Divisional Officer, Trichirappalli District for ascertaining the views of the members of the Panchayat Union Council,
Manikandam Panchayat Union, Trichirappalli District under sub-section (2) of Section 207 of the said Act;

And,  Whereas, the Revenue Divisional Officer, Trichirappalli District convened the Panchayat Union Council
meeting on 12-8-2011 at 10 A.M. and duly recorded the minutes of the meeting. Then she has forwarded the
minutes of the above said meeting to the Government for taking further action as per sub-section (11) of
Section 207 of the above said Act. The Government, after considering the views of the Panchayat Union Council,
have decided to remove Thiru A.T.T. Senguttuvan, Chairman of the Manikandam Panchayat Union, Trichirappalli
District from the office of the said Panchayat Union.
Now, Therefore, in exercise of powers conferred by the sub-section (12) of Section 207 of the said Act, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby removes Thiru A.T.T. Senguttuvan, Chairman of the Manikandam Panchayat
Union, Trichirappalli District from the office of the said Panchayat Union, with immediate effect.
