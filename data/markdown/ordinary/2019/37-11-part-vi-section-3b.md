UNIVERSITY AUTHORITIES
UNIVERSITY OF MADRAS
Elections to the Syndicate, the Senate and the Standing Committee of the Academic Council by the Academic Council.
(No.V.1/AC Election/08-2019/219) 
No.VI-3(b)/26/2019.
It is hereby notified that the election to [i] THE SYNDICATE, [ii] THE SENATE and [iii] THE STANDING COMMITTEE 
OF THE ACADEMIC COUNCIL will be held under Section 18-1[a] - Other Members [2], Section 14-1(a)-Other Members 
[2] and Section 24[k] of the Madras University Act, 1923.
	
(i) To THE SYNDICATE -SECTION 18-1[a] - OTHER MEMBERS [2]
	
FIVE MEMBERS to be elected by the Academic Council from among its members, of which four shall be teachers of 
affiliated Colleges and one shall be a teacher of approved Colleges.
	
(ii) To THE SENATE-SECTION 14-1[a] - OTHER MEMBERS [2]
	
TEN MEMBERS to be elected by the Academic Council from among its members.
	
(iii) To THE STANDING COMMITTEE OF THE ACADEMIC COUNCIL [SECTION 24 [K]
FOUR MEMBERS - Two shall be the Principal or Teacher of Affiliated Colleges and two shall be members of the Syndicate 
to be elected by the Academic Council from among its members.
The above election will be conducted on the date of the meeting of the Academic Council scheduled to be held on 
SATURDAY, the 28th September 2019.
