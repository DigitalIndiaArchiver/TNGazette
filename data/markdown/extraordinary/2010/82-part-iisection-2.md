RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND PANCHAYAT RAJ DEPARTMENT
REMOVAL OF CHAIRMAN OF AYODHIYAPATTINAM PANCHAYAT UNION COUNCIL, SALEM DISTRICT
UNDER TAMIL NADU PANCHAYATS ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 24, Rural Development and Panchayat Raj (P.R.IV),23rd March 2010.]
No. II(2)/RDPR/156(b)/2010.
Whereas, a written notice of intention to make a motion expressing want of confidence in
Thiru M. Narayanan, Chairman, Ayodhiyapattinam Panchayat Union Council, Salem District, signed by thirteen members,
out of the sanctioned strength of nineteen members of the said Council, together with a copy of motion proposed to be
made and a written statement of charges against the said Chairman have been delivered to the Revenue Divisional Officer,
Salem, in person by two members, under the provision of sub-section (2) of Section 212 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats
Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994);
And, Whereas, the Revenue Divisional Officer, Salem caused a copy of the statement of charges alongwith a copy of the
notice of motion to be delivered, to the said Chairman Thiru M. Narayanan and he submitted his reply to the charges;
And, Whereas, a motion expressing want of confidence in Thiru M. Narayanan, Chairman, Ayodhiyapattinam Panchayat Union
Council, Salem District had been carried with a support of sixteen members out of the sanctioned strength of nineteen members
of the said Panchayat Union Council in the meeting convened by the Revenue Divisional Officer, Salem, on 22nd January 2010,
after following the procedures specified in Section 212 of the said Act and forwarded the same to Government;
Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (13) of Section 212 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats
Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby removes Thiru M. Narayanan, Chairman,
Ayodhiyapattinam Panchayat Union Council, Salem District, from the Office of the Chairman of the said Panchayat Union
Council with immediate effect.
 K. ASHOK VARDHAN SHETTY,
Principal Secretary to Government.
