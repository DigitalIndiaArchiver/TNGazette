DTP—II-2 Ex. (463)
TRANSPORT DEPARTMENT
ACQUISITION OF LANDS
[G.O. Ms. No. 197, Transport (I.1), 19th December 2011.]
No. II(2)/TRA/500(d)/2011.
Where in exercise of the powers conferred by clause (1)
of the Articles 258 of the Constitution of India, the President
of India has, in the Ministry of Food, Agriculture, Community
Development and Co-operation (Department of Agriculture)
Notification No. 4(1)/65, General-I & II, dated the 23rd April
1966, entrusted to the Government of Tamil Nadu with their
consent the function of the Central Government under the
Land Acquisition Act, 1894 (Central Act I of 1894) in relation
to acquisition of land for the purpose for the Union in this
State.
And, whereas, it has already been decided that the
compensation shall be paid from the public revenue;
Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by
Section 6 of the said Act, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
declares that the lands specified in the Schedule below and
measuring 0.00.5 (Dry) Hectare and 0.0016.00 (Village
Natham) Sq.Mtr. of 125. Parapatty Village, Salem Taluk,
Salem District  be the same a little more or less are needed
for a public purpose, to wit  for Salem-Broad Gauge Project.
A plan of the lands is kept in the office of the Special
Tahsildar (Land Acquisition), Salem-Karur Broad Gauge
Project, Salem and may be inspected at any time during the
office hours.
SCHEDULE
