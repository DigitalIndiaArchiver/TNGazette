No. SRO B-57/2016.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following rules:—
2. The rules hereby made shall come into force on and from the 23rd August 2016.
RULES
The General and the Special Rules applicable to the holders of the permanent post of Assistant Inspector General of
Registration in the Tamil Nadu Registration Service shall apply to the holders of the temporary post of Senior Systems
Manager, subject to the modifications specified in the following rules:-
2. Constitution.—The post shall constitute a separate category in the said Service.
3. Appointment.—Appointment to the post shall be made as follows:-
(i) by promotion from among the holders of the post of System Analyst;
or
(ii) if no candidate is available for promotion, by recruitment by transfer from any other service;
 or
(iii) if no candidate is available for promotion or by recruitment by transfer, by direct recruitment.
4.  Appointing Authority.— The appointing authority for the post shall be the Government.
5. Qualifications.— (a) Age.- No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post by direct recruitment, if he/she has
completed or will complete thirty years of age, on the 1st day of July of the year in which the notification for recruitment is issued.

RULES
The General and the Special Rules applicable to the holders of the permanent post of District Registrar in the
Tamil Nadu Registration Service shall apply to the holders of the temporary post of System Analyst, subject to the Modifications
specified in the following rules:-
2. Constitution.—The post shall constitute a separate category in the said Service.
3. Appointment.—Appointment to the post shall be made as follows:-
(i) by promotion from among the holders of the post of Programmer;
or
(ii) if no candidate is available for promotion, by recruitment by transfer from any other service;
 or
(iii) if no candidate is available for promotion or by recruitment by transfer, by direct recruitment.
4.  Appointing Authority.— The appointing authority for the post shall be the Government.
5. Qualifications.—(a) Age.- No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post by direct recruitment, if he/she has
completed or will complete thirty years of age, on the 1st day of July of the year in which the notification for recruitment is issued.
