No. I/PUSP/7/2023.—The Government  of
Tamil Nadu record with deep and profound regret 
the untimely death of one of its Senior Officers,
Thiru C. Kathiravan, lAS, Project Director, Tamil Nadu 
Road Sector Project II, Chennai on 17th February 2023 
at the age of 52 years. 
2. He started his career as an Agriculture Oﬃ  cer from 
12.09.1996 and entered into Tamil Nadu State Civil Services 
as Probationary Deputy Collector, in Dharmapuri District 
on 25.01.2002 and held various posts including Revenue 
Divisional Oﬃ  cer Kallakurichi, Aranthangi and Villuppuram; 
Personal Assistant (General) to Collector, Villuppuram; 
District Revenue Oﬃ  cer, Villuppurarm and Tiruvannamalai. 
He has got conformant in the Indian Administrative Service 
on 27.12.2013 and held various posts including General 
Manager, Aavin, Salem; Commissioner, Corporation of 
Madurai; Collector, Krishnagiri and Erode; Managing 
Director, TANMAG, Salem and now the Project Director, 
Tamil Nadu Road Sector Project II, Chennai. 
3. In the death of Thiru C. Kathiravan, lAS, the 
Government of Tamil Nadu has lost one of its Senior 
Oﬃ  cers. The Government places on record its condolence 
to the bereaved family members.
