NOTIFICATIONS BY THE ELECTION COMMISSION OF INDIA
ISSUE OF POSTAL BALLOT TO ELECTORS ABOVE THE AGE OF 80 YEARS AND 
ELECTORS WITH PHYSICAL DISABILITY 
No.SRO G-7/2020.
The following Notiﬁ cation of the Election Commission of India, Nirvachan Sadan, Ashoka Road, New 
Delhi-110 001, dated.17th September, 2020 is published:-
In pursuance of the provisions of clause (c) of Section-60 of the Representation of the People Act, 1951, 
the Election Commission hereby speciﬁ es that the electors above the age of 80 years and electors with physical 
disability as already marked/ﬂ agged in the data base of the electoral rolls of the State/Union Territory and 
who request for voting by postal ballot paper, would be a class of persons to give vote by postal ballot in the 
general elections or bye-elections in the State/Union Territory concerned, to be notiﬁ ed under Section 30 of 
Representation of the People, 1951, as per the provisions in Part-Ill A of the Conduct of Elections Rules, 1961, 
as amended vide Ministry of Law & Justice, Govt. of India, Notiﬁ cation SO No. 3786(E), dated 22nd  October, 
2019, and the directions and guidelines issued by the Election Commission, until further orders.
(By Order)

ISSUE OF POSTAL BALLOT TO ELECTORS IN QUARANTINE DUE TO COVID-19
No. SRO   G-8/2020
The following Notiﬁ cation of the Election Commission of India, Nirvachan Sadan, Ashoka Road, New Delhi-110 001, 
dated 17th September, 2020 is published:
In pursuance of the provisions of clause (c) of Section-60 of the Representation of the People Act, 1951, the Election 
Commission hereby speciﬁ es that the Covid-19 suspect or aﬀ ected persons, certiﬁ ed by competent authority, who request for 
voting by postal ballot paper, would be a class of persons to give vote by postal ballot in the forthcoming general election 
to the Legislative assembly of Bihar and bye-elections in several states, to be notiﬁ ed under Section 30 of Representation 
of the People, 1951, as per the provisions in Part-III A of the Conduct of Elections Rules, 1961, as amended vide Ministry 
of Law & Justice, Govt. of India, Notiﬁ cation SO No. 3786(E), dated 22nd  October, 2019, and the directions and guidelines 
issued by the Election Commission.
(By Order)
