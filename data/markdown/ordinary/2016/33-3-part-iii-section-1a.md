Amendments to the Chennai Corporation Engineering Service Rules.
[G.O. Ms. No. 96, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (MC-3), 29th July 2016, Aadi 14,
Thunmugi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2047.]
No. SRO A-16/2016.—In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (i) of sub-section (1) of Section 86 read with
clause (a) of sub-section (2) of Section 347 of the Chennai City Municipal Corporation Act, 1919
(Tamil Nadu Act IV of 1919), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Chennai
Corporation Engineering Service Rules, 1969, as subsequently amended:-
2. The amendments (1) hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force with effect on and
from the 22nd of August 1996.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules,-
(1) in rule 4, in the Table, under CLASS – II, in column (3), against the entry “Assistant Divisional Engineer
(Electrical)” in column (1),-
(i)
against the entry “By direct recruitment” in column (2), for item (2), the following item shall be substituted,
namely:-
“(2) Must possess a degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering or Electronics and Communication
Engineering or Instrumentation Engineering or Computer Science or Information Technology Engineering of any University
or Institution recognised by the University Grants Commission for purposes of its grant; and”;
  (ii) against the entry “ By Promotion or by appointment on deputation” in column (2), in item 1, for clauses
(a) and (b), the following clauses shall be substituted, namely:-
“(a) A degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering or Electronics and Communication Engineering or
Instrumentation Engineering or Computer Science or Information Technology Engineering of any University or Institution
recognized by the University Grants Commission for purpose of its grant or any other qualification considered equivalent
thereto:
Provided that in the case of promotion must possess a minimum service for a period of not less than five years
as Assistant Engineer (Electrical) in the Corporation Engineering Subordinate Service;
(b) Diploma in Electrical and Electronics Engineering or Electronics and Communication Engineering or
Instrumentation Engineering or Computer Science or Information Technology Engineering of the State Board of Technical
Education and Training or any other qualifications considered equivalent thereto:
Provided that in the case of promotion must possess a minimum period of service and experience of Ten years as
Junior Engineer (Electrical).” ;
(2) for the expression “Assistant Divisional Engineer (Electrical)” wherever it occurs, the expression “Assistant
Executive Engineer (Electrical)” shall be substituted; and
(3) in rule 3, in sub-rule (a), in the Table under CLASS – II, in column (2), for the entries against the entry “Assistant
Executive Engineer (Electrical)” as so amended in column (1), the following entries shall be substituted, namely:-
“(a) By Direct recruitment; or
(b) By promotion from the categories of Assistant Engineer (Electrical) and Junior Engineer (Electrical) in the
Chennai Corporation Engineering Subordinate Service in the ratio of 3:1; or
(c) By appointment of Assistant Executive Engineer (Electrical) in the TANGEDCO of the TNEB Limited on
deputation; or
(d) By appointment of Assistant Executive Engineer (Electrical) in the Public Works Department on
deputation; or
(e) By appointment of persons in the cadre of Assistant Executive Engineer (Electrical) in the Electrical Inspectorate
on deputation.” .
 K. PHANINDRA REDDY,
Principal Secretary to Government.
