APPOINTMENT OF CERTAIN PERSON AS A MEMBER IN THE TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD
UNDER THE WAKF ACT.
[G.O.(2D) No. 6, Backward Classes, Most Backward Classes and
Minorities Welfare (T1), 23rd February  2011.]
No. II(2)/BCMBCMW/90(a)/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (9) of Section 14 read with Section 21 of the
Wakf Act, 1995 (Central Act 43 of 1995), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints
Thiru Mir Leqa Ali (alias) Mahmood as member of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in the casual vacancy
caused due to the death of Thiru N.A. Mirza.
2.
The above member appointed shall hold office so long as the member whose place he fills would
have been entitled to hold office, if such vacancy had not occurred.
M. MALIK FEROZE KHAN,
Secretary to Government.
