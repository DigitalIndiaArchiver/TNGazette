GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD,
No. 1, JAFFAR SYRANG STREET, VALLAL SEETHAKATHI NAGAR, CHENNAI-600 001.
Taking over the Management of Kattu Bava Sahib Pallivasal alias Peer Mohamed Sahib Pallivasal,
Gangaikondan, Tirunelveli District under Direct Management of Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
(Rc. No. 10808/07/B18/TNV.)
No. VI-3(a)/183/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995, the Tamil Nadu
Wakf Board in its proceeding in item No. 40/08, Rc. No. 10808/B18/07/TNV, dated 26th August 2008 has ordered to take
over the management of Kattu Bava Sahib Pallivasal alias Peer Mohamed Sahib Pallivasal, Gangaikondan, Tirunelveli District
under the direct control of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of one year from the date of pronouncement of Board's
Order, dated 26th February 2009 and to appoint the Superintendent of Wakf, Tirunelveli as its Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
10th June 2009.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.

CONDITIONS OF  SALE
1. Lots are to be examined by the intending purchasers before bidding and to be taken with faults and errors
to description.
2. The highest bidder to be the purchaser and in the event of any dispute arising between two or more bidders
the lots so disputed should be put up again and resold.
3. The bid amount shall be paid in full immediately after the bid is knocked down. If this is not possible a deposit
of 25 per cent of bidded amount should be paid on the spot to the auctioneers and a receipt shall be obtained for
the amount paid in case only a deposit of 25 per cent is paid the balance amount should be paid to the Trust
Financial Adviser and Chief Accounts Officer on or before 4-00 p.m. on the third day calculating from the date of
confirmation of sale, failing which the EMD paid will be forfeited and the Trust will resell the lot either in sale by
Tender or by Public Auction at the risk and responsibility of the defaulters.
4. Goods advertised for sale will not be withdrawn by the Trust except on payment of sufficient deposit to cover
the Harbour dues, demurrage charges and other charges due thereon.
5. The goods sold in Auction will not be delivered to the purchaser thereof or his authorized agent unless the
purchaser produced delivery order from the Auctioneers for having paid the amount in full.
6. Goods sold in Auction shall be allowed three free days excluding Sundays and Board’s Holidays from the date
of confirmation of sale and thereafter shall be charged at daily rated as per Scale of Rates
up to and including the date of clearance.
7. The lots sold will be lying at the risk of the purchaser and must be removed within the free period
of three days from the date of confirmation of sale at the purchaser’s expenses.
8. The purchasers are distinctly warned that if the goods are not cleared within eight  days from the date of expiry
of free period they have no claim whatsoever over the materials and the same will be sold whether in sale by Tender
or Public Auction. The first purchaser to be held liable for any loss arising from such resale and to forfeit any
advances together with auctioned commission.
