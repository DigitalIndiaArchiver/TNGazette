Taking over the Direct Management of Isbanthiar Beig Masjid, Kancheepuram District.
(Rc.No. 12360/05/KPM/B4.)
No. VI-3(a)/37/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995, the
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in item No. 6/08, dated 7th July 2010 in R.C. No. 12360/05/C1/KPM has ordered
to take over the management of ISBANTHIAR BEIG MASJID WAKF, KANCHEEPURAM under the direct control of the
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of one year from the date of pronouncement of Board's Order, dated 25th April
2011 and to appoint the Wakf Inspector, Kancheepuram as its Executive Officer.

JUDICIAL NOTIFICATION
IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT MADRAS
(Ordinary Original Civil Jurisdiction)
C.P. No. 242 of 2009
[Roc. No. 4388/2011(O.S.).]
In the Matter of the Companies Act, 1956 (1 of 1956).
and
In the Matter of Shanthi Fortune India Private Limited
No. VI-3(a)/38/2011.
Chokhani Pharma Vet, 6W, Merchant Chamber, 6th Floor, Opp. Patakar Hall, 41, New Marine Lines, Mumbai-400 020.
Represented by its Sole Proprietor Mr. Anil Chokhani—Petitioner
Versus
Shanthi Fortune India Private Limited, a company incorporated under the provisions of the Companies Act, 1956 and
having its registered office at No. 38, Trichy Road, Palladam-641 664, Tamil Nadu—Respondent.
ADVERTISEMENT OF PETITION
Notice is hereby given that a petition for a winding-up of the above named company by the High Court at Madras was
on the 6th day of October 2009 presented to the said Court by the petitioner, Chokhani Pharma Vet, 6W Merchant Chamber,
6th Floor, Opp. Patakar Hall, 41, New Marine Lines, Mumbai-400 020 a creditor and that the said petition is directed to be
heard before the Court on the 8th day of July 2011.
Any creditor, contributory or other person desirous of supporting or opposing the making of an order on the said petition
should send to the petitioner or their Advocates notice of his intention, signed by him or his Advocate, with his name and
address, so as to reach the Petitioner or their Advocates not later than five days before the date fixed for  hearing of the
petition and appear at the hearing for the purpose in person or by his advocate. A copy of the petition will be furnished by
the undersigned to any creditor or contributory on payment of the prescribed charges for the same.
Any affidavit intended to be used in opposition to the petition should be filed in Courts and a copy served on the petitioner
or his advocate, not less than 5 days before the date fixed for the hearing.
