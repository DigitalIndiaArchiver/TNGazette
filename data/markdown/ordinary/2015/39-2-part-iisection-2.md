having been satisfied  that the lands specified in the schedule
below  have to be acquired for Highways Purpose  to wit for

HOME DEPARTMENT
Constitution of separate District Munsif Court at
Pappireddipatti by converting the existing District
Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Pappireddipatti
in Dharmapuri District under the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts
Act.
[G.O. Ms. No. 636, Home (Courts-III), 4th September 2015.]
No. II(2)/HO/560/2015.—In exercise of the powers conferred
by Section 4 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873
(Central Act III of 1873), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, after
consultation with the High Court of Madras, hereby fixes the
number of District Munsif to be appointed to the District Munsif
Court, Pappireddipatti as one with effect from the date of
constitution of separate District Munsif Court at Pappireddipatti
by converting the Principal District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate
Court at Papireddipatti.
NOTIFICATION - II
[G.O. Ms. No. 636, Home (Courts-III), 4th September 2015.]
No. II(2)/HO/561/2015.—In exercise of the powers conferred
by Section 10 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873
(Central Act III of 1873), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby
directs that in the District of Dharmapuri, the Subordinate
Judge's Court, Dharmapuri, shall have and exercise original
Jurisdiction over the District Munsif Court, Pappireddipatti with
effect from the date of constitution of separate District Munsif
Court at Pappireddipatti by converting the Principal District
Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court at Pappireddipatti.
Grant of Licence to Grant Certificates of Marriages between
Indian Christians under the Indian Christian Marriage
Act.
[G.O. Ms. No. 642, Home (Courts-IV), 8th September 2015.]
No. II(2)/HO/562/2015.—Under Section 6 of the Indian
Christian Marriage Act, 1872 (Central Act XV of 1872), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu sanction the issue of a licence to
Pastor A. Edwin Russo, Kovai Inthiya Eka Ratchager Sabai,
No.4, Poonga Nagar, Church Street, Varadharajapuram,
Coimbatore District, to solemnize marriages within the territories
comprising the State of Tamil Nadu in accordance with the
provisions of the said Act.
NOTIFICATION-II
G.O. Ms. No. 642, Home (Courts-IV), 8th September 2015.]
No. II(2)/HO/563/2015.—Under Section 9 of the Indian
Christian Marriage Act, 1872 (Central Act XV of 1872), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu sanctions the issue of a licence to
Pastor A. Edwin Russo, Kovai Inthiya Eka Ratchager Sabai,
No.4, Poonga Nagar, Church Street, Varadharajapuram,
Coimbatore District, to grant certificates of marriages between
Indian Christians, in accordance with the provisions of the said
Act, within the terriitories comprising the State of Tamil Nadu.

Exemption to the provisions of Development Regulation
of Second Master Plan for Chennai Metropolitan Area
relating to the Construction of Office cum Residential
Building at Mylapore, Chennai under the Tamil Nadu
Town and Country Planning Act.
[G.O. (3D) No. 44, Housing and Urban Development
(UD-V), 11th September 2015, ÝõE 25, ñ¡ñî,
F¼õœÀõ˜ Ý‡´-2046.]
No. II(2)/HOU/564/2015.—In exercise of the powers conferred
under Section 113 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning
Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972), the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby exempt Stilt + 4 Floor Office cum Residential
Building with 8 dwelling units at Old Door No.121, New Door
No.218, New Avvai Shanmugam Salai (Lloyds Road),
Gopalapuram in Old S.No.3416, R.S.No.69/98 Block No.4 of
Mylapore from the provisions of Development Regulation
Rule (8) relating to street alignment; Development Regulation
26 (3) Table (C) relating to Front Set Back, Side Set back
(West), Rear Set back, Excess Building height; Development
Regulation 26 (7) relating to Car Parking and provision of
separate stair case for office portions in all floors and relaxing
the depth of the balconies which are protruding onto the set
back space of Second Master Plan for Chennai Metropolitan
Area 2026 subject to shifting the transformer and Gen Set
located from Rear Set back in order to satisfy turning radius
and drive way and altering the size of the existing EB room
and providing the same in the stilt floor as per Development
Regulation norms by the appellant along with an undertaking
that the land required for street alignment / road widening shall
be handed over in future without any claim showing the approved
plan and at free of cost to the  requisitioning body and rectifying
minor defects.
