ACQUISITION OF LANDS
(Roc. CKICP/CLA/15(1)/SH-115/108/2021.)
NOTICE UNDER SECTION 15(1) OF TAMIL NADU HIGHWAYS ACT, 2001(TN ACT 34 OF 2002) 
READ WITH THE TAMIL NADU LAND ACQUISITION LAWS (REVIVAL OF OPERATION, AMENDMENT 
AND VALIDATION) ACT, 2019.
No.VI(1)/220(b-1)/2021.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that the lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below 
are acquired under Chennai – Kanniyakumari Industrial Corridor Project for Highways purpose for the 
formation of By-pass in SH-115, Cheyyur - Vandavasi - Polur Road from KM 47/920 to KM 48/600 at 147.
Biruthur (Addl.) Village, Vandavasi Taluk, Tiruvannamalai District, and it having already been decided that 
the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands to be paid out of the funds controlled and 
managed by the Divisional Engineer(H), Vandavasi (CKICP), Tamil Nadu after having considering the cause 
shown by the owner or other persons having interest on such lands as the case may be do hereby publish 
the following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001
(TN Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) 
read with the Tamil Nadu Land Acquisition Laws (Revival of Operation, Amendment and Validation) Act, 2019, 
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notiﬁ ed that the Dry lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below and measuring 
to an extent of 250 sq.mtr., are acquired in 147.Biruthur (Addl.) Village, Vandavasi Taluk, Tiruvannamalai 
District, to the same, a little more or less is needed for Chennai-Kanniyakumari Industrial Corridor Project 
for State Highways purpose, for the formation of By-pass in SH-115, Cheyyur - Vandavasi - Polur Road from 
KM 47/920 to KM 48/600.
The plan of the lands under acquisition are kept in the oﬃ  ce of the Special District Revenue oﬃ  cer 
(Land Acquisition),Chennai - Kanniyakumari Industrial Corridor Project, Kanchipuram and oﬃ  ce of the Special 
Tahsildar (Land Acquisition), Chennai – Kanniyakumari Industrial Corridor Project, Vandavasi in Tiruvannamalai 
District may be inspected at any time during the oﬃ  ce hours.

The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that the lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below are acquired under 
Chennai – Kanniyakumari Industrial Corridor Project for Highways purpose to wit for formation of By-pass in SH-95, Mohanur 
- Namakkal - Senthamangalam - Rasipuram Road from 39/300 K.M. to 40/350 K.M. 01.050 K.M.  at 17.  Kalappanaickenpatti 
(Bypass) (Part - 2), Senthamangalam Taluk, Namakkal District, and it having already been decided that the entire amount of 
compensation to be awarded for the lands to be paid out of the funds controlled and managed by the Divisional Engineer(H), 
Salem (CKICP), Tamil Nadu after having considering the cause shown by the owner or other persons having interest on 
such lands as the case may be do hereby publish the following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the 
Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (TN Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) read with the 
Tamil Nadu Land Acquisition Laws (Revival of Operation, Amendment and Validation) Act, 2019, the Governor of Tamil Nadu 
hereby notiﬁ ed that the Dry lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below and measuring to an extent of 1290 sq.mtr., are acquired 
in 17.Kalappanaickenpatti (Bypass) (Part-2),  Senthamangalam Taluk, Namakkal District, to the same, a little more or less is 
needed for Chennai-Kanniyakumari Industrial Corridor Project for State Highways purpose, to wit for the formation of By-pass 
in SH-95, Mohanur - Namakkal - Senthamangalam - Rasipuram Road from KM 39/300 to KM 40/350 01.050 K.M. 
The plan of the lands under acquisition are kept in the oﬃ  ce of the Special District Revenue oﬃ  cer (Land Acquisition), 
Chennai - Kanniyakumari Industrial Corridor Project, Salem and oﬃ  ce of the Special Tahsildar (Land Acquisition), Chennai – 
Kanniyakumari Industrial Corridor Project, Namakkal may be inspected at any time during the oﬃ  ce hours.
