TRANSPORT DEPARTMENT
ACQUISITION OF LANDS
[G.O. Ms. No. 81, Transport (E), 3rd May 2012.]
No. II(2)/TRA/235(b)/2012.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that
the Lands specified in the schedule below has have to be
acquired for industrial purposes to wit for the Expansion of
Madurai Airport and it having already been decided that the
entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the
land/lands is to be paid out of the lands controlled or
managed by the State. The following notice is issued
under sub-section (1) of Section of  the  Tamil Nadu
Acquisition of  Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil
Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
acquires the land / lands specified in the schedule below and
measuring 5.44.36 hectares 13.45 acres to the same, a little
more or less needed for industrial purposes, to wit, for the
Expansion of Madurai Airport. The plan of the land / lands
is / are kept in the office of the Special Tahsildar Land Acquisition
Airport Expansion Unit I, Madurai – 20 and may be inspected at
any time during office hours.
