V.H. 44. Narasingapuram Dr. M.G.R. Powerloom Co-operative Society in Vellore District
(Rc. No. 3821/2018/D1)
No.VI(1)/241/2019.
The aﬀ airs of the V.H. 44. Narasingapuram Dr. M.G.R. Powerloom Co-operative Society in Vellore District are ordered 
to be ﬁ nally closed and its Registration cancelled under Section 140 of the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act 30 
of 1983 as per the orders certained in the Proceedings Rc.No.3821/2018/D1, dated 16-11-2018 of the Assistant Director 
of Handlooms and Textiles, Vellore.
Vellore, 
K. MOHAN,
10th May 2019. 
Assistant Director of Handlooms and Textiles.
TNH.150, Tenkasi Anna Weavers Co-operative Production and Sales Society Ltd., Tenkasi,
Tenkasi Taluk, Tirunelveli District.
[CLF. No. 196/2003/E(L)]
No.VI(1)/242/2019.
The aﬀ airs of the TNH.150, Tenkasi Anna Weavers Co-operative Production and Sales Society Ltd. (Liquidated), 
Tenkasi Taluk, Tirunelveli District which were ordered to be wound up under Section 85(1) of the Tamil Nadu Co-operative 
Societies Act 53 of 1961 are ﬁ nally closed and the registration of the above Society is hereby cancelled under Section 140 
of the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act 30 of 1983.
Tirunelveli, 
A. MARIAPPAN,
15th May 2019. 
Assistant Director of Handlooms and Textiles.
