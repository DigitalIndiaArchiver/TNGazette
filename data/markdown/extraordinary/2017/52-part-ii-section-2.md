DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF THE TAMIL NADU COURT-FEES AND SUITS VALUATION
(AMENDMENT) ACT, 2017.
[G.O. (2D) No.47, Home (Cts-VIA), 27th February 2017.]
No. II(2)/HO/139(b)/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Court-fees and Suits
Valuation (Amendment) Act, 2017 (Tamil Nadu Act 6 of 2017), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints
the 1st March 2017 as the date on which the said Act shall come into force.
APURVA VARMA,
Principal Secretary to Government.
