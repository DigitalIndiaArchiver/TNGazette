In partial modification of the G.O. (Ms.) No. 324, Revenue and Disaster Management (D.M-II) Department, 
dated 30-06-2020 the following instructions are hereby issued with reference to para 5.II(i) which shall be read as follows:- 
II. The following activities will be allowed from early morning 00 hrs of 13-07-2020 in all the areas (Except Containment 
Zones) falling under the jurisdiction of Greater Chennai Police Limits. 
 
(i)  IT/ITeS companies are permitted to work with 50% of employee strength at their work premises, subject to 
the condition that 90% of the employees going to work place shall use company arranged common transport facilities. 
However, IT / ITeS companies should encourage their employees to work from home.
