MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION AND WATER SUPPLY DEPARTMENT
DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF THE TAMIL NADU LOCAL AUTHORITIES ENTERTAINMENTS
TAX ACT, 2017
[G.O. Ms. No. 68, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (Election) 29th June 2017.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/532(f)/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Local Authorities
Entertainments Tax Act, 2017 (TN Act 20   of 2017), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the
1st day of July 2017 as the date on which the said Act, except the provisions relating to Direct to Home
service, shall come into force.
HARMANDER SINGH,
Principal Secretary to Government.
