Amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Forest Subordinate Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 17, Environment and Forests (FR.2-II) 1st February 2019,

No. SRO B-15/2019.— In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Forest Subordinate 
Services (Section 12 of Part III B of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual 1970).
2. These amendments shall to deemed to have come into force on the 9th November 1981.
Amendments
In the said Special Rules,-
(1) in rule 1, under Class III, for the entry "1. Senior Draughting Officer " the entries "1. Special Grade Draughting Officer" 
and "1A. Senior Draughting Officer" shall be substituted;
(2) 	in rule 2,-
	 	
(i) in sub-rule (a), in the tabular column, after the entry "Class III" and before the entry "Senior Draughting Officer" 
in column (1) and the corresponding entry "Promotion from "Draughting Officer" in column (2) thereof, the following entries 
shall, respectively, be inserted, namely:-
"Special Grade Draughting Officer"
"Promotion from among the holders of 
the post of Senior Draughting Officer";
	
(ii) in sub-rule (b), after the word "Forester", the expression "Special Grade Draughting Officer" shall be inserted;
(3) in rule 3, in the tabular column, under Class-III, before the entry "Senior Draughting Officer" in column (1), the entry" 
Special Grade Draughting Officer" shall be inserted;
(4) in rule 3 A,-
	
(i) in sub-rule (1), before the expression "Senior Draughting Officer", the expression "Special Grade Draughting 
Officer", shall be inserted;	
	
(ii) for sub-rule (2), the following sub-rule shall be substituted, namely:- 
“(2) Appointment to the posts of Special Grade Draughting Officer, Senior  Draughting Officer, Draughting Officer and 
Junior Draughting Officer shall be made from  a panel of approved candidates found fit for promotion from the Senior 
Draughting  Officer, Draughting Officer, Junior Draughting Officer and Assistant Draughtsman, respectively, drawn up by the 
Principal Chief Conservator of Forests”. 
	
(iii) after sub-rule (2), the following shall be inserted, namely:- 
(5) Nothing contained in these rules shall adversely affect any person holding the  post on the date of issue of these rules.
SHAMBHU KALLOLIKAR,   
Principal Secretary to Government.
