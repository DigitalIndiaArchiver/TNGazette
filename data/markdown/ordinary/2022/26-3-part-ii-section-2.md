No. II(2)/ECCF/531/2022.-In exercise of the powers 
conferred by sub-section (3) of Section 40 of the Water 
(Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1974 (Central Act

the Comptroller and Auditor General of India hereby appoints 
Tvl. N. Muthukumaraswamy & Co. (MD0959) No. 28, 1st 
Floor, Sourashthra Nagar 2nd Street, Choolaimedu, Chennai- 
600 094, Tamil Nadu as auditors for auditing the accounts 
of the Tamil Nadu Pollution Control Board, Chennai, for the 
Financial year 2021-2022.
