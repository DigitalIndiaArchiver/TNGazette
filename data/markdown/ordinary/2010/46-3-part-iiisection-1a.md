Draft Amendments to the Tamil Nadu Factories Rules.
[G.O. Ms. No. 132, Labour and Employment (M2), 21st July 2010, Aadi 5, Thiruvalluvar Aandu 2041.]
No. SRO A-35/2010.—The following draft of amendments to the Tamil Nadu Factories Rules, 1950 which it is proposed
to make in exercise of the powers conferred by Section 112 of the Factories Act, 1948 (Central Act LXIII of 1948), is hereby
published for the information of all persons likely to be affected, there by, as required by Section 115 of the said Act.
2.
Notice is hereby given that the draft amendments will be taken into consideration on or after the expiry of forty-five
days from the date of publication of this Notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette and that, any objection or
suggestion which may be received from any person with respect thereto before the expiry of the aforesaid period will be
considered by the Government of Tamil Nadu. Objection or suggestion, if any, should be addressed to the Secretary to
Government, Labour and Employment Department, Secretariat, Chennai-600 009 through the Chief Inspector of Factories,
Chennai-600 005 in duplicate;
