No. SRO B-43/2011.— In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Adi Dravidar  and
Tribal Welfare Higher Secondary Educational Service:—
2. The amendment hereby made shall come into force on 10-11-2011.
AMENDMENT

FINANCE DEPARTMENT
Ad hoc rules relating to temporary post of System Programmer in the Finance Department.
[G.O. Ms. No. 315, Finance (OP-I),  16th November 2011, Aippasi 30, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No. SRO B-44/2011.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following rules:—
2. The rules hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 15th May, 2001.
RULES
The General and the Special Rules applicable to the holders of the permanent post of the Tamil Nadu General Service
shall apply to the holders of the temporary post of System Programmer, sanctioned from time to time, in the Finance
Department, subject to the modifications specified in following rules:—
2. Constitution.—The post shall constitute a distinct class of the said service.
3. Appointment.—Appointment to the post shall be made as follows:—
(i)
by direct recruitment; or
(ii)
by transfer from any other class or category; or
(iii) by recruitment by transfer from any other service.
4. Preparation of annual list of approved candidates.—For the purpose of preparation of annual list of approved
candidates for appointment to the post shall be the 1st September every year.
5. Appointing Authority.—The appointing authority for the post shall be the Secretary to Government, Finance
Department.
6. Qualifications:
(a) Age.– No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post by direct recruitment, if he has completed or will
complete the age of thirty years on the first day of July of the year in which the selection for appointment is made.
(b) Other Qualification.– No person shall be eligible for eligible for appointment to the post by the method specified in
column (1) of the Table below unless he possesses the qualifications specified in the corresponding entries in

7. Probation:
(a) Every Person appointed to the post by direct recruitment, shall, from the date on which he joins duty, be on
probation for a total period of two years on duty within a continuous period of three years.
(b) Every person appointed to the post by recruitment by transfer shall, from the date on which he joins duty, be on
probation for a total period of one year on duty within a continuous period of two years.
8. Pay.—There shall be paid to the holder of the post monthly pay calculated in the scale of pay of Rs. 6500-200-10500
provided that with effect on and from 30th July 2008 the monthly pay shall be calculated in the scale of pay of
Rs. 8000-275-13500 (pre-revised).

order.
