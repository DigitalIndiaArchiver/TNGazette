No. SRO B-30/2013.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Medical Service
(Section 21 in Volume II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1969).
2. (a) Amendment (1) (a) herey made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 18th May 2010.
(b) Amendment (1) (b) hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 21st September 2010.
(c) Amendment (2) hereby made shall come into force on the date of publication of this notification.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules, in Part II, in Branch-I Medical, in the Annexure [referred to in rule 8 (c) ]—
(1) under the heading “Class-I, Category-14”,—
(a) after the entry “Director, Institute of Paediatrics, Government General Hospital and Professor of Paediatrics,
Madras Medical College, Madras” in column (1) and the corresponding entries in column (2) thereof, the following entries
shall, respectively, be inserted, namely:—
“Director, Institute of
A degree of M.D. in Anaesthesiology of any
Anaesthesiology, Madurai
University or Institution recognized by the
Madical College, Madurai.
Medical Council of India.”;
(b) After the entry “Director, Institute of Anaesthesiology, Madurai Medical College, Madurai” in column (1) and the
corresponding entries in column (2) thereof as so inserted, the following entries shall, respectively, be inserted, namely:—
“Director, Institute of
A degree of M.D. in Anaesthesiology of any
Anaesthesiology and
University or Institution recognized by the
Critical Care, Madras
Medical Council of India.”;
Medical College, Chennai.
(2) in column (2), for the expression “recognized by the University Grants Commission for the purpose of its grant”
wherever it occurs, the expression “recognized by the Medical Council of India” shall be substituted.
