No. II(2)/BCMBCMW/391/2011.—Under Section 48(1) of the
Tamil Nadu Land Acquisition Act, 1894 (Central Act I of 1894),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby withdraws from Acquisition
of Lands specified in the Schedule below, in respect of Notification
issued under sub-section (1) of Section 4 of the said Act
approved in Government Order (Ms.) No. 55, Backward Classes
and Most Backward Classes Welfare Department, dated
6th February 1992 published in the Tamil Nadu Government
Gazette, dated 26th February 1992 in Part II—Section 2,
page 9 and the draft Declaration under Section 6 of the said
Act, approved in Government Order (3D) No. 37, Backward
Classes and Most Backward Classes Welfare Department,
dated 1st March1993 published in the Tamil Nadu Government
Gazette No. 101 (Extraordinary), in Part II—Section 2, page 1,
dated 2nd March 1993 as being required for a public purpose
to wit for the provision of houses to the Most Backward and
Denotified Communities of Pichanur Village, Gudiyatham Taluk,
Vellore District.

COMMERCIAL TAXES AND REGISTRATION DEPARTMENT
Provision for Payment of Consolidation of Stamp Duty
Chargeable in respect of issue of Policies by the
Cholamandalam MS General Insurance Company Limited
under Indian Stamp Act.
[G.O. Rt. No. 404, Commercial Taxes and Registration (J1),
17th August 2011].
No. II(2)/CTR/392/2011.—In exercise of the powers conferred
by clause (b) of sub-section (1) of Section 9 of the Indian Stamp
Act, 1899 (Central Act II of 1899), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby provides for the consolidation of duty of Rs. 7,00,000
(Rupees seven lakhs only) chargeable under the said Act in
respect of issue of policies by the Cholamandalam MS General
Insurance Company Limited for the period from 1st August
2011 to 30th September 2011.
SUNIL PALIWAL,
Secretary to Government.
HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE DEPARTMENT
Declaration of Madha Medical College and Hospital,
Thandalam, Kovur, Chennai to be a teaching Medical
Institution under the Tamil Nadu Anatomy Act.
[G.O. (3D) No. 17, Health and Family Welfare (Z1),
24th August 2011, Aavani 7, Thiruvalluvar Aandu 2042.]
No. II(2)/HF/393/2011.—Under clause (e) of Section 2 of the
Tamil Nadu Anatomy Act, 1951 (Tamil Nadu Act, XVIII of 1951),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby declares Madha Medical
College and Hospital, Thandalam, Kovur, Chennai-600 122 to
be a teaching medical institution for the purposes of that Act.
GIRIJA VAIDYANATHAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.

No. II(2)/HWMP/395/2011.—The Governor of Tamil Nadu
having been satisfied that the lands in the Schedule below
situated in Chitravuthanpalayam Village, Dharapuram Taluk,

been decided that the entire amount of compensation to be

and managed by the Chief Engineer (Projects), Highways and
after having considered the cause shown by the owner or other
person having interest in the said lands, as the case may be
do hereby publish the following notice under sub-section (1)
of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001
(Tamil Nadu Act 34/2002).
