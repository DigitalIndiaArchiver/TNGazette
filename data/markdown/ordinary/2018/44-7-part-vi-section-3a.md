GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAQF BOARD, CHENNAI
Taking over of Chinna Mohallam Mohideen Andavar Pallivasal Waqf, Kollampattarai Theru, Velipattanam, 
Ramnad District  Under Direct Management.  
(Rc.No.16821/12/B3/RMD.)
No. VI-3(a)/176/2018.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Waqf Act 1995, the 
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its Resolution in item No.101/18 in Rc.No.16821/12/B3/RMD, dated : 27-09-2018 has 
ordered to take over the Chinna Mohallam Mohideen Andavar Pallivasal Waqf, Kollampattarai Theru, Velipattanam, 
Ramnad District under the direct Management of the Tamil Nadu Waqf Board for a period of One Year from the date 
of Board's Order (i.e.) 27-09-2018 and to appoint the Waqf Inspector, Ramnad as its Executive Officer.

N.S. MOHAMED ASLAM,
