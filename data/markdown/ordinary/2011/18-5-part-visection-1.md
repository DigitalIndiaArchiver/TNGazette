Except as hereunder mentioned the Presidency Court of Small Causes, Chennai will remain closed for the
Summer Vacation, 2011 from 1st May 2011 to 29th May 2011 (both days inclusive).
Thiru R. Karuppaih, Chief Judge, Court of Small Causes, Chennai will be the Vacation Judge for First turn
from 1st May 2011 to 15th May 2011.
Tmt. M. Vetrichelvi, III Judge, Court of Small Causes, Chennai will be the Vacation Judge for Second turn
from 16th May 2011 to 29th May 2011.

Tmt. J. Barathi XV Judge, Court of Small Causes, Chennai will be Additional Vacation Judge for the First turn
from 1st May 2011 to 15th May 2011.
Tmt. L. Maheswari, XVI Judge, Court of Small Causes, Chennai will be the Addtional Vacation Judge for the Second
turn from 16th May 2011 to 29th May 2011. The Additional Vacation Judges will deal with Rent Control
Original Petitions.
Thiru S. Giri, Registrar, Court of Small Causes, Chennai will be the  Vacation Officer for 1st and 2nd turn
from 1st May 2011 to 29th May 2011.
The Vacation Judges unless otherwise notified will sit on all Thursdays, thereafter during the vacation dispose the
emergent petitions. No planit petition, no Rent Control Appeals or other matters will be received during the period of
vacation, unless it is urgent and it is accompanied by an application duly supported by an Affidavit showing the
urgency, no fresh execution petition in Rent Control matters will be received during the vacation.
The Office of the Copyists will be kept open from Monday to Friday each week between 10.30 A.M. to 4.00 P.M.
for delivering the certified copies which are already made ready. The Office of the Central Nazarath Section will be kept
open from Monday to Friday each week between 10.30 A.M. to 4.00 P.M. and the Process Service Applications will
be receieved on Wednesday and Thursdays only.
The Office of the Registrar will be kept open from Monday to Friday each week but the urgent matters will be
received on Wednesday during the vacation and such matters will be posted before the Judges for hearing on the
succeeding Thursday in open Court.
If any case requires immediate attention, party concerned or his Advocate should give 24 hours notice of the same
to the office in-charge and the necessary papers will be sent to the residence of the vacation Judge for orders. No
application shall be presented at the residence of the vacation Judge under any circurmstances.
Money will be received in the Treasury on Wednesday and Thursday every week and money will be paid out only
on Thursday during when the vacation Judges are holding courts and on orders made by the Judges for
such payments.
With a view to avoid difficulty to the parties, petitioners and the members of the staff, suit, Rent Control Original
Petition, Rent Control Appeals and the petitions of other proceedings which are to be filed on the re-opening days will
also be received and returned. The matteres will be made available for taking returns in the regular courts between
11.00 A.M. to 3.00 P.M. on Thursday only.
