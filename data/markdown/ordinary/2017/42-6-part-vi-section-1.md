JUDICIAL  NOTIFICATIONS
Constitution of Sub-Court at Aranthangi in Pudukkottai District.
(Roc. No. 623/A/2012/G/Judn.)
No. VI(1)/321/2017.
"In exercise of the powers conferred under the ﬁ rst proviso to Section 13 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 
(Central Act III of 1873) the High Court, Madras hereby directs that appeals from the decrees or orders of the District 
Munsif Court, Aranthangi except Keeramangalam Firka of Alangudi Taluk be preferred in the court of Subordinate Judge, 
Aranthangi with effect on and from the date on which the Subordinate Judge, Aranthangi assumes charge of that court."
Constitution of Sub-Court at Lalgudi in Tiruchirapalli District.
 
(Roc. No. 1453/A/2014/G/Judn.)
No. VI(1)/322/2017.
''In exercise of the powers conferred under the ﬁ rst proviso to Section 13 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 
(Central Act III of 1873), the High Court, Madras hereby directs that appeals from the decrees or orders of the District 
Munsif Court, Lalgudi be preferred in the court of Subordinate Judge, Lalgudi with effect on and from the date on which 
the Subordinate Judge, Lalgudi assumes charge of that court.''
 
R. SAKTHIVEL,
High Court, Madras, 
Registrar General.
