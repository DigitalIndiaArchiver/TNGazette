Whereas it appears to the Government of Tamil Nadu
that the lands specified in the schedule below and situated
in the Ponnakudi Village in Palayamkottai Taluk in Tirunelveli
District are needed for a Public Purpose to wit for the formation
of a Flood Carrier Channel by inter-linking Tamirabarani,
Karumeniaru and Nambiyaru Rivers for the benefit of the dry
areas in Tirunelveli and Thuthookudi Districts.  Notice  to that
effect is hereby given to all to whom it may concern in
accordance with the provisions of the sub-section (1) of
Section 4 of the Land Acquisition Act 1894 (Central
Act I of l894).
And whereas it has become necessary to acquire
immediate possession of the lands specified in the schedule
below, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that the
lands be acquired under the provision of sub-section (l) of
Section 17 of the said Act.   Now therefore, in exercise of the
powers conferred by the sub-section (2) of Section 4 of the
said Act, the Governor of Tamil  Nadu  hereby authorizes the
Special Tahsildar (Land Acquisition), Inter-linking of River
Project – Unit III, Palayamkottai and his staff and workmen
to exercise the powers conferred by the said sub-section.
[ 1 ]
Under sub-section (4) of Section 17 of the said Act the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that in view of urgency
of the case under clause ( c) of section 3 of the said Act the
provisions of Section 5(A) of the said Act shall not apply to
this case.
