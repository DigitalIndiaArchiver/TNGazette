(Under Section 24 of the Bharathiar University Act 1981 as amended till date)
ELECTION TO THE SYNDICATE FROM THE SENATE IN RESPECT OF THE FOLLOWING CATEGORIES WILL BE 
HELD ON 10TH JANUARY 2018.
(No. S1/23312/Syn.Ele./2017.)
No.VI-3(b)/35/2017.
1. TWO MEMBERS TO BE ELECTED by the Principals (those who are the Senate members) of afﬁ liated Colleges 
from among themselves in accordance with the System of proportional representation, by means of single transferable vote.
2. TWO MEMBERS TO BE ELECTED by teachers of afﬁ liated Colleges other than Principals, from among themselves, 
who are members of the Senate in accordance with the system of proportional representation by means of a single transferable 
vote.
3. ONE MEMBER from among Senate members not falling under the above categories and University teachers who 
are members of the Senate, to be elected by the Senate.
In this connection, nominations are invited to elect Syndicate members from the above said categories.
Nomination form shall be presented to the Registrar, by Registered Post or in person or by messenger during the ofﬁ ce 
hours on or before the dates and hours ﬁ xed. The details are available in the University website (www.b-u.ac.in).
