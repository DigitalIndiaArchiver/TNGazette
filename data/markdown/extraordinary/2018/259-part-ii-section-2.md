In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (i) of sub-section (1) of Section 67 of the Motor Vehicles 
Act, 1988 (Central Act 59 of 1988), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby issues the following direction to the 
State Transport Authority, Tamil Nadu and the Regional Transport Authorities regarding the ﬁ xation of fares 
for the Ultra  Deluxe  Buses with Toilet Facility, Air Conditioned Sleeper Buses and Non-Air Conditioned 
Sleeper Buses  operated by the State Express Transport Corporation, Ltd., Chennai.

of this Notiﬁ cation in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
