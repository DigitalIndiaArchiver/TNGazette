and Urban Development Department Notification No. II(2)/HOU/2861/84 at page No. 447 of Part II—Section 2, of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette dated the 23rd May 1984.

Village, Hosur Panchayat Union, the expression S.F.739/1A1B with an extent of 0.44 Acres and S.F.No. 1004/1A1B with
an entent of 0.43 Acres, Total extent 0.87 Acres shall be added.
under the heading, Agricultural use Zone (Dry), Chennathur Village S.F.No. 739/1A1B with an extent of 0.44 Acres
shall be deleted.
under the heading, General Industrial use Zone (Dry), Chennathur Village S.F.No.1004/1A1B with an extent of 0.43
Acres shall be deleted.

Act 2 of 1974) the High Court hereby appoints Tmt. E.P. Asha, Tahsildar Trainee in Tiruvallur District to undergo
Magisterial Training for a period of 180 days from the date of assumption of charge and confers upon her the ordinary
powers conferrable under this Code on a Judicial Magistrate to try such cases which were earlier triable and dealt
with by an erstwhile Judicial Second Class Magistrate.
