NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
DTP—II-2 Ex. (76)—1
TOURISM, CULTURE AND RELIGIOUS ENDOWMENTS DEPARTMENT
CANCELLATION OF THE EXEMPTIONS  TO SRI ADHIVYATHIKARA BHAKTHA ANJANEYA TEMPLE, RAMNAGAR,
NANGANALLUR, CHENNAI, UNDER THE HINDU RELIGIOUS AND CHARITABLE ENDOWMENTS ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 55,  Tourism, Culture and Religious Endowments (RE4-1), 19th March 2013,
 Panguni 6, Nandhana, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2044.]
No. II(2)/TCRE/269(a)/2013.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 4 of the Tamil Nadu Hindu Religious and Charitable Endowments
Act, 1959 (Tamil Nadu Act 22 of 1959), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby cancels the exemptions granted to
Sri Adhivyathikara Bhaktha Anjaneya Temple, Ramnagar, Nanganallur, Chennai-61, founded and run by Sri Maruthi
Bhaktha Samajam Trust, from the provisions of  Sections 26, 35, 36, 45, 47, 53, 55, 56, 57, 58, 86 and 103
and also from Sections 23, 24, 25, 27, 29, 30, 31, 32, 33, 34, 36A, 38, 43, 70, 71, 87, 90, 92, 93, 94, 96, 97, 98,
99 and100  of  the said Act and Rules made there under, respectively, in the Commercial Taxes and Religious
Endowments Department Notification No.II (2) / CTRE / 3498 (C)  / 95, dated the 1st November 1995 and published
in Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 1st November 1995
and in the Commercial Taxes and Religious Endowments Department Notification No. II (2) / CTRE / 684 (e) / 96,
dated the 18th March 1996 and published in Part II - Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary,
dated the 18th March 1996, respectively.
R.  KANNAN,
Principal Secretary to Government
