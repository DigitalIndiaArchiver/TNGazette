The Government  of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that the Land/Land specified in the schedule below
has/have to be acquired for industrial purposes to wit for the Expansion of Madurai Airport and it having already
been decided that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the land / lands is to be paid out of the
lands controlled or managed by……………….The following notice is issued under sub-section(1) of Section of the
Tamil Nadu Acquisition of  Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the land / lands specified in the schedule
below and measuring 7.38.00 hectare / 18.23 acres to the same, a little more or less needed for industrial
purposes, to wit, for the Expansion of Madurai Airport. The Plan of the land / lands is / are kept in the office of
the Special Tahsildar (LA) Airport Expansion Unit II, Madurai – 20 and may be inspected at any time during office
hours.
