No. II(2)/AHDF/574/2015.—In exercise of powers conferred
by sub-sections (1) and (2) of section 32 of the Indian
Veterinary Council Act, 1984 (Central Act 52 of 1984), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following
Amendment to the Animal Husbandry, Dairying and Fisheries
(AH3) Department, Notification No. II(2)/AHDF/632/2010
published at page 754 of Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu
Government Gazette, dated the 20th October 2010:—
2. It shall come into force on the 01-09-2015.
AMENDMENT
 In the said Notification, under the heading “(B) the head
of Veterinary Institutions”, for items (1) and (2), the following
item shall be substituted, namely:-
 “The Vice-Chancellor,
 Tamil Nadu Veterinary and Animal Sciences University
- ex-officio”.
S. VIJAYAKUMAR,
Secretary to Government.

No.II(2)/PLAR/587/2015.—Under the Rules governing the
Governor’s Vigilance Medal issued in G.O. Ms. No. 249,
Personnel and Administrative Reforms (N) Department, Dated
25-02-1977, as amended subsequently, the “Governor’s
Vigilance Medal” for the year 2014 is awarded to the following
three Investigating Officers of the Directorate of Vigilance
and Anti-Corruption for their meritorious service and the
names of those officers are hereby published in the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette as required under rule
15 of the said Rules:—
1. Thiru G. Ramesh Kumar, Inspector of Police,
Vigilance and Anti-Corruption, Nagapattinam.
2. Thiru P. Sundararajan, Inspector of Police, Vigilance
and Anti-Corruption, Dindigul.
3. Thiru V. Baskaran, Inspector of Police, Vigilance and
Anti-Corruption, CSU-III, Chennai.
P.W.C. DAVIDAR,
Principal Secretary to Government.
