NOTICE UNDER SECTION 15(1) OF TAMIL NADU HIGHWAYS ACT, 2001 (TAMIL NADU ACT 34 OF 2002), READ WITH 
TAMIL NADU LAND ACQUISITION LAWS. (REVIVAL OF OPERATION, AMENDMENT AND VALIDATION) ACT, 2019.
No. VI(1)/330(b)/2021.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that the lands speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below are acquired 
under Chennai Peripheral Ring Road Project for Highways purpose to wit, for Construction of Road Over Bridge cum 
interchange road connectivity from Ennore Port to Mahabalipuram 51.Thirukatchur Village, Chengalpattu Taluk, Chengalpattu 
District and it having already been decided that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands to be 
paid out of the funds controlled and managed by the Highways Department and after having considering the cause shown 
by the owner or other persons having interest on such lands, as the case may be, do hereby publish the following 
notice under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) read 
with the Tamil Nadu Land Acquisition Laws (Revival of Operation, Amendment and Validation) Act, 2019.
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002), read 
with the Tamil Nadu Land Acquisition Laws (Revival of Operation, Amendment and Validation Act, 2019) the Governor of 
Tamil Nadu hereby notiﬁ es that the Wet and Dry lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below and measuring to an extent of 
56,408  Sq.Mtrs. of lands are acquired in 51. Thirukatchur Village, Chengalpattu Taluk, Chengalpattu District to the same a 
little more or less needed for the purpose to wit for Construction of Road Over Bridge cum interchange road connectivity 
from Ennore Port to Mahabalipuram as part of Chennai Peripheral Ring Road Project, Highways Department. 
The Plan of the lands under acquisition are kept in the Office of the Special District Revenue Officer 
(Land Acquisition), Chennai Peripheral Ring Road Project, Section-III, Chennai-600 058 and in the Oﬃ  ce of the Special Tahsildar 
(Land Acquisition), Chennai Peripheral Ring Road Project, Unit-V, Kanchipuram and may be inspected at any time during 
the oﬃ  ce hours.
