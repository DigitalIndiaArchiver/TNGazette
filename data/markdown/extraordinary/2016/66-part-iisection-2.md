HOME  DEPARTMENT
(Cts-VIA)
DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF THE  TAMIL NADU COURT FEES AND SUITS
VALUATION (AMENDMENT) ACT, 2016.
[G.O. Ms. No. 266, Home (Cts VIA), 3rd March 2016.]
No.II(2)/ HO/173(b)/2016.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Court-fees and
Suits Valuation (Amendment) Act, 2016 (Tamil Nadu Act 1 of 2016), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints
the 3rd March  2016 as the date on which the said Act shall come into force.
APURVA VARMA,
Principal Secretary to Government.
