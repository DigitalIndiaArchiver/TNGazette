RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND PANCHAYATRAJ DEPARTMENT
DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF TAMIL NADU PANCHAYATS (SECOND AMENDMENT) ACT, 2012.
[G.O. Ms. No. 11, Rural Development and Panchayat Raj (PR-1), 31st January 2013.]
No. II(2)/RDPR/140(c)/2013.
Under sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Second Amendment) Act, 2012
(Tamil Nadu Act 44 of 2012), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby appoints the 1st day of February 2013 as
the date on which the said Act shall come into force.
C.V. SANKAR,
Principal Secretary to Government.
