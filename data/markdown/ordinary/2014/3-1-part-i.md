ORDER: No. 5, Public (Special-A), 2nd January 2014.
Under rule 5 of the Tamil Nadu State Judicial Service
(Cadre and Recruitment) Rules, 2007, the Government in
consultation with the Hon’ble High Court of Madras, Chennai,
hereby appoint the following persons as District Judges

The seniority of the candidates among themselves and
their seniority vis-à-vis promotee District Judges will be
determined after the proposed amendments to the statutory
rules.
2.The above District Judges (Entry Level) will draw a pay
of Rs.51,550/- in the scale of pay Rs.51550-1230-58930-
1380-63070 and other usual allowances and they will be on
probation for a total period of 2 years from the date of
joining duty within a continuous  period of three years. The
selected candidates are requested to furnish physical fitness
certificate direct to the Registrar-General, High Court of
Madras under intimation to Government.
3.During the period of probation, the above District
Judges will undergo:-
(i) Training as Civil Judge (Junior Division) for a period
of not less than six moths;
(ii) Training as Civil Judge (Senior Division) for a period
of not less than six months; and
(iii) Training in Forensic Science for a period of not less
than two months.
4.The above District Judges (Entry Level) should also
pass the Account Test for Executive Officers within the
period of probation. Only after satisfactory completion of the
training, the above District Judges will be posted  to act as
District Judge (Entry Level).
5. The posting orders for training as Civil Judges (Junior
Division), Civil Judges (Senior Division), etc., and joining
instructions will be issued by the Hon’ble Madras
High Court. The above District Judges (Entry Level) are
informed that other terms and conditions specified in
Tamil Nadu State Judicial Service (Cadre and Recruitment)
Rules, 2007, shall apply to them.
(By order of the Governor)
SHEELA  BALAKRISHNAN,
