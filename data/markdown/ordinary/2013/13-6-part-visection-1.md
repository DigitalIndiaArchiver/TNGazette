Draft Variation to the Approved Tiruchengode Detailed Development Plan No. 10 of Tiruchengode Local
Planning Authority, Salem Region.
(Roc. No. 23454/2011/DP1.)
No. VI(1)/196/2013.
In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (1) of Section 33 of Town and Country Planning Act 1971 (Act
No. 35 of 1972). The Commissioner of Town and Country Planning in the Proceedings Roc. No. 23454/2011/DP1 dated
12-3-2013, proposes to make the individual draft variation for conversion of Industrial use into Residential use in New T.S.
No. 1/2B. Ward-C, Block-12, (Old S.F. No. 167/1pt & 2pt) with an Extent-1.61 Acres (6520 Sq.m.). to the Approved
Tiruchengode Detailed Development Plan No. 10 in Director of Town and Country Planning proceedings in Roc. No. 5313/
2009 Dp1, dated 9-1-2010.
The said notification was published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No. 44 Part-VI, Section-1, Page No. 320, dated
14-11-2012.
2.
Any person affected or inspected in this draft variation may within sixty days from the date of publication of this
notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette can request in person or submit in writing to the Member Secretary/
Commissioner, Tiruchengode Local Planning Authority/Municipality, any objection and suggestions relating thereto.
3.
The Variation with plan may be inspected at free of cost at any time during office hours at the above said Local
Planning Authority/Municipality Office Tiruchengode.
