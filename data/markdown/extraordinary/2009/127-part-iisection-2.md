Whereas it appears to the Government of Tamil Nadu
that the lands specified in the Schedule below and situated
in the Vickramasingapuram Part-I Village, Ambasamudram
Taluk, Tirunelveli District are needed for a public purpose
for the formation of Gadana Dam Extension Scheme canal
work. Notice to that effect is hereby given to all to whom it
may concern in accordance with the provisions of
sub-section (1) of Section 4 of the Land Acquisition Act,
1894 (Central Act I of 1894).
And, whereas, it has become necessary to acquire
immediate possession of the lands specified in the
schedule below the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs
that the lands be acquired under the provisions of
sub section (1) of Section 17 of the said Act.
Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by
sub-section (2) of section 4 of the said Act, the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby authorises the Revenue Divisional
Officer, Cheranmahadevi and his staff and workmen to
exercise the power conferred by the said sub-section and
under clause (C) of Section 3 of the said Act. The Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the Revenue Divisional
Officer, Cheranmahadevi to perform the functions of a
Collector under section 5A of the said Act.
Under sub-section (4) of Section 17 of the said Act the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that in view of the
urgency of the case the provisions of section 5A of the said
Act shall not apply to this case.
