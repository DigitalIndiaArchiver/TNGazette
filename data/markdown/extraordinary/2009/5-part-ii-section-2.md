NOTIFICATION REGARDING THE  APPOINTMENT OF “TAMIL NADU URBAN INFRASTRUCTURE
FINANCIAL SERVICES LIMITED” AS THE CONSULTANT FOR THE “MINIATURE TAMIL NADU” PROJECT
UNDER TAMIL NADU TRANSPARENCY IN TENDERS ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 4, Finance (Salaries), 6th January 2009, Margazhi 22, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2039.] 
No. II(2)/FIN/(c)/2009.
Under clause (f) of Section 16 of the Tamil Nadu Transparency in Tenders Act, 1998 (Tamil Nadu
Act 43 of 1998), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notifies the appointment of the “Tamil Nadu Urban
Infrastructure Financial Services Limited” as the consultant to render advice to the Tourism and Culture
Department for the "Miniature Tamil Nadu" project till the completion of the works.
K. GNANADESIKAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
