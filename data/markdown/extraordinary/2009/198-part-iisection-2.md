REVENUE DEPARTMENT
APPOINTMENT OF COMMISSION OF INQUIRY TO INQUIRE INTO THE ALLEGATIONS OF FRAUDULENT GRABBING OF

Amendment to Notification
[G.O. Ms. No. 245, Revenue (LD3(2)), 27th July 2009.]
No. II(2)/REV/375(c)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section  3 of the Commissions of Inquiry Act, 1952 (Central Act
LX of 1952), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the public (Law & Order) Department
Notification No. II(2)/PULO/382(b)/2006, published at pages 1-2 of Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette
Extraordinary, dated the 27th July 2006, as subsequently amended:—
AMENDMENT
In the said Notification, in paragraph 4, for the expression “for a further period upto 31st July 2009 Afternoon”, the expression
“for a further period upto 30th September 2009 Afternoon”, shall be substituted.
N.S. PALANIAPPAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
