AMENDMENTS TO THE TAMIL NADU MOTOR VEHICLES RULES, 1989.
[G.O. Ms. No. 886, Home (Transport-I), 20th December 2011.]
No. SRO A-22(a-1)/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Sections 28, 38, 65, 96, 107, 111 and 138 read with Section 211 of the
Motor Vehicles Act, 1988 (Central Act 59 of 1988), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the
Tamil Nadu Motor Vehicles Rules, 1989, the draft of the same having been previously published as required by
sub-section (1) of Section 212 of the said Act:—
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules:—
(i)
in rule 49, after the expression “or through chalan in Government Sub-Treasury or Treasury”, the expression “or by online
payment through the website of Transport Department” shall be inserted;
(ii)
in rule 86, after the expression “or through India post office,” the expression “or by online payment through the website
of Transport Department” shall be inserted;
(iii) in rule 132, after the expression “or through India post office”, the expression “or by online payment through the website
of Transport Department” shall be inserted;
(iv) in rule 279, after the expression “or through India post office,” the expression “or by online payment through the website
of Transport Department” shall be inserted;
(v) in rule 294, after the expression “or through India post office,” the expression “or by online payment through the  website
of Transport Department” shall be inserted;
(vi) in rule 368, after the expression “Government Sub-Treasury or Treasury,” the expression “or by online payment through
the website of Transport Department” shall be added; and

AMENDMENTS TO THE TAMIL NADU MOTOR VEHICLES TAXATION RULES, 1974.
[G.O. Ms. No. 887, Home (Transport-I), 20th December 2011.]
No. SRO A-22(a-2)/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 24 of the Tamil Nadu Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1974 (Tamil Nadu Act 13
of 1974), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu Motor Vehicles Taxation
Rules, 1974:—
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules, in rule 3-A,
(1) in sub-rule (1), after the expression “India post office”, the expression “or by Online payment through the website of
Transport Department” shall be added;
(2) in sub–rule (2)
(i)
in clause 1, after the expression, “bankers cheque” the expression “or by Online payment through the website
of Transport Department”  shall be inserted; and
(ii) in clause 2, after  the expression “bankers cheque”, the expression “or by Online payment through the website
of Transport Department”  shall be inserted; and
(3) In the provisio, after the word “Regional Transport Officer concerned”, the words “or by Online payment through the
website of Transport Department” shall be inserted.
RAMESHRAM  MISHRA,
Principal Secretary to Government.
