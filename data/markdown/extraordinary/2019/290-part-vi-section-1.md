BAR COUNCIL OF TAMIL  NADU AND PUDUCHERRY
LIST OF CANDIDATES ELECTED AS MEMBERS OF THE BAR COUNCIL OF 
TAMIL NADU AND PUDUCHERRY
(R.O.C. No. 2050  of 2019)
No. VI(1)/337(a)/2019.
An Election of 25 Members to the Bar Council of Tamil Nadu and Puducherry was held on 28th March 2018. 
The counting of votes was commenced on 25th April 2018 and completed on 22-09-2018. The Hon’ble Mr. Justice 
A. Ramamurthy, Returning Oﬃ  cer, Bar Council has submitted the list of elected candidates to the Election Tribunal 
No.1, Bar Council of India for its approval. The Election Tribunal has approved the Elected Candidates on 02-07-2019 
and thereafter, the Bar Council of India has also approved the elected members list on 05-07-2019. The following 
25 Members are duly elected as Members of the Bar Council of Tamil Nadu and Puducherry:-
