APPOINTMENT OF CERTAIN PERSON AS ADDITIONAL ADVOCATE GENERAL-I OF TAMIL NADU
AT HIGH COURT OF MADRAS
[G.O. Ms. No. 129, Public (Law Officers), 6th February 2013, Thai 24, Nandhana, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2044.]
No. I/PULO/2(a)/2013.
In exercise of the powers conferred by article 162 of  the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
appoints Thiru A.L. Somayaji, Senior Advocate as Additional Advocate General-I of Tamil Nadu in the High Court of Madras,
Chennai in the newly created post from the date of his assuming office.
A. CHITTARANJAN  MOHANDOSS,
Secretary to Government.
