PLANNING, DEVELOPMENT AND SPECIAL INITIATIVES DEPARTMENT
APPOINTMENT OF COMPETENT AUTHORITY UNDER TAMIL NADU REQUISITIONING
AND ACQUISITION OF IMMOVABLE PROPERTY ACT, 1956.
[G.O. Ms. No. 224, Planning, Development and Special Initiatives (S.I.), 22nd November 2011,
Karthigai 6, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No. II(2)/PDSI/467(a)/2011.
In pursuance of clause (b) of Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Requisitioning and Acquisition of Immovable
Property Act, 1956, the State Government of Tamil Nadu hereby authorizes the District Collector, Chennai to
perform the functions of a competent authority under the said Act for lands temporarily required by
Chennai Metro Rail Project within Chennai District.
K. RAJARAMAN,
Secretary to Government
(Special Initiatives).
