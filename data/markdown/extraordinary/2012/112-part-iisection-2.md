 APPOINTMENT OF  VACATION CIVIL JUDGES FOR THE SUMMER VACATION OF CIVIL
COURTS FOR THE YEAR, 2012 UNDER THE TAMIL NADU CIVIL COURTS ACT.
[G.O. (D) No. 359, Home (Courts-III), 28th April 2012.]
No. II(2)/HO/224(c)/2012.

(Central Act III of 1873), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, in consultation with the High Court, Madras, hereby appoints the Judicial
Officers specified in column (2) of the Table below to be vacation Civil Judges for the District Courts in the Districts specified in
the corresponding entries in column (3) therof for the Summer Vacation of the Civil Courts for the year 2012.
