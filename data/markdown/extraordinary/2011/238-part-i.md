ALLOCATION OF SUBJECTS AMONG MINISTERS
[G.O.Ms.No. 676, Public (Special-B), 25th July 2011, Aadi 9, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No. I/PUSP/17(a)/2011.
Under clause (3) of Article 166 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
directs that the portfolios “Sports and Youth Welfare” held by Thiru C. Karuppasamy, Minister for
Sports and Youth Welfare, who has since been hospitalized, be allotted to Thiru C.Ve. Shanmugam,
Minister for School Education with immediate effect and he be designated as Minister for School
Education, Sports and Youth Welfare. Thiru C. Karuppasamy will continue to be a Minister.
DEBENDRANATH SARANGI,
Chief Secretary.
