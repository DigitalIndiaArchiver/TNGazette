(1) of Section 6 of the Charitable Endowments Act, 1890 
(Central Act VI of 1890), by Thiru S.Arun, 32, KG Layout,

II “SCHEME FOR THE ADMINISTRATION”
WHEREAS an application has been made to the 
Government of Tamil Nadu under clause (b) of sub-section 
(1) of section 6 of the Charitable Endowments Act, 1890 
(Central Act VI of 1890) by Thiru S.Arun, the donor of the 
said Endowment, for settling a scheme for the Administration 
of the said Fund, the Governor of Tamil Nadu, in exercise 
of the powers conferred by Section 5 of the said Act, 
hereby settled the Scheme set forth in the Scheme below 
for the administration of the said Fund and appoints the 
date of application of this notiﬁ cation in the Tamil Nadu 
Government Gazette as the date on which the Scheme 
as so called shall come into operation.

Rs.5000/- to the ﬁ rst rank student of Government College
