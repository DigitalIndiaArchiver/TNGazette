 PUBLIC DEPARTMENT

APPOINTMENT OF COMMISSION OF INQUIRY TO INQUIRE INTO THE CAUSES AND 
CIRCUMSTANCES LEADING TO THE OPENING OF FIRE RESULTING IN DEATH AND INJURIES 
TO PERSONS ON 22ND MAY 2018 AT THOOTHUKUDI UNDER THE COMMISSIONS OF 
INQUIRY ACT.
AMENDMENT TO NOTIFICATION
[G.O. Ms. No. 165, Public (Law and Order-F), 22nd February 2022,
மாசி 10, பிலவ, திருவள்ளுவர் ஆண்டு-2053.] 
No. II(2)/PULO/90(d)/2022.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Commissions of Inquiry 
Act, 1952 (Central Act LX of 1952), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment 
to the Public (Law and Order-F) Department Notification No.II(2)/PULO/481(c)/2018, published at 
pages 1-2 of Part II-Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 
23rd May 2018, as subsequently amended.
Amendment
In the said Notification, in paragraph 4, for the expression "forty five months", the expression 
"forty eight months" shall be substituted. 
	
D. JAGANNATHAN,
	
Secretary to Government.
