Appointment of certain person as Special Public
Prosecutor for conducting cases in Human Rights Court,
Virudhunagar on tenure basis under Protection of
Human Rights Act.
[G.O. (D) No. 1111, Home (Courts-VIA),
13th October 2008.]
No. II(2)/HO/623/2008.— In exercise of the powers
conferred by Section 31 of the Protection of Human Rights
Act, 1993 (Central Act, 10 of 1993), the Governor of Tamil
Nadu hereby appoints Thiru S. Kiruba Vijaya Anand, son of
S.M. Swaminathan, No. 78/3A, I.C.A. Colony, Virudhunagar
as Special Public Prosecutor for the purpose of conducting
cases in the Human Rights Court, Virudhunagar on Tenure
basis for a period of three years from the date of taking
charge of his appointment subject to observance of existing
terms and conditions of appointment.
Ad-Interim Order attaching of Properties due to default in
return of Deposits by Thiruvalargal Guru Benefit Fund,
Lalgudi, Tiruchirappalli under Tamil Nadu Protection of
Interest of Depositors (In Financial Establishments) Act.
[G.O. Ms. No. 1457, Home (Police-XIX),
30th October 2008.]
No. II(2)/HO/624/2008.— Whereas, complaints have been
received from a number of depositors that Guru Benefit
Fund, Lalgudi, Trichy, Financial Establishment which has
defaulted the return of deposits made by the depositors after
maturity;
2, And whereas, the Government are satisfied that the
said Financial Establishment is not likely to return the
deposits to the depositors and hence, the Government have
to protect the interests of such depositors;
3. And  whereas, the money and other properties
specified in the Schedules to this order are alleged to have
been procured by the partners of the said Financial
Establishment in their name and in the name of their family
members from and out of the deposits collected from the
depositors;
4. Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred
by Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Protection of Interests of
Depositors (in Financial Establishments) Act, 1997 (Tamil
Nadu Act 44 of 1997, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
makes an ad-interim order attaching the said properties in
the name of Thiru Shyamsundar, Son of Balakrishnan,
(Partner), Thirumathi Padmavathy, wife of Muthusamy, Partner
and Thiru Velliyangiri son of Natesamuthaliar, one of the
Partner of Sri Guru Benfit Fund, Lalgudi, Trichy, as specified
in the Schedule to this Order and transfers the control over
the said cash and other properties to the Competent Authority,
namely, the Competent authority and District Revenue Officer,
Trichy appointed under the provisions of the said Act, for
the purpose.
5. The competent Authority is requested to pursue further
action in accordance with the procedures laid down in sub-
sections (3) and (4) of section 4 of the said Act and also
the formalities prescribed in the Tamil Nadu Protection
of Interests of Depositors (in Financial Establishments)
Rules, 1997.
6. The Special Public Prosecutor, Special Court for the
Tamil Nadu Protection of Interests of Depositors (in Financial
Establishments) Act, 1997 is requested to render necessary
assistance to the Competent Authority, in filing the application,
before the said Special Court.

Declaration of the newly constructed building exclusively
for Women at the premises of Central Prison,
Cuddalore under the Prisons Act.
[G.O. (Ms.) No. 1430, Home (Pri.IV)

No. II(2)/HO/625/2008.—Under sub-clause (c) of clause
(1) of Section 3 of the Prisons Act, 1894 (Central Act IX
of 1894), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby declares the
newly constructed building for 100 prisoners at the
premises of Central Prison, Cuddalore to be a subsidiary
jail exclusively for women.
