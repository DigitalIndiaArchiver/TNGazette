The Governor of Tamil Nadu having satisfied that the lands specified in the Schedule below are acquired under
TNRSP-II  Highways, purpose, to wit for four laning and strengthening of Oddanchatram-Dharapuram-Tiruppur Road (SH/
37) from km 37/400  to  km 126/200 in   Mandavadi and Kariyampatti Villages in Oddanchatram  Taluk, Dindigul District
and it having already been decided that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the acquisition  lands is
to be paid out of the funds controlled and managed by the Highways Department and after having  considered  the cause
shown by the owner or other persons having interest in the said lands,  as the case may be,  do hereby issue and  publish
the following notice under sub-Section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34/2002)
NOTICE
Under sub-Section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002), the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the dry lands specified in the Schedule below and measuring to an extent of   23,503
Square metres in Mandavadi and Kariyampatti Villages, Oddanchatram  Taluk, Dindigul District  to the same, a little more
or less needed for TNRSP-II Highways  purpose, to wit for four laning and strengthening State Highways (SH37)
Oddanchatram-Dharapuram-Tiruppur  Road from km 37/400  to  km 126/200.
The plan of the lands under acquisition is kept in the Office of the Special District Revenue Officer (Land Acquisition),
Tamil Nadu Road Sector Project-II, Salem and the Special Tahsildar (Land Acquisition), Tamil Nadu Road Sector
Project-II, Tiruppur and may be inspected at any time during office Hours.
