MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION AND WATER SUPPLY DEPARTMENT
AMENDMENTS TO THE TAMIL NADU MUNICIPAL ENGINEERING SERVICE RULES, 1997.
[G.O. Ms. No. 118, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (M.E.III), 7th July 2009.]
No. SRO A-17(b)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 303, read with the Section 77-A of
the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act,1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby makes the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu Municipal Engineering Service Rules, 1997.
2. The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 2nd July 2008.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules,—
(1) in rule 2, for the expression "Category I-Superintending Engineer", the following expression
shall be substituted, namely:—
"Category I
..
Chief Engineer
Category I-A

Superintending Engineer";
