COMMERCIAL TAXES AND REGISTRATION DEPARTMENT
Exemption in respect of tax payable by Thiruvalargal Schieffelin Leprosy Research and Training Centre Private Limited,
Karigiri on sale of certain materials under Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax Act.
Variations to Notification
[G.O. Ms. No. 99, Commercial Taxes and Registration (B2), 17th July 2009, Aadi 1, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. II(1)/CTR/25/2009.—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (3) of Section 30 read with Section 88 of the
Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax Act, 2006 (Tamil Nadu Act 32 of 2006), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
makes the following variations to the Commercial Taxes and Religious Endowments Department Notification
No. II(1)/CTRE/70/81, published at page 10 of Part II—Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the
11th February 1981, as subsequently amended:—
VARIATIONS
In the said Notification,—
(1) for the expression “Schieffelin Leprosy Research and Training Centre Private Limited, Karigiri, North Arcot District”,
the expression “Schieffelin Institute of Health-Research and Leprosy Centre, Karigiri, Vellore District” shall be substituted;
(2) for the expression “on all sales made by the said Training Centre”, the expression “on all sales made by the said
centre” shall be substituted.
Amendments to Notifications
[G.O. Ms. No. 99, Commercial Taxes and Registration (B2), 17th July 2009, Aadi 1, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. II(1)/CTR/26/2009.—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (5) of Section 8 of the Central Sales Tax Act,
1956 (Central Act 74 of 1956), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Commercial Taxes
and Religious Endowments Department Notification No. II(1)/CTRE/71/81, published at page 10 of
Part II—Section 1 of  the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 11th February 1981, as subsequently amended.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Notification,—
(1) for the expression “Schieffelin Leprosy Research and Training Centre Private Limited, Karigiri, North Arcot
District”, the expression “Schieffelin Institute of Health-Research and Leprosy Centre, Karigiri, Vellore District” shall be
substituted;
(2) for the expression “in respect of all sales made by the said Training Centre”, the expression “in respect of all
sales made by the said centre” shall  be substituted.
