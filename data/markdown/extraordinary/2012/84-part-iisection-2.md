The Governor of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that
the lands specified in the Schedule below have to be
acquired for Industrial Purpose, to wit for the formation of
ULTRA MEGA POWER PROJECT, CHEYYUR and it has
already been decided that the funds controlled or managed
by the Officer Authorised Signatory. Coastal Tamil Nadu
Power Ltd., New Delhi and having considered  the cause
shown by the owners or other persons having interest in the
said lands, the Joint Commissioner (Land Reforms)
concerned, the interested party for the lands covered under
Tamil Nadu Land Reforms (fixation of ceiling on land)
Act, 1961, as the case may be, do here publish the following
notice under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the
Tamil Nadu  Acquisition of Land  for Industrial  Purposes
Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu  Act 10 of 1999), by relaxing G.O. (Ms.)
No. 2954, Revenue, dated 29th May 1973.
NOTICE
Under sub–section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Acquisition of Land  for Industrial  Purposes Act, 1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999) the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby acquired the lands specified in the Schedule below
and measuring an extent of  8.94.0 hectares. to the same,
a little more of less are needed for Industrial Purpose, to wit
for the formation of ULTRA MEGA POWER PROJECT,
CHEYYUR, No. 55. Vedal Village, Cheyyur Taluk,
Kancheepuram District.
The plan of the Lands are kept in the Office of Special
Tahsildar (Land Acquisition),  Unit –IV,  ULTRA MEGA POWER
PROJECT, CHEYYUR Scheme and may be inspected at any
time during office hours.
