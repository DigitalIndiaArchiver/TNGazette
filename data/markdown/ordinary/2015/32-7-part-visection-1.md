Final Closing and Cancellation of Registration of certain Co-operative Societies
Ind. No. 5. The Tamil Nadu Scientific Instruments Teaching Aids, Small Scale Manufacturing Industrial
Co-operative Society Ltd., Chennai.
(L.F. 1140/ICA/2009)
No. VI(1)/212/2015.
“In the circumstances stated by the Deputy Director of Industries and Commerce (Industrial Co-operatives), Guindy,
Chennai/Official Liquidator of the Tamil Nadu Scientific Instruments Teaching Aids, Small Scale Manufacturing Industrial
Co-operative Society Ltd. Ind. No. 5/Chennai in the final closure proposals dated 3-07-2015 and in exercise of
the powers delegated under Section 140(1) of Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act, 1983, the registration of the
Tamil Nadu Scientific Instruments Teaching Aids, Small Scale Manufacturing Industrial Co-operative Society Ltd.
Ind. No. 5/Chennai has been ordered to be cancelled and the affairs have been finally closed with effect
from 22nd July 2015 vide the Proceedings No. LF. 1140/ICA/2009 dated  22nd July 2015 of Additional Commissioner
of Industries & Commerce and Registrar of Industrial Co-operatives, Chennai-32”.
Ind. No. 11/MDS. The Computer Stationery Manufacturing Industrial Co-operative Society Ltd.
(L.F. 1147/ICA/2009)
No. VI(1)/213/2015.
“In the circumstances stated by the Deputy Director of Industries and Commerce (Industrial Co-operatives), Guindy,
Chennai/Official Liquidator of the Computer Stationery Manufacturing Industrial Cooperative Society Ltd.
Ind. No.11/MDS in the final closure proposals dated 30-07-2015 and in exercise of the powers delegated under Section
140(1) of Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act, 1983, the registration of the   Computer Stationery Manufacturing
Industrial Cooperative Society Ltd.Ind. No. 11/MDS has been ordered to be cancelled and the affairs have been finally
closed with effect from 22nd July 2015 vide the Proceedings No. LF. 1147/ICA/2009 dated 22nd July 2015 of Additional
Commissioner of Industries & Commerce and Registrar of Industrial Co-operatives, Chennai-32”.
Ind. No. 5. The Chatrapatti Surgical Dressing Cloth Manufacturer Service Industrial Co-operative
Society Ltd., Virudhunagar.
(L.F. 1187/ICA/2012)
No. VI(1)/214/2015.
“In the circumstances stated by the Deputy Director of Industries and Commerce (Industrial Co-operatives), District
Industries Centre, Virudhunagar/Official Liquidator of the Chatrapatti Surgical Dressing Cloth Manufacturer Service Industrial
Co-operative Society Ltd. Ind. No. 5. Virudhunagar in the final closure proposals dated 30-06-2015 and in exercise of
the powers delegated under Section 140(1) of Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act, 1983, the registration of the
Chatrapatti Surgical Dressing Cloth Manufacturer Service Industrial Cooperative Society Ltd.Ind. No. 5/Virudhunagar
has been ordered to be cancelled and the affairs have been finally closed with effect from 22nd July 2015 vide the
Proceedings No. LF. 1187/ICA/2012, dated 22nd July 2015 of Additional Commissioner of Industries & Commerce and
Registrar of Industrial Co-operatives, Chennai-32”.
Chennai-600 032,
REETA HARISH THAKKAR,
22nd July 2015.
Additional Commissioner of Industries and Commerce
and Registrar of Industrial Co-operatives.

Confirmation of Variation to the Approved Sattur Detailed Development Plan No. 5 of
Sattur Local Planning Authority.
(Roc.No. 22687/2011/DP1)
No. VI(1)/215/2015—
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 33(2) of Town and Country Planning Act 1971, the Director Town and
Country Planning in proceedings Roc. No. 22687/2011/DP1, dated 22nd July 2015 hereby confirms the following individual
variation to the deletion of 30’ road widening portion part in proposed 60' “AA” scheme road in
Sattur Municipality/Local Planning Authority Ward-A, Block-22 T.S.No. 13/1pt (Old S.No. 24/2pt) with in approximate
extent of 5535 sq.ft to the Sattur Detailed Development Plan No. 5 which was approved by the Director of Town and Country
Planning Proceeding No. 891/1992, DP-2, dated 8th December 1993 and  the notification was published in
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, Part VI—Section , Page No. 305, dated 30th August 1994.
2. Since No Objection and Suggestion was received on this draft notification within the stripulated time, the same
are here by confirmed and ordered as below.
CONFIRMATION OF VARIATION
.
 Whenever the expression Map No. 4 DDP/MR/DTCP No. 20/993 occurs the expression Map No. 5 DDP
(V)/DTCP No. 8/202 shall be added at the end to be road with.

3. In Schedule IV (Form No.7) in SI.No. 1 entry 30’ widening portion part in proposed “AA” 60’ scheme road shall
be deleted and in column No. 4 “0.9630 Acres” shall be substituted.
