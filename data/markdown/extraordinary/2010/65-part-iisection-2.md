MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION AND WATER SUPPLY DEPARTMENT
APPOINTMENT OF CERTAIN PERSON AS ENGINEERING DIRECTOR OF TAMIL NADU WATER SUPPLY
AND DRAINAGE BOARD UNDER THE TAMIL NADU WATER SUPPLY AND DRAINAGE BOARD ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 44, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (WS.3), 5th March 2010.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/128(e)/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 4 read with Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Water
Supply and Drainage Board Act, 1970 (Tamil Nadu Act 1 of 1971), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints
Thiru V. Mathivanan, Chief Engineer, Project Office, Combined Water Supply Scheme, Ramanathapuram as the
Engineering Director of the Tamil Nadu Water Supply and Drainage Board, with effect on and from the
5th March 2010.
NIRANJAN MARDI,
 Secretary to Government.
