[ELECTIONS - TOWN PANCHAYATS, THIRD GRADE MUNICIPALITIES, MUNICIPALITIES AND CORPORATIONS
- CONDUCT OF - PREPARATION AND PUBLICATION OF ELECTORAL ROLLS - ELECTORAL
REGISTRATION OFFICERS - AUTHORISATION OF NOTIFIED.]
(S.O.No. 6/2017/TNSEC/ME-1)
No.VI(2)/88(a-1)/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred by rule 8 of the Tamil Nadu Town  Panchayats, Third Grade
Municipalities, Municipalities and Corporations (Elections)  Rules, 2006 and in supersession of the Notification
No. I - VI(2)/91(b-l)/2016,  published at Page 1 of Part VI - Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette
Extraordinary No. 177, dated 2nd August 2016, the State Election Commission hereby  authorises the
Executive Officers of the respective Town Panchayats, Third Grade  Municipalities or any other officer
appointed by the State Election Officer / District  Election Officer as the case may be, as the Electoral
Registration Officers for the  purpose of preparing and publishing electoral rolls in respect of the respective
Town  Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities and the wards lying within the respective Town  Panchayats,
Third Grade Municipalities.
NOTIFICATION - II
(S.O.No. 6/2017/TNSEC/ME-1)
No.VI(2)/88(a-2)/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred by rule 8 of the Tamil Nadu Town  Panchayats, Third Grade
Municipalities, Municipalities and Corporations (Elections)  Rules, 2006 and in supersession of the Notification
No. II - VI(2)/91(b-2)/2016,  published at Page 1 of Part VI - Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette
Extraordinary, No. 177, dated 2nd August 2016, the State Election Commission hereby  authorises the
Commissioners of the respective Municipalities as the Electoral  Registration Officers for the purpose of
preparing and publishing electoral rolls in  respect of the respective Municipalities and the wards lying within
the respective  Municipalities.
