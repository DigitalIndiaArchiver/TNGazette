GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
Final closing of Affairs and Cancellation of Registration of certain Co-operative Societies.
IND. No. 190. The Nagercoil Metal Costing Workers Industrial Co-operative Society Ltd.
(L.F. No.998/ICA/1999.)
No. VI(1)/53/2012.
“In the circumstances stated by the Assistant Director of Industries and Commerce (Industrial Co-operatives) /
Official Liquidator of the Nagercoil Metal Costing Workers Industrial Co-operative Society Limited (Under Liquidation) in his final
closure proposals, dated 24-12-2012 and in exercise of the powers delegated under Section 140(1) of Tamil Nadu
Co-operative Societies Act, 1983, the registration of Nagercoil Metal Costing Workers Industrial Co-operative Society Limited
IND No. 190, (Under Liquidation) is hereby cancelled and the affairs are finally closed with effect from
7th January 2012”.
IND. No. 737. The Sulur Adi-Dravidar Cement Pipe Mft. Workers Industrial Co-operative Society Ltd.
(L.F. No. 1059/ICA/2001.)
No. VI(1)/54/2012.
“In the circumstances stated by the Assistant Director of Industries and Commerce (Industrial Co-operatives) /
Official Liquidator of the Sulur Adi-Dravidar Cement Pipe Mft. Workers Industrial Co-operative Society Limited
IND. No. 737 in his final closure proposals, dated 24th December 2011 and in exercise of the powers
delegated under Section 140(1) of Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act, 1983, the registration of the
Sulur Adi-Dravidar Cement Pipe Mft. Workers Industrial Co-operative Society Limited IND No. 737 is hereby cancelled and the
affairs are finally closed with effect from 13th January 2012”.
Chennai-5,
HARMANDER SINGH,
6th January 2012.
Industries Commissioner and Director of Industries
and Commerce/Registrar of Industrial Co-operatives.
