NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
HIGHER EDUCATION DEPARTMENT
APPOINTMENT OF CERTAIN PERSON AS REGISTRAR OF ANNA UNIVERSITY OF TECHNOLOGY,
CHENNAI UNDER ANNA UNIVERSITY OF TECHNOLOGY, CHENNAI ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 304, Higher Education (I1), 18th September 2010, Purattasi 2, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No. II(2)/HE/574(e1)/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 55 of Anna University of Technology, Chennai Act, 2010, (Tamil Nadu
Act 26 of 2010), the Government hereby appoint Dr. S. Gowri, Professor and Chairman, Faculty of Mechanical Engineering,
Anna University, Chennai as the first Registrar of Anna University of Technology, Chennai for a period of three years or till
he attains the age of 60 years whichever is earlier, from the date of his assuming office.
2. The terms and conditions of the appointment will be issued separately.
APPOINTMENT OF CERTAIN PERSON AS REGISTRAR OF ANNA UNIVERSITY OF TECHNOLOGY,
MADURAI UNDER ANNA UNIVERSITY OF TECHNOLOGY, MADURAI ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 305, Higher Education (I1), 18th September 2010, Purattasi 2, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No. II(2)/HE/574(e2)/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 55 of Anna University of Technology, Madurai Act, 2010, (Tamil Nadu
Act 27 of 2010), the Government hereby appoint Dr. E.B. Perumal Pillai, Professor of Civill Engineering and Director,
School of Engineering and Technology, Karpagam University, Coimbatore as the first Registrar of Anna University of
Technology, Madurai for a period of three years or till he attains the age of 60 years whichever is earlier, from the date of
his assuming office.
2. The terms and conditions of the appointment will be issued separately.
D. JOTHI JAGARAJAN,
Principal Secretary to Government
(In-charge).
