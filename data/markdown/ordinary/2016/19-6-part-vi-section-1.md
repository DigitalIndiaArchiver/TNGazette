GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
Final Closing and Cancellation of Registration of Ind. No. 1055. Bommidi Mallapuram Small Match
Producers Service Industrial Co-operative Society Limited.
(L.F. 637/ICA/1987)
No. VI(1)/179/2016.
“In the circumstances stated by the Assistant Director of Industries and Commerce (Industrial Co-operatives),
District Industries Centre, Dharmapuri/Official Liquidator of the Bommidi Mallapuram Small Match Producers
Service Industrial Co-operative Society Limited., Ind. No. 1055. in the final closure proposals, dated 29th March 2016
and in exercise of the powers delegated under Section 140(1) of Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act, 1983, the
Registration of the Bommidi Mallapuram Small Match Producers Service Industrial Co-operative Society Limited.,
Ind. No. 1055. has been ordered to be cancelled and the affairs have been finally closed with effect from
27th April 2016 vide the Proceedings No.L.F. 637/ICA/1987, dated 27th April 2016 of Additional Chief Secretary / Industries
Commissioner and Director of Industries and Commerce and Registrar of Industrial Co-operatives, Chennai-32”.
Chennai- 600 032,
AMBUJ SHARMA,
27th April 2016.
Additional Chief Secretary / Industries Commissioner and
Director of Industries and Commerce and
Registrar of Industrial Co-operatives.
Winding up of the affairs of the  Ind. No. 3/KGI. Hostia Industrial Co-operative Estate, Krishnagiri
District and Appointment of Official Liquidator.
(Rc. No. 2348/ICS/2016)
No. VI(1)/180/2016.
“Under the powers conferred on the Registrar of Industrial Co-operatives under Section 137(2)(b) of the
Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act, 1983 the activities of the Hostia Industrial Co-operative Estate
Ind. No. 3/KGI, Krishnagiri District have been ordered to be wound up with immediate effect by the Additional Chief
Secretary/Industries Commissioner and Director of Industries and Commerce and the Registrar of Industrial
Co-operatives, Chennai-32, vide Proc. Rc. No. 2348/ICS/2016, dated: 21-04-2016.”
Under Section 138(1) of the same Act, Assistant Director (Indl. Co-ops) District Industries Centre, Krishnagiri
has also been appointed as Official Liquidator of the said society for the purpose of Liquidation.
Chennai- 600 032,
AMBUJ SHARMA,
21st April 2016.
Additional Chief Secretary / Industries Commissioner and
Director of Industries and Commerce and
Registrar of Industrial Co-operatives.
