from 15th June 2013 on the classes of Motor Vehicles specified in Column (2) of the table   below which pass through the Dindigul
– Kodaikanal Road, Palani -  Kodaikanal Road and Cochin – Kodaikanal Road at Silver cascade and next to pillar rocks in
between Pillar rock and Moyar point  in Kodaikanal  in Dindigul District at the rates specified in the corresponding entries in
column (3) there of provided that such levy of tolls shall not be applicable to non – commercial motor  vehicles owned by the
