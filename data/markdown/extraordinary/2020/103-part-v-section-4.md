Now, therefore, in pursuance of sub-section (1) of Section 39 and Section 56 of the said Act, the Election Commission 
of India hereby-
(A)	 appoints, with respect to the said election;
	
(a)	 the 13th March, 2020 (Friday), as the last date for making nominations;
	
(b)	 the 16th March, 2020 (Monday), as the date for the scrutiny of the nominations;
	
(c)	 the 18th March, 2020 (Wednesday), as the last date for the withdrawal of candidatures;
	
(d)	 the 26th March, 2020 (Thursday), as the date on which a poll shall, if necessary, be taken; and
	
(e)	 the 30th March, 2020 (Monday), as the date before which the election shall be completed; and
(B)	 fixes the hours from 09:00 am to 04:00 pm, as the hours during which the poll shall, if necessary, be taken in 
all the above mentioned States on the date specified above for the election.
