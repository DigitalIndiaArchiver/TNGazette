ACQUISITION OF LANDS
(D.Dis. M1/3380/09.)
No. VI(1)/97(a)/2009.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that
the lands specified in the schedule below have to be acquired
for a public purpose and it having already been decided that
the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the
lands are to be paid out of funds controlled and managed by
the Government of Tamil Nadu, Public Works Department,
The following declaration is issued under Section 6 of the
Land Acquisition Act, 1894 (Central Act I of 1894).
DECLARATION
Under Section 6 of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894 (Central
Act I of 1894), the Principal Secretary and Commissioner of
Land Administration, Chepauk, Chennai- 600 005 hereby
declares that the lands specified in the schedule below
and measuring 0.99.0 hectare, be the same, a little more
or less are needed for a public purpose, to wit, for the
purpose of the Excavation of Right side High level Canal in
Village of Chinnakkampatty, Oddanchatram Taluk, under
Nanganchair Reservoir Scheme, Idayakottai.
A plan of the lands are kept in the office of the Land
Acquisition Officer/Revenue Divisional Officer, Palani,
Dindigul District and the same may be inspected at any
time during office hours.
