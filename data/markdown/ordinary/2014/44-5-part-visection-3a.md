GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI
Extension of Direct Management of Hazrath Syed Burhansha Taikkal Wakf, Pudupatnam
Village, Tanjore Taluk and District.
(Rc. No. 8789/07/B7/ TNJ.)
No. VI-3(a)/116/2014.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995 as amended by Act
27 of 2013,  the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in Item No. 85/14, in Rc.No. 8789/07/B7/TNJ, dated
26th August 2014 has resolved to ratify the period of direct management of Hazrath Syed Burhansha Taikkal
wakf,  Pudupatnam Village, Tanjore Taluk and District from 29-11-2013 to 25-08-2014 and also to extend the period of
direct management of the above Wakf for the period of one year from the Board's order dated 26th August 2014.
A. MOHAMED MEERAN,
Chennai-600 001,
District Revenue Officer /
14th November 2014.
Chief Executive Officer (In-charge),
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
Extension of Period of Direct Management of Hazrath Syed Sha Hameed Aulia Dargah Wakf,
Kanchipuram District.
(Lr.Rc. No. 10588/10/B4/KPM.)
No. VI-3(a)/117/2014.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995 the Tamil Nadu
Wakf Board in its resolution in item No. 124/14 in Rc. No. 10588/10/B4/Kanchipuram, dated 26th August 2014 has
resolved to extend the period of direct management of Hazrath Syed Sha Hameed Aulia Dargah Wakf,
Kanchipuram District for a further period of the year from 12-09-2014 to 11-09-2015.
A. MOHAMED MEERAN,
Chennai-600 001,
District Revenue Officer /
14th November 2014.
Chief Executive Officer (In-charge),
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
Extension of Direct Management of Sellancheri Thaikka and Dargah Wakf, Sellancheri, Cuddalore District.
(Rc. No. 6574/11/B4/Cuddalore.)
No. VI-3(a)/118/2014.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995 as amended by
Act 27 of 2013, the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in item No. 136/14 in Rc.No. 6574/11/B4/Cuddalore, dated
26th August 2014 has resolved to extend the period of direct management of Sellancheri Thaikka and Dargah Wakf,
Sellancheri, Cuddalore District for a further period of one year from 22-08-2014.
A. MOHAMED MEERAN,
Chennai-600 001,
District Revenue Officer /
14th November 2014.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
