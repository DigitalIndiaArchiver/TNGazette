MINISTER RELIEVED FROM HIS  MINISTERSHIP OF COUNCIL OF MINISTERS
[G.O. Ms. No. 571, Public (Special-B), 3rd July 2011, Aani 18, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No. I/PUSP/16(c-1)/2011.
I, Surjit Singh Barnala, Governor of Tamil Nadu accept the recommendation of the Chief Minister that
Thiru E. Subaya, Minister for Law, Courts and Prisons, may be relieved from the Council of Ministers and direct that
he be relieved of his duties and responsibilities as Minister with immediate effect.
SURJIT   SINGH   BARNALA,
Governor of Tamil Nadu.
ALLOCATION OF SUBJECTS AMONG MINISTERS
[G.O. Ms. No. 572, Public (Special-B), 3rd July 2011, Aani 18, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No. I/PUSP/16(c-2)/2011.
Under Clause (3) of Article 166 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
directs that
(i)
The portfolios "Law, Courts and Prisons, Personnel and Administrative Reforms and Prevention of
Corruption” hitherto held by Thiru E. Subaya be allotted to Thiru G. Senthamizhan. Thiru G. Senthamizhan be
designated as Minister for Information, Law, Courts and Prisons.

(ii) Thiru B.V. Ramana, Minister for Handlooms and Textiles shall continue to hold the portfolios of
"Handlooms and Textiles".
(iii) The portfolios "Food, Civil Supplies, Consumer Protection and Price Control" hitherto held by
Thiru Agri S.S. Krishnamoorthy be allotted to Thiru Budhichandhiran. Thiru Budhichandhiran be designated
as Minister for Food.
(iv) The portfolios "Tourism, Tourism Development Corporation" hitherto held by Thiru Budhichandhiran be
allotted to Thirumathi S. Gokula Indira. Thirumathi S. Gukula Indira be designated as Minister for Tourism.
(v) The portfolios "Commercial Taxes, Registration and Stamp Act" hitherto held by Thirumathi
S. Gokula Indira be allotted to Thiru Agri S.S. Krishnamoorthy. Thiru Agri S.S. Krishnamoorthy be designated as
Minister for Commercial Taxes and Registration.
DEBENDRANATH  SARANGI,
Chief Secretary.
