WORK IN CURTAILING ILLICIT LIQUOR, ON THE OCCASION OF REPUBLIC DAY, 2011.
[G.O. Ms. No. 50, Home, Prohibition and Excise (VII), 29th September 2010.]

Subordinate Service have been selected to receive the award of “Gandhi Adigal Police Medal” on
the occasion of Republic Day, 2011 for their outstanding work in curtailing the illicit liquor. They would
be paid cash prize of Rs. 20,000 (Rupees twenty thousand only) each:—
