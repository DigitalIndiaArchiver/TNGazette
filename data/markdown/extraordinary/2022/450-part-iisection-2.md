HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE DEPARTMENT
APPOINTMENT OF APPROPRIATE AUTHORITY FOR REGULATION OF ASSISTED REPRODUCTIVE 
TECHNOLOGY (REGULATION) ACT, 2021 AND (SURROGACY) ACT, 2021.
[G.O. Ms. No.324, Health and Family Welfare (R1), 19th September 2022,
Purattasi 2, Subakiruthu, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2053.] 
No. II(2)/HFW/757(d)/2022.
In exercise of powers conferred under sub-section (3) (b) of section 12 of the Assisted Reproductive Technology (Regulation) 
Act, 2021 (Central Act 42 of 2021) read with sub-section (3) (b) of section 35 of the Surrogacy (Regulation) Act, 2021 (Central 
Act 42 of 2021) the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the Joint Directors(Health Services) in the concerned Districts 
(except Chennai) as the
District Appropriate Authority to implement and enforce the provisions of the Assisted Reproductive Technology 
(Regulation) Act, 2021 and Surrogacy (Regulation) Act, 2021
“For Chennai District, the Joint Director (Acts) shall be the District Appropriate Authority to enforce the provisions 
of the Assisted Reproductive Technology (Regulation) Act, 2021 and Surrogacy (Regulation) Act, 2021”.
 
P.SENTHILKUMAR, 
 
 
 
Principal Secretary to Government.
