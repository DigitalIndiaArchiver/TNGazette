JUDICIAL NOTIFICATIONS
Amendments to the Madras High Court Service Rules
(R.o.c. No. 137/2006/RAC)
No. SRO C-19/2013.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Article 229(1) and (2) of the Constitution of India and of all other
powers connected thereto, The Hon’ble the Chief Justice is pleased to make the following amendments to the Madras High Court
Service Rules:-
AMENDMENT - I
In the Madras High Court Service Rules, under Division II of Rule 3, - the entries, “Category 8 (b) Copyist” may be
deleted. After such deletion the entries “8(a) Reader/Examiner / Proof Reader” may be substituted as follows:
“8. Reader/Examiner / Proof Reader”.
AMENDMENT - II
In Division II of Rule 3, after the existing entries under the heading ’NOTE’, the following may be added:
 “The post of Copyist is merged with the post of Typist in Category 7 (a).”
AMENDMENT - III
In Rule 14, under the heading “Promotion in Division II” shall be as follows:—
(i)  for the existing entries “To Category 6 from Categories 7, 8 & 9” and the proviso clause thereunder, the following
entries shall be substituted.
“To Category 6 from Categories 7(a) and 8 in the ratio 2 : 1 i.e., for every 3 vacancies in the post of Assistants
(Category 6) two persons from Category 7(a) (Typist) and one person from Category 8 (Reader / Examiner / Proof Reader)
shall be promoted.”
(ii)
for the existing entries “To Category 7 from Category 8” the following entries shall be substituted.
“To Category 7 and 8 from Categories 3, 5 and 6 of Section I and from Categories 1 and 2 of Section II of
Division III subject to their possessing the requisite qualifications prescribed under Rule 6 & 7.
AMENDMENT - IV
In the Schedule to Rule 7, the entries,
“Category 8 (b) Copyist under column (1) and the corresponding entries under column (2) thereof may be
deleted”.
AMENDMENT - V
In the Annexure to Rule 6, under the heading Method of appointment to the service by direct recruitment - Direct
recruitment of-, the entries,
“Copyists” may be deleted.
The above amendments shall be deemed to have come into force with effect from 01-03-2011.
High Court, Madras,
P. KALAIYARASAN,
14th November 2013.
Registrar-General.
