No. II(2)/HE/277/2012.—In exercise of the powers
conferred under 25(b)-Class III-Other Members (3) of the
Alagappa University Act, His Excellency the Governor-
Chancellor hereby nominates the following one University
Professor to the Syndicate of Alagappa University, Karaikudi

Constitution of Separate District Munsif Court at
Tiruchendur by converting the existing Principal District
Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court at Tiruchendur
in Thoothukkudi District-under Tamil Nadu Civil Courts
Act.
NOTIFICATION-I
[G.O.Ms.No. 282, Home (Courts-III), dated 2nd April 2012.]
No. II(2)/HO/278/2012.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by the second paragraph of section 4 of the
Tamil Nadu, Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873),
The Governor of Tamil Nadu after consultation with the High
Court of Madras, hereby fixes the number of District Munsifs
to be appointed to the District Munsif Court, Tiruchendur as
one with effect from the date of constitution of District Munsif
Court at Tiruchendur by converting the Principal District
Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Tiruchendur.
NOTIFICATION-II
[G.O.Ms.No. 282, Home (Courts-III), dated 2nd April 2012.]
No. II(2)/HO/279/2012.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by section 10 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act,
1873 (Central Act III of 1873), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby directs that the Subordinate Judge’s Court,
Thoothukkudi, shall have and exercise original jurisdiction
over the District Munsif Court, Tiruchendur with effect from
the date of consititution of District Munsif Court at Tiruchendur
by converting the Principal District Munsif-cum-Judicial
Magistrate Court, Tiruchendur.
R. RAJAGOPAL,
Principal Secretary to Government.
