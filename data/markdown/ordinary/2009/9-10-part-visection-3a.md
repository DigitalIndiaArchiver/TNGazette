GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI-600 001.
Taking over of Masthan Avulia Dargah Wakf, Wallajabad, Kancheepuram District
under the Direct Control of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
(Rc.No. 5807/08/B3/Kanchee.)
No. VI-3(a)/18/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995, the Tamil Nadu
Wakf Board in its resolution in item No. 172/08 in Rc.No. 5807/08/B3/Kanchee, dated 18th November 2008 has ordered
to take over the Management of Masthan Avulia Dargah Wakf, Wallajabad, Kancheepuram District under the Direct
Control of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of 2 years from the date of pronouncement of the Boards order,
dated 27th January 2009 and to appoint the Inspector of Wakfs, Kancheepuram, as its Executive Officer to look after
the day to day affairs of the Wakf.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
2nd March 2009.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
