AMENDMENT TO NOTIFICATION
No. II(2)/CTR/281(c)/2013.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 54 of the Tamil Nadu Societies Registration Act, 1975
(Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 1975), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Commercial
Taxes and Registration Department Notification  No. II(2)/CTR/730(b)/2012, dated the 6th November 2012,  published on
Page 1 of Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette  Extraordinary, dated the 6th November 2012.
2.
The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force with effect on and from the date of coming
into force of the said Act.
AMENDMENT
In the said Notification, for the expression “(3) Societies which receive financial aid or assistance from the Government;
and”, the following expression shall be substituted, namely,-
“(3) Societies which receive or had received financial aid or grant  or funds or assistance from the
Government; and”.
