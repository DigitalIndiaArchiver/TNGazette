In exercise of the powers conferred by Paragraph 3 of the Tamil Nadu Local Bodies Election Symbols
(Reservation and Allotment) Order, 2016, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission hereby notifies that
the elections to the following offices shall be held on party basis:-
1. Members of Town Panchayats; and
2. Councillors of  Municipalities and Municipal Corporations.
