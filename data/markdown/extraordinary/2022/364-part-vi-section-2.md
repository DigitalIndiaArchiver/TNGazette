No. VI(2)/66(c)/2022.
WHEREAS, Thiru.S.Raja Mohamed, Councillor of Ward No. 26 of Periyakulam Municipality, Theni District has been 
disqualified to act as ward member and Vice Chairman of the said Municipality vide reference first read above, by the 
Municipal Commissioner, Periyakulam;
WHEREAS, the Commissioner, Periyakulam Municipality, has stated in the reference 1st read above that consequent 
on the above disqualification, the seat of ward member No. 26 and the office of Vice Chairman Periyakulam who was 
holding, became vacant and also stated that there are no election petitions pending in any Court of law challenging 
the said vacancy;
WHEREAS, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission, inter-alia, notified casual elections to fill up the vacancy 
of Ward No. 26 of Periyakulam Municipality, Theni District scheduled to be held on 9.7.2022 vide Notification Second 
read above;
WHEREAS, the Election Process commenced and the process of the withdrawal of candidatures has been completed 
as per the schedule of program of casual election notified in the Statutory Order in the reference 2nd read above;

WHEREAS, in the meanwhile Thiru.S.Raja Mohamed, filed I. A. petition in E.O.P on the file of the Principal 
District Judge Court, Theni in I.A.No.01/2022 in EL.O.P No 41/2022, under Section 51(A) of the Tamil Nadu District 
Municipalities Act, 1920 to issue an order of Ad-interim injunction restraining the Respondents 1 to 3 from preventing him 
from discharging his duty as Vice Chairman of Periyakulam Municipality, Theni District pending disposal of the petitions;
WHEREAS, Principal District Judge Court, Theni has ordered in the reference 3rd cited the following ;
“11. ……….This Court finds that the petitioner had established his prima facie right continue to function as 
Vice Chairman of Periyakulam Municipality and therefore this Court restrains the Respondent 1 to 3 from 
preventing the petitioner from discharging his duty as Vice Chairman Periyakulam Municipality, Theni District 
for a period of 8 weeks. Notice to Respondent ordered. Private Notice is also permitted. Call on 18.8.2022…”
WHEREAS, the District Election Officer/District Collector, Theni has also reported the above facts in the reference 
5th in this regard;
WHEREAS, a difficulty has thus arisen in conducting the election to Member of Ward No.26 of Periyakulam 
Municipality on 9.7.2022 and it is, therefore, considered expedient to cancel the election of Ward No. 26 of Periyakulam 
Municipality as it is not possible to complete the election process to the above said Ward as notified by the
Tamil Nadu State Election Commission;
AND WHEREAS, in view of the above orders of the Court of the Principal District Judge, Theni, in I.A.No.01/2022 
in EL.O.P. No. 41/2022, dated: 23.6.2022, and to comply the Orders of the Court, the Tamil Nadu State Election 
Commission has decided to cancel the election to elect the Councillor of Ward number 26 of Periyakulam Municipality, 
Theni District, issued under reference 2nd read above;
 NOW, THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred under 139 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Municipalities 
and Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006, read with rule 22 of the said Rule and section 27 of the Tamil Nadu District 
Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920), the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission hereby issue the following 
amendment to S.O.No.35/2022/TNSEC/ME-I, dated 16.6.2022 published in Gazette No.350, published as Notification 
No. VI(2)/59/(b-1)/2022 at pages 1 to 2 in Part VI—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary 
dated 20.06.2022 and orders that all the election proceedings of the Returning Officer and Assistant Returning Officer 
for the election of said ward in pursuance of rules 23 to 29 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Municipalities and 
Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006, so far as it relates to the above Ward No. 26 be held as null and void.
Amendment
In the said Notification,
in the Annexure, in the Table, under the heading Municipal Councillor, Sl.No. 2 of the table and the entries relating 
thereto shall be deleted.
