LEGISLATIVE  ASSEMBLY  SECRETARIAT
EXTENSION OF SERVICE
[S.O. Ms. No. 92, Legislative Assembly Secretariat, 31st May 2012,
Vaikasi 18, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2043.]
No. II(1)/LAS/14(c)/2012.
The Governor of Tamil Nadu in consultation with the Hon. Speaker, Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly,
direct that Thiru A.M.P. Jamaludeen, Secretary, Legislative Assembly Secretariat, be granted extension of
service on public grounds for five years from 1st June 2012 F.N.
2.
Thiru A.M.P. Jamaludeen, will continue to act as Secretary, Legislative Assembly Secretariat
during this period.
3.
This order issues with the concurrence of the Personnel and Administrative Reforms Department
vide its U.O. No. 20631/Principal Secretary/12, dated 28th May 2012 and the Finance Department
vide its U.O.  No. 1899/FS/P/12, dated 28th May 2012.
(By order of the Governor)
A.M.P.  JAMALUDEEN,
Secretary.
