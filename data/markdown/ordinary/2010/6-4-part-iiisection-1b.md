ANIMAL HUSBANDRY, DAIRYING AND FISHERIES DEPARTMENT
Ad hoc Rules relating to Temporary post of Junior Assistant in the office of the Directorate of Audit for
Milk Co-operatives, Chennai for Tamil Nadu Ministerial Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 8, Animal Husbandry, Dairying and Fisheries (MP.II), 22nd January 2010.]
No.SRO B-8/2010.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following rules:—
2. The rules hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 24th August 2007.
RULES
The General and the Special Rules applicable to the holders of the permanent post of Junior Assistant in the
Tamil Nadu Ministerial Service shall apply to the holder of the temporary post of Junior Assistant in the Office of the
Director of Audit for Milk Co-operatives, Chennai, subject to the modifications specified in the following rules:—
1. Constitution.—The post shall constitute a temporary addition to the said category in the said service.
2. Appointment.—Appointment to the post shall be made as follows—
(i)
by direct recruitment; or
(ii)
by recruitment by transfer.
3. Appointing authority.—The appointing authority for the post shall be the Director of Audit for Milk Co-operatives.
C. MUTHUKUMARASWAMY,
Secretary to Government.
HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE DEPARTMENT
Ad hoc Rules relating to Temporary post of Associate Professor in Hand Surgery for Tamil Nadu Medical Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 15, Health and Family Welfare (A1), 25th January 2010, Thai 12, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No. SRO B-9/2010.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following rules:—
2. The rules hereby made shall come into force on the 25th January 2010.
RULES
The General and the Special Rules applicable to the holders of the permanent post of Associate Professor in
Class I, in Branch-I Medical of the Tamil Nadu Medical Service shall apply to the holder of the temporary post of
Associate Professor in Hand Surgery, subject to the modifications specified in the following rules:—
2. Constitution.—The post shall constitute a distinct category in the said class of the said Branch of
the said Service.
3. Appointment.—Appointment to the post shall be made as follows:—
(i) by transfer from the category of Associate Professor in Class I, Branch-I, Medical of the said Service; or
(ii) by promotion from the category of Assistant Professor in the Government Medical Colleges.

4. Qualification.—No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post unless he possesses the following
qualifications, namely.—
(i) Must Possess MS (Orthopaedics);
(ii) Must possess teaching experience for a period of not less than five years as Assistant Professor in
Orthopaedic Surgery in a Medical College or teaching institution recognized by the Medical Council of
India, New Delhi;
(iii) Must have served for a period of not less than two years in the Department of Hand Surgery; and
(iv) Must possess two years certificate course in Hand Surgery in a Government Medical College or in any
other Institution recognized by the Medical Council of India, New Delhi.
5. Pay—There shall be paid to the holder of the post a monthly pay calculated in the scale of
Rs. 15600-39100 and Grade Pay of Rs. 7600.
V.K. SUBBURAJ,
Principal Secretary to Government.
