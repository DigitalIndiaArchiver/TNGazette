No. II(1)/FIN/13/2016.
The following Notification is Published:-
NOTIFICATION
[No.320 (L)/W&M.I / 2016, dated the 8th November, 2016.]
The Government of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Finance Department Notification
No. 161(L)/(W&M-I)/2006, dated the 28th September 2006 relating to the Scheme for Constitution and Administration of
the Consolidated Sinking Fund of Government of Tamil Nadu.
With effect from June 23, 2014, the nomenclature of Special Ways and Means Advances (Special WMA) to the State
Government has been changed into Special Drawing Facility, by the Reserve Bank of India.
Consequently,  for the words “SPECIAL WMA” appearing in proviso to explanation (d) of clause 4 of the Scheme
for Constitution and Administration of the Consolidated Sinking Fund of Government of Tamil Nadu issued vide Notification
No.161(L)/(W&M-I)/2006, dated 28th September 2006, the words “SPECIAL DRAWING FACILITY” shall be substituted.
All other provisions of the aforesaid Scheme for Constitution and Administration of the Consolidated Sinking Fund
of Government of Tamil Nadu shall continue to be in full force and effect.
(By order and in the name of the Governor of Tamil Nadu)
K. SHANMUGAM,
Additional Chief Secretary to
Government of Tamil Nadu,
Finance Department.
