No. SRO A-35/2020.—In exercise of the powers conferred by rule 41 of the Tamil Nadu Brewery Rules, 1983, the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby exempts Thiruvalargal United Breweries Limited, Aranvoyal Village, Thiruvallur District from 
the operation of sub-rule (2) of rule 10 of the said rules in so far as it relates to the maximum time limit specified therein so 
as to enable the said company to install the New Pasteurizer in Bottling Line-2 and to dispose of the existing old one subject 
to the condition that the said works shall be completed on or before the 31st December 2020. 
2. This Notification shall be deemed to have come into force with effect on and from the 2nd May 2020.
 
S.K. PRABAKAR,
 
Additional Chief Secretary to Government.

No. SRO A-36/2020.—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 30 of the Micro, Small and 
Medium Enterprises Development Act, 2006 (Central Act 27 of 2006), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following 
amendment to the Tamil Nadu Micro and Small Enterprises Facilitation Council Rules, 2017, namely:- 
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖග
In the said Rules, in rule 7, in sub-rule (i), for the expression “the Udyog Aadhar Memorandum (UAM) number,”, the 
expression “Udyam  Registration number  or Udyog Aadhar Memorandum (UAM) number (which shall be valid for a period 
up to the 31st March 2021)” shall be substituted.
 
K. GOPAL,
Principal Secretary to Government (FAC).
