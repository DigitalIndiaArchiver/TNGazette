Variations to the Approved Master Plan for the Cuddalore Local Planning Area

[G.O.(2D) No.110, Housing and Urban Development UD4(1) Department, Dated: 07-07-2015]
No. VI(1)/250/2015.
In exercise of the powers delegated to the Local Planning Authority for Reclassification streamlining procedure under
Town and Country Planning Act 1971 published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No.27, Part II Section-2 Page 228,
dated 15th July 2009, Member Secretary, Cuddalore Local Planning Authority hereby makes the following Variation under
sub-section (4) of Section 32 of Town and Country Planning Act 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972) to the Master Plan
of  Cuddalore  Local Planning Area approved under the said Act and published with the Housing and Urban Development
Department Notification No. II(2)/HOU/490/2006 at Page 326 of Part II—Section-2 of the Tamil Nadu Government
Gazette, dated 20th September 2006.
VARIATIONS
In the said Master Plan in Annexure 3(1) Proposed  Land use zoning.
(1) Under the heading Cuddalore Town “VI AGRICULTURAL USE ZONE”
(i)
Under the sub-heading ward No.7 for the expression 1730pt, 1899 to 1906 before 1933 (Except 1934pt,
1935pt) 1936 to 1945 shall be substituted.
(ii) Under the heading Cuddalore Town 1(b) Mixed Use Zone.
(i) Under sub-heading ward No.7 after the expression 1908 to 1930 of 1934pt, 1935pt shall be inserted.
