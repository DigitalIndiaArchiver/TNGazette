No. SRO A-19/2014.—The following Notification of the Government of India, Department of Personnel and Training,
New Delhi, the 6th June, 2014 is Republished:—
G.S.R. 383 (E):—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the All India Services Act, 1951
(61 of 1951), read with sub-rule (2) of rule 4 of the Indian Administrative Service (Cadre) Rules, 1954 the Central Government,
in consultation with the Government of Tamil Nadu, hereby makes the following regulations further to amend the Indian
Administrative Service (Fixation of Cadre Strength) Regulations, 1955 namely: -
