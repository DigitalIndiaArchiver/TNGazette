NOTIFICATIONS  BY  THE  LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY SECRETARIAT
LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY SECRETARIAT
VACATION OF SEAT IN TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY DUE TO RESIGNATION OF
DR. M. THAMBIDURAI, MEMBER OF THE TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY.
[Letter No. 9138/2009-1, Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Secretariat (B.III), 20th May 2009.]
No. II(1)/LAS/10(a)/2009.
“Whereas, Dr. M. Thambidurai, a Member of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly representing the
78. Bargur Assembly Constituency, in his letter, dated 19th May, 2009 addressed to me as Speaker of the
Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly, has resigned his seat as Member of the said Assembly with effect from
19th May 2009.
Now, therefore, I, R. AVUDAIAPPAN, Speaker of the said Assembly hereby accept the resignation of
Dr. M. Thambidurai, as envisaged under Rule 22 of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Rules as it is
in accordance with Rule 21 of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Rules. Under Article 190(3)(b) of the
Constitution of India, his seat has, therefore, become vacant with effect from the above date.
Secretariat,
R. AVUDAIAPPAN,
Chennai-600 009,
Speaker,
Dated 20th May, 2009.
Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly.
M. SELVARAJ,
Secretary,
Legislative Assembly Secretariat”.
