School Certiﬁcates of the following pupils are reported to have been loﬆ beyond recovery. Necessary steps are being taken 
to issue the duplicate of the said Certiﬁcates. If the Originals were to be found by anybody, they should be treated as invalid 
and sent to the Secretary, Board of Higher Secondary Examination / Directorate of Government Examinations, Chennai-6 / 
Director of Technical Education, Chennai-25 / Inspector / Inspectress of Matriculation and Anglo-Indian Schools as the case 
may be for cancellation:–
