Notifications or Orders of interest to a section of the public
issued by Secretariat Departments.
NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
HOME DEPARTMENT
(Courts-III)
APPEALS FROM THE DECREES OR ORDERS OF THE DISTRICT MUNSIF COURT, JAYANKONDAM TO
BE PREFERRED IN THE COURT OF SUBORDINATE JUDGE, JAYANKONDAM AT ARIYALUR
DISTRICT.
[G.O. Ms. No.352, Home (Courts-III),11th April 2017.]
No. II(2)/HO/230(h)/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred under the first proviso to Section 13 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts
Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873), the High Court, Madras hereby directs that appeals from the decrees or
orders of the District Munsif Court, Jayankondam be preferred in the court of Subordinate Judge, Jayankondam
with effect from the date on which the Subordinate Judge, Jayankondam assumes charge of that Court.
NIRANJAN MARDI,
Additional Chief Secretary to Government.
