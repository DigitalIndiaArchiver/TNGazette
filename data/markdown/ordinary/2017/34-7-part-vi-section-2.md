Appointment of the Subordinate Judges of Dharmapuri, Ariyalur and the Principal Subordinate Judge of
Tiruppur as the Secretary of the Respective District Legal Services Authority.
(TNSLSA No. 4402/E/2017)
No.VI(2)/118/2017.
Under the powers vested under Section 9 (3) of the Legal Services Authorities Act, 1987 (Central Act, 39/1987) as
amended, the Tamil Nadu State Legal Services Authority, appoints the Subordinate Judges listed below to be the Secretary
of the respective District Legal Services Authority to exercise such powers and perform such duties under the Chairman of
that Authority as may be assigned to him by the concerned Chairman.

Constitution of Taluk Legal Services Committee at Sulur in Coimbatore District and Appointment of Judicial
Magistrate, Sulur as Ex-Officio Chairman of Sulur Taluk Legal Services Committee.
(TNSLSA No. 5111/E/2017)
No.VI(2)/119/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 11 (A) (1) of the Legal Services Authorities Act 1987, (Central Act
39/1987) read with Rule 3 (a) of the Tamil Nadu Taluk Legal Services Committee Rules, 1997, the Tamil Nadu State Legal
Services Authority hereby constitutes a Taluk Legal Services Committee at Sulur in Coimbatore District and appoints the
Judicial Magistrate, Sulur as ex-officio Chairman, Sulur Taluk Legal Services Committee to exercise the powers and perform
the functions conferred on, or assigned to the Taluk Legal Services Committee under the Act.
