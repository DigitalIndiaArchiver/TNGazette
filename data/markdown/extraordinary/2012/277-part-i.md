CERTAIN MINISTER RELIEVED FROM THIER MINISTERSHIP OF COUNCIL OF MINISTERS.
[G.O. Ms. No. 843, Public (Special-B), 3rd October 2012, Purattasi 17, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2043.]
No. I/PUSP/33(a-1)/2012.
I, K. Rosaiah, Governor of Tamil Nadu, accept the recommendation of the Chief Minister that
Thiru C.Ve. Shanmugam, Minister for Commercial Taxes & Registration, Law, Courts and Prisons may be relieved
from the Council of Ministers and direct that  he be relieved of his duties and responsibilities as Minister with
immediate effect.
K. ROSAIAH,
Governor of Tamil Nadu.
ALLOCATION OF SUBJECTS AMONG MINISTERS
[G.O. Ms. No. 844, Public (Special-B), 3rd October 2012, Purattasi 17, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2043.]
No. I/PUSP/33(a-2)/2012.
Under Clause (3) of Article 166 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that:
(i) Tmt. B. Valarmathi, Minister for Social Welfare will additionally hold the portfolio of
‘Nutritious Noon Meal Programme’ and she may be designated as Minister for Social Welfare and Nutritious Noon
Meal Programme.
(ii) The portfolio of ‘Environment and Pollution Control’ may be allocated to Thiru M.C. Sampath and he may
be designated as Minister for Environment.
