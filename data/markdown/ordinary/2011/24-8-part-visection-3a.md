    GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI
Taking over the Direct Management of Syed Burre Shaheed Dargah Wakf, Perambakkam,
Tiruvallur Taluk and District.
(Rc.No. 4751/06/TVLR/B4.)
No. VI-3(a)/39/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995, the
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in item No. 168/07, dated 5th October 2010 in R.C. No. 4751/C1/06/TVLR
has ordered to take over the Management of SYED BURRE SHAHEED DARGAH WAKF, PERAMBAKKAM, TIRUVALLUR
TALUK AND DISTRICT under the direct control of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of one year from the
date of publication in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette and to appoint the Wakf Inspector, Tiruvallur as its
Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
5th May 2011.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
