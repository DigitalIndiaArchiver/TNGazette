GENERAL  NOTIFICATION
TAMIL NADU WAQF BOARD, CHENNAI - 600 001
Taking over The Direct Management of Burma Colony Jamia Masjid, Tanjore District By The 
Tamil Nadu Waqf Board.
(Lr.Rc.No. 1822/18/B7/TNJ)
No. VI-3(a)/20/2022.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Waqf Act 1995, the Tamil Nadu 
Waqf Board in its Resolution Item No.189/21 in Rc.No.1822/18/B7/Tanjore dated 02-03-2022 has ordered to take over 
the Management of Burma Colony Jamia Masjid Waqf (Registration No.326/TNJ) Tanjore District under the control 
of Tamil Nadu Waqf Board for a period of SIX MONTHS from 02.03.2022 to 01.09.2022 and to appoint the Waqf 
Inspector, Tanjore as its Executive Oﬃ  cer.
Chennai-600 001.
