    GENERAL NOTIFICATION
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI
Taking over of the Atteshkhan Rahimkhan Thaikka, Palayapettai, Tirunelveli District
under the direct Management.
(Rc. No. 9237/06/B1/TNV)
No. VI-3(a)/42/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995 the
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its proceedings in Item No. 143/07, in Rc. No. 9237/B1/TNV/06, dated 10th November 2010,
has ordered to take over the Management of Atteshkhan Rahimkhan Thaikka, Palayapettai, Tirunelveli District under the
direct control of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of one year from the date of publication in the Tamil Nadu
Government Gazette and to appoint the Superintendent of Wakfs, Tirunelveli as its Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
26th April 2011.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
