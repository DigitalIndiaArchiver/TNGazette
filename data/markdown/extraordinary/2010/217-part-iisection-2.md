ACQUISITION OF LANDS
[G.O. Ms. No. 186, Transport (I.2), 22nd July 2010.]
No. II(2)/TRA/479(a)/2010.
The Governor of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that
the lands specified in the schedule below have to be
acquired for Industrial purpose, to wit, for the Madurai Airport
Expansion Scheme and it having already been decided that
the entire amount of compensation to be, awarded for the
lands to be paid out of the lands, controlled or managed by
Government. The following notice is issued under sub-
section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of land
for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil
Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
acquires the lands specified in the schedule below and measuring
5.76.89 hectares (or) 14.24 Acres, to the same, a little more or
less needed for Industrial Puspose, to wit, for the Madurai
Airport Expansion Scheme.
The Plan of the lands are kept in the office of the Special
Tahsildar (Land Acquisition), Madurai Airport Expansion Scheme,
Unit II, Block No. V, Madurai South Taluk, Madurai  District
and may be inspected at any time during  office hours.
