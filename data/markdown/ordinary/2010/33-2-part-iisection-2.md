HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE DEPARTMENT.
Declaration of the Sathyabama University Dental College
and Hospital, Chennai to be a Teaching Medical
Institution for the purposes of the Tamil Nadu
Anatomy Act.
[G.O. (3D), No. 16, Health and Family Welfare (Z1),
4th August 2010, Aadi 19, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No. II(2)/HF/528/2010.—Under clause (e) of Section 2 of
the Tamil Nadu Anatomy Act, 1951 (Tamil Nadu Act,
XVIII of 1951), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby declares
the Sathyabama University Dental College and Hospital,
Chennai, to be a Teaching Medical Institution for the purposes
of that Act.
V.K. SUBBURAJ,
Principal Secretary to Government.

NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu
Highways Act, 2001 (TN Act 34/2002), the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the land specified in the schedule
below measuring 1509 Square Metres to the same, a little
more or less required for the purpose for widening of
Thirumazhisai Sathiyavedu Road, from two lane to four lane
Carriage way.
The plan of the land under acquisition is kept in the Office
of the Special Deputy Collector (L.A.) Tamil Nadu Urban
Development Project-III, Chennai at Poonamallee and may
be inspected at any time during office hours.

No.II(2)/RDPR/539/2010.—Whereas, the opinion of the
Tamil Nadu State Election Commission has been sought for,
regarding the disqualification of Thiru R. Selvin, 3rd Ward
Member, Ezhuvaraimukki Village Panchayat, Sattankulam
Panchayat Union, Thoothukudi District as required under sub-
section (2) of Section 41 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act,
1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994) as he has absented
himself from the three consecutive meetings of the said Village
Panchayat.
And, Whereas the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission,
has stated that a Notice No.Na.Ka. 1239/2010-2/à.«î.1, dated
04-03-2010 sent to Thiru. R. Selvin, 3rd Ward Member,
Ezhuvaraimukki Village Panchayat, Sattankulam Panchayat
Union, Thoothukudi District by the Commission was returned
with the postal endorsement as “addressee left without any
instructions”.  Besides, his wife Tmt. K. Ezhilarasi has given
a written statement that her  husband has not attended the
meeting since he is in foreign country.  It is, therefore, proved
that he is living in a foreign country and on verification of the
attendance register and records of Ezhuvaraimukki Village
Panchayat, 
it 
has 
been 
confirmed 
that, 
Thiru
R. Selvin, 3rd Ward Member of the said Village Panchayat
has not attended more than three consecutive meetings of
the said Village Panchayat  after the meeting held in the
month of June 2008 and thereby opined that the said Thiru
R. Selvin, 3rd Ward Member, Ezhuvaraimukki Village
Panchayat, Sattankulam Panchayat Union, Thoothukudi District
is disqualified to continue as Ward Member of the said
Ezhuvaraimukki Village Panchayat, under clause (i) of sub-
section (3) of section 38 of the said Act;
Now, therefore, under sub-section (1) of Section 41
of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu
Act 21 of 1994), the Government of Tamil Nadu hereby
dicides that Thiru R. Selvin, 3rd Ward Member, Ezhuvaraimukki
Village Panchayat, Sattankulam Panchayat Union, Thoothukudi
District, has become disqualified from holding the office of
3rd Ward Member of the Ezhuvaraimukki Village Panchayat,
under clause (i) of sub-section (3) of Section 38 of the said
Act as he has absented himself from three consecutive
meetings of the said Village Panchayat after the meeting held
