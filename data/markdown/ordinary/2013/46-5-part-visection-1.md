Final Closing and Cancellation of Registration of IND No. X1291 the Watrap Small Match Producers Service
Industrial Co-operative Society Limited
(L.F. 994/ICA/1999.)
No. VI(1)/435/2013.
“In the Circumstances stated by the Assistant Director of Industries and Commerce (Industrial Co-operatives) / Official
Liquidator of the Watrap Small Match Producers Service Industrial Co-operative Society Limited., IND No. X1291 in his final
closure proposals dated 23-05-2013 and in exercise of the powers delegated under Section 140(1) of
Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act 1983, the registration of the Watrap Small Match Producers Service Industrial
Co-operative Society Limited., IND No. X1291 has been ordered to be cancelled and the affairs have been finally closed
with effect from 31-10-2013 vide the Principal Secretary / Industries Commissioner and Director of Industries and Commerce/
Registrar of Industrial Co-operatives Procds. No. LF.994/ICA/1999, dated 31st  October 2013.

dated 30-10-2013.”

TAICO Bank is appointed as Official Liquidator of the said society for the purpose of liquidation.

Confirmation of Variation to the approved Periyathottam Detailed Development Plan No. 2, of
Kancheepuram Local Planning Area.
(Roc. No. 23530/2010/DPI.)
No. VI(1)/438/2013.
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 33(2) of Town and Country Planning Act 1971.
The Commissioner of Town and Country Planning, Chennai hereby confirms the following variation to the approved
Periyathottam Detailed Development Plan No.2, Kancheepuram Local Planning area, approved by the Special Commissioner
of Town and Country Planning in proceeding Roc. No. 21100/2000/DP2, dated 9-8-2001 and the fact of this approval
in Form No. 12 published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No.44, Part VI, Section-1, dated 21-11-2001, publication
No. VI(1)/684/2001 and the said draft notification was published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No.4 Part-VI,
Section-1, Page No.20, dated 30-01-2008, Publication No VI(1)/16/2008.
2. Since no objections and suggestions have been received on this draft notification within the stipulated time, the
same are hereby confirmed and ordered as below.
CONFIRMATION OF VARIATION
1. The entire Periyathottam Detailed Development Plan No.2 of Kancheepuram Local Planning Area, approved by
the Special Commissioner of Town and Country Planning in Proceedings Roc. No. 21100/2000/DP2, Dated: 09-08-2001
is varied by Periyathottam Detailed Development Plan No.2, Kancheepuram Local Planning Area.
2. The Plan thus varied is numbered as Map No.5,  DDP(V)/DTCP No.2/2008.
3. The Detailed Development Plan clauses and schedules annexed with this plan are made enforceable from the
date of publication of this notification in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
Chennai-600 002,
A. KARTHIK,
5th November 2013.
Commissioner of Town and Country Planning.
Variations to the Approved Master Plan for the Coimbatore Local Planning Area.
(Roc. No. 6678/2012/LPA-2.)
No. VI(1)/439/2013.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-Section (4) of section 32 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning
Act 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972) and in exercise of powers conferred by the G.O.Ms.No.94 Housing and Urban
Development (UD4(1) Department dated 12-06-2009 which has been published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette
No.27 Part II Section 2, page No. 228, dated 15-07-2009 the following variations are made to the master plan
for the Coimbatore Local Planning Area approved under the said Act and published in the Housing and Urban Development
Department Notification No.ll(2)1/HOU/4377/94 at page 1078 of Part II section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette
dated the 9th November 1994.
VARIATIONS
In the said Master Plan, in the “LAND USE SCHEDULE” under the heading “COIMBATORE LOCAL PLANNING AREA
(OTHER THAN COIMBATORE CORPORATION AREA) under the sub-heading (b) Non Notified D.D.Plan Area in (c)
Palladam Taluk, 77, Neelambur Village.
(i) against the entry, “PUBLIC AND SEMI PUBLIC” for the expression “NIL the expression 532/5A, 5B1" shall be
inserted and
(ii). against the entry “INDUSTRIAL (I 30)” for the expression” 530 to 536" the expressiorn "530 to 536
(except 532/5A,5B1)" shall be substituted.
Coimbatore,
S. CHANDRASEKARAN,
22nd November 2013.
Member-Secretary (In-Charge)
