Judicial Notification
The High Court of Judicature at Madras
Amendment to Civil Rules of Practice and Circular Order
(R.O.C. No. 444/2007/F1)
No. SRO C-22/2021.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 122 of the Code of Civil Procedure 1908 (Central Act V of 
1908) and all other powers hereunto enabling and with the previous approval of the Government of Tamil Nadu, the 
High Court of Madras, hereby makes the following amendment to Rule 18-A of the Civil Rules of Practice and Circular Orders 
Volume I, Edition 1941.
Amendment
In the Civil Rules of Practice and Circular Orders, in Volume I, in Part I, Chapter I, in Rule 18-A, sub-rule (5) 
shall be omitted.
High Court, Madras,	
	
P. Dhanabal,
13th July 2021.
