been Satisfied that the lands specified in the
schedule below have to be acquired for industrial

PROJECT, Cheyyur and it has already been decided that
the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the
lands is to be paid out of the funds controlled or managed
by the Officer Authorised Signatory, Coastal Tamil Nadu
Power Limited, New Delhi and having considered the cause
shown by the owners or other persons having interest the
said lands as the case may be do hereby published      the
following Notice under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the
Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes
Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act, 10 of 1999),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands
specified in the Schedule  below and measuring an extent
of 2.90.0 hectares to the same, a little more or less are
[ 1 ]
needed for Industrial Purpose, to wit, for the formation of ULTRA
MEGA POWER PROJET, 54. CHITHARKADU VILLAGE,
CHEYYUR TALUK, Kancheepuram District.
The plan of the lands are kept in the Office of the Special
Tahsildar Land Acquisition, Unit-I,ULTRA MEGA POWER
PROJECT Scheme, Cheyyur and may be inspected at any
time during office hours.
