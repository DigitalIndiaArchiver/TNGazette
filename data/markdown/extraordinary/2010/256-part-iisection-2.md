The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that
the land specified in the Schedule below have to be acquired
for Industrial purpose, to wit, for Second Mine Expansion Scheme
of  Neyveli Lignite Corporation Limited by NLC and it having
already been decided that the entire amount of compensation
to be awarded for the land is to be paid out of the funds
controlled or managed by Neyveli Lignite Corporation Limited,
the following Notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section
3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes
Act, 1997:
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby acquires the land specified in the schedule below and
measuring 0.79.0 hectare, be the same, a little more or less,
needed for industrial purposes, to wit, for Second Mine
Expansion Scheme of  Neyveli Lignite Corporation
Limited by NLC.
The plan of the land is kept in the Office of the Special
Deputy Collector (Land Acquisition), Neyveli -2 and may be
inspected at any time during office hours.
