JUDICIAL NOTIFICATION
Amendment to Rules framed under Section 34(1) of the Advocates Act
Errata to Notification
(R.o.c.No. 5645/2009/F1)
In the 'Amendment to rules framed under Section 34(1) of the Advocates Act' Published as Judicial
Notification No. SRO C-12/2016 published Pages 457 and 458 of Part III—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu
Government Gazette Issue No. 21, dated 25th May 2016 the following errata shall be carried out into
appropriate provisions as read hereunder:
ERRATA
"The sub rule numbered as vii to xii in Rule 14-A to be corrected
and read as (i) to (vi)
and
The sub rule numbered as iv to vi in Rule 14-B to be corrected and
read as (i) to (iii)."
High Court, Madras,
S. BASKARAN,
