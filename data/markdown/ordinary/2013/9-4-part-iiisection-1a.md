Amendments to the Tamil Nadu Document Writers' Licence Rules

No. SRO A-2/2013.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 89-B of the  Registration Act, 1908 (Central Act
XVI of 1908), the Governor of  Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Tamil  Nadu Document Writers'
Licence Rules, 1982, as subsequently amended.
2. The amendments hereby made shall come into force on the 15th day of February 2013.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules, in rule 7, in sub-rule (2),
(1) for the first proviso, the following proviso shall be substituted namely:-
“Provided that in the case of a person who had obtained a licence under the Tamil Nadu Copy Writers’ Licence
Rules, 1971, before 15-2-2013, whether renewed or not should have passed S.S.L.C. examination or its equivalent
examination.” ; and
(2) for the third proviso, the following proviso shall be substituted, namely:-
“Provided also that persons who apply for the Document Writers’ Licence Test should have taken Tamil as a
subject, either as first language or second language, in the S.S.L.C. examination or its equivalent examination.”
SUNIL PALIWAL,
Secretary to Government.
