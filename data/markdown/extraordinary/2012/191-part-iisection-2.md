TRANSPORT DEPARTMENT
ACQUISITION OF LANDS
[G.O. Ms. No. 125, Transport (I.1), 9th July 2012.]
No. II(2)/TRA/368(b)/2012.
Whereas in exercise of the powers conferred by
clause (1) of the article 258 of the Constitution of India, the
President of India has, in the Ministry of Food, Agriculture,
Community Development and Co-operation (Department of
Agriculture) Notification No.4(1)/65, General-II, dated the
23rd April 1966 entrusted to the Government of Tamil Nadu
with their consent, the function of the Central Government
under the Land Acquisition Act, 1894 (Central Act I of 1894)
in relation to Acquisition of Lands for the purpose Union in
this State.
And, whereas it has already been decided that the
Compensation should be paid from the railways.
Now, therefore in exercise of the powers conferred by
Section 6 of the said Act, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
declares that the Lands specified in the Schedule below
and measuring 12949 square metres Dry lands be the
same, a little more or less, or needed for a public purpose,
to wit for the formation of Tiruvallur to Arakkonam 4th Railway
line.
 A plan of the Lands are kept in the Office of the Sub
Collector's  Office, Ranipet  and may be inspected at any
time during the office hours.
