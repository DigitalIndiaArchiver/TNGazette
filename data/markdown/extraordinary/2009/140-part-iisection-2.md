MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION AND WATER SUPPLY DEPARTMENT
DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF THE TAMIL NADU MUNICIPAL LAWS
(FIFTH AMENDMENT) ACT, 2008.
[G.O. Ms. No. 94, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (Election), 9th June 2009.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/273(d)/2009.
 In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Municipal Laws
(Fifth Amendment) Act, 2008 (Tamil Nadu Act 37 of 2008), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the
first day of September 2009 as the date on which the said Act shall come into force.
NIRANJAN MARDI,
 Secretary to Government.
