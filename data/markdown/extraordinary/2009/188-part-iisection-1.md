NOTIFICATION
[G.O. Ms. No. 127, Public Works (N1), 27th July 2009.]
The Mettur Dam will be  opened on 28th July 2009  at 6-00 P.M. for Cauvery Delta Irrigation during the irrigation
year 2009-2010.  Under any circumstances  extension of supply beyond the prescribed  date will not be allowed.
2. If necessary turn system  will be introduced. The ryots are requested to use the water economically and co-operate
with  Water Resources Department officials in proper distribution and efficient utilization of available water.
