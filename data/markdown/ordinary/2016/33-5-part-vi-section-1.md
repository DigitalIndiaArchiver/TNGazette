Act 2 of 1974), the High Court hereby appoints the above said Nine I.A.S. Probationers of 2015 batch to be the

and confers upon them the ordinary powers conferrable under this Code on a Magistrate to try such cases which were
earlier triable and dealt with by an erstwhile Judicial Second Class Magistrate.
