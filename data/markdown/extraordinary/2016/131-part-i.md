ORDER:  No. 528, Public (Special-B), 28th May 2016.
Under Clause (3) of Article 166 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that the
subject "Wakf Board" hitherto allocated to Tmt. S. Valarmathi, Minister for Backward Classes & Minorities Welfare be
allocated to Dr. Nilofer kafeel and she will continue to be designated as Minister for Labour.
