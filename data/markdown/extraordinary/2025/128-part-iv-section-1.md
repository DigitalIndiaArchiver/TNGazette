 STATEMENT OF OBJECTS AND REASONS .
This Bill is introduced in pursucance of Article 206 of the Constitution, to provide for the withdrawal from, and out 
of, the Consolidated Fund of the State, of certain sums being required to meet--
(a)	
the grants made in advance by the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly in respect of estimated 
expenditure of the State for a part of the financial year commencing on the 1st day of April 2025; and 
(b)	
the expenditure charged on the said Fund for the said part of that year, and for the appropriation 
of the said sums.
