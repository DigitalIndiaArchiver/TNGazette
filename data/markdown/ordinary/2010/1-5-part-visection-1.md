GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
Final Closing and Cancellation of Registration of certain Industrial Co-operative Societies.
Ind. No. 775. The Madurai Auto Servicing Industrial Co-operative Society Limited.
(L.F. 186/ICA/82.)
No. VI(1)/1/2010.
In the circumstances stated by the Assistant Director of Industries and Commerce (Industrial Co-operatives)/Official
Liquidator of the Madurai Auto Servicing Industrial Co-operative Society Limited in his final closure proposals dated
8th December 2009 and in exercise of the powers delegated under Section 140(1) of Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies
Act, 1983, the registration of the Madurai Auto Servicing Industrial Co-operatrive Society Limited, Ind. No.775. is hereby
cancelled and the affairs are finally colsed with effect from 17th December 2009.
Ind. No. 726. The SICO Small Match Producers Service Industrial Co-operative Society Limited.
(L.F. 769/ICA/89.)
No. VI(1)/2/2010.
In the circumstances stated by the Assistant Director of Industries and Commerce (Industrial
Co-operatives)/Official Liquidator of the SICO Small Match Producers Service Industrial Co-operative Society Limited in
his final closure proposals dated 9th December 2009 and in exercise of the powers delegated under Section 140(1)
of Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act, 1983, the registration of the SICO Small Match Producers Service Industrial
Co-operative Society Limited, Ind. No. 726 is hereby cancelled and the affairs are finally closed with effect from
17th December 2009.
Ind. No. 761. The Pandian Braided Cord Manufacturers Industrial Co-operative
Society Limited.
(L.F. 861/ICA/92)
No. VI(1)/3/2010.
In the circumstances stated by the Assistant Director of Industries and Commerce (Industrial Co-operatives)/Official
Liquidator of the Pandian Braided Cord Manufactures Industrial Co-operative Society Limited in his final closure proposals
dated 8th December 2009 and in exercise of the powers delegated under Section 140(1) of Tamil Nadu Co-operative
Societies Act, 1983, the registration of the  Pandian Braided Cord Manufacturers Industrial Co-operative Society Limited
Ind. No. 761 is hereby cancelled and the affairs are finally Closed with effect from 17th December 2009.
Chepauk,
G. SUNDARAMURTHI,
Chennai-5,
Industries Commissioner and
17th December 2009.
