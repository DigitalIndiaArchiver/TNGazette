GENERAL  NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI.
Appointment of Executive Officer for the Wakf Estate of A.M. Bhaimia, Chennai.
[Rc. No. 10947/99/B9/Chennai.]
No. VI-3(a)/144/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions of Section 25 of the Wakf Act, 1995, and  in compliance
of the orders of the Hon'ble High Court, Madras in C.S. 640/2000 the Chief Executive Officer, Tamil Nadu Wakf Board
in Proceedings No. 10947/99/B9/Chennai, dated 20-07-2012 has appointed the Wakf Inspector, Chennai (North) as the
Executive Officer of The Wakf Estate of A.M. Bhaimia, Chennai to look after the affairs of the Wakf for a Period of
one year from the date of the order. Accordingly the Wakf Inspector, Chennai (North) / Executive Officer has taken
over the charges of the Wakf and its properties on the Fore Noon of 30-07-2012.
Chennai-600 001,
F. ABDUL RAZICK,
28th September 2012.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
