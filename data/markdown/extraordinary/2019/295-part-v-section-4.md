NOTIFICATIONS BY THE ELECTION COMMISSION OF INDIA
APPOINTMENT OF DATES FOR VARIOUS STAGES OF ELECTION AND FIXATION OF HOURS OF 
POLL FOR 8. VELLORE PARLIAMENTARY CONSTITUENCY IN THE STATE OF TAMIL NADU
No. SRO G-49/2019.
The following Notiﬁ cation of the Election Commission of India, Nirvachan Sadan, Ashoka Road, 
New Delhi-110 001, dated 11th July, 2019 [20 Ashadha, 1941 (Saka)] is republished:—
No. 464/EPS/2019(2):-WHEREAS, the President of India had, by Notification issued under 
sub-section (2) of Section 14 of the Representation of the People Act, 1951 (43 of 1951) and published in 
the Gazette of India on the 19th March, 2019 (Tuesday), been pleased to call upon 8-Vellore Parliamentary 
Constituency of Tamil Nadu, alongwith other Parliamentary Constituencies  mentioned therein to elect members 
to the House of People in accordance with the provision of the said Act and of the rules and orders made 
thereunder; and 
2. In pursuance of Sections 30 and 56 of the said Act, the Election Commission of India had vide notiﬁ cation 
No. 464/EPS/2019(2), dated 19th March 2019, appointed the dates for the schedule of election from the said 
8-Vellore Parliamentary Constituency; and
3. Whereas, in pursuance of the Commission’s Proceedings No.464/ECI/LET/TERR/TN/SS-I/2019, 
dated 16th April 2019, the President of India rescinded the election from said 8-Vellore Parliamentary Constituency 
of Tamil Nadu;
4. Whereas, the President of India vide further notiﬁ cation issued under sub-section (2) of Section 
14 of the Representation of the People Act  has been pleased to call upon 8-Vellore Parliamentary Constituency 
of Tamil Nadu, to elect member to the House of People in accordance with the provisions of said Act, as rule 
and order made therein. 
5. 
Now, therefore, in pursuance of Sections 30 and 56 of the said Act, the Election Commission of India 
hereby-


2
TAMIL NADU GOVERNMENT GAZETTE   EXTRAORDINARY
 
(A) Appoints, with respect to the said election in 8-Vellore Parliamentary Constituency of Tamil Nadu;
 
 
(a) the 18th July, 2019 (Thursday), as the last date for making nominations;
 
 
(b) the 19th July, 2019 (Friday), as the date for Scrutiny of Nominations;
 
 
(c) the 22nd July, 2019 (Monday), as the last date for the withdrawal of candidatures;
  
 
(d) the 5th August, 2019 (Monday), as the date on which a poll shall, if necessary, be taken;
 
 
(e) the 11th August, 2019 (Sunday), as the date before which the election shall be completed; and 
 
(B) Fixes the hours from 7:00 A.M. to 6:00 P.M., as the hours during which the poll shall, if necessary, 
be taken on the date speciﬁ ed above, for the election.
(By Order)
