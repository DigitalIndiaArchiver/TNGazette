Amendments to the Special Rules  for the Tamil Nadu Engineering Subordinate Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 11, Public Works (C-2), 20th January 2009,  Thai 7, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. SRO B-9/2009.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Engineering
Subordinate Service (Section 8 in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Service Manual, 1970).
2.
The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 16th February, 1979.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules,—
(1) In PART I,—
(a) for the expression “Branch X—Mechanical”, the expression “Branch XI—Mechanical” shall be substituted;
(b) In rule 2, in clause (a), under the second proviso, for the expression “Branch X—Mechanical Branch”—
“the expression “Branch XI—Mechanical Branch” shall be substituted;
(2) In PART II, for the expression “Branch X—Mechanical Branch”, the expression “Branch XI—Mechanical Branch”
shall be substituted.
Amendments to the Special Rules  for the Tamil Nadu Engineering Subordinate Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 12, Public Works (C-2), 20th January 2009.]
No. SRO B-10/2009.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India
and in supersession of the adhoc rules published with the Public Works Department Notification No.SROB-201/80 published
at pages 212-224 of Part III—Section 1(b) of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 16th April 1980, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Engineering
Subordinate Service (Section 8 in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1970).
2. The amendments hereby made shall come into force on and from the 2nd August 1984.

(a) the expression “BRANCH VI—Provincialised Work charged Establishment Branch” shall be omitted; and
(b) after the expression “BRANCH XI—Mechanical Branch”, the following expression shall be added, namely;—
“BRANCH XII Miscellaneous Branch”,
2 in PART II,—
(a) the heading “BRANCH VI” and the entries relating thereunder shall be omitted; and

3. Appointing Authority.—The appointing authority for all the categories shall be the Executive Engineer of the Division
concerned.
4. Qualification.—(a) Age.—No person shall be eligible for appointment to any category by direct recruitment, if he has
completed or will complete the age of thirty years on the first day of July of the year in which the selection for
appointment is made.
(b) Other Qualification.—No person shall be eligible for appointment to the categories specified in column (1) of
the Annexure II by the method of recruitment specified in column (2), unless he possesses the qualifications specified
in the corresponding entries in column (3) thereof.

5. Rules of Reservation.—The rule of reservation of appointment (General Rule 22) shall apply for appointment to all
categories by direct recruitment.
6. Probation.—(a)
Every person appointed to any category by direct recruitment, except to the categories of
Gardener, Watchman, Sweeper-cum-Scavenger and Helper, shall be on probation for a total period of two years on duty
within a continuous period of three years.
    (b) Every person appointed to the category of Gardener, Watchman, Sweeper-cum-Scavenger and Helper by
direct recruitment shall be on probation for a period of one year on duty within a continuous period of two years.
7. Savings.—Nothing contained in these rules shall adversely affect the existing incumbents as on date of issue of
these Rules.
