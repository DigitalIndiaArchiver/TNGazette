HOME DEPARTMENT
 Amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Special Police Subordinate Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 39, Home (Police-IX), 13th January 2015.]
No. SRO B-12/2015.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Sections 8 and 10 of the Tamil Nadu District Police Act, 1859
(Central Act XXIV of 1859) and Sections 9 and 11 of the Chennai City Police Act, 1888 (Tamil Nadu Act 3 of 1888), read with
the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India and all other powers hereunto enabling the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby makes the following Amendments to the Tamil Nadu Special Police Subordinate Service Rules, 1978:—
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules,-
(1) in rule 7, in sub-rule (a), in the TABLE, in the second column,-
(a) against the entry “2 (a) Sub-Inspectors” in the first column, in the fifth proviso, for the expression “dependents
of police personnel including ministerial staff of Police Department”, the expression “general pool” shall be substituted;
(b) against the entry “6(a) Constables (Other than those mentioned in (b) to (h) below)” in the first column, in
the third proviso, for the expression “dependents of serving Police Personnel including Ministerial Staff of the Police
Department”, the expression “the general pool” shall be substituted;
(c) in sub-rule (aaa), for the expression beginning with “shall be made by the selection committee” and ending
with “shall be interviewed by the Selection Committee.”, the following expression shall be substituted, namely:—
“shall be made by the Tamil Nadu Uniformed Services Recruitment Board in accordance with rule 22 of Part-II
General Rules of the Tamil Nadu State and Subordinate Services Rules.”; and
(2) in rule 14, in sub-rule (f), in the Table, in column (2), against the entry, “Category 6 Constables” in column 1,
in clause (2), in sub-clause (ii), the first, second and the thrid provisos shall be omitted.
APURVA VARMA,
Principal Secretary to Government.

SCHOOL EDUCATION  DEPARTMENT
 Amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu General Subordinate Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 14, School Education [S.E.4(2)], 19th January 2015, Thai 5, Jaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2046.]
No. SRO B-14/2015.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu General
Subordinate Service (Section 13 in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1970).
2. The Amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 19th January 2015.
AMENDMENT
In the said Special Rules:-
in Part-I and Part-II, under “Class LXXII—School Education Department”, for the expression “Laboratory Assistants
