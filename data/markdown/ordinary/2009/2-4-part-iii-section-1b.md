HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE  DEPARTMENT
Amendments to Special Rules  for Tamil Nadu Public Health Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 429, Health and Family Welfare (L2),23rd December 2008,  Margazhi 8, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2039.]
No. SRO B-1/2009.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for Tamil Nadu Public Health
Service (Section 24, in Volume - II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1969) :—
The Amendments hereby made shall come into force with effect on and from 23rd December 2008.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules,—
(1) In rule, under class I, under the heading “Category” for the category 7, the following shall be substituted:—
“7-Senior Entomologist / District Malaria Officer / District Entomologist / Lecturer in Medical Entomology /
Entomologist / Biologist”.
(2) “The expression 'Senior Entomologist' wherever it occurs, shall be read as follows:—
"Senior Entomologist / District Malaria Officer / District Entomologist / Lecturer in Medical Entomology / Entomologist /
Biologist."
(3) In the Table under Rule 4, for the entry in item (1) in column (3) against the entry “Senior Entomologist”
in column (1) “a) Recruitment by transfer” in column (2) thereof, the following entry shall be substituted:—
