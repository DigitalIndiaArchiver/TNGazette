No. SRO B-11/2013.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for Class XLIII of the Tamil Nadu
General Subordinate Service (Section 13 under Part-II of the Service Rules in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Services
Manual, 1969).
2. The amendments hereby made shall come into force on and from the date of issue of this Notification.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules, in Part-II, in Class XXIII,
1. In Rule 2 under the heading Statistical Inspectors, the existing entries and the Provisos and Explanation thereunder
shall be substituted with the following:-
Statistical Inspectors:
By Promotion from the category of Assistant Statistical Investigator.
2. In Rule 4, order in the Table,
(i)
Against the Category “Statistical Inspector” in column (1), the entries ”Direct Recruitment” in column
(2) and qualifications for Direct Recruitment in column (3) shall be deleted.
(ii)
For the entry in column (3) against the Category “Statistical Inspector” in column (1) the word “or
recruitment by transfer” occurring in column (2) shall be deleted.
(iii)
for the entry in column (3), against the Category “Statistical Inspector” in column (1) and “Promotion” in
column (2) thereon, the following entry shall be substituted, namely :-
(a)
Degree in Statistics;
OR
Degree in Mathematics/Computer Science/Economics/ Computer Applications with Statistics
as an ancillary/allied subject;
OR
Any degree with Post graduate degree in Operations Research/Econometrics/Mathematical
Economics.
(b)
Must have put in a service of not less than five years in the category of Assistant Statistical
Investigator.
Provided that the qualifications specified above shall not be insisted upon in respect of
existing incumbents in the post of Assistant Statistical Investigator as on the date of issue of
this notification:
Provided further, that field experience of three years, in districts other than Chennai in field-
level collection of statistical data in the post of Assistant Statistical Investigator in any of the
schemes in the District shall be necessary prerequisite for which no relaxation shall be
considered under any circumstances whatsoever.
(iv)
for the entry in column (3), against the category “Assistant Statistical Investigator” in column (1) and
“Direct recruitment” in column (2) thereon, the following entry shall be substituted, namely:-

(a)
Degree in Statistics;
OR
Degree in Mathematics/Computer Science/Economics/Computer Applications with Statistics as an
ancillary/allied subject;
OR
Any degree with Post graduate degree in Operations Research/Econometrics/Mathematical
Economics.
Provided the candidates with the above degrees other than Statistics are expected to be fully
conversant with basic level concepts of Statistics including Bayes’ theorem in Probability,
Probability distributions like Binomial, Poisson, Normal, t, Chi-Square & F, Probability Generating
Functions, Moment Generating Function, Theory of Estimation, Testing of Hypothesis using
various Statistical Tests like Z, t, F, Chi-Square, basics of Multiple Regression and Multivariate
analysis and Sampling design. In addition, they should have basic skills in using MS-Excel
for statistical data analysis.
(b)
Must not complete or must not have completed 30 years of - age on the 151 day of July of
the year in which the selection for appointment is made.
(v)
for the entry in column (3), against the category “Assistant Statistical Investigator” in column (1) and
“Recruitment by transfer” in column (2) thereon, the following entry shall be substituted, namely:-—
(a)
Degree in Statistics;
OR
Degree in Mathematics/Computer Science/Economics/Computer Applications with Statistics as
an ancillary/ allied subject;
OR
Any degree with Post graduate degree in Operations Research/Econometrics/Mathematical
Economics.
(b)
Must have put in a service of not less than three years in the category of Junior Assistant or
Typist as the case may be.
(c)
Must have passed the Departmental Test on Statistics.
