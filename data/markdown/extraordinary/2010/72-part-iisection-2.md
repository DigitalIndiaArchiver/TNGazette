NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
DTP—II-2 Ex. (72)
TRANSPORT DEPARTMENT
ACQUISITION OF LANDS
[G.O. Ms. No. 104, Transport (I-1), 12th March 2010.]
No. II(2)/TRA/150(e)/2010.
Whereas in exercise of the powers conferred by clause (1)
of  Article 258 of the Constitution of India, the President  has in
the Ministry of Food, Agriculture, Community Development and
Co-operation (Department of Agriculture) Notification
No. 4(1)/65, General-II, dated 23rd April 1966, entrusted to the
Government of Tamil Nadu with their consent, the functions of
the Union Government under the Land Acquisition Act, 1894
(Central Act I of 1894), in relation to the Acquisition of Lands for
the purpose of the Union in this State and whereas, it has been
decided that the compensation shall be paid from the public
revenue.
Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by
Section 6 of the said Act, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
declares that the land specified in the Schedule below and
measuring 0.43.5 hectare of the same, a little more or less
needed for a public purpose to wit, for the Doubling of Railway
Track between Irgur and Coimbatore Junction and under
Sections 3 and 7 of the same Act, the Revenue Divisional
Officer, Coimbatore is appointed to perform the functions of the
Collector under the Act and directed to take order for the
acquisition of the said land under sub-sections (1) and (2) of
Section 17 of the Act, the Government of Tamil Nadu further
directs that the possession of the said land may be taken on the
expiry of 15 days from the date of publication of the notice
mentioned in Section 9(1) of the Act.
A plan of the land is kept in this Office of the Revenue
Divisional Officer, Coimbatore and it may be inspected at any
time during the office hours.
SCHEDULE
Coimbatore District, Sulur Taluk, No. 14. Irugur Village.
Government, dry, S.F. No. 355, belongs to Ranipadmavathy,
Raveendran, Ragupathiraja and Bhagavan, bounded on the  north
by S.F. No. 354, east and south by S.F. No. 368  and west by
S.F. No. 356—0.13.0 hectare.
Government, dry, S.F. No. 356-4B, belongs to K. Sivashankar,
bounded on the  north by S.F. No. 356-2B, east by S.F. No. 355,
south by S.F. No. 368  and west by S.F. Nos. 356-3 and
356-4—0.30.5 hectare.
Total—0.43.5 hectare.
ATULYA MISRA,
