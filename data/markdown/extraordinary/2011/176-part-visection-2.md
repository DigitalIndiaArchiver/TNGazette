3. Whereas, the District Election Officer/District Collector, Tiruchirappalli District, in his letter/s fourth read above reported
that Tmt. G. Senthamarai contested in the said Casual Election for the office of Village Panchayat President of Gundur Village
Panchayat of Thiruverumbur Panchayat Union, Tiruchirappalli District, failed to lodge copies of her account of election expenses
as directed by this Commission;
4. Whereas, based on the report of the said District Election Officer/District Collector, a show cause notice was issued in
the reference fifth read above to the said contested candidate who failed to lodge a true copy of the account of election expenses
in the Casual Election held during the month of January 2011, through the District Election Officer/District Collector, Tiruchirappalli
District calling for explanation within 20 days of the receipt of the  said Notice, as to why action should not be taken against her
as per sub-section (4) of Section 37 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994);
5. Whereas, the said District Election Officer/District Collector, Tiruchirappalli District in his letter sixth read above, has now
reported that Tmt. G. Senthamarai has neither submitted explanations nor lodged the account of election expenses inspite of
the  Show Cause Notice issued to her; and
6. Whereas, in view of the said report of the District Election Officer/District Collector, Tiruchirappalli District, the
Tamil Nadu State Election Commission is satisfied that the said candidate Tmt. G. Senthamarai, wife of Ganesan, Nadutheru,
Burma Colony, Gundur, Tiruchirappalli District, has failed to lodge an account  of election expenses as required by or under the
said Act and Rules and have no good reason or justification for such failure.
7. Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 37 of the Tamil Nadu
Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994), the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission hereby declares that the
candidate Tmt. G. Senthamarai, wife of Ganesan, Nadutheru, Burma Colony, Gundur, Tiruchirappalli District shall be disqualified,
for being elected for a period of three years from the date of this order
(By Order of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission)
