HOME DEPARTMENT
Draft Amendment to Tamil Nadu Motor Vehicles Rules, 1989.
[G.O. Ms. No. 554, Home (Tr. VII), 13th July 2009, ÝQ 29, F¼õœÀõ˜ Ý‡´-2040.]
No. SRO A-21/2009.—The following draft of an amendment to the Tamil Nadu Motor Vehicle Rules, 1989, which it
is proposed to make in exercise of the powers conferred by Section 95 of the Motor Vehicle Act, 1988 (Central Act 59
of 1988) is hereby published for the information of all persons likely to be affected thereby, as required by sub-section
(1) of Section 212 of the said Act.
2.
Notice is hereby given that the draft amendment will be taken into consideration on or after the expiry of thirty
days from the date of publication of this Notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette and that any objection or
suggestion, which may be received from any person with respect thereto before the expiry of the aforesaid period will
be considered by the Government of Tamil Nadu.
3.
Objection or suggestion, if any, should be addressed in duplicate to the Principal Secretary to Government,
Home Department, Chennai-600 009.
DRAFT AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in rule 3, in clause (i), for the expression “120 kilometres”, the expression “80 kilometres” shall be
substituted.
EXPLANATORY NOTE
At present the minimum route length for Stage Carriage for “Express Service” is prescribed as 120 kms. which is a
drawback for providing “Express Service” by State Transport Corporations undertaking to all moffussil routes. In order to
reduce the journey time considering the interest of travelling public and to cover all moffussil routes it is essential to
reduce the minimum route length for Express Services as 80 kms. The proposed draft amendment will ensure lesser
travel time to public and cover all moffussil routes.
S. MALATHI,
Principal Secretary to Government.
HOME, PROHIBITION AND EXCISE DEPARTMENT
Amendment to Tamil Nadu Liquor (Supply by Wholesale) Rules, 1983.
[G.O. Ms. No. 38, Home, Prohibition and Excise (VIII) 20th July 2009.]
No. SRO A-22/2009.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Sections 17-D and 54 of the Tamil Nadu Prohibition
Act, 1937 (Tamil Nadu Act X of 1937), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the
Tamil Nadu Liquor (Supply by Wholesale) Rules, 1983.
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st April 2008.
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in rule 15, in sub-rule (3), in item (i), for the expression “66.60", the expression “69.72” shall be
substituted.
S. MALATHI,
Principal Secretary to Government.
