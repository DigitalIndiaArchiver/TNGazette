(a) The Elections will be held in accordance with rules and procedures  stipulated for the conduct of Election 
to the University authorities as per the Statutes of this University.
	
(b) Nominations / declarations should be made in the prescribed form which  may be obtained from the 
Registrar (Returning Officer) / downloaded from this University website. 
	
Nomination papers shall be enclosed in an envelope superscribing the above elections (each election separately) 
and sent by Registered Post (with Acknowledgment due), so as to be received by the Returning Officer/Registrar, 
Manonmaniam Sundaranar University, Abishekapatti, Tirunelveli, within the date and hour fixed or delivered either in 
person or by messenger to the Returning Officer within the last date and hour fixed. Nomination papers that are not 
enclosed in an envelope, superscribed and sent by Registered Post or are not delivered in person or by messenger 
as required above shall be declared invalid. 
	
(c) An elector shall be eligible to subscribe either as proposer or as seconder in nomination papers, only as 
many times as there are vacancies but not more. 
	
(d) The following is the programme for the Election:
