conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 125 of the

the date of publication of this notification in the Tamil Nadu 
Government Gazette, the land situated within the boundaries 
of the T.Rajagopalanpatti Panchayat, Thimmarasanaikanur 
Bitt-II Village, Andipatti Taluk, Theni District, with the total 
extent as mentioned in the Schedule be the same or a 
little more or less, shall be excluded from the operation 
of the  said Act.
