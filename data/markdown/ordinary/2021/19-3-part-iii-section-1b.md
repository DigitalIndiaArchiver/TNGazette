No. SRO B-22/2021.— In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, 
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Public Health 
Service (Section 24 in Volume II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 2016.)
2. The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on and from 22nd October, 2018.
Amendments
In the said Special Rules,-
(1)	
In rule-1, under the heading "Class I", for Category 6, the following Categories shall be inserted, namely:-
"6. Chief Entomologist
