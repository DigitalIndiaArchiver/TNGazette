In the Government order ﬁ rst read above, among others, orders have been issued according Administrative 
approval for the implementation of "Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana" at an outlay of Rs.522.70 crore in all the Districts of 
Tamil Nadu except Chennai during 2017-2018 as per the Administrative approval and operational guidelines of "Pradhan 
Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana".
2. 
In the Government Order second read above, the Government have notiﬁ ed the Scale of Finance for the year 
2017-2018 as sum insured for the crops notiﬁ ed during 2017-2018 under "Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana".
3. 
In the Government Order fourth read above, the Government have notiﬁ ed the Crops, Districts, Firkas, Revenue 
Villages, Insurance Units, Approved Premium Rates for Agriculture and Horticulture Crops and Scale of Finance for Agriculture 
and Horticulture Crops including Sugarcane for effective implementation of the Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana during Rabi 
2017-2018 season.
4. 
In this letter dated 7-3-2018, the Director of Agriculture has stated that the 39th State Level Co-ordination Committee 
on Crop  Insurance (SLCCCI) meeting was conducted on 13-10-2017 under the Chairmanship of Agricultural Production 
Commissioner and Principal Secretary, Agriculture Department to notify the Agricultural and Horticultural Crops during Rabi 
2017-2018 under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana. In the minutes of the meeting, it has been informed that the Committee 
has ﬁ nalized the Insurance Units and Approved Premium Rates and accepted for notiﬁ cation and also permitted the Director 
of Agriculture to refer the subject of notifying additional crops (Paddy, Millets, Oilseeds, Sugarcane, Horticultural crops) and 
additional Villages to the Technical Support Unit (TSU) at New Delhi. Accordingly, the Director of Agriculture has referred 
the list of additional crops and Villages proposed for Notiﬁ cation to Technical Support Unit, Government of India and the 
Insurance Companies for conﬁ rmation. The ICICI LOMBARD General Insurance Company Limited accepted for inclusion of 
all additional Villages for all crops during Rabi 2017-2018. In continuation to this, "Go ahead" orders were issued by the 
Director of Agriculture to the Joint Directors of Agriculture for enrolling the farmers in the additional Villages for other Rabi 
crops during 2017-2018.
5. 
The Director of Agriculture has, therefore, requested the Government-
 
(i) 
to ratify his action in having issued the "Go ahead" orders to the District Joint Directors of Agriculture for 
enrolling farmers in the additional units for other Rabi crops during 2017-2018; and
 
(ii) 
to approve the additional units for notiﬁ cation and publication in the Government Gazette;
6. 
The Government, after careful examination of the proposal of the Director of Agriculture accepts the same and 
accordingly hereby ratify his action in having issued "Go ahead" orders to the District Joint Directors of Agriculture for enrolling 
farmers in the additional units for other Rabi crops during 2017-2018. The Government further, notify the additional units for 
other Rabi crops during Rabi 2017-2018 season under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana as shown in the Annexure to this 
order.
7. 
The claims under the Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana Scheme shall be settled on the basis of yield data furnished 
by the Director, Department of Economics and Statistics as per prescribed cut-off dates for the notiﬁ ed crops / districts / 
ﬁ rkas / revenue Villages for the effective implementation of Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana scheme for Rabi 2017-2018 
season and not on the basis of the Annavari Certiﬁ cate / Gazette notiﬁ cation declaring  the area as Drough / Food / Cyclone 
affected etc., by the District Collectors.
(By Order of the Governor)
