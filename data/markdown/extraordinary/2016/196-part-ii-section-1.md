In pursuance of sub-rule (4) of rule 3 of the Citizenship (Registration of Citizens and Issue of National
Identity Cards) Rules, 2003, the Central Government has decided to update the National Population Register
and seeding of Aadhaar Number in the National Population Register database by conducting a field work of
House to House enumeration throughout the Country.
In this context, the Government of Tamil Nadu declares that House to House enumeration for collection
of information relating to all persons who are usually residing within the jurisdiction of Local Registrar for updating
the National Population Register and seeding of Aadhaar Number conducted in the State of Tamil Nadu
from 18th January 2016 to 5th February 2016 and extended up to 31st August 2016 is further extended upto
30th October 2016.
