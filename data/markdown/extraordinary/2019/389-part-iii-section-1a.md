In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of section 8 of the Thanjavur City Municipal Corporation 
Act, 2013 (Tamil Nadu Act 24 of 2013), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby modifies the Tamil Nadu Municipal 
Corporations Constitution of Standing Committees Rules, 1997, by way of making the following amendment 
to the said Rules and also directs that the said Rules shall apply to the Thanjavur City Municipal Corporation, 
subject to the modification hereby made:—
Amendment.
In the said Rules, in rule 2, for sub-rule (1), the following sub-rule shall be substituted, namely:—
	
“(1) In these rules, unless the context otherwise requires,-
	
	
(i) “Act” means the Thanjavur City Municipal Corporation Act, 2013 (Tamil Nadu Act 24 of 2013);
	
	
(ii) “Corporation” means the Thanjavur City Municipal Corporation; and
	
	
(iii) “Council” means the Council of Thanjavur City Municipal Corporation.”.
