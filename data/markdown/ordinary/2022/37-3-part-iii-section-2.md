GENERAL  ADMINISTRATION  DEPARTMENT
Re-naming of Chennai Port Trust as Chennai Port Authority.
(RRC/6444/2015/GA)
NOTIFICATION
No. SRO C-29/2022.
The Ministry of Ports, Shipping and Waterways, Government of India, has appointed the 3rd day of November, 2021 
as the date on which the provisions of Major Port Authorities Act, 2021 (1 of 2021) shall come into force, vide Gazette 
Notification No. S.O. 4504 (E), dated 29.10.2021. Accordingly, the Major Port Authority Act, 2021 came into force w.e.f. 
03.11.2021. 
2. Ministry, thereafter, constituted the first Board of the Major Port Authority of Chennai Port, under sub-section 1 
of Section 3 of the Major Port Authorities Act, 2021 vide Gazette Notification No. G.S.R. 52 (E), dated 28.01.2022. 
3. The Board of the Major Port Authority of Chennai Port, at its first meeting held on 18.02.2022 vide Resolution 
No. 161 have accorded approval to change the name of 'Chennai Port Trust' as 'Chennai Port Authority'. 
4. 	 Accordingly, the name of Chennai Port Trust is changed as Chennai Port Authority and shall be governed under 
the Major Port Authorities Act, 2021. 
5. 	 The Tamil and Hindi version of Chennai Port Authority is as follows: 
 
சென்னைத் துறைமுக ஆணையம் [Tamil version] 
	
	
चेन्नै पत्तन प्राधिकरण [Hindi version]
