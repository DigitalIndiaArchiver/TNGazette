MAKING A PROVISION UNDER SECTION 53(1) OF
ANNAMALAI UNIVERSITY
[G.O. Ms. No. 235, Higher Education (H1)
7th November 2013, äŠðC 21, Müò,
F¼õœÀõ˜ Ý‡´ 2044.]
No. II(2)/HE/850(b-3)/2013.
Whereas the Academic Council of the Annamalai
University which is an Authority of the University, shall consist
of the Ex-officio Members and the Other-Members as
specified in sub-section (1) of Section 22 of the Annamalai
University Act, 2013 (Tamil Nadu Act 20 of 2013).
And Whereas item (e) under the heading “Class I –
Ex-officio Members” in sub-section (1) of Section 22 of the
said Tamil Nadu Act 20 of 2013 provides that the ‘Deans
of Faculties’ shall be the Ex-officio Members of the Academic
Council;
And Whereas item (b) under the heading
“Class II – Other Members” in sub-section (1) of Section 22
of the said Tamil Nadu Act 20 of 2013 provides that three
members from among the Deans of Faculties shall be
nominated to the Academic Council by the Chancellor on
the recommendation of the Vice-Chancellor;
And Whereas it is considered that the provision in item
(b) under the heading “Class II – Other Members” in
sub-section (1) of Section 22 of the said Tamil Nadu Act 20 of
2013, is redundant and there is no need to nominate three
members from among the Deans of Faculties to the Academic
Council under item (b), under the heading “Class II – Other
Members” in sub-section (1) of Section 22 of the said Tamil
Nadu Act 20 of 2013, since all the Deans of Faculties will be
the Ex-officio Members of the Academic Council, by virtue of
item (e) under the heading “Class I – Other Members” in
sub-section (1) of Section 22 of the said Tamil Nadu Act 20 of
2013.
And Whereas sub-section (1) of Section 53 of the said
Tamil Nadu Act 20 of 2013 empowers the Government to
make, by notification, such provision as may appear to them to
be necessary or expedient for removing any difficulty that may
arise as to the first constitution or re-constitution of any authority
of the University.
Now, Therefore, in exercise the powers conferred by
sub-section (1) of Section 53 of the said Tamil Nadu Act 20
of 2013, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the
following provision, namely:—
“No nomination to the Academic Council of the Annamalai
University from among the Deans of Faculties, under item (b)
under the heading “Class II – Other Members” in sub-section
(1) of Section 22 of the Annamalai University Act, 2013
(Tamil Nadu Act 20 of 2013), is necessary, since all the
Deans of Faculties of the University will be the Ex-officio
members of the Academic Council by virtue of item (e)
under the heading “Class I – Ex-officio Members” in
sub-section (1) of Section 22 of the said Tamil Nadu Act
20 of 2013".
APURVA VARMA,
