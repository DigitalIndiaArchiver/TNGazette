AMENDMENT TO THE TAMIL NADU TRANSPARENCY IN TENDERS (PUBLIC PRIVATE 
PARTNERSHIP PROCUREMENT) RULES, 2012.
[G.O. Ms. No. 101, Finance (Salaries), 8th March 2019, Maasi 24, Vilambi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2050.]
No. SRO A-9(a)/2019.
In exercise of the powers conferred by  sub-section (1) of Section 22 of the Tamil Nadu Transparency 
in Tenders Act, 1998 (Tamil Nadu Act 43 of 1998), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following 
amendment to the Tamil Nadu Transparency in Tenders (Public Private Partnership Procurement) Rules, 2012.
2. The amendment hereby made shall come into force on the 8th day of March 2019.
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖග.
In the said rules, for rules 3 and 4, the following rules shall be substituted, namely:-
“3. Procuring methods for Public Private Partnership (PPP) Projects.- (1) The mode of procurement 
for a Public Private Partnership (PPP) Project shall be either a two-stage, open competitive tendering under 
rule 4 or a single stage, two cover, open competitive tendering, as the procuring entity deems it.
 
(2) In a single stage, two cover, opend and competitive tendering the qualiﬁ cation information and technical 
proposal shall be received in one cover and the ﬁ nal oﬀ er shall be received in another separate cover.
