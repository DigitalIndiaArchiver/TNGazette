No. VI(2)/193(a)/2014.
WHEREAS, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission has notified for the conduct of rural and urban local
body casual elections as per Statutory Order (1) S.O. No.78-108/2014/TNSEC/PE1, dated 27-8-2014 and
(2) S.O.No.110-111/2014/TNSEC/ME1, dated 27-8-2014.
WHEREAS,  there is no provision in Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Elections) Rules, 1995 and in Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats,
Third Grade Municipalities, Municipalities and Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006 for affixing the photo of the Polling Agents in
their appointment letter in Form 11; and
WHEREAS,  it is felt expedient to emphasise on the production of photo identity proof as evidence by the Polling Agent at the
time of entry into the polling station for the ensuing Casual Elections to Rural and Urban Local Bodies, 2014 as a measure
to curtail impersonation;
NOW, THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (1) of Section 239 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act,
1994, sub-section (1) of Section 43-B of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920),
sub-section (1) of Section 6-1 of the Chennai City Municipal Corporation Act, 1919(Tamil Nadu Act IV of 1919), sub-section (1)
of Section 10-D of the Madurai City Muicipal Corporation Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act XV of 1971), sub-section (1) of
Section 10-D of the Coimbatore City Municipal Corporation Act, 1981 (Tamil Nadu Act 25 of 1981), read with Section 8 of
theTiruchirappalli City Municipal Corporation Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 1994), Section 8 of the Tirunelveli City Municipal
Corporation Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 28 of 1994), Section 8 of the Tiruppur City Municipal Corporation Act, 2008
(Tamil Nadu Act 7 of 2008), Section 8 of the Erode City Municipal Corporation Act, 2008 (Tamil Nadu Act 8 of 2008),
Section 8 of the Thoothukudi City Municipal Corporation Act, 2008 (Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 2008), Section 8 of the
Thanjavur City Municipal Corporation Act, 2013 (Tamil Nadu Act 24 of 2013) and Section 8 of the Dindigul City Municipal

1995  and rule 38 and 139 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities, Municipalities and Corporations
(Elections) Rules, 2006, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission orders that the Polling Agents (including the relief
agents) shall show any one of the fourteen documentary evidences in original as prescribed for the electors in S.O.No.78/
2011/TNSEC/EG2, dated 21-09-2011 as proof of his/her identity to the Presiding Officer and Police Personnel at the time
of entry into the Polling Station.  The documentary evidence shown by him must have his/her photo identity proof on it.
Further, the Commission specifies that every Contesting Candidate or his/her Election Agent shall appoint one Polling
Agent for each Polling Station and only one Relief Agent to act as the Polling Agent of such candidate at each Polling
Station in the ensuing Casual Elections to Rural and Urban Local Bodies, 2014 and any one of them shall be present
in the Polling Station at a time.
The above identity proof has been prescribed only for the purpose of verifying the identity of the person appointed as
Polling Agent and not for any other purpose of scrutinizing other things.
