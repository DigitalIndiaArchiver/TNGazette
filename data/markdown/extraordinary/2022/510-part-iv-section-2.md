       2. The State Government may appropriate out of the Consolidated  Fund of the State for the 
services and purposes of the financial year which commenced on the 1st day of April 2022, a further
sum not exceeding  Three Thousand Seven Hundred Ninety-Five Crores Seventy-One Lakhs
