ACT, 2019.
 No. VI(1)/488(b-1)/2022.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that the lands speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below to be acquired 
for Highways purpose to wit, for formation of road connectivity from Ennore Port to Mahabalipuram (from Km 17+810 to 
Km 19+840) at No.76. Thatchur-II (Block-II) Village, Ponneri Taluk, Tiruvallur District and it having already been decided that 
the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands to be paid out of the funds controlled and managed by the 
Highways Department. The following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 
2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34/2002).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002), the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the Dry and Manavari Patta lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule 0.48.82 Hectare / 4882 
Sq. Mtrs. to the same, a little more or less needed for the purpose to with for formation of road for providing road connectivity 
from Ennore Port to Mahabalipuram (from Km 17+810 to Km 19+840) as part of Chennai Peripheral Ring Road Project, 
by Highways Department.
The Plan of the lands are kept in the oﬃ  ce of the Special District Revenue Oﬃ  cer (Land Acquisition), Chennai Peripheral 
Ring Road Project, Chennai-600 058 and in the oﬃ  ce of the Special Tahsildar (Land Acquisition), Chennai Peripheral Ring 
Road Project, Unit-I, Ponneri and may be inspected at any time during the oﬃ  ce hours.
