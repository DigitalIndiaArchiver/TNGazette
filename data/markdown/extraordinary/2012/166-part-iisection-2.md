HOME DEPARTMENT
Appointment of Presiding Judge of the Designated Court under the Terrorist and
Disruptive Activities (Prevention) Act.
AMENDMENT TO NOTIFICATION
[G.O. Ms. No. 456, Home (courts-II), 18th June  2012.]
No. II(2)/HO/308(e)/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by  sub-section (4) of Section 9 of the Terrorist and Disruptive Activities
(Prevention) Act, 1987 (Central Act 28 of 1987), the Governor of Tamil Nadu with the concurrence of the Chief
Justice, High Court of Madras, hereby makes the following amendment to the Home Department Notification
No. II(2)/HO/3929/(b-2)/91, published at page I of Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette,
Extraordinary, dated the 11th July 1991, as subsequently amended:
AMENDMENT
In the said Notification, for the expression “Tmt.S.Kalavathi, I Additional City Civil and Sessions Judge,
Chennai”, the following expression shall be substituted namely:-
“Thiru K.Dakshinamoorthy, I Additional City Civil and Sessions Judge, Chennai”.
R. RAJAGOPAL,
Principal Secretary to Government.
