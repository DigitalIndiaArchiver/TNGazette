DEMISE OF THIRU. R. DORAIKKANNU, MINISTER FOR AGRICULTURE, TAMIL NADU.
[Letter  No. 8477/2020-1, Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Secretariat (B.III) 2nd November 2020,

“The Hon. Speaker has learnt with deep regret the demise of Thiru. R. Doraikkannu, 
Hon'ble Minister for Agriculture and a Member of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly on 
31st October 2020".
VACATION OF SEAT IN TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY.

Aippasi 17, Saarvari, Thiruvalluvar Aandu 2051.]

“A vacancy has occurred among the Members of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly, consequent 
on the demise of Thiru R. Doraikkannu, a Member elected to the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly from 
the 172. Papanasam Assembly Constituency, Thanjavur District”.
