AGRICULTURE  DEPARTMENT
Declaration of regulation of  Agricultural Produces in the notified areas of Regulated Markets in 21 Market
Committees under Tamil Nadu Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) Act.
[G.O. Ms. No. 361, Agriculture (AM.II),12th December 2008.]
No.II(1)/AG/60/2008.—In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (d) of sub-section (1) of Section 9  read with Section
3 of the Tamil Nadu Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) Act, 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 1989), the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby declares his intention of regulating the marketing of the Agricultural produces
mentioned in Annexure-I, to the draft notification in the notified market areas of the regulated markets mentioned
in Annexure-II to the draft notification.
Any objection or suggestion, which may be received by the Government of Tamil Nadu within a period of one month
from the date of publication of this Notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette will be considered by the Government.
Objection or suggestion, if any, should be addressed to the Agricultural Production Commissioner and Principal
Secretary to Government, Agriculture Department, Secretariat, Chennai-600 009, through the Commissioner of Agricultural
Marketing and Agri Business, Thiru Vi. Ka. Industrial Estate, Guindy, Chennai-600 032.
ANNEXURE - I
Agricultural Produces
1.
Cereals.—Paddy, Rice in all forms, Wheat in all forms, Cholam (Jowar) in all forms,  Cumbu (Bajra) in all forms,
Ragi in all forms, Maize in all forms, Thinai, Kudiraivali, Varagu, Samai.
2.
Pulses.—Redgram (Thuvarai) in all forms, Black gram (Ulundu) in all forms, Greengram (Pachaippairu) in all forms,
Bengal gram (Kondakadalai) in all forms, Peas (Pattani) in all forms, Lab (Mochai) in all forms, Cowpea (Karamani) in
all forms, Soya beans in all forms, Horsegram (Kollu) in all forms.
3.
Oil Seeds.—Groundnut (Pods and Kernels) (whole or split), Gingelly or Sesamum seed or Ellu, Castor (Pods
or seeds), Sunflower seeds or Kernels, Coconut (unhusked or husked or kopra), Cotton seed, Mustard (Kadugu) seeds,
Nigar seeds, Safflower seeds, Neem seeds, Pungam seeds.
4.
Fibers.—Cotton (Kapas, lint, waste), Coconut coir.
5.
Vegetables.—Brinjal, Bhendi, Potato, Onion, Gourds (snake gourd or pudalai, Bitter gourd or pagal, bottle gourd
or surai, pumpkin or parangikai, ribbed gourd (or) peerkankai, ash gourd or pusanikai), Tomato, Moringa (Murungai), Greens
(Keeraigal), Green Chillies, Yams (all kinds),  Lab Lab Avarai, Cabbage, Cauliflower, Radish (all kinds), Carrot, Beans
(all kinds), Sweet potato, Green, Banana (Vaalaikkai), Chow Chow, Kari leaf (Karuveppilai)), Knol-khol, Turnip, Green peas,
Cluster beans (Kothavarakai), Beetroot, Agathi.
6.
Fruits.—Banana, Mango, Guava, Grapes (Kodimundhari), Citrus (Sathugudi, Loose jacket orange, lime lemon and
grape), Pomegranate (Mathulai), jack, Melons, Pine apple, Apple, Pears, Plums, Papaiya, Sapota (Chikoo), Cucumber.
7.
Drugs and Narcotics.—Tobacco in all forms.
8.
Tubers.—Tapioca tubers, tapioca chips, tapioca flour, tapioca starch.
9.
Condiments and Spices.—Chillies or red chillies, Garlics, Turmeric in all forms (bulb or finger), Coriander (Dhania
seeds), Cardamom, Arecanut (whole or splits), Tamarind in all forms, Cashewnuts in all forms, Ginger in all forms (inji,
sukku, etc.), Betal leaves or vetrilai.
10. Miscellaneous.—Sugarcane jaggery in all forms (jaggery powder, brown sugar, etc.), Palmgur jaggery in all forms,
Raw Rubber in all forms, Pale Latex crepe, sole crepe and centrifuged latex rubber manufacturer’s association grades
on rubber sheets and estates browncrepe grades, Silk cotton in all forms.
