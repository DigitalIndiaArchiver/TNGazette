GENERAL  NOTIFICATIONS
Winding up of the affairs of certain Co-operative Societies and Appointment of Official Liquidators
IND. No. 19/MDS/2006 Invent Infotech Technocrats Labour Contract Industrial Co-operative Service Society Ltd.
(Rc. No. 7211/ICD/2014.)
No. VI (1)/266/2014.
Under the powers conferred on the Registrar of Industrial Co-operatives under Section 137(2)(b) of the Tamil Nadu
Co-operative Societies Act, 1983 (Act 30 of 1983), the activities of the Invent Infotech Technocrats Labour Contract Industrial
Co-operative Service Society Ltd., IND. 19/MDS/2006 have been  ordered to be wound up with immediate effect by the
Principal Secretary / Industries Commissioner and Director of Industries and Commerce and the Registrar of Industrial
Co-operatives, Mandaivelipakkam, Raja Annamalaipuram, Chennai-28. vide Proc. No.7211/ICD/2014, dated 18-7-2014.
Under Section 138(1) of the same Act, Deputy Director (Indl.Co-ops), Guindy, Chennai has been appointed as Official
Liquidator of the said society for the purpose of liquidation.
Chennai-600 028,
SWARAN SINGH,
18th July 2014.
Principal Secretary/Industries Commissioner and .
Director of Industries and Commerce.

(Roc. No. 7212/ICD/2014.)
No. VI (1)/267/2014.
Under the powers conferred on the Registrar of Industrial Co-operatives under Section 137(2)(b) of the Tamil Nadu
Co-operative Societies Act, 1983 (Act 30 of 1983), the activities of the north Chennai Technocrats Labour Contract Industrial
Co-operative Service Society Ltd., IND. 18/MDS/2006 have been  ordered to be wound up with immediate effect by the
Principal Secretary/ Industries Commissioner and Director of Industries and Commerce and the Registrar of Industrial
Co-operatives, Mandaivelipakkam, Raja Annamalaipuram, Chennai-28, vide Proc. No.7212/ICD/2014 dated 28-7-2014.
Under Section 138(1) of the same Act, Deputy Director (Indl.Co-ops), Guindy, Chennai has been appointed as Official
Liquidator of the said society for the purpose of liquidation.

under Section 3 (1) (h) of the Tamil Nadu Nurses and Midwives Act III of 1926.
(Ref. No. 288/NC/2014.)
No. VI (1)/271/2014.
Whereas vacancies have occurred in the membership of the Tamil Nadu Nurses and Midwives Act of 1926
(Madras Act III of 1926) and whereas an election has to be held under Section 3 (1) (h) of the said Act
as subsequently amended.

Officer. Tamil Nadu Nurses and Midwives Council calls upon the registered Nurses and Midwives as per the list published
in the Council Office Notice Board to elect in accordance with the said rules two registered Nurses and one Registered
Midwife for the purpose of filling said vacancy on or before the 1st October 2014.
