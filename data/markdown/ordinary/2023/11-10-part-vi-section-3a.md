GENERAL NOTIFICATION
TAMIL NADU WAQF BOARD, CHENNAI-600 001.
Framing of Bye Law in respect of Jamia Masjid, Tuticorin District (G.S.No. 269/TNV).
(Lr. Rc.No. 813/08/B1/TNV)
No. VI-3(a)/13/2023.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 69 of the Waqf Act 1995, the Tamil Nadu Waqf 
Board in its Resolution in Item No. 310/22 in Rc.No. 813/08/B1/TNV, dated 30-01-2023 has approved the BYE LAW 
for the Waqf in respect of JAMIA MASJID, TUTICORIN DISTRICT for better administration of the Waqf.
