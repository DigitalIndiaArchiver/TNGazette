No. SRO B-24/2015.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitition of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following Amendment to the rules published with the Health and Family Planning
Department Notification No.SRO B-421/76, published at Pages 454 to 457 of Part III-Section 1(b) of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 4th August 1976 as subsequently amended.
AMENDMENT
In the said rules, in the proviso to rule 7, for the expression “other than the Backward Class” the expression
“other than the Backward Class Muslim” shall be substituted.
Amendment to the Adhoc Rules relating to the Post of Electro Cardiogram Operator in Tamil Nadu Medical
Subordinate Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 74, Health and Family Welfare (C2), 12th March 2015, ñ£C 28, äò, F¼õœÀõ˜ Ý‡´-2046.]
No. SRO B-25/2015.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitition of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the follwoing Amendment to the Adhoc Rules published with the Health and Family
Planning Department Notification No.SRO B-513/74, at Pages 538 - 539 of Part III-Section 1(b) of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 11th December 1974.
2. The amendments, hereby made shall come into force on the date of publication of this Notification in the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules,—
(1) in rule 1, for the expression “Government General Hospital, Madras”, the expression “Government Medical Colleges
and Hospitals” shall be substituted.
(2) in rule 4, for the expression “Head of the Medical Institution”, the expression “Head of the Medical Institutions
concerned” shall be substituted;
(3) in rule 7, after the proviso, the following provisos shall be added, namely:—
“Provided further that on and from 1-06-1988, the monthly pay shall be calculated in the scale of pay of
Rs. 1200-30-1560-40-2040:
Provided also that on and from 1-01-1996, the monthly pay shall be calculated in the scale of pay of Rs.4000-100-6000:
Provided also that on and from 1-01-2006, the monthly pay shall be calculated in the scale of pay of Rs.5200-20200+
GP 2400.”;
(4) after rule 7, the following rule shall be added, namely:—
“8. Saving clause.—Nothing contained in these amendments shall adversely affect any person holding the post on
the date of publication of this notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.”.
