Acquisition of Lands

No. II(2)/AG/94(a)/2010.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that
the lands specified in the Schedule below has to be acquired for
the public purpose and it having already been decided that the
entire amount of compensation is to be paid by the Thanjavur
Paddy Processing Research Centre, authorities towards
compensation to be awarded for the land, the following
Declaration  is issued under Section 6 of the Land Acquisition
Act, 1894 (Central Act I of 1894).
DECLARATION
Under Section 6 of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894 (Central
Act I of 1894), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby declares that
the lands specified in the Schedule below and measuring 8.86.0
hectares (21.88 acres) to be the same a little more or less are
needed for a public purpose, to wit, for the construction of
buildings for Paddy Processing Research Centre in Pillayarpatti
and Nanjikkottai Villages, Thanjavur Taluk, Thanjavur District
under section (1)/(2) of Section 17 of the Act, the Governor of
Tamil Nadu direct that the possession of the said land may be
taken immediately after the publication of the notice mentioned
in Section 9(1) of the Act. A plan of the lands is kept in the office
of the Revenue Divisional Officer, Thanjavur and the same may
be perused at any time during office hours.
