NOTIFICATIONS BY THE GOVERNMENT
HOME DEPARTMENT
(Courts-II)
No. I/HO/11/2022.—
[Courts and Judges - District Judges - Transfer and 
Postings in Commercial Court at Chennai - Orders - Issued.]
[G.O. (D). No. 534, Home (Courts-II), 21st April 2022, 
The following Government Order is published:—
Rඍඉඌ:
1. From the Registrar General, High Court of Madras, 
Chennai D.O. letter No.45373-A/2022-B2, dated 20.04.2022.
2. Government letter No.435/2022-1 Public (Special-A) 
Department, Chennai-9, dated 21.04.2022.
Order: No. 534, Home (Courts-II), 21st April 2022.
The Registrar General, High Court of Madras, Chennai, in 
his letter communicated with the Government letter second read 
above, has stated that the High Court has ordered to transfer 
Thiru. L.S. Sathiyamurthy, District Judge, now functioning as 
XIII Additional Judge (CBI Cases), City Civil Court, Chennai, 
From the said post and post him as Judge Commercial 
Court at Chennai, in the newly sanctioned Court, to be 
inaugurated on 23.04.2022. He has requested necessary 
orders of the Government to that eﬀ ect.
2. The Government, after careful examination accepts 
the proposal of the Registrar General, High Court, Madras, 
Accordingly, the Government appoint Thiru. L.S. Sathiyamurthy, 
District Judge, XIII Additional Judge (CBI Cases), City Civil 
Court, Chennai on transfer as Judge in Commercial Court at 
Chennai (newly Sactioned Court). 
(By order of the Governor)
                           S.K. PRABAKAR, 
         
                  
 Chief Secretary to Government.
