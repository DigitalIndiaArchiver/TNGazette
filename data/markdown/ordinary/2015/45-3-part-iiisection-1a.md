No. SRO A-20/2015.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 59 of the Prisons Act, 1894
(Central Act IX of 1894), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu Prison
Rules, 1983, as subsequently amended:—
2. (a) The amendment (1) hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 23rd April 1993.
  (b) The amendment (2) hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 8th January 2015.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules,—
(1) in rule 392, for the expression “Rs. 2/-”, the expression “Rs. 5/-” shall be substituted; and
(2) for rule 392, as so amended, the following rule shall be substituted, namely:-
“392. All the prisoners shall be issued with extra diet on the days of Pongal Festival, Republic Day, Independence
Day and Gandhi Jayanthi, at a cost not exceeding Rs. 50/- (Rupees fifty only) per prisoner per each such day.”
APURVA VARMA,
Principal Secretary to Government.
