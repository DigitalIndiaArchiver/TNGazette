AMENDMENTS TO TAMIL NADU LIQUOR (LICENCE AND PERMIT) RULES, 1981.
[G.O. Ms. No. 27, Home, Prohibition and Excise (VIII),  26th June  2012.]
No. SRO A-18(b)/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Sections 17-C,17-D, 17-E, 18-B, 18-C, 20 and 21 read with Section 54 of the
Tamil Nadu Prohibition Act, 1937 (Tamil Nadu Act X of 1937), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby makes the following
amendments to the Tamil Nadu Liquor (Licence and Permit) Rules, 1981:—
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules, in rule 17, in sub-rule (b),—
(1) in clause II,  for the expression “an annual privilege fee of Rs.4,00,000 (Rupees four lakhs only)”, the expression “an
annual privilege fee of Rs.10,00,000 (Rupees ten lakhs only) for Chennai City and Rs.6,00,000 (Rupees six lakhs only) for other
areas” shall be substituted;
(2) in clause III,
(i)  for the first and second tables, the following tables shall, respectively, be substituted, namely:—-

(ii) After the proviso, the following proviso shall be added, namely:—
“Provided further that the hours of business for five star hotels in Chennai and Trichy, where the International Civil Airports
are situated and the five star hotels in the cities of Coimbatore and Madurai who opt to pay double the amount of privilege fee

“7. The hours of business for five star hotels in Chennai and Trichy, where the International Civil Airports are situated and
the five star hotels in the cities of Coimbatore and Madurai who opt to pay double the amount of privilege fee shall be 24 hours
and for other hotels, the hours of business shall be from 11.00 A.M. to 12.00 midnight and no business should be transacted
before 11.00 A.M. and after 12.00 midnight”.
