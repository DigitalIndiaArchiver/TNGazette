Medal on the occasion of Pongal, 2014.
[G.O. Ms. No.29, Home (SC), 13th January 2014.]
No.II(2) /HO/65/2014.
The members of the Police Force of the ranks of Police
Constables, Police Constables – Grade-I and Head Constables
of the local Police and Havildars of Armed Police, totaling 1,500
including those working in the Directorate of Vigilance and
Anti-Corruption, and other special units whose names are listed
in the Annexure have been awarded the Tamil Nadu Chief
Minister’s Constabulary Medal on the occasion of Pongal, 2014.
2.
They are eligible to draw a monthly allowance of
Rs.200/- (Rupees two hundred only) per month, as specified
in rule 10 of the rules governing the award of the Tamil Nadu
Chief Minister’s Constabulary Medals, issued in the Government
Order G.O.Ms. No.35, Home (SC) Department, dated
14-01-2013, with effect from 1st February 2014.
