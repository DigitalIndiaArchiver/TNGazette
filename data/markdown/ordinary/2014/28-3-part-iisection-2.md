No. II(2)/EF/422/2014.—In exercise of the powers conferred
by Section 16 of the Tamil Nadu Forest Act, 1882 (Tamil Nadu
Act V of 1882), and in supersession of the Environment and
Forest (FR.14) Department Notification No. II(2)/EF/910/2004,
published at pages 506-507 in Part II—Section 2 of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 20th October 2004,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby declares the area, within
the boundaries of which are specified in the Schedule below,
as a Reserved Forest with effect on and from 22-1-2015.

BOUNDARIES.
North:- Starting from the trijunction point of Survey Field
Numbers 146, 226/1 and 144 of Pattukonampatti, the boundary
runs towards east along the southern side of Survey Field
Number 144 of Village Number 58, Pattukonampatti Village upto
the junction of Survey Field Numbers 226/1, 144, 140 and 229
of the said Village.
East:- Thence the boundary generally runs, towards south
along the western sides of Survey Field Numbers 229, 228,
and 227, thence runs towards east, north east, again east
along the south, east again, southern sides of Survey Field
Number 227 and reaches the south-west corner of Survey
Field Number 234 and thence the boundary runs along the
western side of Survey Field Number 235, thence runs along
the north, west again north, west, south again western side
of Survey Field Number 236 and meets the Village boundary
of Village Number 57. Elandakuttapatti Village.
South:- Thence the boundary generally runs towards west
along the common Village boundary of Village Number 57,
Elandakuttapatti and Village Number 58. Pattukonampatti till it
meets the north-east corner of Survey Field Number 226/3 of
Village Number 58. Pattukonampatti. Thence, the boundary
runs along the northern, western side of Survey
Field Number 226/3 and then along the western and southern
side of Survey Field Number 226/2 of Number 58.
Pattukonampatti Village till it meets again the above said
common Village boundary. Thence it runs along the said
common boundary till it reaches the Village trijunction of Village
Number 56. Nonanganur, Village Number 57. Elandakuttapatty,
Village Number 58, Pattukonampatti. Thence it runs towards
west along the common Village boundary of Pattukonampatti
and Nonanganur Village and reaches the trijunction of Survey
Field Numbers 226/1 and 218 of Pattukonampatti Village juction
with Number 56. Nonanganur Village.
West:- Thence the boundary generally runs towards north
along the eastern sides of Survey Field Numbers 218, 219,
220, 225, 149, 148 and 146 all of Pattukonampatti Village and
reaches the starting point.
