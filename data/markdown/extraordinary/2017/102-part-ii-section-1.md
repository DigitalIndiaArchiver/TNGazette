ORDER: NO. 301, Public (Miscellaneous), 10th April 2017.
Consequent on the orders of the Election Commission of India, New Delhi, rescinding its Notification dated 16.03.2017,
calling upon the 11-Dr.Radhakrishnan Nagar Assembly Constituency to elect a member to the Tamil Nadu Legislative
Assembly, the orders already issued in the G.O. first read above declaring the Public Holiday on 12.04.2017 (Wednesday)
for the areas comprised in 11-Dr.Radhakrishnan Nagar Assembly Constituency is hereby cancelled.
(By Order of the Governor)
