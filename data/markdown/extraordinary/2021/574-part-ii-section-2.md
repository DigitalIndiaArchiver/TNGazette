NOTICE OF ELECTION
Election of Members of the Tamil Nadu Waqf Board
     Notice is hereby given that :-
An election is to be held for one member to the Tamil Nadu Waqf Board for the Electoral College consisting of 
Muslim Members of Parliament from the State.
2. Nomination papers in Form–I may be delivered by a candidate whose name ﬁ nds place in the ﬁ nal electoral roll 
to Thiru A.M. Aandiyappan, Returning Oﬃ  cer and Additional Secretary to Government, Backward Classes, Most Backward 
Classes and Minorities Welfare Department, Chennai–9 or to Thiru G. Abdul  Muheeth, Assistant Returning Oﬃ  cer 
and Under Secretary to Government, Backward Classes, Most Backward Classes and Minorities Welfare Department, 
Chennai–9,  between 11.00 A.M. and 3.00 P.M. on any day (other than Public Holidays) not later 
than 8th  December, 2021.
3. Forms of nomination papers may be obtained from 06.12.2021 (Monday) onwards at the place and time aforesaid. 
4. The nomination of candidate belonging to the electoral college of Muslim Members of Parliament from the State 
shall be proposed and seconded by members belonging to the respective electoral college; Provided that there is no 
need for a proposer or seconder when total number of members belonging to the said electoral college is six or less 
than six.
5. The proposer and the seconder shall belong to the electoral college of the candidate nominated.
6. Any member belonging to a particular electoral college may be subscribed either as proposer or as seconder to 
as many nomination paper as there are vacancies to be ﬁ lled to the said category, but not more.
7. A member of an electoral college shall contest, propose or second a candidate belonging to the said electoral 
college only.
8. A member of an electoral college, who proposes or seconds the candidature of another member shall not be 
eligible for himself contesting the election. 
9. Any candidate may withdraw his candidature by a notice in writing as speciﬁ ed in Form–III signed by him 
may be delivered to either of the Oﬃ  cers speciﬁ ed in the paragraph (2) above at his oﬃ  ce before 3.00 P.M. 
on 10th December 2021.
10. If the number of candidates who were duly nominated and who have not withdrawn their candidature is equal to 
the number of vacancies to be ﬁ lled, the Election Authority shall declare all such candidates to have been duly elected.
11. If the number of candidates who were duly nominated and who have not withdrawn their candidature exceeds 
the number of vacancies to be ﬁ lled, the Election Authority shall cause notice to be given of the date and place of 
poll and shall publish the ﬁ nal list of the candidates contesting the election in his oﬃ  ce. 
12. One copy of the ﬁ nal electoral roll of the electoral college shall be supplied to the candidates contesting from 
the said electoral college free of cost and additional copies if required on payment of cost as speciﬁ ed by the Election 
Authority from time to time.
13. No person who suﬀ ers from any of the disqualiﬁ cations under the Act shall be eligible to contest the election.
