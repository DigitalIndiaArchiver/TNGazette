Amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Medical Service
[G.O.Ms. No. 176, Health and Family Welfare (A2), 23rd June 2014, ÝQ 9, üò, F¼õœÀõ˜ Ý‡´-2045.]
No. SRO B-42/2014.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the
Tamil Nadu Medical Service (Section 21 in Volume II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1969).
2.
The amendment hereby made shall come into force on 23rd June 2014.
AMENDMENT
In the said Special Rules, in Part II, under the heading “Branch-I-Medical”, in rule 3, in sub-rule (a), under the
heading “SPECIAL CLASS”, in the Tabular Column, against the item - I in column (1), for the corresponding entry in
column (2), the following entry shall be substituted, namely:-
“Promotion from among the holders of the post of Additional Director of Medical and Rural Health Services in General
line only” .
J. RADHAKRISHNAN,
Secretary to Government.
PUBLIC DEPARTMENT
Amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu General Service
[G.O. Ms. No. 497, Public (Protocol-III), 27th June 2014.]
No. SRO B-41/2014.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the rules for Class XXVIII in the
Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu General Service, (Section 16 in Volume-II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1969).
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force with effect on and from the
1st November 1985.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules,—
(1) in Part I, the expression “Class XXVIII - Assistant Comptroller, Governor's Household” shall be omitted;
(2) in Part II, the heading “Class XXVIII -Assistant Comptroller, Governor's Household” and the rules thereunder
shall be omitted.
JATINDRA NATH SWAIN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
