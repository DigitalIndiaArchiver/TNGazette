The Government of Tamil Nadu, having been satisﬁ ed that the lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule 
below have to be acquired for Industrial purpose, to wit, for Expansion of Tiruchirappalli Airport at 
Kottappattu Block I and surrounding areas, and it having already been decided that the amount 
of compensation to be awarded for the lands to be paid out of the funds controlled. The following 
notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of land for Industrial 
Purpose Act 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999)
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of land for Industrial Purposes 
Act 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), The Government of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the land 
speciﬁ ed in the schedule below and measuring 27298.5 Sq. mtr. (6.74 Acres) to the same, a little more 
of less needed for Industrial Purpose, to wit, for Expansion of Tiruchirappalli Airport at Kottappattu 
and surrounding areas.
The plan of the lands is kept in the oﬃ  ce of the Distrcit Collector, Tiruchirappalli and may be 
inspected at any time during oﬃ  ce hours.
