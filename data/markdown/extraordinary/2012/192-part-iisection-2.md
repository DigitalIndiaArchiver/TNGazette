HIGHWAYS AND MINOR PORTS DEPARTMENT
LEVY OF TOLLS ON VEHICLES ENTERING THE ROADS IN YERCAUD PANCHAYAT UNION AREA IN
SALEM DISTRICT FOR CERTAIN PERIOD UNDER THE INDIAN TOLLS ACT.
[G.O.D.No. 122, Highways and Minor Ports (HP1), 10th July 2012.]
No. II(2)/HWMP/368(c)/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (a) of sub-section (2) of Section 2 of the Indian Tolls Act, 1851
(Central Act VIII of 1851) as amended by the Tamil Nadu Tolls and Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1938
(Tamil Nadu Act VI of 1938) and the Indian Tolls (Tamil Nadu Amdnement) Act 1942 (Tamil Nadu Act XIV of 1942), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby levies the tolls for the period up to the 31st March 2015 on the classes of motor vehicles
specified in column (2) of the Table below which passes through Salem - Yercaud Road and Kuppanur -
Ayothiapattinam   Yercaud Road in the Yercaud Panchayat Union area in Salem District at the rates specified in the
corresponding entries in column (3) thereof.
Provided that such levy of tolls shall not be applicable to:—
(i) the motor vehicles owned by the residents of Yercaud other than the vehicles used for commercial purpose;
(ii) The office vehicles of Yercaud Panchayat Union; and
(iii) The office vehicles of State and Central Government and Government undertakings:—
