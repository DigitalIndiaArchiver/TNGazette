NOTIFICATION UNDER SUB-SECTION (1) OF
SECTION 4 OF THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT 1894.
No. II(2)/PDSI/486(d)/2010.
Whereas it appears to the Government of Tamil Nadu
that the Lands specified in the Schedule below and situated
in Koyambedu Village, Egmore-Nungambakkam Taluk,
Chennai District are needed for a public purpose to wit for the
implementation of the Chennai Metro Rail Project, notice to
that effect is hereby given to all to whom it may concern in
accordance with the provisions of sub-section (1) of Section 4
of the Land Acquisition Act 1894 (Central Act I of 1894) and
whereas, it has become necessary to acquire the immediate
possession of the Lands specified in the Schedule below, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that the lands be
acquired under the provisions of sub-section (1) of the
Section 17 of the said Act.
Now, therefore, in exercise of the  powers conferred by
sub-section (2) of Section 4 of the said Act, the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby authorizes the Special Tahsildar (L.A.),
Chennai Unit I, Chennai Metro Rail Limited, No. 2, Alwarpet
Street, Alwarpet, Chennai-18, and his staff and workmen to
exercise the powers conferred by the said sub-sections.
Under section 3(c) of Act, the Governor of Tamil Nadu,
hereby appoints the Special Tahsildar (L.A.), Chennai Unit I,
Chennai Metro Rail Limited, No. 2, Alwarpet Street, Alwarpet,
Chennai-18 to perform the functions of the Collector under
the said Act.
Under sub-section (4) of Section 17 of the said Act, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that in view of the
urgency of the case, the provisions of Section 5A of the said
Act, shall not apply in this case.
