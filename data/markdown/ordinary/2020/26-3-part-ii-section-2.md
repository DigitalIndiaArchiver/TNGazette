Park and Preparation of State Industrial Water Policy to the 
Centre for Urbanization Building and Environment.

Transparency in Tenders Act, 1998 (Tamil Nadu Act 43

entrustment of work E-Waste inventorization to the National 
Productivity Council by Tamil Nadu Pollution Control Board.
