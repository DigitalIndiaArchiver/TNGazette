(S.O.No.3/2022/TNSEC/PE1 dated the 6th January 2022)
No.VI(2)/4(b)/2022.
	
WHEREAS, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission has issued an Order viz., the Tamil Nadu Local Bodies 
Election Symbols (Reservation and Allotment) Order, 2022 to provide for specification, reservation, choice and allotment 
of symbols at elections to local bodies in the State of Tamil Nadu and for matters connected therewith;
	
WHEREAS, it has become expedient to notify the list of recognised political parties and the symbols respectively 
reserved for them, the list of registered - unrecognised political parties and the lists of free symbols for the use in the 
local bodies election; and
	
WHEREAS, the Election Commission of India has issued updated notification in connection with the 
names of recognised National and State Parties, registered-unrecognised parties vide Notification No.56/2021/PPS-III, 
Dated 23rd September, 2021 and subsequent notification of political parties by the Election Commission of India:
	
NOW, THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred by paragraph 4 defined as clause (h), (j) and (k) of 
paragraph 2 (1) and paragraph 6 of the Tamil Nadu Local Bodies Election Symbols (Reservation and Allotment) Order, 
2022 and in supersession of the earlier notifications, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission hereby notifies.-
(a)	 In Table 1, the recognised political parties and the symbols respectively reserved for them;
(b)	 In Table 1-A, the list of registered - unrecognised political parties;
(c)	 In Table 2, the free symbols for the use in election of Members of Village Panchayats;
(d)	 In Table 3, the free symbols for the use in election of Presidents of Village Panchayats;
(e)	 In Table 4, the free symbols for the use in election of Members of Panchayat Union Councils, 
Members of Town Panchayats, Councillors of Municipalities and Councillors of Municipal Corporations; and
(f)	
In Table 5, the free symbols for the use in election of Members of District Panchayats.
