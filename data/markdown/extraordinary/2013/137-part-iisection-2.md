HIGHER EDUCATION DEPARTMENT
APPOINTMENT OF CERTAIN PERSON AS VICE-CHANCELLOR OF ANNA UNIVERSITY, CHENNAI,
FOR CERTAIN PERIOD UNDER THE ANNA UNIVERSITY ACT.
[G.O. (D) No. 86, Higher Education ( I 1 ), 27th May 2013,
Vaikasi 13, Vijaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2044.]
No. II(2)/HE/391(c)/2013.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-sections (1) and (3) of Section 11 of the Anna University  Act 1978
(Tamil Nadu Act No.30 of 1978), as amended in Anna University, Chennai (Amendment) Act, 2010 and Tamil Nadu
University Laws (Amendment and Repeal) Act, 2011 (Tamil Nadu Act No.20 of 2011) His excellency the
Governor-Chancellor hereby appoints Dr. M. Rajaram, Professor and Head, Department of Electrical and
Electronics Engineering (PG Studies), Government College of Technology, Coimbatore as Vice-Chancellor,
Anna University, Chennai  for a period of three years from the date of his assuming office.
APURVA VARMA,
Principal Secretary to Government.
