I, K. Rosaiah, Governor of Tamil Nadu accept the recommendation of the Chief Minister that
the following Ministers may be relieved from the Council of Ministers and direct  that  they be
relieved of their duties and responsibilities as Ministers with immediate effect.
1. Tmt. S. Gokula Indira, Minister for Tourism.
2. Thiru N.R. Sivapathy, Minister for School Education, Sports and Youth Welfare,
Law, Courts and Prisons.
3. Dr. V.S. Vijay, Minister for Health.
K. ROSAIAH,
Governor of Tamil Nadu.
