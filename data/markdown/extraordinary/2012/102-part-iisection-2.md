The Governor of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that the
lands specified in the Schedule below have to be acquired for
Industrial Purpose, to wit for the formation of ULTRA MEGA
POWER PROJECT, CHEYYUR and it has already been
decided that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded
for the lands is to be paid out of the funds controlled or
managed by the Officer Authorised Signatory, Coastal Tamil
Nadu Power Ltd., New Delhi and having considered the cause
shown by the owners or other persons having interest the said
lands, the Joint Commissioner (Land Reforms), Villupuram
concerned, the interested party for the lands covered under
Tamil Nadu Land Reforms (fixation of ceiling on land) Act
1961, as the case may be, do hereby publish the following
Notice under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil
Nadu Act 10 of 1999), by relaxing G.O. (Ms.) No. 2954, Revenue,
dated 29-5-1973.

Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999) the Governor of Tamil Nadu
