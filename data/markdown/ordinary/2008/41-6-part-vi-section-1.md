JUDICIAL NOTIFICATIONS
Constitution of Seperate District Munsif Court at Avinashi
(Roc. No. 1876/2008/G/Judn.)
No. VI(1)/356/2008.
In modification of the Notification Nos. VI(1)/651(a-6)/95 to No. VI(1)/651(a-10)/95 published at page 2 under
Part VI—Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated 6th June 1995, the following Notifications
are issued in pursuance of G.O. Ms. No. 1110, Home (Cts.III) Department, dated 27th August 2008 for constitution  of
exclusive separate District Munsif Court, Avinashi:—
NOTIFICATION-I
(Roc. No. 1876/2008/G/Judn.)
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873)
the High Court, Madras, hereby fixes Avinashi in Coimbatore District as the place at which the District Munsif Court,
Avinashi, shall be held.
NOTIFICATION-II
(Roc. No. 1876/2008/G/Judn.)
No. VI(1)/357/2008.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 11 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873)
the High Court, Madras, hereby directs that in Coimbatore District,  the District Munsif Court, Avinashi, shall have and
exercise local jurisdiction over the entire Revenue Taluk of Avinashi with effect from the date on which the District
Munsif, Avinashi assumes charge of that Court.
NOTIFICATION-III
(Roc. No. 1876/2008/G/Judn.)
No. VI(1)/358/2008.
The High Court, Madras hereby directs and notifies that the District Munsif, Avinashi in Coimbatore District shall
exercise all the powers conferred on a District Munsif under Section 12 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873
(Central Act III of 1873) as amended up-to-date in regard to original suits and proceedings not otherwise exempted
from his cognizance of which the amount or value of the subject matter does not exceed One Lakh Rupees within the
local limits of his jurisdiction with effect from the date on which the District Munsif, Avinashi assumes charge
of that Court.
NOTIFICATION-IV
(Roc. No. 1876/2008/G/Judn.)
No. VI(1)/359/2008.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 28 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873)
the High Court, Madras, hereby directs that the District Munsif, Avinashi shall have and exercise  jurisdiction of a Court
of Small Causes under the provincial Small Causes Courts Act, 1887 for the trial suits Cognizable by a Court of Small
Causes upto the pecuniary limits of Rs. 5,000 (Rupees five thousand only) with effect from the date on which the
District Munsif, Avinashi assumes charge of that Court.

[ Part VI—Sec.1
Change of Nomenclature of the Court at Avinashi.
(Roc. No. 1876/2008/G/Judn.)
No. VI(1)/360/2008.
Whereas the Government of Tamil Nadu have issued orders in G.O. Ms. No. 1110, Home (Cts.III) Department,
dated 27th August 2008 that a separate District Munsif Court be constituted at Avinashi and the existing District
Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Avinashi be converted as exclusive Judicial Magistrate Court, Avinashi.
Hence, it is hereby informed that on and from the date on which the District Munsif Court, Avinashi is constituted,
the nomenclature of the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Avinashi shall be changed as the Judicial Magistrate
Court, Avinashi.
High Court, Madras,
R. MALA,
6th October 2008.
Registrar-General.
