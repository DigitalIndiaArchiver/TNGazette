NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
DTP—II-2 Ex. (113)
PUBLIC DEPARTMENT
CONSTITUTION OF THE INDIAN CRIMINAL LAW AMENDMENT ACT, 1908.
[G.O. No. SS.1/121-6/2012, Public (SC), 4th May 2013.]
No.II(2)/PU/352(c) /2013.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 16-B of the Indian Criminal Law
Amendment Act, 1908 (Central Act 14 of 1908) as amended by the Indian Criminal Law Amendment
(Tamil Nadu Amendment) Act, 1998 (Tamil Nadu Act 42 of 1998), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
constitutes for the State, a Tribunal for the purpose of Section 16-A of the said Act. The Tribunal will function
in the premises of the High Court of Madras at Chennai.
JATINDRA NATH SWAIN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
