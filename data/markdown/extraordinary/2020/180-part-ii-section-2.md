	    WHEREAS in the reference eleventh read above, the Government have issued orders extending restrictions in the 
territorial jurisdictions of the State of Tamil Nadu upto 17.05.2020 to contain the further spread of COVID-19.
   NOW THEREFORE, the Government based on the proposal of Principal Secretary/Commissioner of Revenue 
Administration issues following amendment to the GO.Ms.No.217, Revenue and Disaster Management (DM-II) Department, dated 
3.5.2020:
1. Sub-clause (ix) under Clause VI be replaced as  
ix)	 Self-Employed Workers like plumbers, electricians, AC mechanics, carpenters etc., and Home Care Providers for 
persons with special needs, after obtaining necessary permission from Commissioner, GCC/District Collector.
2. Sub-clause (xii) under Clause VII be replaced as  
xii) 
Care givers for persons with special needs, differently–abled, senior citizens and patients after obtaining necessary 
permission from District Collector.
