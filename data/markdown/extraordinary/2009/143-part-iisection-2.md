AGRICULTURE  DEPARTMENT
APPOINTMENT OF CERTAIN PERSON AS VICE-CHANCELLOR OF THE TAMIL NADU
AGRICULTURAL UNIVERSITY, COIMBATORE FOR CERTAIN PERIOD UNDER TAMIL NADU AGRICULTURAL
UNIVERSITY ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 98, Agriculture (AU), 10th June 2009.]
No. II(2)/AG/288(b)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-sections (1) and (3) of Section 11 of the Tamil Nadu Agricultural
University Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 8 of 1971), the Chancellor of the Tamil Nadu Agricultural University hereby
appoints Dr. P. Murugesa Boopathi, Director (Planning and Monitoring), Tamil Nadu Agricultural University,
Coimbatore to be the Vice-Chancellor of the Tamil Nadu Agricultural University, Coimbatore for a period of
three years with effect from the date of his taking charge.
K. NANDA KISHORE,
Agricultural Production Commissioner and
Secretary to Government.
