TAMIL NADU VETERINARY AND ANIMAL SCIENCES UNIVERSITY, CHENNAI.
Nomination of a Member of the Board of Management of Tamil Nadu Veterinary and Animal Sciences 
University.  
(U.O. No. 60003/R.I/BOM/2017-F.No. 17131/R.I/BOM/2017)
No.VI-3(b)/17/2018.
It is hereby notiﬁ ed that as per Section 18(2)(f) under heading CLASS-II OTHER MEMBERS, Chapter IV of 
Tamil Nadu Veterinary and Animal Sciences University (Tamil Nadu Act 42 of 1989) Dr. K.K. Vijayan, Director, Central 
Institute of Brackishwater Aquaculture, 75, Santhome High Road, Chennai-600 028 has been re-nominated by the Director 
General, Indian Council of Agricultural Research, New Delhi to serve as a member in the Board of Management of 
Tamil Nadu Veterinary and Animal Sciences University. His term shall be three years with effect from 21-12-2017 as 
per Section 18(5), Chapter IV of Act, 1989 (Tamil Nadu Act 42 of1989).
