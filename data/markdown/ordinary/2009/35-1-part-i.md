No. I/PUSP/16/2009.—The following Notification of the
Government of India, Ministry of Law and Justice (Department
of Justice), Jaisalmer House, 26, Man Singh Road, New
Delhi-110 011, dated 11th August, 2009 is republished:—
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (1) of
Article 217 of the Constitution of India, the President is pleased
to appoint Shri Justice Prafulla Kumar Misra, Judge of the
Madras High Court, to be the Chief Justice of the
Patna High Court, with effect from the date he assumes
charge of his office.
[No. K.13028/2/2009-US.II]

Joint Secretary to the Government of India.

the recommendation of the High Court, Madras to depute
Thiru S. Ethiraj, Additional District Judge, Fast Track Court

District Judge (Entry Level), on adhoc basis in Tamil Nadu State
Judicial Service to Puducherry Judicial Service for being posted
as III Additional District Judge, Puducherry in the place of
Thiru E.M.K.S. Siddarthar, who has been ordered to be
transferred.
2. 
The 
Government 
accordingly 
direct 
that

III Additional District Judge, Puducherry in the place of
Thiru E.M.K.S. Siddarthar, with effect from the date of assuming
offfice as such.

OBITUARY NOTICE
[G.O. Rt. No. 3461, Public (Special-B), 6th  September 2009,
Aavani 21, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. I/PUSP/18/2009.—The Government of Tamil Nadu record
with deep and profound regret the untimely death of one of its
Senior Officers, Thiru G. Ramamoorthy, I.A.S., Commissioner,
ICDS, Chennai at Bharathiraja Hospital, T. Nagar,
Chennai on 6th September 2009 at the age of
56 years.
2. He was recruited as a Deputy Collector and later promoted
to Indian Administrative Service on 13th December 1999. He
served with distinction in various capacities such as Revenue
Divisional Officer, Ramanathapuram / Hosur, Regional Manager,
TASMAC, Coimbatore, Senior Regional Manager, TNCSC,
Nagercoil, Special Officer, Pudukkottai District Co-op. Spinning
Mills, General Manager, TN Police Housing Corporation, Chennai,
Joint Director, Adi Dravidar Welfare, Chennai, Secretary / PO,
TN Slum Clearance Board, Chennai, Appellate Authority, Land
Tribunal, Thanjavur / Chennai, Joint Secretary to Government,
Agriculture Department, Chennai, Collector, Namakkal / Karur
District, CEO, CMDA, Chennai, Joint Commissioner, Sugars,
Chennai and Commissioner, ICDS, Chennai.
3. In the death of Thiru G. Ramamoorthy, IAS., the
Government of  Tamil Nadu have lost one of its senior officers.
The Government place on record its condolence to the bereaved
family members.
D. JOTHI JAGARAJAN,
Secretary to Government.
