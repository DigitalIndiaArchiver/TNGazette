NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
HOME, PROHIBITION AND EXCISE DEPARTMENT
AMENDMENT TO THE  TAMIL NADU LIQUOR (SUPPLY BY WHOLSALE) RULES, 1983.
[G.O. Ms. No. 63, Home, Prohibition and Excise (VIII), 28th November 2012.]
No. SRO A-35(b)/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Sections 17-C and 17-D read with Section 54 of the Tamil Nadu
Prohibition Act, 1937 (Tamil Nadu Act X of 1937), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following
amendments to the Tamil Nadu Liquor (Supply by Wholesale) Rules, 1983:—
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 10th September 2012.
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in rule 15, in sub-rule (1-A), in the tabular column, in the second column,—
for the expression “224.00” against the entry in   item (i) in the first column,  “420.00” shall be substituted;
R. RAJAGOPAL,
Principal Secretary to Government.
