ALTERATION OF THE LIMITATION OF REGISTRATION SUB-DISTRICT OF TAMBARAM IN THE REGISTRATION DISTRICT OF
CHENNAI (SOUTH) IN THE REVENUE DISTRICT OF KANCHEEPURAM BY EXCLUDING CERTAIN VILLAGES AND FORMS
A NEW REGISTRATION SUB-DISTRICT OF SELAIYUR IN THE REGISTRATION DISTRICT OF CHENNAI (SOUTH) UNDER
THE REGISTRATION ACT.
         [G.O. Ms. No. 9, Commercial Taxes and Regisration (U1), 20th January 2011, Thai 6, Thiruvalluvar Aandu 2042.]

(South) in the Revenue district of Kancheepuram by excluding the following villages from the said Registration  sub-district and
forms a new Registration sub-district of Selaiyur in the said Registration district of Chennai (South) in the said Revenue district
