10 of 1999), the Governor  of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands specified in the Schedule below and measuring 3.11.5
hectares to the same a little more or less needed for Industrial purposes, to wit, for Second  Mine Expansion Scheme of Neyveli
Lignite Corporation Limited.

at any  time during office hours.
