NOTIFICATIONS BY THE ELECTION COMMISSION OF INDIA
NAMES OF MEMBERS NOTIFIED IN RESPECT OF 134. ARAVAKURICHI AND 174.THANJAVUR ASSEMBLY
CONSTITUENCIES AS NEWLY ELECTED.
No. SRO G-43/2016.
The following Notification of the Election Commission of India, New Delhi, dated: 22nd November, 2016 is
published:-
No.308/TN-LA/2016:—Whereas, in pursuance of Notification No. SRO D-1/2016, issued by the Governor of the
State of Tamil Nadu on 22nd April, 2016, under sub-section (2) of the Section 15 of Representation of the People
Act, 1951 (43 of 1951), a General Election was called for the purpose of constituting a new Legislative Assembly for
the State of Tamil Nadu from all assembly constituencies; and
Whereas, the Governor of the State was pleased to rescind his aforesaid notification, partially in respect of
134-Aravakurichi & 174-Thanjavur Assembly Constituencies, on the recommendation of the Commission after the
Commission received reports of distribution of cash and gifts by political parties and contesting candidates in these
two constituencies, and deferred the conduct of fresh elections from the said two constituencies till the atmosphere
becomes conducive to holding of free and fair election; and
Whereas, the election was held as scheduled from all the Assembly Constituencies, except
134-Aravakurichi & 174-Thanjavur Assembly Constituencies, and the results of the elections in all Assembly
Constituencies in the said General Election, except aforesaid two Assembly Constituencies, were declared by
the Returning Officers concerned and the Commission notified the names of members elected from those
constituencies, along with their party affiliation, if any, in the SCHEDULE to the notification vide its notification
No.308/TN-LA/2016, dated: 21st May, 2016; and
Whereas, the Commission subsequently assessed the situation prevailing in the two constituencies and found
appropriate to hold the pending elections from two constituencies mentioned above and accordingly, recommended
to the Governor of the State to call upon the said two constituencies on 26th October, 2016 to elect members from
the said two constituencies; and
