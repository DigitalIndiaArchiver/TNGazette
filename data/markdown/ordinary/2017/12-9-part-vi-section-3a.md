GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI.
Extension of Direct Management of Shafi Madhab Peria Pallivasal Wakf, Sivakasi Taluk and Town,
Virudhunagar District.
(Rc. No.14319/09/B3/VNR)
No. VI-3(a)/25/2017.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995 as amended by
Act 27 of 2013, the period of direct management of Shafi Madhab Peria Pallivasal Wakf, Sivakasi Taluk and Town,
Virudhunagar District is extended for the period of one year from 03-03-2017 to 02-03-2018.
Chennai-600 001,
K. RASHEETH ALI,
15th March 2017.
District Revenue Officer /
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
Extension of Direct Management of Syed Meer Sha Hussaini Tomb, Burial Ground and Mosque Waqf,
Vengundram, Vandavasi Taluk, Thiruvannamalai District.
(Rc. No.11609/04/B7/T.Malai)
No. VI-3(a)/26/2017.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Waqf Act, 1995 as amended by
Act 27 of 2013, the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board to extend the period of direct management of the  above Wakf for the
period of one year from dated 07-08-2016.
Chennai-600 001,
K. RASHEETH ALI,
15th March 2017.
District Revenue Officer /
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Waqf Board.
Extension of Direct Management of Khaderpet Ahle Sunnath Jamath Masjid and Madrasa Waqf,
Tirupur District.
(Rc. No.7488/06/B3/TPR)
No. VI-3(a)/27/2017.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Waqf Act, 1995 as amended by
Act 27 of 2013, the period of direct management of Khaderpet Ahle Sunnath Jamath Masjid and
Madrasa Waqf, Tirupur District, is extended for the period of one year from 18-02-2017 to 17-02-2018.
Chennai-600 001,
K. RASHEETH ALI,
15th March 2017.
