FINANCE  DEPARTMENT
Amendments to Tamil Nadu Pension Rules, 1978
[G.O. No. 404, Finance (Pension), 18th September 2008, Puratasi 2, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2039.]
No. SRO B-63/2008.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu Pension Rules, 1978.
2.  The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st April 2003.
AMENDMENTS.
In the said Rules, in rule 43, in sub-rule (2-A),—
(1) in Clause (a), for the second proviso, the following proviso shall be substituted, namely:—
“Provided further that in the case of a Government servant who retires on or after the 1st April 2003, after
completing qualifying service of not less than thirty years, pension shall be calculated at the rate of fifty per cent of pay
last drawn by the Government servant or fifty percent of average emoluments drawn during the last ten months of service
rendered whichever is higher and the maximum qualifying service to become eligible for full pension shall be thirty years”;
(2) in clause (b), for the proviso, the following proviso shall be substituted, namely:—
“Provided that in the case of a Government servant who retires on or after the 1st April 2003, before
completing qualifying service of thirty years, but after completing qualifying service of ten years, the amount of pension
shall be proportionate to the amount of pension admissible under the second proviso to clause (a).”
K. GNANADESIKAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES DEPARTMENT
Amendment to the Special Rules for Tamil Nadu Industries Service
[G.O. Ms. No. 56, Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (EI-I), 30th September 2008.]
No. SRO B-64/2008.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules Tamil Nadu Industries Service
(Section 19 in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Service Manual, 1970).
2. The amendment hereby made shall come into force with effect on and from the date of issue of this order.
AMENDMENT
In the said Special Rules, in the Annexure referred to in rule 4(b), in item 2, in the third column, against the entry
commencing with the words “Public Relations Officer and ending with the words “Director of Industries and Commerce,
Chennai” in the first column thereof, for the expression “Service for a period of not less than five years”, the expression
“Service for a period of not less than three years” shall be substituted.
P. SELVAM,
Principal Secretary to Government.
