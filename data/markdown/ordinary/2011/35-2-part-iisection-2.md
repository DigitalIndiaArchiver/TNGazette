Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (TN Act
34/2002).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu
Highways Act, 2001 (TN Act 34/2002), the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands specified in the schedule
below measuring 1109 Sq.Mtrs. to the same, a little more
or less required for the purpose of Widening and Strengthening
of Chennai - Ennore Road at Thiruvotriyur of Ward-F, Block-
5, Block-6, Ward-J Block-6, Block-5 and Block-1.
The plan of the lands under acquisition is kept in the
Office of the Special Deputy Collector (Legislative Assembly),
Tamil Nadu Urban Development Project-III, Chennai at
Poonamallee and may be inspected at any time during office
