CONSTITUTION OF SEARCH COMMITTEE FOR SELECTION OF VICE-CHANCELLOR TO THE
TAMIL NADU MUSIC AND FINE ARTS UNIVERSITY UNDER THE TAMIL NADU MUSIC AND FINE ARTS

[G.O. Ms. No. 157, Tourism, Culture and Religious Endowments (C1-1), 14th July 2017, Aani 30,

No. II(2)/TCRE/562(d)/2017.
Under section 11 of the Tamil Nadu Music and Fine Arts University Act, 2013 (Tamil Nadu Act 30 of 2013)
as amended by the Tamil Nadu Music and Fine Arts University (Amendment) Act, 2016 (Tamil Nadu Act 16
of 2016), the Government constitute a Search Committee consisting of the following persons, to submit a
panel of three persons to the Government, so as to enable the Government to forward the panel to the
Hon’ble Chief Minister-Chancellor, for appointment of Vice-Chancellor to the Tamil Nadu Music and Fine Arts
University:-
Dr. Sudha Ragunathan,
Chancellor’s Nominee  /
8/1c, Baskarapuram,
Chairperson
East Abhiramapuram, Chennai–600 004.
Thiru V. Thangavelu, I.A.S. (Retd)
Government Nominee /
16, 7th Cross Street, Indira Nagar,
Member
Adyar, Chennai - 600 020.
Dr. S.P. Thyagarajan,
Syndicate Nominee  /
No 3, 1st Main Road, Nehru Nagar,
Member
Adyar, Chennai - 600 020.
2.  The Deputy Secretary to Government (Culture), Tourism, Culture and Religious Endowments Department
shall be the Convener of the above Search Committee.
3.  The Search Committee is requested to submit a panel of three persons who are eminent academicians
in the field of music, fine arts and performing arts along with their Bio-data to the Government.
APURVA VARMA,
Principal Secretary to Government.
