No. SRO B-34/2013.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Rules issued with the Health and Family
Welfare Department Notification No. SRO B-223/91, published at page 176 of Part III-Section 1 (b) of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 4th September 1996, as subsequently amended.
2. The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 11th September 1995.

substituted;

substituted;

AMENDMENTS
In the Said Rules,-

shall be substituted;
2.
for rule 5, the following rule shall be substituted, namely:-
“5. Qualifications.- (i) No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post  unless he possess the experience
for a period of not less than five years in the post of Multipurpose Health Supervisor (Male) under Multipurpose Health
Workers Scheme;
(ii) Must possess Block Health Supervisor certificate awarded on successful completion of one month in service
training conducted by the Director of Public Health and Preventive Medicine,”;
3. to rule 6, the following proviso shall be added, namely:-
“Provided that there shall be paid to the holder of the post, a monthly pay calculated in the scale of pay of
Rs.9300-34800 with Grade Pay of Rs.4500.”
4. after rule 6, the following rule shall be added, namely:-
“7.  Transfer and Postings.- (i) The holders of the post shall be posted anywhere at Block level in the State;
(ii) The authority competent to transfer the holders of the post shall be the Director of Public Health and Preventive
Medicine.”.
