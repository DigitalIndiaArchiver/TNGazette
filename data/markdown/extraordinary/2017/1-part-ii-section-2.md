The  Government of Tamil Nadu, having been satisfied that
the lands specified in the schedule below have to be acquired
for Industrial purpose, to wit, for the Extension of Runway at
Coimbatore Airport and it having already been decided that the
entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands to
be paid out of the funds controlled or managed by Government.
The following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section
3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of land for Industrial Purposes
Act 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999)
NOTICE
Under sub section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Acquisition of land for Industrial Purposes Act 1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Government of Tamil Nadu
hereby acquires the lands specified in the schedule below and
measuring 45425 Sq.mts (11.22 Acre) to the same, a little
more or less needed for Industrial purpose, to wit, for the
extension of Runway at Coimbatore Airport.
The plan of the lands are kept in the office of the Special
Tahsildar (Land Acquisition) extension of Runway at Coimbatore
Airport. Unit-III, Block – VII,  Village No. 28, Uppillipalayam
Village, Coimbatore South Taluk and may be inspected at any
time during office hours.
