AMENDMENT TO NOTIFICATION
[G.O. Ms. No. 50, Commercial Taxes and Registration (J1), 1st March 2016.]
No.II(2)/CTR/163(c)/2016.
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (a) of sub-section (1) of Section 9 of the Indian Stamp
Act, 1899 (Central Act II of 1899), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment
to the Commercial Taxes and Registration Department Notification No.II(2)/CTR/539(c)/2014 published
in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary No.181, dated the 8th September, 2014:-
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 8th September, 2014.
AMENDMENT
For the expression, “999 square meters of land in Survey Numbers 612/1G, 612/2A, 612/3B, 612/2B,
613/1B, 613/1C, 612/2I and 612/2J in Thiruvalam Road, Katpadi, Vellore.” , appearing in the said notification,
the expression, “954 square meters of land in Survey Numbers 612/1G, 612/2A, 612/3B, 612/2B, 612/1B,
612/1C (part), 613/2K (part), 613/2I and 613/2J at Sri Bhavani Nagar, Dharapadavedu Village, Katpadi, Vellore”,
shall be substituted.
Md. NASIMUDDIN,
Principal Secretary to Government (FAC).
