NOTIFICATIONS   BY  GOVERNMENT
HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE DEPARTMENT
[G.O. (D) No. 558, Health and Family Welfare (IM 1-1),
7th June 2011, Vaikasi 24, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No. I/HF/18/2011.—Under the powers vested in clauses
(a) and (b) of Articles 24 read with Article 25 of the Articles of
Association of the TAMPCOL, Chennai, the Government
appoint Tmt. Girija Vaidyanathan, I.A.S., Principal Secretary
to Government, Health and Family Welfare Department,
Chennai, as Ex-officio Chairperson of the TAMPCOL in the
place of Thiru V.K. Subburaj, I.A.S., who has been transferred.
2. The Government also direct that the equity share of
TAMPCOL standing in the name of Thiru V.K. Subburaj, I.A.S.,
bearing No. 20750 be transferred in favour of
Tmt. Girija Vaidyanathan, I.A.S., Principal Secretary to
Government Health and Family Welfare Department.
GIRIJA VAIDYANATHAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
