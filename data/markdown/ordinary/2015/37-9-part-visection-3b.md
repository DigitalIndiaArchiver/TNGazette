UNIVERSITY AUTHORITIES
MANONMANIAM SUNDARANAR UNIVERSITY, TIRUNELVELI
Election of One Member to the Syndicate
(for the residual term upto 11-12-2016)
[Ref.No.MSU/R/M&E/SEN. to SYN./RESIDUAL/ELEC ’2015.]
No. VI-3(b)/32/2015.
It is hereby notified, by direction, that the Election of ONE MEMBER to the Syndicate by the Members of the Senate from
among themselves not falling under any of the following three categories elected by the Senate as per Chapter-IV
Section 23 (b) Class II-Other Members sub-section (4) of Manonmaniam Sundaranar University Act, 1990 for the residual term
upto 11-12-2016:
1. Principals of Affiliated Colleges
2. Teachers of Affiliated Colleges
3. University Professors
(a) The election will be held in accordance with rules and procedures for conduct of election to the University Authorities
in the Act and Statutes of this University.
(b) Nominations should be made in the prescribed form which may be obtained from the Registrar of the University.
Further, nomination papers shall be enclosed in an envelope superscribed the above election, and sent by Registered Post
(Acknowledgment Due), so as to be received by the  Returning Officer-the Registrar, Manonmaniam Sundaranar University,
Abishekapatti, Tirunelveli-627 012 within the date and hour fixed or delivered either in person or by a messenger to the Returning
Officer within the last date and hour fixed. Nomination papers that are not enclosed in an envelope superscribed and sent by
Registered Post or are not delivered in person or by messenger as required above shall be declared invalid.
(c) An elector shall be eligible to subscribe either as a proposer or as a seconder in nomination papers, only as many
times as there are vacancies but not more.
(d) The following programme is scheduled for the election:
