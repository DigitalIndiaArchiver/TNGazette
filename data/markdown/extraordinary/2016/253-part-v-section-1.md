PUBLIC (ELECTIONS) DEPARTMENT
NOTIFICATION CALLING UPON FOR 134.ARAVAKURICHI AND 174.THANJAVUR ASSEMBLY CONSTITUENCIES IN
THE STATE OF TAMIL NADU TO ELECT MEMBERS
Secretariat, October 26, 2016.
No. SRO D-7/2016
WHEREAS, in accordance  with the provisions of  clause (1)  of Article 172 of the Constitution of India,
the term of   the previous Legislative Assembly  of the  State   of Tamil   Nadu, unless sooner  dissolved, was   to
continue up to and including the 22nd May, 2016 and was to thereafter stand dissolved on the
expiration  of its  duration; and
WHEREAS, in terms of sub-section (1) of   Section 15 of   the Representation of  the People Act,  1951   (43  of  1951),
a  general election was  to  be held  for  the  purpose  of constituting a new  Legislative Assembly of the  State; and
WHEREAS,  in  pursuance of sub-section (2) of  15  of the said  Act,  the Election  Commission   of India recommended
to the  Governor  of the  State of Tamil   Nadu, the  22nd April,   2016  as   the   date   on  which he  may,   by notification
published in  the  official  Gazette  of the  State on  the  said  date, be  pleased to  call  upon   all  Assembly  Constituencies
in  the  State, to  elect members  to  the Legislative Assembly  of the  State, in  accordance with  the provisions  of  the
said Act  and of the rules and order made thereunder; and
WHEREAS,   the   Governor  of the   State   issued Notification  No. SRO  D-1/2016 dated 22nd April, 2016 calling
upon all the Assembly Constituencies in   the State of  Tamil  Nadu to elect members  to   the Legislative  Assembly
of the  State;   and
WHEREAS, the Commission also issued  its notification under Sections 30   and 56   of   the Representation of   the
People Act,   1951 appointing  various dates and   hours   of poll  for the  election,  including 16th May, 2016  as  the
date poll  in the entire   State; and
WHEREAS,  the  election  process from  all the  Assembly   Constituencies, except those of 134-Aravakurichi Assembly
Constituency  and 174-Thanjavur Assembly Constituency   was completed   and   the   Commission’s notification   under
Section 73 of the Act duly constituting the  Assembly  was  also  issued on 21st  May,  2016; and
WHEREAS,   the   Commission,  on  reports  of distribution of cash   and gifts   by   political   parties   and contesting
candidates in   aforesaid   two constituencies, deferred the date of  poll in   the said constituencies, initially  to  23rd
May, 2016   and   subsequently  to  13th June, 2016, and detailed orders  were  passed by the  Commission vide its
order  of even No. dated  15th May, 2016; and

WHEREAS, the  Commission vide  its  further  order   dated   27th May, 2016  came  to  the  conclusion that   the
election process in  the  said  two constituencies could  not  be allowed to proceed and  ought  to be rescinded so that
fresh  elections may  be conducted de-novo when  the  atmosphere becomes conducive to holding of free and  fair election
after  a reasonable lapse  of time; and
WHEREAS, the   Commission recommended to  the  Governor of the State   to partially rescind the  notification issued
by  the  Governor dated 22nd  April, 2016, in  respect  of 134-Aravakurichi Assembly Constituency  and 174-Thanjavur
Assembly Constituency; and
WHEREAS, the  Governor of the  State  was  pleased to rescind his  said notification  dated 22nd April,   2016   partially
in   respect  of  the   said 134-Aravakurichi Assembly Constituency and  174-Thanjavur Assembly Constituency  by  his
notification No. SRO D-5/2016, dated 28th May, 2016;  and
WHEREAS, the  Commission has  assessed the  situation prevailing in the  said  two constituencies and  found  it
appropriate to hold  the  pending elections to the  said  constituencies and  accordingly recommended to the Governor
of the  State,  in pursuance  of sub-section (2) of Section 15 of the said Act, 26th October, 2016  (Wednesday), as the  date
on which  he may, by notification published,  in  the  Official Gazette of the  State   on  the  said date,   be  pleased  to
call  upon   134-Aravakurichi Assembly Constituency  and   174-Thanjavur Assembly Constituencies,   to   elect   members
to   the   Legislative Assembly of  the   State,   in accordance with  the  provisions of the  said  Act and  the  rules  and
orders made  thereunder;
NOW, THEREFORE, the  Governor of the  State  of Tamil  Nadu  is hereby pleased to call upon,   under   sub-section
(2)of  Section 15 of the  said  Act, the said two 134-Aravakurichi and 174-Thanjavur Assembly Constituencies to elect
members  to the  Legislative Assembly  of the  State of Tamil Nadu  in  accordance with the provisions  of  the   said
Act and   the rules   and  orders made thereunder.
(By Order of the Governor)
P. RAMA MOHANA RAO,
