The following Notification of the Government of India, Ministry of Law and Justice,
(Department of Justice), Jaisalmer House, 26, Man Singh Road, New Delhi-110 011,
dated 21st October 2013 is republished:—
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (1) of Article 217 of the Constitution of India, the
President is pleased to appoint Shri Justice Rajesh Kumar Agrawal,  Judge of the Madras High Court,
to be the Chief Justice of the Madras High Court with effect from the date he assumes charge
of his office.
