NOTIFICATION BY THE ELECTION COMMISSION OF INDIA
ELECTION COMMISSION’S NOTIFICATION REGARDING EXIT POLL AT THE BYE-ELECTION TO THE TAMIL NADU
LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY FROM 83. PENNAGARAM ASSEMBLY CONSTITUENCY.
No. SRO G-6/2010.
The following Notification of the Election Commission of India, Nirvachan Sadan, Ashoka Road, New Delhi-110 001,
dated 25th March, 2010 is published in supersession of the Notification published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette
Extraordinary (Issue No. 85), dated 24th March 2010:—
 No. 485/Exit Poll/MCPS/2010.—In pursuance of sub-section (2) of Section 126A of the Representation of
People Act, 1951, the Election Commission of India hereby notifies the hours between 8-00 a.m. and 5-30 p.m. of
27th March, 2010, as the period during which no person shall conduct any Exit Poll and publish or publicize by means of print
or electronic media or disseminate in any other manner, whatsoever, the result of any Exit Poll conducted in respect of
bye-election to Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly from 83. Pennagaram Assembly Constituency.
This will be in supersession of the Commission’s Order No. 509/121/2004/J.S.I, dated 17th February 2009 and any other
order issued in this behalf.
Any person, who contravenes the provision of this Section, shall be punishable with imprisonment for a term which may
extend to 2 years or with fine or with both.
