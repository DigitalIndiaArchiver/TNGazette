NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
ANIMAL HUSBANDRY, DAIRYING AND FISHERIES DEPARTMENT
EXTENSION OF TIME LIMIT FOR THE CONSTITUTION OF ALL THE AUTHORITIES OF THE UNIVERSITY FOR
CERTAIN PERIOD UNDER THE TAMIL NADU FISHERIES UNIVERSITY ACT.
[G.O. (D) No. 41, Animal Husbandry, Dairying and Fisheries (FS-2) 7th February 2014,
Thai 25, Vijaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2045.]
No. II(2)/AHDF/109(a)/2014.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 51 read with Section 18 of Tamil Nadu Fisheries
University Act, 2012 (Tamil Nadu Act No.21 of 2012) the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby extends the time limit for the
constitution of all the authorities of the University specified in section 18 of the said Act within the further period of six months
with effect from the 6th September 2013.
S. VIJAYAKUMAR,
Secretary to Government.
