In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Municipal 
Laws (Fifth Amendment) Act, 2018 (Tamil Nadu Act 40 of 2018), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby 
appoints the 1st day of February 2019, as the date on which the said Act shall come into force.
 
HARMANDER SINGH,
 
Principal Secretary to Government.
