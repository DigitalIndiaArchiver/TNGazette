Under the first proviso to sub-section (1) of section 4 of the  Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for 
Industrial Purposes Act, 1997  (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby 
withdraws of the lands specified in the Schedule below from acquisition in respect of which  notice under
sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the said Act was published vide Notification No. II(2)/IND/133(b-8)/2008 
by the Industries (SIPCOT-LA) Department at  pages 22-23 of Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu 
Government Gazette Extraordinary, No.73, dated the 10th March 2008 and actual possesion of which 
was not taken by, or on behalf of, the Government.
