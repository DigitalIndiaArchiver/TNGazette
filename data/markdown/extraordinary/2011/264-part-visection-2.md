In exercise of the powers conferred under rule 9 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities,
Municipalities and Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006 and in supersession of the Notification No. VI(2)/359(a-3)/2006 published
at Page 4 of Part VI—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 30th August, 2006, the
State Election Commission hereby directs the District Election Officers except in the District of Chennai to appoint within their
respective jurisdictions the Executive Officer of the Town Panchayat or Third Grade Municipality or the Commissioner of
Municipality or City Municipal Corporation respectively or any other officer of the Government as Returning Officer for the
conduct of election to the Town Panchayat or Third Grade Municipality or Municipality or City Municipal Corporation, as the
case may be, under the said Rules.
(By Order of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission)
