Constitution  of Sub-Court at Omalur in Salem District
(Roc. No. 2514/2014/G/Judn.)
No.VI(1)/344/2017.
вҖңIn exercise of the powers conferred under the first proviso to Section 13 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873
(Central Act III of 1873), the High Court, Madras hereby directs that appeals from the decrees or orders of the District
Munsif's Court, Omalur be preferred in the Court of Subordinate Judge at Omalur with effect on and from the date on
which the Subordinate Judge, assumes charge of Subordinate Judge's Court at Omalur."
High Court, Madras,
R. SAKTHIVEL,
14th November 2017.
