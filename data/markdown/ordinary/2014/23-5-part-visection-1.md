Confirmation of Variation to the approved Vetturnimadam South Detailed Development Plan No. IV of
Nagercoil Local Planning Area.
(Roc No. 6235/2010/DP3)
No. VI(1)/225/2014.
In exercise of the powers conferred Under Section 33(2) of Town and Country Planning Act 1971. The Commissioner
of Town and Country Planning, Chennai hereby confirms the following variation to the approved Vetturnimadam South
Detailed Development Plan No. IV of Nagercoil Local Planning Area, approved by the Director of Town and Country Planning
in proceeding Roc. No. 8718/1990/Dl, dated: 29-03-1990 and the fact of this approval in Form No.12 Published in Tamil Nadu
Government Gazette VI(1)1006190, Part VI—Section-1, dated:15-08-1990 and the said draft notification was published in
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No. 49, Part VI—Section-1, Page No. 117 dated: 01-04-2009 Publication No. (VI)(1)/555/06.
2. Since no objections and suggestions have been received on this draft notification within the stipulated time, the same
are here by confirmed and ordered as below.
CONFIRMATION OF VARIATION
1.
The entire Vetturnimadam South Detailed Development plan No. IV of Nagercoil Local Planning Area, approved
by the Director of Town and Country Planning in proceedings Roc. No.3262/2003/DP3, dated: 23-02-2009 is
varied by Vetturnimadam South Detailed Development plan No.-IV, Nagercoil Local Planning Authority.
2.
The plan thus varied is numbered as Map No. 5 DDP(V)/DTCP No. 04/2009.
3.
The Detailed Development plan clauses and schedules annexed with this plan are made enforceable from the
date of publication of this notification in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
Chennai-600 002,
R. VENKATESAN,
20th May 2014.
Commissioner of Town and Country Planning
(In-Charge).

Confirmation of Variation to the approved Arappanancheri Detailed Development Plan No. 1 of Kancheepuram
Local Planning Area.
(Roc No. 836/2010/DP1)
No. VI(1)/226/2014.
In exercise of the powers conferred Under Section 33(2) of Town and Country Planning Act 1971. The Commissioner
of Town and Country Planning, Chennai hereby confirms the following variation to the approved Arappanancheri Detailed
Development Plan No.1, Kancheepuram Local Planning Area, approved by the Commissioner of Town and Country Planning
in proceeding Roc. No. 6670/2001/DPI, dated: 03-11-2003 and the fact of this approval in Form No.12 and Published in Tamil
Nadu Government Gazette No. 11, Part VI—Section-1, Page No.79 and 80 dated: 24-03-2004, in Publication No. VI(1)/91/2004
and the said draft notification was published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No. 39, Part VI—Section-1, Page
No. 299 & 300, dated: 06-10-20I0, Publication No. VI(1)/342/2010.
2. Since no objections and suggestions have been received on this draft notification within the stipulated time, the same
are hereby confirmed and ordered as below.
CONFIRMATION OF VARIATION
1.
The entire Arappanancheri Detailed Development Plan No.1 of Kancheepuram Local Planning Area, approved by
the Commissioner of Town and Country Planning in proceedings Roc. No. 6670/2001/DPI, Dated: 03-11-2003
is varied by Arappanancheri Detailed Development Plan No.1, Kancheepuram Local Planning Area.
2.
The plan thus varied is numbered as Map No. 5, DDP(V)/DTCP No. 31/2010.
3.
The Detailed Development plan clauses and schedules annexed with this plan are made enforceable from the
date of publication of this notification in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
Chennai-600 002,
R. VENKATESAN,
22nd May 2014.
Commissioner of Town and Country Planning
(In-Charge).

Pollachi Local Planning Area.
(Roc No. 8063/2005/DP2)
No. VI(1)/227/2014.
In exercise of the powers conferred Under Section 33(2) of Town and Country Planning Act 1971. The Commissioner of
Town and Country Planning, Chennai here by confirms the following variation to the sanctioned Pollachi Detailed Development
plan No.12 of Pollachi Local planning area, sanctioned by R. Dis. 25810/68D2, dated: 14-4-1969 and G.O.No. 46 Rural
Development and Local Administration dated: 5-1-1971 and published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Part II—Section 2
dated: 17-3-1971 and the said draft notification was published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No.19, Part VI—Section 1,
Page No. 158, dated: 19-05-2010.
2. Since no objections and suggestions have been received on this draft notification with in the stipulated time, the same
are here by confirmed and ordered as below.
CONFIRMATION OF VARIATION
1.
The entire Pollachi Detailed Development plan No. 12 of Pollachi Local Planning Area. Sanctioned by
R. Dis. 25810/68D2, dated: 14-4-1969 and G.O. No. 46, Rural Development and Local Administration
dated: 5-1-1971 is varied by Proceedings of the Commissioner of Town and Country Planning, Chennai-
2 Roc.No. 8063/2005 DP2, dated: 20-4-2010
2.
The plan thus varied is numbered as Map No. 5 DDP(V)/DTCP No. 11/2010.
3.
The Detailed Development plan clauses and schedules annexed with this plan are made enforceable from
the date of publication of this notification in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
Chennai-600 002,
R. VENKATESAN,
