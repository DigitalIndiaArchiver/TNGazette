Appointment of certain persons as Government Members
on the Council of Management of the Victoria Technical
Institute, Chennai for certain period.
[G.O. Ms. No.  24, Handlooms, Handicrafts, Textiles and
Khadi (G2), 20th February 2012, Maasi 8, Thiruvalluvar
Aandu-2043.]
No. II(2)/HHTK/145/2012.—The Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby appoints the following persons as Government Members
on the Council of Management of the Victoria Technical Institute,
Chennai for  a further period of three years from 02-02-2012 to
01-02-2015:—
1.
The Secretary to Government,
Handlooms, Handicrafts, Textiles and
Khadi Department,
Chennai-9.
2.
Director,
Directorate of Industries and Commerce,
Chepauk, Chennai-5.
3.
Chairman and Managing Director,
Tamil Nadu Handicrafts Development
Corporation Limited,
Chennai-2.
C.V. SANKAR,
Principal Secretary to Government
(In-charge).
