APPOINTMENT OF MINISTER FOR THE STATE OF TAMIL NADU
[G.O. Ms. No. 1358, Public (Special-B), 11th December 2013,  Karthigai 25, Vijaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2044.]
No. I/PUSP/26(d-1)/2013.
I, K. Rosaiah, Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoint Thiru R.B. Udhaya Kumar  with effect from the  Forenoon  of
 11th December,  2013,  to  be  the  member  of  my  Council  of  Ministers.
K. ROSAIAH,
Governor of Tamil Nadu.
ALLOCATION OF SUBJECTS AMONG MINISTER
[G.O. Ms. No. 1359, Public (Special-B), 11th December 2013,  Karthigai  25,  Vijaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2044.]
No. I/PUSP/26(d-2)/2013.
Under Clause (3) of Article 166 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby allocates
the subjects  “Youth Welfare and Sports Development Department” to Thiru R.B. Udhaya Kumar,  appointed as
Minister  in the Government Order 6th read above. Consequent on the above allocation,  Thiru R.B. Udhaya Kumar
be designated as Minister for Sports and Youth Welfare.
SHEELA BALAKRISHNAN,
Chief Secretary.
