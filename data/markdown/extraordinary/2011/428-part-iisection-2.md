MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION AND WATER SUPPLY DEPARTMENT
DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF THE TAMIL NADU MUNICIPAL LAWS (AMENDMENT) ACT, 2011.
[G.O.Ms. No. 175, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (Election), 16th November 2011.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/455(a)/2011.
Under sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Municipal Laws (Amendment) Act, 2011
(Tamil Nadu Act 2 of 2011), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the 16th day of November 2011,
as the date on which the said Act shall come into force.
S. KARUTHIAH PANDIAN,
Secretary to Government.
