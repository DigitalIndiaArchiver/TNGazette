UNIVERSITY  AUTHORITIES
BHARATHIDASAN UNIVERSITY, TIRUCHIRAPPALLI
Election to the Syndicate  and Standing Committee on Academic Affairs
(Ref. No. BDU/A2/36562/2016)
No.VI-3(b)/29/2016.
I. Election to the Syndicate :
Class II - Other Members - Sub-Sec. (b) (2) of Section 24 under Chapter IV of Bharathidasan University Act, 1981-
Two Members elected by teachers of affiliated Colleges, other than the Principals, from among themselves who are the members
of the Senate, in accordance with the system of proportional representation by means of a single transferable vote.
Note: One Member to be elected to fill a vacancy (Residual Period - Upto 26-12-2017)
II. Election to the Standing Committee on Academic Affairs
Class II - Other Members - Sub. Sec. (2) of Section 23 Under Chapter III of Bharathidasan University Act, 1981 - Two
members elected by Principals of affiliated Colleges from among themselves in accordance with the system of
proportional representation by means of the single transferable vote.
Note: One Member to be elected to fill a vacancy (Residual Period - Upto 26-12-2017)
 III. Election to the Syndicate
One member, not falling under the first three categories (1), (2) and (3) of Class-II (Other Members) of Section 24(b)
Chapter IV of Bharathidasan University Act 1981, elected by the Senate from among its members.
Nominations are invited in the prescribed form which can be obtained from the Registrar and Returning Officer on
a requisition and submission of a Demand Draft for Rs.500/- (Five Hundred only) drawn in favour of Bharathidasan
University, Tiruchirappalli on or after the date of notification, i.e., 18.11.2016 with a stamped self-addressed envelope to
the value of Rs.25/- (Rupees twenty five only) or in person.
Nomination paper shall be enclosed in an envelope superscribed as follows, as the case may be:-
“Nomination to the ————————————————————by————————————————”
The Members are requested to send their nomination by Registered Post (with Acknowledgement due) so as to
reach the Registrar and Returning Officer within the date and hour fixed or delivered to the Registrar and Returning Officer
either in person of by a messenger within the date and hour fixed.
Nomination papers that are not sent as stated above will be declared invalid.
