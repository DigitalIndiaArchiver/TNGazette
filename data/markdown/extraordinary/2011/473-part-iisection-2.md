RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND PANCHAYAT RAJ DEPARTMENT
NOTIFICATION TO CONDUCT OF SOCIO-ECONOMIC AND CASTE CENSUS IN
TAMIL NADU IN THE YEAR 2011.
[G.O. Ms. No.108, Rural Development and Panchayat Raj (CGS-3),  31st December 2011]
No. II(2)/RDPR/527(c)/2011.
The Government of Tamil Nadu has decided to conduct Socio-Economic and Caste Survey of the
population of Tamil Nadu covering both rural and urban local bodies for the year 2011.  The
Socio-Economic and Caste Census would be carried out by the Government of Tamil Nadu with the financial
and technical support of the Government of India.  In pursuance of this, the Government of Tamil Nadu hereby
declares that the Socio-Economic and Caste Census shall be conducted in Tamil Nadu covering both rural
and urban local bodies from December 2011 till its completion.
N.S. PALANIAPPAN,
Principal Secretary to Government,
