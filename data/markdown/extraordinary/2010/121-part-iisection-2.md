RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND PANCHAYAT RAJ DEPARTMENT
DISQUALIFICATION OF THIRU K. KUTTIYANDI, 3RD WARD MEMBER, PITCHAVARAM PANCHAYAT,
PARANKIPETTAI PANCHAYAT UNION, CUDDALORE DISTRICT UNDER THE TAMIL NADU
PANCHAYATS ACT.
[G.O. (D) No. 196, Rural Development and Panchayat Raj (P.R.IV), 27th April 2010.]
No. II(2)/RDPR/224(b)/2010.
Whereas, the opinion of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission has been sought for, regarding the
disqualification of Thiru. K. Kuttiyandi, 3rd Ward Member, Pitchavaram Village Panchayat, Parankipettai Panchayat
Union, Cuddalore District, as required under sub-section (2) of Section 41 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994
(Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994) as he has absented himself from three consecutive meetings of the said Village
Panchayat;
And, whereas the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission after conducting the enquiry by verifying the register
for intimation of the meeting and agenda of the Pitchavaram Village Panchayat, held on 19th May 2008, 27th June
2008, 15th July 2008 and 15th August 2008, the attendance register for the said meetings, the notice served as
required under sub-section (2) of Section 39 of the said Act and the report furnished by the Village Administrative
Officer, 77, Keelaperambai and 78, Kanakkarapattu, Villages, Chidambaram Taluk, has confirmed that the said
Thiru. K. Kuttiyandi, 3rd Ward Member, Pitchavaram Village Panchayat, Parankipettai Panchayat Union, Cuddalore
District has gone to foreign country and has not attended the three consecutive meetings of the said Village
Panchayat, and opined that the said member is disqualified to continue as Ward Member of the said Pitchavaram
Village Panchayat under clause (i) of sub-section (3) of the Section 38 of the said Act;
Now, therefore, under sub-section (1) of Section 41 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act
21 of 1994), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby decides that Thiru. K. Kuttiyandi, 3rd Ward Member, Pitchavaram
Village Panchayat, Parankipettai Panchayat Union, Cuddalore District, has become disqualified from holding the
office of the 3rd Ward Member of the said Pitchavaram Village Panchayat, under clause (i) of sub-section (3) of
Section 38 of the said Act as he has absented himself from the three consecutive meetings of the said Village
Panchayat held on and after 19th May 2008.
.
K. ASHOK VARDHAN SHETTY,
Principal Secretary to Government.
