UNIVERSITY OF MADRAS
Member Elected to the Academic Council by the Senate
(No.V.1/SE-EL/03-2015/460)
No.VI-3(b)/30/2016.
It is hereby notified that Prof. S.Mahalingam, Former Registrar, Thiruvalluvar University, Vellore, Res.No. 1, Kanagasabai
Colony, Ring Road, Koyambedu, Chennai-600 107 has been unanimously elected to the Academic Council by the
Senate from among its members under Section 23[a]-Other Members [1] of the Madras University Act, 1923 for a period
of three years from 17-10-2016 to 16-10-2019 or till the date of cessation of his membership in the Senate whichever
is earlier.
Election of one Member to the Syndicate by the Senate from among its Members
(No.V.1/SE-EL/03-2015/460)
No.VI-3(b)/31/2016.
I hereby declare that Dr. S.S.Sundaram, M.A.,M.Phil, Ph.D., Professor & Head, Department of Indian History, University
of Madras, Chepauk, Chennai-600 005, has been duly elected to the Syndicate by the Members of the Senate from among
its members under Section 18[a] - Other Members - (1) of the Madras University Act 1923, for the residual period upto
29-03-2018 or till the date of cessation of his membership in the Senate/Academic Council whichever is earlier.
Centenary Building,
DR. P. DAVID JAWAHAR,
Chepauk, Chennai-600 005,
