Declaration of  Premises of ‘Precision Manufacturing Facility’ and ‘Precision Machining Centre’, Units of M/s. Larsen & 
Toubro Limited at Malumichampatti Village, Madhukkarai Taluk, Coimbatore District as “prohibited place” under the 
Ofﬁ cial Secrets Act.
[G.O. No. SS.II/330-14/2016, Public (S.C.), 29th January 2018.]
No. II(1)/PUSC/7/2018.—WHEREAS, in the opinion of the Government of  Tamil Nadu, it is necessary to declare the 
premises of ‘Precision Manufacturing Facility’  and ‘Precision Machining Centre’, Units  of M/s. Larsen & Toubro Limited at 
Malumichampatti Village, Madhukkarai Taluk, Coimbatore District, speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below, to be a “prohibited place” 
under sub-clause (d) of clause (8) of section 2 of the Ofﬁ cial Secrets Act, 1923 (Central Act XIX of 1923), on the ground 
that  information with respect thereto, or the destruction or obstruction thereof, or interference therewith, would be useful to 
an enemy;
AND, WHEREAS, in pursuance of clause (1) of Article 258 of the Constitution of India, the Government of 
India, Ministry of Home Affairs, have, in their Notiﬁ cation No.S.O.No.1285, dated the 4th May 1963, published in the 
Gazette of India, dated the 11th May 1963, entrusted to the Government of Tamil Nadu, the functions of the Central 
Government in relation to any matter  speciﬁ ed in sub-clauses (c) and (d) of clause (8) of Section 2 of the Ofﬁ cial 
Secrets Act, 1923 (Central Act XIX of 1923);
NOW, THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-clause (d) of clause (8) of Section 2 of the Ofﬁ cial 
Secrets Act, 1923 (Central Act XIX of 1923), the Governor of Tamil Nadu  hereby declares, with effect on and from the 
date of publication of this Notiﬁ cation in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, the premises of ‘Precision Manufacturing 
Facility’ and ‘Precision Machining Centre’  Units of M/s. Larsen & Toubro Limited at Malumichampatti Village, Madhukkarai 
Taluk, Coimbatore District, as speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below, to be  a “prohibited place” for the purposes of the said 
Act:-
