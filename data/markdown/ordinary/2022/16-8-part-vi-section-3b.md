Nomination of a member by the Tamil Nadu Veterinary Council to the Board  of Management of Tamil Nadu Veterinary 
and Animal Sciences University
(U.O.No. 60070/R1/2022 – F.No.13792 & 13973/Rl/BOM/2021, dated.2.03.2022)
No. VI-3(b)/8/2022.
It is hereby notified that as per Section 18(2)(b) under heading CLASS-II OTHER MEMBERS of the Act of 
Tamil Nadu Veterinary and Animal Sciences University (Tamil Nadu Act 42 of 1989), the following has been nominated 
by the Government to serve as a member in the Board of Management of Tamil Nadu Veterinary and Animal Sciences 
University under Livestock Farmer Category. His term shall be three years with effect from 22.02.2022 as per Section 
18(5) Chapter IV of Act, 1989 (Tamil Nadu Act of 1989).
