No. SRO B-29/2014.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the
Tamil Nadu Forest Subordinate Services (Section 12 of Part—III-B of the Tamil Nadu Service Manual 1970).
2. The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 5th October 1998.
AMENDEMNTS
In the said Special Rules,—
(1) in rule 1, under class-I, after the category “4. Forest Watcher”, the following category shall be inserted,
namely:—
“4-A Forest Watchers Grade-II”;
(2) in rule 2, in the Tabular Column under sub-rule (a),-
(a) in Column (2); for the entry (2) promotion from among the holders of the post of Forest Watchers; or against
the entry “Forest Guard” in Column (1), thereof, the following entries shall be substituted, namely:—
“(2) Promotion from among the holders of the post of Forest Watchers or Forest Watchers Grade-II;or”
(b) after the entries “Forest Watchers and Mali”, in Column (1), and the corresponding entries in column (2), thereof,
the following entries shall respectively be inserted, namely:—
“Forest Watcher Grade-II
From the post of field man Grade-II in the
Cinchona Department”,.
(3) in rule 3, in the Tabular Column under CLASS-I, for the expression “4. Forest Watcher” in the first column against
the entry “Officers incharge of the Division concerned i.e., Divisional Forest Officer /Working Plan Officer / Wildlife
Warden / Forest Utilization Officer, as the case may be “in the second column thereof, the expresiion “4. Forest Watcher and
4-a Forest Watcher Grade-II shall be substituted;
(4) in the Table, under rule 5, for the entry “(ii) Promotion of Forest Watcher” in column -2, against the category “Forest
Guard” in column 1 thereof, the following entry shall be substituted namely:—
(ii) “Promotion of Forest Watcher or Forest Watcher Grade-II”;
(5)  in rule 13, for the expression “Forest Watchers”, the expression, “Forest Watchers, Forest Watchers
Grade-II shall be substituted.
MOHAN VERGHESE CHUNKATH,
Additional Chief Secretary to Government.

No. SRO B-30/2014.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Basic Service
(Section 19 in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1970).
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 2nd February, 1977.
AMENDMENT
In the said Special Rules, in rule 1, Class IV, after the entry “Category 6 - Mazdoors”, the entry “Category 6A-
Field Workers in Medical Department” shall be inserted.
