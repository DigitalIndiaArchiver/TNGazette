Under section 28-A of the Representation of the People Act, 1951 (Central Act XLIII of 1951),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby declares the Director General of Police, Additional Director
General of Police (Law and Order), Inspectors General of Police, Central zone and South zone,
Deputy Inspectors General of Police, Trichy, Thanjavur and Madurai Ranges, Superintendents
of Police, Karur, Thanjavur and Madurai, Commissioner of Police, Madurai and all Police
Officials subordinate to them and up to the level of Police Constables and any other Police
officers/officials performing in any way the election related duties for the conduct of Elections/
Bye-Election to be held on the 19th November 2016 to the Legislative Assembly of this State
from 134.Aravakurichi, 174.Thanjavur and 195.Thiruparankundram Assembly Constituencies,
as Designated Officers and all the said officers shall be deemed to be on deputation to the
Election Commission for the period commencing on and from the date of the notification calling
for the said Elections and ending with the date of declaration of the result of the said Elections
and accordingly, the said officers shall, during that period, be subject to the control,
superintendence and discipline of the Election Commission.
APURVA VARMA
 
Principal Secretary to Government.
