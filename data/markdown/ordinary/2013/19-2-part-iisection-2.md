No.II(2)/HO/380/2013.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by clause (1) of Section 20 of the Tamil Nadu
Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1974 (Tamil Nadu Act 13 of

in rate with effect on and from 23rd April 2013 in regard
to the tax payable in respect of the motor vehicle bearing
Registration No. TN 20 AJ 8380 belonging  to the Cancer
Institute, Chennai-600 020 to one fourth of the normal rate
of tax payable in respect of the said Motor Vehicle for a
period of 5 years as long as the said vehicle is owned by
the said Institute and used for the purpose associated
with the said Institute without letting it for hire or reward.
