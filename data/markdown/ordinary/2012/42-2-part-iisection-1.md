REVENUE DEPARTMENT
Notification Under Tamil Nadu Land Reforms (Fixation of Ceiling on Land) Act.
Holding of Thiru K.K. Muthiah
Modification to Notification
[G.O. Ms. No. 351, Revenue [L.R-I(2)], 9th October 2012.]
No. II(1)/REV/27/2012.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 18-C of the Tamil Nadu Land Reforms (Fixation
of Ceiling on Land) Act, 1961 (Tamil Nadu Act 58 of 1961), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following modifications
to the Revenue Department Notification No.II(1)/REV/52(a)/2003, dated the 14th November 2003, published at pages 1-9 of
Part II-Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 14th November 2003.
MODIFICATIONS
In the said Notification, in the Schedule,
1.
Serial No. 48 in column (1) and the corresponding entries in Columns (2) to (8) thereof shall be omitted;
2.
for the entry “672.92 Ordinary acres equivalent to 225.618 standard acres”, the entry “671.21 ordinary acres equivalent
to 225.193 standard acres” shall be substituted.
K. GNANADESIKAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
