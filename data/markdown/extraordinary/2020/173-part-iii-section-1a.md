NOTIFICATION UNDER THE TAMIL NADU GOODS AND SERVICES TAX ACT, 2017
[G.O. Ms. No.82, Commercial Taxes and Registration (B1), 29th April 2020,  
Chithirai 16, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2051.]
No. SRO A-17(c)/2020.
In exercise of the powers conferred by section 164 of the Tamil Nadu Goods and Services Tax Act, 2017 
(Tamil Nadu Act 19 of 2017) read with clause (c) of rule 9 and rule 25 of the Tamil Nadu Goods and Services Tax 
(Fourth Amendment) Rules, 2019 (hereinafter referred to as the rules), made vide the Commercial Taxes and 
Registration Department Notification No.SRO A-20(a)/2019,  published  at pages 1-12 in Part III-Section 1(a) of the 
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, Extraordinary, dated the 28th June, 2019, the Government, hereby appoints the 
21st day of April, 2020, as the date from which the said provisions of the  rules, shall come into force.
	
	
	
N. MURUGANANDAM,
	
	
	
Principal Secretary to Government (Fac).
