No. SRO A-7/2015.—In exercise of the powers conferred by rule 130 of the Tamil Nadu Juvenile Justice (Care and
Protection of Children) Rules, 2001 the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby forms the District Advisory Committees in all
Districts to perform the role of inspecting the programmes and activities for the effective implementation of the Juvenile
Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2000 (Central Act 56 of 2000) with the following members:—

2. The Government also order that the above District Advisory Committees shall also function as the “District Child
Protection Committee” to monitor the implementation of Integrated Child Protection Scheme in the Districts on the basis of
District specific indicators which will be developed in the process of the Scheme as per the guidelines of Integrated Child
Protection Scheme and whenever the District Advisory Committee is convened to review the implementation of Integrated

3. The Committee shall meet as and when required and at least once in three months to monitor the implementation of
Integrated Child Protection Scheme at District level. The District Child Protection Officer shall be responsible for convening the
Meeting of this Committee and shall send the Minutes of the meetings to the Director of Social Defence periodically. He shall also
communicate the copy of the minutes to the department officers concerned for follow up action wherever necessary.
