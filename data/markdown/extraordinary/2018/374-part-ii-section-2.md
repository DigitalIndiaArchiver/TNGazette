The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied 
that the lands specified in the schedule below have to be 
acquired for industrial purpose to wit for the formation of 
New Broad Gauge Railway line between Tindivanam-Nagari 
and it having already been decided that the entire amount of 
compensation to be awarded for the lands are to be paid out of 
the lands controlled or managed by Government. The following 
notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the 
Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 
1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu 
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu 
hereby acquires the lands specified in the Schedule below 
and measuring 5.09.74 hectares to the same, a little more or 
less needed for Industrial purposes, to wit, for the formation of 
New Broad Gauge Railway line between Tindivanam-Nagari.
The plan of the lands are kept in the office of the 
Tahsildar, WalajahTaluk and may be inspected at any time 
during office hours.
Vellore District, Walajah Taluk, Thazhanur Village.
