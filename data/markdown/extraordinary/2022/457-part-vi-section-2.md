WHEREAS, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission, inter-alia, notiﬁ ed the Schedule of Programme of Election 
to Kadambur Town Panchayat, Thoothukudi District scheduled to be held on 19.2.2022 vide Notiﬁ cation ﬁ rst read above;
WHEREAS, as per the Schedule of Programme of Election,  the last date for making nominations was completed 
on 4.2.2022 and the date of scrutiny of nominations was  held on 5.2.2022;
WHEREAS, during the process of election, the District Election Oﬃ  cer/District Collector, Thoothukudi District, due 
to prevailing situation, recommended the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission to cancel the election and the Election 
Observer, Thoothukudi District, also recommended to cancel the existing electoral process, by agreeing the report of the 
District Election Oﬃ  cer/District Collector, Thoothukudi District. Based on which, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission 
has issued orders, vide reference second read above, by cancelling all the proceedings of the Returning Oﬃ  cer and 
the Assistant Returning Oﬃ  cers of Kadambur Town Panchayat, Thoothukudi District.

WHEREAS, aggrieved over the orders of cancellation, the contesting candidates Thiru. S.V.S.P. Nagaraja 
and two others  have ﬁ led Writ Petitions in the Hon’ble High Court of Madras; WHEREAS, after hearing 
the arguments, the Hon’ble High Court of Madras has,  in its order third read above, quashed the 
order and directed as follows:-
“  xxx    xxx xxx
17. In the Result :
(i)
The Writ Petitions are allowed and the impugned order stands quashed;
(ii)
The petitioners shall be treated as unopposed in respect  of the three wards namely, 
Ward Nos. 1, 2 and 11 and shall be notiﬁ ed as having been elected unopposed 
in accordance with law;
(iii)
It will be open for the respondents to notify the date for election in  respect of 
the other wards in respect of the Kadambur Town Panchayat based on the already 
existing valid nominations and complete the  election expeditiously in any event not 
later than a period of two months from the date of receipt of copy of the order”.
 
  
WHEREAS, in view of the above orders of the Hon’ble High Court of Madras and to comply the orders of the 
Court, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission has decided to issue notiﬁ cation with regard to the election of the 
above Writ Petitioners, i.e., the contesting candidates, as having been elected unopposed in accordance with law in 
respect of Ward No. 1, 2 and 11  of Kadambur Town Panchayat  and the date of elections also  to elect the ward 
members  in respect of other wards of Kadambur Town Panchayat, Thoothukudi District;
WHEREAS,  the District Election Oﬃ  cer/District Collector, Thoothukudi in his  Letter No. வ/10-27535/2021 dated: 
12/9/2022 has now stated that as per the schedule of programme of  election of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission 
ﬁ rst read above,  totally 33  nominations were received upto the last date of making nominations i.e., 4.2.2022 for the 
elections to all the 12 wards of Kadambur Town Panchayat, Thoothukudi District, of which, 6 nominations  were rejected 
and  list of validly nominated candidates in Form 6 for the elections to Ward No. 1 to Ward No. 12 was published by 
the Returning Oﬃ  cer concerned; 
WHEREAS, the District Election Oﬃ  cer/District Collector, Thoothukudi  District further stated that,  on the day 
of withdrawal, i.e., 7.2.2022, one nomination ﬁ led by a candidate in Ward No. 9 has been withdrawn and the same 
was aﬃ  xed in the notice board of the Oﬃ  ce of the Kadambur Town Panchayat by the Assistant Returning Oﬃ  cer in 
Form 8 on the same day;  
WHEREAS, the District Election Oﬃ  cer/District Collector, Thoothukudi District has also stated that in respect of 
Ward No. 1, Ward No. 2 and Ward No. 11, only one candidate’s nomination  for each ward is found valid and that 
on the same day, a distinctive symbol to each contesting candidate assigned to the remaining 23 candidates in respect 
of other nine (9) wards and the same was  informed to them with proper acknowledgement and  supplied with a 
specimen thereof and further also stated that, due to prevailed extra-ordinary situation, the list of contesting candidates in 
Form 9 has not been completed and not published as per sub-rule (4) of Rule 33 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, 
Municipalities and Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006; 
NOW, THEREFORE, as ordered  by the Hon’ble High Court of Madras in the reference third read above,  for the 
purpose of ﬁ lling up of  ordinary vacancies, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission,-
(i) directs the Returning Oﬃ  cer of Kadambur Town Panchayat to publish and notify the contesting candidates, i.e., 
the Writ Petitioners (1) Thiru. S.V.S.P. Nagaraja, S/o. Thiru. S.V.S. Pandian, (2) Tmt. Rajeshwari, W/o. Thiru. Nagaraja 
and (3) Thiru. V. Siva Kumar S/o. Thiru. Vellapandian as having been elected unopposed in accordance with law, 
in respect of wards namely Ward No. 1, 2 and 11 respectively; 
(ii) in exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (1) of Section 43-B of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities 
Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920) and rule 22 and 139 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Municipalities and 
Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006, directs the Returning Oﬃ  cer of Kadambur Town Panchayat in respect of the other 
Wards (Ward No. 3,4,5,6,7,8,9,10,12) of Kadambur Town Panchayat, Thoothukudi District, to publish the day so appointed 
and to commence the election proceedings in pursuance of sub rule (4) of Rule 33 of the said Rules and complete 
the process of election in accordance with law and that the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission  hereby calls upon 
the electors of the said wards of Kadambur Town Panchayat to elect their respective Ward Members, in accordance 
with the following programme of election:-
