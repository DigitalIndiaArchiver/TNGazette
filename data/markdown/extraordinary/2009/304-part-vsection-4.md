NOTIFICATIONS BY THE ELECTION COMMISSION OF INDIA.
APPOINTMENT OF DATES FOR VARIOUS STAGES OF BYE-ELECTION TO TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY FROM
225. TIRUCHENDUR ASSEMBLY CONSTITUENCY AND FIXATION OF HOURS OF POLL TO FILL  A VACANCY CAUSED
BY THE RESIGNATION OF A MEMBER.
No. SRO G-44/2009.
The following Notification of the Election Commission of India, Nirvachan Sadan, Ashoka Road, New Delhi-110 001,
dated: 25th November, 2009 [4 Agrahayana, 1931 (Saka)] is published:—
No. 100/TN-LA/6/2009.—Whereas, the seat of Shri. Anitha R. Radhakrishnan, in the Legislative Assembly of the
Tamil Nadu State, elected from 225. Tiruchendur Assembly Constituency, has become vacant on 31-07-2009, by reason
of his resignation; and
2.
Whereas, a bye-election is to be held for the purpose of filling the vacancy so caused;
3.
Now, therefore, in pursuance of sub-section (1) of Section 150, 30 and 56 of the Representation of the People
Act, 1951 (43 of 1951), the Election Commission of India hereby—
(A) Calls upon the said 225. Tiruchendur Assembly Constituency in the State of Tamil Nadu to elect, before the
31st December, 2009 (Thursday) and in accordance with the provisions of the said Act and of the rules and orders made
thereunder, a person, for the purpose of filling the said vacancy in the Legislative Assembly of the State; and
(B) Appoints, with respect to the said election,—
(a)
the 2nd December 2009 (Wednesday) as the last date for making nominations;
(b)
the 3rd December 2009 (Thursday) as the date for the scrutiny of nominations;
(c)
the 5th December 2009 (Saturday) as the last date for the withdrawal of candidatures;
(d)
the 19th December 2009 (Saturday) as the date, on which a poll shall, if necessary, be taken;
(e)
the 31st December 2009 (Thursday) as the date before which the election shall be completed; and

APPOINTMENT OF DATES FOR VARIOUS STAGES OF BYE-ELECTION TO TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY FROM
51. VANDAVASI (SC) ASSEMBLY CONSTITUENCY AND FIXATION OF HOURS OF POLL TO FILL A VACANCY CAUSED
BY THE DEATH OF A MEMBER.
No. SRO G-45/2009.
The following Notification of the Election Commission of India, Nirvachan Sadan, Ashoka Road, New Delhi-110 001,
dated: 25th November, 2009 [4 Agrahayana, 1931 (Saka)] is published:—
No. 100/TN-LA/7/2009.—Whereas, the seat of Shri S.P. Jayaraman, in the Legislative Assembly of the
Tamil Nadu State, elected from 51. Vandavasi (SC) Assembly Constituency has become vacant on 02-11-2009, by reason
of his death; and
2.
Whereas, a bye-election is to be held for the purpose of filling the vacancy so caused;
3.
Now, therefore, in pursuance of sub-section (1) of Section 150, 30 and 56 of the Representation of the People
Act, 1951 (43 of 1951), the Election Commission of India hereby—
(A) Calls upon the 51. Vandavasi (SC) Assembly Constituency in the State of Tamil Nadu to elect, before the
31st December, 2009 (Thursday) and in accordance with the provisions of the said Act and of the rules and orders made
thereunder, a person, belonging to any of the Scheduled Castes of the State for the purpose of filling the said vacancy
in the Legislative Assembly of the State; and
(B) Appoints, with respect to the said election,—
(a)
the 2nd December 2009 (Wednesday) as the last date for making nominations;
(b)
the 3rd December 2009 (Thursday) as the date for the scrutiny of nominations;
(c)
the 5th December 2009 (Saturday) as the last date for the withdrawal of candidatures;
(d)
the 19th December 2009 (Saturday) as the date, on which a poll shall, if necessary, be taken;
(e)
the 31st December 2009 (Thursday) as the date before which the election shall be completed, and
(C) Fixes the hours from 8.00 A.M. to 5.00 P.M. as the hours during which the poll shall, if necessary, be taken
on the date specified above, for the election.
