HOME, PROHIBITION AND EXCISE DEPARTMENT
Amendment to Notification under Tamil Nadu Prohibition Act
[G.O. Ms. No. 21, Home, Prohibition and Excise (III), 26th April 2010, Chithirai 13, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No. II(1)/HOPE/7/2010.—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 18-B of the Tamil Nadu Prohibition
Act, 1937 (Tamil Nadu Act X of 1937), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Prohibition
and Excise Department Notification No. II(1)/PE/239(d)/81, published at page 2 of Part II—Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Government
Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 1st May 1981, as subsequently amended:—
AMENDMENT
In the said Notification, in the Annexure,
Under the heading “PREMIUM BRANDS”, under the sub-heading “Thiruvalargal Shiva Distilleries Limited”,
the following entries shall be omitted, namely:—
Royal Stag Classic Whisky
Three Kings Classic Reserve Brandy
Imperial Blue Classic Whisky.
S. MALATHI,
Principal Secretary to Government.
PUBLIC DEPARTMENT
(S.C.)
Declaration of the terminal of Indian Oil Corporation Limited at Mullakadu Part-I Village, Thoothukudi Taluk,
Thoothukudi District as a “Prohibited Place” under the Official Secrets Act.
[G.O. No. SS.II/ 363-8/2009, Public (S.C.), 30th April 2010.]
No. II(1)/PUSC/8/2010.—Whereas, in the opinion of the Government of Tamil Nadu, it is necessary to declare the terminal
of Indian Oil Corporation Limited at Mullakadu Part-I Village, Thoothukudi Taluk, Thoothukudi District as  specified in the Schedule
below to be a "Prohibited Place" under sub-clause (d) of clause (8) of Section 2 of the Official Secrets Act, 1923 (Central
Act XIX of 1923), on the  ground that information with respect thereto, or the destruction or obstruction thereof, or interference
therewith, would be useful to an enemy;
And, whereas, in pursuance of clause (1) of Article 258 of the Constitution of India, the Government of India, Ministry of
Home Affairs, have, in their Notification No. S.O. 1285, dated the 4th May 1963, published in the Gazette of India, dated the
11th May 1963, entrusted to the Government of Tamil Nadu the functions of the Central Government in relation to any matter
specified in sub-clauses (c) and (d) of clause (8) of Section 2 of the Official Secrets Act, 1923 (Central Act XIX of 1923);
Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-clause (d) of clause (8) of Section 2 of the Official Secrets
Act, 1923 (Central Act XIX of 1923), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby declares, with effect on and from the date of publication
of this Notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, the terminal of Indian Oil Corporation Limited at Mullakadu Part-I Village,
Thoothukudi Taluk, Thoothukudi District as specified in the Schedule below, to be a “Prohibited Place” for the purposes of
the said Act:—
