is issued to the Ulemas Pension (Tamil Nadu) Scheme, 1981,
published with Commercial Taxes and Religious Endowment
Department Notification No. II(2)/CTRE/2492(1-15)/81,

Notifications of the Tamil Nadu Protection of Interests
of Depositors (in Financial Establishments) Act.
Messers Future Forex India Private Limited,
Perambalur District.
No. II(2)/HO/749/2012.—[Tamil Nadu Protection of Interests
of Depositors (in Financial Establishments) Act, 1997 (Tamil
Nadu Act 44 of 1997)-Messers Future Forex India Private
Limited, Perambalur District-Default in return of deposits by
the financial establishment-Ad-interim order attaching the
movable properties of the said financial establishment under
section 3 of the said Act-Orders-Issued.]
The  following  Government orders published:—
[G.O.Ms.No. 805, Home (Police XIX), 17th October 2012.]
READ:
1.
G.O. Ms. No. 836, Home (Police XIX) Department,
dated 12th December 2011.
2.
From the Additional Director General of Police,
Economic Offences Wing-II, Chennai Letter No.
C1/13032/2011, dated 24th January 2012 and Letter
even No. dated 1st June 2012.
Order: No. 805, Home (Police XIX) 17th October 2012.
Whereas complaints have been received from a number
of depositors that Messers Future Forex India Private Limited,
Perambalur District, a financial establishment has defaulted
the return of deposits made by the depositors after maturity;
And  Whereas, the Government are satisfied that the said
financial establishment is not likely to return the deposits to
the depositors and hence, the Government have to protect
the interests of such depositors;
And Whereas, in the Government order first read above,
the Government have already made an ad-interim order under
section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Protection of Interests of
Depositors (in Financial Establishments) Act, 1997 (Tamil
Nadu Act 44 of 1997) attaching certain immovable properties
of Thiru. K. Siva Kumar, owner and others of the said financial
establishment, as specified in the schedule thereto, and the
said properties are not sufficient for repayment of the
depositors;
And Whereas the movable properties specified in the
Schedule to this Order are alleged to have been procured by
the said financial establishment from and out of the deposits
collected from the depositors.
Now, Therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by
section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Protection of Interests of
Depositors (in Financial Establishments) Act, 1997 (Tamil
Nadu Act 44 of 1997), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
makes an ad-interim order attaching the movable properties
or Messers Future Forex India Private Limited, Perambalur
District as specified in the Schedule to this Order, and transfer
the  control over the said movable properties to the Competent
Authority, namely, the Competent Authority and District
Revenue Officer, Perambalur District appointed under the
said Act, for the purpose in addition to the properties ordered
to be attached in the Government Order first read above.
2. The Competent Authority, is requested to pursue further
action in accordance with the procedure laid down in sub-
sections (3) and (4) of Section 4 of the said Act and also the
formalities prescribed in the Tamil Nadu Protection of interests
of Depositors (in Financial Establishments) Rules, 1997.
3. The Special Public Prosecutor, Special Court for the
Tamil Nadu Protection of Interests of Depositors (in Financial
Establishments) Act, 1997, (Tamil Nadu Act 44 of 1997),
Chennai is requested to render necessary assistance to the
Competent Authority in filling the application before the said
Special Court.
(By order of the Governor)

The following Government Order is published:—
[G.O. Ms. No. 822, Home (Police XIX), 19th October 2012.]
READ:
From the Inspector General of Police,
Economic Offences Wing-II, Chennai,
Letter Rc. No. C1/450/2012, dated
22nd June 2012.
Order: No. 822, Home (Police XIX), 17th October 2012.
Whereas, complaints have been received from a number
of depositors that Tycoons Empire International Limited,
Chennai,  a Financial Establishment has defaulted the return
of deposits made by the depositors after maturity;
And, whereas, the Government are satisfied that the said
Financial Establishment is not likely to return the deposits
to the depositors and hence, the Government have to protect
the interests of such depositors;
And whereas, the immovable properties has specified in
the Schedule to this order is alleged to have been procured
by the said Financial Establishments from and out of the
deposits collected from the depositors;
Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by
Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Protection of Interests of
Depositors (in Financial Establishments) Act, 1997 (Tamil
Nadu Act 44 of 1997), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
makes an ad-interim order attaching the immovable properties
standing in the name of one of the partners of Tycoons
Empire International Limited,  Chennai, as specified in the
Schedule to this order and transfers the control over the said
Immpvable properties to the Competent Authority, namely,
the Competent Authority and District Revenue Officer, Chennai
appointed under the said Act.
2. The Competent Authority Chennai is requested to
pursue further action in accordance with the procedure laid
down in sub-sections (3) and (4) of Section 4 of the said
Act and also the formalities prescribed in the Tamil Nadu
Protection of Interests of Depositors (in Financial
Establishments) Rules, 1997.
3.
The Special Public Prosecutor, Special Court for the
Tamil Nadu Protection of Interests of Depositors (in Financial
Establishments) Act, 1997 Chennai-600 104 is requested to
render necessary assistance to the Competent Authority in
filling the application before the said Special Court.
(By order of the Governor)
