GENERAL NOTIFICATION
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI
Taking over the Direct Management of Sandaipettai Jamia Pallivasal, Kaveripattinam, Krishnagiri District.
(Rc. No. 10732/05/B6/Krishnagiri.)
No. VI-3(a)/115/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995, the Tamil Nadu
Wakf Board in its resolution in Item No. 141/10 in (Rc. No. 10732/05/B6/K.Giri), dated 8th July 2010 has ordered to
take over the Management of Sandaipettai Jamia Pallivasal, Kaveripattinam, Krishnagiri District under the Direct Control
of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of one year from the date of taking over the charge of the above Wakf
by the Superintendent of Wakfs, Salem, who has been appointed as Executive Officer of the above Wakf.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
4th August 2010.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
