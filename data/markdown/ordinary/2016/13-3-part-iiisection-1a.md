No. SRO A-7/2016.вҖ”The following draft amendment to the Inter-State Migrant Workmen (Regulation of Employment
and Conditions of Service) (Tamil Nadu) Rules, 1983, which it is proposed to make in exercise of the powers conferred
by sub-section (1) of Section 35 of the Inter-State Migrant Workmen (Regulation of Employment and Conditions of
Service) Act, 1979 (Central Act 30 of 1979) is hereby published as required by the said sub-section (1) of Section 35
of the said Act.
2. Notice is hereby given that the draft amendment, will be taken into consideration on or after the expiry of the
period of one month from the date of publication of this Notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette. Any
objection or suggestion which may be received from any person with respect thereto before the expiry of the aforesaid
period will be considered by the Government of Tamil Nadu. Objections or suggestions, if any, should be
addressed to the Principal Secretary to Government, Labour and Employment Department, Secretariat, Chennai-600 009,
through the Commissioner of Labour, Chennai - 600 006.
