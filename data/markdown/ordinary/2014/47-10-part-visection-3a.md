GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI
Taking Over of Tirupur Khaderpet Ahle Sunnath Jamath Masjid and Madrasa Wakf, Tirupur District, under
Direct Management [(G.S. 342/TPR (CBE).]
(Rc. No. 7488/06/B3/ TPR & WEA.2/14/E5/TPR.)
No. VI-3(a)/119/2014.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under section 65(1) of the Waqf Act 1995, as
amended by Act 27/2013 the Tamil Nadu Waqf Board in its resolution in item No.76/14 & 102/14 in
RC. No. 7488/06/B3/TPR & WEA.2/14/E5/TPR dated: 26.11.2014 has ordered to take over the Tirupur, Khaderpet Ahle
Sunnath Jamath Masjid and Madrasa Wakf, Tirupur District under the direct Management of the Tamil Nadu Waqf
Board for a period of one year from the date of resolution dated 26.11.2014 and to appoint the Waqf Inspector,
Tirupur as its Executive Officer.
A. MOHAMED MEERAN,
District Revenue Officer /
Chennai-600 001,
Chief Executive Officer (In-charge),
8th December 2014.
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
