Election of a nominee by the Senate to the Committee for recommending a panel of three names to the
Chancellor for appointment of the Vice-Chancellor.
(Ref.No. ME2/ VC-P/S.Nom./Election/2015)
No. VI-3(b)/9/2015.
It is hereby notified that, according to Statute 5 of Chapter V of the laws of the University, Volume-I Calendar of the University,
an election will be held for nominating a person by the Senate by the University to serve on the Committee of three, who are to
suggest a panel of three names to the Chancellor for appointment of the Vice-Chancellor.  The election will be held in accordance
with the Statutes of Chapter V and the rules relating to the elections to the authorities of the University.  Each member of the Senate
shall have a right to nominate not more than one person who is not a member of any of the authorities of the University to the
Committee.  Every nomination shall be in writing signed by the proposer who shall be a member of the Senate and Seconded in
writing by another member of the Senate called the seconder in the form prescribed.  The form prescribed may be obtained from
the Registrar on a written requisition signed by any member of the Senate.  No nomination shall be valid unless it contains the
signature of both the proposer and seconder and the nomination shall also be signed by the person proposed expressing his
consent to be nominated.  Every nomination must be presented before the Registrar either by the proposer or the seconder or
the person proposed before 3.00 p.m. on Thursday the   12th March, 2015.
The scrutiny of nomination will take place at 3.15 p.m.  on Thursday the  12th March, 2015 at the Office of the
Registrar, Madurai Kamaraj University, Madurai - 625 021. The proposer or the seconder or the proposed nominee can be
present at the time of the scrutiny.
