Midwives Act III of 1926 and amended XXVI (26) of 1960.
(Ref. No.432/NC/2017)
No.VI(1)/190/2017.
Whereas vacancy has occurred in the membership of the Tamil Nadu Nurses and Midwives  Act of 1926
(Madras  Act, III of 1926) and Amended Act, XXVI (26) of 1960 and on election has to be held under section 3(1)(i) and 3(1)(j) of
the said Act as Subsequently amended XXVI (26) of 1960.
Now, therefore, in pursuance of Rule 4 of the rules framed by the Local Government under Act, the Returning Officer
Tamil Nadu Nurses and Midwives Council calls upon the registered members of the Training  Nurses  Association of  India
(Tamil Nadu Branch) 3 (1) (i) and Christian Medical  Association of India (South India Branch) 3 (1)(j) as per the list published in the
Council Office Notice Board to elect in accordance with the said  rules a registered candidate for the purpose of filling said vacancy
on order before the 15.08.2017.
