Confirmation of Comprehensive Variation to the Approved Veerappanchatram Detailed Development
Plan No. 11 of Erode Local Planning Area.
(Roc. No. 15867/2009/DPI.)
No. VI(1)/286/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section 2 of Section 33 of Town and Country Planning Act, 1971,
the Director of Town and Country Planning, Chennai hereby confirms the following comprehensive variation to the
approved Veerappanchatram Detailed Development Plan No. 11 of Erode Local Planning Area approved by the Director
of Town and Country Planning Proceedings in Roc. No. 33714/94, DP2, dated 4th September 1994 and published in
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, No. 43, Part VI—Section 1, Page Nos. 1079-1080 dated 9th November 1994 in
publication No. VI(1)/1181/94 and the said draft notification published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No. 11,
Part VI—Section 1, Page No. 111, dated 25th March 2009 in publication No. VI(1)/116/2009.
Since no objections and suggestions have been received on this draft notification within the stipulated time, the same
are hereby confirmed and ordered as below:—
CONFIRMATION OF VARIATION
1.
The entire Veerappanchatram Detailed Development Plan No. 11 of Erode Local Planning Area approved by the
Director of Town and Country Planning Proceedings in Roc. No. 33714/94/DP2, dated 4th September 1994 is varied
by  the Director of Town and Country Planning vide Proceedings in Roc. No. 24502/2004/DPI, dated 15th February 2007
of Veerappanchatram Detailed Development Plan No. 11 of Erode Local Planning Area.
2.
The Plan thus varied is numbered as “Map No. 5 DDP(V)/DTCP No. 4/2007 and
3.
The Detailed Development Plan clauses and schedules annexed with this plan are made enforceable from the
date of publication of this notification in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.

the Director of Town and Country Planning, Chennai hereby confirms the following variation for conversion of Residential
use into commercial use in T.S.No. 1086/1A of Ward 17, Block 10 of (Krishnarayapuram Village), Coimbatore Corporation
to the sanctioned Coimbatore Town Planning Scheme No.1 of Coimbatore Local Planning Area sanctioned vide
G.O. No. 2706 Public Health dated 30th November 1933 and published in the Fort St. George Gazette No. 15,
Part-1-A in Page No. 149, Dated 10-04-1934.
The said Notification was published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No. 27, Part-VI, Section 1, Page Nos.
207-208, dated 15-07-2009 in publication No. VI(1)/192/2009 and Errata published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No.
39, Part-VI, Section 1, Page No. 303, dated 6th October 2010.
Since no objections and suggestions  have been received on this notification within the stipulated time the same
are hereby confirmed and ordered as below:—
CONFIRMATION OF VARIATION
1. The land use of T.S.No. 1086/1A of Block 17, Ward 10, (Krishnarayapuram Village) of Coimbatore Corporation
is varied from the residential use in to commercial use vide proceedings Roc. No. 17780/2008/DP2, dated 22-06-2009.
2. The variation plan is numbered as Map No. 5DDP(V)DTCP No. 13/2009.

(Roc. No. 149/2011/C.)
No. VI(1)/290/2011.
Notice is hereby given that all the parties concerned should apply to the IV Metropolitan Magistrate, Saidapet,
Chennai-600 015 for the return documents filed by them in the cases relating to the years mentioned below within one
month from the date of publication in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette failing which they will be destroyed with the
other records as per rules.
The IV, XI, IX, XVII, XVIII and XXIII Metropolitan Magistrate's Courts, Saidapet, Chennai-600 015, are attached
to Metropolitan Magistrates’ Courts, Saidapet, Chennai-600 015.
(1) Complaints on the Criminal Case instituted by the private parties/which were dismissed under sections 203
and 204 (4) Cr.P.C. and Miscellaneous Petitions disposed off in the year 2004 to 2005.
(2) Summon Cases—Entire calender case records and all the documents filed in all summons cases which
were disposed off in the year 2004 and 2005.
(3) Cases instituted under Motor Vehicle Act—Entire records and documents filed in the cases which were
disposed off in the year 2004 and 2005.
(4) Petty cases—The records in cases under the Police Act-1988 (Act-III of 1988) other than those filed under
sections 37, 45, 46, 53, 64 and 65 thereof and cases under Act, 1911 (Act V of 1911) in the Courts of Metropolitan
Magistrates, Saidapet, Chennai-600 015, disposed off in the year 2008 to 2009.
(5) Records in the cases under Security Proceedings and documents filed therein and diposed off in the year
1999 and 2000.
(6) All the warrant cases and exhibits disposed off in the year 1987 to 1990.
