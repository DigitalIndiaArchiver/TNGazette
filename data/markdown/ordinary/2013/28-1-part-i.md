No.I/PUSP/22/2013.—Accepting the proposal of High Court sent by
the Registrar General, High Court, Madras, the Government direct that
Thiru P. Nallathambi, District Judge of Puducherry Judicial Service,
functioning on deputation in Tamil Nadu State Judicial Service as
Additional District Judge, Virudhunagar be repatriated to his parent
Department viz., Puducherry Judicial Service for being posted as
Member Secretary, Union Territory of Puducherry Legal Services
Authority, Pondicherry in the existing  vacancy.
SHEELA BALAKRISHNAN,
