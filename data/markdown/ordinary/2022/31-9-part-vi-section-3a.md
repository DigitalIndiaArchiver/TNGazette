GENERAL  NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAQF BOARD, CHENNAI-600 001.
Taking over the Direct Management of Begumpur Mosque and Dargah, Dindigul  District. 
(Lr. Rc. No. 13865/09/B8/DGL)
No. VI-3(a)/41/2022.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Waqf Act 1995, the
Tamil Nadu Waqf Board in its Resolution Item No.120/22 In R.C.No. 13865/09/B8/DGL, dated 16.06.2022 has ordered 
to take over the Management of BEGUMPUR MOSQUE AND DARGAH, DINDIGUL DISTRICT under the control of 
Tamil Nadu Waqf Board and to appoint the Superintendent of Waqfs, Madurai as its Executive Officer.
26th July 2022,	
A.B. RAFIULLA,
Chennai-600 001.	
Chief Executive Officer,
	
Tamil Nadu Waqf Board.
Taking over of The Anjuman E Islamiah (G.S.No. 267/NA) Labbai Pura Mosque, Burial Ground Market Mosque and 
Idgah (G.S.No. 288/NA), Tirupattur District under Direct Management of Tamil Nadu Waqf Board.
(Lr. Rc. No. 8641/19/B7/VLR)
No. VI-3(a)/42/2022.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Waqf Act 1995, the
Tamil Nadu Waqf Board in its Special Resolution Item No.020, dated 29.06.2022 has ordered to take over the Management 
of Anjuman E Islamiah (G.S.No.267/NA) (Registration No. 65/NA) Labbai Pura Mosque, Burial Ground Market Mosque 
and Idgah (G.S.No. 288/S.A) (Registration No.70/NA & 326/NA), Tirupattur District under the Direct Management of 
Tamil Nadu Waqf Board and to appoint the Superintendent of Waqfs, Vellore (i/c) as its Executive Officer.
26th July 2022,	
A.B. RAFIULLA,
Chennai-600 001.	
Chief Executive Officer,
	
Tamil Nadu Waqf Board.
