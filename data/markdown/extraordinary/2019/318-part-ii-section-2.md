Under Chapter II, Section 12 (1) and (2) Bharathiar University Act 1981 (Tamil Nadu Act 1 of 1982) and as 
amended in Tamil Nadu Universities Laws (Amendment) Act, 2017, the Government hereby re-constitute the 
Search Committee consisting of the following persons to recommend a panel of three names to the Hon’ble 
Governor - Chancellor for appointment of Vice-Chancellor of Bharathiar University, Coimbatore:-
1.
Dr. M. Sheela Priya, I.A.S.,
Additional Chief Secretary (Retired),
Formerly State Chief Information Commissioner.
Government Nominee
2.
Dr. C. Subramaniam,
Former Vice-Chancellor of Tamil University, 
Thanjavur.
Senate Nominee
3.
Prof. Dr. S.P. Thyagarajan,
Former Vice-Chancellor of University of Madras, 
Chennai.
Syndicate Nominee
2. Dr. M. Sheela Priya, I.A.S., Additional Chief Secretary (Retired), Formerly State Chief Information 
Commissioner as Government nominee will be the Convener of the Search Committee.
3.  The above Search Committee is requested to recommend a panel of three names along with their 
Bio-data to the Chancellor of Bharathiar University, Coimbatore for appointment of Vice-Chancellor to Bharathiar 
University, Coimbatore.
   MANGAT RAM SHARMA,        
Principal Secretary to Government.
