APPOINTMENT OF CERTAIN OFFICIAL AS  ELECTION AUTHORITY FOR CONDUCTING 
ELECTION OF MEMBERS OF THE TAMIL NADU WAQF BOARD UNDER THE WAQF ACT.
[G.O. (2D) No. 06, Backward Classes, Most Backward Classes and Minorities Welfare (T1), 30th June 2021, 
Aani 16, Pilava, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2052.]
No. II(2)/BCMBCMW/345(a)/2021.
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (a) of sub-rule (1) of rule 3 of the Tamil Nadu 
Waqf Board (Conduct of Election for Members) Rules, 1997, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby 
appoints Thiru. A. Karthik, I.A.S., Principal Secretary to Government, Backward Classes, Most Backward 
Classes and Minorities Welfare Department, Chennai-600 009, as Election Authority for the purpose 
of conducting election of Members of the Tamil Nadu Waqf Board as speciﬁ ed in clause (b) of sub-section (1) 
of Section 14 of the Waqf Act, 1995 (Central Act 43 of 1995).
                                                                 A. KARTHIK, 
                                                        Principal Secretary to Government.
