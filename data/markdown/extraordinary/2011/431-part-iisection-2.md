DIRECTION TO STATE TRANSPORT AUTHORITY, TAMIL NADU AND REGIONAL TRANSPORT AUTHORITIES REGARDING
FIXATION OF FARES FOR STAGE CARRIAGES PLYING AS ORDINARY SERVICES UNDER THE ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 783, Home (Transport-VII), 17th November 2011.]
No. II(2)/HO/455(b-4)/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (i) of sub-section (1) Section 67 of the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988
(Central Act 59 of 1988) and in supersession of the Home Department Notification No.II (2)/HO/925(a-3)/2001 published at
page 4 of Part II Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary No.819, dated the 5th December 2001, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby issues the following direction to the State Transport Authority, Tamil Nadu and the Regional
Transport Authorities regarding the fixation of fares for stage carriages plying as ordinary services in the State of Tamil Nadu.
2. The notification hereby issued shall come into force on the 18th November, 2011.
DIRECTION
The fares for the stage carriages plying as “Ordinary Services” on routes/ areas in the State of Tamil Nadu shall not exceed
the fares calculated at rates given in the table below:—
1. (i) 42 paise per kilometre on roads in the plain;
Provided that for a minimum distance of ten kilometres a fare not exceeding Rs. 5.00 shall be charged,
(ii) 47 paise per kilometre on Ghat roads;
Provided that for a minimum distance of six kilometres a fare not exceeding Rs.4.00 Shall be charged.
Explanation :—“Ghat Road” means a road notified in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette as such by the Government under
the Tamil Nadu Traffic Rules, 1938 and indicated in a notice board erected at each end of such road.
2. The fares determined in accordance with the rates specified in paragraph 1 (i) and (ii) above, shall be rounded off to the
next higher multiple of 50 paise.

Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby issues the following direction to the State Transport Authority, Tamil Nadu and the Regional

2. The notification hereby issued shall come into force on the 18th November, 2011.

DIRECTION
The fares for the stage carriages plying as “Express Services” on routes/ areas in the State of Tamil Nadu shall not exceed
the fare calculated at rates given below:—
1. Express - Semi deluxe—
56 Paise per kilometre on roads in the plains;
Provided that for a minimum distance of thirty kilometres a fare not exceeding Rs. 17.00 shall be charged;
(i) 64 paise per kilometre on the Ghat road;
Provided that for a minimum distance of thirty kilometres a fare not exceeding Rs. 20.00 shall be charged;
2. Express-Super deluxe—
(a) 60 paise per kilometre shall be charged if provision is made for a superior class of accommodation for passengers in
the stage carriages plying as super deluxe on plain roads;
Provided that for a minimum distance of thirty kilometres, a fare not exceeding Rs. 18/- shall be charged.
(b) 66 paise per kilometre shall be charged if provision is made for a superior class of accommodation for passengers in
the stage carriages plying as super deluxe on ghat roads;
Provided that for a minimum distance of thirty kilometres a fare not exceeding Rs. 20.00 on ghat roads shall be charged,
3. Air suspension Coaches (Ultra deluxe)—
70 paise per kilometre shall be charged if provision is made for a superior class of accommodation for passengers in the
stage carriages plying as Air suspension Coaches (Ultra deluxe) on plain roads.
Provided that for a minimum distance of 30 Kilometres, fare not exceeding Rs.21.00 shall be charged.
4. Air-conditioned Coaches—
90 paise per kilometre shall be charged if provision is made for a superior class of accommodation for passengers in the
stage carriages plying as A/C coaches on plain roads.
Provided that for a minimum distance of 30 Kilometres, fare not exceeding Rs.27.00 shall be charged.
5. Air-conditioned Volvo Coaches—
110 paise per kilometre shall be charged if provision is made for a superior class of accommodation for passengers in the
stage carriages plying as Volvo type coaches on plain roads.
Provided that for a minimum distance of 30 Kilometres, fare not exceeding Rs.33.00 shall be charged.
Explanation:—Ghat road means a road notified in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette as such by the Government under the
Tamil Nadu Traffic Rules, 1938 and indicated in a notice board erected at each of such road.
6. The fare determined in accordance with the rates specified in paragraph 1,2,3,4 and 5 above shall be rounded off to the next
higher multiple of Re. 1/-.
7. The reservation charge of Rs. 5/- shall be charged for operation of Express Services of all categories.
