FORM D

NOTIFICATION UNDER SUB-SECTION (1) OF SECTION 4 OF THE TAMIL NADU ACQUISITION OF LAND FOR 
INDUSTRIAL PURPOSES ACT, 1997 (TAMIL NADU ACT 10 OF 1999).
[G.O. Ms. No. 98, Industries (MIG-2), 13th May 2022, சித்திரை 30, சுபகிருது, திருவள்ளுவர் ஆண்டு-2053.] 
No. II(2)/IND/391(f)/2022.
Under sub-section (1) of Section 4 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999) the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby withdraws from acquisition of the land/lands specified 
in the schedule below in respect of which notice under sub-section (1) of section 3 was published by the Industries 
(MIG.2) Department at page 1 of Part II - Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary No.136 
dated 17.07.2014 for Block 9 and page 1 of Part II- Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary 
No.249, dated 16.12.2015 for Block 12. Hence the below mentioned lands in the schedule are withdrawn from the land 
acquisition proceedings and physical possession of the Land/Lands which was not taken by Government, or on behalf 
of the Government.
