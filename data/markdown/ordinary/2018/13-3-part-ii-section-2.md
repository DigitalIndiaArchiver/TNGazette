Chennai-3, letter No.3233/RND-A1/2017, dated 02.01.2018.
Order: No. 152, Public (Protocol-II), 26th February 2018.
In the Government Order ﬁ rst to third read above, Special 
Rules for the post of Liaison Ofﬁ cer, State Guest House, 
Chepauk, Chennai -5, were issued. Further, except the post 
of Liaison Ofﬁ cer, all the posts in the State Guest House, 
Chennai, Tamizhagam Guest House, Udhagamandalam and 
Tamil Nadu House, New Delhi are kept out of the purview 
of the Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission. So as to 
maintain the similarity of all posts, Government desires to 
bring the post of Liaison Ofﬁ cer outside the purview of the 
Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission. 
2. In the letter fourth read above, the Tamil Nadu Public 
Service Commission was requested to give its concurrence 
for the exclusion of the post of Liaison Ofﬁ cer. In the
