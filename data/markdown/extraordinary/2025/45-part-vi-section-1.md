COMMISSIONER OF LAND ADMINISTRATION AND THE DISTRICT COLLECTORS.
ACQUISITION OF LANDS IN SALEM DISTRICT
REVENUE DIVISIONAL OFFICER, SANKARI
No. VI(1)/	69(a-1)/2025.
It is notified by the Revenue Divisional Officer of Sankari for acquiring the lands in Katchipalli Village, 
Irupalli Village and Chittur bit II Village by Right to fair compensation and Transparency in Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation 
and Resettlement Act, 2013 (Central Act 30 of 2013) for the purpose of Diversion of flood surplus water from 
Mettur Dam to the Dry tanks in Sarabanga Basin in Salem district by Lift Irrigation project in Edappady Taluk.
FORM - VI
Under Section -19 (1) of the Central Act 30/2013 read with rule 16
DECLARATION
(Roc. No.4903/2023/A)
Whereas it appears to the Revenue Divisional Officer, Sankari that a total extent of 4.96.82 Hectares of land 
in Katchipalli Village, Irupalli Village and Chittur Bit II Village in Edappady Taluk of Salem District for the purpose 
of Diversion of flood surplus water from Mettur Dam to the Dry tanks in Sarabanga Basin in Salem district by Lift 
Irrigation project. The preliminary notification u/s 11(1) under Right to fair compensation and Transparency in Land 
Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Act, 2013 (Central Act 30 of 2013) has been published in the District Gazette 
number 49 on 16.02.2024 and through Tamil (Daily Thanthi) and English (The Times of India) dailies on 24.02.2024 
The validity of 11(1) notification has been extended for further one year from 16.02.2024 to 15.02.2025) by the 
District Collector, Salem under section 19(7) through his proceedings Roc No: 7907/2020/B3 dated  .12.2024.

Now, therefore the declaration is made hereby that a piece of land measuring to an extent of 3.30.89 Hec is under acquisition for the above said project in 
Katchipalli Village, Irupalli Village and Chittur Bit II Village in Edappady Taluk in Salem District as these lands could not be acquired through private negotiation. (1.65.93 
Hectares of land out of 4.96.82 Hectares has been acquired through private negotiation with a consent letter from the land owners voluntarily). 
The District Collector having been satisfied that the lands specified in the schedule below have to be acquired for public purpose after having heard the opinion 
from the public. As no objection has been received from the interested parties the enquiry under section 15(2) of Right to fair compensation and Transparency in Land 
Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Act, 2013 (Central Act 30 of 2013) does not arise.
A plan of land/ Lands is kept in the office of the Land Acquisition Officer and Revenue Divisional Officer, Sankari and may be inspected on any working day 
during office hours.
No family is affected and there are no family is displaced due to this land acquisition. A summary of the Rehabilitation and Resettlement does not arise.
