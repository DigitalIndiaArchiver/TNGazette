Variation to the Approved Master Plan for the Coimbatore Local Planning Area.
(Roc. No. 5878/2007/LPA 2.)
No. VI(1)/410/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4) of  Section 32 of the Tamil Nadu T&CP Act, 1971
(Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972) and in exercise of powers conferred by the G.O. Ms. No. 94, H&UD (UD 4(1) Department,
dated 12th June 2009 which has been published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No. 27, Part II—Section 2,
page No. 228, dated 15-7-2009, the following variations are made to the approved Master Plan for the Coimbatore
Local Planning Area under the said Act and published in the H&UD Department Notifications No. II(2)/HOU/4377/94 at
page 1078 of Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated 9th November 1994.
VARIATIONS
In the said Master Plan in the "LAND USE SCHEDULE", Under the heading "Coimbatore Corporation Area" under
the Sub-heading "No. 28 Telungupalayam Village, Telungupalayam D.D.P. No. 3"—
(i)
against the entry "Residential" the expression “319/B2, 320/1, 321” shall be inserted after the
expression 296/A, B.
(ii) against the entry "Agricultural" for the expression "319/B, 320, 321", the expression "319/B (except 319/B2),
320 (except 320/1)" shall be substituted.
Coimbatore,
M. SEKARAN,
31st October 2011.
Member-Secretary (in-charge),
Coimbatore Local Planning Authority.
