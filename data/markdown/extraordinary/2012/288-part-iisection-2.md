No. II(2)/HOU/668(a)/2012.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that
the lands specified in the Schedule below and situated
Adambakkam Village, Alandur Taluk, Kancheepuram District
having already been decided that the entire amount of
compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid from
the Railway authority. The following Declaration is issued
under Section 6 of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894 (Central Act
I of 1894).
DECLARATION
  Under Section 6 of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894 (Central
Act I of 1894), the Government of Tamil Nadu hereby declares
that the land specified in the Schedule below and measuring
69019 Square feet (or) 06412.0 Square metre be the same a
little more or less are needed for public purpose to wit, for the
M.R.T.S alignment between Velachery to St. Thomas Mount,
under sub-section (1) of Section 17 of the said Act, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu further directs that the possession of
the said lands may be taken on the expiry of 15 days from the
date of publication of the notice mentioned in Section 9(1)
of the Act.
A plan of lands is kept in the office of the Special Tahsildar
(Land Acquisition), M.R.T.S. phase II extension, Thirumailai
Railway Station, Mylapore,  Chennai – 600 004, and may be
inspected at any time during office hours.
