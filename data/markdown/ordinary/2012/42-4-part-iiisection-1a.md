No. SRO A-31/2012.—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-sections (1), (2) and (3) of Section 180 of the
Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act, 1983 (Tamil Nadu Act 30 of 1983), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes
the following Amendments to the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Rules, 1988:—
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules, in rule 151,—
(1) in sub-rule (4),
(i)   for clause (a), the following clause shall be substituted, namely:—
“(a)  The District Recruitment Bureau shall recruit candidates by holding interview only“.;
(ii)  for clause (b), the following clause shall be substituted, namely:—
“(b)  The State Recruitment Bureau shall recruit candidates by holding both written examination and
interview and that the ratio of marks for written examination and interview shall be 85:15".;
(iii)  clause (c) shall be omitted;
(2) in sub-rule (5), for clause (a), the following clause shall be substitued, namely:—
“(a) The members of the District Recruitment Bureau shall award marks to the candidates on the basis
of performance in the interview and rank the candidates in accordance with the marks obtained in the interview".
M.P. NIRMALA,
Secretary to Government.
