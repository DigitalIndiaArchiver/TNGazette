Order: No. 225, Public (Special-B), 29th March 2022.
Under Clause (3) of Article 166 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that the subjects 
Transport, Nationalised Transport and Motor Vehicles Act hitherto dealt with by Thiru R.S. Rajakannappan be allocated to 
Thiru S.S. Sivasankar and the subjects Backward Classes Welfare, Most Backward Classes Welfare and Denotified Communities 
Welfare hitherto dealt with by Thiru S.S. Sivasankar, be allocated to Thiru R.S. Rajakannappan. 
2. Consequent on the above, Thiru S.S. Sivasankar be re-designated as Minister for Transport and Thiru R.S. Rajakannappan 
be re-designated as Minister for Backward Classes Welfare.
(By Order of the Governor) 
V. IRAI ANBU,
Chief Secretary.
