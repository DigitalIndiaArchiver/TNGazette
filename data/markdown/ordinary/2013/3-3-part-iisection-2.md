The Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Chief
Wildlife Warden in his letter read above has stated that the
financial grants sanctioned to Tamil Nadu Bio diversity Board
by the National Bio diversity Authority, Chennai and the State
Government, are being deposited and maintained in
Tamil Nadu Bio diversity Board’s account and operated by
the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Chief Wildlife
Warden, But, the National Bio diversity Authority, Chennai
has stated that any financial grants received from National
Bio diversity Authority should not only be operated in the
Saving Bank Account in the name of Tamil Nadu Bio diversity
Board but it should be operated independently by the Member
Secretary of the Board.
