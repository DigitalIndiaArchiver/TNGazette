ACQUISITION OF LANDS
[G.O. Ms. No. 55, Municipal Administration and Water
Supply (WSI), 2nd May 2013.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/352(a-1)/2013.
 The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that
the lands  specified in the Schedule below have to be acquired
for the construction of Booster Station for Combined Water
Supply Scheme to Vellore Corporation for a public purpose and
it having already been decided that the entire amount of
compensation to be awarded for the land is to be paid out of
public  revenue, the following declaration is issued under section
6 of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894 (Central Act, 1 of 1894)
DECLARATION
Under section 6 of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894 (Central
Act 1 of 1894), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby declares
that the lands specified in the schedule below and measuring
0.81.5 Hectares, be the same, a little more or less is needed
for a public purpose for the construction of Booster Station
for  Combined Water Supply Scheme  to Vellore Corporation
in V. Muthampatti Village, Dharmapuri Taluk, Dharmapuri District.
A Plan of the land is kept in the Office of the Revenue
Divisional Officer, Dharmapuri  and may be inspected at any
time during the office hours.

Dharmapuri District, Dharmapuri Taluk, V. Muthampatti Village,

S.No.357/2B2, south by S.No.417/1 and west by S.No.356—0.05.0 hectare.
Ryotwari, dry, S.No.357/2B2, belonging to Kamala, wife of  Kuppagounder and Raja,  son of Kuppagounder, bounded
on north by  S.No.357 /2B1, east by S.Nos. 416/1,2,3,  south by S.No.417/1 and west by S.No.357 /2A2—0.70.0 hectare.
Ryotwari, dry, S.No.417 /1,  belonging to Mari,  son of Ramasamy,  bounded on north by S.No.357/2B2, east by
S.No. 416/2, south and west by S.No.417/2—0.06.5 hectare.

No. II(2)/MAWS/352(a-2)/2013.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that
the lands  specified in the Schedule below have to be acquired
for the construction of Master Balancing  Reservoir under Vellore
Combined Water Supply Scheme for a public purpose and it
having already been  decided that the entire amount of
compensation to be awarded for the land is to be paid out of
public revenue, the following declaration is issued under section
6 of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894 (Central Act, 1 of 1894).
DECLARATION
Under Section 6 of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894 (Central
Act 1 of 1894), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby declares
that the lands specified in the Schedule below and measuring
0.53.0 hectares, be the same, a little more or less is needed
for a public purpose for the construction of Master Balancing
Reservoir under Vellore  Combined Water Supply Scheme
in Madathahalli Village, Pappireddipatti Taluk, Dharmapuri
District.
A Plan of the Land is kept in the Office of the Revenue
Divisional Officer, Harur and may be inspected at any time
during the office hours.

Dharmapuri District, Pappireddipatti Taluk, V. Madathahalli Village,
