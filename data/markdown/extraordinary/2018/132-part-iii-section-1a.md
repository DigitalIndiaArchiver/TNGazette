In exercise of the powers conferred by Sections 15 and 23-C of the Mines and Minerals (Development and Regulation) Act, 
1957 (Central Act 67 of 1957), and all enabling powers, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments 
to the Tamil Nadu Minor Mineral Concession Rules, 1959, namely:-
2. The amendments hereby made shall come into force from the 10th day of April, 2018.

In the said Rules:-
(1) 
after rule 38-C, the following rule shall be inserted, namely:-
    “38-D. Import of sand for construction purposes:-(1) (a) No person, other than the Public Works Department of the 
State Government of Tamil Nadu shall have the right to sell sand which is imported from other countries or brought from 
other States or Union Territories for construction purposes (hereinafter referred to as “imported sand”), and such right shall 
vest only with the Public Works Department of the State Government.
 (b) No person shall transport the imported sand, either for own use or sale, across the border of the State of 
Tamil Nadu to any other  State or Union Territory or country.
(2) The right to store the imported sand shall vest with the Public Works Department of the State Government of 
Tamil Nadu.

(3) Imported sand shall be accompanied with the documents of the origin of the sand and its legal procurement from 
the country or State or Union Territory, from where the sand is imported or brought, as the case may be.
(4) The imported sand shall fulﬁ l the speciﬁ cations speciﬁ ed by the Public Works Department. The Public Works 
Department shall have the power to collect samples and to verify as to whether the imported sand fulﬁ ls the  speciﬁ cations.
(5) The Public Works Department shall have the power to prohibit the transport of imported sand, which have not 
fulﬁ lled the speciﬁ cations, into the State of  Tamil Nadu. The imported sand which has not fulﬁ lled the speciﬁ cations shall be 
evacuated out of the State of Tamil Nadu through the sea route and it shall not be transported to any other State or Union 
Territory by road or by any other mode of land transport.
(6) The imported sand shall be sold only to the Public Works Department at the rate ﬁ xed by the State Government 
on a case to case basis, depending upon the factors like the country or State or Union Territory from where it is imported 
or brought, the cost of procurement, insurance, freight charges and applicable taxes or duties.
(7) No imported sand shall be transported from any Port within the State of  Tamil Nadu, or from other States or Union 
Territories to the Depots maintained by the Public Works Department without a valid Transport Permit in the prescribed Form 
in Appendix-XXIII, issued by the Public Works Department, duly authenticated by the Assistant Engineer or Assistant Executive 
Engineer (Water Resources Department), Public Works Department.
(8) No person shall transport imported sand from any Depot maintained by the Public Works Department without 
obtaining a Transit Pass in the prescribed Form in Appendix-XXIV, issued by the Public Works Department, duly authenticated 
by the Assistant Engineer or Assistant Executive Engineer (Water Resources Department), Public Works Department.
(9) Any person who contravenes any of the provisions of this rule, shall be punishable with imprisonment for a term 
which may extend to two years or with ﬁ ne which may extend to ﬁ ve lakh rupees or with both.
(10) The Public Works Department may issue instructions regarding the storage, transportation and sale of imported 
sand, from time to time.
Explanation:- For the purpose of this rule “sand” means ordinary sand used for construction purposes which includes
