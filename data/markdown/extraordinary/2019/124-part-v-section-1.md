The following Notiﬁ cation of the Government of India, Ministry of Law & Justice (Legislative 
Department), New Delhi-110 001, dated 19th March, 2019 [28 Phalguna, 1940 (Saka)] is republished:-
S.O. (E).— Whereas it has been decided to hold a general election for the purpose of constituting 
a new House of the People, on the expiration of the term of Sixteenth House of the People; 
Now, therefore, in pursuance of sub-section (2) of Section 14 of the Representation of the 
People Act, 1951 (43 of 1951), the President is pleased to call upon the Parliamentary Constituencies, 
speciﬁ ed, in column (2) of the Table given below comprised within the corresponding States or 
Union Territory, as the case may be of Assam, Bihar, Chhattisgarh, Jammu & Kashmir, Karnataka, 
Maharashtra, Manipur, Odisha, Tamil Nadu, Tripura, Uttar Pradesh, West Bengal and Puducherry as 
speciﬁ ed in column (1) of the said Table, to elect the M embers in accordance with the provisions 
of the said Act and of the rules and orders made thereunder:
