COMMERCIAL TAXES AND REGISTRATION DEPARTMENT
AMENDMENTS TO THE TAMIL NADU GOODS AND SERVICES TAX RULES, 2017.
[G.O. Ms. No. 109, Commercial Taxes and Registration (B1), 31st August 2021, 
Aavani 15, Pilava, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2052.] 
No. SRO A-14(h)2021.
In exercise of the powers conferred by section 164 of the Tamil Nadu Goods and Services Tax Act, 2017 
(Tamil Nadu Act 19 of 2017), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, on the recommendations of the Council, hereby makes the following 
rules further to amend the Tamil Nadu Goods and Services Tax Rules, 2017, namely: —
1. Short title and commencement.- (1) These rules may be called the Tamil Nadu Goods and Services Tax 
(Sixth Amendment) Rules, 2021.
 
(2) Save as otherwise provided in these rules, they shall come into force on the date of their publication in the Oﬃ  cial 
Gazette.
2.  In the Tamil Nadu Goods and Services Tax Rules, 2017, —
(i) in sub-rule (1) of rule 26,-
 
(a)  in the fourth proviso, with eﬀ ect from the 29th August, 2021, for the ﬁ gures, letters and words “31st day of August, 
2021”, the ﬁ gures, letters and words “31st day of October, 2021” shall be substituted;
 
(b) with eﬀ ect from the 1st day of November, 2021, all the provisos shall be omitted;
      (ii)   with eﬀ ect from the 1st day of May, 2021, in rule 138E, after the fourth proviso, the following proviso shall be inserted, 
namely:-
