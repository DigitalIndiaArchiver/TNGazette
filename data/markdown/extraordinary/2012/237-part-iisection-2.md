ACQUISITION OF LANDS
[G.O. Ms. No. 112, Planning, Development and Special
Initiatives (S.I), 30th August 2012,
Aavani 14, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2043.]
No. II(2)/PDSI/548(a)/2012.
Whereas it appears to the Government of Tamil Nadu
that the lands specified in the Schedule below and situated
in St. Thomas Mount (Cantonment), Alandur Taluk,
Kancheepuram District are needed for a public purpose to
wit for the purpose of formation of Alandur to St. Thomas
Mount Track for Chennai Metro Rail project, notice to that
effect is hereby given to all to whom it may concern in
accordance with the provisions of sub-section (1) of Section
4 of the Land Acquisition Act 1894 (Central Act I of 1894)
and whereas, it has become necessary to acquire the
immediate possesion of the lands specified in the
Schedule below, the Governor of Tamil  Nadu hereby directs
that the lands be acquired under the provisions of
Section (1) of the Section 17 of the said Act.
Now therefoe in exercise of the powers conferred by
sub-section (2) of Section 4 of the said Act, the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby authorizes the Special Tahsildar Land
Acquisition, Kancheepuram Unit, Chennai Metro Rail Project,
No.7, Conron Smith Road, Harini Towers, 2nd Floor,
Gopalapuram, Chennai-86, and her staff and workman to
excise the powers conferred by the said sub-sections.
Under sub-section 3(c) of the Act, the Governor of
Tamil Nadu, hereby appoints the Special Tahsildar Land
Acquisition, Kancheepuram Unit, Chennai Metro Rail Project,
No.7,  Conron Smith Road, Harini Towers, 2nd Floor,
Gopalapuram, Chennai-86 to perform the functions of the
Collector under the said Act.
Under sub-section (4) of Section 17 of the said Act, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that in view of the
urgency of the case, the provisions of Section 5 A of the said
Act, shall not apply in this case.
