No. I/PULO/16(a)/2016.-In exercise of the powers conferred under Article 162 of the  Constitution
of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the following Advocates as the Additional
Advocates General of Tamil Nadu, from the date of their assuming office:-
1. Thiru. V.Ayyadurai, Advocate as Additional Advocate General-II of Tamil Nadu,
High Court of Madras, Chennai
2. Thiru. B.Pugalendhi, Advocate as Additional Advocate General-III of Tamil Nadu,
Madurai Bench of Madras High Court, Madurai
3. Thiru. C.Manishankhar, Advocate as Additional Advocate General-IV of Tamil Nadu,
High Court of Madras, Chennai
4. Thiru. S.T.S.Murthi, Advocate as Additional Advocate General-V of Tamil Nadu,
High Court of Madras, Chennai
2.  Thiru.K.Venkatramani, who is presently serving as Additional Advocate General-VII
of Tamil Nadu  in Supreme Court of India is posted as Additional Advocate General-VII of Tamil Nadu
in the High Court of Madras.
3.  The above appointments are subject to the final outcome of the judgement of the Hon’ble High
Court of Madras in W.P.No.18331/2016.
P.RAMA  MOHANA  RAO,
Chief Secretary.
