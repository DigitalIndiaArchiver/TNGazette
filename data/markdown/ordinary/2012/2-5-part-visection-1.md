Final Closing of Affairs and Cancellation of Registration for certain  Co-operative Societies.
Q. 400. Koomapatty Jaggery Manufacturing Co-operative Society.
(Rc. No. 1335/2002/C2)
No. VI(1)/26/2012.
In the circumstances stated by the liquidator of this office Proceedings Rc. No. 1335/2002/C2, dated 23rd December 2011
with final closure report dated 16th December 2011 and in exercise of the powers delegated under Section 140(1) of the
Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act, 1983 the registration of Q. 400. Koomapatty Jaggery Manufacturing Co-operative
Societies Ltd., is hereby cancelled and its affairs are finally closed with effect from 23rd December 2011.
Q. 391. P. Vanniyampatty Jaggery Manufacturing Co-operative Society.
(Rc. No. 6894/89/C2.)
No. VI(1)/27/2012.
In the circumstances stated by the liquidator of this office Proceedings Rc. No. 6894/89/C2, dated 23rd December 2011
with final closure report dated 16th December 2011 and in exercise of the powers delegated under Section 140(1) of the
Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act, 1983 the registration of Q. 391. P. Vanniyampatty Jaggery Manufacturing Co-operative
Societies Ltd., is hereby cancelled and its affairs are finally closed with effect from 23rd December 2011.
Q. 396. W. Pudupatty Jaggery Manufacturing Co-operative Society.
(Rc. No. 6895/89/C2)
No. VI(1)/28/2012.
In the circumstances stated by the liquidator of this office Proceedings Rc. No. 6895/89/C2, dated 23rd December 2011
with final closure report dated 16th December 2011 and in exercise of the powers delegated under Section 140(1) of the
Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act, 1983 the registration of Q. 396. W. Pudupatty Jaggery Manufacturing Co-operative
Societies Ltd., is hereby cancelled and its affairs are finally closed with effect from 23rd December 2011.
