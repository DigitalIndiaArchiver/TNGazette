GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD
No. 1, Jaffar Syrang Street, Vallal Seethakathi Nagar, Chennai-600 001.
Taking over the Management of Kudumiandithope Pallivasal,  Sholinganallur, Tambaram Taluk, Kancheepuram
under Direct Management of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
(Rc. No. 12623/07/B3/Kanchi)
No. VI-3(a)/273/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995, the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its
Resolution No. 205/09 in  R.C. No. 12623/07/B3/KPM, dated 29th July 2009 has ordered to takeover the Management
of Kudimiandithope Pallivasal, Sholinganallur, Kancheepuram under Direct Control of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for
a period of one year from 9th November 2009 and appoints the Wakf Inspector, Kancheepuram
as its Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
13th November 2009.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
