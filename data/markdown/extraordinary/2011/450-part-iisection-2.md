BUILDINGS-CHENNAI CITY CONSTRUCTION OF NEW SECRETARIAT COMPLEX AT OMANDURAR GOVERNMENT ESTATE,
CHENNAI-2 - ALLEGATION OF IRREGULARITIES LIKE EXCESS EXPENDITURE CAUSING LOSS TO THE EXCHEQUER
AND DEFICIENCY IN STANDARDS OF CONSTRUCTION ETC., - APPOINTMENT OF HON'BLE JUSTICE
THIRU R. REGUPATHI, COMMISSION OF INQUIRY, UNDER THE COMMISSIONS OF INQUIRY ACT, 1952 (CENTRAL
ACT LX OF 1952) IN THE PLACE OF HON'BLE JUSTICE THIRU S. THANGARAJ WHO HAS RESIGNED-ORDERED.
[G.O. Ms. No. 1143, Public (Buildings), 2nd December 2011.]
No. II(2)/PUBG/473(c)/2011.
       Whereas, a Commission of Inquiry was appointed by the Government in G.O. Ms.No.530, Public (Buildings)
Department, dated 22nd June 2011 with Hon’ble Justice Thiru S. Thangaraj (Retd.,), High Court Judge, to inquire  into the
causes and circumstances leading to the alleged irregularities like excess expenditure, irregularities causing loss to the
exchequer, whether all statutory approvals and clearances were obtained, inordinate delay and deficiency in standards of
construction in the construction of New  Secretariat  Complex in Omandurar Government Estate, Chennai-2;
       And, Whereas, Hon’ble  Justice Thiru  S.Thangaraj   has   resigned   on   22nd November 2011 and the same has
been accepted  w.e.f.  22nd November 2011;
        And, Whereas, the Government of Tamil Nadu have now decided to appoint Hon’ble Justice Thiru R.Regupathi,  as
Commission of Inquiry in the place of Hon’ble Justice Thiru S.Thangaraj for the purpose of making an inquiry into a definite
matter of public importance hereinafter specified;
       Now, Therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1), read with sub-section (3) of Section 3 of the
Commissions of Inquiry Act, 1952 (Central Act LX of 1952), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints a Commission of
Inquiry consisting of a single member, namely, Hon’ble Justice Thiru R.Regupathi  as Commission of Inquiry in the place of
Hon’ble Justice Thiru S. Thangaraj, Retired Judge of Madras High Court, with the same terms of reference and other provisions
as specified in G.O. Ms.No.530, Public (Buildings) Department, dated 22nd June 2011 and G.O. Ms.No.870, Public (Buildings)
Department, dated, 21st September 2011.
DEBENDRANATH SARANGI,
Chief Secretary.
