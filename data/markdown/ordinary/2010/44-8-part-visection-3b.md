One Member elected to the governing Council of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai by the Deans
or Principals of Affiliated Colleges of this University providing Courses of Study and Training Medical Science from

No. VI-3(b)/46/2010.
Under Sub-Statutes (1) of Statute 5 of Chapter-XXI of the General Statutes of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical
University, Chennai, it is hereby published that one member has been elected unopposed to the Governing Council of
the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai by the Deans or Principals of the affiliated Colleges providing
courses of Study and Training in Medical Science from among themselves under clause (b) of sub-section (4) of Section 21
of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai, Act, 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 37 of 1987) in the residual vacancy.
Dr. C. VAMSADHARA, M.D., Ph.D.,
Dean,
Stanley Medical College,
Chennai-600 001.
2. The tenure of the above member in the Governing Council of this University will be for a period with effect from
1st November 2010 to 22nd August 2011 or the date on which she cease to hold the post of Dean or Principal of affiliated
College of this University, whichever is earlier.
Chennai-600 032,
DR. SUDHA SESHAYYAN,
