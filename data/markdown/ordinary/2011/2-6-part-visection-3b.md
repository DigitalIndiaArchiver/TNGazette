UNIVERSITY AUTHORITIES
MANONMANIAM SUNDARANAR UNIVERSITY, TIRUNELVELI
Election of a nominee by the Senate/Syndicate to the Selection Committee for recommending a panel of three
names to His Excellency Governor-Chancellor for the appointment of the Vice-Chancellor.
(MSU / M&E / VCPANEL / ELECT / 2011)
No.VI-3(b)/3/2011.
It is hereby notified, by direction, that an election will be held for nominating a person by the Senate of the University to
serve on the Selection Committee of three persons who are to recommend a panel of three names to His Excellency
Governor-Chancellor for the appointment of the Vice-Chancellor under Section 11(2) of the Manonmaniam Sundaranar University
Act, 1990.
The election will be held in accordance with rules and procedures as per the Statutes of this University.
Each member of the Senate shall have a right to nominate not more than one person who is not a member of any of
the authorities of the University to the Committee. Every nomination shall be in writing signed by the proposer who shall be
a member of the Senate and Seconded in writing by another member of the Senate called the seconder in the prescribed
form. The prescribed form may be obtained from the Registrar on a written requisition signed by any member of the Senate.
No nomination shall be valid unless it contains the signatures of both the proposer and the seconder and the nomination form
shall also be signed by the person proposed expressing his consent  to be nominated. Every nomination must be presented
before the Registrar either by the proposer or the seconder or the person proposed, before 3.00 p.m. on Thursday,
the 27th January, 2011.
The scrutiny of nomination will take place at 3.30 p.m. on Thursday, the 27th January, 2011 at the office of the Registrar,
Manonmaniam Sundaranar University, Abishekapatti, Tirunelveli-627 012. The proposer or the seconder or the proposed nominee
are entitled to be present at the time of the scrutiny.
The following is the programme for election:—
1.
Last day and hour by which the candidates may
withdraw their candidature by notice in writing
2.
Publication of list of valid Nomination of candidates
3.
Last date of despatch of intimation papers to the
electors if there is a poll.
4.
Day and hour of polling at the University Buildings.
5.
Day and hour of Scrutiny and counting of votes will
take place at the University Buildings.
If the number of persons validly nominated and who have not withdrawn is more than one, an election will be held on
Friday, the 18th February 2011 from 11 a.m. to 3.00 p.m.
