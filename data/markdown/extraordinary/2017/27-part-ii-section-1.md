In exercise of the powers conferred under Section (6) of the Right of Children to Free and 
Compulsory Education Act, 2009 (Central Act 35 of 2009) read with sub rule (4) of Rule 4 under 
Part III of the Tamil Nadu Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Rules, 2011, the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notify to provide Transport/ Escort Facility for the academic year 
2017-2018 as an entitlement under the said Act and Rules to all children in 885 habitations appended 
to this notification, comprising 779 remote habitations with sparse population and 106 urban areas 
where land is unavailable and are un-served by a school as per neighbourhood limits specified under 
Rule 4(1) of the said Rules.
	
D. SABITHA,
	
Principal Secretary to Government.
