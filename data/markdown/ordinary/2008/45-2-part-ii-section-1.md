No. II(1)/AG/54/2008.—The following order of the Gazette of India, Ministry of Agriculture, Department of Agriculture and
Co-operation, New Delhi, the 10th April, 2008 is republished :—
ORDER
S.O. 837 (E).—In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 3 of the Essential Commodities Act, 1955 (10 of 1955),
the Central Government hereby makes the following order further to amend the Fertilizer (Control) Order, 1985, namely :—
1.  (1) This Order may be called the Fertilizer Control (Amendment) Order, 2008.
(2) It shall come into force on the date of its publication in the Official Gazette.
2. In the said order, in Schedule I, in Part A, under the heading “Specifications of Fertilizers”,
(i) in sub-heading 1(f) relating to “Micronutrients”, in serial number 14 relating to “Di-Sodium Tetra Borate Penta
Hydrate”, for entry (IV), the following entry shall be substituted, namely :—
“(IV) Arsenic (as As), per cent by weight, maximum 0.01”.
(ii) in sub-heading 1(h) relating to 100 percent Water Soluble Complex Fertilizers, in serial number 10 relating
to “Potassium, Magnesium Sulphate” for entry (ii), the following entry shall be substituted namely,—
“(ii) Potash content (as K2O) percent by weight, minimum 22.0”.
3.
In schedule IV, in Part A, under the heading “Specification of Organic Fertilizer”, the sub-heading 3-pressmud and
entries relating thereto shall be omitted.
(F.No. 2-1/2007-Fert. Law)
SATISH CHANDER,
Joint Secretary.
