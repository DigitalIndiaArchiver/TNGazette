(Rc.No. 409/ICS/2011)
No. VI(1)/24/2015.

Co-operative Societies Act, 1983 (Act 30 of 1983), the activities of the Sivaganga District Co-operative Industrial Estate Ltd.

Commissioner and Director of Industries and Commerce and the Registrar of Industrial Co-operatives, Mandaivelipakkam,
Raja Annamalaipuram, Chennai-28 vide Proc .No 409/ICS/2011, dated 2-1-2015.”
