APPOINTMENT OF PRESIDING OFFICER TO THE TRIBUNAL HIGH COURT OF MADRAS AT CHENNAI
CONSTITUTION OF THE INDIAN CRIMINAL LAW AMENDMENT ACT, 1908.
[G.O. No. SS.I/121-10/2012, Public (SC), 4th June 2013.]
No.II(2)/PU/398(c)/2013.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 16-B of the Indian Criminal Law
Amendment Act, 1908 (Central Act XIV of 1908) as amended by the Indian Criminal Law Amendment
(Tamil Nadu Amendment) Act, 1998 (Tamil Nadu Act 42 of 1998), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
appoints Thiru Justice T. Sudanthiram, Judge, High Court of Madras at Chennai, as the Presiding Officer
of the Tribunal constituted for the purpose of Section 16-A of the said Act.
JATINDRA NATH SWAIN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
