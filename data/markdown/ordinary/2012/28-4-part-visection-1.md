JUDICIAL NOTIFICATION
Proposal for Appointment of Notary for the Area Comprised in Thoothukkudi District.
(D. No. 5335/A1/2012.)
No. VI(1)/212/2012.
Whereas Thiru M. Srinivasan, B.A., B.L., Advocate, Bar Association, Tiruchendur, Tamil Nadu  has applied to this
court to appoint him as a 'Notary' for the area comprised in Thoothukkudi District or Tiruchendur Taluk in
Thoothukkudi District.  Notice is herewith given that those have objections may file their objections before the
Principal District Court, Thoothukkudi  within fourteen (14) days  from the date of publication of this Notice.
Principal District Court,
J. PRABHUDASS,
Thoothukkudi,
Principal District Judge.
27th June 2012.
