KARUR DISTRICT UNDER THE TAMIL NADU PANCHAYATS ACT, 1994.

WHEREAS, a written notice of the intention to make a motion expressing want of conﬁ dence in Tmt. A. Chandramathi, 
Chairman, Krishnarayapuram Panchayat Union Council, Karur District, signed by sixteen members, out of the sanctioned strength 
of twenty members of the said Council, together with a copy of the motion proposed to be made and a written statement of 
charges against the said Chairman have been delivered to the Revenue Divisional Oﬃ  cer, Kulithalai, Karur District in person 
by two members as per sub-section (2) of Section 212 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994);
AND WHEREAS, the Revenue Divisional Oﬃ  cer, Kulithalai, Karur District caused a copy of the statement of charges 
along with a copy of the notice of motion to be delivered, to the said Chairman Tmt. A. Chandramathi and she submitted 
her reply to the charges;
AND WHEREAS, a motion expressing want of conﬁ dence in Tmt.  A. Chandramathi, Chairman, Krishnarayapuram Panchayat 
Union Council, Karur District had been carried with a support of sixteen members out of the sanctioned strength of twenty members 
of the said Panchayat Union Council in the meeting convened by the Revenue Divisional Oﬃ  cer, Kulithalai, Karur District on the 
28th February 2022 after following the procedures speciﬁ ed in the said Section 212 and forwarded the same to Government;
NOW THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (13) of Section 212 of the 
Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby removes 
Tmt. A. Chandramathi, Chairman, Krishnarayapuram Panchayat Union Council, Karur District from the Oﬃ  ce of the Chairman 
of the said Panchayat Union Council with immediate eﬀ ect.
