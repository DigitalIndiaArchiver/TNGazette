NOTIFICATIONS BY THE ELECTION COMMISSION OF INDIA.
APPOINTMENT OF DATES FOR VARIOUS STAGES AND FIXATION OF HOURS OF POLL FOR BYE-ELECTION TO
COUNCIL OF STATES TO FILL UP THE VACANCY CAUSED DUE TO RESIGNATION OF SITTING MEMBER,
THIRU K.V. RAMALINGAM.
No. SRO/G-20/2011
The following Notification of the Election Commission of India, New Delhi, dated the 5th July, 2011 [Asadha 14, 1933 (Saka)]
is republished:—
No. 100/CS-TN/1/2011 (1):—Whereas, the seat of Shri K.V. Ramalingam in the Council of States, elected by the elected
members of the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu, has become vacant on 20th May, 2011 by the reason of his
resiganation, before the expiration of his term of office which was up to and including the 29th June, 2016; and
Whereas a bye-election is to be held for the purpose of filling the vacancy so caused;
Now, Therefore in pursuance of Section 147, sub-section (1) of Section 39 and Section 56 of the Representation of the People
Act, 1951 (43 of 1951), the Election Commission of India hereby—
(A) Calls upon the elected members of the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu to elect, befoe 25th July, 2011
(Monday), and in accordance with the provisions of the said Act and of the rules and orders made thereunder, a person
for the purpose of filling the said vacancy in the Council of States;
(B) appoints, with respect to the said election:
(a) the 12th July, 2011 (Tuesday), as the last date for making nominations;
(b) the 13th July, 2011 (Wednesday), as the date for the scrutiny of the nominations;
(c) the 15th July 2011 (Friday), as the last date for the withdrawal of candidatures;
(d) the 22nd July 2011 (Friday), as the date on which a poll shall, if necessary, be taken; and
(e) the 25th July 2011 (Monday), as the date before which the election shall be completed; and
(C) fixes the hours from 9.00 A.M. 4.00 P.M. as the hours during which the poll shall, if necessary, be taken on the date
specified above for the election.
