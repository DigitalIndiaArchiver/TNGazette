Amendments to the Ad hoc Rules relating to the Temporary Post of Therapeutic Assistant in Indian Medicine and
Homeopathy Department in Tamil Nadu Medical Subordinate Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 183, Health and Family Welfare [IM2-1], 14th July 2016, ÝQ 30, ¶¡ºA, F¼õœÀõ˜ Ý‡´-2047.]
No. SRO B-45/2016.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Ad hoc rules published with the
Health and Family Welfare Department Notification No.(SRO)B-45/2008 at Page 142 of Part III—(Section)1(b) of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 15th October 2008.
2. The Amendments hereby made shall come into force on the date of publication of this Notification in the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
AMENDMENTS
In the said rules,-
(1) For rule 3, the following shall be substituted, namely:—
"(3) Appointment.—Appointment to the post shall be made by direct recruitment.”
(2) for rule 4, the following shall be substituted, namely:-
"(4) Appointing Authority.—The appointing authority for the post shall be the Commissioner of Indian Medicine and
Homeopathy, Chennai.”
(3) in rule 5 in sub-rule (b), for the expression “the Minimum General Educational Qualification”, the expression
 “a Diploma in Nursing Therapy” shall be substituted.
(4) rule 6 shall be Omitted.
J. RADHAKRISHNAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.

AMENDMENT
In the said Special Rules, after Rule 14, the following rules shall be inserted, namely:-
14-A Recruitment of Transgender Candidates:-
(1) A transgender candidate, who applies as Third Gender, shall be eligible for appointment in the vacancies reserved
for women candidates as well as the vacancies under the general category.
(2) A transgender candidate, who applies as Third Gender, shall produce an Identity Card issued by the Transgenders
Welfare Board.
(3) A transgender may choose to appear as male or female by selecting either of the Gender category at the time of
application.
(4) If a transgender candidate applies as male candidate, then the requirement of Physical measurement Test, Physical
Endurance Test and Physical efficiency Test prescribed for the male category shall apply.
(5) If a transgender candidate applies as female candidate, then the requirement of Physical Measurement Test,
Physical Endurance Test and Physical Efficiency Test prescribed for the Female category shall apply.
(6) If a transgender apply as a Third gender, the Physical Measurement Test, Physical Endurance Test and
Physical efficiency Test prescribed for female candidates shall apply to the Third gender candidates.
