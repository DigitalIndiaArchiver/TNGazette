UNIVERSITY AUTHORITIES
TAMIL NADU VETERINARY AND ANIMAL SCIENCES UNIVERSITY,
MADHAVARAM MILK COLONY, CHENNAI-600 051.
Creation of New Faculty of Food Sciences in
Tamil Nadu Veterinary and Animal Sciences University
(U.O. No. 60064/R.1/BS/2010-F.No.21114/R.1/BS/2009.)
No. VI-3(b)/31/2010.
It is hereby notified that as per Section 24 (1), Chapter IV of the TANUVAS Act read with Clause 3(1) (a) and
Clause 24(1) of the TANUVAS Statutes a New Faculty of Food Sciences is created to take with effect from
21st August 2009 with the approval of the Board of Management in its Sixty fourth meeting held on 24th June 2009 on
the recommendation of the Academic Council and Finance Committee with assent of His Excellency the Governor and
Chancellor of Tamil Nadu Veterinary and Animal Sciences University as per Section 39 (5), Chapter VIII of the TANUVAS
Act (Tamil Nadu Act 42 of 1989).
