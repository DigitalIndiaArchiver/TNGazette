the approved Pandamangalam Detailed Development Plan No. 4, Tiruchirappalli Local Planning Area approved by the 
Director of Town and Country Planning’s proceedings Roc. No. 14830/2005 DP3 dated 21-04-2011 and the fact of this 
approval in Form No. 12 Published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No. 27, Part VI—Section-1, Page Nos. 296 
& 297, dated 20-07-2011 publication No. VI(1)/301/2011.

this notiﬁ cation in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, can represent in person or submit in writing to the Member 
Secretary, Tiruchirappalli Local Planning Authority any objections and suggestions relating  there to.
3. The Variation with plan may be inspected free of cost at any time during ofﬁ ce hours at the above said Local
