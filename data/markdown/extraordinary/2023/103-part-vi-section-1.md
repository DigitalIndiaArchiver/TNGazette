The Government of Tamil Nadu have already been satisﬁ ed that the land/lands speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below 
have to be acquired for the Construction of Road Over Bridge in lieu of existing LC.No.2 at Railway km 1034/1035 
between - Pattabiram and Pattabiram west Railway Stations and it has already been decided that the amount of 
Compensation to be awarded for the land/lands is to be paid out of the funds controlled or managed by the Chief 
Engineer, NABARD and Rural Roads, Chengalpattu, Highways Department and having considered the cause shown 
by the owners or other persons having and interest in the said lands, as the case may do hereby publish the following 
notice under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34/2002.)
NOTICE

the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands speciﬁed in the Schedule below measuring an 
extent of 106 Sq.mtrs of dry lands required for Highways purpose i.e., to the same, a little more or less, required for 
highways purpose to wit, for Construction of Road Over Bridge in lieu of existing LC.No.2 at Railway km 1034 / 1035 
between – Pattabiram and Pattabiram west Railway Stations in Thandurai (Pattabiram) Village, Avadi Taluk of 
Tiruvallur District.
