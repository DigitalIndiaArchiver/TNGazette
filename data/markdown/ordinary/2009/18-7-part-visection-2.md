on the Schedule and measuring the extent noted below be the same a little more or less shall be excluded from the
operation of the said Act:

LOSS OF CERTIFICATES

to issue the duplicate of the said Certificates.  If the Originals were to be found by anybody, they should be treated as invalid
and sent to the Secretary, Board of Higher Secondary Examination / Directorate of Government Examinations,
