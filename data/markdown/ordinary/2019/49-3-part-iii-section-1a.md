No. SRO A-43/2019.— In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 180 of the Tamil Nadu 
Co-operative Societies Acts, 1983 (Tamil Nadu Act 30 of 1983), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following 
amendments to “The Restructured Peraringnar Anna Co-operative Milk Producers Welfare Fund Rules, 2010”.
Amendments
In the said Rule,- in rule 15, -
	
(a) in sub-rule (d), - 
	
	
in clause (i) for the expression Rs.1.75 lakh  (Rupees One lakh seventy five thousand only)”, the expression 
Rs.2.50 lakh (Rupees Two lakh fifty thousand only)”, shall be substituted:
	
(b) in sub-rule (e), - 
	
	
(i)	
in clause (i), for the expression “Rs.10,000/-  (Rupees Ten thousand only)” the expression “Rs.25,000/- 
(Rupees Twenty five thousand only)”, shall be substituted;
	
	
(ii)	
in clause (ii), for the expression, “Rs.10,000/- (Rupees Ten thousand only)”, occurring in two places, the 
expression “Rs.30,000/- (Rupees Thirty   thousand only)”, shall be substituted;
	
	
(iii)	 after clause (ii) the following shall be added, namely,-
	
	
	
(iii) to provide assistance of a sum of Rs.5000/- (Rupees Five thousand only) as funeral expenses to the 
family of deceased milk pouring member. 
K. GOPAL,
 Principal Secretary to Government.
