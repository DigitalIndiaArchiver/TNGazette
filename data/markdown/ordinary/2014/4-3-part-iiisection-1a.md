Amendments to the Rules for the Prisoners' Canteen.

No. SRO A-6/2014.— The following amendments made to the Rules for the Prisoner's Canteens in Part XI of Volume III
of the Tamil Nadu Prison Manual:—
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules, in rule 4,—
1.
for the expression "Rs. 225/- (Rupees two hundred and twenty five only)" the expression
"Rs. 450/- (Rupees four hundred and fifty only)" shall be substituted;
2.
for the expression "Rs. 375/- (Rupees three hundred and seventy five only)" the expression
"Rs. 750/- (Rupees seven hundred and fifty only)" shall be substituted and
3.
for the expression "Prisoner cash property or credit" the expression "Prisoner cash property credit" shall be
substituted.
APURVA VARMA,
Principal Secretary to Government.
HOME, PROHIBITION AND EXCISE DEPARTMENT
Amendments to the Tamil Nadu Liquor (Licence and Permit) Rules
[G.O.Ms. No. 1, Home, Prohibition and Excise (VIII), 2nd January 2014.]
No. SRO A-7/2014.— In exercise of the powers conferred by Sections 17-C, 17-D, 17-E, 18-B, 18-C, 20 and 21 read with
Section 54 of the Tamil Nadu Prohibition Act, 1937 (Tamil Nadu Act  X of 1937),  the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes
the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu Liquor (Licence and Permit) Rules, 1981:—
2.
The amendments hereby made shall come into force with effect on and from the 1st April 2014.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules, in rule 17, in sub-rule (b),—
(i) in clause II,-
(a) for the expression “a licence fee of Rs. 5,000/- (Rupees five thousand only)", the expression “a licence fee of
Rs. 10,000/- (Rupees ten thousand only) shall be substituted;
(b) for the expression "an application fee of Rs. 1,000/- (Rupees one thousand only)", the expression "an application
fee of Rs.3,000/- (Rupees three thousand only)” shall be substituted;
