ENVIRONMENT AND FOREST DEPARTMENT
Declaration of Erranahalli Additional Forest Block in
Palacode Taluk of Dharmapuri District as Reserved
Forest under Tamil Nadu Forest Act.
[G.O. Ms. No. 136, Environment and Forest (FR-14),
26th November 2008.]
No. II(2)/EF/609/2008.—In  exercise of the powers
conferred by Section 16 of the Tamil Nadu Forest Act, 1882
(Tamil Nadu Act V of 1882), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby declares the area, the boundaries of which are
specified in the Schedule below as reserved forest with
effect on and from 24th June 2009:—
SCHEDULE
(1) Name of the District
:
Dharmapuri
(2) Name of the Taluk
:
Palacode
(3) Number and name
:
24. Erranahalli
of the Village
(4) Survey Field Number
:
Survey Field
Number 1270
(5) Name of the
:
Erranahalli Additional
Forest Block
Forest Block
(6) Extent of the
:
115.79.0 hectares.
Forest Block
 BOUNDARIES
North.—The boundary starts from the junction point of
Survey Numbers 1267 and 1270 of Village Number 24.
Erranahalli and Village Number 14. Erudhuguttahalli, the
boundary generally runs towards east along the southern
side of Survey Numbers 1267 and 1269 and reaches the
junction  point  of Survey Numbers 1269, 1126, 1127 and 1270.
East.—Thence the boundary generally runs towards the
south along the western side of Survey Numbers 1127,
1069, 1128 and 1073, western and southern sides of Survey
Number 1074, western side of Survey Number 1075 western,
southern and eastern sides of Survey Number 1129, western
side of Survey Numbers 1045, 1130, 1131, Northern, Western,
Southern sides of Survey Numbers 1132 and 1133, Western
side Survey Number 813 and reaches the junction point of
Survey Numbers 813, 1134 and 1270.
South.—Thence the boundary generally runs towards
south along the Western, Northern side of Survey Number
1134 and thence west along the northern side of Survey
Numbers 732, 733 and 736 and reaches the junction point
of Survey Numbers 736 and 1270 Erranahalli reserved forest.
West.—Thence the boundary generally runs towards north
on the eastern boundary of Erranahalli reserved forest and
thence on the common village boundary of Village No. 13.
Poppidi and Village Number 24 Erranahalli and Village
Number 14. Erudhuguttahalli reserved forest and reaches
the starting point.
NOTE: Survey Field Numbers 831 U.A.W. 1091 and  1092
(patta fields), lie within the forest block is excluded.
ADMITTED RIGHTS

28th November 2008.]
No. II(2)/HO/611/2008.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by Section 30 of the Protection of Human
Rights Act, 1993 (Central Act 10 of 1994), the Governor of
Tamil Nadu, with the concurrence of the Chief Justice of the
High Court of Judicature at Madras, hereby makes
the following amendments to the Home Department
Notification No. II(2)/HO/532/2003, dated the 17th July 2003,
published at page 312 of Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil
Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 3rd September 2003,
as subsequently amended.

“and Theni” in column (2) against the entry “9. Principal
District and Sessions Judge, Madurai” in column (1) thereof
shall be omitted.

Dharmapuri at Krishnagiri” in column (1), the entry
“4. Principal District and Sessions Judge, Dharmapuri”,
shall be substituted;
(iii) after the entry in “27. Principal District and Sessions

Appointment of the Judge for the Additional Special Court
under Narcotic Drugs and Psychotrophic Substances
Act, 1985.
[G.O. Ms. No. 1595, Home (Courts-II),
1st December 2008.]
No. II(2)/HO/612/2008.—Under sub-section (2) of the
Section 36 of the Narcotic Drugs and Psychotrophic
Substances Act, 1985  (Central Act 61 of 1985), the Governor
of Tamil Nadu, with the concurrence of the Chief Justice
of the High Court of Madras, hereby appoints
Thiru V. Ramamoorthy, I Additional District, Judge (PCR),
Thiruchirappalli as the Judge of the Additional Special Court
for exclusive trial of cases under Narcotic Drugs and
Psychotrophic Substances Act, Chennai in the place of
Thiru Dr. G. Jayachandran.
