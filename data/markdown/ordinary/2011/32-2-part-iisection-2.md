Award of the Tamil Nadu Chief Minister's Police Medal
for Excellence in Public Service on the occasion
of the Independence Day, 2011.
[G.O. Ms. No. 453, Home (SC), 12th August 2011.]
No. II(2)/HO/380/2011.—The following members of the
Indian Police Service and State Police Service of the
Tamil Nadu Police have been awarded the Tamil Nadu
Chief Minister's Police Medal for Excellence in Public Service
on the occasion of the Independence Day, 2011:—
(i)
Tmt. Hema Karunagaran, IPS,
Deputy Commissioner of Police (L&O),
Coimbatore City.
(ii)
Tmt. A. Thilagavathi,
Inspector of Police (Tech.),
Police Telecommunication Branch,
Coimbatore District.
2. The recipients of the Medal are eligible to draw a
lump sum grant Rs.5,000/- (Rupees five thousand only)
each, irrespective of the rank, and a gold Medal weighing
eight grams gold and two grams copper, as per the rules
governing the award of this Medal.
3. The Medals will be presented to the recipients by the
Hon'ble Chief Minister at a function, to be held later.
