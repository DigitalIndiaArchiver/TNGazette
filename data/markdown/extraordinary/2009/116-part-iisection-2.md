NOTIFICATION REGARDING THE PROCUREMENT OF CLOTH BY THE TAMIL NADU TEXTILE
CORPORATION FOR FREE UNIFORMS TO THE SCHOOL CHILDREN COVERED UNDER
PURATCHI THALAIVAR M.G.R. NUTRITIOUS MEAL PROGRAMME UNDER TAMIL NADU TRANSPARENCY
IN TENDERS ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 181, Finance (Salaries), 5th May 2009, Chithirai 22, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. II(2)/FIN/195(d)/2009.
Under clause (f) of Section 16 of the Tamil Nadu Transparency in Tenders Act, 1998 (Tamil Nadu
Act 43 of 1998), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notifies the procurement of khaki cloth, white poplin
cloth, blue casement cloth and blue half saree cloth by the Director of Handloom and Textiles required for
the free supply of uniforms to the beneficiaries covered under Puratchi Thalaivar M.G.R. Nutritious Meal
Programme from the Tamil Nadu Textile  Corporation, subject to the condition that the procurement will be
restricted to the said categories of cloth which are actually produced by the mills owned by the Tamil Nadu
Textile Corporation.
K. GNANADESIKAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
