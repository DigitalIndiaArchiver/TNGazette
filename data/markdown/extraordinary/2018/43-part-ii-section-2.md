TOURISM, CULTURE AND RELIGIOUS ENDOWMENTS DEPARTMENT
APPOINTMENT OF VICE-CHANCELLOR DR. PREMEELA GURUMURTHY 
UNDER THE TAMIL NADU MUSIC AND FINE ARTS UNIVERSITY ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 16, Tourism, Culture and Religious Endowments (C1-1), 30th January 2018, 
Thai 17, Hevilambi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2049.] 
No. II(2)/TCRE/100(k)/2018.
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 11 of the Tamil Nadu Music and Fine Arts 
University Act, 2013 (Tamil Nadu Act 30 of 2013) as amended by the Tamil Nadu Music and Fine 
Arts University (Amendment) Act, 2016 (Tamil Nadu Act, 16 of 2016), the Government hereby appoint 
Dr. Premeela Gurumurthy as Vice-Chancellor of Tamil Nadu Music and Fine Arts University for a period of 
three years or till she attains the age of seventy years whichever is earlier from the date of her assuming 
ofﬁ ce.
2.  The terms and conditions of the appointment will be issued separately.
APURVA VARMA,
Principal Secretary to Government.
