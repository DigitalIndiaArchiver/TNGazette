Additional District Judge IV, Coimbatore - Change of name 
from Thiru K. Poorna Jeya Anand to Thiru K. Poorana 
Jeya Anand - Ordered.]
The following Government order is Published:-
[G.O. Ms. No. 648, Public (Special-B), 4th October 2019, 
Purattasi 17, Vikari, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2050.]
Rඍඉඌ :
1. From Thiru K. Poorna Jeya Anand, Additional 
District Judge IV, Coimbatore, letter 
dated : 21-02-2019
2. From the Registrar (District Judiciary), 
High Court of Madras, letter ROC No. 21335/2019/
B1, dated : 26-06-2019
3. Government Letter No. 1414/A2/Spl-B/2019-1 Public 
(Special-B) Department, Dated :02-08-2019.

High Court of Madras, letter ROC No. 21335/2019/
B1, dated : 04-09-2019
Order :  
G.O. Ms. No. 648, Public (Special-B), 4th October
 
2019
In the reference second read above, the Registrar 
(District Judiciary), High Court of Madras has enclosed the
representation of Thiru. K. Poorna Jeya Anand, District 
Judge (Entry Level), formerly Additional District & Sessions 
Judge (PCR), Thanjavur now functioning as IV Additional 
District Judge, Coimbatore wherein the Judicial Oﬃ  cer has 
requested for change of his name as "Thiru K. Poorana 
Jeya Anand instead of Thiru K. Poorna Jeya Anand". 
The Registrar (District Judiciary), High Court of Madras 
has also enclosed necessary documents in support of the 
request of the Judicial Oﬃ  cer.
2. The Government have carefully examined the 
request of the oﬃ  cer together with documents and decided 
to accept the same.
3. Accordingly, the Government, direct that the 
change of name of the Judicial Oﬃ  cer be noted and 
adopted in all official records and correspondence
a s  "Thiru K. Poorana Jeya Anand instead of 
Thiru K. Poor na Jeya Anand".
