NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
II-2 Ex. (99)
HIGHER EDUCATION DEPARTMENT
DATE OF COMMENCEMENT OF THE “TAKSHASHILA UNIVERSITY” TO THE TAMIL NADU 
PRIVATE UNIVERSITIES ACT. 2019 (TAMIL NADU ACT 14 OF 2019.)
 [G.O.Ms. No. 90, Higher Education [K2], 23rd March 2023,  
பங்குனி 9, சுபகிருது, திருவள்ளுவர் ஆண்டு-2054.]
No. II(2)/HE/301(c)/2023.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 10 of the Tamil Nadu Private 
Universities Act, 2019 (Tamil Nadu Act 14 of 2019), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby specifies 
the 23rd March 2023, as the date on which ''Takshashila University'' shall commence to exercise 
its function.
                                 	
 
	
D. KARTHIKEYAN,
