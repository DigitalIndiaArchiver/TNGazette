NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
TAMIL DEVELOPMENT, RELIGIOUS ENDOWMENTS AND INFORMATION DEPARTMENT
DATE OF COMMENCEMENT OF THE TAMIL NADU HINDU RELIGIOUS AND CHARITABLE
ENDOWMENTS (SECOND AMENDMENT) ACT, 2012 (TAMIL NADU ACT 51 OF 2012)
[G.O. Ms. No. 414, Tamil Development Religious Endowments and Information (RE 4.2),
7th December 2012, Karthigai 22, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2043.]
No. II(2)/TDREI/815(e)/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section (1) of the Tamil Nadu Hindu
Religious and Charitable Endowments (Second Amendment) Act 2012 (Tamil Nadu Act 51 of 2012),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the 7th day of December, 2012, as the date on which
the said Act shall come into force.
M. RAJARAM,
Secretary to Government.
