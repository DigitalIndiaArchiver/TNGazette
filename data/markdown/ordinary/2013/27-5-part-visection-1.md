Winding up of the affairs of the Mudaiyur Stone Workers Industrial Co-operative Society Ltd., Ind.1264,
Thiruvannamalai and appointment of Official Liquidator.
(Rc. No. 2023/ICN/2013.)
No.VI(1)/282/2013.
Under the powers conferred on the Registrar of Industrial Co-operatives under Section 137(2)(b) of the Tamil Nadu
Co-operative Societies Act 1983 (Act 30 of 1983), the activities of the Mudaiyur Stone Workers Industrial Co-operative
Society Ltd., Ind. 1264. Thiruvannamalai have been ordered to be wound up with immediate effect by the Principal
Secretary/Industries Commissioner and Director of Industries and Commerce and the Registrar of Industrial Co-
operative Societies, Mandavelipakkam, Raja Annamalaipuram, Chennai-28 vide Proc. No. 2023/ICN/2013 dated 6-6-
2013.
Under Section 138(1) of the same Act, the Assistant Director of Industries and Commerce (IC)/Official Liquidator,
TAICO Bank, Chennai-8, has been appointed as the Official Liquidator of the said society for the purpose of liquidation”.
HARMANDAR SINGH,
Chennai-600 028,
Principal Secretary/Industries and Commissioner and
2013 June 6.
Director of Industries and Commerce/
Registrar of Industrial Co-operatives.

Variation to the Approved Melapalayam Detailed Developments Plan No. 2 of
Tirunelveli Local Planning Authority.
(Roc. No. 16225/2012/DP3.)
No.VI(1)/283/2013.—
In exercise of the powers conferred under sub section(1) of Section 33 of Town and Country Planning, Act 1971,
(Act No. 35 of 1972). The Commissioner of Town and Country Planning, Chennai-2, in the Proceedings Roc No. 16225/
2012 DP2 dated,   6-2013 proposes to make the individual draft variation for entire proposed Detailed Development
Plan roads 80'-0" “CC” road & 50'-0” “E5 E5” road to be deleted  in Melapalayam Ward, Kulavanikarpuram Village –
S.Nos. 982/1pt, 985/1pt & 989/1A1pt, to the confirmed Melapalayam Detailed Development Plan No. 2 by Director of
Town and Country Planning Proceeding Roc. No. 5493/10 DP3 dated:20-5-2011 of Tirunelveli Local Planning Authority.
The said notification was published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No. 22, part VI, Section-1, page 250, dated:
15-6-2011.
Any person affected or interested in this draft variation map within 60 days from the date of publication in the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, can request in person or submit in writing to the Member Secretary, Tirunelveli Local
Planning Authority any Objection & Suggestion relating thereto.
The variation with plan may be inspected of free cost at any time during office hours at the above said Local
Planning Authority office, Tirunelveli.
VARIATION
Wherever the expression Map No. 5 DDP (V)/DTCP No. 26/2010, occurs the expression DDP/V/DTCP No. 13/2013
shall be added at the end and to be read with.
1. In Schedule III, part-I (Form No. 5) the entry proposed “CC” 80-0" & E5 E5 50'-’” Detailed Development
Plan roads shall be deleted.
2. In Schedule–V (Form No.6) all the entries entire proposed ‘CC’ 80'-0" DDP road, Melapalayam ward,
Kulavanikarpuram Village S.Nos. 985/1pt, 989/1A1pt shall be deleted.
3. The variation annexed with the plan is made enforceable from the date of publication of confirmed variation
notification to be issued under Section 33(2) of Town and Country Planning Act 1971 and published in
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
