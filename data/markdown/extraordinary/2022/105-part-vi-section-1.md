NOTICE UNDER SECTION 3(1) OF TAMIL NADU ACQUISITION OF LAND FOR INDUSTRIAL PURPOSES ACT, 1997 READ 
WITH THE TAMIL NADU LAND ACQUISITION LAWS (REVIVAL OF OPERATION, AMENDMENT AND VALIDATION) 
ACT,2019
No. VI(1)/53(b)/2022.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that the land/lands specified in the Schedule below to be 
acquired for industrial purpose to wit to the formation of Doubling of Broad Gauge Railway line between Madurai –
Thoothukudi and it having already been decided that the amount of compensation to be awarded for the land/lands 
is to be paid out of the funds controlled or managed by General Manager (Scheme-II) RVNL. The following notice 
is issued under sub-section (1) of section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997. 
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997. 
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the land/lands specified in the Schedule below 
measuring an extent of 134.5 Sq. mtrs. of Ryot wet to the same, a little more or less needed for industrial purpose 
wit, for the formation of Doubling of Broad Gauge Railway line between Madurai –Thoothukudi work in Avaniyapuram 
Village of Madurai South Taluk in the District of Madurai.
The plan of the land/lands is/are kept in the office in the District Collector, Madurai may be inspected on any day 
during office hours.
