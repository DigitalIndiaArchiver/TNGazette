Extension of Direct Management of Anamalai Peria Pallivasal @ Pudu Pallivasal Wakf,
Pollachi Taluk, Coimbatore District.
(Rc. No. 8147/91/B3/CBE)
No.VI-3(a)/35/2016.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act 1995 as amended by
Act 27/2013, the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in item No.254/15 in Rc.No.8147/91/B3/CBE dated 30-12-2015
has resolved to ratify the period of direct management of Anamalai Peria Pallivasal @ Pudu Pallivasal Waqf, Pollachi Taluk,
Coimbatore District from 21-01-2015 to 29-12-2015 and also to extend the period of direct management of the above Wakf
for a period of one year from the Board’s order dated: 30-12-2015.

(Rc. No. 3637/14/B7/VLR)
No.VI-3(a)/37/2016.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 (1) of the Waqf Act 1995 as amended by
Act 27/2013, the Tamil Nadu Waqf Board in its resolution in item No. 98/15 in Rc.No.3637/14/B7/VLR
dated: 29-12-2015 has resolved to take over Khaderpet Masjid Waqf, Vaniyambadi, Vellore District under the direct
Management of the Tamil Nadu Waqf Board for a period of one year from the Board’s Order dated: 29-12-2015 and appoint
the Waqf Inspector, Thiruvannamalai as its Executive Officer.
