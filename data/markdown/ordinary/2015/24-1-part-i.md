NOTIFICATIONS BY GOVERNMENT
PRINTED AND PUBLISHED BY THE DIRECTOR OF STATIONERY AND PRINTING, CHENNAI
ON BEHALF OF THE GOVERNMENT OF TAMIL NADU
Order: No. 219, Public (Special-A), 5th June 2015.
The Government have considered the recommendation
of the High Court, Madras to depute Thiru C.V. Karthikeyan,
District Judge of Tamil Nadu State Judicial Service,
functioning on other duty as Registrar (Vigilance),
High Court, Madras to the Puducherry Judicial Service for
being posted as Chief Judge, Pondicherry vice
Thiru P. Velmurugan, who has been ordered to be repatriated
to his parent Department viz., Tamil Nadu State Judicial
Service for being posted in Tamil Nadu.
2. 
The 
Government 
accordingly 
direct 
that
C.V. Karthikeyan, District Judge of Tamil Nadu State Judicial
Service, functioning on other duty as Registrar (Vigilance),
High Court, Madras be deputed to the Puducherry Judicial
Service for being posted as Chief Judge, Pondicherry
vice Thiru P. Velmurugan, to be repatriated to Tamil Nadu
State Judicial Service.
(By Order of the Governor)
K. GNANADESIKAN,
Chief Secretary.
