NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
BACKWARD CLASSES, MOST BACKWARD CLASSES AND MINORITIES WELFARE DEPARTMENT
CONDUCT OF ELECTION FOR MEMBERS OF TAMIL NADU WAQF BOARD UNDER THE WAQF ACT, 1995.
DEFERMENT OF MUTAWALLIS ELECTION
[Letter No.4055/T1/2020-02, dated 18-08-2020]
No. II(2)/BCMBCMW/506(d)/2020.
Whereas Election programme for election of members to the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board from the electoral colleges consisting 
of Muslim Members of Parliament, State Legislature, Muslim Ex-Members of Bar Council and Mutawallis has been notiﬁ ed 
in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary dated 15-07-2020. As per the Notiﬁ cation, election for the category of 
Mutawallis was scheduled to be held on 19-08-2020.
The Hon’ble High Court of Madras, Writ Petitions W.P. No.726/2020, W.P. No.8377/2020 and W.P. No.9557/2020 were 
ﬁ led challenging the supersession of the Tamil Nadu Waqf Board and the election notiﬁ cation for reconstituting Waqf Board.
In the above Writ Petitions, Hon’ble High Court of Madras in its order dated 17-8-2020 has upheld the order of the 
supersession of the Tamil Nadu Waqf Board except in respect of two persons elected from Mutawalli category namely, Thiru. 
Syed Ali Akbar and Dr. Haja K. Majeed. 
In view of the above orders of the High Court of Madras, the election scheduled to be held on 19-08-2020 for electing 
two members in the category of Mutawallis is deferred.
 
CHANDRA MOHAN. B,
 
Election Authority and Principal Secretary to Government,  
 
Backward Classes, Most Backward  
 
Classes and Minorities Welfare Department.
