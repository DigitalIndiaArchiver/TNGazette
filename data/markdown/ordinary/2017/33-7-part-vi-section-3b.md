Result of Election Held to the Senate of the Tamil Nadu Dr.M.G.R Medical University, Chennai Election of One Professor
by the Teachers of all affiliated Dental Colleges from among themselves Under Section 18(3)(b) of the Tamil Nadu
Dr. M.G.R Medical University Chennai, Act, 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 37 of 1987).
(R.C. No. EIII(4)/61462/2015.)
No.VI-3(b)/20/2017.
Under Sub-Statute (1) of Statute 5 of Chapter XXI of the General Statutes of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R Medical University,
Chennai, it is hereby published that Dr. J. Johnson Raja, M.D.S., Prof. & Vice Principal, Department of Periodontics, Rajas
Dental College & Hospital, Kavalkinaru Junction, Tirunelveli Dist-627 105 has been elected to the Senate of the Tamil Nadu
Dr. M.G.R Medical University, Chennai by the teachers of all Affiliated Dental Colleges from among themselves Under Clause
(b) of Sub-Section (3) of Section 18 of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R Medical University, Chennai Act, 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act
37 of 1987) for a period of three years from 08-05-2017 to 07-05-2020 (or) so long as he holds the post of Professor of the Affiliated
Dental Colleges of this University, whichever is earlier.
Chennai-600 032.
Prof. Dr. T. BALASUBRAMANIAN,

Section 18(3) (a) of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R Medical University, Chennai Act, 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 37 of 1987).
The membership will be for the period as noted against each or till they hold the post of Professor of the concerned affiliated
College of this University, or till the date of their superannuation whichever is earlier.
