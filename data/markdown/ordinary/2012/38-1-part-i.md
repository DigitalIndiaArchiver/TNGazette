No. I/PUSP/32/2012.—The following Notification of the
Government of India, Ministry of Law and Justice (Department of
Justice), Jaisalmer House, 26, Man Singh Road, New Delhi-110
011, dated 20th September 2012 is republished:—
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (1) of article 217
of the Constitution of India, the President is pleased to appoint
Shri Justice Darmar Murugesan,  Judge of the Madras High Court,
to be the Chief Justice of the Delhi High Court, with effect from the
date he assumes charge of his office.
