Errata to Notification
The following errata shall be issued to the Tamil Nadu Electricity Regulatory Commission Notification No.TNERC/
CGRF & EO /6-7 dated 2nd December 2013, published at pages 618 and 619 of Part VI – Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government
Gazette dated the 25th December, 2013:-
ERRATA
In the said Notification :–
(1)
in the EXPLANATION / STATEMENT, for the expression “Tamil Regulations” read “Regulations”;
(2)
in the Tabular column under the heading “STATEMENT SHOWING THE EXISTING PROVISION AND THE PROVISION
AS AMENDED”,-
(i)
in column (i), for the heading “Existing Proviso” read “Existing Provision”.
(ii)
in column (2), for the heading “Proposed Amendment” read “Provision as amended”; and
(iii)
in columns (1) and (2), for the expression “there members” read “three members”.
