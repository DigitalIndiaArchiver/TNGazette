Amendment to Notification.
[G.O. Ms. No. 678, Home (Courts II), 18th August 2009.]
No. II(2)/HO/448/2009.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 9 of the Terrorist and
Disruptive Activities (Prevention) Act, 1987 (Central Act 28
of 1987), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, with the concurrence
of the Chief Justice, High Court, Madras makes the following
amendment to the Home Department Notification No. II(2)/
HO/3929(b-2)/91, published at page 1 of Part II-Section 2
of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated
the 11th July 1991 as subsequently amended:—
AMENDMENT
In the said Notification, for the expression “Thiru
A. Sampath, I Additional City Civil and Sessions Judge,
Chennai”, the following expression shall be substituted,
namely:—
“Thiru P. Ramalingam, I  Additional City Civil and Sessions
Judge, Chennai.”
S. MALATHI,
Principal Secretary to Government.
Notifications under Tamil Nadu Protection of Interests
of Depositors (in Financial Establishments) Act.
Ad-interiam order attaching of the Share Certificate and
other ancestral properties due to default in return of
deposits by certain Financial Establishments under
the Act.
Thiruvalargal B.S.R. Group of Companies, Madurai.
[G.O. Ms. No. 337, Home (Police-XIX), 24th April 2009.]
No. II(2)/HO/449/2009.—Whereas, complaints have been
received from a number of depositors that Tvl.  B.S.R. Group
of Companies, Madurai a Financial Establishment has
defaulted the return of deposits made by the depositors after
maturity;
2. And, whereas, the Government are satisfied that the
said Financial Establishment is not likely to return the deposits
to the depositors and hence, the Government have to protect
the interests of such depositors;
3. And, Whereas, the ancestral property and the Share
Certificates specified in the Schedule I and Schedule-II
respectively to this Order are alleged to have been owned
by the partners of the said Financial Establishment.
4. Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred
by Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Protection of Interests of
Depositors (in Financial Establishments) Act, 1997 (Tamil
Nadu Act 44 of 1997), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
makes an ad-interim order attaching the Share Certificates
of Tvl. Venkatachalam and Mahadevan and ancestral property
of Tvl. Mahadevan, Venkatachalam, Nagarajan and Kannan,
all are sons of A.V. Sankararaman, Partners of BSR Group
of Companies, Madurai, as specified in the Schedule-I and
Schedule-II respectively to this Order and transfers the control
over the said Share Certificates (672) and other immovable
property to the Competent Authority, namely, the District
Revenue Officer, Madurai, appointed under the provisions of
the said Act, for the purpose;
5. The Competent Authority is requested to pursue further
action in accordance with the procedures laid down in
sub-sections (3) and (4) of Section 4 of the said Act and
also the formalities prescribed in the Tamil Nadu Protection
of Interests of Depositors (in Financial Establishments)
Rules, 1997.
6. The Special Public Prosecutor, Special Court for the
Tamil Nadu Protection of Interests of Depositors  (in Financial
Establishments) Act, 1997 is requested to render necessary
assistance to the Competent Authority, in filing the application,
before the said Special Court.
