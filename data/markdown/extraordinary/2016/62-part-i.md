RELIEVED A MINISTER FROM THEIR MINISTERSHIP OF COUNCIL OF MINISTER.
[G.O. Ms. No. 245, Public (Special-B), 2nd March 2016, Maasi 19, Manmadha, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2047.]
No. I/PUSP/3(C-1)/2016.
I, K. Rosaiah, Governor of Tamil Nadu accept the recommendation of the Chief Minister that  Thiru T.K.M. Chinnayya,
Minister for Animal Husbandry be relieved from the Council of Ministers and direct that he be relieved of his duties and
responsibilities as Minister with immediate effect.
K. ROSAIAH,
Governor of Tamil Nadu.
ALLOCATION  OF  SUBJECTS  AMONG  MINISTER
[G.O. Ms. No. 246, Public (Special-B), 2nd March 2016, Maasi 19, Manmadha, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2047.]
No. I/PUSP/3(C-2)/2016.
Under Clause (3) of Article 166 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that
Tmt. B. Valarmathi, Minister for Social Welfare and Nutritious Noon Meal Programme shall hold full additional charge of the
portfolio ‘Animal Husbandry’ hitherto held by Thiru T.K.M. Chinnayya and direct that Tmt. B. Valarmathi, be designated as
Minister for Social Welfare, Nutritious Noon Meal Programme and Animal Husbandry.
K. GNANADESIKAN,
Chief Secretary.
