Amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu State Treasuries and Accounts Services.
[G.O. Ms. No. 153, Finance (Treasuries and Accounts-I), 26th May 2016, Vaikasi 13, Thunmugi,
Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2047.]
No. SRO B-37/2016.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu State Treasuries
and Accounts Services (Section 28A in Volume-II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1969).
2. The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 10th August 2015.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules,—
1, in rule 3,—
(I) in sub-rule (a), in the TABLE, in column (2) under the heading “Method of Appointment”, in item (i) against the entry
“Class II” in column (1) thereof, for the expression “Class III or”, the expression “Class III or Class III-A or” shall be
substituted.
(II) in sub-rule (bb), the following sub-rule shall be substituted, namely:—
“(bb) Out of vacancies in Class II both permanent and temporary, seventy percent of the vacancies shall be filled up
from among the holders of the post in Class III, ten per cent shall be filled up from among the holders of the post in
Class III-A and twenty per cent of the vacancies shall be reserved for appointment by recruitment by transfer from the category
of Section Officer of the Departments of Secretariat. The vacancies so reserved for the Section Officer of the Finance Department
and the Section Officer under the one unit be in the ratio of 1.3. out of every twenty vacancies in Class II, appointment shall
be made in the following order of rotation:—
1st to 7th Posts
Accounts Officers;
8th Post
Additional Treasury Officer;
9th Post
Section Officer (Finance Department);
10th Post
Section Officer (One unit);
11th to 17th Posts
Accounts Officers;
18th Post
Additional Treasury Officer;
19th and 20th Posts
Section Officer (One Unit).
The seniority of the persons selected to the post shall be fixed in the above cyclic order by which selection is made:
Provided that if no qualified candidate is available in the Finance Department, such vacancy shall be filled up by the
Section Officer of the Departments of Secretariat other than Law and vice-versa:
Provided further that if no qualified candidate is available, either in the Finance Department or in the Departments of
Secretariat other than Law, such vacancy shall be filled up by promotion from among holders of the post in Class III”.
Provided also that if no qualified candidate is available in the post of Additional Treasury Officer, such vacancy shall
be filled up by promotion from the holders of the post in Class III”.

Ad hoc Rules relating to the Temporary Post of Senior Lecturer  in Optometry at the Regional Institute of
Opthalmology and Government Opthalmic Hospital, Chennai in Tamil Nadu Medical Subordinate Service.

No. SRO B-38/2016.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following rules:—
2. The rules hereby made shall come into force on and from the 30th of May 2016.
RULES
The General and Special rules applicable to the holders of the permanent post under category 3 in class XIII of Branch IV
General of  the Tamil Nadu Medical Subordinate Services shall apply to the holders of the temporary posts of Senior Lecturer in
Optometry, sanctioned from time to time to the Regional Institute of Opthalmology and Government Opthalmic Hospital, Chennai
subject to the modifications specified in the following rules:-
2. Constitution.—The post shall constitute a separate category in the said class of Branch IV in the Tamil Nadu Medical
Subordinate Services.
3. Appointment.—Appointment to the post shall be made by direct recruitment.
4. Appointing Authority.—The appointing authority for the post shall be the Director of Medical Education.
5.  Qualification.—(a) Age.- No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post by direct recruitment, if he has
completed or will complete 30 years of age on the first day of July of the year in which the recruitment is made.
(b) Other qualifications.- No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post unless he possesses M.Sc., in
Optometry awarded by any University recognized by the University Grants Commission:
Provided that other things being equal preference shall be given to candidates who possess M.Phil in Optometry or
Ph.D., in Optometry.
6. Reservation of appointments.—The rule relating to the reservation of appointments (General Rule 22) shall apply
for appointment to the post by direct recruitment.
7. Probation.—Every person appointed to the post by direct recruitment shall from the date on which he joins duty be
on probation for a period of two years on duty within a continuous period of three years.
8. Pay.—There shall be paid to the holder of the post a monthly pay calculated in the pay Band of Rs.9300-34800 with Grade
Pay of Rs.4600.
J. RADHAKRISHNAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.

LAW DEPARTMENT
Amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Legal Educational Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 231, Law (LS), 10th June 2016, Vaikasi 28, Thunmugi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2047.]
No. SRO B-39/2016.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Legal Educational Service (Section
33 of Volume II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1969).
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st January 2006.
AMENDMENT
In the said Special Rules, for the expression  “Lecturers (Senior Scale)” and “Lecturers (Selection Grade)” wherever they
occur, the expression “Assistant Professors” and "Associate Professors” shall, respectively, be substituted
Amendment to Ad hoc Rules for the Post of Lecturer (Senior Scale) (Pre-Law) in the Law Colleges in Tamil Nadu
Legal Educational Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 232, Law (LS), 10th June 2016, Vaikasi 28, Thunmugi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2047.]
No. SRO B-40/2016.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Rules published with the Education Department
Notification No. SRO B - 498/85, dated the 19th November 1985, at Pages 612-613 of Part III—Section 1(b) of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 11th December 1985, as subsequently amended.
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st January 2006.

Professor (Pre-Law)” shall be substituted.
