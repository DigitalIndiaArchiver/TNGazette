 APPOINTMENT OF PRESIDING OFFICER TO THE TRIBUNAL HIGH COURT OF MADRAS AT CHENNAI
CONSTITUTION OF THE INDIAN CRIMINAL LAW AMENDMENT ACT, 1908.
[G.O. No. SS.I/121-31/2012, Public (S.C.), 5th January 2017]
No.II(2)/PU/11(a)/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 16-B of the Indian Criminal
Law Amendment Act, 1908 (Central Act XIV of 1908), as amended by the Indian Criminal Law Amendment
(Tamil Nadu Amendment) Act, 1998 (Tamil Nadu Act 42 of 1998), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
appoints Thiru Justice S. Nagamuthu, Judge, High Court of Madras at Chennai, as the Presiding Officer of
the Tribunal constituted for the purpose of Section 16-A of the said Act.
SHIV DAS MEENA,
Principal Secretary to Government.
