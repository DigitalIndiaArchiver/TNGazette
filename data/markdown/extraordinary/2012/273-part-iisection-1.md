RESIGNATION OF HON. THIRU D. JAYAKUMAR, SPEAKER.
[S.O. Ms. No. 149, Legislative Assembly Secretariat, 29th September 2012,
Purattasi 13, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2043.]
No. II(1)/LAS/21(b)/2012.
The Hon. Thiru D. Jayakumar, Speaker, Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly, resigned his office of Speaker
with effect from the forenoon of the 29th September, 2012 under Clause (b) of Article 179 of the Constitution
of India.
2. Consequently, under Clause (1) of Article 180 of the Constitution of India while the office of Speaker is
vacant, the duties of the office shall be performed by the Deputy Speaker, Hon. Thiru P. Dhanapal.
A.M.P. JAMALUDEEN,
Secretary,
Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly.
