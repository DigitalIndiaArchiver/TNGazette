WHEREAS Badlands of Karai Formation with Cretaceous Fossils along Karai-Kulakkanatham Section,
Perambalur District, Tamil Nadu has been declared by Geological Survey of India as National Geological
Heritage site vide Geological Survey of India resolution No. 193/150/CD/Geological Heritage Site/Tamil Nadu/
2015, dated the 6th May 2015;
AND WHEREAS the Director General, Geological Survey of India, Government of India in his letter dated
11th May 2015 has requested the Government of Tamil Nadu to issue necessary notification and
instruction for protection and conservation of the Badlands of Karai Formation with Cretaceous Fossils along
Karai-Kulakkanatham Section, Perambalur District, Tamil Nadu as a Geological Heritage Site;
AND WHEREAS the High Court of Madras in its order dated 07-08-2015 in W.P.No.12794/2011 has directed
the Government of Tamil Nadu to issue necessary notification and instruction for protection and conservation
of the Badlands of Karai Formation with Cretaceous Fossils along Karai-Kulakkanatham Section, Perambalur
District, Tamil Nadu as a Geological Heritage Site;
NOW THEREFORE the Government of Tamil Nadu hereby declare the Badlands of Karai Formation with
Cretaceous Fossiis along Karai-Kulakkanatham Section, Perambalur District, Tamil Nadu, as specified in the
Schedule, as Geological Heritage Site for conservation, protection and maintenance;
