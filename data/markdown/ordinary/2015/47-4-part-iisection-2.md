HOME DEPARTMENT
Appointment of certain person as the Special Judge to
preside over the Special Court for the exclusive Trial
of Bomb Blast Cases, Chennai under the National
Investigation Agency Act.
[G.O. (D) No. 836, Home (Courts-II), 19 ïõ‹ð˜ 2015,

No. II(2)/HO/719/2015.—Under sub-section (3) of
Section 11 read with sub-section (2) of Section 22 of the
National Investigation Agency Act, 2008 (Central Act 34 of 2008)
and in supersession of the Home Department Notification
No. II(2)/HO/560/2014 published at Page 641 of Part II—Section 2
of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the
17th September 2014 the Governor of Tamil Nadu, on the
recommendation of the Chief Justice of the High Court of
Madras, hereby appoints Thiru P. Murugan, Sessions Judge,
Sessions Court for the exclusive trial of Bomb Blast Cases,
Chennai at Poonamallee, as the Judge to preside over the
Special Court constituted at Chennai under sub-section (1) of
Section 22 of the said Act.
APURVA VARMA,
Principal Secretary to Government.
