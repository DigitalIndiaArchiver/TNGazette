HIGHWAYS AND MINOR PORTS DEPARTMENT
LEVY OF TOLLS ON THE VEHICLES ENTERING YERCAUD PANCHAYAT UNION AREA IN SALEM DISTRICT  
A CERTAIN PERIOD UNDER THE INDIAN TOLLS ACT.
[G.O. (D) No. 172, Highways and Minor Ports (HP1), 9th December 2021, 
 
No. II(2)/HWMP/871(a)/2021.
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (a) of  sub-section (2) of Section 2 of the Indian Tolls Act, 
1851(Central Act VIII of 1851), as  amended by the Tamil Nadu Tolls and Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act,1938 
(Tamil Nadu Act VI of 1938) and the  Indian Tolls (Tamil Nadu Amendment)  Act, 1942 (Tamil Nadu Act XIV of 1942), 
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby levies the tolls for the period upto the 31st March 2022 on the classes of motor 
vehicles speciﬁ ed in column (2) of the Table below,  which passes through Salem – Yercaud Road and Kuppanur - 
Ayothipattinam - Yercaud Road in the Yercaud Panchayat Union area in Salem District at the rates speciﬁ ed in the 
corresponding  entries in  column (4) thereof:  
Provided that such levy of tolls shall not be applicable to:-
 
i. 
Vehicles owned by the residents of Yercaud other than the vehicles used for commercial purpose.
 
ii. 
The vehicles of the oﬃ  ce of Yercaud Panchayat Union.
 
iii. 
The vehicles of the oﬃ  ce of the State and Central Government and Government undertakings.
