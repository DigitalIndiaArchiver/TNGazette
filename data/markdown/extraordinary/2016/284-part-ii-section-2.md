NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
COMMERCIAL TAXES AND REGISTRATION DEPARTMENT
EXEMPTION IN RESPECT OF THE TAX PAYABLE ON THE SALE OF GOODS MADE TO THE CONSULATE
OF RUSSIAN FEDERATION IN CHENNAI UNDER THE TAMIL NADU VALUE ADDED TAX ACT, 2006.
[G.O. (Ms)No. 128, Commercial Taxes and Registration (B2), 7th December 2016, Karthigai 22,
Thunmugi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu, 2047.]
No.II(2)/CTR/809(a)/2016.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 30 of the Tamil Nadu value Added Tax Act,
2006 (Tamil Nadu Act 32 of 2006), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes an exemption in respect of the tax
payable under the said Act on the sale of goods made to the consulate of Russian Federation in Chennai and
its officials in Tamil Nadu for official and personal use.
2. This Notification shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st July 2014.
C. CHANDRAMOULI,
Additional Chief Secretary to Government. (FAC)
