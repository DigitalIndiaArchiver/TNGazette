The Government of Tamil Nadu, having been satisﬁ ed 
that the lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below have to 
acquired for industrial purpose to wit, for the establishment of 
Doubling of Broad Gauge Railway Line  between Villupuram 
and Dindigul at 06.Balakrishnapuram Village, and it having 
already been decided that the amount of compensation 
to be awarded for the lands to be paid out of the funds 
controlled  or managed by Southern Railway. The following 
notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the 
Tamil Nadu Acquisition of land for industrial purposes Act 
1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of Tamil Nadu 
Acquisition of land for Industrial Purposes Act 1997 
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999),The Government of
Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands speciﬁ ed in the 
schedule below and measuring an extent of  0.43.53 (Hect.) 
wet land and 1.07.41(Hect.) Dry land and also needed for 
Industrial purpose to wit, for the establishment of  Doubling 
of  Broad Gauge Railway Line  between Villupuram and 
Dindigul at 06.Balakrishnapuram Village.
The plan of the land is kept in the ofﬁ ce of the 
Revenue Divisional Ofﬁ cer, Dindigul and may be inspected 
at any time during ofﬁ ce hours.
