ACQUISITION OF LANDS
[G.O. Ms. No. 24, Housing and Urban Development
(UD3-2), 25th January 2012.]
No. II(2)/HOU/67(a)/2012.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied
that the land specified in the schedule below and situated
in Adampakkam Village, Alandur Taluk, Kancheepuram
District having already been decided that the entire amount
of compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid
from the Railway authority. The following declaration is
issued under Section 6 of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894
(Central Act I of 1894).
DECLARATION
Under Section 6 of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894
(Central Act I of 1894) the Government of Tamil Nadu hereby
declares that the land specified in the schedule below and
measuring 0.3038.0 Square Metre or 32710 Square Feet be
the same a little more or less are needed for public purpose
to wit, for the Mass Rapid Transit System, Adampakkam
Railway Station, under sub-section (1) of Section 17 of the
said Act, the Governor of Tamil Nadu further directs that the
possession of the said lands may be taken on the expiry of 15
days from the date of publications of the notice mentioned
in Section 9 (1) of the Act.
A plan of the Lands is kept in the Office of the Special
Tahsildar (Land Acquisition), M.R.T.S, Phase II Extension,
Thirumailai 
Railway 
Station 
building, 
Mylapore,
Chennai-600 004, and may be inspected at any time during
office hours.
