GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
 Variation to the Approved Master Plan for the Madurai Local Planning Area
(Roc.No. 1773/15 Mathi 2)
[G.O (2D)No. 87, H & UD (UD4 (1) Department, dated 22.6.2016.]
No. VI(1)/308/2016.
 In  exercise of  the  powers conferred  under sub-section (4) of Section 32 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country
Planning  Act,  1971 (Tamil  Nadu  Act 35  of 1972) and Government   Order Ms. No. 94, Housing and Urban Development
UD4  (12)  Department dated 12.06.2009 which has been published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No. 27,
Part  II-Section 2,  Page 228, dated 15.07.2009,  the  following  variation  are  made  to  the  Master  Plan  for  the
Madurai   Local  Planning Area  approved   under the   said   Act and     Published  in   the  Housing  and  Urban
Development  Department Notification  No.II(2)/HOU/645/95 at  page  No. 190  and 191 of part--II, Section 2 of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated  the  2nd February 1995.
VARIATION
In the said Master Plan in part II "LAND USE SCHEDULE"  under Keelamathur Village Number and Name
129. Keelamathur  under the heading VI Agricultural use Zone to IV heading  Educational use Zone.
Against the entry  VI Agricultural use Zone  S.F.No.48/3, 49/1B & 49/6B shall be deleted.
Against the entry IV Educational use Zone S.F.No.48/3, 49/1B & 49/6B shall be substituted.
Madurai,
P. YOGARAJA,
30th August 2016.
Member Secretary (incharge),
Madurai Local Planning Authority.
