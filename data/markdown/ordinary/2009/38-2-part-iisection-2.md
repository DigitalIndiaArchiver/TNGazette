Remission of tax surcharge, additional surcharge and
additional tax payable for certain period on the sale of
Peas and Peas Dhall under the Tamil Nadu Value
Added Tax Act.
[G.O. (2D) No.182, Commercial Taxes and Registration (D2),
1st September 2009, Aavani 16,Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040]
No.II(2)/CTR/486/2009.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by Section 31 read with sub-section (4) of Section 88
of the Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax Act, 2006 (Tamil Nadu
Act 32 of 2006) and read with sub-section (2) of Section
3 and sub-section (2) of Section 3-A of the erstwhile Tamil
Nadu  Sales Tax (Surcharge) Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 24
of 1971), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby remits a sum
of Rs. 96,71,771/- (Rupees ninety six lakhs seventy one
thousand seven hundred and seventy one only) being the
surcharge, additional surcharge and additional tax payable
on the sale of Peas and Peas Dhall in respect of the period
from the 31st July 1984 to the 24th December 1992 under
the said Acts.
RAJEEV RANJAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
