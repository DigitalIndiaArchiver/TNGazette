GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI
Taking over Sunnath Jamath Mosque & Sheik Kairullah Mosque, Theradi, Little Kancheepuram,
Kancheepuram District under Direct Management.
(Lr. Rc. No. 12532/2012/ B4/ KPM.)
No. VI-3(a)/122/2014.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 (1) of the Wakf Act 1995, as amended by
Act 27/2013, the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in item No.126/14 in Rc.No.12532/12/B4/KPM, dated
26-8-2014 has ordered to take over Sunnath Jamath Mosque and Sheik Kairullah Mosque, Theradi, Little Kancheepuram,
Kancheepuram District under the Direct Control of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of one year from the date
of Board’s Order (26-08-2014) and to appoint Wakf Inspector, Kancheepuram as its Executive Officer.
A. MOHAMED MEERAN,
Chennai-600 001,
District Revenue Officer /
18th December 2014.
