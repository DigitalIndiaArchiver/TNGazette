No. SRO B-91/2015.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Engineering
Subordinate Service (Section 8 in Volume-III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1970).
2. (1) The amendment 1 hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 22nd February 1977.
(2)  The amendments 2(a), 2(c) and 3 hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 22nd December
1986.
(3) The amendment 2(b) hereby made shall come into force on the date of issue of this order.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules,-
1. for the expression “Supervisor” wherever it occurs, the expression “Junior Engineer” shall be substituted;
2. in Part I, in rule 2,-
(a) in sub-rule (a), in the first proviso for the existing entry “Supervisor (category 1)” in column (1) and the  corresponding
entry in column (2), under the heading “Branch I-Engineering Branch”, the following entries shall respectively be substituted,
namely:-
“Junior Engineer
Promotion from the posts of Overseer or
(Category 1)
Technical Assistant or Draughting Officer”;
(b) sub-rule (b) and (d) shall be omitted;
(c) sub-rule (c) shall be omitted;
3. in Part II, under the heading “Branch I-Engineering Branch”, in rule 2, in sub-rule (a), for the expression “Promotion from
Overseers or Technical Assistants or Draughting Officers” occurring in column (2,) against the category “Junior Engineer” in
column (1), as so substituted, the expression “Promotion from Overseers, Technical Assistants and Draughting Officers” shall
be substituted.

No. SRO B-94/2015.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Engineering
Subordinate Service (Section 8 in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1970).
2.(1). The amendments 1(a), 1(b), 1(c) and 2(a) hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the
24th November 1982.
(2). The amendment 2(b) hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on 22nd December 1986.
AMENDMENTS.
In the said Special Rules,-
1. in Part I, in rule 2, in sub-rule (a), in the first proviso, -
(a)
under the heading “Branch I-Engineering Branch”, for the expression “Special Grade Technical Officer” in
column (1), the expression “Special Grade Draughting Officer” shall be substituted;
(b)
for the expression “Senior Technical Officer” in column (2), the expression “Senior Draughting Officer” shall be
substituted;
(c)
for the expression “Junior Technical Officer” in the second proviso, the expression “Junior Draughting Officer”
shall substituted.
2. in Part II, under the heading “Branch I - Engineering Branch”, -
(a)
for the expressions “Special Grade Technical Officer”, “Senior Technical Officer”, “Technical Officer”, “Junior
Technical Officer”, wherever they occur, the expressions “Special Grade Draughting Officer”, “Senior Draughting
Officer”, “Draughting Officer”, “Junior Draughting Officer”, shall respectively, be substituted;
(b)
in rule 12, for the expression “Draughtsman”, the expression “Junior Draughting Officer, Draughting Officer,
Senior Draughting Officer including Assistant Draughtsman” shall be substituted.
