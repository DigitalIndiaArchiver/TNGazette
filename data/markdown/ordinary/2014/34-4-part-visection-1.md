Variations to the Approved Master Plan for the Kurichi New Town Development Plan
(Rc. No. 628/2013/KNTDA.)
No. VI (1)/277/2014.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 32 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning
Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu  Act 35 of 1972) and in exercise of powers conferred by the G.O.Ms. No. 94, Housing and Urban
Development (UD 4(1) Department, dated 12-6-2009 which has been published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette
(No. 27) Part II—Section 2, Page No. 228, dated 15-7-2009 the following variations are made to the Master Plan for the
Kurichi New Town Development Plan approved under the said Act and published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette
Part II—Section 2 in Page Nos. 380 and 381, dated 3-5-1995.
VARIATIONS
In the said Master Plan, in the "LAND USE SCHEDULE" under the heading Kurichi New Town Development Plan
under the sub-heading (a) Chapter VIII, under land use schedule.
1.
Under the sub-heading "AGRICULTURAL USE ZONE" in Annexure XI, against the entry No. 59. Kurichi Village,
for the expression "730 to 737" the expression 730 to 737 (except 733/1B, 734/1B, 735/2 & 736/1A) shall be substituted.
1. Under the sub-heading "EDUCATIONAL USE ZONE" in Annexure IX, against the entry No. 59. Kurichi Village,
for the expression 733/1B, 734/1B, 735/2 & 736/1A shall be added after the entry 701.
Coimbatore-18,
G. NAGARAJAN,
28th August 2014.
Member-Secretary,
Kurichi New Town Development Authority.
