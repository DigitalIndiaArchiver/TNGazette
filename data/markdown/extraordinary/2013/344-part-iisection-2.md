No. II(2)/MAWS/884(b)/2013.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that
the lands specified in the schedule below have to be acquire
for a public purpose and it having already been decided that
the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the land
is to be paid out of funds provided by the Corporation of
Chennai. The declaration is issued under Section 6 of the
Land Acquisition Act 1984 (Central Act 1 of 1894).
Under Section 6 of the Land Acquisition Act 1894 (Central
Act 1 of 1894), the Government of Tamil Nadu hereby declares
that the lands specified in the Schedule below and measuring
an extent of 1750 Sq.Ft. be the same, a little more or less are
needed for public purpose, for the formation of Rail Over
Bridge Scheme at Korukkupettai in Tondiarpet Village Fort –
Tondiarpet Taluk, Chennai–3. Under urgency clause, sub-
section (2) of Section 17 of the said Act, the Government of
Tamil Nadu further directs that the possession of the said
lands to be taken on the expire of 15 days from the date of
publication of notice mentioned in Section 9 (1) of the Act. A
plan of lands kept in the Office of the Tahsildar, Fort–Tondiarpet
Taluk, Chennai-3 and may be inspected at any time during
office hours.
