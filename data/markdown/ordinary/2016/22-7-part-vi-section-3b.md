Under Sub-Statute (1) of Statute 5 of Chapter XXI of the General Statutes of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University,
Chennai, it is hereby published that the following members have been elected to the Senate of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R.
Medical University, Chennai, by the teachers of each affiliated Medical College from among themselves under Section
18(3)(a) of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai Act, 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 37 of 1987).
The membership will be for the period as noted against each or till they hold the post of Professor of the concerned
affiliated College of this University, or till the date of their superannuation whichever is earlier.
