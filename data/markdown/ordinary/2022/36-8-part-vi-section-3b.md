UNIVERSITY AUTHORITIES
UNIVERSITY OF MADRAS
Elections to [i] the Syndicate, [ii] the Senate and [iii] the Standing Committee of the Academic Council by the Academic 
Council.
 (No. V.1/AC Election/08-2022/259)
No.VI-3(b)/25/2022. 
It is hereby notified that the election to [i] THE SYNDICATE, [ii] THE SENATE and [iii] THE STANDING COMMITTEE OF 
THE ACADEMIC COUNCIL, will be held under Section 18-1[a] - Other members [2], Section 14-1(a) - Other Members [2] and 
Section 24[k] of the Madras University Act, 1923.
[i] TO THE SYNDICATE - SECTION 18-1[a] - OTHER MEMBERS [2]
FIVE MEMBERS to be elected by the Academic Council from among its members of four shall be teachers (as per statute) of 
affiliated colleges and one shall be a teacher (as per statute) of Approved Colleges
[ii] TO THE SENATE - SECTION 14-1[a] - OTHER MEMBERS [2]
TEN MEMBERS to be elected by the Academic Council from among its members.
[iii] TO THE STANDING COMMITTEE OF THE ACADEMIC COUNCIL [Section 24[k]
TWO MEMBERS - Two shall be the Principal (as per statute) or Teacher (as per statue) of affiliated colleges to be elected by 
the Academic Council
The above election will be conducted on the date of the meeting of the Academic Council scheduled to be helad on SATURDAY, 
the 24th September 2022.
