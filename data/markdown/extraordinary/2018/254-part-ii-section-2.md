The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed 
that the lands Speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below have to be 
acquired for Industrial Purposes, to wit, for Second Mine 
Expansion Scheme of Neyveli Lignite Corporation India Limited 
and it having already been decided that the entire amount 
of compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid 
out of the funds controlled or managed by Neyveli Lignite 
Corporation India Limited. The following notice is issued under 
sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of 
Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997.
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu 
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Government of Tamil Nadu 
hereby acquires the lands speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below 
and measuring 3.43.5 Hectares of R.Dry land to the same, a 
little more or less needed for Industrial purposes, to wit, for 
Second Mine Expansion Scheme of Neyveli Lignite Corporation 
India Limited. 
 
The plans of the lands are kept in the ofﬁ ce of the 
Special Deputy Collector/Land Acquisition, Neyveli-2 and may 
be inspected at any time during ofﬁ ce hours.
