The Government of Tamil Nadu having been  satisﬁ ed that 
the lands  speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below have to be acquired 
for industrial purpose to wit for Rail  Vikas Nigam  Limited, 
Chennai and it having already been decided that the entire 
amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be 
paid out of the funds controlled of managed  by  Rail Vikas 
Nigam  Limited, Chennai. The following notice is issued under 
sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of 
Land for industrial Purposes Act, 1997.
[ 1 ]
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu 
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act,1997 
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu 
hereby acquires the lands speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below 
and measuring  0.35.0  hectare to the same, a little more 
or less needed for industrial purposes, to wit, for Doubling 
of Broad Gauge Line Between Villupuram-Dindigul  Scheme 
of Rail Vikas Nikam  Limited, Chennai.
The plans of the lands are kept in the ofﬁ ce of the 
Revenue Divisional Ofﬁ cer, Vridhachalam  and may be 
inspected at any time during ofﬁ ce hours.
