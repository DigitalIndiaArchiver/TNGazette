TRANSPORT DEPARTMENT
ACQUISITION OF LANDS
[G.O. Ms. No.52, Transport (I.2), 21st March 2012.]
No. II(2)/TRA/173(a)/2012.
The Governor of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that
the lands specified in the schedule below have to be acquired for
industrial purpose, to wit for the Chennai Airport Expansion
Scheme and it having already been decided that the entire
amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands to be
paid out of the funds controlled or managed by Government.
The following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section
3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes
Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Acquisition of Land  for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby acquires the lands specified in the schedule below
and measuring 1.20.6 hectare, to the same, a little more or
less needed for Industrial Purpose, to wit, for the Chennai
Airport Expansion Scheme.
The plan of the lands are kept in the office of the Special
Tahsildar (Land Acquisition), Chennai Airport Expansion
Scheme, Unit III, Sriperumbudur and may be inspected at any
time during office hours.
