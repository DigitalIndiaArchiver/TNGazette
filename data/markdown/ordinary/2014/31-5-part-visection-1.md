[Rc. No. E1(1)/237/99587/2012.]
FORM-A2
[Notice under rule 35A(4) of Tamil Nadu Cinema (Regulation) Rule, 1957.]
No. VI (1)/262/2014.
Whereas M.M. Theatre and Enterprises, Door No. 207, Thiruvottiyur High Road, Tondiarpet, Chennai-81 has applied for
No Objection Certificate for conversion of the existing permanent      M.M. Theatre into Two Permanent Mini AC Theatres viz.
Screen-1 and Screen-2 at Survey No. 3579/2, and Door No. 207, Thiruvottiyur High Road, Tondiarpet, Chennai-81.
Notice is hereby given that any person having any objection to the grant of “No Objection Certificate” above said site on the grounds
of non-compliance by the applicant with any of the provisions of the Tamil Nadu Cinemas (Regulation)
Act, 1955 (T.N.Act  IX of 1955)  or  with any  of  the  Rules thereunder should  file  his  objection  in  writing  with  the Licensing
Authority (Commissioner of Police, Greater Chennai) within 15 days of publication of this Notice.
1. Any objection filed after the period shall be liable to be summarily rejected.

S. GEORGE,
23rd July 2014.

Variations to the Approved Chithode New Town Development Plan of Chithode New Town Development Area
(Roc. No. 331/2013 (CNTDA)
[G.O. Ms. (2D) No. 74, Housing and Urban Development (UD IV-1) Department, dated 17th July 2014.]
No. VI (1)/264/2014.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 32 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning
Act 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act, 35 of 1972) and in exercise of powers conferred by the G.O. Ms. (2D) No. 74, Housing and
Urban Development (UD IV-1) Department, dated 17th July 2014), The Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following
variations to the Chithode New Town Development Plan approved under the said Act and published in the Housing and
Urban Development Department Notification No. II(2)/HOU/1204/99, Page No. 516 of Part-II, Section 2 of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette dated the 27th October 1999.
VARIATIONS
In the said New Town Development Plan in Annexure “C” under the heading ‘Land Use of Chithode New Town
Development Plan’ in the tabular column.
In ‘Annexure V under the heading, 1B Mixed Residential use Zone after serial number 1 in column (1) and the entries
relating there to in columns (2) and (3) there of the following serial number and the entries shall, respectively be added
namely:—
1. MR-I, 58, Chithode Village, S.F. No. 54/8A1 with an extent of 1.22 ¼ Acres.  (0.49.5 Hectares)
In ‘Annexure IV under the heading, 1A Primary Residential Use Zone after serial number 1 in column (1) and the entries
relating there to in columns (2) and (3) there of the following serial number and the entries shall, respectively be deleted
namely:—
1. PR-I, 58, Chithode Village, S.F. No. 54/8A1 with an extent of 1.22 ¼ Acres. (0.49.5 Hectares.)
Erode,
P. RANGANATHAN,
8th August 2014.
Member-Secretary,
Chithode New Town Development Authority.
Draft Variation to the Approved Vishnukanchi Detailed Development Plan No. 3 of Kancheepuram
Local Planning Area.
(Roc. No. 8115/2013/DP1.)
No. VI(1)/265/2014.
In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (1) of Section 33 of Town and Country Planning Act, 1971
(Act No.35 of 1972). The Commissioner of Town and Country Planning, in the Proceedings Roc.No.8115/2013/DP1,
dated 16-7-2014. Proposes to make the following variation to the approved Vishnukanchi Detailed Development Plan
No.3 of Kancheepuram Local Planning Area and approved by the Director of Town and Country Planning Proceeding
Roc.No.17086/2004/DP1, dated, 17-2-2005 and the fact of this approval in Form No.12 Published in Tamil Nadu Government
Gazette No.41, Part VI—Section 1, Page 318, dated, 26-10-2005, Publication No.VI(1)/446/2005.
Any person affected or interested in this draft variation may within SIXTY days from the date of publication of this
notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette can represent in person or submit in writing to the Member-Secretary,
Kancheepuram Local Planning Authority any objections and suggestions relating thereto.
The variation with plan may be inspected on free cost at any time during office hours at the above said Local Planning
Authority Office.
