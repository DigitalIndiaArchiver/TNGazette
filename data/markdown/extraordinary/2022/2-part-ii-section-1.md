“In exercise of the powers conferred upon me by clause (1) of Article 174 of 
the Constitution of India and in supersession of the notification issued in S.O.Ms.
No.101, Legislative Assembly Secretariat, dated the 13th December, 2021 and 
published in an extraordinary issue of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette dated the 
13th December, 2021, I, R.N. RAVI, Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby summon the 
Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly to meet at 10.00 A.M. on Wednesday, the 5th January, 
2022 in the Multi-purpose Hall, 3rd Floor, Kalaivanar Arangam, Omandurar Government 
Estate, Wallajah Salai, Chennai-600 002, instead of Assembly Chamber, Secretariat, 
Chennai-600 009, as already notiﬁ ed.
Raj Bhavan, 
R.N. RAVI,
Guindy,
