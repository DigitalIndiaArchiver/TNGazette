In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Societies Registration
(Amendment) Act, 2010 (Tamil Nadu Act 2 of 2010), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the 1st day of April 2010,
as the date on which the said Act shall come into force.
