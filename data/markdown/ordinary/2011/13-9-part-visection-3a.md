Extension of Direct Management of Syed Sirajudeen Aulia Durgha @ Buddimasha Thaikal and
Perungudi Pallivasal, Madurai Town and District.
(Rc. No. 15954/03/B8/Mdu.)
No. VI-3(a)/25/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per provisions  under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995, the Tamil Nadu Wakf
Board in its resolution in item No. 21/11 (Rc. 15954/03/B8/Mdu.) dated 17th February 2011 has ordered to extend the period
of direct management of Syed Sirajuddin Aulia Durgha @ Buddimasha Thaikkal and Perungudi Pallivasal, Madurai for a further
period of One year from 4th February 2011.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
23rd February 2011.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
Removal of Managing Committee and Taking Over Wakf under Direct Management of Wakf Board,
in respect of Narimedu Muslim Jamath Pallivasal, Madurai Town.
(Rc. No. 15206/09/B14/Mdu.)
No. VI-3(a)/26/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 64(g) of the Wakf Act, 1995, the
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in item No. 161/09 (Rc. No. 15206/09/B14/Mdu.), dated 6th July 2010  has ordered
to remove the managing committee in respect of Narimedu Muslim Jamath Pallivasal Madurai Town from the date of
notification and to take over the above Wakf under the direct management of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board as required as
per Section 65(1) of the Wakf Act by appointing the Wakf Inspector/Madurai as its Executive Officer for a period
of Six Months.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
28th February 2011.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
