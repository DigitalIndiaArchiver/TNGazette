No. SRO B-82/2019.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Government Press 
Subordinate Service under Part III B, (Section 14 in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 2016).
2. 	 (i) The amendments (1), (2)(a), (b) hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on and from the 
27th October, 1979. 
 
(ii) The amendments (2)(c) hereby made shall come into force on the date of publication of this Notification.
 Amendments.
In the said Rules, 
	
(1) in rule (1), under the heading "Class III Machine", for Categories "(7) Senior Machine Minder" and "(8) Junior 
Machine Minder", the Category" (7) Machine Minder" shall be substituted; 
  (2) In the Annexure (referred to in rules 2 and 4(b), in the Table, under the heading "CLASS III - MACHINE",- 
	
(a) 	 for the entries "Senior Machine Minder and Junior Machine Minder" in column (2) and the entry "Senior Machine 
Minder or Junior Machine Minder" in column (3) against the entry "(4) Computor (Machine)" in column (1) thereof, the entry 
"Machine Minder" shall, respectively, be substituted; 
	
(b) 	 for the  entry, "Senior Machine Minder" in columns (2) and (3) against the entry "(6) Machine operator" in 
column (1) thereof, the entry "Machine Minder" shall, respectively, be substituted; 
	
(c) 	 for the categories "(7) Senior Machine Minder" and "(8) Junior Machine Minder" in column (1) and the entries 
relating thereto in columns (2) and (3) thereof, the following Categories and entries shall, respectively, be substituted, namely:-
