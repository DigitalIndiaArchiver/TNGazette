 TREASURIES AND ACCOUNTS DEPARTMENT, TAMIL NADU
(Section 57 of Aadhaar Act 2016 effect from 26-03-2016 permits the use of Aadhaar number 
for establishing the identity of an individual for any purpose pursuant to any law or any contract to this effect)
(Rc. No. 53760/2016/E2)
No. SRO C-30/2017.
Aadhaar Requirements: For the Beneﬁ ciaries under various programmes such as (i) Issue of ID card to the pensioners, 
(ii) Mustering of pensioner through Digital Life Certiﬁ cate availing services through Jeevan Pramaan Portal.
1. 
Treasuries and Accounts Department, Government of Tamil Nadu has decided to issue ID Cards to the pensioners 
as per G.O.Ms.No.87, Finance (Pension) Department, Dated:10-04-2017. 
2. 
It has been decided that mustering of the pensioners may also been done through generation of Digital Life Certiﬁ cate 
by availing the services through Jeevan Pramaan Portal. 
3. 
For availing services of getting pensioners' Identity Card, the pensioners shall use the Aadhaar card as one of the 
Identity proof. Apart from that the pensioners shall also produce any one of the following Identity proof.
1) 
Pension Payment Order 
2) 
Voter ID Card 
3) 
Income Tax, PAN Card 
4) 
Family Card 
5) 
Driving License 
4. 
The Pensioners shall approach the e-Seva Centre / Common Service Centre and with the proof of Identity as above 
can avail the services for receiving the pensioners' ID card. 
5. 
For Annual Mustering purpose, the pensioners can approach the Pension Pay Ofﬁ ce / District Treasury / Sub Treasury 
concerned or any other ofﬁ ces of the Treasury Department or the e-Seva Centre / Common Service Centre and 
shall produce Aadhaar card for the generation of Digital Life Certiﬁ cate by availing the services through the Jeevan 
Pramaan Portal of Government of India. 
6. 
The Pensioners who do not possess Aadhaar No. they can approach any of the Aadhaar Enrollment Centre located 
in the nearest Taluk Ofﬁ ce, Municipal Ofﬁ ce, Collectorates, Zonal Ofﬁ ces of Corporation of Chennai or any other 
Centres.
