ACQUISITION OF LANDS
[G.O. Ms. No. 173, Housing and Urban Development
(UD3-2), 28th November 2011.]
No.II(2)/HOU/467(c)/2011.
Whereas, 
it 
appears 
to 
the 
Government 
of
Tamil  Nadu that the Lands specified in the Schedule below
and situated in Adambakkam Village, Alandur Taluk,
Kancheepuram District are needed for a public purpose to wit
for the implementation of the Mass Rapid Transit System,
Railway Station for Adambakkam between Velachery to
St. Thomas Mount, notice to that effect is hereby given to all
whom it may concern in accordance with the provisions of
sub-section (1) of Section 4 of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894
(Central Act I of 1894).
And, whereas, It has become necessary to acquire the
immediate possession of the Lands specified in the schedule
below, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that the
lands be acquired under the provisions of sub-section (1) of
the Section 17 of the said Act.
Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by
sub-section (2) of Section 4 of the said Act, the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby authorizes the Special Tahsildar (Land
Acquisition), Mass Rapid Transit System, Phase-II, Extension
at Thirumailai Railway Station, Chennai-4 his staff and
workman to exercise the powers conferred by the said
sub-sections.
Under section 3(c) of the Act, the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the Special Tahsildar (Land
Acquisition), 
Mass 
Rapid 
Transit 
System,
Phase-II, Extension at Thirumailai Railway Station, Chennai-4
to perform the functions of the Collector under the said Act.
Under sub-section (4) of Section 17 of the
said Act, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that in
view of the urgency of the case, the provisions of Section 5A
of the said Act, shall not apply in this case.
