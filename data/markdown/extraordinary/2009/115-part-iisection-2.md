NOTIFICATION REGARDING THE PROCUREMENT OF COAL BY THE TAMIL NADU CEMENTS CORPORATION
LIMITED EITHER FROM THE TAMIL NADU ELECTRICITY BOARD OR THE TAMIL NADU NEWSPRINT
AND PAPERS LIMITED UNDER TAMIL NADU TRANSPARENCY IN TENDERS ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 179, Finance (Salaries), 4th May 2009, Chithirai 21, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. II(2)/FIN/195(c)/2009.
Under clause (f) of Section 16 of the Tamil Nadu Transparency in Tenders Act, 1998 (Tamil Nadu
Act 43 of 1998), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notifies the procurement of coal by the Tamil Nadu
Cements Corporation Limited either from the Tamil Nadu Electricity Board or the Tamil Nadu Newsprint and
Papers Limited whichever is advantageous.
K. GNANADESIKAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
