In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-sections (1) and (3) of Section 10  of the Tamil Nadu
Dr. M.G.R. Medical University Act, 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act, 37 of 1987), the Chancellor hereby appoints Prof.
D. Shantharam, Professor of Diabetology, Madras Medical College and Rajiv Gandhi Government General
Hospital, Chennai as the Vice-Chancellor of the said University for a period of three years with effect from
the date of his assuming office or till he attains the age of seventy years, whichever is earlier.
J. RADHAKRISHNAN,
Secretary to Government.
