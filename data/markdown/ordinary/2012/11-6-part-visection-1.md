Variation to the Approved Detailed Development Plan No. 4 of Cumbum Local Planning Area.
(Roc.No.17323/2005/DP2.)
No. VI(1)/97/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (1) Section 33 of Town and Country Planning Act, 1971
(Act No.35 of 1972) the Commissioner of Town and Country Planning, Chennai in his proceedings Roc.No.17323/2005/DP2,
dated 20-2-2012, proposes to make the following variation to the Cumbum Detailed Development Plan No.4 of Cumbum
Local Planning Area approved by the Director of Town and Country Planning proceedings Roc. No.14472/94/DPI, dated
29-6-2004 published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette 6, Part VI—Section I, Pages 130 and 131 dated 15-2-1995
(No. VI(1)/207/95).
2. Any person affected or interested in this variation may within Sixty days from the date of publication of this notification
in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, can represent in person or submit in writing to the Member-Secretary, Cumbum
Local Planning Authority any objections and suggestions relating thereto.
3.
The Variation with plan may be inspected at free of cost at any time during office hours at the above said Local
Planning Authority Office.
VARIATION
1.
The entire Cumbum-Detailed Development Plan No. 4 of Cumbum Local Planning Area is comprehensively varied
by the Commissioner of Town and Country Planning vide Proceedings Roc.No.17323/05/DP2, dated 20-2-2012.
2.
The plan thus varied is numbered as Map No. 5 DDP(V)/DTCP No.4/2012.
3.
The Detailed Development clauses and schedules annexed with this plan are make enforceable from the date
of publication of the confirmed notification to be issued under Section 33 (2) of the Act and published in Tamil Nadu
Government Gazette.

makes the following variations to the New Town Development Plan of the Hosur New Town Development Area approved
under this said Act and published in the Housing and Urban Development Department Notification No.II(2)/HOU/2861/84 at
page 447 of Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 23rd May 1984.
VARIATIONS
In the said Hosur New Town Development Plan  in “Annexure 3” in the tabular column,
1. under the heading Commercial :, in the fourth column, against Serial number “21” in the first column,—
(i) For the expression “101 to 122” the expression “101, 102, 103 pt, 104 to 122” shall be substituted; and
(ii) for the expression “256 to 273” the expression “256 (except 256/1A, 2B, 1A2C, 1B), 259 (except 259/1pt),
265 (except 265/1,2), 267(except 267/1,2), 268 (except 268/2), 269, 270 (except 270/2), 271* shall be substituted;
and
2. under the heading “Hazardous Industrial” in the fourth column against Serial number “36” for the expression “NIL” the
expression “256/1A, 2B, 1A2C, 1B, 257, 258, 259/1pt, 260 to 264, 265/1, 2, 266, 267/1, 2, 268/2, 270/2, 272, 273 of
