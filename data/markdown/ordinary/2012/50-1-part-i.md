No. I/PUSP/39/2012.—The following Notification of the Government
of India, Ministry of Law and Justice (Department of Justice),
Jaisalmer House, 26th Man singh Road, New Delhi-110 011, dated
21st December 2012 is republished:—
In exercise of the powers conferred by Clause (2) of article 124
of the Constitution of India, the President is pleased to appoint Shri
Justice M. Yusuf Eqbal, Chief Justice of the Madras High Court to be
a Judge of the Supreme Court of India, with effect from the date he
assumes charge of his office.
