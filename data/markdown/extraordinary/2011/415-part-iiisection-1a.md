MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION AND WATER SUPPLY (ELECTION) DEPARTMENT
AMENDMENTS TO MODIFICATIONS FOR THE CONSTITUTION OF STANDING COMMITTEES RULES, 1997.
[G.O. Ms. No.168, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (Election), 4th November 2011.]
No. SRO A-18(d-1)/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 8 of the Tiruppur City Municipal Corporation Act, 2008
(Tamil Nadu Act 7 of 2008), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby modifies the Tamil Nadu Municipal Corporations
Constitution of Standing Committees Rules, 1997, by way of making the following amendment to the
said Rules and also directs that the said Rules shall apply to the Tiruppur City Municipal Corporation subject to the modification
hereby made:—
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in rule 2, for sub-rule (1), the following sub-rule shall be substituted, namely:—
“(1) In these rules, unless the context otherwise requires,—
(i)  “Act” means the Tiruppur City Municipal Corporation Act, 2008 (Tamil Nadu Act 7 of 2008);
(ii)  “Corporation” means the Tiruppur City Municipal Corporation; and
(iii) “Council” means the Council of Tiruppur City Municipal Corporation.”.
