No. II(2)/HO/367(a)/2009.
Under Section 28-A of the Representation of the People Act, 1951 (Central Act XLIII of 1951), the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby designates the Director-General of Police, Additional Director-General of Police
(Law and Order), Zonal Inspectors General of Police of the 78. Bargur, 103. Thondamuthur, 134. Cumbum,
197. Ilayangudy and 226. Srivaikundam Assembly Constituencies, the Commissioners of Police/ Superintendents
of Police, Coimbatore, Theni, Dharmapuri, Sivagangai and Thoothukudi districts and all Police Officials
subordinate to them and up to the level of Police Constables and any other Police Officers/Officials
performing in any way election related duties for the conduct of Bye-Elections to be held on the 18th August,
2009 to the Legislative Assembly of this State from the pre-delimited 78. Bargur,
103. Thondamuthur, 134. Cumbum, 197. Ilayangudy and 226.  Srivaikundam Assembly Constituencies of
this State as Designated Officers.  All the said Officers shall be deemed to be on deputation to the Election
Commission for the period commencing on and from the date of the Notification calling for the said Election
and ending with the date of declaration of the result of the said Election  and accordingly, the said Officers
shall, during that period, be subject to the control, superintendence and discipline of the Election Commission.
S. MALATHI,
Principal Secretary to Government.
