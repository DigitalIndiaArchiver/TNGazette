TAMIL NADU STATE ELECTION COMMISSION, CHENNAI. 
[ELECTIONS - CONDUCT OF ELECTIONS TO PANCHAYATS - APPOINTMENT OF STATE ELECTION  
OFFICER - NOTIFICATION  ISSUED.].
(S.O. No.1/2019/TNSEC/PE-1)
No. VI(2)/41(a-1)/2019.
WHEREAS, the Tamil Nadu State  Election Commission appointed the Commissioner / Director of Rural Development 
and Panchayat Raj, Chennai as the State Election Officer for performing the functions specified in sub-rule (1) of rule 
6 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Elections) Rules, 1995 in connection with the conduct of elections to Panchayats 
vide Tamil Nadu State Election Commission's Statutory Order No.1/2017/TNSEC/PE-1, dated 03-03-2017 and the same 
has been published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary Issue No. 58, Part VI—Section 2, dated 
06-03-2017 vide Notification No.VI(2)/27(a-1)/2017;
WHEREAS, the Hon,ble High Court of Madras has recorded in the Contempt Petition No.1697/2017 and CMP 
No.16875/2017 in W.A. No. 1268/2017, dated 07-01-2019 that the necessary notification for conduction local body elections 
will be issued by the end of May 2019 (31-05-2019);
AND WHEREAS, as recorded in the Contempt Petition No.1697/2017 and CMP No.16875/2017 in WA No. 1268/2017 
by the  Hon'ble High Court of Madras, the election notification has to be issued by the Tamil Nadu State Election 
Commission in time and to expedite the process of election immediately;
NOW, THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-rule (1) of the rule 6 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats 
(Elections) Rules, 1995 and in supersession of the Notification No.VI(2)/27(a-1)/2017 has published in Part VI—Section 2 
of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, No.58, dated 06-03-2017 at Page No.1, the Tamil Nadu State 
Election Commission hereby appoints the Commissioner/Director of Rural Development and Panchayat Raj, Chennai 
as the State Election Officer for performing the functions specified in the said rule in connection with the conduct of 
Elections to Panchayats.

(S.O.No.2/2019/TNSEC/PE-1)
No. VI(2)/41(a-2)/2019.
WHEREAS, the Tamil Nadu State  Election Commission appointed the District Collectors concerned excepting the Collecor 
of Chennai as the District Election Officers for their repective Districts for performing the functions specified in sub-rule (2) of 
rule 6 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Elections) Rules, 1995 in connection with the conduct of elections to Panchayats vide 
Tamil Nadu State Election Commission's Statutory Order No.2/2017/TNSEC/PE-1, dated 03-03-2017 and the same 
has been published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, Issue No. 58, Part VI—Section 2, dated 
06-03-2017 vide Notification No.VI(2)/27(a-2)/2017;
WHEREAS, the Hon,ble High Court of Madras has recorded in the Contempt Petition No.1697/2017 and CMP 
No.16875/2017 in W.A. No. 1268/2017, dated 07-01-2019 that the necessary notification for conduction local body elections 
will be issued by the end of May 2019 (31-05-2019);
AND WHEREAS, as recorded in the Contempt Petition No.1697/2017 and CMP No.16875/2017 in WA No. 1268/2017 
by the Hon'ble High Court of Madras, the election notification has to be issued by the Tamil Nadu State Election 
Commission in time and to expedite the process of election immediately;
NOW, THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-rule (2) of the rule 6 of the Tamil Nadu 
Panchayats (Elections) Rules, 1995 and in supersession of the Notification No.VI(2)/27(a-2)/2017 has published in 
Part VI—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary No.58, dated 06-03-2017 at Page No.1-2, the 
Tamil Nadu State Election Commission hereby appoints the District Collectors concerned excepting the Collector of Chennai 
as the District Election Officers for their respective Districts. Subject to the superintendence, direction and control of the 
Tamil Nadu State Election Commission, they shall co-ordinate and supervise the preparation and publication of 
electoral rolls of Panchayats and the conduct of elections to the offices of members of Village Panchayats, Panchayat 
Union Councils and the District Panchayats, Presidents and Vice-Presidents of Village Panchayats, Chairpersons and 
Vice-Chairpersons of Panchayats Union Councils and District Panchayats in their respective Districts. They shall also 
perform such other functions as are entrusted to them by the Tamil Nadu State Election  Commission, from time to 
time, in connection wifh the conduct of Elections to Panchayats.
