NOTIFICATION OF ELECTION COMMISSION OF INDIA REGARDING SPECIAL INTENSIVE REVISION OF 
ELECTORAL ROLLS, 2026
No. SRO G-2/2026.
The following Notification of the Election Commission of India, Nirvachan Sadan, Ashoka Road, 
New Delhi-110 001, dated 18th January, 2026 [28, Pausha, 1947 Saka] is published:-
No.23/2025/ERS:- Whereas, the Commission, vide its letter No. 23/2025-ERS (Vol.II) dated 27.10.2025, had 
announced Special Intensive Revision in 12 States/UTs namely A & N Islands, Chhattisgarh, Goa, Gujarat, Kerala, 
Lakshadweep, Madhya Pradesh, Puducherry, Rajasthan, Tamil Nadu, Uttar Pradesh, and West Bengal.
Whereas, the draft rolls were published on 23.12.2025 and the period of filing claims and objections in the 
State of Kerala was fixed from 23.12.2025 to 22.01.2026 and
Whereas the Hon'ble Supreme Court in its Order in WP (Civil) No. 1133/2025 dated 15.01.2026 has advised 
the Commission to consider the desirability of extending claims and objections for a suitable period.
Whereas Chief Electoral Officer, Kerala has also requested to extend the claims and objections period.
Whereas the Commission, in light of Hon'ble Supreme Court Order, request received from Chief Electoral 
Officer Kerala and other relevant factors and in exercise of the power conferred by proviso to Rule 12 of the 
Registration of Electors Rules, 1960, has decided to extend, the period for filing claims and objections up to 
30.01.2026 for ongoing Special Intensive Revision of electoral rolls with reference to 01.01.2026 as the qualifying 
date in the State of Kerala.
Whereas, the draft rolls were published on 19.12.2025 and the period of filing claims and objections in the 
State of Gujarat & Tamil Nadu was fixed from 19.12.2025 to 18.01.2026 and
