Roc No.13469/2011/DP1, dated: 01-08-2017. Proposes to make the following variation to the approved Pachaiyankuppam
Detailed Development Plan No. 1 of Cuddalore Local Planning Area approved by the Commissioner of Town and Country
Planning Proceedings in Roc.No.5791/2013 DP1, dated:29-08-2008 and the fact of this approval in form No.12,
Published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, No.47, Part-VI, Section-1, Page 376 & 377, dated 03-12-2008, Publication

2. Any person affected or interested in this draft variation may within SIXTY DAYS from the date of publication of
this notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, can represent in person or submit in writing to the Member-
Secretary, Cuddalore Local Planning Authority any objections and suggestions relating there to.
3. The Variation with plan may be inspected free of cost at any time during office hours at the above said Local
Planning Authority Office.
VARIATION
1. The entire Pachaiyankuppam Detailed Development Plan No.1 of Cuddalore Local Planning Area by the
Commissioner of Town and Country Planning Proceeding in Roc No.5791/2013 DP1, dated:29-08-2008 is varied by
the Pachaiyankuppam Detailed Development Plan No.1 of Cuddalore Local Planning Authority.
2. The Plan thus varied is numbered as Map No.5 DDP(V)/DTCP No.07/2017.
3. The Detailed Development Plan clauses and schedules annexed with this plan are made enforceable from the
date of publication of the confirmed variation notification to be issued under Section 33(2) of the Act and Published in
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.

JUDICIAL NOTIFICATIONS
Constitution of Sub-Court at Coonoor in The Nilgiris District.
(Roc. No. 2515/A/2013/G/Judn.)
No.VI(1)/272/2017.
"In exercise of the powers conferred under the first proviso to Section 13 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act,1873
(Central Act III of 1873), the High Court, Madras hereby directs that appeals from the decrees or orders of the District
Munsif Court, Coonoor and the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Kothagiri be preferred in the court of Subordinate
Judge, Coonoor with effect from the date on which the Subordinate Judge, Coonoor assumes charge of that Court."
High Court, Madras,
