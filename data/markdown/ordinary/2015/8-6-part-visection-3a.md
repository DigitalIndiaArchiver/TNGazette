GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI
Framing of Bye Law in Respect of Karumbukadai Sunnath Jamath Pallivasal, Coimbatore District.
(Rc. No. 8063/08/ B3/CBE.)
No. VI-3(a)/5/2015.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 69 of the Wakf Act, 1995 as amended by
Act 27 of 2013, the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its Resolution in item No.64/12 in Rc.No. 8063/08/B3/CBE, dated
26-08-2014 has approved the Bye Law framed for Sunnath Jamath Pallivasal Wakf, Karumbukadai, Coimbatore District.
A. MOHAMED MEERAN,
Chennai-600 001,
District Revenue Officer /
19th February 2015.

Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
