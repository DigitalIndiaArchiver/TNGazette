HOME DEPARTMENT
Exemption from Payment of Life Time Tax and Registration
Fee in respect of Multi Utility Van belonging to the
Tamil Nadu State Legal Services Authority, Chennai
under the Tamil Nadu Motor Vehicles Taxation Act.
[G.O. Ms No. 357, Home (Tr.I), 20th April 2016.]
No.II(2)/HO/321/2016.—In exercise of the powers conferred
by clause (1) of Section 20 of the Tamil Nadu Motor Vehicles
Taxation Act, 1974 (Tamil Nadu Act 13 of 1974), the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby makes an exemption in regard to the
Life Time Tax payable in respect of the 'Multi Utility Van' bearing
Chassis No. MCIE4CHAOGPO34980 and Engine
No. D63019265, belonging to the Tamil Nadu State Legal
Services Authority, Chennai.

[G.O. Ms No. 357, Home (Tr.I), 20th April 2016.]
No.II(2)/HO/322/2016.—In exercise of the powers conferred
by rule 100 of the Tamil Nadu Motor Vehicles Rules, 1989,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby exempts the Tamil Nadu
State Legal Services Authority, Chennai from the Payment of
fee payable for the registration of the 'Multi Utility Van'
bearing Chassis No.MCIE4CHAOGPO34980 and Engine
No. D63019265.

by bifurcating the District Munsif-cum-Judicial

No.II(2)/HO/324/2016.—In exercise of the powers conferred

with the High Court of Madras, hereby fixes the number of
District Munsif to be appointed to the District Munsif Court,

[G.O. Ms. No. 365, Home (Courts-III), 22nd April 2016.]

Act III of 1873), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs
that in the District of Salem, the Subordinate Judge's Court,
Mettur, shall have and exercise original Jurisdiction over the
District Munsif Court, Omalur with effect from the date of
constitution of separate District Munsif Court at Omalur by
bifurcatintg the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court,
Omalur in Salem District.

[G.O. Ms. No. 365, Home (Courts-III), 22nd April 2016.]
No.II(2)/HO/326/2016.—In exercise of the powers conferred
by Section 4 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central
Act III of 1873), the Governor of Tamil Nadu after consultation
with the High Court, Madras hereby fixes the number of District
Munsif to be appointed for the Salem District as SEVEN with
effect from the date on which the District Munsif, Omalur,
assumes charge of that court.

PUBLIC WORKS DEPARTMENT
Exemption to the two numbers of Item Nos. FA 101 and
FA 405 German made Waste Heat Steam Recovery
Steam Generators Supplied by Messrs. Rheinstall,
Germany and Messrs. Koervar and Lersch, Krefeld,
Germany to Messrs Thirumalai Chemicals Limited
Ranipet from the Operation under the Boilers Act.
[G.O. Ms No. 88, Public Works (D2), 21st April 2016,
CˆF¬ó 8, ¶¡ºA, F¼õœÀõ˜ Ý‡´-2047.]
No.II(2)/PW/327/2016.—Whereas the Government of
Tamil Nadu are satisfied that, having regard to the material,
design and construction of Waste Heat Steam Recovery
Generators and to the need for the rapid industrialization of
the country, it is necessary to do so;
Now, Therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by
sub-section (3) of Section 34 of the Boilers Act, 1923
(Central Act V of 1923), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
exempts two numbers of iterm Nos. FA 101 and FA 405
German made Waste Heat Steam Recovery Steam Generators
supplied by Messrs. Rheinstall, Germany and Messrs. Koervar
and Lersch, Krefeld, Germany to Messrs Thirumalai Chemicals
Limited, Ranipet from the operation of the provisions of the
said Act to enable erection and registration under the said Act
subject to the following conditions, namely:—
a. design and construction of boiler component is in
accordance with the international code or standard including
British Standards (BS), American Society of Mechanical
Engineers (ASME) Boiler and Pressure Vessel Code, Tubular
Exchanger Manufacturers Association (TEMA), Technical
Requirements Document (TRD), GOST and Japanese Industrial
Standards (JIS);
b. Material used in the construction are not specifically
prohibited by the said regulations;
c. design, construction and materials of boiler or boiler
components have been satisfied by the State Government by
necessary tests and examination like Remnant Life Assessment,
which shall be carried out by the owner.
