BACKWARD CLASSES, MOST BACKWARD CLASSES AND MINORITIES WELFARE DEPARTMENT
NOTIFICATIONS UNDER THE WAKF ACT
APPOINTMENT OF CERTAIN OFFICIALS AS RETURNING OFFICER AND ASSISTANT RETURNING
OFFICERS FOR CONDUCTING ELECTION OF MEMBERS OF TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD.
[G.O. (2D) No. 21, Backward Classes, Most Backward Classes and
Minorities Welfare (T1), 29th June 2012.]
No. II(2)/BCMBCMW/328(b-1)/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (b) of sub-rule (1) of rule 3 of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board
(Conduct of Election for Members) Rules, 1997, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints
Thiru M.H. Mohideen, Joint Secretary to Government, Backward Classes, Most Backward Classes and
Minorities Welfare Department, Chennai-9, as Returning Officer for the purpose of conducting election of
Members of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board as specified in the said Rules.
[G.O. (2D) No. 21, Backward Classes, Most Backward Classes and
Minorities Welfare (T1), 29th June 2012.]
No. II(2)/BCMBCMW/328(b-2)/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (c) of sub-rule (1) of rule 3 of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board
(Conduct of Election for Members) Rules, 1997, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints
Thiru M. Shajahan, Under Secretary to Government, Backward Classes, Most Backward Classes and
Minorities Welfare Department, Chennai-9, and Thiru Aejaz Ahmed, Under Secretary to Government, Animal
Husbandary  and Fisheries Department as Assistant Returning Officers for the purpose of conducting
election of Members of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board as specified in the said Rules.
THANGA KALIYAPERUMAL,
Secretary to Government.
