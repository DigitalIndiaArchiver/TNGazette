HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE DEPARTMENT
Amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Public Health Subordinate Service.
[G.O. Ms.No.22, Health and Family Welfare (N2), 28th January 2013,
Thai 15, Nandhana, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2044.]
No. SRO B-6/2013.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Public Health
Subordinate Service. (Section 26 in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1989.)
AMENDMENT
In the said Special Rules, in the Annexure referred to in rule 5(b), under the heading “Class-I”, in Column (3) against the
entry “Laboratory Technician Grade-III” in Column (2), after the heading “for appointment by direct recruitment” and the entries
there under the following heading and entries shall be added, namely:-
“for appointment by promotion:
(i)
Must have rendered five years of service in the post of Field Assistant, and
(ii)
Must have a good physique, good vision and capacity to do outdoor work.”
J. RADHAKRISHNAN,
Secretary to Government.

No. SRO B-7/2013.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the ad hoc rules issued with the Home Department
Notification S.R.O.No.A-116 of 1963, published at page 119 of Part V of the Fort St. George Gazette, dated the 30th January
1963.
2.
The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force with effect on and from the 10th October 2008.
AMENDMENT
In the said rules, (1) for rule 1, the following rule shall be substituted, namely:-
“1.
Applicability of General Rules.—The General and Special Rules applicable to the holders of the permanent
posts in the Tamil Nadu General Subordinate Service shall apply to the holder of the temporary post of Driver in the Office
of the Transport Commissioner, Chennai and the offices of the Joint Transport Commissioners, Chennai, subject to the
modifications specified in the following rules.”; and
(2) for rule, 4, the following rule shall be substituted, namely:-
“4.
Appointing authority.—The appointing authority for the post in the office of the Transport Commissioner,
Chennai shall be the Deputy Transport Commissioner, Chennai and for the post in the offices of the Joint Transport
Commissioners, Chennai shall be the Joint Transport Commissioner concerned, Chennai.”
R. MOORTHY,
Deputy Secretary to Government.
