Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the rules published with the Health and Family Welfare 
Department Notification SRO. No. B-232/91, published at page 181 of Part III-Section 1(b) of Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, 
dated the 11th September 1991, as subsequently amended.
