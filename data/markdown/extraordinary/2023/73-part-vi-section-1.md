NOTICE UNDER SECTION 3(1) OF TAMIL NADU ACQUISITION OF LAND FOR INDUSTRIAL PURPOSES ACT, 
1997 READ WITH THE TAMIL NADU LAND ACQUISITION  LAWS  (REVIVAL  OF OPERATION, AMENDMENT 
AND VALIDATION) ACT, 2019 
No. VI(1)/147(a-1/2023.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been  satisﬁed  that  the lands speciﬁed in the Schedule below have 
to be acquired for industrial purposes, for  the  formation  of Corridor 4 - ‘23  –Theliyaragaram Metro Station’ 
(Old Ramachandra Hospital  Metro  Station)’ and    has already decided that the entire amount of compensation 
to be awarded for the lands is to be paid out of the funds managed by the Chennai Metro Rail Ltd. The following 
notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act 
1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes 
Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands speciﬁed in 
the Schedule below and measuring 430 Sq.mtrs to the same, a little more or less needed for industrial 
purposes, to wit, for the formation of ‘23 – Theliyaragaram Metro Station’ (Old Ramachandra Hospital 
Metro Station)
The plans of the lands are kept in the Chengalpattu Collector’s       Office, Taluk Oﬃ  ce, Tambaram and Oﬃ  ce 
of the District Revenue Officer (LA), Chennai Metro Rail Limited, Chennai which may be inspected at any time 
during oﬃ  ce hours.
