GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAQF BOARD, CHENNAI.
Taking over of the Peerullah Sha Makkan Thakiah Waqf, V.Kalathur, Veppanthattai Taluk,  Perambalur District
under the Direct Management
(Rc. No. 9671/14/B5/Perambalur)
No.VI-3(a)/113/2015.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Waqf Act 1995 (as amended),
the Tamil Nadu Waqf Board in its Resolution in Item No. 149/15, Rc. No. 9671/14/B5/Perambalur,  dated 18-08-2015
has ordered to take over the management of Peerullah Sha Makkan Thakiah Waqf, V. Kalathur, Veppanthattai Taluk,
Perambalur District under the direct control of the Tamil Nadu Waqf Board for a period of one year from 18-08-2015
and to appoint the Waqf Inspector, Perambalur as its Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,
K. RASHEETH ALI,

ADVERTISEMENT OF PETITION
Notice is hereby given that a Petition for the winding-up of the above named company by the High Court at Madras  was
on the 27th  day of October 2014, presented to the said Court by the said  Company for winding-up of the Company and
the said petition is directed to be heard before the Court on the 6th day of November 2015.
Any creditor, contributory or other person desirous of supporting or opposing the making of an order on the said petition
should sent to the Petitioner or his advocate notice of his intention signed by him or his advocate with him name and
address, so as to reach the Petitioner of his advocate not less later than 5 days before the date fixed for the hearing of the
petition, and appear at the hearing for the purpose in person or by his advocate. A copy of the petition will be furnished
by the undersigned to any creditor or contributory on payment of the prescribed charges for the same.
Any affidavit intended to be used in opposition to the petition should be filed in Court, and  a copy served on the Petitioner
or his Advocate, not less than 5 days before the date fixed for the hearing.
Dated at Chennai on this the 12th day of October, 2015.
SHRI & SHANKAR ASSOCIATES,
UMA SHANKAR,
 Advocate for Petitioner.
