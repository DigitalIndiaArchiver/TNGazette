[ELECTIONS—URBAN LOCAL BODY—APRIL 2010—CASUAL INDIRECT ELECTIONS TO FILL UP THE VACANCIES IN

COMMITTEE—CONDUCT OF—NOTIFIED.]
(S.O. No. 10/2010/TNSEC/ME-2.)
No. VI(2)/138(c)/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 27 and sub-section (1) of Section 43-B, of the Tamil Nadu District
Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920) and sub-rule (4) of rule 99 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Third Grade
Municipalities, Municipalities and Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission hereby fixes
30-4-2010 as the date for convening a meeting to elect the office of Chairman, members of Taxation Appeals Committee and
Contract Committee as the case may be, shown in the Annexure, in accordance with the following programme:—
1.
Last date for service of notice for the meetings of election of office
of Chairman,members of Taxation Appeals Committee and Contract
Committee as the case may be.
22-4-2010
2.
Statutory interval
23-4-2010 to 29-4-2010
3.
Date for conduct of meeting for the election of office of Chairman,
members of Taxation  Appeals Committee and Contract Committee as
the case may be.
30-4-2010
(By Order of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission)
