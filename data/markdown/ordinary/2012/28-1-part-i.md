[Ministers - Inter-se-Seniority of Ministers and Subjects ordered -
Deletion of the subject 'Cinchona' from Minister (Forests) - Orders -
Issued.]
The following Government Order is published:—
[G.O. Ms. No. 543, Public (Special-B),  5th July 2012, Aani 21,
Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2043.]
READ:
1. G.O. Ms. No. 105, Public (Special.B) Department, dated
27th January 2012.
2. From the Principal Secretary to Government, Environment and
Forests Department, Chennai, D.O. Letter No. 8252/OP.I/2012-1, dated
7th May 2012.
Order: No. 543, Public (Special-B) 5th July 2012.
In the reference first read above, orders were issued regarding
the inter-se-seniority of Ministers and the subjects allocated to them.
2. In the reference second read above, the Principal Secretary to
Government, Environment and Forests Department has stated that in
the year 1990 itself in G.O. Ms. No. 221, Environment and Forests
Department, dated 3rd April 1990, the Department  'Cinchona' was
wound up and the staff were merged with Forests Department and
there is no separate department in the name of  'Cinchona' at present.
The Principal Secretary to Government, Environment and Forests
Department has therefore requested to delete the subject matter
'Cinchona' from the Hon'ble Minister (Forests) by making an
amendment.
3. The Government have examined the above request in detail.
Accordingly, the Government  direct  that, the subject
'Cinchona' be deleted from the subjects allocated to Minister
(Forests) and henceforth Thiru K.T. Pachamal, Minister for Forests
will handle the subject “Forests” alone.
