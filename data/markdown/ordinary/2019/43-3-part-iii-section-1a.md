No. SRO A-40/2019.—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 43 read with Section 18 of the 
Plantations Labour Act, 1951 (Central Act LXIX of 1951), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment 
to the Tamil Nadu Plantations Labour Rules, 1955.
                                                        Amendment
In the said Rules, the CHAPTER VIII-WELFARE OFFICERS and the rules 85 to 90 thereunder shall be omitted.
Md. NASIMUDDIN,
Principal Secretary to Government.

No. SRO A-41/2019.—In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (i) of sub-section (1) of Section 86 and clause (a) of 
sub-section (2) of Section 347 of the Chennai City Municipal Corporation Act, 1919 (Tamil Nadu Act IV of 1919), the Governor 
of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Chennai Corporation Public Health and Medical Service Rules:-
2. 	 (1) The amendment (1) hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 23rd November 2010.
    	 (2) The amendment (2) hereby made shall come into force on the 15th October 2019.
Amendments.
In the said Rules, -
(1)	 for the expression “Health Officer”, “Additional Health Officer” and “Assistant Health Officer” wherever 
they occur, the expression “City Health Officer”, “Additional City Health Officer” and “Zonal Health Officer” 
shall, respectively, be substituted;
(2)	 in rule 3, in sub-rule(1), in “THE TABLE”, against the entry “Category-1 Zonal Health Officer” under Class-II 
in column(1), as so substituted, for item (ii) in column(2) thereof, the following item shall be substituted, 
namely:-
“(ii)	 By Promotion from the categories of Medical Officer(Allopathy) or Medical Officer (Tuberculosis Clinic) or Medical 
Officer in Family Welfare Department”.  
Harmander Singh,
Principal Secretary to Government.
