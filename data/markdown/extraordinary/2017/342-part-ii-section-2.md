COMMERCIAL TAXES AND REGISTRATION DEPARTMENT
WAIVER OF LATE FEE PAYABLE UNDER SECTION 47 FOR THE MONTHS OF AUGUST AND
SEPTEMBER, 2017 UNDER THE TAMIL NADU GOODS AND SERVICES TAX ACT, 2017.
[G.O. Ms. No. 147, Commercial Taxes and Registration (B1), 24th October 2017, Aippasi 7, Hevilambi,
Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2048.]
No. II(2)/CTR/868(e)/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred by section 128 of  the Tamil Nadu Goods and Services Tax Act, 2017
(Tamil Nadu Act 19 of 2017), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, on the recommendations of the Council, hereby
waives the late fee payable under section 47 of the said Act, for all registered persons who failed to furnish
the return in FORM GSTR-3B for the months of August and September, 2017 by the due date.
Dr. C. CHANDRAMOULI,
Additional Chief Secretary to Government (FAC).
