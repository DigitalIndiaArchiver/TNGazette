DISSOLUTION OF THIRTEENTH TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY
[G.O. Ms. No. 412, Public (Estt-I & Leg.), 14th May 2011,
Kara, Chithirai 31, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No. II(1)/PUEL/12(a)/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-clause (b) of clause (2) of Article 174 of the Constitution of
India, I, Surjit Singh Barnala, Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby dissolve the Thirteenth
Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly, with effect from the 14th May 2011.
Raj Bhavan,
SURJIT SINGH BARNALA,
Guindy,
Governor of Tamil Nadu.
14th May 2011.
S. MALATHI,
Chief Secretary.
