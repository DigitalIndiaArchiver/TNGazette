Variations to the Approved Master Plan for the Coimbatore Local Planning Area.
(Roc. No. 4014/2015/LPA-2)
[G.O. (2D) No. 38, Housing and Urban Development UD4(1) Department dated 22-02-2016.]
No. VI(1)/173/2016.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 32 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country
Planning Act 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972) and in exercise of powers conferred by the G.O. Ms. No. 94,
Housing and Urban Development [UD4(1)] Department dated 12-06-2009 which has been published in the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No. 27, Part II—Section 2, Page No. 228, dated 15-07-2009 the following variations
are made to the master plan for the Coimbatore Local Planning Area approved under the said Act and published in
the  Housing and Urban Development Department Notification No. II(2)/HOU/4377/94 at page 1078 of Part II—Section 2
of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette dated, the 9th November 1994.
VARIATIONS
In the said Master Plan, in the “LAND USE SCHEDULE” under the heading “COIMBATORE LOCAL PLANNING
AREA (OTHER THAN COIMBATORE CORPORATION AREA)” under the sub-heading (a) Notified Detailed Development.
Plan Area in Kalapatti Detailed Development. Plan No. 25,
(i) Against the entry, “Industrial” for the expression 572 the following entry 572 (except 572/3, 572/5) shall be
substituted.
(ii) Against the entry, “Residential” for the expression 572/5 shall be added after the entry 572 part.
(iii) Against the entry, “Commercial” the expression 572/3 shall be added.
Coimbatore-12,
C. MATHIVANAN,
28th April 2016.
Member Secretary (incharge),
Coimbatore Local Planning Authority.
Variation to the Approved Master Plan for the Kurichi New Town Development Plan
(Roc. No. 1046/2015/KNTDA)
[G.O.Ms.No. (2D) No. 59, H & UD [UD4(1)] Department, dated 01-03-2016.]
No. VI(1)/174/2016.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 32 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning
Act 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972) and in exercise of powers conferred by the G.O.Ms.No. 94, Housing and Urban
Development [UD4(1)] Department, dated 12-06-2009 which has been published in the Tamil Nadu Government
Gazette No. 27, Part II—Section 2, Page No. 228, dated 15-07-2009 the following variations are made to the Master Plan
for the Kurichi New Town Development Plan approved under the said Act and published in the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Part II—Section 2 in page Nos. 380 and 381, Dt: 3-5-1995.
VARIATION
In the said Master Plan in the “Land use Schedule” (Chapter VIII)
1.Under the sub-heading “General Industrial use zone” in Annexure VIII against the entry No: 67, Seerapalayam
Village, for the expression S.F.No. 246pt the expression, SF.No. 246/3B2B with an extent of 0.07.00 Hec. or 7550 sqft
shall be deleted and for the remaining area of SF.No. 246pt, the existing land use in the approved Kurichi New Town
Development Plan is in force.
2. Under the sub-heading, “Mixed Residential use zone” in Annexure V, against the entry No. 67. Seerapalayam
Village, the expression SF.No. 246/3B2B with an extent of 0.07.00 Hec or 7550 sqft shall be added.
Coimbatore-18,
P. RANGANATHAN,
29th April 2016.
Member Secretary,
Kurichi New Town Development Authority.

Variation to the Approved Master Plan of Mamallapuram Local Planning Authority
(Roc. No. 588/2015/MLPA)
No. VI(1)/175/2016.
1. In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (4) of Section 32 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country
Planning Act 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972) and in exercise of powers confirmed by the G.O. Ms. No. 94,
Housing and Urban Development [UD4(1)] Department, dated 12-06-2009 which has been published in
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No. 27, Part II—Section 2, Page No. 228, dated 15-07-2009.
2. Land Use Zone Conversion from Mixed Residential Use Zone into Commercial use zone order is
G.O. [2D] No. 14, Housing and Urban Development [UD4(1)] Department dated, 19-01-2016 the following variation are
made to the Master Plan of Mamallapuram Local Planning Authority approved under the said Act and published in
G.O. Ms. No. 153, H&UD [UD4(2)] dated 20-06-2013, Notification No. II(2)/HOU/501/2013, at page No. 481 of
Part  II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated 17th July 2013.
VARIATION
In the said master plan under the heading permitted land use in various survey numbers of Mamallapuram Local
Planning Area under sub-heading the following entries should be made.
1. Against the entry 1. Mixed Residential Use Zone survey numbers 1395E/2B of Thaiyur Village, Survey Number
1395E (except 1395E/2A), shall be deleted and 1395E (except 1395E/2A, 1395E/2B) shall be substituted.
2. Against the entry 2. Commercial Use Zone, the expression  survey number. 1395E/2B of Thaiyur Village, shall
be added in Thaiyur Village.
Conditions:
1. The applicant should undertake own water supply arrangement, Sewage Treatment Plant Details and Generator
facilities for proposed building.
2.The 10% Open Space Reservation area of total extent should be maintained by the applicant.
Mamallapuram,
C. BASKARATHONDAIMAN,
29th April 2016.
Member-Secretary (incharge),
Mamallapuram local planning Authority.
