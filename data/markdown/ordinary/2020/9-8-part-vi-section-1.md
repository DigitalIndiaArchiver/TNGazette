SUMMER VACATION 
I. 
All the Civil and Sessions Court of the Unit of the District Judge, Nilgiris at Udhagamandalam will be closed 
from Friday, the 1st day of May, 2020 to Sunday, the 31st day of May, 2020 (both days inclusive). 
DUSSERA HOLIDAYS 
II. 
All the Civil and Sessions Court of the Unit of the District Judge, Nilgiris at Udhagamandalam will be closed 
from Saturday, the 17th day of October, 2020 to Monday, the 26th day of October, 2020 (both days inclusive). 
CHRISTMAS HOLIDAYS 
Ill.  All the Civil and Sessions Court of the Unit of the District Judge, Nilgiris at Udhagamandalam will be closed 
from Friday, the 25th day of December, 2020, to Thursday, the 31st day of December, 2020 (both days inclusive).  
Holidays for all the Courts in the Nilgiris District for the year 2020 are as follows:-

07-11-2020, 21-11-2020 and 05-12-2020.

(d) The Family Court shall work on 1st and 3rd Saturdays of every month except those Saturdays which 
are declared as Public Holidays. All other Saturdays are declared as holidays, except 25-04-2020 which is declared as 
working days.

V. 
No Plaint or Petition except application for grant of copies and for services of execution of process will be 
received by the Civil Courts during the adjournments.
VI. The Oﬃ  ce of the Copyist and Process Service Establishment will be kept open through the Vacation Holidays 
but no arrest warrants will be executed during the Summer Vacation Holidays. 
VII. The Oﬃ  ce of the other Department will remain closed, however arrangements will be made:- 
 
(a) For Furnishing the Copies and Process Service with necessary relevent original records. 
 
(b) For transmitting the records to High Court for reference in appeals and revisions. 
 
(c) For attending all administrative Correspondence.
 
(d) For receiving printing charges and plan fees and respect of Copy Applications.
VIII. Under Section 30 of Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act as amended by Section 4 of Tamil Nadu Act VII of 1959 if 
a Vacation Civil Judge is appointed for the Civil Unit of Nilgiris District, Suits, Appeals and other proceedings or Urgent 
Civil Nature will be received and disposed of by him in terms and rules that may be framed in this regard if not they 
should be presented before the Honble High Court of Madras.
IX. If no Vacation Sessions Judge is appointed or in the absence of the Sessions Judge, from the Division during 
the above adjournments, Urgent applications for grant of Bail should be made to the High Court of Madras. 
X. There will be normally one sitting in the Summer Vacation every week.
