GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAQF BOARD, CHENNAI-600 001.
Taking over the Royapettah, Masjid E Asar E Sherif, Chennai District (G.S. No. 100/Chen) under 
Direct Management of Tamil Nadu Waqf Board.
(Lr. No. 9137/11/B6/Chennai)
No. VI-3(a)/8/2023.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under section 65 of the Waqf Act 1995, the Tamil Nadu Waqf 
Board in its Resolution Item No.291/22 in R.C.No. 9137/11/B6/Chennai dated:21.12.2022 has ordered to take over 
ROYAPETTAH, MASJID E ASAR E SHERIF, CHENNAI DISTRICT (G.S.No.100/Chen) under the Direct Management of 
Tamil Nadu Waqf Board and to appoint the Superintendent of Waqfs, Chennai as its Executive Officer. 
Chennai-600 001, 	
	
	
	
	
	
	
 A.B. RAFIULLA,
13th February 2023.

Framing of Bye Law in respect of Mahboobpalayam Mosque, Thirumangalam Taluk, 
Madurai District (G.S. No. 390/MDU).
(Lr. No. 9911/10/B8/MDU)
No. VI-3(a)/9/2023.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under section 69 of the Waqf Act 1995, the Tamil Nadu Waqf 
Board in its Resolution in item No.326/22 in Rc.No.9911/10/B8/MDU dated:09.01.2023 has approved the BYE LAW for the 
Waqf in respect of MAHBOOBPALAYAM MOSQUE, THIRUMANGALAM TALUK, MADURAI DISTRICT for better administration 
of the Waqf.
