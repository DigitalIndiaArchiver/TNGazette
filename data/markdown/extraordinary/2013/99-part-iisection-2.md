ANIMAL HUSBANDRY, DAIRYING AND FISHERIES DEPARTMENT
APPOINTMENT OF FIRST REGISTRAR OF TAMIL NADU FISHERIES UNIVERSITY FOR
CERTAIN PERIOD UNDER THE TAMIL NADU FISHERIES UNIVERSITY ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 103, Animal Husbandry, Dairying and Fisheries  (FS-4), 17th April 2013,
Chithirai 4, Vijaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2044.]
No. II(2)/AHDF/322(a)/2013.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 50 of the Tamil Nadu Fisheries University Act,  2012
(Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 2012), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints Dr.K. Rathnakumar,
Director in-charge, Institute of Fisheries Technology, Ponneri, Thiruvallur District as the first Registrar of the
Tamil Nadu Fisheries University, Nagapattinam, for a period of three years from the date of
assuming his office.
GAGANDEEP SINGH BEDI,
Secretary to Government.
