ORDER—No. 300, Home (S.C.), 2nd June 2011.
The Additional Director General of Police/Director of Civil
Defence and Additional Commandant General, Home Guards,
Chennai, has sent a proposal second read above requesting
the Government to delete the name of Thiru V. Savaridass,
Company Commander, Home Guards, Dindigul from the
awardee's list of Tamil Nadu Chief Minister's Home Guards
Medal for Outstanding Devotion to Duty (Anna Medal) awarded
on the occasion of Dr. Anna's Birthday on 15th September,
2010 since he has been dismissed from service on
9th December 2010 for his high handed behaviour and
indisciplinary conduct by not adhering to the relevant rules
and regulations as stipulated in Tamil Nadu Home Guards
Act, 1963.
2.
The Government, after careful examination of the
proposal of the Additional Director General of Police/Director
of Civil Defence and Additional Commandant General, Home
Guards, Chennai with reference to rule 16 of the rules
governing the award of the Tamil Nadu Chief Minister's Home
Guards Medal for Outstanding Devotion to Duty (Anna Medal)
have decided to cancel the said Medal awarded to
Thiru V. Savaridass, Company Commander, Home Guards,
Dindigul and also to delete his name from the list of recipients
of the Medal ordered in the Government Order first read
above. The Government accordingly order that the
Tamil Nadu Chief Minister's Home Guards Medal for
Outstanding Devotion to Duty (Anna Medal) awarded to Thiru
V. Savaridass, Company Commander, Home Guards, Dindigul
be cancelled and his name is deleted from the awardee's
list.
(By Order of the Governor)
SHEELA RANI CHUNKATH,
Principal Secretary to Government.
