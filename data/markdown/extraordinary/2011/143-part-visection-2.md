In exercise of the powers conferred under Sections 6-I and 58 of Chennai City Municipal Corporation Act, 1919 (Tamil Nadu
Act IV of 1919) and Section 10-D and Section 67 of Coimbatore City Municipal Corporation Act, 1981 (Tamil Nadu Act XXV of 1981)
read with Section 8 of the Salem City Municipal Corporation Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act XXIX of 1994) and sub-section (1) of
Section 43-B and Section 27 of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu V of 1920) and sub-rule (4) of rule
99 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayts, Third Grade Municipalities, Municipalities and Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006, the
Tamil Nadu State Election Commission hereby fixes 31st May 2011 as the date for convening a meeting to elect the Chairman,
Vice-Chairman and Member / Chairman of Appointment Committee, Chairman of Contract Committee, Chairman of Taxation
Appeals Committee, Member of Town Planning and Development Committee, Member of Health Committee and Chairman of
Wards Committee and Chairman of Standing Committee as the case may be, shown in the Annexure, in accordance with the
