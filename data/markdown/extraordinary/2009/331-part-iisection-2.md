Whereas, a reciprocal agreement in regard to the plying of transport vehicles on Inter-State routes was entered into
between the Governments of Tamil Nadu and Puducherry in G.O. Ms. No. 899, Home, dated the 12th April, 1985
and published with the Home Department, Notification No. II (2)/HO/1990 (h)/1985 at pages 1-14 in Part II—Section 2 of
the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 19th April 1985.
And Whereas, the Governments of Tamil Nadu and Puducherry have agreed to enter into a first supplemental agreement
under Section 88 of the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988 (Central Act 59 of 1988), in respect of operation of Stage Carriage Services
by the State Transport Undertakings and Private Operators on Inter-State routes and also countersignature of goods carriage
permits.
Now, therefore, the following draft of the first Supplemental reciprocal agreement between the Governments of
Tamil Nadu and Puducherry, is hereby published, as required under sub-Section (5) of Section 88 of the Motor Vehicles
Act, 1988 (Central Act 59 of 1988).
Notice is hereby given that the said draft Supplemental agreement will be taken up for consideration by the Secretary
to Government, Home, Prohibition and Excise Department, Government of Tamil Nadu, Chennai-600 009 after the expiry
of 30 days from the date of publication of this Notification in the   Tamil Nadu Government Gazette at its hearing to be held
on 25th January 2010 at 03.00 p.m.in the Conference Hall of the Main Secretariat Building, Secretariat, Chennai-600 009.
Objections, Suggestions, if any should be addressed in duplicate to the Secretary to Government, Home, Prohibition and
Excise Department, Government of Tamil Nadu, Chennai-600 009.

DRAFT AGREEMENT
Whereas, it is decided to control and regulate the plying of Stage Carriage services on inter-State routes between the
State of Tamil Nadu and Union Territory of Puducherry by way of permitting the State Transport Undertakings to operate
on new routes to be included in the agreement and also to permit Private Operators to operate on new routes to be included
in the agreement and to modify the existing routes covered by previous agreement and to perform additional trips on existing
routes, as also to enhance the quota of Goods Carriage permits for each State to be countersigned by the other.
Now, therefore, it is hereby agreed to publish the first supplemental agreement on the following terms and conditions
entered into between the State of Tamil Nadu and Union Territory of Puducherry, subject to the provisions of previous
agreement.
I. Operation of Stage Carriage Services:
1.  It is agreed by the Governments of Tamil Nadu and Union Territory of Puducherry to include the following routes in
the Inter-State Agreement on Single point tax basis:—
(a) Routes of private operators of the Union Territory of Puducherry already covered by previous agreement and
subsequently varied and saved under approved schemes and permits countersigned by the State Transport Authority,
Tamil Nadu as specified in Annexure-I.
(b) Routes of private operators of the State of Tamil Nadu already covered by previous agreement and subsequently
varied and saved under approved schemes and permits countersigned by the State Transport Authority, Puducherry as
specified in Annexure I-A.
(c) Routes now operated by Puducherry Road Transport Corporation on Double point tax as shown in Serial
Numbers 1 to 21 of Annexure-II.
(d) Routes of Private operators of the Union Territory of Puducherry which are plying on Double point tax in pursuance
of the Court Order, as shown in Serial Numbers 22 to 24 of Annexure-II.
(e) New Routes of State Transport Undertakings of Tamil Nadu to ply on 31 routes as shown in Part-1 of
Annexure II-A.
(f) Routes of State Transport Undertakings of Tamil Nadu now plying under Double point tax on 37 routes as shown
in Part-2 of Annexure II-A.
(g) Routes of Private Operators of the State of Tamil Nadu now plying on Double point tax as per the Court Orders,
as shown in Part-3 of Annexure II-A.
2. The existing ratio of parity of permits in respect of Inter-State routes, other than the route Puducherry - Karaikkal,
between the Government of Tamil Nadu and Government of Union Territory of Puducherry, that is, 4:1, is revised as 3:1,
respectively, in future.  The ratio will represent the number of buses.  So far as the route Puducherry-Karikkal  is concerned,
the ratio shall be continued as 1:1.
II. Goods Carriages:
(1) It is agreed to increase the quota system of Goods Carriage permits from 300 to 800 for Union Territory of Puducherry
to be countersigned by the State of Tamil Nadu.
(2) It is agreed to increase the quota system of Goods Carriage permits from 3000 to 8000 for the State of Tamil Nadu
to be countersigned by the Union Territory of Puducherry.
(3) It is agreed to omit the restriction in respect of laden weight.
III. Taxation:
Subject to the provisions contained in the basic agreement, Stage Carriages covered by counter-signature in the
reciprocating State shall be exempted from payment of tax to the reciprocating State.  The benefit of single point tax shall
apply to the Stage Carriages having both regular and temporary permits subject to the routes finding place in
the Inter-State agreement.
