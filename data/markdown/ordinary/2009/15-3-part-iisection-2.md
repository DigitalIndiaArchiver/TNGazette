HOUSING AND URBAN DEVELOPMENT DEPARTMENT
Proposal to declare the local  area comprising in Kayathar
First Grade Town Panchayat to be a local planning area
under Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning Act.
[G.O. Ms. No. 47, Housing and Urban
Development UD4(2), 24th March 2009.]
No.II(2)/HOU/177/2009.—In exercise of the powers conferred
by clause (b) of sub-section (1) of Section 10 of the Tamil Nadu
Town and Country Planning Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35
of 1972), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby declares his
intention to specify the area comprising Kayathar First Grade
Town Panchayat to be a local planning area.
2.
Notice is hereby given that any objection or
suggestion which may be received from any inhabitant or
any local authority or institution in the said local area with
respect thereto before the expiry of two months from the date
of publication of this Notification in the Tamil Nadu
Government Gazette will be duly considered by the
Government of Tamil Nadu. Objection or suggestion in
writing, if any, should be addressed to the Secretary to
Government, Housing and Urban Development Department,
Fort St. George, Chennai-600 009.
SURJIT K CHAUDHARY,
Principal Secretary to Government.
