PUBLIC (ELECTIONS) DEPARTMENT
EXTENSION OF PERIOD FOR FILING OF CLAIMS AND OBJECTIONS IN THE CONSTITUENCIES OF THE
TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY
No. SRO G-31/2011.
The following Notification of the Election Commission of India, New Delhi, dated 8th November,  2011 [Kartika 17, 1933 (Saka)]
is published:—
No. 23/TN/2012.—Whereas, the Commission had approved the schedule for special summary revision of electoral rolls with
reference to 1st January, 2012 as the qualifying date for the State of Tamil Nadu, vide its letter No. 23/TN/2012/989, dated
12th September, 2011 providing inter-alia, 24th October, 2011 as the date of draft publication and the period 24th October, 2011 to
8th November, 2011 for filing claims and objections; and
Whereas, the Commission has further received a request from Chief Electoral Officer-Tamil Nadu vide his letter dated
6th November, 2011 for extension of period for filing claims and objections in view of a number of festivals (holidays) falling within
the aforesaid period and severe rains etc., resulting  in loss of working days for revison process. The Chief Electoral Officer-
Tamil Nadu requested to extend campaign period for filing claims/objections; and
Whereas, the Commission has considered it and has accepted the aforesaid request;
Now, therefore, the Election Commission, in exercise of power conferred by proviso to Rule 12 of Registration of Electors Rules
1960, extends, for all Assembly Constituencies in State of Tamil Nadu, the period for filing claims and objections up to
11th November, 2011 (Friday), for ongoing special revision of electoral roll  with reference to 1st January, 2012. The disposal of
claims/objections will commence from 12th November, 2011 and other schedules of revision process will remain unchanged.
