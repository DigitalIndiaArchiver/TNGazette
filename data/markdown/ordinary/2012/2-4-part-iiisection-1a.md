HOME  DEPARTMENT
 Amendments to the Tamil Nadu Motor Vehicles Rules, 1989
[G.O. Ms. No. 768, Home (Tr.VII), 30th August 2010.]
No. SRO A-2/2012.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 96 of the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988 (Central Act 59 of 1988),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby makes the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu Motor Vehicles Rules, 1989, the draft of
the same having been previously published as required by sub-section (1) of Section 212 of the said Act.
AMENDMENTS.
In the said Rules:—
(1) in rule 172, after sub-rule (10), the following sub-rule shall be added, namely:—
“(11) It shall be a condition of permit of every transport vehicle that the vehicle shall not carry or display
advertisements in contravention of rule 343.” and
(2) in rule 343, in sub-rule (a), for the third proviso, the following provisos shall be substituted, namely:—
“Provided also that in the case of stage carriage, no advertisement shall be carried or displayed on the front
wind screen or rear window or side windows of the vehicle and if there are glass panels above the windows
on the sides of the vehicle, advertisements may be displayed on such panels:
Provided also that the advertisements shall not be offensive, obscene or cause for distraction to the road users.”
K. GNANADESIKAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
