In  exercise of the powers conferred by clause (a) of sub-section (1) and sub section (2) of
Section 2 of the Indian Tolls Act 1851 (Central Act VIII of 1851), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby levies
tolls for a period of three years with effect on and from 15th June 2016 on the classes of Motor Vehicles
specified in Column (2) of the table below which pass through the Dindigul – Kodaikanal Road,
Palani -  Kodaikanal Road and Cochin – Kodaikanal Road at Silver Cascade and next to Pillar rocks
in between Pillar rock and Moyar point  in Kodaikanal  in Dindigul District at the rates specified in the
corresponding entries in column (3) there of provided that such levy of tolls shall not be applicable to
non – commercial motor  vehicles owned by the residents of Kodaikanal Municipal area:
