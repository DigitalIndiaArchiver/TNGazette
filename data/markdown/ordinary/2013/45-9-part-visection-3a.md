GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI.
Taking over of Hazrath Hameed Aulia Dargah, Senkaluneerodai Theru, Big Kanchipuram,
Kanchipuram District under the Direct Management of the Board.
(Lr. Rc. No. 10588/10/B4/KPM.)
No. VI-3(a)/148/2013.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act 1995, the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in
its resolution in item No. 83/13 in Rc.No. 10588/10/B4/KPM dated 12th September 2013  has ordered to take over
Hazrath Hameed Aulia Dargah, Senkaluneerodai Theru, Big Kanchipuram, Kanchipuram District under the direct
control of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of one year from the date of Board’s order (12-09-2013) and to appoint
Wakf Inspector, Kanchipuram as its Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,
F. ABDUL RAZICK,
13th November 2013.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
