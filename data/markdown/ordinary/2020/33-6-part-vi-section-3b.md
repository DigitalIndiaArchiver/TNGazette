Nomination of a Member by the Tamil Nadu Veterinary Council to the  Board of Management of Tamil Nadu Veterinary 
and Animal Sciences University
(U.O.No.  60042/R1/2020 – F.No.  5677/R1/BOM/ 2018 )  
No.VI-3(b)/9/2020.
It is hereby notiﬁ ed that as per Section 18(2)(g) under heading CLASS-II OTHER MEMBERS of the Act of Tamil Nadu 
Veterinary and Animal Sciences University (Tamil Nadu Act 42 of 1989),  the following person has been nominated by the 
Tamil Nadu State Veterinary Council to serve as a member in the Board of Management  of Tamil Nadu Veterinary and Animal 
Sciences   University.  His  term  shall  be  three  years  with  eﬀ ect  from  24th  October  2019  as  per  Section  18(5)  Chapter 
IV of Act, 1989 (Tamil Nadu Act of 1989).

Election of a Member of Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly from Among Themselves to the Board of 
Management of Tamil Nadu Veterinary and Animal Sciences University
(U.O.No. 60041/R1/2020 – F.No.1777/R1/2018)
No.VI-3(b)/10/2020.
 
It is hereby notiﬁ ed that as per Section 18(2)(h) under heading CLASS-II OTHER MEMBERS of the Act of 
Tamil Nadu Veterinary and Animal Sciences University (Tamil Nadu Act 42 of 1989) the following person has been elected 
from among the member of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly to serve as a member in the Board of Management 
of Tamil Nadu Veterinary and Animal Sciences University.  His term shall be three years or till he ceases to be the 
member of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly whichever is earlier with eﬀ ect from 7th May 2020.
