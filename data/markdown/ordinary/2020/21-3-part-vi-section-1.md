GENERAL NOTIFICATION
Variation to the Approved Trichy- Nagapattinam Bye-Pass Road of Thanjavur Local Planning Area.
(Roc.No. 6740/2018/TCP 2 DP) 
No. VI(1)/160/2020.
 In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 33(2) of Town and Country Planning Act 1971, the Director of Town 
and Country Planning, Chennai-2 here by confirms the following variation to the conversion of Industrial use into Residential 
use in Ward: 6, Block No:81, T.S.No:3304/1, 2pt, having an extent of 5487.04 sq.m to the approved Trichy - Nagapattinam 
Bye - Pass road D.D.P.No.III of Thanjavur Local  Planning Area, approved by the Director of Town and Country Planning 
Proceedings in Roc No.8900/2006/DP-3 dated:23-07-2007 and the fact of this approval in Form No.12 published in the 
Tamil Nadu Government  Gazette No.49, Part-Vl—Section-I, Page No.442, dated:19-12-2007 Publication No.VI(1)/605/2007 
and the said draft notification published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No.38, Part-V—Section-I, Page No.318, 
dated: 19-09-2018 Publication No.VI(l)/340/2018. 
2. Since no objections and suggestions have been received on this  draft-notification with in the stipulated time, the 
same are here by confirmed and order as below.
Confirmation of Variation
1. Whenever the expression “MAP No.4 & 5, DDP(TR)IDTCP No.25/2007 occurs the expression DDP(V)/DTCP NO.21/2018 
should be added at the end and to be read with. 
2. In Schedule IX (form No:7) in serial No.1 in column No.1 T.S.No.3304/1, 2pt of Block No.B, Ward No:6 should be 
deleted. 
3. In Schedule IX (form No:7) in serial No.1 in column No.4 Extent 5467.04 sq.mt should be deleted. 
Chennai-2,
