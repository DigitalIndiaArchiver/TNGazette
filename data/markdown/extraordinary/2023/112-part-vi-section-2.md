Amendment to the TNERC (Procedure for Payment of Subsidy by the State Government)
Regulations, 2008
(Notiﬁ cation No.TNERC/PPS/17-1/16-03-2023) 
(Lr.No.TNERC/Legal/451/D.No.360/2023) 
No. VI(2)/6(b)/2023.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 65 of the said Electricity Act, 2003, read with clause (zp) of 
sub-section (2) of Section 181 thereof and all other powers enabling it in this behalf, the Tamil Nadu Electricity 
Regulatory Commission hereby makes the following amendments to the TNERC (Procedure for payment of subsidy 
by the State Government) Regulations, 2008, the draft of the same having been previously published as required 
under sub-section (3) of the said Section 181 of the said Act.
They shall come into force on the date of publication in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary.
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖගඛ
 
(1) In the said Regulation, in Regulation 5, for sub-regulation (ii), the following sub-regulation shall be 
substituted, namely:-
 
(ii) The subsidy shall be payable by the GOTN to the distribution licensee in advance as below as per 
the following schedule of payment ordered by the Commission:—
  
 All category of consumers: Quarterly 
(2) In the said Regulation, in Regulation 5, for sub-regulation (vii), the following sub-regulation shall 
be substituted, namely:-
 
(vii) The distribution licensee shall reconcile the actual subsidy received with actual consumption within 
60 days from the closure of quarterly accounts and send proposal for adjustment of subsidy. The Commission 
may approve the ﬁ nal subsidy.

Distribution Sector Scheme (RDSS), to improve the operational eﬃ  ciencies and ﬁ nancial sustainability of DISCOMs. 
As the Scheme envisages submission of subsidy bills quarterly to the State Government alongwith the reconciliation /
true-up of the subsidy, it has become necessary to amend the regulation. The present amendment seeks to 
adhere the procedure prescribed by the Ministry of Power.
