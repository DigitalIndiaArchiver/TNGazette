NOTIFICATION  BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY SECRETARIAT.
LEGISLATIVE  ASSEMBLY  SECRETARIAT
APPOINTMENT OF PRO-TEM SPEAKER OF TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY
[S.O. Ms. No. 59, Legislative Assembly Secretariat, 21st May 2016,
Vaikasi 8, Thunmugi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu - 2047.]
No. II(1)/LAS/7(O-1)/2016.
“In exercise of the powers conferred by Clause (1) of Article 180 of the Constitution of India, I, Dr. K. ROSAIAH,
Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby appoint Thiru S. Semmalai, being a Member of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly to
perform the duties of the Speaker from the commencement of the first meeting of the Assembly until the Speaker shall have
been chosen by the Assembly under Article 178 of the Constitution.
Raj Bhavan,
K. ROSAIAH,
Guindy,
Governor of Tamil Nadu.
Chennai-600 022.
21st May, 2016.
A.M.P. JAMALUDEEN,
Secretary”.

TAMIL  NADU  GOVERNMENT  GAZETTE  EXTRAORDINARY
APPOINTMENT OF PERSON BEFORE WHOM THE MEMBERS OF TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY MAY MAKE
AND SUBSCRIBE OATH OR AFFIRMATION
[S.O. Ms. No. 60, Legislative Assembly Secretariat, 21st May 2016,
Vaikasi 8, Thunmugi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu - 2047.]
No. II(1)/LAS/7(O-2)/2016.
“In exercise of the powers conferred by Article 188 of the Constitution of India, I, Dr. K. ROSAIAH,
Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby appoint the Speaker Pro-tem, Fifteenth Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly,
Thiru S. Semmalai, as the person before whom the oath or affirmation may be made and subscribed by the Members of
the Legislative Assembly under the said Article.
Raj Bhavan,
K. ROSAIAH,
Guindy,
Governor of Tamil Nadu.
Chennai-600 022.
21st May, 2016.
