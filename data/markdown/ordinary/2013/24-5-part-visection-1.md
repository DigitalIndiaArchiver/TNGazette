GENERAL  NOTIFICATIONS
Confirmation of Variation to the approved Tiruppur Detailed Development Plan No. 18 of Tiruppur Local
Planning Area.
(Roc. No. 15214/2012/DP2.)
No. VI(1)/256/2013—
In exercise  of the powers conferred Under Section 33(2) of Town and Country Planning Act 1971, the Commissioner
of Town and Country Planning, Chennai hereby confirmes the following variation to the approved Tiruppur Detailed
Development Plan No. 18 of Tiruppur Local Planning area, approved by the Director of  Town and Country Planning
in proceeding Roc No. 7883/90/DP2 dated: 1st July 1991 and Published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette
Part VI—Section 1 page No. 650, dated 28th August 1991 and the said draft notification was published in
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, No. 48, Part VI—Section 1, Page No. 348, dated 12th December 2012 in Publication
No.(VI)(1)/359/2012.
2. Since no objections and suggestions have been received on this draft notification within the stipulated time, the
same are here by confirmed and ordered as below.
CONFIRMATION OF VARIATION
1. The entire Tiruppur Local Planning Authority-Detailed Development Plan No. 18 of Tiruppur Local Planning Area,
approved by the Director of Town and Country Planning in proceeding Roc. No. 7883/1990/DP2 is varied by Proceedings
of the Commissioner of Town and Country Planning, Chennai-2, Roc. No. 15214/2012/DP2, dated 9th November 2012
of Details Development Plan No. 18, Tiruppur Local Planning Authority.
2. The Plan thus varied is numbered as Map No. 5, DDP(V)/DTCP No. 24/2012.
3. The Detailed Development plan clauses and schedules annexed with this plan are made enforceable from the
date of publication of this notification in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
Chennai-600 002,
A. KARTHIK,
27th May 2013.
Commissioner of Town and Country Planning.
