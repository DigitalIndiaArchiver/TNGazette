section 8 of the Kumbakonam City Municipal Corporation Ordinance, 2021 (Tamil Nadu Ordinance 7 of 2021), the Governor 
of Tamil Nadu, hereby makes the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Municipalities and Corporations 
(Delimitation of Wards or Divisions and Reservations) Rules, 1996:—
Amendments
In the said Rules,—
(1) in rule 2, in clause (1), for the expression “and the Thoothukudi City Municipal Corporation Act, 2008 (Tamil Nadu 
Act 27 of 2008) in respect of Thoothukudi City Municipal Corporation”, the following expression shall substituted, namely:-
“the Thoothukudi City Municipal Corporation Act, 2008 (Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 2008) in respect of Thoothukudi City Municipal 
Corporation, the Thanjavur City Municipal Corporation Act, 2013 (Tamil Nadu Act 24 of 2013) in respect of Thanjavur City 
Municipal Corporation, the Dindigul City Municipal Corporation Act, 2013 (Tamil Nadu Act 25 of 2013) in respect of Dindigul City 
Municipal Corporation, the Hosur City Municipal Corporation Act, 2019 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 2019) in respect of Hosur City 
Municipal Corporation, the Nagercoil City Municipal Corporation Act, 2019 (Tamil Nadu Act 11 of 2019) in respect of Nagercoil 
City Municipal Corporation, the Avadi City Municipal Corporation Act, 2019 (Tamil Nadu Act 24 of 2019) in respect of Avadi 
City Municipal Corporation, the Cuddalore City Municipal Corporation Ordinance, 2021 (Tamil Nadu Ordinance 1 of 2021) in 
respect of Cuddalore City Municipal Corporation, the Kancheepuram City Municipal Corporation Ordinance, 2021 (Tamil Nadu 
Ordinance 2 of 2021) in respect of Kancheepuram City Municipal Corporation, the Sivakasi City Municipal Corporation Ordinance, 
2021 (Tamil Nadu Ordinance 3 of 2021) in respect of Sivakasi City Municipal Corporation, the Karur City Municipal Corporation 
Ordinance, 2021 (Tamil Nadu Ordinance 4 of 2021) in respect of Karur City Municipal Corporation, the Tambaram City Municipal 
Corporation Ordinance, 2021 (Tamil Nadu Ordinance 5 of 2021) in respect of Tambaram City Municipal Corporation and the 
Kumbakonam City Municipal Corporation Ordinance, 2021 (Tamil Nadu Ordinance 7 of 2021) in respect of Kumbakonam City 
Municipal Corporation” ;
(2) in rule 3,—
	
(i) in sub-rule (1), the expression “by the Inspector of, in the case of” shall be omitted;
	
(ii) in the Explanation, the expression “or the Inspector of” shall be omitted;
(3) in rule 5, to sub-rule (2), the following proviso shall be added, namely:—
“Provided that, where after completion of one full term, no ordinary election is held to the offices of the Chairman of Town 
Panchayats and Municipalities and the Mayor of Corporations before expiry of the ten year period, the reservation to such offices 
shall be rotated for the next ordinary election in such Town Panchayats, Municipalities and Corporations.”.
