ACQUISITION OF LANDS
NOTICE UNDER SECTION 15(1) OF TAMIL NADU HIGHWAYS ACT, 2001 (TAMIL NADU ACT 34 OF 2002) READ 
WITH TAMIL NADU LAND ACQUISITION LAWS (REVIVAL OF OPERATION, AMENDMENT AND VALIDATION)

The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that the lands speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below are 
acquired under Chennai Peripheral Ring Road Project for Highways Department purpose to wit, for formation of road 
for providing connectivity from Ennore Port to Mahabalipuram (from K.M.32+750 to K.M.34+796) at No.74 Athangikavanur 
Village, Uthukottai Taluk, Tiruvallur District and it having already been decided that the entire amount of compensation 
to be awarded for the lands to be paid out of the funds controlled and managed by the  Highways Department and 
after having considering the cause shown by the owners or other persons having interest on such lands, as the case 
may be, do hereby publish the following notice under  sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 
2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) read with the Tamil Nadu Land Acquisition Laws (Revival of Operation, Amendment 
and Validation) Act, 2019.
NOTICE
 
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) read 
with the Tamil Nadu Land Acquisition Laws (Revival of Operation, Amendment and Validation) Act, 2019, the Governor 
of Tamil Nadu hereby notify that the Dry and Wet patta lands speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below and measuring to a total 
extent of 1,04,647  Sq.mtrs. are acquired in No.74 Athangikavanur Village, Uthukottai Taluk, Tiruvallur  District to the 
same, a little more or less, needed for the purpose to wit, for formation of road for providing connectivity from Ennore 
Port to Mahabalipuram (Chennai Peripheral Ring Road Project) by Highways Department. 
The Plan of the lands under acquisition are kept in the oﬃ  ce of the Special District Revenue Oﬃ  cer (Land 
Acquisition), Chennai Peripheral Ring Road Project, Chennai-600 058 and in the oﬃ  ce of the Special Tahsildar (Land 
Acquisition), Chennai Peripheral Ring Road Project, Unit-II, Thatchur at Tiruvallur and may be inspected at any time 
during the oﬃ  ce hours.
