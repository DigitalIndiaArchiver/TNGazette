the Formation of Oil Refinery Project by SIPCOT.
The plan of the lands are kept in the office of the Special District Revenue Officer (LA), Allikulam Oil Refinery 
Industry Formation, SIPCOT Complex, Thoothukudi which may be inspected on any day during office hours.
