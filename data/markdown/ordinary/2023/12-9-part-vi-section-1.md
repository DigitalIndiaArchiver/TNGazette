Confirmation of variation to the Approved Cumbum Detailed Development Plan No.5 of Cumbum Local 
Planning Authority
(Roc. No. 8068/2022/TCP1)
No. VI(1)/212/2023.
In exercise of the power conferred under sub-section (2) of Section 33 of Town  and Country Planning Act 1971 (Act No. 35 of 
1972), the Director of Town and Country Planning hereby confirms the individual draft variation for “Conversion of Higher Secondary 
School purpose use into Residential use in T.S.No. 6/25, 6/26 (Old Survey No: 749/9pt, 740, 755/2 and 757/2) of Ward-D, 
Block -25, Extent 1.6577 Acres,Cumbum Municipality, Uthamapalayam Taluk, Theni District to the Approved Cumbum Detailed 
Development Plan No.5 of Cumbum Local Planning Authority/Municipality, approved by Director of Town and Country Planning in 
Proceeding Roc. No. 14473/94/DP2, dated: 29.11.1994 and the fact of this approval in Form No. 12 published in 
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No.6, Part  VI—Section 1, Page 1 No.131, Dated:15.02.1995, Published No. VI (1)/210/1995. 
and the said notification published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No.36, Part  VI—Section 1, Page No. 421 , Dated 
07.09.2022, Published No.VI(1)/373/2022.
Since no objection and suggestion have been received on the draft notification within the stipulated time, the same is 
hereby confirmed and ordered as below. 
Confirmation of Variation 
1. Where the expression Map No.4, DDP (MR) / DTCP No. 87/1994 occurs the expression of DDP (V)/DTCP 
No. 04/2022 should be added at the end and to be read with. 
2. In Schedule No. IV (Form No.7) the against In Sl.No.7 in Column-2, Comprising T.S.No. 6/25 and 6/26 should 
be deleted. 
3. The draft made enforceable from the date of publication of the confirmed variation notification issued under 
Section 33(2) of the Act in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.

Dated:03.03.2023 proposes to make the following individual draft variation for conversion of Agricultural use into 
Residential use in T.S.Nos.43/5, 43/6, Ward:A, Block-3, (New T.S.Nos:39/5A, 39/6A) of Extent: 2.44 Acres Ambur 
Municipality, Tirupattur District to the approved Ambur Detailed Development Plan No. 4, Tirupattur Local Planning 
Area approved by Director of Town and Country Planning’s Proceedings Roc. No.4443/95/DP3, Dated: 27.03.1995 and 
the fact of this approval in Form No.12 published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No. 23, Part VI—Section 1, 
Page No. 658, 659, 660 dated: 14.06.1995, publication No.VI(1)/694/95

Planning Authority office.
