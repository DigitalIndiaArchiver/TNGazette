Preliminary Notification
No. II(2)/PWD/197(a-2)/2020.                        
Whereas it appears that the appropriate Government has given Administrative Sanction that a total of 1.60.5 hectares of land is required in the Kuravarkulam Village 
in Palayamkottai Taluk in Tirunelveli District for public purpose namely Tamirabarani – Karumeniyar -  Nambiyar Interlinking Flood Carrier Canal Project.  Environmental 
impact Assessment study for the irrigation project (i.e) formation of Flood Carrier canal from Kannadian Channel to Drought prone Areas of Sattankulam & Tisayanvillai 
by interlinking Tamirabarani, Karumeriyar&  Nambiyar Rivers in Tirunelveli and Thoothukudi Districts in Tamilnadu has been carried out by “WAPCOS’ under the section 
3 (3) of the Environmental Protection Act 1986. The land is required for the formation of Flood Carrier Canal.  As the Environment Impact Assessment Study has been 
carried out for this irrigation project, Social Impact Assessment shall not apply according to the provisions of sub section 2 of section 6 of Right to Fair Compensation 
and Transparency in Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Act 2013 The Requisitioning  Body had not entered upon the land till date and canal has not 
been dug for the Flood Carrier Project.  No family was displaced due to the land acquisition.  The Sub Collector, Tirunelveli  has been appointed as Administrator for 
the purpose of Rehabilitation and Resettlement.  Therefore, it is notified that for the above said project in Kuravarkulam Village of Palayamkottai Taluk in Tirunelveli 
District, land measuring an extent of 1.60.5  hectares, the details of which are specified in the Schedule below, is under acquisition:

staff to enter upon a survey land, take levels of any land, dig or bore into the sub-soil and do all other acts required for the proper execution of their work as provided and

Whereas it appears that the appropriate Government has given Administrative Sanction that a total of 0.22.0 hectares of land is required in Itteri Village in 
Palayamkottai Taluk in Tirunelveli District for public purpose, namely, Tamirabarani – Karumeniyar -  Nambiyar interlinking Flood Carrier Canal Project.  Environmental 
impact Assessment study for the irrigation project (i.e) formation of Flood Carrier canal from Kannadian Channel to Drought prone Areas of Sattankulam and Tisayanvillai 
by Interlinking Tamirabarani, Karumeniyar and Nambiyar Rivers in Tirunelveli and Thoothukudi Districts in Tamilnadu has been carried out by “WAPCOS’ under the 
section 3 (3) of the Environmental Protection Act 1986.  The land is acquired to wit  for the formation of Flood Carrier Canal.  As the Environment Impact Assessment 
Study has been carried out for this irrigation project, Social Impact Assessment shall not apply according to the provisions of sub section 2 of section 6 of the Right 
to Fair Compensation and Transparency in Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Act 2013.    The Requisitioning body had entered on the project area 
on 03.12.2010 and canal was dug for the flood carrier project.  No family was displaced due to the land acquisition.  The Sub Collector, Tirunelveli is appointed as 
Administrator for the purpose of Rehabilitation and Resettlement.  Therefore, it is notified that for the above said project in Itteri Village in Palayamkottai Taluk in 
Tirunelveli District, land measuring an extent of 0.22.0 hectares, the details of which are specified in the Schedule below, is under acquisition:

This notification is made under sub-section (1) of section 11 of the Right to Fair Compensation and Transparency in Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement 
Act, 2013 (Central Act 30 of 2013), to all whom it may concern.
A plan of the land may be inspected in the office of the Collector and Land Acquisition Officer on any working day during the working hours.
The Government is pleased to authorize the requisitioning department, viz. Executive Engineer, Water Resources Department, Special Project Division, Tirunelveli and 
his staff to enter upon the survey land, take levels of any land, dig or bore into the sub-soil and do all other acts required for the proper execution of their work as provided 
and specified in section 12 of the said Act.
Under sub-section (4) of section 11 of the said Act, no person shall make any transaction or cause any transaction of land i.e., sale/purchase, etc., or create any 
encumbrances on such land from the date of publication of this notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette without prior approval of the Collector.
Objections to the acquisition, if any, may be filed by the person interested within 60 (Sixty) days from the date of publication of this notification in the 
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette as provided under Section 15 of the said Act before the collector.
