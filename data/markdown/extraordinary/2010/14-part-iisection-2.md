AGRICULTURE  DEPARTMENT
Acquisition of Lands
[G.O. Ms. No. 8, Agriculture (AU), 12th January 2010.]
No. II(2)/AG/32(a)/2010.
Whereas in exercise of the powers conferred by
clause (1) of Article 258 of the Constitution of India the
President has in Notification No. 4(1)65 General, dated
23rd April 1966 entrusted to the Government of Tamil
Nadu with their consent the functions of the Central
Government under the Land Acquisition Act, 1894
(Central Act I of 1894) in relation to acquisition of land for
the purpose of the Union in this State.
And, whereas the land specified in the scheduled
below are needed for a public purpose, to wit, for
construction of buildings for Paddy Processing Research
Center, notice, to that effect is hereby given to all whom
it may concern in accordance with the provisions of
Section 4(1) of the said Act.
Now therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by
the aforesaid section, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
specially authorises the Revenue Divisional Officer,
Thanjavur, his staff and workmen to exercise the powers
conferred by Section 4(2) of the said Act. Under
Section 3(c) of the said Act, the Governor of Tamil Nadu
appoints the Revenue Divisional Officer, Thanjavur, to
perform the functions of a Collector under Section 5A of
the said Act.
