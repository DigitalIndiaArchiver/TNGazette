MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION AND WATER SUPPLY DEPARTMENT
REMOVAL OF CERTAIN PERSON AS VICE-CHAIRMAN, LALPETTAI TOWN PANCHAYAT, CUDDALORE DISTRICT
UNDER THE TAMIL NADU DISTRICT MUNICIPALITIES ACT.
[G.O. (D) No. 510, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (TP.II), 5th October 2010,
Purattasi 19, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No. II (2)/MAWS/604(d)/2010.
Whereas, a written notice of intention to make a motion expressing want of confidence in Thiru M.K. Haja Mohideen,
Vice Chairman, Lalpettai Town Panchayat, Cuddalore District, signed by thirteen councillors, out of the sanctioned strength of the
fifteen councillors of the said Town Panchayat council together with a copy of the motion proposed to be made have been delivered
to the Assistant Director of Town Panchayats, Cuddalore Zone in person by the said councillors under the provisions of
sub-section (2) of Section 40-A of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920);
And, Whereas, the said motion expressing want of confidence in Thiru M. K. Haja Mohideen, Vice Chairman of the said Town
Panchayat has been carried out with the support of fourteen councillors out of the sanctioned strength of the fifteen councillors
in the special meeting convened by the Assistant Director of Town Panchayats, Cuddalore Zone, on 11th June 2010, after following
the procedures specified in Section 40-A of the said Act;
Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (12) of Section 40-A of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities
Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby removes Thiru. M. K. Haja Mohideen, Vice Chairman,
Lalpettai Town Panchayat, Cuddalore District, from the Office of Vice Chairman of the said Town Panchayat.
    K. ASHOK VARDHAN SHETTY,
  Principal Secretary to Government.
