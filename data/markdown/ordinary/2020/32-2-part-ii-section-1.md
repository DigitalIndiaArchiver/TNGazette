No.II(1)/AG/13/2020.—In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (a) of sub-section (1) of Section 9 read with 
Section 3  of the Tamil Nadu Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) Act, 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 1989), the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby declares his intention of excluding the Notiﬁ ed Market areas of Kallakurichi, Chinnaselam, 
Thiyagathurgam, Ulundurpet, Tirunavalur, Sankarapuram, Mungilturaipattu, Manalurpet regulated markets and Vadakkanandal 
sub-regulated market from the Notiﬁ ed areas of Villupuram Market Committee.
2. Objection or suggestion which may be received by the Government of Tamil Nadu, within one month from the 
date of publication of this Notiﬁ cation in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette will be considered by them.
3. Objection or suggestion, if any, should be sent to the Agricultural Production Commissioner and Principal Secretary 
to Government, Agriculture Department, Secretariat, Chennai-600 009, through the Commissioner of Agricultural Marketing 
and Agri Business, CIPET Road, Guindy, Chennai-600 032.

No.II(1)/AG/14/2020.— In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Agricultural Produce Marketing 
(Regulation) Act, 1987, (Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 1989), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby declares his intention of regulating 
the marketing of Paddy, Groundnut (Pots and Kernals) whole or split, Gingelly or Seasamum Seed or Ellu, Cotton, (Kapas, 
Lint, Waste), Blackgram (Ulundu) in all forms, Greengram (Pachaippairu) in all forms, Cashewnuts in all forms, Sugarcane 
Jaggery in all forms (Jaggery Powder, brown sugar, etc.,) Coriander (Dhania Seeds), Turmeric in all forms (bulb in ﬁ nger), 
Cowpea (Karamani) in all forms, Varagu, Neem Seeds and Cotton seeds in the notiﬁ ed market areas of Kallakurichi, 
Chinnaselam, Thiyagathurgam, Ulundurpet, Tirunava!ur, Sankarapuram, Mungilturaipattu, Manalurpet regulated markets and 
Vadakkanandal sub-regulated market in Kallakurichi District. 
2. Objection or suggestion which may be received by the Government of Tamil Nadu within one month from the 
date of publication of this Notiﬁ cation in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette will be considered by them. 
3. Objection or suggestion, if any, should be sent to the Agricultural Production Commissioner and Principal Secretary 
to Government, Agriculture Department, Secretariat, Chennai - 600 009, through the Commissioner of Agricultural Marketing 
and Agri Business, CIPET Road, Guindy, Chennai-600 032.
