Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994) and in partial modification of the Rural Development and Panchayat Raj

2nd October 1960, published at page 56  of Part II-Section 2,   the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby excludes the local areas
specified in the Table below, from the Pennagaram panchayat development block so as to constitute them as a separate
panchayat development block.
