Election of One Nominee by the Members of the Senate to the Vice-Chancellor Search Committee for
recommending a panel of names for Appointment as Vice-Chancellor.
(Lr. No. El (1)/30656/2015.)
No. VI-3(b)/29/2015.
Under Statute 7 of Chapter V of the General Statutes of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai and as directed
by the Governing Council of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai, the Registrar In-Charge of the University hereby,
notifies that a permanent vacancy is about to occur in the office of the Vice-Chancellor with effect from 13-12-2015 F.N. consequent
on the expiry of the term of office of the present Vice-Chancellor, Prof.Dr. D. Shantharam, M.D., D.Diab.,
Necessary action to elect one nominee by the Members of the Senate to the Committee referred to in Section 10 (2) of the
Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai, Act, 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 37 of 1987), to recommend a panel of three names
to the Chancellor for making the appointment of the next Vice-Chancellor, will be taken on the publication of the Notification in the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette as specified in the Annexure.
Necessary action to elect one nominee by the Members of the Governing Council to the said Committee as provided under
Statute 12(2) of Chapter V of the said Statutes will be taken on the publication of the Notification in the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
