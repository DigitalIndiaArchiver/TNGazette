NOTIFICATION TO THE AWARD OF  PROCUREMENT OF THE CONSULTANCY SERVICES TO UNDERTAKE ENVIRONMENT
IMPACT ASSESSMENT/ENVIRONMENT MANAGEMENT PLAN OF THE PROJECT CERTAIN AREA OF THE SCHEME
UNDER THE TAMIL NADU TRANSPARENCY IN TENDERS ACT, 1998.
[G.O. Ms. No. 30, Finance (Salaries), 31st January 2014, Thai 18, Vijaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2045.]
No. II(2)/FIN/65(b)/2014.
Under clause (f) of Section 16 of the Tamil Nadu Transparency in Tenders Act, 1998 (Tamil Nadu Act 43 of 1998), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notifies  the award of procurement of the Consultancy Services to undertake Environment Impact
Assessment/Environment Management Plan of the project area of the Scheme  “Formation of Flood carrier Canal from
Kannadian Channel to drought prone areas of Sathankulam and Thisaiyanvilai by inter-linking of Thamiraparani, Karumeniyar and
Nambiyar Rivers in Thirunelveli and Thoothukudi Districts”, on Nomination basis to Messrs Water and Power Consultancy
Services (WAPCOS) Limited.
K. SHANMUGAM,
Principal Secretary to Government.
