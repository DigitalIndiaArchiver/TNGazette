REVENUE  DEPARTMENT
CONSTITUTION OF LAND TRIBUNAL IN EACH DISTRICT UNDER THE TAMIL NADU LAND
REFORMS  (FIXATION OF CEILING ON LAND) ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 78, Revenue [LR-I(1)], 6th March 2012.]
No. II(2)/REV/145(a)/2012.
In exercise of  the powers conferred by Section 76  read with sub-section (1) of Section 77 of the
Tamil Nadu Land Reforms (Fixation of Ceiling on Land) Act, 1961 (Tamil Nadu Act 58 of 1961), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby constitutes the Land Tribunal in each District (except Chennai District)
consisting of the Additional Collector or the District Revenue Officer, as the case may be, having jurisdiction
over their respective district for the purposes of the said Act.
RAMESHRAM  MISHRA,
Principal Secretary to Government.
