TAMIL NADU VETERINARY AND ANIMAL SCIENCES UNIVERSITY, CHENNAI
Nomination of a Member of the Board of Management of Tamil Nadu Veterinary and
Animal Sciences University
(U.O. No. 60157/R.1/BOM/2011-F.No. 15516/R.1/BOM/2011, dated 21-12-2011.)
No. VI-3(b)/6/2012.
It is hereby notified that as per Section 18(2) (f) under heading Class-II Other Members, Chapter IV of Tamil Nadu
Veterinary and Animal Sciences University (Tamil Nadu Act 42 of 1989) Dr. Madan Mohan, Assistant Director General (Marine
Fisheries), Krishi Anusandhan Bhavan-II, Pusa, New Delhi-110 012 has been nominated by the Director General, Indian
Council of Agricultural Research, New Delhi to serve as a member in the Board of Management of Tamil Nadu Veterinary and
Animal Sciences University. His term shall be three years with effect from 28th October 2011 as per Section 18(5), Chapter
IV of Act, 1989 (Tamil Nadu Act 42 of 1989).
