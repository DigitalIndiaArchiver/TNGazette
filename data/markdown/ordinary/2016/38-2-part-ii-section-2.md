HOME DEPARTMENT
Constitution of Separate District Munsif Court at Keeranur
in Pudukkottai District under the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts
Act.
[G.O. Ms. No. 557, Home (Cts-III), 29th July 2016.]
No.II(2)/HO/643/2016.—In exercise of the powers conferred
by the second paragraph of Section 4 of the Tamil Nadu Civil
Courts Act,1873 (Central Act III of 1873), the Governor of
Tamil Nadu, after consultation with the High Court, Madras,
hereby fixes the number of District Munsif to be appointed for
the District Munsif Court at Keeranur as one with effect from
the date of constitution of separate District Munsif Court at
Keeranur in Pudukkottai District by bifurcating the existing District
Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court at Keeranur.
NOTIFICATION-II
[G.O. Ms. No. 557, Home (Cts-III), 29th July 2016.]
No.II(2)/HO/644/2016.—In exercise of the powers conferred
by Section 10 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central
Act III of 1873), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that
in the District of Pudukkottai, the Subordinate Judge’s Court,
Pudukkottai, shall have and exercise appellate jurisdiction over
the District Munsif Court at Keeranur with effect from the date
of constitution of separate District Munsif Court at Keeranur
by bifurcating the existing District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate
Court at Keeranur in Pudukottai District.
Constitution of the Fast Track Court at Magisterial Level
at Eraniel by Shifting the existing Fast Track Court at
Magisterial Level No. II at Nagercoil under the Code of
Criminal Procedure.
Amendment to Notification
[G.O. Ms. No. 640, Home (Courts-II), 7th September
2016, ÝõE 22, ¶¡ºA, F¼õœÀõ˜ Ý‡´-2047.]
No.II(2)/HO/645/2016.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by sub-section (1) of the Section 11 of the Code
of Criminal Procedure 1973 (Central Act 2 of 1974 ), the
Government of Tamil Nadu, after consultation with the High
Court, Madras, hereby makes the following amendment to
the Home Department Notification No.II(2)/HO/323/2016,
published at page 330, of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette
Part II—Section 2 dated 11th May 2016.
AMENDMENT
In the said Notification, for the expression “(Fast Track Court
of Madras at Magisterial Level)”, the expression “(Fast Track
Court at Magisterial Level)” shall be substituted.
APURVA VARMA,
Principal Secretary to Government.

of Second Master Plan for Chennai Metropolitan Area
2026 relating to the Existing Hospital Building,

No.II(2)/HOU/646/2016.—In exercise of powers conferred
Under Section 113 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country
Planning Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972), the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby exempt the existing hospital building of
Ground Floor + 2 Floors + 3rd Floor (pt.) of Main Block and
Ground Floor + 2 Floors Ramanamma Block at
Old S.No. 325 (pt.), T.S. No.16 & 21, Block No. 14, Ward
No.E of Tambaram Village from the provisions of DR (25),
Table 7(E) relating to Plot coverage, DR 25 Table (7 F) relating
to setbacks allaround of Second Master Plan for Chennai
Metropolitan Area 2026, subject to condition that clear Set Back
(Drive Way) of 3.0m should be provided in between the temple
and the main building (Sri Ramanamma Block) and required
car parking should be provided as per DR requirements and
an undertaking to assure that the additional land amalgamated
shall be used only for car parking and two wheeler parking
and it will not be put to any other use later should be furnished.
Exemption from the provisions of Development Regulation
of Second Master Plan for Chennai Metropolitan Area
2026 relating to the Proposed Construction of Hospital
Building at Villivakkam Village, Chennai under the Act.

No.II(2)/HOU/647/2016.—In exercise of powers conferred
under Section 113 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning
Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972) the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby exempt the proposed construction of Triple
Basement Floor (meant for parking) + Ground
Floor + 6 Floors Hospital Building at Old S.No.151 part &
152 part, Present T.S.No.24/2, Block No.41 of Villivakkam Village,
within Chennai Corporation Limit from the provisions of DR(28)
(2) (G) in respect of Set backs all around subject to production
of fresh NOC from DF & RS for the proposed triple Basement
floor + ground floor + 6 floors building and DR 28 (2) (D) in
respect of FSI subject to payment of Premium FSI charges
for the excess FSI area and provision of car parking, generator
room and other MSB parameters as per Development
Regulations of the Second Master Plan for Chennai Metropolitan
Area 2026.
