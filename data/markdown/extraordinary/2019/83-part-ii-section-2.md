Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby 
acquires the lands in No.08, Gollalakuppam Village in respect 
of Pallipattu Taluk of Tiruvallur District as specified in the 
Schedule below and measuring 29565 Sq.meters to the 
same, a little more or less needed for industrial purposes, to 
wit, for formation of Tindivanam to Nagari New Broad Gauge 
Railway Line.
The plan of the lands kept in the Office of the Land 
Acquisition Officer and District Revenue Officer, Tiruvallur and 
Office of the Special Tahsildar, (LA), Taluk Office @ Pallipattu 
and may be inspected at any time during office hours.
