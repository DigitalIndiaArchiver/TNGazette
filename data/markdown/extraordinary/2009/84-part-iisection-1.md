NOTIFICATIONS  BY  THE  LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY SECRETARIAT
LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY SECRETARIAT
VACATION OF SEAT IN TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY DUE TO RESIGNATION OF
THIRU M. KANNAPPAN, MEMBER OF THE TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY.
[Letter No. 6436/2009-1, TNLAS (B.III), 25th March 2009.]
No. II(1)/LAS/7(a)/2009.
Whereas, Thiru M. Kannappan, a Member of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly representing
the 103. Thondamuthur Assembly Constituency, in his letter, dated 25th March, 2009 addressed to me as
Speaker of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly, has resigned his seat as Member of the said Assembly
with effect from 25th March 2009.
Now, therefore, I, R. AVUDAIAPPAN, Speaker of the said Assembly hereby accept the resignation of
Thiru  M. Kannappan, as envisaged under Rule 22 of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Rules as it
is in accordance with Rule 21 of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Rules. Under Article 190(3)(b) of
the Constitution of India, his seat has, therefore, become vacant with effect from the above date.
Secretariat,
R. AVUDAIAPPAN,
Chennai-600 009,
Speaker,
Dated 25th March 2009.
Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly.
