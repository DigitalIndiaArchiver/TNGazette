The Government of Tamil Nadu has been satisﬁ ed that the 
lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below have to be acquired due 
for industrial purposes to wit for a formation of Tindivanam 
to Nagari New Broad Gauge Railway Line, and it having 
already been decided that the entire amount of compensation 
to be awarded for the lands is to be paid out of the lands 
controlled or managed by Southern Railway. The following 
notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the 
Tamil Nadu Acquisition of land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu 
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu 
hereby acquires the lands in No.15, Kodivalasa Village in 
respect of Pallipattu Taluk of Tiruvallur District as speciﬁ ed in 
the Schedule below and measuring 154099 Sq.meters to the 
same, a little more or less needed for industrial purposes, to 
wit, for formation of Tindivanam to Nagari New Broad Gauge 
Railway Line.
