The following Notification of the Government of India, Ministry of Law and Justice (Department of Justice),
New Delhi, dated 28th March, 2011 is republished:—
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (1) of Article 217 of the Constitution of India, the President is
pleased to appoint (i) Shri Justice R.S. Ramanathan, (ii) Shri Justice Balasundaram Rajendran, (iii) Shri Justice Raja
Elango, (iv) Shri Justice Duraisamy Hariparanthaman, (v) Shri Justice Cyril Thamarai Selvam, (vi) Shri Justice Chinnasamy
Swaminathan Karnan, (vii) Shri Justice Nadesa Gounder Kirubakaran, (viii) Shri Justice M.M. Sundresh, (ix) Shri Justice
Tirunelveli Subbiah Sivagnanam, (x) Shri Justice M. Duraiswamy, Additional Judges, to be Judges of the Madras
High Court, in that order of seniority, with effect from the dates they assume charge of their offices.
2.
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (1) of Article 224 of the Constitution of India, the President is
pleased to appoint (i) Shri T. Raja, (ii) Kumari R. Mala, (iii) Smt. Aruna Jagadeesan and (iv) Shri G.M. Akbar Ali, to
be Additional Judges of the Madras High Court in that order of seniority, for a period of two years with effect from
31st March 2011.
