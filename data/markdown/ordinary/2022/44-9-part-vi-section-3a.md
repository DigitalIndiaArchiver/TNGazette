GENERAL NOTIFICATION
TAMIL NADU WAQF BOARD, CHENNAI-600 001.
Taking over the Kalvath Nayagam Taika, Kalvath Nayagam Taika Street, Kadayanalloor, Tenkasi District under direct 
Management of Tamil Nadu Waqf Board.
(Lr.Rc. No.15305/B1/77/TNV)
No. VI-3(a)/80/2022.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under section 65 of the Waqf Act 1995, the Tamil Nadu 
Waqf Board in its Resolution Item No.160/22 in R.C. No. 15305/B1/77/TNV dated:26.07.2022 has ordered to take 
over KALVATH NAYAGAM TAIKA, KALVATH NAYAGAM TAIKA STREET, KADAYANALLOOR, TENKASI DISTRICT 
(G.S.No.341/TNV) under the Direct Management of Tamil Nadu Waqf Board and to appoint the Superintendent of 
Waqfs, Tirunelveli as its Executive Officer.
Chennai, 	
 A.B. RAFIULLA,
28th October 2022.	
Chief Executive Officer, 
 
 
	
Tamil Nadu Waqf Board.
