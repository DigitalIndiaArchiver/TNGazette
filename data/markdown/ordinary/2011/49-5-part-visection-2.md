be treated as invalid and sent to the Secretary, Board of Higher Secondary Examination / Directorate of
Government  Examinations, Chennai-6 / Director of Technical Education, Chennai-25 / Inspector / Inspectress of Matriculation
and Anglo-Indian Schools as the case may be for cancellation:—
