RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND PANCHAYAT RAJ DEPARTMENT
DISQUALIFICATION OF THIRU G. ELUMALAI, 2nd  WARD MEMBER, ANDAMPALLAM VILLAGE PANCHAYAT,
THIRUVANNAMALAI PANCHAYAT UNION, THIRUVANNAMALAI DISTRICT UNDER THE TAMIL NADU PANCHAYATS ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 115, Rural Development and Panchayat Raj (PR-IV), 6th October 2009.]
No. II(2)RDPR/510(e)/2009.
Whereas, the opinion of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission has been sought for, regarding the disqualification of
Thiru G. Elumalai, 2nd Ward Member, Andampallam Village Panchayat, Thiruvannamalai Panchayat Union, Thiruvannamalai
District,  as required under sub-section (2) of Section 41 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994
(Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994) as he has absented himself from the three consecutive meetings of the said Village
Panchayat.
And, whereas the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission, after conducting the enquiry, has stated that a
Notice No. Na.Ka.5258/2009-2/á.«î.1, dated 23rd July 2009 sent to Thiru G. Elumalai, 2nd Ward Member, Andampallam
Village Panchayat, Thiruvannamalai Panchayat Union, Thiruvannamalai District was returned on 30th July 2009 with the
postal endorsement as ‘returned to sender as party residing abroad’ and it is therefore proved that he is living in a foreign
country and on verification of the attendance register of  Andampallam Village Panchayat, it is also confirmed that,
Thiru G. Elumalai, 2nd Ward Member has not attended more than three consecutive meetings of the said Village
Panchayat after the meeting held on 3rd December 2007 and thereby opined that the said Thiru G. Elumalai,
2nd Ward Member, Andampallam Village Panchayat, Thiruvannamalai Panchayat Union, Thiruvannamalai District, is disqualified
to continue as Ward Member of the Andampallam Village Panchayat, under clause (i) of sub-section (3) of Section 38 of the said
Act;
Now, therefore, under sub-section (1) of Section 41 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act  21 of 1994),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby decides that Thiru G. Elumalai, 2nd Ward Member, Andampallam Village Panchayats,
Thiruvannamalai Panchayat Union, Thiruvannamalai District, has become disqualified from holding the office of 2nd Ward
Member of the Andampallam Village Panchayat, under clause (i) of sub-section (3) of Section 38 of the said Act as he
has absented himself from the three consecutive meetings of the said Village Panchayat after the meeting
held on 3rd December 2007.
 K. ASHOK VARDHAN SHETTY,
Principal Secretary to Government.
