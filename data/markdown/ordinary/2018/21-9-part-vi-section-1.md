Judicial NOTIFICATIONS
Modified Notification Regarding the Arrangements Made By Principal Judge, Chennai Division, Chennai For 
Attending Urgent Civil Matters During Summer Vacation, 2018.
(Roc. No. 113 /2018/E1)
(Dis. No. 7482/2018)
No.VI(1)/183/2018.
1. Consequent to the Hon'ble High Court's Notification No.69/2018 in Roc. No.9/2018-Con. B2 dated 04-05-2018 and 
Official Memorandum in Roc. No.9/2018-Con. B2 dated 04-05-2018 the following sitting arrangement is made for 
attending urgent Civil matters during Summer Vacation, 2018 for the period from 05-05-2018 to 03-06-2018 (Both 
days inclusive).
2. Tmt. C. Karthika, M.A., B.L., XIV Assistant Judge, City Civil Court, Chennai will be the Civil Vacation Judge from 
05-05-2018 to 15-05-2018.
3. Thiru. S. Ganapathysamy, B.A., B.L., the then XV Assistant Judge, City Civil Court, Chennai, now transferred and posted 
as the X Assistant Judge, City Civil Court, Chennai, will be the Civil Vacation Judge from 16-05-2018 to 03-06-2018.
City Civil Court,	
S. Subadevi,	
	
 
Chennai-600 104.	
Principal Judge.	
	
	
	
	
Conferment of Powers 
(Roc.No.25700/2018/B6)
No.VI(1)/184/2018.
No.67/2018—In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 13 of the Criminal Procedure Code 1973, (Central 
Act 2 of 1974),  the High Court hereby appoints the following 8 Deputy Tahsildars in Pudukottai District, 8 Deputy 
Tahsildars in Karur District, one Tahsildar and 6 Deputy Tahsildars in Perambalur District, 2 Tahsildars in Ariyalur District, 
one Tahsildar Trainee in Vellore (HQDT, Nemili Taluk), to undergo Magisterial Training for a period of 120/180 days, as 
the case may be, from the date of assumption of charge and confers upon them the ordinary power conferrable under 
this code on a Judicial Magistrate to try such cases which were earlier triable and dealt with by an erstwhile Judicial 
Second Class Magistrate.
