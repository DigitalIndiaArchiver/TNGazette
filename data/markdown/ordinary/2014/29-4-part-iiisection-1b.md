AMENDMENT
In the amendments, issued in G.O. Ms. No. 147, Personnel and Administrative Reforms (B) Department, dated
19th December 2013 and notified in Personnel and Administrative Reforms (B) Department Notification No. SRO B-10/2014,
published in Part-III-Section 1(b) of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 5th February 2014, in amendment (1),
for the expression “Assistant in the Backward Classes Welfare Department (Non-Technical) (One out of every two substantive
vacancies)”, the expression “Assistant in the Backward Classes Welfare Department (Non-Technical) One out of every three
substantive vacancies)” shall be substituted.
P.W.C. DAVIDAR,
Principal Secretary to Government.
