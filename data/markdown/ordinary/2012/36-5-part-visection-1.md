GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
Confirmation of Variation to the Approved Railway Station Area Detailed Development Plan of
Pattukottai Local Planning Area.
(Roc. No. 2025/2004/DP3.)
No.VI(1)/272/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 33(2) of Town and Country Planning Act 1971.  The Commissioner
of Town and Country Planning, Chennai here by confirmes the following Variation to the approved Railway Station Area
Detailed Development Plan of Pattukottai Local Planning Area, approved in Director of Town and Country Planning proceeding
in ROC No. 42209/90 DP1, dated 29th October 1990 and the fact of this approval in Form No. 12 published in Tamil Nadu
Government Gazette Part VI—Section 1 page No. 326 & 327, dated 17th April 1991 and the said draft Notification was
published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No. 49, Part VI—Section 1, Page No. 410, dated
20th December 2006 Publication No. (VI)(1)/555/2006.
2. Since no objections and suggestions have been received on this draft notification with in the stipulated time, the same
are here by confirmed and ordered as below.
CONFIRMATION OF VARIATION
1. The entire Railway Station Area Detailed Development plan of Pattukkottai Local Planning Area.  Approved in Director
of Town and Country Planning proceedings Roc. No. 42209/90 DP1, dated 29th October 1990 is varied by Railway Station
Area Detailed Development Plan of Pattukkottai Local Planning Area.
2. The plan thus varied is numbered as Map No. 5 DDP/(V)DTCP No. 40/2006 and
3. The Detailed Development plan clauses and schedules annexed with this plan are made enforceable from the date
of publication of this Notification in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
Chennai-600 002,
A. KARTHIK,
31st July 2012.
Commissioner of Town and Country Planning.
Comprehensive Draft Variation to the Kottaikulam Detailed Development Plan of Pattukottai
Local Planning Area.
(Roc. No. 15101/2004/DP3.)
No. VI(1)/273/2012.
In exercise of the Powers conferred under sub-section (1) of Section 33 of  Town and Country Planning Act 1971,   (Act
No. 35 of 1972). The Commissioner of Town and Country Planning,  in the  Proceedings in ROC No. 15101/04 DP3, dated
1st August 2012 proposes to make the comprehensive draft variation to the Kottaikulam Detailed Development Plan of
Pattukkottai Local Planning Area approved vide proceedings Roc 12949/90 DP3, dated 6th April 1990 of Director of Town
and Country Planning and published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No. 35 Part VI—Section 1
Page No. 737 publication No. VI (1)/117/90, dated 5th September 1990.
2. Any person affected or interested in this draft variation may within Sixty days from the date of publication of this
notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, can represent in person or submit in writing to the Member-
Secretary, Local Planning Authority any objections and suggestions relating there to.
3. The Variation with plan may be inspected free of cost at any time during office hours at the above said Local
Planning Authority Office.
VARIATION
1. The entire Kottaikulam Detailed Development plan of Pattukkottai Local Planning Area is comprehensively varied by
the Commissioner of Town and Country Planning vide proceedings Roc. No. 15101/04 DP3, dated 1st August 2012.
2. The plan thus varied is numbered as Map No. 5 DDP/(V)DTCP No. 16/2012 and
3. The Detailed Development clauses and Schedules annexed with this plan are made enforceable from the date of
publication of the confirmed variation Notification to be issued under Section 33(2) of the Act and published in the in Tamil
Nadu Government Gazette.
Chennai-600 002,
A. KARTHIK,
1st August 2012.
Commissioner of Town and Country Planning.

Country Planning Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972) and G.O. Ms. No. 94, Housing and Urban Development UD4(1)

approved under the said Act and published in the Housing and Urban Development Department, Notification
No. II(2)/HOU/645/95 at pages 190 and 191 of Part II—Section 2 of the  Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the
2nd Fabruary 1995.

and Name 5 PARAVAI under the heading VI Agricultural Use Zone to heading IV Educational Use Zone.
