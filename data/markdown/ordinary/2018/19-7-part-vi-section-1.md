Adjournment of Court of Small Causes, Chennai for Summer Vacation, 2018.
(Dis.No. 1164/2018)
No.VI(1)/158/2018.
 
The Presidency Court of Small Causes, Chennai will remain closed for the Summer Vacation of 2018.
The Summer Vacation (for the year 2018) of this Court will be from 01-05-2018 to 03-06-2018. The First turn will 
be from 01-05-2018 to 16-05-2018 and the Second turn of the Vacation will be from 17-5-2018 to 03-06-2018.
The Ofﬁ ce of the Court of Small Causes, Chennai will function from Monday to Wednesday and Friday each 
week from 10 A.M to 4.30 P.M and on Thursday (i.e) court sitting day, from 10 A.M to 5.45 P.M. during the Summer 
Vacation 2018.
Thiru. S. Muthukumaravel, Special Sub Judge-II, Court of Small Cause, Chennai will be the Vacation Judge for ﬁ rst 
turn from 01-05-2018 to 16-05-2018.
Thiru. G. Vijayakumar, VI Judge, Court of Small Cause, Chennai will be the Vacation Judge for Second turn from 
17-05-2018 to 03-06-2018.
Thiru. D. Baskar, XIII Judge, Court of Small Cause, Chennai will be the Additional Vacation Judge for First turn 
from 01-05-2018 to 16-05-2018. The Additional Vacation Judge will deal with Rent Control Original petitions.
Thiru. P. Sundarajan, XI Judge, Court of Small Cause, Chennai will be the Additional Vacation Judge for Second 
turn from 17-05-2018 to 03-06-2018. the Additional Vacation Judge will deal with Rent Control Original petitions.
Thiru. N. Kothandaraj, Registrar; Court of Small Causes, Chennai will be the Vacation Ofﬁ cer for the Period from 
01-05-2018 to 03-06-2018. (First turn and Second turn).
The Filing Section will be functioning on every Wednesdays and the sitting of the Judges will be on every Thursdays 
during the Summer Vacation 2018. The Vacation judges unless otherwise notiﬁ ed will sit on all Thursday of every week 
during the Vacation, to dispose the emergent petitions. No fresh plaint petition, no Rent Control Appeals or other matters 
will be received during the period of Vacation, unless it is urgent and it is accompanied by an application duly supported 
by an Afﬁ davit showing the urgency.
The Copyist Section of the Court of Small Causes will be Kept open during Summer Vacation between 10.30. A.M 
to 3.00 P.M for the purpose of receiving copy application and also for delivering the certiﬁ ed copies, which were already 
made ready, Further no fresh certiﬁ ed copy will be made ready during Summer Vacation.
The Nazir Section will function during Summer Vacation and Process Application will be received between 
10.30. A.M to 3.00 P.M. on Wednesdays. Warrants will be issued only on Wednesdays and Thursdays of the respective 
weeks, which were already ﬁ led and made ready. Warrants which are being ﬁ led on Wednesday will be made ready and 
will be issued only on the next day (i.e) Thursday. If the Warrants returned for reasons, (i.e.) since Police protection 
not available), the same will be re-issued only on next Wednesday or Thursday, if the Petitioner/Decree holder wants 
to execute the Warrant and makes such endorsement.
The Ofﬁ ce of the Registrar will be kept open during Summer Vacation but the urgent matters will be received on 
Wednesdays during the Vacation and such matters will be posted before the Vacation Judges and the Additional Vacation 
Judges for the hearing on the succeeding Thursday in the open court.
Money will be received in the Treasury Section on Wednesdays and Thursdays of every week and money will be 
paid out only on Thursdays during the Vacation, when the Vacation judges are holding Courts and on orders made by 
the Vacation Judges for such payments.

Constitution of a Separate Judicial Magistrate Court at Chengam by converting the existing Principal District Munsif-
cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Chengam in Tiruvannamalai District and Constitution of a separate District Munsif 
Court at Chengam by converting the existing Additional District Munsif Court at Chengam in Tiruvannamalai District.
 
(Roc. No. 2991/A/2009/G/Judn)
No.VI(1)/159/2018.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873), 
the High Court, Madras, hereby ﬁ xes Chengam in Tiruvannamalai District as the place at which the District Munsif Court, 
Chengam, shall be held.
NOTIFICATION-II
          (Roc. No. 2991/A/2009/G/Judn)
No.VI(1)/160/2018.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 11 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 
1873), the High Court, Madras, hereby directs that in Tiruvannamalai District, the District Munsif Court, Chengam, 
shall have local jurisdiction over the entire Chengam Taluk with effect from the date of constitution of a separate District 
Munsif Court at Chengam by converting the existing Additional District Munsif Court, Chengam.
NOTIFICATION-III
          (Roc. No. 2991/A/2009/G/Judn)
No.VI(1)/161/2018.
The High Court, Madras hereby directs and notiﬁ es that the District Munsif, Chengam in Tiruvannamalai District shall 
exercise all the powers conferred on a District Munsif under Section 12 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 
1873) as amended up-to-date in regard to original suits and proceedings not otherwise exempted from his cognizance of which 
the amount or value of the subject matter does not exceed One Lakh Rupees within the local limits of his jurisdiction with effect 
from the date of constitution of a separate District Munsif Court at Chengam by converting the existing Additional District Munsif 
Court, Chengam.
NOTIFICATION-IV
      (Roc. No. 2991/A/2009/G/Judn)
No.VI(1)/162/2018.
In exercise of the power conferred by Section 28 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873) the 
High Court, Madras, hereby directs that the District Munsif, Chengam shall have and exercise jurisdication of a Court of Small 
Causes under the provincial Small Causes Court Act, 1887 for the trial of suits cognizable by a Court of Small Causes upto the 
pecuniary limits of Rs.5000/- (Rupees ﬁ ve thousand only) with effect from the date of constitution of a separate District Munsif 
Court at Chengam by converting the existing Additional District Munsif Court, Chengam.
Notiﬁ cation for Re-Designation of Court at Chengam in Tiruvannamalai District
(Roc. No. 2991/A/2009/G/Judn)
No.VI(1)/163/2018.
whereas the Government of Tamil Nadu have issued orders in G.O.Ms.No.798, Home (Cts.II) Department, dated 
6-11-2014 that a separate Judicial Magistrate Court be constituted at Chengam by converting the existing Principal District 
Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Chengam and in G.O.Ms.No.297, Home (Cts-III) Department, dated 05-04-2017 have 
issued orders that the existing Additional District Munsif Court, Chengam be converted as a separate District Munsif Court at 
Chengam.
Hence, it is hereby informed that on and from the date on which the District Munsif Court and judicial Magistrate Court at 
Chengam are constituted, the existing Principal District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Chengam shall be re-designated as 
Judicial Magistrate Court, Chengam and the existing Additional District Munsif Court, Chengam shall be re-designated as District 
Munsif Court, Chengam.
