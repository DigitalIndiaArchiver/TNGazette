HOME DEPARTMENT
DECLARATION OF CERTAIN POLICE OFFICERS/PERSONNEL AS DESIGNATED OFFICERS
UNDER THE CONTROL OF THE ELECTION COMMISSION.
[G.O. Ms. No. 174, Home (Pol.-XIII), 7th March 2011.]
No. II(2)/HO/134(a)/2011.
Under Section 28-A of the Representation of the People Act, 1951 (Central Act XLIII of 1951), the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby declares all Police Officers from the level of Director-General of Police, Additional
Director-General of Police (Law and Order), Additional Director-General of Police and Commissioner of Police,
Chennai, Commissioner of Police, Chennai Sub-urban Commissionerate, Additional Directors General of Police,
Inspectors General of Police, Zonal Inspectors General of Police, North, South, Central and West, Commissioners
of Police, Salem, Madurai, Coimbatore, Tiruchirappalli and Tirunelveli, Range Deputy Inspectors General of Police,
Superintendents of Police of the Districts, Sub-Divisional Police Officers, Officers immediately subordinate to
them and up to the level of Head Constables and Police Constables (including Home Guards) and any other Police
Officer performing in any way the election related duties, as Designated Officers for the conduct of General
Elections to the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly, 2011. All the said Officers shall be deemed to be on deputation
to the Election Commission for the period commencing on and from the date of the notification calling for the said
elections and ending with the date of declaration of the results of the said elections and accordingly, the said
Officers shall, during that period, be subject to the control, superintendence and discipline of the Election
Commission.
K. GNANADESIKAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
