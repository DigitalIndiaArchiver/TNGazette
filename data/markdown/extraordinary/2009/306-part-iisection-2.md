Under section 28-A of the Representation of the People Act , 1951 (Central Act XLIII of 1951),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby designates the Director General of Police/Additional Director
General of Police (Law and Order)/ Inspector General of Police (North/South Zone)/ Deputy
Inspector General of Police, Vellore Range and Deputy Inspector General of Police, Tirunelveli
Range/Superintendent of Police, Tiruvannamalai District/Superintendent of Police, Thoothukudi
District and all Police Officials subordinate to them and up to the level of Police Constables and
any other Police Officers/Officials performing in any way election related duties for the conduct
of Bye-Elections to be held on the 19th December, 2009 to the Legislative Assembly of this State
from the pre-delimited 51. Vandavasi (SC) and 225. Tiruchendur Assembly Constituencies as
Designated Officers. All the said Officers shall be deemed to be on deputation to the Election
Commission for the period commencing on and from the date of the Notification calling for the
said Election and ending with the date of declaration of the result of the said Election and
accordingly, the said officers shall, during that period, be subject to the control, superintendence
and discipline of the Election Commission.
    D. JOTHI JAGARAJAN,
Secretary to Government (In-charge).
