No. SRO B-21/2018.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following rules:- 
2. The rules hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on and from the 11th September 2008.
RULES
The General and the Special Rules applicable to the holders of the permanent post of Private Secretary in Class XII, 
category 4 of the Tamil Nadu General Service shall apply to the holders of the temporary post of Personal Secretary to the 
Honourable President in the State Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission sanctioned from time to time, subject to the 
modiﬁ cations speciﬁ ed in the following rules:-
2. Constitution.- The post shall constitute a separate category in a distinct class of the said service.
3. Appointment.- Appointment to the post shall be made as follows:-
 
(I) by promotion from among the holders of the posts of Appeal Examiner and Court Ofﬁ cer in the State Consumer 
Disputes Redressal Commission; or
 
(II) by recruitment by transfer from among holders of the post of Personal Assistant in the Tamil Nadu Judicial 
Ministerial Service, if no suitable person is available by the above method.
4. Appointing Authority.- The Appointing Authority for the post shall be the Registrar, State Consumer Disputes Redressal 
Commission.
5. Qualiﬁ cations.- No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post, unless he possesses a degree in any University 
recognized by the University Grants Commission.
6. Probation.- Every person appointed to the post by recruitment by transfer shall, from the date on which he joins duty, 
be on probation for a total period of one year on duty within a continuous period of two years.
7. 
Pay.- The holder of the post shall be paid a monthly pay calculated in the Pay Band PB3 of Rs.15600 - 39100 
with Grade Pay of Rs.5700/-.

No. SRO B-23/2018.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following rules:- 
2. The rules hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on and from the 14th May 2007.
 RULES
The General and the Special Rules applicable to the holders of the permanent post of Personal Assistant in Category 
12 of the Tamil Nadu Ministerial Service shall apply to the holders of the temporary post of personal Assistant (Court Work), 
sanctioned from time to time, for the Ofﬁ ce of the State Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission, Chennai, subject to the 
modiﬁ cations speciﬁ ed in the following rules:-
2. Constitution.- The post shall constitute a temporary addition to the said category in the said service.
3. Appointment.- Appointment to the post shall be made by Promotion from among the holders of the post of Steno-
Typist Grade - III working in the ofﬁ ce of the State Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission or in Circuit Bench, Madurai 
or in the Ofﬁ ce of the District Fora which shall be made with reference to the combined seniority list.
4. Appointing Authority.- The Appointing Authority for the post shall be the Registrar, State Consumer Disputes Redressal 
Commission.
5. Qualiﬁ cations.- No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post of Personal Assistant (Court Work) unless he 
possesses the following qualiﬁ cations, namely:-
 
(i) Must possesses B.A. / B.Sc., / B. Com., / B.A. (Honors) / B.Com., (Honors), degree of any University recognized 
by the University Grants Commissions.
 
(ii) Must served as Steno Typist Grade - III for a period of not less than ﬁ ve years.
 
(iii). Must have passed the Government Technical Examinations in Typewriting and Shorthand in Tamil and English 
both by Higher Grade.
6. Pay.- The holder of the post shall be paid a monthly pay calculated in the Pay Band PB2 of Rs.9300 - 34800 with 
Grade Pay of Rs.4600/-.
7. Saving Clause.- Nothing contained in these rules shall adversely affect any persons holding the post, on the date 
of publication of these rules in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.

[G.O. Ms. No. 392, Health and Family Welfare (AA1), 8th November 2017, Aippasi 22, 
Hevilambi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu 2048.] 
No. SRO B-27/2018.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special rules for the Tamil Nadu Medical Services 
(Section 21, Volume II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1969)
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 19th September 2015.
AMENDMENT
In the said Special Rules, in part II, under the heading 'Branch I - Medical',
1. 
in rule 3, in sub-rule (a), under Class V, in category-5, for the entries in second column against the entry "Assistant 
Professor in Pharmaceutical Chemistry, Assistant Professor in Pharmaceutics, Assistant Professor in Pharmacognosy and 
Assistant Professor in Pharmacology in Government  Medical Colleges" in ﬁ rst column, the following entries shall be substituted, 
namely:-
 
(1) By transfer from the holders of the post of Lecturer in the respective speciality in Government Medical Colleges; or 
 
(2) By recruitment by transfer from any Category int he Tamil Nadu Medical Subordinate Service; or
 
(3) By direct recruitment, if no qualiﬁ ed candidate is available by the above two methods.
2. 
in the Annexure referred to in Rule 8(c),
Under the heading Class-V, category-V, in column 2 the heading "(1) By Promotion" shall be substituted as "(1) By 
transfer and by recruitment by transfer,".
