under Indian Veterinary Council Act.
[G.O. Ms. No. 120, Animal Husbandry, Dairying and
Fisheries (AH3), 15th September 2010,
Avani 30, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No.II(2)/AHDF/632/2010.—In exercise of powers conferred
by sub-sections (1) and (2) of Section 32 of the Indian Veterinary
Council Act, 1984 (Central Act 52 of 1984), the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby establishes the Tamil Nadu State Veterinary
Council consisting of the following members, namely:—
(A) Four members elected from among themselves by
Veterinary Practitioners registered in the State
Veterinary Register:
(1) Dr. R. Jeyaprakash
(2) Dr. M. Om Murugan.
(3) Dr. A. Rajendran.
(4)
Dr. D. Ramamurthy
(B) The heads of Veterinary Institutions:
(1) Dean, Veterinary College and Research Institute,
Namakkal – ex-officio.
(2) Dean, Madras Veterinary College, Chennai-7.—
ex-officio.
(C) Three members nominated by the State Government:
(1) Dr. K. Panneerselvam, Thiruvannamalai District
(2) Dr. K. Murugesan, Virudhunagar District
(3) Dr. C. Elango, Thiruppur District
(D) Director of  Veterinary Services of the State (by
whatever name called) ex-officio:
The Commissioner of Animal Husbandry and Veterinary
Services-ex-officio.
(E) One member nominated by the State Veterinary
Association:
Dr. B. Saravanan, Thanjavur District
(F) Registrar of the State Veterinary Council, ex-officio:
The Registrar, Tamil Nadu State Veterinary Council,
Chennai-35-ex-officio.
M.P. NIRMALA,
Secretary to Government.
COMMERCIAL TAXES AND REGISTRATION
DEPARTMENT.
Exemption in respect of the tax payable by any dealer on
the sale of furnace oil to industrial consumers
registered for certain period under Tamil Nadu Value
Added Tax Act.
[G.O. Ms. No. 169, Commercial Taxes and
Registration (B2), 5th October 2010,
Purattasi 19, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No.II(2)/CTR/633/2010.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 30 of the Tamil Nadu
Value Added Tax Act, 2006 (Tamil Nadu Act 32 of 2006), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes an exemption in
respect of the tax payable under the said Act by any dealer
on the sale of furnace oil to industrial consumers registered
under the said Act, for use in generation of electrical energy
through captive generators, subject to production of a
certificate every month, as appended to this Notification.

upto and inclusive of the 31st March 2011.
