Vesting of Properties and Scheme of Administration
relating to “Kandiah Rajammal Memorial Charitable
Endowment Fund” tenable at Government Arts College
for Women, Ramanathapuram under the Charitable
Endowments Act.
[G.O. Ms. No. 18, Higher Education (D2), 15th February
2012, Maasi 3, Thiruvalluvar Aandu 2043.]
No. II(2)/HE/164/2012.—In the matter of the Charitable
Endowments Act, 1890 (Central Act VI of 1890) and in the
matter of “Kandiah Rajammal Memorial Charitable Endowment
Fund” (hereafter referred to as “the said Endowment”) tenable
in the Government Arts College for Women, Ramanathapuram.
I. VESTING OF PROPERTIES.
Whereas, an application has been made to the Government
of Tamil Nadu under clause (b) of sub-section (1) of
Section 6 of the Charitable Endowments Act, 1890 (Central
Act VI of 1890) by Mrs. Lalitha Natarajan, residing at No. C6,
V.J. Flats, 52A Barakha Road, Secretariat Colony, Kellys,
Chennai-600 010, the donor of the said Endowment for
vesting the property specified in the Schedule below, in the
Treasurer of Charitable Endowments, Chennai, appointed
under Section 3 of the said Act for the State of Tamil Nadu,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu, in exercise of the powers
conferred by Section 4 of the said Act, hereby orders that
the said property be vested in the Treasurer aforesaid, and
be held by him and his successors in office subject to the
provisions of the said Act and the Rules which from time
to time, may be or shall be made or deemed to have been
made thereunder and subject to the conditions setforth in
the Scheme settled under Section 5 of the said Act, for the
administration of the said Endowment.
THE SCHEDULE.
THE PROPERTY.
Rs.25,000/- (Rupees Twenty five thousand only)
II. SCHEME FOR ADMINISTRATION.
Whereas, an application has been made to the Government
of Tamil Nadu under clause (b) of sub-section (1) of Section
6 of the Charitable Endowments Act, 1890 (Central Act VI
of 1890) by Mrs. Lalitha Natarajan, residing at No. C6, V.J.
Flats, 52A, Barakha Road, Secretariat Colony, Kellys, Chennai-
600 010, the donor of the said Endowment specified above,
for settling a scheme for administration of the said Endowment,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu, in exercise of the powers
conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Charitable
Endowments Act, 1890 (Central Act VI of 1890), hereby
settles the Scheme setforth in the Schedule below for the
administration of the said Endowment and appoints the
Principal, Government Arts College for Women,
Ramanathapuram, to administer the property. The Scheme
shall come into operation from the date of publication of this
Notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
