the Governor on the 9th October 2020 and is hereby published for general

ACT No. 25 OF 2020.

3. (1) On and from the appointed date, the Madras School of Economics 
shall be a body corporate by the same name.
Incorporation of 
Institute.

(a) to advance and disseminate learning and knowledge of
