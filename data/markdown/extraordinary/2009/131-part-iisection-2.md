Notifications under Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act.
REMOVAL OF VICE-CHAIRMAN OF DEVARSOLA TOWN PANCHAYAT, THE NILGIRIS DISTRICT
[G.O. (D) No. 215, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (TP.II), 28th May 2009.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/261(a-1)/2009.
Whereas a written notice of intention to make a motion expressing want of confidence in Thiru R. Moorthy, Vice-
Chairman of Devarsola Town Panchayat signed by sixteen Councillors of Devarsola Town Panchayat of The Nllgiris
District out of the sanctioned strength of the eighteen councillors of the said council together with a copy of the motion
proposed to be made have been delivered to the Assistant Director of Town Panchayats. Udhagamandalam in person
by three Councillors under the provisions of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920);
And, Whereas the said motion expressing want of confidence in Thiru R. Moorthy, has been carried out with the
support of sixteen Councillors out of the sanctioned strength of the eighteen Councillors in the meeting convened by
the Assistant Director of Town Panchayats, Udhagamandalam on the 11th September 2008 after following the procedure
specified in section 40-A of the said Act;
Now, therefore in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (12) of section 40-A of the Tamil Nadu District
Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby removes Thiru R. Moorthy,
Vice-Chairman, Devarsola Town Panchayat, The Nilgiris District from the office of Vice-Chairman of the said Town
Panchayat.

REMOVAL OF VICE-CHAIRMAN OF SOLUR TOWN PANCHAYAT, THE NILGIRIS DISTRICT
[G.O. (D) No. 216, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (TP.II), 28th May 2009.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/261(a-2)/2009.
Whereas a written notice of intention to make a motion expressing want of confidence in Tmt. D. Lakshmi,
Vice-Chairman of Solur Town Panchayat signed by fourteen councillors of Solur Town Panchayat of The Niligiris District
out of the sanctioned strength of the fifteen councillors of the said council together with a copy of the motion proposed
to be made have been delivered to the Assistant Director of Town Panchayats Udhagamandalam in person by three
councillors under the provisions of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920);
And, Whereas the said motion expressing want of confidence in Tmt. D. Lakshmi has been carried out with the
support of fourteen councillors out of the sanctioned strength of the fifteen councillors in the meeting convened by the
Assistant Director of Town Panchayats, Udhagamandalam on the 16th December, 2008 after following the procedure
specified in section 40-A of the said Act;
Now, therefore in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (12) of section 40-A of the Tamil Nadu District
Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby removes Tmt. D. Lakshmi,
Vice-Chairman, Solur Town Panchayat, The Nilgiris District from the office of Vice-Chairman of the said Town Panchayat.
NIRANJAN MARDI,
