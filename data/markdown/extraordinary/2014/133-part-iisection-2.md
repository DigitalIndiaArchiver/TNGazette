APPOINTMENT OF COMMISSION OF INQUIRY TO INQUIRE THE  INCIDENT OF COLLAPSE OF THE
MULTI-STOREYED BUILDING AT MOULIVAKKAM, SRIPERUMBUDUR TALUK, KANCHEEPURAM DISTRICT
UNDER COMMISSIONS OF INQUIRY ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 557, Public (Law  & Order - F) 3rd July 2014, Aani 19, Jaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2045.]
No. II(2)/PULO/405(b)/2014.
WHEREAS the Government of Tamil Nadu are of the opinion that it is  necessary to appoint a Commission of
Inquiry headed by a Retired Judge of the  High Court of Madras for the purpose of making an Inquiry into a definite
matter of public importance, herein after specified;
NOW, THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section  (1) of Section 3 of the Commissions
of Inquiry Act, 1952 (Central Act LX of 1952), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints a Commission of Inquiry
consisting of a single member, namely Justice Thiru R. Regupathi, Retired Judge of the High Court of Madras.
3. The terms of references of the above Commission of Inquiry shall be as follows:-
(a) to inquire into all aspects, causes and circumstances leading to the  collapse of the Multi-storeyed Building
at Moulivakkam,  Sriperumbudur Taluk, Kancheepuram District on the evening of  28-6-2014 resulting in death of
about 55 persons and injuries to  several others;
(b) to fix responsibility on the persons whose negligence caused the  mishap that led to the death of a large
number of construction  workers and injuries to several others;
and
(c) to recommend remedial measures to be taken to avoid recurrence of  such mishaps in future.
4. The Commission will complete its inquiry and submit its report (both in  English and Tamil) to the State
Government within a period of two months from  the date of publication of this Notification in the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.

5. AND WHEREAS, the Government of Tamil Nadu are of the opinion  having regard to the nature of the inquiry
to be made by the Commission  of Inquiry and other circumstances of the case, that all the provisions of
sub-section (2), (3), (4) and (5) of Section 5 of the Commissions of Inquiry  Act, 1952 (Central Act LX of 1952) shall
be made applicable to the Commission of Inquiry;
6. NOW, THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred by  sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Commissions
of Inquiry Act, 1952 (Central Act LX of 1952), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that all the  provisions of
the said sub-section (2), (3), (4) and (5) of the said Section 5 of the said Act shall apply to the Commission of Inquiry.
JATINDRA NATH SWAIN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
