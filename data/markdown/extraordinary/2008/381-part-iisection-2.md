HOME DEPARTMENT

APPOINTMENT OF PRESIDING JUDGE OF THE DESIGNATED COURT UNDER THE TERRORIST AND DISRUPTIVE
ACTIVITIES (PREVENTION) ACT.
Amendment to Notification.
[G.O. Ms. No. 1664, Home (Courts II), 23rd December 2008.]
No. II(2)/HO/608(f)/2008.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 9 of the Terrorist and Disruptive Activities (Prevention)
Act, 1987 (Central Act 28 of 1987), the Governor of Tamil Nadu with the concurrence of the Chief Justice, High Court,
Madras, makes the following amendment to the Home Department Notification No. II(2)/HO/3929(b-2)/91 published at
page 1 of Part II Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, Extraordinary, dated the 11th July 1991, as subsequently
amended:—
AMENDMENT
In the said Notification, for the expression "Thiru A. Ahamadullah, I Additional City Civil and Sessions Judge, Chennai.",
the following expression shall be substituted, namely:—
"Thiru A. Sampath, I Additional City Civil and Sessions Judge, Chennai."
S. MALATHI,
 Principal Secretary to Government.
