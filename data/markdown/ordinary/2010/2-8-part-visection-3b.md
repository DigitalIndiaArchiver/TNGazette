Election of Two Members to the syndicate by the Teachers of affiliated Colleges of this University (Other
than Principals) from among themselves who are Members of the Academic Council.
(No. TU/R/Electn. Notifn.-Publication/2009.)
No.VI-3(b)/3/2010.
1.
Under Chapter IV, Section 23, sub-section (5) of the Thiruvalluvar University Act, 2002 (Tamil Nadu Act 32 of
2002), TWO MEMBERS to the SYNDICATE by the teachers of affiliated colleges of this University (other than Principals)
from among themselves who are members of the Academic Council are to be elected in accordance with the system
of proportional representation by means of the single transferable vote. The Election schedule is given below.
2.
The persons getting elected will hold office as a member of the Syndicate for a period of THREE YEARS from
the date of declaration of the results.
3.
Nomination Papers shall be enclosed in an envelope addressed to the Registrar and superscribed “NOMINATION
TO THE SYNDICATE BY THE TEACHERS WHO ARE MEMBERS OF THE ACADEMIC COUNCIL” as indicated below
and sent by Registered Post with Ack. Due so as to be received by the Registrar or delivered to the Registrar during
Office Hours either in Person or by messenger within the date and hour fixed.
4.
Nomination Papers that are not enclosed in an envelope superscribed and sent by Registered Post or not delivered
in person or by messenger as required above shall be declared invalid. An elector shall be eligible to subscribe either
as proposer or as seconder in Nomination Papers as many times as there are vacancies only but not more.
