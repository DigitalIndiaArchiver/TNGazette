Chennai District and appointment of official Liquidator.
(Rc. No. 52569/ICF/2007-2.)
No. VI(1)/229/2011.

Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act, 1983 (Act 30 of 1983). The Chennai Transport Drivers Industrial Co-operative
Society Limited No. IND. 3/MDS Chennai District have been ordered to be wound up with immediate effect by the

Under Section 138(1) of the same Act, Assistant Director (Industrial Co-operatives)/Official Liquidator, TAICO Bank,
Chennai-8 is appointed as the Official Liquidator of the said Society for the purpose of liquidation.

the Special Commissioner of Town and Country  Planning in proceedings Roc. No. 9543/2002/DP3, dated
