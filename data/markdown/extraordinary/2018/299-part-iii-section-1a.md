(b) for Explanation (1), the following Explanation shall be substituted, namely:- 
“(1) Upgrading of a road from lower surface condition to higher surface condition by providing sub-base and / or 
Base courses with or without Black Topping will be deemed as an original work. For example,  re-laying of Water Bound 
Macadam (WBM) layers(s) or Wet Mix Macadam (WMM) with or without Black Topping (BT) and re-laying or renewal of 
bituminous layer as a whole length or full length will be deemed as original work and not maintenance work.”;
	
(c) for Explanation (3), the following Explanation shall be substituted, namely:-
	
“(3) Repair or re-laying work undertaken in parts of a road will be deemed as maintenance work.”;
	
(d) in Explanations (4) and (5), for the expression “Assistant Engineers”, the expression “Assistant Engineers / 
Block Engineers” shall be substituted;
