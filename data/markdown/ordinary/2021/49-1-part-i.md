Rඍඉඌ:
1.  
From Thiru S.R.Gandhi, IRPFS, Senior Divisional 
Security Commissioner, Railway Protection Force, 
Secunderabad Division, South Central Railway, 
letter dated 22-07-2021. 
2.  
Government Letter No.970/2021-2, Public (Special-A) 
Department, Dated 12-10-2021. 
3. 
From the Director/RPF, Railway Board, Ministry of 
Railways, Government of India, Letter No. 2018/
Sec(E)/DP-1/26, Dated 09-11-2021. 
Order: No. 989, Public (Special-A) 26th November 2021.
Thiru S.R.Gandhi, IRPFS, Senior Divisional Security 
Commissioner, Railway Protection Force, Secunderabad 
Division, South Central Railway has requested to consider 
his request for inter cadre deputation with Government of 
Tamil Nadu for any suitable post for his qualiﬁ cation. 
2. The Director General/Railway Protection Force, 
Railway Board, Ministry of Railways, New Delhi was requested 
to obtain the approval of the cadre controlling authority 
(i.e. Railway Board, Ministry of Railways, Government of 
India) and Vigilance Clearance in respect of Thiru S.R.Gandhi, 
IRPFS, Senior Divisional Security Commissioner, Railway 
Protection Force, Secunderabad Division, South Central 
Railway and Communicate the same to this State Government 
to take further action on the request of Thiru S.R. Gandhi, 
IRPFS for deputation with Government of Tamil Nadu for a 
period of three years. 
3. The Director/Railway Protection Force, Railway Board, 
Ministry of Railways, Government of India, New Delhi has 
issued the Vigilance Clearance and Cadre Clearance Certiﬁ cate 
in respect of Thiru S.R.Gandhi, IRPFS, Senior Divisional 
Security 
Commissioner, 
Railway 
Protection 
Force, 
Secunderabad Division, South Central Railway, Government 
of India for deputation with  Government of Tamil Nadu. 
4. The Government of Tamil Nadu have agreed for 
deputation of Thiru S.R.Gandhi, IRPFS with this State 
Government for a period of three years with pay protection 
from the date of his reporting for duty in this Government.
5. Thiru S.R. Gandhi, IRPFS, Senior Divisional Security 
Commissioner, Railway Protection Force, Secunderabad 
Division, South Central Railway, Government of India 
on deputation to Government of Tamil Nadu is posted 
to act as the Director, Art and Culture, Chennai vice 
Thiru G. Prakash, IAS, on leave.
6. During the period of his deputation, he is allowed to draw 
his pay in his time scale of pay, allowances and entitlements as 
applicable to him in his parent cadre and other perquisites as 
eligible to the post to which he is posted.
(By Order of the Governor)
 
V. IRAI ANBU,
 
Chief Secretary.
