 No. II(1)/AG/31/2014.—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 4 of the Seeds Act, 1966
(Central Act 54 of 1966), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby declares the DNA Finger Print Laboratory, Coimbatore as
“State DNA Finger Print Laboratory” for the analysis of seed samples, seedlings and declaration of results.
NOTIFICATION-II
[G.O.Ms. No. 81, Agriculture (AP2), 27th June 2014, ÝQ 13,  üò, F¼õœÀõ˜ Ý‡´-2045.]
No. II(1)/AG/32/2014.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 12 of the Seeds Act, 1966 (Central Act 54 of 1966),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the Assistant Director of Seed Certification (Seed Analyst), Office of the Directorate
of seed Certification and Organic Certification, Coimbatore to be the “Seed Analyst for State DNA Finger Print laboratory and
directs that he shall exercise jurisdiction for the whole of the State of Tamil Nadu.
