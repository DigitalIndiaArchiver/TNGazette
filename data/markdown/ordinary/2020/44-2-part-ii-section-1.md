No. II(1)/HOPE/19/2020.—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 18-B of the Tamil Nadu 
Prohibition Act, 1937 (Tamil Nadu Act X of 1937), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to 
the Prohibition and Excise Department's Notiﬁ cation No.II(1)/PE/239(d)/81, published at page 2 of Part II–Section 1 of the 
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated 1st May 1981, as subsequently amended, namely:-
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖගඛ.
In the said Notiﬁ cation, in the TABLE, in the Annexure.— 
1. 
Under the heading "MEDIUM BRANDS", under the sub-heading "Thiruvalargal  Mohan Breweries and Distilleries 
Limited”, after the entry "TI's Master XXX Rum" the entry "Kadamark Original Brandy" shall be added.
2. 
Under the heading "PREMIUM BRANDS", under the sub-heading "Thiruvalargal  Mohan Breweries and 
Distilleries Limited”, after the entry "Blender's Classic Superior French Grape Brandy", the following entries shall be 
added, namely:—
 
“Old Monk Gold Reserve Super Premium Rum”;
 
“Old Monk Premium XXX Rum”; and
 
“Old Monk Lemon Original Citrus Rum.".
