HIGHER EDUCATION DEPARTMENT
APPOINTMENT OF CERTAIN PERSON AS VICE-CHANCELLOR OF TAMIL NADU
OPEN UNIVERSITY FOR CERTAIN PERIOD UNDER TAMIL NADU OPEN UNIVERSITY ACT.
[G.O. 1D No. 11, Higher Education (K1), 27th January 2009,
Thai 14, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. II(2)/HE/32(d)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 10(1) and (3) of the Tamil Nadu
Open University Act, 2002, His Excellency the Governor-Chancellor hereby appoints
DR. M.D.V. Kalyani Annie, Principal, Baktavatchalam Memorial College for Women,
Chennai-600 080, as Vice-Chancellor, Tamil Nadu Open University, Chennai, for a period of
three years from the date of her assuming office.
M. KUTRALINGAM,
Principal Secretary to Government (In-charge).
