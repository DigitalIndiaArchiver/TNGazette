shall be the same, as determined for the purpose of revenue
administration.

Alteration of the limitation of the Tiruppur Registration

[G.O. Ms. No. 195, Commercial Taxes and

No. II(2)/CTR/719/2010.—Under sub-section (1) of Section 5
of the Registration Act, 1908 (Central Act XVI of 1908), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby, with effect on and from the

from the said Registration district and the said Registration

shall be the same, as determined for the purpose of revenue
administration.

HOME DEPARTMENT
Fixation of Court Fee payable in respect of declaration of
title over the immovable properties belonging to
Temples and Charitable Institutions under Tamil Nadu
Court Fees and Suits Valuation Act.
[G.O. Ms. No. 363, Home (Courts-VIA), 9th April 2010.]
No. II(2)/HO/720/2010—In exercise of the powers conferred
by Section 73 of the Tamil Nadu Court-Fees and Suits
Valuation Act, 1955 (Tamil Nadu Act XIV of 1955), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby reduces to a maximum of
rupees one hundred, the fee payable under the said Act in
respect of the Suits filed by religious institutions for restoration
of immovable properties and for declaration of title over the
immovable properties of temples under the control of the
Hindu Religious and Charitable Endowments Department.
