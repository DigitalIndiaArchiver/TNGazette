In exercise of the powers conferred under Sections 13(1) & (3) of the Alagappa University Act, 1985, His
Excellency the Governor-Chancellor hereby appoints Dr. S. Sudalaimuthu, Professor and Head, Department of
Corporate Secretaryship and Dean, Faculty of Management, Alagappa University, Karaikudi as the Vice-Chancellor
