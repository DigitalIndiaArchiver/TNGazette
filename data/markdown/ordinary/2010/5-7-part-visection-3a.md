GENERAL NOTIFICATION
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD
No.1, Jaffar Syrang Street, Vallal Seethakathi Nagar, Chennai-600 001.
Taking over of the Management of Mohideen Andavar Pallivasal, Vilandakandam Village,
Kumbakonam Taluk, Tanjore District under the direct control of Tamil Nadu Wakf Borad.
(Rc.No.16960/B11/99/TNJ)
No.VI-3(a)/16/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions the Wakf Act, 1995, and as per the orders dated
11th December 2008 of the Honourable High Court, Madras in W.P.No. 35622/2007. The Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in
its proceedings in item No. 129/09 in R.C.No.16960/B11/99/TNJ, dated 3rd November 2009 has ordered to take over
the Management of the Wakf Viz, Mohideen Andavar Pallivasal, Vilandakandam, Kumbakonam Taluk, Tanjore District
under the direct control of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of 6 months from the date of the Board's order
i.e., 3rd November 2009 and to appoint the Superintendent of Wakfs, Tanjore as its Executive Officer.
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
Chief Executive Officer,
Chennai-600 001,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
16th December 2009.
