FINANCE DEPARTMENT
EXEMPTING THE WASTE TO ENERGY PROJECT IN TIRUNELVELI MUNICIPAL CORPORATION  UNDER
PUBLIC PRIVATE PARTNERSHIP MODE FOR THE TAMIL NADU  INFRASTRUCTURE DEVELOPMENT ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 228, Finance (Infrastructure Cell), 17th September 2014, Purattasi 1,
Jaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2045.]
No. II(2)/FIN/568(a)/2014.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 34 of the Tamil Nadu Infrastructure Development Act, 2012
(Tamil Nadu Act 22 of 2012), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby exempts the Waste to Energy Project in
Tirunelveli Municipal Corporation under Public Private Partnership mode from the provisions of the said Act,
since the Government have accorded the Administrative Sanction for the said Project prior to the enactment
of the said Act.
K. SHANMUGAM,
Principal Secretary to Government.
