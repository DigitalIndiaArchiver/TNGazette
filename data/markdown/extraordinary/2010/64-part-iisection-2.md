ACQUISITION OF LANDS
[G.O. Ms. No. 88, Highways and Minor Ports (HW2),
5th March 2010, Maasi 21, Thiruvalluvar Aandu 2041.]
No. II(2)/HWMP/128(d)/2010.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied
that the lands specified in the Schedule below,  are required
for the purpose of the Construction of Road Over Bridge in
lieu of existing Railway Level Crossing No.30 in between
Tambaram and Irumbuliyur Railway Station and it having
already been decided that the entire amount of
compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid out
of the funds controlled and managed by the Chief Engineer
(Projects), Highways Department, Chennai -15 and after
having considered the cause shown by the Owner or other
person having interest in the said lands, as the case may
be, do hereby publish the following Notice is issued under
sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways
Act,2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of Tamil Nadu
Highways Act,2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002), the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands specified in the schedule
below and  measuring 7180.2 Sq.Metres to the same, a little
more or less are needed for the purpose of the construction of
Road Over Bridge in lieu of existing Railway Level Crossing
No.30 between Tambaram and Irumbuliyur  Railway Station.
The plan of the lands is kept in the office of the
Special Tahsildar(Land Acquisition), Inner Ring Road
Scheme, Alandur Municipality Campus, Alandur, Chennai -
16 and may be inspected at any time during office hours.
