No. SRO A-30/2017.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 52 of the Tamil Nadu Agricultural Produce
Marketing (Regulation) Act, 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 1989), the Governor  of Tami Nadu, hereby makes the following
amendments to the Tamil Nadu Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) Rules, 1991.
AMENDMENTS.
In the said Rules, -
(1)
for rule 55, the following rule shall be substituted, namely:-
“55. Passing of a bill - The executive authority concerned shall pass any bill for payment without the
previous sanction of the Board or the market committee, as the case may be, subject to the provisions of fund in the
Budget, -
(a)
for the payment of the salary and other allowances including travelling allowance of such establishment;
(b)
for repayment to the Government of any money under sub-section (3) of Section 22 of the Act or
sub-section (6) of Section 37 of the Act, as the case may be;
(c)
for meeting other expenditures by the executive authority of the market committee upto Rs. 5,00,000/-
(Rupees five thousand);
(d)
for meeting other expenditures by the executive authority of the Board upto Rs. l0,000/- (Rupees ten
thousand); and
(e)
for payment of all rent, rate and taxes, Electricity charges, Telephone charges, Legal fees, loans and
advances etc.
Explanation- For the purpose of this rule, the following Table shall be the scale of expenditure to be incurred
by the executive authority of the market committee or the Board, as the case may be, with the approval of the authorities

AMENDMENT.
In the said Rules, in rule 3, in sub-rule (b), for the expression commencing with the words “treasury receipts” and
ending with the words “four thousand”, the following expression shall be substituted, namely:-
“treasury receipts in proof of payment of an application fee of rupees two thousand, a fee of rupees eight
lakhs for the grant of privilege of manufacturing spirit and a licence fee of rupees twenty thousand”.
