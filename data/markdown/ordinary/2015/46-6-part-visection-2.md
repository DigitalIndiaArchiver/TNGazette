WITHDRAWAL NOTIFICATION

the Collector of Cuddalore District as per Tamil Nadu Panchayat Act, 1994 (T.N. Act XXI of 1994) direct that from and after
the date of publication of this notification in the District Gazette and Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, the land vested to the
undermentioned village and Panchayat at the undermentioned Taluk of Cuddalore District on the Schedule and measuring the
extent noted below be the same a little more or less shall be excluded from the operation of the said Act.

Cuddalore District, Vridhachalam Taluk, Gangaikondan Panchayat, Gangaikondan Village.
