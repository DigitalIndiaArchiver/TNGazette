EXEMPTION OF THE WORLD BANK CHENNAI OFFICE, IN THE RESPECT OF THE ELECTRICITY TAX
PAYABLE UNDER THE TAMIL NADU TAX ON CONSUMPTION OF SALE OF ELECTRICITY ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 9, Energy (A2), 19th February 2014,  ñ£C 7, Müò, F¼õœÀõ˜ Ý‡´-2045.]
No. II(2)/EGY/207(b)/2014.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 14 of the Tamil Nadu Tax on Consumption
or Sale of Electricity Act, 2003 (Tamil Nadu Act 12 of 2003), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby exempts the
World Bank, Chennai Office, in respect of the electricity tax payable under the said Act on their own consumption of
electricity by Capitive Generating Plant.
RAJESH LAKHONI,
Secretary to Government.
