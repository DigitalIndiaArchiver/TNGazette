GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI
Extension of Direct Management of Chanda Hussain Thaikkal Pudupattinam, Tanjore District.
(Rc. No.16234/01/B7/TNJ)
No. VI-3(a)/31/2017.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 (5) of the Wakf Act, 1995 as amended
by Act 27 / 2013, the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board ratify the period of direct management of Chanda Hussain Thaikkal
Waqf, Pudupattinam, Tanjore District from 22.10.2014 to 26-02-2017 and also extend the period of direct management
of the above Wakf for the period of one year from 27-02-2017.
Chennai-600 001,
K. RASHEETH ALI,
11th April 2017.
District Revenue Officer/
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
Extension of Direct Management of Bakarsha Oliyullah Makizambu Thaikka Pallivasal, Rajakiri, Papanasam Taluk,
Tanjore District.
(Rc. No.5708/15/B7/TNJ)
No. VI-3(a)/32/2017.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 (5) of the Wakf Act, 1995 as amended
by Act 27 / 2013, the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board extend the period of direct management of Bakarsha Oliyullah
Makizambu Thaikka Pallivasal, Rajakiri, Papanasam Taluk, Tanjore District for the period of one year from 18-02-2017.
Chennai-600 001,
K. RASHEETH ALI,
12th April 2017.
District Revenue Officer/
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
