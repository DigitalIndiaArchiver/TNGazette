APPOINTMENT OF CHAIRMAN OF THE TAMIL NADU ELECTRICITY REGULATORY 
COMMISSION UNDER THE ELECTRICITY ACT, 2003
 [G.O.Ms.No. 1, Energy (D1), 8th January 2025, மார்கழி 24,  குரோதி, திருவள்ளுவர் ஆண்டு-2055.]

In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (5) of Section 82 of the Electricity Act, 2003
(Central Act 36 of 2003), based on the recommendation of the Selection Committee constituted under 
section 85 of the said Act for the purpose referred to therein, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints 
Thiru.R.Manivannan  as Chairman  of the Tamil Nadu Electricity Regulatory Commission, Chennai.
