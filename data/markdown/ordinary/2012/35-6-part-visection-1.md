Notification regarding Christmas Holidays 2011 Sitting arrangements for Madurai Bench of
Madras High Court, Madurai—Nomination of Vacation Sitting Judges—Modification.
(R.O.C. No. 4091-A/2011/C3)
NOTIFICATION No. 253/2011
No.VI(1)/270/2012.
In Partial Modification of the High Court's Notification No. 252/2011, dated 22nd December 2011, it is hereby notified
that Hon'ble Tmt. Justice S. Vimala, will be the Vacation sitting Judge, for the Madurai Bench of Madras High Court,
Madurai, instead of Hon'ble Thiru Justice C.S. Karnan.
All other arrangements notified earlier in this connection remain unchanged.
High Court, Madras,
G. CHOCKALINGAM,
26th December 2011.

Proposal for Appointment of Notary for the Area Comprised in Thoothukkudi District.
(D. No. 6945/A1/2012.)
No. VI(1)/271/2012.
Whereas Thiru P. Albert Durai, B.Com.,B.L., Advocate, Bar Association, Tiruchendur, Tamil Nadu has applied to this
Court to appoint him as a 'Notary' for the area comprised in Tiruchendur Taluk in Thoothukkudi District.
Notice is herewith given that those have objections may file their objections before the Principal District Court,
Thoothukkudi within fourteen (14) days from the date of publication of this Notice.
Principal District Court,
Thoothukkudi,
J. PRABHUDASS,
13th August 2012.
Principal District Judge.
