No. VI(1)/A(a-1)/2023.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁed that the lands speciﬁed in the Schedule below to be acquired for 
industrial purposes, to wit to the Doubling of Broad Gauge Railway Line between Tiruvananthapuram and Kanniyakumari 
in Kottaram East Village, Agasteeswaram Taluk of Kanniyakumari District, and it having already been decided that the amount 
of compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid out of the funds controlled or managed by the Southern Railway. 
The following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes 
Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu 
Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands speciﬁed in the schedule below measuring an extent 
of 4056 Sq.mtrs., of Wet and Dry lands in S.No. 595/10B and etc., to the same, a little more or less needed for Industrial 
purpose wit, for the Doubling of Broad Gauge Railway Line between Tiruvananthapuram and Kanniyakumari in Kottaram 
East Village, Agasteeswaram Taluk in the District of Kanniyakumari.
The plan of the lands are kept in the office of the Collector, Kanniyakumari which may be inspected on any day during 
office hours.
