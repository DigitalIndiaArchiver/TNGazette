APPOINTMENT OF COMMISSION OF INQUIRY TO INQUIRE INTO CAUSES AND CIRCUMSTANCES LEADING

FOR HOLDING OF JALLIKATTU UNDER THE COMMISSIONS OF INQUIRY ACT.
Amendment to Notification
[G.O. Ms. No. 370, Public (Law and Order-F), 1st May 2017, Chithirai 18, Hevilambi,
Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2048.]
No. II(2)/PULO/317(c)/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of section 3 of the Commissions of Inquiry Act, 1952
(Central Act LX of 1952), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Public
(Law and Order-F) Department Notification No.ll(2)/PULO/76(f)/2017, published at pages 1-2 of
Part ll-Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 31st January 2017.
AMENDMENT
In the said Notification, in paragraph 4, for the expression “three  months”, the expression “six months”
shall be substituted.
    SHIV DAS MEENA,
    Principal Secretary to Government.
