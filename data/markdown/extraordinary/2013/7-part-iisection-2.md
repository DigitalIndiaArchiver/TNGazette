FINANCE DEPARTMENT
(Salaries)
NOTIFICATION OF TAMIL NADU CO-OPERATIVE MILK PRODUCERS FEDERATION LIMITED AND THE
SABARMATI ASHRAM GAUSHALA, BIDAJ IN GUJARAT AT MUTUALLY AGREED PRICES FROM TIME TO
TIME UNDER THE TAMIL NADU TRANSPARENCY IN TENDERS ACT, 1998.
[G.O. Ms. No. 3, Finance (Salaries), 7th January 2013.]
No. II(2)/FIN/52(c)/2013.
Under clause (f) of Section 16 of the Tamil Nadu Transparency in Tenders Act, 1998 (Tamil Nadu
Act 43 of 1998), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notifies that the procurement of quality Frozen Semen
Straws by the Animal Husbandry and Veterinary Services Department shall be made directly,- (a) from the
Tamil Nadu Co-operative Milk Producers Federation Limited and (b) from the Sabarmati Ashram Gaushala,
Bidaj in Gujarat at Mutually agreed prices from time to time.
K. SHANMUGAM,
    Principal Secretary to Government.
