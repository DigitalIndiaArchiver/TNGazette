GENERAL  NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI
Taking Over the Direct Management of Aminjikarai Mosque and Burial Ground Wakf, Aminjikarai, Chennai.
(Rc. No. 8522/86/B9/Chennai.)
No. VI-3(a)/67/2013.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under section 65 of the Wakf Act 1995, the Tamil  Nadu Wakf
Board in its resolution in Item.No.124/12, dated. 29-11-2012 in R.C.No.8522/86/B9/Chennai has ordered to take over the
management of Aminjikarai Mosque and Burial Ground Wakf, Aminjikarai, Chennai under the direct control of the Tamil Nadu
Wakf Board for a period of one year from the date of the Board's Order and to appoint the Wakf Inspector, Chennai (Central)
as its Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,
F. ABDUL RAZICK,
5th February 2013.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
