Exemption from Thiruvalargal Midas Golden Distilleries Private Limited, Kancheepuram District under the
operation of Tamil Nadu Indian-Made Foreign Spirits (Manufacture) Rules 1981.
[G.O. Ms. No. 38, Home, Prohibition and Excise (III), 24th June 2013.]
No.-SRO A-19/2013.—In exercise of the powers conferred by rule 35 of the Tamil Nadu Indian-made Foreign Spirits
(Manufacture) Rules, 1981, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby exempts Thiruvalargal Midas Golden Distilleries Private
Limited, Indian-made Foregn Spirits manufacturing unit, from the operation of the provisions of sub-rule (3-A) of rule 25 of
the said Rules, so as to enable the said Company to produce 13,54,166 cases of Indian-made Foreign Spirits per month
or 1,62,50,000 cases per annum from 1st July 2013.
NIRANJAN MARDI,
Principal Secretary to Government.
