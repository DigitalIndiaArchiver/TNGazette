HOME, PROHIBITION AND EXCISE DEPARTMENT
Amendment to the Tamil Nadu Rectified Spirit Rules, 2000.
[G.O. Ms. No. 51, Home, Prohibition and Excise (VI), 29th September 2010.]
No. SRO A-33/2010.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Sections 16, 18-A, 18-B, 20-A, 21, 23 and 54 of the
Tamil Nadu Prohibition Act, 1937 (Tamil Nadu Act X of 1937), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following
amendment to the Tamil Nadu Rectified Spirit Rules, 2000:—
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in rule 14, for the provisos, the following provisos shall be substituted, namely:—
‘‘Provided that the Commissioner shall issue import permit for the import of rectified spirit, absolute alcohol, neutral
spirit or silent spirit after obtaining prior approval of the Government :
Provided further that in respect of grape spirit, malt spirit, tapioca spirit, concentrate or other special spirit, the
Commissioner may issue import permit without the prior concurrence of the Government.’’
K. GNANADESIKAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
