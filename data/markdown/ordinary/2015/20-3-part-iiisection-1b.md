No. SRO B-40/2015.
In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby order to rescind the Tamil Nadu Harijan Welfare Educational Service (Section 16-A) of Service Rules in Volume-II of
Madras Services Manual, 1969.
2. The order shall come into force with effect from 1st July 1978.
ORDER
The Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Harijan Welfare Educational Service contained in Section 16-A of Volume-II of Madras
Services Manual, 1969 is hereby rescinded.
P. SIVASANKARAN,
Principal Secretary to Government (F.A.C.).

Amendment to the Adhoc Rules relating to the Post of Assistant Medical Officer (Yoga and Naturopathy) and
Assistant Lecturer (Yoga and Naturopathy) in Government College of Yoga and Naturopathy in Indian
Medicine and Homeopathy Department
[G.O. Ms. No. 129, Health and Family Welfare (IM2-1), 27th April 2015,
CˆF¬ó 14, ñ¡ñî, F¼õœÀõ˜ Ý‡´-2046.]
No. SRO B-41/2015.
In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the rules published with the Health and Family Welfare Department,
Notification No. SRO No. B-21/2015 at Pages 30-31 of Part III—Section 1(b) of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette,
dated the 4th March, 2015.
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on and from the 23rd February 2004.
AMENDMENT
In the said Ad-hoc Rules, for the expression "Assistant Medical Officer-cum-Assistant Professor" occuring in three places, the
expression "Assistant Medical Officer / Assistant Lecturer" shall be substituted.
J. RADHAKRISHNAN,
Secretary to Government.

No. SRO B-42/2015.
In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu General Subordinate Service (Section 13
in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Service Manual, 1970).
2. The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 12th February 1985.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules,-
"(i)  in PART I, "Class XXVIII" and the entries thereunder shall be omitted;
 (ii) in PART II, "Class XXVIII" and the rules made thereunder shall be omitted."
N.S. PALANIAPPAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
