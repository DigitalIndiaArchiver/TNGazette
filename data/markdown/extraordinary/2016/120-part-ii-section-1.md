NOTIFICATIONS  BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY SECRETARIAT.
LEGISLATIVE  ASSEMBLY SECRETARIAT
SUMMONING OF TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY
[S.O. Ms. No. 61, Legislative Assembly Secretariat, 23rd May 2016,
 Vaikasi 10, Thunmugi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2047.]
No. II(1)/LAS/7(q)/2016.
“WHEREAS the Fifteenth Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu has been duly constituted in
accordance with the Constitution of India and has to be summoned to meet for its first session under the provisions
of the Constitution.
Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred upon me by clause (1) of Article 174 of the Constitution
of India, I, Dr. K. ROSAIAH, Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby summon the Legislative Assembly of the
State of Tamil Nadu to meet for its First session at 11.00 A.M. on Wednesday, the 25th May, 2016 in the
Legislative Assembly Chamber, Secretariat, Chennai-600 009.
Raj Bhavan,
K. ROSAIAH,
Guindy,
Governor of Tamil Nadu.
Chennai-600 022,
Dated: 23rd May, 2016.
A.M.P. JAMALUDEEN,
Secretary.”
