The Government of Tamil Nadu having been 
satisfied that the  lands specified in the schedule 
below have to be acquired for industrial purposes to 
wit to the formation of Double way Broad – Guage 
Railway line by changing the existing single Way 
Broad-Guage Railway line from  Omalur – Mettur 
Dam in Salem District and it having already been 
decided that the entire amount of compensation 
to be awarded for the lands is to be paid out 
of the lands controlled or managed by Southern 
Railway. The following notice is issued under 
sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu 
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposed Act, 
1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the 
Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial 
Purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), 
the Government of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the 
lands in 38, Kuttpatti Village, Mettur Taluk, Salem 
District as specified in the Schedule below and 
measuring 3.91.94 hectare (or) 39194 Sq.M to the 
same, a little more or less needed for industrial 
purposes, to wit, for formation of Double Way 
Broad – Guage Railway Line by Changing the 
existing Single Way Broad-Guage Railway line from 
Omalur to Mettur Dam.
The plan of the lands are kept in the office 
of the Land Acquisition Officer and District 
Revenue Officer, Salem and the Special Tahsildar, 
Omalur – Mettur Dam Double Line Broad Guage 
Railway Track, Room No.414, 4th Floor, Collectorate 
Campus, Salem and may be inspected at any time 
during office hours.
