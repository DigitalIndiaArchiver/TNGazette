NOTIFICATION – III
[G.O. Ms. No. 48, Home (Transport-VII), 28th January 2018.]
No. II(2)/HO/100(d-3)/2018.
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (i) of sub-section (1) Section 67 of the Motor Vehicles 
Act, 1988 (Central Act 59 of 1988) and in partial modification of the Home Department Notification 
No.II(2)/HO/79 (d-4)/2018 published at page 6 of Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary 
No. 22, dated the 19th January 2018, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby issues the following direction to the State 
Transport Authority, Tamil Nadu and the Regional Transport Authorities regarding the ﬁ xation of fares for stage carriages 
plying as ordinary services in the State of Tamil Nadu.
2. The Notiﬁ cation hereby issued shall come into force on the 29th January, 2018.
DIRECTION
The fares for the stage carriages plying as “Ordinary Services” on routes/ areas in the State of Tamil Nadu shall 
not exceed the fares calculated at rates given below :-  
1. (i)  58  paise per kilometer on roads in the plains; Provided that for a minimum distance of 10 kilometers a 
fare not exceeding Rs.6.00/- shall be charged.
  (ii)  Basic fare of plains + 20% shall be charged from passengers in the stage carriages plying in Ghats 
Roads.  Provided that for a minimum distance of 6 kilometers a fare not exceeding Rs.6./- shall be charged.
2. Applicable Goods and Services Tax on bus fare shall be collected as and when orders are passed by 
Central / State Government.  
3. In respect of special services operated on occasions like fairs, festivals, weekend etc., Special fare shall be 
charged not exceeding one and a half times the normal fare of the type of services operated.
4. The fares shall be rounded off to the next higher multiple of one rupee.
Explanation-I: “Ghat Road” means a road notiﬁ ed in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette as such by the Government 
under the Tamil Nadu Trafﬁ c Rules, 1938 and indicated in a notice board erected at each end of such road.
Explanation-II:  The fares determined in accordance with the rates speciﬁ ed in paragraph 1(i) and (ii) above shall 
be rounded off to the next higher multiple of one rupee.

NOTIFICATION – IV
[G.O. Ms. No. 48, Home (Transport-VII), 28th January 2018.]
No. II(2)/HO/100(d-4)/2018. 
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (i) of sub-section (1) of Section 67 of the Motor 
Vehicles Act, 1988 (Central Act 59 of 1988) and in partial modification of the Home Department Notification 
No.II(2)/HO/79(d-5)/2018, published at pages 6-7 of Part II Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary 
No. 22, dated 19th January 2018 the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby issues the following direction to the State Transport 
Authority, Tamil Nadu and the Regional Transport Authorities regarding the ﬁ xation of fares for stage carriages plying as 
Express Services in the State of Tamil Nadu. 
2. The notiﬁ cation hereby issued shall come into force on the 29th day of January 2018.
DIRECTION
The fares for the stage carriages plying as “Express Services” on routes / areas in the State of Tamil Nadu shall 
not exceed the fares calculated at the rates given below :-  
1. Express – Semi deluxe:
   (a)  75 paise per kilometre on roads in the plain; Provided that for a minimum distance of 30 kilometres a 
fare not  exceeding Rs.23/- shall be charged.
   (b) Basic fare of plain + 20% shall be charged from passengers in the stage carriages plying as semi deluxe 
on ghat roads:
 
 
Provided that for a minimum distance of 30 kilometres a fare not exceeding Rs.28/- (Basic fare of plain + 20%) 
shall be charged.
2.  Express Super deluxe:
 
(a)  85 paise per kilometre shall be charged if provision is made for a superior class of accommodation for 
passengers in the stage carriages plying as super deluxe on plain roads:
 
 
Provided that for a minimum distance of 30 kilometres a fare not exceeding Rs.26/- shall be charged.
 
(b) Basic fare of plain + 20% shall be charged from passengers in the stage carriages plying as super deluxe 
on ghat roads:
 
 
Provided that for a minimum distance of 30 kilometres a fare not exceeding Rs.32 (Basic fare of plain+20% 
on ghat roads) shall be charged.
3.  Air Suspension Coaches (Ultra Deluxe):
 
(a) 100 paise per kilometre shall be charged if provision is made for a superior class of accommodation for 
passengers in the stage carriages plying as Air suspension Coaches (Ultra Deluxe) on plain roads:
 
 
Provided that for a minimum distance of 30 kilometers a fare not exceeding Rs.30 shall be charged.
 
(b) Basic fare of plain + 20% shall be charged from passengers in the stage carriages plying as Ultra deluxe 
on ghat roads:
 
 
Provided that for a minimum distance of 30 kilometres a fare not exceeding Rs.36 (Basic fare of plain+20% 
on ghat roads) shall be charged.
4.  Air-conditioned  Coaches: 
130 paise per kilometre shall be charged if provision is made for a superior class of accommodation for passengers 
in the stage carriages plying as A/C Coaches on plain roads:
 
Provided that for a minimum distance of 30 kilometers a fare not exceeding Rs.39/- shall be charged.

5.  Air-conditioned Volvo Coaches: 
170 paise per kilometre shall be charged if provision is made for a superior class of accommodation for passengers 
in the stage carriages plying as Volvo type Coaches  on plain roads.
Provided that for a minimum distance of 30 kilometers a fare not exceeding Rs.51/- shall be charged.
Explanation :  “Ghat Road” means a road notiﬁ ed in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette as such by the Government

6.  In respect of special services operated on occasions like fairs, festivals, weekend etc., Special fare shall be 
charged not exceeding one and a half times of the normal fare of the type of services operated.
7.  The fare determined in accordance with the rates speciﬁ ed in paragraph 1, 2, 3, 4 and 5 above shall be 
rounded off to the next higher multiple of one rupee.   
8. The reservation charges of Rs.5/- shall be charged for operation of Express Services of all categories as is in 
vogue. 
9.  Applicable Goods and Services Tax on bus fare shall be collected as and when orders are passed by 
Central / State Government.
