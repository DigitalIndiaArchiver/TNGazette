The Governor of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that the
lands specified in the Schedule below have to be acquired for
Industrial purpose. To wit for the formation of ULTRA MEGA
POWER PROJECT, CHEYYUR and it have already been
decided that the funds controlled or  managed by the Officer
Authorised Signatory, Coastal Tamil Nadu Power Ltd., New
Delhi and having considered the cause shown by the owners
or other persons having interest the said lands as the case
may be do here published the following notice under sub-
section (1) of Section 3 of  the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land
for Industrial Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999);
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Industrial Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999) the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby acquired the lands  specified in the
Schedule below and measuring an extent of 7.68.5 Hectares
to the same, a little more or less are needed for Industrial
Purposes.  To wit for the formation of ULTRA MEGA POWER
PROJECT, CHEYYUR, No. 61. Panaiyur Village, Cheyyur Taluk,
Kancheepuram District.
The plan of the Lands are kept in the office of the Special
Tahsildar (Land Acquisition) Unit -5, ULTRA MEGA POWER
PROJECT, CHEYYUR Scheme, and  may be inspected at
any time during office hours.
