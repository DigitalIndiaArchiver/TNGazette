Constitution of Sub-Court at Thuraiyur in Tiruchirappalli District.
(Roc.No. 2350/2011/G/Judn.)
No. VI(1)/234/2016.
“In exercise of the powers conferred under the first proviso to section 13 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central
Act III of 1873), the High Court, Madras hereby directs that appeals from the decrees or orders of the District Munsif Court,
Thuraiyur be preferred in the Court of Subordinate Judge, Thuraiyur with effect from the date on which the Subordinate Judge,
Thuraiyur assumes charge of that Court.”

Constitution of Separate District Munsif Court at Vaniyambadi by converting the existing Additional District Munsif
Court, Vaniyambadi in Vellore District.
(Roc.No. 2273/2010/G/Judn.)
No. VI(1)/235/2016.
In exercise of the powers conferred by section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873) the
High Court, Madras, hereby fixes Vaniyambadi in Vellore District as the place at which the District Munsif Court, Vaniyambadi,
shall be held.
NOTIFICATION-II
(Roc.No. 2273/2010/G/Judn.)
No. VI(1)/236/2016.
In exercise of the powers conferred by section 11 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873) the
High Court, Madras, hereby directs that in Vellore District, the District Munsif Court, Vaniyambadi, shall have local jurisdiction
over the entire Vaniyambadi Taluk with effect from the date of Constitution of Separate District Munsif Court at Vaniyambadi
by Converting the Existing Additional District Munsif Court, Vaniyambadi.
NOTIFICATION-III
(Roc.No. 2273/2010/G/Judn.)
No. VI(1)/237/2016.
The High Court, Madras hereby directs and notifies that the District Munsif, Vaniyambadi in Vellore District shall exercise
all the powers conferred on a District Munsif under section 12 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of
1873) as amended up-to-date in regard to original suits and proceedings not otherwise exempted from his cognizance of
which the amount or value of the subject matter does not exceed One Lakh Rupees within the local limits of his jurisdiction
with effect from the date of constitution of Separate District Munsif Court at Vaniyambadi by converting the Existing Additional
District Munsif Court, Vaniyambadi.
NOTIFICATION-IV
(Roc.No. 2273/2010/G/Judn.)
No. VI(1)/238/2016.
In exercise of the power conferred by section 28 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873) the
High Court, Madras, hereby directs that the District Munsif, Vaniyambadi shall have and exercise jurisdiction of a Court of
Small Causes under the provincial Small Causes Court Act, 1887 for the trial suits congnizable by a Court of Small Causes
upto the pecuniary limits of Rs.5000/- (Rupees five thousand only) with effect from the date of constitution of Separate District
Munsif Court at Vaniyambadi by converting the Existing Additional District Munsif Court, Vaniyambadi.

bifurcating the existing District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Keeranur in Pudukkottai District.
(Roc.No.1883/2011/G/Judn.)

In exercise of the powers conferred by section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873) the
High Court, Madras, hereby fixes Keeranur in Pudukottai District as the place at which the District Munsif Court, Keeranur,
shall be held.
NOTIFICATION-II
(Roc.No.1883/2011/G/Judn.)
No. VI(1)/240/2016.
In exercise of the powers conferred by section 11 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873) the
High Court, Madras, hereby directs that in Pudukottai District, the District Munsif Court, Keeranur, shall have local jurisdiction
over Kulathur Taluk, IIIuppur Taluk and Viralimalai Taluk with effect from the date of constitution of separate District Munsif
Court at Keeranur.
NOTIFICATION-III
(Roc.No.1883/2011/G/Judn.)
No. VI(1)/241/2016.
The High Court, Madras hereby directs and notifies that the District Munsif, Keeranur in Pudukottai District
shall exercise all the powers conferred on a District Munsif under section 12 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central
Act III of 1873) as amended up-to-date in regard to original suits and proceedings not otherwise exempted from his
cognizance of which the amount or value of the subject matter does not exceed One Lakh Rupees within the local limits
of his jurisdiction with effect from the date of Constitution of Separate District Munsif Court at Keeranur.
NOTIFICATION-IV
(Roc.No.1883/2011/G/Judn.)
No. VI(1)/242/2016.

High Court, Madras, hereby directs that the District Munsif, Keeranur shall have and exercise jurisdiction of a Court of Small
Causes under the provincial Small Causes Court Act, 1887 for the trial suits congnizable by a Court of Small Causes upto
the pecuniary limits of Rs.5000/- (Rupees five thousand only) with effect from the date of Constitution of Separate District
Munsif Court at Keeranur.
Re-Designation of Court at Keeranur in Pudukottai District
(Roc.No.1883/2011/G/Judn.)
No. VI(1)/243/2016.
Whereas the Government of Tamil Nadu have issued orders in G.O.Ms.No.776, Home (Cts.III) Department, dated
28.10.2015 that a Separate District Munsif Court be constituted at Keeranur and in G.O.(Ms).No.789, Home (Cts.II)
Department, dated 30.10.2015, that a Separate Judicial Magistrate Court be constituted at Keeranur by bifurcating the existing
District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Keeranur.
Hence, it is hereby informed that on and from the date on which the District Munsif Court, Keeranur is constituted,
the existing District Munsif-cum-judicial Magistrate Court, Keeranur shall be re-designated as Judicial Magistrate Court,
Keeranur.
