No. SRO B-23/2017.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 read with Article 313 of the
Constitution of India and of all other powers hereunto enabling, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following
amendment to the Fundamental Rules.
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 26th August, 2016.
AMENDMENT
In the said Fundamental Rules, in rule 26, in sub-rule (d), after Note 14, the following Note shall be added, namely:—
"Note 15.—The Director of Handlooms and Textiles shall issue the certificate of officiation in respect of  Joint Directors,
Deputy Directors and Assistant Directors of the Handlooms and Textiles Department in foreign serivce".
S. SWARNA,
Secretary to Government.
