Order:- No. 92, Home (SC), 5th February 2014.
The Hon’ble Chief Minister made announcement No. 10 (Demand No. 22) on the floor of the Tamil Nadu Legislative
Assembly on 23-4-2013 that cash prize of Rs. 5 lakhs will be given for the winners of the Gallantry Medals in future
occasions.
2. Accordingly the following amendment is issued to the Tamil Nadu Chief Minister’s Police Medal Rules issued with
the G.O. first read above:-
AMENDMENT-I
In the said Rules, in rule 13 in the table, under the Title “For Gallantry”, for the expression “Monthly allowance of
Rs. 300/- P.M.” in column (2) against the entry, “Irrespective of Ranks” in column (1), the expression “Cash Prize of
Rs.5,00,000/- (Rupees five lakhs only). No monthly allowance will be given” shall be substituted.
3. The following amendment is issued to the Tamil Nadu Chief Minister’s Prison Service Medal Rules, issued in the
Government order second read above:-
AMENDMENT-II
In the said Rules, in rule 12 in the table, under the Title “For Gallantry”, for the expression “Monthly allowance of
Rs. 300/- P.M.” in column (2) against the entry, “Irrespective of Ranks” (Total 1 Medal) in column (1), the expression
“Cash prize of Rs.5,00,000/- (Rupees five lakhs only). No monthly allowance will be given” shall be substituted.
4. The following amendment is issued to the Tamil Nadu Chief Minister’s Fire  Service Medal Rules, issued in the
Government order Third read above:-
AMENDMENT-III
In the said Rules, under rule 13, the  “Monthly monetary allowance of Rs. 500/- P.M.” attached  to Gallantry medal
shall be read as  “Cash prize of Rs.5,00,000/- and no  monthly allowance will be given”.
5. The above revised rules sanctioning the cash prize of Rs. 5 Lakhs to the awardees of the Hon’ble Tamil Nadu
Chief Minister’s Gallantry Medals for Police, prison and fire and rescue Services will come into effect from the date of
this order and in future cases.
6. It is also ordered that the existing medal awardees of Tamil Nadu Chief Minister’s Gallantry Medal of Police,
prison and fire and Rescue Services Department will continue to get their monthly monetary allowance.
