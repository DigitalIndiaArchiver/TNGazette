WHEREAS in pursuance of the orders of the Hon’ble Supreme Court of India in I.A.No.182868 of 
2019 in Civil Appeal Nos. 5467-5469 of 2017, dated the 6th December 2019, the Delimitation Commission 
constituted under the Tamil Nadu Delimitation Commission Act, 2017 (Tamil Nadu Act 23 of 2017), has 
submitted its recommendation of the territorial wards of Town Panchayats in the nine newly constituted 
districts to the Government, after having published the draft proposal for delimitation of the territorial wards 
of the Town Panchayats based on the last preceding census of which the relevant figures have been 
published (2011 Census) for general information by inviting and processing the objections / suggestions 
received thereon;
AND WHEREAS, under clause (11-B) of section 3 of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 
(Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920), the Director of Town Panchayats has been appointed by the State Government 
to exercise and perform the powers and duties of the Inspector in respect of Town Panchayats under the 
said Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920 in G.O.(Ms) No.150, Municipal Administration and Water Supply Department, 
dated the 31st May 1994 and published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, Extraordinary, dated the 
31st May 1994.
NOW, THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (1) of section 3-X read 
with clause (11-B) of section 3 of the said Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920 and based on the recommendation 
of the Delimitation Commission, the Inspector hereby, after consulting the Town Panchayats concerned, 
makes the following amendments to Notification No.VI(2) / 160(a-1) / 2018 published at pages 1-584 in 
Part VI-Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, Extraordinary, dated the 14th December 2018:-
