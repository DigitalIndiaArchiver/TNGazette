The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that the lands specified in the schedule below have to be
acquired for the expansion of the existing Thoothukkudi Airport purpose, and it having already been decided that the entire
amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands are to be paid out of the funds controlled or managed by Transport
Department,  the following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for
Industrial Purpose Act, 1997.
NOTICE.
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act 1997 (Tamil Nadu
Act 10 of 1999). The Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands specified in the schedule below measuring
22.11.0 hectares to the same, a little more or less needed for the existing Thoothukkudi Airport expansion purposes, to
wit for the establishment of Airport Development by the Transport Department of Tamil Nadu State Govenment.
The plan of the lands are kept in the office of the Collector, Thoothukkudi and may be inspected at any time during
office hours.
