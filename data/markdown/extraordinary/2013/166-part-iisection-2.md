And Whereas, by virtue of sub-section (1) of Section 31 of the said Act, the Finance Committee of the said School consists
of the following members, namely:—
(a) The Vice-Chancellor;
(b) The Secretary to Government in-charge of Finance;
(c) The Secretary to Government in-charge of Law; and
(d) A member of the Southern India Regional Council of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India to be nominated
by the Executive Council;
Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (3) of Section 53 of the said Act, the Governor of Tamil
Nadu hereby specifies 18th June 2013, as the date on which the General Council, the Executive Council, the Academic Council
and the Finance Committee shall commence to exercise their functions.
