animal husbandry, dairying and fisheries department
Amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Fisheries Subordinate Service.
[G.O.Ms.No. 1, Animal Husbandry, Dairying and Fisheries (Fs2), 3rd January 2020,  
Margazhi 18, Vikari, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2050.]
No. SRO B-3/2020.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor 
of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Fisheries Subordinate Service 
(Section 11 part III B of the Special Rules in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 2016). 
Amendments.
In the said Special Rules,-
(i)	
in the ANNEXURE to rule 6, in the Tabular Column, for the expression “degree of B.A., or B.Sc., with 
Zoology, Botany or Chemistry” in Column (3), against the entry “Direct Recruitment” in   Column (2) and 
the corresponding entry “7A. Laboratory Assistant” in Column (1) thereof, the following expression shall 
be substituted, namely:-
	
“undergraduate degree with Zoology or Botany or Chemistry”.
(ii) 	 for rule 14, the following rule shall be substituted, namely:-
	
“14. Reservation of appointment.- The provisions contained in Section 27 of the Tamil Nadu Government 
Servants (Conditions of Service) Act, 2016 (Tamil Nadu Act 14 of 2016) shall apply for appointment to the 
posts by direct recruitment where the cadre strength of the category is more than one”.
k. gopal,
Principal Secretary to Government.

No. SRO B-4/2020.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor 
of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Ministerial Service (Section

2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 20th February 2015.
Amendment
In the said Special Rules, in Annexure-IX, in rule 8, in the tabular column, against the entry "(v) Next one year and 
eight months" in column (1), for the entry "In Collector's Office as Assistant" in column (2) thereof, the following entry shall
