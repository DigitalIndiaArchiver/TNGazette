 GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
Variation to the Approved Contonment Detailed Development Plan No. 2 of Tiruchirappalli Local Planning Area
(Roc. No. 979/2011/DP2)
No. VI(1)/365/2016.
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 33(2) of Town and Country Planning Act 1971, The Commissioner
of Town and Country Planning, Chennai Proceedings Roc. No. 979/2011/DP2, dated: 26-10-2016 hereby confirms the
following variation to the Approved Contonment Detailed Development Plan No. 2 of Tiruchirappalli Local Planning Area
Approved by DTCP’s Proceeding in Roc. No. 21272/98/DP3, dated: 24-12-1999 and fact of this approval in Form
No. 12 and Published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No. 29 Part VI—Section 1 Page 293 & 294,
dated: 26-07-2000 in Publication No.VI(1)/411/2000 and the said draft notification Published in Tamil Nadu Government
Gazette No.19, Part VI—Section 1 Page 226, dated: 25-05-2011 in Publication No. VI(1)/231/2011.
Since an objections and suggestions have been received on this draft notification within the stipulated time which
is accepted and incorporated and hence the same are hereby confirmed and ordered as below:—
VARIATION
1. The entire Contonment Detailed Development Plan No. 2 of Tiruchirappalli Local Planning Area approved by
DTCP’s Proceedings in Roc. No. 21272/98 DP3, dated: 24-12-1999 is varied by the Proceeding of the DTCP Chennai
Roc.No. 979/2011 DP2, dated 26-10-2016 of Contonment Detailed Development Plan No. 2 of Tiruchirappalli Local
Planning Area.
2.The Plan thus varied is numbered as “Map No. 5 DDP(V)/DTCP No.05/2016.
3.The Detailed Development Plan Clauses and Schedules annexed with this plan are made enforceable from the
date of publication of this Notification in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
Chennai-600 002,
SHAMBHU KALLOLIKAR,
26th October 2016.
 Commissioner  of Town and
Country Planning (In-charge).

No. VI(1)/367/2016.

Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972) and in exercise of powers conferred by the G.O.Ms.No.94, Housing and Urban

Coimbatore Local Planning Area approved under the said Act and published in the Housing and Urban Development
Department Notification No.II(2)/HOU/4377/94 at page 1078 of Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette,
dated the 9th November 1994.

In the said Master Plan, in the “LAND USE SCHEDULE” under the heading “COIMBATORE LOCAL PLANNING

(i)

59 (except 59/2A, 2B), 61 (except 61/2) 62 to 66 shall be substituted.
(ii)
