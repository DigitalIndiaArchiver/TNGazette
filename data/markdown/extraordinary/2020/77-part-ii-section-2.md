In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Commissions of Inquiry Act, 
1952 (Central Act LX of 1952), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the 
Public (S.C.) Department Notification No. II(2)/PUSC/793(g)/2017, published at Page-1 of Part II—Section 2 of the 
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 25th September 2017, as subsequently amended.

shall be substituted.
