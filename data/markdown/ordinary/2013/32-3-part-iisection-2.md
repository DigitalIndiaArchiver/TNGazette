[Tamil Nadu Protection of Interest of Depositors (in
Financial Establishments) Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 44 of
1997)–M/s. Star Vision Innovative Marketing Private Limited,
Virudhunagar District–Default in return of deposits by the
financial establishment–Ad-Interim order attaching the
movable properities of the said financial establishment under
section 3 of the said Act–Orders–Issued.]
The following Government Order is puplished:-
[G.O. Ms. No. 464, Home (Police XIX), 8th July 2013,
ÝQ 24, Müò, F¼õœÀõ˜ Ý‡´-2044.]
READ:
From the Additional Director General of Police Economic
Offences Wing-II, Chennai, Letter No.C1/2410/2012,
Dated 14-9-2012 and even No. dated 29-3-2013.
Order: No. 464, Home (Police XIX), 8th July 2013.
Whereas, complaints have been received from a number
of depositors that Messers Star Vision Innovative Marketing
Private Limited, Virudhunagar District, a financial
establishment, has defaulted the return of deposits made by
the depositore after maturity;
And Whereas, the Government is satisfied that the said
financial establishment is not likely to return the deposits to
the depositors and hence, the Government have to protect
the interests of such depositors;
And Whereas, the movable properties specified in the
Schedule to this order are alleged to have been procured
by the said financial establishment from and out of the
deposits collected from the depositors;
Now, Therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by
section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Protection of Interests of
Depositors (in Financial Establishments) Act, 1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 44 of 1997), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby makes an Ad-interim order attaching the movable
properties standing in the name of the proprietor of Messer’s
Star Vision Innovative Marketing Private Limited, Virudhunagar
District as specified in the Scheduled to this order and
transfers the control over the said movable properties to the
Competent Authority, namely, the District Revenue Officer,
Virudhunagar District appointed under the said Act.
2. The Competent Authority, Virudhunagar District is
requested to pursue further action in accordance with the
procedure laid down in sub-sections (3) and (4) of Section
4 of the said Act and also the formalities prescribed in the
Tamil Nadu Protection of Interests of Depositors (in Financial
Establishments) Rules, 1997.
3.
The Special Public Prosecutor, Special Court for the
Tamil Nadu Protection of Interests of Depositors (in Financial
Establishments) Act, 1997, Madurai is requested to render
necessary assistance to the Competent Authority in filling
the application before the said Special Court.
(By order of the Governor)
