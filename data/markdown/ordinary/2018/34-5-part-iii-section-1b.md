No. SRO B-65/2018.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following Rules:- 
RULES
The provisions contained in the Tamil Nadu Government Servants (Conditions of Service) Act, 2016 (Tamil Nadu Act 14 
of  2016) and the Special Rules applicable to the holders of the permanent posts in of class I of Branch-I-Medical of the 
Tamil Nadu Medical service shall apply to the holders of the temporary post of Demonstrator  (Yoga) in Indian Medicine 
and Homoeopathy Department sanctioned from time to time, subject to the modiﬁ cations speciﬁ ed in the following Rules:–
2. Constitution:– The post shall constitute a separate category in the said class of said service. 
3. Appointment:– Appointment to the post shall be made by direct recruitment.
4. Qualiﬁ cation:– (a) Age.- No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post by direct recruitment, if he has 
completed or will complete 35 years of age on the 1st day of July of the year, in which the selection for the appointment 
is made.
 
(b) Other Qualiﬁ cations: – No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post unless he possesses the following 
qualiﬁ cations, namely: – 
 
 
(i)  Must have passed a Degree in Naturopathy and Yogic Science (BNYS) or Diploma in Naturopathy with 
not less than four and half years academic duration awarded by a recognized University; and
 
 
(ii)  Must have registered with the Tamil Nadu Board of Indian medicine, Government of Tamil Nadu under 
Class A Practitioner.
5. Reservation of Appointment:– The Principle of reservation of appointment provided in Section 27 of the Tamil Nadu 
Government Servants (Conditions of Service) Act, 2016 (Tamil Nadu Act 14 of 2016) shall apply for appointment to the post 
by direct recruitment.
6. Probation:– Every person appointed to the post by direct recruitment shall from the date on which he joins duty, be 
on probation for a total period of two years on duty within a continuous period of three years. 
7. Tests:-(a) Every person appointed to the post, shall within the period of probation pass the Account Test for Executive 
Ofﬁ cers; and
 
 
(b) The Departmental Test in the Tamil Nadu Medical Code within a period of four years from the date of 
appointment to the post, failing which his increment in the time scale of pay applicable to the post shall be postponed without 
cumulative effect till he passes the said tests.
8. Private Practice:- The holders of the post shall be allowed consulting practice:
   Provided that if he acts as an Inspecting Medical Ofﬁ cer in the Directorate of Indian Medicine and Homoeopathy, he 
shall not be allowed for private practice.
9. Appointing Authority:– The appointing authority for the post shall be the Commissioner of Indian Medicine and 
Homoeopathy.
10. Pay:– There shall be paid to the holders of the post, a monthly pay calculated in the revised scale of pay level 
22-56100-177500.
