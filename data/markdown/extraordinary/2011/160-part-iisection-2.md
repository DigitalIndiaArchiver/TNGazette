No.II(2)/PDSI/241(b)/2011.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that
the land specified in the Schedule below and situated
Periyakudal Village, Egmore-Nungambakkam Taluk, Chennai
District have to be acquired for a public  purpose, and it having
already been decided that the entire amount of compensation
to be awarded for the lands is to be paid out of  funds provided
by the Chennai Metro Rail Limited, the following Declaration is
issued under Section 6 of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894
(Central Act I of 1894).
DECLARATION
  Under Section 6 of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894 (Central
Act I of 1894), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby declares
that the land specified in  the Schedule below and measuring
2079.0 square metres (or) 22378.35 square feet be the same
a little more or less are needed for public purpose to wit, for
the implementation of Chennai Metro Rail Project. Under
sub-section (1) of Section 17 of the said Act, the Governor of
Tamil Nadu further directs that the possession of the said land
may be taken on the expiry of 15 days from the date of
publication of the Notice mentioned in Section 9 (1)
of the Act.
A plan of land is kept in  the Office of the Special Tahsildar
(Land Acquisition),  Chennai Metro Rail Limited, Chennai
Unit-II, Harini Towers, No. 7, Contran Smith Road, Gopalapuram,
Chennai-86 and may be inspected at any time during
office hours.
SCHEDULE
Chennai District, Egmore-Nungambakkam Taluk,
Periyakudal Village.
Block-1, T.S. No. 120/1 (New No. 120/3), register
holder State Housing Board, interested person Kandasamy
College, classified as Ryotwari Manai, bounded on the north by
T.S. No. 120/1part vacant,  east by T.S.No. 120/2 vacant and
building,  south by Block 14, 2nd Avenue Road and west by
Block 14, 3rd Avenue Road, Water Sump-1, 2 h.p. motor-1,
Drainage Pipe, Neem tree-2, Nona tree-2, Vadhanarayana tree-7,
Kattuvagai tree-1, Maravalli tree-2, on south and western side
compound wall with black stone, Valueless tender Neem
trees, Tamarind tree-1—2079.0 square metres (or)
22378.35 square feet
