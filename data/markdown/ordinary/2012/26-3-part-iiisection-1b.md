  Amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Engineering Service in Public Works Department.
[G.O. Ms. No. 153, Public Works (A2),  15th June 2012, ÝQ 1, F¼õœÀõ˜ Ý‡´-2043.]
No. SRO B-20/2012.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the  Tamil Nadu Engineering
Service (Section 10 in Volume II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1969).
2  (1) The  amendment (1) hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 10th July 1960.
2.
The  amendments (2) and (3) hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 24th August
1981.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Special rules, in Part II, under the heading “Branch I - Public Works”:—
(1) for the expression “Head Draftsman” or “Head Draughtsman” wherever it occurs, the expression “Special
Grade Draughtsman” shall be substituted;
(2) for the expression “Chief Head Draughtsman” or “Chief Head Draftsman” wherever it occurs, the expression
“Chief Technical Officer” shall be substituted;
(3) for the expression “Draughtsman Grade-I” or “Draftsman Grade-I” wherever it occurs, the expression “senior
Technical Officer” shall be substituted.
[G.O. Ms. No. 154, Public Works (A2),  15th June 2012, ÝQ 1, F¼õœÀõ˜ Ý‡´-2043.]
No. SRO B-21/2012.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the  Tamil Nadu Engineering
Service (Section 10 in Volume II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1969).
2   (1)  The  amendment (1) hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 19th September 1981.
(2)  The  amendment (2) hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 24th November 1982.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Special rules, in Part II, under the heading “Branch I - Public Works”:—
(1) for the expression “Special Grade Draughtsman”  wherever it occurs, the expression “Special Grade Technical
Officer” shall be substituted;
(2) for the expressions “Senior Technical Officer”, “Chief Technical Officer” “Special  Grade Technical Officer”
wherever they occur, the expressions  “Senior Draughting Officer”, “Head Draughting Officer”, Special Grade Draughting
Officer” shall respectively,  be substituted;
M. SAI KUMAR,
Secretary to Government.

  Amendment to the Rules relating to the Temporary Posts of Head Master and Music Teacher in the Tamil Nadu
Government Music Schools in Tamil Nadu Government Music College Subordinate Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 89, Tourism and Culture (C1-2),  30th May 2012, Vaigasi 17, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2043.]
No. SRO B-22/2012.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Rules published with the Tourism and Culture
Department Notification No. SRO B-34/2007 at pages 49-51 of part III—Section 1(b) of the Tamil Nadu Government
Gazette, dated the 13th June 2007.
2. The  amendment hereby made shall come into force on and from the 30th May of 2012.
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in rule 5, in sub-rule (b), in the Table; for the entries “must have passed 10th Standard” in Column
(3), against the 1. category I. Headmaster and 2. Music Teacher in column (1) and “Direct Recruitment” in Column (2),
the following entries shall be substituted, namely:—
“(i)  Must have passed the Higher Secondary Examinations.”
