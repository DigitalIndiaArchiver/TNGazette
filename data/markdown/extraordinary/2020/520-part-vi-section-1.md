 The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that the lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below are acquired 
under Tamil Nadu Road Sector project –II, for Highways purpose for Widening of SH-15, Chithode - Erode Road at 
KM (153/600 to 161/815) Erode Town Village in Erode Taluk, Erode,  District, and it having already been decided that 
the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands to be paid out of the funds controlled and managed 
by the Divisional Engineer(H), Tiruppur (TNRSP-II), Tamil Nadu after having considering the cause shown by the owner 
or other persons having interest on such lands as the case may be do hereby publish the following notice is issued 
under sub-section (1) of section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (TN Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
 
Under sub-section (1) of section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) read 
with the Tamil Nadu Land Acquisition Laws (Revival of Operation Amendment and Validation) Act ,2019, the Governor 
of Tamil Nadu hereby notiﬁ es that the R.Manai lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below and measuring to an extent of 
445 sq.mts. of R.Manai lands are acquired in Erode Town Village in Erode Taluk, Erode,  District, to the same, a little 
more or less needed for Tamil Nadu Road Sector project –II, for State Highways purpose, for Widening of SH-15, 
Chithode - Erode Road at KM (153/600 to 161/815). 
The plan of the lands under acquisition are kept in the oﬃ  ce of the Special District Revenue oﬃ  cer (Land Acquisition), 
Tamil Nadu Road Sector Project-II, Salem and oﬃ  ce of the Special Tahsildar (Land Acquisition), Tamil Nadu Road Sector 
Project-II in Tiruppur may be inspected at any time during the oﬃ  ce hours.
