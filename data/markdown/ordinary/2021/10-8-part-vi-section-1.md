Period of adjournments for vacation and holidays for all the Subordinate 
Courts in the State for the year 2021 
(Rc. No. Rs1/789/2021)
No.VI(1)/78/2021.
Notice is hereby given that the Sessions and Civil Courts in Combined Vellore District (Vellore, Ranipet and Tirupattur)
will be closed for the Vacation and Holidays for 2021 as follows:
(CIVIL COURTS)
The Principal District and Sessions Court, Vellore District, Vellore, the Additional District and Sessions Court, Vellore, 
Ranipet, Tirupattur and the Subordinate Judges Court, Vellore, Ranipet, Arakkonam, Gudiyatham, Vaniyambadi, Tirupattur.
The Court of the District Munsif’s of Vellore, Sholinghur, Arakkonam, Gudiyatham, Katpadi, Ambur, Vaniyambadi, Tirupattur 
and the Court of District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate, Walajah, Ranipet, Arcot and Ambur on the Civil Side only.
SUMMER VACATION
Saturday, the 1st day of May, 2021 to Monday, the 31st day of May, 2021.
DUSSEHRA HOLIDAYS 
Saturday, the 9th day of October, 2021 to Monday, the 18th day of October, 2021.
CHRISTMAS HOLIDAYS
Saturday, the 25th day of December, 2021 to Friday, the 31st day of December, 2021.
2. THE HOLIDAYS FOR ALL THE SUBORDINATE COURTS IN THE STATE OF TAMIL NADU 
 
(a) All Sundays are holidays for all the Subordinate Courts including Family Courts functioning in the State of 
Tamil Nadu. 
 
(b) The following 10 Saturdays are working days for all the Subordinate Courts including Family Courts functioning 
in the State of Tamil Nadu.
 
 
23-01-2021, 06-03-2021, 17-04-2021, 05-06-2021, 
 
 
03-07-2021, 07-08-2021, 04-09-2021, 23-10-2021, 
 
 
20-11-2021 and 04-12-2021.  
 
(c) Except the above Saturdays, all other Saturdays are holidays for all the Subordinate Courts, except Family 
Courts. 
 
(d) All the Family Courts functioning in the State of Tamil Nadu shall work on 1st and 3rd Saturdays of every 
month except those Saturdays which are declared as Public Holidays. All other Saturdays are declared as holidays, except 
23-01-2021 and 23-10-2021 which are declared as working days.
