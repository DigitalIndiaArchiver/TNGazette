RELIEVED A MINISTER FROM THEIR MINISTERSHIP OF COUNCIL OF MINISTER.
[G.O. Ms. No.  804, Public (Special-B), 6th September 2014,  Aavani  21, Jaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2045.]
No.  I/PUSP/28(a-1)/2014.
I, K. Rosaiah, Governor of Tamil Nadu accept the recommendation of the Chief Minister that
Thiru  V. Moorthy,  Minister  for  Milk  and  Dairy  Development  be  relieved  from  the  Council of  Ministers and direct that he
be relieved of his duties and responsibilities as Minister with immediate effect.
K. ROSAIAH,
Governor of Tamil Nadu.
APPOINTMENT OF MINISTER FOR THE STATE OF TAMIL NADU
[G.O. Ms. No.  805, Public (Special-B), 6th September 2014,  Aavani  21, Jaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2045.]
No. I/PUSP/28(a-2)/2014.
I, K. Rosaiah, Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoint Thiru B.V. Ramanaa with effect from the Afternoon of
6th September  2014 to  be  the Member of my Council of Ministers.
K. ROSAIAH,
Governor of Tamil Nadu.

ALLOCATION  OF  SUBJECTS  AMONG  MINISTERS
[G.O. Ms. No.  806, Public (Special-B), 6th September 2014,  Aavani  21, Jaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2045.]
No. I/PUSP/28(a-3)/2014.
Under Clause (3) of Article 166 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby allocates  the
subjects Milk and Dairy Development  to Thiru B.V. Ramanaa, appointed as a Minister in the Consequent
on  the above allocation, Thiru B.V. Ramanaa be  designated as Minister for Milk and Dairy Development.
MOHAN VERGHESE CHUNKATH,
Chief Secretary.
