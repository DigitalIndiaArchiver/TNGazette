Notice is hereby given that an election by direct polling is to be held in the University premises for electing Two Members
to the Governing Council of the Tamil Nadu DR. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai, as per clause (b) of sub-section (4) of
Section 21 of The Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R Medical University, Chennai, Act 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 37 of 1987) by the Deans/
Principals of the Affiliated Colleges providing courses of Study and Training in Medical Science from among themselves to be fill
up the arising vacancies. The term of membership in the Governing Council will be for a period of three years from the date of
publication of the results of the election (i.e. 18-12-2017) or the date on which he/she holds the post of Deans/Principals of the
Affiliated Colleges whichever is earlier.
2. Forms of Nomination papers may be obtained from the Registrar, who is the Returning Officer from 19-11-2014 to
01-12-2014 between 11.00 a.m and 3.00 p.m. (on all working days.)

“Nomination for election to the Governing Council” as indicated below, and sent by Registered Post (Acknowledgement Due), so
as to be received by the REGISTRAR / RETURNING OFFICER not later than 4.00 p.m. on 01-12-2014 or delivered to the
Returning Officer during office hours in person or by his proposer or by messenger under acknowledgement within the date
and hour mentioned above.
