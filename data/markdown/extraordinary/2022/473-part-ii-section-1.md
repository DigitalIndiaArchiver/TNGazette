AND WHEREAS, the above said Schemes, implemented by Tamil Nadu Generation and Distribution Corporation 
Limited (TANGEDCO) are funded by the Government of Tamil Nadu, which involve expenditure from the Consolidated 
Fund of the State;
NOW, THEREFORE, under section 7 of the Aadhaar (Targeted Delivery of Financial and Other Subsidies, Beneﬁ ts 
and Services) Act, 2016 (Central Act 18 of 2016), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notiﬁ es the following, namely,-
(1) An eligible individual desirous of availing beneﬁ ts under the above Schemes is required to furnish proof of 
possession of Aadhaar number or undergo Aadhaar authentication.
(2) An eligible individual desirous of availing beneﬁ ts under the above Schemes, who does not possess an Aadhaar 
number or has not yet enrolled for Aadhaar, is hereby required to apply for Aadhaar enrolment provided he or she is 
entitled to obtain Aadhaar as per Section 3 of the said Act.
(3) The applicants are hereby requested to apply in any Aadhaar enrolment center or permanent enrolment center 
(list available at www.uidai.gov.in) to get enrolled for Aadhaar:
Provided that till the time Aadhaar is assigned to the individual, beneﬁ ts under the above Schemes shall be given 
to such individuals subject to production of the following identiﬁ cation documents, namely:- 
(a)
(i)
If he or she has enrolled, his or her Aadhaar  Enrolment Identity Slip; 
                             (or)
(ii)
Copy of his or her request made for Aadhaar Enrolment.
(b)
Any one of the following documents, namely:-
(i)
Bank passbook with photograph; (or)
(ii)
Voter identity card issued by the Election Commission of India; (or)
(iii)
Ration card; (or)
(iv)
Permanent Account Number (PAN) Card issued by the Income Tax 
Department; (or)
(v)
Indian Passport; (or)
(vi)
Driving Licence issued by the Licensing Authority under the Motor Vehicles 
Act, 1988 (Central Act 59 of 1988); (or)
(vii)
Certiﬁ cate of identity, bearing a passport size photograph of such member 
issued by a Gazetted Oﬃ  cer on an Oﬃ  cial letter head; (or)
(viii)
Any other documents speciﬁ ed by the Ministry of Electronics and 
Information Technology, Government of India.
In order to avail the beneﬁ ts in a convenient and hassle free manner by the beneﬁ ciaries, the Tamil Nadu Generation 
and Distribution Corporation Limited (TANGEDCO) shall make a wide publicity through electronic and Print media to 
make beneﬁ ciaries aware of the requirements of Aadhaar under the above schemes and they may be advised to get 
themselves enrolled in the nearest Aadhaar Enrolment Centers available in their areas, in case they are not already 
enrolled.
