GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI
Taking over of Khazimar Peria Pallivasal Wakf, Madurai District under Direct Management.
(Rc. No. 13575/02/B8/Madurai.)
No. VI-3(a)/11/2014.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 38 of the Wakf  Act, 1995, the Tamil Nadu Wakf
Board in its resolution in item No. 92/12, dated 23rd October 2013 in Rc. No. 13575/02/B8/Madurai has ordered to take
over the management of Khazimar Peria Pallivasal Wakf, Madurai district under the direct control of the  Tamil Nadu Wakf
Board for a period of two years from the date of the Board’s Order and to appoint the wakf  Superintendent, Madurai
as its Executive Officer.
F. ABDUL RAZICK,
Chennai-600 001,
Chief Executive Officer,
8th January 2014.
