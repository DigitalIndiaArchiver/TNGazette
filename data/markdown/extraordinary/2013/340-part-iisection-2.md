DTP—II-2 Ex. (340)
TOURISM, CULTURE AND RELIGIOUS ENDOWMENTS DEPARTMENT
 DATE OF  COMMENCEMENT OF THE TAMIL NADU MUSIC AND FINE ARTS UNIVERSITY ACT,  2013
(TAMIL NADU ACT 30 OF 2013).
[G.O. (Ms) No. 292, Tourism, Culture and Religious Endowments (C1-1), 14th November 2013,
Aippasi 28, Vijaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2044.]
No. II(2)TCRE/865(b)/2013.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Music and Fine Arts
University Act, 2013 (Tamil Nadu Act  30 of  2013), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the 14th  November 2013
as the date on which the said Act shall come into force.
R. KANNAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
