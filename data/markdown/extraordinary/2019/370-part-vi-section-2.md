Read:
	
	
1. 	 Government Lr. (D) No.149, Rural Development and Panchayat Raj (PR-1) Department, dated 17-6-2019.
	
	
2.	 G.O. (D) No.246, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (Election) Department, dated the 25th June 2019.
No. VI(2)/91(a)/2019.
In exercise of the powers conferred by paragraph 3 of the Tamil Nadu Local Bodies Election Symbols (Reservation 
and Allotment) Order, 2019, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission hereby notifies that the elections to the following 
offices shall be held on party basis:- 
	
1.	
Ward Members of Town Panchayats;
	
2.	
Councillors of Municipalities and Municipal Corporations;
	
3.	
Chairmen of Town Panchayats and Municipalities; 
	
4.	
Mayors of Municipal Corporations;
	
5.	
Ward Members of Panchayat Unions; and
	
6.	
Ward Members of District Panchayats.
(By Order of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission)
