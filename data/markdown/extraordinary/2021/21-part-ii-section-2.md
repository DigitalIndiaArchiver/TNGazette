CONSTITUTION OF SEARCH COMMITTEE TO RECOMMEND A PANEL OF THREE NAMES TO THE CHANCELLOR 
FOR SELECTION OF VICE-CHANCELLOR, THE TAMIL NADU Dක. AMBEDKAR LAW UNIVERSITY, CHENNAI UNDER 
THE TAMIL NADU Dක.AMBEDKAR LAW UNIVERSITY, ACT.

Under Section 12 of the Tamil Nadu Dr. Ambedkar Law University Act, 1996 (Tamil Nadu Act 43 of 1997), the 
Government hereby constitute a Committee consisting of the following persons for recommending a panel of three names 
to the Chancellor for appointment of Vice-Chancellor of the Tamil Nadu Dr.Ambedkar Law University, Chennai:- 
1.
Hon’ble Justice P.Kalaiyarasan, 
Retired Judge of the High Court, Madras, 
Plot No.64, 3rd Cross Street, 1st Main Road, 
CBI Colony, Kandanchavadi, Chennai – 600 096.
Government Nominee
2.
Prof. Kamala Shankaran,
Former Vice-Chancellor,
The Tamil Nadu National Law University,
Campus Law Centre,
Faculty of Law, University of Delhi, Delhi - 110 007.  
Syndicate Nominee
3.
Dr. S.S.P. Darwesh,
Former Vice-Chancellor,
The Tamil Nadu Dr.Ambedkar Law University, 
No.17, Perambur High Road,
Shanthi Nagar, Chennai - 600 012.
Academic Senate
Nominee
2. Hon’ble Justice P.Kalaiyarasan (Retd.) will be the Chairman of the above Committee.
3. The above Committee is requested to recommend a panel of three names along with their bio-data to the 
Chancellor for appointment of Vice-Chancellor of the Tamil Nadu Dr.Ambedkar Law University, Chennai. 
  
C. GOPI RAVIKUMAR,
