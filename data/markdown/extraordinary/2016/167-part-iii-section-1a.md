In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-sections (1) and (1-A) of Section 15 of the Mines and Minerals
(Development and Regulation) Act, 1957 (Central Act 67 of 1957), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
makes the following amendment to the Tamil Nadu Minor Mineral Concession Rules, 1959.
 AMENDMENT
In the said rules, in rule 42, in clause (iii), for the expression “four hundred and fifty days”,
the expression  “six hundred and thirty days” shall be substituted.
C.V. SANKAR,
Additional Chief Secretary to Government.
