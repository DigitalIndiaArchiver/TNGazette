 2. The Honorarium and allowances, and other terms and conditions of services of the Chairperson and Members 
shall be as per the Tamil Nadu State Commission for Women (Honorarium, Allowances and Conditions of Service of 
Chairperson and Members) Rules, 2013. 
3. The Chairperson and Members of the said Commission shall hold office for a period of three years.
4. The tenure of other two Members of the Tamil Nadu State Commission for Women who have been nominated by the 
Government from among the Members of the Legislative Assembly will cease to be the Members of the Commission from 
the date on which they cease to be the Members of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly or on completion of the tenure 
for three years whichever is earlier.
5. There shall not be any change in the official members of the Commission.
