LATE NOTIFICATION:
NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
HIGHWAYS AND MINOR PORTS DEPARTMENT
Delegation of powers of Collector to District Revenue
Officer under Tamil Nadu Highways Act.
[G.O.Ms.No. 176, Highways and Minor Ports (HF.1), 11th
May 2010, Chitthirai 28, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No.II(2)/HWMP/498/2010.—In exercise of the powers con-
ferred by sub-section (5) of Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Highways
Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby appoints the District Revenue Officer of a district to
perform the functions of Collector of the district concerned under
the said Act.
G. SANTHANAM,
Secretary to Government.
