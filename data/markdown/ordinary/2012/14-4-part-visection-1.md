Individual Variation to the Approved Melur D.D. Plan No. 1 of Melur Local
Planning Area-Deletion of "B1B1" 50'-0'' Scheme Road.
(Roc. No. 27024/04/DP2.)
No.VI(1)/112/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section 2 of Section 33 of Town and Country Planing Act, 1971
the Director of Town and Country Planning, Chennai hereby confirms the folllowing Individual variation for Deletion of
“B1B1; 50'-0" Scheme Road to the approved Melur D.D. Plan No. 1 of Melur Local Planning Authority. The said
Notification was published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No. 12, Part VI—Section 1, page 149, dated
22nd March 2006 in Publication No. VI(1)/186/06.
Since no objections and suggestions have been received on this draft notification within the stipulated time the same
are hereby confirmed and ordered as below:—
CONFIRMATION OF VARIATION
1. Wherever the expression in Map No. 4, DDP(MR)/DTCP No. 36/94 occurs the expression DDP(V)/DTCP
No. 7/2006 shall be added at the end to be read with.
2. In Schedule III, Part I all the entries to "B1B1" road shall be deleted.
3. In Schedule IV (Form No. 7) in serial No. 1, the entry "B1B1" shall be deleted and in Column No. 4, the following
enry "9.11983" shall be substituted.
4. In schedule V (Form No. 6) all the entries in the "B1B1" road shall be deleted.
A. KARTHIK,
Chennai-600 002,
Commissioner of Town and Country Planning.
