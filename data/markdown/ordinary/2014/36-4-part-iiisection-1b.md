No. SRO B-51/2014.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following rules.—
2.  The rules hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on and from the 2nd September 2005.
RULES
The General Rules and the Special Rules applicable to the holders of the permanent posts in the Tamil Nadu Basic
Service shall apply to the holders of the temporary posts of Marker in the Technical Education Department, subject to the
modifications specified in the following rules:—
2. Constitution.—The post shall constitute a separate category in a distinct class in the said service.
3. Appointment.—Appointment to the post shall be made by direct recruitment.
4. Appointing authority.—The appointing authority for the post shall be the Principal of the Engineering College or the
Polytechnic concerned.
5. Qualification.—No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post, unless he shall be able to read and write in Tamil.
6. Pay.—There shall be paid to the holder of the post a monthly pay calculated in the scale of
Rs.2550-55-2660-60-3200:
Provided that with effect on and from the 1st January 2006, the monthly pay shall be calculated in the Pay Band of
Rs.4800-10000 with Grade Pay of Rs. 1,300.
7. Saving.—Nothing contained in these rules shall adversely affect the persons holding the post on the date of publication
of these rules in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
HEMANT KUMAR SINHA,
Principal Secretary to Government.
PLANNING, DEVELOPMENT AND SPECIAL INITIATIVES DEPARTMENT.
Amendment to Ad hoc Rules relating to the Temporary Posts of Senior Research Assistant and
Research Assistant  in the Evaluation and Applied Research Department
[G.O. Ms. No. 79, Planning, Development and Special Initiatives (ST.2&E), 28th August 2014,
Aavani 12, Jaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu - 2045.]
No. SRO B-52/2014.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following Amendment to the Ad hoc rules published with the Planning, Development
and Special Initiatives Department Notification No. SRO B-26/2014, published in pages 35-36 in Part III—Section 1 (b) of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 26th March 2014.
The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on and from 27-02-2014.
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in Rule 4, in sub-rule (b), in the Tabular columns, in column (2), against the entry "Senior Research
Assistant" in column (1) and the corresponding entry in column (3) thereof, the following entry shall be inserted, namely:-
"By promotion from the post of Research Assistant in Evaluation and Applied Research Department".
S. KRISHNAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
