Amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Highways Engineering Subordinate Service.
[G.O.Ms. No. 122, Highways and Minor Ports (HM1), 3rd October 2020, ¹óì¢ì£C 17, ê£˜õK, F¼õœÀõ˜ Ý‡´-2051.]
No. SRO B-33/2020.— In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, 
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Highways 
Engineering Subordinate Service (Section 15 in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 2016). 
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖගඛ.
In the said Special Rules,- 
1. 
in rule 3, in the TABLE, for entry “(2) Promotion from the posts of Road Inspectors and Field Assistants who possess 
the Diploma/Licentiate in Civil engineering or Diploma/licentiate in Mechanical Engineering qualiﬁ cation and have rendered 
service for a period of not less than ﬁ ve years in the category they serve:” in column(2), against the entry “Junior Draughting 
Oﬃ  cers” in category 4 in column(1) thereof, the following entry shall be substituted, namely:- 
 
‘‘(2) Promotion from the posts of Road Inspectors and Field Assistants who possess a Diploma in Civil Engineering 
and have rendered service for a period of not less than two years in the category they serve:” ;
2. in Schedule II (referred to in rule 8), in the Tabular column(s),- 
 
(a)  in column (1), for the entries “3 and 4. Head Draughting Oﬃ  cers in the oﬃ  ce of the Chief Engineer (Highways), 
Civil Draughtsmen in the oﬃ  ce of the Chief Engineer (General), Highways and elsewhere, Senior Draughting Oﬃ  cers, Draughting 
Oﬃ  cers and Junior Draughting Oﬃ  cers”, the following entries shall be substituted, namely:- 
“3 and 4. Head Draughting Oﬃ  cers, Civil Draughtsmen, Senior Draughting Oﬃ  cers, Draughting Oﬃ  cers and Junior 
Draughting Oﬃ  cers.” ; 
 
(b)  in column (2), against the entries “3 and 4. Head Draughting Oﬃ  cers, Civil Draughtsmen, Senior Draughting 
Oﬃ  cers, Draughting Oﬃ  cers and Junior Draughting Oﬃ  cers” as so substituted in column (1) thereof,- 
 
(i) 
the following items shall be omitted, namely:- 
 
“(ii)  A pass in sections A and B (Civil) of the Associate Member of Institution of Engineers (India)
Examinations; or 
 
(iii)  A pass in the Trade Test for Draughtsmen (Civil) conducted by the Director General of Resettlement and 
Employment and by the National Council for Vocational Training, Government of India:” 
(ii) for item (iv), the following item shall be substituted namely:- 
 
“(ii)  The qualiﬁ cations speciﬁ ed below shall be necessary for appointment to the post of Junior Draughting Oﬃ  cer, 
by promotion or by recruitment by transfer, namely:- 
 
(a) Members of Category 5 (Assistant Draughtsman) who have acquired the qualiﬁ cation of a Diploma in Civil 
Engineering subsequently shall have a service of not less than two years in that category after acquiring such qualiﬁ cation; 
and 
 
(b) Members of Road Inspectors and Field Assistants who have acquired the qualiﬁ cation of a Diploma in Civil 
Engineering subsequently shall have a service of not less than two years in that category after acquiring such qualiﬁ cation: 
Provided that while making appointment by direct recruitment to the post of Junior Draughting Oﬃ  cer, preference shall 
be given if other things being equal, to those who have undergone one year of apprenticeship training under the Government 
of India Scheme or the State Government Apprenticeship Scheme.” 
A. KARTHIK,
Principal Secretary to Government.

No. SRO B-34/2020.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 read with 
Article 313 of the Constitution of India and of all other powers hereunto enabling, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes 
the following amendment to the Fundamental Rules and the Instructions thereunder. 
2. Amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 23rd day of March 2020. 
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖග.
In the said Fundamental Rules, in Annexure-VII, under the Executive Instructions regarding Casual Leave under ruling 
(3) under rule 85, under the heading “II. Special Casual Leave”, after rule 14, the following rule shall be added, namely:- 
“15. Special Casual Leave for six days in a calendar year shall be granted to Government Servants having children with 
special needs on production of a supportive document obtained either from the authority competent to issue the certiﬁ cate 
of disability under the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 (Central Act 49 of 2016) or from a Registered Medical 
Practitioner attending to the child, certifying that he/she is a special child suﬀ ering from disability that requires parental support 
for daily life activities.”.

Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for Tamil Nadu Basic Service (Section 
19 in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 2016).
2.  (i) 
The amendments (1) and (4) hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 20th October 
1993.

(1) after the ﬁ rst proviso, the following proviso shall be inserted, namely :- 
“Provided further that the candidates belonging to Most Backward Classes and Denotiﬁ ed Communities, who do not
