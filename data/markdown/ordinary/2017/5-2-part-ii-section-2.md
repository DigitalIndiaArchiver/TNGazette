No. II(2)/CTR/77/2017.—In exercise of the powers conferred
by clause  (b) of sub-section (1) of Section 9 of the
Indian Stamp Act, 1899 (Central Act II of 1899), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby provides for the consolidation
of duty of Rs. 4,20,00,000/- (Rupees Four Crore and Twenty
Lakhs only) chargeable under the said Act in respect of issue
of policies by Life Insurance Corporation of India,
Chennai-2, throughout its branches in the State of Tamil Nadu
for the period from 1st January 2017 to 31st March 2017.
K. ARUNACHALAM,
Joint Secretary to Government.

No. II(2)/HWMP/ 78/2017.—The Governor of Tamil Nadu
having satisfied that the lands specified in the Schedule below
have to be acquired for Highways Purpose, to wit for the
formation of Road  Over Bridge at Km 1/8 of Hasthampatty-
Cherry road in lieu of existing L.C.No.184 at railway
Km 190/300-400 between Salem Market and Salem Town
Railway Station in Salem District, Salem Taluk, Kumarasamipatti,
Periyeri and Salem Town Villages and it having already been
decided that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded
for the lands is to be paid out of the funds controlled or
managed by the  Divisional Engineer (Projects), Highways
Department after having considered the cause shown by the
owner (or) other person having interest on such lands as the
case may be do hereby Publish the following notice  under
sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act,
2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE

Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the Ryot Manai lands specified
in the Schedule below measuring to an extent of 0.5217.5
Sqmts to the same, a little more or less needed for the
formation of Road  Over Bridge at Km 1/8 of Hasthampatty-
Cherry road in lieu of existing L.C.No.184 at railway Km 190/
300-400 between Salem Market and Salem Town Railway
Station in Salem District, Salem Taluk, Kumarasamipatti, Periyeri
and Salem Town Villages
The plan of the lands is kept in the office of the District
Collector, Salem and may be inspected at any time during office
hours.
