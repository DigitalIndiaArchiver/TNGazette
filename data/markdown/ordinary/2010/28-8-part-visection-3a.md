GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI
Taking over the Direct Management of Muslim Muhallam Wakf, Colachel, Kanyakumari district under direct control of
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
(Rc. No. 14602/07/B19/K.K./RTI)
No. VI-3(a)/105/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act 1995, the Tamil Nadu
Wakf Board in its proceedings in Item No. 184/09 in Rc. No. 14602/07/B19/K.K./RTI, dated 19th January 2010 has ordered
to take over the management of Muslim Muhallam Wakf, Colachel, Kanyakumari District, under the direct control of the Tamil
Nadu Wakf Board for the period of one year from the date of notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette and to
appoint the Superintendent of Wakfs, Tirunelveli as its Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001.
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
1st July 2010.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
