V.O. CHIDAMBARANAR PORT TRUST, TUTICORIN.
Recruitment Rules for the Posts of Senior Mechanic Gr-I, Chargeman (Mechanical) and Operator Gr-I (Class- III)
 
Amendment to the Schedule II of Tuticorin Port Trust Employees (Recruitment, Seniority and Promotion) 
Regulations, 2017.
(MEE-CMEEL-EST-CHROPR-VI-21)
No.SRO C-7/2022.  
 
 
 
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (i) of Section 124, read with sub-section (1)(a) of the Major Port 
Trusts Act, 1963 (38 of 1963) the Board of Trustees, V.O. Chidambaranar Port Trust hereby approves the revised manner 
of recruitment (Recruitment Rules) for Class-III posts under Regulation 5 of Tuticorin Port Trust Employees (Recruitment, 
Seniority and Promotion) Regulations, 2008 as set out in the Schedule annexed to this Notiﬁ cation.
2. The said Recruitment Rules shall come into effect from  the date of publication of this Notiﬁ cation in the 
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette. 
SCHEDULE
(1) In the said Schedule under Regulations, in the Recruitment Rules for the post of Senior Mechanic Gr-I (Mechanical 
& Electrical Engineering Department) (Sl.No.115), the provisions under Column No.12 shall be substituted by the following-
Promotion:
Senior Mechanic with ﬁ ve years regular service in the grade under the scheme of skilled scales subject to passing 
the trade test prescribed and conducted by the Port, failing which senior Mechanic with 10 years regular service put 
together in the grades of Senior Mechanic and Mechanic/ Assistant Mechanic / Blacksmith /Auto Electrician / Welder in 
the Port subject to passing the trade test prescribed and conducted by the Port.
(2) In the said Schedule under Regulations, in the Recruitment Rules for the post of Chargeman (Mechanical) of 
(Mechanical & Electrical Engineering Department) (Sl.No.94), the provisions under Column No. 12 shall be substituted 
by the following:-
Promotion:
Assistant Chargeman (Mech) with 5 years regular service failing which Sr. Mechanic / Sr. Fitter / Sr. Platter / Operator 
Special Grade with 5 years regular service in the grade in the port.
(3) In the said Schedule under Regulations, in the Recruitment Rules for the post of Operator Gr-I of (Mechanical & 
Electrical Engineering Department) (Sl.No.101), the provisions under Column No.12 shall be substituted by the following:-
Promotion:
 
 
(i) Operator Grade II with three years regular service in the grade in the port.
 
 
(ii) Should pass the trade test prescribed and conducted by the port

Notiﬁ cation regarding exchange of land pertaining to Nanjai Edaiyar Sankara Kandaswami Kandar Trust, 
Paramathi Velur, Namakkal District.
(ந.க. எண். 141/A4/2021)
NO.SRO C-8/2022. 
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section 4 of section 45 of Tamil Nadu Vanniyakula Kshatriya Public 
Charitable Trusts and Endowments [Protection and Maintenance] Act, 2018 and as per the resolution passed in the 
15th Board Meeting held on 08.10.2021, the Tamil Nadu Vanniyakula Kshatriya Public Charitable Trusts and Endowments 
Board has approved for the exchange of the land described below between the Nanjai Edaiyar Sankara Kandaswami 
Kandar Trust, Paramathi Velur, Namakkal District and Tmt. M. Soundaram W/o Thiru Rakkiannan, since the Board and 
Trust had not received any objection or suggestion within the prescribed period mentioned in the Notiﬁ cation No.29 
Dated: 21.07.2021 published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
