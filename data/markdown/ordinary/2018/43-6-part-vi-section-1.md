Appointment of Oﬃ  cial Liquidator
S. 1709 P. Mannarpalayam Labour Contract Co-operative Society Ltd., Salem District.
(Rc. No. 3727/PACCS/2012)
No.VI(1)/381/2018.
Under the powers conferred on the Deputy Registrar of Co-operatives under section 137(2)(b)-of the Tamil Nadu 
Co-operative Societies Act, 1983 (Act 30 of 1983) the activities of the P. Mannarpalayam Labour contract Co-operative 
Society Ltd., S. 1709 Salem District have been ordered to be wound up with immediate eﬀ ect by the Deputy Registrar 
of Co-operative Societies, Salem Circle, Salem vide Proc.No. 3727/2012/ PACCS Date 24-05-2013.
Under Section 138(1) of the same Act, Co-operative Sub Registrar/Oﬃ  cial Liquidator, Oﬃ  ce of the Deputy Registrar 
of Co-operative Societies, Salem Circle, Salem has been appointed as Oﬃ  cial Liquidator of the said society for the 
purpose of liquidation.
S. 1294 Sri Rajendra Akilandeshwari Mills Employees Co-operative Credit Society Ltd., Salem District.
(Rc. No. 3710/2013/NAVA)
No.VI(1)/382/2018.
Under the powers conferred on the Deputy Registrar of Co-operatives under section 137(2)(b)-of the Tamil Nadu 
Co-operative Societies Act, 1983 (Act 30 of 1983) the activities of the Sri Rajendra Akilandeshwari Mills Employees 
Co-operative Credit Society Ltd., S. 1294-Salem District have been ordered to be wound up with immediate eﬀ ect by 
the Deputy Registrar  of Co-operative Societies, Salem Circle, Salem vide Proc.No. 3710/2013/NAVA Dated: 24-05-2013.
Under Section 138(1) of the same Act, Co-operative Sub Registrar/Oﬃ  cial Liquidator, Oﬃ  ce of the Deputy Registrar 
of Co-operative Societies, Salem Circle, Salem has been appointed as oﬃ  cial Liquidator of the said society for the 
purpose of liquidation.
