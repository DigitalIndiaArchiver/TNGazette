No. SRO B-56/2015.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Secretariat Service
(Section 29 in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Service Manual, 1986):—
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 24th December 2012.
AMENDMENT
In the said Special Rules, in rule 14, in the "TABLE", for the entries “(i) Minimum general educational qualification
prescribed in Schedule I to the General Rules in Part II of the Tamil Nadu State and Subordinate Service Rules or its equivalent
qualifications” and “(a) Minimum general educational qualification in column (3), against the “Category-2A Personal Assistants
in the departments of Secretariat other than Finance Department” in column (1), and the corresponding entries “Recruitment
by transfer" and "Promotion" in  column (2) thereof, the entries "(i) A Bachelor's Degree" and "(a) A Bachelor's Degree" shall
respectively, be substituted.
P.W.C. DAVIDAR,
Principal Secretary to Government.
