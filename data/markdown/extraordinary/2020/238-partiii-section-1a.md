The following amendments are made to the Law Oﬃ  cers of High Court of Madras and its Bench at Madurai (Appointment) 
Rules, 2017:- 
 
2. The amendments hereby made shall come into force on the 16th June 2020. 
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖගඛ
In the said Rules,-
(1) in rule 2,- 
  
(a) for clause (f), the following clause shall be substituted, namely:-
 
“(f) “Government Pleader” means an Advocate appointed by the Government to assist the Advocate General and 
to conduct civil cases and prepare pleadings for and on behalf of State in the Madurai Bench of Madras High Court”.;
  
 (b) after clause (k), the following clause shall be added, namely:-
  
 
“(l) “State Government Pleader” means an Advocate appointed by the Government to assist the Advocate 
General and to conduct civil cases and prepare pleadings for and on behalf of the State both in the High Court of Madras 
and its Bench at Madurai.”;
