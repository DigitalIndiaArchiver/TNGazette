No. II(2)/MAWS/840(e-1)/2021.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (3) of Section 4 of the Tamil Nadu District 
Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby declares the 
entire area comprised in the following local authorities to be the Kollencode Municipality, the draft of the 
same having been previously published, as required under sub-section (1) of the said section 4:—
Sl.No.
Name of the local authority
(1)
Kollencode Town Panchayat
(2)
Ezhudesam Town Panchayat
Based on the above declaration, wards will be divided for the next ordinary election.

In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (3) of Section 4 of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 
1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby declares the entire area comprised in the following 
local authorities to be the Pugalur Municipality, the draft of the same having been previously published, as required 
under sub-section (1) of the said section 4:— 
Sl.No.
Name of the local authority
(1)
Punjai Pugalur Town Panchayat
(2)
TNPL Pugalur Town Panchayat
Based on the above declaration, wards will be divided for the next ordinary election.
