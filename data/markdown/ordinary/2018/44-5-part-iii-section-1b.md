No. SRO B-92/2018.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu makes the following Adhoc Rules:-
2.  (i) The Rules hereby made for the temporary posts of console operator, Junior Draughting Officer and Driver shall 
be deemed to have come into force on the 13th October, 1995. 
	
(ii) The Rules hereby made for the temporary post of Jeep Driver shall be deemed to have come into force on the 
7th May, 1997. 
Rules
The Tamil Nadu Government Servants (Conditions of Service) Act, 2016 (Tamil Nadu Act 14 of 2016) and the Special 
Rules applicable to the holders of the permanent posts in the Tamil Nadu Forest Subordinate Service shall apply to the holders 
of temporary posts of Console Operator, Junior Draughting Officer, Driver and Jeep Driver in the Environment Department, 
subject to the modifications specified in the following rules. 
2. Constitution:- The posts shall constitute as separate categories in a distinct class of the said service. 
3. Appointment:- Appointment to the posts specified in Column (1) of the Table below shall be made by the methods 
specified in the corresponding entries in Column (2) thereof.

5. Appointing Authority:- The appointing authority for the said posts shall be the Director of Environment.

or will complete the age of thirty years on the first day of July of the year in which the selection for appointment is made.

7. Probation:- Every person appointed to the posts by direct recruitment, shall from the date on which he joins duty, 
be on probation for a total period of two years on duty within a continuous period of three years: 
Provided that every person appointed to the post by recruitment by transfer, shall from the date on which he joins duty, 
be on probation for a total period of one year on duty within a continuous period of two years. 
8. Preparation of annual list of approved candidates:- The list of approved candidates for appointment to the posts 
by recruitment by transfer, shall be prepared annually and the crucial date on which the candidates should have acquired 
the prescribed qualification, shall be 1st December of every year.
