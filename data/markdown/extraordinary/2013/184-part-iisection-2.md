HOME DEPARTMENT
DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF THE TAMIL NADU SPECIAL POLICE YOUTH BRIGADE ACT, 2013.
[G.O. (Ms) No. 431, Home (Pol.XIV), 1st July 2013.]
No. II(2)/HO/448(c)/2013.
Under sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Special Police Youth Brigade Act, 2013 (Tamil Nadu
Act 3 of 2013), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints 1st day of July 2013 as the date on which the said Act
shall come into force.
NIRANJAN MARDI,
Principal Secretary to Government.
