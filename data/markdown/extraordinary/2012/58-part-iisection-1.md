“In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-clause (a) of clause (2) of Article 174 of the Constitution
of India, I, Dr. K. ROSAIAH, Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby prorogue with effect from the
date of this notification, the session of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly which commenced on the
30th January 2012.

K. ROSAIAH,
