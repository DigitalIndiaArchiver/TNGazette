INDUSTRIES, INVESTMENT PROMOTION AND COMMERCE DEPARTMENT
AMENDMENT TO THE TAMIL NADU BUSINESS FACILITATION RULES, 2017.
[G.O. Ms. No. 59, Industries, Investment Promotion and Commerce (MIB.1), 10th March 2023, 
மாசி 26, சுபகிருது, திருவள்ளுவர் ஆண்டு-2054.]
No. SRO A-10(b)/2023.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of section 34 of the Tamil Nadu Business Facilitation 
Act, 2018 (Tamil Nadu Act 7 of 2018), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the 
Tamil Nadu Business Facilitation Rules, 2017, namely:-
Amendment.
In the said Rules,  for rule 13, the following rule shall be substituted, namely:-
“13. Deemed approvals.- The provisions of approval under sub-section (1) of Section 12 of the Act 
shall be applicable to the clearances specified in S.No.2.1.1 to 2.1.8 in Annexure 2.1, S.No.2.2.1, 
S.No.2.2.2 and S.No.2.2.9 in Annexure 2.2 and S.No.2.5.1, S.No.2.5.2, S.No.2.5.77, S.No.2.5.78 
and S.No.2.5.135 in Annexure 2.5 to these rules, in case the Competent Authority concerned fails 
to decide on the application within the time limit specified under rule 12. The deemed approval 
shall be issued to the applicant under the name and seal of the Competent Authority concerned, 
through single window portal with a copy to the respective Competent Authority, after obtaining 
concurrence of the Respective Committee”.
	
S. KRISHNAN,	
	
	
	
	
Additional Chief Secretary to Government.
