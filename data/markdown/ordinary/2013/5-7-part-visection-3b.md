The election will be held in accordance with rules and procedures for conduct of election by the University authorities.
Nominations should be made in the prescribed form which may be obtained from the Returning Officer. Nomination
papers shall be enclosed in an envelope superscribed “Nomination to the Senate by Teachers of each affiliated College",
"Nomination to the Senate by the Secretaries of the College Committees of the Private Colleges, as defined in the
Tamil Nadu Private Colleges (Regulation) Act, 1976 within the University Area" and "Nomination to the Senate by Headmasters
of Completed Higher Secondary Schools" as indicated below and sent by Registered Post with Acknowledgement due, so
as to be received by the Returning Officer within the date and hour fixed or delivered either in person or by messenger to the
Returining Officer within the last date and hour fixed. Nomination papers that are not enclosed in an envelope superscribed and
sent by Registered Post or are not delivered in person or by messenger as required above shall be declared invalid. An elector
shall be eligible to subscribe either as proposer or as seconder in nomination papers only as many times as there are vacancies
but not more.
