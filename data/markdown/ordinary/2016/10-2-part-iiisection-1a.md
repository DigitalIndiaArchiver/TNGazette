Amendments to the Tamil Nadu Ministers and Presiding Officers of Legislature (Furnishing of Private Houses and
Provision of Amenities) Rules, 1977.

No. SRO A-4/2016.—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 14 read with sub-section (2)
of Section 6-B of the Tamil Nadu payment of Salaries Act, 1951 (Tamil Nadu Act XX of 1951), the Governor of Tamil
Nadu hereby makes the following  amendments to the Tamil Nadu Ministers and Presiding Officers of Legislature
(Furnishing of Private Houses and Provision of Amenities) Rules, 1977.
2.
The amendments hereby made shall come into force on the 3rd March 2016.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules, in rule 1,—
(i) in sub-rule (2), for the expression “twenty thousand rupees”, the expression “seventy thousand rupees” shall
be substituted;
(ii) in sub-rule (3), for the expression “twenty thousand rupees”, the expression “seventy thousand rupees” shall
be substituted.
