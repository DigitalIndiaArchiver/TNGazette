Tamil Nadu State Election Commission has been sought
for, regarding the disqualification of Tmt. Ananthanayaki
Murugavel,  President, Vandalai Gudalur Village Panchayat,
Pullampadi Panchayat Union, Tiruchirappalli District as required
under sub-section (2) of Section 41 of the Tamil Nadu

is having interest in a subsisting contract work of construction

Development Scheme to Vandalai Gudalur Village by awarding
the said work in favour of her husband.

President, Vandalai Gudalur Village Panchayat has failed in
her public duties and allowed her family to get benefit
therefor. The said Commission is accordingly of the opinion
that the said President, Vandalai Gudalur Village Panchayat,
Pullampadi Panchayat Union, Tiruchirappalli District is
disqualified to continue as President of the said Vandalai
Gudalur Village Panchayat, under clause (c) of
sub-section 3 of Section 38 of the said Act;

Village Panchayat, Pullampadi Panchayat Union, Tiruchirappalli
District  has become disqualified from holding the office of
the President of the said Vandalai Gudalur Village Panchayat,

Act as she has failed in her public duties by having acquired
interest in a subsisting contract work of construction of
overhead tank to Vandalai Gudalur Village awarded in favour
of her husband.
