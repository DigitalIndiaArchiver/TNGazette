Nomination of Chairman for the High Court Legal Services Committee
(TNSLSA No. 2259/E/2021)
No. VI(2)/67/2021.
In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (2) of Section 8A of the Legal Services 
Authorities Act, 1987, (Central Act 39 of 1987) the Hon’ble the Chief Justice, High Court, Madras is pleased  to nominate 
Hon’ble Thiru Justice T. Raja, Judge, High Court, Madras, to be the Chairman of the High Court Legal Services 
Committee with eﬀ ect from 19-06-2021.
