MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION AND WATER SUPPLY DEPARTMENT
UPGRADATION OF HOSUR SELECTION GRADE MUNICIPALITY CLASSIFIED AS SPECIAL GRADE
MUNICIPALITY IN KRISHNAGIRI DISTRICT UNDER THE TAMIL NADU DISTRICT MUNICIPALITIES ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 62, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (MA.5), 25th June 2012.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/319(f)/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (6) of Section 4 of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act,
1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby classifies the Hosur Selection Grade
Municipality in Krishnagiri District into Special Grade Municipality.
SHEELA BALAKRISHNAN,
Additional Chief Secretary to Government.
