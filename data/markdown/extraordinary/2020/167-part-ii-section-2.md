2. Now, based on the proposal of Principal Secretary/Commissioner of Revenue Administration, the Government after 
careful examination ordered to consider permitting the select additional activities in the non containment zones by invoking 
the permissions available, for such activities under item  10, 11, 16, 18 and 19 in the revised consolidated guidelines of the 
Ministry of Home affairs on 15.4.2020 with further restrictions.
List of proposed items within the permitted items in the consolidated instructions issued in the Ministry of Home Affairs 
order read in the reference third read above.
	
1.	 MNREGA works, especially those related to irrigation and water conservation in rural areas excluding containment 
zones and  Red Blocks listed in the annexure I (as updated from time to time by the Health and Family Welfare Department) 
subject to the condition that the workers should be provided with masks and not more than 1/3rd of the required number of 
labourers should be mobilized and strict social distancing guidelines should be followed.
	
2.	 Construction projects in rural areas including irrigation, de-silting of tanks and channels, ongoing works with 
regard to dam safety, buildings (including the construction and maintenance of Medical Colleges and hospitals), roads and 
bridges, water supply and sanitation, brick kilns, hardware material supply on call basis, electricity related works (generation, 
transmission and distribution). 
	
3.	 State/Central Government offices for the maintenance of essential services with attendance not exceeding 33 
percent of the total staff.
Before allowing these select additional activities the district administration shall ensure that all preparatory arrangements with 
regard to the standard operating procedures (SOPs) for social distancing in offices, work places, factories and establishments 
as also other sectoral requirements are in place.
(By order of the Governor)
