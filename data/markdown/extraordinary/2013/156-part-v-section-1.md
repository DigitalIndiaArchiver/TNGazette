PUBLIC (ELECTIONS) DEPARTMENT
BIENNIAL ELECTIONS TO COUNCIL OF STATES TO FILL VACANCIES OF MEMBERS
RETIRING DURING JULY, 2013.
Secretariat, 10th June 2013.
No. SRO G-1/2013.
The following Notification of the Government of India, Ministry of Law and Justice, Legislative Department,
New Delhi, dated 10th June, 2013 [Jyaistha 20, 1935 (Saka)] is republished:—
S.O. (E).—In pursuance of Section 12 of the Representation of the People Act, 1951 (43 of 1951),
the President is pleased to call upon the elected members of the Legislative Assembly of the
State of Tamil Nadu to elect, in accordance with the provisions of the said Act and of the rules and orders
made thereunder, members for the purpose of filling the seats of six (6) members of the
Council of States whose term of office is due to expire on the 24th July, 2013 on the expiration of their
term of office.
