BIENNIAL ELECTIONS TO COUNCIL OF STATES TO FILL VACANCIES OF
MEMBERS RETIRING DURING JUNE, 2016
No. SRO D-3/ 2016.
The following Notification of the Government of India, Ministry of Law and Justice, Legislative Department, New Delhi,
dated 24th May, 2016 [Jyaistha 3, 1938 (Saka)] is republished:-
S.O.........(E).—In pursuance of Section 12 of the Representation of the People Act, 1951 (43 of 1951), the President is
pleased to call upon the elected members of the Legislative Assembly of the following 15 States to elect, in accordance
with the provisions of the said Act and of the rules and orders made thereunder, 57 new members for the purpose of filling
the seats which are due to fall vacant (a) on the retirement of members of the Council of States, on the expiration of their
term of office; and (b) resignation of one member each from Karnataka and Rajasthan, as per details given below:-
