MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION AND WATER SUPPLY DEPARTMENT
FIXING THE NUMBER OF COUNCILLORS TO BE ELECTED TO THE COUNCILS OF THE NEWLY CONSTITUTED 
CITY MUNICIPAL CORPORATIONS OF SIVAKASI, TAMBARAM, EXPANDED CITY MUNICIPAL CORPORATION 
OF NAGERCOIL AND 28 MUNICIPALITIES - ORDERS - NOTIFIED.
[G.O.Ms. No.127, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (Election), 16th December 2021, 
No. II(2)/MAWS/882(b-1)/2021
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of section 5 of the Sivakasi City Municipal Corporation 
Ordinance, 2021 (Tamil Nadu Ordinance 3 of 2021), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby ﬁ xes the number of councillors 
to be elected to the council of the Municipal Corporation of Sivakasi as forty eight (48), with eﬀ ect on and from the 
next ordinary election.
NOTIFICATION-II
[G.O.Ms. No.127, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (Election), 16th December 2021, 
No. II(2)/MAWS/ 882(b-2)/2021
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of section 5 of the Tambaram City Municipal Corporation 
Ordinance, 2021 (Tamil Nadu Ordinance 5 of 2021), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby ﬁ xes the number of councillors 
to be elected to the council of the Municipal Corporation of Tambaram as seventy (70), with eﬀ ect on and from the 
next ordinary election.
NOTIFICATION-III
[G.O.Ms. No.127, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (Election), 16th December 2021, 
No. II(2)/MAWS/ 882(b-3)/2021
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of section 5 of the Nagercoil City Municipal Corporation Act, 
2019 (Tamil Nadu Act 11 of 2019), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby ﬁ xes the number of councillors to be elected to 
the council of the Municipal Corporation of Nagercoil as ﬁ fty two (52), with eﬀ ect on and from the next ordinary election.
