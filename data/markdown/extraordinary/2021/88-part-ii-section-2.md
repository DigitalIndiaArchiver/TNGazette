In the G.O ﬁ rst read above, orders were issued freezing of administrative units/boundaries viz., municipalities, 
revenue villages, towns, tehsils, police stations,  sub-divisions, districts etc., and  ban on formation of new units, from 
1st January 2020 to 31st March 2021.
2. In the G.O. 2nd read above, the date of freezing of boundaries was extended upto 31.12.2020.  
3. In the letter 3rd read above, the Registrar General and Census Commissioner, India has stated that owing to 
the prevailing conditions due to COVID-19 pandemic in the country the time period for conducting the ensuing census 
is not yet decided. In view of this, the date of freezing of boundaries has been further extended upto 31.03.2021 and 
therefore has requested to issue necessary instructions to all concerned in this State to give eﬀ ect to the changes in 
the administrative boundaries, if any latest by 31.03.2021.

4. In view of the circumstances stated above, the Government of Tamil Nadu hereby extend the date of freezing 
of administrative boundaries of all the municipalities, revenue villages, towns, tehsils, police stations, sub-divisions, 
districts, etc.,  and ban on formation of new units in the State of Tamil Nadu upto 31st March, 2021. The boundaries 
of administrative units for the ensuing Census will now be frozen from 31st March 2021 to till the completion of Census. 
5. All the pending proposals, if any aﬀ ect the changes in the existing boundaries of municipalities, revenue villages, 
towns, tehsils, police stations, sub-divisions, districts etc., may therefore be ﬁ nalized on or before 31st March 2021 under 
intimation to the Director of Census Operations, Tamil Nadu. 
6. The Commissioner of Revenue Administration, Chennai, the Director General of Police, Chennai, the Commissioner 
of Rural Development and Panchayat Raj, Chennai, the Director of Municipal Administration, Chennai, the Director of Town 
Panchayats, Chennai, the Collectors of all Districts, the Commissioner of Greater Chennai Corporation, the Commissioner 
of Trichy, Madurai, Salem, Coimbatore,  Tirunelveli, Vellore, Thoothukudi, Tirupur, Erode, Thanjavur, Dindigul, Nagercoil, 
Hosur and Avadi Municipal Corporations are directed to adhere to the above decision of the Government.
