LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT  DEPARTMENT
 Amendments to the Tamil Nadu Trade Unions Regulations, 1927.
[G.O. Ms. No. 100, Labour and Employment (K2), 8th November 2011.]
No. SRO A-22/2011.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 29 of the Trade Unions Act, 1926
(Central Act XVI of 1926), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Tamil Nadu Trade
Unions Regulations, 1927, the draft of the same having been previously published as required under sub-sections (1) and
(2) of Section 30 of the said Act:—
AMENDMENTS.
In the said Regulations, in Form E,—
(1) in the heading for the expression "31st March, 19............", the expression "31st December, 20.............." shall
be substituted;
(2) in Part A, in item 15, for the expression "31st March 19..........", the expression "31st December 20........" shall be
substituted;
(3) in Part D, in the heading, for the expression "the 31st day of March 19.......", the expression "the 31st day
of December 20..........." shall be substituted.
MOHAN PYARE,
Principal Secretary to Government.
