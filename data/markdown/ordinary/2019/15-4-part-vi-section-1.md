Constitution of a Separate Judicial Magistrate Court at Eraniel by Converting the Existing Additional District 
Munsif Court at Eraniel and a Separate District Munsif Court at Eraniel by Converting the Existing Principal District 
Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Eraniel, in Kanniyakumari District.
(Roc. No. 274/2013/G/Judn.)
No. VI(1)/177/2019.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873), 
the High Court, Madras, hereby ﬁ xes Eraniel in Kanniyakumari District as the place at which the District Munsif Court, 
Eraniel, shall be located.
NOTIFICATION-II
(Roc. No. 274/2013/G/Judn.)
No. VI(1)/178/2019.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 11 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 
1873), the High Court, Madras, here by directs that in  Kanniyakumari District, the District Munsif Court, Eraniel, 
shall have local jurisdiction over Kurunthancode Firka and Colachel Firka and over the Villages viz., Eraniel, Nullivilai 
A, Nullivilai B, Aathivilai of Thiruvithamcode Firka, of Kalkulam Taluk with eﬀ ect from the date of constitution of a separate 
District Munsif Court at Eraniel by converting the existing Principal District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Eraniel.
NOTIFICATION-III
(Roc. No. 274/2013/G/Judn.)
No. VI(1)/179/2019.
The High Court, Madras, hereby directs and notiﬁ es that the District Munsif, Eraniel in Kanniyakumari District, 
shall exercise all the powers conferred on a District Munsif under Section 12 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 
(Central Act III of 1873) as amended up-to-date in regard to original suits and proceedings not otherwise exempted 
from his cognizance of which the amount or value of the subject matter does not exceed One Lakh Rupees within the 
local limits of his jurisdiction with eﬀ ect from the date of constitution of a separate District Munsif Court at Eraniel by 
converting the existing Principal District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Eraniel.

NOTIFICATION FOR RE-DESIGNATION OF COURTS AT ERANIEL IN KANNIYAKUMARI DISTRICT
(Roc. No. 274/2013/G/Judn.)
No. VI(1)/181/2019.
Whereas the Government of Tamil Nadu have issued orders in G.O. Ms. No.132, Home (Cts.II) Department, dated 
14-2-2017 that a Separate Judicial Magistrate Court at Eraniel be constituted by converting the existing Additional District 
Munsif Court, Eraniel and in G.O.Ms. No. 297, Home (Courts-III) Department, dated 5-4-2017 have issued orders that 
the existing Principal District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Eraniel by Converted as Separate District Munsif 
Court at Eraniel.
Hence, it is hereby informed that on and from the date on which the District Munsif Court and Judicial Magistrate 
Court at Eraniel are constituted, the existing Principal District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Eraniel shall 
be re-designated as District Munsif Court, Eraniel and the existing Additional District Munsif Court, Eraniel shall be 
re-designated as Judicial Magistrate Court, Eraniel.
High Court, Madras, 
C. KUMARAPPAN,
26th March 2019.
