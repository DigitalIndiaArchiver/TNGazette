FIXING THE NUMBER OF MEMBERS OF THE COUNCIL OF DHALI TOWN PANCHAYAT

No. VI(2)/133(a)/2021.
NOTIFICATION
WHEREAS Kurumalai, Melkurumalai, Poochikottamparai and Thirumoorthimalai settlement areas of Dhali-2 
Revenue Village were included with the local authority of Dhali Town Panchayat in Tiruppur District by 
G.O.(Ms)No.110, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (Election) Department, dated the 25th November 
2021 and published in Part II – Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, Extraordinary, dated 
the 25th November 2021;
AND WHEREAS, under clause (11-B) of section 3 of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 
1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920), the Director of Town Panchayats  has been appointed by the State 
Government to exercise and perform the powers and duties of the Inspector in respect of Town Panchayats, 
under the said Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920 in G.O (Ms) No.150, Municipal Administration and Water Supply 
Department, dated 31st May 1994 and published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, Extraordinary, 
dated the 31st May 1994;
AND WHEREAS the average distance between Dhali Town Panchayat and the included settlement 
areas of Dhali-2 Revenue Village (except Thirumoorthimalai Settlement) is 63 kilometres and therefore the 
Inspector proposed to create two additional wards exclusively for the inhabitants of the said settlement 
areas and accordingly sent proposal to Delimitation Commission;
AND WHEREAS the Delimitation Commission constituted under the Tamil Nadu Delimitation Commission 
Act, 2017 (Tamil Nadu Act 23 of 2017) has submitted its recommendation to State Government under 
section 4(1)(a) of the said Tamil Nadu Act 23 of 2017 for increasing the total number of members of the 
council of the Dhali Town Panchayat from 15 members to 17 members;

AND WHEREAS, based on the recommendation of Delimitation Commission, the State Government have amended 
rule 2 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats (Fixation of Strength of Councillors) Rules, 1996 by G.O.(Ms)No.132, 
Municipal Administration and Water Supply (Election) Department, dated the 22nd December 2021 and published in 
Part III – Section 1(a) of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, Extraordinary, dated the 22nd December 2021 to enable 
the Inspector to increase the number of councillors not exceeding two in a Town Panchayat with the prior approval of 
State Government for reasons to be recorded in writing;
AND WHEREAS, it is considered that the number of councillors for Dhali Town Panchayat may be increased from 
15 to 17, for the following reasons, namely:—
(a) the four settlement areas, recently included within the local authority of Dhali Town Panchayat are in hilly 
regions;
(b) the distance between the settlement areas from Dhali Town Panchayat except Thirumoorthimalai is not less 
than seventy three kilometres;
(c) the said Thirumoorthimalai settlement area is at a distance of ﬁ fty ﬁ ve kilometres from the other settlement 
areas;
(d) to provide two wards for the said settlement areas;
AND WHEREAS, the Government of Tamil Nadu in G.O.(Ms)No.132, Municipal Administration and Water Supply 
Department, dated 22.12.2021 has granted prior approval to increase the number of councillors for Dhali Town Panchayat 
from 15 to 17.
NOW, THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of section 3-T of the Tamil Nadu 
District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920), read with the proviso to rule 2 of the Tamil Nadu Town 
Panchayats (Fixation of Strength of Councillors) Rules, 1996, the Inspector, with the prior approval of the Government of 
Tamil Nadu hereby ﬁ xes the number of members of the council of Dhali Town Panchayat in Tiruppur District, inclusive 
of the Chairman, as seventeen (17), with eﬀ ect on and from the next ordinary election.
