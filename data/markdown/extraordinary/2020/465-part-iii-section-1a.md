In exercise of the powers conferred by Sub-Section (1) and (2) of Section 23-C of the Mines and Minerals 
(Development and Regulation) Act, 1957 (Central Act 67 of 1957), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes 
the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu Prevention of Illegal Mining, Transportation and Storage of Minerals 
and Mineral Dealers Rules, 2011.
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖගඛ
In the said rules, -
(1) 
in rule 4, in sub-rule (1), for the expression “Rs.5000/- (Rupees ﬁ ve thousand only)”, the expression 
“Rs.10000/- (Rupees ten thousand only)” shall be substituted;
(2) 
in rule 5, in sub rule (3), -
(i)  in clause (b), for the expression “Rs.5000/- (Rupees ﬁ ve thousands only)”, the expression 
“Rs.10000/- (Rupees ten thousand only)” shall be substituted; 
(ii)  in clause (c), for the expression “ﬁ ve years”, the expression “ten years” shall be substituted.
