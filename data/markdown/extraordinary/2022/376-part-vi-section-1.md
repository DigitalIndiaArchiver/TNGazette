No. VI(1)/288(d-1)/2022.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that the lands specified in the schedule below to be acquired 
for highways purpose to wit to for the formation of road widening of six-lane from four-lane from Thirunindravur to Padi in 
Chennai – Tiruthani – Renigunda Road and it having already been decided that the amount of compensation to be awarded 
for the lands is to be paid out of the lands controlled or managed by the Chief Engineer (Construction and Maintenance), 
Highways Department The following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001. 
(Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002)
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001. (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) the Governor of 
Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands specified in the Schedule below measuring 6938 sq.mtrs to the same, a little more or 
less needed for industrial purpose wit for the formation of road widening of six-lane from four-lane from Thirunindravur to Padi 
in Chennai – Tiruthani – Renigunda Road.
The plan of lands is kept in the office of the Special District Revenue Officer(LA), Chennai - Tiruttani – Renigunda Road 
Widening Project, Avadi, Chennai-54 and in the office of the Special Tahsildar, (LA), CTRRP, Avadi  may be inspected at any 
time  during office hours.
