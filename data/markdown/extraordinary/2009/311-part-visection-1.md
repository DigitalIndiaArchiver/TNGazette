JUDICIAL NOTIFICATION
DRAFT  NOTIFICATION
CONSTITUTION OF SUB COURT AT PERUNDURAI BY SHIFTING THE EXISTING II-ADDITIONAL
SUB COURT, GOBICHETTIPALAYAM UNDER TAMIL NADU CIVIL COURTS ACT.
(Roc. No. 753/2007/G-Judn.)
No. VI(1)/356(a)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred under the first proviso to Section 13 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts
Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873) the High Court, Madras, hereby directs that appeals from the decrees or
orders of the District Munsif Court, Perundurai be preferred in the Court of Subordinate Judge, Perundurai with
effect from the date on which the Subordinate Judge, Perundurai assumes charge of that Court.
High Court, Madras,
A. ARUMUGHA SWAMY,
1st December 2009.
