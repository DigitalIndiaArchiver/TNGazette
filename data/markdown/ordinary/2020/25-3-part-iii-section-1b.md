No. SRO B-18/2020.— In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (3) of Article 187 of the 
Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu, after consultation with the Speaker of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly, 
hereby makes the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Secretariat Service Rules.
2. The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on 2nd February, 2018.
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖගඛ
 
In the said Rules,-
 
(1) in rule 4, under the heading “Class II- Non Gazetted Superior posts”, for the entry “Category 2- Reporters”, the 
following entries shall be substituted, namely:-
 
“Category 2A- English Reporter.
 
 Category 2B- Tamil Reporter”.
 
(2) in rule 6-A in the tabular column, in column (1), for the entries, “2 (a) English Reporters in category 2 of Class 
II of the Service” and “(b) Tamil Reporters in Category 2 of Class II of the service”, the entries “2A English Reporter in 
category 2A of class II of the service” and “2B Tamil Reporter in category 2B of Class II of the service” shall, respectively, 
be substituted;
 
(3) in rule 9, in the ﬁ rst proviso, for clause (c), the following clause shall be substituted, namely:- 
“(c) by promotion to the categories of English Reporter and Tamil Reporter”; 
 
(4) in rule 19, under the heading “(2) Class-II-Non-Gazetted Superior Posts”, for the entry “Category 2-Reporters”, 
the following entries shall be substituted, namely:-
 
“Category 2A-English Reporter 
 
 Category 2B-Tamil Reporter”; 
 
(5) in APPENDIX-I, in the tabular column, against Class II in column (1), for the entry “2. Reporters”, in column (2) 
thereof, the following entries shall be substituted, namely:-
 
“2A- English Reporter
 
 2B- Tamil Reporter”;
 
(6) in APPENDIX-II, in the tabular column, against Class II in column (1) and category 2 in column (2), for the entry, 
“Reporters”, in column (3) thereof, the following entries shall be substituted, namely:- 
 
“2A- English Reporter
 
 2B- Tamil Reporter”;
 
(7) in APPENDIX-III, in the tabular column, against Class II  in column (1),- 
(a) against category 2A in column (2) and the post “English Reporter” in column (3) and the corresponding entry “Promotion 
or transfer” in column (4) thereof, in column (5), for item (i), the following item shall be substituted, namely:-
(i) “Must hold a Bachelor’s degree granted by any university or Institution recognized by the University Grants Commission”;
(b) in column (2), for the entry “2(b)”, the entry “2B”, shall be substituted;
(c) against the entry 2B in column (2),  as so substituted, and the post “Tamil Reporter” in column (3) and the corresponding 
entry “(i) Promotion or transfer” in column (4) thereof, in column (5), for item (i), the following item shall be substituted, namely:-
(i) “Must hold a Bachelor’s degree granted by any university or Institution recognized by the University Grants Commission”;
 
(8) in APPENDIX-IV, against Class II in column (1), for “Category” 2 in column (2), and the post “Reporter” in column 
(3) thereof, the following categories and posts shall, respectively, be substituted, namely:-
 
“2A English Reporter
 
 2B Tamil Reporter”.
