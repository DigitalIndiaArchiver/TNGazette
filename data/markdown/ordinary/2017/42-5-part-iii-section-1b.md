MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES DEPARTMENT
Amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Industries Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 62,  Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (E.I.1), 20th September 2017, ¹ó†ì£C 4, «ýM÷‹H,
F¼õœÀõ˜ Ý‡´-2048.]
No. SRO B-64/2017.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Industries
Service (Section 19 of Part III-A of the Special Rules for the State Services in Volume II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual,
2016).
2. The amendments hereby made shall come in to force on the 20th September 2017.
AMENDMENTS
In rule 4 (b) of the said Special Rules, in the Annexure, for the entries in column (3), against the category “Senior Chemist”
in column (1) and the method of appointment “Direct recruitment” in column (2) thereof, the following entries shall be substituted,
namely:-
“1. A Post Graduate degree in Chemistry or Industrial Chemistry or an Associateship Diploma of the Institute of Chemists
(India), and.
2
Practical experience in the Analysis of Industrial Products for a period of not less than 3 years:
Provided that other things are being equal, preference shall be given to the candidate who possess, in addition, all
or any of the following qualifications:-
(i) Publication of Scientific Papers;
(ii) Teaching experience in chemistry at H.Sc or its equivalent standard or higher standard for a period of two years
from the Institution recognized by the Government. The above experience should be obtained  after acquiring the prescribed
educational qualifications and from an authority competent to issue such certificate”.
MANGAT RAM SHARMA,
Prinicipal Secretary to Government.

PERSONNEL AND ADMINISTRATIVE REFORMS DEPARTMENT
Amendments to the Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission Regulations.
[G.O. Ms. No. 125,  Personnel and Administrative Reforms (M), 19th September 2017, ¹ó†ì£C 3,
«ýM÷‹H, F¼õœÀõ˜ Ý‡´-2048.]
No. SRO B-65/2017.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Article 318 of the Constitution of India, the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission Regulations, 1954
(Volume-I of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1987).
AMENDMENTS
In the said Regulations, in ANNEXURE V, in Clause (2),-
(1) for sub-clause (a)(i) the following sub-clause shall be substituted, namely:-
“(a) (i) Every person appointed to the post of Assistant, Typist and Personal Clerk shall pass the test in the
Tamil Nadu Government Office Manual and shall complete the Foundational Training at Civil Services Training Institute,
Bhavanisagar and pass the test conducted at the end of training within the period of probation or within a period of two
years from the date of appointment where no probation is prescribed and also from promotion as Assistant Section Officer.
Every person appointed to the post of Assistant Section Officer-cum-Programmer by direct recruitment shall, from the
date on which he joins duty, be on probation for a total period of two years on duty within a continuous period of three years.”;
(2) for sub-clause (a)(ii), the following sub-clause shall be substituted, namely:-
“(a) (ii) Every person appointed to the post of Assistant Section Officer by direct recruitment shall pass the
Departmental Test in the Tamil Nadu Services Manual Volume-I and the Tamil Nadu Government Office Manual and shall
also complete the Foundational Training at Civil Services Training Institute, Bhavanisagar and pass the test conducted at
the end of training within the period of probation and also for promotion as Section Officer”.
     S. SWARNA,
Secretary to Government.

No. SRO B-66/2017.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,

Chennai District)”.

No. SRO B-67/2017.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the adhoc rules issued with Transport Department
Notification No. SRO B-289/79, Published at Pages 342 to 344 of Part III—Section 1(b) of the Tamil Nadu Government
Gazette, dated the 6th June 1979:—
2. The amendment (2) hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force with effect on and from the
17th May 2012.
AMENDMENTS
In the said adhoc rules,—
(1) for the expression, “Motor Vehicles Maintenance Organisation” wherever it occurs, the expression “Motor Vehicles
Maintenance Department” shall be substituted;
(2) for the expression “Works Manager” wherever it occurs, the expression “General Manager” shall be substituted;
(3) in rules 3, clause (2) shall be omitted;
(4) in rule 4 in the Table,-
(i) in column (2), against the entry (1) “By Promotion” in column (1), clause (1) shall be omitted; and
(ii) The entry “(2) Recruitment by Transfer” in column (1) and the corresponding entries relating thereto in
column (2) shall be omitted.

Additional Chief Secretary to Government.
