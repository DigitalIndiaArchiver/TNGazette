Grant of ‘No Objection Certificate
[Rc.No. E2(1)/1121/106886/2009.]
FORM “B”
(Rule 36 in Part-II and Rule 106 in Part VII)
No. VI(1)/263/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred by rule 36 in part – II of the Tamil Nadu Cinemas (Regulation)
Rule 1957, the Commissioner of Police, Chennai Police, Egmore, Chennai-8, hereby grants the certificate that
there is No Objection to Thiruvalargal Vijaya Productions Private Limited, “Citi Towers” 7th Floor, Thyagaraya
Road, T.Nagar, Chennai-17 for construction of a permanent Nine Screen Multiplex along with Food Court,
Restaurants, Shops and Commercial Units at T.S. Nos. 5/1, 5/3, 5/4, 5/5 and 5/7 in Block No. 5 of Saligramam
Village, Egmore-Nungambakkam Taluk, Chennai District with Door Nos. 183 to 190, N.S.K.Salai (Arcot Road),
Vadapalani, Chennai-26. The Site where the cinemas is to be located is at T.S.Nos. 5/1, 5/3, 5/4, 5/5 and 5/
7 in Block No. 5 of Saligramam Village, Egmore-Nungambakkam Taluk, Chennai District with Door Nos. 183 to
190, N.S.K.Salai, (Arcot Road), Vadapalani, Chennai-26.
The No Objection Certificate is valid for a period of five years in the case of permanent Cinema from the
date here of and if within this period a proposed Cinema is not put up, a fresh ‘No Objection Certificate’ should
be applied for Date this 24th day of June 2010.
Chennai-600 008,
         T. RAJENDRAN,
24th June 2010.
   Commissioner of Police,
    Chennai Police.

Variation to the Approved Thuraiyur Detailed Development Plan No. 7 of
Thuraiyur Local Planning Area.
[Roc.No. 2032/2010/DP3.]
No. VI(1)/264/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred under sub section (1) of Section 33 of Town and Country Planning Act, 1971,
(Act No. 35 of 1972) the Director of Town and Country Planning in his Proceedings Roc. No. 2032/2010 DP3, dated
16-6-2010, proposes to make the following variation to the Thuraiyur Detailed Development Plan
No. 7 of Thuraiyur Local Planning Area approved vide proceedings Roc. No. 38117/91 DP2, dated
14th January 1992 of Director of Town and Country Planning and published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette
No. 4, Part VI—Secton 1, Page No. 275, dated 11th March 1992.
2. Any person affected or interested in this draft variation may within SIXTY days from the date of publication of
this notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette can represent in person or submit in writing to the Member-
Secretary, Thuraiyur Local Planning Authority any objections and suggestions relating thereto.
3. The variation with plan may be inspected free of cost at any time during office hours in the above said Local
Planning Authority office.
VARIATION
1. The entire Thuraiyur Detailed Development Plan No. 7 of Thuraiyur Local Planning Area is comprehensively
varied by the Director of Town and Country Planning vide Proceedings Roc.No. 2032/2010 DP3, dated
16th June 2010.
2. The plan thus varied is numbered as Map No. 5, DDP(V)/DTCP No. 23/2010.
3. The Detailed Development Plan clauses and schedule annexed with this plan are made enforceable from the date
of publication of the confirmed variation notification to be issued under Section 33(2) of the Act and published in
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
Chennai,
 PANKAJ KUMAR BANSAL,
16th June 2010.
Director of Town and Country Planning.

No. VI(1)/265/2010.

Part VI—Section I, Page No. 1257, dated 28th December 1994.
2. Any person affected or interested in this draft variation may within SIXTY days from the date of publication of
this notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette can represent in preson or submit in writing to the Member-
Secretary, Melur Local Planning Authority and objections any suggestions relating thereto.
3. The variation with plan may be inspected free of cost at any time during office hours in the aboved said Local
Planning Authority office.
VARIATION

of Town and Country Planning in Proceedings Roc.No. 14483/94 DP2, dated 4th May 1994 is varied by Melur Detailed
Development Plan No. 4 of Melur Local Planning Area.
2. The plan thus varied is numbered as Map No. 5 DDP(V)/DTCP No. 24/2010.
3. The Detailed Development Plan clauses and schedules annexed with this plan are made enforceable from the
date of publication of the confirmed variation notification to be issued under Section 33(2) of the Act and published
in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
