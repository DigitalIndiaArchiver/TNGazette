The Government of Tamil Nadu, having been satisﬁ ed that the lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below have to be 
acquired for Industrial purpose, to ﬁ t, for the establishment of new SIPCOT Industrial Park by SIPCOT at Kannudaiyanpatti

lands to be paid out of the funds controlled or managed by SIPCOT Industrial Park by SIPCOT. The following notice 
is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of land for Industrial Purposes Act 1997 
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE 
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of land for Industrial Purposes Act 1997 
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Government of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below 
and measuring 3.68.0 Hectares (9.09 Acres) to the same, a little more or less needed for Industrial Purpose, to wit, for 
the establishment of new SIPCOT Industrial park by SIPCOT at Kannudaiyanpatti and surrounding areas.
The plans of the lands are kept in the oﬃ  ce of the District Collector, Tiruchirappalli and may be inspected at any
