NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
COMMERCIAL TAXES AND REGISTRATION  DEPARTMENT
  REDUCTION OF TAX RATE UNDER TAMIL NADU VALUE ADDED TAX ACT.
Cancellation of Notification
[G.O. Ms. No. 81, Commercial Taxes and Registration (B2), 12th July 2011,
Aani 27, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No. II(1)/CTR/12(s)/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (3) of Section 30 of the Tamil Nadu Value Added
Tax Act, 2006 (Tamil Nadu Act 32 of 2006), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby cancels the Commercial Taxes and
Registration Department Notification No. II(1)/CTR/12(R-27)/2011, published at page 15 of Part II—Section 1 of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 11th July 2011.
This notification shall come into force on the 12th July 2011.
SUNIL PALIWAL,
Secretary to Government.
