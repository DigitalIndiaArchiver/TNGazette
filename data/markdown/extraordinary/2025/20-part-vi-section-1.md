THE COMMISSIONER OF LAND ADMINISTRATION CHEPAUK, CHENNAI-600 005.
ACQUISITION OF LANDS
FORM C
NOTICE UNDER SECTION 3(1) OF THE TAMIL NADU ACQUISITION OF LAND FOR 
INDUSTRIAL PURPOSES ACT, 1997 
(LA-I(1)/9225305/2024)
No.VI(1)/38(b-1)/2025.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that the lands specified in the Schedule below to be 
acquired for Industrial Purposes to wit for the Doubling of Broad-Gauge Railway Line between Eraniel and Parassala 
in Kaliyakkavilai Village of Vilavancode Taluk in Kanniyakumari District, and it having already been decided that the 
amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands are to be paid out of the funds controlled or managed by Southern 
Railways. The following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for 
Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands specified in the Schedule 
below measuring 52016 Sq.mtrs., to the same, a little more or less needed for Industrial Purpose to wit for the 
Doubling of Broad-Gauge Railway Line between Eraniel and Parassala in Kaliyakkavilai Village of Vilavancode Taluk 
in Kanniyakumari District.
The plan of the lands are kept in the office of the District Collector, Kanniyakumari District which may be inspected 
at any day during office hours.
