NOTIFICATION OF VINAYAKAR CHATHURTHI CELEBRATIONS, 2009—IDOL IMMERSION PROCESSIONS—PLACES FOR
IMMERSION OF VINAYAKAR IDOLS IN CHENNAI CITY AND IN CHENNAI SUBURBAN AREA.
[G.O. Ms. No. 773, Public (Law and Order-B), 19th August 2009.]
No. II (1)/PULO/26(c)/2009.
The Commissioner of Police, Chennai Police, in his letter No. 628/SB.XIX/2009-6, dated 7th August 2009, has requested
that the following places identified by him in Chennai City for immersion of Vinayakar Idols, may be notified by the Government:—
1.
Srinivasapuram at Foreshore Estate.
2.
Near Fishing Harbour, Kasimedu.
3.
Behind Popular Weigh Bridge, Thiruvottriyur.
4.
Behind Universal Carborandum, Thiruvottriyur.
5.
Palgalai Nagar, Neelangarai.
2. The Commissioner of Police, Chennai Suburban Police, in his letter No. 61/CIW/CSP/09, dated 10th August 2009,  has
requested that the following place identified by him in Chennai Suburban Area for immersion of Vinayakar Idols,
may be notified by the Government:—
1.
Ramakrishna Nagar Seashore, Ennore.
3.  The Government has considered the recommendation of the Commissioner of Police, Chennai Police and accordingly
notifies the following places for immersion of Vinayakar Idols during Vinayakar Chathurthi Celebrations, 2009 in respect of
Chennai City:—
1.
Srinivasapuram at Foreshore Estate.
2.
Near Fishing Harbour, Kasimedu.
3.
Behind Popular Weigh Bridge, Thiruvottriyur.
4.
Behind Universal Carborandum, Thiruvottriyur.
5.
Palgalai Nagar, Neelangarai.
