V.O. CHIDAMBARANAR PORT TRUST, TUTICORIN
RECRUITMENT RULES FOR THE POST OFJUNIOR ENGINEER (MECHANICAL) JUNIOR ENGINEER 
(ELECTRIAL) (CLASS III)
Amendment to the Schedule II of Tuticorin Port Trust Employees (Recruitment, Seniority and Promotion 
Regulations, 2017.
(MEE-CMEEL-EST-AMDRR-VI-17)
No. SRO c-12/2019.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (i) of Section 124, read with sub-section (1)(a) of the Major Port 
Trusts Act, 1963 (38 of 1963) the Board of Trustees V.O. Chidambaranar Port Trust hereby approves the revised manner 
of recruitment (Recruitment Rules) for Class-III posts under Regulation 5 of Tuticorin Port Trust Employees (Recruitment, 
Seniority and Promotion) Regulations, 2008 as set out in the Schedule annexed to this Notification.
2.	
The said Recruitment Rules shall come into effect from the date of publication of this Notification in the 
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
Schedule
(1)		 In the said Schedule under Regulations, in the Recruitment Rules for the post of Junior Engineer (Mechanical), 
(Mechanical & Electrical Engineering Department), (Sl.No.90), the provisions under Column No. 8 shall be 
substituted by the following:-
Essential:
(i) Degree in Mechanical Engineering of a recognised University or its equivalent (OR)
(ii) Diploma In Mechanical Engineering of a recognised Institution with three years post qualification experience in 
the relevant field of Mechanical Engineering.
The Provisions under Column No. 9 shall be substituted by the following
	
(a) Age 	
: No
	
(b) Qualifications	: Yes
	
(c) Experience	
: No.
The provisions under Column No.12 shall be substituted by the following—
Promotion:
Employees (except common categories viz Lower Division Clerk, Upper Division Clerk, Senior Assistant, Peon 
etc.) working in the Mechanical & Electrical Engineering Department of the port on regular basis, failing which 
Employees working in the Port, who hold lower Posts and are in possession of the qualifications prescribed 
for direct recruitment.
(2)	 In the said Schedule under Regulations, in the Recruitment Rules for the post of Junior Engineer (Electrical) 
(Mechanical & Electrical Engineering Department) (SI.No.91), the provisions under Column No.8 shall be substituted by 
the following—
Essential:
(i) 	 Degree in Electrical/Electronics / Electrical & Electronics/ Electronics & Communication Engineering of a 	
	
recognised university or its equivalent (OR) 
(ii) Diploma in Electrical/Electronics/Electrical & Electronics/ Electronics & Communication Engineering of a 		
	
recognised Institution with three years post qualification experience in the relevant field of above discipline.
