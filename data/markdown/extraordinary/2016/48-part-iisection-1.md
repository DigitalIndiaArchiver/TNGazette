VACATION OF SEATS IN TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY DUE TO RESIGNATION OF
CERTAIN MEMBERS OF THE TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY.
[Letter No.1975/2016-1, Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Secretariat (B.III), 21st February 2016,
Maasi 9, Manmadha, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2047.]
No. II(1)/LAS/7(c-1)/2016.
Whereas, Thiru M. Arun Subramanian, a Member of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly representing the
3. Tiruttani Assembly Constituency, in his letter dated 21st February, 2016 addressed to me as Speaker of the
Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly, has resigned his seat as Member of the said Assembly with effect from
21st February, 2016.
Now, therefore, I,
P.
DHANAPAL, Speaker of the said Assembly hereby accept the resignation of
Thiru M. Arun Subramanian, as envisaged under Rule 22 of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Rules as it is in
accordance with Rule 21 of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Rules. Under Article 190 (3)(b) of the Constitution of
India, his seat has, therefore, become vacant with effect from the above date.
[Letter No. 1975/2016-2, Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Secretariat (B.III), 21st February 2016,
Maasi 9, Manmadha, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2047.]
No. II(1)/LAS/7(c-2)/2016.

177. Peravurani Assembly Constituency, in his letter dated 21st February, 2016 addressed to me as Speaker of the
Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly, has resigned his seat as Member of the said Assembly with effect from
21st February, 2016.
