In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 86 and clause (a) of sub-section (2) of Section 347 of 
the Chennai City Municipal Corporation Act, 1919 (Tamil Nadu Act IV of 1919), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes 
the following amendments to the Rules published with the Municipal Administration and Water Supply Department Notification 
No. SRO A-122/96 at page 276 of Part III-Section 1(a) of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 11th December 1996:- 
Amendments
In the said Rules,- 
(1) 	 in rule 3, for items (i) to (vi) excluding the explanation thereto, the following shall be substituted, namely;- 
(i) 	 by appointment of District Revenue Officers or Municipal  Commissioners of Special Grade and above 
from Tamil Nadu Municipal Commissioner Service on deputation; 
(ii) 	 by promotion from among the holders of the post of Executive Engineer or Divisional Electrical Engineer; 
(iii) 	 by promotion from among the holders of the post of Chief Accounts Officer or by transfer from among 
the holders of the post of Additional Revenue Officers; 
(iv) 	by promotion from among the holders of the post of Additional City Health Officer. 
	
The ratio for appointment to the post of Zonal Officer for the Officers mentioned in items Nos. (i), (ii), (iii) and 
(iv) shall be 5:8:1:1; 
	
	
Provided that in case of non-availability of officers in any one of the above said categories, the post will be 
filled up from available officers from other category"; 
(2) 	 in rule 5, in clause (ii), for the expression "3 years", the expression "one year" shall be substituted.
