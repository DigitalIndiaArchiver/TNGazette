No. I/PUSP/12/2012.—The following Notification of the Government of
India, Ministry of Law and Justice (Department of Justice), Jaisalmer
House, 26, Man Singh Road, New Delhi-110 011, dated 20th March, 2012
is Republished:—
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (1) of article 224 of
the Constitution of Inida, the President is pleased to appoint
Shri Muthiah Thevar Vijayaraghavan, to be an Additional Judge of the
Madras High Court with effect from the date he assumes charge of his
office till 16th November, 2013.
