Whereas, in the Notification issued with the S.O. first and second read above, this Commission directed that all
the contesting candidates in the elections listed therein shall lodge a true copy of their accounts of election expenses
kept by them or by their respective election agent under  Rule 116 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayat, Third Grade
Municipalities, Municipalities and Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006, in the format prescribed therefor  with the
officers mentioned therein, within thirty days from the date of declaration of the result of the elections;
2. Whereas, this Commission conducted Casual elections to the urban local bodies during February 2007 as
notified in the S.O. third read above;
3. Whereas, the District Election Officer/Commissioner, Chennai City Municipal Corporation, in his letter fourth read
above reported that 112 candidates contested in the said Casual elections for the office of Corporation Councillors
in various wards of Chennai City Municipal Corporation have failed to lodge their account of election expenses as
directed by this Commission;
4. Whereas, based on the report of the said District Election Officer/Commissioner, Chennai City Municipal Corporation,
a show cause notice was issued in the reference fifth read above to each of the contested candidate, who failed to
lodge a true copy of the account of election expenses in the Casual elections held during the month of February 2007,
through the District Election Officer/Commissioner, Chennai City Municipal Corporation, calling for explanation within 21
days of the receipt of the said notice, as to why action should not be taken against him as per sub-section (5) of
Section 52 of the Chennai City Municipal Corporation Act, 1919 (Tamil Nadu Act IV of 1919);
5. Whereas, the said District Election Officer/Commissioner, Chennai City Municipal Corporation in his letters sixth
read above, has now reported that out of the said 112 contested candidates, 19 contested candidates lodged the
account of election expenses and the remaining 93 contested candidates have neither submitted their explanations
nor lodged the account of election expenses inspite of the show cause notices issued to them;
6. And, whereas, in view of the said report of the District Election Officer/Commissioner, Chennai City Municipal
Corporation, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission is satisfied that the 93 candidates specified in column (3)
of the Table below against the election contested as mentioned in the corresponding entry in column (2) thereof, have
failed to lodge their account of election expenses as required by or under the said Act and Rules and have no good
reason or justification for such failure.
7. Now, therefore, ln exercise of the powers conferred by sub-Section (5) of Section 52 of the Chennai City Municipal
Corporation Act, 1919 (Tamil Nadu Act IV of 1919), the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission hereby declares that
the candidates specified in column (3) of the Table below against the election contested as mentioned in the
corresponding entry in column (2) thereof, shall be disqualified, for being elected as a Councillor for a period of three
years from the date of this Order:
