HOME DEPARTMENT
APPOINTMENT OF VACATION CIVIL JUDGES FOR THE SUMMER VACATION OF CIVIL COURTS FOR THE YEAR, 2013
UNDER THE TAMIL NADU CIVIL COURTS ACT.
[G.O. (D) No. 264, Home (Courts-III), 26th April 2013.]
No. II(2)/HO/339(a)/2013.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 30 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central
Act III of 1873), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, in consultation with the High Court of Madras, hereby appoints the Judicial
Officers specified in column (2) of the Table below to be vacation Civil Judges for the District Courts in the Districts specified
in the corresponding entries in column (3) thereof for the Summer Vacation of the Civil Courts for the year 2013.
