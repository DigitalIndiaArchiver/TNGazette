DECLARATION 
No. II(2)/PWD/854(c)/2020.
Whereas it appears to the Government that a total of 1.60.5 hectares of land is required in 
the Kuravarkulam Village in Palayamkottai Taluk in Tirunelveli District for public purpose, namely 
Inter linking of Tamirabarani, Nambiyaru and Karumeniyar rivers for the Flood Carrier Canal Project.
Now, therefore a declaration is made hereby that a piece of land measuring 1.60.5 hectares 
is under acquisition for the above said project in the Kuravarkulam Village in Palayamkottai Taluk 
in Triunelveli District whose detailed description is as follows:
