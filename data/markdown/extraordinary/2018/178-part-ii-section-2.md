The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied 
that the lands specified in the schedule below have to be 
acquired for Industrial Purposes, to wit, for Mine I A Scheme 
of Neyveli Lignite Corporation India Limited and it having 
already been decided that the entire amount of compensation 
to be awarded for the lands is to be paid out of the funds 
controlled or managed by Neyveli Lignite Corporation India 
Limited. The following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of 
section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial 
Purposes Act, 1997.
Notice
Under sub-section (1) of section 3 of the Tamil Nadu 
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Government of Tamil Nadu 
hereby acquires the lands specified in the Schedule below and 
measuring 4457 sq.meters of R.Manai lands to the same, a 
little more or less needed for Industrial purposes, to wit, for 
Mine IA Scheme of Neyveli Lignite Corporation India Limited. 
The plans of the lands are kept in the office of the Special 
Deputy Collector / Land Acquisition, Neyveli-2 and may be 
inspected at any time during office hours.
