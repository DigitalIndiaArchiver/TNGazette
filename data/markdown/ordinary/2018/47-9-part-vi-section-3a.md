No. VI-3(a)/193/2018.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Waqf Act 1995, the Tamil Nadu 
Waqf Board in its resolution item No.97/18 in Rc.No.15953/10/B9/Chennai dated 28-08-2018 has ordered to take over 
the Mangement of HAZRATH SYED HUSSAIN SHA KHADERI DARGAH AND MASJID @ DIMMASAMY DARGAH, 
CHENNAI DISTRICT under the Direct Management of Tamil Nadu Waqf Board for a period of ONE YEAR from 
28-08-2018 or til the date of constitution of Mangement committee whichever is earlier and to appoint the Waqf Inspector, 
Chennai (Central) as its Executive Officer.

N.S. MOHAMED ASLAM,
