The Governor of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that
the lands specified in the schedule below have to be
acquired for Industrial purpose to wit to formation of path
way to take water to Thermal Power Project  and it having
already been decided that the entire amount of compensation
to be awarded for the lands is to be paid out of the funds
controlled or managed by Tamil Nadu Electricity Board and
having considered the cause shown by the owners or other
persons having interest the said lands, the Assistant
Commissioner (LR) concerned, the interested party for the
lands covered under Tamil Nadu Land Reforms (fixation of
ceiling on land) Act 1961, as the case may be,
[ 1 ]
do hereby publish the  following notice is issued under
sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition
of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10
of 1999) by relaxing G.O.(Ms.) No. 2954, Revenue Department,
dated 29th May 1973.
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby acquires the  lands specified in the schedule below
and measuring 27.50.0 hectares  to the same, a little more
or less needed for Industrial purposes, to wit for formation
of path way to take water to Thermal Power Project.
The plan of the lands are kept in the office of the District
Collector, Thoothukudi  and may be inspected at any time
during office hours.
