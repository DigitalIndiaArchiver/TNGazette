(Roc. No. H1/1164380/2021)

Notice under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) and 
Tamil Nadu Acquisition Laws (Revival of operation, Amendment and Validation) Act, 2019 (Tamil Nadu Act 38/2019)
The Government of Tamil Nadu having  been satisfied that the lands specified in the schedule below here to 
be required for the public purpose, for the formation of new road from Thoothukkudi - Vanchimaniyachi through 
Highways Department at Savarimangalam Village, Ottapidaram  Taluk, in the  District of Thoothukkudi  through 
Highways Department, and it has  already been decided that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for 
the lands to be paid out of the funds  controlled and  managed by the Divisional Engineer (Highways), Construction 
and Maintenance,  Thoothukkudi  and after having considered the cause shown by the owners or the other persons 
having interest on such lands as the case may do hereby publish the following notice is issued under sub-section (1) 
of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002).
 NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) it 
is hereby acquired the lands specified in the schedule below and measuring to an extent of 233798 sq.mtrs. of 
Dry,  lands,  to the same, a little more or less is needed for the purpose for the formation of new road from 
Thoothukkudi - Vanchimaniyachi comprised in S.No. 87/2 etc. at Savarimangalam  Village, Ottapidaram  Taluk, 
Thoothukkudi District.  The plan of the lands are kept in the Office of the District Collector, Thoothukkudi and Special 
Tahsildhar (LA),  Thoothukkudi may be inspected on any working day during office hours.
Thoothukkudi  District, Ottapidaram Taluk, Savarimangalam Village.
