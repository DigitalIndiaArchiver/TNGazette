The following Notification of the Government of India, 
Ministry of Law and Justice (Department of Justice), Jaisalmer 
House, 26, Man Singh Road, New Delhi-110 011, dated 
4th March 2020 is republished.
In exercise of the power conferred by clause (1) of 
Article 222 of the Constitution of India, the President, after 
consultation with the Chief Justice of India, is pleased to transfer 
Shri Justice Subramonium Prasad, Additional Judge of the 
Madras High Court, as an Additional Judge of the Delhi 
High Court and to direct him to assume charge of his office 
in the Delhi High Court.
