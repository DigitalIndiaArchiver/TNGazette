The Government  of  Tamil Nadu, having  been satisﬁ ed that 
the lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below have to be acquired 
for Industrial purpose, to wit for the Chennai Airport Expansion 
Scheme and it having already been decided that the entire 
amount of compensation to be awarded  for the lands to be 
paid out of the funds controlled or managed by Government. 
The following  notice is issued under  sub – section (1) of 
Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of land for Industrial 
Purpose Act,1997, (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
 
Under  sub – section (1) of Section 3 of the 
Tamil Nadu Acquisition  of land  for Industrial Purpose Act, 
1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu 
hereby acquires the lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule  below and 
measuring 20.33.0 Hectares ( or) 50.21 Acres, to the same, 
a little more or less needed for Industrial purpose, to wit, for 
the  Chennai Airport Expansion Scheme.
 
The Plan of the lands are kept in the ofﬁ ce of the 
Special Tahsildar (Land Acquisition), Chennai Airport Expansion 
Scheme, Sriperumbudur and may  be inspected  at any time 
during the ofﬁ ce hours.
