The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied 
that the lands specified in the schedule below have to be 
acquired for Industrial Purposes, to wit, for Mine-I Expansion 
Scheme of NLC India Limited and it having already been 
decided that the entire amount of compensation to be 
awarded for the lands is to be paid out of the funds 
controlled or managed by NLC India Limited.  The following 
notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the 
Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes 
Act, 1997.
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the 
Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu 
is hereby acquires the lands specified in the Schedule 
below and measuring 5.46.5 hectares be the same 
a little more or less needed for Industrial purposes, 
to wit, for Mine-I Expansion Scheme of NLC India Limited. 
The plans  of the lands are kept in the office of the 
Special Deputy  Collector / Land Acquisition, Neyveli-2 and 
may be inspected at any  time during office hours.
