Variation to the approved Palavanthan Kattalai Detailed Development Plan No. 9 of Kumbakonam Local
Planning Area.
(Roc. No. 11725/2015/DP2)
No. VI(1)/319/2015.
In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (1) of Section 33 of Town and Country Planning Act, 1971
(Tamil Nadu Act No. 35 of 1972) The Director of Town and Country Planning, in the Proceedings
Roc. No. 11725/2015 DP2,  dated 18-11-2015 Proposes to make the following variation to the approved Palavanthan
Kattalai Detailed Development Plan No.9 of Kumbakonam Local Planning Area, approved by the Commissioner of Town
and Country Planning in proceeding Roc.No.16498/2008/DP3, Dated: 20-05-2009 and the fact of this Approval in Form
No.12, Published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No.38, Part-VI, Section-1, Page No. 280 & 281, Dated 30-09-2009
Publication No. VI(1)/283/2009.
2. Any person affected or interested in this draft variation may within SIXTY days from the date of publication of this
notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, can represent in person or submit in writing to the Member Secretary,
Kumbakonam Local Planning Authority any Objections and Suggestions relating thereto.
3. The variation with plan may be inspected free of cost at any time during office hours at the above said Local
Planning Authority — Office.
VARIATION
1. The entire Palavanthan Kattalai Detailed Development Plan No. 9 of Kumbakonam Local Planning Area, Approved
by the Commissioner of Town and Country Planning in proceedings Roc. No.16498/2008/DP3, Dated 20.05.2009 is varied
by the Palavanthan Kattalai Detailed Development Plan No.9. Kumbakonam Local Planning Authority.
2. The plan thus varied is numbered as Map No.5 DDP(V)/DTCP No.16/2015.
3. The Detailed Development Plan clauses and schedules annexed with this plan are made enforceable from the date
of publication of the confirmed variation notifcation to be issued under section 33(2) of the Act and published in
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
Chennai-600 002,
R. KIRLOSH KUMAR,
18th November 2015.
Director of Town and Country Planning.
Confirmation of Variation to the approved Melapalayam Detailed Development Plan No. 5 of Tirunelveli Local
Planning Area.
(Roc. No. 635/2008/DP2)
No. VI(1)/320/2015.
In exercise of the powers conferred Under Section 33(2) of Town and Country Planning Act, 1971, the Director of
Town and Country Planning, Chennai hereby confirmes the following variation to the approved Melapalayam Detailed
Development Plan No. 5 of Tirunelveli Local Planning area, approved in Commissioner of Town and Country Planning
in proceeding ROC No. 43065/97/DP2, dated:31-03-1998 and the fact of this approval in Form No.12 Published in
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No.39 part VI-Section -1 page No.454, 455 dated:04-10-2000 and the said Comprehensive
draft variation notification was published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No.25, Part VI—Section-I, Page No. 195
dated: 25-06-2008 in publication No. VI(1)/216/2008.
2. Since No Objections and Suggestions have been received on this draft notification with in the stipulated time, the
same are hereby confirmed and ordered as below.
CONFIRMATION
OF VARIATION
1. The entire Melapalayam Detailed Development Plan No.5 of Tirunelveli Local Planning Area, Approved by the
Commissioner of Town and Country Planning in proceedings Roc. No.43065/1997/DP2, dated: 31-03-1998 was varied by
Commissioner of Town and Country Planning in Proceeding Roc.No. 635/2008/DP3, No.5, dated:05-06-2008
2. The plan thus varied is numbered as Map No.5 DDP(V)/DTCP No.13/2008.
3. The Detailed Development Plan clauses and schedules annexed with this plan are made enforceable from the date
of publication of this notification in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
Chennai-600 002,
R. KIRLOSH KUMAR,
24th November 2015.
Director of Town and Country Planning.
