No.I/PUSP/14/2019.—The following Notification of the 
Government of India, Ministry of Law and Justice, Department 
of Justice, Jaisalmer House, 26, Man Singh Road, New 
Delhi-110 011, dated 22nd August, 2019 is republished:—
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (1) of Article 
217 of the Constitution of India, the President is pleased to 
appoint (i) Smt. Justice S. Ramathilagam (ii) Smt. Justice 
R. Tharani, (iii) Justice P. Rajamanickam, (iv) Smt. Justice 
T. Krishnavalli, (v) Shri Justice R. Pongiappan, and (vi) Smt. 
Justice R. Hemalatha, Additional Judges of the Madras 
High Court, to be Judges of the Madras High Court with effect 
from the date they assume charge of  their respective offices.

The following Government Order is Published:—
[G.O. Ms. No. 572, Public (Special-A), 26th August 2019, 
Aavani 9, Vikari, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2050.]
Read:
1. From Dr. N. Rajakumar, IRS, Joint Commissioner of 
Income Tax, Tiruppur, Tamil Nadu, Letter dated 04-04-2019.
2. From the Government of India, Ministry of Finance/
Department of Revenue, Central Board of Direct Taxes Letter 
F.No.HRD/CM/152/172/2018-19/3194, dated 16-08-2019
Order: No.572, Public (Special-A), 26th August 2019.
The Government of Tamil Nadu have agreed for the 
deputation of Dr. N. Rajakumar, IRS, Joint Commissioner of
Income Tax, Tiruppur, Tamil Nadu with this State Government 
for a period of three years with effect from the date of his 
reporting for duty in the Government of Tamil Nadu.
2. Dr. N. Rajakumar, IRS, (Civil Code:09120), Joint 
Commissioner of Income Tax, Tiruppur, Tamil Nadu on 
deputation to the Government of Tamil Nadu is posted to act 
as Joint Commissioner of Commercial Taxes, Services Tax Cell 
Office of Commissioner of Commercial Taxes Chennai.
3. During the period of his deputation, he is allowed to draw 
his pay in his time scale of pay, allowances and entitlements as 
applicable to him in his parent cadre and other perquisites as 
eligible to the post to which he is posted.
(By Order of the Governor)
	
K. SHANMUGAM,	
	
	
Chief Secretary.
