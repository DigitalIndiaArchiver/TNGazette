GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI.
Extension of period of Direct Management of Jannathul Bakiyath
Madrasa and Pallivasal, Erode.
(Lr. Rc. No. 14597/04/B8/ERD.)
No. VI-3(a)/3/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995,
and due to administrative reasons the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in item No. 182/10
(Rc.No.14597/04/B8/ERD), dated 6th October 2010 has ordered to extent the period of Direct Management
of Jannathul Bakiyath Madrasa and Pallivasal, Erode Town and District (which is under the Direct Control of the
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board) for a further period of One Year from the date of 25th September 2010.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
9th November 2010.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
Framing of Scheme in respect of Mohideen Andavar Jumma Pallivasal,
Nallur, Aruppukottai, Virudhunagar District.
(Rc. No. 7649/01/B-17/VNR.)
No. VI-3(a)/4/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 69 of the Wakf Act, 1995,
the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its Proceedings in item No. 193/10 in Rc.No.7649/01/B17/VNR, dated 6th October 2010
has framed a Scheme for the Wakf, viz. Mohideen Andavar Jumma Pallivasal, Nallur, Arupukkottai, Virudhunagar District
for better administration of the Wakf.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
10th December 2010.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
Extension of period of Direct Management of Masjid E Sayeedia Wakf, Chennai.
(Lr. Rc. No. 9582/08/Chennai/B6.)
No. VI-3(a)/5/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995,
the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in item No. 165/10 (Rc. No. 9582/08/B2/Chennai), dated 4th August 2010
has ordered to extent the period of direct management of Masjid E Sayeedia Wakf, Triplicane, Chennai  for a further
period of One Year from 28th July 2010.
Extension of period of Direct Management of Masjid E Khudusiya Wakf, Chennai.
(Lr.Rc.No. 9583/08/Chennai/B6.)
No. VI-3(a)/6/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995,
the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in item No. 166/10 (Rc.No.9583/08/B2/Chennai), dated 4th August 2010
has ordered to extent the period of direct management of Masjid E Khudusiya Wakf, Triplicane, Chennai  for a further
period of One Year from 28th July 2010.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
16th December 2010.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
