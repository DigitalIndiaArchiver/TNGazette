GENERAL  NOTIFICATION
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI
Extension of period of Direct Management of Sheik Mohamed Ali Masjid Nasarethpettai,
Kancheepuram District.
(Rc. No. 1212/07/B4/KPM)
No. VI-3(a)/44/2015.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act 1995 as amended by
Act 27 of 2013, the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in item No. 31/15 in Rc. No. 1212/07/B4/KPN, dated
29-05-2015 has  resolved to ratify the period of Direct Management of Sheik Mohamed Ali Masjid, Nasarethpettai,
Kancheepuram District from 24-10-2014 to 28-05-2015 and also to extend the period of Direct Management of the
above Wakf for a further period of ONE Year from the date of Board's Order (29-05-2015).
Chennai-600 001,
K. RASHEETH ALI,
9th July 2015.
District Revenue Officer /
Chief Executive Officer,

ADVERTISEMENT OF PETITION
Notice is hereby given that a Petition for a winding-up of the above named company by the High Court at Madras was on
the 23rd day of June 2015. presented to the said Court by the petitioner, Paharpur Cooling Towers Limited having its registered
office at 8/1/B Diamond Harbour Road, Kolkatta - 700 027 a creditor and that the said petition is directed to be heard before the
Court on the 27th day of July 2015.
Any creditor, contributory or other person desirous of supporting or opposing the making of an order on the said petition
should send to the Petitioner or their advocates notice of his intention, signed by him or his Advocate, with his name and
address, so as to reach the Petitioner or their Advocates not later than five days before the date fixed for hearing of the Petition,
and appear at the hearing for the purpose in person or by his advocate. A copy of the Petition will be furnished by the undersigned
to any creditor or contributory on payment of the prescribed charges for the same.
Any affidavit intended to be used in opposition to the petition should be filed in Courts and a copy served on the petitioner
or his advocate, not less than 5 days before the date fixed for the hearing.
