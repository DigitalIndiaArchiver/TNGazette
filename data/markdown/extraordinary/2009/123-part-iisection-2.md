Whereas, the State Government is of opinion that the Rajapalayam Municipal Council persistently makes default in
performing duties imposed on it by law ;
And, Whereas, the explanations of the Rajapalayam Municipal Council have been called for under sub-section (1-A)
of Section 41 of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920) and its explanations have
been considered by the State Government ;
And, Whereas, it is considered that there is reasonable basis to dissolve the said Municipal Council ;
Now, therefore, in exercise of powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 41 of the Tamil Nadu District
Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby dissolves the Rajapalayam
Municipal Council with immediate effect and also directs that the said Municipal Council shall be reconstituted
with effect from 12th November 2009.
And, in exercise of the powers conferred by clause (b) of sub-section (3) of Section 41 of the said Act, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the Regional Director of Municipal Administration, Tirunelveli, to exercise and
perform all the functions of the Rajapalayam Municipal Council and its Chairman during the period of dissolution
of the said Municipal Council.
NIRANJAN MARDI,
Secretary to Government.
