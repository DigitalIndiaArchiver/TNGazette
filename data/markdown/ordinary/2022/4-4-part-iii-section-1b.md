Framing of Adhoc Rules relating to the Temporary Post of Professor (Unani), Government Unani Medical College, 
Chennai in Indian Medicine and Homoeopathy Department for the Tamil Nadu Medical Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 13, Health and Family Welfare (IM I-1), 23rd January 2019,
]
No. SRO B-5/2022.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, 
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following Rules:- 
2. The Rules hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on and from the 4th day of September, 2008.
Rඝඔඍඛ. 
The provisions contained in the Tamil Nadu Government Servants (Conditions of Service) Act, 2016 (Tamil Nadu Act 14 of 
2016) and the Special Rules applicable to the holders of the permanent posts in Branch I - Medical of the Special Rules for the 
Tamil Nadu Medical Service shall apply to the holder of the temporary post of Professor (Unani), subject to the modiﬁ cations 
speciﬁ ed in the following Rules. 
2. Constitution.- The post shall constitute a separate category in a Distinct class in the said Branch of the said service. 
3. Appointment.- (a) Appointment to the post shall be made as follows:- 
(i) By transfer from among the holders of the post of Reader (Unani) or 
(ii) by promotion from among the holders of the post of Lecturer (Unani), if no candidate is available in the
 
above method. or 
(iii) by direct recruitment: 
(b) Notwithstanding anything contained in clause (v) of section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Government Servants 
(Conditions of Service) Act, 2016 (Tamil Nadu Act 14 of 2016) persons in any of the services of the Government of India 
or the Government of any other States shall also be eligible for the appointment to the post by direct recruitment. 
4. Appointing Authority.- The appointing authority for the post shall be the  Government. 
5. Qualiﬁ cation.- (a) Age.- No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post by direct recruitment, if he has completed 
or will complete forty years of age on the ﬁ rst day of July of the year in which the selection for the appointment is made. 
(b) Other qualiﬁ cations.- No person shall be eligible for appointment to the  post, unless he possesses the following 
qualiﬁ cations, namely:- 
(i) Must possess the qualiﬁ cation of Master Degree (Unani) 
(ii) Must possess ten years of teaching experience, of which ﬁ ve years shall be in the post of Reader (Unani) or a 
total period of ten years teaching experience in a college of Indian Medicine recognized by the Central Council 
of Indian Medicine, Government of India, New Delhi. 
(iii)  Must have registered with the Tamil Nadu Board of Indian Medicine, Chennai under Class "A" or Class "A" 
Special: 
6. Rule of Reservation.- The provisions relating to reservation of  appointment i.e., section 27 of the Tamil Nadu 
Government Servants (Conditions of Service) Act, 2016 (Tamil Nadu Act 14 of 2016) shall apply for appointment to the post by 
direct recruitment.
 
Provided that if no eligible candidate is available for the vacancy reserved for a communal category other than the 
Backward Class Muslim, for appointment to the post of Professor (Unani) by the method of direct recruitment, then the said 
vacancy shall be ﬁ lled up from among the candidates belonging to any of the other communal categories, based on merit. 
7. Probation.-Every person appointed to the post by direct recruitment shall, from the date on which he joins duty, be on 
probation for a total period of two years on duty within a continuous period of three years. 
8. Tests.- (a) Every person appointed, to the post by direct recruitment shall, within the period of his probation, pass 
the Account Test for Executive Oﬃ  cers, failing which is increment shall be stopped without cumulative eﬀ ect, till he passes 
the said test.

(b) Every person appointed to the post by direct recruitment, shall also pass the Departmental Test in Tamil Nadu Medical 
Code within a period of four years from the date of his appointment to the said post, failing which is increment shall be stopped 
without cumulative eﬀ ect, till be passes the said test. 
9. Private Practice.- The holder of the post shall be allowed private practice to the level of prescribing medicines only. 
10. Pay.- There shall be paid to holder of the post, a monthly pay calculated as on 04-09-2008 in the scale of 
Rs.15,600 - 39,100 with Grade Pay of 7600/- and subsequently as per the orders issued by the Finance Department in 
G.O.(Ms).No.303, Finance (PC) Department, dated:11-10-2017 in the level 26 of pay of Rs.61,900 - 196700/-. 
11. Saving.- Nothing contained in these rules shall adversely aﬀ ect the holders of the posts on the date of publication of 
these rules.
