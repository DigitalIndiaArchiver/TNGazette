NOTIFICATION OF ENTRUSTMENT OF THE WORK OF TRANSPORTATION OF EVMs FROM
UTTAR PRADESH TO TAMIL NADU TO THE CONTAINER CORPORATION OF INDIA LIMITED UNDER
THE TAMIL NADU TRANSPARENCY IN TENDERS ACT, 1998.
[G.O. No. 12, Finance (Salaries), 7th January 2011, Margazhi 23, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No. II(2)/FIN/8(f)/2011.
Under clause (f) of Section 16 of the Tamil Nadu Transparency in Tenders Act, 1998 (Tamil Nadu
Act 43 of 1998), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notifies the entrustment of the work of transportation
of Electronic Voting Machines (EVMs) from Uttar Pradesh to Tamil Nadu by rail to the Container
Corporation of India Limited (CONCOR).
K. SHANMUGAM,
Principal Secretary to Government.
