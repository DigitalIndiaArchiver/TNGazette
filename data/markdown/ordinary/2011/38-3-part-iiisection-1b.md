REVENUE DEPARTMENT
Amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Revenue Subordinate Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 274, Revenue [Ser.10(2)], 21st September 2011.]
No. SRO B-35/2011.—In exercise of the powers conferred by  the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the
Tamil Nadu Revenue Subordinate Service (Section 28 in Volume-III of the Tamil Nadu Service Manual, 1970:—
AMENDMENT
In the said  Special Rules, in rule 7, after the proviso to sub-rule (b), the following proviso shall be added namely:—
“Provided further that nothing contained in this sub-rule shall make a Deputy Tahsildar ineligible to get his name
included in the list of approved candidates for appointment as Tahsildar, if he has completed police training for a period
of two months but has not exercised the magisterial powers for a period of four months, due to any administrative reasons.
However, the Tahsildar so appointed shall not be eligible to get posting as ‘Taluk Tahsildar’ or function as ‘Taluk Executive
Magistrate’ unless he has exercised the magisterial powers for a period of four months”.
 R. RAJAGOPAL,
Principal Secretary to Government.
