The following Notification of the Government of India, Ministry of Law and Justice, (Department of
Justice), Jaisalmer House, 26, Man Singh Road, New Delhi-110 011, dated 10th October 2013 is
republished:—
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (1) of Article 217 of the Constitution of India, the
President is pleased to appoint (i) Smt. Justice S. Vimala and (ii) Shri Justice Muthiah Thevar
Vijayaraghavan, Additional Judges of the Madras High Court, to be Judges of the Madras High Court
with effect from the date they assume charge of their respective offices.
