NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
PUBLIC   DEPARTMENT
  DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF THE
TAMIL NADU PAYMENT OF SALARIES (AMENDMENT) ACT, 2011.
[G.O. Ms. No. 393, Public (Estt.I & Leg.), 25th April 2012.]
No. II(2)/PU/224(a)/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the  Tamil Nadu Payment of
Salaries (Amendment) Act, 2011 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 2011), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the
25th day of April 2012, as the date on which the said Act shall come into force.
KUMAR JAYANT,
Secretary to Government.
