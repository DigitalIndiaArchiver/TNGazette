NOTIFICATIONS  BY  THE  ELECTION  COMMISSION  OF  INDIA
NOTIFICATION OF ELECTION COMMISSION OF INDIA REGARDING AMENDMENTS TO THE LIST OF
RETURNING OFFICERS FOR THE PARLIAMENTARY CONSTITUENCIES IN THE STATE OF TAMIL NADU.
No. SRO G-14/2009.
The following Notification of the Election Commission of India, Nirvachan Sadan, Ashoka Road, New Delhi-110 001,
dated the 2nd April, 2009 [12 Chaitra, 1931 (Saka)] is  republished:—
No. 434/TN/2009 (1).—In pursuance of the provisions of   Section 21 of the Representation of the People Act, 1951
(43 of 1951), the Election Commission hereby directs that the following amendments shall be made in its Notification
No. 434/TN/2008 (1), dated 18th June, 2008 as amended from time to time, namely:—
1. In the column (2) of the table appended to the said notification, the existing entry “Commissioner, Corporation of
Chennai, Chennai-3.” shown against “4-Chennai Central” in the column (1) of the said table shall be substituted by the
entry, “Deputy Commissioner (Health), Office of the Commissioner, Corporation of Chennai, Chennai-600 003”;
2. In the column (2) of the table appended to the said notification, the existing entry “Special District Revenue Officer
(Land Acquisition), SIPCOT, Sriperumbudur” shown against “5-Sriperumbudur” in the column (1)  of the said table
shall be substituted by the entry, “Special District Revenue Officer (Land Acquisition), SIPCOT, Chennai / Sriperumbudur.”;
3. In the column (2) of the table appended to the said notification, the existing entry “Special Officer, Kallakurichi
Co-operative Sugar Mills, Kallakurichi” shown against  “14-Kallakurichi” in the column (1) of the said table shall be
substituted by the entry, “Special Officer, Kallakurichi Co-operative Sugar Mills, Moongilthuraipattu”, and
4. In the column (2) of the table appended to the said notification, the existing entry “Deputy Commissioner,
Coimbatore Municipal Corporation” shown against “18-Tiruppur” in the column (1) of the said table shall be substituted by
the entry, “Collector of Tiruppur, Tiruppur”;
(By order)
