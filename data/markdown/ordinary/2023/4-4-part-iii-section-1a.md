No. SRO A-2/2023.—In exercise of the powers conferred under Sections 30 and 44 read with clauses (h) and (m) of 
sub-section (2) of section 102 of the Consumer Protection Act, 2019 (Central Act 35 of 2019) the Governor of Tamil Nadu 
hereby makes the following amendment to the Tamil Nadu Consumer Protection (Salary, allowances and Conditions of service 
of President and Members of the State Commission and District Commission) Rules, 2021:-
 Amendment
In the said Rules, after rule 14, the following rule shall be added, namely:-
	
"15. The Government may transfer any President or Member of the District Commission from one district to another 
district on the recommendation of the President, State Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission, Chennai, on request 
or on administrative grounds."
	
J. RADHAKRISHNAN,
	
Additional Chief Secretary to Government.
