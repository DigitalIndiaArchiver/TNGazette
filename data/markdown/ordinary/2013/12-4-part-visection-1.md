Ind. No.10/Kovai/2006, Angalakuruchi, Pollachi Taluk, Coimbatore District And Appointment of Official Liquidator.
(Rc. No. 3668/ICH/2012)
No.VI(1)/190/2013.
Under the Powers conferred on the Industries Commissioner and Director of Industries and Commerce Registrar of
Industrial Co-operatives Under Section 137(2)(b) of The Tamil Nadu Co-operatives Act 1983 (Act 30 of 1983)
The Activities Tamil Nadu Co-operatives Act 1983, The Affairs of the Angalakuruchi Coir Workers Co-operative Cottage
Industrial Society Ltd. Ind. No.10/Kovai/2006, Angalakuruchi, Pollachi Taluk, Coimbatore District are ordered to be wound up
with immediate effect by the Industries Commissioner and Director of Industries and Commerce and Registrar of Industrial
Co-operatives, Chennai-28.
Under Section 138(1) of the same Act, The Assistant Director (Industrial Co-operatives) Office of the General Manager,
District Industries Centre, Coimbatore, is appointed as the Official Liquidator for the purpose of liquidation.

Variations to the Approved Master Plan for the Coimbatore Local Planning Area.
(Roc. No. 9624/2011/LPA-3)
No. VI(1)/191/2013.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 32 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning
Act 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972) and in exercise of powers conferred by the G.O.Ms.No. 94 Housing and Urban
Development (UD4(1) Department dated 12-06-2009 which has been published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette
No. 27 part II, Section 2, Page No. 228, dated 15-07-2009 the following variations are made to the master
plan for the Coimbatore Local Planning Area approved under the said Act and published in the Housing and Urban
Development Department Notification No. II(2)/HOU/4377/94 at page 1078 of Part II to Section 2 of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette dated the 9th November 1994.
VARIATIONS
In the said Master Plan. In the “LAND USE SCHEDULE” under the heading Coimbatore Local Planning Area (OTHER
THAN CORPORATION AREA) under the sub-heading (b) Non  Notified Detailed Development Plan Area In Coimbatore
Taluk, No. 8 Kondayam Palayam Village.
(i)  Against the entry “AGRICULTURAL (AG 7)” for the expression “351 to 361, 412 to 419” the expression “351
to 361 (except 354/1A, 1B) and the expression 412 to 419 (except 414/1, 2) shall be substituted.
(ii)  Against the entry “EDUCATIONAL” the expression “NIL” the expression 354/1A, 1B, 414/1, 2  shall be added.
Coimbatore,
P. YOGARAJA,
20th March 2013.

Act 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972) and in exercise of powers conferred by the G.O. Ms.No. 94 Housing and Urban
Development (UD4(1) Department dated 12-06-2009 which has been published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette
No. 27 part II, Section 2, Page No. 228, dated 15-07-2009 the following variations are made to the master
plan for the Coimbatore Local Planning Area approved under the said Act and published in the Housing and Urban
Development Department Notification No. II(2)/HOU/4377/94 at page 1078 of Part II—Section 2 of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette dated the 9th November 1994.

In the said Master Plan, in the “LAND USE SCHEDULE” under the heading “COIMBATORE LOCAL PLANNING AREA
(Other Than COIMBATORE CORPORATION AREA) (b) Non  Notified Detailed Development Plan Area In Coimbatore
Taluk, under the sub-heading No. 15 Keeranatham Village.
(i)  Against the entry “AGRICULTURAL” for the expression “531 to 557” the expression “531 to 557 (except 553)
shall be substituted.
(ii)  Against the entry “INDUSTRIAL (I-10)” “the expression” 553 shall be added after the entry 525.

Variations to the Modified Master Plan for the Tiruppur Local Planning Area.
(Roc. No. 1525/2011/TLPA)
[G.O. (2D) No. 196, Housing and Urban Development (UD 4-1), Department, dated 28th November 2013.]
No. VI(1)/193/2013.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 32 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning
Act 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972) the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following Variations to the
Master Plan of the Tiruppur Local Planning Area approved under the said Act and published in the Housing and Urban
Development Department Notification No. II(2)/HOU/573/2006 at page 375 of Part II—Section 2 of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 22nd November 2006.
VARIATIONS
In the said Master Plan, in the “LAND USE SCHEDULE” for Nallur Village. Under the heading “TIRUPPUR LOCAL
PLANNING AREA” IN THE TABULAR COLUMN, IN COLUMN (3).
(i)  Under the heading “MIXED RESIDENTIAL USE (MR-34)”, In Column (2) against the Serial No. (I-b) in column
(3) for the expression “12” shall be substituted as 12/1A, 12/2A to 12/2L.
(ii)  Under the heading “SPECIAL AND HARZARDOUS INDUSTRIES USE (SHI)” in column (2) against the serial
No. III (c) the Survey No. “12/1B” shall be added in column No. 3.
Tiruppur,
C. JEEVANANDHAM,
