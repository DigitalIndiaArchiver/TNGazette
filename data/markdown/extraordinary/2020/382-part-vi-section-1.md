ACQUISITION OF LANDS
NOTICE UNDER SECTION 15 (1) OF TAMIL NADU HIGHWAYS ACT, 2001 (TN ACT 34 OF 2002) READ WITH 
TAMIL NADU ACT 38 OF 2019.
(Roc. No. I1/7605/2020.)
No.VI(1)/324(a)/2020.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having  been satisﬁ ed that the lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below are acquired 
under Tamil Nadu Road Sector Project-II  for Highways purpose to wit for 2 way line to 4 way line of SH- 39 Tirunelveli 
– Sengottai - Kollam Road at  K.M 5/000 to 50/600 K.M., Alangulam Village & Taluk, Tirunelveli   District  and it 
having already been decided that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands to be paid out of the 
funds  controlled and  managed by the Divisional Engineer, Tirunelveli (TNRSP-II)  Tamil Nadu after having considering 
the cause shown by the owner or other persons having interest on said lands as the case may do hereby publish the 
following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (TN Act 38/2019).
NOTICE
Under  sub-section (1)  of Section 15 of the Tamil  Nadu  Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 38/2019) the 
Governor  of  Tamil Nadu  hereby  notiﬁ ed  that  the Wet, Dry & Natham lands  speciﬁ ed in the schedule below 
and measuring to an extent  of 9884 sq.mtr. of lands are acquired in   Alangulam Village & Taluk, Tirunelveli  District 
to be the same, a little more or less needed for Tamil Nadu Road Sector Project  for Highways  purpose, to wit for 
2 way line to 4 way line of SH-39 Tirunelveli – Sengottai – Kollam Road at K.M 5/000 to 50/600 K.M.,
The  plan of the lands under acquisition is kept in the oﬃ  ce of the Special District Revenue oﬃ  cer (Land Acquisition), 
Tamil Nadu Road Sector Project-II, Tirunelveli and oﬃ  ce of the Special Tahsildar (Land Acquisition) TNRSP-II  may be 
inspected at any time during the oﬃ  ce hours.
