APPOINTMENT OF COMMISSION OF INQUIRY TO INQUIRE THE INCIDENT OF DEATH OF
THIRU E. ILLAVARASAN, SON OF P. ELANGO ON 4TH JULY 2013 AT DHARMAPURI DISTRICT UNDER
THE COMMISSIONS OF INQUIRY ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 670, Public (Law & Order-F), 8th July 2013.]
No. II(2)/PULO/455(h)/2013.
  WHEREAS the Government of Tamil Nadu are of the opinion that it is necessary to appoint a
Commission of Inquiry headed by a Retired Judge of the High Court of Madras for the purpose of making
an Inquiry into a definite matter of public importance, herein after specified, namely, the incident of death
of Thiru E. Illavarasan, Son of P. Elango on 04-07-2013.
2. NOW,  THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the
Commissions of Inquiry Act, 1952 (Central Act LX of 1952), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints a
Commission of Inquiry consisting of a single member, namely, Justice Thiru S.R. Singharavelu, Retired
Judge of the High Court of Madras to inquire into the facts and circumstances leading to the death of
Thiru E. lllavarasan, Son of  P. Elango, Natham Colony, Dharmapuri District.
3. The term of reference of the Commission of Inquiry shall be as follows:—
“To inquire into the cause of death and circumstances that led to the death of Thiru E. lllavarasan,
son of P. Elango aged about 20 years of Natham Colony, Dharmapuri who was found dead on 4-7-2013
on the Railway Track behind the Government Arts College, Dharmapuri (Adhiyamankottai Police Station
limits, Dharmapuri District).
4. The commission will complete its inquiry and submit its report (both in English and Tamil) to the
State Government within a period of two months from the date of publication of this Notification in the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.

5. AND WHEREAS, the Government of Tamil Nadu are of the opinion having regard to the nature of the
inquiry to be made by the Commission of Inquiry and other circumstances of the case, that all the provisions
of sub-sections (2), (3), (4) and (5) of Section 5 of the Commissions of Inquiry Act 1952 (Central Act LX of
1952) shall be made applicable to the Commission of Inquiry.
6. NOW, THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Commissions of  Inquiry Act, 1952 (Central Act LX of 1952), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that
all the provisions of the said sub-sections (2), (3), (4) and (5) of the said Section 5 of the said Act shall apply
to the Commission of Inquiry.
JATINDRA NATH SWAIN,
