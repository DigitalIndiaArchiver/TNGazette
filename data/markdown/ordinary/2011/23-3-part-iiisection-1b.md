Amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Medical Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 209, Health and Family Welfare (AA1), 23rd July 2010, Aadi 7, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No. SRO B-26/2011.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to  Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Medical
Service (Section 21 in Volume II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1969).
AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules, in Part II, under the heading “Branch I-Medical”, in the Annexure referred to in rule 8 (c),
in column (2) against the entry “Class X-Dieticians in Government Hospitals” in column (1) thereof,—
(1) the first proviso shall be omitted.;
(2) in the second proviso, the expression “further”  shall be omitted.
V.K. SUBBURAJ,
Principal Secretary to Government.
