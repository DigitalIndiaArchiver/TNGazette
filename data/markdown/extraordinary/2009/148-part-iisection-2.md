ACQUISITION OF LANDS
[G.O. Ms. No. 101, Municipal Administration and
Water Supply (MC2), 17th June 2009.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/296(a)/2009.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied
that the lands specified in the Schedule below have to be
acquired for a public purpose and it has already been
decided that the entire amount of compensation to be
awarded for the lands is to be paid out of public revenues.
The following declaration is issued under Section 6 of the
Land Acquisition Act, 1894 (Central Act 1 of 1894).
DECLARATION
Under Section 6 of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894 (Central
Act 1 of 1894), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby declare
that the lands specified in the schedule below and measuring
0.35.0 hectare, be the same little more or less are needed
for a public purpose to wit for the formation of new Bye-pass
Road between Udaiyarpatti to Bye-pass Road.
A plan of the lands is kept in the Office of the Revenue
Divisional Officer, Tirunelveli and may be inspected at any
time during office hours.
