JUDICIAL NOTIFICATIONS
Amendments to the Madras High Court Service Rules.
(Roc.No. 01/2013/RAC.)
No. SRO C-17/2013.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Article 229 (1) and (2) of the Constitution of India and all other powers
connected thereto, the Hon’ble the Acting Chief Justice is pleased to make the following amendments to the Madras High
Court Service Rules:-
AMENDMENT-I
The existing entires in Rule 1.(k) of the Madras High Court Service Rules shall be deleted and the following shall
be substituted thereof:
“(k). “Recruited direct”-A candidate is said to be “recruited direct” to the service when at the time of his first
appointment thereto, he is neither a full member nor an approved probationer in any other service other than the Madras
High Court Service and Tamil Nadu Judicial Ministerial Service.
AMENDMENT-II
In the Special Rules of the Madras High Court Service, in Rule 20, the following shall be added after sub rule (e).
“(f). If no suitable candidate is available for Destitute Widow, Woman and Ex-serviceman turns reserved for them,
then it can be filled by a Woman candidate other than Destitute Widow, Male and non Ex-Serviceman candidates
respectively.”
The above amendments shall come into force with effect from 11th October 2013.
High Court, Madras,
P. KALAIYARASAN,
11th October 2013.
