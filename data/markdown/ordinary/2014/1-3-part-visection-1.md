GENERAL   NOTIFICATIONS
Comprehensive Draft Variation to the Approved Vaigundaperumal Koil Area Extension Detailed Development
plan of Kancheepuram Local Planning Area.
(Roc. No. 5809/2013/DP1.)
No. VI(1)/1/2014.
In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (1) of Section 33 of Town and Country Planning Act, 1971.
(Act No. 35 of 1972) The Commissioner of Town and Country Planning, in the Proceedings Roc No. 5809/2013/DP1,
dated  09-12-2013 Proposes to make the following Comprehensive  Variation to the Approved Vaigundaperumal Koil Area
Extension Detailed Development Plan of Kancheepuram Local Planning Area and Approved by the DTCP Proceeding  in
Roc No. 535/2001 DP1, dated: 30-11-2003 and the fact of this Approval in form No.12, was Published in
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No. 11, Part-VI, Section-1, Page-81, dated: 24-03-2004, Publication No. VI(1)/94/2004.
2.
Any person affected or interested in this draft variation may within SIXTY days from the date of publication  of this
notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, can represent in person or submit in writing to the Member-Secretary,
Kancheepuram Local Planning Authority any objections and suggestions relating thereto.
3.
The Variation with Plan may be inspected free of cost at any time during office hours at the above said Local
Planning Authority Office.
VARIATION
1.
The entire "Vaigundaperumal Koil Area Extension Detailed Development Plan of Kancheepuram Local Planning
Area" is approved by the Director of Town and Country Planning in Proceeding Roc No. 535/2001 DP1,
dated: 30-11-2003 is Comprehensively varied by "Vaigundaperumal Koil Area Extension Detailed Development Plan
of Kancheepuram Local Planning Area".
2.
The Plan thus varied is numbered as Map No. 5, DDP (V) DTCP No. 24/2013.
3.
The Detailed Development Plan Clauses and Schedules annexed with this Plan are made enforceable from the
date of Publication of the confirmed variation notification to be issued under Section 33(2) of the Act and published
in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.

JUDICIAL  NOTIFICATIONS
Constitution of the Sub-Court at Uthangarai in Krishnagiri District.
(Roc. No. 21-C/2011/G/Judn.)
No. VI(1)/5/2014.
In exercise of the powers conferred under the first proviso to Section 13 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873
(Central Act III of 1873) the High Court, Madras hereby directs that appeals from the decrees or orders of the District
Munsif-cum-Judicial magistrate Courts at Uthangarai and Poochampalli be preferred in the court of Subordinate Judge,
Uthangarai with effect on and from the date on which the Subordinate Judge, assumes charge of the Subordinate Judge's
Court at Uthangarai.
Conferment of Powers
(Roc. No. 14792/2013-B6)
No. VI(1)/6/2014.
No.237/2013.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 13 of the Code of Criminal Procedure 1973 (Central Act
2 of 1974) the High Court hereby appoints the following 4 Tahsildars and 2 Deputy Tahsildars in Krishnagiri and
4 Tahsildars in Tiruppur District, to undergo Magisterial Training for a period of 120 days from the date of assumption of
charge and confers upon them the ordinary powers conferrable under this Code on a Judicial Magistrate to try such cases
which were earlier triable and dealt with by an erstwhile Judicial Second Class Magistrate.
