No. SRO B-56/2014.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Rules published with Social Welfare and
Nutritious Meal Programme Department Notification No. SRO B-98/2003 published at page 126 of Part III—Section 1(b)
of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette dated the 3rd December 2003.
2. The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on and from the 12th July 2012.
AMENDMENTS.
In the said Rules,—
(1) In rule 1 for the expression "Social Welfare Department", the expression "Welfare of Differently Abled Persons
Department" shall be substituted;
(2) In rule 3, for the expression "Department of Social Welfare", the expression "Welfare of Differently Abled Persons
Department" shall be substituted.
P. SIVASANKARAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
