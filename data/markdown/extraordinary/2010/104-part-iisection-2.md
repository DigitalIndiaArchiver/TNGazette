MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION AND WATER SUPPLY DEPARTMENT
NOTIFICATIONS UNDER TAMIL NADU DISTRICT MUNICIPALITIES ACT, 1920.
REMOVAL OF CERTAIN PERSON AS CHAIRMAN, KUHALUR TOWN PANCHAYAT,
ERODE DISTRICT UNDER THE ACT.
[G.O. (D) No. 159, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (TP-II), 7th April 2010,
Panguni 24, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/200(a-1)/2010.
Whereas, a written notice of intention to make a motion expressing want of confidence in
Thiru N.R. Subramaniam, Chairman, Kuhalur Town Panchayat, Erode District, signed by twelve Councillors,
out of the sanctioned strength of the fifteen Councillors of the said Town Panchayat Council together
with a copy of the motion proposed to be made have been delivered to the Assistant Director of Town
Panchayats, Erode Zone in person by four Councillors under the provisions of sub-section (2) of Section
40-A of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920);
 And, whereas, the said motion expressing want of confidence in  Thiru N.R. Subramaniam, Chairman
of the said Town Panchayat has been carried out with the support of twelve councillors out of the sanctioned
strength of the fifteen councillors, in the special meeting convened by the Assistant Director of Town
Panchayats, Erode Zone, on 5th November 2009, after following the procedures specified in Section 40-A
of the said Act;
 Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (12) of Section 40-A of the
Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby removes Thiru N.R. Subramaniam, Chairman, Kuhalur Town Panchayat, Erode District from the
Office of Chairman of the said Town Panchayat.

TAMIL  NADU  GOVERNMENT  GAZETTE  EXTRAORDINARY
REMOVAL OF CERTAIN PERSON AS CHAIRMAN, MANAVALAKURICHI TOWN PANCHAYAT,
KANYAKUMARI DISTRICT UNDER THE ACT.
[G.O. (D) No. 160, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (TP-II), 7th April  2010,
Panguni 24, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/200(a-2)/2010.
Whereas, a written notice of intention to make a motion expressing want of confidence in
Tmt. K. Josphin Rita, Chairman, Manavalakurichi Town Panchayat, Kanyakumari District, signed by twelve Councillors,
out of the sanctioned strength of the fifteen Councillors of the said Town Panchayat Council together with a copy
of the motion proposed to be made have been delivered to the Assistant Director of Town Panchayats, Nagercoil
Zone in person by two Councillors under the provisions of sub-section (2) of Section 40-A of the Tamil Nadu District
Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920);
And, whereas, the said motion expressing want of confidence in Tmt. K. Josphin Rita, Chairman, of the said Town
Panchayat  has been carried out with the support of twelve Councillors out of the sanctioned strength of the fifteen
Councillors in the special meeting convened by the Assistant Director of Town Panchayats, Nagercoil Zone, on the
4th November 2009, after following the procedures specified in Section 40-A of the said Act;
Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (12) of Section 40-A of the
Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
removes Tmt. K. Josphin Rita, Chairman, Manavalakurichi Town Panchayat, Kanyakumari District from the Office of
Chairman of the said Town Panchayat.
