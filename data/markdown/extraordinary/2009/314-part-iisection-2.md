HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE  DEPARTMENT
APPOINTMENT OF CERTAIN PERSON AS VICE-CHANCELLOR OF TAMIL NADU DR. M.G.R. MEDICAL
UNIVERSITY, CHENNAI FOR CERTAIN PERIOD UNDER TAMIL NADU DR. M.G.R. MEDICAL
UNIVERSITY ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 397, Health and Family Welfare (MCA 1), 2nd December 2009,
Karthigai 16, Thiruvalluvar Aandu 2040.]
No. II(2)/HF/646(b)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-sections (1) and (3) of Section 10 of the Tamil Nadu
Dr. M.G.R. Medical University Act, 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act, 37 of 1987), the Chancellor hereby appoints
Prof. Mayil Vahanan Natarajan, Head, Department of Orthopaedic Surgery, Madras Medical College and Government
General Hospital, Chennai as the Vice-Chancellor of the said University for a period of three years with effect from
the date of his assuming office or till he attains the age of sixty five years, whichever is earlier.
V.K. SUBBURAJ,
Principal Secretary to Government.
