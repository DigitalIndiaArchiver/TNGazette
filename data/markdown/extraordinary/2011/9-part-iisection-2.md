ACQUISITION OF LANDS
[G.O. Ms. No. 2, Planning, Development and Special
Initiatives (S.I), 6th January 2011, Margazhi 22,
Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No.II(2)/PDSI/8(c)/2011.
Whereas it appears to the Government of Tamil Nadu that
the Lands specified in the Schedule below and situated in
Nerkundram Village, Ambattur Taluk, Tiruvallore District are
needed for a public purpose to wit for the implementation of the
Chennai Metro Rail Project, notice to that effect is hereby given
to all to whom it may concern in accordance with the provisions
of sub-section (1) of Section 4 of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894
(Central Act I of 1894) and whereas, it has become necessary
to acquire the immediate possession of the Lands specified in
the Schedule below, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs
that the lands be acquired under the provisions of
sub-section (1) of the Section 17 of the said Act.
Now, therefore in exercise of the powers conferred by
sub-section (2) of Section 4 of the said Act, the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby authorizes the Special Tahsildar
(Land Acquisition), Kancheepuram Unit, Chennai Metro Rail
Limited, No. 2, Alwarpet Street, Alwarpet, Chennai-18, and his
staff and workman to exercise the powers conferred by the said
sub-sections.
Under section 3(c) of the Act, the Governor of Tamil Nadu,
hereby appoints the Special Tahsildar (Land Acquisition),
Kancheepuram Unit, Chennai Metro Rail Limited, No. 2, Alwarpet
Street, Alwarpet, Chennai–18 to perform the functions of the
Collector under the said Act.
Under sub-section (4) of Section 17 of the said Act, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that in view of the
urgency of the case, the provisions of Section 5A of the said
Act, shall not apply in this case.
