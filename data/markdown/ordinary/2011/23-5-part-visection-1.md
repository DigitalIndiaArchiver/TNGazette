Whereas in exercise of the powers conferred by clause (1) of Article 258 of the Constitution of India, the President
has in the Ministry of Food, Agriculture, Community Development and Co-operation (Department of Agriculture) Notification
No. 4(1)/65, General II, dated on the 23rd April 1966, entrusted to the Government of Tamil Nadu with their consent,
the functions of the Central Government under the Land Acquistion Act, 1894 (Central Act 1 of 1894), in relation to
acqusition of lands for the purpose of the Union in the State.

Commissioner of Land Administration, Chepauk, Chennai-5, hereby declares that the lands specified in the schedule
below and measuring an extent of 0.40.3 hectare are needded to wit, for formation of a New Parallel Broad Gauge
Railway Line between Chengalpattu to Arakkonam.
A plan of the lands is kept in the office of the Special Tahsildar (Land Acquisition), Broad Gauge Railway Line,
Madurandagam and it may be inspected at any time during the office hours.

Confirmation of Variation to the Approved Big Fort Rampart Improvement Detailed Development Plan Part-II
of Thanjavur Local Planning Area.
(Roc.No. 11240/2011/DP3.)
No. VI(1)/265/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 33 (2) of Town and Country Planning Act, 1971, the Director of
Town and Country Planning, Chennai hereby confirms the following variation to the approved Big Fort Rampart
Improvement Detailed Development Plan Part-II of Thanjavur Local Planning Area approved by the Director of Town and
Country Planning in Proceedings Roc. No. 52352/89/D4, dated 13th January 1990 and published in
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, No. 16, Part VI—Section 1, Page Nos. 331-332, dated 25th April 1990 in publication
No. VI(1)/514/90 and the said draft notification was published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No. 44,
Part VI—Section 1, Page No. 336, dated 10th November 2010 in publication No. VI(1)/377/2010.
Since no objections and suggestions have been received on this draft notification within the stipulated time, the same
are hereby confirmed and ordered as below:—
CONFIRMATION OF VARIATION
1.
The entire “Big Fort Rampart Improvement Detailed Development Plan Part-II” of Thanjavur Local Planning Area
approved by the Director of Town and Country Planning in proceedings Roc. No. 52352/89/D4,
dated 13th January 1990 is varied by proceedings of the Director of Town and Country Planning in Roc.No. 3295/2006/DP3,
dated 20th October 2010 of Big Fort Rampart Improvement Detailed Development Plan Part-II of Thanjavur Local
Planning Area.
2.
The Plan thus varied is numbered as Map No. 5 DDP(V)/DTCP No. 40/2010 and,
3.
The Detailed Development Plan clauses and schedules annexed with this plan are made enforceable from the
date of publication of this notification in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
Chennai,
A. KARTHIK,
31st May 2011.
Director of Town and Country Planning.

The following Erratum is issued to Notification No. VI(1)/67/2011, published in Part VI—Section 1 at
