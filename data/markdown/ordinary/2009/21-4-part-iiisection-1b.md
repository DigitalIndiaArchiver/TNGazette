Amendments to Special Rules for Tamil Nadu General Subordinate Service
[G.O.Ms.No.103, Higher Education (F2), 18th May 2009, Vaikasi 4, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. SRO B-34/2009.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu General
Subordinate Service.
2.
The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st January 1986.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules, in Class XIV,—
(1) in rule 1, under the heading “Category,” the expression “Graduate Librarians” shall be omitted;
(2) in rule 2,—
(i)
in sub-rule (a), in the tabular column, the entry “1. Graduate Librarian” in first column and the corresponding
entries in second column shall be omitted;
(ii) in sub-rule (b), the figure “1” shall be omitted;
(3) in rule 4, in the TABLE, for the entries “Graduate Librarians and Librarian” in column (1), the entry “Librarian”
shall be substituted;
(4) in rule 6,—
(i)
in sub-rule (a), in the TABLE, the entries, “Graduate Librarians in Government Colleges” in column (1) and
the corresponding entries in column (2) thereof shall be omitted;
(ii) in sub-rule (b), in the TABLE, the entries, “Graduate Librarians in Government Colleges” in column (1) and
the corresponding entries in column (2) thereof shall be omitted;
(5) in rule 7, in sub-rule (b), the expression “1. Graduate Librarians” shall be omitted;
(6) rule 8 shall be omitted.
Amendments to Special Rules for Tamil Nadu Collegiate Educational Service
[G.O.Ms.No. 104, Higher Education (F2), 18th May 2009, Vaikasi 4, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No.SRO B-35/2009.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Collegiate
Educational Service (Section 8-B in Volume II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1969).
The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st January 1986.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules,—
(1) in rule 1, after Class VIII in column (1) and the corresponding entries in columns (2) and (3) thereof, the
following Class and entries shall, respectively, be added, namely:—
“IX —
College Librarian.”;
(2) in rule 2, in sub-rule (a), after Class VIII in column (1) and the corresponding entries in column (2) thereof,
the following Class and entries shall, respectively, be added, namely:—
“Class IX—
College Librarian
Direct recruitment”;
