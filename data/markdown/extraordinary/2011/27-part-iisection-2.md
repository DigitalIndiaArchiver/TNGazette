The Government having being satisfied the land specified
in the Schedule below has to be acquired for public purpose
and it having already been decided that the amount is to be
paid out of the public revenue towards compensation to be
awarded for the land. The following declaration is issued
under Section 6 of the Land Acquisition Act (Act 1 of 1894)
DECLARATION
 Under Section 6 of the land Acquisition Act, 1894 (Central
Act I of 1894) of Governor of Tamil Nadu herey declares that
the lands specified in the schedule below and measuring
1.31.34 hectares be the same, a little more or less, is needed
for a public purpose for the formation of public road to Anna
Nagar and Kamaraj Nagar hamlets by Rural Development
and Panchayat Raj Department. A plan of the land kept in
the office of the Revenue Divisional Officer, Coonoor Division
and may be inspected at any time during Office hours.
