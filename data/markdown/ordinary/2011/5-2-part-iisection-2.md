PUBLIC DEPARTMENT
(SC)
Order of Detention of certain person and direct to appear
before certain Official under Conservation of Foreign
Exchange and Prevention of Smuggling Activities Act.
[G.O. No. SR.I/476-13/2010, Public (SC), 22nd January 2011.]
No. II(2)/PUSC/71/2011.—Whereas, an order had been
made by the Government of Tamil Nadu in G.O.
No. SR.I/476-7/2010, Public (SC) Department, dated 4th
November 2010 under Section 3(1)(i) of the Conservation
of Foreign Exchange and Prevention of Smuggling Activities
Act, 1974) (Central Act 52 of 1974), directing that Thiru
Ahamed Ibrahim Abdul Jaleel, son of Thiru Ahamed Ibrahim,
resident of No. 4/3, Mela Theru, S.P. Pattinam Post,
Ramanathapuram district, be detained;
And whereas, the said Thiru Ahamed Ibrahim Abdul
Jaleel, son of Thiru Ahamed Ibrahim, resident of No. 4/3,
Mela Theru, S.P. Pattinam Post, Ramanathapuram district,
cannot be found;
And whereas, the Government of Tamil Nadu have
reasons to believe that the said Thiru Ahamed Ibrahim Abdul
Jaleel, son of Thiru Ahamed Ibrahim, resident of No. 4/3,
Mela Theru, S.P. Pattinam Post, Ramanathapuram district
in respect of whom the detention order has been made,
has absconded and is concealing himself so that the said
detention order cannot be executed.
Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by
Section 7(1) (b) of the Conservation of Foreign Exchange
and Prevention of Smuggling Activities Act, 1974 (Central
Act 52 of 1974), the Government of Tamil Nadu hereby
direct the said Thiru Ahamed Ibrahim Abdul Jaleel, son of
Thiru Ahamed Ibrahim, resident of No. 4/3, Mela Theru,
S.P. Pattinam Post, Ramanathapuram district, to appear
before the Superintendent of Police, Ramanathapuram
District, Ramanathapuram at his office within a period of
15 days (Fifteen days) from the date of publication of this
order in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
S. KARUTHIAH PANDIAN,
Secretary to Government.
