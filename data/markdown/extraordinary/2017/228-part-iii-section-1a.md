AMENDMENTS TO THE TAMIL NADU TRANSPARENCY IN TENDERS RULES
[G.O.(Ms) No. 215, Finance (Salaries), 13th July 2017, Aani 29, Hevilambi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2048.]
No.SRO A-33(b)/2017
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 22 of the Tamil Nadu Transparency in Tenders Act, 
1998 (Tamil Nadu Act 43 of 1998), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu 
Transparency in Tenders Rules, 2000.
2. The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st day of July, 2017.
AMENDMENTS
In the said rules, in rule 29, in sub-rule (2) in clause (d),-
 
(i) for the expression "all central duties such as customs duty and central excise duty and sales tax", the expression 
"State Goods and Services Tax, Central Goods and Services Tax, Integrated Goods and Services Tax and all central duties such 
as customs duty" shall be substituted; 
 
(ii) in sub-clause (ii), for the expression "excise duty", occurring in two places the expression "State Goods and Services 
Tax, Central Goods and Services Tax and Integrated Goods and Services Tax" shall be substituted; 
 
(iii) sub-clause (iii) shall be omitted; and
 
(iv) sub-clause (iv) shall be omitted.
