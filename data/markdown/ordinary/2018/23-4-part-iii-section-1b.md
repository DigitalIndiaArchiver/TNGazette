Ad-hoc Rules Relating to the Temporary post of Superintendent, Assistant and Typist in Department of 
Environment in Tamil Nadu Ministerial Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 65, Environment and Forests (EC.2), 14th May 2018,

No. SRO B-42/2018.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following rules:- 
2.  (i) The rules hereby made for the posts of Superintendent and Assistant shall be deemed to have come into force 
on the 13th October 1995. 
 
(ii) The rules hereby made for the post of Typist shall be deemed to have come into force on the 20th October 2003.
RULES 
The General and the Special Rules applicable to the holders of the permanent posts in Categories 1,12 and 17 of the 
Tamil Nadu Ministerial Service shall, respectively, apply to the holders of the temporary posts of Superintendent, Assistant 
and Typist, sanctioned from time to time for the Department of Environment, subject to the modiﬁ cations speciﬁ ed in the 
following rules:- 
2. Constitution.- The posts shall constitute as temporary additions to the respective categories in the said Service. 
3. Appointing authority.- The appointing authority for the posts shall be the Director, Department of Environment. 
4. Promotions.-(1) Promotion to the post of Superintendent shall be with reference to the seniority list of Assistant in 
the Department of Environment. 
(2) Promotion to the post of Assistant shall be with reference to the combined seniority list of Junior Assistant, Typist 
and Steno- Typist Grade-III in the Department of Environment. 
5. Tests:-(1) No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post of Superintendent by promotion, unless he has 
passed the Account Test for Subordinate Ofﬁ cers, Part - II. 
 
(2) No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post of Assistant by promotion unless he has passed the 
Account Test for Subordinate Ofﬁ cers; Part - I and the Tamil Nadu Government Ofﬁ ce Manual Test. 
6. Pay.- (1) The holders of the post of Superintendent shall be paid a monthly pay calculated in the scale of
Rs.1600-50-2300-60-2660 and the holders of the post of Assistant shall be paid a monthly pay calculated in the scale of
Rs. 1200-30-1560-40-2040 : 
Provided that on and from the 1st January 1996, the holders of the post of Superintendent shall be paid a monthly pay 
calculated in the scale of Rs.5700-175-9200 and the holders of the post of Assistant shall be paid a monthly pay calculated 
in the scale of Rs. 4000-100-6000: 
Provided also that on and from the 1st January 2006, the holders of the post of Superintendent shall be paid a monthly 
pay calculated in the Pay Band of PB2 Rs.9,300-34,800 with Grade Pay of Rs. 4,300 and the holders of the post of Assistant 
shall be paid a monthly pay calculated in the Pay Band of PBl Rs.5,200-20, 200 with Grade Pay of Rs 2,800. 
 
(2) The holders of the post of Typist shall be paid a monthly pay calculated in the scale of Rs.3200-85-4900: 
Provided that on and from the 1st January of 2006, the holders of the post of Typist shall be paid a monthly pay 
calculated in the Pay Band of PB2 Rs.5200-20200 with Grade Pay of Rs.2,400. 
This order issue with the concurrence of Finance Department vide its U.O.No.19532/(AHDF)/ 18 dated: 07-05-18.
Md. NASIMUDDIN,
Principal Secretary to Government.

No. SRO B-43/2018.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following rules:- 
The rules hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force with effect from on 22nd September, 1970. 
RULES
The General and the Special Rules applicable to the holders of the permanent post in the Tamil Nadu Handlooms 
and Textiles Subordinate Service shall apply to the holders of the temporary post of Driver in the Handlooms and Textiles 
Department, subject to the modiﬁ cations speciﬁ ed in the following rules, namely: – 
2. Constitution:– The post shall constitute a separate category in the said service. 
3. Appointment:– Appointment to the post shall be made as follows: – 
 
(i)  By direct recruitment (or) 
 
(ii)  By recruitment by transfer from Tamil Nadu Basic Service. 
4. Appointing Authority:– The appointing authority for the post shall be as follows: –

5. Qualiﬁ cation: – (a) Age: No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post by direct recruitment, if he has 
completed or will complete thirty years of age on the ﬁ rst day of July of the year in which the selection for appointment is made. 
 
(b). Other Qualiﬁ cations: – No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post unless he possesses the 
following qualiﬁ cations, namely: – 
 
 
(i)  Must have passed VIII Standard; 
 
 
(ii)  Must possess a current driving license (i.e. Light Motor Vehicle and Heavy Motor Vehicle) issued by a 
licensing authority speciﬁ ed in rule 4 of the Tamil Nadu Motor Vehicles Rules, 1940; and 
 
 
(iii)  Must have practical experience in driving light motor vehicles for a period of not less than two years. 
6. Reservation of appointments:– The rule relating to reservation of appointments (General rule 22) shall apply for 
appointment to the post by direct recruitment.
7. Probation:– Every person appointed to the post by direct recruitment shall, from the date on which he joins duty, be 
on probation for a total period of two years on duty within a continuous period of three years. 
Provided that every person appointed to the post by recruitment by transfer shall, from the date on which he joined duty, 
be on probation for a total period of one year on duty within a continuous period of two years; 
8. Pay:– The holder of the post will be paid a monthly pay calculated in the scale of pay Basic Scale 50-1-60 and as 
revised from time to time. Provided that the monthly pay shall be calculated in the pay band of Rs.5200-20200 Grade pay 
of Rs. 2400 with effect from 01-01-2006. 
Provided further that the monthly pay shall be calculated in the Pay Matrix of level-8 - Rs.19,500- 62,000 with effect 
from 01-01-2016. 
9. Saving Clause:- Nothing contained in these rules shall adversely affect any person holding the post on the date of 
issue of these rules.
