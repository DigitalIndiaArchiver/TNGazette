JUDICIAL NOTIFICATION
CONSTITUTION OF SUB COURT AT GUDALUR IN NILGIRIS DISTRICT
(Roc. No. 133/2013/G/Judn.)
No. VI(1)/279(a)/2017.
“In exercise of the powers conferred under the first  proviso to Section 13 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873
(Central Act III of 1873) the High Court, Madras hereby  directs that appeals from the decrees or orders of
the District  Munsif Court, Gudalur including two villages viz., Naduvattam and Masinagudi of Udhagamandalam
Taluk be preferred in the court of Subordinate Judge, Gudalur with effect on and from the date on which the Subordinate
Judge, Gudalur assumes charge of that Court.”
High Court, Madras,
R. SAKTHIVEL,
6th September 2017.
