ENERGY   DEPARTMENT
CONSTITUTION OF SELECTION COMMITTEE FOR SELECTING OF THE MEMBER
OF THE TAMIL NADU ELECTRICITY REGULATORY COMMISSION UNDER THE
ELECTRICITY ACT 2003.
AMENDMENT TO NOTIFICATION
[G.O. Ms. No. 95, Energy (D1), 26th September 2013, Purattasi 10,
Vijaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2044.]
No. II(2)/EGY/749(b)/2013.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 85 of the Electricity Act,
2003 (Central Act 36 of 2003), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the
following amendment to the Energy Department Notification No.II(2)/EGY/692(a)/2013,
dated the 4th September, 2013, published on page 1 of Part II—Section 2 of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 4th September, 2013.
AMENDMENT
In the said Notification, in the first paragraph, for the expression “3. The Chairperson of the
Central Electricity Regulatory Commission”, the expression “3. The Chairperson of the
Central Electricity Authority” shall be substituted.
RAJESH LAKHONI,
      Secretary to Government.
