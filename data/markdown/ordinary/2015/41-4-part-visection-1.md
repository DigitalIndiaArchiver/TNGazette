(L.F. 1072/ICA/2002)
No.VI(1)/256/2015.
“In the circumstances stated by the Assistant Director of Industries and Commerce (Industrial Co-operatives),
District lndustries Centre, SalemIOfficial Liquidator of the Annathanapatty Harijan Cotton Rope Workers Industrial
Co-operative Society Ltd. Ind. No. 921 in the final closure proposals dated 21-08-2015 and in exercise of the powers
