MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION AND WATER SUPPLY DEPARTMENT
AMENDMENTS TO THE TAMIL NADU URBAN LOCAL BODIES LICENSING OF HOARDINGS AND LEVY
AND COLLECTION OF ADVERTISEMENT TAX RULES, 2003.
[G.O. Ms. No. 58, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (MC2), 29th March 2010.]
No. SRO A-11(b)/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 430 of the Coimbatore City Municipal Corporation Act, 1981
(Tamil Nadu Act 25 of 1981), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu
Urban Local Bodies Licensing of Hoardings and Levy and collection of Advertisement Tax Rules, 2003:—
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules, in rule 6,—
(1)
The following proviso shall be added to sub-rule (1), namely:—
“Provided that in respect of a hoarding to be erected upon a bus shelter and public convenience built by
Coimbatore City Municipal Corporation, the maximum size shall be 3 metres X 9 metres (Horizontal Position)
irrespective of the width of the road ”;
(2)
after sub-rule (6), the following sub-rule shall be added, namely:—
“(7) Nothing contained in sub-rules (3) and (6) shall apply to any hoarding erected upon a bus shelter, and
public convenience built by Coimbatore City Municipal Corporation.  Provided such hoardings shall be erected after
leaving sufficient space for the use of pedestrians”.
NIRANJAN MARDI,
Secretary to Government.
