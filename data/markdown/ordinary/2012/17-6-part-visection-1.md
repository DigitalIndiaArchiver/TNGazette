GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
Final Closing and Cancellation of Registration of certain Co-operative Societies
KVI NGL 475. Manoor Jaggery Manufacturing Co-operative Society
(R.C. No. 1410/C2/1990.)
No. VI(1)/140/2012.
In the circumstances stated by the Liquidator in his final closer proceedings, dated 24th February 2012 and in
secrecies of the powers delegated under Section 140(1) of the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act, 1983, the
registration of the society KVI NGL 475 is hereby cancelled and its affairs are finally closed with effect from
26th March 2012.
KVI  292. Vazhathottam Jaggery Manufacturing Co-operative Society
(R.C.. No. 8835/C2/1990.)
No. VI(1)/141/2012.
In the circumstances stated by the Liquidator in his final closer proceedings, dated 24th February 2012 and in
secrecies of the powers delegated under Section 140(1) of the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act, 1983, the
registration of the society KVI 292 is hereby cancelled and its affairs are finally closed with effect from
26th March 2012.
KVI NGL  13. Kuzhivilai Jaggery Manufacturing Co-operative Society
(Roc. No. 13073/C2/1986.)
No. VI(1)/142/2012.
In the circumstances stated by the Liquidator in his final closer proceedings, dated 24th February 2012 and in
secrecies of the powers delegated under Section 140(1) of the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act, 1983, the
registration of the society KVI NGL 13 is hereby cancelled and its affairs are finally closed with effect from
26th March 2012.
Nagercoil,
G. ARUNACHALAM,
