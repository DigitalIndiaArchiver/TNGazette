B)	
the sub-clause v and viii under Clause VI shall be replaced as 	
v.	
Shops selling essential goods including Tea Shops (Parcel only) will function from 6:00 am to 7:00 pm. 
In the Tea Shops, the Social distancing norms to be followed strictly. The tea shop should be maintained 
clean and hygienic by disinfecting five times a day. No customers should be permitted to consume food 
items inside the tea shops. Any violation of these conditions, the tea shops will be sealed immediately.  
viii.	
All standalone and neighborhood shops (except barber shops, salons, spa & beauty parlours), shops 
selling construction hardware, cement, construction materials, sanitary ware, electrical materials and 
standalone shops selling and servicing mobile phones, computers, household appliances, motors, 
spectacles will function from 10:30 am to 6:00 pm
C)	
the following sub-clause shall be added under Clause VII 	
xx.	
Shops selling essential goods including Tea Shops (Parcel only) will function from 6:00 am to 7:00 pm. 
In the Tea Shops, the Social distancing norms to be followed strictly. The tea shop should be maintained 
clean and hygienic by disinfecting five times a day. No customers should be permitted to consume food 
items inside the tea shops. Any violation of these conditions, the tea shops will be sealed immediately.  
xxi.	
Petrol pumps will function from 6:00 am to 8:00 pm.
xxii.	
Private Offices(Standalone Air conditioners only permitted) including construction companies & real 
estate developers back office will function from 10:00 am to 7:00 pm with 33% staff. Not more than 5 
employees permitted for Post production activities like Editing, Dubbing, DI, Re-Recording Sound 
Design/Mixing and VFX/CGI (10 to 15 persons only) for Television & film content from 10:00 am to 
7:00 pm. Permission should be obtained from concerned District Collectors for employees.
D)	
the sub-clause xiv, xv, xvi and xix under Clause VII shall be replaced as	
xiv.	
Shops selling construction hardware, cement, construction materials, sanitary ware and electrical items 
shall be permitted to function from 10:00 am to 7:00 pm in both urban and rural areas. There shall be no 
restriction on transport of construction materials.
xv.	
All standalone shops selling and servicing mobile phones, computers, household articles, motors, 
spectacles shall be permitted to function from 10:00 am to 7:00 pm in both urban and rural areas.
xvi.	
All standalone shops (except barber shops, salons, spa & beauty parlours) in Rural areas will be 
permitted to function from 10:00 am to 7:00 pm.
xix.	
District Collector may decide on opening of all other standalone shops (except Malls and Market 
Complexes) in Corporations and Municipal Limits based on the prevailing conditions from 10:00 am to 
7:00 pm (barber shops, salons, spa & beauty parlours are not permitted)
 E)	
the following sub-clause shall be added under Clause IX. 
xx.	
Petrol pumps on National and State Highways will function 24 Hours.
