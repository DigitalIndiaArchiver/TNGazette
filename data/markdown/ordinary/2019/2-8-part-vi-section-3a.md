GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI.
Taking Over the Management of Hazrat Syed Gulam Asadullah (RH) Hazrat Syed Zahoorullah Sha Kadari (RH), 
Triplicane, Chennai District.
(Rc No. 19453/08/B6/Chennai)
No. VI-3(a)/7/2019.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act 1995, the 
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in item No.183/18 in Rc.No.19453/08/B6/Chennai dated 4-12-2018 has ordered 
to take over the management of Hazrat Syed Gulam Asadullah (RH) Hazrat Syed Zahoorullah Sha Kadari (RH), 
Ice House, Triplicane, Chennai-5 under the Direct control of the Tamil Nadu Waqf Board for a period of one year 
from 4-12-2018 to 3-12-2019 and  has appointed the Waqf Inspector, Chennai (South) as its Executive Oﬃ  cer.
Chennai - 600 001, 
N.S. MOHAMED ASLAM,
3rd January 2019.

Chief Executive Oﬃ  cer,

(Rc No. 813/08/B1/Tuti)
No. VI-3(a)/8/2019.

the Tamil Nadu Waqf Board in its Resolution in item No.80/18, in Rc.No.813/08/B1/Tuticorin, dated 4-12-2018 have 
resolved to cancel the By Law framed for Jamia Pallivasal, Tuticorin District notiﬁ ed in the Tamil Nadu Government 
Gazette Notiﬁ cation No. 44, Part VI-3(a) Section, dated:31-10-2018.
