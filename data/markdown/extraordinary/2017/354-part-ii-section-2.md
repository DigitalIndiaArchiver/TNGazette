APPOINTMENT OF COMMISSION OF INQUIRY TO INQUIRE INTO CAUSES AND CIRCUMSTANCES LEADING TO THE
LAW AND ORDER DISTURBANCES WHICH TOOK PLACE ON 23rd JANUARY 2017 AT CHENNAI, MADURAI,
COIMBATORE AND OTHER PARTS OF THE STATE FOLLOWING THE AGITATION FOR HOLDING OF JALLIKATTU
UNDER THE COMMISSIONS OF INQUIRY ACT.
AMENDMENT TO NOTIFICATION
[G.O. Ms. No. 928, Public (Law & Order-F) 1st November 2017, Aippasi 15, Hevilambi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2048.]
No. II(2)/PULO/908(a)/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Commissions of Inquiry Act, 1952
(Central Act LX of 1952), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Public
(Law and Order-F) Department Notification No.ll(2)/PULO/76(f)/2017, published at pages 1-2 of Part ll -Section 2 of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, Extraordinary, dated the 31st January 2017, as subsequently amended.
AMENDMENT
In the said Notification, in paragraph 4, for the expression “nine  months”, the expression “twelve months” shall be
substituted.
P.SENTHILKUMAR,
Principal Secretary to Government (FAC).
