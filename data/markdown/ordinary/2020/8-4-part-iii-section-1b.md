AGRICULTURE DEPARTMENT
Amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Agricultural Extension Service.
[G.O. Ms. No.21, Agriculture (AA1), 27th January 2020.]
No. SRO B-8/2020.— In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Agricultural Extension 
Service [Section 36 in Volume II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 2016].
 2. The amendments hereby made shall come into force with eﬀ ect on and from 27th January 2020.
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖග.
In the said Special Rules, in the Annexure, for the entry in column (3) against the entry "Joint Director of Agriculture 
(Extension)" under Class-I in column (1), the following entry shall be substituted, namely:-
 “Must have served as Deputy Director of Agriculture (Extension) for a period of not less than one year"
Amendment to the Ad hoc Rules for the Post of Additional Director of Agriculture (Extension) in Tamil Nadu 
Agricultural Extension Service.
[G.O. Ms. No.22, Agriculture (AA1), 27th January 2020.]
No. SRO B-9/2020.— In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, 
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Ad hoc Rules, published in the Agriculture 
Department Notiﬁ cation No. S.R.O.310/84, Agriculture, dated the 30th July 1984 at page 373 of part III–Section 1(b) of the 
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 22nd August 1984 as subsequently Amended.
(3) The Amendment hereby made shall come into force with eﬀ ect on and from 27th January 2020.
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖග.
In the said Rules, in rule 4, for the expression "one year" the expression "six months", shall be substituted.
GAGANDEEP SINGH BEDI, 
Agricultural Production Commissioner and 
Principal Secretary to Government.
