Extension of period of Direct Management of Ilayangudi Nesavu Pattadai Tholugai Medai Pallivasal,
Ilayangudi, Sivagangai District.
(Lr. Rc. No. 6291/99/SVG/B16.)
No. VI-3(a)/110/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act 1995, the Tamil Nadu
Wakf Board in its resolution in Item No. 136/10 (Rc. No. 6291/99/B16/SVG), dated, 18th July 2010 has ordered to
extend the period of direct management of Ilayangudi Nesavu Pattadai Tholugai Medai Pallivasal, Ilayangudi-630 702
