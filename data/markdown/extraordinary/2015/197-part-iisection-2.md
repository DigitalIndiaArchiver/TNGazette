sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of

1999).
NOTIFICATION

Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act 1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999) the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
acquires the lands specified in the schedule below and
measuring  18.63.5 Hectares  to the same a little more or
less needed for the formation of SIPCOT Panapakkam
Industrial Park Scheme,  Arakkonam.

Deputy Collector  (Land Acquisition) SIPCOT Panapakkam
Scheme Arakkonam Taluk and may be inspected at any time
during office hours.
