MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION AND WATER SUPPLY DEPARTMENT
UPGRADATION OF KARAIKUDI SELECTION GRADE MUNICIPALITY CLASSIFIED AS SPECIAL GRADE MUNICIPALITY
IN SIVAGANGAI DISTRICT UNDER THE TAMIL NADU DISTRICT MUNICIPALITIES ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 74, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (MA.5), 28th May 2013, Vaikasi 14, Vijaya,
Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2044.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/391(f-1)/2013.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (6) of Section 4 of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920
(Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby classifies the Karaikudi Municipality in Sivagangai District
into Special Grade Municipality.
UPGRADATION OF SIVAKASI SELECTION GRADE MUNICIPALITY CLASSIFIED AS SPECIAL GRADE MUNICIPALITY
IN VIRUDHUNAGAR DISTRICT UNDER THE TAMIL NADU DISTRICT MUNICIPALITIES ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 75, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (MA.5), 28th May 2013, Vaikasi 14, Vijaya,
Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2044.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/391(f-2)/2013.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (6) of Section 4 of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920
(Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby classifies the Sivakasi Selection Grade Municipality in
Virudhunagar District into Special Grade Municipality.
PHANINDRA REDDY,
Secretary to Government.
