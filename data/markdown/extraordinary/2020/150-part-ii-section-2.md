TAMIL  NADU  GOVERNMENT  GAZETTE  EXTRAORDINARY
WHEREAS based on the 2nd and 3rd addendum sent by the Government of India, Ministry of Home Affairs amendments 
were issued in the Government orders third and fourth read above respectively to the Notification issued in the order second 
read above. 
WHEREAS the Principal Secretary/Commissioner of Revenue Administration in the letter sixth read above has stated 
that the Government of India, Ministry of Home Affairs in the letter fifth read above issued 4th Addendum to the guidelines 
and the addendum has been got reconciled with the Government orders second, third and fourth read above and sent the 
amendment proposal to the Notification issued in the Government order second cited read with Notification issued in the 
order third and fourth read above.
NOW THEREFORE the State Government of Tamil Nadu based on the proposal of Principal Secretary and Commissioner 
of Revenue Administration issues following amendment to the Notification issued in the order second read above read with 
Notification issued in the order third and fourth read above.
	
A.	 the sub-clause (m) and (n) in exception to clause 4 may be added.
	
	
(m)	Shops of agriculture machinery, its spare parts (including its supply chain) and repairs to remain open.
 
 
(n) Shops for truck repairs on highways, preferably at fuel pumps.
	
B.	 the sub-clause (f) in exception to clause 5 may be added.
	
	
(f)	 Tea industry, including plantation with maximum of 50% workers.
Note: As specified in the lockdown measures, social distancing and proper hygiene practices must be ensured in each of 
the above activities as well as the activities permitted by earlier orders. It will be the responsibility of the head of the 
organization/establishment to ensure compliance of such norms.  The district authorities will ensure strict enforcement.
(By order of the Governor)
K.SHANMUGAM,
Chief Secretary.
