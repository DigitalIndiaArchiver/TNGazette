The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that the lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below are acquired 
under Chennai – Kanniyakumari Industrial Corridor Project for Highways purpose to Widening and Strengthening in SH-142, 
Thuraiyur - Perambalur Road from KM 11/500 to KM 15/300 at 65.Nakkasalem Village in Alathur Taluk, Perambalur District, 
and it having already been decided that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands to be paid out of

NOTICE

are acquired in 65. Nakkasalem Village in Alathur Taluk, Perambalur District, to the same, a little more or less is needed for

in SH-142, Thuraiyur - Perambalur Road from KM 11/500 to 15/300. 
The plan of the lands under acquisition are kept in the oﬃ  ce of the Special District Revenue oﬃ  cer (Land Acquisition), 
Chennai - Kanniyakumari Industrial Corridor Project (CKICP) Salem and oﬃ  ce of the Special Tahsildar (Land Acquisition),

the oﬃ  ce hours.
