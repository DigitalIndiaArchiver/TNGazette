NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
ANIMAL HUSBANDRY, DAIRYING AND FISHERIES DEPARTMENT
APPOINTMENT OF FIRST VICE-CHANCELLOR  OF  TAMIL NADU FISHERIES UNIVERSITY
FOR CERTAIN PERIOD UNDER THE TAMIL NADU FISHERIES UNIVERSITY ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 199, Animal Husbandry, Dairying & Fisheries (FS-4), 26th November 2012,
Karthigai 11, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2043.]
No. II(2)/ADFI/762(f)/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 49 of the  Tamil Nadu Fisheries University Act, 2012
(Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 2012) the Government hereby appoint Dr. M.C. Nandheesha, Special Officer,
Tamil Nadu Fisheries University as the First Vice-Chancellor of Tamil Nadu Fisheries University,
Nagapattinam for a period of three years from the date of his assuming office.
2. The terms and conditions of the appointment will be issued separately.
GAGANDEEP SINGH BEDI,
Secretary to Government.
