NOTIFICATIONS  BY  THE  LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY SECRETARIAT
LEGISLATIVE  ASSEMBLY SECRETARIAT
PROROGATION OF TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY
[S.O. Ms. No. 115, Legislative Assembly Secretariat, 8th September, 2014,
Aavani 23, Jaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2045.]
No. II(1)/LAS/34(d)/2014.
“In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-clause (a) of clause (2) of Article 174 of the Constitution
of India, I, Dr. K. ROSAIAH, Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby prorogue with effect from the
date of this notification, the session of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly which commenced on the
30th January, 2014.
Raj Bhavan,
K. ROSAIAH,
Guindy,
Governor of Tamil Nadu.
Chennai-600 022,
Dated: 6th September, 2014.
A.M.P. JAMALUDEEN,
Secretary”.
