No.II(2)/EF/285/2018.—In exercise of the powers 
conferred by Section 16 of the Tamil Nadu Forest Act, 1882 
(Tamil Nadu Act V of 1882) the Governor of Tamil Nadu 
hereby declares the area, the limits of which are speciﬁ ed 
in the Schedule below as reserved forest with effect on 
and from the 10-10-2018.
SCHEDULE
1. Name of the District 
: 
Dindigul
2. Name of the Taluk 
: 
Kodaikanal
  
  3. Number and name of the  
: 
1 Kookal
    Village
  4. Survey Field Number 
 : 
273
  5. Name of the Forest Blook  : 
Kookal-II
  6. Extent of the Forest Block  : 
0.53.50   
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Hectares
BOUNDARIES
North.- The Boundary line starts from the junction 
point of Survey Field Numbers 272, 250, 274 and 273 of 
Number 1 Kookal Village and runs towards eastern side 
along the southern boundaries of Survey Field Number 
274 of Kookal Village and meets the Junction Point of 
Survey Field Numbers 274 and 273 of Village Number 1 
Kookal Village.
East.- Thence, the boundary generally runs towards 
south side along the western boundaries of Survey Field 
Numbers 274 and 275 of Kookal Village and meets the 
tri-junction point of Survey Field Numbers 275, 267 and 
273 of Kookal village.
South.- Thence, the boundary generally runs towards 
west along the northern boundaries of Survey Field Number 
267 of Kookal Village and meets the tri-junction point of 
Survey Field Numbers 267, 272 and 273 of Kookal Village.
West.- Thence, the boundary generally runs towards 
north along the eastern boundary of Survey Field Number 
272 of Kookal Village till it meets the starting point.
