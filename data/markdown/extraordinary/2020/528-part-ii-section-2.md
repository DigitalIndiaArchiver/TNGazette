On the recommendations of the expert team of Doctors and Public Health specialists and based on 
the directives of Government of India, Ministry of Home Aﬀ airs, State-wide lockdown was extended from 
time to time and lastly extended till 24:00 Hrs of 31-12-2020 under the Disaster Management Act, 2005 
with certain restrictions and many relaxations along with guidelines in G.O.Ms.No.673, Revenue and Disaster 
Management (DM IV) Department, Dated : 30-11-2020.
2. Now, in the Hon'ble Chief Minister Press Release, dated 16-12-2020, it has been stated that 
following the Standard Operating Procedures, Social, Political, Sports, Academic, Cultural, Entertainment 
and Religious gatherings shall be permitted in open spaces from 19-12-2020, with a maximum of 50% 
of the participants of the total capacity and size of the open spaces where the above said gatherings 
are proposed and by strictly adhering to social distancing norms. It has been further stated that prior 
permission shall be obtained from the Commissioner of Police in respect of Chennai City and from the 
District Collector in respect of other districts and Additional Chief Secretary and Commissioner of Revenue 
Administration has sent the amendment proposal accordingly.
3. In accordance with the Hon'ble Chief Minister Press Release, dated 16-12-2020, and based 
on the proposal of the Additional Chief Secretary and Commissioner of Revenue Administration, the 
Government hereby issue following amendment to the G.O.Ms.No.673, Revenue and Disaster Management 
(DM IV) Department, Dated 30-11-2020 as detailed below:

vii. Further Social, Political, Sports, Academic, Cultural, Entertainment and Religious gatherings, shall be
