ENERGY DEPARTMENT
NOTIFICATION REGARDING EXEMPTION IN RESPECT OF THE ELECTRICITY TAX PAYABLE BY THE
BRITISH DEPUTY HIGH COMMISSION, CHENNAI  UNDER THE TAMIL NADU TAX ON CONSUMPTION OR
SALE OF ELECTRICITY ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 7, Energy (B1), 21st January 2010.]
No. II(2)/EGY/32(d)/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 14 of the Tamil Nadu Tax on Consumption
or Sale of Electricity Act, 2003 (Tamil Nadu Act 12 of 2003), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby exempts the
British Deputy High Commission, Chennai,  in respect of the Electricity Tax payable under the said Act on the
consumption of electricity for own use  by Captive Generating Plant.
P.W.C. DAVIDAR,
Secretary to Government.
