The Governor of Tamil Nadu is pleased to institute the Award of the "Tamil Nadu Chief Minister's Medal for Excellence 
in Forensic Science" to be conferred on the Forensic Science Staff from the rank of Junior Scientiﬁ c Ofﬁ cer to the Joint 
Director and hereby makes the following Rules governing the award of the said Medal:- 
1. The Medal shall be designated as the "Tamil Nadu Chief Minister's Medal for Excellence in Forensic Science" and 
shall be applicable to all those from the rank of Junior Scientiﬁ c Ofﬁ cer to the Joint Director. 
2. The award shall be in the form of a Medal styled and designated as the "Tamil Nadu Chief Minister's Medal for 
Excellence in Forensic Science" (Hereinafter referred to as the Medal).

4. The riband of the Medal, an inch and three eighth in width shall be full violet with ﬁ ve vertical white lines. 
5. The Medal shall only be awarded to those members of the Tamil Nadu Forensic Sciences Department mentioned in 
rule 1. 
6. The Medal shall be awarded:- 
 
(i)  For contributing innovative scientiﬁ c methodology in any of the ﬁ elds of Forensic Science, having potential 
application in the identiﬁ cation and individualization of physical evidence; 
 
(ii)  For contributing towards scientiﬁ c research in Forensic Science and/or scientiﬁ c publications in peer-reviewed 
national and international journals on Forensic Science; 
 
(iii)  For locating/collecting/identifying physical evidence or clue materials at the scene of occurrence [SOC] and/or 
reconstruction of sequence of events that had taken place at SOC, which solely contribute to the detection of sensational 
cases; 
 
(iv)  For those who contribute ﬁ eld criminalistics services towards successful detection of crimes through scientiﬁ c 
means in maximum number of cases; and 
 
(v)  For exhibiting conspicuous devotion to duty over a period of 10 years or more in service. For consideration 
under this clause, service seniority should be taken into account. The members will be eligible only once in their service for 
being awarded a Medal under this clause. 
7. For awarding a Medal under rule 6(i) to 6(iv), there shall be a gap of three years. Service seniority should be taken 
into account when all other criteria for the selection of the above Medal are equal.
8. Total number of Medals awarded in a year shall not exceed two.

9. The names of those to whom the Medal may be awarded shall be published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette 
and a Register of such names shall be kept by the Directorate of Forensic Science as well as the Government of Tamil Nadu 
in the Home Department by such person as the Governor of Tamil Nadu may direct. 
10. The recipients of this Medal shall be granted lump-sum grant for different ranks as indicated below:- 
 
(i)  Junior Scientiﬁ c Ofﬁ cer/  
 
Rs.10,000/-  
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Scientiﬁ c Ofﬁ cer 
 
(ii)  Assistant Director/Deputy Director/  
Rs.20,000/-  
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Joint Director 
11. As the Medal will carry the Chief Minister's name, the nominations for this award should be made only after meticulous 
and detailed consideration. 
12. The Medal is liable to be forfeited if the holder subsequently displays lack of integrity, disloyalty, indiscipline, negligence, 
cowardice in action or such conduct as in the opinion of the Governor brings the department into disrepute. The lump-sum 
grant paid will also be recovered from the recipient. 
13. The Medal Board in each range consisting of Deputy Directors under a senior Deputy Director as Chairman, 
recommend eligible names to the Directorate of Forensic Science. The Ofﬁ ce Medal Committee at the Directorate of Forensic 
Science, Chennai, will vet the proposals, select the suitable nominee and forward its recommendation to Government by 31st 
July each year. A Scrutinizing Committee consisting of the following shall scrutinize the recommendations of the Director and 
ﬁ nalize the case to be recommended for the award before 15th of August every year. 
 
(i)  Secretary to Government, Home Department; 
 
(ii)  Director General of Police, Chennai; 
 
 
(in the absence of Director General of Police, the senior most Additional Director General of Police   
 
 
      or Inspector General of Police).
 
(iii)  Additional Director General of Police, Crime Branch CID. 
 
(iv)  Director, Forensic Sciences Department. 
 
(v)  Director, Tamil Nadu Judicial Academy. 
14. The Medal shall be awarded on the occasion of the birthday of Dr. Anna on 15th September, every year, and 
presented to the recipients by the Hon'ble Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu at a function. 
15. The expenditure should be debited to the following head of account:- 
 
"2055 00 Police 116 Forensic Science - State's Expenditure - AA Forensic Science and Chemical Laboratory - 59

(DP Code 2055 00 116 AA 5903)
