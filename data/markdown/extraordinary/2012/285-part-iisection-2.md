NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
DTP—II-2 Ex. (285)
TRANSPORT DEPARTMENT
ACQUISITION OF LANDS
[G.O. Ms. No.160, Transport (I-1), 9th October 2012.]
No. II(2)/TRA/647(d)/2012.
Whereas, in exercise of the powers conferred by clause (1)
of Article 258 of the Constitution, the President has entrusted
to the Government of Tamil Nadu in Letter No. 4(1)/65
Gentral  II Food, Agriculture, Community Development and
Co-operation (Agriculture), dated 23rd April 1966, with their
consent the functions of the Central Government under the
Land Acquisition Act, 1894 (Central Act I of 1894), in relation
to acquisition of lands for the purpose of the Union in this
State. And, whereas, it has already been decided that the
compensation should be paid by the public revenues;
Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by
Section 6 of the  said Act, the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby declares that the lands specified in the Schedule
below and measuring 0.04.205 hectare, be the same, a
little more or less, is needed for a public purpose, to wit,
for the Transshipment facility at Thiruvarur Junction between
Mayiladuthurai-Thiruvarur-Karaikudi  Section in Thiruvarur
Town, Thiruvarur Taluk of Thiruvarur District and under
Sections 3 and 7 of the same Act. The Revenue Divisional
Officer Thiruvarur is appointed to perform the functions of
a Collector under the Act, and directed to take order for the
acquisition of the said lands. Under sub-sections (1) and
(2) of Section 17 of the Act, the Governor of Tamil Nadu
further directs that the possession of the said lands may be
taken immediately after the publication of the notice
mentioned in Section 9(1) of the Act. A plan of the lands is
kept in the office of the Revenue Divisional Officer, Thiruvarur
and may be inspected at any time during office hours.
SCHEDULE
Thiruvarur District, Thiruvarur  Taluk,
Thiruvarur Town.
Ward-3, Block-13.
Dry, T.S.No. 537/61, belonging to Sundari, wife of
Ramakrishnan, bounded on the north by T.S.No. 537/59,
east by T.S.No. 537/62, south by T.S.No. 537/59 and west
by T.S.No. 537/60—0.02.105 hectare.
Dry, T.S.No. 537/62, belonging to Veerasamy, son of
Tholan, bounded on the north by T.S.No. 537/59, east by
T.S.No. 536, south by T.S.No. 536 and west by T.S.Nos.
537/61 and 537/59—0.02.100 hectare.
Total—0.04.205 hectare.
