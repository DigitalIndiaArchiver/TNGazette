Election of Two Members to the Syndicate, Two Members to the Senate and Three Members to the
Standing Committee of the Academic Council by the Academic Council.
(LR. NO. 1123/REGR/ACA/A1/2012.)
No. VI-3(b)/21/2012.
It is hereby notified that the election to [i] THE SYNDICATE [ii] THE SENATE and [iii], THE STANDING COMMITTEE,
OF THE ACADEMIC COUNCIL will be held under Section 18[a]-Other Members [2], Section 14(a) - Other Members [2] and
Section  24 [k] of the Madras University Act, 1923.
[i]
To the Syndicate-Section 18[a]-Other Members [2]
TWO MEMBERS to be elected by the Academic Council from among its members of one shall be teacher of affiliated
colleges and one shall be a teacher of Approved Colleges.
The person getting elected will hold office as a member of the Syndicate for the residual period/full term (three years)
from the date of receipt of nomination / the withdrawal / the period of present member term to be ceased / the date
of election whichever is applicable for the election, as the case may be.
[ii]
To the Senate-section 14[a] - Other Members [2]
TWO MEMBERS to be elected by the Academic Council from among its members.
[iii] To the Standing Committee of the Academic Council [Section 24[k]
TWO MEMBERS viz., One Member shall be member of the Syndicate and One Member shall be the Principal or
Teacher of Affiliated Colleges.
The newly elected members will hold office as a Member of the Standing Committee of the Academic Council for
the residual  period from the date of election / the date of withdrawal / the date of election / the date of declaration
of results  whichever is applicable for the election.
Nomination forms are available in the Registrar's Office.  Nomination should be sent so as to be received by the Registrar,
