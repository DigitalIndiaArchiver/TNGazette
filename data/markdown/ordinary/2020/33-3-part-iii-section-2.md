NOTIFICATION
In pursuance of recommendations of the Pay Revision Committee for introduction of uniform designations in Major Port 
Trusts for posts carrying same pay of scales were considered and communicated by the Ministry of Shipping in its letter No.

Employees’ (Recruitment, Seniority and Promotion) Regulations, 2008, the Chennai Port Trust Board vide its Resolution No.142, 
dated 19.03.2020 has approved to adopt of new designations and to amend  the Recruitment Rules for the following Class- II 
posts of General Administration / HR Department, Traﬃ  c Department, Engineering Departments (Civil, Mechanical & Electrical 
Engineering etc.), Finance Department, Medical Department and Marine Department in the schedule-II annexed to the Chennai 
Port Trust Employees’ (Recruitment, Seniority and Promotion) 2008.

amendment made to the existing Recruitment Rules in respect of Class-II posts of all the departments of the Trust is cause for 
publication.

existing Recruitment Rules for various posts in various departments of the Trust.

Schedule-II of the Chennai Port Trust Employees’ (Recruitment, Seniority and Promotion) Regulations 2008, the following 
amendments made to the existing Recruitment Rules to various posts in various departments of the Trust, is cause for publication.

(i) In the said Schedule-II, under said Regulations, the Recruitment Rules for the post of Assistant Secretary Gr. II (Class-II) 
[Sl.No 1] in General Admn Department, the entries under Column 11 and 12, the following shall be substituted:
(i)  In column 11, for the entries, the following entries shall be substituted, namely:-
“By Promotion failing which by absorption / deputation, failing both by direct recruitment”
(ii)  In column 12, for the entries, the following entries shall be substituted, namely:-

General Administration Dept., failing which by deputation failing both by Direct Recruitment.
In case of Deputation / Absorption will be from the employees holding analogous posts or post of Oﬃ  ce Superintendent 
with 2 years Regular Service in the grade in a Major Port Trust or Government / Semi-Government / PSU or Autonomous 
Bodies.”
