In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-sections (1) and (2) of Section 29 read with Section 19 of
the Hajj Committee Act, 2002 (Central Act 35 of 2002), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints
Thiru Md. Nasimuddin, I.A.S., Principal Secretary to Government, Youth Welfare and Sports Development
Department as the Executive Officer of the Tamil Nadu State Hajj Committee and he shall also act as the
Secretary of the said Committee.
