NOTIFICATIONS BY THE ELECTION COMMISSION OF INDIA 
APPOINTMENT OF DATES FOR VARIOUS STAGES AND FIXATION OF HOURS OF POLL FOR BYE-ELECTION 
TO THE COUNCIL OF STATES TO FILL UP THE VACANCY CAUSED DUE TO THE RESIGNATION OF 
THIRU R.VAITHILINGAM VACANT FROM 07-05-2021
No. SRO  G-33/2021
The following Notiﬁ cation of the Election Commission of India, New Delhi, dated 15th September, 2021 [Bhadrapada 24, 
1943 (Saka)] is republished:-
No. 100/CS-TN/07/2021 (1):- WHEREAS, the seat of Thiru. R. Vaithilingam, in the Council of States, elected by the elected 
members of the Legislative Assembly of Tamil Nadu, has become vacant on 07-05-2021 by the reason of his resignation, 
before the expiration of his term of oﬃ  ce which was up to and including 29-06-2022; and
WHEREAS, a bye-election is to be held for the purpose of ﬁ lling the vacancy so caused;
NOW, THEREFORE, in pursuance of Section 147, sub-section (1) of Section 39 and Section 56 of the Representation 
of the People Act, 1951 (43 of 1951), the Election Commission of India hereby-
(A) 
calls upon the elected members of the Legislative Assembly of Tamil Nadu to elect, before 6th October, 2021 
and in accordance with the provisions of the said Act and of the rules and orders made thereunder, a person for 
the purpose of ﬁ lling the said vacancy in the Council of States;
(B) 
appoints, with respect to the said election;
(a) the 22nd September, 2021 (Wednesday), as the last date for making nominations;
(b) the 23rd September, 2021 (Thursday), as the date for the scrutiny of the nominations;
(c) the 27th September, 2021 (Monday), as the last date for the withdrawal of candidatures;
(d) the 4th October, 2021 (Monday), as the date on which a poll shall, if necessary, be taken; and 
(e) the 6th October, 2021 (Wednesday), as the date before which the election shall be completed; and
