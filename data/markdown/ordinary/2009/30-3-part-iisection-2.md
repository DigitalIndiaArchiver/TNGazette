AGRICULTURE DEPARTMENT
Appointment of non-official person as President of
Tamil Nadu State Agricultural Marketing Board under
Tamil Nadu Agricultural Produce  Marketing
(Regulation) Act, 1987.
[G.O. Ms. No. 122, Agriculture (AM-1), 15th July 2009.]
No. II(2)/AG/376/2009.—Under clause (a) of sub-section
(1) of Section 36 of the Tamil Nadu Agricultural Produce
Marketing (Regulation) Act, 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 1989)
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints Thiru
K.P.T. Ganesan, M.Sc. (Agri.) as the President of the
Tamil Nadu State Agricultural Marketing Board vice Thiru
K. Nanda Kishore, IAS., Agricultural Production Commissioner
and Secretary to Government, Agriculture Department,
Secretariat, Chennai-9.
K. NANDA KISHORE,
Agricultural Production Commissioner
and Secretary to Government.

No. II(2)/CTR/377/2009.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by clause (a) of sub-section (1) of Section 9 of
the Indian Stamp Act, 1899 (Central Act II of 1899), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby remits the duty chargeable
under the said Act in respect of the instruments of sale of
land and buildings constructed by the Evangelical Fellowship
of India Commission on Relief, a voluntary organization
comprised in survey numbers 354 and 355 in MGR Thittu,
Killai North Village of Chidambaram Taluk in Cuddalore District,
to be executed in favour of 160 families affected by Tsunami
in that Village.
Remission of registration fee payable for instruments of
sale of land and buildings constructed by the
Evangelical Fellowship  of India Commission on Relief,
to be executed in favour of Tsunami affected victims
in Chidambaram Taluk, Cuddalore District under the
Registration Act.
[G.O. Ms. No. 100, Commercial Taxes and Registration (J1),

No. II(2)/CTR/378/2009.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by Section 78-A of the Registration Act, 1908
(Central Act XVI of 1908), as the Governor of Tamil Nadu
is of the opinion that it is necessary so to do in the public
interest, hereby remits the fee payable under the said Act
in respect of instruments of sale of land and buildings
constructed by the Evangelical Fellowship of India Commission
on Relief, a voluntary organization, comprised in Survey
Numbers 354 and 355 in MGR Thittu, Killai North Village
of Chidambaram Taluk in Cuddalore District to be executed
in favour of 160 families affected by Tsunami in that Village.

[G.O. Ms. No.125, Highways and Minor Ports (HP1),
7th July 2009, Aani 23, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. II(2)/HWMP/379/2009.—The Government of Tamil
Nadu having been satisfied that the lands specified in the
Schedule below have to be acquired for Highways Department
to wit for construction of Railway over Bridge in lieu of existing
level crossing No. 9 at Railway Km 0/10 of Omalur-Sankagiri

amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands is to
be paid  out of the funds controlled or managed by Highways
Department, the following notice is issued under
sub-section 1 of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act,
2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE

Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002), the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby declares  that the lands specified in
the schedule below measuring  0.2330.0 sq.mt. a litte more
or less needed for Highways, for construction of Railway Over
Bridge in lieu of existing level crossing No. 9 at Railway
Km 0/10 of Omalur-Sankagiri Road.
The plan of the lands are kept in the office of the District
Collector, Salem District and may be Inspected at any time
during office hours.

January to December.

minimum rates of wages as fixed above, the employees shall
be paid dearness allowance as indicated below:—
(i)
The dearness allowance is linked to the Average
Chennai City Consumer Price Index for the year
2000 (i.e. 475 points with base 1982=100) and for
every rise of one point over and above 475 points,
an increase of Rs.3.80 (Rupees three and paise
eighty only) shall be paid as dearness allowance
per month.
(ii)
The dearness allowance shall be calculated every
year on the 1st April on the basis of the average
of the Consumer Price indices for the preceeding12
(Twelve) months, that is, from January to December.
(iii)
The first calculation shall thus be effective from the
date of publication of this Notification in the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette based on the
Average Consumer Price Index of the previous
year.

rated employees will be subject to eight hours of work in
a day. Where any piece-rate employee works less than eight
hours a day, he shall be entitled to payment of proportionate
dearness allowance only.
(3) To arrive at daily wages, the monthly wages shall be
divided by 26.
(4) To arrive at the monthly wages, the daily wages shall
be multiplied by 30.
(5) Where the nature of work is the same, no distinction
in the payment of wages shall be made between male and
female employees.

minimum wages fixed herein, the same shall be continued
to be paid.
