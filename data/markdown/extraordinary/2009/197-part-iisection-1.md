NOTIFICATIONS  BY  THE  LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY SECRETARIAT
LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY SECRETARIAT
VACATION OF SEAT IN TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY DUE TO RESIGNATION OF
THIRU ANITHA R. RADHAKRISHNAN, MEMBER OF THE TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY.
[Letter No. 14904/2009-1, Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Secretariat (B.III), 31st July 2009.]
No. II(1)/LAS/24(a)/2009.
Whereas, Thiru Anitha R. Radhakrishnan, a Member of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly representing
the 225. Tiruchendur Assembly Constituency, in his letter, dated 31st July, 2009 addressed to me as Speaker
of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly, has resigned his seat as Member of the said Assembly with effect
from 31st July 2009.
Now, therefore, I, R. AVUDAIAPPAN, Speaker of the said Assembly hereby accept the resignation of
Thiru Anitha R. Radhakrishnan, as envisaged under Rule 22 of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Rules as
it is in accordance with Rule 21 of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Rules. Under Article 190(3)(b) of the
Constitution of India, his seat has, therefore, become vacant with effect from the above date.
Secretariat,
R. AVUDAIAPPAN,
Chennai-600 009,
Speaker,
Dated 31st July, 2009.
Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly.
