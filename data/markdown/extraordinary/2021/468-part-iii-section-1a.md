COMMERCIAL TAXES AND REGISTRATION DEPARTMENT
AMENDMENTS TO THE TAMIL NADU SOCIETIES REGISTRATION RULES, 1978.
[G.O. Ms. No. 126, Commercial Taxes and Registration (M1) Department, dated 6th October 2021, 
Purattasi 20, Pilava, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2052.]
No. SRO A-19(a)/2021.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 56 of the Tamil Nadu Societies Registration Act, 1975 (Tamil Nadu Act 27 
of 1975), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following Amendments to the Tamil Nadu Societies Registration Rules, 
1978:-
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖගඛ
 
In the said Rules, 
 
 
(1) For rule 43, the following rule shall be substituted, namely:-
 
 
 
''43. Fees to be paid.— The fees payable for the several matters mentioned in the Schedule to the  
 
 
Act shall be paid in cash or by demand draft or by electronic mode,''
 
 
(2) after rule 52, the following rule shall be added, namely:—
 
 
 
''53 Use of electronic records.—Notwithstanding anything contained in these rules-
 
(a) submission of application, notice and ﬁ ling of documents;
 
(b)  receipt  of application, ﬁ ling and maintaining of documents, acknowledgment of registration and
      documents, issuing of certiﬁ cate of registration and any communication thereof;
 
(c) receipt of fee and ﬁ ne and refund of fee,
 
                   may also be made by electronic means''.
 
B. JOTHI NIRMALASAMY, 
 
 
 
Secretary to Government.
