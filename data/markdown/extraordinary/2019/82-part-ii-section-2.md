APPOINTMENT OF Commission of Inquiry to inquire into the demise of the 
late Hon’ble Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu Selvi J Jayalalithaa on 5th December 2016 
TO Extension of the period of the commission of inquiry by four months from 
25th february 2019 under the commission of inquiry act.
amendment to notification
[G.O. Ms. No. 151, Public (Law and Order-F), 25th February 2019, Maasi 13, Vilambi,  
Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2050.]
No. II(2)/PULO/165(i)	/2019.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Commissions of Inquiry Act, 1952 
(Central Act LX of 1952), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Public 
(S.C.) Department Notification No. II(2)/PUSC/793(g)/2017, published at page-1 of Part II—Section 2 of the 
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 25th September 2017, as subsequently amended.
Amendment
In the said Notification, in paragraph 3, for the expression “seventeen months”, the expression “twenty one 
months” shall be substituted.
