ORDER: No. 321, Home, 26th March 2010.
In the Government order first read above orders have been
issued for the ad-interim attachment of properties belonging to
Thiru Neelarajan, one of the Proprietors of Thiruvalargal Ganesh
Bankers, Eathamozhy, Kanniyakumari District under Section 3
of the Tamil Nadu Protection of Interests of Depositors (in
Financial Establishments) Act, 1997  for default in return of
deposits made by the depositors after maturity.
2. The Competent Authority and District Revenue Officer,
Kanniyakumari in the letter second read above has stated that
the Deputy Superintendent of Police, Economic Offences Wing-II,
Nagercoil has reported that Thiru. Neelarajan, son of Pugal
Neela Rathna Samy and Krishnappa, son of Thankavel Nadar
have settled the entire dues to the depositors and no further
complaint was received from any depositor. As the settlement
was over, no IA or OA has been filed against the accused.
Based on the report of the Deputy Superintendent of Police,
Economic Offences Wing-II, Nagercoil the depositors were
summoned for enquiry. Some of the depositors appeared for
the enquiry and the were personally heard and they deposed
that they got back the entire amount due to them. Hence, the
Competent Authority and District Revnue Officer, Nagercoil has
requested the Government to cancel the ad-interim order made
in the G.O. first read above.
3. The Inspector General of Police Economic Offences
Wing-II, Chennai has stated that in the case in Economic
Offences Wing-II, Nagercoil Cr. No. 8/03 under Sections 420
and 409 IPC registered against the said P. Neelarajan and
Krishnappa and as they have settled the entire amount of
Rs. 1,28,47,906/- to 207 depositors further action was dropped
on 25th July 2008 by the Chief Judicial Magistrate, Nagercoil
as the accused settled the entire amount to the concerned.
As the matter has been settled among the depositors and the
proprietors, the case was referred as FAD, by the Chief Judicial
Magistrate, Nagercoil and hence the order of ad-interim
attachment of property made in the G.O.Ms.No. 112, Home,
dated 13th February 2006 may be raised.
4. The Government after careful examination have decided
to accept the proposal of the Competent Authority and District
Revenue Officer, Kanniyakumari District as recommended by
the Inspector General of Police, Economic Offences Wing-II,
