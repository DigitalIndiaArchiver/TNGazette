 IND. No. ED. 7.  The Arignar Anna Engineering Workers Industrial Co-operative Society Ltd.
(L.F. 1184/ICA/2011)
No. VI(1)/70/2018.
"In the circumstances stated by the Assistant Director of Industries and Commerce (Industrial Co-operatives), District 
Industries Centre, Erode/Ofﬁ cial Liquidator of the Arignar Anna Engineering Workers Industrial Co-operative Society Ltd., 
IND No. ED. 7 (Under Liquidation) in the ﬁ nal closure proposal dated 02-11-2017 and in exercise of the powers delegated 
under Section 140(1) of Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act, 1983, the registration of the Arignar Anna Engineering 
Workers Industrial Co-operative Society Ltd., IND No. ED.7 (Under Liquidation) has been ordered to be cancelled 
and the affairs have been ﬁ nally closed with effect from 05-02-2018 vide the Proceedings No.LF.1184/ICA/2011, dated 
05-02-2018 of the Principal Secretary/Industries Commissioner and Director of Industries and Commerce and Registrar 
of Industrial Co-operatives, Chennai-32".
IND. No. 3/KRR. The Karur District Ladies Tailoring Industrial Co-operative Society Ltd.
(L.F. 1192/ICA/2013)
No. VI(1)/71/2018.
"In the circumstances stated by the Assistant Director of Industries and Commerce (Industrial Co-operatives), District 
Industries Centre, Karur/Ofﬁ cial Liquidator of the Karur District Ladies Tailoring Industrial Co-operative Society Ltd., 
IND. No. 3/KRR. (Under Liquidation) in the ﬁ nal closure proposal dated 04-10-2017 and in exercise of the powers 
delegated under Section 140(1) of Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act, 1983, the Registration of the Karur District 
Ladies Tailoring Industrial Co-operative Society Ltd., IND. No. 3/KRR. (Under Liquidation) has been ordered to be cancelled 
and the affairs have been ﬁ nally closed with effect from 05-02-2018 vide the Proceedings No.LF. 1192/ICA/2013, dated 
05-02-2018 of the Principal Secretary/Industries Commissioner and Director of Industries and Commerce and Registrar 
of Industrial Co-operatives, Chennai-32".
