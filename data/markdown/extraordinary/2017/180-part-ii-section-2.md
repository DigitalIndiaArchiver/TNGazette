Under Section 11(1) and (2) of the Anna University Act, 1978 (Act 30 of 1978) as amended in Tamil Nadu University
Laws (Amendment & Repeal) Act 2011 and Tamil Nadu Ordinance 2 of 2017, the Government re-constitute a Search
Committee, consisting of the following persons to recommend a panel of three names to the Hon'ble Governor-Chencellor
for appointment of Vice-Chancellor, Anna University, Chennai:-
1.
Hon'ble Justice R.M. Lodha,
Governor-Chancellor's Nominee and Convener
Former Chief Justice of India, New Delhi.
2.
Dr. N. Sundara Devan, I.A.S., (Retired),
Government Nominee
Former Additional Chief Secretary to Government.
3.
Prof. K. Anantha Padmanabhan,
Syndicate Nominee
Former Director, IIT kanpur,
Res: Block 1, 4C, Ceebros Orchid,
      263, Velacherry Main Road, Chennai-42.
The above committee is requested to recommend a panel of three names along with their Bio-data to the Hon'ble
Governor-Chancellor of Anna University, Chennai for appointment of Vice-Chancellor to Anna University, Chennai.
