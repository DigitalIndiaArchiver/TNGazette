No. SRO B-3/2022.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Secretariat Service 
(Section-29 in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 2016):-
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 7th June 2021.
Amendment
In the said Special Rules, for the expressions "Personnel and Administrative Reforms" as well as "Personnel and 
Administrative Reforms (Training)", wherever they occur, the expression "Human Resources Management" shall be substituted.
MYTHILI K. RAJENDRAN,
Secretary to Government.

No. SRO B-4/2022.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Survey and Land 
Records Service Rules.
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 25th February 2021.
Amendment
In the said Special Rules, in rule 4 in the Table, in column (4) against the entries "I" in column (1) and the Corresponding 
entries in columns (2) and (3) thereof, for the expression "fifty seven years of age", the expression "fifty nine years of age" 
shall be substituted.
M.A. SIDDIQUE, 
Principal Secretary to Government (FAC).
