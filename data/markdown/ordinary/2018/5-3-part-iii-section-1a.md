No. SRO A-3/2018.—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of section 38 of the Right of Children to 
Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 (Central Act 35 of 2009), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following 
amendment to the Tamil Nadu Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Rules, 2011. 
The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st day of April 2015.
Amendments
In the said Rules, under the heading "PART-VI TEACHERS" to rule 16, after sub-rule (2), the following proviso shall be 
added namely:- 
    “Provided further that every teacher appointed or in position as on the 31st March 2015, who does not possess 
minimum qualifications as laid down under sub-section (1) of Section 23 of the Act shall acquire such minimum qualifications 
within a period of four years from the date of commencement of the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education 
(Amendment) Act, 2017 (Central Act 24 of 2017).”
