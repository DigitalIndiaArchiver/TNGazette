Amendments 
In the said Rules, in rule 8,— 
	
(1) after the expression ''by direct recruitment'' at the end, the expression ''in which the cadre strength is more than 
one under the jurisdiction of each appointing authority'' shall be added;
	
(2) for the expression ''The rule of reservation of appointments (General Rule 22)'', the expression ''The principle 
of reservation of appointments as provided in Section 27 of Tamil Nadu Government Servants (Conditions of Service) Act, 
2016 (Tamil Nadu Act 14 of 2016)'' shall be substituted.
