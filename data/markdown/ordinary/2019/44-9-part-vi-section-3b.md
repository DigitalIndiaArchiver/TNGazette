Election to the Syndicate
(Ref.No.BDU/A2/Or.Sen.Syn.Elec./24099/2019.) 
No.VI-3(b)/30/2019.
One member, not falling under the first three categories (1), (2) and (3) of Class-II (Other Members) of Section 24 (b) 
Chapter IV of Bharathidasan University Act 1981, elected by the Senate from among its members.
Nominations are invited in the prescribed form which can be obtained from the Registrar and Returning Officer on 
a requisition and submission of a Demand Draft for Rs.590/- (Including GST - Rupees Five Hundred and Ninety only) 
drawn in favour of Bharathidasan University, Tiruchirappalli on or after the date of notification, i.e. 13-11-2019 with a 
stamped self-addressed envelope to the value of Rs.25/- (Rupees twenty five only) or in person.
Nomination paper shall be enclosed in an envelope superscribed as follows, as the case may be:-
                    ''Nomination for Election to the                                            by                                           ''
The Members are requested to send their nomination by Registered Post (with Acknowledgement due) so as to reach the 
Registrar and Returning Officer within the date and hour fixed or delivered to the Registrar and Returning Officer either in person 
or by a messenger within the date and hour fixed.
Nomination papers that are not sent as stated above will be declared invalid.
