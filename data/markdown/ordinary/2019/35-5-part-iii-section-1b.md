No. SRO B-56/2019.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to clause (3) of Article 320 of the Constitution of India, 
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission Regulations, 
1954 (Volume-I of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1987):-
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on 26th February 2018.
Amendment
In the said Regulations, in ANNEXURE I-A referred to in clause (b) of regulation 16, under the heading 
"A STATE SERVICES", after the entry "141. Senior Manager in the State Intelligence wing of Police Department", the following 
entry shall be added, namely:-
"142. Liaison Officer, State Guest House, Chepauk, Chennai".

No. SRO B-57/2019.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to clause (3) of Article 320 of the Constitution of India, 
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission Regulations, 
1954 (Volume-I of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1987):-
2. (i) The amendment (1) hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on 15th July 2015.
   (ii)  The amendment (2) hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on 8th November 1990.
Amendment
In the said Regulations, in ANNEXURE I-A, under the heading "B. SUBORDINATE SERVICES", in clause 3, in 
item (XL),—
(1) the expression "B.T. Assistants and Tamil Pandit, Grade-I" shall be omitted;
(2) for of the expression "Department of Approved Schools and Vigilance Service", the expression "Department of Social 
Defence" shall be substituted.
