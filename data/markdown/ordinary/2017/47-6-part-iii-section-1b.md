In exercise of powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Information Technology
Service (Section 52 in volume II of Tamil Nadu Services Manual):-
2) The amendments hereby made shall come into force with effect from the date of publication in
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules,-
a) in rule 6 and in the provisos thereunder, the entries “ on the 1st day of July of the year in which the selection for
appointment is made” shall be replaced by “ as on the 1st day of July of the year in which the vacancy is notified”.
b) in  rule 7, in THE TABLE, in Column (3), the proviso in item (b) against the categories “4(a) Assistant System
Engineer” and “4(b) Assistant System Analyst” corresponding to entry  “(i) By Direct Recruitment” in Column (2) shall be
deleted.
