Notifications under Tamil Nadu Land Reforms (Fixation of Ceiling on Land) Act.
Cancellation of Notification
[G.O.  Ms. No. 410, Revenue (L.R.-I(2)), 30th October 2009.]
No. II(1)/REV/44/2009—In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 18-C of the Tamil Nadu Land Reforms
(Fixation of Ceiling on Land) Act, 1961 (Tamil Nadu Act 58 of 1961), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby cancels the
Revenue Department Notifications No. II(1)/REV/89/86, published at page 1 of the Supplement to Part II—Section 1 of
the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 2nd July 1986 and No. II(1)/REV/169/90, published at page 5 of the
Supplement to Part II—Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated 19th September 1990.
