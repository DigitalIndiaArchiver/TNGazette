warrant of appointment of governor.
[G.O. Ms. No. 791, Public (Special.B), 18th September 2021, Purattasi 2, Pilava, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2052.]
no. I/Pusp/14(a-1)/2021.
By virtue of the power vested in me by Article 155 of the Constitution of India, I, Ram Nath Kovind, President 
of India, hereby appoint Shri R.N. Ravi, to be the Governor of Tamil Nadu with effect from the date he assumes 
charge of his office.
Given at Rashtrapati Bhavan, New Delhi, this ninth day of September in the year two thousand twenty-one 
(18th Bhadrapada, 1943 Saka), in the seventy-second year of the Republic of India.
	
Ram Nath Kovind,	
	
	
	
	
President of India.
	
V. Irai Anbu,	
	
	
	
	
Chief Secretary.
proclamation of assumption of office of the governor.
[G.O. Ms. No. 792, Public (Special.B), 18th September 2021, Purattasi 2, Pilava, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2052.]
proclamation
no. I/Pusp/14(a-2)/2021.
Whereas the President of India has appointed Thiru Banwarilal Purohit, Governor of Tamil Nadu as Governor 
of Punjab and Administrator of Union Territory of Chandigarh and Whereas the President of India has appointed 
Thiru R.N. Ravi as Governor of Tamil Nadu, in place of Thiru Banwarilal Purohit.
It is hereby notified that Thiru R.N. Ravi has assumed the Office of Governor of Tamil Nadu on the forenoon of 
this, the eighteenth day of September, 2021.
	
V. Irai Anbu,	
	
	
	
	
Chief Secretary.
