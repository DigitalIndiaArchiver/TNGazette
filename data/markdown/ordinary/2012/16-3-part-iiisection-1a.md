HOME, PROHIBITION AND EXCISE DEPARTMENT
Amendment to the Tamil Nadu Liquor (Supply by Wholesale) Rules, 1983
[G.O. Ms. No. 13, Home, Prohibition and Excise (VIII), 30th March 2012.]
No. SRO A-14/2012.—In exercise of the powers conferred sections 17-D and 54 of the Tamil Nadu Prohibition Act, 1937
(Tamil Nadu Act X of 1937), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the
Tamil Nadu Liquor (Supply by Wholesale) Rules, 1983.
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st April 2011.
 AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in rule 15, in sub-rule (3), in item (i), for the expression "73.30", the expression "74.30" shall be
substituted.
E. GOPALAKRISHNAN,
                               Additional Secretary to Government.
LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT DEPARTMENT
Amendment to the Tamil Nadu Payment of Gratuity Rules, 1973
[G.O. Ms. No. 41, Labour and Employment (M1), 21st March 2012, Panguni 8, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2043.]
No. SRO A-15/2012.—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Payment of Gratuity
Act, 1972 (Central Act 39 of 1972), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the
Tamil Nadu Payment of Gratuity Rules, 1973.
  AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in rule 2, after clause (d), the following clause shall be inserted, namely:—
 “(dd) “seasonal establishment” means any establishment which is exclusively engaged in trade fair, circus, sports,
laying of road, construction activities, markets, shandies or any seasonal public amusement or entertainment  during
fair and festival seasons and any establishment which are of seasonal character and wherein the work is performed
intermittently and not exceeding 120 days during a period of 12 calendar months and includes such establishment, as
the Central Government may, by notification, declare to be an establishment for the purposes of the Act”.
      MOHAN PYARE,
Principal Secretary to Government.
