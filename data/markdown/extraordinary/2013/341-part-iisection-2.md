NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
DTP—II-2 Ex. (341)
TOURISM, CULTURE AND RELIGIOUS ENDOWMENTS DEPARTMENT
 APPOINTMENT OF FIRST VICE-CHANCELLOR TMT. VEENA  E. GAYATHRI, UNDER THE TAMIL NADU
MUSIC AND FINE ARTS UNIVERSITY ACT, 2013.
[G.O. (Ms) No. 293, Tourism, Culture and Religious Endowments (C1-1), 14th November 2013,
Aippasi 28, Vijaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2044.]
No. II(2)TCRE/865(c)/2013.
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 52 of the Tamil Nadu Music and Fine Arts University Act, 2013
(Tamil Nadu Act 30 of 2013) the Government hereby appoint Tmt. Veena E. Gayathri as the First Vice-Chancellor of
Tamil Nadu Music and Fine Arts University for a period of three years or till she attains the age of seventy years whichever
is earlier from the date of her assuming office.
2.  The terms and conditions of the appointment will be issued separately.
R. KANNAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
