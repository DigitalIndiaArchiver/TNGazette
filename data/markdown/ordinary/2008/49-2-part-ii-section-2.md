HOME, PROHIBITION AND EXCISE DEPARTMENT
Exemption from operation of certain rule to carryout
modifications in respect of Thiruvalargal Empee
Distilleries Limited, Mevaloorkuppam at Kancheepuram
District under the Tamil Nadu Indian Made Foreign
Spirits (Manufacture) Rules, 1981.
[G.O. Ms. No. 99, Home, Prohibition and Excise (III),
2nd December 2008.]
No. II(2)/HOPE/607/2008.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by rule 35 of the Tamil Nadu Indian-made Foreign
Spirits (Manufacture) Rules, 1981, the Governor of Tamil
Nadu hereby exempts Thiruvalargal Empee Distilleries
Limited, Mevaloorkuppam, Kancheepuram District from the
operation of sub-rule (2) of rule 11 of the said Rules for
the period from 4th October 2007 to 12th March 2008 in
having completed the following modifications on 12th March
2008, namely:—
(i)
Installation of 50 Kilo litres purification plant.
(ii) Installation of 5 numbers of stainless steel tanks,
2 numbers of neutral spirit receiver tanks  and 3 numbers
of neutral spirit storage tanks.
(iii) Installation of 3 numbers of head spirit storage
tanks and 3 numbers mild steel tanks.
(iv) Installation of one stainless steel oil tank in the
proposed purification plant.
(v) Erecting new 16 tons boiler by replacing one of
the two 5 tons boilers.
2. This Notification shall be deemed to have come into
force on the 4th day of October 2007.
S. MALATHI,
Principal Secretary to Government.
