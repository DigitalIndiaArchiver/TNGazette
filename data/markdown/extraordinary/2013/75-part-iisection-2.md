[G.O. Ms. No. 34, Highways and Minor Ports (HV1)
19th March 2013]
No. II(2)/HWMP/257(d)/2013.
[National Highways-NH 205 - Stretch from Padi to
Thirunindravur section (Km 59/6 - 81/6) - declared as State
Highways Urban stretch (SU) - Orders -Issued.]
READ:—
1.
From the District Collector, Tiruvallur Letter
No. 3604/2013/PL, dated 28-2-2013.
2.
From the CE (C&M) Letter No.402/Thittam1/2012,
dated 12-3-2013.
ORDER:—
No. 34, Highways and Minor Ports (HV1), 19th March 2013.
The Government of India, Ministry of Road Transport and
Highways, had notified the Padi-Tirupathi Road as NH-205
on  6th January 1999, and entrusted this road to National
Highways Authority of India on 27th September 2005.
This road has been taken over by National Highways Authority
of India (NHAI) under National Highways Development
Programme (NHDP) Phase III.  The National Highways
Authority of India has now taken up the four laning of this
Road and awarded the project to M/s.  Transstroy Tirupathi
Tiruvallur Chennai Tollway Private Limited, Chennai on
12th July 2010, excluding the 22km Stretch i.e. 59/6-81/6
from Padi to Tirunindravur.  The total length of TN/AP
Border-Renigunda-Tiruthani-Chennai Road (NH-205) is 81.60
Km (Km 0/0-81/6).
2.  The District Collector, Tiruvallur in his letter first read
above has stated that, Padi-Thirunindravur road is one among
the four National Highways roads branching from the Chennai
city,  which leads to Tiruthani and Tirupathi.  Most of the
pilgrims are using only this road for travel to Tirupathi and
Tiruthani from Chennai.  Moreover, the stretch of Road from
Km81/6-59/6 is passing through thickly populated urban area
and Ambattur Industrial Estate area.  He has therefore
requested the Government to take up the works of widening
and strengthening the stretch from Km 59/6 (Thirunindravur)
to 81/6 (Padi).
3.  The Chief Engineer (Construction and Maintenance)
Highways Department in his letter second read above has
requested the Government to declare the stretch km
59/6-81/6 of NH 205, Padi-Tirunindravur as State Highways
Urban Stretch so that development works can be taken up
in the plan schemes during the next financial year.


2

4. The issue was discussed in greater detail with the
officials of Highways Department and after having elaborate
discussion, the Government have decided to accept the
proposals of the District Collector, Tiruvallur and Chief
Engineer (Construction and Maintenance) Highways
Department.  Accordingly the Government hereby declare
that the stretch from Padi-Tirunindravur of NH 205
(Km 59/6-81/6) as State Highways Urban stretch so as
enable the Government to take up widening and
strengthening of the stretch from Padi-Tirunindravur
(Km 59/6-81/6).
NIRANJAN MARDI,
