(Roc. No. 247/2011/GLPA.)
No. VI(1)/236/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by  sub-section (4) of Section 32 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning
Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972), Government in the order G.O. Ms. 122, Housing and Urban Development
UD 4(1) Department dated 02-07-2012 has permitted to change of land use of a survey numbers making the following
variation to the Master Plan for the Gummidipoondi Local Planning area, approved under the Said Act and published
in the Housing and Urban Development Department Notification No. II(2)/HOU/610/2000, dated 7-6-2000, at page
No. 295 of Part—II, Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated 7th June 2000.
VARIATIONS
In the said Master Plan, in the “LAND USE SCHEDULE” of VILLAGE NUMBER 192. Getnamallee under the
heading:—
II.
COMMERCIAL USE ZONE: Survey Numbers, instead of the expression “78 to 80” the expression 78 (except
78/1B, 1C, 1D1, 3A, 3B1, 3C1, 83/1I, 1J, 1K, 1L, 1M, 1N, 1O, 1P, 1Q, 1R, 1S1, 1T1, 1V, 1W, 2, 84/2B,
2C1pt, 2C2, 2D, 2E, 3Bpt, 3C, 3D, 4A1pt, 4A2, 4B, 4C1pt, 4C2) 79, 80 shall substituted.
and under the heading III. INDUSTRIAL USE ZONE: III(b) GENERAL INDUSTRIAL USE ZONE: Survey
Numbers the expression “78/1B, 1C, 1D1, 3A, 3B1, 3C1, 83/1I, 1J, 1K, 1L, 1M, 1N, 1O, 1P, 1Q, 1R, 1S1,
1T1, 1V, 1W, 2, 84/2B, 2C1pt, 2C2, 2D, 2E, 3Bpt, 3C, 3D, 4A1pt, 4A2, 4B, 4C1pt, 4C2” shall be inserted
