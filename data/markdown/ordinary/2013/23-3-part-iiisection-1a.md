HOME, PROHIBITION AND EXCISE DEPARTMENT
Amendments to the Tamil Nadu Liquor (Licence and Permit) Rules
[G.O. Ms. No. 32, Home, Prohibition and Excise (VIII), 24th May 2013.]
No. SRO A-16/2013.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Sections 17-C, 17-D, 17-E, 18-B, 18-C, 20 and 21 read
with Section 54 of the Tamil Nadu Prohibition Act, 1937 (Tamil Nadu Act X of 1937), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby
makes the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu Liquor (Licence and Permit) Rules, 1981:—
AMENDMENTS.
In the said Rules,—
(1) in rule 25, in clause II, in sub-clause (a), in the proviso, after the expression “F.L. 3”, the expression”,
“F.L. 3(A)” shall be inserted; and
(2) in Form F.L. 3-A, after condition number 9, the following condition shall be added, namely:—
“10. No sale of liquor shall be made under the licence on the Thiruvalluvar day falling in the month of January,
Gandhi Jayandhi day, namely, the 2nd October, Birth day eve of Nabigal Nayagam, Mahaveer Jayandhi day, Vallalar Ninaivu
Naal, Independence day, Republic day and May day in every year.”
NIRANJAN MARDI,
Principal Secretary to Government.
