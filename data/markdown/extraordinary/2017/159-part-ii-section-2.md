Under clause (f) of Section 16 of the Tamil Nadu Transparency in Tenders Act, 1998
(Tamil Nadu Act 43 of 1998), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notifies to engage the services of National
Association of Software and Service Companies (NASSCOM) directly for the establishment of Startup Warehouse
in the premises of Tidel Park Coimbatore Limited (TPCL), Coimbatore by the Electronics Corporation of
Tamil Nadu (ELCOT) Ltd.
