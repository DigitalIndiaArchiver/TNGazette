LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT DEPARTMENT
Draft Amendments to the Tamil Nadu Trade Unions
Regulations under the Trade Unions Act.
[G.O.Ms. No.36, Labour and Employment (K2),
30th April 2014, Chithirai 17, Jaya,
Thiruvalluvar Aandu 2045.]
No.II(2)/LE/340/2014.—The following draft amendments to
the Tamil Nadu Trade Unions Regulations, 1927 which they
are proposed to make in exercise of the powers conferred
by Section 29 of the Trade Unions Act, 1926 (Central Act
XVI of 1926), are hereby published for general information
as required by Section 30 of the said Act.
2. Notice is hereby given that the draft amendments will
be taken into consideration on or after the date of expiry of
three months from the date of publication of this Notification
in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette and that any
objection or suggestion which may be received from any
person with respect thereto, before the expiry of the period
aforesaid will be considered by the Government of Tamil
Nadu. The Objections and suggestions, if any, should be
addressed to the Secretary to Government Labour and
Employment Department, Fort St.George, Secretariat,
Chennai-600 009 through the Commissioner of Labour,
Chennai-600 006.
DRAFT AMENDMENTS
In the said Regulations,—
(1) in clause 14, for the expression "Rs. 100/- (Rupees
one hundred only)", the expression "Rs. 1000/- (Rupees one
thousand only)" shall be substituted;
(2) in clause 15, in sub-clause (2), for the expression
"Rs.25/-(Rupees twently five only)", the expression
"Rs. 250/- (Rupees two hundred and fifty only)" shall be
substituted.

Provided that in case of non-availability or difficulty in procurement of field cumblies with specification mentioned above,

than 220 centimetres in length, 125 centimetres in breadth an weighing not less than 1500 grams, having a compositition

Provided further that the variation in the dimension of the cumblies, rugs and bed-sheets of good quality upto
5 per cent may be allowed by the Chief Inspector of Plantations:

quality of rug, the Chief Inspector of Plantations may, after recording the reasons therefor, accord approval for the same
without insisting on the weight of such rug.
