MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION AND WATER SUPPLY
DEPARTMENT
Constitution of the Officers and Servants of the Accounts
Wing of the Municipal Councils Under the Tamil Nadu
District Municipalities Act.
[G.O. Ms. No. 113, Municipal Administration
and Water Supply (ME.1), 25th July 2013.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/670/2013.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by sub-section (1) by Section 77-A, of the
Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act
V of 1920), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby constitutes
the officers and servants of the accounts wing of the Municipal
councils constituted under the said Act into a municipal
service for the State of Tamil Nadu, Namely, the Tamil Nadu
Municipal Accounts Service.
