 appointment of pro-tem speaker of the tamil nadu legislative assembly
[S.O. Ms. No. 42, Legislative Assembly Secretariat, 8th May 2021, Chithirai 25, 
Pilava, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2052.]
No.II(1)/LAS/8(g-1)/2021.
“In exercise of the powers conferred by Clause (1) of Article 180 of the Constitution of India, 
I, BANWARILAL PUROHIT, Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby appoint Thiru. K. Pitchandi, being a Member of 
the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly to perform the duties of the Speaker from the commencement of the first 
meeting of the Assembly until the Speaker shall have been chosen by the Assembly under Article 178 of the 
Constitution.

“In exercise of the powers conferred by Article 188 of the Constitution of India, 
I, BANWARILAL PUROHIT, Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby appoint  the Speaker Pro-tem, Sixteenth 
Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly, Thiru. K. Pitchandi, as the person before whom the oath or affirmation may
