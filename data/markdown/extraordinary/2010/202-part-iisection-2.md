NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
DTP—II-2 Ex. (202)
COMMERCIAL TAXES AND REGISTRATION DEPARTMENT
EXEMPTION IN RESPECT OF THE LUXURY TAX PAYABLE BY THE EMBASSIES OR CONSULATES OF
CERTAIN COUNTRIES VISITING TAMIL NADU UNDER TAMIL NADU TAX ON LUXURIES ACT.
VARIATION TO NOTIFICATION
[G.O. Ms. No. 121, Commercial Taxes and Registration (C2), 5th July 2010.]
No. II(2)/CTR/398(G)/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 23-A of the  Tamil Nadu Tax on Luxuries
Act, 1981 (Tamil Nadu Act 6 of 1981), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following variation to
the Commercial Taxes and Registration Department Notification No. II(2)/CTR/119(b-1)/2008, published at
pages 1 to 3 of Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the
29th February 2008, as subsequently varied:
VARIATION.
In the said Notification, the item "(36) FRANCE" shall be omitted.
MD. NASIMUDDIN,
Secretary to Government.
