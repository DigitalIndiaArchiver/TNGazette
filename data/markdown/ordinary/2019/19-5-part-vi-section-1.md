Variation to the Approved Kailasanathar Koil Area Extension Detailed Development Plan of Kancheepuram Local 
Planning Area/Greater Municipality.
(Roc. No. 2445/2019/DP1)
No. VI(1)/210/2019.
In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (1) of Section 33 of Town and Country Planning Act, 1971 
(Act No.35 of 1972). The Commissioner of Town and Country Planning in the Proceedings Roc. No.2445/2019/DP1, dated 
24-04-2019 proposes to make the individual draft variation "Conversion of controlled Industrial use into Residential use 
in T.S. Nos. 2343, 2344, 2345 - Ward-3, Block-31, Extent-1.23Acres in the Approved Kailasanathar Koil Area Extension 
in Detailed Development Plan of Kancheepuram Local Planning Area / Greater Municipality" approved by the Director 
of Town and Country Planning proceeding in Roc. No.12895/2002/DP1, Dated: 07-06-2007 and the fact of this approval 
Published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No. 43, Part-VI—Section-1, page. 406, Dated 07-11-2007, Publication 
No.VI(1)/652/2007. 
2. Any person affected or interested in this draft variation may within SIXTY days from the date of publication 
of this notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, can represent in person or submit in writing to the 
Member Secretary, Kancheepuram Local Planning Authority any objections and suggestions relating thereto. 
9. The Variation with plan may be inspected free of cost at any time during office hours at the above said Local 
Planning Authority 
Variation
1.	
Wherever the expression Map No.4 & 5, DDP(CH-R)/DTCP No.23/2007 occurs the expression DDP(V)/DTCP 
No.6/2019 shall be added at the end and to be read with. 
2.	
Based on the variation, the details specified in DD Plan schedules-Ill (part-I), IV, V & IX shall be deleted or 
substituted accordingly. 
3.	
The draft variation made enforceable from the date of publication of the confirmed variation notification to be 
issued u/s 33(2) of the Act in TNGG. 
Chennai-600 002,	
Rajesh Lakhoni,
24th April 2019.	
Commissioner of Town and Country Planning (F.A.C.).
Variations to the Approved Review Master Plan for the Tiruchirappalli Local Planning Area.
(Roc. No. 644/2018/TLPA 2)
No. VI(1)/211/2019.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 32 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning Act, 
1971 (Tamil Nadu Act No.35 of 1972) and in exercise of the powers conferred by the G.O.Ms.No. 94, Housing and Urban 
Development Department [UD 4 (1)], dated 12-6-2009, which has been published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette 
No. 27, Part II—Section 2, Page No. 228, dated 15-7-2009. The following variations are made to the Review Master 
Plan for the Tiruchirappalli Local Planning area approved under the said Act in G.O.Ms.No. 143, Housing and 
Urban Development Department, dated 11-8-2009 and published in the Housing and Urban Development Department 
Notification No. II (2)/Housing/453/2009 at page No. 319 of Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, 
dated 2nd September 2009.
Variations
In the said Master Plan in the ‘Land use schedule’ under the heading Tiruchirappalli Corporation, ”Kottapattu Village’’.
(i) Against the entry	
The expression Commercial use S.F. No. 90/1B,2A2, 4A2 shall be added in 	
	
	
	
	
between 87 part and 298 part.
	(ii) Against the entry	
The expression Mixed Residential use S.F. Nos. 88 to 93 shall be deleted and 88,  	
	
	
	
89, 90 part (except 90/1B, 2A2, 4A2), 91 to 93 shall be added.
Tiruchirappalli,	
R. Selvaraj,
2nd May 2019.	
Member-Secretary,
	
Tiruchirappalli Local Planning Authority.
