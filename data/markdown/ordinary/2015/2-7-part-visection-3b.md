[Rc. No. EIII (3)/50439/2014.]
No. VI-3(b)/1/2015.
Under Sub-Statue (1) of Statute 5 of Chapter XXI of the General Statutes of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University,

M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai by the Senate from among its members  under section  21(4) (a) of the Tamil Nadu
Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai, Act, 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 37 of 1987) in the regular Vacancies.

and Chairman,

Coimbatore-641 012.

M.Phill (N), Ph.D (N), MBA (HM),
Principal,
Apollo College of Nursing,
Chennai-600 095.
2. The tenure of the above Members in the Governing Council of this University will be for the regular period of three
years from 19-12-2014 to 18-12-2017 or the date on which they cease to be a Member of the Senate, whichever is earlier.
