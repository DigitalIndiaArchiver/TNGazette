COMMERCIAL TAXES AND REGISTRATION DEPARTMENT
ALTERATION OF LIMITATION OF REGISTRATION SUB-DISTRICT OF KUNDRATHUR IN THE
REGISTRATION DISTRICT OF CHENNAI SOUTH BE TRANSFERED TO AND FORM PART OF THE
REGISTRATION SUB-DISTRICT OF AVADI UNDER THE REGISTRATION ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 24, Commercial Taxes and Registration (G), 17th February 2012.]
No. II(2)/CTR/111(b)/2012.
Under sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Registration Act, 1908 (Central Act XVI of 1908), the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby alters that with effect on and from the 1st March, 2012 the Villages Sekkadu,
Soranjeri, Chokkanallur bearing numbers 611, 6 and 107 respectively which now form part of the Registration
sub-district of Kundrathur in the Registration District of Chennai South be transferred to, and form part of
the Registration sub-district of Avadi in the said Registration District.
2.
The limits of the said Villages shall be the limits which shall, from time to time, be determined for
the purpose of revenue administration.
SUNIL PALIWAL,
Secretary to Government.
