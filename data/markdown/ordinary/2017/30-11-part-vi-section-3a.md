TAMIL NADU MARITIME BOARD
Direction regarding Scale of rates Levied by the Tamil Nadu Maritime Board
Amendment to Notification
(Letter No. 1381/S1/2012)
No. VI-3(a)/58/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Sections 37, 38 and 39 of Tamil Nadu Maritime Board Act, 1995
(Tamil Nadu Act 4 of 1996) the Tamil Nadu Maritime Board makes the following Amendment to Section XII of the
appendix to the Notification No. VI-(3a)/233(a)/2008, published at pages 1-10 in Part VI - Section 3(a) of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette (No. 285), dated 24-09-2008, with the sanction of Government of Tamil Nadu in
G.O.Ms.No. 31, Highways and Minor Ports (HM1) Department, dated 21-04-2017, as follows:–
AMENDMENT
In the said appendix, for Section XII, the following shall be substituted namely:-
SECTION XII
Licence fee for construction of Jetties, Piers, Quays, Wharves or any other Marine Structure shall be, at the rates
specified in the table below:–
