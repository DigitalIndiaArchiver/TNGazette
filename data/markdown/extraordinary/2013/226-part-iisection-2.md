Whereas, it appears to the Government of Tamil Nadu
that the lands specified in the schedule below and situated in
the Thottakudi Village in Nanguneri Taluk in Tirunelveli District
are needed for a Public Purpose, to wit, for the formation of a
Flood Carrier Channel by interlinking Tamirabarani, Karumeniyar
and Nambiyar Rivers for the benefit of the dry areas in
Tirunelveli and Tuticorin Districts. Notice to that effect is hereby
given to all to whom it may concern in accordance with the
provisions of sub–section (1) of Section 4 of the Land
Acquisition Act, 1894 (Central Act I of 1894);
And whereas, it has become necessary to acquire the
immediate  possession of the lands specified in the Schedule
below, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that the
lands be acquired under the provision of sub–section (1) of
Section 17 of the said Act;
Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by
sub-section (2) of Section 4 of the said Act, the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby authorizes the Special Tahsildar (Land
Acquisition), Interlinking of River Project – Unit II, Nanguneri
Taluk and his staff and workmen to exercise the powers
conferred by the said sub-section.
Under sub–section (4) of Section 17 of the said Act, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that in view of urgency
of the case under caluse (c) of Section 3 of the said Act, the
provisions of Section 5 (A) of the said Act shall not apply
to this case.
