Government of India, Department of Personnel and Training, 
New Delhi, dated 6th June, 2022 is republished:-

Rule 5 of the Indian Administrative Service (Cadre) Rules, 
1954 and with the concurrence of the State Governments

hereby transfers Ms. Ekam J Singh, IAS (TN:2020) from

Shri Raspreet Singh, IPS (WB:2020)".
