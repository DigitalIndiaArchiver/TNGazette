NOTICE UNDER SECTION 15(1) OF TAMIL NADU HIGHWAYS ACT, 2001(TN ACT 34 OF 2002) READ WITH THE 
TAMIL NADU LAND ACQUISITION LAWS (REVIVAL OF OPERATION, AMENDMENT AND VALIDATION) ACT, 2019.

No. VI(1)/311(b-1)/2020.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that the lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below are acquired 
under Chennai – Kanyakumari Industrial Corridor Project for Highways purpose to wit for strengthening and widening of 
SH-58-2, Kancheepuram – Arakkonam –Thirutthani Road at KM 87/820 to KM 90/000 No.53. Illuppaithandalam (Part -2) 
village, Arakkonam Taluk, Ranipet District, and it having already been decided that the entire amount of compensation to be 
awarded for the lands to be paid out of the funds controlled and managed by the Divisional Engineer(H), Kancheepuram 
(CKICP), Tamilnadu after having considering the cause shown by the owner or other persons having interest on such 
lands as the case may do hereby publish the following Notice is issued under sub-section (1) of section 15 of the 
Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (TN Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notiﬁ ed that the Wet and Dry lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below and measuring to 
an extent 63458 of sq.mt of lands are acquired in No.53. Illuppaithandalam (Part -2) village, Arakkonam Taluk, Ranipet 
District, to the same, a little more or less needed for Chennai – Kanyakumari Industrial Corridor Project for State 
Highways purpose, to wit for Strengthening and widening of SH-58-2, Kancheepuram – Arakkonam –Thirutthani Road 
at KM 87/820 to KM 90/000.
The plan of the lands under acquisition are kept in the oﬃ  ce of the Special District Revenue oﬃ  cer (Land Acquisition), 
Chennai - Kanyakumari Industrial Corridor Project, Kancheepuram and oﬃ  ce of the Special Tahsildar (Land Acquisition), 
Chennai – Kanyakumari Industrial Corridor Project Arakkonam in Ranipet District may be inspected at any time during 
the oﬃ  ce hours.
