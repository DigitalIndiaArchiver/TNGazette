ANIMAL HUSBANDRY, DAIRYING AND FISHERIES DEPARTMENT
Amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Animal Husbandry Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 143, Animal Husbandry, Dairying and Fisheries (AH-7), 27th November 2009.]
No. SRO. B-88/2009.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Animal
Husbandry Service (Section 2 in Volume II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1969).
2.
The amendment hereby made shall come into force on the day of 27th November 2009.
AMENDMENT
In the said Special Rules, in rule 9, for sub-rule (c), the following sub-rule shall be substituted, namely:—
“(c) Every person appointed by direct recruitment to a post in Class IV shall, undergo the Foundational “A” course
training, for a period of not less than eight weeks at the A and B wing Foundational Course Training Institute of
Anna Institute of Management, Chennai and pass the examination conducted at the end of the training within the period
of his probation and every person appointed to a post in Class V shall undergo the Foundational ‘B’ Course training for
a period of not less than three weeks at the A and B wing Foundational Course Training Institute of Anna Institute of
Management, Chennai and pass the examination conducted at the end of the training within the period of his probation.”
C. MUTHUKUMARASWAMY,
Secretary to Government.
MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES DEPARTMENT
Amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Industries Serivice.
[G.O. Ms. No. 152,  Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (E1.1), 19th October 2009.]
No. SRO B-89/2009.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tami Nadu Industires
Serivice (Section 19 in Volume II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1969):—
2. The  amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 27th July 1976.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules.—
(1) in rule 1, in category-3, after the category of officer “Principal, Institute of Tool Engineering, Dindigul” the
following category of officer shall be inserted, namely:—
“Personal Assistant (Administration) to the Principal Technical Training Centre, Guindy”;
(2) In rule 2, in the Tabular column under sub-rule (a), after the expression “Public Relations Officer, Information
Officer, Information Section, Guindy.
Personal Assistant (General) and Personal Assistant (Cottage Industries) to the Industries Commissioner and
Director of Industries and Commerce, in the first column, the following expression shall be added, namely:—
“Personal Assistant (Administration) to the Principal Technical Training Centre, Guindy”,
(3) in the Annexure in the Tabular Column, after the expression “Personal Assistant (General) and Personal
Assistant (Cottage Industries) to the Industries Commissioner and Director of Industries and Commerce, Chennai in the
first column the following expression shall be added, namely:—
“Personal Assistant (Administration) to the Principal, Technical Training Centre, Guindy.”

No. SRO B-90/2009.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Industires
Service (Section 19 in Volume II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1969).
2. The  amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 10th September 1984.
AMENDMENTS
in the said Special Rules,—
(1)  In rule, I in cateory-3, after the category of officer “Personal Assistant (Administration) to the Principal,
Technical Training Centre Guindy”, the following category of officer shall be inserted namely:—
 “Assistant Director of Industries and Commerce (Non-Technical) Industrial Escort Serivce”;
(2) In rule 2, in the Tabular column under sub-rule (a) after the expression” Personal Assistant (Administration) to
the Principal, Technical Training Centre, Guindy in the first column the following expression shall be added, namely:—
“Assistant Director of Industries and Commerce (Non-Technical), Industries Escort Serivice”;

Principal, Technical Training Centre, Guindy” in the first column, the following expression shall be added, namely:—
“Assistant Director of Industries and Commerce (Non-Technical),  Industrial Escort Service.”
