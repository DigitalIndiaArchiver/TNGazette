RURAL DEVELOPMENT  AND PANCHAYAT RAJ DEPARTMENT
RE-APPOINTMENT OF  CERTAIN OFFICIAL AS TAMIL NADU STATE ELECTION COMMISSIONER
 FOR CERTAIN PERIOD UNDER TAMIL NADU PANCHAYATS ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No.  44, Rural Development and Panchayat Raj (PR.1), 15th May 2013.]
No. II(2)/RDPR/385(a)/2013.
Under Article 243-K of the Constitution of India, read with clause (b) of sub-section (2) of Section 239
of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby  re-appoints Dr. S. Ayyar, I.A.S. (Retired), as the Tamil Nadu State Election Commissioner
for the second term upto  9th of March 2015.
SHEELA BALAKRISHNAN,
Chief Secretary.
