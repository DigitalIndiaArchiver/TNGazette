ERRATA
THE TAMIL NADU APPROPRIATION BILL, 2013
(L.A. Bill No. 5 of 2013)
and
THE TAMIL NADU APPROPRIATION (VOTE ON ACCOUNT) BILL, 2013
(L.A. Bill No. 6 of 2013)
In the English version of Tamil Nadu Appropriation Bill, 2013 (L.A. Bill No. 5 of 2013) and The
Tamil Nadu Appropriation (Vote on Account) Bill, 2013 (L.A. Bill No. 6 of 2013) published at pages 27-43 of
Part  IV—Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated 28-3-2013, the following corrections
may be made:-
At page 27 —
Under the caption ‘contents’, under the heading ‘Bills’, for the words and figures ‘L.A.Bill No. 5 of 2013 - Tamil
Nadu Appropriation (Vote on Account) Act’, substitute the words and figures, “L.A. Bill No. 6 of 2013 - Tamil Nadu
Appropriation (Vote on Account) Bill”.
In the contents, under the heading ‘Bills’, for the words and figures, ‘L.A. Bill No. 6 of 2013 - Tamil Nadu
Appropriation Act’, substitute the  words and figures “L.A. BiII No. 5 of 2013 - Tamil Nadu Appropriation Bill”.
At page 28 —
In the heading for the words and figures, ‘L.A. Bill No.5 of 2013’, substitute the words and figures
‘L.A. Bill No. 6 of 2013’.
In the enacting formula, for the word, ‘in’, substitute the word, “it”.

At page 36 —
In the heading, for the words and figures, ‘L.A. Bill No. 6 of 2013’, substitute the words and figures,
‘L.A. BiII No. 5 of 2013’.
In the enacting formula, for the word, ‘in’, substitute the word “it”.
At page 37—
In the 27th line, for the figure ‘1,000’ occuring in column 5, substitute the figure “11,000”.
In the 28th line, for the figure ‘2,000’ occuring in column 5, substitute the figure “4,000”.
