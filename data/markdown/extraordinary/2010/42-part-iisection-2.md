MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION AND WATER SUPPLY DEPARTMENT
NOTIFICATIONS UNDER TAMIL NADU DISTRICT MUNICIPALITIES ACT, 1920.
REMOVAL OF CERTAIN PERSON AS CHAIRMAN, NASIYANUR TOWN PANCHAYAT,
ERODE DISTRICT UNDER THE ACT.
[G.O. (D) No. 53, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (TP-II), 17th February 2010,
Maasi 5, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/111(b-1)/2010.
Whereas a written notice of intention to make a motion expressing want of confidence in
Tmt. D. Meenakshi, Chairman, Nasiyanur Town Panchayat, Erode District signed by twelve Councillors, out
of the sanctioned strength of the fifteen Councillors of the said Town Panchayat Council together with a
copy of the motion proposed to be made have been delivered to the Assistant Director of Town Panchayats,
Erode Zone in person by twelve Councillors under the provisions of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities
Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920);
 And, whereas the said motion expressing want of confidence in  Tmt. D. Meenakshi, Chairman of the said
Town Panchayat has been carried out with the support of twelve councillors out of the sanctioned strength of
the fifteen councillors, in the special meeting convened by the Assistant Director of Town Panchayats, Erode
Zone on 30th June 2009, after following the procedures specified in Section 40-A of the said Act;
 Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (12) of Section 40-A of the
Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
removes Tmt. D. Meenakshi, Chairman, Nasiyanur Town Panchayat, Erode District from the Office of the
Chairman of the said Town Panchayat.

COIMBATORE DISTRICT UNDER THE ACT.
[G.O. (D) No. 54, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (TP-II), 17th February 2010,
Maasi 5, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/111(b-2)/2010.

Tmt. K. Vetriselvi, Chairman, Sarcar Samakulam Town Panchayat, Coimbatore District signed by thirteen Councillors out
of the sanctioned strength of fifteen Councillors of the said Town Panchayat Council together with a copy of the motion
proposed to be made have been delivered to the Assistant Director of Town Panchayats, Coimbatore Zone in person
by eleven Councillors under the provisions of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920);
And, whereas the said motion expressing want of confidence in Tmt. K. Vetriselvi, Chairman, of the said Town Panchayat
has been carried out with the support of fourteen Councillors out of the sanctioned strength of the fifteen councillors
in the meeting convened by the Assistant Director of Town Panchayats, Coimbatore Zone, on the 13th July 2009, after
following the procedures specified in Section 40-A of the said Act;
Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (12) of Section 40-A of the
Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
removes Tmt. K. Vetriselvi, Chairman, Sarcar Samakulam Town Panchayat, Coimbatore District from the Office of Chairman
of the said Town Panchayat.
