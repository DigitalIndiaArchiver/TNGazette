DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE THE TAMIL NADU MUNICIPAL CORPORATIONS LAWS (AMENDMENT) ACT, 2011.
(TAMIL NADU ACT 8 OF 2011)
[G.O. Ms. No. 71, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (Election), 12th May 2011.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/216(b1)/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Municipal Corporations Laws
(Amendment) Act, 2011 (Tamil Nadu Act 8 of 2011), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the 12th day of May 2011, as
the date on which the said Act shall come into force.
K. ASHOK VARDHAN SHETTY,
Principal Secretary to Government.
