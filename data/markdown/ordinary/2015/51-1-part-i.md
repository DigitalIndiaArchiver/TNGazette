No.I/PUSP/18/2015.—The following Notification of the
Election Commission of India, New Delhi, dated
20th November 2015 is republished:—
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of
Section 13A of the Representation of the People Act, 1950
(43 of 1950), the Election Commission of India, hereby
nominates Shri Rajesh Lakhoni, I.A.S., (TN:1992) as the
Chief Electoral Officer, Tamil Nadu with effect from the date
he takes over charge and until further orders.
2.Shri Rajesh Lakhoni, I.A.S. (TN:1992) shall cease to
hold and hand over forth with charges of all or any work
under the Government of Tamil Nadu, which he may be
holding before such assumption of office.
3.Shri Rajesh Lakhoni, I.A.S., (TN:1992) while functioning
as the Chief Electoral Officer, Tamil Nadu shall not hold any
additional charge whatsoever under the Government of
Tamil Nadu except that he should be designated as Secretary
to the Government in charge of Election Department in the
State Secretariat.
[No. 154/TN/2015-EPS]
SUMIT MUKHERJEE,
Secretary.
(By Order of the Governor)
K. GNANADESIKAN,
Chief Secretary.
