NOTIFICATIONS BY GOVERNMENT
PUBLIC DEPARTMENT
(Rehabilitaion-II(2)
DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF THE TAMIL NADU NON-RESIDENT TAMIL'S WELFARE ACT, 2011.  
(TAMIL NADU ACT 4 OF 2011)
[G.O. Ms. No. 341, Public (Rehabilitaion-II(2), 2nd May 2022, Chithirai 27, Subakiruthu, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2053.] 
No. II(2)/PURE/332(g)/2022.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Non-Resident Tamils' Welfare 
Act, 2011 (Tamil Nadu Act 4 of 2011), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints dated the 2nd May 2022, as the date on 
which the said Act shall come into force.
	
	
	
	
	
	
	
	
	
	
  D. JAGANNATHAN,
