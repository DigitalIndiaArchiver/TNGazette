The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that the land / lands speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below to be 
acquired for industrial purpose to wit for formation of 40 feet width Link Road from Karur Railway Station to Salem

NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the land / lands speciﬁ ed in the Schedule 
below measuring 3463.0 Sq.Metre to the same, a little more or less needed for industrial purpose to wit, for formation 
of 40 feet width Link Road from Karur Railway Station to Salem National Highways. The plan of the land /lands
