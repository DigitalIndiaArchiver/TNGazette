Amendments.
In the said rules, -
1.	
in rule 17, for Note (1), (2), (3) and (4), the following Note shall be substituted, namely:-
	
“(1) The existing quantum of Fixed Travelling Allowance shall have enhanced by 100% sanctioned with effect from 
1-9-1998 and which was not revised after 1-9-1998.
	
(2) The existing quantum of conveyance allowance paid to employee shall be enhanced by 100%.
	
(3)  Consequent on the increase in the Basic Pay and taking note of the increase in Daily Allowance ordered in 
G.O. Ms. No.237, Finance Department, dated 01-06-2009, the existing monthly ceiling on Travelling Allowance shall be 
reduced from 20% to 10% of revised pay. Wherever the existing ceiling is 27% and 25%, it shall be reduced to 13% and 
12% of revised pay.
	
(4) In respect of Officers having jurisdiction over more than one District and those having jurisdiction more than two 
Districts, the monthly ceiling shall be at 12% of pay and at 13% of pay, respectively.”.

2. The Amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st day of October 2017.
Amendments.
In the said rules, -
1. in rule 17, for Note (1), (2), (3) and (4), the following Note shall be substituted, namely:-
“(1)	 The existing quantum of Fixed Travelling Allowance shall have enhanced by 100% wherever it has not been enhanced 
after the last pay revision i.e. with effect from 1-6-2009 subject to rounding off to next 50 rupees. However, the quantum of 
Fixed Travelling Allowance allowed to employees on percentage of basic pay shall be reduced to 50%.
