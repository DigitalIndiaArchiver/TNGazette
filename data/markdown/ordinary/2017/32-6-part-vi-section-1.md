GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
Variations to the Approved Modified Master Plan for Dindigul Local Planning Area
(Roc. No.397/2016/DLPA)
[G.O.Ms.(2D) No.91, Housing and Urban Development [UD 4(1)] Department, 30th May 2017]
No.VI(1)/255/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred by the sub-section (4) of Section 32 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning
Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972) and in exercise of powers conferred by the G.O Ms.No.94, Housing and Urban
Development [UD4(1)] Department, dated 12-06-2009 which has been published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette
No.27, Part II – Section 2, Page No. 228, dated 15-07-2009 the following variations are made to the Modified Master
Plan for the Dindigul Local Planning Area approved under the said Act and published in the Housing and Urban
Development  Department  Notification  No.II(2)/HOU/611/2001, at page No.308 of part II—Section 2 of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 15th August 2001.
VARIATIONS
In the said Modified Master Plan in the “LAND USE SCHEDULE” under the heading V.No:62, “Mullipadi Village”.
1.
Against the entry for the expression “Mixed Residential Use”  the comprising S.F.Nos:292 to 299 shall be changed
as 292 to 297, 298 (except 298/1A4A and 298/3B), 299.
2.
Against the entry for the expression next to “Commercial Use” –
“Educational use” – 298/1A4A, 298/3B shall be  added.
Dindigul,
B. RAMESH KUMAR,
3rd August 2017.
Member Secretary,
Dindigul Local Planning Authority.

JUDICIAL NOTIFICATIONS
Constitution of Sub-Court at Kangeyam in Tiruppur District.
(Roc. No. 2195/2013/G/Judn)
No.VI(1)/258/2017.
"In exercise of the powers conferred under the first proviso to Section 13 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873
(Central Act III of 1873), the High Court, Madras hereby directs that appeals from the decrees or orders of the District Munsif
Court, Kangeyam be preferred in the court of Subordinate Judge, Kangeyam with effect from the date on which the
Subordinate Judge, Kangeyam assumes charge of that Court."
High Court, Madras,
R. SAKTHIVEL,
27th July 2017.
Registrar General.
