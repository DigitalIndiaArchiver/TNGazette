NOTIFICATION
[G.O. Ms. No. 223, Public Works (N1), 23rd July 2010, Aadi 7, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
The Mettur Dam will be opened on 28-7-2010 at 6.00 P.M. for Cauvery Delta Irrigation during the year of 2010-2011.
There will not be supply of water beyond the prescribed date of 28-1-2011.
2. The ryots of Cauvery Delta region are requested to use the water economically and co-operate with Water
Resources Department officials in proper distribution and efficient utilization of available water. If necessary, turn system will
be introduced.
