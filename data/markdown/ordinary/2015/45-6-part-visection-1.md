 Variation to the Approved Railway Affected Area Detailed Development Plan
Part-II of Tiruchirappalli Local Planning Area.
(Roc.No. 17829/2015/DP2)
No. VI(1)/278/2015.
In exercise of the powers, conferred under sub-section (1) of Section 33 of Town and Country Planning Act 1971,
(Act No. 35 of 1972) the Director of Town and Country Planning in the Proceedings Roc. No. 17829/2015/DP2, dated
9th October 2015 proposes to make the following individual draft variation for Conversion of
Agriculture use into Public Purpose use in T.S. No. 2015/2pt, Block-42 (T.S.No. 2015/6 of Ward-2, Block-42) in
Extent 17150 Sq.ft. Thimmaraya Samuthiram Village, Srirangam Division, Tiruchirappalli Corporation to the
approved Railway affected area Detailed Development Plan Part—Il, Tiruchirappalli Local Planning Area approved by
the Commissioner of Town and Country Planning’s Proceedings Roc. No. 23662/2004/DP3, dated 11th December 2008
and the fact of this approval in Form No.12 published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No. 51, Part-VI—Section 1,
Page Nos. 403 and 404, dated 31st December 2008, Publication No. VI(1)/458/2008.
2. Any person affected or interested in this draft variation may within SIXTY DAYS from the date of Publication
of this notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, can represent in person or submit in writing to the
Member Secretary, Tiruchirappalli Local Planning Authority any objection and suggestions relating there to.
3. The variation with plan may be inspected free of cost at any time during office hours at the above said Local
Planning Authority Office.
