No. II-(2)/TRA/434(e)/2013.—Whereas in exercise of the
powers conferred by clause (1) of Article 258 of the
Constitution the President of India has entrusted to the
Government of Tamil Nadu in Letter No. 4(1)/65 Central II
Food, Agriculture Community Development and Co-
operation (Agriculture) dated 23rd April 1966, with their
consent, the functions of the Central Government under the
Land Acquisition Act, 1894 (Central Act I of 1894) in relation
to Acquisition of land for the Purposes of the Union in the
State and whereas it has already been decided that the
compensation should be paid from the public revenues.
Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by
section 6 of the said act, the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby declares that the lands specified in the schedule
below and Measuring 4.26.0 hectares, be the same a little
more or less, is needed for a public purpose, to wit, for the
formation of new Broad Gauge Railway line between
Tindivanam and Tiruvannamalai at 163. Kunnakuppam
Village of Tiruvannamalai Taluk in Tiruvannamali District
and under section 3 and 7 of the said Act the Revenue
Divisional Officer, Tiruvannamalai is appointed to perform
the functions of a Collector under the act, and directed to
take order for the acquisition of the said lands. Under
sub-sections (1)/(2) of Section 17 of the Act, the Governor
of Tamil Nadu further directs that the possession of the
said lands may be taken immediately after the publication
of the notice mentioned in section 9(1) of the Act. A plan of
the land is kept in the office of the Revenue Divisional
Officer, Tiruvannamalai and may be inspected at any time
during office hours.
