ERratum to Notification
 
No. II(2)/CTR/318(d)/2019.
The following erratum is issued to the Commercial Taxes and Registration Department Notification 
No.II(2)/CTR/269(c-1)/2019, published at pages 1-18 in Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government 
Gazette, Extraordinary, dated the 29th March, 2019:—
Erratum.
	
In the said Notification, 
(a) at page 3, 
	
in line 10, in column (5) of the Table, for “tax” read “state tax”; and 
	
in line 13, in column (5) of the Table, for “eighteen” read “nine”.
(b) at page 17, in line 28, for “18” read “18(9 + 9)”.
