In exercise of the powers conferred by section 113 read with section 122 of the Tamil Nadu 
Town and Country Planning Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972), the Governor of Tamil Nadu 
hereby makes the following amendment to the Tamil Nadu Regularisation of Unapproved Layouts 
and Plots Rules, 2017, namely:- 
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖග
 In the said Rules to rule 10, the following proviso shall be added, namely:-
  
 
“Provided that in cases where all basic infrastructure facilities have been provided up to the satisfaction 
of the local authority, the development charges collected for such layout may be utilized for providing basic 
infrastructure facilities such as roads, drinking water and storm water drains in other areas falling with in that 
particular local authority.”
