MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION AND WATER SUPPLY DEPARTMENT
DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF THE TAMIL NADU MUNICIPAL LAWS
(SECOND AMENDMENT) ACT, 2009.
[G.O. Ms. No. 155, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (Election), 25th August 2009.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/424(d)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Municipal Laws
(Second Amendment) Act, 2009 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 2009), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints
the 1st day of September 2009, as the date on which the said Act shall come into force.
NIRANJAN  MARDI,
Secretary to Government.
