FINANCE DEPARTMENT
Amendment to the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Service Adhoc Rules for the posts of Joint Director of
Co-operative Audit and Assistant Director of Co-operative Audit Department
[G.O. Ms. No. 228, Finance (Co-operative Audit) 25th August 2011, Aavani 8 Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No. SRO B-32/2011.—In exercise of the powers conferred by  the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the rules issued with the Finance Department's Notification
No. SRO B-222/87, dated the 26th June 1987, published at pages 294-298 of Part III-Section-I(b) of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 22nd July 1987.
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 17th June 1981.
AMENDMENT
(1) In the said  Rules, in rule 6(b), in the Table for the expression, "the District Office Manual Test" occurring in b(iii) in column
(3) of the Table, against the entry "(ii) Recruitment by Transfer" in column (2) relating to the post of "Assistant Director of
Co-operative Audit" in column (1) threof, the following expression shall be substituted namely:—
"(iii) the Tamil Nadu Government Officer Manual Test"
(2) After the expression so substituted above, the following proviso shall be added namely:
"Provided that the members of composite Co-operative Department, absorbed permanently in the Co-operative Audit
Department pursuant to the orders issued in the G.O. Ms. No. 26, Finance (Audit-II) Department, dated: 12th January 1989 need
not pass the Account Test for Executive Officers for appointment to the post of Assistant Director of Co-operative Audit by way
of recruitment by transfer".
K. SHANMUGAM,
Principal Secretary to Government.
