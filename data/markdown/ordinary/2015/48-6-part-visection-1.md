Variations to the Approved  Master  Plan for the Mamallapuram  Local Planning Area
(Roc. No. 38/2015/MLPA)
No. VI(1)/305/2015.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 32 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country
Planning Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972), Government in the order G.O.(2D) No.180, Housing and Urban
Development [UD4(1)] Department, dated 26-10-2015 has permitted change of land use of Survey Numbers making the
following variation to the Master Plan for the Mamallapuram Local Planning Area, approved under the
said Act and published in the G.O. Ms. No. 153, Housing and Urban Development [UD4(2)] dated 20-06-2013, Notification
No. II (2)/HOU/501/2013, at Page No. 481 of Part  II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated
17th July 2013.


Dec. 2, 2015]
TAMIL NADU  GOVERNMENT  GAZETTE

VARIATIONS
In the said Master Plan, in the “LAND USE SCHEDULE” of Village 36, Egattur under the heading 3(b) General,
Industrial Zone, the Survey Numbers the expression “84 to 90” shall be deleted and 84,85 [except 85/1B, 2A]
86 to 90 shall be substituted.
And under the heading 1. Mixed Residential Use Zone the Survey No. 85/1B, 2A shall be added.
K.SHANMUGAM,
Mamallapuram,
Member-Secretary (In-charge),
25th November 2015.
Mamallapuram Local Planning Authority.
Variations to the Approved  Master  Plan for the  Coimbatore  Local Planning Area
(Roc. No. 7265/2013/LPA-3.)
No. VI(1)/306/2015.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 32 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country
Planning Act 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972) and in exercise of powers conferred by the G.O. Ms. No. 94, Housing
and Urban Development [(UD4(1)] Department dated 12-06-2009 which has been published in the Tamil Nadu Government
Gazette No. 27 Part lI—Section 2, Page No. 228, dated 15-07-2009 the following variations are made to the
Master Plan for the Coimbatore Local Planning Area approved under the said Act and published in the Housing and
Urban Development Department Notification No. II(2)/HOU /4377/94 at Page 1078 of Part II—Section 2 of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 9th November 1994.
VARIATIONS
In the said Master Plan, in the “LAND USE SCHEDULE” under the heading “[COIMBATORE LOCAL PLANNING
AREA (CORPORATION AREA]” Coimbatore Municipal Town,
The entry “Residential” the expression “Ward 1(9), Block-25, T.S.No. 596/20A,  596/20B” shall be added before
sub-heading Kalapatti D.D. Plan No. 9.
C. MATHIVANAN,
Coimbatore,
Member-Secretary (In-charge),
26th November 2015.
Coimbatore Local Planning Authority.
Variations to the Approved  Review Master  Plan for the Nagercoil  Local Planning Area
(Roc. No. 698/2014/NLPA)
[G.O. (2D) No. 93, Housing and Urban Development [UD4(1)] Department, dated 18th June 2015.]
No. VI(1)/307/2015.
 In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 32 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country
Planning Act 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972) Government in the order G.O. (2D)  No. 93, Housing and
Urban Development [UD4(1)] Department,  dated 18-06-2015 has permitted to change of land use Survey Numbers with
condition making the following variation to the Review Master Plan for the Nagercoil Local Planning Area
approved under the said Act and Published in the G.O.Ms.No.213 H&UD (UD4-2) Department dated 14-10-2008
Notification No. II(2)/HOU/43/2008 at Page No 363 of Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette,
dated 5th November 2008.
VARIATIONS
 In the said Master Plan in the Land Use Schedule Neendakarai ‘A’ Village.
Against the entry I(b) Mixed Residential Use Zone for the expression R.S. No. 443 the expression R.S. No. 443
(except 443/3,443/4B) shall be substituted.
Against the entry V Public and Semi Public Use Zone the expression R.S. No. 443/3,443/4B shall be added.
P. MARIADHASAN,
Nagercoil,
Member-Secretary (In-charge),
26th November 2015.
Nagercoil Local Planning Authority.
