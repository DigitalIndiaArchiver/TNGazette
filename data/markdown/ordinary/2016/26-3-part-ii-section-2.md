No. II(2)/HWMP/391/2016.—The  Governor of Tamil Nadu
having been satisfied that the lands in the schedule below have
to be acquired for the formation of Mudukulathur Bye Pass
Road in the Melamudukulathur Village, Mudukulathur Taluk in
Ramanathapuram District and it having already been decided
that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the
lands to be paid out of the funds controlled and managed by
the Chief Engineer (NABARD and Rural Roads), Highways
Department, Chennai and having considered the cause shown
by the owner or other person having interest in the said lands,on
the cause may be do hereby publish the following notice under
sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act
2001(Tamil Nadu Act34 of 2002).
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu
Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquired the dry lands
specified in the schedule below and measuring an extent
of  25023   squre metre to the same, little more or less, are
needed for the purpose of construction of a Bye Pass Road
in the Mela mudukulathur Village, Mudukulathur Taluk of
Ramanathapuram District.

the District Revenue Officer, Ramanathapuram and may be
inspected at any time during office hours.

Notifications under the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act.
Constitution of Separate District Munsif Court at
Paramathy in Namakkal District under the Act.
[G.O. Ms. No. 455, Home (Cts-III), 9th June 2016.]
No. II(2)/HO/393/2016.—In exercise of the powers conferred
by the second paragraph of Section 4 of the
Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu, after consultation with the High
Court, Madras, hereby fixes the number of District Munsif,
to be appointed to the District Munsif Court at Paramathy
as one with effefct from the date of constitution of separate
District Munsif Court at Paramathy in Namakkal Distirct by
bifurcating the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court
at Paramathy.
