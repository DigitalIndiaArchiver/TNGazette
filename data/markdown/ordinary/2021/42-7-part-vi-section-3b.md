(Letter No. 1587/REGR/TNNLU/2021)
No. VI-3(b)/24/2021.
In the Notiﬁ cation No.VI–3(b)/15/2016, dated: 15-06-2016 “the Tamil Nadu National Law University Duties and 
Powers of Oﬃ  cers and Service Regulations, 2016” have been published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette. 
In the said regulation, the provisions of  “Recruitment and Qualiﬁ cations of Employees other than Oﬃ  cers and 
Academic Staﬀ  of Tamil Nadu National Law University” have been published as Annexure - I and “Pay Scales 
of Academic Staﬀ  & Pay Scales of Non Academic Staﬀ ” have also been published as Annexure – II to the 
Tamil Nadu National Law University Duties and Powers of Oﬃ  cers and Service Regulations, 2016.
2. Now, in exercise of the powers conferred under Section 25 (a) of the Tamil Nadu National Law University 
Act, 2012 (Tamil Nadu Act No.9 of 2012) and in supersession of the provisions of “Recruitment and Qualiﬁ cations 
of Employees other than Oﬃ  cers and Academic Staﬀ  of Tamil Nadu National Law University” as Annexure - I 
and “Pay Scales of Academic Staﬀ  & Pay Scales of Non Academic Staﬀ ” as Annexure – II to the Tamil Nadu 
National Law University Duties and Powers of Oﬃ  cers and Service Regulations, 2016 which were published in the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette vide Notiﬁ cation No.VI–3(b)/15/2016, published at pages 45-50 of Part VI-Section 3(b) 
of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated 15th June 2016, the Executive Council of the Tamil Nadu National Law 
University hereby revises the “Recruitment and Qualiﬁ cations of Employees other than Oﬃ  cers and Academic Staﬀ 
of Tamil Nadu National Law University” as Annexure - I and “Pay Scales of Academic Staﬀ  & Pay Scales of 
Non Academic Staﬀ ” as Annexure – II to the Tamil Nadu National Law University Duties and Powers of Oﬃ  cers 
and Service Regulations, 2016. 
3. The above revised provisions i.e., Recruitment and Qualiﬁ cations of Employees other than Oﬃ  cers and 
Academic Staﬀ , Pay Scales of Academic Staﬀ  and Pay Scales of Non Academic Staﬀ  shall come into force on 
and from the date of its publication in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
4. The revised Recruitment and Qualiﬁ cations of Employees other than Oﬃ  cers and Academic Staﬀ  are as 
follows:-
