CANCELLATION OF NOTIFICATION RELATING TO THE HOLDING OF LANDS OF THIRU R.B.S. MANI, PALLADAM TALUK,
COIMBATORE DISTRICT UNDER THE ACT.
[G.O.Ms.No.36, Revenue [LR-I(2)], 4th February 2013.]
No.II(1)/REV/4/2013.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 18-C of the Tamil Nadu Land Reforms
(Fixation of Ceiling of Land) Act, 1961 (Tamil Nadu Act 58 of 1961), the Governor of Tamil  Nadu hereby cancels the Revenue
Department Notification II(1)/REV/204/83, dated the 1st October 1983, published at page 1 of Supplement to
Part II-Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 9th November 1983, as subsequently amended.
RAJEEV RANJAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.

ORDER : No. 174, Public (Miscellaneous), 21st February 2013
In the Government Order read above, the list of Public Holidays under Negotiable Instruments Act 1881, for the year
2013 has been notified.  Among others, 30.09.2013 was declared as Public Holiday under the Negotiable Instruments Act, for
Half Yearly Closing of Accounts for Commercial Banks and Co-operative Banks.  The Government of India, Ministry of Finance,
Department of Financial Services in their letter read above have requested all the State Governments/Union Territories, not
to notify 30th September as a Bank Holiday under Section 25 of Negotiable Instruments Act 1881.
2.  Accordingly, the Government direct that the holiday for Half Yearly closing of accounts for Commercial Banks and Co-
operative Banks on 30.09.2013 notified in the G.O. read above under Section 25 of Negotiable Instruments Act 1881, be cancelled
and the day on 30.09.2013 is declared as working day for all Commercial Banks and Co-operative Banks.
(By order of the Governor)
SHEELA BALAKRISHNAN,
Chief Secretary.
