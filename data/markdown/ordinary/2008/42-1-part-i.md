No.I/PUSP/38/2008.—The following Notification of the
Government of India, Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances
and Pensions, Department of Personnel and Training, dated the
25th September 2008 is republished:—
In exercise of the powers conferred by Rule 6(1) of the Indian
Administrative Service (Cadre) Rules, 1954, and with the
concurrence of the Governments of Tamil Nadu and Karnataka,
the Central Government hereby accords approval for the inter
cadre deputation of Smt. Sandhya Venugopal Sharma, I.A.S.
(TN:95) from Tamil Nadu cadre to Karnataka cadre for a period
of three years from the date of assumption of the charge of the
post or until further orders, whichever is earlier.
[No.13017/12/2008 AIS(I)]

Deputy Secretary to Government of India.
