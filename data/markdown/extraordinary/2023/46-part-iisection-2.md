UNDER THE TAMIL NADU CIVIL COURTS ACT.

No. II(2)/HO/62(f-1)/2023.
In exercise of the powers conferred by the ﬁrst paragraph of section 4 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts 
Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby alters the number of Subordinate 
Judges to be appointed under the said Act for Tiruchirappalli District as ten with eﬀ ect from the date on which 
the Subordinate Judge, Musiri assumes charge of that Court.
NOTIFICATION-II

No. II(2)/HO/62(f-2)/2023.
In exercise of the powers conferred by the second paragraph of section 4 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts 
Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, after consultation with the High Court, Madras, 
hereby ﬁxes the number of Subordinate Judges to be appointed for the Subordinate Judge’s Court at Musiri 
in Tiruchirappalli District as one with eﬀ ect from the date on which the Subordinate Judge, Musiri assumes 
charge of that Court.

In exercise of the powers conferred by section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873), the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby ﬁxes Musiri in Tiruchirappalli District as the place at which the Subordinate Judge’s Court, 
Musiri shall be held.
NOTIFICATION–IV
[G.O. Ms. No. 40, Home [Courts-III], 31st January 2023,
¬î 17, ĀபÐĉĄ, F¼õœÀõ˜ Ý‡´-2054.]
No. II(2)/HO/62(f-4)/2023.
In exercise of the powers conferred by section 10 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873), 
the Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby directs that in the District of Tiruchirappalli, the Subordinate Judge’s Court at 
Tiruchirappalli shall cease to have jurisdiction over the District Munsif Court at Musiri and the District Munsif-cum-Judicial 
Magistrate Court at Thottiam and hereby ﬁxes the local limits of the District Munsif Court at Musiri and the District Munsif-
cum-Judicial Magistrate Court at Thottiam as the local limits of jurisdiction of the Subordinate Judge’s Court at Musiri with 
eﬀ ect from the date on which the Subordinate Judge, Musiri assumes charge of that Court.
