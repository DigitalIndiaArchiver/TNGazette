GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
Final Closing and Cancellation of Registration of Ind. No. 1303 The Usilampatti Pasumpon Small Match
Producers Service Industrial Co-operative Society Limited
(L.F. 674/ICA/88)
No. VI(1)/61/2010.
In the circumstance stated by the Assistant Director of Industries and Commerce (Industrial Co-operatives)/
Official Liquidator of the Usilampatti Pasumpon Small Match Producers Service Industrial Co-operative Society
Limited, in his final closure proposals, dated 28th January 2010 and in exercise of the powers delegated under
Section 140(1) of Tamil Nadu Co-operative Society Act, 1983, the registration of the Usilampatti Pasumpon Small
Match Producers Service Industrial Co-operative Society Limited. Ind. No. 1303 is hereby cancelled and the affairs
are finally closed with effect from 1st February 2010.
