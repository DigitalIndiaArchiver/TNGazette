APPOINTMENT OF MINISTER FOR THE STATE OF TAMIL NADU
[G.O. Ms. No. 606, Public (Special-B), 19th July 2012, Aadi  4, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2043.]
No. I/PUSP/25(b-1)/2012.
I, K. Rosaiah, Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoint Thiru Thoppu N.D. Venkatachalam with effect from  the
Forenoon of 19th July, 2012, to be the member of my Council of Ministers.
K. ROSAIAH,
Governor of Tamil Nadu.
ALLOCATION OF SUBJECTS AMONG MINISTERS
[G.O. Ms. No. 607, Public (Special-B), 19th July 2012, Aadi  4, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2043.]
No. I/PUSP/ 25(b-2)/2012.
Under Clause (3) of Article 166 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby allocates the subjects
Revenue, District Revenue Establishment, Deputy Collectors, Weights and Measures, Debt Relief including legislation
on Money lending, Chits, Registration of Companies to Thiru Thoppu N.D. Venkatachalam, appointed as a Minister in the
Government Order 12th read above. Consequent on the above allocation, Thiru Thoppu N.D. Venkatachalam be designated
as Minister for Revenue.
DEBENDRANATH  SARANGI,
Chief Secretary.
