HOME, PROHIBITION AND EXCISE DEPARTMENT
Amendment to the Tamil Nadu Liquor Retail Vending (in Shops and Bars) Rules, 2003
[G.O. Ms. No. 34, Home, Prohibition and Excise (VI), 23rd July 2012.]
No. SRO A-21/2012.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Sections 17-C, 17-D, 21 and 22-D read with
Section 54 of the Tamil Nadu Prohibition Act, 1937 (Tamil Nadu Act X of 1937), the Governor of  Tamil Nadu
hereby makes the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu Liquor Retail Vending (in Shops and Bars) Rules, 2003:—
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules,—
(1) for rule 11, the following rule shall be substituted, namely:—
“11. Working hours of shop and bar: No business shall be transacted at the shop and bar except during the
hours as may be specified by the Government, by order.”; and
(2) in Form I, for condition No. 10, the following condition shall be substituted, namely:—
“10. No business shall be transacted at the shop and bar except during the hours as may be specified by
the Government, by order.”
R. RAJAGOPAL,
Principal Secretary to Government.
