JUDICIAL NOTIFICATION.
Amendments in Form No. 1 in Appendix B and Form No. 6 in Appendix G of Code of Civil Procedure.
(Roc. No. 1661-A/2010/F1)
No. SRO C-11/2014.
By Virtue of the powers conferred by Section 122 of the Code of Civil  Procedure, 1908 (Central Act V of 1908) and all
other powers hereunto enabling and with the previous approval of the Government of Tamil Nadu, the High Court,
Madras make the following amendments in Form No.1 in Appendix B and Form No.6 in Appendix G of Code of Civil
Procedure, 1908.
1. ln the said Code of Civil Procedure, 1908 (Central Act V of 1908), in the First Schedule, in Appendix B, in Form No.1,
appended to Order 5, Rules 1 and 5 after the second paragraph, the following paragraph shall be inserted, namely:-
“You are also hereby informed that you may resort to one of the modes of  alternative dispute resolution as provided under
Section 89 of Civil Procedure Code, 1908, namely:-
(a) arbitration;
(b) conciliation;
(c) judicial settlement, including settlement through Lok Adalat; or
(d) mediation.”
2.1n the said Code of Civil Procedure, 1908 (Central Act V of 1908), in the  First Schedule, in Appendix G, in Form No.6,
appended to Order 41, Rule 14 after the second paragraph, the following paragraph shall be inserted, name!y:-
“You are also hereby informed that you may resort to one of the modes of  alternative dispute resolution as provided
under Section 89 of Civil Procedure  Code, 1908, namely:-
(a) arbitration;
(b) conciliation;
(c) judicial settlement, including settlement through Lok Adalat; or
(d) mediation.”
High Court, Madras,
P. KALAIYARASAN,
6th June 2014.
