TAMIL NADU GOODS AND SERVICES TAX RULES, 2017

No. SRO A-22(b)/2020.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 164 of the Tamil Nadu Goods and Services Tax Act, 2017 
(Tamil Nadu Act 19 of 2017) read with rule 3 of the Tamil Nadu Goods and Services Tax (Fifth Amendment) 
Rules, 2020 (hereinafter referred to as the rules), made vide the Commercial Taxes and Registration Department 
Notiﬁ cation No.SRO A-17(e)/2020, published at pages 1-2 in Part III-Section 1(a) of the Tamil Nadu Government 
Gazette, Extraordinary, dated 11th May, 2020, the Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby appoints the 8th day of 
June, 2020, as the date from which the said provisions of the rules, shall come into force.
2. This notiﬁ cation shall be deemed to have come into force with eﬀ ect from the 8th day of June, 2020.
