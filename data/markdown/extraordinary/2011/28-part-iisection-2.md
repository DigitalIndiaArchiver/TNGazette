ORDER:—NO. 4, Adi Dravidar and Tribal Welfare [ADW-6(1)], 4th January 2011.
The Supreme Court of India in its orders dated on 5th October 2007 ordered to issue notification appointing District Magistrate or a
Sub-divisional Magistrate as Executive Authority as required in terms or Section 5(1) of the Employment of Manual Scavengers and
Construction of Dry Latrines (Prohibition) Act, 1993 so as to carry out investigation and to impose penalties on the defaulters
in terms of Section 14 of the Act.
2. In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 5(1) of the Employment of Manual Scavengers and Construction of
Dry Latrines (Prohibition) Act, 1993 (Central Act 46 of 1993), the Governor of Tamil Nadu  hereby appoints all the District Collectors,
who are District Magistrates, as Executive Authorities to exercise jurisdiction within the local limits of the district concerned covering
all Corporations, Municipalities, Town and Village Panchayats and in respect of Chennai Corporation, the Commissioner,
Corporation of Chennai to exercise powers as Executive Authority.
3. The Executive Authorities are requested to ensure that the provisions of the said Act are strictly implemented
in letter and spirit.
(By Order of the Governor)
VISHWANATH SHEGAONKAR,
