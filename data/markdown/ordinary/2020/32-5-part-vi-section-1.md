JUDICIAL NOTIFICATIONS
Constitution of District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court at Peravurani in Thanjavur District.
[Roc.No.1347/A/2014/G/Judn (Phase-II)]
No.VI(1)/269/2020.—
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873) 
the High Court, Madras, hereby ﬁ xes peravurani in Thanjavur District as the place at which the District Munsif-cum-
Judicial Magistrate Court, Peravurani, shall be located.
NOTIFICATION-II
[Roc.No.1347/A/2014/G/Judn (Phase-II)]
No.VI(1)/270/2020.—
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 11 of the Tamil Nadu Civil courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873) 
the High Court, Madras, hereby directs that in Thanjavur District, the District Munsif Court, Pattukottai, shall cease to 
have local jurisdiction over the entire Peravurani Taluk and the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Peravurani 
shall have and exercise local jurisdication over the entire Taluk of Peravurani with eﬀ ect from the date on which the 
District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate, Peravurani assumes charge of that Court.
NOTIFICATION-III
[Roc.No.1347/A/2014/G/Judn (Phase-II)]
No.VI(1)/271/2020.—
The High Court, Madras hereby directs and notiﬁ es that the District Munsif-cum-Judical Magistrate, Peravurani in 
Thanjavur District shall exercise all the powers conferred on a District Munsif under Section 12 of the Tamil Nadu Civil 
Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873) as amended up-to-date in regard to original suits and proceedings not otherwise 
exempted from his cognizance of which the amount or value of the subject matter does not exceed One Lakh rupees 
within the local limits of his jurisdication with eﬀ ect from the date on which the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate, 
Peravurani assumes charge of that Court.
NOTIFICATION-IV
[Roc.No.1347/A/2014/G/Judn (Phase-II)]
No.VI(1)/272/2020.—
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 28 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 
1873) the High Court, Madras, hereby directs that the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate, Peravurani, shall have 
and exercise jurisdiction of a Court of Small causes under the Provincial Small Cause Act, 1887 for the trial of suits 
cognizable by a Court of Small Causes upto the pecuniary limits of Rs. 5000/- (Rupees ﬁ ve thousand only) with eﬀ ect 
from the date on which the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Peravurani, Assumes charge of the Court.
