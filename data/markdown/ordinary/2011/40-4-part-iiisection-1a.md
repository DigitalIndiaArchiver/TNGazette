Amendment to the Tamil Nadu State Human Rights Commission (Salaries and Allowances and other terms and
conditions of service of Members) Rules, 1996.
[G.O. Ms. No. 926, Public (Human Rights), 11th October 2011.]
No. SRO A-18/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) and clause (a) of sub-section (2) of Section 41 of the Protection
of Human Rights Act, 1993 (Central Act 10 of 1994), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment
to the Tamil Nadu State Human Rights Commission (Salaries and Allowances and other terms and conditions of service of
Members) Rules, 1996.
2. The amendment hereby made shall come into force on the 11th October, 2011.
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in rule 5, in sub-rule (C) for the expression “Rs. 7,500/-” and “Rs. 6,000/-”, the expression “Rs. 15,000”
and “Rs. 12,000/-” shall respectively be substituted.
T.N. RAMANATHAN,
Secretary to Government.
