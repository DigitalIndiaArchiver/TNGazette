No. II(1)/RDPR/32(a-1)/2014.
  In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (i) of sub-section (4) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act,
1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994) and in partial modification of the Rural Development and Local Administration
Department Notification R.O.No.B.486 of 1960  published at pages 1 and 2 of Part I-A-Extraordinary of the
Fort. St. George Gazatte, dated the 13th April 1960 and Rural Development and Local Administration Department
Notification II—2 No. 289 of 1961 of published at pages 1 to 4 of Part II—Section 2-Extraordinary of the
Fort.  St. George Gazette, dated the 13th April 1961, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby excludes the local areas specified
in the Table below, from the Kattumannarkoil, Keerapalayam and Kammapuram panchayat development blocks so as
to constitute them as a separate panchayat development block.
The above exclusion shall come into effect only at the time of next ordinary  elections to local bodies.

FORMATION OF NEW PANCHAYAT UNION HEADQUARTER OF SRIMUSHNAM IN CUDDALORE DISTRICT UNDER THE
TAMIL NADU PANCHAYATS ACT 1994 (TAMIL NADU ACT 21 OF 1994).
No. II(1)/RDPR/32(a-2)/2014.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (3) of  Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act,
1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994),  the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby declares the local areas specified in  the
Table below, to be a panchayat development block, namely, Srimushnam Panchayat Development Block and also
declares the said  panchayat development block to be a panchayat union, namely, Srimushnam Panchayat union, the
intention to declare the same having been published as required under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the said Act.  The
formation of the above Panchayat Development Block and Panchayat Union shall come into effect only at the time of next
ordinary elections to local bodies.
