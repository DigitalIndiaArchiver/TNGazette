LEGISLATIVE  ASSEMBLY SECRETARIAT
RESIGNATION OF HON. THIRU P. DHANAPAL, DEPUTY SPEAKER.
[S.O. Ms. No. 164, Legislative Assembly Secretariat, 9th October 2012,
Purattasi 23, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2043.]
No. II(1)/LAS/21(d-1)/2012.
The Hon. Thiru P. Dhanapal, Deputy Speaker, Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly, resigned his Office of Deputy
Speaker with effect from the forenoon of the 9th October, 2012 under clause (b) of Article 179 of the Constitution
of India.
A.M.P. JAMALUDEEN,
Secretary,
Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly.

APPOINTMENT OF SPEAKER PRO-TEM OF THE TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY
[S.O. Ms. No. 165, Legislative Assembly Secretariat, 9th October 2012,
Purattasi 23, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2043.]
No. II(1)/LAS/21(d-2)/2012.
"Whereas the Offices of Speaker and Deputy Speaker of Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly are vacant, in
exercise of the powers conferred by clause (1) of Article 180 of the Constitution of India, I, Dr. K. ROSAIAH,
Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby appoint Dr. C.K. Thamizharasan, being a Member of the Tamil Nadu
Legislative Assembly to perform the duties of the Speaker till the election of the Speaker by the said House
on 10th October, 2012.
Raj Bhavan,
K. ROSAIAH,
Guindy,
Governor of Tamil Nadu.
Chennai-600 022.
Dated: 9th October, 2012.
A.M.P. JAMALUDEEN,
Secretary”.
