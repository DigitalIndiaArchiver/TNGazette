Appointment of certain officials as Registering Authority under the Act
[G.O. Ms. No. 254, Agriculture (AP III), 22nd October 2010.]
No. II(1)/AG/9/2011.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Clause 26 of the Fertilizer (Control) Order, 1985 and in
supersession of Agriculture Department Notifications No. II(1)/AG/51/88 and II(1)/AG/53/88 published at Page 30 of
Part II—Section I of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 30th March 1988, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
appoints the Joint Director of Agriculture of the district to be the registering authority for the purpose of issuing certificate
of Manufacturer of the Nitrogen, Phosphate, Potassium Mixtures (Physical or Granulated), Micronutrient Mixtures, Special
Mixture, Organic Fertilizer and Bio Fertilizer within their respective jurisdiction and the Additional Director of Agriculture
(Seeds) in the office of the Commissioner of Agriculture, Chennai to be the registering authority for the purpose of issuing
certificate of manufacturer for the notified grades of micronutrient mixtures of other States to be manufactured in
Tamil Nadu and Marketed outside the State.
Appointment of certain officials as Notified Authority under the Act
[G.O. Ms. No. 254, Agriculture (AP III), 22nd October 2010.]
No. II(1)/AG/10/2011.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Clause 26 of the Fertilizer (Control) Order, 1985 the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints all the Assistant Director of Agriculture (Quality Control Cell) to be the Notified
Authorities for the acceptance of the Memorandum of Intimation and for issuing letter of authorization and renewal of letter
of authorization in respcet of wholesale dealers/retail dealers under the said order within their respective jurisdiction and
the Deputy Director of Agriculture (Fertilizer) in the Office of the Commissioner of Agriculture, Chennai to be the Notified
authority for the acceptance of Memorandum of Intimation and for issuing letter of authorization and renewal to the
manufactures/importers of straight, Complex Fertilizers, Straight Micronutrients and for the Bio Fertilizers (Liquid/Solid),
Organic Fertilizers, Micronutrient Mixtures, any other grades of notified Fertilizer coming from other States.
Appointment of certain officials as Appellate Authorities under the Act
[G.O. Ms. No. 254, Agriculture (AP III), 22nd October 2010.]
No. II(1)/AG/11/2011.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Clause 32A of the Fertilizer (Control) Order, 1985 and
in supersession of Agriculture Department Notification No. II(1)/AG/54/88 published at Page 31 of Part II—Section 1 of
the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated 30th March 1988, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints,
(1) The Deputy Director of Agriculture (Fertilizer) in the Office of the Commissioner of Agriculture, Chennai as the appeallate
authority to here any appeal filed against the orders passed by the Assistant Director of Agriculture (Quality Control)
regarding:—
(i)
Suspending or cancelling authorization letter or barring from carrying on the business of selling Fertilizer;
(ii) Non-issuance of authorization letter within the stipulated period and
(iii) Non-issuance of amendment in authorization letter within the stipulated period;
(2) The Additional Director of Agriculture (Seeds), in the Office of the Commissioner of Agriculture, Chennai as the
appellate authority in respect of appeal filed against the orders passed by the Joint Director of Agriculture or Deputy Director
of Agriculture (Fertilizer) in the Office of the Commissioner of Agriculture, Chennai;
(3) The Commissioner of Agriculture, Chennai as the appellate authority to here the appeals filed against the orders
passed by the Additional Director of Agriculture (Seeds) in the Office of the Commissioner of Agriculture, Chennai.
P. RAMA MOHANA RAO,
