HOME, PROHIBITION AND EXCISE DEPARTMENT
AMENDMENTS TO THE  TAMIL NADU LIQUOR (SUPPLY BY WHOLSALE) RULES, 1983.
[G.O. Ms. No. 61, Home, Prohibition and Excise (VIII), 12th December 2011.]
No. SRO A-21(a)/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Sections 17-C and 17-D read with Section 54 of the Tamil Nadu
Prohibition Act, 1937 (Tamil Nadu Act X of 1937), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following
amendments to the Tamil Nadu Liquor (Supply by Wholesale) Rules, 1983:—
2. The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 11th July 2011
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules, in rule 15, in sub-rule (1-A), in the tabular column, in the second column,—
(1) for the expression “142.00” against the entry in   item (i) in the first column,  “224.00” shall be substituted;
and
(2) for the expression  “36.00” against the entry in item (iii) in the first column, the expression “51.00” shall
be substituted.
RAMESHRAM  MISHRA,
Principal Secretary to Government.
