TAMIL NADU STATE LEGAL SERVICES AUTHORITY, CHENNAI
Constitution of Taluk Legal Services Committee at Peraiyoor in Madurai District and Appointment of District
Munsif-cum-Judical Magistrate as Chairman of Peraiyoor Taluk Legal Services Committee
(TNSLSA No. 5720/E/2013.)
No.VI(2)/57/2014.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 11 (A) (1) and 29 (A) of the Legal Services Authorities
Act 1987, (Central Act 39/1987) read with Rule 3(a) of the Tamil Nadu Taluk Legal Services Committee Rules 1997, the
Tamil Nadu State Legal Services Authority hereby constitutes a Taluk Legal Services Committee at Peraiyoor in Madurai District
and appoints the District Munsif-cum-Judical Magistrate, as Chairman, Peraiyoor Taluk Legal Segal Services Committee to
excise the powers and perform the functions conferred on, or assigned to the Taluk Legal Services Committee, under the Act.
The district Munsif-cum-Judical Magistrate, Peraiyoor is requested to assume charge as Chairman, Peraiyoor Taluk Legal
Services Committee.
North Fort Road,
K. ARUL,
High Court Campus,
District Judge / Member-Secretary (FAC)
Chennai-600 104.

taken to issue the duplicate of the said Certificates. If the Originals were to be found by anybody, they should be treated
as invalid and sent to the Secretary, Board of Higher Secondary Examination / Directorate of Government  Examinations,
