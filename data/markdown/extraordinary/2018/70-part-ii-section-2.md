BACKWARD CLASSES, MOST BACKWARD CLASSES AND MINORITIES WELFARE DEPARTMENT
APPOINTMENT OF CERTAIN PERSON AS MEMBER OF THE TAMIL NADU WAQF TRIBUNAL AT CHENNAI FOR 
CERTAIN PERIOD UNDER THE WAQF ACT, 1995.
[G.O. (D) No. 10, Backward Classes, Most Backward Classes and Minorities Welfare (T2), 
22nd February 2018,  Maasi 10, Hevilambi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2049.]
No. II(2)/BCMBCMW/167(c-1)/2018.
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (c) of sub-section (4) of Section 83 of the Waqf Act, 1995 (Central Act 43 
of 1995) read with clause (c) of rule 3 and rule 5 of the Tamil Nadu Waqf Tribunal (Conditions of Service and Procedure) 
Rules, 2016, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints Thiru S.M. Meera Mohideen, Advocate as a Member of the 
Tamil Nadu Waqf Tribunal at Chennai, for a period of three years from the date on which he enters upon his ofﬁ ce or till he 
attains 62 years of age whichever is earlier.
CONSTITUTION OF TAMIL NADU WAQF TRIBUNAL AT CHENNAI UNDER THE WAQF ACT, 1995.
[G.O. (Ms.) No. 15, Backward Classes, Most Backward Classes and Minorities Welfare (T2), 
22nd February 2018, Maasi 10, Hevilambi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2049.]
No. II(2)/BCMBCMW/167(c-2)/2018.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 83 of the Waqf Act, 1995 (Central Act 43 of 1995) and 
in supersession of the Commercial Taxes and Religious Endowments Department, Notiﬁ cation No.II(2)/CTRE/2093(a)/97, dated 
the 24th October, 1997, published at pages 1 to 2 of Part II - Section 2 of the  Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, 
dated the 24th October, 1997 as subsequently amended, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby constitutes the  Tamil Nadu Waqf 
Tribunal at Chennai having local limits and jurisdiction over the  entire  State of Tamil Nadu, for the purposes speciﬁ ed in the 
said sub-section, with effect on and from the date on which the Chairman of the Tamil Nadu Waqf Tribunal assumes charge.
 
A. KARTHIK,
