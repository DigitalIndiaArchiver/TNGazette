The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that the lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below are 
acquired under Highways Department for the construction of Elevated corridor in Highways at Km.441/0 - 0 -443/2 
in Varanasi - Kanniyakumari Road via Periyar Bus Stand to Yanaikkal at Melmadurai Village in Madurai West Taluk, 
Madurai District and it having already been decided that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the 
lands to be paid out of the funds controlled and managed by the Divisional Engineer (Building & Maintenance), 
Highways Department, Madurai, Tamil Nadu and afer having considered the cause shown by the owners or other 
persons having interest in the said lands, as the case may be do hereby published the following notice under 
sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (TN Act 38 of 2019).
NOTICE

a little more or less needed for the construction of Elevated Corridor in Highways at Km.441/0 - 0 - 443/2 in 
Varanasi - Kanniyakumari Road via Periyar Bus Stand.
