Under clause (b) of sub-section (2) of section 6 of the Legal Services Authorities Act, 1987 (Central 
Act 39 of 1987), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, in consultation with the Chief Justice of the High Court of 
Madras, hereby nominates Honourable Justice Mr. M. Duraiswamy, Judge, High Court of Madras as the 
Executive Chairman of the Tamil Nadu State Legal Services Authority, Chennai with immediate effect.
S.K. PRABAKAR,
