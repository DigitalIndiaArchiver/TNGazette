No. SRO B-73/2016.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu General Service (Section 16
in volume-II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1969).
2. The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force with effect from the 1st September 2014.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules,
(1)
in Part II, under Class XXXIX, after rule 3, the following rule shall be inserted, namely:-
“4.
Qualification.
No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post of Administrative Officer unless he has served
for a period of not less than two years as Superintendent in the Highways Department.”;
(2)
rule ‘4’ shall be re-numbered as rule ‘5’”.
RAJEEV RANJAN,
Additional Chief Secretary to Government.
