COMMERCIAL TAXES AND REGISTRATION DEPARTMENT
DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF THE TAMIL NADU GOODS AND SERVICES TAX (AMENDMENT) 
ORDINANCE, 2019.
[G.O. Ms. No. 202, Commercial Taxes and Registration (B1), 31st December 2019, Margazhi 15, Vikari, 
Thiruvalluvar Aandu&2050.] 
No. II(2)/CTR/997(c)/2019.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Goods and Services Tax 
(Amendment),  Ordinance, 2019 (Tamil Nadu Ordinance No.10/2019), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the 
1st day of January, 2020,  as the date on which the provisions of sections 2 to 21, except section 2, section 7, section 10 and 
sections 14 to 20 of the Tamil Nadu Ordinance No.10/2019, shall come into force.
 
Ka. BALACHANDRAN,
