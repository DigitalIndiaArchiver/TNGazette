GENERAL  NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI.
Extension of Direct Management of Mohideen Pallivasal Inam Wakf (G.S.No. 248)
G. Tummalapatti, Nilakottai, Dindigul District.
(Rc. No. 11196/12/B8/DGL)
No. VI-3(a)/14/2015.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act 1995, as amended
by Act 27/2013, the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in item No. 41/15 in Rc. No. 11196/12/B8/DGL
dated : 05-03-2015 has resolved to ratify the period of direct management from 18-02-2015 to 05-03-2015 and also
to extend the period of direct management of MOHIDEEN PALLIVASAL INAM WAKF, G. TUMMALAPATTI, NILAKOTTAI,
DINDIGUL DISTRICT which is under the Direct Control of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a further period of one year
from the date of Board's Order (05-03-2015).
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED MEERAN,
13th April 2015.
District Revenue Officer /
Chief Executive Officer (In-charge),
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
