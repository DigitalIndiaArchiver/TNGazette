ADVERTISEMENT OF PETITION
Notice is hereby given that a petition for the winding up of the above named company by the High Court at Chennai
was presented on the 31st day of July 2009 to the said court by the said Mr. K. Jayaraj Proprietor of M/s. Raj Metals
and Alloys, 38, Sattanna Naicken Street, Choolai, Chennai-600 112 in the capacity as creditor and that the said petition
was directed to be heard before the court on the 20th day of April 2010.
Any creditor, contributory or other persons desirous of supporting or opposing the making of an order on the said
petition should send to the petitioner or his advocate, notice of his intention signed by him to his advocate with his
name and address, so as to reach the petitioner or his advocate not later than 3 days before the date fixed for the
hearing of the petition and appear at the hearing for the purpose in person or by his advocate. A copy of the petition
will be furnished by the undersigned to any creditor or contributory on payment of the prescribed charges for the same.
Any affidavit intended to be used in opposition to the petition should be filed in court and a copy served on the
petitioner or his advocate not less than 5 days before the date fixed for the hearing.
