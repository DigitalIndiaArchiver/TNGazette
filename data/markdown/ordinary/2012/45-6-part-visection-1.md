Winding up of the affairs of the Kunniyur Adi-dravidar Coir and Coir products Industrial Co-operative
Society Ltd., Ind. 1891, Kunniyur, Mavur Post, Thiruvarur District and appointment of Official Liquidator.
[Rc. No. 52018/ICH/2008(ii).]
No. VI(1)/329/2012.
“Under the Powers conferred on the Industries Commissioner & Director of Industries & Commerce Under Section
137(2)(b) of Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act 1983, the affairs of the Kunniyur Adi-dravidar Coir and Coir products
Industrial Co-operative Society Ltd., Ind. 1891, Kunniyur, Mavur Post, Thiruvarur District are ordered to be wound up with
immediate effect by the Industries Commissioner & Director of Industries & Commerce, Chennai-600 028”.
Under Section 138(1) of the same Act, the Assistant Director (Industrial Co-operatives), Office of the General Manager,
District Industries Centre, Thiruvarur is appointed as the Official Liquidator for the purpose of liquidation.
HARMANDER SINGH,
Chennai,
