GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
Final Closing and Cancellation of Registration of certain Co-operative Societies.
No. G.762, Mambalam Co-operative Credit Society Ltd.,
(R.c. No. 678/2003/SAPA-2 (2).)
No.VI(1)/354/2014.
“In the circumstances stated by the Co-op. Sub-Registrar (Prosecution and Liquidation) in his final closure report dated
25-10-2014 as the affairs of the Mambalam Co-operative Credit Society Ltd., No. G.762 have been completed by winding up and
in exercise of the powers delegated under section 140(1) of the T.N.C.S. Act 30 of 1983 the registration of the Mambalam
Co-operative Credit Society Ltd., No. G. 762 is hereby cancelled and its affairs are finally closed with effect from 11-11-2014 in
proceeding R.C. No. 678/2003/SAPA2/dated 11-11-2014 of the Deputy Registrar (Credit) Chennai”.
No. XC-203, India Meters Employees Co-operative Thrift and Credit Society Ltd.,
(R.c. No. 4749/2003/SAPA-2 (2).)
No.VI(1)/355/2014.
“In the circumstances stated by the Co-op. Sub-Registrar (Prosecution and Liquidation) in his final closure report dated
25-10-2014 as the affairs of the India Meters Employees Co-operative Thrift and Credit Society Ltd., No. XC-203, have been
completed by winding up and in exercise of the powers delegated under section 140(1) of the T.N.C.S. Act 30 of 1983 the
registration of the India Meters Employees Co-operative Thrift and Credit Society Ltd., No. XC-203 is hereby cancelled and its affairs
are finally closed with effect from 11-11-2014 in proceeding R.C. No. 4749/2003/SAPA 2/dated 11-11-2014 of the Deputy Registrar
(Credit) Chennai”.
No. XC-100, The Government of India Regional Stationery Depot Madras Employees Co-operative
Thrift and Credit Society Ltd.,
(R.c. No. 5276/2009/SAPA-2 (2).)
No.VI(1)/356/2014.
“In the circumstances stated by the Co-op. Sub-Registrar (Prosecution and Liquidation) in his final closure report dated
25-10-2014 as the affairs of the The Government of India Regional Stationery Depot Madras Employees Co-operative Thrift and
Credit Society Ltd., No. XC-100, have been completed by winding up and in exercise of the powers delegated under section 140(1)
of the T.N.C.S. Act 30 of 1983 the registration of the Government of India Regional Stationery Depot Madras Employees
Co-operative Thrift and Credit Society Ltd., No. XC-100 is hereby cancelled and its affairs are finally closed with effect from
11-11-2014 in proceeding R.C. No. 5276/2009/SAPA 2/dated 11-11-2014 of the Deputy Registrar (Credit) Chennai”.
No. XC-74, Kamadhenu Co-operative Super Market Employees Co-operative Thrift and Credit Society Ltd.,
(R.c. No. 3628/2012/SAPA-2 (2).)
No.VI(1)/357/2014.
“In the circumstances stated by the Co-op. Sub-Registrar (Prosecution and Liquidation) in his final closure report dated
25-10-2014 as the affairs of the Kamadhenu Co-operative Super Market Employees Co-operative Thrift and Credit Society Ltd.,
No. XC-74, have been completed by winding up and in exercise of the powers delegated under section 140(1) of the T.N.C.S. Act
30 of 1983 the registration of the Kamadhenu Co-operative Super Market Employees Co-operative Thrift and Credit Society Ltd.,
No. XC-74 is hereby cancelled and its affairs are finally closed with effect from 11-11-2014 in proceeding
R.C. No. 3628/2012/SAPA 2/dated 11-11-2014 of the Deputy Registrar (Credit) Chennai”.
