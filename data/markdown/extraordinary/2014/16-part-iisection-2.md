The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that
the lands specified in the schedule below have to be acquired
for industrial purpose to wit, for the setting up of Industrial
Complex by SIPCOT Sriperumbudur Expansion Scheme and
Complex and  it having already been decided that the entire
amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be
paid out of the funds controlled or managed by the Government,
the following Notice is issued under sub-section (1) of
Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial
Purposes Act, 1997  (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTIFICATION
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby acquires the lands specified in the schedule below and
measuring 0.56.0 hectare to the same, a little more or less
needed for Industrial Complex  by SIPCOT.
The plans of the lands are kept in the Office of the Special
Tahsildar (Land Acquisition), SIPCOT Sriperumbudur Expansion
Scheme and Complex, Kancheepuram District and may be
inspected at any time during office hours.
