No. SRO B-4/2023.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, 
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Jail Subordinate 
Service (Section 17 in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 2016):- 
	
Amendments
In the said Special Rules, in the Annexure:—
(1)	  Under the heading "Class I General Section" against the posts Assistant Jailors, Sub-Assistant Superintendent in 
Borstal School and Jailors in Sub-Jail, Grade I" in column (1) and the corresponding entry "Direct recruitment" in 
column (2) thereof, in column (3), in item (ii), in sub-item (a), after the proviso, the following proviso shall be added, 
namely:-
	
"Provided further that the physical measurement prescribed above shall not be applicable to the departmental 
candidates";
(2)	 under the heading "Class II Women's Section against the post "Assistant Jailor (women)" in column (1) and the 
corresponding entry "(i) Direct recruitment" in column (2) thereof, in column (3), in item (i), in sub-item (a), after 
the proviso, the following proviso shall be added, namely:-
	
"Provided further that the physical measurement prescribed above shall not be applicable to the departmental 
candidates".
