THE CENTRAL UNIVERSITIES ORDINANCE, 2009
No. 3 of 2009
Promulgated by the President in the Fifty-ninth Year of the Republic of India.
An Ordinance to establish and incorporate universities for teaching and research in the various
States and to provide for matters connected therewith or incidental thereto.
WHEREAS a Bill to establish and incorporate universities for teaching and research in the
various States and to provide for matters connected therewith or incidental thereto was
introduced in the House of the People and referred to the Department-related Parliamentary
Standing Committee on Human Resource Development which gave its report on the 17th day
of December, 2008 but the said Bill has not been passed;
AND WHEREAS Parliament is not in session and the President is satisfied that circumstances
exist which render it necessary for her to take immediate action to give effect to the
provisions of the said Bill with certain modifications;
NOW, THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred by clause (I) of article 123 of the
Constitution, the President is pleased to promulgate the following Ordinance:—
1.
(1) This Ordinance may be called the Central Universities Ordinance, 2009.
(2) It shall come into force at once.
