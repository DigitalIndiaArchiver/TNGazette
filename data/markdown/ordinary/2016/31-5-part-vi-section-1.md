Variation to the Approved Chithode New Town Development Plan of Chithode New Town Development Authority.
(Roc.No. 313/2013 CNTDA)
No.VI(1)/256/2016.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 32 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning
Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972) and in exercise of powers conferred by the G.O. Ms. No. 94, Housing and Urban
Development [UD4 (1)] Department, dated 12-06-2009 which has been published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette
(Issue No. 27), Part II—Section 2, Page 228, dated 15-07-2009, of the following variations are made to the Chithode
New Town Development Plan approved under the said Act and Published in the Housing and Urban Development
Department Notification No.II(2)/HOU/1204/99, page 516, Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated
the 27th October 1999.
VARIATION
In the said New Town Development Plan in “Annexure -C” under the heading “Land use of Chithode New Town
Development Plan” in the Tabular Column.
(i) In “Annexure-X” under the heading “VI Agricultural use Zone” against “A-5, 61. Suriyampalayam Village”.
(ii) for the expression 202, the expression 202 except 202/8, 202/9, 202/11, 202/12 shall be substituted and
(iii) for the expression 208, the expression 208 except 208/1, 208/2, 208/3, 208/5 shall be substituted.
2. In “Annexure VII” Under the heading “III Industrial use zone” after serial number 18 in column (1) and the entries
relating thereto in columns (2) and (3) thereof the following serial number and the entries shall, respectively be added
namely:-
18. I-18, 61. Suriyampalayam Village, R.S.No. 202/8, 202/9, 202/11, 202/12 and 208/1, 208/2, 208/3, 208/5.
Erode,
A. VIJAYAN,
28th July 2016.

(Roc.No. 1221/2012 ELPA-2)
No.VI(1)/258/2016.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 32 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country
Planning Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972) and in exercise of powers conferred by the G.O. Ms.No.94, Housing and
Urban Development [(UD4(1)] department, dated 12th June 2009, which has published in the Tamil Nadu Government
Gazette (issue No.27), Part-II—Section-2, Page 228, dated 15th July 2009, of the following variations are made to
the Master Plan for the Erode Local Planning Area Review approved under the said Act and published in the Housing
and Urban Development Department Notification No. II (2)/HOU/85/2011, at page 98 of Part-II—Section-2 of
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated 23rd February 2011.
VARIATIONS
In the said Master Plan in the land use Schedule Tabular Column.
(i)
Under the heading “Local Planning Area Villages” under the sub-heading “PR-6” V.No: 73 Erode Part -II,
Kasipalayam Municipality against the entry in (1) (a) Primary Residential, the Expression R.S.No: “423pt” shall be read
as the expression 423pt (Except 423/3B) and
(ii) Under the heading “Local Planning Area Villages” under the sub-heading “C-15”, V.No: 73. Erode Part-II,
Kasipalayam Municipality against entry in the expression 423/3B shall be added.
