NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu 
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999). The Governor of Tamil Nadu 
hereby acquires the lands speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below 
and measuring 3.70.0 hectares be the same a little more or 
less needed for Industrial purposes, to wit, in favour of Mine-1 
Expansion Scheme of NLC India Limited.
