COMMERCIAL TAXES AND REGISTRATION
DEPARTMENT
Exemption of tax payable by any dealer on the right to
use of  Cinematographic Equipments, Studios,
Laboratories, Recording Theatres and Lease of Audio,
Video and Satellite Rights for certain period under
Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax Act.
[G.O.Ms.No. 137, Commercial Taxes and Registration (B2),
13th August 2010, Aadi 28, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No. II(2)/CTR/543/2010.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 30 of the Tamil Nadu
Value Added Tax Act, 2006 (Tamil Nadu Act 32 of 2006),  the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby exempts the tax payable by
any dealer under Section 4  of the said Act on the right to use
of Cinematographic equipments falling under Entry 17 of
Part-C of First Schedule and  Cine equipments in Studios,
Laboratories, Recording Theatres and lease of Audio, Video
and satellite rights.
2. This Notification shall come into force on the
29th September 2010 and shall remain in force upto and
inclusive of the 28th September 2012.
MD. NASIMUDDIN,
Secretary to Government.

Vesting of Properties and Scheme of administration
relating to Saraswathi Shanmugam Charitable
Endowment Fund tenable in the Government
Arts College, Paramakudi under Charitable
Endowments Act.
[G.O.Ms.No. 230, Higher Education  (D2), 2nd August 2010,
Aadi 17, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No. II(2)/HE/544/2010.—In the matter of the Charitable
Endowments Act, 1890 (Central Act VI of 1890) and in the
matter of “Saraswathi Shanmugam Charitable  Endowment
Fund” [hereafter referred to as “the said Endowment”] tenable
in the Government Arts College, Paramakudi.
I. VESTING OF PROPERTIES
Whereas, an application has been made to the Government
of Tamil Nadu under clause (b) of sub-section (1) of Section 6
of the Charitable Endowments Act, 1890 (Central Act VI of
1890) by S. Banumathi, Retired Principal, residing at No. 3,
4th Street, Uma Nagar, Thanjavur-613 007, the donor of the
said endowment for vesting the property specified in the
Schedule below, in the Treasurer of Charitable Endowments,
Chennai, appointed under Section 3 of the said Act for the
State of Tamil Nadu, the Governor of Tamil Nadu, in exercise
of the powers conferred by Section 4 of the said Act, hereby
orders that the said property be vested in the Treasurer
aforesaid, and be held by him and his successors-in-office,
subject to the provisions of the said Act and the rules which,
from time to time, may be or shall be made or deemed to

II. SCHEME FOR ADMINISTRATION
Whereas, an application has been made to the Government
of Tamil Nadu under clause (b) of sub-section (1) of Section 6
of the Charitable Endowments Act, 1890 (Central Act VI of
1890) by S. Banumathi, Retired Principal, residing at No. 3,
4th Street, Uma Nagar, Thanjavur-613 007, the donor of the
said Endowment specified above, for settling a Scheme for
administration of the said Endowment, the Governor of
Tamil Nadu, in exercise of the powers conferred by
sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Charitable Endowments
Act, 1890 (Central Act VI of 1890), hereby settles the Scheme
setforth in the Schedule below, for the administration of the
said Endowment and appoints the Principal, Government Arts
College, Paramakudi, to administer the property. The Scheme
shall come into operation from the date of publication of this
Notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
THE SCHEDULE
(1) The fund shall be called “Saraswathi Shanmugam
Charitable Endowment Fund” tenable in the Government Arts
College, Paramakudi.
(2) The fund shall be administrated by the Principal,
Government Arts College, Paramakudi.
(3) From and out of the annual interest accruing on the
fund the best outgoing student of all the Under Graduate
Courses of the Government Arts College of Paramakudi, shall
be given a prize every year.
(4) If more than one student securing the same marks, the
prize shall be equally distributed among them.
(5) The mode by which the interest amount shall be given
as a prize  to the best outgoing student shall be specified by
the administration.
(6) The un-utilized amount of the interest shall be added
to the corpus of the Fund.
(7) The decision of the administrator shall be final.
(8) The amount of the said Fund shall be invested and
reinvested in Government Securities.
K. GANESAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
