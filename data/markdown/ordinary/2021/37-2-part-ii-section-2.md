From the Joint Director and Special Inspector General 
of Police, Economic Oﬀ ences Zone-II, CBI, New Delhi, 
letter No.DP ME12020E/C1/RC8(E)/2018, dated Nil.
Order: No. 352, Home (Police XIX), 26th August 2021.
WHEREAS, complaints have been received from a 
number of depositors that Messers. Parivar Dairies and 
Allied Limited, Gwalior and Messers.PDA Foundation, 
New Delhi have defaulted the return of deposits made by 
the depositors after maturity; 
AND WHEREAS, the Government is satisﬁ ed that the said 
ﬁ nancial establishment is not likely to return the deposits to 
the depositors and hence, the Government have to protect 
the interests of such depositors; 
AND WHEREAS, the immovable properties speciﬁ ed 
in the Schedule to this order are alleged to have been 
procured by the said ﬁ nancial establishments from and out 
of the deposits collected from the depositors; 
NOW, THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred 
by Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Protection of Interests 
of Depositors (in Financial Establishments) Act, 1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 44 of 1997), the Governor of Tamil Nadu 
hereby makes an ad-interim order attaching the immovable 
properties and the bank accounts of Messers.Parivar Dairies 
and Allied Limited, Parivar Bhavan, 302-303 Ganesh Plaza, 
Gole Ka Mandir, Gwalior and Messers.PDA Foundation, 
House No.25,26,G-4, Ground Floor, Sector 16, Rohini, 
New Delhi-110 085, and others, as speciﬁ ed in the Schedule 
to this order and transfers the control over the said immovable 
properties to the Competent Authority, namely the Competent 
Authority/ District Revenue Oﬃ  cer, Madurai District, appointed 
under the said Act. 
(2) The Competent Authority / District Revenue Oﬃ  cer 
Madurai District is requested to pursue further action in 
accordance with the procedure laid down in sub-sections 
(3) and (4) of Section 4 of the said Act and also the 
formalities prescribed in the Tamil Nadu Protection of Interests 
of Depositors (in Financial Establishments) Rules, 1997. 
(3) The Special Government Pleader, Special Court for the 
Tamil Nadu Protection of Interests of Depositors (in Financial 
Establishments) Act, 1997 Madurai, is requested to render 
necessary assistance to the Competent Authority / District 
Revenue Oﬃ  cer, Madurai District for ﬁ ling the application 
before the Special Court constituted under the said Act.
(By order of the Governor)

period; or
