PUBLIC DEPARTMENT

DECLARATION OF PUBLIC HOLIDAY ON 6TH DECEMBER 2016 AS A MARK OF RESPECT TO
SELVI J JAYALALITHAA HON'BLE CHIEF MINISTER OF TAMIL NADU UNDER THE
NEGOTIABLE INSTRUMENTS ACT.
[G.O.Ms.No.927, public (Special-B) 5th December 2016, Karthigai 20, Thunmugi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2047.]
No. II(1)/PUSP/14(b)/2016.
Under the Explanation to Section 25 of the Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881 (Central Act XXVI of 1881) read with
Notification of the Government of India, Ministry of Home Affairs No.20-25-26, Public -1, dated 8th June 1957 the
Government of Tamil Nadu hereby declares that Tuesday, the 6th December, 2016 as a public holiday as a mark of respect
to Selvi J Jayalalithaa, Hon'ble Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu.
2. The above notified Public Holiday shall also apply to all State Government Undertakings / Corporations /
Boards, etc.
3. The day will also be treated as a paid holiday for all industrial employees on regular work charged and industrial
establishments paid on monthly basis and the labour hired on daily wages for the entire month.
(By Order of the Governor)
P. RAMA MOHANA RAO,
 Chief Secretary.
