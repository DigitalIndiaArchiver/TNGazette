[ELECTIONS—PANCHAYATS - APPOINTMENT OF RETURNING OFFICERS AND ASSISTANT RETURNING OFFICERS -
DISTRICT ELECTION OFFICERS AUTHORISED TO MAKE APPOINTMENTS - NOTIFICATION - ISSUED.]
(S.O. No. 7/2016/TNSEC/PE-1)
No. VI(2)/91(d-3)/2016.
In exercise of the powers conferred under rule 8,  and 10 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Elections) Rules, 1995, the Tamil
Nadu State Election Commission hereby authorises the District Election Officers to make appointment of Returning Officers and
Assistant Returning Officers for the conduct of election of Ward Members of the District Panchayats, Panchayat Union Councils
and Village Panchayats and also for the conduct of the election of Presidents of Village Panchayats within their respective
jurisdiction in connection with the conduct of Ordinary Elections, 2016 to Panchayats and subsequent Casual Elections in the State.
(By Order of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission)
T.S. RAJASEKAR,
Secretary.

No. VI(2)/91(d-4)/2016.

     (S.O.No. 17/2011/TNSEC/ME-1, Dated 11th August 2011.)
In exercise of the powers conferred under rule 9,  of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities,
Municipalities and corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006 and in supersession of the Notification No. 264 under

Chennai to appoint within their respective jurisdictions the executive Officer of the Town Panchayat or Third Grade Municipality
or the Commissioner of Municipality or City Municipal Corporation respectively or any other officer of the Government as
