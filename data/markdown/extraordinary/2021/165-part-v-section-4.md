NOTIFICATION BY THE ELECTION COMMISSION OF INDIA
APPOINTMENT OF DATES FOR VARIOUS STAGES OF BYE-ELECTION TO LOK SABHA FROM 
39. KANNIYAKUMARI PARLIAMENTARY CONSTITUENCY, 2021 AND FIXATION OF HOURS OF POLL 
TO FILL A VACANCY CAUSED BY THE DEATH OF A MEMBER.
No. SRO G - 16/2021.
The following Notiﬁ cation of the Election Commission of India, Nirvachan Sadan, Ashoka Road, New Delhi-110 001, 
dated 12th March, 2021 [21 Phalguna, 1942 (Saka)] is published:-
No.100/TN-HP/1/2021 (1):- WHEREAS, the seat of Shri H. Vasanthakumar, in the House of the People, 
elected from 39- Kanniyakumari Parliamentary Constituency in the State of Tamil Nadu, has become vacant on 
28.08.2020,  by reason of his death.
2.   WHEREAS, a bye-election is to be held for the purpose of ﬁ lling the vacancy so caused;
3.  NOW, THEREFORE, in pursuance of  sub-section (1) of Section 149, and Sections 30 and 56  of the 
Representation of the People  Act, 1951 (43 of 1951), the Election Commission of India hereby -  
(A) calls upon the  said 39- Kanniyakumari Parliamentary Constituency in the State of Tamil Nadu, to elect, 
before the 04.05.2021 (Tuesday) and in accordance with the provisions of the said Act and of the rules and orders 
made thereunder, a person for the purpose of ﬁ lling the said vacancy in the House of the People;
(B) appoints, with respect to the said election, -
(a) the 19.03.2021 (Friday), as the last date for making nominations;
(b) the  20.03.2021 (Saturday) as the date for the scrutiny of nominations;
(c) the  22.03.2021 (Monday), as the last date for the withdrawal of candidatures;
(d) the 06.04.2021 (Tuesday), as the date on which a poll shall, if necessary, be  taken;  and
(e) the  04.05.2021 (Tuesday) as the date before which the election shall be  completed;  and
