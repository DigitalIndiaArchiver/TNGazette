The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that
the lands specified in the schedule below have to be acquired
for industrial purpose, to wit, for Industrial purpose for setting up
SIPCOT Industrial Complex and it having already been decided
that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the
lands is to be paid out of the funds controlled or managed by
Government, the following Notice is issued under sub-section
(1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for
Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10/1999).
NOTIFICATION
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil
Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
acquires the lands specified in the schedule below and
measuring 4.54.0 hectares, to the same, a little more or less
needed for industrial purposes, to wit for Industrial purpose of
setting up SIPCOT Industrial Complex, Hosur.
The plan of the lands is kept in the office of the Special
Tahsildar  (Land Acquisition), SIPCOT, Hosur and may be
inspected at any time during office hours.
