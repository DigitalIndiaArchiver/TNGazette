TAMIL NADU SIDDHA MEDICAL COUNCIL AND TAMIL NADU BOARD OF INDIAN MEDICINE, CHENNAI.
Notification
(L.No. 3294/TNSMC/2013)
No. VI(1)/252/2016.
It is hereby informed that in the Tamil Nadu Board of Indian Medicine, as per the announcement made by the
Hon'ble Chief Minister in the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly, вҖңAвҖқ Class Registration Certificates were issued with
2D barcode to eliminate bogus certificates. While issuing 2D barcode certificates, old registration numbers issued by
the erstwhile Central Board of Indian Medicine from the year 1933 were cancelled and new numbers were given
separately for Ayurveda and Unani. As discussed with the Central Council of Indian Medicine officials, the old registration
certificates with old registration number will not be valid from 01-01-2017. Likewise, in Tamil Nadu Siddha Medical
Council, the old вҖңAвҖқ Class registration certificates issued by the Tamil Nadu Board of Indian Medicine before formation
of Tamil Nadu Siddha Medical Council in 1998 will not be valid from 01-01-2017.
Arumbakkam, Chennai-600 106,
G. RAJASEKARAN,
15th July 2016.
