The Government of Tamil Nadu, having been satisﬁ ed that 
the lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below have to be acquired 
for the Industrial purpose of setting up of SIPCOT Industrial 
Park and it having already been decided that the entire amount 
of compensation to be awarded for the lands to be paid out 
of the funds controlled or managed by the Government. The 
following notice is issued under sub – section (1) of Section 
3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purpose 
Act, 1997. (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999)
NOTIFICATION
 
Under sub – section (1) of Section 3 of the 
Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu 
hereby acquires the dry lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule 
below and 6.03.0 Hectares to the same, a little more or 
less needed for Industrial purpose, to wit, for the Industrial 
purpose of setting up of SIPCOT Industrial Park, Dhoripalli 
Village, Krishnagiri Taluk and District.
 
The Plan of the lands are kept in the ofﬁ ce of the 
District Collector, Krishnagiri District and may be inspected at 
any time during the ofﬁ ce hours.
