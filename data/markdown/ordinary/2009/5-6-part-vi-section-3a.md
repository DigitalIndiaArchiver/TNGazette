GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD
NO. 1, JAFFAR SYRANG STREET, VALLAL SEETHAKATHI NAGAR, CHENNAI-600 001.
Taking over the Direct Management of Naina Mohamed Jumma Pallivasal, Rahamaniyapuram, Kadayanallur,
Tenkasi Taluk, Tirunelveli District.
(Lr. Rc. No. 6405/B18/03/TNV.)
No. VI-3(a)/6/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995, the Tamil Nadu
Wakf Board in its proceedings in item No. 144/2004 in Rc.No. 6405/B18/03/TNV, dated 29th November 2007 has ordered
to take over the Management of Naina Mohamed Jumma Pallivasal, Rahamaniyapuram, Kadayanallur, Tenkasi Taluk,
Tirunelveli District under the direct control of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of two years from the date of
Board's order and to appoint the Superintendent of Wakfs, Tirunelveli as its Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
21st January 2009.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
Taking over the Direct Management of Lalkhan Pallivasal, Chidambaram Taluk, Cuddalore District.
(Lr. Rc. No. 13529/B5/96/CUD.)
No. VI-3(a)/7/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995, the Tamil Nadu
Wakf Board in its proceedings in item No. 81/2008 in Rc.No. 13529/B5/96/CUD, dated 18th November 2008 has ordered
to take over the Management of Lalkhan Pallivasal, Chidambaram Taluk, Cuddalore District under the direct control of
the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of one year from the date of Board's order and to appoint the Superintendent
of Wakfs, Cuddalore as its Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
29th January 2009.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
