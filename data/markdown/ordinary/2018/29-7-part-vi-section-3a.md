GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAQF  BOARD, CHENNAI.
Taking Over of The Sheik Ismail Subedar Pallivasal Waqf, Uraiyur, Trichy District under the Direct Management.
(Rc. No. 14961/17/B5/Trichy)
No. VI-3(a)/61/2018.
 In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under  Section 65 of the Waqf Act, 1995 (as amended), 
the Tamil Nadu Waqf  Board in its Resolution in Item No. 41/18, Rc.No. 14961/17/B5/TRICHY, dated 02-03-2016 has 
ordered to take over the management of Sheik Ismail Subedar Pallivasal, Uraiyur, Trichy District under the direct control 
of the  Tamil Nadu Waqf Board for a period of six months from 19-06-2018 and  appointed the Superintendent of 
Waqf, Trichy as its Executive Ofﬁ cer. 
Chennai-600 001,            
N.S. MOHAMED ASLAM,
10th July 2018.
