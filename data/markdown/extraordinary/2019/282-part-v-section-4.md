NOTIFICATIONS BY THE ELECTION COMMISSION OF INDIA
APPOINTMENT OF DATES FOR VARIOUS STAGES AND FIXATION OF HOURS OF POLL FOR BIENNIAL 
ELECTIONS TO THE COUNCIL OF STATES TO FILL VACANCIES OF MEMBERS RETIRING DURING JULY, 2019
No. SRO G-47/2019.
The following Notification of the Election Commission of India, New Delhi, dated: 1st July, 2019 [Ashadha 10, 1941(Saka)] 
is republished:-
No.318/CS-TN/2019(1):- Whereas, the President of India has, by notification issued under Section 12 of the Representation 
of the People Act, 1951 (43 of 1951) and published in the Gazette of India on 01st July, 2019, been pleased to call upon the 
elected members of Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly, to elect 6 (six) members to the Council of States to fill the vacancies 
in that house to be caused due to the retirement of the 6 (six) sitting members on 24th July, 2019 on the expiration of their 
term of office;
Now, therefore, in pursuance of sub-section (1) of Section 39 and Section 56 of the said Act, the Election Commission 
of India hereby:—
(A)	 appoints, with respect to the said election;
	
(a)	 the 8th July, 2019 (Monday), as the last date for making nominations; 
	
(b)	 the 9th July, 2019 (Tuesday), as the date for the scrutiny of the nominations;
	
(c)	 the 11th July, 2019 (Thursday), as the last date for the withdrawal of candidatures;
	
(d)	 the 18th July, 2019 (Thursday), as the date on which a poll shall, if necessary, be taken; and
	
(e)	 the 22nd July, 2019 (Monday), as the date before which the election shall be completed; and 
(B)	 fixes the hours from 9:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m., as the hours during which the poll shall, if necessary, be taken in all 
the above mentioned States on the date specified above for the election.
(By Order)
