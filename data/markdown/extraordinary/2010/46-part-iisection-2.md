[G.O. Ms. No.18, Industries (MIE-2), 22nd February 2010.]
No. II(2)/IND/111(d-1)/2010.
The Government of Tamil Nadu, having  been satisfied that the lands specified in the Schedule below have to be acquired
for industrial purposes  for setting up of Multi Product Special Economic Zone , Perambalur and it has already been decided
that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid out of the funds controlled or managed
by Tamil Nadu Industrial Development Corporation Limited (TIDCO).  The following Notice is issued under sub-section (1) of
Section 3 of  the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of  Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu
Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of  Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands specified in the schedule below and measuring
1.07.5 hectares or 2.66 acres to the same, a little more or less needed for industrial purposes,  i.e., Multi Project Special
Economic Zone, Perambalur.
The plan of the lands are kept in the office of the  District Collector, Perambalur, and may be inspected at any time during
office hours.
THE SCHEDULE
Perambalur District, Veppanthattai Taluk, 36. Eraiyur Village.
Dry, S.No. 68-2 (Patta No. 144), Land owner/interested person Vaiyapuri, son of Perumal—1.07.5 hectares.
