Amendment to the Tamil Nadu Minor Ports Harbour Craft Rules, 1953.
[G.O. Ms. No. 25, Highways and Minor Ports (HN2), 4th February 2009, Thai 22, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. SRO A-3/2009.—In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (k) of sub-section (1) of Section 6 of the Indian Ports
Act, 1908 (Central Act XV of 1908), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the
Tamil Nadu Minor Ports Harbour Craft Rules, 1953, the draft of the same having been previously published as required under
sub-section (2) of Section 6 of the said Act.
