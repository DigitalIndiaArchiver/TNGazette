RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND PANCHAYAT RAJ DEPARTMENT 
Appointment of special officers to village panchayats, panchayat unions and 
district panchayats under the tamil nadu panchayats act.
amendment to notification
[G.O. Ms. No. 185, Rural Development and Panchayat Raj (PR-1), 31st December 2018.]
No. II(2)/RDPR/1099(f-1)/2018.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 261-A of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994 
(Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Rural 
Development and Panchayat Raj Department Notification No.II(2)/RDPR/687(d-1)/2016, published at page 1 
of Part II - Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, Extraordinary, dated the 24th October 2016, 
as subsequently amended. 
2. This amendment shall come into force with effect on and from the 1st January 2019.
Amendment
In the said Notification, for the expression “until the 31st day of December 2018”, the expression “until the 
30th day of June 2019” shall be substituted.
