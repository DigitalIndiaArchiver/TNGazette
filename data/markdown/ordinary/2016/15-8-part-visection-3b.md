Member Elected to the Syndicate by the Academic Council
(No. V.1/Gazette/03-2016/130)
No. VI-3(b)/7/2016.
It is hereby notified that Dr. M. Selvaraj, M.Sc., Ph.D., Principal, Guru Nanak College, Chennai has been unanimously
elected to the Syndicate by the Academic Council from among its members of whom shall be teachers of affiliated colleges
under Section 18[a] - Other Members [2] of the Madras University Act 1923 for the residual period upto 08-10-2016 or till the
date of cessation of his membership in the Academic Council whichever is earlier.
