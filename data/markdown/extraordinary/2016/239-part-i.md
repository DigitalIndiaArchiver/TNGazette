ALLOCATION OF BUSINESS DURING THE PERIOD OF MEDICAL TREATMENT OF HON'BLE CHIEF MINISTER
No. I/PUSP/20(a)/2016.
The following Government Order is Published:—
 [G.O.Ms. No. 819, Public (Special-B), 11th October 2016, Purattasi 25, Thunmugi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2047.]
READ:
G.O.Ms.No. 512, Public (Special-B) Department, dated 23rd May 2016
Order No: 819, Public (Special-B), 11th October 2016.
Under clause (3) of Article 166 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that during
the period of medical treatment of Selvi J Jayalalithaa, Hon'ble Chief Minister, the subjects i.e., “Public, Indian Administrative
Service, Indian Police Service, Indian Forest Service, General Administration, District Revenue Officers, Police, Home” dealt
with by the Hon'ble Chief Minister be allocated to Thiru O. Panneerselvam, Minister for Finance, Personnel and Administrative
Reforms.
2. Thiru O. Panneerselvam, Minister for Finance, Personnel and Administrative Reforms will preside over the Cabinet
Meetings during the period of medical treatment of Selvi J Jayalalithaa, Hon'ble Chief Minister.
3. The files which require circulation to the Hon'ble Chief Minister as per the Business Rules be circulated to
Thiru O. Panneerselvam, Minister for Finance, Personnel and Administrative Reforms during the period of medical treatment
of Selvi J Jayalalithaa, Hon'ble Chief Minister
(By Order of the Governor)
P. RAMA MOHANA RAO
Chief Secretary.
