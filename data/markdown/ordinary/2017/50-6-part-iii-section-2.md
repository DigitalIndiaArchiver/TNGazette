Errata to Notification
(Rc. No. K-38/1/2017/TNeGA)
The following Errata are issued to the Commissionerate of e-Governance Notification No. SRO C-27/2017, Published at
pages 144-146 of Part III—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette issue No. 43, dated 25th October 2017.
ERRATA
In the said notification,-
(1) The title shall be read as:—
Under Section 57 of the Aadhaar (Targeted Delivery of Financial and other Subsidies, Benefits and Services)
Act, 2016 (Central Act 18 of 2016) for the State Resident Data Hub (SRDH) Portal
(http://makkal.tn.gov.in)
(2) after the title, the 1st Paragraph shall be read as:-
WHEREAS, the National Population Register (NPR) is a Register of usual residents of the country, which is being
prepared at the local  (Village/sub-Town), sub-District, District, State and National level under the provisions of the Citizenship
Act, 1955 (Central Act No. 57 of 1955) and the Citizenship (Registration of Citizens and issue of National Identity Cards) Rules,
2003 and hence it is an authenticated database. It is mandatory for every usual resident of India to register in the
NPR. A usual resident is  defined for the purposes of NPR as a person who has resided in a local area  for the past six months
or more or a person who intends to reside in that area for the next six months or more;
(3) the 2nd Paragraph of page No. 144 shall be read as:-
AND WHEREAS, ‘State Resident Data Hub’ has been created by the  Government of Tamil Nadu as a secure, unified,
digitized data repository  derived from the National Population Register to service all departments and  it is envisaged as the
single source of data for Tamil Nadu State’s residents  including address and has provision for Aadhaar authentication and
it will  help the Government in better targeting, effective service delivery, greater  accountability and more efficient monitoring
of schemes;
(4) the 6th Paragraph in page No. 144 shall be read as:-
NOW, THEREFORE, in pursuance of the provisions of Section 57 of  the Aadhaar (Targeted Delivery of Financial
and other Subsidies, Benefits  and Services) Act, 2016 (Central Act 18 of 2016), the Commissioner of  e-Governance and
Chief Executive Officer, Tamil Nadu e-Governance Agency  hereby authorizes the use of Aadhaar number for establishing
the identity of  an individual for purpose specified hereunder:-
(5) after 6th Paragraph the point No. (1), (2) & (3) shall be read as:-
(1)
All residents who wish to avail the services of the ‘State Resident  Data Hub (SRDH)’ portal
(‘http://makkal.tn.gov.in) are required to  furnish proof of possession of Aadhaar number or undergo  Aadhaar
authentication.
(2)
Such residents of Tamil Nadu desirous of correcting or changing  their demographic details like name, age,
Gender, address etc. to avail the services offered by various departments of the  Government of Tamil Nadu
are hereby required to provide their  Aadhaar number or undergo Aadhaar authentication and then correct their
demographic details in the ‘State Resident Data Hub  (SRDH)’ portal (‘http://makkal.tn.gov.in) in any of the
e-Sevai  Centres by furnishing any documents listed in the Annexure as  proof of identity and proof of address.
