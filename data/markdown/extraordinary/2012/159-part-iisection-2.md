NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
FINANCE DEPARTMENT
DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF THE TAMIL NADU INFRASTRUCTURE DEVELOPMENT ACT, 2012
(ACT 22 OF 2012).
[G.O. Ms. No. 211, Finance (BPE), 14th June 2012, Vaikasi 32, Thiruvalluvar Aandu 2043.]
No. II(2)/FIN/308(a-1)/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4)  of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Infrastructure
Development Act, 2012 (Tamil Nadu Act 22 of 2012), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the
15th day of June 2012, as the date on which the said Act shall come into force.
ESTABLISHMENT OF TAMIL NADU INFRASTRUCTURE DEVELOPMENT BOARD WITH EFFECT
FROM THE 15TH DAY OF JUNE 2012 UNDER THE TAMIL NADU INFRASTRUCTURE
DEVELOPMENT ACT, 2012.
[G.O. Ms. No. 212, Finance (BPE), 14th June 2012, Vaikasi 32, Thiruvalluvar Aandu 2043.]
No. II(2)/FIN/308(a-2)/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1)  of Section  3 of the Tamil Nadu Infrastructure
Development Act, 2012 (Tamil Nadu Act 22 of 2012), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby establishes the
Tamil Nadu Infrastructure Development Board with effect from the 15th day of June 2012.
K. SHANMUGAM,
Principal Secretary to Government.
