TAMIL NADU WAQF BOARD, CHENNAI.
Framing of scheme in respect of Mohideen Andavar Dargah Pallivasal and Mina Nuruddin Oulia Dargah, 
South Gate, Madurai District.
(Rc. No. 11006/18/B8/MDU)
No. VI-3(a)/2/2022.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 69 of the Waqf Act, 1995, the Tamil Nadu 
Waqf Board in its Proceedings in item No.114/2021 in Rc.No.11006/18/B8/MDU, dated 24.11.2021 has approved the 
Scheme framed for the Waqfs Viz., Mohideen Andavar Dargah Pallivasal and Mina Nuruddin Oulia Dargah, South 
Gate, Madurai District for better administration of the Waqfs (G.S.No.135/Mdu and G.S.No.139/Mdu).
Chennai,  
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
         Dක. D. FARITHA BANU,
1st February 2022. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
          Chief Executive Oﬃ  cer/
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
           Tamil Nadu Waqf Board.
Taking over the Direct Management of Hazrath Noor Mohamed Sha Aulia Dargah, Panruti, Cuddalore District.
(Lr. Rc. No. 16379/92/B10/Cudd)
No. VI-3(a)/3/2022.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 64 of the Waqf Act, 1995, the Tamil Nadu 
Waqf Board in its Resolution in item No. 94/21 in Rc. No.16379/92/B10/Cudd, dated: 25.11.2021 has ordered to take 
over the Management of HAZRATH NOOR MOHAMED SHA AULIA DARGAH, PANRUTI, CUDDALORE DISTRICT under 
the control of Tamil Nadu Waqf Board for a period of SIX MONTHS from 25.11.2021 to 24.05.2022 and to appoint 
the Superintendent of Waqfs, Cuddalore as its Executive Oﬃ  cer.
Chennai,  
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
           Dක. D. FARITHA BANU,
1st February 2022. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
           Chief Executive Oﬃ  cer/
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
           Tamil Nadu Waqf Board.
Taking over the Direct Management of Sevalur Pallivasal and part of Jumma Pallivasal, 
Manaparai Taluk, Trichy District.
(Lr. Rc. No. 12046/92/B5/TRY)
No. VI-3(a)/4/2022.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Waqf Act, 1995, the 
Tamil Nadu Waqf Board in its Resolution in item No.140/21 dated: 27.10.2021 has ordered to take over the Management 
of SEVALUR PALLIVASAL AND PART OF JUMMA PALLIVASAL, MANAPARAI TALUK, TRICHY DISTRICT under the 
Direct management of Tamil Nadu Waqf Board for a period of SIX MONTHS from 27.10.2021 and to appoint the 
Superintendent of Waqfs, Trichy as its Executive Oﬃ  cer.
Chennai,  
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
           Dක. D. FARITHA BANU,
