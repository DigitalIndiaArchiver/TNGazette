[Courts and Judges—District Judges—Deputation of one
District Judge from Tamil Nadu State Judicial Service to
Puducherry Judicial Service—Repatriation of one District Judge
from Tamil Nadu State Judicial Service to Puducherry Judicial
Service and Deputation of two District Judges from Puducherry
Judicial Service to Tamil Nadu State Judicial Service—
Orders—Issued.]
The following Government Order is published:—
[G.O.  (D) No. 220, Public (Special-A), 11th May 2011,
Chithirai 28, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
READ:
From the Registrar General, High Court, Madras,
D.O. Letter No. 13/2011, Con. B2, dated 27th April 2011.
Order: No. 220, Public (Special-A), 11th May 2011.
Accepting the proposal of the High Court of Madras sent
by the Registrar General, High Court of Madras, Chennai, the
Government direct that the following orders of deputation,
repatriation and appointment in respect of certain District
Judges:–
NOTIFICATIONS   BY  GOVERNMENT
(i) Thiru A.M. Basheer Ahamed, District Judge of
Tamil Nadu State Judicial Service, functioned as Principal
District Judge, Dindigul, now under compulsory wait, be
deputed to the Puducherry Judicial Service for being posted
as Judge, Family Court, Pondicherry in the place of
Thiru E.M.K.S. Siddharthar;
(ii) Thiru D. Ramabathiran, District Judge of Puducherry
Judicial Service, functioning on deputation as Principal District
Judge, Cuddalore, be repatriated to his parent department
viz., Puducherry Judicial Service for being posted in the Union
Territory of Puducherry;
(iii) Thiru K. Uthirapathy, District Judge of Puducherry
Judicial Service, functioning on deputation as Member
Secretary, Union Territory of Puducherry Legal Services
Authority, Pondicherry be appointed as Principal District Judge,
Cuddalore, in the place of Thiru D. Ramabathiran on deputation
basis in the Tamil Nadu State Judicial Service;  and
(iv) Thiru E.M.K.S. Siddharthar, District Judge of Puducherry
Judicial Service, functioning as Judge, Family Court,
Pondicherry be appointed as District and Sessions Judge,
District Court No. II, Kancheepuram in the existing vacancy
on deputation basis in the Tamil Nadu State Judicial Service.
(By order of the Governor)
S. MALATHI,
Chief Secretary.
