NOTIFICATION
[G.O. Ms. No. 90, Public Works (N-1), 30th May 2011, Vaikasi 16, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
The Mettur Dam will be opened on 6-6-2011 for Cauvery Delta Irrigation during the year of 2011-2012. There will
not be supply of water beyond the prescribed date of 28-1-2012.
2. The ryots of Cauvery Delta region are requested to use the water economically and co-operate with Water
Resources Department officials in proper distribution and efficient utilization of available water. If necessary, turn system
will be introduced.
