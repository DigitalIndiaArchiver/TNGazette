WHEREAS on considering the recommendations of the expert team of Doctors and Public Health Specialists and based 
on the directives of Government of India, Ministry of Home Aﬀ airs, State-wide lockdown was extended from time to time 
and lastly extended till 24:00 hrs of 31-08-2020 under the Disaster Management Act, 2005 in GO.Ms.No.396, Revenue and

2. The Government of India, Ministry of Health and Family welfare issued a guidelines on Preventive Measures to 
Contain Spread of COVID-19 in Yoga Institutes and Gymnasiums on 3-8-2020.
  
3. Hon'ble Chief Minister in the Press Release, dated 5-8-2020 announced that the Private Gymnasiums (stand alone 
only), with members of less than 50 years of age will be permitted  with eﬀ ect from 10-8-2020, and Standard Operating 
Procedure (SOPs) will be issued separately.  
 
4. The Principal Secretary and Commissioner of Revenue Administration has now sent the following amendment to 
the G.O.Ms.No.396, Revenue and Disaster Management Department, dated 31-07-2020.
The contents under Caption I and in section (ix) the word “Gymnasiums” is deleted and the para will be read as,
“All Cinema halls, swimming pools, entertainment parks, theatres, bars, auditoriums, assembly halls, Beaches, 
Tourist places, Zoos, Museums and similar places. Sports complex and stadia will be permitted to open without 
spectators”. 
5. Now, therefore the Government hereby accept the proposal of the  Principal Secretary and Commissioner of Revenue 
Administration and amend the G.O.Ms.No.396, Revenue and Disaster Management Department, dated 31-07-2020 as detailed 
below with eﬀ ect from 10-8-2020.

WHEREAS on considering the recommendations of the expert team of Doctors and Public Health Specialists and based 
on the directives of Government of India, Ministry of Home Aﬀ airs, State-wide lockdown was extended from time to time 
and lastly extended till 24:00 hrs of 31-08-2020 under the Disaster Management Act, 2005 in GO.Ms.No.396, Revenue and 
Disaster Management (DM-II) Department, dated 31-07-2020 with various relaxations and certain restrictions.
 
2. Hon'ble Chief Minister in the Press Release, dated 8-8-2020 announced to permit the small temples with annual income 
of less than Rs.10,000/-, small mosques, Darghas, Churches and other religious places in the Corporation areas throughout 
the State for public worship from 10-08-2020 by obtaining prior of approval from Commissioner, Greater Chennai Corporation 
in respect of Greater Chennai City Corporation and from concerned District Collector in respect of other Corporations and 
permit the functioning of Driving Schools throughout the State from 10-8-2020 by following the Standard Operating Procedures.
 
3. The Principal Secretary and Commissioner of Revenue Administration accordingly sent amendment proposals in line 
with the above announcements to the G.O.Ms.No.396, Revenue and Disaster Management Department, dated 31-07-2020.
4. Now, therefore the Government accept the proposal of the Principal Secretary and Commissioner of Revenue Administration 
and hereby amend the G.O.Ms.No.396, Revenue and Disaster Management Department, dated 31-07-2020 with eﬀ ect from 
10-8-2020 as detailed below:
Under the heading Prohibited Activities in Caption I, 
(A) In section (i) the existing paragraph is modiﬁ ed and will be read as follows: 
(i)(a). All religious places/places of worship except small temples with annual income of less than Rs.10,000/-, small 
Mosques, Darghas, Churches and other religious places in all corporation areas and big religious places/places of 
worship throughout the State will be closed for public worship”. 
 (B) The following paragraph is also added: 
(i)(b). Following the Standard Operating Procedures issued already, in respect of opening of small temples with annual 
income of less than Rs.10,000/-, small mosques, Darghas, Churches and other religious places in all Corporation areas public 
worship will be allowed with prior permission from the Commissioner, Greater Chennai Corporation in respect of  the areas 
falling under Greater Chennai Corporation limits  and from the respective District Collectors in respect of other Corporations 
in the State of Tamil Nadu. 
(C) In section (vi) the existing paragraph is modiﬁ ed and will be read as follows 
“Schools, Colleges, Educational/Training/Coaching Institutions except driving schools (under Training Institutions) will 
remain closed.  Online / Distance learning shall continue to be permitted and shall be encouraged”.
