Constitution of a District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court at Uthukuli in Tiruppur District.
(Roc. No. 980/2014/G/Judn.)
No.VI(1)/355/2019.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873) the 
High Court, Madras, hereby fixes Uthukuli in Tiruppur District as the place at which the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate 
Court, Uthukuli, shall be located.
NOTIFICATION-II
(Roc. No. 980/2014/G/Judn.)
No.VI(1)/356/2019.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 11 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873) the 
High Court, Madras, hereby directs that in Tiruppur District, the District Munsif Court, Avinashi, shall cease to have local 
jurisdiction over the entire Uthukuli Taluk and the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Uthukuli shall have and exercise 
local jurisdiction over the  entire Taluk of Uthukuli with effect from the date on which the District  Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate, 
Uthukuli, assumes charge of that Court.

NOTIFICATION-III
(Roc. No. 980/2014/G/Judn.)
No.VI(1)/357/2019.
The High Court, Madras hereby directs and notifies that the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate, Uthukuli in Tiruppur 
District shall exercise all the powers conferred on a District Munsif under Section 12 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central 
Act III of 1873) as amended up-to-date in regard to original suits and proceedings not otherwise exempted from his cognizance of 
which the amount or value of  the subject matter does not exceed One Lakh Rupees within the local limits of his jurisdiction with 
effect from the date on which the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate, Uthukuli assumes charge of that Court.
NOTIFICATION-IV
(Roc. No. 980/2014/G/Judn.)
No.VI(1)/358/2019.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 28 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873) the 
High Court, Madras, hereby directs that the District Munsif-cum- Judicial Magistrate, Uthukuli, shall have and exercise 
jurisdiction of a Court of Small Causes under the Provincial Small Cause Courts Act, 1887 for the trial of suits cognizable by a Court 
of Small Causes upto the pecuniary limits of Rs.5000/- (Rupees five thousand only) with effect from the date on which the District 
Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Uthukuli, assumes charge of that Court.
High Court, Madras, 	
C. KUMARAPPAN,
28th June 2019.

Constitution of a Fast Track Court - V at Magisterial Level for Trial of Cases under 138 Negotiable Instrument 
Act at Saidapet, Chennai
(Roc. No. 1298/2017/D)
No.VI(1)/359/2019.
In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (3) of Section 16 of the C               ode of Criminal Procedure, 1973, 
(Central Act 2 of 1974), and in modification of the notification (s) dated, 28-04-2017 on the subject, the Chief Metropolitan Magistrate, 
Egmore, Chennai, hereby authorizes the Fast Track Court - V at Magisterial Level for trial of cases under Negotiable Instrument 
Act at Saidapet in Chennai, which is constituted as per G.O. (Ms) No.202, Home (Courts-II) Department, dated 10-03-2017, to 
exercise the jurisdiction over the local limits over which, the Metropolitan Magistrate Fast Track Court - V, Saidapet was hitherto 
exercising jurisdiction in respect of Criminal Cases arising under Section 138 of the Negotiable Instruments Act 1881, (26 of 1881) 
and all other Metropolitan Magistrates exercising jurisdiction within the said local limits shall cease to have jurisdiction to deal with 
cases arising under Section  138 of Negotiable Instrument Act with effect from the date of Constitution of Fast Track Court-V  at 
Magisterial Level for trial of cases under Section 138 Negotiable Instrument Act at Saidapet.
Egmore, Chennai, 	
K. RAVI,
9th July 2019.	
Chief Metropolitan Magistrate.
