No. II(2)/ECCF/139/2023.— In exercise of the powers 
conferred by Section 16 of the Tamil Nadu Forest Act 
1882 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1882), the Governor of 
Tamil Nadu hereby declares that the area, the boundaries 
of which are specified in the Schedule below, as reserved 
forest with effect on and from 31-07-2023.

BOUNDARIES
North.- The boundary of the Forest Block starts 
from the junction point of Survey Numbers 84/1A and 
84/1B of No.30, Oddanchatram Village, it generally runs 
towards East along the Southern boundary of Survey 
Number 84/1A and reaches the tri-junction point of 
Survey Numbers 84/1A, 84/1B of No.30, Oddanchatram 
Village and Western side of Northern slope reserved 
forest boundary. 
East.- Thence, the boundary generally runs towards 
south along the Western boundary of northern slope 
reserved forest and reaches the junction point of Survey 
Number 84/1B of No.30, Oddanchatram Village and 
northern slope reserved forest. 
South.- Thence, the boundary runs towards West 
along the Northern boundary of northern slope reserved 
forest and reaches the tri-junction point of Survey 
Numbers 84/1A and 84/1B of No.30, Oddanchatram 
Village and northern slope reserved Forest. 
West.- Thence, the boundary runs towards North 
along the eastern side of Survey Number 84/1A of No.30, 
Oddanchatram Village and reaches the starting point. 
Admitted Rights 
No Rights admitted in the Forest Block.
