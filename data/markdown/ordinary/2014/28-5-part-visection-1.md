Final Closing and Cancellation of Registration of certain Co-operative Societies.
No. X-306. Binny Employees Co-operative Thrift and Credit Society  Limited.
(R.C. No. 14264/2003/SAPA-2.)
No. VI(1)/251/2014.
In the circumstances stated by the Co-operative Sub-Registrar (Prosecution and Liquidation) in his final closure
report, dated 30-6-2014 as the affairs of the Binny Employees Co-operative Thrift Credit Society Limited, No. X-306
have been completely wound up and in exercise of the powers delegated under Section 140(1) of the Tamil Nadu
Co-operative Societies Act 30 of 1983, the registration of the Binny Employees Co-operative Thrift & Credit Society
Limited, No. X-306 is hereby cancelled and its affairs are finally closed with effect from 30-6-2014 in proceeding
R.C. No. 14264/2003/SAPA-2, dated 30-6-2014 of the Deputy Registrar (Credit), Chennai-600 108.
No. X-278. Binny Engineering Works Employees Co-operative Thrift and Credit Society  Limited.
(R.C. No. 3276/2007/SAPA-2.)
No. VI(1)/252/2014.
In the circumstances stated by the Co-operative Sub-Registrar (Prosecution and Liquidation) in his final closure report
dated 30-6-2014 as the affairs of the Binny Engineering Works Employees Co-operative Thrift & Credit Society Limited,
No. X-278. have been Completely wound up and in exercise of the powers delegated under Section 140(1) of the
Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act 30 of 1983 the registration of the Binny Engineering Works Employees Co-operative
Thrift and Credit Society Limited, No. X-278, is hereby cancelled and its affairs are finally closed with effect from 30-6-2014
in proceeding R.C. No. 3276/2007/SAPA-2, dated 30-6-2014 of the Deputy Registrar (Credit) Chennai-600 108.
