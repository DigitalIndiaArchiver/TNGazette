Draft Variation to the Approved Pollachi Detailed Development Plan No. 12 of Pollachi Local Planning Area.
(Roc. No. 7117/2014/DP1.)
No. VI (1)/256/2014.
In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (1) of Section 33 of Town and Country Planning Act, 1971
(Act No. 35 of 1972), the Commissioner of Town and Country Planning in the Proceedings Roc. No. 7117/2014/DP2,
dated 4-7-2014, proposes to make the following individual draft variation for “conversion of Commercial use into Residential
use in T.S. No. 760pt of Ward-II, Block-13 (New T.S. Nos. 760/2pt, 760/3pt, 760/5pt, Ward-B, Block-13) in Extent 2.45 Acres
with the condition the Proposed DD 60'.0” Road pt, CC 80'-0" widening Road pt, F5F5 40'-0" road pt & EE 50'-0"
Road pt to be developed before Handed over to the Pollachi Municipality by gift deed to the approved Pollachi Detailed
Development Plan No. 12 of Pollachi Local Planning Area approved by the Director of Town and Country Planning Proceedings
in Roc. No. 8063/05/DP2, dated 21-5-2014 and to fact of this approval in Form No. 12 published in the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No. 23, Part VI—Section1 Page No. 265, dated:18-6-2014.
2. Any person affected or interested in this draft variation may within SIXTY DAYS from the date of publication of this
notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, can represent in person or submit in writing to the Municipal

Draft Variation to the approved Ganapathy Detailed Development Plan No. 2
of Coimbatore Local Planning Area.
(Roc. No. 4132/2014/DP2.)
No. VI (1)/258/2014.
In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (1) of Section 33 of Town and Country Planning Act, 1971
(Act No. 35 of 1972), the Commissioner of Town and Country Planning, in the Proceedings Roc No. 4132/14/DP2, dated
14-7-2014 proposes to make the following individual draft variation for “conversion of Residential
use into Industrial use in S.Nos. 437pt & 440pt (Ward-V22, Block-50, T.S.Nos.75/1, 76/1pt) in Extent 9535.5 sq.m of
Ganapathy Village, Coimbatore Corporation subject to leaving a buffer zone in the western side and obtaining NOC from
TNPCB at the time of getting Planning Permission in the approved Ganapathy Detailed Development Plan No. 2 of Coimbatore
Local Planning Area approved by the Director of Town and Country Planning Proceedings in Roc.No. 2971/94/DP3, dated
8-2-1994 and to fact of this approval in Form No. 12 published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette
No. Part VI—Section 1, Page Nos. 216, 217, dated 22-2-1995 Publication No. VI (1)/257/95.
2. Any person affected or interested in this draft variation may within SIXTY DAYS from the date of publication of this
notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, can represent in person or submit in writing to the Member Secretary,
Coimbatore Local Planning Authority any objections and suggestions relating there to.
3. The Variation with plan may be inspected free of cost at any time during office hours at the above said Local
Planning Authority Office.
VARIATION
