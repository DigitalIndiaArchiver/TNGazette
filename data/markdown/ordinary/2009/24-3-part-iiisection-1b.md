PERSONNEL AND ADMINISTRATIVE REFORMS DEPARTMENT
AMENDMENT TO AD HOC RULES RELATING TO TEMPORARY POST OF LECTURER IN COMPUTER-CUM-SYSTEM
ANALYST IN THE CIVIL SERVICE TRAINING INSTITUTE, BHAVANISAGAR FOR TAMIL NADU GENERAL SERVICE.
[G.O. Ms. No. 68, Personnel and Administrative Reforms (Trg.I), 8th June 2009,  Vaikasi-25, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. SRO B-39/2009.— In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the ad hoc rules published with the Personnel
and Administrative Reforms Department Notification No. SRO B-15/2006 at pages 15-16 of Part III—Section 1(b) of
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 25th January 2006.
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st June 1999.
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, after rule 7, the following rule shall be added, namely:—
“8. Saving clause-Nothing contained in these rules shall adversely affect the holder of the post on the date of
coming into force of this rule”.
K.N. VENKATARAMANAN,
Secretary to Government (in-charge).
