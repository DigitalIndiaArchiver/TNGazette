JUDICIAL NOTIFICATION
CONSTITUTION OF SUB COURT AT JAYANKONDAM IN ARIYALUR DISTRICT
(Roc No. 640/2012/G/Judn)
No. VI(1)/102(a)/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred under the first proviso to Section 13 of the Tamil Nadu
Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873), the High Court, Madras hereby directs that appeals
from the decrees or orders of the District Munsif Court, Jayankondam be preferred in the court of
Subordinate Judge, Jayankondam with effect from the date on which the Subordinate Judge,
Jayankondam assumes charge of that Court.
High Court, Madras,
R. SAKTHIVEL,
11th April 2017.                                                         Registrar General.
