Variations to the  Approved Master Plan for the Coimbatore Local Planning Area
(Roc. No. 6813/2010/LPA-1)
No. VI(1)/186/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4)  of Section 32 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country
Planning Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972) and in exercise of powers conferred by the G.O. Ms. No. 94, Housing
and Urban Development UD4(1) Department, dated 12th June 2009 which has been published in the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette  No. 27, Part II—Section 2, page No. 228, dated 15th July 2009, the following
variations are made to the Master Plan for the Coimbatore Local Planning Area approved under the said Act and
published in the Housing and Urban Development Department Notification No. II(2)/HOU/4377/94 at page 1078 of
Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 9th November 1994.
 VARIATIONS
 In the said Master Plan, in the “LAND  USE  SCHEDULE” under the heading “COIMBATORE LOCAL PLANNING
AREA (OTHER THAN COIMBATORE CORPORATION AREA) under the sub-heading (a) Notified Detailed Development
Plan Area in Coimbatore Taluk, No. 70. VELLALORE VILLAGE, VELLALORE DETAILED DEVELOPMENT
PLAN No. 12
(i) Against the entry “EDUCATIONAL” the expression  "643/1B part, 643/1C, 645/1,2, 646/2J,2K,2L,2M,
647/1D, 2 part", shall be added and
  (ii) Against the entry  "AGRICULTURAL" for the expression "643/1B part, 1C, 645/1,2, 646/2J,2K,2L,2M,
647/1D, 2 part" the expression "643 to 648, (except 643/1B part, 1C, 645/1,2, 646/2J,2K,2L,2M, 647/1D, 2 part shall
be substituted.
Coimbatore,
G. ESWARAN,
22nd March 2012.
Member-Secretary (In-charge),
Coimbatore Local Planning Authority.

Approval of Velampalayam Detailed Development Plan No. 2 Tiruppur Local Planning Authority.
(Roc. No. 348/2002)
FORM No. 12
(Under Rule 17 of the Preparation and Sanction of Detailed Development Plan Rules.)
No. VI(1)/187/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 29 of the Town and Country Planning Act 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act
No. 35 of 1972) the Director of Town and Country Planning has approved in his proceedings Roc. No. 18725/2005 DP2
dt. 31-03-2011 Velampalayam Detailed Development Plan No. 2 of Tiruppur Local Planning Authority. The Map of this
Detailed Development Plan is numbered as Map No. 4 and 5 DDP(CR)/DTCP No. 33/2011 the Velampalayam Detailed
Development Plan No. 2 prepared for the Planning area Bounded by
North:
North by Southern boundary of Rakkiyapalayam Village
South:
South by Northern boundary of S.F. Nos. 17, 37, 36, 34, 287 and 284
East:
East by Western boundary of S.F. Nos. 295, 296, 297 and 283.
West:
West by Eastern boundary of S.F. Nos. 14, 12, 11, 20, 22 and 23
Comprising Survey Nos. 18, 19, 24 to 33, 288 to 294
Total Extent: 180.95 Acres (Approximate)
2. It shall come in to operation of from the date of Publication of the Notification in the Tamil Nadu Government
Gazette.
3. A copy of the map of the area included in the Plan will be kept for inspection and also available for sale, during
office hours in the office of the Local Planning Authority for a period of three months.
