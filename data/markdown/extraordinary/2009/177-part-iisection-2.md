NOTIFICATIONS UNDER TAMIL NADU TRANSPARENCY IN TENDERS ACT
PROCUREMENT OF THE PROVISION OF COMMON USER GROUP MOBILE PHONE SERVICES
FROM BHARAT SANCHAR NIGAM LIMITED BY TAMIL NADU FOREST DEPARTMENT UNDER THE ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 302, Finance (Salaries), 17th July 2009, Aadi 1, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. II(2)/FIN/358(c-1)/2009.
Under clause (f) of Section 16 of the Tamil Nadu Transparency in Tenders Act, 1998 (Tamil Nadu Act 43 of 1998), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notifies the procurement of provision of Common User Group Mobile Phone services from Bharat
Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL) by the Tamil Nadu Forest Department.
APPOINTMENT OF "INDIAN NATIONAL TRUST FOR ART AND CULTURAL HERITAGE" AS THE CONSULTANT FOR
THE TRANQUEBAR DEVELOPMENT PROJECT UNDER THE ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 303, Finance (Salaries), 17th July 2009, Aadi 1, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. II(2)/FIN/358(c-2)/2009.
Under clause (f) of Section 16 of the Tamil Nadu Transparency in Tenders Act, 1998 (Tamil Nadu Act 43 of 1998), the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby notifies the appointment of the "Indian National Trust for Art and Cultural Heritage" (INTACH) as the consultant
to render Consultancy, Monitoring and Supervising  Services for the Tranquebar Development Project.
K. GNANADESIKAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
