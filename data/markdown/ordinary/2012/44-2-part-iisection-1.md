No.II(1)AG/30/2012
[AGRICULTURE – OIL PALM – PERMISSION TO TITLE CHANGE OF THE COMPANY FROM “CAUVERY PALM OIL
LIMITED” TO “GODREJ AGROVET LIMITED” IN THE MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING (MOU) – ORDERS –
ISSUED.]
The following Government order is published:—
[G.O.(Ms) No.238, Agriculture (OS), 19th October 2012.]
READ:
1.  G.O. (Ms) No. 29, Agriculture (OS) Department, dated 24-01-2008.
2.  From the Commissioner of Agriculture Letter No. OPC1/67824/2012, dated 10-07-2012 and 30-07-2012.
ORDER: No. 238, Agriculture (OS) 19th October 2012.
In the G.O. first read above permission was accorded to M/s. Cauvery Palm Oil Limited to continue the activities of
Oil Palm Processing Unit at Cuddalore and Villupuram Districts permanently and the Director of Agriculture was permitted
to execute the memorandum of understanding with the above firm.  Accordingly, the Director of Agriculture has executed
a Memorandum of understanding with the Cauvery Palm Oil Limited for the promotion of Oil Palm crop cultivation and
to set up an Oil Palm extraction Mill in Cuddalore and Villupuram Districts.
2. Now, the Commissioner of Agriculture in his letter second read above, has informed that the Cauvery Palm Oil
Limited has been merged/amalgamated with the Company “Godrej Agrovet Limited” as per the provision of the Sections
391 to 394 read with sections 78 and 100 to 103 and other applicable provisions of the Companies Act 1956 and it has
been sanctioned by the Hon’ble Bombay High Court vide its order dated 16-03-2012.
3. The Commissioner of Agriculture has therefore requested the Government to sanction necessary permission for
Title change of the company in the memorandum  of understanding from the existing “Cauvery Palm Oil Limited” to “Godrej
Agrovet Limited”.  He has also stated that on receipt of permission, the Memorandum of understanding already executed
has to be redrafted in the name of Godrej Agrovet Limited.
4. The Commissioner of Agriculture has further informed that as per clause 29 of the Memorandum of Understanding
dated 28-02-2008 the Memorandum of Understanding may be altered, amended or dropped by mutual consent of the
parties and the amended or dropped by mutual consent of the parties and the Additional Government Pleader has opined
that the change of name from Cauvery Palm Oil Limited to Godrej Agrovet Limited in the memorandum of understanding
dated 28-02-2008 is permissible.
5. After careful examination, the Government accept the request of Commissioner of  Agriculture and permission is
granted for Title change of the company from the existing “Cauvery Palm Oil Limited”  to Godrej Agrovet Limited” in the
Memorandum of understanding already executed with Cauvery Palm Oil Limited on 28-02-2008.  The Government also
direct the Commissioner of Agriculture to send a copy of the re-drafted memorandum of understanding in the name of
Godrej Argovet Limited.
(BY ORDER OF THE GOVERNOR)
SANDEEP SAXENA,
Agricultural Production Commissioner
and Secretary to Government.
