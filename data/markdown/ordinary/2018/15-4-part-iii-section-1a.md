No. SRO A-15/2018.— In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) read with clause (xiii) of sub-section (2) 
of Section 116 of the Tamil Nadu Hindu Religious and Charitable Endowments Act, 1959 (Tamil Nadu Act 22 of 1959), the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Religious Institutions (Lease of Immovable Property) 
Rules,1963:- 
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in rule 5, in clause (viii), for the expression, “other objectionable trade or business.”, the expression 
“other objectionable trade or business contrary to the Hindu religious sentiments” shall be substituted. 
EXPLANATORY NOTE
The Religious Institutions (Lease of Immovable Property) Rules, 1963 prescribe conditions for lease of properties and 
rights of religious institutions. Rule 5 of the said Rules prescribed the particulars to be furnished in the auction notice. Since 
the conditions for Lease were prescribed long ago, amendments have been issued with G.O.(Ms).No.155, Tamil Development, 
Culture and Religious Endowments Department, dated 28-07-2006 clause (viii) of rule 5 of the said Rules, as amended by 
the said Government Order provides that the shops and buildings shall not be leased out to run liquor shops, mutton shops 
or any other objectionable trade or business. 
2.  In the Eleventh report of the Committee on Delegated Legislation (Thirteenth Assembly), the Committee has suggested 
to consider, whether the expression, “contrary to the Hindu Religious Sentiments”, be included after the term, "objectionable 
trade or business” in the above said clause. The said suggestion of the Committee has been considered by the Government 
and it is decided to amend the existing rule accordingly. 
3.  The Notiﬁ cation is intended to give effect to the above decision.
