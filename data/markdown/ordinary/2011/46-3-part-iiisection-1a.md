ENERGY  DEPARTMENT
 Amendment to the Regulations for the Grant of Certificates of Competency to Wireman and Supervisors and Power
Generating Station Operators and Licences to the Electrical Contractors.
[G.O. Ms. No. 96, Energy (B1), 10th November 2011.]
No. SRO A-21/2011.—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-rule (1) of rule 45 of the Indian Electricity Rules,
1956, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Regulations for the grant of Certificates of
Competency to Wireman Helper, Wireman, Supervisors, Power Generating Station Operator and Licences to Electrical
Contractors published with the Energy Department Notification No. SRO A-249/87 at pages 719-730 of Part III—Section 1(a)
of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 4th November 1987, as subsequently amended.
AMENDMENT
In the said Regulations, in regulation 6, in clause (3), in sub-clause (a), item (iii) shall be omitted.
RAMESH KUMAR KHANNA,
Principal Secretary to Government.
