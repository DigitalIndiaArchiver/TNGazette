Amendments to Adhoc Rules relating to the post of Multipurpose Health Workers (Male) and Multipurpose

[G.O. Ms. No. 60, Health and Family Welfare (L1), 18th February 2022, 
Maasi 6, Pilava, Thiruvalluvar Aandu–2053.]
No. SRO B-14/2022.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, 
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Rules issued with the erstwhile Health, Indian 
Medicine and Homeopathy and Family Welfare Department Notiﬁ cation No.SRO B-301/89, published at pages 208-209 of 
Part III- Section 1 (b) of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 6th September 1989, as subsequently amended.
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖග 
In the said Rules, for the expression "Multi Purpose Health Worker (Male)" wherever it occurs the expression "Health 
Inspector, Grade-II" shall be substituted.

[G.O. Ms. No. 60, Health and Family Welfare (L1), 18th February 2022, 
Maasi 6, Pilava, Thiruvalluvar Aandu–2053.]
No. SRO B-15/2022.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Rules published with the erstwhile Health, Indian 
Medicine and Homeopathy and Family Welfare Department, Notiﬁ cation SRO B-303/89, published at pages 209-210 of 
Part III - Section 1 (b) of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 6th September 1989, as subsequently amended. 
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖග

Inspector,Grade-I" shall be substituted.
