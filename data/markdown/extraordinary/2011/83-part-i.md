APPOINTMENT OF Dr. ADISH C. AGGARWALA, SENIOR ADVOCATE AS ADDITIONAL
ADVOCATE GENERAL-VI OF TAMIL NADU AT HIGH COURT OF MADRAS.
[G.O. Ms. No. 202, Public (Law Officers), 1st  March 2011.]
No. I/PULO/6(a)/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Article 165 of  the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints Dr. Adish C. Aggarwala, Senior Advocate as
Additional Advocate General-VI of Tamil Nadu from the date of his assuming office.
2. The Additional Advocate General-VI of Tamil Nadu will look after the cases
pertaining to the State of Tamil Nadu before the Supreme Court of India, New Delhi.
S. KARUTHIAH PANDIAN,
Secretary to Government.
