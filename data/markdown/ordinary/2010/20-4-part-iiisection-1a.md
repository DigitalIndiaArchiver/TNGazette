HOME  DEPARTMENT
Draft Amendment to the Tamil Nadu Motor Vehicles Rules, 1989
[G.O. Ms. No. 437, Home (Transport.VII), 6th May 2010.]
No. SRO A-17/2010.—The following draft of an amendment to the Tamil Nadu Motor Vehicles Rules, 1989, which it
is proposed to be made in exercise of the powers conferred by Section 95 of the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988
(Central Act 59 of 1988), is hereby published for the information of all persons likely to be affected thereby, as required
by sub-section (1) of Section 212 of the said Act.
2. Notice is hereby given that the draft amendment will be taken into consideration on or after the expiry of the period
of thirty days from the date of publication of this Notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette and that any
objection and suggestion, which may be received from any person with respect thereto before the expiry of the aforesaid
period will be considered by the Government of Tamil Nadu.
3. Objection and suggestion, if any, should be addressed in duplicate to the Principal Secretary to Government,
Home Department, Secretariat, Chennai-600 009.
DRAFT AMENDMENT
