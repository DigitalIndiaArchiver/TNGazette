ENERGY  DEPARTMENT
AUTHORISATION OF THE TAMIL NADU ELECTRICITY BOARD TO CONTINUE TO FUNCTION
AS THE STATE TRANSMISSION UTILITY AND A LICENSEE UNDER THE ELECTRICITY ACT, 2003.
[G.O. Ms. No. 57, Energy (B2),15th July 2009.]
No. II(2)/EGY/358(b)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to clause (a) of Section 172 of the Electricity Act, 2003
(Central Act 36 of 2003), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby authorises the Tamil Nadu Electricity Board
to continue to function as the State Transmission Utility and a licensee under the provisions  of the said
Act for a further period upto 15th December 2009 beyond 15th June 2009, as mutually decided  by the
Central Government and this Government.
P.W.C. DAVIDAR,
 Secretary to Government.
