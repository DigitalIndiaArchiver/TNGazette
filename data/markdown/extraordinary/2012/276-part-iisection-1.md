No. II(1)/LAS/21(c)/2012.
“In exercise of the powers conferred upon me by clause (1) of Article 174 of the Constitution
of India and in supersession of the notification issued in S.O. Ms. No. 145, Legislative Assembly
Secretariat, dated the 24th September  2012, and published in an extraordinary issue of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the  24th September  2012, I, Dr. K. ROSAIAH, Governor of
Tamil Nadu, hereby summon the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly to meet at 10.00 A.M. on
Wednesday, the 10th October, 2012 in the Assembly Chamber, Secretariat, Chennai-600 009,
instead of  at 11.00 A.M. on Tuesday, the 30th October, 2012 as already notified.
Raj Bhavan,
K. ROSAIAH,
Guindy,
Governor of Tamil Nadu.
Chennai-600 022,
Dated: 29th September, 2012.
A.M.P. JAMALUDEEN,
Secretary”.
