Election of one Member to the Syndicate by the Senate from among its Members
(No.V.1/Gazette-Res/11-2021/453.)
No.VI-3(b)/1/2022.
It is hereby declare that Dr. B. Venugopal, Professor and Head, Department of Legal Studies, University of Madras, 
Chepauk Campus, Chennai-600 005, has been duly elected to the Syndicate by the Senate from among its members 
under Section 18-1[a] - Other Members [1] of the Madras university Act, 1923, for the residual period up to 30-03-2024 
of till the date of cessation of his membership in the Senate / Academic Council whichever is earlier.
Centenary Building, 
Dr. N. MATHIVANAN,
Chepauk, Chennai-600 005, 
Registrar (In-charge).
