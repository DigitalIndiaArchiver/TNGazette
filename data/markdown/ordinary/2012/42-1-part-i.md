No. I/PUSP/35/2012.—The following Notification of the
Government of India, Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances and
Pensions, Department of Personnel and Training New Delhi, dated
4th October 2012 is republished:—
In exercise of the powers conferred by Rule 6(1) of the Indian
Administrative Service (Cadre) Rules, 1954, and with the
concurrence of the Government of Tamil Nadu and Ministry of
Home Affairs, the Central Government has approved extension of
inter cadre deputation of Shri R. Lalvena, IAS (TN:01) from
Tamil Nadu cadre to AGMUT cadre (Mizoram segment) for a period
of two years beyond 10th August 2012.
