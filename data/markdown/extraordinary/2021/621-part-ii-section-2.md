In exercise of the powers conferred by section 50-A of the Coimbatore City Municipal Corporation Act, 
1981 (Tamil Nadu Act 25 of 1981) read with section 8 of the Kumbakonam City Municipal Corporation 
Ordinance, 2021 (Tamil Nadu Ordinance 7 of 2021), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that 
the powers exercisable by the Government under sub-sections (4), (5) and (6) of section 5 of the said 
Tamil Nadu Ordinance 7 of 2021 and section 51 of the said Tamil Nadu Act 25 of 1981, in respect of the 
Corporation of Kumbakonam, be exercisable by the Commissioner, Corporation of Kumbakonam. 
 
 
 
 
SHIV DAS MEENA, 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Additional Chief Secretary to Government.
