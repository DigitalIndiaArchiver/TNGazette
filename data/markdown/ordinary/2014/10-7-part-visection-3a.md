GENERAL NOTIFICATION
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI-600 001.
Taking over of Anjumane Islamia Jamia Masjid Wakf, Pettai, Namakkal District
under the direct management.
(Rc. No. 14879/07/B2/NMKL.)
No. VI-3(a)/23/2014.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995 as amended by
Act 27 of 2013,  the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in item No. 122/13 in Rc.No. 14879/07/B2/NMKL,
dated 31st December 2013 has ordered to take over the Anjumane Islamia Jamia Masjid Wakf, Pettai,
Namakkal District under the direct Management of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of one year from the date
of publication and to appoint the Wakf Superintendent, Salem as its Executive Officer.
F. ABDUL RAZICK,
Chennai-600 001,
Chief Executive Officer,
17th February 2014.
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
