HIGHWAYS AND MINOR PORTS DEPARTMENT
AMENDMENT TO THE TAMIL NADU HIGHWAYS RULES, 2003.
[G.O. Ms. No. 100, Highways and Minor Ports (HF1), 1st September 2014.]
No. SRO A-16(b)/2014.
In exercise of powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 66 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001
(Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the
Tamil Nadu Highways Rules, 2003.
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in rule 5, wherever the expression “the Government, or the Collector, or the Special
District Collector (Land Acquisition), Tamil Nadu Urban Development Project-III” occurs the expression “the
Government or the Collector or the Special District Collector (Land Acquisition), Tamil Nadu Urban Development
Project-III or the Special District Revenue Officer (Land Acquisition) shall be substituted.
RAJEEV RANJAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
