of 2006), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Commercial Taxes and Registration 
Department Notiﬁ cation No.II(2)/CTR/369(b)/2020, published at page 1 of Part II-Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government 
Gazette, Extraordinary, dated the 9th June, 2020, namely:-
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖගඛ.
In the said Notiﬁ cation,- 
(1) in the ﬁ rst paragraph,-
(a) for the expression “29th day of June, 2020”, the expression “30th day of August, 2020” shall be substituted; 
(b) for the expression “30th day of June, 2020”, the expression “31st  day of August, 2020” shall be substituted; and
(2) in the second paragraph, for the expression “30th day of June, 2020”, the expression “31st day of August, 2020” 
shall be substituted.
