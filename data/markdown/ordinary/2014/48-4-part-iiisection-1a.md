In the G.O. first read above, orders were issued, by defined the duties and responsibilities of the Panchayat Secretary
to assist the execution authority in all the tasks to be performed by the Village Panchayat. In the G.O. second read
above, orders were issued sanctioning 180 days of maternity leave to female Government Employees. In the G.O, third
read above separate service Rules were framed for the post of Panchayat Secretaries.
2. In the letter fourth read above, the Director of Rural Development and Panchayat Raj has requested the Government
that to issue an amendment to rule 12(2) of G.O. third read above, by enhancing the maternity leave to female Panchayat
Secretaries from 90 days to 180 days as in the case of Female Government Servants.
3. The Government after careful examination of the proposal of the Director of Rural Development and Panchayat Raj
and direct that the maternity leave admissible to married women Panchayat Secretaries which is 90 days in the G.O. third
read above at present be enhanced to 180 days with salary which may be spread over from the pre-confinement rest
to post-confinement recuperation at the option of the women Panchayat Secretaries. The maternity leave will be admissible
to married Female Panchayat Secretaries with less than two surviving Children.
4. Necessary amendment to Tamil Nadu Village Panchayat Secretaries (Conditions of Service) Rules 2013 will be
issued separately.
(By order of the Governor)
N.S. PALANIAPPAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
