Variation to the Approved Dharmapuri Detailed Development Plan No. 5 of Dharmapuri Local Planning Area.
(Roc. No. 21040/2010/DP1.)
No. VI(1)/205/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (1) of Section 33 of Town and Country Planning Act, 1971,
(Act No. 35 of 1972) the Director of Town and Country Planning, in the proceedings Roc. No. 21040/2010/DP1, dated
21st April 2011 proposes to make the following variation to the approved Dharmapuri Detailed Development
Plan No. 5 of Dhamapuri Local Planning Area approved by the Director of Town and Country Planning in proceedings
Roc. No. 46831/97/DP3, dated 3rd March 2000. The fact of this approval in Form No. 12, published in
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No. 35, Part VIвҖ”Section 1, page No. 543 dated 12th September 2001, (Publication
No. VI(1)/540/2001).
2. Any person affected or interested in this draft variation may within SIXTY days from the date of publication of
this notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, can represent in person or submit in writing to the
Member-Secretary, Dharmapuri Local Planning Authority/the Commissioner, Dharmapuri Municipality any objections and
suggestions relating thereto.
3. The variation with plan may be inspected at free of cost at any time during office hours at the above said Local
Planning Authority office.
VARIATION
1.
The entire Dharmapuri Detailed Development Plan No. 5 of Dhamapuri Local Planning Area is comprehensively
varied by the Director of Town and Country Planning vide Proceedings Roc. No. 21040/2010/DP1, dated
21st April 2011.
2.
The plan thus varied is numbered as Map No. 5 DDP(V)/DTCP No. 5/2011.
3.
The detailed Development Clauses and Schedules annexed with this plan are made enforceable from the
date of publication of the confirmed notification to be issued under Section 33 (2) of the Act and published
in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
Chennai-600 002,
PANKAJ KUMAR BANSAL,
21st April 2011.
