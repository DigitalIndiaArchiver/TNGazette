TAMIL NADU STATE LEGAL SERVICES AUTHORITY, CHENNAI
Appointment of Subordinate Judge, Perambalur as Secretary,
Perambalur District Legal Services Authority
(TNSLSA No. 6924/E/2010.)
No. VI(2)/540/2010.
Under the powers vested under Section 9(3) of the Legal Services Authorities Act, 1987 (Central Act 39 of 1987) as
amended, the Tamil Nadu State Legal Services Authority appoints the Subordinate Judge, Perambalur to be the Secretary
of the Perambalur District Legal Services Authority to exercise such powers and perform such duties under the Chairman
of the District Legal Services Authority, Perambalur as may be assigned to him by the Chairman.
Chennai-600 104,
B. GOKULDAS,
1st December 2010.
Member-Secretary.
GOVERNMENT  EXAMINATIONS / EDUCATIONAL  OFFICERS
LOSS OF CERTIFICATES
It is hereby notified that the Original Higher Secondary Certificates / Matriculation Certificates / Diploma Certificates/
School Certificates of the following pupils are reported to have been lost beyond recovery. Necessary steps are being
taken to issue the duplicate of the said Certificates. If the Originals were to be found by anybody, they should be
treated as invalid and sent to the Secretary, Board of Higher Secondary Examination / Directorate of Government
Examinations, Chennai-6 / Director of Technical Education, Chennai-25 / Inspector / Inspectress of Matriculation and
Anglo-Indian Schools as the case may be for cancellation:—
