AMENDMENT TO NOTIFICATION
No. II(2)/LE/934 (a-1)/2913,
 In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 42 of the Building and Other Construction Workers
(Regulation of Employment and Conditions of Service) Act, 1996 (Central Act 27 of 1996), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
makes the following amendment to the Labour and Employment Department Notification No. II(2)/LE/161/2010, published on page
129 of Part II-Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 24th March 2010, as subsequently amended:-
AMENDMENT
In the said Notification, for the expression “Additional Chief Inspector of Factories”, the expression “Director of Industrial
Safety and Health” shall be substituted.
