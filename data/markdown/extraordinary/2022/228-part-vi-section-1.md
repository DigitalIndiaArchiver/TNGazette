NOTICE UNDER SECTION 3(1) OF TAMIL NADU ACQUISITION OF LAND FOR INDUSTRIAL PURPOSES ACT, 1997 READ 
WITH THE TAMIL NADU LAND ACQUISITION LAWS (REVIVAL OF OPERATION, AMENDMENT AND VALIDATION) 
ACT, 2019. 
No. VI(1)/173(b)/2022.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that the lands specified in the Schedule below have to be acquired 
for industrial purposes to wit for the formation of  New Broad Gauge Railway Line between Tindivanam and  Nagari and 
having already been decided that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands are to be paid out of the funds 
managed by the Deputy Chief Engineer (Buildings), Southern Railways, Tambaram. The following notice is issued under 
sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial purposes Act, 1997 
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands specified in the Schedule below and measuring 
8215 sq.mtrs to the same, a little more or less needed for industrial purposes, to wit, for the formation of New Broad Gauge 
Railway Line between Tindivanam and Nagari in Meesarakandapuram Village, R.K.Pet Taluk of Tiruvallur District.
The plans of the lands are kept in the office of the Land Acquisition Officer / District Revenue Officer, Tiruvallur and in the 
office of the Special Tahsildar (L.A), Tindivanam Nagari New BG, Railway Line, Tiruttani, may be inspected at any time during 
office hours.
