LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY SECRETARIAT
VACATION OF SEAT IN TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY DUE TO RESIGNATION OF
THIRU S. RAMACHANDRAN, MEMBER OF THE TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY.
[Letter No. 15448/2013-1, TNLAS (B-III), 10th December 2013]
No. II(1)/LAS/24(c)/2013.
Whereas, Thiru S. Ramachandran, a Member of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly representing the
28. Alandur Assembly Constituency, in his letter dated 10th December, 2013 addressed to me as Speaker
of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly, has resigned his seat as Member of the said Assembly with
effect from 10th December 2013.
Now, therefore, I, P. DHANAPAL, Speaker of the said Assembly hereby accept the resignation of
Thiru S. Ramachandran, as envisaged under Rule 22 of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Rules as
it is in accordance with Rule 21 of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Rules. Under Article 190 (3)(b)
of the Constitution of India, his seat has, therefore, become vacant with effect from the above date.
Secretariat,
P. DHANAPAL,
Chennai-600 009,
Speaker,
Dated 10th December 2013.
Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly.
A.M.P. JAMALUDEEN,
Secretary,
  Legislative Assembly Secretariat.
