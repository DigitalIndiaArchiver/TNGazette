NOTIFICATION UNDER THE TAMIL NADU GOODS AND SERVICES TAX ACT, 2017.
[G.O. Ms. No. 113, Commercial Taxes and Registration (B1), 21st September 2017, 
Purattasi 5, Hevilambi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2048.] 
No. II(2)/CTR/793(c-3)/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of section 11 of the Tamil Nadu Goods and Services Tax Act, 
2017 (Tamil Nadu Act 19 of 2017), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, on being satisﬁ ed that it is necessary in the public interest 
so to do, on the recommendations of the Council, hereby exempts intra-State supply of heavy water and nuclear fuels falling 
in Chapter 28 of the First Schedule to the Customs Tariff Act, 1975 (Central Act 51 of 1975) by the Department of Atomic 
Energy to the Nuclear Power Corporation of India Ltd from the whole of the state tax leviable thereon under section 9 of the 
Tamil Nadu Goods and Services Tax Act, 2017 (Tamil Nadu Act 19 of 2017).
2. This notiﬁ cation shall come into force with effect from the 21st day of September, 2017.
