COMMERCIAL TAXES AND REGISTRATION
DEPARTMENT
Notifications under Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax Act.
Remission of Tax payable on the Second  point of sale of
Indian Made Foreign Liquor purchased from the
Tamil Nadu Marketing Corporation Limited by the retail
wine shop for certain period under the Act.
[G.O. (2D) No. 236, Commercial Taxes and Registration (D2),
16th November 2009, Aippasi 30,
Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. II(2)/CTR/667/2009.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by Section 31 read with sub-section (4) of
Section 88 of the Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax Act, 2006
(Tamil Nadu Act 32 of 2006), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby remits a sum of  Rs. 14,61,896 (Rupees fourteen
lakhs sixty one thousand eight  hundred and ninety six only)
being the tax payable on the second point of sale of Indian
Made Foreign Liquor purchased from the Tamil Nadu Marketing
Corporation Limited by the retail wine shop owners in respect
of the period from the 15th June 1992 to the 11th March
1993 under the said Act.
Remission of difference of Tax, Surcharge and Penalty
payable on the sale of Polyethylene colour granules
(Master Batches) under the  Act.
[G.O. (2D) No. 236, Commercial Taxes and Registration (D2),
16th November 2009, Aippasi 30,
Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. II(2)/CTR/668/2009.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by Section 31 read with sub-section (4) of Section 88
of the Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax Act, 2006 (Tamil Nadu
Act 32 of 2006), and read with sub-section (2) of Section
3 and sub-section (2) of Section 3A, of the erstwhile Tamil
Nadu Sales Tax (Surcharge) Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 24
of 1971), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby remits a sum
of Rs. 37,35,262 (Rupees Thirty seven lakhs thirty five
thousand two hundred and sixty two  only) being the difference
of tax at 2 per cent for the period from 1st April 1993 to
16th July 1996 and consequential surcharge and additional
surcharge and difference of tax at 4 per cent for the period
from 17th July 1996 to 20th July 1998 and penalty payable
on the sale of Polyethylene colour granules (Master Batches)
under the said Acts.
Remission of difference of Tax and Surcharge  payable by
the dealers on the sale of arrack for certain period
under the Act.
[G.O. (2D) No. 236, Commercial Taxes and Registration (D2),
16th November 2009, Aippasi 30,
Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. II(2)/CTR/669/2009.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by Section 31 read with sub-section (4) of Section
88 of the Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax Act, 2006 (Tamil
Nadu Act 32 of 2006), and read with sub-section (2) of
Section 3 of the erstwhile Tamil Nadu Sales Tax (Surcharge)
Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 24 of 1971),  the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby remits the difference of tax between
25 per cent (twenty five per cent) and 6 per cent (six per
cent) and surcharge amounting to Rs. 16,14,079 (Rupees
sixteen lakh fourteen thousand and seventy nine only) payable
by the dealers on the sale of arrack in respect of the period
from the 26th March 1985 to 29th March 1985 under the
said Acts.
Remission of Tax and Surcharge payable on the sale of
fertilizers by Thiruvalargal Food Corporation of India,
Chennai for certain period under the Act.
[G.O. (2D) No. 236, Commercial Taxes and Registration (D2),
16th November 2009, Aippasi 30,
Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. II(2)/CTR/670/2009.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by Section 31 read with sub-section (4) of
Section 88 of the Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax Act, 2006
(Tamil Nadu Act 32 of 2006), and read with sub-section (2)
of Section 3 of the erstwhile Tamil Nadu Sales Tax (Surcharge)
Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 24 of 1971), the Governor of Tamil
Nadu hereby remits a sum of Rs. 42,05,989 (Rupees forty
two lakhs five thousand nine hundred and eighty nine only)
being the tax and surcharge payable on the sale of
fertilizers by Thiruvalargal Food Corporation of India, Chennai
in respect of the years 1969-1970 to 1971-1972 under the
said Acts.
Remission of Penalty Payable on the sale of REP licence
by Thiruvalargal Bilal Hussain and Company, Chennai
for certain period under the Act.
[G.O. (2D) No. 236, Commercial Taxes and Registration (D2),
16th November 2009, Aippasi 30,
Thiruvalluvar  Aandu-2040.]
No. II/(2)CTR/671/2009.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by Section 31 read with sub-section (4) of
Section 88 of the Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax Act, 2006
(Tamil Nadu Act 32 of 2006),  the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby remits a sum of Rs. 24,84,912 (Rupees twenty four
lakhs eighty four thousand nine hundred and twelve only)
being the penalty  payable on the sale of REP licence by
Thiruvalargal Bilal Hussain and Company, Chennai  in respect
of the period 1989-90 and 1990-91 under said the Act.
RAJEEV RANJAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
