GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
Variations to the Approved Master Plan for the Tiruvallur Local Planning Area
(Roc. No. 2762/2009/F1)
No. VI(1)/199/2013
   In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 32 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning Act,
1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972), Government in the order G.O. (2D) Ms. 207, Housing and Urban Development UD 4(1),
Department, dated 14th December 2012 has permitted to change of land use  of a survey numbers making the following
variation to the Master Plan for the Tiruvallur Local Planning Area, approved under the said Act and published in the Housing
and Urban Development Department Notification No. II(2)/HOU/4650/93, dated 6th October 1993 at page No. 1061  of Part
II Section—2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, No. 39, dated 6th October 1993.
VARIATIONS
In the said Master Plan, in the “LAND USE SCHEDULE” of VILLAGE Number: 20, Periyakuppam Village
under the heading:—
I. (a) PRIMARY RESIDENTIAL USE ZONE : (PR.13) Survey Numbers, in the expression “759/2A, 2B,
774/1, 2, 726/2Apt. 726/2B” shall be deleted.
VI. AGRICULTURE USE ZONE: Survey Numbers in the expression “760, 763/1, 2, 765/1, 766, 767/1 to 18,
768, 769, 770/1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6A, 6B, 772, 775, 777, 778, 779/1, 2, 3, 727” shall be deleted.
and under the heading
IV. EDUCATIONAL USE ZONE: and sub-heading E.4 the Survey Numbers in the expression “726/2Apt, 2B
and 727” shall be added before the expression “736 and the expression “759/2A, 2B, 774/1, 2, 760, 763/1,
2, 765/1, 766, 767/1 to 18, 768, 769, 770/1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6A, 6B, 772, 775, 777, 778, 779/1, 2, 3 shall be
added after the expression “737”.
Tiruvallur,
K. PARTHASARATHY,
3rd April 2013.
 Member-Secretary (i/c)/Commissioner (i/c),

JUDICIAL NOTIFICATIONS
Conferment of  Powers
(R.O.C. No. 1521/2013-B6)
No. VI (1)/200/2013.
No. 49/2013—In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 13 of the Code of Criminal Procedure 1973 (Central Act
2 of 1974) the High Court hereby appoints the following 21 Deputy Tahsildars, 8 each in Villupuram and Ramanathapuram,
5 in Kanniyakumari, 8 Tahsildars in Kancheepuram and one Tahsildar Trainee in Tiruvannamalai District to undergo Magisterial
Training for a period of 120/180 days from the date of assumption of charge and confers upon them the ordinary powers
conferrable under this Code on a Judicial Magistrate to try such cases which were earlier triable and dealt with by an
erstwhile Judicial Second Class Magistrate.
