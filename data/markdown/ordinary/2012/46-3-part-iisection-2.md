Declaration of Thattamparai Forest Block Bit-I in Pandalur
(Previously Gudalur Taluk), The Nilgiris District as
Reserved Forest under the Tamil Nadu Forest Act.
[G.O.(Ms) No. 214, Environment and Forests (FR.14),
27th August 2012.]
No. II(2)/EF/790/2012.—In exercise of the powers conferred
by section 16 of the Tamil Nadu Forest Act, 1882 (Tamil
Nadu Act V of 1882), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
declares the  areas, the boundaries of which are specified
in the Schedule below, as Reserved Forest with effect on
and from 6th June 2013.
THE SCHEDULE.
1. Name of the District
: The Nilgiris.
2. Name of the Taluk
: Pandalur (previously
Gudalur Taluk).
3. Number and Name
: No. 3 (previously No. 10)
of the village
Cherangode Village.
4. Name of the Forest Block : Thattamparai Forest
Block Bit-I.
5. Area of the Forest Block
: 5.60 Hectares.
6. Survey Number of the
: (Resurvey Numbers 247/1
Forest Block
and 247/2 (O.S.No.74/B1
Part)
THE BOUNDARIES
North-Starting from the trijunction point of Resurvey
Numbers 242, 241 and 247/1 of Numbers 3, Cherangode
village, the boundary generally runs South-east along the
Southwest side and Resurvey Number 241, Western side of
Resurvey Number 240 and 282 till it meets the tri-junction
of Resurvey Numbers 282, 249 and 247/2 all of said village.
South-Thence the boundary runs towards west wards
along the Northern side of Resurvey Number 249 and 248
till it meets the tri-junction of Resurvey Numbers 248, 246
and 247/2 all of Numbers Cherangode village.
West-Thence, the boundary generally runs towards
Northwest and North wards along the Northeast side of
Resurvey Number 246 and eastern side of Resurvey Numbers
244, 243 and 242 till it meets the starting point.
Appointment of certain Person as Auditors for Tamil Nadu
Pollution Control Board-during the financial year
2012-2013 under the Water (Prevention and control)
Board Act.
 [G.O.(Ms) No. 243, Environment and Forests (EC.1),
31st October 2012.]
No. II(2)/EF/791/2012.—In exercise of the powers conferred
by section (3) of section 40 of the Water (Prevention and
Control of Pollution) Act, 1974 (Central Act 6 of 1974), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu, on the advice of the Comptroller
and Auditor General of India,  hereby appoints Tvl. Velu and
Co. (MD0019), New No. 145/83, Triplicane High Road,
Triplicane, Chennai-600 005, as auditors for auditing the
accounts of the Tamil Nadu Pollution Control Board, Chennai,
for the financial year 2012-13.
C.V. SANKAR,
Principal Secretary to Government.

Award of the Governor’s Medal for Vigilance

[G.O.(3D) No. 8, Personnel and Administrative Reforms (N),

No. II(2)/PLAR/795/2012.—Under the Rules governing the
Governor’s Vigilance Medal issued in G.O.Ms.No. 249,
Personnel and Administrative Reforms (N) Department, dated
25-2-1977, as amended subsequently the Governor of Tamil
Nadu is pleased to award the Governor’s Vigilance. Medal
for the year 2010 to the following three Investigating Officers
of the Directorate of Vigilance and Anti-Corruption for their
meritorious service and the names of those officers are
hereby published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette
as required under rule 15 of the said Rules:—
(i)
Thiru S. Gangadharan, Inspector of Police Vigilance
and Anti-corruption, Kancheepuram.
(ii) Thiru P. Pandithurai, Inspector of Police Vigilance and
Anti-corruption, Tiruchirappalli.
(iii) Thiru A. Surakumaran, Inspector of Police Vigilance
and Anti-corruption, Tiruchirappalli.
