HIGHWAYS AND MINOR PORTS DEPARTMENT
Acquisition of Lands
[G.O. (D). No. 4, Highways and Minor Ports (HP-2), 9th
January 2013, Margazhi 25, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2043.]
No. II(2)/HWMP/160/2013.вҖ”The Governor of Tamil Nadu
having  been satisfied that the lands specified in the Schedule
below have to be acquired for Highways purpose  for the
Construction of Road over Bridge in Municipal Road (No.1,
Trichy Road) of Thanjavur Town in lieu of Level Crossing
No.304 at Railway Km. 354/4-5 inbetween  Thanjavur  Railway
Station and Railway Yard and it having already been decided
that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for
the  lands is to be  paid out of the funds controlled and
managed by Divisional  Engineer (NABARD  and Rural
Roads), Highways Department, Thanjavur after considering
the cause shown by  the owner  (or) other person having
interest on such land as the case may be. The following
notice is issued under Sub-Section (1) of Section 15 of the
Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001. (Tamil Nadu  Act 34 of
2002).
NOTICE
Under Sub-Section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu
Highways Act, 2001. (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu  hereby acquires the  Ryot manai
lands specified in the Schedule below and measuring to an
extent of 0.94.21 hectares  to the same, a little more or less
needed  for the Construction of Road  Over Bridge in Municipal
Road (No.1, Trichy Road) of Thanjavur Town in lieu of existing
Level Crossing No. 304 at Railway Km 354/4-5, inbetween
Thanjavur Railway Station and Railway Yard in Puthupattinam
Village, Thanjavur   Taluk and  District.
The plan of the  lands is kept in the office of the Tahsildar
Thanjavur Taluk and   District and may be inspected  at any
time during office hours.

Extra Wages:
Dhoti.- Extra wages shall be paid for the extra work. The
rate of extra wages vary from 5% to 50% or even more on the
basic wages depending upon the size of the pettu border
design.
Sarees.- Extra wages shall be paid for the extra work. The

Explanations.- (1) Dearness Allowances.- In addition to
the minimum wages fixed above, dearness allowance shall
be paid as indicated below:-
(i) Dearness allowance shall be paid at the rate of
10% of the basic wages plus extra wages linked with the
productivity from the date of publication of this Notification in
the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
(ii) Additional increase of 5% on the prevailing basic
wages plus extra wages shall be grated every year on the
1st April till the next revision.
(2) Where the nature of work is the same no distinction in
the payment of wages shall be made between men and women
employees.
(3) Wherever the existing wages are higher than the
minimum wages fixed herein, the same shall be continued to
be paid.
