Under clause (d) of section 2 of the Tamil Nadu State Minorities Commission Act, 2010 
(Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 2010), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby recognises the persons belonging 
to the following minority communities and are natives of Tamil Nadu, as 'Religious Minorities' for the 
purposes of that Act, namely:-
(1) Christians;
(2) Muslims;
(3) Jains;
(4) Sikhs; and
(5) Buddhists.

Under clause (d) of section 2 of the Tamil Nadu State Minorities Commission Act, 2010 
(Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 2010), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby recognises the persons 
belonging to the minority communities and are natives of Tamil Nadu with the following languages 
as their mother tongue, as 'Linguistic Minorities' for the purposes of that Act, namely:-
(1) Telugu;
(2) Urdu;
(3) Kannada;
(4) Malayalam;
(5) Sourashtra; and
(6) Marathi
