5. Reservation of appointment.-The rule relating to reservation of appointments as provided in rule 22 of the General Rules 
for the Tamil Nadu State and Subordinate Services shall apply for appointment to the post of Junior Architect by direct recruitment;
Provided that on and from the 14th September 2016, Section 27 of the Tamil Nadu Government Servants 
(Conditions of Service) Act, 2016 (Tamil Nadu Act 14 of 2016) shall be applicable of  selection for appointment to the said post in 
the matter of reservation.
6. Probation.- Every person appointed to the post of Jumor Architect by direct recruitment from the date on which he joins 
duty, be on probation for a total period of two years on duty within a continuous period of three years.
7. Tests.- Every person appointed as Junior Architect by direct recruitment shall, within the period of his probation pass 
theTamil Nadu Government Oﬃ  ce Manual Test and the Account Test for Subordinate Oﬃ  cers, Part-I. He shall not be liable to be 
discharged for failure to pass that said tests within the period of probation, but his probation shall be extended up to a maximum 
period of ﬁ ve years and his second and subsequent increments shall be stopped without cumulative eﬀ ect till he passes the said 
tests. if he does not pass the said tests even within the maximum period of ﬁ ve years, his probation shall be terminated.
8. Preparation of annual list of approved candidates.- The crucial date on which a person should be qualiﬁ ed for selection 
for appointment to the categories by promotion, shall be the 1st September of every year.
9. Postings and transfers.-The Chief Architect shall be the competent authority to issue order of postings and transfers of 
Assistant Architects and Junior Architects.
10. Savings.-Nothing contained in these rules shall adversely aﬀ ect the service of any person holding the post on the date 
of publication of these rules in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
