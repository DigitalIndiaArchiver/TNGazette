Whereas, in the Notification issued with the S.O. first and second read above, this Commission directed that all the
contesting candidates in the elections listed therein shall lodge a true copy of their election expenses kept by them or by their
respective election agent  under sub-rule (1) of Rule 120 of the Tamil Nadu  Panchayats (Elections) Rules, 1995, in the format
prescribed therefor under sub-rule (2) of rule 120 of the said Rules with the Oficers mentioned therein, within one month from
the date of declaration of the result of the elections;
2. Whereas, this Commission conducted Casual election to Rural Local Bodies during February 2009;
3. Whereas, the District Election Officer/District Collector, Ariyalur District, in his letter/s third read above reported that
Thiru D. Manimaran, contested  in the said Casual Election for the Office of Panchayat Union Ward  Member (Ward  No. 12)
of Ariyalur Panchayat Union, Ariyalur District, failed to lodge copies of his account of election expenses as directed by this
Commission;

4. Wheareas, based on the report of the said District Election Officer/District Collector,  a show cause notice was issued
in the reference fourth read above to the said contested candidate who failed to lodge a true copy of the account of election
expenses in the Casual Election held during the month of February 2009, through the District Election Officer/District
Collector,Ariyalur District calling for explanation within 20 days of the receipt of the said Notice, as to why action should not be
taken against him as per sub-section (4) of Section 37 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994);
5. Whereas, the said District Election Officer/District Collector, Ariyalur District in his letter fifth read above, has now reported
that out of the three contested candidates, Thiru D. Manimaran, has neither submitted explanations  nor lodged the account of
election expenses inspite of the show cause notice issued to him; and
6. Whereas, in view of the said report of the District Election Officer/District Collector, Ariyalur District, the Tamil Nadu State
Election Commission is satisfied that the  one candidate Thiru. D. Manimaran, son of Durairaj, Anna Nagar, Namakkal Post,
Ariyalur Taluk, Ariyalur District, has failed to lodge an account of election expenses as required by or under the said Act and
Rules and have no good reason or justifiation for such failure.
7. Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 37 of the Tamil Nadu
Panchayats  Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994),  the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission hereby declares that the
candidate Thiru D. Manimaran, son of  Durairaj, Anna Nagar, Namakkal Post, Ariyalur Taluk, Ariyalur District,  shall be
disqualified, for being elected for a period of three years from the date of this Order.
