TAMIL NADU STATE ELECTION COMMISSION, CHENNAI-600 106.
[ELECTIONS – ORDINARY ELECTIONS –  URBAN LOCAL BODIES – APPOINTMENT OF POLLING AGENTS – 
ORDERS – ISSUED.]
 (S.O.No.6/2022/TNSEC/ME1, dated the 29th January 2022.)
Read:
(S.O.No.5/2022/TNSEC/ME1, dated the 26th January 2022.)
No. VI(2)/13(c)/2022.
WHEREAS, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission has notified the schedule for the conduct of urban local 
body elections as per Statutory Order read above.
WHEREAS, since there is no provision in Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Municipalities and Corporations (Elections) 
Rules, 2006 for affixing the photo of the Polling Agents in their appointment letter in Form 11, and  	
WHEREAS, it is felt expedient to emphasise on the production of photo identity proof as evidence by the Polling 
Agent at the time of entry into the polling station for the ensuing Ordinary Elections to Urban Local Bodies as a measure 
to curtail impersonation;	
NOW, THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (1) of section 43-B of the
Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920), sub-section (1) of section 6-I of the Chennai 
City Municipal Corporation Act, 1919 (Tamil Nadu Act IV of 1919), sub-section (1) of section 10-D of the Madurai City 
Municipal Corporation Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act XV of 1971), sub-section (1) of section 10-D of the Coimbatore City 
Municipal Corporation Act, 1981 (Tamil Nadu Act 25 of 1981) read with section 8 of the Tiruchirappalli City Municipal 
Corporation Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 1994), section 8 of the Tirunelveli City Municipal Corporation Act, 1994 
(Tamil Nadu Act 28 of 1994), section 8 of the Salem City Municipal Corporation Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 29 of 1994), 
section 8 of the Tiruppur City Municipal Corporation Act, 2008 (Tamil Nadu Act 7 of 2008), section 8 of the Erode 
City Municipal Corporation Act, 2008 (Tamil Nadu Act 8 of 2008), section 8 of the Vellore City Municipal Corporation 
Act, 2008 (Tamil Nadu Act 26 of 2008), section 8 of the Thoothukkudi City Municipal Corporation Act, 2008 
(Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 2008), section 8 of the Thanjavur City Municipal Corporation Act, 2013 (Tamil Nadu Act 24 of 2013), 
section 8 of the Dindigul City Municipal Corporation Act, 2013 (Tamil Nadu Act 25 of 2013), section 8 of the Hosur City 
Municipal Corporation Act, 2019 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 2019), section 8 of the Nagercoil City Municipal Corporation Act, 2019

(Tamil Nadu Act 11 of 2019), section 8 of the Avadi City Municipal Corporation Act, 2019 (Tamil Nadu Act 24 of 2019), 
section 8 of the Cuddalore City Municipal Corporation Ordinance, 2021 (Tamil Nadu Ordinance 1 of 2021), section 8 of the 
Kancheepuram City Municipal Corporation Ordinance, 2021 (Tamil Nadu Ordinance 2 of 2021), section 8 of the Sivakasi 
City Municipal Corporation Ordinance, 2021 (Tamil Nadu Ordinance 3 of 2021), section 8 of the Karur City Municipal 
Corporation Ordinance, 2021 (Tamil Nadu Ordinance 4 of 2021), section 8 of the Tambaram City Municipal Corporation 
Ordinance, 2021 (Tamil Nadu Ordinance 5 of 2021), section 8 of the Kumbakonam City Municipal Corporation Ordinance, 
2021 (Tamil Nadu Ordinance 7 of 2021) and rule 38 and 139 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Municipalities and 
Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission specifies that the Polling Agents (including 
the relief agents) shall show any one of the twelve documentary evidences in original as prescribed for the electors in 
S.O.No.100/2019/TNSEC/EG2, dated 11.12.2019 and Circular No.C-10/2022 dated 20.01.2022 as proof of his/her identity 
to the Presiding Officer and Police Personnel at the time of entry into the Polling Station. The documentary evidence 
shown by him must have his/her photo identity proof of it. Further, the Commission specifies that every Contesting 
Candidate or his/her Election Agent shall appoint one Polling Agent for each Polling Station and only one Relief Agent 
to act as the Polling Agent of such candidate at each Polling Station in the ensuing Ordinary Elections to Urban Local 
Bodies and any one of them shall be present in the Polling Station at a time.
	
The above identity proof has been prescribed only for the purpose of verifying the identity of the person 
appointed as Polling Agent and not for any other purpose of scrutinizing other things.
