ENVIRONMENT AND FORESTS DEPARTMENT
DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF THE TAMIL NADU FORESTS (AMENDMENT) ACT, 1998.
[G.O. Ms. No. 139,  Environment and Forests (FR4), 27th November 2008.]
No. II(2)/EF/32(a)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Forest (Amendment)
Act, 1998 (Tamil Nadu Act 33 of 2002), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the
27th day of November 2008 as the date on which the said Act shall come in to force.
N. SUNDARADEVAN,
 Principal Secretary to Gover nment.
