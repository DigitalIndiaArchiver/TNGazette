NOTICE UNDER SECTION 3(1) OF TAMIL NADU ACQUISITION OF LAND FOR INDUSTRIAL PURPOSES ACT, 1997 
READ WITH THE TAMIL NADU LAND ACQUISITION LAWS (REVIVAL OF OPERATION, AMENDMENT, 
AND  VALIDATION)  ACT,  2019
No. VI(1)/17(a)/2022.
The Government of Tamil Nadu has been satisﬁed that the lands speciﬁed in the Schedule below have to be 
acquired for industrial purposes, for the expansion of Mine-IA-Neyveli Lignite Corporation India Limited and it having 
already been decided that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands are to be paid out of the funds 
managed by the Neyveli Lignite Corporation India Limited. The following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 3 
of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act 1997 
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands speciﬁed in the Schedule below and measuring 
3.11.0 hect. to the same, a little more or less needed for industrial purposes, to wit, for the Expansion of  Mine IA-Neyveli Lignite 
Corporation India Limited.
The  plans  of  the lands are kept in the O/o Special District  Revenue Officer (LA), Neyveli–2, which  may be 
inspected during office hours.
