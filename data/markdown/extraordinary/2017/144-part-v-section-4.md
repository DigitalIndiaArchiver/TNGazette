ORDER OF THE ELECTION COMMISSION OF INDIA
DISQUALIFICATION OF CANDIDATES CONTESTED FROM 74. VILLUPURAM, 77. ULUNDURPET,
106. GOBICHETTIPALAYAM, 108. UDHAGAMANDALAM, 188. MELUR, 191. MADURAI NORTH,
195. THIRUPARANKUNDRAM, 196. THIRUMANGALAM, 197. USILAMPATTI, 200. BODINAYAKANUR,
220. VASUDEVANALLUR, 225. AMBASAMUDRAM ASSEMBLY CONSTITUENCIES IN THE GENERAL
ELECTIONS TO TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY, 2016 FOR FAILUR TO LODGE ACCOUNTS OF
ELECTION EXPENSES UNDER SECTION 10A OF THE REPRESENTATION OF THE PEOPLE ACT, 1951.
No. SRO G-12/2017.
The following Order of the Election Commission of India, Nirvachan Sadan, Ashoka Road,
New Delhi-110 001, dated 5th May, 2017 [15 Vaisakha, 1939 (Saka)] is re-published:---
No. 76/TN-LA/2016.
Whereas, the Election Commission of India is satisfied that each of the contesting condidate specified
in column 4 of the Table below at the General Election to the Legislative Assembly, 2016 from the State of
Tamil Nadu specified in column 2 and held from the constituency specified in column 3 against his/her name
has failed to lodge the account of his/her election expenses as shown in column 5 of the said Table as
required by the Representation of the People Act, 1951 and the Rules made thereunder;
And whereas, the said condidates have either not furnished any reason or explanation for the said failure
even after due notice by the Election Commission or after considering the representation made by them, if
any the Election Commission is satisfied that they have no good reason or justification for the said failure;
Now, therefore, in pursuance of Section 10A of the Act, the Election Commission hereby declares the
persons specified in column 4 of the Table below to be disqualified for being chosen as and for being a
member of either House of Parliament or the Legislative Assembly or Legistative Council of a State or of Union
Territory for a period of three years from the date of this order:-
