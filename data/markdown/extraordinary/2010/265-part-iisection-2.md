In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (3) of Section 15 of the
Right to Information Act, 2005 (Central Act 22 of 2005), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby appoints Thiru K.S.Sripathi, I.A.S. (Retired) as State Chief Information
Commissioner in the Tamil Nadu Information Commission.
2.
The State Chief Information Commissioner shall hold office for a term of five years
from the date on which he enters upon his office or the date on which he attains the
age of sixty-five years, whichever is earlier.
