LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT DEPARTMENT
Draft Notification regarding Revision of Guaranteed time
rate of wages for Employment in Beedi Rollers in the
Employment in Tobacco (including Beedi Making)
Manufactory under Minimum Wages Act.
[G.O.(2D) No. 6, Labour and Employment (J1),
28th February 2011.]
No. II(2)/LE/581/2012.—The following draft of a Notification
which it is proposed to issue in exercise of the powers
conferred by clause (b) of sub-section (1) of Section 3 and
sub-section (2) of Section 5 read with clause (c) of
sub-section (2) of Section 3 of Minimum wages Act, 1948
(Central Act XI of 1948), is hereby published for information
of all persons likely to be affected thereby as required by
clause (b) of sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the said Act.
2. Notice is hereby given that the draft Notification will
be taken into consideration on or after the expiry of two
months from the date of publication of this Notification in the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette and that any objection or
suggestion, which may be received from any person with
respect thereto before the expiry of the aforesaid period will
be considered by the Government of Tamil Nadu Objection
or suggestion, if any, should be addressed to the Principal
Secretary to Government, Labour and Employment
Department, Secretariat, Chennai-600 009 through the
Commissioner of Labour, Chennai-600 006.
DRAFT NOTIFICATION
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (b) of
sub-section (1) of Section 3 and sub-section (2) of Section
5 read with clause (c) of sub section (2) of Section 3 of
the Minimum wages Act, 1948 (Central Act XI of 1948) and
in supersession of the Labour and Employment Department
Notification No. II(2)/LE/240/2007, Published at page 193 of
Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette,
dated the 9th May 2007, the governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
revises the guaranteed time rate of wages payable to beedi
rollers in the employment in tobacco (including beedi making)
manufactory as follows:—
(i) Where the employer fails to supply sufficient
quantum of good raw materials (including tobacco leaves)
to roll one thousand beedies per day, the employee (beedi-
rollers) shall be entitled to get Rs.24.80 (Rupees twenty four
and paise eight only) as guaranteed time rate of wages.
(ii)  The payment of guaranteed time rate of wages
as aforesaid shall be subject to the following conditions,
namely:-
  (a) that the employee shall not be entitled for
payment of guaranteed time rate of wages, if he does not
fully make use of the raw materials supplied by the employer,
even if it is inadequate to roll one thousand beedies;
      (b) that the employee shall not be entitled to get
the guaranted time rate of wages, if he earns less than the
amount of guaranteed time rate of wages on any day, on
account of his unwillingness to work for any reason whatsoever.
      (c) that the employee shall not be entitled to get
the guaranteed time rate of wages, if the failure of the
employer to supply raw materials is due to tempest, fire, rain,
catastrophe, epidemics, civil commotion or any other similar
casue which is beyond the control of the employer; and
      (d) that the employee who works for more then
one employer shall not be entitled to get the guaranteed time
rate of wages from any one of those employers.
