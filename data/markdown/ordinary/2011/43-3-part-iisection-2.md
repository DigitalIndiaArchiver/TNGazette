[G.O. Ms. No. 720, Home (Courts-IV), 31st October 2011.]
No. II(2)/HO/447/2011.—Under Section 6 of the Indian
Christian Marriage Act, 1872 (Central Act XV of 1872), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu sanctions the issue of a licence to
Pastor. G. Suresh Kumar, Life Changing Church, No. 9A,
"E" Block, MMDA Colony, Arumbakkam, Chennai-106 to
solemnize Marriage within the territories comprising the
State of Tamil Nadu in accordance with the provisions of the
said Act.
NOTIFICATION II
[G.O. Ms. No. 720, Home (Courts-IV), 31st October 2011.]
No. II(2)/HO/448/2011.—Under Section 9 of the Indian
Christian Marriage Act, 1872 (Central Act XV of 1872), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu sanctions the issue of a licence to
Pastor. G. Suresh Kumar, Life Changing Church, No. 9A,
