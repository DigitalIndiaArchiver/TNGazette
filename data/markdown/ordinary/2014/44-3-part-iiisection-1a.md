HOME, PROHIBITION AND EXCISE DEPARTMENT
Exemption to the Thiruvalargal Southern Agrifurane Industries Private Limited, Villupuram District for certain period
under the Tamil Nadu Indian Made Foreign Spirits (Manufacture) Rules.
[G.O. Ms. No. 29, Home, Prohibition and Excise (III), 30th October 2014.]
No. SRO A-32/2014.—Under Rule 35 of the Tamil Nadu Indian-made Foreign Spirits (Manufacture) Rules, 1981, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby exempts Thiruvalargal Southern Agrifurane Industries Private Limited, Villupuram District from
the operation of sub-rule (2) of rule 11 of the said Rules, to complete the construction works of Empty Bottle Storage Shed,
Packing Material Godown, Office Building and Reverse Osmosis Plant subject to the condition that the said works shall be
completed within six months from the date of publication of this Notification.
2. This Notification shall be deemed to have come into force with effect on and from the 12th July 2013.
 APURVA VARMA,
Principal Secretary to Government.
