Variation to the Sanctioned Railway Station Area T.P. Scheme Part-II of
Karaikudi Local Planning Area.
(Roc. No. 2053/2012/DP3.)
No.VI(1)/134/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (1) of Section 33 of Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning
Act, 1971 (Act No. 35 of 1972) the Commissioner of Town and Country Planning, Chennai In the Proceedings
Roc. No. 2053/2012 DP3, dated 29th March 2012. Proposes to make the followng variation to the sanctioned Railway
Station Area T.P. Scheme, Part-II of Karaikudi Local Planning Area, sanctioned in G.O.No. 2445, Rural Development
and Local Administration Department, dated 15th December 1969 published in the Notification of Tamil Nadu Government
Gazette, supplement to Part II—Section 2, page No. 33, dated 17th January 1970.
2. Any Person affected or interested in this draft variation may within sixty days from the date of publication of this
notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette can represent in person or submit in writing to the Member
Secretary, Karaikudi Local Planning Authority any objection and suggestions relating thereto.
3. The variation with plan may be inspected at free of cost at any time during office hours at the above said
Local Planning Authority Office/Karaikudi Municipality.

 Variations to the  Approved Master Plan for the Coimbatore Local Planning Area
(Roc. No. 4678/2008/LPA-1)
No. VI(1)/135/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4)  of Section 32 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country
Planning Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972) and in exercise of powers conferred by the G.O. Ms. No. 94, Housing
and Urban Development (UD4(1) Department, dated 12th June 2009 which has been published in the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette  No. 27, Part II—Section 2, page No. 228, dated 15th July 2009, the following
variations are made to the Master Plan for the Coimbatore Local Planning Area approved under the said Act and
published in the Housing and Urban Development Department Notification No. II(2)/HOU/4377/94 at page 1078 of
Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 9th November 1994.
 VARIATIONS
 In the said Master Plan, in the “LAND PLAN SCHEDULE” under the heading “Corporation Area Coimbatore
Municipal  Town, KUMARAPALAYAM DETAILED DEVELOPMENT PLAN No. 7, under the sub-heading (E) No. 29.
KUMARAPALAYAM VILLAGE.
(i) Against the entry “AGRICULTURAL” for the expression “514/2pt, 515, 518/1 523/1A, 1B,  523/2pt, 524pt”,  the
expression 513 to 515 (except 514/2pt), 522 to 527 (except 523/1A, 1B, 2pt, 524pt) shall be subsituted and the
expression 515, 518/1 shall be omitted.
(ii) Against the entry “MIXED RESIDENTIAL” 514/2pt, 515 shall be added before the entry “516” and 518/1
523/1A, 1B, 2pt, 524pt shall be added before the entry 536 pt.
