TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI
Extension of Direct Management of Shafi Madhab Peria Pallivasal Wakf,
Sivakasi Taluk and Town, Virudhunagar District.
(Rc. No. 14319/09/B3/ VNR)
No.VI-3(a)/57/2016.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act 1995 as amended by
Act 27/2013, the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in Item No. 64/16 in Rc.No.14319/09/B3/VNR, dated 03-03-2016
has extended the period of direct management of Shafi Madhab Peria Pallivasal Waqf, Sivakasi Taluk and Town, Virudhunagar
District for the period of one year from 03-03-2016.
Chennai-600 001,

Extension of Direct Management of Khaderpet Ahle Sunnath Jamath Masjid and
Madrasa Wakf, Tiruppur District.
(Rc. No. 7488/06/B3/ TPR.)
No.VI-3(a)/58/2016.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act 1995 as amended by
Act 27/2013, the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in Item No.40/16 in Rc.No. 7488/06/B3/TPR dated:18-02-2016 has
resolved to ratify the period of direct management of Khaderpet Ahle Sunnath Jamath Masjid and Madrasa Wakf, Tiruppur
District from 26-11-2015 to 17-02-2016 and also to extend the period of direct management of the above Wakf for the period
of one year from the Board’s order dated: 18-02-2016.
Chennai-600 001,
K. RASHEETH ALI,
5th May 2016.

No.VI-3(a)/59/2016.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 69 of the  Wakf Act 1995, the Tamil Nadu
Wakf Board in its Proceedings in Item No. 82/16 in Rc.No.13737/14/B9/CHN, dated 03-03-2016 has approved the Scheme
framed for the Wakf Viz., Jumma Masjid, Purasaiwalkam, Chennai District for better administration of the Wakf.
