NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
DTP—II-2 Ex. (207)
HOME,  PROHIBITION  AND  EXCISE  DEPARTMENT
DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF THE TAMIL NADU PREVENTION OF DANGEROUS ACTIVITIES OF
BOOTLEGGERS, DRUG-OFFENDERS, FOREST-OFFENDERS, GOONDAS, IMMORAL TRAFFIC
OFFENDERS, SAND-OFFENDERS, SLUM-GRABBERS AND VIDEO PIRATES (AMENDMENT) ACT, 2014.
[G.O. Ms. No. 26, Home, Prohibition and Excise (XVI), 13th October 2014.]
No. II(2)/HOPE/593(f-1)/2014.
Under sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Prevention of Dangerous Activities of
Bootleggers, Drug-offenders, Forest-offenders, Goondas, Immoral Traffic Offenders, Sand-offenders,
Slum-grabbers and Video Pirates (Amendment) Act, 2014 (Tamil Nadu Act 19 of 2014),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the  13th October 2014,  as the date on which the said Act
shall come into force.

[G.O. Ms. No. 27, Home, Prohibition and Excise (XVI), 13th October 2014.]
No. II(2)/HOPE/593(f-2)/2014.
Under sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Prevention of Dangerous Activities of
Bootleggers, Drug-offenders, Forest-offenders, Goondas, Immoral Traffic Offenders, Sand-offenders,
Slum-grabbers and Video Pirates (Second Amendment) Act,  2014 (Tamil Nadu Act 20 of 2014), the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the 13th October 2014, as the date on which the said Act
shall come into force.
APURVA VARMA,
Principal Secretary to Government.
