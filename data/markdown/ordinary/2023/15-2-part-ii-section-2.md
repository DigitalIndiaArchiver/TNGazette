In the Act first read above, Government have 
enacted the Tamil Nadu State Commission for the 
Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes Act, 2021 
(Tamil Nadu Act 17 of 2021), Dated: 22.09.2021.  The 
Act came into force with effect from 13.10.2021 by a 
Notification vide Government Order second read above.
2. In the Government Order third read above, 
the Chairperson, Vice-Chairperson and Members were 
appointed for the Tamil Nadu State Commission for the 
Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes.
3. In the Government Order fourth read above, the 
Tamil Nadu State Commission for the  Scheduled Castes 
and Scheduled Tribes Rules, 2022, Dated: 17.02.2022 
was made and Notified.
4. In the Government Order fifth read above, the 
Chairperson, Vice-Chairperson and the Members were 
sanctioned Honorarium at the rate Rs. 2,50,000/-, 
Rs.2,00,000/- and Rs.1,50,000/- respectively per month.
5. In the Act sixth read above, the Tamil Nadu 
State Commission for scheduled Castes and Scheduled 
Tribes Act, 2021 has been amended to pay Salaries and 
Allowances instead to Honorarium to the Chairperson, 
Vice-Chairperson and the Members of the Commission.
6. In the Government Order seventh read above, 
amendment has been made to the Tamil Nadu 
State Commission for Scheduled Castes and Scheduled 
Tribes Rules, 2022 to pay Salaries and Allowances to 
the Chairperson, Vice-Chairperson and Members instead 
to Honorarium and Allowances and the amendment to 
Act is deemed to come into force from 13.10.2021 
which is as follows:-
(a) For Chairperson:- The Chairperson of the 
Commission shall be paid salary and allowances as 
are admissible to a serving Judge of the High Court:
Provided that if a person appointed as 
Chairperson, is in receipt of any pension, his salary 
shall be reduced by the gross amount of pension 
drawn by him.
(b) For Vice-Chairperson:- The Vice-Chairperson shall 
be paid salary and allowances as are admissible 
to the rank of Secretary to the State Government.
(c) For Members:- The other Members shall be paid 
salary and allowances as are admissible to the rank 
of Additional Secretary to the State Government.
7. In the letter eighth read above, the Member 
Secretary, Tamil Nadu State Commission for the Scheduled 
Castes and Scheduled Tribes have  requested to fix 
the pay and payment of salary and allowances for the 
Chairperson, Vice-Chairperson, and the Members of the 
Commission from the date of their joining as noted against 
each below as the Act has been amended and it is 
deemed to come into force with effect from 13-10-2021.
