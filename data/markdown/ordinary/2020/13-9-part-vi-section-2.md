TAMIL NADU STATE LEGAL SERVICES AUTHORITY
Nomination of Chairman for the High Court Legal Services Committee
(TNSLSA No. 1413/E/2020)
No.VI(2)/13/2020.
In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (2) of Section 8A of the Legal Services Authorities 
Act, 1987, (Central Act 39 of 1987) the Hon’ble the Chief Justice, High Court, Madras is pleased to nominate 
Hon’ble Mr. Justice M. Sathyanarayanan, Judge, High Court, Madras to be the Chairman of the High Court Legal Services 
Committee with effect from 09-03-2020.
Chennai-600 104,	
	
	
	
	
	
	
K. RAJASEKAR,
10th March 2020.	
	
	
	
	
	
District Judge / Member-Secretary.
GOVERNMENT EXAMINATIONS / EDUCATIONAL OFFICERS
Loss of Certificates
It is hereby notified that the Original Higher Secondary Certificates / Matriculation Certificates / Diploma Certificates / School 
Certificates of the following pupils are reported to have been lost beyond recovery. Necessary steps are being taken to issue the 
duplicate of the said Certificates. If the Originals were to be found by anybody, they should be treated as invalid and sent to the 
Secretary, Board of Higher Secondary Examination / Directorate of Government Examinations, Chennai-6 / Director of Technical 
Education, Chennai-25 / Inspector / Inspectress of Matriculation and Anglo-Indian Schools as the case may be for cancellation:-
