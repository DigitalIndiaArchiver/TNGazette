ORDER
WHEREAS, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission conducted ordinary elections to the urban local bodies
during October 2011 as notified in the Statutory Order first read above;
2. WHEREAS, the District Election Officer/District Collector, Tiruvarur in his letters second read above, has reported that
out of the 108 contested candidates, 9 contested candidates lodged their accounts of election expenses, 12 contested
candidates lodged their accounts of election expenses belatedly and the remaining 87 contested candidates have neither
submitted their explanations nor lodged the account of election expenses inspite of the show cause notices issued to
them and recommended to disqualify the said 99 candidates;

3. WHEREAS, in view of the said report of the District Election Officer/District Collector, Tamil Nadu State Election
Commission satisfied that out of 99 candidates 87 candidates have failed to lodge their account of election expenses
as required by or under the said Act and have no good reason or justification for the failure and that it has been, inter
alia, stated in Para 8 of the aforesaid orders third read above that further report with regard to remaining 12 candidates
had been called for from the District Election Officer, Tiruvarur;
4. WHEREAS, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission in its Statutory Order third  read above, had issued orders
disqualifying the said 87 candidates of Tiruvarur District for being elected as a Chairman or a Councillor or a Ward Member for
a period of three years from the date of that Order for having failed to lodge their account of election expenses as required by
or under the said Act; and
5. WHEREAS, the District Election Officer/District Collector, Tiruvarur in his further report fifth read above has, reported
that the remaining 12 candidates lodged their accounts of election expenses belatedly and recommended for disqualification
stating that the accounts of election expenses were lodged belatedly but no other reasons.
6. NOW THEREFORE, in view of the reports received from the District Election Officer/District Collector, Tiruvarur and in
exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2-A) of section 49 of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920
(Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920), the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission having satisfied with the further report of the said
District Election Officer/District Collector, hereby decides and declares that the belated submission of explanation and
lodging of election expenses accounts by 12 candidates listed in column (3) of the Table below against the contested
election mentioned in the corresponding entry in column (2) thereof, shall be disqualified, for being elected as a Chairman
or a Councillor or a Ward Member for a period of three years from the date of Order.
