GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI.
Extension of Direct Management of Ellaiamman Kovil Street Mosque (Alias) Bappu Hakeem Pallivasal
Tanjore District.
(Rc. No.15909/03/B7/TNJ)
No. VI-3(a)/28/2017.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 (5) of the Wakf Act, 1995 as amended
by Act 27 of 2013, the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board ratify the period of direct management of Ellaiamman Kovil Street
Mosque (Alias) Bappu Hakeem Mosque, Tanjore from 29.05.2013 to 22-02-2017 and also to extend the period of direct
management of the above Wakf for the period of one year from 23-02-2017.
Chennai-600 001,
K. RASHEETH ALI,
23rd March 2017.
District Revenue Officer/
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
Extension of Direct Management of Asar Pakkir Thaikkal Waqf, Pattukottai Taluk, Tanjore District.
(Rc. No.18456/10/B7/TNJ)
No. VI-3(a)/29/2017.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 (5) of the Wakf Act, 1995 as amended
by Act 27 of 2013, the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board ratify the period of direct management of Asar Pakkir Thaikkal Waqf,
Pattukottai Taluk, Tanjore District from 03.09.2015 to 26-02-2017 and also to extend the period of direct management
of the above Wakf for the period of one year from 27-02-2017.
Chennai-600 001,
K. RASHEETH ALI,
23rd March 2017.
District Revenue Officer/
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
