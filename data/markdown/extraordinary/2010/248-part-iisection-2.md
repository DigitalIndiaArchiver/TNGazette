HIGHWAYS AND MINOR PORTS DEPARTMENT
LEVY OF TOLLS ON VEHICLES ENTERING THE ROADS IN YERCAUD PANCHAYAT UNION AREA IN SALEM DISTRICT
FOR CERTAIN PERIOD UNDER THE INDIAN TOLLS ACT.
               [G.O. D. No. 136, Highways and Minor Ports (HP.1), 13th August 2010, Aadi 28, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No. II (2)/HWMP/517(b)/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (a)  of sub-section  (2) of Section 2 of the Indian Tolls Act ,1851 (Central
Act VIII of 1851), as amended by the Tamil Nadu Tolls and Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1938 (Tamil Nadu Act
VI of 1938) and the Indian Tolls Tamil Nadu Amendment Act, 1942 (Tamil Nadu  Act XIV of 1942) the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby levies the tolls for the period upto the  31st March 2011 on the classes of motor vehicles specified in column (2)
of the Table below which passes through Salem – Yercaud Road and Kuppanur – Ayothiapattinam – Yercaud Road in the
Yercaud Panchayat Union area in Salem District at the rates specified in the corresponding entries in column (3)  there of:
Provided that such  levy of tolls shall not be applicable to—
(i)
the motor  owned by the residents of Yercaud other than the Vehicles used for commercial purpose,
(ii)
the office vehicles of Yercaud Panchayat Union; and
(iii)
the office vehicles of State and Central Governments and Government undertakings:—
