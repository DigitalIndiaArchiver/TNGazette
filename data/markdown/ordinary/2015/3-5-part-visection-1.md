GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
Final Closing and Cancellation of Registration of IND No. 3/NKL Namakkal District Technicians Labour
Contract Service Industrial Co-operative Society Ltd.
(L.F.1151/ICA/2010.)
No. VI(1)/18/2015.
In the circumstances Stated by the Assistant Director of Industries and Commerce (Industrial Co-operatives)/Official
Liquidator of the Namakkal District Technicians Labour Contract Service Industrial Co-operative Society Ltd.
IND No.3/NKL in his final closure proposals dated 21.08.2014 and in exercise of the powers delegated under Section 140(1)
of Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act, 1983, the registration of the Namakkal District Technicians Labour Contract
Service Industrial Co-operatives Society Ltd. IND No.3/NKL (Under Liquidation) has been ordered to be cancelled and the
affairs have been finally closed with effect from 29.12.2014 in the Procds. of Principal Secretary/Industries Commissioner
and Director of Industries & Commerce and Registrar of Industrial Co-operatives in LF.No.1151/ICA/2010, daed: 29-12-2014.
Winding up of the Affairs of Certain Co-operative Societies in certain Districts and Appointment of Official
Liquidator.
IND. No.1/SVG/2009, Sivagangai District Adi Dravidar Chamber Brick Workers Industrial Co-operative
Society Ltd. Sivagangai District.
(Rc.No.12948/ICN/2008)
No. VI(1)/19/2015.
“Under the powers conferred on the Registrar of Industrial Co-operatives under Section 137(2)(b) of the Tamil Nadu
Co-operative Societies Act, 1983 (Act 30 of 1983), the activities of Sivagangai District Adi Dravidar Chamber Brick Workers
Industrial Co-operative Society Ltd, IND.No.1/SVG/2009, Sivagangai District have been ordered to be wound up with immediate
effect by the Principal Secretary/Industries Commissioner and Director of Industries and Commerce and the Registrar of
Industrial Co-operatives, Mandaivelipakkam, Raja Annamalaipuram, Chennai-28 vide Proc.No.12948/ICN/2008
dated 29-1-2014”.
Under Section 138(1) of the same Act, Assistant Director (Industrial Co-operatives), District Industries Centre, Sivagangai
has been appointed as Official Liquidator of the said society for the purpose of Liquidation.
IND No. 708. Nehruji Polythene Workers Industrial Co-operative Society Ltd., Kundrakudi, Sivagangai District.
(Rc.No.28979/ICN/2013)
No. VI(1)/20/2015.
“Under the powers Conferred on the Registrar of Industrial Co-operatives under Section 137(2)(b) of the Tamil Nadu
Co-operative Societies Act, 1983 (Act 30 of 1983), the Activities of the Nehruji Polythene Workers Industrial Co-operative
Society Ltd., Kundrakudi IND. No. 708, Sivagangai District have been ordered to be wound up with immediate effect by the
Principal Secretary/Industries Commissioner and Director of Industries and Commerce and the Registrar of Industrial
Co-operatives, Mandaivelipakkam, Raja Annamalaipuram, Chennai-28 vide Proc.No.28979/ICN/2013, dated 29-12-2014”.
Under Section 138(1) of the same Act, Assistant Director (Industrial Co-operatives) / Official Liquidator, TAICO Bank,
Chennai has been appointed as Official Liquidator of the said society for the purpose of Liquidation.

Local Planning Area.
(Roc.No.22395/2012/DP1.)
No. VI(1)/21/2015.

of Town and Country Planning, Chennai-2 in Commissioner of Town and Country Planning Proceedings in

Village, Cuddalore Taluk, Guddalore Local Planning Area, Approved by the Director of Town and Country Planning proceeding
in Roc.No.22892/2009 DP1 dated:3.7.2010 and the fact of this approval in Form No.12 published in Tamil Nadu Government
Gazette No.34 Part VI—Section 1 page 274 & 275, dated 01-09-2010 Publication No.VI(1)/318/2010 and the said draft
notification published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazatte No. 6, Part VI—Section 1, Page No.83, dated:13-2-2013 Publication
No.VI(1)/116/2013.
