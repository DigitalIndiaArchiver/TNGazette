In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 113 read with Section 122 of the Tamil Nadu Town and 
Country Planning Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1971), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the 
following amendment to the Tamil Nadu Regularisation of Unapproved Layouts and Plots Rules, 2017, namely:- 
Amendment
In the said Rules, in rule 5, in sub-rule (1), for the words "one year", the words "eighteen months" shall 
be substituted.
