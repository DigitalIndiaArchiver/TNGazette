DECLARATION OF CERTAIN POLICE OFFICERS/PERSONNEL AS DESIGNATED OFFICERS

No. II(2)/HO/123(e)/2010.
Under Section 28-A of the Representation of the People Act, 1951 (Central Act XLIII of 1951),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby designates the Director General of Police/Additional Director
General of Police (Law and Order)/Inspector General of Police (West Zone)/Deputy Inspector General
of Police, Salem Range and Superintendent of Police, Dharmapuri District and all Police Officials
subordinate to them and up to the level of Police Constables and any other Police Officers/Officials
performing in any way election related duties for the conduct of Bye-Elections to be held on the
27th March, 2010 to the Legislative Assembly of this State from the pre-delimited 83. Pennagaram
Assembly Constituency as Designated Officers. All the said Officers shall be deemed to be on
deputation to the Election Commission for the period commencing on and from the date of the
Notification calling for the said Election and ending with the date of declaration of the result of the said
Election and accordingly, the said officers shall, during that period, be subject to the control,
superintendence and discipline of the Election Commission.
 S. MALATHI,
 Principal Secretary to Government.
