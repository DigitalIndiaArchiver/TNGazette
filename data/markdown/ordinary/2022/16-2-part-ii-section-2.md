BOUNDARIES.
North.-  Generally, the boundary starts from the 
tri-junction point of Survey Numbers 30, 4 and 1/1 of Kottur village 
runs towards the eastern side along the southern boundary of 
Survey Number 4 of Kottur village and till it reaches the 
tri-junction point of Survey Numbers 4, 3 and 1/1 of 
Kottur village.
East.-  Thence, the boundary runs towards south east side 
along the south west boundary of the Survey Numbers 3, 2 of 
Kottur village and turns towards south and runs along western 
boundary of Survey Number 530 of Jambuthuraikottai village 
and runs along the south west boundary of 1/2 of Kottur village 
and 1/3 of Nariyuthu village and reaches the tri-junction point 
of Survey Number 1/3 of Nariyuthu village, Survey Numbers 
60 and 1/1 of Kottur village.
South.-  Thence, the boundary runs towards the north and 
western side along the east and northen boundary of Survey 
Number 59 of Kottur village and reaches the tri-junction point of 
Survey Numbers 59, 58 and 1/1 of Kottur village.

1581, Ponnaiah Rajapuram, Coimbatore-1 the donor of the

the powers conferred by Section 4 of the said Act, hereby 
orders that the said property be vested with the Treasurer 
aforesaid and be held by him and his successors in Office, 
subject  to  the  provisions  of  the  said Act and the 
rules which, from time to time, may be or shall be deemed 
to have been made there under from upon the Trust for 
the purposes and subject to the conditions set forth in the 
Scheme, settled under Section 5 of the said Act for the 
administration of the said Endowment.
The Schedule
Rs. 25,000/-(Rupees Twenty five Thousand only)
II. SCHEME FOR THE ADMINISTRATION
WHEREAS an application has been made to 
the Government  of Tamil Nadu under clause (b) of 
sub-section (1) of Section 6 of the Charitable Endowments 
Act, 1890 (Central Act VI of 1890) by Dr. S. Subramaniam, 
the donor of the said Endowment,  for settling a scheme 
for the Administration of the said Fund, the Governor of 
Tamil Nadu, in exercise of the powers conferred by Section 
5 of the said Act, hereby settled the Scheme set forth in 
the Scheme below for the administration of the said Fund 
and appoints 03.03.2009 as the date on which the Scheme 
as so called shall come into operation.
The Scheme.
1. This fund shall be called "Covai Dentist Dr.S.Subramaniam 
Endowment Fund" tenable at the Tamil Nadu Government 
Music College, Thiruvaiyaru  for  awarding  books 
to  the  student  of Tamil Nadu Government Music 
College,  Thiruvaiyaru,  studying 3rd year  B.A. (Music) 
who gets first rank in Veena.
2.	 The Endowment fund which consist of Rs.25,000/- 
(Rupees Twenty Five Thousand only) shall be invested 
and from time to time reinvested in a Fixed Deposit 
in any of  the  Nationalised Banks in the name of 
the said Endowment.
3.	 The Principal, Tamil Nadu Government Music College, 
Thiruvaiyaru , shall be the administrator of the fund.
4. Out of the interest accruing annually from the above 
said Fund there shall be an award of "Books" which 
shall be called as "Covai Dentist Dr. S. Subramaniam 
award".
5.	 The said Books shall be awarded to the student of 
Tamil Nadu Government   Music  College,  Thiruvaiyaru, 
studying  3rd   year B.A. (Music) who gets first rank 
in Veena .
6.	 In the case of a tie, the award shall be divided 
equally among the students eligible for the award.
7.	 All the interest accruing on the fund that may not be 
utilized or required for the award shall be accumulated 
and such accumulations shall from time to time be 
reinvested in the same bank and be added to the 
Corpus of the fund.
8.	 The decision of the administrator of the fund shall 
be final in all matters relating to the administration 
of the fund.
