ANIMAL HUSBANDRY, DAIRYING AND FISHERIES DEPARTMENT
DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE THE TAMIL NADU FISHERIES UNIVERSITY (AMENDMENT) ACT, 2014
(TAMIL NADU ACT 5 OF 2014).
[G.O. Ms. No. 52, Animal Husbandry, Dairying & Fisheries (FS-2), 27th February 2014, Maasi 15,
Vijaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2045.]
No. II(2)/AHDF/238(c-1)/2014.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Fisheries
University (Amendment) Act, 2014 (Tamil Nadu Act 5 of 2014), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the
27th February 2014, as the date on which the said Act shall come into force.
DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE THE TAMIL NADU VETERINARY AND ANIMAL SCIENCES UNIVERSITY
(AMENDMENT) ACT, 2014 (TAMIL NADU ACT 4 OF 2014).
[G.O. Ms. No. 53, Animal Husbandry, Dairying & Fisheries (AH-6), 27th February 2014, Maasi 15,
Vijaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2045.]
No. II(2)/AHDF/238(c-2)/2014.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Veterinary and Animal
Sciences University (Amendment) Act, 2014 (Tamil Nadu Act 4 of 2014), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
appoints the 27th February 2014, as the date on which the said Act shall come into force.
S. VIJAYAKUMAR,
Secretary to Government.
