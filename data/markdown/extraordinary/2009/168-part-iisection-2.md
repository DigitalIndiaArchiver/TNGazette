HIGHWAYS AND MINOR PORTS DEPARTMENT
NOTIFICATIONS UNDER INDIAN TOLLS ACT.
SPECIFICATION OF NO TOLLS FOR THE PASSAGE OF THE VEHICLES OF THE LOCAL RESIDENTS
OF THE NILGIRIS DISTRICT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 129, Highways and Minor Ports (HQ1), 10th July 2009.]
No. II(2)/HWMP/347(a-1)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (b) of Section 4 of the Indian Tolls Act, 1851
(Central Act VIII of 1851), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby specifies that no tolls shall be
paid for the passage of the vehicles of the local residents of the Nilgiris District with effect from
2nd  July 2009.

LEVY OF TOLLS ON ROADS, WITH A FACILITY FOR INGRESS INTO THE NILGIRIS DISTRICT
FOR CERTAIN PERIOD.
[G.O. Ms. No. 129, Highways and Minor Ports (HQ1), 10th July 2009.]
No. II(2)/HWMP/347(a-3)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (a) of sub-section (1) read with sub-section (2) of Section 2
of the Indian Tolls Act, 1851 (Central Act VIII of 1851), as amended by the Tamil Nadu Tolls and Motor Vehicles
Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1938 (Tamil Nadu Act VI of 1938) and the Indian Tolls (Tamil Nadu Amendment)
Act, 1942 (Tamil Nadu Act XIV of 1942), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby levies tolls for a further period
of two years beyond the 1st July 2009 and with effect on and from the 2nd July 2009 in respect of the following
roads in the Nilgiris District with a facility  for ingress into the Nilgiris District namely:—
(i)
Kunjapanai - Kotagiri - Mettupalayam road
(ii)
Pattavayal - Sultan Bathery - Gudalur road
(iii)
Thaloor - Sultan Bathery - Gudalur road
(iv)
Nadugani - Calicut - Gudalur road
(v)
Choladi - Calicut - Gudalur road
