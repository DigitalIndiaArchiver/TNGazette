ORDER OF THE ELECTION COMMISSION OF INDIA
GENERAL ELECTIONS TO TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY 2011–168.TIRUVARUR, ASSEMBLY
CONSTITUENCY OMISSION OF  NAME OF THE SHRI. RAMASAMY K.R. NAME OF THE SAID SHRI
RAMASAMI ALIAS TRAFFIC RAMASAMI  K.R. APPEARING AT SL.NO.27 IN THE COMMISSION’S ORDER
NO.76/TN-LA/2011, DATED:10TH JUNE 2013, LIST OF DISQUALIFIED CANDIDATES WITH EFFECT FROM
17TH FEBRUARY 2014.
No. SRO G6/2014.
The following order of the Election Commission of India, Nirvachan  Sadan, Ashoka Road, New Delhi – 110 001,
dated 17th February 2014, 28 Magha 1935 (Saka) is republished.
No.76/TN-LA/2011 - Whereas, Shri Ramasami alias Traffic Ramasami. K.R. resident of 11/88. Thenmerkku
Boag Road, Thiyagaraya Nagar, Chennai-17 who had contested the General Election to the Legislative Assembly
of Tamil Nadu held in 2011 from 168. Tiruvarur Assembly Constituency, was disqualified by the Election
Commission of India, vide its order No.76/TN-LA/2011 dated 10th June, 2013 under section 10A of the Representation
of the People Act, 1951, for failure to lodge his account of election expenses as required by the said Act and the
Rules and order made there under’ and
Whereas, Shri Ramasami alias Traffic Ramasami, K.R. had not lodged his account of election expenses by
13.06.2011, the due date on or before which account of election expenses was to be filed with District Election
Officer and Collector, Tiruvarur and accordingly notice dated 17th August, 2011 was issued to him which was
received by him on 29.09.2011. After receiving the said notice, Shri Ramasami alias Traffic Ramasami, K.R did not
file his account of election expenses with the District Election Officer, Tiruvarur or Election Commission of India
either, and
Whereas, the District Election Officer and District Collector, Tiruvarur District further intimated vide his letter
No.5415/E.3/2010 dated:21.10.2011 that the candidate had not filed his account of election expenses as on the date
of reporting inspite of affording him opportunity: and
Whereas, the above said candidate was disqualified by the Commission on the basis of the District Election
Officer and District Collector, Tiruvarur’s report of the for failure to lodge his accounts of election expenses at all,
vide its Order  No.76/TN-LA/2011, dated 10th June, 2013; and

Whereas Shri Ramasami alias Traffic Ramasami, K.R. has submitted, alongwith Affidavit, vide letter dated
01.07.2013, 22.01.2014 and 31.01.2014 claiming that he had duly submitted the accounts in Xerox and got
endorsements in the original which was later stolen and a complaint was filed with police with the jurisdictional police
station B.2 Esplanade Police Station,Chennai-1: and
Whereas, the Commission has considered the petition so filed and keeping in view the circumstance under
which Shri Ramasami could not produce his account of election expenses with the Election Commission of India
/ District Election Officer. The Commission in exercise of the power conferred by under Section 11 of the
Representation of the People Act, 1951, has decided to remove the disqualification imposed upon the above said
candidate  Shri Ramasami    alias  Traffic Ramasami, K.R. with effect from 17th February, 2014 Consequently, the
name of the said Shri Ramasami alias Traffic Ramasami, K.R. appearing at Sl.No.27 in the Commission’s Order
No.76/TN-LA/2011, dated: 10.06.2013   shall be deemed to have been omitted from the said order with effect from
17.02.2014.
