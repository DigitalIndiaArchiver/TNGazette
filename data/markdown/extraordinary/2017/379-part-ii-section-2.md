The Governer of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that 
the lands specified in the schedule below are acquired for 
industrial purposes to wit to the formation of Double way 
Broad – Guage Railway Line by changing the existing single 
way Broad-Guage Railway Line from Omalur – Mettur Dam 
of Salem District and it having already been decided that the 
entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands 
is to be paid out of the funds controlled and managed by 
Southern Railway, Chennai after considering the cause shown 
by the owner or other persons having interested on such lands 
as the case may be do here by publish the following notice is 
issued under sub-section (1) of section 3 of the Tamil Nadu 
Acquisition of land for Industrial Purposed Act, 1997 (Tamil 
Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
Notice
Under sub-section (1) of section 3 of the Tamil Nadu 
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu 
hereby acquires the lands in 14.Veerakkal Pudur Village, Mettur 
Taluk of Salem District as specified in the Schedule below 
and measuring to 0.02.5 hectare to the same, a little more 
or less required for industrial purposes, to wit,for formation 
of Double way Broad – Guage Railway line by changing the 
existing single way Broad-Guage Railway line from Omalur 
– Mettur Dam of Salem District. 
The plan of the lands are kept in the office of the Land 
Acquisition Officer and District Revenue Officer, Collectorate 
Salem and the Special Tahsildar (LA), Omalur – Mettur Dam 
Double Line Broad Guage Railway Track, Room No.414, 4th 
Floor, Collectorate Campus, Salem and may be inspected at 
any time during office hours.
