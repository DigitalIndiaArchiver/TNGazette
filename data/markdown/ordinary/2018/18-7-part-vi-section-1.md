(Roc.No.17502/2011/DP1)
No.VI(1)/153/2018.- 
1. In exercise of the powers conferred Under Section 33(2) of Town and Country Planning Act 1971, the Commissioner 
of Town and Country Planning, Chennai is hereby conﬁ rms the variation to the Approved Nagercoil Detailed Development 
Plan No. VI of Nagercoil Local Planning Area, varied by Director of Town and Country Planning in Proceedings in 
Roc.No.17502/2011/DP1, dated: 29-10-2015 and was published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No. 46, 
Part VI—Section-1, Page No.320, dated 18-11-2015 (Publication No.(VI)(1)/283/2015).
2. 
Since no objection and suggestion was received on this draft notiﬁ cation within the stipulated time, the same 
are hereby conﬁ rmed and ordered as below.
CONFIRMATION OF VARIATION
1. The entire Nagercoil Detailed Development Plan No.VI of Nagercoil Local Planning Area, varied by the Director 
of Town and Country Planning Proceeding in Roc.No.17502/2011/DP1, dated: 29-10-2015 under section 33(1) 
of Town and Country Planning Act 1971 is hereby conﬁ rmed under Section 33(2) of Town and Country 
Planning Act 1971.
2. The Varied Plan is numbered as Map No.5/DDP(V)/DTCP No.15/2015.
3. The Detailed Development plan clauses and schedules annexed with this plan are made enforceable from 
the date of publication of this notiﬁ cation in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
