The Governor of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that
the lands specified in the schedule below are  acquired for
Highways Purpose to wit for upgrading  Omalur – Sankari
– Trichengode – Paramathi Road (SH-86) Km 54/800 to Km
81/000 in Pudhupuliyampatti and Nallur Villages,
Trichengode  and Paramathi Velur Taluks, Namakkal
District and it having already been decided that the entire
amount of compensation to be awarded for the land is to be
paid out of the funds controlled and managed by Highways
Department after considering the cause shown by the owner
or other persons having interest on such lands as the case
[ 1 ]
may be do hereby publish the following Notice  under
sub-section (1) of  Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways
Act 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act No. 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu
Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands specified
in the schedule below and measuring to an Extent  325
Sq.mtrs of dry and manai lands in Pudhupuliyampatti and
Nallur Villages, Trichengode  and Paramathi Velur Taluks,
Namakkal District to the same, a little more or less required
for Highways purpose for upgrading Omalur – Sankari –
Trichengode – Paramathi Road (SH-86) at Km 54/800 to
Km 81/000.
The plan of the lands is kept in the office of the Special
District Revenue Officer (Land Acquisition), Tamil Nadu
Road Sector Project-II, Salem and  may be inspected at
any time during office hours.
