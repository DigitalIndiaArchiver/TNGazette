The  Governor of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that
the lands specified in the schedule below have to be acquired
for Industrial Purpose, to wit for the formation of ULTRA
MEGA POWER PROJECT, Cheyyur  and it has already been
decided that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded
for the lands is to be paid out of the funds controlled or
managed by the Officer Authorised Signatory, Coastal Tamil
Nadu Power Ltd., New  Delhi and having considered the
cause shown by the owners or other persons having interest
the said lands as the case may be do hereby publish the
following notice under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil
Nadu Acquisition of Land for industrial purposes Act, 1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
 Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Industrial purposes Act 1997 (Tamil Nadu  Act 10 of 1999) the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquired the lands specified in
the Schedule below and measuring an extent of  9.16.0 Hectare
to the same, a little more or less are needed for INDUSTRIAL
purpose, to wit, for the formation of ULTRA MEGA POWER
PROJECT, 54. CHITHARKADU VILLAGE, CHEYYUR TALUK,
Kancheepuram District.
The plan of the lands are kept in the office of Special
Tahsildar, Land Acquisition, Unit-I ULTRA MEGA POWER
PROJECT Scheme, Cheyyur and may be inspected at any
time during office hours.
