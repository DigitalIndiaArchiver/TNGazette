In the G.O ﬁ rst read above, orders were issued  freezing of administrative units/boundaries viz., municipalities, revenue

to 31st March 2021.

2. The Registrar General and Census Commissioner, India in the letter 2nd read above has stated that after the outbreak 
of COVID-19 pandemic, the ﬁ eld work of phase-I of Census 2021 and the other related ﬁ eld activities were postponed until 
further orders. Subsequent to the postponement of Census activities, requests were received from several State/ Union 
Territory Governments seeking clariﬁ cations/ permission for creation/ extension /up-gradation of diﬀ erent administrative units. 
Hence, it was decided to extend the date of freezing of boundaries upto 31st December, 2020 for the ensuing Census 2021 
and the boundaries of the administrative units will be frozen on 31st December, 2020. If any changes in the administrative 
boundaries may be eﬀ ected latest by 31-12-2020 and no changes will be eﬀ ected after 31-12-2020 to till the Census is over.
3. In the circumstances stated above, in supersession of the orders issued in the G.O ﬁ rst read above, the Government 
of Tamil Nadu hereby extend the date of freezing of administrative boundaries of all the municipalities, revenue villages, 
towns, tehsils, police stations, sub-divisions, districts, etc.,  and ban on formation of new units in the State of Tamil Nadu upto
31st December, 2020 for the ensuing Census.
4. All the pending proposals, if any aﬀ ect the changes in the existing boundaries of municipalities, revenue villages, 
towns, tehsils, police stations, sub-divisions, districts etc., may therefore be ﬁ nalized on or before 31st December 2020 under 
intimation to the Director of Census Operations, Tamil Nadu. The boundaries of administrative units for the ensuing Census 
will now be frozen from 31st December 2020 to till the completion of Census. 
5. The Commissioner of Revenue Administration, Chennai, the Director General of Police, Chennai, the Commissioner 
of Rural Development and Panchayat Raj, Chennai, the Director of Municipal Administration, Chennai, the Director of Town 
Panchayats, Chennai, the Collectors of all Districts, the Commissioner of Greater Chennai Corporation, the Commissioner 
of Trichy, Madurai, Salem, Coimbatore, Tirunelveli, Vellore, Toothukudi, Tirupur, Erode, Thanjavur, Dindigul, Nagercoil, Hosur 
and Avadi Municipal Corporations are directed to adhere to the above decision of the Government.
(By Order of the Governor)
