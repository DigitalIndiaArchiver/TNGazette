CO-OPERATION, FOOD AND CONSUMER PROTECTION DEPARTMENT
Removal of (Licensing Requirements, Stock Limits and Movement Restrictions) on Specified
Foodstuffs (Amendment) Order, 2011.
[G.O.Ms.No. 119, Co-operation, Food and Consumer Protection (D1), 26th December 2011.]
No. II(1)/CFCP/3/2012.—The following order of the Gazette of India, Ministry of Consumer Affairs, Food and Public
Distribution (Department of Consumer Affairs), New Delhi, the 27th September 2011 is republished:—
S.O. 2227 (E).—In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 3 of the Essential Commodities Act, 1955 (10 of 1955),
the Central Government hereby makes the following Order further to amend the Removal of (Licensing Requirements, Stock
Limits and Movement  Restrictions) on Specified Foodstuffs Order, 2002, namely:-
1.
(1) This order may be called the Removal of (Licensing Requirements, Stock Limits and Movement Restrictions) on
Specified Foodstuffs (Amendment) Order, 2011.
(2) It shall come into force on the 1st day of October 2011.
2. In the Removal of (Licensing Requirements, Stock Limits and Movement Restrictions) on Specified Foodstuffs Order,
2002, in clause 7,—
(a) in sub-clause (1), for items (i), (ii) and (iii), the following items shall be substituted, namely:—
“(i) pulses, edible oils and edible oilseeds for a period upto 30th September 2012.
(ii) sugar for a period upto 30th November 2011 and
(iii) paddy and rice for a period upto 31st October 2011,”;
(b) in sub-clause (2), for the words, brackets and figure “sub-clause (i)", the words, brackets and figure
“sub-clause (1)” shall be substituted.
[No. 10/1/2006-ECR&E]
PANKAJ AGRAWALA,
Additional Secretary to Government of India.
(Republished by Order of the Governor)
M.P. NIRMALA,
Secretary to Government.

Removal of (Licensing Requirements, Stock Limits and Movement Restrictions) on Specified Foodstuffs
(Third Amendment) Order, 2011.
[G.O. Ms. No. 119, Co-operation, Food and Consumer Protection (D1), 26th December 2011.]
No. II(1)/CFCP/4/2012.—The following order of the Gazette of India, Ministry of Consumer Affairs, Food and Public
Distribution (Department of Consumer Affairs), New Delhi, the 28th October 2011 is republished:—
S.O. 2447 (E).—In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 3 of the Essential Commodities Act, 1955 (10 of 1955),
the Central Government hereby makes the following Order further to amend the Removal of (Licensing Requirements, Stock
Limits and Movement Restrictions) on specified Foodstuffs Order, 2002, namely:—
1.  (1) This order may be called the Removal of (Licensing Requirements, Stock Limits and Movement Restrictions) on
specified Foodstuffs (Third Amendment) Order, 2011.
(2) It shall come into force on the 1st day of November 2011.
2. In the Removal of (Licensing Requirements, Stock Limits and Movement Restrictions) on specified Foodstuffs Order,
2002, in clause 7, for item (iii) the following shall be substituted, namely:-
“(iii) Paddy and rice for a period upto 30th November 2011.
In respect of the purchase, movement, sale, supply, distribution or storage for sale of the commodities.”
[No. 10/1/2006-ECR&E]
RAJIV AGARWAL,
Secretary to Government of India.
(Republished by Order of the Governor)
M.P. NIRMALA,
Secretary to Government.
Removal of (Licensing Requirements, Stock Limits and Movement Restrictions) on Specified Foodstuffs
(Fourth Amendment) October, 2011.
[G.O. Ms. No. 119, Co-operation, Food and Consumer Protection (D1), 26th December 2011.]
No. II(1)/CFCP/5/2012.—The following order of the Gazette of India, Ministry of Consumer Affairs, Food and Public
Distribution (Department of Consumer Affairs), New Delhi, the 29th November 2011 is republished:—
S.O. 2716 (E).—In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 3 of the Essential Commodities Act, 1955 (10 of 1955),
the Central Government hereby makes the following Order further to amend the Removal of (Licensing Requirements, Stock
Limits and Movement Restrictions) on specified Foodstuffs Order, 2002, namely:—
1.  (1) This order may be called the Removal of (Licensing Requirements, Stock Limits and Movement Restrictions) on
specified Foodstuffs (Fourth Amendment) October, 2011.
(2) It shall come into force on the 1st day of December 2011.
2. In the Removal of (Licensing Requirements, Stock Limits and Movement Restrictions) on specified Foodstuffs Order,
2002, in clause 7, in sub-clause (1),—for item (iii), the following item shall be substituted, namely:—
  “(iii) Paddy and rice for a period of one year with effect from 1st December 2011 upto 30th November 2012 in respect
of the States of Andhra Pradesh, National Capital Territory of Delhi, Manipur, Maharashtra, Tamil Nadu and Jharkahand and
Union territory of Andaman and Nicobar Islands as per their specific request”.
