MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION AND WATER SUPPLY DEPARTMENT
DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF THE TAMIL NADU MUNICIPAL LAWS
(FOURTH AMENDMENT) ACT, 2008.
[G.O. Ms. No. 255, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (MC1), 18th December 2008,
Margazhi 3, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2039.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/608(b)/2008.
 In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Municipal Laws
(Fourth Amendment) Act, 2008 (Tamil Nadu Act 24 of 2008), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints
the 18th day of December 2008 as the date on which the said Act shall come into force.
NIRANJAN MARDI,
 Secretary to Government.
