(D.No. 2341/2020)
(A1. No. 166/2020)
No.VI(1)/20/2021
Notice is hereby given that the list of Public Holidays and Court Holidays are as follows for Criminal Units of Cuddalore 
District, Cuddalore.
The Holidays for all the Subordinate Courts in the State of Tamil Nadu, for the year 2021 are as follows:- 
 
(a) All Sundays are holidays for all the Subordinate Courts including Family Courts functioning in the State of Tamil Nadu. 
 
(b) The following 10 Saturdays are working days for all the Subordinate Courts including Family Courts functioning 
in the State of Tamil Nadu: 
 
 
23-01-2021, 06-03-2021, 17-04-2021, 05-06-2021, 03-07-2021, 07-08-2021, 04-09-2021, 23-10-2021, 
 
 
20-11-2021 and 04-12-2021.  
 
(c) Except the above Saturdays, all other Saturdays are holidays for all the Subordinate Courts, except Family 
Courts. 
 
(d) All the Family Courts functioning in the State of Tamil Nadu shall work on 1st and 3rd Saturdays of every 
month except those Saturdays which are declared as Public Holidays. All other Saturdays are declared as holidays, except 
23-01-2021 and 23-10-2021 which are declared as working days.
