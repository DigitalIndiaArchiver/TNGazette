Ordinance, 2021 (Tamil Nadu Ordinance 1 of 2021), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby modiﬁ es the Tamil Nadu 
Municipal Corporations Constitution of Standing Committees Rules, 1997, by way of making the following amendment 
to the said Rules and also directs that the said Rules shall apply to the Cuddalore City Municipal Corporation, subject 
to the modiﬁ cation hereby made:—

In the said Rules, in rule 2, for sub-rule (1), the following sub-rule shall be substituted, namely:—
“(1) In these rules, unless the context otherwise requires,-
 
(i) 
“Corporation” means the municipal corporation of Cudddalore;
 
(ii) 
“Council” means the council of municipal corporation of Cuddalore; and
 
(iii) “Ordinance” means the Cuddalore City Municipal Corporation Ordinance, 2021  
 
 
(Tamil Nadu Ordinance 1 of 2021).”.
