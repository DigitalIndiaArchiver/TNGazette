Purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands specified in the Schedule below 
measuring 73612 Sq.mtrs., to the same, a little more or less needed for Industrial Purpose wit, for the Formation 
of New Broad-Gauge Railway Line between Morappur and Dharmapuri in Naikkanahalli Village of Dharmapuri Taluk 
in Dharmapuri District.

Tahsildar (LA), Dharmapuri which may be inspected on any day during office hours.
