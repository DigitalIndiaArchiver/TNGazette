WHEREAS on considering the recommendations of the expert team of Doctors and Public Health Specialists and based 
on the directives of Government of India, Ministry of Home Aﬀ airs, State-wide lockdown was extended from time to time and 
lastly extended till 24:00 hrs of 30.4.2021 under the Disaster Management Act, 2005 in GO.Ms.No.339, Revenue and Disaster 
Management (DM-IV) Department, dated 31.3.2021 with various relaxations and certain restrictions.
2.Taking into account the increasing trend of Covid 19 cases across the State, among others restrictions have been 
imposed for functioning of Cinemas/Theatres/Multiplex with 50% of the seating capacity and permitting public worship in all 
religious places / places of worship till 8.00 P.M. for the eﬀ ective containment of Covid-19 in the State vide G.O. Ms.No.342, 
Revenue and Disaster Management Department, dated 8.4.2021. 
3. The Additional Chief Secretary / Commissioner of Revenue Administration has informed that in the Government Press 
Release 23rd read above, it has been stated that  all Beaches in 3 Districts viz., Chennai, Chengalpattu and Tiruvallur 
shall be closed for public on all Saturdays, Sundays and other Government Holidays from 11.4.2021. It has also 
been stated that the restriction imposed for the public worship in all religious places / places of worship till 8.00 P.M. 
has now been relaxed and public worship shall be permitted in all religious places / places of worship as per the 
usual timings of religious places / places of worship or upto 10.00 P.M.  It has been further stated that one special / 
additional show for the New Films released during this period shall be permitted in the Cinemas/Theatres/Multiplex 
for the ﬁ rst 7 days only with 50% of the capacity.

4. Accordingly, the Additional Chief Secretary / Commissioner of Revenue Administration has sent an amendment proposal 
to G.O. Ms.No.339, Revenue and Disaster Management (DM IV), Department, Dated : 31.3.2021 and G.O. Ms.No.342, 
Revenue and Disaster Management (DM IV), Department, Dated : 8.4.2021.
5. Now therefore the Government hereby accept the proposal of the Additional Chief Secretary / Commissioner of 
Revenue Administration and to issue the following amendments to G.O. Ms.No.339, Revenue and Disaster Management 
(DM IV), Department, Dated : 31.3.2021 read with G.O. Ms.No.342, Revenue and Disaster Management (DM IV), Department, 
Dated : 8.4.2021:- 
A. 
The following content may be added as Section (iv) under Caption I (Under Prohibited Activities):-
iv.  
All Beaches in 3 Districts viz., Chennai, Chengalpattu and Tiruvallur Districts shall be closed for 
public on all Saturdays, Sundays and other Government Holidays from 11.4.2021.
B.  
The following content in Section (viii) and (xiv) under Caption I-A – (Under Permitted Activities) may be 
read as:-
viii. 
 Cinemas/theatres/Multiplexes shall be permitted to function with 50% of the seating capacity by 
following the Standard Operating Procedure issued already. Further, one special / additional show for 
the New Films released during this period shall be permitted in the Cinemas/Theatres/Multiplex for 
the ﬁ rst 7 (seven) days only, with 50% of the capacity till the restriction on the seating capacity 
is relaxed.
xiv.  All religious places / places of worship shall be permitted for public worship as per the usual 
timings of religious places / places of worship or upto 10.00 P.M. by strictly following the Standard 
Operating Procedures issued by the Government on 31.08.2020.  However, essential rituals 
are permitted with the participation of only the employees of the respective religious place. 
Religious congregations and ‘Tiruvizhakkal’ shall remain prohibited in all religious places / places 
of worship.
