Amendment to the Tamil Nadu Motor Vehicles Rules.
[G.O. Ms. No. 125, Home (Transport-VII), 27th February 2019,

In exercise of the powers conferred by Sections 95 and 96 of the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988 (Central Act 59 of 1988), 
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Tamil Nadu Motor Vehicles Rules, 1989, the draft 
of the same having been previously published, as required by sub-section (1) of Section 212 of the said Act.
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖග
In the said Rules, to Rule 227, the following proviso shall be added, namely:-
“Provided that a stage carriage of State Transport Undertaking may be used in a public place without conductor, if 
tickets are issued before the commencement of the journey by any self-sustaining mechanism set up for this purpose or by 
any other means or by a person who is not a traveller in the vehicle”.
NIRANJAN MARDI,
Additional Chief Secretary to Government.

In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 12 of the Tamil Nadu Industrial Establishments (National, Festival and 
Special Holidays) Act, 1958 (Tamil Nadu Act XXXIII of 1958), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following 
amedments to the Tamil Nadu Industrial Establishments (National and Festival Holidays) Rules, 1959, namely:-
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖගඛ
In the said Rules,-
1. 
for the expression “National and Festival Holidays” wherever it occurs, the expression “National, Festival and 
Special Holidays” shall be substituted;
2. 
in rule 8, for the expression “fifty rupees”, the expression “five thousand rupees” shall be substituted.
SUNIL PALIWAL,
Principal Secretary to Government.
