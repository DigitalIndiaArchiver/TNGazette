The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that
the lands specified in the schedule below have to be acquired
for Industrial Purpose to wit for Mine IA Expansion Scheme of
Neyveli Lignite Corporation India Limited and it having already
been decided that the entire amount of compensation to be
awarded for the lands is to be paid out of the funds controlled
or managed by Neyveli Lignite Corporation India Limited.  The
[ 1 ]
following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 3
of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes
Act, 1997.
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Government of
Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands specified in the
Schedule below and measuring 3.97.0 hectares to the
same, a little more or less needed for Industrial Purposes,
to wit, for Mine IA Expansion Scheme of Neyveli Lignite
Corporation India Limited.
The plans of the lands are kept in the office of the
Special Deputy Collector / Land Acquisition, Neyveli-2 and
may be inspected at any time during office hours.
