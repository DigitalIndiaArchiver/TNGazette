No. II(2)/HFW/232/2021.—Under sub-section (1) of 
Section 7 of the Registration of Births and Deaths Act, 1969 
(Central Act 18 of 1969), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby 
appoints the Multipurpose Health Supervisors (Male) of the

Hospital for Thoracic Medicine, Tambaram.
