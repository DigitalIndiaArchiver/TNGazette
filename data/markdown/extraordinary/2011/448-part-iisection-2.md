ENERGY   DEPARTMENT
CONSTITUTION OF SELECTION COMMITTEE FOR SELECTING THE CHAIRMAN OF THE TAMIL NADU
ELECTRICITY REGULATORY COMMISSION UNDER THE ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 118, Energy (A1), 30th November 2011.]
No. II(2)/EGY/473(a)/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 85 of the Electricity Act, 2003 (Central
Act 36 of 2003), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby constitutes a Selection Committee consisting of the
following for the purpose of selection  of Chairman of the Tamil Nadu Electricity Regulatory Commission:—
(a) Thiru Justice K. Sampath, Retired Judge
:
Chairperson
(b) The Chief Secretary to Government of Tamil Nadu
:
Member
(c) The Chairperson of the Central Electricity Regulatory
:
Member
Commission, New Delhi.
2.   This Selection Committee shall
(a) finalize the Selection of Chairman within three months from the date on which the reference is
made to it;
(b) recommend a panel of two names for every vacancy referred to it;
(c) satisfy itself, before recommending any person for appointment as Chairman of the Tamil Nadu
Electricity Regulatory Commission, that such person does not have any financial or other interest
which is likely to affect prejudicially his functions as Chairman.
DEBENDRANATH SARANGI,
Chief Secretary.
