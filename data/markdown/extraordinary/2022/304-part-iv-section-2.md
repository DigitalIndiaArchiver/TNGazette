The following Act of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly 
received the assent of the Governor on the 26th May 2022 and

ACT No.  25  of 2022
  An Act to amend the Tamil Nadu State Commission  for the 
Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes Act, 2021.
BE  it enacted by the Legislative Assembly  of the State of 
Tamil Nadu in the Seventy-third Year of the Republic of India as 
follows:- 
Short  title and 
commencement.
    1. (1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu State 
Commission for the Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes 
(Amendment) Act, 2022.
       (2)  It shall be deemed to have come into force on the
13th day of October 2021.
Amendment of 
section 17. 
 2.  In section 17 of the Tamil Nadu State Commission for the 
Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes Act, 2021 (hereinafter 
referred to as the principal Act), for sub-section (1), the following 
sub-section shall be substituted, namely:-
Tamil Nadu Act
17 of 2021.
    “(1) The Government may, make rules, either prospectively 
or retrospectively, for carrying out all or any of the purposes of this 
Act.” .
Substitution of 
expression.

allowances” occurring in three places,  the expression “salaries and 
allowances”  shall be substituted.
