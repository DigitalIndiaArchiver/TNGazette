ACQUISITION OF LANDS
(D.Dis. M3/22853/09.)
No. VI(1)/24(a)/2010.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that
the lands specified in the schedule below have to be acquired
for a public purpose to wit for the formation of Broad Gauge
Line by the Southern Railway Depatment and it
having already been decided that the entire amount of
compensation to be awarded for the lands to be
paid out of the public fund, the following declaration is
issued under Section 6 of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894
(Central Act 1 of 1894).
DECLARATION
Under Section 6 of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894
(Central Act 1 of 1894). The Principal Secretary and
Commissioner of Land Administration, Chepauk, Chennai-
600 005, hereby declares that the lands specified in the
schedule below and measuring to an extent of 0.91.5
hectare, be the same, a little more or less are needed for
a public purpose, to wit, for the purpose of the formation of
Broad Guage Line from Nagore to Karaikal by the Southern
Railway, department in 54. Panangudi Village of
Nagapattinam Taluk, Nagapattinam District.
A plan of the land is kept in the office of the Land
Acquisition Officer/Revenue Divisional Officer, Nagapattinam,
Nagapatinam District   and the same may be inspected at
any time during office hours.
