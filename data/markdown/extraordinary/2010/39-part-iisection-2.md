NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
FINANCE DEPARTMENT
DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF THE TAMIL NADU FISCAL RESPONSIBILITY
(AMENDMENT) ACT, 2010.
[G.O. Ms. No. 47, Finance (Resources-I), 15th February 2010, Maasi 3,
Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No. II(2)/FIN/94(c)/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Fiscal
Responsibility (Amendment) Act, 2010 (Tamil Nadu Act 11 of 2010), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby appoints the 15th day of February 2010, as the date on which the said Act shall come into
force.
K. GNANADESIKAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
