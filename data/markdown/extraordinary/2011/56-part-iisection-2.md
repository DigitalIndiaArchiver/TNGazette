COMMERCIAL TAXES AND REGISTRATION  DEPARTMENT
ALTERATION OF THE LIMITATION OF THE REGISTRATION SUB-DISTRICTS OF SRIPERUMPUDUR, SALAVAKKAM IN
THE REGISTRATION DISTRICT OF CHENGLEPATTU AND WALAJABAD IN THE REGISTRATION DISTRICT OF
KANCHEEPURAM IN THE REVENUE DISTRICT OF KANCHEEPURAM BY EXCLUDING CERTAIN VILLAGES AND
FORMS A NEW REGISTRATION SUB-DISTRICT OF SUNGUVARCHATTIRAM IN THE REGISTRATION DISTRICT OF
CHENGLEPATTU UNDER THE REGISTRATION ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 23, Commercial Taxes and Registration (G)14th February 2011.]
No.II(2)/CTR/71(d)/2011.
Under Section 5 of the Registration Act, 1908 (Central Act XVI of 1908), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby, with effect on
and from the 15th February 2011 alters the limits of the Registration sub-districts of Sriperumpudur, Salavakkam in the
Registration district of Chenglepattu and Walajabad in the Registration district of Kancheepuram, in the Revenue district of
Kancheepuram and excluding the villages specified in column (2) from the said Registration sub-districts specified in
column (3) of the Table below and forms a new Registration sub-district of Sunguvarchattiram in the said Registration district of
Chenglepattu in the said Revenue district of Kancheepuram, with the villages specified in column (2) of the Table below:—
