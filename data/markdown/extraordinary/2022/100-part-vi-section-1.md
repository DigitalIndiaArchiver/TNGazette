The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that the land/lands specified in the Schedule below to be 
acquired for industrial purpose to wit to the formation of Doubling of Broad Gauge Railway Line between Madurai - 
Thoothukudi situated in Melakottai and Nilayur Bit -1 Village of Thirumangalam and Thirupparankundram Taluk respectively 
in the District of Madurai, and it having already been decided that the amount of compensation to be awarded for 
the land/lands is to be paid out of the funds controlled or managed by RVNL. The following notice is issued under 
sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997. (Tamil Nadu 
Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the land/lands specified in the Schedule 
below measuring an extent of 14100 Sq. mtrs to the same, a little more or less needed for industrial purpose wit, for 
the formation of Doubling of Broad Gauge Railway Line between Madurai - Thoothukudi work in Melakottai and Nilayur 
Bit-1 Village of Thirumangalam and Thirupparankundram Taluk respectively in the District of Madurai.
 The plan of the land/lands is/are kept in the office of the Collector, Madurai may be inspected on any day during 
office hours.
