COMMERCIAL TAXES AND REGISTRATION DEPARTMENT
DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF THE REGISTRATION (TAMIL NADU AMENDMENT) ACT, 2012.
(TAMIL NADU ACT 29 OF 2012.)
[G.O. Ms. No. 149, Commercial Taxes and Registration (J2), 29th November 2012.]
No. II(2)/CTR/795(d)/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (3) of Section 1 of the Registration (Tamil Nadu Amendment)
Act, 2012 (Tamil Nadu Act 29 of 2012), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the
1st day of December 2012 as the date on which the said Act shall come into force.
SUNIL PALIWAL,
Secretary to Government.
