NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
HIGHER EDUCATION DEPARTMENT
APPOINTMENT OF CERTAIN PERSON AS FIRST VICE-CHANCELLOR OF THE ANNA UNIVERSITY OF
TECHNOLOGY, CHENNAI FOR CERTAIN PERIOD UNDER THE ANNA UNIVERSITY OF TECHNOLOGY,
CHENNAI ACT, 2010.
[G.O. Ms. No. 169, Higher Education (I-1), 19th June 2010, Aani 5, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No. II(2)/HE/379(d-1)/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 54 of the Anna University of Technology, Chennai Act, 2010
(Tamil Nadu Act 26 of 2010), the Government hereby appoint Dr. C. Thangaraj, Vice-Chancellor, Kalasalingam University,
Virudhunagar as the First Vice-Chancellor of Anna University of Technology, Chennai for a period of three years or till he
attains the age of 65 years whichever is earlier from the date of his assuming office.
APPOINTMENT OF CERTAIN PERSON AS FIRST VICE-CHANCELLOR OF THE ANNA UNIVERSITY OF
TECHNOLOGY, MADURAI FOR CERTAIN PERIOD UNDER THE ANNA UNIVERSITY OF TECHNOLOGY,
MADURAI ACT, 2010.
[G.O. Ms. No. 170, Higher Education (I-1), 19th June 2010, Aani 5, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No. II(2)/HE/379(d-2)/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 54 of the Anna University of Technology, Madurai Act, 2010
(Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 2010), the Government hereby appoint Dr. R. Murugesan, Principal, Ranganathan Engineering
College, Coimbatore as the First Vice-Chancellor of Anna University of Technology, Madurai for a period of three years or
till he attains the age of 65 years whichever is earlier from the date of his assuming office.
K. GANESAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
