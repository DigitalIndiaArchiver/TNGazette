CONSTITUTION OF STANDING COMMITTEES IN THE CITY MUNICIPAL CORPORATIONS OF CERTAIN DISTRICTS
UNDER THE COIMBATORE CITY MUNICIPAL CORPORATION ACT.
NOTIFICATION-I.
[G.O. Ms. No. 169, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (Election), 4th November 2011.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/439(e-1)/2011.
 In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 6 of the Coimbatore City Municipal Corporation Act, 1981
(Tamil Nadu Act 25 of 1981), read with Section 8 of the Tiruppur City Municipal Corporation Act, 2008 (Tamil Nadu Act 7 of 2008),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby constitutes the following six Standing Committees for the purpose specified in
sub-section (1) of Section 6 of the said Tamil Nadu Act 25 of 1981, namely:—
(1) Standing Committee on Accounts;
(2) Standing Committee on Education;
(3) Standing Committee on Public Health;
(4) Standing Committee on Taxation and Finance;
(5) Standing Committee on Town Planning; and
(6) Standing Committee on Works.
