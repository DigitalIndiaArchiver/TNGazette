HOME, PROHIBITION AND EXCISE DEPARTMENT
AMENDMENTS TO THE NOTIFICATIONS UNDER THE TAMIL NADU PROHIBITION ACT, 1937.
[G.O. Ms. No. 18, Home, Prohibition and Excise (III), 7th March 2022, ñ£C 23, Ãyt, ÂUtŸSt® M©L-2053.]
 No. II(1)/HOPE/18(f-1)/2022.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of section 18-B of the Tamil Nadu Prohibition Act, 1937 
(Tamil Nadu Act X of 1937), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Prohibition 
and Excise Department Notification No.II(1)/PE/239(d)/81, published at page 2 of Part II—Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu 
Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 1st May 1981, as subsequently amended.
Amendments
In the said Notification, in the TABLE, under the entry “Indian Made Foreign Spirits (brand names of which are 
specified in the Annexure) other than sacramental wine”,—
(1)	 for the expression "Rs.174.00 (Rupees one hundred and seventy four only) per proof litre" in column (2) against 
the entry under the heading “(i) ORDINARY BRANDS” in column (1), the expression "Rs.198.00 (Rupees one hundred 
and ninety eight only) per proof litre" shall be substituted;
(2) for the expression "Rs.214.00 (Rupees two hundred and  fourteen only) per proof litre" in column (2) against 
the entry under the heading “(ii) MEDIUM BRANDS” in column (1), the expression “Rs.262.00 (Rupees two hundred 
and sixty two only) per proof litre” shall be substituted;
(3)	  under the  heading  “(iii) PREMIUM BRANDS” in column (1), 
	
(i)	
for the expression "Rs.239.00 (Rupees two hundred and thirty nine only) per proof litre" in column (2), 
the expression “Rs.287.00 (Rupees two hundred and eighty seven only) per proof litre” shall be substituted;
	
(ii)	
for the expression "Rs.269.00 (Rupees two hundred and sixty nine only) per proof litre" in column (2), 
the expression “Rs.317.00 (Rupees three hundred and seventeen only) per proof litre” shall be substituted.

 No. II(1)/HOPE/18(f-2)/2022.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of section 18-B of the Tamil Nadu Prohibition Act, 1937 
(Tamil Nadu Act X of 1937), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Prohibition 
and Excise Department Notification No.II(1)/PE/239(d)/81, published at page 2 of Part II—Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu 
Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 1st May 1981, as subsequently amended.
Amendment.
In the said Notification, in the TABLE, for the expression “Rs.11.00 (Rupees eleven only) per bulk litre” in 
column (2) against the entry “Beer” in column (1), the expression “Rs.15.00 (Rupees fifteen only) per bulk litre” shall 
be substituted.
