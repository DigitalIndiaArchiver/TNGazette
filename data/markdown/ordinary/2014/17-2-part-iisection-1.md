Notifications  under the Tamil  Nadu Agricultural Produce Marketing  (Regulation) Act.
[G.O.Ms.No.7, Agriculture (AM 2), 9th January 2012.]
No.II(1)/AG/9/2014.-In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (a) of sub-section (1) of Section 9 of the Tamil Nadu
Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) Act, 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 1989), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby excludes
the notified market areas of Dharapuram, Vellakovil, Kunnathur, Kangayam, Mulanur, Alangiam and Muthur from the Notified
Areas of Erode Market Committee and Notified Market Areas of Tiruppur, Avinashi, Sevur, Palladam, Pethamppampatti,
Udumalaipettai, Madathukulam and Pongalur from the Notified Areas of Coimbatore Market Committee, the intention to make
such an exclusion have been previously published as required by Section 3 of the said Act.
NOTIFICATION – II
[G.O.Ms.No.7, Agriculture (AM 2), 9th January 2012.]
No.II(1)/AG/10/2014.-In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section  4 of the Tamil Nadu Agricultural
Produce Marketing (Regulation) Act, 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 1989), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby declares the
Tiruppur District to be notified area for the purpose of the said Act in respect of Paddy, Cholam (Jowar) in all forms, Cumbu
(Bajra) in all forms, Ragi in all forms, Maize in all forms, Groundnut (Pods or Kernals) (whole or split), Gingelly or
Sesamum seeds or Ellu, Castor (pods or seeds), Sunflower seeds or Kernals, Cocount in all forms except Tender
Cocount, Cotton (Kapas, lint, waste,) Tobacco in all forms, Chillies or Red Chillies, Turmeric in all forms (bulb or finger),
Sugarcane, Jaggery in all forms (Jaggery powder, brownsugar, etc.), palmgur Jaggery in all forms in the Notified Market
Areas of Dharapuram, Vellakovil, Kunnathur, Kangayam, Mulanur, Alangiam and Muthr Regulated Markets in Tiruppur
District and Cotton (Kapas, lint, Waste), Groundnut (pods and kernals) (whole or split), Tobacco in all forms, Turmeric
in all forms (bulb or finger), Maize in all forms, Sugarcane, jaggery in all forms (Jaggery powder, brown sugar, etc.), Paddy,
Chillies or red Chillies, Cholam (Jowar) in all forms, Cumbu (Bajra) in all forms, Ragi in all forms, Gingelly or Sesamum
seeds or Ellu, Cocount in all forms except Tender Cocount, Onion, Arecanut (whole or splits) in the Notified Market Areas
of Tiruppur, Avinashi, Sevur, Palladam, Pethappampatti, Udumalaipettai, Madathukkulam and Pongalur Regulated Markets
in Tiruppur District, the intention to make such declaration having been previously published as required by Section 3 of
the said Act.
NOTIFICATION – III
[G.O.Ms.No.7, Agriculture (AM 2), 9th January 2012.]
No.II(1)/AG/11/2014.-In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Agricultural
Produce Marketing (Regulation) Act, 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 1989), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby establish a Market
Committee for the Tiruppur District comprising the Notified Market Area of Dharapuram, Vellakovil, Kunnathur, Kangayam
Mulanur, Alangiam, Muthur, Tiruppur, Avinashi, Sevur, Palladam, Pethappampatti, Udumalaipettai, Madathukulam and Pongalur
Regulated Markets with effect on and from the 9-1-2012 and the said Market Committee shall be known as Tiruppur Market
Committee.
NOTIFICATION – IV
[G.O.Ms.No.7, Agriculture (AM 2), 9th January 2012.]
No.II(1)/AG/12/2014.-In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Agricultural
Produce Marketing (Regulation) Act, 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 1989), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby establish a Market
Committee for the Coimbatore District comprising the Notified Market Area of Coimbatore, Sulur, Thondamuthur, Pollachi,
Kinathukadavu, Anaimalai, Senjeri, Annur, Karamadai and Negamam Regulated Markets with effect on and from the
9.1.2012 and the said Market Committee shall be known as Coimbatore Market Committee.
NOTIFICATION – V
[G.O.Ms.No.7, Agriculture (AM 2), 9th January 2012.]
No.II(1)/AG/13/2014.-In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Agricultural
Produce Marketing (Regulation) Act, 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 1989), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby establish a Market
Committee for the Erode District comprising the Notified Market Area of Erode, Anthiyur, Avalpoondurai, Bhavani, Boothapadi,
Chittode, Elumathur, Gopichettipalayam, Kavunthappadi, Kodumudi, Mylambadi, Nambiyur, Perundurai, Punjai Puliyampatti,
Sathyamangalam, Sivagiri, Thalavadi and Vellankoil Regulated Markets with effect on and from the 9-1-2012 and the said Market
Committee shall be known as Erode Market Committee.
