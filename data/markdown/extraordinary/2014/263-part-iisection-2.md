EXEMPTION FROM THE PAYMENT OF VALUE ADDED TAX ON PURCHASE OF CERTAIN GOODS BY THE CANCER
INSTITUTE (WIA), CHENNAI UNDER THE TAMIL NADU VALUE ADDED TAX ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 95, Commercial Taxes and Registration (B-2), 12th December 2014,
Karthigai 26, Jaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2045.]
No. II(2)/CTR/751(d-1)/2014.
 In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-sections (1)  and (2) of Section 30 of the Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax Act, 2006
(Tamil Nadu Act 32 of 2006), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes an exemption in respect of tax payable under the said
Act by any dealer on the sale of the  goods specified in column (2) of the Table below, subject to the maximum value of the goods
specified in column (3) thereof, to the Cancer Institute (WIA), Chennai-600 020, subject to the following conditions, namely:-
(i) the dealer shall furnish a declaration in the following format duly filled in and signed by the said Institute, along with the
monthly return, to the assessing officer concerned;
(ii) the goods shall be used by the said Institute for the purpose of construction of the Diamond Jubilee Building for the
Cancer Institute (WIA), Chennai.

EXEMPTION FROM THE PAYMENT OF VALUE ADDED TAX ON PURCHASE OF CERTAIN GOODS BY THE
HINDU MISSION HOSPITAL, TAMBARAM, CHENNAI UNDER THE TAMIL NADU VALUE ADDED TAX ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 96, Commercial Taxes and Registration (B-2), 12th December 2014,
Karthigai 26, Jaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2045.]
No. II(2)/CTR/751(d-2)/2014.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-sections (1) and (2) of Section 30 of the Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax
Act, 2006 (Tamil Nadu Act 32 of 2006), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes an exemption in respect of tax payable
under the said Act by any dealer on the sale of the following goods to Tvl. Hindu Mission Hospital, Tambaram (West), Chennai-
600 045, for a period of two years, subject to the following conditions, namely:-
(i) the dealer shall furnish a declaration in the following format duly filled in and signed by the said hospital, along
with the monthly return, to the assessing officer concerned;
(ii) the goods purchased shall be for the bonafide use of running Tvl. Hindu Mission Hospital, Tambaram (West),
Chennai-600 045; and
(iii) the hospital shall not charge more than the cost of purchase of the  goods, and it should not be a source of
profit.
1. Drugs;
2. Oxygen;
3. X-ray films;
4. Surgical equipments; and
5. Furniture and fixtures.
2. The exemption hereby made shall remain in force for two years from the date of publication of this Notification.
