No. II(2)/PWI/144(a)/2009.
Whereas, the Government of Tamil Nadu having been
satisfied that the land specified in the Schedule below
have to be withdrawn from the land acquisition, which was
required for the public purpose to wit for the construction
of Public Works Department office and staff quarters under
the  Shenbagathoppu Reservoir Project at Padavedu Village
of Polur Taluk, Thiruvannamalai District as the scheduled
land is not required at present  for the said  purpose;
Now,  therefore, in  exercise of the powers conferred
under sub-section (1) of  Section 48 of  the Land Acquisition
Act, 1894 (Central Act I of 1894), the Governor of
Tamil Nadu  hereby withdraws from the acquisition of land
specified in  the Schedule below, in respect of which,
Public Works Department Notifications No.II(2)/PWI/58/2000
was published at page 21 of  Part II—Section 2 (Supplement)
of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the
12th January 2000 and No. II(2)/PW/150(e-2)/2000 was
published at pages 2 and 3 of Part II—Section 2 of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary,  dated  the
7th February 2000, under sub-section(1) of Section 4 and
Section 6 of the said Act, respectively, as it  is not required
for the public purpose, to wit, for the construction of Public
Works Department office and staff quarters under
Shenbagathoppu Reservoir Project at Padavedu Village of
Polur Taluk, Thiruvannamalai District.

and west by Reserved Forest—0.04.5 hectare (0.11 cents)
only out of 0.99.5 hectare.
