No. VI(2)/610(a)/2011.
Whereas, the ordinary election to fill up the office of the Ward Member of Ward No. 16 of Avinashi Town
Panchayat, Tiruppur District, was inter-alia, notified to be held in accordance with the programme of election fixed
in the notification of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission published with S.O. No. 45/2011/TNSEC/ME-1, dated
21st September 2011;
Whereas, the District Election Officer/District Collector of Tiruppur and the Returning Officer/Executive Officer,
Avinashi Town Panchayat have reported that due to the death of candidate of the respective Town Panchayat, the
election to fill up the said office has been postponed as per rule 34(1)(c) of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats,
Third Grade Municipalities, Municipalities and Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006.
Whereas, after taking all material circumstances into account and in pursuance of rule 34 of Tamil Nadu Town
Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities, Municipalities and Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006, the Tamil Nadu
State Election Commission has considered necessary to commence the election process afresh to the respective
ward of the said Town Panchayat.
Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (1) of Section 43-B of the Tamil Nadu
District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920) and rule 22 of Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Third Grade
Municipalities,  Municipalities and Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission,
