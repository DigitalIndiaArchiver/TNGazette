The Secretary, Tamil Nadu Lokayukta has declared that the vacation for the Tamil Nadu Lokayukta for the year 2021 will be 
observed from 01.05.2021 to 31.05.2021 (both days inclusive). 
During the vacation period, the Registry and other offices of the Tamil Nadu Lokayukta will function on all the working days as 
other Government offices are functioning. To meet the Urgent work, necessary arrangement has been made.
