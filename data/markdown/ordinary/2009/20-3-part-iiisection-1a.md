COMMERCIAL TAXES AND REGISTRATION DEPARTMENT
Amendment to Indian Stamp Rules for the Supply and Distribution of Stamps.
[G.O. Ms. No. 45, Commercial Taxes and Registration (J), 7th May 2009, Chithirai 24, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. SRO A-13/2009.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 74 of the Indian Stamp Act,
1899 (Central Act II of 1899), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Rules for the
Supply and Distribution of Stamps:—
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in rule 37, in clause (iv), after sub-clause (b), the following sub-clause shall be added, namely:—
“(c) The rate of discount for electronically generated Stamp Certificates issued by the Stock Holding Corporation
of India Limited shall be Rs. 0.65 per cent”.
RAJEEV RANJAN,
Secretary to Government.
