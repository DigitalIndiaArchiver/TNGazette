No. II(2)/FIN/322(d)/2019.
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (bb) of Section 16 of the Tamil Nadu Transparency 
in Tenders Act, 1998 (Tamil Nadu Act 43 of 1998), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby declares to 
entrust the work of Market Information System Platform Development to International Crops Research 
Institute for the Semi-Arid Tropics (ICRISAT) and also to engage ICRISAT as technical consultant 
for the implementation of Supply Chain Management Project on Public Private Partnership mode in 
the selected districts under D3Si initiatives of National Institution for Transforming India (NITI) Aayog 
from the component of Integrated Information Technology Platform and other components of Supply 
Chain Management Project.
    
K. SHANMUGAM,
