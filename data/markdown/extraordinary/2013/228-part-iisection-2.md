The Government having been satisfied that the land specified in the schedule below has to be acquired for
public purpose and it having already been decided that the amount is to be paid out of public revenue towards
compensation to be awarded for the land. So Under Section 6 of the Land Acquisition Act 1894 (Central Act
I of 1894) the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby declares that the land specified in the schedule below and
measuring 0.81.0 hectares  or 2.00 acres to be same, a little more or less is needed for a public purpose to
wit, for taking up the Establishment of 110/11 KV Sub-station by the Tamil Nadu Electricity Board.
A plan of the Land is kept in the Office of the Revenue Divisional Officer, Gudalur Division and may be
inspected at any time during Office hours.
