LOSS OF CERTIFICATES
It is hereby notified that the Original Higher Secondary Certificates / Matriculation Certificates / Diploma Certificates / School
Certificates of the following pupils are reported to have been lost beyond recovery.  Necessary steps are being taken to issue
the duplicate of the said Certificates. If the Originals were to be found by anybody, they should be treated as invalid and sent
to the Secretary, Board of Higher Secondary Examination / Directorate of Government Examinations, Chennai-6/ Director of
Technical Education, Chennai-25 / Inspector / Inspectress of Matriculation and Anglo-Indian Schools as the case may be for
cancellation:—
