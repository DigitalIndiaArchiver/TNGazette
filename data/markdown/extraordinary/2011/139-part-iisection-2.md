No.II(2)/HOU/216(d)/2011.
Whereas, it appears to the Government of Tamil Nadu that
the Lands specified in. the schedule below and situated in
No. 4 Madipakkam I Village, Sozhinganallur Taluk and
Adambakkam Village, Alandur Taluk and Town,
Kancheepuram District are needed for a public purpose to wit
for the implementation of the Mass Rapid Transit System R.T.S.
Station for Puzhuthivakkam between Velachery to St.Thomas
Mount, notice to that effect is here by given to all to whom it may
concern in accordance with the provisions of sub-section (I) of
Section 4 of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894 (Central Act I of
1894). And Whereas, it has become necessary to acquire the
immediate possession of the Lands specified in the Schedule
below, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that the lands
be acquired under the provisions of sub-section (1) of the
Section 17 of the said Act.
Now, therefore in exercise of the powers conferred by
sub-section (2) of Section 4 of the said Act, the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby authorizes the Special Tahsildar
(Land Acquisition), Mass Rapid Transit System, Phase-II,
Extension at Thirumailai Railway Station, Chennai-4 his staff
and workman to exercise the powers conferred by the said
sub-sections.
Under Section 3 (c) of the Act, the Governor of Tamil Nadu,
hereby appoints the Special Tahsildar (Land Acquisition), Mass
Rapid Transit System, Phase-II, Extension Thirumailai Railway
Station, Chennai-4 to perform the functions of the Collector
under the said Act.
Under sub-section (4) of Section 17 of the said Act, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that in view of the
urgency of the case, the provisions of Section 5A of the said
Act, shall not apply in this case.
