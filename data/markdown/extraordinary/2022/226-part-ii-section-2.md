NOTIFICATIONS BY GOVERNMENT
RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND PANCHAYAT RAJ DEPARTMENT
REMOVEL OF CHAIRMAN OF AYOTHIYAPATTINAM PANCHAYAT UNION COUNICIL, 
SALEM DISTRICT UNDER THE TAMIL NADU PANCHAYATS ACT, 1994.
[G.O. Ms. No. 54, Rural Development and Panchayat Raj (PR-4), 18th April 2022, 
 No. II(2)/RDPR/243(c)/2022.
WHEREAS, a written notice of the intention to make a motion expressing want of confidence in 
Tmt.M.Parvathi Mani, Chairman, Ayothiyapattinam Panchayat Union Council, Salem district, signed by ﬁ fteen members, 
out of the sanctioned strength of nineteen members of the said Council, together with a copy of the motion proposed to 
be made and a written statement of charges against the said Chairman have been delivered to the Revenue Divisional 
Oﬃ  cer, Salem, Salem district in person by two members as per sub-section (2) of Section 212 of the Tamil Nadu 
Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994);
AND WHEREAS, the Revenue Divisional Oﬃ  cer, Salem, Salem district caused a copy of the statement of charges 
along with a copy of the notice of motion to be delivered, to the said Chairman Tmt.M.Parvathi Mani and she submitted 
her reply to the charges;
AND WHEREAS, a motion expressing want of conﬁ dence in Tmt.M.Parvathi Mani, Chairman, Ayothiyapattinam 
Panchayat Union Council, Salem district had been carried with a support of sixteen members out of the sanctioned 
strength of nineteen members of the said Panchayat Union Council in the meeting convened by the Revenue Divisional 
Oﬃ  cer, Salem, Salem district on the 28th February 2022 after following the procedures speciﬁ ed in the said section 212 
and forwarded the same to Government;
NOW THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (13) of Section 212 of  the Tamil Nadu 
Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby removes Tmt.M.Parvathi Mani, 
Chairman, Ayothiyapattinam Panchayat Union Council, Salem district from the oﬃ  ce of the Chairman of the said Panchayat 
Union Council with immediate eﬀ ect.
   
P. AMUDHA,
