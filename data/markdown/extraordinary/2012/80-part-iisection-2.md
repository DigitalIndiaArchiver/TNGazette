COMMERCIAL TAXES AND REGISTRATION DEPARTMENT
NOTIFICATION UNDER THE INDIAN STAMP ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 46, Commercial Taxes and Registration (J1), 27th March 2012, Panguni 14, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2043]
No. II(2)/CTR/173(g)/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (a) of sub-section (1) of Section 9 of the Indian Stamp Act, 1899 (Central Act
II of 1899), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Commercial Taxes Department Notification
No.II(2)/CT/779(d-1)/2003, published at page 1 of Part II-Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, Extraordinary, dated
the 21st November 2003.
2. The amendment hereby made shall come into force on the 1st April 2012.
AMENDMENT.
In the said Notification, for the expression “six rupees”,  the expression “five rupees” shall be substituted.
SUNIL PALIWAL,
Secretary to Government.
