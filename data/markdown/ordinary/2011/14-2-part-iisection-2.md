No. II(2)/HWMP/170/2011.—The Governor of Tamil Nadu
having been satisfied that the lands specified in the Schedule
below have to be acquired for Highways purpose to wit for
providing approach road to the Bridge at Melpatampakkam
Village and it having already been decided that the entire
amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be
paid out of the funds controlled or managed by Divisional
Engineer, Tamil Nadu Sugarcane Road Development Project,
Cuddalore. The following notice is issued under
sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways
Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu
Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002), the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands specified in the
Schedule below and measuring to an extent 0.30.0 hectare in
Melpattampakkam Village to the same, a little more or less
required, for providing approach road to the bridge at
Melpattampakkam Village, Panruti Taluk at Cuddalore District.
The plan of the land is kept in the office of the Revenue
Diviional Officer, Cuddalore and may be inspected at any
time during office hours.
