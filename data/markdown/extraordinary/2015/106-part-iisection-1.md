LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY SECRETARIAT
VACATION OF SEAT IN TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY DUE TO RESIGNATION OF
THIRU P. VETRIVEL, MEMBER OF THE TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY.
[Letter No. 6424/2015-1, TNLAS (B-III), 17th May 2015]
No. II(1)/LAS/13(b)/2015.
Whereas, Thiru P. Vetrivel, a Member of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly representing the
11. Dr. Radhakrishnan Nagar Assembly Constituency, in his letter dated 17th May 2015 addressed to me as
Speaker of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly, has resigned his seat as Member of the said Assembly with
effect from 17th May 2015.
Now, therefore, I, P. DHANAPAL, Speaker of the said Assembly hereby accept the resignation of
Thiru P. Vetrivel, as envisaged under Rule 22 of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Rules as it is in accordance
with Rule 21 of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Rules. Under Article 190 (3)(b) of the Constitution of India,
his seat has, therefore, become vacant with effect from the above date.
Secretariat,
P. DHANAPAL,
Chennai-600 009,
Speaker,
Dated 17th May 2015.
Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly.
A.M.P. JAMALUDEEN,
Secretary,
  Legislative Assembly Secretariat.
