Election of Six Members to the Syndicate and Five Members to the Academic Council
from among the Members of the Senate.
[No. V.1/ELSEN 2012/02-2012/043.]
No. VI-3(b)/4/2012.
It is hereby notified that the election to [i] THE SYNDICATE and [ii] THE ACADEMIC COUNCIL will be held under
Section 18[a]-Other Members sub-section [1] and Section 23[a] - Other Members, sub-section [1] of the Madras University
Act, 1923 respectively.
[i]
To the syndicate [Section 18(a)-Other Members Sub-Section(1)] Six Members to be elected by the Senate from
among its members.
The newly elected members will hold office as Member of the Syndicate for a period of three years from the date
of receipt  of nomination/the date of withdrawal/the date of vacancies arises/the date of election/the date of declaration
of results whichever is applicable for the election.
[ii] [a] To the Academic Council [Section 23(a)-Other Members sub-section(1) Two Members to be clected by the
Senate from among its members who are not engaged in teaching for the full Term of three years.
The newly elected members will hold office as Member of the Academic Council for a period of three years from
the date of receipt  of nomination/the date of withdrawal/the date of election/the date of declaration of results, as the
case may be, whichever is applicable for the election.
[ii] [b] To the Academic Council [Section 23(a)-Other Members sub-section (1) Three Members to be elected by
the Senate from among its members who are not engaged in teaching for the Residual Period upto 25-02-2013.
The newly elected members will hold office as Member of the Academic Council for the residual period upto
25-02-2013 from the date of receipt of nomination/the date of withdrawal/the date of election/the date of declaration of
results, as the case may be, whichever is applicable for the election.
The elections will be held in accordance with the Statutes of Chapter XIX, Volume-1, University Calendar 2001.
The above elections will be conducted on the date of the meeting of the Senate scheduled to be held on Friday,
the 30th March 2012.
Nomination forms can be had from the Registrar in-charge, University of Madras on requisition and the filled in
Nomination Forms shall be sent in an envelope, duly superscribed, addressed to the Registrar-in-Charge by name
[DR. T. LEO ALEXANDER] as per the below furnished programme fixed for the said elections.
