Read:
From the Registrar General, High Court, Madras, 
D.O. Letter No.6566/2015/RG-B2, Date 02-08-2019.
Order: No. 391, Public (Special-A), 16th August 2019.
The Government have considered the recommendation of 
the High Court, Madras to depute Thiru G.Sendil Kumar, District 
Judge of Puducherry Judicial Service, functioning as I Additional 
District Judge, Cuddalore in the Tamil Nadu State Judicial 
Service on deputation basis has been recommended to be 
transferred to Puducherry Judicial Service.
2. The Government accordingly direct that Thiru 
G.Sendil Kumar, District Judge of Puducherry Judicial 
Service, 
functioning 
as 
I 
Additional 
District 
Judge, 
Cuddalore in the Tamil Nadu State Judicial Service on 
Deputation basis be repatriated to his parent department viz., 
Puducherry Judicial Service.
