The following Members of the Tamil Nadu State Police Service and the Tamil Nadu State Police Subordinate 
Service have been selected to receive the award of “Gandhi Adigal Police Medal” for the year 2017 on the 
occasion of Republic day, 2018 for their outstanding work in curbing illicit liquor. They would also be paid a 
cash prize of Rs.40,000/- (Rupees Forty Thousand only) each:-
(i) 
Thiru. S. Kannan, 
Additional Superintendent of Police, 
Prohibition Enforcement Wing, Thanjavur District.
(ii) 
Thiru. G. Ramakrishnan, 
Sub-Inspector of Police, 
Central Investigation Unit, Salem Zone.
(iii) Thiru. G. Narayanan, 
Head Constable 668, 
Ponnai Police Station, Vellore District.
(iv) Thiru. I. Joseph, 
Head Constable 1798, 
Prohibition Enforcement Wing, Villupuram District.
(v) Thiru. G. Narayanan,
Grade I Police Constable 36042,
S-9, Pazhavanthangal Police Station, 
 
Greater Chennai Police.
