DTP—II-2 Ex. (89)
MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION AND WATER SUPPLY DEPARTMENT
APPOINTMENT OF CERTAIN OFFICIAL AS ENGINEERING DIRECTOR OF THE TAMIL NADU WATER
SUPPLY AND DRAINAGE BOARD UNDER THE TAMIL NADU WATER SUPPLY AND DRAINAGE
BOARD  ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 55, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (WS3), 1st April 2009.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/152(b)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of  Section 4 of the Tamil Nadu Water Supply and
Drainage Board Act, 1970 (Tamil Nadu Act 4 of 1971), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints
Thiru A. Vivekanandam, Chief Engineer, Tamil Nadu Water Supply and Drainage Board, Northern Region,
Vellore as the Engineering Director of the Tamil Nadu Water Supply and Drainage Board, with effect on and
from the 1st April 2009.
NIRANJAN MARDI,
 Secretary to Government.
