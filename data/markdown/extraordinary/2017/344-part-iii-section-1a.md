In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 38 of the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory 
Education Act, 2009 (Central Act 35 of 2009), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the 
Tamil Nadu Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Rules, 2011. 
The amendments hereby made shall come into force on the date of their publication in the Ofﬁ cial Gazette.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules in Part-VI,
 (1) in rule 18, after sub-rule (c) the following sub-rule shall be added namely:-
“(d) To evaluate the performance of pupil with reference to the deﬁ ned learning outcomes”.
(2) in rule 20,-
     (a) in sub-rule 1 for clause (c), the following clause shall be substituted namely:-
“(c) prepare guidelines for putting into practice continuous and comprehensive evaluation to achieve the 
deﬁ ned learning outcomes”.
(b) after  clause (f), the following clause shall be added, namely:-
“(g) prepare class-wise, subject-wise learning outcomes for all elementary classes”.
