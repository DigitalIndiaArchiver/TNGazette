No. SRO B-15/2020.— In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, 
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Engineering 
Subordinate Service (Section 8 in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 2016):-
Amendments.
In the said Special Rules, in Part II, under the heading “Branch I - Engineering  Branch”,- 
(1) in rule 2, in sub-rule (a),- 
	
(i) 	 for the entries in column (2) against the entries under category 4(a),  “(iii) Junior Draughting Officer” in column 
(1) thereof, the following entries  shall be substituted, namely:- 
	
	
"(i) by direct recruitment; or 
	
	
(ii) by promotion from Assistant Draughtsman; or 
	
	
(iii) by promotion from the category of Work Inspector in Branch XII; or 
	
	
(iv) by promotion from the category of Irrigation Inspector in Branch Ill: 
Provided that fifty percent (50%) of the substantive vacancies arising  in the post of Junior Draughting Officer during a 
year shall be filled up by  direct recruitment through Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission and the  remaining fifty percent 
(50%) of the vacancies shall be filled up by other  methods as follows:- 
	
	
	
(a) by promotion from Assistant Draughtsman 	
40% 
	
	
	
(b) by promotion from Work Inspector	
	
8% 
	
	
	
(c) by promotion from Irrigation Inspector	 	
2% 
Provided further that if, sufficient number of qualified and suitable  persons are not available for appointment by any one 
of the methods  mentioned above, the unfilled vacancies shall be filled up by the method of  direct recruitment”; 
(ii) 	 for the entries, in column (2) against the entries under category 6,  “Assistant Draughtsman” in column (1) thereof, 
the following entries shall  be substituted, namely:- 
	
"(i) 	 by direct recruitment; or 
	
(ii) 	 by promotion or transfer from any other category; or 
	
(iii) 	 by recruitment by transfer from any other service: 
Provided that seventy percent (70%) of the substantive vacancies  arising in the post of Assistant Draughtsman during a 
year shall be filled  up by direct recruitment through Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission  and the remaining thirty percent 
(30%) of the vacancies shall be filled up  by other methods as follows:— 
	
	
	
(a) 	
by promotion or transfer from any category 	
20% 
	
	
	
(b) 	
by recruitment by transfer from any other service 	
10% 
Provided further that if sufficient number of qualified and suitable  persons are not available for appointment by any one 
of the methods  mentioned above, the unfilled vacancies shall be filled up by the method of  direct recruitment”.
