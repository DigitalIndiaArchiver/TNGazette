AMENDMENT TO NOTIFICATION
No. II(2)/PULO/444(a)/2011.
In exercise of  the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Commissions of Inquiry Act, 1952
(Central Act LX of 1952), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Public (Law
and Order-F) Department Notification No.ll(2)/PULO/396 (K) / 2011, published at pages 1-2 of Part ll—Section 2
of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 13th September 2011.
AMENDMENT
In the said Notification, in paragraph 4, for the expression "two months", the expression "five months" shall be
substituted.
KUMAR JAYANT,
Secretary to Government.
