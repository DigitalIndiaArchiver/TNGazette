HOME DEPARTMENT
Amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Jail Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 1034, Home (Pri.I), 27th November 2013.]
No. SRO B-52/2013.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Jail Service.
2. The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come in to force on 10-08-2011.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules, in rule 7,-
1.  the existing rule shall be numbered as sub-rule (1); and
2.  after sub-rule (1) as so numbered, the following sub-rule shall be added, namely:-
“(2) No female shall be eligible for appointment by direct recruitment to the post of Jailor in Category 5 in Class-I.”
NIRANJAN MARDI,
Principal Secretary to Government.

No. SRO B-53/2013.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 read with Article 313 of the
constitution of India and of all other powers hereunto enabling, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following
amendments to Fundamental Rules.
2. The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 15th November 2007.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Fundamental Rules,
(i) In the rule 26, in sub-rule (d), in Note 6, in clause (b), for the expression “Additional Registrar of Co-operative
Societies (Credit)”, the expression “Additional Registrar of Co-operative Societies (Finance and Banking)” shall be
substituted;
(ii) In APPENDIX II-Part I,-
(a) Under the heading “Delegation-Fundamental Rule 47-Instruction 7”, in the Table, for the entry “Additional
Registrar of Co-operative Societies (Credit)” in column (2) against the entry “(4) Power to sanction the undertaking of work
and the acceptance of fees in connection with examination held by a University of other examining body (vide Note 2 to
Instructions 4)” in column (1), the entry “Additional Registrar of Co-operative Societies (Finance and Banking)” shall be
substituted;
(b) Under the heading “Delegations under Rule 49”, in clause (31), in sub-clause (ii), for the expression “The
Additional Registrar of Co-operative Societies (Credit)” the expression.  “The Additional Registrar of Co-operative Societies
(Finance and Banking)” shall be substituted.
ANITA PRAVEEN.
Princial Secretary to Government.
