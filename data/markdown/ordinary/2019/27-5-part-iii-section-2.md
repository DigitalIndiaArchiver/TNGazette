V.O. CHIDAMBARANAR PORT TRUST, TUTICORIN
RECRUITMENT RULES FOR THE POST OF SENIOR ELECTRICIAN
GRADE-I & SENIOR MECHANIC (CLASS III)
Amendment to the Schedule II of Tuticorin Port Trust Employees (Recruitment, Seniority and Promotion 
Regulations, 2017.
(MEE-CMEEL-EST-UNION-VI-18)
(No.S-3/2/2019-E.I./D.1251) 
No. SRO-C-7/2019.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (i) of Section 124, read with sub-section (1)(a) of the Major Port 
Trusts Act, 1963 (38 of 1963) the Board of Trustees V.O. Chidambaranar Port Trust hereby approves the revised manner 
of recruitment (Recruitment Rules) for Class-III posts under Regulation 5 of Tuticorin Port Trust Employees (Recruitment, 
Seniority and Promotion) Regulations, 2008 as set out in the Schedule annexed to this Notiﬁ cation.
2. 
The said Recruitment Rules shall come into eﬀ ect from the date of publication of this Notiﬁ cation in the 
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
Sඋඐඍඌඝඔඍ
(1)  In the said Schedule under Regulations, in the Recruitment Rules for the post of Senior Electrician Grade-I, 
(Mechanical & Electrical Engineering Department), (Sl.No.129), the provisions under Column No. 12 shall be 
substituted by the following:-
Promotion:
Senior Electrician with ﬁ ve years regular service in the grade under the scheme of skilled scales subject to 
passing the Trade Test prescribed and conducted by the port, failing which Senior Electrician with 10 years 
regular service put together in the grades of Sr. Electrician and Electrician/Lineman/Auto Electrician in the 
port subject to passing the Trade Test prescribed and conducted by the port.
(2) In the said Schedule under Regulations, in the Recruitment Rules for the post of Senior Mechanic (Mechanical 
& Electrical Engineering Department) (Sl.No. 116), the provisions under Column No. 12, shall be substituted 
by the following:-
Promotion:
 Mechanic with five years regular service in the grade under the Scheme of Skilled Scales subject to
 
passing the trade test prescribed and conducted by the Port, failing which Blacksmith / Welder / Machinist /  
 
Tinker / Turner Fitter / Auto Electrician with ﬁ ve years regular service in the grade under the scheme of 
 
skilled scales subject to passing the trade test prescribed and conducted by the Port.
