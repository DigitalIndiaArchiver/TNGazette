The following Notification of the Government of India, Ministry of Law and Justice
(Department of Justice), Jaisalmer House, 26, Man Singh Road, New Delhi-110 011,
dated 15th December 2008 is republished:—
In exercise of the powers conferred by caluse (2) of Article 124 of the Constitution of India,
the President is pleased to appoint Shri Justice Ashok Kumar Ganguly, Chief Justice,
Madras High Court to be a Judge of the Supreme Court of India, with effect from the date he
assumes charge of his office.
