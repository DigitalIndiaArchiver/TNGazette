HOME DEPARTMENT
 Amendments to the Tamil Nadu State Legal Services Authority  Rules.
[G.O. Ms. No. 557, Home (Courts-IV), 22nd August  2014.]
No. SRO A-18/2014.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 28 of the Legal Services Authorities Act, 1987
(Central Act 39 of 1987), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, in consultation with the Chief Justice of the High Court of Judicature
at Madras, hereby makes the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu State Legal Services Authority Rules, 1997, as
subsequently amended:—
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules, in Rule 4, in sub-rule (b),-(1) for clause (i), the following clauses shall be substituted, namely:—
“(i) The Chairman, High Court Legal Services Committee, Chennai.
 (i-A)
The Advocate General, Chennai”.
(2)  in clause (xi), for the expression “Four Chairman”, the expression “Three Chairman” shall be substituted”.
APURVA VARMA,
                                                                Principal Secretary to Government.
