No. SRO A-29/2018.­—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) and clause (a) of sub-section (2) of 
Section 41 of the Protection of Human Rights Act, 1993 (Central Act 10 of 1994), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes 
the following  amendments to the Tamil Nadu State Human Rights Commission (Salaries and Allowances and other terms 
and conditions of service of Members) Rules, 1996:-
Amendments
In the said Rules,- 
 	
(1) In rule 3,
	
    (i) in sub-rule (a) for the expression ‘‘Rs. 90,000/- per month’’, the expression ‘‘Rs.2,50,000/- per 
month’’ shall be substituted;
	
    (ii) in sub-rules (b) and (c), for the expression ‘‘Rs. 80,000/- per month’’, the expression ‘‘Rs.2,25,000/- per 
month’’ shall be substituted;
	
(2) in rule 4, for the expression ‘‘Rs. 80,000/- per month and above’’, the expression ‘‘Rs. 2,25,000/- per month 
and above’’ shall be substituted;
	
(3) in rule 5,
	
	
(i) in sub-rule (b),-
	
	
	
(a) for the expression ‘‘Rs. 80,000/- per month and above’’, the expression ‘‘Rs. 2,25,000/- per month 
and above’’ shall be substituted;
	
	
	
(b) in sub-rule (b), as so amended, the following shall be added at the end, namely:-
	
‘‘Where a Judge does not avail himself of the use of an official residence, he may be paid every month an 
allowance equivalent to an amount of twenty-four per centum of the salary which shall be increased at the rate of :- 
	
	
	
(a) Twenty-seven per centum, when Dearness Allowance crosses twenty-five per centum; and
	
	
	
(b) Thirty-per centum, when Dearness Allowance crosses fifty per centum’’; and
	
	
(ii) in sub-rule (c), for the expressions ‘‘Rs. 7,500/- per month’’ and ‘‘Rs. 6,000/- per month’’ the expressions 
‘‘Rs.34,000/- per month’’ and ‘‘Rs.27,000/- per month’’ shall respectively be substituted.
	
P. SENTHILKUMAR,
Principal Secretary to Government (Fac).
