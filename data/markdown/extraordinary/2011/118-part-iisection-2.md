The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that
the land specified in the Schedule below have to be acquired
for a public purpose and it having already been decided that
the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the land
is to be paid out of public revenues, the following Declaration
is issued under Section 6 of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894
(Central Act I of 1894).
DECLARATION
Under Section 6 of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894 (Central
Act I of 1894), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby declares
