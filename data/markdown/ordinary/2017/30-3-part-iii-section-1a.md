In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (i) of sub-section (1) of Section 86 read with clause (a) of sub-section 
(2) of Section 347 of the Chennai City Municipal Corporation Act, 1919 (Tamil Nadu Act IV of 1919), the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Chennai Corporation Public Health and Medical Service Rules, 1969:-
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force with effect on and from the 5th November 2015.
AMENDMENTS

(1) in rule 2, in the tabular column, in columns (2) and (3) against the expression Class I-B, after the

following expressions shall, respectively, be added, namely:—
“Category 3” and “Health Education Ofﬁ cer.”;
