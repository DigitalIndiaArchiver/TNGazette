TAMIL NADU WAqF BOARD, CHENNAI
 Extension of Direct Management of Hazrath Syed Sha Shahabuddin and Hazrath Syed Sha Bahahuddin Dargah 
Waqf, Krishnagiri District.
(Rc. No. 5271/11/B2/K.Giri)
No. VI-3(a)/40/2018.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the waqf Act, 1995 as amended by Act 27/2013, 
the Period of Direct Management of Hazrath Syed Sha Shahabuddin and Hazrath Syed Sha Bahahuddin Dargah Waqf 
Krishnagiri District is extended for further period of one year from 26-08-2017 to 25-08-2018 or till the election is conducted 
whichever is earlier.
Chennai-600 001,	
Dr. Darez ahmed,
28th March 2018.                                                                                                                        Chief Executive Officer (FAC),
