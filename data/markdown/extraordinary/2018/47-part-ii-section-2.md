WHEREAS the Hon’ble High Court of Madras has appointed Justice Thiru. E. Padmanabhan, Hon’ble Judge of 
Madras High Court (Retired) as an Arbitrator to adjudicate a dispute in relation to certain provisions of the 13th Wage 
Settlement signed between the Management and Trade Unions of State Transport Undertakings on 04-01-2018;
 AND WHEREAS the Hon’ble High Court of Madras directed the Government to Act as per Section 10-A of the Industrial 
Disputes Act, 1947 (Central Act 14 of 1947);
NOW, THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred by  sub-section (1) of Section 10-A of the Industrial Disputes 
Act, 1947 (Central Act 14 of 1947), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints Justice Thiru. E. Padmanabhan, Hon’ble 
Judge of Madras High Court (Retired) as an Arbitrator.
3. The terms of reference of the arbitration shall be as follows, namely:-
 (i)   Whether the demand of the workmen to have a multiplier factor of 2.57 instead of 2.44 is justiﬁ ed;
 (ii)   If so, the date from which it should be applied.
4. The Arbitrator shall submit the report to the Hon’ble Madras High Court, within one month from the date of publication 
of the Notiﬁ cation under Section 10-A(3) of the Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 (Central Act 14 of 1947) as ordered by the 
Hon’ble Madras High Court.
 
5. The provisions of Section 10-A of the said Act shall apply to the arbitration.
