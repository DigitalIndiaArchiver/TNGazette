REVENUE  DEPARTMENT
Amendments to Special Rules  for the Tamil Nadu General Subordinate Service
[G.O. Ms. No. 42, Revenue (Ser. 8(2)), 2nd February 2009.]
No. SRO B-15/2009.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu General
Subordinate Service (Section 13 in Volume - III of the Tamil Nadu Service Manual, 1970).
2. The amendments hereby made shall come into force on the 2nd February 2009.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules, in Part II, in class XII,—
1.
in rule 2, in item (i) in column (2) against the entry: 9, Jeep Drivers engaged in General Revenue Administration
and in the Office of the Commissioner of Revenue Administration in column (1), after sub-item (b), the following
sub-item shall be added, namely:—
“(c) Masalchi qualified for the post of Driver, if no qualified and willing Office Assistant and Watchman are
available."
2.
In rule 3, in the TABLE, under category 9, after item (c), the following item shall be added, namely:—
"(d) if no qualified and willing Office Assistant and Watchman are available, appointment shall be made from
the list of qualified and willing Masalchi who are on the time scale of pay equivalent to the post of Office Assistant and
Watchman in Revenue Department, strictly on the basis of the date of their regularisation of service in the cadre of
Masalchi."
 K. DEENABANDU,
Principal Secretary to Government.
