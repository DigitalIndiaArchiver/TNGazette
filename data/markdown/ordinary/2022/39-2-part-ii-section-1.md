BACKWARD CLASSES, MOST BACKWARD CLASSES AND MINORITIES WELFARE DEPARTMENT
Amendment to the entry relating to Vannar Community classiﬁ ed as the List of Most Backward Classes under the 
Tamil Nadu Backward Classes, Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes (Reservation of seats in Educational 
Institutions and of appointments or posts in the services under the State) Act.
[G.O. Ms. No. 72, Backward Classes, Most Backward Classes and Minorities Welfare (BCC), 24th August 2022, 
Aavani 8, Subakiruthu, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2053.]
No.II(1)/BCMBCMW/39/2022.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Clause (a) of Section 3 of the 
Tamil Nadu Backward Classes, Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes (Reservation of seats in Educational 
Institutions and of appointments of posts in the Services under the State) Act, 1993 (Tamil Nadu Act 45 of 1994), 
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Backward Classes, Most Backward 
Classes and Minorities Welfare Department Notiﬁ cation No.II(1)/BCMBCMW/36(a)/2008, published at pages 1-9 of Part 
II-Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 5th September 2008:-
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖග
In the said Noiﬁ cation, in THE SCHEDULE, under the heading, LIST OF BACKWARD CLASSES, BACKWARD CLASS 
CHRISTIANS, BACKWARD CLASS MUSLIMS, MOST BACKWARDS CLASSES AND DENOTIFIED COMMUNITIES, 
under the sub-heading IV-List of Most Backward Classes, for Serial No.38 and the entry relating thereto, the following 
entry shall, be substituted, namely:-
''38 Vannar (including Agasa, Madivala, Ekali, Rajakula, Veluthadar and Rajaka) (except in Kanniyakumari District 
and Shencottah Taluk of Tenkasi District where the community is a Scheduled Caste)''
