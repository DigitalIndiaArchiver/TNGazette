the University Professors from among themselves.
[Notification No. (No. EIII(3)/28829/2015]
No. VI-3(b)/30/2015.
Notice is hereby given that an election by direct polling is to be held in the University premises for electing One member
to the Govering Council of The Tamil Nadu Dr.M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai, as per clause (c) of sub-section (4) of
Section 21 of The Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai, Act, 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 37 of 1987) by the University
Professors from among themselves to fill up the existing one vacancy. The term of membership in the Governing Council
will be for a period of three years from the date of publication of the results of the election (i.e. 05.10.2015) or the date
on which he/she ceases to be member of this University.
2. Forms of Nomination may be obtained from the Registrar, who is the Returning Officer, from 02.09.2015 to 14.09.2015
between 11.00 a.m. and 3.00 p.m. (on all working days).
3. The filled-in Nominaion paper may be sent through the enclosed enveloped addressed to the Registrar i/c / Returning
Officer and superscribing as "Nomination for election to the Governing Council" as indicated below, and sent by Registered
post (Acknowledgement Due), so as to be received by the REGISTRAR i/c / Returning Officer not later than 4.00 p.m. on
14.09.2015 or delivered to the Returning Officer during Office hours in person or by his/her Proposer or by messenger under
acknowledgement on or before the date and hour mentioned above.

The nomination papers which are not sent by Registered post with acknowledgment due or not delivered in person by the
nominee or by his proposer or by messenger as required above shall be declared as invalid.
4. Nomination papers will be taken up for scrutiny at 4.15 p.m. on 14.09.2015
5. List of valid nominations will be published at 5.00 p.m. on 14.09.2015 in the University premises.
6. The candidate may withdraw his/her candidature by notice in writing sent by Registered Post so as to reach the
Returning Officer or delivered to the Returning Officer or any other person duly authorized by him/her under acknowledgement
not later than 3.00 p.m. on 21.09.2015. The candidate, who has withdrawn his/her candidature, shall not be allowed to cancel
the withdrawal or to be re-nominated as a candidate for the same election.
7. List of final valild nominations will be published in the notice board of the University as well as in the University Website
at 5.00 p.m. on 21.09.2015. A Copy of the Final list of valid nominations will also be sent to the candidates concerned.
8. Polling will be conducted in the permises of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University at No. 69, Anna Salai,
Guindy, Chennai-600 032 between 10.00 a.m. and 3.00 p.m. on 05.10.2015.

Elections of Two Members to the Senate by the Academic Council.
(No. V.1/AC Election/08-2015/362A)
No. VI-3(b)/31/2015.
It is hereby notified that the election to THE SENATE will be held under Section 14(a) - Other Members [2] of the Madras
University Act, 1923.
The person getting elected will hold office as a member of the Senate for the residual period / full term (three years) from the
date of receipt of nomination / the withdrawal / the period of present member turn to be ceased / the date of election whichever
is applicable for the election, as the case may be.
[i] To the Senate - Section 14 [a] - Other Members [2]
Two Members to be elected by the Academic Council from among its members.
The above election will be conducted on the date of the meeting of the Academic Council scheduled to be held on
SATURDAY, the 26th September 2015.
