    GENERAL NOTIFICATION
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI
Taking over the Direct Management of Meer Bhas Khan Thaikkal Wakf, Sundaraperumal Koil Village,
Kumbakonam Taluk, Thanjavur District.
(Rc. No. 493/10/B7/TNJ.)
No. VI-3(a)/54/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65(1) of the Wakf Act, 1995, the
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in item No. 216/10 in Rc. No. 493/10/B11/TNJ, dated 29th December 2010
has ordered to take over the Management of Meer Bhas Khan Thaikkal, Sundaraperumal Koil Village, Kumbakonam
Taluk, Thanjavur District under the direct control of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of one year from the
date of publication in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette and to appoint the Superintendent of Wakfs, Thanjavur as
its Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,
TURAB AHMED,
3rd June 2011.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
