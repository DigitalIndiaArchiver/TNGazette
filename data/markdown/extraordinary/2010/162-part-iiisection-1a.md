DTP—III-1(a) Ex. (162)
MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION AND WATER SUPPLY DEPARTMENT
UPGRADATION OF KANGEYAM PANCHAYAT TOWN IN TIRUPPUR DISTRICT AS SECOND GRADE
MUNICIPALITY UNDER THE TAMIL NADU MUNICIPALITIES (NORMS FOR CLASSIFICATION OF MUNICIPALITIES)
RULES, 2008.
[G.O. Ms. No. 99, Municipal Administration and Water Supply  (M.A.5), 1st June 2010.]
No. SRO A-17(a)/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (6) of Section 4 of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920
(Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920), read with rule 3 of the Tamil Nadu Municipalities (Norms for Classification of Municipalities)
Rules, 2008, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby classifies the local areas comprised in Kangeyam Panchayat Town
in Tiruppur District, as Second Grade Municipality.
NIRANJAN MARDI,
Principal Secretary to Government.
