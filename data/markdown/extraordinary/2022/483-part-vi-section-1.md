Cheyyar Industrial Complex by SIPCOT in 95.Chozhavaram Village, Vembakkam Taluk of Tiruvannamalai District.
The plan of the land is kept in the office of the Special Tahsildar (LA) Unit– 3, SIPCOT Cheyyar Expansion Scheme,
