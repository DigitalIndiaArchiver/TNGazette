MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION AND WATER SUPPLY DEPARTMENT 
AMENDMENT TO THE TAMIL NADU MUNICIPAL CORPORATIONS CONSTITUTION OF STANDING 
COMMITTEES RULES, 1997
[G.O.Ms.No. 1, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (Election), 6th January 2022, 
Margazhi 22, Pilava, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2052.]
No. SRO A/H/2021
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of section 8 of the Kumbakonam City Municipal Corporation 
Ordinance, 2021 (Tamil Nadu Ordinance 7 of 2021), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby modifies the Tamil Nadu 
Municipal Corporations Constitution of Standing Committees Rules, 1997, by way of making the following amendment to 
the said Rules and also directs that the said Rules shall apply to the Kumbakonam City Municipal Corporation, subject 
to the modification hereby made:—
Amendment.
In the said Rules, in rule 2, for sub-rule (1), the following sub-rule shall be substituted, namely:—
	
“(1) In these rules, unless the context otherwise requires,-
	
(i) “Corporation” means the municipal corporation of Kumbakonam;
	
(ii) “Council” means the council of municipal corporation of Kumbakonam; and
	(iii) “Ordinance” means the Kumbakonam City Municipal Corporation Ordinance, 2021 (Tamil Nadu 
Ordinance  7 of 2021).”.
	
SHIV DAS MEENA,
 	
Additional Cheif Secretary to Government.
