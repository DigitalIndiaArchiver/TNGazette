CO-OPERATION, FOOD AND CONSUMER PROTECTION DEPARTMENT
Amendments to the Tamil Nadu Consumer Protection Rules, 1988.
[G.O. Ms. No.105, Co-operation, Food and Consumer Protection (H1), 12th August 2010.]
No.SRO A-34/2010.—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 30 of the Consumer Protection
Act, 1986 (Central Act 68 of 1986), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu
Consumer Protection Rules, 1988.
2.
The Amendments hereby made shall come into force on the 22nd day of October, 2008.
AMENDMENTS
(1) In the said Rules,—
(i) in rule 3, in sub-rule (i), for the portion commencing with the expression “Other members if sitting on whole
time basis” and ending with the expression “the conveyance allowance of Rs. 150 (Rupees one hundred and fifty only) per
month”, the following shall be substituted, namely:—
“The members of Independent District Fora shall receive a consolidated honorarium of Rs. 5,500 (Rupees five thousand
five hundred only) in addition to conveyance allowance of Rs.1000 (Rupees one thousand only) per month, and the members
of Combined District Fora shall receive a consolidated honorarium of Rs. 4,000 (Rupees four thousand only) in addition to
the conveyance allowance of Rs. 600 (Rupees six hundred only) per month”;
(2) in rule 7, In sub-rule (1), for the portion commencing with expression “Other members if sitting on whole time
basis” and ending  with the expression “the conveyance allowance of Rs.1,000 (Rupees one thousand only) per month”
the following shall be substituted namely:—
“The members of the State Commission, shall receive a consolidated honorarium of Rs. 8,500 (Rupees eight thousand
and five hundred only) per month, in addition to the conveyance allowance of Rs. 1,500 (Rupees one thousand and five
hundred only) per month”.
SWARAN SINGH,
Principal Secretary to Government.
