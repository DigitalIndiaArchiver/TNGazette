RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND PANCHAYAT RAJ DEPARTMENT
RESERVATION FOR THE OFFICES OF THE PRESIDENTS OF VILLAGE PANCHAYATS
UNDER TAMIL NADU PANCHAYATS ACT.
Amendment to Notification
[G.O. Ms. No. 93, Rural Development and Panchayat Raj (PR.1), 27th August 2009.]
No. II(2)/RDPR/447(c)/2009.
Under Section 57 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994), the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Rural Development and Panchayat Raj
Department Notification No. II(2)/RDPR/453(f-1)/2006, published at pages 1 to 144 of Part II—Section 2 of
the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary (Issue No. 228), dated the 2nd September 2006.
AMENDMENT
In the said Notification, under the heading “Reservation for Village Panchayat Presidents Name of the
District: Villupuram”, in the Tabular Column, under the sub-heading “6. KANAI PANCHAYAT UNION”,
in column (3), against the entry “Sl. No. 1” in column (1) and “Athiyurthirukkai” in column (2) thereof, for
the expression “ST General”, the expression “General” shall be substituted.
K. ASHOK VARDHAN SHETTY,
Principal Secretary to Government.
