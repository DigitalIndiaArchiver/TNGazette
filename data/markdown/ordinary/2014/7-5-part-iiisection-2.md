JUDICIAL NOTIFICATIONS
Draft Amendment to Rule 10-A of Order XXII of the Code of Civil Procedure.
(Roc. No. 322/2011/F1)
No. SRO C-4/2014.
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 122 of the Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, the High Court, Madras propose
to make the following amendment to Rule 10-A (inserted by the Civil Procedure Code (Amendment) Act, 1976-
Central Act 104of 1976) in Order XXII in the First Schedule of the Code and same are published hereunder, for information
of the public at large, in general, and all those stake-holders, whose interests, rights or claims may be or likely to be affected,
compromised or prejudiced, by virtue of such amendment in particular. Such stake-holders, if they are so desirous of putting
forth some or any valid suggestions or objections, may notify them, in writing, addressed to the undersigned, not later than a
period of 30 days from the date of issue of the initial publication of the amendment:—
In Rule 10-A of Order XXII in the First Schedule to the Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, between the words
“about it”, and  “and the court shall.........” the following sentence shall be included; “and he shall also furnish the details of the
legal representatives of the deceased, wherever possible.”
High Court, Madras,
P. KALAIYARASAN,
17th January 2014.
Registrar-General.
