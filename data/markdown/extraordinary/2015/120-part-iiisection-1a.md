RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND PANCHAYAT RAJ DEPARTMENT
AMENDMENT TO THE TAMIL NADU VILLAGE PANCHAYATS (REGULATION OF SINKING OF WELLS AND
SAFETY MEASURES) RULES, 2015.
[G.O.Ms. No.84, Rural Development and Panchayat Raj (SGS-3), 3rd June 2015.]
No. SRO A-10(a)/2015.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 242 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayat Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Tamil Nadu Village Panchayats (Regulation of sinking
of wells and safety measures) Rules, 2015.
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in rule 3, in sub-rule (1), of rule for the expression “Rs.5000/- (Rupees five thousand only)”,
the expression “Rs.100/- (Rupees one hundred only)” shall be substituted.
           GAGANDEEP SINGH BEDI,
Secretary to Government.
