WHEREAS, a written notice of the intention to make a motion expressing want of confidence in Tmt.M.Umamageswari, 
Chairman, Gugiliamparai Panchayat Union Council, Dindigul district, signed by eleven members, out of the sanctioned 
strength of thirteen members of the said Council, together with a copy of the motion proposed to be made and a 
written statement of charges against the said Chairman have been delivered to the Revenue Divisional Officer, Palani, 
Dindigul district in person by two members as per sub-section (2) of Section 212 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 
1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994);
AND WHEREAS, the Revenue Divisional Officer, Palani, Dindigul district caused a copy of the statement of charges 
along with a copy of the notice of motion to be delivered, to the said Chairman Tmt.M.Umamageswari and she submitted 
her reply to the charges;
AND WHEREAS, a motion expressing want of confidence in Tmt.M.Umamageswari, Chairman, Gugiliamparai Panchayat 
Union Council, Dindigul district had been carried with a support of eleven members out of the sanctioned strength of 
thirteen members of the said Panchayat Union Council in the meeting convened by the Revenue Divisional Officer, 
Palani, Dindigul district on the 28th January 2022 after following the procedures specified in the said Section 212 and 
forwarded the same to Government;
NOW THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (13) of Section 212 of  the Tamil Nadu 
Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby removes Tmt.M.Umamageswari, 
Chairman, Gugiliamparai Panchayat Union Council, Dindigul district from the office of the Chairman of the said Panchayat 
Union Council with immediate effect.
  	
P. AmuDha,
