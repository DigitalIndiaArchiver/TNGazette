TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI
Extension of period of Direct Management of Hazarath Syed Hussain Sha Khadiri Dargah and Mosque
@ Dimmasamy Dargah, Perambur, Chennai.
(Lr. Rc. No. 1040/05/Chennai/B1.)
No. VI-3(a)/134/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995, the Tamil Nadu
Wakf Board in its resolution in Item No.195/10 (Rc. No.1040/05/B1/Chennai), dated 5th October 2010 has ordered to
extend the period of direct Management of Hazarath Syed Hussain Sha Khadiri Dargah and Mosque @ Dimmasamy
Dargah, Perambur, Chennai for a further period of one year from 26th August 2010.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
29th October 2010.

Taking over the Direct Management of Hazarath Sherun Sha Khadiri Thaikkal, Pudupattinam,
Thanjavur District.
(Lr. Rc. No. 8537/B11/06/TNJ & 4993/04/B11/TNJ.)
No. VI-3(a)/135/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995, the Tamil Nadu
Wakf Board in its resolution in Item No.116/2007 in Rc. No. 8537/B11/06/TNJ & 4993/04/B11/TNJ and Item No. 110/07 in
WEA. 7/06/E5/TNJ, dated 6th October 2010 has ordered to take over the Management of Hazarath Sherun Sha Khadiri Thaikkal,
Pudupattinam, Thanjavur District under the direct control of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of one year from the date
of publication in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette  and to appoint the Superintendent of Wakfs, Thanjavur as its
Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
29th October 2010.

ADVERTISEMENT OF PETITION
Notice is hereby given that a petition for the winding up of the abovenamed company by the High Court at Madras
was on the 2nd day of March 2010 presented to the said Court by Messrs Gateway Terminals India Pvt. Ltd., GTI
House, JNPT, Nhava Sheva, Navi, Mumbai-400 707 in its capacity as a creditor of the abovenamed company and that
the said petition is directed to be heard before the Court on the 6th day of December 2010.
Any creditor, contributory or other person desirous of supporting or opposing the making of an order on the said
petition should send to the petitioner or his advocate notice of his intention signed by him or his advocate with his
name and address, so as to reach the petition or his advocate not later than 5 days before the date fixed for the
hearing of the petition, and appear at the hearing for the purpose in person or by his advocate. A copy of the petition
will be furnished by the undersigned to any creditor or contributory on payment of the prescribed charges for the same.
Any affidavit intended to be used in opposition to the petition should be filed in Court, and a copy served on the
petitioner or his advocate, not less than 5 days before the date fixed for the hearing.
M/s. V.P. RAMAN

Counsel for the petitioner.
