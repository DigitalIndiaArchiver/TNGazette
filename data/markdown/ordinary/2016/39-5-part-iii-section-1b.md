No. SRO B-62/2016.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following rules:--
The rules hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 27th December 1979.
RULES
The General and Special Rules applicable to the holders of the permanent post in the Tamil Nadu Medical Subordinate
Service shall apply to the holders of the temporary posts of Dialysis Technician Grade I and Grade II sanctioned from time
to time for the Government Teaching Medical Institutions, subject to the modifications specified in the following rules:—
2. Constitution:—The posts shall each constitute a separate category in a distinct class of the said service.
3. Appointment:—Appointment to the posts specified on column (1) of the Table below shall be made by the methods
specified in the corresponding entries in column (2) thereof:—

No. SRO B-64/2016.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Secretariat Service
(Section 29 in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1986):—
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 31st July 2015.
AMENDMENT
In the said Special Rules, in rule 14, in the TABLE, against the entry ''Category 3(a) Assistant in the Departments of
Secretariat other than the Law and the Finance Departments and the Governor's Secretariat” in column (1) and the
corresponding entry “(ii) Recruitment by transfer from among the holders of the posts of Assistants and Junior Assistants
in the Tamil Nadu Ministerial Service and the Tamil Nadu Judicial Ministerial Service” in column (2) thereof, for the entry
“(ii) Service for a period of not less than five years either in the category of Junior Assistant or in the category of Assistant
or in both the categories put together, in the Tamil Nadu Ministerial Service or in the Tamil Nadu Judicial Ministerial Service
on the first day of August of the year in which the notification inviting applications for appointment is issued “in column (3),
the entry” (ii) Service for a period of not less than three years after acquiring Bachelor's Degree, in the Category of Junior
Assistant or in the category of Assistant or in both the categories put together, in the Tamil Nadu Ministerial Service or in
the Tamil Nadu Judicial Ministerial Service on the first day of August of the year in which the notification inviting applications
for appointment is issued” shall be substituted.
