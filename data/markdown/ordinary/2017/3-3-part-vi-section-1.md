Society Limited, Thanjavur District.
(Rc. No.8950/2014/ SF)
No. VI(1)/20/2017.
Under the powers conferred to the Joint Registrar of Co-operative Societies in Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies
Act 1983 (Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act 30 of 1983) and G.O(2D) No.108, Co-operation, Food and Consumer
Protection Department, dated 31-08-2005, the Joint Registrar of Co-operative Societies, Thanjavur has ordered
disqualifying Thiru. Kovi. Saminathan, then President of Z.813. Perappadi Primary Agriculture Co-operative Credit Society

negligence in management of the affairs of the Z.813 Perappadi Primary Agriculture Co-operative Credit Society Ltd.,
under Section 36(1) of in Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act 1983 in his Order Rc.No.8950/2014 SF
Dated: 11-06-2015.

ADJOURNMENT OF CIVIL AND SESSIONS COURTS IN DINDIGUL DISTRICT FOR THE YEAR 2017
(D. No. 10425)
No. VI(1)/26/2017.
Adjournment of Civil and Sessions courts in Dindigul District for the year 2017 in pursuance of the directions
contained in the High Court’s Official Memorandum in Roc.No. 81073-A/2016/C3 dated 21.11.2016.
Notice is hereby given that the Civil and Sessions Courts in the Dindigul District will remain closed for
Summer Vacation for 2017 as follows:
The Principal District and Sessions Court, Dindigul.
The Additional District and Sessions Court, Dindigul.
The Principal Sub Court, Dindigul.
The Additional Sub Court, Dindigul.
The Sub Court, Palani.
The Sub Court, Vedasandur.
The Special Sub Court, to deal with MCOP Cases, Dindigul.
The Principal District Munsif Court, Dindigul.
The Additional District Munsif Court, Dindigul.
The District Munsif Court, Palani.
The District Munsif Court, Oddanchatram.
The District Munsif Court, Nilakkottai.
Will remain closed from Monday, the 1st day of May 2017 to Wednesday,  the 31st   day of May 2017
(both days inclusive) for Summer Vacation.  The said courts shall re-open on Thursday, the 1st  day of  June 2017.
2. The above mentioned courts will remain closed from Saturday, the 23rd day of September 2017 to Monday,
the 2nd day of October 2017 (both days inclusive), for Dussera Holidays.  The said courts shall re-open on Tuesday
the 3rd  day of October 2017.
3. The above mentioned courts will remain closed from  Saturday, the  23rd   day of December 2017 to Sunday,
the 31st day of December 2017 (both days inclusive) for Christmas Holidays.
4.The Office of the Court of Official Receiver, Dindigul will remain open throughout the Summer
Vacation 2017.
5. (i) No Plaints/Petitions except Applications for grant of copies and for service and execution of process  will
ordinarily be received during the vacation.
(ii) In case of Vacation Civil Judge is appointed by the Government for this Dindigul District, Urgent Plaints, Appeals
and Petitions in the Civil cases alone will be received and disposed off by the Vacation Sitting Judge in exercise of the
powers vested in him under Section 30 of Central Act III of 1973 as Amended by the Tamil Nadu Act  XVII of 1950.
6. The Office of  the Copying Establishment, Process Service Establishment, will be kept open throughout the vacation.
No arrest warrants will be executed during the vacation and Central Record Room will also be kept open.
7. The Office of other sections will remain closed.  Arrangements will however be made for the following works.
(i) For transmission of  records in Appeals etc., to the High Court.
(ii) For attending to all Administrative  correspondences.
(iii) For furnishing the copies and process service Establishment with necessary records and  original orders and
(iv) For receiving printing charges and plan copying fees etc., deposited into Court in  respect of Copy Applications.
