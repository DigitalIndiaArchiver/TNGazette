[Elections – Conduct of Elections to Panchayats - Newly Constituted Districts - 
Appointment of District Election Officers - Notification Issued.]
(S.O.No.2/2020/TNSEC/PE-1, dated the 19th February 2020)
Read:
1)	
The  Tamil   Nadu   State   Election   Commission’s   S.O.No.2/2019/TNSEC/PE1, dated 10.4.2019.
2) 	
G.O. (Ms).No(s). 425, 427, 429, 431, Revenue and Disaster Management Department, Revenue 
Administration Wing,[RA 1(1)] Section, dated 12.11.2019.
3) 	
The Hon’ble Supreme Court of India order in  IA No.182868/2019 in Civil Appeal  Nos.5467-5469/2017, dated 
06.12.2019.
4) 	
G.O.(Ms)No.21, Rural Development and Panchayat Raj (PR1) Department, dated 27.01.2020.  
5) 	
G.O. (Ms)No.32,   33,   34,   Rural   Development   and   Panchayat   Raj   (PR1) Department, dated 15.02.2020. 
No. VI(2)/9(a-3)/2020.
WHEREAS, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission appointed the District Collectors concerned except the District 
Collector, Chennai, as the District Election Officers for their respective Districts for performing the functions specified in 
sub-rule (2) of rule 6 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Elections) Rules, 1995 in connection with the conduct of elections to 
Panchayats vide Tamil Nadu State Election Commission’s Statutory Order 1st read above and published in the Tamil Nadu 
Government Gazette Extraordinary Issue No.172, Part VI-Section 2, dated 12-4-2019 vide Notification No.VI(2)/41(a-2)/2019;
WHEREAS, in the G.Os. 2nd read above, the Government have bifurcated the Villupuram District into Villupuram 
and Kallakurichi Districts; the Tirunelveli District into Tirunelveli and Tenkasi Districts; the Kancheepuram District into 
Kancheepuram and Chengalpattu Districts and trifurcated the Vellore District into Vellore, Tirupathur and Ranipet Districts 
respectively;
WHEREAS, consequent on the bifurcation of Villupuram, Tirunelveli and Kancheepuram and trifurcation of Vellore 
districts,  the Government in the G.Os. 4th and  5th read above, have issued orders of constituting the District Panchayats 
for the newly constituted districts; 
NOW, THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-rule (2) of the rule 6 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats 
(Elections) Rules, 1995, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission hereby appoints the District Collectors of five newly 
constituted districts viz. Kallakurichi, Tenkasi, Chengalpattu, Tirupathur and Ranipet Districts as the District Election Officers 
for their respective Districts, subject to the superintendence, direction and control of the Tamil Nadu State Election 
Commission, they shall co-ordinate and supervise the preparation and publication of electoral rolls of panchayats and 
the conduct of elections to the offices of members of Village Panchayats, Panchayat Union Councils and the District 
Panchayats, Presidents and Vice-Presidents of Village Panchayats, Chairpersons and Vice-Chairpersons of Panchayat 
Union Councils and District Panchayats in their respective Districts.  They shall also perform such other functions as 
are entrusted to them by the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission, from time to time, in connection with the conduct 
of Elections to Panchayats.
(By Order of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission)

(S.O.No.3/2020/TNSEC/PE-1, dated the 19th February 2020)
No. VI(2)/9(a-4)/2020.
WHEREAS, the Government have bifurcated the Villupuram District into Villupuram and Kallakurichi Districts; the 
Tirunelveli District into Tirunelveli and Tenkasi Districts; the Kancheepuram District into Kancheepuram and Chengalpattu 
Districts and trifurcated the Vellore District into Vellore, Tirupathur and Ranipet Districts vide G.O.(Ms).No(s).425, 427, 429, 
431, Revenue and Disaster Management Department, Revenue Administration Wing,[RA 1(1)]Section, dated 12.11.2019 
respectively;
WHEREAS, consequent on the bifurcation of Villupuram, Tirunelveli and Kancheepuram and trifurcation of Vellore 
district,  the Government  have issued orders of constituting the District Panchayats for the newly constituted districts 
vide G.O.(Ms)No.21, Rural Development and Panchayat Raj (PR1) Department, dated 27.01.2020;  and G.O.(Ms)No(s).32, 
33, 34, Rural Development and Panchayat Raj (PR1) Department, dated  15.02.2020; 
NOW, THEREFORE,  in exercise of the powers conferred by rule 8 and 10 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats 
(Elections) Rules, 1995 and as per S.O.No.9/2019/TNSEC/PE1, dated 28.08.2019 published, inter-alia, in the Tamil Nadu 
Government Gazette Extraordinary Issue No.350, dated 03.09.2019 at page No.1 of Part VI–Section 2 vide Notification 
No.VI(2)/82(a-1)/2019,  the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission hereby authorises the District Election Officers of 
newly constituted districts viz. Kallakurichi, Tenkasi, Chengalpattu, Tirupathur and Ranipet Districts to make appointment 
of Returning Officers and Assistant Returning Officers for the conduct of election of Ward Members of the District 
Panchayats, Panchayat Union Councils and Village Panchayats and also for the conduct of the election of Presidents 
of Village Panchayats within their respective jurisdiction in connection with the conduct of Elections to Panchayats.
(By Order of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission)
