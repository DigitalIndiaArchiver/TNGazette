AMENDMENTS TO TAMIL NADU LIQUOR (SUPPLY BY WHOLESALE) RULES, 1983.
[G.O. Ms. No.  17, Home, Prohibition and Excise (VIII), 20th August 2014.]

In exercise of the powers conferred by section 54 read with sections 17-C, 17-D, 17-E, 18-B, 18-BB, 18-C, 20 and 21 of
the Tamil Nadu Prohibition Act, 1937 (Tamil Nadu Act X of 1937), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following
amendments to the Tamil Nadu Liquor (Supply by Wholesale) Rules, 1983:—
