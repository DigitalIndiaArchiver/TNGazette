(Central Act XVI of 1908), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that in the offices of the Sub-Registrar
specified in Column (3) attached to the Registration districts as specified in Column (2) thereof, in the Table
below, the process of registration of documents shall be completed and copying done with the help of the
electronic devices like computers, scanners and compact disks and copies preserved on such devices and
