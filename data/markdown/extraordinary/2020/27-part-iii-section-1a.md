In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 110 of the Juvenile Justice (Care and 
Protection of Children) Act, 2015, (Central Act 2 of 2016), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following 
amendments to the Tamil Nadu Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Rules, 2017.
Amendments
In the said Rules,-
(1) in rule 4, in sub-rule (1), in item (iii), for the expression “Commissioner of Police/District Superintendent of 
Police”, the expression “Commissioner of Police or any other Police officer not below the rank of Superintendent 
of Police deputed by the Commissioner of Police/District Superintendent of Police, as the case may be” shall 
be substituted; and
(2) in rule 17, in sub-rule (1), in item (iii), for the expression “Commissioner of Police or District Superintendent 
of Police as the case may be”, the expression “Commissioner of Police or any other Police officer not below the 
rank of Superintendent of Police deputed by the Commissioner of Police or District Superintendent of Police, 
as the case may be” shall be substituted.
