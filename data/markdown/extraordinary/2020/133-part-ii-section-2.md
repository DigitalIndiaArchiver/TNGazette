The Commission will assess and analyse the reasons for the less number of   Government school students getting admissions 
in MBBS course and recommend / suggest  measures  to the Government.
3. The Terms of Reference for the Commission shall be as follows:-
a)	
Discuss the issues and suggest remedial measures for the improvement of State Government School 
students admission in MBBS course.
 
b)	
The Commission submit its report to the Government within one month.
c)	
The Secretarial work and other assistance needed to the Chairman of the Commission, will be given by 
the Director of Medical Education and Selection Committee. 
	
     d)	   The Commission can be a first class Commission for the purpose of Travelling Allowance and Daily 	 	
	
	
  Allowance and sitting allowance and all the expenses shall be charged under the Personal Deposit  
	
	
  account of the Selection Committee.
e)    The Member Secretary is to organize and convene the meeting as and when needed. 
   4. The Director of Medical Education and Additional Director of Medical Education / Secretary Selection Committee 
are directed to provide the required details and make necessary arrangements for convening the meeting from time to time.
