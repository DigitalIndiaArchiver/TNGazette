Amendments to Ad hoc Rules relating to Temporary Posts of Publication Assistant,  Receptionist Grade I, and
Grade II, Information Assistant and Upper Division Accountant for Tamil Nadu General Subordinate Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 31, Tourism and Culture (T.2), 16th February 2010, Maasi 4, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No. SRO B-14/2010.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the rules published with the Information and
Tourism Department Notification No. SRO B-461/84 at pages 568 to 572 of Part III—Section 1(b) of the Tamil Nadu
Government Gazette, dated the 26th December 1984.
2.
(i) Amendments 1 and 2(a) hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 17th November 2005.
(ii) Amendments 2(b), 3(a), 3(b) and 5(a) hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the
22nd August 1995.
(iii) Amendments 2(c), 3(c) and 5(b) hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the
30th March 1995.
(iv) Amendment 4 hereby made shall come into force on the 16th February 2010.
AMENDMENTS
(I) In the said Rules,—
1.
For the expressions “Publication Assistant”, "Receptionist, Grade-I” and “Receptionist, Grade-II” wherever they
occur, the expressions “Assistant Publication Officer”, “Assistant Tourist Officer, Grade-I” and “Assistant Tourist Officer,
Grade-II” shall respectively be substituted;
2.
In rule 1,—
(a)
for the expressionss “Receptionist Grades I and II”, the expressions “Assistant Tourist Officer, Grade-I”
and “Assistant Tourist Officer, Grade-II” shall be substituted;
(b)
the post “Information Assistant” shall be omitted;
(c)
The post “Upper Division Accountant” shall be omitted;
3.
In rule 3, in sub rule (a), in the Table,—
(a)
the expression “and Information Assistant” in column (2) against the post “Assistant Publication Officer”
as so substituted, in column (1) thereof shall be omitted;
(b)
the post “Information Assistant” in column (1) and the corresponding entries in column (2) thereof
shall be omited;
(c)
the post “Upper Division Accountant” in column (1) and the corresponding entries in column (2) thereof
shall be omitted;
4.
In rule 5, in sub rule (b), in the Table,—
(a)
For the entries in column (2) against the post “Assistant Tourist Officer, Grade-II” as so substituted
in column (1) thereof the following entries shall be substituted, namely:—
“(a) for direct recruitment,—
(i)
A degree in Travel and Tourism; (or) any degree with Diploma in Tourism; (or) any degree with a subject
on Tourism; and
(ii)
Must have undergone a basic course in computer;
