The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied

acquired for the industrial purposes to wit for the formation
of new railway road of Thoothukudi – Madurai(via :
Aruppukottai) situated in Therku Veerapandiyapuram Village
of Ottapidaram Taluk in the District of Thoothukudi, and it
has already been decided that the entire amount of
compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid out

southern Railway. The following notice is issued under
Sub-Section (1) Of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition
of land for the Industrial purposes Act 1997(Tamil Nadu Act
10/1999).

NOTICE
Under Sub-Section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
acquisition of land for Industrial Purposes Act 1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby acquires the lands specified in the schedule below
measuring 9.26.09 Hectares to the same, a little more or
less needed for industrial purposes to wit for the formation
of new railway road of Thoothukudi – Madurai (via :
Aruppukottai) situated in Therku Veerapandiyapuram Village
of Ottapidaram Taluk in the District of Thoothukudi.
The Plan of lands are kept in the office of the Collector,
Thoothukudi / Revenue Divisional Officer, Kovilpatti / Office
of Special Tahsildar(Land Acquisition –Railway) Taluk Office,
Ettiahpuram may be inspected at any time during office
hours.
