The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that
the lands specified in the schedule below have to be acquired
for industrial purpose to wit, for the expansion of Sriperumbudur
Industrial Complex by SIPCOT and  it having already been
decided that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded
for the lands is to be paid out of the funds controlled or
managed by the Government, the following Notice is issued
under sub-section (1) of  Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition
of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997  (Tamil Nadu
Act 10 of 1999).
NOTIFICATION
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997
(Tamil Nadu Act, 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby acquires the lands specified in the schedule below
and measuring 5.30.5 hectares to the same, a little more
or less needed for Industrial Complex  by SIPCOT.
The plans of the lands are kept in the Office of the
Special Tahsildar (Land Acquisition), Sriperumbudur and
Irungattukottai Scheme, Irungattukottai, Kancheepuram
District and may be inspected at any time during office
hours.
