No. II(1)/AGFW/5/2023.—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 4 of the Seeds Act, 
1966 (Central Act 54 of 1966), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby declares the seed Testing Laboratory at Tiruppur 
as State Seed Laboratory, for the purpose of carrying out analysis of seeds of any notiﬁ ed kind or variety by seed 
Analyst under that Act.

No. II(1)/AGFW/6/2023.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 12 of the Seeds Act, 1966 (Central Act 
54 of 1966), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the Senior Agricultural Oﬃ  cer, Seed Testing Laboratory at 
Tiruppur to be “Seed Analyst’’ and he shall exercise jurisdiction in Tiruppur District.

No. II(1)/AGFW/7/2023.—In exercise of the powers conferred by clause 28A of the Fertilizer (Inorganic, Organic 
or Mixed) (Control) Order, 1985 and in super session of Agriculture Department Notiﬁ cation No. 8 Published at 
page 13 of Part II-Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette dated the Second March 2011, the Governor of 
Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the District Joint Director of Agriculture within their respective jurisdiction to be the Notiﬁ ed 
Authority for keeping the Fertilizer Samples in his/her custody and for sending the second part for analysis to National 
Test House and keep the third sample for refree analysis.
