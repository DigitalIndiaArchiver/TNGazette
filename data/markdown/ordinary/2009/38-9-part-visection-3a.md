GENERAL  NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD,
No. 1, Jaffar Syrang Street, Vallal Seethakathi Nagar, Chennai-600 001.
Taking over the Management of Abdul Gani Khan Bahadur Kabarasthan Wakf, Triplicane, Chennai-5 under Direct
Management of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board, Chennai-600 001.
(Rc. No. 46/09/B2/Chennai.)
No. VI-3(a)/249/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995, the Tamil Nadu
Wakf Board in its resolution in item No. 6/09 in R.C. 46/09/B2/Chennai, dated 29th July 2009 has ordered to take
over the management of Abdul Gani Khan Bahadur Kabarasthan Wakf, Triplicane, Chennai-600 005, under the Direct
Control of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of Two years from 29th July 2009 and appoints the wakf Inspector/
Chennai (South) as its Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
23rd September 2009.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
