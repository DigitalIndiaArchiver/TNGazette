No. VI(1)/84(a)/2020.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section 3 of Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Goods and Services 
Tax Act, 2017, the Commissioner of State Tax hereby delegates the powers conferred on him vide Notification 
No. II(2)/CTR/793(d-1)/2017, dated 22nd September 2017, No. II(2)/CTR/793(d-2)/2017, dated 22nd September 2017, 
No. II(2)/CTR/858(a-8)/2017, dated 13th October 2017 and No. II(2)/CTR/858(a-9)/2017, dated 13th October 2017 in 
respect of affidavit filed by the taxpayer voluntarily foregoing any actionable claim or enforceable right in respect of brand 
name to the territorial Joint Commissioner having jurisdiction over the taxpayer or to the Joint Commissioner (Policy and 
Planning) in the office of the Commissioner of Commercial Taxes for the State.
2. This notification shall come into force immediately.
