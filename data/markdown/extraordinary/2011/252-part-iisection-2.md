DTP—II-2 Ex. (252)
HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE DEPARTMENT
THE PRINCIPAL SECRETARY TO GOVERNMENT, HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE DEPARTMENT BE
NOTIFIED AS COMMISSIONER OF FOOD SAFETY FOR THE STATE OF TAMIL NADU
UNDER THE FOOD SAFETY AND STANDARDS ACT
[G.O. Ms. No. 197, Health and Family Welfare (AB2), 4th August 2011,
Aadi 19, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No. II(2)/HF/358(c)/2011.
In exercise of powers conferred by sub-section (i) of Section 30 of the Food Safety and Standards
Act, 2006 (Central Act 34 of 2006), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notifies the Principal Secretary to
the Government, Health and Family Welfare Department, Chennai-9 as the Commissioner of Food Safety
for the State of Tamil Nadu.
GIRIJA VAIDYANATHAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
