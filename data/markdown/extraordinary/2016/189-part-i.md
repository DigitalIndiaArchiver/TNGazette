I, K. Rosaiah, Governor of Tamil Nadu accept the recommendation of  the Chief Minister that
Thiru S.P. Shunmuganathan, Minister for Milk & Dairy Development be relieved from the Council
of Ministers and direct that he be relieved of his duties and responsibilities as Minister with
immediate effect.
K. ROSAIAH,
Governor of Tamil Nadu.
