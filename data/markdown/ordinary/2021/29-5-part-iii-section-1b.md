Adhoc Rules to the Temporary Post of State Maternal and Child Health Oﬃ  cer in 
Tamil Nadu Public Health Service.
[G.O.Ms.No. 284, Health and Family Welfare (L2), 14th June 2021, Vaikasi 31, 
Pilava, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2052.]
No. SRO B-29/2021.— In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, 
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following rules:- 
2. The rules hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on and from the 28th July 2014. 
Rඝඔඍඛ.
The General and the Special Rules applicable to the holders of the permanent post of Class-1 of the Tamil Nadu Public 
Health Service shall apply to the holders of the temporary post of State Maternal and Child Health Oﬃ  cer, sanctioned from time 
to time in the Directorate of Public Health and Preventive Medicine, subject to the modiﬁ cations speciﬁ ed in the following rules: 
Provided that with eﬀ ect on and from the 15th September 2016, the provisions contained in the Tamil Nadu Government 
Servants (Conditions of Service) Act, 2016 (Tamil Nadu Act 14 of 2016) and the Special Rules applicable to the holders of 
the permanent posts in Class-I of the Tamil Nadu Public Health Service shall apply to the holder of the post. 
2. 
Constitution - The post shall constitute a distinct class of the said service. 
3. 
Appointment - Appointment to the post shall be made by promotion from among the holders of the post of Joint 
Maternal and Child Health Oﬃ  cer in the Tamil Nadu Public Health Service in the Directorate of Public Health and Preventive 
Medicine. 
4.  Appointing Authority - The appointing authority for the post shall be the Government 
5.  Qualiﬁ cation - No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post unless he possesses the service qualiﬁ cation 
for a period of not less than one year in the post of Joint State Maternal and Child Health Oﬃ  cer. 
6.  Preparation of annual list of approved candidates and crucial date - For the purpose of preparation of annual 
list of approved candidates for appointment to the post, the crucial date on which the candidates should have acquired the 
prescribed qualiﬁ cations shall be the 15th February of every year. 
7.  Pay - There shall be paid to the holder of the post, monthly pay calculated in the Pay Band-4 of 
Rs.37400 - 67000 + Grade Pay of Rs.8,700/- : 
Provided that, monthly pay shall be calculated in Level 28 - Rs.123100 - 215900 under the Tamil Nadu Revised Pay 
Rules, 2017, notionally with eﬀ ect from the 1st January, 2016, and with monetary beneﬁ t from 1st October, 2017. 
8.  Saving - Nothing contained in these rules shall adversely aﬀ ect any person holding the post as on the date of 
publication of these rules in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette. 
 
J. RADHAKRISHNAN, 
 
Principal Secretary to Government.
