HOME, PROHIBITION AND EXCISE DEPARTMENT
Amendment to the Tamil Nadu Liquor (Supply by Wholesale) Rules, 1983.
[G.O. Ms. No. 31, Home, Prohibition and Excise (VIII), 17th July 2010, Aadi 1, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No. SRO A-27/2010.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Sections 17-D and 54 of the Tamil Nadu Prohibition Act,
1937 (Tamil Nadu Act X of 1937), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Tamil Nadu
Liquor (Supply by Wholesale) Rules, 1983:
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st April 2009.
AMENDMENT
in the said Rules, in rule 15, in sub-rule (3), in item (iv), for the expression "16.19", the expression "17.25" shall be
substituted.
K. GNANADESIKAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
