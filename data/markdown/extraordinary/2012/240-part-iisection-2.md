It has also been ordered therein that two vacancies arisen in Bar Council as well as two vacancies in Mutawalli
Categories will be filled up by notifying and conducting the election as per sub-section (2) of Section 14 of the Wakf Act.
1995 (Central Act 43 of 1995).
3.
Accordingly, the Bar Council Members and Mutawallis election was held on 29.08.2012 by secret ballot as per the
procedure prescribed and counting of votes was completed on 30.08.2012. Under rule 20 of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board
(Conduct of Election for Members) Rules, 1997 the Returning Officer has declared the following as elected members and
also given certificates of election under 21 of the said rules and accordingly, under sub-clauses (iii) and (iv) of clause
(b) of sub-section (1) and sub-section (9) of Section 14 of the Wakf Act 1995 (central Act 43 of 1995) the Governor of Tamil
Nadu hereby appoints the following persons as members of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
Bar Council Members:
1.
Ms. A.S. Bibi John
2.
Thiru M.K. Khan
Mutawallis
1.
Thiru M. Mohammed Sikkandar
2.
Dr. Haja K. Majeed
4.
The term of office for members of the Board shall be for a period of five years from the date of issue of the
notification.
5.
The Chief Executive Officer, Tamil Nadu Wakf Board, Chennai-1 is requested to take necessary action to convene
the first meeting of the Board for electing the Chiarperson under Section 14(8) of the Wakf Act, 1995 (Central Act 43 of
1995).
6.
The Notification appended to this order will be published in an Extraordinary issue of the Tamil Nadu Government
Gazette, 31st day of August 2012.
