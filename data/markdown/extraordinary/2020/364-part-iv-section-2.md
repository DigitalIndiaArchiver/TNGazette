TAMIL NADU ORDINANCE No. 10 OF 2020
An Ordinance further to amend the Tamil Nadu Public 
Health Act,1939.
WHEREAS the Legislative Assembly of the State is not in 
session and the Governor of Tamil Nadu is satisﬁ ed that 
circumstances exist which render it necessary for him to take 
immediate action for the purposes hereinafter appearing;
NOW, THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred by 
clause (1) of Article 213 of the Constitution, the Governor hereby 
promulgates the following Ordinance:—
1.  (1) This Ordinance may be called the Tamil Nadu Public 
Health (Second Amendment) Ordinance, 2020.

commencement.
  
(2) It shall come into force at once.

EXPLANATORY STATEMENT.
To contain the spread of COVID-19, lockdown and social distancing measures are taken by the 
Government.  Violences against the persons implementing these measures have been brought to the notice 
of the Government.  Such acts of Violence hinders the measures taken to control the spread COVID-19 in 
the community and deters the advancement of Public Health.  It is therefore considered necessary to make 
the violations against lockdown and social distancing measures taken to contain the spread of COVID-19 as 
oﬀ ences and also to prohibit the acts of violence against the persons implementing these measures.
2. Accordingly, the Government have decided to amend the Tamil Nadu Public Health Act, 1939 
(Tamil Nadu Act III of 1939) suitably for the aforesaid purposes.
3. The Ordinance seeks to give eﬀ ect to the above decision.
