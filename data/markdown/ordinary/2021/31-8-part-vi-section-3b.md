Election of a nominee by the SENATE to the Committee for recommending a panel of three names to the 
Chancellor for appointment of the Vice-Chancellor
(ME-2/VC-P/Sen.& Syn.Nom./2021)
No.VI-3(b)/16/2021.
It is hereby notiﬁ ed that, according to Statute 6 (2) of Chapter V of the laws of the University, Volume-I Calendar 
of the University, an election will be held for nominating a person* by the SENATE by the University to serve on 
the Committee of three, who are to suggest a panel of three names to the Chancellor for the appointment of the 
Vice-Chancellor.  The election will be held in accordance with the Statutes of Chapter V and the rules relating to the 
elections to the authorities of the University.  Each member of the SENATE shall have a right to nominate not more 
than one person who is not a member of any of the authorities of the University or shall not be connected with 
University or any college or any recognized institution of the University. Every nomination shall be in writing signed by 
the proposer who shall be a member of the SENATE and seconded in writing by another member of the SENATE called 
the seconder in the form prescribed.  The form prescribed may be obtained from the Registrar on a written requisition 
signed by any member of the SENATE.  No nomination shall be valid unless it contains the signature of both the 
proposer and seconder and the nomination shall also be signed by the person proposed expressing his consent to be 
nominated.  Every nomination must be presented before the Registrar either by the proposer or the seconder or the 
person proposed before 3.00 pm on Monday, the 23rd August, 2021.
The scrutiny of nomination will take place at 3.15 pm on Monday,  the 23rd August, 2021 at the Oﬃ  ce of the 
Registrar, Madurai Kamaraj University,  Madurai - 625 021.  The proposer or the seconder or the proposed nominee 
can be present at the time of the scrutiny.
