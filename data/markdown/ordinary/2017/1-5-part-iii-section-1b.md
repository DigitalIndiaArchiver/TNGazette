No. SRO B-1/2017.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, Hon’ble
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following Rules:-
2. The rules hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on and from the 28th day of November, 2014.
RULES
The General and the Special Rules applicable to the holders of the permanent posts in Branch III — Nursing of the
Tamil Nadu Medical Subordinate Service shall apply to the holder of the temporary post of Grief Counsellor in the Department
of Surgical Gastroentrology and Liver Transplantation Centre and Cadaver Maintenance Programme
at Government Stanley Hospital, Chennai subject to the modification specified in the following rules:-
2. Constitution.- The post shall constitute a separate category in the said Branch of the said Service.
3. Appointment.- Appointment to the post shall be made by direct recruitment.
4. Qualification:.- (a) Age.- No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post by direct recruitment, if he has
completed or wiil complete the age of 32 years on the first day of July of the year in which the selection for appointment
to the post is made.
(b) Other Qualification.- No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post unless he possess a Master Degree
in Social Work (Medical & Psychiatry) or its equivalent to any other master degree recognized by the University Grants
Commission.
5. Probation.-(a) Every person appointed to the post by direct recruitment shall from the date on which he joins duty,
be on probation for a total period of two years on duty within a continuous period of three years.
6. Reservation.-The rule relating to reservation of appointments (General Rule 22) shall apply for appointment to
the post by direct recruitment.
7. Tests.-(a) Every person appointed to the post shall pass the Account Test for Executive Officers within the period
of his probation; and
(b) Every person appointed to the post shall also pass the Departmental Test in the Tamil Nadu Medical
Code within a period of four years from the date of appointment, failing which future increments shall be withheld without
cumulative effect till he passes the said test.
8. Pay.- There shall be paid to the holder of the post a monthly pay calculated in the Pay Band 2 Rs. 9300 - 34800
with Grade Pay of Rs.4600/-.
9. Saving Clause.- Nothing contained in these rules shall adversely affect the person appointed to the post prior
to the date of publication of the rules in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
J. RADHAKRISHNAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
