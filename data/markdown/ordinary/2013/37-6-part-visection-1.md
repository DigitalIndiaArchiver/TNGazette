GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
Variations to the Approved Master Plan for the Tiruppur Local Planning Authority.
(Roc. No. 290/2013/TLPA.)
[G. O. (2D) No. 86, Housing and Urban Development (UD 4-1) Department, dated 27th August 2013.]
No. VI(1)/373/2013.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 32 of the Town and Country Planning Act  , 1971
(Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972) the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following Variation to the Master Plan of the
Tiruppur Local Planning Area approved under the said Act and published with the Housing and Urban Development
Department Notification No. II(2)/HOU/573/ 2006 at pages 375 of Part II Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government
Gazette, dated the 22nd November 2006.
VARIATIONS
In the said Master Plan in the “LAND USE SCHEDULE” for Mangalam Village under the heading “TIRUPPUR
LOCAL PLANNING AREA” in the tabular column, in column (3).
(1) under heading ‘AGRICULTURAL USE (AG 60)' in column (2) against the Serial No. VI in column (VI-f) for the
expression 500 to 510 shall be substituted as 502 to 510 except 507/2B.
(2) under the heading ‘CONTROLLED INDUSTRIES use (CI)’ in column (2) against the serial No. III (a) the Survey
No. 507/2B shall be added.
Tiruppur-4,
B. RAMESH KUMAR,
13th September 2013.
Member Secretary (In-Charge),
Tiruppur Local Planning Authority.
