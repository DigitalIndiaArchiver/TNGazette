NOTIFICATION UNDER THE TAMIL NADU TRANSPARENCY IN TENDERS ACT, 1998
[G.O. Ms. No. 332, Finance (Salaries), 5th September 2020, Aavani 20, Saarvari,Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2051.]
No. II(2)/FIN/534(h)/2020.
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (bb) of section 16 of the Tamil Nadu Transparency 
in Tenders Act, 1998 (Tamil Nadu Act 43 of 1998), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby declares that the 
procurement of services of the National Institute for Research in Tuberculosis to conduct the state level 
prevalence survey along with the National Prevalence survey within a year, to cover all the districts with 
support of Central Tuberculosis Division, who will provide seven mobile X ray units.
