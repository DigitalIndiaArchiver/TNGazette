APPOINTMENT OF COMMISSIONER OF POLICE, AVADI CITY AND TAMBARAM CITY AS  EXECUTIVE MAGISTRATES 
AND ADDITIONAL DISTRICT MAGISTRATES.
NOTIFICATION-I

No. II(2)/HO/(B-1)/2022. 
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 20 of the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 
(Central Act 2 of 1974), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the Commissioner of Police for Avadi and the 
Commissioner of Police for Tambaram to be Executive Magistrates, in respect of the City of Avadi and the City of 
Tambaram, respectively.
NOTIFICATION-II  
[G.O.Ms. No. 6, Home (Police-XIV), 1st January 2022, ñ£˜èN 17, Ãyt, ÂUtŸSt®  M©L-2052.]
No. II(2)/HO/(B-2)/2022. 
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 20 of the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 
(Central Act 2 of 1974), read with Section 4 of the Chennai City Police (Extension to the cities of Avadi and Tambaram) 
Ordinance, 2021 (Tamil Nadu Ordinance No. 1 of 2022), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the Commissioners 
of Police and Executive Magistrates for Avadi and Tambaram to be Additional District Magistrates and empowers them 
to exercise and perform in relation to Avadi City and Tambaram City, respectively, the following powers and functions, 
namely:-
(1) 
The powers conferred under sub-section (4) of Section 24, Sections 133 and 144 of the Code of Criminal 
Procedure, 1973 (Central Act 2 of 1974); and
(2) 
The functions of the nature speciﬁ ed in clause (b) of sub-section (4) of Section 3 of the said Code, 
exercisable by an Executive Magistrate.
