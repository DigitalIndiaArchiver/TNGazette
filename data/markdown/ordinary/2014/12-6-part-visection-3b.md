UNIVERSITY OF  MADRAS
Election of One Member to the Senate by the Academic Council from among its own body.
(No. V.1/College/2-2014/138)
No. VI-3(b)/14/2014.
I hereby declare that Dr. R. Dayalan, M.A.,M.Phil.,Ph.D., Associate Professor, Department of History, D.R.B.C.C.C.
Hindu College, Dharmamurthi Nagar, Pattabiram, Chennai-600 072 has been duly elected to the Senate
by the Academic  Council from among its own body of whom not less than Five shall be teachers of affiliated Colleges
under Section 14(a) - Other Members (2) of the Madras University Act,  1923, for the residual period from
22nd February 2014 to 28th September 2016 or till the date of cessation of his membership in the
Academic Council whichever is earlier.
