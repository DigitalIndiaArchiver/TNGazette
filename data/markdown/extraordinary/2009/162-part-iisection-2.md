NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
DTP—II-2 Ex. (162)
MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION AND WATER SUPPLY DEPARTMENT
TRANSFER OF OVERALL CONTROL OF THE KODAIKANAL LAKE IN SURVEY WARD B, BLOCK 30,
T.S.No. 3, R.S. No. 234 TO THE KODAIKANAL MUNICIPALITY FROM FISHERIES DEPARTMENT UNDER
THE TAMIL NADU DISTRICT MUNICIPALITIES  ACT, 1920.
[G.O. (Ms.) No. 112, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (MA5), 6th July 2009,
Aani 22, Thiruvalluvar Aandu 2040.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/323(d)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 125 of the Tamil Nadu District
Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920), the Governor of Tamil Nadu after consultation with the
Kodaikanal Municipal Council, hereby  defines that the control of the Kodaikanal lake in Ward B, Block 30,
T.S. No. 3, R.S.No. 234 hitherto with the Fisheries Department is hereby transferred to Kodaikanal Municipal
Council subject to the condition that the fishing rights shall continue to be with the Fisheries Department
in accordance with the provisions of the Kodaikanal Fishing Rules, 1969 issued with G.O. Ms. No. 1123,
Agriculture Department dated the 10th April 1970.
NIRANJAN  MARDI,
Secretary to Government.
