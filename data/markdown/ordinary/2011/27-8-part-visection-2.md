Amendment to Regulation 1 of the Power Procurement from New and Renewable
Sources of Energy Regulations.
(Notification No. TNERC/NCES Regn./16/10, dated 1st July 2011.)
[Lr. No. TNERC/AD(L)/F.Notification/D-835 /2011.]
No.VI(2)/399/2011.
Whereas the Tamil Nadu Electricity Regulatory Commission under Section 181 read with Sections 61(h) and 86(1)(e) of
the Electricity Act, 2003 (Central Act 36 of 2003), specified and notified in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the
27th February 2008, the Power Procurement from New and Renewable Sources of Energy Regulations, 2008.
And, Whereas, it is considered necessary to issue certain amendments to the above Regulations and such amendments
shall be subject to the condition of the previous publication and accordingly undergone such previous publication.
Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by Section 181 read with Sections 61(h) and 86(1)(e) of the said
Electricity Act, 2003 (Central Act 36 of 2003), and all other powers enabling it in that behalf, the Tamil Nadu Electricity
Regulatory Commission hereby makes the following Amending Regulations to the Power Procurement from New and
Renewable Sources of Energy Regulations, 2008 (herein referred to as the Principal Regulations) issued under
Notification No. TNERC/NCES Regn./16/1, dated 8th February, 2008 and published in Part VI—Section 2 of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 27th February 2008.
Amending Regulations:
1. Short title and commencement.—
(a) These Regulations may be called the Power Procurement from New and Renewable Sources of Energy (Amendment)
Regulations, 2011.
(b) They shall come into force on the date of their publication in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
2. Amendment of regulation 1 of the Principal Regulations.—
In sub-regulation (3) of regulation 1 of the Principal Regulations, the fourth sentence commencing with the expression
“When the generator/distribution licensee” and ending with the expression “the date of such option” shall be deleted.
(By order of the Tamil Nadu Electricity Regulatory Commission)
