ALLOCATION OF SUBJECTS AMONG MINISTER
[G.O.Ms.No. 558, Public (Special-B), 1st November 2020, Aippasi 16, Saarvari, 
Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2051.] 
No. I/PUSP/25(a)/2020.
Under Clause (3) of Article 166 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu 
hereby directs that the subjects “Agriculture, Agricultural Engineering, Agro Service Co-operatives, 
Horticulture, Sugarcane Cess, Sugarcane Development and Waste Land Development’’ held by 
(late) Thiru R. Doraikkannu, Minister for Agriculture, be allotted to Thiru K.P. Anbalagan, Minister 
for Higher Education. Consequent on the above allocation, Thiru K.P. Anbalagan, be re-designated 
as Minister for Higher Education and Agriculture. 
 
K. SHANMUGAM, 
 
 
 
Chief Secretary.
