notifications under the tamil nadu goods and services tax act, 2017
[G.O. Ms. No. 130, Commercial Taxes and Registration (B1), 4th September 2019, Aavani 18, 
Vikari, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2050.]
No. II(2)/CTR/733(a)/2019.
In exercise of the powers conferred by section 148 of the Tamil Nadu Goods and Services 
Tax Act, 2017 (Tamil Nadu Act 19 of 2017) (hereinafter referred to as the said Act), the  Governor 
of Tamil Nadu, on the recommendations of the Council, hereby notifies the registered persons 
required to furnish the details of challans in FORM ITC-04 under sub-rule (3) of rule 45 of the 
Tamil Nadu Goods and Services Tax Rules, 2017 (hereinafter referred to as the said rules), read with 
section 143 of the said Act, as the class of registered persons who shall follow the special procedure 
such that the said persons shall not be required to furnish FORM ITC-04 under sub-rule (3) of rule 
45 of the said rules for the period July, 2017 to March, 2019:
	
	
Provided that the said persons shall furnish the details of all the challans in respect of 
goods dispatched to a job worker in the period July, 2017 to March, 2019 but not received from a 
job worker or not supplied from the place of business of the job worker as on the 31st March, 2019, 
in serial number 4 of FORM ITC-04 for the quarter April-June, 2019.
