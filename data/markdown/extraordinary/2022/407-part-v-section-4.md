NOTIFICATION BY THE ELECTION COMMISSION OF INDIA
PLACE AND TIME FOR COUNTING OF VOTES FOR VICE-PRESIDENTIAL ELECTION, 2022.
No. SRO G-35/2022.
The following Notiﬁ cation of the Election Commission of India, Nirvachan Sadan, Ashoka Road, New Delhi-110 001, 
dated 27th July, 2022 [5 Shravana, 1944 (Saka)] is republished :-
480/2022:- In pursuance of rule 27 of the Presidential and Vice-Presidential Elections Rules, 1974, the Election Commission 
of India hereby appoints the 6th August, 2022 (Saturday), as the day on which and 6.00 p.m. of that day as the time at 
which, the counting of votes for the Vice-Presidential Election, 2022 shall take place in the oﬃ  ce of Returning Oﬃ  cer in
Room No. 63, First Floor, Parliament House, New Delhi. 
(By Order)
