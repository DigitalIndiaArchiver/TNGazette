Election of a nominee by the Syndicate to the Committee for recommending a panel of three names to the
Chancellor for appointment of the Vice-Chancellor.
(Ref.No. ME2/VC-P/Syn.Nom./Election/2015.)
No. VI-3(b)/8/2015.
It is hereby notified that, according to Statute 5 of Chapter V of the laws of the University, Volume-I Calendar of the University,
an election will be held for nominating a person by the Syndicate by the University to serve on the Committee of three, who are
to suggest a panel of three names to the Chancellor for appointment of the Vice-Chancellor.  The election will be held in accordance
with the Statutes of Chapter V and the rules relating to the elections to the authorities of the University.  Each member of the
Syndicate shall have a right to nominate not more than one person who is not a member of any of the authorities of the University
to the Committee.  Every nomination shall be in writing signed by the proposer who shall be a member of the Syndicate and
seconded in writing by another member of the Syndicate called the seconder in the form prescribed.  The form prescribed may
be obtained from the Registrar on a written requisition signed by any member of the Syndicate.  No nomination shall be valid unless
it contains the signature of both the proposer and seconder and the nomination shall also be signed by the person proposed
expressing his consent to be nominated.  Every nomination must be presented before the Registrar either by the proposer or the
seconder or the person proposed before 3.00 p.m. on Thursday 5th March 2015.
The scrutiny of nomination will take place at 3.15 p.m. on Thursday the  5th March 2015 at the Office of the Registrar,
Madurai Kamaraj University, Madurai - 625 021.  The proposer or the seconder or the proposed nominee can be present at the
time of the scrutiny.
