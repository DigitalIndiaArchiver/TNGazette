TAMIL NADU STATE LEGAL SERVICES AUTHORITY
Constitution of Taluk Legal Services Committee at Pandalur in Nilgiris District and Appointment of
Judicial Magistrate As Ex-Officio Chairman of Pandalur Taluk Legal Services Committee.
(TNSLSA No. 580/E/2015)
No. VI(2)/85/2015.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 11(A)(1) of the Legal Services Authorities Act 1987,
(Central Act 39/1987) read with Rule 3(a) of the Tamil Nadu Taluk Legal Services Committee Rules 1997, the Tamil Nadu State
Legal Services Authority hereby constitutes a Taluk Legal Services Committee at Pandalur in Nilgiris District and appoints the
Judicial Magistrate, Pandalur as ex-officio Chairman, Pandalur Taluk Legal Services Committee to exercise the powers and
perform the functions conferred on, or assigned to the Taluk Legal Services Committee under the Act.
High Court Campus,
RMT. TEEKAA RAMAN,
Chennai-600 104.
