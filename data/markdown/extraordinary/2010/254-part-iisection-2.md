 In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax
(Second Amendment) Act, 2010 (Tamil Nadu Act 22 of 2010), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the
19th day of August 2010, as the date on which the said Act shall come into force.
