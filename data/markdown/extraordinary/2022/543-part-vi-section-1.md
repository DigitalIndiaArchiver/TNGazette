The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that the lands speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below to be acquired for 
Highways purpose to wit, for formation of road for providing connectivity from Ennore Port to Mahabalipuram (from 73.566 KM 
to 74.966 KM) at Kancheepuram District, Sriperumbudur Taluk, No.42, Sriperumbudur-B (Block-I) Village and it having already 
been decided that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands to be paid out of the funds controlled and 
managed by the Highways Department. The following notice is issued under sub section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu 
Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34/2002)
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002), the Governor 
of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the Wet and Dry Patta lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule 4.6937 Hectare / 46937 Sq.Mtrs. to the 
same, a little more or less needed for the purpose to wit for formation of road for providing road connectivity from Ennore 
Port to Mahabalipuram (from 73.566 KM to 74.966 KM) as part of Chennai Peripheral Ring Road Project, by Highways 
department. The following notice is issued under sub section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil 
Nadu Act 34/2002).
The Plan of the lands under acquisition are kept in the oﬃ  ce of the Special District Revenue Oﬃ  cer (Land Acquisition) 
(FAC), Chennai Peripheral Ring Road Project, Chennai-600 058 and in the oﬃ  ce of the Special Tahsildar (Land Acquisition),

inspected at any time during the oﬃ  ce hours.
