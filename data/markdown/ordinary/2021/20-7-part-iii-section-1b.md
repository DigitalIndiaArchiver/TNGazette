No. SRO B-23/2021.— In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India and 
in supersession of the Health, Indian Medicine and Homoeopathy and Family Welfare Department Notification No.SRO.B-22/90 
published at page 13 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 24th January 1990, the Governor of Tamil Nadu 
hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Medical Service (Section 21 in Volume II 
of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 2016.)
2. 	 (i) The amendments except amendment (3)(i) hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on and from 
the 15th February, 2019. 
	
(ii) The amendment (3)(i) hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on and from the 
17th October 2012.
Amendments
In the said Special Rules, in Part II, under the  heading "Branch I - Medical",-
(1)	
In rule-1, under the heading Class I, after the entry "Category 4 Director, King Institute Guindy", the entry 
"Category 4A Joint Director, King Institute, Guindy", shall be inserted;
(2)	
in rule 2, in the tabular column, under the heading "Chief Civil Surgeons' Cadre" in column (1), after the entry 
"Category 4 Director, King Institute, Guindy", the entry "Category 4A Joint Director, King Institute, Guindy", shall 
be inserted;
(3)	
in rule 3, in sub-rule (a), in the tabular column under the heading "Class I",-
(i)	
for the entries in column (2), against the entry "Category 4 Director, King Institute, Guindy" in column (1) 
thereof, the following entry shall be substituted, namely:-
"Promotion from among the holders of the post 
of Joint Director, King Institute, Guindy".
(ii)	
after the entry "Category 4 Director, King Institute, Guindy" in column (1) and the corresponding entry 
relating thereto in column (2) thereof, as so amended, the following entries shall, respectively, be inserted, 
namely:-
"Category 4A Joint Director, 
King Institute, Guindy
Promotion among the holders of the post of 
Deputy Director, King Institute, Guindy.";
(4)	
in rule 4, in sub-rule (a), after the entry "Director, King Institute, Guindy", the entry "Joint Director, King Institute, 
Guindy", shall be inserted;
(5)	
in rule 12, in sub-rule (a), after the entry "Director, King Institute, Guindy", the entry "Joint Director, King Institute, 
Guindy", shall be inserted; 
(6)	
in rule 13, in sub-rule (a), after the entry "Director, King Institute, Guindy", the entry "Joint Director, King Institute, 
Guindy", shall be inserted;
(7)	
in the Annexure [referred to in rule 8 (c)], in the tabular column (1) under the heading "Class I", after the 
entry "Category 3 Director of the Tuberculosis, Institute of Tuberculosis Clinics and Professors of Tuberculosis, 
Medical College, Chennai" in column (1) and the corresponding entries in column (2) thereof, the following 
entries shall, respectively, be inserted, namely:-
"Category 4A Joint Director, 
King Institute, Guindy
(i)	 Must have obtained the degree of M.B.B.S.,
(ii)	 Ph.D. (Microbiology) or Post Graduate degree in Microbiology; and
(iii)	Must possess experience for a period of not less than five years in 
the cadre of Deputy Director, King Institute, Guindy".
