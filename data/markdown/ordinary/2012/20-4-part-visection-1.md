Variations to the Sanctioned Tiruppur Detailed Development Plan No. 16 of
Tiruppur Local Planning Authority.
(Roc. No. 26649/2011/DP2.)
No. VI(1)/157/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section 1 of Section 33 of Town and Country Planning Act, 1971
(Act No. 35 of 1972). the Commissioner of Town and Country Planning, Chennai in the Proceedings Roc. No. 26649/2011
DP2, dated: 2nd May  2012. Proposes to make the following Deletion of Proposed “F1F1” 40'.0" Road pt, ''D1D1" 60'.0" Road
pt, “E2E2” 50’0.” Road pt, conversion of Lorry Parking pt, Industrial Area pt. and Reserved for School purpose pt, into
Residential Area in T.S. No. 7/1C, 7/1D, 7/1E of Block-9, Ward-F (Old Mannarai Village S.No. 251pt) of Tiruppur
Corporation to the Tiruppur Detailed Development Plan No. 16, Approved by Director of Town and Country Planning in
his Proceedings Roc. No.10799/1990 DP2, dated: 7th March 1991 of Tiruppur Local Planning Authority and the said
Notification was published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No. 20, Part VI—Section 1, Page No. 389 Publication
No. (VI) (1)/678/91, dated  29th May 1991.
2. Any person affected or interested in this draft variation may within 60 days from the date of publication of this
notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette can represent in person or submit in writing to the Member - Secretary,
Tiruppur Local Planning Authority and Commissioner, Tiruppur Corporation any objections and suggestions relating thereto.
3. The variation with plan may be inspected at free of cost at any time during office hours at the above said
Local Planning Authority Office.
VARIATIONS
1.
Wherever the expression in MAP No. 4 DDP/CN No. 1/1991 occurs the expression MAP No.5 DDP(V)/DTCP
No. 9/2012 shall be added at the end to be read with.
2.
In Schedule-III, Part-I all the entries to the “F1F1” 40'.0" Road pt. “D1D1” 60'.0" Road pt, “E2E2” 50'.0" Road pt, shall
be deleted.
3.
In Schedule-IV (Form No.7) in Serial No.1 the entry proposed “F1F1” 40'.0" Road pt, “D1D1” 60'.0"  Road pt, “E2E2”
50'.0" Road pt, shall be deleted and in Column No. 4 the following entry “16.96 Acres” shall be substituted.
4.
In Schedule-IV (Form No.7) in Serial No.3 the entry proposed Lorry Parking pt. shall be deleted and in Column
No. 4 the following entry “0.67 Acres” shall be substituted.
5.
In Schedule-IV (Form  No.7) in Serial No.8 the entry Reserved for School pt, shall be deleted and in Column No. 4
the following entry “2.87 Acres” shall be substituted.
6.
In Schedule-V (Form No.6) all the entries in the proposed “F1F1” 40'.0" Road pt, “D1D1” 60'.0" Road pt. “E2E2” 50'.0"
Road pt, Reserved for Lorry Parking pt, School Area pt, shall be deleted.
7.
In Schedule-IX, Form No.7, Ward-F, Block-9, T.S. No. 7/1C, 7/1D, 7/1E, (Old Mannarai Village S. No. 251pt) Industrial
Area “4.85 Acres” shall be deleted.
Chennai-600 002,
A. KARTHIK,
2nd May 2012.
Commissioner of Town and Country Planning.
