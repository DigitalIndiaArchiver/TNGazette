GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI.
Taking over the Direct Management of Masjid-E-Mahamoodi, Thousand Lights, Chennai.
(Lr.Rc.No. 19914/10/Chennai/B6.)
No. VI-3(a)/1/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995, the
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its Proceedings  in Item No. 112/10 in WEA.11/09/E5/Chennai, dated 6th July 2010 has
ordered to take over the management of Masjid-e-Mahamoodi, Thousand Lights, Chennai, under the Direct Control of
the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of one year from the date of Publication of the notification in the Tamil Nadu
Government Gazette and has appointed the Wakf Inspector, Chennai (Central) as its Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
23rd December 2010.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
Taking over the Direct Management of Mosque Inam @ Thamaraikulam Thaikka,
Polur Town, Thiruvannamalai District.
(Lr.Rc.No. 1210/07/VLR/B4.)
No. VI-3(a)/2/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995, the
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its Proceedings  in Item No. 67/10 (Rc.No.1210/07/B4/Vellore) dated 29th April 2010 has
ordered to remove Thiru Syed Roshan from the Post of Muthavalli of Mosque Inam @ Thamaraikulam Thaikka, Polur
Taluk, Thiruvannamalai District from the date of Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Notification and ordered to take from
the Management under the Direct Control of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board from the date of notification
Under Section 65(1) of the Wakf Act, 1995 and to appoint Superintendent of Wakfs/Vellore as its Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
27th December 2010.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
