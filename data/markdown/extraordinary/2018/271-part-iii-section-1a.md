RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND PANCHAYAT RAJ DEPARTMENT
AMENDMENT TO THE STANDING COMMITTEES OF THE DISTRICT PANCHAYAT 
(ELECTION OF MEMBERS) RULES, 1998.
[G.O. Ms. No. 106, Rural Development and Panchayat Raj (PR-1), 30th July 2018.]
No. SRO A-39(c)/2018.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 242 of the Tamil Nadu 
Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes 
the following  amendment to the Standing Committees of the District Panchayat (Election of 
Members) Rules, 1998:-
AMENDMENT.
In the said Rules, in the Appendix, in Form II, for the expression “Presiding of the meeting”, 
the expression “President” shall be substituted.
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
HANS RAJ VERMA,
