RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND PANCHAYAT RAJ DEPARTMENT
REMOVAL OF CHAIRMAN OF VEDASANDUR PANCHAYAT UNION COUNCIL, 
DINDIGUL DISTRICT UNDER THE TAMIL NADU PANCHAYATS ACT, 1994.

WHEREAS, a written notice of the intention to make a motion expressing want of confidence in Tmt.S.Savithri, 
Chairman, Vedasandur Panchayat Union Council, Dindigul district, signed by thirteen members, out of the sanctioned 
strength of fifteen members of the said Council, together with a copy of the motion proposed to be made and a written 
statement of charges against the said Chairman have been delivered to the Revenue Divisional Officer, Palani, Dindigul 
district in person by thirteen members as per sub-section (2) of section 212 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994 
(Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994);
AND WHEREAS, the Revenue Divisional Officer, Palani, Dindigul district caused a copy of the statement of charges 
along with a copy of the notice of motion to be delivered, to the said Chairman Tmt.S.Savithri and she submitted her 
reply to the charges;
AND WHEREAS, a motion expressing want of confidence in Tmt.S.Savithri, Chairman, Vedasandur Panchayat Union 
Council, Dindigul district had been carried with a support of twelve members out of the sanctioned strength of fifteen 
members of the said Panchayat Union Council in the meeting convened by the Revenue Divisional Officer, Palani, 
Dindigul district on the 11th April 2022 after following the procedures specified in the said section 212 and forwarded 
the same to Government;
NOW THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (13) of section 212 of the Tamil Nadu 
Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby removes Tmt.S.Savithri, 
Chairman, Vedasandur Panchayat Union Council, Dindigul district from the Office of the Chairman of the said Panchayat 
Union Council with immediate effect.
P. AMUDHA,	
Principal Secretary to Government.
