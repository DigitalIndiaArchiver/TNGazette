AGRICULTURE DEPARTMENT
The Organic Fertilizer Testing Laboratories at Coimbatore and Tiruchirappalli to be the Laboratories for the
purpose of Legal Validity under the Fertilizer (Control) Order.
[G.O.Ms.No. 259, Agriculture (AP 3), 9th September 2016.]
No. II (1)/AG/12/2016.—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-clause (1B) of clause 29 of the Fertilizer (Control) Order,
1985, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notifies that the Organic Fertilizer Testing Laboratories at Coimbatore and Trichirappalli
shall be the Laboratories for the purpose of the said clause.
GAGANDEEP SINGH BEDI,
Agricultural Production Commissioner
and Secretary to Government.
