  ENTRUSTING THE PREPARATION OF DETAILED PROJECT REPORT TO NABARD
CONSULTANCY SERVICES PRIVATE LIMITED (NABCONS), CHENNAI FOR ESTABLISHMENT
OF COLD CHAIN AND TERMINAL MARKET INFRASTRUCTURE UNDER THE ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 149, Finance (Salaries), 26th May 2016, Vaikasi 13, Thunmugi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2047.]
No. II(2)/FIN/345(b-1)/2016.
Under clause (f) of Section 16 of the Tamil Nadu Transparency in Tenders Act, 1998
(Tamil Nadu Act 43 of 1998), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notifies the entrustment of the preparation
of Detailed Project Report to Nabard Consultancy Services Private Limited (NABCONS), Chennai for the
establishment of Cold Chain and Terminal Market Infrastructure at Madurai, Tirunelveli, Coimbatore and Krishnagiri
in Tamil Nadu.

in Tamil Nadu Transparency in Tenders Act, 1998 (Tamil Nadu Act 43 of 1998) and Tamil Nadu Transparency in

percentage procured from own production centre and registered artisans;
(c) Tamil Nadu Handicrafts Development Corporation Limited shall charge reasonable service charges to
ensure that their quotes are cost competitive.
