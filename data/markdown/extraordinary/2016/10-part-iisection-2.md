In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 20 of the Prohibition of Employment as
Manual Scavengers and their Rehabilitation Act, 2013 (Central Act 25 of 2013), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
appoints the Sanitary Officers or the Sanitary Inspectors, as the case may be, of the City Municipal Corporations
and the Municipalities (except Sembakkam Municipality), the Municipal Commissioner  of the Sembakkam
Municipality and the Executive Officers of the Town Panchayats, to be Inspectors for the purposes of the said Act
and defines their respective territorial jurisdictions to be the local limits within which they shall exercise their
powers under the said Act.
K. PHANINDRA REDDY,
Principal Secretary to Government.
