No. SRO A-6/2019.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Sections 17-C, 17-D, 17-E, 18-B, 18-C, 20 and 21 read 
with Section 54 of the Tamil Nadu Prohibition Act, 1937 (Tamil Nadu Act X of 1937), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby 
makes the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu Liquor (Licence and Permit) Rules, 1981. 
2. The amendments hereby made shall come into force on and from the 1st April 2019.

and an application fee of Rs.3,000/- (Rupees three thousand only)", the expression "an annual privilege fee of Rs.12,00,000/- 
(Rupees twelve lakhs only) for Chennai City and Rs.8,00,000/- (Rupees eight lakhs only) for the other areas, a licence fee 
of Rs.25,000/- (Rupees twenty five thousand only) and an application fee of Rs.7,500/- (Rupees seven thousand and five 
hundred only)" shall be substituted.
