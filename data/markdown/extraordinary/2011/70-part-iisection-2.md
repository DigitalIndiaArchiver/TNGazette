In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 6 of the Tamil Nadu Manual Workers’
(Regulation of Employment and Conditions of Work) Act, 1982 (Tamil Nadu Act 33 of 1982), the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby establishes the "Tamil Nadu Cooking Food Workers' Welfare Board" for the
employment in cooking food in the State of Tamil Nadu.
