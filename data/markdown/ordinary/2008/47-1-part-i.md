Order: No. 1329, Public (Special-B), 1st December 2008.
Thiru Veerapandi S. Arumugam, Minister for Agriculture,
has been hospitalised for treatment  at Chennai and it will
take some more time for him to resume normal work. In
view of the above, under Clause (3) of Article 166 of the
Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
directs that the subjects “Agriculture, Agricultural Engineering,
Agro Service Co-operatives, Horticulture, Sugarcane Cess,
Sugarcane Development and Wasteland Development”
hitherto held by Thiru Veerapandi S. Arumugam, Minister for
Agriculture, be temporarily allocated to Thiru K. N. Nehru,
Minister for Transport in addition to the subjects now held by
him.
K. S. SRIPATHI,
Chief Secretary.
