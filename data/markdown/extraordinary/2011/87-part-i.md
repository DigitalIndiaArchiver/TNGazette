In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (3) of Section 15 of the Right to
Information Act, 2005 (Central Act 22 of 2005), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints
(1) Dr. P.A. Ramiah, I.A.S., (Retd), (2) Thiru C. Manoharan and (3) Thiru A. Arumuga Nainar
as State Information Commissioners in the Tamil Nadu Information Commission.
The State Information Commissioner shall hold office for a term of five years form the
date on which he enters upon his office or the date on which he attains the age of
sixty-five years, whichever is earlier.
S. MALATHI,
Chief Secretary.
