APPOINTMENT OF SPEAKER AND DEPUTY SPEAKER OF THE  TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY

No. II(1)/LAS/7(x)/2016.
"In exercise of the powers conferred by Article 188 of the Constitution of India, I, Dr. K. Rosaiah,
Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby appoint Thiru P. Dhanapal, Speaker, Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly and
Thiru Pollachi V. Jayaraman, Deputy Speaker, Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly, as the persons before whom
the Oath or Affirmation may be made and subscribed by the Members of the Legislative Assembly under the
said Article during the term of their office.
Raj Bhavan,
K. ROSAIAH,
Guindy,
Governor of Tamil Nadu.
Chennai-600 022.
3rd June, 2016.
A.M.P. JAMALUDEEN,
Secretary."
