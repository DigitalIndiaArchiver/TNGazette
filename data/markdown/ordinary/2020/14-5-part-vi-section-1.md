Periods of Adjournments of Subordinate Courts in Pudukkottai District for

(R.No.7667/2019) 
(D.No.1032/2020-A)

 In pursuance of the instructions of the High Court of Judicature, Madras in Official Memorandum in R.O.C. 
No.88001-A/2019/C3, dated:11-12-2019, it is hereby notified that the Subordinate Civil Courts in Pudukkottai District will be 
closed for Summer Vacation, Dussehra Holidays, Christmas Holidays and Public Holidays for the year 2020 as follows:-
I. (a) SUMMER VACATION:
The Principal District and Sessions Court, the Additional District 
Court, the Principal Sub Court (Assistant Sessions Court), 
Additional Sub Court (Assistant Sessions Court), Pudukkottai, 
Sub Court (Assistant Sessions Court), Aranthangi, All the 
District Munsif's Courts and only the Civil Unit of District 
Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Courts in Pudukottai District.
Will be closed from Friday, the 1st day of May, 2020 to 
Sunday, the 31st day of May 2020 (both days inclusive).
(b) DUSSEHRA HOLIDAYS:
The Principal District and Sessions Court, the Additional District 
Court, The Principal Sub Court (Assistant Sessions Court), 
Additional Sub Court (Assistant Sessions Court), Pudukkottai, 
Sub Court (Assistant Sessions Court), Aranthangi, All the 
District Munsif"s Courts and only the Civil Unit of District 
Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Courts in Pudukkottai District.
Will be closed from Saturday, the 17th day of October, 2020 
to Monday, the 26th day of October (both days inclusive).
(c) CHRISTMAS HOLIDAYS:
The Principal District and Sessions Court, the Additional District 
Court, The Principal Sub Court (Assistant Sessions Court), 
Additional Sub Court (Assistant Sessions Court, Pudukkottai, 
Sub Court (Assistant Sessions Court), Aranthangi, All the 
District Munsif"s Courts and only the Civil Unit of District 
Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Courts in Pudukkottai District.
Will be closed from Friday, the 25th day of December, 
2020 to Thursday, the 31st day of December (both days 
inclusive).

[Part  VI—Sec. 1
Constitution of a District Munsif Court at Alangulam in Tirunelveli District 
(Roc.No. 20937/2017/G/Judn.) 
No. VI(1)/156/2020
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts, Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 
1873) the High Court, Madras, hereby fixes Alangulam in Tirunelveli District as the place at which the District Munsif 
Court, Alangulam, shall be located.
NOTIFICATION-II
(Roc.No. 20937/2017/G/Judn.) 
No. VI(1)/157/2020
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 11 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873) 
the High Court, Madras, hereby directs that in Tirunelveli District, the Principal District Munsif Court, Ambasamudram 
shall cease to have local jurisdication over Kuthapanjan Village in Puthupatti Firka and entire Vengadampatti Firka of 
Alangulam Taluk; the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Cheranmahadevi shall cease to have local jurisdiction 
over entire Puthupatti Firka ((except Kuthapanjan Village) of Alangulam Taluk and the District Munsif Court, Tenkasi shall 
cease to have local jurisdiction over entire Nettur Firka, Alangulam Firka and Keelapavoor Firka of Alangulam Taluk and 
the District Munsif Court, Alangulam shall have and exercise local jurisdiction over the entire Taluk of Alangulam with 
effect from the date on which the District Munsif, Alangulam assumes charge of that Court.
NOTIFICATION-III
((Roc.No. 20937/2017/G/Judn.) 
No. VI(1)/158/2020
The High Court, Madras hereby directs and notifies that the District Munsif, Alangulam in Tirunelveli District shall 
exercise all the powers conferred on a District Munsif under Section 12 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central 
Act III of 1873) as amended up-to-date in regard to original suits and proceedings not otherwise exempted from his 
cognizance of which the amount or value of the subject matter does not exceed One Lakh Rupees within the local 
limits of his jurisdication with effect from the date on which the District Munsif, Alangulam assumes charge of that Court.
NOTIFICATION-IV
(Roc.No. 20937/2017/G/Judn.) 
No. VI(1)/159/2020
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 28 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 
1873) the High Court, Madras, hereby directs that the District Munsif, Alangulam, shall have and exercise jurisdiction 
of a Court of Small Causes under the Provincial Small Cause Courts Act, 1887 for the trial of suits cognizable by a 
court of small causes upto the pecuniary limits of Rs.5000/- (Rupees five thousand only) with effect from the date on 
which the District Munsif Court, Alangulam, assumes charge of that Court.
High Court, Madras,
