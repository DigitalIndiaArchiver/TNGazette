No. SRO B-6/2017.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following rules:-
2. The rules hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on and from 12th January 2010.
RULES
The General and Special rules applicable to the holders of permanent posts of Non-Technical Personal Assistants to
Principals of Government Engineering Colleges, Polytechnics and Special Institutions in Class LII of the Tamil Nadu General
Service shall apply to the holder of the temporary post of Regional Officer (Non-Technical) in the Directorate of Technical
Education, Chennai, subject to the modifications specified in the following rule:-
2.Constitution.—The post shall constitute a temporary addition to the said class in the said service.
