Amendment to the Tamil Nadu Slum Clearance Board Community Development Service Rules
[G.O. Ms. No. 93, Housing and Urban Development [SC2(2)], 9th June 2014.]
No. SRO A-13/2014.—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 38 read with Section 70 of
the Tamil Nadu Slum Areas (Improvement and Clearance) Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 11 of 1971), the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Tamil Nadu Slum Clearance Board Community Development
Service Rules, 1993.
AMENDMENT
