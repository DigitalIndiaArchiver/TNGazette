GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI.
Extension of period of Direct Management of Bappu Hakeem Pallivasal,
Ellaiamman Koil Street, Thanjavur Town.
(Lr.Rc.No. 15909/03/B11/TNJ.)
No. VI-3(a)/141/2010.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995, the
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its Order in item No.45/10 in Rc.No.15909/03/B11/TNJ, dated 29th April 2010 has ordered
to ratify the period of direct management of the above Wakf from 24th July 2009 to 28th April 2010 and to extend
the period of direct management for a further period of one year from 29th April 2010 of Bappu Hakeem Pallivasal,
Ellaiamman Koil Street, Thanjavur Town & District (which is under the direct control of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board).
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
9th November 2010.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
