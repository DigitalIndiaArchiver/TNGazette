Grant of No Objection Certificate
[Rc. No. E2(1)/1352/142591/2011.]
FORM-A2
[Notice under Rule 35A(4) of Tamil Nadu Cinemas (Regulation) Rule, 1957.]
No. VI(1)/83/2012.
Whereas Tr. Vimal Kumar Galada, residing at No. 112, Choolai High Road and V.V. Koil Street, Choolai, Chennai-112
has applied to the Commissioner of Police, Chennai Police, Chennai-8 for the grant of “No Objection Certificate” for the
construction of a permanent multiplex complex containing Five Screens, at Survey No. 11/1, at Door No. 2, Officers Line,
GST Road, Pallavaram, Chennai-43.
1. Notice is hereby given that any person having any objection to the grant of “No Objection Certificate” to the above
said site on the grounds of non-compliance by the applicant with any of the provisions of the Tamil Nadu Cinemas
(Regulation) Act, 1955 (T.N. Act IX of 1955) or with any of the Rules thereunder should file his objection in writing with
the Licensing Authority (Commissioner of Police) within 15 days of publication of this Notice.
2. Any objection filed after the period shall be liable to be summarily rejected.
Egmore, Chennai-600 008,
TRIPATHI,
19th January 2012.

Variations to the Approved Master Plan for the Coimbatore Local Planning Area
(Roc.No. 6122/2011/LPA-2.)
No. VI(1)/85/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-Section (4) of section 32 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning
Act  1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972) and in exercise of powers conferred by the G.O.Ms.No. 94, Housing and Urban
Development (UD4(1) Department, dated 12th June 2009 which has been published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette
No. 27 part II, section 2, page No. 228, dated 15th July 2009 the following variations are made to the Master plan for the
Coimbatore Local Planning Area approved under the said Act and published in the Housing and Urban Development
Department Notification No. Ii(2)/HOU/4377/94 at page 1078 of Part II section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette,
dated the 9th November 1994.
VARIATIONS
In the said Master Plan, in the  “LAND USE SCHEDULE” under the heading Corporation Area Coimbatore Municipal
Town under the subheading COIMBATORE DETAILED DEVELOPMENT PLAN NO: ‘15’
(i) against the entry “Residential” the expression “608/3, 609/1A, 1B, 1C, 1D, 610/1, 2pt; 611/1” shall be inserted before
the entry 615.
(ii) against the entry “Industrial” the expression “608/3, 609/1A,1B,1C,1D, 610/1, 2pt 611/1” the expression “608pt (except
608/3), 609 to 614 (except 609/1A,1B,1C,1D, 610/1, 2pt 611/1) shall be submitted.
Coimbatore,
G. EASWARAN,
24th February 2012.
Member-Secretary (Incharge),
Coimbatore Local Planning Authority.
