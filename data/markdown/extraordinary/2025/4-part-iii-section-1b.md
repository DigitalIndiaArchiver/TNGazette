In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the rule 4(a) of the Special Rules for the  Tamil Nadu Commercial 
Taxes Service (Section 6 in Volume II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 2016).
Amendments.
In the said Special Rules, in the ANNEXURE to Rule 4(a)-
 
(i) 
in item (ii) of column (2) “Qualifications” against Sl.No.2  “Direct Recruitment” in column (1) for the expression 
“thirty years”, the expression “thirty-two years” shall be substituted;
 
(ii) 
in the first proviso under item (ii) of the column (2) “Qualifications” against Sl.No.2 “Direct Recruitment” in 
column(1) for the expression “thirty-one years”, the expression “thirty-three years” shall be substituted;
 
(iii)  in the second proviso under item (ii) of the column (2) “Qualifications” against Sl.No.2 “Direct Recruitment” 
in column (1) for the expression “thirty-five years”, the expression “thirty-seven years” shall be substituted;
 
(iv) in the third proviso under item (ii) of the column (2) “Qualifications” against Sl.No.2 “Direct Recruitment” in 
column(1) for the expression “thirty-six years”, the expression “thirty-eight years” shall be substituted.
2. 
The Notification shall be deemed to have come into force on the 17th July, 2018.
  	
BRAJENDRA NAVNIT,
	
Principal Secretary to Government.
