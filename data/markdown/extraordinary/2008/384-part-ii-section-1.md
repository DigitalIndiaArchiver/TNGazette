(Elections)
DECLARATION OF FRIDAY, 9TH JANUARY, 2009 AS A PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN CONNECTION WITH THE
BYE-ELECTION TO TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY FROM 137. THIRUMANGALAM ASSEMBLY
CONSTITUENCY IN TAMIL NADU.
[G.O. Ms. No. 1399, Public (Elections-III) 27th December 2008.]
No. II(1)/PUE/62(a)/2008.
Under the Explanation to Section 25 of the Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881 (Central Act XXVI of 1881),
read with the Notification of the Government of India, Ministry of Home Affairs,  No.20-25-26, Public-I,
dated the 8th June, 1957, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby declares Friday, the 9th January, 2009, the
date on which the poll for the bye-election to Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly from 137. Thirumangalam
Assembly Constitutency will  take place, to be a public holiday for the areas comprised in the above
Assembly Constituency lying in Madurai District.
All Government offices including industrial establishments of the Government and Government controlled
bodies and all the educational institutions throughout the areas comprised in 137. Thirumangalam Assembly
Constituency lying in Madurai District will remain closed on 9th January 2009.
K.S. SRIPATHI,
 Chief Secretary.
