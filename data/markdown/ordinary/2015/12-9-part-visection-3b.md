TAMIL NADU VETERINARY AND ANIMAL SCIENCES UNIVERSITY, CHENNAI
Nomination of a Member of the Board of Management of Tamil Nadu Veterinary and Animal Sciences University.
[U.O. No. 60108/R.I./BOM/2014 - F.No. 17319/R.I/BOM/2014.]
No. VI-3(b)/12/2015.
It is hereby notified that as per Section 18(2)(f) under heading Class-II Other Members, Chapter IV of Tamil Nadu
Veterinary and Animal Sciences University (Tamil Nadu Act 42 of 1989) Dr. K.K. Vijayan, Director, Central Institute of
Brackishwater Aquaculture, 75, Santhome High Road, Chennai - 600 028, has been nominated by the Director General,
Indian Council of Agricultural Research, New Delhi to serve as a Member in the Board of Management of Tamil Nadu
Veterinary 
and 
Animal
Sciences University. His term shall be three years with effect from 21-12-2014 as per Section 18(5), Chapter IV of Act, 1989
(Tamil Nadu Act 42 1989).
Chennai-600 051,
T.J. HARIKRISHNAN,
16th March 2015.

UNIVERSITY OF MADRAS
Election [1] to the Syndicate by the Senate from Among its Members [2] to the Academic Council by the
Senate from Among its Members  [who are not engaged in Teaching.]
(No. V.I/Senate Election/02-2015/`139)
No. VI-3(b)/13/2015.
It is hereby notified that the election to [i] The Syndicate and [ii] The Academic Council will be held under Section 18(a) - Other
Members, sub-section [1] and section 23[a] - Other Members, sub-section [1] of the Madras University Act, 1923 respectively.
[i] To The Syndicate [Section 18(a) - Other Members sub-section (I) SIX MEMBERS to be elected by the Senate
from  among its members,
The newly elected Members will hold office as Member  of the Syndicate for a period of three years from 30-03-2015
to 29-03-2018 or till the date of cessation of his/her Membership in the Senate/Academic Council whichever is earlier.
[ii] To The Academic Council [Section 23(a)-Other Members sub-section (I)] Three Members  to be elected by
the Senate from among its members who are not engaged in teaching,
The Newly elected Members will hold office as Member of the Academic Council for a period or three years from the
date of receipt of nomination / the date of withdrawal / the date of election/the date of declaration of results whichever is
applicable for the election.
The above election will be conducted on the date of the meeting of the Senate Scheduled to be held on SATURDAY,
the 28th March 2015.
University Buildings,
DR. P. DAVID JAWAHAR,
Chepauk, Chennai - 600 005.
 Registrar.
