(Rc. No. E1(1)/48/4578/2015)
FORM “B”
(Rule 36 in Part - II and Rule 106 in Part VII)
No.VI(1)/306/2017.

“No Objection” to Thiru. E.Mohan, No. 53, By-Pass Road, SKLS Building, Red Hills, Chennai-52 for construction of

Village, Redhills, Madhavaram Taluk, Thiruvallur District, Chennai-52.

Naravarikuppam Village, Redhills, Madhavaram Taluk, Thiruvallur District, Chennai-52.
This No Objection Certificate is valid for a period of five years in the case Board of permanent Cinema from the date
here of and if within this period, the proposed Cinema is not put up, a fresh 'No Objection Certificate' should be applied
for date this 16th day of September 2017.

JUDICIAL NOTIFICATIONS
Conversion of existing Judicial Magistrate Court, Alangudi as District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court,
Alangudi in Pudukottai District.
(Roc. No. 80/C/2008/G/Judn)
No.VI(1)/307/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873)
the High Court, Madras, hereby fixes Alangudi in Pudukottai District as the place at which the District Munsif-cum-Judicial
Magistrate Court, Alangudi, shall be held.
NOTIFICATION-II
(Roc. No. 80/C/2008/G/Judn)
No.VI(1)/308/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 11 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873)
the High Court, Madras, hereby directs that in Pudukottai District, the District Munsif Court, Pudukottai, shall cease to have
local jurisdiction over the entire Alangudi Taluk comprising of Firkas viz., Malaiyur, Vallanadu, Vennavalkudi and Alangudi
and the District Munsif Court, Aranthangi shall cease to have local jurisdiction over the Keeramangalam Firka of Alangudi
Taluk and the District Munsif-cum-judicial Magistrate Court, Alangudi shall have and exercise local jurisdiction over the
entire Alangudi Taluk with effect from the date on which the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate, Alangudi, assumes
charge of that court.

NOTIFICATION-III
(Roc. No. 80/C/2008/G/Judn)
No.VI(1)/309/2017.
The High Court, Madras hereby directs and notifies that the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate, Alangudi in
Pudukottai District shall exercise all the powers conferred on a District Munsif under Section 12 of the Tamil Nadu Civil
Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873) as amended up-to-date in regard to original suits and proceedings not otherwise
exempted from his cognizance of  which the amount or value of the subject matter does not exceed One lakh Rupees
within the local limits of his jurisdiction with effect from the date of constitution of District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate
Court at Alangudi.
NOTIFICATION-IV
(Roc. No. 80/C/2008/G/Judn)
No.VI(1)/310/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 28 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873)
the High Court, Madras, hereby directs that the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate, Alangudi shall have and exercise
jurisdiction of a Court of Small Causes under the provincial Small Causes Court Act, 1887 for the trial suits cognizable
by a Court of Small Causes upto the pecuniary limits of Rs.5000/- (Rupees five thousand only) with effect from the date
of constitution of District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court at Alangudi.
Constitution of Separate District Munsif Court and Judicial Magistrate Court at Perundurai by converting the
existing District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Perundurai in Erode District.
(Roc. No. 1320/A/2010/G/Judn)
No.VI(1)/311/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873) the
High Court, Madras, hereby fixes Perundurai in Erode District as the place at which the District Munsif Court, Perundurai, shall
be held.
NOTIFICATION-II
(Roc. No. 1320/A/2010/G/Judn)
No.VI(1)/312/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 11 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873)
the High Court, Madras, hereby directs that in Erode District, the District Munsif Court, Perundurai, shall have local
Jurisdiction over the entire Perundurai Taluk with effect from the date of constitution of separate District Munsif Court
at Perundurai.
NOTIFICATION-III
(Roc. No. 1320/A/2010/G/Judn)
No.VI(1)/313/2017.
The High Court, Madras hereby directs and notifies that the District Munsif, Perundurai in Erode District shall exercise
all the powers conferred on a District Munsif under Section 12 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of
1873) as amended up-to-date in regard to original suits and proceedings not otherwise exempted from his cognizance of
which the amount or value of the subject matter does not exceed One Lakh Rupees within the local limits of his jurisdiction
with effect from the date of constitution of separate District Munsif Court at Perundurai.
NOTIFICATION-IV
(Roc. No. 1320/A/2010/G/Judn)
No.VI(1)/314/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 28 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873) the
High Court, Madras, hereby directs that the District Munsif, Perundurai shall have and exercise jurisdiction of a Court of Small
Causes under the provincial Small Causes Court Act, 1887 for the trial suits cognizable by a Court of Small Causes upto
the pecuniary limits of Rs. 5000/- (Rupees five thousand only) with effect from the date of constitution of separate District
Munsif Court at Perundurai.

[ Part VI—Sec.1
RE-DESIGNATION OF COURT AT PERUNDURAI IN ERODE DISTRICT
(Roc. No. 1320/A/2010/G/Judn)
No.VI(1)/315/2017.
Whereas the Government of Tamil Nadu have issued orders in G.O. Ms. No. 128, Home (Courts-III) Department, dated
14-02-2017 that a separate District Munsif Court be constituted at Perundurai and in G.O. (Ms). No. 912, Home
(Courts-II) Department, dated 19-12-2016, that the existing District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Perundurai be
converted as separate Judicial Magistrate Court at Perundurai in Erode District.
Hence, it is hereby informed that on and from the date on which a separate District Munsif Court at Perundurai is
constituted, the existing District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Perundurai shall be re-designated as Judicial
Magistrate Court, Perundurai.
