The Governor of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that the

for the lands covered under Tamil Nadu Land Reforms (fixation
of ceiling on land) Act, 1961, as the case may be, do here
publish the following notice under sub-section (1) of Section 3
of the Tamil Nadu  Acquisition of Land  for Industrial purposes
Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), by relaxing
G.O. Ms. No. 2954, Revenue,  dated 29th May 1973.
NOTICE

Acquisition of Land for Industrial purposes Act, 1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999) the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby acquired the lands specified in the Schedule below and
measuring an extent of 7.03.0 Hectares to the same, a little
more of less are needed for Industrial purpose, to wit for the
formation of ULTRA MEGA POWER PROJECT, CHEYYUR
No. 55. Vedal Village, Cheyyur Taluk, Kancheepuram  District.
The plan of the lands are kept in the office of Special
Tahsildar (Land Acquisition), Unit –IV, ULTRA MEGA POWER
PROJECT, CHEYYUR Scheme  and may be inspected at any
time during office hours.
