Principal District Munsif Court, Thoothukudi, District Munsif Court, Kovilpatti and attaching the same to the

Cancellation of Notification
(R.O.C. No. 1395/2009/G/JUDN.)
No. VI(1)262/2009.
The Notification under Section 11 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 for realignment of civil jurisdiction by
detaching the villages and firkas of Ottapidaram taluk under the jurisdiction of Principal District Munsif Court, Thoothukudi,
District Munsif Court, Kovilpatti and attaching the same to the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Vilathikulam
published in Issue No. 1 under Part VI—Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated 7th January 2009
stands cancelled.
