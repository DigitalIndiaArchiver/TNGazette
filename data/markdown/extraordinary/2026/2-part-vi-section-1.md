In exercise of the powers conferred by 2nd proviso of sub-section-7 of section-19 of the Right to Fair Compensation 
and Transparency in Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Act 2013,(Central Act-30 of 2013), as an 
appropriate Government conferred by the G.O.Ms.No.526 Revenue and Disaster Management Department, Land 
Administration wing- LA-1(1) Section, Dated.26.09.2020 hereby extend the time limit to the Notification issued in 
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary Notification No.45 Part VI—Section 1, Dated.29.01.2025 per sub section-1 
of section-19.
TIME EXTENSION
As per G.O.Ms.No.526 Revenue and Disaster Management Department, Land Administration wing- LA-1(1) 
Section, Dated.26.09.2020, Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary Notification No. 45 Part VI—Section 1, 
Dated.29.01.2025 has been issued in accordance with the provisions of sub-section-1 of section-19 of  Right to Fair 
Compensation and Transparency  in Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Act-2013 (Central Act-30 of 
2013), with regards to the acquisition of 5.10.07 Hectares Patta Land for Mettur Lift Irrigation Scheme (Filling  up of 100 
Dry Lakes in Sarabanga Basin by Surplus Flood Water from Mettur Dam by Lift Irrigation). 
AND WHEREAS, The Land Acquisition Officer and Revenue Divisional Officer, Mettur and Sankari. reported that as 
the funds allocated for the Lift Irrigation Scheme (Filling  up of 100 Dry Lakes in Sarabanga Basin by Surplus Flood Water 
from Mettur Dam by Lift Irrigation) have not been released it has not been possible to disburse due compensation to land 
owner who have consented to land acquisition through private negotiations, further, there has been a delay in passing 
final award in respect of landowners who have not consented to land acquisition and they above officers requested to 
extent the time  limit to the Notification issued in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette  Extraordinary Notification No.45 
Part VI—Section 1, Dated.29.01.2025 per  sub section-1 of section-19.
