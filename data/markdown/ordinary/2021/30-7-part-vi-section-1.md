Notice under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) and 
Tamil Nadu Acquisition Laws (Revival of Operation, Amendment and Validation) Act, 2019 (Tamil Nadu Act 38/2019)
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that the lands specified in the schedule below here to be 
required for the public purpose, for the formation of new road from Thoothukkudi - Vanchimaniyachi through Highways 
Department, Saminatham Village, Ottapidaram Taluk, in the District of Thoothukkudi through Highways Department, and 
it has already been decided that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands to be paid out of the 
funds controlled and managed by the Divisional Engineer (Highways), Construction and Maintenance, Thoothukkudi 
and after having considered the cause shown by the owners or the other persons having interest on such lands as 
the case may do hereby publish the following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu 
Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002). 
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) it is hereby 
acquired the lands specified in the schedule below measuring to an extent of 42479 sq.mtrs. of Dry, lands, to the same, 
a little more or less is needed for the purpose for the formation of new road from Thoothukkudi - Vanchimaniyachi 
comprised in S. No. 513/1 etc., Saminatham Village, Ottapidaram Taluk, Thoothukkudi District. The plan of the lands 
are kept in the office of the District Collector, Thoothukkudi and Special Tahsildar (Land Administration), Thoothukkudi 
may be inspected on any working day during office hours.
