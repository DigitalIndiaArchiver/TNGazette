APPOINTMENT OF MEMBERS IN THE EXISTING VACANCIES OF THE STATE HUMAN RIGHTS 
COMMISSION UNDER THE PROTECTION OF HUMAN RIGHTS ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No.835, Public (Human Rights),  29th December 2022,
மார்கழி 14, சுபகிருது, திருவள்ளுவர் ஆண்டு-2053.] 
No. II(2)/PUHR/1028(a)/2022.
In exercise of powers conferred under Section 22 (1) of the Protection of Human Rights Act, 1993
(Central Act 10 of 1994) as amended by Central Act 43 of 2006, the Governor of Tamil Nadu is pleased to appoint 
Thiru Justice Raja. Elango, High Court Judge (Retired) and Thiru V. Kannadasan, Advocate, as Members of 
the State Human Rights Commission, in the existing vacancies for a period of three years with effect from the 
date of assumption of charge.	
	
	
	
	
D. JAGANNATHAN,
	
Secretary to Government.
