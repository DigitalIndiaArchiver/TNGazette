The Governor of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that the
lands specified in the Schedule below are acquired under
TNRSP-II  Highways Purpose, to wit for Upgrading Malliyakarai-
Rasipuram–Thiruchengode-Erode Road (SH 79) from
Km 0/000 to  Km 30/600 and Km 51/400 to Km 71/300 in
Agaram Village, Thiruchengode Taluk, Namakkal District
and it having already been decided that the entire amount of
compensation to be awarded for the acquisition of lands is to
be paid out of the funds controlled and managed by the Highways
Department and after having  considered  the cause shown by
the owner or other persons having interest in the said lands,
[ 1 ]
as the case may be,  do hereby issue and  publish the
following notice under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the
Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act, 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu
Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act, 34 of 2002), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the manai lands
specified in the Schedule below and measuring to an
extent of 40 Sq.mtrs in Agaram Village, Thiruchengode
Taluk, Namakkal District to the same, a little more or less
needed for TNRSP-II Highways  Purpose for Upgrading
Malliyakarai-Rasipuram–Thiruchengode–Erode Road (SH 79)
from Km 0/000 to  Km 30/600 and Km 51/400 to Km 71/300.
The plan    of the    lands   under    acquisition  is   kept
in the Office of the  Special District Revenue Officer     (Land
Acquisition), Tamil  Nadu Road Sector Project -II, Salem
and may be  inspected at  anytime during office hours.
