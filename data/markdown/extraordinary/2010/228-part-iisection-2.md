NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
LAW DEPARTMENT
APPOINTMENT OF CERTAIN PERSON AS VICE-CHANCELLOR OF THE TAMIL NADU DR. AMBEDKAR
LAW UNIVERSITY, CHENNAI FOR CERTAIN PERIOD UNDER THE TAMIL NADU DR. AMBEDKAR
LAW UNIVERSITY ACT.
[G.O. (R.) No. 83, Law (LU), 28th July 2010.]
No. II(2)/LAW/486(e)/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-sections (1) and (3) of Section 12 of the Tamil Nadu Dr. Ambedkar
Law University Act, 1996 (Tamil Nadu Act 43 of 1997), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints Dr. V. Vijayakumar,
Professor of Law, UNHCR Chair on Refugee Law, National Law School of India University, Bangaluru-560 072 as the
Vice-Chancellor of the Tamil Nadu Dr. Ambedkar Law University, Chennai, for a period of three years from the date of his
assuming office.
S. DHEENADHAYALAN,
Secretary to Government.
