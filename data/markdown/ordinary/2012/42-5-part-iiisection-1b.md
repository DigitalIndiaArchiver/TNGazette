TAMIL DEVELOPMENT, RELIGIOUS ENDOWMENTS AND INFORMATION DEPARTMENT.
Amendment to the Ad hoc Rules relating to the Post of Joint Director in Information and
Public Relations Department.
[G.O. Ms. No. 353, Tamil Development, Religious Endowments and Information (ADMN.I), 25th October 2012.]
No. SRO B-47/2012.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Rules issued with Notification
No. SRO B-436 of 1988, dated the 22nd November 1988 published on Pages 291-292 of Part III—Section 1(b) of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 14th December 1988.
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in rule 4, in sub-rule (b), under the Table, for the expression "not less than three years" in column (2), against
the entry "2. By promotion" in column (1) there of the expression "not less than one year" shall be substituted.
M. RAJARAM,
Secretary to Government.
