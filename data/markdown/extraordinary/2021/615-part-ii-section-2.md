The Governor of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that the 
lands speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below  have to be acquired 
for Highways purpose for construction of Western Ring-road 
to Coimbatore City at Kalikkanaickenpalayam Village, Perur 
Taluk, Coimbatore District and it has already been decided 
that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the 
lands is to be paid out of the funds controlled or managed by 
the  Chief Engineer, Construction and Maintenance, Highways 
Department after considering the cause shown by the owner 
(or) other person having interest on such lands as the case 
[ 1 ]
may be do hereby publish the following notice  under 
sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways 
Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the 
Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 
of 2002), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires 
the land speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below measuring to 
an extent 146701 sq.mtr. of wet and dry lands 143780 
sq.mts of R. Dry  lands and 2921 sq.mts of R. Wet 
lands in  Kalikkanaickenpalayam Village, Perur Taluk, 
Coimbatore District for construction of Western Ring-road 
to Coimbatore City.
The plan of the lands is kept in the oﬃ  ce of the 
District Revenue Oﬃ  cer, Coimbatore and may be inspected 
at any time during oﬃ  ce hours.
Perur Taluk, Kalikkanaickenpalayam Village.
