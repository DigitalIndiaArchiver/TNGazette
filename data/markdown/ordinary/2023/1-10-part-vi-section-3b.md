from among themselves of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai Act, 1987.
(Rc.No. EIII(3)/35142/2022)
No. VI-3(b)/1/2023.
Under sub-statute (1) of Statute 5 of Chapter-XXI of the General Statutes of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical 
University, Chennai it is hereby published that the following (03) members have been elected to the Senate of the 
Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R Medical University, Chennai, by the teachers of each affiliated Medical Colleges from 
among themselves under Section 18(3)(a) of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R Medical University, Chennai Act, 1987 
(Tamil Nadu Act 37 of 1987). 
The period of membership of the following Professors shall be (03) three years or as long as they hold the post 
of Professor in the same Medical College, or the termination of, whichever is earlier.
