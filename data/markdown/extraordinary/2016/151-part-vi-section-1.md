JUDICIAL NOTIFICATION
CONFERMENT OF POWERS
(R.O.C. No. 68/2006-Con.B2.)
No. VI(1)/206(a)/2016.
No. 74/2016.—In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 18(1) of the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973
(Central Act 2 of 1974) read with Section 32(1) of the said Code, the High Court, Madras, hereby appoints the following
Presiding Officers of the Courts of Small Causes, Chennai, as Special Metropolitan Magistrates, for a period of another one
year with effect from 12-06-2016, to try and dispose of the cases under Section 138 of Negotiable Instruments Act, which
are pending in large number and which are being transferred from the files of Metropolitan Magistrate Courts, Chennai from
time to time and they shall exercise all the powers of Metropolitan Magistrate under the said Code for the said purpose:
