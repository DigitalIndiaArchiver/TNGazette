Elections to the Senate and the Standing Committee of the Academic Council by the Academic Council.
(No. V.1/ACElection/09-2018/132)
No.VI-3(b)/34/2018.
It is hereby notiﬁ ed that the election to [i] THE SENATE and [ii] THE STANDING COMMITTEE OF THE ACADEMIC 
COUNCIL will be held under Section 14-1 (a) - Other Members [2] and Section 24[k] of the Madras University Act, 1923. 
 
   
[i] 
To the Senate - Section 14-1[a] - Other Members [2]
THREE MEMBERS have to be elected by the Academic Council from among its members. 
The newly elected members will hold ofﬁ ce as a member of the Senate from 29-09-2018 for the residual period 
upto 28-09-2019. 
 
   
[ii] 
To  the  Standing  Committee of the Academic Council [Section 24[k] 
One Member shall be a Principal or a Teacher of Afﬁ liated Colleges, have to be elected by the Academic Council 
from among its members. 
The newly elected member will hold ofﬁ ce as a member of the Standing Committee of the Academic Council from 
29-09-2018 for the residual period upto 28-09-2019. 
The above election will be conducted on the date of the Meeting of the Academic Council scheduled to be held 
on SATURDAY, the 29th SEPTEMBER 2018.
