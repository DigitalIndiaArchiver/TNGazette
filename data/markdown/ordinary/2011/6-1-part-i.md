No. I/HF/4/2011.—Under the powers vested in clauses (a)
and (b) of Article 24 read with Article 25 of the Articles of
Association of the TAMPCOL, Chennai, the Government
appoint Thiru V.K. Subburaj, I.A.S., Principal Secretary to
Government, Health and Family Welfare Department, Chennai,
as Ex-Officio Chairman of the TAMPCOL in the place of
Thiru Ramesh Kumar Khanna, I.A.S., who has been
transferred.

Chief Secretary.

No. I/PUSP/5/2011.—The following Notification of the
Government of India, Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances
and Pensions, Department of Personnel and Training, New
Delhi, dated 25th January 2011 is republished:—
The President is pleased to direct that Shri Anil Kumar,
a member of the Indian Administrative Service, borne on the
Cadre of Tamil Nadu (TN: 91) is deemed to have resigned
from the Indian Administrative Service effective from 1st August
2004 in terms of Rule 7(2) of the AIS (Leave) Rules, 1955.

NAVNEET MISRA,
