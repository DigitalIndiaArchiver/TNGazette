MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION AND WATER SUPPLY DEPARTMENT
AMENDMENTS TO THE TAMIL NADU MUNICIPAL CORPORATIONS ENGINEERING AND
WATER SUPPLY SERVICE RULES, 1996.
[G.O. Ms. No. 59, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (MC5), 3rd March 2016,
Maasi 20, Manmadha, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2047.]
No. SRO A-3(g-1)/2016.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 106 of the Madurai City Municipal Corporation Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 15
of 1971), Section 108 of the Coimbatore City Municipal Corporation Act, 1981 (Tamil Nadu Act 25 of 1981),
Section 8 of the Tiruchirappalli City Municipal Corporation Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 1994) Section 8 of the Tirunelveli
City Municipal Corporation Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 28 of 1994) and Section 8 of the Salem City Municipal Corporation
Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 29 of 1994), the Government of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the
Tamil Nadu Municipal Corporations Engineering and Water Supply Service Rules, 1996.
2.
This amendment hereby made shall come into force on 3rd March 2016.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules,—
(1) in rule 3, in `THE TABLE', for the entires in column (2) against the entry “Assistant Engineer” in column (1) the
following entries shall be substituted, namely:—
(i)
By direct recruitment; and
(ii) by recruitment by transfer from the holders of the post of Junior Engineer in the Tamil Nadu Municipal Corporation
Engineering and Water Supply Subordinate Service:
Provided that appointment by direct recruitment and by recruitment by transfer shall be made in the
ratio of 3:1.
EXPLANATION
The Junior Engineers of the Tamil Nadu Municipal Corporations Engineering and Water Supply Subordinate Service
shall, on acquiring the qualification of B.E., Degree, be qualified for recruitment by transfer to the post of Assistant Engineer

AMENDMENTS TO THE TAMIL NADU TOWN PANCHAYATS, THIRD GRADE MUNICIPALITIES,
MUNICIPALITIES AND CORPORATIONS (ELECTIONS) RULES, 2006.
[G.O. Ms. No. 60, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (Election), 3rd March 2016.]
No. SRO A-3(g-2)/2016.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 303 of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of
1920), Section 347 of the Chennai City Municipal Corporation Act, 1919 (Tamil Nadu Act IV of 1919), Section 431 of the Madurai
City Municipal Corporation Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 15 of 1971), Section 430 of the Coimbatore City Municipal Corporation Act,
1981 (Tamil Nadu Act 25 of 1981), read with Section 8 of the Tiruchirappalli City Municipal Corporation Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu
Act 27 of 1994) and Section 8 of the Tirunelveli City Municipal Corporation Act, 1994, (Tamil Nadu Act 28 of 1994) and Section
8 of the Salem City Municipal Corporation Act, 1994, (Tamil Nadu Act 29 of 1994), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes
the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities, Municipalities and Corporations
(Elections) Rules, 2006, namely:—
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules,—
(1) in rule 2, in sub-rule (1), after clause (xxxii), the following clause shall be inserted, namely:-
“(xxxii-a) “Vice-Chairman” means the Vice-Chairman of a Town Panchayat or of a Third Grade Municipality or of a
Municipality, as the case may be”; and
(2) In rule 99, for the explanation, the following explanation shall be substituted, namely:-
“Explanation:—Notwithstanding anything contained in these Rules, for the purpose of this rule and rules 100 to 115,
“members” means, the Councillors of a Town Panchayat or a Third Grade Municipality or a Municipality, or a Municipal
Corporation, as the case may be, the Mayor of a Municipal Corporation and Chairman of a Town Panchayat or a Third Grade
Municipality or a Municipality, as the case may be.”.
