ESTABLISHMENT OF MARKET COMMITTEE FOR THE SATHANGADU IRON AND STEEL MARKET AREA SITUATED
WITHIN THE CHENNAI METROPOLITAN PLANNING AREA UNDER THE ACT.
NOTIFICATION-I.
[G.O. Ms. No. 91, Housing and Urban Development (UD 3(1),  12th June 2009,
Vaikasi 29, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. II(2)/HOU/288(c-1)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Specified Commodities Markets
(Regulation of Location) Act, 1996 (Tamil Nadu Act 24 of 1996), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby establishes the market
committee for the Sathangadu Iron and Steel Market Area situated within the Chennai Metropolitan Planning Area.

[G.O. Ms. No. 91, Housing and Urban Development (UD 3(1),  12th June 2009,

In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 9 read with Section 10 of the Tamil Nadu Specified
Commodities Markets (Regulation of Location) Act, 1996 (Tamil Nadu Act 24 of 1996), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
nominates the Member-Secretary, Chennai Metropolitan Development Authority, Chennai-600 008 as the Chairman and the
Senior most Chief Planner, Chennai Metropolitan Development Authority, Chennai-600 008 as the Vice-Chairman of the
market committee for the Sathangadu Iron and Steel Market Area.

DECLARATION OF WHOLESALE TRADE ACTIVITIES IN IRON AND STEEL MARKET WITHIN THE LOCAL AREA OF
CHENNAI METROPOLITAN PLANNING AREA TO BE CARRIED ON IN SATHANGADU IRON AND STEEL MARKET AREA
UNDER THE ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 92, Housing and Urban Development (UD 3(1),  12th June 2009,
Vaikasi 29, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. II(2)/HOU/288(c-5)/2009.
Whereas Iron and Steel trade is a “Specified Commodity” as defined in clause 13 of Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu
Specified Commodities Markets (Regulation of Location) Act, 1996. (Tamil Nadu Act 24 of 1996);
And whereas a new market complex has been constructed, developed and completed by Chennai Metropolitan
Development Authority for the purpose of carrying on the wholesale trade activities in the other part of the local area known
as “Sathangadu Iron and steel Market Complex” and the said market complex is ready for occupation;
And whereas, the Government have issued Notification under Section 4 of the said Act in the Tamil Nadu Government
Gazette, dated the 1st March 1999, declaring the Iron and Steel Market at Sathangadu as the Market Area in respect of
wholesale trade in Iron and Steel goods in Chennai Metropolitan Area with effect from 1st March 1999.
Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by clause (d) of sub-section (1) of section 7 of the Tamil Nadu
Specified Commodities Markets (Regulation of Location) Act, 1996 (Tamil Nadu Act 24 of 1996), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby declares that with effect on and from the 12th June 2009 the wholesale trade of Iron and Steel within the local area
of Chennai Metropolitan Planning Area shall be carried on in Sathangadu iron and steel market area.
