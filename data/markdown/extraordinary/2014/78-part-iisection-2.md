EXEMPTING CHENNAI MONORAIL PROJECT UNDER THE TAMIL NADU INFRASTRUCTURE
DEVELOPMENT ACT, 2012 (TAMIL NADU ACT 22 OF 2012.)
[G.O. Ms. No. 99, Finance (Infrastructure Cell), 7th April 2014,
Panguni 24,  Vijaya,  Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2045.]
No. II(2)/FIN/299(a)/2014.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 34 of the Tamil Nadu Infrastructure Development Act, 2012
(Tamil Nadu Act 22 of 2012), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby  notifies that the Chennai Monorail Project is
exempted from the said Act, since the Project was conceived and the Project related activities have commenced
prior to the commencement of the said Act.
K. SHANMUGAM,
Principal Secretary to Government.
