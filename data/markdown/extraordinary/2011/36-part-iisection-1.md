NOTIFICATIONS  BY  THE  LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY SECRETARIAT
LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY SECRETARIAT
DEATH OF THIRU POLUR M. VARADHAN,  MEMBER OF THE TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY.
[Letter No. 1038 A/2011-1, Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Secretariat (B.III), 28th January 2011,
Thai 14, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No. II(1)/LAS/3(a-1)/2011.
"The Honourable Speaker has learnt with deep regret the death of Thiru Polur M. Varadhan,
a Member of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly on 27th January 2011".
VACATION OF SEAT IN TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY.
[Letter No. 1038 A/2011-1, Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Secretariat (B.III), 28th January 2011,
Thai 14, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No. II(1)/LAS/3(a-2)/2011.
"A vacancy has occurred among the Members of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly, consequent
on the death of Thiru Polur M. Varadhan, a Member elected to the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly
from the 42. Chengam (SC) Assembly Constituency in Thiruvannamalai District".
