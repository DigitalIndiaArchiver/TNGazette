Under the Explanation to Section 25 of the Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881 (Central Act XXVI of 
1881) read with Notification of the Government of India, Ministry of Home Affairs No. 20-25-26, Public-1, 
dated 8th June 1957, the Government of Tamil Nadu hereby declares that Wednesday the 11th September 
2019 would be a Public Holiday on account of Muharram instead of 10th September 2019.
2. The above Public Holiday notified shall also apply to all State Government Undertakings/Corporations/
Boards etc.
