The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that 
the lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below to be acquired for 
the industrial purpose to wit for the formation of New Broad 
Gauge Railway line between Villupuram - Dindigul situated 
in Madampatty Village of  Manaparai Taluk in the District of 
Tiruchirappalli, and it having already been decided that the 
entire amount of compensation  to be awarded for the land/
lands is to be paid out of the lands controlled or managed 
by District Revenue Ofﬁ cer, Tiruchirappalli. The following 
notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the 
Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for the Industrial Purposes 
Act 1997.
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu 
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act 1997 
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999). The Governor of Tamil Nadu 
hereby acquires the land/lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below 
measuring of land to the same, a little more or less needed 
for industrial purpose wit for the formation of New Broad 
Gauge Railway line between Villupuram - Dindigul situated 
in Madampatty Village of Manaparai Taluk in the District of 
Tiruchirappalli.
The Plan of lands are kept in the ofﬁ ce of the Collector, 
Tiruchirappalli and Sub Collector, Tiruchirappalli may be 
inspected on any time during ofﬁ ce hours.
