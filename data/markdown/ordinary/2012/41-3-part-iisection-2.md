No. II(2)/HO/684/2012.—
[Tamil Nadu protection of Interests of Depositors (in
Financial Establishments) Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 44 of
1997)-Messers Susi Emu Farms India Private Limited and
other sister concerns, Erode District-Default in return of
deposits by the farm-Ad-interim order attaching the movable
properties of the farm under Section 3 of the said Act-Orders-
Issued.
The following Government Orders is published:—
 [G.O. Ms. No. 703, Home (Police XIX),
21st September 2012.]
Read:
From the Additional Director General of Police,
Economic Offences Wing II, Chennai, Letter
No. C1/8672/2012, dated‘ 21st August 2012.
Order: No 703, Home (Police XIX), 21st September 2012.
Whereas, complaints have been received from a number
of depositors that Messers Susi Emu Farms India Private
Limited and its sister concerns, Erode District, have defaulted
the return of deposits made by the depositors after maturity;
And Whereas, the Government are satisfied that the said
farm is not likely to return the deposits to the depositors and
hence, the Government have to protect the interests of such
depositors;
And Whereas, the movable properties i.e 12 vehicles and
cash in bank accounts worth Rs. 21,96,409.18 as specified in
the  Schedule to this order are alleged to have been procured
by the said farm from and out of the deposits collected from
the depositors;
Now, Therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by
Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Protection of Interests of Depositors
(in Financial Establishments) Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 44 of
1997), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes an ad-
interim order attaching the cash and other movable properties
standing in the name of Messers Susi Emu Farms India
Private Limited, Erode District and its partners as specified in
the Schedule to this order and transfers the control over the
said cash and other movable properties to the Competent
Authority, namely, the Competent Authority and District Revenue
Officer, Erode appointed under of the said Act.
2. The Competent Auithority, is requested to pursue further
action in accordance with the procedure laid down in
sub-sections (3) and (4) of Section 4 of the said Act and also
the formalities prescribed in the Tamil Nadu Protection of
Interests of Depositors (in Financial Establishment) Rules, 1997.
3. The Special Public Prosecutor, Special Court for the
Tamil Nadu Protection of Interests of Depositors (in Financial
Establishments) Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 44 of 1997),
Coimbatore is requested to render necessary assistance to
the Competent Authority in filling the application before the
said Special Court.
(By order of the Governor)

NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
ANIMAL HUSBANDRY DAIRYING AND FISHERIES DEPARTMENT.
ACQUISITION OF LANDS.
[G.O. Ms. No. 173, Animal Husbandary, Dairying and Fisheries (FS-4), 8th October 2012,
Purattasi 22, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2043.]
No.II(2)/AHDF/701/2012.—Whereas, it appears to the Government of Tamil Nadu that the lands specified in the Schedule
below and situated in Nagoor (Muttam) Village, Nagapattinam Taluk, Nagapattinam District are needed to the proposed
to be acquired for the formation of Tamil Nadu Fisheries University at Nagapattinam Notice to that effect is hereby given
to all to whom it may concern in accordance with the provisions of sub-section (1) of Section 4 of the Land Acquisition Act
1894 (Central Act I of 1894).
Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 4 of the said Act, the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby authorizes the Revenue Divisional Officer Nagapattinam, and his staff and workmen to exercise the powers
conferred by the said sub-sections and under Section 3(c) of the Act, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the Revenue
Divisional Officer Nagapattinam, to perform the functions of the Collector under Section 5(a) of the said Act.
