HIGHER EDUCATION DEPARTMENT
Amendments to the Special Rules for Tamil Nadu Collegiate Educational Service
[G.O. Ms. No. 213, Higher Education (F2), 5th September 2018, ÝõE 20, M÷‹H, F¼õœÀõ˜ Ý‡´-2049.]
No. SRO B-73/2018.— In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for Tamil Nadu Collegiate Educational 
Service [Section 8 B in Volume-II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 2016]. 
2.  (a) The amendments 1, 2, 3, 5 and 6 shall be deemed to have come into force on the 15th September 2016. 
 
(b) The amendments 4 (i) & (ii) hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st January 2006. 
 
(c) The amendment 4 (iv) hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force with effect from 21-11-2017. 
AMENDMENTS.
In the said Special Rules, 
 
(1) in rule 2(b), for the expression "sub rule (14) of rule 2 in part I Preliminary of the Tamil Nadu State and 
Subordinate Service Rules" the expression "Section 3(r) of the Tamil Nadu Government Servants (Conditions of Service) Act, 
2016 [Tamil Nadu Act, 14 of 2016]" shell be substituted. 
 
(2)  in rule 3, for the expression "(General Rule 22)" the expression "[Section 27 of the Tamil Nadu Government 
Servants (Conditions of Service) Act, 2016 (Tamil Nadu Act, 14 of 2016)]" shall be substituted. 
 
(3)  in rule 4 (a), for the expression "(General Rule 21)" the expression "[Section 26 of the Tamil Nadu Government 
Servants (Conditions of Service) Act, 2016, Tamil Nadu Act, 14 of 2016)]" shall be substituted.
 
(4)  in rule 5, in sub-rule (a), in the Table, in column (2), 
 
 
(i)  against the entry "3(a) Assistant Professors of Arts Colleges" in column (1), for the expression "35 years", 
the expression "57 years" shall be substituted.
 
 
(ii)  against the entry "3 (b) Assistant Professors of Training Colleges" in column (1) for the expression
"35 years" the expression "57 years" shall be substituted.
 
 
(iii)  against the entry "4. Director of Physical Education including Director of Physical Education in Law, Medical 
and Veterinary Colleges" in column (1) for the expression "35 years" the expression "57 years" shall be substituted.
 
 
(iv)  against the entry "5. College Librarian" in column (1) for the expression "35 years" the expression
"57 years" shall be substituted. 
 
(5)  in the 'Explanation' under rule 5, for the entries, "the ﬁ rst day of July of the year in which the selection for 
appointment is made the entries "the ﬁ rst day of July of the year in which the vacancy is notiﬁ ed" shall be substituted;
 
(6)  in the seventh Proviso to rule 6(a), for the expression "General rule 22" the expression "[Section 27 of the 
Tamil Nadu Government Servants (Conditions of Service) Act, 2016 (Tamil Nadu Act, 14 of 2016)]" shall be substituted. 
MANGAT RAM SHARMA ,
Principal Secretary to Government.

AMENDMENT.
In the said rules, in rule 5, in sub-rule (b), in THE TABLE for the entry "3. Stock Veriﬁ er" in column (1) and the entries 
relating thereto in column (2), the following entries, shall, respectively, be substituted, namely:-
"3. Stock Veriﬁ er 
(i) Must possess a Diploma in Mechanical  Engineering or Automobile 
Engineering awarded by the State Board of Technical Education 
and Training, Chennai or of a recognized Institution or Board and;
(ii) Must have special knowledge of Automobile Spare parts and must 
possess practical experience for not less than 3(three) years in any 
Automobile Workshop.
Explanation:- 
(i)  An Automobile Workshop shall mean an Automobile Workshop 
satisfying the deﬁ nition of "factory" in Section 2(m) of the Factories 
Act, 1948.
(ii) Factory shall mean "factory" as deﬁ ned in Section 2(m) of the 
Factories Act, 1948".
