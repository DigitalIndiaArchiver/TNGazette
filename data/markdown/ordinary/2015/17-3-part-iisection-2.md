Exemption from the Provisions of Regulation of Second
Master Plan for Chennai Metropolitan Area relating to
multi storied building for Odisha Bhavan at Pallikaranai
Village, Chennai under Tamil Nadu Town and Country
Planning Act.
[G.O. (3D) No. 15, Housing and Urban Development
(UD-V), 6th April 2015, ðƒ°Q 23, üò, F¼õœÀõ˜

No. II(2)/HOU/208/2015.—In exercise of powers conferred
under Section 113 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country
Planning Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972) the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby exempt the construction of Stilt Floor
+ 6 Floors building for Odisha Bhavan (Parking in stilt floor,
conference room, dining and kitchen in 1st floor, 20 guest
rooms in 2nd & 3rd floor, dormitories and 6 guest rooms
in 4th floor, auditorium in 5th & 6th floor and meter room
in terrace floor) at S.No.26/3 of Pallikaranai Village, 60 feet
Road of Tambaram - Velachery Road, Pallikaranai, Chennai
from the provisions of violation of Development Regulation
28(2)(A) relating to site extent; Development Regulation
28(2)(B) relatinq to plot frontage; Development Regulation 28
(2)G relating to set back violation on the eastern side;
Development Regulation 28(2)6 relating to Corridor width; of
Second Master Plan for Chennai Metropolitan Area 2026,
allowing architectural features at the top floor level coming
within the set back space at all four corners subject to
condition that the applicant has to relocate the transformer
and generator room proposed in the southern side to stilt
area and make the portico on the north as unsupported,
rectifying all the other defects and furnishing of No Objection
Certificates from Director of Fire and Rescue Service, Traffic
Police, Public Works Department and other usual No Objection
Certificates must be obtained.
