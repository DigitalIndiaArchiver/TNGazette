Election of Two Members to the Senate by the Academic Council From Among its own Body
[No. V.1/College/02-2015/163.]
No. VI-3(b)/14/2015.
I hereby declare that the following Two persons have been duly elected to the Senate by the
Academic Council from among its own body of whom not less than Five shall be teachers of Affiliated Colleges
under Section 14(a)-Other Members (2) of the Madras University Act, 1923 for the Residual Period upto 28-09-2016
or till the date of cessation of their Membership in the Academic Council whichever is earlier.
1. Dr. M. Venkatramanan, M.A., M.Phil., Ph.D.,
Principal,
D G, Vaishnav College,
Chennai-600 106.
2. Dr. Fatima Vasanth, M.A., (SW), M. Phil., Ph.D.,
Principal,
Patrician College of Arts and Science,
Chennai-600 020.
University Buildings,
Chepauk, Chennai - 600 005.
