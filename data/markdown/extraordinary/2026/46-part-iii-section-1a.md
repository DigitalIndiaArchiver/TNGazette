AMENDMENTS TO THE TAMIL NADU LIFTS AND ESCALATORS RULES, 1997.
[G.O.Ms.No.13, Energy (D2), 3rd Feburary 2026, தைை 20, விசுவாாவசு, திருவள்ளுவர் ஆண்டு-2057.]

In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of section 22 of the Tamil Nadu Lifts and Escalators Act, 1997 
(Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1997), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu 
Lifts and Escalators Rules, 1997:-
Amendments.
In the said Rules, -
(1)	  in rule 2, in sub-rule (1), after clause (g), the following clauses shall be inserted, namely:
“(h) 	“Addition to or Alteration of” in relation to a lift or an escalator shall mean any modification involving,-
(i)	  replacement or modification of the control system; or
(ii)	  change in the rated capacity or rated load; or 
(iii)	  change in the total travel and includes any combination of the above, but does not include routine
