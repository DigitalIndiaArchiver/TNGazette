GENERAL NOTIFICATION
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI-600 001.
Taking over of the Syed Abdullah Arab Wakf, Valayalkara Street, Trichy District under the direct
management.
(Rc. No. 4417/12/B5/Try.)
No. VI-3(a)/22/2014.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995 as amended by
the Act 27 of 2013,  the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in Item No. 107/13 in Rc. No. 4417/12/B5/Try,
dated 31st December 2013 has ordered to take over the management of Syed Abdullah Arab Wakf, Valayalkara Street,
Trichy District under the direct management of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of one year from the date of
Publication of this notification and to appoint the superintendent of Wakfs, Trichy as its Executive Officer.
F. ABDUL RAZICK,
Chennai-600 001,
Chief Executive Officer,
25th February 2014.
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
