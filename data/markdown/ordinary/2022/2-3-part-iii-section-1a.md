No. SRO A-1/2022—The Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Gandhi Adigal Police 
Medal Rules published with Prohibition and Excise Department Notification No. SRO/A-90(a)/96, at pages 1-3 (in English) of 
Part III-Section 1(a) of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary dated the 24th September 1996 as subsequently 
amended:-
Amendments.
In the Said Rules,- 
(1) in rule 1, for the expression "Officers of and below the rank of superintendent of Police belonging to the Tamil Nadu 
Police Service and other Subordinate ranks in the Subordinate Services of the Tamil Nadu Police", the expression "Indian 
Police Service Officer of and below the rank of Superintendent of Police borne to Tamil Nadu cadre, Tamil Nadu Police 
Service Officer of and below the rank of Superintendent of Police working in the State of Tamil Nadu and subordinate ranks 
in the Subordinate services of Tamil Nadu Police", shall be substituted; and  
(2) in rule 2, for the expression "curtailing the illicit liquor", the expression "Prohibition Enforcement", shall be substituted.
