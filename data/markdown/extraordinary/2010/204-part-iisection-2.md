The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied
that the lands specified in the Schedule below are required
for the purpose of the construction of Road Over Bridge in
lieu of existing Railway Level Crossing No.40 in between
Vandaloor and Guduvancheri Railway Station and its having
already been decided that the entire amount of compensation
to be awarded for the lands is to be paid out of the funds
controlled and managed by the Chief Engineer (Projects)
Highways Department, Chennai-15 and after having
considered the cause shown by the owner or other persons
having interest in the said lands, as the case may be do
hereby publish the following notice under sub-section (1) of
Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil
Nadu Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
 Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of Tamil Nadu
Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002),  the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands specified
in the schedule below and measuring 2446.5 square meters
to the same, a little more or less needed for the purpose of
the construction of Road Over Bridge in lieu of existing
Railway Level Crossing No. 40  between Vandaloor and
Guduvancheri Railway Stations.
The plan of the lands is kept in the office of the Special Tahsildar
(Land Acquisition) Inner Ring Road Scheme, Alandur Municipality
Campus, Alandur, Chennai-16.
