Explanatory Note.
Since, the New Comprehensive Scheme, 2011 formulated was set aside by the Hon’ble Madurai Bench of Madras 
High Court, by its order dated 18.04.2018 in W.P (MD) No.2893 of 2011 and M.P (MD) No.2 of 2011 with a direction to 
re-do the exercise, the present notification is proposed to be made as per the provisions contained in sub-section (3) 
of Section 100 of the Motor  Vehicles  Act,  1988 (Central Act 59 of 1988).  This is a New Comprehensive Scheme, 
2024 drawn for the entire State of Tamil Nadu, in the interests of public to provide an efficient, adequate, economic 
and properly co-ordinated road transport service to the people of villages/ hamlets/ habitations that have a population 
of 100 or more families, and to ensure that no route is left unconnected so as to reach the nearest bus stand or 
nearest town/ city where adequate bus services are provided to various destinations.
