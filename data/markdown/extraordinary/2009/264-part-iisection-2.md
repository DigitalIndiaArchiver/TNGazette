HOME, PROHIBITION AND EXCISE DEPARTMENT
AWARAD OF “GANDHI ADIGAL POLICE MEDAL” TO POLICE PERSONNEL FOR OUTSTANDING
WORK IN CURTAILING ILLICIT LIQUOR, ON THE OCCASION OF REPUBLIC DAY, 2010.
[G.O. Ms. No. 59, Home, Prohibition and Excise (VII), 29th September 2009.]
No. II(2)/HOPE/510(d)/2009.

Service have been selected to receive the award of “Gandhi Adigal Police Medal” on the occasion of Republic
day, 2010 for their outstanding work in curtailing the illicit liquor. They would be paid cash prize of
