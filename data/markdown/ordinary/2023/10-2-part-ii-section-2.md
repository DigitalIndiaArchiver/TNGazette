No. II(2)/CFCP/779/2005, published at page 422 of 
Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette,

Amendment.
In the said Notification, in the table, in column (2) 
under the heading “Powers”, for the entries in item (i), 
against the entry “Co-operative Sub-Registrar” in column 
(1) thereof, the following entries shall be substituted, 
namely:-

society, all the powers of a Registrar under Sections 72, 
80, 87, 90, 118, 129, 143 and 144 subject to the condition 
that the Co-operative Sub-Registrars shall so exercise 
the powers under Section 87 in respect of monetary 
cases involving a sum not exceeding Rs.2,00,000/-, under 
Section 90 in respect of monetary cases involving a sum 
not exceeding Rs.7,00,000/- and under Section 144 in 
respect of monetary cases involving a sum not exceeding 
Rs.1,00,000/-.”.
