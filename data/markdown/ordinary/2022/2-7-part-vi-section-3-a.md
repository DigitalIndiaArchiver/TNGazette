Framing of Scheme in Respect of Islamic Cultural Association, Nagercoil, Kanyakumari District.
(Lr.Rc.No. 1495/15/B1/TNV)
No. VI-3(a)/1/2022.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under section 69 of the Waqf Act 1995, the Tamil Nadu 
Waqf Board in its Resolution in item No.96/21 in Rc.No.1495/15/B1/TNV dated 27-10-2021 has approved the Scheme 
registered under the Societies Registration Act, 1964 as Scheme for the Waqf in respect of Islamic Cultural Association, 
Nagercoil, Kanyakumari District for better administration of the Waqf.
