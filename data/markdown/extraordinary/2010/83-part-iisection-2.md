HIGHWAYS AND MINOR PORTS DEPARTMENT
ACQUISITION OF LANDS
[G.O. Ms. No. 109, Highways and Minor Ports (HW2),
23rd March 2010, Panguni 9, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No. II(2)/HWMP/156(c)/2010.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that
the lands specified in the Schedule below, are required for the
purpose of the Construction of Road Over Bridge in lieu of
existing Railway Level Crossing No.54 in between Thirumani
and Chengalpattu Railway Station and it having already been
decided that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded
for the lands is to be paid out of the funds controlled and
managed by the Chief Engineer (Projects), Highways
Department, Chennai -16 and after having considered the cause
shown by the Owner or other person having interest in the said
lands, as the case may be, do hereby publish the following
Notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the
Tamil Nadu Highways Act,2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
 Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of Tamil Nadu High
ways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002), the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands specified in the Schedule
below and measuring 11763.0 Sq.Metres (Chengalpattu
Town 7401.0 Sq.Metres and Melamaiyur Village 4362.0
Sq.Metres) to the same, a little more or less are needed for
the purpose of the construction of Road Over Bridge in lieu
of existing Railway Level Crossing No.54 between Thirumani
and Chengalpattu Railway Station.
The plan of the lands is kept in the office of the Special
Tahsildar (Land Acquisition), Inner Ring Road Scheme,
Alandur Municipality Campus, Alandur, Chennai -16 and may
be inspected at any time during office hours.
