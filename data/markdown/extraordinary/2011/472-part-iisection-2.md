No. II(2)/PWD/527(b-1)/2011.
Whereas, it appears to the Government of Tamil Nadu that
the lands specified in the Schedule below and situated in the
Vellankuli Village in Ambasamudram Taluk in Tirunelveli District
are needed for a public purpose, to wit, for the formation of
a Flood Carrier Channel by interlinking Tamirabarani,
Karumeniaru and Nambiyaru Rivers for the benefit of the dry
areas in Tirunelveli District, notice to that effect is hereby given
to all to whom it may concern in accordance with the provisions
of the sub-section (1) of Section 4 of the Land Acquisition
Act, 1894 (Central Act I of 1894);
And, Whereas, it has become necessary to acquire the
immediate possession of the lands specified in the Schedule
below, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that the
lands be acquired under the provisions of sub-section (1) of
Section 17 of the said Act.
Now, Therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by
sub-section (2) of Section 4 of the said Act, the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby authorizes the Special Tahsildar (Land
Acquisition), Inter-linking of River Project - Unit I,
Ambasamudram Taluk at Cheranmahadevi and his staff and
workmen to exercise the powers conferred by the said
sub-section.
Under sub-section (4) of Section 17 of the said Act, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that in view of the
urgency of the case of the provisions of Section 5-A of the
said Act shall not apply to this case.
