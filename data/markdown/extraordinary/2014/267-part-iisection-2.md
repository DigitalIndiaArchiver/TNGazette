The Government of Tamil Nadu having been  satisfied that the lands specified in the Schedule below have to be acquired
for a public purpose, and it having already been decided that the entire amount of compensation to be  awarded for the lands
is to be paid by the Executive Engineer and Administrative Officer,  Salem Housing Unit, Salem. Out of the fund controlled or
Managed by the Chairman, Tamil Nadu Housing Board, Chennai and the following declaration is issued Under Section  6 of the
Land Acquisition Act, 1894 (Central Act 1 of 1894) read with Section 24 (1) (a)  of the Right to Fair Compensation and
Transparency in Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Act, 2013 (Central Act 30 of 2013).
DECLARATION
 Under Section 6 of the Land Acquisition Act 1894 (Central Act 1 of 1894), read with Section 24 (1) (a)  of the Right
to Fair Compensation and Transparency in Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Act, 2013 (Central Act 30
of 2013) the Governor of Tamil Nadu  hereby declares that the lands specified in the schedule below and measuring
7.10 acres, be the same a little more or less are needed for a public purpose to wit, for the construction of houses under
the Neighbourhood Scheme in No. 68 Vagurampatti Village, Namakkal Taluk, Namakkal District.
 A plan for the lands is kept in the office of the Special Tahsildar, (Land Acquisition) Neighbourhood Scheme, Salem  may
be inspected at any time during office hours.
