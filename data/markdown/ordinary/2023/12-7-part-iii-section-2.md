JUDICIAL NOTIFICATION
 THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT MADRAS
Amendment to Appendix G of the Code of the Civil Procedure, 1908
(R.O.C. No. 53767/2018/F1)
No. SRO C-4/2023.
	 In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 122 of the Code of Civil Procedure, 1908 (Central Act V of 
1908) and of all other powers thereunto enabling and with the approval of the Government of Tamil Nadu, the 
High Court, Madras, makes the following Amendment to Appendix G of the Code of Civil Procedure, 1908. The 
Amendment shall come into force with effect from the date of publication in the Official Gazette.
Amendment
In the Code of Civil Procedure, 1908 (Central Act V of 1908), in the Appendix G, after the end of existing 
Form No. 6B and before Form No.7, insert the following new Forms as Form No. 6C (After Admission of the 
Second Appeal) and Form No. 6D (Before Admission of the Second Appeal).
