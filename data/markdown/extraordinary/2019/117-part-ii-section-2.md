The Governor of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that the 
lands  speciﬁ ed in the schedule below have to be acquired 
for industrial purpose to wit for Tamil Nadu Electricity Board 
and it having already been decided that the entire amount or 
compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid out 
of the funds controlled of managed  by  Tamil Nadu Electricity 
Board. The following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of 
Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for industrial 
Purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu 
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil 
Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby 
acquires the lands speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below and 
measuring 1.34.90  hectare to the same, a little more or less 
needed for industrial purposes, to wit, for Bhavani Barrage 
Kattalai Kathavani -II  Scheme of Tamil Nadu Electricity Board.
The plans of the lands are kept in the oﬃ  ce of the District 
Revenue Oﬃ  cer, Erode  and may be inspected at any time 
during oﬃ  ce hours.
