Remission of tax payable by Government of Tamil Nadu
due to fixation of maximum safe laden weight by
Government of India, for certain period under
Tamil Nadu Motor Vehicles Taxation Act.
[G.O. Ms. No. 404, Home (Transport-V), 20th May 2009,
¬õè£C 6, F¼õœÀõ˜ Ý‡´-2040.]
No. II(2)/HO/262/2009.—In exercise of the powers
confirmed under Section 20-A of the Tamil Nadu Motor
Vehicles Taxation Act, 1974, the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby remits the tax payable due to fixation of maximum
safe laden weight by Government of India in their notification
No. S.O. 690 (E), dated the 25th September 1982 and in
notification No. S.O. 416 (E), dated the 8th June 1989 issued
in supersession of the above notification and as subsequently
amended in 1991 in respect of the goods vehicles plied in
the State of Tamil Nadu between 27th September 1982 and
9th May 1991.
Ad-Interim Order  attaching of the properties due to default
in return of deposits by Sterling Tree Magnum (India)
Limited, Alwarpet, Chennai under Tamil Nadu
Protection of Interests of Depositors (in Financial
Establishments) Act.
[G.O. Ms. No. 347, Home (Police XIX), 24th April 2009.]
No. II(2)/HO/263/2009.—Whereas, complaints have been
received from a number of depositors that Sterling Tree
Magnum (India) Limited, Alwarpet, Chennai, a Financial
Establishments, which has defaulted the return of deposits
made by the depositors after maturity;
2. And, whereas, the Government are statisfied that the
said Financial Establishment is not likely to return the
deposits to the depositors and hence, the Government have
to protect the interests of such depositors;
3. And, whereas, immovable properties specified in the
Schedule to this order are alleged to have been procured
by Thiru Palani alias S.V. Velmurugan, Manager Plantation
of the said Financial Establishment from and out of the
deposits collected from the depositors;
4. Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred
by Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Protection of Interests of
Depositors (in Financial Establishments) Act, 1997 (Tamil
Nadu Act 44 of 1997), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
makes an ad-interim order attaching the properties of Thiru
Palani alias S.V. Velmurugan, Manager, Plantation of the
said Sterling Tree Magnum (India) Limited, Alwarpet,
Chennai, as specified in the schedule to this order and
transfers the control over the said properties to the
Competent Authority, namely, the District Revenue Officer,
Chennai appointed under the provisions of the said Act,
for the purpose.
5. The Competent Authority is requested to pursue
further action in accordance with the procedure laid down
in sub-sections (3) and (4) of Section 4 of the said Act
and also the formalities prescribed in the Tamil Nadu
Protection of Interests of Depositors (in Financial
Establishments) Rules, 1997.
6. The Special Public Prosecutor, Special Court for the
Tamil Nadu Protection of Interests of Depositors (in
Financial Establishments) Act, 1997 is requested to render
necessary assistance to the Competent Authority, in filing
the application, before the said Special Court.
