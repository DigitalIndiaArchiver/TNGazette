In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (1) of Section 6-I and Section 58 of the Chennai City Municipal
Corporation Act, 1919 (Tamil Nadu Act IV of 1919) sub-section (1) of Section 10-D and Section 65 of the Madurai City Municipal
Corporation Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act XV of 1971) sub-section (1) of Section 10-D and Section 67 of the Coimbatore City Municipal
Corporation Act, 1981 (Tamil Nadu Act 25 of 1981) read with Section 8 of the Tiruchirappalli City Municipal Corporation Act, 1994
(Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 1994), Section 8 of the Tirunelveli City Municipal Corporation Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 28 of 1994),
Section 8 of the Salem City Municipal Corporation Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 29 of 1994), Section 8 of the Tiruppur City Municipal
Corporation Act, 2008 (Tamil Nadu Act 7 of 2008), Section 8 of the Erode City Municipal Corporation Act, 2008 (Tamil Nadu Act
8 of 2008), Section 8 of the Vellore City Municipal Corporation Act, 2008 (Tamil Nadu Act 26 of 2008) Section 8 of the Thoothukudi
City Municipal Corporation Act, 2008 (Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 2008), sub-section (1) of Section 43-B and Section 27 and of the
Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920) and sub-rule (3) of  rule 99 of the Tamil Nadu Town
Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities, Municipalities and Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006, the Tamil Nadu State Election
Commission hereby fixes 20-11-2011 as the date for convening a meeting to elect Chairman of Wards Committee of all the
Corporations and 21-11-2011 as the date for convening a meeting to elect the Members of Taxation Appeals Committee of all the
Municipalities and Town Panchayats, Members of Appointment Committees of all the Corporations, Municipalities and Town
Panchayats and Members of Contract Committees of all the Municipalities in   accordance with the following programme:—
