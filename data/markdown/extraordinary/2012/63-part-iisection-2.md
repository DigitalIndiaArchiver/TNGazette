COMMERCIAL TAXES AND REGISTRATION DEPARTMENT
DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF THE TAMIL NADU VALUE ADDED TAX
(SECOND AMENDMENT) ACT, 2012.
[G.O. Ms. No. 31, Commercial Taxes and Registration (B1), 9th March 2012,
Maasi 26, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2043.]
No. II(2)/CTR/145(c)/2012.
In exercise of the powers  conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the
Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax (Second  Amendment) Act, 2012  (Tamil Nadu Act
4 of 2012),  the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the 10th day of March 2012,
as the date on which the said Act shall come into force.
SUNIL PALIWAL,
Secretary to Government.
