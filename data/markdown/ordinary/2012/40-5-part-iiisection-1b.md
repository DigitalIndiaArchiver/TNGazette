NOTIFICATIONS BY GOVERNMENT
ANIMAL HUSBANDRY, DAIRYING AND FISHERIES DEPARTMENT.
Amendments to Adhoc Rules relating to the Post of Livestock Inspector, Grade-I,
in Tamil Nadu Animal Husbandry Subordinate Service.
[G. O. Ms. No.159, Animal Husbandry, Dairying and Fisheries (AH-1), 14th September 2012,
Aavani 29, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2043..]
No. SRO B-46/2012.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution
of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the rules for the post of
Livestock Inspector, Grade-I, published with the Agriculture Department Notification No. SRO-B 641-75, dated the
2nd December 1975, published at page 712 of Part-III Section 1(b) of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette,
dated the 31st December 1975 as subsequently amended:-
(i) The amendments (a), (b) and (d) hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the
19th September 1977.
(ii) The amendment (c) hereby made shall come into force on the 14th September  2012.
AMENDMENTS.
In the said Rules,—
(a)
in rule 1, for the expression, ''Livestock Assistants, Grade-I'' the expression "Livestock Inspectors,
Grade-I'' shall be substituted;
(b)
In rule 3, for the expression,  ''Livestock Assistants, Grade-II'' the expression "Livestock
Inspectors, Grade-II'' shall be substituted;
(c)
in rule 4, for the word “eight'' the word “five'' shall be substituted;
(d)
Rule 5 shall be omitted.
GAGANDEEP SINGH BEDI,
Secretary to Government.
