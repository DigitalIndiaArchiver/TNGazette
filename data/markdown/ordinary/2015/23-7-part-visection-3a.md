GENERAL  NOTIFICATION
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI
Extension of period of Direct Management of Dasthagir Sahib Dargah, Pallivasal and Burial Ground,
Krishnampet, Chennai District.
(Lr. Rc. No. 1077/2014/B9/Chennai.)
No. VI-3(a)/33/2015.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995 the
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in item No. 48/14 in Rc. No. 1077/14/B9/CHN, dated 27-11-2014 has resolved
to ratify the period of direct management of Husra Thaikal, Royapuram Chennai from 25-07-2005 to 26-11-2014
and to extend the period of direct management of the Wakf for a further period of one year from the date of
Board's Order (27-11-2014).
Chennai-600 001,
K. RASHEETH ALI,
5th June 2015.
District Revenue Officer /
Chief Executive Officer
Tamil Nadu Waqf Board.
