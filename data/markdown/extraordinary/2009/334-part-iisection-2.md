In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (3) of Section 1 of the Motor Transport  Workers
(Tamil Nadu Amendment) Act, 2008 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 2009), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
appoints 23rd December, 2009 as the date on which the said Act shall come into force.
