APPOINTMENT OF Tmt. V. CHELLAMMAL MOORTHY, SPECIAL GOVERNMENT PLEADER
IN MADURAI BENCH OF HIGH COURT OF MADRAS AS ADDITIONAL ADVOCATE
GENERAL-V OF TAMIL NADU FOR MADURAI BENCH OF HIGH COURT OF MADRAS
AT MADURAI.
[G.O. Ms. No. 789, Public (Law Officers), 25th  August 2009.]
No. I/PULO/14(a)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Article 165 of  the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints Tmt. V. Chellammal Moorthy, Special
Government Pleader in Madurai Bench of High Court of Madras as Additional Advocate
General-V for the State of Tamil Nadu in the Madurai Bench of High Court of Madras at
Madurai,  from the date of her assuming office.
2. The Additional Advocate General-V of Tamil Nadu will look after all the cases
pertaining to all departments before Madurai Bench of High Court of Madras.
D. JOTHI JAGARAJAN,
Secretary to Government.
