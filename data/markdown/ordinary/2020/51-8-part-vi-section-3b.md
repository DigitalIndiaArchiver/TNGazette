Election to the Standing Committee on Academic Aﬀ airs
Class II - Other Members - Sub-Sec.(2) of Section 23 Under Chapter III of Bharathidasan University Act 1981 - 
Two members elected by the Principals of aﬃ  liated colleges from among themselves in accordance with the system of 
proportional representation by means of the single transferable vote. 
In this connection, nominations are invited in the prescribed format which can be obtained from the Registrar and 
Returning Oﬃ  cer on requisition and on submission of Demand Draft for Rs.590/- (Including GST - Five Hundred and 
Ninety only) drawn in favour of Bharathidasan University, Tiruchirappalli on or after the date of notiﬁ cation viz. 23.12.2020 
with a stamped self-addressed envelope to the value of Rs.25/- (Rupees twenty ﬁ ve only) or in person. 
Nomination papers shall be enclosed in an envelope superscribed as follows, as the case may be 
"NOMINATION FOR ELECTION TO THE SYNDICATE - BY THE PRINCIPALS OF THE AFFILIATED COLLEGES 
FROM AMONG THEMSELVES 
or 
"NOMINATION FOR ELECTION TO THE SYNDICATE - BY THE TEACHERS OF THE AFFILIATED COLLEGES 
OTHER THAN PRINCIPALS FROM AMONG THEMSELVES WHO ARE THE MEMBERS OF THE SENATE" 
or 
"NOMINATION FOR ELECTION TO THE STANDING COMMITTEE ON ACADEMIC AFFAIRS - BY THE PRINCIPALS 
OF AFFILIATED COLLEGES FROM AMONG THEMSELVES". 
The Members are requested to send the nomination by Registered Post with (Acknowledgement Due) so as to 
reach the Registrar and Returning Oﬃ  cer within the date and hour ﬁ xed or delivered to the Registrar and Returning 
Oﬃ  cer either in person or by messenger within the date and hour ﬁ xed. 
Nomination papers that are not being sent as stated above shall be declared invalid.
