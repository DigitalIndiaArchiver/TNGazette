No. SRO B-76/2017.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Agricultural
Engineering Service. (Section 38 in Volume—II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1969).
2. The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on and from 19th September, 2016.
AMENDMENTS
1. In rule 2,
(i) In sub-rule (a), in the Table,-
(a)
In column (3), against the expression “Class IV” in column (1) and the expression “Assistant Executive
Engineer (Agricultural Engineering)” "in column (2) thereof, the expression, “(i) by direct recruitment” shall be omitted;
(b) item “(ii)” shall be re-numbered as item “(i)”;
(c) item “(iii)” shall be re-numbered as item “(ii)”;
(ii)
For sub-rule (b), the following sub-rule shall be substituted, namely :-
“(b) Appointment to vacancies arising in Class IV shall be made by  promotion from among the holders
of the Post of Assistant Engineer in Class V and  by recruitment by transfer from among the holders of the post of Junior
Engineer in  the Tamil Nadu Agricultural Subordinate Service in the ratio of 3 : 1 and shall be  filled up in the following
order of rotation, namely:-
(i) By Promotion;
(ii) By recruitment by transfer;
(iii) By Promotion; and
(iv) By Promotion
2. In rule 5, for the expression “to the Posts of Assistant Executive Engineer  (Agricultural Engineering) and Assistant
Engineer (Agricultural Engineering)”, the  expression “to the Post of Assistant Engineer (Agricultural Engineering)” shall
be substituted.
3. In rule 6, in sub-rule (a) in the TABLE, the entry “Class IV” in column (1)  and the corresponding entry ” 32 years”
in column (2) thereof shall be omitted.
4. In rule 7,-
(i) sub-rule (a) shall be omitted;
(ii) sub-rule “(b)” shall be re-numbered as sub-rule “(a)” of  that rule;
(iii)
(a) sub-rule “(c)” shall be re-numbered as sub-rule  “(b)” of that rule;
(b)
in sub-rule (b) as so re-numbered, the expression “Assistant Executive Engineer (or)” shall be omitted;
(iv) sub-rule (d) shall be omitted.
5. In rule 8, in sub-rule (a), the expression “by direct recruitment or” shall be omitted;
6. In the Annexure for the entries in columns (2) and (3), against the  expression “CLASS-IV Assistant Executive
Engineer (Agricultural Engineering)” in  column (1) thereof, the following entries shall, respectively, be substituted,
