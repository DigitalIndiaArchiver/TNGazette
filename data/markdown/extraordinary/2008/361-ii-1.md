NOTIFICATIONS  BY  THE  LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY SECRETARIAT
LEGISLATIVE  ASSEMBLY  SECRETARIAT
PROROGATION OF TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY.
(S.O. Ms. No. 232, Legislative Assembly Secretariat, 1st December 2008,
Karthigai 16, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2039.)
No. II(1)/LAS/57(b)/2008.
“In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-clause (a) of clause (2) of Article 174 of the Constitution of India,
I, SURJIT SINGH BARNALA, Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby prorogue with effect from the date of this notification, the
session of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly which commenced on the 10th November 2008.
Raj Bhavan,
SURJIT SINGH BARNALA,
Guindy,
Governor of Tamil Nadu.
Chennai-600 022,
Dated: 30th November, 2008.
M. SELVARAJ,
Secretary”.
