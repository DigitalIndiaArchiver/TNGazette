[G.O.Ms.No. 293, Health and Family Welfare (Z1),

No.II(2)/HFW/687/2020.—In 
exercise 
of 
the 
powers 
conferred under sub-section (2) of Section 11 of the 
Tamil Nadu Nurses and Midwives Act, 1926 (Tamil Nadu 
Act III of 1926), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes 
the following amendments to the Rules framed under the 
Tamil Nadu Nurses and Midwives, Act, 1926 (Tamil Nadu Act 
III of 1926), the draft of the same having been previously 
published as required by sub-section (1) of Section 11 of 
the said Act.
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖග
In the said Rules,
(1) 
in rule 32, after the expression “Section I or II”, 
the expression “Section I,II,III or IV” shall be inserted;
(2) 
in rule 32-A, for the expression, “Section I or II”, 
the expression “Section I,II,III or IV” shall be inserted;
(3) 
in Appendix B, in Form IV-A, under the heading 
“Instructions for ﬁ ling in form”, in item (4), after the 
Sub-heading “Section II” and the entries relating thereto, 
the following Sub-headings and the entries relating thereto, 
shall, respectively be added, namely:-
SECTION III
The names of Nurses who were not ﬁ rst registered with 
another State in India and shall be divided into the following 
ﬁ ve Parts, namely:-
Part A   – General Nursing (Transgender Women Nurses)
Part B – General Nursing (Transgender Men Nurses)
Part C – Midwives (Transgender)
Part D – Health Visitors (Transgender)
Part E – Auxiliary Nurse-Midwives (Transgender)
SECTION IV
The names of Nurses who were not ﬁ rst registered with 
another State in India and shall be divided into the following 
ﬁ ve Parts, namely:-
Part A – General Nursing (Transgender Women Nurses)
Part B – General Nursing (Transgender Men Nurses)
Part C – Midwives (Transgender)
Part D – Health Visitors (Transgender)
Part E – Auxiliary Nurse-Midwives (Transgender)”.
