MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION AND WATER SUPPLY DEPARTMENT
DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF THE TAMIL NADU MUNICIPAL LAWS
(AMENDMENT) ACT, 2009.
[G.O. Ms. No. 150, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (Election), 20th August 2009.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/424(a)/2009.
 In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Municipal Laws
(Amendment) Act, 2009 (Tamil Nadu Act 15 of 2009), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the
1st day of September 2009, as the date on which the said Act shall come into force.
NIRANJAN MARDI,
 Secretary to Government.
