DEATH OF HIGH DIGNITARIES – THIRU BHAIRON SINGH SHEKHAWAT,
FORMER VICE PRESIDENT OF INDIA
[G.O. Ms. No. 442, Public (Protocol-I), 15th May 2010.]
No. II(2)/PUPRO/283(b)/2010.
The Government of Tamil Nadu announce with profound regret the death of
Thiru Bhairon Singh Shekhawat, Former Vice President of India on 15th May 2010.
The National Flag will be flown at half mast on 15th May 2010 throughout the State.
(By Order of the Governor.)
K.S. SRIPATHI,
Chief Secretary.
