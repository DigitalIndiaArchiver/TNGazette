NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
DTP—II-2 Ex. (337)
HOME DEPARTMENT
[Commissioner of Police, Tiruppur-appointment as Additional District Magistrate under the Chennai City Police
(Extension to the City of Tiruppur) Act, 2012  (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 2012)]
[G.O. Ms. No. 922, Home(Pol.XIV) Department, 6th December 2012.]
No. II(2)/HO/815(b)/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 20 of the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 (Central Act 2
of 1974) read with Section 4 of the Chennai City Police (Extension to the City of Tiruppur)  Act, 2012  (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 2012),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby appoints the Commissioner of Police for Tiruppur City, to be an Additional District Magistrate
and empowers him to exercise and perform in relation to City of Tiruppur, the following powers and functions, namely:-
(1) the powers conferred  under  sub-section (4)  of Section 24, Sections 133 and 144 of the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973
(Central Act  2 of 1974); and
(2) the functions of the nature specified in clause (b) of sub-section (4) of Section 3 of the said Code, exercisable  by an
Executive  Magistrate.
R.  RAJAGOPAL,
 Principal Secretary to Government.
