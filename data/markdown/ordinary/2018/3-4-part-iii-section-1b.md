No. SRO B-4/2018.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Police Service 
(Section 22 in Volume II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 2016).
AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules.—
 
(1) in rule 2, in the tabular column, in column (2), under the heading “Class IA”,  against the entry "Category (2) 
Deputy Superintendent of Police (Technical)'' in column (1), for clauses (a) and (b), the following clause shall be substituted, 
namely:-
“Recruitment by transfer from the post of Inspector (Technical)”. and
(2) in rule 4, in sub-rule (F), in the Table, against the entry “Deputy Superintendent of Police (Technical)” in column (1),—
 
(i) the entry “(i) Direct Recruitment” in column (2) and the corresponding entries relating thereto in column (3) shall 
be omitted;
 
(ii) in the entry “(ii) Recruitment by transfer” in column (2), the expression “(ii)” shall be omitted.
NIRANJAN MARDI,
Additional Chief Secretary to Government.

No. SRO B-5/2018.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Labour Service 
(Section 20-B in Volume II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1979).
2. The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 26th August 2010.
AMENDMENTS.
In the said Rules.-
(1) for the expression “Joint Commissioner of Labour” wherever it occur, the expression “Additional Commissioner of 
Labour” shall be substituted;
(2) for the expression “Deputy Commissioner of Labour” wherever it occur, the expression “Joint Commissioner of Labour” 
shall be substitued;
(3) for the expression “Assistant Commissioner of Labour (Establishment and General)” wherever it occur, the expression 
“Deputy Commissioner of Labour (Establishment and General)” shall be substituted; 
(4) for the expression “Assistant Commissioner of Labour” wherever it occur, the expression “Deputy Commissioner of 
Labour” shall be substituted;
(5) for the expression “Labour Ofﬁ cers” wherever it occur, the expression “Assistant Commissioners of Labour” shall be 
substituted; 
(6) for the expression “Labour Ofﬁ cer” wherever it occur, the expression “Assistant Commissioner of Labour” shall be 
substituted;
(7) for the expression “Inspectors of Labour” wherever it occur, the expression “Assistant Commissioners of Labour 
(Enforcement)” shall be substituted;
(8) for the expression “Inspector of Labour” wherever it occur, the expression “Assistant Commissioner of Labour 
(Enforcement)” shall be substituted;
(9) for the expression “Inspectors of Plantations”, wherever it occur, the expression “Assistant Commissioners of Labour 
(Plantation)” shall be substituted;
(10) for the expression “Inspector of Plantations”, wherever it occur, the expression “Assistant Commissioner of Labour 
(Plantation)” shall be substituted;
(11) for the expression “Inspectress of Labour”, wherever it occur, the expression “Assistant Commissioner of Labour 
(Women Welfare)” shall be substituted;
MANGAT RAM SHARMA,
Principal Secretary to Government.
