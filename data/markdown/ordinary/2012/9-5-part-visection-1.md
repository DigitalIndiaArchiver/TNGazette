TAMIL NADU MEDICAL COUNCIL, CHENNAI
Elections to the Tamil Nadu Medical Council, Chennai during the year 2012.
(Ref. No. TNMC/G81/2012.)
PRECEPT
No. VI(1)/89/2012.
In accordance with rule 2, in regard to the Elections to the Tamil Nadu Medical Council, Chennai, under clause (f)
of sub-section (1) of section 5 of the Madras Medical Registration Act, 1914 (Act IV of 1914) as amended by the Madras
Medical Registration (Amendment) Act XVI of 1938, notice is hereby given that an election of Seven members of the
Council to be elected from amongst themselves by the registered Medical Practitioners whose names appear in the
printed Medical Registers (Electoral Roll-Compact Disc) available in the Medical Council Office upto the year 2011
will be held.
Nomination paper in Form II under the rule will be supplied by the Returning Officer to any elector who may apply
for the same. These must be filed in accordance with the instructions thereon and returned to the Returning Officer not
later than 3 P.M on Monday, the 27th February, 2012. The scrutiny of nomination papers will take place at 11 A.M. on
Tuesday the 28th February, 2012 at the office of the Returning Officer. A list of valid nominations will be published in
the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette on Wednesday the 14th March, 2012. Last date for withdrawal of nomination is
3 P.M. on Wednesday the 28th March, 2012. Publication of final list of valid nominations is on Wednesday the
11th April, 2012. Voting papers and other forms connected therewith will be sent by Post by the Returning Officer on
or before Friday, the 25th May, 2012 to all electors with instructions printed therein and the voting papers are returnable
to the Returning Officer not later than 5 P.M. on Monday, the 18th June, 2012. The scrutiny and counting of votes will
take place at the office of the Returning Officer from 19th June, 2012 (Tuesday) 11 A.M. onwards.
If for any reason of the dates fixed for the various proceedings in the conduct of the election happens to fall on a
holiday the proceedings fixed for that day will take place in the next day and at the time fixed already.
All Communications regarding the elections should be addressed as hereunder.
The Returning Officer, Tamil Nadu Medical Council, Chennai Elections, New No. 914, Old No. 569, Poonamallee
High Road, Arumbakkam, Chennai-600 106.
