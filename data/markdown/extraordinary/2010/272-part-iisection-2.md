TOURISM AND CULTURE DEPARTMENT
APPOINTMENT OF ONE MAN COMMISSION OF INQUIRY TO INQUIRE INTO THE STRIKE BY
STUDENTS OF GOVERNMENT FINE ARTS COLLEGE, CHENNAI UNDER THE COMMISSIONS OF
INQUIRY ACT.
AMENDMENT TO NOTIFICATION
[G.O. Ms. No. 199, Tourism and Culture (CA 1-1), 7th September 2010,
Aavani 22, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No. II(2)/TC/554(f)/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Commissions of Inquiry Act, 1952
(Central Act LX of 1952), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the
Tourism and Culture (CA1-1) Department Notification No.II(2)/TC/186(c)/2010, published at
pages 1-2 of Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the
5th April 2010:—
AMENDMENT
In the said Notification, in Paragraph 5, for the expression “three months”, the expression “six months”
shall be substituted.
V. IRAI ANBU,
Secretary to Government.
