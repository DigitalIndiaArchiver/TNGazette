(Roc. CKICP/CLA/15(1)/SH-79/14/2022)
NOTICE UNDER SECTION 15(1) OF TAMIL NADU HIGHWAYS ACT, 2001(TN ACT 34 OF 2002) READ 
WITH THE TAMIL NADU LAND ACQUISITION LAWS (REVIVAL OF OPERATION, AMENDMENT AND 
VALIDATION) ACT, 2019.
No. VI(1)/335(a-1)/2022.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that the lands specified in the schedule below are acquired 
under Chennai – Kanniyakumari Industrial Corridor Project for Highways purpose to wit for Strengthening and widening of 
SH-79, Tiruchengode - Erode Road from KM 83/350 to KM 86/300 at Thokkavadi (Part-VI) Village in Tiruchengode 
Taluk, Namakkal District, and it having already been decided that the entire amount of compensation to be 
awarded for the lands to be paid out of the funds controlled and managed by the Divisional Engineer (H), 
Salem (CKICP), Tamil Nadu after having considering the cause shown by the owner or other persons having 
interest on such lands as the case may be do hereby publish the following notice is issued under sub-section 
(1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (TN Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) 
read with the Tamil Nadu Land Acquisition Laws (Revival of Operation, Amendment and Validation) Act, 2019, 
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notified that the Dry lands specified in the schedule below and measuring 
to an extent of 99 sq.mtr., are acquired in Thokkavadi (Part-VI) Village in Tiruchengode Taluk, Namakkal 
District, to the same, a little more or less is needed for Chennai-Kanniyakumari Industrial Corridor Project for 
State Highways purpose, to wit for strengthening and widening of SH-79, Tiruchengode - Erode Road from 
KM 83/350 to KM 86/300. 
The plan of the lands under acquisition are kept in the Office of the Special District Revenue Officer
(Land Acquisition), Chennai–Kanniyakumari Industrial Corridor Project, Salem and office of the Special Tahsildar 
(Land Acquisition), Chennai–Kanniyakumari Industrial Corridor Project, Tiruchengode Unit which may be inspected 
at any time during the office hours.

(Roc. CKICP/CLA/15(1)/SH-95/15/2022)
No. VI(1)/335(a-2)/2022
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that the lands specified in the schedule below are acquired under 
Chennai–Kanniyakumari Industrial Corridor Project for Highways purpose to wit for formation of By-pass in of SH-95, Mohanur- 
Namakkal-Senthamangalam-Rasipuram Road from KM 25/065 to KM 26/450 at Chithambarapatti (By-pass)(Part-2) Village 
in Senthamangalam Taluk, Namakkal District, and it having already been decided that the entire amount of compensation to 
be awarded for the lands to be paid out of the funds controlled and managed by the Divisional Engineer(H), Salem (CKICP),
Tamil Nadu after having considering the cause shown by the owner or other persons having interest on such lands as the 
case may be do hereby publish the following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways 
Act, 2001 (TN Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) read with the
Tamil Nadu Land Acquisition Laws (Revival of Operation, Amendment and Validation) Act, 2019, the Governor of Tamil Nadu 
hereby notified that the Dry lands specified in the schedule below and measuring to an extent of 244 sq.mtr. are acquired in 
Chithambarapatti (By-pass)(Part-2) Village in Senthamangalam Taluk, Namakkal District, to the same, a little more or less is needed 
for Chennai-Kanniyakumari Industrial Corridor Project for State Highways purpose, to wit for purpose to wit for formation of By-pass in 
SH-95, Mohanur- Namakkal-Senthamangalam-Rasipuram Road from KM 25/065 to KM 26/450. 
The plan of the lands under acquisition are kept in the office of the Special District Revenue Officer (Land Acquisition), 
Chennai-Kanniyakumari Industrial Corridor Project, Salem and office of the Special Tahsildar (Land Acquisition), Chennai– 
Kanniyakumari Industrial Corridor Project, Tiruchengode Unit which may be inspected at any time during the office hours.
