ENVIRONMENT AND FORESTS DEPARTMENT
Amendment to the Tamil Nadu Timber Transit Rules, 1968
[G.O. Ms. No. 28, Environment and Forests, 30th March 2009.]
No.SRO A-16/2009.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Sections 35 and 36 of the Tamil Nadu Forest Act, 1882
(Tamil Nadu Act V of 1882), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Tamil Nadu Timber
Transit Rules, 1968:—
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, after rule 9, the following rule shall be added, namely:—
“10.  Penalties:—Whoever infringes any of the provisions of these rules in respect of timber other than scheduled
timber shall be punished with imprisonment which may extend to one month or with fine which may extend to two hundred
rupees or with both”.
DEBENDRANATH SARANGI,
Principal Secretary to Government (In-charge).
HOME DEPARTMENT
Amendments to Tamil Nadu Cinemas (Regulation) Rules, 1957
[G.O. Ms. No. 405, Home (Cinema), 20th May 2009.]
No.SRO A-17/2009.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 10 of the Tamil Nadu Cinemas (Regulation) Act,
1955 (Tamil Nadu Act IX of 1955), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu
Cinemas (Regulation) Rules, 1957.
2. The amendments hereby made except clause (2) (a) shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st day of
January 2007.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules,—
(1) in rule 3, after clause (g), the following clause shall be inserted, namely:—
“(gg) ‘Multiplex’ means the building wherein a minimum of two theatres together with or without multi activities on
commercial basis or with Malls”;
(2) in rule 83, in sub-rule (1A),—
  (a) after clause (a), the following clause shall be inserted, namely:—
“(aa) The licensing authority may on application by the licensee alter the rates of admission for any class of
accommodation, if there has been any change in the amenities provided either in that class of accommodation or generally
in the theatre or for any other valid reasons”;
   (b) for clauses (c) and (d), the following clauses shall be substituted, namely:—
