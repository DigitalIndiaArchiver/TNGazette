JUDICIAL NOTIFICATIONS
Amendment to the Civil Procedure Code Rules
(Roc. No. 322/2011/F1)
No. SRO C-8/2015.
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 122 of the Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, the High Court,
Madras to make the following amendment to Rule 10-A [inserted by the Civil Procedure Code (Amendment)
Act, 1976 - Central Act 104 of 1976] in Order XXII in the First Schedule of the Code.
AMENDMENT
In Rule 10-A of Order XXII in the First Schedule to the Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, between the words
"about it", and "and the court shall........." the following sentence shall be included; "and he shall also furnish
the details of the legal representatives of the deceased, wherever possible”.
Amendment to the Tamil Nadu Mediation Rules
(Roc. No. 4711-A/2013/F1)
No. SRO C-9/2015.
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 122 of the Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, the High Court,
Madras to make the following amendment to sub-rule (b) of Rule 5 of the Tamil Nadu Mediation Rules, 2010.
AMENDMENT
The existing sub-rule (b) of Rule 5 of the Tamil Nadu Mediation Rules 2010, the following words shall be
substituted.
“(b) Legal practitioners with five years standing at the Bar”.
High Court, Madras,
P. KALAIYARASAN,
3rd February 2015.
Registrar General.
