In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 96 of the Tamil Nadu Goods and Services Tax Act, 2017 
(Tamil Nadu Act 19 of 2017), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby constitutes the Tamil Nadu Authority for Advance Ruling 
consisting of the following members for Advance Ruling, namely:- 
(i) Tmt Manasa Gangotri Kata, IRS (C&CE), Joint Commissioner of GST & Central Excise, Tamil Nadu & 
 
Puducherry; and 
(ii) Thiru C. Palani, Joint Commissioner (State Tax) (Taxation) (Full Additional Charge).
