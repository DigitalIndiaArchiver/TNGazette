[Notice under Rule 35A(4) of Tamil Nadu Cinemas (Regulation) Rule, 1957]
No. VI(1)/5/2010.
Whereas Thiru R. Rangarajan son of  late Thiru Ramabhadran, aged 45 years, residing at Flat No. 2-D, ‘River
Heights’, No. 315, Valluvarkottam High Road, Nungambakkam, Chennai-34 has applied to the Commissioner of Police,
Chennai Police, Chennai-8 for the grant of “No Objection Certificate” towards location clearance for permanent multiplex
containing 5 Screens, at T.S. No. 2, Block No. 13, Villivakkam Village, Perambur-Purasawakkam Taluk, Door No. 1/1,
Mettu Street, Chennai-49, belonging to Thiruvalargal A.G.S. Cinemas Private Limited, Chennai-17.
1. Notice is hereby given that any person having any objection to the grant of “No Objection Certificate” above said
site on the grounds of non-compliance by the applicant with any of the provisions of the Tamil Nadu Cinemas
(Regulation) Act, 1955 (Tamil Nadu Act IX of 1955) or with any of the Rules there under should file his objection in
writing with the Licensing Authority (Commissioner of Police) within 45 days of publication of this Notice.

Arrangements for the Principal Seat at Madras and for the Madurai Bench for Christmas Holidays during the
year 2009.
(R.O.C. No. 4514-A/2009/C3)
No. VI(1)/19/2010.
Notification No. 249/2009.— It is hereby notified that for the Christmas holidays 2009 (24-12-2009 to 31-12-2009)
and from (01-01-2010 to 03-01-2010), Hon’ble Thiru Justice C. Nagappan and Hon’ble Thiru Justice P. Jyothimani, will
sit at Principal Seat at Madras as Vacation Judges on Wednesday, the 30th day of December 2009, to dispose of only
urgent matters. Their Lordships will first constitute a Division Bench to dispose of urgent Bench matters and after the
Bench work, Their Lordships will sit single to dispose of the urgent matters as follows:—
Hon’ble Thiru Justice C. Nagappan
:
Criminal side matters
Hon’ble Thiru Justice P. Jyothimani
:
Writ and Civil matters
2. Likewise, Hon’ble Thiru Justice R.S. Ramanathan and Hon’ble Thiru Justice T.S. Sivagnanam, will sit at Madurai
Bench as Vacation Judges on Wednesday, the 30th day of December 2009, to dispose of only urgent matters. Their
Lordships will first constitute a Division Bench to dispose of urgent Bench matters and after the Bench Work, Their
Lordships will sit single to dispose of the urgent matters as follows:—
Hon’ble Thiru Justice R.S. Ramanathan
:
Criminal side matters
Hon’ble Thiru Justice T.S. Sivagnanam
:
Writ and Civil matters
3. Tmt. R. Susheela Devi, Deputy Registrar (R.T.I. Act), Tmt. V. Uma, Assistant Registrar (Admn. II) and Thiru
J. Kumar, Assistant Registrar (C.S. IV), will be the Vacation Officers for the Principal Seat at Madras and Thiru
R. Ramachandran, Deputy Registrar (Lok Adalat/Mediation and Conciliation Centre), Thiru M.K. Jayaraman, Assistant
Registrar (Criminal Side) and Tmt. K. Varathalakshmi, Assistant Registrar (Records), will be the Vacation Officers for
Madurai Bench.
4. Notice of any application of urgent matters alone shall be presented along with the papers to the Vacation
Officers on Tuesday, the 29th day of December 2009, before 1.30 P.M. both in Principal seat at Madras and at Madurai
Bench. Sitting of the Vacation Court will be from 10.30 A.M. to  4.45 P.M. on Wednesday, the 30th day of December
2009, both in Principal seat at Madras and at Madurai Bench.
5. On Court sitting days, the Registry will function from 10.00 A.M. to 5.45 P.M. or till the rising of the Court,
whichever is later. On filing day (29-12-2009) and other days, the Registry (both the Principal seat at Madras and
Madurai Bench) will function from 10.00 A.M. to 4.45 P.M.
