HOME  DEPARTMENT
[G.O.  Ms. No. 1076, Home (SC), 24th December 2010.]
No.I/HO/2/2011—The following Notification of the
Government of India, Ministry of Home Affairs, dated
23-12-2010, is republished:—
XXX Consequent upon inclusion of the name of
Ms. E. Selvi Hema Piramila in the Select List of 2008 at
Sl.No. 5A below the name of Ms. A. Radhika (Sl.No.5) and
above  the name of Ms. R. Lalitha Lakshmi (Sl.No.6) on the
recommendations of a Review Selection Committee which met
on 24-09-2010 for reconsideration of her case for inclusion of
her name in the Select List of 2008 for appointment to the Indian
Police Service by promotion from Tamil Nadu Police Service in
compliance with order dated 28-04-2010 of the Hon'ble CAT,
Madras Bench in O.A. No. 1122/2009 filed by her, which has
been approved by the Union Public Service Commission on
09-12-2010, the President in exercise of the powers conferred
by sub-rule (1) of  rule 9 of the Indian Police Service (Recruitment)
Rules, 1954, read with sub-regulation (1) of regulation 9 of the
Indian Police Service (Appointment by Promotion) Regulations,
1955, is pleased to appoint Ms. E. Selvi Hema Piramila
(D.O.B:30-04-1967), a member of Tamil Nadu Police Service to
the Indian Police Service on probation and to allocate her to the
cadre of Tamil Nadu under sub-rule (1) of the rule 5 of the Indian
Police Service (Cadre) Rules, 1954.
The appointment will take effect from the date of issue of
this notification.

2. The  above District Judges (Entry Level) will draw
a pay of Rs. 51,550/- in the Scale of Pay of Rs. 51550-1230-
58930-1380-63070 and other usual allowances and they will be
on probation for a total period of 2 years from the date of joining
duty within a continous period of three years.
The selected candidates are requested to furnish Physical
Fitness Certificate direct to the Registrar General, High Court of
Madras under intimation to Government.
3. During the period of probation, the above District Judges
will undergo:—
(i)
Training as Civil Judge (Junior Division)
for a period of not lelss than six months;
(ii)
Training as Civil Judge (Senior Division)
for a period of not less than six months; and
(iii)
Training in Forensic Science for a period of not less
than two months.
4.
The above District Judges (Entry Level) should also
pass the Account Test for Executive Officers within
the period of probation.  Only after satisfactory
completion of the training, the above District Judges will
be posted to act as District Judge (Entry Level).
5.
The posting orders for training as Civil Judges (Junior
Division), Civil Judges (Senior Division), etc. and joining
instructions will be issued by the Hon'ble Madras High
Court.  The above District Judges (Entry Level) are
informed that other terms and conditions specified in
Tamil Nadu State Judicial Service (Cadre and
Recruitment) Rules, 2007, shall apply to them.
