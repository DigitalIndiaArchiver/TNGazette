 In the Government Order 1st read above orders were issued accepting the Resignation of Thiru. P.K. Vairamuthu 
from the post of the Chairman Tamil Nadu Housing Board, with eﬀ ect from 10-03-2021.
 
2. In the Government Order 2nd read above, orders were issued to the eﬀ ect that the Principal Secretary, Housing 
and Urban Development Department shall also the function as the Chairman, Tamil Nadu Housing Board until further order.
 
3. Now, the Government appoint Thiru. Poochi S. Murugan, as the Chairman of Tamil Nadu Housing Board with eﬀ ect 
from 22-01-2022.
 
4. The terms and conditions of appointment of the above individual will be issued separately.
