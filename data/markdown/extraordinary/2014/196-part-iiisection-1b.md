In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the rules published with the
Higher Education Department Notification No. S.R.O. B 159/97 at pages 170 to 171 Part III—Section 1(b) of
the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 17th December 1997.
2.
The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st January 2006.
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules,  In rule 4, in sub-rule (a), for the expression “35 years”, the expression
“Fifty-Seven Years” shall be substituted.

In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu
Educational Services (Section 8 in volume II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual 1969).
2.
The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st January 2006.
AMENDMENT
In the said Special rules,  In rule 5,  in sub-rule (a), in the Table in column (2), against the entry
“10. Assistant Professors in Engineering Colleges” in column (1), for the expression  “Thirty-five years”, the expression
“Fifty-seven Years” shall be substituted.
