Exemption of the Land in T.S. No. 2, Villivakkam, Chennai
from certain provisions of Development Control Rules
for Chennai Metropolitan Area under the Act.
[G.O. Ms. No. 210, Housing and Urban Development (UDI),
17th November 2009.]
No. II(2)/HOU/651/2009.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by Section 113 of the Tamil Nadu Town and
Country Planning Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972)
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby exempts the land in
T.S. No. 2, Block No. 3 of Villivakkam, Door No.1/1, Mettu Street,
Chennai-49, from the provisions of items 1(b), 2, 3 (i)(b) and 6
of Annexure  IX referred to in sub-rule (a) of Rule 17 of
the Development Control Rules for Chennai Metropolitan
Area, for regularisation of DBF+GF+3 Floors commercial
building with 5 Theatres and Restaurants at the above land,
subject to the condition that, the applicant should rectify the
other discrepancies and should maintain the truss as a non
habitable area and semi permanent in nature, and in case
if truss is converted into permanent structure, it will be
considered as a violation and will be proceeded against
accordingly by the Chennai Metropolitan Development
Authority. The appellant should gift sufficient land on the
western side and satisfy the car parking requirement as
already agreed to by him.
SURJIT K CHAUDHARY,
Principal Secretary to Government.

verification of their records and efficiency of Investigation
work done by them:—

accordingly sent proposal for the award of Governor’s Medal
for Vigilance for the year 2007 with a cash award at the
