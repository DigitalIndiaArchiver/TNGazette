LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY SECRETARIAT
SUMMONING FOR THE FIRST MEETING OF THE FOURTEENTH TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY
[S.O.Ms. No. 58, Legislative Assembly Secretariat, 19th May 2011, Vaikasi 5,
Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No. II(1)/LAS/12(c-1)/2011.
"Whereas the Fourteenth Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu has been duly constituted in accordance
with the Constitution of India and has to be summoned to meet for its first session under the provisions of the
Constitution.
Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred upon me by clause (1) of Article 174 of the Constitution of
India, I, SURJIT SINGH BARNALA, Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby summon the Legislative Assembly of the State
of Tamil Nadu to meet for its First session at 12.30 P.M. on Monday the 23rd May 2011 in the Legislative Assembly
Chamber, Secretariat, Chennai-600 009.
Raj Bhavan,
SURJIT SINGH BARNALA,
Guindy,
Governor of Tamil Nadu.
Chennai-600 022,
19th May 2011.
A.M.P. JAMALUDEEN,
Secretary.”

APPOINTMENT OF PRO-TEM SPEAKER OF TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY
[S.O. Ms. No. 59, Legislative Assembly Secretariat, 19th May 2011, Vaikasi 5,
Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No. II(1)/LAS/12(c-2)/2011.
"In exercise of the powers conferred by Clause (1) of Article 180 of the Constitution of India,
I, SURJIT SINGH BARNALA, Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby appoint Dr. C.K. Thamizharasan, being a Member of
the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly to perform the duties of the Speaker from the commencement of the first meeting
of the Assembly until the Speaker shall have been chosen by the Assembly under Artice 178 of the Constitution.
Raj Bhavan,
SURJIT SINGH BARNALA,
Guindy,
Governor of Tamil Nadu.
Chennai-600 022,
19th May 2011.
A.M.P. JAMALUDEEN,
Secretary.”
APPOINTMENT OF PERSON BEFORE WHOM THE MEMBERS OF TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY

No. II(1)/LAS/12(c-3)/2011.
"In exercise of the powers conferred by Article 188 of the Constitution of India, I, SURJIT SINGH BARNALA,
