NOTICE UNDER SECTION 3(1) OF TAMIL NADU ACQUISITION OF LAND FOR INDUSTRIAL PURPOSES ACT
1997 READ WITH THE TAMIL NADU LAND ACQUISITION LAWS (REVIVAL OF OPERATION, AMENDMENT AND 
VALIDATION) ACT, 2019.
No. VI(1)/141(b-1)/2022.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that the lands specified in the Schedule below have to be acquired 
for industrial purposes, for the expansion of Mine-II, Neyveli Lignite Corporation India Limited and having already been 
decided that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands are to be paid out of the funds managed by the 
Neyveli Lignite Corporation India Limited. The following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu 
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu 
Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the Wet lands specified in the Schedule below and measuring 
0.12.00 Hect., to the same, a little more or less needed for industrial purposes, to wit, for the expansion of Mine –II of Neyveli 
Lignite Corporation India Limited.
The plans of the lands are kept in the O/o. Special Deputy Collector (Land Acquisition) Neyveli-2 and may be inspected 
at during office hours.

No. VI(1)/141(b-2)/2022.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that the lands specified in the Schedule below to be acquired for 
industrial purpose to wit to Corridor-4 25 -Kattupakkam Metro Station and it having already been decided that the amount of 
compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid out of the lands controlled or managed by Chennai Metro Rail Limited. 
The following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes 
Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands specified in the Schedule below measuring 
578 sq.mtrs., to the same, a little more or less needed for industrial purpose wit, for Corridor- 4 - 25- Kattupakkam Metro 
Station’ under Phase-II of Chennai Metro Rail Limited situated in Kattupakkam Village, Poonamallee Taluk, Tiruvallur District.
The plan of the lands are kept in the O/o District Collector, Tiruvallur, Taluk office, Poonamallee and O/o. District Revenue 
Officer (LA), Chennai Metro Rail Limited, Chennai may be inspected on any day during office hours.

NOTICE UNDER SUB SECTION (1) OF SECTION 15 OF THE TAMIL NADU HIGHWAYS ACT, 2001 (TN ACT 34 OF 
2002) READ WITH THE TAMIL NADU LAND ACQUISITION LAWS (REVIVAL OF OPERATION, AMENDMENT AND
VALIDATION) ACT, 38 OF 2019.
No. VI(1)/141(b-3)/2022. 
The Government of Tamil Nadu having  been satisfied that the lands specified in the schedule below  to be acquired 
for Highways purpose to wit to  for the formation of Kumbakonam bye-pass Phase III  at Valayapettai Village, Kumbakonam 
Taluk, in the  District of Thanjavur,  and it having already been decided that the  amount of compensation to be awarded 
for the lands to be paid out of the funds  controlled and  managed by the Divisional Engineer (Highways), Construction and 
Maintenance,  Thanjavur.  The following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 
(Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002).
 NOTICE
	
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) the Governor 
of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the land/lands specified in the Schedule below measuring  an extent of 2285 Sq.Mtr.,  to the 
same, a little more or less needed  for Highways purpose wit,  for the purpose for the formation of Kumbakonam bye-pass 
Phase III comprised in S.No. 243/1, 243/4  and 243/5  at  Valayapettai Village, Kumbakonam Taluk of Thanjavur District.
The plan of the lands are kept in the office of the District Collector, Thanjavur may be inspected on any  day during office
