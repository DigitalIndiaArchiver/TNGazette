RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND PANCHAYAT RAJ DEPARTMENT
APPOINTMENT OF CERTAIN OFFICIAL AS TAMIL NADU STATE ELECTION COMMISSIONER FOR CERTAIN PERIOD
UNDER TAMIL NADU PANCHAYATS ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 54, Rural Development and Panchayat Raj  (PR-I), 22nd May 2010.]
No. II(2)/RDPR/288(a)/2010.
Under Article 243-K of the Constitution of India, read with Section 239 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994
(Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994), as amended in the Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Fifth Amendment) Act, 2007, the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby appoints Thiru Syed Munir Hoda, I.A.S., Additional Chief Secretary/Chairman cum Managing Director,
Tamil Nadu Power Finance and Infrastructure Development Corporation Limited (POWERFIN), Chennai-35 as the
Tamil Nadu State Election Commissioner for a period of two years with effect on and from the 22nd May 2010.
K.S. SRIPATHI,
Chief Secretary.
