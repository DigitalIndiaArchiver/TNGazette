ENERGY  DEPARTMENT
 CONSTITUTION OF SELECTION COMMITTEE FOR SELECTING A MEMBER OF THE TAMIL NADU
ELECTRICITY REGULATORY COMMISSION UNDER THE ELECTRICITY ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 34, Energy (A1), 24th April 2009.]
No. II(2)/EGY/178(b)/2009.
In exercise of powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 85 of the Electricity Act, 2003 (Central
Act 36 of 2003), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby constitutes the Selection Committee consisting of the
following Members for the purpose of selecting a Member of the Tamil Nadu Electricity Regulatory
Commission:—
(1)
Thiru Justice V. Kanagaraj, Retired Judge
..
Chairperson
(2)
The Chief Secretary to Government of Tamil Nadu
..
Member
(3)
The Chairperson of the Central Electricity Authority, New Delhi
..
Member
2. This Selection Committee shall,—
(a) finalise the selection of member within three months from the date on which the reference is
 made to it.
(b) recommend a panel of two names for every vacancy referred to it;  and
(c) satisfy itself, before recommending any person for appointment as Member of Tamil Nadu Electricity
Regulatory Commission, that such person does not have any financial or other interest which is
likely to affect prejudicially his functions as Chairperson or Member, as the case may be.
P.W.C. DAVIDAR,
Secretary to Government (in-charge).
