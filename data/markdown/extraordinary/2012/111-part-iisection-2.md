Whereas, in exercise of the powers conferred by clause (1) of Article 258 of the Constitution of India, the President of
India has in the Ministry of Food, Agriculture, Community Development and Co-operation (Department of Agriculture)
Notification No. 4(1)/65 General II, dated 23rd April 1966, entrusted to the Government of Tamil Nadu, with their consent, the
functions of the Central Government under the Land Acquisition Act, 1894 (Central Act I of 1894) in the relation of
Acquisition of the Land for the purpose of the Union in the State.   Now, therefore by virtue of the authorization by Government
under Section 4(1) of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894 (Central Act I of 1894), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that
the land / lands specified in the Schedule below are needed for a public purpose to wit, for establishment of Mountain Training Wing
by the Defence authorities shall be required for the said purpose.  The Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby Authorizes the Revenue
Divisional Officer, Coonoor, his Staff and Workmen to exercise the powers conferred by sub-section (27) of Section 4 of the said
Act.
THE SCHEDULE
The Nilgiris District, Udhagai Taluk, Naduvattam Village.
The Nilgiris District, Udhagai Taluk, Naduvattam Village, S.No.344, belonging to Mahavir Plantations Ltd., bounded on the north
by S.No. 344, east by S.No.332/2, south by S.No. 344  and  west by S.Nos. 345, 347 and 350—Extent of Land to be acquired
23.00 acres.
Total—23.00 acres.
