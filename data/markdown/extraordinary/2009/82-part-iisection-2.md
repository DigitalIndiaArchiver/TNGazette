FINANCE  DEPARTMENT
(Salaries)
NOTIFICATION REGARDING THE PROCUREMENT OF GROCERIES (OTHER THAN THE CONTROLLED COMMODITIES)
AND VEGETABLES FROM THE CO-OPERATIVE SOCIETIES BY THE PRISON DEPARTMENT UNDER TAMIL NADU
TRANSPARENCY IN TENDERS ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 115, Finance (Salaries), 24th March 2009, Panguni 11, Thiruvalluvar Aandu–2040.]
No. II(2)/FIN/136(d-1)/2009.
Under clause (f) of Section 16 of the Tamil Nadu Transparency in Tenders Act, 1998 (Tamil Nadu Act 43 of 1998), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notifies the procurement of groceries (other than the commodities  viz., Rice, Wheat, Sugar,
Rava and Maida notified in the Government Order Ms. No. 483, Finance (Salaries) Department, dated 11-11-2008) and
vegetables required by the Prison Department directly from the Co-operative Societies.
NOTIFICATION REGARDING THE APPOINTMENT OF TAMIL NADU ROAD DEVELOPMENT COMPANY LIMITED AS THE MANAGING
ASSOCIATE, FOR THE WORK OF CALLING FOR TENDERS AND CO-ORDINATION OF THE EXECUTION OF WORKS DURING
THE PERIOD OF CONSTRUCTION OF OUTER-RING ROAD FROM VANDALUR TO NEMILICHERY UNDER BUILD, OPERATE
AND TRANSFER-ANNUITY BASIS AS PER THE TERMS AND CONDITIONS UNDER TAMIL NADU TRANSPARENCY
IN TENDERS ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 116, Finance (Salaries), 24th March 2009, Panguni 11, Thiruvalluvar Aandu–2040.]
No. II(2)/FIN/136(d-2)/2009.
Under clause (f) of Section 16 of the Tamil Nadu Transparency in Tenders Act, 1998 (Tamil Nadu Act 43 of 1998), the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby notifies the appointment of the Tamil Nadu Road Development Company Limited (TNRDC) as the
Managing Associate, for the work of calling for tenders and co-ordination of the execution of works during the period of
construction of Outer Ring Road from Vandalur to Nemilichery under Build, Operate and Transfer-Annuity basis, as per the terms
and conditions laid down in paragraph 5 of G.O.Ms. No. 32, Highways and Minor Ports (HF2) Department, dated 25-02-2009.
K. GNANADESIKAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
