NOTIFICATIONS UNDER TAMIL NADU PANCHAYATS ACT
CONSTITUTION OF DISTRICT PANCHAYAT FOR COIMBATORE DISTRICT
AMENDMENT TO NOTIFICATION
[G.O. Ms. No. 26, Rural Development and Panchayat Raj (PR-II), 25th February 2011.]
No. II(2)/RDPR/90(f-1)/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 24 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu
Act 21 of 1994), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Rural Development Department
Notification No. II(2)/RUL/1439(b-17)/95, published at page 12 of Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette
Extraordinary, dated the 13th April 1995.
2. The amendment hereby made shall come into force with effect on and from 25th February 2011.
AMENDMENT
In the said Notification, the following entries under the heading “Name of the Panchayat Unions” shall be omitted:-—
“1. Palladam
2.
Pongalur
5.
Tiruppur
7.
Avinashi
   11.
Gudimangalam
   13.
Udumalpet
    14.
Madathukulam”.
Consequent on the above amendment, the territorial wards will be divided for the next ordinary election.

Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 13th April 1995.

AMENDMENTS
In the said Notification,—
1. for the expression “Periyar District,” the expression “Erode District” shall be substituted; and

“5.
Uthukuli
7.
Dharapuram
8.
Mulanur
9.
Kundadam
10.
Kangeyam
11.
Vellakoil”.
Consequent on the above amendments, the territorial wards will be divided for the next ordinary election.
CONSTITUTION OF DISTRICT PANCHAYAT FOR TIRUPPUR DISTRICT
[G.O. Ms. No. 26, Rural Development and Panchayat Raj (PR-II), 25th February 2011.]
No. II(2)/RDPR/90(f-3)/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 24 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu
Act 21 of 1994), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby constitutes with effect on and from 25th February 2011, a District Panchayat
for the Tiruppur District comprising the local areas forming the Panchayat Unions specified below, namely:—
Name of the Panchayat Unions:
1.
Avinashi.
2.
Gudimangalam
3.
Madathukulam
4.
Palladam
5.
Pongalur
6.
Tiruppur
7.
Udumalpet
8.
Dharapuram
9.
Kundadam
10.
Mulanur
11.
Kangeyam
12.
Vellakoil
13.
Uthukuli.
Based on the above constitution, the territorial wards will be divided for the next ordinary election.
