The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that the lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below, have to be 
acquired for industrial Purposes to wit, for setting up of Industrial Park by SIPCOT through Private Negotiation and it 
having already been decided that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid out of 
the lands controlled or managed by Government the following Notice is issued under sub section (1) of Section 3 of 
the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10/1999).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 10/1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands in 92, Thirumudivakkam Village, 
Kundrathur Taluk, Kancheepuram District, speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below and measuring 10.19.5 Hectares (or) 25.18 
acres to the same a little more or less needed for industrial purposes, to wit for setting up Industrial Park by SIPCOT.
The plans of the lands are kept in the oﬃ  ce of the Special Tahsildar(LA), Oragadam Expansion Scheme, Unit-1, 
SIPCOT, Sriperumbudur and may be inspected at any time during oﬃ  ce hours.
