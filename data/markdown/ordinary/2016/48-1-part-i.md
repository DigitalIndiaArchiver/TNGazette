No.I/PUSP/25/2016.—The following Notification of the
Government of India, Ministry of Law and Justice, Department
of Justice, Jaisalmer House, 26, Man Singh Road,
New Delhi-110 011, dated 10th November, 2016
is republished:—
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (1) of Article
224 of the Constitution of India, the President is pleased to
appoint (i) Shri R.M.T. Teeka Raman, (ii) Shri N. Sathish
Kumar and (iii) Shri N. Seshasayee, to be Additional Judges
of the Madras High Court, in that order of seniority, for a
period of two years with effect from the date they assume
charge of their respective offices.
