No.II(2)/CTR/409/2012.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by clause (b) of sub-section (1) of Section 9 of
the Indian Stamp Act, 1899 (Cental Act II of 1899), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby provides for the consolidation
of duty of Rs. 5,00,000/- (Rupees five lakh only) chargeable
under the said Act in respect of issue of policies by the
Shriram Life Insurance Company Limited for the period from
1st July 2012 to 31st December 2012 in the State
of Tamil Nadu.

In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (b) of sub-
section (1) of Section 3 and sub-section (2) of Section 5 read
with clause (c) of sub-section (2) of Section 3 of the Minimum
Wages Act, 1948 (Central Act XI of 1948) and in supersesson
of the Labour and Employment Department Notification
No.II(2)/LE/240/2007, 
published 
at 
page 
193 
of
Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette,
dated the 9th May 2007, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
revises the guaranteed time rate of wages payable to beedi
rollers in the employment in Tabacco (including beedi
making) manufactory as follows:—
(i)
Where the employer fails to supply sufficient quantum
of good raw materials (including tobacco leaves) to roll one
thousand beedies per day, the employees (beedi-rollers) shall
be entitled to get Rs. 24.80 (Rupees twenty four and paise
eighty only) as guaranteed time rate of wages.
(ii)
The payment of guaranteed time rate of wages as
aforesaid shall be subject to the following conditions,
namely:—
(a) that the employee shall not be entitled for payment
of guaranteed time rate of wages, if he does not fully make
use of the raw materials supplied by the employer, even if it
is inadequate to roll one thousand beedies;
(b) that the employee shall not be entitled to get the
guaranteed time rate of wages, if he earns less than the
amount of guaranteed time rate of wages on any day, on
account of his unwillingness to work for any reason
whatsoever;
(c) that the employee shall not entitled to get the
guaranteed time rate of wages, if the failure of the employer
to supply raw materials is due to tempest, fire, rain,
catastrophe, epidemics, civil commotion or any other similar
cause which is beyond the control of the employer; and
(d) that the employee who works for more than one
employeer shall not be entitled to get the guaranteed time
rate of wages from anyone of those employers.
