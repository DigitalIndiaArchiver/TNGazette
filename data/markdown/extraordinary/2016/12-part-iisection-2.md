NOTIFICATION
No. II(2)/PUBG/62(a)/2016.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Commissions of Inquiry
Act, 1952 (Central Act LX of 1952), the Governor  of  Tamil  Nadu  hereby  makes  the following amendment  to
the Public (Buildings) Department’s Notification No. II(2)/PUBG/286(c)/2011, dated the 22nd June 2011,
published at  pages 1–2 of Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the
22nd June 2011, as subsequently amended.
AMENDMENT
In the said Notification, in paragraph 3, for the expression “fifty five”, the expression “fifty eight”
shall be substituted.
    JATINDRA NATH SWAIN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
