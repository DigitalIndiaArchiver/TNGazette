PERSONNEL AND ADMINISTRATIVE REFORMS DEPARTMENT
Amendments to Tamil Nadu Government Servants' Conduct Rules, 1973.
[G.O.  Ms. No. 38, Personnel and Administrative Reforms (A), 3rd April 2009, Panguni 21, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. SRO B-25/2009.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu Government Servant's
Conduct Rules, 1973.—
2.
The amendment hereby made shall come into force on the 3rd April 2009.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules, for rule 10, the following rule shall be substituted namely:—
"10. Communication of official information.—Every Government servant shall, in performance of his duties in
good faith, communicate information to a person in accordance with the Right to Information Act, 2005
(Central Act 22 of 2005) and the rules made thereunder:
Provided that no Government servant including the Government Pleaders, Public Prosecutors and other officials
who are not full-time officers, but are engaged by the Government to do specified work without prejudice to the regular
exercise of their professions in other respects shall, except in accordance with any general or special order of the
Governmet or in performance in good faith of the duties assigned to him, communicate, directly or indirectly, any official
document or any part thereof or classfied information either in hard copy or in electronic format to any Government
servant or any other person to whom he is not authorized to communicate such official document or classified information
(including in electronic format):
Provided further that the above proviso shall not apply to the Special Public Prosecutors and Pleaders engaged
to do Government work generally in specified local areas or specially in any particular case or class of cases".
K.N. VENKATARAMANAN,
Secretary to Government.

Amendments to Tamil Nadu General Subordinate Service.
[G.O.  Ms. No. 47, Social Welfare and Nutritious Meal Programme (SW-9), 23rd March 2009,
Panguni 10, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. SRO B-26/2009.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the rules published with the
Social Welfare Department Notification SRO B-231/86, dated the 18th April 1986 at pages 304-308 of
Part III—Section 1(b) of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 21st May 1986:—
2.
The amendments hereby made shall come into force on the 23rd March 2009.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules,—
(1) in rule 3, in the TABLE, for the entries in column (2) against the entry "Junior Rehabilitation Officer"
in column (1) thereof, the following entries shall be substituted, namely:—
"(i) By direct recruitment; or
(ii) By promotion from the posts of—
(a) Vocational Instructor;
(b) Orthotic Technician; and
(c) Multipurpose Rehabilitation Aide:
Provided that appointment to the post by direct recruitment and by promotion shall be made in the
ratio of 1:4
Provided further that appointment to the post by promotion from the posts of Vocational Instructor, Orthotic
Technician and Multipurpose Rehabilitation Aide shall be made in the ratio 1:3:1";
(2) in rule 4, for the expression "Joint Director of Social Welfare", the expression "State Commissioner for the
Disabled" shall be substituted;
(3) in rule 5, sub rule (b),—
(a) for the expression "No person shall be eligible for appointment by direct recruitment, to the post specified
in column (1) of the Table below unless he possesses the educational qualifications specified  in the corresponding
entries in column (2) thereof", the following expression shall be substituted, namely:—
"No person shall be eligible for appointment to the posts specified in column (1) of the Table below by
the method specified in column (2), unless he possesses the educational qualifications specified in the corresponding
entries in column (3) thereof";
(b) in the Table,—
(i)
a new column under the heading "Method of Appointment" shall be inserted as column (2) and the
exisiting column (2) under the heading "Qualification" shall be renumbered as column (3) ;
(ii) in column (2) as so inserted, against the entry "Junior Rehabilitation Officer" in column (1) and the
entries “(i) Post Graduate degree in Psychology or Post Graduate degree in Social Work or Sociology; and
Not less than two years experience in administration and interpretation of Psychological tests;
or in placement of the handicapped" in column (3) as so renumbered, the entry “(1) By direct recruitment"
shall be inserted;
