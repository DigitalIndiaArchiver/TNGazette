No. II(2)/IND/884(c-1)/2013.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied
that the lands specified in the schedule below have to be
acquired for Industrial Purpose to wit for Mines Expansion
Scheme of Neyveli Lignite Corporation Limited and it having
already been decided that the entire amount of
compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid out
of the funds controlled or managed by Neyveli Lignite
Corporation Limited.  The following notice is issued under
sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition
of Land for Industrial Purpose Act, 1997.
NOTIFICATION
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Acquisition of Land for Industrial  Purpose Act, 1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999),  the Governor  of  Tamil Nadu
hereby  acquires the lands  specified  in the  Schedule
below and measuring 3.40.0 hectares be the same a little
more or less needed for Industrial purposes, to wit, for
Mines Expansion Scheme of Neyveli Lignite Corporation
Limited.
The plan  of the lands are kept in the office of the Special
Deputy  Collector / Land Acquisition, Neyveli-2 and may be
inspected at any  time during office hours.
