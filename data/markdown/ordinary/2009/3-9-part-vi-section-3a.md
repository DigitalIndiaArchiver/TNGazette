GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD
1, Jaffar Syrang Street, Vallal Seethakathi Nagar, Chennai-600 001.
Taking over the Direct Management of Illayangudi Nesavu Pattadai Tholugai Medai Pallivasal,
Illayangudi-630 702, Sivagangai District.
(Rc.No. 6291/99/B16/SVG.)
No. VI-3(a)/3/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995, the Tamil Nadu
Wakf Board in its proceedings in item No. 13/08 in Rc.No. 6291/99/B16/SVG, dated 27th May 2008 has ordered to
take over the Management of Illayangudi Nesavu Pattadai Tholugai Medai Pallivasal, Illayangudi-630 702, Sivagangai
District under the Direct Control of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of six months from the date of
pronouncement of the Board's order, dated 24th December 2008 and to appoint the Superintendent of Wakfs,
Ramanathapuram as its Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
21st January 2009.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
Taking over the Management of the Mohideen Poora Masjid, Ambur,
Vaniyambadi Taluk, Vellore District.
(Rc.No. 8503/2007/B4/Vellore.)
No. VI-3(a)/4/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per provision of the Wakf Act, 1995, the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its
resolution in item No. 53/2008, dated 18th November 2008 has ordered to take over the Management of the Mohideen
Poora Masjid, Ambur, Vaniyambadi Taluk, Vellore District and its properties under the Direct control of the Tamil Nadu
Wakf Board for the period of one year from 18th November 2008 under Section 65 of Wakf Act, 1995 by appointing
the Superintendent of Wakfs, Vellore as its Executive Officer of the above Wakf by removing
Janab.T.M. Anwar from the post of the Muthawalliship for his Mismanagement of the above Wakf and for illegal
dealings of wakf  properties under Section 64 of the Wakf Act, 1995.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
21st January 2009.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.

CONDITIONS OF SALE
1.
Lots are to be examined by the intending purchasers before bidding and to be taken with faults and errors
to description.
2.
The highest bidder to be the purchaser and in the event of any dispute arising between two or more bidders
the lots so disputed should be put up again and resold.
3.
The bid amount shall be paid in full immediately after the bid is knocked down. If this not possible a deposit
of 25 per cent of bidded amount should be paid on the spot to the auctioneers and a receipt shall be obtained for
the amount paid in case only a deposit of 25 per cent is paid the balance amount should be paid to the Trust
Financial Adviser and Chief Accounts Officer on or before 4.00 p.m. on the third day calculating from the date of
confirmation of sale, failing which the EMD paid will be forfeited and the Trust will resell the lot either in sale by
Tender or by Public Auction at the risk and responsibility of the defaulters.
4.
Goods advertised for sale will not be withdrawn by the Trust except on payment of sufficient deposit to cover
the Harbour dues, demurrage charges and other charges due thereon.
5.
The goods sold in Auction will not be delivered to the purchaser thereof or his authorized agent unless the
purchaser produced delivery order from the Auctioneers for having paid the amount in full.
6.
Goods sold in Auction shall be allowed three free days excluding Sundays and Board’s Holidays from the
date of confirmation of sale and thereafter shall be charged at daily rated as per Scale “B” for goods in the bonded
warehouse under Chapter X  of Books of the Scale of Rates up to and including the date of clearance.
7.
The lots sold will be lying at the risk of the purchaser and must be removed within the free period
of three days from the date of confirmation of sale at the purchaser’s expenses.
8.
The purchasers are distinctly warned that if the goods are not cleared within eight  days from the date of
expiry of free period they have no claim whatsoever over the materials and the same will be sold whether in sale
by Tender or Public Auction. The first purchaser to be held liable for any loss arising from such resale and to forfeit
any advances together with auctioned commission.
9.
The Trust shall reserve the rights to withdraw any of the items notified for sale without assigning any reason
for such withdrawal.
10. Bidders shall gain admission into the Harbour on the days of Inspection and as the passes can be obtained,
on payment from the Programmer Computer Pass Section, near Main Gate, opposite to Customs House, Chennai-
600 001. The successful bidder should also obtain pass on payment for clearing the goods.
11. Terms Cash or Demand Draft or Pay Order of any Nationalised Bank.
12. All the Lots sold in the auction are subject to confirmation.
13. If any packages are not sold in the auction for which they are listed they will be included for sale on the
succeeding sale by Tender or in Public Auction.
