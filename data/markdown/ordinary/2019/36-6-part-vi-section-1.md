(Roc.No. 1347/A/2014/G/Judn.)
Erratum
No.VI(1)/432/2019.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 11 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 
1873) the High Court, Madras, hereby issues this Erratum to the Notiﬁ cation No.VI(1)/344/2018 published at page 321 
of Part VI—Section 1 in Issue No.38, of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated 19th September, 2018, as follows:
In the said Notiﬁ cation, the words "entire Radhapuram Taluk" and "entire Taluk of Radhapuram" are substituted 
by the words "Radhapuram Firka and Thisayanvilai Firka of Radhapuram Taluk".
High Court, Madras,  
C. KUMARAPPAN,

Constitution of a District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court at Singampunari in Sivagangai District
(Roc. No. 50317/2018/G/Judn)
No.VI(1)/433/2019.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 
1873) the  High Court, Madras, hereby ﬁ xes Singampunari in Sivagangai District as the place at which the District 
Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Singampunari, shall be located.
NOTIFICATION-II
(Roc. No. 50317/2018/G/Judn)
No.VI(1)/434/2019.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 11 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 
1873) the High Court, Madras, hereby directs that in Sivagangai District, the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, 
Thirupathur, shall cease to have local jurisdiction over the entire Singampunari Taluk and the District Munsif-cum-Judicial 
Magistrate Court, Singampunari shall have and exercise local jurisdiction over the entire Taluk of Singampunari with 
eﬀ ect from the date on which the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate, Singampunari assumes charge of that Court.
NOTIFICATION-III
(Roc. No. 50317/2018/G/Judn)
No.VI(1)/435/2019.
The High Court, Madras hereby directs and notiﬁ es that the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate, Singampunari 
in Sivagangai District shall exercise all the powers conferred on a District Munsif under Section 12 of the Tamil Nadu 
Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873) as amended up-to-date in regard to original suits and proceedings not 
otherwise exempted from his cognizance of which the amount or  value of the subject matter does not exceed One 
Lakh Rupees within the local limits of his jurisdiction with eﬀ ect from the date on which the District Munsif-cum-Judicial 
Magistrate, Singampunari assumes charge of that Court.

NOTIFICATION-IV
(Roc. No. 50317/2018/G/Judn)
No.VI(1)/436/2019.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 28 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873) 
the High Court, Madras, hereby directs that the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate, Singampunari, shall have and 
exercise jurisdiction of a Court of Small Causes under the Provincial Small Cause Courts Act, 1887 for the trial of suits 
cognizable by a Court of Small Causes upto the pecuniary limits of Rs.5000/- (Rupees ﬁ ve thousand only) with eﬀ ect 
from the date on which the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Singampunari, assumes charge of that Court.
