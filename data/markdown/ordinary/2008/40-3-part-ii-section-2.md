Vesting of properties and scheme for administration
relating to Kizhnoor Perumal-Nagammal Charitable
Endowment Fund, tenable in the Arignar Anna
Government Arts College, Cheyyar under Charitable
Endowments Act.
[G.O.Ms.No. 323, Higher Education (D2), 11th August
2008, Aadi 27, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2039.]
No. II(2)/HE/458/2008.—In the matter of the Charitable
Endowments Act, 1890 (Central Act VI of 1890) and in the
matter of “Kizhnoor Perumal-Nagammal Charitable
Endowment Fund” (hereafter reffered to as “the said
Endowment”) tenable in the Arignar Anna Government Arts
College, Cheyyar, Thiruvannamalai District.
I. VESTING OF PROPERTIES.
Whereas, an application has been made to the
Government of Tamil Nadu under clause (b) of sub-section
(1) of section 6 of the Charitable Endowments Act, 1890
(Central Act VI of 1890) by Dr. P. Elumalai, Ph.D.,
Vinayagapuram, Thirumani Post, Vandavasi Taluk,
Thiruvannamalai-604 504, the donor of the said Endowment
for vesting the property specified in the the Schedule below,
in the Treasurer of Charitable Endowments, Chennai,
appointed under section 3 of the said Act for the State of
Tamil Nadu, the Governor of Tamil Nadu, in exercise of the
powers conferred by section 4 of the said Act, hereby orders
that the said property be vested in the Treasurer aforesaid,
and be held by him and his sucessors in office, subject
to the provisions of the said Act and the Rules which from
time to time, may be or shall be made or deemed to have
been made thereunder and subject to the conditions set
forth in the Scheme settled under section 5 of the said Act,
for the administration of the said Endowment.
THE SCHEDULE.
The Property.
Rs. 30,000/-(Rupees Thirty thousand only.)
II. SCHEME FOR ADMINISTRATION.
Whereas, an application has been made to the
Government of Tamil Nadu under clause (b) of sub-section
(1) of section 6 of the Charitable Endowments Act, 1890
(Central Act VI of 1890) by Dr. P. Elumalai, Ph.D.,
Vinayagapuram, Thirumani Post, Vandavasi Taluk,
Thiurvannamalai-604 504, the donor of the said Endowment
specified above, for settling a Scheme for administration of
the said Endowment, the Governor of Tamil Nadu, in exercise
of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 5 of
the Charitable Endowments Act, 1890 (Central Act VI of
1890), hereby settles the Scheme set forth in the Schedule
below for the administration of the said Endowment and
appoints the Principal, Arignar Anna Government Arts
College, Cheyyar, Thiruvannamalai District to administer the
property. The scheme shall come into operation from the
date of publication of this Notification in the Tamil Nadu
Government Gazette.
THE SCHEDULE

5. The amount of the said Fund shall be invested from

Notifications under Tamil Nadu Protection of Interest of
Depositors (in Financial Establishment) Act.
Ad-interim order attaching of properties due to default
in return of deposits by Valluvar Chit Fund,
Thopputhurai, Vedaranyam, Nagappattinam District.
[G.O. Ms. No. 1066, Home (Police XIX)
20th August 2008.]
No. II(2)/HO/468/2008.—Whereas, complaints have been
received from a number of depositors that Valluvar Chit
Fund, Thopputhurai, Vedaranyam, Nagappattinam District,
a Financial Establishment, which has defaulted the return
of deposits  made by the depositors after maturity;
2.
And, Whereas, the Government are satisfied that the
said Financial Establishment is not likely to return the
deposits to the depositors and hence, the Government have
to protect the interests of such depositors;
3.
And, Whereas, in the G.O. Ms. No. 1440, Home
(Pol. 19), dated 15th October 2007 and G.O. Ms. No. 1846,
Home (Pol. 19), dated 17th December 2007, the Government
have already made an ad-interim order under Section 3 of
the Tamil Nadu Protection of Interests of Depositors
(in Financial Establishments) Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 44
of the 1997) attaching certain immovable properties of some
of the partners of the said Financial Establishment as
specified in the schedule thereto, and the said properties
are not sufficient for repayment of the deposits;
4.
And, Whereas the property specified in the Schedule
to this order is alleged to have been procured by Thiru
Palanisamy, one of the Partners of the said Financial
Establishment from and out of the deposits collected from
the depositors;
5.
Now, Therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred
by Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Protection of Interests of
Depositors (in Financial Establishments) Act, 1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 44 of 1997), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby makes an ad-interim order attaching the property of
the said Thiru. Palanisamy, one of the partners of Valluvar
Chit Fund, Thopputhurai, Vedaranyam, Nagapattinam District,
as specified in the Schedule to this Order and transfere the
control over the said property to the Competent Authority,
namely, the District Revenue Officer, Nagappattinam
appointed under the provisions of the said Act, for the
purpose in addition to the properties ordered to be attached
in the G.O. Ms. No. 1440, Home (Pol. 19), dated 15th
October 2007 and G.O. Ms. No. 1846, Home (Pol. 19), dated
17th December 2007.
6.
Competent Authority is requested to pursue further
action in accordance with the procedure laid down in
sub-sections (3) and (4) of Section 4 of the said Act and
also the formalities prescribed in the Tamil Nadu Protection
of Interests of Depositors (in financial Establishments) Rules,
1997.
7.
The Special Public Prosecutor, Special Court for the
Tamil Nadu Protection of Interests of Depositors (in Financial
Establishments) Act, 1997 is requested to render necessary
assistance to the Competent Authority, in filling the application,
before the said Special Court.
