Final Closing of affairs and Cancellation of Registration of certain Co-operative Societies
The Vallanadu Adi Dravidar Coir Workers Industrial Co-operative Society Limited, Ind.No. 1823
(L.F. No. 1009/ICA/2000.)
No. VI(1)/183/2009.
In the circumstances stated by the Assistant Director of Industries and Commerce (Industrial Co-operatives)/Official
Liquidator of the Vallanadu Adi Dravidar Coir Workers Industrial Co-operative Society Limited in his final closure
proposals, dated 20th March 2009 and in exercise of the powers delegated under Section 140(1) of Tamil Nadu
Co-operative Societies Act, 1983, the registration of the Vallanadu Adi Dravidar Coir Workers Industrial Co-operative
Society Limited Ind.No. 1823 is hereby cancelled and the affairs are finally closed with effect from 30th March 2009.
Chepauk, Chennai-600 005,
G. SANTHANAM,
30th March 2009.
Industries Commissioner and Director
of Industries and Commerce/Registrar of
Industrial Co-operatives.
Ganesapuram Stone Cutters Labour Contract Society V V 2769.
(Liq. No. 7/96-97.)
No. VI(1)/184/2009.
In the circumstances stated by the Co-operative Sub-Registrar (Stores), in the final closure note, dated 27th March
2008 and in exercise of the powers delegated under Section 140 (1) of the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act, 1983,
the registration of the Ganesapuram Stone Cutters Labour Contract Society VV 2769 is hereby cancelled and its affairs are
finally closed with effect from 27th March 2008.
E.I.D. Parry India Limited Employees Co-operative Canteen C 2064.
(Liq. No. 124/02-03.)
No. VI(1)/185/2009.
In the circumstances stated by the Co-operative Sub-Registrar (Stores), in the final closure note, dated
26th November 2007 and in exercise of the powers delegated under Section 140 (1) of the Tamil Nadu Co-operative
Societies Act, 1983, the registration of the E.I.D. Parry India Limited Employees Co-operative Canteen C 2064 is hereby
cancelled and its affairs are finally closed with effect from 30th November 2007.
Vellore,
A. SANTHAKUMARAN,
15th June 2009.
Deputy Registrar of Co-operative Societies,
Vellore Circle.
