NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
DTP—II-2 Ex. (258)
TRANSPORT DEPARTMENT
ACQUISITION OF LANDS
[G.O.Ms. No. 119, Transport (I-1), 9th August 2011.]
No.II(2)/TRA/358(e)/2011.
Whereas, in exercise of the powers conferred by clause (1)
of Article 258 of the Constitution of India, the President of India,
has in the Ministry of Food, Agriculture, Community Development
and Co-operation (Department of Agriculture) Notification No.4(1)/
65, General II, dated the 23rd April 1966, entrusted to the
Government of Tamil Nadu with their consent, the functions of
the Central Government under the Land Acquisition Act, 1894
(Central Act I of 1894), in relation to acquisition of lands for the
purpose of the Union in this State;
And, whereas, it has already been decided that the
compensation should be paid from the public revenues;
Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by
Section 6 of the said Act, the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby declares  that the lands specified in the schedule below
and measuring 1800 square metres of Dry lands in Sathumadurai
Village, be the same, a little more or less, are needed for a
public purpose, to wit, Villupuram - Katpadi Broad Gauge Railway
Line for the formation of Pennathur Railway Station.
A plan of the lands are kept in the Office of the Tahsildar,
Vellore and may be inspected at any time during office hours.
SCHEDULE
Vellore District, Vellore Taluk, Sathumadurai Village.
Government, dry, S.No.11-2, belonging to Kasturi,
Gandhimathi, Palani and Gnanapazham, are daughters and sons
of Dakshinamoorthy, bounded on the north by S.No. 11-3, east
by S.Nos. 11-11 and 11-12, south by S.No. 16 and west by
S.No. 11-1—0.07.0 hectare.
Government, dry, S.No.11-3, belonging to Kulla Gounder,
son of Chinnappa Gounder, bounded on the north by S.No. 11-4,
east by S.Nos. 11-10 and 11-11, south by S.No. 11-2 and west
by S.No. 11-1—0.07.0 hectare.
Government, dry, S.No.11-4A, belonging to Arputhammal,
wife of Duraisamy, bounded on the north and  east by
S.No. 11-4(part), south by S.No. 11-3 and west
by S.No. 11-1—0.04.0 hectare.
Total—0.18.0 hectare.
