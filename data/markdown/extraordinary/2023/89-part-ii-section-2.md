UNDER THE TAMIL NADU PANCHAYATS ACT, 1994.

WHEREAS, a written notice of the intention to make a motion expressing want of confidence in Tmt. S. Preethi, 
Vice-Chairman, Kodumudi Panchayat Union Council, Erode District, signed by five members, out of the sanctioned strength of 
six members of the said Council, together with a copy of the motion proposed to be made and a written statement of charges 
against the said Vice-Chairman have been delivered to the Revenue Divisional Officer, Erode in person by two members as 
per sub-section (2) of Section 212 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994);
AND WHEREAS, the Revenue Divisional Officer, Erode caused a copy of the statement of charges along with a copy of the 
notice of motion to be delivered, to the said Vice-Chairman Tmt. S. Preethi and she has submitted her reply to the charges;
AND WHEREAS, a motion expressing want of confidence in Tmt. S. Preethi, Vice-Chairman, Kodumudi Panchayat Union 
Council, Erode District had been carried with a support of five members out of the sanctioned strength of six members of the 
said Panchayat Union Council in the meeting convened by the Revenue Divisional Officer, Erode on the 13th August 2021 
after following the procedures specified in the said Section 212 and forwarded the same to Government;
NOW THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (13) of Section 212 of  the Tamil Nadu Panchayats 
Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby removes Tmt. S. Preethi, Vice-Chairman, Kodumudi 
Panchayat Union Council, Erode District from the office of the Vice-Chairman of the said Panchayat Union Council with 
immediate effect.
	
P. AMUDHA,	
	
	
	
	
Principal Secretary to Government.
