The Seventh Economic Census wholly ﬁ nanced by 
Government of India through Central Statistical Oﬃ  ce is 
proposed to be conducted through State Governments/
Union Territories during the year 2019. The ﬁ eld work 
of the Seventh Economic Census is expected to be 
conducted in July 2019 and slated to be completed in 
three months time. The enumeration work for the 
7th Economic Census will be carried out by Common Service 
Centre (CSC), Field Operations Division (FOD) of NSSO 
and State Government UTs have been entrusted with the 
responsibility of supervising the enumeration work carried 
out by CSC on sample basis, in the designated information 
Technology Platform using Oﬃ  cers/Staﬀ  of Department of 
Economics and Statistics, Department of Industries and 
Commerce and Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises - 
Development Institute.
2. During the enumeration work, each and every 
household in the urban and rural areas would be enumerated 
to capture the information on all household enterprises/own 
account enterprises within ﬁ xed premises or without ﬁ xed 
premises, as well as other public and private establishment in 
the State which aims to provide a frame of all establishments 
engaged in various economic activities both in organized 
and unorganized sectors of the Tamil Nadu economy.
3. The Ministry of Statistics and Programme 
Implementation, Government of India in the letter second read 
above has stated that a broad framework for implementation 
of the 7th Economic Census has been ﬁ rmed up and 
therefore requested to constitute a State Level Co-ordination 
Committee and a District Level Co-ordination Committee 
which would provide necessary support for the activities of 
the 7th Economic Census in the State.
4. The Government accordingly decided to constitute the 
State Level Co-ordination Committee under the Chairmanship 
of Chief Secretary to Government, District Level Co-ordination 
Committee (other than Chennai) under the Chairmanship 
of District Collector and Co-ordination Committee for 
Greater Chennai Corporation, under the Chairmanship of 
Commissioner, Greater Chennai Corporation as detailed 
below for the smooth conduct of Seventh Economic Census 
during the year 2019.
