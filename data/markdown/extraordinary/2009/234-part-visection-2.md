[ELECTIONS—URBAN LOCAL BODIES—SEPTEMBER 2009—CASUAL INDIRECT ELECTIONS TO FILL UP THE VACANCIES
IN THE OFFICE OF VICE-CHAIRMAN, MEMBER OF APPOINTMENT COMMITTEE, MEMBER OF WORKS COMMITTEE,
MEMBER OF TAXATION  APPEAL  COMMITTEE—POSTPONED—NOTIFIED.]
(S.O. No. 46/2009/TNSEC/ME.2.)
NO. VI(2)/476(a)/2009.
The Tamil Nadu State Election Commission notified Casual Indirect Elections to certain Urban Local Bodies schedule to be
held on 4-9-2009 in the S.O. No. 44/2009/TNSEC/ME2, dated 18-8-2009.
WHEREAS, on account of the announcement declaring holiday on 4.9.2009 under the Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881 by the
Tamil Nadu State Government vide G.O. Ms. No. 874, Public (Protocal-I), dated 3-9-2009, the Tamil Nadu State Election
Commission decides that conducting of the said Local Body Casual Indirect Elections on 4-9-2009 is not feasible.
AND, THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred under Rule 115 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Third Grade
Municipalities, Municipalities and Corporation (Elections), Rules 2006 the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission hereby directs
that the Local Body Indirect Elections which are scheduled to be held on 4-9-2009 stand postponed.
The date for conducting the said Indirect Elections will be fixed and announced in due course of time.
(By Order of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission)
