TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY 
AMENDMENT TO THE TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY 
SECRETARIAT SERVICE RULES. 
[S.O.Ms. No. 102, Legislative Assembly Secretariat, 21st August 2018, Aavani 5, Vilambi, 
Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2049.]
No. SRO B-68(a)/2018.
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (3) of Article 187 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of 
Tamil Nadu, after consultation with the Speaker of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly, hereby makes the following 
amendments to the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Secretariat Service Rules:-
Amendment
In the said rules, in APPENDIX II referred to in rule 6, in Class II, for the entries in column (4) against the entry 
''Assistants'' in colum (3) and the entry ''Category 6'' in column (2) thereof, the following entries shall be substituted, 
namely:-
“(i) By Direct recruitment;
 (ii) (a)  By Promotion from the post of Typists including Tamil Typists, Record Assistant, Record Keeper, Record 
Clerk, Telephone Operator, Library Assistant, Driver and Rota Print Operator;
       (b)   By Promotion from the post of Office Assistant, Chowkidar and Cleaner;
(iii)  By Recruitment by transfer from the post of Assistant in the Tamil Nadu Secretarit Service or  from the 
category of Junior Assistant or Assistant in the Tamil Nadu Ministerial Service or in the Tamil Nadu Judicial 
Ministerial Service:
