 WHEREAS in supersession of G.O. first read above, Orders have issued in the order second read above on effective 
and immediate measures need to be continued to prevent and contain the spread COVID-19 applicable through the State 
with effect from 25-3-2020 which came into force for a period of 21 days with effect from 25-3-2020.
WHEREAS based on the 2nd 3rd and 4th addendum sent by the Government of India, Ministry of Home Affairs 
amendments were issued in the Government orders third, fourth and fifth read above respectively to the Notification issued 
in the order second read above. 
WHEREAS the Principal Secretary/Commissioner of Revenue Administration in the letter seventh read above has stated 
that the Government of India, Ministry of Home Affairs in the letter sixth read above issued 5th Addendum to the guidelines 
and the addendum has been got reconciled with the Government orders second, third, fourth and fifth read above and sent 
the amendment proposal to the Notification issued in the Government order second read above read with Notification issued 
in the order third, fourth and fifth read above.
NOW THEREFORE the State Government of Tamil Nadu based on the proposal of Principal Secretary and Commissioner 
of Revenue Administration issues following amendment to the Notification issued in the order second cited read with Notification 
issued in the order third, fourth and fifth read above.
Addition of sub-clause (o) in exception to clause 4
 (o)  Operations of the fishing (marine) / aquaculture industry, including feeding & maintenance, harvesting, processing, 
packaging, cold chain, sale and marketing; hatcheries, feed plants, commercial aquaria, movement of fish/shrimp and fish 
products, fish seed/feed and workers for all these activities.
