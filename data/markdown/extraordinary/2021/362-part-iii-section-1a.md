In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of section 34 of the Tamil Nadu Business Facilitation Act, 2018 
(Tamil Nadu Act 7 of 2018), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu Business 
Facilitation Rules, 2017, namely:-
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖගඛ.
In the said Rules,-
 
(1) in rule 11, sub-rules (8) and (9) shall be omitted;
  
(2) for rule 13, the following rule shall be substituted, namely:-
"(13) The provisions of approval under sub-section (1) of section 12 of the Act shall be applicable to the 
clearances speciﬁ ed in S.No.2.1.1 to 2.1.8 in Annexure 2.1, S.No. 2.2.1 and S.No.2.2.2 in Annexure 2.2 and 
S.No.2.5.1, S.No.2.5.77 and S.No.2.5.78 in Annexure 2.5 to these rules, in case the Competent Authority 
concerned fails to decide on the application within the time limit speciﬁ ed under rule 12.  The deemed 
approval shall be issued to the applicant under the name and seal of the Competent Authority concerned, 
through single window portal with a copy to the respective Competent Authority, after obtaining concurrence 
of the Respective Committee”;
 
(3) in rule 21, in sub-rule (2), for clause (ii), the following clause shall be substituted, namely:-
 
 
“(ii) expiry of stipulated time limits under sub-rule (4) of rule 5, sub-rule (14) of rule 11 or sub-rule (3) of rule 14”;
