No. SRO A-14/2017.—The Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Gandhi Adigal
Police Medal Rules published with Prohibition and Excise Department's Notification No. SRO/A-90(a)/96, dated the
24th September, 1996 at pages 1-3 (in English) of Part III—Section 1(a) of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette
Extraordinary, dated the 24th September, 1996, as subsequently amended:-
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules:—
(1) in rule 12, for the expression “Rs. 20,000/-”, the expression “Rs. 40,000/-” (Rupees forty thousand only)
shall be substituted.
(2) in rule 16, for items (iii), (iv) and (v), the following items shall be substituted namely:-
“(iii ) The Additional Director General of Police (Enforcement), Chennai; and
(iv)
The Additional Director General of Police (Law and Order), Chennai.”
