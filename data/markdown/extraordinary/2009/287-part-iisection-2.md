BACKWARD  CLASSES,  MOST  BACKWARD  CLASSES  AND  MINORITIES
 WELFARE  DEPARTMENT
 APPOINTMENT OF THIRU SALAHUDDIN MOHAMMED AYUB AS MEMBER OF
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD UNDER WAKF ACT, 1995.
[G.O. (2D) No. 46, Backward Classes, Most Backward Classes and Minorities Welfare (T1),
10th November 2009. ]
No. II(2)/BCMBCMW/588(b)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 21 of the Wakf Act, 1995 (Central Act, 43 of 1995), the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints Thiru Salahuddin Mohammed Ayub, Chief Khazi as a member of Tamil Nadu
Wakf Board.
T.N. RAMANATHAN,
 Secretary to Government.
