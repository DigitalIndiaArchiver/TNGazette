BACKWARD  CLASSES,  MOST  BACKWARD  CLASSES  AND  MINORITIES
 WELFARE  DEPARTMENT
 APPOINTMENT OF THIRU KAVIKO ABDUL RAHMAN, CHAIRMAN, TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD
AS MEMBER OF TAMIL NADU STATE HAJJ COMMITTEE UNDER HAJJ COMMITTEE ACT.
[G.O. (Ms.) No. 47, Backward Classes, Most Backward Classes and Minorities Welfare (S1),
23rd June 2009. Aani 9, Thiruvalluvar Aandu 2040.]
No. II(2)/BCMBCMW/296(b)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 18(1) (V) of the Hajj Committee Act, 2002 (Central Act, 35
of 2002), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints Thiru Kaviko Abdul Rahman, Chairman, Tamil Nadu
Wakf Board as member of Tamil Nadu State Hajj Committee.
T.N. RAMANATHAN,
 Secretary to Government.
