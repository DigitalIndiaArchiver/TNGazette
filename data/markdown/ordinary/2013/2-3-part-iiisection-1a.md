No. SRO A-1/2013.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 89-B of the  Registration Act, 1908 (Central Act
XVI of 1908), the Governor of  Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Tamil  Nadu Document Writers'
Licence Rules, 1982, as subsequently amended.
2. The amendment hereby made shall come into force on the 17th day of December 2012.
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in rule 5, in clause (m), the expression “or receiving any income from any other sources”
shall be omitted.
SUNIL PALIWAL,
Secretary to Government.
