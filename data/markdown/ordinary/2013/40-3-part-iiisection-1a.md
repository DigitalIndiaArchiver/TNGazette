HOME  DEPARTMENT
Amendment to the Tamil Nadu Suspension of Sentence Rules.
[G.O. Ms. No. 734, Home (Prison-V), 24th September 2013,

No. SRO-A-29/2013.—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (5) of Section 432 of the Code of Criminal
Procedure, 1973 (Central Act 2 of  1974), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the
Tamil Nadu Suspension of Sentence Rules, 1982, as subsequently Amended:—
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in rule 13, after the Proviso, the following proviso shall be added namely:—
“Provided further that where a prisoner has already exhausted the maximum period of emergency leave eligible
to him, the Superintendent of prisons or the Inspector General of Prisons, as the case may be, may grant one day
emergency leave to him to attend the funeral of the prisoner's spouse, father, mother, son, daughter, full brother
or full sister, on each occasion.”.
NIRANJAN MARDI,
Principal Secretary to Government.
REVENUE DEPARTMENT
Amendment to the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Rules.
[G.O. Ms. No. 367, Revenue (L.A.I(1), 23rd September 2013.]
No. SRO-A-30/2013.—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 25 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition
of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the
following Amendment to the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Rules, 2001:—
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in rule 6, in sub-rule (b) for the expression “Within the time prescribed in rule 3 or 4,” the expression
“Within the time specified in the show cause notice and publish notice in Form A and Form B, respectively,” shall be
substituted.
GAGANDEEP SINGH BEDI,
Secretary to Government.
