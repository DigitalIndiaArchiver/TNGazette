The following Notification of the Government of India, Minister of Law and Justice (Department of Justice), Jaisalmer
House, 26, Man Singh Road, New Delhi-110 011, dated 14th July 2014, shall be republished:—
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (1) of Article 222 of the Constitution of India, the President, after
consultation with the Chief Justice of India, is pleased to transfer Shri Justice Sanjay Kishan Kaul, Chief Justice of the
Punjab & Haryana High Court, as the Chief Justice of the Madras High Court and to direct him to assume charge of the
office of the Chief Justice of the Madras High Court on or before 28th July, 2014.
