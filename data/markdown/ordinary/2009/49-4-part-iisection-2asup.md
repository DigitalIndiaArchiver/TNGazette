The following Government Order is published:—
[G.O. Rt. No. 207, Agriculture (AP1), 20th August 2009.]
READ:
1. From the Commissioner of Agriculture letter
 No. M1/12153/09, dated 15-4-2009 and 28-6-2009.
2. From the Regional Manager, Agricultural Insurance
Company of India Limited letter No. 586/09, dated 10-7-2009.
ORDER:—Rt. No. 207, Agriculture (AP1), 20th August 2009.
The Commissioner of Agriculture has requested the
Government to issue necessary orders notifiying the Crops,
Districts, Blocks and Firkas as approved by State Level
Co-ordination Committee on Crop Insurance for Kharif,  2009
and also requested the Government to notifiy the Seasonalty
discipline in the State Gazette for effective implementation
of National Agricultural Insurance Scheme in Tamil Nadu
during Kharif 2009. The Government accept the
recommendation of the Commissioner of Agriculture.
2. The Government of Tamil Nadu under National
Agricultural Insurance Scheme hereby notify the Crops,
Districts, Blocks and Firkas for implementation of National
Agricultural Insurance Scheme for Kharif 2009 as shown in
the Annexure I, II and III to this order.
3. Further, the claims under National Agricultural
Insurance Scheme shall be settled on the basis of yield
data furnished by the Director, Economics and Statistics for
the notified Crops, Districts, Blocks and Firkas for
implementation of National Agricultural Insurance Scheme
for Kharif 2009 and not on the basis of Annavari Certificate/
Gazette notification declaring the area as Drought/Flood/
Cyclone affected etc. by the District Collectors.
(By Order of the Governor)
K. NANDA KISHORE,
Agricultural Production Commissioner
and Secretary to Government.
