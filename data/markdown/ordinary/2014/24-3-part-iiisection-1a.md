LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT DEPARTMENT
Draft Amendments to the Tamil Nadu Plantations Labour Rules
[G.O. Ms. No. 39, Labour and Employment (K2), 8th May 2014, Chithirai 25, Jaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2045.]
No. SRO A-12/2014.—The following draft of amendments to the Tamil Nadu Plantations Labour Rules, 1955 which it is
proposed to make in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-sections (1) and (2) of Section 43 of the Plantations
Labour Act, 1951 (Central Act LXIX of 1951), is hereby published as required by the said sub-section (1) of Section 43
of the said Act.
2. Notice is hereby given that the draft amendments will be taken into consideration on or after the expiry of six weeks
from the date of publication of this Notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette and that any objection or suggestion
which may be received from any persons with respect thereto, before the expiry of the aforesaid period will be considered
by the Government of Tamil Nadu. Objection and suggestion, if any should be addressed to the Principal Secretary to
Government of Tamil Nadu, Labour and Employment Department, Secretariat, Fort St. George, Chennai-600 009, through the
Commissioner of Labour, Chennai-600 006.
DRAFT AMENDMENTS.
In the said Rules,—
(1) in Chapter VI,—
(a) in the heading, the words "AND MATERNITY" shall be omitted;
(b) rules 72, 73, 74, 75, 76, 77, 78, 79 and 80 shall be omitted;
(2) Form Numbers 8, 9, 10 and 11 shall be omitted.
MOHAN PYARE,
Principal Secretary to Government.
