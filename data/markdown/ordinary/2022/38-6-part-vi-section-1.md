TAMIL NADU MEDICAL COUNCIL, CHENNAI.
Conduct of the Election
(Ref.TNMC/Elec.5(1)(f)/2023)
No. VI(1)/387/2022.
 In accordance with rule 17 of the rules for the conduct of the subsequent elections to the Tamil Nadu Medical Council, 
Chennai, under clause (f) of sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Madras Medical Registration Act, 1914 (Act IV of 1914) as 
amended by the Madras Medical Registration (Amendment Act, 1938), an election of Seven members of the Council will 
have to be held in the month of January / February 2023.
