OFFICER—NOTIFICATION ISSUED.]
(S.O. No. 1/2016/TNSEC/PE-I)
No. VI(2)/88(a)/2016.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-rule (1) of rule 6 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Elections)
Rules, 1995, the State Election Commission hereby appoints the Commissioner/Director of Rural Development
and Panchayat Raj, Chennai, as the State Election Officer for performing the functions specified therein in
connection with the conduct of Ordinary Elections, 2016 to Panchayats and subsequent Casual Elections
in the State.
(By order of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission)
