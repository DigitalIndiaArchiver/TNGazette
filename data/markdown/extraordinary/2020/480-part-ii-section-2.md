The Government of Tamil Nadu, having been 
satisﬁ ed that the lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below 
have to be acquired for Industrial purpose to Wit for 
the Establishment of New SIPCOT Industrial Park by 
SIPCOT at 29.Thadangam Village and surrounding 
areas, and it having already been decided that the 
entire amount of compensation to be awarded for 
the lands to be paid out of the lands controlled or 
managed by SIPCOT. The following notice is issued 
under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu 
Acquisition of land for Industrial Purposes Act 1997 (Tamil 
Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil 
Nadu Acquisition of land for Industrial Purposes Act 1997 
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu 
hereby acquires the lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below and 
measuring 7.54.50 Hectares (18.63 Acres) of dry lands to 
the same a little more or less needed for Industrial Purposes, 
to wit, for setting up of New SIPCOT Industrial Park.
The plan of the lands are kept in the oﬃ  ce of the 
Special District Revenue Oﬃ  cer/Land Acquisition, SIPCOT, 
Dharmapuri Taluk, Dharmapuri District and may be inspected 
at any time during oﬃ  ce hours.
