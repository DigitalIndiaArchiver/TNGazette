No. VI(1)/308/2011.
No. 135/2011.—The Government in their letter No. 44977/Cts-1/2011-1, Home (Cts.I) Department, dated 24th June 2011,
have requested the High Court to confer Magisterial Powers on one Tahsildar Trainee in Tiruvallur District for a period of
180 days from the date  of assumption of charge, so that the may try cases which were earlier triable and dealt with by
a Judicial II Class Magistrate and complete his magisterial training in Tiruvallur District:—
On considering the above request, in exercise of the powers conferred by Section 13 of the Code of Criminal Procedure
1973 (Central Act 2 of 1974), the High Court hereby appoint the following one Tahsildar Trainee in Tiruvallur District, to be
the Special Judicial Magistrate for a period of 180 days from the date of assumption of charge and confers upon him the
ordinary powers conferrable under this Code on a Judicial Magistrate to try such cases which were earlier triable and dealt
with by an erstwhile Judicial Second Class Magistrate in Tiruvallur District.
