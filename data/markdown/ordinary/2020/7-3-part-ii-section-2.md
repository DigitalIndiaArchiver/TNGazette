HOME DEPARTMENT
Constitution of  3 Family Courts one each at Perambalur, 
Pudukottai and Tiruvannamalai Districts under the 
Family Courts Act.
[G.O. Ms. No. 26, Home (Courts-II), 10th January 2020,   
ñ£˜èN- 25, Mè£K, F¼õœÀõ˜ Ý‡´-2050.]
No.II(2)/HO/110/2020.—In exercise of the powers 
conferred by clause (b) of sub-section (1) read with 
sub-section (2) of Section 3 of the Family Courts 
Act, 1984 (Central Act 66 of 1984), the Governor of 
Tamil Nadu, after consultation with the High Court 
of Madras, hereby establishes a Family Court at 
Perambalur with effect from the date of assumption 
of charge of the Judge of the said Court and also 
hereby specifies that the jurisdiction of the said Family 
Court shall extend to the local limits of the Perambalur 
Town, inclusive of Perambalur Taluk, Veppanthattai Taluk, 
Kunnam Taluk and Alathur Taluk in Perambalur District.
NOTIFICATION-II.
[G.O. Ms. No. 26, Home (Courts-II), 10th January 2020,   
ñ£˜èN- 25, Mè£K, F¼õœÀõ˜ Ý‡´-2050.]
No.II(2)/HO/111/2020.—In exercise of the powers 
conferred by clause (b) of sub-section (1) read with 
sub-section (2) of Section 3 of the Family Courts 
Act, 1984 (Central Act 66 of 1984), the Governor of 
Tamil Nadu, after consultation with the High Court of 
Madras, hereby establishes a Family Court at Pudukottai 
with effect from the date of assumption of charge of the 
Judge of the said Court and also hereby specifies that 
the jurisdiction of the said Family Court shall extend 
to the local limits of the Pudukottai City, inclusive of 
Pudukottai Taluk.
NOTIFICATION-III.
[G.O. Ms. No. 26, Home (Courts-II), 10th January 2020,

No.II(2)/HO/112/2020.—In exercise of the powers 
conferred by clause (b) of sub-section (1) read with 
sub-section (2) of Section 3 of the Family Courts 
Act, 1984 (Central Act 66 of 1984), the Governor of 
Tamil Nadu, after consultation with the High Court 
of Madras, hereby establishes a Family Court at 
Tiruvannamalai with effect from the date of assumption of 
charge of the Judge of the said Court and also hereby 
specifies that the jurisdiction of the said Family Court 
shall extend to the local limits of the Tiruvannamalai 
City, inclusive of Tiruvannamalai Taluk, Chengam Taluk, 
Kalasapakkam Taluk, Kilpennathur Taluk and Thandarampet 
Taluk in Tiruvannamalai District.
