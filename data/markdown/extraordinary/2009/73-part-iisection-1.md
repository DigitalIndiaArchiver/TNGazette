LEGISLATIVE  ASSEMBLY  SECRETARIAT
AMENDMENT TO DELETION OF THE TAMIL NADU  SUGARCANE  FARM CORPORATION LIMITED
FROM THE LIST OF PUBLIC UNDERTAKINGS.
[Letter No. 5075/2009-1, Tamil Nadu  Legislative Assembly Secretariat (B.III), 12th March 2009,
Maasi 28, Thiruvalluvar Aandu 2040.]
No. II(1)/LAS/2(b)/2009.
 “Under Rule 211(1) of the Tamil Nadu  Legislative Assembly Rules, it is hereby notified that the name
of   “The Tamil Nadu Sugarcane Farm Corporation Limited” be deleted from the list of other Public Undertakings
specified in Appendix-E of the said Rules. Consequently the following amendment is also issued:—
AMENDMENT
In 
Appendix-E 
of 
the 
Tamil 
Nadu 
Legislative 
Assembly 
Rules, 
under 
the 
heading
“Other Public Undertakings”, “The Tamil Nadu Sugarcane Farm Corporation Limited” occurring against
Serial Number 18 shall be deleted.
(By order of the Speaker.)
Secretariat,
M. SELVARAJ,
Chennai-600 009,
 Secretary”.
