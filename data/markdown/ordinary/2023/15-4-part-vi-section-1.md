JUDICIAL NOTIFICATIONS
Constitution of a District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court at Viralimalai in Pudukkottai District
(Roc. No. 1555/A/2019/G/Judn)
No. VI(1)/257/2023.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873) 
the High Court, Madras, hereby ﬁ xes Viralimalai in Pudukkottai District as the place at which the District Munsif-cum-
Judicial Magistrate Court, Viralimalai, shall be held.

NOTIFICATION-ll
(Roc. No. 1555/A/2019/G/Judn)
No. VI(1)/258/2023.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 11 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 
1873) the High Court, Madras, hereby directs that in Pudukkottai District, the District Munsif Court at Keeranur shall 
cease to have local jurisdiction over the entire Viralimalai Taluk and the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, 
Viralimalai shall have and exercise local jurisdiction over the entire Taluk of Viralimalai with eﬀ ect from the date on 
which the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate, Viralimalai assumes charge of that Court. 
NOTIFICATION-III
(Roc. No. 1555/A/2019/G/Judn)
No. VI(1)/259/2023.
The High Court, Madras hereby directs and notiﬁ es that the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate, Viralimalai in 
Pudukkottai District shall exercise all the powers conferred on a District Munsif under Section 12 of the Tamil Nadu 
Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873) as amended up-to-date in regard to original suits and proceedings not 
otherwise exempted from his/her cognizance of which the amount or value of the subject matter does not exceed One 
Lakh Rupees within the local limits of his/her jurisdiction with eﬀ ect from the date on which the District Munsif-cum-
Judicial Magistrate, Viralimalai assumes charge of that Court. 
NOTIFICATION-IV
(Roc. No. 1555/A/2019/G/Judn)
No. VI(1)/260/2023.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 28 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 
1873) the High Court, Madras, hereby directs that the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate, Viralimalai, shall have and 
exercise jurisdiction of a Court of Small Causes under the Provincial Small Cause Courts Act, 1887 for the trial of suits 
cognizable by a Court of Small Causes upto the pecuniary limits of Rs.5000/- (Rupees ﬁ ve thousand only) with eﬀ ect 
from the date on which the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Viralimalai, assumes charge of that Court.
