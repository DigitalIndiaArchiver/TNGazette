GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
Cancellation of Registration of TH.118. Namakkal Powerloom Textiles Manufactures Co-operative Marketing
Society Ltd. in Komarapalayam Taluk, Namakkal District.
[G.L.4/2007/L(2).]
No.VI(1)/214/2016.
It is hereby notified that the affairs of the T.H.118. Namakkal Powerloom Textiles Manufactures Co-operative
Marketing Society Ltd., in Komarapalayam Taluk, Namakkal District has been completely wound and its Registration
has been cancelled under Section 140(1) of the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act, 1983 with effect from
24th May 2016.
M. MUTHUSAMY,
Tiruchengode,
Assistant Director,
24th May 2016.

Variation to the Approved Chithode New Town Development Plan of Chithode New Town
Development Authority.
(Roc. No. 312/2013 CNTDA.)
No.VI(1)/215/2016.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 32 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning
Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972) and in exercise of powers conferred by the G.O. Ms. No. 94, Housing and
Urban Development [UD4(1)] department, dated 12th June 2009, which has published in the Tamil Nadu
Government Gazette  (Issue No.27), Part II—Sectlon-2, Page 228, dated 15th July 2009, of the following variations are
made to the Chithode New Town Development Plan approved under the said Act and published in the Housing and
Urban Development department Notification No. II(2)/HOU/1204/99, page 516, Part II—Section-2 of
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated 27th October 1999.
VARIATION
In the said New Town Development Plan in “Annexure-C” under the heading “Land use of Chithode
New Town Development Plan” in the Tabular Column.
(i) In “Annexure-X” under the heading “VI Agricultural use Zone” against “A-5, 61. Suriyampalayam Village”.
(ii) for the expression 201, the expression 201 except 201/1, 201/2, 201/3, 201/4A, 201/5, 201/6A, 201/11A,
201/12, 201/13, 201/14, 201/15, 201/16A shall be substituted and
(iii) for the expression 203, the expression 203 except 203/1, 203/2, 203/3A, 203/4A, 203/5, 203/6A, 15A1,
16, 17 shall be substituted.
2. In “Annexure VII” Under the heading “III Industrial use zone” after serial number 17 in column (1) and the
entries relating thereto in columns (2) and (3) thereof the following serial number and the entries shall, respectively
be added namely:-
17. I-17-61. Suriyampalayam Village, R.S. Nos. 201/1, 201/2, 201/3, 201/4A, 201/5, 201/6A, 201/11A, 201/12,
201/13, 201/14, 201/15, 201/16A and 203/1, 203/2, 203/3A, 203/4A, 203/5, 203/6A, 15A1, 16, 17.
A. VIJAYAN,
Erode,
Member Secretary (In-charge),
15th June 2016.
