Adjournment of Principal District and Sessions Court, Sivagangai and other 
Subordinate Civil Courts
In Sivagangai District for the Year 2020
(D.No.07/Shr(A)/2020.)
(Notiﬁ cation of the Honourable High Court in ROC.No.88001-A/2019/C3, dated:12/12/2019.)
No.VI(1)/49/2020.
Notice is hereby given that the Principal District and Sessions Court, Sivagangai and other Subordinate Civil Courts in Sivagangai 
District will be closed for Summer Vacation for the year 2020 as detailed below.
SUMMER VACATION – 2020
1. The Principal District and Sessions Court of Sivagangai, The Additional District and Sessions Court, Sivagangai and 
the Civil Courts of the Subordinate and Assistant Sessions Judges of Sivagangai, Manamadurai and Devakottai will remain 
closed from Friday, the 1st day of May-2020 to Sunday, the 31st day of May -2020. (both days inclusive).
2. The District Munsif of Sivagangai, Devakottai, the Court of Principal District Munsif, Manamadurai, the Court of Additional 
District Munsif, Karaikudi, the Courts of District Munsif cum Judicial Magistrates, Karaikudi, Thiruppathur and Ilayangudi, 
Thiruppuvanam, Singampunari (Civil side) and the Court of Additional District Munsif cum Judicial Magistrate, Manamadurai 
(Civil Side) will remain closed from Friday, the 1st day of May-2020 to Sunday, the 31st day of May -2020 (both days inclusive).
3. The Fast Track Mahila Court, the Family Court, the Special Court for exclusive trial of SC/ST (POA), 1992 Act, the 
Special Court for Exclusive Trial of Cases under POCSO Act, Sivagangai and the Special Court for exclusive trial of Prevention 
of corruption cases will remain open through out the vacation.
4. The Oﬃ  ce of the Oﬃ  cial Receiver, Sivagangai will remain open through out the Vacation.
5.  (i) No Plaint or Petitions except applications for grant of copies and for service and execution of process will be 
received during the vacation.
 
(ii) 
In case, a Vacation Civil Judge is appointed by the Government for this District, urgent plaints, appeals 
and petitions in Civil cases alone will be received and disposed oﬀ  by him in exercise of the powers vested in him under 
Section 30 of the Central Act III of 1873, as amended by Madras Act 17 of 1959.
6. 
The Oﬃ  ce of the Copyist and Process Service Establishment will be kept open through out the Vacation, but no 
arrest warrants will be executed during the Vacation.
7. 
The Oﬃ  ce of the other departments will remain closed. Arrangements will however be made
 
(a) for transmitting records in appeals etc., to the Honourable High Court;
 
(b) for attending the all administrative correspondences;
 
(c) for furnishing the copies and process service with necessary records and Original Orders; and
 
(d) for receiving the printing charges and plan fees etc., deposited into Court in respect of Copy application.
8. 
Urgent applications for bail during the absence of the Sessions Judge from the Sivagangai Division during the 
Vacation should be made to the Honourable Madurai Bench of Madras High Court, Madurai provided no Vacation Sessions 
Judge is appointed for Sivagangai Division.
DUSSERA HOLIDAYS
9. The Principal District Court and all other Civil and Sessions Courts in this District will remain closed for the Dussehra 
Holidays from Saturday, the 17th day of October – 2020 to Monday, the 26th day of October – 2020. (both days inclusive).
CHRISTMAS HOLIDAYS
10. The Principal District Court and all other Civil and Sessions Courts in this District will remain closed for the Christmas 
Holidays from Friday, the 25th day of December, 2020 to Thursday, the 31st day of December – 2020. (both days inclusive).
