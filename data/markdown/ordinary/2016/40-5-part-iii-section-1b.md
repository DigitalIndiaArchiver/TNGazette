3. Appointment.- Appointment to the post shall be made as follows:-
(i) by promotion from among the holders of the post of Theatre Assistant or
(ii) by recruitment by transfer from any other service; or
(iii) by direct recruitment, if no qualified candidate is available by the above two methods.
4. Appointing Authority.- The appointing authority for the post shall be the Head of the Institution concerned.
5. Qualification.- (a) Age.- No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post by direct recruitment, if he has completed
or will complete 30 years of age on the first day of July of the year in which the selection for appointment is made or Notification
issued, as the case may be.
(b) Other Qualification.- No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post unless he possesses the following
qualifications, namely:
(i) Must have passed HSC with Physics, Chemistry, Botany and Zoology; or Physics, Chemistry, Biology with any one of
the related subjects; and
(ii) Must have passed a one year certificate course in Operation Theatre Technician from Government Medical Institutions
under the control of the Director of Medical Education or in any other institution recognized by the state or Central Government.
6. Reservation of appointments.- The rule relating to reservation of appointments (General Rule 22) shall apply for
appointment to the post by direct recruitment.
7. Probation.- Every person appointed to the post, by direct recruitment shall, from the date on which he joins duty be
on probation for a total period of two years on duty within a continuous period of three years.
Provided that every person appointed to the post by recruitment by transfer shall, from the date on which he joins
duty be on probation for a period of one year on duty within a continous period of two years.
8. Pay.- There shall be paid to the holder of the post a monthly pay calculated in the Pay Band of PB1 Rs. 5200-20200
with Grade pay of Rs. 1,900.

No. SRO B-67/2016.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Employment and Training Service
(Section 9A of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, Volume II of 1969).
2. The amendment hereby made shall  come into force with effect on and from the 5th April 2010.
AMENDMENT

Training Institute” and “Assistant Director of Training” in column (1),—
(i)  against the method of appointment “Recruitment by transfer” in column (2), for the qualifications in column (3) thereof,
the following shall be substituted, namely:-
“A degree in any branch or Engineering or Technology of any recognised University or Institution under the purview of
All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and practical experience in a workshop or factory for a period of not less than
three years; or
