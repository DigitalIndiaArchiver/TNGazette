Framing of By Law in respect of Ahale Sunnath Jamath Masjith & Madharasa Waqf, Coimbatore District.
(Lr.Rc.No. 18697/10/B3/CBE)
No.VI-3(a)/27/2021.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 69 of the Waqf Act 1995, Tamil Nadu Waqf 
Board in Resolution item No. 32/20 Rc.No.18697/10/B3/CBE, dated 08-12-2020 has approved the Bye Law framed for 
the Waqf viz., Ahale Sunnath Jamath Masjith & Madharasa Waqf, Coimbatore District for better administration of the 
Waqf.
