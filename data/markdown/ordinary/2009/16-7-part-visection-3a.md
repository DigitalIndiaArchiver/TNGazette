Extension of the Period of the Direct Management of Nanbai Makkan Wakf and Shaik Hussain Malick Sha
Dargha Wakf, George Town, Chennai-600 001.
(Rc. No. 4193/B1/2006/Chennai.)
No. VI-3(a)/71/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred and as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995, the Tamil
Nadu Wakf Board in its order in item No. 23/08 in Rc. 4193/C2/06/Chennai, dated 28th February 2008 resolved to
take over the Management of Nanbai Makkan Wakf, George Town,  Chennai-600 001 and Shaik Hussain Malick Sha
Dargha Wakf, George Town, Chennai-600 001 under direct control of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of six
months from the date of the pronouncement of the Board’s order on 29th May 2008 and appointed the Wakf Inspector
Chennai (North) as Executive Officer.
As the period of the direct management was lapsed on 28th November 2008 and due to the administrative reason
the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in item.  No. 254/08 in Rc. 4193/06/B1/Chennai, dated 26th February 2009
has resolved to recognize the period of the direct management from 29th November 2008 to 25th February 2009 and
extended the period of the direct management for a further period of one year from 26th February 2009 to 25th February
2010.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
24th April 2009.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.

CONDITIONS OF SALE
1.
Lots are to be examined by the intending purchasers before bidding and to be taken with faults and errors
to description.
2.
The highest bidder to be the purchaser and in the event of any dispute arising between two or more bidders
the lots so disputed should be put up again and resold.
3.
The bid amount shall be paid in full immediately after the bid is knocked down. If this not possible a deposit
of 25 per cent of bidded amount should be paid on the spot to the auctioneers and a receipt shall be obtained for
the amount paid in case only a deposit of 25 per cent is paid the balance amount should be paid to the Trust Financial
Adviser and Chief Accounts Officer on or before 4.00 p.m. on the third day calculating from the date of confirmation
of sale, failing which the EMD paid will be forfeited and the Trust will resell the lot either in sale by tender or by
Public Auction at the risk and responsibility of the defaulters.
4.
Goods advertised for sale will not be withdrawn by the Trust except on payment of sufficient deposit to cover
the Harbour dues, demurrage charges and other charges due thereon.
5.
The goods sold in Auction will not be delivered to the purchaser thereof or his authorized agent unless the
purchaser produced delivery order from the Auctioneers for having paid the amount in full.
6.
Goods sold in Auction shall be allowed three free days excluding Sundays and Board’s holidays from the date
of confirmation of sale and thereafter shall be charged at daily rated as per Scale of Rates up to and including the
date of clearance.
7.
The lots sold will be lying at the risk of the purchaser and must be removed within the free period of three
days from the date of confirmation of sale at the purchaser’s expenses.
8.
The purchasers are distinctly warned that if the goods are not cleared within eight days from the date of expiry
of free period they have no claim whatsoever over the materials and the same will be sold whether in sale by Tender
or Public Auction. The first purchaser to be held liable for any loss arising from such resale and to forfeit any advances
together with auctioned commission.
9.
The Trust shall reserve the rights to withdraw any of the items notified for sale without assigning any reason
for such withdrawal.
10. Bidders shall gain admission into the Harbour on the days of Inspection and as the passes can be obtained
on payment from the Programmer, Computer Pass Section near Main Gate opposite to Customs House, Chennai-
600 001. The successful bidder should also obtain pass on payment for clearing the goods.
11. Terms Cash or Demand Draft, Pay Order of any Nationalized Bank.
12. All the Lots sold in the auction are subject to confirmation.
13. If any packages are not sold in the auction for which they are listed they will be included for sale on the
succeeding sale by Tender or in Public Auction.
