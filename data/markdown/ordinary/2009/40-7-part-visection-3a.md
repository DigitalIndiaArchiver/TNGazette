GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD
Taking over the Direct Management of Batlagundu Tholugai Pallivasal,
Batlagundu Town, Nilakkottai Taluk, Dindigul District.
(Rc. No. 616/06/B15/Dgl.)
No. VI-3(a)/253/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995, the
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its proceedings in item No. 31/09, 62/09, 60/09, dated 28th July 2009 has ordered to take
over the management of the Batlagundu Tholugai Pallivasal, Batlagundu Town, Nilakkottai Taluk, Dindigul District under
the direct control of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of one year from the date of the Board's order and
to appoint the Superintendent of Wakfs, Madurai as its Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
18th September 2009.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
