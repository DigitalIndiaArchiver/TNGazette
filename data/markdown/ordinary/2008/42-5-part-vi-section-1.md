No. VI(1)/366/2008.
The affairs of the 0.1117 Sundarapandiapuram Weavers Co-operative Production and Sales Society Ltd., (Liquidated),
Tenkasi Taluk, Tirunelveli District which were ordered to be wound up under Section 85(1) of the Tamil Nadu

(Rc. No. 50996/ICF/04-2.)
No. VI(1)/367/2008.
Under powers conferred on the Registrar of Industrial Co-operatives under Section 137(1) of the Tamil Nadu
Co-operative Societies Act, 1983 (Act 30 of 1983), the Activities of the Erode District Auto Rickshaw Drivers
Industrial Co-operative Society Ltd., No. IND. 1889. Erode have been ordered to be wound up with immediate effect by
the Industries Commissioner and Director of Industries and Commerce, Chepauk, Chennai-5 vide proceedings
Rc. No. 50996/ICF/04-1, dated 4th September 2008.
Under Section 138(1) of the same Act, Assistant Director (i/c.)/Liquidator, TAICO Bank, Chennai is appointed as
Official Liquidator for the purpose of Liquidation.

Industries Commissioner and
