 THE COMMERCIAL COURTS, COMMERCIAL DIVISION AND
COMMERCIAL APPELLATE DIVISION OF HIGH COURTS ACT, 2015.
(Roc. No. 3233/A/2016/G4)
NOTIFICATION-I
No.VI(1)/348(a-1)/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 4 of the Commercial Courts, Commercial Division and Commercial
Appellate Division of High Courts Act, 2015 (No. 4 of 2016) the Hon'ble The Chief Justice is pleased to constitute
Commercial Division in the High Court of Judicature at Madras consisting of two single Benches, at its Principal Seat
at Madras for the purpose of exercising the jurisdiction and powers conferred on the Commercial Division, by the Act.
NOTIFICATION-II
(Roc. No. 3233/A/2016/G4)
No.VI(1)/348(a-2)/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 5 of the Commercial Courts, Commercial Division and Commercial
Appellate Division of High Courts Act, 2015 (No. 4 of 2016), the Hon'ble The Chief Justice is pleased to constitute
Commercial Appellate Division in the High Court of Judicature at Madras, consisting of two Division Benches, one at
its Principal Seat at Madras and one at its Madurai Bench of Madras High Court, for the purpose of exercising the
jurisdiction and powers conferred on the Commercial Appellate Division, by the Act.
