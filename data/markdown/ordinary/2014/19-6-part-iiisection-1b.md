MUNICIPAL  ADMINISTRATION AND WATER SUPPLY DEPARTMENT
Amendments to the Special Rules for Tamil Nadu Municipal Commissioner's Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 46, Municipal  Administration and Water Supply (ME.1), 5th May 2014,]
No. SRO B-34/2014.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for Tamil Nadu Municipal Commissioner's
Service (Section 21-B in Volume-II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1969).
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force with immediate effect.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules, in rule 6
1.
in the second proviso to sub-rule(i) for the expression "two Section  Officers", the expression "Three Section Officers"
shall be substituted;
2. in sub-rule (ii),—
(a) for the expression "ten years", the expression "five years" shall be substituted; and
(b) in the proviso, for the expression "two Superintendent" the expression "three Superintendents" shall be substituted.
K. PHANINDRA REDDY,
Principal Secretary to Government.
