No. II(2)/CTR/260/2012.—In exercise of the powers conferred
by clause (b) of sub-section (1) of Section 9 of the Indian Stamp
Act, 1899 (Central Act II of 1899), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby provides for the consolidation of duty of Rs. 14,00,000/-

Insurance Company Limited for the period from 1st April
2012 to 31st March 2013.

Acquisition of Lands.
[G.O.(D) No. 73, Highways and Minor Ports (HP.2),
4th May 2012.]
No. II(2)/HWMP/263/2012.—The Governor of Tamil Nadu
having been satisfied that the lands specified in the Schedule
below have to be acquired for Highways for the Reconstruction
of bridge across the river Vattaru at km 29/4-6 of Kumbakonam-
Kodavasal-Koradachery-Mavuor Road in Thiruvarur District
and it has already been decided that the entire amount of
compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid out of
the funds controlled and managed by Divisional Engineer
(Highways), Thiruvarur after considering the cause shown by
the owner (or) other person having interest on such land as the
case may be.  The following notice is issued under sub-section
(1) of Section 15 of Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001
(Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE.
Under sub-section(1) of Section 15 of Tamil Nadu
Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002), the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands specified in the
Schedule below and measuring to an extent of 0.01.29
hectares to the same, a little more or less required for the
Reconstruction of bridge across river Vettaru at km 29/4-6 of
Kumbakonam-Kodavasal-Koradachery-Mavuor Road in
Pathur Village, Kodavasal Taluk in Tiruvarur District.
The plan of the lands is kept in the office of the District
Collector, Thiruvarur and may be inspected at any time during
office hours.

HOME PROHIBITION AND EXCISE DEPARTMENT.
Amendments to Tamil Nadu Prohibition Act.
[G.O. Ms. No. 15, Home, Prohibition and Excise (III),
8th May 2012.]
No. II(2)/HOPE/265/2012.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by sub-section (1) of section 18-B of the Tamil Nadu
Prohibition Act, 1937 (Tamil Nadu Act X of 1937), the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments
to the Prohibition and Excise Department Notification
No. II(1)/PE/239(d)/81, dated the 1st May 1981, published at
page 2 of Part II—Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Government
Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 1st May 1981, as
subsequently amended:—
AMENDMENTS.
In the said Notification, in the Table, in the Annexure
under the entry “Indian Made Foreign Spirits (brand names
of which are specified in the Annexure) other then
sacramental wine”,—
(1) under the heading “ORDINARY BRANDS”, after the
sub-heading
“Thiruvalargal Golden Vats (Private) Limited” and the
entries thereunder, the following sub-heading and entries
shall be added, namely:—
“Thiruvalargal Imperial Spirits and Wine Private Limited
Imperial’s Express Special Brandy
Imperial’s Express Special XXX Rum”;
(2) Under the heading “MEDIUM BRANDS”, after the
sub-heading “Thiruvalargal Golden Vats (Private) Limited” and
the entries thereunder, the following sub-heading and entries
shall be added, namely:—
“Thiruvalargal Imperial Spirits and Wine Private Limited
Emperor Deluxe Brandy
Black Magic Superior Brandy”; and
(3) under the heading “PREMIUM BRANDS”, after the
sub-heading “Thiruvalargal Golden Vats (Private) Limited” and
the entries thereunder, the following sub-heading and entries
shall be added, namely:—
“Thiruvalargal Imperial Spirits and Wine Private Limited
Imperial’s Gold Napoleon Reserve VSOP Brandy
Imperial’s Club Class Brandy
Imperial’s Marcel XO Excellance Brandy
Bootz Authentic Dutch Grape Brandy
BOLS Premier X.O. Excellence Brandy
Imperial XO French Premium Brandy
Courrier Napoleon Ultra French Brandy
TI’s Mansion House Ultra French Brandy
Mansion House Silk Premium French Brandy
TI’s V.S.O.P. Ultra Brandy
Imperial’s Exclusive Select VSOP Brandy
Flame Ultra Premium Vodka
Mont Castle Finest Grape Brandy
Roulette XO Select Grape Brandy’.
