No. I/EF/24/2018.—The following Notification of the 
Government of India, Ministry of Environment, Forests and 
Climate Change, New Delhi, dated the 13th April 2018 is 
republished:—   
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-rule (2) of 
rule 5 of the Indian Forest Service (Cadre) Rules, 1966, the 
Central Government with the concurrence of the Government 
of Madhya Pradesh and Tamil Nadu hereby transfer 
Sh. Abhishek Tomer, IFS.,  (MP: 2014) from Madhya Pradesh 
to Tamil Nadu Cadre with immediate effect, on marriage 
grounds.
