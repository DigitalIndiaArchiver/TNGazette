TAMIL NADU STATE LEGAL SERVICES AUTHORITY CHENNAI.
Amendments to the Regulations Framed by the Tamil Nadu State Legal Services Authority For the
High Court Legal Services Committee.
(TNSLSA No. 3845/E/2012.)
No. VI(2)/313/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 29A of the Legal Services Authorities Act 1987 (39 of 1987) the Tamil Nadu
State legal Services Authoity hereby makes the following amendments to the Regulations framed by the Tamil Nadu State Legal
Services Authority for the High Court Legal Services Committee dated 7th January 1998.
AMENDMENTS
In the Said Regulations:
1.
The Regulation 4 (4) is deleted.
2.
In Regulation 4, for the expression “Secretary of the High Court Legal Services Committee” Wherever it occur the
expression “Secretary to the High Court Legal Services Committee” Shall be substituted.
High Court Compus,
K. ARUL,
Chennai-600 104,
District Judge / Member Secretary.(FAC).
