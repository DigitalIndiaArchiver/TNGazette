No. II(2)/HOU/300/2012.—In exercise of powers conferred
under Section 113 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country
Planning Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972), the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby exempt the construction of Commercial
and Residential building at Door No. 7, “A” Block, Second
Avenue, 4th Street, Arignar Anna Nagar, Chennai-102 from
the provisions of Regulation 26(4) relating to Height of the
Building, 26(4)E(i) relating to Front Set Back, 26(4)E(ii) relating
to Side Set Back (South), 26(4)E(iii) relating to Rear Set
Back, 26(4)D relating to Floor Space Index and 26(7) relating
to Car Parking requirements respectively of Second Master
Plan for Chennai Metropolitian Area, 2026. Subject to condition
that Thiru A.V.S. Raja shall provide the temporary work area
as required for by the CMRL during the construction period.
J. RADHAKRISHNAN,
Secretary to Government (in-charge).
