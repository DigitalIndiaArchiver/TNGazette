Under clauses (a) and (e) of section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Admission in Professional Educational Institutions
Act, 2006  (Tamil Nadu Act 3 of 2007), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby authorizes the Annamalai University
to select and allot students for admission to the following professional educational courses of that University:—
(i)
Bachelor of Engineering;
(ii)
Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery;
(iii)
Bachelor of Dental Surgery;
(iv)
Bachelor of Science (Agriculture);
(v)
Bachelor of Science (Horticulture);
(vi)
Bachelor of Science (Nursing);
(vii)
Bachelor of Pharmacy;
(viii)
Bachelor of Physiotherapy.
HEMANT KUMAR SINHA,
