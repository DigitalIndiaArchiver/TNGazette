Election of Four Members to the Senate by the Members of the Academic Council.
(B2/14804/2011.)
No. VI-3(b)/17/2011.
It is hereby notified, by direction of the Vice-Chancellor that under Section 15, Class II Other Members sub-clause (2)
of the Annamalai University Act of 1928, an election will be held to elect Four members to the Senate by the members of
the Academic Council.
Each voter will be at liberty to nominate qualified person to fill the vacancy. Every nomination shall be made by an elector
in writing and shall be seconded in writing by another elector. Every such nomination shall be accompanied by the consent
in writing of the nominee agreeing to serve on the Senate, if elected and the nomination should reach the Registrar not later
than 4.00 p.m. on 31st January 2011.
