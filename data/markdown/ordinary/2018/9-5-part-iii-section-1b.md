No. SRO B-15/2018:—In exercise  of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following Amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Basic Service.
2.	
The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 22nd December 2011.  
Amendments
In the said Rules, in the Annexure,- 
(1) 	 Under CLASS I, under CATEGORY-4, under the heading "Miscellaneous Department", after the entry "Office of the 
Government Museum" in Column (1) and the corresponding entry " Director of Museum" in Column (2) there of, the entries 
"Office of the Commissioner, Tamil Nadu Food Safety and Drug Administration Department" and "Deputy Commissioner 
(Administration)" shall, respectively, be inserted; 
(2) 	 Under CLASS III, IV & V, under the heading "Miscellaneous Department", after the entry "Market Committees" in 
Column (1) and the corresponding entry "Secretaries of the Market Committee Concerned" in Column (2) there of, the entries 
"Office of the Tamil Nadu Food Safety and Drug Administration" and "Deputy Director (Administration)" shall, respectively, be 
added.
S. SWARNA,
 Secretary to Government.
