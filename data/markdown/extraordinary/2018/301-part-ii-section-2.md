The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied 
that the land specified in the schedule below has to be 
acquired for industrial purpose to wit to form Sriperumbudur 
Industrial Complex by SIPCOT and it having already been 
decided that the entire amount of compensation to be 
awarded for the lands is to be paid out of the funds 
controlled or managed by the Government, the following 
Notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the 
Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 
1997  (Tamil Nadu Act 10/1999).
NOTICE 
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu 
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 
(Tamil Nadu Act, 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu 
hereby acquires the lands specified in the schedule below 
and measuring 0.02.0 hectare to the same, a little more or 
less needed for formation of the Sriperumbudur Industrial 
Complex by SIPCOT. 
The plans of the lands are kept in the Office of 
the Special Tahsildar (LA), SIPCOT, Irungattukottai and 
Sriperumbudur Schemes, Irungattukottai SIPCOT Project 
Office Compound Irungattukottai-602 117, Sriperumbudur 
Taluk, Kanchipuram  and may be inspected at any time 
during office hours.
