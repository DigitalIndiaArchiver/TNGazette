APPOINTMENT OF CERTAIN OFFICIAL AS ELECTION AUTHORITY FOR CONDUCTING
ELECTION OF MEMBERS OF TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD UNDER THE WAKF ACT.
[G.O. (2D) No. 19, Backward Classes, Most Backward Classes and Minorities Welfare (T),
21st June 2012]
No. II(2)/BCMBCMW/319(c)/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (a) of sub-rule (i) of rule 3 of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board
(Conduct of Election for Members) Rules 1997, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints
Thiru Thanga Kaliyaperumal, I.A.S., Secretary to Government, Backward Classes, Most Backward Classes
and Minorities Welfare Department, Chennai-9, as Election Authority for the purpose of conducting election
of Members of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board as specified in the said Rules.
THANGA KALIYAPERUMAL,
Secretary to Government.
