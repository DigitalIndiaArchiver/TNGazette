  EXPLANATORY STATEMENT
The Commission has been mandated to ﬁ x Renewable Energy Purchase Obligation (RPO) under Section 86(1)(e) of 
the Electricity Act,2003. Accordingly, the Commission has notiﬁ ed the RPO  until  FY 2017-18 in its RPO Regulations. 
Distribution Licensee, Open Access Consumers and Captive Consumers are the obligated entities under the above 
Regulation. The RPO ﬁ xed by the Commission for FY 2017-18 continues to be applicable for further period until any 
revision is eﬀ ected by the Commission as per the Commission’s RPO Regulations.
The Ministry of Power in consultation of Ministry of New and Renewable Energy has in the order dt.14-6-2018 
notiﬁ ed a long term growth trajectory of Renewable Energy Purchase Obligations (RPO) for Solar and Non Solar for 
consideration of State Electricity Regulatory Commissions to notify RPO in their respective States. In consideration to
