amendments  to the Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Preparation of plans and estimates for works and mode and conditions of

Explanations:—(1) Upgrading of a road from lower surface condition to higher surface condition by providing sub-base and
/ or Base courses with or without Black Topping will be deemed as  an original work. For example, re-laying of Water Bound
Macadam (WBM) layer(s) and Black Topping (BT) layer as a whole will be deemed as an original work and not maintenance
work.
(2) Improvements, upgradation or strengthening of existing damaged Black Topped or Non-Black Topped roads by
providing sub-base and/or Base courses with or without Black Topping will be deemed as an original work.
(3) Re-laying or renewal of Bituminous Layer along with or without patches will be deemed as  a maintenance work.
(4) Assistant Engineers shall personally inspect not less than fifty per cent of the proposed works before according
technical sanction. Assistant Executive Engineers and Executive Engineers shall personally inspect at least twenty five per cent
of the proposed works before according technical sanction to check the adequacy and correctness of the provisions made in
the estimate with reference to the requirement of the site and also the suitability of the site for the proposed work.
(5) Assistant Engineers and Assistant Executive Engineers shall make a joint inspection of all works which are to be
submitted for technical sanction to the Superintending Engineer to check the adequacy and correctness of the provisions made
in the estimate.
(6) Executive Engineers should inspect atleast twenty per cent of the works which are to be submitted for technical
sanction to the Superintending Engineer to check the adequacy and correctness of the provisions made in the estimate.
(7) Any person, working in the Rural Development and Panchayat Raj Department, holding technical posts equivalent to
Assistant Engineer, Assistant Executive Engineer and Executive Engineer are also competent to accord technical sanction as
indicated above.
