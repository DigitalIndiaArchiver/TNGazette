GENERAL  NOTIFICATIONS
Loss of “C” Declaration Form
(R.c. No. 740/2009/C2)
No. VI(1)/215/2009.
It has been brought to the notice of the undersigned by the Assistant Commissioner (Commercial Taxes),
Ponneri Assessment Circle, RC. 113/2009/A2/dated 23-1-2009 that Thiruvalargal U.V. Boards Limited, No. 1/138,
Ellamman Koil Street, Athipedu Village, Azhinjivakkam Post, Sholavaram, Chennai-600 067 a registered dealers in the
books of the Assistant Commissioner (Commercial Taxes), Ponneri Assessment Circle, that the “Form C” bearing
Sl. Nos. TN 2005-C-AA-1029707 to TN 2005-C-AA-1029731 in Triplicate issued to them had been lost,
(TN 2005-C-AA-1029707 to TN 2005-C-AA-1029717 details filled but not signed and blank from
TN 2005-C-AA-1029718 to TN 2005-C-AA-1029731).
The above said dealer has lodged a complaint to the Inspector of Police, E5 Sholavaram Police Station
on 23rd January 2009 for the missing of Form ‘C’.
It is hereby notified for the information of the General Public and Authorities concerned that the above form is
declared as invalid. Anyone using the above form will render himself/herself liable to penal action as per law.
Kanchipuram,
K. KANCHANA,
1st July 2009.
Deputy Commissioner (CT).
Final Closing of affairs and Cancellation of Registration of
T.A. 111. Therku Valliyoor Women Beedi Workers Co-operative Thrift and Credit Society Limited.
(G.L.F. No. 4/2003)
No.VI(1)/216/2009.
The affairs of the T.A. 111. Therku Valliyoor Women Beedi Workers Co-operative Thrift and Credit Society Limited.
have ordered to be wound up as per the Proceedings R.C.2612/03 LDB, Dated 31-12-2003. The Final closure proposal
submitted by the Official Liquidator Cheranmahadevi, dated 30-6-2009 was examined and accepted.
2.
In the circumstances stated by the Co-operative Sub-Registrar/Liquidator, the affairs of the T.A. 111. Therku
Valliyoor Women Beedi Workers Co-operative Thrift and Credit Society Limited are deemed to be finally closed. The
registration of the above society is hereby Cancelled under Section 140 of the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies
Act,1983.
Cheranmahadevi,
I. IYANU,
30th June 2009.
Deputy Registrar of Co-operative Societies.
