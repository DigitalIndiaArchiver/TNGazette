Notice under Section 15 (1) of Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (TN Act 34 of 2002), read with the Tamil Nadu Land Acquisition 
Laws (Revival of Operation Amendment and Validation) Act, 2019.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that the lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below are to be acquired 
for the public purpose, that is, for the construction of Western Ring-Road to Coimbatore City, in  Madampatti Village of Perur 
Taluk, in Coimbatore District, and it has already been decided that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the 
lands, is to be paid out of the funds controlled and managed by the Chief Engineer, Highways (Construction and Maintenance), 
Chennai and after having considered the cause shown by the owners or the other persons having interest on the said lands, as 
the case may be, do hereby publish the following notice under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 
(Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002), the Commissioner of 
Land Administration, hereby acquires the lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below and measuring to an extent of 1,00,994 square 
meters of Dry Lands. To the same, a little more or less is needed for construction of Western Ring-Road to Coimbatore City, 
between KM 166/6 of Salem-Kochin Road and KM 360/6 of NH 67 Nagapattinam - Gudalur - Mysore Road, in Madampatty 
Village of Perur Taluk, in Coimbatore District.
 
The plan of lands under acquisition is kept at the oﬃ  ce of the District Collector, Coimbatore and may be inspected at 
anytime during oﬃ  ce hours.
