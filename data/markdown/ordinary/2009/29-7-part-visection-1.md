GENERAL  NOTIFICATIONS
Winding up the affairs of the Musiri Sri Alaganachiamman Handloom Weavers Co-operative Production and
Sales Society Limited TR(H) 136, Musiri, Tiruchirappalli District with effect from 30-6-2009 and Appointment of the
Liquidator.
(RC. No. 5541/07/C-2)
No. VI(1)/219/2009.
The Assistant Director of Handlooms and Textiles, Tiruchirappalli orders the winding up of the affairs of the Musiri
Sri Alaganachiamman Handloom Weavers Co-operative Production and Sales Society Limited TR(H), 136, Tiruchirappalli
District under Section 137(2) of the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act, 30 of 1983 as the Society ceased to fulfil
the objects of its Registration.
Under Section 138(1) of the same Act the Handloom Officer/Liquidator, Tiruchirappalli District Central Co-operative
Bank, Tiruchirappalli is appointed as its Liquidator.
Tiruchirappalli,
T.K. EGAMBARAM,
30th June 2009.
Assistant Director of Handlooms and Textiles (FAC).
Draft Variation to the sanctioned Kathalampatti Town Planning Scheme of
Virudhunagar Local Planning Area.
(Roc. No. 14363/2008/DP2)
No. VI(1)/220/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (1) of Section 33 of Town and Country Planning Act, 1971,
(Act No. 35 of 1972), the Commissioner of Town and Country Planning, Chennai in his proceedings
Roc. No. 14363/2008/DP2, dated 8-7-2009 proposes to make the following sanctioned to the Kathalampatti Town
Planning Scheme, G.O. Ms. No. 4010, Health Department, dated 24-11-1950 of Virudhunagar Local Planning Area and
Published in the Fort St. George Gazette No. 48-A, Supplement to Part 1-A, dated 5-12-1950.
2.
Any person affected or interested in this draft variation may within SIXTY days from the date of publication of
this notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, can represent in person or submit in writing to the
Member-Secretary, Virudhunagar Local Planning Authority any objection and suggestions relating thereto.
3.
The variation with plan may be inspected free of cost any time during office hours at the above said
Local Planning Authority office.
