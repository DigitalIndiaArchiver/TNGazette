COMMERCIAL TAXES AND REGISTRATION DEPARTMENT
Amendments to Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Commercial Taxes Service
[G.O. Ms. No. 25, Commercial Taxes and Registration (EI), 27th February 2014,
Maasi 15, Vijaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu 2045.]
No. SRO B-24/2014.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Commercial Taxes
Service (Section 6 in Volume II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual 1970):—
2.
The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 30th July 2008.
AMENDEMNTS
In the said Special Rules,—
(1) for the expression "Joint Commissioner" wherever it occurs, the expression "Additional Commissioner"
shall be substituted;
(2) for the expression "Deputy Commissioners" wherever it occurs, the expression “Joint Commissioners”
shall be substituted;
(3) for the expression “Deputy Commissioner" wherever it occurs, the expression “Joint Commissioner”
shall be substituted;
(4) for the expression “Assistant Commissioners” wherever it occurs, the expression “Deputy Commissioners”
shall be substituted;
(5) for the expression “Commercial Tax Officers” wherever it occurs, the expression “Assistant Commissioners”
shall be substituted;
(6) for the expression “Commercial Tax Officer” wherever it occurs, the expression “Assistant Commissioner”
shall be substituted;
(7) for the expression “Deputy Commercial Tax Officers” wherever it occurs, the expression “Commercial Tax Officers”
shall be substituted;
(8) for the expression “Deputy Commercial Tax Officer” wherever it occurs, the expression “Commercial Tax Officer”
shall be substituted.
S.K. PRABAKAR,
Principal Secretary to Government.
