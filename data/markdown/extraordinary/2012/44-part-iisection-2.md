HOUSING AND URBAN DEVELOPMENT DEPARTMENT
AMENDMENT TO NOTIFICATION UNDER LAND ACQUISITION  ACT.
[Letter No. 6419/UD3(2)/2011-10, 20th February 2012.]
No. II(2)/HOU/111(d)/2012.
Whereas, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby direct the following amendment be made to notification issued
in Notification No. 139, published at page No. 2 in the Part II—Section-2 of Tamil Nadu Government Gazette
Extra-ordinary dated 17th May 2011, approved in G.O. (Ms.) No. 84, Housing and Urban Development (UD3-2)
Department, dated 17th May 2011.
AMENDMENT
Under the schedule in the above notification, the following para shall be deleted since the said land is excess
vacant land.
(i)
"Manavari, Dry Land, S.No. 21/3A1A1A, Register Holder Thiruvengadamudaliyar, bounded on the
north by S.Nos. 21/3A1A1B, 5B, 4B and 2A1A1B, south by S.Nos. 25 and 21/3A1A2, east by
S.No. 21/3A1A1B and west by S.No. 21/3A1A2—0.11.7 Hectare.”
(ii)
“The total appearing 0.60.6 Hectare under schedule may be read as 0.48.9 Hectare”.
K. PHANINDRA REDDY,
Secretary to Government.
