4. Appointing Authority.- The Appointing Authority for the posts shall be the Government. 
5. Probation.- Every person appointed to the post of Regional Probation Oﬃ  cer shall, from the date on which he joins 
duty, be on probation for a total period of one year on duty within a continuous period of two years. 
6. Declaration of Probation.- The authority competent to declare the satisfactory completion of probation for the 
post of Regional Probation Oﬃ  cer shall be the Director/Commissioner of Social Defence. 
7. Training.- Every person appointed to the posts of Chief Probation Oﬃ  cer and Regional Probation Oﬃ  cer shall undergo 
training for a period of sixty days in the Juvenile Justice Board, Child Welfare Committee, Tamil Nadu State Legal Services 
Authority, Oﬃ  ce of the Commissioner of Police, Chief Metropoiitan Magistrate Courts or Chief Judicial Magistrate Courts, 
Probation wing in the Prison Department, Oﬃ  ce of the Director of Prosecution, Tamil Nadu Commission for Protection of 
Child Rights and in the State Human Rights Commission. 
8. Leave, Postings and Transfers.- 
(a) Postings and transfers of holders of the posts of Chief Probation Oﬃ  cer and Regional Probation Oﬃ  cer shall be 
made by the Government. 
(b) Leave to the holders of the posts of Chief Probation Oﬃ  cer and Regional Probation Oﬃ  cer shall be granted by the 
Director/Commissioner of Social Defence. 
9. Preparation of annual list of approved candidates.- The crucial date for preparation of annual list of approved 
candidates for appointment to the categories by promotion or by recruitment by transfer, on which the candidates shall be 
qualiﬁ ed, shall be the 15th February of every year. 
10. Pay.- There shall be paid to the holders of the post, a monthly pay calculated in the level 25, (Rs.59,300-1,87,700/-) 
in the pay matrix for regular employees for the post of Chief Probation Oﬃ  cer and level 22, (Rs.56,100-1,77,500/-) in the 
pay matrix for regular employees for the post of Regional Probation Oﬃ  cer. 
11. Saving Clause.- Nothing contained in these Ruies shall adversely aﬀ ect any person who is holding the post in this 
service on the date of publication of these Rules.
