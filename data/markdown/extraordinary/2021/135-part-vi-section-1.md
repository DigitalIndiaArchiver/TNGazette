ACQUISITION OF LANDS
(CPRRP /CLA/15(1) Section I / 1 /2021)
NOTIFICATION UNDER SUB-SECTION(1) OF SECTION 15 OF THE TAMIL NADU HIGHWAYS 
ACT,2001(TAMIL NADU ACT 34 OF 2002) READ WITH TAMIL NADU LAND ACQUSITION LAWS
(REVIVAL OF OPERATION, AMENDEMENT AND VALIDATION) ACT,2019
No.VI(1)/70(b-1)/2021.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having  been satisﬁ ed that the land speciﬁ ed in the schedule 
below are acquired under Chennai Peripheral Ring Road  Project  for Highways purpose for laying of 
Northern Port Access  Road, Amoor Village in Ponneri Taluk, Tiruvallur District,  and it having already 
been decided that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands to be paid out of 
the funds  controlled and  managed by the Divisional Engineer(H), Tiruvallur, Tamil Nadu  after having 
considering the cause shown by the owner or other persons having interest on such land  as the case 
may be do hereby publish the following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the 
Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (TN Act 34 of 2002)
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 
(Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) Read with Tamil Nadu Land Acquisition Laws (Revival of Operation, 
Amendment and Validation) Act, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notiﬁ es that the Wet land speciﬁ ed 
in the schedule below and measuring to an extent 2962 of sq.meters  of land are acquired in Amoor 
Village in Ponneri Taluk, Tiruvallur District,  to the same, a little more or less needed for Chennai 
Peripheral Ring Road  Project  for State Highways purpose, for laying of  Northern Port Access  Road.
