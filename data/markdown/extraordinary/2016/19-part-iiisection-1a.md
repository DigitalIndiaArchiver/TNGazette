AMENDMENTS TO THE TAMIL NADU DISTRICT MUNICIPALITIES (BUILDING) RULES, 1972

In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 191 and sub-section (1) of Section 303 of the Tamil Nadu District
Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to
the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities (Building) Rules, 1972 , as subsequently amended:-
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules, in Appendix –I,-
1. for item 1, the following item shall be substituted, namely:-
“ 1. In the following categories of buildings in which there is a system of installation for supplying hot water, a provision shall
be made for an auxiliary solar assisted water heating system,-
(a) hospitals and nursing homes exceeding 150 Square metre in floor area;
(b) hotels and lodges including guest houses exceeding  150 Square metre  in floor area;
(c) hostels of Schools, Colleges, Training Centres etc., having fifteen rooms or more;
(d) barracks of armed forces, paramilitary forces and police;
(e) individual residential buildings including flats exceeding 150 Square metre in plinth area;
(f)
functional buildings of railway stations and airports like waiting rooms, retiring rooms, rest-rooms, inspection bungalows
and catering units etc;

ORDER: No.19,  Municipal Administration and Water Supply (MA.1), 29-01-2016.
In the Government order first read above orders issued amendments to the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Building
Rules, 1972 and Tamil Nadu District Municipalities (Hill Stations) Building Rules, 1993 so as to include provisions for Solar
Powered Water Heating System in Hospitals, Nursing Homes, Hotels, Colleges, Barracks of Armed Forces, residential building
having  more  than  150  Square metres  plinth  area,  etc.
2. In the Government order second read above, the Government have issued orders specifying the minimum floor area for
all categories of buildings, in the Chennai Metropolitan Area, for installation of Solar Water Heating Systems.
3. In the letter third read above, the Commissioner of Municipal Administration has sent proposal to Government for
amendment to the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities (Building) Rules, 1972 and the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities
(Hill Stations) Building Rules, 1993.
4. In the letter  fourth read above, the Commissioner of Municipal Administration has stated that a meeting was held on
07.12.2012  with the officials of Municipal Administration and it was decided that orders on the following is required and he has
requested the Government to issue orders there for:-
(I)
All the ‘authorities’ shall identify a nodal officer and inform their names to the Deputy Secretary, Municipal Administration
and Water Supply Department, Government of Tamil Nadu who shall be the state level nodal officer in this regard.
(II)
All the nodal officers shall send the progress and monitoring reports to the Deputy Secretary in the Format-A
attached herewith (in soft & hard forms) in the first week of January and first week of June every year.
(III) The Deputy Secretary shall consolidate the state level data in Format-B and submit the same to the Joint Secretary,
Ministry of New and Renewable Energy, Government of India in third week of January and third of week of June every year.
(IV).Time Line :
(a) Actions in item-I above shall be completed by all ‘authorities’ within one month from date of issue of this order.
(b) Actions on item-II above shall be start from the ensuing month of January or June whichever is earlier.
(c) Actions on item-III shall be taken by the state level nodal officer followed by the action on item-II.
5. The Government after careful examination have decided to accept the proposal of Commissioner of Municipal Administration.
They accordingly direct that:-
(i)
All the executive officers of the Urban Local Bodies  and the Zonal Assistant Commissioners of Corporation of
Chennai shall identify a nodal officer and inform their names to Commissioner of Municipal Administration/ Director of Town
Panchayats / Commissioner, Corporation of Chennai concerned.
