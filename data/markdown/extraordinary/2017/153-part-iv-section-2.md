The following Ordinance which was promulgated by the Governor on
the 31st May 2017 is hereby published for general information:-
TAMIL NADU ORDINANCE No. 3 OF 2017.
An Ordinance further to amend the Tamil Nadu  Agricultural Produce
Marketing (Regulation) Act, 1987.
WHEREAS the Legislative Assembly of the State is not in session and
the Governor of Tamil Nadu is  satisfied that circumstances exist which render
it necessary for him to take immediate action for the purpose hereinafter
appearing;
NOW, THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred by clause (1) of
Article 213 of the Constitution, the Governor hereby promulgates the following
Ordinance:-
1. (1) This Ordinance may be called the Tamil Nadu Agricultural Produce
Marketing (Regulation) Second Amendment Ordinance, 2017.
(2) It shall come into force at once.
2. In section 33 of the Tamil Nadu Agricultural  Produce Marketing (Regulation)
Act, 1987, in sub-section (1), in the proviso, for the expression “six years”,
the expression “seven years” shall be substituted.
31st May 2017.
CH. VIDYASAGAR RAO,

EXPLANATORY STATEMENT.
Sub-section (1) of section 33 of the Tamil Nadu Agricultural Produce
Marketing (Regulation) Act, 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 1989) empowers the
Government to appoint a Special Officer to manage the affairs of the market
committee pending the constitution of a new market committee for a period
not exceeding three years in the aggregate. By virtue of the said provision,
Special Officers were appointed to the 23 market committees to manage the
affairs of the said market committees and their term was extended by the
Tamil Nadu Act 8 of 2017 up to six years. The tenure of Special Officers
expires on 30th May 2017. As it will take some more time to nominate new
members to the market committees, there is a compelling necessity to extend
the term of the said Special Officers for a further period of one year beyond
30.05.2017 or till the reconstitution of the market committees, whichever is
earlier. The Government have, therefore, decided to amend the said Tamil Nadu
Act 27 of 1989 for the above purpose.
2. The Ordinance seeks to give effect to the above decision.
