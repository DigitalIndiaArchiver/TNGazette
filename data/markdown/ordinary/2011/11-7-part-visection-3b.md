(Rc. No. EIII(1)/50337/2010)
No. VI-3(b)/11/2011.
Under Sub-Statues (1) of Statute 5 of Chapter-XXI of the General Statutes of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University,
Chennai it is hereby published that the following member are elected to the Governing Council of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical
University, Chennai by the Senate from among its members under Clause (a) of sub-section (4) of Section 21 of the Tamil Nadu
Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai, Act, 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 37 of 1987) in the residual vacancies.
1.
Dr. T. SIVAKUMAR, M.Pharm., Ph.D.,
Principal,
Nandha College of Pharmacy,
Koorapalayam "Pirivu",
Pitchandampalayam Post,
Erode-638 052.
2.
Dr. R. MUTHUKRISHNAN, M.S., M.Ch.,
Professor and Head,
Dept. of Surgical Gastroenterology & Proctology,
Government Rajaji Hospital,
Madurai Medical College,
Madurai-625 020.
2. The tenure of the above members in the Governing Council of this University will be for the residual period from
3rd March 2011 to 22nd August 2011 or the date on which they cease to be a member of the Senate, whichever is earlier.
Chennai-600 032,
Dr. SUDHA SESHAYYAN,
9th March 2011.
