JUDICIAL NOTIFICATIONS
Shifting of Metropolitan Magistrate Courts and Offices from Egmore Court Campus to
Allikulam Commercial Complex, Moore Market, Chennai.
(Roc. No. 1166/2015/D)
No. VI(1)/202/2015.
As per the order of the Hon’ble High Court’s Official Memorandum in Roc. No. 9400/1974/D2,
Dated 13-03-2015 and 09-07-2015, the Metropolitan Magistrate Courts at Egmore are being shifted from Egmore
to Allikulam Commercial Complex, Moore Market, Chennai-3, from 15-07-2015 onwards in phased manner till
31-07-2015 for the purpose of construction of new buildings to be under taken in Egmore Court Complex.
The Metropolitan Magistrate Courts, Egmore will function in the Allikulam Commercial Complex, Moore Market,
Chennai-3, till completion of the new construction and further order of the Hon‘ble High Court.
Egmore, Chennai-600 008,
A. MURUGAN,
16th July 2015.
Chief Metropolitan Magistrate.

Procedure 1973 (Central Act 2 of 1974), the High Court hereby appoints the following 6 Deputy Tahsildars in Namakkal
District, 5 Deputy Tahsildars in Kanniyakumari District, 4 Deputy Tahsildars in Theni District, one
Tahsildar Trainee in Kancheepuram District and one Tahsildar Trainee in Coimbatore District, to undergo Magisterial
Training for a period of 120 I 180 days, from the date of  assumption of charge and confers upon them the ordinary
powers conferrable under this Code on a Judicial Magistrate to try such cases which were earlier triable and dealt with
by an erstwhile Judicial Second Class Magistrate.
