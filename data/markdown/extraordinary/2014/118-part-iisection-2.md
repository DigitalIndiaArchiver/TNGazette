No. II(2)/LE/350(a-1)/2014.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 6 of the Tamil Nadu Manual Workers (Regulation of
Employment and Conditions of Work) Act, 1982 (Tamil Nadu Act 33 of 1982), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the
following amendment to the Labour and Employment Department Notification No. II(2)/LE/453(e-1)/2006, published on
Pages 1 and 2 of Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 1st September 2006.
AMENDMENT
In the said Notification, in “THE TABLE”, in column (2), against   Serial Number 1 in column (1), for the expression “The
Tamil Nadu Auto Rickshaws and Taxi Drivers Welfare Board”, the expression “The Tamil Nadu Unorganised Drivers Welfare
Board” shall be substituted.
AMENDMENTS TO THE TAMIL NADU AUTO-RICKSHAWS AND TAXI DRIVERS SOCIAL SECURITY AND
WELFARE SCHEME, 2006.

No. II(2)/LE/350(a-2)/2014.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 4 of the Tamil Nadu Manual Workers (Regulation of Employment and
Conditions of Work) Act, 1982   (Tamil Nadu Act 33 of 1982), the Governor of   Tamil Nadu after consultation with Advisory
Committee, hereby makes the following amendments to the   Tamil Nadu Auto Rickshaws and Taxi Drivers Social Security and
Welfare  Scheme, 2006, published with the Labour and Employment Department Notification No.II(2)/LE/453(e-4)/2006 at
Pages 30-57 of Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 1st September 2006,
as  subsequently amended :-

2

AMENDMENTS
In the said Scheme,—
(1) in clause 1, in sub-clause (1), for the expression “Tamil Nadu Auto Rickshaws and Taxi Drivers Social Security and
Welfare Scheme”, the expression “Tamil Nadu Unorganised Drivers Social Security and Welfare Scheme” shall be substituted;
(2) for the expression “Tamil Nadu Auto Rickshaws and Taxi Drivers Welfare Board” wherever it occurs including the
Forms appended to the said Scheme, the expression “Tamil Nadu Unorganised Drivers Welfare Board” shall be substituted;
(3) for the expression “Tamil Nadu Auto Rickshaws and Taxi Drivers Social Security and Welfare Fund” wherever it
occurs, the expression “Tamil Nadu Unorganised Drivers Social Security and Welfare Fund” shall be substituted.
