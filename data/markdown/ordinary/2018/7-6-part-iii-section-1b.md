ANIMAL HUSBANDRY, DAIRYING AND FISHERIES DEPARTMENT
Amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Fisheries Service.
[G.O.Ms.No. 10, Animal Husbandry, Dairying and Fisheries (FS2), 24th January 2018,
Thai 11, Hevilambi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu 2049.]
No. SRO B-13/2018. In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor 
of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Fisheries Service (Section14 
in Volume II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual).
2 
(a) The amendments (i) hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 23rd February, 1985. 
 
(b) The amendment (ii) hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 2nd September 1978.
 
(c) The amendment (iii) hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 3rd June 2013. 
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules, in rule 4, in sub-rule (b), in ‘the TABLE,- (i) for the entries in items (1)(a)(i) and (2)(a) in column(3) 
against the category “5. Assistant Director of Fisheries” in column (1) and the entry “By Recruitment by transfer” in column(2) 
thereof, the following entries shall, respectively, be substituted, namely:-
 
“(1) (a) (i). Must possess a ﬁ rst or second class degree of B.Sc., in Zoology or Chemistry or Botany or Marine 
Biology or B.F.Sc., or’’,
 
“(2) (a) Must possess a ﬁ rst or second class degree of M.Sc., in Zoology or Chemistry or Marine Biology or M.F.Sc, 
and’’. 
 
 
 
(ii)  in the proviso to item (2)(b), in column (3) against the entry ‘By recruitment by transfer’ in column (2) 
and the category ‘5. Assistant Director of Fisheries’ in column (1) the word ‘or Curator’ shall be deleted. 
 
 
 
(iii) in the proviso to item 2 (b) in column (3) against the entry ‘By recruitment by transfer’ in column (2) 
and the category ‘5.Assistant Director of Fisheries’ in column (1) the word ‘or Research Assistant’ shall be deleted. 
K.GOPAL,
Principal Secretary to Government.
