DATE OF BRINGING INTO FORCE OF THE REGISTRATION (TAMIL NADU AMENDMENT) ACT, 2021
(TAMIL NADU ACT 24 OF 2022).
[G.O. Ms. No.130, Commercial Taxes and Registration (J2), 12th September 2022,

No. II(2)/CTR/735(a-1)/2022.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (3) of section 1 of the Registration (Tamil Nadu Amendment) Act, 2021 
(Tamil Nadu Act 24 of 2022), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the 12th September, 2022 as the date on which 
the said Act shall come into force.
