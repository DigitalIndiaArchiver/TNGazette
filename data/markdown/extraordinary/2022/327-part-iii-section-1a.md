In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of section 45 of the Tamil Nadu Regulation of Rights and Responsibilities 
of Landlords and Tenants Act, 2017 (Tamil Nadu Act 42 of 2017), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following 
amendments to the Tamil Nadu Regulation of Rights and Responsibilities of Landlords and Tenants Rules, 2019, namely:—
Amendments.
(1) In the said Rules, –
	
(1) for rule 6, the following rule shall be substituted, namely:
	
“6. Intimation of sub-tenancy. – Every sub-tenancy shall be informed to the Rent Authority as required in 
sub-section (2) of section 7 of the Act, jointly by the landlord and tenant, in Form III.” ;
	
	
(2) in rule 8, in sub-rule (1) for the expression “under sections 10, 14, 15 and 20”, the expression “under sections 
10, 14, 15, 19 and 20” shall be substituted;
	
	
(3) in rule 10, after the expression “sub-section (2) of section 21”, the expression “or sub-section (1) of section 22” 
shall be inserted;
	
	
(4) for Form III, the following Form shall be substituted, namely:-
