University, Chennai, it is hereby published that the following members have been elected to the Senate of the Tamil Nadu 
Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai, by the teachers of each affiliated Medical College from among themselves Under 
Section 18 (3) (a) of the Tamil Nadu Dr.M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai Act, 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 37 of 1987). 
The membership will be for the period as noted against each or till they hold the post of Professor of the concerned 
affiliated ColIege of this University, or till the date of their superannuation whichever is earlier.
