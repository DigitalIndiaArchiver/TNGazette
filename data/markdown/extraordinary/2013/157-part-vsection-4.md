 NOTIFICATIONS BY THE ELECTION COMMISSION OF INDIA
APPOINTMENT OF DATES FOR VARIOUS STAGES AND FIXATION OF HOURS OF POLL FOR BIENNIAL ELECTIONS
TO COUNCIL OF STATES TO FILL VACANCIES OF MEMBERS RETIRING  DURING JULY, 2013.
No. SRO G-11(a-1)/2013
The following Notification of the Election Commission of India,  New Delhi, dated 10th June, 2013 [Jaistha 20, 1935
(Saka)] is republished:—
No. 318/CS-TN/2013(1).—Whereas, the President of India has, by notification issued under Section 12 of the
Representation of the People Act, 1951 (43 of 1951) and published in the Gazette of India on 10th June, 2013, been pleased
to call upon the elected members of the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu to elect six members to the Council
of States;
Now, therefore, in pursuance of sub-section (1) of Section 39 and Section 56 of the said Act,
the Election Commission of India hereby—
(A) appoints, with respect to the said election-
(a) the 17th June, 2013 (Monday), as the last date for making nominations;
(b) the 18th June, 2013 (Tuesday), as the date for the scrutiny of the nominations;
(c) the 20th June, 2013 (Thursday), as the last date for the withdrawal of candidatures;
(d) the 27th June, 2013 (Thursday), as the date on which a poll shall, if necessary, be taken; and
(e) the 29th June, 2013 (Saturday), as the date before which the election shall be completed; and
(B)  fixes the hours from 9.00 A.M. to 4.00 P.M., as the hours during which the poll shall, if necessary, be taken on
the date specified above for the election.
(By Order)
