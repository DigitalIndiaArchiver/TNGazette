(Roc. No. 12515/2009/DP2.)
No. VI(1)/252/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section 2 of Section 33  of Town and Country Planning Act, 1971,
(Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972), the Director of Town and Country Planning, Chennai hereby confirms the following
conversion of Cinema Theatre use into Residential use in S.No. 666/1Apt. (Ward C, Block No. 16, T.S.No. 100/2) to the
approved Extension of Thiruthangal Road, Pallipatti Road North Detailed Development Plan of Sivakasi Local Planning
Area. The said notification was published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette (No. 28), Part VI—Section 1, page 222,
dated 16th July 2008.
Since no objections and suggestions have been received on this draft notification within the stipulated time, the same
are hereby confirmed and ordered as below:—
CONFIRMATION OF VARIATION
1. Wherever the expression Map No. 5 DDP (RK) No. 10/87 occurs the expression DDP(V)/DTCP No. 14/2008
shall be added at the end to be read with.
2. In Schedule IV, Form No. 7, against in Sl.No. 2, the entries shall be deleted and the following entries
shall be substituted:—
 Land bounded on North by approved layout plots, east by R.S. No. 666/1B, south by R.S.No. 666/1Apt.,
west by D8D8 Road in comprising S.No. 666/1Apt.

the figure “0.51” shall be substituted.
