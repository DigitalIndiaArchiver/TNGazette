ENERGY DEPARTMENT
APPOINTMENT  OF  MEMBER  (LEGAL)  IN  THE  TAMIL  NADU  ELECTRICITY  REGULATORY 
COMMISSION  UNDER  THE  ELECTRICITY  ACT,  2003.
[G.O. Ms. No. 5, Energy (D1), 6th January 2023, மார்கழி 22, சுபகிருது, திருவள்ளுவர் ஆண்டு-2053.] 
No. II(2)/EGY/19(d)/2023.
Under sub-section (5) of Section 82 of the Electricity Act, 2003 (Central Act 36 of 2003), on the recommendation 
of the Selection Committee referred to in Section 85 of the said Act, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints 
Thiru. B. Mohan as Member (Legal) of the Tamil Nadu Electricity Regulatory Commission, Chennai.
	
RAMESH CHAND MEENA,
