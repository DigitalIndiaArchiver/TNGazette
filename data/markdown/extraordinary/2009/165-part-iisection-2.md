[G.O. Ms. No.71 Industries (MIE 2), 7th July 2009.]
No. II(2)/IND/323(e)/2009.
The Government of Tamil  Nadu have been satisfied that
the lands specified in the schedule below have to be
acquired for industrial purposes for setting up of Multi Product
Special Economic Zone, Perambalur and it has already
been decided that the entire amount of compensation to be
awarded for the lands is to be paid out of the funds controlled
or managed by Tamil Nadu Industrial Development
Corporation Limited.  The following notice is issued under
sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of
Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10
of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997(Tamil
Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
acquires the lands specified in the schedule below and
measuring 5.67.5 hectares / 13.98 acres  to the same, a
little more or less needed for   Industrial purposes, i.e. Multi
Product Special Economic Zone, Perambalur.
The plan of the lands are kept in the Office of the  District
Collector, Perambalur and may be inspected at any time during
office hours.
