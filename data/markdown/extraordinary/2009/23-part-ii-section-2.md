DEATH OF THIRU R. VENKATARAMAN, FORMER PRESIDENT OF INDIA.
[G.O. Ms. No. 93, Public (Protocol-1), 27th January 2009.]
No. II(2)/PUPRO/32(e)/2009.
The Government of Tamil Nadu announce with profound regret the death of Thiru R. Venkataraman,
Former President of India on 27th January 2009. State mourning will be observed for seven days
from 27th January 2009 to 2nd February 2009  (both days inclusive). The National Flag will be flown at half
mast during the period of mourning throughout the State. There will be no official entertainment during this
period.
(By Order of the Governor.)
K.S. SRIPATHI,
Chief Secretary.
