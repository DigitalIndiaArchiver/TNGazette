HIGHWAYS AND MINOR PORTS DEPARTMENT
ACQUISITION OF LANDS
[G.O. Ms. No. 226, Highways and Minor Ports (HQ2),
5th July 2010.]
No. II(2)/HWMP/398(e-1)/2010.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied
that the lands specified in the schedule below have to be
acquired for Highways purpose, to wit, for the formation of
I.T. Corridor Express Way (i.e.) Phase II to extend to six
lane of Old Mahabalipuram Road and it have already been
decided that the entire amount of compensation to be
awarded for the lands is to be paid out of the funds controlled
or managed by the Chief Engineer (General), Highways
Department and having considered the cause shown by the
owners or other person having interest in the said  lands
as the case may be do hereby published the following
Notice under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil
Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu
Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands
specified in the schedule below and measuring an extent of
27679 Square metres to the same, a little more or less are
needed for Highways purpose, to wit, for the formation of
I.T. Corridor Express Way (i.e.) Phase II to extend to six
lane of Old Mahabalipuram Road.
The plan of the lands are kept in the office of the Special
Tahsildar, Land Acquisition, Radial Road Scheme, Tambaram
and may be inspected at any time during office hours.

Thiruvarur Road and it having already been decided that the
entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands is

Divisional Engineer (Highways), Tamil Nadu Road Sector
Project, Kumbakonam after considering the cause shown by
the owner or other person having interest on such land as the
case may be the following notice is issued under sub-section
(1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001
(Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu
Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002), the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands specified in the
schedule below  and measuring 0.02.21 hectares to the same,
a little more or less needed for Highways purpose, to wit, for
widening of Kumbakonam to Thiruvarur Road.
The plan of the lands is kept in the office of the Special
Tahsildar (Land Acquisition), Tamil Nadu Road Sector Project,
Thanjavur, Thanjavur District and may be inspected at any
