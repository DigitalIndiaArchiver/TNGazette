WHEREAS in supersession of G.O. first cited, Orders have issued in the order second cited on effective and immediate 
measures need to be continued to prevent and contain the spread COVID-19 applicable through the State with effect from 
25.3.2020 which came into force for a period of 21 days with effect from 25.3.2020.
WHEREAS the Principal Secretary/Commissioner of Revenue Administration in the letter fourth cited has stated in 
continuation of the above, the Government of India has issued notification in the reference third cited and the above addendum 
has been got reconciled with the G.O. 2nd cited and sent the amendment proposal to the G.O. 2nd cited.
NOW THEREFORE the State Government of Tamil Nadu based on the proposal of Principal Secretary and Commissioner 
of Revenue Administration and issues following amendment to G.O.(Ms).No. 172 Revenue and D.M. (DM.II)  Department, 
dated: 25.3.2020.
A.	 Against Clause 2 the sub-clauses (h) & (i) under exceptions may be added:
	
(h) Agencies engaged in procurement of agriculture products, including MSP operations.
	
(i) “Mandis” operated by the Agriculture Produce Market Committee. 
B.	  Against Clause (4); Sub-Clause (a) in exceptions it may be  read as follows:
a)	
Shops including rations shops (under PDS and FCI operations) dealing with food, grozeries, fruits and vegetables, 
diary and milk booths, meat and fish, animal fodder and includes shops for seeds and pesticides and fertilizers. 
However district authorities may encourage and facilitate home delivery to minimize the movement of individuals 
outside their homes.
C. Against Clause 4, the following two sub-clauses may be added under exceptions;
	
   (k) 	 Farming operations by Farmers and Farm workers in the field.
	
   (l) 	 Custom Hiring Centres (CHC)’ related to farm machinery.
D.  Against Clause 5 the following exception sub-clause may be added: 
       (e)	 Manufacturing and packaging units of Fertilizers, Pesticides and Seeds.
E.  Against Clause 6, the following exception sub-clause as (f)  may be added: 
	
   (f) Intra and inter-state movement of harvesting and slowing related machines like combined harvester and other 
agriculture/horticulture implements.  	
	
(By order of the Governor)
