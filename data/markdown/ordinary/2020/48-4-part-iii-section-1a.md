 SOCIAL WELFARE AND NUTRITIOUS MEAL PROGRAMME DEPARTMENT
Amendments to the Tamil Nadu Maintenance and Welfare of Parents and Senior Citizens Rules, 2009.
[G.O. Ms. No. 39, Social Welfare and Nutritious Meal Programme [SW6(1)], 
6th November 2020, Aippasi 21, Saarvari, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2051.]
No.SRO A-40/2020.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 32 of the Maintenance and Welfare of Parents and 
Senior Citizens Act, 2007 (Central Act 56 of 2007), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to 
the Tamil Nadu Maintenance and Welfare of Parents and Senior Citizens Rules, 2009.
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖගඛ
In the said Rules, in the Schedule:-
 
(i) for the expression "[See Rule (16)]", the expression "[See rule 19]" shall be substituted;
 
(ii) under the sub-heading "Physical Facilities’’, in serial No.3, after item (viii). the item ''(ix) a Register/Suggestion 
Box or Complaint Box" shall be inserted.
 
S. MADUMATHI,
 
Secretary to Government.
