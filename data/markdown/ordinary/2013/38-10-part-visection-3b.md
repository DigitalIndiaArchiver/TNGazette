ELECTION TO THE AUTHORITIES OF THE UNIVERSITY
Senate and Academic Council Election 2013.
(Ref. No. ME-2/S/Ele./PU/2013)
No.VI-3(b)/29/2013.
It is hereby notified that election will be held to elect one Member from each of the following revenue districts to the
Senate by the Chairmen of Municipal Councils and of Panchayat Union Councils from among such Chairmen and the
Members of the Municipal Councils, Panchayat Union Councils and Panchayats in the District concerned under Section
15 (a) – Class III – Other Members (6) of the Madurai Kamaraj University Act, 1965:—
1. Madurai District
2. Dindigul District
3. Theni District
4. Virudhunagar District
The last date and hour for receipt of nomination is fixed as 4th October. 2013 at 4.00 P.M..
The Collectors of the Districts concerned are requested to hold the election and communicate the result by
21st October 2013.
Palkalai Nagar,
Dr. S.V. HARIHARAN,
Madurai-625 021,
Registrar (i/c).
19th September 2013.
(Ref. No. ME-2/S and /AC/HM/Ele./2013.)
No.VI-3(b)/30/2013.
It is hereby notified that election will be held to elect one Member each to the SENATE and ACADEMIC COUNCIL. From
each of the following revenue districts by the Headmasters of  High Schools/Higher Secondary Schools from among
themselves in the following Districts in the University area under Section  15 (a) – Class III – Other Members (5) and Section
24 (a) – Class II – Other Members (6) of the Madurai Kamaraj University Act, 1965:—
1. Madurai District
2. Dindigul District
3. Virudhunagar District
4. Theni District
The last date and hour for receipt of nomination is fixed as 4th October. 2013 at 4.00 P.M.
The Director of School Education or his nominee is requested to hold the election and communicate the result by
21st October 2013.
Palkalai Nagar,
Dr. S.V. HARIHARAN,
Madurai-625 021,
Registrar (i/c).

THIRUVALLUVAR UNIVERSITY, SERKKADU, VELLORE.
Elections to the Standing Committee of the Academic Council
[No. TV/R/Stan.Com.election Notification, Publication/2013/7076.]
No. VI-3(b)/31/2013.
 It is hereby notified that the elections to the STANDING COMMITTEE OF THE  ACADEMIC COUNCIL
will be held under section 51 read along with section 61(3) of the Thiruvalluvar University Act, 2002
and Chapters XVI and XIX of the Laws of the University of Madras (Statutes, Ordinances and Regulations) (Volume I).
THE STANDING COMMITTEE OF THE ACADEMIC COUNCIL
TWO MEMBERS
To be elected by the Academic Council to the Standing Committee of the Academic Council from among the
members of the Syndicate.
TWO MEMBERS
To be elected to the Standing Committee of the Academic Council from among the Principals  or Teachers of affiliated
colleges who are members of the Academic Council.
The newly elected members will hold office as a member of the Standing Committee  of the Academic Council for a period of
three years from the date of election or from the date of declaration of results.
The election will be conducted according to the Statutes contained in Chapter XIX,  Volume I, Madras University, Calendar
2001 read with the provisions of the Thiruvalluvar University Act, 2002.
Nomination forms are available in the Registrar’s Office. Nomination should be sent  so as to be received by the Registrar,
Thiruvalluvar University, Serkkadu, Vellore-632 115,  not later than Tuesday. 1.10.2013 upto 4.00 P.M. The scrutiny of nominations
will take place on the same day, i.e. 1.10.2013 at 4.15 P .M. in the Registrar’s Office, at Thiruvalluvar University.
Nomination Paper shall be enclosed in an envolope addressed to the Register and superscribed “NOMINATION TO
THE STANDING COMMITTEE OF THE ACADEMIC COUNCIL [FROM AMONG THE MEMBERS OF THE SYNDICATE] /
NOMINATION TO THE STANDING COMMITTEE OF THE ACADEMIC COUNCIL [FROM AMONG THE PRINCIPALS OR TEACHERS
OF AFFILIATED COLLEGES (who are members of the Academic  Council)] BY THE ACADEMIC COUNCIL”, as the case may be,
as indicated below and sent by Registered Post [Ack. Due], so as to be received by the Registrar within the date and hour
fixed, or delivered to the Registrar either in person or by messenger. Nomination papers  that are not enclosed in an envelope
superscribed and sent by Registered Post or are not  delivered in person or by messenger as required above shall be declared
invalid. An elector shall be eligible to subscribe either as proposer or as seconder in nomination papers only as
many time as there are vacancies but not more.
