The Governor of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that the
lands specified in the Schedule below have to be acquired for
Industrial Purpose, to wit for the formation of ULTRA MEGA
POWER PROJECT, CHEYYUR and it has already been decided
that the funds controlled or managed by the Officer Authorised
Signatory.  Coastal Tamil Nadu Power Ltd., New Delhi and
having considered  the cause shown by the owners or other
persons having interest the said lands, the Joint Commissioner
(Land Reforms) concerned, the interested party for the lands
covered under Tamil Nadu Land Reforms (fixation of ceiling on
land) Act, 1961, as the case may be, do here publish the
following notice under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the
Tamil Nadu  Acquisition of Land  for Industrial Purposes
Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu  Act 10 of 1999), by relaxing
G.O (Ms.) No. 2954, Revenue, dated 29-5-1973.
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Acquisition of Land  for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997
(Tamil Nadu  Act 10 of 1999) the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby acquired the lands specified in the Schedule below and
measuring and extent of  4.34.0 Hectares to the same, a
little more or less are needed for industrial Purposes, to
wit, for the formation of ULTRA MEGA POWER PROJECT,
54.  CHITHARKADU VILLAGE, CHEYYUR TALUK.
The Plan of the lands are kept in the office of the Special
Tahsildar, Land Acquisition, Unit-II,  ULTRA MEGA POWER
PROJECT Scheme, Cheyyur and may be inspected at any time
during office hours.
