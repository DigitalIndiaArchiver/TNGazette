Under the powers conferred on the Registrar of Industrial co-operatives under Section 137(1) of the Tamil Nadu
Co-operatives Act 1983 (Act 30 of 1983) The Activities of the Thalavadi Adi-Dravidar Coir Workers Industrial Co-operative

Erode, Chennai is appointed as Official Liquidator of the said Society for the purpose of Liquidation.
