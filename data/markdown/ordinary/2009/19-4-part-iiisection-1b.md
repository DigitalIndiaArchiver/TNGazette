Amendments to Special Rules for Tamil Nadu Police Subordinate Service
[G.O.  Ms.  No. 324, Home (Police-VI), 21st April 2009, CˆF¬ó 8, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. SRO B-33/2009.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Sections 8 and 10 of the Tamil Nadu District Police Act, 1859
(Central Act XXIV of 1859) and Sections 9 and 11 of the Chennai City Police Act, 1888 (Tamil Nadu Act III of 1888) read with
the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments
to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Police Subordinate Service (Section 34 in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Services
Manual, 1946).
    2. The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 30th August 2006.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules, in rule 3,—
(i) sub-rule (c) shall be omitted;
(ii)  in sub-rule (d),—
(a)    Clause (ii) shall be omitted;
(b) in clause (iii), for the paragraph commencing with words "The Superintendents of Police"
and ending with the words "final orders", the following  paragraph shall be substituted, namely:—
"In respect of districts, the Superintendents of Police concerned shall submit the list of eligible
Head Constables to the Range Deputy Inspector General of Police. In respect of Chennai City, the
Joint Commissioner of Police shall submit the list of eligible Head Constables to the Commissioner of
Police. In respect of other cities, the Deputy Commissioner of Police (Head Quarters), who is incharge
of administration shall submit the list of eligible Head Constables to the Commissioner of Police. The
Range Promotion Board shall, after scrutiny of the list, submit its recommendation based  upon seniority
and service records to the Deputy Inspector General of Police or Commissioner of Police concerned, who
shall decide and approve the numbers and names of Head Constables or Head Constables (Armed
Reserve) to be included in the select list as per estimated vacancies. The Range Promotion Board shall,
thereafter select the person for training prescribed in rule 18 in accordance with seniority and service records
and submit the list to the Deputy Inspector General of Police or Commissioner of Police concerned for
approval and final orders".
         S. MALATHI,
Principal Secretary to Government.
