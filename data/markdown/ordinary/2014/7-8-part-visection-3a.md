GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI-600 001.
Taking over the Direct Management of G. Tummalapatti Mohideen Pallivasal Inam Wakf (G.S. No. 248),
Nilakottai, Dindugal District.
(Lr. Rc. No. 11196/12/B8/DGL)
No. VI-3(a)/19/2014.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act 1995 as amended by
Act 27 of 2013,  the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in Item No. 51/13 in Rc. No. 11196/12/B8/DGL, dated
12th September 2013 has ordered to take over of G. Tummalapatti Mohideen Pallivasal Inam Wakf (G.S. No. 248)
under the Direct Control of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of one year from the date of Publication in the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette and to appoint Superintendent of Wakfs, Madurai as its Executive Officer.
F. ABDUL RAZICK,
Chennai-600 001,
Chief Executive Officer,
31st January 2014.
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
Framing Scheme in respect of  Rawlathul Janna Pallivasal Wakf, Keel Madurai Town and District.
(Rc. No. 9589/10/B8/MDU)
No. VI-3(a)/20/2014.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 69 of the Wakf Act 1995, the
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in item No. 19/12 & 156/13 in R.C. No. 9589/10/B8/MDU, dated
19th November 2013 has framed a Scheme for the Wakf viz., Keel Madurai Rawlathul Janna Pallivasal Wakf, Madurai District
for better administration of the Wakf.
F. ABDUL RAZICK,
Chennai-600 001,
Chief Executive Officer,
10th January 2014.
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
Taking over the Direct Management Kabrasthan Thoppu Inam Wakf, Villupuram District.
(Rc. No. 988/13/B4/Villupuram)
No. VI-3(a)/21/2014.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act 1995 as amended by
Act 27 of 2013,  the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in Item No. 16/13, dated 30th December 2013 in
Rc.No. 988/13/B4/Villupuram has ordered to take over the management of Religious Endowment Wakf alias Kabrasthan
Thoppu Inam Wakf, Villupuram District  under the direct control of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of one year
from the date of Publication of the Notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette and to appoint the
Wakf Superintendent, Villupuram as its Executive Officer.
F. ABDUL RAZICK,
Chennai-600 001,
Chief Executive Officer,
28th January 2014.
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
