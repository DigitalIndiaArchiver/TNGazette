by the Divisional Engineer (Highways), Tirunelveli. The following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the 
Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE

of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands speciﬁed in the schedule below and measuring an extent of 23080 Sq.mtrs of Wet 
and Dry lands to the same a little more or less are needed for construction of Tenkasi By-pass Road in Tenkasi Town of 
Tenkasi District and Taluk.
