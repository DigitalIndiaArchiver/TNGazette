HIGHER EDUCATION DEPARTMENT
DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF THE TAMIL NADU UNIVERSITY LAWS
(AMENDMENT AND REPEAL) ACT, 2011.  (TAMIL NADU ACT 20 OF 2011).
[G.O. Ms. No. 144, Higher Education (I1), 23rd July 2012,  Aadi 8, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2043.]
No. II(2)/HE/431(i)/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 1  of the Tamil Nadu
University Laws (Amendment and Repeal) Act, 2011 (Tamil Nadu Act 20 of 2011), the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the 1st August 2012, as the date on which the said Act
shall come into force.
T.S. SRIDHAR,
Additional Chief Secretary to Government.
