AND WHEREAS, now the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission has come to a stage of withdrawing the Statutory Order
No.46/2013/TNSEC/PE-2, dated 19-12-2013 which withholds the Statutory Order No.39/2013/TNSEC/PE-2, dated
7-10-2013 disqualifying the candidates who contested for elections in Rural Local Bodies in Tiruvallur District and not submitted
their election expenses accounts.
Now, THEREFORE, taking into consideration of the above facts, Tamil Nadu State Election Commission, in exercise of the
powers conferred under Article 243-K of Constitution of India and sub-section (4) of section 37 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats
Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994), read with sub-rule (13) of rule 120 and sub-rule (1) of rule 143 of the Tamil Nadu
Panchayats (Elections) Rules, 1995, hereby orders that the S.O. No. 46/2013/TNSEC/PE-2, dated 19-12-2013 is revoked
except in cases of the candidates whose names are mentioned against the SI.Nos. 1, 248, 658 and 754 of S.O.
No.39/2013 TNSEC/PE-2, dated 7-10-2013 and the disqualification ordered in respect of all other candidates of Rural Local
Bodies in Tiruvallur District issued in its Statutory Order first read above holds good.

AND WHEREAS, now the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission has come to a stage of withdrawing the Statutory Order
No. 46/2013/TNSEC/PE2, dt.19-12-13 which withholds the Statutory Order No.35/2013/TNSEC/ME2, dt. 4-10-13 disqualifying
the candidates who contested for elections in urban local bodies in Tiruvallur District and not submitted their election
expenses accounts;
NOW, THEREFORE, taking into consideration of the above facts, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission decided to hold
a detailed enquiry with regard disqualification of the candidates mentioned in the Table shown above and the candidate
Tmt. S. Govindhammal, No.3, Paul Mohandoss Street, Poonamallee Municipality, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission
in exercise of the powers conferred under Article 243-ZA of the Constitution of India and sub-rule (13) of rule 116 and rule 139
of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities, Municipalities and Corporation (Elections) Rules, 2006,
pending enquiry with regard to candidates mentioned above hereby orders that the Statutory Order No.46/2013/TNSEC/PE2,
dt.19-12-13 is revoked except in cases of the candidates whose names are mentioned against the SI.Nos. 2, 4, 7, 35, 37, 49,
50, 145, 163, 210, 268, 388, 413 and 426 of Statutory Order No.35/2013/TNSEC/ME2, dt. 4-10-2013 and the disqualification

holds good.
