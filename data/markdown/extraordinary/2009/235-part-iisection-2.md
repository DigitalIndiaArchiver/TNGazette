HIGHER EDUCATION DEPARTMENT
APPOINTMENT OF CERTAIN PERSON AS  VICE-CHANCELLOR OF THIRUVALLUVAR UNIVERSITY,
VELLORE FOR CERTAIN PERIOD UNDER THIRUVALLUVAR UNIVERSITY ACT.
[G.O. (1D) No. 210, Higher Education (K2), 3rd September 2009, Aavani 18, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. II(2)/HE/454(a)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred under Sections 12 (1) and (3) of the Thiruvalluvar University  Act, 2002, His
Excellency the Governor-Chancellor hereby appoints Dr. A. Jothi Murugan, Joint Director of Collegiate Education,
Chennai Region (Retired) as Vice-Chancellor, Thiruvalluvar University, Vellore for a period of three years from the date of  his
assuming office.
K. GANESAN,
 Principal Secretary to Government.
