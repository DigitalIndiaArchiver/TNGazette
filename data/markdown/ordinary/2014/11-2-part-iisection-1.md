Proforma 10-01
PUBLIC NOTIFICATION
No. II(1)/TDRE/7/2014.
Notice is hereby given to all users of Railway lines and premises situated on the completed section of the Coimbatore North
Jn - Mettupalaiyam section of the Southern Railway that the 25,000 Volt 50Hz., ac. Overhead Traction wires will be energized
on or after 31-03-2014. On and from the same date the Overhead Traction line shall be treated as ‘LIVE’ at all t imes and no
unauthorized person shall approach or work in the proximity of the said overhead lines.
Egmore, Chennai. 600 008,
M. PRABHAKARAN,
16th January 2014.
For Chief Project Manager,

Introduction of ac 25kV Traction
Proforma 10-02
"WARNING TO ROAD USERS"
No. II(1)/TDRE/8/2014.
It is notified for information of the public that in connection with introduction of 25kV ac electric traction over the Coimbatore
North Jn - Mettupalaiyam section of Southern Railway on and from 31-03-2014 height gauges have been erected at all the level
crossings with clear height of 4.67m, above road level with a view to prevent loads of excessive height from coming into contact
or dangerous proximity to “LIVE” Traction Wires.


TAMIL NADU GOVERNMENT GAZETTE

[Part II—Sec.1
Public are hereby, notified to observe the height specified above for the purpose of loading Vehicles and to see that the loads
carried in road vehicles do not infringe the height gauges under any circumstances.
The Dangers of a load of excessive height are as follows:—
(i)
Danger to the height gauge and consequent obstruction to the Road as well as the Railway line.
(ii)
Danger to the materials or equipments carried or the vehicle itself.
(iii)
Danger of fire and risk of life due to contact with or dangerous proximity to the conductor.
