In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (3) of Section 1 of the Industrial Disputes
(Tamil Nadu Amendment) Act, 2013 (Tamil Nadu Act 7 of 2015), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
appoints the 23-09-2015 as the date on which the said Act shall come into force.
