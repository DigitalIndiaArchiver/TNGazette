TAMIL NADU STATE ELECTION COMMISSION, CHENNAI - 600 106.
[ELECTIONS – URBAN LOCAL BODIES - INDIRECT ELECTIONS – ORDINARY AND CASUAL VACANCIES IN THE 
OFFICE OF THE  CHAIRMAN, VICE CHAIRMAN AND MEMBERS OF TAXATION APPEALS COMMITTEE, MEMBERS 
OF APPOINTMENT COMMITTEE AND MEMBERS OF CONTRACT COMMITTEE - CONDUCT OF - NOTIFIED.]
(S.O.No. 34/2022/TNSEC/ME-2, dated the 11th May 2022.)
No. VI(2)/46(a)/2022.
In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (1) of Section 43-B and Section 27 of the 
Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920) and sub-rule (3) and (4) of rule 99 of the 
Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Municipalities and Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006, the Tamil Nadu State 
Election Commission hereby fixes 25.05.2022 as the date for convening meetings to elect the Chairman, 
Vice Chairman, as the case may be, and 26.05.2022 as the date for convening meetings to elect the Members 
of Taxation Appeals Committee, Members of Appointment Committee and Members of Contract Committee, as the 
case may be, shown in the Annexure, in accordance with the following programme:-
1.
Last date for service of notice for the meetings of election of Chairman, 
Vice Chairman and Members of Taxation Appeals Committee, Members 
of Appointment Committee and Members of Contract Committee, as the 
case may be.
 16.05.2022
2.
Statutory interval
17.05.2022 
to
   24.05.2022
3.
Date for conduct of meeting for the election of Chairman, Vice Chairman, 
as the case may be
25.05.2022
4.
Date for conduct of meeting for the election of Members of Taxation 
Appeals Committee, Members of Appointment Committee and Members 
of Contract Committee, as the case may be.
26.05.2022
