ACQUISITION OF LANDS
(Roc. CPRR/CLA/15(1)/Section 1/1/2020)
NOTIFICATION UNDER SUB-SECTION(1) OF SECTION 15 OF THE TAMIL NADU HIGHWAYS ACT,2001(TAMIL NADU 
ACT 34 OF 2002) READ WITH TAMIL NADU LAND ACQUISITION LAWS(REVIVAL OF OPERATION, AMENDEMENT 
AND VALIDATION) ACT, 2019
No. VI(1)/5(a)/2021.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having  been satisﬁ ed that the  Wet & Dry lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below 
and measuring to an extent of  30811 Sq.metres under Chennai Peripheral Ring Road Project for Highways purpose 
for laying of Chennai Peripheral Ring Road, Ennore at  KM 21/100 to KM  21/577 No.106 Jaganathapuram I  Village, 
Ponneri Taluk, Tiruvallur District,  and it having already been decided that the entire amount of compensation to be 
awarded for the lands to be paid out of the funds  controlled and  managed by the Divisional Engineer -III, Tamil Nadu 
Road Infrastructure Development Corporation, Chennai-600 002. Tamil Nadu  after having considering the cause shown 
by the owner or other persons having interest on such lands as the case may be do hereby publish the following notice 
is issued under Sub-Section (1) of section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (TN Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
Under Sub-Section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) Read with 
Tamil Nadu Land Acquisition Laws(Revival of Operation, Amendment and Validation) Act, 2019,the Governor of Tamil Nadu 
hereby notiﬁ es that the  Wet & Dry  lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below and measuring to an extent 30811 Sq.metres of 
lands are acquired in No.106, Jaganthapuram-I Village in Ponneri Taluk, Tiruvallur District,  to the same, a little more or less 
needed for Chennai  Peripheral Ring Road Project for State Highways purpose. for laying of Chennai Peripheral Ring Road, 
Ennore.  KM  21/100 to KM  21/577 The plan of the lands under acquisition are kept in the oﬃ  ce of the Special District 
Revenue oﬃ  cer (Land Acquisition), Tamil Nadu Road Sector Project –II, Guindy, Chennai -32 and oﬃ  ce of the Special Tahsildar 
(Land Acquisition), Chennai Peripheral Ring Road Project, Unit I, Ponneri in Tiruvallur District   which may be inspected at 
any time during the oﬃ  ce hours.
