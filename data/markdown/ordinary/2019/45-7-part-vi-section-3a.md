Amendment to Tamil Nadu State Waqf Board Service Regulations, 1971
(RC.No. 786/E1/2018)
No.VI-3(a)/210/2019.
The following amendment to regulation 4 of the Tamil Nadu State Waqf Board Service Regulations, 1971 
approved in G.O.MS.No. 65, Backward Classes, Most Backward Classes and Minorities Welfare (S2) Department 
Dated: 11-10-2019.
Amendment
In the Tamil Nadu State Waqf Board Service Regulations, 1971, in regulation 4, in the Table, after the entries 
’’A degree of any recognized University" in column (2) against the entry "6. Junior Assistant" in column (1), thereof, 
the entry "Preference shall be given to those who acquired a degree in Law" shall be added.
