HOME, PROHIBITION AND EXCISE DEPARTMENT
AWARD OF “GANDHI ADIGAL POLICE MEDAL” FOR THE YEAR 2015 TO POLICE PERSONNEL FOR THEIR
OUTSTANDING WORK IN CURBING ILLICIT LIQUOR  ON THE OCCASION OF REPUBLIC DAY 2016.
[G.O. Ms. No. 28, Home, Prohibition and Excise (P&E (VII), 1st October 2015, Purattasi 14, Manmadha,
Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2046.]
No. II(2)/HOPE/573(b)/2015.
The following members of the Tamil Nadu State Police Service and the Tamil Nadu State Police Subordinate Service have been
selected to receive the award of “Gandhi Adigal Police Medal” for the year 2015  on the occasion of Republic day, 2016 for
their outstanding work in curbing illicit liquor. They would also be paid a cash prize of Rs.20,000/- (Rupees Twenty Thousand only)
each:-
(i)
Thiru. K. Rajendhran,
Additional Superintendent of Police,
Prohibition Enforcement Wing,
Thanjavur District.
(ii)
Thiru. S. Ramamurthy,
Special Sub-Inspector of Police,
Pudupattinam Police Station, OD @ SB
Nagapattinam District.
(iii)
Thiru. M. Raju,
Head Constable  1834,
Eriyur Police Station,
OD @ Prohibition Enforcement Wing Special  Party,
Dharmapuri District.
 APURVA VARMA,
Principal Secretary to Government.
