NOTIFICATIONS UNDER THE TAMIL NADU CIVIL

CONSTITUTION OF SUB COURT AT JAYANKONDAM IN
ARIYALUR DISTRICT.
[G.O.Ms.No. 351, Home (Cts-III), 11th April 2017.]
No. II(2)/HO/230(f-1)/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred by the first paragraph
of Section 4 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central
Act III of 1873), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby alters
the number of Subordinate Judges to be appointed under
the said Act for Ariyalur District as Four with effect from the
date on which the Subordinate Judge assumes charge of
the Subordinate Judge's Court at Jayankondam.
NOTIFICATION - II
[G.O.Ms.No. 351, Home (Cts-III), 11th April 2017.]
No. II(2)/HO/230(f-2)/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred by the second
paragraph of Section 4 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act,
1873,  (Central Act III of 1873), the Governor of Tamil Nadu,
after consultation with the High Court, Madras hereby fixes
the number of Subordinate Judges to be appointed to
Subordinate Judge's Court at Jayankondam in Ariyalur
District as One with effect from the date on which the
Subordinate Judge assumes charge of that court.

NOTIFICATION - III
[G.O.Ms.No. 351, Home (Cts-III), 11th April 2017.]
No. II(2)/HO/230(f-3)/2017.
Under Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873
(Central Act III of 1873), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby
fixes Jayankondam as the place at which the Subordinate
Judge's Court shall be held.
NOTIFICATION - IV
[G.O.Ms.No. 351, Home (Cts-III), 11th April 2017.]
No. II(2)/HO/230(f-4)/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 10 of the
Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873,  (Central Act III of 1873),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby directs that the
Subordinate Judge's Court at Ariyalur shall cease to have
jurisdiction over the District Munsif Court, Jayankondam and
hereby fixes the local limits of  the district Munsif Court,
Jayankondam as local limits of jurisdiction of the Subordinate
Judge's Court at Jayankondam with effect on and from the
date on which the Subordinate Judge assumes charge of
the Subordinate Judge's Court at Jayankondam.
