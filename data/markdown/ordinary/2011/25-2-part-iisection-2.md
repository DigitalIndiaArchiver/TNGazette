COMMERCIAL TAXES AND REGISTRATION
DEPARTMENT
Provision for payment of Consolidation of Stamp duty
chargeable in respect of issue of Debentures by
M/s. Apollo Hospitals Enterprise Limited under Indian
Stamp Act.
[G.O. Rt. No. 313, Commercial Taxes and
Registration (J1), 24th June 2011.]
No. II(2)/CTR/305/2011.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by clause (b) of sub-section (1) of Section 9
of the Indian Stamp Act, 1899 (Central Act II of 1899),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby provides for the
consolidation of duty of Rs. 12,50,000/- (Rupees Twelve lakh
and fifty thousand only) chargeable under the said Act,
in respect of issue of Debentures by M/s. Apollo Hospitals
Enterprise Limited bearing Serial Nos. 501 to 1000.
P. SURIYAKUMAR,
Additional Secretary to Government.
HOME DEPARTMENT
Grant of Licence to Grant Certificates of Marriages
between Indian Christians under Indian Christian
Marriage Act.
NOTIFICATION-I
[G.O. Ms. No. 332, Home (Courts-IV), 20th June 2011.]
No. II(2)/HO/306/2011.—Under Section 6 of the Indian
Christian Marriage Act, 1872 (Central Act XV of 1872), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu sanctions the issue of a licence to
Thiru S. Balarama, 273/2, Church of Christ Street, K.K.Nagar,
Chengalpattu, Kancheepuram District to solemnise marriages
within 
the 
territories 
comprising 
the 
State 
of
Tamil Nadu in accordance with the provisions of the
said Act.
NOTIFICATION-II
[G.O. Ms. No. 332, Home (Courts-IV), 20th June 2011.]
No. II(2)/HO/307/2011.—Under Section 9 of the Indian
Christian Marriage Act, 1872 (Central Act XV of 1872), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu sanctions the issue of a licence to
Thiru S. Balarama, 273/2, Church of Christ Street, K.K.Nagar,
Chengalpattu, Kancheepuram District to grant certificates of
marriages between Indian Christians, in accordance with the
provisions of the said Act, within the territories comprising
the State of Tamil Nadu.
RAMESHRAM MISHRA,
Principal Secretary to Government.
HOUSING AND URBAN DEVELOPMENT DEPARTMENT
Notifications under Tamil Nadu Buildings
(Lease and Rent  Control), Act.
AMENDMENTS TO NOTIFICATION.
NOTIFICATION-I
[G.O. Ms. No. 92, Housing and Urban Development (Bud),
17th June 2011.]
No. II(2)/HOU/308/2011.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by clause (a) of sub-section (1) of Section 23
of the Tamil Nadu Buildings (Lease and Rent Control) Act,
1960 (Tamil Nadu Act 18 of 1960), the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the
Home Department Notification II-I No. 3006 (f) of 1973,
published at page 3 of Part II Section 1 of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the
30th June, 1973, as subsequently amended.
2. The amendment hereby made shall come into force
with effect from the date on which the District Judge, Ariyalur
District assumes charge.
AMENDMENT
In the said Notification, in THE TABLE, in column (2),
against the entry “All subordinate Judges within their respective
jurisdictions” in column (1), after the expression “Tiruppur
District”, the expression “Ariyalur District” shall be added.
NOTIFICATION-II
[G.O. Ms. No. 92, Housing and Urban Development (Bud),
17th June 2011.]
No. II(2)/HOU/309/2011.—Under Clause (3) of Section 2
of the Tamil Nadu Buildings (Lease and Rent Control) Act,
1960 (Tamil Nadu Act 18 of 1960), the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the
Home 
Department 
Notification 
II(2)/HO/2997/74,
published at pages 319 and 320 of Part II—Section 2 of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 19th June, 1974,
as subsequently amended.
2 The amendment hereby made shall come into force
with effect from the date on which the District Judge, Ariyalur
District assumes charge.
AMENDMENT
In the said Notification, in the Tabular column,
in column (2), against the entry “Every District Munsif
within his local jurisdictions”  in column (1), after the expression
“Tiruppur District”, the expression “Ariyalur District” shall be
added.
S. PALANISAMY,
