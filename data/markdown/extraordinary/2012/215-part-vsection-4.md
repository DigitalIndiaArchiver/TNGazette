ERRATA ISSUED TO THE TAMIL NADU GOVERNMENT GAZETTE EXTRAORDINARY
No. 213, DATED 23-07-2012.
No. SRO G-34/2012.
The following errata is issued to the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary No. 213,
dated. 23-07-2012:—
“Errata to Notification No. 213, Dated. 23rd July, 2012
In the List of Contesting Candidates, omit the word ‘Shri’   appearing in the column ‘Name of Candidate’
against the names at Sl. Nos.1 and 2.”
