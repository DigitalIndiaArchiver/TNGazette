ALLOCATION OF SUBJECTS  AMONG MINISTER
No. I/PUSP/3(c)/2017.
The following Government Order is Published:-
[G.O. Ms. No. 147, Public (Special.B), 23rd February 2017, Maasi  11,  Thunmugi,  Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2048.]
READ:
1. G.O.Ms.No. 133, Public (Spl.B) Department, dated: 16th February 2017
2. G.O.Ms.No. 134, Public (Spl.B) Department, dated: 16th February 2017
Order No:  147 Public (Special-B) 23rd February 2017.
Under clause (3) of Article 166 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that the
subjects “Finance, Planning, Legislative Assembly, Elections and Passports, personnel and Administrative Reforms,
Personnel and Administrative Reforms (Training)” which are presently held by Thiru Edappadi K. Palaniswami,
Hon’ble Chief Minister be reallocated to Thiru D. Jayakumar, Minister for Fisheries in addition to the portfolios now
held by him. Consequent on the above allocation, Thiru D. Jayakumar, Minister for Fisheries be re-designated as
Minister for Fisheries, Finance and Personnel & Administrative Reforms.
(By order of the Governor)
GIRIJA  VAIDYANATHAN,
Chief Secretary .
