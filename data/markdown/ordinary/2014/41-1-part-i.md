No. I/PUSP/37/2014.—The following Notification of the Election Commission of
India, New Delhi, dated 6th October 2014 is republished:—
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 13A of the
Representation of the People Act, 1950 (43 of 1950), the Election Commission of India,
in consultation with the Government of Tamil Nadu, hereby nominates
Dr. Sandeep Saxena, IAS (1989) as the Chief Electoral Officer for the State of
Tamil Nadu with effect from the date he takes over charge and until further orders.
2. Dr. Sandeep Saxena, IAS shall cease to hold and hand over forthwith the charge
of all or any charges of work under the Government of Tamil Nadu, which he may be
holding before such assumption of office.
3. Dr. Sandeep Saxena, IAS while functioning as the Chief Electoral Officer,
Tamil Nadu shall not hold any additional charge whatsoever under the Government of
Tamil Nadu except that he should be designated Secretary to the Government in charge
of Election Department in the State Secretariat.
[No.154/TN/2014-EPS.]
SUMIT MUKHERJEE,
Secretary.
(By Order of the Governor)
MOHAN  VERGHESE  CHUNKATH,
Chief Secretary.
