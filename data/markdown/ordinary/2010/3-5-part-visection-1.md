[Notice under Rule 35A(4) of Tamil Nadu Cinemas (Regulation) Rule, 1957).]
No. VI(1)/25/2010.
Whereas Thiru M. Stephen Daniel, son of Thiru Ritchell Daniel, General Manager, Thiruvalargal Vijaya Productions
Private Limited, aged 61 years, residing at ‘Citi Towers’ 7th Floor, No. 117, Thyagaraya Road, T. Nagar, Chennai-17
has applied to the Commissioner of Police, Chennai City, Chennai-8 for the grant of “No Objection Certificate” for the
construction of permanent multiplex complex contains 9 Screens, at Door Nos. 183 to 190, Arcot Road, Vadapalani,
Chennai-26, T.S. Nos. 5-1, 5-3, 5-4, 5-5 and 5-7 of Saligramam Village in Block No. 5, Egmore-Nungambakkam Taluk,
Saligramam Village, Chennai-93, belonging to Tvl. Vijaya Productions Private Limited, Chennai-17.
1. Notice is hereby given that any person having any objection to the grant of “No Objection Certificate” at the
above said site on the grounds of non-compliance by the applicant with any of the provisions of the Tamil Nadu
Cinemas (Regulation) Act, 1955 (Tamil Nadu Act IX of 1955) or with any of the Rules there under should file his
objection in writing with the Licensing Authority (Commissioner of Police) within 15 days of publication of this Notice.
2. Any objection filed after the period shall be liable to be summarily rejected.
