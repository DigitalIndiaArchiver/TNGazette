Winding up the affairs of the Tiruppur Hosiery Garments Manufacturers Service Industrial Co-operative Society Ltd.
IND. No. 3/CBE/2001, Tiruppur District and appointment of Official Liquidator.
(Rc. No. 16578/ICF/2012.)
No.VI(1)/83/2014.вҖ”Under the powers conferred on the Registrar of Industrial Co-operatives under Section 137(2)(b) of
the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act 1983, (Act 30 of 1983), the activities of the Tiruppur Hosiery Garments
Manufacturers Service Industrial Co-operative Society Ltd., IND. No. 3/CBE/2001, Tiruppur District have been ordered to be
wound up with immediate effect by the Principal Secretary/Industries Commissioner and Director of Industries and Commerce
and the Registrar of Industrial Co-operatives, Mandaivelipakkam, Raja Annamalaipuram, Chennai-28,
vide Proc. No. 16578/ICF/2012, dated the 1st February 2014.
Under Section 138(1) of the same Act, Assistant Director (Industrial Co-operatives)/Official Liquidator, TAICO Bank,
Chennai-8 has been appointed as Official Liquidator of the said society for the purpose of liquidation.
