NOTIFICATION
The trades of Manufacturing and Sales of food for pet Animals and Sale of articles used for pet Animals are to be
included in the existing trades list under the category of Micro Cottage Industries for issuing trade license by fixing license
fee as Rs. 2000/- for the shops having the area of upto 1000 sq. feet and Rs. 5000/- for the shops having the area of more
than 1000 sq.  feet. This Notification is published as per Section 98-A of Chennai City Municipal Corporation Act, 1919.
The proposal would be taken for consideration by the Chennai Corporation after 30 days from the day of publication.
Hence any objection in this regard would be received within 30 days for consideration.
Objection if any in this regard shall be informed to the Principal Secretary/Commissioner, Corporation of Chennai,
Ripon Buildings, Chennai-600 003.
