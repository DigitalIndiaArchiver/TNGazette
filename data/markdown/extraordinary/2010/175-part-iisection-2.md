NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
LAW DEPARTMENT
NOMINATION OF CERTAIN PERSONS AS MEMBERS FOR SEARCH COMMITTEE TO RECOMMEND
A PANEL OF THREE NAMES TO THE CHANCELLOR FOR SELECTION OF VICE-CHANCELLOR,
TAMIL NADU DR. AMBEDKAR LAW UNIVERSITY, CHENNAI UNDER TAMIL NADU DR. AMBEDKAR
LAW UNIVERSITY ACT.
[G.O. (Rt.) No. 61, Law (LS), 10th June 2010.]

Chennai :—

Kabaleeswarar Nagar,
