HIGHER EDUCATION DEPARTMENT
Amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Collegiate Educational Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 384, Higher Education (F2), 30th October 2009, äŠðC 13, F¼õœÀõ˜ Ý‡´ - 2040.]
No. SRO B-75/2009.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Collegiate
Educational Service (Section 8-B in Volume II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1969).
2. The amendment hereby made shall come into force on the 30th October, 2009.
AMENDMENT.
In the said Special Rule, in the ANNEXURE, for the entires in column (3), against the entry “College Librarian in
Government Colleges” in column (1) and the corresponding entry in column (2) thereof, the following entries shall be
substituted, namely:—
“(i) Master's degree in Library Science or Information Science or Documentation Science or an equivalent professional
degree with not less than fifty five percentage of marks or its equivalent grade, where grading system is practised and a
consistently good academic record with knowledge on computerization of Library; and
(ii) pass in the National Eligibility Test (NET) or other accredited test like State Level Eligibility Test (SLET) in Library
Science conducted for the purpose by the University Grants Commission or any other agency approved by the University
Grants Commission:
“Provided that a person who is or has been awarded a Ph.D. Degree through a process of admission, registration,
course work and external evaluation as laid down in the University Grants Commission (minimum standards and procedure
for award of Ph.D. Degree), Regulation 2009, is exempted from passing the above tests”.
K. GANESAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.

No. SRO B-76/2009.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Jail
Subordinate Service (Section 17 in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1970).

LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT DEPARTMENT
Amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Labour  Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 163, Labour and Employment (E1), 2nd  November 2009.]
No. SRO B-77/2009.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Labour
Service (Section 20-B in Volume II of the  Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1969).
2. The Amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force with effect on and from the
23rd October 1980.
AMENDMENT
In the said Special Rules, in rule 2, in EXPLANATION-II, after clause (c), the following clause shall be added,
namely:—
“(d) For the purpose of drawing up of annual list of approved candidates for promotion to the posts of
Assistant Commissioner of Labour, Deputy Commissioner of Labour and Joint Commissioner of Labour,
the crucial date on which the candidates should be qualified shall be 15th April of every year”.
[G.O. Ms. No. 164, Labour and Employment (E1), 2nd  November 2009.]
No. SRO B-78/2009.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Labour
Service (Section 20-B in Volume II of the  Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1969).
2. The Amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force with effect on and from the
11th July 2007.
AMENDMENT
In the said Special Rules, in rule 2, in EXPLANATION-II, after clauses (c) and (d), the following clause shall be
substituted, namely:—
“(c) For the purpose of drawing up of the annual list of approved candidates for appointment to the posts in this
service, the crucial date on which the candidates should be qualified shall be 1st January of every year.”
T. PRABHAKARA RAO,
Principal Secretary to Government.
