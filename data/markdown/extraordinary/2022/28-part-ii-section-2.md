ENERGY DEPARTMENT
APPOINTMENT OF MEMBER IN THE TAMIL NADU ELECTRICITY REGULATORY COMMISSION 
UNDER THE ELECTRICITY  ACT, 2003.
[G.O. Ms. No. 2, Energy (D1), 10th January  2022,  
மார்கழி 26, பிலவ, திருவள்ளுவர் ஆண்டு-2052.] 
No. II(2)/EGY/29(i)/2022.	
Under sub-section (5) of the section 82 of the Electricity Act, 2003 (Central Act 36 of 2003), on the recommendation 
of the Selection Committee referred to in section 85 of the said Act, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints 
Thiru. R. Jarard Kishore as a Member of the Tamil Nadu Electricity Regulatory Commission, Chennai.
	
RAMESH CHAND MEENA,
