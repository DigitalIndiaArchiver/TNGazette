HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE DEPARTMENT
[G.O. Ms. No. 69, Health and Family Welfare (E1),
12th February 2009, Thai 30, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
Vesting of Properties and Scheme for administration
relating to Professor Dr. A. Jesudos and professor
Dr. K. Balasubramanian Endowment Fund tenable by
the Head of Department of ENT, Thanjavur
Medical College, Thanjavur for conducting  Endowment
for awarding Gold Medals under Charitable
Endowments Act.
No.II(2)/HF/197/2009.—In the matter of the Charitable
Endowments Act, 1890 (Central Act VI of 1890) and in the
matter of Endowment Fund for “Prof. Dr. A. Jesudos and
Prof.  Dr. K. Balasubramanian Endowment”  for awarding
Gold Medals for Under Graduate students of ENT in the
Competitive Examination (Written and Orals) to be held
every year fixed by the Head of Department of ENT, Thanjavur
Medical College, Thanjavur.
I. VESTING OF PROPERTY.
Whereas an application has been made to the Government
of Tamil Nadu under clause (b) of sub-section (i) of Section
6 of the Charitable Endowments Act, 1890 (Central
Act VI of 1890) by Prof. Dr. A. Jesudos and
Prof. Dr. K. Balasubramanian, the donor of the Endowment
Fund specified above for vesting Rs. 25,000/- (Rupees twenty
five thousand only) in the Treasurer of Charitable Endowments
appointed under the said Act, for the State of Tamil Nadu.
The Governor of Tamil Nadu in exercise of the powers
conferred by section 4 of the said Act, hereby directs, that
the said amount be vested in the Treasurer aforesaid and
be held by him and his successors in office, subject to the
provisions of the said Act and to any rules, which, from time
to time, may be or shall be deemed to have been made
thereunder, upon trust for the purpose and subject to the
conditions set-forth in the scheme settled under section 5
of the said Act for the administration of the said Fund.
II. SCHEME FOR ADMINISTRATION
Whereas an application has been made to the Government
of Tamil Nadu under clause (b) of sub section (i) of Section
6 of the Charitable Endowments Act, 1890 (Central
Act VI of 1890) by Prof. Dr. A. Jesudos and Prof.
Dr. K. Balasubramanian,Thanjavur Medical College, Thanjavur,
the donor of the Endowment Fund Specified above, for
settling the Scheme for the administration of the said Fund.
The Governor of Tamil Nadu in exercise of the powers
conferred by section 5 of the said Act, hereby settles the
Scheme set-forth in the schedule below for the administration
of the said Fund and appointed from the date of publication
of this Notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette,
as the date on which the Scheme as settled shall come into
operation.
THE SCHEDULE:
1. The 
Endowment 
Fund 
shall 
be 
called 
as
“Prof. Dr. A. Jesudos and Prof. Dr. K. Balasubramanian
Endowment”, The above Fund has to be settled for the
purpose of awarding Gold Medals to the Under Graduate
Students of ENT in the Competitive Examinaton (Written and
Orals) to be held every year fixed by the Head of Department
of ENT, Thanjavur Medical College, Thanjavur.
2. The Thanjavur Medical College, emblem shall be
embossed on the obverse side and the insignia / words “Prof.
Dr. A. Jesudos and Prof. Dr. K. Balasubramanian, Medal for
ENT-UnderGraduate” shall be affixed on the reverse side of
the Gold Medal.
3. The Dean, Thanjavur Medical College, Thanjavur shall
be the administrator of the Fund and shall deposit the amount
furnished by the Donor Prof. Dr. A. Jesudos and Prof.
Dr. K. Balasubramanian with the Treasurer of Charitable
Endowments, Government of Tamil Nadu, Chennai-2 who will
from time to time invest or reinvest the amount and proceeds
in Government securities to the best interest of the Fund.
4.The interest accrued annually shall be utilised for
purchase of Gold Medals every year and it shall be given
to the best students annually on the college day.
5. As and when there is need for more funds to conduct
this examination, funds will be contributed by the donor or
any one of his family members to conduct this examination
periodically every year.
6. The unutilised amount accrued as into rests shall be
added to the corpus of the Fund.
7. The best students shall be selected by the adjudicating
Committee comprising the following Members:—
(i)
Professor and Head of Department of ENT,
Thanjavur Medical College, Thanjavur.
(ii) Additional Professors of Department of ENT,
Thanjavur Medical College, Thanjavur.
G. RAJASEKARAN,
Additional Secretary to Government.
