The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that the lands specified in the Schedule below have to be 
acquired for Highways purposes to wit, for the “Construction of High-Level Bridge from Km 72/0-72/6 of Cuddalore – 
Chittor Road across Thenpennai River” in Keezhaiyur Village, Thirukoilur Taluk, Kallakurichi District and it having 
already been decided that the amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid out of the funds 
controlled or managed by the Special District Revenue Officer (L.A),Highways Viluppuram after having considering the 
cause shown by the owner or other person having interest on such lands if any as the case may be do hereby publish the 
following notice under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002), the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands specified in the schedule below measuring an extent of 00639 
Sq.mtrs of Dry and R.Manai Lands in Survey No. 88 & 176/1B., for Highways purposes to wit, for the “Construction of 
High-Level Bridge from Km 72/0-72/6 of Cuddalore – Chittor Road across Thenpennai River” in Keezhaiyur Village, 
Thirukoilur Taluk, Kallakurichi District.
The plan of the lands are kept in the office of the Special District Revenue Officer (L.A), Highways, Viluppuram and 
in the O/o Special Tahsildar (L.A), Highways (Unit-4), Kallakurichi which may be inspected on any day during office hours.
