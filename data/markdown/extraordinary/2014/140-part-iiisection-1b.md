HOME   DEPARTMENT
AMENDMENTS  TO  THE SPECIAL RULES FOR THE TAMIL NADU FORENSIC SCIENCES
SUBORDINATE SERVICE.
[G.O. Ms. No. 514, Home (Police.XVIII), 28th July 2014.]
No. SRO B-42(a-1)/2014.
In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Forensic Sciences
Subordinate Service (Section 49 in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1970).
2. The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force with effect on and from the
26th August 2010.
AMENDMENTS.
In the said Special Rules,-
(1) for the expression “Scientific Assistant, Grade-l”, wherever it occurs, the expression “Scientific
Officer” shall be substituted; and
(2) for the expression “Scientific Assistant, Grade-Il”, wherever it occurs, the expression “Junior Scientific
Officer” shall be substituted.

AMENDMENTS  TO  THE SPECIAL RULES FOR THE TAMIL NADU FORENSIC SCIENCES SERVICE.
[G.O. Ms. No. 515, Home (Police.XVIII), 28th July 2014.]
No. SRO B-42(a-2)/2014.
In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Forensic Science
Service (Section 43 under Part III A in Volume-Il of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual,1969).
2. The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 26th August 2010.
AMENDMENTS.
In the said Special Rules,-
(1)
for the expressions “Deputy Director”, “Assistant Director” and ”Scientific Officer”, wherever they
occur, the expressions “Joint Director”, “Deputy Director” and “Assistant Director” shall, respectively,
be substituted; and
(2)
for the expression “Scientific Assistant Grade I”, wherever it occurs, the expression “Scientific Officer”
shall be substituted.
