Under sub-section (1) of Section 4 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby withdraws from acquisition of the land/lands specified in the 
schedule below in respect of which notice under sub-section (1) of Section 3 was published by the Industries (SIPCOT-LA) 
Department at page 5 of Part-II, Section-2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary No.37, dated 28.01.2011 
and the Errata was published by the Industries (SIPCOT-LA) Department at page No.315 of   Part-II, Section-2 of the 
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No.8, dated 26.02.2014.  The Government, have also decided to withdraw the land acquisition 
proceedings in G.O.(Ms).No.268, Industries (SIPCOT-LA) Department, dated 21.12.2020. Hence, the below mentioned lands 
in the schedule are withdrawn from the land acquisition proceedings and actual possession of which was not taken by or 
on behalf of the Government.
