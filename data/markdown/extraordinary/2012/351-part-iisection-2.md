MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION AND WATER SUPPLY DEPARTMENT
DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF THE CHENNAI METROPOLITAN WATER SUPPLY AND SEWERAGE
(SECOND AMENDMENT) ACT, 2012 (TAMIL NADU ACT 48 OF 2012).
[G.O. Ms. No. 130, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (Election), 26th December 2012.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/901(a)/2012.
Under sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Chennai Metropolitan Water Supply and Sewerage (Second Amendment)
Act, 2012 (Tamil Nadu Act 48 of 2012), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the 1st day of January 2013
as the date on which the said Act shall come into force.
SHEELA BALAKRISHNAN,
Additional Chief Secretary to Government.
