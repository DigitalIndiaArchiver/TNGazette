The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that 
the lands specified in the schedule below have to be acquired 
for industrial purpose to wit for the formation of new Broad 
Gauge Railway line between Tindivanam and Thiruvannamalai 
and it having already been decided that the entire amount 
of compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid 
out of the lands controlled or managed by District Collector, 
Viluppuram the following notice is issued under sub-section (1) 
of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial 
Purposes Act, 1997. 
NOTIce 
 Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu 
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act.1997 
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu 
hereby acquires the land / lands specified in the schedule 
below and measuring 6.02.0 Hectare of Dry Land, 0.47.5 
Hectare of Wet Land and 00316 Sq. Mt. of Natham Land to 
the same, a little more or less needed for Industrial Purpose, 
to wit for the formation of new Broad Gauge Railway line 
between Tindivanam and Thiruvannamalai. 
The plan of the lands are kept in the Collector's Office, 
Viluppuram and in the office of the Sub Collector, Tindivanam, 
Viluppuram district and may be  inspected at any time during 
office hours.
Viluppuram District, Gingee Taluk, 156, Uranithangal Village.
Extent of land under acquisition : 6.02.0 Hectare of Dry Land
