No. SRO B-52/2015.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the rules published with the Industries, Labour and
Co-operation Department, Notification S.R.O. No.A.402 of 1960 dated the 17th September 1960 at pages 380-383 of Part V
of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 19th October 1960.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules,-
(1) in rule 6,-
(i) for the expression “twenty eight”, the expression “thirty” shall be substituted;
(ii) the first proviso shall be omitted;
(iii) in the second proviso, for the expression “Provided further that”, the expression “Provided that” shall be
substituted.
(2) In rule 7, in the Table,
(i) in column (3), against the post “Industrial Co-operative Officer” in column (1) thereof,-
(a) against “item (a)” in column (2), for item 1, the following item shall be substituted, namely:-
“1. Must possess any degree of any University or Institution recognized by the University Grants Commission
for the purpose of its grant”;
(b) against “item (b)” in column (2), for item (1), the following item shall be substituted, namely:-
“1. Must possess any degree of any University or Institution recognized by the University Grants Commission
for the purpose of its grant and”.
(ii) in column (3), against the post “Supervisor of Industrial Co-operatives” in column (1) thereof.-
(a)
against “item (a)” in column (2), for item 1, the following item shall be substituted, namely:-
“1. Must possess any degree of any University or Institution recognized by the University Grants Commission
for the purpose of its grant”;
(b) against “item (c)” in column (2), for item 1, the following item shall be substituted, namely:-
“1. Must possess any degree of any University or Institution recognized by the University Grants Commission
for the purpose of its grant and”.
KUMAR JAYANT,
