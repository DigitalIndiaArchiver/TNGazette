In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 21 of the Prohibition of Employment
as Manual Scavengers and their Rehabilitation Act, 2013 (Central Act 25 of 2013), the Governor of
Tamil Nadu, hereby confers on all the Executive Magistrates in the grade of Revenue Divisional Officer in
the State, the powers  of a Judicial Magistrate of the first class, for the trial of offences under the said Act.
P. ANNAMALAI,
Principal Secretary to Government.
