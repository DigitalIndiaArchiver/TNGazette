The  Governor of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that
the lands specified in the schedule below have to be
acquired for Industrial Purpose, to wit for the formation of
ULTRA MEGA POWER PROJECT, Cheyyur  and it has already
been decided that the entire amount of compensation to be
awarded for the lands is to be paid out of the funds controlled
or managed by The Officer Authorised Signatory, Costal
Tamil Nadu Power LTD., New  Delhi and having considered
the cause shown by the owners or other persons having
interest the said lands as the case may be do hereby
publish the following notice under sub-section (1) of Section
3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for industrial
purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999)
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Industrial purposes Act 1997 (Tamil Nadu  Act 10 of 1999)
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquired the lands
specified in the schedule below and measuring an extent of
10.17.5 Hectare to the same, a little more or less are needed
for INDUSTRIAL purpose, to wit, for the formation of ULTRA
MEGA POWER PROJECT 54 CHITHARKADU VILLAGE,
CHEYYUR TALUK, Kancheepuram District.
[ 1 ]
The Plan of the lands are kept in the office of Special
Tahsildar, Land Acquisition, Unit-II,  ULTRA MEGA POWER
PROJECT Scheme, Cheyyur may be inspected at any time
during office hours.
