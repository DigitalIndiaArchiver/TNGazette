Amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Special Police Subordinate Service
[G.O. Ms. No. 1135, Home (Pol.9), 31st December 2009.]
No. SRO B-2/2010.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Sections 8 and 10 of The Tamil Nadu District Police Act,
1859 (Central Act XXIV of 1859) and Sections 9 and 11 of the Chennai City Police Act, 1888 (Tamil Nadu Act III of
1888) read with the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the
following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Special Police Subordinate Service, 1978 (Section 44 in
Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1986).
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force with effect on and from the
19th May, 2008.

PERSONNEL AND ADMINISTRATIVE REFORMS DEPARTMENT
Amendment to the Tamil Nadu Government Servants' Conduct Rules, 1973
[G.O. Ms. No. 171, Personnel and Administrative Reforms (A), 18th December 2009.]
No. SRO B-3/2010.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Tamil Nadu Government Servants' Conduct
Rules, 1973.
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 5th October 2009.
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in the fourth proviso to rule 24-A, for the expression “Pilgrim pass”, the expression “Temporary
Hajj passports with a validity of eight months for Saudi Arabia”, shall be substituted.
K.N. VENKATARAMANAN,
Secretary to Government.

RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND PANCHAYAT RAJ DEPARTMENT
Adhoc rules relating to Temporary Post of Superintending Engineer, Executive Engineer, Assistant
Executive Engineer and Assistant Engineer in Rural Development Department.
Amendment to Notification
[G.O. Ms. No. 4, Rural Development and Panchayat Raj (E3), 11th January 2010.]
No. SRO B-4/2010.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the rules issued with the
Rural Development Department Notification No. SRO B-62/2000 at pages 74 and 75 of Part III—Section 1(b) of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated 24th May 2000.
2. The amendment hereby made shall come into force on and from the 11th January 2010.
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in rule 4, of The Table, column (2) for item (2) against the entries “(b) Recruitment by transfer
from the Junior Engineer and Senior Draughting Officer in the Rural Development Department” in column (1) thereof, the
following item shall be substituted, namely:—
“(2) Service for a total period of not less than five years on duty in the Tamil Nadu Rural Development and
Panchayat Raj Department as Junior Engineer or as Senior Draughting Officer either separately or in combination as
Junior Engineer and Senior Draughting Officer”.
 K. ASHOK VARDHAN SHETTY,
 Principal Secretary to Government.
