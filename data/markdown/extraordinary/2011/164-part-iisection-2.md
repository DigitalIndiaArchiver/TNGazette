DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF THE TAMIL NADU MARINE FISHING REGULATION
(AMENDMENT) ACT, 2011.
[G.O.Ms. No. 59, Animal Husbandry, Dairying and Fisheries(FS4), 6th June 2011,
Vaikasi 23, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No. II(2)/AHDF/245(b)/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Marine Fishing
Regulation (Amendment) Act, 2011 (Tamil Nadu Act 5 of 2011), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
appoints the 6th day of June 2011, as the date on which the said Act shall come into force.
GAGANDEEP SING BEDI,
Secretary to Government.
