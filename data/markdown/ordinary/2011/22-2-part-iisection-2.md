by Section 10 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873
(Central Act III of 1873), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby
varies the local limits of the jurisdiction of the Subordinate
Judge’s Court, Mannargudi, to the effect that the said
Subordinate Judge’s Court shall have and exercise original
Jurisdiction over the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate
Court, Needamangalam, in addition to its existing jurisdiction,
with effect from the date on which the District Munsif-cum
Judicial Magistrate, Needamangalam assumes charge of that
Court.
NOTIFICATION-III
[G.O. Ms. No. 298, Home (Courts. III), 27th May 2011.]
No. II(2)/HO/266/2011.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 11 of the Code of
Criminal Procedure, 1973 (Central Act 2 of 1974), the Governor
of Tamil Nadu, after consultation with the High Court, Madras,
hereby specifies Needamangalam in Tiruvarur District, as a
place at which a Court of Judicial Magistrate shall be
established.
NOTIFICATION-IV
[G.O. Ms. No. 298, Home (Courts. III), 27th May 2011.]
No. II(2)/HO/267/2011.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 11 of the Code of
Criminal Procedure, 1973 (Central Act 2 of 1974), the Governor
of Tamil Nadu, after consultation with the High Court, Madras,
hereby specifies Valangaiman in Tiruvarur District, as a place
at which a Court of Judicial Magistrate shall be established.
