The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed 
that the lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below have to be 
acquired for industrial purposes to wit for a formation of Fourth 
Railway Track from Korukkupettai to Ennore Railway Station 
and it having already been decided that the entire amount of 
compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid out 
of the lands controlled or managed by Southern Railway. The 
following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 3 
of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of land for Industrial Purposes 
Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu 
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu 
hereby acquires the lands in Tiruvottiyur and Ernavoor Villages 
in Tiruvottiyur Taluk of Tiruvallur District as speciﬁ ed in the 
Schedule below and measuring 10803 Sq.M to the same, 
a little more or less needed for industrial purposes, to wit, 
for formation of Fourth Railway Track from Korukkupettai to 
Ennore Railway Stations.
The plan of the lands kept in the ofﬁ ce of the Land 
Acquisition Ofﬁ cer and District Revenue Ofﬁ cer, Tiruvallur and 
at the Taluk Ofﬁ ce, Tiruvottiyur and may be inspected at any 
time during ofﬁ ce hours.
