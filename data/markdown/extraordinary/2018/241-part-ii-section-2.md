APPOINTMENT  OF COMMISSION OF INQUIRY TO INQUIRE THE INCIDENT OF DEATH OF THIRU E. ILLAVARASAN 
SON OF P. ELANGO ON 4TH JULY 2013 AT DHARMAPURI DISTRICT UNDER THE COMMISSIONS OF INQUIRY ACT.
AMENDMENT  TO  NOTIFICATION
[G.O. Ms. No. 474, Public (Law & Order-F), 5th July 2018,  Aani 21, Vilambi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2049.]
No.II(2)/PULO/591(c)/2018.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Commissions of Inquiry Act, 1952 
(Central Act LX of 1952), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Public
(Law and Order-F) Department Notiﬁ cation No.ll(2)/PULO/455(h)/2013, published at pages 1-2 of Part ll—Section 2 of the 
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 8th July 2013, as subsequently amended:-
AMENDMENT
In the said Notiﬁ cation, in paragraph 4, for the expression “sixty months”, the expression “sixty one months and 
twenty four days” shall be substituted.
 
 
 
 
P. SENTHILKUMAR, 
 
 
 
Principal Secretary to Government (FAC).
