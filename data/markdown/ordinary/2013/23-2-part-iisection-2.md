В¬ГөГЁВЈГӘВӨ 6Вў, ГөВӨГјГІ, Г®ВӨВјГөГ·ВўГҖГөГіВў ГқГӯВўВҙ-2044.]

From the Superintendent of Police, Central Bureau

Letter No. RC MEI 2011 E 0012, dated

2013.
Order: No. 324, Home (Police XIX), 20th May 2013.
Whereas, complaints have been received from a number
of depositors that Messers   Paazee Forex Trading India
Private Limited, Tirupur District a financial establishment,
has defaulted the return of deposits made by the depositors
after maturity;
And whereas, the Government is satisfied that the said
financial establishment is not likely to return the deposits
to the depositors and hence, the Government have to protect
the interests of such depositors;
And whereas, the immovable properties specified in the
Schedule to this order are alleged to have been procured
by the said financial establishment from and out of the
deposits colleted from the depositors;
Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by
Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Protection of Interests of
Depositors (in Financial Establishments) Act, 1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 44 of 1997), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby makes an ad-interim order attaching the immovable
properties procured in the name of the partners of Messers
Paazee Forex Trading India Private Limited, Tirupur District
and another person as specified in the Schedule to this
order and transfer the control over the said immovable
properties to the Competent Authority, namely, Competent
Authority and District Revenue Officer, Coimbatore District
appointed under the said Act.
2.
The Competent Authority, is requested to pursue
further action in accordance with the procedure laid down
in sub-sections (3) and (4) of Section 4 of the said Act and
also the formalities prescribed in the Tamil Nadu Protection
of Interests of Depositors (in Financial Establishments)
Rules, 1997.
3.
The Special Public Prosecutor, Special Court for the
Tamil Nadu Protection of Interests of Depositors (in Financial
Establishments) Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 44 of 1997)
Coimbatore is requested to render necessary assistance
to the Competent Authority in filing the application before the
said Special Court.
(By order of the Governor)

The following Government Order is published:-
[G.O. (3D) No. 20, Home (Prison -IV), 29th May 2013,
В¬ГөГЁВЈГӘВӨ 15Вў, ГөВӨГјГІ, Г®ВӨВјГөГ·ВўГҖГөГіВў ГқГӯВўВҙ-2044.]
READ:
1. G.O. (2D) No. 118, Home (Prison-IV), Department,
dated 17-4-2003.
2. From the Inspector General of Police, Intelligency
(Internal Security), 'Q' Branch CID, Chennai letter
No. 13793/X/2012/C, dated 23-11-2012.
3. From the Inspector General of Police, Intelligency
(Internal Security), 'Q' Branch CID, Chennai letter
No. 15010/X/2012/C, dated 02-01-2013.
Order: No. 20, Home (Prison-IV), 29th May 2013.
In the G.O. first read above, the Government have issued
orders notifying the Cheyyar  Sub-Jail as вҖҳSpecial CampвҖҷ to
accommodate the inmates of Chengalpattu Special Camp.
2.
The Inspector General of Police, Intelligence (Internal
Security), вҖҳQвҖҷ Branch CID, Chennai in his letter second read
above has stated that the Special Camp.  Cheyyar was
functioning from 19-01-2005 to 24-08-2006 and eleven ENDLF
cadres were accommodated in Cheyyar Special Camp.
These inmates were sent back to Srilanka at their own
willingness.   Subsequently, the Cheyyar Special Camp was
handed over to Prison Department on 01-04-2011.  Since
then, this is functioning as Sub-Jail.  After renovation works
are completed, the Sub-Jail, Cheyyar may be handed over to
the District Administration for use as вҖңSpecial Camp for
accommodating Srilankan Tamils lodged in Chengalpattu
Special Camp.
3.
The Inspector General of Police, Intelligence (Internal
Security), вҖҳQвҖҷ Branch CID, Chennai in his letter third read
above has stated that the Additional Director General of
Police/Inspector General of Prisons, Chennai is pursuing
the matter to convert Chengalpattu Special Camp into
District Prison for accommodating the Prisoners now lodged
in Central Prison, Puzhal in order to ease the problems faced
during the escort of prisoners from Central Prison, Puzhal to
various courts for their regular hearing in respective
districts.  Hence, it becomes necessary to shift the inmates
of Chengalpattu Special Camp to Cheyyar Special Camp.
4.
The Government after careful consideration have
decided to accept the above proposal.  Accordingly, the
Government hereby orders that the Cheyyar Sub Jail be
notified as вҖңSpecial CampвҖқ Cheyyar to accommodate the
inmates of Chengalpattu Special Camp, against whom
orders have been issued  under Section 3(2)(e) of the
Foreigners, Act, 1946, to restrict their free movement in the
state.
5.
The Additional Director General of Police/Inspector
General of Prisons shall make necessary arrangements
for handing over the Sub Jail, Cheyyar to the Collector of
Tiruvannamalai immediately.  The Collector of Tiruvannamalai
will take administrative and operation control of the Special
Camp, Cheyyar.
6.
The Inspector General of Police,  Intelligence (Internal
Security), вҖҳQвҖҷ Branch CID, Chennai shall send necessary
proposals to the Government in Public Department for issuing
fresh orders under Section 3(2) (e) of Foreigners, Act in respect
of inmates of Chengalpattu Special Camp for transferring them
to вҖҳSpecial CampвҖҷ Cheyyar.
(By order of the Governor.)

В«ГөВ¬ГҙГөВЈЕ’Е В№ (В®1)Г®Вў В¶В¬Гё, 6 В«Гұ 2013, CЛҶFВ¬Гі 23,
MГјГІ, FВјГөЕ“ГҖГөЛң ГқвҖЎВҙ 2044.]
No. II(2)/LE/419/2013.вҖ”ГһвҖ°Г® ГқВ¬ГӯJВЎ ГһВ¬ГӯЕ Hв„ў
В°PЕ HвҖ ВҙЕ“Г· ВӘГ°ВЈВјЕ“ ВӘГ®ВЈГ¬ЛңГ°ВЈГЁ Г®IвҖәГҜВЈВҙ ГңГіВІ
В«Г°ВЈвҖҡВ°ГөГіЛҶВ¶ ГЁГ¶ГЁвҖ№, В«ГӘГҙвҖ№ В«ГЁВЈвҖ Г¬вҖ№-1 ГўВЎГё GЛңГөВЈГЁЛҶFЕёВ°вҖ№
Г®IвҖәГҜВЈВҙ ГңГіВІ В«Г°ВЈвҖҡВ°ГөГіЛҶВ¶ ГЁГ¶ГЁ ГЎNГІЛңГЁЕ“ ГӘЖ’ГЁвҖ№ ГўВЎГё
ВӘГ®ВЈNЕёГӘЖ’ГЁЛҶFЕёВ°IВ¬Г¬В«ГІ ВӘГ®ВЈNЕёГ®ГЁГіВЈГҒ ГўВҝвҖ°В¶Е“Г·В¶ ГўВЎГҒ
ГңГіВІ ГЁВјВ¶ГөГ®ВЈВҪвҖ№;
В«ГұЕёВӘГӘВЈВЎГ№ Г®ГЁГіВЈВ¬Гё ВӘГ®ВЈNГҙВЈГ·Лң cFГұВЎГё bЛңЕ В№вҖҡГЁВЈГЁ
ГңГӮЕ В№ГөВ¶ ГңГөCГІВӘГұВЎГҒ Г®IвҖәГҜВЈВҙ ГқГҖГҜЛң ГңГөЛңГЁЕ“
ГЁВјВ¶ГөГ®ВЈВҪвҖ№;
ГўГ№В«Гө 1947-ГқвҖ№ ГқвҖЎВҙ ВӘГ®ВЈNв„ў Г®ГЁГіВЈГҒГЁЕ“ ГӘвҖ Г¬ЛҶFВЎ
(ГұЛҶFГІ ГӘвҖ Г¬вҖ№ XIV/1947) 10(1) (c) HKMВҪвҖ№, 10 (1) (d)
HKMВЎ ГөГівҖ№В№ GГ°вҖ°Г®В¬Г№JВҪвҖ№ ГөГ¶Ж’AВ»Е“Г· ГңFГЁВЈГіЖ’ГЁВ¬Г·вҖҡ
ВӘГЁВЈвҖЎВҙ Г®IвҖәГҜВЈВҙ ГқГҖГҜЛң ГңГөЛңГЁЕ“ В«ГұЕёВӘГӘВЈВЎГ№ Г®ГЁГіВЈГҒ
В«ГӘГҙвҖ№ ВӘГ®ВЈNГҙВЈГ·Лң cFГұВЎГё bЛңЕ В№вҖҡГЁВЈГЁ ГңГӮЕ Г°Е Г°Г¬ В«ГөвҖЎВҙвҖ№
ГўВЎГҒ ГһГ®Г№ВЈв„ўГқВ¬ГӯJВҙAГёВЈЛң.
В«ГұВҪвҖ№, 1947 ГқвҖ№ ГқвҖЎВҙ ВӘГ®ВЈNв„ў Г®ГЁГіВЈГҒГЁЕ“ ГӘвҖ Г¬ЛҶFВЎ
10(2ГЈ) HKMВЎWвҖә, ГһвҖ°Г® ГқВ¬ГӯВ¬ГІЕ  ВӘГ°ЕёГҒвҖҡВӘГЁВЈвҖЎГ¬
ГҜВЈOLВјвҖ°В¶ ГҚВЎГҒ ГұВЈГ®Ж’ГЁГҖвҖҡВ°Е“ bЛңЕ В№ ГңOвҖҡВ°ГұВЈГҒ В«ГӘГҙвҖ№,
