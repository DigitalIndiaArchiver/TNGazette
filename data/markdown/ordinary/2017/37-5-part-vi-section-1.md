Notice under Rule 35A(4) of Tamil Nadu Cinemas (Regulation) Rules, 1957.
No. VI(1)/280/2017.
 Whereas M/s. Sugam Vanijya Holdings Private Limited, 44, Pillaiyar Koil Street, Jawaharlal Nehru Road, 
Anna Nagar, Chennai-40 has applied for No Objection Certificate for the construction of a multiplex containing 
10 Screens with Food Court, Hotel, Restaurants, Shops, Fun Centre and Commercial Complex at T.S. Nos. 1/1, 
1/2, 2/4 of Block No. 26 and T.S. No. 3/2 of Block No. 27 in Koyambedu, Ambattur Taluk, Chennai District. 
Notice is hereby given that any person having any objection to the grant of "No Objection Certiﬁ cate" for the above 
said site on the grounds of non-compliance by the applicant with any of the provisions of the Tamil Nadu Cinemas 
(Regulation) Act, 1955 (T.N. Act IX of 1955) or with any of the Rules thereunder should ﬁ le his objection in writing with 
the Licensing Authority (Commissioner of Police) within 21 days of publication of this Notice. 
1. Any objection ﬁ led after the period shall be liable to be summarily rejected.

(Roc. No. 3411/2009/DP1)
No.VI(1)/281/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 33(2) of Town and Country Planning Act, 1971, the Commissioner 
of Town and Country Planning, Chennai is hereby conﬁ rms the variation to the Review approved Thiruvalluvar Detailed 
Development Plan of Dindugal Local Planning Area, Varied by Director of Town and Country Planning Proceedings in
Roc. No. 3411/2009/DP2, dated: 17-03-2010 and was published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No. 8, 
Part-Vl—Section-1, Page. No. 100, dated: 02-03-2011 (Publication No. VI(l)/97/2011). 
2. Since No objections and suggestions was received on this draft notiﬁ cation within the stipulated time, the same 
are hereby conﬁ rmed and ordered as below: 
CONFIRMATION OF VARIATION
1. 
The entire Thiruvalluvar Detailed Development Plan of Dindugal Local Planning Area, varied by the Director of 
Town and Country Planning proceedings in Roc. No. 3411/2009/DP2, dated: 17-03-2010 under Section 33(1) of Town 
and Country Planning Act, 1971 is hereby conﬁ rmed under Section 33(2) of Town and Country Planning Act, 1971. 
2. The Varied Plan is numbered as Map No. 5 DDP(V)/DTCP No. 4/2010. 
3. 
The Detailed Development Plan clauses and schedules annexed with this plan are made enforceable from the 
date of publication of this notiﬁ cation in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
