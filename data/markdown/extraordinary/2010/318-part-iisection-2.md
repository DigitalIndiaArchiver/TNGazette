MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION AND WATER SUPPLY DEPARTMENT
DRAFT VARIATION TO THE LIST OF PARKS, PLAY - FIELDS AND OSR LAND FOR TIRUCHIRAPPALLI
CORPORATION, SRIRANGAM DIVISION UNDER TAMIL NADU PARKS, PLAY-FIELDS AND OPEN SPACES
(PRESERVATION AND REGULATION) ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 249, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (MC2), 19th October 2010.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/631(e)/2010.
Whereas in G.O. (Ms.) No. 562, Education Department, dated 17-04-1967 a list of Parks, Play-fields and Open
Spaces in the area within the jurisdiction of the then Tiruchirappalli Municipal Council was approved under
sub-section (2) of Section 4 of the Tamil Nadu Parks, Play-fields and Open Spaces  (Preservation and Regulation)
Act, 1959 (Tamil Nadu Act 26 of 1959) and the same was published. In the said list among others, an extent of
22000 Sq. feet in Old T.S. Nos. 293 and 294/1 (New T.S.No. 44 part) was notified as Play-field.
2.
And Whereas, the Director of Town and Country Planning from the Commissioner of Town and Country
Planning letter Roc. No. 20158/2008/MP3, dated 12th November 2008 has stated that the Directorate approved
the building plan for the construction of Library building in T.S.No.36, inside the campus of the St.Joseph College,
Tiruchirappalli. The management of the St. Joseph College has represented that contrary to the site for which the
plan for library building was approved to be constructed, the said library building has been constructed in T.S. No.
44 part, which was notified as Play-field site. The Petitioner has requested that an alternative site in the same
campus in T.S.No. 40 with the same extent may be renotified as Play-field site instead of T.S. No. 44 part.
3.
And Whereas, the Government, after careful examination of the proposal of the Director of Town and
Country Planning regarding the representation of the management of the St.Joseph College, have decided to
accept the same.
4.
Now Therefore, the following draft of the variation to the approved list of Parks and Play-fields published
in the Education Department notification No.75 of 1967 in Part II—Section 3 at pages 534 to 540 of the
Fort St. George Gazette, dated the 14th June 1967, which it is proposed to make in exercise of the powers
conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Parks, Play-fields and Open Spaces (Preservation
and Regulation) Act, 1959 (Tamil Nadu Act 26 of 1959) is hereby published as required under sub-section (2) of
the said section.

5.
Notice is hereby given that objections or suggestions, if any, received in writing, in respect of the said draft
variation to the said list from any person interested, within seven days from the date of publication of this
Notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, will be taken into consideration by the Government of
Tamil Nadu.
6.
Objections or suggestions, if any, should be addressed to the Principal Secretary to Government,
Municipal Administration and Water Supply Department, Secretariat, Fort. St. George, Chennai-600 009.
DRAFT VARIATION
In the said Notification, in THE SCHEDULE, under the heading ‘Play - fields’,—
(1) the entry ‘293’ in column (2) and the entries relating thereto in columns (3) to (8) against the entry
“20 St. Joseph’s College Play-field” in column (1) shall be omitted;
(2) for the entry ‘294/1’, in column (2) and the entry “0 6, 160” in column (3) against the entry
“20 St. Joseph’s College Play-field” in column (1) thereof, the entry  “New S.No. 40 part” and the entry “ 0 22,000”
shall respectively be substituted.
