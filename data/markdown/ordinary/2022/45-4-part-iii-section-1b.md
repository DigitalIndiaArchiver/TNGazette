ANIMAL HUSBANDRY, DAIRYING, FISHERIES AND FISHERMEN WELFARE  DEPARTMENT
Amendment to the Special Rules  for the Tamil Nadu Fisheries Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 118, Animal Husbandry, Dairying, Fisheries and Fishermen Welfare (Fs-2), 14th October 2022, 
Purattasi  27, Subakiruthu, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2053]
 
No. SRO B-52/2022.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Animal Husbandry, Dairying and Fisheries  Department, 
Notification No. SRO B-16/2020, published at  page 36  of Part III—Section 1(b) of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, 
dated the 3rd June, 2020. 
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on and from the  3rd June, 2020.
Amendment
In the said Notification, in the preamble portion, in para 1, for the entries "(Section 14 of Tamil Nadu Service Manual 
Volume II of 1987)", the entries "(Section 14 - Volume II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 2016)" shall be substituted.
