In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 180 of the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act, 
1983 (Tamil Nadu Act 30 of 1983), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments 
to the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Sugar Mills Managerial Common Cadre System Rules, 2018 published 
with the Industries Department Notiﬁ cation No. SRO A-55(b)/2018, published at Page Nos. 1-20 of
Part III –Section 1(a) of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 10th November, 2018.

In the said Rules,-
 
(1) for the expressions “Cadre Authority”, wherever it occurs, the expression “Common Cadre 
Authority” shall be substituted; 
 
(2) for the expressions “Boiler Operation Engineer”, “Boiler Operation Engineering” and “Boiler 
Operation Engineering qualiﬁ cation” wherever they occur, the expression “Certiﬁ cate of Boiler Operation 
Engineer” shall be substituted; 
 
(3) in rule 3, in sub-rule (g), for the expression “Cadre Controlling Authority”, the expression “Common 
Cadre Authority” shall be substituted; 
 
(4) in rule 8, in the tabular column, under Category-B,-
 
 
(a) after the entry “Deputy Chief Chemist” in column (1) and the corresponding entries relating 
thereto in column (2), the following entry shall, respectively, be inserted, namely:-
 
 
“Cane Development Oﬃ  cer 1 per Mill 18”;
