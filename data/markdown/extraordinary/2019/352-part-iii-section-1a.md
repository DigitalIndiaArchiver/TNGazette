The following amendments are made to the Law Officers of High Court of Madras and its Bench at Madurai 
(Appointment) Rules, 2017:-
2. The amendments hereby made shall come into force on the 4th September 2019.
Amendments
In the said Rules,-
(1) in rule 2, in clause (a), after the words “in the High Court”, the words “or in the Supreme Court” shall be added ;
(2) in rule 4, in clause (i), –
	
(a) in sub-clause (b), after the words “Public Prosecutor”, the words “or Additional Public Prosecutor” shall be inserted ;
	
(b) after sub-clause (g), the following sub-clauses shall be added namely:-
	
“(h) as an Additional Advocate General unless he has appeared in at least ten cases per year or cumulatively seventy 
cases for the last seven years in the High Court or Supreme Court ;
	
(i)	 as a Government Pleader or Public Prosecutor or Special Government Pleader or Additional Public Prosecutor 
unless he has appeared in at least five cases per year or cumulatively thirty five cases for the last seven years in the High 
Court ;
	
(j)	 as an Additional Government Pleader unless he has appeared in at least two cases per year or cumulatively ten 
cases for the last five years in the High Court ;

(k)	 as a Government Advocate unless he furnishes a letter given by his senior to the effect that he has assisted him 
and worked in the High Court for not less than three years”;
(3) in rule 5, –
	
(a) in sub-rule (3), after the word ‘applications’, the expression “in the format prescribed in Annexure-I” shall be inserted ;
	
(b) in sub-rule (7), for the word “reputation”, the following words shall be substituted namely :-
“appearances in court, advocacy, legal acumen, quality of drafting pleadings, reported and unreported Judgments, 
academic background, behavior, general reputation” ;
