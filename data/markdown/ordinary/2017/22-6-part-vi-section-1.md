GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
Final Closing and Cancellation of Registration in certain Co-operative Societies in Chennai District
No. XNC-541. The Southern Railway Employees Consumer Co-operative Stores Ltd.
(R.c. No. 2956/2012/NuKu.2)
No.VI(1)/166/2017.
“In the Circumstances stated by the Co-operative Sub-Registrar (Prosecution and Liquidation) in his Final Closure
Report dated 18-01-2017 and in exercise of the powers delegated under Section 140(1) of the Tamil Nadu Co-Operative
Societies Act 1983, the Registration of The Southern Railway Employees Consumer Co-operative Stores Ltd.
No. XNC-541. has been cancelled with effect from 12-5-2017 and the affairs of The Southern Railway Employees
Consumer Co-operative Stores Ltd. No.XNC-541 has been completely wound up”.
No. X-371. The Saint George Orphanage School Student Co-operative Stores
(R.c. No. 1304/2013/NuKu.2)
No.VI(1)/167/2017.
“In the Circumstances stated by the Co-operative  Sub-Registrar (Prosecution and Liquidation) in his Final Closure
Report dated 18-01-2017 and in exercise of the powers delegated under Section 140(1) of the Tamil Nadu Co-Operative
Societies Act 1983, the Registration of The Saint George Orphanage School Student Co-operative Stores No. X-371 has
been cancelled with effect from 12-5-2017 and the affairs of Saint George Orphanage School Student Co-operative Stores
No. X-371 has been completely wound up”.
No. XNC-800. The Tarapore Leganathan Higher Secondary School for Girls and Staff and Students
Co-operative Stores Ltd.
[R.c. No.1304/2013/NuKu.2(9)]
No.VI(1)/168/2017.
“In the Circumstances stated by the Co-operative Sub-Registrar (Prosecution and Liquidation) in his
Final Closure Report dated 18-01-2017 and in exercise of the powers delegated under Section 140(1) of the Tamil Nadu
Co-Operative Societies Act 1983, the Registration of The Tarapore Leganathan Higher Secondary School for Girls and
Staff and Students Co-operative Stores Ltd. No. XNC-800 has been cancelled with effect from 12-5-2017 and the affairs
of The Tarapore Leganathan Higher Secondary School for Girls and Staff and Students Co-operative Stores Ltd.
No. XNC-800. has been completely wound up”.
Chennai-600 108,
V. SELVARAJ,

JUDICIAL NOTIFICATIONS
Conferment of Powers
(Roc.No.34938/2017-B6)
No.VI(1)/169/2017.
No. 79/2017.—In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 13 of the Code of Criminal Procedure 1973
(Cenral Act 2 of 1974), the High Court hereby appoints the following 8 Deputy Tahsildars in Salem District for a period
of 120 days, from the date of assumption of charge and confers upon them the ordinary powers conferrable under
this Code on a Judicial Magistrate to try such cases which were earlier triable and dealt with by an erstwhile Judicial
Second Class Magistrate.
