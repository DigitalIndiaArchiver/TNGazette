GENERAL  NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAQF BOARD, CHENNAI-600 001.
Framing of Bye Law in Respect of the Sunnath Jamath Mosque @ Sheik Kairullah Mosque situated 
in T.S. No. 1211 of Division No. 2 of Kancheepuram Municipality, Gandhi Road, Little Kancheepuram,  
Kancheepuram District.
(Lr. Rc.No.12523/12/B4/KPM)
No. VI-3(a)/72/2022.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 69 of the Waqf Act, 1995, the Tamil Nadu 
Wakf Board in its Resolution in item No. 133/22 in Rc. No. 12523/12/B4/KPM dated : 29-06-2022 has approved 
the BYE LAW for the Waqf in respect of THE SUNNATH JAMATH MOSQUE @ SHEIK KAIRULLAH MOSQUE 
(G.S. No. 93/KPM) SITUATED IN T.S. No. 1211 OF DIVISION No. 2 OF KANCHEEPURAM MUNICIPALITY, GANDHI 
ROAD, LITTLE KANCHEEPURAM. KANCHEEPURAM DISTRICT for better administration of the Waqf.
Chennai,	
A.B. RAFIULLAH,
9th September 2022.	
Chief Executive Officer,
	
Tamil Nadu Waqf Board.
Framing of  Scheme in Respect of Sheik Ismail Subedar Mosque Waqf, Trichy District.
(Lr. Rc.No.14961/17/B5/TRY)
No. VI-3(a)/73/2022.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under section 69 of the Waqf Act, 1995, the Tamil Nadu 
Waqf Board in its Resolution in item No. 158/22 in Rc. No. 14961/17/B5/TRY dated : 10-08-2022 has approved the 
Scheme framed for the Waqf in respect of Sheik Ismail Subedar Mosque Waqf, Trichy District for better administration 
of the Waqf.
Chennai,	
A.B. RAFIULLAH,
9th September 2022.	
        Chief Executive Officer,
	
        Tamil Nadu Waqf Board.
