APPOINTMENT OF MINISTER FOR THE STATE OF TAMIL NADU
[G.O. Ms. No.  1021, Public (Special-B), 9th August 2015,  Aadi  24, Manmadha, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2046.]
No.  I/PUSP/11(c-1)/2015.
I, K. Rosaiah, Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoint Thiru M.S.M. Anandan, with effect from the afternoon of
9th August 2015 to be the member of my Council of Ministers.
K. ROSAIAH,
Governor of Tamil Nadu.
ALLOCATION OF SUBJECTS AMONG MINISTER
[G.O. Ms. No.  1022, Public (Special-B), 9th August 2015,  Aadi  24, Manmadha, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2046.]
No. I/PUSP/11(c-2)/2015.
Under Clause (3) of Article 166 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby allocates  the
subject Forests to Thiru M.S.M. Anandan, appointed as a Minister in G.O. Ms. No. 1021, Public (Special-B), 9th August 2015.
Consequent  on  the above allocation, Thiru M.S.M. Anandan be designated as Minister for Forests.
K. GNANADESIKAN,
Chief Secretary.
