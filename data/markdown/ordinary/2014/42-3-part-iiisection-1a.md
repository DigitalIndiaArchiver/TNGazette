Amendments to the Tamil Nadu Prison Rules.
[G.O. Ms. No. 770, Home (Prison-V), 14th October 2014, ¹ó†ì£C 28, üò, ÂUtŸSt® M©L-2045.]
No. SRO A-28/2014.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 59 of the Prisons Act, 1894 (Central Act IX of 1894)
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu Prison Rules, 1983, as subsequently
amended:—
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules,–
(1) in rule 20, in sub-rule (8), for clause (b), the following clause shall be substituted, namely:—
"(b) He shall visit the District Jails under his control atleast once in a month and inspect them half yearly and make
surprise visits to the District Jails and Sub-Jails under his control, as and when required".; and
(2) in rule 30, after sub-rule (33), the following sub-rule shall be added namely:—
"(34) (a) When working as Superintendent in District Jails, he shall visit the Sub-Jails/Special Sub-Jails/Borstal Schools/
Open Air Jails in the respective District once in a month, inspect them half yearly and make surprise visits to them as and
when required and shall submit his reports to the respective Superintendent of Prisons.
(b) When working in Central Prison, he shall visit the Sub-Jails/Special Sub-Jails/Borstal Schools/Open Air Jails in the
Districts under his control, once in a month, inspect them half yearly and make surprise visits to them, as and when required
and shall submit his reports to the respective Superintendent of Prisons".
APURVA VARMA,
Principal Secretary to Gover n ment.
LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT DEPARTMENT
Draft Amendment to the Tamil Nadu Industrial Employment (Standing Orders) Rules.
[G.O. Ms. No. 74, Labour and Employment (G1), 18th July 2014, Aadi 2, Jaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2045.]
No. SRO A-29/2014.—The following draft of an amendment to the Tamil Nadu Industrial Employment (Standing Orders)
Rules, 1947. Which it is proposed to make in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-sections (1) and (2) of Section 15
of the Industrial Employment (Standing Orders) Act, 1946 (Central Act XX of 1946), is hereby published for information of all
persons likely to be  affected thereby as required by sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the said Act.
2.
Notice is hereby given that the draft amendment, will be taken into consideration on or after the expiry of two months
from the date of publication of this Notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette and that any objection or suggestion,
which may be received from any person with respect thereto, before the expiry of the aforesaid period will be considered
by the Government of Tamil Nadu. Objection or Suggestion, If any, should be addressed to the  Principal Secretary to
Government, Labour and Employment Department, Government of Tamil Nadu, Chennai-600 009 through the Commissioner
of Labour, Chennai-600 006.
DRAFT AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in the "Model Standing Orders Applicable to Workmen under the Schedule I, In paragraph 17,
in clause (4), after sub-clause (bb), the following sub-clause shall be inserted namely:-
"(bbb)-In the inquiry, the workman shall be entitled to appear in person or to be represented by an office-bearer
of a trade union of which he is a member".
MOHAN PYARE,
Principal Secretary to Government.
