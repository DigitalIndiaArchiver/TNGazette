In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following rules:—
(2) The rules hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on and from the
7th August 2008.
RULES
The Special Rules applicable to the holders of the permanent posts in the Tamil Nadu Basic Service
shall apply to the holders of the temporary post of Marker sanctioned from time to time, in the
Government Law Colleges in the Department of Legal Studies subject to the modifications specified in
the following Rules:—
2. Constitution.-The Post shall constitute a separate category in a distinct class of the said service.
3. Appointment.- Appointment to the post shall be made by direct recruitment.
4. Appointing Authority.- The appointing authority for the post shall be the Principal of the
Government Law College concerned.
5. Qualifications.- (a) Age.- No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post by direct
recruitment, if he has completed or will complete fifty one years of age on the first day of July of the
year in which the selection for appointment is made:—
Provided that candidates belonging to Scheduled Castes/Scheduled Tribes shall be eligible for
appointment to the post, if he has not completed fifty six years of age and candidates belonging to
Most Backward Classes / Denotified Communities shall be eligible for appointment to the post, if he has
not completed fifty three years of age.
(b) Other qualifications.- No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post, unless he
is able to read and write in Tamil.
6. Savings.- Nothing contained in these rules shall adversely affect the holders of the post on the
date of coming into force of these rules.
7. Pay.- There shall be paid to the holder of the post, a monthly pay calculated in the Pay Band of
Rs. 4800-10000 with Grade Pay of Rs. 1300.
G. JAYACHANDRAN,
Secretary to Government.

AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules, in Class LXXVIII,—
(1) for the expression “Personal Assistant (Household Supervision)” , the expression “Senior Superintendent”
shall be substituted;
