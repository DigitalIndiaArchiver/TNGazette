 (S.O. No. 4/2020/TNSEC/ME-1, dated the 27th February, 2020.)
No. VI(2)/111(a-1)/2021.
In exercise of the powers conferred under rule 12, rule 15, rule 16 and rule 18 of the Tamil Nadu Town 
Panchayats, Municipalities and Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006 and in supersession of the Notification 
No. VI(2)/9(a)/2020, published at pages 1-7 of Part VI–Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette 
Extraordinary No. 87, dated the 28th February, 2020, the State Election Commission hereby directs that,-
1. the polling stations for the purpose of conducting poll under the said Rules shall be provided by adopting the 
polling stations used in the recently held Assembly Elections and only in cases where it becomes absolutely necessary, 
changes as per requirement of individual 	wards may be carried out to create new polling station taking into account 
the field situation and in the manner prescribed in the Instructions–2021 issued in the form of Handbook titled 
"நகர்ப்புற உள்ளாட்சி ேதர்தல்கள்-கையேடு-1-வாாக்குச்சாாவடிகள் அமைத்தல் மற்றும் வாக்காள
ர் பட்டியல் தயாாரிததலுக்காான 
அறிவுரைகள்-2021". 
2. the approved list of polling stations shall be published by the Returning Officer at the following places namely:-
	
(i)	
Office of the District Election Officer,
	
(ii)	
Office of the Town Panchayat, Municipality and Corporation
	
(iii) 	such other places as the Returning Officer may consider necessary;
3. the number of names of electors to be included in any part of the electoral roll and the manner of preparing 
the electoral roll, sketch of the polling station, the title and the specimen of the certificate to be given by the concerned 
Urban Local Body Electoral Registration Officer at the end of each part shall be as indicated in the Annexure I, II(a), 
II(b), II(c), III, IV, V(a) and V(b) respectively appended to this notification;
4. the every part of the electoral roll shall have the sketch of the polling area enclosed next to the title page;

6. two copies of the electoral roll shall be supplied free of cost to the recognised political parties as notified by 
this Commission from time to time.

Corporations and around 1400 in the case of Chennai, 
Madurai and Coimbatore Corporations and where only 
one polling station has been provided 
In one part.
2.
in a ward voting strength exceeding 1200 electors in the 
case of Town Panchayats / Municipalities / Corporations 
except Chennai, Madurai and Coimbatore Corporations 
and exceeding 1400 electors in the case of Chennai, 
Madurai and Coimbatore Corporations but not exceeding 
2400 electors or 2800 electors as the case may be and 
where two polling stations have been provided
(a) in one part, if one of the polling station is exclusively 
for men electors and other is exclusively for women 
electors.
(b) in two parts, if both the polling stations are for all 
electors.
3.
in a ward voting strength exceeding 2400 electors in the 
case of Town Panchayats / Municipalities / Corporations 
except Chennai, Madurai and Coimbatore Corporations 
and exceeding 2800 electors in the case of Chennai, 
Madurai and Coimbatore Corporations but not exceeding 
3600 electors or 4200 electors as the case may be and 
where three polling stations have been provided
(a) in two parts, if one of the polling stations is for all 
electors and remaining two are for men and women 
electors separately.
(b) in three parts, if all the three polling stations are 
provided for all electors.
