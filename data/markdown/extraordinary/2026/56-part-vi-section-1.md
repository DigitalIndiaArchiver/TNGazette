Maintenance, Chennai and after having considering the cause shown by the owner or other persons having interest on 
such lands if any, as the case may be, do hereby publish the following notice under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the 
Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002).

of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands specified in the Schedule below measuring an extent of 38283 Sq.mtrs., to the

SIPCOT Phase – I for “Formation of Link Road Connectivity from Valayakaranai to Manampathy” at No.09.
Vengaram Village, Uthiramerur Taluk, Kancheepuram District.
