In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 43 read with sub-section (2) of Section 46 of the Real 
Estate (Regulation and Development) Act, 2016 (Central Act 16 of 2016), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby constitutes the 
Tamil Nadu Real Estate Appellate Tribunal and appoints Hon’ble Justice Thiru. B. Rajendran, (Retired), High Court of Madras 
as the Chairperson of the said Tribunal, in Consultation with the Chief Justice of High Court, Madras.
