Variation to the Approved Mettur Detailed Development Plan Part III-B of Mettur Local Planning Area.
(Roc. No. 22244/2005/DP1.)
No. VI(1)/327/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section(1) of Section 33 of Town and Country Planning Act, 1971
(Act No. 35 of 1972) the Director of Town and Country Planning, Chennai-2 in his Proceedings Roc. No. 22244/2005/DP1,
dated 19th August 2010, proposes to make the following variation to the Mettur Detailed Development Plan
Part III-B of Mettur Local Planning Area.  Approved vide proceedings Roc. No. 25895/1986/DP1, dated, 30th September
1986 of Director of Town and Country Planning and published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, Part VI—Section 1
page No. 96, dated 9th March 1988.
2.
Any person affected or interested in this draft variation may within SIXTY days from the date of publication
of this notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette can represent in person or submit in writing to the Member-
Secretary, Mettur Local Planning Authority any objections and suggestions relating thereto.
3.
The variation with plan may be inspected at free of cost at any time during office hours at the above said
Local Planning Authority Office.
VARIATION
1. The entire Mettur Detailed Development Plan Part III-B of Mettur Local Planning Area is comprehensively varied
by the Director of Town and Country Planning vide Proceedings Roc. No. 22244/2005/DP1, dated 19th August 2010.
2. The Plan thus varied is numbered as Map No. 5 DDP(V)/DTCP No. 29/2010.
3. The Detailed Development clauses and schedules annexed with this plan are made enforceable from the date
of publication of the confirmed variation notification to be issued under Section 33(2) of the Act and published in the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
Chennai-2,
PANKAJ KUMAR BANSAL,
19th August 2010.
Director of Town and Country Planning.
