In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Districts Limits Act, 1865 (Tamil Nadu Act 1 of 
1865), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that with eﬀ ect on and from 12th November 2019.
  
(i) Kancheepuram District is bifurcated into Kancheepuram and Chengalpattu Districts as follows:-
     
 a. Kancheepuram District with Head Quarters at Kancheepuram.
     
 b. Chengalpattu District with Head Quarters at Chengalpattu.
  
(ii) Two Divisions of Kancheepuram &  Sriperumbudur (New) and Five Taluks of Kancheepuram, Uthiramerur, 
Sriperumbudur, Walajabad & Kundrathur (New) shall constitute the Kancheepuram District.
  
(iii)  Three Divisions of Chengalpattu, Maduranthagam & Tambaram and Eight Taluks of Chengalpattu,  Maduranthagam, 
Cheyyur, Thirukalukundram, Thiruporur, Tambaram, Pallavaram & Vandalur (New) shall constitute the Chengalpattu New 
District.
