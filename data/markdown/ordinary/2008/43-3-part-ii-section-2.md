HIGHER EDUCATION DEPARTMENT
Vesting of properties and scheme for administration
relating to "Physics Honours and PG Class of 1957
Golden Jubilee Charitable Endowment Fund” tenable
in the Presidency College, Chennai under Charitable
Endowments Act.
[G.O.Ms. No. 389, Higher Education (D2),
23rd September 2008, Purattasi 7, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2039.]
No. II(2)/HE/516/2008.—In the matter of the Charitable
Endowments Act, 1890 (Central Act VI of 1890) and in the
matter of “Physics Honours and PG Class of 1957 Golden
Jubilee Charitable Endowment Fund” (hereinafter referred to
as “the said Endowment”) tenable in the Presidency College,
Chennai-600 005.
I. VESTING OF PROPERTIES
Whereas, an application has been made to the Government
to Tamil Nadu, under Clause (b) of sub-section (1) of Section 6
of the Charitable Endowments Act, 1890 (Central Act VI of
1890) by Dr. S. Srinivasan and L.V. Krishnan, B6, Madhurima
Apartments, No. 32, Conransmith Road, Gopalapuram,
Chennai-86, the donors of the said Endowment, for vesting
the property specified in the Schedule below, in the Treasurer
of Charitable Endowments, Chennai, appointed under Section 3
of the said Act for the State of Tamil Nadu, The Governor
of Tamil Nadu, in exercise of the powers conferred by
Section 4 of the said Act, hereby orders that the said property
be vested in the Treasurer aforesaid, and be held by him
and his successors-in-office, subject to the provisions of the
said Act and Rules which, from time to time, made or shall
be deemed to have been made thereunder and subject to
the conditions set forth in the Scheme settled under Section
5 of the said Act, for the administration of the said Endowment.
THE SCHEDULE
The Property
Rs. 50,000/- (Rupees Fifty thousand only)
II. SCHEME FOR ADMINISTRATION
Whereas, an application has been made to the Government
of Tamil Nadu under Clause (b) of sub-section (1) of Section 6
of the Charitable Endowments Act, 1890 (Central Act VI of
1890) by Dr. S. Srinivasan and L.V. Krishnan, B6, Madhurima
Apartments, No. 32, Conransmith Road, Gopalapuram,
Chennai-86, the donors of the said Endowment Specified
above, for setting a Scheme for administration of the said
Endowment, the Governor of Tamil Nadu, in exercise of the
powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the
Charitable Endowments Act, 1890 (Central Act VI of 1890),
hereby settles the Scheme set forth in the Schedule below,
for the administration of the said Endowment, and appoints
the Principal, Presidency College, Chennai-5 to administer
the property. The Scheme shall come into operation from
the date of publication of this Notification in the Tamil Nadu
Government Gazette.
THE SCHEDULE
1. The Fund shall be called "Physics Honours and PG
Class of 1957 Golden Jubilee Charitable Endowment Fund"
tenable in the Presidency College, Chennai-5.
2. The annual interest accruing to the above mentioned
amount shall be provided for the Endowment Prize, which
shall be in the form of an award of a Medal and / or books
and a certificate to the First Rank holder in M.Sc. (Physics)
in the University Examinations from Presidency College,
Chennai. In the case of the Medal, the words "Physics
Honours and PG Class of 1957 Medal” shall be inscribed
on the Medal, alongwith the name of the Awardee. In case,
if more than one student get the First Rank, the Prize amount
shall be divided equally among them.
3. The unutilized amount of the interest shall be added
to the corpus of the Fund.
4. The amount of the said Fund shall be invested from
time to time and re-invested in Government Securities.
5. The decision of the Administrator shall be final in all
matters relating to the administration of the said Fund.
K. GANESAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.

(U.D1), 20th October 2008.]
No. II(2)/HOU/520/2008.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by Section 113 of the Tamil Nadu Town and
Country Planning Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby exempts the land in the
approved residential layout of Tamil Nadu Housing Board at
S.Nos. 738 part, 739 part, 742 and 743 part of Perambur
Village, Central Avenue Road, M.K.B. Nagar, Vyasarpadi,
Chennai from the provisions of sub-clause (i) of Clause (a)
of sub-regulation (1) of Regulation 24 of the Development
Regulations of Second Master Plan for Chennai Metropolitan
Area 2026 for the construction of four blocks of Stilt+10 floors
multistoreyed buildings for residential purpose, (440 dwelling
units-Block A to D) one block of Stilt + 2 floors for shops
(Block E) and a service block in the above land by the
Tamil Nadu Housing Board subject to the following conditions,
namely:—
(i) The Tamil Nadu Housing Board shall furnish patta for
S.No. 742 of Perambur Village to Chennai Metropolitan
Development Authority; and
(ii) The Tamil Nadu Housing Board shall obtain approval
for revision of layout for dereservation from residential to
commercial purpose for construction of shops (Area 763 Sq.mt)
(Block E) from the Chennai Metropolitan Development
Authority.
