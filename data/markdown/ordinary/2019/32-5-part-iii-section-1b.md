In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following Rules.
Rඝඔඍඛ
The provisions contained in the Tamil Nadu Government Servants (Conditions of Service) Act, 2016 (Tamil Nadu Act, 
14 of 2016) and the Special Rules applicable to the holders of the permanent posts of Foremen Instructor in category 2 of 
class III in th Tamil Nadu Technical Education Subordinate Service, shall apply to the holders of the the temporary post  of 
Foremen instructor in the Government Polytechnic Colleges (other than in Special Institutions) sanctioned from time to time, 
in the Department of Technical Education, subject to the modiﬁ cations speciﬁ ed in the following rules:-
(2)
Constitution.- The Post shall constitute a separate category in a distinct class of the said Service.
(3)
Appointment.- Appointment to the post shall be made as follows:-
(i)
Promotion from Workshop instructor in Government Polyechnic Colleges and Special Institutions;
(ii)
If no qualiﬁ ed and suitable candidates are available for appointment by method (i) above then by 
direct recruitment.
(4)
Appointing Authority.-The appointing authority for the post shall be the Additional Director of Technical 
Education (Polytechnics).
(5)
Qualiﬁ cations.-
(a)
Age:-No. Person shall be eligible for appointment to the post by direct recruitment, if he has completed 
or will complete 36 years of age on the ﬁ rst day of July of the year in which the vacancy is notiﬁ ed.
(b)
Other Qualiﬁ cations.-No Person shall be eligible for appointment to the post unless he possesses 
the following qualiﬁ cations, namely:-
(i)
(a) 
A Diploma in any branch of Engineering/Technology; and
(b)
Practical experience for a period of not less than one year in a recognized Workshop/Lab;
or
(ii)
(a)
"An Industrial Training Institute Certiﬁ cate/National Trade Certiﬁ cate/National Apprenticeship 
Training Certiﬁ cate in any trade; and
(b)
Practical experience for a period of not less than ﬁ ve years in a recognized Workshop/Lab.
*Explanation:
''A Recognised Workshop or Factory'' means a Workshop or Factory satisfying the deﬁ nition of 
Factory in Section 2(m) of the Factories Act, 1948 (Central Act LXIII of 1948) and includes a 
Workshop or Laboratory attached to a Technical Educational Institution. Hence, if Candidates 
have practical experience in Factory, then the experience Certiﬁ cate should be obtained from 
Head of the Factory and if candidates have practical experience in Workshop or Laboratory 
in Technical Educational Institutions, then the experience certiﬁ cate should be obtained from 
the Principal of the Concerned Institution''
(6)
Reservation of Appointments.- The Provisions contained in Section 27 of the Tamil Nadu Government 
Servants (Conditions of Service) Act, 2016 (Tamil Nadu Act 14 of 2016) shall apply for Appointment to the 
post by direct recruitment.
(7)
Probation.- Every person appointed to the post by direct recruitment shall, from the date on which he joins 
duty, be on probation for a total period of two years, on duty within a continuous period of three years.
(8)
Pay.- The holders of the post shall be paid a monthly pay calculated in the level pay of Rs. 35,400-1,12,400.
