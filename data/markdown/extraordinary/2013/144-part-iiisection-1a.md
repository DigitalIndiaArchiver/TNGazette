In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 16 of the Tamil Nadu Entertainments Tax Act, 1939 (Tamil Nadu Act X of
1939), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu Entertainments Tax Rules, 1939:-
2. The amendments hereby made shall come into force on  the 29th May, 2013.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules,-
(1) in rule 21-G,-
(a) in sub-rule (1), after the expression “The Indian Premier League conducting any cricket tournament”, the
expression “or the Board of Control for Cricket in India conducting the Champions League Twenty 20 cricket
tournament” shall be inserted;
(b) in sub-rule (4), after the expression “Indian Premier League”, the expression “or the Board of Control for
Cricket in India conducting the Champions League Twenty 20 cricket tournament” shall be inserted;
(c) in sub-rule (6), after the expression “Indian Premier League conducting any cricket tournament”, the
expression “or the Board of Control for Cricket in India conducting the Champions League Twenty 20 cricket
tournament” shall be inserted;
(d) in sub-rule (7), after the expression “The Indian Premier League conducting any cricket tournament”, the
expression “or the Board of Control for Cricket in India conducting the Champions League Twenty 20 cricket tournament”
shall be inserted;
