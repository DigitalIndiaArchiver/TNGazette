In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (b) of sub-section (3) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Catering
Establishments Act, 1958 (Tamil Nadu XIII of 1958), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that the said
Act shall come into force on 01-06-2017 in all the areas in the State of Tamil Nadu, where the provisions
of said Act have not been brought into force so far by invoking clauses (a) and (b) of sub-section (3) of
Section 1 of the said Act.
