Whereas it has become expedient to notify the list of additional free symbols for the use in the local body elections, where the
number of contesting candidates exceeds 30.
Now, therefore in exercise of the powers conferred by paragraphs 4 and 6 of the Tamil Nadu Local Bodies Election Symbols
(Reservation and Allotment) Order, 2011 and in continuation of the Statutory Order cited, the Commission hereby notifies a list of
additional free symbols common for all local body elections.
The list of additional free symbols for the use in the election of Members/Presidents of Village Panchayats and
Members of Panchayat Unions/District Panchayats/Members/Chairmen/Mayors and Town Panchayats/
Third Grade Municipalities and councillors of Municipalities/Corporations.
