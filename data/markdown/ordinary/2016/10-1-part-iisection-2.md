dearness allowance is linked to the Consumer price  Index

Notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, based
on the Average  Chennai City Consumer Price Index  of the
previous year, that is, from January to December.

Municipalities and Cantonments.
(iii) Zone-3 -
shall include other areas not covered in

women employees.

be multiplied by 30.
(6) The total wage packet, that is, basic wages plus dearness
allowance shall be the criterion for the purpose of compliance
of this Notification.
(7) Wherever the existing wages are higher than the
minimum wages fixed herein, the same shall be continued
to be paid.
