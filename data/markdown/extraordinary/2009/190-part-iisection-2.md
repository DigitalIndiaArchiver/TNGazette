APPOINTMENT OF CERTAIN PERSON AS MEMBER OF TAMIL NADU ELECTRICITY REGULATORY
COMMISSION UNDER THE ELECTRICITY ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 59, Energy (A1), 27th July 2009.] 
No. II(2)/EGY/367(d)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (5) of Section 82 of the Electricity Act, 2003
(Central Act 36 of 2003) and on the recommendation of the Selection Committee referred to in Section 85
of the said Act, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints Thiru K. Venugopal as Member, Tamil Nadu
Electricity Regulatory Commission.
P.W.C. DAVIDAR,
Secretary to Government.
