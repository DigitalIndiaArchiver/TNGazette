PUBLIC DEPARTMENT
(Human Rights)
AUTHORISING CERTAIN MEMBER OF STATE HUMAN RIGHTS COMMISSION TO ACT AS
THE CHAIRPERSON OF THE COMMISSION UNDER THE PROTECTION OF
HUMAN RIGHTS ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 1344, Public (Human Rights) 4th December 2013.]
No. II(2)/PUHR/913(b)/2013.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 25 of the Protection of
Human Rights Act, 1993 (Central Act No. 10 of 1994), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
authorises Tmt. Jayanthi, I.A.S., (Retired) Member, State Human Rights Commission Tamil
Nadu to act as the Chairperson of the Commission instead of Thiru K. Baskaran, until the
appointment of a new Chairperson to the Commission.
JATINDRA NATH SWAIN
Principal Secretray to Government.
