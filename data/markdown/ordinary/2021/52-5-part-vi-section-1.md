3. (i) No plaints or petitions except applications for grant of copies and for service and execution of process will ordinarily 
be received during the Vacation.

in the Civil cases alone will be received and disposed of by him in exercise of powers vested in him under Section 30 of 
Central Act III of 1873 as amended by the Tamil Nadu Act XVII of 1950.
4. The Oﬃ  ce of the Copyists, Process Service Establishment will be kept open throughout the Vacation, but no arrest 
warrants will be executed during the Vacation.
5. The Oﬃ  ce of other Sections will remain closed. Arrangements will however be made.

Constitution  of District Munsif-cum-Judicial  Magistrate  Court at Ottapidaram in Thoothukudi District.
(Roc.No.1347/A/2014/G/Judn/Phase II)
No. VI(1)/383/2021.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873) the High 
Court, Madras, hereby ﬁ xes Ottapidaram in Thoothukudi District as the place at which the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate 
Court, Ottapidaram, shall be located. 
NOTIFICATION-II
(Roc. No.1347/A/2014/G/Judn/Phase II)
No. VI(1)/384/2021.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 11 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873) 
the High Court, Madras, hereby directs that in Thoothukudi District, the District Munsif Court at Kovilpatti, District Munsif Court at 
Thoothukudi and District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Vilathikulam shall cease to have local jurisdiction over the entire 
Ottapidaram Taluk and the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Ottapidaram shall have and exercise local jurisdiction 
over the entire Taluk of Ottapidaram with eﬀ ect from the date on which the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate, Ottapidaram 
assumes charge of that Court.

No. VI(1)/385/2021.
The High Court, Madras hereby directs and notiﬁ es that the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate, Ottapidaram in 
Thoothukudi District shall exercise all the powers conferred on a District Munsif under Section 12 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts 
Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873) as amended up-to-date in regard to original suits and proceedings not otherwise exempted 
from his cognizance of which the amount or value of the subject matter does not exceed One Lakh Rupees within the local limits 
of his jurisdiction with eﬀ ect from the date on which the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate, Ottapidaram assumes charge 
of that Court. 
NOTIFICATION-IV
(Roc.No.1347/A/2014/Judn/Phase-II)
No. VI(1)/386/2021.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 28 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873(Central Act III of 1873) 
the High Court, Madras, hereby directs that the District, the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate, Ottapidaram 
shall have and exercise jurisdiction of a Court of Small Causes under the provincial Small Causes Courts Act, 1887 for the trial of 
suits cognizable by a Court of small causes upto the Pecuniary limits of Rs. 5000/- (Rupees ﬁ ve thousand only) with eﬀ ect from 
the date on which the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate, Ottapidaram assumes charge of that Court.
