NOTIFICATIONS BY GOVERNMENT
II-2 Ex.  (8)
HIGHER EDUCATION DEPARTMENT
CONSTITUTION OF SEARCH COMMITTEE TO RECOMMEND A PANEL OF THREE NAMES TO THE CHANCELLOR 
FOR APPOINTMENT OF VICE CHANCELLOR TO THE MANONMANIAM SUNDARANAR UNIVERSITY, TIRUNELVELI 
UNDER THE MANONMANIAM SUNDARANAR UNIVERSITY ACT. 
[G.O.(D). No.4, Higher Education (H2), 4th January 2022,
No. II(2)/HE/G/2022. 
Under Section 11(1) & (2) of the Manonmaniam Sundaranar University Act, 1990, and as amended in Tamil Nadu 
Universities Laws (Amendment) 2017, the Hon'ble Governor-Chancellor has constituted the Search Committee consisting  of 
the following members to recommend a panel of three names to the Hon'ble Governor-Chancellor for appointment of next 
Vice-Chancellor of Manonmaniam Sundaranar University:-
1.
Thiru. K. Allaudin, I.A.S. (Retd.),
No.31, First Street, 12th Main Road,
H-Block, Anna Nagar West,
Chennai-600 040.
Government Nominee/ 
Convener
2.
Dr. V.B. Athreya, B.Tech., Ph.D.,
Adjunct Professor,
15/8, 9th Cross Street, Sastri Nagar,
Adayar, Chennai-600 020
Senate Nominee
3.
Dr. A. Kalyani, M.A., M.Phil., Ph.D.,
Former Vice-Chancellor,
Tamil Nadu Open University,
58, II Street, Aspiran Gardens,
Kilpauk, Chennai-600 010
Syndicate Nominee
 
2. The above Committee is requested to recommend a panel of three names along with their Bio-data to the Chancellor 
of Manonmaniam Sundaranar University.
 
D. KARTHIKEYAN,
 
Principal Secretary to Government.
