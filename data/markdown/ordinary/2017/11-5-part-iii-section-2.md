CHENNAI PORT TRUST
Chennai Port Trust Employees’ (Grant of Advances for Festival and Natural Calamities)
Amendment Regulations, 2016
(RRC/9642/2015/GA)
No. SRO C-5/2017.
The following Notification GSR No. 1185 (E) dated the 30th December 2016 issued by  the  Government of India,
Ministry of Shipping  and published in Part II Section 3 sub-section (i) of the Extraordinary issue of the Gazette of India,
dated 30th December 2016 is republished hereunder as required under Section 132(2) of the Major Port Trusts Act,
1963:-
GSR No. 1185 (E).—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (i) of Section 124, read with sub-section (i)
of Section 132 of the Major Port Trusts Act, 1963 (38 of 1963), the Central Government hereby approves the Chennai
Port Trust Employees’ (Grant of Advances for Festival and Natural Calamities) Amendment Regulations, 2016, made by
the Board of Trustees for the Port of Chennai and are set out in the Schedule annexed to this Notification.
2.
The said Regulations shall come into force on date of publication of this Notification in the Official Gazette.
SCHEDULE
CHENNAI PORT TRUST
Chennai Port Trust Employees’ (Grant of Advances for Festival and Natural Calamities)
Amendment Regulations, 2016
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 28 of the Major Port Trusts Act, 1963 (38 of 1963), the Board of
Trustees of the Chennai Port hereby makes the following Regulations further to amend the Chennai Port Trust
Employees’ (Grant of Advances for Festival and Natural Calamities) Regulations, 1994, namely:-
1.
Short Title and commencement:-
(i) These Regulations may be called Chennai Port Trust Employees’ (Grant of Advances for Festival and Natural
Calamities) Amendment Regulations, 2016;
(ii) They shall come into force on the date of publication of the approval of the Central Government as required
under the provisions of Section 124 and 132 of the Major Port Trusts Act, 1963, in the Gazette of India.
2.
In the Chennai Port Trust Employees’ (Grant of Advances for Festival and Natural Calamities) Regulations, 1994
the figure “Rs. 10000” appearing in Sub-Regulation (1) of Regulation 4, shall be substituted by the figure “Rs. 20000”.
Foot Note: The principal regulations were published vide G.S.R. 56 (E), dated 02-02-1994 and amended vide
G.S.R.14 (E), dated 09/01/1996 and G.S.R. 825(E), dated 09-11-2011.
