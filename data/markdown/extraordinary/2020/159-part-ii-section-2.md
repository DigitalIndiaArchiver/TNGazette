PRELIMINARY NOTIFICATION
Whereas it appears that the appropriate Government has given Administrative Sanction that a total of  12.58.	53 hectares 
of land is required in the  Ponnakudi  Village in  Palayamkottai Taluk in  Tirunelveli District for public purpose, namely 
Tamirabarani-Karumeniyar-Nambiyar Rivers interlinking Flood Carrier Canal Project. Environmental Impact Assessment study for 
the irrigation project(i.e) Formation of flood carrier canal from Cannadian channel to the Drought Prone Areas of Sathankulam 
& Thisayanvilai by interlinking Tamirabarani, Karumeniyar & Nambiyar rivers in Tirunelveli and Tuticorin Districts in Tamil Nadu 
has been carried out by 'WAPCOS' under the Section 3(3) of the Environmental Protection Act, 1986. The land is acquired to 
wit for the formation of a Flood Carrier Canal. As the Environmental Impact Assessment Study has been carried out for this 
Irrigation Project, Social Impact Assessment shall not apply according to the provision of Sub Section 2(6) of Right to Fair 
Compensation, And Transparency in Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Act, 2013. The Requisitioning body 
had entered on the project area on  19-12-2010 in the Survey No.1/1A, on 20-12-2010 in the Survey nos.27/6A, 27/7A1 
and 27/8A1A, and on 12-1-2010 in the Survey nos.322/4B and 323/2B etc., and canal was dug for Flood carrier Project. 
No family was displaced due to the land acquisition. The Sub Collector, Tirunelveli is appointed as Administrator for the 
purpose of Rehabilitation and Resettlement. Therefore, it is notified that for the above said project in the  Ponnakudi  Village 
of Palayamkottai Taluk  in  Tirunelveli District, land measuring an extent of 12.58.53 Hec., the details of which are specified 
in the Schedule below, is under acquisition:

Land acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Act, 2013 (Central Act 30 of 2013), to all whom it may concern.
A plan of the land may be inspected in the office of the Collector and Land Acquisition Officer on any working day 
during the working hours.
The Government is pleased to authorize the requisitioning department’s Executive Engineer, WRD, Special Project Division, 
Tirunelveli and his staff to enter upon a survey land, take levels of any land, dig or bore into the sub-soil and do all other 
acts required for proper execution of their work as provided and specified in section 12 of the said Act.
Under sub-section (4) of 
section 11 of the said Act, no person shall make any transaction or cause any transaction of land i.e. sale / purchase, 
etc or create any encumbrances on such land from the date of publication of this notification in  the Tamil Nadu Government 
Gazette without prior approval of the Collector Objections to the acquisition, if any, may be filed by the person interested
