Declaration of Tirumakottai Gas Turbine Power Station of Tamil Nadu Electricity Board, situated at Tirumakottai-I
Village, Mannargudi Taluk, Tiruvarur District as a "Prohibited place" under the Act.
[G.O. No. SS.II/57-21/2010, Public (S.C.), 30th September 2011.]
No. II(1)/PUSC/19/2011.—Whereas in the opinion of the Government of Tamil Nadu, it is necessary to declare the
Tirumakottai Gas Turbine Power Station of Tamil Nadu Electricity Board, situated in the Survey Nos. 501-3A, 501-3B,
501-3C, 502-1A, 502-1B, 502-2, 504-1, 504-2A, 504-2B, 504-2C, 505-1B, 506-1A, 506-2A, 507-1A, 507-1B, 508-3A1,
508-3A2, 508-3B, 509-3A, 509-3B, 510-3, 513-0, 514-1, 514-2, 515-2A, 515-2B, 516-1A, 516-3A, 516-3B1, 517-1,
507-2, 508-4, 515-1 and 516-2 to the extent of 20.22.50 hectares at Tirumakottai-I Village, Mannargudi Taluk, Tiruvarur
District as specified in the Schedule below, to be a "prohibited place" under sub-clause (d) of clause (8) of Section 2
of the Official Secrets Act, 1923 (Central Act XIX of 1923), on the ground that information with respect thereto, or the
destruction or obstruction thereof, or interference therewith, would be useful to an enemy;
And Whereas in pursuance of clause (1) of Article 258 of the Constitution of India, the Government of India, Ministry
of Home Affairs, have in their Notification No. S.O. 1285, dated the 4th May 1963, published in the Gazette of India.
dated the 11th May 1963, entrusted to the Government of Tamil Nadu, the functions of the Central Government in relation
to any matter specified in sub-clauses (c) and (d) of clause (8) of Section 2 of the Official Secrets Act, 1923 (Central
Act XIX of 1923);
Now, Therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-clause (d) of Clause (8) of Section 2 of the Official Secrets
Act, 1923 (Central Act XIX of 1923), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby declares, with effect on and from the date of
publication of this Notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, Tirumakottai Gas Turbine Power Station of

of 20.22.50 hectares at Tirumakottai-I Village, Mannargudi Taluk, Tiruvarur District as specified in the Schedule below,
to be a "prohibited place"  for the Purposes of the said Act:—

Declaration of the Valuthur Gas Turbine Power Station, Phase-I and Phase-II of Tamil Nadu Electricity Board,
Valantharavai Village, Ramanathapuram District as a “Prohibited Place” under the Act.
[G.O. No. SS.II/59-29/2010, Public (S.C.), 10th October 2011.]
No. II(1)/PUSC/20/2011.—Whereas in the opinion of the Government of Tamil Nadu, it is necessary to declare the
Valuthur Gas Turbine Power Station, Phase-I and Phase-II of Tamil Nadu Electricity Board situated in the Survey Nos. 256,
259, 260, 269, 270 and 271 to the extent of 17.68.5 hectares at Valantharavai Village,Ramanathapuram District as
specified in the Schedule below, to be a “Prohibited Place” under sub-clause (d) of clause (8) of Section 2 of the Official
Secrets Act, 1923 (Central Act XIX of  1923), on the ground that information with respect thereto, or the destruction or
obstruction thereof, or interference therewith, would be useful to an enemy;
And Whereas in pursuance of clause (1) of Article 258 of the Constitution of India, the Government of India, Ministry
of Home Affairs, have, in their Notification No. S.O. 1285, dated the 4th May 1963, published in the Gazette of India, dated
the 11th May 1963, entrusted to the Government of Tamil Nadu, the functions of the Central Government in relation to any
matter specified in sub-clauses (c) and (d) of clause (8) of Section 2 of the Official Secrets Act, 1923 (Central Act
XIX of 1923);
Now, Therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-clause (d) of clause (8) of Section 2 of the Official Secrets
Act, 1923 (Central Act XIX of 1923), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby declares, with effect on and from the date of
publication of this Notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, the premises of Valuthur Gas Turbine Power
Station, Phase-I and Phase-II of Tamil Nadu Electricity Board, Valantharavai Village, Ramanathapuram District to the extent
of 17.68.5 hectares as specified in the Schedule below, to be a ‘prohibited place’ for the purposes of the said Act:—
