AND WHEREAS, based on the recommendation of the Delimitation Commission, the Government have 
determined the number of Councillors for the Municipal Councils of the aforesaid Municipalities under section 7 
of the said Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920 in G.O.(Ms)No.127, Municipal Administration and Water Supply Department, 
dated the 16th December 2021 and published at pages 1-2 in Part II - Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government 
Gazette, Extraordinary, dated the 16th  December 2021; 
AND WHEREAS, the Delimitation Commission constituted under the Tamil Nadu Delimitation Commission Act, 
2017 (Tamil Nadu Act 23 of 2017), has submitted its recommendations on the delimitation of territorial Wards of 
twenty eight (28) newly constituted Municipalities, to the Government, after having published the draft proposal for 
delimitation of the territorial Wards of the said Municipalities, based on the last preceding census of which the relevant 
figures have been published, for general informatlon, by inviting and processing the objections / suggestions 
received thereon; 
NOW, THEREFORE, in partial supersession of earlier Notification issued in this matter and published in the 
Director of Town Panchayats, Chennai-108 Notification No.VI(2)/160 (a-1)/2018, at relevant paqes in Part VI - Section 
2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, Extraordinary, dated the 14th December 2018 and in exercise of the 
powers conferred under sub-section (1) of section 43 of the said Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920 and the powers 
delegated to the Commissioner of Municipal Administration in G.O.(Ms.) No.133, Municipal Administration and Water 
Supply Department, dated the 6th June 1996, published in Municipal Administration and Water Supply Department, 
Notification No. II(2)/MW/1197(c-2)/96 at page 1 in Part II- Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, 
Extraordinary, dated the 6th  June 1996 and based on the recommendations of the Delimitation Commission, the
