JUDICIAL NOTIFICATIONS
Constitution of two District Munsif Courts, one each at Sendamangalam and Kumarapalayam Taluks in 
Namakkal District.
(Roc.No.72208/2017/G/Judn)
No.VI(1)/116/2020.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873) 
the High Court, Madras, hereby ﬁ xes Sendamangalam in Namakkal District as the place at which the District Munsif 
Court, Sendamangalam, shall be located. 
NOTIFICATION-II
(Roc.No.72208/2017/G/Judn)
No.VI(1)/117/2020.
 In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873) 
the High Court, Madras, hereby ﬁ xes Kumarapalayam in Namakkal District as the place at which the District Munsif 
Court, Kumarapalayam, shall be located. 
NOTIFICATION-III
(Roc.No.72208/2017/G/Judn)
No.VI(1)/118/2020.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 11 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873) 
the High Court, Madras, hereby directs that in Namakkal District, the District Munsif Court, Namakkal, shall cease to have 
local jurisdiction over the entire Sendamangalam Taluk and Kolli Hills Taluk and the District Munsif Court, Sendamangalam 
shall have and exercise local jurisdiction over the entire Taluk of Sendamangalam Taluk and Kolli Hills Taluk with eﬀ ect 
from the date on which the District Munsif, Sendamangalam assumes charge of that Court.

NOTIFICATION-IV
(Roc.No.72208/2017/G/Judn)
No.VI(1)/119/2020.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 11 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 
1873) the High Court, Madras, hereby directs that in Namakkal District, the District Munsif Court, Tiruchengode, shall 
cease to have local jurisdiction over the entire Kumarapalayam Taluk and the District Munsif Court, Kumarapalayam shall 
have and exercise local jurisdiction over the entire Taluk of Kumarapalayam Taluk with eﬀ ect from the date on which 
the District Munsif, Kumarapalayam assumes charge of that Court. 
NOTIFICATION-V
(Roc.No.72208/2017/G/Judn)
No.VI(1)/120/2020.
The High Court, Madras hereby directs and notiﬁ es that the District Munsif, Sendamangalam in Namakkal District 
shall exercise all the powers conferred on a District Munsif under Section 12 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 
(Central Act III of 1873) as amended up-to-date in regard to original suits and proceedings not otherwise exempted 
from his cognizance of which the amount or value of the subject matter does not exceed One Lakh Rupees within the 
local limits of his jurisdiction with eﬀ ect from the date on which the District Munsif, Sendamangalam assumes charge 
of that Court.
NOTIFICATION-VI
(Roc.No.72208/2017/G/Judn)
No.VI(1)/121/2020.
The High Court, Madras hereby directs and notiﬁ es that the District Munsif, Kumarapalayam in Namakkal District 
shall exercise all the powers conferred on a District Munsif under Section 12 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 
(Central Act III of 1873) as amended up-to-date in regard to original suits and proceedings not otherwise exempted 
from his cognizance of which the amount or value of the subject matter does not exceed One Lakh Rupees within the 
local limits of his jurisdiction with eﬀ ect from the date on which the District Munsif, Kumarapalayam assumes charge 
of that Court.
NOTIFICATION-VII
(Roc.No.72208/2017/G/Judn)
No.VI(1)/122/2020.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 28 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873) 
the High Court, Madras, hereby directs that the District Munsif Sendamangalam, shall have and exercise jurisdiction of a 
Court of Small Causes under the Provincial Small Cause Courts Act, 1887 for the trial of suits cognizable by a Court 
of Small Causes upto the pecuniary limits of Rs.5000/- (Rupees ﬁ ve thousand only) with eﬀ ect from the date on which 
the District Munsif Court, Sendamangalam, assumes charge of that Court.
NOTIFICATION-VIII
(Roc.No.72208/2017/G/Judn)
No.VI(1)/123/2020.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 28 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873) 
the High Court, Madras, hereby directs that the District Munsif Kumarapalayam, shall have and exercise jurisdiction of a 
Court of Small Causes under the Provincial Small Cause Courts Act, 1887 for the trial of suits cognizable by a Court 
of Small Causes upto the pecuniary limits of Rs.5000/- (Rupees ﬁ ve thousand only) with eﬀ ect from the date on which 
the District Munsif Court, Kumarapalayam, assumes charge of that Court.
