No. II(2)/EGY/647(b-1)/2012.
The Governor of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that the
land specified in the Schedule below have to be acquired for
Industrial purpose, to wit for the formation of ULTRA MEGA
POWER PROJECT, CHEYYUR and it has already been
decided, that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded
for the lands is to be paid out of the funds controlled or managed
by the Officer Authorised Signatory,  Coastal Tamil Nadu Power
Ltd., New Delhi and having considered  the cause shown by the
owners or other persons having interest the said lands, the
Joint Commissioner (Land Reforms), Villupuram concerned,
the interested party for the lands covered under Tamil Nadu
Land Reforms (fixation of ceiling on land) Act 1961, as the case
may be, do here by publish the following notice under
sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu  Acquisition of
Land  for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu
Act 10 of 1999);
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Acquisition of Land  for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997
(Tamil Nadu  Act 10 of 1999) the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby acquired the land specified in the Schedule below and
measuring an extent of  4.37.0 Hectares to the same, a little
more or less are needed for Industrial purpose. To wit for the
formation of ULTRA MEGA POWER PROJECT, 55, VEDAL
VILLAGE, CHEYYUR TALUK, Kancheepuram District.
The Plan of the land are kept in the office of Special Tahsildar,
Land Acquisition, Unit-3 ULTRA MEGA POWER PROJECT
Scheme, Cheyyur may be inspected at any time during office
hours.
