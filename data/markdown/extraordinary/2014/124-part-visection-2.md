TO SUBMIT EXPLANATION AND ACCOUNTS - DISQUALIFICATION ORDERED-FURTHER REPORT
RECEIVED- DISQUALIFICATION RECONSIDERED FOR SI.No. 336 IN S.O.No. 7/2013/TNSEC/PE-2,
DATED 26-08-2013-DELETION OF SI.No. 336 - ORDERED.]
(S.O. No. 4/2014/TNSEC/PE-2.)
No. VI(2)/151(a)/2014.
Whereas, in the S.O. read above, published as Notification No. VI(2)/169(a)/2013, at pages 1 to 37 of Part VI—Section 2 of
the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, No. 246, dated the 30th August 2013, by invoking sub-section (4) of
Section 37 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994), the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission
declared inter-alia Tmt. S. Palaniyammal, W/o. S. Sekar, President of Kolathupalayam Village Panchayat, Kodumudi Panchayat
Union, Erode District under the Table, in the Serial No. 336 disqualified for being chosen as a member or president and for being
the president for a period of three years from the date of the said Order issued based on the report of the District Election Officer/
District Collector, Erode;
2. Whereas, on the basis of the news published in one of the Tamil Daily Newspapers, Chennai Edition, dated 19-12-2013,
the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission came to know about the disqualification of the elected President of Kolathupalayam
Village Panchayat, Kodumudi Panchayat Union, Erode District and further report called for from the District Election Officer/
District Collector, Erode District;
3. Whereas, the District Election Officer/District Collector, Erode District sent a report that the candidate had already
submitted her accounts belatedly and without explanation, but it was wrongly reported earlier to the Tamil Nadu State Election
Commission that she did not submit her accounts;
4. Whereas, the Commission conducted a detailed enquiry in this matter and decided to give an opportunity to the individual
and further report from the District Election Officer/District Collector, Erode District, was called for;

5. Whereas, the District Election Officer/District Collector, Erode District, in his letter third read above recommended to
accept the explanation given by Tmt. S. Palaniyammal, W/o. S. Sekar, Kolathupalayam Village Panchayat, Kodumudi
Panchayat Union, Erode District; and
6. Whereas, based on the enquiry conducted by the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission and in view of the recommendation
of the District Election Officer/District Collector, Erode District, in the reference third read above, the Tamil Nadu State Election
Commission decided to condone the delay in submission of accounts on election expenses by Tmt. S. Palaniyammal,
W/o. S. Sekar, Kolathupalayam Village Panchayat, Kodumudi Panchayat Union, Erode District and to accept the accounts and
explanation submitted by her.
7. Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 37 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act,
1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994) read with sub-rule (1) of rule 143 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Elections) Rules, 1995, the
Tamil Nadu State Election Commission hereby directs that Serial No. 336 in the order issued in S.O. No.7/2013/TNSEC/PE-2,
dated, the 26th August 2013 and published as Notification No. VI(2)/169(a)/2013, at pages 1 to 37 of Part VI—Section 2 of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, No. 246, dated the 30th August 2013, declaring Tmt. S. Palaniyammal,
W/o. S. Sekar, Kolathupalayam Village Panchayat, Kodumudi Panchayat Union, Erode District to have been disqualified for being
chosen as a member or president and for being a president for three years shall be deemed to have been deleted.
(By Order of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission)
