GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI
Taking over of Ondipudur Sunnath Jamath Pallivasal Wakf, Coimbatore District under Direct Management.
(W.E.A. 1/10/E5/CBE & Rc. No. 13654/09/B3/CBE)
No. VI-3(a)/12/2014.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act 1995, the
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in item No. 169/10 in W.E.A.1/10/E5/CBE & Rc. No. 1365/09/B3/CBE,
dated the 24th October 2013 has ordered to take over the Ondipudur Sunnath Jamath Pallivasal Wakf Coimbatore
District under the direct management of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of one year from the date of Board's
Order (i.e.) 24-10-2013 and to appoint the Wakf Superintendent, Coimbatore as its Executive Officer.
F. ABDUL RAZICK,
Chennai-600 001,
Chief Executive Officer,
29th January 2014.
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
Extension of Period of Direct Management of Sharfunissa Begum Sahiba Wakf, Teynampet, Chennai.
(Lr. Rc. No. 9534/2010/B6/CH)
No. VI-3(a)/13/2014.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act 1995, the
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in item No. 131/13 in Rc. No. 9534/10/B6/CHN, dated the
19th November 2013 has resolved to ratify the period of direct management of Sharfunnisa Begum Sahiba Wakf,
Chennai from 29-06-2012 to 18-11-2013 and to extend the period of direct management of the Wakf for a further
period of one year from the date of Board's Order (19-11-2013).
F. ABDUL RAZICK,
Chennai-600 001,
Chief Executive Officer,
29th January 2014.
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
Publication of Framing of Scheme in Respect of Fakir Labbai Jamath Pallivasal, Krishnampet, Triplicane,
Chennai District.
(Lr. Rc. No. 3140/11/B6/CHN)
No. VI-3(a)/14/2014.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 69 of the Wakf Act 1995, the
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its Proceedings in item No. 69/13 in R.C. No. 3140/11/B6/CHN, dated 22nd August 2013
has approved the Scheme framed for the Wakf viz., Fakir Labbai Jamath Pallivasal, Krishnampet,
Triplicane, Chennai District for better administration of the Wakf.
F. ABDUL RAZICK,
Chennai-600 001,
Chief Executive Officer,
29th January 2014.
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
