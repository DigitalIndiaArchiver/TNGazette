LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT DEPARTMENT
Draft Amendment to the Inter-State Migrant Workmen (Regulation of Employment and Conditions of Service) 
(Tamil Nadu) Rules.
[G.O. Ms. No. 46, Labour and Employment (K2), 10th April 2018, ðƒ°Q 27, «ýM÷‹H, F¼õœÀõ˜ Ý‡´-2049.]
No. SRO A-21/2018.—The following draft of amendment to the Inter-State Migrant Workmen (Regulation of Employment 
and Conditions of Service) (Tamil Nadu) Rules, 1983, which it is proposed to made in exercise of the powers conferred by 
sub-section (1) of Section 35 of the inter-State Migrant Workmen (Regulation of Employment and Conditions of Service) Act, 
1979 (Central Act 30 of 1979) is hereby published as required by the said sub-section (1) of Section 35 of the said Act.
2. Notice is hereby given that the draft amendment will be taken into consideration on or after the expiry of the period of 
one month from the date of publication of this Ntoﬁ cation in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette. Any objection or suggestion 
which may be received from any person with respect thereto, before the expiry of the aforesaid period will be considered by 
the Government of Tamil Nadu. Objection or suggestion, if any, should be addressed to the Principal Secretary to Government, 
Labour and Employment Department, Secretariat, Chennai-600 009 through the Commissioner of Labour, Chennai-600 006.
