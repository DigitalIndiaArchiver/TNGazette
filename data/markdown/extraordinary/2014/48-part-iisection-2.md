ENERGY  DEPARTMENT
RECONSTITUTION OF SELECTION COMMITTEE FOR SELECTING THE CHAIRMAN OF
THE TAMIL NADU ELECTRICITY REGULATORY COMMISSION UNDER THE ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 14, Energy (D1), 27th February 2014, Maasi 15, Vijaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2045.]
No. II(2)/EGY/238(d)/2014.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 85 of the Electricity Act, 2003,
(Central Act 36 of 2003), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby reconstitute the Selection Committee consisting of
the following Members for the purpose of selecting the Chairman of the Tamil Nadu Electricity
Regulatory Commission:-
1. Thiru Justice R. Regupathi, Retired Judge of the Madras High Court
-
Chairperson
2. The Chief Secretary to Government of Tamil Nadu
-
Member
3. The Chairperson of the Central Electricity Authority
-
Member
4. The Selection Committee shall :-
(a) Finalise, the selection of the Chairman of the Tamil Nadu Electricity Regulatory Commission, within
three months from the date of receipt of copy of  the Hon’ble High Court Order dated 7th February 2014 in writ
petition Nos. 24888 and 25156 of 2012.
(b) Recommend, a panel of two names for every vacancy referred to it; and
(c) Satisfy itself, before recommending any person for appointment as Chairman of Tamil Nadu Electricity
Regulatory Commission, that such person  does not have any financial or other interest which is likely to affect
prejudicially his/her functions as Chairman of the Tamil Nadu Electricity Regulatory Commission.
RAJESH LAKHONI,
Secretary to Government.
