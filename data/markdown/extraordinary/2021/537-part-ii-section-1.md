In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 65 of the Tamil Nadu Agricultural Produce Marketing 
(Regulation) Act, 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 1989), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following 
amendment to the Schedule of the said Act.
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖග
In the said Schedule, in column (2), against the entry '' IV. Fibres'', in column (1) thereof,-
    (a) the entry ''1. Cotton (Kapas)" shall be omitted; and 
    (b) for the entry "2. Coconut coir", the entry "Coconut coir" shall be substituted.
