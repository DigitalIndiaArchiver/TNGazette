COMMERCIAL TAXES AND REGISTRATION DEPARTMENT 
EXTENSION OF TIME LIMIT TO FURNISH THE AUDIT REPORT IN ELECTRONIC FORM WW FOR 
THE FINANCIAL YEAR 2020-2021 UNDER THE TAMIL NADU VALUE ADDED TAX ACT, 2006.
[G.O. Ms. No. 185, Commercial Taxes and Registration (B1), 31st December 2021, 
Margazhi 16, Pilava, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2052.]
No. II(2)/CTR/957(c)/2021.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 86A of the Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax Act, 2006 
(Tamil Nadu Act 32 of 2006), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby speciﬁ es that the time limit to furnish the 
audit report in electronic Form WW for the ﬁ nancial year 2020-2021, as stipulated in sub-rule (1) of rule 
16-A of the Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax Rules, 2007, stands extended upto the 31st day of March, 2022.
