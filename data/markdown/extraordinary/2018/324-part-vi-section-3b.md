ELECTION OF A NOMINEE BY THE SYNDICATE TO THE COMMITTEE FOR RECOMMENDING A
PANEL OF THREE NAMES TO THE CHANCELLOR FOR APPOINTMENT OF THE VICE-CHANCELLOR.
[No.ME-2/VC-P/Syn.Nom./2018]
No.VI-3(b)/33(a)/2018. 
Hon’ble Division Bench of High Court of Madras in its Order dated 14-06-2018 in W.P.No.12788 of 2017 and 
14705 of 2017 has set aside the appointment of Dr.P.P.Chellathurai as Vice – Chancellor of Madurai Kamaraj University. 
Consequent on the orders of the Hon’ble High Court, a permanent vacancy occurred in the ofﬁ ce of the Vice-Chancellor. 
It is hereby notiﬁ ed that, according to Statute 6(2) of Chapter V of the laws of the University, Volume-I Calendar 
of the University, an election will be held for nominating a person* by the SYNDICATE by the University to serve on 
the Committee of three, who are to suggest a panel of three names to the Chancellor for the appointment of the 
Vice-Chancellor.  The election will be held in accordance with the Statutes of Chapter V and the rules relating to the 
elections to the authorities of the University.  Each member of the SYNDICATE shall have a right to nominate not 
more than one person who is not a member of any of the authorities of the University or shall not be connected 
with the University or any college or any recognized institution of the University.  Every nomination shall be in writing 
signed by the proposer who shall be a member of the SYNDICATE and seconded in writing by another member of the 
SYNDICATE called the seconder in the form prescribed.  The form prescribed may be obtained from the Registrar on 
a written requisition signed by any member of the SYNDICATE.  No nomination shall be valid unless it contains the 
signature of both the proposer and seconder and the nomination shall also be signed by the person proposed expressing 
his consent to be nominated.  Every nomination must be presented before the Registrar either by the proposer or the 
seconder or the person proposed before 03.00 p.m. on Thursday the 27th September, 2018.

* Part-II-Amendment to the Madurai Kamaraj University Act, 1965:
2. 
In Section 11 of the Madurai Kamaraj University Act 1965, for sub-section (2) including the proviso thereto, the following 
sub-sections shall be substituted, namely:
 
(2) for the purpose of sub-section (1), the Committee shall consist of-
 
 
(i) 
a nominee of the Chancellor, who shall be a retired Judge of the Supreme Court or any High Court 
or an eminent educationist:
 
 
(ii) 
a nominee of the Senate who shall be an eminent educationist;  and
 
 
(iii) a nominee of the Syndicate who shall be an eminent educationist.
Explanation – for the purpose of this sub-section, “eminent educationist” means a person – 
 
 
(i) 
Who is or has been a Vice-Chancellor of any University established by the State Government or 
Central Government; or
 
 
(ii) 
Who is a distinguished academician, with a minimum of ten years of experience as Professor in a 
State or Central University or in both taken together; or 
 
 
(iii) Who is or has been a Director or Head of any institute of national importance:
 
Provided that the person so nominated shall not be a member of any of the authorities of the University or 
shall not be connected with the University or any college or any recognized institution of the University.
