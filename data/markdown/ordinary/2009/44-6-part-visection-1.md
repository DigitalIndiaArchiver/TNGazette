NOTIFICATION No. 205/2009
No. VI(1)/326/2009.
Subject : High Court, Madras—Vacation and Holidays for the year 2010 and also increase of Court sitting hours
by 15 minutes during lunch interval—Notified.
Read :
High Court's Notification No. 223/2007, dated 14-12-2007.
In consonance with the resolution passed in the Chief Justices' Conference, 2009, it is hereby notified that from
January 2010, the court sitting hours of the Hon'ble Judges of the Madras High Court (both Principal Seat at Madras
and Madurai Bench) is increased by 15 minutes by reducing the lunch interval by 15 minutes from the existing timing
1.30 p.m. to 2.30 p.m. as 1.30 p.m. to 2.15 p.m. The revised court sitting hours will be, from 10.30 a.m. to 1.30 p.m.
and from 2.15 p.m. to 4.45 p.m.
