APPOINTMENT OF VACATION CIVIL JUDGES FOR THE SUMMER VACATION OF CIVIL COURTS
FOR THE YEAR 2016 UNDER THE TAMIL NADU CIVIL COURTS ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 348, Home (Cts-III), 28th April 2016.]
No. II(2)/HO/320(a)/2016.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 30 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central
Act III of 1873), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, in consultation with the High Court, Madras, hereby appoints the Judicial Officers
specified in column (2) of the Table below to be vacation Civil Judges for the District Courts and Subordinate courts  in
the Districts specified in the corresponding entries in column (3) thereof for the Summer Vacation of the Civil Courts for
the year 2016.
