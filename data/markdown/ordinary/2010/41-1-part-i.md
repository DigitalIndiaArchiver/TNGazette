No. I/PUSP/23/2010.—The following Notification of the
Government of India, Ministry of Law and Justice (Department
of Justice), Jaisalmer House, 26, Man Singh Road, New Delhi-
110 011, dated 13th October, 2010 is republished:—
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (1) of
Article 222 of the Constitution of India, the President, after
consultation with the Chief Justice of India, is pleased to
transfer Shri Justice Muttaci Jeyapaul, Judge, Madras
High Court, as a Judge of the Punjab and Haryana High Court
and to direct him to assume charge of his office in the Punjab
and Haryana High Court on or before 28th October, 2010.
                                         [No. K.11017/16/2010-US.II (X)]
S.K. SHRIVASTAVA,
                            Deputy Secretary to the Government of India.
                  (By order of the Governor.)
S. MALATHI,
            Chief Secretary.

No. I/PUSP/24/2010.—The following Notification of the
Government of India, Ministry of Law and Justice (Department
of Justice), Jaisalmer House, 26, Man Singh Road, New Delhi-
110 011, dated 13th October, 2010 is republished:—
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (1) of
Article 222 of the Constitution of India, the President, after
consultation with the Chief Justice of India, is pleased to
transfer Shri Justice Vinod Kumar Sharma, Judge, Punjab and
Haryna High Court, as a Judge of the Madras High Court and to
direct him to assume charge of his office in the Madras High
Court on or before 28th October, 2010.
