NOTIFICATION BY THE ELECTION COMMISSION OF INDIA
PLACE AND TIME FOR COUNTING OF VOTES FOR PRESIDENTIAL ELECTION, 2022.
No. SRO G-30/2022.
The following Notification of the Election Commission of India, New Delhi, dated 5th July 2022 (14 Ashadha, 1944 
(Saka)) is republished::-
No. 479/16/2022- In pursuance of Rule 27 of the Presidential and Vice-Presidential Elections Rules, 1974, the Election 
Commission of India hereby appoints the 21st July, 2022 (Thursday) as the day on which, and 11:00 A.M. of that day as 
the time at which, the counting of votes, for the Presidential Election, 2022, shall take place at the office of the Returning 
Officer at Room No. 63, First Floor, Parliament House, New Delhi.
(By order)
	
Suman Kumar Das,
	
Secretary,
