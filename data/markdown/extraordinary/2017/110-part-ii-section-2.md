CONSTITUTION OF FOUR FAMILY COURTS ONE EACH AT ARIYALUR, CUDDALORE,  NAGERCOIL IN
KANNIYAKUMARI AND KARUR DISTRICTS UNDER THE FAMILY COURT ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 365, Home (Courts-II), 18th April 2017, CˆF¬ó 5, «ýM÷‹H, F¼õœÀõ˜ Ý‡´-2048.]
No. II(2)/HO/295(a-1)/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (b) of sub-section (1) read with sub-section (2) of Section 3 of the
Family Courts Act, 1984 (Central Act 66 of 1984), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, after consultation with the High Court of
Madras, hereby establishes a Family Court at Ariyalur, with effect from the date of assumption of charge of the Judge of
the said Court and also hereby specifies that the jurisdiction of the said Family Court shall extend to the local limits of
the Ariyalur City, inclusive of Ariyalur Taluk.

of the said Court and also hereby specifies that the jurisdiction of the said Family Court shall extend to the local limits
of the Cuddalore City, inclusive of Cuddalore Taluk.
