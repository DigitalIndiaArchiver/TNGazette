NOTIFICATIONS BY THE ELECTION COMMISSION OF INDIA
EXTENSION OF PERIOD FOR FILING OF CLAIMS AND OBJECTIONS IN THE 
CONSTITUENCIES OF THE TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY
No. SRO G-33/2017.
The following Notiﬁ cation of the Election Commission of India, New Delhi, dated 23rd October, 2017 [1 Kartika, 1939 
(Saka)] is published: 
No. 23/NOT/ECI/FUNC/ERD-ER/2017 (Vol.II). Whereas, the Commission had announced the schedule for Special 
Summary Revision of electoral rolls with reference to 01.01.2018 as the qualifying date, vide its letter No.23/LET/ECI/FUNC/
ERD-ER/2017 (VoI.II), dated 23rd August, 2017; and 
Whereas, the period of ﬁ ling claims and objections was ﬁ xed from 3rd October, 2017 to 31st October, 2017; and 
Whereas, the draft rolls were published on 3rd October, 2017 as scheduled; and 
Whereas, the Election Commission has reviewed the ongoing exercise of Special Summary Revision of Electoral Rolls 
in the State; and 
Whereas, the Election Commission has, on such review, decided that the period of ﬁ ling claims and objections in the State 
should be extended up to 30th November, 2017, so that a Special Veriﬁ cation Drive within the ongoing Summary Revision 
can be undertaken in which Booth Level Ofﬁ cers would visit house to house for the purpose; 
Now, therefore, the Election Commission, in exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Rule 12 of the Registration 
of Electors Rules, 1960, hereby extends the period for ﬁ lling claims and objections in the State upto and including 
30th November, 2017 for ongoing Special Summary Revision of electoral rolls with reference to 01.01.2018 as the 
qualifying date.
