HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE DEPARTMENT
Amendment to Special Rules for Tamil Nadu Medical Subordinate Service
[G.O. Ms. No. 3, Health and Family Welfare (IM2-I), 5th January 2009,
Margazhi  21, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2039.]
No. SRO B-13/2009.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Medical
Subordinate Service (Section 21 of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, Volume III, 1970):—
2.
The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on 4th May 2007.
AMENDMENT
In the said Special Rules, in BRANCH-II—INDIGENOUS MEDICINE, in the Annexure, for the entries in
column (2) against ‘Category 9 Masseur’ in column (I) thereof, the following entries shall be substituted, namely:—
“(1) Must have passed Secondary School Leaving Certificate Examination or a qualification equivalent thereto; and
(2) Must have passed the one year Certificate Course in Masseur training conducted by the Government of
Tamil Nadu at Arignar Anna Government Hospital of Indian Medicine, Chennai.”
V.K. SUBBURAJ,
Principal Secretary to Government.
