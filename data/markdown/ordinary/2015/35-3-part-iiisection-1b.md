FINANCE DEPARTMENT
Amendment to the Ad-hoc Rules relating to the Post of Junior Co-operative Auditor of Department of Co-operative
Audit in Tamil Nadu Co-operative Subordinate Service.
 [G.O. Ms. No. 225, Finance (Co-operative Audit), 11th August 2015, Aadi 26, Manmadha, Thiuvalluvar Aandu-2046.]
No. SRO B-70/2015.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the rules published with the Finance Department
Notification No. SRO B-149/86, published at pages 165 to 173 of Part III — Section 1(b) of the Tamil Nadu Government
Gazette, dated the 16th April 1986.
6. The amendment hereby made, shall be deemed to have come into force with effect on and from the 27th January 2010.
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in rule 8, in sub-rule (b), in the Table, in column (3), against the method of appointment “(ii) Direct
recruitment” in column (2) and the corresponding entry the posts of “3. Junior Co-operative Auditor” in column (1),
thereof, :-
(1) for the expression commencing with the words “Must possesses B.Com. degree”, and ending with the words
“B.A. (Corporate Secretaryship) degree”, the following expression shall be substituted, namely:-
“Must possess B.B.M. Degree or B.Com. Degree or B.Com. (Computer Application) Degree or B.A. (Co-operation) Degree
or B.A. (Economics) Degree or B.A. (Corporate Secretaryship) Degree or B.B.E. Degree awarded by any University:”:
(2) after the proviso, the following proviso shall be added, namely:-
“ Provided further that nothing contained in these rules shall adversely affect the person in service who were recruited
and allotted to the Department of Co-operative Audit by holding the degree of B.B.E. and B.Com. (Computer
Application) prior to the 27th January 2010”.
K. SHANMUGAM,
Principal Secretary to Government.
