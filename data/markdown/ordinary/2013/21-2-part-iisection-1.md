Declaration of the premises of M/s. Indian Oil Corporation Limited, Repeater-cum-Catholic Protection Station
located at Arungulam Village, Tiruttani Taluk, Tiruvallur district as “prohibited place”
under the Official Secrets Act.
[G.O. No. SS.II/23-22/2012, Public (S.C), 20th May 2013.]
No. II(1)/PUSC/14/2013.—Whereas in the opinion of the Government of Tamil Nadu, it is necessary to declare the
premises of M/s. Indian Oil Corporation Limited, Repeater-cum-Cathodic Protection Station, Located at Arungulam Village
Tiruttani Taluk, Tiruvallur district to the extent of 0.24.0 hectares situated in survey No. 283/2C1B as specified in the Schedule
below as a “prohibited place” under sub-clause (d) of clause (8) of Section  2 of the Official Secrets Act, 1923
(Central Act XIX of 1923), on the ground that information with respect thereto, or the destruction or obstruction thereof,
or interference therewith, would be useful to an enemy;
And Whereas in pursuance of clause  (1) of Article 258 of the Constitution of India, the Government of India, Ministry
of Home Affairs, have in their Notification No. S.O. 1285, dated the 4th May 1963, published in the Gazette of India, dated
the 11th May 1963, entrusted to the Government of Tamil Nadu, the functions of the Central Government in relation to
any matter specified in sub-clauses (c) and (d) of clause (8) of Section 2 of the Official Secrets Act, 1923
(Central Act XIX of 1923);
Now, Therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-clause (d) of clause (8) of section 2 of the Official
Secrets Act, 1923 (Central Act XIX of 1923), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby declares, with effect on and from the date
of publication of this Notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, the premises of M/s.  Indian Oil Corporation
Limited, Repeater-cum-Cathodlic Protection Station, at Arungulam Village Tiruttani Taluk, Tiruvallur district to the extent
of 0.24.0 hectares situated in survey No. 283/2C1B  as specified in the Schedule below, to be a ‘prohibited place’
for the purposes of the said Act:-
