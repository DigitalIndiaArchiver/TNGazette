the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby publishes notice of his intention to issue, on or after the expiry of two months from the date
of the first publication of this notice in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, a notification under sub-section (2) of the said section,
authorising the carrying out of field firing and artillery practice throughout the area as specified in columns (1) and (2) in the
Schedule below during the period specified in the corresponding entry in column (3) thereof.
