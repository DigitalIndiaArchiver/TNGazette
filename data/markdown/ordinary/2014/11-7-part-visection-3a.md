Taking over of Anamalai Peria Pallivasal @ Pudu Pallivasal Wakf, Pollachi Taluk, Coimbatore District
under the Direct Management.
(Rc. No. 8147/91/B3/CBE.)
No. VI-3(a)/24/2014.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995 as amended by
Act 27/2013, the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in item No.3/14 in Rc.No. 8147/91/B3/CBE, dated 29-01-2014 has
ordered to take over the Anamalai Peria Pallivasal @ Pudu Pallivasal Wakf, Anamalai, Pollachi Taluk, Coimbatore District
under the direct Management of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of one year from the date of publication and
to appoint the Wakf Superintendent, Coimbatore as its Executive Officer.
