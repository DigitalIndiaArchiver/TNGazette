AMENDMENT TO THE TAMIL NADU VALUE ADDED TAX RULES, 2007.
[G.O. Ms. No. 136, Commercial Taxes and Registration (B1), 31st October 2013, Aippasi 14,
Vijaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2044.]
No. SRO A-34(a-1)/2013.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 80 of the Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax
Act, 2006 (Tamil Nadu Act 32 of 2006), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment
to the Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax Rules, 2007:—
2. The amendment hereby made shall come into force on the 31st October 2013.
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in rule 16-A, in sub-rule (1), for the expression “seven months”, the expression
“nine months” shall be substituted.
