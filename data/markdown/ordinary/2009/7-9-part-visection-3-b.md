Election of Six members to the Syndicate and five members to the Academic Council from
among the members of the Senate to be held on 28th March 2009.
(No. V.1/ACEL.2k9/3-2009/69.)
No. VI-3(b)/4/2009.
It is hereby notified that elections to (i) THE SYNDICATE and (ii) THE ACADEMIC COUNCIL will be held under
Section 18(a)—Other Members Sub-Section (1) and Section 23(a)—Other Members, Sub-Section (1) of the Madras
University Act, 1923 respectively.
(i) To the Syndicate [Section 18(a)—Other Members Sub-Section (1).]
Six Members to be elected by the Senate from among its members.
The newly elected members will hold office as Member of the Syndicate for a period of three years from the date of receipt
of nomination/the date of withdrawal/the date of vacancies arises/the date of election/the date of declaration of results
whichever is applicable for the election.
(ii) To the Academic Council [Section 23(a)—Other Members Sub-Section (1).]
Five members to be elected by the Senate from among its members who are not engaged in teaching.
The newly elected members will hold office as member of the Academic Council for a period of three years from the date
of receipt of nomination/the date of withdrawal/the date of election/the date of declaration of results whichever is applicable
for the election.
The elections will be held in accordance with the Statutes of Chapter XIX, Volume-1, University Calendar 2001.
The above elections will be conducted on the date of the meeting of the Senate scheduled to be held on Saturday,
the 28th March 2009.
Nomination forms can be had from the Registrar-in-charge, University of Madras on requisition and the filled in Nomination
Forms shall be sent in an envelope, duly superscribed, addressed to the Registrar-in-charge by name [Dr. M. RANGANATHAM]
as per the below furnished programme fixed for the said elections.
