Amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Jail Subordinate Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 580, Home (Prison-II), 11th August 2016, Ý® 27, ¶¡ºA, F¼õœÀõ˜ Ý‡´-2047.]
No. SRO B-50/2016.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Jail
Subordinate Service (Section 17 in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1986):-
AMENDMENT
In the said Special Rules, in PART-II, under the heading "Branch-I Executive Staff", for rule 5, the following rule shall be
substituted, namely:—
"5. Qualification, Sex.—(a) No man shall be eligible for appointment to any post in the Women's Section.
(b) No transgender shall be eligible for appointment by direct recruitment to the Posts of Warders in Central, District,
Special Jails, Special Sub-Jails and Sub-Jails and Petty Officer in Borstal Schools- II Grade in Category 6 of Class I (General
Section) and Women Warders - II Grade in Category 5 of Class II (Women's Section).".
Amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Fire Subordinate Service
[G.O. Ms. No. 585, Home (Police-XVII), 12th August 2016, Ý® 28, ¶¡ºA, F¼õœÀõ˜ Ý‡´-2047.]
No. SRO B-51/2016.—In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (b) of sub-section (2) of Section 27 of the
Tamil Nadu Fire Service Act, 1985 (Tamil Nadu Act 40 of 1985) read with the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Fire Subordinate
Service (Section 13 in Volume II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1969):-
AMENDMENTS
In the said Special rules, in rule 5, after sub-rule (c), the following sub-rule shall be added, namely:—
"(d) No transgenders shall be eligible for appointment by direct recruitment to the Posts of Fireman, Fireman Orderly,
Fireman Messenger, Fireman Labourer, Fireman Sentry and Fireman Attendant in Category 5 under Class II".
APURVA VARMA,
Principal Secretary to Government.
