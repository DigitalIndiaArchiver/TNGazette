ENERGY DEPARTMENT
NOTIFICATION REGARDING EXEMPTION IN RESPECT OF THE ELECTRICITY TAX PAYABLE UNDER
THE TAMIL NADU TAX ON CONSUMPTION OR SALE OF ELECTRICITY ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 79, Energy (B1), 14th September 2009.]
No. II(2)/EGY/467(d)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 14 of the Tamil Nadu Tax on Consumption
or Sale of Electricity Act, 2003 (Tamil Nadu Act 12 of 2003), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes
exemption in respect of the Electricity Tax payable under the said Act on the consumption of electricity for own
use by H.T. consumers using their captive generating plants namely, gensets for a further period from the
1st April 2009 to 30th September 2009.
P.W.C. DAVIDAR,
Secretary to Government.
