UNIVERSITY AUTHORITIES
MADURAI KAMARAJ UNIVERSITY
 Election to the Authorities of the University
(Ref. No. ME-2/S/Ele./PU/2010.)
No. VI-3(b)/23/2010.
It is hereby notified that elections shall be held  to elect one member from each of the following revenue
districts by the Chairmen of Municipal Councils and of Panchayat Union Councils from among such Chairmen and
the members of the Municipal Councils, Panchayat Union Councils and Panchayats in the districts concerned
under Section 15(a) Class III-Other members (6) of the Madurai Kamaraj University Act, 1965:—
1. Madurai District
2. Dindigul District
3. Theni District
4. Virudhunagar District
The Collectors of the districts concerned are requested to hold the election and communicate the result by
telegram on 5th July 2010.
