In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of section 6 of the Coimbatore City Municipal Corporation 
Act, 1981 (Tamil Nadu Act 25 of 1981) read with section 8 of the Thanjavur City Municipal Corporation Act, 2013 
(Tamil Nadu Act 24 of 2013), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby constitutes the following six Standing Committees for 
the purpose specified in sub-section (1) of section 6 of the said Tamil Nadu Act 25 of 1981, namely:—
	
(1) Standing Committee on Accounts;
	
(2) Standing Committee on Education;
	
(3) Standing Committee on Public Health;
	
(4) Standing Committee on Taxation and Finance;
	
(5) Standing Committee on Town Planning; and 
	
(6) Standing Committee on Works.
