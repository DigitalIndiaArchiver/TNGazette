No. I/PUSP/5/2013.—The following Notification of the
Government of India, Ministry of Personnel and Public
Grievances and Pensions, Department of Personnel and
Training, New Delhi, dated 24th September 2012 is
republished:—
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-rule (2) of
Rule 5 of the Indian Administrative Service (Cadre) Rules,
1954, and with the concurrence of the Government of
Tamil Nadu and Ministry of Home Affairs, the Central
Government hereby transfers Ms. Neha Bansal, IAS
(TN: 10) from Tamil Nadu Cadre to AGMUT cadre on the
ground of marriage to Shri Deepak Yadav, IPS (AGMUT: 11).
[No. 13017/34/2012-AIS-I]
S.S. SHUKLA,
Under Secretary to the Government of India.
(By order of the Governor)
SHEELA BALAKRISHNAN,
Chief Secretary.

No. I/PUSP/6/2013.—The following Notification of the
Government of India, Ministry of Law and Justice,
(Department of Justice), Jaisalmer House, 26, Man Singh
Road, New Delhi-110 011, dated 22nd February, 2013 is
republished:—
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (1)
of article 217 of the Constitution of India, the President is
pleased to appoint Shri Justice Chockalingam Nagappan,
Judge of the Madras High Court, to be The Chief Justice
of the Orissa High Court with effect from the date
he assumes charge of his office.
[No. K. 13027/01/2013-US-I]
