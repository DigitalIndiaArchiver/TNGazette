Variations to the Approved Master Plan for Thanjavur Local Planning Area.
(Roc. No. 27/2015/TLPA)
No.VI(1)/350/2017.
In exercise of the  powers conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 32 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning
Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972) and also in exercise of powers conferred by G O. Ms.No. 94, Housing and Urban
Development [UD(4)1] Department dated 12-06-2009 published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette
No. 27, Part II—Section 2, in Page No. 228, dated 15-07-2009, after getting the approval from the Government vide
G.O.(2D) No.138 Housing and Urban Development Department dated: 21-7-2017 the following variations are made to
the Master Plan for Thanjavur Local Planning Area approved under the said Act and published in the Housing and Urban
Development Department Notification at Page No. 879 of Part VI—Section 2 of Tamil Nadu Government Gazette,
date, 11th October 1995.
VARIATIONS
In the land use Schedules of the said Master Plan, under the heading “Thanjavur Master Plan – Zoning Schedules”
under the  heading;
1.
Residential use zone-I (a) Primary Residential use zone, the S.F.No. 111 part appears against the Zoning
No.PR-4, shall be deleted and replaced as 111pt except 111/1.
2.
Residential use zone–I (b) Mixed Residential use zone, the S.F.No.111pt appears against the  Zoning
No.MR-2, shall be deleted and replaced as 111pt except 111/1.
3.
Proposed Road (100’0’’ wide), the S.F.No.111pt appears against the Village 107 Nilagiri Therku Thottam
Village shall be deleted and replaced as 111/1 part.
