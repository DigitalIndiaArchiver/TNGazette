CHENNAI PORT TRUST
 
General Administration Department.
(RRC/7877/2017/GA)
Chennai, dated the 25th May, 2018.
NOTIFICATION
No. SRO C-19/2018.—
In exercise of the powers conferred by Regulation 5 of Chennai Port Trust Employees' (Recruitment, Seniority and 
Promotion) Regulations, 2008, the Port Trust Board vide its Resolution No. 14, dated 25-05-2018 has approved the 
amendments made to the existing Recruitment Rules for the post of Mazdoor (Civil & Project) and its promotional 
posts viz. Asst. Maistry (C&P), Maistry (C&P) and Head Maistry (C&P) which have been re-designated as Basic 
Tradesman (Junior), Basic Tradesman, Tradesman and Tradesman (Senior) respectively in the Civil Engineering Department 
as per recommendations of One Man Committee headed by  Shri D.K.  Afzulpurkar communicated by the Ministry of 
Shipping in its letter No. LB-11021/11/2007-L, Dated  09-06-2014. 
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1)(a) of 132 of the Major Port Trusts Act, 1963 (38 of 1963), 
under Schedule-Il of the Chennai Port Trust Employees' (Recruitment, Seniority and Promotion) Regulations 2008, the 
following amendments made to the existing Recruitment Rules for the Post of Mazdoor (Civil &  Project) and its 
promotional posts in the Civil Engineering Department is cause for publication. 
AMENDMENTS 
(i) In the said Schedule-Il under the said Regulations, the words "Mazdoor (C&P)" appearing in the Recruitment 
Rules at Sl. No. 62 and 63 in the Civil Engineering Department has been substituted as "Basic Tradesman (Junior)". 
(ii) In the said Schedule-II under the said Regulations, the Recruitment Rules  for the post of "Mazdoor (C&P) at 
SI. No. 63 in the Civil Engineering Department, the entries under Column No. 8 shall be substituted by the following:- 
"Must have passed 8th Standard. 
NOTE: The qualiﬁ cation regarding experience is relaxable at the discretion of the Chairman in the case of candidates 
belonging to Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes if at any stage of Selection, the Chairman is of the opinion 
that sufﬁ cient number of candidates from these communities possessing the requisite experience are not likely to be 
available to ﬁ ll up the vacancies reserved for them." 
(iii) In the said Schedule-II under the said Regulations, the words "Asst. Maistry (C&P)" appearing in the Recruitment 
Rules at SI. No. 17 and 62 in the Civil Engineering Department has been substituted as "Basic Tradesman". 
(iv) In the said Schedule-Il under the said Regulations, words "Maistry (C&P)" appearing in the Recruitment Rules 
at SI.No. 16 and 17 in the Civil Engineering Department has been substituted as "Tradesman ". 
(v) In the said Schedule-II under the said Regulations, the words "Head  Maistry (C&P)" appearing in the Recruitment 
Rules at SI.No. 16 in the Civil Engineering Department has been substituted as "Tradesman (Senior)". 
Foot Note: The Recruitment Rules in respect of Class Il, III and IV posts of all the departments of the Trust included 
in the Schedule-II of the Chennai Port Trust Employees' (Recruitment, Seniority and Promotion) Regulations 
2008 was published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette vide SRO  C- 2/2016, dated 22nd May 2015.
 
C. MOHAN, 
 
 
 
Secretary,  
 
 
 
Chennai Port Trust.

Amendment to Rule 4 of the Rules in regulate the Public Interest Litigations ﬁ led under Article 226 of the 
Constitution of India.
(R.O.C. No. 5232-A/2018/F1)
No. SRO C-20/2018.— 
By virtue of Article 225 of the Constitution of India and of all other powers hereunto enabling, the High Court makes 
the following amendment to Rule 4 of the Rules to regulate the Public Interest  Litigations ﬁ led under Article 226 of the 
Constitution of India as published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, Part III––Section 2, Issue No.31, dated 11-08-2010. 
 
AMENDMENT:-After Rule 4 of Rules to Regulate Public Interest Litigations framed by the High Court, the 
following shall be added: 
"4(a) - The petitioner shall ﬁ le a photo copy of a document having Petitioner's proof of 
identity in  the form of Passport, PAN Card, Driving Licence,  Aadhar Card, Photo afﬁ xed 
Ration Card/Family  Card, Photo Identity Card issued by the employer in case of an Ofﬁ cer/
Employee of the Government, Government Company, Government undertaking, Statutory 
Authority, Public Company Limited by Shares, Employees of High Court and other Courts. 
A photo copy of one such document duly attested by the Ofﬁ cer of the Court shall be ﬁ led 
along with the Writ Petition."
