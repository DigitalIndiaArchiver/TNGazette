[ELECTIONS - PANCHAYATS - CONDUCT OF - HOURS OF POLL – NOTIFIED – 
AMENDMENTS - ISSUED.]
(S.O. No. 13/2021/TNSEC/PE-1, dated 8th  September 2021.)
 
Rඍඉඌ:
 
 
 
S.O. No.6/2021/TNSEC/PE-1, dated 31st August, 2021.
No. VI(2)/83(b)/2021.
WHEREAS due to prevailing COVID-19 pandemic situation, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission 
has, inter-alia, ﬁ xed hours for poll under Article 243K of the Constitution of India and rule 24 and rule 
25 read with rule 143 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Elections) Rules, 1995, between 7.00 A.M. and 
7.00 P.M. on the day notiﬁ ed for poll of Panchayat elections;
WHEREAS, during the meeting with all recognised political parties held on 06.09.2021  the representatives 
of all the parties requested the hours of poll may be reconsidered and suggested to revise the hours for 
poll, taking into consideration of prevailing ground realities; and
WHEREAS, most of the District Election Oﬃ  cer/District Collectors also suggested in the video conference 
held in 08.09.2021 that the hours of poll may be ﬁ xed between 7.00 A.M. and 6.00 P.M. and further 
suggested that the last one hour between 5.00 P.M. and 6.00 P.M. may be exclusively ﬁ xed for the electors, 
having temperature more than 98.4˚F with COVID-19 symptoms or undergoing treatment for COVID-19.
NOW, THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred under Article 243K of the Constitution of 
India and rule 24 and rule 25 read with rule 143 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Elections) Rules, 1995 
the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission hereby makes the following amendment to the Notiﬁ cation 
issued in the Statutory Order read above, published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary 
No.391, Part VI – Section 2, Dated 1.9.2021 vide Notiﬁ cation No.VI(2)/83(a)/2021 and the District Gazette, 
Dated 1st September 2021.
