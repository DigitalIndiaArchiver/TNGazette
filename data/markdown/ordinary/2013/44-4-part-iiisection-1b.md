Amendment to the Tamil Nadu Special Police Subordinate Service Rules.
[G.O. Ms. No. 827, Home (Pol. IX), 10th October 2013.]
No. SRO B-38/2013—In exercise of the powers conferred by sections 8 and 10 of the Tamil Nadu District Police Act,
1859 (Central Act XXIV of 1859) and sections 9 and 11 of the Chennai City Police Act, 1888 (Tamil Nadu Act III of 1888)
read with the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the
following amendment to the Tamil Nadu Special Police Subordinate Service Rules, 1978 (Section 44 in Volume-III of the Tamil
Nadu Services Manual, 1986):—
2. The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force with effect on and from the
11th December 2006.
AMENDMENTS
In the Said Rules in rule 14:—
(1) in sub rule (a), in the tabular column:-
(a) in the second proviso in column (2) against the entry ”Category 2 (Men and Women)” in column (1) after the
expression “Ex-Servicemen” the following expression shall be inserted, namely:—
“and the Ex-personnel of the Central Para-Military Forces like Central Reserve Police Force, Central
Industrial Security Force, Border Security Force and Indo-Tibetan Border Police;”
(b) in the second proviso in column (2) against the entry ”Categories 4,5,6" in column (1), after the expression
”Ex-Servicemen” the following expression shall be inserted, namely:—
“and Ex-personnel of Central Para-Military Forces like Central Reserve Police Force, Central Industrial
Security Force, Border Security Force and Indo-Tibetan Border Police;”
(2) in sub-rule (f), in the tabular column, in the 'Explanation' in Column(2) against the "Category 6 Constables
(men and women)" in column (1), after the expression "Ex-Servicemen" the following expression shall be inserted, namely:—
"and Ex-personnel of the Central Para-Military Forces".
