NOTIFICATIONS BY THE ELECTION COMMISSION OF INDIA
APPOINTMENT OF RETURNING OFFICER FOR PRESIDENTIAL ELECTION
No.  SRO  G-13/2017.
The following Notification of the Election Commission of India, Nirvachan Sadan, Ashoka
Road, New Delhi-110 001, dated 12th June, 2017  [22 Jyaistha, 1939 (Saka)] is republished:-
No.479/3/2017(I).—In pursuance of sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Presidential and
Vice-Presidential Elections Act, 1952 (31 of 1952), the Election Commission of India, in consultation
with the Central Government, hereby appoints the Secretary General to the Lok Sabha, Parliament
House, New Delhi, to be the Returning Officer for the Presidential Election, 2017.
(By Order)
