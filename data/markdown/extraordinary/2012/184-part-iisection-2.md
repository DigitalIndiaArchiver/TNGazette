TRANSPORT DEPARTMENT
ACQUISITION OF LANDS
[G.O. Ms. No. 120, Transport (I.1), 3rd July 2012.]
No. II(2)/TRA/328(c)/2012.
Whereas in exercise of the powers conferred by
clause (1) of the Article 258 of the Constitution of India, the
President of India has, in the Ministry of Food, Agriculture,
Community Development and Co-operation (Department of
Agriculture) Notification No.4(1)/65, General-II, dated the 23rd
April 1966 entrusted to the Government of Tamil Nadu with
their consent, the functions of the Central Government under
the Land Acquisition Act, 1894 (Central Act I of 1894) in
relation to Acquisition of Lands for the purpose for the Union
in this State.
And, whereas it has already been decided that the
Compensation shall be paid from the public revenues.
Now, therefore in exercise of the powers conferred by
Section 6 of the said Act, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
declares that the Lands specified in the Schedule below
and measuring 0.79.0 hect. of Vagurampatti Village,
Namakkal Taluk, Namakkal District, be the same a little
more or less are needed for public purpose for provide ROB
and approach Road due to the formation of Salem-Karur
Broad Gauge Railway Line Project. A plan of the Lands is
kept in the Office of Special Tahsildar (Land Acquisition),
Salem-Karur Broad Gauge Railway Line Project, Namakkal
and may be inspected at any time during the Office Hours.
