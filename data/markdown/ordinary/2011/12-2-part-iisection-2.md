Order of detention of certain person and asking
appearance before officials under conservation of
Foreign Exchange and Prevention of Smuggling
Activities Act.
[G.O.No. SR.I/723-12/2010, Public (S.C.), 18th March 2011.]
No. II(2)/PUSC/156/2011.—Whereas, an order had been
made 
by 
the 
Government 
of 
Tamil 
Nadu 
in
G.O. No. SR.I/723-5/2010, Public (SC) Department, dated
30th December 2010 under Section 3(1)(i) of the Conservation
of Foreign Exchange and Prevention of Smuggling Activities
Act, 1974 (Central Act 52 of 1974), directing that Thiru
Zahir Hussain, son of Thiru Sahul Hameed, resident of
No. 9, Big Street, Triplicane, Chennai-5, be detained;
And Whereas, the said Thiru Zahir Hussain, son of Thiru
Sahul Hameed, resident of No. 9, Big Street, Triplicane,
Chennai-5, cannot be found;
And, Whereas, the Government of Tamil Nadu have
reasons to believe that the said Thiru Zahir Hussain, son
of Thiru Sahul Hameed, in respect of whom the detention
order has been made, has absconded and is concealing
himself so that the said detention order cannot be
executed.
Now, Therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by
Section 7(1) (b) of the Conservation of Foreign Exchange
and Prevention of Smuggling Activities Act, 1974 (Central Act
52 of 1974), the Government of Tamil Nadu hereby direct
the said Thiru Zahir Hussain, son of Thiru Sahul Hameed,
resident of No. 9, Big Street, Triplicane, Chennai-5, to appear
before the Commissioner of Police, Egmore, Chennai-8, at
his office within a period of 15 days (fifteen days) from the
date of publication of this order in the Tamil Nadu Government
Gazette.
S. KARUTHIAH PANDIAN,
Secretary to Government.
