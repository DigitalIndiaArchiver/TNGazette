In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (1) of section 43-B and section 27 of the Tamil Nadu District 
Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920) and sub-rule (3) of rule 99 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, 
Municipalities and Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission hereby fixes 03.11.2022 
as the date for convening meetings to elect the Members of Taxation Appeal Committee and Member of Appointment 
Committee of Kadambur Town Panchayat, Thoothukudi District as shown in accordance with the following programme:-
1.

Appeal Committee and Member of Appointment Committee.
 26.10.2022
2.
Statutory interval
27.10.2022
to
 02.11.2022
3.
Date for conduct of meeting for the election of Members of Taxation 
Appeal Committee and Member of Appointment Committee.
