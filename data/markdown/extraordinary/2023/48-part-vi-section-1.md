JUDICIAL NOTIFICATION
CONSTITUTION OF A SUB COURT AT MUSIRI IN TIRUCHIRAPPALLI DISTRICT OF THE 
TAMIL NADU CIVIL COURTS ACT, 1873.
(Roc. No. 8115/A/2019/G/Judn.) 
No. VI(1)/76(a)/2023.
In  exercise of the powers conferred under the ﬁ rst proviso to Section 13 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts 
Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873) the High Court, Madras, hereby directs that appeals from the Decrees or 
Orders of the District Munsif Court at Musiri and District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court at Thottiam 
be preferred in the Court of Subordinate Judge, Musiri with eﬀ ect from the date on which the Subordinate 
Judge, Musiri assumes charge of that Court.
