ENERGY DEPARTMENT
APPOINTMENT OF MEMBER (LEGAL) IN THE TAMIL NADU ELECTRICITY REGULATORY 
COMMISSION UNDER THE ELECTRICITY ACT, 2003.
[G.O. Ms. No. 47, Energy (D1), 27th June 2019, Aani 12, Vikari, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2050.] 
No. II(2)/EGY/498(a)/2019.
Under sub-section (5) of Section 82 of the Electricity Act, 2003 (Central Act 36 of 2003) and on the 
recommendation of the Selection Committee referred to in Section 85 of the said Act, the Governor of 
Tamil Nadu hereby appoints Thiru. K. Venkatasamy as Member (Legal), Tamil Nadu Electricity 
Regulatory Commission, Chennai. 
 
Md. NASIMUDDIN, 
 
 
 
Principal Secretary to Government.
