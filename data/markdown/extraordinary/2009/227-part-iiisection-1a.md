ANIMAL HUSBANDRY, DAIRYING AND FISHERIES DEPARTMENT
TAMIL NADU REGULATION OF JALLIKATTU RULES, 2009.
[G.O. Ms. No. 100, Animal Husbandry, Dairying and Fisheries (AH3), 26th August 2009,
Aavani 10, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. SRO A-24(a)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 11 of the Tamil Nadu Regulation of Jallikattu Act, 2009
(Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 2009), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following Rules:—
RULES
1. Short title.— These rules may be called the  Tamil Nadu Regulation of Jallikattu Rules, 2009.
2. Definition.—  In these Rules, unless the context  otherwise requires—
(a) ‘Act’ means the Tamil Nadu Regulation of Jallikattu Act, 2009 (Tamil Nadu  Act 27 of 2009);
(b)  “event” means Jallikattu and includes manjuvirattu,  “oormaadu”, "vadamaadu”, “erudhu vidum vizha” and all such
events involving taming of bulls.
3. Examination of  bulls.—(1) Every owner of bulls shall bring the bulls participating in the event, to the authorities  of the
Animal Husbandry Department   authorized by the  Collector for testing of the bulls at least 3 (three)  hours  before the
commencement of the event.
(2) The authorities of the Animal Husbandry Department shall, after examining the  bulls, issue a certificate  in the Form
appended to these Rules, if they are satisfied  that no performance enhancement  drugs have been administered  to the bulls in
any form.
(3) If the authorities of the Animal Husbandry Department have reason to believe that performance enhancement
drugs have been administered to the bulls,  they may refuse to issue the certificate.
(4) The decision of the authorities of Animal Husbandry Department is final.

4. Responsibility of Organiser.— In addition to the responsibilities specified in Section 4 of the Act, every person who organise
the event shall carry out the following responsibilities, namely:—
(a) the bulls must be allowed into the arena one after another;
(b) a bull that has once participated in the event should not be permitted again in the same event.
5. Arrangement to be made by the Collector.—In addition to the arrangements specified in Section 5 of the Act,
the Collector shall ensure that the selected venue should be on a level ground to avoid  causing of injury to the participants, bulls
and spectators.
