MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION AND WATER SUPPLY (ELECTION) DEPARTMENT
AMENDMENTS TO THE TAMIL NADU TOWN PANCHAYATS, THIRD GRADE MUNICIPALITIES, MUNICIPALITIES AND
CORPORATIONS (ELECTIONS) RULES, 2006.
[G.O. Ms. No. 238, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (Election), 8th December 2009.]
No. SRO A-41(b)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 303 of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920
(Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920), Section 347 of the Chennai City Municipal Corporation Act, 1919 (Tamil Nadu
Act IV of 1919), Section 431 of the Madurai City Municipal Corporation Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 15 of 1971), Section 430
of the Coimbatore City Municipal Corporation Act, 1981 (Tamil Nadu Act 25 of 1981), read with Section 8 of the Tiruchirappalli
City Municipal Corporation Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 1994), Section 8 of the Tirunelveli City Municipal Corporation
Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 28 of 1994), Section 8 of the Salem City Municipal Corporation Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 29 of 1994),
Section 8 of the Tiruppur City Municipal Corporation Act, 2008 (Tamil Nadu Act 7 of 2008), Section 8 of the Erode City Municipal
Corporation Act, 2008 (Tamil Nadu Act 8 of 2008), Section 8 of the Vellore City Municipal Corporation Act, 2008 (Tamil Nadu Act
26 of 2008) and Section 8 of the Thoothukudi City Municipal Corporation Act, 2008 (Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 2008) the Governor of
Tamil Nadu, after consultation with the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission, hereby makes the following amendments to the
(a) Municipal Administration and Water Supply Department (Election) Notification No. SROA-28(a)/2006, published at pages 1-55
of part III—Section 1(a) of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated 28th August 2006; and (b) the Tamil Nadu
Town Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities, Municipalities and Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006:—
AMENDMENTS
(1)
In the said Notification, in the preamble, for the expression “and Section 8 of the Salem City Municipal Corporation Act,
1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 29 of 1994)”, the following expression shall be substituted, namely:—
“Section 8 of the Salem City Municipal Corporation Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 29 of 1994), Section 8 of the Tiruppur City
Municipal Corporation Act, 2008 (Tamil Nadu Act 7 of 2008), Section 8 of the Erode City Municipal Corporation Act, 2008
(Tami Nadu Act 8 of 2008), Section 8 of the Vellore City Municipal Corporation Act, 2008 (Tamil Nadu Act 26 of 2008) and
Section 8 of the Thoothukudi City Municipal Corporation Act, 2008 (Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 2008)”;

(2)
in the said Rules, in rule 2, in sub rule (1),—
(a) (i)
in clause (i), the word “and” occuring after the expression “the Tirunelveli City Municipal Corporation Act, 1994
(Tamil Nadu Act 28 of 1994) in respect of Tirunelveli City Municipal Corporation;” shall be omitted;
(ii)
for the expression “the Salem City Municipal Corporation Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 29 of 1994) in respect of
the Salem City Municipal Corporation.”, the following expression shall be substituted, namely:—
“the Salem City Municipal Corporation Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 29 of 1994) in respect of the Salem City
Municipal Corporation;
“the Tiruppur City Municipal Corporation Act, 2008 (Tamil Nadu Act 7 of 2008) in respect of Tiruppur City
Municipal Corporation;
the Erode City Municipal Corporation Act, 2008 (Tamil Nadu Act 8 of 2008) in respect of Erode City Municipal
Corporation;
the Vellore City Municipal Corporation Act, 2008 (Tamil Nadu Act 26 of 2008) in respect of Vellore City
Municipal Corporation; and
the Thoothukudi City Municipal Corporation Act, 2008 (Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 2008) in respect of Thoothukudi
City Municipal Corporation.”;
(b) in clause (vii), for the words “Tirunelveli and Salem,” the expression “Tirunelveli, Salem, Tiruppur, Erode, Vellore and
Thoothukudi;” shall be substituted.
