ANIMAL HUSBANDRY, DAIRYING AND FISHERIES DEPARTMENT
DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF THE TAMIL NADU REGULATION OF JALLIKATTU ACT, 2009.
[G.O. Ms. No. 99, Animal Husbandry, Dairying and Fisheries (AH3), 26th August 2009,
Aavani 10, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. II(2)/AHDFI/447(b)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Regulation of
Jallikattu Act, 2009 (Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 2009), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the
26th August 2009, as the date on which the said Act shall come into force.
C. MUTHUKUMARASWAMY,
Secretary to Government.
