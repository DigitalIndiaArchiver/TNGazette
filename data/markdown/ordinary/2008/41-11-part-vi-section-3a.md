GENERAL  NOTIFICATION
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD
No. 1, JAFFAR SYRANG STREET, VALLAL SEETHAKATHI NAGAR, CHENNAI-600 001.
Calling for tenders to develop the properties of Syedus Shoda Hazrath Imam Hussain Wakf No. 6, Woods Road,
Anna Salai, Chennai-600 002 for residential, commercial and religious purposes on Joint Venture basis
under Wakf Act.
(Rc. No. 8609/B1/2007/Chennai.)
No. VI-3(a)/234/2008.
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 51 of the Wakf Act, 1995, the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution
in item No. 4/08 in Rc. 8609/B1/2007/Chennai, dated 28th February 2008 has resolved to develop the properties of the
above Wakf comprised in Survey No. 34, Certificate No. 711 measuring to an extent of 3640 Sq.ft. at Door No. 6, Woods
Road, Anna Salai, Chennai-600 002 for residential, commercial and religious purposes on Joint Venture basis. (The building
contains Ground Floor, 1st Floor and 2nd Floor).
Any interested parties/capable Developers, Promoters for the development of the above properties on Joint Venture
basis may submit their tenders as well as suggestions and objections regarding the development of the properties within
15 days from the date of publication of this Notification. The tenders should be submitted in Sealed Covers addressed
to the Chief Executive Officer, Tamil Nadu Wakf Board, No. 1, Jaffar Syrang Street, Vallal Seethakathi Nagar,
Chennai-600 001.
The Tamil Nadu Wakf Board reserves all rights either to accept or reject all or any of the tenders without assigning
any reasons therefor.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
15th October 2008.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
