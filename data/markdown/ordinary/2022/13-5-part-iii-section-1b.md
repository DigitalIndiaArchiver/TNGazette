TAMIL DEVELOPMENT AND INFORMATION DEPARTMENT
Amendment to the Special Rules to the Posts of Junior Photographer in Information and Public Relations Department 
in Tamil Nadu General Subordinate Service.
Tamil Development and Information (ADMN.3.1)
No. SRO B-22/2022.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, 
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu General 
Subordinate Service (Sec.13 of Part III B of Service Rules contained in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Service Manual, 1970).
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖග
In the said Special Rules,
In Part-II under the heading "Class XLIV - INFORMATION AND PUBLIC RELATIONS DEPARTMENT", in 
Rule 4, in sub-rule (b), in the Table in Column (3), against the entry "Junior Photographer" in Column (1) and the 
corresponding entry" Direct Recruitment" in Column (2), the expression, "and practical experience in the ﬁ eld of 
Still Photography, Adobe Photoshop and Coral Draw for a period of not less than three years" shall be omitted.
