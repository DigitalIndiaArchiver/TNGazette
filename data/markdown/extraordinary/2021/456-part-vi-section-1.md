ACQUISITION OF LANDS
NOTICE U/S 15(1) OF TAMIL NADU HIGHWAYS ACT, 2001 (TAMIL NADU ACT 34 OF 2002), READ WITH TAMIL NADU 
LAND ACQUISITION LAWS. (REVIVAL OF OPERATION, AMENDMENT AND VALIDATION) ACT, 2019.
[Rc.No.CPRRP/CLA/15(1)/0/2021]
No. VI(1)/268(d)/2021.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that the lands speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below are acquired under Chennai 
Peripheral Ring Road Project for Highways purpose to wit, for formation of road connectivity from Ennore Port to Mahabalipuram 
(from Km.72.757 to Km. 73.566) at No.51, Ayakolathur Village, Sriperumbudur Taluk, Kanchipuram District and it having already 
been decided that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands to be paid out of the funds controlled and 
managed by the Highways Department and after having considering the cause shown by the owner or other persons having 
interest on such lands, as the case may be, do hereby publish the following notice under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the 
Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) read with the Tamil Nadu Land Acquisition Laws (Revival of Operation, 
Amendment and Validation) Act, 2019.
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002), read with the
Tamil Nadu Land Acquisition Laws (Revival of Operation, Amendment and Validation Act, 2019) the Governor of Tamil Nadu 
hereby notiﬁ es that the Wet and Dry lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below and measuring to an extent of 49,625 Sq.Mtrs. of lands 
are acquired in No.51, Ayakolathur Village, Sriperumbudur Taluk, Kanchipuram District to the same a little more or less needed for 
the purpose to wit for formation of road connectivity from Ennore Port to Mahabalipuram (from Km.72.757 to Km. 73.566) as part 
of Chennai Peripheral Ring Road Project, Highways Department.
The Plan of the lands under acquisition are kept in the oﬃ  ce of the Special District Revenue Oﬃ  cer (Land Acquisition), 
Chennai Peripheral Ring Road Project, Section-III, Chennai-600 058 and in the oﬃ  ce of the Special Tahsildar (Land Acquisition), 
Chennai Peripheral Ring Road Project, Unit-V, Kanchipuram and may be inspected at any time during the oﬃ  ce hours.
