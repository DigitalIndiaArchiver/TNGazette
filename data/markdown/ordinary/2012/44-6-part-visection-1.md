(Roc. No. 18708/2005/DP3)
VI(1)/325/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (1) of Section 33 of Town and Country Planning Act 1971,
(Act No. 35 of 1972). The Commissioner of Town and Country Planning, Chennai in his Proceedings Roc. No. 18708/2005/DP3,
dated   September 2012 proposes to make the following comprehensive variation to the approved Director of Town and
Country Proceeding Roc. 42211/90/DP3, dated 29th October 1990 and the fact of this approval in form No. 12
published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No. 15 Part VI—Section-1, Page No. 327, dated 17th April 1991
publication No. VI(1)/567/1991.
2. Any person affected or interested in this draft variation may within Sixty days from the date of publication of this
notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, can represent in person or submit in writing to the
Member-Secretary, Local Planning Authority any objections and suggestions relating thereto.
3. The variation with plan may be inspected at free of cost at any time during office hours at the above said Local
Planning Authority Office.
VARIATION
1. The entire Thangavel Nagar Area Detailed Development Plan of Pattukottai Local Planning Area is approved by
the Director of Town and Country Planning vide Proceedings Roc. No. 42211/90/DP3, dated 29th October 1990 is
varied by Thangavel Nagar Area of Pattukkottai Local Planning Area.
2. The Plan thus varied is numbered as Map No. 5 DDP(V)/DTCP No. 20/2012.
3. The Detailed Development clauses and schedules annexed with this plan are made enforceable from the date
of publication of the confirmed variation notification to be issued under Section 33(2) of the Act and published in
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.

JUDICIAL NOTIFICATION
Constitution of Sub-Court at Vedasandur in Dindigul District
(Roc. No. 2878/A/2008/G/Judn)
VI(1)/328/2012.
     In exercise of the powers conferred under the First proviso to Section 13 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873
(Central Act III of 1873) the High Court of Madras hereby directs that appeals from the decrees or orders of the District
Munsif-cum-Judical  Magistrate Court, Vedasandur be preferred in the Court of Subordinate Judge, Vedasandur with effect
from the date on which the Subordiante Judge, Vedasandur assumes charge of that Court.
High Court, Madras,
25th October 2012.
G. CHOCKALINGAM,
   Registrar-General.
