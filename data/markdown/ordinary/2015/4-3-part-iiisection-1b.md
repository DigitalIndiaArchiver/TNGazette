No. SRO B-8/2015.
In exercise of the  powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Govenor of
Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Rules published with Health and Family Welfare
Department, Notification No. SRO B-274/76, dated the 22nd May 1976 (at Pages 311 - 312 of Part III—Section 1 (b) of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 9th June 1976.
2. The amendments hereby made shall come into force on the date of publication of this Notification in the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules,-
 (1) in rule 1, for the expression, “Artificial Limb Centre, Government General Hospital, Madras”, the expression
“Government Medical Institutions”, shall  be substituted;
  (2) in rule 5, in sub-rule (b), for clause (1) and clause (2), the following clause shall be substituted, namely:—
“(1) Must hold a Post Graduation in Social work with Medical and Psychiatric Social Work as special subject, and;"
(3) in rule 7, after the Proviso, the following Proviso shall be added, namely:—
 "Provided further that on and from the 1st January 2006 the monthly pay shall be calculated in the scale of pay
Rs. 9300-34800+G.P. of Rs.4500/-”;
 (4) after rule 7 the following rule shall be added, namely:—
     “8. Saving Clause.—Nothing contained in these rules shall adversely affect the holder of the post as on 26-12-2014”.

No. SRO B-9/2015.
In exercise of the  powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following Amendments to the Ad hoc Rules published with the erstwhile Health, Education
and Local Administration Department Notification  No. S.R.O. A-3530 of 1959, dated the 27th May 1959, at Pages 841 and
842 of Part I of the Fort St. George Gazette, dated the 17th June 1959, as subsequently amended.
2. The amendments hereby made shall come into force on the date of publication of this Notification in the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
AMENDMENTS
 In the said Rules,-
 (1) for rule 5, the following rule shall be substituted, namely:—
“5. Qualifiation.— (a) Age.- No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post by direct recruitment, if
he has completed or will complete 30 years of age on the first day of July of the year in which the selection for appointment
is made.
