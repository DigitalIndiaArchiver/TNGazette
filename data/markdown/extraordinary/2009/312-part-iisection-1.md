LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY SECRETARIAT
DEATH OF THIRU P.N. PERIANNAN,  MEMBER OF THE TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY
[Letter No. 21604/2009-1, Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Secretariat (B.III), 2nd December 2009,
Karthigai 16, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. II(1)/LAS/43(a-1)/2009.
"The Honourable Speaker has learnt with deep regret the death of Thiru P.N. Periannan,
a Member of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly on 1st December 2009".
VACATION OF SEAT IN TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY
[Letter No. 21604/2009-1, Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Secretariat (B.III), 2nd December 2009,
Karthigai 16, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. II(1)/LAS/43(a-2)/2009.
"A vacancy has occurred among the Members of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly, consequent
on the death of Thiru P.N. Periannan, a Member elected to the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly
from the 83. Pennagaram Assembly Constituency in Dharmapuri District".
