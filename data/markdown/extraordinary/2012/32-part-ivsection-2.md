An Act further to amend the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies
(Appointment of Special Officers) Act, 1976.
BE it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the
Sixty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows:—
1.
(1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies (Appointment
of Special Officers) Amendment Act, 2012.
(2)   It shall come into force at once.
2.  In section 4 of the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies (Appointment of Special
Officers) Act, 1976, in sub-section (1), for the expression “thirty five years and eight
months”, the expression “thirty six years and two months” shall be substituted.
