No. SRO C-2/2011.
The Notification No. G.S.R.1025(E), dated the 29th December 2010 issued by the Government of India,
Ministry of Shipping (Ports Wing) and published in Part II—Section 3 sub-section (i) of the Extraordinary issue of the
Gazette of India, dated the 29th December 2010 is republished hereunder as required under Section 132(2) of the
Major Port Trusts Act, 1963:—
No.G.S.R.1025(E).—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 132 of the Major Port Trusts
Act, 1963 (38 of 1963), the Central Government hereby publishes the following with regard to Chennai Port Trust as set
out in the Schedule annexed to this Notification.

2.
Construction of reference to “Madras”:—Unless the context otherwise requires any reference to the name
“Madras” appearing in the title or in any of the Rules, Regulations, Bye-Laws, Notifications or orders issued under any
such Rules, Regulations, Bye-Laws or in any Instrument shall after these are published in the official Gazette be
Construed as reference to “Chennai”.
3.
Validation and Savings: Nothing contained in this notification substituting the name “Chennai” in place of
“Madras” shall affect the previous operations of any Notification, Order, Instrument, Rule, Regulation or Bye-Laws or any
right, privilege, obligation or liability already acquired or incurred under any such Notification, Order, Instrument, Rule,
Regulation or Bye-Law.
