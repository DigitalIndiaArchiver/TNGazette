APPOINTMENT OF COMMISSION OF INQUIRY TO INQUIRE THE ALLEGATION OF IRREGULARITIES LIKE
EXCESS EXPENDITURE CAUSING LOSS TO THE EXCHEQUER AND DEFICIENCY  IN STANDARDS OF
CONSTRUCTION OF NEW SECRETARIAT COMPLEX AT OMANDURAR GOVERNMENT ESTATE, CHENNAI
UNDER THE COMMISSIONS OF INQUIRY ACT.
Amendment to Notification
[G.O. Ms. No. 870, Public (Buildings), 21st September 2011.]
No. II(2)/PUBG/404(a)/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Commissions of Inquiry Act, 1952
(Central Act LX of 1952), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the
Public (Buildings) Department's Notification No. II(2)/PUBG/286(c)/2011, dated the 22nd June 2011, published at
pages 1-2 in Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 22nd June 2011.
AMENDMENT
In the said Notification, in paragraph 3, for the expression “three months”, the expression “six months” shall be
substituted.
KUMAR JAYANT,
 Secretary to Government.
