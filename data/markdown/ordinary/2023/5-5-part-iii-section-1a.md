Amendment to the Indian Administrative Service (Pay) Rules, 2016. 
[G.O. Ms. No. 65, Public (Special-A), 12th January 2023, Margazhi 28, Subakiruthu, Thiruvalluvar Aandu&2053.]
No. SRO A-5/2023.—The following Notification of the Government of India Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances and 
Pensions Department of Personnel and Training, New Delhi, dated 30th December, 2022 is republished:-
G.S.R. 930(E).-In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the All India Services Act, 1951 
(61 of 1951), the Central Government, in consultation with the Government of Tamil Nadu, hereby makes the following rules 
further to amend the Indian Administrative Service (Pay) Rules, 2016, namely:-
1. 
(1) These rules may be called the Indian Administrative Service (Pay) Fourth Amendment Rules, 2022.
 
(2) They shall come into force on the date of their publication in the Official Gazette.
2.
