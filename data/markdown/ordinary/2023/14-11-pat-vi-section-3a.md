GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAQF BOARD, CHENNAI
Taking over the Amanusha Pallivasal, Pettai, Tirunelveli District (G.S.No.584/TNV) 
under Direct Management of Tamil Nadu Waqf Board.
(Lr.Rc. No. 5372/09/B1/TNV)
No. VI-3(a)/17/2023.
	
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Waqf Act 1995, the Tamil Nadu 
Waqf Board in its Resolution Item No.43/23 in R.C.No.5372/09/B1/TNV dated:28.02.2023 has ordered to take over 
AMANUSHA PALLIVASAL, PETTAI, TIRUNELVELI DISTRICT (G.S.No.584/TNV) under the Direct Management of 
Tamil Nadu Waqf Board and to appoint the Waqf Superintendent, Tirunelveli as its Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,	
A.B. RAFIULLA,
31st March 2023. 
Chief Executive Officer,
	
Tamil Nadu Waqf Board.
Errata to Notification
(Lr.No.11965/09/B1)
TNGG No.7, Part VI-Section 3(a)7/2023 Page No.6, dated 15.02.2023
No. VI-3(a)/18/2023.
	
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Waqf Act 1995, the Tamil Nadu Waqf in 
its Resolution Item No.315/22 in R.C.No.11965/09/B1/TNV dated: 09.01.2023 has ordered to take over SADAY FAKKEER 
LALAMINH PALLIVASAL, PALAYAPETTAI KANDIAPERI, TIRUNELVELI DISTRICT (G.S.No.812/TNV) UNDER THE 
Direct Management of Tamil Nadu Waqf Board and to appoint the Waqf Inspector, Trichy as its Executive Officer.
''ERRATA''
In the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette (TNGG No.7, Part  VI-Section 3(a)7/2023 published on 15.02.2023) 
in the Last Line the words ''Waqf Inspector, Trichy ''shall be read as ''Superintendent of Waqfs, Tirunelveli''.
Chennai-600 001,	
A.B. RAFIULLA,
31st March 2023. 
Chief Executive Officer,
	
Tamil Nadu Waqf Board.
