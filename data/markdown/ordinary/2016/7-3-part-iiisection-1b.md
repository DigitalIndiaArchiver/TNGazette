No. SRO B-14/2016.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Medical
Subordinate Service.
AMENDMENT
In the said special rules, in Part-II, under the heading Branch II-Indigenous Medicine, in the annexure, for the entry
'Diploma in Indian System of Medicine' in column 1, against the entry 'category 6 Pharmacist, Indigenous Medicine' in Column 1,
the following entries shall be substituted, namely:-
 "(1) Diploma in Indian System of Medicine; or
  (2) Diploma in Pharmacy in Siddha / Ayurveda / Unani (Saparately for each system); or
  (3) Diploma in Integrated Pharmacy (DIP) conducted by the Government of Tamil Nadu."
Amendment to the Adhoc Rules for the Post of Pharmacist (Homoeopathy), in Indian Medicine and
Homoeopathy in Tamil Nadu Medical Subordinate Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 26, Health and Family Welfare (IM2-1), 27th January 2016,

No. SRO B-15/2016.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Adhoc rules for the Post of Pharmacist
(Homoeopathy) published with the Health and Family Welfare Department Notification No. SRO. B-293/81 at Pages 316-317
of Part III—Section 1(a) of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 8th July 1981.
AMENDMENT
In the said Ad-hoc rules, in rule 5, for sub-rule (b), after clause (2) the following clause shall be added, namely:-
"or
(3) Must have passed the Diploma in Integrated Pharmacy course conducted by the Government of Tamil Nadu."
J. RADHAKRISHNAN,
Secretary to Government.

No. SRO B-16/2016.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Industries
Subordinate Service (Section 16 in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1970).
2. The amendments hereby made shall come into force on the 20-1-2016.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules, in the Annexure,--
(1) for item (i), under the method of recruitment commencing with the expression, “1. By promotion” and ending with the
expression “Chemical Testing and Analytical Laboratory, Guindy, Chennai” in column (3) against Class I in column (1) and
Category “2. Assistant Engineer (Industries)” in column (2) thereof, the following item shall be substituted, namely:-
“(i) Must possess a Bachelor of Engineering or Bachelor of Technology degree of any discipline except Civil
Engineering and Architectural Engineering of any University recognised by the University Grants Commission or
Institution recognised by the All India Council for Technical Education.”;
(2) for item (i), under the method of recruitment commencing with the expression, “2. By direct recruitment” and ending
with the expression “recruitment by transfer from any other service” in column (3) against Class I in column (1) and
Category “2. Assistant Engineer (Industries) in column (2) thereof, the following item shall be substituted, namely:-
“(i) Must possess a Bachelor of Engineering or Bachelor of Technology degree of any discipline except Civil
Engineering and Architectural Engineering of any University recognised by the University Grants Commission or
Institution recognised by the All India Council for Technical Education.”.
