No. I/PUSP/10/2018.—The following Notification of 
the Election Commission of India, New Delhi, dated 
21st February 2018 is republished:-
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section 
(1) of section 13A of the Representation of the People 
Act, 1950 (43 of 1950), the Election Commission of India 
in consultation with the Government of Tamil Nadu hereby 
nominates Shri Satyabrata Sahoo, IAS., (TN:1997) as the 
Chief Electoral Officer for the State of Tamil Nadu with 
effect from the date he takes over charge and until further 
orders vice Shri Rajesh Lakhoni, IAS.
2. Shri Satyabrata Sahoo shall cease to hold and 
hand over forthwith the charge of all or any charges of 
work under the Government of Tamil Nadu, which he may 
be holding before such assumption of office.
3. Shri Satyabrata Sahoo while functioning as the Chief 
Electoral Officer, Tamil Nadu shall not hold any additional 
charge whatsoever under the Government of Tamil Nadu 
except that he should be designated Secretary to the 
Government in charge of Election Department in the State 
Secretariat.
