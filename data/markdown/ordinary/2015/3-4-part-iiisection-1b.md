AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules,—
(1) in rule 12-A, for the expression “category of Junior Reporter of the Shorthand Bureau”, the expression
“Categories of Sub-Inspectors of Police (Finger Print) and Junior Reporter of the Shorthand Bureau” shall be substituted;
(2) in Annexure I, in the tabular Column,-
(a) for the entry in Column 4, against the entry “Category 1A Inspector of Police (Finger Print)” in column (1), the
entry “Inspector General of Police, State Crime Records Bureau,” shall be substituted;
(b) in Column 3, against the entry “Category 2-A Sub-Inspectors of Police (Finger Print)” in column (1), the following
expression shall be added, namely:-
“Not more than twenty per cent of vacancies shall be filled up from among the members of the
Service in categories 7 and 8 in Class-I of rule 2 and from among the members holding the posts
in categories 4, 5 and 6 of rule 2 of the Tamil Nadu Special Police Subordinate Service Rules:
Provided that if departmental candidates are not available to fill up the above twenty per cent vacancies, then the unfilled
Vacancies shall be filled up by eligible candidates from open market:
Provided further that the provisions of rule 21 (b) of the General Rules for the Tamil Nadu State and Sub-ordinate Service
shall apply in respect of reservation for women”;
(c) for the entry in column 4, against the entry “Category 2A Sub-Inspectors of Police (Finger Print)” in column (1),
the entry “Inspector General of Police, State Crime Records Bureau” shall be substituted;
(3) in Annexure II, in the Tabular column, under Class-I, in column (3), against the entry “Category-2A,
Sub-Inspector of Police (Finger Print) (Men & Women)” in column (1),-
(i) in clause (1),-
(a) to sub-clause (i), the following proviso shall be added, namely:-
“Provided that in respect of male candidates for selection to the post of Sub-Inspector of Police
(Finger Print), the height must not be less than 163 centimeters and in the case of Scheduled Caste or Scheduled Tribe
candidates the height must not be less than 160 centimeters.”;
(b) to sub-clause (ii), the following proviso shall be added, namely:-
“Provided that in respect of female candidates for selection to the post of Sub-Inspector of Police,
(Finger Print), the height must not be less than 154 centimeters and in the case of Scheduled Caste or Scheduled Tribe
candidates, the height must not be less than 152 centimeters.”;
(c) to sub-clause (iii), the following proviso shall be added, namely:-
“Provided that there shall be no chest measurement for all candidates, that is, both men and women for
selection to the post of Sub-Inspector of Police (Finger Print)”.;
(ii) in clause (3), in sub-clause (i), in item (c), after the first proviso, the following provisos shall be inserted, namely:-
“Provided further that for selection to the post of Sub-Inspector of Police (Finger Print), there shall be no
Physical Endurance Test and Physical Efficiency Test in respect of non departmental candidates and there shall be no
Physical Measurement Test, Endurance Test and Physical Efficiency Test in respect of department candidates:
Provided also that in respect of the post Sub-Inspector of Police (Finger Print) the total marks reckoned for
selection will be only 85, as the said post is exempted from Physical Efficiency Test”.
