OFFICE OF MAYOR AND COUNCILLORS—FRESH POLL IN CERTAIN POLLING STATIONS—SAFEGUARDS
AGAINST PERSONATION—MARKING INDELIBLE INK—DIRECTIONS ISSUED.]
(S.O.No. 103/TNSEC/2011/ME1.)
No. VI(2)/519(a)/2011.
In pursuance of sub-rule (1) (a) of rule 47 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Elections) Rules, 1995 and rule 45 and 67
of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities, Municipalities and Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006,
the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission had  directed in the  S.O. No. 31/2011/TNSEC/EG2, dated the 14th September 2011,
that before delivering the ballot paper or ballot papers to the elector, or before allowing to record his/her vote in Electronic
Voting Machine, an indelible ink should be applied as a line from the top end of the nail to the bottom of the first joint of
the left forefinger.
Whereas, fresh poll to seven polling stations of Chennai City Municipal Corporation for the election
of Mayor and Councillors has been ordered by the Commission and fixes, 20th October 2011 for said poll vide
S.O. 102/2011/TNSEC/ME1, dated the 18th October 2011.
Now, therefore, in view of the fresh poll ordered, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission directed that in the fresh
poll to seven polling stations i.e. 1730 M, 1730 W, 1731 AV of Ward No. 103, 1740 M, 1743 M, 1746 AV of Ward No. 104
and 1836 W of Ward No. 107 of Chennai City Municipal Corporation, for the election of Mayor/Ward Councillors of the said
Corporation scheduled on 20th October 2011, before allowing to record his/her vote in Electronic Voting Machine
indelible ink should be applied as a line from the top end of the nail to the bottom of the first joint of the left middle finger.
(By Order of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission)
V.M. XAVIER CHRISSO NAYAGAM,
Chennai-600 106,
Secretary.
