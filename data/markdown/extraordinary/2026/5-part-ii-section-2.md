WITHDRAWAL FROM THE LAND CLASSIFICATION UNDER THE TAMIL NADU URBAN LOCAL BODIES ACT, 1998.
[G.O.Ms.No.5, Municipal Administration and Water Supply [MA.5(1)], 9th January 2026,
மாார்கழி 25, விசுவாாவசு, திருவள்ளுவர் ஆண்டு-2056.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/16(b)/2026.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of section 118 of the Tamil Nadu Urban Local Bodies Act, 
1998 (Tamil Nadu Act 9 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby withdraws the land situated within the limits of 
Srivilliputhur Council, Srivilliputhur Taluk, Virudhunagar District with the total extent, as mentioned in the Schedule below, 
be the same or a little more or less, from the control of the Srivilliputhur Municipal Council, with effect on and from the 
date of publication of this Notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
