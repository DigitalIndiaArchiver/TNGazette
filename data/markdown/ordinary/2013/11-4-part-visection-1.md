Winding up of the affairs of the Coimbatore Auto Servicing Industrial Co-operative Society Limited. IND. No. 798 and
Appointment of Official Liquidator
(Rc. No. 10873/ICD./2010.)
No. VI (1)/180/2013.
Under the powers conferred on the Registrar of Industrial Co-operatives under Section 137(2)(b) of Tamil Nadu
Co-operative Societies Act, 1983 (Act 30 of 1983), The affairs of the Coimbatore Auto Servicing Industrial Co-operative
Society Limited, IND. No.798 have been ordered to be wound up with immediate effect by the industries Commissioner
and Director of Industries and Commerce and Registrar of Industrial Co-operatives, Mandavelipakkam,
Raja Annamalaipuram, Chennai-28 Vide Proc. No. 10873/ICD/2010, dated 21-2-2013.
Under Section 138(1) of the same Act, Assistant Director of Industries and Commerce (IC), District Industries
Centre, Coimbatore has been appointed as the Official Liquidator of the said society for the purpose of liquidation.

HARMANDER SINGH,

Variations to the Approved Master Plan for the Gummidipoondi Local Planning Area
(Roc.No.190/2010/GLPA.)
No. VI(1)/188/2013.—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 32 of the Tamil Nadu
Town and Country Planning Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972), Government in the order G.O. (2D) Ms. 17,
Housing and Urban Development UD4(1) Department, dated 22nd February 2013 has permitted to change of land use
of a survey numbers making the  following variation to the Master Plan for the Gummidipoondi  Local Planning Area,
approved under the said Act and published in the Housing and Urban Development Department
Notification No. II(2)/HOU/610/2000, dated 7th June 2000, at page No. 295 of Part II—Section 2 of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated 7th June 2000.
VARIATIONS
In the said Master Plan, in the “LAND USE SCHEDULE” Serial Number. 22, VILLAGE NUMBER :238, Pappankuppam
Village under the heading:—
I. RESIDENTIAL USE ZONE:  & sub heading 1(a) PRIMARY RESIDENTIAL USE ZONE: Survey Numbers in the
expression “128/6B, 128/11, 128/12B” shall be deleted.
and under the heading III. INDUSTRIAL USE ZONE: and sub heading III(b) GENERAL INDUSTRIAL USE ZONE:
Survey Numbers the expression “128/6B, 128/11, 128/12B” shall be added after the expression “49”.
Chengalpattu,
S. DHANARASU,
15th March 2013.
Member-Secretary (In-charge),
Gummidipoondi  Local Planning Aurhtority.
