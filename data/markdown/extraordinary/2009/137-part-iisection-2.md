The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that
the lands specified in the schedule below have to be  acquired
for a Public purpose and it having already been decided that
the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the
lands is to be paid out of public revenues. The following
declaration is issued under Section 6 of the Land Acquisition
Act, 1894 (Central Act I of 1894):—
DECLARATION
Under Section 6 of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894  (Central
Act I of 1894) the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby declare that
the lands specified in the schedule below and measuring
2.38.5 hectares be the same a little more or less are needed
for a public purpose for Gadana Dam Extension Scheme for
canal work.
A  plan of the lands are kept  in the Office of the Revenue
Divisional Officer, Cheranmahadevi and may be inspected at
any time during office hours.
