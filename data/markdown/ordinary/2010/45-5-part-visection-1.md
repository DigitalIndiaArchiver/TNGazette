Variations to the Approved Master Plan for the Salem Local Planning Area.
(Roc. No. 1463/2007 SLPA.)
No. VI(1)/382/2010.
In  exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 32 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning
Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972) and in exercise of the powers conferred by the Government Order Ms. No. 94,
Housing and Urban Development (UD4-1) Department, dated 12th June 2009 which has been published in the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No. 27, Part II—Section 2, Page No. 228, dated 15th July 2009 the following
variations are made to the Master Plan for the Salem Local Planning Area approved under the said Act and published
in the Housing and Urban Development Department Notification No. II(2)/HOU/233/2005 on Page 168 of
Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 13th April 2005.
VARIATIONS
In the said Master Plan.  In the “ANNEXURE-E” under the heading “USE ZONES - SALEM LOCAL PLANNING
AREA” under the heading “VILLAGES INCLUDED IN SALEM CORPORATION V. No. 25. KAMINAYAKKANPATTI”.
(1) Under the Sub-heading “General Industrial Use Zone” under the entries “G.I.” for the expression
“S.F. Nos. 1/3, 2/9” shall be substituted.
(2) Under the Sub-heading “Agriculture Use” under the entries “Ag-23” for the expression “Protected Area
(Mines) S.F. No. 1P” the expression “Protected Area (Mines) S.F. No. 1P (Except 1/3, 2/9)” shall be substituted.
Under the heading “VILLAGES INCLUDED IN SALEM CORPORATION V. No. 26. REDDIPATTTI”
(1) Under the Sub-heading “General Industrial Use Zone” under the entries “G.I.” for the expression
“S.F. No. 163/1” shall be substituted.
(2) Under the Sub-heading “Agricultural Use” under the entries “Ag 23” for the expression “Protected Area
(Mines) S.F. No. 163P” the expression “Protected Area (Mines) S.F. No. 163P” (Except 163/1) shall be substituted.
Salem-5,
