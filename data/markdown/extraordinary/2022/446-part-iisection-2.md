WHEREAS, the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning (Second Amendment) Act, 2022 
(Tamil Nadu Act 38 of 2022) was published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette on the 21st June 2022 and 
section 6 of that Act came into force on that date;
NOW, in exercise of the powers conferred by clause (ii) of sub-section (2) of section 1 of the said 
Tamil Nadu Act 38 of 2022, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the 14th day of September 2022 as 
the date on which all the remaining provisions of the said Act, shall come into force.
