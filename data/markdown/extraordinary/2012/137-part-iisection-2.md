CONSTITUTION OF ONE SUBORDINATE JUDGE'S COURT TO DEAL WITH MCOP CASES AT
DINDIGUL UNDER TAMIL NADU CIVIL COURTS ACT.
NOTIFICATION – I

No. II(2)/HO/276(b-1)/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by the first paragraph Section 4 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873
(Central Act III of 1873), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, after consultation with High Court, Madras, hereby fixes the
number of Subordinate Judges to be appointed under the said Act for Dindigul District as FOUR with effect on and from
the date on which the Subordinate Judge assumes charge of the Subordinate Judge’s Court, Dindigul.

No. II(2)/HO/276(b-2)/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by the second paragraph of section 4 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act,
1873 (Central Act III of 1873), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, after consultation with High Court, Madras, hereby fixes the
number of Subordinate Judge to be appointed to the Subordinate Judge’s Court at Dindigul in Dindigul District as ONE
with effect on and from the date on which the Subordinate Judge assumes charge of that Court.

No. II(2)/HO/276(b-3)/2012.

NOTIFICATION UNDER MOTOR VEHICLES ACT
CONSTITUTION OF THE SUBORDINATES JUDGES COURT, DINDIGUL AS MOTOR  ACCIDENTS CLAIMS TRIBUNAL
FOR THE DINDIGUL DISTRICT UNDER THE ACT.
NOTIFICATION-I
[G.O. Ms. No. 378, Home (Tr-IV), 28th May 2012, ¬õè£ê¤ 15, î¤¼õ÷¢Àõó¢ Ý‡´-2043.]
No. II(2)/HO/276(b-5)/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 165 of the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988
(Central Act 59 of 1988), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby constitutes the Subordinates Judges Court, Dindigul as
Motor Accidents Claims Tribunal for the Dindigul District.
APPOINTMENT OF THE SUBORDINATE JUDGE, DINDIGUL AS THE MEMBER OF THE MOTOR
ACCIDENT CLAIMS TRIBUNAL, DINDIGUL UNDER THE ACT.
NOTIFICATION-II
[G.O. Ms. No. 378, Home (Tr-IV), 28th May 2012, ¬õè£ê¤ 15, î¤¼õ÷¢Àõó¢ Ý‡´-2043.]
No. II(2)/HO/276(b-6)/2012.
Under sub-section (2) of Section 165 of the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988 (Central Act 59 of 1988), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the Subordinate Judge, Dindigul  as the member of the Motor
Accidents Claims Tribunal, Dindigul.
