The Governor of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that the
lands specified, Kancheepuram District, Alandur Taluk,
Meenambakkam Village in the Schedule below have to be
acquired for highways purpose, to wit for the formation of
widening of G.S.T  Road K.m. 15/9 – 16/7 on the right side
near Chennai Airport and it have already been decided that
the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the
lands is to be paid out of the fund controlled or managed
by the Chief Engineer (Constructions & Maintenance),
Highways Department, Chennai – 5, and having considered
the cause shown by the owners or other persons having
interest the said lands as the case may be so hereby
published the following Notice under sub-section (1) of
Section 15 of Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu
Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) Section 15 of Tamil Nadu Highways
Act 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34/2002). The Governor of Tamil
Nadu hereby acquires the lands specified in the Schedule
below and measuring an extent of 00493 square metres to the
same, a little more or less are needed for Highways Purpose,
to wit, for the formation of widening of G.S.T Road K.m.
15/9 – 16/7 on the right side near Chennai Airport.
The plans of the lands are kept in the office of the Special
Tahsildar (Land Aquisition) Chennai Metro Rail Limited,
Kancheepuram Unit, Gopalapuram and may be inspected at
any time during office hours.
