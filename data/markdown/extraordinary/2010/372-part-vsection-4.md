                            M. Rajasekaran—Appellant

                           R. Natarajan and others—Respondents.
ORDER
This Court has heard the learned counsel for the parties at length and in detail.
The learned Counsel for the appellant seeks permission to withdraw the appeal.
The  permission, as prayed for, is granted. The appeal stands disposed of as
withdrawn.
However, it is clarified  that it would be open to the appellant to urge all the points
mentioned in the memorandum of appeal before the Election Court at
appropriate stage.
