WITHDRAWAL FROM ACQUISITION OF LANDS
[G.O. Ms. No.81, Transport (I.1), 17th August 2022, ஆவணி 1, சுபகிருது, திருவள்ளுவர் ஆண்டு-2053.]  
No. II(2)/TRA/678(a)/2022.
Under sub-section (1) of Section 4 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Lands for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999) the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby withdraws from acquisition of the land / lands specified in the 
Schedule below in respect of which notice under sub-section (1) of section 3 was published by the Transport Department at 
page Nos. 4-6, Part II—Section 2, of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary No.236, dated 16.06.2020, the 
actual possession of which was not taken by, or on behalf of the Government.
