[ELECTIONS - TOWN PANCHAYATS, MUNICIPALITIES AND CORPORATIONS - CONDUCT OF - 
HOURS OF POLL - NOTIFIED.]
  
(S.O.No.4/2022/TNSEC/ME1, dated the 20th January 2022)
No.VI(2)/13(a)/2022.
WHEREAS, rule 22 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Municipalities and Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006, 
provides that the hours during which the poll will be open for the elections to the Town Panchayats, Municipalities and 
Corporations, being not less than ten hours between 7.00 A.M. and 5.30 P.M.
WHEREAS, due to prevailing Covid-19 pandemic situation and the guidelines (Standard Operating Procedures) 
are being issued by the Government, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission, as a precautionary measure and 
to facilitate the electors, having temperature more than 98.4 F with Covid-19 symptoms or undergoing treatment for 
Covid-19, to exercise franchise, has decided to extend the hours of poll to 11 hours instead of 10 hours in elections 
to Urban Local Bodies; and
WHEREAS, a diﬃ  culty has thus arisen for the extension of hours of poll;
NOW, THEREFORE, for removing the diﬃ  culty, in exercise of the powers conferred under Article 243ZA of 
Constitution of India and rule 22 read with rule 139 the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Municipalities and Corporations 
(Elections), Rules, 2006, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission hereby ﬁ xes that the hours of poll in elections to 
Town Panchayats, Municipalities and Corporations, shall be between 7.00 A.M. and 6.00 P.M. and ﬁ xes the last one 
hour between 5.00 P.M. and 6.00 P.M. exclusively for the electors having temperature more than 98.4 F with Covid-19 
symptoms or undergoing treatment for Covid-19, on the day notiﬁ ed for poll.
(By Order of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission)
