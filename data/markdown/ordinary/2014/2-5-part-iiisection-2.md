JUDICIAL NOTIFICATIONS
Amendment to the Madras High Court Service Rules
(Roc. No. 08/2013/RAC)
No. SRO C-1/2014.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Article 229 (1) and (2) of the Constitution of India and of all other
powers connected thereto, The Hon’ble the Chief Justice is pleased to make the following amendment to the Madras High Court
Service Rules:—
AMENDMENT
In Rule 14, the entry 'To Category 1A and 1B from Categories 2, 3 and 4' under the Heading “PROMOTION IN
SECTION-I OF DIVISION-III" shall be substituted as follows after deletion of the post of Driver (Category 2).
“To Category 1A and 1B from Categories 3 and 4.”
Provided that promotions made between 08-01-1987 and the date of issue of this amendment shall not be affected
notwithstanding the retrospective effect of this amendment.
This amendment shall be deemed to have come into force with effect from 08-01-1987.
High Court, Madras,
P.  KALAIYARASAN,
14th November 2013.
Registrar General.
