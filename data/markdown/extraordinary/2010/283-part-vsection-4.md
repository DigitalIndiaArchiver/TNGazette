DISQUALIFICATION OF A CANDIDATE CONTESTED FROM 13. VILLUPURAM (SC) PARLIAMENTARY CONSTITUENCY
IN THE GENERAL ELECTIONS TO LOK SABHA HELD IN MAY, 2009  FOR FAILURE TO LODGE ACCOUNTS OF
ELECTION EXPENSES UNDER SECTION 10A OF THE REPRESENTATION OF THE PEOPLE ACT, 1951.
No. SRO G-23  /2010.
The following Order of the Election Commission of India, Nirvachan Sadan,  Ashoka Road, New Delhi-110 001,
dated 30th August, 2010 [8 Bhadrapada, 1932 (Saka)] is republished:—
No. 76/TN-HP/2009(10).—Whereas, the Election Commission of India is satisfied that Sh. Poyyathu, S., a contesting
candidate at the General Election to Lok Sabha, 2009 from 13. Villupuram (SC)  Parliamentary Constituency has failed to lodge
his account of election expenses as required by the Representation of the People Act, 1951 and the Rules made thereunder;
And, whereas, the said candidate could not submit his representation/account of election expenditure till date
under the said rule;
And, whereas, in the supplementary report of the District Election Officer and District Collector, Villupuram District
has stated that he has not yet filed his account of election expenses;
Now, therefore, in pursuance of  Section 10A of the said Act, the Election Commission hereby declares Sh. Poyyathu, S.,
to be disqualified for being chosen as and for being a member of either House of Parliament or the Legislative Assembly or
Legislative Council of a State or Union Territory for a period of three years from the date of  this Order.
(By Order)
TAPAS KUMAR,
Principal Secretary,
Election Commission of India.
Secretariat,
PRAVEEN KUMAR,
Chennai-600 009,
Chief Electoral Officer and
Dated 16th September, 2010.
Secretary to Government.
