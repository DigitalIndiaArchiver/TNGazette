No. II(2)/CFCP/746/2016.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 172 of the
Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act, 1983 (Tamil Nadu
Act 30 of 1983), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
authorises the Additional Registrar of Co-operative Societies,
Chennai Region and all the Regional Joint Registrars of
Co-operative Societies, to exercise, within their respective
jurisdiction, the powers vested in the Government by
sub-section (7) of Section 33 of the said Act for the purpose
of appointing the Managing Director to the  Employees
Co-operative Credit Societies which have an annual turnover
of not less than rupees five crores and continuously made
profit for not less than three years as per the audited
accounts, in the cadre not higher than that of the
Co-operative Sub-Registrar of the Co-operative Department,
for a period of one year from the 9th November 2016.
PRADEEP YADAV,
