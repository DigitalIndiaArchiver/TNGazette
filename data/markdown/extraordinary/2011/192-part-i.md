APPOINTMENT OF MINISTER FOR THE STATE OF TAMIL NADU
[G.O. Ms. No. 557, Public (Special-B), 29th June 2011, Aani 14, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No. I/PUSP/16(b-1)/2011.
I, Surjit Singh Barnala, Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoint Thiru A. Mohammed John, with effect from the
Forenoon of 29th June, 2011, to be the Minister of my Council of Ministers.
SURJIT  SINGH  BARNALA,
Governor of Tamil  Nadu.
ALLOCATION  OF  SUBJECTS  AMONG  MINISTERS
[G.O. Ms. No. 558, Public (Special-B), 29th June 2011, Aani 14, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No. I/PUSP/16(b-2)/2011.
Under Clause (3) of Article 166 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby allocates
the subjects Backward Classes, Most Backward Classes and Denotified Communities, Overseas Indians,
Refugees and Evacuees and Minorities including Wakf to Thiru A. Mohammed John, appointed as a Minister in the
G.O. Ms. No. 557, Public (Spl.B) Department, dated 29th June 2011.  Consequent on the above allocation,
Thiru A. Mohammed John be designated as Minister for Backward Classes and Minorities Welfare.
DEBENDRANATH  SARANGI,
Chief  Secretary.
