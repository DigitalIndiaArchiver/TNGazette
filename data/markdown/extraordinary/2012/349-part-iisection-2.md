[G.O. Ms. No. 1065, Public (Buildings), 21st December 2012.]
AMENDMENT TO NOTIFICATION
No. II(2)/PUBG/886(b)/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Commissions of Inquiry Act, 1952
(Central Act LX of 1952), the Governor  of  Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment  to the
Public (Buildings) Department’s Notification No.II(2)/PUBG/286(c)/2011, dated the 22nd June 2011,
published at  pages 1–2 of Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary,
dated the 22nd  June 2011,  as subsequently amended.
AMENDMENT
In the said Notification, in paragraph 3, for the expression “eighteen months”, the expression “twenty four
months” shall be substituted.
A. CHITTARANJAN MOHANDOSS,
Secretary to Government.
