HOME DEPARTMENT
DRAFT AMENDMENTS TO THE TAMIL NADU MOTOR VEHICLES RULES, 1989.
[G.O. Ms. No. 784, Home (Transport-1), 18th November 2011.]
No. SRO A-20(c)/2011.
In Supersession of the Home Department Notification No.SRO A-26/2010, published at page 48 of
Part III – Section 1 (a) of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 28th July 2010, the following draft of
amendments to the Tamil Nadu Motor Vehicles Rules, 1989, which it is proposed to make in exercise of the powers
conferred by Sections 28, 38,65,96,107,111 and 138 read with Section 211 of the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988
(Central Act 59 of 1988) is hereby published for the information of all persons likely to be affected thereby, as required
by sub-section (1) of Section 212 of the said Act.
2.  Notice is hereby given that the draft amendments will be taken into consideration on or after the expiry of
thirty days from the date of publication of this Notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette and that any
objection or suggestion, which may be received from any person with respect thereto before the expiry of the
aforesaid period will be considered by the Government of Tamil Nadu.
3.  Objection or suggestion, if any, should be addressed in duplicate to the Principal Secretary to Government,
Home, Prohibition and Excise Department, Chennai-600 009.
DRAFT AMENDMENTS.
In the said Rules:—
(i)
in rule 49, after the expression “or through chalan in Government sub-treasury or treasury”, the expression
“or by on line payment through the website of Transport Department” shall be inserted;
(ii)
in rule 86, after the expression “or through India Post Office”, the expression “or by online payment through
the website of Transport Department” shall be inserted;

EXPLANATORY NOTE
At present, taxes and fees prescribed under the Tamil Nadu Motor Vehicles Rules, 1989 are paid in the Regional
Transport Offices/Unit Offices or at Treasuries and through authorized Banks. Now the Government have decided
to authorize “Online Payment through the website of Transport Department”, so as to facilitate more convenience
and transparency in tax payment.
