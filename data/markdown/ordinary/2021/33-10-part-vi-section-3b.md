Under sub-statue (1) of Statute 5 of Chapter-XXI of the General Statutes of the Tamil Nadu  Dr. M.G.R. Medical 
University, Chennai it is hereby published that the following two members are unopposly elected to the Governing Council 
of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai by the Senate from among  its members Under Section 21 
(4) (a)  of  The  Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai, Act, 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 37 of 1987) in the 
regular vacancies.

              Nagore Main Road,
              Nagapattinam-611 002.

  Prof. Dr. A. Jayasudha, M.Sc(N)., Ph.D.,(N)  
             Principal,
             P.S.G. College of  Nursing,
             Avinashi Road,
             Peelamedu
             Coimbatore-641 004.
2. The tenure of the above members in the Governing Council of this University shall be for a period of 03 (Three) 
years from 09.02.2021 to 08.02.2024 or the date on which they cease to be  members of the Senate, whichever is earlier.
