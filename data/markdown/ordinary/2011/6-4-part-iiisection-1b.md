Amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Geology and Mining Subordinate Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 177, Industries (E1), 23rd December 2010.]
No. SRO B-7/2011.—In exercise of the powers conferred by  the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Geology and
Mining Subordinate Service.
2. The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 19th January 1987.

No. SRO B-8/2011.—In exercise of the powers conferred by  the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Geology and Mining

AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules,—
(1) in rule 2, for clause (a), the following clause shall be substituted, namely:—
 “(a) By direct recruitment from among the categories of Municipal Employees in Class-l or Class-I ‘A’ of the
Tamil Nadu Municipal General Service and the candidates to be appointed shall be selected based on seniority and
assessment by a Selection Committee consisting of the following officials:—
The Director/Commissioner of Municipal  Administration.
Chairman.
The second level officer of the Directorate/Commissionerate of
Member-Secretary.
Municipal Administration in-charge of the Municipal Commissioners’
establishment
An officer nominated by the Secretary to Government,
Member.
Municipal Administration and Water Supply Department
  Provided that the appointment shall be made by the Government with the concurrence of the Commission.
  Provided also that the guidelines followed in respect of selection through Departmental Promotion Committee
be followed by the Selection Committee.”;
(2) in rule 4, in the Table, in column (1), for item (a), the following item shall be substituted, namely:—
 “(a) By direct recruitment from the categories of Municipal Employees in Class I or Class IA of the
Tamil Nadu Municipal General Service.”;
(3) in rule 5, after the expression “15th March of every year”, the following expression shall be added, namely:—
 “for the purpose of recruiting Municipal Employees as Municipal Commissioner, Grade-II by direct recruitment
through a Selection Committee specifically constituted for the purpose, the crucial date on which the Municipal Employees
should possess the prescribed qualifications shall be 15th March of every year.” ;
(4) in rule 6, after the words “Open Market” the words “or from among Municipal Employees” shall be inserted;
(5) in rule 7, after the words “Open Market” the words “or from among Municipal Employees” shall be inserted.
