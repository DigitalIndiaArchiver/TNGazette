GENERAL  NOTIFICATION
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI.
Appointment of Executive Officer to Jumma Pallivasal, Kallakurichi, Villupuram District as per
Section 38 of the Wakf Act, 1995.
(Lr. Rc. No. 8878/07/B4/Vpm.)
No. VI-3(a)/146/2013.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the Provisions under section 38 of the Wakf Act 1995, the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board
in its resolution in item No. 80/13 in Rc.No. 8878/07/B4/Vpm dated: 22nd August 2013 has resolved to appoint Executive
Officer to Jumma Pallivasal, Kallakurichi, Villupuram District. The Wakf Inspector, Villupuram, who was appointed as
Executive Officer of the Wakf on 28th February 2013, shall continue as the Executive Officer, under this order. The Board
has also resolved to ratify the period of direct management from 22nd July 2013 to 22nd August 2013.
Chennai-600 001,
F. ABDUL RAZICK,
24th October 2013.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
