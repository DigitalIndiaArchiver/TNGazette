In the terms and conditions of service for the State 
Chief Information Commissioner and State Information 
Commissioners laid down in sub-section (5) of Section 16 of 
the Right to Information Act, 2005, (Central Act 22 of 2005) 
provisions are available only for salaries and allowances. 
Accordingly, the pay in respect of persons holding the 
posts of State Chief Information Commissioner and State 
Information Commissioners are ﬁ xed at the rate of Rs.90,000/- 
and Rs.80,000/-p.m. respectively in the pre-revised scale. 
The term "Allowances"  found in the said section 16(5) of the 
said Act, is silent regarding the grant of pension to State 
Chief Information Commissioner and State Information 
Commissioners for the services rendered by them in the 
State Information Commission.
2. In the order ﬁ rst read above, the Central Information 
Commission recognizing the high position held by Information 
Commissioners in preserving the paramountcy of the 
democratic ideal, among others, has framed Terms and 
Conditions of Service for sanction of Pension to the Chief 
Information Commissioner and Information Commissioners in 
the Central Information Commission. In the order second 
read above the quantum of pension was revised with eﬀ ect 
from 01.01.2006 to both Chief Information Commissioner 
and Information Commissioners at the rate of Rs.16,020/-
per annum in respect of each completed year of service. 
3. The Government have examined the proposal to. 
sanction pension to the retired State Chief Information 
Commissioners and Information Commissioners in detail. There 
is no speciﬁ c provision for grant of pension in the Right 
to Information Act 2005. The retired Government oﬃ  cials 
who acted as Information Commissioners would be receiving 
'service pension' and any pension contemplated under the 
Right to Information Act for the services rendered in the 
Commission would be by way of an 'additional pension' 
to them whereas those who are not retired Government 
Oﬃ  cials and acted as State Chief Information Commissioners 
and Information Commissioners are not in receipt of any 
pension.  
4. The Government, after careful consideration has 
decided to sanction pension only to persons who are not 
retired Government oﬃ  cials and held the posts of State Chief 
Information Commissioner' / State Information Commissioners 
for a minimum period of three completed years, at the rate of 
Rs.16,020/- per annum (pre-revised) in respect of each 
completed year of service in the Tarnil Nadu State Information 
Commission and direct accordingly. 
5. The Terms and Conditions for sanction of pension 
to the State Chief Information Commissioner and State 
Information Commissioners are as follows. 
i.  
Persons 
served 
as 
State 
Chief 
Information 
Commissioners / Information Commissioners and 
who are not retired Government Oﬃ  cials are eligible;
ii.  those who have served for not less than three years 
as State Chief Information Commissioner and State 
Information Commissioners are eligible; and 
iii.  the quantum of pension shall be Rs.16,020/- per 
annum (pre-revised) for each completed year 
of service in the Tamil Nadu State Information 
Commission. 
6. This order shall take eﬀ ect from the next day of 
demitting oﬃ  ce by the State Chief Information Commissioner 
and State Information Commissioners concerned. 
7. This order issues with the Concurrence of Finance 
(Pension) Department, vide its U.O.No. 7666/Fin (Pen)/2018, 
dated 07-03-2018.
