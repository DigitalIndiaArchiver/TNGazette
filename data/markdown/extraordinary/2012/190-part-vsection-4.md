 NOTIFICATION BY THE ELECTION COMMISSION OF INDIA
PLACE AND TIME FOR COUNTING OF VOTES FOR PRESIDENTIAL ELECTION, 2012.
No. SRO G-32/2012.
The following Notification of the Election Commission of India,  New Delhi, dated 10th July, 2012
[19 Asadha, 1934 (Saka)] is republished:—
No.479/16/2012.—-In pursuance of rule 27 of the Presidential and Vice-Presidential Elections Rules,
1974, the Election Commission of India hereby appoints the 22nd July, 2012 (Sunday) as the day on
which, and 11.00 A.M. of that day as the time at which, the counting of votes for the Presidential Election,
2012, shall take place at the office of the Returning Officer in Room No.63, First Floor, Parliament
House, New Delhi.
(By Order)
K.N. BHAR,
Secretary,
Election Commission of India.
PRAVEEN  KUMAR,
Secretariat,

Chennai-600 009,
