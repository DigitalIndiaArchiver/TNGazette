NOTIFICATION  BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY SECRETARIAT.
LEGISLATIVE  ASSEMBLY  SECRETARIAT
APPOINTMENT OF DEPUTY SPEAKER  OF THE  TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY AS THE PERSON BEFORE
WHOM THE MEMBERS MAY MAKE AND SUBSCRIBE OATH OR AFFIRMATION.
[S.O. Ms. No. 181, Legislative Assembly Secretariat, 2nd November 2012,
Aippasi 17, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2043.]
No. II(1)/LAS/27(b)/2012.
“In exercise of the powers conferred by Article 188 of the Constitution of India, I, Dr. K. ROSAIAH, Governor of Tamil Nadu,
hereby appoint Thiru Pollachi V. Jayaraman, Deputy Speaker, Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly, as the person before
whom the Oath or Affirmation may be made and subscribed by the Members of the Legislative Assembly under the
said Article during the term of his office.
Raj Bhavan,
K. ROSAIAH,
Guindy,
Governor of Tamil Nadu.
Chennai-600 022.
Dated: 31st October, 2012.
A.M.P. JAMALUDEEN,
Secretary.”
