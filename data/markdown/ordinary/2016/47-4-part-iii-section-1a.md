No. SRO A-22/2016.—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 77-A of the Tamil Nadu
District Municipalities Act,1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following
Rules, namely:-
RULES
1. Short Title and commencement.—(a) These rules may be called the Tamil Nadu Municipal Information Technology
Service Rules, 2016.
(b) They shall be deemed to have come into force with effect on and from the 30th October 1998.

(2) The Commissioner of Municipal Administration shall call for a list of eligible candidates from the local Employment
Exchange and the Commissionerate of Employment and Training, Chennai. He shall also notify
the vacancies, inviting applications from eligible candidates, in atleast two local newspapers having wide circulation in the
State, one of which shall be in Tamil. On receipt of the list of candidates and the applications, he shall place the names
and details of eligible candidates before the Selection Committee. The Selection Committee shall select the required
number of candidates for appointment after conducting a preliminary written test and an oral interview.
7. Appointing Authority.—The appointing authority for all the posts in the Service shall be the Commissioner of
Municipal Administration.
8. Age.-No person shall be eligible for appointment to any of the post in the Service by direct recruitment if he has
completed the age mentioned below, on the first day of July of the year in which the selection for appointment is made:-
(a) For Scheduled Caste and Scheduled Tribe
-
40 years
(b) Others
-
35 years
9. Test.- No person shall be eligible for promotion to any of the posts in the Service unless he had passed the
following departmental tests, namely:-
(a) Account Test for Subordinate Officers - Part I
(b) Tamil Nadu Government Office Manual Test
(c) Any other test or training that may be specified by the Government from time to time.
10. Probation.-(1) Every person appointed to a post in the Service by direct recruitment, shall be on probation for
a total period of two years on duty within a continuous period of three years from the date of joining duty in that post.
(2) The Regional Director of Municipal Administration, shall be the authority competent to declare the completion of
probation in the posts of Assistant Programmer and Data Entry Operator.
(3) No person shall be eligible for promotion unless he has satisfactorily completed probation in anyone of the posts
in the Service.
11. Preparation of Annual list of approved candidates.-For the purpose of drawing up of the annual list of approved
candidates for appointment by promotion or by direct recruitment to the posts specified in the column (2) of the Table
