APPOINTMENT OF THIRU M.K. STALIN, MINISTER FOR RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND LOCAL ADMINISTRATION AS
DEPUTY CHIEF MINISTER
[G.O. Ms. No. 467, Public (Special-B), 29th May 2009,
Vaikasi 15, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. I/PUSP/7(a-1)/2009.
I, Surjit Singh Barnala, Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoint Thiru M.K. Stalin, Minister for
Rural Development and Local Administration as Deputy Chief Minister with immediate effect.
SURJIT SINGH BARNALA,
 Governor of Tamil Nadu.
ALLOCATION OF SUBJECTS AMONG MINISTERS
[G.O. Ms. No. 468, Public (Special-B), 29th May 2009,
Vaikasi 15, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. I/PUSP/7(a-2)/2009.
Under Clause (3) of Article 166 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that the subjects,
General Administration, District Revenue Officers, Industries, Minorities Welfare, Passports, Special Initiatives and
Social Reforms hitherto held by Hon'ble Chief Minister be re-allocated to Thiru M.K. Stalin, Deputy Chief Minister in
addition to the subjects held by him as Minister for Rural Development and Local Administration.
   K.S. SRIPATHI,
    Chief Secretary.
