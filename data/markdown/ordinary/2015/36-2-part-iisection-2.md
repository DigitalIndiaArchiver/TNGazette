LAW DEPARTMENT
Removal of certain Notary Name in the Register of
Notaries in Chennai under the Notaries Act.
[G.O. Ms. No. 289, Law (Administration), 13th August 2015.]
No. II(2)/LAW/530/2015.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by clause (d) of Section 10 of the Notaries Act,
1952 (Central Act LIII of 1952) read with sub-rule (13) of
rule 13 of the Notaries Rules, 1956, the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby removes the name of Thiru N. Kumar,
Notary in the City of Chennai from the Register of Notaries
maintained by the Government under Section 4 of the
said Act.
