HOME DEPARTMENT
Amendment to the Tamil Nadu Special Police Subordinate Service Rules.
[G.O. Ms. No. 1043, Home (Pol.IX), 28th November 2013.]
No. SRO B-1/2014.—In exercise of the powers conferred by  Sections 8 and 10 of the Tamil Nadu District Police Act, 1859,
(Central Act XXIV of 1859) and Sections 9 and 11 of the Chennai City Police Act, 1888 (Tamil Nadu Act III of 1888) read with
the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment ot the
Tamil Nadu Special Police Subordinate Service Rules, 1978 (Section 44 in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual,
1986):—
2. The amendments hereby made and shall be deemed to have come into force with effect on and from the
18th April 2011.
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in rule 14 in sub-rule (f), in the tabular column, the Explanation in column (2), excepting the provisos
thereunder, against the entry "Category 6 Constables (men and women) in column (1)" shall be omitted.
Amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Jail Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 1146, Home (Prison-I), 13th December 2013.]
No. SRO B-2/2014.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Jail Service
(Section 20 of the Special Rules in Volume-II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1969).
2. The amendment hereby made and shall be deemed to have come into force on the 26th December 1990.
AMENDMENT
In the said Special Rules, for the expression "District Office Manual", wherever it occurs, the expression "Tamil Nadu
Government Office Manual" shall be substituted.
NIRANJAN MARDI,
Principal Secretary to Government.
LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT DEPARTMENT
Amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Employment and Training Subordinate Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 292, Labour and Employment (P1), 13th December 2013, Karthigai 27, Vijaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2044.]
No. SRO B-3/2014.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Employment and
Training Subordinate Service (Section 7B of the Tamil Nadu Service Manual Volume-III).
AMENDMENT
In the said Special Rules, in rule 2, in the tabular column under sub-rule (a), under the heading "CLASS-I", in column (2),
against the Category "(1) Training Officers in the Industrial Training Institutes/Centres." In column (1) thereof, for item (ii) and
the entries relating thereto, the following shall be substituted, namely:—
(ii) Promotion in the ratio of 1:1 as between,-
(a) Degree/Diploma holders; and
(b) Certificate holders with S.S.L.C. and five years service as Assistant Training Officers;.

No. SRO B-4/2014.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the rules published with the Information, Tourism and Tamil
Culture Department Notification SRO No. B-48/82, at pages 64 and 65 of Part III—Section 1(b) of the Tamil Nadu Government
Gazette, dated the 10th February 1982.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules:—
(1) for rule 3 the following rule shall be substituted, namely:—
"3. Appointment.
Appointment to the post shall be made:—
(i) by direct recruitment; or
(ii) by promotion from among the holders of the post of Librarian-cum-Caretaker in various Memorials under the control
of Information and Public Relations Department:
(iii) by recruitment by transfer from any other service".
(2) In rule 5 for sub-rule (b), the following sub-rule shall be substituted, namely:—
“(b) Other Qualifications:—
No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post, unless be possesses the following qualifications, namely:-
(1) for direct recruitment and by recruitment by transfer from any other service:-
 (i) Passed S.S.L.C. or X Standard; and
 (ii) Experience as Guide for a period of not less than two years in a concern recognised by the State or Central
Government.
(2) for promotion, must have completed service of not less than two years in the post of Librarion-cum-Caretaker in various
Memorials under the control of Information and Public Relations Department.".
M. RAJARAM,
Secretary to Government.
