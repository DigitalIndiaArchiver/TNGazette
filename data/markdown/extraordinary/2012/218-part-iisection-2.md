The Governor of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that
the lands specified in the schedule below have to be
acquired for Industrial purpose, to wit for the formation of
ULTRA MEGA POWER PROJECT, CHEYYUR and it has
already been decided that the entire amount of compensation
to be awarded for the lands is to be paid out of the funds
controlled or managed by the Officer Authorised Signatory,
Coastal Tamil Nadu Power Ltd., New Delhi and having
considered  the cause shown by the owners or other persons
having interest the said lands, the Joint Commissioner (Land
Reforms), Villupuram concerned, the interested party for the
lands covered under Tamil Nadu Land Reforms (fixation of
ceiling on land) Act 1961, as the case may be, do hereby
publish the following notice under sub-section (1) of
Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu  Acquisition of Land  for Industrial
Purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu  Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub – section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Acquisition of Lands  for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999) the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby acquired the lands specified in the Schedule below
and measuring an extent of 2.85.0 Hectares to the same, a
little more of less are needed for Industrial Purposes, to wit
for the formation of ULTRA MEGA POWER PROJECT,
CHEYYUR No.55, Vedal Village, Cheyyur Taluk,
Kancheepuram District.
The Plan of the Lands are kept in the office of the special
Tahsildar, (land Acquisition) Unit –III, ULTRA MEGA POWER
PROJECT CHEYYUR scheme, and may be inspected at any
time during office hours.
