No. SRO B-8/2013.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu General Subordinate
Service (Section 13 in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1970).
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 8th June 2012.
AMENDMENT
In the said Special Rules, in Part-II, in Class XXII, in rule 8, for the expression “and Craft Attenders (Category-4) by direct
recruitment”, the expression “Craft Attenders (Category-4) and Stamping Smiths (Category-8), by direct recruitment” shall be
substituted.
M. KUTRALINGAM,
Principal Secretary to Government.
TOURISM AND CULTURE DEPARTMENT
Amendment to Ad hoc Rules relating to the Post of Artist Modeller in Government Museum, Chennai.
[G.O.Ms. No. 24, Tourism and Culture (MA1), 11th February 2013, Thai 29, Nandhana,Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2044.]
No.SRO B-9/2013.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Rules published with the Education and Public Health Department
Notification No. SRO A 246 of 1961, at page 325 of Part-V of the Fort St. George Gazette, dated the 15th March 1961.
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force with effect from 23rd August 1989.
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in rule 5, for clause (i) the following clause shall be substituted namely:—
(i)  In the case of Artist Grade-I, must have passed the S.S.L.C. and must possess a Diploma in Advanced painting or
Modelling issued by the School / College of Arts and Crafts, Chennai and must possess capacity and aptitude for technical work
such as preparing models and moulds of Museum  specimens and painting of the interior of show cases in the Museum Galleries
to the satisfaction of the appointing authority.
In the said rule 6, the following rule shall be substituted namely:—
3. There shall be paid to the holders of the posts monthly pay calculated in the scale detailed below or in any other revised
scale as may be introduced subsequently:—
Artist Grade-I-Rs. 1400-40-1600-50-2300-60-2600.
Provided that nothing contained in this clause shall adversely affect the holders of the post on the 11th February 2013.
RAMESHRAM MISHRA,
Principal Secretary to Government.

Revision of Scale of Pay Artist Grade-I
No.SRO B-10/2013.
[Public Services—Government Museum, Chennai—Post of Artist Modeller redesignated as Artist Grade I—Revision of Scale
of Pay—Orders—Issued.]
The following Government Order is published:
[G.O.Ms. No. 25, Tourism and Culture (MA1), 11th February 2013, Thai 29, Nandhana, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2044.]
READ:
    [G.O.Ms. No. 24, Tourism and Culture Department, dated: 11-2-2013.]
Order:   No. 25, Tourism and Culture (MA1), 11th February 2013
Based on the recommendations of the V Tamil Nadu Pay Commission, Government issued orders in their Letter
No. 88189/PC-IA/89-1, Finance (PC) Department, dated 23-08-1989 classifiying the posts of Artist/Cameraman/Photographer in
all Departments under common categories and into three grades and fixed the revised Scale of Pay for these grades as given
below:—
Grade      I - Rs. 1400-2600
Grade      II - Rs. 1200-2040
Grade     III - Rs.  950-1500
2. In Government letter No. 133073/PC-IA/89-1, Finance (Pay Commission) Department, dated 10-11-89, Government
further classified that the qualifications prescribed in the Ad hoc Rules for the post alone should be taken into account for the
purpose of classification and not the qualification possessed by the individuals. Based on the directions of Hon'ble High Court
of Madras, the Principal Secretary  and Commissioner of Museums has sent the proposal for redesignation of the post of Artist
Modeller as Artist Grade-I in the Department of Museums. Accordingly, the amendment of the Ad hoc Rules for the post of Artist
Modeller as Artist Grade-I and the revised scale of pay has been notified in the orders cited.
3. Consequent on the revision of scale of pay as order in the G.O.cited, the Government hereby orders that
Thiru M.P. Raja Sivaprakash, Artist Grade-I shall get the benefits as per further revision of scale of pay as on 1-1-1996
and 1-1-2006.
4. This orders issues with the concurrence of Finance Department vide its U.O. No. 69335/Edn-II/2012, dated 05--02-2013.
(By order of the Governor)
RAMESHRAM MISHRA,
Principal Secretary to Government.
