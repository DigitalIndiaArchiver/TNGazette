NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
DTP—II-2 Ex. (262)
TAMIL DEVELOPMENT, RELIGIOUS, ENDOWMENTS AND INFORMATION DEPARTMENT
DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF THE TAMIL NADU HINDU RELIGIOUS AND CHARITABLE
ENDOWMENTS (AMENDMENT) ACT, 2009.
[G.O. (Ms.) No. 299, Tamil Development, Religious, Endowments and Information (RE 4-2),
1st October 2009, Purattasi 15, Thiruvalluvar Aandu 2040.]
No. II(2)/TDREI/510(b)/2009.
In exercise of the powers  conferred by sub-section (2) of  Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Hindu Religious
and Charitable Endowments (Amendment) Act, 2009 (Tamil Nadu Act 31 of 2009), the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the 1st day of October 2009 as the date on which the provisions of the said Act
shall come into force.
G. MUTHUSAMY,
Secretary to Government.
