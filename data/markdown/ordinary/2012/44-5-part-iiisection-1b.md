ANIMAL HUSBANDRY, DAIRYING AND FISHERIES DEPARTMENT
Amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Fisheries Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 179, Animal Husbandry, Dairying and Fisheries (FS-2), 18th October 2012,
Aippasi 2, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2043.]
No. SRO B-48/2012.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Fisheries
Service (Section 14 in Volume-II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1970).
2. The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 2nd August 2012.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules,—
(1) in rule 1, after the entry, “Category-1—Director of Fisheries”, the entry “Category 1(a)—Additional Director of
Fisheries” shall be inserted;
(2) in rule 2,—(i) in sub-rule (a), in the Table, after the entry “1. Director of Fisheries”, in column (1) and the
corresponding entry in column (2) thereof, the entries “1 (a). Additional Director of Fisheries” and “By promotion from among
the holders of the post of Joint Director of Fisheries” shall respectively be inserted;
(ii) in sub-rule (b), after the expression, “Promotion to the posts of Director of Fisheries”, the expression, “Additional
Director of Fisheries” shall be inserted;
(iii) in sub-rule (d),  under the heading “Appointing Authority”, in the Table, after the entry, “Director of Fisheries”
in column (1) and the corresponding entry in column (2) thereof, the entries “Additional Director of Fisheries” and “Government”
shall respectively be inserted;
(3) in rule 4, in sub-rule (b), in the Table, after the entry, “Director of Fisheries” in column (1) and the corresponding
entries in Column (2) and column (3) thereof, the entries “1 (a). Additional Director of Fisheries”, “By Promotion” and “Must
have put in a service of not less than three years as Joint Director of Fisheries” shall respectively be inserted.
GAGANDEEP SINGH BEDI,
Secretary to Government.
