[ELECTIONS—PANCHAYATS, TOWN PANCHAYATS, THIRD GRADE MUNICIPALITIES,
MUNICIPALITIES AND CORPORATIONS — CONDUCT OF —
HOURS OF POLL—NOTIFICATION OF.]
(S.O. No. 40/2011/TNSEC/PE)
No. VI(2)/457(i-3)/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred under rules 24 and 25 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Elections) Rules,
1995 and rules 22 and 23 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities, Municipalities and Corporations
(Elections) Rules, 2006 and in supersession of the earlier notifications, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission
hereby directs that the hours of poll in elections to Panchayats, Town Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities,
Municipalities and Corporations shall be 10 hours between 7.00 A.M. and 5.00 P.M. on the day notified for poll.
(By Order of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission)
