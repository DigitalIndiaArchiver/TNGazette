TAMIL NADU STATE ELECTION COMMISSION, CHENNAI.
[ELECTIONS - CONDUCT OF ELECTIONS TO PANCHAYATS - APPOINTMENT OF STATE ELECTION
OFFICER - NOTIFICATION ISSUED.]
(S.O.No. 1/2017/TNSEC/PE-I)
No.VI(2)/27(a-1)/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-rule (1) of rule 6 of the Tamil Nadu  Panchayats (Elections)
Rules, 1995, the State Election Commission hereby appoints the Commissioner/Director of Rural Development
and Panchayat Raj, Chennai, as the State Election Officer for performing the functions specified therein in
connection with the  conduct of Elections to Panchayats.
(By Order of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission)
Chennai-600 106,
T.S. RAJASEKAR,
3rd March 2017.
Secretary,
Tamil Nadu State Election Commission.
[ELECTIONS - CONDUCT OF ELECTIONS TO PANCHAYATS - APPOINTMENT OF DISTRICT ELECTION
OFFICERS - NOTIFICATION ISSUED.]
(S.O.No. 2/2017/TNSEC/PE-I)
No.VI(2)/27(a-2)/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-rule (2) of rule 6 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Elections)
Rules, 1995, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission hereby appoints the District Collectors concerned
excepting the Collector of Chennai as the District Election Officers for their respective Districts. Subject to the
superintendence, direction and control of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission, they shall co-ordinate

[ELECTIONS - PANCHAYATS - CONDUCT OF PREPARATION OF ELECTORAL ROLLS - APPOINTMENT OF
BLOCK DEVELOPMENT OFFICER (BLOCK PANCHAYAT) AS PANCHAYAT ELECTORAL REGISTRATION
OFFICER - NOTIFICATION ISSUED.]
(S.O.No. 3/2017/TNSEC/PE-I)
No.VI(2)/27(a-3)/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 30 of the Tamil  Nadu Panchayats
Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994) read with rule 7 and 14 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Elections)
Rules, 1995, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission hereby designates the Block Development Officers
(Block Panchayat) to be the Panchayat Electoral Registration Officers for the purposes of preparing and
publishing panchayat electoral rolls in respect of wards of panchayats lying within their respective jurisdiction
in connection with the conduct of Elections to Panchayats.
(By Order of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission)
Chennai-600 106,
T.S. RAJASEKAR,
