As per the High Court’s Official Memorandum in R.O.C. No. 4056/2010/C3, dated 3rd December 2010, it is hereby
notified that all the Civil and Sessions Court in the Civil Unit of the District Judge, Udhagamandalam, viz., District Court,

Udhagamandalam, Coonoor, Kotagiri and Gudalur and Additional District Munsif Court, Gudalur will be closed from
Sunday, the 1st day of May 2011 to Sunday, the 29th day of May 2011 (both days inclusive).

Saturday, the 1st day of October 2011 to Sunday, the 9th day of October 2011 (both days inclusive).

All the Civil and Sessions Court of the Unit of the District Judge, Nilgiris at Udhagamandalam will be closed from
Saturday, the 24th day of December 2011 to Saturday, the 31st day of October 2011 (both days inclusive).
Holidays for District and Sessions Court, Udhagamandalam, Sub-Court, Udhagamandalam, District Munsif's Court,

Notice is hereby given that the Civil and Sessions Courts in the District of Madurai will remain closed for Annual
Summer Vacation for 2011 as follows:—
1. The District and Sessions Court, Madurai, the Courts of Subordinate Judges/Assistant Sessions Judges of Madurai
and the Courts of District Munsif’s, Madurai Town, Madurai Taluk at Madurai, Thirumangalam and Melur will remain
closed from Sunday, the 1st May, 2011 to Sunday, the 29th May, 2011 (both days inclusive). They shall re-open on
Monday, the 30th May 2011 after Summer Vacation.
2. The Office of the Part-Time Official Receiver, Madurai will remain open throughout the Summer Vacation.

3. (i) No plaints or petitions except applications for grant of copies and for service and execution of process will

 (ii) In case of Vacation Civil Judge is appointed by the Government for this District, urgent plaints, Appeals and

Sessions Judges of Madurai and the Courts District Munsifs, Madurai Town, Madurai Taluk at Madurai, Tirumangalam
and Melur will remain closed from Saturday, the 24th December 2011 to Saturday, the 31st December 2011 (both days
inclusive).

Adjournment of Civil and Sessions Courts in Tiruvarur District for Summer Holidays 2011 and Holidays
for all Subordinate Civil and Sessions Courts of Tiruvarur District for the year 2011.
 (D.No. 4492/2010.)
No. VI(1)/20/2011.
In consonance with the instructions issued by Hon'ble High Court, Madras in its Official Memorandum
Roc. No. 4056/2010/C3, dated 3rd December  2010 it is hereby notified that the Civil and Sessions Courts in Tiruvarur
District will be closed for summer vacation during the year 2011 as hereunder and the following days are declared as
holidays for Subordinate Civil and Sessions Courts of Tiruvarur District during the year 2011.
SUMMER VACATION 2011
The District and Sessions Court of Tiruvarur District at Tiruvarur, the Courts of Subordinate and Assistant Sessions
Judge, Tiruvarur, the Court of Subordinate and Assistant Sessions Judge of Mannargudi and District Munsif Courts of
Tiruvarur, Tiruthuraipoondi, Mannargudi and Nannilam, Principal District Munsif and Additional District Munsif, Valangaiman
will be closed from Sunday, the 1st day of May, 2011 to Sunday, the 29th day of May, 2011 (both days inclusive).
The Office of the Part Time Official Receiver, Tiruvarur will remains open throughout the Summer Vacation.
No Plaints, or petition except applications for grant of copies and for service and execution of process will be
received during the above holidays.
The Office of the Copyist and Process Service Establishments will be kept open throughout the vacation, but no
arrest warrants will be executed during the period of adjournment.
The Office of the other Establishments shall remain closed. Arrangements shall however be made for the following:
1. For furnishing necessary records to the Copyist and Process Service Establishments for preparation of copies
and process respectively.
2. For transmitting records in Appeal and Sessions cases to Hon'ble High Court, Madras.
3. For all administrative matters.
The vacation Civil Judge appointed will attend to all urgent Civil matters during summer vacation.
Urgent application for bail should be made to Hon'ble High Court of Judicature, Madras in the absence of the
Sessions Judge from Sessions Division during the above adjournment.
All Civil Courts viz., District Court, Tiruvarur, Subordinate Judges Court, Tiruvarur and Mannargudi,  District Munsif
Courts, Tiruvarur, Tiruthuraipoondi, Mannargudi and  Nannilam, Principal District Munsif Court and Additional District
Munsif Court, Valangaiman will be closed for DASARA HOLIDAYS from Saturday the 1st day of October 2011 to
Sunday, the 9th day of October 2011 (both days inclusive) and for CHRISTMAS HOLIDAYS from Saturday,
the 24th day of December 2011 to Saturday, the 31st day of December 2011 (both days inclusive).
