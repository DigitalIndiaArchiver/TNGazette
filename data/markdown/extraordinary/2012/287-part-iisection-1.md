NOTIFICATION  BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY SECRETARIAT.
LEGISLATIVE  ASSEMBLY  SECRETARIAT
APPOINTMENT OF SPEAKER  OF THE  TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY AS THE PERSON BEFORE WHOM THE
MEMBERS MAY MAKE AND SUBSCRIBE OATH OR AFFIRMATION.
[S.O. Ms. No. 168, Legislative Assembly Secretariat, 11th October 2012,
Purattasi 25, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2043.]
No. II(1)/LAS/22(b)/2012.
“In exercise of the powers conferred by Article 188 of the Constitution of India, I, Dr. K. ROSAIAH, Governor of Tamil Nadu,
hereby appoint Thiru P. Dhanapal, Speaker, Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly, as the person before whom the Oath or
Affirmation may be made and subscribed by the Members of the Legislative Assembly under the said Article during the term
of his office.
Raj Bhavan,
K. ROSAIAH,
Guindy,
Governor of Tamil Nadu.
Chennai-600 022.
Dated: 10th October, 2012.
A.M.P. JAMALUDEEN,
Secretary.”
