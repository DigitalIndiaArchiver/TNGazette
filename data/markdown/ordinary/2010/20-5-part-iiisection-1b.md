HOME  DEPARTMENT
Amendment to Special Rules for Tamil Nadu Special Police Subordinate Service, 1978.
[G.O. Ms. No. 408, Home (Pol.IX), 27th April 2010.]
No. SRO B-41/2010.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Sections 8 and 10 of the Tamil Nadu District Police
Act, 1859 (Central Act XXIV of 1859) and Sections 9 and 11 of the Chennai City Police Act, 1888 (Tamil Nadu Act III of 1888)
read with the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following
amendment to the Tamil Nadu Special Police Subordinate Service Rules, 1978 (Section 44 in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu
Services Manual, 1986).
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force with effect on and from the
20th November 2009.
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in rule 16, for sub-rule (1), the following sub-rule shall be substituted, namely:—
“(1) Every person appointed to the posts of Inspector, Havildar and Naik, appointed otherwise than by promotion shall,
within the prescribed period of his probation, undergo training in Tamil Nadu Special Police work for a period of not less than
ten months to the satisfaction of the Commandant. The aforesaid period of training in respect of the post of Constables
shall be 22 weeks followed by one month practical training.”.
S. MALATHI,
Principal Secretary to Government.
TOURISM  AND  CULTURE  DEPARTMENT
Amendments to Ad hoc Rules relating to the post of Carpenter in Government Museum, Chennai.
[G.O. Ms. No. 77, Tourism and Culture (MA-1), 29th April 2010, Chithirai 16, Thiruvalluvar Andu-2041.]
No. SRO B-42/2010.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments  to the rules published with the Education and Public Health
Department Notification SRO No. A-246 of 1961, published at page 325 of Part V of the Fort St. George Gazette,
dated the 15th March 1961.
2.
The amendments hereby made shall come into force on 29th April 2010.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules, in rule 5,—
(1) for clause (ii), the following clause shall be substituted, namely:—
“(ii) In the case of Carpenter, must have passed the Secondary School Leaving Certificate Examination and must
possess Industrial Training Institute Certificate in Carpentry Trade and experience for a period of not less than three years
in carpentry work”.
(3) “Note (1) and Note (2) shall be omitted.”
Saving Clause:
Nothing contained in these rules should adversely affect the holders of the post on the date of issue of order.

Amendments to Ad hoc Rules relating to the post of Cabinet Maker, Painter/Polisher
in Government Museum, Chennai.
[G.O. Ms. No. 78, Tourism and Culture (MA-1), 29th April 2010, Chithirai 16, Thiruvalluvar Andu-2041.]
No. SRO B-43/2010.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby makes the following amendments  to the rules published with the Tamil Development-Culture
Department Notification SRO No. B. 295/89, at pages 205-206 of Part III—Section 1(b) of the Tamil Nadu Government
Gazette, dated the 30th August 1989.
2.
The amendment hereby made shall have come into force on 29th April 2010.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules, in rule 5, in sub-rule (b), in the Table:—
(1) for the entries in column (3) against the post “Cabinet Maker” in column (1) thereof, the following entries
shall be substituted, namely:—
(a) Must have passed the Secondary School Leaving Certificate Examination;
(b) Must possess Industrial Training Institute Certificate in Carpentary work;
(c) Must possess three years experience in Cabinet Making works;
(d) Must have passed the tests conducted by Curator, Design and Display Section of the Government Museum
in preparing Cabinet and preparing show cases.
(2) for the entries in column (3), against the post “Painter/Polisher”, in column (1) thereof, the following entries
shall be substituted, namely:—
(a) Must have passed the Secondary School Leaving Certificate Examination;
(b) Must possess Industrial Training Institute Certificate in Painting/Polishing;
(c) Must possess three years experience in Painting/Polishing works.
Saving clause:
Nothing contained in these rules should adversely affect the holders of the post on the date of issue of order.
