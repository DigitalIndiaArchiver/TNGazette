GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI.
Taking over the Direct Management of Hazarath Syed Ahmed Kabeer Jalaluddin Shahinsha Baba Dargah,
Pudur, Orakkadu Village, Ponneri Taluk, Tiruvallur District under Direct Control of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
(Rc. No. 11904/02/B3/TVLR.)
No. VI-3(a)/128/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995 the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board, in its
order in Item 93/10 in Rc. No. 11904/02/B3/TVLR, dated 1st June 2010 (Pronounced on 1st October 2010) has ordered
to take over the management of Hazarath Syed Ahmed Kabeer Jalaluddin Shahinsha Baba Dargah, Pudur, Orakkadu
Village, Ponneri Taluk, Tiruvallur District under Direct Control of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of one year
from the date of Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Publication and has appointed the Wakf Inspector, Tiruvallur as its
Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
7th October 2010.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
