WHEREAS, The Dhali Town Panchayat has passed a resolution to include the Kurumalai, Melkurumalai, Poochikottamparai 
and Thirumoorthimalai settlement areas of Dhali-2 Revenue Village, which are contiguous, within its local limits;
AND WHEREAS, on the recommendation of the District Collector, Tiruppur, the Commissioner of Town Panchayats 
has requested the Government to include the said local areas in the Dhali Town Panchayat;
AND WHEREAS, the said settlement areas of Dhali-2 Revenue Village do not form part of any other Panchayat Town;
Now, THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-clause (i) of clause (c) of sub-Section (3) of 
section 3-P of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920), the Governor of Tamil Nadu 
hereby includes the following local area within the local authority of Dhali Town Panchayat:-
Name of the Local area
Whether full or part
Revenue Village of Dhali-2
Full [Kurumalai Settlement Area, Melkurumalai 
Settlement Area, Poochikottamparai Settlement 
Area and Thirumoorthimalai Settlement Area]
Based on the above inclusion, wards will be divided for the next ordinary election.
