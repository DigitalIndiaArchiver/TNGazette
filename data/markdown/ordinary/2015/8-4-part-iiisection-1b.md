HOME DEPARTMENT
 Amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Jail Subordinate Service.

No. SRO B-17/2015.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Jail Subordinate
Service (Section 17 in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1970).
AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules, in Part-II, in Branch-I-(1) in rule 8, in the Table, under the heading “Class I”,in column (2),
against the entry “Warders, II Grade” in column (1), for the expression “Six months”, the expression “seven months” shall
be substituted;
(2) in rule 8-A, in sub-rule (a), for the expression “six months” the expression “Seven months” shall be substituted.
APURVA VARMA,
Principal Secretary to Government.
RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND PANCHAYAT RAJ DEPARTMENT
 Amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu General Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 03, Rural Development and Panchayat Raj (E3), 12th January 2015.]
No. SRO B-18/2015.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu General Service
(Section 16 in Volume II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1969).
2. The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into on the 2nd April 2014.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules, –
(i)
in Part I, the headings and entries relating to Class XLVII-Chief Radio Officer and Regional Officer to
Panchayat Radio Maintenance Organisation shall be omitted.
(ii) in Part II, Class XLVII-Chief Radio Officer and Regional Radio Officer to Panchayat Radio Maintenance
Organisation and the rules there under shall be omitted.
GAGANDEEP SINGH BEDI,
Secretary to Government.
