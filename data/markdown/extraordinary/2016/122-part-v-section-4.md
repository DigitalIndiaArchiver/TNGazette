Now, Therefore, in pursuance of sub-section (1) of Section 39 and Section 56 of the said Act, the Election Commission
of India hereby.—
(A) appoints, with respect to the said election:
(a) the 31st May, 2016 (Tuesday), as the last date for making nominations;
(b) the 1st June, 2016 (Wednesday), as the date for the scrutiny of the nominations;
(c) the 3rd June, 2016 (Friday), as the last date for the withdrawal of candidatures;
(d) the 11th June, 2016 (Saturday), as the date on which a poll shall, if necessary, be taken; and
(e) the 13th June, 2016 (Monday), as the date before which the election shall be completed; and
(B) fixes the hours from 9:00 A.M. to 4.00 P.M., as the hours during which the poll shall, if necessary, be taken in all
the above mentioned States on the date specified above for the election.
