No. SRO B-46/2019.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Ministerial Service 
(Section 22 in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 2016).
2. The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 19th January 2018.

In the said Special Rules,— 
(1) in rule 35, in sub-rule(ab), after the proviso, the following proviso shall be added, namely:-
 
"Provided further that on and from the 19th January 2018, a person with the disability of 100% blindness 
or 100% deafness and appointed as Assistant by promotion from the post of Typist or by transfer from the post of 
Steno-typist, Grade-III, need not undergo the said Foundational Training.";
(2) in Annexure-V, under the heading, "Tests to be passed, training to be undergone or other qualiﬁ cations to be acquired 
by persons appointed to the service:", in the tabular column,—
(i) in item No.8, after the third proviso, the following proviso shall be added, namely:-
 
"Provided also that on and from the 19th January 2018, a person with the disability of 100% blindness or 100% 
deafness and appointed as Junior Assistant by direct recruitment, need not undergo the said Foundational Training.";
(ii) in item No.8A, after the proviso, the following proviso shall be added, namely:-
 
"Provided further that on and from the 19th January 2018, a person with the disability of 100% blindness or 100% 
deafness and appointed as Junior Assistant or Junior Assistant-cum-Typist by recruitment by transfer, need not undergo the 
above Foundational Training.";
(iii) in item No.8B, after the proviso, the following proviso shall be added, namely:-
 
"Provided further that on and from the 19th January 2018, a person with the disability of 100% blindness or 100%
