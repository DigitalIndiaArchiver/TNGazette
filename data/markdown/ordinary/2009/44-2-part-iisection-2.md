COMMERCIAL TAXES AND REGISTRATION
DEPARTMENT.
Notifications under the Tamil Nadu value Added Tax Act.
Exemption in respect of the tax payable by Thiruvalargal
Helan Keller Service Society for the Disabled,
Madurai on the purchase of raw materials under
the Act.
[G.O. Ms. No. 153, Commercial Taxes and Registration (B2),
20th October 2009, Aippasi 3,
Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. II(2)/CTR/590/2009.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 30 read with Section
88 of the Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax Act, 2006 (Tamil Nadu
Act 32 of 2006), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes
an exemption in respect of tax payable under Section 12 of
the said Act by Thiruvalargal Helan Keller Service Society for
the Disabled, Madurai (Registered No. 49/79) on the purchase
of raw materials such as paper and paste used for envelope
making project.
2. This Notification shall be deemed to have come into
force on the 1st April 2003 and deemed to have remained in
force upto and inclusive of the 31st December 2006.
Appointment of certain person as Additional Member
(Judicial) of the Tamil Nadu Sales Tax Appellate
Tribunal (Additional Bench), Madurai under the Act.
[G.O. (2D) No. 219, Commercial  Taxes and Registration
(E2), 20th October 2009, Purattasi-3,
Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. II(2)/CTR/591/2009.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by sub-section (2) read with sub-section (3) of
Section 50 of the Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax Act, 2006
(Tamil Nadu Act 32 of 2006), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby appoints Tmt. V. Amaravathi, Sub-Judge, Poonamallee
as Additional Member (Judicial) of the Tamil Nadu Sales Tax
Appellate Tribunal (Additional Bench), Madurai, in the place
of Thiru K. Govindarajalu who has been transferred.
RAJEEV RANJAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.

HOUSING AND URBAN DEVELOPMENT DEPARTMENT
Exemption of the Land in R.S. No. 654-1, Fort. Tondiarpet
Taluk, Vepery, Chennai from certain provisions of
Development Control Rules for Chennai Metropolitan
Area under certain conditions under the Tamil Nadu
Town and Country Planning Act.
[G.O. Ms. No. 186, Housing and Urban Development (UD-I),
26th October 2009.]
No. II(2)/HOU/593/2009.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by Section 113 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country
Planning Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972), the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby exempts the land in R.S. No. 654/1,
Block No. 15, Fort-Tondiarpet Taluk, Door No. I-50,
E.V.K. Sampath Salai, Vepery, Chennai from the provisions
of Item 3(i)(b), relating to minimum required setback spaces
and spacing between blocks, items 5(1)(a), 5(4) and 6 of
ANNEXURE-IX, referred to in sub-rule (a) of rule 17 of the
Development Control Rules for Chennai Metropolitan Area,
for the additional construction of 2nd Floor over the existing
Ground Floor+First Floor, Periyar Urban Health Centre Block
at the above land, subject to the fulfilment of the following
conditions:—
(1)
The copy of the patta attested by the Revenue
Officials should be furnished; and
(2)
The plan tallying with boundary measurement as
per patta should be furnished.
SURJIT K CHAUDHARY,
Principal Secretary to Government.
