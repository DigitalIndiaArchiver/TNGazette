AMENDMENT TO THE TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY RULES
[Letter No.7637/2024-1,TNLAS (Bills III), dated : 07-01-2025.]
No. SRO A-B/2025.
"Under Rule 211(1) of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Rules, it is hereby notified that "TN Champions Foundation" 
may be added under the caption "Other Public Undertakings" specified in Appendix 'E' of the said Rules, the working of 
which shall be examined by the Committee of Public Undertakings of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly.
Consequently the following amendment is issued:-
Amendment.
In Appendix-E of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Rules, under the caption "Other Public Undertakings", after 
Serial No. 64, the following item shall be added:
	
65. TN Champions Foundation"
