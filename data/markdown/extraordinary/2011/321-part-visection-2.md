(S.O. No. 23/2011/TNSEC/ME-1.)
No. VI(2)/445(e-1)/2011.
READ:  S.O. No. 53/2006/TNSEC/ME, dated the 4th September, 2006.
In exercise of the powers conferred under  sub-rule (2) of rule 10 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Elections) Rules, 1995 and
sub-rule (2)  of rule 11 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities, Municipalities and Corporations (Elections)
Rules, 2006 and in supersession of the earlier Notifications, the State Election Commission hereby directs that the Assistant
Returning Officer appointed under the said Rules to assist the Returning Officer shall, subject to the control of the Returning
Officer, be competent to perform all the duties and functions of the Returning Officer except the following:—
1. Issue of notice of election; and
2. Declaration of result of election other than the Office of Councillor in respect of the Corporation.
(By Order of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission)
Chennai-600 106,
V.M. XAVIER CHRISSO NAYAGAM,
7th September 2011.
