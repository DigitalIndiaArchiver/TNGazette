No.SRO A-52/2017.—In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (i) of sub-section (1)  of Section 86 read with clause
(a) of sub-section (2) of Section 347 of  the Chennai City Municipal Corporation Act, 1919 (Tamil Nadu Act IV of 1919),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Chennai Corporation General Service
Rules, 1970, as  subsequently amended:-
(2)  (i) The amendments hereby made in respect of the post of  Assistant Accounts Officer shall be deemed to have
come into force with  effect on and from the 31st of August, 2010;
(ii) The amendments hereby made in respect of the post of  Chief Reporter shall be deemed to have come
into force with effect on  and from the 30th April, 2015;
(iii) The amendments hereby made in respect of the post of  Sports Officer shall be deemed to have come into
force with effect on  and from the 27th May, 2015.
