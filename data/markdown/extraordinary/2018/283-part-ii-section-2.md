DEMISE OF HIGH DIGNITARIES THIRU M. KARUNANIDHI, FORMER CHIEF MINISTER OF

[G.O. Ms. No. 595, Public (Protocol-I), 7th August 2018,  Aadi 22, Vilambi,

No. II(2)/PUSP/695(e)/2018.
The Government of Tamil Nadu announce with profound regret, the demise of
the former Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu, Thiru M. Karunanidhi, at 18.10 Hrs on 07-08-2018.
2. He had the distinction of holding the office of Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu, five times since
1969. He was a Champion of social justice. The demise of the former Chief Minister of
Tamil Nadu, Thiru M. Karunanidhi, is an irreparable loss to the people of Tamil Nadu.
(By Order of the Governor)
GIRIJA VAIDYANATHAN,
Chief Secretary.
