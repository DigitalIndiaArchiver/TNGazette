No. I/PUSP/11/2009.—The following Notification of the
Government of India, Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances
and Pensions, Department of Personnel and Training,
New Delhi, dated 20th May 2009 is republished:—
In exercise of the powers conferred by Rule 6(1) of the
Indian Administrative Service (Cadre) Rules, 1954, and with
the concurrence of the Government of Tamil Nadu and the
Ministry of Home Affairs as the respective cadre controlling
authorities, Central Government hereby accords approval for
the inter cadre deputation of Shri R. Lalvena, IAS (TN: 2001)
from Tamil Nadu Cadre to AGMUT cadre (Mizoram) for a
period of three years with effect from the date Shri R. Lalvena
assumes charge of the post.
[No. 13017/10/2007 AIS(I)]
R.K. GUPTA,
Under Secretary to Government of India.
(By Order of the Governor)
K.S. SRIPATHI,
Chief Secretary.
