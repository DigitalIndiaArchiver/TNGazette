HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE DEPARTMENT
Framing of Adhoc Rules relating to the Posts of Research Assistant and Microbiologist at Leptospirosis 
Lab at Madras Medical College, Chennai in Tamil Nadu Medical Service
[G.O.Ms. No. 367, Health and Family Welfare (AA1) 23rd September 2020, Purattasi 7, Saarvari, 
Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2051.]
No. SRO B-30/2020.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following Rules:-
2. The Rules hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on and from the 16th December 2014.
Rඝඔඍඛ.
The General Rules for the Tamil Nadu State and Subordinate Service and the Special Rules applicable to the holders 
of the permanent post in Branch-I Medical of the Tamil Nadu Medical Service shall apply to the holder of the temporary 
post of Research Assistant at the Institute of Microbiology, Madras Medical College, Chennai. Subject to the Modiﬁ cations 
speciﬁ ed in the following Rules:
Provided that with eﬀ ect on and from the 15th September 2016, the provisions contained in the Tamil Nadu Government 
Servants (Conditions of Service) Act, 2016 (Tamil Nadu Act 14 of 2016) and the Special Rules applicable to the holders of 
the permanent posts in Branch-I Medical of the Tamil Nadu Medical Service shall apply to the holder of the post.
2. Constitution.-The post shall constitute a distinct class in the said service.
3. Appointment.-Appointment to the post shall be made as follows:-
 
(i) 
by recruitment by transfer from the Tamil Nadu Medical Subordinate Service;
 
(ii) 
by direct recruitment, if no qualiﬁ ed and suitable candidate is available for Appointment to the post by the 
above method.
4. Qualiﬁ cations.-(a) Age.- No. person shall be eligible for Appointment to the post by direct recruitment, if he has 
completed or will complete thirty two years of age on the ﬁ rst day of July of the year in which the selection for appointment 
is made;
   (b) Other Qualiﬁ cation.-No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post, unless he possesses the following 
qualiﬁ cations namely:-
 
(i) 
a Post Graduate degree in Microbiology of any University recognized by the University Grants Commission;  
 
 
and
 
(ii) 
must have served in Leptospirosis Laboratory for a period of not less than ﬁ ve years.
5. Probation.-(a) Every person appointed to the post by direct recruitment shall, from the date on which he joins duty, 
be on probation for a total period of two years on duty within a continuous period of three years;
 
(b) Every person appointed to the post by recruitment by transfer shall from the date on which he joins duty, be 
on probation for a total period of one year on duty within a continuous period of two years.
6. Tests.-(a) Every person appointed to the post shall pass the Account Test for Executive Oﬃ  cers within the period of 
his probation;
 
(b) Every person appointed to the post shall also pass the Departmental Test in the Tamil Nadu Medical Code 
within a period of four years from the date of appointment, failing which future increments in the time scale pay shall be 
postponed without cumulative eﬀ ect till he pass the said test.
7. Pay.- There shall be paid to the holder of the post, a monthly pay calculated in the Pay Band of 
PB3 of Rs. 15600-39000 with Grade pay of Rs. 5400:
Provided that with effect on and from the 1st January 2016, a monthly pay shall be calculated in Level 
22-Rs.56100-177500 under the Tamil Nadu Revised Pay Rules, 2017.
8. Saving.- Nothing contained in these rules shall adversely aﬀ ect any person holding the post as on the date of 
publication of these Rules in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
