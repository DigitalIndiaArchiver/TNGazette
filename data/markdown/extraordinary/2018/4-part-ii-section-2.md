The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that 
the lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below have to be acquired 
for industrial purposes to wit to the formation of Double way 
Broad – Guage Railway line by changing the existing single 
way Broad-Guage Railway line from Omalur – Mettur Dam 
and it having already been decided that the entire amount of 
compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid out 
of the lands controlled or managed by Southern Railway. The 
following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 3 
of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposed 
Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu 
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Government of Tamil Nadu 
hereby acquires the lands in 13.P.N.Patti Village, Mettur Taluk 
of Salem District as speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below and 
measuring 1.73.5 hectares (or) 17350 Sq.M to the same, 
a little more or less needed for industrial purposes, to wit, 
for formation of Double way Broad – Guage Railway line by 
changing the existing single way Broad-Guage Railway line 
from Omalur – Mettur Dam.
The plan of the lands are kept in the ofﬁ ce of the Land 
Acquisition Ofﬁ cer and District Revenue Ofﬁ cer, Salem and 
the Special Tahsildar, Omalur – Mettur Dam Double Line 
Broad-Guage Railway Track, Room No.414, 4th Floor, 
Collectorate Campus, Salem and may be inspected at any 
time during ofﬁ ce hours.
