Kottar, Kanyakumari District.
(Rc. No. 14827/07/B1/KK)
No. VI-3(a)/15/2015.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act 1995, as amended
by Act 27/2013, the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in item No. 7/15 in Rc. No. 14827/07/B1/KK
dated : 05-03-2015 has resolved to ratify the period of direct management from 31-01-2015 to 04-03-2015 and also
to extend the period of direct management of HAZRATH FAZILSHA AVULIA THAIKKA @ THAIKKA PALLI, KOTTAR,
KANYAKUMARI DISTRICT for a further period of Two years from the date of Board's Order (05-03-2015).
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED MEERAN,
23rd April 2015.
District Revenue Officer /
Chief Executive Officer (In-charge),
