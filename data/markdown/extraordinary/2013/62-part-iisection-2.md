ANIMAL HUSBANDRY, DAIRYING AND FISHERIES DEPARTMENT
APPOINTMENT OF CERTAIN PERSON AS FIRST VICE-CHANCELLORE OF TAMIL NADU FISHERIES UNIVERSITY FOR
CERTAIN PERIOD UNDER THE TAMIL NADU FISHERIES UNIVERSITY ACT.
[G.O.Ms.No. 66, Animal Husbandry, Dairying and Fisheries (FS-4) 7th March 2013, Maasi-23, Nandhana,
Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2044.]
No.II(2)/ADF/241(b)/2013.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 49 of theTamil Nadu Fisheries University Act, 2012 (Tamil Nadu Act
21 of 2012) and in supersession of the Animal Husbandry, Dairying and Fisheries Department Notification No.II(2)/ADFI/
762(f)/2012, Published at page 1 of Part II–Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette
Extraordinary, dated the 26th November 2012, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints  Dr. Baskaran Manimaran,
Professor, Fisheries College and Research Institute, Tamil Nadu Veterinary and Animal Sciences University, who has
been deputed on Foreign Service terms and conditions as Director (Technical), Coastal Aquaculture Authority, Ministry of
Agriculture, Government of India, Chennai-6, as the first Vice-Chancellor of the Tamil Nadu Fisheries University, Nagapattinam
for a period of three years from the date on which he assumes office or till he completes the age of seventy years,
whichever is earlier.
2. The terms and conditions of the appointment will be issued separately.
GAGANDEEP SINGH BEDI,
Secretary to Government.
