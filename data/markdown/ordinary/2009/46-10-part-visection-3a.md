GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD
No. 1, Jaffar Syrang Street, Vallal Seethakathi Nagar, Chennai-600 001.
Taking over the Direct Management of Balasamudram Pallivasal, Kanni Beevi Dargah and Mamanaradha
Dargah, Palani Taluk, Dindigul District.
(Lr. Rc. No. 1591/06/B15/DGL)
No. VI-3(a)/264/2009.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995, due to administrative
reasons the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its Resolution in item No. 57/09 in Rc. No. 1591/06/B15/DGL, dated
23rd September 2009 has ordered to take over the Management of Balasamudram Pallivasal, Kanni Beevi Dargah and
Mamanaradha Dargah, Palani Taluk, Dindigul District under the direct control of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period
of six months from the date of Board's order and to appoint the Executive Officer of Begampur Pallivasal, Dindigul
as its Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
30th October 2009.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
