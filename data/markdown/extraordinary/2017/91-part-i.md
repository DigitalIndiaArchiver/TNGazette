No. I/PUSP/9(a)/2017.:—The following Notification of the Government of India, Ministry of
Law and Justice  (Department of Justice) Jaisalmer House, 26, Man Singh Road, New Delhi-
110 011, dated 31st March 2017, is republished:-
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (1) of article 217 of the Constitution of India,
the President is pleased to appoint Kumari Indira Banerjee, Judge of the Delhi High Court,
to be the Chief Justice of the Madras High Court with effect from the date she assumes
charges of her office.
