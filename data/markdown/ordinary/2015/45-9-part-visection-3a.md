GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAQF BOARD, CHENNAI.
Extension of Period of Direct Management of Hazrath Syed Sha Hameed Aulia Dargah Waqf, Kanchipuram District.
(Lr. Rc.No. 10588/10/B4/KPM)
No.VI-3(a)/121/2015.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Waqf Act 1995, the Tamil Nadu
Waqf Board in its Resolution in Item No. 164/15 in Rc. No. 10588/10/B4/Kanchipuram  dated 30th September 2015 has
resolved to extend the period of direct management of Hazrath Syed Sha Hameed Aulia Dargah Waqf, Kanchipuram District
for one year from 12th September 2015 to 11th September 2016.
Chennai-600 001,
K. RASHEETH ALI,
5th November 2015.
District Revenue Officer/
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Waqf Board.
Extension of Direct Management of Hazrath Syed Sha Shahabuddin and Hazrath Syed Sha Bahahuddin Dargah
Waqf, Krishnagiri District.
(Rc.No. 5271/11/B2/K.Giri)
No.VI-3(a)/122/2015.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Waqf Act 1995 as amemded by
Act 27/2013, the Tamil Nadu Waqf Board in its Resolution in Item No. 112/15 in Rc. No. 5271/11/B2/K.Giri dated
30th September 2015 has extended the period of direct management of Hazrath Syed Sha Shahabuddin and Hazrath Syed
Sha Bahahuddin Dargah Waqf Krishnagiri District for the period of two years from 26th August 2015.
Chennai-600 001,
K. RASHEETH ALI,
5th November 2015.
District Revenue Officer/
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Waqf Board.
Extension of Direct Management of 5th George Arabic Madrasa Waqf, Pettai, Namakkal District.
(Rc.No. 4161/12/B2/NMKL)
No.VI-3(a)/123/2015.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Waqf Act 1995 as amemded by
Act 27/2013, the Tamil Nadu Waqf Board in its Resolution in Item No. 113/15 in Rc. No. 4161/12/B2/NMKL dated
30th September 2015 has  resolved to ratify the period of Direct Managaement of 5th George Arabic Madrasa Waqf, Pettai,
Namakkal District from 22nd August 2014 to 29th September 2015 and also to extend the period of Direct Management
of the above Waqf for the Period of two years from the Board's order dated 30th September 2015.
Chennai-600 001,
K. RASHEETH ALI,
5th November 2015.
District Revenue Officer/
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Waqf Board.
