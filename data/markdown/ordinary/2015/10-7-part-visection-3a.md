ADVERTISEMENT
Notice is hereby given that a petition for winding up of the above named respondent company M/s.Mount Mettur
Pharmaceutical Ltd., by the Hon’ble High Court of Madras on this the 13th day of February 2015 presented to the Court
by the petitioner above named. The said petition is directed to be heard before the Hon’ble Court on 20-03-2015. Any creditor,
contributory or other persons desirous of supporting or opposing the making of an order on the said petition should be
send to the petitioner or his advocate notice of his intention signed by him or his advocate with his name and address,
so as to reach the petitioner or his advocate not less than 5 days before the date fixed for the hearing of the petition
and appear at the hearing for the purpose in person or by his advocate. A copy of the petition will be furnished by the
under signed to any creditor of contributory on the payment of prescribed charges for the same. Any affidavit intended to
be used in opposition to the petition should be filed in the Court and a copy to be served on the petitioner or his advocate,
not less than 5 days before the date fixed for the hearing.
Dated at Chennai on this the 13th day of February 2015.
