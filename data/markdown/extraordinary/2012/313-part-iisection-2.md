COMMERCIAL TAXES AND REGISTRATION DEPARTMENT
NOMINATION OF MEMBERS TO THE COMMITTEE OF SOCIETY BY THE GOVERNMENT, EXEMPTING CERTAIN
CLASSES OF SOCIETIES UNDER THE TAMIL NADU SOCIETIES REGISTRATION ACT, 1975.
[G.O. Ms. No. 141, Commercial Taxes and Registration (M1) 6th November 2012, Aippasi 21, Thiruvalluvar Aandu 2043.]
No. II(2)/CTR/730(b)/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 54 of the Tamil Nadu Societies Registration Act, 1975 (Tamil Nadu
Act 27 of 1975), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby exempts the following classes of registered societies from the
provisions of sub-sections (3) and (4) of Section 15 of the said Act for the purpose of nomination of members to the
Committee of those Societies by the Government without holding an election, namely:—
(1) Societies formed by the Government by an order;
(2) Societies where funds of the Government are invested;
(3) Societies which receive financial aid or assistance from the Government; and
(4) Societies in which Officers of the Government are members in their ex-officio capacity.
The provisions of sub-sections (3) and (4) of Section 15 of the said Act shall apply, in respect of appointment of
members of the Committee other than those nominated by the Government.
2.
This Notification shall be deemed to have come into force with effect on and from the date of coming into force
of the said Act.
SUNIL PALIWAL,
Secretary to Government.
