LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT DEPARTMENT
Amendments to the Tamil Nadu Plantations Labour Rules.
[G.O. Ms. No. 77, Labour and Employment (K2), 15th May 2015.]
No. SRO A-15/2015.—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-sections (1) and (2) of Section 43 of the Plantations
Labour Act, 1951 (Central Act LXIX of 1951), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to
the Tamil Nadu Plantations Labour Rules, 1955, the draft of the same having been previously published  as required
by the said sub-section (1) of Section 43 of the said Act.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules,-
(1) in rule 5, in sub-rule (4), for the expression "the adolescents and children employed," the expression
"the adolescents employed" shall be substituted; and
(2) in rule 27, for the expression "the latter", the expression "State Government upon request by the Chief
Inspector" shall be substituted.
KUMAR JAYANT,
Secretary to Government (in-charge).
