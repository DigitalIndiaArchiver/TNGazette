No. SRO B-44/2014—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,  the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Forest
Subordinate Service (Section 12 of Part III B of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1970).
2.
(i) The amendments relating to the post of Forest Guard with Driving Licence hereby made shall be deemed
to have come into force on the 5th October 2005.
(ii) The amendments regarding the physical standards for female candidates hereby made shall be deemed
to have come into force on the 29th May 1990.
(iii) All the other amendments hereby made shall come into force with effect on and from the 9th July 2014.

“ 3-B Selection of Foresters, Forest Guards by promotion and recruitment by transfer.- (i) Selection of Foresters and
Forest Guards by promotion and recruitment by transfer shall be made by the Regional Committee, which consists of three or
four officers, constituted for the purpose by the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests for every year. The respective Region
falling within the geographical jurisdiction of each territorial circle comprising of various territorial divisions and wildlife units shall
be the unit for the above purpose. Separate lists shall be prepared for promotion from posts of Forest Guards, Forest Guard
with Driving Licence, Assistant Draughtsman Forest watchers Malis and recruitment by transfer from among the members of
the Tamil Nadu Ministerial Services, Tamil Nadu Basic Services in the Forest Department.
(ii) The Selection of Forest Guards, Forest Guard with Driving Licence, Assistant Draughtsman, Forest Watcher, Mali,
members of the Tamil Nadu Ministerial Service, members of the Tamil Nadu Basic Service in the Forest Department in each
region for promotion, or for recruitment by transfer as Foresters or Forest Guards as the case may be, finalised by the Regional
Committee concerned. With reference to the recommendation rolls are to be sent by the District Forest Officers, Divisional
Forest Officers or Other Unit Officers under whom the Forest Guards, Forest Guard with Driving Licence, Assistant Draughtsman,
Forest, Watchers, Malis, members of the Tamil Nadu Ministerial Service or, members of the Tamil Nadu Basic Service are
working.
(iii) The finalised recommendation rolls shall be sent to the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests by the Regional
Committee concerned. The Statewide lists so prepared shall be consolidated and be approved by the Principal Chief Conservator
of Forests and it shall be communicated to the individuals in the consolidated list through the officers under whom they are
serving.
3-C Selection of Forester, Forest Guards, Forest Guard with Driving Licence and Forest Watcher by direct
recruitment.—Selection of Forester, Forest Guards, Forest Guard with Driving Licence and Forest Watcher by direct recruitment
shall be made by the Tamil Nadu Forest Uniformed Services Recruitment Committee. The said Committee, every year and as
and when need arises, shall publish notification in leading daily newspapers inviting applications from the eligible candidates as
per qualification prescribed for that post by following the rule of reservation for the selection of the said posts by direct recruitment
to the estimate of vacancies approved by the Government. On receipt of the applications, the  same will be scrutinised and
finalised according to their eligibility by the said Recruitment Committee. The eligible candidates shall attend physical and
endurance tests as prescribed in these rules, followed by written and oral tests. The selection shall be made in the order of
physical and endurance tests, written and oral tests. The candidates shall have to qualify themselves at every stage before they
move to the next stage. After completion of the selection process, a list of selected candidates shall be approved by the Tamil
Nadu Forest Uniformed Services Recruitment Committee and the candidates in the approved list will be deputed to undergo
training. On successful completion of training, they will be allotted to the respective divisions:
Provided that the appointments of Forest Watchers are made from among the eligible members in the Statewide seniority
list of Plot Watchers / Village Social Forestry Workers. Selection of Forest Watchers by direct recruitment shall be made by the
Tamil Nadu Forest Uniformed Services Recruitment Committee as detailed above after the Statewide seniority list of Plot
Watchers / Village Social Forestry Workers is exhausted.

promotion from among the holders of the posts of Assistant Draughtsman, Forester, Forest Guards, Forest Guard with Driving
