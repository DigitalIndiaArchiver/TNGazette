The Election Petition praying that this Hon'ble Court be pleased to (a) declare
the election of the returned candidate Ms. J. Jayalalitha in R.K.Nagar Constituency,
Chennai in the Tamil Nadu General Assembly Elections as null and void and set
aside the election held on 16-05-2016 in R.K.Nagar Constituency, Chennai.
(b) Pass an order directing fresh poll in R.K.Nagar Assembly Constituency
(Tamil Nadu as per provision of Representation of People’s Act 1951 and conduct
of Election Rules 1961’s order consequential contesting and declaration of results.
(c) Directing the first respondent to pay the costs of this election petition.
The above election petition coming on this day before this court for hearing
in the presence of Mr. N.C. Ashok Kumar, advocate for M/s. S. Senthil,
K.C. Panneer Selvam, N. Barani Kumar, N. Saravanya, advocates for the
4th respondent herein and the petitioner-in-person and respondents 1 to 3 not
appearing in person or by advocate,
