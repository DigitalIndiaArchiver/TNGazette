APPOINTMENT OF COMMISSION OF INQUIRY TO INQUIRE INTO THE DEMISE OF THE 
LATE HON’BLE CHIEF MINISTER OF TAMIL NADU SELVI J JAYALALITHAA ON 5th DECEMBER 2016 
TO EXTENSION OF THE PERIOD OF THE COMMISSION OF INQUIRY BY SIX MONTHS FROM 
25th JUNE 2018 UNDER THE COMMISSION OF INQUIRY ACT.
AMENDMENT TO NOTIFICATION
[G.O. Ms. No. 451, Public (Law & Order-F), 22nd June 2018, Aani 8, Vilambi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2049.]
No. II(2)/PULO/549(c)/2018.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Commissions of Inquiry Act, 1952 
(Central Act LX of 1952), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Public 
(S.C.) Department Notiﬁ cation No.ll(2)/PUSC/793(g)/2017, published at page-1 of Part ll - Section 2 of the 
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 25th September 2017.
 AMENDMENT
 In the said Notiﬁ cation, in paragraph 3, for the expression “nine  months”, the expression “thirteen  months” 
shall be substituted.
P. SENTHILKUMAR, 
     
 
Principal Secretary to Government (FAC).
