No. SRO A-24/2022.—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) read with clause (xxii) of sub-section (2) 
of Section 116 of the Tamil Nadu Hindu Religious and Charitable Endowments Act, 1959 (Tamil Nadu Act 22 of 1959), the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Management and Preservation of Properties of 
Religious Institutions Rules:-
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖගඛ.
In the said Rules,−
(1) in rule 2, for sub-rule (i), the following sub-rule shall be substituted, namely:-
  
“(i) “appropriate authority” means−
 
   (a) the Government in respect of all religious institutions, if the estimated cost of the works 
exceeds Rs.1,00,00,000/-;
 
 
(b) the Commissioner in respect of all religious institutions, if the estimated cost of the works exceeds 
Rs.15,00,000/- but does not exceed Rs.1,00,00,000/-;
 
 
(c) The Joint Commissioner,- 
  
 
 
(i) in respect of all religious institutions included in the list published under Sections 46(i), 46(ii) and 46(iii) 
of the Act, if the estimated cost of the works does not exceed Rs.15,00,000/-;
  
 
 
(ii) in respect of all religious institutions not published under Section 46 of the Act, if the estimated cost 
of the works exceeds Rs.5,00,000/- but does not exceed Rs.15,00,000/-;
 
  
 
(iii) in respect of religious institutions having Executive Officer in the cadre of Deputy Commissioner, if 
the estimated cost of the works exceeds Rs.10,00,000/- but does not exceed Rs. 15,00,000/-;
  
 
 
(iv) in respect of religious institutions having Executive Officer in the cadre of Assistant Commissioner, if 
the estimated cost of the works exceeds Rs.5,00,000/- but does not exceed Rs. 15,00,000/-;
 
 
(d) the Executive Officer concerned in respect of religious institutions having an Executive Officer in the cadre 
of Joint Commissioner, if the estimated cost of the works does not exceed Rs.15,00,000/-;
 
 
(e) the Executive Officer concerned in respect of religious institutions having an Executive Officer in the cadre 
of Deputy Commissioner, if the estimated cost of the work does not exceed Rs.10,00,000/-;
 
 
(f) the Assistant Commissioner having jurisdiction in respect of religious institution not published under 
Section 46, if the estimated cost of the work does not exceed Rs.5,00,000/-;
 
 
(g) the Executive Officer concerned in respect of religious institutions having an Executive Officer in the cadre 
of Assistant Commissioner, if the estimated cost of the work does not exceed Rs.5,00,000/-;
 
 
(h) the Executive Officer concerned in respect of religious institutions having an Executive Officer in the cadre 
of Grade I to IV, if the estimated cost of the work does not exceed Rs.2,00,000/-;”
