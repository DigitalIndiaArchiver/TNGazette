APPOINTMENT OF VACATION CIVIL JUDGES FOR SUMMER VACATION OF CIVIL COURTS
FOR THE YEAR 2009
[G.O. (4D) No. 31, Home (Courts.III), 28th April 2009.]
No. II(2)/HO/178(c)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 30 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873
(Central Act III of 1873), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, in consultation with the High Court, Madras, hereby appoints the
Judicial Officers specified in column (2) of the Table below to be Vacation Civil Judges for the District Courts in the Districts
specified in the corresponding entries in column (3) thereof for the Summer Vacation of the Civil Courts for the year 2009.
