WHEREAS, in the Notification issued with the S.O. first and second read above, this Commission directed that all the
contesting candidates in the elections listed therein shall lodge a true copy of their accounts of election expenses kept by
them or by their respective election agents under sub-rule (1) of rule 116 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Third Grade
Municipalities, Municipalities and Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006, in the format prescribed therefore with the officers
mentioned therein, within thirty days from the date of declaration of the result of the elections;
2. WHEREAS this Commission conducted Casual elections to the urban local bodies during July 2010 as notified in the
S.O. third read above;
3. WHEREAS, the District Election Officers/District Collector,  Salem District, in his letter fourth read above reported that
out of three candidates contested in the said Casual election for the office of Municipal Ward Member in Ward No. 28 of Attur
Municipality one candidate Tmt. M. Kalaiselvi, W/o. Mohan failed to lodge copies of her account of election expenses as
directed by this Commission;
4. WHEREAS, based on the report of the said District Election Officer/District Collector, Salem, a show cause notice was
issued in the reference fifth read above to the contested candidate, who failed to lodge a true copy of the account of election
expenses in the Casual election held during the month of July 2010, through the District Election Officer/District Collector,
calling for explanation within 21 days of the receipt of the said notice, as to why action should not be taken against her as per
sub-section (2-A) of Section 49 of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920);
5. WHEREAS, the said District Election Officers/District Collector,  Salem, in his letters sixth read above, has now reported
that the said contested candidate has neither submitted her explanations nor lodged the account of election expenses inspite
of the show cause notices issued to her; and

6.  WHEREAS, in view of the said report of the District Election Officer/District Collector, Salem, the Tamil Nadu State
Election Commission is satisfied that the candidate Tmt. M. Kalaiselvi, W/o. V. Mohan, 287F, Dr. Muthulakshmi Street, Attur,
Salem District contested in the casual election to Ward No. 28 of Attur Municipality held during July 2010 has failed to lodge
her account of election expenses as required by or under the said Act and Rules and has no good reason or justification for
such failure.
7.  NOW, THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (2-A) of  Section 49 of the Tamil
Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920), the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission hereby
declares that the said candidate Tmt. M. Kalaiselvi, W/o. V. Mohan, Attur shall be disqualified, for being elected as a
Councillor/Chairman for a period of three years from the date of this Order.
