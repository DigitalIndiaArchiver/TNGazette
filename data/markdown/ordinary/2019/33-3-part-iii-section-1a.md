Amendments to the Tamil Nadu Liquor Retail Vending (in Shops and Bars) Rules.

No. SRO A-26/2019.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 54 of the Tamil Nadu Prohibition Act, 1937 
(Tamil Nadu Act X of 1937), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu Liquor 
Retail Vending (in Shops and Bars) Rules, 2003.
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖගඛ.
In the said Rules,—
(1) in rule 2,—
(a) in clause (i), for the expression " the shortest pathway", the expression "the shortest public pathway" shall 
be substituted;
(b) after clause (p), the following clause shall be inserted, namely:-
"(pp) "Population" means the figure of population as officially published in the latest census.";
(2) in rule 8,—
(a) in sub-rule (1), for the expression "in other areas 100 (hundred) metres" the expression "in other areas 
within 100 (hundred) metres" shall be substituted;
(b) in sub-rule (1), after the provisos thereto, the following Explanations shall be added, namely:-
"Explanation (1).- (a) wherever the distance of the shop from the place of worship or educational institution 
needs to be determined, distance shall be measured from the entry door of the shop to the-
(i) nearest gate of the place of worship or educational institution, when such place of worship or educational 
institution has a compound wall; or
(ii) nearest entrance of the place of worship or educational institution, when such place of worship or 
educational institution has no compound wall.
(b) When the retail vending shop is located alongwith other shops within a building, the distance would be, 
the sum total of the length of the shortest public pathway, lane, Street or Road, generally used by the 
memberes of the public till the entry gate of the building in which the shop is located and thereafter, the 
shortest pathway used by the people to reach the entry door of the shop.
Explanation (2).- (a) Wherever the distance of the shop from the outer edge of National or State Highway or 
service lane along the Highway needs to be determined, the same shall be measued from the entry door 
of the shop to the outer edge of the National or State Highways or of a service lane along such highway.
(b) when the retail vending shop is located alongwith other shops within a building, the distance would be, the 
sum total of the length of the shortest public pathway, lane, Street or Road, generally used by the members 
of the public from the outer edge of National or State Highways or service lane along the Highways till 
the nearest entry gate of the building in which the shop is located and thereafter, the shortest pathway 
used by the people to reach the entry door of the shop."
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
NIRANJAN MARDI,
Additional Chief Secretary to Government.

Amendment to the Tamil Nadu Farmers' Management of Irrigation System Election Rules.
[G.O. Ms. No. 81, Public Works (W2), 8th July 2019, ÝQ 23, Mè£K, F¼õœÀõ˜ Ý‡´-2050.]
No. SRO A-27/2019.—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 50 of the Tamil Nadu Farmers 
Management of Irrigation Systems Act, 2000 (Tamil Nadu Act 7 of 2001), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the 
following amendment to the Tamil Nadu Farmer's Management of Irrigation Systems Election Rules, 2003:-
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖග.
In the said Rules, after rule 6, the following rule shall be inserted, namely:-
"6-A. Appointment of competent authority to decide election disputes:- The Government may, by notification, appoint 
such officer to be the competent authority to decide all election disputes for the purposes of these Rules".
