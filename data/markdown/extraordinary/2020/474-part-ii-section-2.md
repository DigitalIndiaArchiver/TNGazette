In G.O.Ms.No.324, Revenue and Disaster Management (DM II), Department, Dated: 30-06-2020 it has been ordered 
under caption-I that operation of both privated and Government buses within the districts will remain suspended from 
01-07-2020 to 15-07-2020 throughout the state and that the interstate public transport will continue to remain prohibited 
until  further orders during the lockdown period throughout the state till 31-07-2020.
2. In this connection, as per the guidelines issued by the Government of India, necessary permission was already 
accorded by the Government of Tamil Nadu for the goods traﬃ  c, operation of railways for transportation of goods and 
parcels, operation of airports/ seaports/ landports and inland container depots for cargo movement, movement of all 
trucks and other goods / carrier vehicles without any restriction vide G.O.Ms.No.193, Revenue and Disaster Management 
Department, dated 15.4.2020. Further, as already stated above operation of both Public and Private Bus Transport 
throughout the State has been permitted by the Government from 07.09.2020 after following the Standard Operating 
Procedures and that the Inter State/Intra State Rail Transport in the authorised routes has also been permitted. 
3. In G.O.Ms.No.609, Revenue & Disaster Management (DM-IV) Department, dated 31-10-2020 operation of both 
Private and Public Bus Transport between Tamil Nadu and Puducherry without E-registration has been permitted. 
4. In G.O.Ms.No. 632, Revenue & Disaster Management (DM-IV) Department, dated 11-11-2020, considering the 
request of the Transport Department, the Inter-State bus services between Karnataka and Tamil Nadu by the State 
Transport Undertakings were permitted for six days from 11-11-2020 to 16-11-2020 without E-registration by duly following 
the prescribed guidelines/Standard Operating Procedures. 
5. The Additional Chief Secretary / Commissioner of Revenu Administration has informed that in the Government 
Press Release date 13-11-2020, it has been stated that in view of the Diwali festival permission was granted to operate 
interstate public bus transport fron 11-11-2020 to 16-11-2020 between Tamil Nadu and Karnataka State. It has been 
further stated that considering the request of the Hon'ble Chief Minister of Karnataka and the general public, operation

of Revenue Administration and accordingly amend the G.O.Ms.No.613, Revenue and Disaster Management (DM IV), 
Department, Dated : 31-10-2020 as follows:
The content of Caption II-A (Under Permitted Activities) shall be replaced as:
II-A. Operation of both Private and Public Bus Transport between Tamil Nadu and Karnataka State shall 
continue to be permitted beyond 16-11-2020 without E-registration.
