NOTIFICATIONS  BY  THE ELECTION   COMMISSION  OF  INDIA
NOTIFICATION OF ELECTION COMMISSION OF INDIA REGARDING AMENDMENT TO THE LIST OF
RETURNING OFFICERS FOR THE PARLIAMENTARY CONSTITUENCIES IN THE STATE OF TAMIL NADU.
No. SRO G-8/2009.
The following Notification of the Election Commission of India, Nirvachan Sadan, Ashoka Road, New Delhi-110 001,
dated the 2nd March, 2009 [13 Magha, 1930 (Saka)] is  republished:—
No. 434/TN/2009 (1):—In pursuance of the provisions contained in Section 21 of the Representation of the People
Act, 1951 (43 of 1951), the Election Commission in consultation with the State Government of Tamil Nadu hereby makes
following amendments in its Notification No. 434/TN/2008 (1), dated 18th June, 2008 namely:—
1. In the column (2) of the table appended to the said notification, the existing entry “Collector of Kancheepuram,
Kancheepuram” shown against “5-Sriperumbudur” in the column (1) of the said table shall be substituted by “Special District
Revenue Officer (Land Acquisition), SIPCOT, Sriperumbudur”;
2. In the column (2) of the table appended to the said notification, the existing entry “Additional Collector/District
Revenue Officer, Kancheepuram” shown against “6-Kancheepuram (SC)” in the column (1) of the said table shall be
substituted by “Collector of Kancheepuram, Kancheepuram”;
3. In the column (2) of the table appended to the said notification, the existing entry “Additional Collector/District
Revenue Officer, Tiruvannamalai” shown against “12-Arani” in the column (1) of the said table shall be substituted by
“Special District Revenue Officer, SIPCOT (Land Acquisition) Extn., Cheyyar”;
4. In the column (2) of the table appended to the said notification, the existing entry “Additional Collector/District
Revenue Officer, Villupuram” shown against “14-Kallakurichi” in the column (1) of the said table shall be substituted by
“Special Officer, Kallakurichi Co-operative Sugar Mills, Kallakurichi”;
