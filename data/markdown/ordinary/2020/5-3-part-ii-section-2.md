on which the Subordinate Judge, Pappireddipatti assumes 
charge of that Court.

Notiﬁ cation under the Payment of Bonus Act, 1965 (Central 
Act 21 of 1965) Application of the Payment of Bonus 
Act to the establishments under the Building and other 
Construction workers (Regulation of Employment and 
Conditions of Service) Act, (Central Act 27 of 1996).
 [G.O.Ms.No. 09, Labour and Employment (H1), 
7th January 2020, ñ£˜èN 22, Mè£K,

No.II(2)/LE/77/2020.—WHEREAS, the Government of 
Tamil Nadu are of the opinion that the Payment of Bonus 
Act, 1965 (Central Act 21 of 1965) shall be made applicable 
to the establishments within the meaning of clause (j) of 
sub-section (1) of Section 2 of the Building and Other 
Construction Workers (Regulation of Employment and 
Conditions of Service) Act, 1996 (Central Act 27 of 1996) 
in which less than twenty workers and not less than ten 
workers are employed or were employed on any day during 
the accounting year 2019;
NOW, THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred 
by the proviso to sub-section (3) of Section 1 of the Payment 
of Bonus Act, 1965 (Central Act 21 of 1965), notice of 
their intention to apply the provisions of the said Act to 
the establishments aforesaid from the accounting year 
commencing on any day in the year 2019 and in respect 
of every subsequent accounting years, is hereby given as 
required therein.

from any person with regard to the proposed application 
of the provisions of the said Act to the establishments
