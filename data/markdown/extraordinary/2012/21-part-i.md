CERTAIN MINISTERS RELIEVED FROM THEIR MINISTERSHIP OF COUNCIL OF MINISTERS.
[G.O. Ms. No. 99, Public (Special-B), 26th January 2012, Thai 12, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2043.]
No. I/PUSP/6(a-1)/2012.
I, K. Rosaiah, Governor of Tamil Nadu accept the recommendation of the Chief Minister that the following Ministers
may be relieved from the Council of Ministers and direct that they be relieved of their duties and responsibilities as Ministers
with immediate effect:—
1. Thiru Agri S.S. Krishnamoorthy, Minister for School Education and Sports & Youth Welfare.
2. Thiru S.P. Velumani, Minister for Revenue.
K. ROSAIAH,
Governor of Tamil Nadu.
ALLOCATION OF SUBJECTS AMONG MINISTERS.
[G.O. Ms. No. 100, Public (Special-B), 26th January 2012, Thai 12, Thiruvalluvar Aandu--2043.]
No. I/PUSP/6(a-2)/2012.
Under Clause (3) of Article 166 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that the portfolios
"Revenue, District Revenue Establishment, Deputy Collectors, Weights and Measures, Debt Relief including legislation on
Money lending, Chits, Registration of Companies" be allotted to Thiru K.A. Sengottaiyan and he be designated as Minister
for Revenue.
DEBENDRANATH  SARANGI,
Chief Secretary.
