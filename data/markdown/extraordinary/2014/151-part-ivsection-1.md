A Bill to amend the Tamil Nadu Fishermen and Labourers engaged in
Fishing and other Allied Activities (Social Security and Welfare) Act, 2007.
BE it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the
Sixty-fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
1.
(1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu Fishermen and Labourers
engaged in Fishing and other Allied Activities (Social Security and Welfare)
Amendment Act, 2014.
(2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government
may, by notification, appoint.
2. In section 9 of the Tamil Nadu Fishermen and Labourers engaged in
Fishing and other Allied Activities (Social Security and Welfare) Act, 2007,
in sub-section (4), in clause (vi), for the expression “Public Works and Forest
Departments”, the expression “Public Works, Fisheries and Forest Departments”
shall be substituted.

STATEMENT OF OBJECTS AND REASONS.
As per clause (vi) of sub-section (4) of section 9 of the Tamil Nadu
Fishermen and Labourers engaged in Fishing and other Allied Activities (Social
Security and Welfare) Act, 2007,  (Tamil Nadu Act 19 of 2007), a contribution
at the rate of five per cent of the lease amount for fishing collected by the
respective authorities in Revenue, Hindu Religious and Charitable Endowments,
Public Works and Forest Department  and Local authorities is collected
towards the Tamil Nadu Fishermen and Labourers engaged in Fishing and
other Allied Activities (Social Security and Welfare) Fund established under
the said Act.  Fisheries Department is also leasing out the water bodies under
its control in various districts for fishing. The Tamil Nadu Fishermen Welfare
Board has by its resolution dated 17.02.2009 recommended to the Government
to amend the said Tamil Nadu Act 19 of 2007 to include Fisheries Department
in clause (vi) of sub-section (4) of section 9 of the said Act so as to collect
contribution to the said fund, from the lease amount collected by authorities
in Fisheries Department.  Accordingly, the Government have decided to amend
the said Tamil Nadu Act 19 of 2007, suitably for the purpose.
2. The Bill seeks to give effect to the above decision.
