The Governor of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that 
the lands speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below have to be acquired 
for Highways purpose for Forming Road Over Bridge in 
Salem District, Mettur Taluk, P.N.Patti (P.Nallagoundampatti), 
Veerakkalpudhur and Virthasampatti Villages at
Km 17/10 of Tharamangalam-Nangavalli- Kunchandiyur 
State Highways road in lieu of existing Level Crossing 
No.21 railway Km 31/9-10 between Mechery and Mettur 
Railway Stations and it has already been decided that 
the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the 
lands is to be paid out of the funds controlled or managed 
by the Divisional Engineer (Highways) Projects, Salem 
District after considering the cause shown by the owner 
(or) other person having interest on such lands as the 
case may be do hereby publish the following notice under
sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways 
Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of section 15 of the 
Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 
2002) the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the 
land speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below measuring to an 
extent of Acquisition of 13514.0 Sq.mtrs of R.Wet and 
R.Dry lands for forming Road Over Bridge in Salem 
District, Mettur Taluk, P.N.Patti (P.Nallagoundampatti), 
Veerakkalpudhur and Virthasampatti Villages at Km 17/10 of 
Tharamangalam-Nangavalli-Kunchandiyur State Highways
road in lieu of existing Level Crossing No.21 railway
Km 31/9-10 between Mechery and Mettur Railway Stations.
The plan of the lands is kept in the oﬃ  ce of the 
District Revenue Oﬃ  cer, Salem and may be inspected at 
any time during oﬃ  ce hours.
