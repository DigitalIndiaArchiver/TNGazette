AMENDMENT TO NOTIFICATION
No. II(2)/PULO/412(a)/2016.
In exercise of  the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Commissions of Inquiry Act,
1952 (Central Act LX of 1952), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the
Public (Law and Order-F) Department Notification No.ll(2)/PULO/455(h)/2013, published at Pages 1-2 of
Part ll—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 8th July 2013.
AMENDMENT
In the said Notification, in paragraph 4, for the expression “thirty six months”, the expression
“forty eight months” shall be substituted.
SHIV DAS MEENA,
Principal Secretary to Government (FAC).
