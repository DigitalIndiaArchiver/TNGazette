TRANSPORT DEPARTMENT
ACQUISITION OF LANDS
[G.O. Ms. No. 79, Transport (I.1), 28th June 2011.]
No. II(2)/TRA/286(e-1)/2011.
Whereas, in exercise of the powers conferred by clause (1)
of the Article 258 of the Constitution of India, the President of
India has, in the Ministry of Food, Agriculture, Community
Development and Co-operation (Department of Agriculture)
Notification No.4 (1)/65, General II, dated the 23rd April 1966
entrusted to the Government of Tamil Nadu with their consent,
the functions of the Central Government under the Land
Acquisition Act, 1894 (Central Act I of 1894) in relation to
Acquisition of Land for the purpose for the Union in this State;
And, whereas, it has already been decided that the
compensation shall be paid from the public revenues;
Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred
by Section 6 of the said Act, the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby declares that the Land specified in the
Schedule below and measuring 0.31.0 hectare of Keeranur
Village, Rasipuram Taluk, Namakkal District, be the same a
little more or less are needed for public purpose, to wit, dumping
the excavated cut soil due to the formation of Salem-Karur
Broad Gauge Railway Line Project.  A plan of the Lands is kept
in the Office of Special Tahsildar  (Land Acquisition), Unit-I,
Salem-Karur Broad Gauge Railway Line Project, Namakkal and
may be inspected at any time during the office hours.
