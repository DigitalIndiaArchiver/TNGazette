Amendment to Tamil Nadu State Human Rights Commission (Salaries and Allowances and other terms and
conditions of service of Members) Rules, 1996.
[G.O. Ms. No. 912, Public (Human Rights), 10th September 2009, ÝõE 25, F¼õœÀõ˜ Ý‡´-2040.]
No. SRO A-31/2009.—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) and clause (a) of sub-section (2) of
Section 41 of the Protection of Human Rights Act, 1993 (Central Act 10 of 1994), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes
the following amendment to the Tamil Nadu State Human Rights Commission (Salaries and Allowances and other terms and
conditions of service of Members) Rules, 1996.
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 28th November, 2006.
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in  Rule 5, after sub-rule (b), the following sub-rule shall be added, namely:—
“(c). The Chairperson of the State Commission shall receive a sum of Rs. 7,500 per month and the Members of the
State Commission shall receive a sum of Rs. 6,000 per month as Sumptuary Allowance.”
D. JOTHI JAGARAJAN,
Secretary to Government.
