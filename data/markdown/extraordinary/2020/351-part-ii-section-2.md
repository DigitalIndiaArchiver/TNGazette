The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that 
the lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below have to be acquired 
for industrial purpose to wit for the formation of new Broad 
Gauge Railway line between Tindivanam and Thiruvannamalai 
and it having already been decided that the entire amount 
of compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid 
out of the lands controlled or managed by District Collector, 
Viluppuram the following notice is issued under sub-section 
(1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for 
Industrial Purposes Act, 1997.
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the 
Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act.1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu 
hereby acquires the land / lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule 
below and measuring 5.23.0 Hectare of Dry Land and 3.82.5 
Hectare of Wet Land to the same, a little more or less needed 
for Industrial Purpose, to wit for the formation of new Broad 
Gauge Railway line between Tindivanam and Thiruvannamalai. 
The plan of the lands are kept in the Collector’s Oﬃ  ce, 
Viluppuram and in the oﬃ  ce of the Sub Collector, Tindivanam, 
Viluppuram district and may be inspected at any time during 
oﬃ  ce hours.
