No. SRO B-27/2022.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, 
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following rules:-
2. The rules hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 11th March 2020.
Rඝඔඍඛ
The provisions contained in the Tamil Nadu Government Servants (Conditions of Service) Act, 2016 
(Tamil Nadu Act 14 of 2016) and the Special Rules applicable to the holders of the permanent post of Deputy Director of 
Survey and Land Records in Category 2 of Class 1 of the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Survey and Land Records 
Service shall apply to the  holders of the temporary post of Deputy Director (Technical), subject to the modiﬁ cations speciﬁ ed 
in the following rules, namely:-
2. Constitution:- The post shall form a temporary addition to the said class of the said service.
3. Appointment:- Appointment to the post shall be made by promotion from among the holders of the post of Assistant 
Director (Technical) on the grounds of merit and ability, seniority being considered only where the merit and ability are 
approximately equal.
4. Pay:- There shall be paid to the holder of the post, a monthly pay calculated in the level of pay in the pay matrix 
level-25 of Rs. 59,300 - 1,87,700.

No. SRO B-28/2022.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following rules:-
2. The rules hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 10th June 2020.
Rඝඔඍඛ
The provisions contained in the Tamil Nadu Government Servants (Conditions of Service) Act, 2016 
(Tamil Nadu Act 14 of 2016) and the Special Rules applicable to the holders of the permanent post of Joint Director of 
Survey and Land Records in Category 1 of Class 1 of the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Survey and Land Records 
Service shall apply to the  holders of the temporary post of Joint Director (Technical), subject to the modiﬁ cations speciﬁ ed 
in the following rules, namely:-
2. Constitution:- The post shall form a temporary addition to the said class of the said service.
3. Appointment:- Appointment to the post shall be made by promotion from among the holders of the post of 
Deputy Director (Technical) on the grounds of merit and ability, seniority being considered only where the merit and ability 
are approximately equal.
4. Pay:- There shall be paid to the holder of the post, a monthly pay calculated in the level of pay in the pay matrix 
level-26 of Rs. 61,900 - 1,96,700.
