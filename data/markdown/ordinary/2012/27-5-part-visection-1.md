In exercise of the powers conferred by the sub-section (4) of Section 32 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country
Planning Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972) the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following Variations to
the Master Plan of the Dindigul Local Planning Area approved under the said Act and published with the Housing
and Urban Development Department, Notification No. II/(2)HOU/611/2001, at page No. 308  of Part  II—Section 2 of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 15th August 2001.

VARIATIONS
In the said Master Plan, under the heading "Munnilaikottai Village" under the sub-heading "Agricultural use" in the first
column, against the entries 531 to 541 in the second column respectively.
(1) For the expression 531 to 541 in the second column the entry "Agricultural use" in the first column the
expression 531, 532/1A, 532/1B, 532/2B, 532/3A, 533/1, 2A, 3A, 5, 534/1A, 1B, 1C, 1D, 1E, 1F, 2 shall be substituted.
(2) In the first and second column after the entry "Agricultural use" and the relating thereto the entry "Educational
Use" and the expression "532/2A, 532/3B, 532/4, 532/5, 533/2B, 533/3B, 533/4, 533/6, 533/7, 533/8, 534/3" shall
respectively be inserted.
Dindigul,
C. MATHIVANAN,
2nd July 2012.
Member-Secretary,

Whereas Thiru A. Rajeshkannan, B.A., M.L.M., B.L.,  P.G.D.T.A., M.Phil., Advocate, Bar Association, Kovilpatti has
