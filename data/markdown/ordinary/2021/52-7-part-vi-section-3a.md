GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAQF BOARD, CHENNAI.
Taking over of the Direct Management of Nawab Chanda Mian Masjid @ MIR Momin Tomb Waqf, 
Tiruvannamalai District
(Lr. No. 13535/09/B7/VLR)
No.VI-3(a)/62/2021.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under section 65 of the Waqf Act 1995, the Tamil Nadu Waqf 
Board in its Resolution Item No.137/21 dated:27-10-2021 has ordered to take over the Management of NAWAB CHANDA 
MIAN MASJID @ MIR MOMIN TOMB WAQF under the Direct Management of Tamil Nadu Waqf Board for a period of 
SIX MONTHS from 27-10-2021 and appointed the Superintendent of Waqfs, Vellore as its Executive Oﬃ  cer.
Chennai, 
Dr. D. FARITHA BANU,
21st December 2021. 
Chief Executive Oﬃ  cer/Estate Oﬃ  cer.
Framing of Scheme in Respect of Muslimpur Thotipattarai Masjid and Faruqiya Madrasa Waqf, 
Vaniyambadi, Vellore District.
(Lr. Rc. No. 2225/13/B7/VLR)
No.VI-3(a)/63/2021.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under section 69 of the Waqf Act 1995, the Tamil Nadu Wakf 
Board in its Resolution in item No.136/21 in Rc. No.2225/13/B7/VLR dated: 27-10-2021 has approved the Scheme framed 
for the Waqf in respect of MUSLIMPUR THOTIPATTARAI MASJID AND FARUQIYA MADRASA WAQF, VANIYAMBADI, 
VELLORE DISTRICT for better administration of the Waqf.
Chennai, 
Dr. D. FARITHA BANU,
21st December 2021. 
Chief Executive Oﬃ  cer/Estate Oﬃ  cer.
