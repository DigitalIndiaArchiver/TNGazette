The Governor of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that
the lands specified in the Schedule below, are required for the
purpose of the construction of Road Over Bridge in lieu of
existing Railway Level Crossing No.29 in between Tambaram
and Sanatorium Railway Stations and it having already been
decided that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded
for the lands is to be paid out of the funds controlled and
managed by the Chief Engineer (Projects), Highways
Department, Chennai-15 and after having considered the cause
shown by the owners or other persons having interest in the
said lands, as the case may be, do hereby publish the following
notice under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu
Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu
Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002), the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands specified in the Schedule
below and measuring an extent of 928.5 Sq.metres to  the
same, a little more or less required for the purpose of
construction of Road Over Bridge in lieu of existing Railway
Level Crossing No.29 in between Tambaram and Sanatorium
Railway Stations.
The Plan of the lands are kept in the office of the Special
Tahsildar, Land Acquisition, Inner Ring Road Scheme, Alandur
Municipality Campus, Alandur, Chennai-16 and may be inspected
at any time during office hours.
