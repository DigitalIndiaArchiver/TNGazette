GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI
Taking over of Hazrath Khader Masthan Oliyullah Dargah and Sunnath Jamath Pallivasal, Chinnakarai,
Palladam Taluk, Tirupur District under Direct Management.
(Rc. No. 2087/11/B3/ TPR.)
No. VI-3(a)/110/2014.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995 as amended by Act
27 of 2013,  the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in Item No. 10/13, in Rc.No. 2087/11/B3/TPR, dated
25th June 2014 has ordered to take over Hazrath Khader Masthan Oliyullah Dargah and Sunnath Jamath Pallivasal,
Chinnakarai, Palladam Taluk, Tirupur District under the direct Management of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of
one year from the Board's Order dated 25th June 2014 and appoint the Wakf Inspector, Tirupur (Incharge) as its Executive
Officer.
A. MOHAMED MEERAN,
Chennai-600 001,
District Revenue Officer /
28th October 2014.
Chief Executive Officer (Incharge),
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
Extension of Direct Management of Melachinthamani Pallivasal Wakf, Tiruchy District.
(Rc. No. 2949/05/B5/TRY.)
No. VI-3(a)/111/2014.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995 as amended by
Act 27 of 2013,  the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its Resolution in Item No. 128/14, Rc.No. 2949/05/B5/TRY, dated
26th August 2014 has resolved to ratify the period of direct management from 5th April 2014 to 25th August 2014 and
also to extend the period of direct management of Melachinthamani Pallivasal Wakf, Trichy which is under the direct control
of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a further period of one year from the date of Board's Order 26-8-2014.
A. MOHAMED MEERAN,
Chennai-600 001,
District Revenue Officer /
28th October 2014.
Chief Executive Officer (Incharge),
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.

Dargah  Wakf, Krishnagiri District.
(Rc. No. 5271/11/B2/K.Giri.)
No. VI-3(a)/112/2014.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995 as amended by Act
27/2013, the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in item No. 46/14, in Rc.No. 5271/11/B2/K.Giri, dated 26th August
2014 has resolved to ratify the period of direct management of Hazrath Syed Sha Shahabuddin and Hazrath Syed Sha
Bahahuddin Dargah  Wakf, Krishnagiri District from 3rd January 2014 to 25th August 2014 and also extended the period
of direct management of the above Wakf for the period of one year from the Board's order dated 26-8-2014.

District Revenue Officer /
