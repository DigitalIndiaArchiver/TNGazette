Reduction of stamp duty and registration fees in respect of instruments evidencing

under the indian stamp act

No. II(2)/CTR/205(j-1)/2019.
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (a) of sub-section (1) of Section 9 of the Indian Stamp Act, 1899 
(Central Act II of 1899), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby reduces the duty chargeable under the said Act in respect of 
instruments of transfer of property relating to amalgamation or reconstruction of companies to two percent of the market value 
of the immovable property or 0.6 percent of the aggregate of the market value of the shares, whichever is higher.
notification-II
Reduces the fee payable in respect of instruments of transfer of immovable property 
relating to amalgamation or reconstruction of companies under the registration act
[G.O. Ms. No. 29, Commercial Taxes and Registration (J1), 1st March 2019,  
ñ£C 17, M÷‹H, F¼õœÀõ˜ Ý‡´-2050.]
No. II(2)/CTR/205(j-2)/2019.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 78-A of the Registration Act, 1908 (Central Act XVI of 1908), the Governor 
of Tamil Nadu is of the opinion that it is necessary so to do in the public interest hereby reduces the fee payable under the 
said Act to Rs.30,000/- (Rupees thirty thousand only) in respect of instruments of transfer of immovable property relating to 
amalgamation or reconstruction of companies.
