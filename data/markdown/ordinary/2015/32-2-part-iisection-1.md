The Tamil Nadu Oilpalm (Regulation of Production and Processing) Act, 1994 come into force with effect from
12th December 1994. As per sec 3(1) of the above said Act, a Statutory Committee called “The Tamil Nadu Oilpalm Project
Management Committee” has to be constituted by the Government consisting of members as stipulated under section 3 (2)
of the said Act. Accordingly, in the Government Order first read above Government had constituted the “Tamil Nadu Oilpalm
Project Management Committee” . The Committee was again reconstituted in the Government Orders second and third read
above. The term of the above committee is three years.
2. In his letter fourth read above the Director of Agriculture has stated that the term of the Tamil Nadu Oilpalm Project
Mnagement Committee expired on 03-01-2014 and as such the committee may be reconstituted for three more years. He
has accordingly, sent a necessary proposal to Government to reconstitute the committee from the date of issue of this order.
3. Government after careful consideration reconstitute the “Tamil Nadu Oilpalm Project Management Committee”
including members from other entrepreneurs under section 3(1) of the Tamil Nadu Oilpalm (Regulation of Production and
Processing) Act, 1994 for a further period of three years. The composition of the committee is as detailed below:-

4. The Headquarters of the committee shall be at Chennai and it shall meet as often as deemed fit, but not less than
twice a year.
5. The Committee shall be treated as First Class Committee for the purpose of Travelling Allowance I Daily Allowance
for non-official members. The expenditure towards Travelling Allowance I Daily Allowance for non-official
members shall be debited to “2401-00-Crop Husbandary-114- Development of Oilseeds - Schemes in the Tenth Five Year
Plan-VI Schemes shared between State and Centre - UE Oilpalm Development Project - 60 Travelling Allowance / Daily
Allowance to non-official members (DPC 2401 00 114 UE 6007).
6. The expenditure towards Travelling Allowance I Daily Allowance in respect of Chairman and Official members shall
be debited to Heads of Account in which their pay and allowances are drawn.
7. This order issues with the concurrence of Finance Department vide its U.O.No. 568, Finance(Agri)/2013,
