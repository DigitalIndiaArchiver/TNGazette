Notifications by the Election Commission of India
EXTENSION OF PERIOD FOR FILING OF CLAIMS AND OBJECTIONS IN THE
CONSTITUENCIES OF THE TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY
No. SRO G-35/2017.
The following Notification of the Election Commission of India, New Delhi,
dated 30th November, 2017  [9 Agrahayana, 1939 (Saka)] is published:–
No. 23/Spl. Drive/NOT/ECI/FUNC/ERD-ER/2017 (Vol.III).—Whereas, the Election Commission
had announced the schedule for special Summary Revision of electoral rolls in the State of
Tamil Nadu with reference to 01-01-2018 as the qualifying date vide its letter No.23/LET/ECI/
FUNC/ERD-ER/2017 (VoII), dated 23rd August, 2017; and
Whereas, the period for filing claims and objections was fixed from 3rd October, 2017 to
30th November, 2017; and
Whereas, the draft rolls were published on 3rd October 2017 as scheduled; and
Whereas, the Election Commission has reviewed the progress of ongoing Special Drive being
conducted during the period for filing claims and objections in the State; and
Whereas, the Election Commission has, on such review and also on request received from
CEO., decided that the period for filing claims and objections in the State shall be further extended
up to 15th December, 2017, so that period of Special Drive during the said period for filing claims
and objections, also be extended to enable the Booth Level Officers to complete ongoing house
to house verification in their respective polling station areas;

Now, therefore, the Election Commission, in exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Rule 12 of the

including 15th December, 2017 for the current Special Summary Revision of electoral rolls with reference to 01-01-2018,
as the qualifying date.
