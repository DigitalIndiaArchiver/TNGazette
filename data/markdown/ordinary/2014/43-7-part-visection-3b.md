Election to the Syndicate and Standing Committee on Academic Affairs.
(Ref. No. BARD/A1/OR.SEN./SYN.ELC./43398/2014.)
No. VI-3(b)/32/2014.
I. ELECTION TO THE SYNDICATE:
Class II - Other Members - Sub-Section (b) (1) of Section 24 under Chapter IV of the Bharathidasan University Act, 1981 -
* Two Members elected by the Principals of the affiliated colleges from among themselves, in accordance with the system of
proportional representation by means of a single transferable vote.
Class II - Other Members - Sub-Section (b) (2) of Section 24 under Chapter IV of the Bharathidasan University Act, 1981 -
* Two Members elected by the teachers of the affiliated colleges other than Principals from among themselves who are members
of the senate in accordance with the system of proportional representation by means of a single transferable vote.
II. ELECTION TO THE STANDING COMMITTEE ON ACADEMIC AFFAIRS:
Class II - Other Members - Sub-Section (2) of Section 23 under Chapter III of Bharathidasan University Act, 1981 -
Two Members elected by the Principals of the affiliated colleges from among themselves, in accordance with the system
of proportional representation by means of a single transferable vote.
