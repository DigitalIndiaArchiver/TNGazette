Cancellation Notification
[G.O.  Ms.  No. 80, Higher Education (G2), 30th March 2009, Panguni, 17, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. SRO B-24/2009.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby direct that the orders issued in G.O.(Ms.) No. 174, Higher Education (G2) Department,
dated 2nd June 2005, be cancelled.
K. GANESAN,
