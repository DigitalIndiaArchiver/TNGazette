Amendments to Fees and Fines Regulations 2004
(Notification No.TNERC / Regulations /F&F/4/2-8,  dated 13-5-2014)
No.VI(2)/134/2014.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Clause (g) of sub-section (1) of Section 86 read with Section 181 of  the Electricity
Act, 2003 (Central Act 36 of 2003) and all other powers enabling it in this behalf, the Tamil Nadu Electricity Regulatory
Commission hereby makes the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu Electricity Regulatory Commission – Fees and Fines
Regulations, 2004, the draft of the same having been previously published as required by sub-section (3) of Section 181 of
the said Act.

EXPLANATORY STATEMENT
The Tamil Nadu Electricity Regulatory Commission has decided to revise the quantum of fees levied for certain categories
of petitions filed before the Commission, considering fees structure prevailing in other States.  The proposed amendments seek
to give effect to the above decision.
