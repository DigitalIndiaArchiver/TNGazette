Variation to the Sanctioned Thottapattu Detailed Development Plan, No. 1 of Cuddalore Local Planning
Authority, Villupuram Region.
(Roc. No. 22395/2012/DP2.)
No.VI(1)/116/2013.
In exercise of the powers conferred under Sub-section (1) of Section 33 of Town and Country Planning Act 1971 (Act
No.35 of 1972). The Commissioner of Town and Country Planning Chennai in the Proceeding Roc. No.22395/12 DPI
dated:17-1-13. Proposes to make the individual draft variation for Deletion of proposed “B5B5” (40’0") 12.0M scheme road
part deleted in Thottapattu village S.Nos. 76/1, 76/2, 77/3 & 169/17B-with an extent 26,650 sq.m. to the sanctioned-
Thottapattu Detailed Development Plan No.1 of Cuddlore Local Planning Authority in Director of Town
and Country Planning Proceeding Roc. No. 22892/09 DPI dated: 3-7-10. The said notification was published in
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No. 34, part VI, section-1, Page No. 274, 275, dated: 1-9-2010
(Publication No.VI(1)/318/2010).
Any person affected or interested in this draft variation may with in 60 days from the date of publication of this
notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette can request in person or submit in writing to the Member Secretary
Cuddalore Local Planning Authority, Objection and Suggestion relating there to.
The variation with plan may be inspected at free of cost at any time during office hours at the above said Local
Planning Authority Office Cuddalore.
VARIATION
(1)
Wherever the expression Map No.4, DDP/VR/DTCP. No.21/2010, occurs the expression Map No.5,
DDP/V/DTCP No.2013 shall be added at the end to be read with.
(2)
In schedule III, part-1, proposed “B5B5” 12.0m (40'.0") scheme road part entry 76/1pt, 76/2pt, shall be
deleted.
(3)
In Schedule-IV ( From No.7 ) in serial No.1 entry proposed “B5B5” 12.0m (40'.0") scheme road deleted
and B5B5 road part to be added at that same place and in column No.4 instead of 4.61.76 Hectare
“4.47.67” Hectare shall be substituted.
(4)
In schedule-V(Form No.6) S.No.76/1pt, 76/2pt entries in the proposed “B5B5” 12.0m (40'.0") scheme road
Part shall be deleted.
