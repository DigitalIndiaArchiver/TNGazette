Amendments to the High Court Fees Rules, 1956
(R.O.C.No.12396-A/2021/F1)

Court of Judicature at Madras and all other powers hereunto enabling and with the conﬁ rmation of the Government of 
Tamil Nadu, the High Court of Madras hereby makes the following amendments to the High Court Fees Rules, 1956. 
The Amendments shall come into force with eﬀ ect from 01-01-2022.
