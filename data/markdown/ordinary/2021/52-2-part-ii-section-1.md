"The Commissionerate of Transport will be changed as Commissionerate of Transport and Road Safety." 
2. Based on the above Announcement, the Transport Commissioner has sent necessary proposal to Government in the 
reference second read above and requested that the designation of Commissionerate of Transport may be re-designated as 
Commissionerate of Transport and Road Safety. 
3. The Government have examined the proposal of the Transport Commissioner in detail. The Government direct that 
the nomenclature of the "Commissionerate of Transport" be changed as "Commissionerate of Transport and Road Safety". 
4. This order shall come into force with immediate effect. 
5. Necessary amendments to this effect to the Tamil Nadu Business Rules, 1978 will be issued by the Government in 
Human Resources Management Department separately.
