No. II(2)/CTR/562(a)/2017.—The following errata are issued to the Commercial Taxes and
Registration Department Notification No.II(2)/CTR/532(d-4)/2017, published at pages 3 to 68 of
Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 29th June 2017:—
ERRATA
In the said Notification,-
(i)
 at page 12, in serial number 180, in column (2), for “30”, read “30 or any Chapter”;
(ii)  at page 34, in serial number 42, in column (3), omit the words “other than those”; and
(iii)  at page 52, , in serial number 411, in column (3), omit the words “goggles and the like,
 corrective, protective or other”.
