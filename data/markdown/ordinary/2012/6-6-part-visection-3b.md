THE TAMIL NADU DR. M.G.R. MEDICAL UNIVERSITY, CHENNAI.
Two Members Elected to the Governing Council of The Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai by  the
Deans /Principals of Affiliated Colleges providing courses of study and training in Medical Science.
[Rc. No. EIII(1)/32611/2011.]
No. VI-3(b)/2/2012.
Under Sub-Statues (1) of Statute 5 of Chapter XXI of the General Statutes of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical
University, Chennai, it is hereby published that the following member are elected to the Governing Council of the
Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai, by the Deans and Principals of affiliated colleges providing courses
of study and training in Medical Sciences under Clause (b) of sub-Section (4) of Section 21 of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R.
Medical University, Chennai Act, 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 37 of 1987) in the regular vacancies.
1. Prof. S. Anigrace Kalaimathi, M.Sc.(N),
Principal,
MIOT College of Nursing
1/70, Mariamman Koil Street,
Mugalivakkam, Chennai-600 116.
2. Dr. T. Sivakumar, M. Pharm., Ph.D.,
Principal,
Nandha College of Pharmacy,
Koorapalayam ''Privu''
Perundurai Main Road, Erode-638 052.
2. The tenure of the above members in the Governing Council of this University will be for the regular peroid from
20-12-2011 to 19-12-2014 or the date on which they cease to be a member of the Deans and Principals of affiliated Colleges
providing courses of study and training in Medical Sciences, whichever is earlier.
DR. R. SRILAKSHMI,
Registrar (FAC)/Returning Officer.
