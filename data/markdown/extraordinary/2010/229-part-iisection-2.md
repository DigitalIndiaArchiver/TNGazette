ENERGY DEPARTMENT
AUTHORIZATION OF THE TAMIL NADU ELECTRICITY BOARD TO CONTINUE TO FUNCTION AS THE
STATE TRANSMISSION UTILITY AND A LICENSEE UNDER THE ELECTRICITY ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 72, Energy (B2), 28th July 2010.]
No. II(2)/EGY/486(f)/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to clause (a) of Section 172 of the Electricity Act, 2003
(Central Act 36 of 2003), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby authorizes the Tamil Nadu Electricity Board to
continue to function as the State Transmission Utility and a Licensee under the provisions of the said Act for a
further period upto 15-09-2010 beyond 15-06-2010 as mutually decided by the Central Government and
this Government.
P.W.C. DAVIDAR,
Principal Secretary to Government.
