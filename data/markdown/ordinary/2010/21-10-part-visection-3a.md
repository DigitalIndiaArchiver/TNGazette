GENERAL NOTIFICATION
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD
 No. 1, Jaffar Syrang Street, Vallal Seethakathi Nagar, Chennai-600 001.
Taking over the Direct Management of Moulana Mohamed Ali Munaetra Sangam, Dharmapuri District.
(Rc. No. 3598/85/B6/DPI)
No. VI-3(a)/77/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995, the
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in Item No. 80/10 in Rc. No. 3598/85/B6/DPI, dated 29th April 2010 has ordered
to take over the Management of Moulana Mohamed Ali Munaetra Sangam (Mohamed Ali Memorial Club, Madrasa
Mehrazol Uloom Arabic College, Majeeth-e-Bilal and Islamia Kalvi Sangam), Dharmapuri District under the Direct Control
of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of one year from the date of the Board's order dated 29th April 2010
and has appointed the Superintendent of Wakfs, Salem as its Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
18th May 2010.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
