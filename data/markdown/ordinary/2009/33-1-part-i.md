No. I/PUSP/15/2009.—The following Notification of the Government of India, Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances
and Pensions, Department of Personnel and Training,  New Delhi, dated 4th August 2009  is republished:—
In exercise of the powers conferred by the Rule 6(1) of the Indian Administrative Service (Cadre) Rules,
1954 and with the concurrence of the Governments of Tamil Nadu and Rajasthan, the Central Government hereby
accords approval for inter cadre deputation of Shri Har Sahay Meena, IAS (TN:97) from IAS cadre of  Tamil Nadu
to the IAS cadre of Rajasthan for a period of three years with effect from the date of his joining the
Rajasthan Government.
[No. 13017/17/2009 AIS(I)]
