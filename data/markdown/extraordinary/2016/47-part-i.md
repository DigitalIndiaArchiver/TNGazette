RELIEVED A MINISTER FROM THEIR MINISTERSHIP OF COUNCIL OF MINISTERS.
[G.O. Ms. No. 197, Public (Special-B), 20th February 2016, Maasi 8, Manmadha, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2047.]
No. I/PUSP/4(a-1)/2016.
I, K. Rosaiah, Governor of Tamil Nadu accept the recommendation of the Chief Minister that  Thiru B.V. Ramanaa,
Minister for Milk and Dairy Development  be relieved from the Council of Ministers and direct that he be relieved of his duties
and responsibilities as Minister with immediate effect.
K. ROSAIAH,
Governor of Tamil Nadu.
ALLOCATION  OF  SUBJECTS  AMONG  MINISTER
[G.O. Ms. No. 198, Public (Special-B), 20th February 2016, Maasi 8, Manmadha, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2047.]
No. I/PUSP/4(a-2)/2016.
Under Clause (3) of Article 166 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that
Thiru P.  Mohan, Minister for Rural Industries and Labour shall hold full additional charge of the portfolio ‘Milk and Dairy
Development’ hitherto held by Thiru B.V. Ramanaa and direct that Thiru P. Mohan   be designated as Minister for
Rural Industries, Labour, Milk and Dairy Development.
K. GNANADESIKAN,
Chief Secretary.
