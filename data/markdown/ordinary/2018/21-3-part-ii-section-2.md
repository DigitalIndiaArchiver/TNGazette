No.II(2)/AG/468/2018.вҖ”Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima 
Yojana (PMFBY) will be implemented in all the Districts of 
Tamil Nadu (except Chennai) with the State Government 
subsidy during 2018-2019 for Kharif Crops. 
I. 	 Objectives of Pmfby: 
PMFBY aims at supporting sustainable production in 
agriculture by way of 
(a)	 Providing financial support to farmers suffering 
crop loss/damage arising out of unforeseen events. 
(b)	 Stabilizing the income of farmers to ensure their 
continuance in farming. 
(c)	 Encouraging farmers to adopt innovative and 
modern Agriculture practices. 
(d)	 Ensuring flow of credit to the agriculture sector, 
which will, contribute to food security, crop diversification 
and enhancing growth and competitiveness of agriculture 
sector besides protecting farmers from production risks. 
II. 	Coverage of Farmers: 
All farmers including share croppers and tenant farmers 
growing the notified crops in the notified areas are eligible 
for coverage. 
вҖў	
Compulsory for Loanee farmers. 
вҖў	
Optional for Non - Loanee farmers. 
Ill. 	Coverage of Risks and Exclusions: 
The following stages of the crop and Risks leading to 
crop loss are covered  under the scheme: 
1. Prevented Sowing/ Planting Risk- If Insured area is 
prevented from sowing / planting due to deficit rainfall or 
adverse seasonal conditions and non release of irrigation 
water on scheduled date of opening of dam for irrigation 
in its command areas. 
2..  Standing Crop (Sowing To Harvesting),-
Comprehensive risk insurance is provided to cover yield 
losses due to non - preventable risks viz. Drought, dry 
spells, flood, inundation, pests and diseases, landslides, 
natural fire and lightening, storm, hailstorm, cyclone, typhoon, 
tempest, hurricane and tornado. 
3. Post-Harvest Losses- Coverage against specific 
perils of cyclone, cyclonic rains and unseasonal rains is 
available only upto a maximum period of  two weeks from 
harvesting, for those crops which are allowed to dry in cut 
and  spread condition in the field after harvesting. 
4. Localized Calamities- Loss/damage resulting due to 
occurrence of identified localized risks viz., hailstorm, landslide 
and inundation affecting isolated farms in the notified area. 
In the 40th SLCCCI meeting conducted on 27-03-2018 
for notification of crops during Kharif 2018, the Committee 
has authorized the District Level Monitoring Committee under 
the Chairmanship of District Collector to fix the seasonality 
discipline for prevented sowing/failed sowing and Post harvest 
losses at farm level and invoking mid-season adversity for 
on-account payment (vide G.O.Ms.No.293, Agriculture (AP6) 
Department, dated 12-9-2016) as done during 2016-2017 
and 2017-2018.

FarmerвҖҷs Premium:
