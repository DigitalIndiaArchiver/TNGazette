NOTICE UNDER SECTION 15(1) OF TAMIL NADU HIGHWAYS ACT, 2001(TN ACT 34 OF 2002) READ WITH THE 
TAMIL NADU LAND ACQUISITION LAWS (REVIVAL OF OPERATION, AMENDMENT AND VALIDATION) ACT, 2019.
No. VI(1)/220(c)/2021.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that the lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below are acquired 
under Chennai – Kanniyakumari Industrial Corridor Project for Highways purpose for the formation of Bypass in of
SH-95 Mohanur - Namakkal - Senthamangalam - Rasipuram Road from KM 31.850 to KM 32.050 and KM 32.540 to 
KM 34.060 (Length 01.720 KM) at Bommasamuthiram (Bypass) Village in Senthamangalam Taluk, Namakkal District, 
and it having already been decided that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands to be paid 
out of the funds controlled and managed by the Divisional Engineer(H), Salem (CKICP), Tamil Nadu after having 
considering the cause shown by the owner or other persons having interest on such lands as the case may be do 
hereby publish the following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001
(TN Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) read with 
the Tamil Nadu Land Acquisition Laws (Revival of Operation, Amendment and Validation) Act 2019, the Governor of 
Tamil Nadu hereby notiﬁ ed that the Dry and Wet lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below and measuring to an extent of 
55565 sq.mtr., are acquired in Bommasamuthiram (Bypass) Village in Senthamangalam Taluk, Namakkal District, to the 
same, a little more or less is needed for Chennai-Kanniyakumari Industrial Corridor Project for State Highways purpose 
for the formation of Bypass in SH-95 Mohanur - Namakkal - Senthamangalam - Rasipuram Road from KM 31.850 to 
KM 32.050 and KM 32.540 to KM 34.060 (Length 01.720 KM). 
The plan of the lands under acquisition are kept in the oﬃ  ce of the Special District Revenue oﬃ  cer (Land Acquisition), 
Chennai - Kanniyakumari Industrial Corridor Project, Salem and oﬃ  ce of the Special Tahsildar (Land Acquisition), Chennai 
– Kanniyakumari Industrial Corridor Project, Namakkal and may be inspected at any time during the oﬃ  ce hours.
