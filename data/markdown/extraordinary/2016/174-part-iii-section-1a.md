AMENDMENTS TO THE TAMIL NADU PANCHAYATS (RESERVATION OF SEATS AND ROTATION OF
RESERVED SEATS) RULES.
[G.O. Ms. No. 87, Rural Development and Panchayat Raj (PR-1), 25th July 2016.]
No. SRO A-13(b-1)/2016.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 242 read with Sections 11, 20, 32 and 57 of the
Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following
amendments to the Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Reservation of Seats and Rotation of Reserved Seats) Rules, 1995:-
 AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules, in rule 7, in sub-rule (2),-
(1) in clause (a),-
(a) for the expression “Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes in the Wards”, the expression “Scheduled
Castes and Scheduled Tribes and Women in the Wards” shall be substituted; and
(b) after the expression “relevant figures have been published”, the expression “or for women” shall be inserted;
(2) in Explanation 1 to clause (a),-
(a) after the expression “relevant figures have been published”, the expression “or for women” shall be inserted;
(b) after the expression “the population of the Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes”, the expression
“or women” shall be inserted; and
(c) after the expression “percentage of Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes”, the expression “or of women”
shall be inserted.

[G.O. Ms. No. 88, Rural Development and Panchayat Raj (PR-1), 25th July 2016.]
No. SRO A-13(b-2)/2016.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 242 read with Sections 11, 20, 32 and 57 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act,
1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the
Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Election) Rules, 1995:-
 AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules.-
(1) in rule 24, in sub-rule (2),-
(i) in clause (ii), for the expression “11-00 a.m. and 3-00 p.m.”, the expression “10-00 a.m. and 5-00 p.m.”
shall be substituted;
(ii) in clause (iii), for the expression “11-00 a.m.”, the expression “10-00 a.m.” shall be substituted;
(2) in rule 29, in the proviso to sub-rule (7), for the expression “11-00 a.m.”, the expression “10-00 a.m.” shall be
substituted;
