Confirmation of Variation to the Approved Palavanthankattalai Detailed Development Plan No. 4 of
Kumbakonam Local Planning Area.
(Roc.No. 4196/2012/DP2)
No. VI(1)/243/2015.
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 33(2) of Town and Country Planning Act 1971, the Director of
Town and Country Planning, Chennai Proceedings Roc. No. 4196/2012/DP2, dated 22-8-2015 of hereby confirms the
following variation to the approved Palavanthankattalai Detailed Development Plan No. 4, Kumbakonam Local
Planning Area approved by the Director of Town and Country Planning in Proceedings Roc. No. 3963/1998/DP3,
dated 16-4-1998 and published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No. 9. Part-VI—Section-1, Page-439, dated 10-3-1999 in
Publication No. VI(I)/398/1999 and the said draft notification was published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No. 39,
Part-VI—Section-1, Page-339, dated 2-10-2013 in Publication No.VI(l)/381/2013.
Since no objections and suggestions have been received on this draft notification within the stipulated time, the
same are hereby confirmed and ordered as below:
CONFIRMATION OF VARIATION
1. The entire Palavanthankattalai Detailed Development Plan No. 4 of Kumbakonam Local Planning Area, approved
by the Director of Town and Country Planning in Proceedings Roc. No. 3963/1998/DP3 is varied by Palavanthankattalai
Detailed Development Plan No. 4 of Kumbakonam Local Planning Area.
2. The plan thus varied is numbered as Map No.5, DDP(V)/DTCP No. 17/2013 and,
3. The Detailed Development Plan clauses and schedules annexed with this plan are made enforceable from the
date of publication of this notification in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
