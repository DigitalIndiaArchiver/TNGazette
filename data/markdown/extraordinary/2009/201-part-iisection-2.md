No. II(2)/IND/375(e)/2009.
The Government of Tamil Nadu, have  been satisfied that
the lands specified in the schedule below have to be
acquired for industrial purposes in favour of Tamil Nadu
Industrial Development Corporation Limited (TIDCO) for
setting up of Petrochem Park Project, Tiruvallur, and it has
already been decided that the entire amount of compensation
to be awarded for the lands to be paid out of the funds
controlled or managed by TIDCO Limited.  The following
notice is issued under sub-section (1) of  Section 3 of  the
Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act,
1997, (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999):—
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Acquisition of  Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of  Tamil Nadu
hereby acquires the lands specified in the Schedule below
measuring 1.71.0 hectares (or) 4.22 acres to the same, a
little more or less needed for industrial purposes, i.e., in
favour of TIDCO Limited for setting up of Petrochem Park
Project in Tiruvallur District :—
The plans of the lands are kept in the office of the  Special
Tahsildar (LA), Petrochem Park Scheme, Tamil Nadu
Industrial Development Corporation Limited (TIDCO), Egmore,
Chennai–600 008, and may be inspected at any time during
office hours.
