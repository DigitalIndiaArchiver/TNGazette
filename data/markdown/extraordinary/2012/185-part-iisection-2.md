COMMERCIAL TAXES AND REGISTRATION DEPARTMENT
AMENDMENT TO REMISSION OF DUTY CHARGEABLE IN RESPECT OF INSTRUMENTS OF SALE INVOLVING
TRANSFER OF IMMOVABLE PROPERTY FOR CERTAIN VALUE UNDER THE INDIAN STAMP ACT.
[G.O.Ms.No.93 Commercial Taxes and Registration (J-1), 4th July 2012.]
No. II(2)/CTR/368(a-1)/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (a) of sub-section (1) of Section 9 of the Indian Stamp Act, 1899 (Central
Act II of 1899), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Commercial Taxes Department
Notification No.II(2)/CT/1582/98, published at page 330 in Part II-Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated
the 12th August 1998 as subsequently amended:—
2. The amendment hereby made shall come into force on the 5th July, 2012.
AMENDMENT
In the said Notification, for the expression “Rs.5,000 (Rupees five thousand only)”, the expression
“Rs.25,000 (Rupees twenty five thousand only)”, shall be substituted.


2
TAMIL  NADU  GOVERNMENT  GAZETTE  EXTRAORDINARY
AMENDMENT TO TABLE OF FEES UNDER REGISTRATION ACT.
[G.O.Ms.No.93 Commercial Taxes and Registration (J-1), 4th July 2012.]
No. II(2)/CTR/368(a-2)/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 78 of the Registration Act, 1908 (Central Act XVI of 1908), the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Table of Fees published with the erstwhile Judicial Department
Notification No.177, dated the 30th March 1909, at pages 316 to 319 of Part II of the Fort St. George Gazette, dated the
30th March 1909, as subsequently amended:—
2. The amendment hereby made shall come into force on the 5th  July, 2012.
AMENDMENT
In the said Table of Fees, in Article 1, in clause (k), in item (c-89),  for the expression “Rs.5,000 (Rupees five thousand
only)”, the expression “Rs.25,000 (Rupees twenty five thousand only)” shall be substituted.
SUNIL PALIWAL,
Secretary to Government.
