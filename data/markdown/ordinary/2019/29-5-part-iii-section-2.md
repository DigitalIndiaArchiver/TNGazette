JUDICIAL NOTIFICATIONS
Amendments to Rules 54(2) and 67(2) in Order XXI of the Civil Procedure Code, 1908.
(Roc.No. 3198/2008/F1) 
No. SRO C-11/2019.
By virtue of powers conferred by Section 122 of the Code of Civil Procedure, 1908 (Central Act V 1908) and 
all other powers hereunto enabling and with the previous approval of the Government of Tamil Nadu, the High Court, 
Madras  make the following amendments to Rules 54(2) and 67(2) in Order XXI of the Code of Civil Procedure, 1908.
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖගඛ
 
In the Code of Civil Procedure, 1908 (Central Act V of 1908), in the First Schedule, in Order XXI-
(1) In rule 54, after sub-rule (2) the following Explanation shall be added, namely:—
 
"Explanation:- The words 'other customary mode' will include announcement in any regional language as well 
as in English language through Public Address System comprising of microphones, ampliﬁ ers and related equipments."
(2) In rule 67, in sub-rule (2), after the words, "Oﬃ  cial Gazette or in a local newspaper or in both", the following 
words shall be inserted, namely:-
 
"and by distribution of handbills printed in regional language as well as in English language in the locality 
where the sale is conducted."
