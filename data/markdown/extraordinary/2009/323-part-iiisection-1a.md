In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 96 of the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988 (Central Act 59 of 1988),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Tamil Nadu
Motor Vehicles Rules, 1989, the same having been previously  published as required by sub-section (1) of
Section 212 of the said Act:—
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in rule 149, for the expression “delegate to the Secretary to Transport Commissioner,
Secretaries to State Transport  Authority and Assistant Secretaries”, the expression “delegate to the Regional
Transport Authority, Secretary to Transport Commissioner, Secretaries to State Transport Authority and
Assistant Secretaries” shall be substituted.
S. MALATHI,
Principal Secretary to Government.
