BOUNDARIES 
PART-I 
North.-The boundary of the Forest Block is starts from 
the tri-junction point of Survey Field Numbers 711 (Stream), 
739 and 5 of No.25, Kurichi Village and it runs generally 
towards east and north east along the southern side of 
Survey Field Numbers 739, 738 and 4 and reaches the 
tri-junction point of Survey Field Numbers 4, 25/4 and 16 
and thence, it turns east wards and runs along the southern 
boundary of Survey Field Numbers 25/4 and reaches the 
tri-junction point of Survey Field Numbers 25/4, 16 and 17 
all of No.25, Kurichi Village.
East.-Thence, the boundary runs towards south-east 
and south along the western side of Survey Field Number 
17 and  if  reaches the tri-junction point of Survey Field 
Numbers 17, 18 and 15 of No.25 Kurichi Village. 
South.-Thence, the boundary runs towards south, west 
and west along the northern side of Survey Field Numbers 
18, 17 and reaches the tri-junction point of Survey Field 
Numbers 17, 11 and 14 of No.25, Kurichi Village. Thence, 
it runs towards west along the Common boundary of Survey 
Field Numbers 11 and 14 and turns towards south along 
the western side of Survey Field Numbers 11, 9, 7/2B 
and northern side of Survey Field Numbers 7/3B, 6/2C1B, 
6/1B and 6/2C1 of No.25, Kurichi Village and reaches the 
tri-junction point of Survey Field Numbers 6/2A, 5 and 702 
all of No.25, Kurichi Village. 
West.-Thence, the boundary turns towards west and 
north along the northern side of Survey Field No.702 and 
western side of Survey ﬁ eld Number 711 of No.25, Kurichi 
Village and reaches the starting point. 
PART-II
North.-The boundary of the Forest Blocks is starts 
from the bi-junction point of Survey Field No.4/2 and 4/3 
of No.25 Kurichi Village. The boundary runs towards east 
along the southern side of Survey Field No.4/2 and reaches 
the tri-Junction point of Survey Field Numbers 4/2,4/3 and 
4/1 of No.25, Kurichi Village. 
East.-Thence, the boundary runs towards south-west, 
south-east and south along the western side of Survey 
Field No.4/1 and reaches the tri-junction point of Survey 
Field Numbers 4/1, 4/2 and 4/3 of No.25 Kurichi Village. 
South.-Thence, the boundary runs towards south along 
the northern side, of Survey Field No.4/2 and reaches the 
tri-Junction point of Survey Field Numbers 4/3,4/1 and 4/2 
of No.25 Kurichi Village.  
West.-Thence, the boundary runs towards north along 
the eastern side of Survey Field No.4/2 of No.25 Kurichi 
Village and reaches the starting point.
