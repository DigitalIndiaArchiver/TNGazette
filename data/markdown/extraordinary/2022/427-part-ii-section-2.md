HOME DEPARTMENT
DESIGNATION OF MAHILA COURTS/FAST TRACK MAHILA COURTS AS ADDITIONAL SPECIAL COURT 
AND THE EXISTING SPECIAL COURT FOR EXCLUSIVE TRIAL OF CASES IN CERTAIN DISTRICTS AS PRINCIPAL 
SPECIAL COURTS UNDER THE POCSO ACT
[G.O. Ms No.402, Home (Courts-II), 1st August 2022,
ஆடி 16, சுபகிருது, திருவள்ளுவர் ஆண்டு-2053.]  
No. II(2)/HO/668(c)/2022.
In exercise of powers conferred by sub-section (1) of section 28 of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act, 
2012 (Central Act 32 of 2012) and in supersession of Home Department Notification No.II(2)/HO/ 927(a-2)/2019 published at 
pages 2-4 of Part II – Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated 20.11.2019, as subsequently amended, the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu, in consultation with the Chief Justice of the High Court, Madras, hereby designates the following 
(Special Courts) specified in column (3) of the table below as Principal Special Courts exclusively to try the offences under 
the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act, 2012 (Central Act 32 of 2012) within the local area of the respective 
districts specified in column (2) thereof and designates the existing Mahila Courts / Fast Track Mahila Courts specified in 
column (4) of table below as Additional Special Courts and also designates the existing Mahila Courts / Fast Track Mahila 
Courts specified in column (5) of table below as Special Courts to try the offences under the said Act in addition to the 
regular work, within the local area of the respective districts specified in column (2) thereof:-
