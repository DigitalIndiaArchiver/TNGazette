APPOINTMENT OF HONOURABLE JUSTICE MRS. R. BANUMATHI, SENIOR JUDGE, HIGH COURT
OF MADRAS AS THE EXECUTIVE CHAIRPERSON OF THE TAMIL NADU STATE LEGAL
SERVICES AUTHORITY UNDER THE ACT.
[G.O. (D). No. 538, Home (Courts-IV), 14th July 2013.]
No. I/HO/480(e)/2013.
Under clause (b) of sub-section (2) of Section 6 of the Legal Services Authorities Act, 1987
(Central Act 39 of 1987), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, nominates Honourable Justice Mrs. R. Banumathi,
Senior Judge, High Court of Madras as the Executive Chairperson of the Tamil Nadu State Legal
Services Authority, Chennai with effect from 15th July 2013.
NIRANJAN MARDI,
Principal Secretary to Government.
