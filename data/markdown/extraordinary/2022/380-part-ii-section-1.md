RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND PANCHAYAT RAJ
DECLARATION OF HOLIDAY ON THE DATE OF POLL ON 9TH JULY 2022 (SATURDAY) AS HOLIDAY 
IN CONNECTION WITH THE POLL DAY FOR ORDINARY/CASUAL ELECTION TO THE RURAL AND 
URBAN LOCAL BODIES ELECTION, 2022.
[G.O. Ms. No. 81, Rural Development and Panchayat Raj (PR-1(1)), 7th July 2022, 
ஆனி 23, சுபகிருது, திருவள்ளுவர் ஆண்டு-2053]
No. II(1)/RDPR/36(c)/2022.
Under the Explanation to Section 25 of the Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881 (Central Act XXVI of 1881) 
read with the Notification of the Government of India, Ministry of Home Affairs No. 20-25-26, Public-I, dated the 
8th June 1957, the Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby declares Saturday, the 09th July 2022, the date on which the 
poll for the Ordinary / Casual Elections to Rural and Urban Local Bodies will take place, to be a public holiday 
in those areas wherever such poll takes place in the State of Tamil Nadu.
 
V. IRAI ANBU,
Chief Secretary.
