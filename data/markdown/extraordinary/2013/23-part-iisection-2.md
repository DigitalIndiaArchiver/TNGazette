In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the  Tamil Nadu Co-operative
Societies (Sixth Amendment) Ordinance, 2012 (Tamil Nadu Ordinance 1 of 2013), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
appoints, the 31st day of January 2013 as the date on which all the provisions of the said Ordinance,
except Sections 14 and 15 shall come into force and Sections 14 and 15 of the said Ordinance shall come into
force on the 9th day of February 2013.
