RELIEVED A MINISTER FROM THEIR MINISTERSHIP OF COUNCIL OF MINISTERS.
[G.O. Ms. No. 982, Public (Special-B), 27th July 2015, Aadi 11, Manmatha, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2046.]
No. I/PUSP/11(a-1)/2015.
I, K. Rosaiah, Governor of Tamil Nadu accept the recommendation of the Chief Minister that  Thiru V. Senthil Balaji,
Minister for Transport  be relieved from the Council of Ministers and direct that he be relieved of his duties and
responsibilities as Minister with immediate effect.
K. ROSAIAH,
Governor of Tamil Nadu.
ALLOCATION  OF  SUBJECTS  AMONG  MINISTER
[G.O. Ms. No. 983, Public (Special-B), 27th July 2015, Aadi 11, Manmatha, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2046.]
No. I/PUSP/11(a-2)/2015.
Under Clause (3) of Article 166 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that
Thiru P.  Thangamani, Minister for Industries shall hold full additional charge of the portfolio ‘Tansport, Nationalised
Transport, Motor Vehicles Act’ hitherto held by Thiru V. Senthil Balaji and direct that Thiru P. Thangamani   be designated
as Minister for Industries and Transport.
K. GNANADESIKAN,
Chief Secretary.
