Election of a Nominee by the Senate to the Selection Committee for Recommending a Panel of Three Names
to His Excellency Governor-Chancellor for the Appointment of the Vice-Chancellor.
(MSU/M&E/VCPANEL/ELECT/2015.)
No. VI-3(b)/16/2015.
It is hereby notified, by direction, that an election will be held for nominating a member by the Senate of the University
to serve on the Selection Committee of three persons who are to recommend a panel of three names to His Excellency
Governor-Chancellor for the appointment of the Vice-Chancellor under Section 11(2) of the Manonmaniam Sundaranar University
Act, 1990.
The election will be held in accordance with rules and procedures as per the Statutes of this University.
Each member of the Senate shall have a right to nominate not more than one person who is not a member of any
of the authorities of the University to the Committee. Every nomination shall be in writing signed by the proposer who shall
be a member of the Senate and seconded in writing by another member of the Senate called the seconder in the prescribed
form. The prescribed form may be obtained from the Registrar on a written requisition signed by any member of the Senate.
No nomination shall be valid unless it contains the signatures of both the proposer and the seconder and the
nomination form shall also be signed by the person proposed expressing his consent to be nominated. Every nomination must
be presented before the Registrar either by the proposer or the seconder or the person proposed, before 3.00 p.m. on Friday,
the 8th May, 2015.
The scrutiny of nomination will take place at 3.00 p.m. on the same day at the office of the Registrar, Manonmaniam
Sundaranar University, Abishekapatti, Tirunelveli-627 012. The proposer or the seconder or the proposed nominee are entitled
to be present at the time of the scrutiny.
