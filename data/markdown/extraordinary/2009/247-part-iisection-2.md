ALTERATION OF THE LIMITATION OF THE REGISTRATION
SUB-DISTRICT OF THAMARAIPATTI IN THE
REGISTRATION DISTRICT OF MADURAI (NORTH) IN
THE REVENUE DISTRICT OF MADURAI BY EXCLUDING
CERTAIN 
VILLAGES 
AND 
FORMS 
A 
NEW
REGISTRATION SUB-DISTRICT OF OTHAKADAI
UNDER THE REGISTRATION ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 138, Commercial Taxes and Registration (G),
16th September 2009,
Aavani 31, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. II(2)/CTR/474(c)/2009.
Under Section 5 of the Registration Act, 1908  (Central
Act XVI of 1908), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby, with
effect on and from the 17th September, 2009 alters the limits
of the Registration sub-district of Thamaraipatti in the
Registration district of Madurai (North) in the Revenue district
of Madurai by excluding the following villages from the said
Registration sub-district and forms a new Registration
sub-district of Othakadai in the said Registration district of
Madurai (North) and in the said Revenue district of Madurai.
The limits of the said villages shall be the same, as
determined for the purpose of revenue administration.
