In exercise of the powers conferred by sections 131 and 133 of the Electricity Act, 2003
(Central Act 36 of 2003), read with clauses 9 and 12 of the Tamil Nadu Electricity (Reorganisation and
Reforms) Transfer Scheme, 2010, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to
the said Scheme:-
2.  The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st November  2015.
AMENDMENT
In the said Scheme, in clause 9, in sub-clause (1), for the expression “All transfer of personnel from the
Board to TANGEDCO  under clause 6 shall be provisional for a period of five years from the date of transfer”,
the expression “All transfer of personnel from the Board to TANGEDCO  under clause 6 shall be provisional
for a period of seven years from the date of transfer” shall be substituted.
VIKRAM KAPUR,
