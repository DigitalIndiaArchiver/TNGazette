TRANSPORT  DEPARTMENT
ACQUISITION OF LANDS
[G.O. Ms. No. 132, Transport (E), 17th July 2012.]
No. II(2)/TRA/408(f)/2012.
The Governor of Tamil Nadu, having been satisfied that the lands specified in the schedule below have to be
acquired for industrial purpose, to wit, for the Madurai Airport Expansion Scheme and it having already been decided that
the entire amount of compensation to be, awarded for the lands to be paid out of the lands, controlled or managed by
Government.   The following notices is issued und*er sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of land
for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purpose Act, 1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands specified in the schedule below and
measuring 18.96 acres [7.67.65 hectares] to the same, a little more or less needed for industrial Purpose, to wit, for the
Madurai Airport Expansion Scheme.
The plan of the lands are kept in the office of the Special Tahsildar (Land Acquisition), Madurai Airport Expansion
Scheme,   Unit-II, Block No. III, Madurai South Taluk, Madurai District and may be inspected at any time during office hours.
