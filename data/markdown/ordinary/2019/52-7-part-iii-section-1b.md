No. SRO B-97/2019.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309  of the Constitution of India, 
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes  the following amendments to the Rules published with the Home  Department 
Notification No.SRO B-217/85, dated the 4th May 1985, published at pages 229 to 251 of Part III—Section 1(b) of the 
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 5th June 1985, as  subsequently amended.  
2.	
The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force with effect on and from the 
1st June 1988.
Amendments.
In the said rules,-  
	
(1)	 in rule 3, in the TABLE, in column (2), against the entry  "15.Chargeman" in column (1),-  
	
	
i)	
in item (i), for the expression "post of Tool Keeper and  Fitter, Grade- Il", the expression "post of Tool 
Keeper and  Fitter, Grade-I" shall be substituted;  
	
	
ii) 	 in item (ii), for the expression "Tool Keeper or Fitter,  Grade-Il", the expression "Tool Keeper or Fitter, 
Grade-I"  shall be substituted;  
	
(2)	 in rule 5, in sub-rule (b), in the TABLE, in column (4),-  
	
	
i)	
against the entries "11", "Fitter Grade- II" and "By  recruitment by transfer from any other service" in 
columns  (1), (2) and (3) respectively, for the expression "Helper",  the expression "Fitter, Grade- Il" shall be substituted; 
	
	
ii)	
against the entries "25", "chargeman" and "By promotion"  in columns (1), (2) and (3) respectively for 
the expression  "Must have put in service for not less than three years in  the post of Tool Keeper or Fitter, Grade-lI", the 
expression  "Must have put in service for not less than three years in  the post of Tool Keeper or Fitter, Grade-I" shall be 
substituted; and  
	
(3)	 In rule (9), after the expression "Police Transport  Workshop-Cum-Training School, Avadi", the expression "or 
in the Regional Police Transport Workshop, Trichy”, shall be inserted.
