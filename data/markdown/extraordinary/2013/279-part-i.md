The following Notification of the Government of India, Ministry of Law and Justice, (Department of
Justice), Jaisalmer House, 26, Man Singh Road, New Delhi-110 011, dated 24th September 2013.
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (1) of Article 217 of the Constitution of India, the
President is pleased to appoint (i) Shri Justice T. Mathivanan, (ii) Shri Justice Ardhanariswamy
Arumughaswamy, (iii) Miss Justice K.B.K. Vasuki, (iv) Shri Justice Kandasami Ravichandra Baabu,
(v) Shri Justice P. Devadass and (vi) Shri Justice R. Karuppiah, Additional Judges of the Madras High
Court to be Judges of the Madras High Court with effect from the date they assume charge of their
respective offices.
