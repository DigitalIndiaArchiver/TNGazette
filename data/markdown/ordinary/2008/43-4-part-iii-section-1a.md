Amendments  to Tamil Nadu Payment of Wages Rules, 1937.
[G.O. (D) No. 394, Labour and Employment (H1), 29th September 2008.]
No.SRO A-44/2008.— In exercise  of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 26 of the Payment of Wages Act,
1936 (Central Act IV of 1936), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following Amendments to the Tamil Nadu
Payment of Wages Rules, 1937, the Draft of the same having been previously published as required by sub-section (5) of
Section 26 of the said Act.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules,—
(1) after Rule 6A, the following Rule shall be inserted, namely:—
“6B. Self Certification by Information Technology Software Establishments and Information Technology
Software Enabled Services.—The Paymaster of every Information Technology Software Establishment and Information
Technology Software Enabled Service shall furnish to the Assistant Inspector of Labour at the end of each half year, a
certificate of maintenance or exhibition of registers, records and notices, which are required to be maintained or exhibited under
the provisions of these Rules, in Form VIII (in duplicate). The certificate for the half-year ending 30th June should be furnsihed
before the 31st July of that year and the certificate for the half-year ending 31st December should be furnished before the
31st January of the succeeding year.”;
(2) after Form VII, the following Form shall be added, namely:—
“FORM VIII
(See Rule 6B)
