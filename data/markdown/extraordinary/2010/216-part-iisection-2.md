ACQUISITION OF LANDS
[G.O. Ms. No. 184, Transport (I.2), 19th July 2010.]
No. II(2)/TRA/433(c)/2010.
The Governor of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that the
lands specified in the schedule below have to be acquired for
Industrial purpose, to wit, for the Madurai Airport Expansion
Scheme and it having already been decided that the entire
amount of compensation to be, awarded for the lands to be
paid out of the lands, controlled or managed by Government.
The following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of
Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of land for Industrial
Purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil
Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
acquires the lands specified in the schedule below and measuring
39.22 acres (or) 15.88 hectares, to the same, a little more or
less needed for Industrial Puspose, to wit, for the Madurai
Airport Expansion Scheme.
The Plan of the lands are kept in the office of the Special
Tahsildar (Land Acquisition), Madurai Airport Expansion Scheme,
Unit I, Block V, Madurai South Taluk, Madurai  District and may
be inspected at any time during  office hours.
