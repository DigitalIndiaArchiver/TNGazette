GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI
Taking over of Syed Mirsha Hussaini Tomb, Burial Ground and Mosque, Venkundram, Vandavasi,
Tiruvannamalai,  under Direct Management.
(Rc. No. 11609/04/B7/TVM)
No. VI-3(a)/68/2014.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995, as amended by
Act 27 of 2013, the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in item No. 134/13 in Rc.No.11609/04/B7/TVM dated the 19th
February 2014 has ordered to take over the Syed Mirsha Hussaini Tomb, Burial Ground and Mosque, Venkundram, Vandavasi,
Tiruvannamalai under the direct management of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of one year from the date of
Publication and to appoint the Wakf Inspector, Tiruvannamalai as its Executive Officer.
A. MOHAMED MEERAN,
Chennai-600 001,
District Revenue Officer/
31st July 2014.
Chief Executive Officer (in charge),
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
Framing of Scheme in respect of Uthuman Lebbai Sahib Mosque Trust, Thittuvilai, Kanyakumari District.
(Lr. Rc. No. 17228/09/B1/KK)
No. VI-3(a)/69/2014.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 69 of the Wakf (Amendment), Act 2013, the Tamil
Nadu Wakf Board in its Proceedings in item No.19/14 in Rc.No.17228/09/B1/KK, dated 29-01-2014 has approved the Scheme
of administration of the Wakf viz., Uthuman Lebbai Sahib Mosque Trust, Thittuvilai, Kanyakumari District.
A. MOHAMED MEERAN,
Chennai-600 001,
District Revenue Officer/
31st July 2014.
Chief Executive Officer (in charge),
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
Extension of period of Direct Management of K.Maqdoom Maracair Waqf, Chennai.
(Lr.Rc. No. 4592/07/B9/CHN)
No. VI-3(a)/70/2014.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995, the Tamil Nadu Wakf
Board in its resolution in item No.22/14 in Rc.No.4592/07/B9/CHN, dated 19-2-2014 has resolved to ratify the period of direct
management of K.Maqdoom Maracair Waqf from 29-11-2013 to 19-02-2014 and to extend the period of direct management
for a further period of one year from the date of Board's order (19.02.2014).
A. MOHAMED MEERAN,
Chennai-600 001,
District Revenue Officer/
31st July 2014.
Chief Executive Officer (in charge),
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
