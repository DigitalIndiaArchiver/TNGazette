ERRATA TO NOTIFICATIONS
THE TAMIL NADU HINDU RELIGIOUS AND CHARITABLE ENDOWMENTS
(AMENDMENT) BILL, 2013.
(L.A. Bill No. 25 of 2013).
In the English Version of the Tamil Nadu Hindu Religious and Charitable
Endowments (Amendment) Bill, 2013 (L.A. Bill No. 25 of 2013) Published at
page 173 of Part IV-Section-1 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary,
dated 28-10-2013, the following corrections may be made:—
At page 173—
In the contents, under the heading ‘Bills’, for the expression
“No. 24 of 2013 - Tamil Nadu Hindu Religious and Charitable Endowments
(Amendment) Act, 2013.”.
Substitute the expression—
“No. 25 of 2013- Tamil Nadu Hindu Religious and Charitable Endowments
(Amendment) Act, 2013.”.
A.M.P. JAMALUDEEN,
Secretary.
