Rules, 1995 and in supersession of the Notification No. VI(2)/32(a)/2017, Published at Part VI – Section 2 of the 
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, No.64, dated March 16, 2017, at Pages 1-5, the Tamil Nadu State 
Election Commission hereby directs that,-
