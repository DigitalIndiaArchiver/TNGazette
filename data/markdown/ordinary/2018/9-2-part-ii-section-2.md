Rights:
Zamin Singampatti Village Part II Survey No.251 is 
registered as “Forest” (å¶‚èŠð†ìè£´) in ‘A’ register. Bombay 
Burmah Trading Corporation is a lessee under the lease 
deed dated 12-02-1929 for 99 years executed between 
the Singampatti Zamindar and the company and the area 
under lease is 8373.57 acres (3388.78.0 Hectare). Bombay 
Burmah Trading Corporation filed a claim petition No.5/79 
Under Section 10 of the Tamil Nadu Forest Act 1882, 
(Tamil Nadu Act V of 1882) to hold and exclude the 
above said land from the limits of proposed Reserved 
Forest.  Finally the Forest Settlement Officer rejected the 
claim of the company in proceeding No.5368/08 dated 
06-01-2010 under Section 10 of Tamil Nadu Forest Act 1882, 
(Tamil Nadu Act V of 1882).  Against the Forest Settlement 
Officer’s order, the Bombay Burmah Trading Corporation filed 
an appeal in District Court, Tirunelveli in CMA 4/2010 which 
was also dismissed by the District Court by judgment dated 
1-9-2015. As against the said order of the District Court 
Bombay Burmah Trading Corporation has preferred revision 
in CRP(MD) 2745 of 2015 on the file of Madurai Bench 
of Madras High Court, which was later transferred to the 
Principal Bench and directed to be tagged along with the 
3 other writ petitions namely WP Nos.41358, 41359 and 
41360 of 2005 filed by Bombay Burmah Trading Corporation 
challenging the proposal for revision of lease rent, show 
cause notice for termination of lease and Mandamus claiming 
patta respectively. The Hon’ble High Court by common 
order dated 1-9-2017 passed in the above said case 
was pleased to reject the claims made by M/s. Bombay 
Burmah  Trading  Corporation  and  granted  a  part  relief 
namely that the Bombay Burmah Trading Corporation shall 
remain in possession of the leased area till 11-02-2028 as 
ordered by the Board of Revenue under Section 19A of the 
Tamil Nadu Estate (Abolition and Conversion into Ryotwari) 
Act, 1948 (Tamil Nadu Act XXVI of 1948).
Accordingly, as directed by the Hon’ble High Court 
the Survey No.251 of Zamin Singampatti village part II is 
hereby declared as Reserved Forest under Section 16 of 
the Tamil Nadu Forest Act, 1882 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 
1882). The claim of Bombay Burmah Trading Corporation 
is admitted taking into consideration of the Hon’ble High 
court order dated 01-09-2017,  under Section 19A of the 
Tamil Nadu Estate (Abolition and Conversion into Ryotwari) 
Act, 1948 alone, which comes to an end on 11-2-2028 
or termination of lease , whichever is earlier, after which 
Bombay Burmah Trading Corporation shall have no rights in 
the Zamin Singampatti village part II Survey No. 251 and 
in pursuance of Hon’ble High court order dated 01-09-2017, 
the said claim will cease to exist.
