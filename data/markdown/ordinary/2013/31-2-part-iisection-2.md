In the Government order fifth read above orders have
been issued appointing Thiru C. Ayyam Perumal son of
A. Chelliah, No. 96/80, Ganapathy Nagar, M.G.R. Street,
Villapuram Madurai-2 as Official Receiver for Madurai District
for a period of three years with effect on and from the date
on which he assumes charge of his Office.
2. In the letter 6th read above, the Registrar (Administration),
Madurai Bench of Madras High Court has opined that the
appointment of Thiru C. Ayyam Perumal, Advocate, Madurai,
as Official Receiver for Madurai District, is in contravention of
Rule 2 of the Part-Time Official Receiver (conditions of Service)
Rules as the said Rule 2 contemplates that the entire panel
of Advocates should be sent by the District Judge and the High
Court shall in turn forward the entire panel to the Government
with its recommendations and the Government will take a final
decision on merits.  Even as per Rule 5 of the said Rules,
the Government in the event of terminating the earlier Official
Receiver has to call for fresh panel from the High Court for
such appointment.
In the instant case, i.e., in the appointment of Thiru
C. Ayyam Perumal, Advocate, Madurai, as Official Receiver
Madurai District, both Rule 2 and Rule 5 of the Part-Time
Official Receiver (Conditions of Service) Rules have been
overlooked and Contravened.”
The Registrar (Administration), Maduai Bench of Madras
High Courts, Madurai has therefore requested that the
appointment of Thiru C Ayyam Perumal, Advocate, Madurai
as Official Receiver for Madurai District may be cancelled
and the Panel of Advocates already forwarded by the High
Court may be considered for appointment of Official Receiver
for Madurai District.
3. The Government have examined the case and have
decided to cancel the appointment of Thiru C. Ayyam
Perumal as Official Receiver for Madurai District.
Accordingly, the Government  direct that the orders issued
in G.O. fifth read above appointing Thiru C. Ayyam Perumal
son of A. Chelliah, No. 96/80, Ganapathy Nagar, M.G.R.
Street, Villapuram Madurai-2 as Official Receiver for District
Madurai be cancelled.
