Under the Explanation to Section 25 of the Negotiable Instruments Act 1881 (Central Act XXVI of 1881) read with
Notification of the Government of India, Ministry of Home Affairs No.20-25-26, Public - 1, dated 8th June 1957 the Government
of Tamil Nadu hereby declares that Monday 6th October 2014 shall be a Public Holiday on account of Bakrid instead of
the 5th October 2014.
The above Public Holiday notified shall also apply to all State Government Undertakings/Corporation/Boards etc.,
