Winding up of the Affairs of the Computer Stationery Manufacturing and Printing Industrial Co-operative Society
Limited, Ind. No.11/MDS, Chennai and appointment of Official Liquidator.
(Rc. No. 19440/ICM/2006-2.)
No. VI(1)/286/2009.
Under the powers conferred on the Registrar of Industrial Co-operatives under Section 137(1) of the Tamil Nadu
Co-operative Societies Act, 1983 (Act 30 of 1983), the Activities of the Computer Stationery  Manufacturing and Printing
Industrial Co-operative Society Limited, Ind.No. 11/MDS, Chennai have been ordered to be wound up with immediate
effect by the Industries Commissioner and Director of Industries  and Commerce and Registrar of Industrial
Co-operative Societies, Chepauk, Chennai-5, vide Proceedings R.C. No. 19440/ICM/2006-1, dated 12th September 2009.
Under Section 138(1) of the same Act, Deputy Director (In-charge), Guindy, Chennai-32 is appointed as the Official
Liquidator of the said Society for the purpose of Liquidation.
Chepauk, Chennai-600 005,
DHEERAJ KUMAR,
12th September 2009.
Industries Commissioner and Director of
Industries and Commerce/Registrar of
 Industrial Co-operative Societies.
Variation to the Approved Detailed Development Plan No. 1 of Thuraiyur Local Planning Area.
(Roc. No. 22597/08/DP3.)
No.VI(1)/287/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (1) of Section 33 of Town and Country Planning Act, 1971
(Act 35 of 1972), the Commissioner/Director of Town and Country Planning in the proceedings Roc.No. 22597/08 DP3,
dated 8th September 2009, proposes to make the following variation to the Thuraiyur Detailed Development Plan
No. 1 approved vide proceedings Roc.No. 56022/91/DP2, dated 4th January 1992, of the Director of Town and Country
Planning and published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette (Issue No. 10), VI(1)/366/92, Page No. 274,
Part VI—Section 1, dated 11th March 1992.
2. Any person affected or interested in this draft variation may within SIXTY days from the date of publication of
this notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, can represent  in person or submit in writing to the Member-
Secretary, Thuraiyur Local Planning Authority any objections and suggestions relating thereto.
3. The variation with plan may be inspected free of cost at any time during office hours at the above said Local Planning
Authority Office.
VARIATION
1. The entire Detailed Development Plan No. 1 of Thuraiyur Local Planning Area is comprehensively
varied by the Commissioner of Town and Country Planning  vide proceedings Roc. No. 22597/08 DP3,
dated 8th September 2009.
2.
The Plan thus varied is numbered as Map No. 5 DDP(V)DTCP No. 20/2009.
3. The Detailed Development Plan clauses and schedules annexed with this plan area made enforceable from the
date of publication of the confirmed variation notification to be issued Under Section 33(2) of the Act and published in
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
Chennai-600 002,
ASHOK DONGRE,
8th September 2009.
Commissioner of Town and Country Planning.
