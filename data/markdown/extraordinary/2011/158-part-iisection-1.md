NOTIFICATION  BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY SECRETARIAT.
LEGISLATIVE  ASSEMBLY  SECRETARIAT
APPOINTMENT OF SPEAKER AND DEPUTY SPEAKER OF THE  TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY
AS THE PERSONS BEFORE WHOM THE MEMBERS MAY MAKE AND SUBSCRIBE OATH OR AFFIRMATION.
[S.O. Ms. No. 70, Legislative Assembly Secretariat, 27th May 2011,
Vaikasi 13, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No. II(1)/LAS/(12(i)/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Article 188 of the Constitution of India, I, SURJIT SINGH BARNALA,
Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby appoint Thiru D. JAYAKUMAR, Speaker, Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly and
Thiru P. DHANAPAL, Deputy Speaker, Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly, as the persons before whom the Oath or Affirmation
may be made and subscribed by the Members of the Legislative Assembly under the said Article during the term
of their office.
Camp at Chandigarh,
SURJIT SINGH BARNALA,
27th May, 2011.
Governor of Tamil Nadu.
A.M.P. JAMALUDEEN,
Secretary.
