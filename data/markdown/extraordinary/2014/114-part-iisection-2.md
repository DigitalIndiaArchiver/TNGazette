DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF THE CHENNAI CITY POLICE (EXTENSION TO THE CITIES OF
SALEM, TIRUCHIRAPPALLI AND TIRUNELVELI) AMENDMENT ACT, 2014.
(TAMIL NADU ACT 2 OF 2014)
[G.O. Ms. No. 409, Home (Pol.XIV), 5th June 2014.]
No. II(2)/HO/347(a-1)/2014.
Under sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Chennai City Police (Extension to the Cities of Salem,
Tiruchirappalli and Tirunelveli) Amendment Act, 2014 (Tamil Nadu Act 2 of 2014), the  Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby appoints    the 5th June 2014 as the date on which the said Act shall come into force.
