"The Honourable Speaker has learnt with deep regret the death of Honourable Thiru C. Karuppasamy,
a Member of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly on 22nd October 2011".

[Letter No. 12707/2011-1, Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Secretariat (B-III), 28th October 2011,
Aippasi 11, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No. II(1)/LAS/21(a-2)/2011.
"A vacancy has occurred among the Members of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly, consequent on the death of
Honourable Thiru C. Karuppasamy, a Member elected to the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly from the
219. Sankarankovil (SC) Assembly Constituency in Tirunelveli District".
