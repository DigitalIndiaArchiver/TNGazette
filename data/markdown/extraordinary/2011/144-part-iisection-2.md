NOTIFICATION REGARDING THE APPOINTMENT OF NATIONAL INSTITUTE OF OCEANOGRAPHY
(NIO) AND NATIONAL ENVIRONMENTAL ENGINEERING RESEARCH INSTITUTE (NEERI) TO TAKE UP
ENVIRONMENTAL IMPACT ASSESSMENT STUDY (EIA) FOR DISPOSAL OF THE EFFLUENTS FROM
TEXTILE DYEING UNITS UNDER THE MARINE DISCHARGE PROJECT UNDER THE TAMIL NADU
TRANSPARENCY IN TENDERS ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 142, Finance (Salaries), 19th May 2011, Vaikasi 5, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No.II(2)/FIN/223(a)/2011.
Under clause (f) of Section 16 of the Tamil Nadu Transparency in Tenders Act, 1998 (Tamil Nadu
Act 43 of 1998), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notifies the appointment of National Institute of
Oceanography (NIO) and National Environmental Engineering Research Institute (NEERI) to take up
Environmental Impact Assessment Study (EIA) for disposal of the effluents from textile dyeing units under the
Marine Discharge Project.
K. SHANMUGAM,
Principal Secretary to Government.
