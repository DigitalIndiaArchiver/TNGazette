WHEREAS on considering the recommendations of the expert team of Doctors and Public Health Specialists and based 
on the directives of Government of India, Ministry of Home Aﬀ airs, State-wide lockdown was extended from time to time 
and lastly extended till 24:00 hrs of 30.09.2020 under the Disaster Management Act, 2005 in GO.Ms.No.447, Revenue and 
Disaster Management (DM-II) Department, dated 31.08.2020 with various relaxations and certain restrictions.
2. At present, operation of both Public and Private Bus Transport within the district and Metropolitan Bus Services in 
Greater Chennai has been permitted from 1.9.2020 after following the Standard Operating Procedures and that the Inter State 
Rail Transport in the authorised routes has also been permitted. 
3. Hon'ble Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu in the Press Release, dated 2.9.2020 it has been stated that considering the 
representation received from the Public and welfare of the General Public, operation of both Public and Private Bus Transport 
throughout the State has been permitted from 07.09.2020 and that the Passenger Rail Transport within the State has also 
been permitted to commence from 07.09.2020.
  
4. Accordingly, the Principal Secretary and Commissioner of Revenue Administration has sent the amendment proposal 
to the G.O.Ms.No.447, Revenue and Disaster Management Department, dated 31.08.2020.
5. Now therefore the Government hereby accept the proposal of the Principal Secretary and Commissioner of Revenue 
Administration and amend the G.O.Ms.No.447,Revenue and Disaster Management Department, dated 31.08.2020 as detailed 
below:-
