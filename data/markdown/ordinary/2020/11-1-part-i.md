ORDER: G.O. Ms. No. 113, Home (Courts-I), 21st February  
 
2020
The Registrar (Recruitment), High Court of Madras, 
in his letter third read above has stated that Tvl. G.Arjunan 
(Sl.No.48) and J.Praveen (Sl.No.59) provisionally selected 
candidates for the post of Civil Judge and appointed in the 
Government Order second cited, by direct recruitment, but 
have not joined their duty as they have taken alternative 
employment. He has further stated that when the above 
NOTIFICATIONS BY GOVERNMENT
PRINTED AND PUBLISHED BY THE DIRECTOR OF STATIONERY AND PRINTING, CHENNAI 
ON BEHALF OF THE GOVERNMENT OF TAMIL NADU
intimations were placed before the Hon'ble Selection and 
Appointment Committee, the Registry of High Court of 
Madras, has been directed to inform the Government of 
Tamil Nadu and the Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission for 
appropriate action. 
2. Further the Registrar (Recruitment), High Court of 
Madras, in his letter fourth read above, has stated that one 
Thiru. S.Balaji has informed that he was appointed to act 
temporarily as Civil Judge in the Tamil Nadu State Judicial 
Service in the Government Order 2nd read above and owing 
to his personal reasons, he has decided to resign from the 
said post and prayed to crave leave from the High Court of 
Madras and requested to treat it as his resignation letter, which 
was also placed before Hon'ble Selection and Appointment 
Committee and the Hon'ble Committee considered his 
request and accepted the same and further directed to inform 
Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission and the Government 
of Tamil Nadu about the decision of Thiru. S. Balaji (SI. No.10), 
Chennai. 
3. Further the Registrar (Recruitment) in his D.O.Letter 
ﬁ fth read above, has stated among others, that while arriving 
the estimated vacancies of 176 posts as on 6-6-2019, the 
abovesaid 3 unﬁ lled vacancies of Tvl. G.Arjunan (SI.No48), 
J.Praveen (SI.No.59) and S. Balaji (SI.No.10) who have not 
joined duty, was included. The Secretary, Tamil Nadu Public 
Service Commission, Chennai, in his letter seventh read above, 
has also accorded its concurrence for the removal of the names 
of the candidates viz., Tvl. S.Balaji (SI-No 10), G.Arjunan 
(SI.No.48) and J.Praveen (SI.No.59) and requested to issue 
formal orders removing of their names from the approved list 
of candidates selected provisionally for appointment by direct 
recruitment to the post of Civil Judge in the Tamil Nadu State 
Judicial Service for the years 2014-2015 to 2016-2017. 
4. The Government, after careful examination, have 
decided to accept the request of the Secretary, Tamil Nadu 
Public Service Commission, Chennai. Accordingly, the 
Government hereby remove the names of the candidates viz., 
Tvl. S.Balaji (SI.No.10), G.Arjunan (SI.No.48) and J.Praveen 
(SI.No.59), from the list of approved candidates selected 
provisionally for appointment by direct recruitment to the post 
of Civil Judge in the Tamil Nadu State Judicial Service for the 
years 2014-2015 to 2016-2017, issued in G.O.4D.No.56, Home 
Department, dated 28-12-2018.
(By order of the Governor.)
