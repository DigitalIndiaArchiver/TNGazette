NOTICE UNDER SECTION 15(1) OF TAMIL NADU HIGHWAYS ACT, 2001(TN ACT 34 OF 2002) READ WITH THE 
TAMIL NADU LAND ACQUISITION LAWS (REVIVAL OF OPERATION, AMENDMENT AND VALIDATION) ACT, 2019.
No. VI(1)/197(c-1)/2021.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that the lands specified in the schedule below 
are acquired under Chennai – Kanniyakumari Industrial Corridor Project for Highways purpose of formation of 
Bypass in SH-95 Mohanur - Namakkal - Senthamangalam - Rasipuram Road from KM 45.600 to KM 46.800 at 
5. Belukuruchi (Bypass) Village in Senthamangalam Taluk, Namakkal District, and it having already been decided that 
the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands to be paid out of the funds controlled and managed 
by the Divisional Engineer(H), Salem (CKICP), Tamil Nadu after having considering the cause shown by the owner or 
other persons having interest on such lands as the case may be do hereby publish the following notice is issued under 
sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (TN Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
 
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) read 
with the Tamil Nadu Land Acquisition Laws (Revival of Operation, Amendment and Validation) Act 2019, the Governor 
of Tamil Nadu hereby notiﬁ ed that the Dry and S.Manai lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below and measuring to an 
extent of 18906.5 sq.mtr. are acquired in 5. Belukuruchi (Bypass) Village in Senthamangalam Taluk, Namakkal District, 
to the same, a little more or less is needed for Chennai-Kanniyakumari Industrial Corridor Project for State Highways 
purpose of formation of Bypass in SH-95 Mohanur - Namakkal - Senthamangalam - Rasipuram Road from KM 45.600 
to KM 46.800. 
The plan of the lands under acquisition are kept in the Oﬃ  ce of the Special District Revenue Oﬃ  cer (Land 
Acquisition), Chennai - Kanniyakumari Industrial Corridor Project, Salem and oﬃ  ce of the Special Tahsildar (Land Acquisition), 
Chennai – Kanniyakumari Industrial Corridor Project, Namakkal and may be inspected at any time during the oﬃ  ce hours.
