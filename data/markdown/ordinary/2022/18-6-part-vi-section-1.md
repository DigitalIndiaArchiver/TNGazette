Variation to the Approved Master Plan for the Madurai Local Planning Area.
(Roc. No. 413/2021/ Mathi 2)
No. VI(1)/192/2022.
In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (4) of Section 32 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning 
Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following variations to the approved 
Master Plan of the Madurai Local Planning Area under the said Act and issued in G.O. Ms. No.122 Housing and Urban 
Development UD4, Department, dated 06.02.1995 and published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No. 7, Part II-Section 2,
Page 190-191 dated 22.02.1995.
Variation
In the approved Madurai Master Plan under the heading permitted land use in various S.F.No. of Madurai Local Planning 
Area, Madurai District, Madurai North Taluk. Village No. 61 page No.163 in Kulamangalam Panchayat, Madurai West Panchayat 
Union, Kulamangalam Bit I Village, S.F.Nos. 139/2A, 139/2B & 139/3A the following entries should be made.
Under the heading 1. Residential use zone the following Kulamangalam Bit I Village S.F.Nos. 139/2A, 139/2B & 139/3A shall 
be added.
Under the heading 1. Residential use zone the following Kulamangalam Bit I Village S.F.Nos. 139/2A, 139/2B & 139/3A 
shall be deleted.
Errata Variation
	
In the approved Madurai master Plan under the heading permitted land use in various S.F.No. of Madurai Local Planning 
Area, Madurai District, Madurai North Taluk. Village No. 61 page No.163 in Kulamangalam Panchayat, Madurai West Panchayat 
Union, Kulamangalam Bit I Village, S.F.Nos. 139/2A, 139/2B & 139/3A the following entries should be made.
	
Under the heading VI. Agriculture use zone the following Kulamangalam Bit I Village S.F.Nos. 139/2A, 139/2B & 139/3A shall
