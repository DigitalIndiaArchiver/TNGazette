In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (bb) of sub-section (2) of Section 122 read with clause 
(45-A) of Section 2 and section 35-A of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 
of 1972), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Tamil Nadu Transfer of Development 
Rights Rules, 2019, namely :-
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖග.
In the said Rules, after rule 10, the following rule shall be added, namely:-
 
“11. Savings.- (1) Development Right Certiﬁ cates issued under the erstwhile Development Regulations for Chennai 
Metropolitan Area or the Tamil Nadu Combined Development and Building Rules, 2019 shall be deemed to have been 
issued under these Rules; 
 
 
(2) The value of the Development Right Certiﬁ cates in sub-rule(1) shall be calculated as per the formula 
hereunder, subject to the following conditions, namely:-
“Development Right Certiﬁ cate Value = Floor Space Index  credit x (1/Permissible Floor Space Index  of 
Generating site) x Guide Line Value of Utilizing Site
(a) 
The above value shall be applicable only for partially utilized or un-utilized Development Right Certiﬁ cates; 
(b) 
For utilization of Transfer of Development Rights, the Guide Line Value at the time of issue of the Development 
Right Certiﬁ cate or at the time of utilization, whichever is higher, shall be applicable;
(c) 
The Development Right Certiﬁ cate holders shall exercise option for utilizing the Development Right Certiﬁ cate 
in terms of Floor Space Index credit or in terms of money value.”
