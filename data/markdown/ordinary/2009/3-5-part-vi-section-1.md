Constitution of Separate District Munsif Court at Coonoor
(Roc. No. 1897/2008/G/Judn.)
No. VI(1)/32/2009.
In modificaton of the Notification No. VI(1)/852/81 to No. VI(1)/855/81 published at pages 1 and 2 under
Part VI-Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated 8th July 1981, the following Notifications are issued in
pursuance of G.O. Ms. No. 1108, Home (Cts. III) Department, dated 27th August 2008 for constitution of exclusive
separate District Munsif Court, Coonoor.
NOTIFICATION-I
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873),
the High Court, Madras, hereby fixes Coonoor in Nilgiris District as the place at which the District Munsif Court, Coonoor,
shall be held.
NOTIFICATION-II
(Roc. No. 1897/2008/G/Judn.)
No. VI(1)/33/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 11 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873),
the High Court, Madras, hereby directs that in Nilgiris District, the District Munsif Court, Coonoor, shall have and exercise
local jurisdiction over the entire Revenue Taluk of Coonoor with effect from the date on which the District Munsif, Coonoor
assumes charge of that court.

NOTIFICATION-III
(Roc. No. 1897/2008/G/Judn.)
No. VI(1)/34/2009.
The High Court, Madras hereby directs and notifies that the District Munsif, Coonoor in Nilgiris District shall exercise
all the powers conferred on a District Munsif under Section 12 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Court Act, 1873 (Central Act III
of 1873) as amended up-to-date in regard to original suits and proceedings not otherwise exempted from his cognizance
of which the amount or value of the subject matter does not exceed one Lakh Rupees within the local limits of his
jurisdiction with effect from the date on which the District Munsif, Coonoor assumes charge of that court.
NOTIFICATION-IV
(Roc. No. 1897/2008/G/Judn.)
No. VI(1)/35/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 28 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Court Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873)
the High Court, Madras, hereby directs that the District Munsif Court, Coonoor, shall have and exercise jurisdiction of a
Court of Small Causes under the provincial Small Causes Court  Act, 1887 for the trial suits cognizable by a court of
small cuases upto the pecuniary limits of Rs. 5,000 (Rupees five thousand only) with effect from the date on which the
District Munsif, Coonoor assumes charge of that court.
Change of Nomenclature of the Court at Coonoor
(Roc. No. 1897/2008/G/Judn.)
No. VI(1)/36/2009.
Whereas the Government of Tamil Nadu have issued orders in G.O. Ms. No. 1108, Home (Cts. III) Department,
dated 27th August 2008 that a separate District Munsif Court, Coonoor will be constituted at Coonoor and the existing
district Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Coonoor be converted as exclusive Judicial Magistrate Court, Coonoor.
Hence, it is hereby informed that on and from the date on which the District Munsif Court, Coonoor is constituted,
the nomenclature of the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Coonoor shall be changed as the Judicial
Magistrate Court, Coonoor.
