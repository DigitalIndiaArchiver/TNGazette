GENERAL  NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI.
Appointment of Executive Officer for Jumma Pallivasal Kallakuruchi, Villupuram District.
(Rc. No. 8878/07/VPM/B4.)
No. VI-3(a)/106/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions of Section 25 of the Wakf Act 1995, and as per
the Special Resolution No. 5, dated 14th June 1990, the Chief Executive Officer, Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in
Proceedings No. 8878/07/Vpm/B4, dated 23rd July 2012 has appointed the Superintendent of Wakfs/Cuddalore
as the Executive Officer of the Wakf Jumma Pallivasal, Kalakuruchi, Villupuram District to look after the affairs of the
Wakf for a period of one year from the date of the order. Accordingly the Superintendent of
Wakfs / Cuddalore / Executive Officer has taken over the charges of the Wakf and its properties on the
Fore Noon of 27th July 2012.
Chennai-600 001,
F. ABDUL RAZICK,
6th August 2012.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
