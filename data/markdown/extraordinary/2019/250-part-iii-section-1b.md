In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 read with Article 313 of the Constitution, the Governor 
of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Tamil Nadu Travelling Allowance Rules.
2. The amendment hereby made shall come into force on and from the 10th day of June, 2019.
