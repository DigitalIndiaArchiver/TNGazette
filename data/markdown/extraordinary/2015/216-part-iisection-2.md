The Government of Tamil Nadu, having been satisfied that the lands specified in the schedule below have to be acquired for
Industrial purpose, to wit, for the Extension of runway at Coimbatore Airport and it having already been decided that the entire
amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands to be paid out of the funds controlled or managed by Government.
The following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of land for Industrial Purposes Act,
1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of land for Industrial Purposes Act 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10
of 1999), the Government of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands specified in the schedule below and measuring 37477 Sq.mts.
(9.26 Acre) to the same, a little more or less needed for Industrial purpose, to wit, for the extension of runway at Coimbatore Airport.
The plan of the lands are kept in the office of the Special Tahsildar (Land Acquisition) extension of runway at Coimbatore Airport.
Unit-III,  Block – VI,  Village No. 28, Uppillipalayam Village, Coimbatore South Taluk and may be inspected at any time during
office hours.
