AMENDMENTS TO THE TAMIL NADU REGISTRATION OF MARRIAGES RULES, 2009.
[G.O. Ms. No. 758, Home (Courts-IV), 26th August 2010.]
No. SRO A-31(a)/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of  Section 24 of the Tamil Nadu Registration of Marriages Act
(Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 2009), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following Amendments to the Tamil Nadu Registration
of Marriage Rules, 2009.
2. The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force with effect on and from the 26th August 2010.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules,
(i) Rule 5, for sub-rule(1),the following sub-rule shall  be substituted, namely:—
“(1)
(a) Every memorandum for Registration of marriage shall be in Form-I or Form – IA (in the case of persons
whose marriage was solemnized under Muslims Personal Law) and it shall be delivered in person or sent to the Registrar along
with an application in Form –II within ninety days from the date of marriage.  Form I and Form – IA shall be supplied free of cost
to the parties to a marriage by the Registrar.
(b) An Acknowledgement for having received the memorandum for registration of marriage in Form-I or
Form – IA alongwith the application in Form-II shall be given by the Registrar; and
