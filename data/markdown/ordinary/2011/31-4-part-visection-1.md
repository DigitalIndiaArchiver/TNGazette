GENERAL NOTIFICATION
Confirmation of Variation to the Approved Gowripuram Area Detailed Development Plan of
Karur Local Planning Area.
(Roc. No. 1723/2009-DP3.)
No. VI(1)/325/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 33(2) of Town and Country Planning Act 1971, the Director of
Town and Country Planning, Chennai hereby confirms the following variation "Conversion of Public purpose into Residential
use in the approved layout DDP (CN) No. 8/80 in Ward No. 3, Block No. 8, T.S. No. 343 part, to the approved
Gowripuram  Area Detailed Development Plan of Karur Local Planning Area approved by the Director of Town and
Country Planning in proceedings Roc. No. 5343/80D1, dated 1st October 1981 and Form No.12 published in
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Part VI—Section 1, Page No. 520, dated 11th November 1981 and the said draft
notification published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette  No. 43, Part VI—Section 1, Page No. 1415, dated 5th
November 1997 in Publication No.VI(1)/1403/1997.
Since no objections and suggestions have been received on this draft notification within the stipulated time, the
same are hereby confirmed and ordered as below:
CONFIRMATION OF VARIATIONS
1. Wherever the expression "Map No.5 DDP(CN)No.6/81" occurs the expression "DDP(V)DTCP No.10/97" shall be
added at the end and to be read with.
2. In Schedule-IV (Form No.7) all entries in Serial No. 8 shall be deleted.
3. The above variation is made enforceable from the date of publication of this notification in the Tamil Nadu
Government Gazette.
Chennai,
A. KARTHIK,
28th July 2011.
Director of Town and Country Planning.

Variations to the Approved Master Plan for Chennai Metropolitan Area of Chennai Metropolitan Development Authority,
Kottur Village, Chennai District.
(Lr. No. R2/18720/2010.)
No. VI(1)/327/2011.
In exercise of the powers delegated by the Government of Tamil Nadu in G.O.Ms. No. 419, Housing and Urban
Development Department, dated 1st June 1984 under Section 91(1) of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning Act,
1971, the Member-Secretary, Chennai Metropolitan Development Authority hereby makes the following variation under
sub-section (4) of section 32 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972)
to the Gandhi Nagar Area D.D.P. approved in G.O.Ms. No. 1230, Housing and Urban Development Department, dated
22nd September 1980 and published as Notification in Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
VARIATION
In the said D.D.P. in Clause 2(a) 3(xi) and 9(d) after the expression Map No. 4 D.D.P(S)/MMDA No. 7/79, the
expression and Map P.P.D./D.D.P(V) No. 8/2011 shall be added.
In form 6:
In Column No. (2) under the heading "PRIMARY RESIDENTIAL USE ZONE" and under the sub-heading of "Block
No. 39, Kottur Village" under whole T.S.No. the expression '9/1 & 9/2' shall be deleted.  In Column No. (4) an extent
of "849 sq.m." shall be deducted from the total extent.
In Column No. (2) under the heading "COMMERCIAL USE ZONE" and under the sub-heading of "Block No. 39,
Kottur Village", the following shall be added.
Block No. 39, Kottur Village:
T.S.No. 9/1 & 9/2
849 Sq.m.
Commercial Use Zone
Vacant
EXPLANATORY NOTE
(This is not part of variation. It intends to bring out the purport)
T.S.No. 9/1 & 9/2, Block No. 39 of Kottur Village, Chennai District classified as "Primary Residential Use
Zone" is now reclassified as "Commercial Use Zone" as per this notification.
