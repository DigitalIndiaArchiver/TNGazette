RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND PANCHAYAT RAJ DEPARTMENT
AMENDMENTS TO ESTABLISHMENT OF THE TAMIL NADU STATE EMPLOYMENT GUARANTEE FUND FOR
THE PURPOSES OF IMPLEMENTATION OF THE TAMIL NADU RURAL EMPLOYMENT GUARANTEE
SCHEME UNDER NATIONAL RURAL EMPLOYMENT GUARANTEE ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 99, Rural Development and Panchayat Raj (CGS.1), 25th October 2010.]
No. II(2)/RDPR/644(f)/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred by  sub-section (1) of Section 21 of the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural
Employment Guarantee Act, 2005 (Central Act 42 of 2005), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the
following amendments to the Rural Development Department notification No. II(2)/RUL/79(c)/2006, published in
Part II—Section-2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 3rd February 2006:—
AMENDMENTS.
In the said Notification,—
(1) for the expression “Tamil Nadu State Employment Guarantee Fund”,  the expression “State Employment
Guarantee Fund—Tamil Nadu” shall be substituted;
(2) for the expression “Tamil Nadu Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme”,  the expression “Mahatma
Gandhi National Rural Employment  Guarantee Scheme, Tamil Nadu” shall be substituted.
K. ALLAUDIN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
