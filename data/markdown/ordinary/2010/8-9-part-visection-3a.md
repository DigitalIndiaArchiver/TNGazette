GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD
No. 1, Jaffar Syrang Street, Vallal Seethakathi Nagar, Chennai-600 001
Extension of Direct Management of Mohamediapuram Pallivasal, Dindigul.
(Lr. Rc. No. 9032/01/B15/DGL)
No.VI-3(a)/20/2010.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995, the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board
in its resolution in item No. 9/088, in Rc.No. 9032/01//B5/DGL, dated 19th January 2010 has ordered to extend the period of
direct management of Mohamediapuram Pallivasal, Dindigul which is under the direct control of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board
for a further period of one year from the date of Board’s order.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
9th February 2010.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
