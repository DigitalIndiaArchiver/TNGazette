APPOINTMENT OF VACATION CIVIL JUDGES FOR THE SUMMER VACATION OF CIVIL COURTS
FOR THE YEAR, 2011 UNDER THE TAMIL NADU CIVIL COURTS ACT.
[G.O. (D) No. 420, Home (Courts-III), 29th April 2011.]
