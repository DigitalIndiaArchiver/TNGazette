Variations to the Approved Master Plan, for Tiruchirappalli Local Planning Area.
(Roc. No.635/2010 TLPA-2)
No.VI(1)/62/2014.—
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section 2 of Section 32 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning Act, 1971
(Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972) and in exercise of powers conferred by the G.O.Ms.No.143, Housing and Urban
Development UD 4(1) Department dated 11-8-2009 which has been published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No.34,
Part II—Section 2, Page No.379, dated 2nd September 2009 the following Variations are made to the Review Master Plan for
Tiruchirappalli Local Planning Area.
VARIATIONS
In the said Master Plan in the ‘LAND USE SCHEDULE’ under the heading No. 51 PANAYAKURICHI.
(i) against the entry, the expression General Industrial use S.F.No.30/1A and 41/3.
(ii) against the entry, for the expression Agricultural use zone S.F.Nos.19 to 30, Changed as 19 to 29, 30 Part and
S.F.Nos.40 to 43 changed as 40, 41 Part, 42 and 43.
P. MUTHAIYAN,
Tiruchirappalli,
Member-Secretary,
29th January 2014.
Tiruchirappalli Local Planning Authority.
Variations to the Approved Master Plan for the Coimbatore Local Planning Area.
(Roc. No.732/2013 LPA-2)
No.VI(1)/63/2014.—
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 32 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning Act, 1971
(Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972) and in exercise of powers conferred by the G.O.Ms.No.94, Housing and Urban
Development UD 4(1) Department dated 12-6-2009 which has been Published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No.27,
Part II—Section 2, Page No.228, dated 15-7-2009 the following variations are made to the Master plan for the Coimbatore Local
Planning Area approved under the said Act and published in the Housing and Urban Development Department Notification
No.II(2)/HOU/4377/94 at page 1078 of Part II section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 9th November 1994.
VARIATIONS
In the said Master Plan, in the “LAND USE SCHEDULE” under the heading “COIMBATORE LOCAL PLANNING AREA
(OTHER THAN COIMBATORE CORPORATION AREA)” under the sub heading (b) Notified Detailed Development
Plan Area, (c) PALLADAM TALUK, No: 73, KANIYUR VILLAGE.
(i)
Against the entry “AGRICULTURAL (AG-45)” for the expression “(504 to 522)” the expression “504 to 522
(except 511/2B, 512/2A1, 2A3, 2B, 513/1A, 2A, 2B, 2C2A, 515/2B)” Shall be substituted.
(ii)
Against the entry “RESIDENTIAL (MR-20)” 511/2B, 512/2A1, 2A3, 2B, 513/1A, 2A, 2B, 2C2A, 515/2B
Shall be added after the entry 503.
S. CHANDRASEKARAN,
Coimbatore,
Member-Secretary (In-charge),
31st January 2014.
Coimbatore Local Planning Authority.
