No. II(2)/TCRE/B/2015.
The following amendment is issued to the Notification No. II(2)/TCRE/391(a)/2014 published in
Part II—Section 2 of the  Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated 25th June 2014.
AMENDMENT
Under Section 19(2)(b) (Class II – other members) of the  Tamil Nadu Music and Fine Arts University Act, 2013
(Tamil Nadu Act 30 of 2013), Tmt. Krishnakumari Narendran, Director of Abhinaya Natyalaya, Chennai-18 is
nominated as a member in the place of Thiru U. Srinivas, Mandolin Maestro in the Syndicate of the Tamil Nadu
Music and Fine Arts University for the left over period issued in G.O.(Ms) No.105, Tourism, Culture and
Religious Endowments Department, dated 25th June 2014.
R. KANNAN,
Additional Chief Secretary to Government.
