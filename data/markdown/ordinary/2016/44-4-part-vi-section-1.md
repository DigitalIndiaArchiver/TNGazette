Ind.No. 778. The Coimbatore District Taxi Drivers Industrial Co-operative Society Ltd.
(L.F. 848/ICA/1992)
No.VI(1)/348/2016.

Industries Center Coimbatore / Official Liquidator of the Ind. No. 778.Coimbatore District Taxi Drivers Industrial
Co-operative Society Ltd., in the final closure proposals dated 29-9-2016 and in exercise of the powers delegated under

Industrial Co-operative Society Ltd. Ind. No. 778 has been ordered to be cancelled and the affairs have been finally closed
with effect from 17-10-2016 vide the Proceedings No.LF.848/ICA/1992 dated 17-10-2016 of Additional Chief
Secretary/Industries Commissioner and Director of Industries and Commerce and Registrar of Industrial Co-operatives,
Chennai-32”.

JUDICIAL NOTIFICATIONS
Constitution of a Judicial Magistrate Court at Kallakurichi by Converting the existing III Additional District
Munsif Court, Kallakurichi in Villupuram District.
(Roc. No. 2259/2007/G/Judn)
No.VI(1)/353/2016.
Whereas the Government of Tamil Nadu have issued orders in G.O.(Ms).No.981, Home (Cts.II) Department, dated
29-12-2015 that a Judicial Magistrate Court be constituted at Kallakurichi by converting the existing III Additional District
Munsif Court at Kallakurichi.
Hence, it is hereby informed that on and from the date on which the III Additional District Munsif Court is converted as
Judicial Magistrate Court, Kallakurichi, the nomenclature of the following courts is changed as follows:—

-
Judicial Magistrate Court No.I, Kallakurichi.
Kallakurichi.

Proposed Judicial Magistrate Court,
-
Judicial Magistrate Court No.II, Kallakurichi.
