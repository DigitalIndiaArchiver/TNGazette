 NOTIFICATIONS BY THE ELECTION COMMISSION OF INDIA
APPOINTMENT OF RETURNING OFFICER FOR PRESIDENTIAL ELECTION
No. SRO G-18/2012.
The following Notification of the Election Commission of India, Nirvachan Sadan, Ashoka Road,
New Delhi-110 001, dated 14th June, 2012 [24 Jyaistha, 1934 (Saka)] is republished:—
479/3/2012(I).—In  pursuance of sub-section (1) of section 3 of the Presidential and
Vice-Presidential Elections Act, 1952 (31 of 1952), the Election Commission of India, in consultation with the
Central Government, hereby appoints the Secretary General to the Rajya Sabha, Parliament House,
New Delhi, to be the Returning Officer for the Presidential Election, 2012.
(By Order)
K.N. BHAR,
Secretary,
Election Commission of India.
