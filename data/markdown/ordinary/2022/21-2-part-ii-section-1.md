No.II(1)/AGFW/31/2022.—In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (d) of sub-section (1) of Section 9 of the 
Tamil Nadu Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) Act, 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 1989), the Governor of 
Tamil Nadu hereby conﬁ rms the regulation of marketing of the Agricultural Produces mentioned in Annexure-I to the Notiﬁ cation 
in the notiﬁ ed market areas of the Regulated Markets mentioned in Annexure II to the Notiﬁ cation. The intention to 
make such a declaration having been previously published with Agriculture Department Notiﬁ cation No.II(1)/AG/60/2008. 
Publised at paged 108-112 of Part II—Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 24th December 2008, as 
required by Section 3 of the said Act.
