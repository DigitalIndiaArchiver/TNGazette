Order: No.600, Revenue and Disaster Management,  27th October 2020
Whereas on considering the recommendations of the expert team of Doctors and Public Health specialists and based 
on the directives of Government of India, Ministry of Home Aﬀ airs, State-wide lockdown was extended from time to time 
and lastly extended till 24:00 Hrs of 31-10-2020 under the Disaster Management Act, 2005 with certain restrictions and 
many relaxations along with guidelines vide G.O.(Ms)No.541, Revenue and Disaster Management [DM-IV] Department, 
dated 30-09-2020.
2.  Whereas the Additional Chief Secretary/ Commissioner of Revenue Administration in the letter seventh read above 
has sent the proposal for amendment to the G.O fourth read above.
3.  Now Therefore the State Government of Tamil Nadu based on the proposal of Additional Chief Secretary/Commissioner 
of Revenue Administration issue following amendments to G.O.(Ms)No.447, Revenue and Disaster Management [D.M-IV] 
Department, dated 31.08.2020.  The following content in Section (i) and Section (xv) under Caption II – (Under Permitted 
Activities) may be read as:- 
i.    Movement of General Public is permitted throughout the State of Tamil Nadu (between the districts) without E-Pass. 
However, the persons who are coming from foreign countries and from other States to Tamil Nadu by Air, Rail and 
other Vehicles should register their details viz., Aadhar/Ration Card, Travel Ticket, Telephone/Mobile Numbers in 
Tamil Nadu E-registration portal and proceed with their travel with an e-Registration. 
XV. Travelling to Nilgiris District and all other hill stations like Kodaikannal and Yercaud, tourists should register their 
details viz., Aadhar/Ration Card, Travel Ticket, Telephone/Mobile Numbers in Tamil Nadu E-Registration portal 
and proceed with their travel with an e-Registration.
