No. II(2)/HOU/207(e-1)/2014.
Whereas  it appears to the Government of Tamil Nadu
that the lands specified below and situated in No. 2, Vandalur
Village, Block-1 Chengalpattu Taluk, Kanchipuram District
are needed for a public purpose to reduce the traffic
congestion and to meet out the growing transportation needs
in Chennai Mofussil Bus Terminus at Koyambedu and also
considering the movement and operation of the public
transports, half of them are operated from Koyambedu
bounded to Southern Districts,  and for the early speedy and
comfortable journey for the passengers  has been proposed
to develop a Moffusil Bus Terminus at Vandalur, notice to
that effect is hereby given to all to whom it may concern in
accordance with the provisions of sub section (1-A) of Section
4 of Land Acquisition Act, 1894 (Central Act 1 of 1894).
Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by sub
section (2) of Section 4 of the said Act, the Governor of Tamil
Nadu hereby authorise the Special Tahsildar (LA), Vandalur
Moffusil Bus Terminus, Chennai Metropolitan Development
Authority @ Koyambedu,Chennai-92, his staff and workmen to
exercise the powers conferred by the said sub-section and
under Clause (C) of Section 3 of the said Act, the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the Special Tahsildar (LA), Vandalur
Moffusil Bus Terminus, Chennai Metropolitan Development
Authority @ Koyambedu, Chennai-92, to perform the functions
under Section 5-A of the said Act.
