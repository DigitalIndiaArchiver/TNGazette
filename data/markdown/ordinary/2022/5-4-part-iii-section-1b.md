Amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Medical Subordinate Services
[G.O. Ms. No. 5, Health and Family Welfare (C1), 4th January 2022,  
Margazhi 20, Pilava, Thiruvalluvar Aandu–2052.]
No. SRO B-6/2022.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the constitution of India, the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Medical Subordinate 
Service (Section 21 in Volume-III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1970).
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on and from 1st March 2019.
Amendment
In the said Rules, in Part II, under the heading Branch I Medical, in the Annexure II in the tabular column, under the 
sub-heading, "Class I" in column (1), for the expression, "A diploma in Pharmacy", in column (2), against the expression 
"Category 2 Pharmacists" in column (1), thereof, the expression "(I) A diploma in Pharmacy or Bachelor of Pharmacy or 
Pharm. D", shall be substituted.
