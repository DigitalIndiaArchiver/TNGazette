No. II(2)/CTR/360(e-2)/2020. 
Under sub-section (1) of section 5 of the Registration Act, 1908 (Central Act XVI of 1908), the Governor of Tamil Nadu 
hereby, with effect on and from 01-06-2020 alters the limits of the Registration Sub-District of Vilaathikulam in the Registration 
District of Tuticorin by transferring the villages Goundanpatti, Sengottai, Pattithevanpatti, Vowalthothi, Nagalapuram, Melakalloorani 
and K.Duraisamypuram which form part of the Registration Sub-District of Vilaathikulam in the Registration District of Tuticorin 
to the Registration Sub-District of Puthur in the Registration District of Tuticorin.
