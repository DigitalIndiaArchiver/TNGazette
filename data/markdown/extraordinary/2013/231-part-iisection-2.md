NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
HOME DEPARTMENT
(Courts-V)
EXEMPTED THE RESERVATION FOR PERSONS WITH DISABILITIES TO APPOINTMENT
FOR THE POST OF JUDICIAL OFFICERS.
[G.O. Ms. No. 589, Home (Courts-V), 13th August 2013.]
No. II(2)/HO/585(b)/2013.
In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Section 33 of the Persons with Disabilities
(Equal Opportunities, Protection of Rights and Full Participation) Act, 1995 (Central Act 1 of 1996),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby exempts the Posts in the Madras High Court Service from the provisions
of said Section 33 subject to the condition that three per cent of the vacancies in the said service to be
filled up by direct recruitment shall be reserved for Orthopaedically disabled persons.
NIRANJAN MARDI,
Principal Secretary to Government.
