No. SRO A-14/2013.—In exercise of the Powers conferred by sub-section (1) and (2) of Section 180 of the
Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act, 1983 (Tamil Nadu Act 30 of 1983), the Governor of Tamil Nadu  hereby makes
the following amendment to the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Rules 1988:—
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules in rule 85,  in the first proviso to sub-rule (2) for clause (iii) the following shall be substituted,
namely:—
"(iii) rupees one lakh fifty thousand in the case of an apex society, unless, the prior sanction of the Registrar is
obtained."
M.P. NIRMALA,
Secretary to Government.
