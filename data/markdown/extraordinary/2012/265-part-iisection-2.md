Amendment to Notification
No. II(2)/PUBG/615(b)/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) Section 3 of the Commissions of Inquiry Act, 1952
(Central Act LX of 1952), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Public
(Buildings) Department's Notification No. II(2)/PUBG/286(c)/2011, dated the 22nd June 2011, published at
pages 1-2 of Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 22nd June 2011.
AMENDMENT
In the said Notification, in paragraph 4, for the expression "fifteen months", the expression "eighteen months"
shall be substituted.
DEBENDRANATH SARANGI,
Chief Secretary.
