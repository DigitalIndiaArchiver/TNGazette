No. SRO B-45/2015.— In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Adi Dravidar Welfare
Service (Section 40 in Volume II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1969).
2. The amendments hereby made shall come into force on and from the date of publication of this Notification.
AMENDMENT
In the said Special Rules, in rule 3,-
(1) in item (i), for the expression “the Account Test for Executive Officers; and”, the expression “the Account Test for
Executive Officers” shall be substituted;
(2) item (ii) shall be omitted.
P. SIVASANKARAN,
Principal Secretary to Government (FAC).

No. SRO B-46/2015.— In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the rules published with the Health and Family Planning
Department Notification No. SRO B-17/2012, at Page 34 of Part III—Section 1(b) of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette,
dated the 27th June 2012.
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st April 1989.
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in rule 1, for the expression “Regional Family Planning Training Centres at Salem and Egmore”, the
expression “Health and Family Welfare Training Centres under the Control of the Public Health and Preventive Medicine
Department” shall be substituted.
J. RADHAKRISHNAN,
Secretary to Government.

No. SRO B-47/2015.— In exercise of powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the rules for Class XXXVII in the Special Rules for the
Madras General Services (Section 16 in Volume II of the Tamil Nadu Service Manual, 1969).
AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules,
(i) in Part I, the expression “Class XXXVII - Personal Assistants to the Director of Industries and Commerce” shall be
omitted;
(ii) in Part II, the heading “Class XXXVII - Personal Assistants to the Director of Industries and Commerce and the rules
thereunder shall be omitted”.
KUMAR JAYANT,
Secretary to Government.
