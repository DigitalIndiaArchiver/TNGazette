Winding up the affairs of Industrial Co-operative Societies and appointment of Official Liquidator.
T.T. 12. Thirumalai Mill Employees Co-operative Thrift and Credit Society.
(Rc. No. 242/2006 Pu.Vi.)
No. VI(1)/145/2009.
As per Rc. 242/2006 Pu.Vi., dated 23rd July 2008 the Deputy Registrar of Co-operative Societies, Tirunelveli has
orderd to wind up the affairs of the T.T. 12. Thirumalai Mill Employees Co-operative Thrift and Credit Society under
Section 137(2)(b) of the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act, 1983 and the Co-operative Sub-Registrar (Stores),
Office of the Deputy Registrar of Co-operative Societies, Tirunelveli-7 has been appointed as the official liquidator under
Section 138 (1) of the said Act.
Tirunelveli-7,
N. SUGUMAR,
23rd March 2009.
Deputy Registrar.
No. IND. 937. The Sultan-E-Hind Backward Class Transport Drivers Industrial
 Co-operative Society Limited, Chennai
(Rc. No. 16936/ICF/2006-2)
No. VI(1)/146/2009.
Under the powers conferred on the Registrar of Industrial Co-operatives under Section 137(1) of the Tamil Nadu
Co-operative Societies Act, 1983 (Act 30 of 1983). The Activities of the Sultan-E-Hind Backward Class Transport Drivers
Industrial Co-operative Society Limited No. Ind. 937/Chennai have been ordered to be wound up with immediate effect
by the Industries Commissioner and Director of Industries and Commerce and Registrar of Industrial Co-operative
Societies, Chepauk, Chennai-5 vide proceedings Rc. No. 16936/ICF/2006-1, dated 25th March 2009.
Under Section 138(1) of the same Act, Assistant Director (Industrial Co-operatives)/Liquidator, TAICO Bank,
Chennai-8 is appointed as Official Liquidator of the said Society for the purpose of Liquidation.
Chepauk, Chennai-5,
G. SANTHANAM,
3rd April 2009.
Industries Commissioner and
Director of Industries and Commerce.
