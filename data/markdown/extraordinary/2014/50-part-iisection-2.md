DTP—II-2 Ex. (50)
COMMERCIAL  TAXES  AND  REGISTRATION  DEPARTMENT
EXEMPTION FROM PAYMENT OF ENTRY TAX TO THE TAIPEI ECONOMIC AND CULTURAL
CENTRE (TECC ) IN CHENNAI UNDER THE TAMIL NADU TAX ON ENTRY OF MOTOR VEHICLES INTO
LOCAL AREAS ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 26, Commercial Taxes and Registration (C2), 28th February 2014
Maasi 16, Vijaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2045.]
No. II(2)/CTR/238(f)/2014.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 12 of the Tamil Nadu Tax on Entry of Motor Vehicles into
Local Areas Act, 1990 (Tamil Nadu Act 13 of 1990), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, having considered it necessary
to do so in the public interest, hereby exempts any importer from payment of the whole of the tax payable under
said Act in respect of entry of cars and two wheelers into any local area for sale thereon to Thiruvalar Taipei
Economic and Cultural Centre in Chennai and its home-based officials in Tamil Nadu for their official and personal
use under reciprocal arrangements with India.
2. This Notification shall come into force with effect on and from 28th February 2014.
S.K. PRABAKAR,
Principal Secretary to Government.
