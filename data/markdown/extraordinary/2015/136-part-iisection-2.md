MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION AND WATER SUPPLY DEPARTMENT
DRAFT VARIATION LIST OF PARK, PLAY- FIELDS AND OPEN SPACE REGULATION LAND AT TIRUVARUR MUNICIPALITY
UNDER THE TAMIL NADU PARKS, PLAY - FIELDS AND OPEN SPACES (PRESERVATION AND REGULATION)
ACT, 1959
[G.O. Ms. No. 87, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (MA-5), 15th June 2015,
Vaikasi 32, Manmadha, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2046.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/303(b)/2015.
WHEREAS G.O.(Ms) No. 2072, Education and Public Health Department, dated 3.12.1965 a list of Parks, Play - Fields and
Open Spaces in the area within the Jurisdiction of the Tiruvarur Municipal Council was approved under sub-section (2) of
Section 4 of the Tamil Nadu Parks, Play- Fields and Open Spaces (Preservation and Regulation)  Act, 1959  (Tamil Nadu Act
26 of 1959) and the same was published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette. In the said list among others, an extent of
42560 sq.ft., in old T.S. No. 2756/3, (New T.S.No.23) was notified as Park.
2. AND WHEREAS, the Commissioner of Tiruvarur Municipality in his letter From the Commissioner, Tiruvarur Municipality,
Na.ka.No.9988/ 2013/F1, dated 22.08.2014 has stated that orders were issued in G.O. (Ms.) No.71, Tamil Development and
Information Department, dated 26.5.2014 sanctioning of a sum of Rs.1,10,00,000/- (Rupees one crore and ten lakhs only) for
Construction of “ManuneethiCholan Manimandapam” by the Public Works Department to an extent of 247.5 sq.m., out of
4115 sq.m. in Somasundaram Park areas in Tiruvarur Municipality and to hand over the site after the construction work is over
for maintenance of the Mandapam and the Park by the Municipality.  He has also requested the Government to sanction order
for enter upon the premises.
3.
AND WHEREAS, the Commissioner of Municipal Administration in his letter From the Commissioner of Municipal
Administration Na.ka.No. 44534/2014/TP4, dated 15.09.2014 has forwarded the proposal of the Commissioner, Tiruvarur
Municipality and has requested to issue variation to the approval of list Published under sub-section (2) of Section 4 of the said
Act in order to construct the “Manuneethi Cholan Manimandapam” and to sanction orders for enter upon permission to the Public
Works Department.

4.
AND WHEREAS, the Commissioner of Town and Country Planning in his letter From the Commissioner of Town and
Country Planning, Roc.No. 18862/2014/MP2, dated 24.12.2014 has stated that in that department has not given any approval to
delineate the park under reference through any Development Plan or layout plan as far as the records available with that
Department.  As per sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Parks and Play -Fields and Open Spaces (Preservation and
Regulation) Act 1959 (Tamil Nadu Act 26 of 1959), the  Government may at any time either suo-motu or at the instance of the
local authority concerned or of any person interested, vary or revoke a list published under Sections 4.  The Municipal Council
in its Resolution No.284, dated 02.12.2014 has resolved to request the Government that the approval for variation to the approval
of Parks and Play - Fields published vide G.O.(Ms.) No 2072, Education and Public Health Department dated 3.12.1965, under
sub-section (2) of Section 4 of the Tamil Nadu Parks, Play – Fields and Open Spaces (Preservation and Regulation) Act, 1959
(Tamil Nadu Act 26 of 1959).
5.
AND WHEREAS, the Government after careful examination on the recommendation of Commissioner of Municipal
Administration regarding the proposal Commissioner of Tiruvarur Municipality, have decided to accept the same.
6.
NOW THEREFORE, the following of the variation to the approved list of Parks and Play-Fields published in the
Fort St. George Gazette on 22nd December 1965, which is proposed to make in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section
(1) of section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Parks, Play- Fields and Open Spaces (Preservation and Regulation)  Act, 1959 (Tamil Nadu
Act 26 of 1959) is hereby published as required under sub-section (2) of said section.
7.
Notice is hereby given that objections or suggestions, if any, received in writing, in respect of the said draft variation to
the said list from any person interested, within seven days from the date of publication of this Notification in the Tamil Nadu
Government Gazette, will be taken into consideration by the Government of Tamil Nadu.
8.  Objections or suggestions, if any, should be addressed to the Principal Secretary to Government, Municipal Administration
and Water Supply Department, Secretariat, Fort St. George, Chennai – 600 009.
DRAFT VARIATION
In the said Notification, in THE SCHEDULE under the heading ‘Parks’ for the entry “42560 sq.ft” in Column(3) against the
entries “Ward No.2, Block No.46 T.S.No.2756/3” in Column (2) and “Municipal Somasundaram Park” in Column (3) the entry
“39896 sq.ft” shall be substituted.
