(No. R/MSU/M&E/SEN. TO SYN./ELECT'2010)
Election of one Member as per Chapter IV - Section 23(b) sub-section (4) not falling under any of the three
Categories of Chapter -IV Section 23(b) Class II - Other Members sub-sections (1), (2) and (3) of the
Manonmaniam Sundaranar University Act, 1990 to the Syndicate by the Senate from among its Members.
No. VI-3(b)/28/2010.
It is hereby notified, by direction, that an election will be held to elect ONE MEMBER not falling under any of the following
three categories as per Chapter-IV Section 23 (b) Class II - Other Members sub-section (4) of Manonmaniam Sundaranar
University Act, 1990 to the Syndicate by the Senate from among its members:—
(1)   Principals of affiliated colleges
-
Chapter III - Section 19 (a) Class I - Ex-officio Members -
sub-section (10)
(2) Teachers of affiliated
-
Chapter III-Section 19 (a) Class-II Other Members-sub-section (1)
colleges (Elected Members)
(3) University Professors
-
Chapter III-Section 19 (a) Class-I Ex-officio Members-sub-section (9)
The election will be held in accordance with rules and procedures for conduct of election to the University Authorities.
Nominations should be made in the prescribed form which may be obtained from the Registrar of the University.
Nomination papers shall be enclosed  in   an envelope superscribed “Nomination to the election of one member not falling
under any of the three categories of  Chapter IV - Section 23(b) Class II - Other Members sub-sections (1), (2) and (3) of
the Manonmaniam Sundaranar University Act, 1990 to the Syndicate by the Senate from among its Members” as indicated
below, and sent  by Registered Post (Acknowledgment Due),  or  delivered either in person or by a messenger, so as to be
received  by the  Registrar  within the date and hour  fixed. Nomination papers that are not sent or delivered as instructed
above will be declared invalid.  An elector shall be eligible to subscribe either as proposer or as seconder in nomination
papers, only as many times as there are vacancies but not more.
