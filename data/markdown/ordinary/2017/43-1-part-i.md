New Delhi, dated 26th September 2017 is republished:—

Indian Administrative Service (Cadre) Rules, 1954 and with

Tamil Nadu, the Central Government hereby accords approval 
to the proposal for inter cadre deputation of Shri K. Srinivasan, 
IAS (JH: 2005) from Jharkhand Cadre to Tamil Nadu cadre 
for a period of three years on personal grounds from the date 
of assumption of charge or until further orders, whichever is 
earlier.
