HOME DEPARTMENT
DECLARATION OF CERTAIN POLICE OFFICERS / PERSONNEL AS DESIGNATED OFFICERS
UNDER THE CONTROL OF THE ELECTION COMMISSION.
[G.O. Ms. No. 507, Home (Pol. XIII), 14th September 2011.]
No. II(2)/HO/399(f)/2011.
Under Section 28-A of the Representation of the People Act, 1951 (Central Act XLIII of 1951), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
designates the Director General of Police / Additional Director General of Police (Law and Order) / Additional Director General of
Police / Inspector General of Police (Central Zone), Tiruchirappalli / Commissioner of Police, Tiruchirappalli / Deputy Inspector
General of Police, Tiruchirappalli Range and Superintendent of Police, Tiruchirappalli District and all Police Officials subordinate
to them and up to the level  of Police Constables and any other Police Officers / Officials performing election related duties in any
way for the conduct of Bye-Elections to be held on the 13th October 2011 to the Legislative Assembly of this State from 140.
Tiruchirappalli (West) Assembly Constituency as Designated Officers and all the said Officers shall be deemed to be on deputation
to the Election Commission for the period commencing on and from the date of the Notification calling for the said Election and
ending with the date of declaration of the result of the said Election and accordingly, the said officers shall, during that period, be
subject to the control, superintendence and discipline of the Election Commission.
RAMESHRAM MISHRA,
Principal Secretary to Government.
