No. II(1)/CTR/63/2008.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 31 read with sub-section (4) of Section 88
of the Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax Act, 2006 (Tamil Nadu Act 32 of 2006), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby remits

8% (eight percent) and 2% (two percent) payable in respect of the period on and from the 1st April 1998 to the
11th July 2000 on the sale of Polyester Yarn under the said Act.
