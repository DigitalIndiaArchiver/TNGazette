Order:- No. 26, Revenue and Disaster Management Department, 12th January 2021.
On the recommendations of the expert team of Doctors and Public Health specialists and based on the directives of 
Government of India,  Ministry of Home Aﬀ airs, State-wide lockdown was extended from time to  time and lastly extended 
up to 31-01-2021 under the Disaster  Management Act, 2005 with certain restrictions and relaxations along with guidelines 
vide G.O.Ms.No.820, Revenue and Disaster Management  (D.M.IV) Department, dated 31.12.2020. Further in the above 
Government Order public were not allowed in all beaches including Marina  Beach on 16.01.2021 in order to prevent the 
spread of Covid-19 as large  number of general public would assemble in the Beaches in the event of  ‘Kanum Pongal’ 
(Uzhavar Thirunal). 
2. In the Government Press Release No. 18, dated 12-01-2021, it  has been stated that on the Pongal Holidays at 
Vandalur Zoological Park  and all Tourist Places in Mamallapuram in Chengalpat District, Guindy  National Park in Chennai 
and all Beaches including Marina Beach public  would assemble in large numbers and as a precautionary measure public 
should not be allowed in the above places on holidays on 15-01-2021,  16-01-2021 and 17-01-2021 in order to prevent the 
spread of Covid - 19.
