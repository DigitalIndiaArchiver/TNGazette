The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that 
the lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below have to be acquired 
for Industrial Purposes, to wit, for First Mine Expansion Scheme 
of Neyveli Lignite Corporation India Limited and it having 
already been decided that the entire amount of compensation 
to be awarded for the lands is to be paid out of the funds 
controlled or managed by Neyveli Lignite Corporation India 
Limited.  The following notice is issued under sub-section (1) 
of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes 
Act, 1997.
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu 
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Government of Tamil Nadu 
hereby acquires the lands speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below 
and measuring 1.27.0 hectare of R.Dry lands to  the same, 
a little more or less needed for Industrial purposes, to wit, for 
First Mine  Expansion Scheme of Neyveli Lignite Corporation 
India  Limited.
The plans of the lands are kept in the ofﬁ ce of the Special 
Deputy  Collector / Land Acquisition, Neyveli-2 and may be 
inspected at any  time during ofﬁ ce hours.
