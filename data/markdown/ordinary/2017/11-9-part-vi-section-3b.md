I. ELECTION TO THE SYNDICATE:
Class II - Other Members - sub-sec.(b) (1) of Section 24 under Chapter IV of the Bharathidasan University Act,
1981 -  * Two Members Elected by Principals of affiliated colleges from among themselves in accordance with the system
of proportional representation by means of the single transferable vote.
Note: One Member to be elected to fill vacancy (for a residual period- Upto 26-12-2017)
Nominations are invited in the prescribed form which can be obtained from the Registrar and Returning Officer on a
requisition and submission of a Demand Draft for Rs.500/- (Five Hundred only) drawn in favour of Bharathidasan University,
Tiruchirappalli on or after the date of Notification, i.e. 17-03-2017 with a stamped self addressed envelope to the value of
Rs.25/- (Rupees twenty five only) or in person.
Filled-in Nomination paper shall be enclosed in a envelope superscribed as follows:
 “NOMINATION FOR ELECTION TO THE SYNDICATE - BY PRINCIPALS OF AFFILIATED COLLEGES FROM
AMONG THEMSELVES”
The Members are requested to send their nomination by Registered Post (with Acknowledgement due) so as to reach
the Registrar and Returning Officer within the date and hour fixed or delivered to the Registrar and Returning Officer either
in person of by a messenger within the date and hour fixed.
Nomination papers that are not sent as stated above will be declared invalid.
