HOUSING AND URBAN DEVELOPMENT DEPARTMENT
NOTIFICATION FOR CANCELLATION OF "PETROLEUM, CHEMICALS AND PETROCHEMICALS INVESTMENT 
REGION (PCPIR)" UNDER THE TAMIL NADU TOWN AND COUNTRY PLANNING ACT, 1971.
[G.O. Ms. No. 36, Housing and Urban Development (UD4(2), 21st February 2020, Masi 9,  
Vikari, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2051.]
No. II(2)/HoU/148(g)/2020.
In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (4) of section 10 of the Town and Country 
Planning Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972) read with section 15 of the General Clauses Act, 1891 
(Tamil Nadu Act I of 1891), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby cancels Notification No.II(2)/HOU/574/2017 published at pages 
607-608 of Part II-Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 19th July, 2017.
