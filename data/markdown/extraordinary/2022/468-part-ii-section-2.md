WHEREAS, a written notice of the intention to  make a motion expressing want of conﬁ dence in Tmt.R.Sathya, District 
Panchayat Chairman, Thoothukudi district, signed by thirteen members, out of the sanctioned strength of seventeen members 
of the said District Panchayat, together with a copy of the motion proposed to be made and a written statement of charges 
against the said District Panchayat Chairman have been delivered to the District Collector, Thoothukudi district in person 
by two members as per sub-section (2) of section 212 read with section 213 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994 
(Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994);
AND WHEREAS, the District Collector, Thoothukudi district caused a copy of the statement of charges along with a copy 
of the notice of motion to be delivered, to the said District Panchayat Chairman, Tmt.R.Sathya and she had not submitted 
her reply to the charges;
AND WHEREAS, a motion expressing want of conﬁ dence in Tmt.R.Sathya, District Panchayat Chairman, Thoothukudi 
district had been carried with a support of ﬁ fteen members out of the sanctioned strength of seventeen members of the said 
District Panchayat in the meeting convened by the District Collector, Thoothukudi district on the 13th September 2022 after 
following the procedures speciﬁ ed in the said section 212 read with section 213 and forwarded the same to Government;
NOW THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred by section 213 read with sub-section (13) of section 212 of  the 
Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby removes Tmt.R.Sathya, 
District Panchayat Chairman, Thoothukudi district from the oﬃ  ce of the District Panchayat Chairman, Thoothukudi district 
with immediate eﬀ ect.
