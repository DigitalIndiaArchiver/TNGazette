WHEREAS in the reference third cited, the District Collector, The Nilgiris District has reported that the Hon'ble High Court
has granted interim stay for conducting the election of Vice-Chairman of Thevarsolai Town Panchayat, in the Nilgiris District
and the Government Pleader, Chennai has also opined not to conduct the said election until further Orders are passed
by the Hon'ble High Court.
In view of the report sent by the District Collector, The Nilgiris District, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission
hereby makes the following amendment to the S.O. second cited:—
AMENDMENT
In the said order, in the Annexure, under the heading “TOWN PANCHAYAT” in SI.No. 4, the District Nilgiris and the
corresponding entries relating thereto shall be omitted.
