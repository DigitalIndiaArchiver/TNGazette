BILL INTRODUCED IN THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF THE
STATE OF TAMIL NADU
Under Rule 130 of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Rules, the following Bill
which was introduced in the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu on
13th July, 2017 is published together with Statement of Objects and Reasons for
general information:—
L.A. Bill No. 36 of 2017.
A Bill further to amend the Tamil Nadu Agricultural Produce Marketing
(Regulation) Act,1987.
BE it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in
the Sixty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
 1. (1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu Agricultural Produce
Marketing (Regulation) Second Amendment Act, 2017.
 (2) It shall be deemed to have  come into force on the 31st day
of May 2017.
 2. In section 33 of the Tamil Nadu Agricultural Produce Marketing
(Regulation) Act, 1987, in sub-section (1), in the proviso, for the expression
“six years”, the expression “seven years” shall be substituted.
  3. (1) The   Tamil   Nadu  Agricultural Produce  Marketing  (Regulation)
Second Amendment Ordinance, 2017 is hereby repealed.
 (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken
under the Tamil Nadu Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) Act, 1987,
as amended by the said Ordinance, shall be deemed to have been done or
taken under the said Act, as amended by this Act.

STATEMENT OF OBJECTS AND REASONS.
Sub-section (1) of Section 33 of the Tamil Nadu Agricultural Produce
Marketing (Regulation) Act, 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 1989) empowers the
Government to appoint a Special Officer to manage the affairs of the market
committee pending the constitution of a new market committee for a period
not exceeding three years in the aggregate. By virtue of the said provision,
Special Officers were appointed to  the 23 market committees to manage
the affairs of the said market committees and their term was extended by
the Tamil Nadu Act 8 of 2017  up to six years. The tenure of the Special
Officers was  to expire on 30-05-2017 and as it will take some more time
to nominate  market committees, there was a compelling necessity to extend
the tenure of the said Special Officers for a further period of one year beyond
30.05.2017 or till the reconstitution of the market committees, whichever is
earlier. The Government have therefore, decided to amend the said
Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 1989 for the above purpose.
2. Accordingly, the Governor has promulgated Tamil Nadu Agricultural
Produce Marketing (Regulation) Second Amendment Ordinance, 2017
(Tamil Nadu Ordinance 3 of 2017) to extend the term of office of the Special
Officers for a further period of one year beyond the 30th May 2017 or till
reconstitution of market committes, whichever is earlier.
3. The Bill seeks to replace the said Ordinance.
