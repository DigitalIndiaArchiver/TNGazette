Bill which was introduced in the Legislative Assembly of the Tamil Nadu on 
20th February, 2024 is published together with Statement of Objects and Reasons 
for general information:—
L.A Bill No. 5 of 2024

Fund of the State for the services and purposes of a part of the financial year 
commencing on the 1st day of April 2024.
