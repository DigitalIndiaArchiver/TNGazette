No. SRO A-21/2019.—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-sections (1) and (2) of Section 53 of the Legal Metrology 
Act, 2009 (Central Act 1 of 2010) the Governor of Tamil Nadu after consultation with the Central Government, hereby make 
the following amendment to the Tamil Nadu Legal Metrology (Enforcement) Rules, 2011, the Draft of the same have been 
Previously published as required under sub-section (4) of the said Section 53 of the said Act.
Amendments.
