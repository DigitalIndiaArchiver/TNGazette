APPOINTMENT OF PERSONS AS MEMBERS OF TAMIL NADU WAQF BOARD IN THE 
CATEGORY OF MUTAWALLIS UNDER THE WAQF ACT, 1995.
 [G.O. (2D) No.19, Backward Classes, Most Backward Classes and Minorities Welfare (T1)
Department,12th September 2020, Aavani 27, Saarvari, Thiruvalluvar Aandu – 2051.]
No. II(2)/BCMBCMW/550(a)/2020.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-clause (iv) of clause (b) of sub-section (1) and 
powers conferred by sub-section (9) of section 14 of the Waqf Act, 1995 (Central Act 43 of 1995), 
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the following persons as members of Tamil Nadu 
Waqf Board, subject to further orders of the Hon’ble Supreme Court of India and the outcome of 
the Special Leave Petition ﬁ led by the Government of Tamil Nadu (Diary No.17419/2020) against 
the orders of the High Court of Madras in W.P. Nos.726/2020, 8377/2020 and 9557/2020 in the 
Supreme Court of India namely:-
Mutawallis
1. Thiru A. Abdul Rahman
2. Thiru M. Mohamed Basheer
