Rescission of Notification
No. II(2)/HO/362/2009.
[Tamil Nadu Protection of Interests of Depositors
(in Financial establishments) Act, 1997  (Tamil Nadu Act 44
of 1997) - Thiruvalargal V.R.M.S. Finance & Investment,
K.G. Chavadi, Coimbatore-Ad-interim  order of attachment
of properties of said Financial Establishments under Section
3 of the said Act - Ordered - Raising the Ad-interim order
of attachment - Orders - Issued.]
The following Government Order is published:—
[G.O. Ms. No. 343, Home (Police XIX), 24th April 2009.]
READ AGAIN:
(1) G.O.Ms.No.816, Home, (Courts IIA), dated
4th September 2006.
(2) Orders of the Special Judge under TNPID Act,
Chennai-104, in O.A.Nos. 48/2007, 51/2007 and 52/2007,
dated 2nd November 2007.
(3) Orders of the Special Judge under TNPID Act,
Chennai-104, in O.A.Nos. 49/2007, 50/2007 and 53/2007,
dated 20th November 2007.
READ ALSO:—
(4)  From the Competent Authority and District Revenue
Officer, Coimbatore Lr.No. 11309/2004/J1, dated
3rd November 2007.
(5) From the Inspector General of Police, Economic
Offences Wing, Chennai Lr.No.C2/1719/EOW/2008,
dated 6th May 2008 and 29th October 2008.
Order: No. 343, Home (Police XIX), 24th April 2009
In the Government Order first read above, Government
have issued orders for the ad-interim attachment of properties
mentioned in the Schedule to the said Order, belonging to
Thiruvalargal V.R.M.S. Finance & Investments, K.G. Chavadi,
Coimbatore.
2.
In the Order second and third read above, the Special
Court under Tamil Nadu Interests of Depositors (in Financial
Establishments) Act, 1997, Chennai has raised the ad-interim
order of attachment on the properties effected by Government
in the order first read above.
3.
The Competent Authority and District Revenue Officer,
Coimbatore in his letter fourth read above, has sent the
Special Court orders second and third read above and
requested the orders of Government. The Inspector General
of Police, Economic Offences Wing, Chennai has also
endorsed the above proposal.
4.
In compliance to the Orders of the Special Court,
Tamil Nadu Protection of Interest of Depositors (in Financial
Establishments) Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 44 of 1997),
Chennai, second read above and in exercise of the powers
conferred by Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Protection of
Interests of Depositors (in Financial Establishments) Act,
1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 44 of 1997), the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby rescinds the said Government Order
issued in G.O.Ms. No.816, Home (Courts IIA) Department,
dated, the 4th September 2006.
(By Order of the Governor)

Draft Notification regarding Revision of Minimum rates of
Wages for employment in Agriculture and works allied
to Agriculture under the Act.
[G.O. (2D) No. 59, Labour and Employment,
30th June 2009.]
No. II(2)/LE/366/2009.—The following draft of a Notification,
which it is proposed to be issued in exercise of the powers
conferred by clause (b) of sub-section (1) of Section 3 and
sub-section (2) of Section 5 of the Minimum Wages Act, 1948
(Central Act XI of 1948), is hereby published for information
of all persons likely to be affected thereby, as required by
clause (b) of sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the said Act.
2. Notice is hereby given that the draft Notification will
be taken into consideration on or after the expiry of two
months from the date of publication of this Notification in the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette and that any objection or
suggestion, which may be received from any person with
respect thereto before the expiry of the aforesaid period will
be considered by the Government of Tamil Nadu. Objection
and suggestion, if any, should be addressed to the Principal
Secretary to Government of Tamil Nadu, Labour and
Employment Department, Fort St.George, Secretariat,
Chennai-600 009 through the Commissioner of Land
Administration and Commissioner of Land Reforms, Chennai-
600 005.

Explanations I.— (1) The above revised minimum rates
of wages shall also apply to the Agricultural labourers employed
in Colleges and Research Stations under the Tamil Nadu
Agricultural University and Agricultural Farms maintained by
the Agriculture, Horticulture and other Government
Departments.
(2) Where there is no difference in the daily hours of
work and where the same work or work of a similar nature
is performed no distinction in the payment of wages should
be made between men and women workers as per the Equal
Remuneration Act, 1976.
(3) For the purpose of this Notification, minimum rate of
wages specified above shall be all inclusive of rate allowing
for the basic rate, cost of living allowance and the cash value
of concession, if any, but it does not include the cost of food,
if any, supplied to the employees and hence the cost of food
shall not be deducted from the minimum rates of wages paid
to the employees.
