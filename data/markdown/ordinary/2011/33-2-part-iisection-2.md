HIGHER EDUCATION DEPARTMENT
Nomination of Two Headmasters of Higher Secondary
Schools within the University Area as Members to the
Academic Council of Thiruvalluvar University for certain
period under the Thiruvalluvar University Act.
[G.O.(1D) No. 146, Higher Education (K2), 5th August
2011, Aadi 20, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No. II(2)/HE/384/2011.—Under Chapter III, sub-section
1(q) of the Section 20 of the Thiruvalluvar University Act,
2002, the Pro-Chancellor of Thiruvalluvar University has
nominated the following Two Headmasters of the Higher
Secondary School within the university area as members to
the Academic Council of Thiruvalluvar University for a period
of three years with effect from the date of issue of this order.
(i)
Thirumathi M. Rajakumari, M.Sc., M.Ed., M.Phil,
Headmistress.
Government Higher Secondary School,
Veppur, Cuddalore District.
(ii)
Thiru N. Krishnamurthi,
Headmaster,
Government Higher Secondary School,
Onnupuram, Arani Taluk,
Thiruvannamalai District.
R. KANNAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.

RESCISSION OF NOTIFICATION
[G.O. Ms. No. 437, Home (Police XIX), 5th August 2011.]
No. II(2)/HO/385/2011.-In exercise of the powers conferred
by Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Protection of interests of
Depositors (in Financial Establishments) Act, 1997 (Tamil
Nadu Act 44 of 1997), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
rescinds the Home Department Notification No. II(2)/HO/
303/2004, published at Pages 192 and 193 of Part II -
Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the
21st April 2004.
RAMESHRAM MISHRA,
Principal Secretary to Government.
HOUSING AND URBAN DEVELOPMENT DEPARTMENT
Declaration of the area Comprising Punjaipuliyampatti Third
Grade Municipality to be a Local Planning Area under
the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning Act.
[G.O. Ms. No. 113, Housing and Urban Development
[UD 4(2), 9th August 2011.]
No. II(2)/HOU/386/2011.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 10 of the Tamil Nadu
Town and Country Planning Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of
1972) the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby declares the area
comprising Punjaipuliyampatti Third Grade Municipality to be
Punjaipuliyampatti Local Planning Area, the intention to do so
has been previously published as required by clause (b) of
sub-section (1) of Section 10 of the said Act.
K. PHANINDRA REDDY,
   Secretary to Government.
