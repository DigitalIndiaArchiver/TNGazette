NOTICE
Under sub-section(1) of section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands specified in the Schedule below 
measuring an extent of 12.1950 hectares to the same, a little more or less needed for industrial purpose wit, for 
SIPCOT - SIPCOT NAMAKKAL INDUSTRIAL PARK SCHEME.
The plan of the lands are kept in the office of the Special DRO (LA), Namakkal and Special Tahsildar(LA), Unit, 
Namakkal which may be inspected on any day during office hours.
