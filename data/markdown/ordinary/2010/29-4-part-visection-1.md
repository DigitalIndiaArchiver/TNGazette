Confirmation of Comprehensive variation to the approved South West Extension T.P. Scheme Part-IX
of Madurai Local Planning Area.
(Roc. No. 20206/2005/DP2.)
No. VI(1)/272/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section 33(2) of Town and Country Planning Act, 1971, the Director
of Town and Country Planning, Chennai hereby confirms the following comprehensive variation to the approved South
West  Extension T.P. Scheme Part-IX of Madurai Local Planning Area approved by Director of Town and Country
Planning in his proceedings Roc.No. 10905/92/DP2, dated 28th April 1992.  The fact of this approval in Form No. 12
published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No. 8, Part VI—Section-1, Page No. 56, dated 3rd March 2004.
Since no objections and suggestions have been received on this draft notification within the stipulated time the same
are hereby confirmed and ordered as below:—
CONFIRMATION VARIATION
1. ‘‘The entire South West Extension Detailed Development Plan Part—IX of Madurai Local Planning Area approved
by the Director of Town and Country Planning Roc. No.10905/92 DP2, dated 28th April 1992 is varied by South West
Extension Detailed Development Plan Part-IX of Madurai Local Planning Area.’’
2. The above comprehensive variation is made enforceable from the date of publication of this notification in the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.

 PANKAJ KUMAR BANSAL,

No. VI(1)/273/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (1) of Section 33 of Town and Country Planning Act, 1971
(Act No. 35 of 1972) the Director of Town and Country Planning in his Proceedings Roc. No. 4591/2004/ DP3, dated
6th July 2010, proposes to make the following variation to the Gowripuram Area Detailed Development Plan of Karur
Local Planning Area approved vide Proceedings Roc. No. 5343/80, dated 1st October 1981 of Director of Town and
Country Planning and published in the  Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, Part VI—Section I, Page No. 520,
dated 11th November 1981.
2. Any person affected or interested in this draft variation may within Sixty days from the date of publication of this
Notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette can represent in preson or submit in writing to the
Member-Secretary, Karur Local Planning Authority any objections and suggestions relating thereto.
3. The variation with plan may be inspected free of cost at any time during office hours at the above said Local
Planning Authority office.

1. The entire Gowripuram Area Detailed Development Plan of Karur Local Planning Area is comprehensively varied
by the Director of Town and Country Planning vide proceedings Roc.No. 4591/04/DP3, dated 6th July 2010.

3. The Detailed Development clauses and schedules annexed with this plan are made enforceable from the date
of publication of the confirmed variation notification to be issued under Section 33(2) of the Act and published in the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
