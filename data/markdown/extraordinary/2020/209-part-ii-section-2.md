A)	 The sub-clause “In relaxation to earlier orders, the permission to operate industries are relaxed further and permitted 
to operate with 100 % of employees in the industries working with less than 100 persons and 50 % of employees or 
a minimum of 100 persons in the industries working with more than 100 persons in all Corporations, Municipalities 
and Town Panchayats in all areas across the State, except for areas under the jurisdiction of Greater Chennai 
Police.” under Clause V  shall be replaced as 
“In relaxation to earlier orders, the permission to operate industries are relaxed further and permitted to operate with 
100 % of employees in the industries working with less than 100 persons and 50 % of employees or a minimum 
of 100 persons in the industries working with more than 100 persons in all Corporations, Municipalities and Town 
Panchayats in all areas across the State, except for areas under the jurisdiction of Greater Chennai Police. In 
respect of areas under the jurisdiction of Greater Chennai Police, 17 industrial estates including Guindy & Ambattur 
shall be permitted to operate with 25 % of employees (Except in containment zones) provided that workers above 
55 years are avoided and those with symptoms of fever, cold & cough shall not be allowed. Checking employee’s 
body temperature using Thermal Scanner daily, social distancing and wearing of face mask by employees shall 
be strictly ensured. Further frequent hand wash using soap & sanitizers by employees, sanitization of work place 
both in the morning & evening and disinfecting toilets once every 2 hours shall be strictly ensured in addition to 
following Standard Operating Procedures issued by Government.”
