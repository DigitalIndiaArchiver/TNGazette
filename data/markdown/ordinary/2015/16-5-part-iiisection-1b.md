AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules, in the Annexure referred to in Rule 2,—
(1) under the heading “Class-I”, under Category 4, under the sub-heading “Miscellaneous Department”,
the entry “Office of the Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission” in column (1) and the corresponding entry “Assistant Secretary,
Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission” in column (2) thereof shall be omitted;
(2) under the heading “Class-III, IV & V” under the sub-heading “Local Fund Audit Department”, the entry
“Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission” in column (1) and the corresponding entry “Assistant Secretary, Tamil Nadu Public
Serivce Commission” in column (2) thereof shall be omitted.
 P.W.C. DAVIDAR,
Principal  Secretary  to  Government.
