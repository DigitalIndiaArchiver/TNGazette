HIGHER EDUCATION DEPARTMENT
DATE OF COMMENCEMENT OF SRI VENKATESWARAA UNIVERSITY UNDER THE TAMIL NADU 
PRIVATE UNIVERSITIES ACT, 2019. (TAMIL NADU ACT 14 OF 2019)
[G.O.Ms. No. 78, Higher Education (K2), 27th May 2022, வைகாசி 13, சுபகிருது, திருவள்ளுவர் ஆண்டு-2053.]
No. II(2)/HE/411(g)/2022.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 10 of the Tamil Nadu Private 
Universities Act, 2019 (Tamil Nadu Act 14 of 2019), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby specifies the 
27th May, 2022, as the date on which Sri Venkateswaraa University shall commence to exercise its function.
	
D. KARTHIKEYAN,
	
Principal Secretary to Government.
