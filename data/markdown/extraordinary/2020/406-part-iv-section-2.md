An Act further to amend the Tamil Nadu Town and Country 
Planning Act, 1971. 
BE it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in 
the Seventy-ﬁ rst Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
1. 
(1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu Town and Country 
Planning (Amendment) Act, 2020.
(2) It shall come into force at once. 
2. 
In section 20 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning Act, 1971 
(hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in sub-section (2),-
 
(1) clause (b) shall be omitted;
       (2) for clause (c), the following clause shall be substituted, namely:-
              “(c) the area covered by the plan;”.
3. 
 In section 21 of the principal Act, the expression “and after consulting 
in the prescribed manner the owners of lands and buildings in the area” shall 
be omitted.

commencement.
