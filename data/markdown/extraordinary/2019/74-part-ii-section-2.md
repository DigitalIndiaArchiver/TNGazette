Under Section 18 of the Prohibition of Employment as Manual Scavengers and their Rehabilitation Act, 
2013 (Central Act 25 of 2013), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby confers the powers and impose the duties 
on the local authority, as deﬁ ned in clause (f) of sub-section (1) of Section 2 of the said Act and on the District 
Magistrate, to ensure that the provisions of the Act are properly carried out, as speciﬁ ed below:-
I. Local Authority:
(i)
survey of insanitary latrines existing within its jurisdiction;
(ii)
publishing a list of such insanitary latrines;
(iii)
demolition and removal of insanitary  latrines;
(iv)
construction of sanitary community latrines in necessary areas and in adequate numbers;
(v)
take all necessary steps towards elimination of the practice of open defecation;
(vi)
ensure that no person is engaged or employed, either directly or indirectly, for hazardous 
cleaning of a sewer or a septic tank;
(vii)
carry out a survey of manual scavengers in necessary circumstances;
(viii)
creation of awareness that no insanitary latrine shall be constructed, used or maintained; and
(ix)
use appropriate technological appliances for cleaning of sewers, septic tanks and other 
spaces within its control.

II. District Magistrate:
(i)
rehabilitation of manual scavengers identiﬁ ed under the Act;
(ii)
ensuring prohibition of engagement of a person as a manual scavenger by any 
person or institution within his territorial jurisdiction;
(iii)
ensuring that no person or institution constructs, maintains, uses or makes available 
for use, an insanitary latrines;
(iv)
ensuring that persons contravening the provisions of the said Central Act 25 of 2013 
are investigated and prosecuted  under the relevant provisions of the Act; and
(v)
ensuring due compliance of all provisions of the Act applicable within his jurisdiction 
including by the local authorities.
Further, under Section 18 of the said  Central Act 25 of 2013, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby 
authorizes the local authority and the District Magistrate to specify the subordinate oﬃ  cers, who shall 
exercise all or any of the powers, and perform all or any of the duties and the local limits within which 
such powers or duties shall be carried out by the oﬃ  cer or oﬃ  cers so speciﬁ ed, strictly in accordance 
with the provisions of the said Central Act 25 of 2013 and the relevant provisions of the other Central 
and State Legislations.
