Notification regarding certain person as a member to
the Tamil Nadu Veterinary Council nominated by the
State Veterinary Association under Indian Veterinary
Council Act.
[G.O. Ms. No. 121, Animal Husbandry, Dairying and
Fisheries (AH3-2), 30th September 2009.]
No. II(2)/AHDFI/571/2009.—Under sub-section (2) of
Section 32 of the Indian Veterinary Council Act, 1984 (Central
Act 52 of 1984), the Government of Tamil Nadu hereby
notifies Dr. B. Saravanan, MVSc., Veterinary Assistant
Surgeon, Veterinary Clinician Centre, Thanjavur as a member
to the Tamil Nadu Veterinary Council nominated by the State
Veterinary Association under clause (e) of Sub-section (i) of
Section 32 of the said Act.
C. MUTHUKUMARASWAMY,
Secretary to Government.
