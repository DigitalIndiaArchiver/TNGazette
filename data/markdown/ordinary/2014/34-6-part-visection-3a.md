GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAQF BOARD, CHENNAI
Taking over of Asar Pakkir Thaikkal Wakf, Pattukottai Taluk, Tanjore District
under Direct Management.
(Rc. No. 18456/10/B7/TNJ.)
No. VI-3(a)/83/2014.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65(1) of the Waqf  Act, 1995, as amended by
Act 27/2013, the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in item No. 38/14 in Rc. No. 18456/10/B7/TNJ,
dated 19-02-2014 has ordered to take over the Asar Pakkir Thaikkal Wakf, Pattukottai Taluk, Tanjore District under the direct
Management of the Tamil Nadu Waqf Board for a period of One year from the date of publication and to appoint the
Superintendent of Waqfs, Tanjore as its Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED MEERAN,
27th August 2014.
District Revenue Officer /
Chief Executive Officer (Incharge),
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
Taking over of Kadamburar Mosque,  Vandavasi Taluk, Tiruvannamalai District
under Direct Management.
(Rc. No. 10108/00/B7/TVM.)
No. VI-3(a)/84/2014.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Waqf  Act, 1995, as amended by
Act 27/2013, the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in item No. 40/14 in Rc. No. 10108/00/B7/TVM,
dated 19-02-2014 has ordered to take over the Kadamburar Mosque, Vandavasi Taluk, Tiruvannamalai District under the direct
Management of the Tamil Nadu Waqf Board for a period of One year from the date of publication and to appoint the Waqf
Inspector, Tiruvannamalai as its Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED MEERAN,
27th August 2014.
District Revenue Officer/
Chief Executive Officer (Incharge),
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
Extension of Direct Management of Jamia Masjid, Salem.
(Rc. No. 15983/08/B2/SLM.)
No. VI-3(a)/85/2014.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995 as amended by
Act 27/2013, the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in item No. 66/14 in Rc. No. 15983/08/B2/SLM,
dated 26-06-2014 has resolved to ratify the period of direct Management of Jamia Masjid, Salem from 24-11-2013 to
25-06-2014 and also to extend the period of direct Management of the above Wakf for the period of 6 months from the
Board's order dated 26-06-2014.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED MEERAN,
27th August 2014.
District Revenue Officer/
Chief Executive Officer (Incharge),
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
