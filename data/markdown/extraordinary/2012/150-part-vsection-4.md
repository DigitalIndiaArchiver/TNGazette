 NOTIFICATIONS BY THE ELECTION COMMISSION OF INDIA
ELECTION COMMISSION’S NOTIFICATION REGARDING EXIT POLL AT THE BYE-ELECTION
TO THE TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY FROM 180. PUDUKKOTTAI ASSEMBLY
CONSTITUENCY.
No. SRO G-17/2012.
The following Notification of the Election Commission of India, Nirvachan Sadan, Ashoka Road,
New Delhi-110 001, dated 29th May, 2012 is published:—
No. 576/EXIT/2012/SDR(BYE-LA&HP).—In exercise of the powers under sub-section (1) of Section
126A of the Representation of the People Act, 1951, and having regard to the provisions of clause (b)
of sub-section (2) of the said Section, the Commission hereby notifies the period between 8 A.M. and
5.30 P.M. on 12th June 2012 (Tuesday), as the period during which conducting any exit poll or
disseminating in any manner whatsoever, the result of any exit poll in connection with the current
bye-elections announced vide the Commission’s Press Note dated 24th April, 2012 from 39-Nellore
Parliamentary Constituency, 286-Tirupati, 253-Allagadda, 244-Rajampet, 247-Rayachoti, 104-Parkal,
227-Ongole, 127-Narasannapeta, 152-Payakaraopet (SC), 272-Anantapur Urban, 263-Yemmiganur,
267-Rayadurg, 246-Kodur (SC), 242-Udayagiri, 177-Narasapuram, 212-Prathipadu (SC),
161-Ramachandrapuram, 220-Macherla & 186-Polavaram (ST) Assembly Constituencies in Andhra
Pradesh and 183-Maheshwar (SC) Assembly Constituency in Madhya Pradesh, 20-Nalchar (SC)
Assembly Constituency in Tripura, 230-Daspur & 252-Bankura Assembly Constituencies in West
Bengal, 82-Mant Assembly Constituency in Uttar Pradesh, 180-Pudukkottai Assembly Constituency in
Tamil Nadu, 232-Kaij(SC) Assembly Constituency in Maharashtra and 64-Hatia Assembly Constituency
in Jharkhand, would be prohibited.

It is further clarified that under Section 126(1)(b) of the Representation of the People Act, 1951, displaying

of each of the aforesaid bye-elections.
