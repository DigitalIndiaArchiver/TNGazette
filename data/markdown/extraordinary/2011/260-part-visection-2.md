[ELECTIONS—PANCHAYATS—CONDUCT OF—PREPARATION OF ELECTORAL ROLLS—
APPOINTMENT OF BLOCK DEVELOPMENT OFFICER (BLOCK PANCHAYAT) AS PANCHAYAT
ELECTORAL REGISTRATION OFFICER-NOTIFICATION OF.]
(S.O. No. 15/2011/TNSEC/PE-I.)
No. VI(2)/420 (b-1)/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 30 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats
Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994) read with rule 7 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Elections)
Rules, 1995, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission hereby designates the Block Development
Officers (Block Panchayat) to be the Panchayat Electoral Registration Officers for the purposes of
preparing and publishing Panchayat Electoral Rolls in respect of Wards of Panchayats lying within
their respective jurisdiction in connection with the conduct of ordinary elections, 2011 to Panchayats
and subsequent casual elections in the State.
(By Order of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission)
M. PARAMESWARAN,
Chennai-106,
Secretary (in-charge).
9th August 2011.
Tamil Nadu State Election Commission.

[ELECTIONS—PANCHAYATS—APPOINTMENT OF RETURNING OFFICERS AND ASSISTANT
RETURNING OFFICERS, DISTRICT ELECTION OFFICERS AUTHORISED TO MAKE
APPOINTMENTS—NOTIFICATION OF.]
(S.O. No. 16/2011/TNSEC/PE-I.)
No. VI(2)/420 (b-2)/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred by rule 8 and 10 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Elections)
Rules, 1995, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission hereby authorises the District Election
Officers to make appointment of Returning Officers and Assistant Returning Officers for the conduct
of election of Ward Members of the District Panchayats, Panchayat Union Councils and Village
Panchayats and also for the conduct of the election of Presidents of Village Panchayats within their
respective jurisdiction in connection with the conduct of ordinary elections, 2011 to Panchayats and
subsequent casual elections in the State.
(By Order of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission)
M. PARAMESWARAN,
Chennai-106,
Secretary (in-charge).
