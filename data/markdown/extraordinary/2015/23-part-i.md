Order: No. 115, Public (Special-B), 26th January  2015.
Under Clause (3) of Article 166 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs
that the portfolios “Hindu Religious and Charitable Endowments” held by Thiru P. Chendur Pandian,
Minister for Hindu Religious and Charitable Endowments, who has since been hospitalized, be allotted to
Thiru R. Kamaraj, Minister for Food with immediate effect and he be designated as Minister for Food, Hindu
Religious and Charitable Endowments, Thiru P. Chendur Pandian will continue to be a Minister.
(By Order of the Governor)
K. GNANADESIKAN,
Chief Secretary.
