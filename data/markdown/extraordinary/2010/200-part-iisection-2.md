The Governor of Tamil Nadu, having been satisfied that
the lands specified in the schedule below have to be acquired
for Industrial purpose, to wit, for the Madurai Airport Expansion
Scheme and it having already been decided that the entire
amount of compensation to be, awarded for the lands to be paid
out of the lands, controlled or managed by Government. The
following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 3
of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of land for Industrial Purposes
Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby Acquires the lands specified in the schedule below
and measuring 15.31.0 hectares (or) 37.82 acres to the
same, a little more or less needed for Industrial Purpose,
to wit, for the Madurai Airport Expansion Scheme.
The plan of the lands are kept in the office of the
Special Tahsildar (Land Acquisition), Madurai Airport
Expansion Scheme, Unit II, Block No. 4, Madurai South
Taluk, Madurai District and may be inspected at any time
during office hours.
