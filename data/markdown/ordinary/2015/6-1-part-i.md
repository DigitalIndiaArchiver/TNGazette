[G.O. Ms. No. 114, Public (Special A), 23rd January 2015,
Thai 9, Jaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2046.]
No. I/PUSP/1/2015.—The following Notification of the
Government of India, Ministry of Law and Justice, (Department
of Justice), Jaisalmer House,  26, Man Singh Road, New Delhi-
110 011, dated 22nd January, 2015 is republished:—
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 95 of the
Constitution of Jammu and Kashmir, the President is pleased
to appoint Shri Justice Narayanan Nadar Paul Vasanthakumar,
Judge of the Madras High Court, to be the Chief Justice of the
Jammu and Kashmir High Court with effect from the date he
assumes charge of his office.
[No. K.13021/04/2014-US.II]
PRAVEEN GARG,
Joint  Secretary to the Government of India.
(By Order of the Governor)
K. GNANADESIKAN,
Chief Secretary.

OBITUARY NOTICE
[G.O. Rt. No. 182, Public (Special-B),15th January 2015,
Thai 1, Jaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2046.]
No.I/PUSP/2/2015.—The Government of Tamil Nadu record
with deep and profound regret the untimely death of one of its
Senior Officers, Tmt. Shanthini Kapoor, I.A.S., Principal
Secretary to Government, Special Programme Implementation
Department on 14th January, 2015 at the age of 54 years.
2. She was appointed into the Indian Administrative
Service on 30-08-1983. She started her career as Assistant
Collector (Training), Coimbatore and served with distinction in
various capacities such as Under Secretary to Government of
India, Department of Personnel & Training, Collector, Chennai,
Member-Secretary, Sports Development Authority of
Tamil Nadu, Special Secretary to Government, Revenue/
Home Department, Commissioner of Tribal Welfare,
Special Officer & Managing Director, Co-optex, Principal
Secretary to Government, Youth Welfare & Sports
Development Department and Principal Secretary to
Government, Special Programme Implementation Department.
3. In the death of Tmt. Shanthini Kapoor, I.A.S.r the
Government of Tamil Nadu have lost one of its senior officers.
The Government places on record its condolence to the
bereaved family members.
JATINDRA NATH SWAIN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
