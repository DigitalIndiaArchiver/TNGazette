TAMIL  NADU  GOVERNMENT  GAZETTE  EXTRAORDINARY
The Civil Judges (Junior Division) functioning in the Court of Small Causes, Chennai, have commenced the trial of cases
under Section 138 of Negotiable Instruments Act with effect from 12th June 2007. Now, the Government in their
letter No. 5108/Cts.II/2008-5, Home (Courts-II) Department, dated 10th December 2008, have requested to confer upon the
Civil Judges (Junior Division) functioning in the Court of Small Causes, Chennai, under sub-section (1) of Section 18 read
with sub-section (1) of Section 32 of the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 (Central Act 2 of 1974), the powers conferred
under the said Code on the Metropolitan Masgistrate so as to try and dispose of the cases under Section 138 of Negotiable
Instruments Act.
II
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 18(1) of the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 (Central Act 2 of 1974)
read  with Section 32(1) of the said Code, on the request of the State Government, the High Court, Madras, hereby appoints
the following Presiding Officers of the Courts of Small Causes, Chennai, as Special Metropolitan Magistrates, for a period
of another one year with effect from 12th June 2008, to try and dispose of the cases under Section 138 of Negotiable
Instruments Act, which are pending in large number and which are being transferred from the files of Metropolitan Magistrate
Courts, Chennai from time to time and they shall exercise all the powers of Metropolitan Magistrate under the said Code
for the said purpose:
