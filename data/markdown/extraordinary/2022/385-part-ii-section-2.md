2. In the Government Order second read above, Government have constituted the District Level Vigilance Committee for

each Sub-Division level as per Section 24(3) of the Prohibition of Employment as Manual Scavengers and their Rehabilitation

necessary action to notify the constitution of District Vigilance Committee and Sub Divisional Level Vigilance Committees so

6. The District Level and Sub-Division Level Vigilance Committee shall meet at least once in every three months to perform 
the following functions as per Section 25 of the Prohibition of Employment as Manual Scavengers and their Rehabilitation 
Act, 2013:- 
 
(a) to advise the District Magistrate or, as the case may be, the Sub-Divisional Magistrate, on the action which needs 
to be taken, to ensure that the provisions of this Act or of any rule made thereunder are properly implemented:- 
 
(b) to oversee the economic and social rehabilitation of manual scavengers;
 
(c) to co-ordinate the functions of all concerned agencies with a view to channelise adequate credit for the rehabilitation 
of manual scavengers;
 
(d) to monitor the registration of oﬀ ences under this Act and their investigation and prosecution. 
7. The above said Committees have to perform the said functions as per the provision of the said Act and the progress 
report about the implementation of the Prohibition of Employment as Manual Scavengers and their Rehabilitation Act, 2013 
shall be furnished to Government periodically. 
8. The term of oﬃ  ce of the non-oﬃ  cial Members who are nominated in the said Committees shall be a period of three 
years from the date of appointment in the Committee.
