COMMERCIAL TAXES AND REGISTRATION DEPARTMENT
DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF THE TAMIL NADU SOCIETIES REGISTRATION (AMENDMENT) ACT, 2011.
[G.O. Ms. No. 124, Commercial Taxes and Registration (M1), 12th October 2011.]
No. II(2)/CTR/422(d-1)/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Societies Registration
(Amendment) Act, 2011 (Tamil Nadu Act 24 of 2011), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the
14th day of October 2011 as the date on which the said Act shall come into force.
DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF THE TAMIL NADU VALUE ADDED TAX (FIFTH AMENDMENT) ACT, 2011.
[G.O. Ms. No. 127, Commercial Taxes and Registration (B1), 12th October 2011,
Purattasi 25, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No. II(2)/CTR/422(d-2)/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax
(Fifth Amendment) Act, 2011 (Tamil Nadu Act 28 of 2011),  the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the
1st day of November 2011, as the date on which the said Act shall come into force.
SUNIL PALIWAL,
