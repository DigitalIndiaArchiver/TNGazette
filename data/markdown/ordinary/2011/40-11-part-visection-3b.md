I. ELECTION TO THE SYNDICATE
Class II—Other Members—sub-section (b) (2) of Section 24 under Chapter IV the Bharathidasan University Act, 1981—
*Two Members elected by the Principals of the affiliated colleges from among themselves, in accordance with the system of
proportional representation by means of a single transferable vote.
Class II—Other Members—sub-section (b) (2) of Section 24 under Chapter IV the Bharathidasan University Act, 1981—
*Two Members elected by the Teachers of the affiliated colleges other than the Principals from among themselves, who are
the members of the Senate in accordance with the system of proportional representation by means of a
single transferable vote.
II. ELECTION TO THE STANDING COMMITTEE ON ACADEMIC AFFAIRS
Class II—Other Members—sub-section (2) of Section 23 under Chapter III of Bharathidasan University Act, 1981—
Two Members elected by the Principals of affiliated colleges from among themselves in accordance with the system of
proportional representation by means of a single transferable vote.
