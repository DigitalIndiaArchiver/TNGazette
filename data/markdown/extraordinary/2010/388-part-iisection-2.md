COMMERCIAL TAXES AND REGISTRATION  DEPARTMENT
REDUCTION IN RATE OF TAX PAYABLE OF MEDICAL X-RAY FILMS IN REVIVAL OF HINDUSTAN
PHOTO FILMS, OOTACAMUND UNDER THE TAMIL NADU VALUE ADDED TAX ACT, 2006.
[G.O.Ms. No. 209, Commercial Taxes and Registration(B2), 23rd December 2010,
Margazhi-8, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041]
No. II(2)/CTR/749(b)/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 30 of the Tamil Nadu Value Added
Tax Act, 2006 (Tamil Nadu Act 32 of 2006), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes a reduction in rate
of tax from 12.5 per cent to 4 per cent in respect of tax payable under the said Act on the sale of the
Medical X-ray Films by Thiruvalargal Hindustan Photo Films Manufacturing Company Limited, Ootacamund.
MD. NASIMUDDIN,
Secretary to Government.
