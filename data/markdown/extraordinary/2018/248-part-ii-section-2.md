The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed 
that the lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below have to 
be acquired for industrial purposes to wit for formation of 
New Broad Gauge Railway Line between Tindivanam and 
Nagari and it having already been decided that the entire 
amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands are 
to be paid our to the lands controlled or managed by 
Government. The following notice is issued under sub–section 
(1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for 
Industrial Purposes Act 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub–section (1) of Section 3 of the 
Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes 
Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of 
Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands speciﬁ ed in the 
Schedule below and measuring 0.36.96 hectares to the 
same, a little more or less needed for industrial purposes, 
to wit, for the formation of New Broad Gauge Railway Line 
between Tindivanam and  Nagari.
The plan of the lands are kept in the ofﬁ ce of the 
Thasildar, Arcot Taluk and may be inspected at any time 
during ofﬁ ce hours.

to wit, for Expansion of Tiruchirappalli Airport.
