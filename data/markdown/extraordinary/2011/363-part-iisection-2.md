 TRANSPORT DEPARTMENT
ACQUISITION OF LANDS
[G.O. Ms. No. 136, Transport (I.1), 23rd September 2011.]
No. II (2)/TRA/404(d)/2011.
Whereas in exercise of the powers conferred by clause
(1) of Article 258 of the Constitution of India, the President
has in the Ministry of Food, Agricultural, Community
Development and Co-operation (Department of Agriculture)
Notification No. 4 (1)/65, General II dated, on the 23rd April
1966, entrusted to the Government of Tamil Nadu with their
consent, the functions of the Central Government under the
Land Acquisition Act, 1894 (Central Act 1 of 1894) in relation
to acquisition of lands for the purpose of the Union in this
State.
And, whereas, it has already been decided that the
compensation should be paid from the public revenue.
Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by
sub-section 6 of the said Act, the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby declares that the lands specified in the schedule below
and measuring an extent of 12318.6 Sq.mtrs. are needed
to wit, for formation of a New parallel Broad Gauge Railway
Line between Chengalpattu and Arakkonam.
A plan of the lands is kept in the office of the Special
Tahsildar (Land Acquisition) Broad Gauge Railway Line,
Madurandagam and it may be inspected at any time during the
office hours.
