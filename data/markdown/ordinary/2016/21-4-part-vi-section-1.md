JUDICIAL NOTIFICATIONS
Adjournment of Court of Small Causes, Chennai for Summer Vacation, 2016
(Dis.No.860/2016)
No.VI(1)/200/2016.
Except as hereunder mentioned the Presidency Court of Small Causes, Chennai will remain closed for the Summer
Vacation 2016 from 01.05.2016  to 31.05.2016.
The Summer Vacation (for the year 2016) of this Court will be from 01.05.2016 to 31.05.2016. The First turn will be from
01.05.2016 to 16.05.2016 and the Second turn of the vacation will be from 17.5.2016 to 31.05.2016.
The Office of the Court of Small Causes, Chennai will function from Monday to Wednesday and Friday each week from  10
A.M to 4.30 P.M and on Thursday (i.e) court sitting day, from 10.A.M to 5.45 P.M. during the Summer Vacation 2016.
Thiru G. Vijyakumar, VII Judge, Court of Small Cause, Chennai will be the Vacation Judge for First turn from 01.05.2016 to
16.05.2016.
Tmt. C. Umamageshwari, IX Judge, Court of Small Cause, Chennai will be the Vacation Judge for Second turn from 17.05.2016
to 31.05.2016.
Thiru R. Karl Marx, XVI Judge, Court of Small cause, Chennai will be the Additional Vacation Judge for First turn from 01.05.2016
to 16.05.2016. The Additional Vacation Judge will deal with Rent Control Original petitions.
Tmt. L. Maheswari, X Judge, Court of Small Cause, Chennai will be the Additional Vacation Judge For Second turn from
17.05.2016 to 31.05.2016. The Additional Vacation Judge will deal with Rent Control Original petitions.
Thiru G. Christian, Registrar, Court of Small Causes, Chennai will be the Vacation Officer for the period from 01.05.2016 to
31.05 2016 (First turn and Second turn).
The Filing Section will be functioning on every Wednesdays and the sitting of the Judges will be on every Thursdays of the
respective week during the Summer Vacation 2016. The Vacation judges unless otherwise notified will sit on all Thursday of every
week during the vacation, to dispose the emergent petitions. No fresh plaint petition, no Rent Control Appeals or other matters will
be received during the period of vacation, unless it is urgent and it is accompanied by an application duly supported by an Affidavit
showing the urgency.
The Copyist Section of the Court of Small Causes will be kept open from Monday to Friday each week and between 10.30.
A.M to 3.00 P.M for the purpose of receiving copy application and also for delivering the certified copies, which were already made
ready. Further no fresh certified copy will be made ready during summer vacation.
The Nazir Section will function from Monday to Friday each week and Process Application will be received between
10.30 A.M to 3.00 P.M on Wednesdays. Warrants will be issued only on Wednesdays and Thursdays of the respective Weeks,
which were already filed and made ready. Warrants which are being filed on Wednesday will be made ready and will be issued
only on the next day (i.e) Thursday. If the Warrants returned for reasons, (i.e, since Police Protection not available), the same will
be re-issued only on next Wednesday or Thursday, if the Petitioner/Decree holder wants to execute the Warrant and makes such
endorsement.
The Office of the Registrar will be kept Open from Monday to Friday each week, but the urgent matters will be received on
Wednesdays during the vacation and such matters will be posted before the Vacation Judges and the Additional Vacation Judges
for the hearing on the succeeding Thursday in the open court.
Money will be received in the Treasury Section on Wednesdays and Thursdays of every week and money will be paid out only
on Thursdays during the Vacation, when the Vacation Judges are holding courts and on orders made by the vacation Judges for
such payments.
With a view to avoid difficulty to the learned advocates/parties/petitioners and the staff members, Suit, Rent Control Original
Petition, Rent Control Appeal cases and the petitions of other proceedings which are to be filed on the re-opening day, will also
be received and returned. The case bundles will be made available for taking returns in the regular courts between 11.00 A.M. and
3.00 P.M on Thursday only.
Court of Small Causes,
  P. RAJAMANICKAM,
Chennai-104,
Chief Judge (FAC).
29th April 2016.
  G. CHRISTIAN,
     Registrar.
