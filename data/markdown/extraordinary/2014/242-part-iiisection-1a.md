In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (6) of Section 4 read with Section 303 of the
Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby makes the following Rules, namely:-
RULES
1. Short title, application and commencement.—(1) These rules may be called the Tamil
Nadu Town Panchayats (Norms for  Classification) Rules, 2014.
(2) They shall apply to all the Town Panchayats in the State.
(3) They shall come into force at once.
2. Definition.—In these rules, unless the context otherwise requires “Average Annual income”
means the average annual income of a Town Panchayat other than borrowings, time-barred
deposits, non-statutory and developmental grants of three preceeding financial years.
