The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied 
that the lands specified in the schedule below have to be 
acquired for industrial purpose to wit  for the formation 
of New Broad Gauge Railway line between Tindivanam 
and Thiruvannamalai and it having already been decided 
that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded 
for the  lands is to be paid out of the lands controlled 
or managed by District Collector, Viluppuram the following 
notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the
Tamil Nadu Acquisition of  Land for Industrial Purposes 
Act, 1997.
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the 
Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purpose Act.1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu 
hereby acquires the land / lands specified in the schedule 
below and measuring 2.89.5 Hect. of Dry Lands, 3.45.0  Hect. 
of Wet Lands  to the same,  a  little more or less needed 
for Industrial Purpose, to wit for the formation of new Broad 
Gauge Railway line between Tindivanam and Thiruvannamalai. 
The plan of the lands  are  kept in the Collector’s Office, 
Viluppuram and in the office of the Sub Collector, Tindivanam, 
Viluppuram District and may be inspected at any time during 
office hours. 
Viluppuram District,  Tindivanam Taluk, 14. Vizhukkam Village.
