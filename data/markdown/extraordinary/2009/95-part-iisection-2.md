TRANSPORT  DEPARTMENT
ACQUISITION OF LANDS
[G.O. Ms. No. 54, Transport (I-2), 6th April 2009.]
No. II(2)/TRA/152(d)/2009.
The Government of Tamil  Nadu having been satisfied
that the lands specified in the Schedule below have to be
acquired for a public purpose, and is having already been
decided that the entire amount of compensation to be
awarded for the lands is to be paid by the Postal Department,
Government of India, out of the funds controlled and managed
by Postal Department, the following Declaration is issued
under Section 6 of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894
(Central Act I of 1894).
DECLARATION
 Under Section 6 of the Land Acquisition  Act,
1894 (Central Act I of 1894), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby declares that  the lands Specified in the
Schedule below and measuring 0.40.5 hectare, be the same,
a little more or less are needed for a public purpose,
to 
wit 
for 
the 
construction 
of 
New 
Building
for the Head Post Office, Perambalur at Perambular Taluk
and District.
The plan of the lands is kept in the Office of the  Revenue
Divisional Officer,  Perambalur and may be inspected
at any time during office hours.
SCHEDULE
Perambalur District, Perambalur Taluk,
No. 7. Perambalur (South)  Village.
Government, dry, S.F. No. 145-1B, belongs to Ekambaram,
son of Lakshmana Mudaliar, bounded on the north by
Perambalur (North), S.F.No. 334 (B.T.Road), east and south
by S.F.No. 145-2C1 and west by S.F.No. 145-1A—0.02.0
hectare.
Government, dry, S.F. No. 145-2C1, belongs to
Ekambaram, son of Lakshmana Mudaliar, bounded on the
north by Perambalur (North) Village, S.F.No. 334 (B.T.Road),
east by S.F.Nos. 145-2C2 and 145-5A1A, south by
S.F.No. 146 and west by S.F.Nos. 145-1A, 145-1B and
145-3C—0.36.0 hectare.
Government, dry, S.F. No. 145-3C, belongs to
Ekambaram, son of Lakshmana Mudaliar, bounded on the
North and east by S.F.No. 145-2C1, south by S.F.No. 146
and west by S.F.No. 145-1A—0.02.5 hectare.
Total—0.40.5  hectare.
