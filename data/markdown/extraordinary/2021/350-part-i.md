Order:- No. 312, Home (Courts-IV) 6th August 2021
In exercise of the powers conferred by rule 5 of the Law Oﬃ  cers of High Court of Madras and its Bench 
at Madurai (Appointment) Rules, 2017 and in supersession of the orders issued in the Government Order 
second read above, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints Tvl. V. Krishnamurthy and Amit Anand Tiwari, Advocates, 
as the Additional Advocates General of Tamil Nadu.
2. The Additional Advocates General of Tamil Nadu will look after the cases pertaining to the State of Tamil Nadu 
before the Supreme Court of India, New Delhi.
(By Order  of the Governor)
 
S.K. PRABAKAR,
