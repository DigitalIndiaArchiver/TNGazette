GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
Final closing and cancellation of Registration of certain Industrial Co-operative Societies.
Ind. No. 885. The Five Star Match Workers Industrial Co-operative Society Limited.
(L.F. 641/ICA/87.)
No. VI(1)/73/2010.
In the circumstances stated by the Assistant Director of Industries and Commerce (Industrial Co-operatives)/Official
Liquidator of the Five Star Match Workers Industrial Co-operative Society Limited in his final closure proposals dated
7th December 2009 and in exercise of the powers delegated under Section 140(1) of Tamil Nadu Co-operatives
Societies Act, 1983, the registration of the Five Star Match Workers Industrial Co-operative Society Limited Ind. No. 885
is hereby cancelled and the affairs are finally closed with effect from 8th December 2009.
Ind.No. 3/VNR. The Virudhunagar District Backward Classes Auto Drivers Industrial Co-operative Society Limited.
(L.F. 1084/ICA/03.)
No. VI(1)/74/2010.
In the circumstances stated by the Deputy Director of Industries and Commerce (Industrial Co-operatives)/Official
Liquidator of the Virudhunagar District Backward Classes Auto Drivers Industrial Co-operative Society Limited in his final
closure proposals dated 29th November 2009 and in exercise of the powers delegated under Section 140(1) of Tamil
Nadu Co-operative Societies Act, 1983, the registration of the Virudhunagar District Backward Classes Auto Drivers
Industrial Co-operative Society Limited Ind. No. 3/VNR is hereby cancelled and the affairs are finally closed with effect
from 8th December 2009.
Ind. No. 1091. The Musiri Small Match Producers Service Industrial Co-operative Society Limited.
(L.F. 656/ICA/87.)
No. VI(1)/75/2010.
In the circumstances stated by the Assistant Director of Industries and Commerce (Industrial Co-operatives)/Official
Liquidator of the Musiri Small Match Producers Service Industrial Co-operative Society Limited in his final closure
proposals dated 5th December 2009 and in exercise of the powers delegated under Section 140(1) of Tamil Nadu
Co-operative Societies Act, 1983, the registration of the Musiri Small Match Producers Service Industrial Co-operative
Society Limited Ind. No. 1091 is hereby cancelled and the affairs are finally closed with effect from 9th December 2009.
