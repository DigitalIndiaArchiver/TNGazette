No.I/PLAR/7/2017.—On completion of six years of tenure, Dr. S. Paneerselvam,
Ceases to hold office as Member, Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission on the
afternoon of 23-2-2017 without prejudice to the criminal case filed on 13-10-2011 by
the Director of Vigilance and Anti-Corruption in Cr.No. 22/2011 U/S 120(B) IPC,
13(2) r/w 13(1)(d), 15 of the Prevention of Corruption Act, 1988 and Sections
175 & 186 IPC and without prejudice to the Writ Petition filed by him in
W.P. No. 24517 of 2011, pending before the Hon’ble High Court of Madras.
