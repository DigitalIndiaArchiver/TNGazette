DRAFT AMENDMENT TO THE TABLE UNDER RULE 132 OF TAMIL NADU MOTOR VEHICLES
RULES, 1989
[G.O. Ms. No. 276, Home (Transport-VI), 31st March 2012.]
No. SRO A-11(a)/2012.
The following draft of amendment to the Tamil Nadu Motor Vehicles Rules, 1989 which it is proposed to make in exercise of the
powers conferred by sections 65 and 211  of the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988 (Central Act 59 of 1988) is hereby published for the
information of all persons likely to be affected thereby, as required by sub-section (1) of section 212 of the said Act.
(2) Notice is hereby given that the draft amendment will be taken into consideration on or after the expiry of seven days from the
date of publication of this notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary and that any objection or suggestion which
may be received from any person with respect thereto before the expiry of the aforesaid period will be considered by the Government
of Tamil Nadu.
(3) Objection or suggestion, if any, should be addressed in duplicate to the Principal Secretary to Government,
Home Department, Chennai-9.
