DTP  IV-2 Ex. (135)—1
An Act further to amend the Tamil Nadu Co-operative
Societies  Act, 1983.
BE it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the
Sixty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows:—
1. (1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies
(Second Amendment) Act, 2012.
(2) It shall come into force at once.
2. In section 89 of the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act, 1983, in
the proviso to sub-section (1), for the expression “eleven years”, the expression
“eleven years and six months” shall be substituted.
