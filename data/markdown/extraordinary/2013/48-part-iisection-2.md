WITHDRAWAL NOTIFICATION UNDER SECTION 48 OF
THE LAND ACQUISITION ACT 1894
[Letter No.1050/PD&SI (S.I)/2013.]
Under Section 48 of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894
(Central Act I of 1894) the Government of Tamil Nadu hereby
withdraws the land specified in the Schedule below and
measuring 1658.5 square feet for which declaration has
been approved in G.O. Ms. No. 232, Planning, Development
and Special Initiatives (SI) Department, dated 28-11-2011
and published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette
Extraordinary, Issue No. 446 dated 28-11-2011 to wit, for the
implementation of Chennai Metro Rail Project.
