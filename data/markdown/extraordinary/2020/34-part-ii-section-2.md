Under clause (f) of Section 16 of the Tamil Nadu Transparency in Tenders Act, 1998 (Tamil Nadu Act 43 of 
1998), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notifies to purchase a set of following three items of Stainless Steel 
vessels at the rate of Rs.250/-(Rupees Two Hundred and Fifty only) per set  from M/s. Steel Authority of  India 
Limited  (SAIL), Central Marketing  Organisation at Salem so as to supply for all the 18,893 Sri Lankan Tamil 
families accommodated in the refugee camps, namely:- 
(a)	 9 inch metal plate	
1 Number
(b)	 11 inch cook and serve tope vessel	
1 Number
(c) 	6 inch water tumbler (approx 180 to 200ml)	
1 Number
