WHEREAS, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission appointed the Block Development Oﬃ  cers (Block Panchayat) 
as the Panchayat Electoral Registration Oﬃ  cers for performing the functions speciﬁ ed in sub-section (2) of Section 30 of 
the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994) read with rule 7 and 14 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats 
(Elections) Rules, 1995 for the purposes of preparing and publishing panchayat electoral rolls in respect of wards of panchayats 
lying within their respective jurisdiction in connection with the conduct of Elections to Panchayats vide Tamil Nadu State 
Election Commission's Statutory Order No.3/2019/TNSEC/PE-1, dated 10.4.2019 and the same has been published in the 
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, issue No. 172, Part VI--Section 2, Dated the 12th April 2019 vide notiﬁ cation 
No. VI(2)/41(a-3)/2019; 
WHEREAS, in the Government Orders 2nd read above, the Government have bifurcated the Villupuram District into 
Villupuram and Kallakurichi Districts; the Tirunelveli District into Tirunelveli and Tenkasi Districts; the Kancheepuram District 
into Kancheepuram and Chengalpattu Districts and trifurcated the Vellore District into Vellore, Tirupathur and Ranipet Districts 
respectively;


2

WHEREAS, consequent on the bifurcation of Kancheepuram, Villupuram and  Tirunelveli and trifurcation of Vellore 
Districts, the Government in the Government Orders 3rd and 4th read above, have issued orders constituting the District 
Panchayats for the newly constituted Districts; 
AND WHEREAS, the Hon'ble Supreme Court of India in its Order 5th read above granted extension of time to delimit 
the nine newly constituted districts and directed to complete the entire process including publication/notiﬁ cation of election 
schedule and the result thereof before 15th September, 2021. 
NOW, THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 30 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats 
Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994) read with rule 7 and 14 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Elections) Rules, 1995, the 
Tamil Nadu State Election Commission hereby designates the Block Development Oﬃ  cers (Block Panchayat) of ﬁ ve newly 
constituted districts viz. Chengalpattu, Kallakurichi, Tenkasi, Ranipet and Tirupathur Districts as the Panchayat Electoral 
Registration Oﬃ  cers for the purposes of preparing and publishing panchayat electoral rolls in respect of wards of panchayats 
lying within their respective jurisdiction in connection with the conduct of Elections to panchayats.
(By order of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission.)
