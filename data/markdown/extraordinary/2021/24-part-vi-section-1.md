NOTICE UNDER SECTION 15(1) OF TAMIL NADU HIGHWAYS ACT, 2001(TN ACT 34 OF 2002) READ WITH THE 
TAMIL NADU LAND ACQUISITION LAWS (REVIVAL OF OPERATION, AMENDMENT AND VALIDATION) ACT, 2019. 
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that the lands speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below are acquired 
under Chennai Peripheral Ring Road Project for Highways Department purpose to wit, for formation of road for providing 
connectivity from Ennore Port to Mahabalipuram (from K.M.24+491 to K.M.29+533) at No.90 Kannigaipair (Krishnapuram 
Kandigai) Village, Block-II, Uthukottai Taluk, Tiruvallur District and it having already been decided that the entire 
amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands to be paid out of the funds controlled and managed by the 
Highways Department and after having considering the cause shown by the owners or other persons having interest 
on such lands, as the case may be, do hereby publish the following notice under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the 
Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) read with the Tamil Nadu Land Acquisition Laws (Revival 
of Operation, Amendment and Validation)  Act, 2019.
NOTICE
 
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) read 
with the Tamil Nadu Land Acquisition Laws (Revival of Operation, Amendment and Validation) Act, 2019, the Governor 
of Tamil Nadu hereby notiﬁ es that the Wet and Manavari patta lands speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below and measuring a 
total extent of 97,121 Sq.Mtrs. (9.71.21 Hectares) are acquired in No.90 Kannigaipair (Krishnapuram Kandigai) Village, 
Block-II, Uthukottai Taluk, Tiruvallur  District to the same, a little more or less, needed for the purpose to wit, for 
formation of road for providing connectivity from Ennore Port to Mahabalipuram (from K.M.24+491 to K.M.29+533) under 
Chennai Peripheral Ring Road Project by the Highways Department. 
The Plan of the lands under acquisition is kept in the office of the Special District Revenue Officer 
(Land Acquisition), Chennai Peripheral Ring Road Project, Chennai-600 058 and in the oﬃ  ce of the Special Tahsildar 
(Land Acquisition), Chennai Peripheral Ring Road Project, Unit-II, Thatchur at Tiruvallur and may be inspected at any 
time during the oﬃ  ce hours.
