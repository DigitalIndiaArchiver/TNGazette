APPOINTMENT OF ELECTORAL REGISTRATION OFFICER FOR THE PRE-DELIMITED ASSEMBLY
CONSTITUENCY IN TAMIL NADU.
No. SRO G-48/2009.
The following Notification of the Election Commission of India, Nirvachan Sadan, Ashoka Road, New Delhi-110 001,
dated 23rd December, 2009 [2, Pausa, 1931 (Saka)] is published:—
NOTIFICATION
No. 429/TN/2009-(2).—In Pursuance of the provisions sub-section (1) of the Section 13 (B) of the Representation of
the People Act, 1950 (43 of 1950), the Election Commission of India hereby designates in consultation with the
Government of Tamil Nadu, the officer of the Government specified in column (2) of the Table below as the
Electoral Registration Officer of the pre-delimited Assembly Constituency as specified in column (1) of the said table
against such officer.
The appointment of the Electoral Registration Officer in respect of 83-Pennagaram Assembly Constituency shall
cease to be effective on completion of process of election in the constituency concerned.
