NOTIFICATION CALLING UPON ALL ASSEMBLY CONSTITUENCIES IN THE
STATE OF TAMIL NADU TO ELECT MEMBERS.
Secretariat, March 19, 2011.
No. SRO D-2/2011.
WHEREAS, in accordance with the provisions of clause (1)  of Article 172 of the Constitution of India, the existing Legislative
Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu, unless sooner dissolved, shall continue up to and including the 16th May, 2011, and shall
thereafter stand dissolved on the expiration of its duration; and
Whereas, in terms of sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Representation of the Pepole Act, 1951 (43 of 1951), a General
Election has to be held for the purpose of constituting a new Legislative Assembly of the State;
Now, therefore, as recommended by the Election Commission of India in pursuance of sub-section (2) of Section 15 of the
said Act, the Governor of the State of Tamil Nadu, is hereby pleased to call upon all Assembly Constituencies in the State, to elect
members to the Legislative Assembly of the State, in accordance with the provisions of the said Act and of the rules and orders
made thereunder.
(By Order of the Governor)
S. MALATHI,
Chief Secretary.
