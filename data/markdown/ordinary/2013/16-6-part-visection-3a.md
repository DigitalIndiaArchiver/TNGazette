GENERAL  NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI.
Taking over of the Vadasery Kuthba Pallivasal, Nagercoil, Kanyakumari District under the Direct Management
(Rc. No. 12158/07/B1/KK)
No. VI-3(a)/107/2013.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the  Wakf Act 1995, the Tamil Nadu
Wakf Board in its Resolution in Item No. 12/13 in R.C.No. 12158/07/B1/Tnv, dated 05-03-2013 has ordered to take
over the Management of Vadasery Kuthba Pallivasal, Nagercoil, Kanyakumari District under the direct control of the
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of one year from the date of Board’s order and to appoint the Wakf Inspector,
Kanyakumari as its Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,
F. ABDUL RAZICK,
19th April 2013.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.

In pursuance of the said order and as directed therin, further notice is hereby given that a meeting of the equity
shareholders of the said company will be held at the registered office of the Company “Kil Kotagiri Estate Post Box 2,
Kil Kotagiri Post, Nilgiris-643 216” on Monday the 13th day of May 2013 at 11.00 A.M. at which time and place the said
equity shareholders are requested to attend.
Copies of the said scheme of Amalgamation and of the statement under Section 393 of the Companies Act, 1956 can
be had free of charge at the Registered Office of the company “Kil Kotagiri Estate, Post Box 2, Kil Kotagiri Post,
Nilgiris-643 216” or at the office of its advocates M/s. V. Ramakrishnan, Mekala Ganapathy, G. Sivashankaran and
S. Sathyaganesh at No. 9/2, First Cross Street, Seethammal Colony, Alwarpet, Chennai-600 018.
Persons entitled to attend and vote at the meeting may vote in person or by proxy, provided that all proxies in the
prescribed form are deposited at the Registered Office of the company “Kil Kotagiri Estate, Post Box 2, Kil Kotagiri Post,
Nilgiris-643 216” not later than 48 hours before the meeting. The Quorum for the meeting shall be 5 members present
in person or by proxy. Forms of proxy can be had at the Registered Office of the Company.
The Court has appointed Mr J. Ashok Kumar, Advocate, Coimbatore and failing him Mrs. Suryakala Selvaraj, Advocate,
Coimbatore as Chairman of the meeting. The above mentioned Scheme of Amalgamation, if approved by the meeting,
will be subject to the subsequent approval of the Court.
