(2) in rule 56-B, for sub-rule (2), the following sub-rule shall be substituted, namely:- 
 
"(2) The fee payable for the examination and testing of pressure plant or vessel or  water sealed gas holder shall 
be as follows:- 
(i)  ` 800/-(Rupees eight hundred only) per plant or vessel irrespective of its size or capacity, for the external 
examination; 
(ii) ` 2000/- (Rupees two thousand only) per plant or vessel upto 4 kgf/sq.cm(g) for the Hydrostatic test; 
(iii) ` 4000/- (Rupees four thousand only) per plant or vessel above 4 kgf/sq.cm(g) for the Hydrostatic test; 
(iv) ` 2000/- (Rupees two thousand only) per plant or vessel irrespective of the size or capacity, for the internal 
examination; and 
(v) ` 8000/- (Rupees eight thousand only) per plant or vessel irrespective of the size or capacity, for the non-
destructive ultrasonic thickness gauging test.";

" ` 200/- (Rupees two hundred only)” shall be substituted;

two hundred only)“ shall be substituted ; and

" ` 200/- (Rupees two hundred only)”, shall be substituted.
