LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY SECRETARIAT 
VACATION OF SEAT IN TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY DUE TO RESIGNATION OF
THIRU H. VASANTHA KUMAR, MEMBER OF THE TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY. 
[Letter No. 6157/2019-1, Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Secretariat (B-III), 29th May, 2019, 
Vaikasi 15, Vikari, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2050.] 
No.II(1)/LAS/9(d)/2019. 
Whereas, Thiru H. Vasantha Kumar, a Member of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly, representing the 227. Nanguneri 
Assembly constituency, in his letter dated 29th May, 2019 addressed to me as Speaker of the Tamil Nadu Legislative 
Assembly, has resigned his seat as Member of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly with eﬀ ect from 29th May, 2019. 
Now, therefore, I, P. DHANAPAL, Speaker of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly hereby accept the resignation 
of Thiru. H. Vasantha Kumar, as envisaged under Rule 22 of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Rules and is in 
accordance with Rule 21 of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Rules. Under Article 190 (3)(b) of the Constitution of 
India, his seat has, therefore, become vacant with eﬀ ect from 29th May, 2019.
