NOTICE UNDER SECTION 3(1) OF TAMIL NADU ACQUISITION OF LAND FOR INDUSTRIAL PURPOSES 
ACT, 1997 READ WITH THE TAMIL NADU LAND ACQUISITION LAWS (REVIVAL OF OPERATION, 
AMENDMENT AND VALIDATION) ACT, 2019
No. VI(1)/12(d)/2022.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that the land/lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule 
below to be acquired for industrial purpose to wit to conversion of existing meter gauge into broad 
gauge in between Tiruvarur and Mayiladuthurai and it having already been decided that the amount of 
compensation to be awarded for the land/lands is/are to be paid out of the funds controlled or managed 
by Deputy Chief Engineer, Gauge conversion/II, Southern Railway, Thiruchirapalli. The following notice is 
issued under sub-section (1) of section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes 
Act, 1997. (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999)
NOTICE
 
Under sub-section (1) of section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes 
Act, 1997. (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the land/lands 
speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below measuring 12300 Sq.mtrs., to the same, a little more or less needed 
for industrial purpose wit for conversion of existing meter gauge into broad gauge in between Tiruvarur 
and Mayiladuthurai.
 The plan of lands are kept in the oﬃ  ce of the District Revenue oﬃ  cer Tiruvarur and may be 
inspected at any time during oﬃ  ce hours.
