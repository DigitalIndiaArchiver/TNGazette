TAMIL NADU ELECTRICITY REGULATORY COMMISSION, CHENNAI
Amendment to the Tamil Nadu Electricity Distribution Code
(Notification No.TNERC/DC/8-20.)
No.VI(2)/237/2014.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 181 read with Section 46 of the Electricity Act, 2003 (Central Act 36 of 2003)
and all other powers enabling it in this behalf, the Tamil Nadu Electricity Regulatory Commission hereby makes the following
amendment to the Tamil Nadu Electricity Distribution Code.
AMENDMENT
In the said Code, in Regulation 39, in sub-regulation (2), in clause (ii), for the expression “D = Number of days of billing cycle
period as specified  in regulation 8(3) of the Supply Code/proposed period of use whichever is less”  the following expression shall
be substituted, namely:—
“D = Number of days of billing cycle period as specified  in regulation 8(1) of the Supply Code/proposed period of use
whichever is less”.
(By order of the Tamil Nadu Electricity Regulatory Commission)
Chennai-600 008,
S. GUNASEKARAN,
 11th September 2014.
Secretary,
 Tamil Nadu Electricity
Regulatory Commission.
