Under clause (f) of Section 16 of the Tamil Nadu Transparency in Tenders Act, 1998
(Tamil Nadu Act 43 of 1998), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notifies the entrustment of work of
establishing New Ice-Cream plant of 10,000 litre per shift in Madurai Dairy premises at an estimated cost
of Rs. 43.95 Crore, on turnkey basis, to Messrs. National Dairy Development Board, Anand in Guajarat.
