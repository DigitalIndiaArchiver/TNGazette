REVENUE DEPARTMENT
Amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Civil Service
[G.O. Ms. No. 267, Revenue (Ser-1), 20th June 2014, Aani 6, Jaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2045.]
No. SRO B-38/2014.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Government of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Civil Service
(Section-5 of Part III A in Volume II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1969).
2. The amendment hereby made shall come into force on 20-06-2014.
AMENDMENT
In the said Special Rules, in the Annexure referred to in sub-rule (a) of rule 3, for the entries in item (i) in column (2)
against the entry “Recruitment by Transfer” in column (1) thereof, the following entries shall be substituted, namely:-
“Must have a minimum of one year of service before superannuation as on the 31st December of the preceding year
in which the selection for appointment is made”.
GAGANDEEP SINGH BEDI,
Secretary to Government.

No. SRO B-39/2014.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the General Rules in Part II of  the Tamil Nadu State and
Subordinate Services Rules (Volume I of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1987).
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 18th August, 2009.
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules,-
In rule 19, in the Explanation, for item (i), the following item shall be substitued, namely:—
"(i) In cases where the Special Rules prescribe a diploma or a degree or a post-graduate degree as a  qualification
for appointment, then,-
(a) a diploma obtained, after completion of S.S.LC. or Higher Secondary; [10+3 (3 years Diploma)] or
[10+2+2 (Lateral Entry)]
(b) a degree obtained, after completion  of S.S.L.C. and Higher Secondary; (10+2+3 or more)
(c) a post-graduate degree obtained, after completion of S.S.L.C., Higher Secondary and a degree; (10+2+3+2 or 3)
from any University or institution, recognized by the University Grants Commission mentioned in Schedule II to this part
shall be recognized as the qualification."
P.W.C. DAVIDAR,
Principal Secretary to Government.
