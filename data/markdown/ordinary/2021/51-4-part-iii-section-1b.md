HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE DEPARTMENT
Amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Public Health Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 278, Health and Family Welfare (L2), 9th June 2021, Vaikasi 26, Pilava, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2052.]
No. SRO B-61/2021.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, 
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Public Health 
Service (Section 24 in Volume II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1969).
Amendment.
In the said Rules, in rule 4, in sub-rule (1), in the TABLE, under the heading Class-I in column (1),-
for the entries "(i) M.Sc., degree in Statistics or Bio-Statistics or Demography  "in Column (3) against the entry "(b) Direct 
recruitment" in Column (2) and against the entries "4 Statistical Officer" and "4A Assistant Director (State Bureau of Health 
Intelligence)" in Column (1) thereof the following entries shall be substituted namely:-
"(1) M.Sc., degree in Statistics or Bio-Statistics".
J. RADHAKRISHNAN, 
Principal Secretary to Government.
