No.II(2)/HO/224/2019.—In exercise of the powers 
conferred by the first paragraph of Section 4 of the 
Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873), 
the Governor of Tamil Nadu, after consultation with the High 
Court, Madras hereby alters the number of District Munsifs 
to be appointed under the said Act for Erode District as 
NINE with effect from the date on which the District Munsif 
assumes charge of the District Munsif's Court at Perundurai.

[G.O. Ms. No. 134, Home (Cts-III), 1st March 2019, ñ£C 17,

Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873), 
the Governor of Tamil Nadu, after consultation with the 
High Court, Madras, hereby fixes the number of District 
Munsifs to be appointed to the District Munsif Court, at 
Perundurai as ONE with effect on and from the date of 
constitution of separate District Munsif Court at Perundurai in 
Erode District by converting the District Munisif-cum-Judicial 
Magistrate Court, Perundurai.

conferred by Section 10 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 
1873 (Central Act III of 1873), the Governor of Tamil Nadu 
hereby directs that in the District of Erode the Subordinate 
Judge's Court at Perundurai shall have and exercise original 
jurisdiction over the District Munsif's Court at Perundurai with 
effect on and from the date on which the District Munsif 
assumes charge of the District Munsif's Court at Perundurai.

conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 10 of the Tamil Nadu 
Town and Country Planning Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35
