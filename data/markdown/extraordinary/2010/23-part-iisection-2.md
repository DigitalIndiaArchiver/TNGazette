MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION AND WATER SUPPLY DEPARTMENT
REMOVAL OF CHAIRMAN OF KOTTAIYUR TOWN PANCHAYAT, SIVAGANGAI DISTRICT
UNDER THE TAMIL NADU  DISTRICT MUNICIPALITIES  ACT.
[G.O. (D) No. 33, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (TP-II), 29th January 2010,
Thai 16, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/68(b)/2010.
Whereas a written notice of intention to make a motion expressing want of confidence in
Thiru Suba. Vairavan, Chairman, Kottaiyur Town Panchayat, Sivagangai District signed by twelve Councillors
out of the sanctioned strength of the fifteen Councillors of the said council together with a copy of the
motion proposed to be made have been delivered to the Assistant Director of Town Panchayats, Sivagangai
Zone in person by three Councillors under the provisions of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920
(Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920);
 And, whereas the said motion expressing want of confidence in Thiru Suba. Vairavan, Chairman of the said
Town Panchayat has been carried out with the support of twelve Councillors out of the sanctioned strength
of the fifteen councillors in the meeting convened by the Assistant Director of Town Panchayats, Sivagangai
Zone, on the 8th September 2009, after following the procedures specified in Section 40-A of the said Act;
 Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (12) of Section 40-A of the
Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby removes Thiru Suba. Vairavan, Chairman, Kottaiyur Town Panchayat, Sivagangai District from the
office of Chairman of the said Town Panchayat.
  NIRANJAN MARDI,
Secretary to Government.
