6. Probation.- Every person appointed to the post by direct recruitment shall, from the date on which he joins duty, 
be on probation for a total period of two years on duty within a continuous period of three years.
7. Preparation of annual list of approved candidates.- (1) For the purpose of drawing up of annual list of 
approved candidates for appointment to the post of District Diﬀ erently Abled Welfare Oﬃ  cer by promotion, the crucial 
date on which the candidate should be qualiﬁ ed shall be the 1st June of every year.
 (2) For the purpose of drawing up of annual list of approved candidates for appointment to the posts of Skilled 
Assistant Grade-I by promotion, the crucial date on which the candidate should be qualiﬁ ed shall be the 1st July of 
every year.
 (3) For the purpose of drawing up of annual list of approved candidates for appointment to the posts of Junior 
Rehabilitation Oﬃ  cer by  promotion, the crucial date on which the candidate should be qualiﬁ ed shall be the 1st September 
of every year.

8. Test.- (1) Every person appointed to the post of Junior Rehabilitation Oﬃ  cer by direct recruitment shall pass the 
following tests within the period of probation:-
 
(a) Government Oﬃ  ce Manual Test;
 
(b) Account Test for Sub-Ordinate Oﬃ  cers Part I and II; and
 
(c) Deputy Inspector Test.
 Provided that if the person appointed to the post of Junior Rehabilitation Oﬃ  cer does not pass the test within the 
period of probation, the next increment will be sanctioned notionally and the monetary beneﬁ ts shall be sanctioned with 
eﬀ ect from the date of passing the tests.
9. Reservation of appointment.- (1) The provisions relating to reservation of appointments (contained in 
Section 27 of the Tamil Nadu Government Servants (Conditions of Service) Act, 2016 (Tamil Nadu Act 14 of 2016) 
shall apply for appointment to the categories of post for which candidates are appointed by direct recruitment where 
the cadre strength of each category is more than one.
