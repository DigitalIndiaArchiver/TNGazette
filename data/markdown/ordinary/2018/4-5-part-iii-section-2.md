 TAMIL NADU CORPORATION FOR DEVELOPMENT OF WOMEN LIMITED. 
(Section 57 of Aadhaar Act 2016 effect from 14-09-2016 Permits the use of Aadhaar Number for establishing the 
identity of an individual for any purpose pursuant to any law or any contract to this effect) 
(Ref. No. 2923/2016/TNSRLM - ATWS)
No. SRO C-1/2018.
   Aadhaar Requirement: Amma Two Wheeler Scheme of Government of Tamil Nadu.  
1.   Tamil Nadu Corporation for Development of Women Limited has decided to use Aadhaar Number of the beneﬁ ciaries
for the new scheme of Government of Tamil Nadu called "Amma Two Wheeler Scheme" (ATWS). 
2.   Beneﬁ ciaries who are in possession of Aadhaar Card, shall register the Aadhaar Number towards their enrollment 
to the newly implemented scheme called as "Amma Two Wheeler Scheme" (ATWS).   
 
3.  The use of Aadhaar of the beneﬁ ciaries for the new scheme implemented by Tamil Nadu Corporation for 
Development of Women Limited will result in accuracy of the beneﬁ ciary's details. This will also help in 
ascertaining identities of the beneﬁ ciaries at the grass root level.
4.  Applicants who are not yet enrolled for Aadhaar are hereby requested to apply in any Aadhaar enrolment 
Centre/Permanent Enrollment Centre to get enrolled for Aadhaar.     
 
5.  Those beneﬁ ciaries who have enrolled for Aadhaar and have not received Aadhaar should register the 28 digit 
Aadhaar enrolment ID printed on the Aadhaar enrolment slip.  
6.  Department of Rural Development, Government of Tamil Nadu through Tamil Nadu Corporation for Development 
of Women Limited (TNCDW Ltd.) will inorganically seed the Aadhaar number for its beneﬁ ciaries.
7.  The above provisions shall be applicable in all Districts of Tamil Nadu.
