Amendments to the Special Rules for Tamil Nadu Police Service.

No. SRO B-41/2011.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for Tamil Nadu Police Service
(Section 22 in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1986).
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules,—
1. In rule 2, in sub-rule (i), in the tabular column, under the heading “class II”, for the entries in column (2) against the
entry “category (3) Assistant Commandant in the Tamil Nadu Special Police” in column (1),  the following entries shall be
substituted, namely:—
“Recruitment by transfer from Adjutant Inspectors and inspectors in General Category (Regular Line) in the Tamil Nadu
Special Police Subordinate Service”;
2. In rule 4, for sub-rule (c), the following sub-rule shall be substituted, narmely:—
“(c) Except in very special circumstances, no Inspector Adjutant or Inspector in General Category (Regular Line) of
the Tamil Nadu Special Police shall be eligible for appointment as Assistant Commandant and no Inspector Adjutant or
Inspector of Tamil Nadu Special Police Subordinate Service shall be eligible for appointment as Assistant Commandant (Small
Arms), as the case may be, by recruitment by transfer unless—
(i)  he has completed five years of service in the respective category, officiating or permanent; and
(ii)  he is a holder of SSLC, eligible for college courses of study.”.
Amendments to the Tamil Nadu Special Police Subordinate Service Rules.
[G.O. Ms. No. 734, Home (Police-1), 8th November 2011, äŠðC 22, F¼õœÀõ˜ Ý‡´-2042.]
No. SRO B-42/2011.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Sections 8 and 10  of the  Tamil Nadu District Police Act, 1859
(Central Act XXIV of 1859) and Sections 9 and 11 of the Chennai City Police Act, 1888 (Tamil Nadu Act III of 1888)
read with the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following
amendments to the Tamil Nadu Special Police Subordinate Service Rules, 1978 (Section 44 in Volume III of the
Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1986).
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules, in rule 7, in sub-rule (a), in the TABLE, for the entries in second column against the entry “(i) Inspector
Adjutant” in the first column, the following entries shall be substituted, namely:—
“1.  Promotion from Inspectors, General category (Regular Line);
(or)
2.   If no qualified and suitable Inspector from General category (Regular Line) is available, direct recruitment or
recruitment by transfer from any other service.”.
RAMESHRAM MISHRA,
