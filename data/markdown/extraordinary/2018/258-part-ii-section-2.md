Under clause (e) of Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Admission in Professional Educational Institutions 
Act, 2006 (Tamil Nadu Act 3 of 2007), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notiﬁ es the under graduate 
courses in the Indian System of Medicine namely, Ayurveda (Bachelor of Ayurveda Medicine and 
Surgery [B.A.M.S]), Yoga and Naturopathy (Bachelor of Naturopathy and Yogic Sciences [B.N.Y.S]), 
Unani (Bachelor of Unani Medicine and Surgery [B.U.M.S]), Siddha (Bachelor of Siddha Medicine 
and Surgery [B.S.M.S]) and Homeopathy (Bachelor of Homoeopathy Medicine and Surgery [B.H.M.S]) 
as professional educational courses.
