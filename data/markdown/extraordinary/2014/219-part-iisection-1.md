HOME, PROHIBITION AND EXCISE DEPARTMENT
AMENDMENT TO NOTIFICATION UNDER TAMIL NADU PROHIBITION ACT
[G.O. Ms. No. 30, Home, Prohibition and Excise (III), 31st October 2014.]
No. II(1)/HOPE/36(c)/2014.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 18-B of the Tamil Nadu  Prohibition  Act, 1937
(Tamil Nadu  Act  X  of  1937),  the  Governor  of  Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Prohibition
and Excise Department Notification No.II(1)/PE/239(d)/81, dated the 1st May 1981, published at Page 2 of
Part II - Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary,  dated the 1st May 1981, as subsequently
amended.
2. The amendments hereby made shall come into force on the 1st November, 2014.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Notification, in the TABLE, under the entry “Indian Made Foreign Spirits (brand names of which are specified
in the Annexure) other than sacramental wine” in column (1),-
(1)  under the heading “(i) ORDINARY BRANDS”, for the expression “which does not exceed Rs.478 per case in
respect of brands manufactured in the State”, the expression “which does not exceed Rs.588 per case in respect of
brands manufactured  in  the  State”  shall be substituted;
(2)   under the heading “(ii) MEDIUM BRANDS”, for the expression “between Rs.479 to Rs.547 per case in respect
of brands manufactured in the State”, the expression “between Rs.589 to Rs.657 per case in respect of brands manufactured
in the State”  shall be substituted; and
(3)   under the heading “(iii) PREMIUM BRANDS”,-
(a) in clause (a), for the expression “Rs.548 to Rs.2047 per case”, the expression “Rs.658 to Rs.2157 per case”
shall be substituted; and
     (b) in clause (b), for the expression “Rs.2048 and above per case”, the   expression “Rs.2158 and above per
case” shall be substituted.
APURVA VARMA,
Principal Secretary to Government.
