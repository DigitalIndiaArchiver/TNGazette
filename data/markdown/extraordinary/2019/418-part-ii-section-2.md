In exercise of the powers conferred in clause (a) under  sub-section (1) of section 34-A of the Tamil 
Nadu Societies Registration Act, 1975 (Act 27 of 1975), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints 
Tmt.P.V.Geetha, Assistant Inspector General of Registration, O/o DIG, Chennai., as Special Officer to manage 
the affairs of the South Indian Artistes Association, (Registration No.50/1952), Chennai, till the adjudication 
of election related disputes in the W.P.No.17583 of 2019 and others, that are pending on the file of the 
Hon’ble High Court or for a period of one year from 06.11.2019 as Special Officer subject to the outcome 
of said cases which ever is earlier.
