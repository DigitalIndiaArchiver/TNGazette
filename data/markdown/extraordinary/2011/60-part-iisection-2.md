FINANCE DEPARTMENT

APPOINTMENT OF CONFEDERATION OF INDIAN INDUSTRIES (CII) AS STATE CONSULTANT FOR
IMPLEMENTING “EMPLOYABLE SKILL TRAINING TO THE UNEMPLOYED EDUCATED YOUTH” SCHEME
UNDER TAMIL NADU TRANSPARENCY IN TENDERS ACT.
[G.O. No. 47, Finance (Salaries), 15th February 2011, Maasi 3, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No. II(2)/FIN/71(e)/2011.
Under clause (f) of Section 16 of the Tamil Nadu Transparency in Tenders Act, 1998
(Tamil Nadu Act 43 of 1998), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notifies the appointment of Confederation of
Indian Industries (CII) as State Consultant for the implementation of  “Employable Skill Training to the Unemployed
Educated Youth Scheme” of Labour and Employment Department.
K. SHANMUGAM,
 Principal Secretary to Government.
