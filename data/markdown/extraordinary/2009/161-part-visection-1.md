ACQUISITION OF LANDS
(D.Dis.H2/14313/09.)
No. VI(1)/181(a)/2009.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied
that the lands specified in the schedule below have to be
acquired for a public purpose and it having already been
decided that the entire amount of compensation to be
awarded for the lands is to be paid out of public revenues,
The following declaration is issued under section 6 of the
Land Acquisition Act,1894 (Central Act I of 1894).
DECLARATION
Under Section 6 of the Land Acquisition Act 1894 (Central
Act I of 1894) the Commissioner of land Administration,
Chepauk, Chennai 600 005  hereby declares that the lands
specified in the schedule below and measuring 2.65.5
hectares be the same a little more or less are needed for
public purpose  to wit for the formation of a new Tank Bund
and supply Channal, 13. Palaviduthi village, Kulithalai Taluk,
Karur Dist rict.
A plan of the land is kept in the office of the Revenue
Divisional Officer, Kulithalai and it may be inspected at any
time during office hours.
