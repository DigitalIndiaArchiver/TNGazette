 NOTIFICATIONS BY THE ELECTION COMMISSION OF INDIA
APPOINTMENT OF ASSISTANT RETURNING OFFICERS FOR PRESIDENTIAL ELECTION
No.  SRO G-19/2012.
The following Notification of the Election Commission of India, Nirvachan Sadan, Ashoka Road,
New Delhi-110 001, dated 14th June, 2012  [24 Jyaistha, 1934 (Saka)] is republished:—
479/3/2012(II).—In pursuance of sub-section (1) of section 3 of the Presidential and Vice-Presidential
Elections Act, 1952 (31 of 1952), the Election Commission of India, hereby appoints, in addition to the
Officers appointed vide the Commission’s notification No.479/3/2012(III),  dated 14th June, 2012, the
following Officers of the Rajya Sabha Secretariat, Parliament House, New Delhi, to be the Assistant
Returning Officers at Headquarters of the Returning Officer for the Presidential Election, 2012:
1. Shri S.K. Ganguli, Joint Secretary (G),
2. Shri Deepak Goyal, Joint Secretary (DG).
(By Order)
K.N. BHAR,
Secretary,
Election Commission of India.
PRAVEEN KUMAR,
Secretariat,
Chief Electoral Officer and
Chennai-600 009,
Principal Secretary to Government,
14th June, 2012.
