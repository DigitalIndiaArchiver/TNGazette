ACQUISITION OF LANDS
(Roc. No. S2/1566716/2021)
NOTICE UNDER SECTION 15(1) OF TAMIL NADU HIGHWAYS ACT, 2001 READ WITH THE TAMIL NADU LAND 
ACQUISITION LAWS (REVIVAL OF OPERATION, AMENDMENT AND VALIDATION) ACT, 2019.
No. VI(1)/38(b)/2022.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that the lands speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below have to be 
acquired for the purpose of Construction of Storm Water drain and Widening and Strengthening of Poonamallee – 
Kundrathur – Pallavaram Road from 2 lane to 6 lane, Essa Pallavaram Village, Pallavaram Taluk, Chengalpattu District 
and it have already been decided that the entire amount of Compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid 
out of  the funds controlled or managed by the Divisional Engineer (Highways), Construction and Maintenance, Chennai 
City Roads Division, Chennai-600 015 and having considered the cause shown by the owners or other persons having 
interest in the said lands ,as the case may do hereby publish the following notice under sub-section (1) of Section 15 
of Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34/2002).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act 2001, (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002), the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below measuring an extent of 988 Sq.Mtrs., 
required for Highways purpose i.e., to the same, a little more or less, required for Storm Water drain and Widening 
and Strengthening of Poonamallee – Kundrathur – Pallavaram Road from 2 lane to 6 lane, Essa Pallavaram Village, 
Pallavaram Taluk, Chengalpattu District.
The plan of the lands under acquisition, are kept in the Oﬃ  ce of the Special Tahsildar (L.A),  Inner  Ring  Road 
Scheme,  Chromepet, Chennai- 600 044, Chengalpattu District and may be inspected on any day during oﬃ  ce hours.
