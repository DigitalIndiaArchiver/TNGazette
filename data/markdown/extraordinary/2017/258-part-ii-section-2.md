NOTIFICATION
Under  sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997.
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby acquires the lands specified in the schedule  below
and 8.57.0 hectares to the same, a little more or less needed
for Industrial purpose, to wit, for  setting up SIPCOT Industrial
Complex, Maruthandapalli, Shoolagiri Taluk and Krishnagiri
District.
The Plan of the lands are kept in the office of the
Revenue Divisional Office, Krishnagiri and may  be inspected
at any time during the office hours.
