[Courts and Judges - District Judge - Thiru P. Dhanabal,
District Judge of Tamil Nadu State Judicial Service - Deputation
from Tamil Nadu State Judicial Service to the Puducherry
Judicial Service - Orders - Issued.]
The following Government order is published:—
[G.O. (D) No. 422, Public (Special-A), 7th November 2017,
Aippasi 21, Hevilambi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2048.]
READ:
From the Registrar General, High Court, Madras,
D.O. Letter  No.78385-A/2017-B2, dated 09-10-2017.
Order: No. 422, Public (Special-A), 7th November 2017.
The Government have considered the recommendation
of the High Court, Madras to depute Thiru  P. Dhanabal,
District Judge of Tamil Nadu State Judicial Service,
functioning as Principal District Judge, Cuddalore to
Puducherry Judicial Service for being posted as Chief Judge,
Puducherry in the existing vacancy.
2. 
The 
Government 
accordingly 
direct 
that
Thiru P. Dhanabal, District Judge of Tamil Nadu State
Judicial Service, functioning as Principal District Judge,
Cuddalore be deputed to Puducherry Judicial Service for
being Posted as Chief Judge, Puducherry in the existing
vacancy.
(By Order of the Governor)
GIRIJA VAIDYANATHAN,
Chief Secretary.
