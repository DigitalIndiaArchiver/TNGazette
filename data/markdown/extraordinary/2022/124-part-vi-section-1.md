NOTICE UNDER SECTION 15(1) OF THE TAMIL NADU HIGHWAYS ACT, 2001 (TAMIL NADU ACT 34 OF 2002) READ WITH 
THE TAMIL NADU LAND ACQUISITION LAWS (REVIVAL OF OPERATION, AMENDMENT AND VALIDATION) ACT, 2019.
No. VI(1)/70(b-1)/2022.
 
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that the lands speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below are 
acquired under Chennai Peripheral Ring Road Project for Highways Department purpose to wit, for formation of road for 
providing connectivity from Ennore Port to Mahabalipuram (from K.M.29+533 to K.M.31+197) at No.99 Athivakkam Village 
Block-I, Uthukottai Taluk, Tiruvallur District and it having already been decided that the entire amount of compensation 
to be awarded for the lands to be paid out of the funds controlled and managed by the Highways Department and 
after having considered the cause shown by the owners or other persons having interest on such lands, as the case 
may be, do hereby publish the following notice under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 
2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) read with the Tamil Nadu Land Acquisition Laws (Revival of Operation, Amendment 
and Validation) Act, 2019.
 NOTICE
 
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) read 
with the Tamil Nadu Land Acquisition Laws (Revival of Operation, Amendment and Validation) Act, 2019, the Governor 
of Tamil Nadu hereby notiﬁ es that the Wet patta lands speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below and measuring to a total extent 
of 67,478 Sq.mtrs. are acquired in No. 99 Athivakkam Village Block-I, Uthukottai Taluk, Tiruvallur District to the same, 
a little more or less, needed for the purpose to wit, for formation of road for providing connectivity from Ennore Port 
to Mahabalipuram (Chennai Peripheral Ring Road Project) by Highways Department. 
The Plan of the lands under acquisition is kept in the Oﬃ  ce of the Special District Revenue Oﬃ  cer (Land Acquisition), 
Chennai Peripheral Ring Road Project, Chennai-58 and in the oﬃ  ce of the Special Tahsildar (Land Acquisition), Chennai 
Peripheral Ring Road Project, Unit-II, Thatchur at Tiruvallur and may be inspected at any time during the oﬃ  ce hours.
