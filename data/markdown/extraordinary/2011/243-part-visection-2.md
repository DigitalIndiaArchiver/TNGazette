TAMIL NADU STATE ELECTION COMMISSION, CHENNAI
[Elections—Conduct of Elections to Panchayats—Appointment of
State Election Officer—Notification Issued.]
(S.O. No. 12/2011/TNSEC/PE-I.)
No. VI(2)/418(a)/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-rule (1) of rule 6 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats
(Elections) Rules, 1995, the State Election Commission hereby appoints the Commissioner/Director
of Rural Development and Panchayat Raj, Chennai, as the State Election Officer for performing the
functions specified therein in connection with the conduct of ordinary elections, 2011 to Panchayats
and subsequent casual elections in the State.
(By order of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission)
