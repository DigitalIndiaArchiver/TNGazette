GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD
No. 1, Jaffar Syrang Street, Vallal Seethakathi Nagar, Chennai-600 001.
Taking over the Management of Neelam Basha Dargha, Ice House, Triplicane, Chennai-5
under Direct Management of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board, Chennai-1.
(WEA.No. 4/01/E5/Chennai.)
No. VI-3(a)/240/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995, and as per
the Judgement in O.A.1/2005, dated 30th April 2008 in the Wakf Tribunal, Chennai, the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in
its resolution in Item No.165/01, W.E.A.4/01/E5/Chennai, dated 29th June 2009 has ordered to take over the Management
of Neelam Basha Dargha, Ice House, Triplicane, Chennai-600 005 under Direct Control of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board
till a new Muthavalli is appointed by the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board and to appoint the Wakf Inspector, Chennai (South)
as its Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
6th August 2009.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.

JUDICIAL  NOTIFICATIONS
IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT MADRAS
(Original Jurisdiction)
C.P. No. 140 of 2006
[Roc. No. 3703/2009(O.S)]
In the matter of Companies Act, 1956
And
In the matter of Messrs Asscard Spinners Private Limited
No. VI-3(a)/242/2009.
Messrs Sri Lakshmi Cotton Traders, Represented by its Proprietor, K.P. Rajaram, No. 2/55, Sennikulam Post,
Sankarankoil Taluk, Tirunelveli District-627 753—Petitioner/Creditor
Versus
Messrs Asscard Spinners Private Limited, Having its registered office at C 1/5, Rathinasamy Road,
Madurai-625 014—Respondent/Debtor.
ADVERTISEMENT PETITION
Notice is hereby given that a petition for a winding-up of the above named company by the High Court of Judicature
at Madras was on the 20th day of February 2002 presented to the said Court by the said petitioner creditor, viz.,
Messrs Sri Lakshmi Cotton Traders, Represented by its Proprietor, K.P. Rajaram, No. 2/55, Sennikulam Post,
Sankarankoil Taluk, Tirunelveli District-627 753, and that the said petition is directed to be heard before the Court on
the 24th day of August 2009.
Any creditor, contributory or other person desirous of supporting or opposing the making of an order on the said
petition should send to the petitioner or his Advocate notice of his intention signed by him or his Advocate with his
name and address,  so as to reach the petitioner or his Advocate not later than 5 days before the date fixed for
the hearing  of the petition, and appear at the hearing for the purpose in person or by his Advocate. A copy of the
petition will be furnished by the undersigned to any creditor or contributory on payment of the prescribed charges for
the same.
Any affidavit intended to be used in opposition to the petition should be filed in Courts and a copy served on
the petitioner or his Advocate, not less than 5 days before the date fixed for the hearing.
M/s. A.V. ARUN,
Y. PRAKASH.
Advocates for Petitioner.
High Court, Madras,
V. SEKAR,
31st July 2009.
