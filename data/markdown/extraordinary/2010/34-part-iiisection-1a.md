HOME,  PROHIBITION AND EXCISE DEPARTMENT
AMENDMENT TO GANDHI ADIGAL POLICE MEDAL RULES
[G.O. Ms. No. 6, Home, Prohibition and Excise (VII), 5th February 2010.]
No. SRO A-6(a)/2010.
The Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Gandhi Adigal Police Medal
Rules published with Prohibition and Excise Department Notification No. SRO/A-90(a)/96, dated the
23rd September, 1996 at pages 1-3 (in English) of Part III—Section 1(a) of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette,
Extraordinary No. 496, dated the 24th September, 1996, as subsequently amended.
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in rule 3, for the word “bronze”, the words, “gold plated” shall be substituted.
