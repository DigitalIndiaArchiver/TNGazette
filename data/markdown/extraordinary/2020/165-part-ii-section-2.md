2.	 Based on the recommendations given by the Expert Committee and discussions held under the Chairmanship of Hon'ble 
Chief Minister in the State Disaster Management Authority meeting on 20.4.2020, the Government after careful examination 
order as follows:
	
(i)	 To continue the lock down until 3rd May 2020 with the conditions notified in GO.Ms.No.172, Revenue and Disaster 
Management (DM-II) Department, dated 25.3.2020 and addendums issued thereon;
	
(ii)	 The relaxations to be considered after 20th April 2020 suggested by Government of India and notified in the GO.Ms.
No.193, Revenue and Disaster Management (DM-II) Department, dated 15.4.2020 will not be made applicable in Tamil Nadu; 
and 
	
(iii)	The already existing exemptions for essential activities and services which were in vogue will however 
continue.	
(By order of the Governor)
