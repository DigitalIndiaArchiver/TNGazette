notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE

(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands specified in the Schedule below measuring 
an extent of 114050 Sq. mtrs. of Dry lands to the same, a little more or less needed for industrial purpose wit, for the expansion 
of Cheyyar Industrial Complex by SIPCOT in 135. Mangal Village, Vembakkam Taluk of Tiruvannamalai District.
