Confirmation of Variation to the Approved Coleroon North Detailed Development
Plan No. 4 of Tiruchirappalli Local Planning Area.
(Roc. No. 19963/2007/DP3.)
No.VI(1)/325/2014.
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 33 (2) of Town and Country Planning Act, 1971. The Commissioner
of Town and Country Planning, in Proceeding Roc.No.19963/07/DP3, dated: 23-09-2014 hereby confirms the following variation
to the approved Coleroon North Detailed Development  Plan No. 4 of Tiruchirappalli Local Planning Area, approved by DTCP's
Proceeding in Roc. No. 4023/95/DP3, dated: 10-02-1995 and the fact of this approval in Form No.12 Published in
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, (No.25), Part VI, Section-1 Page No. 773, dated: 28-06-1995 in Publication No.VI(1)/723/1995
and the said draft notification was published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette (No.1), Part-VI, Section-1 Page
Nos. 2 and 3, dated: 02-01-2013 in Publication No.VI(1)/1/2013.
Since no objections and suggestions have been received on this draft notification within the stipulated time, the same are
hereby confirmed and ordered as below:—
CONFIRMATION OF VARIATION
1. The entire “Coleroon North Detailed Development  Plan No.4 of Tiruchirappalli Local Planning Area Approved by DTCP's
Proceeding in Roc.No.4023/95/DP3, dated: 10-02-1995, is varied by “Coleroon North Detailed Development Plan No.4 of
Tiruchirappalli Local Planning Area”.
2. The plan thus varied is numbered as Map No.5 DDP(V)/DTCP No.27/2012.
3. The Detailed Development Plan clauses and schedules annexed with this plan are made enforceable from the date
of publication of this notification in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
Chennai-600 002,
R. VENKATESAN,
23rd September 2014.
 Commissioner of Town and Country Planning.

Variations to the Approved Master Plan for the Coimbatore Local Planning Area.
(Roc. No. 2259/2014/LPA-2.)
No. VI(1)/327/2014.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 32 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning
Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972) and in exercise of powers conferred by the G.O.Ms. No. 94, Housing and
Urban Development [UD4(1)] Department, dated 12-06-2009 which has been published in the Tamil Nadu Government
Gazette (No. 27), Part II—Section-2, Page No. 228, dated: 15-07-2009 the following variations are made to the master plan
for the Coimbatore Local Planning Area approved under the said Act and published in the Housing and Urban Development
Department Notification No. II(2)/HOU/4377/94 at Page 1078 of Part-II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette,
dated the 9th November 1994.
VARIATIONS
In the said Master Plan, in the "LAND USE SCHEDULE" under the heading "COIMBATORE LOCAL PLANNING AREA"
under the sub-heading (a) Non Notified Area—
(i)
Against the entry "AGRICULTURE" for the expression "802 pt, 804 pt, 805 pt, 806 pt" the expression
"802 pt" (Except 802/1F, 1H, 802/1J pt) (804/1, 2 pt) (805/1pt) (806/1) shall be substituted.
(ii)
Against the entry "PROPOSED OUTER RING ROAD" for the expression "802pt, 804pt, 805pt, 806pt."
for the  expression "802pt" (Except 802/1F, 1H, 1J pt) (804/1, 2pt) (805/1pt) (806/1) shall be substituted.
(iii)
Against the entry "PROPOSED INNER RING ROAD" for the expression"806pt" the expression 806 pt (Except
806/1) shall be substituted.
(iv) Against the entry "EDUCATIONAL" the expression "802/1F, 1H, 1J pt, 804/1, 804/2pt, 805/1 pt, 806/1"
shall be added.
