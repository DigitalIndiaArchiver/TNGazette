The Governor of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that
the lands specified in the Schedule below have to be acquired
for Highways purpose to wit for formation of Perambalur
Bye-pass Road in Elambalur Village, Perambalur Taluk,
Perambalur District and it having already been decided that
the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the
lands is to be paid out of the funds controlled and managed
by Chief Engineer (C&M), Highways Department,
Chennai. The following Notice is issued under sub-section (1)
of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001.
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu
Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002), the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands specified in the
Schedule below and measuring an extent 209000  square
metres to the same a little more or less required for the
purpose of formation of Perambalur Bye-pass Road in
Elambalur Village, Perambalur Taluk, Perambalur District.
The plan of the lands are kept in the office of the District
Collector, Perambalur and may be inspected at any time
during office hours.
