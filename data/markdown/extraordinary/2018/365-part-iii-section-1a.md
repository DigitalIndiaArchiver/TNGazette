and Country Planning Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes 
the following amendment to the Tamil  Nadu Regularisation of Unapproved Layouts and Plots Rules, 2017, 
namely:-

“(5) Annexure-XI to the Development Regulations of Chennai Metropolitan Planning Area in respect of 
Aquifer Recharge Area subject to modiﬁ cation that the minimum plot extent and  the minimum frontage 
speciﬁ ed therein in respect of other areas shall be considered as 220 sq.m. and 12 m., respectively, for
