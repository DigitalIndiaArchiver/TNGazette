TAMIL NADU STATE LEGAL SERVICES AUTHORITY 
Nomination of Chairman for the High Court Legal Services Committee
(TNSLSA No. 343/E/2023)
No. VI(2)/4/2023.
In exercise of the powers conferred under Sub Section (2) of Section 8A of the Legal Services Authorities Act, 1987 
(Central Act 39 of 1987) the Hon’ble the Acting Chief Justice, High Court, Madras is pleased to nominate Hon’ble 
Mr. Justice R. Mahadevan, Judge, High Court, Madras to be the Chairman of the High Court Legal Services Committee 
with  effect  from 12th January 2023.
Chennai-600 104,	
 A. NAZIR AHAMED,
