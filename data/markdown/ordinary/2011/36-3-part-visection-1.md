notification was published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No. 51, Part VI, Section 1, Page No. 404, dated
31st December 2008 in Publication No. VI(1)/459/2008.

are hereby confirmed and ordered as below:

1.
The entire Sevilimedu Detailed Development Plan No. 3 of Kancheepuram Local Planning Area approved by
the Commissioner of Town and Country Planning in Proceedings Roc. No. 42637/97/DP2 dated 16-4-98 is
varied by Proceedings of the Director of Town and Country Planning in Roc. No. 5608/2008/DPI, Dated
11th December 2008 of Sevilimedu Detailed Development Plan No. 3 of Kancheepuram Local Planning Area.
2.
The plan thus varied is numbered as Map No. 5 DDP/(V)/CTCP No. 21/2008; and
3.
The Detailed Development Plan clauses and schedules annexed with this plan are made enforceable from the
date of publication of this notification in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.

JUDICIAL NOTIFICATIONS
Constitution of separate District Munsif Court, Cheyyar in Tiruvannamalai District by converting the existing
Principal District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Cheyyar.
(Roc. No. 2387/2007/G/Judn.)
No. VI(1)/359/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873)
the High Court, Madras, hereby fixes Cheyyar in Tiruvannamalai District as the place at which the District Munsif Court,
Cheyyar, shall be held.
NOTIFICATION-II
No. VI(1)/360/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 11 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873)
the High Court, Madras, hereby directs that in Tiruvannamalai District, the District Munsif Court, Cheyyar, shall have local
jurisdiction over the entire Revenue Taluk of Cheyyar with effect from the date of constitution of separate District Munsif
Court at Cheyyar by converting the Principal District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court No. I, Cheyyar.
NOTIFICATION-III
No. VI(1)/361/2011.
The High Court, Madras hereby directs and notifies that the  District Munsif, Cheyyar in Tiruvannamalai District shall
exercise all the powers conferred on a District Munsif under Section 12 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central
Act III of 1873) as amended up-to-date in regard to original suits and proceedings not otherwise exempted from his
congnizance of which the amount or value of the subject matter does not exceed One Lakh Rupees within the local limits
of his jurisdiction with effect from the date of constitution of separate District Munsif Court at Cheyyar by converting the
Principal District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court No. I, Cheyyar.
NOTIFICATION-IV
No. VI(1)/362/2011.
In exercise of the power conferred by Section 28 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873)
the High Court, Madras, hereby directs that the District Munsif, Cheyyar, shall have and exercise jurisdiction of a Court
of Small Causes under the provincial Small Causes Court Act, 1887 for the trial suits cognizable by a court of small
causes upto the pecuniary limits of Rs.5000/- (Rupees five thousand only) with effect from the date of constitution of
separate District Munsif Court at Cheyyar by converting the Principal District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court
No. I, Cheyyar.
NOTIFICATION FOR RE-DESIGNATION OF COURTS AT CHEYYAR IN TIRUVANNAMALAI DISTRICT
No. VI(1)/363/2011.
Whereas the Government of Tamil Nadu have issued orders in G.O. Ms. No. 395, Home (Cts.III) Department, dated
15-7-2011 that a separate District Munisf Court be constituted at Cheyyar by converting the existing Principal District
Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court No. I, Cheyyar, and the said court may be re-designated as Prinicipal District Munsif
Court, Cheyyar and the existing Judicial Magistrate Court No. II Cheyyar may be re-designated as Judicial Magistrate
Court, Cheyyar.
Hence it is hereby informed that on and from the date on which the District Munsif Court, Cheyyar is constituted,
the existing Prinicipal District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court No. I, Cheyyar shall be redesignated as the Principal
District Munsif Court, Cheyyar. Consequent to the sanction of separate District Munsif Court at Cheyyar, the existing
Judicial Magistrate Court No.II, Cheyyar shall be redesignated as Judicial Magistrate Court, Cheyyar.
High Court, Madras,
Sk. AMEERJAN,
