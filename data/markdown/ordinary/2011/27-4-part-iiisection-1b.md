Amendment to the Fundamental Rules.
[G.O. Ms. No. 50, Personnel and Administrative Reforms (FR-IV), 16th May 2011, ¬õè£C 2, F¼õœÀõ˜ Ý‡´-2042.]
No. SRO B-28/2011.—In exercise of the powers conferred by  the Proviso to Article 309, read with Article 313 of the
Constitution of India and of all other powers hereunto enabling, the Governor of Tamil Nadu herby makes the following
amendment to the Fundamental  Rules:—
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st January 2006.
AMENDMENT
In the said  Rules, for  Rule 23, the following rule shall be substituted, namely:—
“23. The holder of the post, the scale of pay (pay band) and the grade pay or the grade pay of which is changed,
shall be treated as if he was transferred to a new post on the new scale of pay (pay band) and grade pay or grade pay
as the case may be. If the revision of the scale of pay (pay band) and grade pay or grade pay is without any increase in
duties and responsibilities, pay shall be fixed with effect from the date of revision of the scale of pay (pay band) and grade
pay or grade pay, at the same pay in the pay band and the difference in grade pay between the two posts. If the pay, in
the pay band is lesser than the minimum of the new scale of pay (pay band), minimum of the new pay band shall be fixed,
in both the cases, the next increment in the new scale (pay band) and grade pay or grade pay as the case may be, shall
accure on the normal date, namely, the date on which the next increment is due in the old pay band or grade pay”.
 S. MALATHI,
Chief Secretary.

All India Services (Conduct) Amendment Rules, 2011.
[G.O. Ms. No. 556, Public (Special-B), 29th June  2011, Aani 14, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No. SRO B-29/2011.—The following notification of the Government of India, Ministry of Personnel, Public Grivances and
Pensions, Department of Personnel and Training, New Delhi, dated 5th May 2011 is republished:
In exercise of the powers conferred by  sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the All India Services Act, 1951 (61 of 1951),
the Central Government, after consulation with the State Government, hereby makes the following rules further to amend
the All India Services (Conduct) Rules, 1968, namely:—
1. (1) These rules may be called the All India Services (Conduct) Amendment Rules, 2011.
 (2) They shall come into force on the date of their publication in Offical Gazette.

2. In the All India Services (Conduct) Rules, 1968, in rule 16:—
  (a)
In sub-rule (4), for the letters and figures Rs. 15,000/-the words “two months' basic pay of the member of
Service” shall be substituted:
  (b)
In Explanation I, in clause (a), for the words “fifteen thousand rupees or one sixth of the total annual
emoluments received by the member of the Service from the Government, whichever is less”, the words “two months' basic
