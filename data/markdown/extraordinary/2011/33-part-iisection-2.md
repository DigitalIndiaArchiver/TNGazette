HIGHER  EDUCATION  DEPARTMENT
EXTENSION THE TIME LIMIT FOR THE CONSTITUTION OF THE SYNDICATE AND ACADEMIC
COUNCIL UNDER ANNA UNIVERSITY, CHENNAI (AMENDMENT) ACT.
[G.O. (1D) No. 15, Higher Education (I1), 27th January 2011, Thai 13, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No. II(2)/HE/44(a)/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 22 of the Anna University, Chennai
(Amendment) Act 2010 (Tamil Nadu Act 24/2010), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby extends the time limit
for the constitution of the Syndicate and Academic Council upto 15th February 2011 with effect from
19th September 2010.
K. GANESAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
