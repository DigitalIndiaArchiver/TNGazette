No. II(2)/HWMP/161(a-4)/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Maritime Board Act, 1995
(Tamil Nadu Act 4 of 1996), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby applies the provisions of the said Act to the Panaiyur
Port in Kancheepuram District to which the Indian Ports Act, 1908 (Central Act XV of 1908) has been extended by the
Government under section 4 of that Act, with effect on and from the 1st March 2017.
