I, K. Rosaiah, Governor of Tamil Nadu accept the recommendation of the Chief Minister that
the following Ministers may be relieved from the Council of Ministers and direct  that  they be relieved of their
duties and responsibilities as Ministers with immediate effect:
1. Thiru S.T. Chellapandian, Minister for Labour.
2. Thiru A. Mohammedjan, Minister for Backward Classes and Minorities Welfare.
K. ROSAIAH,
Governor of Tamil Nadu.
