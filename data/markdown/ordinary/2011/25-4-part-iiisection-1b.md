No.SRO B-27/2011.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Educational
Service (Section 8-Tamil Nadu Services Manual, Volume-II).
2. The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 5th May 2010.
AMENDMENTS.
In the said Special Rules,—
(1) in rule 1,  under the heading "Section A - Administrative Posts", in Class V, for Categories "3. Lecturers in
Engineering Subjects in Engineering Colleges" and "4. Lecturers in Non-Engineering Subjects in Engineering Colleges", the
following Categories shall be substituted, namely:—
"3. Assistant Professor in Engineering subjects in Engineering Colleges.
 4. Assistant Professor in Non-Engineering subjects in Engineering Colleges." ;
(2) In rule 2, in sub-rule (a), in the tabular columns, under Class V, in the first column,—
(i) for "Category 3—Lecturers in Engineering subjects, Engineering Colleges", the following Category shall be
substituted, namely:—
"Category 3—Assistant Professor in Engineering subjects in Engineering Colleges" ;
(ii) for "Category 4—Lecturers in Non-Engineering Subjects, Engineering Colleges", the following Category shall
be substituted, namely:—
"Category 4—Assistant Professor in Non-Engineering subjects in Engineering Colleges";
(3) in rule 3, in the proviso, for item 3, the following item shall be substituted, namely:—
"3. Assistant Professors in Engineering Colleges." ;
(4) In rule 11, for the expression "Lecturers in Engineering subjects and Lecturers in Non-Engineering subjects in
Engineering Colleges", the expression "Assistant Professor in Engineering subjects and Assistant Professor in
Non-Engineering subjects in Engineering Colleges" shall be substituted;
(5) In the Annexure, in the tabular columns,—
(i) for the entry "Lecturers in Engineering subjects in Engineering Colleges" in column (3) and the corresponding
entries in columns (4) and (5) thereof, against Class "V" in column (1) and Category "3" in column (2), the following entries
