No. II(2)/BCMBCMW/309/2016.—The Government have
carefully considered the appeal petition dated 17.11.2014
preferred by Thiru. A. Syed Yousuff under sub-section (2) of
Section 65 of the Waqf Act, 1995 (Central Act 43 of 1995)
to pass order of stay pursuant to the orders of the
Tamil Nadu Waqf Board passed in its resolution item
No.134/13 in Rc.No.11609/04/B7/Thiruvannamalai, dated
19.02.2014. Stay was not granted. It is found that the
Tamil Nadu Waqf Board had issued orders bringing the Syed
Mirsha Hussaini Tomb Burial Ground and Mosque,
Venkundram, Vandavasi Taluk, Thiruvannamalai District
under its direct management, as Thiru. A. Syed Yousuff has
been continuously functioning as Mutawalli even after the
period of the Committee expired on 02.09.2007, without the
recognition of the Tamil Nadu Waqf Board. After careful
consideration of the issues in the aforesaid appeal, the
Government have come to the conclusion that there is no
illegality or incorrectness or impropriety in the order passed
by the Tamil Nadu Waqf Board in its resolution
item No.134/13 in Rc.No.11609/04/B7/Thiruvannamalai,
dated 19.02.2014 and so there is no reason to interfere with
the taking over of the management by the Waqf Board.
Accordingly, 
the 
appeal 
petition 
preferred 
by
Thiru A. Syed Yousuff is hereby rejected.
T. UDHAYACHANDRAN,
Secretary to Government (In-charge).
