In exercise of the powers conferred under Rule 24 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Elections) Rules, 1995 and Rule 22 
of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities, Municipalities and Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006 and 
in supersession of the Notiﬁ cation No.VI(2)/120(a-2)/2016, Published at Part VI—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government 
Gazette Extraordinary No.201, dated September 8, 2016, at page 3, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission hereby directs 
that the hours of poll in elections to Panchayats, Town Panchayats, Municipalities and Corporations shall be 10 hours between 
7.00 A.M. and 5.00 P.M. on the day notiﬁ ed for poll.

15 ems. The counterfoil shall contain the following:- 
 
A. space for stitching at the top-centre of the counterfoil; 
 
B. a black border of 1 em at the top of the counterfoil; 
 
C. 
in the case of elections listed in Serial Number 1 and 2 of the above table, the Village Panchayat, Ward Number

D.  in the case of elections listed in Serial Number 3 and 4 of the above table, the name and particulars of the

the counterfoil. 
III. 
There shall be one block of lines or shades of 1 em below the perforated rule/disjointed straight line on the ballot 
paper. 
IV. In the case of elections listed in Serial Number 1 and 2 of the above table, the front face of the ballot paper will 
contain the expression of Village Panchayat Ward Member Election and Village Panchayat President Election respectively. 
The ballot papers shall contain the symbols allotted to the contesting candidates. 
V. 
In the case of elections listed in Serial Number 3 and 4 of the above table, the front face of the ballot paper will 
contain the name and particulars of the Panchayat, Ward No., month and year of election and whether it is ordinary or casual. 
The ballot papers shall contain the names of the candidates in Tamil along with the symbols allotted to them.
VI. Serial number of the ballot paper shall be printed on the left-hand side either on the front or on the back, as may 
be convenient and also in the counterfoil. 
VII. The width of the ballot paper will be between 3 inch and 4 inch as may be considered convenient for printing the 
ballot paper. 
VIII. In the case of the ballot papers for the elections listed in Serial Number 3 and 4 of the, above Table, the name of 
the candidate, in Tamil, will appear on the left side and his symbol on the right side within a panel, in the ballot papers. 
IX. The width of the space allotted to each candidate will be 6½ ems and the width of the shaded area between the 
space allotted to any two candidates will be 3 ems. There will be a thick black border of 1 em width at the bottom. 
X. 
The size of each symbol will be not more than 9 ems x 5½ ems. 
XI. 
 
(a) In the case of the ballot papers for the elections listed in Serial Number 1 and 2 of the above Table, the 
symbols may be arranged in one or more columns as may be convenient.

(b)  In the case of elections listed in Serial Number 3 and 4 of the above Table the number of candidates in 
one column should not ordinarily exceed nine. Where the number of contesting candidates exceeds nine, the ballot paper 
shall be printed in two or more columns depending upon the number of contesting candidates. 
 
 
(c) The width of the ballot paper and its counterfoil shall be dependent upon the number of columns which 
are provided on the ballot paper. Each column shall be separated from the other by a shaded area of 2½ ems width from 
top to bottom. 
 
 
(d) In the case of elections listed in Serial Number 3 and 4 of the above Table, the names of the contesting 
candidates and/or their symbols will appear in the correct order of arrangement in Tamil alphabet one below the other in those 
columns, starting with the name/symbol of the candidate at serial number 1 in the list of contesting candidates appearing at 
serial number 1 in the ﬁ rst column, candidate at serial number 10 in the list of contesting candidates as the ﬁ rst candidate 
in the second column, and so on. If the number of contesting candidates is not exactly divisible by the number of candidates 
in each column, the end panel on the right side of the ballot paper which do not contain the name of any candidate shall 
be completely shaded. 
XII. The ballot papers will be stitched into bundles of ﬁ fty, with consecutive serial numbers. It should be ensured that 
the serial numbers on each ballot paper and its counterfoil are identical.
