GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
"Winding up the affaris of the Southern Railway Employees Families Labour Contract Service Industrial
Co-operative Society, IND. No.11/TRY and appointment of Official Liquidator.”
(Rc.No.50987/ICD/2010)
No. VI(1)/144/2013.—"Under the powers conferred on the Registrar of Industrial Cooperatives under Section 137(2)
(b) of the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies, Act, 1983 (Act 30 of 1983), the affairs of the Southern Railway Employees’
Families Labour Contract Service Industrial Co-operative Society, IND. No.11/TRY have been ordered to be wound up with
immediate effect by the Industries Commissioner and Director of Industries and Commerce and the Registrar of Industrial
Co-operatives, Mandavelipakkam, Raja Annamalaipuram, Chennai-28 vide Proc.No.50987/ICD/2010 dated
12-02-2013.
Under Section 138(1) of the same Act, Assistant Director of Industries and Commerce (IC), District Industries Centre,
Tiruchy is appointed as the Official Liquidator of the said society for the purpose of liquidation.
Chennai-600 028,
HARMANDER SINGH,
12th February 2013.
Industries Commissioner and
Director of Industries and Commerce/
Registrar of Industrial Co-operatives.
“Cancellation of Registration of the AHDCP.39, Vadachitoor Poultry, Breeders Co-operative Society.”
(Rc.No.489/E/2010)
No. VI(1)/145/2013.—The affaris of AHDCP. 39, Vadachitoor Poultry, Breeders Co-operative Society have been
completely wound up under Section 140(1) of Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act, 1983 and its registration is
cancelled in Regional Joint Director of Animal Husbandry, Coimbatore Proceeding Roc. No 489/E/2013. dated 31st
January 2013.”
Coimbatore,
V. MUTHUGOPALAKRISHNAN.
5th February 2013.
Regional Joint Director,
Animal Husbandry.
Variations to the Approved Master Plan for the Coimbatore Local Planning Area
PRINTED AND PUBLISHED BY THE DIRECTOR OF STATIONERY AND PRINTING, CHENNAI
ON BEHALF OF THE GOVERNMENT OF TAMIL NADU
(Roc.No.4352/2010/LPA-1.)
No. VI(1)/146/2013.—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 32 of the Tamil Nadu Town and
Country Planning Act 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972) and in exercise of  powers conferred by the
G.O. Ms. No. 94, Housing and Urban Development (UD4(1) Department, dated 12th June 2009 which has been
published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No. 27 Part II—Section 2, Page No. 228, dated 15th July 2009 the
following variations are made to the Master Plan for the Coimbatore Local Planning Area approved under the said Act
and published in the Housing and Urban Development Department Notification No. II(2)/HOU/4377/94 at page 1078 of
Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 9th November 1994.
VARIATIONS
In the said Master Plan, in the “LAND USE SCHEDULE” under the heading COIMBATORE LOCAL PLANNING AREA
(Other than COIMBATORE CORPORATION AREA)” under the sub-heading (b) Non Notified Detailed Development Plan
Area in Coimbatore Taluk, No. 68, OTTAKALMANDAPAM VILLAGE.
(i)  Against the entry “AGRICULTURAL (AG 71)” for the expression “599 to 607”, the expression “599 to 607 (except
603, 604, 605/2, 606, 607/2), 616 to 625 (except 617, 618, 619, 620, 622, 623) shall be substituted.
(ii)  Against the entry “EDUCATIONAL (E9)” the expression “603, 604, 605/2, 606, 607/2, 608/2, 617, 618, 619, 620,
622, 623” shall be added before the entry 668”.
Coimbatore,
P. YOGARAJA,
1st March 2013.
Member-Secretary (In-charge),
Coimbatore Local Planning Aurhtority.
