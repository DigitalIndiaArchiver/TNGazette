I.
Election to the Syndicate:
One Member, not falling under the first three categories, viz., (1), (2) & (3) of Class-II (Other Members) of Section
24(b) of Chapter IV of Bharathidasan University Act, 1981, elected by the Senate from among its members.
II.
Election to the Standing Committee on Academic Affairs:
[Class-II (other members) — Sub-section 2(a) — Section 23 — Chapter-III of Bharathidasan University Act, 1981]
Two Members* elected by Principals of affiliated colleges from among themselves in accordance with the system
of proportional representation by means of the single transferable vote.
(* out of two members one member has already been elected whose tenure is 26-12-2011. Election is to be
conducted for one vacancy only). The above vacancies are caused due to efflux of time.
Nominations are invited in the prescribed from which can be obtained from the Registrar and Returning Officer on
a requisition and submission of a Demand Draft for Rs. 500 (Five Hundred only) drawn in favour of “Bharathidasan University,
Tiruchirappalli” on or after the date of notification, i.e., 26-12-2009, with a stamped self-addressed envelope to the value
of Rs. 20 (Rupees twenty only) or in person.
Nomination papers shall be enclosed in an envelope superscribed as follows, as the case may be:—
“NOMINATION FOR ELECTION TO THE SYNDICATE”
(OR)
“NOMINATION FOR ELECTION TO THE SCAA”
The Members are requested to send their nominations by Registered Post (with Ack. Due) so as to reach the Registrar
and Returning Officer within the date and hour fixed or delivered to the Registrar and Returning Officer either in person
or by a messenger within the date and hour fixed.
Nomination papers that are not sent as stated above will be declared invalid.
