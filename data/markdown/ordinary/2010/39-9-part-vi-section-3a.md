GENERAL NOTIFICATION
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI.
Extension of Period of Direct Management of Mohideenpura Masjid, Ambur, Vellore District.
(Lr. Rc. No. 8503/07/B4/VLR.)
No. VI-3(a)/123/2010.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995, the
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board, in its resolution in item No. 159/10 in Rc. No. 8503/07/B4/VLR, dated 4th August 2010
has ordered to extend the period of direct management of Mohideenpura Masjid, Ambur, Vellore District
(which is under the direct control of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board) for a further period of one year from 4th August 2010.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
13th August 2010.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
