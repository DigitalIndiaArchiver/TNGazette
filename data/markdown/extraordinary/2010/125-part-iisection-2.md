NOTICE UNDER SUB-SECTION (1) OF SECTION 3 OF THE

PURPOSES ACT, 1997 (TAMIL NADU ACT 10 OF 1999).
[G.O. Ms. No. 43, Industries (MIE2), 29th April 2010.]
No. II(2)/IND/232(b)/2010.
The Government of Tamil Nadu, having  been satisfied that
the lands specified in the schedule below have to be acquired
for industrial purposes for setting up of Multi Product Special
Economic Zone, Perambalur and it having already been decided
that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the
lands is to be paid out of the funds controlled or managed by
Tamil Nadu Industrial Development Corporation Limited
(TIDCO).  The following notice is issued under sub-section (1)
of  Section 3 of  the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for
Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Acquisition of  Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of  Tamil Nadu
hereby acquires the lands specified in the schedule below and
measuring 1.14.5 hectare (or) 2.83 acres to the same, a little
more or less needed for industrial purposes, i.e. for Multi Product
Special Economic Zone, Perambalur.
The plan of the lands are kept in the office of the  District
Collector, Perambalur, and may be inspected at any time
during office hours.
