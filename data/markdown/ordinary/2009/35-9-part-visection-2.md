TAMIL NADU STATE LEGAL SERVICES AUTHORITY
Appointment of Subordinate Judge, Tiruvarur as Secretary of Tiruvarur District Legal Services Authority
under Tamil Nadu State Legal Services Authorities Act.
(TNSLSA No. 8050/E/2008)
No. VI(2)/478/2009.
Under the powers vested under Section 9(3) of the Legal Services Authorities Act, 1987 (Central Act 39 of 1987) as
amended, the Tamil Nadu State Services Authority appoints the Subordinate Judge, Tiruvarur to be the Secretary  of the
Tiruvarur District Legal Services Authority to exercise such powers and perform such duties under the Chairman of the District
Legal Services Authority, Tiruvarur as may be assigned to him by the Chairman.
North Fort Road, High Court Campus,
T. MATHIVANAN,
Chennai-600 104,
District Judge/Member-Secretary.
24th August 2009.

LOSS OF CERTIFICATES

to issue the duplicate of the said Certificates.  If the Originals were to be found by anybody, they should be treated as invalid
and sent to the Secretary, Board of Higher Secondary Examination / Directorate of Government Examinations,
