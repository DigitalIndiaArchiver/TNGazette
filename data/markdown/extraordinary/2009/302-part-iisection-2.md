HOME DEPARTMENT
DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF THE TAMIL NADU REGISTRATION OF MARRIAGES ACT, 2009
[G.O. Ms. No. 987, Home (Courts-IV), 24th November 2009.]
No. II(2)/HO/608(d-1)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (3) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Registration of
Marriages Act, 2009 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 2009), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the
24th November 2009 as  the date on which the said Act shall come into force.
NOTIFICATIONS UNDER THE TAMIL NADU REGISTRATION OF MARRIAGES ACT, 2009.
APPOINTMENT OF THE REGISTRAR-GENERAL OF MARRIAGES
UNDER THE ACT
[G.O. Ms. No. 987, Home (Courts-IV), 24th November 2009.]
No. II(2)/HO/608(d-2)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 4 of the Tamil Nadu Registration of
Marriages Act, 2009 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 2009), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the Inspector
General of Registration appointed by the Government of Tamil Nadu under the Indian Registration Act, 1908
(Central Act XVI of 1908) to be the Registrar - General of Marriages for the State of Tamil Nadu.
APPOINTMENT OF THE  DISTRICT REGISTRAR OF  MARRIAGES
UNDER THE ACT
[G.O. Ms. No. 987, Home (Courts-IV), 24th November 2009.]
No. II(2)/HO/608(d-3)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 4 of the Tamil Nadu Registration of
Marriages Act, 2009 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 2009), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the District
Registrar of Registration District appointed by the Government of Tamil Nadu under the Indian Registration
Act, 1908  (Central Act XVI of 1908) located in the headquarters of every Revenue district to be the  District
Registrar of Marriages for that district.

APPOINTMENT OF THE  REGISTRAR OF  MARRIAGES UNDER THE ACT
[G.O. Ms. No. 987, Home (Courts-IV), 24th November 2009.]
No. II(2)/HO/608(d-4)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (3) of Section 4 of the Tamil Nadu Registration
of Marriages Act, 2009 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 2009), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the Sub-Registrars
appointed by the Government of Tamil Nadu under the Indian Registration Act, 1908 (Central Act XVI of 1908) to be the
Registrar of Marriages for the respective sub-districts formed under the said Central Act XVI of 1908.
