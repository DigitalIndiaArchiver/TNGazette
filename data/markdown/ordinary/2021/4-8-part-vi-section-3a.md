GENERAL NOTIFICATION
TAMIL NADU WAQF BOARD, CHENNAI-600 001.
Taking over of Mosque burial Ground, Nasuk Rathan Bee Dargah waqf, Konavattam Village, Vellore District under 
Direct Management of Tamil Nadu waqf Board
(Rc.No. 15986/15/B7/VLR)
No.VI-3(a)/24/2021.
In exercise of powerws conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Waqf Act 1995, as amended by 
Act 27/2013 the Tamil Nadu Waqf Board in its resolution in item No.7/20 in Rc.No.15986/15/B7/VLR dated:07-12-2020 
has ordered to take over Mosque Burial Ground, Nasuk Rathan Bee Dargah Waqf, Konavattam Village, Vellore under 
the direct Management of the Tamil Nadu Waqf Board for a period of one year from the date of resolution dated 
07-12-2020 and to appoint the Superintendent of Waqfs, Vellore as its Executive Oﬃ  cer.
Chennai-600 001, 
VASEER AHAMED LEBBAI,
19th January 2021. 
Chief Executive Oﬃ  cer (In-Charge).
 
Tami Nadu Waqf Board.
