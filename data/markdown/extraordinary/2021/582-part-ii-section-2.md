No. II(2)/MAWS/840(f)/2021.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (3) of section 4 of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 
1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby includes the entire areas comprised in the 
local authority of Dharasuram Town Panchayat within the jurisdiction of Kumbakonam Municipality, the draft of the 
same having been previously published, as required under sub-section (1) of the said section 4. 
Based on the above inclusion, wards will be divided for the next ordinary election.
