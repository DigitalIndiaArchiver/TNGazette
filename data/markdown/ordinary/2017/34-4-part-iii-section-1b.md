No. SRO B-50/2017.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Highways
Engineering Subordinate (Work charged) Service (Section 15B in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual).
2.  The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force with effect from 26-08-2010.
AMENDMENT
In the said Special Rules, for the expressions, “Road Inspector Grade-I” and “Road Inspector Grade-II” wherever they
occur, the expressions “Skilled Assistant Grade-I” and “Skilled Assistant Grade-II” shall respectively, be substituted.
RAJEEV RANJAN,
Additional Chief Secretary to Government.

No. SRO B-51/2017.—The following Errata are issued to the Information Technology Department Notification
No. SRO B-36(a)/2017, published at pages 1-10 of Part III—Section 1(b) of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extra Ordinary
No.176, dated the 14th June 2017:-
ERRATA
In the said Notification:-
(a) in rule 7, in THE TABLE, in column  (3), in item (b) against category “3(a) System Engineer” in column  (1) and
the corresponding   entry “(ii) By Deputation” in column (2), the expression “Rs.15600-39100+GP 5400” shall be read as “Rs.15600-
39100+GP 5100”
(b) in rule 7, in THE TABLE, in column (3), in item (a) against category ”4(a) Assistant System Engineer”
in column (1) and the corresponding entry ”(ii) By Deputation” in column (2), the expression and entries thereon shall
be read as,-
“(a) Must hold a degree of Bachelor of Engineering (B.E) or Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) with Computer Science
and Engineering or Computer Engineering or Information Technology or Electronics and Communication Engineering or
Electrical and Electronics Engineering as main subject from any recognized University; and”.
T.K. RAMACHANDRAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.

India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu, after consultation with the Speaker of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly,
hereby makes the following amendment to the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Secretariat Service Rules :-
