Under clause (iii) of sub–section (7) of Section 33 of the Tamil Nadu Co–operative Societies Act, 1983 (Tamil Nadu Act 30 of
1983),  the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notifies the Primary Weavers  Societies, which have an annual  turnover  of not less
than sixty  lakhs rupees and continuously made profit for not less than two years as per the audited accounts,  for the purpose
of appointment of Managing Directors by the State Government.
