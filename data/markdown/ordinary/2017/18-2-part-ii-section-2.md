No. II(2)/BCMBCMW/318/2017.—The Government have
carefulIy considered the appeal petition dated.27.02.2015
preferred by Thiru R. Ameer Basha under sub-section (2) of
Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995 (Central Act 43 of 1995) to
set aside the orders of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in
its item No.126/14 in Rc.No.12532/Ý4/12/Kanchi, dated
26.08.2014, and the stay petition dated 27.02.2015.
Stay was not granted. It is found that the Tamil Nadu
Wakf Board had issued order bringing the Sunnath Jamath
Jumma Mosque @ Sheik Khairullah Mosque, Theradi,
Kancheepuram District under its direct management as
Thiru. R. Ameer Basha has been functioning as Muthavalli
without the recognition of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board. After
careful consideration of the issues in the appeal petition,
Government have come to the conclusion that there is no
illegality or incorrectness in the orders passed by the
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in item No.126/14 in
Rc.No.12532/B4/12/Kanchi, dated 26.08.2014. Accordingly,
the appeal petition and the stay petition filed by
Thiru R. Ameer Basha is hereby rejected.
