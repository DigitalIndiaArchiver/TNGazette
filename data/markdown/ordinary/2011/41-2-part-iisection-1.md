Appointment of Inspectors of Fertilizers, Registering Authority, Notified Authority and
Appellate Authorities under Fertilizer (Control) Order, 1985.
ERRATUM TO THE NOTIFICATION
[G.O. Ms. No. 174, Agriculture (AP3), 29th September 2011, Purattasi 12, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No. II(1)/AG/22/2011.—The following erratum is issued to the Agriculture Department Notification No.II(1)/AG/10/2011
published at page 13 of Part II—Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 2nd March 2011.
ERRATUM
In the said Notification, for "Clause 26", read "Clause 26A".
AMENDMENTS TO NOTIFICATION
[G.O. Ms. No. 175, Agriculture (AP3), 29th September 2011, Purattasi 12, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No. II(1)/AG/23/2011.—In exercise of the powers conferred by clause 27 of the Fertilizer (Control) Order, 1985, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Agriculture Department Notification
No. II(1)/AG/8/2011, published at page 12 of Part II—Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the
2nd March 2011.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Notification, in the Table, in column (1),
(1) against the Serial Number 8, for the expression "Assistant Director of Agriculture (Quality Control Cell)”, the
expression "Assistant Director of Agriculture (Quality Control)" shall be substituted;
(2) against the Serial Number 9, for the expression "Assistant Director of Agriculture (Quality Control Cell)" the
expression "Assistant Director of Agriculture (Quality Control), Thiruvallur" shall be substituted.
[G.O. Ms. No. 175, Agriculture (AP3), 29th September 2011, Purattasi 12, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No. II(1)/AG/24/2011.—In exercise of the powers conferred by clause 26A of the Fertilizer (Control) Order, 1985, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Agriculture Department Notification
No. II(1)/AG/10/2011, published at page 13 of Part II—Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the
2nd March 2011.
AMENDMENT
In the said Notification, for the expression "Assistant Director of Agriculture (Quality Control Cell)", the expression "Assistant
Director of Agriculture (Quality Control)" shall be substituted.
K. ARULMOZHI,
Agricultural Production Commissioner and
Principal Secretary to Government.
