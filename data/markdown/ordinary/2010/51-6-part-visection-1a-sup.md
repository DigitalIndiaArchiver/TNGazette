BAR COUNCIL OF TAMIL NADU AND PUDUCHERRY
Election Notification
NOTICE OF ELECTION UNDER RULE 5 OF THE BAR COUNCIL OF TAMIL NADU ELECTION RULES, 1975
READ WITH RULES CONTAINED IN PART-III OF THE RULES FRAMED BY THE BAR COUNCIL OF INDIA.
(Roc. No. 1661 of 2010)
No. VI(1)/418/2010.
1. An Election will be held on Friday, the 4th day of March 2011 to elect 25 Members to the Bar Council of Tamil Nadu and
Puducherry of whom not less than 13 Members shall be persons, who have for atleast ten years been advocates on a State Roll.
The Election will be held in accordance with the system of proportional representation by means of Single Transferable Vote
from amongst Advocates on the Electoral Roll.
2. Every candidate for Election as a Member of the Bar Council of Tamil Nadu and Puducherry shall be proposed by two
voters as in Form No.A, prescribed under the Election Rules of the Bar Council of Tamil Nadu. The nomination form duly filled up
shall be delivered to the Secretary, Bar Council of Tamil Nadu and Puducherry, Chennai by the candidate himself or by any one
of the proposers. The nomination also may be sent by registered post to the Secretary, Bar Council of Tamil Nadu and Puducherry
so as to reach him on or before 4th day of February 2011. The nomination will be accepted by the office between 11.00 a.m. and
4.00 p.m. on all working days from 31st day of January 2011 to 4th day of February 2011 (both days inclusive). Each contesting
candidate shall pay a sum of Rs. 3,000 (Rupees three thousand only) towards non-refundable nomination fee along with the
nomination. The amount may be paid by way of crossed Demand Draft payable to “The Secretary, Bar Council of Tamil Nadu
and Puducherry, Chennai” payable at Chennai. The amount also may be paid directly into the S.B. Account No.484042697
with the Indian Bank, High Court Branch, Chennai-600 104.
3. The nomination filed will be scrutinised by the Secretary as and when received.
4. Any person whose name has been proposed as a candidate may withdraw his candidature by communication in writing
and signed by him so as to reach the Secretary, Bar Council of Tamil Nadu and Puducherry, Chennai on or before 10th day of
February 2011. The fee of Rs.3,000 (Rupees three thousand only) paid by the candidate along with the nomination will be refunded
to the candidate, who withdraws his candidature within the prescribed time.

5. In the event of the Election being contested, a Poll will take place by voting in person on Friday, the 4th day of March 2011
between 10.00 a.m. and 05.00 p.m. at the places mentioned hereunder. Voting papers will be handed over to the voters on request
in person by the respective Polling Officers after obtaining their signatures for having received the same in Book kept
for this purpose.
6. The counting of votes will commence from 11.00 a.m. on 9th day of March 2011 at the Bar Council Premises, High Court
Campus, Chennai-600 104, or any other place as may be decided by the Returning Officer subject to the receipt of Ballot Boxes
from various Polling Booths in the State.
7. Forms of nomination papers may be obtained free of cost from the Secretary, Bar Council of Tamil Nadu and Puducherry,
High Court Campus, Chennai-600 104 during Office hours on any working day either in person or by post from 31st day of
January 2011 to 4th day of February 2011. Election Rules are available in the Bar Council.
