Now, therefore, in pursuance of sub-section (1) of section 39 and section 56 of the said Act, the Election Commission 
of India hereby-
(A) appoints, with respect to the said election;
 
(a) the 31st May, 2022 (Tuesday), as the last date for making nominations;
 
(b) the 01st June, 2022 (Wednesday), as the date for the scrutiny of the nominations;
 
(c) the 03rd June, 2022 (Friday), as the last date for the withdrawal of candidatures;
 
(d) the 10th June, 2022 (Friday), as the date on which a poll shall, if necessary, be taken; and
 
(e) the 13th June, 2022 (Monday), as the date before which the election shall be completed; and
(B) ﬁ xes the hours from 09:00 am to 04:00 pm, as the hours during which the poll shall, if necessary, be taken in all 
the above mentioned States on the date speciﬁ ed above for the election.
