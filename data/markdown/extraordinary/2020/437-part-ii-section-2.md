Considering the recommendations of the expert team of Doctors and Public Health specialists and based on the directives 
of Government of India, Ministry of Home Aﬀ airs, State-wide lockdown was extended from time to time and lastly extended 
up to 31.10.2020 under the Disaster Management Act, 2005 with certain restrictions and relaxations along with guidelines 
vide G.O.Ms.No.541,Revenue and Disaster Management (DM.IV) Department, dated 30.9.2020. 
2. In G.O.Ms.No,447, Revenue and Disaster Management (D.M.IV) Department, dated 31.8.2020, all shops across the 
State including Chennai were permitted to function upto 8.00 P.M. and in G.O.Ms.No.541, Revenue and Disaster Management 
(D.M.IV) Department, dated 30.9.2020 the restaurants and tea shops were permitted to function from 6.00 A.M. to 9.00 P.M. 
and take away (parcel food) was permitted upto 10.00 P.M. 
3. The Additional Chief Secretary and Commissioner of Revenue Administration has stated that in the Hon'ble Chief 
Minister Press Release dated 21.10.2020, it has been stated that considering the ensuing festive season and the need to 
revive the economy further and taking into account the decrease in the spread of pandemic, all shops including vegetables 
shops, provision shops, restaurants and tea shops and shopping malls/market complexes except in containment zones are 
permitted to operate till 10.00 P.M. with eﬀ ect from 22.10.2020.

4. Accordingly, the Additional Chief Secretary and Commissioner of Revenue Administration has sent the amendment 
proposal to G.O.Ms.No.447, Revenue and Disaster Management (DM IV), Department, Dated: 31.08.2020 and
G.O.Ms.No.541, Revenue and Disaster Management (DM IV), Department, Dated : 30.09.2020 taking into account the 
permission already accorded for the functioning of all shops upto 8.00 P.M. and restaurants, tea shops from 6.00 A.M. to 
9.00 P.M. and take away (parcel food) upto 10.00 P.M. respectively. 
5. Now therefore the Government hereby accept the proposal of the Additional Chief Secretary and Commissioner of 
Revenue Administration and to issue following amendments:- 
I)  
G.O.Ms.No.447, Revenue and Disaster Management (DM IV), Department, Dated: 31.08.2020, the 
content in Section (vi) under Caption II - (Under Permitted Activities) shall be read as follows:- 
vi.  
All shops including vegetables shops, provision shops and shopping malls/market complexes across 
the State including Chennai are permitted to function till 10.00 P.M. with eﬀ ect from 22.10.2020 
by duly following the Standard Operating Procedures issued already. 
II)  
In G.O.Ms.No.541, Revenue and Disaster Management (DM IV), Department, Dated : 30.09.2020, 
the content in Section (i) under Caption II - (Under Permitted Activities) shall be read as follows:-
(i) 
Following the Standard Operating Procedures already issued by Government, Restaurants and Tea 
Shops are permitted to function from 6.00 A.M. to 10.00 P.M. with eﬀ ect from 22-10-2020 and take 
away (parcel food) will be permitted upto 10.00 P.M.
