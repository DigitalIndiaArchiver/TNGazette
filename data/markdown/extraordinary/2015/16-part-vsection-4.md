NOTIFICATIONS BY THE ELECTION COMMISSION OF INDIA
APPOINTMENT OF DATES FOR VARIOUS STAGES OF BYE-ELECTION TO TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY FROM
139. SRIRANGAM ASSEMBLY CONSTITUENCY AND FIXATION OF HOURS OF POLL TO FILL A VACANCY CAUSED BY
THE DISQUALIFICATION OF A MEMBER
No. SRO G-3/2015.
The following Notification of the Election Commission of India, Nirvachan Sadan, Ashoka Road, New Delhi-110 001,
dated 19th January, 2015 [29 Pausha, 1936 (Saka)] is published:-
No. 100/TN-LA/1/2015:—Whereas, the seat of Ms. J Jayalalithaa in the Legislative Assembly of the Tamil Nadu State, elected
from 139-Srirangam Assembly Constituency, has become vacant on 27th September, 2014, by reason of her disqualification under
Section 8 of the Representation of the People Act, 1951 read with Article 191(1) (e) of the Constitution of India; and
2. Whereas, a bye-election is to be held for the purpose of filling the vacancy so caused; and
3. Now, therefore, in pursuance of sub-section (1) of Section 150, and Sections 30 and 56 of the representation of the People
Act, 1951 (43 of 1951), the Election Commission of India hereby:-
(A) Calls upon the said 139-Srirangam Assembly Constituency in the State of Tamil Nadu to elect, bebore the 18-02-2015
(Wednesday) and in accordance with the provisions of the said Act and of the rules and orders made thereunder, a person, for
the purpose of filling the said vacancy in the Legislative Assembly of the State; and
(B) Appoints, with respect to the said election,-
(a) the 27-01-2015 (Tuesday), as the last date for making nominations;
(b) the 28-01-2015 (Wednesday), as the date for scrutiny of nominations;
(c) the 30-01-2015 (Friday), as the last date for withdrawal of candidatures;
(d) the 13-02-2015 (Friday), as the date, on which a poll shall, if necessary, be taken;
(e) the 18-02-2015 (Wednesday), as the date before which the election shall be completed, and
