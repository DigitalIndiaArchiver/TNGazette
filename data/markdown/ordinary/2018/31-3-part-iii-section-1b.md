Amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Fisheries Subordinate Service
[G.O. Ms. No. 140, Animal Husbandry, Dairying and Fisheries (FS2), 19th July 2018, Aadi 3, Vilambi,
Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2049.]
No. SRO B-61/2018.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Fisheries Subordinate 
Service (Section 11 Part III B of the Special Rules in Volume-III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1970).
     (i) The amendments 6 (iii) and 7 hereby made shall come into force from the date of publication of the notiﬁ cation 
in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
     (ii) All other amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on and from the 3rd June of 2013.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules,-
(1) in rule 1, the following entry shall be omitted, namely:-
     “1. Research Assistant”;
(2) in rule 2, -
 
(iii) in sub-rule (a), in the Table,-
 
(a) the entries “1. Research Assistant” in column (1) and the corresponding entries in column (2) thereof, shall be 
omitted;
 
(b) for the entries in item (ii) in column (2) against the entry “2. Inspector of Fisheries” in column (1) thereof, the 
following entries shall be substituted, namely:-
 
 
“(ii) Promotion from among the holders of the category of Sub-Inspector of Fisheries or in the category of 
Laboratory Assistant:
 
Provided that out of every ﬁ ve substantive vacancies arising in the category of Inspector of Fisheries, the ﬁ rst 
vacancy shall be ﬁ lled up by direct recruitment; the second vacancy shall be ﬁ lled up by promotion from Sub-Inspector of 
Fisheries ; the third vacancy shall be ﬁ lled up by direct recruitment ; the fourth vacancy shall be ﬁ lled up by promotion from 
Laboratory Assistant and the ﬁ fth vacancy shall be ﬁ lled up by direct recruitment: 
     Provided further that if sufﬁ cient number of candidates are not available for promotion to the fourth vacancy from 
among the Laboratory Assistants such vacancy shall be ﬁ lled up by promotion from Sub-Inspector of Fisheries.  
 
Provided also that the vacancies arising in the category of Inspector of Fisheries shall be ﬁ lled up by direct recruitment 
and by promotion by following the ratio 3:2 (60:40)”;
 
 
(ii) in sub- rule (b), the expression “Research Assistant”,  shall be omitted;
(3) in rule 3, in the Table, under the heading “Category” in column (1), the following entry shall be omitted :-
    “(1) Research Assistant”;
(4) in rule 4, in the Table, the following entry in item (i), shall be omitted :-
     “Research Assistant”;
(5) in rule 5, in the Table, under the heading “Categories” in column (1), the following entry in item (1) shall be omitted:-
     “Research Assistant and”;
(6) in the Annexure to rule 6, in the Table,-
 
(i) 
the entries “1. Research Assistant” in column (1) and the corresponding entries in columns (2) and (3) thereof, 
shall be omitted;
 
(ii) 
the existing entries in column (3), against the entry “2. Inspector of Fisheries” in column (1) and the entry “(ii) 
Promotion” in column (2) thereof, shall be re-numbered as (i);

Amendments to the Special Rules for the Post of Inspector of Fisheries Revision of rules relating to Educational 
qualiﬁ cation for direct Recruitment in Tamil Nadu Fisheries Subordinate Service. 
[G.O. Ms. No. 141, Animal Husbandry, Dairying and Fisheries (FS2), 19th July 2018, Aadi 3, Vilambi,
Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2049.]
No. SRO B-62/2018.— In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for Tamil Nadu Fisheries Subordinate 
Service (Section 11 part III B of the Special Rules in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual 1970).
 
The amendments hereby made shall come into force from the date of issue of this notiﬁ cation. 
AMENDMENTS
 
In the said special rules, in the ANNEXURE to rule 6, in the TABLE,-
 
(a) for the items (i), (ii) and (iii) and the proviso thereunder in column (3) against the entry “(i) Direct Recruitment” 
in column (2) and the corresponding entry “2. Inspector of Fisheries” in column (1) thereof, the following entries shall be 
substituted, namely.-
“Must possess a degree of Bachelor of Fisheries Science awarded by the Tamil Nadu Fisheries 
University or any other University or Institution recognised by the University Grants commission.
Or
Must possess a degree of M.Sc. in Zoology or Marine Biology or Coastal Aquaculture or Mariculture 
or Special Zoology or Coastal Engineering or Oceanography awarded by any University or Institution 
recognised by the University Grants Commission”.
 
(b) for the entries in item (i) in column (3) against the entry “(ii) Promotion” in column (2) and the corresponding 
entry “2. Inspector of Fisheries” in column (1) thereof the following entries shall be substituted, namely.- 
“Must have experience of not less than ﬁ ve years in the post of Sub-Inspector of Fisheries in the Fisheries Department”.
