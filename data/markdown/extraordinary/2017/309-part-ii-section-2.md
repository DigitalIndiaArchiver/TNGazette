The Governor of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that the 
lands speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below are acquired for Highways 
Purpose, to wit for Upgrading Paruvakudi - Kovilpatti-Ettayapuram 
– Villathikulam - Vembar Road (SH-44) from  Km. 22/500 to 
Km. 38/750  and Km. 41/300  to Km. 56/700 in Ayyaneri 
Village, Kovilpatti Taluk,  Thoothukudi District  and it having 
already been decided that the entire amount of compensation 
to be awarded for the acquisition of lands is to be paid out of 
the funds controlled and managed by the Divisional Engineer, 
Highways Department, Thoothukudi and after having  considered 
the cause shown by the owner or other persons having interest 
in the said lands,  as the case may be, do hereby issue and 
[1]
publish the following notice under sub-section (1) of Section 
15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu 
Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the 
Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act, 34 of 2002), 
The Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the dry 
lands speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below and measuring to 
an extent of 528 Sq.mtrs. in Ayyaneri Village, Kovilpatti 
Taluk, Thoothukudi District to the same, a little more 
or less needed for TNRSP-II Highways  Purpose, to 
wit for upgrading State Highways Paruvakudi-Kovilpatti-
Ettayapuram- Villathikulam- Vembar Road (SH-44) from Km. 
22/500 to Km. 38/750  and Km. 41/300  to Km. 56/700.
The plan of the lands under acquisition is kept in the Ofﬁ ce 
of the Special District Revenue Ofﬁ cer (Land Acquisition), 
Tamil Nadu Road Sector Project -II, Tiruchirappalli and the 
Special Tahsildar (Land  Acquisition), Tamil Nadu Road 
Sector  Project-II, Tirunelveli and may be  inspected at  any 
time during ofﬁ ce hours.
