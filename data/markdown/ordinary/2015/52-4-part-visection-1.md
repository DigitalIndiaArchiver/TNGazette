Variation to the approved Palavanthan Kattalai Detailed Development Plan No. 7 of Kumbakonam
Local Planning Area
(Roc. No. 6397/2014/DP2)
No. VI(1)/323/2015.
In exercise of the powers, conferred under sub-section (1) of Section 33 of Town and Country Planning Act, 1971
(Act No. 35 of 1972) the Director of Town and Country Planning in the Proceedings Roc. No. 6397/2014 DP2, dated:
26-11-2015 proposes to make the following variation to the Approved Palavanthan Kattalai Detailed Development
Plan No. 7 of Kumbakonam Local Planning Area. Approved by the DTCP Proceeding in Roc.No.3968/1998 DP3,
dated: 11-3-1998 and the fact of this Approval in Form No. 12, Published in TNGG No. 44, Part-VI, Section-1, Page:
1675 and 1676, dated 18-11-1998, Publication No.VI(1)/1854/98.
2. Any person affected or interested in this draft variation may within SIXTY DAYS from the date of publication of
this notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, can represent in person or submit in writing to the Member-
Secretary, Kumbakonam Local Planning Authority any objection and suggestions relating thereto.
3. The variation with plan may be inspected free of cost at any time during office hours at the above said Local
Planning Authority Office.
