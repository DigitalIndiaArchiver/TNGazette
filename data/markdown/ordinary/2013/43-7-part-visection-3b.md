Election of Five Members to the Academic Council by the Senate from among its members
 who are not Engaged in Teaching.
(No. V.1/AC Election/09-2013/1092.)
No. VI-3(b)/39/2013.—
It is hereby notified that the election of Five Members to the Academic Council by the Senate from among  its members
who are not engaged in teaching will be held under Section 23(a) – Other Members (1) of the Madras University Act,
1923, on the day of the Annual Meeting of the Senate scheduled on 26th October, 2013.
FIVE VACANCIES
THE ACADEMIC COUNCIL [SECTION 23(A) – OTHER MEMBERS SUB-SECTION (1)]:
"FIVE MEMBERS to be elected by the Senate from among its members who are not engaged in teaching”.
The newly elected Five Members will hold office as members of the Academic Council for a period of three years
from the date of receipt of nomination/the date of withdrawal/the date of election/the date of declaration of results whichever
is applicable for the election.
The election will be conducted according to the Statutes contained in Chapter XIX, Volume I, University Calendar
2001 and the provisions of the Madras University Act 1923.
The above election will be conducted on the date of the meeting of the Senate Scheduled to be held on Saturday,
the 26th October 2013.
University Building,

Chepauk, Chennai-600 005.
