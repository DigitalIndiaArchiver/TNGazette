ANIMAL HUSBANDRY, DAIRYING AND FISHERIES DEPARTMENT
Amendment  to the Special Rules for Tamil Nadu Fisheries Subordinate Service
[G.O.MS No.59, Animal Husbandry, Dairying and Fisheries (FS2) 23rd March 2015,
Panguni-9, Jaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2046.]
No. SRO B-26/2015.
In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for Tamil Nadu Fisheries Subordinate Service (Section 11 in
Part II B of the Special Rules in Vol.III of the Madras Services Manual 1970).
(2) (i) The amendment (1) hereby made shall deemed to be come into force  on the 27th July 1972.
(ii) The amendment (2) hereby made shall come into force on the 23rd day of March 2015.
AMENDMENT
 In the said Special Rules, in rule 11, Under the heading  “Training,-11 (1) for the expression “undergo departmental
training in the Staff Training Institute of the Fisheries Department  for such periods as may be prescribed from time to time”
the following expression shall be substituted, namely:-
“undergo departmental training in the Staff Training Institute of the Fisheries Department for a period of one year”.
(2) after the third proviso, the following proviso shall be added, namely:-
“Provided also that a person who possess the qualification of B.F.Sc.,/ M.F.Sc., degree, and recruited directly to
any one of the above said posts, shall undergo a departmental  training for a period of six months only”.
 Amendment  to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Fisheries Service
[G.O.MS No.60, Animal Husbandry, Dairying and Fisheries (FS2), 23rd March 2015
Panguni-9, Jaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2046]
No. SRO B-27/2015.
In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for Tamil Nadu Fisheries Service (Section 14 in Volume- II of
the Madras Service Manual, 1969)
(2) The amendment hereby made shall come into force on the 23rd day of March 2015.
AMENDMENT
In the said Special Rules, in rule 6, in sub-rule (a), after the expression “departmental training in the fisheries
department previously”, the following expression shall be inserted,  namely:-
“and every such person who possess the qualification of M.F.Sc.,/ B.F.Sc., shall undergo  training for a period
of six months only”.
S. VIJAYAKUMAR ,
Secretary to Government.
