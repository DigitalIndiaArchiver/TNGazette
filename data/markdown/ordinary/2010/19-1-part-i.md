[Courts and Judges—District Judges - Deputation of two
District Judges from Tamil Nadu State Judicial Service to
Puducherry Judicial Service and repatriation of one District
Judge from Tamil Nadu State Judicial Service to Puducherry
Judicial Service—Orders—Issued.]
The following Government Order is published:—
[G.O. (D)  No. 108, Public (Special-A), 29th April 2010,
Chithirai 16, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
READ :
From the Registrar General, High Court, Madras,
D.O. Letter No. 15/2010-Con.B2, dated 21st  April
2010.
ORDER :  No. 108, Public (Special-A), 29th April 2010
Accepting the proposals of High Court sent by the
Registrar General, High Court, Madras, the Government direct
that Thiru T. Ramasamy, District Judge of the Tamil Nadu State
PRINTED AND PUBLISHED BY THE DIRECTOR OF STATIONERY AND PRINTING, CHENNAI
ON BEHALF OF THE GOVERNMENT OF TAMIL NADU
Judicial Service functioning as Special Judge, Special Court
under T.N.P.I.D. Act, Chennai, be deputed to the Puducherry
Judicial Service for being posted as Judge, Family Court,
Pondicherry in the place of Thiru V. Arumugam, who has been
ordered to be repatriated to his parent department viz.,
Tamil Nadu State Judicial Service and Thiru T. Mohandoss,
District Judge of Tamil Nadu State Judicial Service, functioning
as Presiding Officer, II  Additional Labour Court, Chennai  be
deputed to the Puducherry Judicial Service for being posted
as II Additional District Judge, Pondicherry in the place of  Thiru
E.M.K.S. Siddharthar, who has to be transferred and posted as
III Additional District Judge, Pondicherry in the existing vacancy.
2. The Government also direct that Ms. R. Margaret
Rosaline, District Judge of Puducherry Judicial Service
functioning on deputation in Tamil Nadu State Judicial Service
as Presiding Officer, Additional Labour Court, Vellore be
repatriated to her parent department viz., Puducherry Judicial
Service for being posted as Additional District Judge, Karaikal
vice Thiru D. Jayachandran.
(By Order of the Governor)
K.S. SRIPATHI,
Chief Secretary.
