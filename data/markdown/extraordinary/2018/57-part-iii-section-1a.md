AMENDMENT TO THE TAMIL NADU MINOR MINERAL CONCESSION RULES, 1959.

No. SRO A-4(a)/2018.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-sections (1) and (1-A) of Section 15 of the Mines and Minerals
(Development and Regulation) Act, 1957 (Central Act 67 of 1957), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes
the following amendment to the Tamil Nadu Minor Mineral Concession Rules, 1959, namely:-
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in rule 43, after sub-rule (7), the following sub-rule shall be added, namely:-
“(8) The mining plan, modified mining plan and scheme of mining shall be submitted to the
Additional Director of Geology and Mining or the Joint Director of Geology and Mining Authorised by the
Government for approval”.
K. GNANADESIKAN,
Additional Chief Secretary to Government.
