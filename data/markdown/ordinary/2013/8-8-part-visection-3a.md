GENERAL  NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI
Extension of Direct Management of Lalkhan Pallivasal Wakf, Chidambaram Taluk, Cuddalore District.
(R.C. No. 13529/96/CUDD/B4.)
No. VI-3(a)/77/2013.
In continuation of the Board Resolution in Item No. 8/10, R.C. No. 13529/96/CUDD/B4 dated 30-04-2010, the Tamil Nadu
Wakf Board in its resolution in Item No. 81/12, R.C. No. 13529/96/CUDD/B4 dated 29-11-2012 has resolved to extend the
period of Direct Management of Lalkhan Pallivasal Wakf, Chidambaram Taluk, Cuddalore District upto 28-11-2013.
Chennai-600 001,
F. ABDUL RAZICK,
18th February 2013.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
Taking over the Direct Management of  Anjumane Mohammadia Wakf, Villivakkam, Tiruvallur District.
(Lr. Rc. No. 15035/05/TVLR/B4.)
No.VI-3(a)/78/2013.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act 1995, the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board
in its resolution in Item No. 102/12 dated 27-11-2012, in Rc.No. 15035/05/TVLR/B4 has ordered to take over the
Management of Anjumane Mohammadia Wakf, Villivakkam, Tiruvallur District under the direct control of the Tamil Nadu
Wakf Board for a period of 2 years from the date of the Board's Order and to appoint the Wakf Inspector, Tiruvallur as its
Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,
F. ABDUL RAZICK,
18th February 2013.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
Extension of the Direct Management of Diwan Sahib Burial Ground Wakf, Ice House, Triplicane, Chennai-5.
(R.C. No. 48/84/B6/Chennai).
No.VI-3(a)/79/2013.
In continuation of the Board Resolution in Item No. 55/10, R.C. No. 48/84/B2/Chennai, dated 30-03-2010, the
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in Item No. 73/12, R.C. No. 48/84/B6/Chennai, dated 29-11-2012 has resolved to
extend the period of Direct Management of Diwan Sahib Burial Ground Wakf, Ice House, Triplicane, Chennai up to 28-11-2014.
Chennai-600 001,
F. ABDUL RAZICK,
18th February 2013.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.

Taking over the Direct Management of  Syed Buransha Thaikal Wakf, Tanjore.
(R.C. No. 8789/07/B7/TNJ).
No.VI-3(a)/80/2013.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the wakf Act 1995, the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board
in its resolution in Item No. 87/12 in R.C. No. 8789/07/B7/TNJ, dated 28-11-2012 has ordered to take over the Management
of SYED BURANSHA THAIKAL WAKF, TANJORE under the direct control of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of
one year from the date of the Board's orders and to appoint the Superintendent of Wakf, Tanjore and its Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,
F. ABDUL RAZICK,
20th February 2013.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
Taking Over the Direct Management of Ellaiamman Kovil Street Mosque (Alias)
Bappu Hakeem Pallivasal, Tanjore.
(R.C. No. 15909/03/B7/TNJ).
No.VI-3(a)/81/2013.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the wakf Act 1995, the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board
in its resolution in Item No. 84/12 in Rc.No. 15909/03/B7/TNJ, dated 29-11-2012 has ordered to take over the Management
of ELLAIAMMAN KOVIL STREET MOSQUE (ALIAS) BAPPU HAKEEM PALLIVASAL, Tanjore under the direct control of the
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of six month from the date of the Board's orders and to appoint the Superintendent
of Wakf Tanjore as its Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,
F. ABDUL RAZICK,
20th February 2013.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
