NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
ENERGY DEPARTMENT
AUTHORISING TAMIL NADU TRANSMISSION CORPORATION LIMITED (TANTRANSCO)
AS “STATE TRANSMISSION UTILITY” UNDER THE ELECTRICITY ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 119, Energy (B2), 20th December 2010.]
No. II(2)/EGY/738(c)/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 39 of the Electricity Act, 2003
(Central Act 36 of 2003) the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notifies the Tamil Nadu Transmission Corporation
Limited (TANTRANSCO) as the “State Transmission Utility”.
P.W.C. DAVIDAR,
Principal Secretary to Government.
