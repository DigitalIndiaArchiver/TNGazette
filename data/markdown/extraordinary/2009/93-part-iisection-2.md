HOME, PROHIBITION AND EXCISE DEPARTMENT
EXEMPTION FROM PAYMENT OF FEE PAYABLE BY TAMIL NADU STATE MARKETING CORPORATION
FOR CERTAIN PERIOD UNDER TAMIL NADU LIQUOR (SUPPLY BY WHOLESALE) RULES, 1983.
[G.O. Ms. No. 16, Home, Prohibition and Excise (VIII), 3rd April 2009.]
No. II(2)/HOPE/152(c)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by rule 32 of the Tamil Nadu Liquor (Supply by Wholesale)
Rules, 1983, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby exempts the Tamil Nadu State Marketing Corporation from
the operation of the provisions sub-rule (3) of rule 15 of the said Rules, in respect of the arrears of special
privilege fee for the financial years of 2003-2004 and 2004-2005.
2. This Notification shall be deemed to have come into force on and from the 29th day of November 2003
and shall remain in force upto and inclusive of the 1st day of April, 2005.
S. MALATHI,
Principal Secretary to Government.
