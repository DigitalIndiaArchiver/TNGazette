Taking over the Direct Management of Jannathul Bakiyath Madrasa and Pallivasal, Erode District
(Lr. Rc. No.14597/04/B8/ERD)
No. VI-3(a)/259/2009.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995, due to
administrative reasons the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its Resolution in item No. 42/09 in Rc. No. 14597/04/B8/ERD, dated
24th September 2009 has ordered to take over the Management of Jannathul Bakiyath Madrasa and Pallivasal,
Erode District under the direct control of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of one year from the date of Board’s
order and to appoint the Wakf Inspector, Erode as its Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
21st October 2009.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
Taking over the Direct Management of Mohideen Andavar Pallivasal @ Jamia Pallivasal, Melacauvery,
Kumbakonam, Thanjavur District.
(Lr. Rc. No.16031/07/B11/TNJ)
No. VI-3(a)/260/2009.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995, due to administrative
reasons the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its Resolution in item No. 196/08 in Rc.No. 16031/07/B11/TNJ, dated
23rd September 2009 has ordered to take over the Management of Mohideen Andavar Pallivasal @ Jamia Pallivasal,
Melacauvery, Kumbakonam, Thanjavur District under the direct control of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of
six months from the date of Board’s order i.e. 23rd September 2009 and to appoint the Superintendent of Wakfs,
Thanjavur as its Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
21st October 2009.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
