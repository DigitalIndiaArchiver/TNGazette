RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND PANCHAYAT RAJ DEPARTMENT
REMOVAL OF CHAIRMAN OF THE THOTTIYAM PANCHAYAT UNION COUNCIL, TIRUCHIRAPPALLI DISTRICT  
UNDER THE TAMIL NADU PANCHAYATS ACT, 1994.

 WHEREAS, a written notice of the intention to make a motion expressing want of confidence in Tmt.K. Punitharani, 
Chairman, Thottiyam Panchayat Union Council, Tiruchirappalli District, signed by fourteen members, out of the sanctioned 
strength of nineteen members of the said Council, together with a copy of the motion proposed to be made and a written 
statement of charges against the said Chairman have been delivered to the Revenue Divisional Officer, Musiri, Tiruchirappalli 
District in person by two members as per sub-section (2) of section 212 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994 
(Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994);
AND WHEREAS, the Revenue Divisional Officer, Musiri, Tiruchirappalli district caused a copy of the statement of charges 
along with a copy of the notice of motion to be delivered, to the said Chairman Tmt. K. Punitharani and she submitted her 
reply to the charges;
AND WHEREAS, a motion expressing want of confidence in Tmt. K. Punitharani, Chairman, Thottiyam Panchayat Union Council, 
Tiruchirappalli District had been carried with a support of sixteen members out of the sanctioned strength of nineteen members of 
the said Panchayat Union Council in the meeting convened by the Revenue Divisional Officer, Musiri, Tiruchirappalli District on the 
 27th April 2022 after following the procedures specified in the said section 212 and forwarded the same to the Government;
NOW THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (13) of section 212 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats 
Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby removes Tmt.K.Punitharani, Chairman, Thottiyam 
Panchayat Union Council, Tiruchirappalli District from the Office of the Chairman of the said Panchayat Union Council with 
immediate effect.                                                   
	
                                                           P. AMUDHA,
	
                                                                      Principal Secretary to Government.
