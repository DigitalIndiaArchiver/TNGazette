Notifications under Tamil Nadu Anatomy Act.
Declaration of Thiruvarur Medical College, Thiruvarur to
be a Teaching Medical Institution under the Act.
[G.O. (3D) No. 11, Health and Family Welfare (Z1),
11th May 2011, Chithirai 25, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No. II(2)/HFW/242/2011.—Under clause (e) of Section 2
of the Tamil Nadu Anatomy Act, 1951 (Tamil Nadu Act XVIII
of 1951), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby declares the
Government Thiruvarur Medical College, Thiruvarur, to be a
Teaching Medical Institution for the purposes of that Act.
Declaration of Sri Muthukumaran Medical College Hospital
and Research Institute, Chikkarayapuram, Chennai-69,
to be a Teaching Medical Institution under the Act.
[G.O. (D) No. 480, Health and Family Welfare (Z1),
13th May 2011, Chithirai 30, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No. II(2)/HFW/243/2011.—Under clause (e) of Section 2
of the Tamil Nadu Anatomy Act, 1951 (Tamil Nadu Act XVIII
of 1951), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby declares,
Sri Muthukumaran Medical College Hospital and Research
Institute, Chikkarayapuram, Chennai-600 069 to be a Teaching
Medical Institution for the purposes of that Act.
V.K. SUBBURAJ,
Principal Secretary to Government.
HOME DEPARTMENT
Appointment of certain Sessions Judge, Sessions Court
for Trial of Bomb Blast Cases, Coimbatore on transfer
as Member Secretary, Tamil Nadu State Legal Services
Authority, Chennai under Legal Services Authorities
Act.
[G.O. (D) No. 454, Home (Courts IV), 11th May 2011.]
No. II(2)/HO/244/2011.—Under sub-section (3) of
Section 6 of the Legal Services Authorities Act, 1987 (Central
Act 39 of 1987), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints
Thiru K. Ganesan, Sessions Judge, Sessions Court for Trial
of Bomb Blast Cases, Coimbatore on transfer as Member
Secretary, Tamil Nadu State Legal Services Authority, Chennai
on deputation basis vice Thiru C.S. Murugan.
K. GNANADESIKAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
