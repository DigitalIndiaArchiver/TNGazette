GENERAL  NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI
Taking over the Direct Management of Hazarath Syed Sha Shahabudeen and Hazarath
Syed Sha Bahavudin Aulia Dargah, Krishnagiri District.
(Lr.Rc. No. 5271/11/B2/KGIRI.)
No. VI-3(a)/109/2013.
In exercise of Powers conferred as per the provisions under section 65 of the Wakf Act 1995, the Tamil Nadu Wakf
Board its resolution item No. 155/12 in Rc. No. 5271/11/B2/KGIRI dated 3-1-2013 has ordered to take over the
Management of Hazarath Syed Sha Shahabudeen and Hazarath Syed Sha Bahavudin Auliz Dargah, Krishnagiri District under
the control of Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of one year from 3-1-2013 to 2-1-2014 and to appoint the Superintendent of
Wakf, Salem as its Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,
F. ABDUL RAZICK,
10th May 2013.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
Extension of Direct Management of Narimedu Muslim Jamath Pallivasal,
Madurai District and Town.
(Lr. Rc. No. 15206/09/B8/MDU.)
No. VI-3(a)/110/2013.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act 1995, the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board
in its resolution in item No. 4/12 in Rc. No. 15206/09/B8/MDU dated 3-1-2013 has resolved to ratify the period of direct
management of Narimedu Muslim Jamath Pallivasal, Madurai District and Town from 25-11-2011 to 3-1-2013 and also to extend
the period of direct management of the above Wakf from the Board’s order dated: 3-1-2013 to till the approval of committee by
the Board.
Chennai-600 001,
F. ABDUL RAZICK,
10th May 2013.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
Extension of Period of Direct Management of Isbandiarbaig Masjid Wakf,
Kanchipuram District.
(Lr. Rc. No. 12063/05/B9/KPM.)
No. VI-3(a)/111/2013.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act 1995, the Tamil Nadu Wakf
Board in its resolution in item No. 141/12 in Rc. No. 12063/05/B9/Kanchipuram dated 3-1-2013 has resolved to ratify the
period of direct management of Isbandiarbaig Masjid Wakf, Kanchipuram District from 25-4-2012 to 3-1-2013 and also
to extend the period of direct management of the above Wakf for a further period of one year from the date of Board’s
order (3-2-2013).
Chennai-600 001,
F. ABDUL RAZICK,
10th May 2013.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.

free of charge at the Registered Office of the Company or at the office of its advocates M/s. T.K. Bhaskar, Srinath Sridevan &

Office of the Company at TVS Building, 7-B, West Veli Street, Madurai-625 001 not later than 48 hours before the meeting.
