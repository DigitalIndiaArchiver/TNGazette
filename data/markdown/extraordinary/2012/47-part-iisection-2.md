No. II(2)/HOU/111(f-1)/2012.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that
the land specified in the schedule below and situated in
No. 4. Madipakkam I Village, Sozhinganallur Taluk,
Kancheepuram District having already been decided that the
entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands is
to be paid from the Railway authority. The following declaration
is issued under Section 6 of the Land Acquisiton Act, 1894
(Central Act I of 1894).
DECLARATION
Under Section 6 of the Land Acquistion Act, 1894 (Central
Act I of 1894) the Government of Tamil Nadu hereby declares
that the land specified in the schedule below and measuring
0.48.9 ares be the same a little more or less are needed for
public purpose to wit, for the Mass Rapid Transit System,
R.T.S. Station for Puzhuthivakkam between Velacherry to
St. Thomas Mount, under sub-section (1) of Section 17 of said
Act, the Governor of Tamil Nadu further directs that the
possession of the said lands may be taken on the expiry of
15 days from the date of publications of the notice mentioned
in Section 9(1) of the Act.
A plan of lands is kept in the office of the Special Tahsildar
(Land Acquisition)  M.R.T.S., Phase II Extension, Thirumailai
Railway Station Building, Mylapore, Chennai-600 004, and may
be inspected at any time during office hours.
