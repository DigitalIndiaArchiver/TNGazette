No. II(2)/PWD/749(c-1)/2013.
Whereas it appears to the Government of Tamil Nadu
that the lands specified in the schedule below and situated
in the Kuravarkulam village in Palayamkottai Taluk in
Tirunelveli District are needed for a public purpose to wit
for the formation of a Flood Carrier Channel by Interlinking
Tamirabarani, Karumeniaru and Nambiyaru Rivers for the
benefit of the dry areas in Tirunelveli and Tuticorin Districts.
Notice to that effect is hereby given to all to whom it may
concern in accordance with the provisions of the
sub-section (1) of Section 4 of the Land Acquisition Act,
1894 (Central Act I of 1894).
And whereas it has become necessary to acquire
immediate possession of the lands specified in the
schedule below, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs
that the lands be acquired under the provision of
sub-section (1) of Section 17 of the said Act.
Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by
the sub-section (2) of Section 4 of the said Act, the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby authorizes the Special Tahsildar
(Land Acquisition), Interlinking of Rivers Project – Unit III,
Palayamkottai Taluk and his staff and workmen to exercise
the powers conferred by the said sub-section.
Under sub-section (4) of Section 17 of the said Act,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that in view
of urgency of the case under clause (c) of Section 3 of the
said Act, the provision of Section 5(A) of the said Act shall
not apply to this case.

No. II(2)/PWD/749(c-2)/2013.
Whereas it appears to the Government of Tamil Nadu
that the lands specified in the schedule below and situated
in the No. 27. Karadiputhur Village, Gummidipoondi Taluk,
Thiruvallur District, are needed for a public purpose for the
formation of Kannankottai Thervoykandigai Reservoir
Scheme Land Acquisition work. Notice to that effect is hereby
given to all to whom it may concern in accordance with the
provisions of sub-section (1), of section 4 of the Land
Acquisition Act, 1894, (Central Act 1 of 1894).
And, whereas, it has become necessary to acquire
immediate possession of the lands specified in the
Schedule below, the Governor to Tamil Nadu hereby directs
that the lands be acquired under the provisions of
sub-section (1), of Section 17 of the said Act.
Now, therefore. in exercise of the powers conferred by
sub-section (2) of Section 4 of the said Act, the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby authorizes the Special Tahsildar (LA),
Unit-IV, Kannankottai Thervoy Kandigai Reservoir Scheme
and his staff and workmen to exercise the powers conferred
by the said sub-section and under clause (c) of Section 3
of the said Act. The Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints
the Special Tahsildar (LA), Unit-IV, Kannankottai
Thervoykandigai Reservoir Scheme to perform the functions
of a Collector under Section 5A of the said Act.
Under sub-section (4) of Section 17 of the said Act, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that in view of the
urgency of the case, the provisions of Section 5A of the
said Act shall not apply to this case.
