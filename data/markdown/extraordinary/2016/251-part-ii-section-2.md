Rural Development and Panchayat Raj Department
APPOINTMENT OF SPECIAL OFFICERS to village PANCHAYATS UNDER THE TAMIL NADU panchayat ACT.
[G.O. (Ms) No. 124, Rural Development and Panchayat Raj (PR-1), 24th October 2016,  
Aippasi 8, Thunmugi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2047.]
No. II(2)/rdpr/687(d-1)/2016.
WHEREAS the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission has issued Notification for the purpose of filling up of ordinary 
vacancies in the offices of ward members and Presidents of the Village Panchayats and called upon the electors to elect 
their respective Ward Members and Presidents in accordance with programme specified therein;
AND WHEREAS the  High Court of Madras in its interim order dated the 4th October 2016 in W.P.No.33984/2016 among 
others, has quashed the Election Notifications issued for the conduct of Local Body Election by the Tamil Nadu State Election 
Commission and directed to issue fresh notifications, conduct election and complete the election process, at the earliest, not 
later than the 31st December 2016 and has also directed the Government  to administer the Local Bodies by appointment 
of Special Officers till the elections are held, as the present terms of the present Local Bodies are to expire soon and the 
same cannot be extended beyond five years;
AND WHEREAS the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission and the Director of Rural Development and Panchayat Raj 
have requested the Government to appoint Special Officers to administer the Village Panchayats as the terms of the Village 
Panchayats are due to expire on the 24th October 2016;
AND WHEREAS the Governor of Tamil Nadu has promulgated The Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Third Amendment Ordinance), 
2016 (Tamil Nadu Ordinance 1 of 2016), whereby section 261-A was inserted in the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil 
Nadu Act 21 of 1994), enabling the Government to appoint Special Officers to the Village Panchayats;
AND WHEREAS, in view of the circumstances aforesaid, it is necessary to appoint Special Officers to exercise the powers 
and to discharge the functions of the Village Panchayats under the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 
of 1994), until the day on which the first meeting of the Village Panchayats are held after ordinary elections to the Village 
Panchayats after the date of commencement of the said Ordinance or until the 31st December 2016, whichever is earlier;
NOW, THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred by section 261-A of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil 
Nadu Act 21 of 1994), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the Block Development Officers (Village Panchayats) of 
the respective Panchayat Union as Special Officers of the respective Village Panchayats. 
2. This notification shall come into force on the 25th October 2016.
	
HANS RAJ VERMA
	
Principal Secretary to Government.

vacancies in the offices of ward members of the Panchayat Union Councils and called upon the electors to elect their 
respective Ward Members in accordance with programme specified therein;
AND WHEREAS the  High Court of Madras in its interim order, dated the 4th October 2016 in W.P.No.33984/2016 among 
others, has quashed the Election Notifications issued for the conduct of Local Body Election by the Tamil Nadu State Election 
Commission and directed to issue fresh notifications, conduct election and complete the election process, at the earliest, not 
later than the 31st December 2016 and has also directed the Government  to administer the Local Bodies by appointment 
of Special Officers till the elections are held, as the present terms of the present Local Bodies are to expire soon and the 
same cannot be extended beyond five years;
AND WHEREAS the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission and the Director of Rural Development and Panchayat Raj 
have requested the Government to appoint Special Officers to administer the Panchayat Union Councils as the terms of those 
councils are due to expire on the 24th October 2016;
AND WHEREAS the Governor of Tamil Nadu has promulgated The Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Third Amendment Ordinance), 
2016 (Tamil Nadu Ordinance 1 of 2016), whereby section 261-A was inserted in the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil 
Nadu Act 21 of 1994), enabling the Government to appoint Special Officers to the Panchayat Union Councils;
AND WHEREAS  in view of the circumstances aforesaid, it is necessary to appoint Special Officers to exercise the powers 
and to discharge the functions of the Panchayat Union Councils under the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu 
Act 21 of 1994), until the day on which the first meeting of the Panchayat Union Councils are held after ordinary elections 
to the Panchayat Union Councils after the date of commencement of the said Ordinance or until the 31st December 2016, 
whichever is earlier;
NOW, THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred by section 261-A of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil 
Nadu Act 21 of 1994), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the Assistant Director (Panchayats) and Assistant Director 
(Audit) of Rural Development and Panchayat Raj Department of the respective Districts specified in column (3) of the Table 
below as Special Officers of the Panchayat Unions specified in Column (2) thereof. 
2. This notification shall come into force on the 25th October 2016.
