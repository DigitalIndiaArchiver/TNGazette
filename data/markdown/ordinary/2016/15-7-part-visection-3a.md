TAMIL NADU WAQF BOARD, CHENNAI
Extention of Period of Direct Management of Nawab Sathathullah Khan Masjid Waqf
Kancheepuram District.
(Rc. No. 3853/15/B4/KPM)
No.VI-3(a)/50/2016.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Waqf Act 1995, the
Tamil Nadu Waqf Board in its resolution in item No. 203/15 in Rc. No. 3853/15/B4/KPM, dated: 13-11-2015
has resolved to ratify the period of Direct Management from 24-10-2014 and also to extend the period of
direct management of NAWAB SATHATHULLAH KHAN MASJID for a period of ONE YEAR from the date of
the Board's order (13-11-2015).
Chennai-600 001,
K. RASHEETH ALI,
7th April 2016.
District Revenue Officer/
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Waqf Board.
