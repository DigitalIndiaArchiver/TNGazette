Notification Regarding the Revocation Orders for the Approved Detailed Development Plan No.13 of  
Coimbatore Local Planning Area.
(Roc. No. 10248/2010/DP2.)
No.VI(1)/14/2018.
In exercise of the powers conferred under section 81 of Town and Country Planning Act 1971, the undersigned issues the 
following order. 
In the proceedings of the Commissioner of Town and Country Planning, Chennai-2 in Roc. No. 10248/2010/DP2, 
dated: 04-07-2017 and notification published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No. 29, Part- VI, Section-1 page No. 226, 
dated: 19-07-2017, publication No.VI(1)/200/2017 confirmation order of variation of de-reservation of playground purpose into 
residential use in Ward No.10, block No.42  T.S.No.2919/3, 2920/1 and reduction of A1A1 100'-0" wide road to 60'-0" wide road 
and renamed as KK road and D1D1 40'-0" wide road reduced to 23'-0" wide road and renamed as "JJ" road in Ramanathapuram 
village, Coimbatore Municipal Corporation with an extent of 1.40 Acre of Coimbatore Local Planning Area was issued.
Earlier, a draft variation order was issued vide proceedings of the Director of Town and Country Planning, 
Chennai in Roc. No.10248/2010/DP2,  dated: 02-06-2010 notification of which was published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette 
No.25  Part-Vl, Section-1, page No.213 & 214, dated: 30-06-2010, publication No.VI(1)/249/2010 for the above said variation. 
The above two orders have been rviewed based on the representations from the Thiru. Kulandaisamy President Kovai 
Ramanathapuram Veerappathevar colony residents welfare association and after hearing the petitioner and the concerned 
officials, the above said two orders are hereby revoked so as to retain the 100 ft. A1A1 Road, 40 ft. D1D1 Road and the play 
ground reservation as originally proposed in the Coimbatore approved Detailed Development Plan No.13. The said D.D. Plan has 
already been approved by the Director of Town and Country Planning proceedings in Roc.No.6349/1990/DP2, dated: 10-05-1991 
which was published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No.6, Part-VI, Section-1, Page-108, dated: 12-02-1992.
