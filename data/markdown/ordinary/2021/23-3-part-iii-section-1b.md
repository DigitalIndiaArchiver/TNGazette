No. SRO/B24/2021.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Adi Dravidar Welfare 
Service (Section 40 in Volume II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 2016). 
2. The amendment hereby made shall come into force on and from the date of issue of order.
Amendment
In the said Special Ruies,- 
	
(1) for the rules 1 and 2, the following rules shall be substituted, namely:- 
	
	
“ 1. Constitution.- The service shall consist of the posts of following categories, namely,- 
 
 
 
Category 1: (a) District Adi Dravidar and Tribal Welfare Officer; 
	
	
	
Category 2: (b) Personal Assistant to Director of Adi Dravidar and Tribal Welfare. 
2. Appointment.- (a) Appointment to the post of Category 1 shall be made by recruitment by transfer from the 
Deputy Collectors in the Tamil Nadu Civil Service, Superintendents of Adi Dravidar and Tribal Welfare Department in the 
Tamil Nadu Ministerial Service and the Investigator Grade-I in the office of the Director of Adi Dravidar Welfare in the 
Tamil Nadu Ministerial Service in the ratio of 11:4:1. The cyclic order in which the ratio of 11:4:1 for appointment to the post 
shall be followed as indicated in the annexure to these rules; 
	
(b) Appointment to the post of Category 2 shall be made by recruitment by transfer from the Superintendents of 
Adi Dravidar Welfare and Tribal Welfare Department in the Tamil Nadu Ministerial Services or Investigator Grade - I in the 
office of the Director of Adi Dravidar Welfare in the Tamil Nadu Ministerial Service: 
Provided that if no qualified and eligible candidates are available for appointment by recruitment by transfer from the post 
of Investigator Grade-I, the vacancy shall be filled from the post of Superintendents of the Adi Dravidar and Tribal Welfare.'';
(2) after the rule 2, as so amended, the following rule shall be inserted, namely:- 
	
“ 2A. Interchangeability.- The posts in Category 1 and 2 shall be interchangeable “; 
(3) for the rule 3, the following rule shall be substituted, namely:- 
	
“ 3. Qualification.- No person from the category of Superintendent of Adi Dravidar and Tribal Welfare Department 
or Investigator Grade-I in the office of the Director, Adi Dravidar Welfare shall be eligible for appointment to the post by 
recruitment by transfer unless he has passed the Account Test for Sub-ordinate officers, Part-I or the Account Test for Executive 
Officers''; 
(4) For the “ Annexure '' the following “Annexure” shall be substituted, namely,-
