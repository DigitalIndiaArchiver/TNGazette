Constitution of District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court at Madhavaram in Tiruvallur District
(Roc.No. 1347/A/2014/G/Judn)
No.VI(1)/410/2019.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873) 
the High Court, Madras, hereby п¬Ғ xes Madhavaram in Tiruvallur District as the place at which the District Munsif-cum-
Judicial Magistrate Court, Madhavaram, shall be located. 
NOTIFICATION-II
(Roc.No. 1347/A/2014/G/Judn)
No.VI(1)/411/2019.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 11 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873) 
the High Court, Madras, hereby directs that in Tiruvallur District, the District Munsif Court, Tiruvottiyur, shall cease to have 
local jurisdiction over the entire Madhavaram Taluk and the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Madhavaram 
shall have and exercise local jurisdiction over the entire Taluk of Madhavaram with eп¬Җ ect from the date on which the 
District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate, Madhavaram assumes charge of that Court.
NOTIFICATION-III
(Roc.No. 1347/A/2014/G/Judn)
No.VI(1)/412/2019.
The High Court, Madras hereby directs and notiп¬Ғ es that the  District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate, Madhavaram in Tiruvallur 
District shall exercise all the powers conferred on a District Munsif under Section 12 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts 
Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873) as amended up-to-date in regard to original suits and proceedings not otherwise 
exempted from his cognizance of which the amount or value of the subject matter does not exceed One Lakh Rupees 
within the local limits of his jurisdiction with eп¬Җ ect from the date on which the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate, 
Madhavaram assumes charge of that Court.
NOTIFICATION-IV
(Roc.No. 1347/A/2014/G/Judn)
No.VI(1)/413/2019.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 28 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873) 
the High Court, Madras, hereby directs that the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate, Madhavaram, shall have and 
exercise jurisdiction of a Court of Small Causes under the Provincial Small Cause Courts Act, 1887 for the trial of suits 
cognizable by a Court of Small Causes upto the pecuniary limits of Rs. 5000/- (Rupees п¬Ғ ve thousand only) with eп¬Җ ect 
from the date on which the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Madhavaram, assumes charge of that Court.
