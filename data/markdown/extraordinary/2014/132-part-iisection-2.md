The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that
the lands specified in the schedule below have to be acquired
for Industrial purpose, to wit, for  setting up of SIPCOT
Industrial Park Phase II and it having already been decided
that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the
lands is to be paid out of the funds controlled or managed by
Government, the following Notice is issued under
sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of
Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu
Act 10 of 1999).
 NOTIFICATION
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby acquires the lands specified in the schedule below and
measuring 23.54.5 hectares, to the same, a little more or less
needed for Industrial Park Phase II by SIPCOT.
The plans of the lands are kept in the Office of the Special
Tahsildar (LA), Thoothukkudi SIPCOT Phase II, Thoothukkudi
District , Thoothukkudi and may be inspected at any time
during office hours.
