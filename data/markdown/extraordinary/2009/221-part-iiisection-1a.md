In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 80 of the Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax Act, 2006
(Tamil Nadu Act 32 of 2006), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu Value
Added Tax Rules, 2007:—
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules,-
(1) in rule 7, in sub-rule (1), after clause (e), the following clause shall be added, namely:—
"(f) Every registered dealer who opts to pay tax under Section 6-A shall file a return for each month in Form L-1 on or
before the 20th of the succeeding month to the assessing authority along with proof of payment of Tax.";
(2) after Form L, the following Form shall be inserted, namely:—
