HIGHWAYS AND MINOR PORTS DEPARTMENT
ACQUISITION OF LANDS
[G.O.Ms. No. 2, Highways and Minor Ports (HF-2),
2nd January 2009.]
No. II(2)/HWMP/(a-1)/2009.
The Governor of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that
the lands specified in the Schedule below are required for
the purpose of widening of Jayamkondam-Kumbakonam
Road and it having already been decided that the entire
amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands is to
be paid by Divisional Engineer (Highways), Tamil Nadu
Road Sector Project, Kumbakonam after considering the
cause shown by the owner or other person having interest
on such lands, as the case may be, do hereby publish the
following notice under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the
Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of
2002).
NOTICE.
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the
Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of
2002), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the
lands specified in the Schedule below admeasuring
0.04.39 hectare, a little more or less, required for the
purpose of widening of Jayamkondam-Kumbakonam Road.
The plan of the lands is kept in the Office of the
Special Tahsildar (Land Acquisition), Tamil Nadu Road
Sector Project, Thanjavur may be inspected at any time
during office hours.
