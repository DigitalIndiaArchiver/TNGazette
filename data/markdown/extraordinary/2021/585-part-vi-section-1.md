The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that the lands speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below to be acquired 
for industrial purpose to wit to formation of Doubling of Broad Gauge Railway line between Madurai – Thoothukudi under 
Rail Vikas Nigam Limited situated in E.Kumaralingapuram and other 4 Villages, Sattur Taluk, Virudhunagar District 
and it having already been decided that the amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid out of 
the funds controlled or managed by Rail Vikas Nigam Limited. The following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of 
section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE

(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the land/lands speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below 
measuring 88163 Sq.mtrs., to the same, a little more or less needed for industrial purpose wit, formation of Doubling 
of Broad Gauge Railway line between Madurai – Thoothukudi under Rail Vikas Nigam Limited.
The plan of the lands are in the oﬃ  ce of the District Collector, Virudhunagar, oﬃ  ce of the Taluk Tahisildar, Sattur
