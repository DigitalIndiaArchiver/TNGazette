Considering the recommendations of the expert team of Doctors and Public Health specialists and based on the 
directives of Government of India, Ministry of Home Aﬀ airs, State-wide lockdown was extended from time to time and 
lastly extended till 24:00 Hrs of 31.12.2020 under the Disaster Management Act, 2005 with certain restrictions and 
many relaxations along with guidelines in G.O.Ms.No.673, Revenue and Disaster Management (DM IV), Department, 
Dated: 30.11.2020.
2. Now, in the Press Release dated 21.12.2020, it has been stated that it is expected that a large number of 
people would participate in the celebrations usually organized in the Restaurants, Hotels, Clubs, Resorts (including 
beach resorts) and other similar places on 31.12.2020 for the New Year 2021.  Further, large number of general public 
would also assemble on 31.12.2020 in the Beaches and Roads to celebrate the New Year 2021 which would ultimately 
lead to increase in the rate of infection of Covid-19 which has been brought under control. It has been further stated 
that Covid-19 virus has again hit some foreign countries and hence it becomes essential to intensify the preventive 
measures for Covid-19.  It has also been stated that though Restaurants, Hotels, Clubs, Resorts (including beach 
resorts) and other similar places have been permitted to function as usual, New Year 2021 celebrations shall not be 
permitted in the Restaurants, Hotels, Clubs, Resorts (including beach resorts) on 31.12.2020 night.  Further, as New 
Year 2021 celebrations in the Beaches and Roads have not been permitted, the general public shall not be allowed 
in the beaches on 31.12.2020 and 01.01.2021.
3. In accordance with the Press Release No.984, dated 21.12.2020, the Additional Chief Secretary / Commissioner 
of Revenue Administration has sent proposal for issue of an order in this regard.

4. In accordance with the Press Release No.984, dated 21.12.2020 and based on the proposal of the Additional 
Chief Secretary / Commissioner of Revenue Administration, the Government hereby issue the following order:-
i. 
New Year 2021 celebrations in the Restaurants, Hotels, Clubs, Resorts (including beach resorts) and 
other similar places on 31.12.2020 night shall not be permitted.
ii. As the New Year 2021 celebrations in the Beaches and Roads have not been permitted, the general 
public shall not be allowed in the beaches on 31.12.2020 and 01.01.2021.
