No. II(2)/HO/761/2021.—In exercise of the powers 
conferred by Section 31 of the Protection of Human 
Rights Act, 1993 (Central Act 10 of 1993), the Governor of 
Tamil Nadu hereby appoints Thiru. C. Sudhakar 
S/o. Chinnakutty, No.104/A, Dr.Ambethkar Street,

Taluk, Chengalpattu District as Special Public Prosecutor 
for the purpose of conducting cases in Human Rights at 
Human Rights Court, Chengalpattu District on tenure basis 
for a period of three years from the date of taking charge 
of his appointment subject to observance of existing terms 
and conditions of appointment.
