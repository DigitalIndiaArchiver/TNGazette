“In exercise of the powers conferred under Section (6) of the Right of Children to Free 
and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 (Central Act 35 of 2009) read with sub rule (4) of Rule 
4 under Part III of the Tamil Nadu Right to Children for Free and Compulsory Education 
Rules, 2011, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notify to provide transport / escort facility for 
the academic year 2020-21 as an entitlement under the said Act and Rules to all children in 
1193 habitations appended to this notification, comprising 1069 remote habitations with sparse 
population and 124 urban areas, where land is unavailable, and are un-served by a school 
as per neighbourhood limits specified under Rule 4(1) of the said Rules.
      
	
PRADEEP YADAV,
	
Principal Secretary to Government.
