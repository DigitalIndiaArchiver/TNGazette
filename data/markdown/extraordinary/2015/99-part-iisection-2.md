APPOINTMENT OF VACATION CIVIL JUDGES FOR THE SUMMER VACATION 2015.
[G.O. (D). No. 292, Home (Courts-III), 30th April 2015.]
No. II(2)/HO/208(b)/2015.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 30 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central
Act III of 1873), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, in consultation with the High Court, Madras, hereby appoints the Judicial Officers
sepcified in column (2) of the Table below to be vacation Civil Judges for the District Courts and Subordinate Courts in the
Districts specified in the corresponding entries in column (3) thereof for the Summer Vacation of the Civil Courts for the
year 2015.
