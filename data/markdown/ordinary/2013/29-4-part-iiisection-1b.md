Amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu State Treasuries and Accounts Service.

Aani 17, Vijaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2044.]
No.SRO B-23/2013.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu State
Treasuries and Accounts Service (Section 28-A in Volume II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1969).
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 5th January 1985.
AMENDMENTS

No.SRO B-24/2013.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu State
Treasuries and Accounts Service (Section 28-A in Volume II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1969).
2. The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 16th June 1995.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules, in rule 2,
(1) Under Class II,
(a) In Category 2, for the expression “Pay and Accounts Officer”, the expression “Personal Assistant (Administration)
to the Commissioner of Treasuries and Accounts” shall be substituted;
(b) in Category 3, for the expression “Selection Grade Accounts Officer”, the expression “Personal Assistant
(Treasury Control) to the Commissioner of Treasuries and Accounts” shall be substituted;
(c) in Category 4, for the expression “Selection Grade Treasury Officer”, the expression “Pension Pay Officer” shall
be substituted;
(d) after “Category 4: Selection Grade Treasury Officer” as so substituted as Pension Pay Officer  the following
Category and expression shall be added, namely:-
“Category 5. Assistant Superintendent of Stamps”;
(2) under Class III, the expression “Category 3 – Assistant Superintendent of Stamps” shall be omitted;
(3) for rule 4, the following rule shall be substituted, namely:-

No.SRO.B-25/2013-In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu State
Treasuries and Accounts Service (Section 28-A in Volume II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1969).
2. The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 10th February 1995.

(1) in rule 2, after Class III and the entries there under, the following Class and entry shall be inserted, namely:-
“Class-III - A
“Additional Treasury Officer”;

No.SRO B-26/2013.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu State
Treasuries and Accounts Service (Section 28-A in Volume II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1969).
2. The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 7th December 2000.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules, in rule 5, in the Table, in Column (3),—
(i) For the expression “(4) Must have passed the Accountancy Lower Grade”, against the expressions “Recruitment
by transfer from among the Sub-Treasury Officers and Superintendent in the Treasuries and Pay and Accounts Offices”
in column (2), and the expression “Class IV” in column (1), the following expression shall be substituted, namely:-
“(4)
Must have passed the Accountancy Lower Grade:
Provided that the person who possess the B.Com degree shall not be required to pass the Accountancy Lower
Grade Examination”.;
(ii) For the expression “(3) Must have passed the Accountancy Lower Grade” in column (3), against the expression
“Class IV” in column (1), and the corresponding entries in column (2) thereof, the following expression shall be
substituted, namely:-
“(3) Must have passed the Accountancy Lower Grade:
Provided that the person who possess the B.Com degree shall not be required to pass the Accountancy Lower
Grade Examination”:
