V.O. CHIDAMBARANAR PORT TRUST, TUTICORIN
RECRUITMENT RULES FOR THE PORT OF TUG MASTER GR. II and SUB-OFFICER (CLASS III)
Amendment to the Schedule II of Tuticorin Port Trust Employees (Recruitment, Seniority and Promotion) 
Regulations, 2017.
(No.M-84/1/2018-Estt/127) 
No. SRO-C-3/2019.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (i) of Section 124, read with sub-section (1) (a) of the Major 
Port Trusts Act, 1963 (38 of 1963) the Board of Trustees V.O. Chidambaranar Port Trust hereby approves the revised 
manner of recruitment (Recruitment Rules) for Class-III posts under Regulation 5 of Tuticorin Port Trust Employees 
(Recruitment, Seniority and Promotion) Regulations, 2008 as set out in the Schedule annexed to this Notiﬁ cation.
2. 
The said Recruitment Rules shall come into eﬀ ect from the date of publication of this Notiﬁ cation in the 
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
Sඋඐඍඌඝඔඍ
(a) In the said Schedule under Regulations, in the Recruitment Rules for the post of Tug Master Gr. II (Sl. No. 49) 
in Marine Department, the existing provisions under Col. No. 12 shall be substituted by the following:-
Promotion:
(i) 
Serang with three years regular service in the grade in the Port. failing which;
(ii) 
Serang and Sukkani with ﬁ ve years regular service put together. Out of which one year regular service 
as Serang.
(b) In the said Schedule under Regulations, in the Recruitment Rules for the post of sub-oﬃ  cer (Sl.No.41) in 
Marine Department, the existing provisions under Col. Nos. 12 shall be substituted by the following:-
Promotion:
(i) 
Leading Fireman with four years regular service in the grade in the Port. failing which
(ii) Leading Fireman with Seven years combined service as Leading Fireman & Fireman. Out of which 
one year regular services as Leading Fireman. failing both.
(iii) Fireman Driver with Seven years combined service in the grade of Fireman Driver and Fireman in the 
Port. Out of which one year regular Services as Fireman Driver.
