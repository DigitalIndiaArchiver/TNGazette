The Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in Item No. 94/10, dated 2nd June 2010 has resolved to ratify the period of the

Taking over the Direct Managment of Hasan Raza Dargah, Pallivasal and Kabrasthan Periya Hasanpura,
Arcot, Vellore District.
(Lr. Rc. No. 12510/B4/09/VLR.)
No. VI-3(a)/109/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995, the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board
in its proceedings in item No. 4/2010 in Rc. No. 12510/B4/09/VLR, dated 29th April 2010 has ordered to take over the Management
of Hasan Raza Dargah, Pallivasal and Kabrasthan Periya Hasanpura, Arcot, Vellore District under the direct control of the
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of one year from the date of pronouncement of Board's order (i.e. from 1st July 2010) and
to appoint the Superintendent of Wakfs, Vellore asits Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001.
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
