NOTIFICATIONS BY THE ELECTION COMMISSION OF INDIA
ELECTION COMMISSION'S NOTIFICATION REGARDING EXIT POLL AT THE GENERAL ELECTION
TO 8. VELLORE PARLIAMENTARY CONSTITUENCY
No. SRO G-51/2019.
The following Notiﬁ cation of the Election Commission of India, Nirvachan Sadan, Ashoka Road, New Delhi-110 001, 
dated 26th July, 2019  is published:-
No.576/EXIT/2019/SDR-Vol.I:—Whereas, the schedule for the Election from 8-Vellore Parliamentary Constituency in the 
State of Tamil Nadu has been announced by the Commission vide Press Note No.ECI/PN/70/2019, dated 4th July, 2019.
2. And whereas, as per the provisions of Section 126A of the Representation of the Peolple Act, 1951, there shall be 
restrictions on conduct of any exit poll and publication and dissemination of result of any exit poll during such period as may 
be notiﬁ ed by the Election Commission in this regard;
3. Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers under sub-section (1) of Section 126A of the Representation of the Peolple 
Act, 1951, the Election Commission, having regard to the provisions of sub-section (2)(b) of the said Section, hereby notiﬁ es 
the period between 7.00 A.M.  and 6.30 P.M. on 05-08-2019 (Monday), as the period during which conducting any exit poll 
and publishing or publicizing by means of the print or electronic media or dissemination in any other manner whatsoever, the 
result of any exit poll in connection with the said election announced vide  the Commission’s Press Note No.ECI/PN/70/2019, 
dated 4th July, 2019 from 08-Vellore Parliamentary Constituency in Tamil Nadu shall be prohibited.
4. It is further clariﬁ ed that under Section 126(1)(b) of the Representation of the Peolple Act, 1951, displaying any election 
matter including results of any opinion poll or any other poll survey, in any electronic media, would be prohibited during the 
period of 48 hours ending with the hours ﬁ xed for conclusion of poll for the aforesaid election.
(By Order)
