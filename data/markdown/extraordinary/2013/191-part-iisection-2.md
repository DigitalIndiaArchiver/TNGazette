Whereas it appears to the Government of Tamil Nadu
that the lands specified below and situated in the Cherangode
Village, Pandalur Taluk, The Nilgiris District is needed for
the Public purpose for taking up the Establishment of
110/11 K.V. Sub-Station by Tamil Nadu Electricity Board, the
Nilgiris District, notice to that effect is hereby given to all the
whom it may concern in accordance with the provision of
sub-section (1) of Section 4 of the Land Acquisition
Act, 1894  (Central Act 1 of 1894).
As  the land specified below is required for Public
purpose urgently in exercise of powers conferred by the
sub-section (1) of the Section 17 of the above said Act, the
Governor of Tamilnadu hereby orders to acquire the land.
Now therefore in exercise of the powers conferred by
sub-section (2) of Section 4 of the said Act, the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby authorise the Revenue Divisional Officer,
Gudalur his staff and workmen to exercise the powers
conferred  by the said sub-section and under clause (c) of
Section  3 of the said Act.
In exercise the powers conferred  by the sub-section (4)
of the Section 17 of the said Act,  The Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby Orders that Section  5 (A) of the above
said Act is not applicable to this case because of the urgency
nature of this case.
THE SCHEDULE
The Nilgiris District, Pandalur Taluk, Cherangode Village
Government  Dry S.No. 1219/2A total extent 105.63.5 Hectares,
belonging to M/S Harrison Malayalam Limited, land to be
acquired is 0.81.0 Hectares bounded on the  North by
S.No. 1219/2A part , South by S.No. 1213, East by S.No.
1219/2A part and west by S.No. 1219/2A part.
