2. In the letter fourth read above, the Director 
of Agriculture has stated that the Special State Level 
Coordination Committee on Crop Insurance (SLCCCI) meeting 
was conducted on 24-07-2017 under the Chairmanship of 
Agricultural Production Commissioner and Principal Secretary 
to Government, Agriculture Department to notify Paddy II 
Crop during 2017-2018 under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima 
Yojana. Further, in the minutes of the 39th State Level 
Coordination Committee on Crop Insurance meeting held 
on 13-10-2017 for notiﬁ cation of Rabi Crops under Pradhan 
Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) during 2017-2018, it 
has been informed that the Committee has ﬁ nalized the 
insurance units and approved premium rates and accepted 
for notiﬁ cation and also permitted the Director of Agriculture 
to refer the subject of notifying additional Villages to the 
Technical Support Unit (TSU) at New Delhi. Accordingly, the 
Director of Agriculture addressed the Technical Support Unit 
regarding the notiﬁ cation of additional Villages. The list of 
additional Villages were sent to the Technical Support Unit, 
Government of India and the Insurance Companies. The 
New India Assurance Company Limited gave its consent 
for including the additional units of Paddy II crop during 
2017-2018 while ICICI Lombard General Insurance Company 
Limited accepted for inclusion of all additional Villages for 
all crops during Rabi, 2017-2018. In continuation to this, 
"Go ahead" orders were issued to enrol the farmers in the 
additional Villages for Paddy II during 2017-2018.
3. The Director of Agriculture has, therefore, requested 
the Government to ratify his action in having issued 
"Go ahead" orders to the District Joint Directors of 
Agriculture for enrolling farmers in the additional units for 
other Paddy II crops during 2017-2018 and also to approve 
the additional units for notiﬁ cation and publication in the 
Government Gazette. 
4. The Government, after careful examination of the 
proposal of the Director of Agriculture accept the same 
and accordingly hereby ratify his action in having issued 
"Go ahead" orders to the District Joint Directors of Agriculture 
for enrolling farmers in the additional units for Paddy II 
crop during 2017-2018. The Government, further, notify the 
additional units for Paddy II crop during 2017-2018 season 
under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana as shown in the 
Annexure to this order.
