Holdings of (Late) K. Duraisamy Gounder
Cancellation of Notification.
[G.O.Ms.No.137, Revenue [LR-I(2)], 18th March 2010.]
No.II(1)/REV/11/2010.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 18-C of the Tamil Nadu Land Reforms (Fixation of
Ceiling on Land) Act, 1961 (Tamil Nadu Act No.58 of 1961), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby cancels the Revenue Department
Notification No.II(1)/REV/21/2004, published at page 40 of Part II-Section 1 of the  Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated
the 10th March 2004.
