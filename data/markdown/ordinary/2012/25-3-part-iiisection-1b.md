  Ad hoc rules relating to the Temporary Post of Health Education Extension Officer in the
Regional Family Planning Training Centres of the Tamil Nadu Public Health Service.
(G.O. Ms. No. 825, Health and Family Planning,  29th March 1973.)
No. SRO B-17/2012.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following Rules:—
The rules hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 29th October 1965.
RULES
The General and the Special Rules applicable to the holders of the permanent posts  in the Tamil Nadu Public Health Service
shall apply to the holders of the temporary posts of Health Education Extension Officers in the Regional Family Planning Training
Centres at Salem and Egmore, subject to the modifications specified in the following rules:—
2. Constitution.—The post shall constitute a distinct category in  the said service.
3. Appointment.—Appointment to the post shall be made as follows:—
(i) Direct  recruitment; or
(ii) by recruitment by transfer from any category in the Tamil Nadu Public Health Subordinate Service or from the
Tamil Nadu Medical Subordinate Service or from any other Service.
4. Qualifications.—(a) Age.—No person shall be  eligible for appointment to the post by  direct recruitment
unless he
has completed or will complete 25 years of age but has  not completed or will not complete 35 years of age on the 1st day of
July of the year in which selection for appointment is made.
(b) Other Qualifications.—No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post unless he possesses the following qualifications
namely:—
2.
Experience in teaching and or social work including Family Planning for a period of not less than 5 years.
Provided that preference shall be given to those with a diploma in Health Education.
5. Probation.—Every Person appointed to the post shall from the date on which he joins duty, be on probation for a total
period of two years on duty within a continuous period of three years.
6. Pay.—There shall be pay to the holders of the post a monthly pay calculated in the scale of
Rs. 225-10-275-15-350 Provided that as and from 2nd October 1970 a monthly pay shall  be calculated in the scale of
Rs. 400-15-475-20-575-25-650.

2. Constitution.—The post shall constitute a distinct category in  the said service.
3. Appointment.—Appointment to the post shall be made as follows:—

(ii) by transfer from any other category in the Tamil Nadu Public Health Subordinate Service; or

4. Appointing Authority.—The Appointing Authority for the post shall be the Director of Health Services and Family
Planning, Madras.
5. Qualifications.—(a) Age.—No person shall be  eligible for appointment to the post by direct recruitment unless he has
completed or will complete 25 years of age but has not completed or will not complete 35 years of age on the 1st day of July
of the year in which  selection for appointment is made.
(b) Other Qualifications.—No person shall be eligible for appointment to the  post unless he
possesses the following qualification:—
1. Second Class Masters Degree in Social Science, Social Works or Education.
2. Experience in teaching and or Social Work including. Family Planning for a period  of not less than
5 years. Provided that preference shall be given to those  with a Diploma in Health Education.
6. Probation.—Every Person appointed to the post shall from the date on which he joins duty, be on probation
for a total period of two years on duty within a continuous period of three years.
7. Pay.—There shall be pay to the holders of the post a monthly pay calculated in the scale of
Rs. 225-10-275-15-350.

No. SRO B-19/2012.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following anendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu School Educational
Service (Section 8-A in Volume-II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1969).
2. The amendments hereby made shall come into force on 25th May 2012.
AMENDMENTS
In  the said  Special Rules,
(1) in rule 2, in sub-rule (a), under class V, in column (2), against the entry “Headmasters and Headmistresses
including Headmasters and Headmistresses in the Model High Schools attached to Training Colleges in the Collegiate
Education Department” in column (1) thereof;
(i) after the expression “Physical Directors  in the Tamil Nadu Educational Subordinate Service”, the expression
“Assistant Elementary Educational Oficers in the Tamil Nadu Elementary Educational Subordinate  Service” shall be
added;
