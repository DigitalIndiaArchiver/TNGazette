NOTIFICATIONS  BY  THE  LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY SECRETARIAT
LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY SECRETARIAT
DEATH OF THIRU D. SUDHARSSANAM,  MEMBER OF THE TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY.
[Letter No. 9464/2010-1, Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Secretariat (B.III), 29th June 2010,
Aani 15, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No. II(1)/LAS/15(a-1)/2010.
"The Honourable Speaker has learnt with deep regret the death of Thiru D. Sudharssanam,
a Member of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly on 26th June 2010".
VACATION OF SEAT IN TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY.
[Letter No. 9464/2010-1, Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Secretariat (B.III), 29th June 2010,
Aani 15, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No. II(1)/LAS/15(a-2)/2010.
"A vacancy has occurred among the Members of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly, consequent
on the death of Thiru D. Sudharssanam, a Member elected to the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly
from the 28. Poonamallee Assembly Constituency in Tiruvallur District".
