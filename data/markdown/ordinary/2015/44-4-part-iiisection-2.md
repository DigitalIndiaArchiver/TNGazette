CONDITION  OF  SALE
1. Lots to be examined by the intending purchaser before bidding and to be taken with all faults and errors of description.
2. The goods are to be taken on ‘AS IS WHERE IS’ basis. The Trust shall not take the responsibility with reference to
weights, measurements, quantities, qualities, etc.,
3. E-Auction will be conducted online by the Trust’s Auctioneer, M/s.MSTC Limited, Southern Regional Office, Leelavathi
Building, II Floor, 69, Armenian Street, Chennai – 600 001, through their website www.mstcecommerce.com.
4. Earnest Money Deposit (EMD) by way of Demand Draft/Pay Order drawn in favour of “The Chairman, Chennai Port
Trust” should be deposited with the Auctioneer before bidding.
5. The demand draft/pay order of the unsuccessful bidder will be returned back after completion of E-Auction by the
Auctioneer. Chennai Port Trust will not pay any interest to the bidder on the EMD amount/Balance amount/Refund  amount
or any deposits.
6. The highest bidder to be the purchaser and in the event of any dispute arising between two or more bidders, the lots
so disputed should be put up again and resold.

7. The bid amount shall be paid in full, immediately after the bid is knocked down.  If this not possible, the balance
amount should be paid to the Trust’s Financial Adviser and Chief Accounts Officer on or before 4.00 p.m. on the third day,
calculating from the date of confirmation of sale, failing which the amount paid will be forfeited and the Trust will resell the
lot either privately or by Public Auction or E-Auction at the risk and responsibility of the defaulters.
8. Goods advertised for sale will not be withdrawn by the Trust, except on payment of sufficient deposit to cover the
Harbour dues, Demurrage charges and other charges due thereon.
9. The goods sold in Auction will not be delivered to the purchaser unless the purchaser produces Delivery Order from
the Auctioneers for having paid the amount in full. Also the bidder should complete the Customs formalities, such as payment
of Customs duty, if any.
10. Goods sold in Auction shall be allowed three free days excluding Sundays and Board’s holidays from the date of
confirmation of sale and thereafter shall be charged applicable Port charges at daily rates as per Scale of Rates, upto and
including the date of clearance.
11. Within the free period, the bidder shall collect Bills of Entry with assessed Customs duty amount from the Trust and
remit the same to Customs with proper receipt.
12. The lots sold will be lying at the risk of the purchaser and must be removed within the free period of three working
days from the date of confirmation of sale at the purchaser’s expenses.
13. The purchasers are distinctly warned that if the goods are not cleared within eight days from the date of expiry of
free period, the sale of lot shall stand cancelled and the EMD/bid amount will be forfeited.  The bidder will have no claim
whatsoever over the material and the same will be sold either privately or by Public Auction or E-Auction.  The first purchaser
will in such a situation be held liable for any loss arising from such resale and will forfeit any advances together with
auctioneer’s commission.
14. The Trust shall reserve the right to withdraw any of the items notified for sale without assigning any reason for such
withdrawals.  However, if any payment has been made, it would be refunded.
15. The Port shall reserve the right to take such action against the defaulting bidder as deemed fit by the Trust and/or
can debar the said bidder from bidding in the current and/or future auctions also.
16. The bidders shall gain admission into the Harbour on the days of Inspection by obtained Harbour Entry Permit (HEP)
on payment from the Assistant Traffic Manager, Computer Pass Section, Chennai Port Trust,  Chennai – 600 001.  The
successful bidder should also obtain HEP on payment for clearing the goods.
17. The delivery of the lots would be allowed from 1000 Hrs. to 1300 Hrs. and 1400 Hrs to 1700 Hrs. only.
18. The VAT (Sales Tax) and Income Tax as applicable will be levied additionally as per rules in force.
19. Terms: Cash or Demand Draft or Pay Order of any Nationalized Bank.
20.  All the lots sold in the auction are subject to confirmation.
21.  If any packages are not sold in the auction for which they are listed they will be included for sale on the succeeding
sale by Tender or in Public Auction.
22.  The Trust reserves the right to amend and modify ‘Terms and Conditions’ and announce the same if need be while
the auction is in progress or prior to commencement of Auction.
