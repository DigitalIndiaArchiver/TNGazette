In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 43-B of Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu
Act V of 1920) and Rules 22 and 139 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities, Municipalities and
Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission hereby makes the following amendment
to the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission's S.O. No. 47/2009/TNSEC/ME-1, dated 10th September 2009 notified and
published in the Tamil Nadu Government Extraordinary Gazette, dated the 16th September, 2009.
AMENDMENT
In the said Notification, in the Table, under the heading “TOWN PANCHAYAT WARD MEMBER”, in Serial No. 4, under
the column “Reservation”, “SC(G)” shall be substituted instead of “General”.
(By Order of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission)

17th September 2009.
