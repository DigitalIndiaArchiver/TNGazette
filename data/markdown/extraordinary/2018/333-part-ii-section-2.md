RESIGNATION OF CERTAIN PERSON AS MEMBER OF TAMIL NADU WAQF BOARD. 
[G.O. (2D) No.22,  Backward Classes, Most Backward Classes and Minorities Welfare (T1), 
20th September 2018, Purattasi 4, Vilambi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2049.]
No. II(2)/BCMBCMW/849(C-1)/2018.
The resignation tendered by Thiru Md. Nasimuddin, I.A.S., as Member, Tamil Nadu Waqf Board is 
accepted by the Government.
APPOINTMENT OF CERTAIN PERSON AS MEMBER OF THE TAMIL NADU WAQF BOARD 
UNDER THE WAQF ACT.
[G.O. (2D) No.22,  Backward Classes, Most Backward Classes and Minorities Welfare (T1), 
20th September 2018, Purattasi 4, Vilambi Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2049.]
No. II(2)/BCMBCMW/849(C-2)/2018.
In exercise of powers conferred by clause (e) of sub-section (1) of Section 14 read with 
Section 21 of the Waqf Act, 1995 (Central Act 43 of 1995), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints 
Thiru M.A. Siddique, I.A.S., Secretary to Government (Expenditure), Finance Department as 
a Member of the Tamil Nadu Waqf Board in the vacancy caused due to the resignation of 
Thiru Md. Nasimuddin, I.A.S.
