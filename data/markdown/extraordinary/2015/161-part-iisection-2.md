DEATH OF THIRU A.P.J ABDUL KALAM, FORMER PRESIDENT OF INDIA
[G.O. Ms. No. 991, Public (Protocol-1), 27th July 2015,  Aadi 11, Manmadha,
Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2046.]
No. II(2)/PUPRO/402(d)/2015.
The Government of Tamil Nadu announce with profound regret the death of
Thiru A.P.J Abdul Kalam, Former President of India on 27th July 2015 at Shillong. State Mourning  will
be observed for seven days from 27th July 2015 to 2nd August 2015 (both days inclusive).
The National Flag will be flown at half mast on all buildings where it is flown regularly, during the period
of mourning throughout the State. There will be no official entertainment during this period.
(By Order of the Governor)
K. GNANADESIKAN,
Chief Secretary.
