Whereas in exercise of the powers conferred by
Clause (1) of Article 258 of the Constitution of India, the
President has entrusted to the Government of Tamil Nadu in
Letter No. 4(1)/65 Central II Food, Agriculture Community
Development and Co-operation (Agriculture), dated
23rd April 1966, with their consent the functions of the Central
Government under the land acquisition Act, 1894 (Central
Act I of1894) in relation to acquisition of land for the purposes
of the union in this state and whereas it has alredy been decided
that the compensation should be paid by the public revenues.
Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by
Section 6 of the said Act, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
declares that the lands specified in the schedule below and
measuring 2.04.0 hectares, be the same a little more or
less, is needed for a public purpose, to wit, for the formation
of new Broad Gauge Railway Line between Tindivanam and
Tiruvannamalai at So. Kzhilnachipattu Village of Tiruvannamalai
Taluk of Tiruvannamalai District and under Sections 3 and 7 of
the same Act the Revenue Divisional Officer, Tiruvannamalai
is appointed to perform the functions of a collector under the Act,
and directed to take order for the acquisition of the said lands.
Under sub-section(1) and (2) of Section 17 of the Act, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu further directs that the possession
of the said lands may be taken immediately after the
publication of the notice mentioned in Section 9 (1) of the Act.
A plan of the land is kept in the office of the Revenue
Divisional Officer, Tiruvannamalai and may be inspected at
any time during office hours.
