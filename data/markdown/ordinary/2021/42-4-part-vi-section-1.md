No. 244/2021 In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 13 of the Criminal Procedure Code, 1973 (Central Act 2 of 1974), the
High Court hereby appoints the following (i) 4 Deputy Tahsildars in Ranipet District and (ii) 8 Deputy Tahsildars in 
Ramanathapuram District, to undergo Magisterial Training for a period of 120 days, from the date of assumption of
charge and confers upon them the ordinary powers conferable under this Code on a Judicial Magistrate to try such cases 
which were earlier triable and dealt with by an erstwhile Judicial Second Class Magistrate.
