[Magha  01, 1935 (Saka)] is republished:—

Section 12 of the Representation of the People Act, 1951 (43 of 1951) and published in the
Gazette of India on 21st January, 2014 been pleased to call upon the elected members of each of the
Legislative Assemblies of the States, specified in columns (1) of the TABLE below, to elect to the
Council of States, the number of members specified against that State in column (2) of the said TABLE;

Now, therefore, in pursuance of sub-section (1) of Section 39 and Section 56 of the said Act,
the Election Commission of India hereby—
(A) appoints, with respect to the said election in each of the above mentioned States,·
(a)  the 28th January, 2014 (Tuesday), as the last date for making nominations;
(b)  the 29th January, 2014 (Wednesday), as the date for the scrutiny of the nominations;
(c)  the 31st January, 2014 (Friday), as the last date for the withdrawal of candidatures;
(d) the 7th February, 2014 (Friday), as the date on which a poll shall, if necessary, be
 taken; and
(e)  the 10th February, 2014 (Monday), as the date before which the election shall be
  completed; and
(B) fixes the hours from 9.00 A.M. to 4.00 P.M., as the hours during which the poll shall, if
necessary, be taken in all the above mentioned States, on the date specified above, for the election.
