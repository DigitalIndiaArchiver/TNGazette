Variations to the Approved Master Plan for the Erode Local Planning Area.
(Roc. No. 875/2006 ELPA-2.)
No. VI(1)/303/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 32 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning
Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972), and in exercise of powers conferred by the G.O. Ms. No. 94, Housing and Urban
Development 
[UD4(1)] 
Department, 
dated 
12th 
June 
2009 
which 
has 
been 
published 
in 
the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette (Issue No. 27), Part II—Section 2, Page No. 228, dated 15th July 2009 the following
variations are made to the master plan for the Erode Local Planning Area approved under the said Act and published
in the Housing and Urban Development Department Notification No. II(2)/HOU/1104/90, at page 115
of Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 28th February 1990.
VARIATIONS
In the said Master Plan, in the land use schedule tabular column,
(i) Under the heading “Local Planning Area-Villages” under the sub-heading “PR2” V.No. 75 Surampatti,
against the entry in column No. 7, the expression "24 to 104" shall be read as the expression "24 to 88, 89 (except
89/1part), 90 to 104” and
(ii) Under the heading “Local Planning Area Villages” under the sub-heading “C47", V.No. 75 Surampatti
against the entry in column No. 7, the expression “89/1part” shall be inserted before the expression “146/p, 168/p,
169/p, 154/3, 4”.
Erode-9,
 K. SABAPATHY,
22nd June 2010.
Member-Secretary (In-charge),
Erode Local Planning Authority.

Variations to the Approved Master Plan for the Thanjavur Local Planning Area.
(Roc. No. 303/10/TLPA.)
No. VI(1)/305/2010.—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 32 of the Town and Country
Planning Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972) and in exercise of powers conferred by the Government Order
Ms. No. 94, Housing and Urban Development (UDA 4(1) Department, dated 12th June 2009. Which has been published
in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette (Issue No. 27) Part II—Section 2 page No. 228, dated 15th July 2009 the
following Variation are made to the Master Plan for the Thanjavur Local Planning Area approved under the said Act
and published in the Housing and Urban Development Department. Notification at page 879 of Part VI—Section 2 of
the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated 11th October 1995.
VARIATIONS
In the said Master Plan in the "LAND USE SCHEDULE" under the heading "THANJAVUR MASTER PLAN-ZONING
SCHEDULES".
(i) Under the heading V public and semi public, the expression No. 107, Nilagiri Therkuthottam Village
S.F. No. 244/2" shall be inserted and
(ii) Under the heading 1(b) Mixed Residential Use Zone for the expression 107, Nilagiri Therku Thottam Village
against the entry 244 pt. the expression "244 pt (except 244/2)" shall be substituted.
Thanjavur,
P. MOHAN KUMAR,
2nd August 2010.
Member-Secretary (In-charge),
Thanjavur Local Planning Authority.
