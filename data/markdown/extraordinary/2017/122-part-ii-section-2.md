No.II(2)/HWMP/335(a-1)/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (b) of Section 4 of the Indian Tolls Act, 1851 (Central Act VIII of 1851),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby specifies  that no tolls shall be paid for the passage of the Vehicles of the local
residents of the Nilgiris District with effect on and from the 08-05-2017.

Levy of Tolls on Roads with a Facility for Ingress into the Nilgiris District for certain period.

No.II(2)/HWMP/335(a-3)/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (a) of sub section (1) read with  sub-section (2) of Section 2 of the Indian
Tolls Act, 1851 (Central Act VIII of 1851), as amended by the Tamil Nadu Tolls and Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment)
Act, 1938 (Tamil Nadu Act  VI of 1938) and the Indian Tolls (Tamil Nadu Amendment) Act,1942 (Tamil Nadu Act XIV of
1942), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby levies tolls for a period of five years  with effect on and from the 08-05-2017
in respect of the following roads in the Nilgiris District with a facility for ingress into the Nilgiris District namely:-
(i)
Kunjapanai-Kotagiri-Mettupalayam Road;
(ii)
Pattavayal-Sultan Bathery-Gudalur Road;
(iii)
Thaloor-Sultan Bathery-Gudalur Road;
(iv)
Nadugani-Calicut-Gudalur Road;
(v)
Choladi-Calicut-Gudalur Road.
