No.I/PUSP/26/2009.—The following Notification of the Government
of India, Ministry of Law and Justice (Department of Justice), Jaisalmer
House, 26, Man Singh Road, New Delhi-110 011, dated
7th December 2009 is republished:—
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (1) of Article 217 of the
Constitution of India, the President is pleased to appoint Shri Justice
Sudhansu Jyoti Mukhopadhaya, Judge of the Madras High Court, to be the
Chief Justice of the Gujarat High Court with effect from the date he
assumes charge of his office.
