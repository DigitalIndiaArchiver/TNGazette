The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that the lands specified in the Schedule below have to be acquired 
for industrial purpose to wit to the formation of alternate Road situated in Servaikaranmadam Village of Thoothukudi Taluk 
in the District of  Thoothukudi, and it having already been decided that the amount of compensation is to be awarded for 
the lands to be paid out of the funds controlled by the Government of Tamil Nadu (Industries Department). The following 
notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997. 
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu 
Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands specified in the Schedule below measuring an  extent 
of 38188 Sq.Mtrs  of dry lands in S.No.25/1A1A1 and etc., to the same, a little more or less needed for  Industrial purpose 
for the formation of alternate Road situated in Servaikaranmadam Village of Thoothukudi Taluk in the District of Thoothukudi. 
The plan of the lands are kept in the office of the Collector, Thoothukudi and Sub-Collector Thoothukudi may be inspected 
at any time during office hours.
