Tamil Development and Information (OP1.1),  
No. SRO B-24/2022.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, 
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for Class XIII of the Tamil Nadu 
General Service. (Section 16 in Volume II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 2016).

In the said special  Rules, in the Annexure referred to in sub-rule (b) of rule 4, in the TABLE, in column (2) under the 
heading "Method of appointment", against the category "3. Assistant Director of Translations" in column (1) thereof:-
(a) in item 1, for the expression "ten years" the expression "ﬁ ve years" shall be substituted;
