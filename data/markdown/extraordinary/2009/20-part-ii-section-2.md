NOTIFICATIONS UNDER TAMIL NADU FARMERS’ MANAGEMENT IRRIGATION SYSTEMS ACT.
APPOINTMENT OF COMPETENT AUTHORITY TO DISTRIBUTORY COMMITTEE UNDER THE ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 15, Public Works (W2), 22nd January 2009.]
No. II(2)/PW/32(b-1)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 26 of the Tamil Nadu Farmers’ Management Irrigation
Systems Act, 2000 (Tamil Nadu Act 7 of 2001), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the Executive Engineer of
Water Resources Department, under whose jurisdiction the area of operation of the Distributory Committee lies, as the
Competent Authority for the Distributory Committee constituted under Section 7 of the said Act for carrying out the purposes
of the said Act.
APPOINTMENT OF COMPETENT AUTHORITY TO PROJECT COMMITTEE UNDER THE ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 15, Public Works (W2), 22nd January 2009.]
No. II(2)/PW/32(b-2)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 26 of the Tamil Nadu Farmers’ Management Irrigation
Systems Act, 2000 (Tamil Nadu Act 7 of 2001), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the Superintending Engineer
of Water Resources Department, under whose jurisdiction the area of operation of the Project Committee lies, as the
Competent Authority for the Project Committee constituted under Section 10 of the said Act for carrying out the purposes
of the said Act.
S. AUDISESHIAH,
Principal Secretary to Government.
