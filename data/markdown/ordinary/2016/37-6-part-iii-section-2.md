V.O. CHIDAMBARANAR PORT TRUST, TUTICORIN
Recruitment Rules of Class III & IV Staff
Amendment to the Schedule II of Tuticorin Port Trust Employees (Recruitment, Seniority and Promotion)
Fourth Amendment Regulations, 2016
(No. S-3/3/2015-E.I/D.3078, dt: 24-8-2016.)
No. SRO C-18/2016.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (i) of Section 124, read with Sub Section (1 )(a) of the Major Port
Trusts Act, 1963 (38 of 1963) the Board of Trustees, V.O. Chidambaranar Port Trust hereby approves the revised manner
of recruitment (Recruitment Rules) for Class III and IV posts under Regulation 5 of Tuticorin Port Trust Employees
(Recruitment, Seniority and Promotion) Regulations, 2008 as set out in the Schedule annexed to this Notification.
2.
The said Recruitment Rules shall come into effect from the date of publication of this Notification in the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
SCHEDULE
(1) In the said Schedule under Regulations, the Recruitment Rules for the post of Superintendent
(General Administration) (Sl. No.5), the provisions under Column No.12 shall be substituted by the following—
Promotion: Head Clerk with five years regular service in the Port failing which Head Clerk with seven years regular
service put together in the grades of Head Clerk and Senior Assistant failing both Head Clerk with ten years regular service
put together in the grades of Head Clerk, Senior Assistant and Upper Division Clerk.
(2) In the said Schedule under Regulations, the Recruitment Rules for the post of Upper Division Clerk (General
Administration) (Sl.No.8), the provisions under Column No.12 shall be substituted by the following—
Promotion:
Lower Division Clerks with three years regular service in the grade in the Port.
(3) in the said Schedule under Regulations, the Recruitment Rules for the post of Supervisor (Traffic) (Sl.No.26),
the provisions under Column No.12 shall be substituted by the following—
Promotion:
Out Door Clerk/Tally Clerk with five years regular service in the grade in the Port. However, minimum educational
qualification of the Out Door Clerk shall be matriculation
(4) In the said Schedule under Regulations, the Recruitment Rules for the post of Chargeman (Mech.)
(Mech & Electrical Engineering) (Sl. No. 94), the provisions under Column No.11 shall be substituted by the following—
By promotion failing which by transfer failing both by direct recruitment. The provisions under Column No. 12 shall
be substituted by the following.
Promotion:
Assistant Chargeman (Mechanical) with five years regular service failing which, Senior Mechanic/Senior Fitter / Senior
Plater with five years regular service in the grade in the Port.
Transfer:
Employees working in the equivalent grades such as Senior Mechanic Grade I / Senior Fitter Grade I / Senior
Blacksmith Grade I / Senior Turner Grade I and in possession of the qualifications as at Col.8.
(5) In the said Schedule under Regulations, the Recruitment Rules for the post of Chargeman (Electrical)
(Mech & Electrical Engineering) (Sl. No. 95), the provisions under Column No.9 shall be substituted by the following—
Qualifications: Yes.
