HIGHWAYS AND MINOR PORTS DEPARTMENT
AMENDMENTS TO  THE SPECIAL RULES FOR THE TAMIL NADU HIGHWAYS ENGINEERING
SUBORDINATE  (WORK CHARGED) SERVICE.
[G.O. Ms. No. 16, Highways and Minor Ports (HK3), 21st January 2009.]
No. SRO B-5(a)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Highways
Engineering Subordinate (Work Charged) Service (Section 15B in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Services
Manual, 1970):—
2. The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force with effect on and from the
1st day of August 2008.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules, in rule 4, in sub-rule (b), in the Table in column (2):—
(1) for item against the entry “(1), Direct Recruitment” in column (1) the following item shall be
substituted, namely:—
“(i) Must have passed 10th Standard  examination from a recognized school or its equivalent
examination duly recognized by  Government of Tamil Nadu;” and
(2)  for item (i)  against the entry “(2) Recruitment by transfer from the post of Gang Mazdoor in the
Tamil Nadu Basic Service”, in column (1), the following item shall be substituted, namely :—
“(i) Must have passed 10th Standard  examination from a recognized school or its equivalent
examination duly recognized by Government of Tamil Nadu.”
S. AUDISESHIAH,
Principal Secretary to Government (Addl. Charge).
