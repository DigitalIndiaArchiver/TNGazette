NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
ENERGY DEPARTMENT
AMENDMENT TO THE TAMIL NADU ELECTRICITY (REORGANIZATION AND REFORMS)
TRANSFER SCHEME 2010
[G.O. Ms. No. 23, Energy (B2), 8th March 2013.]
No. II(2)/EGY/241(d)/2013.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sections 131 and 133 of the Electricity Act, 2003
(Central Act 36 of 2003) read with clause 9 and 12 of the Tamil Nadu Electricity (Reorganization and
Reforms) Transfer Scheme, 2010, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment
to the said Scheme: -
2. The amendment hereby made shall come into force on the 1st November 2012.
AMENDMENT
In the said Scheme in clause 9, in sub-clasue (1), for the expression “for a period of two years’,
the expression, “for a period of two years and six months” shall be substituted.
RAJESH LAKHONI,
Secretary to Government.
