APPOINTMENT  OF COMMISSION OF INQUIRY TO INQUIRE THE INCIDENT OF DEATH OF
THIRU E. ILLAVARASAN SON OF P. ELANGO ON 4TH JULY 2013 AT DHARMAPURI DISTRICT UNDER
THE COMMISSIONS OF INQUIRY ACT.
AMENDMENT TO NOTIFICATION
[G.O. Ms. No. 9, Public (Law & Order-F), 5th January 2018,  Margazhi 21, Hevilambi,
Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2048.]
No. II(2)/PULO/39(a)/2018.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Commissions of Inquiry Act,
1952 (Central Act LX of 1952), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the
Public (Law and Order-F) Department Notification No.ll(2)/PULO/455(h)/2013, published at pages 1-2 of
Part ll -Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, Extraordinary, dated the 8th July 2013, as
subsequently amended : -
AMENDMENT
In the said Notification, in paragraph 4, for the expression “fifty four months”, the expression
“fifty seven months” shall be substituted.
P. SENTHILKUMAR,
Principal Secretary to Government. (FAC).
