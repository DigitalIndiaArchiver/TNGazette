NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
FINANCE DEPARTMENT
(Salaries)
NOTIFICATION TO PROCUREMENT OF GOLD COINS FOR VARIOUS MARRIAGE ASSISTANCE SCHEMES
OF SOCIAL WELFARE DEPARTMENT FROM THE PUBLIC SECTOR BANKS—UNDER TAMIL NADU
TRANSPARENCY IN TENDERS ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 190, Finance (Salaries),12th July 2011, Aani 27, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No. II(2)/FIN/309(f)/2011.
Under clause (f) of Section 16 of the Tamil Nadu Transparency in Tenders Act, 1998 (Tamil Nadu
Act, 43  of 1998), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notifies the procurement of gold coins from
Public Sector Banks/State Bank of India and its Associates by the Director of Social Welfare for
providing 4 (four) grams of 22 ct. (twenty two carat) gold at “free of cost” for making “Thirumangalyam”
to the beneficiaries under the following marriage assistance schemes, namely:—
1.
“Moovalur Ramamirtham Ammaiyar Ninaivu Marriage Assistance Scheme.
2.
Dr. Dharmambal Ammaiyar Ninaivu Widow Remarriage Assistance Scheme.
3.
E.V.R. Maniammaiyar Ninaivu Marriage Assistance Scheme for Daughters of Poor Widows.
4.
Annai Theresa Ninaivu Marriage Assistance Scheme for Orphan Girls and
5.
Dr. Muthulakshmi Reddy Ninaivu Inter-Caste Marriage Assistance Scheme”.
K. SHANMUGAM,
Principal Secretary to Government.
