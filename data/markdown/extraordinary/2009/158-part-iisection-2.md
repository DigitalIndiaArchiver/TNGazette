HIGHER  EDUCATION DEPARTMENT
CONSTITUTION OF SEARCH COMMITTEE TO RECOMMEND A PANEL OF THREE NAMES TO THE CHANCELLOR FOR
SELECTION OF VICE-CHANCELLOR TO THIRUVALLUVAR UNIVERSITY, VELLORE UNDER THIRUVALLUVAR
UNIVERSITY ACT.
[G.O. (1D) No. 151, Higher Education (K2), 3rd July 2009, Aani 19, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. II(2)/HE/323(a)/2009.
Under Section 12 (1) and (2) of Thiruvalluvar University Act, 2002, the Government constitute a Search Committee
consisting of the following persons to recommend a panel of three names to the Chancellor for appointment of
Vice-Chancellor, Thiruvalluvar University, Vellore:—
(1) Dr. S. Sivasubramanian
..
Chancellor's Nominee/Convener
Former Vice-Chancellor, Bharathiar University,
Now Vice-Chancellor, Noorul Islam University,
Kumarakoil, Kanyakumari District-629 180.
(2) Dr. S. Bhaskaran,
..
Government Nominee/Member
Member-Secretary,
Tamil Nadu State Council for Higher Education,
Chennai.
(3) Prof. M.S. Palanichamy, B.E., M.Tech., Ph.D.,
..
Syndicate Nominee/Member
Former Vice-Chancellor,
Tamil Nadu Open University, Chennai-25.
2. Dr. S. Sivasubramanian, former Vice-Chancellor, Bharathiar University, as the Chancellor's Nominee, will be the
Convener of the above Search Committee.
3. The above Committee is requested to recommend a panel of three names along with their Bio-data to the Chancellor
of Thiruvalluvar University for appointment of Vice-Chancellor to Thiruvalluvar University, Vellore.
K. GANESAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
