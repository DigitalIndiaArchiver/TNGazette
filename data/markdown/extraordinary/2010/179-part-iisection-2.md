NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
HIGHWAYS AND MINOR PORTS DEPARTMENT
LEVY OF TOLLS ON VEHICLES ENTERING THE KODAIKANAL MUNICIPAL AREA IN DINDIGUL DISTRICT
FOR CERTAIN PERIOD UNDER INDIAN TOLLS ACT.
[G.O. (D) No. 78, Highways and Minor Ports (HS1), 15th June 2010, Aani 1, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No. II(2)/HWMP/ 346(d)/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred  by clause (a) of sub-section (1) and sub-section (2) of Section 2 of the Indian Tolls
Act 1851 (Central Act VIII of 1851), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby levies  tolls for a period of three years with effect on
and from 15th June 2010 on the classes of Motor Vehicles specified in Column (2) of the table below which pass through the
Dindigul – Kodaikanal Road, Palani – Kodaikanal Road and Cochin – Kodaikanal Road at Silver cascade and next to pillar
rocks in between Pillar rock and Moyar point in Kodaikanal in Dindigul District at the rates specified in the corresponding entries
in column (3) thereof provided that such levy of tolls shall not be applicable  to non - commercial motor vehicles owned by
the residents of Kodaikanal Municipal area:—
