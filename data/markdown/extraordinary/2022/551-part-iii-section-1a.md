In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-sections (1) and (1A) of section 15 of the Mines and Minerals (Development 
and Regulation) Act, 1957 (Central Act 67 of 1957), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to 
the Tamil Nadu Minor Mineral Concession Rules, 1959, namely:-
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖග.

Tiger Reserves, Elephant Corridors and Reserve Forests”, the  expression “the National Parks, Wild Life Sanctuaries,
Tiger Reserves and Elephant Corridors” shall be substituted.
