No.II(1)/PUEL/7(n)/2016.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-clause (b) of clause (2) of Article 174 of the Constitution of India,
I, Dr. K. ROSAIAH, Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby dissolve the Fourteenth Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly, with effect from
the 21st May, 2016.
Raj Bhavan, Guindy,
K. ROSAIAH,
21st May 2016.
Governor of Tamil Nadu.
K. GNANADESIKAN,
