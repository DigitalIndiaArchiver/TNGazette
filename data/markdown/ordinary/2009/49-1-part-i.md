[G.O. Ms. No. 1025, Home (SC), 2nd December 2009.]
No. I/HO/25/2009.—The following Notification of  the
Government of India, Ministry of  Home Affairs, Notification
No. I-14011/9/2008-IPS.I(II), dated 16th November 2009 is
republished in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette:—
Consequent upon declaration of inclusion of the name of
Smt. V. Jayashree as unconditional in the Select List of
2008 by the Union Public Service Commission, as
communicated through its letter dated 23rd June 2009 and
in continuation to Order dated 8th September 2009 of the
Hon'ble Central Administrative Tribunal, Madras Bench in
M.A. No. 330 of 2009 in OA. No. 441 of 2009 through which
the Order dated 8th May 2009 of the Hon'ble Central
Administrative Tribunal, Madras Bench in OA. No. 441 of
2009 has been vacated through which the Hon'ble Court had
ordered to keep the remaining post in the Tamil Nadu Cadre
of IPS vacant till the disposal of the OA and in pursuance to
Order dated 10th November 2009 of the Hon'ble Central
Administrative Tribunal, Madras Bench in O.A. No. 968 of
2009 through which the Hon'ble Court has ordered for
appointment of the officer to Indian Police Service by
promotion from Tamil Nadu Police Service from the Select
List of 2008, the President, in exercise of the
powers conferred by sub-rule (1) of Rule 9 of the
Indian Police Service (Recruitment) Rules, 1954,
read with sub-regulation (1) of Regulation 9 of the
Indian Police Service (Appointment by Promotion)
Regulations, 1955 is pleased to appoint
Smt. V. Jayashree (D.O.B. 19-12-1966), a member
of the Tamil Nadu Police Service to the Indian
Police Service on probation and to allocate her to
the Tamil Nadu Cadre under sub-rule (1) of the
Rule (5) of the Indian Police Service (Cadre) Rules,
1954. The appointment will take effect from the
date of issue of this notification.
The aforementioned appointment will be subject
to the result of the Civil Appeal [SLP (C) No. 2459/
2006], pending before the Hon'ble Supreme Court
and O.A. No. 929 of 2008 pending before the
Hon'ble C.A.T., Chennai Bench.
[No.I-14011/9/2008-IPS.I(II)]
O.S. ASHOK,
Under Secretary to the Government of India.
