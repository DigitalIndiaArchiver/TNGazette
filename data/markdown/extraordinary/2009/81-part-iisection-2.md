HIGHWAYS  DEPARTMENT
LEVY OF TOLLS ON VEHICLES ENTERING THE ROADS IN YERCAUD PANCHAYAT UNION AREA
IN SALEM DISTRICT FOR CERTAIN PERIOD UNDER THE INDIAN TOLLS ACT.
[G.O. (D). No. 52, Highways (HP-1), 19th March 2009.]
No. II(2)/HW/136(c)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (a) of sub-section (2) of Section 2 of the Indian Tolls Act, 1851 (Central
Act VIII of 1851), as amended by the Tamil Nadu Tolls and Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1938 (Tamil Nadu
Act VI of 1938) and the Indian Tolls Tamil Nadu Amendment Act, 1942 (Tamil Nadu Act XIV of 1942), the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby levies the tolls for the period from 1st April 2009 to 31st March 2010 on the classes of motor vehicles
specified in column (2) of the Table below which passes through Salem - Yercaud Road and Kuppanur - Ayothiapattinam-
Yercaud Road in the Yercaud Panchayat Union area in Salem District at the rates specified in the corresponding entries
in column (3) thereof provided that such levy of tolls shall not be applicable to (i) the motor vehicles owned by the residents
of Yercaud other than the vehicles used for commercial purpose, (ii) the office vehicles of Yercaud Panchayat Union and
(iii) the office vehicles of State and Central Governments and Government undertakings:—
