[Courts and Judges—District Judge—Thiru M. Md. Jafarullah Khan,
District Judge of Tamil Nadu State Judicial Service —
Deputation from Tamil Nadu State Judicial Service to the
Puducherry Judicial Service—Orders—Issued.]
The following Government Order is published:—
[G.O. (D) No. 111, Public  (Special. A), 29th May 2009,
Vaikasi 15, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
READ :
From the Registrar General, High Court, Madras,
D.O. Letter No. 16/2009-Con. B2, dated 5th May 2009.
Order: No. 111, Public (Special-A), 29th May 2009.
The Government have considered the recommendation of
the High Court, Madras to depute Thiru M. Md. Jafarullah Khan,
PRINTED AND PUBLISHED BY THE DIRECTOR OF STATIONERY AND PRINTING, CHENNAI
ON BEHALF OF THE GOVERNMENT OF TAMIL NADU
District Judge of the Tamil Nadu State Judicial Service functioning
as Principal District Judge, Thanjavur to Puducherry Judicial
Service for being posted as Chief Judge, Puducherry in the
place of Thiru D. Krishnaraja, who has been ordered to be
repatriated to his parent Department viz., Tamil Nadu State
Judicial Service.
2. The Government accordingly direct that Thiru
M. Md. Jafarullah Khan, District Judge of Tamil Nadu State
Judicial Service functioning as Principal District Judge,
Thanjavur be deputed to the Puducherry Judicial Service for
being posted as Chief Judge, Puducherry in the place of
Thiru D. Krishnaraja, with effect from the date of assuming
office as such.
(By Order of the Governor)
K. S. SRIPATHI,
Chief Secretary.
