Whereas, the State Government is of the opinion that the Koradacheri Town Panchayat Council persistently makes
default in performing duties imposed on it by law;
And, Whereas, the explanations of the Koradacheri Town Panchayat Council have been called for under sub-section
(1-A) of Section 41 of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920) and its explanations have
been considered by the State Government;
And, Whereas, it is considered that there is reasonable basis to dissolve the said Town Panchayat Council;
Now, therefore, in exercise of powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 41 of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities
Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby dissolves the Koradacheri Town Panchayat
Council with immediate effect and also directs that the said Town Panchayat Council shall be reconstituted with effect from
the 25th February 2010.
And, in exercise of the powers conferred by clause (b) of sub-section (3) of Section 41 of the said Act, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the Assistant Director of Town Panchayats, Thanjavur Zone to exercise and
perform all the functions of the Koradacheri Town Panchayat Council and its Chairman during the period of dissolution of
the said Town Panchayat Council.
NIRANJAN MARDI,
Secretary to Government.
