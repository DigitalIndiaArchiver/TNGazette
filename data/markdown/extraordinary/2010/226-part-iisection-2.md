The Governor of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that
the lands specified in the Schedule below are required for the
purpose for widening of Ullikkottai-Vadaseri road via
Thalikkottai and it having already been decided that the
entire amount of compensation to the awarded for the lands
is to be paid by the Divisional Engineer (NH), Thanjavur after
considering the cause shown by the owner or other person
having interest on such land as the case may be do hereby
publish the following notice under sub-section (1) of Section
15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34
of 2002).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu
Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002), the
Government of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the land specified
in the Schedule below measuring 0.12.01 hectare or little
more needed for the purpose of widening of Ullikkottai-
Vadaseri road via Thalikkottai.
The plan of the land is kept in the office of the District
Collector, Tiruvarur and may be inspected at any time during
office hours.
