Amendments to the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission Basic Service Rules
[G.O. Ms. No. 56, Rural Development and Panchayat Raj (PR-1), 5th May 2016.]
No. SRO B-34/2016.
In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby makes the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission Basic Service Rules, 2002.
(2) The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 7th May 2008.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules.-
1. in rule 5, in the TABLE, in column (3), against Serial Number 1 in column (1), the following Proviso shall be added,
namely:-
“Provided that holders of the Post of Driver in the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission Service, who
have been declared medically unfit to continue in that Post shall be appointed as Office Assistant by
recruitment by transfer.”; and
2. to rule 9, the following proviso shall be added, namely:-
“Provided that notwithstanding anything contained in clause (a) of sub-rule (1) of rule 56 of the Fundamental
Rules, any person appointed by recruitment by transfer to the category of Office Assistant from the post
of Driver shall retire from service on attaining the age of fifty eight years and shall continue to draw
pay in the scale applicable to the post of Driver.”
GAGANDEEP SINGH BEDI,
Secretary to Government.
