MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION AND WATER SUPPLY ELECTION DEPARTMENT
APPOINTMENT OF SPECIAL OFFICERS TO TOWN PANCHAYATS, MUNICIPALITIES AND 
CORPORATIONS UNDER THE ACT.
AMENDMENT TO NOTIFICATIONS
[G.O. Ms. No. 68, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (Election), 30th June 2018.,]
No. II(2)/MAWS/567(d-1)/2018.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 375-B of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu 
Act V of 1920), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Municipal Administration and 
Water Supply Department Notiﬁ cation No. II(2)/MAWS/687(c-1)/2016, published at pages 1 to 11 of Part II—Section 2 of the 
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, Extraordinary, dated the 24th October 2016, as subsequently amended. 
2. This Notiﬁ cation shall come into force with effect on and from the 1st July 2018.
AMENDMENT
In the said Notiﬁ cation, for the expression “until the 30th day of June 2018”, the expression “until the 31st day of December 
2018” shall be substituted.
