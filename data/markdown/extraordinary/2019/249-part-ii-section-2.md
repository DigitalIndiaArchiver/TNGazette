No. II(2)/IND/428(b-1)/2019.
The Government of Tamil Nadu, having been satisfied 
that the lands specified in the schedule below have to be 
acquired for Industrial purpose to wit for Establishment of New 
SIPCOT Industrial Park by SIPCOT at Adhagapadi Village 
and surrounding area and it having already been decided 
that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for 
the lands to be paid out of the lands controlled or managed 
by SIPCOT. The following notice is issued under sub-section 
(1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for 
Industrial purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act, 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu 
Acquisition of land for Industrial Purposes Act 1997 (Tamil Nadu 
Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires 
the lands specified in the schedule below and measuring 8.14.5 
Hect. (20.11 Acres) of dry lands to the same a little more or 
less needed for Industrial Purposes, to wit, for setting up of 
new Industrial Park by SIPCOT.
The plan of the lands are kept in the office of the Special 
District Revenue Officer/Land Acquisition, SIPCOT, Dharmapuri 
Taluk, Dharmapuri District and may be inspected at any time 
during office hours.
