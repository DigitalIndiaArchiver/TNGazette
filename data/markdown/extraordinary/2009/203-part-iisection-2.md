RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND PANCHAYAT RAJ DEPARTMENT
DISQUALIFICATION OF THIRUMATHI J. JEYA, PRESIDENT, PALLAPATTY PANCHAYAT, NILAKKOTTAI PANCHAYAT UNION,
DINDIGUL DISTRICT UNDER TAMIL NADU PANCHAYATS ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 85, Rural Development and Panchayat Raj (PR4),5th August 2009.]
No. II(2)RDPR/404(a)/2009.
Whereas, the opinion of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission has been sought for, regarding the disqualification of
Thirumathi J. Jeya, President, Pallapatty Panchayat,  Nilakkottai Panchayat Union, Dindigul District as required under sub-section
(2) of section 41 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994) as she has got elected to the office of
President, Pallapatty Panchayat, reserved for Scheduled Caste (Women) Candidate by falsely stating that she belonged to
Scheduled Caste Community.
And, whereas the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission, after conducting the enquiry, has stated that it is proved that the
Hindu Scheduled Caste Certificate given to Thirumathi J. Jeya by the Tahsildar, Nilakkottai in N. Dis/7910/05, dated
10th September 2005 bearing permanent Community Certificate No. 3343167, has been cancelled vide proceedings of
Revenue Divisional Officer, Dindigul N. Dis No.04284/07/Ü4, dated 16th February 2008 since the same is false and
thereby, opined that the said Thirumathi J. Jeya, President, Pallapatty Panchayat, Nilakkottai Panchayat Union, is disqualified
to continue as President of the said Panchayat under clause (e) of sub-section (3) of section 38 of the said Act;
Now, therefore, under sub-section (1) of Section 41 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby decides that Thirumathi J. Jeya, President, Pallapatty Panchayat, Nilakkottai Panchayat
Union, Dindigul District, has become disqualified from holding the office of President of the said Panchayat, under clause (e) of
sub-section (3) of Section 38 of the said Act with effect from 5th August 2009 as it is found that she does not belong to Scheduled
Caste, but has been elected from the seat reserved for Scheduled Caste (Women) subject to the out come of the order of the
High Court Madras,in WP 1654/08 filed by Thirumathi J. Jeya which is pending before the said Court.
NIRANJAN MARDI,
Secretary to Government (In-charge).
