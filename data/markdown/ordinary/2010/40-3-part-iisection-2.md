No. II(2)/CTR/621/2010.вЂ”In exercise of the powers
conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 30 read with sub-
section (4) of Section 88 of the Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax
Act, 2006 (Tamil Nadu Act 32 of 2006), the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby makes a reduction from ten per cent to
four per cent in respect of tax payable under that Act, on the
sale of Linear Alkyl Benzene (LAB) feedstock by
Tvl. Madras Refineries Limited (now known as Chennai
Petroleum Corporation Limited) to Tvl. Tamil Nadu Petro
Products Limited.
This Notification shall be deemed to have come into force
on the 1st April 1987 and shall be deemed to have remained
in force upto the 11th March 1993.
[G.O. (Ms.) No. 163, Commercial Taxes and Registration (B2),
24th September 2010, Purattasi 8, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No. II(2)/CTR/622/2010.вЂ” In exercise of the powers conferred
by sub-section (1) of Section 30 read with sub-section (4) of
Section 88 of the Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax Act, 2006
(Tamil Nadu Act 32 of 2006), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby makes a reduction from twelve per cent to three per cent
in respect of tax payable under that Act, on the sale of Linear
Alkyl Benzene (LAB) feedstock by Tvl. Madras Refineries
Limited (now known as Chennai Petroleum Corporation
Limited) to Tvl. Tamil Nadu Petro Products Limited.
This Notification shall be deemed to have come into force
on the 12th March 1993 and shall be deemed to have remained
in force upto the 16th July 1996.

No. II(2)/PUSC/625/2010.вЂ”Whereas, an order had been
made by the Government of Tamil Nadu in G.O.No.SR.I/161-5/2010,
Public (SC) Department, dated 25th May 2010 under Section
3(1)(i) of the Conservation of Foreign Exchange and Prevention
of Smuggling Activities Act, 1974 (Central Act 52 of 1974),
directing that Thiru S. Mohamed Raja Ali, son of Thiru
A. Seeni Mohamed, resident of S.K.M. Illam, Maricair Street,
Pudupattinam, Ramanathapuram District, Ramanathapuram,
be detained.
And, Whereas, the said Thiru S. Mohamed Raja Ali, son
of Thiru A. Seeni Mohamed, cannot be found;
And, Whereas, the Government of Tamil Nadu have reasons
to believe that the said Thiru S. Mohamed Raja Ali, son of
Thiru A. Seeni Mohamed, in respect of whom the detention
order has been made, has absconded and is concealing
himself so that the said detention order cannot be executed.
Now, Therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by
Section 7(1) (b) of the Conservation of Foreign Exchange and
Prevention of Smuggling Activities Act, 1974 (Central Act 52
of 1974), the Government of Tamil Nadu hereby direct the
said Thiru S. Mohamed Raja Ali, son of Thiru
A. Seeni Mohamed, resident of S.K.M. Illam, Maricair Street,
Pudupattinam, Ramanathapuram District, to appear before
the Superintendent of Police, Ramanathapuram District at his
office within a period of 15 days (Fifteen days) from the date
of publication of this order in the Tamil Nadu Government
Gazette.
