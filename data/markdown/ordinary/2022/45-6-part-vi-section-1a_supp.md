CAUVERY - VAIGAI - GUNDAR INTERLINKING CANEL PROJECT - TIRUCHIRAPPALLI DISTRICT
FORM-IV
[G.O. (Ms) No. 102. Public Works (N2), 27th April 2020 & G.O.(Ms) No.304. Public Works (N2), 11th December 2020.]
Preliminary Notification under Sub-section (1) of Section II of RECTLARR Act, 2013
(R.C.No.C1/310312020)  
No.VI(1)/489/2022.
Whereas it appears to the appropriate Government has given an  Administrative sanction that a total of  22.41.00 
hectares of land is required in Nagamangalam Village in Srirangam Taluk in Thiruchirappalli District for public purpose 
namely Cauvery-Vaigai-Gundar interlinking Flood Carrier Canal Project.  As per the Government Order No. 40 PUBLIC 
WORKS (2D) DEPARTMENT, Dated: 09.07.2020, M/s.“WAPCOS” is appointed to carry out the Environmental Impact 
Assessment study for the irrigation project (i.e) formation of Flood Water Carrier Canal from Cauvery River by interlinking 
Cauvery-Vaigai-Gundar Rivers in Karur, Trichy and Pudukottai Districts in Tamil Nadu, under section 3 (3) of The 
Environmental Protection Act 1986, the report was received.  The land is required for the formation of flood carrier 
canal for the drought prone areas in Karur, Trichy and Pudukottai Districts.  As the Environmental Impact Assessment 
study is being carried out for this irrigation project, Social Impact Assessment shall not apply according to the provision 
of sub section 2 of Section 6 of Right to Fair Compensation and Transparency in Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and 
Resettlement Act 2013. The Requisition body had not given enter upon. The Revenue Divisional Officer, Srirangam is 
appointed as Administrator for the purpose of Rehabilitation and Resettlement. Therefore, it is notified that for the above 
said project in Nagamangalam village in Srirangam Taluk in Thiruchirappalli District land measuring an extent of 19.00.74 
hectares, the details of which are specified in the schedule below, is under acquisition.
