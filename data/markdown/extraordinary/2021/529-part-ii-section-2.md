In exercise of the powers conferred by section 4 of the Tamil Nadu Manual Workers (Regulation of Employment and 
Conditions of Work) Act, 1982 (Tamil Nadu Act 33 of 1982), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, after consultation with the Advisory 
Committee, hereby makes the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu Unorganised Drivers and Automobile Workshop Workers 
Social Security and Welfare Scheme, 2006, as subsequently amended.
