In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 85 of the Electricity Act, 2003 (Central Act 
36 of 2003), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby constitutes a Selection Committee consisting of the following 
Members,  for the purpose of Selecting a Member of the Tamil Nadu Electricity Regulatory Commission:-
(i)
Thiru. S.R. Singharavelu, Retired Judge of the Madras High Court 
Chairperson
(ii)
The Chief Secretary to the Government of Tamil Nadu 
Member
(iii)
The Chairperson of the Central Electricity Regulatory Commission
Member
2.  The Selection Committee shall:- 
(a)
Finalise the Selection of Member within three months from the date on which the reference 
is made to it; 
(b)
Recommend a panel of two names for every vacancy referred to it; and 
(c)
Satisfy itself, before recommending any person for appointment as Member of the
Tamil Nadu Electricity Regulatory Commission that such person does not have any ﬁ nancial 
or other interest which is likely to aﬀ ect prejudicially his function as Member.
