Now, therefore, in pursuance of sub-section (1) of Section 39 and Section 56 of the said Act, the Election Commission
of India hereby—
(A)Appoints, with respect to the said election:
(a)
the 7th June, 2010 (Monday), as the last date  for making nominations;
(b)
the 8th June, 2010 (Tuesday), as the date for the scrutiny of the nominations;
(c)
the 10th June, 2010 (Thursday), as the last date for the withdrawal of candidatures;
(d)
the 17th June, 2010 (Thursday), as the date on which  a poll shall, if necessary, be taken; and
(e)
the  19th June, 2010 (Saturday), as the date before which the election shall be completed; and
(B) Fixes the hours from 9.00 A.M. to 4.00 P.M. as the hours during which the poll shall, if necessary, be taken in
all the above mentioned States, on the date specified above for the election.
