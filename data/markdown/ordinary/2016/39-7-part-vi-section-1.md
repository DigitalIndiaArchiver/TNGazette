GENERAL  NOTIFICATIONS
 Variations to the Approved Master Plan for the Coimbatore Local Planning Area
(Roc. No. 6644/2015/LPA-3)
[G.O. (2D) No.125, Housing and Urban Development Dept. [UD 4(1)] Dated 1-09-2016]
No.VI(1)/317/2016.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 32 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning Act,
1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972) and in exercise of powers conferred by the G.O.Ms.No.94, Housing and Urban Development
[UD4(1)] Department, dated 12-06-2009 which has been published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette
No.27, part II—Section 2, page No. 228, dated 15-07-2009 the following variations are made to the master plan for the
Coimbatore Local Planning Area approved under the said Act and published in the Housing and Urban Development
Department Notification No. II(2)/HOU/4377/94 at page 1078 of Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette,
dated the 9th November 1994.
VARIATIONS
 In the said Master Plan, in the “LAND USE SCHEDULE” under the heading “COIMBATORE LOCAL PLANNING AREA,
(COIMBATORE CORPORATION AREA)” under the sub-heading (a) Notified Detailed Development Plan No.14, Saravanampatti
Village (C) Saravanampatti Detailed Development Plan No.2
(i)
Against the entry “PUBLIC AND SEMI PUBLIC” for the expression “364 part” the
expression 364 Part (except 364/1B, 364/2A) shall be substituted.
(ii) Against the entry “RESIDENTIAL” the expression “364/1B, 364/2A shall be
added after the entry 364 part.
Coimbatore,
C. MATHIVANAN,
22nd September 2016.
Member Secretary (in-charge),
Coimbatore Local Planning Authority.
