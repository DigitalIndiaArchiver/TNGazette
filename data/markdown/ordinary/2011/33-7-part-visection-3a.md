GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI
Framing of Scheme in respect of Jamia Pallivasal, Vickramasingapuram,
Ambai Taluk, Tirunelveli District.
(Rc. No.14448/01/B1/Tnv.)
No. VI-3(a)/59/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 69 of the Wakf Act, 1995, the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board
in its Proceedings in Item No.187/07, Rc. No.14448/01/B1/Tnv, dated 10th November 2010, has approved the Scheme framed
for the Wakf viz., Jamia Pallivasal, Vickramasingapuram, Ambai Taluk, Tirunelveli District for better administration of the Wakf.
Chennai-600 001,
TURAB AHAMED,
10th June 2011.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
Framing of Scheme in respect of Pitchai Annavi Wakf - Flag Hoisting at Mohideen Andawar Pallivasal
Jamath, Senkottai, Melur, Tirunelveli District.
(Rc. No. 6863/11/B1/Tnv.)
No. VI-3(a)/60/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 64(g) (j) and (k) of the Wakf Act, 1995, the
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its Proceedings in Item No.172/10, in W.E.A.3/10/E5/Tnv, dated 29th December 2010, has
unanimously resolved to remove Jb.Mohideen Pitchai Annavi from the Muthavalliship of Pitchai Annavi Wakf - Flag Hoisting at
Mohideen Andawar Pallivasal Jamath, Senkottai, Melur, Tirunelveli District and as per Section 63 of the Wakf Act 1995 appointed
the Mohideen Andavar Pallivasal Jamath as the Managing Committee for a period of three years from the date of above order.
Chennai-600 001,
TURAB AHAMED,
10th June 2011.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.

ADVERTISEMENT OF PETITION
Notice is hereby given that a petition for a winding-up of the above named company by the High Court at Madras was
on the 18th day of October 2010 presented to the said Court by the Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Ltd., represented
by its Manager-Customer Relation, No. 17, Jamshedji Tata Road, Church Gate, Mumbai-400 020,
in the capacity as Creditor and that the said petition is directed to be heard before the Court on the
19th day of September 2011.
Any creditor, contributory or other person desirous of supporting or opposing the making of an order on the said
petition should send to the petitioner or his advocate notice of his intention signed by him or his advocates with his
name and address, so as to reach the petitioner or his advocates not later than 5 days before the date fixed for the
hearing of the petition, and appear at the hearing for the purpose in person or by his advocate. A copy of the petition
will be furnished by the undersigned to any creditor or contributory on payment of the prescribed charges for the same.
Any affidavit intended to be used in opposition to the petition should be filed in Courts and a copy served on the petitioner
or his advocate, not less than 5 days before the date fixed for the hearing.
