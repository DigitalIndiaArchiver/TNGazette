The Office Report on default for not filing statement of cases in Appeals above-
mentioned being called on for hearing before this Chamber of Hon'ble Judge on
the 7th day of September, 2011; UPON perusing the record and hearing counsel
for the appearing parties above-mentioned, the Respondent Nos. 2 to 13 not
appearing though served; THIS COURT DOTH PASS THE FOLLOWING ORDER:
"The learned counsel for the appellant states at the Bar that appeals have
become infructuous and be disposed of accordingly.
In view of the statement made at the Bar, the appeals are disposed of as
having become infructuous."
AND THIS COURT DOTH FURTHER ORDER that this ORDER be actually
observed and carried into execution by all concerned;
Witness the Hon'ble Shri Sarosh Homi Kapadia, Chief Justice of India, at the
Supreme Court, New Delhi, dated this the 7th day of September, 2011.
