Housing and Urban Development Department Notification No. II(2)/HOU/4377/94 at page 1078 of Part II—Section 2 of
the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 9th November 1994.—

and under the sub-heading No. 50. Chinnavedampatti-Chinnavedampatti D.D.Plan No. 8:—

and 394/1” shall be inserted after the expression “371pt.”

“372pt.”, (except 373/1, 2pt., 374, 375, 376, 377, 389/2, 391, 392, 393, 394/1)” shall be inserted after the
expression “373 to 443”.
