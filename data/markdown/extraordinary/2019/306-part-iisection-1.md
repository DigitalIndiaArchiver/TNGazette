Under the Explanation to Section 25 of the Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881 (Central Act XXVI of

2. Further, all employees working in Vellore and the neighbouring Districts, but registered as voters 
in 8. Vellore Parliamentary Constituency, shall also be given a paid holiday on 05-08-2019.
