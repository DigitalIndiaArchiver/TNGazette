The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that the land/lands speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below 
to be acquired for industrial purpose to wit to the   acquisition of lands for an extent of 0.03.05 hect of wet 
lands in S.No.128 in Manakkal (west) Village of Lalgudi Taluk, Tiruchirapalli District for the formation of Doubling of

of compensation to be awarded for the land/lands is to be paid out of the funds controlled or managed by 
RVNL.  The following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Lands for 
Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil 
Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the land/lands speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below 
measuring an extent of 0.03.05 hectares to the same, a little more or less needed for industrial purpose wit, for 
the formation of Doubling of Broadguage Railway Line between Villupuram and Dindigul in Manakkal (west) Village of 
Lalgudi Taluk in the District of Tiruchirapalli.
The plan of the land/lands is/are kept in the oﬃ  ce of the District Collector, Tiruchirapalli may be inspected on any 
day during oﬃ  ce hours.
