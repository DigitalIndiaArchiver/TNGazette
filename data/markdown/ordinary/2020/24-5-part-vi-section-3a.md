GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
Tamil Nadu waKf board, chennai.
Taking  Over the Direct Management of Nallur Mohideen Andavar Pallivasal Wakf,  
Aruppukottai, Virudhunagar District.
(Rc.No.7649/01/B3/VNR) 
No.VI-3(a)/50/2020.
In exercise of powers conferred under the provisions of the Wakf Act, 1995, the Special Officer Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in 
Pro.Rc.No.7649/01/B3/VNR dated: 4-03-2020 has ordered to Extend the direct management of Nallur Mohiadeen Andaver 
Pallivasal Waqf Arupukottai, Virudhunagar District under the direct control of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for further period of one 
year from the date of order i.e. 4-3-2020.
Chennai-600 001,	
R. JAINULAPTHEEN,
3rd June 2020.	
Chief Executive Officer,
	
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
Extension of period of Direct Management of Jumma Mosque, Kutchery Road, Mylapore, Chennai.
(Rc.No.12933/11/B6/chen) 
No.VI-3(a)/51/2020.—
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions  under Section 65 of the Waqf Act, 1995, the Special Officer, Tamil 
Nadu Waqf Board by order dated: 24-05-2020 has ordered to extend the period of Direct Management by one year from the date 
of order, or earlier, if revoked by the Board and also ratifies the period of Direct Management from 21-07-2019 to 23-05-2020.
Chennai-600 001,	
R. JAINULAPTHEEN,
3rd June 2020.	
District Revenue Officer/	
	
	
	
Chief Executive Officer,
	
Tamil Nadu Waqf Board.
