KVI-1 Sirumalai Bee Keeping Workers Co-operative Society
(Liq. No. 1607/86/C)
No.VI(1)/171/2012.
In the circumstance stated by the Khadi Inspector (Liquidator) with final closure Liq. No. 1607/86/C, dated
21st December 2011 and in exercise of the powers delegated under Section 140(1) of the Tamil Nadu Co-operative
Societies Act 1983 the registration of the KVI-1 Sirumalai Bee Keeping Workers Co-operative Society is hereby
cancelled and its affairs are finally closed.
This order shall take effect from 27th April 2012.
