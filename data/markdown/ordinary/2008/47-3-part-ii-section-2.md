No. II(2)/LE/571/2008.вҖ”Whereas, in the G.O. (D)

withdrawal of the ID No. 449/2000 from the file of the HonвҖҷble

to the HonвҖҷble Industrial Tribunal at Chennai, under
Section 33-B of the Industrial Disputes Act, 1947;
Now, Therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by
sub-section (1) of Section 33-B of the Industrial Disputes
Act, 1947 (Central Act XIV of 1947), the Governor of
Tamil Nadu, for the reasons stated above, hereby withdraws
the ID No. 449/2000 from the file of the HonвҖҷble Ist Additional
Labour Court, Chennai and transfers it to the HonвҖҷble
Industrial Tribunal at Chennai for disposal.
