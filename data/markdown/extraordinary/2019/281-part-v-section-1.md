PUBLIC (ELECTIONS) DEPARTMENT
BIENNIAL  ELECTIONS TO THE COUNCIL OF STATES To FILL VACANCIES OF MEMBERS  
RETIRING DURING JULY, 2019member in that House by the seat of No.12, Perambur Assembly Constituency having 
been declared vacant.
No. SRO D-4/2019.
The following Notification of the Government of India, Ministry of  Law  and Justice, Legislative Department, New Delhi, 
dated 1st July, 2019 [Ashadha 10, 1941 (Saka)] is republished:-
S.O. ….... (E).––In pursuance of Section 12 of the Representation of the People Act, 1951 (43 of 1951), the President 
is pleased to call upon the elected members of the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu to elect, in accordance 
with the provisions of the said Act and of the rules and orders made thereunder, six (6) members for the purpose of filling 
the seats of six (6) members of the Council of States whose term of office is due to expire on the 24-07-2019 on the 
expiration of their term of office.
