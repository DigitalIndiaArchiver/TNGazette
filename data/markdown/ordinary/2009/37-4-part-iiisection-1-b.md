NOTIFICATIONS  BY GOVERNMENT
FINANCE DEPARTMENT
Amendments to Tamil Nadu Co-operative  Subordinate Service Rules
[G.O. Ms. No. 397, Finance (Co-operative Audit), 21st August 2009, Aavani 5, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. SRO B-51/2009.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the rules published within the Finance Department
Notification No. SRO B-149 of 1986, dated the 31st March 1986, at pages 165 to 173 of Part lIl—Section 1(b) of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 16th April 1986.
2. The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 20th September 2000.
AMENDMENTS
In the said  Rules—
(1)  in rule 3,—
(i) in sub-rule (a), in the Table, the expression “(i) Direct recruitment; or” in column (2) against the post “2.
Senior Co-operative Auditor” in column (1), shall be omitted;
(ii) sub-rule (c) shall be omitted;
(2) in rule 6, for the expression “posts of Senior Co-operative Auditors and Junior Co-operative Auditors”, the
expression “post of Junior Co-operative Auditors” shall be substituted;
(3) in rule 8,—
(i) in sub-rule (a), for the expression “posts of Senior Co-operative Auditor or Junior Co-operative Auditor”, the
expression “post of Junior Co-operative Auditor” shall be substituted;
(ii) in sub-rule (b), in the Table, the entry “(ii) Direct recruitment” in column (2) and the corresponding entry
in column (3) thereof, against the post “2. Senior Co-operative Auditor” in column (1) shall be omitted;
(4) in rule 9, in the Table, the expression “Senior Co-operative Auditor appointed by direct recruitment” in column
(1) and the corresponding entry in column (2) thereof shall be omitted;
(5) in rule 10,—
(i) in sub-rule (a),—
(a) in the Table, the expression “1. Senior Co-operative Auditors appointed by direct recruitment” in
column (1) and the corresponding entry in column (2) thereof shall be omitted;
(b) in the fifth proviso, the expression “and Senior Co-operative Auditors” shall be omitted;
(c) the sixth proviso shall be omitted;
(ii) in sub-rule (b), the expression “Senior Co-operative Auditors and” shall be omitted; and
(6) in rule 11, in the Table, the expression “1. Senior Co-operative Auditors appointed by direct recruitment” in
column (1) and the corresponding entries in columns (2) and (3) thereof shall be omitted.
K. GNANADESIKAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
