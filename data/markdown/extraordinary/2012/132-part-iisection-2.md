(Salaries)
NOTIFICATION REGARDING THE PROCUREMENT OF SANITARY NAPKINS FOR POSTNATAL MOTHERS FROM SELF
HELP GROUPS THROUGH TAMIL NADU CORPORATION FOR DEVELOPMENT OF WOMEN LIMITED FOR THE
IMPLEMENTATION OF THE MENSTRUAL HYGIENE PROGRAMME IN TAMIL NADU UNDER TAMIL NADU
TRANSPARENCY IN TENDERS ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 162, Finance (Salaries), 18th May 2012, Vaikasi-5, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2043.]
No. II(2)/FIN/259(c)/2012.
Under clause (f) of Section 16 of the Tamil Nadu Transparency in Tenders Act, 1998 (Tamil Nadu Act 43 of 1998), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notifies the procurement of 25,40,195 packs of Sanitary Napkins for postnatal mothers from
Self Help Groups through Tamil Nadu Corporation for Development of Women Limited for the implementation of the
Menstrual Hygiene Programme in Tamil Nadu.
K. SHANMUGAM,
Principal Secretary to Government.
