HOUSING AND URBAN DEVELOPMENT
DEPARTMENT
ACQUISITION OF LANDS
[G.O. Ms. No. 225, Housing and Urban Development (UD3-2),
16th October 2012.]
No. II(2)/HOU/668(c)/2012.
Whereas, it appears to the Government of Tamil Nadu that
the Lands specified in the  Schedule below and situated in
Adambakkam Village, Alandur Taluk, Kancheepuram District
are needed for a public purpose to wit for the implementation
of the Mass Rapid Transit System alignment between
Velachery to St. Thomas Mount, Notice to that effect is hereby
given to all whom it may concern in accordance with the
provisions of sub-section (1) of Section 4 of the Land Acquisition
Act 1894 (Central Act I of 1894); And whereas, it has become
necessary to acquire the immediate possession of the lands
specified in the Schedule below, The Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby directs that the lands be acquired under the provisions
of sub-section (1) of the Section 17 of the said Act.
Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by
sub-section (2)  of Section 4 of the said Act, The Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby Authorizes The Special Tahsildar
(Land Acquisition), Mass Rapid Transit System,
Phase-II, Extension  at Thirumailai Railway Station,
Chennai-4 his staff and workman to exercise the powers
conferred by the said sub-sections.
Under Section 3(c) of the Act, the Governor of Tamil Nadu,
hereby appoints The Special Tahsildar (Land Acquisition), Mass
Rapid Transit System, Phase-II, Extension at Thirumailai Railway
Station, Chennai-4 to perform the  functions of the Collector
under the said Act.
Under sub-section (4) of Section 17 of the said Act, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that in view of the
urgency of the case, the provisions of Section 5A of the
said Act shall not apply in this case.
