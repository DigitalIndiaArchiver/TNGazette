TAMIL NADU MEDICAL COUNCIL, CHENNAI.
Decision of Special Business Meeting held on 27th August, 2019.
(Ref.No. TNMC/G496/2019)
No. VI(1)/550/2019.
The Tamil Nadu Medical Council at its Special Business Meeting held on 27th August, 2019 has passed the 
following Resolution among others. 
II. Any other matters 
Penalty for not registering with Tamil Nadu Medical Council in respect of faculties working in Corporate 
Hospitals/Teaching Hospitals and Candidates of other states pursuing PG degrees in Tamil Nadu/Pondicherry - 
imposed - Regarding.
1.1. Brief
Candidates from other states who are pursuing post graduate and DNB courses in Tamil Nadu and Pondicherry 
have to register themselves in Tamil Nadu Medical Council within 3 months of Admission. Similarly faculties from other 
States working in Medical Colleges, Corporate Hospitals and CRRI approved Institutions in Tamil Nadu have to register in 
Tamil Nadu Medical Council prior to commencing their work in the above mentioned places. Unfortunately the post 
graduate students and faculties from other states fail to register in Tamil Nadu Medical Council inspite of many instructions 
in this regard. 
1.2. Hence, the above matter was brought to the notice of the Tamil Nadu Medical Council during the Special 
business Meeting held on 27th August, 2019. 
1.3 The Tamil Nadu Medical Council considered the issue and discussed the matter in detail and subsequently 
resolved that, 
вҖң ---- (i) to impose a penalty Clause that Rs. 5,000/- (Rupees Five Thousand only) would be levied as п¬Ғ ne to 
the candidates of other states, who are studying PG courses in Tamil Nadu/Pondicherry, if not registered within the 
prescribed time (3 months). 
(ii) The penalty for such candidates for Registering at the end of the course would be Rs. 10,000/- (Rupees Ten 
Thousand only). 
(iii) The penalty for faculties from other states working in Medical Colleges and other corporate hospitals who fail 
to register prior to commencing their work would be Rs. 10,000/- (Rupees Ten thousand only) 
1.4. All the Universities / Corporate Hospitals are requested to take note of this notiп¬Ғ cation and inform all those 
Other state post Graduate Students, studying in their Universities and other state Faculties working in the Corporate 
Hospitals and Medical Colleges.
