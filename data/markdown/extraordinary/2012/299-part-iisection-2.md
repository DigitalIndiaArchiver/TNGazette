No. II(2)/PDSI/701(c)/2012.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that
the lands specified in the Schedule below have to be acquired
for a public purpose, and it having already been decided that
the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the
lands is to be paid out of funds provided by the  Chennai Metro
Rail Limited, the follwing declaration is issued under Section 6
of the Land Acquisition Act 1894 (Central Act I of 1894);
DECLARATION
Under Section 6 of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894, (Central
Act I of 1894) the Government of Tami Nadu hereby declares
that the land specified in the Schedule below and measuring
2306 Sq. Mtr., by the same a little more or less are needed for
public purpose to wit, for the implementation of Ashok Nagar
to St. Thomas Mount Track for Chennai Metro Rail Project.
Under sub-section (1) of Section 17 of the said Act, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu further directs that the possession of
the said land may be taken on the expiry of 15 days from the
date of publication of the Notice mentioned in Section 9 (1) of
the Act.
A plan of lands is kept in the Office of the Special
Tahsildar(Land Acquisition), Kancheepuram Unit, Chennai
Metro Rail Project, No.7, Conron Smith Road, Harini Towers,
2nd Floor, Gopalapuram, Chennai-86, and may be
inspected at any time during office hours.
