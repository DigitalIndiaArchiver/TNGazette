NOTIFICATION BY THE  ELECTION COMMISSION OF  INDIA
[ 1 ]
PLACE AND TIME FOR COUNTING OF VOTES FOR VICE-PRESIDENTIAL ELECTION, 2012.
No. SRO G-35/2012.
The following Notification of the Election Commission of India, Nirvachan Sadan, Ashoka Road, New Delhi-110 001,
dated 30th July, 2012 [8 Sravana, 1934 (Saka)] is republished:—
No.480/2012-CC & BE—In pursuance of rule 27 of the Presidential and Vice-Presidential Elections Rules, 1974, the
Election Commission of India hereby appoints the 7th August, 2012 (Tuesday), as the day on which and 6.00 p.m. of that
day as the time at which, the counting of votes for the Vice-Presidential Election, 2012 shall take place in the office of
Returning Officer in Room No.63 of the Parliament House, New Delhi.
(By order)
K.N. BHAR,
