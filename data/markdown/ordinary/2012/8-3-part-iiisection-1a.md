Draft Amendments to Tamil Nadu Factories Rules, 1950
[G.O. Ms. No. 30, Labour and Employment (M-2), 22nd February 2012,
Maasi 10, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2043.]
No. SRO A-7/2012.—The following draft of amendments to the Tamil Nadu Factories Rules, 1950 which it is proposed
to make in exercise of the powers conferred by Section 112 read with section 7-AA of the Factories Act,  1948 (Central Act
LXIII of 1948) is hereby published for the information of all persons likely to be affected thereby, as required by Section 115
of the said Act.
2.
Notice is hereby given that the said draft amendments will be taken into consideration of or after the expiry of
forty five days from the date of publication of this Notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette and any objection
or suggestion, which may be received from any person with respect thereto before the expiry of the aforesaid period will
be considered by the Government of Tamil Nadu.  Objection or suggestion, if any, should be addressed to the Principal
Secretary to Government, Labour and Employment Department, Secretariat, Chennai-600 009 through the Chief Inspector
of Factories, Chennai-600 005 in duplicate.
