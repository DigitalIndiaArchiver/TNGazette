RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND PANCHAYAT RAJ DEPARTMENT
AMENDMENT TO THE TAMIL NADU LOCAL BODIES (ELECTION OF MEMBERS
TO THE DISTRICT PLANNING COMMITTEE) RULES, 1999.
[G.O. Ms. No. 09, Rural Development and Panchayat Raj (PR-1), 24th January 2014.]
No. SRO A-5(a)/2014.
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (a) of sub-section (2) of Section 241 and
sub-section (1) of  Section 242 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Tamil Nadu Local Bodies
(Election of  Members to the District Planning Committee) Rules, 1999.
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in rule 2, for clause (xviii), the following clause shall be substituted, namely:—
“(xviii) “Voter” means a member of the District Panchayat, in the case of election to Rural Segments
and a member of a Town Panchayat or a councilor of a Municipal Council or Municipal Corporation,
in the case of election to Urban Segments, whose name is entered in the voters list of the District concerned
for the time being in force.”.
N.S. PALANIAPPAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
