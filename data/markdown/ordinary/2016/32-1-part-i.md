NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
PUBLIC DEPARTMENT
(Special-A)
Secretariat, 10th August 2016
No.I/PUSP/17/2016
[Courts and Judges - District Judges - Repatriation of Thirumathi
V. Sofana Devi, District Judge of Puducherry Judicial Service on Deputation as
XII Additional Judge (CBI Cases), City Civil Court, Chennai, to her parent Department
viz., Puducherry Judicial Service - Orders - Issued.]
The Following Government Order is Published:—
[G.O. (D) No.291, Public (Special-A) 20th July 2016,
Aadi-5, Thunmugi Thiruvallur Aandu-2047.
READ:
From the Registrar General, High Court, Madras, D.O. Letter
No.11499-A/2016/2016-B2, dated 28-03-2016.
ORDER: No. 291, Public (Special-A), 20th July 2016.
Accepting the proposal of High Court sent by the Registrar General High Court,
Madras, the Government direct that Thirumathi V. Sofana Devi, District Judge of
Puducherry Judicial Service, functioning on deputation in the Tamil Nadu State Judicial
Service as XII Additional Judge (CBI Cases), City Civil Court, Chennai be repatriated to
her parent Service viz., Puducherry Judicial Service for being posted as member
Secretary, Union Territory of Puducherry Legal Services Authority, Pondicherry in the
existing vacancy.
(By Order of the Governor)
RAMA MOHANA RAO,
                                            
    Chief Secretary.
