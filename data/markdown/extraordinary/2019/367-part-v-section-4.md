NOTIFICATION BY THE ELECTION COMMISSION OF INDIA
APPOINTMENT OF DATES FOR VARIOUS STAGES OF BYE-ELECTION TO TAMIL NADU 
LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY FROM 227.NANGUNERI ASSEMBLY CONSTITUENCY AND 
FIXATION OF HOURS OF POLL TO FILL A VACANCY CAUSED BY THE RESIGNATION 
OF A MEMBER
No.  SRO G-54/2019.
The following Notification of the Election Commission of India, Nirvachan Sadan, Ashoka 
Road, New Delhi-110 001, dated 23rd September, 2019 [1 Asvina, 1941 (Saka)] is published:
No.100/TN-LA/24/2019:- Whereas, the seat of Shri H Vasantha Kumar, in the Legislative 
Assembly of the Tamil Nadu State, elected from  227-Nanguneri Assembly Constituency has 
become vacant on 29-05-2019, by reason of resignation; and
2.	
Whereas, a bye-election is to be held for the purpose of filling the vacancy so caused; 
and
3.	
Now, therefore, in pursuance of sub-section (1) of Section 150 and Sections 30 
and 56 of the Representation of the People Act, 1951 (43 of 1951), the Election Commission of 
India hereby:-
	
(A)  Calls upon the said 227-Nanguneri Assembly Constituency in the State of 
Tamil Nadu to elect, before the 27-10-2019 (Sunday) and in accordance with the provisions of 
the said Act and of the rules and orders made thereunder, a person, for the purpose of filling the 
said vacancy in the Legislative Assembly of the State; and

 (B)	Appoints, with respect to the said election,-	
	
	
	
(a)  the 30-09-2019 (Monday), as the last date for making nominations;
	
	
(b)  the 1-10-2019 (Tuesday),  as the date for scrutiny of nominations;
	
	
(c)  the 03-10-2019 (Thursday), as the last date for withdrawal of candidatures; 
	
	
(d)	  the 21-10-2019 (Monday), as the date, on which a poll shall, if necessary, be taken;
	
	
(e)  the 27-10-2019 (Sunday), as the date before which the election shall be completed, and 
	
 (C)	Fixes the hours from 7.00 A.M. to 6.00 P.M. as the hours during which the poll shall, if necessary, be taken 
on the date specified above, for the election.
