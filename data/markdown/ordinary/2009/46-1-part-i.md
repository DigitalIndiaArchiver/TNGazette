No.I/PUSP/23/2009.—The Government have considered the
recommendation of the High Court, Madras to depute Thiru D. Jayachandran,
District Judge of the Tamil Nadu State Judicial Service functioning as Additional
Chief Metropolitan Magistrate (E.O-II), Egmore, Chennai to Puducherry Judicial
Service for being posted as Additional District Judge, Karaikal in the  place of
Thiru P. Velmurugan, who has been ordered to be repatriated to his parent
Department viz., Tamil Nadu State Judicial Service.
2. The Government accordingly  direct that Thiru D. Jayachandran,
District Judge of Tamil Nadu State Judicial Service functioning as Additional
Chief  Metropolitan Magistrate (E.O-II), Egmore, Chennai be deputed to the
puducherry  Judicial Service for being posted as Additional District Judge,
Karaikal in the place of Thiru P. Velmurugan, with effect from the date of
assuming office as such.
K.S. SRIPATHI,
Chief Secretary.
