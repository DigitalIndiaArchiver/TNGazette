Elections to the Syndicate, the Senate and the Standing Committee of the Academic Council
By the Academic Council.
[No. V.1/AC Election/01-2016/064.]
No. VI-3(b)/3/2016.
It is hereby notified that the election to [i] THE SYNDICATE, [ii] THE SENATE AND [iii] THE STANDING COMMITTEE
OF THE ACADEMIC COUNCIL will be held under Section 18[a] - Other Members [2], Section 14(a) - Other Members [2]
and Section 24[k] of the Madras University Act, 1923.
[i] TO THE SYNDICATE - SECTION 18[a] - OTHER MEMBERS [2].
TWO MEMBERS  to be elected by the Academic Council from among its members of one shall be teacher of affiliated
colleges and one shall be a Teacher of Approved Colleges.
The person getting elected will hold office as a member of the Syndicate for the residual period/ full term
(three years) from the date of receipt of nomination / the withdrawal / the period of present member term to be ceased
/ the date of election whichever is applicable for the election, as the case may be.
[ii] TO THE SENATE - SECTION 14[a] - OTHER MEMBERS [2]
TWO MEMBERS  to be elected by the Academic Council from among its members.
[iii] TO THE STANDING COMMITTEE OF THE ACADEMIC COUNCIL [Section 24 [k]
ONE MEMBER shall be the Principal or Teacher of Affiliated Colleges.
The newly elected members will hold office as a member of the Standing Committee of the Academic Council for
the residual period from the date of election/the date of withdrawal/the date of election/the date of declaration of results
whichever is applicable for the election.
The above election will be conducted on the date of the meeting of the Academic Council scheduled to be held
on SATURDAY, THE 27th February 2016.
