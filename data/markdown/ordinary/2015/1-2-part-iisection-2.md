No. II(2)/HWMP/1/2015.—The Governor of Tamil Nadu
having been satisfied  that the lands in the schedule below
are required for the purpose of provision of Project facilities
such as  construction of Bus Bay, Junction Improvement, etc.
in Chennai Outer Ring Road, Phase-I  at No. 66.
Malayambakkam Village in Sriperumbudur Taluk, Kancheepuram
District and having already been decided that the entire amount
of compensation to be awarded for lands is to be paid by the
Highways Department and after having considered the cause
shown by the owner or other person having interest in the said
land, as the case may be, do hereby publish the following
notice under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu
Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34/2002).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu
Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) the Government
of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands specified in the
Schedule below and measuring 3914 Square Meters to be the

Bus Bay, Junction Improvement, etc. in Chennai Outer Ring
Road, Phase-I at No. 66.Malayambakkam Village in
Sriperumbudur  Taluk, Kancheepuram District.
The plan of the lands are kept  in the office of the District
Revenue Officer (Land Acquisition), Chennai Outer Ring Road
Project, Chennai Metropolitan Development Authority,
Koyambedu, Chennai -600 092 and may be inspected at any
time during office hours.
