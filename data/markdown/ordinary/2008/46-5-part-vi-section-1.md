Modified Declaration of Multistoreyed Residential and Amenities Blocks Complex Building Area in Thalambur
Village, Chengalpattu Taluk of Mamallapuram Local Planning Area.
(Roc. No. 830/2008/MP1.)
No. VI(1)/405/2008.
The land comprising in S.F.Nos. 178/1A, 2, 179/1,2, 181/1A, 3A1, 182/1,2,3A, 183/2A, and 184/1- Thalambur Village,
Chengalpattu Taluk to an extent of 2140 Hectares, (52.85 Acres) is declaring as Multistoreyed Residential and amenities
Blocks complex Building area upto 60 Meters height under rule 4(a) of Multistoreyed and Public Building Rules 1973. In
addition to that it is also requested to ensure the compliance of the following conditions at the time of clearing of Building
Plans.
Conditions:
1.The Multistoreyed Residential and amenities Complex Building should be constructed to a Maximum height of
60 Metres with conditions that the building should be satisfying the Floor Space Index, Plot coverage Parameteres and
the applicant should obtain necessary clearance from the Authorities Viz. Commissioner of Town and Country Planning,
Highways, Police Department, Fire Services department, Microwave Authority and the applicant should obtain EIA
clearance from the Ministry of Environment and forest Department, Government of India as per Central Government's
latest Notification and submit it to the authority concerned.
2. The Multistoreyed Residential and amenities Complex Building should be constructed as Earth quake resistance
structure. The building shall be structurally designed by the registered professional structural Engineer, who shall also
sign in all the plans.
3. The Multistoreyed Residential and amenities Complex Building should be designed based on NBC 2005, and an
undertaking should be given by the developer to this office.
4. Ramp must be provided to lift room for physically handicapped persons.
5. Necessary parking space should be provided near the entrance for physically challenged persons.
6. Suitable drainage facilities and sewage treatment plant should be provided with in the complex.
7. Sufficient lifts must be provided as per Tamil Nadu Lifts Act and Rules 1997 and the building should satisfy all
the Multistoreyed and Public Building Rules 1973.
8. Fire Extinguishes should be provided wherever necessary and also no-objection certification must be obtain from
the Fire Services Authority.
9. The Rain Water Harvesting  must be provided as per the G.O.Ms. No. 138, Municipal Administration and Water
Supply department, dated 11th October 2002 and the arrangements should follow the countour at the site Detailed Map
should be produced.
10. Suitable arrangements should be made for tapping solar energy in the buildings, as per G.O.MS. No. 112
Municipal Administration and Water Supply department, dated 16th August 2002.
11. FAR and plot coverage should be adopted as per Rule 9, Table 3 of the Multistoreyed and Public Building
Rules 1973.
12. Ramp ratio should be mentioned in the plan as 1:10.
13. The floor Height will be minimum 3.00 Meters
14. Open stair case for emergency escape should be provided in the building
15. Expansion joints are to be provided in the building at 45 Meters intervals.
16. Necessary lightning arrester should be provided.
17. NOC should be obtained from Highways Department about road widening if any.
18. NOC should be obtained from Airport Authority and Microwave etc.
19. A Detailed report should be obtained from Anna University and Tamil Nadu Pollution Control Board about STP
adequacy.
20. Clearance Certificate should be obtained from CRAC.

Constitution of Separate District Munsif Court At Sankarapuram.
(Roc. No. 2469/C/2007/G/Judn.)
NOTIFICATION-I
(Roc. No. 2469/C/2007/G/Judn.)
No. VI(1)/410/2008.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873)
the High Court Madras, hereby fixes Sankarapuram in Villupuram District as the place at which the District Munsif
Court, Sankarapuram, shall be held.
NOTIFICATION-II
(Roc. No. 2469/C/2007/G/Judn.)
No. VI(1)/411/2008.
In exercise  of the powers conferred by section 11 of the Tamil  Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of
1873) the High Court Madras, hereby directs that in Villupuram District, the District Munsif Cout, Kallakurichi, shall
cease to have local jurisdiction over the entire Revenue Taluk of Sankarapuram and the District Munsif Court,
Sankarapuram shall have and exercise local jurisdiction over the entire Revenue Taluk of Sankarapuram with effect
from the date on which the District Munsif  Sankarapuram assumes charge of that court.
NOTIFICATION-III
(Roc. No. 2469/C/2007/G/Judn.)
No. VI(1)/412/2008.
The High Court, Madras hereby directs and notifies that the District  Munsif  Sankarapuram in Villupuram District
shall exercise all the powers conferred on a District Munsif under Section 12 of the Tamil Nadu  Cilvil courts Act, 1873
(Central Act III of 1873) as amended up-to-date in regard to original suits and proceedings not otherwise exempted
from his cognizance of which the amount or value of the subject matter does not exceed One Lakh Rupees within the
local limits of his jurisdiction with effect from the date on which the District Munsif Sankarapuram assumes charge of
that court.
NOTIFICATION-IV
(Roc. No. 2469/C/2007/G/Judn.)
No. VI(1)/413/2008.
In exercise  of  the power by Section 28 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act  III of 1873) the
High Court, Madras, hereby directs that the District Munsif,’ Sankarapuram shall have and exercise jurisdiction of a
court of Small Causes under the provincial Small Causes Court Act, 1887 for the trial suits cognizable by a court of
small causes upto the pecuniary’ limits of Rs.5000 (Rupees five thousand only) with effect from the date on which the
District Munsif, Sankarapuram assumes charge of that Court.

Constitution of Separate District Munsif Court at Palladam.
(Roc. No. 806/2008/G/Judn.)
No. VI(1)/414/2008.
In modification of the Notification No. VI (1)/651 (a-1)/95 to No. VI (1)/651 (a-5)/95 published at pages 1 and 2
under part VI Section 1 of  the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary dated 6th June 1995, the following
Notification are issued in pursuance of G.O. Ms. No. 1081, Home (Cts. III) Department, dated 22nd August 2008 for
constitution of exclusive separate District Munsif  Court, Palladam.
NOTIFICATION-I
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873)
the High Court, Madras, hereby fixes Palladam in Coimbatore District as the place at which the District Munsif Court,
Palladam, shall be held.
 NOTIFICATION-II
(Roc. No. 806/2008/G/Judn.)
No. VI(1)/415/2008.
In exercise of the powers conferred by section 11 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873)
the High Court, Madras, hereby directs that in Coimbatore District, the District Munsif Court,  Palladam, shall have and
exercise local jurisdiction over the entire Revenue Taluk of Palladam with effect from the date on which the District
Munsif, Palladam assumes charge of that court.
NOTIFICATION-III
(Roc. No. 806/2008/G/Judn.)
No. VI(1)/416/2008.
The High Court, Madras hereby directs and notifies that the District Munsif, Palladam in Coimbatore District shall
exercise all the powers conferred on a District Munsif under Section 12 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873
(Central Act III of 1873) as amended up-to-date in regard to original suits and proceedings not otherwise exempted
from his cognizance of which the amount or value of the subject matter does not exceed One Lakh Rupees within the
local limits of his jurisdiction with effect from the date on which the District Munsif Palladam assumes charge of
that court
NOTIFICATION-IV
(Roc. No. 806/2008/G/Judn.)
No. VI(1)/417/2008.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 28 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873)
the High Court, Madras, hereby directs that the District Munsif  Palladam shall have and exercise jurisdiction of a Court
of Small Causes under the provincial Small Causes Court Act, 1887 for the trial suits cognizable by a Court of small
causes upto the pecuniary limits of Rs.5000 (Rupees five thousand only) with effect from the date on which the District
Munsif Palladam, assumes charge of  that Court.
 Change of Nomenclature of the Court at Palladam.
(Roc. No. 806/2008/G/Judn.)
No. VI(1)/418/2008.
Whereas the Government of Tamil Nadu have issued orders in . G.O.Ms. No. 1081, Home (Cts. III) Department
dated 22nd August 2008 that a separate District Munsif Court be constituted at Palladam and the existing District
Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Palladam be converted as exclusive Judicial Magistrate Court, Palladam.
Hence, it is hereby informed that on and from the date on which the District Munsif  Court,  Palladam is constituted,
the nomenclature of the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Palladam shall be changed as the Judicial
Magistrate Court, Palladam.
High Court, Madras,
R. MALA,
10th November 2008.

Certain Chennai Matropolitan Area, Vichoor Village, Thiruvallur District.
(Letter No. R1/3934/2008.)
No. VI(1)/419/2008.

Development Department, dated 01-06-1984 under Section 91(1) of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning Act,
1971, the Member-Secretary, Chennai Metropolitan Development Authority hereby makes the following Variation under

No. 266, Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 2nd September 2008.

The expression "Map P.P.D./M.P. II (V) No. 6/2008"
to be read with "Map No. MP-II/CMA (VP) 53/2008".

S. Nos. 129/3 and 134/1 of Vichoor Village, Thiruvallur District classified as "Primary Residential Use Zone" is now
reclassified as "Industrial Use Zone" as per this Notification.
