COMMISSIONERATE OF TOWN PANCHAYATS, CHENNAI - 600 028.
AMENDMENT TO THE TOWN PANCHAYATS,ADMINISTRATION - DELIMITATION OF WARDS IN 
DHALI TOWN PANCHAYAT, TIRUPPUR DISTRICT.
(Roc. No. 7344/2019/C2)
No.VI(2)/135(a)/2021.
WHEREAS the Government have included the settlement areas of Kurumalai, Melkurumalai, Poochikottamparai 
and Thirumoorthimalai in the local areas of Dhali-2 Revenue Village within the local authority of Dhali Town 
Panchayat under section 3-P of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920(Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920) 
in G.O.(Ms)No.110, Municipal Administration and Water Supply Department, dated the 25th November 2021 
and published at page 1 in Part II-Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated 
the 25th November 2021; 
AND WHEREAS, based on the recommendation of the Delimitation Commission, the Inspector, has ﬁ xed the number 
of members of the council of Dhali Town Panchayat in Tiruppur District, inclusive of the Chairman, as seventeen (17) 
under section 3 - T of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920) read with the proviso 
to rule 2 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats (Fixation of Strength of Councillors) Rules, 1996, with the prior approval 
of the Government of Tamil Nadu obtained in G.O.(Ms) No.135, Municipal Administration and Water Supply Department, 
dated the 24th December 2021; 
AND WHEREAS the Delimitation Commission constituted under the Tamil Nadu Delimitation Commission Act, 2017 
(Tamil Nadu Act 23 of 2017) has submitted its recommendation on the Delimitation of the territorial Wards of Dhali 
Town Panchayat to the Government, after having published the draft proposal, based on the last preceding census 
(2011 census) of which the relevant ﬁ gures have been published for general information by inviting and processing the 
objections / suggestions received thereon; 
AND WHEREAS, under clause (11-B) of section 3 of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 
(Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920), the Director of Town Panchayats has been appointed by the State Government 
to exercise and perform the powers and duties of the Inspector in respect of Town Panchayats under the said 
Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920 in G.O.(Ms.) No.150, Municipal Administration and Water Supply Department, dated 
the 3st May 1994; and published in Part II-Section 2 in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, Extraordinary, 
dated the 24th December 2021;
