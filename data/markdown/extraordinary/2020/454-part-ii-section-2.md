TAMIL  NADU  GOVERNMENT  GAZETTE  EXTRAORDINARY
Order: No. 611, Revenue and Disaster Management 31st October 2020.
Considering the recommendations of the expert team of Doctors and Public Health specialists and based on the directives 
of Government of India, Ministry of Home Aﬀ airs, State-wide lockdown was extended from time to time and lastly extended 
up to 31-10-2020 under the Disaster Management Act, 2005 with certain restrictions and relaxations along with guidelines 
vide Government Order 7th read above.
2. In the Government orders 2nd and 3rd read above, the TASMAC Liquor Retail Vending Shops were permitted to 
function (other than Chennai) from 10.00 A.M. to 8.00 P.M., and in Chennai from 10.00 A.M. to 8.00 P.M. based on the orders 
issued in 4th and 5th read above all shops in the State were permitted to open till 8.00 P.M. Further, in the Government 
Order 8th read above all shops across the State were permitted to function upto 10.00 P.M.
3. In the reference 9th read above, the Managing Director, TASMAC has requested to issue necessary orders for permitting 
the TASMAC Liquor Retail Vending Shops to function at its usual time from 12.00 Noon to 10.00 P.M.
 4. The Government after careful examination of the request of the Managing Director, TASMAC have decided to  permit 
the TASMAC Liquor Retail Vending Shops to function at its usual time from 12.00 Noon to 10.00 P.M. and accordingly amend 
the GO.Ms.No.541, Revenue and Disaster Management (DM-IV) Department, dated 30-09-2020 as follows:-
Under Caption II–(Permitted Activities) the following Section (vi)  shall be added:
(vi) TASMAC Liquor Retail Vending Shops shall be permitted  to function at  its usual time 
 
   from 12.00 Noon to 10.00 P.M.
 (By order of the Governor)
