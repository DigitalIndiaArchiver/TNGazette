 ACQUISITION OF LANDS
(D.Dis. H2/30584/10.)
No. VI(1)/156(b)/2011.
The Principal Secretary and Commissioner of Land
Administration, Chennai having been satisfied that the lands
specified in the Schedule below have to be acquired for a
public purpose and it having already been decided that  the
entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands is
to be paid out of public revenues, the following Declaration is
issued Under Section 6 of the Land Acquisition Act 1894
(Central Act 1 of 1894).
DECLARATION
Under Section 6 of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894 (Central
Act 1 of 1894) the Principal Secretary  and Commissioner of
Land Administration hereby declares that the lands specified
in the Schedule below and measuring 1.34.5 hectare being
the same a little more or less are needed for public purpose
to wit for the constructing  across Vilangudi Village Odai at
Ayanathur Village in Ariyalur Taluk to Supply Water Vilangudi
Eri and Periya Eri at Thelur Village in favour of Public Works
Department.  A plan of the lands is kept in the office of the
Revenue Divisional Office, Ariyalur and may be inspected
any time duringoffice hours.
