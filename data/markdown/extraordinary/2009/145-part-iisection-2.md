No. II(2)/HWMP/288(d)/2009.
The Governor of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that
the lands specified in the Schedule below are required for
the purpose of formation of Puducherry Byepass Road and
it having already been decided that the entire amount of
compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid by
Divisional Engineer (H) (NH), Villupuram after considering
the cause shown by the owner or other person having
interest on such lands, as the case may be do hereby
publish the following notice under sub-section (1) of Section
15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act
34 of 2002).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu
Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands specified
in the schedule below admeasuring 4.20.5 hectares of wet
and dry lands and 00185 sq.m of Natham Poramboke
lands, a little more or less required for the purpose of
formation of Puducherry Byepass Road.
The plan of the lands is kept in the office the Revenue
Divisional Officer, Villupuram District and may be inspected
at any time during office hours.
