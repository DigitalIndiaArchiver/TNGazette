In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (1) of Section 43-B, Section 23-A, Section 68 and Section 73
of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920) and sub-rule (4) of Rule 99 of the
Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities, Municipalities and Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006, the
Tamil Nadu State Election Commission hereby fixes 2nd November 2009 as the date for convening a meeting to elect
Chairman, Vice-Chairman, Member of Appointment Committee, Member of Taxation Appeal Committee, Member  of
