Amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Medical Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 344, Health and Family Welfare (B2), 20th November 2015,
Karthigai 4, Manmadha, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2046.]
No. SRO B-95/2015.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Medical
Service (Section 21 in volume II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1969).
2. The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on from the 6th July1993.
AMENDMENT
In the said Special Rules, in Part II, in Branch-I-Medical, for the expressions “Assistant Dental Surgeon” and
“Assistant Dental Surgeons” wherever they occur, the expressions “Assistant Surgeon (Dental)” and
“Assistant Surgeons (Dental)” shall, respectively, be substituted.
J. RADHAKRISHNAN,
Secretary to Government.

No. SRO B-96/2015.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Sections 8 and 10 of the Tamil Nadu District Police Act, 1859
(Central Act XXIV of 1859) and Sections 9 and 11 of the Chennai City Police Act, 1888 (Tamil Nadu Act III of 1888) read
with the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following
amendments to the Tamil Nadu Special Police Subordinate Service Rules (Section 44 in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu
Services Manual, 1986), as subsequently amended:—
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules,-
(1) in rule 7, in sub-rule (a), in the Table, in column (2), against the entry “6(a) Constables (other than those mentioned
in (b) to (h) below)” in column (1), for clause (1), the following clauses shall be substituted namely:—
“(1) By absorption of members of the Tamil Nadu Special Police Youth Brigade who have successfully qualified in the
common state-level written examination conducted by the Tamil Nadu Uniformed Services Recruitment Board under
rule 6 of the Tamil Nadu Special Police Youth Brigade Rules, 2013, based on merits and availability of vacancies, subject to
communal reservation;
(1-A) If no suitable candidate is available for appointment by the above method, by direct recruitment; (or)”;
(2) in rule 14, in sub-rule (f), in the Table, in column (2), against the entry “Category 6 Constables” in column (1),
in clause (2), after sub-clause (ii), the following explanation shall be added, namely:—
“Explanation.- This rule shall not apply to appointment to the post by absorption of members of the Tamil Nadu
Special Police Youth Brigade,”; and
(3) in rule 16, to sub-rule (1), the following proviso shall be added, namely:—
“Provided further that a person appointed as constable by absorption of the members of the Tamil Nadu
Special Police Youth Brigade shall undergo six months of basic institutional training followed by one month practical training”.

No. SRO B-97/2015.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu
Jail Subordinate Service (Section 17 in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1970).
AMENDMENT
In the said Special Rules, in Part II, under the heading “Branch I- Executive Staff ”, to rule 6, the following proviso shall
be added, namely:-
“Provided that all the physical measurements prescribed in the Annexure shall be rounded off to the nearest centimeter
or the nearest 0.5 centimeter, as the case may be.”.

Principal Secretary to Government.
