MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION AND WATER SUPPLY DEPARTMENT
APPOINTMENT OF CERTAIN PERSON AS ENGINEERING DIRECTOR OF TAMIL NADU WATER SUPPLY
AND DRAINAGE BOARD UNDER THE TAMIL NADU WATER SUPPLY AND DRAINAGE BOARD ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 143, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (WS.3), 2nd August 2010.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/486(k)/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 4 of the Tamil Nadu Water Supply and
Drainage Board Act, 1970 (Tamil Nadu Act 4 of 1971), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints
Thiru A. Dhanusu, Chief Engineer,  Tamil Nadu Water Supply and Drainage Board, Southern Region, Madurai as
the Engineering Director of the Tamil Nadu Water Supply and Drainage Board, with effect on and from the
2nd August 2010.
K. ASHOK VARDHAN  SHETTY,
 Principal Secretary to Government.
