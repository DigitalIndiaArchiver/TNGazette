HOME, PROHIBITION AND EXCISE DEPARTMENT
Amendment to the Tamil Nadu Liquor Retail Vending (in shops and Bars) Rules 2003.
[G.O. Ms. No. 75, Home, Prohibition and Excise (VI), 16th December 2013.]
No. SRO A-33/2014.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Sections  17-C, 17-D, 21 and 22-D read with Section 54 of the
Tamil Nadu Prohibition Act, 1937 (Tamil Nadu Act X of 1937), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the
following amendment to the Tamil Nadu Liquor Retail Vending (in Shops and Bars) Rules, 2003:-
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 29th March 2013.
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, for rule 7, the following rule shall be substituted, namely :-
“7. Renewal of licence.—The licence issued to the Corporation under rule 4 is renewable by the Commissioner of
Prohibition and Excise on payment of annual privilege fee Rs.1,00,000/- (Rupees one lakh only), licence fee of
Rs.1,000/- (Rupees one thousand only) and application fee of Rs.100/- (Rupees one hundred only) on or before the
28th November.”
 NIRANJAN MARDI,
Principal Secretary to Government.
