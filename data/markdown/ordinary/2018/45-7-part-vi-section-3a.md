TAMIL NADU WAQF BOARD, CHENNAI
Taking over of Direct Management of Gudu Bee Jumma Pallivasal, Thangal, Tiruvallur District.  
(Rc.No.2486/18/B4/TVLR.)
No. VI-3(a)/191/2018.
In exercise of powers conferred as the provisions under Section 65 of the Waqf Act 1995, as amended by Act 
27/2013 the Tamil Nadu Waqf Board in its resolution in item No. 135/18 in R.C.No.2486/18/B4/TVLR, dated: 28-09-2018 
has resolved to take over the Gudu Bee Jumma Pallivasal, Thangal, Tiruvallur District under direct Management for a 
period of SIX MONTHS from the date of Publication and to appoint the Superintendent of Waqfs, Poonthemallee Zone 
as its Executive Oﬃ  cer.
