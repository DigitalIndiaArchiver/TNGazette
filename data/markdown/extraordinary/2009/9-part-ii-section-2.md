NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
DTP—II-2 Ex. (9)
HIGHWAYS AND MINOR PORTS DEPARTMENT
ACQUISITION OF LANDS
[G.O.Ms. No. 8, Highways and Minor Ports (HF-2),
7th January 2009.]
No. II(2)/HWMP/22(a)/2009.
The Governor of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that
the lands specified in the Schedule below are required for
the purpose of formation of Chidambaram Byepass Road
and it having already been decided that the entire amount
of compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid
by Divisional Engineer (Highways), Tamil Nadu Road Sector
Project, Vridhachalam after considering the cause shown
by the owner or other person having interest on such lands,
as the case may be, do hereby publish the following notice
under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu
Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the
Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of
2002), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the
lands specified in the Schedule below admeasuring
0.01.80 hectare, to the same, a little more or less required
for the purpose of formation of Chidambaram Byepass Road.
The plan of the lands is kept in the Office of the Special
Tahsildar (Land Acquisition), Tamil Nadu Road Sector
Project, Vridhachalam, Cuddalore District and may be
inspected at any time during office hours.
THE SCHEDULE
Cuddalore District, Chidambaram Taluk,
Parameswaranallur Village.
Government, wet, Old S.No. 174, New S.No. 174-2,
Owner/Person Interested, S. Senthil—0.01.80 hectare.
Total—0.01.80 hectare.
S. AUDISESHIAH,
