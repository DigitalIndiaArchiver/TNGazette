“Winding up the affairs of Mohamed Ameen Auto Drivers Industrial Co-operative Society Ltd., IND.No.27/MDS/2011

Co-operatives, Chennai vide Proc. No.4260/ICF/2015 dated 10-09-2019 and Under Section 138(1) of the same Act, 
the Deputy Director (Indl Co-ops), Chennai, has also been appointed as Official Liquidator of the said society for the 
purpose of liquidation.”
