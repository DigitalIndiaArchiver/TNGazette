No. I/Pu/6/2020.— 
[Raj Bhavan, Chennai-Major APS Rathore (IC-75817N) 
(Indian Army) Aide-de-Camp to Hon'ble Governor of 
Tamil Nadu-Extension of Tenure for a period of four months 
with effect from 9-2-2020 -Notified - Orders - Issued.]
The Following Government order is Published:-
[G.O. Rt. No. 827, Public (Protocol.III), 28th February 2020.]
Read:
1.	 G.O.Ms. No. 370, Public (Protocol.III) Department, dated 
24-5-2018
2.	 From the Deputy Secretary to Governor and Comptroller, 
Raj Bhavan, Chennai, Letter No. 233/G3/2020, dated 
19-2-2020 
ORDER: No. 827, Public (Protocol-III), 28th February 2020
In the G.O. first read above, Major APS Rathore 
(IC-75630) from Indian Army was appointed as Aide-de-Camp 
(Army) to Hon'ble Governor of Tamil Nadu on deputation basis 
for a period of two years with effect from 9-2-2018 FN on the 
terms and conditions of the services intimated by Indian Army, 
New Delhi and his tenure expired on 8-2-2020. Now, Ministry of 
Defence, New Delhi, has stated that the Competent Authority 
has approved the extension of tenure of IC-75817N Major Ajay 
PS Rathore, ADC to Governor of Tamil Nadu for four months 
beyond 9-2-2020 and has requested to issue necessary orders 
immediately under intimation to MoD. In the letter second read 
above the Deputy Secretary to Governor and Comptroller, 
Raj Bhavan, Chennai, has requested the Government to 
issue necessary orders for extending the tenure of Major Ajay 
PS Rathore, ADC to Hon'ble Governor of Tamil Nadu for four 
months beyond 9-2-2020.
2. The Government is hereby accord sanction for 
extension of the deputation tenure of Major APS Rathore 
(IC-75817N) from Indian Army as Aide-de-Camp to Hon'ble 
Governor of Tamil Nadu for a further period of four months with 
effect from 9-2-2020 under the existing terms and conditions 
issued vide G.O.Ms. No. 370, Public (Protocol.III) Department, 
dated 24-5-2018.
3. This order issues with the concurrence of Finance 
department 
vide 
its 
U.O.No.10147/DS(PAP)/Finance 
(Public)/2020, dated 28-2-2020.
