Ex-III 1(a)-(45)
MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION AND WATER SUPPLY DEPARTMENT
AMENDMENT TO THE TAMIL NADU PANCHAYATS, MUNICIPALITIES AND CORPORATIONS (DELIMITATION OF 
WARDS OR DIVISIONS AND RESERVATIONS) RULES, 1996.
[G.O. Ms. No. 13, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (Election), 17th January 2022, 
தை 4, பிலவ, திருவள்ளுவர் ஆண்டு-2053.]
No. SRO-A1(b)/2022.
In exercise of the powers conferred by section 303 of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 
1920), section 347 of the Chennai City Municipal Corporation Act, 1919 (Tamil Nadu Act IV of 1919), section 431 of the Madurai 
City Municipal Corporation Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 15 of 1971), section 430 of the Coimbatore City Municipal Corporation Act, 
1981 (Tamil Nadu Act 25 of 1981), read with section 8 of the Tiruchirappalli City Municipal Corporation Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu 
Act 27 of 1994), section 8 of the Tirunelveli City Municipal Corporation Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 28 of 1994), section 8 of the 
Salem City Municipal Corporation Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 29 of 1994), section  8 of the Tiruppur City Municipal Corporation 
Act, 2008 (Tamil Nadu Act 7 of 2008), section  8 of the Erode City Municipal Corporation Act, 2008 (Tamil Nadu Act 8 of 2008), 
section  8 of the Vellore City Municipal Corporation Act, 2008 (Tamil Nadu Act 26 of 2008), section  8 of the Thoothukudi City 
Municipal Corporation Act, 2008 (Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 2008), section 8 of the Thanjavur City Municipal Corporation Act, 2013 
(Tamil Nadu Act 24 of 2013), section 8 of the Dindigul City Municipal Corporation Act, 2013 (Tamil Nadu Act 25 of 2013), section 
8 of the Hosur City Municipal Corporation Act, 2019 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 2019), section 8 of the Nagercoil City Municipal 
Corporation Act, 2019 (Tamil Nadu Act 11 of 2019), section 8 of the Avadi City Municipal Corporation Act, 2019 (Tamil Nadu Act 
24 of 2019), section 8 of the Cuddalore City Municipal Corporation Ordinance, 2021 (Tamil Nadu Ordinance 1 of 2021), section 
8 of the Kancheepuram City Municipal Corporation Ordinance, 2021 (Tamil Nadu Ordinance 2 of 2021), section 8 of the Sivakasi 
City Municipal Corporation Ordinance, 2021 (Tamil Nadu Ordinance 3 of 2021), section 8 of the Karur City Municipal Corporation 
Ordinance, 2021 (Tamil Nadu Ordinance 4 of 2021), section 8 of the Tambaram City Municipal Corporation Ordinance, 2021 
(Tamil Nadu Ordinance 5 of 2021) and section 8 of the Kumbakonam City Municipal Corporation Ordinance, 2021 (Tamil Nadu 
Ordinance 7 of 2021), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby makes the following amendment to the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, 
Municipalities and Corporations (Delimitation of Wards or Divisions and Reservations) Rules, 1996:— 
Amendment
    	 In the said Rules, the proviso to sub-rule (3) of rule 4 shall be omitted. 
	
SHIV DAS MEENA,
	
Additional Chief Secretary to Government.
