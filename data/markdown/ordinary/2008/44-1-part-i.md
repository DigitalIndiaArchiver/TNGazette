No. I/PUSP/39/2008.—The following Notification of the
Government of India, Ministry of Law and Justice (Department
of Justice), Jaisalmer House, 26, Man Singh Road,
New Delhi-110 011, dated the 22nd October 2008
is republished :—
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (1) of
Article 222 of the Constitution of India, the President, after
consultation with the Cheif Justice of India, is pleased to transfer
Shri Justice K. Kannan, Additional Judge, Madras High Court as
an Additional Judge of the Punjab and Haryana High Court and
to direct him to assume charge of his office in the Punjab and
Haryana High Court on or before 6th November, 2008.
