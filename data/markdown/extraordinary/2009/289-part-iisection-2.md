ALTERATION OF THE LIMITATION OF THE REGISTRATION
SUB-DISTRICT OF THIRUVIDAIMARUDUR IN THE
REGISTRATION DISTRICT OF KUMBAKONAM IN THE
REVENUE DISTRICT OF THANJAVUR BY EXCLUDING
CERTAIN VILLAGES AND FORMS A NEW REGISTRATION
SUB-DISTRICT OF THIRUPPANANDAL UNDER THE
REGISTRATION ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 162, Commercial Taxes and Registration (G),
12th November 2009, Aippasi 26, Thiruvalluvar
Aandu-2040.]
No. II(2)/CTR/601(b-1)/2009.
Under Section 5 of the Registration Act, 1908 (Central Act
XVI of 1908), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby, with effect on
and from the 13th November 2009  alters the limits of the
Registration sub-districts specified in column (3) of the Table
below  and in the Kumbakonam Registration district and the
Revenue district of Thanjavur  by excluding the corresponding
villages specified in column (2) thereof from the said Registration
sub-districts and forms a new Registration sub-district of
Thiruppanandal in the said Registration district and in the said
Revenue district.
 The limits of the said villages shall be the same, as
determined for the purpose of revenue administration.

ALTERATION OF THE LIMITATION OF THE REGISTRATION
SUB-DISTRICT OF PAPANASAM IN THE REGISTRATION
DISTRICT OF KUMBAKONAM IN THE REVENUE
DISTRICT OF THANJAVUR BY EXCLUDING CERTAIN
VILLAGES AND FORMS A NEW REGISTRATION
SUB-DISTRICT OF AYYAMPETTAI UNDER THE
REGISTRATION ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 163, Commercial Taxes and Registration (G),
12th November 2009, Aippasi 26, Thiruvalluvar
Aandu-2040.]
No. II(2)/CTR/601(b-2)/2009.
Under  Section 5 of the Registration Act, 1908 (Central
Act XVI of 1908), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby, with
effect on and from the 13th November 2009 alters the limits
of the Registration sub-district of Papanasam in the
Kumbakonam Registration district and in the Revenue district
of Thanjavur, by excluding the following villages from the
said Registration sub-district and forms a new Registration
sub-district of  Ayyampettai  in the said Registration district
of Thanjavur and in the said Revenue district.
The limits of the said villages shall be the same, as
determined for the purpose of revenue administration.
