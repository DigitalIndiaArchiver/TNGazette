LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT DEPARTMENT
Draft Amendments  to Tamil Nadu Standards of Weights and Measures (Enforcement) Rules, 1989.
[G.O. Ms.No. 35, Labour and Employment (G1), 19th March 2009, Panguni 6, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No.SRO A-8/2009.—The following draft of the amendments to the Tamil Nadu Standards of Weights and Measures
(Enforcement) Rules, 1989, which it is proposed to make in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 72
of the Sandards of Weights and Measures (Enforcement) Act, 1985 (Central Act 54 of 1985), is hereby published for the
information of all the persons likely to be affected thereby as required by sub-section (4) of Section 72 of the said Act.
2. Notice is hereby given that the said draft amendments will be taken into consideration on or after the expiry of a period
of six weeks from the date of publication in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette and that any objection or suggestion, which
may  be received from any person with respect thereto, before the expiry of the aforesaid period will be considered by the
Government of Tamil Nadu, Objection or suggestion, if any, should be addressed to the Secretary to Government, Labour
and Employment Department, Chennai-600 009 through the Commissioner of Labour, Teynampet, Chennai-600 006
in  duplicate:—
DRAFT AMENDMENTS.
(1) In the said Rules,—
in rule 14 in sub-rule (1), after the fourth proviso, the following proviso shall be added, namely:—
"Provided also that for fabric plastic and steel tape measures, no re-verification shall be required"; and
(2) in Schedule XII, under the heading "3. LENGTH MEASURES".—
(i) for item (b) and the entries thereunder, the following item and entries shall, respectively, be substituted,
namely:—
"(b) Flexible:
Class I
:
20 paise/metre
Class II
:
15 paise/metre
Class III
:
10 paise/metre."; and
         (ii) items (c) and (d) shall be omitted.
T. PRABHAKARA RAO,
Principal Secretary to Government.
