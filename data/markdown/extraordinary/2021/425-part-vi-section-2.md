(S.O. No. 54/2021/TNSEC/ME1, dated the 16th September 2021)
Read:
(S.O.No. 15-53/2021/TNSEC/PE-1, dated 13th September 2021)
No.VI(2)/83(f)/2021.
WHEREAS, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission has notified for the conduct of rural local body elections as per 
Statutory Order read above; 
WHEREAS, there is no provision in Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Elections) Rules, 1995 for affixing the photo of the Polling 
Agents in their appointment letter in Form 11; and  
WHEREAS, it is felt expedient to emphasise on the production of photo identity proof as evidence by the Polling Agent 
at the time of entry into the polling station for the ensuing Ordinary Elections to Rural Local Bodies as a measure to curtail 
impersonation;
NOW, THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (1) of section 239 of the Tamil Nadu 
Panchayats Act, 1994 and rule 40 and 143 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Elections) Rules, 1995, the Tamil Nadu State 
Election commission specifies that the Polling Agents (including the relief agents) shall show any one of the twelve documentary 
evidences in original as prescribed for the electors in S.O.No.100/2019/TNSEC/EG2, dated 11.12.2019 and Circular No. C-21/2021 
dated 05.08.2021 as proof of his/her identity to the Presiding Officer and Police Personnel at the time of entry into the Polling 
Station.  The documentary evidence shown by him must have his/her photo identity proof of it.  Further, the Commission 
specifies that every Contesting Candidate or his/her Election Agent shall appoint one Polling Agent for each Polling Station

Polling Station in the ensuing Ordinary Elections to Rural Local Bodies and any one of them shall be present in the Polling

The above identity proof has been prescribed only for the purpose of verifying the identity of the person appointed as 
Polling Agent and not for any other purpose of scrutinizing other things.
