Tamil Nadu waqf board, chennai.
Taking Over of the Direct Management of Big Mosque, Poonamallee.
                                                                                                            
(Rc.No.14851/85/B4/TVLR) 
No.VI-3(a)/48/2020.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Waqf Act 1995 and as per Tamil Nadu Waqf 
Board's Special Resolution No.5/90 dated 14-06-1990, the Special Officer has ordered on 20-05-2020 to take over the Management 
of BIG MOSQUE, POONAMALEE, TIRUVALLUR DISTRICT under the Direct Management of Tamil Nadu Waqf Board and the 
Superintendent of Waqfs, Poonamallee is appointed as its Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,	
R. JAINULAPTHEEN,	
	
2nd June 2020.	
District  Revenue Officer/
	
Chief Executive Officer,
