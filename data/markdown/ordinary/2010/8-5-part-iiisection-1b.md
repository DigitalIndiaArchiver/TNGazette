Amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Medical Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 32, Health and Family Welfare (C1), 9th February 2010, Thai 27, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No. SRO B-11/2010.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the Proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Medical
Service, (Section 21 in Volume-III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1969):
2.
(i) The amendment (1) hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 12th August 1980.
(ii) The amendment (2) hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 27th April 1994.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules, in Part-II in Branch II-Nursing
1.
for the expression “Head Nurse”, wherever it occurs, the expression “Nursing Supervisor” shall be
substituted; and
2.
for the expression “Nursing Supervisor”, as so amended, wherever it occurs, the expression “Nursing
Superintendent Grade-III”, shall be substituted.
Amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Medical Subordinate Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 33, Health and Family Welfare (C1), 9th February 2010, Thai 27, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No. SRO B-12/2010.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Medical
Subordinate Service (Section 21 in Volume-III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1969):
2.
The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 27th April 1994.
AMENDMENT
In the said Special Rules, in Part-II, in Branch-III Nursing, for the expression “Nursing Supervisor”, wherever it occurs,
the expression “Nursing Superintendent Grade-III” shall be substituted.
V.K. SUBBURAJ,
Principal Secretary to Government.
