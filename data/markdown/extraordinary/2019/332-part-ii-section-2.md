appointment of chairman in the Tamil Nadu Electricity regulatory 
commission under the electricity act, 2003.
[G.O. Ms. No. 63, Energy (D1), 16th August 2019, Aadi 31, Vikari, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2050.] 
No. II(2)/EGY/677(b)/2019.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (5) of Section 82 of the Electricity Act, 2003 
(Central Act 36 of 2003), based on the recommendation of the Selection Committee constituted 
under Section 85 of the said Act for the purpose referred to therein, the Governor of Tamil Nadu 
hereby appoints Thiru. M. Chandrasekar, as the Chairman of the Tamil Nadu Electricity Regulatory 
Commission, Chennai.
