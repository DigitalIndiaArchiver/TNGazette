AMENDMENT
In the said Notiﬁ cation, in the Schedule the revised Toll 
rates for the use of (i) Between Chennai (Akkarai) to places 
upto Mahabalipuram and vice versa (Four lane) and (ii) 
Between Mahabalipuram to Places upto Puducherry limit and 
vice versa (Two lane) and (iii) Between Chennai (Akkarai) to 
places upto Puducherry limit and vice versa (Four lane and 
Two lane) are hereby prescribed.
