NOTICE UNDER SECTION 15 (1) OF TAMIL NADU HIGHWAYS ACT, 2001 READ WITH THE TAMIL NADU 
LAND ACQUISITION LAWS (REVIVAL OF OPERATION, AMENDMENT, AND VALIDATION) ACT, 2019.
No. VI(1)/532(b-1)/2022.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that the lands specified in the Schedule below to 
be acquired for Highways purposes to wit “Reconstruction of ROB at km 314/10 of Chennai - Trichy - Dindigul 
Road (Old NH 45 Near Thiruvanaikoil) in lieu of existing old bridge No. 380A at Railway km 329/8-9 between 
Tiruchirappalli and Srirangam Railway Stations” at Jembukeswaram & Thimmarayasamuthiram Villages, 
in Srirangam Taluk of Tiruchirappalli District and it having already been decided that the amount of 
compensation to be awarded for the lands are to be paid out of the funds controlled or managed by the Special 
District  Revenue Officer (L.A & M), Tiruchirappalli. The following notice is issued under sub-section (1)  of 
Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002), 
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands specified in the Schedule below measuring to an extent 
of 3211 Sq.mtrs. to the same, a little more or less needed Highways purpose, to wit, for “Reconstruction of 
ROB at Km 314/10 of Chennai-Trichy-Dindigul Road (Old NH 45 Near Thiruvanaikoil) in lieu of existing 
Old Bridge No. 380A at Railway Km 329/8-9 between Trichirappalli & Srirangam Railway Stations”. 
The plans of the lands are kept in the Office of the Special District Revenue Officer (L.A & M), Collectorate, 
Tiruchirappalli may be inspected on any day during office hours.
