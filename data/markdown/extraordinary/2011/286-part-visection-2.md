   S.O. No. 37/2006/TNSEC/ME, dated the 29th August 2006.
No. VI(2)/427(d-1)/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred by rule 8 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities, Municipalities
and Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006 and in supersession of the Notification No. I - VI(2)/359 (a-1)/2006, published at Page 1
of Part VI - Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary No. 211, dated 30th August 2006, the State Election
Commission hereby authorises the Executive Officers of the respective Town Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities or any other
officer appointed by the State Election Officer / District Election Officer as the case may be, as the Electoral Registration Officers
for the purpose of preparing and publishing electoral rolls in respect of the respective Town Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities
and the wards lying within the respective Town Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities.
NOTIFICATION -II
(S.O. No. 21/2011/TNSEC/ME-1)
No. VI(2)/427(d-2)/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred by rule 8 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities, Municipalities
and Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006 and in supersession of the Notification No. II - VI(2)/359 (a-1)/2006, published at
Page 1 of Part VI - Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary No. 211, dated 30th August 2006, the State
Election Commission hereby authorises the Commissioners of the respective Municipalities as the Electoral Registration Officers
for the purpose of preparing and publishing electoral rolls in respect of the respective Municipalities and the wards lying within the
respective Municipalities.
