No. II(2)/HWMP/515/2015.—The Governor of Tamil Nadu
having been satisfied that the lands specified in the schedule
below have to be acquired for Highways purpose to wit for
the formation of by-pass road to Sivagangai Town in
Sivagangai District and it having already been decided that
the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the
lands is to be paid out of the funds controlled or managed
by District Revenue Officer, Sivagangai after considering the
cause shown by the owners or other persons having interest
in the said lands as the case may be, do hereby publish
the following Notice under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of
the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of
2002).
NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu
Highways Act 2001, (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002), the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands specified in the
Schedule below and measuring an extent of 11.61.03 hectares
of dry and manai lands in Kanjirangal, Surakkulam, Paiyur
Pillaivayal, Alangulam and Keelakandani Villages, Sivagangai
Taluk, Sivagangai District to the same, a little more or less
needed for the Highways purpose for the formation of by-pass
road to Sivagangai Town in Sivagangai District.
The Plan of the lands is kept in the office of the District
Collector, Sivagangai and may be inspected at any time
during the office hours.
