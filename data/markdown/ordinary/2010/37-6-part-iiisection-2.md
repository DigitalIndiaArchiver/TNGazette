TUTICORIN PORT TRUST
AMENDMENTS IN THE TUTICORIN PORT TRUST (LICENSING OF STEVEDORES) REGULATIONS, 2010.
(T-38/1/2009-10/SC)
No. SRO C-4/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 123 of the Major Port Trust Act, 1963 (Act 38 of 1963), and in
supersession of the existing Tuticorin Port Trust (Licensing of Stevedores) Regulations, 1985 published the Tuticorin Port
Trust (Licensing of Stevedores) Regulations, 2010 in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Notification dated March 31,
2010 and April 7, 2010, the Board of Trustees of Tuticorin Port Trust hereby notify the following amendments in the
schedule.
SCHEDULE
TUTICORIN PORT TRUST
Tuticorin Port Trust (Licensing of Stevedores) Regulations, 2010.
1. Condition No.  5(b) of the Regulation, "The Stevedore shall carry out the operations with his own gear arranged
by him" is amended as, "The Stevedore shall under take to provide minimum  Equipment / Gear either owned or hired."
2. Condition No.  9(iii) of the Regulation, "Low productivity" is amended as, "The firm handles less than the minimum
guaranteed throughput / the prescribed parameters as fixed by the Port."
3. The following two conditions are added under Reg. 9.
9(xi) Violates security related rules and instructions like ISPS code compliance etc.
9(xii) Indulges in illegal / corrupt practices.
The above changes comes into effect from the date of Notification.
