Chennai Port Trust Employees’ (Classification, Control and Appeal) (Amendment) Regulations, 2013.
(RRC/1353/2010/GA)
No. SRO C-9/2014.
The Notification GSR No. 376(E), dated the 18th June 2013 issued by the Government of India, Ministry of Shipping
and published in Part—II Section—3 sub-section (i) of the Extraordinary issue of the Gazette of India, dated 18th June
2013 is republished hereunder as required under Section 132(2) of the Major Port Trusts Act, 1963:-
G.S.R. No. 376(E) - In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 124, read with sub-section (1)
of Section 132 of the Major Ports Act, 1963 (38 of 1963), the Central Government hereby approves the Chennai Port trust
Employees’ (Classification, Control and Appeal) (Amendment) Regulations 2013, made by the Board of Trustees for the Port
of Chennai further to amend the Chennai Port Trust Employees’ (Classification, Control and Appeal) Regulations 1988 and
are set out in the Schedule annexed to this Notification.
SCHEDULE
Chennai Port Trust Employees’ (Classification, Control and Appeal) (Amendment) Regulations, 2013.
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 28 of the Major Port Trusts Act, 1963 (38 of 1963), the Trustees of the
Port of Chennai hereby makes the following Amendment Regulations further to amend the Chennai Port Trust Employees’
(Classification, Control and Appeal) Regulations, 1988 namely:-
1. Short Title and Commencement:
(i)  These Regulations shall be called the Chennai Port Trust Employees’ (Classification, Control and Appeal)
(Amendment) Regulations, 2013.
(ii)  These Regulations shall come into force from the date of publication in the official Gazette.
2. In the Chennai Port Trust Employees’ (Classification, Control and Appeal)  Regulations, 1988, the following amendments
shall be made:
(i)  In sub-Regulation (10) of the aforesaid Regulation 7, the words “ninety days from the date of
order of suspension” shall be substituted by the words “ninety days from the effective date of suspension”.
(ii) The clause (11) of Regulation 7 shall be substituted as under:
“(11) Subject to the provisions contained in sub-regulation (6) or (7), an order of  suspension made or deemed
to have been made under sub-regulations, (1) or (2) or (3) of this regulation shall not be valid after a period of ninety days unless
it is extended after review, for a further period before the expiry of ninety days:
Provided that no such review of suspension shall be necessary in the case of deemed suspension under sub-regulation (3),
if the employee continues to be under suspension at the time of completion of ninety days of suspension and the ninety days’
period in such case will count from the date the employee detailed in custody is released from detention or the date on which
the fact of his release from detention is intimated to his appointing authority, whichever is later.”
(iii)  The clause (iii) (a) under Regulation 8 (a) shall be substituted as under:
“(iii)(a) reduction to a lower stage in a scale of pay by one stage for a period not exceeding
three years, without cumulative effect and not adversely affecting his pension.”
(iv) The following provisos shall be inserted under clause (2) to Regulation 12 of the aforesaid Regulations:
“Provided that where there is a complaint of sexual harassment within the meaning of Regulation 4-A of Chennai Port Trust
Employees’ (Conduct) Regulations 1987, the Complaints Cell constituted in the Trust for inquiring into such complaints, shall
be deemed to be the Inquiring Authority appointed by the Disciplinary Authority for the purpose of these Regulations and the
Complaints Cell shall hold, if separate procedure has not been prescribed for the Complaints Cell for holding the inquiry into the
complaints of sexual harassment, the inquiry as far as practicable in accordance with the procedure laid down in
these Regulations.”
(v)
In the aforesaid regulations the existing title of “SCHEDULE” renamed as “SCHEDULE - I”.
(vi)
In the aforesaid regulations, the following shall be added as “SCHEDULE-II”.

CHENNAI PORT TRUST EMPLOYEES’ (GRANT OF CONVEYANCE ADVANCE)
(AMENDMENT) REGULATIONS, 2013.
(RRC/11951/2010/GA)
No. SROC-10/2014.
The Notification GSR No. 729 (E), dated the 8th November 2013 issued by the Government of India, Ministry of Shipping
and published in Part—II Section—3 sub-section (i) of the Extraordinary issue of the Gazette of India, dated 8th November
2013 is republished hereunder as required under section 132(2) of the Major Port Trusts Act, 1963 :-
G.S.R. No. 729(E) - In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of section 124, read with Sub-section (1)
of Section 132 of the Major Port Trusts Act, 1963 (38 of 1963), the Central Government hereby approves the Chennai Port
Trust Employees’ (Grant of Conveyance Advance) (Amendment) Regulations, 2013, made by the Board of Trustees for
the Port of Chennai further to amend the Chennai Port Trust Employees’ (Grant of conveyance advance) Regulations,
1994 and are set out in the Schedule annexed to this Notification.
SCHEDULE
CHENNAI PORT TRUST EMPLOYEES’ (GRANT OF CONVEYANCE ADVANCE)
(AMENDMENT) REGULATIONS, 2013.
In exercise of the powers conferred under section 28 of the Major Port Trusts Act, 1963 (38 of 1963), the Trustees of
the Port of Chennai hereby makes the following Amendment Regulations further to amend the Chennai Port Trust Employees’
(Grant of Conveyance Advance) Regulations, 1994 namely:-
1. Short Title and Commencement:-
(i) These Regulations may be called the Chennai Port Trust Employees’ (Grant of Conveyance Advance) (Amendment)
Regulations, 2013.
(ii) The amendment Regulations shall come into force from the date of publication in the Official Gazette.
2. In the Chennai Port Trust Employees’ (Grant of Conveyance Advance) Regulations, 1994, the following amendments shall
be made:-
(i) In regulations 4, in sub-regulation (l), clause (i) shall be substituted as under.-
“(i) All the Trust’s Officers whose Basic Pay is Rs.20,600 per month and above are eligible for
the grant of Motor Car Advance for the purchase of Motor Car”.
(ii) In regulation 4, in sub-regulation (1), clauses (iii) and (v) shall be deleted.
(iii) In regulation 4, in sub-regulation (2), in clause (i), the words and figures “Rs.4,600/-” shall be
substituted by words and figures “Rs. 10,100/-”.
(iv) In regulation 4, in sub-regulation (2), in clauses (ii), the words and figures “Rs. 30,000/-” shall
be substituted by words and figures “Rs 50,000/-”.
(v) In regulation 4, in sub-regulation (2), in clauses (iii), the words and figures “Rs.24,000/- shall be substituted by words
and figures “Rs.40,000/-”.
(vi) In regulation 4, in sub-regulation (2), clause (v) shall be deleted.
(vii) In regulation 5, in sub-regulation (2), the words and figures “Rs.1,500/-” shall be substituted by
the words and figures “Rs.2,000/-”.
