GENERAL NOTIFICATION
TAMIL NADU WAQF BOARD, CHENNAI.
Extension of  Period of Direct Management of Theradi Sunnath Jamath Pallivasal @ Sheik Khairunisha Masjid,
Kancheepuram District under direct management.
(Rc.No. 12523/12/B4/KPM)
No.VI-3(a)/125/2015.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Waqf Act 1995 as amended by
Act 27 of 2013, the Tamil Nadu Waqf Board in its resolution in item No. 110/15 in Rc. No. 12523/12/B4/KPM., dated 30-09-2015
has resolved to extend the period of direct management of Theradi Sunnath Jamath Pallivasal @ Sheik Khairunisha Masjid
Kancheepuram District for two years from 26-08-2015.
Chennai-600 001,
K. RASHEETH ALI,
17th December 2015.
District Revenue Officer/
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Waqf Board.
