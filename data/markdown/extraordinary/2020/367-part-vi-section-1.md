ACQUISITION OF LANDS
NOTICE UNDER SECTION 15(1) OF TAMIL NADU HIGHWAYS ACT, 2001(TN ACT 34 OF 2002) READ WITH THE 
TAMIL NADU LAND ACQUISITION LAWS (REVIVAL OF OPERATION, AMENDMENT AND VALIDATION) ACT, 2019.
(Roc.No.CKICP/CLA/15(1)/SH-58-1/28/2020)
No.VI(1)/305(a-1)/2020.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that the lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below are acquired 
under Chennai – Kanniyakumari Industrial Corridor Project for Highways purpose to wit for strengthening and widening 
of SH-58-1 Chengalpattu - Kanchipuram Road at (From KM 30/000 to KM 31/615(LHS) and From KM 30/000 to KM 
31/622(RHS) Authur B (Part–I) Village in Chengalpattu Taluk, Chengalpattu District, and it having already been decided 
that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands to be paid out of the funds controlled and managed 
by the Divisional Engineer(H), Kancheepuram (CKICP), Tamil Nadu after having considering the cause shown by the 
owner or other persons having interest on such lands as the case may be do hereby publish the following notice under 
sub-section (1) of section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (TN Act 34 of 2002) read with the Tamil Nadu 
Land Acquisition Laws (Revival of Operation, Amendment and Validation) Act, 2019.
NOTICE
 
Under sub-section (1) of section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) 
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notiﬁ ed that the Dry, manai and Maanavari lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below 
and measuring to an extent 5771 of sq.mt of lands are acquired in Authur B (Part–I) Villages in Chengalpattu Taluk, 
Chengalpattu District, to the same, a little more or less is needed for Chennai – Kanniyakumari Industrial Corridor 
Project for State Highways purpose, to wit for Strengthening and widening of SH-58-1 Chengalpattu – Kanchipuram 
Road at (From KM 30/000 to KM 31/615(LHS) and From KM 30/000 to KM 31/622(RHS)) The plan of the lands under 
acquisition are kept in the oﬃ  ce of the Special District Revenue oﬃ  cer (Land Acquisition), Chennai - Kanniyakumari 
Industrial Corridor Project, Kancheepuram and oﬃ  ce of the Special Tahsildar (Land Acquisition), Chennai – Kanniyakumari 
Industrial Corridor Project Kanchipuram in Kanchipuram District may be inspected at any time during the oﬃ  ce hours.
