The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that
the lands specified in the Schedule below have to be acquired
for a public purpose and it having already been decided that the
entire amount of compensation to be a awarded for the lands
is to be paid out of public revenues, the following declaration is
issued under Section 6 of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894 (Central
Act 1 of 1894).
DECLARATION
Under Section 6 of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894 (Central
Act 1 of 1894), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby declares
that the lands specified in the Schedule below and measuring
9.94.0 hectares of dry and manavari lands, be the same, a
little more or less, are needed for a public purpose, to wit,
for Kannankottai –Thervoy Kandigai Reservoir Scheme land
acquisition works.
A plan of the lands is kept in the Office of the Special
Tahsildar (Land Acquisition), Unit-I, Kannankottai-
Thervoykandigai Reservoir Scheme, Tiruvallur District and
may be inspected at any time during office hours.
