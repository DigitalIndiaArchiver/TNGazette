In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (1) of Section 43-B of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities
Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920), sub-section (1) of Section 10-D of the Coimbatore City Municipal Corporation
Act, 1981 (Tamil Nadu Act 25 of 1981) read with Section 8 of the Tiruchirappalli City Municipal Corporation Act, 1994
(Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 1994) and rule 22 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities, Municipalities
and Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission, for the purpose of filling up of
casual vacancies in the offices of the Ward Members/Councillors of Town Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities,
Municipalities and Corporation specified in the Table annexed to this Notification, in Consultation with the State
Government, hereby calls upon the electors of the said wards to elect their respective Ward Members/Councillors, in
accordance with the following programme of election:—

In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 43-D of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920
(Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920) and Section 68-A of the Coimbatore City Municipal Corporation Act, 1981 (Tamil Nadu Act 25 of 1981)
read with Section 8 of Tiruchirappalli City Municipal Corporation Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 1994) the Tamil Nadu State
Election Commission hereby specifies that in the Casual Elections to fill up the casual vacancies in the offices of the Members
of the Town Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities, Municipalities and Councillor of Corporation notified in the S.O. No. 18/2010/
TNSEC/ME1, dated 28th June 2010, the giving and recording of votes by electronic voting machine shall be adopted.
