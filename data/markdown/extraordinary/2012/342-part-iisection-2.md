NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
FINANCE DEPARTMENT
DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF TAMIL NADU TRANSPARENCY IN TENDERS (SECOND
AMENDMENT) ACT, 2012 (TAMIL NADU ACT 31 OF 2012).
[G.O. No. 422, Finance (Salaries), 7th December 2012, Karthigai 22, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2043.]
No. II(2)/FIN/815(g)/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu  Transparency
in Tenders (Second Amendment) Act, 2012 (Tamil Nadu Act 31 of 2012), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby appoints the 7th day of December 2012 as the date on which the said Act shall come
into force.
K. SHANMUGAM,
Principal Secretary to Government.
