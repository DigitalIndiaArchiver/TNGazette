Tirunelveli Local Planning Authority.
(Roc. No. 1612/2015/DP1)
No.VI(1)/327/2017.
1. In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 33(2) of Town and Country Planning Act, 1971 the Commissioner
of Town and Country Planning Proceeding Roc. No.1612/2015/DP1, dated: 9-10-17 is hereby confirms the individual
draft variation for “Conversion of School & Play Ground use into Residential use in Melapalayam Ward, Ward-BP,
Block - 36,  T.S.No. 58/2A, 2B, (Kulavanigarpuram Village - Rs.No: 657/1A1 Part)-Extent:3066 sqm. to the “Comprehensive
Variation Confirmed Perumalpuram Extension D.D.Plan No. 10 of Tirunelveli Local Planning Authority, which was draft
variated by the Commissioner of Town and Country Planning Proceeding Roc. No. 1612/2015/DP1, dated: 05-01-2017
under Section 33(1) and the notification was published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No. 4, Part VI—Section 1,
Page No.35, dated: 25-01-2017 Publication No.VI(1)/32/2017.
2. Since no objections and suggestions were received on this individual draft variation notification within the
stipulated time, the same are hereby confirmed and ordered as below:-
CONFIRMATION OF VARIATION
1. Whenever the expression D.D.Plan Map No:5 DDP(v)/DTCP No.24/2001 occurs, the expression DDP (V)/DTCP
No:8/2016 shall be added at the end and to be read with.
2. In the Schedule IV (Form No.7) SI.No.5, 2nd Column R.S.No. 657/1 part shall be deleted and substituted as
R.S. No. 657/1 part (Excluding R.S.No. 657/1A1 Block-36, T.S.No.58/2A, 2B).
3. This draft variation made enforceable from the date of publication in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
