NOTIFICATION OF TAMIL NADU STATE LEGAL SERVICES AUTHORITY NOMINATED AS
EXECUTIVE CHAIRMAN UNDER LEGAL SERVICES AUTHORITIES ACT.
[G.O. (D) No. 962, Home (Courts-IV), 22nd November 2013.]
No. II(2)/HO/884(d)/2013.
Under clause (b) of sub-section (2) of Section 6 of the Legal Services Authorities Act, 1987
(Central Act 39 of 1987), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, in consultation with the Chief Justice of the  High Court
of Madras, hereby nominates Honourable Justice Mr. S.K. Agnihotri, the Senior Most Judge of the High Court
of Madras as the Executive Chairman of the Tamil Nadu State Legal Services Authority, Chennai with
immediate effect.
NIRANJAN MARDI,
Principal Secretary to Government.
