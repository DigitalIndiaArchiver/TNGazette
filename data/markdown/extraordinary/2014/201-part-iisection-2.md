The following members of the Tamil Nadu State Police Service and the Tamil Nadu State Police Subordinate Service have
been selected to receive the award of "Gandhi Adigal Police Medal" for the year 2014 on the occasion of Republic day, 2015
for their outstanding work in curbing illicit liquor. They would also be paid a cash prize of Rs.20,000 (Rupees Twenty Thousand
only) each:-
(i)
Dr. K. Balakrishnan,
Additional Deputy Commissioner of Police,
Prohibition Enforcement Wing,
Trichy City.
(ii)
Thiru. K. Shanmugam,
Additional Superintendent of Police,
Prohibition Enforcement Wing, Kancheepuram District.
(iii) Tmt. P. Padmavathi,
Inspector of Police, Central Investigation Unit,
Trichy Zone, Trichy.
(iv) Thiru. M. Babu,
Sub-Inspector of Police,
Prohibition Enforcement Wing,
Villupuram, Villupuram District.
APURVA VARMA
Principal Secretary to Government.
