Order: No.632, Revenue and Disaster Management,11th November 2020,
In G.O.Ms.No.324, Revenue and Disaster Management (DM II), Department, Dated : 30.06.2020 it has been ordered under 
caption I that operation of both private and Government buses within the districts will remain suspended from 01.07.2020 to 
15.07.2020 throughout the state and that the interstate public transport will continue to remain prohibited until further orders 
during the lockdown period throughout the state till 31.07.2020.
2. In this connection, as per the guidelines issued by the Government of India, necessary permission was already 
accorded by the Government of Tamil Nadu for the goods traﬃ  c, operation of railways for transportation of goods and parcels, 
operation of airports/ seaports/ landports and inland container depots for cargo movement, movement of all trucks and other 
goods / carrier vehicles without any restriction vide G.O.Ms.No.193, Revenue and Disaster Management Department, dated 
15.4.2020. Further, as already stated above operation of both Public and Private Bus Transport throughout the State has 
been permitted by the Government from 07.09.2020 after following the Standard Operating Procedures and that the Inter 
State/Intra State Rail Transport in the authorised routes has also been permitted. 
2. In G.O.Ms.No.609, Revenue & Disaster Management (DM-IV) Department, dated 31.10.2020 operation of both Private 
and Public Bus Transport between Tamil Nadu and Puducherry without E-registration has been permitted.
3. The Transport Department has now requested orders for the consent for operation of Inter-State bus services between 
Karnataka and Tamil Nadu by the State Transport Undertakings of Inter-State reciprocal Transport Agreements between both 
the States duly following the prescribed guidelines/Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) may be accorded for six days 
(i.e.11.11.2020 to 16.11.2020) as a special operation.
4. The Government after careful consideration, on considering the request of the Transport Department hereby to permit 
the Inter-State bus services between Karnataka and Tamil Nadu by the State Transport Undertakings by duly following the 
prescribed guidelines/Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) for six days (i.e.,11.11.2020 to 16.11.2020) without E-registration 
and accordingly amend the GO.Ms.No.613, Revenue and Disaster Management (DM-IV) Department, dated 31.10.2020 as 
follows:--
 
Under Caption II – (Under Permitted Activities) the following Section II-A  shall be added:
  II-A. Operation of Inter-State bus services between Karnataka and Tamil Nadu by the State Transport Undertakings 
shall be permitted from 11.11.2020 to 16.11.2020 without E-registration". 
 (By order of the Governor)
