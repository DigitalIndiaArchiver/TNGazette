No. SRO B-49/2017.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, 
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Adhoc rules published with the Education and 
Public Health Department Notiﬁ cation S.R.O. No. A-365 of 1967, published at pages 351-352 of Part V of the Fort St. George, 
Gazette, dated the 26th April, 1967.
(2) The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on and from the 1st January, 1996.
AMENDMENT
In the said rules, in rule 1, for the expression “temporary post of Draftsman”, the expression “temporary post of Junior 
Draughting Ofﬁ cer” shall be substituted.
