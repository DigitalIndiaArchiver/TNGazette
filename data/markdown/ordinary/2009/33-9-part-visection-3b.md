Election of one nominee by the Members of the Senate to the Committee for recommending
a Panel of names for appointment as Vice-Chancellor.
[D.O. RC.No. E1(1)/15209/2009.]
No. VI-3(b)/17/2009.
Under Statute 7 of Chapter V of the General Statutes of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai and as
directed by the Governing Council of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai, the Registrar In-Charge of the
University hereby notifies that a permanent vacancy is about to occur in the office of the Vice-Chancellor with effect from 27th
November 2009 F.N. consequent on the expiry of the term of office of the present Vice-Chancellor,
Prof. Dr. K. Meer Mustafa Hussain.
Necessary action to elect one nominee by the Members of the Senate to the Committee referred to in Section 10(1)
and (2) of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai, Act, 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 37 of 1987), to recommend
a panel of three names to the Chancellor for making the appointment of the next Vice-Chancellor, will be taken on the
publication of this Notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette as specified in the Annexure.
Necessary action to elect one nominee by the Members of the Governing Council to the said Committee as provided
under Statute 12(2) of Chapter V of the said Statutes will be taken on the publication of this Notification in the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.

Notice is hereby given that an election by direct polling is to be held for electing one member to the Governing Council
of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai, as per clause (c) of sub-section (4) of Section 21 of the
Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai, Act, 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 37 of 1987) by the University Professors from
among themselves. The term of membership will be for a period of three years from the date of declaration of the result
of election or the date on which he/she ceases to hold the post of Professor in this University, whichever
is earlier.

6. A Candidate may withdraw his candidature by notice in writing sent by Registered Post so as to reach the

not later than 3.00 p.m. on 14th September 2009. A candidate who has withdrawn his/her candidature shall not be allowed to cancel
