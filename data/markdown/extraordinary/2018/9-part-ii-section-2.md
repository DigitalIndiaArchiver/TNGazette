The Government  of  Tamil Nadu, having  been 
satisfied that the lands specified in the schedule 
below have to be acquired for the Industrial purpose 
of setting up of SIPCOT Industrial Park and it having 
already been decided that the entire amount of 
compensation to be awarded  for the lands to be 
paid out of the funds controlled or managed by the 
Government.  The following  notice is issued 
under sub – section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu 
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purpose Act, 1997. 
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under  sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu 
Acquisition  of Land  for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu 
hereby acquires the dry lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule  below 
and 10.60.0 Hectares to the same, a little more or less needed 
for Industrial purpose, to wit, setting up of SIPCOT Industrial 
Park in  Kurubarapalli Village, Krishnagiri  Taluk and District.
The Plan of the lands are kept in the ofﬁ ce of the Revenue 
Divisional Ofﬁ ce, Krishnagiri and may  be inspected  at any 
time during the ofﬁ ce hours.
