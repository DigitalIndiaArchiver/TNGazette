In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 85 of the Electricity Act, 2003 (Central Act 36 of 2003), 
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby constitutes a Selection Committee consisting of the following members for the purpose 
of selecting a Member of the Tamil Nadu Electricity Regulatory Commission:-
(a)
Justice Thiru.C.T. Selvam, Retired Judge of High Court  of Madras
:
Chairperson 
(b)
The Chief Secretary to Government of Tamil Nadu
:
Member 
(c)
The Chairperson of the Central Electricity Authority, New Delhi 
:
Member 
  
2. The Selection Committee shall, 
(a)
ﬁ nalise the selection of Member within three months from the date on which the 
reference has been made to it; 
(b)
recommend a panel of two names for every vacancy referred to it; and 
(c)
satisfy itself, before recommending any person for appointment as Member of 
Tamil Nadu Electricity Regulatory Commission that such person does not have any 
ﬁ nancial or other interest which is likely to aﬀ ect prejudicially his function as Member.
