The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that the lands speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below are acquired 
under Chennai Peripheral Ring Road Project for Highways purpose to wit, for Construction of Road Over Bridge 
cum interchange in lieu of existing LC No.47 at Singaperumalkovil in between Guduvancherry and Singaperumal Koil 
Railway Station for road connectivity from Ennore Port to Mahabalipuram Singaperumalkovil Village, Chengalpattu Taluk, 
Chengalpattu District and it having already been decided that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the 
lands to be paid out of the funds controlled and managed by the Highways Department and after having considering 
the cause shown by the owner or other persons having interest on such lands, as the case may be, do hereby publish 
the following notice under sub-section (1) of  Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 
of 2002) read with the Tamil Nadu Land Acquisition Laws (Revival of Operation, Amendment and Validation) Act, 2019.
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002), read 
with the Tamil Nadu Land Acquisition Laws (Revival of Operation, Amendment and Validation Act, 2019) the Governor of 
Tamil Nadu hereby notiﬁ es that the Wet, and Ryot Manai lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below and measuring to an 
additional extent of 00116 Sq.Mtrs. of lands are acquired in Singaperumalkoil Village, Chengalpattu Taluk, Chengalpattu 
District to the same a little more or less needed for the purpose to wit for Construction of Road Over Bridge cum 
interchange in lieu of existing LC No.47 at Singaperumalkovil in between Guduvancherry and Singaperumalkovil Railway 
Station for road connectivity from Ennore Port to Mahabalipuram under Chennai Peripheral Ring Road Project, Highways 
Department. 
The Plan of the lands under acquisition are kept in the oﬃ  ce of the Special District Revenue Oﬃ  cer (Land Acquisition), 
Chennai Peripheral Ring Road Project, Section-III, Chennai-600 058 and in the oﬃ  ce of the Special Tahsildar (Land 
Acquisition), Chennai Peripheral Ring Road Project, Unit-V, Kanchipuram and may be inspected at any time during the 
oﬃ  ce hours.
