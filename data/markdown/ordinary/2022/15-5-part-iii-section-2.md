Re-naming of V.O. Chidambaranar Port Trust as V.O. Chidambaranar Port Authority.
(File No. GAD-LDASO BOAD/1/2022-GAD/D-373)
No.SRO C-12/2022. 
 
As Published in the Gazette of India (S.O.4504E) Notiﬁ cation Extraordinary-Part-II-Section 3 Sub-Section (ii) 
No. CG-DL-E-30102021-230848 dated 29th October 2021, the Major Port Authorities Act, 2021 (1 of 2021) will be 
eﬀ ective from 3rd November 2021. Subsequently vide the Gazette of India (G.S.R.72E) Notiﬁ cation Extraordinary-
Part-II-Section 3 Sub-Section (i) No.CG-DL-E-02022022-233059 dated 31.01.2022 the Board of Major Port Authority for 
the V.O. Chidambaranar Port is constituted. Accordingly, the following changes are eﬀ ected from 31st January, 2022:
Existing Name as per Major Port Trusts Act, 1963
Name as per Major Port Authorities Act, 2021
V.O. Chidambaranar Port Trust
V.O. Chidambaranar Port Authority
