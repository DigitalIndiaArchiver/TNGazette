Constitution of a District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court at Thirukkuvalai in Nagapattinam District 
(Roc. No. 1347/A/2014/G/Judn.Phase III)
No. VI(1)/535/2022.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873) the 
High Court, Madras, hereby fixes Thirukkuvalai in Nagapattinam District as the place at which the District Munsif-cum-Judicial 
Magistrate Court, Thirukkuvalai shall be located.
NOTIFICATION-II
(Roc. No. 1347/A/2014/G/Judn.Phase III)
No. VI(1)/536/2022.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 11 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873) the 
High Court, Madras, hereby Directs that in Nagapattinam District, the District Munsif Court, Nagapattinam, shall cease to 
have local jurisdiction over the entire Thirukkuvalai Taluk and the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Thirukkuvalai 
shall have and exercise local jurisdiction over the entire Taluk of Thirukkuvalai with effect from the date on which the District 
Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate, Thirukkuvalai assumes charge of that Court.
NOTIFICATION-III
(Roc. No. 1347/A/2014/G/Judn.Phase III)
No. VI(1)/537/2022.
The High Court, Madras hereby directs and notifies that the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate, Thirukkuvalai in 
Nagapattinam District shall exercise all the powers conferred on a District Munsif under Section 12 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts 
Act,  1873 (Central Act III of 1873) as  amended up-to-date in regard to original suits and proceedings not otherwise exempted 
from his cognizance of which the amount or value of the subject matter does not exceed One Lakh Rupees within the local limits 
of his/her jurisdiction with effect from the date on which the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate, Thirukkuvalai assumes charge 
of that Court.
NOTIFICATION-IV
(Roc. No. 1347/A/2014/G/Judn.Phase III))
No. VI(1)/538/2022.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 28 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873) the 
High Court, Madras, hereby directs that the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate, Thirukkuvalai, shall have and exercise 
jurisdiction of a Court of Small Causes under the Provincial Small Cause Courts Act, 1887 for the trial of suits cognizable by a Court 
of small Causes upto the pecuniary limits of Rs.5000/- (Rupees five thousand only) with effect from the date on which the District 
Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Thirukkuvalai, assumes charge of that Court.
High Court, Madras,	
	
	
	
	
	
                              P. DHANABAL,
