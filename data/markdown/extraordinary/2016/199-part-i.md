NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
PUBLIC DEPARTMENT
(Special-B)
WARRANT OF APPOINTMENT OF GOVERNOR
[G.O. Ms. No. 764, Public (Special-B), 2nd September 2016, Aavani 17, Thunmugi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2047.]
No. I/PUSP/17(g-1)/2016.
By virtue of the power vested in me by Articles 155  and 160 of the Constitution of India, I Pranab Mukherjee, President
of India, hereby appoint Shri Chennamaneni Vidyasagar Rao, Governor of Maharashtra to discharge the functions of the
Governor of Tamil Nadu with effect from the date he assumes charge of his office.
Given at Rashtrapati Bhavan, New Delhi, this thirty-first day of August in the year two thousand sixteen
(9th  Bhadrapada, 1938 Saka), in the sixty-seventh year of the Republic of India.
PRANAB MUKHERJEE,
President of India.
P. RAMA MOHANA RAO,
Chief Secretary.
PROCLAMATION OF ASSUMPTION OF OFFICE OF THE GOVERNOR
[G.O. Ms. No. 765, Public (Special-B), 2nd September 2016, Aavani 17, Thunmugi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2047.]
PROCLAMATION
No. I/PUSP/17(g-2)2016.
Whereas Thiru K. Rosaiah, Governor of Tamil Nadu has on the 2nd September, 2016 demitted the Office of the
Governor of Tamil Nadu and Whereas the President of India has appointed Thiru Chennamaneni Vidyasagar Rao,
Governor of Maharashtra to discharge the functions of the Governor of Tamil Nadu.
It is hereby notified that Thiru Chennamaneni Vidyasagar Rao, has entered upon his Office on 2nd September 2016.
P. RAMA MOHANA RAO,
Chief Secretary.
