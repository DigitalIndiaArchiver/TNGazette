GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
Winding up of the affairs of the No.9/MDS/2011, Thippusulthan Auto Drivers Industrial Co-operative
Society Ltd., Chennai and appointment of Official Liquidator.
(Roc. No. 4259/ICF/2015)
No. VI(1)/82/2015.
“Under the powers conferred on the Registrar of Industrial Co-operatives under Section 137(2)(b) of the Tamil Nadu
Co-operative Societies Act, 1983 (Act 30 of 1983), the activities of the Thippusulthan Auto Drivers Industrial Co-operative
Society Ltd., No. 9/MDS/2011, Chennai have been ordered to be wound up with immediate effect by the Principal Secretary/
Industries Commissioner and Director of Industries and Commerce and the Registrar of Industrial Co-operatives,
Mandaivelipakkam, Raja Annamalaipuram, Chennai-28 vide Proc.No.4259/ICF/2015, dated 27th February 2015.”
Under Section 138(1) of the same Act, Deputy Director (Industrial Co-operatives), Chennai has been appointed as
Official Liquidator of the said society  for the purpose of liquidation.
Chennai-600 028,
SWARAN SINGH,
27th February 2015.
Principal Secretary/Industries Commissioner and
Director of Industries and Commerce/Registrar of
Industrial Co-operatives.
Variations to the Approved Master Plan for the Coimbatore Local Planning Area.
[Roc. No. 3391/2014-LPA-1.]
No. VI(1)/83/2015.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 32 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning
Act 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972) and in exercise of powers conferred by the G.O.Ms.No. 94, Housing and Urban
Development (UD4(1) Department dated 12-06-2009 which has been published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette
No. 27, Part II-Section 2, Page No. 228, dated 15-7-2009 the following variations are made to the Master Plan for the
Coimbatore Local Planning Area approved under the said Act and published in the Housing and Urban Development
Department Notification No. ll(2)/HOU/4377/94 at page 1078 of Part II-Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette,
dated the 9th November 1994.
VARIATIONS
In the said Master Plan, in the “LAND USE SCHEDULE” under the heading ”COIMBATORE LOCAL PLANNING AREA
(OTHER THAN COIMBATORE CORPORATION AREA)”’ now Corporation area under the subheading (b) Non Notified
Detailed Development Plan Area in Coimbatore South Taluk, Thirumalayampalayam Village.
(i) Against the entry “AGRICULTURE” for the expression “331, 338” the expression ”331,338" (except 331/1,
331/2A, 338/1A, 1B, 1C) shall be substituted.
(ii) Against the entry “EDUCATIONAL” the expression 331/1, 331/2A, 338/1A, 338/1B, 338/1C Shall be added
before the entry 95.
Coimbatore,
C. MATHIVANAN,
19th March 2015.
Member-Secretary (Incharge),
Coimbatore Local Planning Authority.
