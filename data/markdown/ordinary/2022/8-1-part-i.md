PUBLIC DEPARTMENT
(Special-A)
No. I/PUSP/6/2022.—
[Deputation Thiru S. Annadurai, Central Labour Service (CLS), 
Regional Labour Commissioner (Central), Government 
of India, Chennai - Deputation to Government of 
Tamil Nadu - Notified.]
The following Government Order is Published:—
[G.O. Ms. No. 152, Public (Special-A), 11th February 2022, 
Thai 29, Pilava, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2053.]
Read:
1.	 From Thiru S. Annadurai, Central Labour Service 
(CLS), Regional Labour Commissioner (Central), 
Government of India, Chennai letter dated 30.09.2021. 
2.	 The Government Letter No.1449/2021-2, Public 
(Special-A) Department, Dated 26.10.2021. 
3.	 From the Government of India, Ministry of Labour and 
Employment File No.A-19011/35/2014-CLS-I, Dated 
18.01.2022.
	
Order: No. 152, Public (Special-A), 11th February 2022.
In the reference 1st read above, Thiru S. Annadurai, 
Central Labour Service (CLS), Regional Labour Commissioner 
(Central), Government of India, Chennai has requested 
for inter cadre deputation with Government of Tamil Nadu. 
The Government of India, Ministry of Labour and Employment, 
New Delhi has conveyed its concurrence for inter cadre 
deputation of Thiru S.Annadurai, CLS. 
2. The Government after careful examination considered 
the request of Thiru S. Annadurai, CLS, Regional Labour 
Commissioner (Central), Government of India, Chennai and 
agreed to his deputation to the Government of Tamil Nadu 
for the period of 3 (Three) years with pay protection. 
3. The following postings is notified:- 
Thiru S.Annadurai, CLS, Regional Labour Commissioner 
(Central), Government of India, Chennai is posted as Director 
of Tribal Welfare in the place of Thiru V.C.Rahul, IFS on 
deputation basis in the pay matrix (Central) level 11, i.e. 
Rs.67,700/- to 2,08,700/-. 
4. 	 During the period of deputation, Thiru S.Annadurai, 
CLS is allowed to draw his pay in his time scale of pay, 
allowances and entitlements as applicable to him in his parent 
cadre and other perquisites as eligible to the post to which 
he is posted.
(By Order of the Governor)  
	
	
	
	
V. IRAI ANBU,	
	
	
	
	
 Chief Secretary.
