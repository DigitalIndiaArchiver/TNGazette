HOUSING AND URBAN DEVELOPMENT DEPARTMENT
APPOINTMENT OF CERTAIN PERSON AS CHAIRMAN OF THE TAMIL NADU HOUSING BOARD
UNDER THE TAMIL NADU STATE HOUSING BOARD ACT.
[G.O. (D). No. 5, Housing and Urban Development [HB1(1)], 10th January 2012.]
No. II(2)/HOU/22(d)/2012.
Under Section 4 of the Tamil Nadu State Housing Board Act, 1961 (Tamil Nadu Act 17 of 1961), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints Thiru R. Murugiah Pandian, former Member of Legislative
Assembly as the Chairman of the Tamil Nadu Housing Board with effect on and from 10th January 2012.
K. PHANINDRA REDDY,
Secretary to Government.
