No. II(2)/CTR/527/2008.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by clause (a) of sub-section (1) of Section 9 of
the Indian Stamp Act, 1899 (Central Act II of 1899), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby remits the difference of duty
chargeable under the said Act amounting to Rs.30,304/-
(Rupees Thirty thousand three hundred and four only) in
respect of the instruments of sale relating to Document
Numbers 50/90, 52/90 and 351/90, registered at Sub-Registrar
Office, Subramaniapuram of Pattukottai Registration District.
Remission of difference of Registration Fee payable for
instruments of sale relating to certain Documents
registered at Pattukottai Registration District under
the Registration Act.
[G.O.Rt. No. 1002, Commercial Taxes and Registration (J2),
20th October 2008.]
No. II(2)/CTR/528/2008.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by Section 78-A of the Registration Act, 1908
(Central Act XVI of 1908), as  the Governor of Tamil Nadu
is of the opinion that it is necessary so to do in the public
interest, hereby remits the difference of fees payable under
the said Act amounting to Rs. 2,530/- (Rupees two thousand
five hundred and thirty only) in respect of the instruments
of sale relating to Document Numbers 50/90, 52/90 and
351/90, registered at Sub-Registrar Office, Subramaniapuram
of Pattukottai Registration District.
Remission of Tax payable by Thiruvalargal Thanjavur Art
Plate Workers’ Industrial Co-operative Society Limited,
Thanjavur on the sale of Art Plates for certain period
under the Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax Act.
[G.O. (2D) No. 155, Commercial Taxes and Registration (D2),
17th October 2008, Aippasi 1, Thiruvalluvar Andu-2039.]
No.II(2)/CTR/529/2008.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by Section 31 read with Section 88 of the Tamil
Nadu Value Added Tax Act, 2006 (Tamil Nadu Act 32 of
2006), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby remits the penalty
of Rs.84,920/- (Rupees Eighty four thousand nine hundred
and twenty only) payable by Thiruvalargal Thanjavur Art Plate
Workers’ Industrial Co-operative Society Limited, Thanjavur,
on the sale of Art Plates in respect of the period 2002-2003
under the said Act.

as the case may be, do hereby publish the following Notice
under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu
Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002).

Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002), the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the land specified, in the

Thiruninravur.

of the Spceial Deputy Collector (L.A), Tamil Nadu Urban
