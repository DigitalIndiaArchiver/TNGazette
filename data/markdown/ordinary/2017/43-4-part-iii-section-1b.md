No. SRO B-68/2017.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu General Service 
(Section 16 in Volume II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual). 
AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules-  
 
(1) in Part I, for the expression "Class XLIV-Legal Advisor to the Deputy Inspector-General of Police (C.I.D.), 
Chennai"  the following expression shall be substituted, namely:-
 
 
"Class XLIV-Legal Advisor in the Crime Branch Criminal Investigation Department, Chennai.";
 
(2)  in Part II, for the heading “Class XLIV - Legal Advisor to the Deputy Inspector - General of Police (C.I.D.), 
Chennai” and the rules thereunder, the following heading and rules shall be substituted, namely:- 
 
 
“Class XLIV - Legal Advisor in the Crime Branch Criminal Investigation Department, Chennai. 
1. Constitution.-This Class shall consist of the Post of Legal Advisor in the Crime Branch Criminal Investigation Department, 
Chennai. 
2. Appointment.- Appointment to the post shall be made by transfer from among the holders of the Post of Assistant 
Public Prosecutor, Grade-l in Class XLIX of the Tamil Nadu General Service. 
3. Postings.- Postings to the post on return from leave shall be made by Additional Director General of Police, Crime 
Branch C.I.D.”.
NIRANJAN MARDI,
Additional Chief Secretary to Government.
