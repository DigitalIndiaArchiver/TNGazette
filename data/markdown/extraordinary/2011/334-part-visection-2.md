MUNICIPAL CORPORATIONS—HOLDING ON PARTY BASIS—NOTIFICATION OF.]
(S.O. No. 28/2011/TNSEC/ME-1.)
No. VI(2)/457(b)/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred by paragraph 3 of the Tamil Nadu Local Bodies Election
Symbols (Reservation and Allotment) Order, 2011, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission hereby
notifies that the elections to the following offices shall be held on party basis:—
1. Members of Town Panchayats;
2. Councillors of Municipalities and Municipal Corporations;
3. Chairman of Town Panchayats and Municipalities; and
4. Mayors of Municipal Corporations.
(By Order of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission)
