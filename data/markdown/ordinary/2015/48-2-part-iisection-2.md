No. II(2)/PUSC/753/2015.—Whereas, an order had been made
by the Government of Tamil Nadu in G.O.No.SR.I/64-7/2015,
Public (SC) Department, dated 21-08-2015, under section 3(1)
of the conservation of Foreign Exchange and Prevention of
Smuggling Activities Act, 1974 (Central Act 52 of 1974), directing
that Thiru C. Vargheese, son of Thiru Chellayan, resident of
Veppuvilai House, Vizhunthaiyambalam, Kanyakumari District,
be detained;

And Whereas, Thiru C. Vargheese, son of Thiru Chellayan,
resident of Veppuvilai House, Vizhunthaiyambalam,
Kanyakumari District cannot be found;
And Whereas, the Government of Tamil Nadu have reasons
to believe that Thiru C. Vargheese, in respect of whom the
detention order has been made has absconded and is concealing
himself so that the said detention order cannot be executed.
Now, Therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by
section 7(1)(b) of the Conservation of Foreign Exchange and
Prevention 
of 
Smuggling 
 
Activities 
Act, 
1974
(Central Act 52 of 1974), the Government of Tamil Nadu hereby
direct Thiru C. Vargheese, son of Thiru Chellayan, resident of
Veppuvilai House, Vizhunthaiyambalam, Kanyakumari District,
to appear before the Superintendent of Police
Kanyakumari District, at his office within a period of 15 days
(fifteen days) from the date of publication of this order in the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
