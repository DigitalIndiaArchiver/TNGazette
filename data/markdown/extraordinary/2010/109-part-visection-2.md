TAMIL NADU STATE ELECTION COMMISSION, CHENNAI-600 106.
[ELECTIONS—CASUAL ELECTIONS—ELECTIONS TO THE OFFICES OF WARD MEMBERS OF
KORADACHERRY TOWN PANCHAYAT, TIRUVARUR DISTRICT—CONDUCT OF—USE OF
ELECTRONIC VOTING MACHINES—ORDERED.]
(S.O. No. 9/2010/TNSEC/ME 2.)
No. VI(2)/138(b)/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 43-D of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu
Act V of 1920), the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission hereby specifies that in the Casual Elections to fill up the
casual vacancies in the offices of Ward Members of Koradacheri Town Panchayat, Tiruvarur District notified in the
S.O. No. 7/2010/TNSEC/ME1, dated 27th March 2010, the giving and recording of votes by electronic voting Machine, shall be
adopted.
(By Order of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission)
Chennai-600 106,
NIRMALA RAMANATHAN,
8th April 2010.
Secretary (i/c),
Tamil Nadu State Election Commission.
