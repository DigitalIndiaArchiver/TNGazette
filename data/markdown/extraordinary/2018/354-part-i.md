No. I/PULO/30(a)/2018.—The  Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints Thiru.C.Emalias,  Additional 
Public Prosecutor and Public Prosecutor (i/c) as Additional Advocate General-XI of Tamil Nadu in the 
High Court of Madras in the post upgraded in the G.O.Ms. No. 814, Public (Law Officers) Department, 
dated 15th October 2018 read above. He is allowed to continue to look after Criminal matters in his 
capacity as Additional Advocate General-XI.
