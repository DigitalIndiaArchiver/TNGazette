LABOUR WELFARE AND SKILL DEVELOPMENT DEPARTMENT
AMENDMENTS TO THE TAMIL NADU MANUAL WORKERS (CONSTRUCTION WORKERS) WELFARE SCHEME, 1994.
[G.O. Ms. No. 52, Labour Welfare and Skill Development (I2), 30th May 2022, 
No. II(2)/LWSD/411(j)/2022.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 4 of the Tamil Nadu Manual Workers (Regulation of Employment and 
Conditions of Work) Act, 1982 (Tamil Nadu Act 33 of 1982), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, after consultation with the Advisory 
Committee, hereby makes the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu Manual Workers (Construction Workers) Welfare 
Scheme, 1994:-
Aඕඍඖඌඕඍඖගඛ.
In the said Scheme,-
(1) in clause 3, after item (9), the following item shall be added, namely:-
“(10) Housing Scheme Assistance to the registered Construction Workers”;
(2) after clause 17-B, the following clause shall be inserted, namely:-
“17-C Housing Scheme Assistance to the registered Construction Workers.- (1) Eligibility,- Every manual worker 
whose name has been registered as per clause 5 and having three years of registration and periodically renewed as per 
clause 6:
 
Provided that the manual worker or his / her family should not own any other pucca concrete houses anywhere 
and should not have been beneﬁ tted under any other housing scheme of the Government:
  
Provided further that the manual worker’s annual income should be less than three lakhs, should own a site 
of suﬃ  cient dimension to construct minimum 300 square feet house, should have clear patta for the site in the name 
of registered construction worker or a joint patta with the registered Construction worker and any other family member. 
(for availing BLC)

(2) Claim,- In the Beneﬁ ciary Led Construction stream, minimum unit size of construction of house is 300 square feet 
for which unit cost of Rs.2.76 lakh and an additional cost of Rs.1.25 lakh shall be sanctioned. Under the Beneﬁ ciary Led 
Construction, if the land owned by the beneﬁ ciary is in rural area, the release of the above mentioned assistance will be 
made through District Rural Development Agency (DRDA) following the norms prescribed under the Pradhan Mantri Awas 
Yojana (Rural) [PMAY(R)] scheme and if it is in urban area, the assistance is released through the Tamil Nadu Urban Habitat 
Development Board following the appropriate norms under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) [PMAY(U)]. 
If the beneﬁ ciary opts for a tenement constructed by the Tamil Nadu Urban Habitat Development Board following the 
norms under PMAY(U) Scheme, the amount to be paid by the beneﬁ ciary shall be sanctioned as assistance subject to a 
maximum of Rs.4 lakh only.
In the above mentioned streams the ﬁ nancial assistance is restricted to Rs.4.00 lakh (a sum of Rs.2.76 lakh as unit 
cost and Rs.1.25 lakh as additional cost) shall be borne by the Tamil Nadu Construction Workers Welfare Board as Housing 
Scheme Assistance to the beneﬁ ciary.
(3) The amount shall be sanctioned by the Assistant Commissioner of Labour (Social Security Scheme) of the respective 
District only if the following conditions are fulﬁ lled, namely:-
1.  (a) Beneﬁ ciary shall be selected from among the members based on the date of registration in the Tamil Nadu 
Construction Workers Welfare Board and the  seniority list shall be made accordingly by the Tamil Nadu Construction Workers 
 Welfare Board;
 
(b) The beneﬁ ciaries of the housing scheme can choose either of the two streams for  availing ﬁ nancial assistance 
viz. Beneﬁ ciary Led Construction (BLC) and allotment  of tenements constructed by the Tamil Nadu Urban Habitat Development 
Board;
 
(c) In the Beneﬁ ciary Led Construction stream, minimum unit size of construction of house is 300 square feet 
for which unit cost of Rs.2.76 lakhs and an additional cost of Rs.1.25 lakh shall be sanctioned. Under the Beneﬁ ciary Led 
Construction if the land owned by the beneﬁ ciary is in rural area the release of the above mentioned assistance will be through 
District Rural Development Agency (DRDA) following the norms prescribed under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Rural) 
(PMAY(R)) scheme and if it is in urban area, the assistance is released through the Tamil Nadu Urban Habitat Development 
Board following the appropriate norms under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) [PMAY(U)]. 
Explanation,- A beneﬁ ciary should have clear patta for the site in the name of a registered construction worker or a 
joint patta with the registered construction worker and any other family member for availing the Beneﬁ ciary Led Construction 
scheme. Eligible beneﬁ ciaries shall be selected provisionally by the District Assistant Commissioner of Labour (Social Security 
Scheme) of the respective District and the list should contain all the details such as the name of the beneﬁ ciary, address, 
registration number, aadhaar number, bank account number, duration of registration, building plan, ownership of land etc. 
The District Assistant Commissioner of Labour (Social Security Scheme) of the respective district shall examine every 
application and after due veriﬁ cation shall send the application along with documents to the District Collector for veriﬁ cation 
that the beneﬁ ciary has not availed Housing Scheme Assistance under any other Government Scheme and thereafter to the 
Tamil Nadu Construction Workers Welfare Board for necessary ﬁ nal approval. The District Assistant Commissioners of Labour 
(Social Security Scheme) shall handover the list of the ﬁ nal approval order and sanction order of Housing Scheme Assistance 
to the concerned Block Development Oﬃ  cer (if the site is in Rural area) under intimation to Collector or to the Tamil Nadu 
Urban Habitat Development Board (if the beneﬁ ciary’s site is in urban area) for the execution of the Scheme. The claim shall 
be made in Form L in Schedule II.
 
(d) If the beneﬁ ciary opts for a tenement constructed by the Tamil Nadu Urban Habitat Development Board following 
the norms under PMAY(U) Scheme the amount to be paid by the beneﬁ ciary shall be sanctioned as assistance subject to a 
maximum of Rs.4,00,000/- (Rupees four lakh only).
Explanation,- Eligible beneﬁ ciaries shall be selected provisionally by the District Assistant Commissioners of Labour (Social 
Security Scheme) of the respective district and the list should contain all the details such as the name of the beneﬁ ciary, 
address, registration number, aadhaar number, bank account number, duration of registration etc., and seniority list shall be 
made accordingly. The District Assistant Commissioner of Labour (Social Security Scheme) of the respective district shall 
examine every application and after due veriﬁ cation shall send the application along with documents to the District Collector 
for veriﬁ cation that the beneﬁ ciary has not availed Housing Scheme Assistance under any other Government Scheme 
and thereafter to the Tamil Nadu Construction Workers Welfare Board for necessary ﬁ nal approval. The District Assistant 
Commissioner of Labour (Social Security Scheme) shall submit the list of eligible beneﬁ ciaries along with the approval order 
and sanction order of Housing Scheme Assistance to the Tamil Nadu Construction Workers Welfare Board to hand over to 
the Tamil Nadu Urban Habitat Board for allotment. The claim shall be made in Form-M in Schedule II.
 
(e) If the number of beneﬁ ciaries is more than 10,000 then it may be restricted to 10,000 beneﬁ ciaries per annum.
