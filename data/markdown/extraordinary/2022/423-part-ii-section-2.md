WHEREAS, the Asian Elephant is considered to be a premier ﬂ agship species whose presence is a crucial indicator of 
the health of a forest eco-system,
AND WHEREAS the Government of Tamil Nadu is deeply committed to conserve Elephants and their habitats, 
AND WHEREAS the forest areas of Kalakad Mundanthurai Tiger Reserve and Kanniyakumari Wildlife Sanctuary in 
Agasthyamalai landscape constitute most important habitats of Asian Elephants in the southernmost part of the Western Ghats,
AND WHEREAS the Government of Tamil Nadu has decided to ensure conservation of the rich elephant habitats in the 
area and to also to enrich the health and connectivity of the elephant migratory paths in Agasthyamalai landscape through 
proper research and eﬀ ective management techniques,  
AND WHEREAS the Government of India, in their letter No. F. No. 7-2/2022-PE, dated 13.07.2022 have conveyed their 
consent for creation of Agasthyamalai Elephant Reserve in the State of Tamil Nadu for the purpose of elephant conservation, 
AND WHEREAS for the afore stated purposes, the Government of Tamil Nadu have decided to notify Agasthyamalai 
Elephant Reserve with a total area of 1,19,748.26 hectares, constituting following areas listed in the Schedule below.
