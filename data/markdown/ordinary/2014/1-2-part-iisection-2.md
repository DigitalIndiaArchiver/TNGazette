No. II(2)/1/2014.—The Governor of Tamil Nadu having been
satisfied that the lands in the schedule below are required for
the purpose of widening and strengthening from two lane to
four lane of Mount Madipakkam Road at Km 0/0-2/0 and
1/6–8/6 of  Ullagaram Village it having already been decided
that the entire amount of Compensation to be awarded for the
lands is to be paid out of the funds controlled and managed
by the Division Engineer, Chennai Metropolitan Development
Project-II, Division-I, Guindy, Chennai – 32 and after having
considered the cause shown by the owner or other person
having interest in the said lands, as the case may be, do hereby
publish the following notice under sub-section (1) of Section
15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act
34/2002).
NOTICE
Under  sub  section  (1)  of  Section  15  of  the
Tamil Nadu  Highways  Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu  Act
34/2002), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the wet
lands specified in the schedule below measuring 1697 Sq. Mtr.
to the same, a little more or less required for the purpose of
construction of widening to four lane and Strengthening of
Mount Madipakkam Road at KM 0/0 - 2/0 and 1/6 – 8/6 of
Ullagaram Village.
The plan of the land under Acquisition kept in the Office
of the Special Deputy Collector (L.A), Tamil Nadu Urban
Development Project-III, Chennai at Poonamallee and may be
inspected at any time during office hours.
