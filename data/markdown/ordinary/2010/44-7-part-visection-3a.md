TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI
Taking over the Direct Management of Ponnamapettai Sunnath Jamath Masjith,
Salem Taluk and District.
(Rc. No. 9612/B7/Salem/08)
No. VI-3(a)/130/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act, 1995, the Tamil Nadu Wakf
Board in its resolution in item No.153/10 (Rc. No. 9612/B7/ Salem/08), dated 4th August 2010 has ordered to take over the
mangement of Ponnamapettai Sunnath Jamath Masjith, Salem Taluk and District under the Direct Control of the
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of Six Months from the date of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, publication of
the above Wakf and the Wakf Inspector, Salem, has been appointed as its Executive Officer of the above Wakf.
Chennai-600 001,
A. MOHAMED JAMALUDDIN,
