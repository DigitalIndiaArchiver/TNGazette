The Governor of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that
the lands specified in the schedule below are acquired under
TNRSP-II  Highways purpose, to wit for Upgrading Sadras-
Chengalpattu Road (SH-58) from km 0/000  to  km 26/800
Nathamkariyacheri, Keerapakkam, Mangalam, Egai,
Thirukazhukundram, Nenmeli and Neikupi Villages,
Thirukazhukundram Taluk, Kancheepuram District  and it
having already been decided that the entire amount of
compensation to be awarded for the acquisition of lands is
to be paid out of the funds controlled and managed by
Highways Department and after having  considered  the
cause shown by the owner or other persons having interest
in the said lands,  as the case may be,  do hereby issue and
publish the following notice under sub-section (1) of
Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001
(Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu
Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act, 34 of 2002), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the dry and manai
lands specified in the Schedule below and measuring to an
extent of 2754 Sq.mtrs in Nathamkariyacheri, Keerapakkam,
Mangalam, Egai, Thirukazhukundram, Nenmeli and Neikupi
Villages, Thirukazhukundram Taluk,  Kancheepuram  District
to the same, a little more or less needed for TNRSP-II
Highways purpose, to wit for upgrading State Highways
(SH-58 ) Sadras–Chengalpattu Road from Km 0/000 to Km
26/800.
The plan    of the   lands  under   acquisition  is   kept
in the Office of the  Special District Revenue Officer     (Land
Acquisition), Tamil    Nadu    Road    Sector   Project -II,
Chennai and the Special Tahsildar (Land  Acquisition),
Tamil  Nadu  Road  Sector  Project-II, Kancheepuram and
may be  inspected at  anytime during office hours.
