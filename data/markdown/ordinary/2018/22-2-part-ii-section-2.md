No. II(2)/HE/490/2018.—In the matter of the Charitable 
Endowments Act, 1890 (Central Act VI of 1890) and in the 
matter of "Dr. Amirthavalli Raghupathy Charitable Endowment 
Fund "(hereafter referred to as "the said Endowment") 
tenable in the Presidency College, Chennai. 	
I. VESTING OF PROPERTIES
WHEREAS, an application has been made to the 
Government of Tamil Nadu under clause (b) of 
sub-section (1) of Section 6 of the Charitable Endowments 
Act, 1890 (Central Act VI of 1890) by N.K. Raghupathy, 
I.A.S., (Retired), 30, Coastal Road, Besant Nagar, Chennai- 
600 090 the donor of the said Endowment for vesting  the 
property, specified in the Schedule below, in the Treasurer 
of Charitable Endowments, Chennai, appointed under Section 
3 of the said Act for the State of Tamil Nadu, the Governor 
of Tamil Nadu, in exercise of the powers conferred by 
Section 4 of the said Act, hereby orders that the said 
property be vested in the Treasurer aforesaid, and be 
held by him and his successors- in- office, subject to the 
provisions of the said Act and the rules, which from time to 
time, may be or shall be made or deemed to have been 
made thereunder and subject to the conditions setforth in 
the Scheme settled under Section 5 of the said Act, for 
the administration of the said Endowment. 
THE SCHEDULE. 
THE PROPERTY. 
Rs. 1,00,000/- (Rupees One Lakh Only). 
II. Scheme For Administration
WHEREAS, an application has been made to 
the Government of Tamil Nadu under clause (b) of 
sub-section (1) of Section 6 of the Charitable Endowments 
Act, 1890 (Central Act VI of 1890) by N.K.Raghupathy, 
I.A.S.,(Retired), 30, Coastal Road, Besant Nagar, Chennai- 
600 090, the donor of the said Endowment specified 
above, for settling a scheme for administration of the said 
Endowment, the Governor of Tamil Nadu, in exercise of the 
powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the said 
Act, hereby settles the Scheme set forth in the Schedule 
below, for the administration of the said Endowment and 
appoints the Endowment Committee Presidency College, 
Chennai to administer the property. The Scheme shall come 
into operation from the date of publication of this Notification 
in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette. 
The Schedule
(1) 	The Fund shall be called "Dr.Amirthavalli Raghupathy 
Charitable Endowment Fund" tenable in Presidency 
College,Chennai- 600 005. 
(2) 	The Fund shall be administered by the Endowment 
Committee, Presidency College,Chennai-600 005. 
(3) 	From and out of the annual interest accruing on 
the Fund, the prizes shall be given to the top 
Ranker of Bachelor of Science, Chemistry students 
of Presidency College,Chennai-600 005. 
(4) 	If more than one student securing the same marks, 
the prizes shall be equally distributed among them. 
(5) 	The mode by which the interest amount shall be 
given to the students may be specified by the 
administration. 
(6) 	The unutilized amount of the interest shall be added 
to the corpus of the Fund. 
(7) 	The decision of the administrator shall be final in 
all matters relating to the administration of the Fund. 
(8) The amount of the said Fund shall be invested in 
Government Securities.
