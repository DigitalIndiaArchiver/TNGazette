Ad hoc Rules relating to the Tamil Nadu Engineering Subordinate Service for the Temporary Posts of
Technical Assistants in the Fisheries Department.
[G.O. Ms. No. 307, Animal Husbandry and Fisheries, 22nd September 1992.]
No. SRO B-86/2009.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following rules:—
2. The rules hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on and from the 12th January 1983.
RULES
The General and the Special Rules applicable to the holders of the permanent posts in the Tamil Nadu Engineering
Subordinate Service shall apply to the holders of the temporary posts of Technical Assistants (Civil/Electrical) sanctioned
in the Fishing Harbour Project Circle under the control of the Fisheries Department subject to the modifications specified
in the following rules.
2. Constitution.—The posts shall constitute a distinct class in the said service.
3. Appointment.— Appointment to the post shall be made as follows:—
(1) by direct recruitment; or
(2) by recruitment by transfer from any other service.
4. Appointing Authority.—The appointing authority for the post shall be the Superintending Engineering, Fishing Harbour
Projects Circle, Madras.
5. Qualification—(a) Age—No Person shall be eligible for appointment to the post by direct recruitment if he has
completed or will complete 28 years of age on the first day of July of the year in which the selection for appointment
is made:
Provided that the age limit shall be increased by 5 years in respect of candidates belonging to Backward Classes,
Scheduled Castes or Scheduled Tribes:
Provided further that in the case of persons recruited from work charged establishment, the period of their continuous
service thereon shall be reduced while computing the maximum age limit.
(b) Other Qualification: No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post by the method of appointment
specified in column (1), of the Table below, unless he possesses the qualifications specified in the corresponding entries
in column (2) thereof:—

6. Reservation of Appointments.— The rule relating to reservation of appointment (General Rule 22) shall apply for
appointment to the post by direct recruitment.
7. Probation.—Every person appointed to the post by direct recruitment shall from the date on which he joins duty
be on probation for a total period of two years on duty within a continuous period of three years.
8. Test.—  A probationer in the category of Technical Assistant (Civil/Electrical) shall, within the period of his probation,
pass the Account Test for Public Works Officers and Subordinates, part I and part II, failing which his second and
subsequent increments shall be stopped without cumulative effect till, such time he passes the test. Such stoppage shall
not have the effect of postponing his further increments, after he has passed the test, provided that persons who have
already passed the test need not pass the test again.
9. Unit for seniority.—Seniority shall be maintained on a Statewide basis.
10. Postings and Transfers.— Postings and transfers shall be made by the Superintending Engineer, Fishing Harbours
Projects Circle, Madras within the Division and from one division to another.
11. Pay.—The acting incumbents in the posts shall be paid in the scale of pay of Rs.1350-30-1440-40-1800-50-2200,
as applicable to the staff working in the Public Works Department.
