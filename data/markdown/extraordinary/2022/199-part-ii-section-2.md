No. II(2)/PUB/205(e)/2022.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of section 16-B of the Indian Criminal Law Amendment 
Act, 1908 (Central Act 14 of 1908) as amended by the Indian Criminal Law  Amendment (Tamil Nadu Amendment) 
Act, 1998 (Tamil Nadu  Act 42 of 1998), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notiﬁ es the cessation of the 
Tribunal  constituted for the State for the purpose of section 16-A of the said Act, in Public (SC) Department 
Notiﬁ cation No. II(2)/PU/352(c)/2013, published at page 1 of Part II-Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government 
Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 4th May 2013, with eﬀ ect from 4th April, 2022.
