Amendments to the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Rules
[G.O. Ms. No. 54, Co-operation, Food and Consumer Protection (CJ1), 29th June 2011,

No. SRO A-10/2011.—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-sections (1), (2) and (3) of Section 180 of the
Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act, 1983 (Tamil Nadu Act 30 of 1983), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the
following amendments to the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Rules, 1988:—
AMENDMENTS.
In the said Rules, in Schedule II, in Part-A,—
(1) under the heading “1, Tamil Nadu State Apex Co-operative Bank Ltd., Chennai”, in item (4), for the expression
“The Managing Director of the Bank”, the expression “The Chief Executive Officer of the Bank” shall be substituted;
(2) under the heading “13 (A) Central Co-operative Banks (other than Chennai Central Co-operative Bank)”,—
(a) in item (l),—
(i) in sub-item (a), for the expression “Primary Agricultural Service Co-operative Societies” the expression
“Primary Agricultural Co-operative Credit Societies” shall be substituted;
(ii) in sub-item (b),—
(A) for the expression “(iii) Urban Banks”, the expression “(iii) Co-operative Urban Banks” shall be substituted;
(B) for the expression “(vii) Co-operative Spinning Mills and Sugar Mills”, the expression “(vii) Co-operative Spinning
Mills and Co-operative Sugar Mills” shall be substituted;
(b) in item (4), for the expression “The Managing Director of the Bank”, the expression “The Chief Executive
Officer of the Bank” shall be substituted; and
(3) under the heading 13 (B) Chennai Central Co-operative Bank Limited, Chennai, in item (4) for the expression “The
Managing Director of the Bank”, the expression “The Chief Executive Officer of the Bank” shall be substituted.
JATINDRA NATH SWAIN,
Secretary to Government.

5.
Appeal against a decision regarding nature of, or refusal to issue, a Disability Certificate.—(1) Any person
aggrieved by the nature of Certificate issued to him or by refusal to issue such a Certificate in his favour, may appeal against
such a decision to the Joint Director (Medical Services) of the District concerned.

legal guardian.

(3) On receipt of an appeal the appellate Authority shall, after giving the appellant an opportunity of being heard, pass
such orders on it as it may deem appropriate.
