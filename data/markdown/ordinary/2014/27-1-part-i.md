No. I/PLAR/21/2014.—On completion of the term of six
years from the date of entry upon the office of the Member of
the Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission Thiru G. Shanmuga
Murugan, ceases to hold office as Member, Tamil Nadu Public
Service Commission on the afternoon of 1st July 2014 without
prejudice to the outcome of the W.P.No. 24653 of 2011,
pending in the Hon'ble High Court of Madras and
Cr.No. 22/2011 registered by the Director of Vigilance and
Anti-corruption on 13th October 2011 under Section 120(B)
IPC, 13(2) read with 13(1)(d), 15 of the Prevention of Corruption
Act, 1988 and Sections 175 and  186 IPC.
MOHAN VERGHESE CHUNKATH,
Chief Secretary.

No. I/PUSP/22/2014.—The Government direct that the period
of deputation of Thiru P. Karupasamy, Director, Central
Secretariat Service with Government of Tamil Nadu be extended
for a period of one year beyond 22nd April 2014 with the
Concurrence of Government of India, Department of Personnel
and Training, New Delhi. He is allowed to continue in the post
of Additional Commissioner of Labour, Chennai.
MOHAN VERGHESE CHUNKATH,
