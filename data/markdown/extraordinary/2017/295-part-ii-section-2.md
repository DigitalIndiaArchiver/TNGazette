FINANCE DEPARTMENT
(Salaries)
NOTIFICATIONS UNDER THE TAMIL NADU TRANSPARENCY IN TENDERS ACT
[G.O. Ms. No. 269, Finance (Salaries), 19th September 2017, Purattasi 3, Hevilambi,
Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2048.]
No. II(2)/FIN/783(d)/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu
Transparency in Tenders (Amendment) Act, 2017 (Tamil Nadu Act 45 of 2017), the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the 19th day of September 2017 as the date on which the said
Act shall come into force.
K. SHANMUGAM,
     Additional Chief Secretary to Government.
