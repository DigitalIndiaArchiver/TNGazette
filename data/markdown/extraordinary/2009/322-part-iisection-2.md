NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION AND WATER SUPPLY DEPARTMENT
REMOVAL OF CERTAIN PERSON AS CHAIRMAN, GUDIYATHAM MUNICIPAL COUNCIL UNDER
TAMIL NADU DISTRICT MUNICIPALITIES  ACT, 1920.
[G.O. (D) No. 528, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (MA-V), 10th December 2009.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/666(a)/2009.
Whereas a written notice of intention to make a motion expressing want of confidence in
Thiru. R.S. Sivaprakasam, Chairman, Gudiyatham Municipal Council signed by thirty Councillors of the
said Municipal Council, out of the sanctioned strength of thirty six members (1 post of Councillor is
vacant in Ward No. 14) of the said Council together with a copy of the motion proposed to be made
have been delivered to the Regional Director of Municipal Administration, Vellore, in person by twenty
Councillors under the provisions of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu
Act V of 1920);
 And, whereas the said motion expressing want of confidence in  Thiru. R.S. Sivaprakasam,  has been
carried with the support of twenty nine members which is more than the four-fifth of the sanctioned strength
of thirty six members, in the meeting convened by the Regional Director of Municipal Administration,
Vellore on the 21st October 2009, after following the procedures specified in Section 40-A of the said Act;
 Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (12) of Section 40-A of the
Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby removes Thiru. R.S. Sivaprakasam, Chairman, Gudiyatham Municipal Council, from the Office
of Chairman of the said Municipal Council.
  NIRANJAN MARDI,
Secretary to Government.
