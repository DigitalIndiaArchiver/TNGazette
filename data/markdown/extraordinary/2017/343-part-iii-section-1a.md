In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 164 of the Tamil Nadu  Goods and Services Tax Act, 2017 (Tamil Nadu 
Act 19 of 2017), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following rules further to amend the Tamil Nadu Goods and 
Services Tax Rules, 2017, namely:- 
 
(1) These rules may be called the Tamil Nadu Goods and Services Tax (Eighth Amendment) Rules, 2017.
2. In the Tamil Nadu Goods and Services Tax Rules, 2017, with effect from the 29th June, 2017, after “FORM GST 
RFD-10”, the Form  “FORM GST RFD-11”, “Bond for export of goods or services without payment of integrated tax”, and 
“Letter of Undertaking for export of goods or services without payment of integrated tax” shall be inserted, namely:-
