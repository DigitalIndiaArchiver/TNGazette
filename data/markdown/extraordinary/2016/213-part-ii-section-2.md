NOTIFICATION - I
No. II(2)/MAWS/640(c-3)/2016
In exercise of the powers conferred by section 50-A of the Coimbatore City Municipal Corporation Act, 1981 (Tamil Nadu
Act 25 of 1981) read with section 8 of the Thanjavur City Municipal Corporation Act, 2013 (Tamil Nadu Act 24 of 2013),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that the powers exercisable by the Government under sub-sections (4), (5)
and (6) of section 5 of the said Tamil Nadu Act 24 of 2013 and section 51 of the said Tamil Nadu Act 25 of 1981, in
respect of the Corporation of Thanjavur, be exercisable by the Commissioner, Corporation of Thanjavur, also.
NOTIFICATION - II
In exercise of the powers conferred by section 50-A of the Coimbatore City Municipal Corporation Act, 1981 (Tamil Nadu
Act 25 of 1981) read with section 8 of the Dindigul City Municipal Corporation Act, 2013 (Tamil Nadu Act 25 of 2013), the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that the powers exercisable by the Government under sub-sections (4), (5) and (6) of section 5
of the said Tamil Nadu Act 25 of 2013 and section 51 of the said Tamil Nadu Act 25 of 1981, in respect of the Corporation of
Dindigul, be exercisable by the Commissioner, Corporation of Dindigul, also.
K. PHANINDRA REDDY,
