Exclusion of certain Lands from operation of Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act.
(Rc. 45807/2009/E4.)
No. VI(2)/72/2010.

of 1994) I, A. Suganthi, I.A.S., Collector of Pudukkottai district hereby directs that from and after the date of publication of this
Notification in the Pudukkottai District Gazette/Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, the plot of land as detailed in the Schedule
below, lying within the limits of Thirukalambur Panchayat of Ponnamaravathy Taluk, Pudukkottai District shall be excluded from
the operation of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994.
