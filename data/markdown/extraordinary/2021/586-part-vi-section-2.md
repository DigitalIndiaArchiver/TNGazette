COMMISSIONERATE OF TOWN PANCHAYATS, CHENNAI - 28.
DETERMINATION OF WARDS IN TOWN PANCHAYATS FOR THE RESERVATION TO THE PERSONS BELONGING 
TO SCHEDULED CASTES, SCHEDULED TRIBES, WOMEN  BELONGING TO SCHEDULED CASTES, SCHEDULED 
TRIBES AND WOMEN.
(Roc.No.1169/2020/C2)
No. VI(2)/125(a)/2021.
WHEREAS in pursuance of the orders of the Hon’ble Supreme Court of India in I.A.No.182868 of 2019 in Civil 
Appeal Nos.5467-5469 of 2017, dated the 6th December 2019, based on the recommendations of the Delimitation 
Commission constituted under the Tamil Nadu Delimitation Commission Act, 2017 (Tamil Nadu Act 23 of 2017), the 
delimitation of territorial wards of Town Panchayats in the nine newly constituted districts of Kancheepuram, Chengalpattu, 
Tirunelveli, Tenkasi, Vellore, Ranipet, Tirupathur, Villupuram and Kallakurichi have been published in Commissioner of Town 
Panchayats Notiﬁ cation No.VI(2)/111(b)/2021 in Part VI - Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, Extraordinary, 
dated the 2nd November 2021.
AND WHEREAS, the Delimitation Commission has submitted its recommendations for reservation of seats in the 
territorial wards of the Town Panchayats for the persons belonging to the Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes, 
Women belonging to Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes and Women under section 4(1)(f) of the Tamil Nadu 
Delimitation Commission Act, 2017 (Tamil Nadu Act 23 of 2017).
NOW, THEREFORE, under sub-sections (1), (2) and (3) of section 3-W of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 
1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920), read with rule 4 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Municipalities and Corporations 
(Delimitation of Wards or Divisions and Reservation) Rules, 1996 and as authorised by the Government in G.O.(Ms)
No.65, Municipal Administration and Water Supply Department, dated the 7th August 2006, the Commissioner of Town 
Panchayats hereby reserves the wards for the persons belonging to the Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes, 
Women belonging to Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes and Women as speciﬁ ed in the following amendments to 
the Director of Town Panchayats, Notiﬁ cation No.VI(2)/46(c)/2019, published in Part VI – Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu 
Government Gazette, Extraordinary, dated the 24th May 2019:—
