ADVERTISEMENT  OF  PETITION
Take Notice that a Petition under Sections 433(a) and 439 of the Companies Act, 1956 for the voluntary winding
up of M/s.BPV Classic Tea Factory P. Ltd., by the High Court of Judicature at Madras was on this
the 25th day  of October 2006, presented to the said Court by the Petitioner abovenamed and the said petition is
directed to be heard before the Court on the 20th day of January 2014. Any creditor contributory or other persons
desirous of supporting or opposing the making of an order on the said petition should send to the Petitioner or his
Advocate notice of his intention signed by him or his advocate with his name and address, so as to reach the
Petitioner or his advocate not later then 5 days before the date fixed for the hearing of the Petition and appear at
the hearing for the purpose in person or by his advocate. A copy of the petition will be furnished by the undersigned
to any creditor or contributory on payment of the prescribed charges for the same. Any Affidavit intended to be used
in opposition to the Petition should be filed in Courts and a copy served on the Petitioner or his Advocate, not less
than 5 days before the date fixed for the hearing.
Dated at Chennai, this the 20th day of November, 2013.
