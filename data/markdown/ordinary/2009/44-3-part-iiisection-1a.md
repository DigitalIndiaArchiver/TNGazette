LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT DEPARTMENT
Amendment to the Tamil Nadu Standards of Weights and Measures (Enforcement) Rules, 1989.
[G.O. Ms. No. 152, Labour and Employment (K2), 15th October 2009, Purattasi 29, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. SRO A-38/2009.—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 72 of the Standards of
Weights and Measures (Enforcement) Act, 1985 (Central Act 54 of 1985), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, after consultation
with the Central Government, hereby makes the following amendment to the Tamil Nadu Standards of Weights and
Measures (Enforcement) Rules, 1989, the draft of the same having been previously published as required under
sub-section (4) of Section 72 of the said Act.
2.
The amendment hereby made shall come into force with effect on and from the date of publication of this
Notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules.—
(1) In rule 14, in sub-rule (a), after the fourth proviso, the following proviso shall be added, namely:—
"Provided also that for fabric plastic and steel tape measures, no reverification shall be required”;
(2) in Schedule XII, under the heading “3.LENGTH MEASURES”:—
(i) for item (b) and the entries thereunder, the following item and entries shall, respectively, be substituted,
namely:—
“(b) Flexible:
Class I
:
20 paise/metre
Class II
:
15 paise/metre
Class III
:
10 paise/metre”
(ii) items (c) and (d) shall be omitted.
T. PRABHAKARA RAO,
Principal Secretary to Government.
