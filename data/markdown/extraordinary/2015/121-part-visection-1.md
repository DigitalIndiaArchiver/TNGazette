GENERAL NOTIFICATION
THE COMMISSIONER OF FOOD SAFETY AND DRUG ADMINISTRATION, GOVERNMENT OF TAMIL NADU
Prohibition on Manufacture, Storage, Distribution or sale of Six Noodles under
the Food Safety and Standards Act 2006
(No.3721/2015/S6/FSSA)
No. VI(1)/150(a-1)/2015.
WHEREAS the Central Government, State Government and the Food Safety Commissioner are mandated:
to endeavour to achieve the appropriate level of protection of human life and health as per section 18(1)(a) of Food
Safety and Standards Act, 2006.
to carry out risk management as per section 18(1) (b) of Food Safety and Standards Act, 2006.
to take preventive steps in cases where there are reasonable grounds to suspect that a food article may present a risk
for human health under section 18(1)(f) of Food Safety and Standards Act, 2006.
AND WHEREAS the Food Safety Commissioner has a power and duty under section 30(2)(a) of the Food Safety and Standards
Act, 2006 to prohibit in the interest of public health manufacture, storage, distribution or sale of any article of food;
AND WHEREAS the samples of Maggi 2-Minute noodles with  Tastemaker were tested by the Tamil Nadu Food Safety Authorities
for determining whether they conform to relevant norms,
AND WHEREAS Maggi 2-Minute noodles with Tastemaker are proprietary food under section 22 and the maximum permissible
norm for presence of lead in such unspecified foods is 2.5 parts per million by weight,
AND WHEREAS during the test it was found that the amount of lead present in the tastemaker is beyond the permissible norm,
AND WHEREAS due to the presence of lead beyond the permissible norms, the Maggi 2-Minute noodles with Tastemaker is
unsafe for human consumption,
AND WHEREAS the consumption of Maggi 2-Minute noodles with Tastemaker will endanger public health.
THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred by clause (a) of sub-section (2) of Section 30 of the Food Safety and
Standards Act, 2006 (Central Act 34 of 2006), the Commissioner of Food Safety of the State of Tamil Nadu, in the interest of
public health, hereby prohibits the manufacture, storage, distribution or sale of Maggi 2-Minute noodles with Tastemaker in the
State of Tamil Nadu for a period of 3 months with effect from 04th June 2015.
