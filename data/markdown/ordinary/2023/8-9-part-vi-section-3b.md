THE TAMIL NADU Dr. M.G.R. MEDICAL UNIVERSITY, CHENNAI
Notice of Election 
Result of Election of One Professor Held to the Senate of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, 
Chennai  by the Teachers of all Affiliated Pharmacy Colleges from among themselves under Section 18(3)(d) of the 
Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai Act, 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 1987)
(Rc. No. EIII(1)/09066/2022) 
No. VI-3(b)/3/2023.
Under Sub-statute (1) of Statute 5 of Chapter XXI of the General Statutes of the Tamil Nadu 
Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai, it is hereby published that  Dr. P. Dheen Kumar, M.Pharm., Ph.D., Principal, 
G.P. Pharmacy College, Jolarpettai, Tirupattur District-635 851, has been elected to the Senate of the Tamil Nadu 
Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai by the teachers of all Affiliated Pharmacy Colleges from among themselves 
under clause (d) of sub-section (3) of Section 18 of the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai Act, 
1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 37 of 1987) for a period of (03) three years from 07.10.2022 to 06.10.2025 (or) so long 
as he holds the post of Professor of the Affiliated Pharmacy Colleges of this University, whichever is earlier. 
Chennai-600 032,                                                Dr. M.B. ASWATH NARAYANAN,
17th February  2023.                                                                Registrar and Returning Officer.
