The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that the lands specified in the schedule below have to be acquired for
the purpose of Expansion of Existing Thoothukudi AirPort , and it having already been decided that the entire amount of
compensation to be awarded for the lands are to be paid out of the funds controlled or managed by Transport Department, the
following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of section 3 of Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purpose Act 1997
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purpose Act 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act
10 of 1999). the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquired the lands specified in the schedule below and measuring 14.38.5
hectares to the same, a little more or less needed for the Expansion of Existing Thoothukudi AirPort, to wit for the establishment
of Airport Development   by the Transport Department of T.N. State Govt.
The plan of the lands are kept in the office of the Collector, Thoothukudi and may be inspected at any time during office hours.
