MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION AND WATER SUPPLY DEPARTMENT
DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF THE TAMIL NADU MUNICIPAL LAWS (AMENDMENT) ORDINANCE, 2012.
[G.O. Ms. No. 11, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (Election), 27th January 2012.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/67(b-1)/2012.
Under sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Municipal Laws (Amendment) Ordinance, 2012
(Tamil Nadu Ordinance 3 of 2012), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the  25th October 2011 as
the date on which the said Ordinance shall come into force.
DATE OF COMING INTO FORCE OF THE CHENNAI METROPOLITAN WATER SUPPLY AND SEWERAGE
(AMENDMENT) ORDINANCE, 2012.
[G.O. Ms. No. 11, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (Election), 27th January 2012.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/67(b-2)/2012.
Under sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Chennai Metropolitan Water Supply and Sewerage (Amendment)
Ordinance, 2012 (Tamil Nadu Ordinance 4 of 2012), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the
25th October 2011 as the date on which the said Ordinance shall come into force.
SHEELA BALAKRISHNAN,
Additional Chief Secretary to Government.
