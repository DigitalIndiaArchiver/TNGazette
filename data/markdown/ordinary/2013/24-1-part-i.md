No.I/PU/20/2013.—Accepting the proposals of High Court
sent by the Registrar General, High Court, Madras, the
Government direct that Tmt. S. Mary Anselam, District Judge
of Tamil Nadu State Judicial Service, be deputed to the
Puducherry Judicial Service for being posted as
II Additional District Judge, Pondicherry in the existing
vacancy, on deputation basis and Tmt. R. Margaret
Roseline, District Judge of Puducherry Judicial Service be
appointed as III Additional District Judge, Vellore at
Tirupathur, on deputation basis in the Tamil Nadu State
Judicial Service.
SHEELA BALAKRISHNAN,
