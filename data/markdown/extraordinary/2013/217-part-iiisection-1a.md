In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-sections (1) and (2) of Section 180 of the Tamil Nadu
Co-operative Societies Act, 1983 (Tamil Nadu Act 30 of 1983), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the
following amendments to the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Rules, 1988:-
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules, in Schedule II, under the heading “PART A”,-
(1) In item 13 (A),-
(a)
for the expression “Chennai District Central Co-operative Bank”, the expression “Chennai
Central Co-operative Bank Limited” shall be substituted;
(b)
in sub-item (3), for the expression “(iii) the Chief Executive Officer of the Bank concerned”,
the expression “(iii) the Managing Director of the Bank concerned” shall be substituted;
(2)  in item 13 (B), in sub-item (3), for the expression “(iii) the Chief Executive Officer of the Bank”, the
expression “(iii) the Managing Director of the Bank” shall be substituted.
M.P. NIRMALA,
