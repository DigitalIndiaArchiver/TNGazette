ACQUISITION OF LANDS
[G.O. Ms. No. 77, Municipal Administration and
Water Supply (MC2), 25th May 2009.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/241(d)/2009.
Whereas it appears to the Government of Tamil Nadu
that the lands specified below and situated in the village of
Manimoortheeswaram, Tirunelveli Taluk, Tirunelveli District
is needed for a public purpose to wit for the formation of
New Bye-pass Road between Udaiyarpatti to Bye-pass Road
notice to the effect is hereby given to all whom it may concern
in accordance with the provision of sub-section (1) of Section  (4)
of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894 (Central Act 1 of 1894).
Now, therefore in exercise  of the powers conferred by
sub-section (2) of Section 4 of the said Act, the Governor of
Tamil Nadu hereby authorise the Revenue Divisional Officer,
Tirunelveli and his staff and workmen to exercise the powers
conferred by the said Section and under clause (c) of the
Section 3 of the said Act.  The Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby appoints the Revenue Divisional Officer, Tirunelveli
perform the function of a Collector under the said Act.
Under sub-section (4) of Section 17 of the Act, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu directs, that in view of the urgency
of the case the provisions of Section 5-A of the Act shall not
apply to this case.
