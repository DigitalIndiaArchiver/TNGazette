Constitution of a Sub Court at Thiruthuraipoondi in Tiruvarur District
(Roc.No.21416/2017/G/Judn)
No. VI(1)/101/2022.
In exercise of the powers conferred under the ﬁ rst proviso to Section 13 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central 
Act III of 1873), the High Court, Madras hereby directs that appeals from the decrees or orders of the District Munsif Court, 
Thiruthuraipoondi be preferred in the Court of Subordinate Judge at Thiruthuraipoondi with eﬀ ect from the date on which the 
Subordinate Judge, Thiruthuraipoondi assumes charge of that Court.
High Court, Madras, 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
P. DHANABAL,
18th February 2022. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
        Registrar General. 
Constitution of a Sub Court at Polur in Tiruvannamalai District
(Roc.No.67976/A//2019/G/Judn)
No. VI(1)/102/2022.
In exercise of the powers conferred under the ﬁ rst proviso to Section 13 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central 
Act III of 1873), the High Court, Madras hereby directs that appeals from the decrees or orders of the District Munsif Court, 
Polur and the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Kalasapakkam be preferred in the Court of Subordinate Judge 
at Polur with eﬀ ect from the date on which the Subordinate Judge, Polur assumes charge of that Court.
High Court, Madras, 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
P. DHANABAL,
