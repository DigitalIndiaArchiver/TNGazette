GENERAL  NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI
Extension of Direct Management of Naina Mohamed Tholugai Pallivasal, Rahmaniapuram,
Kadayanallur, Tirunelveli District.
(Lr.Rc. No. 6405/03/B1/Tnv.)
No. VI-3(a)/74/2013.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the Wakf Act 1995, the Tamil Nadu
Wakf Board in its resolution in item No. 115/12 in Rc. No. 6405/03/B1/Tnv dated 29-11-2012 has resolved to ratify the period
of direct management from 31-12-2008 to 28-11-2012 and also to extend the period of direct management of
Naina Mohamed Tholugai Pallivasal, Rahmaniapuram, Kadayanallur, Tirunelveli District which is under the direct control of
the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a further period of one year from the date of Board’s order (29-11-2012).
Chennai-600 001,
F. ABDUL RAZICK,
14th February 2013.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
Extension of Direct Management of Nagal Nagar Jumma Pallivasal and
Junction Pallivasal, Dindigul District.
(Lr.Rc. No. 14109/04/B8/DGL.)
No.VI-3(A)/75/2013.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the wakf Act 1995, the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board
in its resolution in item No. 90/12 in Rc.No. 14109/04//B8/DGL dated 29-11-2012 has resolved to ratify the period of direct
management from 10-8-2011 to 29-11-2012 and also to extend the period of direct management of Nagal Nagar Jumma
Pallivasal and Junction Pallivasal, Dindigul District which is under the direct control of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a
further period of 6 months from the date of Board’s order (29-11-2012).
Chennai-600 001,
F. ABDUL RAZICK,
14th February 2013.
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.

In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the wakf Act 1995, the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board
in its resolution in item No. 53/11 in Rc.No. 15864/05//B8/DGL dated 29-11-2012 has resolved to ratify the period of direct
management from 19-3-2011 to 29-11-2012 and also to extend the period of direct management of Muslim Dharma
Paribalana Sangam, Palani Taluk and Town, Dindigul District which is under the direct control of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board
