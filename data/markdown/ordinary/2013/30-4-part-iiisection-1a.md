PUBLIC DEPARTMENT
Amendment to the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Travelling Allowance Rules.
[G.O. Ms. No. 691, Public (Estt.I & Leg.), 11th July 2013.]
No. SRO A-21/2013.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 14 of the Tamil Nadu Payment of Salaries
Act, 1951 (Tamil Nadu Act XX of 1951), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the
Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Travelling Allowance Rules, 1955.
AMENDMENT.
In the said Rules, after rule 19-B, the following rule shall be added, namely:-
"19-C.  In the event of the death of the Member, the travelling allowances and daily allowances due till the
date of his death, shall be paid to his legal heir, after recovering the dues, if any, to the Legislative Assembly
Secretariat or to the Government".
JATINDRA NATH SWAIN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
