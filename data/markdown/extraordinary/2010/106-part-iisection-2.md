RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND PANCHAYAT RAJ DEPARTMENT
REMOVAL OF CHAIRMAN OF THIRUPPANANTHAL PANCHAYAT UNION COUNCIL, THANJAVUR DISTRICT
UNDER TAMIL NADU PANCHAYATS ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 36, Rural Development and Panchayat Raj (P.R.IV), 8th April 2010.]
No. II(2)/RDPR/200(b)/2010.
Whereas, a written notice of intention to make a motion expressing want of confidence in
Thiru G. Alayamani, Chairman, Thiruppananthal Panchayat Union Council, Thanjavur District, signed by fourteen members, out
of the sanctioned strength of seventeen members of the said Council, together with a copy of motion proposed to be made and
a written statement of charges against the said Chairman have been delivered to the Revenue Divisional Officer, Kumbakonam,
Thanjavur District in person by fourteen members, under the provision of sub-section (2) of Section 212 of the Tamil Nadu
Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994);
And, Whereas, the Revenue Divisional Officer, Kumbakonam caused a copy of the statement of charges alongwith a copy
of the notice of motion to be delivered, to the said Chairman Thiru G. Alayamani and he submitted his reply to the charges;
And, Whereas, a motion expressing want of confidence in Thiru G. Alayamani, Chairman, Thiruppananthal  Panchayat Union
Council, Thanjavur District had been carried with a support of fourteen members out of the sanctioned strength of seventeen
members of the said Panchayat Union Council in the meeting convened by the Revenue Divisional Officer, Kumbakonam, on
4th December 2009, after following the procedures specified in Section 212 of the said Act and forwarded the same to
Government;
Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (13) of Section 212 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats
Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby removes Thiru G. Alayamani, Chairman, Thiruppananthal
Panchayat Union Council, Thanjavur District, from the Office of the Chairman of the said Panchayat Union Council with
immediate effect.
 K. ASHOK VARDHAN SHETTY,
Principal Secretary to Government.
