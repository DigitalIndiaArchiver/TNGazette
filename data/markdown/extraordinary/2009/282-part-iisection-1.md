LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY SECRETARIAT
DEATH OF THIRU S.P. JAYARAMAN,  MEMBER OF THE TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY.
[Letter No. 20400/2009-1, Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Secretariat (B.III), 3rd November 2009,
Aippasi 17, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. II(1)/LAS/38(a-1)/2009.
"The Honourable Speaker has learnt with deep regret the death of Thiru S.P. Jayaraman,
a Member of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly on 2nd November 2009".
VACATION OF SEAT IN TAMIL NADU LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY.
[Letter No. 20400/2009-1, Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Secretariat (B.III), 3rd November 2009,
Aippasi 17, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. II(1)/LAS/38(a-2)/2009.
"A vacancy has occurred among the Members of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly, consequent
on the death of Thiru S.P. Jayaraman, a Member elected to the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly
from the 51. Vandavasi (S.C.) Assembly Constituency in Tiruvannamalai District".
