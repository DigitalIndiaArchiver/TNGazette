No. SRO G-10/2020.
The following Notification of the Election Commission of  India, Nirvachan Sadan, Ashoka Road,
New Delhi-110 001, dated 7th October, 2020 [15 Asvina, 1942 (Saka)] is published :-
No. 508/TN/2020:- In pursuance of the provision contained in sub-sections (1) and (2) of Section 13AA
of the Representation of the People Act, 1950 (43 of 1950), and in supersession of its Notification
No. 508/TN/2010, dated 01st October, 2010 the Election Commission, in consultation with the State Government of 
Tamil Nadu hereby:-
(1) Designates for each district/area in the State of Tamil Nadu speciﬁ ed in column (1) of the Table below the oﬃ  cer 
speciﬁ ed against it in column (2) thereof as the District Election Oﬃ  cer for the District/Area; and
(2) Speciﬁ es the area, in respect of which each such oﬃ  cer shall exercise jurisdiction, in column (3) of the Table.
