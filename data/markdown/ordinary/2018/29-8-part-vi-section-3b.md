Election of One Nominee by the Members of the Senate to the Vice-Chancellor Search Committee for 
Recommending a Panel of Names for Appointment as vice-chancellor.
[Lr. No. EI (1)/37296/2018]     
No.VI-3(b)/29/2018.     
Under Statute 7 of Chapter V of the General Statutes of  the  Tamil  Nadu  Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, 
Chennai  and  as  directed  by the Governing Council of  the Tamil Nadu  Dr.  M.G.R.  Medical  University, Chennai, 
the Registrar   of  the University  hereby notiﬁ es that a permanent vacancy is about  to  occur in  the ofﬁ ce of the 
Vice-Chancellor  with  effect  from  28-12-2018  F.N.  consequent on the expiry of the term of ofﬁ ce of the present 
Vice-Chancellor, Dr. S. GEETHALAKSHMI, M.D., Ph.D.,                                                  
Necessary action to elect one nominee by the Members of  the Senate to the Committee referred to in Section 10(2) 
of the  Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai , Act, 1987 ( Tamil Nadu Act 37 of 1987), to recommend 
a panel of  three names  to the Chancellor for making the appointment of the next Vice-Chancellor, will be taken on 
the publication of the Notiﬁ cation in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette as speciﬁ ed in the Annexure.
Necessary action to elect one nominee by the Members of  the Governing Council to the said Committee as 
provided under Statute 12(2) of Chapter V of the said Statutes will be  taken  on  the publication  of  the Notiﬁ cation 
in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
