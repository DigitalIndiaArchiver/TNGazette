Acquisition Laws (Revival of Operation, Amendment and Validation) Act, 2019.

Pallavaram Town, Survey No.3/1 part & etc., is required for Highways purpose i.e. for Construction of Grade Separator at 
the intersection of Sandhai Road and Kundrathur Road with GST Road at Pallavaram with pedestrian subway under Chennai

of compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid out of funds controlled and managed by the Chief Engineer, 
Highways Projects, Chennai-16 and after having considered that cause shown by the owners or the other persons having 
interest in the said lands as the case may do hereby publish the following notice is issued under sub - section (1) of Section 
15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34/2002). 
NOTICE

of Land Administration (FAC) hereby acquires the lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below and measuring 970.5 square meters 
to the same a little more or less are needed for the purpose of Construction of Grade Separator at the intersection of Sandhai 
Road and Kundrathur Road with GST Road at Pallavaram with pedestrian subway under Chennai Metropolitan Development 
Project Division - Ill, Chennai-32.
