The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that the land/lands speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below to be acquired 
for industrial purpose to wit to the formation of Doubling of Broad Gauge Railway Line between Madurai – Thoothukudi situated 
in Thiruparankundram Taluk and Village respectively in the District of Madurai, and it having already been decided that the 
amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid out of the funds controlled or managed by RVNL. The 
following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 
1997. (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu 
Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below measuring an extent 
of 2989 Sq. mtrs. of wet land to the same, a little more or less needed for industrial purpose wit, for the formation of Doubling 
of Broad Gauge Railway Line between Madurai - Thoothukudi work in Thiruparankundram Taluk and Village respectively in the 
District of Madurai.
 The plan of the lands are kept in the oﬃ  ce of the Collector, Madurai may be inspected on any day during oﬃ  ce hours.
