In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (1) of Section 43-B of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities
Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920), sub-section (1) of Section 6-I of the Chennai City Municipal Corporation
Act, 1919 (Tamil Nadu Act IV of 1919), sub-section (1) of 10-D of the Madurai City Municipal Corporation Act, 1971
(Tamil Nadu Act XV of 1971), sub-section (1) of Section 10-D of the Coimbatore City Municipal Corporation Act, 1981
(Tamil Nadu Act 25 of 1981) read with Section 8 of the Erode City Municipal Corporation Act, 2008
(Tamil Nadu Act 8 of 2008)  and rule 22 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities, Municipalities
and Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission, for the purpose of filling up of
casual vacancies in the offices of the Ward Members / Councillors of Town Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities,
Municipalities and Corporations specified in the Table annexed to this Notification, in Consultation with the State
Government, hereby calls upon the electors of the said wards to elect their respective Ward Members/Councillors, in
accordance with the following programme of election:—

PANCHAYATS—COUNCILLORS / MEMBERS—CONDUCT OF—USE OF ELECTRONIC VOTING MACHINES—ORDERED.]
(S.O. No. 103/2010/TNSEC/ME-2.)
No. VI(2)/577(a-2) /2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 43-D of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act
V of 1920), Section 59-A of the Chennai City Municipal Corporation Act, 1919 (Tamil Nadu Act IV of 1919), Section 66-A of
Madurai City Municipal Corporation Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act XV of 1971), Section 68-A of the Coimbatore City Municipal
Corporation Act, 1981 (Tamil Nadu Act 25 of 1981) read with Section 8 of the Erode City Municipal Corporation Act, 2008 (Tamil
Nadu Act 8 of 2008) the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission hereby specifies that in the Casual Elections to fill up certain
casual vacancies in the offices of the Members / Councillors of the Town Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities, Municipalities
and Corporations notified in the S.O. No. 71/2010/TNSEC/ME1, dated 16th December 2010, the giving and recording of votes
by electronic voting machine shall be adopted.
