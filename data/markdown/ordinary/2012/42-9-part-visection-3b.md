UNIVERSITY AUTHORITIES
UNIVERSITY OF MADRAS
Member Elected to the Syndicate by the Academic Council from among the Teachers of Affiliated Colleges.
(No. V.1/College/9-2012/514.)
No. VI-3(b)/24/2012.
It is hereby notified that Dr. K. Sambathkumar, M.A., M.Phil., Ph.D., Associate Professor and Head, Department of
Philosophy, Pachaiyappa's College, Chetpet, Chennai-600 030 has been unanimously elected to the Syndicate by the
Academic Council from among the Teachers of Affiliated Colleges  under Section 18[a]-Other Members [2], of the Madras
University Act, 1923, for the residual period from 07-09-2012 to 08-10-2013 or till the date of cessation of his membership
in the Academic council whichever is earlier.
Members Elected to the Senate by the Academic Council
(No. V.1/College/9-2012/514.)
No. VI-3(b)/25/2012.
It is hereby notified that the following members have been unanimously elected to the Senate by the Academic Council
from among its own body under Section 14[a]- Other Members [2] of the Madras University Act, 1923 for the residual
period (i.e., from 31-08-2012 to 28-09-2013) or till  the date of cessation of their membership in the Academic Council
whichever is earlier
(1) Dr. G. Ravindran, M.Com., M.Phil., Ph.D., Associate Professor of Commerce, S.I.V.E.T. College, Velachery Main
Road, Gowrivakkam, Chennai-600 073
(2) Dr. S. Thirunavukkarasu, Associate Professor and HOD of Economics, Ramakrishna Mission Vivekananda College,
(autonomous), No. 72, 70 Sir P.S. Sivaswami Salai, Mylapore, Chennai-600 004.
Members Elected to the Standing Committee of the Academic Council by the Academic Council
(Members of the Syndicate)
(No. V.1/College/9-2012/514.)
No. VI-3(b)/26/2012.
It is hereby notified Dr. S. Thirumagan, M.Sc., M.Phil., MBA., M.A., Ph.D. (Tamil), D.FOL., PGDBA., Principal,
K.C.S. Kasi Nadar College of Arts and Science, Chennai and Member of the Syndicate has been unanimously elected
to the Standing Committee of the Academic Council by the Academic Council as per the Section 24[k] and Statute 2- Regulation,
Constitution-Chapter XVI - Standing Committee of the Academic Council, Constitution, Powers and Duties of Madras
University Act, 1923 for the Residual Period (i.e. from 02-09-2012 to 10-01-2014) or till the date of cessation of his
membership in the Academic Council/Syndicate whichever is earlier.
Members Elected to the Standing Committee of the Academic Council by the Academic Council
(Principals or Teachers of Affiliated Colleges)
(No. V.1/College/9-2012/514.)
No. VI-3(b)/27/2012.
It is hereby notified Dr. S. Murugesan, M.A., M.Phil., B.Ed., Ph.D., Assistant Professor of Tamil, Loganatha Narayanaswamy
Govt. College (Autonomous), Ponneri, Thiruvallur District has been unanimously elected to the Standing Committee of the
Academic Council by the Academic Council as per the Section 24[k] and Statute 2-Regulation, Constitution-Chapter XVI-
Standing Committee of the Academic Council, Constitution, Powers and Duties of Madras University Act, 1923 for the
Residual Period (i.e. from 31-08-2012 to 25-09-2013) or till the date of cessation of his membership in the Academic
Council whichever is earlier.
Chennai-600 005,
DR. G. KOTESWARA PRASAD,
17th October 2012.
  Registrar In-charge.
