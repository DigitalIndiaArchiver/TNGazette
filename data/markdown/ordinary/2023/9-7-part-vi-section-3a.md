GENERAL NOTIFICATION
TAMIL NADU WAQF BOARD, CHENNAI-600 001.
Framing of Bye Law in respect of Tanjore Road, New Mosque Waqf, Trichy District.
(G.S.No. 823/Try).
(Lr. No. 7123/03/B5/TRY)
No. VI-3(a)/12/2023.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 69 of the Waqf Act 1995, the Tamil Nadu Waqf Board 
in its Resolution in Item No. 325/22 in Rc.No. 7123/03/B5/TRY dated: 09-01-2023 has approved the Amended BYE LAW 
for the Waqf in respect of TANJORE ROAD, NEW MOSQUE WAQF, TRICHY DISTRICT for better administration of the Waqf.
