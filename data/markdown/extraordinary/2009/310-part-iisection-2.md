CONSTITUTION OF SUB COURT AT PERUNDURAI IN ERODE DISTRICT
UNDER THE TAMIL NADU CIVIL COURTS ACT.
NOTIFICATION - I
[G.O. Ms. No. 1019, Home (Courts-III), 1st December 2009.]
No. II(2)/HO/625(d-1)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 4 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu, after consultation with the High Court, Madras, hereby fixes the number of Subordinate Judges
to be appointed to the Subordinate Judge’s Court, Perundurai in Erode District as one with effect from the date on which
the Subordinate Judge, Perundurai assumes charge of that Court.
NOTIFICATION - II
[G.O. Ms. No. 1019, Home (Courts-III), 1st December 2009.]
No. II(2)/HO/625(d-2)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby fixes Perundurai as the place at which the Subordinate Judge’s Court, Perundurai
shall be held.
NOTIFICATION - III
[G.O. Ms. No. 1019, Home (Courts-III), 1st December 2009.]
No. II(2)/HO/625(d-3)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 10 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that the Subordinate Judge's Court, Erode shall cease to have original jurisdiction
over the District Munsif Court, Perundurai, and that the Subordinate Judge’s Court, Perundurai  shall have and exercise original
jurisdiction over the District Munsif Court, Perundurai with effect from the date on which the Subordinate Judge, Perundurai
assumes charge of that Court.
D. JOTHI JAGARAJAN,
Secretary to Government (In-charge).
