No. SRO B-43/2013.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso of Article 309 of the constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Registration Service
(Section 25 in Volume II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1969), as subsequently amended.
2. (i) Amendment (1) hereby made shall be deemed to come into force with effect on and from the 29th March 1996.
(ii) Amendment (2) hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force with effect on and from the 21st May 1996.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules, in rule 7, in sub-rules (b) and (c),—
(1) for the expression “Foundational Training Course - B for a period of three weeks”, the expression “Foundational
Training Course - A for a period of eight weeks” shall be substituted;
(2)  for the expression “eight weeks” as so amended, the expression “five weeks” shall be substituted.

FINANCE DEPARTMENT
 Amendment to the General Provident Fund (Tamil Nadu) Rules
[G.O. No. 412, Finance (Allowances), 17th October 2013, Purattasi 31,, Vijaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2044.]
No. SRO B-44/2013.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the constitution of India and of all
other powers hereunto enabling, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to The General Provident
Fund (Tamil Nadu) Rules:—
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, in rule 30, after clause (ii), the following clause shall be added, namely:—
“(iii) When a person eligible, by means of nomination or otherwise as provided in clauses (i) or (ii), for a share of the amount
standing to the credit of the subscriber dies before receiving the amount, such amount shall be payable to the remaining eligible
persons in equal share.”
K. SHANMUGAM
Principal Secretary to Government.
