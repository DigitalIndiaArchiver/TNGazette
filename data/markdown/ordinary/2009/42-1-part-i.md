No.I/PUSP/21/2009.—The following Notification of the
Government of India, Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances
and Pensions, Department of Personnel and Training, New
Delhi, dated 15th September 2009 is republished:—
“In exercise of the powers conferred by Rule 6(1) of the
Indian Administrative Service (Cadre) Rules, 1954 and with
the concurrence of the Governments of Tamil Nadu and
Bihar, the Central Government hereby accords approval for
inter cadre deputation  of Shri Dev Raj Dev, IAS (TN:99) from
IAS cadre of Tamil Nadu to the IAS cadre of Bihar for a period
of three years with effect from the date of his joining the
Bihar Government.”
[No.13017/5/2009 AIS(I).]
