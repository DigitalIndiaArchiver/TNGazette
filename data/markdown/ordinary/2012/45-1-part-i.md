No. I/PUSP/38/2012.—The following Notification of the Government
of India, Department of Personnel and Training New Delhi, dated
24th September 2012, is republished:—
In exercise of the powers conferred by  sub-rule (2) of Rule 5 of
the Indian Administrative Service (Cadre) Rules, 1954, and with the
concurrence of the Governments of Tamil Nadu and Uttarakhand, the
Central Government hereby transfers Ms. Ranjana, I.A.S. (TN: 10)
from Tamil Nadu cadre to Uttarakhand cadre on the ground of marriage
to Shri Ram Chandra Rajaguru, I.F.S. (UK: 09).
