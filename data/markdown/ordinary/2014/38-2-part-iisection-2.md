No. II(2)/HE/587/2014.—In exercise of the powers conferred
under Section 24(b) - Class II - Other Members (3) of the

Tiruchirappalli-620 024.
2.
Dr. K. Kumaraswamy,
Professor,
Department of Geography,
Bharathidasan University,
Tiruchirappalli-620 024.
