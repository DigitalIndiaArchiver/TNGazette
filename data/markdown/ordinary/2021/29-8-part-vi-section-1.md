Tamil Nadu Land Acquisition Laws(Revival of Operation, Amendment and Validation) Act, 2019

of provision of Project facilities such as construction of Junction Improvement and Bus Bay to Chennai Outer Ring 
Road Phase-II at 112. Attanthangal Village and having already been decided that the entire amount of compensation

Constitution of District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court at Kilvelur in Nagapattinam District.
[Roc. No. 1347/A/2014/G/Judn. (Phase-II)]
No.VI(1)/201/2021.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 
(Central Act III of 1873), the High Court, Madras, hereby fixes Kilvelur in Nagapattinam District as the place at 
which the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Kilvelur, shall be located. 
NOTIFICATION-II
[Roc. No. 1347/A/2014/G/Judn. (Phase-II)]
No.VI(1)/202/2021.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 11 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 
(Central Act III of 1873), the High Court, Madras, hereby directs that in Nagapattinam District, the District Munsif 
Court, Nagapattinam, shall cease to have local jurisdiction over the entire Kilvelur Taluk and the District Munsif-cum-
Judicial Magistrate Court, Kilvelur shall have and exercise local Jurisdiction over the entire Taluk of Kilvelur with 
effect from the date on which the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate, Kilvelur assumes charge of that Court.

NOTIFICATION-III
[Roc. No. 1347/A/2014/G/Judn. (Phase-II)]
No.VI(1)/203/2021.
 The High Court, Madras hereby directs and notifies that the District Munsif-cum-Judicial magistrate, Kilvelur 
in Nagapattinam District shall exercise all the powers conferred on a District Munsif under Section 12 of the 
Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873) as amended up-to-date in regard to original suits and 
proceedings not otherwise exempted from his/her cognizance of which the amount or value of the subject matter 
does not exceed One Lakh rupees within the local limits of his/his jurisdiction with effect from the date on which 
the District Munsif-cum-Judicial magistrate, Kilvelue assumes charge of that Court.
NOTIFICATION-IV
[Roc. No. 1347/A/2014/G/Judn. (Phase-II)]
No.VI(1)/204/2021.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 28 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 
(Central Act III of 1873) the High Court, Madras, hereby directs that the District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate, Kilvelur, 
shall have and exercise jurisdiction of a Court of Small Causes under the Provincial Small Cause Courts Act, 1887 for 
the trial of suits cognizable by a Court of Small Causes up to the pecuniary limits of Rs.5000/- (Rupees five thousand 
only) with effect from the date on which the District Munsif-cum- Judicial Magistrate Court, Kilvelur, assumes charge 
of that Court.

(Roc. No. 2106/2015/G/Judn.)
No.VI(1)/205/2021.
