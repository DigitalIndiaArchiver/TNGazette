The Government in appreciation of her bravery is
awarding the вҖңAnna Medal for GallantryвҖқ for the year 2009.
вҖңJEEVAN RAKSHA PADAKвҖқ
Thiru R. Radhakrishnan, son of Thiru Raju,
Pudugraman, Tuticorin District
(Now working as Inspector of Police, Perambur PS,
Mayiladuthurai)
On 28th January 2007 at 12.30 p.m., when Thiru
R. Radhakrishnan, Inspector of Police went along with his
police party in search of prohibition offenders on the banks of
Cauvery, he heard the hue and cry  of the public that a small
child was being dragged away in the Cauvery water. At that
time, he was standing at a height of 25 feet from the water
level and he noticed that a 3 year old female child was
struggling in the water with no one to rescue her. He jumped
into the water immediately not minding the depth of the river,
rescued the drowning child and saved her life.
The Government of India in appreciation of his bravery,
awarded him the вҖңJeevan Raksha Padak AwardвҖқ
