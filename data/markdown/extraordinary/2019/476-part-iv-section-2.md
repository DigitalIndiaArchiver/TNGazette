          TAMIL NADU ORDINANCE NO.11 OF 2019.

1.	
(1) 	 This Ordinance may be called the  Tamil Nadu Dr.Ambedkar 
Law University (Amendment) Ordinance, 2019.

 EXPLANATORY STATEMENT
     The Syndicate of the Tamil Nadu Dr.Ambedkar Law University in its 158th meeting and 163rd meeting 
held on 31.10.2018 and 13.09.2019, respectively, taking into consideration the qualification prescribed for 
the post of Registrar by the University Grants Commission and the suggestion of the State Government 
thereon, has resolved to send proposal to the Government to amend the qualification prescribed for the post 
of Registrar of the University. The Registrar(i/c) of the University has forwarded the minutes of the 163rd  
Syndicate meeting and has requested to amend the Tamil Nadu Dr.Ambedkar Law University Act, 1996 
(Tamil Nadu Act 43 of 1997)  for the purpose. The Government, after careful consideration, have decided to 
revise the qualification for the post of Registrar of the Tamil Nadu Dr.Ambedkar Law University and to amend 
section 15 of the said Tamil Nadu Act 43 of 1997 suitably for the purpose.
	
2. The Ordinance seeks to give effect to the above decision.
