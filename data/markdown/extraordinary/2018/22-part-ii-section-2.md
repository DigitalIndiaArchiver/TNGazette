In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (i) of sub-section (1) of section 67 of the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988 
(Central Act 59 of 1988) and in supersession of the Home Department Notification No. II (2)/HO/455(b-4)/ 2011 published at 
page 5 of Part II Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary No.431, dated 17th November 2011, the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby issues the following direction to the State Transport Authority, Tamil Nadu and the Regional 
Transport Authorities regarding the fixation of fares for stage carriages plying as Ordinary Services in the State of Tamil Nadu. 
2. The notification hereby issued shall come into force on the 20th day of January 2018.
 
DIRECTION   
The fares for the stage carriages plying as “Ordinary Services” on routes / areas in the State of Tamil Nadu shall  not 
exceed the fares calculated at rates given below :-
1.	 (i)  60 paise per kilometer on roads in the plains;	
	
	
Provided that for a minimum distance of 10 kilometers a fare not exceeding Rs.6.00/- shall be charged.
	
(ii) Basic fare of plains + 20% shall be charged from passengers in the stage carriages plying in Ghats Roads.
   		
Provided that for a minimum distance of 6 kilometers a fare not exceeding Rs.6./- shall be charged.
2. Applicable Goods and Services Tax on bus fare shall be collected as and when orders are passed by Central / State 
Government.  
3. In respect of special services operated on occasions like fairs, festivals, weekend etc., Special fare shall be charged 
not exceeding one and a half times the normal fare of the type of services operated.
4. The fares shall be rounded off to the next higher multiple of one rupee.
Explanation-I: “Ghat Road” means a road notified in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette as such by the Government 
under the Tamil Nadu Traffic Rules, 1938 and indicated in a notice board erected at each end of such road.
Explanation-II:  The fares determined in accordance with the rates specified in paragraph 1(i) and (ii) above shall be 
rounded off to the next higher multiple of one rupee.

No. II(2)/HO/79(d-5)/2018.  
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (i) of sub-section (1) of Section 67 of the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988 
(Central Act 59 of 1988) and in supersession of the Home Department Notification No. II (2)/HO/455(b-5)/2011, published at 
pages 5-6 of Part II Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary No.431, dated 17th November 2011 the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby issues the following direction to the State Transport Authority, Tamil Nadu and the Regional 
Transport Authorities regarding the fixation of fares for stage carriages plying as Express Services in the State of Tamil Nadu. 
2. The notification hereby issued shall come into force on the 20th day of January 2018.
DIRECTION   
The fares for the stage carriages plying as “Express Services” on routes / areas in the State of Tamil Nadu shall  not 
exceed the fares calculated at the rates given below :-  
1. Express – Semi deluxe
  (a)  80 paise per kilometre on roads in the plain;
	
	
Provided that for a minimum distance of 15 kilometres a fare not exceeding Rs.12/- shall be charged.

(b) Basic fare of plain + 20% shall be charged from passengers in the stage carriages plying as semi deluxe on ghat roads;
	
Provided that for a minimum distance of 15 kilometres a fare not exceeding Rs.15/- (Basic fare of plain + 20%)  shall 
be charged.
2.  Express Super deluxe
	
(a)  90 paise per kilometre shall be charged if provision is made for a superior class of accommodation for passengers 
in the stage carriages plying as super deluxe on plain roads;
	
	
Provided that for a minimum distance of 30 kilometres a fare not exceeding Rs.27/- shall be charged.
	
(b) Basic fare of plain + 20% shall be charged from passengers in the stage carriages plying as super deluxe on ghat 
roads;
	
	
Provided that for a minimum distance of 30 kilometres a fare not exceeding Rs.33 (Basic fare of plain + 20% on 
ghat roads) shall be charged.
3.  Air suspension Coaches (Ultra Deluxe)
	
(a) 110 paise per kilometre shall be charged if provision is made for a superior class of accommodation for passengers 
in the stage carriages plying as Air suspension Coaches (Ultra Deluxe) on plain roads.
	
	
Provided that for a minimum distance of 30 kilometers a fare not exceeding Rs.33  shall be charged.
	
(b) Basic fare of plain + 20% shall be charged from passengers in the stage carriages plying as Ultra deluxe on ghat 
roads;
	
	
Provided that for a minimum distance of 30 kilometres a fare not exceeding Rs.40 (Basic fare of plain + 20% on 
ghat roads) shall be charged.
4.  Air-conditioned  Coaches 
	
140 paise per kilometre shall be charged if provision is made for a superior class of accommodation for passengers 
in the stage carriages plying as A/C Coaches on plain roads.
	
Provided that for a minimum distance of 30 kilometers a fare not exceeding Rs.42/- shall be charged.
5.  Air-conditioned  Volvo Coaches 
	
170 paise per kilometre shall be charged if provision is made for a superior class of accommodation for passengers 
in the stage carriages plying as Volvo type Coaches  on plain roads.
	
Provided that for a minimum distance of 30 kilometers a fare not exceeding Rs.51/- shall be charged.
Explanation: “Ghat Road” means a road notified in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette as such by the Government 
under the Tamil Nadu Traffic Rules, 1938 and indicated in a notice board erected at each end of such road.
6.  In respect of special services operated on occasions like fairs, festivals, weekend etc., Special fare shall be charged 
not exceeding one and a half times of the normal fare of the type of services operated.
7.  The fare determined in accordance with the rates specified in paragraph 1, 2, 3, 4 and 5 above shall be rounded off 
to the next higher multiple of one rupee.   
8. The reservation charges of Rs.5/- shall be charged for operation of Express Services of all categories as is in vogue. 
9.  Applicable Goods and Services Tax on bus fare shall be collected as and when orders are passed by Central / State 
Government.
