ORDER: No. 609, Revenue and Disaster Management 31st October 2020.
In G.O.Ms.No.324, Revenue and Disaster Management (DM II), Department, Dated : 30-06-2020 it has been ordered 
under caption I that operation of both private and Government buses within the districts will remain suspended from 
01-07-2020 to 15.07-2020 throughout the state and that the interstate public transport will continue to remain prohibited until 
further orders during the lockdown period throughout the state till 31-07-2020.
2. In G.O.Ms.396, Revenue and Disaster Management DM II, dated: 31-07-2020 under caption I(XI). It has been ordered 
that interstate private and public bus transport and intra state private and public bus transport will continue to remain prohibited 
until further orders though out state till 31-08-2020.
3. In G.O.Ms.No.447, Revenue and Disaster Management DM II, dated: 31-08-2020 under caption II(III) it has been stated 
that operation of both public and private bus transport within the district and metropolitan bus transport in Greater Chennai 
shall be permitted from 01-09-2020 following the Standard Operating Procedure (SOP).
4. In G.O. Ms.No.462, Revenue and Disaster Management (DM IV(1)), Department, dated 04.09-2020 the following 
amendment has been issued for the contents in section III under caption 2 permitted activities mentioned in G.O.Ms.No.447, 
Revenue and Disaster Management (DM IV(1)), Department, dated 31-08-2020.
 
III. Operation of both public and private bus transport within the districts and metropolitan bus services in Greater 
 
Chennai has been permitted from 01-09-2020 and operation of both public and private bus transport throughout 
 
the state shall be permitted from 07-09-2020 following the Standard Operating Procedure (SOP).
5. The Hon’ble Chief Minister of Puducherry by citing the following reasons has requested to resume the Inter-state Bus 
Transport between Tamil Nadu and Puducherry considering the fact that Puducherry has unique geographical location with 
Tamil Nadu. 
i.  
JIPMER, IGGMC&H and six more private medical colleges are existing in Puducherry and JIPMER in Karaikal is 
also serving general public of Puducherry and as well as most parts of neighbouring districts of Tamil Nadu, in the 
health sector. Patients from Tamil Nadu will get more beneﬁ t if the public transportation is allowed.
ii.  Most of the farmers from neighbouring places of Tamil Nadu are selling their agricultural products in Puducherry 
and Karaikal regions.
iii.  Small traders like Street Vendors and other traders are travelling regularly between the parts of Tamil Nadu and 
Puducherry
iv.  Many industries and business establishments are located in Puducherry and Karaikai regions. People from various 
parts of Tamil Nadu are working these areas are travelling regularly to Puducherry for their employment. Also many 
people from Tamil Nadu are coming to Puducherry to work in the unorganized sector like construction, services, etc.
v.  People from neighbouring areas of Tamil Nadu are visiting Puducherry for purchase of goods like electrical products, 
plumbing Items, home appliances, dress materials, etc.,
6. In this connection it is submitted that as per the guidelines issued by the Government of India, necessary permission 
was already accorded by the Government of Tamil Nadu for the goods traﬃ  c, operation of railways for transportation of 
goods and parcels, operation of airports/seaports/landports and inland container depots for cargo movement, movement of 
all trucks and other goods / carrier vehicles without any restriction vide G.O.Ms.No.193, Revenue and Disaster Management 
Department, dated 15-4-2020. Further, as already stated above operation of both Public and Private Bus Transport throughout 
the State has been permitted by the Government from 07-09-2020 after following the Standard Operating Procedures and 
that the Inter State/Intra State Rail Transport in the authorised routes has also been permitted. But so for Interstate bus 
transport not permitted.

7. The Additional Chief Secretary/Commissioner of Revenue Administration has therefore stated that considering the 
request of the Hon’ble Chief Minister of Puducherry and taking into account the fact that Tamil Nadu has unique geographical 
connection with Puducherry State, suitable orders may be passed for the operation of both Private and Public Bus Transport 
between Tamil Nadu and Puducherry.
8. The Government after careful consideration, on considering the request of the Hon’ble Chief Minister of Puducherry 
and taking into account the fact that Tamil Nadu has unique geographical connection with Puducherry State, hereby permit the 
operation of both Private and Public Bus Transport between Tamil Nadu and Puducherry without E-registration and accordingly 
amend the GO.Ms.No.541, Revenue and Disaster Management (DM-IV) Department, dated 30-09-2020 as follows:
Under Caption II – (Under Permitted Activities) the following Section II-A  shall be added:
  II-A. Operation of both Private and Public Bus Transport between Tamil Nadu and Puducherry shall be permitted without 
E-registration" with immediate eﬀ ect.
