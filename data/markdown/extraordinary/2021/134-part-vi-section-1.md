The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that the lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below are acquired under 
Tamil Nadu Road Sector Project II for Highways purpose for Widening of SH-222, Omalur – Mecheri Road at Chinnasathapadi 
and Periyasathapadi villages in Mettur Taluk, Salem District and it having already been decided that the entire amount of 
compensation to be awarded for the lands to be paid out of the funds controlled and managed by the Divisional Engineer(H), 
Salem (TNRFSP II), Tamil Nadu after having considering the cause shown by the owner or other persons having interest on 
such lands as the case may be do hereby publish the following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of section 15 of the 
Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (TN Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
 
Under sub-section (1) of section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) the Governor 
of Tamil Nadu hereby notify that the Dry lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below and measuring to an extent of 3821 sq.mts. 
of Dry lands are acquired in Chinnasathapadi and Periyasathapadi in Mettur Taluk, Salem District to the same, a little more 
or less needed for Tamil Nadu Road Sector Project II for State Highways purpose for Widening of SH-222, Omalur – Mecheri 
Road.
The plan of the lands under acquisition are kept in the oﬃ  ce of the Special District Revenue oﬃ  cer (Land Acquisition), 
Tamil Nadu Road Sector Project II, Salem and oﬃ  ce of the Special Tahsildar (Land Acquisition), Tamil Nadu Road Sector 
Project II in Salem, may be inspected at any time during the oﬃ  ce hours.
