GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
Final Closing and Cancellation of Registration of IND. No. 1299. Arignar Anna Small Match
Producers Service Industrial Co-operative Society Ltd.
(L.F. 718/ICA/1988)
No. VI(1)/180/2015.
In the circumstances stated by the Assistant Director of Industries and Commerce (Industrial Co-operatives) District
Industries Centre, Coimbatore / Official Liquidator of the Arignar Anna Small Match Producers Service Industrial Co-
operative Society Ltd. Ind. No. 1299 in the final closure proposals dated 1-04-2015 and in exercise of the powers
delegated under Section 140(1) of Tamil  Nadu Co-operative Societies Act, 1983, the Registration of Arignar Anna
Small Match Producers Service Industrial Co-operative Society Ltd. Ind. No. 1299 has been ordered to be cancelled
and the affairs have been finally closed with effect from 23-06-2015 vide the Proceedings No. LF. 718/ICA/1988,
dated 23-06-2015 of Additional Commissioner of Industries and Commerce and Registrar of Industrial Co-operatives,
Chennai-32.

Variations to the Approved Master Plan for the Coimbatore Local Planning Area.
(Roc. No. 922/2014/LPA-3)
No. VI(1)/182/2015.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 32 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country
Planning Act 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972) and in exercise of powers conferred by the G.O. Ms. No. 94,
Housing and Urban Development [UD4(1)] Department, dated 12-06-2009 which has been published in the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No. 27 Part II—Section 2, Page No. 228, dated 15-07-2009 the following variations
are made to the Master Plan for the Coimbatore Local Planning Area approved under the said Act and published in
the Housing and Urban Development Department Notification No.II(2)/HOU/4377/94 at Page 1078 of
Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 9th November 1994.
VARIATIONS
In the said Master Plan, in the “LAND USE SCHEDULE” under the heading "COIMBATORE LOCAL PLANNING
AREA (OTHER THAN CORPORATION AREA)" under the sub-heading (b).
(i) Against the entry “INDUSTRIAL (I-8)” for the expression “103/1A, 1B, 1C, 1D, 2A, 2B, 2C,
3A, 4A, 5 (Except 103/3B, 4B) and 104/1A, 2, 3, 4A, 4B, 4C1 (except 104/1B, 4C2, 4C3, 4C4, 4D1, 4D2) shall be
substituted.
(ii) Against the entry and Agricultural Dry (AG-8) for the expression 105/1A, 1B, 2, 3, (except 105/4A, 4B),
106/1A, 1B, 2, 3A, 3B1 (except 106/3B2, 3C), 107/9, 10, 11A (except 107/1, 2A, 3A, 4A, 5A, 3B, 4B 5B1, 5B2,
6, 7, 8, 11B) shall be substituted.
(iii)  Against the entry “RESIDENTIAL (MR-5)” the expression 103/3B, 4B, 104/1B, 4C2, 4C3, 4C4, 4D1, 4D2,
105/4A, 4B, 106/3B2, 3C, 107/1, 2A, 3A, 4A, 5A, 3B, 4B, 5B1, 5B2, 6, 7, 8, 11B shall be added after the
entry 42 to 58, 60.
Coimbatore,
