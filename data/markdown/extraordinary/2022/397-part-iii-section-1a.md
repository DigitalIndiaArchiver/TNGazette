In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 38 read with Section 70 of the Tamil Nadu Slum Areas 
(Improvement and Clearance) Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 11 of 1971), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following 
amendments to the Tamil Nadu Urban Habitat Development Board Engineering Oﬃ  cers Service Rules, 1972:—

In the said Rules, – (1) in rule 3, in the TABLE, in column (2), against the entry "Category 1 Chief Engineer" in column 
(1), for item (ii), the following item shall be substituted, namely:-
"(ii) By appointment of a Chief Engineer in the Tamil Nadu Engineering Service or  in the Tamil Nadu Highways 
Engineering Service or in the service of a local or an autonomous body on foreign service terms"; 
 
(2) In rule 7, in the TABLE , in column (2), against the entry "Category-1 Chief Engineer" in column (1), for item 2, the 
following item shall be substituted, namely:-
"2. A minimum period of 2 years of service as Superintending Engineer.".
