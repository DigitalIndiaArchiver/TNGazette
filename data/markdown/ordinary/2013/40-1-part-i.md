No. I/PUSP/25/2013.—The following Notification of the
Government of India, Ministry of Law and Justice
(Department of Justice) Jaisalmer House, 26, Man Singh
Road, New Delhi-110 011, dated 18th September, 2013, is
republished:—
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (1) of Article
222 of the Constitution of India, the President, after consultation
with the Chief Justice of India, is pleased to transfer Shri
Justice Satish Kumar Agnihotri, Judge of the Chhattisgarh
High Court, as a Judge of the Madras High Court and to
direct him to assume charge of his office in the  Madras
High Court  on or before 3rd October, 2013.

PRAVEEN GARG,
