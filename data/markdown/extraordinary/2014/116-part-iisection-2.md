ENERGY DEPARTMENT
APPOINTMENT OF CERTAIN PERSON AS CHAIRMAN IN THE TAMIL NADU ELECTRICITY REGULATORY
COMMISSION UNDER THE ELECTRICITY ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 41, Energy (D1), 6th June 2014.]
No. II(2)/EGY/347(b)/2014.
Under sub-section (5) of Section 82 of the Electricity Act, 2003 (Central Act 36 of 2003) and
on the recommendation of the selection committee referred to in Section 85 of the said Act, the Governor
of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints Thiru S. Akshaya Kumar as Chairman, Tamil Nadu Electricity Regulatory
Commission, Chennai.
RAJESH LAKHONI,
Secretary to Government.
