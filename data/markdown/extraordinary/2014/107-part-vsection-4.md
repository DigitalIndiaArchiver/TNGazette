NOTIFICATIONS BY THE ELECTION COMMISSION OF INDIA
APPOINTMENT OF ADDITIONAL ASSISTANT RETURNING OFFICERS FOR THE PARLIAMENTARY
CONSTITUENCIES IN THE STATE OF TAMIL NADU.
No. SRO G-22/2014.
The following Notification of the Election Commission of India, Nirvachan Sadan, Ashoka Road,
New Delhi-110 001,  dated  15th May, 2014 [25 Vaisakha, 1936 (Saka)] is republished:—
No.434/TN-HP/2014(3).—In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 22 of the Representation of
the People Act, 1951 (1951 of 43), the Election Commission hereby appoints the officer of the Government specified in
column (2) of the Table given below, as Additional Assistant Returning Officers to assist the Returning Officer of the
Parliamentary Constituencies and Assembly Constituency specified in Column (1), in the performance of his/her functions with
respect of counting of votes in General Election to the House of the People and 28-Alandur Assembly Constituency in Tamil Nadu
State to be held on 16th May 2014 as:—
