The Governor of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that
the lands specified in the schedule below have to be acquired
for Industrial purpose to wit to formation of Industries  and
it having already been decided that the entire amount of
compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid out
of the lands controlled or managed by Tamil Nadu Electricity
Board the following notice is issued under sub-section (1)
of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial
Purposes Act, 1997.
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby acquires the lands specified in the Schedule below
and measuring  54.09.0 hectare to the same, a little more
or less needed for industrial purposes, to wit for formation
of Industries.
The plan of the lands are kept in the office of the
Collector, Thoothukudi and may be inspected at any time
during office hours.
