TAMIL NADU STATE ELECTION COMMISSION, CHENNAI
[ELECTIONS—CONDUCT OF ELECTIONS TO PANCHAYATS—APPOINTMENT OF
DISTRICT ELECTION OFFICER—NOTIFICATION ISSUED.]
(S.O. No. 13/2011/TNSEC/PE-I.)
No. VI(2)/418(b)/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-rule (2) of rule 6 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats
(Elections) Rules, 1995, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission hereby appoints the District
Collectors excepting the Collector of Chennai as the District Election Officers for their respective
Districts. Subject to the superintendence, direction and control of the Tamil Nadu State Election
Commission, they shall co-ordinate and supervise the preparation and publication of electoral
rolls of Panchayats and the conduct of elections to the offices of members of Village Panchayats,
Panchayat Union Councils and the District Panchayats, Presidents and Vice-Presidents of Village
Panchayats, Chairpersons and Vice-Chairpersons of Panchayat Union Councils and District
Panchayats in their respective Districts. They shall also perform such other functions as are
entrusted to them by the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission from time to time.
(By Order of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission)
