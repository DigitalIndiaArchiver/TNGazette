HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE DEPARTMENT
Vesting of Properties and Scheme for administration
relating  to Professor Dr. K.V. Thiruvengadam
Endowment Oration tenable by the Madras Medical
College, Chennai under Charitable Endowments Act.
[G.O. Ms. No. 421, Health and Family Welfare (E-1),
17th December 2008,
Margazhi 2, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2039.]
No. II(2)/HF/231/2009.—In the matter of the Charitable
Endowments Act, 1890 (Central Act VI of 1890) and in the
matter of Endowment Fund for "Prof. Dr. K.V. Thiruvengadam,
Endowment Oration" to invite a distinguished Medical
Specialist/Researcher in any clinical discipline of Medicine
to deliver a lecture on a topic of his interest or specialization
at Madras Medical College, Chennai-3 during October of
every year preferably on 11th October being the birthday of
Prof. Dr. K.V. Thiruvengadam.
I. VESTING OF PROPERTY
Whereas an application has been made to the Government
of Tamil Nadu under clause (b) of sub-section (i) of
Section 6 of the Charitable Endowments Act, 1890 (Central
Act VI of 1890) by the Students and Secretary, Prof.
Dr. K.V. Thiruvengadam Endowment Committee, Chennai the
Donor of the Endowment Fund specified above for vesting
Rs. 2,00,000 (Rupees Two lakhs only) in the Treasurer of
Charitable Endowments appointed under the said Act for the
State of Tamil Nadu. The Governor of Tamil Nadu, in exercise
of the powers conferred by Section 4 of the said Act, hereby
directs that the said amount be vested in the Treasurer
aforesaid and be held by him and his successors-in office,
subject to the provisions of the said Act, and to any rules,
which, from time to time, may be or shall deemed to have
been made thereunder, upon trust for the purpose and the
subject to the conditions set-forth in the Scheme settled
under Section 5 of the said Act for the administration of the
said Fund.
II. SCHEME FOR ADMINISTRATION
Whereas an application has been made to the Government
of Tamil Nadu under clause (b) of sub-section (i) of
Section 6 of the Charitable Endowments Act, 1890 (Central
Act, VI of 1890) by the Students and Secretary of the Prof.
Dr.K.V. Thiruvengadam Endowment Committee, Chennai, the
donor of the Endowment Fund specified above, for settling

the Scheme for the administration of the said Fund. The
Governor of Tamil Nadu in exercise of the powers conferred
by Section 5 of the said Act, hereby settles the Scheme

SCHEDULE

amount furnished by the donor, Students and Secretary Prof.
Dr. K.V. Thiruvengadam Endowment Committee, Chennai
with the Treasure of Charitable Endowments, Government of
Tamil Nadu, Chennai who will invest or reinvest the amount
and proceeds in Government securities to the best interest
of the Funds.
4. A copy of the delivered lecture may be kept in the
Madras Medical College, Chennai library for the benefit of
the students.
5. All the interest accrued on the Fund to be used for
travel expenses a citation and memento to the lecturer and
for conducting the lecture.
6. The heirs of Prof. Dr. K.V. Thiruvengadam, shall be
invited for the oration every year.

Ad-Interim order attaching of Properties due to default in
return of Deposits by Balaji Nidhi Niruvanam,
Kuruppanaickenpalayam, Bhavani Taluk, Erode District
under the Act.
[G.O. Ms. No. 214, Home (Police-XIX), 6th March 2009.]
No. II(2)/HO/235/2009.—Whereas, complaints have been
received from a  number of depositors that Balaji Nidhi
Niruvanam, Kuruppanaickenpalayam, Bhavani Taluk, Erode
District, a Financial Establishment, which has defaulted the
return of deposits made by the depositors after maturity;
2. And, Whereas, the Government are satisfied that the
said Financial Establishment is not likely to return the deposits
to the depositors and hence the Government have to protect
the interests of such depositors;
3. And, Whereas, the properties specified in the Schedule
to this Order are alleged to have been procured by the
Managing Partners of the said Financial Establishment;
4.
Now, Therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred
by Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Protection of Interests of
Depositors (in Financial Establishments) Act, 1997 (Tamil
Nadu Act 44 of 1997), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
makes an ad-interim order attaching the properties of
Thiruvalargal M. Rangasamy, son of Mottaiya Gounder and
Tmt. Valli, wife of Challavel, Managing Partner of the said
Balaji Nidhi Niruvanam, Kuruppanaickenpalayam, Bhavani
Taluk, Erode District, as specifed in the Schedule  to this
Order and transfers the control over the said properties to
the Competent Authority, namely, the District Revenue Officer,
Erode District appointed under the provisions of the said Act,
for the purpose.
5.
The Competent Authority  is requested to pursue
further action in accordance with the procedure laid down
in sub-sections (3) and (4) of Section 4 of the said Act and
also the formalities prescribed in the Tamil Nadu Protection
of  Interests  of Depositors (in Financial Establishments)
Rules, 1997.
6.
The Special Public Prosecutor, Special Court for the
Tamil Nadu Protection of Interests of Depositors (in Financial
Establishments) Act, 1997 is requested to render necessary
assistance to the Competent Authority, in filling the application,
before the said Special Court.
