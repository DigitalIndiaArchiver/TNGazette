No. SRO A-13/2023.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 180 of the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies 
Act, 1983 (Tamil Nadu Act 30 of 1983), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the 
Tamil Nadu Co-operative Sugar Mills Managerial Common Cadre System Rules, 2018 published with the 
Industries Department Notification (No.) SRO A-55(b)/2018, published at pp. 1-20 of Part III-Section 1(a) of the 
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary dated the 10th November, 2018.
Amendments 
  In the said Rules, in rule 9,-
(a)
Under the heading "I Category A: Chief Officers", in the tabular column, in Serial No.3, under 
the heading "(a) Promotion" in column (3), against the entry "Chief Cane Officer" in column (2) 
thereof, for the entry "M.Sc., (Agri)", the entry "B.Sc., (Agri)" shall be substituted; and
(b)
Under the heading "II. Category B: Deputy Chief Officers (By promotion)", in the tabular column, 
in Serial Number 2, for the entries in column (3) against the entry "Deputy Chief Chemist 
(Sugar)" in column (2) thereof, the following entries shall be substituted namely:- 
"B.E (Chemical Engineering) / B.Tech., (Chemical Engineering)   with Associateship of 
National Sugar Institute (Sugar Technology)/Associateship of Vasantdada Sugar Institute 
(Sugar Technology)/P.G. Diploma in Sugar Technology (Anna University) 
or
B.Sc., (Chemistry) or any equivalent bachelor degree with Associateship of National 
Sugar Institute (Sugar Technology) / Associateship of Vasantdada Sugar Institute 
(Sugar Technology) / P.G. Diploma in Sugar Technology (Anna University)". 
                                                                       C. SAMAYAMOORTHY, 
	
                           Agricultural Production Commissioner 
                            and Secretary to Government.
ENVIRONMENT, CLIMATE CHANGE AND FOREST DEPARTMENT
Amendments to the Tamil Nadu Regulation of Wood Based Industries Rules, 2010.
[G.O. Ms. No. 41, Environment, Climate Change and Forest (FR.13), 28th February 2023,

No. SRO A-14/2023.—In exercise of the powers conferred by section 26 read with Sections 35 and 63 of the 
Tamil Nadu Forest Act, 1882 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1882), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following 
amendments to the Tamil Nadu Regulation of Wood Based Industries Rules, 2010.
Amendments 
    In the said Rules, in rule 9,-	
		
	
	
	
(1)
in sub-rule (1), in clause (a), for the expression "the Government" the
expression "the Head of concerned Regional Office of the Central Government
in the Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Chage'', shall be substituted;
(2)
in sub-rule (2), for the expression "the Government or the Conservator of Forests or 
the Field Director of Tiger Reserve, as the case may be, shall decide the same", 
the expression "the Head of concerned Regional Office of the Central Government 
in the Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change shall, within 60 days 
of filing the appeal, pass an appropriate Order", shall be substituted;
(3)
in sub-rule (3), for the expression "the Government or the Conservator of Forests 
or the Field Director of Tiger Reserve, as the case may be", the expression 
"the Head of concerned Regional Office of the Central Government in the Ministry 
of Environment, Forest and Climate Change", shall be substituted;
