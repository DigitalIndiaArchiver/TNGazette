by the Teachers of all Affiliated Dental Colleges from among themselves.
[No. EI(2)/11786/2012]
No. VI-3(b)/29/2012.
Notice is hereby given that an election by postal voting is to be held  for electing One Professor to the Senate of the
Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai, as per clause (b) of sub-section (3) of Section 18 of The Tamil Nadu
Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai Act, 1987 (Tamil Nadu Act 37 of 1987) by the Teachers of all Affiliated Dental Colleges
from among themselves. The term of membership in the regular vacancy will be for a period of three years from the date
of election or upto the date on which he/she ceases to hold the post of Professor of an Affiliated Dental College, whichever
is earlier.
2. Forms of Nomination papers may be obtained from the office of the undersigned from 01-11-2012 to 09-11-2012
between 11.00 a.m. and 3.00 p.m. (on all working days)
3. Nomination papers duly filled in shall be submitted in an envelope addressed to the Registrar
(Full Additional Charge) / Returning Officer and superscribed “Nomination for election to the Senate under Section 18 (3) (b)”
as indicated below, by Registered Post  with Acknowledgement Due, so as to be received by the Registrar
(Full Additional Charge) / Returning Officer not later than 4.00 p.m. on 09-11-2012 or to be delivered to the Returning Officer
during office hours in person or by his/her  proposer or by messenger under Acknowledgement within the date
and hour mentioned above.

8. A numbered declaration paper, a ballot paper, with the name of the candidates bearing on it, the Returning Officer’s

prescribed and within the time specified, does not exceed or is less than the number of vacancies to be filled, such
candidate shall be declared to be duly elected.
