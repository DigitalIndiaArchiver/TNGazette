In exercise of the powers conferred by clause 4 of the Tamil  Nadu Local Bodies Election symbols (Reservation and
Allotment) order, 2011, and also other powers enabling it in this behalf, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission hereby
makes the following amendments to S.O.No. 4/2013/TNSEC/ME1, dated 1-4-2013 published vide Notification No. 16,
published at pages 154-168 of Part VI—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated
24th April 2013, as subsequently amended:-
AMENDMENTS
In the said Notification in Table 1-A,
(1) after serial number 27 in first column and the corresponding entries in second and third columns thereof, the
following serial number and entries shall be deleted, namely:-
28. Manipur People’s Party
People’s Road, Imphal-795 001, Manipur.
after serial number 29 in first column and the corresponding entries in second and third columns thereof, the following
serial number and entries shall be deleted, namely:-
30. National Peoples’ Party
2nd Floor, MDU Building, MG Avenue,
Imphal-795 001, Manipur.
(2)after serial number 168 in first column and the corresponding entries in second and third columns thereof, the
following serial numbers and entries shall, respectively, be added, namely:-
169. Thrinamool Tamil Nadu Congress
189/1, Amaravathi Street (Near Five Roads), Reddiyur Village,
Salem District-636 004, Tamil Nadu.
170. Manitha Uimaigal Kalaagam
14/41, Goods Shed Street, Madurai-625 001, Tamil Nadu.
(3) against serial number 141 and 167 in first column, for the existing entries in third column, the  following  entries
shall, respectively, be substituted, namely:-
No. 49, West Nerkundram Pathai, Vadapalani, Chennai-600 026, Tamil Nadu.
No. 31, First Floor, South West Boag Road, T. Nagar, Chennai-600 017, Tamil Nadu.
