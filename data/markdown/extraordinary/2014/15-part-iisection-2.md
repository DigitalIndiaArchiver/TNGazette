No. II(2)/HE/33(b-1)/2014.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (3) of Section 57 of the
Annamalai University Act, 2013 (Tamil Nadu Act 20 of 2013), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
specifies the 24th day of January, 2014 as the date on which the Faculties of the
Annamalai University, reconstituted under sub-section (2) of the said Section 57 by the Administrator,
Annamalai University shall commence to exercise their functions.

No. II(2)/HE/33(b-2)/2014.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (3) of Section 57 of the
Annamalai University Act, 2013 (Tamil Nadu Act 20 of 2013), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
specifies the 24th day of January, 2014 as the date on which the Boards of Studies of the
Annamalai University, reconstituted under sub-section (2) of the said Section 57 by the
Administrator, Annamalai University shall commence to exercise their functions.
