Amendments to Ad hoc Rules for the Tamil Nadu Medical Subordinate  Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 230, Health and Family Welfare (R-2), 13th August 2010, Aadi 28, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2041.]
No. SRO B-79/2010.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the rules issued with the
Health and Family Welfare Department Notification No. SRO B-232/91, published at page 181 of Part III—Section 1(b)
of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 11th September 1991, as subsequently amended.
2.
The amendments shall come into force on the thirteenth, August, 2010.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules,—
(1) for rule 3, the following rule shall be substituted, namely:—
“3. Appointment—
Appointment to the post shall be made as follows:—
(a)
(i) by promotion from the category of Block Extension Educator; or
(ii) by recruitment by transfer from the category of Health Inspector Grade I; or
(iii) by direct recruitment, if no qualified candidate is available in any of the above methods.
(b)
The ratio for appointment among the methods specified in items (i) and (ii) shall be 9:1.”;
