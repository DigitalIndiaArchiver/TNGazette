Whereas, in exercise of the powers conferred by
Clause (1) of Article 258 of the Constitution the President has
entrusted to the Government of Tamil Nadu in letter No.4(1)65
Central II Food and agriculture community Development and
Co-operation (Agriculture), dated 23rd April 1966 with their
consent to the functions of the central Government under Land
Acquisition Act, 1894 (Central Act 1 of 1894) in relations to
acquisitions of land for the purpose of the union in State.
And, whereas the lands specified in schedule below and
situated in Sannanallur, Nannilam Taluk, Tiruvarur District are
needed for a Public purpose, to wit for the formation of Broad
Gauge Railway line between Tiruvarur and Mayiladuthurai notice
to that effect to hereby given to all to whom it may concern in
accordance with the provisions of sub-sections 1 of Section 4
of the land acquisition Act, 1894. (Central Act of 1 of 1894)
Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by
aforesaid section the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby Specially
Authorizes the Revenue Divisional Officer, Tiruvarur his staff
and workmen to exercise the power conferred by the
Section 4 (2) of the Act under Section 3(c) of the same Act. the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints Revenue Divisional
Officer, Tiruvarur to perform the functions of the collector under
sub-section 4 of the Section 17 of the said Act. the Governor of
Tamilnadu hereby directs that in view of the urgency of the case
the provisions of the 5A of the Act shall not apply to
this case.

Whereas, in exercise of the powers conferred by
Clause (1) of Article 258 of the Constitution of India, the
President of India, has in the Ministry of Food, Agriculture,
Community Development and Co-operation (Department of
Agriculture) Notification No.4(1)/65, General-II, dated 23rd April
1966, entrusted to the Government of Tamil Nadu with their
consent, the functions of the Central Government under the
Land Acquisition Act, 1894 (Central Act 1 of 1894), in relation to
acquisition of lands for the purpose of the union in this State.
And whereas, the lands specified in the schedule below are
needed for a public purpose, to wit, for the formation of Doubling
Broad Gauge Railway line between Villupuram and Dindigul.
Notice to that effect is hereby given to all to whom it may
concern, in accordance with the provisions of sub-section (1) of
Section 4 of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894 (Central Act 1 of
1894).
And, whereas it has become necessary to acquire the
immediate possession of the Lands specified in the Schedule
below, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that the
Lands to be acquired under the provisions of sub-section (2) of
Section 17 of the said Act.
Now, therefore the exercise of the powers conferred by
sub-section (2) of Section 4 of said Act, the Governor of Tamil
Nadu hereby authorizes the Revenue Divisional Office,
Perambalur and his staff and workmen, to exercise the powers
conferred by the said sub-section under clause (c) of Section 3
of the said Act, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the
Revenue Divisional Officer, Perambalur to perform the functions
of the Collector under the said Act.
Under sub-section (4) of Section 17 of the said Act, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that, in view of the
urgency of the case, the provisions of Section 5-A of the Act
shall not apply to this case.
