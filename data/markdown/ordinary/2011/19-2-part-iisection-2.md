COMMERCIAL TAXES AND REGISTRATION
DEPARTMENT
Provision for payment of consolidation of Stamp Duty for
issue of Debentures by Messrs. Apollo Hospitals
Enterprise Limited under Indian Stamp Act.
[G.O. Rt. No. 229, Commercial Taxes and Registration (J1),
5th May 2011.]
No. II(2)/CTR/224/2011.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by clause (b) of sub-section (1) of Section 9 of
the Indian Stamp Act, 1899 (Central Act II of 1899), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby provides for the consolidation
of duty of Rs. 12,50,000 (Rupees Twelve lakh and fifty
thousand only chargeable under the said Act in respect of
issue of Debentures by Messrs Apollo Hospitals Enterprise
Limited bearing Serial Nos. 1 to 500.
P. SURIYAKUMAR,
Additional Secretary to Government.
HOME DEPARTMENT
Appointments of Special Judges of certain Special Courts
for Trial of Cases for certain District under the
Prevention of Corruption of Act.
NOTIFICATION-I
[G.O. Ms. No. 254, Home (Courts II), 3rd May 2011.]
No. II(2)/HO/225/2011.—In exercise of the powers
conferred  by sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Prevention
of Corruption Act, 1988 (Central Act 49 of 1988), the Governor
of Tamil Nadu, hereby appoints Special Judge for the Chennai
Metro-Politan Sessions Division to try the cases under the
said Act, investigated by the State Police including the
Directorate of Vigilance and Anti-Corruption.
NOTIFICATION-II
[G.O. Ms. No. 254, Home (Courts II), 3rd May 2011.]
No. II(2)/HO/226/2011.—In exercise of the powers
conferred  by sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Prevention
of Corruption Act, 1988 (Central Act 49 of 1988), the Governor
of Tamil Nadu, hereby appoints Special Judge for the  Sessions
Divisions of Coimbatore, Erode and the Nilgiris to try the
cases under the said Act, investigated by the State Police
including the Directorate of Vigilance and Anti-Corruption.
NOTIFICATION-III
[G.O. Ms. No. 254, Home (Courts II), 3rd May 2011.]
No. II(2)/HO/227/2011.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Prevention
of Corruption Act, 1988 (Central Act 49 of 1988), the Governor
of Tamil Nadu, hereby appoints a Special   Judge for the
Sessions Divisions of Tiruchirappalli, Karur, Thanjavur and
Nagappattinam to try the cases under the said Act, investigated
by the State Police including the Directorate of Vigilance and
Anti-Corruption.
NOTIFICATION-IV
[G.O. Ms. No. 254, Home (Courts II), 3rd May 2011.]
No. II(2)/HO/228/2011.—In exercise of the powers
conferred  by sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Prevention
of Corruption Act, 1988 (Central Act 49 of 1988), the Governor
of Tamil Nadu, hereby appoints a Special Judge for the
Sessions Divisions of Madurai, Dindigul, Theni and
Virudhunagar  to try the cases under the said Act, investigated
by the State Police including the Directorate of Vigilance and
Anti-Corruption.
K. GNANADESIKAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.

No. II(2)/LE/239/2011.—Whereas, in G.O. (D) No. 444,
Labour and Employment Department, dated 29th July 2009,
the Government have issued orders referring the Industrial
dispute in Sri Munipachaiappan Textiles (P) Limited, on the
issue of 20% bonus for the year 2004-05, to the Labour
Court, Vellore for adjudication.
And Whereas the Presiding Officer, Principal Labour Court,
Vellore has requested to refer the industrial dispute ordered
in G.O. (D) No. 444, Labour and Employment Department,
dated 29th July 2009 to the proper forum. The Commissioner
of Labour, Chennai has recommended to transfer the industrial
dispute referred to the Labour Court, Vellore in G.O. (D) No.
444, Labour and Employment Department, dated 29th  July
2009 to the Industrial Tribunal, Chennai, in exercise of the
power vested with the Government under Section 33-B of
the Industrial Disputes Act, 1947. (Central Act XIV of 1947);
And Whereas the bonus is a matter specified in the Third
Schedule to the said Act and as the workmen involved in
this “Bonus Issue” are more than one hundred (i.e.) four
hundred and nineteen, the Industrial Tribunal is the authority
