CONSTITUTION OF SUB COURT AT VEDASANDUR IN DINDIGUL DISTRICT UNDER THE
TAMIL NADU CIVIL COURTS ACT.
NOTIFICATION – I
[G.O. Ms. No. 823, Home (Courts III), 22nd October  2012.]
No. II(2)/HO/701(b-1)/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by the first paragraph of Section 4 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873
(Central Act III of 1873), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby alters the number of  Subordinate Judges to be appointed
under the said Act for Dindigul District as Five with effect on and from the date on which the Subordinate Judge
assumes charge of the Subordinate Judge’s Court at Vedasandur.
NOTIFICATION – II
[G.O. Ms. No. 823, Home (Courts III), 22nd October  2012.]
No. II(2)/HO/701(b-2)/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by the second paragraph of Section 4 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873
(Central Act III of 1873), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, after consultation with the High Court, Madras, hereby fixes the number
of Subordinate Judges to be appointed under the said Act for the Subordinate Judge’s Court at Vedasandur in
Dindigul District as One.

NOTIFICATION – III
[G.O. Ms. No. 823, Home (Courts III), 22nd October  2012.]
No. II(2)/HO/701(b-3)/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of
1873), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby fixes Vedasandur as  the place at which the Subordinate Judge’s Court
shall be held.
NOTIFICATION – IV
[G.O. Ms. No. 823, Home (Courts III), 22nd October  2012.]
No. II(2)/HO/701(b-4)/2012.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 10 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu,  hereby  directs that the Subordinate Judge’s  Court, Dindigul shall cease to have
jurisdiction over the District  Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Vedasandur and hereby fixes the local limits of the District
Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Vedasandur as the local limits of jurisdiction of the Subordinate Judge’s Court,
Vedasandur with effect on and from the date on which the Subordinate Judge, assumes charge of the
Subordinate Judge’s Court, Vedasandur.
 R. RAJAGOPAL,
