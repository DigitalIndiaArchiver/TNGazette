The following Notification of the Government of India, Ministry of Law and Justice (Department of Justice), Jaisalmer House,
26, Man Singh Road, New Delhi-110 011 Dated 11th February, 2014 is republished:—
In exercise of the powers conferred by article 223 of the Constitution of India, the President is pleased to appoint
Shri Justice Satish Kumar Agnihotri, Seniormost Judge of the Madras High Court, to perform the duties of the office of
Chief Justice of that High Court with effect from the date Shri Justice Rajesh Kumar Agrawal relinquishes charge of the office of
the Chief Justice of the Madras High Court consequent upon his appointment as a Judge of the Supreme Court of India.
