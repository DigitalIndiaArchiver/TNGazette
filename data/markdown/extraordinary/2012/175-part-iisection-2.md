AMENDMENT TO NOTIFICATION
In exercise of  the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Commissions of Inquiry
Act, 1952 (Central Act LX of 1952), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to
the Public (Law and Order-F) Department Notification No. ll(2)/PULO/396(K)/2011, published  at pages 1-2 of
Part ll—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 13th September 2011,
as subsequently amended.
AMENDMENT
In the said Notification, in paragraph 4, for the expression “nine months and 17 days”, the expression
“fourteen months and 18 days”  shall be substituted.
KUMAR JAYANT,
Secretary to Government.
