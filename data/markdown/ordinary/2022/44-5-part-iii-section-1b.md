No. SRO B-51/2022.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the ad hoc rules for the post of District Child Protection 
Officer in the Department of Social Defence published with the Social Welfare and Nutritious Meal Programme Department, 
Notification No. SRO B-82/2018, at pages 138-140 of Part III—Section 1(b) of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 
17th October, 2018. 
2. The amendment hereby made shall come into force on the 17th October, 2022.
Amendment
In the said Rules, in rule 5, in sub-rule (b) in "THE TABLE" in column (2), against "by direct recruitment" in column (1), 
the expression "A Diploma or a Certificate course in Computer Operations in the recognized Institutions is desirable", shall 
be omitted.
