WHEREAS, the Tamil Nadu Waqf Board has been reconstituted vide Government Orders first and second 
read above as per sub-section (1) of Section 14 of the Waqf Act, 1995 (Central Act 43 of 1995).
2. AND WHEREAS, as per sub-section (4) of Section 14 of the Waqf Act, 1995, the elected 
members shall at all times be more than the nominated members. As the elected members were less than the nominated

members and the Board was unable to perform its functions as per the waqf Act, 1995, the Government have superseded the 
Tamil Nadu Waqf Board for a period of six months and appointed Thiru M.A. Siddique, I.A.S., the then Principal
Secretary to Government (Expenditure), Finance Department as Special Officer to exercise and perform all the powers and 
duties under the provisions of the Waqf Act, 1995 on behalf of the Tamil Nadu Waqf Board during the period of supersession vide 
Government order third read above. The period of supersession expires on 17-03-2020.
3. AND WHEREAS, it is proposed to reconstitute the Tamil Nadu Waqf Board by appointing members 
elected through election process and by nomination as per Section 14 of the Waqf Act, 1995 read with Tamil Nadu 
Waqf Board (Conduct of Election for Members) Rules, 1997. Whereas the election process such as constituting the 
electoral College, publishing of electoral rolls, filling and scrutiny of nominations, voting process and declaration of results will take 
three months time.
4. AND THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred by clause (a) of sub-section (3) of Section 99 of the Waqf Act, 1995 
(Central Act 43 of 1995), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby extends the period of supersession of the Tamil Nadu Waqf Board by 
another six months with effect from the 18th March 2020.
5. Further in exercise of the powers conferred by clause (b) of sub-section (2) of Section 99 of the Waqf Act, 1995, the 
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that Thiru M.A. Siddique, I.A.S., Principal Secretary to Government, Commissioner of 
Commercial Taxes, Chennai-5 shall exercise and perform all the powers and duties under the provisions of the said Act on behalf 
of the Tamil Nadu Waqf Board during the extension period of supersession, as Special Officer.
CHANDRA MOHAN. B,
Principal Secretary to Government.
