HOME, PROHIBITION AND EXCISE DEPARTMENT.
Amendments to the Tamil Nadu Liquor (Licence and Permit) Rules.
[G.O.(Ms) No. 50, Home, Prohibition and Excise (VIII), 29th October 2012.]
No. SRO A-32/2012.-—In exercise of the powers conferred by Sections 17-C, 17-D, 17-E, 18-B, 18-C, 20 and 21 read
with section 54 of the Tamil Nadu Prohibition Act, 1937 (Tamil Nadu Act X of 1937), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
makes the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu Liquor (Licence and Permit) Rules, 1981:-
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules,—
(1) in rule 25, in clause II, in sub-clause (a), for the proviso, the following proviso shall be substituted, namely:-
“Provided that no sale of liquor shall be made, by licensees holding F.L.2, F.L.3 and F.L.3 (AA) licences on the Thiruvalluvar
day falling on the month of January, Gandhi Jayanthi day, namely, the 2nd October, Birth day eve of Nabigal Nayagam,
Mahaveer Jayanthi day, Vallalar Ninaivu Naal, Independence day, Republic day and May day in every year.”
(2) in Form F.L.2, for condition Number 7, the following condition shall be substituted, namely:-
“7 No sale of liquor shall be made under the licence on the Thiruvalluvar day falling in the month of January, Gandhi
Jayandhi day,  namely, The 2nd October, Birth day eve of Nabigal Nayagam, Mahaveer Jayanthi, Vallalar Ninaivu Naal,
Independence day, Republic day and May day in every year.”;
(3) in Form F.L.3, for condition Number 6, the following condition shall be substituted, namely:-
“6 No sale of liquor shall be made under the licence on the Thiruvalluvar day falling on the month of January,
Gandhi Jayandhi day, namely, the 2nd October, Birth day eve of Nabigal Nayagam, Mahaveer Jayandhi day, Vallalar
Ninaivu Naal, Independence day, Republic day and May day in every year”; and
(4) in Form F.L.3(AA) for condition Number 7, the following condition shall be substituted namely:-
“7 No sale of liquor shall be made under the licence on the following days in every year.
1. Thiruvalluvar day in the month of January
2. Gandhi Jayandhi day namely, the 2nd October
3. Birth day eve of Nabigal Nayagam
4. Mahaveer Jayandhi day
5. Vallalar Ninaivu Naal
6. Independence day
7. Republic day and
8. May day.”
R. RAJAGOPAL,
Principal Secretary to Government.
