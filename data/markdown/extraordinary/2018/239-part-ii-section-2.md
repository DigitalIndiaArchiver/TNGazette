[G.O. Ms. No. 472, Public (Law & Order-F), 4th July 2018, Aani 20, Vilambi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2049.]
No. II(2)/PULO/591(a)/2018.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of section 3 of the Commissions of lnquiry Act, 
I952 (Central Act LX of 1952), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the 
Public (Law & Order.F) Department Notiﬁ cation No.II(2)/PULO/481(c)/2018, published at pages 1-2 of 
Part Il-Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 23rd May 2018:-
AMENDMENT
In the said Notiﬁ cation, in paragraph 3, in the terms of reference, for item (i) the following item shall be 
substituted, namely :-
"(i) To inquire into the causes and circumstances leading to the opening of ﬁ re resulting in death 
and injuries to persons on 22.05.2018 and subsequent events at Thoothukudi and nearby areas, 
arising out of law and order disturbances including damage to public and private properties".
