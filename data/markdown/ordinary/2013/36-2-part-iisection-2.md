allowance as follows:-

average Consumer Price Index Number for the city of Chennai
for the year 2000, namely 475 points (with base 1982=100)
and for every rise of one point over and above 475 points,
Rs.6.05 (Rupees six and paise five only) per point per month
shall be paid, as dearness allowance.

Government Gazette based on the Average Consumer Price
Index Number of the previous year.

in the payment of wages shall be made in the case of
men and women employees.

be multiplied by 30.
(5) Wherever the existing wages are higher than the
minimum wages fixed hereing, the existing wages shall be
continued to be paid.
