DRAFT VARIATION TO THE  APPROVED LIST OF PARKS AND PLAY-FIELDS IN THE AREA WITHIN THE
JURISDICTION OF THE TIRUCHIRAPPALLI  MUNICIPAL COUNCIL UNDER TAMIL NADU PARKS,
PLAY-FIELDS AND OPEN SPACES (PRESERVATION AND REGULATION) ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 313, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (MC. II), 23rd December 2010.]
No. II(2)/MAWS/749(c)/2010.
Whereas in G.O. Ms. No. 562, Education Department, dated 17th April 1967, a list of Parks and
Play-fields in the area within the jurisdiction of the then Tiruchirappalli Municipal Council was approved,
under sub-section (2) of Section 4 of the Tamil Nadu Parks, Play-fields and Open Spaces (Preservation and
Regulation) Act, 1959 (Tamil Nadu Act 26 of 1959) and the same was published.  In the said list,
among others, an extent of 4 acres 3469 Sq.feet in Old T.S.No. 1094/1D was notified as Periyar Training
School and Play-fields.
2.  And Whereas, the Director of Town and Country Planning, Letter Roc.No. 5832/09/ºF.3,
dated  23rd September 2010 has stated that, the Tiruchirappalli Corporation in its Resolution No. 428, dated
29th September 2000 has resolved to forward the representation of the petitioner to Government for deletion
from the Park and Play-field list and that the Local Planning Authority, Tiruchirappalli also in its Resolution
No. 4, dated 17th January 2001 has resolved to recommend the petitioner's request to Government.
He has therefore, requested the Government to consider the proposal to delete the Play-fields situated
in the Campus of Periyar Training School as notified in  G.O. Ms. No. 562, Education Department,
dated 17th April 1967, based on the Resolution passed by the Tiruchirappalli Corporation and Local Planning
Authority in this regard.  The Director of Municipal Administration, Letter No. 49190/2010/MCA3,
dated 8th November 2010 has suggested to issue appropriate orders on the recommendations of the
Director of Town and Country Planning.

3. And Whereas, the Government, after careful examination of the proposal of the Director of Town and
Country Planning regarding the representation of the Periyar Self Respect Propaganda Institution for
deletion of play-field from the list as notified in G.O. Ms. No. 562, Education Department,
dated 17th April 1967 have decided to accept the same.
4. Now, Therefore, the following draft of the variation to the approved list of Parks and Play-fields
as published in the Education Department Notification No. 75 of 1967 in Part II–Section 3
at  pages 534 to 540 of the Fort. St. George Gazette, dated the 14th June 1967, which it is proposed to
make in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Parks,
Play-fields and Open Spaces (Preservation and Regulation) Act, 1959 (Tamil Nadu Act 26 of 1959) is
hereby published as required under sub-section (2) of the said section.
5. Notice is hereby given that objections or suggestions, if any, received in writing, in respect of the said
draft variation to the said list from any person interested within 7 (Seven) days from the date of publication
of this notification in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, will be taken into consideration by the Government
of Tamil Nadu.
6. Objections or suggestions, if any, should be addressed to the Principal Secretary to Government,
Municipal Administration and Water Supply Department, Secretariat, Fort. St.George, Chennai-600 009.
DRAFT VARIATION.
In the said Notification, in The Schedule, under  the  heading `Play-fields:—the entry “17 Periyar Training
School ” in column (1) and the corresponding entries relating thereto in columns (2) to (8)
shall be omitted.
