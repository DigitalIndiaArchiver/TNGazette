In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 65 of Madurai City Municipal Corporation Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu
Act XV of 1971), Section 27 and sub-section (1) of Section 43-B of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920
(Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920) and sub-rule (4) of rule 99 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities,
Municipalities and Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission hereby fixes
4-8-2009 as the date for convening a meeting to elect Chairman of Ward Committee of Corporation,
Chairman/Vice-Chairman/Member of Appointment Committee, Member of Taxation Appeal Committee as the case may be,
