Order:- No.77, Agriculture (OS), 30th March 2020.
The Tamil Nadu Oilpalm (Regulation of Production and Processing) Act, 1994 come into force with eﬀ ect from 12th December 
1994. As per sec 3(1) of the above said Act, a Statutory Committee called “The Tamil Nadu Oilpalm Project Management Committee” 
has to be constituted by the Government consisting of members as stipulated under Section 3 (2) of the said Act. Accordingly, in the 
Government Order 1st  read above the  Government had constituted the “Tamil Nadu Oilpalm Project Management Committee”. 
The Committee was again reconstituted in the Government Orders 2nd  and 3rd  read above. The term of the above committee is 
three years. The above Committee was lastly reconstituted for three years in the G.O. 4th read above.
2.  In his letter 5th read above, the Director of Agriculture has stated that the term of the Tamil Nadu Oilpalm Project 
Management Committee expired on 08-07-2018 and as such, the committee may be reconstituted for three more years. He has 
accordingly sent  necessary proposal to Government to reconstitute the committee for a further period of 3 years from the date of 
issue of  order for the purpose.
3. The Government have carefully examined the proposal of the Director of Agriculture and decided to reconstitute  the
“Tamil Nadu Oilpalm Project  Management Committee ” under Section 3(1) of the of the Tamil Nadu Oilpalm (Regulation of 
Production and Processing) Act, 1994 for a further period of three years as detailed below:-
