Under clause (f) of
Section 16 of the Tamil Nadu Transparency in Tenders Act, 1998
(Tamil Nadu Act 43 of 1998), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notifies permission to the Managing Director,
Tamil Nadu Medical Services Corporation Limited to procure and supply a quantity of 2,30,000 packs of
belt type sanitary napkins per month from the Prison Department.
