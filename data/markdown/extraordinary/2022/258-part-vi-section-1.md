The Government of Tamil Nadu having  been satisﬁ ed that the  Grama Natham lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule 
below and measuring to an extent of  611 Sq.metres  under Chennai Peripheral Ring Road Project for Highways purpose in 
No.78, Anuppampattu-II  Village, Ponneri Taluk, Tiruvallur District,  and it having already been decided that the entire amount of 
compensation to be awarded for the lands to be paid out of the funds  controlled and  managed by the Divisional Engineer (C&M), 
Thiruvallur, Tamil Nadu  after having considering the cause shown by the owner or other persons having interest on such lands as 
the case may be do hereby publish the following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways 
Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002).
NOTIFICATION
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) 
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notiﬁ ed that the Grama Natham lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below and measuring 
to an extent 611 Sq.metres of lands are acquired in No.78, Anuppampattu Village in Ponneri Taluk, Tiruvallur District, to the same, 
a little more or less needed for Chennai Peripheral Ring Road Project for State Highways purpose.
The plan of the lands under acquisition are kept in the oﬃ  ce of the Special District Revenue Oﬃ  cer (Land Acquisition), 
Tamil Nadu Road Sector Project–II, Guindy, Chennai-32 and oﬃ  ce of the Special Tahsildar (Land Acquisition), 
Chennai peripheral Ring Road Project, Unit I, Ponneri in Tiruvallur District  may be inspected at any time during the oﬃ  ce hours.
