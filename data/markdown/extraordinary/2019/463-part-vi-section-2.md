In excercise of the powers conferred under Section 57-A of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Panchayat 
Act 21 of 1994), the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission hereby specifies that in the Elections to fill up the Ordinary 
vacancies in the offices of the Ward Members  of Village Panchayats, Village Panchayat Presidents, Panchayat Union Ward 
Members and  District Panchayat Ward Member in Melpuram  Panchayat Union of Kanniyakumari District notified in the 
S.O.No.(s) 66/2019/TNSEC/PE-1 and S.O.No.87/2019/TNSEC/PE-1, dated 07-12-2019, the giving and recording of votes by 
Electronic Voting Machine shall be adopted.

In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-rule (1) of rule 59-B of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Elections) Rules, 
1995, the State Election Commission specifies  that the balloting unit of the voting machine shall contain ballot paper and 
address tag.  The ballot paper shall contain the details such as Sl. No. of ballot paper, Code No. and Name of the local 
body, Ward No., Nature of election (Ordinary/Casual) and Month and Year of election, Sheet Number on the top of the Ballot 
paper and Candidate’s Sl.No, Candidate’s name and/or their Election symbol. The names of the contesting Candidates in 
the ballot papers for the election of Ward Member of District Panchayat and Ward Member of Panchayat Union shall be 
printed in the Tamil alphabetical order as in Form 9 with their symbols.  The symbols of the contesting Candidates in the 
election of Ward Member of Village Panchayat and President of Village Panchayat shall be printed in the order of Table 2 and 
Table 3 of S.O.No.16/2019/TNSEC/EG1, dated 30-09-2019.  The Commission also specifies that the said ballot papers shall 
be in the following form.
1.	
The total length of the ballot paper will be 456 mm and the width will be 139 mm.
2.	
At the top of the ballot paper, there shall be a space provided for indicating the particulars of Sl.No. of the ballot 
paper, code No. and name of the local body, Ward No., nature of election (Ordinary /Casual) and year of election and sheet 
number.
3.	
In the space so provided on the left hand corner, the Sl.No. of the ballot paper shall be printed.  On the top right hand 
corner, sheet number shall be printed if the ballot paper is printed on more than one sheet, where the number of contesting 
candidates exceeds sixteen.  The particulars of the election shall also be printed in the said space.  These particulars will 
contain the code number and name of the local body, Ward No., nature of election (Ordinary/Casual) and year of election 
and shall be printed in Tamil.
	
Provided that where the No. of contesting candidates is less than sixteen, the above particulars may be printed 
either at the top or at the bottom most portion (space meant for the candidate at Sl.No. sixteen), as may be considered 
convenient from the point of view of printing.
	
Provided further that where the No. of contesting candidates is sixteen or more, the above particulars may be 
printed in a vertical column, on the left hand side of the ballot paper, with a thick line dividing the said column and the panels 
containing the serial numbers and names of the contesting candidates.
4.	
Below the space provided at the top for printing the particulars of the election, there shall be a thick black line of 
1.00 mm for BEL make machines.
5.	
Below this line, there shall be printed the serial number of each contesting candidate, his name and the symbol 
allotted to him, in a separate panel for each candidate.
6.	
The size of the panel for each contesting candidate shall be 27.5 mm (length) x 140 mm (width).

7.	
The panels of the candidates shall be separated from each other by a thick black line of 1.00 mm.
8.	
The names and/or symbols of the contesting candidates shall be arranged on the ballot paper in the same order 
in which their names appear in the list of contesting candidates (Form 9).
9.	
The names of not more than sixteen candidates shall be arranged on one sheet of a ballot paper.  If the number 
of contesting candidate is less than sixteen, the space below the panel for last contesting candidate shall be kept blank, 
except where the particulars of election, etc., are printed at the bottom under clause (3) above.
10.	 If the number of contesting candidates exceeds sixteen, the ballot paper shall be printed on two sheets of the 
above mentioned size and dimensions if the number of candidates is between seventeen and thirty two, on three sheets if 
the number of contesting candidates is between thirty three and forty eight, on four sheets if their number is between forty 
nine and sixty four.
11.	 In such cases where the ballot paper is printed on more than one sheet, the names of contesting candidates from 
S.No.17 to 32 shall be printed on the second sheet, from S.No.33 to 48 on the third sheet and from 49 onwards on the 
fourth sheet.  If the number of candidates is less than thirty two the space below the panel for the last contesting candidate 
in the second sheet shall be kept blank.  Similarly, for the third and fourth sheets of the ballot papers.
12.	 On each such sheet, in the space provided for indicating the particulars of the election, its number shall be indicated 
in bold words and letters, e.g., ‘Sheet No.1’, ‘Sheet No.2’, etc.
13.	 The names of the contesting candidates shall be printed in the same language or languages in which the list of 
contesting candidates has been prepared.  The serial number of the candidate shall be indicated in the international form of 
Indian numerals.
14.	 Serial number and the name of the candidate shall be printed on the left hand side and his symbol on the right 
hand side in the panel meant for him.
15.	 Unless otherwise directed by the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission in any particular case, the ballot papers 
shall be printed on white paper.
Before fixing the ballot paper in the balloting unit, the Returning Officer shall sign on the back side of each ballot paper 
duly noting down the  Sl.No. of the balloting unit in the issue register.  
After closing the ballot paper screen, the Returning Officer shall affix address tag and seal the ballot paper screen.  The 
address tag shall contain the details of election, Name of the local body, the unit No. (ID Number) of the control unit/balloting 
unit, Sl.No. and name of Polling Station and date of poll.
