Confirmation of Variation to the Approved Karanthai Detailed Development Plan Part-X
of Thanjavur Local Planning Area.
(Roc. No. 2009/2005/DP3)
No. VI(1)/293/2014.
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 33(2) of Town and Country Planning Act, 1971. The Commissioner of
Town and Country Planning, Chennai proceeding Roc.No. 2009/2005/DP3, dated 27-08-2014 hereby confirmes the following
variation to the Approved Karanthai Detailed Development Plan Part-X of Thanjavur Local Planning Area, approved by the
Director of Town and Country Planning in proceeding Roc.No. 24254/1981/D2, dated 18-07-1983 and the fact of this approval
in Form No. 12, Published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette (No. 38), Part VI—Section-1 page No. 498, dated 28-09-1983
in publication No. VI(1)874/1983 and the said draft notification published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette (No. 2),
Part VI—Section 1, Page No. 7, dated 08-01-2014 in Publication No. VI(1)/11/2014.
2.
Since no objections and suggestions have been received on this draft notification within the stipulated time, the
same are hereby confirmed and ordered as below:
CONFIRMATION OF VARIATION
1.
The entire Karanthai Detailed Development Plan Part-X of Thanjavur Local Planning Area, approved by the
Director of Town and Country Planning proceedings in Roc.No. 24254/81/D2, dated, 18-07-1983 is varied by Karanthai
Detailed Development Plan, Part-X of Thanjavur Local Planning Area.
2.
The plan thus varied is numbered as Map No. 5, DDP(V)/DTCP No. 25/2013.
3.
The Detailed Development Plan clauses and schedules annexed with this plan are made enforceable from the
date of publication of this notification in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
Confirmation of Variation to the Approved Karanthai Detailed Development Plan, Part-VII
of Thanjavur Local Planning Area.
(Roc. No. 29052/2005/DP3)
No. VI(1)/294/2014.
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 33(2) of Town and Country Planning Act, 1971. The Commissioner of
Town and Country Planning, Chennai proceeding Roc. No. 29052/2005/DP3 dated 27-08-2014 hereby confirmes the following
variation to the Approved Karanthai Detailed Development Plan Part-VII of Thanjavur Local Planning Area, approved by the
Director of Town and Country Planning in proceeding Roc. No. 28925/1977/D1, dated 11-04-1979 and the fact of this approval
in Form No. 12, Published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Part VI—Section 1 page No. 531, dated 05-09-1979 in
publication No. VI(1)929/1979 and the said draft notification was published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette (No. 18),
Part VI—Section 1, Page No. 150, dated 08-05-2013 in Publication No. VI(1)/216/2013.
2.
Since no objections and suggestions have been received on this draft notification within the stipulated time, the
same are hereby confirmed and ordered as below:—
CONFIRMATION OF VARIATION
1.
The entire Karanthai Detailed Development Plan, Part-VII of Thanjavur Local Planning Area, approved by the
Director of Town and Country Planning in proceedings  Roc. No. 28925/1977/D1, dated 11-04-1979 is varied by Karanthai
Detailed Development Plan, Part-VII of Thanjavur Local Planning Area.
2.
The plan thus varied is numbered as Map No. 5, DDP(V)/DTCP No. 9/2013.
3.
The Detailed Development Plan clauses and schedules annexed with this plan are made enforceable from
the date of publication of this notification in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
Chennai-600 002,
R. VENKATESAN,
27th August 2014.
Commissioner of Town and Country Planning.

Variation to the Approved Master Plan for the Gummidipoondi Local Planning Area
(Roc. No. 160/2013/GLPA.)
No. VI(1)/295/2014.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 32 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning
Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972), Government in the order G.O. (2D) No. 88, Housing and Urban Development
UD 4(1) Department, dated 28-08-2014 has permitted to change of land use of a survey numbers making the
following variation to the Master Plan for the Gummidipoondi Local Planning Area, approved under the said Act and
published in the Housing and Urban Development Department Notification No. II(2)/HOU/610/2000 dated 7-6-2000,
at page No. 295 of Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated 7th June 2000.
VARIATIONS
In the said Master Plan, in the “LAND USE SCHEDULE” of Serial No.5 VILLAGE NUMBER: 192. Getnamallee
under the heading:—
VI. AGRICULTURAL USE ZONE : Survey Numbers, instead of the expression “118 to 120" the expression
“118, 119" shall be substituted.
and under the heading III INDUSTRIAL USE ZONE: III (b) GENERAL INDUSTRIAL USE ZONE: Survey Numbers
the expression “120/1, 2, 3” shall be added before the expression” 337 to 341 “.
Chengalpattu,
V.M. RAJENDRAN
17th September 2014.
Member Secretary (in-charge)
Gummidipoondi Local Planning Authority.
