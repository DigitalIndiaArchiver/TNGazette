WHEREAS, a written notice of the intention to make a motion expressing want of conﬁ dence in 
Tmt.B. Subalakshmi  Babu, Chairman and Tmt.R. Krishnaveni Thanigachalam, Vice - Chairman, Lathur Panchayat 
Union Council, Chengalpattu district, signed by twelve members, out of the sanctioned strength of ﬁ fteen members of the 
said Council, together with a copy of the motion proposed to be made and a written statement of charges against the said 
Chairman  and  Vice - Chairman have been delivered to the Revenue Divisional Oﬃ  cer,  Maduranthagam, Chengalpattu
district in person by twelve members as per sub-section (2) of Section 212 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994 
(Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994);
AND WHEREAS, the Revenue Divisional Oﬃ  cer, Maduranthagam, Chengalpattu district caused a copy of the 
statement of charges along with a copy of the notice of motion to be delivered, to the said  Chairman  Tmt.B.Subalakshmi 
Babu and Vice - Chairman Tmt.R.Krishnaveni Thanigachalam and they submitted their reply to the charges;
AND WHEREAS, a motion expressing want of conﬁ dence in Tmt.B.Subalakshmi Babu, Chairman and 
Tmt.R.Krishnaveni Thanigachalam, Vice - Chairman, Lathur Panchayat Union Council, Chengalpattu district had been 
carried with a support of twelve members out of the sanctioned strength of ﬁ fteen members of the said Panchayat 
Union Council in the meeting convened by the Revenue Divisional Oﬃ  cer, Maduranthagam, Chengalpattu district on the 
13th October 2023 after following the procedures speciﬁ ed in the said section 212 and forwarded the same to the 
Government;
NOW THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (13) of Section 212 of the 
Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby removes 
Tmt.B.Subalakshmi Babu, Chairman, Lathur Panchayat Union Council, Chengalpattu district from the oﬃ  ce of the 
Chairman of the said Panchayat Union Council and Tmt.R.Krishnaveni Thanigachalam, Vice - Chairman, Lathur 
Panchayat Union Council, Chengalpattu district from the oﬃ  ce of the Vice - Chairman of the said Panchayat Union Council, 
with immediate eﬀ ect.
