REVENUE  DEPARTMENT
HOLDINGS OF THIRU. MUTHURAMAN CHETTIAR
CANCELLATION OF NOTIFICATION.
[G.O. Ms. No. 141, Revenue [LR.I(1)], 24th March 2015.]
No. II(1)/REV/11/2015.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 18-C of the Tamil Nadu Land Reforms (Fixation of Ceiling on Land)
Act, 1961 (Tamil Nadu Act 58 of 1961), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby cancels the Revenue Department
Notification No. II(1)REV/445/79, dated the 4th June 1979, published at pages 40 to 43 of the supplement to
Part II—Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 27th June 1979.
R. VENKATESAN,
Secretary to Government.
