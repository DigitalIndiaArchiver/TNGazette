Declaration of Naduvattam Extension-XXXIV Forest
Block in Uthagamandalam Taluk, The Nilgiris District as
Reserved Forest under the Tamil Nadu Forest Act.

No. II(2)/EF/15/2014:—In exercise of the power conferred
by Section 16 of the Tamil Nadu Forest Act, 1882 (Tamil Nadu
Act V of 1882) the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby
declares the area, the boundaries of which are specified in the
Schedule below as reserve forest with effect on and from the
7th July 2014.
THE SCHEDULE
1. Name of The District
:
The Nilgiris
2. Name of the Taluk
:
Udhagamandalam
3. Number and Name
:
10, (Old No. 3) Naduvattam
of the Village
4. Name of the Forest Block :
Naduvattam Extension-
XXXIV
5. Survey Number of the
:
New Revised Survey
Forest Block
No. 308 (Old Survey No. 8)
6. Area of the Forest Block :
2.91 Acres (or) 1.18.0
Hectares.
BOUNDARIES
North, East, South and West.—The Naduvattam
Extension XXXIV Forest Block is surrounded by reserve
forest in all sides, which is declared as Naduvattam reserve
forest in Forests and Fisheries Department Notification
No.II(2)FRF1/1441/77 dated 25th February 1977 and
published at pages 158-159 of part-II Section 2 of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette dated the 30th March 1977.
No Rights allowed.
MOHAN VERGHESE CHUNKATH,
Additional Chief Secretary to Government.
