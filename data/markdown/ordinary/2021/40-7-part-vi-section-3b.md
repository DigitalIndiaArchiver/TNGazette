Elections to the Syndicate, the Senate and the Standing Committee of the Academic Council.                                   
(No.V.1/AC-Ele/Gazette/Sept.2021/341)
No. VI-3(b)/21/2021.                                 
It is hereby notiﬁ ed that the election to [i] THE SYNDICATE., [ii] THE SENATE and [iii] THE STANDING COMMITTEE 
OF THE ACADEMIC COUNCIL will be held under Section 18-1[a] - other Members [2], Section 14-1(a) - other 
Members [2] and Section 24 [k] of the Madras University Act, 1923 respectively.
[i] 
To the Syndicate - Section 18-1[a] - Other Members [2]
 
One Member to be elected by the Academic Council from among its members shall be teachers of 
aﬃ  liated colleges.
[ii] To the Senate - Section 14-1[a] - Other Members [2]
 
One Member to be elected by the Academic Council from among its members.
[iii] To the Standing Committee of the Academic Council - Section 24[k]
 
Two Members - Two shall be members of the syndicate to be elected by the Academic Council from among 
its members.
The above election will be conducted on the date of the meeting of the Academic Council scheduled to be held 
on Saturday, the 25th September 2021.
