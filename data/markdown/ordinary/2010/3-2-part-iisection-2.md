ORDER—No. 423, Higher Education (D2), 24th December 2009
In the Government Order first read above orders were
issued to take over the Management of Chikkaiah Naicker
College, Erode under Section 30(1) of the Tamil Nadu
Private Colleges (Regulation) Act, 1976 for a period of two
years with effect from 19-12-2002 and appointed the Regional
Joint Director of College Education, Coimbatore Region, as
caretaker to run the Management of the said College.
2. In the Government order second read above, the
Government have ordered the extension of take over of the
Management of the Chikkiah Naicker College, Erode for a
period of one year with effect from 19-12-2004 and also to
extend the term of the Special Officer appointed as per the
G.O. first read above to run the Management of the said
College.
3. In the G.Os. 3rd to 6th read above, the Government
sanctioned the further extension of the take over and
continuance of term of the caretaker year by year upto
18-12-2009. The present sanction expires on 18-12-2009.
4. In the letter seventh read above, the Director of
Collegiate Education has stated that the term of the period
of the Special Officer expires on 18-12-2009. The
W.P. No. 11019/2002 filed by Thiru R. Aswath, Secretary,
Chikkiah Naicker Educational Board, Erode and
W.P. No. 4399/2001 filed by Thiru T.A. Shanmugasundaram,
Secretary, Mahajana Educational Board, Erode to hand over
the Management of the said College was dismissed by the
High Court. Against these orders the petitioners filed Writ
Appeals in W.A. Nos. 1670-1672/2005. They were also
dismissed by the High Court of Madras by it’s order dated
12-1-2009. The Special Leave Petition Nos. 15102 and
12104 of 2009 filed by Thiru R. Aswath was also dismissed
by the Supreme Court. The Administration of this College
cannot be transferred to any other agency at present. The
Director of Collegiate Education has stated that taking over
of this College on a permanent basis could not be made
and there is no possibility to constitute an Educational Board
and hence the period of taking over of the Management and
term of the Special Officer may be extended for a further
period of one year with effect from 19-12-2009 as per
proviso to Section 30(3) of the Tamil Nadu Private Colleges
(Regulation) Act, 1976.
5. The Government, after careful consideration, have
decided that it is expedient in the interests of student’s
education to extend the take over of the Management of
Chikkiah Naicker College, Erode, Now, therefore, in
exercise of the powers conferred under the proviso to
sub-section (3) of Section 30 of the Tamil Nadu Private
Colleges (Regulation) Act, 1976 (President’s Act 19 of 1976),
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby extends the take over
of the management of the Chikkiah Naicker College, Erode
for a further period of one year with effect from 19-12-2009
to 18-12-2010 and appoints the Regional Joint Director of
Collegiate Education, Coimbatore as Care Taker to run the
management of the said College.
(By Order of the Governor)
K. GANESAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.

[G.O. Ms. No. 948, Home (Police XIX),

No. II(2)/HO/43/2010.—Whereas, complaints have been
received from a number of depositors that Light House
Group of Finance, namely Light House Enterprises, Light
House Associates, Light House Finance and Investments,
Light House Financiers and Light House Combines, Karur
a Financial Establishment, has defaulted the return of
deposits made by the depositors after maturity;
2. And, whereas, the Government are satisfied that the
said Financial Establishment is not likely to return the
deposits to the depositors and hence, the Government have
to protect the interests of such depositors;
3. And, whereas, the immovable properties specified in
the Schedule to this order belong to Tvl. V. Sridharan,
V. Sureshkumar, Sasikala Sureshkumar, K.P. Jeganathan
and P. Govindarajan, Partners of the said financial
institutions.
4. Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred
by Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Protection of Interests of
Depositors (in Financial Establishments) Act, 1997
(Tamil Nadu Act 44 of 1997) the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby makes an ad-interim order attaching the immovable
properties of the said Tvl. V. Sridharan, V. Sureshkumar,
Sasikala Sureshkumar, K.P. Jeganathan and P. Govindarajan,
Partners of Light House Group of Finance, namely Light
House Enterprises, Light House Associates, Light House
Finance and Investments, Light House Financiers and Light
House Combines, Karur as specified in the Schedule to this
order and transfers the control over the said immovable
properties to the Competent Authority, namely, the Competent
Authority and District Revenue Officer, Karur appointed under
the said Act, for the purpose.
5. The Competent Authority is requested to pursue further
action in accordance with the procedure laid down in
sub-sections (3) and (4) of Section 4 of the said Act and
also the formalities prescribed in the Tamil Nadu Protection
of Interests of Depositors (in Financial Establishments)
Rules, 1997.
6. The Special Public Prosecutor, Special Court for the
Tamil Nadu Protection of Interests of Depositors (in Financial
Establishments) Act, 1997 is requested to render necessary
assistance to the Competent Authority in filing the application
before the said Special Court.
