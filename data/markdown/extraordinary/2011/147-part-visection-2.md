No.VI(2)/287(c)/2011.
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 6-I and 58 of Chennai City Municipal Corporation Act, 1919 (Tamil Nadu
Act IV of 1919), and Section 10-D and Section 67 of Coimbatore City Municipal Corporation Act, 1981 (Tamil Nadu
Act XXV of 1981) read with Section 8 of the Salem City Municipal Corporation Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act XXIX of 1994) and
sub-section (1) of Section 43-B and Section 27 of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920)
and sub-rule (4) of rule 99 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities, Municipalities and Corporations
(Elections) Rules, 2006, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission notified the date as 31st May 2011 for conduct of meeting
for the election of Chairman, Vice-Chairman and Member/Chairman of Appointment Committee, Chairman of Contract
Committee, Chairman of Taxation Appeals Committee, Member of Town Planning and Development Committee, Member of
Health 
Committee 
and 
Chairman 
of 
Wards 
Committee 
and 
Chairman 
of 
Standing 
Committee,
as per the S.O. No. 8/2011/TNSEC/ME2, dated 19th May 2011, as published in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette
Extra-ordinary No.VI(2)/287(a)/2011 on 19th May 2011.
Whereas in the letter No. 11271/PR.1/2011-1, Rural Development and Panchayat Raj Department, dated 21st May 2011,
the Government have requested to postpone all indirect elections notified in the S.O. No. 8/2011/TNSEC/ME2,
dated 19th May 2011 of the Commission, till Ordinary elections 2011 in view of the short period available for the conduct of
Ordinary Elections due to be held in October 2011.
In view of the request received from the Government and in exercise of the power conferred under rule 115 of the
Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities, Municipalities and Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006 the
Tamil Nadu State Election Commission hereby orders the postponement of all elections notified in the aforesaid
Statutory Orders.
(By Order of the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission)
