Confirmation of variation to the approved Tiruppur Detailed Development Plan No. 13 of
Tiruppur Local Planning Area.
(Roc. No. 5638/2006/DP2.)
No. VI(1)/253/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 33(2) of Town and Country Planning Act, 1971, the Commissioner
of Town and Country Planning,  Chennai hereby confirms the following variation in T.S. No. 11/2, Block 4, Ward G of
Tiruppur Municipal Corporation for the conversion of Residential use zone into Commercial use to the Approved Tiruppur
Detailed Development Plan No. 13 of Tiruppur Local Planning Area. The said notification was published in the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette (Issue No. 15), Part VI—Section 1, page No. 192, dated 18th April 2007, Publication
No. VI(1)/254/2007.
Since no objections or suggestions have been received on this draft notification within the stipulated time the same
are hereby confirmed and ordered as below:
CONFIRMATIN OF VARIATION
1. Wherever the expression Map No. 4 and 5 DDP (CR) DTCP No. 3/99 occurs, the expression to read with
DDP (V) DTCP No. 6/2007 shall be added at the end. The variation in the Approved Detailed Development Plan
No. 13 of Tiruppur Local Planning Area is hereby confirmed.
2.
The above variation is made enforceable from the date of publication of this notification in the Tamil Nadu
Government Gazette.
Chennai-600 002,
ASHOK DONGRE,
3rd August 2009.
Commissioner of  Town and Country Planning.

1. The draft detailed development plan prepared by this Local Planning Authority of Tenkasi. Detailed Development
Plan No. 8 consented by the Commissioner of Town and Country Planning, Chennai-2, in his Proceedings
Roc. No. 20671/08/DPI, 30th March 2009, under Section 25 of the Town and Country Planning Act, 1971 for the area
described in the schedule is hereby published.
2. Any person affected by the detailed development plan or interested in the plan may before sixty days from the
date of intimation communicate in writing or represent in person to the Member- Secretary of the Local Planning Authority
any objection or suggestion relating thereto.
3. The detailed development plan with all its enclosures may be inspected free of cost during office hours at the
office of the Local Planning Authority. Copies of the detailed development plans are also available at the Office of Local
Planning Authority for sale at the rate of Rs. 250/- (Rupees two hundred and fifty only) for each copy.
