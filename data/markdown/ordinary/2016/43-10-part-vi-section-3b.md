Election [1] to the Syndicate  by the Senate from among its Members [2] to the Academic Council by the
Senate from among its Members [who are not engaged in Teaching]
(No.V.1/Senate-election/11-2016/422)
No.VI-3(b)/28/2016.
It is hereby notified that the election to [i] THE SYNDICATE and [ii] THE ACADEMIC COUNCIL will be held under
Section 18[a]-Other Members, Sub-Section [1] and Section 23[a]-Other Members, Sub-section [1] of the Madras University
Act, 1923 respectively.
[i]
TO THE SYNDICATE [Section18(a)- Other Members Sub-Section (1)] ONE MEMBER to be elected by
the Senate from among it members.
The newly elected members will hold office as Member of the Syndicate for the residual period upto 29-03-2018 or till
the date of cessation of his/her membership in the Senate/Academic Council whichever is earlier.
[ii]
TO THE ACADEMIC COUNCIL [Section 23(a)-Other Members Sub-Section(1)] FIVE MEMBERS to be
elected by the Senate from among its members who are not engaged in teaching.
The newly elected members will hold office as member of the Academic Council for a period of three years from the
date of receipt of nomination / the date of withdrawal/the date of election/the date of declaration of results whichever is applicable
for the election.
The above election will be conducted on the date of the meeting of the Senate scheduled to be held on SATURDAY,
the 5th November 2016.
