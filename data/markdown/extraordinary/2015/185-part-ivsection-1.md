Appropriation.
A Bill provide  for  the  withdrawal of certain sums from and  out  of the Consolidated  Fund
of  the  State  for  the services and purposes of a part of the financial year which commenced
on the 1st day of April 2015.
BE  it  enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the Sixty-sixth
Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
1. This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu Appropriation (Vote on Account)
(No.2) Act, 2015.
2. The State Government may  withdraw from and out  of  the Consolidated  Fund   of
the  State sums not exceeding one lakh thirteen thousand seven hundred and seventeen
crore twelve lakh sixty six thousand rupees, which shall be inclusive of the sum of ninety six
thousand one hundred and eighty four crore fifty three lakh thirty one thousand rupees
specified in section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Appropriation (Vote on Account) Act,2015 being
moneys required to meet—
(a) the  grants   made   in advance by   the   Tamil  Nadu  Legislative Assembly  for
a part of  the  financial year which commenced on the 1st day of April 2015, as  set
forth  in  column (3) of the Schedule; and
(b) the expenditure charged on the Consolidated  Fund  of  the State for the said part
of that  year, as set forth in column (4) of the Schedule.
3. The sums authorised to be withdrawn from and out  of  the Consolidated  Fund  under
section 2 shall be appropriated for the services and purposes expressed in the Schedule in
relation to that year.

Act 4 of 2015.
