CHENNAI CITY MUNICIPAL CORPORATION
NOTIFICATION OF RESERVATION OF WARDS TO SCHEDULED CASTE (GENERAL), SCHEDULED
CASTE (WOMEN) AND WOMEN (GENERAL)
[RD(Election) C.No.005392/2011]
NO.  VI(2)/445(d)/2011.
In exercise of the powers delegated by the Government under sub-sections (3), (4) and (5) of
Section 5 and Section 45 of the Chennai City Municipal Corporation Act, 1919 in G.O.Ms.No.133, Municipal
Administration and Water Supply Department, dated 6th June 1996 and in accordance with the Tamil Nadu Town
Panchayats, Municipalities and Corporation (Delimitation of Wards or Divisions and Reservation) Rules, 1996 issued
in G.O. Ms.No.136, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (Election), Department, Dated 11th June 1996,
G.O.Ms.No.85, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (Election) Department, dated 18th June 2001 and in
accordance with the G.O.Ms.No.75, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (Election) Department, Dated 1st
August 2006 issued in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette (No.221), dated  1st August 2006 and also in accordance
with G.O.Ms.No.124, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (Election) Department, dated 7th September 2011
issued in Tamil Nadu Government Gazette (No.311), dated 7th September 2011, the Commissioner, Corporation
of Chennai, hereby effects reservation of 58 Wards to Women (General) based on Census Population 2001 and
continue to reserve 26 seats to Scheduled Caste as per the last preceding  census, for the purpose of elections
to be held for the Corporation Council, notifies as follows:-
