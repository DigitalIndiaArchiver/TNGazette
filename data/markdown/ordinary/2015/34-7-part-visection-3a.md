GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI
Taking Over of the Islamic Cultural Association Pallivasal, Nagercoil, Agastheeswaram Taluk, Kanyakumari
District under the Direct Management
(Lr.Rc.No.1495/15/B1/KK)
No.VI-3(a)/99/2015.
In exercise of powers conferred as per the provisions under Section 65 of the wakf Act (as amended) 1995, the
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its Resolution in item No. 23/15 in Rc.No. 1495/15/B1/KK dated 28-05-2015 has ordered to take
over the management of Islamic Cultural Association Pallivasal, Nagercoil, Agastheeswaram Taluk, Kanyakumari District
under the Direct Control of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a period of one year from 28-05-2015 and to appoint the Wakf
Inspector, Tirunelveli as its Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001,
K. RASHEETH ALI,
19th August 2015.
District Revenue Officer/
Chief Executive Officer,
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
