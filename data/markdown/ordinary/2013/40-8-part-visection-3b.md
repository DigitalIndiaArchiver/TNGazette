BHARATHIDASAN UNIVERSITY, TIRUCHIRAPPALLI
Election to the Syndicate
(Ref. No. BDU/6486/A2/2013.)
No. VI-3(b)/32/2013.
Class II-Other Members sub-section (b) (2) of Section 24 under Chapter IV of the Bharathidasan University
Act, 1981-TWO Members elected by teachers of affiliated colleges, other than principals, from among themselves
who are members of the Senate in accordance with the system of proportional representation by means of the single
transferable vote.
Out of the above vacancies one member has already been elected whose tenure is upto 26-12-2014. Election is to
be conducted for one vacancy for the residual period upto 26-12-2014. This election is conducted for the casual vacancy.
Nominations are invited in the prescribed form which can be obtained form the Registrar i/c and Returning Officer
on a requisition and submission of a Demand Draft for Rs. 500/- (Five Hundred only) drawn in favour of
Bharathidasan University, Tiruchirappalli on or after the date of notification, i.e., 9th October 2013 with a stamped
self-addressed envelope to the value of Rs. 25/- (Rupees twenty five only) or in person.
Nomination paper shall be enclosed in an envelope superscribed as follows, as the case may be:—
“NOMINATION FOR ELECTION TO THE SYNDICATE”
The Members are requested to send their nomination by Registered Post (with Acknowledgement due) so as to reach the
Registrar i/c and Returning Officer within the date and hour fixed or delivered to the Registrar i/c and Returning Officer either in
person of by a messenger within the date and hour fixed.
Nomination papers that are not sent as stated above will be declared invalid.
