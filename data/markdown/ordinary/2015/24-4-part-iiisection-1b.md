RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND PANCHAYAT RAJ DEPARTMENT
Amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Panchayat Development Engineering Subordinate Service.
 [G.O. Ms. No. 49, Rural Development and Panchayat Raj (E3), 31st March 2015.]
No. SRO B-50/2015.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Panchayat
Development Engineering Subordinate Service. (Section 56 in Part III of Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1970).
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force with effect on and from 22nd day of July 2013.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules,-
(1) in rule 1,- for the expressions “Category 2. Road Inspector, Grade I” and “Category 3. Road Inspector,
Grade II” the following expression shall be substituted, namely:—
“Category 2. Road Inspector”;
(2) in rule 2, in THE TABLE, for the expressions “Road Inspector Grade I” and “Road Inspector Grade II” in
Column (1) and the corresponding entries in column (2) thereof, the following expression and entry shall, respectively, be
substituted, namely:—
“Road Inspector” and “Direct recruitment”;
(3) in rule 4,-
(i) in sub-rule (a), for the expression “Road Inspector Grade II”, the expression “Road Inspector” shall be
substituted;
(ii) in sub-rule (b), in THE TABLE,-
   (a) in column (3), against the expression “Overseer” in column (1) and against the entry “Promotion” in column
(2) thereof, in item (2), for the expression “Road Inspector Grade I for a period of not less than five years” the
expression “Road Inspector for a period of not less than seven years” shall be substituted;
   (b) for the expressions “Road Inspector Grade I” and “Road Inspector Grade II” in column (1) and the entries
relating thereto in columns (2) and (3) thereof, the following expression and entries shall, respectively, be substituted,
namely:—
“Road Inspector
Direct Recruitment
Must possess an I.T.I.
Certificate in Civil
Draughtsman from a
Government recognised Institute”;
(4) in rule 8, in sub-rule, (a), for the expression “Road Inspector, Grade I or Road Inspector, Grade II”, the expression
“Road Inspector” shall be substituted; and
(5) in rule 10, for the expression “Road Inspector, Grade-II”, the expression “Road Inspector” shall be substituted.
GAGANDEEP SINGH BEDI,
 Secretary to Government.
