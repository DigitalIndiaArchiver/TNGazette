No. I/PLAR/11(b-1)/2015.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (3) of Section15 of the Right to Information Act, 2005
(Central Act 22 of 2005), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints Thiru K. Ramanujam, IPS (Retd)
as State Chief Information Commissioner in the Tamil Nadu Information Commission.
The State Chief Information Commissioner shall hold office for a term of five years from the date on which
he enters upon his office or the date on which he attains the age of sixty-five years, whichever is earlier.

No. I/PLAR/11(b-2)/2015.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (3) of Section 15 of the Right to Information Act, 2005
(Central Act 22 of 2005), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints Thiru R. Dakshinamurthy and
Thiru G. Murugan as State Information Commissioners in the Tamil Nadu Information Commission.
The State Information Commissioners shall hold office for a term of five years from the date on which they
enter upon their office or the date on which they attain the age of sixty-five years, whichever is earlier.
P.W.C. DAVIDAR,
