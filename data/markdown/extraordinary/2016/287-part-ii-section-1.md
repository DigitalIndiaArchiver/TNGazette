Under the Explanation to Section 25 of the Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881 (Central
Act XXVI of 1881) read with Notification of the Government of India, Ministry of Home Affairs
No. 20-25-26, Public-1, dated 8th June 1957 the Government of Tamil Nadu hereby declares that
Tuesday the 13th December 2016 would be a Public Holiday on account of Milad-un-Nabi instead of
12th December 2016.
2. The above Public Holiday notified shall also apply to all State Government Undertakings/
Corporations / Boards, etc.
P. RAMA MOHANA RAO,
