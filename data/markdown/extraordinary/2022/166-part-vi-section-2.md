on the file of the Hon’ble High Court of Madrass
No. VI(2)/31(c)/2022.
WHEREAS the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission has fixed 26-03-2022 as the date for convening a meeting to 
elect the Chairman and Vice Chairman of Aduthurai Town Panchayat of Thanjavur District vide reference first read above;
WHEREAS, a writ petition in W.P.No. 5433/2022 has been filed by Thiru Ma.Ka. Station, 12th Ward Member of 
Aduthurai Town Panchayat along with 7 other ward members of that town panchayat before the Hobn’ble High Court of 
Madras and the case has been heard by the Hon’ble High Court on 9-3-20222, 10-3-2022, 11-03-2022 and 15-3-2022. 
On 11-3-2022, the Honble High Court directed to produce the CCTV footage of the day of election (4-3-2022) on the 
next date of hearing ie. 15-3-2022;
WHEREAS, the case has been taken for futhher hearing on 15-3-2022, he Retainer of this Commission, in the 
reference second read above, stated that the Hon’ble High Court of Madras, after viewing the contents of the CCTV 
footage of election meeting held on 4-3-2022, expressed theat the Returning Offier could have exercised due care 
in holding the indirect elections and directed the Commission to revisit the decision of the Commission to 
hold the indirect election on 26-3-2022 for Aduthurai Town Panchayat and to fix the earliest possible date for 
holding the indirect elections for convening a meeting to elect the Chairman and Vice Chairman of Aduthurai 
Town Panchayat of Thanjavur District, and


2	
TAMIL  NADU  GOVERNMENT  GAZETTE  EXTRAORDINARY
Whereas in view of the above observation of the Hon’ble High Court of Madras in W.P. No. 5433/2022, dated 
15-3-2022, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission has decided to advance the date already fixed for the meeting 
to elect the Chairman and Vice Chairman of Aduthurai Town Panchayat of Thanjavur District.
Now, Therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (1) of section 43-B and section 27 of the 
Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920) and sub-rule (3) of rule 99 and rule 115 of 
the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Municipalities and Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006, the Tamil Nadu State Election 
Commission hereby advance the date already fixed by superseding the earlier notification first read above and hereby 
fixes 23-3-2022 as the date for convening a meeting to elect the Chairman and Vice Chairman of Aduthurai Town 
Panchayat of Thanjavur District in accordance with the following programme:-
	
1.
Last date for service of notice for the meetings of 
election of Chairman and Vice Chairman
15-03-2022
2.
Statutory Interval
16-03-2022 to 22-03-2022
3.
Date for conduct of meeting for the election of 
Chairman and Vice Chairman
23-03-2022
