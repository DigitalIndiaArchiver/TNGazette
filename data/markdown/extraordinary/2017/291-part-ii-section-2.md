The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed 
that the lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below have to 
be acquired for the Industrial Purpose for the formation 
of New Railway Road of Thoothukudi – Madurai 
(Via Aruppukottai) situated in Valasamuthiram Village of 
Ottapidaram Taluk in the District of Thoothukudi, and 
it has already been decided that the entire amount of 
compensation is to be awarded for the lands to be paid 
out of the funds controlled or managed by Divisional 
Engineer Southern Railway. The following Notice 
is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the 
Tamil Nadu Acquisition of land for the Industrial purposes 
Act 1997.
[1]
NOTICE
 
 Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the 
Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes
Act 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999). The Government 
of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands speciﬁ ed in 
the Schedule below measuring 12.19.50 Hectares to 
the same, a little more or less needed for Industrial 
Purpose for the formation of New Railway Road of 
Thoothukudi – Madurai (Via Aruppukottai) situated in 
Valasamuthiram Village of Ottapidaram Taluk in the 
District of Thoothukudi.
 
 The Plan of lands are kept in the ofﬁ ce of the 
Collector, Thoothukudi and Revenue Divisional Ofﬁ cer, 
Kovilpatti may be inspected at any time during ofﬁ ce 
hours.
