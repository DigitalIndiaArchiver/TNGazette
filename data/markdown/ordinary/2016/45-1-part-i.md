to the Puducherry Judicial Service - Orders - Issued.]
The following Government Order is Published:—
[G.O. (D). No. 381, Public (Special-A), 26th October 2016,
Iyappasi 10, Thunmugi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2047.]
READ:
From the Registrar General, High Court, Madras,
D.O. Letter No.72794-A/2016-B2, dated 07-10-2016.
Order: No. 381, Public (Special-A), 26th October 2016.
The Government have considered the recommendation
of the High Court, Madras to depute Thirumathi
S. Ramathilagam, District Judge of Tamil Nadu State Judicial
Service, functioning as Principal District Judge, Namakkal to
Puducherry Judicial Service for being Posted as Chief Judge,
Pondicherry in the existing vacancy.
2. The Government accordingly direct that Thirumathi
S. Ramathilagam, District Judge of Tamil Nadu State Judicial
Service, functioning as Principal District Judge, Namakkal
be deputed to Puducherry Judicial Service for being Posted
as Chief Judge, Pondicherry in the existing vacancy.
(By Order of the Governor)
RAMA MOHANA RAO,
Chief Secretary.
