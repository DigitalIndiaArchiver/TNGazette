OF ELECTION EXPENSES - DIRECTIONS ISSUED.]
(S.O. No.21/2019/TNSEC/EE-2)
Rඍඉඌ:
(S.O. No. 20/2016/TNSEC/EE, Dated the 17th September, 2016.)
No. VI(2)/98(a-2)/2019.
In pursuance of sub-rule (3) of rule 120 of the Tamil Nadu Panchayats (Elections) Rules, 1995 and sub-rule (3) 
of rule 116 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities, Municipalities and Corporations (Elections) 
Rules, 2006 and in supersession of the Notiﬁ cation No. VI(2)/120(e-2)/2016, published as Part VI - Section 2 of the 
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary No. 221, dated 19-9-2016 at Page No. 3, the Tamil Nadu State Election 
Commission, hereby directs that all the contesting candidates in the elections listed out in column (2) of the Table below 
shall lodge a true copy of their accounts of election expenses kept by them or by their respective election agent or agents, 
under sub-rule (1) of rule 120 and sub-rule (1) of rule 116 respectively of the said Rules in the format prescribed in 
S.O.No. 20/2019/TNSEC/EE2, dated 18-10-2019 in accordance with the provisions of sub-rule (2) of rule 120 and
sub-rule (2) of rule 116 of the said Rules respectively with the oﬃ  cers mentioned in the corresponding entry in column 
(3) of the Table, within one month from the date of declaration of the result of the said elections.
