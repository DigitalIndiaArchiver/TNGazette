11th October 2018, Purattasi 25, Vilambi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2049.]

section 19 of the Haj Committee Act, 2002 (Central Act 35 of 2002), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, 
hereby nominates Thiru. A. Anwhar Raajhaa, Member of Parliament Chairperson of the Tamil Nadu 
Waqf Board  as a Member of the Tamil Nadu State Haj Committee.
