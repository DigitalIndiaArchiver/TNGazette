ELECTION TO THE SYNDICATE
(Ref. No. 12396/A2/2022)
No. VI-3(b)/28/2022.
Class II - Other Members - Sub-Sec. (b) (2) of Section 24 under Chapter IV of the Bharathidasan University Act, 
1981 - Two Members elected by teachers of aﬃ  liated colleges, other than the Principals, from among themselves, who 
are the members of the Senate in accordance with the system of proportional representation by means of a single 
transferable vote. 
FILLING OF CASUAL VACANCY: 
Section 47 under Chapter X of Bharathidasan University Act, 1981 (Tamil Nadu Act 2 of 1982). 
The total number of vacancy in the Syndicate is one (Residual Vacancy). The membership of the another 
member in the Syndicate continues, as on date. 
The tenure of oﬃ  ce is upto 22.01.2024 from the date of declaration of the result of the election. 
In this connection, nomination is invited in the prescribed form which can be obtained from the Registrar and 
Returning Oﬃ  cer on a requisition and submission of a Demand Draft for Rs.590/- (Including GST - Five Hundred 
and Ninety only) drawn in favour of Bharathidasan University, Tiruchirappalli on or after the date of notiﬁ cation, 
i.e. 30.09.2022 with a stamped self-addressed envelope to the value of Rs.25/- (Rupees twenty ﬁ ve only) or in person. 
Nomination paper shall be enclosed in an envelope superscribed as follows, as the case may be:- 
"NOMINATION FOR ELECTION TO THE SYNDICATE - BY THE TEACHERS OF AFFILIATED COLLEGES OTHER 
THAN PRINCIPALS FROM AMONG THEMSELVES \ WHO ARE MEMBERS OF THE SENATE"
The Members are requested to send their nomination by Registered Post (with Acknowledgement due) so as to 
reach the Registrar and Returning Oﬃ  cer within the date and hour ﬁ xed or delivered to the Registrar and Returning 
Oﬃ  cer either in person or by a messenger within the date and hour ﬁ xed. 
Nomination papers that are not sent as stated above will be declared invalid.
