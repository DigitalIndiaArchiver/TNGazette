NOTIFICATIONS BY THE ELECTION COMMISSION OF INDIA
APPOINTMENT OF DATES FOR VARIOUS STAGES AND FIXATION OF HOURS OF POLL FOR BYE-ELECTION TO
COUNCIL OF STATES TO FILL UP THE VACANCY CAUSED DUE TO DISQUALIFICATION VACANT FROM 17-4-2014.
No. SRO G-23/2014.
The following Notification of the Election Commission of India, New Delhi,  dated  16th June 2014 [Jyaishtha 26, 1936 (Saka)]
is republished:—
No. 100/CS-TN/12/2014.—(1), Whereas the seat of Shri T.M. Selvaganapathi in the Council of States, elected by the elected
members of the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu, has become vacant on 17th April, 2014 in terms of sub-clause
(a) of clause (3) of Article 101 of the Constitution by the reason of his incurring disqualification under
Section 8 of the Representation of the People Act, 1951 read with sub-clause (e) of clause (1) of Article 102 of the
Constitution, before the expiration of his term of office which was up to and including the 29th June, 2016; and
Whereas, a bye-election is to be held for the purpose of filling the vacancy so caused;
Now, therefore, in pursuance of Section 147, sub-section (1) of Section 39 and Section 56 of the Representation of the
people Act, 1951 (43 of 1951), the Election Commission of India hereby—
(A) calls upon the elected members of the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu to elect, before 5th July 2014
(Saturday), and in accordance with the provisions of the said Act and of the rules and orders made thereunder, a person
for the purpose of filling the said vacancy in the Council of States;
(B) appoints, with respect to the said election;
(a) the 23rd June, 2014 (Monday), as the last date for making nominations;
(b) the 24th June, 2014 (Tuesday) as the date for the scrutiny of the nominations;
(c) the 26th June 2014 (Thursday), as the last date for the withdrawal of candidatures;
(d) the 3rd July 2014 (Thursday), as the date on which a poll shall, if necessary, be taken; and
(e) the 5th July, 2014 (Saturday), as the date before which the election shall be completed; and
