No. II(2)/IND/987(a-1)/2013.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that the lands specified in the schedule
below have to be acquired for Industrial Purpose for the establishment of an Industry for the production
of Multilayer Double Coated Paper Board by Tamil Nadu Newsprint and Papers Limited and it having
already been decided that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be
paid out of the funds controlled or managed by Tamil Nadu Newsprint and Papers Limited.  The
following notice is issued under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land
for Industrial Purpose Act, 1997.
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purpose
Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands specified
in the Schedule below and measuring 15.34.5 hectares (or) 37.91 acres of dry lands be the same
a little more or less needed for Industrial purposes, to wit, for the establishment of an Industry for
the production of Multilayer Double Coated Paper Board by Tamil Nadu Newsprint and Papers Limited.
The plan of the lands are kept in the office of the Special Deputy Collector / Land Acquisition,
Manapparai and may be inspected at any time during office hours.
