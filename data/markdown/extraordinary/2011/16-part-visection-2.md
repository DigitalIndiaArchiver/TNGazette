In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 27, sub-section (1) of Section 43-B  of the Tamil Nadu District
Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920), Section 67 of Coimbatore City Municipal Corporation Act, 1981
(TN Act XXV of 1981) and sub-rule (4) of rule 99 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities,
Municipalities and Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission hereby fixes
28th January 2011 as the date for convening a meeting to elect the Chairman, Vice-Chairman, Member of Taxation Appeals
Committee and Member of Town Planning Committee as the case may be, shown in the Annexure, in accordance with the
following programme:—
