LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT DEPARTMENT
FIXATION OF CERTAIN PERCENTAGE OF TOTAL ESTIMATE COST OF BUILDING CONSTRUCTION WORK AS
CONTRIBUTION TO THE FUND FOR BENEFIT OF MANUAL WORKERS EMPLOYED IN BUILDING WORKS.
[G.O. Ms. No. 295, Labour and Employment (I2), 17th  December 2013, ñ£˜èN 2, Müò, F¼õœÀõ˜ Ý‡´-2044.]
No. II(2)/LE/934(h)/2013.
Under sub-section (1) of Section 8-A of the Tamil Nadu Manual Workers (Regulation of Employment and Conditions of Work)
Act, 1982  (Tamil Nadu Act 33 of 1982), the Governor of Tamil Nadu, in supersession of the Labour and Employment Department
Notification No.II(2)/LE/1420(b-1)/97, published at page 1 of Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary,
dated the 2nd July 1997, hereby fixes one per cent of total estimated cost of the building or construction work proposed to be
constructed, as contribution to the fund constituted for the benefit of the manual workers in the employment in construction or
maintenance of dams, bridges, roads or in any building operations, under a scheme framed under Section 3 of the said Act.
MOHAN PYARE,
Principal Secretary to Government.
