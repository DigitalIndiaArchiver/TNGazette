In exercise of the powers conferred by section 23-A read with Clause (a) of section 2  of the 
Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor 
of Tamil Nadu hereby  appoints the Commissioner of  Land  Administration, Chennai to perform the 
functions of the Collector under that Act and authorise the Commissioner of Land Administration to exercise 
the powers of the Government vested in them under sub-sections (1) and (3) of section 3 of the said 
Act.
