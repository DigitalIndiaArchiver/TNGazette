 TAMIL NADU VETERINARY COUNCIL
(Statutory Body)
(Affiliated to the Veterinary Council of India)
Appointment of dates for holding Election of Four Members to the Tamil Nadu Veterinary Council.
(Roc. No.1/09/Election)
No. VI-3(b)/10/2009.
In pursuance of sub-rule 8 under Rule 3 Part II of the Tamil Nadu State Veterinary Council Rules, 2004, the undersigned
hereby appoints the following dates for holding the election of the four members from amongst the persons
enrolled in the State Veterinary Register of the Tamil Nadu Veterinary Council under Section 32(1)(a) of the Indian
Veterinary Council Act, 1984 (52 of 1984) in accordance with the provisions of the said Rules:—
(a) The date of making Nomination
:
17-06-2009 Wednesday 10.00 A.M. to 3.00 P.M.
(b) Last date for making Nomination
:
23-06-2009 Tuesday 3.00 P.M.
(c) The time and the date for Scrutiny
:
29-06-2009 Monday 10.00 A.M. at Council Office.
of the Nomination
(d) The last date for withdrawal of candidature
:
01-07-2009 Wednesday 3.00 P.M.
(e) The date and time on which poll,
:
19-08-2009 Wednesday 10.00 A.M. to 5.00 P.M. at Council Office.
if necessary be taken
(f)
The date, time and place for counting of
:
20-08-2009 Thursday 10.00 A.M. onwards at Council Office.
votes and for declaration of results
Nominations from the eligible candidates are hereby invited for election of 4 (four) members to the Tamil Nadu Veterinary
Council in the Form III. Nomination papers should be sent by Registered post with acknowledgement due or deliver in person
to Dr. K. Sivaramalingam, Returning officer, Office of the Tamil Nadu Veterinary Council No. 2, Pasumpon Muthuramalingam
Salai, Nandanam, Chennai-600 035 not later than 3.00 P.M. on 23rd June 2009. Further details of election process can be
seen under sub-rule 8 to 20 of Rule 3 under Part II of the Tamil Nadu State Veterinary Council Rules 2004.
Nandanam,
DR. K. SIVARAMALINGAM,
Chennai-600 035,
