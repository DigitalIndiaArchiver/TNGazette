VARIATIONS
In the said Master Plan in the “LAND USE SCHEDULE” under the heading “COIMBATORE LOCAL PLANNING
AREA” under the sub-heading (b) Non-Notified Detailed Development Plan Area, Chettipalayam Village.
(i) Against the entry, “AGRICULTURAL (AG-35)” for the expression,” 510 to 528 (except 521/1), 538 to 543 (except
542, 543), 546 to 552 (except 546, 547, 548, 549/1B, 549/2pt, 3, 550/2), 576 to 591 (except 578/2, 582/5)" shall be
substituted.
(ii) Against the entry, “MIXED RESIDENTIAL (MR.16)” the expression 521/1 shall be added before the entry “531pt”
and 542, 543, 546, 547, 548, 549/1B, 549/2pt, 549/3, 550/2, 578/2, 582/5 shall be added after the entry 531pt.

Development (UD I) Department, dated 02.09.2008, as Housing and Urban Development Department Notification in
No.266, Part II - Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the 2nd September 2008.
