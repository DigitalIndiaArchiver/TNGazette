JUDICIAL NOTIFICATIONS
Constitution of Sub-Court at Melur in Madurai District.
(Roc. No. 1993/2011/G/Judn)
No.VI(1)/277/2017.
“In exercise of the powers conferred under the first proviso to Section 13 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873
(Central Act III of 1873), the High Court, Madras hereby directs that appeals from the decrees or orders of the District
Munsif Court, Melur be preferred in the Court of Subordinate Judge, Melur with effect on and from the date on which
the Subordinate Judge, Melur assumes charge of that Court.”
High Court, Madras,
R. SAKTHIVEL,
