HOME DEPARTMENT
Amendment to Rules for the Prisoners' Canteens in Part XI of Volume III of Tamil Nadu Prison Manual.
[G.O.Ms.No. 917, Home (Prison-V), 23rd October 2009.]
No. SRO A-40/2009.—The following amendment is made to the Rules for the Prisoners' Canteens in Part XI of
Volume III of Tamil Nadu Prison Manual:—
AMENDMENT
In the said Rules, for rule 4, the following rule shall be substituted, namely:—
“4. Limits for purchase of articles by prisoners:—
Every 
'B' 
class 
convicted 
prisoner, 
ordinary 
remand 
and 
undertrial 
prisoner 
and 
ordinary
detenu 
detained 
under 
various 
detention 
laws 
may 
purchase 
articles 
worth 
not 
more 
than
Rs. 225 (Rupees Two hundred and twenty five only) in a week. Every 'A' class prisoner, special class remand and
undertrial prisoner and special class detenu detained under various detention laws and prisoner assisting in
canteen may purchase articles worth not more than Rs. 375 (Rupees Three hundred and seventy five only) in a week.
The purchase shall be either from their prisoner cash property or credit”.
D. JOTHI JAGARAJAN,
Secretary to Government.
