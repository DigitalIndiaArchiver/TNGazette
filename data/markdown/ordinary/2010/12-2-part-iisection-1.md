CO-OPERATION, FOOD AND CONSUMER PROTECTION DEPARTMENT
Solvent, Raffinate and Slop (Acquisition Sale, Storage and Prevention of use in Automobiles)
Amendment Order, 2009.
[G.O. Ms. No. 29, Co-operation, Food and Consumer Protection (C1), 12th March 2010.]
No. II(1)/CFCP/5/2010.—The following order of the Gazette of India, Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas, New Delhi,
dated the 30th July, 2009 is republished:—
G.S.R. 561(E).—In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 3 of the Essential Commodities Act, 1955 (10 of 1955),
the Central Government hereby makes the following order, further to amend the Solvent, Raffinate and Slop (Acquisition
Sale, Storage and Prevention of use in Automobiles) Order, 2000, namely:—
1. (1) This order may be called to Solvent, Raffinate and Slop (Acquisition Sale, Storage and Prevention of use
in Automobiles) Amendment Order, 2009.
  (2) It shall come into force on the date of its publication in the Official Gazette.
2.
In the Schedule to the Solvent, Raffinate and Slop (Acquisition Sale, Storage and Prevention of use in
Automobiles) Order, 2000 after serial number 14 and entry relating thereto, the following shall be inserted, namely:—
“(15) Mixed Fuel Oil (MFO)”
[F.No. P.11013/1/2009-Dist.]
APURVACHANDRA,
Joint Secretary to Government of India.
(Republished by Order of the Governor)
K. SHANMUGAM,
Principal Secretary to Government.
[G.O. Ms. No. 29, Co-operation, Food and Consumer Protection (C1), 12th March 2010.]
No. II(1)/CFCP/6/2010.—The following order of the Gazette of India, Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas, New Delhi,
dated the 19th November, 2009 is republished:—
G.S.R. 835(E).—In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 3 of the Essential Commodities Act, 1955 (10 of 1955),
the Central Government hereby makes the following order, further to amend the Solvent, Raffinate and Slop (Acquisition
Sale, Storage and Prevention of use in Automobiles) Order, 2000, namely:—
1. (1) This order may be called the Solvent, Raffinate and Slop (Acquisition Sale, Storage and Prevention of use
in Automobiles), Amendment Order, 2009.
  (2) It shall come into force on the date of its publication in the Official Gazette.
2.
In the Solvent, Raffinate and Slop (Acquisition Sale, Storage and Prevention of use in Automobiles)
Order, 2000, in the Schedule, serial number 14 and the entry relating thereto, shall be omitted.
[F.No. P.11013/3/2007-Dist.]
APURVACHANDRA,
Joint Secretary to Government of India.
(Republished by Order of the Governor)
K. SHANMUGAM,
Principal Secretary to Government.
