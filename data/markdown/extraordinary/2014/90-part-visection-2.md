In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (1) of Section 6-I and Section 58 of the Chennai City Municipal
Corporation Act, 1919 (Tamil Nadu Act IV of 1919), sub-section (1) of Section 10-D and Section 65 of the Madurai City Municipal
Corporation Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act XV of 1971), sub-section (1) of Section 10-D and Section 67 of the Coimbatore City
Municipal Corporation Act, 1981 (Tamil Nadu Act 25 of 1981) read with Section 8 of the Thoothukudi City Municipal Corporation
Act, 2008 (Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 2008), sub-section (1) of Section 43-B and Section 27 of the Tamil Nadu District MunicipalitiesAct,
1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920) and sub-rule (4) of Rule 99 of the Tamil Nadu Town Panchayats, Third Grade Municipalities,
Municipalities and Corporations (Elections) Rules, 2006, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission hereby fixes 18-5-2014 as
