[G.O. Ms. No. 244, Municipal Administration and Water Supply (Election), 15th October 2010.]

Corporation Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 27 of 1994), the Governor of Tamil Nadu after consultation with the Municipal
Corporation of Tiruchirappalli, proposes to alter the limits of the city of Tiruchirappalli so as to include therein the
