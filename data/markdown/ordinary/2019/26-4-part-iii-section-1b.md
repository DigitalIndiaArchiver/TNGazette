Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following Rules:- 
2. The rules hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force  on and from the 6th September 2006. 
Rules

Service shall  apply to the holders of the temporary post of Art Gallery Demonstrator  sanctioned from time to time, in the

in the Tamil Nadu General Subordinate Service shall apply to the  holders of the aforesaid post.-
