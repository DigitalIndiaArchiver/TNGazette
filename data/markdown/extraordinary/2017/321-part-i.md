WARRANT OF APPOINTMENT OF GOVERNOR
[G.O. Ms. No. 841, Public (Special.B), 06th October 2017, Purattasi 20, Hevilambi,
Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2048.]
No. I/PUSP/20(a-1)/2017.
By virtue of the power vested in me by Article 155 of the Constitution of India, I, Ram Nath Kovind,
President of India, hereby appoint Shri Banwarilal Purohit, to be the Governor of Tamil Nadu with effect from the date
he assumes charge of his office.
Given at Rashtrapati Bhavan, New Delhi, this twenty- ninth day of September in the year two thousand seventeen
(07th Asvina, 1939 Saka), in the sixty-eighth year of the Republic of India.
RAM  NATH  KOVIND,
President of India.
GIRIJA VAIDYANATHAN,
Chief Secretary.
PROCLAMATION OF ASSUMPTION OF OFFICE OF THE GOVERNOR
[G.O. Ms. No. 842, Public (Special.B), 06th October 2017, Purattasi 20, Hevilambi,
Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2048.]
PROCLAMATION
No. I/PUSP/20(a-2)/2017.
Whereas  Thiru   Ch.   Vidyasagar   Rao,   Governor   of Tamil Nadu has on the 5th October, 2017 demitted the Office
of the Governor of Tamil Nadu and Whereas the President of India has appointed Thiru Banwarilal Purohit,
as Governor of Tamil Nadu in the place of the said Thiru Ch. Vidyasagar  Rao.
It is hereby notified that Thiru Banwarilal Purohit, has entered upon his Office on 06th October 2017.
GIRIJA VAIDYANATHAN,
Chief Secretary.
