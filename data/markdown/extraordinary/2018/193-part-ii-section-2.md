RECONSTITUTION OF CERTAIN PERSON AS MEMBER OF THE TAMIL NADU STATE 
HAJ COMMITTEE UNDER THE HAJ COMMITTEE ACT.
[G.O. (D). No. 31, Backward Classes, Most Backward Classes and Minorities Welfare (S1),
29th May 2018, Vaikasi 15, Vilambi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2049.]
No. II(2)/BCMBCMW/481(h)/2018.
  In exercise of powers conferred by clause (iv) of sub-section (1) of Section 18 and Section 19 read with
sub-section (1) of Section 26 of the Haj Committee Act, 2002 (Central Act 35 of 2002), the Governor of 
Tamil Nadu hereby nominates Dr. Haja K. Majeed as a member of the Tamil Nadu State Haj Committee in the 
vacancy caused due to the resignation of Thiru M. Hameed.
 
 
A. KARTHIK,
Secretary to Government.
