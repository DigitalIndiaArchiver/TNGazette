GENERAL  NOTIFICATIONS
Winding up of the affairs of certain Co-operative  Societies in certain districts and Appointment of
Official Liquidators
IND No. 1715,  Poondi Puthumaimatha  Coir Indl. Co-operative Society Limited, Thanjavur District.
(Rc. No. 24358/ICH/2010)
No. VI (1)/432/2013.
"Under the powers conferred on the Registrar of Industrial Co-operatives under Section 137(2)(b) of the Tamil Nadu
Co-operative Societies Act, 1983 (Act 30 of 1983), the activities  of the Poondi Puthumaimatha Coir Indl. Co-operative
Society Ltd No. IND 1715, Thanjavur District have been ordered to be wound up with immediate effect by the Principal
Secretary/Industries Commissioner and Director of Industries and Commerce and the Registrar of Industrial
Co-operatives, Chennai-28 vide Proc. No. 24358/ICH/2010 dated 30th October 2013."
Under Section 138 (1) of the same Act, Assistant Director (Indl. Co-ops) District Industries Centre, Thanjavur has
also been appointed as Official Liquidator of the said Society for the purpose of liquidation.
IND No. 15/2007, Dr. Kalaingar Auto Drivers Industrial Co-operative Society Ltd., Tiruppur District.
(Rc. No. 6550/ICF/2012)
No. VI (1)/433/2013.
"Under the powers conferred on the Registrar of Industrial Co-operatives under Section 137(2)(b) of the Tamil Nadu
Co-operative Societies Act, 1983 (Act 30 of 1983), the activities  of Dr. Kalaingar Auto Drivers Industrial Co-operative
Society Ltd., No. IND 15/2007, Tiruppur have been ordered to be wound up with immediate effect by the Principal Secretary/
Industries Commissioner and Director of Industries and Commerce and the Registrar of Industrial Co-operatives,
Mandaivelipakkam, Raja Annamalaipuram, Chennai-28 vide Proc. No. 6550/ICF/2012 dated 30th October 2013."
Under Section 138 (1) of the same Act, Assistant Director (Indl. Co-ops) District Industries Centre, Tiruppur is
appointed as Official Liquidator of the said Society for the purpose of liquidation.
Kindly acknowledge receipt of this communication early.
Chennai-600 028,
SWARAN SINGH,
30th October 2013.
Principal Secretary/Industries Commissioner and
Director of Industries and Commerce.
 No. EE 138 Eral Murugan Sengunthar Weavers Co-operative Production and Sale Society Ltd, Eral,
Srivaikundam Taluk, Thoothukudi District.
(Rc. No. 8104/2011/E)
No. VI (1)/434/2013.
The affairs of the Eral Murugan Sengunthar Weavers Co-operative Production and Sale Society Ltd No. EE. 138,
Eral in Srivaikundam Taluk, Thoothukudi District is ordered to be wound up under Section 137 (2) (b) of the Tamil
Nadu Co-operative Societies Act 30 of 1983, and the Handloom Officer (Statutory and Liquidation) of the Office of the
Assistant Director of Handlooms and Textiles, Tirunelveli is appointed as its Official Liquidator under Section 138 of
the same Act with effect from 25th October 2013.
Tirunelveli,
R. THINAKARAN,
25th October 2013.
Assistant Director of Handlooms and Textiles.
