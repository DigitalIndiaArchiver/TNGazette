TAMIL NADU STATE LEGAL SERVICES AUTHORITY, CHENNAI.
Constitution of Taluk Legal Services Committee at Alangulam in Tirunelveli District and Appointment of
Judicial Magistrate, Alangulam as Ex-officio Chairman of Alangulam Taluk Legal Services Committee.
(TNSLSA No. 240/E/2017)
No.VI(2)/89/2017.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 11 (A) (1) of the Legal Services Authorities Act 1987, (Central Act 39/1987)
read with Rule 3(a) of the Tamil Nadu Taluk Legal Services Committee Rules, 1997, the Tamil Nadu State Legal Services
Authority hereby constitutes a Taluk Legal Services Committee at Alangulam in Tirunelveli District and appoints the Judicial
Magistrate, Alangulam as ex-officio Chairman, Alangulam Taluk Legal Services Committee to exercise the powers and perform
the functions conferred on, or assigned to the Taluk Legal Services Committee under the Act.
High Court Campus,
A. NAZIR AHAMED,
Chennai-600 104,
District Judge/Member Secretary.
5th June 2017.
