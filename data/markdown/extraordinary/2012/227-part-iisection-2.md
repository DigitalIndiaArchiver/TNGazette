TRANSPORT DEPARTMENT
ACQUISITION OF LANDS
[G.O. Ms. No. 140, Transport (I.1), 3rd August 2012.]
No. II(2)/TRA/454(d)/2012.
Whereas, in exercise of the powers conferred by clause
(1) of the Article 258 of the Constitution of India, the
President of India, has, in the Ministry of Food, Agriculture,
Community Development and Co-operation (Department of
Agriculture) Notification No. 4(1)/65, General-II, dated
the 23rd April 1966, entrusted to the Government of
Tamil Nadu with their consent, the functions of the Central
Government under the Land Acquisition Act, 1894 (Central
Act I of 1894), in relation to Acquisition of Lands for the
purpose for  the Union in this State;
And,  whereas, it has already been decided that the
Compensation shall be paid from the public revenues;
Now, therefore in exercise of the powers conferred by
Section 6 of the said Act, the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby declares that the Land specified in the Schedule
below and measuring 0.0737 Sq.m. of Rasipuram Town,
Rasipuram Taluk, Namakkal District, be the same a little
more or less are needed for public purpose for provide
ROB and approach road due to the formation of
Salem-Karur Broad Gauge Railwayline Project.
A  plan of the Lands is kept in the Office of Special
Tahsildar (L.A.), Salem-Karur Broad Gauge Railwayline
Project, Namakkal and may be inspected at any time during
the Office Hours.
