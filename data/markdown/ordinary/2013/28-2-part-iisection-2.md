Judicial Magistrate, Coimbatore as referred charge sheet
No. 2/2003.

Offences Wing-II has therefore requested the Government to
cancel the orders issued in G.O. Ms. No. 189, Home dated

to accept the proposal of the Additional Director General
of Police accordingly the said Government Order in
G.O. Ms. No. 189, Home dated 8-03-2002 is hereby cancelled.
