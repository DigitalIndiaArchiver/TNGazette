Tamil Nadu Act
2 of 2008.
Repeal.
An Act to repeal the Tamil Nadu Tamil New Year (Declaration) Act, 2008.
BE it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the
Sixty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:—
1. (1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu Tamil New Year
(Declaration) Repeal Act, 2011.
   (2)  It shall come into force at once.
2. The Tamil Nadu Tamil New Year (Declaration) Act, 2008 is hereby
repealed.
(By order of the Governor)
G. JAYACHANDRAN,
Secretary to Government,
Law Department.
