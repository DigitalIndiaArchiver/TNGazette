Election of Fifteen Members to the Senate by the Registered Graduates
(No. ME2/Election/RRG/2010.)
No. VI-3(b)/37/2010.
It is hereby notified, by direction of the Vice-Chancellor, that an election will be held to elect Fifteen Members to the Senate
by the Registered Graduates of the University from among themselves, under Section 15(a) Class III (1) of the Madurai Kamaraj
University Act, 1965, as amended by Act 9 of 1992.
The election will be held in accordance with the system of proportional representation by means of the single transferable vote
and in accordance with the Statutes for regulating the method of elections to the authorities of the Madurai Kamaraj University.
All Graduates whose names are in the Register of Graduates as on 1st January 2010 are eligible to vote in such election.
Nominations of eligible candidates should be made in the form prescribed which may be obtained from the Registrar of the
University.  Nomination papers shall be enclosed in an envelope superscribed ‘Nomination to the Senate by Registered
Graduates’ as indicated below, and sent by Registered Post (Acknowledgment due) so as to be received by the Registrar within
the date and hour fixed or delivered either in person or by messenger to the Registrar within the last date and hour fixed.
Nomination papers that are not enclosed in an envelope, superscribed as stated above and sent by registered post or are not
delivered in person or by messenger as required above, shall be declared invalid.  An elector shall be eligible to subscribe either
as proposer or as seconder in nomination papers, only as many times as there are vacancies but not more and contravention of
the same will result in the rejection of the more than the times in accordance with the number printed in the nomination
paper i.e., the earlier 15 numbered nomination will be taken for consideration and the later number 16 or thereafter will be summarily
rejected as invalid.
