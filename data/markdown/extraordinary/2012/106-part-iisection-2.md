NOTIFICATIONS  BY  GOVERNMENT
CO-OPERATION, FOOD AND CONSUMER PROTECTION DEPARTMENT
NOTIFIES THAT THE STATE COMMISSION AT MADURAI SHALL HAVE THE JURISDICTION AND POWERS FOR THE TIME
BEING VESTED IN THAT STATE COMMISSION IN RESPECT OF CASES ARISING IN THE CERTAIN DISTRICTS UNDER
THE CONSUMER PROTECTION ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 54, Co-operation, Food and Consumer Protection (H2), 21st April 2012.]
No. II(2)/CFCP/217(c)/2012.
Under Section 17B of the Consumer Protection Act, 1986 (Central Act 68 of 1986), the Governor of Tamil Nadu,
in consultation with the State Commission, hereby notifies that the State Commission at Madurai shall have the
jurisdiction and powers for the time being vested in that State Commission in respect of cases arising in the Districts
of Dindigul, Kanyakumari, Karur, Madurai, Pudukkottai, Ramanathapuram, Sivaganga, Virudhunagar, Thanjavur, Theni,
Thoothukkudi, Tirunelveli and Tiruchirappalli in the State of Tamil Nadu.
M.P. NIRMALA,
Secretary to Government.
