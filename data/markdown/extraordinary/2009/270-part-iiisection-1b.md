ENVIRONMENT AND FORESTS DEPARTMENT
AMENDMENTS TO THE SPECIAL RULES FOR THE TAMIL NADU FOREST SUBORDINATE SERVICE.
[G.O. (Ms.) No. 140, Environment and Forests (FR-2), 13th October 2009.]
No. SRO B-57(a)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby makes the following amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Forest Subordinate Service (Section 12
of Part III-B, in Volume III of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1970.)
AMENDMENTS
 In the said Special Rules,—
(1) in rule 4, in sub-rule (b) for clause (i), the following clause shall be substituted, namely:—
“(i) No person shall be eligible for appointment as Forest Apprentice if he has not completed 18 years of the age
or has completed or will have completed 30 years of age on the 1st day of April of the year in which the selection
for admission to the Northern Forest Ranger’s College, Deharadun, or to the Tamil Nadu Forest Academy,
Coimbatore, is made:
Provided that in the case of candidates belonging to Scheduled Caste and Scheduled Tribes, Backward Classes,
Most Backward Classes / Denotified Communities and Destitute Widows of all Castes the maximum age limit
shall be 35 years:
Provided further that in the case of Ex-servicemen the maximum age limit shall be 37 years irrespective of caste.”;
(2) in rule 5, in the Table, in column (3), for the entries against the entry “Forest Apprentice” in column (1), the following
