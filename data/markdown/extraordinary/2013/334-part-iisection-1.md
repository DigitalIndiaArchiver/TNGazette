No. II(1)/PUMIS/21(c)/2013.
Under the Explanation to Section 25 of the Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881 (Central Act XXVI of 1881)
read with Notification of the Government of India, Ministry of Home Affairs No. 20-25-26, Public-1,
dated 8th June 1957, the Government of Tamil Nadu hereby declares that Friday 15th November 2013
shall be a Public Holiday on account of Muharram instead of the 14th of November 2013.
2. The above Public Holiday notified shall also apply to all State Government undertakings/
Corporations/Boards etc.
SHEELA BALAKRISHNAN,
Chief Secretary.
