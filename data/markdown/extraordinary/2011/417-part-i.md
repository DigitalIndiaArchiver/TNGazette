PUBLIC  DEPARTMENT

CERTAIN MINISTERS RELIEVED FROM THEIR MINISTERSHIP OF COUNCIL OF MINISTERS.
[G.O. Ms. No. 1073, Public (Special-B), 4th November 2011, Aippasi 18, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
The following notification shall be published in an Extraordinary issue of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette,
dated 4th November, 2011:—
No. I/PUSP/23(a)/2011.
I, K. Rosaiah, Governor of Tamil Nadu accept the recommendation of the Chief Minister that the following Ministers may
be relieved from the Council of Ministers and direct that they be relieved of their duties and responsibilities as Ministers with
immediate effect:—
1. Thiru C. Shanmugavelu, Minister for Rural Industries.
2. Thiru R.B. Udhayakumar, Minister for Information Technology.
3. Thiru S.P. Shanmuganathan, Minister for HR & CE.
4. Thiru N.R. Sivapathi, Minister for Animal Husbandry.
5. Thiru G. Senthamizhan, Minister for Information, Law, Courts and Prisons.
6. Thiru Budhichandhiran, Minister for Food.
K. ROSAIAH,
Governor of Tamil Nadu.
