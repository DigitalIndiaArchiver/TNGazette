CONFERMENT OF POWERS
(Roc. No. 68/2006/Con. B2)
No. VI(1)/357(a)/2010.
No. 181/2010.—In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 18(1) of the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 (Central
Act 2 of 1974) read with Section 32(1) of the said Code, on the request of the State Government, the High Court, Madras, hereby
appoints the following Presiding Officers of the Courts of Small Causes, Chennai, as Special Metropolitan Magistrates, for a period
of another one year with effect from 12-06-2010, to try and dispose of the cases under Section 138 of Negotiable Instruments Act,
which are pending in large number and which are being transferred from the files of Metropolitan Magistrate Courts, Chennai
from time to time and they shall exercise all the powers of Metropolitan Magistrate under the said Code for the said purpose:—
