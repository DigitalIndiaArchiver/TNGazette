No.II(2)/HO/1/2020.—Under Section 6 of the Delhi Special 
Police Establishment Act, 1946 (Central Act 25 of 1946), 
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby accords his consent to 
the extension of the powers and jurisdiction of members of 
the Delhi Special Police Establishment in the whole of the 
State of Tamil Nadu to investigate the case registered in 
Greater Chennai, Central Crime Branch-I Crime No. 327 of 
2019 registered under Section 174 of the Code of Criminal 
Procedure, 1973 (Central Act 2 of 1974).
	
S.K. Prabakar,
	
Additional Chief Secretary to Government.

No.II(2)/LE/2/2020.—Whereas, 
the 
Government 
by 
the Government Order first read above, have referred 
the dispute, with regard to lock-out of 19 workers of 
S.N.Y. Autotech Private Limited, Irungattukottai to Labour 
Court, Kancheepuram for adjudication;
And whereas the Government after considering the report 
of the Commissioner of Labour, the circumstances of the 
dispute and the reluctance of the Management and the 
Union to set right the issues through conciliation and as 
the, strike is going on for more than one year (i.e. from 
04.09.2018 to till date) are of opinion that the strike in 
M/s. S.N.Y. Autotech Private Limited, Irungattukottai, should 
be prohibited. 
Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred 
by Section 10(3) read with Section 23(b) of the Industrial 
Disputes Act, 1947, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby 
makes the following order :- 
“Pending adjudication of the industrial disputes referred 
for adjudication to the Labour Court, Kancheepuram in 
the Government order first read above, the Government 
prohibit the continuance of strike by the workmen of 
M/s. S.N.Y. Autotech Private Limited, Irungattukottai by this 
order, with immediate effect”.

housing and urban development Department
Amendment to the Guidelines for the Exemption of 
Buildings, 2017.
[G.O. Ms. No. 196, Housing and Urban Development [UD4(3)], 
20th December 2019.]
No.II(2)/HOU/13/2020.—In exercise of the powers 
conferred by Section 113-C of the Tamil Nadu Town and 
Country Planning Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972), 
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following 
amendment to the Guidelines for the Exemption of Buildings, 
2017, namely:-
Amendment.
In the said Guidelines, in guideline number 4, for the 
words “within thirty months”, the words “within thirty six 
months” shall be substituted.
