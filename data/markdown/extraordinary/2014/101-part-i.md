RELIEVED A MINISTER FROM THEIR MINISTERSHIP OF COUNCIL OF MINISTERS.
[G.O. Ms. No. 374, Public (Special-B), 20th May 2014, Vaikasi 6, Jaya,
Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2045.]
No.  I/PUSP/11(c-1)/2014.
I, K. Rosaiah, Governor of Tamil Nadu accept the recommendation of the Chief Minister that
Thiru K.P. Munusamy, Minister for Labour be relieved from the Council of Ministers and direct that he be relieved of his duties
and responsibilities as Minister with immediate effect.
K. ROSAIAH,
Governor of Tamil Nadu.
ALLOCATION OF SUBJECTS AMONG MINISTER
 [G. O. Ms. No. 375, Public (Special-B), 20th May 2014, Vaikasi 6, Jaya, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2045.]
No. I/PUSP/11(c-2)/2014.
Under Clause (3) of Article 166 of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby directs that
Thiru P. Mohan, Minister for Rural Industries shall hold full additional charge of the portfolio “Labour, Population,
Employment and Training, Newsprint Control, Census and Urban and Rural Employment” hitherto held by
Thiru K.P. Munusamy and direct that Thiru P. Mohan be designated as Minister for Rural Industries and Labour.
MOHAN VERGHESE CHUNKATH,
Chief Secretary.
