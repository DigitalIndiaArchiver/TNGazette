On the recommendations of the expert team of Doctors and Public Health specialists and based on the directives of 
Government of India, Ministry of Home Aﬀ airs, State-wide lockdown was extended from time to time and lastly extended till 
24:00 Hrs of 30.11.2020 under the Disaster Management Act, 2005 with certain restrictions and many relaxations along with 
guidelines in G.O.Ms.No.613, Revenue and Disaster Management (DM IV) Department, Dated : 31.10.2020.
2. Now, the Government after careful examination hereby issue following amendment to the G.O.Ms.No.613, Revenue 
and Disaster Management (DM IV), Department, Dated : 31.10.2020 as detailed below:
        The following activity shall be allowed under Caption-II  (Under Permitted Activities) except in containment 
zones:
         
“All Government functions like laying of foundation stones, inauguration of projects etc., through video conference 
which are structured in nature and within the closed space where entry and exit can be controlled shall be permit-
ted from 17.11.2020 with the number of persons permitted to participate in such functions shall not exceed 50% 
of the capacity of the hall/premises and  following the National Directives for COVID 19 management. Further prior
permission for conducting such function shall be obtained from Commissioner of Police in respect of Chennai District 
and from the District Collector in respect of other Districts.”
