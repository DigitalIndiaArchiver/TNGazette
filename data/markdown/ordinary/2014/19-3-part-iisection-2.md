WITHDRAWAL NOTIFICATION
[Letter No. 900/PD &SI(S.I) 2014, Planning Development
and Special Initiatives, 5th May 2014.]
No. II(2)/PDSI/332/2014.— Under Section 48 of the Land
Acquisition Act, 1894 (Central Act I of 1894) the Government
of  Tamil Nadu hereby withdraws the land specified in the
Schedule below and measuring 110.5 square meter for which
Declaration has been approved in G.O.Ms.No. 159, Planning,
Development and Special Initiatives (Sl) Department, dated
18-8-2011, and published in No. II(2)/PDSI/379(c)-4/2011
of Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary Issue No.
270, dated 18th August 2011 to wit, for the implementation
of Chennai Metro Rail Project.

Periyakudal Village,
Block 14, T.S. No. 5/1 (New No. 5/37), Registered Holder
Tamil Nadu State Housing Board, Classified as Ryotwari Manai,
out of extent declared 799.0 Sq.m. withdrawal  of New
T.S. No. 5/39 bounded on the North by T.S.No. 5/37, already
acquired land and west by T.S. No. 5/37, already acquired
land and south by T.S. No. 5/37 already acquired land and
East by T.S. No. 5/1—110.5 sq. m.
