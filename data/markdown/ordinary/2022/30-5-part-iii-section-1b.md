No. SRO B-38/2022—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, 
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following rules:-
2. The rules hereby made shall come into force on the date of issue of order.
Rඝඔඍඛ
The Tamil Nadu Government Servants (Conditions of Service) Act, 2016 and the Special rules applicable to the 
holders of the permanent post of Personal Assistant to the Director of Approved Schools and  Vigilance  Service  in the 
Tamil Nadu General Service shall apply to the holders of the temporary post of Assistant Director of Social Defence, 
subject to the modiﬁ cations speciﬁ ed in the following Rules:-
2. Constitution:- The post shall constitute a separate category in the said class of the said service.
3. Appointment:- Appointment to the posts shall be made by recruitment by transfer from among the holders of the 
post of Oﬃ  ce Superintendents and Oﬃ  ce Manager / Chief Store Keeper in the Government Children Homes / Vigilance Home 
in the Department of Social Defence in the Tamil Nadu Ministerial Service.
4. Appointing Authority:- The appointing authority for the post shall be the Government.
5. Qualiﬁ cations:- No person shall be eligible for appointment to the post unless,
(i) he possesses any degree from any of the University recognized by the University Grants Commission; and
(ii) he has put in a service for a period of not less than ﬁ ve years in the post of Oﬃ  ce Superintendent / Oﬃ  ce Manager / 
Chief Store Keeper in the Department of Social Defence.
6. Probation:- Every person appointed to the post shall from the date on which he joins duty, be on probation for 
a total period of one year on duty within a continuous period of two years.
7. Test:- Every  person appointed to the post shall, within the period of probation, pass the Account Test for Executive 
Oﬃ  cers:
Provided that the probationer who has already passed the Account Test for sub-ordinate oﬃ  cers Part-I, need not pass 
the Account Test for Executive oﬃ  cers.
8. Authority Competent to declare Probation:- The Director of Social Defence shall be the competent authority to 
declare the satisfactory completion of probation of the holder of the post.
9. Preparation of Annual list of approved candidates and crucial date:- A list of approved candidates for 
appointment to the posts by transfer shall be prepared annually and the crucial date on which the candidates should 
have acquired the prescribed qualiﬁ cation shall be the 1st September of every year.
 SHAMBHU KALLOLIKAR,
Additional Chief Secretary to Government.
