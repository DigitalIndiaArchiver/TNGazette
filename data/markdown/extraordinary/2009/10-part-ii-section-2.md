NOTIFICATION REGARDING THE  PROCUREMENT OF PROVISION OF COMMON USER GROUP MOBILE
PHONE SERVICES FROM BHARAT SANCHAR NIGAM LIMITED BY THE TAMIL NADU
POLICE DEPARTMENT UNDER TAMIL NADU TRANSPARENCY IN TENDERS ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 7, Finance (Salaries), 7th January 2009, Margazhi 23, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2039.] 
No. II(2)/FIN/22(b)/2009.
Under clause (f) of Section 16 of the Tamil Nadu Transparency in Tenders Act, 1998 (Tamil Nadu
Act 43 of 1998), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notifies the procurement of provision of Common User
Group Mobile Phone services from Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL) by the Tamil Nadu Police
Department.
K. GNANADESIKAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
