"In exercise of the powers conferred by Article 188 of the Constitution of India, I, BANWARILAL PUROHIT, 
Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby appoint Thiru. M. Appavu, Speaker, Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly and 
Thiru. K. Pitchandi, Deputy Speaker, Tamil Nadu Legislative Assemby, as the persons before whom the Oath 
or Affirmation may be made and subscribed by the Members of the Legislative Assembly under the said Article 
during the term of their office.
Raj Bhavan,	
BANWARILAL PUROHIT, 
Guindy,
