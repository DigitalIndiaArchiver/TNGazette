 NOTIFICATION REGARDING THE SPOT PURCHASE OF DOMESTICALLY PRODUCED TOOR DHAL FROM
THE PRODUCTION CENTRES SHALL BE MADE BY THE TAMIL NADU CIVIL SUPPLIES CORPORATION
FOR THE PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION SYSTEM UNDER THE TAMIL NADU TRANSPARENCY IN TENDERS
ACT, 1998
[G.O. Ms. No. 142, Finance (Salaries), 13th April 2009, Punguni-31, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. II(2)/FIN/160(a)/2009.
Under clause (f) of Section 16 of the Tamil Nadu Transparency in Tenders Act, 1998 (Tamil Nadu Act 43
of 1998), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notifies the spot purchase of domestically produced Toor Dhal
from the production centres shall be made by the Tamil Nadu Civil Supplies Corporation (TNCSC) for the
Public Distribution System till 30th June 2009.
K. GNANADESIKAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
