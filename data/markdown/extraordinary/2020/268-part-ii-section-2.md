The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed 
that the lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below have to be 
acquired for industrial purpose to wit for the formation of new 
Broad Gauge Railway line between Tindivanam and Nagari 
and it having already been decided that the entire amount 
of compensation to be awarded for the lands is to be paid 
out of the lands controlled or managed by District Collector, 
Tiruvannamalai the following notice is issued under sub-section 
(1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for 
Industrial Purposes Act, 1997.
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu 
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purpose Act.1997 (Tamil 
Nadu Act 10 of 1999), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby 
acquires the land / lands speciﬁ ed in the schedule below and 
measuring 118781 Sq.Mt of Wet and Dry Land to the same, 
a little more or less needed for Industrial Purpose, to wit for 
the formation of new Broad Gauge Railway line between 
Tindivanam and Nagari.
The plan of the lands are kept in the Collector’s Oﬃ  ce, 
Tivannamalai and in the oﬃ  ce of the Revenue Divisional 
Oﬃ  ce, Cheyyar, Tiruvannamalai district and may be inspected 
at any time during oﬃ  ce hours. 
Tiruvannamalai District, Vandavasi Taluk , 110.Mummuni Village.
