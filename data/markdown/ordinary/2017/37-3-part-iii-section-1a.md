No. SRO A-41/2017.—ln exercise of the powers conferred by Section 14 read with sub - section (4) of Section 12
of the Tamil Nadu Payment of Salaries Act, 1951 (Tamil Nadu Act XX of 1951), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes
the following amendments to the Tamil Nadu Legislators’ (Medical Attendance and Treatment to former Members)
Rules,  1982.
2. The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st July 2017.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Rules, -
(1) in rule 3, in the first proviso, for the expression “Rs.12,000/- (Rupees Twelve thousand only)”, the expression
“Rs. 25,000/- (Rupees Twenty five thousand only)” shall be substituted;
(2) in the APPENDIX, in Certificate under Form ‘C’, for the expression, “Rs.12,000/- (Rupees Twelve thousand
only)”, the expression “Rs. 25,000/- (Rupees Twenty five thousand only)” shall be substituted.
