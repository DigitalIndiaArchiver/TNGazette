TAMIL  NADU  GOVERNMENT  GAZETTE  EXTRAORDINARY
WHEREAS in the G.O. eighth read above  the Government have extended the restrictions in the territorial jurisdictions of 
the State to contain the spread of COVID-19 upto 03.05.2020 to contain the spread of COVID-19 by adhering the following 
Consolidated Revised Guidelines stipulated by Government of India under the Disaster Management Act, 2005. 
WHEREAS the Principal Secretary/Commissioner of Revenue Administration has stated that the Government of India, 
Ministry of Home Affairs in the order ninth read above has ordered to include the following in the Consolidated Revised 
Guidelines for strict implementation by State Government:
AMENDMENT TO NOTIFICATION
I. Sub-clause (viii) under Clause 6 'A' on All Agricultural and horticultural activities:
 
viii. Collection, harvesting and processing of Minor Forest Produce (MFP)/Non Timber Forest Produce (NTFP) by 
Scheduled Tribes and other forest dwellers in forest areas.
II. Sub-clause (iii) under Clause 6 'C' on Plantations:
	
iii.	 Bamboo, Coconut, Arecanut, Cocoa, spices plantation and their harvesting, processing, packaging sale and marketing.
III. Sub-clause (v) & (vi) under Clause 7 on Financial Sector:
 
v. Non-Banking financial institutions (NBFCs) including Housing Finance Campanies (HFCs) and Micro Finance 
Institutions (NBFC-MFIs) with bare minimum staff.
	
vi.	 Co-operative Credit Societies.
IV. Clause 16 (i) on construction activities in rural areas, includes, water supply and sanitation, laying/erection of 
power transmission lines and laying of telecom optical fiber and cable along with related activities.
NOW THEREFORE the State Government of Tamil Nadu based on the proposal of Principal Secretary and Commissioner 
of Revenue Administration ordered to include the following in the Consolidated Revised Guidelines for strict implementation.
I. Sub-clause (viii) under Clause 6 'A' on All Agricultural and horticultural activities:
 
viii. Collection, harvesting and processing of Minor Forest Produce (MFP)/Non Timber Forest Produce (NTFP) by 
Scheduled Tribes and other forest dwellers in forest areas.
II. Sub-clause (iii) under Clause 6 'C' on Plantations:
	
iii.	 Bamboo, Coconut, Arecanut, Cocoa, spices plantation and their harvesting, processing, packaging sale and marketing.
III. Sub-clause (v) & (vi) under Clause 7 on Financial Sector:
 
v. Non-Banking financial institutions (NBFCs) including Housing Finance Campanies (HFCs) and Micro Finance 
Institutions (NBFC-MFIs) with bare minimum staff.
	
vi.	 Co-operative Credit Societies.
IV. Clause 16 (i) on construction activities in rural areas, includes, water supply and sanitation, laying/erection of 
power transmission lines and laying of telecom optical fiber and cable along with related activities.
