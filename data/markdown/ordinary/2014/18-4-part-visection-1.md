JUDICIAL NOTIFICATIONS
Adjournment of Court of Small Causes, Chennai for Summer Vacation, 2014.
(Dis. No. 1031/2014.)
No. VI(1)/211/2014.
Except as hereunder mentioned the Presidency court of Small Causes, Chennai will remain closed for the Summer
Vacation, 2014 from 01-05-2014 to 01-06-2014 (both days inclusive).
The Summer vacation for the year 2014 of this Court will be from 01-05-2014 to 01-06-2014 (both days inclusive). The
First turn will be from 01-05-2014 to 16-05-2014 and the Second turn of the vacation will be from 17-05-2014 to
01-06-2014.
The Office of the Court of Small Causes, Chennai will function from Monday to Wednesday and Friday each
week from 10 A.M. to 4.30 P.M on Thursday (i.e.) court sitting day, from 10 A.M. to 5.45 P.M. during the summer
vacation 2014.
Tmt. S.D. Parimala, III Judge, Court of Small Causes, Chennai (Transfered as II Judge, Court of Small Causes,
Chennai) will be the Vacation Judge for First turn from 01-05-2014 to 16-05-2014.
Tmt. S. Meena Kumari, IV Judge, Court of Small Causes, Chennai (Transfered as III Judge, Court of Small Causes,
Chennai) will be the Vacation Judge for Second turn from 17-05-2014 to 01-06-2014.
Thiru. N. Sachidhanandam, XV Judge, Court of Small Causes, Chennai will be the Additional Vacation Judge for First
turn from 01-05-2014 to 16-05-2014.  The Additional Vacation Judge will deal with Rent Control Original petitions.
Tmt. B.S. Kalaivani, XIV Judge, Court of Small Causes, Chennai will be the Additional Vacation Judge for Second
turn from 17-05-2014 to 01-06-2014.  The Additional Vacation Judge will deal with Rent Control Original petitions.
Thiru. A. Phillip Nicolas Alex, Registrar, Court of Small Causes, Chennai will be the Vacation Officer for First turn
from 01-05-2014 to 16-05-2014.
Tmt. B.S. Kalaivani, XIV Judge, Court of Small Causes, Chennai will be the Vacation Officer for Second turn from
17-05-2014 to 01-06-2014.
The Filling Section will be functioning on every Wednesdays and the sitting of the Judges will be on every
Thursdays of the respective week during the summer vacation 2014.
The Vacation Judges unless otherwise notified will sit on all Thursday of every week during the vacation, to dispose the
emergent petitions. No fresh plaint petition, no Rent Control Appeals or other matters will be received during the period of
vacation, unless it is urgent and it is accompanied by an application duly supported by an affidavit showing the urgency.
The Copyist Section of the Court of Small Causes will be kept open from Monday to friday each week and between
10.30 A.M. to 3.00 P.M. for receiving copy application and for delivering the certified copies, which ware already made ready
and no fresh certified copy will not be made ready during the summer vacation.
The Nazir Section will function from Monday to Friday each week and Process Application will be received between
10.30 A.M. to 3.00 P.M. on Wednesdays, Warrants will be issued only on Wednesdays and Thursdays of the respective
weeks, which were already filed and made ready. Warrants which are being filed on Wednesday will be made ready and
will be issued only on the next days (i.e.) Thursday.  If the warrants returned for reasons, (i.e. since police protection not
available), the same will be re-issued only on next Wednesday or Thursday, if the Petitioner/Decree Holder wants to
execute the Warrants and makes such endorsement.
Since 01-05-2014 (Thursday) falls on Public Holiday Process Application will be received on 02-05-2014. Process
already filled and warrant made ready will be issued only on 02-05-2014 during the first week only.
The Office of the Registrar will be kept open from Monday to Friday each week but the urgent matters will be
received on Wednesdays during the vacation and such matters will be posted before the Vacation Judges and the Additional
Vacation Judges for the hearing on the succeeding Thursday in the open court.
