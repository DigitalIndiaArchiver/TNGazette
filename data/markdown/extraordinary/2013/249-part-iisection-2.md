CONSTITUTION OF SELECTION COMMITTEE FOR SELECTING THE CHAIRMAN OF THE TAMIL NADU
ELECTRICITY REGULATORY COMMISSION UNDER THE ACT.

No. II(2)/EGY/692(a)/2013.
In exercise of powers conferred by sub-section (1) of  Section 85 of the Electricity Act, 2003 (Central Act 36 of
2003), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby constitutes the Selection Committee consisting of the following Members for
the purpose of selecting a Member of the Tamil Nadu Electricity Regulatory Commission:—
1.
Thiru. Justice R. Regupathi,
-
Chairperson
(Retired Judge of the Madras High Court)
2.
The Chief Secretary to Government of Tamil Nadu
-
Member
3.
The Chairperson of the Central Electricity Regulatory Commission
-
Member
2. The Selection Committee shall,—
(a)  finalise the selection of member within three months from the date on which the reference is
made to it.
(b) 
recommend a panel of two names for every vacancy referred to it
and
(c) 
satisfy itself, before recommending any person for appointment as Member of Tamil Nadu Electricity
Regulatory Commission, that such person does not have any financial or other interest which is likely to
affect prejudicially his function as Member.
RAJESH LAKHONI,
Secretary to Government.
