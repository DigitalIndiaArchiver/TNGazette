HIGHER EDUCATION DEPARTMENT
Amendment to the Government order for Creation of
the lalpet Venkataratnam Endowment Under the
Charitable Endowments Act.
[G.O. Ms. No. 57, Higher Education (D2) 10th April 2013,
  Panguni 28,  Nandana, Thiruvalluvar Aandu 2044.]
No. II(2)/HE/359/2013.—Whereas, the Governor of
Tamil Nadu, in exercise of the powers conferred by Section
4 of the Charitable Endowments Act, 1890 (Central Act VI of
1890) ordered that Rupees five thousand be vested in the
Treasurer of charitable Endowments and be held by him and
his successors in office, subject to the provisions of the said
Act, and to any rules which from time to time, be or shall be
deemed to have been made thereunder upon trust for the
purposes and subject to the conditions setforth in the Scheme
settled under Section 5 of the said Act for the administration
of the Lalpet Venkataratnam Scholarship Endowment Fund;
And whereas, the High Court of Madras in its order dated
29th January 2010 in A. No. 79 of 2010 in TOS No. 18 of 1963
in the matter of the Estate of Lalpet Venkataratnam considering
the wish of the Testator, has directed the official Trustee of
Tamil Nadu, to contribute the sum of Rs. 2,56,457/- to the
“Lalpet Venkataratnam Scholarship Endowment” with a direction
that it shall form part of the corpus;
Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by
section 4 of the Charitable Endowments Act, 1890
(Central Act VI of 1890), the Governor of Tamil Nadu,
hereby makes the following amendment to the Education,
Science and Technology Department Notification
No. 19 published at pages 399-400 of Part II Section 2 of the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated 18th May 1983.
AMENDMENT
In the said Notification, under the heading  “The Schedule”
after the expression ” Rs. 5,000/- (Rupees Five thousand only)”,
the expression “and Rs. 2,56,457/- (Rupees Two lakhs fifty six
thousand four hundred and fifty seven only” shall be added.
APURVA VARMA,
Principal Secretary to Government.

PANCHAYAT RAJ DEPARTMENT
Disqualification of Thiru K. Victor alias K. Vasikaran
President of Gerugambakkam Panchayat, Kundrathur
Panchayat Union, Kancheepuram District under the
Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act.
[G.O. (D) No. 170, Rural Development and
Panchayat Raj (PR-4), 19-4-2013,  CˆF¬ó 6, Müò,
F¼õœÀõ˜ Ý‡´-2044.]
No. II(2)/RDPR/367/2013.—Whereas a question has arisen
also whether Thiru K. Victor alias K. Vasikaran of
Gerugambakkam Panchayat, Kundrathur Panchayat Union,
Kancheepuram District has become disqualified under clause
(e) of sub-section (3) of Section 38 of the Tamil Nadu
Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994), as he
does not belong to Scheduled Caste but has been elected
from the seat reserved for Scheduled Caste;
And Whereas, the opinion of the Tamil Nadu State Election
Commission has been sought for regarding the disqualification
of the said President of the said Panchayat, as required under
sub-section (2) of Section 41 of the said Act;
And Whereas, the Tamil Nadu State Election Commission
has conducted an enquiry on 21-2-2013. During the enquiry
Thiru K. Victor alias K. Vasikaran has stated that he has
obtained a Community Certificate as Hindu (Adi-Dravida) in
1996 and also in 2005. He has also obtained the same
certificate during the year 2011 for the purpose of contesting
in the local body election. He has also stated that his brother
is a Priest. Besides, he has produced a Notification
No. 22 Part IV Section-4 dated 15-6-2011 published in the
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette under the head of Individual
Notification. The  said Notification depicts that Thiru K. Victor,
Christian, son of Thiru T. Krishnan, born on 28th March 1972
(native district Kancheepuram) residing at No. 2/381,
Thiruvallur Street, Gerugambakkam, Kancheepuram District,
has re-converted to Hinduism with the name of K. Vasikaran
on 5th June 2011. It is mentioned in the Notification that it
cannot be considered as legal document when there is any
dispute over it;
And Whereas, the said commission has also stated that
on 
verification 
of 
the 
relevant 
documents 
of
Thiru K. Victor alias K. Vasikaran, it has been confirmed that
Thiru K. Victor alias K. Vasikaran has obtained a false
Community Certificate as Hindu Adi-Dravida for the purpose
of contesting in the local body election held in the month of
October 2011 and has opinted that the said Thiru
K. Victor alias Vasikaran, President of Gerugambakkam
Panchayat, Kundrathur Panchayat Union, Kancheepuram
District is disqualified to continue as President of the said
Village Panchayat under clause (e) of sub-section (3) of Section
38 of the said Act;
Now Therefore, under sub-section (1) of Section 41 of the
Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994)
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby decides that Thiru K. Victor
alias K. Vasikaran, President of Gerugambakkam Panchayat,
Kundrathur Panchayat Union, Kancheepuram District has
become disqualified from holding the office of President of the
said Village Panchayat under clause (e) of sub-section (3) of
section 38 of the said Act as he, being a Christian (Adi-Dravida),
has contested in the local body election held during the month
of October 2011 by producing a false Community Certificate
as Hindu (Adi-Dravida and holds the Post of President,
Gerugambakkam Panchayat which is reserved for
SC (Hindu).
C.V. SANKAR,
