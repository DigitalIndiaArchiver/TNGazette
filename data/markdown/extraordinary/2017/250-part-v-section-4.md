NOTIFICATIONS BY THE ELECTION COMMISSION OF INDIA
PLACE AND TIME FOR COUNTING OF VOTES FOR VICE-PRESIDENTIAL ELECTION, 2017
No. SRO G-31/2017.
The following Notification of the Election Commission of India, Nirvachan Sadan, Ashoka Road, 
New Delhi-110 001, dated 27th July, 2017 [5 Shravana, 1939 (Saka)] is republished:-
No. 480/2017-BE.—In pursuance of rule 27 of the Presidential and Vice-Presidential Elections Rules, 1974, the Election 
Commission of India hereby appoints the 5th August, 2017 (Saturday), as the day on which and 6.00 p.m. of that day as the time 
at which, the counting of votes for the Vice-Presidential Election, 2017 shall take place in the ofﬁ ce of Returning Ofﬁ cer in Room 
No. 62, First Floor, Parliament House, New Delhi.
(By order)
 
R.K. SRIVASTAVA, 
 
Senior Principal Secretary,
