The Governor of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied that
the lands specified in the Schedule below have to be
acquired for Highways purpose, to wit for improving and
widening to four lanes of Ennore Expressway and
Development of adequate road connectivity to Chennai and
Ennore Ports and it having already been decided that the
entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the lands
is to be paid out of the funds controlled or managed by the
Chief Engineer (General) Highways Department and after
having considered the cause shown by the owners of other
persons having interest in the said lands, as the case may
be, do hereby publish, the following Notice under sub-section (1)
of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001
(Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002).
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu
Highways Act 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the lands specified
in the schedule below and measuring 142.0 sq.metres to
be the same a little more or less are needed for Highways
purpose, to wit for improving and widening to Four Lanes
of Ennore Expressway and development of adequate
road connectivity to Chennai and Ennore Ports.
SCHEDULE
Tiruvallur District, Madhavaram Taluk,
47, Tiruvottiyur Village.
(Chennai Corporation Zone I)
Government, dry, ward J, Block 12, T.S.No.26 (Part) newly
sub divided as T.S.No. 26/2, Registered holders Indian
Standard Metal Company and interested persons
M/s.Indhumathi Refineries Private Limited and State Bank of
India bounded on the north by T.S.No. 26-1 and T.S.No. 25,
east by T.S.No. 25, south by T.S.No. 105 and west by
T.S. No.26-1—Area 77.0 Sq.metre.
Government, dry, ward J, Block No. 12, T.S.No. 105 (Part)
newly sub divided as T.S.No. 105-2, Registered holder
Bharath Petroleum Satheesh Service Station bounded on the
north by T.S.No. 26-2, east by T.S.No. 25, south by
T.S.No. 106 and T.S.No. 25 and west by T.S.No.105-1—Area
65.0 Sq.metre.
Total Area—142 Sq. metres.
