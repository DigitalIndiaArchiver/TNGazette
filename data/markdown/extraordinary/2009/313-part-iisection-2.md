HIGHWAYS AND MINOR PORTS DEPARTMENT
ACQUISITION OF LANDS
[G.O. Ms. No. 232, Highways and Minor Ports (HV2),
2nd December 2009, Karthigai 16, Thiruvalluvar
Aandu-2040.]
No. II(2)/HWMP/646(a)/2009.
The Governor of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied
that the lands specified in the schedule below have to
be acquired for Highways purpose, to wit for improving
and widening to Four lanes of Ennore Expressway and
Development of adequate  road connectivity to Chennai
and Ennore Ports and it having already been decided
that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded
for the lands is to be paid out of the funds controlled or
managed by the Chief Engineer (General), Highways
Department and after having considered the cause shown
by the owners or other persons having interest in the
said lands, as the case may be do hereby publish, the
following notice under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of
the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34
of 2002):—
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the
Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 3 of
2002), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby acquires the
lands specified in the schedule below and measuring
0 Hectare 11 Ares and 96 Square Metres to be the same
a little more or less are needed for Highways purpose,
to wit for improving and widening to Four lanes of Ennore
Expressway and development of adequate road connectivity to
Chennai and Ennore Ports.
The plan of the lands is kept in the Office of the Special
Tahsildar (L.A), Thiruvottiyur Ponneri Panchetty Salai Schemes,
Ponneri and may be inspected at any time during the office
hours.
SCHEDULE
Tiruvallur District, Ambathur Taluk,  No. 47. Thiruvottiyur
Village (Thiruvottiyur Municipality).
Government, dry, Ward No.1, Block No.1, T.S. No. 5-1 (Part),
newly sub-divided as T.S. No. 5-1B, Registered holders
Tvl. WIMCO Limited, Thiruvottiyur and interested person Joint
Commissioner (Land Reforms), Villupuram, bounded on the
north by  Ward-I, Block-1, T.S. No. 4-2, east by Block-2, south
by T.S. No. 5-2B and west by T.S. No. 5-1A—0 Hectare
04  Ares and 46 Square Metres.
Government, dry, Ward No. I, Block No. 1,  T.S. No. 5-2
(Part), now sub-divided as T.S. No. 5-2B, Registered holder
Tvl. M.R.F. Limited, Thiruvottiyur and interested  person Joint
Commissioner (Land Reforms), Villupuram, bounded on the
north by  Ward-I, Block-1, T.S. No. 5-1B, east by Block-2, south
by T.S. No. 6-2 and west by  T.S. No. 5-2A— 0 Hectare 07 Ares
and 50 Square Metres.
Total: 0 Hectare 11 Ares and 96 Square Metres.
