Government Pleader / Advocate as Additional Advocates General of Tamil Nadu in the High

Tamil Nadu, High Court of Madras, Chennai.

Tamil Nadu, Madurai Bench of Madras High Court, Madurai.

General-VIII of Tamil Nadu, High Court of Madras, Chennai.
4. Thiru. S.R. Rajagopal, Advocate as Additional Advocate General-IX of
