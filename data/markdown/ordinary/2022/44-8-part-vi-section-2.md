TAMIL NADU STATE LEGAL SERVICES AUTHORITY, CHENNAI-104.
Nomination of Chairman for the High Court Legal Services Committee.
(TNSLSA. No. 7399/E/2022)
No. VI(2)/100/2022.
In exercise of the powers conferred under sub-section (2) of Section 8A of the Legal Services Authorities Act, 
1987 (Central Act 39 of 1987) the Hon’ble the Acting Chief Justice, High Court, Madras is pleased to nominate 
Hon’ble Thiru. Justice S. Vaidyanathan, Judge, High Court, Madras to be the Chairman of the High Court Legal 
Services Committee with effect from 27.09.2022.
