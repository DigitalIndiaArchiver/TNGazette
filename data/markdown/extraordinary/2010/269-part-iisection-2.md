ENERGY  DEPARTMENT
APPOINTMENT OF CERTAIN PERSON AS MEMBER IN TAMIL NADU
ELECTRICITY REGULATORY COMMISSION UNDER THE ELECTRICITY ACT.
[G.O. Ms. No. 84, Energy (A1), 3rd September 2010.]
No. II(2)/EGY/554(d)/2010.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (5) of Section 82 of the Electricity Act, 2003 (Central
Act 36 of 2003), and on the recommendation of the selection committee referred to in Section 85 of the said Act,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints Thiru S. Nagalsamy, as Member, Tamil Nadu Electricity
Regulatory Commission.
P.W.C.  DAVIDAR,
Principal Secretary to Government.
