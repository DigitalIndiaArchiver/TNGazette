No. I/PUSP/ 3 /2010.—The following Notification of the
Government of India, Ministry of Law and Justice (Department
of Justice), Jaisalmer House, 26, Man Singh Road,
New Delhi-110  011, dated 15th February, 2010 is republished:—
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (1) of
article 224 of the Constitution of India, the President is pleased to
appoint (1) Shri T. Mathivanan, (2)  Shri Ardhanariswamy
Arumughaswamy and (3) Miss K.B.K. Vasuki, to be Additional
Judges of the Madras High Court, in that order of seniority, for a
period of two years with effect from the dates they assume charge
of their respective offices.
