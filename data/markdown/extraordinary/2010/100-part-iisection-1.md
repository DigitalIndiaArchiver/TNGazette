[G.O. Ms. No. 307, Public (Military), 1st April 2010.]
No. II(1)/PUM/6(b-2)/2010.
Under sub-section (3) of Section 9 of the Manoeuvres, Field Firing and Artillery Practice Act, 1938 (Central
Act V of 1938), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby publishes notice of his intention to issue, on or after the
expiry of two months from the date of the first publication of this notice in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette,
a notification under sub-section (2) of the said section, authorising the carrying out of field firing practices
over the whole or any part of the area specified in columns (1) and (2) of the Schedule below, during the
period specified in the corresponding entry in column (3) thereof:—
