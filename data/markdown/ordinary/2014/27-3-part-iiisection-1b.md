HOME DEPARTMENT
Amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu General Service
[G.O. Ms. No. 439, Home (Prison-I), 20th June 2014.]
No. SRO B-40/2014.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor  of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Special Rules for the
Tamil Nadu General Service (Section 16 in Volume II of the Tamil Nadu Service Manual, 1969).
AMENDMENT
In the said Special Rules, in Serial Number 5 under Class XLV Gazetted Assistant to Inspector General of Prisons,
for the expression “a period of one year” the expression “a total period of one year”  shall be substituted.
APURVA VARMA,
Principal Secretary to Government.
PUBLIC DEPARTMENT
Amendments to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu General Service
[G.O. Ms. No. 497, Public (Protocol-III), 27th June 2014.]
No. SRO B-41/2014.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India,
the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendments to the rules for Class XXVIII in the
Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu General Service, (Section 16 in Volume-II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1969).
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force with effect on and from the
1st November 1985.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Special Rules,—
(1) in Part I, the expression “Class XXVIII - Assistant Comptroller, Governor's Household” shall be omitted;
(2) in Part II, the heading “Class XXVIII -Assistant Comptroller, Governor's Household” and the rules thereunder
shall be omitted.
JATINDRA NATH SWAIN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
