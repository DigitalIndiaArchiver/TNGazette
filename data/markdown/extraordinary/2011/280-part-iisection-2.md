FINANCE DEPARTMENT

ENTRUSTMENT OF THE WAVE TRANQUILITY MODEL STUDIES OF COLACHEL FISHING HARBOUR TO
CENTRAL WATER AND POWER RESEARCH STATION UNDER THE TAMIL NADU TRANSPARENCY
IN TENDERS ACT, 1998.
[G.O. Ms. No. 227, Finance (Salaries), 25th August 2011, Aavani 8, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042.]
No. II(2)/FIN/383(c)/2011.
Under clause (f) of Section 16 of the Tamil Nadu Transparency in Tenders Act, 1998 (Tamil Nadu
Act 43  of 1998), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notifies the entrustment of the wave tranquility
model studies of Colachel Fishing Harbour to Central Water and Power Research Station, Pune for the
construction of Fishing Harbour at Colachel in Kanniyakumari District.
K. SHANMUGAM,
Principal Secretary to Government.
