No.II(1)/CFCP/27/2017.-The following Order of the Government of India, Ministry of Consumer Affairs, Food and
Public Distribution (Department of Food and Public Distribution), New Delhi, the 28th April, 2017 is republished:-
S.O.1346 (E).– In exercise of the powers conferred by  Section 3 of the Essential Commodities Act, 1955 (10 of 1955),
read with the clause 5 of the Sugar (Control) Order, 1966, the Central Government hereby makes the following amendment
in the Notification of the Government of India, in the Ministry of Consumer Affairs, Food and Public Distribution, Department
of Food and Public Distribution Number S.O.3348 (E), dated the 28th October, 2016, published in the Gazette of India,
Extraordinary, Part II-Section 3, sub-section (ii), dated the 28th October, 2016.
In the said Notification, in clause 2, for the figures, letters and words “28th day of April, 2017”, the figures, letters and
words “28th October, 2017” shall be substituted.
