No. I/HO/8/2010.—The following Notification of the Government
of India, Ministry of Home Affairs, New Delhi is republished:—
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-rule (2) of Rule 5 of
the Indian Police Service (Cadre) Rules, 1954 and with the
concurrence of the Government of Tamil Nadu and the Ministry of
Home Affairs, Government of India, the Central Government hereby
transfers Thiru Vikas Pathak, I.P.S. (Tamil Nadu: 2008) from IPS
Cadre of Tamil Nadu to the Rajasthan Cadre with immediate
effect on the ground of his marriage with Ms. Priti Chandra, I.P.S.
(Rajasthan: 2008).
[No. I-11014/9/2009-IPS.IV.]
