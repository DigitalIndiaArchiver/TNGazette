No. SRO B-9/2017.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 read with Article 313 of the
Constitution of India and of all other powers hereunto enabling, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following
amendments to the Fundamental Rules:-
2. The amendments hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st January, 2006.
AMENDMENTS
In the said Fundamental Rules,-
(1) in rule 9,-
(i) in Clause (8-B), for the expression “Grade Pay” the expression “Cadre Pay” shall be substituted;
(ii) after clause (8-B) as so amended, the following clause shall be inserted, namely:- “(8-C) Grade Pay means
the fixed amount payable in addition to the pay in the pay band”;
(iii)  in Clause (25), under the heading “Rulings”, in ruling (5) in the paragraph commencing with the expression
“For certain regular posts” and ending with the expression “special pay arise”, for the expression ‘grade pay’ the expression
`cadre pay’ shall be substituted;
(2) in rule 114, under the heading “Delegations under Rule 114”, in sub-rule 1, in clause (a), in “THE TABLE” for
the expressions “Grade Pay” “grade pay” and “Grade Pay” wherever they occur, the expression “cadre pay” shall be
substituted.

No. SRO B-10/2017.—In exercise of the powers conferred by Article 318 and the proviso to clause (3) of Article 320
of the Constitution of India, the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following amendment to the Tamil Nadu Public
Service Commission Regulations, 1954 (Volume-I of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual, 1987).
2. The amendment hereby made shall be deemed to have come into force on the 27th October 2015.
AMENDMENT
In the said Regulations, in Annexure I-A, under the heading “A. STATE SERVICES”, after the entry “124. A. Assistant
Surgeon and Assistant Surgeon (Dental) in the Tamil Nadu Medical Service”, the following entry shall be inserted,
namely:-
“124. B. Assistant Medical Officer (Siddha/Ayurvedha/Unani/Homeopathy) in the Tamil Nadu Medical Service.”
