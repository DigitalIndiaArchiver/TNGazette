In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 14 read with Section  12-D of the Tamil Nadu Payment of Salaries 
Act, 1951 (Tamil Nadu Act XX of 1951), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following rules:-
Rඝඔඍඛ
1. 
Short title and Commencement - (1)  These rules may be called the Tamil Nadu Legislators (Railway fare 
to former Members) Rules, 2018.
 
(2)  These rules shall be deemed to have come into force on the 13th August, 2018.
2. 
Deﬁ nitions.-  In these rules, unless the context otherwise requires,-
 
(a)  “Act” means the Tamil Nadu Payment of Salaries Act, 1951 (Tamil Nadu Act XX of 1951);
 
(b) “Assembly” means the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly;
 
(c) “Authorised Oﬃ  cer” means an oﬃ  cer travelling not below the rank of an Under  Secretary, Legislative 
Assembly Secretariat authorised by the Secretary to counter- sign and claim the railway fare bills of the former Members 
and to make payment of such railway fare;
 
(d) “Bank” means a Nationalised Bank;

(e) “Celebration” means Independence Day or Republic Day celebration held at Chennai.
 
(f) “Council” means the Tamil Nadu Legislative Council;
 
(g) “Form” means the forms appended to these rules;
 
(h) “Former Member”  means every Member, who had been a Member of the Legislative Assembly or the 
Legislative Council;
 
(i) “Secretary” means the Secretary to the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly; and
 
(j) “State” means the State of Tamil Nadu.
3. Applicability.- These rules shall apply only to journeys undertaken by the former Member for the purpose of participating 
in the Celebrations.
 4. Railway fare to former Members.-  Every former Member shall be eligible for railway fare for his journey in an 
accommodation not higher than that of two-tier air-conditioned class, excluding tatkal Charges, for attending the celebration from 
his usual place of residence to Chennai and back therefrom:
 
Provided that if the place of residence of the former Member happens to be outside the State, the actual railway fare shall 
be calculated from the railway station within Tamil Nadu which is nearest to his place of residence:
 
Provided further that railway fare under this section shall not be paid to any former Member, who is in receipt of free 
railway pass issued by the Central Government or by any other authority for having been a Member of Parliament.
5.  Authority for payment of railway fare to former Members.- The Secretary or the authorised oﬃ  cer shall be the 
authority competent to make the payment of railway fare to the former Member.
 6. Payment of railway fare.- (1) Every former Member, who has attended the celebration shall prefer the claim within two 
months as prescribed in Form-A appended to these rules and delivered at the oﬃ  ce of the Secretary.
 
(2)  If a claim for railway fare is preferred by a former Member after the expiry of three months from the date of its 
becoming due, such claim  shall be regarded as time-barred and rejected summarily.
7. Claims of payments for railway fare.- On receipt of the claim from the former Member, a bill for payment of such fare 
shall be prepared in Form-B appended to these rules, scrutinised and passed for payment by the Secretary or the authorised 
oﬃ  cer.
8. Mode of payment of railway fare.- The railway fare shall be paid by the Secretary or the authorised oﬃ  cer to the Bank 
Account of the former Member in which his pension is credited.
9. Death of Former Member.-  In the event of the death of a former Member, the unclaimed and undisbursed amount of 
railway fare for journeys for attending the celebration at Chennai, if any, shall be paid to his or her legal heir, after recovering the 
dues, if any, to the Legislative Assembly Secretariat or to the Government.
10. Receipt of payment of railway fare.- The former Member or in the event of death of a former Member, his or her legal 
heir shall furnish a stamped receipt in duplicate, for the receipt of the payment of railway fare in Form-C appended to these rules.
11. Over payments or wrong payments.-  If any over payment or wrong payment is made, the amounts so paid shall 
be adjusted in the subsequent payment payable under these rules, if any, or recovered from the pension payable to the former 
Member or in the event of the death of a former Member from his or her legal heir or recovered under the Tamil Nadu Revenue 
Recovery Act, 1864 (Tamil Nadu Act II of 1864) as if, it were an  arrear of land revenue.
12. Special Provisions.-  The decision of the Secretary on all matters relating to the payment of railway fare, not speciﬁ cally 
provided for in these rules, shall be ﬁ nal.
