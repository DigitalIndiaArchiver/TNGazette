It is hereby notiﬁ ed that the following election will be held under Section mentioned hereunder as per the Madurai 
Kamaraj University Act, 1965.
“Election will be held to elect ONE MEMBER to the Academic Council from each Post-Graduate College who is 
not the Principal of such College elected by the Teachers of the College including Physical Directors but excluding the 
Principal, Tutors, Demonstrators and Physical Instructors from among themselves under Section 24 (a) Class II-Other 
Members (4) of the Madurai Kamaraj University Act, 1965. The Principals of the Colleges concerned are requested to 
hold the election and communicate the result”.
