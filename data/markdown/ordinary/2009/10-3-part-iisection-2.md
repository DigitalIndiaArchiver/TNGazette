Variations to the approved Master Plan for the Erode
Local Planning Area under the Act.
[G.O. (2D) No. 80, Housing and Urban Development,
2nd March 2009, Maasi 18, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2040.]
No. II(2)/HOU/135/2009.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 32 of the Tamil Nadu
Town and Country Planning Act, 1971 (Tamil Nadu Act 35 of
1972), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the
following variations to the Master Plan for the Erode Local
Planning Area, approved under the said Act and published
with the Housing and Urban Development Department
Notification No.II(2)HOU/1104/90, at page 115 of Part II—
Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, dated the
28th February 1990:—
PRINTED AND PUBLISHED BY THE DIRECTOR OF STATIONERY AND PRINTING, CHENNAI
ON BEHALF OF THE GOVERNMENT OF TAMIL NADU
VARIATIONS
In the said Master Plan, under the heading “Land Use
Zones”, in the tabular column,—
(1) Under the heading “Industrial Zone”, in column (7),
against the entries “I 13” and “V.No.73/1 Erode Part (I)” in
columns (1) and (2) respectively, for the expression “225 to
317”, the expression “225 to 268; 269 (except 269-3B2, 4,8);
270; 271; 272; (except 272-2A1); 273 (except 273-1A); 274;
275 (except 275-4A, 4B, 5A, 5B, 6, 7A, 7B, 7C, 7D); 276 to
317” shall be substituted; and
(2) Under the heading “Residential Zone” under the
sub-heading “(1) Mixed Residential”, under the sub-heading
“Local Planning Area Villages”, in column (7), against the
entries “MR 18” and “V.No.73/1 Erode Part (I)” in columns (1)
and (2) respectively, after the expression “153 to 174;”, the
expression “269-3B2, 4, 8, 272-2A1; 273-1A, 275-4A, 4B, 5A,
5B, 6, 7A, 7B, 7C, 7D;” shall be inserted.
P. SATHYA SINGH,
Joint Secretary to Government.
Variations to the approved Master Plan for the
Thanjavur Local Planning Area under the Act.
[G.O.(2D) No. 85, Housing and Urban Development
(UD4-1), 6th March 2009.]
No. II(2)/HOU/136/2009.—In exercise of the powers
conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 32 of the
Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning Act, 1971
(Tamil Nadu Act 35 of 1972), the Governor of Tamil Nadu
hereby makes the following variations to the Master Plan for
the Thanjavur Local Planning Area approved under the said
Act and published with the Housing and Urban Development
Department Notification No. II(2)/HOU/3333/95, at Page
No. 879 of Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government
Gazette, dated the 11th October 1995.
VARIATIONS
In the said Master Plan in the “Zoning Schedules” in
the tabular column,—
(1) Under the heading “Agricultural Wet” in the fourth
column, against the entries “AGW-5”, “2” and “3” in the
first, second and third columns, respectively:—
(i)
for the expression  “parts of 82 to 86”,
the expression “82pt, 83, 84pt (except 84/2C, 4B, 5B), 85,
86” shall be substituted;
(ii)
for the expression “4420, 4421”, the
expression “4420 (except 4420/2)” shall be substituted;
(2) Under the heading “IV Educational Use Zone”,
in the first, second, third and fourth columns, above the
entry “E6” in the first column and the entries relating thereto
in the second, third and fourth columns, the following
entries shall, respectively, be inserted, namely:—
“E-5A”, “2”, “3” and “84/2C, 4B, 5B, 4420/2, 4421”.
SURJIT K CHAUDHARY,
Principal Secretary to Government.
