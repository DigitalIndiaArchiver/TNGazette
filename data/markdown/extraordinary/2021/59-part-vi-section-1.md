ACQUISTION OF LANDS 
(Rc. No. CPRRP/CLA/15(1)/004/2021.)
NOTICE UNDER SECTION 15(1) OF THE TAMIL NADU HIGHWAYS ACT, 2001 (TAMIL NADU ACT 34 OF 2002) 
READ WITH THE TAMIL NADU LAND ACQUISITION LAWS (REVIVAL OF OPERATION, AMENDMENT AND 
VALIDATION) ACT, 2019.
No. VI(1)/27(b)/2021.
The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisﬁ ed that the lands speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below are acquired 
under Chennai Peripheral Ring Road Project for Highways Department purpose to wit, for formation of road for providing 
connectivity from Ennore Port to Mahabalipuram (from K.M.31+197 to K.M.32+866) at No.80 Alapakkam Village, Uthukottai 
Taluk, Tiruvallur District and it having already been decided that the entire amount of compensation to be awarded for the 
lands to be paid out of the funds controlled and managed by the  Highways Department and after having considering 
the cause shown by the owners or other persons having interest on such lands, as the case may be, do hereby publish 
the following notice under  sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 
2002) read with the Tamil Nadu Land Acquisition Laws (Revival of Operation, Amendment and Validation)  Act, 2019.
NOTICE
Under sub-section (1) of Section 15 of the Tamil Nadu Highways Act, 2001 (Tamil Nadu Act 34 of 2002) read with 
the Tamil Nadu Land Acquisition Laws (Revival of Operation, Amendment and Validation) Act, 2019, the Governor of 
Tamil Nadu hereby notiﬁ es that the Dry, Wet and Manavari patta lands speciﬁ ed in the Schedule below and measuring 
to a total extent of 82,531 Sq.mtrs. are acquired in No.80 Alapakkam Village, Uthukottai Taluk, Tiruvallur  District to the 
same, a little more or less, needed for the purpose to wit, for formation of road for providing connectivity from Ennore 
Port to Mahabalipuram (Chennai Peripheral Ring Road Project) by Highways Department. 
The Plan of the lands under acquisition is kept in the oﬃ  ce of the Special District Revenue Oﬃ  cer (Land Acquisition), 
Chennai Peripheral Ring Road Project, Chennai-600 058 and in the oﬃ  ce of the Special Tahsildar (Land Acquisition), 
Chennai Peripheral Ring Road Project, Unit-II, Thatchur at Tiruvallur and may be inspected at any time during the oﬃ  ce 
hours.
