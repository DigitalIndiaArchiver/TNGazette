TAMIL NADU  WAQF BOARD ELECTION FOR MUTAWALLIS CATEGORY
No. II(2)/BCMBCMW/534(e)/2020.
 
The Elections for election of members to the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board from the electoral colleges consisting of Muslim 
Members of Parliament, State Legislature, Muslim Ex-Members of Bar Council and Mutawallis was notiﬁ ed in the Tamil Nadu 
Government Gazette Extraordinary dated 15.07.2020. As per the Notiﬁ cation, election was scheduled to be held on 19.08.2020.
 
 Whereas W.P. No.726/2020, W.P. No.8377/2020 and W.P. No.9557/2020 were ﬁ led before the Hon’ble High Court 
of Madras challenging the supersession of the Tamil Nadu Waqf Board and the election notiﬁ cation for reconstituting the 
Tamil Nadu Waqf Board. In the above Writ Petitions, Hon’ble High Court of Madras in its order dated 17.8.2020 set aside the 
order of the supersession of the Tamil Nadu Waqf Board and the consequential orders insofar as the election of two persons 
namely, Thiru. Syed Ali Akbar and Dr. Haja K. Majeed, alone are concerned. 
 In view of the above orders of the High Court of Madras, the election scheduled to be held on 19.08.2020  for electing 
two members in the category of Mutawallis was deferred.
Whereas in the Special Leave Petition ﬁ led by the State of Tamil Nadu (Diary No.17419/2020) against the orders of the 
High Court of Madras in W.P. Nos.726/2020, 8377/2020 and 9557/2020 on 28.08.2020, the Supreme Court of India has 
passed the following orders:-
“Permission to ﬁ le Special Leave Petition without Certiﬁ ed / Plain copy of the impugned order is granted. Issue Notice. 
Learned Counsel appearing for the caveators accept notice.  Two weeks time, as prayed for is granted to ﬁ le Counter 
Aﬃ  davit.  There shall be a stay of the operation of the impugned judgment dated 17.08.2020 passed by the High 
Court of Madras.”
 
In view of above, in exercise of the powers conferred under rule 8 of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board (Conduct of Election 
for Members) Rules 1997 and in partial modiﬁ cation of the earlier Notiﬁ cation No.284 published in Tamil Nadu Government 
Gazette in Part II – Section 2, dated 15.07.2020, the Election  Authority hereby notiﬁ es the election for Mutawallis category, 
subject to the outcome of the Special Leave Petition ﬁ led by the Government of Tamil Nadu (Diary No.17419 / 2020) in the 
Hon’ble Supreme Court of India and subject to further orders of the Hon’ble Supreme Court of India.

NOTICE OF ELECTION
 
(a) the election will be held on 9th September 2020 (Wednesday) between 10.00 A.M. to 4.00 P.M. at the Oﬃ  ce of 
Tamil Nadu Waqf Board, No.1, Jaﬀ ar Syrang Street, Vallal Seethakathi Nagar, Chennai – 600 001 for electing two members 
in the category of Mutawallis.
 
(b) The votes already polled through postal ballot and received will be taken into account for the purpose of election.
 
(c) Those who have opted for postal ballot but not yet cast their votes will be able to cast their votes on 9th September 
2020 at the polling booth established for the purpose at the Oﬃ  ce of Tamil Nadu Waqf Board, No.1, Jaﬀ ar Syrang Street, Vallal 
Seethakathi Nagar, Chennai – 600 001. However such a member will be required to give an undertaking in the prescribed 
format that he has not cast his postal ballot / or not received it. This will be veriﬁ ed with the list maintained at the polling 
booth. Only those who have not cast their vote through postal ballot will be allowed to vote.
 
(d) Those who have not opted for postal ballot can cast their votes at the polling booth.
 
(e) Counting will be taken up on 10th September 2020 (Thursday).
