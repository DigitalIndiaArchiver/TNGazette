CHENNAI  PORT  TRUST.
Chennai Port Trust (General Provident Fund) Amendment Regulations, 2020
(RRC/3064/2019/GA)
No. SRO C-6/2020.
The following Notiﬁ cation GSR No. 27 (E) dated the 13th January 2020 issued by  the  Government of India, Ministry of 
Shipping and published in Part II Section 3 sub-section (i) of the Extraordinary issue of the Gazette of India, dated 13th January 
2020 is republished hereunder as required under Section 132(2) of the Major Port Trusts Act, 1963 :-
GSR No. 27 (E). – In exercise of the powers conferred by Sub-section (1) of Section 124, read with Sub-section (1) of 132 of 
the Major Port Trusts Act, 1963 (38 of 1963), the Central Government hereby approves the Chennai Port Trust (General Provident 
Fund) Amendment Regulations, 2020, made by the Board of Trustees of the Chennai Port Trust, as set out in the Schedule 
annexed to this Notiﬁ cation.
2.The said Regulations as approved shall come into force on the date of publication of this notiﬁ cation in the Oﬃ  cial Gazette. 
SCHEDULE
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 28 of the Major Port Trusts Act, 1963 (38 of 1963), the Board of Trustees 
of the Chennai Port hereby makes the following Regulations further to amend the Chennai Port Trust (General Provident Fund) 
Regulations, 1997, namely:- 
1. 
(1)These regulations may be called the Chennai Port Trust (General Provident Fund) Amendment Regulations, 2020;
 (2)They shall come into force on the date of publication of the approval thereto by the Central Government in the 
Gazette of India.
2.      In the Chennai Port Trust (General Provident Fund) Regulations, 1997, regulation-14 shall be omitted. 
Foot Note: - The principal regulations were published in the Gazette of India, extraordinary, vide number G.S.R.282(E) dated the 
 27th May, 1997 and was subsequently amended vide number No..G.S.R 51(E) dated the 17th January, 2000
