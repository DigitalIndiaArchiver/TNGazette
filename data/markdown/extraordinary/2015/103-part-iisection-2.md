In exercise of the powers conferred under Section 12(1) and (3) of the Tamil Nadu Physical Education and
Sports University Act, 2004, His Excellency the Governor-Chancellor hereby appoints Dr. A.M. Moorthy, Former
Principal, Koviloor Andavar College of Physical Education, Koviloor, Sivagangai residing at No. 8, 9th Street,
Subramaniapuram, Karaikudi - 630 003, Sivagangai District as the Vice-Chancellor, Tamil Nadu Physical Education
and Sports University, Chennai for a period of three years with effect from the date of his assuming office or till he
attains the age of seventy, whichever is earlier.
HEMANT KUMAR SINHA,
