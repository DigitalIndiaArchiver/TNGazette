ACT No. 30 OF 2010.
An Act further to amend the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act, 1983.
BE it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the
Sixty-first Year of the Republic of India as follows:—
1. (1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies(Fourth Amendment)
Act, 2010.
    (2) It shall come into force at once.
2.  In section 89 of the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act, 1983, in the proviso to
sub-section (1), for the expression “nine years and six months”, the expression “ten years”
shall be substituted.
(By order of the Governor)
