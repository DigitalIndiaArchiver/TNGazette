LAW DEPARTMENT
THE TAMIL NADU ADMISSION IN LAW INSTITUTIONS RULES, 2018.
 [G.O.Ms.No.187,  Law (LS), 28th June 2018, Aani 14, Vilambi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2049.]
No.SRO A-36(a)/2018.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of Section 10 of the Tamil Nadu Admission in 
Professional Educational Institutions Act, 2006 (Tamil Nadu Act 3 of 2007), the Governor of Tamil Nadu 
hereby makes the following Rules:-
RULES.
1.
Short title.- These Rules may be called the Tamil Nadu Admission in Law Institutions Rules, 2018.
2. 
Deﬁ nitions.- In these Rules, unless the context otherwise requires,-
(i)
“Act” means the Tamil Nadu Admission in Professional Educational Institutions Act, 2006 
(Tamil Nadu Act 3 of 2007);
(ii)
words and expressions used but not deﬁ ned in these Rules shall have the meaning respectively 
assigned to them in the Act.
3.
Authority to make admission.- The admission to every Government seat in Law Institutions shall be made 
by the Tamil Nadu Dr. Ambedkar Law University, Chennai.
4.
Relevant subjects.- The relevant subject for admission to seats in Law Institutions shall be as follows:-
(i) 
for 5 year B.L. Degree Course including Honors Degree Course, the percentage of marks obtained 
by the candidates in the Plus Two examinations in aggregate of all the subjects, excluding languages, 
shall be taken into account;

(b)
by lot;
6.
Information to be mentioned in the prospectus.- Information relating to admission to seats in Law Institutions 
shall be given in the prospectus issued in this behalf by the authority concerned.
