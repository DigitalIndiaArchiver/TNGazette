ENERGY DEPARTMENT
AUTHORIZATION OF THE TAMIL NADU ELECTRICITY BOARD TO CONTINUE TO FUNCTION AS
THE STATE TRANSMISSION UTILITY AND A LICENSEE UNDER THE ELECTRICITY ACT, 2003.
[G.O. Ms. No. 104, Energy (B2), 16th December 2009.]
No. II(2)/EGY/690(b)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to clause (a) of Section 172 of the
Electricity Act, 2003 (Central Act 36 of 2003), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby authorizes the
Tamil Nadu Electricity Board to continue to function as the State Transmission Utility and a licensee
under the provisions of the said Act for a further period upto 15th March 2010 beyond
15th December 2009 as mutually decided  by the Central Government and this Government.
P.W.C. DAVIDAR,
Secretary to Government.
