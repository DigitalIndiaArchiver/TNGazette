In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (b) of sub-section (1) of Section 20 of the Tamil Nadu 
Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1974 (Tamil Nadu Act 13 of 1974) and in supersession of the Home Department 
Notiﬁ cation No.II(2)/HO/445/2008, Published at page 321 of Part II—Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Government 
Gazette, dated the 1st October 2008,  the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby exempts  all  Battery Operated 
Vehicles, both Transport and Non-Transport (Electric Vehicles) from payment of motor vehicle tax under that 
Act for the period commencing on and from the 3rd November 2020 and ending with the 31st December 2022.
Explanation:-For the purpose of this Notiﬁ cation, ‘battery operated vehicle’ shall have the meaning assigned 
to it in clause (u) of rule 2 of the Central Motor Vehicles Rules, 1989.
