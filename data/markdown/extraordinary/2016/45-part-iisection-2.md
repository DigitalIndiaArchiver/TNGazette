Under  sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Municipal Laws (Amendment) Act, 2015
(Tamil Nadu Act 12 of 2015), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints the 20th February, 2016 as the
date on which the said Act shall come into force.
