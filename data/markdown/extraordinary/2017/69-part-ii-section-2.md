No. II(2)/FIN/184(f-1)/2017.
Under clause (f) of Section 16 of the Tamil Nadu Transparency in Tenders Act, 1998 (Tamil Nadu Act 43 of 1998), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notifies the entrustment of the consultancy services to undertake the Comprehensive
Environmental Impact Assessment Study for  the proposed Athikadavu - Avinashi Irrigation, Groundwater Recharge and
Drinking Water Supply Scheme in Tamil Nadu on nomination basis to Messrs Water and Power Consultancy Services
(WAPCOS) Limited, Chennai.
K. SHANMUGAM,
Additional Chief Secretary to Government.
[G.O. Ms. No. 71, Finance (Salaries), 20th March 2017, Panguni 7, Thunmugi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu 2048.]
No. II(2)/FIN/184(f-2)/2017.
Under clause (f) of Section 16 of the Tamil Nadu Transparency in Tenders Act, 1998 (Tamil Nadu Act 43 of 1998), the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby notifies the entrustment of conducting feasibility study and preparation of Detailed Project
Report to NABARD consultancy services (NABCONs) for setting up of Women Bank under National Rural Livelihood
Promotion (NRLP) by Tamil Nadu Corporation for Development of Women Limited.
K. SHANMUGAM,
Additional Chief Secretary to Government.
