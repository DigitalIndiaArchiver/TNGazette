LAW DEPARTMENT
APPOINTMENT OF CERTAIN PERSON AS VICE-CHANCELLOR OF THE TAMIL NADU 
DR. AMBEDKAR LAW UNIVERSITY, CHENNAI FOR CERTAIN PERIOD UNDER THE TAMIL NADU 
DR. AMBEDKAR LAW UNIVERSITY ACT.
[G.O. (Rt.) No. 31, Law  (LS), 22nd March 2018, Panguni 8, Hevilambi, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2049.] 
No. II(2)/LAW/294(c)/2018.
In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-sections (1) and (3) of Section 12 of the Tamil Nadu 
Dr. Ambedkar Law University Act, 1996 (Tamil Nadu Act 43 of 1997), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby appoints 
Dr. Tamma Suryanarayana Sastry, Professor and Co-ordinator, HREP. Department of Law, Savitribai Phule 
Pune University, Pune as the Vice-Chancellor of the Tamil Nadu Dr. Ambedkar Law University, Chennai, for a 
period of three years from the date of his assuming ofﬁ ce.
     
S.S. POOVALINGAM,
 
Secretary to Government.
