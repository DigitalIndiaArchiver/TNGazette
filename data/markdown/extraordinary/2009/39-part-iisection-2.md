CONSTITUTION OF AN ADDITIONAL SUB-COURT AT KARUR IN KARUR DISTRICT
UNDER THE TAMIL NADU CIVIL COURTS ACT.
NOTIFICATION-I
[G.O. Ms. No. 140, Home (Courts-III), 12th February 2009.]
No. II(2)/HO/72 (a-1)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 4 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central
Act III of 1873), the Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby alters the number of Subordinate Judges to be appointed under the
said Act for Karur District as three with effect on and from the date on which the Additional Subordinate Judge, Karur
assumes charge of that Court.
NOTIFICATION-II
[G.O. Ms. No. 140, Home (Courts-III), 12th February 2009.]
No. II(2)/HO/72 (a-2)/2009.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 4 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central
Act III of 1873), the Governor of Tamil Nadu after consultation with the High Court, Madras, hereby varies the number of
Subordinate Judges to be appointed to the Subordinate Court, Karur as two with effect on and from the date on which
the Additional Subordinate Judges, Karur assumes charge of that Court.
S. MALATHI,
Principal Secretary to Government.
