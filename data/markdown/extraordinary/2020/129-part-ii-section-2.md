The Government of Tamil Nadu having been satisfied 
that the lands specified in the schedule below have to be 
acquired for Industrial Purposes, to wit, in favour of Mine-1A 
Expansion Scheme of NLC India Limited and it having already 
been decided that the entire amount of compensation to be 
awarded for the lands is to be paid out of the funds controlled 
or managed by NLC India Limited.  The following notice is 
issued under sub section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu 
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997. 
Notice
Under sub section (1) of Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu 
Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 
(Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1999). The Governor of Tamil Nadu is 
hereby acquires the lands specified in the Schedule below and 
measuring 3.78.0 hectares be the same a little more or less 
needed for Industrial purposes, to wit, in favour of Mine-1A 
Expansion Scheme of NLC India Limited. 
 The plans of the lands are kept in the office of the Special 
Deputy  Collector / Land Acquisition, Neyveli-2 and may be 
inspected at any  time during office hours.
