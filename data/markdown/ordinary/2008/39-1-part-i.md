ENVIRONMENT AND FORESTS DEPARTMENT
[G.O. (D) No. 243, Environment and Forests (FR.Spl. A),
2nd September 2008.]
No. I/EF/34/2008.—Thiru Deepak Siddappa Bilgi, IFS,
(Probationer), appointed on the basis of the results of 2006
Indian Forest Service Examination and allotted to Tamil Nadu
Cadre of Indian Forest Service shall undergo “On the Job
Training” (Practical Training and Office Training) for the period
of 36 weeks from the date of the joining and the first leg of
training will commence in Vellore Division.
N. SUNDARADEVAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
PUBLIC DEPARTMENT
(Special-A)
[G.O. Ms. No. 1030, Public (Special-A), 8th September
2008, Avani 23, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2039.]
No. I/PUSP/35/2008.—The following Notification of the
Government of India, Ministry of Law and Justice
PRINTED AND PUBLISHED BY THE PRINCIPAL SECRETARY TO GOVERNMENT AND
COMMISSIONER OF STATIONERY AND PRINTING, CHENNAI ON  BEHALF OF THE GOVERNMENT OF TAMIL NADU
(Department of Justice), Jaisalmer House, 26, Man Singh
Road, New Delhi-110 011, dated 5th September, 2008
is republished:—
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (1) of
article 224 of the Constitution of India, the President is pleased
to appoint Shri P.R. Shivakumar and Shri G. Rajasuria, to be
Additional Judges of the Madras High Court, in the same
order of seniority, for a period of two years with effect from
18th September, 2008.
[K.13024/2/2008-US.I]
RAMESH  ABHISHEK,
Joint Secretary to the Government of India.
(By Order of the Governor)
K.S. SRIPATHI,
Chief Secretary.
