AMENDMENTS TO NOTIFICATION
No. VI(2)/118(b)/2019.
Whereas, in the Tamil Nadu Municipal Laws (Fifth Amendment) Ordinance, 2019 (Tamil Nadu Ordinance 8 
of 2019), the Government have issued amendment to the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920, Chennai 
City Municipal Corporation Act, 1919, Madurai City Municipal Corporation Act, 1971, Coimbatore City Municipal 
Corporation Act, 1981 relating to change in the manner of election of Mayor of Corporations, Chairman of the 
Municipalities and Town Panchayats from Direct to Indirect method.
Now, Therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred under clause 11 of the Tamil Nadu Local 
Bodies Election Symbols (Reservation and Allotment) Order, 2019, the following amendments are issued 
to the said Order published vide Notiﬁ cation No.362, at pages 1 to 16 of Part VI—Section 2 of the 
Tamil Nadu Government Gazette Extraordinary dated 13-09-2019:-

as the case may be, in any urban Local Body" shall be omitted.
