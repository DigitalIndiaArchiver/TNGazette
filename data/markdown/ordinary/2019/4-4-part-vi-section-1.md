GENERAL NOTIFICATIONS
Grant of No Objection Certificate
(Rule 36 in Part – II and Rule 106 in Part – VII)
FORM  B 
(Rc. No. E2(1)/717/126716/2018)
No.VI(1)/34/2019.
In exercise of powers conferred in Rule 36 in Part-II / Rule 106 in Part-VII of Tamil Nadu Cinemas (Regulation) 
Rules, 1957, it hereby grants this certificate that there is "No Objection" from police point of view is issued to 
TvI. Suresh Ravindran & T.R. Jayanandan, Old No.  18, New No. 39, Nungambakkam High Road, Chennai-34 for the 
construction of a permanent multiplex Theatre containing 10 screens with Restaurant at Survey No.  3/488 B1A and 
3/700, No. 17, East Coast Road, Uthandi Village, Sholinganallur (TK), Kancheepuram District situated comes under the 
licencing authority of Commissioner of Police, Greater Chennai Police. 
The "No Objection Certificate" is valid for a period of five years as per G.O. Ms No. 324 Home (Cinema-I) 
Department, dated 16-04-2002 in the case of permanent cinema, from the date of this order. If the proposed cinema 
theatre is not constructed within this period, the applicant should apply for a fresh "No Objection Certificate". 
The No Objection Certificate is also subject to the following special condition:-
 "The applicant should get NOC from Traffic point of view for the proposed restaurant after completion of 
the seating arrangements".
Dated this 31st day of December 2018.
Vepery, Chennai - 600 007,	
A.K. VISWANATHAN,
