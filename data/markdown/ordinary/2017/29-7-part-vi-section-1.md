JUDICIAL NOTIFICATIONS
Notification for Re-Designation of Court at Vaniyambadi in Vellore District
(Roc. No. 2273/2010/G/Judn)
No.VI(1)/201/2017.
Whereas the Government of Tamil Nadu have issued orders in G.O.(Ms) No. 206, Home (Courts-III) Department,
dated 3-3-2015 that a separate District Munsif Court be constituted at Vaniyambadi by converting the existing Additional
District Munsif Court, Vaniyambadi and in G.O.(Ms) No. 302, Home (Courts-II) Department, dated 05-04-2017, that the
existing Principal District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Vaniyambadi be converted as separate Judicial Magistrate
Court at Vaniyambadi.
Hence, it is hereby informed that on and from the date on which the District Munsif Court and Judicial Magistrate
Court at Vaniyambadi are constituted, the existing Principal District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Vaniyambadi
shall be re-designated as Judicial Magistrate Court, Vaniyambadi and the existing Additional District Munsif Court,
Vaniyambadi shall be re-designated as District Munsif Court, Vaniyambadi.
Constitution of Sub-Court at Mettupalayam in Coimbatore District
(Roc. No. 2709/2010/G/Judn)
No.VI(1)/202/2017.
“In exercise of the powers  conferred under the first proviso to Section 13 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873
(Central Act III of 1873), the High Court, Madras hereby directs that appeals from the decrees or orders of the District
Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Mettupalayam be preferred in the court of Subordinate Judge, Mettupalayam with
effect from the date on which the Subordinate Judge, Mettupalayam assumes charge of that court.”
High Court Madras,
R. SAKTHIVEL,
