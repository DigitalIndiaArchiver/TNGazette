HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE DEPARTMENT
Amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Medical Service.
[G.O. Ms. No. 251, Health and Family Welfare (B2), 17th October 2016, Iyppasi 1, Thunmugi,
Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2047.]
No. SRO B-75/2016.—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to Article 309 of the Constitution of India, the
Governor of Tamil Nadu hereby makes the following Amendment to the Special Rules for the Tamil Nadu Medical Service
(Section 21 in Volume II of the Tamil Nadu Services Manual 1969).
The amendments hereby made may be deemed to have come into force on the date of issue of this notification.
AMENDMENT
In the said Special Rules in Part-II under the heading Branch I-Medical, after rule 22, the following rule shall be added,
namely:-
23. Special provisions regarding service conditions of Assistant Surgeons (Dental) appointed on or after the date of this
notification.
(a) The appointing authority in  respect of Assistant Surgeon (Dental) recruited on or after the date of issue of Notification
shall be the Director of Medical and Rural Health Services.
(b) The leave, posting and transfer of Assistant Surgeon (Dental) shall be made by the appointing authority.
J. RADHAKRISHNAN,
Principal Secretary to Government.
