(i) Constitution of a separate District Munsif Court at Karaikudi by converting the existing Principal District

Karaikudi by converting the existing Additional District Munsif Court at Karaikudi in Sivagangai District.
(Roc. No. 1154/A/2015/G/Judn.)
No.VI(1)/99/2021.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 5 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 
1873), the High Court, Madras, hereby ﬁ xes Karaikudi in Sivagangai District as the place at which the District Munsif 
Court, Karaikudi, shall be located. 
NOTIFICATION-II
(Roc. No. 1154/A/2015/G/Judn.)
No.VI(1)/100/2021.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 11 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III 
of 1873), the High Court, Madras, hereby directs that in Sivagangai District, the District Munsif Court, Karaikudi, 
shall have local jurisdiction over the entire Karaikudi Taluk in Sivagangai District with eﬀ ect from the date of constitution 
of a separate District Munsif Court at Karaikudi by converting the existing Principal District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate 
Court, Karaikudi.
NOTIFICATION-III
(Roc. No. 1154/A/2015/G/Judn.)
No.VI(1)/101/2021.
 The High Court, Madras hereby directs and notiﬁ es that the District Munsif, Karaikudi in Sivagangai District, shall 
exercise all the powers conferred on a District Munsif under Section 12 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 
(Central Act III of 1873) as amended up-to-date in regard to original suits and proceedings not otherwise exempted 
from his cognizance of which the amount or value of the subject matter does not exceed One Lakh Rupees within the 
local limits of his jurisdiction with eﬀ ect from the date of constitution of a separate District Munsif Court at Karaikudi 
by converting the existing Principal District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Karaikudi
NOTIFICATION-IV
(Roc. No. 1154/A/2015/G/Judn.)
No.VI(1)/102/2021.
In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 28 of the Tamil Nadu Civil Courts Act, 1873 (Central Act III of 1873) 
the High Court, Madras,  hereby directs that the District Munsif, Karaikudi, shall have and exercise  jurisdiction of a 
Court of Small Causes under the provincial Small Causes  Court Act, 1887 for the trial of suits cognizable by a Court 
of Small Causes  upto the pecuniary limits of Rs.5000/- (Rupees ﬁ ve thousand only) with  eﬀ ect from the date of 
constitution of a separate District Munsif Court at  Karaikudi by converting the existing Principal District Munsif-cum-Judicial 
Magistrate Court, Karaikudi.

Whereas the Government of Tamil Nadu in G.O.(Ms.) No.601, Home (Cts.llI) Department, dated 08.11.2019 have 
issued orders for constitution of a separate District Munsif Court at Karaikudi by converting the existing  Principal District 
Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Karaikudi and in G.O.(Ms.) No.165, Home (Cts.II) Department, dated 17.03.2020 have 
issued orders for constitution of separate Judicial Magistrate Court at Karaikudi by converting the existing Additional District 
Munsif Court at Karaikudi. 
Hence, it is hereby informed that on and from the date on which the District Munsif Court and Judicial Magistrate Court 
at Karaikudi are constituted, the existing Principal District Munsif-cum-Judicial Magistrate Court, Karaikudi are re-designated 
as District Munsif Court, Karaikudi and the existing Additional District Munsif Court, Karaikudi as Judicial Magistrate Court, 
Karaikudi.
