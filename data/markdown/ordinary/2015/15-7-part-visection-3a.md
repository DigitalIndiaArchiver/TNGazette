GENERAL  NOTIFICATIONS
TAMIL NADU WAKF BOARD, CHENNAI.
Extension of Direct Management of Mohideen Andavar Pallivasal and Dargah Wakf,
Musiri Taluk, Pulivalam, Trichy District.
[Rc. No. 12378/97/B5/Try]
No. VI-3(a)/12/2015.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under section 65 of the Wakf Act 1995, as amended by
Act 27/2013, the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in item No. 40/15 in Rc. No. 12378/97/B5/Try, dated : 05-03-2015
has resolved to ratify the period of direct management from 22-10-2014 to 04-03-2015 and also to extend the period of
direct management of MOHIDEEN ANDAVAR PALLIVASAL AND DARGAH WAQF, MUSIRI TALUK, PULIVALAM, TRICHY
DISTRICT which is under the Direct Control of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a further period of one year from the date
of Board's Order (05-03-2015).
Chennai-600 001.
A. MOHAMED MEERAN,
6th April 2015
District Revenue Officer /
Chief Executive Officer (I/c.)
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
Taking over of Syed Sadak Mosque, Mangadoo Village, Poonamallee (P.O.) Sriperumbudur Taluk,
Kancheepuram District under Direct Management.
(Rc. No. 10731/B9/04/KPM)
No. VI-3(a)/13/2015.
In exercise of the powers conferred as per the provisions under section 65 of the Wakf Act 1995, as amended by
Act 27/2013, the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board in its resolution in item No. 152/12 in Rc. No. 10731/B9/KPM dated : 02-01-2013
has ordered to take over the SYED SADAK MOSQUE, MANGADOO VILLAGE, POONAMALLEE (P.O.), SRIPERUMBUDUR
TALUK, KANCHEEPURAM DISTRICT under the direct management of the Tamil Nadu Wakf Board for a Period of one year
from the date of pronouncement of Board's order dated 03-03-2015 and to appoint the Superintendent of Wakfs, Poonamallee
as its Executive Officer.
Chennai-600 001.
A. MOHAMED MEERAN,
6th April 2015
District Revenue Officer /
Chief Executive Officer (I/c.)
Tamil Nadu Wakf Board.
